 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1/3 cup half & half 
 1/8 teaspoon  salt 
 1 cup plus 2 tablespoons caramel sauce 
 1 1/2 teaspoons  vanilla extract 
 1/2 cup  sour cream 
 1/3 cup  sliced almonds , lightly toasted 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice  and half & half in 2-quart saucepan. 
Cook over medium heat 6 to 7 minutes, stirring constantly. 
Stir in  salt  and 3/4 cup caramel sauce. 
Cook 10 minutes, stirring constantly. 
Remove from heat and stir  vanilla  and  sour cream . Cool 15 minutes. 
Divide pudding into 6 serving dishes. 
Drizzle each serving with 1 tablespoon caramel sauce and  almonds .
