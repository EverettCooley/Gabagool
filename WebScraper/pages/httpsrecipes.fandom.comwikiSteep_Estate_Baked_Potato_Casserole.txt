Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Steep Estate in Elmo, Texas in 1989.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 8 cooked peeled and diced  potatoes 
 1 pound  American cheese  sliced in strips 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 ½ cup chopped white  onion 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 2 teaspoons freshly ground  black pepper 
 ½ pound fried  bacon  drained and copped 
 Directions : 
 Combine  potatoes ,  cheese ,  mayonnaise  and  onion  and place in casserole dish and top with  bacon . 
 Bake at 325°F for 1 hour then season with salt and  pepper .
