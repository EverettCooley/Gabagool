 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 white sauce 
 1 tbsp betty's  butter 
 1 tbsp  arrowroot 
 1 cup  evaporated milk 
 1/8 tsp  salt 
 1/8 tsp  white pepper 
 Cheese  sauce 
 1/4 cup low-fat  Cheese  sauce 
 -shredded 
 Directions : 
 Heat  butter  in a small non-stick skillet, then in  arrowroot . Gradually add  milk , cook stirring until sauce thickens. Stir in  salt  &  pepper . Variations: thicker sauce...add more  butter  and more  arrowroot  for each cup of  milk .  Cheese  sauce: add ¼ cup shredded low-fat  Cheese  to hot sauce; cook, stirring, until melted.
