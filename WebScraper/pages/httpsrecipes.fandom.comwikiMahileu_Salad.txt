 Ingredients : 
 100 g boiled  beef 
 1  onion 
 1  carrot 
 200 g  black radish 
 2  eggs  (hard boiled) 
 4 tbsp  mayonnaise 
 pepper 
 salt 
 greens 
 Directions : 
 Slice the boiled  beef . 
 Cut the  onion  in half rings, cut the  carrot  and  black radish  into shoestrings, mince the  eggs  finely. 
 Mix the ingredients, add  salt ,  pepper  and  mayonnaise . 
 Decorate the salad with greens.
