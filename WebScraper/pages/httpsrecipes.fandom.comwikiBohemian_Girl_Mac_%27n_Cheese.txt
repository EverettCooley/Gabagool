 Description : 
 Always check the ingredients to make sure the product is vegan.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/4 cup  flour 
 1/2 cup  nutritional yeast 
 1/3 cup soy marg 
 1 1/2 cup  water 
 1-2 sloshes soy sauce 
 2 tsp  mustard 
 pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Toast  flour  and  yeast  in a pan until they smell nutty. 
 Add marg and stir til bubbly. 
 Add  water  and cook until it thickens, stirring constantly. 
 When it is thick, stir in the remaining ingredients. 
 Stir this into a pot of shells or elbows or  penne .
