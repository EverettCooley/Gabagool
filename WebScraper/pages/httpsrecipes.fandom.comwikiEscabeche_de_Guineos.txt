green  banana  salad
 
 Ingredients : 
 5 lbs. Green  bananas 
 2 large Spanish  onions 
 1 head of  garlic 
 2 tbsp  black olives 
 1/2 cup virgin  olive oil 
 salt  (to taste) 
 pepper  (to taste) 
 Directions : 
 Boil the  bananas  in  salt   water  until cooked, about 20 minutes. 
 Cut  onions  into thin slices. Take  garlic  and puree in food chopper or blender, add  olive oil ,  salt  and  pepper  to taste (you may add one envelope of plain sazon). Cut  bananas  into 1/2 inch pieces (straight circles). Add  bananas ,  garlic ,  onions , and  olive oil , mix well. 
 Place in oven for 20–30 minutes at 325¡ F. Remove and serve warm. this dish can be served with  rice , meat, pasteles, or alone.
