An apple pie shake is a delicious treat! It's a very good recipe for parties, or just in the summer heat!
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups of cooled spiced  apple cider  of your choice 
 A scoop of vanilla  ice cream  of your choice 
 canned  whipped cream 
 apple slice, garnish 
 Directions : 
 Put 2 cups of cooled apple cider and ice cream in the blender 
 Blend the mixture until mixed 
 Pour contents into a glass and put the whipped cream on it 
 Garnish with the apple slice and enjoy!
