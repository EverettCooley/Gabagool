      Sassy Sloppy Joes

 2   Directions

 3   See also 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound ground  Beef 
 1 medium  Onion , chopped (1/2 cup) 
 1/3 cup chopped  celery 
 1/3 cup chopped green  bell pepper 
 1/3 cup  ketchup 
 1/4 cup  water 
 1 tablespoon  worcestershire sauce 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1/8 teaspoon red pepper sauce 
 6 hamburger buns, split and toasted 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Beef  and  Onion  in 10-inch skillet over medium heat 8 to 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until  Beef  is brown; drain.  
Stir in remaining ingredients except buns. 
Cover and cook over low heat 10 to 15 minutes or just until vegetables are tender. Fill buns with mixture.
 
 Makes 6 servings
