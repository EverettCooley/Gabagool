Description : 
 An uncommon combination of  cinnamon  with  garlic  and Italian herbs season this simple  chicken  dish.
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Submitted by Jenni Schmidt to ediets.com 
 Prep Time: 10 Minutes | Cook Time: 30 Minutes | Total Time: 40 Minutes 
 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 4 skinless, boneless  chicken breast  halves 
 1 tsp ground  cinnamon 
 2 tbsp  Italian-style seasoning 
 1½ tsp  garlic powder 
 3 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). 
 Place  chicken  in a lightly greased 9x13 inch baking dish. 
 Sprinkle evenly with ground  cinnamon , seasoning, garlic powder,  salt  and  pepper  ( note : you can be liberal with the seasoning,  garlic powder ,  salt  and  pepper ; however,the  cinnamon  should only be a little bit). 
 Bake at 350°F (175°C) for about 30 minutes or until  chicken  is cooked through and juices run clear. 
 Nutritional information : 
 138.65 Calories | 1.74 Total Fat | 3.01g Total Carbs | 27.75g Protein
