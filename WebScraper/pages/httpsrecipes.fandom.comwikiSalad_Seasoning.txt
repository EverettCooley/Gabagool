 ¾ teaspoon  salt  or  salt substitute

 ½ teaspoon  celery seeds 
 ¼ teaspoon  garlic powder 
 ¼ teaspoon coarse ground  black pepper 
 dash  cayenne pepper . 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients
