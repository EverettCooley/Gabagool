 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 6  eggs 
 6 tablespoons  Sugar 
 6 tablespoons breadcrumb 
 1 tablespoon  flour 
 1 tablespoon  baking powder 
 Sauce: 
 1 cup  water 
 2 cups  Sugar 
 1 cup  dark rum 
 Directions : 
 In a blender, mix all ingredients. 
 Pour into a greased baking pan, and bake at 350 for 45 minutes. 
 Let sit for 5 minutes 
 Mix sauce ingredients and bring to a boil. 
 Pour over cake
