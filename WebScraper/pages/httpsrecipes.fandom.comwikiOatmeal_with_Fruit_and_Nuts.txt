Ingredients : 
 3 cups  water 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 1 small  apple , chopped 
 2 tbsp sliced  almonds 
 2⅔ cup  skim milk 
 ½ tsp ground  cinnamon 
 1⅓ cup regular  rolled oats 
 ¼ cup pitted whole  dates , snipped 
 1 tbsp  brown sugar 
 Directions : 
 In a medium saucepan combine  water ,  cinnamon , and  salt . 
 Bring to boiling; stir in  oats . 
 Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
 Let stand, covered, till of desired consistency. 
 Divide  oatmeal  among 4 serving bowls. 
 Top each bowl with chopped  apple , snipped  dates , [[almond. 
 Sliced almonds|sliced almonds]], and  brown sugar . 
 Divide  milk  among serving bowls. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving: 
 
 204 calories | 7g protein | 38g carbohydrates | 4g fat | 1mg cholesterol | 146mg sodium | 315mg potassium
