Ingredients : 
 1 large or 2 medium  onions 
 2 hard boiled  eggs 
 1 cup  walnuts 
 1 small can  peas , drained 
 Directions : 
 Chop onions and saute until colour changes (mixture should be slightly liquidy). 
 Process hard boiled eggs and walnuts. 
 Mash drained peas. 
 Mix all together well.
