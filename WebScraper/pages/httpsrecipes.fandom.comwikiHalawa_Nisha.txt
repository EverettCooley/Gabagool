Description : 
 A corn flour sweet
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 tablespoons  corn flour 
 5 tablespoons  sugar 
 1.2 litre (2 pints)  water 
 1 tablespoon  rose water 
 1 teaspoon ground  cardamom 
 handful ground  pistachios  or  almonds 
 Directions : 
 Place  water  in a large saucepan, add the  corn flour  and mix well until texture is smooth. 
 Add  sugar ,  rosewater , ground  cardamom  and mix well. 
 Place saucepan on medium heat stirring constantly for about 5 minutes until mixture thickens. 
 Spoon the mixture onto a serving platter. 
 Decorate with ground pistachios or  ground almonds  whilst mixture is still hot.
