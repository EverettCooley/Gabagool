 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup  water 
 1 10-ounce package frozen chopped  spinach 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 cup (8 ounces) dry curd  cottage cheese 
 1 cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon seasoned  pepper 
 1/4 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 5  eggs , divided 
 1 cup fine dry bread crumbs, divided 
 
 Directions : 
 Bring  water  to a boil in small saucepan. Add  spinach  and cook just until it is thawed. Drain and squeeze dry. Combine  spinach ,  rice , cheeses,  salt ,  pepper  and  garlic powder  in mixing bowl. Beat 3  eggs  just enough to blend  yolks . Add to  rice  mixture and mix well. 
 
 Grease a 13x9x2-inch baking pan. Coat with 2/3 cup crumbs. Spoon  rice  mixture over crumbs. Beat remaining 2  eggs  until frothy. Brush over  rice  mixture. Sprinkle with remaining crumbs. Bake at 350 degrees (325 degrees if using glass dish) 30 minutes or until firm. Cut into 1-1/4-inch squares. Serve warm or cold.
