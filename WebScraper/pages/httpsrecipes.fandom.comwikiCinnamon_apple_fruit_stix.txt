 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 24 oz Jar MOTTS  cinnamon   Apple 
 -Sauce 
 2 tsp  cinnamon 
 1/2 tsp  food coloring , if desired 
 Directions : 
 1 Heat oven to 250. 
2 Line jelly roll pan with plastic wrap, securing ends with tape. 
3 In medium bowl, combine all ingredients. 
4 Pour into prepared pan; spread evenly. 
5 Bake at 250 °F for 1 ¼ hours. 
6 Turn off oven. 
7 Let stand in oven 2 to 3 hours until dry. 
8 Cut into 8 squares carefully remove plastic wrpa. 
9 Roll up jelly roll fashion. 
10 8 snacks. 
11 tip: for ease in spreading, place another piece of plastic wrap on top of  Apple  sauce mixture; spread evenly with knife. 
12 Remove top piece of plastic wrap before baking.
