 Ingredients : 
 3 x 2½ oz each jars of baby food: either  beef  or  chicken 
 ¼ cup  dry milk powder 
 ¼ cup  wheat germ 
 Directions : 
 Combine ingredients in a bowl and mix well. 
 Roll into small bails and place on well-greased cookie sheet. 
 Flatten slightly with a fork. 
 Bake in preheated 350°F oven for 15 minutes or until brown. 
 Cool on wire racks and store in refrigerator. 
 Also freezes well. 
 Notes:  cream of wheat  can be substituted for the  wheat germ .
