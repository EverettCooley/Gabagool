Description : 
 Contributed  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ pound  ground beef 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 3½ cups water 
 2  beef bouillon  cubes 
 1 can diced  tomatoes  with peppers 
 ½ cup frozen  corn 
 ½ cup frozen  peas 
 ¼ cup  fast cooking rice 
 Directions : 
 Brown meat and garlic in medium saucepan. 
 Add other ingredients except for rice, bring to boil and simmer for 5 minutes. 
 Add rice and simmer for 5 more minutes. 
 Garnish with sliced green onion.
