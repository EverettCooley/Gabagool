Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source:  Come into the Kitchen 
 Serves 4 to 6 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound dry  white beans 
 2 quarts  water 
 1  onion , chopped 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 2 tsp  olive oil 
 1  ham hock   or   ham  bone,  or  ¼ pound diced  salt pork 
 1  tomato , peeled and chopped 
 ½ tsp  thyme 
 1 tbsp  wine vinegar 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Soak  beans  in  water  overnight and drain. 
 If  salt pork  is used, sauté it in a skillet until browned, adding  onion  and  garlic  during the last few minutes, or sautéing  onion  and  garlic  in  oil . 
 Add to crock cooker along with  beans ,  water  and all other ingredients except  vinegar ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Cook on low setting 10 to 12 hours. 
 At serving time, stir in  vinegar , mash  beans  slightly and add  salt  and  pepper  to taste.
