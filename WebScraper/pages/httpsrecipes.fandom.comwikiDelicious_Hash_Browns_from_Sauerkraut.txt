      Delicious Hash Browns from Sauerkraut     
 Description : 
 For 2 persons
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 finely chopped  shallot 
 40 g of lean diced  ham 
 1 teaspoon  butter 
 250 g of raw  sauerkraut 
 25 g  flour 
 1  egg 
 1  egg yolk 
 60 g diced  feta  cheese 
 1 tablespoon  oil 
 1 pinch each  salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Simmer the shallots with the ham in hot butter glaze and then put the mixture into a mixing bowl. 
 The drained sauerkraut, flour, eggs, egg yolks, add feta cheese, salt and pepper and mix all ingredients well. 
 4 balls from the dough forms, flatten and in hot oil about 4 minutes each side until golden brown.
