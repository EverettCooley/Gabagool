 Ingredients : 
 0.75 shot De Kuyper Crème de Bananes 
 0.5 shot De Kuyper Blue  curaçao 
 0.5 shot Stolichnaya  vodka 
 Top up  orange juice 
 garnish: Slice of  banana 
 Glass: Wine Glass 
 Directions : 
 Mix the Crème de Bananes, the Blue  curacao  and the  vodka  together in a cocktail shaker. 
 Pour into a hurricane glass half filled with crushed  ice . Top up with  orange juice . 
 Mix together with a muddling spoon. Garnish with a slice of  banana  if desired.
