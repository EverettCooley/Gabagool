Description : 
 Vietnamese Goi Buoi is a salad that is also known under the name of " pomelo  and crab salad". It is a refreshing salad.
 
       Goi Buoi Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 crab  meat 
 chicken breasts 
 carrots 
 cucumbers 
 pomelo  fruit 
 fish sauce 
 mint  leaves 
 ground  peanuts 
 chopped  cilantro 
 shallots 
 Directions : 
 Mix the ingredients, toss them in a bowl and add condiments if needed.
