Description : 
 Makes: 10 servings, ½ cup each
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups boiling  water 
 1 pkg (8-serving size) Jell-O brand  strawberry daiquiri flavor gelatin 
 ice  cubes 
 1 cup cold  water 
 2 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1½ cups sliced  strawberries 
 Directions : 
 Stir boiling  water  into dry  gelatin  mix in large bowl at least 2 minutes. 
 Until completely dissolved. 
 Add enough  ice  to cold  water  to measure 2 cups. 
 Add to  gelatin ; stir until slightly thickened. 
 Remove any unmelted  ice . 
 Add  lemon juice ; stir until well blended. 
 Refrigerate 15 minutes or until thickened (spoon drawn through leaves definite impression). 
 Gently stir in  strawberries . 
 Pour into 1½-qt. serving bowl; cover.
