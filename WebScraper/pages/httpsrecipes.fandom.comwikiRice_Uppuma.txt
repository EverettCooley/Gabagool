Description : 
 Oh la yummy rice uppuma (rice pudding) is ready to serve with coconut chutney or mango pickle.
 
 Ingredients : 
 200 g of raw  rice 
 1 tablespoon of  thor dhall  ( lentil ) 
 1 tablespoon of  pepper 
 cumin  seeds 
 cooking  oil 
 1  red chili 
 mustard seed 
 hing 
 500 ml of water 
 curry leaves , for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Take the raw rice, add the thor dhall, pepper and cumin seeds. 
 Dry grind in the mixer the contents to the consistency slightly larger than sugar. Say like breadcrumbs. 
 In a sauce pan, take a tablespoon of cooking oil, deep fry a red chili, mustard, hing. 
 Add the water and allow it to boil. 
 Add the rice, dhall, pepper mixture into the pan. Keep stirring till it becomes a paste. 
 Cover the pan with a lid and leave for 10 minutes. If required add more water till the rice is cooked. 
 Switch off the stove. 
 Garnish with curry leaves.
