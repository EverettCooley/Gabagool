Description : 
 Making this dessert in parfait glasses or beautiful dessert bowls adds to the fun, especially for kids, because you can see all the tasty layers you're about to enjoy.
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Source:  South Beach Diet Taste of Summer Meal Plan 
 Prep time: 15 minutes | Cook time: 10 minutes 
 Makes 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup slivered  almonds 
 ⅔ cup part-skim  ricotta cheese 
 ⅔ cup low-fat or nonfat plain  yogurt 
 2 tbsp granular  sugar substitute 
 3 medium  peaches , cubed (2 cups) 
 ground  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Heat the oven to 275 degrees f. 
 Spread almonds on a baking sheet and toast, stirring occasionally, until fragrant and golden, about 10 minutes. 
 Transfer to a plate to cool. 
 In a blender or food processor, combine ricotta, yogurt, and sugar substitute and process until smooth. 
 Spoon ¼ cup of the peaches into each of 4 parfait glasses or dessert dishes. 
 Spoon 2 tablespoons of the ricotta mixture over the peaches. 
 Sprinkle with a pinch of cinnamon and ½ tablespoon of the almonds. 
 Top evenly with remaining peaches, remaining ricotta mixture, a pinch of cinnamon, and remaining almonds. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per 1 cup serving:
 
 160 Calories | 8g Fat | 2.5g Saturated fat | 9g protein | 15g Carbs | 2g Fiber | 80mg Sodium
