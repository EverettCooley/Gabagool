Ingredients : 
 1 cup 2%  milk 
 1 cup frozen unsweetened  strawberries 
 1/2 cup frozen unsweetened  raspberries 
 3 tablespoons  sugar 
 1 cup ice cubes 
 Instructions : 
 Place the milk, berries and sugar in a blender 
 Cover and process until smooth. 
 Add ice cubes; cover and process until smooth.
