Ingredients : 
 1½ cups  all purpose flour 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 ½ tsp  mace  or  nutmeg 
 ½ tsp  salt , optional 
 ½ tsp  orange rind , grated 
 1½ cups non-fat  yogurt , plain 
 1 tbsp  cornstarch , dissolved in ¼ cup  non-fat milk 
 1 large  apple , peeled chopped 
 2 tsp  vanilla extract 
 oil  to grease pan 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Sift  flour ,  sugar ,  baking soda ,  cinnamon ,  mace  and  salt  into large bowl. 
 Add  orange  rind,  yogurt , dissolved  cornstarch ,  apple  and vanilla. 
 Blend by hand until well mixed. 
 Spoon into greased 9x13" baking pan. 
 Bake 350°F for 35 – 40 minutes until a knife inserted in center comes out clean. 
 Serve warm or cool.
