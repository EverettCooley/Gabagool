 Description : 
 The official guacamole for Cinco de  mayo !
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 large  California avocados , peeled 
 1 (8-ounce) container  sour cream 
 ½ Cup  orange juice 
 ¼ Cup  lime juice 
 1 to 2  garlic  cloves 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Pulse all ingredients in a food processor until smooth, stopping to scrape down sides. 
 Cover and chill 1 hour. Serve over salad greens, and top with  mandarin oranges  and  avocado  slices.
