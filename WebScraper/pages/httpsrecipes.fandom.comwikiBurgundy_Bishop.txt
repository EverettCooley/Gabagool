 Description : 
 A  rum  drink
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  light rum 
 red wine 
 Juice of 1/4  lemon 
 1 tsp  powdered sugar 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Shake with  ice  and strain into a glass half filled with  ice cubes . 
 Fill with  red wine  and stir gently. 
 Top with an  orange  wedge and a  cherry .
