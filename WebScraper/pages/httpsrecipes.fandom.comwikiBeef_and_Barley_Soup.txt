Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Cookbook: Caring & Cooking for the Hyper-Active Child 
 Author: Mary Jane Finsand 
 Typed By: Susan alexanderjamesmom@gmail.com 
 Yield: 1½ qt. (1.5 l) 
 Ingredients : 
 2 qt.  beef stock  (post later) 2 l 
 ½ cup  barley  125 ml 
 ½ cup  onions  (diced) 125 ml 
 3  potatoes  (cooked and diced) 3 
 2 cups  beef  (cooked and diced) 500 ml 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Combine beef stock, barley, and onions in soup kettle. 
 Bring to boil; reduce heat. 
 Cook for 1 hour or until barley is tender. 
 Add potatoes and beef. 
 Add salt and pepper to taste. 
 Cook 15 minutes. 
 Serve hot. 
 Microwave : 
 Combine as above. 
 Cook on high for about 5 minutes and reduce heat to low. 
 Cook 20 minutes. 
 Add remaining ingredients. 
 Cook 5 minutes. 
 Serve hot.
