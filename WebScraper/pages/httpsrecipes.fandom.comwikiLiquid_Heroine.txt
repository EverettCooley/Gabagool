Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz  Rumple Minze® peppermint liqueur 
 1½ oz  Jägermeister ®  herbal liqueur 
 Directions : 
 Mix Rumple Minze and  Jägermeister  in an iced shaker and strain into a shot glass.
