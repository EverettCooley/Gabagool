Description : 
 Excellent with mashed  potatoes  and  broccoli  or  carrots . Leftovers will keep well, covered and refrigerated, up to 4 days or freeze up to 2 months.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: Chatelaine Magazine, November 2005 
 Prep. time: 8 minutes 
 Cooking time: 4 to 6 hours 
 Makes 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 bottle  beer 
 10 oz can undiluted  mushroom soup 
 Â½ cup  BBQ sauce 
 2 to 3 lbs  pork shoulder 
 Directions : 
 In bowl of slow cooker, whisk  beer  with soup and BBQ sauce. 
 Mixture will be lumpy. 
 Remove string from  pork  and trim off excess fat. 
 Place  pork  in beer mixture. 
 Cover and cook till  pork  is tender (4 to 5 hours on high or 6 to 8 hours on low)
