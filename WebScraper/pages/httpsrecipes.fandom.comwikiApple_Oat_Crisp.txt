Ingredients : 
 3 small  apples , cored and sliced 
 ½ cup  applesauce 
 ½ tsp  lemon rind 
 1½ tsp  lemon juice 
 1 tsp  sugar 
 ¼ tsp  cinnamon 
 Topping : 
 1 oz  quick oats 
 2 tbsp  whole wheat flour 
 2 tbsp  brown sugar 
 ¼ tsp  cinnamon 
 1 tbsp + 1 tsp diet  margarine 
 Directions : 
 Mix the base mix in an 8" baking dish. 
 Combine the topping mix and crumble over the  apples . 
 Bake at 350 °F for 35 – 40 minutes. 
 Nutritional information : 
 35 calories 
 Exchanges: ½ bread | ½ fat | 1 fruit,
