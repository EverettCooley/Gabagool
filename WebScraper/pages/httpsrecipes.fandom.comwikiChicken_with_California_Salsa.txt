 Description : 
 Grilled  Chicken  with a tangy  avocado  based salsa, yum yum!!
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  California avocado , chopped into small pieces 
 1 ripe  tomato , finely chopped 
 ¼ Cup  cilantro , very finely chopped 
 ¼ Cup  pineapple , finely chopped 
 1 ½ Tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 ½ Tbsp  lime juice 
 4  Chicken  breasts 
 Directions : 
 Combine first six ingredients together
 Grill  Chicken  breasts to desired doneness
 Evenly distribute  avocado  mixture on top of  Chicken  breasts.
