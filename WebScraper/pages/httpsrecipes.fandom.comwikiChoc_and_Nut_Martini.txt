 Ingredients : 
 2 shots Stolichnaya Red  vodka 
 1 shot  Frangelico   hazelnut liqueur 
 1 shot De Kuyper  crème de cacao  (white or brown) 
 ¼ shot chilled  water 
 garnish: Wipe rim with  orange  and dust with  cocoa powder 
 Glass: Martini 
 Directions : 
 Shake all ingredients with  ice  and fine strain into a chilled glass (already garnished as above).
