 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup sliced  green onions 
 ½ cup diagonally sliced  celery 
 2 cups diced roast  pork 
 2 tablespoons  peanut oil  or  vegetable oil 
 3 cups cold cooked  rice 
 1 cup sliced  water chestnuts 
 ⅛ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1  egg , slightly beaten 
 3 tablespoons  soy sauce 
 Directions : 
 Cook  onions ,  celery  and  pork  in  oil  until vegetables are tender crisp. Do not overcook. 
 Add  rice  and  water chestnuts . 
 Combine  pepper ,  egg  and soy sauce. 
 Stir into  rice  mixture. Cook, stirring, 2 to 3 minutes or until heated.
