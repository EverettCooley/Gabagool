 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pack saltfish ( salted cod ), preferably without bones 
 pepper 
 a little  Sugar 
 1  hot pepper 
 lots of  lime juice 
 vegetable oil 
 Onion 
 tomato 
 cucumber 
 1 hardboiled  egg 
 chive 
 sweet pepper 
 1 leaf chadon bene, cut up finely ( optional ) 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Boil  salt  fish in  water  to extract some  salt , then drain and clean, if necessary. Cut up very small 
 While fish is boiling, cut finely the  onions ,  tomatoes ,  cucumber ,  chive ,  sweet pepper  and  hot pepper , chadon bene and crush hard boiled  egg  with a fork 
 peel and remove seeds from  cucumber  and cut up finely 
 Add vegetables to  salt  fish and season with  pepper , lots of  lime juice  and  oil ( it takes several tablesps. of  oil  ), and a little  Sugar .
