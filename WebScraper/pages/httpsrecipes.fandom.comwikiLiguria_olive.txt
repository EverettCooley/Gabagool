      Liguria olives     
 About Liguria olives : 
 The  liguria olive  (or Liguriano) is an elliptical-shaped, small blackish-brown olive that is native to Italy. It is brine-cured and provides a somewhat salty flavor with a nutty almond aftertaste. It is a good complement to add as a food topping or a relish.
