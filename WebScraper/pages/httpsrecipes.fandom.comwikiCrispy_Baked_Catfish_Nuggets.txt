Crispy Baked Catfish
 
 Description : 
 A  catfish  recipe. Serves 4 and makes about ⅔ cup (6 servings) of homemade tartar sauce
 
 Ingredients : 
 vegetable oil or  cooking spray 
 ¼ cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 ½ cup plain, dry Panko  breadcrumbs 
 ¼ teaspoon  garlic powder 
 dash  white pepper 
 ⅓ cup fresh  lemon juice 
 2 tablespoons  olive oil 
 4 farm-raised  catfish  fillets 
 1 teaspoon Chef Q For Hire 
 Homemade tartar sauce : 
 1 cup light  mayonnaise 
 1 teaspoon  Dijon-style mustard 
 1 tablespoon finely chopped  parsley 
 1 tablespoon chopped, drained  sweet pickle   or  chopped, drained  capers  (optional) 
 1 tablespoon fresh  lemon juice 
 salt  and  pepper , to taste 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 400°F degrees. 
 Coat baking sheet with vegetable oil or  cooking spray  several times to coat. 
 In a shallow dish, combine  cheese , bread crumbs,  garlic powder , Half Chef Q Season All and  white pepper . 
 In another dish, whisk together  lemon juice  and  olive oil . 
 Roll nuggets in beaten egg whites. Then, roll in  cheese  mixture. 
 Dust with  paprika  and spray lightly with  cooking spray . 
 Place  catfish  on prepared baking sheet. 
 Bake for 10–12 minutes. 
 Reduce the heat to 350°F, and bake for an additional 8–10 minutes, until crust is golden and fish flakes easily. 
 Serve with  homemade tartar sauce . 
 Homemade tartar sauce : 
 Combine and blend well. 
 Serve with crispy baked  catfish  nuggets.
