Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source:  Baking with Mixes 
 Makes 3½ dozen. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 roll refrigerated sugar cookie dough 
 ½ cup chopped  pecans 
 ¾ teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Slice cookie dough in ¼ inch slices. 
 Combine pecans and cinnamon; dip one side of cookies in pecan mixture. 
 Place nut side up on ungreased cookie sheets. 
 Bake in 375°F oven for 8 to 10 minutes or till cookies are lightly browned.
