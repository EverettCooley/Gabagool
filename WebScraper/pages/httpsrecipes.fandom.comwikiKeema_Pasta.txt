 Description : 
 Serves: 	4 
 Prep Time: 	ABOUT 30MINS 
 Occasion: 	Any 
 Effort: 	Easy 
 Ingredients : 
 1KG KEEMA 
 HANDFUL OF  Peas 
 500G  Pasta 
 2 SMALL  tomatoes 
 2  onions 
 2 GREEN CHILLIES 
 1 TABLESPN  oil 
 1 TSP ZEERA POWDER 
 1 TSP  chilli powder 
 1/2 TSP HALDI 
 1 TSP CORRIANDER POWDER 
 1 TSP  garam masala  POWDER 
 1 clove  garlic  CRUSHED 
 BUNCH OF FRESH CORRIANDER LEAVES 
 Directions : 
 PUT IN  oil , FRY GARLI AND  onions ,THEN ADD ONE  chilli  FOR 2-3 MINS 
 THEN ADD KEEMA COOK FOR 3 MINS THEN ADD ZEERA,  garam masala  AND CORRIANDER POWDER AND COOK FOR 15 MINS 
 ADD HALDI OTHER GREEN  chilli ,  chilli powder .COOK FOR FURTHER 5MIN 
 THEN ADD TOMS AND STIR UNTIL TOMS MELT.ADD  Peas 
 SEPARATELY BOIL  Pasta  WHILE KEEMA IS ON SLOW POER TO COOK.ADD  water  TO KEEMA IF NECCESSARY 
 DRAIN  Pasta  ADD TO KEEMA, STIR FOR 5 MINS THEN ADD FRESH CORRIANDER AND SERVE.
