Makes 6-8 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound lean ground  Beef 
 1 cup cooked  rice 
 1/2 cup finely crushed gingersnaps 
 1 teaspoon  garlic salt 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1  egg , slightly beaten 
 2 tablespoons soy sauce 
 Glaze 
 1 teaspoon  ground ginger 
 2 teaspoons dry  mustard 
 2 tablespoons  brown sugar 
 2 tablespoons  lemon juice 
 1 cup catsup 
 Directions : 
 Combine  Beef ,  rice , gingersnaps,  salt ,  pepper ,  egg  and soy sauce. Mix well. #Form into bite-size balls. 
 Brush with glaze and bake at 450 degrees for 10 minutes. 
 Brush with remaining glaze. Keep warm. 
 Glaze 
 
 Blend  ginger ,  mustard ,  Sugar ,  lemon juice  and catsup.
