Serve this recipe with  Cilantro Garlic Pita Crisps . This recipe is from Bon Appetit.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons vegetable oil 
 3 cups chopped sweet onions (such as Vidalia or Maui; about 2 medium) 
 1 1/2 teaspoons garam masala 
 1 8-ounce container crème fraîche or sour cream (1 cup) 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Heat oil in heavy large skillet over medium heat. Add onions and sauté until slightly softened, about 5 minutes. Reduce heat to medium-low and cook until onions are deep brown and begin to crisp slightly, stirring often, about 40 minutes. Add garam masala; stir 1 minute. Transfer to small bowl and cool completely. Mix in crème fraîche. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours.
 DO AHEAD: Can be made 2 days ahead. Keep refrigerated.
 Source: Bon Appetit
