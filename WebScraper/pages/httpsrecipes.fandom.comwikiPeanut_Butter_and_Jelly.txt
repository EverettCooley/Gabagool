       Peanut Butter and Jelly     
 Peanut Butter and Jelly (or PBJ) is a very popular type of lunch
 
 Ingredients : 
 Peanut Butter 
 Jelly 
 2 slices of  bread 
 Directions : 
 First, lay out the slices of bread. 
 Spread peanut butter on one side of one of the slices. 
 Do the same for the jelly on the other slice of bread. 
 Place one of the slices on top of the other slice so that the peanut butter and jelly are touching. 
 Cut in half and enjoy.
