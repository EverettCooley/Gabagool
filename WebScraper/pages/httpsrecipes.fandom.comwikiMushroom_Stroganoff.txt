Ingredients : 
 150 g  mushrooms  (cut in strips) 
 1  onion  (medium, preferably brown) 
 1  garlic  clove (or 2 small ones) 
 10 ml  oil  or sautéeing substitute 
 1 or 2 tbsp low-fat  sour cream  or substitute 
 5 to 10 ml  balsamic vinegar 
 Directions : 
 Sauté chopped  onion  and  garlic  over moderate heat till soft. 
 Add  mushrooms . 
 When soft (usually 2 minutes), add seasoning, low-fat  sour cream  or substitute and  vinegar . 
 Stir gently. 
 When heated serve on  rice  or noodles or baked  potatoes . 
 Tip: when sautéeing  mushrooms  use little sautéeing agent and cook lightly. Never overcook  mushrooms !!!
