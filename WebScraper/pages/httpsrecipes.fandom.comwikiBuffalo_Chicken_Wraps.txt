Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 2/11/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp  hot pepper sauce 
 3 tbsp  white vinegar , divided 
 ½ tbsp  cayenne pepper 
 2 tbsp extra virgin  olive oil 
 1 lb  chicken  tenders 
 2 tbsp  mayonnaise 
 2 tbsp plain  yogurt 
 pepper  to taste 
 ½ cup crumbled  blue cheese 
 4 x 8"  tortilla  shells 
 1 cup shredded  lettuce 
 1 cup sliced  celery 
 1 large  tomato , diced 
 Directions : 
 Whisk  hot pepper sauce , 2 tbsp  vinegar  and  cayenne pepper  in a bowl. 
 Heat  oil  in large skillet over medium-high heat. 
 Add  chicken  tenders, cook until cooked through and no longer pink in the middle, 3-4 minutes per side. 
 Add to the bowl with the hot sauce; toss to coat well. 
 Whisk  mayonnaise ,  yogurt ,  pepper  and the remaining 1 tbsp  vinegar  in a small bowl. 
 Stir in  blue cheese . 
 To assemble wraps: lay a tortilla on a plate. 
 Spread with 1 tbsp  blue cheese  sauce and top with ½ of the  chicken ,  lettuce ,  celery  and  tomato . 
 Drizzle with some of the hot sauce remaining in the bowl and roll into a wrap sandwich. 
 Repeat with the remaining tortillas.
