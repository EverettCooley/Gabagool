Description : 
 Contributed by Jenn B aka Mom2Sam and Tiny at  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large baking  potato , peeled, cubed 
 2 cups fat-free  half-and-half 
 1 cup  corn  kernels 
 ½ small  red bell pepper , finely chopped 
 2 tsp minced  jalapeño , seeds and veins removed 
 ½ cup  water 
 1½ tsp ground  cumin 
 ½ tsp  chili powder 
 salt  and freshly ground  black pepper , to taste 
 Tabasco , to taste 
 1 cup fresh  cilantro , chopped 
 Directions : 
 In a medium saucepan, combine all ingredients except  cilantro . 
 Simmer, covered, until  potatoes  are done, 15 to 20 minutes. 
 Add  cilantro . 
 Serve. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per 1 cup serving: 
 
 195 Calories | 8g Protein | 38g Carbohydrates | 1g Fat (0g Saturated) | 20mg Cholesterol | 4g Fiber | 242mg Sodium
