Ingredients : 
 10 cups  rolled oats 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 ¼ cup  honey 
 1 x 10-oz jar ( apricot  or any flavor) jam 
 Directions : 
 Mix together thoroughly in a large bowl, using your hands. 
 Spread this mixture out on a large cookie sheet. 
 Bake at 350°F for about a half hour until it's starting to brown, taking it out every five minutes or so to stir it around.
