Description : 
 Fondant
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 glasses very finely ground  blanched almonds   or  ½ finely ground + ½ more coarsely ground 
 1½ glasses  icing sugar 
 enough  orange blossom water  to make a stiffly ground mix 
 cloves 
 Directions : 
 Form into small  pear  or other fruit shapes and stick a clove on top of each to make stems. 
 Place in small paper cases and serve on a pretty cake plate.
