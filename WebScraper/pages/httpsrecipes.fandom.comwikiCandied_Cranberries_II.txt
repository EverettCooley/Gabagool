Ingredients : 
 2 1/2 cup  sugar 
 1 1/2 cups  water 
 4 cups  cranberries 
 Sugar 
 Directions : 
 In pan, mix the 2 1/2 cups sugar and the water. Bring to boil. Stir occasionally. 
 Put cranberries in 10 x 6Â inch baking pan. Pour boiling syrup over cranberries. Bake in 300 oven uncovered for 40 minutes. Stir occasionally. 
 Cover loosely and store overnight. 
 The next day, drain off syrup into a medium pan. Bring syrup to boil. Boil 2 minutes. 
 Pour syrup over cranberries and cool without stirring. Repeat twice. 
 Transfer berries to baking sheet. Cover loosely. Let stand for 3 days to erg. Turn several times for even drying. 
 Roll dried cranberries in sugar and store in tightly covered container. Use berries as a garnish for roast poultry. Makes 3 1/2 cups.
