 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 1/2 cups  low-fat milk 
 2 cups cooked  rice 
 1/2 cup  Sugar 
 1/4 cup  semisweet chocolate  chips 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 Directions : 
 Combine  milk ,  rice ,  Sugar  and  chocolate chips  in 2- to 3-quart saucepan. 
 Cook over medium heat, stirring often, until pudding is thick and creamy, stirring often. 
 Remove from heat; stir in  vanilla . Spoon into serving dishes. 
 Let stand 10 minutes. Serve warm or cold.
