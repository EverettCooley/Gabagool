Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes For Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Source:  1,001 Delicious Recipes for People with Diabetes 
 Yield: 6 servings and about ¼ cup of dressing 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ large  jícama , peeled (about 12oz) 
 1½ medium  zucchini , sliced 
 1 small  orange , peeled, cut into segments 
 2 – 3 thin slices  red onion 
 Cilantro Lime Dressing  (recipe to follow) 
 salt  and  pepper , to taste 
 lettuce  leaves 
 Cilantro Lime Dressing : 
 2 tbsp  lime juice 
 1 tbsp  orange juice 
 1 – 2 tbsp  olive , or  vegetable oil 
 2 tbsp finely chopped  cilantro 
 2 tsp  sugar  or  Splenda  sweetener 
 Directions : 
 Cut jícama into sticks about 1½ x ½ inches. 
 Combine jícama, zucchini, orange, and onion in bowl. 
 Pour  Cilantro Lime Dressing  over and toss; season to taste with salt and pepper. 
 Cilantro Lime Dressing : 
 Mix all ingredients; refrigerate until serving time. 
 Mix again before using. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving (⅙ of recipe):
 
 77 Calories | 2.4g Fat | 0mg Cholesterol | 1mg Sodium | 1.3g Protein | 13.5g Carbs (less if using Splenda in dressing) 
 Diabetic exchanges: 1 vegetable | ½ fruit | ½ fat
