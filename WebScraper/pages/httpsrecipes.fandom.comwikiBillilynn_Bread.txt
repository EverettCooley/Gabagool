 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 4-6 c.  flour 
 2 c. goat  milk 
 3 tbsp. real  butter 
 1 tsp.  seasalt 
 2 tsp.  honey 
 1 pkg.  yeast 
 Directions : 
 Warm  milk  and  butter  in saucepan. Add  salt  and  Honey , stir to mix. Add mixture to  flour , stir, then knead; add  yeast  according to  yeast  directions. After kneading, cover and let dough rise for 1 hour. Knead to satiny consistency, divide in half, roll with rolling pin to a flat square. Fold square in half, pinch together sides, put seam side on base of table, one half of dough in baking pan, let rise, bake at 350 to 400 degrees for 35â€“45 minutes. Serve with  butter .
