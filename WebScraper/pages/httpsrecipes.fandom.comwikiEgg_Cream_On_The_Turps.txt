Description : 
 An alcoholic egg cream, (or chocolate collins), prepared in an unorthodox fashion for such a beverage. Tanqueray and San Pellegrino may also be replaced by 6 ounces Guinness® extra stout, making the drink a "Motor City Black Cow", so named because it is the alcoholic version of a Detroit Egg Cream.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 ounces Pat O'Brian's  Sweet and Sour Mix ® 
 3 ounces  Tanqueray ® 
 3 ounces Vermeer Dutch Chocolate Cream Liqueur® 
 3 ounces  San Pellegrino ® 
 3 ounces Ovaltine Malt® 
 Directions : 
 Pre–chill a 16 ounce collins glass. 
 Fill shaker with ice. 
 Combine all ingredients and shake well. 
 Strain and serve.
