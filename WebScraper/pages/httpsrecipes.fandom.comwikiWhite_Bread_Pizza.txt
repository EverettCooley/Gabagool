 Prep Time: 10 minutes 
 Cook time: 10 minutes 
 Serves: 2 - 4 per person 
 
 Ingredients : 
 sliced white  bread 
 whole tinned  tomatoes 
 cheese 
 desired topping (eg. sliced  mushroom ,  ham ,  pineapple , etc.) 
 parsley 
 Directions : 
 Open the whole tinned tomatoes and pour the juice into a deep container. Chop the tomatoes and add to the juice 
 Grate the Cheese 
 Prepare your chosen topping (ie. chop the ham, slice the mushrooms, etc.) 
 Grill one side of the sliced white bread and turn over and slightly cook the other side 
 Place a few spoons of the tomato on the bread and spread out evenly 
 Top with cheese and your chosen toppings and place back under the grill 
 Grill until the cheese has melted and serve with a sprig of parsley
