Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 oz  cream cheese , softened 
 1 small bottle  Spanish olives , chopped 
 1 small  onion , chopped fine 
 Directions : 
 Mix these with enough mayonnaise to make a mixture that is easy to spread. 
 This is better if made a day before you make sandwiches.
