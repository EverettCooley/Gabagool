Description : 
 A simple Gujarati dish made of rice flour.
 
 Serves 2 - 3 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup (115 g) 3¾ oz  rice flour 
 1½ cups (375 ml) 12 oz  water 
 ½ tsp  cumin  (jeera) seeds 
 ¼ tsp  red chile powder 
 ¼ tsp  asafoetida  (hing) 
 ½ tsp  green chiles , chopped 
 salt  to taste 
 ¼ tsp  baking soda 
 2 tbsp (30 ml) 1 oz  vegetable oil 
 1 tbsp (4 g)  cilantro  (aara dhaniya), chopped 
 Directions : 
 Bring water to a boil. 
 Add cumin seeds, red chile powder, asafoetida, green chiles, and salt. 
 Mix well. 
 Add the rice flour and baking soda, stir vigorously with a wooden spoon to avoid lumps. 
 Cover and simmer for 10 - 12 minutes. 
 Add the oil and cilantro. 
 Mix well. 
 Serve hot with  ghee , crushed  pappadum ,  pickle  and  yoghurt .
