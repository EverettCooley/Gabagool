Description : 
 This recipe is very flexible. It is different every time I make it. Please feel free to add any additional veggies or spices.
 
 Makes about 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 large  onions , chopped 
 2 large  carrots , cut in bite size pieces 
 4  garlic  cloves, chopped 
 spices to your taste ( chili powder ,  cayenne pepper ,  black pepper ,  hot pepper sauce , or anything else you would like to add) 
 1 can  tomato paste 
 3 large cans  tomato sauce 
 1 cup pre-cooked  brown rice 
 1 cup kidney or  pinto beans 
 Directions : 
 Boil  onions ,  carrots , and  garlic  in Â½ cup of  water  until tender. 
 Add spices,  tomato  sauce, and  tomato  paste, and simmer on medium-low heat for 15 minutes or so. 
 Add  rice  and beans. 
 Simmer for an additional 15 minutes.
