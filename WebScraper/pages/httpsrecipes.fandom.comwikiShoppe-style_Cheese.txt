Ingredients : 
 500 g  brined sheep's cheese 
 40 g  butter 
 1 – 2  tomatoes 
 1 – 2  peppers 
 black pepper 
 paprika 
 5  eggs 
 Directions : 
 Cut the  cheese  into five equal slices and place into butter-lined (earthenware) bowls. 
 Top with  tomato  slices,  pepper  rings and so me  butter , bake in a hot oven some 5-6 minutes. 
 Break an  egg  a top of each bowl and add the remaining  butter  with  pepper  and  paprika . 
 Bake until a crust is formed. 
 Serve hot, garnished with slices of  tomato ,  parsley  and a  chilli , as desired.
