 Ingredients 
 6  chicken  breasts 
 2 cups  ketchup 
 1 cup  Coca-Cola 
 1 large  onion , chopped 
 Directions 
 Place salted chicken in pan, skin side up. 
 Mix ketchup and Coke. Pour over meat. 
 Top with onion. Add black pepper to taste. 
 Bake uncovered at 375 degrees for 1 hour. 
 Serve over  rice .
