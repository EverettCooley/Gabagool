Info 
 Cook Time : About an hour and 15 minutes total 
 Serves : 24
 
 Ingredients 
 1  egg white 
 1/2 teaspoon  vanilla flavoring 
 3/4 cup light  brown sugar 
 2 cups chopped  pecans 
 Directions 
 1.Beat egg white and flavoring together. Add sugar, beat until stiff. 
 
2.Add nuts, stirring only until well-coated. 
 
3.Drop from teaspoon onto baking sheet. Bake at 250° for 30 minutes. 
 
4.Turn off oven and do not remove pecan kisses for an additional 30 minutes.
 
 Tips 
 These can be placed in pretty holiday tins for gift-giving. 
 Source 
 Peanut & Pecan Recipes  by the South Carolina Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
