Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can cream-style  corn 
 1 cup  cracker  crumbs 
 3  eggs  or ¾ cup Egg Beaters™ 99%  egg substitute 
 2 tbsp  margarine  — optional 
 ½ cup skimmed  evaporated milk 
 pepper  — to taste 
 Directions : 
 With electric mixer, beat together  corn , crumbs,  eggs ,  milk  and  pepper . 
 Beat very well, and it will be messy. 
 The secret to this recipe is in the beating! pour into a lightly greased casserole. 
 Dot with  margarine , if desired. 
 Bake uncovered at 375°F for 30 minutes or until center is firm. 
 Enjoy.
