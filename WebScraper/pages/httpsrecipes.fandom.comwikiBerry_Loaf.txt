Ingredients : 
 2 	cups 	 all-purpose flour 
 1 	cup 	 Sugar 
 1 1/2 	tsp 	 baking powder 
 1 	tsp 	 salt 
 1/2 	tsp 	 baking soda 
 3/4 	cup 	 orange juice 
 1 	tsp 	Freshly grated  orange peel 
 2 	tbsp 	 shortening 
 1 		 egg , slightly beaten 
 1 	cup 	Chopped fresh  cranberries 
 1 	cup 	Hershey's Mini Chips  semi-sweet chocolate 
 3/4 	cup 	Chopped  nuts 
 powdered sugar Glaze : 
 Beat 1 cup  powdered sugar 
 1 tablespoon  milk 
 1 teaspoon softened  butter 
 1/2 teaspoon  vanilla extract  in small bowl until smooth. 
 (If thinner glaze is desired; add additional  milk , 1 teaspoon at a time.) 
 About 1/2 cup glaze. 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 350 °F. Grease 9x5x3-inch loaf pan. 
 Stir together  flour ,  Sugar ,  baking powder ,  salt  and  baking soda  in large bowl. 
 Add  orange juice ,  orange peel ,  shortening  and  egg ; stir with spoon until well blended. 
 Stir in  cranberries , small  chocolate chips  and  nuts . Pour batter into prepared pan. 
 Bake 1 hour and 5 to 10 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean. 
 Cool 10 minutes; remove from pan to wire rack. 
 Prepare  powdered sugar  Glaze, if desired; spread over top of loaf. 
 Cool completely. 1 loaf (14 servings).
