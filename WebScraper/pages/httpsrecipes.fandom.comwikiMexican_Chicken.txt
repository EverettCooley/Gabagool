 Ingredients : 
 1½ lbs bones skinless  chicken breast  cut into bite size chunks 
 1 can  chicken broth 
 1 can (8 oz)  tomato sauce 
 1 pkg.  taco seasoning  mix 
 1 cup frozen  corn 
 1½ cups  instant rice 
 tortilla chips 
 shredded  cheddar  /  jack cheeses 
 Optionals : 
 diced  tomatoes 
 diced  hot peppers 
 sour cream 
 Directions : 
 In a large frying pan, cook  chicken  until no long pink. 
 Mix together  chicken broth ,  tomato  sauce and  taco seasoning  and pour over  chicken . 
 Bring to boil. 
 Add  corn . 
 Return to boil. 
 Stir in  rice , remove from heat, cover and let rest 10 minutes. 
 Serve over/with tortilla chips,  cheese ,  tomatoes .
