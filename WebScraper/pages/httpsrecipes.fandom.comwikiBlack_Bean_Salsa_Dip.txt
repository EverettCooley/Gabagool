black bean  Salsa Dip
 
 1 Can  black beans , Rinsed And Drained (15 Oz) 
 1/3 Cup Medium or Hot Salsa 
 1 1/2 T. fresh  lime juice 
 1 clove  garlic , Minced 
 Chopped  tomato ,  cilantro , Red  bell pepper 
 Tortilla Chips
 
Process All Ingredients, Except Garnishes And Tortilla Chips in Food Processor or Blender Until Smooth. Spoon Into Bowl And Sprinkle With Garnishes. Serve With Tortilla Chips. Serves 8Source: Unknown
