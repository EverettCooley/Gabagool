Description : 
 This recipe was taken from Cathy's Menu and recipes for the week of 2/11/07
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can creamed  corn 
 2  eggs , beaten 
 2 tsp  sugar 
 2 tsp  flour 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 cup shredded  Cheddar  cheese 
 1 package (10 Oz) frozen chopped  broccoli 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F 
 Mix together  corn ,  eggs ,  sugar ,  flour  and  salt . 
 Add the  cheese  and  broccoli 
 Mix well and pour into a 2 quart casserole dish. 
 Bake for 1 hour.
