Description : 
 Serves four people and takes 25 minutes to prepare.
 
 Ingredients : 
 400 g boneless  chicken 
 100 g  yellow peppers 
 100 g  red peppers 
 2  oranges 
 2 sweet  limes 
 Â¼  pineapple 
 100 ml  mayonnaise 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 200 g  iceberg lettuce 
 50 g  green grapes , halved 
 50 g  black grapes , halved 
 Directions : 
 BOIL  Chicken  in very little  water  and  salt . 
 When cool, cut into cubes. 
 Deseed  yellow  and  red peppers  and cut into cubes. 
 Clean and cut the  oranges , sweet  limes  and  pineapple  into small pieces.
