Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup crushed  cornflakes 
 ¾ cup  Parmesan cheese 
 1 envelope ranch dressing mix 
 8 boneless  chicken breasts 
 ½ cup  butter 
 Directions : 
 In a shallow bowl, combine the cornflakes, Parmesan cheese and salad dressing. 
 Dip chicken in butter, then roll in cornflakes mixture. 
 Place in a 9x13 baking dish. 
 Bake uncovered at 350°F for 45 minutes or until juices run clear.
