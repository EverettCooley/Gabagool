Serves 6.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can  evaporated milk , chilled 
 2  egg  whites, beaten 
 1 (4 ounce) jar  maraschino cherry 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Blend  milk  and  cherries  and their liquid in a blender, only for a moment or two;  cherries  should not be too well blended. 
 Thoroughly whip  egg  whites. Fold in  cherry  mixture. 
 Freeze to desired consistency. 
 If you like a stronger flavor, add from 1/2 to 1 teaspoon  almond extract .
