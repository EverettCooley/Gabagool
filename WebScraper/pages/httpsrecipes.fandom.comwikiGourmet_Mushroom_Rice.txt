Description : 
 Makes 6-8 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound fresh  mushrooms , sliced in half 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 ¼ cup diced fully cooked  ham 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 ¼ cup dry  sherry 
 ¼ cup chopped fresh  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Cook  mushrooms ,  onion  and  ham  in  butter  in large skillet over medium heat until  onion  is golden. 
 Stir in  salt ,  pepper  and  sherry ; heat. 
 Pour over  rice  and toss lightly. 
 Garnish with  parsley .
