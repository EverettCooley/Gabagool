Description : 
 This bold recipe is made with only three ingredients - pineapple juice, orange marmalade, and steak sauce - and is great for marinating and dipping, as it delivers a natural aroma that it relishes and enhances with fruit juice.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ of a 6-oz. can frozen  pineapple juice  concentrate 
 ¼ cup  orange marmalade 
 2 tbsp. bottled  steak sauce 
 Directions : 
 In a pan, combine the concentrate, marmalade, and steak sauce. 
 Cook and stir until heated and blended. 
 Use to baste poultry and pork during the last 10-15 minutes of grilling time. Pour, funnel, or spoon contents into the fresh container. Reserve the fruit-based sauce in the refrigerator.
