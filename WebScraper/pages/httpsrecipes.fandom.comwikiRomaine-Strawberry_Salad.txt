Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 10 oz pkg. chopped  romaine lettuce 
 2 cups  broccoli  slaw 
 1½ cups fresh  strawberries , quartered 
 4 fresh  green onions , sliced diagonally 
 ½ cup  Raspberry Vinaigrette  salad dressing 
 Directions : 
 In small salad bowl, toss ingredients. 
 When ready to serve, use about ½ cup  Raspberry Vinaigrette  salad dressing and toss.
