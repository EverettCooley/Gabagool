Ingredients : 
 ½ lb  black-eyed peas  soaked 
 4  garlic  cloves crushed 
 2 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  black pepper 
 4 tbsp  water 
 oil  for frying 
 lime juice  to taste 
 Directions : 
 When  peas  have softened, rub off skins and soak an additional 30 minutes. 
 Drain and rinse. 
 In a food processor, process  peas ,  garlic ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Add  water  while continuing to process. 
 Add enough  water  to get a smooth, thick puree. 
 Preheat oven to 250°F. 
 In a large skillet, heat 2 to 3 inches of  oil  and fry 1 tbsp of the batter till golden brown. 
 Repeat until all of the batter has been fried. 
 Keep in oven to keep hot. 
 Serve piping hot, sprinkled with  salt  and  lime juice .
