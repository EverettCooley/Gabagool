Ingredients : 
 3 or 4  plantains 
 2 cups of  oil 
 Directions : 
 Peel very green  plantains  and slice very thin. 
 Fry in deep  oil  until golden brown and crisp then drain on paper towel. 
 Sprinkle with  salt . 
 Keep in sealed containers.
