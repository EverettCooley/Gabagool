Ingredients : 
 2  California avocados , seeded and peeled 
 8 slices each  prosciutto  and  smoked salmon 
 Fresh  lemon  or  lime 
 Directions : 
 Cut each  avocado  into 8 slices. 
 Diagonally wrap each with a prosciutto or salmon slice. 
 Arrange wraps on serving platter, garnish with  lemon  or  lime  and serve.
