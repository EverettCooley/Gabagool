      Custard     
 Description : 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 large  eggs 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 2½ cups  milk 
 ½ cup  heavy whipping cream 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 2 teaspoons  vanilla extract 
 1 teaspoon  unsalted butter 
 apples 
 Directions : 
 Break  eggs  into large bowl. 
 Add  sugar ,  milk ,  cream ,  salt  and  vanilla . 
 Beat lightly with whisk to mix well. 
 Gently place  apples  in a buttered casserole and pour custard around them. 
 Place casserole in a pan surrounded with hot  water  half way up sides. 
 Bake in 300°F oven for 50 minutes.
