 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  flour 
 1 1/4 cups  Sugar 
 2 teaspoons  baking soda 
 2 teaspoons  cinnamon 
 3/4 teaspoon  salt 
 3  eggs , beaten 
 1 cup  vegetable oil 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 3 cups shredded  carrots 
 1 1/3 cups  coconut  (3 ounce can) 
 1/2 cup  Raisins 
 Directions : 
 Mix  flour  with  Sugar ,  baking soda ,  cinnamon , and  salt . 
 Add  eggs ,  oil , and  vanilla ; blend well. Stir in the  carrots  and  Raisins .
 Pour batter into a greased and floured 9x5x3-inch loaf pan. 
 Bake at 350Â° for 1 hour and 15 minutes, or until a wooden pick or cake tester inserted in center comes out clean. 
 Cool thoroughly on rack before slicing.
