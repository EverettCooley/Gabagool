Blue Ribbon  Beef  Nachos
 
 1 Lb. Ground  Beef 
 1 Small  Onion , Chopped 
 16 Oz. Can  refried beans 
 16 Oz. Jar Salsa 
 6 Oz. Can Pitted  ripe olives , Chopped 
 1/2 C. Shredded  Cheddar   Cheese 
 2 Tbsp.  chili powder 
 1  green onion , Chopped 
 1 T.  salt 
 Tortilla Chips
 Sliced  ripe olives  And Chopped  green onions  And  tomatoes , Optional
 
 in a Skillet, Cook  Beef  And  Onion  Over Medium Heat Until Meat is no Longer Pink; Drain. Stir in beans, Salsa, The Can of Chopped,  Cheese ,  chili powder , The 1  green onion  And  salt ; Heat Through.
 
Serve Over Tortilla Chips And Top With The Optional,  green onions  And  tomatoes .
 
 6 Servings
