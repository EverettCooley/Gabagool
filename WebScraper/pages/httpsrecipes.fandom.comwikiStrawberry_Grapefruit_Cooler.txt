 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 ounces frozen sliced California  strawberries  in syrup, partially thawed 
 4 ounces pink  grapefruit juice 
 4  Sugar  cubes 
 1 scoop  ice 
 Splash of lemon- lime  soda 
 Whole fresh California  strawberry 
 Directions : 
 In a blender combine all ingredients except whole berry and lemon- lime  soda. 
 Garnish with whole berry.
