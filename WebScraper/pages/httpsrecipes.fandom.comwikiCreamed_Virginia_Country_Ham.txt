Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Mclean Estate in Irving, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 3 tablespoons  butter 
 3 tablespoons minced  onion 
 3 tablespoons  green pepper 
 3 tablespoons  flour 
 2 cups  milk 
 1 cup chopped  Virginia country ham 
 1 tablespoon chopped  parsley  or  chives 
 ⅛ teaspoon  paprika 
 2 tablespoons dry  sherry 
 Directions : 
 In a skillet melt  butter  over medium heat and sauté the  onion  and green  pepper  until light brown then sprinkle in the  flour  and stir a few minutes. 
 Add  milk  slowly stirring constantly then add the  ham  and simmer stirring until thickened. 
 Remove the pan from the heat and season with the remaining ingredients. 
 Serve over hot buttered toast or  corn bread .
