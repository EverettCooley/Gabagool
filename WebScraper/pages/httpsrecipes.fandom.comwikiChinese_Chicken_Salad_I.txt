 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice , cooled 
 1 1/2 cups cooked  Chicken  breast cubes (about 1 whole breast) 
 1 cup sliced  celery 
 1 8-ounce can sliced  water   chestnuts , drained 
 1/2 cup sliced fresh  Mushrooms 
 1/4 cup sliced  green onions 
 1/4 cup diced red  bell pepper 
 1/4 cup sliced  black olives 
 2 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 2 tablespoons  lemon juice 
 1 tablespoon soy sauce 
 1/2 teaspoon  ground ginger 
 1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon ground  white pepper 
 lettuce  leaves 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  Chicken ,  celery ,  water   chestnuts ,  Mushrooms ,  onions , red pepper and in large bowl. 
 
 Place  oil ,  lemon juice , soy sauce,  ginger  and  white pepper  in small jar; shake well. Pour over  rice  mixture. Toss lightly. Serve on  lettuce  leaves.
