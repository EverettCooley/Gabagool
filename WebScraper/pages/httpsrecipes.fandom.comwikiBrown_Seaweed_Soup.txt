 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 5 tbsp soy sauce 
 brown seaweed 
 1 tbsp chopped  garlic 
 7- 8 cups of  water 
 Directions : 
 Bring to a boil. When boiling, reduce heat to low. Cook for 2 hours, partially covered, stirring frequently. Soup is done
