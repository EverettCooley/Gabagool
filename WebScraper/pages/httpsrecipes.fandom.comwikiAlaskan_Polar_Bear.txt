Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  Amaretto   almond liqueur 
 1 oz  Frangelico ®  hazelnut liqueur 
 1 oz  cocoa   cream 
 1 oz  chocolate syrup 
 1 scoop  ice cream 
 1½ oz  whipped cream 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients until of milkshake consistency. 
 Serve in a highball glass and garnish with  whipped cream .
