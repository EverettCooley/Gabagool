 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp mazola  corn oil 
 1  Onion 
 2  garlic  clove 
 1  zucchini 
 1 green  bell pepper 
 1 cup  celery 
 28 oz canned  tomatoes 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  oregano 
 1/2 tsp dried  basil 
 8 oz cooked mueller's  macaroni 
 Directions : 
 In large skillet heat  corn oil  over medium heat. Add  Onion  and  garlic ; saute 2–3 minutes. Add  zucchini ,  pepper  and  celery ; saute 3–4 minutes. Stir in  tomatoes ,  salt ,  oregano  and  basil . Reduce heat to low; simmer 12–15 minutes. Toss with  macaroni .
