Ingredients : 
 1 cup of  palm oil 
 4 – 6  plantains , slightly ripe 
 1 – 2 hot  chile peppers , cleaned and chopped 
 1  onion , chopped 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Heat the  oil  in a large skillet. 
 Peel  plantains . 
 Cut  plantains  into disks of equal thickness. 
 Fry the  plantains  in the hot  oil  for several minutes, until they are golden brown. 
 Remove them from the  oil  and place them on absorbent paper. 
 Heat 2 tsp  oil  in a deep pot. 
 Fry the  peppers  and  onion  over high heat for a few minutes, stirring often. 
 Add the fried  plantains  to the  peppers  and  onion . 
 Add a spoonful of  water , cover and simmer at a low heat for a few minutes. 
 Add  salt  to taste. 
 Serve hot, alone or as a side dish.
