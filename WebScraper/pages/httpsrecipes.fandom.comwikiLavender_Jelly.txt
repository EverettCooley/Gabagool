 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 
 
 1/2 cup fresh  lavender  flowers 
 
 
 2 Tablespoons  white wine vinegar 
 
 
 4 cups  Sugar 
 
 
 1 three-ounce pouch liquid pectin 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Bring two cup  water  to boil- remove from heat and add flowers- 
 steep for twenty minutes and strain. In heavy saucepan, add infused  water ,
 vinegar  and  Sugar , bring to boil, stirring constantly for 10 minutes or until;
 Sugar  is dissolved. Add pectin, return to boil and boil for one minute.
 
 Remove from heat and skim off foam. Pour in hot sterilized jars and seal.
