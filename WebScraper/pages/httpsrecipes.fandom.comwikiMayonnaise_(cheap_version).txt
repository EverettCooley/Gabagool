Description : 
 Although  Mayonnaise  is not a bitch's invention, it has become a staple with bitch chips. Of course the bitches have made a cheaper version of  Mayonnaise , which is easy to make.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ liter  water 
 1½ tbsp  corn flour 
 1  egg yolk 
 1 tbsp  white wine vinegar 
 3 tsp  vegetable oil 
 salt ,  pepper  to taste 
 1 tbsp  mustard 
 Directions : 
 Mix the  egg   yolk  with  salt ,  pepper  and  mustard . 
 Mix in the  corn flour  and as much as the  water  as is necessary to get a smooth paste. 
 Pour this paste into the rest of the  water  that you have brought to the boil and bring back to the boil. 
 Take off the heat and mix in the  vegetable oil . 
 Leave to cool and use as  mayonnaise .
