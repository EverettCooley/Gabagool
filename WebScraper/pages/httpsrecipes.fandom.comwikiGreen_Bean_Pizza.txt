Ingredients : 
 Dough : 
 230 g water  
 1 tsp sugar  
 1 ½ tsp dried instant yeast  
 20 g extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for greasing 
 100 g wholemeal bread flour  
 300 g strong white bread flour, plus extra for flouring 
 1 tsp fine sea salt  
 Topping : 
 1500 g water  
 150 g green beans, cut in pieces (2-3 cm) 
 150 g mozzarella, drained, cut in pieces 
 150 g cream cheese  
 ½ tsp fine sea salt  
 1 pinch ground black pepper  
 50 g basil pesto (optional) 
 20 g extra virgin olive oil (optional)
