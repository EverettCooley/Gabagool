Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2  poblano peppers , roasted 
 2 tablespoons  lime juice 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 1 tablespoon fresh chopped  cilantro 
 1  garlic  clove, crushed 
 Â¼ teaspoon  salt 
 1 medium-size  red pepper , chopped 
 1 x 16-ounce can  black beans , rinsed and drained 
 rice 
 Directions : 
 Combine  poblano peppers ,  lime juice ,  olive oil ,  cilantro ,  garlic  and  salt  in blender. 
 Blend until smooth. 
 Combine  rice ,  black beans  and  red pepper  in large bowl, add poblano pesto; mix well.
