 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs medium size Bajan  sweet potatoes 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 1.4 lbs  onions 
 ½ tsp  white pepper 
 ¾ cup  milk 
 1 tbsp grated  cheddar  cheese 
 2 oz  flour 
 1 oz  butter 
 Directions : 
 Parboil  sweet potatoes , peel and slice thinly. Also slice  onions . 
 Grease a baking dish with  butter . 
 Put the  potatoes  and  onions  in it in alternate layers. 
 Sprinkle between layers with a mixture of  flour ,  salt  and  white pepper . 
 Dot with  butter  and grated  Cheese  and almost cover with  milk . 
 Bake in moderate oven.
