Apricot Mist Punch
 1 6 oz. can frozen pink lemonade
1 cup Milk
1 10 oz. pkg. frozen strawberry
1 pint Vanilla ice cream
Fresh strawberries -- optional
 In a blender, place first four
ingredients in the order given; blend until smooth. Pour into glasses.
Garnish with fresh strawberries if desired. yield: 4-6 servings.
 Enjoy.
