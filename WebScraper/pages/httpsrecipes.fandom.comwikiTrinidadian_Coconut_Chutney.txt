Ingredients : 
 2 cups of fresh  coconut , shredded 
 10  dry red chillies 
 1 sprig  curry leaves 
 a large pinch of  hing  (asoefetida) 
 Â½ tsp  methi  ( fenugreek ) seeds 
 2 tsp udad  dhal 
 2 tsp channa  dhal 
 1 small  tomato 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Heat some  oil  in a pan and add the dhals,  curry leaves ,  hing ,  methi  and red chillies. 
 Fry till done. 
 Add this to the  coconut  and  tomato  and blend into a smooth paste. 
 Add  salt  to taste. 
 Goes well with dosas, idli and plain  rice .
