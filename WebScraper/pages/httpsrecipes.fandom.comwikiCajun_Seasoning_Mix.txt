 Description : 
 Similar to the homemade barbecue rub, this countrified seasoning mix is made and packed with real herbs and herb and spice mixes and is great for meat, poultry, seafood, or sauces.
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 tbsp.  paprika 
 3 tsp.  salt 
 6 tbsp.  garlic powder 
 2 tbsp.  black pepper 
 2 tbsp.  onion powder 
 2 tbsp.  cayenne pepper 
 2 tbsp.  oregano 
 2 tbsp.  thyme 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients thoroughly. 
 Store in an airtight jar. 
 Add  salt  to the herb and spice mixture. This is delicious on meat,  chicken , seafood, in casseroles, or vegetables. 
 Reserve the homemade seasoning mix in the cabinet. 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Servings:  2 teaspoons
