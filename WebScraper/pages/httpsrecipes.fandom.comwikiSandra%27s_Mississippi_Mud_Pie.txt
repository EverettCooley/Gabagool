Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  flour 
 ½ cup chopped  nuts 
 1 large container  Cool Whip 
 2 pkg.  instant chocolate pudding 
 3 cups  milk 
 ½ cup melted  butter 
 1 large pkg.  cream cheese 
 1 cup  powdered sugar 
 Directions : 
 Combine flour, melted butter and nuts. 
 Press into 9 x 13 inch pan. 
 Bake at 350°F for 18 minutes. 
 Beat cream cheese with ½ Cool Whip and 1 cup powdered sugar. 
 Spread over cool crust. 
 Prepare pudding with milk. 
 Pour into crust and chill. 
 Serve with remainder of cool whip on top. 
 Enjoy.
