Description : 
 Makes 4 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 teaspoons  sesame oil 
 1 teaspoon  vegetable oil 
 1 x 16-ounce package frozen  Broccoli 
 red peppers 
 bamboo shoots 
 straw mushrooms 
 2 cups cooked  rice 
 1 tablespoon reduced-sodium  soy sauce 
 Directions : 
 Heat oils in large skillet over medium-high heat. Add vegetables; stir-fry several minutes. Add  rice  and soy sauce; cook and stir until  rice  is heated, gently separating grains. Serve with additional soy sauce, if desired.
