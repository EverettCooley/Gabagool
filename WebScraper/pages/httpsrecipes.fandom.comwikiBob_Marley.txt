Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 ounce  dark rum 
 1 ounce  Tía Maria 
 1 ounce  pineapple juice 
 Directions : 
 Pour  rum  and  Tía Maria  in a cordial glass. 
 Float the pineapple juice on top by holding a teaspoon bottom-side up over the glass and pouring the  pineapple juice  over it slowly.
