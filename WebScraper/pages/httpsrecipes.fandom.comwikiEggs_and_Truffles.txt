 Ingredients : 
 8  eggs 
 4 tablespoons non salted  butter 
 1 cup  milk 
 1Â½ cup dry  white wine 
 salt 
 pepper 
 1  truffle  (white or black) 
 Directions : 
 Beat  eggs  in a large bowl until well mixed. 
 In a frying pan, heat the  butter  over medium heat until the  butter  is brown. 
 Reduce heat and add in the  eggs  and cook, stirring constantly with a spatula. 
 As soon as the  eggs  are ready, pour in the  milk  and mix well. 
 Add in the  salt  and  pepper  to taste. 
 Put the mixture into the dog's bowls and grate the  truffles  over the top of the  eggs .
