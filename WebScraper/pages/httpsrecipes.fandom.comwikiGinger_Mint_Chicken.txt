Description : 
 mint  is seldom used in cooking but more as a medicine for cold. This is one rare recipe.
 
       Ginger Mint Chicken     
 Ingredients : 
 Chicken , cut into small pieces 
 ginger , finely chopped 
 mint , chopped 
 green chillies, chopped 
 fish sauce 
 lime juice 
 Sugar 
 peanuts , ground 
 saseme 
 tapioca flour (or other starch), for thickening 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in pan and fry  ginger  and chilie 
 Add  chicken  and fry for several minutes 
 Add fish sauce and let it sizzles. 
 Add 1/2 some  water , cover and cook till done. 
 Add a pinch of  Sugar ,  lime juice  and  mint  and  peanuts . 
 Thicken with starch, sprinkle  sesame seeds  and serve.
