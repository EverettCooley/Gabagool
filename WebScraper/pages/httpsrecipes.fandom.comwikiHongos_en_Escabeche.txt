 3.1   Marinade 
 
 
 
 
 Description : 
 Pickled Mushrooms
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 kilo of  pine mushrooms 
 1  orange peel 
 1  lemon peel 
 1 head  garlic 
 2 or 3  bay leaves 
 1  onion 
 1 cup  oil 
 1 cup  vinegar 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 2 cups  water 
 Directions : 
 Clean the mushrooms, cut into pieces and boil with water for 10 minutes. 
 Marinade : 
 Put oil in a pan, add orange peel and lemon, after they have been cooked a bit, add the sliced onions and whole garlic cloves, bay leaf, vinegar, water and salt. 
 Let it cook a while. 
 Add the mushrooms that have been boiled previously. 
 And leave until they absorb the flavor of the marinade. The longer they marinade, the better they tastes.
