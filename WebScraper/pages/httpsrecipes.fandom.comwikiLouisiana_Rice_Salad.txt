Description : 
 Makes 4 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups cooked  rice , cooled 
 2 cups  crab  meat 
 1 8-ounce can sliced  water chestnuts , drained 
 1 cup  celery  slices 
 ⅓ cup sliced  green onions 
 ⅓ cup plain low fat  yogurt 
 ⅓ cup  sour cream 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅛ teaspoon  ground red pepper 
 ½ teaspoon  hot pepper sauce 
 lettuce  leaves 
 tomato  wedges for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice , crab meat,  water chestnuts ,  celery  and  green onion  in large mixing bowl. 
 Blend  yogurt ,  sour cream ,  salt , red pepper and  hot pepper sauce . 
 Add to  rice  mixture; toss lightly. 
 Serve on  lettuce  leaves; garnish with  tomatoes .
