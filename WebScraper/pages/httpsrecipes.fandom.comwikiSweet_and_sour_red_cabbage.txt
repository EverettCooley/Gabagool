 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 small head  red cabbage 
 1/2 cup  water 
 2 tsp  cornstarch 
 1/2 cup  cider vinegar 
 3 tbsp  fructose 
 1/4 tsp  salt 
 1/2 tsp  caraway seeds 
 Directions : 
 Shred  cabbage  steam over rapidly boiling  water  10 minutes, or until crisp-tender. Remove from heat thoroughly. Dissolve  cornstarch  in  water ; add remaining ingredients; cook over medium heat until slightly thickened. Put this dish with either in large mixing bowl; add sauce and mix well. Use this dish with either hot or cold meat. Note: this recipe is provided through the courtesy of sweet lite  fructose .
