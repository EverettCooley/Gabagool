 Ingredients
 
 12 ounces iceburg lettuce 
 12 ounces romaine lettuce 
 1/2  red pepper , chopped 
 1/2  green pepper , chopped 
 4 slices  bacon , cooked and crumbled 
 1 cup garlic-flavored  croutons 
 ½ cup  Parmesan cheese , shredded 
 ½ cup  mozzarella  cheese, shredded 
 Dressing
 
 ½ cup  red wine vinegar 
 6 anchovies, finely chopped 
 ½ teaspoon  oregano 
 ¾ teaspoon  pepper 
 ¾ teaspoon  salt 
 1  garlic  clove, minced 
 1 tablespoon  sugar 
 2 tablespoons  cornstarch 
 1 cup  olive oil  or  vegetable oil 
 Directions
 
 Toss lettuces,  peppers ,  bacon , mozzarella, and ¼ cup  Parmesan cheese  into large mixing/salad bowl, set aside. 
 Dressing
 
 Combine  vinegar , anchovies,  oregano ,  salt ,  pepper ,  garlic ,  sugar , cornstarch, and remaining  Parmesan cheese  into blender. 
 Mix in blender, slowly adding 1 cup  oil . 
 Refrigerate until ready to use. 
 Combine salad, croutons and dressing, serve.
