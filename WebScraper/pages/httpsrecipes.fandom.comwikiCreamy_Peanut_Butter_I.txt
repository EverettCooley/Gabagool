Ingredients : 
 2 cups of  peanuts 
 Optional ingredients : 
 2 Tblsp  honey 
 4 tsp  sugar 
 2 tsp  pure vanilla extract  - or any other extract you like. 
 ¼ cup mini  chocolate chips 
 ¼ cup  rice cereal 
 Directions : 
 Put 2 cups of  peanuts  in a blender and blend until smooth. 
 For added texture and/or flavoring add one or more of the optional ingredients listed above. 
 The possibilities are endless... Use your imagination!
