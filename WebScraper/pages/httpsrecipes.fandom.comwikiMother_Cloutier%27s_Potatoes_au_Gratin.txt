Description : 
 Serves 2-3
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 large  potatoes 
 a little salted  water 
 1/2 tsp Tony's seasoning 
 ½ to ¾tbls of  butter 
 ½  onion , chopped 
 1½ cups  milk 
 3 tbsp  flour 
 4 slices  American cheese 
 Directions : 
 Cook  potatoes  in a little salted  water  until not quite tender 
 Slice potatoes thin, put in casserole dish coated with non-stick spray (or butter) (after draining) 
 Melt  butter  in seperate pan and add chopped  onion 
 Sauté in the butter until tender 
 Add 1½ cups  milk 
 When bubbly, add 3 tbsp  flour  to thicken 
 Add 4 slices  American cheese 
 Stir until melted 
 Pour over  potatoes  and lightly mix so as not to break potatoes 
 Bake at 350°F until brown
