Description 
 Source:  Catfish Strips, Breaded, Frozen 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 3 strips  catfish , cooked 
 1 hoagie or submarine  roll , sliced in half lengthwise 
 ¼ cup chopped  lettuce 
 3 slices red  tomato 
 1 slice  onion  (optional) 
 2 tablespoons  Tabasco sauce  (optional) 
 Directions 
 Preheat oven to 400 °F. Place frozen catfish strips on ungreased cookie sheet. 
 Bake for 8 to 9 minutes until internal temperature of 155 °F. 
 On roll, add lettuce and tomato slices and other optional toppings. 
 Add catfish after cooking. 
 Serve immediately. 
 Variations 
 Add  catsup ,  cocktail sauce ,  Tabasco sauce ,  mustard  or  Italian dressing  for additional flavor.
