Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Williams Estate in Tyler, Texas in 1994.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Dated 1927 
 Ingredients : 
 4 ounces  All-Bran cereal 
 12 ounces hot strong  tea 
 1  egg 
 12 ounces cake and  pastry flour 
 1 teaspoon  baking powder 
 6 tablespoons  golden syrup 
 12 ounces mixed  dried fruit 
 1 pinch  salt 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl mix dried fruit and  bran . 
 Pour hot tea over this mixture and let stand covered overnight. 
 In the morning add  egg , flour ,  salt  and syrup and mix well then divide between 2 well-greased loaf pans. 
 Bake at 350 for 55 minutes. 
 Let cool slightly in pans then turn out onto cooling racks. 
 Serve sliced and buttered.
