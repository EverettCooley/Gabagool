 YieldÂ : 4
 
 Ingredients : 
 2x  garlic  cloves, peeled 
 1-2 Fresh red chiles, seeded, veins removed 
 1/2 cup  water 
 2 tbsp fish sauce 
 juice of 1  lime 
 3 tbsp  Sugar 
 Shredded  carrot  for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Combine  garlic , chiles and  water  and liquefy in a blender or food processor. 
 Mix together with fish sauce,  lime juice  and  Sugar . Stir to dissolve  Sugar . 
 Add shredded  carrot .
