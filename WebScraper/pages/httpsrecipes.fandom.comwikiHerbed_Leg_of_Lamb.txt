Description 
 Lamb, Leg or Shoulder  by the US Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Serves : 13 
 Ingredients 
 
 2 (8 pound) legs of  lamb 
 1 tablespoon cooking spray   
 4 teaspoon  salt 
 ¾ teaspoon ground  pepper 
 ¾ teaspoon wet   rosemary  leaves 
 Directions 
 Place lamb, thin side up, in a deep roasting pan. 
 Brush cooking spray over lamb. 
 Mix salt, ground pepper, and rosemary. Coat over lamb. 
 Bake at a 400 °F oven temperature for about 3 hours. 
 Let stand 2 minutes before carving.
