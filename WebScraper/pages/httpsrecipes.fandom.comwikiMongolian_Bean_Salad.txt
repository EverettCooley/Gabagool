 Ingredients : 
 1 cup garbanzo beans 
 1 cup  kidney beans 
 1 cup  wax beans 
 1 cup  Ham , julienned 
 1 cup  Swiss cheese 
 1/2 cup  sour cream  (lowfat) 
 1 cup  Mayonnaise  (lowfat) 
 1/4 cup  lemon juice 
 1 tablespoon  dill weed 
 2 tablespoons prepared  mustard 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 1. Drain and rinse beans.
2. Combine all ingredients and chill.
