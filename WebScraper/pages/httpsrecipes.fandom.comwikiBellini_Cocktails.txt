 Ingredients : 
 2 oz  peach  nectar 
 1 tsp fresh  lemon juice 
 1 oz  peach  schnapps 
 crushed  ice 
 3 oz chilled, dry Champagne 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix the  peach  nectar,  lemon juice  and schnapps in a chilled glass. 
 Add half a cup (or more) of crushed  ice , stir, and add the champagne.
