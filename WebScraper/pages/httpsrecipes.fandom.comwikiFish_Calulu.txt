Ingredients : 
 1 kg  dried fish 
 1 kg fresh  fish 
 1 large  onion 
 3 medium large  tomatoes 
 Â½ kg  okra 
 1 kg  sweet potato leaves   or   spinach 
 3 cloves of  garlic 
 2 glasses of  palm oil 
 2  zucchini 
 Directions : 
 Leave the dried fish in hot  water  for a while but not too long so that some of the  salt  remains. 
 Season the fresh fish with  garlic ,  salt ,  vinegar  or preferably  lemon . 
 In a sauce pan alternate layers of dried fish, fresh fish, sliced  onion , sliced  tomatoes ,  spinach  or  sweet potato  leaves and sliced  okra . 
 Add the  palm oil  and simmer until everything is cooked. 
 Serve with a  manioc flour  pudding (funge) and palm oil beans.
