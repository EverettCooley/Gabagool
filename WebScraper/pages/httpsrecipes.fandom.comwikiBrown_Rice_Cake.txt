brown rice  Cake
 
 
 5 C.  rice 
 2 Tbsp.  soy sauce 
 3 Tbsp.  sesame oil 
 2 C.  brown sugar 
 20 Pieces  chestnuts 
 20 Pieces  fig  Nuts 
 1/2 Tbsp.  cinnamon 
 4 Tbsp.  molasses  Syrup 
 Put The  rice  in a Pot of Cold  water  And Let Soak For 4 Hours.Shell  chestnuts  And Cut in Half. Cut  fig  Nuts in Half And Remove The Seed.Steam The  rice  For 20 Minutes, Then Add One Bowl of  salt   water . Cook For 5More Minutes
 Mix  soy sauce  And  sesame oil ,  brown sugar ,  molasses  Syrup, And  cinnamon . AddTo The Cooked  rice . Cook it Again For Approximately 30 Minutes And Stir ItAll Together.
 Place it in a Flat Cookie Sheet And Add  peanuts  on The Top. Let it Cool.
