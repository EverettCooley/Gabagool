Ingredients : 
 1 kg  pumpkin 
 0.5 l  milk 
 15 g  yeast 
 3  eggs 
 3 tbsp  flour 
 1 tbsp  butter 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 fat 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Peel the  pumpkin , boil and mash. 
 Add the blended  yeast ,  eggs ,  flour  and mix thoroughly. 
 Put the dough in warm place for 2 hours. 
 Then add melted  butter ,  sugar ,  salt , mix finely, let the dough rise again and fry.
