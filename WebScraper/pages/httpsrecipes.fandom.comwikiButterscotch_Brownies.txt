 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/4 cup melted  butter 
 3/4 cup  flour 
 1 cup  brown sugar 
 1 teaspoon  baking powder 
 1/4 teaspoon  salt 
 1  egg 
 (1 cup chopped  nuts , optional) 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients. 
 Spread in a greased 8" x 8" pan. 
 Bake 25 minutes. 
 Cut in squares or strips while warm.
