 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  sugar 
 1 cup  canola oil 
 4  eggs 
 2 teaspoons  vanilla 
 ¼ cup  sour milk  (add 1 teaspoon of  vinegar  or  lemon juice  to  fresh milk ) 
 1 teaspoon soda 
 4 cups  flour 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 4 cups chopped  apples 
 1 cup  walnuts  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Mix together all ingredients. Note: Batter will be stiff. 
 Divide batter between 2 greased loaf pans. 
 Bake for 1 hour at 350 °F (at times i have to bake the loaves for 10 or 15 minutes more until they feel set when tapped).
