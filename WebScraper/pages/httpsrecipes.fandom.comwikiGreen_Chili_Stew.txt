      Green Chili Stew     
 Description

 Serves : 8 
 Ingredients 
 2 pounds  pork ,  mutton ,  lamb , or  beef , cut into small pieces 
 3 ears  corn  (scrape kernels from cob) or about 3 cups frozen or canned 
 3 stalks  celery , diced 
 3 medium  potatoes , peeled and diced 
 2 medium  tomatoes , diced 
 5 roasted  green chiles , peeled, seeded, and diced (or 2 tablespoons canned) 
 Directions 
 Brown meat in large pot. 
 Add remaining ingredients along with water to make a stew consistency. 
 Cover pot and simmer for approximately 1 hour.
