Description : 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Date and source unknown. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 package yellow cake mix 
 ½ cup  vegetable oil 
 1 package instant  vanilla  pudding 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 4  eggs 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 1 teaspoon  cinnamon 
 ½ cup chopped  pecans 
 Directions : 
 Beat cake mix,  oil , pudding,  sour cream  and  eggs  together very well. 
 In separate bowl mix together  Sugar ,  cinnamon  and  pecans . 
 Place ½ cake mix in greased tube pan. 
 Place ½ of  Sugar  mixture on top of cake mixture. 
 Top with remaining cake batter, then top with remaining  Sugar  mixture. 
 Bake at 325 °F for 45 minutes.
