Ingredients : 
 1 boneless beef top round roast (4 lbs.)
 
2 cups water
 
2 T. Italian seasoning
 
1 t. each  salt , dried  oregano , dried  basil , garlic powder, dried parsley flakes and pepper
 
1 bay leaf
 
14 French rolls (5 inches long)
 
 Directions : 
 Cut roast in half. Place in a 5 quart slow cooker. Combine the water and seasonings; pour over roast. Cover and cook on low for 10 to 12 hours or until the meat is very tender. Discard bay leaf. Remove meat and shred with a fork. Skim fat from cooking juices. Return meat to slow cooker. Serve on rolls. Makes 14 servings.
