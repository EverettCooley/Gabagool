Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Stewart Estate in Quinlan, Texas in 1994.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 5 whole  habaneros  seeded 
 10 whole  tomatillos  husked and rinsed 
 2 whole  Vidalia onions  skinned 
 6 whole  sweet red peppers  seeded 
 2 whole smoked  habaneros 
 3 whole  chipotle peppers 
 1 tablespoon  cumin 
 2 ounces  balsamic vinegar 
 Directions : 
 Put all ingredients except  balsamic vinegar  in a blender and process until smooth. 
 Drizzle  vinegar  over top and let marinate overnight.
