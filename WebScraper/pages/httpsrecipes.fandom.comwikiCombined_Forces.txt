 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Unknown 
 Ingredients : 
 4 to 5  ice  cubes 
 ½ fresh  lemon 
 2 measures  vodka 
 1 measure  dry vermouth 
 ½ tbsp  triple sec  (white  curaçao ) 
 Directions : 
 Put the  ice  cubes in a mixing glass. 
 Add the remaining ingredients. 
 Stir vigorously, strain and pour into a chilled cocktail glass.
