 Ingredients : 
 4 medium baking  potatoes 
 ½ cup shredded  colby  cheese 
 4 tbsp Oscar Mayer  bacon bits 
 1 bunch  green onions 
 ½ cup  sour cream 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Wash potatoes very well, score them with a knife and bake for one hour. 
 Remove potatoes and allow them to cool, so that you can handle them. 
 When potatoes are cool, cut them in half and scoop out the potato pulp. 
 Be sure to leave at least ¼ inch of potato with the skin. 
 Spray a baking dish or lightly grease and place the scooped out halves in the pan. 
 Sprinkle the potatoes with cheese and bacon. 
 Place the potatoes back in the oven for 5 – 10 minutes or until cheese is melted. 
 Remove from oven. 
 Chop the tops of the green onion off, and sprinkle them on the potatoes. 
 Serve with sour cream.
