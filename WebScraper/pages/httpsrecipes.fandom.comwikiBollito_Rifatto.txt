Boiled beef with onions
 
 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 700 g [1 lb 6 oz] stewing  steak 
 4 large  onions 
 6 tbsp  olive oil 
 1 cup  tomato purée 
 salt 
 freshly ground  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Heat the  oil  in a saucepan, slice the  onions  and cook over a low heat until softened, moistening with 1 cup  water . 
 After 10 minutes add the  tomato  puree, season with  salt  and  pepper  and continue to cook over a low heat for 10 minutes. 
 Add the stewing  steak  cut into small pieces and continue to cook for 3 – 4 minutes then serve.
