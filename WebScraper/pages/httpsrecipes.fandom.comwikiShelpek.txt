Ingredients : 
 4-5 cups of  flour 
 1.5 cups of  sour  or unboiled  milk 
 2 tablespoons of melted  butter 
 2 tablespoons of  sour cream 
 20-30 g of  yeast 
 0.5 cup of  water 
 1 teaspoon of  Sugar 
 2 cups of  oil . 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Dough is made as for baursaks , twisted in plaits, cut in pieces 3–5 cm each and fried in hot oil to a golden color.
