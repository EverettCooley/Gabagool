Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  macaroni , cooked 
 2 or more lbs  shrimp , cook, peel and cut in ½ inch or smaller 
 Salad dressing : 
 ¼ cup  vinegar 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 ¼ tsp  celery seed 
 ¼ tsp  Old Bay seasoning 
 Directions : 
 Mix the macaroni and shrimp, add salt and pepper to taste. 
 Then mix dressing. 
 Salad dressing : 
 Cook sugar and vinegar until sugar is dissolved. 
 Add old bay, celery seed and boil 1 minute. 
 Mix this with 1 pint of salad dressing, then mix into the macaroni and shrimp. 
 Makes a large bowl full.
