Ingredients : 
 ½ pint  split peas 
 1 tbsp  curry powder 
 ½  onion 
 2 cloves  garlic 
 ThtbybtybtDirections : 
 Soak  peas  in  water  for 5 hrs. 
 Put  onion ,  garlic  and split  peas  into a food processor. 
 Grind into a fine textured paste. 
 Add  salt  and  curry  powder. 
 Heat  oil . 
 Use a small tablespoon to scoop out mixture and drop into the hot  oil . 
 Fry until golden brown. 
 Serve with  mango chutney .
