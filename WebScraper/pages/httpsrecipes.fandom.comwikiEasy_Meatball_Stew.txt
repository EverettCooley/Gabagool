 Serves : 4 
 Ingredients 
 1 can  meatball  stew 
 1 (15.5 ounce) can  tomatoes , drained 
 1 (15.5 ounce) can  corn , drained 
 1 (15.5 ounce) can  green beans , drained 
 Directions 
 Cut meatballs into 4 or 5 slices. Cut up tomatoes into small chunks and mix together in a 2-quart pot. Add corn and green beans. 
 Add a half can of water and bring to a boil. Turn down heat and simmer 5 minutes. 
 Serve with crackers.
