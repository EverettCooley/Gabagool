Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup Chick pea  flour 
 ½ tsp  curry  powder 
 ¼ cup  onions  (finely chopped) 
 ½ tsp  garlic  (crushed in a pistol) 
 1 tsp  hot pepper  (Chopped) 
 ¼ cup red pepper or  sweet pepper  or  bell pepper 
 1 tbsp  parsley 
 ½ tsp Caraway 
 ½ tsp  yeast  or 1 tsp  baking powder 
 ½ cup  water 
 ½ tsp  salt  or more 
 oil  for deep frying 
 1 tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 a mixing bowl, mix chick pea  flour  and  onions . 
 Add  garlic , then  parsley  and  curry  powder, mix well. 
 Add caraway, red pepper, and  hot pepper , mix well. 
 Then add  baking powder , mix well again.add a tsp  salt 
 Add  water  a teaspoon at a time, until it becomes a heavy mixture. Almost like a heavy cake mixture. 
 Leave it to rise for a few minutes. 
 In medium-high heat; Heat the  oil  in a pan, for deep frying. 
 Drop a teaspoon full of the  butter  mixture in hot  oil  to deep fry until it turns golden brown color. 
 Remove from pan, put on paper towel or (chujio la nazi if you are in Tanzania) until it cools down.
