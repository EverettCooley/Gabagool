Ingredients : 
 mamaliga 
 1 cup crumbled sheep  feta  cheese 
 sour cream 
 mujdey sauce (mixture of ground  walnuts  finely minced  garlic  with  salt  and thinner with  water ) 
 Directions : 
 Make a medium soft polenta using equal quantities of  water  and  milk . 
 When it is done, add a cup of crumbled sheep  feta   cheese  and mix until the  cheese  is well blended in. 
 Pour on plates and serve very hot with  sour cream  and mujdey.
