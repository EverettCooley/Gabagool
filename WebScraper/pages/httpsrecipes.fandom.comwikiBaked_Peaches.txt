Description : 
 Serve with pork chops or roast, even barbecued ribs 
 
 Ingredients : 
 6  peaches 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 2 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 cup water 
 2 tbsp dairy  butter 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 Peel, halve, and remove pit. 
 In a pan over medium-high heat, blend the sugar, lemon juice, butter, and water to boiling. 
 Lower heat and simmer for about 5 mins. 
 Place in a baking pan. 
 Add the syrup. 
 Cover and bake for 30 mins. or until fruit is tender. 
 Serve, drained as a garnish or a fruit course.
