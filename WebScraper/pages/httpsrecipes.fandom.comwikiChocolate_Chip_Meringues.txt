Description : 
 The kids love them! 
 
 Formatted by Elaine Radis 
 Ingredients : 
 2  egg whites 
 ⅔ cup  sugar 
 6 oz  chocolate chips 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F in small mixing bowl; beat  eggs . 
 Add  sugar  after  eggs  are "more than foamy" - they should be light and fluffy. 
 Beat for 8 minutes. 
 Fold in chips. 
 Drop by tbsp onto greased cookie sheet. 
 Turn oven off, leave overnight.
