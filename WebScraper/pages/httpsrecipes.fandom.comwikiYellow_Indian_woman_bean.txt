 About Yellow Indian woman bean : 
 The Yellow Indian Woman Bean was originally brought to Montana by a Swedish family. Is a rare heirloom bean that is now found in Native American communities in Montana. The Yellow Indian Woman is a small, yellowish-gold bean that, when cooked, is creamy and resembles the flavor of  Pinto  and  Black beans . It is ideal for slow cooked dishes, as the bean holds its shape while cooking.
