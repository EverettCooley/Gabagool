Ingredients : 
 1 cup  shortening 
 5 – 6 cups  flour 
 1 cup  molasses 
 1 cup  buttermilk 
 1 cup  sugar 
 4 tsp  baking soda 
 1 tsp  salt 
 2 tsp  ginger 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 ½ tsp ground  cloves 
 ½ tsp ground  nutmeg 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Whip shortening and sugar in a bowl. 
 Add molasses and beat until fluffy. 
 Mix 2 cups flour with all remaining dry ingredients. 
 Alternate adding flour mixture and buttermilk to molasses batter, keeping fluffiness. 
 Add flour until mixture is soft but not sticky. 
 Roll out to about ¼ inch thickness and cut using a floured edge. 
 Sprinkle with sugar. 
 Bake for 9 – 11 minutes on ungreased cookie sheet.
