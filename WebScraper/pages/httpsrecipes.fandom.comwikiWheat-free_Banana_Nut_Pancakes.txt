Description : 
 Makes 16, 4-inch pancakes.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cup  rice flour 
 2 teaspoon  baking powder 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  xanthan gum 
 1¼ cup  water 
 3 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 2 tablespoons  honey 
 2  egg yolks  - beaten 
 2 large  bananas , mashed 
 ½ cup chopped  walnuts 
 2  egg whites 
 vegetable oil  for frying 
 Directions : 
 Combine  flour ,  baking powder ,  salt  and  xanthan gum  in medium bowl. 
 Stir in  water ,  oil ,  honey ,  egg yolks ,  banana  and  nuts . 
 Beat  egg whites  in medium bowl until stiff peaks form. 
 Fold into batter. 
 Pour batter onto hot oiled griddle or skillet; cook on both sides until golden brown.
