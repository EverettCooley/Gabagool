Description : 
 Spicy minced meat
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 medium size  onions , chopped 
 2 spoons sunflower or other  vegetable oil 
 50 cc chili paste ( berbere ) 
 50 cc  zeTekhelese tesmi  (spiced butter) 
 1 teaspoon chopped  ginger 
 1 teaspoon chopped  garlic 
 6 large  tomatoes , skinned 
 1 kilo  beef  or  lamb , shredded 
 pepper  and  salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Heat the  oil  in a frying-pan and fry the  onions  light golden. 
 Add the  berbere  and the tegelese tesmi and some  water  if necessary and simmer it on a low fire. 
 Add the  ginger  and the  garlic  after 10 minutes and the sliced skinned  tomatoes , some  salt  and some  pepper  after another 5 minutes. 
 After 15 minutes add the meat and simmer it until the meat is done. 
 Add some  water  if necessary. Serve with injera.
