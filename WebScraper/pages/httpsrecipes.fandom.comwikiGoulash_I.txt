Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 8/19/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  hamburger 
 1  onion  chopped 
 1 can  mushrooms 
 1 pkg. plain  macaroni 
 1 can diced  tomatoes  (14.5 oz) 
 1 can  tomato soup 
 1 small can  tomato juice 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 ¼ tsp  chili powder 
 garlic powder  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Brown hamburger and onion, drain. 
 Add remaining ingredients and simmer for about 20 minutes.
