Description : 
 Contributed by Randy Rigg 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 quart  popped corn 
 ½ lbs  gum drops 
 ½ lbs  peanuts 
 1 lbs  marshmallows 
 ½ cup melted  margarine 
 ½ cup  corn oil 
 Directions : 
 Melt marshmallows,  margarine , and  corn oil . 
 Pour over  popcorn ,  nuts  and gum drops. 
 Mix together and pat in a bundt cake pan or angel food cake pan. 
 Cool.
