Description : 
 Makes 4 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups shredded  cabbage 
 1½ cups cooked  rice , cooled 
 ½ cup grated  carrots 
 ¼ cup chopped  onion  (optional) 
 2 tablespoons  sour cream 
 2 tablespoons reduced-calorie  mayonnaise 
 1 teaspoon  sugar 
 ¼ teaspoon  celery seed 
 ⅛ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅛ teaspoon ground  white pepper 
 Directions : 
 Combine  cabbage ,  rice ,  carrots  and  onion  in mixing bowl. 
 Blend  sour cream ,  mayonnaise ,  sugar ,  celery seed ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Add to  rice  mixture; toss lightly. Chill.
