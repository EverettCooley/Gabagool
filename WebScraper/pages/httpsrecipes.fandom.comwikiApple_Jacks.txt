 Prep Time: 10 minutes 
 Cook time: 10–15 minutes On mini gril 
 Serves: 4-6 
 
 Description : 
 Delitious Pork And Apple Burgers To Temp Even The Fussyest Of Eaters!
 
 Ingredients : 
 500g minced pork 
 1 onion 
 1 medium sized bramley apple 
 8 cream crackers 
 dijon mustard 
 salt and pepper 
 1 egg 
 4-6 ciabatta 
 Directions : 
 Crush the crackers to fine crumbs in a large bowl. 
 add the mince and finely chopped onion and apple. 
 Add dijon mustard, egg and salt and pepper to taste. 
 Using hands, mix well. 
 divide mixture into 4-6 into rectangular approx. ciabatta. 
 cook under either medium heat grill or on a mini gril machine approx. 10 mins (golden brown) 
 Serve On light toasted ciabatta with garnish of your choice 
 Enjoy! 
 
 For Best Results Serve With Home Made Wedges :)
