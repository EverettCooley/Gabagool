 Ingredients : 
 2  chicken breasts 
 1 pkg  broccoli  spears 
 1 can  cream of chicken  soup 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 2 tbsp  lemon juice 
 ¼ cup sharp  Cracker Barrel cheese 
 buttered  bread crumbs 
 wild rice 
 Directions : 
 Boil 2 breasts of chicken done. 
 Cool and bone. 
 In the chicken broth, cook 1 package broccoli spears 5 minutes. 
 Drain. 
 Now place spears in bottom of baking dish. 
 Put chicken pieces over that. 
 Then mix 1 can cream of chicken soup, ½ cup mayonnaise and 2 tablespoons lemon juice. 
 Pour this over broccoli and chicken. 
 Grate ¼ cup sharp cracker barrel cheese and sprinkle over mix. 
 Then sprinkle buttered bread crumbs over it. 
 Bake at 400°F for 35 minutes. 
 Serve over wild rice.
