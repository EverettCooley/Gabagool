Ingredients : 
 2 quart  apple cider , chilled 
 6 oz can lemonade concentrate, thawed 
 28 oz bottle  ginger ale , chilled 
 ice cubes 
 Directions : 
 In a large pitcher or punch bowl, combine cider and thawed lemonade concentrate. 
 Add gingerale and  ice ; stir to blend. 
 If desired, garnish with  apple  slices. 
 Makes 24 (4oz) servings.
