Description : 
 Grilled Kafta
 
       Arayes Kafta     
 Ingredients : 
 16 oz  kafta 
 1½  pitta  bread rounds, cut into 12 pieces 
 2 tbsp  butter 
 Directions : 
 Spread some  butter  on the inner part of  bread  pieces. 
 Divide kafta into 12 portions. 
 Spread over buttered  bread . 
 Put pieces in a  grill  or an oven at 200 °C for 5 minutes or till kafta is cooked. 
 Serve hot with  yoghurt .
