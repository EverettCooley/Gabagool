Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2.5 liter  water 
 200 g  broad beans 
 300 g  smoked meat 
 200 g  potatoes 
 1  onion 
 salt  (to taste) 
 3 tablespoon  sour cream 
 Directions : 
 Wash and soak the beans for about 3-5 hours. 
 Put the beans and soak  water  into a pot, add the rind of smoked meat, finely chopped  onion  and  salt , bring to a boil. 
 Cook until almost soft and then add the smoked meat and sliced  potatoes . 
 Serve hot with  sour cream .
