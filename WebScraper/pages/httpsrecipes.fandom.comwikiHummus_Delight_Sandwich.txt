Ingredients : 
 2 slices whole wheat vegan bread 
 2 tablespoon  hummus 
 alfalfa sprouts 
 Vlasic sandwich stackers (garlic flavor)  pickles 
 ½ teaspoon  Dijon mustard 
 1 large, ripe  tomato 
 Directions : 
 You take 1 slice of whole  wheat  vegan bread and spread 2 good size tablespoons of hummus on it. 
 Then you add a generous amount of sprouts and a layer of fresh  tomato  slices. 
 Top with two Vlasic stacker slices and add ½ tsp of Dijon mustard to other slice of vegan bread and put them together.
