Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Ramon Estate in Mesquite, Texas in 1988.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 part  vodka 
 1 part sour  apple schnapps 
 1 part  apple juice 
 sour apple hard candy 
 apple peel curls 
 Directions : 
 Pour vodka, schnapps and apple juice into a cocktail shaker with ice. 
 Shake well and strain into pitcher or chilled martini glasses. 
 Drop a sour apple candy in each glass and garnish with a curl of apple peel.
