 Description : 
 figs  grow well in Arabia and are a popular dessert, plain or dressed up like this.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2  lemon 
 water 
 1/4 cup  Sugar 
 2 Tbs. honey 
 1 whole  clove 
 18  walnut  halves 
 18 fresh  figs  or one 17-ounce jar whole  figs , drained 
 1/2 cup plain  yogurt 
 1/4 tsp.  vanilla 
 Chopped  walnuts 
 Directions : 
 1) With vegetable peeler, cut a thin strip of peel about 2Â inches long from  lemon . Set aside. 
 2) Squeeze juice from  lemon ; add  water  to measure 1/2 cup. 
 3) In a small saucepan combine  lemon peel  strip,  lemon juice  mixture,  Sugar , honey, and  clove . Bring to boiling; reduce heat. Cook and stir till syrup is thickened and bubbly. Remove from heat; discard  clove  and  lemon peel . Cool slightly.
 4) Insert one  walnut  half into each  fig . Place stuffed  figs  in individual dessert dishes. Stir together cooled syrup,  yogurt , and  vanilla . 
 5) Spoon  yogurt  mixture over  figs . Cover and chill till serving time. 
 6) Sprinkle each serving with chopped  walnuts . Makes 6 servings.
