Description : 
 Cuban rice pudding
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  rice 
 1½ cups  water 
 1 small piece of  lemon peel 
 1 small  cinnamon  stick 
 4 cups  milk 
 1 cup  sugar 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  vanilla extract 
 cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Wash the  rice . 
 Cook the  rice  in the  water ,  lemon peel  and  cinnamon  stick until  rice  is soft (about 10 minutes or so). 
 Add the  milk ,  salt ,  vanilla  and  sugar . 
 Bring to just under a boil and turn down heat. 
 Cook at medium-low heat, uncovered, until it gets thick and  rice  has absorbed most of the  milk , stirring occasionally to prevent sticking. 
 Remove and discard the  cinnamon  stick pour into one large bowl or several smaller bowls and sprinkle with  cinnamon .
