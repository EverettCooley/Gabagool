Description : 
 Quick and EASY - excellent  avocado  and  shrimp  sandwich
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 slices of bread of your choice 
 1 sliced  California avocado 
 Cooked  shrimp , chilled 
 1 Tbsp  salad dressing  or  mayo 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Using bread of your choice, spread on your favorite  salad dressing  or  mayo 
 Add sliced  avocado  and fresh/frozen/canned  shrimp 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Enjoy a cool and delicious lunch or munchie
