Description : 
 Recipe by Joslin
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lbs extra-lean  ground beef 
 1 small  onion 
 1 tbsp fresh  jalapeno chile , minced 
 Seasonings mixture : 
 ¼ tsp  ground coriander 
 ¼ tsp ground  cumin 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  pepper , fresh ground 
 Cheese : 
 1 non-fat  mozzarella  cheese 
 Serving : 
 4  tortillas 
 4  tomato  slices 
 cilantro  sprigs 
 1 tbsp commercial  taco sauce 
 Directions : 
 Mix the burgers ingredients together; form into burgers of equal size about ¼ inch thick without compacting the meat. 
 Sprinkle the seasoning mixture of spices on both sides of patty. 
 Grill or broil, 4" from source of heat, for 3 minutes on each sides for medium-rare patties. 
 Place the  mozzarella  cheese on center of each patty. 
 Cook for another minute and until  cheese  melts. 
 Serve each patty on a warmed 8" flour  tortilla , topped with a  tomato  slice,  cilantro  sprigs, and taco sauce. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Food exchanges per serving: 
 
 2½ medium/fat exchanges + 1½ bread/starch exchange 
 cal: 289; pro: 21g; car: 23g; fat: 13g; (calories from fat 40%) fib: 2g; cho: 54 mg; sod: 305 mg; pot: 367 mg
