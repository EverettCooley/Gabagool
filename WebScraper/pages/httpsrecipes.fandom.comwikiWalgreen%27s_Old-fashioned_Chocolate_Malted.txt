 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz  chocolate syrup 
 3 scoops vanilla  ice cream 
 5½ oz cold  milk 
 1 heaping tsp  malted milk  powder (plain) 
 whipped cream  for topping 
 Directions : 
 Place all ingredients in a blender except whipped cream. 
 Blend until smooth pour into a chilled tall glass and top with whipped cream serve with 2 straws for sharing.
