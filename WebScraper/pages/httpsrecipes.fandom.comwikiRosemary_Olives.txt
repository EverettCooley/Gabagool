rosemary  * 1 Pint Mixed (2 Cups)
 
 1/4 Cup  olive oil 
 3 Sprigs Fresh  rosemary  Leaves, Picked 
 
In a Pan Over Low Heat, Combine The ,  oil  And  rosemary  Leaves. Warm Until Heated Through, About 7 Minutes. Serve Warm or at Room Temperature, Alongside a Bowl of Store Bought Spiced Nuts. You Can Make The Early in The Day And Leave Them at Room Temperature For up to Several Hours.Source: Real Simple
