 Ingredients : 
 ½ kg  ground beef 
 ¼ cup  breadcrumbs 
 ½ cup  milk 
 ½ cup chopped  onions 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  pepper 
 ½ teaspoon  oregano 
 1  egg 
 Directions : 
 Combine the above ingredients and form into 1" size meatballs. Brown in skillet and drain off excess fat. 
 Simmer (covered) for about 15 minutes to allow the flavor to blend. Delicious over  rice  or noodles.
