Description : 
 This Burmese style  beef  satay is great as a starter or as the accompaniment to  rice . With meaty aroma and tangy consistency, every skewer of beef is red-hot, smoky fun!
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds extra lean  beef 
 ½ cup  soy sauce , regular or light only 
 2 tablespoons  honey 
 2 tablespoons fresh  lime juice 
 1 tablespoon  curry powder 
 1 teaspoon  chili powder 
 1 cup  onion , grated, red, white, or yellow only 
 2 tablespoons  garlic , minced or grated 
 bamboo  skewers 
 Directions : 
 Cut  beef  into thin strips. In a bowl, put all the other ingredients and mix well. 
 Add to the  beef  strips and let stand for 30 minutes. 
 Thread the strips onto the bamboo skewers. Grill for 8–10 minutes, turning and basting often. 
 Arrange  beef  satay on a plate. Serve hot and plain with rice.
