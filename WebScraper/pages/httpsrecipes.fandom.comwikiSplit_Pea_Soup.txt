Description : 
 Today's preparation is Split Pea Soup. Good comfort food for cool
weather. 
 enjoy, 
Glenn
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb dried  split peas 
 2  carrots , chopped 
 3 cloves  garlic , chopped 
 2 stalks  celery , chopped. 
 1 small  onion , chopped 
 2 tbsp  bacon bits 
 2 cups  chicken stock 
 3 cups  water 
 Directions : 
 Soak the split peas in the water. 
 Coarsely chop all the other veggies, then run them through a food processor, to smooth them out. 
 Put all the ingredients into a slow cooker, bring to a boil, then 
 simmer till the consistency is right. 
 
 Notes : 
 Possible seasonings include Sage, Marjoram, Thyme, bay leaves, black
pepper, and salt. Season to taste.
