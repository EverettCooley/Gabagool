Ingredients : 
 2 cups  sugar 
 2 cups  corn syrup 
 18 oz  peanut butter 
 8 cups  Rice Krispies 
 6 oz  butterscotch morsels  
 6 oz  semi-sweet chocolate  chips 
 Directions : 
 Bring  sugar  and syrup to a boil. 
 Add  peanut   butter  and cook, stirring until well blended. 
 Remove from heat and pour hot mixture over cereal. 
 Mix quickly and thoroughly. 
 Spread in a greased 9x12x2 inch pan. 
 Sprinkle butterscotch and  chocolate morsels  over top. 
 Press morsels into bar mixture lightly with spoon. 
 When cool, cut into bars and store at room temperature.
