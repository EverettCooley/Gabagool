Description : 
 Contributed by Delma at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves 6. 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cans  black beans 
 3 cups green giant frozen  corn 
 1  green pepper , diced 
 1  red pepper , diced 
 1 cup  scallions , diced 
 1½ cloves  garlic , chopped fine 
 ½ cup Food Lion  Italian dressing 
 1 bunch  cilantro , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Drain  black beans  and pour into bowl. 
 In medium sauce pan, sauté  corn  until kernels begin to turn brown. 
 Add  corn  to  black beans  with remaining ingredients. 
 Mix together until coated. 
 Top with fresh  cilantro  and serve with your favorite grilled fish,  pork  or  chicken . 
 Serve with ice cold Luzianne tea.
