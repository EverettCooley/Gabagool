Description : 
 Yields 6 tablespoons. Equivalent to 2 whole  eggs  or ½ cup  egg substitute .
 
 Ingredients : 
 3  egg whites 
 ¼ cup  non-fat milk 
 1 tbsp regular  non-fat dry milk 
 1 tsp  vegetable oil 
 Directions : 
 Beat  egg  whites lightly with a fork. 
 Add remaining ingredients and beat until thoroughly blended.
