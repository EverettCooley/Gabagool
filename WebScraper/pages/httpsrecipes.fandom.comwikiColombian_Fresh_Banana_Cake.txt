Ingredients : 
       Colombian Fresh Banana Cake     
 1 pkg  yellow cake mix 
 ⅛ tsp  baking soda 
 2  eggs 
 ¾ cup  Coca-Cola 
 1 cup mashed ripe  bananas 
 2 tsp  lemon juice 
 ⅓ cup finely chopped  nuts 
 2 large  egg whites 
 1½ cup  light brown sugar  packed 
 ⅛ tsp  cream of tartar 
 ⅓ cup  Coca-Cola 
 1 tsp  vanilla extract 
 1 dash  salt 
 Directions : 
 In a mixing bowl combine the cake mix  baking soda  and  eggs . 
 Add to the batter. 
 Blend ingredients for 3 minutes. 
 In a bowl combine the  bananas  with the  lemon juice ;add to the batter. 
 Add the chopped  nuts  to the batter and beat for 1 minute. 
 Turn the batter into a baking disk. 
 Bake in a preheated 350ºF oven for about 40 minutes. 
 Cool on a rack for 15 minutes. 
 Remove cake from pan and turn right side up on a rack to finish cooling. 
 Combine all of the ingredients except the vanilla extract. 
 Beat for 1 minute. 
 Place over boiling  water . 
 Beat for 7 minutes. 
 Remove from boiling  water . 
 Add the  vanilla extract  and continue beating about 2 minutes. 
 Spread on the sides and top of the cold  banana  cake.
