Info 
 Cook Time : 
 Serves : 
 
 Ingredients 
 Crust 
 1 1/2 cups  graham cracker  crumbs 
 1/4 cup granulated  sugar 
 1/3 cup  butter  or  margarine  -- melted 
 Cheesecake 
 3 (8 ounces each) pkgs  cream cheese  -- softened 
 1 cup granulated sugar 
 1/4 cup  light brown sugar 
 1 3/4 cups  sweet potatoes , mashed 
 2  eggs 
 2/3 cup  evaporated milk  -- undiluted 
 2 tablespoons  cornstarch 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 1/8 teaspoon ground  nutmeg 
 Topping 
 2 cups  sour cream  -- room temperature 
 1/3 cup granulated  sugar 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 Directions 
 Crust 
 1.Combine crumbs, sugar, and butter in bowl. 
 
2.Press onto bottom and 1 inch up on sides of 9 inch springform pan. 
 
3.Bake at 350 degrees F for 6 to 8 minutes. Do not allow to brown. Remove from oven and cool. 
 
 Cheesecake 
 1.Beat cream cheese, sugar and brown sugar in large bowl. 
 
2.Beat in sweet potatoes, eggs, and milk. Add cornstarch, cinnamon and nutmeg. Beat well.
 
3.Pour into crust. 
 
4.Bake at 350 degrees F for 55 to 60 minutes or until edge is set. 
 
 
 Topping 
 1.Combine sour cream, sugar and vanilla. 
 
2.Spread over warm cheesecake. 
 
3.Return to 350 degree F oven and bake 5 minutes. Cool on wire rack. Remove side of pan and chill several hours or overnight.
