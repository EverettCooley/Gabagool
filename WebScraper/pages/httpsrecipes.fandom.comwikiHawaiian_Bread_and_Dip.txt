Ingredients : 
 1 loaf King's Hawaiian Bread (or Triskets or crackers or chips) 
 8 oz.  sour cream 
 1 pkg. Knorr vegetable soup mix 
 Directions : 
 Mix sour cream and dry soup mix. 
 Let stand in refrigerator (covered) several hours or overnight.
