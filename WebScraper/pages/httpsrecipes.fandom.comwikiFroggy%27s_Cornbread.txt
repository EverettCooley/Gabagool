Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  cornmeal 
 1 cup  flour 
 2  eggs 
 buttermilk  enough to make batter 
 ½ stick melted  butter 
 Directions : 
 Place batter in preheated, pregreased iron skillet. 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Bake for 35 minutes or until golden brown.
