 Description : 
 The first time you might want to use just part of the pudding for smunchies. Some whipped topping recipes and mixes freeze OK for a few days but haven't tested any longer than a week.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pack sugar-free  chocolate pudding 
 2 cups  non-fat milk 
 3 cups  peanut butter 
 70  graham cracker  squares 
 low-calorie  whipped topping 
 Directions : 
 Mix  chocolate  pudding according to directions on package using nonfat  milk . 
 Cool thoroughly. Mix  Peanut   butter  with pudding. 
 Drop 1 tbsp. onto 1 square graham cracker. 
 Place (1 tbspÂ ?) low-calorie whipped topping on top of pudding mixture, cover with second graham cracker square. 
 Freeze until ready to use.
