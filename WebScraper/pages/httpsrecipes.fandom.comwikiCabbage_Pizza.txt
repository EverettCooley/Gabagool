INGREDIENTS
 2cups shredded red cabbage
 1cup sliced brussels sprouts
 2teaspoons olive oil
 2teaspoons chopped rosemary
 8ounces store-bought pizza dough
 1/2cup shredded Asiago
 1/4cup balsamic vinegar
 2teaspoons raw sugar
 DIRECTIONS
 Heat oven to 450Â°. In a bowl, toss 2 cups shredded red cabbage, 1 cup sliced brussels sprouts, 2 tsp olive oil, and 2 tsp chopped rosemary. Transfer to a baking sheet; bake 8 minutes. Roll out 8 oz store-bought pizza dough into a 10-inch circle. Top with cabbage mixture and 1/2 cup shredded Asiago; bake 20 minutes. In a small saucepan, simmer 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar and 2 tsp raw sugar until syrupy, about 4 minutes. Drizzle over pizza and serve.
