 Description : 
 This is a nice  Endive  and beetroot salad
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 heads  Endive , the  bitter  core removed and finely chopped ( chicory ) 
 2 cooked beets, coarsely grated or chopped in cubes 
 1  Apple , coarsely grated or chopped in cubes 
 3 tablespoons  sour cream , more if you like 
 1 teaspoon  mustard 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix the  sour cream  with the  mustard  and add  salt  and  pepper . 
 Combine with the rest of the ingredients. Variations: use (as many as you like) chopped hard-boiled  eggs  instead of the beets, then leave out the  mustard  and use some  curry  powder in the  sour cream . Add some chopped  green onion . 
 Use 4-6 oz cubed or chopped  Cheese  of your choice instead of the beets.
