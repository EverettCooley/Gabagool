pink beans  And Sausages
 
 1/2 Lb. Link Sausages 
 2 Cups  water 
 2 T.  margarine 
 1 Cup Chopped  Onion 
 1 Large clove  garlic , Minced 
 4 Cups Cooked or Canned Pink or  pinto beans , Drained 
 1/3 Cup Firmly Packed  light brown sugar 
 1/2 Cup  Catsup 
 2 T.  cider vinegar 
 2 T. Prepared  mustard 
 2 T.  chili powder 
 3 Cups Hot Cooked  white rice 
 
Simmer  Sausage  in  water  in Medium Pan For About 5 Minutes. Remove Sausages, Drain Them And Cut Into Thin Slices. Turn Sausages Into Large Skillet With  margarine ,  Onion  And  garlic  And Over Medium Heat, Saut? Until  onions  Are Translucent. Add Remaining Ingredients Except  rice  And Cook, Stirring Occasionally Until beans Are Heated Through, About 15 Minutes. Serve Over Hot  rice .
 Serves 6
