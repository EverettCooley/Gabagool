Cocoa Applesauce Cake 
 1/2 cup reduced-cal tub margarine 
1/2 cup sugar 
1 1/2 cups applesauce 
2 1/4 cups flour 
3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder 
2 teaspoons baking soda 
1 teaspoon cinnamon 
1/4 teaspoon cloves, ground 
1/4 teaspoon salt 
1 pinch nutmeg 
1 pinch allspice 
1 1/2 oz pecans, chopped 
 Preheat oven to 350. 
 Spray a 12 cup Bundt pan with non-stick cooking spray. 
 In a large bowl, with mixer on high speed, beat margarine and sugar until creamy. 
 Stir in applesauce. 
 In a medium bowl, combine flour, cocoa, baking soda, cinnamon, cloves, salt, nutmeg, and allspice. 
 Add to margarine mixture, and mix well. 
 Gently stir in nuts. Spoon into prepared pan. 
 Bake 50-60 minutes, until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. 
 Cool on rack.
