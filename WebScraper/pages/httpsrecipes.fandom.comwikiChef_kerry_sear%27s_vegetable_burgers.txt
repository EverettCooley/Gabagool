 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tbsp  olive oil 
 1 cup minced  Onion 
 1 cup  Grated Carrot 
 1 cup grated  turnips 
 1 cup grated  zucchini 
 1 cup grated beets 
 1/2 tbsp minced  garlic 
 1/2 tbsp ground  cumin 
 1 tbsp  dill 
 1 tbsp  tarragon 
 1/4 cup  instant oats 
 1/4 cup  water 
 1 to 1 Â½ cups mashed 
 potatoes 
 1/4 cup chopped  hazelnuts 
 1/3 cup cooked  rice 
 salt  &  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Saute  Onion ,  carrots ,  turnips ,  zucchini , beets,  garlic ,  cumin , and herbs until vegetables begin to wilt (3 - 5 minutes). Allow to cool slightly. Soak  Oats  for 5 minutes in  water  - drain & press out  water . Add to vegetable mixture along with  potatoes ,  nuts , and  rice . Season with  salt  and  pepper  to taste. Form into 8 patties. Grill on well oiled grill for 3 -4 minutes each side or saute in  olive oil  until golden brown.
