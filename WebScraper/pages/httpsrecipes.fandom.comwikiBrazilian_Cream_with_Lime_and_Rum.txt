 Ingredients : 
 3  California avocados , halved and peeled 
 1½ tbsp  confectioner's sugar  (to taste) 
 ¼ cup each:  lime juice ,  light rum 
 1½ cups  heavy cream , whipped 
 lime  slices or wedges, for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Purée avocados with  sugar ,  lime juice , and  rum  until smooth. 
 Fold in  cream . 
 Spoon into compotes, champagne glasses, coupes, pot de cremes, individual soufflé dishes or into  avocado  shells.
