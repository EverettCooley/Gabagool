 Ingredients : 
 1⅔ cups  all-purpose flour 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 5 tbsp  unsalted butter , softened 
 ¾ cup  sugar  or  Splenda 
 ¼ cup Egg Beaters or other fat-free  egg substitute 
 1 tsp  vanilla extract 
 cherry jam (sugar free if desired) 
 16 whole  cherries , fresh or frozen (no added sugar) 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 375°F. 
 Chop fresh or frozen cherries. 
 In a small bowl, stir together flour and baking powder. 
 In a large bowl, mix the butter and sugar until smooth then beat in egg and vanilla. 
 Slowly blend in the dry ingredients. 
 Form dough into 1½ inch balls, and place onto ungreased cookie sheets. 
 Scoop on ½ teaspoons of cherry jam and ½ teaspoon of chopped cherries. 
 Bake 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until golden. 
 Let stand on cookie sheet two minutes before removing to cool on wire racks.
