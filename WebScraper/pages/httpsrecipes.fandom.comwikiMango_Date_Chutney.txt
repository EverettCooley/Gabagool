Ingredients : 
 2 tsp  salt 
 1Â½ cups  vinegar 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 6 pitted  dates , chopped 
 6 cloves  garlic , minced 
 6 tsp  dried red chilies , crushed 
 2 tsp  mustard seed 
 1 tbsp  oil 
 1 lb [green [mango]]s 
 Directions : 
 Grate peeled  mangos  coarsely and sprinkle with  salt . 
 Boil  vinegar . 
 Add  sugar ,  dates  and  mangos . 
 Cook over low heat until  mango  is soft, about 10 minutes. 
 Add all remaining ingredients except  mustard seeds . 
 Fry  mustard seeds  in a tablespoon of  oil  before adding to mixture. 
 Add and continue simmering slowly until thick. 
 Cool and pack in small jars.
