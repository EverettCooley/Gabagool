Description : 
 Contributed by Dorie at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pkg.  lemon flavor Jell-O 
 1½ cups boiling water 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 cup cold water 
 ice cubes 
 ½ cup  Miracle Whip 
 1½ cups diced  apples 
 ¾ cup  celery 
 ¼ cup chopped  walnuts 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve gelatin in boiling water. 
 Add lemon juice. 
 Combine cold water and ice cubes to make 2 cups. 
 Add to gelatin, stirring until slightly thickened. 
 Remove any unmelted ice. 
 Stir in salad dressing with wire whisk; chill until thickened. 
 Fold apples, celery and walnuts into gelatin mixture. 
 Pour into mold chill until firm, about 3 hours.
