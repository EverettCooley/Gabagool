Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Clovers Estate in Arlington, Texas in 1989.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  broccoli 
 ⅓ cup chopped white  onion 
 1 cup grated  Swiss cheese 
 ⅓ cup chopped  red pepper 
 3  eggs 
 1 cup  milk 
 ½ cup all purpose  baking mix 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Cook broccoli for 10 minutes then drain and put in pie plate. 
 Sprinkle onion, cheese and pepper over broccoli. 
 Combine eggs, milk, baking mix, salt and pepper and beat well. 
 Pour egg mixture over broccoli mixture then bake at 375°F for 45 minutes.
