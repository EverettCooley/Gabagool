 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 part baby's prepared  peaches  or pureed fresh  peaches 
 1 part baby's prepared  cottage cheese  or pureed  cottage cheese 
 1 part ripe  California avocado  puree 
 Directions : 
 Spoon desired amount of  peaches  and  cottage cheese  into a small bowl. 
 Top with desired amount of  avocado  and serve.
