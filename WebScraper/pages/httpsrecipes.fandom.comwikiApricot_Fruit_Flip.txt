apricot  Fruit Flip
 
Yield: 4 Servings
 
 1 cn  apricot  Nectar (12 Oz) 
 1 md Ripe  banana 
 1/4 c Instant Nonfat Dry  milk  Powder 
 2 ts  lemon juice 
 1 tb  Honey 
 1 ts Grated  lemon  Rind 
 ice  Cubes
 
Combine All Ingredients, Except  ice  Cubes, in Container of an ElectricBlender. Add Enough  ice  Cubes to Measure 4 Cups. Blend Until Smooth.
 
Enjoy.
