description : 
 Recipe from  The Sneaky Kitchen .
 
 Ingredients : 
 29 oz can of  pear  halves 
 1 stick  cinnamon 
 3 whole  cloves 
 8 oz reduced-fat  cream cheese  or  Neufchatel , softened 
 ¼ cup  honey 
 ¼ cup  graham cracker crumbs 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Drain  pear  halves and set aside. 
 Place the juice in a saucepan and add the  cinnamon  stick and cloves. 
 Bring to a boil, then turn down to a simmer while you prepare the rest of the dish. 
 Combine  cream cheese  and honey; beat until blended. 
 Fill  pear  halves with  cream cheese  mixture. 
 Place in a shallow baking dish. 
 Strain juice onto  pears , discarding the cloves and  cinnamon  stick. 
 Sprinkle with crumbs. 
 Bake for 15 to 20 minutes.
