      American Potato Salad, Italian style     
 Ingredients : 
 5 lbs  red potatoes 
 1 cup chopped  green onions 
 1 (8 ounces) bottle  Italian dressing 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 ½ cup  salad dressing 
 Directions : 
 Boil  potatoes  with skins on, until tender. 
 Cool. 
 Peel, and cut into cubes. 
 Add  onions  and  italian dressing . 
 Mix gently, but well. 
 Refrigerate at least one hour. 
 Add rest of ingredients. 
 Mix well. 
 chill  several hours. 
 mix  again before serving. 
 garnish  with  eggs  if desired.
