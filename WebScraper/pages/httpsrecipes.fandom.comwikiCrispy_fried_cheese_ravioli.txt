Who likes crispy fried foods? Who likes delicious ravioli?  If so, this recipe is for you!
 
 Ingredients : 
 Cheese  ravioli  (Such as  Buitoni  brand, it's a personal favorite) 
 Vegetable oil  (Enough to fill most of the pot, not all) 
 Directions : 
 Pour the oil in the pot and put the pot on the stove. 
 Wait until it boils. 
 Put your ravioli in the pot, but be careful, the pot is VERY HOT! 
 Wait for a few minutes and get a spaghetti strainer and pour the contents into it. 
 Wait for the ravioli to cool and enjoy!
