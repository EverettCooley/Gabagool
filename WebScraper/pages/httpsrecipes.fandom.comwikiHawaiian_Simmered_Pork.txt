Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Thomas Estate in Kaufman, Texas in 1991.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4 pounds boneless  pork loin 
 ¾ cup  white vinegar 
 ½ cup  soy sauce 
 ½ cup  brown sugar  firmly packed 
 ½ teaspoon ground  ginger 
 Directions : 
 Place pork loin in 5 quart kettle. 
 Then in bowl mix together vinegar, soy sauce, brown sugar and ginger and pour over pork. 
 Cover and simmer 3 hours.
