Here'a recipe for the soup. I've made this, it's not fancy, but plain, simple and very good. Really easy to make. — Jane
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Makes 4 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp  butter  (no substitutions) 
 4 medium  green onions , chopped (¼ cup) 
 2 tbsp  flour 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 ¼ tsp  ground mustard  (dry, i use Coleman's) 
 1 cup whole  milk 
 1 (10½ oz) can condensed  chicken broth 
 2 cups shredded Wisconsin  cheddar  cheese (8 oz) 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter  in 2 quart saucepan over medium heat. 
 Cook  onion  in butter about 2 minutes, stirring occasionally, until tender. 
 Stir in flour,  pepper  and  mustard . 
 Stir in  milk  and broth. 
 Heat to boiling over medium heat, stirring constantly. 
 Boil and stir 1 minute. 
 Stir in  cheese ; heat over low heat, stirring occasionally, just until cheese is melted.
