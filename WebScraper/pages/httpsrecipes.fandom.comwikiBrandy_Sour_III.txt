 Ingredients : 
 2 parts (50mls)  brandy  (Cyprus cocktail  brandy  is not strong) 
 1 part (25mls)  lemon juice  - as pure as possible 
 2 drops  Angostura Bitters 
 top up with soda add lots of  ice  cubes stir 
 Directions : 
 Take a long tall glass and add the ingredients. 
 Decorate with a slice of fresh  lemon  and serve immediately.
