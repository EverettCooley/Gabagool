 Description : 
       Coconut Bondas     
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  coconut , grated 
 5  potatoes , boiled and mashed 
 6 green chillies, chopped 
 1 tsp  cummin  seeds 
 1 tsp  mustard seeds 
 3 bread slices 
 3 tbsp  coriander leaves , chopped 
 Few  Raisins 
 A few chopped  cashew   nuts 
 salt  to taste 
 oil  for deep frying 
 2 tbsp  oil , for saut√©ing  coconut 
 1  egg , beaten 
 Directions : 
 Heat 2 tbsp of  oil  in a pan. Fry the  mustard seeds  and  cummin  seeds, till they splutter. Add the green chillies,  coconut ,  salt ,  Raisin  and  cashew   nuts . Fry for 5 minutes. Let the mixture cool. Dry grind the bread slices. Add to the mashed  potatoes ,  salt  and bread powder. Mix well to make a soft dough.
 Take a small portion of the  Potato  dough, flatten on your palm and fill with  coconut  stuffing and shape into a small ball. Repeat with the rest of the dough and stuffing. Roll in the beaten  egg  and deep fry in hot  oil  till golden brown. Serve with green chutney or  tamarind  chutney.
