 Description : 
 Pretty and tasty!
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 bag fresh  spinach 
 2 ripe avocados 
 1  pomegranate 
 1 small  red onion 
 1 Cup plain  yogurt 
 Â¼ tsp  garlic powder 
 fresh  lemon juice 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Prepare the dressing:
 Mash the avocados in a medium bowl or food processor. 
 Add the  yogurt  and blend well. 
 Season with  garlic powder ,  lemon juice ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Let sit while preparing the salad. If dressing is too thick, you may add a little  water  or  milk . 
 Slice  Onion  into thin rings. 
 Separate  pomegranate seeds . 
 Wash greens, and toss with  Onion  and  pomegranate  in large bowl. 
 Serve with dressing.
