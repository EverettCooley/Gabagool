Ingredients : 
 1 drained rinsed can  garbanzo beans 
 3 to 5 cloves of  garlic  depending on how you feel 
 ¼ to ½ cup  onion  or  green  or  red pepper 
 juice from a whole  lemon  or  lime 
 1 dribble of  sesame oil  (leave out/in at your own risk!!) 
 fresh  cilantro  to taste 
 another twist = fresh ground  cumin 
 Directions : 
 Place the above in a food processor and blend until nearly smooth 
 Spread in pita pockets heated in micronuker better. 
 Place slices of fresh  tomatoes  in pitas and fresh ground  pepper  along with spread of hummus.
