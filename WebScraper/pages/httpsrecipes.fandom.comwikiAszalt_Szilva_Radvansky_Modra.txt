Description : 
 Baked prunes
 
 Ingredients : 
 24  prunes 
 ½ juice of  lemon 
 1 tsp  all-purpose flour 
 ½ cup  sour cream 
 ¼ cup  bread crumbs 
 4 tbsp  butter 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 325°F. 
 Cook  prunes  in  lemon juice  and 2 cups  water  until they are soft. 
 Remove cooked  prunes  with a slotted spoon. 
 Put liquid over lowest possible heat and reduce it by half. 
 Stir  flour  into  sour cream  and whip the mixture into the  prune  liquid. 
 Simmer together for 5 minutes. 
 Place the  prunes  in an ovenproof casserole. 
 Pour thickened liquid over  prunes  sprinkle with  bread crumbs  and dot with pieces of  butter . 
 Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
