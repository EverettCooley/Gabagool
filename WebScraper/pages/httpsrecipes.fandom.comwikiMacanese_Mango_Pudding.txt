 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 packs Jell-O 85g ( mango  or  peach  or  pineapple ) 
 3 cups Boiling  water 
 1 cup  water 
 1 can  evaporated milk  170g 
 1  egg 
 1 Tbsp  Sugar 
 3-4 Sweet  mangos 
 Directions : 
 Place the Jell-O powder and the  Sugar  into a mixing bowl; add in the boiling  water  and mix until all the powder dissolve. Add in a scrambled  egg . 
 After mixing in the  egg , mix in the  evaporated milk  and the remaining  water . Pour the solution into serving size Jell-O bowls or one big container. Finally, add in the sliced  mango  pieces. 
 Refrigerate overnight; be sure not to cover the  mango  pudding when it cool.
