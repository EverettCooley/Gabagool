 1½ tsp  baking soda

 Directions : 
 Add all ingredients into a bowl and mix until batter is smooth. 
 Lightly grease a frying pan with vegan  margarine , and spoon mixture into pan. 
 Flip pancakes when the bottoms start to brown. 
 Once both sides are nicely browned, place in a dish and leave in a warm oven while cooking the remaining pancakes.
