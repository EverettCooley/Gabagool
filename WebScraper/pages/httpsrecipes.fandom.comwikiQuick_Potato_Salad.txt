Description : 
 Contributed by Delma at  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 4 to 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds small  red potatoes  — cooked 
 ½ cup  green onions  — sliced 
 1 cup  Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® with Bacon dressing 
 paprika 
 chives  — optional 
 Directions : 
 Cut warm  potatoes  into 1-inch cubes. 
 Combine  potatoes ,  onions  and dressing in large bowl; toss gently. 
 Dust top with  paprika . 
 Garnish with  chives , if desired.
