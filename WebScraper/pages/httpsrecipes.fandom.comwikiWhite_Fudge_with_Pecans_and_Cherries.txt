Description : 
 From my Aunt Maggie's collection. This one has been around for as long as I can remember. She made this for me when I was very young, so I know it's been around at least since the early 60s. Tried and True. I make it for my cookie exchange I host every year at Christmas. 
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2¼ cups  granulated sugar 
 2 teaspoons  butter 
 ¼ cup  milk 
 1 cup chopped  pecans 
 2 teaspoons  vanilla extract 
 ½ cup  sour cream 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon  light corn syrup 
 ⅓ cup  candied cherry , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Combine  sugar ,  butter ,  milk ,  sour cream ,  salt  and syrup in a saucepan. 
 Cook over medium heat until mixture boils. 
 Remove from heat and let set about an hour. 
 Add  vanilla  and beat until candy loses its gloss and holds its shape. 
 Stir in  nuts  and  cherries  and pour into a greased, square pan. 
 Allow to cool completely before cutting into squares.
