 Description : 
 Bun  Tom Xao is the Vietnamese name for Vietnamese  lemon grass   shrimp  salad with  vermicelli . It is the proffered salad in Vietnamese restaurants.
 
       Bun Tom Xao Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 Shrimp : 
 shrimp 
 lemon grass 
 peanut oil 
 cloves  garlic 
 red pepper flakes 
 onions 
 fish sauce 
 sugar 
 Salad : 
 shredded  iceberg lettuce 
 bean sprouts 
 cilantro 
 basil  sprigs 
 chopped  peanuts 
 rice vermicelli 
 nuoc nam 
 Directions : 
 Boil the  water , stir in  vermicelli , add  garlic ,  lemon grass ,  red pepper flakes  and  onions . 
 Prepare a mixture of  fish sauce  and add  shrimp . 
 Add this to other ingredients. 
 Cook noodles and place them over the vegetables.
