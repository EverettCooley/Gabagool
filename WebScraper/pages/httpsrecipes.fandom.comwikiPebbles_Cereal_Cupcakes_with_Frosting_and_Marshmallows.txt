 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes about 2 dozen pebbles cupcakes. 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ stick  butter 
 6 cups miniature  marshmallows 
 8½ cups Cocoa or Fruity Pebbles cereal 
 16 oz can frosting 
 Directions : 
 Microwave butter in bowl on high 45 seconds or until melted. 
 Add marshmallows, mix to coat. 
 Microwave 1½ minutes. 
 Or until marshmallows are melted and smooth. 
 Add cereal, mix to coat. 
 Press cereal mixture into greased muffin cups to form cupcakes. 
 Cool, remove from pans. 
 Spread with frosting before serving. 
 Decorate as desired. 
 Some decoration ideas are sprinkles, gumdrops, and jelly beans.
