Ingredients
 
 30 ounces  dark chocolate , grated 
 1¼ cup  heavy cream 
 ¼ tablespoon  rose extract 
 ½ cup  powdered sugar 
 ½ teaspoon  cinnamon 
 ¼ teaspoon  nutmeg 
 ¼ teaspoon  almond extract 
 ½ cup  milk , as needed 
 Directions : 
 In a saucepan over medium heat, melt the chocolate with the heavy cream. 
 Gradually add milk, and blend thoroughly to make a smooth sauce. 
 Stir in cinnamon, nutmeg, rose extract, and almond extract. 
 Continue to heat, stirring frequently, until the mixture is smooth. 
 Use as a dip.
