Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 From:  1,001 Recipes For People with Diabetes  by Surrey Books 
 Yield: 6 servings (about ½ cup each) 
 Ingredients : 
 1¼ cups dried  pinto beans 
 vegetable  cooking spray 
 1 medium  onion , coarsely chopped 
 salt  and  pepper , to taste 
 Directions : 
 Wash and sort beans, discarding any stones. 
 Cover beans with 2 inches water in large saucepan; heat to boiling and boil, uncovered, 2 minutes. 
 Remove from heat; let stand, covered, 1 hour. 
 Drain beans; cover with 2 inches water and heat to boiling. 
 Reduce heat and simmer, covered, until beans are tender 1½ to 2 hours. 
 Drain, reserving 2 cups liquid. 
 Spray large skillet with cooking spray; heat over medium heat until hot. 
 Saute onion until tender, 3 to 5 minutes. 
 Add 1 cup beans and 1 cup reserved liquid to skillet; cook over high heat, mashing beans until almost smooth with end of meat mallet or potato masher. 
 Add half the remaining beans and liquid; continue cooking, mashing beans. 
 Repeat with remaining beans and liquid. 
 Season to taste with salt and pepper. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving (⅙ of recipe):
 
 106 Calories | 0.4g Fat | 0mg Cholesterol | 2mg Sodium | 6.1g Protein | 20g Carbs 
 Diabetic Exchanges: 1½ bread
