Ingredients : 
 1Â¾ cup  barley 
 10 cup  vegetable broth 
 2  onions , chopped in large pieces 
 3  potatoes , white, chunked 
 1 large  sweet potato , chunked 
 1 lbs  Brussels sprouts 
 2 tbsp  tamari , low sodium 
 1 tsp  parsley  flakes 
 2 tsp  oregano 
 1 tsp  dill weed 
 3  tomatoes , wedged 
 Directions : 
 Place  barley  and  water  in large pot. 
 Bring to a boil. 
 Add remaining ingedients, except the  tomatoes . 
 Reduce heat, cover, and simmer about 50 minutes. 
 Add the  tomatoes . 
 Cook an additional 10 minutes. 
 Serve hot. 
 Can be prepared in a slow cooker, but don't add the  tomatoes  until just before serving.
