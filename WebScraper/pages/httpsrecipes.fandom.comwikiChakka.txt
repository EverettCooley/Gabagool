Description : 
 Curds with greens
 
 Ingredients : 
 500 g (18 oz)  suzma 
 ½ bundle (2 oz)  green onions 
 ½ bunch (½ oz)  fresh coriander  and  dill 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Grate suzma well, put in a bowl, mix with chopped greens,  green onions ,  salt  and  pepper .
