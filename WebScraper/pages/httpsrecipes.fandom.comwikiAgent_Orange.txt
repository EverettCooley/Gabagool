Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz Yukon Jack® Canadian  whiskey 
 1 oz Jim Beam®  Bourbon whiskey 
 1 oz  Apple schnapps 
 1 oz  vodka 
 1 oz  light rum 
 1 oz  triple sec 
 ½ oz  grenadine syrup 
 2 oz  orange juice 
 Directions : 
 Have a hurricane glass filled with ice. 
 Add ingredients, and shake. 
 Garnish with  cherry  and  orange .
