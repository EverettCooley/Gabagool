Description : 
 Contributed by Eddie Rickenbacker at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups  butter , softened 
 5 – 6 cups  confectioners' sugar , divided 
 1 cup  peanut butter 
 1¾ cups  marshmallow  cream 
 2 tsp  vanilla 
 dipping  chocolate 
 Directions : 
 Cream butter and 2 cups confectioners sugar in mixing bowl until light and fluffy. 
 Add peanut butter, marshmallow cream and vanilla; blend well. 
 Gradually add remaining confectioners sugar. 
 Add enough to make an easily molded consistency. 
 Form egg shapes dip in chocolate.
