 Ingredients : 
 1  avocado 
 1  lemon 
 1 tsp  chives 
 ½ tsp  dill 
 1  egg 
 2 Tbsp  Mayonnaise 
 1 Tbsp dijon  mustard 
 ¾ Cup Italian seasoned  bread  crumbs 
 1 Pound crabmeat 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 garlic  seasoned  olive oil 
 Directions : 
 Chop  avocado . 
 Cut  lemon  in 1/2 and squeeze 1/2 over  avocado . 
 Beat  egg , add mayonnaise,  mustard ,  chives ,  dill  and 1/4 cup of  bread  crumbs. 
 Add crab and  avocado . 
 Season with  salt  and  pepper .
 Make into 6 patties. 
 Dip in remaining  bread  crumbs. 
 Heat  oil  and fry patties, turning once until browned. 
 Use remaining 1/2  lemon  to slice and garnish.
