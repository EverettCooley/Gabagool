Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 200 g  Potato  (peeled, boiled in salted  water  until soft and drained) 
 25 g  Onion  (sliced thinly) 
 50 ml  coconut cream 
 50 g Smoked  Tuna  (thinly sliced) 
 1  cherry pepper  (chopped) 
 Juice of 1  lime 
 salt  to season 
 Directions : 
 Crush the  Onion  and  cherry pepper  with  lime juice  and  salt . 
 Add in the  Tuna  and crush them well. Add in the  Potato , crush and mix all the ingredients together. 
 Bind with  coconut cream . Adjust seasoning.
