Ingredients : 
 2 cups flaked  fish , canned or freshly cooked 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise  or  salad dressing 
 ½ cup  celery , diced(optional) 
 ½ cup cooked  peas  or beans 
 2 tablespoon chopped  onion 
 3 hard-boiled  eggs 
 6 – 8  lettuce  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients except the  eggs  and  lettuce , being careful not to break the fish into too small pieces. 
 Serve on  lettuce  leaves. 
 Garnish with sliced  eggs .
