Vacuum Cleaner Cookies
 1 box yellow cake mix
1/2 cup margarine
3 eggs
8 oz cream cheese
1 box powdered sugar
1/2 cup shredded coconut
1/2 cup walnuts.
 Combine margarine, cake mix and 1 egg. Stir & spread in 10 x15 baking 
pan. Then beat 2 eggs, cream cheese & powdered sugar. Stir in coconut & 
nuts. Spread over cake mixture in pan. Bake at 325 deg for 45 min till 
golden. Cut into bars.
