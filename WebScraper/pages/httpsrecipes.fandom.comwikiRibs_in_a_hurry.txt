 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 	tablespoons  oil 
 3 	lbs Country-Style  Pork  Ribs (thick Bone-In Ribs Will Turn OutBetter Because They Hold Together Better) 
 Rib Seasoning : 
 1 	teaspoon  paprika 
 1/2 	teaspoon  garlic powder 
 1/2 	teaspoon  pepper 
 1 	teaspoon  salt 
 Cooking Liquid : 
 2 	chipotle Chiles in Adobo, Whole 
 3 	 garlic  cloves, Whole 
 1/2 	sweet  Onion , in Large Chunks 
 1/2 	cup Dark  beer 
 1/4 	cup  cider vinegar 
 1 	tablespoon  worcestershire sauce 
 Grilling Sauce : 
 1 cup Barbecue Sauce, With 1/4 c of Cooking Liquid Stirred In 
 Directions : 
 1. Heat Pressure Cooker Over Medium Heat, Add  oil . 
 2. Rub Meat With Rib Seasoning, And Brown Them in The Cooker One OrTwo at a Time so You Don't End up Steaming Them. 
 3. Once The Ribs Are Browned on All Sides, Add Them All Back to TheCooker With The Cooking Liquid Ingredients. 
 4. Close The Pressure Cooker, Cook According to Your PressureCooker Instructions, or About 18-20 Mins at High Pressure. 
 5. Pre-Heat Your Grill Pan or Barbeque Over Low Heat. 
 6. When The Timer Goes Off, Remove The Cooker From Heat And RunUnder Cool  water  in The Sink. 
 7. Remove Ribs From Cooking Liquid. 
 8. Stir 1/4 Cup of The Cooking Liquid Into Your Barbeque Sauce OfChoice (optional: Add One of The Chipotles From The Cooking LiquidInto The Sauce to Spice it Up). 
 9. Grill Ribs Over Low Heat Basting With Bbq Sauce And TurningEvery 5 Minutes or so Until a Nice Glaze Has Formed All Over The Ribs.
