Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 1 teaspoon  cinnamon 
 2 medium  apples , cored and chopped 
 1 cup chopped  walnuts 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 ½ cup sliced  celery 
 3 cups cooked  brown rice  (cooked in equal parts  water  and  apple juice ) 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Heat  butter  and  cinnamon  in large skillet over medium-high heat. Add  apples ,  walnuts ,  onion  and  celery ; sauté until vegetables are tender crisp. 
 Stir in  rice  and  salt ; heat thoroughly.
