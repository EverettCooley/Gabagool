Description : 
 Portuguese-style steak
 
 This recipe taken from www.Portuguese-recipes.com 
 Serves 4. 
 Ingredients : 
 4 pieces  London broil  or  sirloin  strip 
 1 large  onion , sliced 
 1 cup  white wine 
 1 tsp  crushed red pepper 
 1  garlic  clove, chopped 
 1 tbsp  paprika 
 1 tsp  salt 
 4  eggs 
 Directions : 
 Marinate  steak  for 6 hours. 
 In large skillet fry  steak  and  onions  together. 
 After about 20 minutes, pour marinade in skillet; cook until absorbed. 
 When done, drop raw  egg  over each  steak  and cover and cook until  egg  is cooked.
