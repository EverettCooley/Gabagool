 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 	cup 	 cornmeal 
 1 	cup 	 flour 
 1 	cup 	Diced  Apple 
 1/2 	cup 	 brown sugar 
 4 	tsp 	 baking powder 
 1 1/4 	cup 	 milk  Alternative -- * see 
 Note 
 Mixed with 1 tsp.  Apple 
 cider vinegar 
 2 	tbsp 	 oil 
 1/2 	cup 	 orange   marmalade  -- (or 
 Other jam) 
 Directions : 
 1	Plain  rice milk , lite  soy milk ,  almond milk , etc.
 2 Mix all ingredients in order listed.
 3 	Put one half of the batter in a greased 8 or 9 inch square baking pan.
 4	Evenly top batter with ½ cup jam and top with remaining batter.
 5	Bake at 400 °F for 25 minutes.
