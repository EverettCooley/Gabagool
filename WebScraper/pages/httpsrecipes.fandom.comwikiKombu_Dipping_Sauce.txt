Ingredients : 
 1 cup  seaweed stock 
 ¼ cup  dark soy sauce 
 ¼ cup  mirin  (seasoned  rice wine ) 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 1 tbsp  salt 
 green onions  or  sesame seeds  (optional) 
 Seaweed stock : 
 several pieces of  kombu  (thick  kelp  leaves) 
 water , let it sit overnight. 
 Directions : 
 Make a seaweed stock by immersing several pieces of  kombu  in cool  water , let it sit overnight. 
 To make dipping sauce, simmer the ingredients. 
 Add some  green onions  or  sesame seeds  if you like.
