Ann Malone's Best Decorator Icing
 Categories: Frostings, Cakes
 Yield: 1 Recipe
 
 1 1/2 c  shortening 
 1/2 c  water  or  whipping cream 
 (more if Needed)
 
 1/2 ts  salt 
 2 lb Confectioners  sugar 
 2 ts Flavoring ( orange ,  vanilla , 
 -  almond , Cream Bouquet,  rum 
- or  lemon )
 
 1 ts  butter  Flavoring 
 
Beat  shortening  at High Speed For 5 Minutes. on Low Speed, Add  water  OrWhipped Cream And  salt .
 
Slowly Add  sugar  And Flavorings. Beat on High Until Light And Fluffy.Adjust Liquid or Flavorings if Necessary. Makes Enough For One 9-Inch,Two-Layer Cake.
 
Note: This Recipe May be Cut in Half For hand Mixers or Doubled For LargeMixers.
 
Enjoy.
