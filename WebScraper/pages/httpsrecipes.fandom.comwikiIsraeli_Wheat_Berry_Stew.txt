 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 5 cup  water 
 1 1/2 cup  great Northern beans 
 1 cup  wheat berries 
 6 sm  potatoes  cut in half 
 1 lrg  Onion  sliced 
 4 cloves  garlic  minced 
 5 tsp  cumin  ground 
 3 tsp  turmeric 
 1/2 tsp  black pepper  ground 
 Directions : 
 Mix together all ingredients in crockpot. 
 Cook at high 8 to 10 hours.
