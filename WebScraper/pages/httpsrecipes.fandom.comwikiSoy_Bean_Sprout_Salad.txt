 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 250 g/8 oz soy  bean sprouts 
 1 tablespoon oriental  sesame oil 
 2 tablespoons  peanut oil 
 2 tablespoons toasted  sesame seeds , crushed 
 3 tablespoons Korean soy sauce 
 2 teaspoons  ginger juice  pressed from grated  ginger 
 2 spring  onions  (scallions), finely chopped 
 Directions : 
 Trim roots off  bean sprouts  and discard. If sprouts are long cut into convenient lengths. 
 Drop into a pan of lightly salted boiling  water  and boil until just tender. 
 Drain, drop into iced  water  and drain again. Combine all other ingredients and toss with sprouts. 
 Serve chilled.
