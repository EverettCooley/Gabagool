Ingredients : 
 1  California avocado , chopped 
 12 oz (375 g) cooked  turkey , cut into strips 
 ½ bunch salad  onions , chopped 
 ½  red pepper , deseeded and chopped 
 ¼ pint  mayonnaise 
 2 tsp  lemon juice 
 1 tbsp freshly chopped  coriander 
 1 tbsp (to 2) mild  curry powder 
 1 tsp  cumin 
 1 tsp  garam masala 
 salt  and freshly ground  black pepper 
 to serve: shredded  lettuce 
 to garnish:  fresh coriander 
 Directions : 
 Place all the ingredients in a bowl and mix thoroughly. 
 Arrange the shredded  lettuce  on a serving plate, spoon over the curried  turkey  and garnish with the coriander.
