Ingredients : 
 12  peppers 
 5  onions 
 1 lb / 500 g  tomatoes 
 3 tablespoons  oil 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Wash the  peppers , take the cores out and cut into very narrow strips. 
 Fry in hot  oil  until soft. 
 Put aside. 
 Chop the  onions  and fry in hot  oil  until soft. 
 Add the crashed  tomatoes  without peel. 
 Adjust the  salt  and let fry at slow heat until most of the liquid is absorbed. 
 Add the fried  peppers  to the fried  tomatoes  and mix it. 
 Serve cold.
