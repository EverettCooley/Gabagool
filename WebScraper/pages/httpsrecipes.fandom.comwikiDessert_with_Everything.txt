Description : 
 In Romanian: Bacanie
 
 Ingredients : 
 6  eggs 
 1 cup  confectioner's sugar 
 7 oz / 200 g  butter 
 6 tablespoons  flour 
 4 oz / 100 g  peanuts 
 4 oz / 100 g  walnuts 
 4 oz / 100 g  almonds 
 4 oz / 100 g  Raisins 
 4 oz / 100 g  rahat-lokum 
 4 oz / 100 g  figs 
 4 oz / 100 g  orange peel  from preserves 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 1 teaspoon  baking powder 
 oil 
 Directions : 
 Slightly roast the  peanuts ,  walnuts  and  almonds , then chop roughly. 
 The  raisins , rahat-lokum and  figs  are also chopped roughly. 
 cream  the  butter  with the  sugar , add the  yolks  one by one, then the  baking powder ,  vanilla  and  flour . 
 Mix well. 
 Then add all the fruits and  nuts . 
 In the end, add the whipped  egg whites , gently fold them in and pour the whole thing in an oiled pan, covered with oiled paper. 
 Bake at medium heat and let cool in the pan. 
 Cut into rectangles.
