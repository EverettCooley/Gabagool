Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes ½ gallon. 
 Ingredients : 
 2  eggs 
 1 cup  sugar 
 2 cups dairy  eggnog 
 1½ cups  whipping cream 
 2 tsp  vanilla extract 
 1 tsp  rum extract 
 ½ tsp  cinnamon 
 ½ tsp  nutmeg 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Beat eggs until foamy. 
 Gradually add sugar. 
 Beat until thickened. 
 Add remaining ingredients; mix well. 
 Chill 1 hour. 
 Churn freezer according to manufacturer's instructions. 
 After freezing. 
 Transfer to plastic container. 
 Let set (ripen) in freezer at least 2 hours before serving.
