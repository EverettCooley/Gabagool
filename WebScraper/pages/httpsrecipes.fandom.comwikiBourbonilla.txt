Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz  bourbon 
 1 oz brown  crème de cacao 
 2  vanilla beans   or  ½ tsp  vanilla extract 
 ½ cup  ice 
 Directions : 
 Put everything in a glass and refrigerate for at least one hour. 
 Shake well, remove beans, and serve straight up.
