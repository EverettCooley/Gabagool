Ingredients : 
 2 tsp  olive oil 
 ¼ tsp  crushed red pepper 
 4 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1 medium-sized  red onion , quartered and thinly sliced 
 1 medium-sized  red bell pepper , cut into 3-inch strips 
 3 oz  vegetarian Canadian bacon , diced 
 2 lbs fresh  green beans , cut into 2-inch pieces 
 8 oz  cremini mushrooms , sliced 
 2  plum tomatoes , diced 
 3 tbsp vegan  soy Parmesan cheese 
 1½ tsp dried  basil 
 1 tsp dried  marjoram 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in saucepan over medium-high heat. 
 Add  crushed red pepper , and sauté for 1 minute. 
 Add  garlic ,  onion ,  bell pepper  and  bacon , and cook 3 minutes. 
 Add  green beans  and  mushrooms , and cook 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
 Add  tomatoes , soy Parmesan,  basil  and  marjoram . 
 Reduce heat to low, and cook 3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
