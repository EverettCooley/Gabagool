 Description : 
 This is snack for kids and adults that’s quick and easy to prepare. You can use diced leftover bread to make this recipe too.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 package  garlic  and  Onion  croutons 
 1/2 cup melted  butter 
 2 tablespoons  sesame seeds 
 2 teaspoons  celery salt 
 1 teaspoon sweet  paprika 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in a bowl and toss. Then transfer onto a cookie sheet and toast in 275 °F oven for 20 minutes. Stir every 5 minutes. Let croutons cool before serving with your kids’ favorite beverage.
