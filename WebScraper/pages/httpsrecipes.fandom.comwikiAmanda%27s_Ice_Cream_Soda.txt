Amanda's Ice Cream Soda
 Your favorite ice cream
1 tall glass -- can be chilled
Coke or root beer
 Fill your glass with soda (not all the way to the top, leave room for ice cream
). Scoop out ice cream and put into glass, watch out it will fizz! Dive in and
have a great time!!!
