 Description : 
 Flavorful fried  plantains  cubes. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 6  plantain   bananas ; ripe, peeled and cut into bite-sized cubes 
 1/2 teaspoon  cayenne pepper  or red pepper 
 1/2 teaspoon peeled, grated  fresh ginger  root 
 teaspoon  salt 
 2 tablespoons  water 
 palm oil 
 Directions : 
 Grind together grated  ginger root ,  pepper , and  salt , then mix with  water . 
 In a glass bowl toss together the  plantain  cubes and spice mixture. 
 Heat  oil  in a deep skillet to 350 degrees F. 
 Fry  plantains , turning once, until golden brown on both sides. 
 Drain on absorbent paper, keep in warmed oven until all the  plantains  are fried. 
 Serve immediately.
