Ingredients : 
 ¼ tsp hot  paprika 
 ½ tsp  onion powder 
 1 clove  garlic , crushed 
 ½ tsp grated  ginger 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 ½ cup  jalapeño jelly 
 ½ cup  mango jam 
 ½ cup  oil 
 ½ cup  soy sauce 
 2 cups  ketchup 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients to make sauce. 
 Brush on  pork  or  chicken  while cooking.
