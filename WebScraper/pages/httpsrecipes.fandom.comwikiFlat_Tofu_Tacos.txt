 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 12 ounces  tofu 
 1/2 cup cooked  brown rice  or  bulgur wheat 
 1/4 cup  peanuts 
 1/2 cup  green pepper , diced 
 2 cloves  garlic , crushed 
 1/4 teaspoon  chili powder 
 1/4 cup catsup 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 2 to 3 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 6 tortillas 
 Taco sauce 
 Directions : 
 Garnishes
Chopped  tomato 
Minced  Onion 
Shredded  lettuce 
 Combine  tofu ,  rice ,  peanuts ,  green pepper ,  garlic ,  chili powder , ketchup and  salt  in a large bowl; mash thoroughly.
 Heat the  oil  in a skillet and fry the tortillas. Top each tortilla with your choice of garnishes. Spoon on the  tofu  mixture and season with taco sauce.
