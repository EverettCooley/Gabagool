No Bake Fruit Squares
 
 1 C. Seedless  Raisins 
 1/2 C.  dried apricots 
 1/2 C. Dried  figs 
 1 C.  dates 
 1/2 C.  candied orange peel 
 1/4 C.  Candied cherries 
 1 C. Chopped  walnuts 
 3 Tbsp.  orange juice 
 Confectioners'  sugar 
 
Wash The  Raisins ,  apricots  And  figs . Grind TogetherAlong With  dates ,  candied orange peel ,  cherries  AndWalnuts. Add Enough  orange juice  to Hold The FruitsAnd Nuts Together. Mix Well. Press Into a Greased 8 x
 
 8 x 2 Inch Pan. 
 
Chill Several Hours or Overnight in Refrigerator. CutIn Squares. Roll Squares in Confectioners'  sugar .
 Makes 2 Dozen Pieces.
