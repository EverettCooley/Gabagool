Ingredients : 
 1 (8 oz) container plain  yogurt 
 2 tbsp  cocoa  or  carob  powder 
 2 tbsp  brown sugar  or  honey 
 popsicle sticks and cups 
 Directions : 
 Liquify in a blender, pour into molds, insert popsicle sticks, and freeze.
