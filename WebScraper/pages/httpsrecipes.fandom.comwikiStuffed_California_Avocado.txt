 Description : 
 This recipe is good to serve with a frozen margarita or a bottle of Corona.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2  California avocados 
 ½ Pound small shrimps 
 ½ Cup of chopped white  onions 
 2 Tbsp of mayonaise 
 1 Tbsp of  ketchup 
 ½  lime 
 1  cilantro  leaf 
 salt 
 pepper 
 ¼ Cup of  Shrimp  broth 
 Directions : 
 Cook the shrimps in hot  water  for five minutes (Save the Broth). 
 Cut the shrimps in halves and mix the  onions  and the  cilantro , add  salt  and  pepper . 
 After all is mixed, add the mayonaise and the  ketchup . 
 Add one or two tablespoons of the broth and some drops of  lime .
 Cut the avocados in halves, take the seed out.
 Now you are ready to stuff the avocados and serve them with some saltine cookies.
