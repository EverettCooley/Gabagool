Description : 
 This recipe does not work well for waffles. The addition of  eggs  gives them more the consistency of  wheat flour  pancakes.
 
 Recipe makes one batch. 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cup  brown rice flour 
 ½ cup  potato flakes 
 1 tablespoon non- alum   baking powder 
 1 teaspoon  sea salt  (optional) 
 3 tablespoons unrefined  vegetable oil 
 2 cups  water  or  soy milk 
 2  eggs  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Mix dry and liquid ingredients separately; then combine and stir well. 
 Pour small amounts of batter onto a hot, non-stick griddle and bake. 
 Serve with syrup.
