Description : 
 Makes 5 cups, 4 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  chicken , cooked, cut up 
 ½ cup  zucchini , chopped 
 ½ cup  radishes , chopped 
 ¼ cup 2%  yogurt 
 1 tbsp  soy sauce 
 ½ tsp bulk granulated artificial  sweetener , like Sugar Twin 
 ½ tsp  ginger , ground 
 3 cups  lettuce , shredded 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl, combine  chicken ,  zucchini , and  radishes . 
 In a cup, combine yogurt, soy sauce, sweetener and  ginger . 
 Pour over  chicken  mixture to coat evenly. 
 Refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving, then toss well gently. 
 Divide into 4 portions. 
 Nutritional information : 
 car: 2g | pro: 15g | fat: 7g | cal: 131
