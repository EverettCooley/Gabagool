Ingredients : 
 1 tbsp  granulated sugar 
 4 tbsp  sugar replacement 
 ⅓ cup  buttermilk 
 1  egg  (well beaten) 
 1 cup  flour 
 ⅛ tsp  baking soda 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 nutmeg 
 1  cinnamon 
 1  vanilla extract 
 1  salt 
 1  oil  for deep-fat frying 
 Directions : 
 Combine sugars,  buttermilk , and  egg ; beat well. 
 Add remaining ingredients, except  oil . 
 Beat just until blended. 
 Heat  oil  to 375°F. 
 Drop dough from doughnut dropper into hot fat. 
 Fry until golden brown, turning often. 
 Drain. 
 Nutritional information : 
 1 serving (1 donut - did you think it would be 3 or 4?) = 1 bread, 1 fat calories = 130 (yep still only 1 donut)
