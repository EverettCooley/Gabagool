Description : 
 Shredded  beef  with tender  cabbage 
 
 Ingredients : 
 400 g  beef 
 1 stalk  spring onion 
 200 g  tender cabbage 
 1  red pepper 
 Seasoning : 
 2 tbsp  light soy sauce 
 ¼ tbsp  soda powder 
 1 tbsp minced  ginger 
 1 tbsp  cornflour 
 1 tbsp  salt 
 ½ tbsp  yellow wine 
 Directions : 
 Shredded  beef , add seasoning and mix well, marinate for 30 minutes. 
 Chop spring  onion  and  red pepper , add to  beef  for use. 
 Stir fry tender  cabbage  with 2 tbsp of  oil  and ½ tbsp of  salt  until cooked. 
 Dish up tender  cabbage  and arrange on surface as garnish. 
 Heat wok with 2 tbsp of  oil . 
 Stir fry marinated  beef  of in high heat until 80% cooked. 
 Pour  beef  on surface of tender  cabbage  with all sauce, serve.
