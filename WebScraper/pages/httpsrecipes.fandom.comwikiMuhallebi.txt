Description : 
 Milk pudding
 
 Ingredients : 
 5 tablespoons  corn flour 
 4 tablespoons  sugar 
 1.2 litre (2 pints) semi skimmed or full fat  milk 
 1 tablespoon  rose water 
 1 teaspoon ground  cardamom 
 1 heaped tablespoon ground  pistachios 
 Directions : 
 Place  corn flour  in a large saucepan, and gradually add  milk , mix well until texture is smooth. 
 Next add ground  cardamom ,  Sugar ,  rosewater  and mix well. 
 Place saucepan on medium heat, stirring for about 10 minutes until mixture thickens. 
 Immediately spoon hot mixture onto a serving plate. 
 Sprinkle with ground pistachios and leave to cool for 2 hours.
