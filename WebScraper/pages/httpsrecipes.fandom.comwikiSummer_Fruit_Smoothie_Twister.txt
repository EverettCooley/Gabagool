Description : 
 Contributed by  Indiancuisineandculture Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  nectarine 
 6 ounces fat-free peach frozen  yogurt 
 ½ cup Dole  pineapple juice  (or orange-guava juice) 
 ½ cup sugar-free lemonade (or you can use Crystal Light) 
 Directions : 
 Put all ingredients into blender and blend until smoothie consistency is reached!.
