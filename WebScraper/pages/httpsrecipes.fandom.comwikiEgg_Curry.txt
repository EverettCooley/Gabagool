 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 6  eggs , 
 3  potatoes , 
 1 Tbsp.  curry  powder, 
 1/2  Onion . 
 1/4 cup  oil , 
 2 cloves  garlic , 
 1 cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Boil the  potatoes  with the  egg  until  potatoes  are cooked. Peel  potatoes  and  eggs . Heat  oil  in a pan, and fry the  eggs  until brown on all sides. Drain the  eggs . 
 Put 1 Tbsp. of the  oil  in a pot, heat and add the ground  onions  and  garlic . Fry for 1 minute, then add the  curry  powder. Fry again, for 3 mintues stirring often so that it does not burn. 
 Add  potatoes  and  eggs  with the  water . Bol for appros 10 minutes. Serve with  rice  or roti.
