Ingredients : 
 1 oz  vodka 
 1 oz  Kahlúa 
 Directions : 
 Fill pint glass with  ice . 
 Fill 2 to 3 inches from top with  cream  and top off with coke. 
 Transfer between two glass for foamy effect. 
 Variations: add Baileys® add  amaretto  omit  vodka  to get a frosty cake. 
 Serve in: highball glass.
