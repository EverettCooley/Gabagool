 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb.  Beef  liver liquefied in food processor or blender 
 1 box Jiffy  corn  muffin mix 
 2 teaspoons  garlic salt 
 6 bags  raspberry  tea (optional); great for bitches being bred and for intestinal problems. 
 Directions : 
 Mix well, and spread into cookie sheet sprayed with Pam. Bake 350 degrees for 18-20 minutes. Cool and cut into squares, refrigerate. Will keep for about 2 weeks (if they last that long) or you can freeze them!
