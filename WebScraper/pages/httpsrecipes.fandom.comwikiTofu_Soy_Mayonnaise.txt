 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cube  tofu 
 2/3 cup powdered  soymilk 
 1 small  Onion 
 1/4 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 2/3 teaspoon chicken-style seasoning 
 1 teaspoon  dillweed 
 Juice of 1  lemon 
 Directions : 
 Blend in a blender for 1 minute. If too wet, add 1 tablespoon more powdered  soymilk .
