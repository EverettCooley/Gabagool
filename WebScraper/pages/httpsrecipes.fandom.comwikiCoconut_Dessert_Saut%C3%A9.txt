 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 bannannas, peeled and sliced 
 2 mangoes, peeled and cubed (optional) 
 1  pineapple , peeled, cored, and cubed 
 2  oranges , peeled and cubed 
 3 tbsp  coconut oil 
 ¼ cup  white Grape  Juice 
 ½ tsp ground coriander 
 Directions : 
 In a wok or large skillet, sauté all ingredients until thick texture occurs. Serve over  vanilla   ice   cream . Garnish with fresh  raspberries  and  orange  zest.
