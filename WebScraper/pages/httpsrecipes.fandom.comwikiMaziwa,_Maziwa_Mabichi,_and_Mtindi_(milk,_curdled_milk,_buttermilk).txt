 Description : 
 milk  is an important part of the African diet in those parts of Africa where cattle or camels can be kept. (The tsetse fly and the diseases it spreads has made keeping livestock impossible in many parts of Africa.)
 
 Ingredients : 
 milk  or Soured  milk  or  butter   milk  or Curdled  milk 
 
 
 Directions : 
 In Eastern Africa,  milk  is consumed fresh, as soured  milk  or  buttermilk , or made into curdled  milk  (similar to what is called  cottage cheese  in the United States).  butter  is also made and is used to make  ghee  (a sort of  clarified butter ) which is used as an  oil  for frying.
