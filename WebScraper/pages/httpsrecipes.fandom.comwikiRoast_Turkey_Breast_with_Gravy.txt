Description : 
This turkey dish is enriched with honey, mustard, butter, and lemon juice, cooked to white-hot perfection, and served with gravy. This is one savory turkey dish the family will never forget.       Roast Turkey Breast with Gravy     
 Ingredients : 
 1 large  turkey  breast for roasting (about 2 lbs.) 
 3 tbsp.  honey 
 2 tbsp. melted  butter 
 1 tbsp.  Dijon mustard 
 1 tbsp.  lemon juice 
 salt  and  pepper  for seasoning 
 1 ½ cup  water , hot or lukewarm only 
 1  chicken bouillon  cube 
 1 tbsp.  soy sauce , dark or light only 
 1 tbsp.  orange juice 
 1 tbsp.  orange   marmalade 
 2 tsp.  cornstarch 
 1 tbsp.  red wine  or  sherry , optional 
 Directions : 
 For the roasting base : 
 Preheat the oven to 375° F (or 190° C). 
 In a small bowl, mix together the  honey ,  mustard ,  butter , and  lemon juice . 
 Brush all over the  turkey  breast. Season with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Place  turkey  in a baking dish, then roast in the oven at 375° F for about 40 - 50 minutes, until cooked. 
 Gravy : 
 In a small sauce pan, bring  water  to a boil. 
 Mix up the  chicken  cube until it dissolves. 
 Add the  orange   marmalade , juice, wine, and soy sauce. 
 Dissolve the  cornstarch  in a little cold  water , then mix up the gravy. 
 Let it simmer for 1-2 minutes until it thickens. Season with  salt  and  pepper . 
 To serve : 
 Arrange the roast  turkey  in the center of the serving plate. 
 Surround with grilled  tomatoes  and steamed  peas . 
 Serve with mashed  potatoes  and the gravy, as well as with rice, if desired.
