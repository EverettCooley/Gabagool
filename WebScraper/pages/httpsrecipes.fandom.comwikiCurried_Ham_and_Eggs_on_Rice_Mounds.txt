Description : 
 Makes 4 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x 10¾ ounce can condensed  cheese  soup 
 1 tablespoon  chili sauce 
 1 x 4 ounce can sliced  mushrooms , drained 
 1 cup diced cooked  ham 
 1 teaspoon  curry powder 
 ¼ teaspoon  garlic salt 
 4 hard-cooked  eggs , cut into quarters 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Combine  cheese  soup,  chili sauce ,  mushrooms ,  ham ,  curry  and  salt . 
 Heat thoroughly. 
 Fold in  eggs . 
 Serve over fluffy  rice .
