 Description : 
 Calm your stressed-out stomach with this warm and soothing herbal drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  lemon 
 1/4 cup  mint  leaves 
 4 cups boiling  water 
 Directions : 
 1) Carefully remove rind from  lemon  using a vegetable peeler, making sure not to get any of the white pithy part of the rind, and reserve  lemon  for another use. 
 2) Place rind,  mint  leaves, and boiling  water  in a large bowl; cover and steep mixture 5 minutes. Strain mixture through a fine sieve into a bowl, and discard rind and  mint . 
 3) Serve immediately.
