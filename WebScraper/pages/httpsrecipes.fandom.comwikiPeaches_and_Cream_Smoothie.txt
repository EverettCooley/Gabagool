Description : 
 A great fruit smoothie high in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2  bananas 
 1 cup of frozen  peaches 
 1 cup  apple juice 
 Directions : 
 Put all the ingredients in the blender in the order listed and mix on high until fully blended.
