Easy Meatball Parm Hero Sandwiches
 
These Sandwiches Are Good, i Had One Like These at a Sub Sandwich Place One Time. Meatballs in  spaghetti  Sauce With  Cheese  on Rolls. Yummy.
 
 2 Cups Traditional Italian Sauce 
 16 Frozen Cooked Meatballs 
 4 Long Hard Rolls, Split 
 1 Cup Shredded  mozzarella   Cheese 
 Grated  Parmesan cheese 
 
Heat Sauce And Meatballs in Saucepan to a Boil. Reduce Heat And Cook 20 minutes. or Until Meatballs Are Heated Through. Serve Meatballs And Sauce in Rolls, Sprinkle With Cheeses. Makes 4 Sandwiches.*
