 Description : 
 Nga Baung Doke  (Sesame  coconut  Steamed in Leaves) is a popular Burmese dish. You may simply use aluminum foil if  banana  or bamboo leaves are not available.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1Â kg fillets 
 1 cup desiccated  coconut 
 1/2 cup hot  water 
 2 cloves  garlic 
 1 tablespoon  ginger , finely grated 
 2 tablespoons toasted  sesame seeds 
 2 teaspoons  salt 
 1 teaspoon  ground turmeric 
 1 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 2 tablespoons  rice flour 
 3 tablespoons  coriander leaves , chopped 
 juice of half a  lemon 
 banana  or bamboo leaves and aluminum foil for wrapping 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Wash the fillets and dry. Cut into serving pieces. Set aside. Put desiccated  coconut , hot  water ,  garlic  and grated  ginger  in a blender. Blend until  coconut  is finely ground. Pour mixture in a bowl. Add in the other seasonings. Mix well. Put fillets on  banana  or bamboo leaves. Put 3-4 teaspoons of  coconut  mixture on top of fillet and wrap in foil. Steam for 15 minutes.
