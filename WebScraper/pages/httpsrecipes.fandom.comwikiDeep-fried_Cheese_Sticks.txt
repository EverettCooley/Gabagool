Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 oz  cheese , cut in 4 pieces and cut again, or string cheese 
 milk  (enough to wet the cheese pieces) 
 ½ cup  flour 
 beaten  egg 
 4 cups seasoned  crumbs 
 Directions : 
 Using four bowls, place each of the last four ingredients in its own bowl 
 Dip strips of cheese in each bowl in order given (milk, flour, egg, crumbs). 
 Freeze on cookie sheet 25 minutes and deep fry 35 – 40 seconds.
