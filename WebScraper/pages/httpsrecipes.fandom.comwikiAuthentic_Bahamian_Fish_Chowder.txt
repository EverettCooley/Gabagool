Ingredients : 
 2 lbs boneless  grouper 
 1 medium  onion , chopped 
 2 ribs  celery , chopped 
 1 medium  bell pepper , minced 
 2 medium chopped  potatoes 
 4 cups diced  tomatoes 
 5 cups  fish stock 
 ½ tsp  thyme 
 ½ tsp  allspice 
 2  scotch bonnet chiles 
 1  bay leaf 
 1 tbsp  lime juice 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 1 tbsp seasoning  salt 
 Directions : 
 Dice the fish into approximately ½ inch pieces and place in mixing bowl. 
 Marinate with  lime  and spices and place in fridge for 2 – 3 hours. 
 Bring  fish stock  to a boil. 
 Add all ingredients and simmer for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
