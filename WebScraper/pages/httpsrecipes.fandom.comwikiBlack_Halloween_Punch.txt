Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (13-oz) envelope unsweetened  grape soft drink mix 
 1 (13-oz) envelope unsweetened  orange soft drink mix 
 2 c white  sugar 
 3 qt cold  water 
 1 liter  ginger ale 
 Directions : 
 To make frozen hand, wash disposable glove, fill with water, seal with rubber band & freeze until hard. 
 Stir together grape soft drink mix, orange soft drink mix, sugar, & water until solids are dissolved. Combine with chilled ginger ale just before serving. 
 Dip frozen hand briefly in warm water, then peel off glove. Float prepared hand in punch bowl for ghastly effect.
