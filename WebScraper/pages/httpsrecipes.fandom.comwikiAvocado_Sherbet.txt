 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 Cup  milk 
 1 Cup  Sugar 
 6  California avocados , pureed 
 ¾ Cup  orange juice 
 ½ Cup  pineapple juice 
 1/3 Cups  lemon juice 
 2 Tbsp finely chopped  orange  zest 
 2 Tbsp finely chopped  lemon  zest 
 ¼ tsp pure  almond extract 
 1/8 tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Heat  milk  and  Sugar  until  Sugar  is dissolved; do not boil. 
 Cool to room temperature. 
 Beat together  milk  mixture and remaining ingredients. 
 Freeze in an  ice   cream  machine according to manufacturer¹s instructions.
