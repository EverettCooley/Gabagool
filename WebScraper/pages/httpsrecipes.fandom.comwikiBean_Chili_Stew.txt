Bean  chili  Stew
 
 1/2 Tbsp.  olive oil 
 1 Tbsp. Crushed  garlic 
 1 Cup Each of Diced  carrot ,  celery ,  Onion , And  okra 
 1 Cup  corn  Kernels 
 2 Cups  vegetable stock 
 1 16-Oz Can Stewed  tomatoes 
 1/2 Cup  tomato  Paste 
 3  bay leaves 
 1/2 Tsp Cayene  pepper 
 1/4 Tsp.  salt 
 1/2 Tsp.  anise seeds 
 1/2 Tsp. Ground  black pepper 
 3/4 Cup Each of The Following Cooked beans: Azuki beans,  black beans , Chickpeas , Fava beans,  Lentils ,  kidney beans , And  pinto beans . 
 
in a Large Pot, Heat  oil . Add  garlic  And Vegetables And Saute Until Tender. Add  vegetable stock , Stewed  tomatoes ,  tomato  Paste, Spices, And beans. Simmer 30 Minutes. Serve With  Cornbread . Serves 16.
