Description : 
 In Romanian: Rata cu varza acra sau dulce
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  duck 
 1 large can  sauerkraut 
 3 tablespoons  lard 
 1 tablespoon  tomato sauce 
 some  borş  (optional) 
 some  unripe tomatoes  (optional) 
 ½ teaspoon  paprika 
 a few  peppercorns 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Clean, wash and  salt  the duck. 
 Let sit for one hour. 
 In the meantime, fry the  sauerkraut  in  lard , adding  paprika ,  tomato sauce ,  pepper  and  salt  (if needed). 
 Arrange the  sauerkraut  in a uniform layer in a roasting pan and place the duck on top and optionally add some  borş  and unripe  tomatoes  to give the dish a little tart taste. 
 The duck was previously dried and then greased with  lard . 
 Set into the oven and baste occasionally with the liquid in the pan. 
 After the duck has been roasted on one side, turn onto the other side.
