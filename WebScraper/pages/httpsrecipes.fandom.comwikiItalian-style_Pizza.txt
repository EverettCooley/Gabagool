Description : 
 Contributed by Jenn B aka Mom2Sam and Tiny at  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Prep: 20 minutes | Bake: 15 minutes 
 Makes 4 to 6 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x 10-ounce package refrigerated pizza crust 
 1 pound bulk  Italian sausage 
 8 ounces shredded  mozzarella  cheese 
 1 x 8-ounce can  pizza sauce 
 ½ cup chopped, pitted  ripe olives 
 Directions : 
 Preheat the oven to 425°F. 
 Press dough into a greased 13x9x2-inch baking pan. 
 Build up edges of crust slightly. 
 In a skillet brown the ground meat. Drain off fat. 
 Place about ¾ of the cheese over the crust. 
 Pour the sauce over the cheese. (If desired, reserve some of the sauce to drizzle over finished pizza.) 
 Sprinkle with cooked meat, then sprinkle the remaining cheese over meat. 
 Bake in the 425°F oven for 15 to 20 minutes or until crust is done. 
 Sprinkle with .
