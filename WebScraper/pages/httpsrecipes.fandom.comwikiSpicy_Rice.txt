 Recipe reprinted with permission from HealthRecipes.com
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  rice 
 ⅔ cup  salsa  sauce 
 2 cups  water 
 1 chopped cooking  onion 
 ½ tsp  curry powder 
 ½ cup  mushrooms 
 ½ cup diced  bok choy  or  Chinese cabbage 
 Directions : 
 Cook  rice ,  onions , bok choy and  mushrooms  together. 
 When  rice  starts to dry out, add salsa. 
 Stir and serve.
