Ingredients : 
 1 pound  apricots , peeled and pitted 
 3 cups alcohol, cooking 
 2 cups  sugar 
 lemon  rinds, grated 
 Directions : 
 Boil  sugar  in enough  water  to make syrup. 
 Add  apricots  and simmer for several minutes. 
 Strain and allow to cool. 
 Add alcohol, 2 cups of  water  and the rinds. 
 Pour off into a bottle and store for at least 1 month. 
 Strain and rebottle for future use when ready.
