 Description : 
 In Romanian: Zacusca pentru iarna II
 
 Ingredients : 
 eggplants 
 oil 
 peppers 
 vinegar 
 tomatoes 
 salt 
 sugar 
 ground  pepper  and  juniper berries 
 Directions : 
 Slice the  eggplants  crosswise,  salt  and let drain for 1 hour. 
 Then fry in  oil  on both sides. 
 Wash the  peppers , quarter them, remove seeds and scald in  vinegar , letting them sit in the hot  vinegar  for 10 – 15 minutes. 
 Then remove and drain well. 
 In a glass jar, arrange one layer of fried  eggplants , one layer of  peppers . 
 Repeat this procedure until the jar is ¾ full. 
 Fill the balance with boiled and strained  tomatoes  to which you added  salt , a little  sugar , ground  pepper  and  juniper berries . 
 Leave 2 inches empty space at the top. 
 Tie with a double layer of cellophane and boil in the double boiler for 25 minutes. 
 Let cool in the  water  in which they boiled. 
 Keep in a cool and dry place.
