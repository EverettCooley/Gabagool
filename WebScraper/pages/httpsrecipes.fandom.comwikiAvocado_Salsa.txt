 Description : 
A nice, traditional alternative to the modern, homemade salsa, this fruit-based salsa is made and packed with real vegetables, spices, and a big hint of fruit juice and is great with other meals.       Avocado Salsa     
 Ingredients : 
 1 ripe  California avocado , diced 
 1  tomato , diced, red only 
 ½ cup  corn  kernels  or  (1 can cream-style corn) 
 juice from 1-2  limes 
 2 tbsp. chopped  cilantro 
 3 tbsp.  salt , rock or iodized only 
 ground  black pepper 
 ground red  cayenne pepper , optional 
 minced jalapeño  peppers , optional 
 Directions : 
 Combine  avocado ,  tomato ,  corn ,  lime juice , and  cilantro . Toss together gently. 
 Season to taste with the  salt  and  pepper . For a spicier salsa, season with  cayenne  or minced jalapeños. 
 Just scoop the avocado-based salsa in a large bowl then reserve this in the refrigerator. Pair this salsa with corn, potato or tortilla chips or other side dishes.
