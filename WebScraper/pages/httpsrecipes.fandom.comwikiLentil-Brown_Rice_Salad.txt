Description : 
 Makes 4 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups cooked  brown rice  (cooked in  chicken broth ), cooled 
 1 cup cooked  lentils , cooled 
 1 cup diced fresh  tomatoes 
 ⅓ cup sliced  green onions 
 1 tablespoon chopped fresh  parsley 
 2 tablespoons  red wine vinegar 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 2 teaspoons fresh  lime juice 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1 teaspoon Dijon-style  mustard 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 2 tablespoons  water 
 lettuce  or  spinach  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  Lentils ,  tomatoes ,  onions  and  parsley  in medium bowl. 
 Whisk  vinegar ,  oil , juice,  garlic ,  mustard ,  pepper  and  water  in small bowl; add to  rice  mixture and toss. 
 Serve on  lettuce  leaves.
