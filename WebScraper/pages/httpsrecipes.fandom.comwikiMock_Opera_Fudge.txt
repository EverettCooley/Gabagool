MOCK OPERA FUDGE
 2 sticks butter
1 (8 oz.) pkg. cream cheese
2 lb. 10x sugar
2 tsp. vanilla
Chocolate chips
 Mix all of the above ingredients except the chocolate chips. Roll into balls. Let stand in refrigerator until the outer parts become set. Melt chocolate
chips or other chocolate used for coating. Enjoy.
