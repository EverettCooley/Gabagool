 Ingredients : 
 32 oz beans 
 2/3 c.  catsup 
 1 tsp.  mustard 
 1 tbsp.  worcestershire sauce 
 1/2 c.  brown sugar 
 1 lg.  Onion , chopped 
 1  bell pepper , chopped 
 salt  &  pepper  to taste 
 butter 
 1Â lb. Polish  Sausage , chopped 
 6 slices  Bacon , cooked & crumbled 
 Directions : 
 Saute  Onion  and  bell pepper  in saucepan with  butter . 
 Remove from heat, 
 Drain beans. 
 Mix all ingredients together in baking dish. 
 Bake 1 hour at 350 degrees with cover on dish. 
 Remove cover and bake 30 minutes longer; stir occasionally.
