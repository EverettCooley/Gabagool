Ingredients : 
 1 lb  beans  450 g 
 ½ tsp  pepper  2 ml 
 ½ tsp  thyme  2 ml 
 1 tsp  salt  5 ml 
 2 plugs  garlic  (crushed) 
 1 medium  onion  (sliced) 
 1 dried  coconut  (grated) (to give 1 cup  milk ) 
 2 lbs  rice  900 g 
 1 small  pig's tail  or  salted beef  (optional, trim fat) or bacon 
 6 – 8 cups  water 
 Directions : 
 Soak  beans  for 4 – 6 hours. 
 Boil  beans  until tender with  garlic ,  onion , and pig's tail (or use pressure cooker). 
 Season  beans  with  black pepper ,  thyme , and  salt . 
 Add  coconut milk . 
 Stir and taste. 
 Let boil. 
 Clean  rice . 
 Add clean  rice  to seasoned  beans . 
 Stir, then cover. 
 Cook until  water  is absorb or  rice  is tender. 
 If necessary, add more  water  gradually until  rice  is tender.
