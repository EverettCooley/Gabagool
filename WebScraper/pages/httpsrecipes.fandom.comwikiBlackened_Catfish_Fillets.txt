Description : 
 A  Catfish  recipe.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  paprika 
 1 teaspoon  onion powder 
 1 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 1 teaspoon  cayenne pepper 
 ¾ teaspoon  white pepper 
 ¾ teaspoon  black pepper 
 ½ teaspoon dried  thyme 
 ½ teaspoon dried  oregano 
 dash of  salt 
 4 u.s. farm-raised  catfish  fillets 
 ½ cup  butter , melted 
 for garnish:  lemon  wedges 
 Directions : 
 Heat a large, cast-iron skillet or heavy aluminum skillet over high heat for 10 minutes. 
 Mix  paprika ,  onion powder ,  garlic powder ,  cayenne pepper ,  white pepper ,  black pepper ,  thyme ,  oregano  and  salt  in a small bowl. 
 Place melted  butter  in a shallow dish. 
 Dip fillets in melted  butter , then sprinkle with the seasoning mixture. 
 Place fillets in the hot skillet and cook for 2 to 3 minutes on each side, or until blackened and fish flakes easily when tested with a fork. 
 Serve with a squeeze of fresh  lemon juice .
