INGREDIENTS: : 
 1 bag of Blueberry Lavender Tea 
 1 c hot water 
 2 T heavy cream 
 3 T tapioca pearls 
 INSTRUCTIONS: : 
 Steep tea bag in hot water for 7 minutes. 
 Pour into a clear mug. 
 Add cream and stir. 
 Add pearls and let them settle to the bottom.
