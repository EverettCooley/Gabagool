       Vegetarian Turkish Pide     
 Vegetarian Turkish Pide  is a delicious variation on the traditional pizza.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup sanitarium  TVP 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 1  onion , diced 
 170 ml hot  water 
 1 Turkish  pide   or   focaccia 
 2 teaspoons ground  cumin 
 1 teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 2 tablespoons  currants 
 2 tablespoons  water , extra 
 2 cups shredded English  spinach 
 80 g  feta  cheese, crumbled 
 1 tablespoon  pine nuts 
 ⅓ cup  tomato paste 
 Directions : 
 Combine  TVP  and hot  water , cover and set aside. 
 Heat the  oil  in a non-stick fry pan. 
 Sauté the  onion  until golden. 
 Add the  cumin ,  cinnamon ,  currants , pine nuts,  TVP  and extra  water . 
 Cook for 2 minutes. 
 Spread pide with  tomato paste  and top with shredded  spinach ,  TVP  mixture and feta  cheese . 
 Bake for 15 minutes or until crisp and golden, slice.
