      Chorizo and Bean Dip     
 Ingredients : 
 ½ lb  chorizo 
 1 can  black beans 
 ½ cup  onion  chopped 
 8 oz can  tomato sauce 
 2 tbsp  taco sauce 
 ¾ cup  cheddar  cheese shredded 
 2¼ oz can  black olives  sliced 
 ¼ cup  scallions   o r  green onions  chopped 
 1 bag Mission™  tortilla chips 
 Directions : 
 Brown chorizo in a nonstick skillet. 
 Drain on paper towels. 
 Cook  beans  and  onions , covered, over medium heat for approximately 5 minutes. 
 Stir occasionally to separate vegetables. 
 Mash  beans  slightly. 
 Combine with chorizo,  tomato sauce , and taco sauce. 
 Combine  cheese , and  green onions . 
 Stir one half the  cheese  mixture into bean mixture. 
 Pour mixture into a 1-quart baking dish and bake in a 350°F oven for 25 minutes, or until bubbly around edge. 
 Top with remaining  cheese  mixture. 
 Use tortilla chips as dippers.
