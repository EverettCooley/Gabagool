 Description : 
 This natural, peanut-butter glaze is great with other desserts.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  margarine 
 ¼ cup  peanut butter , smooth or crunchy 
 1-3 tsp.  vanilla extract 
 ½ tsp.  salt 
 2 ½ cups  icing sugar 
 5-6 tbsp. fresh  milk 
 Directions : 
 Cream the margarine, peanut butter, vanilla and salt. 
 Add sugar alternately with milk and beat until creamy. 
 May be stored in the refrigerator or freezer; return to room temperature before use. 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Servings:  3 ⅞ cups or 4-7 tablespoons
