Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 2 cups grated fresh  carrots 
 ¼ cup grated  Onion 
 2 tablespoons  flour 
 1 x 11-ounce can condensed  Cheddar  cheese soup 
 1  egg , slightly beaten 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1 teaspoon  worcestershire sauce 
 dash hot pepper sauce 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  carrots ,  Onion  and  flour . Blend soup,  egg ,  salt ,  pepper ,  worcestershire sauce  and  pepper  sauce; add to  rice  mixture. 
 Pour into buttered 8-inch ring mold. Bake at 350 degrees 30 minutes. 
 Cool 10 minutes before turning out on serving plate. Serve with green vegetables, if desired.
