      Cabbage Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 1 firm head of  cabbage  (as white as possible) about 1½ pounds 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 medium  onion 
 1 large  apple , peeled and cored 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 3 – 4 tablespoons thin  carrot  shavings 
 sugar  to taste 
 parsley  sprigs and/or thin wedges of red-skinned  apples . 
 Directions : 
 Cut the  cabbage  in half and carve out the core and then make it julienne strips. 
 Sprinkle the  cabbage  with ½ teaspoon of  salt  and rub the strips * cut the  onion  and  apple  into julienne strips and combine with the  cabbage ,  mayonnaise , and  carrot  shavings. 
 Add  salt  and  sugar  to taste, then chill.
