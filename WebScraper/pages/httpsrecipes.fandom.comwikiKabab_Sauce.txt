 Description : 
 This can be used as a marinade with any meat or fish dish 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 medium  onions , peeled 
 2 cups [nonfat] plain  yoghurt 
 dash of  lemon juice   or   rice vinegar 
 sugar 
 pepper  to taste 
 2 tbsp ground  cumin  seeds 
 2 tbsp ground  coriander seeds 
 ½ - ¾ tsp  cayenne  pepper or  chili paste 
 2 tsp fresh  ground ginger  and  garlic  paste 
 chopped  cilantro  to taste 
 ½ tsp  turmeric 
 Directions : 
 Liquify in blender. 
 Simmer until thickened slightly and flavor is set.
