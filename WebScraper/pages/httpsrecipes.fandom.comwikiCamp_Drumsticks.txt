Camp Drumsticks
 
 1 Lb. Ground  Beef 
 1 T.  garlic powder 
 1/2 T.  black pepper 
 1/2 T.  salt 
 3 or 4 Slices  Bacon ,optional 
 Skewers
 bread 
 
Combine  Beef ,  garlic powder ,  pepper  And  salt ; Mix Well. Divide Mixture Into 3 or 4 Parts. Press 1 Part Around Each Skewer, Making Certain it is Tightly Packed Around The Skewer. Wrap With  Bacon  Pressing Into Meat, if Desired. Hold Meat Wrapped Skewers Over Hot Coals, Turning to Cool All Sides Evenly. Cook 10 to 20 Minutes, Depending on Size of Meat And Heat of Fire. to Serve, Hold Meat With a Thick Slice of  bread  And Twist Skewer Until Meat Comes Loose. Makes 3 or 4 Drumsticks.Source: Marlboro Country Cookbook
