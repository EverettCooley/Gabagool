 Ingredients : 
 1 cup raw  Cauliflower , very finely chopped 
 1 cup raw  Broccoli  heads, very finely chopped 
 2  celery  stalks, very finely chopped 
 2 tsp Spanish  Onion , finely chopped 
 1/8 tsp  garlic powder 
 1/8 tsp  Onion  powder 
 1/4 tsp vegit or salt-free seasoning (optional) 
 braggs all purpose seasoning, or  sea salt  to taste 
 1 tsp  fruit sugar 
 1 tbsp good health  Mayonnaise 
 Directions : 
 Mix the ingredients together, and enjoy.
