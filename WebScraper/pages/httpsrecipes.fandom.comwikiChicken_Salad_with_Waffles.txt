 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 Chicken  Salad: 
 3 Cups chopped cooked  Chicken 
 14 (6-ounce) can pitted whole  black olives , drained 
 2  celery  ribs, chopped 
 1 red  bell pepper , sliced 
 ½ Cup chopped purple  Onion 
 4 Cups mixed salad greens 
 avocado  Dressing 
 2 small  California avocados , peeled and chopped 
 ¼ Cup  sour cream 
 2 Tbsp chopped fresh  oregano 
 2 Tbsp  olive oil 
 2 Tbsp  white vinegar 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 avocado  Waffles 
 1 1/3 Cups biscuit mix 
 1 ½ Cups shredded  Cheddar   Cheese 
 ½ Cup  cornmeal 
 ½ tsp dried  Italian seasoning 
 1 Cup  buttermilk 
 1 large  egg 
 2 Tbsp  olive oil 
 2 large  California avocados , peeled and mashed 
 2  garlic  cloves, pressed 
 Directions : 
 Chicken  Salad:
 Toss together first 6 ingredients.
 Serve on salad greens with  avocado  Waffles.
 avocado  Dressing
 Process all ingredients in a blender until smooth, stopping to scrape down sides.
 avocado  Waffles
 Stir together first 4 ingredients in a large bowl. 
 Stir together  buttermilk ,  egg , and  oil  until blended.
 Stir in mashed  avocado  and  garlic 
 Add to dry ingredients, stirring just until moistened.
 Bake waffles in a preheated, oiled waffle iron just until crisp
