Ingredients : 
 1  eggplant 
 egg white 
 lemon pepper 
 oregano 
 red pepper 
 crushed corn flakes 
 PAM 
 Directions : 
 Slice the  eggplant , peel or not as pleases you. 
 Dip slices in egg white with  lemon pepper ,  oregano , red pepper to taste. 
 Coat with crushed  corn  flakes and place on baking sheet sprayed with PAM. 
 Bake in hot oven for about 20 minutes. 
 Hot these are yummy. 
 Cold they make a crispy satisfying toothsome treat.
