 Description : 
 In Romanian: Compot de caise
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs/1 kg firm  apricots , 
 10 oz/300 g  Sugar , 
 3 cups  water , 
 juice from 1/2  lemon 
 Directions : 
 Set the  water  with  Sugar  to boil. 
 Peel the  apricots  and let sit in cold  water . 
 When the  Sugar  has dissolved, add the  apricots  and  lemon juice . 
 Let simmer for a while, removing the foam as it forms. 
 Serve cold.
