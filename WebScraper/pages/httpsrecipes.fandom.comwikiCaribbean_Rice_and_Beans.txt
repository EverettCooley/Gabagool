Description : 
 Makes 6 to 8 servings. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup chopped  onion 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 4½ cups drained cooked  beans  (any color) 
 1 medium  tomato , chopped 
 ½ cup dry  white wine 
 ½ teaspoon  chili powder 
 ⅛ teaspoon ground  cloves 
 salt  and ground  black pepper  to taste 
 2 tablespoons chopped  cilantro  or  parsley 
 4 cups hot cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Sauté  onion  and  garlic  in  olive oil  and  butter  until  onion  is tender. 
 Stir in beans,  tomato , wine,  chili powder , cloves,  salt  and  pepper ; cover and simmer 20 minutes. 
 Stir in  cilantro . Serve over hot  rice .
