 Cook Time : About 15 minutes to prepare, 3 hours to freeze 
 Serves : 8 
 Ingredients 
 1 package instant pudding (chocolate, vanilla, butterscotch, or lemon) 
 2 cups of chilled soy protein formula 
 2 cups nondairy  whipped topping 
 Directions 
 Read the label of pudding mix to see that no milk or other milk product has been included. 
 Prepare pudding as directed, substituting soy protein formula for milk. 
 Gently fold in whipped topping. 
 Pour into freezer container, cover, and freeze until firm, about 3 hours. 
 Makes 1 quart. 
 Variations 
 Nut Delight : Fold in 1 cup of your favorite chopped nuts with the whipped topping (not permitted on soft diet).
