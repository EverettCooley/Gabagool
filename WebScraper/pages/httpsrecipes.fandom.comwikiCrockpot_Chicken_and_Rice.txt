Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves 6 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ lb  mushrooms , fresh, sliced 
 ½ cup  onions , chopped 
 2 lb  chicken , raw, skin removed 
 1 tsp  chicken bouillon 
 1 tsp  poultry seasoning 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 2 cups  water 
 ¾ cup  rice , uncooked 
 Directions : 
 Brown  mushrooms ,  onion , and  chicken  pieces on all sides over medium heat about 15 minutes. 
 Stir in seasonings and transfer to crockpot. 
 Can be refrigerated overnight. 
 Start crockpot on low. 
 When ingredients are heated,add  rice , stir, and cook until done.
