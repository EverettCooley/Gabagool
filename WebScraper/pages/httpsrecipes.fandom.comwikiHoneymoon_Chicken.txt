Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 boneless, skinless  chicken breasts 
 1 can  cream of celery  soup, diluted with 1 cup water 
 2 cups uncooked  Minute rice 
 1 envelope dry  onion soup mix 
 1 can  mushroom soup , diluted with 1 cup water 
 Directions : 
 Butter a large casserole that has a lid. 
 Pour in celery soup. 
 Place chicken on soup. 
 Pour rice over chicken. 
 Sprinkle on soup mix and top with mushroom soup. 
 Cover and bake at 350°F for 1½ – 2 hours.
