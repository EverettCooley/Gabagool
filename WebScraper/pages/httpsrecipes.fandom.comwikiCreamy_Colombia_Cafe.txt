 Description : 
       CREAMY COLOMBIA CAFE     
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 c. Colombian  coffee , chilled 
 1 c.  whipping cream 
 2 tbsp.  powdered sugar 
 coffee   ice cubes  (frozen  coffee  in an  ice  tray) 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Blend  coffee  cubes,  coffee ,  whipping cream  and  powdered sugar  in a blender until smooth and creamy. 
 Pour in a frosted mug and sprinkle with  cinnamon  and  Sugar .
