 Description : 
 Serves  4, approx £0.80 per portion 
 Preparation time:  5 minutes 
 Cooking time:  none 
 Ingredients : 
 8 thin slices of ginger cake 
 1 full quantity  stewed Bramley Apples 
 200 g tub extra thick  double cream 
 2  chocolate  flake bars, crumbled 
 Directions : 
 Spoon a little of the apple purée into the base of 4 glass bowl, top with a slice of ginger cake, then more apple, a spoon or two of cream and chocolate flakes. 
 Repeat the layers and serve.
