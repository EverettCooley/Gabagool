 Cucumber Salad
 
       Agurksalat     
 Ingredients : 
 1  European cucumber 
 ⅔ cup (1½ dl)  white vinegar 
 ⅔ cup (1½ dl)  water 
 3½ tbsp  sugar 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 dash of  white pepper 
 fresh  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Thinly slice a  European cucumber . 
 Mix dressing and pour over  cucumber  slices in serving bowl. 
 Make salad to let dressing penetrate (about one-half hour). 
 Sprinkle with fine chopped fresh  parsley .
