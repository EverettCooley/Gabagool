Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 8¼-ounce can  pineapple  chunks 
 2 cups diced cooked  ham 
 1½ cups grated  Swiss cheese 
 1  green pepper , chopped 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 1 teaspoon  prepared mustard 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Measure  rice  into large mixing bowl. 
 Drain  pineapple ; reserve juice. 
 Cut chunks in half. 
 Add  pineapple ,  ham ,  cheese ,  green pepper  and  onion  to  rice . 
 Blend  mayonnaise ,  mustard ,  salt ,  pepper  and  pineapple juice . 
 Stir into  rice  mixture. 
 Turn into buttered shallow 2-quart baking dish. 
 Sprinkle with  paprika , if desired. 
 Bake at 350 °F degrees 30 minutes.
