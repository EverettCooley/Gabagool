 About Framboise : 
 Wikipedia Article  About Framboise on Wikipedia 
 Framboise (from the French word for 'raspberry') is a Belgian fruit beer fermented using raspberries which are native to Belgium. It is also known by the Flemish name Frambozen.
 Framboise is typically made from a lambic or Flemish brown ale base.
 Framboise is similar to Kriek, which uses cherries instead of raspberries.
