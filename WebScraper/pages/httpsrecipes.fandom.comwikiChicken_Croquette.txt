Ingredients : 
 1 cup cooked  chicken breast 
 1 tsp  thyme 
 1 tablespoon fresh  parsley 
 2  eggs 
 2 cups  bread crumbs 
 1 cup  white sauce 
 Â¼ cup cooking  oil 
 salt , black, and  hot pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Combine  chicken ,  thyme ,  parsley ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Add wine sauce but keep mixture firm. 
 Set aside in refrigerator for 1 hr. 
 Form mixture into patties and coat with  flour . 
 Dip in  eggs  then bread crumbs. 
 Fry in  oil  pan until both sides are golden brown.
