 Ingredients : 
 2 	cup 	Bisquick baking mix 
 1/2 	cup 	Shredded  Cheddar   Cheese 
 2 	tbsp 	Melted  butter /  margarine 
 1/2 	cup 	 beer 
 Sesame/ poppy seeds 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 450 degrees. 
Mix baking mix,  Cheese  and  beer  until soft dough forms; beat vigorously; 20 strokes. 
Gently smooth dough into ball on floured cloth board. 
Knead 5 times. 
Roll dough into rectangle, 16 x 10Â inches. 
Cut into 2-inch squares, cut squares diagonally into halves. 
Spread with melted  margarine / butter ; sprinkle with  sesame seeds . 
Separate and place on ungreased cookie sheets. 
Bake until brown, about 8 minutes.
