Description : 
 Special  garlic  Dressing from the Smoke House Restaurant
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: my old recipes 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 1 tbsp  dry mustard 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 1 tbsp  msg  (optional) 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 tbsp  red wine vinegar 
 1 tbsp coarsely ground  black pepper 
 1 tbsp  garlic powder 
 1  egg yolk 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Mix  mayonnaise   mustard ,  sugar ,  msg ,  lemon juice ,  vinegar ,  pepper , garlic powder  and  egg  yolk and season to taste with  salt . 
 Whip well. 
 Chill dressing thoroughly. 
 Serve over green salad.
