Ingredients : 
 4  apples 
 4  mandarins 
 4  peaches 
 4  bananas 
 4  oranges 
 1  papaya 
 1 jar of  dulce de leche 
 1 bag of gummie bears (or other gummie candy) 
 Directions : 
 Peel and cut all the fruit in small squares 
 Add all the fruit to a large bowl 
 Add in the dulce de leche and small candies. 
 Chill in the fridge
