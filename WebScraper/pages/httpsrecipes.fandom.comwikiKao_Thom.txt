Description : 
 Sweet sticky rice
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ kg  sticky rice 
 1½ cups  coconut cream 
 1½ cups  sugar 
 10  bananas 
 1 cup  black beans 
 banana leaves 
 Directions : 
 Soak  rice  overnight, drain. 
 Mix with  coconut cream ,  salt , and  sugar  and stir until well mixed. 
 Peel  bananas . 
 Put  banana leaves , put mixture of  rice  on top of leaves. 
 Put  banana  of top of  rice  and roll and fold. 
 Repeat rolling until mixture is finished. 
 Cook the rolled bunch in a steamer for 45 minutes.
