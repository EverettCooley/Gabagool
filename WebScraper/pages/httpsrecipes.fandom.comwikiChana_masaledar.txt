 Directions : 
 1 	Heat  oil  in heavy pot.
 2 	Add  cumin  seeds.
 3 	When they begin to darken, add the  Onion .
 4 	Stir & fry for 7 minutes.
 5 	Turn heat to low & add  cinnamon ,  nutmeg , cloves & coriander.
 6 	Mix well & add  garlic  &  ginger .
 7 	Stir & fry for 2 minutes.
 8 	Add paste.
 9 	Add chick  Peas ,  salt ,  Cayenne  &  amchoor .
 10 	Mix well, cover & cook for 10 minutes.
 11 	You may have to add an extra 2 tablespoons or so of stock
