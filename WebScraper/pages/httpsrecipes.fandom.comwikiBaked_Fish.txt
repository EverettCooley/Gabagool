 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound fish fillets (sole,  Flounder  or red  Snapper ) 
 1 Tb chopped  parsley 
 Juice of one fresh  lemon  (1 Tb) 
 a Ts. Seasoned  salt  (or spice such as Old Bay Seasoning) 
 3 Tb  olive oil 
 1 Medium  Onion , thinly sliced 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1 Large  tomato , thinly sliced 
 3 Slices  lemon 
 2 Tb  white wine 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Arrange the fish in an 8- or 9- inch-square baking dish. 
 Sprinkle with the  parsley ,  lemon juice  and seasoned  salt . 
 Heat the  oil  in a skillet and fry the  Onion  and  garlic  until limp. 
 Top the fish with the  Onion  mixture, including the  oil  from the skillet. 
 Arrange the  tomatoes  on top of the  Onion  mixture, than place the  lemon  slices between the  tomato  slices. 
 Pour the wine over all and bake at 350F for 30â€“35 minutes or until the fish flakes with a fork.
