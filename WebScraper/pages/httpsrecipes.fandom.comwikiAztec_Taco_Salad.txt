Aztec Taco Salad
 
 1/2 Lb. Ground  Beef 
 1 Can  kidney beans , Drained (15 Oz) 
 1 Lb.  Velveeta  Mexican  Cheese  Spread, Cubed 
 1 1/2 Quarts Shredded  lettuce 
 1/2 Cup French Dressing (catalina Brand) 
 1/2 Cup Coarsely Crushed  corn  Chips 
 1 Cup Chopped  tomato 
 1/2 Cup Chopped  green pepper 
 
Brown Meat. Drain. Add beans And  Velveeta   Cheese  Spread, Cubed. Stir Over Low Heat Until  Cheese  Spread Melts. Mix  lettuce  And Dressing. Toss Lightly. on Serving Platter, Layer  lettuce , Meat Mixture.  corn  Chips,  tomatoes  And  green pepper . Serve at Once. Serves 4Source: my Old Recipes
