Ingredients : 
 2 cups frozen strawberries 
 1/2 cup frozen cauliflower 
 1 1/3 cups milk of choice 
 1/2 cup plain or vanilla yogurt 
 2 tablespoons honey 
 Instructions : 
 Place all the ingredients in a high powered blender and blend until smooth.
