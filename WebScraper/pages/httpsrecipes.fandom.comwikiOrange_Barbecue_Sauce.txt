Description : 
 Yield: about 1½ cups.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup packed  brown sugar 
 ½ tsp dry  mustard 
 ⅛ tsp ground  cloves 
 ½ tsp  worcestershire sauce 
 ⅓ cup chopped  onion 
 1½ tsp grated  orange peel 
 ⅓ cup  orange juice 
 ¾ cup  ketchup 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients in a 1-quart glass measuring cup. 
 Cook in microwave oven 4 minutes at high. Stir twice.
