 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  rice , 
 4 Tbsp.oil, 
 2 cups cooked  Beef  or  Chicken , 
 1  Onion , 
 2 Tbsp.  soy sauce , 
 1/2 tsp. fresh grated  ginger , 
 8 stalks  celery  sticks, 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Put  rice  into a pot with  water  to cover  rice . Boil until soft (not too soft). Set to cool for 3 minutes. Heat  oil  in a wok or pan. 
 Add chopped  onions , diced meat,  ginger  and chopped  celery . Stir and let it fry for 3 minutes. Then add  rice  and  soy sauce . 
 Fry over medium heat, stirring frequently. Add  salt  to taste and fry for 5 minutes.
