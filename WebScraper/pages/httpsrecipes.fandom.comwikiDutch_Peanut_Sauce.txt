Description : 
 A nice sauce made out of  peanut butter . Originally used on dishes like sateh, but nowadays also used on chips and anything else the Dutch can come up with.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp  oil 
 2 â€“ 3 tsp  cumin 
 1  onion 
 2  garlic  cloves (minced) 
 300 g  peanut butter 
 300 g  ketchup 
 milk   or   water 
 1 tsp  ginger  powder 
 sambal oelek  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Heat the  oil  and add the  cumin . Cook for about 2 minutes. 
 Add  onion ,  garlic  and a teaspoon of  sambal . Stir well. 
 Cook for 5 minutes. Add  peanut butter . Cook for another few minutes, continue stirring. 
 Then add the  ketchup . Add  milk  (or  water ) to desired consistency, stir well. 
 Blend in the  ginger  powder, again stirring well.
