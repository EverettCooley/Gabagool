Description : 
 Makes 1⅓ cups of dressing.
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 oz  tomato sauce , 1 can 
 2 tbsp  catsup 
 4 tsp  lemon juice 
 1 tsp  onion , grated 
 ¼ tsp Tabasco  hot pepper sauce 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ¼ cup  blue cheese , crumbled 
 Directions : 
 Combine all of the ingredients except the  blue cheese  together in a blender container, cover, and blend until smooth. 
 Add the  blue cheese  and cover. 
 Chill until serving time. 
 Serve over crisp salad greens. 
 Nutritional information : 
 1 tbsp = 25 calories.
