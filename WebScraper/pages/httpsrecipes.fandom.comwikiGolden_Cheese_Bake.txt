Golden  Cheese  Bake
 
Simmer 3 Cups Shredded  carrots ,
 
 2/3 Cup Long  grain   rice , And, 
 1/2 Teaspoon  salt  in 1 1/2 Cups  water , Covered, For 25 Minutes. do Not Drain. Stir in 1 1/2 Cups Shredded Process  American cheese , 1 Cup  milk , 2 Beaten  eggs , 2 TablespoonsInstant Minced  Onion , And 1/4 Teaspoon  pepper . Bake in 1 1/2 Quart Casserole, Uncovered, at 350 Degrees About 1 Hour. Top With 1/2 Cup Shredded Process  American cheese .Heat Until  Cheese  Melts. Serves 6. 
 
Enjoy.
