 Ingredients : 
 1 large  chocolate  bar 
 3 to 4  cassava  tubers 
 200 g  sugar 
 1 glass  milk 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 2 teaspoons  vanilla extract 
 3  eggs 
 100 g  raisins 
 1 tablespoon  cinnamon 
 150 g  butter 
 Directions : 
 Grate  coconut  meat and  cassava  tubers, then mix together. 
 Beat  eggs ,  sugar , and  vanilla extract  until thick. 
 Melt  butter  and mix with  coconut ,  cassava ,  eggs ,  milk ,  raisins ,  cinnamon  and  salt . 
 Butter inside of baking pan with  butter  and pour mixture in this. 
 Put some  butter  on top. 
 Bake for about 1 hour in pre-heated oven until done (thermostat 3 or 4, 125-175 °C).
