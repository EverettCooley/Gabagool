 Prep Time: 5 minutes 
 Cook time: Depends on amount usually 10 to 15 mins 
 Serves: As many as needed 
 
 Description : 
 Normal pancakes with vanilla and sugar
 
 Ingredients : 
 pancake mix 
 1 teaspoon of  vanilla  for every 10 people 
 3 shakes of  cinnamon  for every ten people 
 chocolate chips  if you wish 
 Directions : 
 You have to either buy pancake mix or make them from scratch 
 Add to your mix as much vanilla as needed 
 Then add as much cinnamon as needed 
 Put the pancakes on pan or griddle and cook into the pancakes start to bubble If using chocolate chips put them in 
 Flip then put on plate
