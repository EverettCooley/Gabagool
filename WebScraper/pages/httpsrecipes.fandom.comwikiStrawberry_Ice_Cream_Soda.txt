Ingredients : 
 ⅓ cup blackberries and/or strawberries 
 3 tbs  milk 
 Strawberry ice cream 
 club soda 
 Directions : 
 Put ⅓ cup crushed sweetened strawberries, 3 tbs milk and a scoop of strawberry ice cream in a tall glass. 
 Add  club soda , stir to mix.
