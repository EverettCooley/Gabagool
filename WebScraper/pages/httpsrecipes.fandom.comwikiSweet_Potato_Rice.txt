 Description : 
 Vegetarian recipe for dieters
 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅔ cup raw  rice 
 2 cups  water 
 2 tbsp  parsley , chopped 
 ¼ tsp  sage 
 ½ cup  sweet potato , cooked and mashed 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 ¼ cup  wheat germ 
 1 tbsp  yeast , brewers (nutritional) 
 4 oz  yogurt 
 Directions : 
 Cook  rice  over low heat for 40 minutes in 2 cups  water . 
 Stir in and heat throughly the  parsley ,  sage ,  sweet potato ,  pepper ,  wheat germ , and brewer's  yeast . 
 If desired, serve warm or cold with  yogurt  (add 50 calories and ½  milk  exchange) 
 Nutritional information : 
 1 serving = 272cal; 3 meat, 2 breads, ½ fat
