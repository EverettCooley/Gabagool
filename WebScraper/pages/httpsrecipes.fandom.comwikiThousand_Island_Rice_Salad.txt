Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3½ cups cooked  rice 
 ½ cup chopped  celery 
 ¼ cup chopped  green pepper 
 2 tablespoons finely chopped  onion 
 ¼ cup bottled Thousand Island dressing 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅛ teaspoon ground  white pepper 
 Directions : 
 Combine rice, celery, green pepper, onion, dressing, salt and pepper in a mixing bowl. 
 Notes : 
 Goes well with with cooked crawfish tails or small cooked shrimp 
 Top with grated parmesan or cheddar cheese if desired
