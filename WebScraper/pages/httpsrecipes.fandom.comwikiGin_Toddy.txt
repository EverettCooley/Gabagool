      Description     
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz  gin 
 2 tsp  water 
 1/2 tsp  powdered sugar 
 1 twist  lemon peel 
 Directions : 
 Mix  powdered sugar  and  water  in an old-fashioned glass. Add  gin  and one  ice  cube. Stir, add the twist of  lemon peel , and serve.
