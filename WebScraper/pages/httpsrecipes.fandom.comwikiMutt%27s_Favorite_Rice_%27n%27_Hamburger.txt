 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  rice 
 ½ pound  hamburger 
 1 tsp  vegetable oil 
 1 clove  garlic 
 ½ cup  carrots   or   broccoli   or   spinach 
 4 cups  water 
 Directions : 
 Put all ingredients into a large pot, boil until done, then cool off and serve. 
 I feed my dogs this kind of meal or a variation every day, instead of store-bought food. 
 They've got shiny coats, are full of energy and love eating dinner again!.
