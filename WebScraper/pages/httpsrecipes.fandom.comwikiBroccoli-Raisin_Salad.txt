Description 
 Raisins, Seedless  by the US Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Ingredients 
 1 large head  broccoli  washed and trimmed 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 ½ cup  raisins 
 ½ cup chopped  pecans  or  walnuts  (optional) 
 ½ pound  bacon , fried crisp and crumbled (optional) 
 ¾ cup low fat  mayonnaise 
 2 tablespoons  vinegar 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 Directions 
 Cut broccoli into small florets and ½” stem pieces. 
 Combine broccoli, onions, raisin, nuts (optional), and bacon (optional). 
 In a small separate bowl, make dressing of mayonnaise, vinegar, and sugar. 
 Pour dressing over broccoli. 
 Refrigerate, covered, overnight to soften broccoli. 
 Toss before serving.
