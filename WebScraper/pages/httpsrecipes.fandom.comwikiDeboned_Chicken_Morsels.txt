 Ingredients : 
 3 lbs boneless  Chicken  breast 
 1 lb  Chinese noodles , linguini or  vermicelli . 
 2 stalks  green onion 
 1 tsp  ground ginger  (fresh is best) 
 2 tsp  black pepper 
 salt  and  rice wine vinegar  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Boil  Pasta  until al dente. Cut up  Chicken  into morsels and sauté. Add  green onion ,  ginger ,  pepper . Adjust flavor with  salt  and  vinegar .
