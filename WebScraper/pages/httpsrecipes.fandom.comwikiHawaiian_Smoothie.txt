Description : 
 Source:  Recipes for Fat Free Living Cookbook  by Jyl Steinback
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  papaya 
 1  banana 
 ½ cup plain fat-free  yogurt 
 ½ cup  apple juice 
 crushed  ice 
 Directions : 
 Place ingredients in a blender. 
 Blend until smooth.
