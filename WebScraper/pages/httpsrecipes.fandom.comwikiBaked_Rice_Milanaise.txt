Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 ½ cup chopped  green pepper 
 2 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 3 hard-cooked  eggs , chopped 
 1½ cups (6 ounces) grated  Cheddar  cheese, divided 
 salt  and ground  black pepper  to taste 
 ½ teaspoon  basil  leaves, crushed 
 1 x 8-ounce can  tomato sauce 
 Directions : 
 Cook  onion  and  green pepper  in  oil  until tender but not brown. 
 Combine with  rice ,  eggs , 1 cup  cheese ,  salt ,  pepper  and  tomato sauce . 
 Turn into buttered shallow 1½-quart casserole. Top with remaining ½ cup  Cheese . 
 Bake at 350 °F for 20 to 25 minutes
