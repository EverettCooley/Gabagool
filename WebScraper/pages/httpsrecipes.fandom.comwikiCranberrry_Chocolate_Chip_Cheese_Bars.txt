 Description : 
 purchased from the Colvin Estate in Little Rock, Arkansas in 1989. Notation on card indicates this is a favorite bake sale “goodie”. Recipe from Old Farmers Almanac. Exact date unknown. These are incredible. I have made them at least once a month for the past year and I never make enough (I triple the recipe). They are gobbled up in no time. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  unsalted butter  at room temperature 
 1 cup packed  light brown sugar 
 2 cups  cake flour 
 1-1/2 cups  rolled oats 
 2 teaspoons grated  orange  zest 
 12 ounces  semisweet chocolate  chips 
 1 cup dried  cranberries 
 8 ounces  cream cheese  at room temperature 
 1 can  sweetened condensed milk 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Beat  butter  and  brown sugar  in large bowl until creamy. 
 Beat in  flour ,  Oats  and  orange peel  until crumbly. 
 Stir in  chocolate chips  and  cranberries  (reserving 2 cups). 
 Press remaining mixture onto bottom of greased baking pan. 
 Bake at 350 for 15 minutes. 
 Beat  cream cheese  in small bowl until smooth. 
 Gradually beat in  sweetened condensed milk . 
 Pour over hot crust. 
 Sprinkle with reserved oat mixture. 
 Return to oven and bake 25 minutes. 
 Cool in pan on wire rack.
