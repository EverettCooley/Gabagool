Marinated  Shrimp  Vinaigrette For Antipasto
 
 1 Pound Medium  Shrimp , Cooked 
 6 T.  olive oil 
 2 T.  red wine vinegar 
 1 T. Minced  Shallots 
 3 cloves  garlic , Cut in Half 
 1 T. Minced  parsley 
 1 T. Minced  chives 
 1/2  bay leaf 
 
Combine All The Ingredients in a Bowl And Toss to Coat Evenly. Cover And Store in The Refrigerator For Several Hours.Source: With Love From Darling's Kitchen
