 In Romanian: Chiftelute de varza.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 small  cabbage 
 1  egg 
 2 â€“ 3 tablespoons  flour 
 salt 
 pepper 
 1 teaspoon mixed chopped  parsley  and  dill 
 3 tablespoons  lard  or  oil 
 Directions : 
 Julienne the  cabbage , rub some  salt  into it and scald with  water . 
 Drain well and grind. 
 Then mix with  egg ,  flour ,  parsley  and  dill ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Make pancakes from the mixture. 
 Dredge with  flour  or bread crumbs and fry in hot  lard .
