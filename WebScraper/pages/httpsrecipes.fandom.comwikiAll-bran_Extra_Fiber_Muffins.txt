 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup  Sugar 
 1 tbsp  baking powder 
 2 cup kellogg's all- bran  cereal 
 1 1/4 cup skim  milk 
 1/4 cup skim  milk 
 1  egg  beaters 99% real  egg  pro 
 1 1/4 cup  flour 
 Directions : 
 Stir together  flour ,  Sugar  &  baking powder  . 
 In a large mixing bowl combine cereal &  milk . Let stand 5min or until cereal softens. 
 Add  egg  beater and  applesauce . Beat well. 
 Add  flour  mixture stirring only until combined. 
 Pour into 12 muffin cups. 
 Bake at 400 °F for 20 minutes until lightly brown. 
 Serve warm. 
 Nutrition (per serving): 146 calories total, fat 1 g (4% of calories)
