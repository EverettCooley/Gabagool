Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. It was obtained when the family collection was purchased from the Burnett Estate in Terrell, Texas in 1981.
 
 Ingredients : 
 12 Green or  red peppers 
 3 Cups  vinegar 
 1 Cup  salad oil 
 1 Tablespoon  salt 
 1 Cup pickling  onions , peeled 
 Directions : 
 Wash  peppers  then remove seeds and centers and cut into serving pieces. 
 Bring  vinegar ,  oil  and  salt  to a boil. 
 Add a few  peppers  and onions at a time turning in boiling liquid until  peppers  change color. 
 Cool and store in refrigerator until ready to use.
