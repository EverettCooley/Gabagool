 Source: The Best of Bridge 
 Serves 20 , but may be cut in half for smaller gatherings. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb lean  ground beef 
 1 medium to large  onion , chopped 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 can  refried beans  (14 oz) 
 1 can (4 oz)  green chilies , drained and chopped 
 ½ lb shredded Monterey  jack cheese  or mild  cheddar  cheese 
 ¾ cup  taco sauce  (try hot) 
 1 medium  avocado , mashed or 6 oz pkg. frozen  avocado  dip 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 ¼ cup  green onion , chopped 
 1 cup pitted  ripe olives , sliced 
 Tostados round  tortilla chips 
 Directions : 
 Brown beef and  onion , crumbling  beef  into fine pieces and drain. 
 Season with  salt . 
 Spread  refried beans  in a large shallow ovenproof dish. 
 Top with meat mixture. 
 Sprinkle green chilies over beans, then  cheese . 
 Drizzle taco sauce over all. 
 If made ahead, cover and chill at this point. 
 Bake at 400°F for 20 minutes. 
 Spread mashed  avocado  over mixture, then  sour cream , garnish with  green onions  and. 
 Serve with tortilla chips. 
 To reheat any leftovers, grate enough  cheese  to make a new top layer and bake until heated through.
