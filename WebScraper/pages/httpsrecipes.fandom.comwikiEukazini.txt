The  Eukazini  is a cocktail invented on the December 17, 2006. It was invented by bartender Kaz Santiago and customer Euan Morse in Edinburgh's Ryan's Bar.

 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 1 oz or jigger of  Vodka 
 1 oz or jigger of  Cointreau 
 1 oz or jigger of  Midori 
 1 oz or jigger of  Pimms 
 Garnish 
 *A small triangle of orange to complete the drink. 
 Directions 
 Fill a shaker with ice, then add all ingredients. 
 Shake ingredients till shaker is frosted, (shake very vigorously). 
 Then strain into a chilled martini glass rimmed with granulated sugar. 
 Garnish with orange and serve.
