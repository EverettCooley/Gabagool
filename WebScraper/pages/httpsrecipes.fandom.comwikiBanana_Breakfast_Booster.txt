Info 
 Cook Time : 
 Serves : 
 
 Ingredients 
 3 ripe  bananas 
 3 cups cold  milk 
 3 tablespoons  honey 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 Directions 
 1.Peel and carefully slice bananas. Put in blender container. 
 
2.Pour milk over banana slices. Put honey and vanilla in a blender. Put top on blender. Hold top with hand. Press BLEND button and let mix just until smooth and frothy. 
 
3.Carefully pour into glasses. Serve immediately.
