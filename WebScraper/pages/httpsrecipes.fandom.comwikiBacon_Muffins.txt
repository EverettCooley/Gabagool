Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Henson Estate in Dallas, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  flour 
 ½ cup fried  bacon  crisp and chopped 
 3 tablespoons  sugar 
 2½ teaspoons  baking powder 
 1  egg  well beaten 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 cup  milk 
 3 tablespoons  bacon grease 
 Directions : 
 Sift together in a wooden mixing bowl the  flour ,  sugar , baking powder and  salt  then stir in  bacon . 
 In a separate wooden bowl combine beaten  egg ,  milk  and cooled melted  bacon grease . 
 Make a well in dry ingredients and pour in liquid mixture. 
 Stir just enough to lightly blend. 
 The mixture should have a rather rough appearance. 
 Grease muffin pan and fill ⅔ full. 
 Bake at 400°F for 20 minutes.
