 Ingredients : 
 4 ripe  plantains 
 2 cups of  milk 
 1 cup of  sugar 
 ½ cup  butter 
 ¼ cup of  vermouth 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 ¼ tsp ground  cinnamon 
 oil 
 Directions : 
 Mash the  plantains  in a bowl. 
 Add the  milk ,  Sugar ,  butter ,  vermouth  and  vanilla . 
 Mix well. 
 Grease a frying pan with  oil  and warm it up. 
 Pour the  plantain  mixture into the pan. 
 Cook on very low heat until fully cooked. 
 Sprinkle with ground  cinnamon .
