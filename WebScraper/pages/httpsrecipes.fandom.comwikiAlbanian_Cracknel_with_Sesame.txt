 Ingredients : 
 ¼ kg (about 9 ounces) of  pork  fat or  butter 
 ¼ kg of  powdered sugar 
 4  eggs 
 1 teaspoon of  baking soda 
 juice from ½ of a  lemon 
 2 packs of  vanilla 
 150 g (about 5 ounces) of  sesame 
 Directions : 
 Melt the  butter  or  pork  fat, combine with the  sugar , add the previously beaten  eggs , the  baking soda  and the  lemon juice . 
 Mix everything well then add  flour  to obtain a dough with average solidity. 
 Spread the dough to a piece of a height of 1 cm (less than half an inch). 
 Use a cup to cut circle shaped pieces of cracknel. 
 Dip the pieces of cracknel in  milk , spread sesame on them and place them on a baking pan. 
 Cook in a hot oven. 
 Cracknels with sesame do not lose their freshness for a long time.
