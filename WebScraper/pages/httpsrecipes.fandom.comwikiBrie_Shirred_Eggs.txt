Brie  Shirred  eggs , Makes 4 Servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 Non-Stick  cooking spray 
 1/4 Lb.  Brie   Cheese , Diced 
 1/4 Lb. Cooked  Ham , Diced 
 2  green onions , Minced 
 4 Large  eggs 
 1/4 C.  heavy cream 
 pepper 
 instructions : 
 Preheat Oven to 400 Degrees.
 
With  cooking spray , Grease Four 6-Ounce Custard Cups; Place  Cheese ,  Ham  And  onions  in Cups, Dividing Evenly.
 
Break One  egg  Into Each Cup; Drizzle 1 Tablespoon  heavy cream  Into Each. Sprinkle With  pepper .
 
Place Custard Cups in Large Roasting Pan; Place Pan on Oven Rack. Fill Pan With Enough Hot  water  to Come up 1 Inch on Sides of Custard Cups. Bake 18 Minutes, or Until  eggs  Are Set to The Desired Degree of Doneness.
 
Serve  eggs  From Cups.
