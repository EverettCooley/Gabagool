 Description : 
 Easy and scrumptious. Even people who say they don't like avocados like this.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 ripe  avocado 
 2 Cups clear  chicken broth  (I can Campbell's + 1 can  water 
 ½ tsp  curry 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 pepper  to taste 
 1 Cup  cream  or heavy or 1/2 &1/2 
 good squirt of  lemon juice 
 dash of  Onion  powder 
 Directions : 
 Blend all in blender. If using  heavy cream , may have to adjust seasonings, including more  avocado .
 Chill, serve cold.
