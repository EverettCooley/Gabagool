Fried Bologna Roll
 Makes 6 Servings
 
 6 Hard Rolls, Split 
 1/2 C. Prepared Sandwich Spread With Pickles 
 6 Oz. Bologna, Thinly Sliced 
 2 Tbsp.  butter  or  margarine 
 8 Large  eggs 
 1/2 T.  salt 
 1/4 T.  pepper 
 2 Tbsp.  water 
 4 Oz.  Swiss cheese , Thinly Sliced 
 6 Leaves  lettuce 
 
 Preheat Oven to 300 Degrees.
 
Spread Cut Sides of Each Roll With Sandwich Spread, Dividing Evenly. Place on Baking Sheet, Cut Side Up. Heat in Oven Until Warm.
 
Quarter Bologna Slices. in Medium Skillet Over Medium Heat, Fry Bologna Until Lightly Browned And Curled. Place on Bottom Havles of Rolls, Dividing Evenly. Keep Bologna Rolls Warm in Oven.
 
in Same Skillet, Melt  butter . in Bowl, With Fork, Beat  eggs  With  salt ,  pepper  And  water  Until Blended. Place  egg  Mixture in Skillet; Cook, Stirring Occasionally, Until  eggs  Are to The Desired Degree of Doneness.
 
Place  eggs  on Bologna on Rolls, Dividing Evenly; Top With  Cheese ,  lettuce  And Top Halves of Rolls. Serve.
