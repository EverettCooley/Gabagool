chocolate  French Toast
 
Ingredients:
 
 3  eggs 
 3/4 Cup  milk 
 3 Tablespoons  sugar 
 2 Tablespoons Hershey's  cocoa 
 1/4 Teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 1/8 Teaspoon  salt 
 1/4 Teaspoon  cinnamon (optional) 
 8 to 10 Pieces Thickly Sliced  bread 
 powdered sugar (optional)
 pancake syrup (optional)
 
Directions:
 
 1. Beat  eggs ,  milk ,  sugar , Hershey's  cocoa ,  vanilla ,  salt  And  cinnamon ,If Desired, in Large Bowl Until Smooth. 
 2. Heat Griddle or Skillet Over Medium-Low Heat. Grease Griddle WithMargarine, if Necessary. 
 3. Dip  bread  in  egg  Mixture. Place on Griddle. Cook About 2 to 4 MinutesOn Each Side. Serve Immediately With  powdered sugar  or  pancake syrup , ifDesired.
