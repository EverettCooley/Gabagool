Ingredients : 
 1/4 cup.  milk 
 1/2 lb.  marshmallows 
 2 cups.  graham crackers , crushed 
 1/2 cup.  butter , melted 
 4 to 5  peaches 
 1/2 lg. container  Cool Whip 
 Directions : 
 Melt the milk and marshmallows together and set to cool. 
 Mix together the graham crackers and the butter. 
 Press into a 9-inch pan. 
 Bake at 350 degrees for 6 to 8 minutes. 
 Cool. 
 Dice 4 or 5 large peaches and add to 1/2 container Cool Whip. 
 Mix the Cool Whip and the peaches with the marshmallow mixture. 
 Pour into crust and refrigerate. 
 This may be frozen.
