Description : 
 It is a drink dessert, served cool.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅓ cup  vodka 
 ½ cup  orange juice 
 honey  to taste 
 1 tbsp  lemon , sliced 
 Directions : 
 In a blender, mix all ingredients, and blend them fully. 
 Serve with  ice .
