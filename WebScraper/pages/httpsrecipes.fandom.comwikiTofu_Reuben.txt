 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 recipe  tofu  Cutlets 
 8 slices good-quality  rye bread 
 1 cup fresh-pack  sauerkraut 
 1/4 cup plain  yogurt  or  Mayonnaise 
 2 tablespoons  catsup 
 Directions : 
 While toasting  rye bread  and heating  sauerkraut  in saucepan, mix  yogurt  or  Mayonnaise  and  catsup  together. Spread  yogurt  sauce on toasted  bread . Arrange  tofu  cutlets on  bread . Spread  sauerkraut  on top and serve.
