Ingredients : 
 6 cups  beef broth 
 1 medium  Onion 
 1 cup  oatmeal 
 1 lbs  asparagus 
 1/3 cup  rice 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Saute  onions  in  butter  or  oil  until soft. 
 Add all other ingredients except  asparagus  tips and  rice  or  Barley . 
 Bring to a boil over medium heat and simmer for 30 minutes, then place in blender to puree. 
 Return to pot and add other ingredients and simmer for 5-10 minutes, until tips are soft. Do not over cook or tips will disintegrate.
