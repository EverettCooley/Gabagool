Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 c.  milk 
 1 ripe  banana , cut into halves 
 1/2 tsp.  vanilla extract 
 1-3  ice  cubes 
 Directions : 
 Place  milk ,  banana ,  vanilla  and  ice  cubes in blender container; cover. Process for 20 seconds. Pour into glass. Serve and enjoy. Yields: one smoothie.
