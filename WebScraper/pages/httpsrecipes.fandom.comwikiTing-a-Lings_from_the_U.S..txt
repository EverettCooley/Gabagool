 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group

 Makes 42 clusters 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pkgs (6 oz. each)  semisweet chocolate  bits 
 4 cups  Cheerios 
 Directions : 
 Melt chocolate over hot water. 
 Cool at room temperature. 
 Gently mix in cheerios. 
 Drop by tablespoons onto waxed paper. 
 Refrigerate to set, about 2 hours.
