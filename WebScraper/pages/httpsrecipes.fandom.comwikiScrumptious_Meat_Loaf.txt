Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Source: The National Heart, Lung and Blood Institute (NHLBI) 
 Servings: 6 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb extra-lean  ground beef 
 ½ cup  tomato paste 
 ¼ cup  onion 
 ¼ cup  green bell pepper 
 ¼ cup  red bell pepper 
 1 cup  tomatoes , fresh, blanched, chopped 
 ½ tsp  mustard , low sodium 
 ¼ tsp  black pepper , ground 
 ½ tsp red chile pepper, ground 
 2 cloves  garlic , chopped 
 2 stalks  scallions  (green onions), chopped 
 ½ tsp  ginger , ground 
 ⅛ tsp  nutmeg , ground 
 1 tsp  orange peel  (zest), grated 
 ½ tsp  thyme , crushed 
 ¼ cup  bread crumbs , fine, dry 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together. 
 Place in 1-pound loaf pan (preferably with drip rack) and bake covered at 350°F for 50 minutes. 
 Uncover pan and continue baking for 12 minutes. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 193 Calories | 9g Total Fat | 3g Saturated Fat | 91mg Sodium | 11g Carbs | 2g Dietary Fiber | 45mg Cholesterol | 17g Protein 
 Carb Choices: ½
