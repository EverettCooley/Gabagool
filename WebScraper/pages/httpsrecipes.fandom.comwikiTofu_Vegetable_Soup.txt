Ingredients : 
 4 cans  vegetable broth  or 6 vegetable bouillon cubes with  water 
 ½ small  turnip , cut into small pieces 
 4  carrots , sliced 
 ½  green bell pepper , chopped into small pieces 
 2  potatoes , cut into small pieces 
 3 stalks  celery , sliced 
 1 medium  onion , diced 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 ½ head  cabbage , shredded 
 1 box extra  firm tofu , cut into small cubes 
 Directions : 
 Add broth,  turnip ,  carrots ,  pepper ,  potatoes ,  celery ,  onion . 
 Cook until the vegetables are tender. 
 Add the  cabbage  and the  tofu  when the vegetables reach the tender stage. 
 Cook until the  cabbage  is done.
