Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 1 diced  red pepper 
 1 diced  green pepper 
 2 small hot  green chili  peppers, seeded and minced 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1 to 2 tablespoons  olive oil 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1â…” cups cooked  red beans 
 2 tablespoons  capers 
 salt  and ground  white pepper 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Onion ,  peppers  and  garlic  in  oil  in large skillet over medium heat until  onion  is soft but not brown. 
 Stir in  rice ,  beans ,  capers ,  salt  and  pepper ; cook until thoroughly heated.
