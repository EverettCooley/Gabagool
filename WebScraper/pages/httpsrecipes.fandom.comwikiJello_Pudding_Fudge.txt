JELLO PUDDING FUDGE
 
 1 (4 oz.) pkg. chocolate pudding filling
 2 tbsp. butter
 1/4 c. milk
 1 1/2 c. sifted confectioners' sugar
 1/4 c. chopped nuts
 
 Combine pudding mix, butter and milk. Bring to a boil, gently 1 minute, stirring constantly. Remove from heat. Quickly blend in the sugar and add nuts.
 Pour into greased 8x4 inch pan. Cool, then cut into squares. Makes 1 pound. Enjoy.
