Description : 
 Contributed by Paula in California at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  flour 
 ½ tsp  baking powder 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 3 tbsp  shortening  (melted) 
 ¾ hot water 
 Directions : 
 Combine flour, baking powder and salt in bowl, add melted shortening and hot water. 
 Mix until all is moist. 
 Cover and set in warm place for half an hour. 
 Separate the dough into balls, about 2 tbsp each. 
 Roll out to size you need, put on griddle and cook until lightly golden. 
 Enjoy.
