 Ingredients : 
 ½ kg minced  beef  /  mutton  (qeema) 
 4 – 5 tbsp fresh  carder  (dhania) 
 1 tsp  peppers  (ground) 
 mint  leaves (podina) as required 
 6  green chilies  (cut to slices) 
 oil  for shallow frying 
 salt  to taste 
 red  chili powder  to taste 
 ½ tsp  turmeric powder 
 1 tsp  garam masala 
 1 tbsp  poppy seeds  (khashkhaash) 
 2 tbsp  ginger  +  garlic  paste 
 1 tbsp  seramum seeds  (til) 
 2  onions  (fried and chopped) (cut to slices) 
 1 tbsp raw musk (poppaya) 
 2 tbsp  soy sauce 
 2 tbsp  flour of pulse  - parched (baisan) 
 1  egg 
 Directions : 
 Take minced  beef  in a bowl. 
 Add all the ingredients except  oil  and mix well. 
 Prepare kebabs of reasonable thickness and size of this mixture. 
 Shallow fry the kebabs on medium heat (never press while frying). 
 Serve with ketchup or chutney.
