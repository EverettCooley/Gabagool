 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup  Mayonnaise 
 4 teaspoons  lemon juice 
 4 teaspoons  Sugar 
 1/4 teaspoon  salt 
 3 cups cooked  rice , cooled 
 1 12-1/2-ounce can  Tuna , packed in  water , drained 
 3 ribs  celery , chopped 
 1 medium  carrot , grated 
 1 8-ounce can crushed  pineapple  in juice 
 1/2 cup  Raisins 
 lettuce  leaves 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix  Mayonnaise , juice and  salt  in large bowl. Add  rice ,  Tuna ,  celery ,  carrot ,  pineapple  and  Raisins  in large bowl. Toss lightly. Serve on  lettuce  leaves.
