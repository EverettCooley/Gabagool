 Description : 
       Amish Baked Chicken     
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 c.  Flour 
 2 tsp.  Paprika 
 1 tsp.  Pepper 
 1/4 tsp. Dry  Mustard 
 3 tsp.  Salt 
 1 cut up  Broiler  or young  Chicken 
 1/4Â lb.  Butter 
 Directions : 
 Mix the dry ingredients well . 
 Coat the cut up  Chicken  parts with the mixture. 
 Melt the  butter . 
 Place the  Chicken  parts in the pan. 
 Bake the  Chicken  at 350 degrees for 1 1/2 to 2 hours .
