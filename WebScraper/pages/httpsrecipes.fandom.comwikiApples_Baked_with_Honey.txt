Ingredients : 
 500 g  apples 
 4 tbsp  honey 
 50 g  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Wash the tart  apples , cut out the cores and fill the hollows with  sugar . 
 Place the  apples  in a baking pan, pour in some  water  and put in the oven to bake. 
 Before serving pour over with honey.
