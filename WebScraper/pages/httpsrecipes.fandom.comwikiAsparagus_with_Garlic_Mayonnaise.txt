 Ingredients : 
 1 to 1½ lbs.  asparagus , tough ends trimmed 
 Garlic mayonnaise : 
 1 cup  soy mayonnaise 
 ½ cup chopped fresh herbs, such as  basil ,  tarragon  or  chervil 
 1 tbsp fresh  lemon juice 
 2 small  garlic  garlic, minced 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 ⅛ tsp freshly ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Garlic mayonnaise : 
 In medium bowl, mix all ingredients. 
 Cover and chill for at least 1 hour or up to 2 days. 
 Whisk well before serving. 
 Asparagus : 
 Steam  asparagus  until crisp-tender, about 4 minutes. 
 Drain, arrange  asparagus  on plates and serve hot with spoonful of garlic mayonnaise. 
 Alternatively, serve  asparagus  cold. 
 After cooking, drain  asparagus , rinse with cold  water  until cool, then drain again. 
 Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.
