This is a family favorite and the recipe is always requested when I take them to an event.
 
       Ham Rollups     
 Ingredients : 
 8 	 large  flour  tortillas 
 1 lb  Ham , chopped very fine 
 1 	can  black olives , chopped very fine 
 6-10 	 green onions  with tops, chopped very fine 
 5 (8 	ounces) packages  cream cheese , softened 
 Directions : 
 Use a food processor to grind the  Ham ,  black olives  and  green onions  very fine. 
 Mix the  Ham ,  black olives  and  green onions  into the softened  cream cheese . 
 Spread onto  flour  tortillas, roll and cut into 1/2" slices.
