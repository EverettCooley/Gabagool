Ingredients : 
 250g  chickpea flour 
 100 ml  olive oil 
 1 litre  water 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Mix the chick pea flour, olive oil and water (if possible leave overnight) 
 Pre-heat the oven to 200 °F and bake the mixture in a tray for 45 minutes (or until firm). 
 Eat warm and sprinkle some salt and pepper before eating.
