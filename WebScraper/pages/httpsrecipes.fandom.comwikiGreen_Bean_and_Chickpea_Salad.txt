Ingredients : 
 1 pound  green beans , trimmed and cut into 2 inch pieces 
 1 can (16 oz.)  chickpeas , drained and rinsed 
 ¼ chopped  onion 
 2 tsp  red wine vinegar 
 1 tsp chopped fresh  basil 
 ¼ tsp  black pepper 
 1 large  garlic  clove, crushed 
 1⅓ tsp  olive oil  or  salad oil 
 Directions : 
 Steam the  green beans  until they are “tender-crisp” (about 5 minutes). 
 Place  beans ,  chickpeas , and  onion  in a large bowl. 
 In a small bowl, combine all the dressing ingredients. 
 Add the dressing to the bean mixture, tossing the ingredients well.
