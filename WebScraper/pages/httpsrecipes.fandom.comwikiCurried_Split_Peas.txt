Ingredients : 
 ½ – 1 lb  split peas  450 g 
 ½ medium  onion  (chopped) 
 2 cloves  garlic  (crushed) 
 1 tbsp  coconut oil  15 ml 
 ½ tsp  cumin  2 ml 
 ¾ tsp  salt  4 ml 
 1 tbsp  yellow ginger  15 ml 
 Directions : 
 In medium sized skillet, put 4 cups  water , add  peas  and boil until tender. 
 Add more  water  if necessary. 
 Blend cooked  peas  in blender on medium speed for 15 seconds. 
 Return blended  peas  to pot. 
 Chonko (sauté)  onion  and  garlic  in hot fat. 
 Add to blended  peas . 
 Season with  cumin , yellow  ginger  and  salt . 
 Cook over low heat for 8 – 10 minutes. 
 Serve with  white rice . 
 Accompaniment to the main dish.
