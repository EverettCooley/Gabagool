Ingredients : 
 1 cup  shortening 
 1½ cups  oil 
 ¾ cup  orange juice 
 3 tbsp  sugar 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 3 cups sifted  all purpose flour 
 1 cup finely chopped  walnuts 
 4 cups sifted  pastry flour 
 Syrup : 
 2 cups  sugar 
 2 cups  water 
 ⅔ cup liquid  honey 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 325°F. 
 Beat together  shortening  and  oil  until light. 
 Gradually add  orange juice  and  sugar , beating until well blended. 
 Sift together  baking powder , soda and all purpose  flour . 
 Add these sifted dry ingredients gradually, beating thoroughly. 
 Add  nuts  and  pastry flour . 
 Knead in some of the  flour  if necessary on a lightly floured board. 
 Roll into six inch long strips, one inch thick. 
 Cut diagonally into one-inch long pieces. 
 Place on ungreased baking sheet and bake for 30 minutes or until lightly browned. 
 Syrup : 
 Combine ingredients in a saucepan and simmer 10 minutes. 
 Skim and cool. 
 Dip hot cookies in cooled syrup, using a slotted spoon.
