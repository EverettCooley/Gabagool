Description : 
 Always check the ingredients to make sure the product is vegan.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 large  eggplants 
 2 cloves  garlic 
 juice of 1  lemon 
 2 – 3 tbsp  olive oil 
 ¼ cup  nayonnaise 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Bake  eggplant  at 400°F for 45 minutes. 
 Skin should be charred and the middle soft. 
 Let the  eggplant  cool. 
 Once cooled, scoop out the flesh of the  eggplant  into a bowl, and mix in the rest of the ingredients. 
 Blend until smooth. 
 Serve with pita chips.
