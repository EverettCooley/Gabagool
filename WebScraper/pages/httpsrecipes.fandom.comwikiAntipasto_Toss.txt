Antipasto Toss
 1 can garbanzo beans, drained (15 oz)
1 jar marinated artichoke hearts (6 oz)
1/2 cup pitted ripe olives, cut into halves
1/2 cup herb and garlic or Italian salad dressing
2 bunches romaine, torn into bite size piece
1 bunch leaf lettuce, torn into bite size pieces
1/2 cup sliced pepperoni, if desired
Freshly ground pepper
 Toss beans, artichoke hearts with liquid, olive and salad dressing. cover and refrigerate. Just before serving, toss with remaining ingredients.
