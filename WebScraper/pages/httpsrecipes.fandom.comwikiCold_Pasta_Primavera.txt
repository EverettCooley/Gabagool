Ingredients : 
 8 ounce package (400 – 500 grams) any  pasta 
 2 pounds (1 – 1½ kilogram bag) frozen assorted vegetables 
 1 – 2 jars clear Italian dressing 
 Directions : 
 Cook  pasta   al dente  (not too soft) and drain. 
 Cook frozen vegetables also not too mushy, if has big pieces in the bag, cut up to bite-size. 
 When each are cool and well-drained, mix and pour on dressing then mix and serve. 
 It is better if. done an hour or so before serving and refrigerated.
