Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound lean ground  Beef  or  Turkey 
 1 medium  Onion , chopped 
 2 tablespoons  chili powder 
 1 teaspoon ground  cumin 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 3 cups cooked  brown rice 
 1 16-ounce can  pinto beans , drained 
 2 4-ounce cans diced green chiles 
 1 medium fresh  tomato , seeded and chopped (optional) 
 fresh  cilantro  for garnish (optional) 
 Directions : 
 In large skillet over medium-high heat, cook meat until brown, stirring to crumble; drain. Return meat to skillet. Add  Onion ,  chili powder ,  cumin  and  salt ; cook until  Onion  is tender. Stir in  rice , beans and chiles; thoroughly heat. Top with  tomato  and garnish with  cilantro  if desired. Serve immediately.
