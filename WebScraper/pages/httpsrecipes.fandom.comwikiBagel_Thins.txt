 Description : 
 These are low calorie and low salt
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 each  bagel 
 2 tsp  soft margarine , melted 
 1 tsp  oregano , dried 
 Directions : 
 Using a very sharp serrated knife, slice  bagel  into very thin rounds. 
 Arrange in single layer on baking sheet; brush with  margarine . 
 Sprinkle with  oregano . 
 Bake in 350 °F oven for 12 minutes. 
 Let cool and store in airtight container for up to 1 week. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per 1 piece serving: 14 cal; .5g fat, 0 cholesterol, 15 mg  salt
