 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup  margarine  or  butter 
 1 cup  semisweet chocolate  chips 
 1 1/2 cup Gold Medal  all-purpose flour 
 1 cup  Sugar 
 1 cup Chopped  nuts 
 1/2 tsp  baking powder 
 1/2 tsp  vanilla 
 1/4 tsp  salt 
 2 each  eggs 
 48 each Miniature marshmallows 
 
 
 Directions : 
 1 Heat oven to 400 degrees. 
2 Heat  margarine  and ½ cup of the  chocolate chips  in 1 qt. saucepan over low heat, stirring frequently, until melted; remove from heat and cool. 
3 Mix remaining  chocolate chips , the melted  chocolate  mixture and remaining ingredients except marshmallows. 
4 Drop dough by rounded teaspoonfuls about 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheet. 
5 Press 1  marshmallow  in center of each bake about 8 minutes or until almost no indentation remains when touched. 
6 Immediately remove from cookie sheet. 
7 Cook on wire rack. 
8 Makes about 4 dozen cookies.
