      Blinchiki With Tvorogv     
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  flour 
 1 cup  milk 
 3  eggs 
 salt 
 oil  to fry 
 Filling : 
 200 g tvorog ( cottage cheese ) 
 Â½ cup  milk 
 100 g  poppy seeds 
 1 cup  raisins 
 sugar 
 Directions : 
 Put ground  poppy seeds  and  raisins  in hot  milk  and heat until dense, add  sugar  and cool down. 
 Combine the mixture with tvorog and 2 yolks. 
 Sift  flour  in a bowl, add  milk  and  salt . 
 Stir in 1 whole  egg  and 2 whites and knead dough. 
 Bake thin pancakes, but fry only on one side and put a pancake fried side on a plate, spread the filling and roll into a log. 
 Put all the rolls on a baking sheet, brush with  butter  and bake in the oven. 
 Serve with  milk  or juice.
