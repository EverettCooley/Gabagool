Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  flour 
 5  eggs 
 1 cup  sugar 
 250 g  butter 
 peel of 1  lemon 
 Directions : 
 Whip  butter  and  sugar  in a mixer, add  eggs , whip until  sugar  is dissolved and add  flour  gradually. 
 Grate  lemon peel  and add to the dough. 
 Put the dough in a well greased mold and bake in a preheated oven for 35 minutes on 200°C.
