Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes for the week of 2/25/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pkg. frozen  broccoli  chopped 
 3 cups cooked diced  chicken 
 3 cans  cream of chicken  soup 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 2 tbsp  lemon juice 
 ½ tbsp  curry powder 
 ½ cup  bread crumbs 
 2 tbsp  butter  melted 
 1 cup shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Cook  broccoli  according to directions. 
 Drain well. 
 Arrange in the bottom of a 9x13 baking dish. 
 Arrange  chicken  on top. 
 Combine soup,  mayo ,  lemon juice ,  curry powder . 
 Stir well to blend. 
 Pour over  chicken . 
 Combine  bread crumbs  and  butter . 
 Sprinkle over top of casserole. 
 Bake at 350°F for 30 minutes. 
 Sprinkle with shredded  cheddar  and return to oven for an additional 5 minutes of cook time until  cheese  melts.
