Description : 
 Makes 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound boneless  chicken breast , cut into 1-inch cubes* 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 1 x 5.2-ounce package quick cooking  chicken -flavored  long-grain  and  wild rice , prepared according to package instructions 
 1 x 6-ounce jar sliced  mushrooms , drained 
 Â½ cup dry  white wine 
 1 teaspoon dried  tarragon  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Cook  chicken  in hot  oil  in large skillet over medium-high heat 2 to 3 minutes, turning to brown both sides. 
 Add  rice ,  mushrooms , wine and  tarragon . Simmer uncovered 5 to 10 minutes or until  chicken  is cooked.
