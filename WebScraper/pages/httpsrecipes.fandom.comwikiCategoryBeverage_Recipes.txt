The term  beverage  usually refers to any drinkable liquid, excluding water. Some may refer to a beverage as "liquid refreshment". A more common term for beverage is  drink .
 Usually beverages are divided into the non-alcoholic (dry) and alcoholic (wet) categories.  Liquor  is a term that refers to alcoholic beverages.
