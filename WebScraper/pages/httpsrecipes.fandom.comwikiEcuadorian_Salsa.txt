Ingredients : 
 ½ cup diced raw  carrots 
 ¼ cup diced  bell pepper  (preferably red) 
 ¼ medium  onion , diced 
 2 medium-sized juicy  tomatoes , deseeded and chopped (Do not use Italian  tomatoes , too dry) 
 1  garlic  clove 
 juice of ½  lime 
 10 – 15 sprigs  cilantro  (leaves only) 
 salt  and  chile peppers  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Toss all the ingredients above together in a bowl.
