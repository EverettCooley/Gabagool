Info 
 Cook Time : About 5 minutes 
 Serves : 2
 
 Ingredients 
 1/2 cup  mango , fresh or frozen 
 1/2 cup  cantaloupe , fresh or frozen 
 1 cup  strawberries , fresh or frozen 
 1 cup  pineapple juice  (or more to thin) 
 Directions 
 Place all ingredients in a blender and blend for 10 seconds or until smooth. 
 Source 
 Fresh Hawaiian Smoothie  from the  Public Health Cookbook  -- original source of recipe, public domain government resource
