Ingredients 
 2 x 8 oz tubes  crescent rolls 
 2 sticks  butter 
 4 firm  peaches , peeled and quartered 
 1½ cups  sugar 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 1 x 12 oz Mountain Dew (soft drink) 
 Directions 
 Melt butter, add sugar and cinnamon, unroll crescents and place peach quarters in each one. 
 Roll from large end to small. 
 Place in 12" x 10" x 2" pan. 
 Pour butter mixture over them and then pour Mountain Dew on top. 
 Bake at 350 °F oven for 45 minutes.
