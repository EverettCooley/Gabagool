Description : 
 Makes 6 servings. Serve with broiled  chicken , roast  turkey  or white fish.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon  ground ginger 
 1 teaspoon  chili powder 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 ½ cup canned  tomatoes , drained 
 ½ cup flaked  coconut 
 ⅔ cup chopped  cashew   nuts , divided 
 1 cup  yogurt 
 Directions : 
 Cook  onion  and  garlic  in  butter  until tender. 
 Add  rice ,  salt ,  ginger ,  chili powder ,  pepper ,  tomatoes ,  coconut  and ⅓ cup  nuts . Toss lightly. 
 Fold in  yogurt . Heat through. 
 Top with remaining ⅓ cup  nuts .
