Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  jack cheese , grated 
 1 – 2 medium  tomatoes , chopped 
 1 medium-large  yellow onion , chopped 
 4 – 5  green onions , chopped 
 1 (4 oz) can chopped Ortega  chilies 
 1 (4 oz) small can chopped  olives 
 cilantro  to taste 
 1 recipe Good Seasons  Italian dressing 
 tortilla chips  or Fritos 
 Directions : 
 Melt all ingredients together except Italian dressing. 
 When ready to serve; stir in Italian dressing and dip chips.
