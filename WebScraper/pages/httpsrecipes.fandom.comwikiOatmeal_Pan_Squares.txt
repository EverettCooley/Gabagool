Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 30 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  flour 
 1 tbsp  soda 
 ½ tbsp  salt 
 ½ tbsp  cinnamon 
 1 cup  margarine , softened 
 ¾ cup  sugar 
 ¾ cup  brown sugar 
 2  eggs 
 1 tbsp  vanilla 
 3 cups  rolled oats 
 6 oz pkg.  chocolate chips 
 6 oz pkg.  butterscotch chips 
 Directions : 
 In small bowl, combine  flour , soda,  salt  and  cinnamon . 
 Set aside. 
 In a large bowl, combine  margarine ,  sugar ,  brown sugar ,  eggs  and  vanilla . 
 Beat until light. 
 Add dry ingredients. 
 Fold in  oats  along with  chocolate chips  and butterscotch chips. 
 Spread into greased 10x13-inch baking pan. 
 Bake at 375°F for 20 to 25 minutes. 
 Cool and cut into squares.
