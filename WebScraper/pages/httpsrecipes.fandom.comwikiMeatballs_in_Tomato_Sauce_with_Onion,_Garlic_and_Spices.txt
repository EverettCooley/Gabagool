Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup  bulgur 
 8 oz  ground beef 
 ½ cup finely chopped  onion 
 ¼ tsp ground  cinnamon 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp ground  allspice 
 ½ tsp  pepper 
 3 tsp  canola oil 
 1 tbsp minced  garlic 
 28 oz can crushed  tomatoes 
 1 tsp  sugar 
 ½ cup packed  cilantro , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Mix bulgur, beef, onion, cinnamon, ½ tsp salt, allspice and ¼ tsp pepper. 
 Roll rounded tablespoons into balls. 
 Heat 1 tsp oil in a skillet. 
 Add meatballs, cook until browned. 
 Transfer to a plate. 
 Heat remaining oil in a skillet. 
 Add garlic, sauté 1 minute. 
 Stir in tomatoes, sugar, remaining salt and pepper and meatballs. 
 Bring to a simmer, cover and cook 25 minutes or until bulgur is tender. 
 Serve sprinkled with cilantro.
