 Description : 
 A very tasty Pizza with Canadian layout. Try it out, it's delicious !
You can replace the Hormel  Bacon  with other  Bacon  in case it's not available. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (12-ounce) package refrigerated  buttermilk  biscuits
1/4 cup pizza sauce
1 1/4 cups shredded  mozzarella   Cheese 
1 HORMEL® Pizza Style Canadian  Bacon  (3.5-ounce) package
1 (8-ounce) can  pineapple  tidbits, drained
1/2 cup chopped green  bell pepper 
 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 400 °F. Separate biscuits and press each into a 4-inch circle on greased baking sheets. Spread pizza sauce over biscuits. Top biscuits with  Cheese , Canadian  Bacon  slices,  pineapple  and  bell pepper . Bake 12–16 minutes or until  Cheese  is melted and biscuits are golden brown.
