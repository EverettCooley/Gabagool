Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Simple and Delicious 
 Makes 1 dozen 
 Ingredients : 
 4 squares (1 oz each)  white baking chocolate , divided 
 12 metal or plastic spoons 
 8  caramels 
 2 tsp  heavy whipping cream 
 Directions : 
 In a microwave safe bowl, melt two squares of white chocolate; stir until smooth. 
 Dip six spoons into chocolate; tap spoon handles on edge of bowl to remove excess chocolate. 
 Place on waxed paper; let stand until set. 
 Repeat with remaining chocolate and spoons. 
 In a microwave safe bowl, melt caramels and cream; stir until smooth. 
 Drizzle over spoons. 
 Let stand until set. 
 Tip: as a variation, drizzle spoons with melted dark or milk chocolate.
