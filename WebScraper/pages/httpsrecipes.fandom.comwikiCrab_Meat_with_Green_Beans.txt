Ingredients : 
 3  red crabs 
 1 tsp  rice wine 
 1  egg 
 2 tbsp  corn oil 
 Sauce : 
 ½ cup  green beans 
 1 tbsp  rice wine 
 1 tbsp  soy sauce 
 ½ cup  beef broth 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  sugar 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 1 tbsp  cornstarch 
 Directions : 
 Scrub fresh red crabs with brush to clean and steam 5 minutes in boiling, salted  water . 
 Break shells, remove meat and sprinkle meat with wine. 
 Parboil  green beans  in salted, boiling  water  for 2 minutes and cut diagonally. 
 Beat  egg , add dash of  salt  and mix with crab meat. 
 Preheat  oil  in frying pan, pour beaten egg and crabmeat into pan, and fry slowly. 
 Mix  oil , wine and soy sauce in pan and sauté  green beans  in this sauce. 
 Add  beef broth  to  green beans  and season with  salt ,  sugar  and  pepper . 
 Dissolve 1 tablespoon  cornstarch  in 2 tablespoons  water  and add to  green bean  mixture, stirring 1 minute until it thickens. 
 Place fried crab meat on serving dish and pour sauce over.
