 Description : 
 In Romanian: Supa de sfecla
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 qts / 3 l  water 
 2 lbs / 1 kg red  beets 
 1 tablespoon  flour 
 1 tablespoon  sugar 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 2 tablespoons  vinegar 
 salt 
 1 teaspoon  butter 
 boiled  noodles   or  toasted  bread 
 Directions : 
 Clean the beets, wash and grate. 
 Set to boil with the  water  for 30â€“40 minutes (uncovered). 
 Strain and then add to the liquid the  salt ,  sugar  and  vinegar . 
 After it comes to another boil, add the  butter  mixed with the  flour  and  sour cream , mix well and let it boil for a little while. 
 Serve with boiled noodles or toast.
