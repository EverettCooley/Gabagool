 3.1   Cheese sauce 
 
 
 
 
 Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 12 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 3½ pounds  broccoli 
 2 pounds  cauliflower 
 Cheese sauce : 
 3 tbsp  unsalted butter 
 3 tbsp  flour 
 1¾ cups  milk 
 2 cups shredded sharp  cheddar  cheese 
 1 tbsp  Dijon mustard 
 4.25 oz can diced  green chilies , drained 
 ¼ tsp  hot pepper sauce 
 ⅛ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Cut  broccoli  and  cauliflower  into florets. 
 In a pot, bring 8 cups of lightly salted  water  to a boil. 
 Add  cauliflower , cook 2 minutes. 
 Add  broccoli , cook an additional 6 minutes or until vegetables are tender, drain. 
 Cheese sauce : 
 In a saucepan, melt  butter . 
 Whisk in  flour  and cook 1 minutes. 
 Whisk in  milk , continue to whisk until smooth. 
 Bring to a boil. 
 Remove from heat and stir in  cheese  and next 4 ingredients. 
 Stir until smooth. 
 To serve, place vegetables in serving dish and pour  cheese  sauce over the top. 
 Serve immediately.
