Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 to 4 lbs shoulder  pork  roast (boneless) 
 1 large can Ortega green chile  salsa 
 1 medium can Ortega chopped  green chilies 
 garlic salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 In crock pot place  pork  roast (sprinkled with garlic salt to taste), can of green salsa and chopped chilies. 
 Turn crock pot to medium setting. 
 Cook 6 to 8 hours until  pork  is tender. 
 Enjoy.
