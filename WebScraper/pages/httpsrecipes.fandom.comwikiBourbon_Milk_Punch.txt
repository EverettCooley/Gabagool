Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz  bourbon 
 3 oz  half and half 
 1 tsp  superfine sugar 
 ¼ tsp  vanilla extract 
 garnish: ¼ tsp grated  nutmeg 
 Directions : 
 Shake all the ingredients in a shaker with  ice  cubes and strain into an old-fashioned glass. 
 Garnish with  nutmeg  on top.
