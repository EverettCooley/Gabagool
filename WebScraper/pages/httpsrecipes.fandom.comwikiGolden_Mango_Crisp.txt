Ingredients : 
 8 cups  mangos , sliced 
 1 tsp ground  cinnamon 
 1 tsp ground  nutmeg 
 ⅓ cup  butter 
 1 package golden pound  cake mix 
 ½ cup chopped  nuts 
 Directions : 
 Mix  mangos ,  cinnamon  and  nutmeg . 
 Place in bottom of ungreased 13x9" pan. 
 Cut  butter  into cake mix until crumbly. 
 Stir in  nuts . 
 Bake at 350°F for 50 – 60 minutes. 
 Serve warm with  whipped cream  or  ice   cream .
