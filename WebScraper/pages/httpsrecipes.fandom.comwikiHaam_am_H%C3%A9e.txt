 "Ham in Hay" - This recipe comes from the very north of Luxembourg.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 shoulder of  ham , lightly smoked, of about 4 kilos 
 hay 
 3 â€“ 5 litres  water 
 Directions : 
 Take a very large metal container, even a washing boiler, and pad it out with the hay. 
 Pour between three and five litres of  water  over the hay. 
 Lay the  ham  on the hay in such a manner that it does not touch the  water , so that it is cooked by steaming. 
 If necessary add hay during the cooking process. 
 The pot should be covered. 
 Place the pot on the heat. 
 From time to time add  water , to replace that which has evaporated. 
 Cooking time is about 20 to 25 minutes per half kilo. 
 The cooked  ham  should be presented on a bed of hay, and served with  pommes frites  and salad.
