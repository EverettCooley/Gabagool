Description : 
 Frying spice brings out the flavor and health benefits of essential oils.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ghee 
 mustard seeds 
 cumin 
 other spices (depending on dosha) 
 ginger 
 coriander 
 turmeric 
 Directions : 
 Heat  ghee  in skillet over medium high heat. 
 Add a few  mustard seeds , when the seeds start to pop turn the heat to medium. 
 Add the rest of seeds and as they pop add  cumin  seeds then add other seeds and spices. 
 When popping slows add  ginger  and saute until golden brown. 
 Add  coriander seeds  for 20 seconds and then  turmeric  for 10 seconds 
 Now your ready for  tomatoes  or other vegetables for  curry .
