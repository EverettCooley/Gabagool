 Description : 
 In Romanian: Prajitura cu zmeura
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 oz / 250 g  cottage cheese 
 2 hard boiled  yolks 
 3 raw  yolks 
 2 oz / 50 g  butter 
 salt 
 sugar  to taste 
 1 lb / 500 g  raspberries 
 shortening  for the pan 
 Directions : 
 Sieve the  cottage cheese , mix with the two hard boiled  yolks , also sieved and with 3 raw  yolks . 
 Add 2 oz/50 g  butter ,  salt  and  sugar  to taste. 
 Mix everything well, to get a creamy mixture. 
 Add 3 whipped  egg  whites. 
 Grease a pan and pour the batter in a uniform layer. 
 Arrange  raspberries  on top. 
 Bake at medium heat. 
 Cut according to taste.
