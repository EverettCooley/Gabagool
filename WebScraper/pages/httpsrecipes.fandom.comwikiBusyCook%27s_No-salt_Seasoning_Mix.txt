Description : 
 Make your own salt free seasoning mix to add punch to your recipes with no sodium.
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Source: Linda Larsen,  Your Guide to Busy Cooks . 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tbsp  garlic powder 
 2 tsp dried  thyme  leaves 
 2 tsp  onion powder 
 ½ tsp  garlic powder 
 2 tsp  paprika 
 2 tsp  celery seed 
 1½ tsp ground  white pepper 
 1 tbsp  dry mustard  powder 
 2 tsp dried finely chopped  lemon peel 
 1 tsp  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Mix all the ingredients together in a small mixing bowl, stirring until thoroughly combined. 
 Store in a dark, cool place in a tightly sealed container. 
 Use on meats, poultry and fish, or at the table for a salt-free seasoning.
