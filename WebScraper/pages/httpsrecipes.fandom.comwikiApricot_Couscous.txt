 Description : 
 This type of  couscous  is sweet and savory and is great as an appetizer. You have to try it!
 
 Ingredients : 
 125 g.  dried apricots 
 450 g. uncooked  couscous 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 2 tablespoon roughly chopped  coriander or parsley 
 100 g.  spring onions , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Chop the  apricots  and put them in a bowl. 
 Put in enough boiling  water  to cover, then soak for about 10 minutes. 
 While the  apricots  are soaking, put the  couscous  in a large bowl and add enough boiling  water  to cover them. 
 Put clingfilm over the bowl to make it airtight and allow it to stand for 10 minutes so it cooks. 
 Drain any excess  water  off the  apricots  and add to the  couscous , mixing briefly with a fork. 
 Add the remaining ingredients and stir it really well. Serve hot and plain or with meat or poultry. 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Servings:  4 tablespoons per plate
