      Recipe by Hooked on Heat     
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  chicken stock  / broth 
 1-inch piece of  ginger 
 2 – 3 cloves of  garlic 
 2 red  chilies 
 Fresh  basil  leaves 
 1 stalk of  lemon grass 
 thin  rice noodles   or   vermicelli 
 ½ cup sliced  onions 
 ½ cup fried pieces of  chicken   or   tofu 
 freshly chopped  coriander leaves 
 ½ cup  bean sprouts 
 1 tbsp chopped  peanuts 
 Directions : 
 Soak noodles in a bowl of hot water for 5 minutes and set aside. 
 Add ginger, garlic, red chilies, basil and lemon grass to chicken stock and bring to a boil. 
 Strain the soup and add to noodles.
