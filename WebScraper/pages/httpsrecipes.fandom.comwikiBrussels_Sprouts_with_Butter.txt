 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs / 1 kg  Brussels sprouts 
 3 tablespoons  butter 
 1 tablespoon  breadcrumbs 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Choose small sprouts, remove yellow leaves, wash well and set to boil in salted  water , at high temperature, for about 20 minutes. 
 Drain well. Place the  butter  in a pan and fry the bread crumbs until golden brown. 
 Add the sprouts and keep on heat for a few minutes longer, shaking the pan until the sprouts are well mixed with the fried bread crumbs. Add  salt .
