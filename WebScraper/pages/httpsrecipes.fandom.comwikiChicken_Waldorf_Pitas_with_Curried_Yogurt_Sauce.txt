Ingredients : 
 2 whole  chicken  breasts, skinned, boned, cooked and cubed 
 ½ cup Italian dressing 
 1 medium green  apple , cubed 
 ¼ cup thinly sliced  celery 
 ¼ cup  sliced almonds , toasted 
 3 tbsp  Raisins 
 2  California avocados , seeded, peeled and cubed 
 4 pita breads, halved 
 8 pieces curly  green-leaf lettuce 
 cherry tomatoes , optional 
 Curried yogurt sauce : 
 1 cup plain low-fat  yogurt 
 ½ tsp to 1 teaspoon  curry powder 
 ¼ tsp  mace 
 Directions : 
 Prepare curried yogurt sauce. 
 Marinate  chicken  in Italian dressing 4 to 8 hours. 
 Stir in  apple ,  celery ,  almonds  and  raisins . 
 Add curried  yogurt  sauce. 
 Gently fold in avocados. 
 Place  lettuce  in pita halves and fill with  chicken  mixture. 
 Garnish with  tomatoes  sauce preparation stir ingredients together.
