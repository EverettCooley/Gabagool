 Ingredients : 
 2 cups crushed  pretzels 
 ½ – 1 cup melted  butter 
 3 tablespoons  sugar 
 1 package  Dream Whip  (prepare as directed on package) 
 1 (8 ounce) package  cream cheese , softened 
 ¾ cup  sugar 
 1 package  strawberry Jell-O gelatin dessert  (prepared as directed on package) 
 1 package frozen  strawberries , thawed 
 Directions : 
 Mix pretzels, melted  butter  and 3 tablespoons  sugar  in bowl. 
 Press into 13 x 9 inch pan and bake for 10 minutes at 350°F; cool. 
 In medium bowl, blend  cream cheese  and ¾ cup  sugar , then add the prepared  Dream Whip . 
 Pour on top of crust and chill. 
 Prepare Jell-O as directed on package and partially set; add  strawberries . 
 Pour over  cream cheese  mixture and chill until completely jelled. 
 Cut into squares.
