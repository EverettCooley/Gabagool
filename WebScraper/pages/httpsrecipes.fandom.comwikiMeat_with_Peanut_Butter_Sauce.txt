Good with  chicken  too.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tsp  oil 
 1  onion , chopped 
 2 lbs chopped  meat 
 salt ,  pepper 
 1 tsp chopped  parsley 
 1 can  tomato paste 
 1  bay leaf 
 ¼ tsp  ginger 
 ¼ tsp  cloves 
 ¼ tsp  thyme 
 1 cup  peanut butter 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil . 
 Brown  onions  and meat. 
 Add seasoning and 1 cup  water . 
 Cook 10 minutes. 
 Add  peanut butter  softened in  water  and about 4 more cups of  water . 
 Cover and simmer until tender.
