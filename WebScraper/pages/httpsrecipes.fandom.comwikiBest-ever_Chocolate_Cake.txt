Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Pouter Estate in Wylie, Texas in 1988.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 package devil's food  cake mix 
 1 small package  instant chocolate pudding mix 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 1 cup  milk 
 ½ cup  vegetable oil 
 ½ cup water 
 4  eggs 
 3 cups  chocolate chips  divided use 
 6 tablespoons  unsalted butter 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Grease and flour tube pan. 
 In large bowl combine cake mix, pudding mix, sour cream, milk, oil, water and eggs. 
 Beat until blended then mix in 2 cups of the chocolate chips. 
 Pour batter into prepared pan and bake 50 minutes. 
 Cool 15 minutes in pan then turn out onto a wire rack and cool completely. 
 Melt butter in saucepan over medium heat. 
 Add 1 cup chocolate chips and stir until smooth then drizzle over cake.
