Description : 
 A TNT recipe from Mom Marcon's kitchen.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: My Mom and my old recipes 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ lb lean  ground beef , or use a little more 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 1 clove  garlic , crushed 
 1 pkg, frozen  spinach , thawed (10 oz) 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 cup grated  jack cheese 
 4  eggs , beaten 
 ¾ cup  milk 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  pepper 
 1 can (15 oz)  tomato sauce 
 ½ tsp each  basil ,  oregano  and seasoned  pepper 
 pepper  (optional) 
 1 tsp  garlic salt 
 ½ cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 Directions : 
 Sauté beef, onion and garlic until meat loses it's read color. 
 Add spinach, rice and jack cheese. 
 Mix well. 
 Mix the eggs, milk, salt and pepper. 
 Stir into rice/meat mixture. 
 Turn into greased shallow 2 quart casserole. 
 Bake at 350°F for 30 minutes or until set. 
 Meanwhile. 
 Blend tomato sauce and seasonings. 
 Simmer about 5 minutes. 
 Cut quiche into squares and serve topped with tomato sauce and Parmesan cheese. 
 Nutritional information : 
 If cut into 6 servings, each serving is about 375 calories.
