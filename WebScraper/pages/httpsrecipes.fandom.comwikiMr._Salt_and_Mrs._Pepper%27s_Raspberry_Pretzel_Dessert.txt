 Ingredients : 
 2 cups crushed  pretzel  sticks 
 3 tbsp  sugar 
 ¾ cup melted  butter 
 12 oz  Cool Whip 
 8 oz softened  cream cheese 
 1 large box of  raspberry Jell-O 
 2 cups boiling water 
 1 lb frozen  raspberries 
 Directions : 
 Mix pretzels, sugar and butter. 
 Pat into 9x13 pan and bake at 400°F for seven minutes. 
 Let cool. Carefully fold cool whip into cream cheese and spread on top of pretzel layer. 
 Mix the Jell-O and water; stir in frozen raspberries. Pour over second layer. Refrigerate overnight.
