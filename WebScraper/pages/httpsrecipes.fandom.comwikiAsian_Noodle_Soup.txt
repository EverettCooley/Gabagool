 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 pkgs oriental flavored  ramen  noodles 
 8 c.  water 
 4 large  carrots , peeled and diced 
 1  portobello mushroom  cap, cleaned and diced 
 4 stalks  celery , diced 
 1/2 c. sliced green  Onion 
 1/3 c. soy sauce 
 1/4 c. Dijon  mustard 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Place  water , 4 flavor packages,  carrots , and  celery  in a pot and bring to a boil. 
 Add noodles and cook until tender. 
 While noodles are cooking, combine soy sauce and  mustard . 
 Add sauce mixture,  Mushrooms , and  Onion  to cooked noodle mixture. 
 Ladle into bowls and serve. 4-5 servings
