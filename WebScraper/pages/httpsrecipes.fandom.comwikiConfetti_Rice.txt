Ingredients : 
 ½ lb diced  bacon 
 1 cup  rice , cooked 
 1 cup diced  carrots , parboiled 
 1 cup  celery , parboiled 
 1 cup fresh  peas  or frozen  peas 
 soy sauce 
 Directions : 
 Cook  bacon  in lg skillet until crisp. 
 Drain on paper towels. 
 To skillet, add cooked  rice , and vegetables. 
 Cook and stir for 7 – 8 minutes. 
 Stir in  bacon . 
 Serve with soy sauce.
