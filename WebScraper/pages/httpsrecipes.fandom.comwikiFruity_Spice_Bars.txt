Ingredients : 
 ½ cup + 1 tsp  flour 
 ¾ tsp  apple pie spice 
 ½ tsp  baking soda 
 ¼ tsp  baking powder 
 ¾ oz instant  rolled oats 
 2 tbsp + 2 tsp  margarine 
 2  eggs 
 1½ oz mixed  dried fruit , sliced 
 ¼ cup dark  raisins 
 1 tsp  confectioner's sugar 
 ¼ cup + 2 tsp firm packed  brown sugar 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 In a small mixing bowl, sift  flour ,  apple pie spice , soda and powder together. 
 Add  oats , stirring to combine; set aside. 
 In a medium mixing bowl,  cream  together  brown sugar  and  margarine  until thoroughly combined. 
 Add  eggs , one at a time, beating until smooth. 
 Gradually beat in sifted ingredients. 
 Fold in dried fruit and  raisins . 
 Spray an 8 x 8 x 2 baking dish with nonstick  cooking spray . 
 Pour batter into baking dish. 
 Bake for 30 minutes, or until toothpick comes out dry. 
 Tranfer to wire rack and let cool. 
 Dust top with  confectioner's sugar . 
 Cut into 8 equally sized bars. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Each serving provides: ½ fruit; 1 fat; ¼ protein; ½ bread; 30 cal.
