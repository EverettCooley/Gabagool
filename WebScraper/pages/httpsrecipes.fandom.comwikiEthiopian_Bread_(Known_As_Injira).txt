 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cup Warm  water 
 2 1/2 cup Self rising  flour  (can't be traditional can it) 
 3 tbl  soda water 
 Directions : 
 The Ethiopians make some very rich and spicy food. Great to serve to people who don't mind rich, buttery sauces and love hot food. 
 In a blender mix  water  and  flour . Blend on slow and then high. Add  soda water  Using a 4 oz. ladle, ladle into a 400 degree electric fry pan. Pour into one side and quickly tip the pan to spread. Cook til the edges curl. 
 DO NOT TURN.
