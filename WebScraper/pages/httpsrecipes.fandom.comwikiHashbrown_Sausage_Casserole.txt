Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 10/29/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  pork sausage 
 2 cups shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 1 (10¾ oz)  cream of chicken  soup 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 1 (8 oz) French onion dip 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 ¼ cup  green  and  red pepper 
 ⅛ tsp  pepper 
 1 (30 oz) frozen shredded  hash browns 
 Directions : 
 Brown and drain sausage. 
 In large bowl combine ¾ cup cheese and next 7 ingredients. 
 Spread into greased 9x13 baking dish. 
 Top with sausage. 
 Sprinkle with remaining cheese. 
 Cover and bake at 350°F for 45 minutes. 
 Uncover and bake another 10 minutes.
