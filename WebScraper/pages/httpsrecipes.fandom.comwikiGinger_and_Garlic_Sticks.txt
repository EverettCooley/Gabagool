Serves: 	serve with ketchup and french fries 
 Occasion: 	Any 
 Effort: 	Easy 
 Ingredients : 
 Chicken  =6-8 leg pieces 
 garlic  paste =1 table spoon 
 ginger = 1 table spoon 
 Onion  paste =1 table spoon 
 green  chilli  paste =1/2 table spoon 
 oil  for deep fry 
 venigar=3 table spoon 
 soy sauce =2 table spoon 
 red  chilli  =1/4 table spoon 
 black peper=1/2 table spoon 
 chat misala=1/2 table spoon 
 salt  =as desired 
 bread crumbs=as desired 
 egg =1 
 Directions : 
 made small cuts on the leg pieces . 
 made mixture of  vinegar / soy sauce /red  chilli / black pepper  /chat misala/ salt  and all the pastes. 
 now put  Chicken  and this mixture in bowl and mix them. 
 marinate  Chicken  for overnight . 
 heat the  oil , put the  Chicken  pieces in  egg , roll in the bread crumbs and fry them.
