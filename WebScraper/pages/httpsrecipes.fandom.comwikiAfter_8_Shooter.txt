      After 8 Shooter     
 Ingredients : 
 ½ shot Stolichnaya  vodka 
 ½ shot De Kuyper  crème de cacao  (white) 
 ½ shot De Kuyper  crème de menthe  (green) 
 glass: shot 
 Directions : 
 Shake all ingredients with  ice  and strain into glass.
