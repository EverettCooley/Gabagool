Description 
 Milk, Nonfat, Dry Non-Instant  by the US Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Serves : 6 
 Ingredients 
 2 tablespoons  cornstarch 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 1  egg 
 2 cups reconstituted  non-fat dry milk 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 Directions 
 Mix cornstarch and sugar in a saucepan. 
 Beat egg in a separate bowl. 
 Add, with milk to cornstarch and sugar mixture. 
 Cook and stir, over medium heat, until mixture is smooth and thickened. 
 Cook for 1 minute. 
 Blend in butter and vanilla. Cool.
