Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 6/10/07
 
 Contributed by Dorie at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs frozen  hash browns  (slightly thawed) 
 ½ cup melted  butter 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 1 can  cream of chicken  or  celery soup 
 8 oz  sour cream 
 2 cups shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 ¾ cup crushed  potato chips 
 Directions : 
 Combine all except ½ cup cheese and potato chips. 
 Pour into 9x13 baking dish. 
 Cover with remaining cheese and potato chips. 
 Baked 1¼ hrs at 350°F.
