 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 1  green pepper , cut into 1-inch squares 
 1½ tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 2 x 10¾-ounce cans condensed  cream of chicken  soup 
 2 cups cooked  turkey  cubes 
 1 cup cooked  ham  cubes 
 1 x 8¼-ounce can crushed  pineapple , drained 
 ⅓ cup toasted sliced  almonds 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 3 tablespoons chopped fresh  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Cook  onion  and  pepper  in  butter  in large saucepan in  butter  until tender crisp. 
 Stir in soup,  turkey ,  ham  and  pineapple ; heat thoroughly. 
 Sprinkle with  almonds . 
 Serve over  rice  tossed with  parsley .
