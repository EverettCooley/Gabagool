 Description : 
 A wonderful dip for Tortilla Chips and watching Football games! Try it, we all love it alot at the household.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 16oz. can of  refried beans 
 1 ripe  California avocado , peeled and cut up 
 1 8oz. package  cream cheese 
 1 Cup  sour cream 
 1 Cup taco sauce (salsa will also work) 
 1 tsp  garlic powder 
 Use as much of the following as you wish: 
 Shedded  lettuce 
 tomato 
 1 can of Sliced  black olives 
 1 Cup or more shredded  Cheddar   Cheese 
 Bag of Tortilla Chips 
 Directions : 
 In a 9x13 pan, spread can of  refried beans 
 In a blender, add the following ingredients:  avocado ,  cream cheese ,  sour cream , taco sauce/salsa, and  garlic powder .
 Blend until smooth and then layer on top of the  refried beans . 
 Cut up the  lettuce ,  tomato ,  black olives , and  Cheese  and layer on top of mixture.
 Refrigerate till chilled and serve with tortilla chips 
 Just dip away. It is a wonderful and filling dip!
 ENJOY! We all do here!
