 Makes 6 cups 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups shredded Monterey  jack cheese 
 1 can chopped  green chilies 
 1 can sliced ripe  olives , drained (2.25 oz) 
 4  green onions , sliced 
 3 medium  tomatoes , seeded and diced 
 Â½ cup minced fresh  parsley 
 1 envelop  Italian salad dressing  mix 
 tortilla chips 
 Directions : 
 In a large mixing bowl, beat together the cheese, chilies, olives, onions, tomatoes and parsley until blended. 
 Prepare salad dressing mix as package directs. 
 Pour over cheese mixture and mix well. 
 Serve at once with tortilla chips.
