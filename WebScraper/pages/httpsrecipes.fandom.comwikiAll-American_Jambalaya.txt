      All American Jambalaya     
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  butter 
 1 lb  ground beef 
 ½ cup  onion , diced 
 ¼ cup  green pepper , diced 
 1 (28 ounces) can diced  tomatoes 
 1 small  bay leaf 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  sugar 
 1½ cups  Minute rice 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter  in large  skillet . 
 Sauté  onion ,  green pepper , and  beef  until browned. 
 Drain fat. 
 Drain  tomatoes , and add enough  water  to juice to make 1½ cups. 
 Add  tomatoes ,  bay leaf ,  salt ,  sugar , and liquid. 
 Bring to a slow boil. 
 Cover and simmer for 5 minutes. 
 Add  rice . 
 Cover and  simmer  another 5 minutes.
