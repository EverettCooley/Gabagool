Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Gingham Estate in Fort Worth, Texas in 1992.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 8 ounces  cream cheese  softened 
 2 tablespoons  skim milk 
 ½ cup  sour cream 
 2 tablespoons  green onions  sliced 
 2 tablespoons  green bell pepper  chopped 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 1 jar sliced  dried beef  rinsed drained and finely chopped 
 ¼ cup fresh  parsley  chopped 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 350°F. 
 In medium bowl combine  cream cheese  and  milk  then beat until smooth. 
 Stir in remaining ingredients except  parsley  and mix well. 
 Spoon and spread mixture in ungreased pie pan then bake at 350 for 15 minutes. 
 Sprinkle with  parsley  and serve immediately with crackers.
