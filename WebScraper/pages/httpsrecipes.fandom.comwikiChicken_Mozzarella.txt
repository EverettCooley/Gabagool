Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 8/5/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 ⅔ cup  Bisquick 
 2 tbsp grated  Parmesan cheese 
 2 tsp  Italian seasoning 
 1 tsp  paprika 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 6 large  chicken breast  halves 
 ⅔ cup  chili sauce 
 ¾ cup  mozzarella  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Brush bottom of 9x13 baking dish with oil. 
 Mix Bisquick, Parmesan cheese, Italian seasoning, paprika and pepper; coat chicken. 
 Arrange skin sides down in pan. 
 Bake 45 minutes. 
 Turn chicken; divide chili sauce among breast halves. 
 Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese. 
 Bake until melted, about 5 minutes longer. 
 Sprinkle with parsley if desired.
