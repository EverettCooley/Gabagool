 Name Variations : 
 black lentils 
 black beluga lentils 
 About Beluga lentil : 
 Beluga lentils are black. They tend to glisten when they're cooked, which makes them look like beluga caviar.  They're great in soups or salads. If you can't find these,  French green lentils  are a good substitute.
