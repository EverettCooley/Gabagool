Description : 
 Serve hot as a meat accompaniment. Makes 6 - 8 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x 11-ounce can  mandarin orange  segments, drained 
 ¾ cup chopped  celery 
 ½ cup chopped  chutney 
 ⅓ cup flaked  coconut 
 ¼ cup chopped  nuts 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 cup  heavy cream , whipped 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Fold  oranges ,  celery , chutney,  coconut ,  nuts  and  salt  into  whipped cream . 
 Spoon  rice  into greased 2-quart baking dish; spread  cream  mixture on top. 
 Bake at 350 °F for 30 minutes.
