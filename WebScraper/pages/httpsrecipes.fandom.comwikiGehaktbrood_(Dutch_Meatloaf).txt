 Description : 
 Dutch Meatloaf is a staple ingredient in many meals, although nowadays it has mostly been replaced by Dutch Meat balls.  Beef  is often replaced by  Pork , but the following recipe is for "half om half", or half  Beef  and half  Pork .
 
 Ingredients : 
 500gr ground  Beef 
 500gr ground  Pork 
 2 medium  potatoes 
 1  egg 
 2 teaspoons ground  nutmeg , to taste 
 1 1/2 teaspoons  salt , to taste 
 2 tablespoons  parsley  flakes 
 1 teaspoon  black pepper  or  white pepper 
 Directions : 
 Chop skinned  potatoes  into small pieces and boil until soft. 
 Pre-heat oven to 180C. 
 Place in bowl and mash the  potatoes . 
 Add all other ingredients and knead with hands until mixed. 
 Mold into an ungreased 9x13x2 pan and bake for 1 hour. 
 Loaf will pull away from sides of pan and be surrounded by fat from the meat (more or less depending on the fat content of the meat you used). 
 Cut into slices and serve with  potatoes  and vegetables.
