      Coarse Sugar     
 Name Variations : 
 Sanding sugar 
 Coarse sugar 
 About Decorator's sugar : 
 Decorator's sugar  is a large crystal sugar that is sprinkled on top of baked goods, usually before baking. They reflect light and give the product a sparkling appearance.
