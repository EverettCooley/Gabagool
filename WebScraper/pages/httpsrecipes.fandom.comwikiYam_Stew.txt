 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 medium  yam 
 4  carrots 
 1 cup cooked beans 
 1 tsp  curry  powder 
 2  onions 
 1/2 tsp  salt 
 2 cups  water 
 1 tablespoon fat 
 Directions : 
 Wash peel and cube the  yam . 
 Scrape the  carrots  and cut into thin rounds. 
 Peel and slice the  Onion  and fry for a few minutes. 
 Add  curry  powder and  yams  and fry gently then add  carrots , beans and  water  and simmer until cooked. 
 Serve with ugali,  rice , mashed  bananas  or crushed  maize .
