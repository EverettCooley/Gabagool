Ingredients : 
 1 medium  cow foot 
 2 Irish  potatoes  (diced) 
 1 large  carrot  (chopped) 
 8 - 10  spice seeds 
 1 tsp (5ml)  salt 
 ¼ lb  macaroni  (broken into small pieces) 
 1  onion  (sliced) 
 2 plugs  garlic  (crushed) 
 1 can  vegetable soup  (optional) 
 ½ tsp (2 ml)  black pepper 
 ½ tsp (2 ml)  thyme 
 ½ - 1 lb  tripe  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Clean cow foot well. Boil for about 15 minutes and throw off the  water  (to make less sticky). 
 Add fresh  water  and spice seeds. Boil with tripe until tender. (Use a pressure cooker if available). 
 Add  potatoes ,  carrots ,  macaroni ,  onion  and  garlic . 
 Season to taste with  salt ,  pepper  and  thyme . 
 Add soup. Cook for about 3 – 5 minutes more. Serve hot.
