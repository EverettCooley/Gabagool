 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 ingredients: 
 4 cup dry bread cubes 
 1 cup  celery , chopped 
 1  Onion , chopped 
 2 tbsp  parsley  leaves 
 -freshly chopped 
 1 cl  garlic , minced 
 1 cup hot  water   or  1 cup  Turkey  broth 
 1/4 tsp  black pepper 
 3/4 tsp ground  sage 
 1/4 tsp dried  marjoram 
 1/2 tsp dried  thyme 
 Directions : 
 Directions: combine all the ingredients in a bowl. Toss to mix well. Stuff into a 10-pound  Turkey  or four cornish hens. Serves 4 one serving = calories: 100 carbohydrates: 20 protein: 5 fat: 2 sodium: 199 potassium: 177 cholesterol: 0 exchange value: 1 bread exchange + 1 vegetable exchang
