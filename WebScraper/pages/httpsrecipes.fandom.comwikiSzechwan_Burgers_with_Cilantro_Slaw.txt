Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source:  Southern Living  2007 Cookoff Winner, Category Quick Weeknight Favorites 
 Prep: 20 minutes | Chil: 30 minutes | Grill: 10 minutes 
 Makes 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅓ cup Kraft cucumber  ranch dressing 
 2 tbsp minced fresh  cilantro 
 2 tsp  orange juice 
 1 tsp  kosher salt , divided 
 1½ cups loosely packed shredded  cole slaw  mix 
 ½ cup diced  green onion 
 1 tbsp  black sesame seeds 
 vegetable  cooking spray  for grilling 
 1½ lb  ground chicken  breast 
 ¼ cup Kraft Asian toasted sesame dressing 
 2 tsp minced  garlic 
 1½ tsp  Szechwan seasoning 
 6 sesame seed  hamburger buns 
 Directions : 
 Whisk together first 3 ingredients and ¼ tsp salt in a large bowl. 
 Stir in coleslaw mix, green onions, and sesame seeds. 
 Cover and chill at least 30 minutes, up to 2 hours. 
 Coat a cold cooking grate with cooking spray and place on grill. 
 Preheat grill to 300°F to 350°F (medium). 
 Combine ground chicken, next 3 ingredients, and remaining ¾ tsp salt in a large bowl until blended. 
 Shape into 6 (¾-inch) patties. 
 Grill patties, covered with grill lid, 5 minutes on each side or until done. 
 Grill buns cut side down 1 – 2 minutes until lightly toasted. 
 Place 1 burger on top of each bottom bun; top evenly with slaw mixture, and tops of buns.
