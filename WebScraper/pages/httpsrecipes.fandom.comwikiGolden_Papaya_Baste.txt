Ingredients : 
 9 oz fresh  papaya , mashed 
 ¼ tsp  ginger  powdered 
 1 tbsp  honey 
 2 tbsp  white vinegar  or  fruit juice 
 2 tbsp  salad oil 
 2 tbsp  butter , melted 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients. 
 Simmer 10 – 15 minutes. 
 Use to coat  chicken  or  pork  before baking in oven.
