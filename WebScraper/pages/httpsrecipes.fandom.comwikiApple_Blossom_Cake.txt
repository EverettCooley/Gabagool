Apple  Blossom Cake
 
 3 c  all-purpose flour 
 1 tb  baking powder 
 1 1/2 ts  baking soda 
 1 ts Cloves 
 1 ts  nutmeg 
 1 ts  cinnamon 
 1 ts  salt 
 2 c  sugar 
 1 1/2 c Vegetable Cooking  oil 
 1 ts  vanilla extract 
 3  eggs 
 3 c  Apples  -- Diced And Unpared 
 1 c  pecans  -- Chopped 
 
Sift Dry Ingredients Into Large Bowl. Pour  oil ,  vanilla  And  eggs  Into Mixture. Beat With Spoon Until Mixed. Add  Apples  And  pecans . Place in Greased And Floured Tube Pan. Bake at 325 For 1 Hour And 15 Minutes. Cool Cake on Wire Rack.
