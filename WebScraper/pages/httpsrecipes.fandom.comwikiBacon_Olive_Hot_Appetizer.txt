Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Pernice Estate in Mesquite, Texas in 1988.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 6 slices  bacon  fried drained and crumbled 
 1 cup ripe  olives  chopped 
 ½ cup  green onion  tops chopped 
 1½ cups sharp  cheddar  cheese shredded 
 1 teaspoon  curry 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 12 slices  bread 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients except  bread . 
 Toast  bread  on one side. 
 Spread mixture on untoasted side then broil until brown.
