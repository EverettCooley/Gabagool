Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Steep Estate in Elmo, Texas in 1989.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 8 ounces medium  noodles 
 1½ cups creamed  cottage cheese 
 1½ cups dairy  sour cream 
 ½ cup white  onion  chopped 
 1 clove  garlic  minced 
 ½ teaspoon  Worcestershire sauce 
 ¼ teaspoon  red pepper sauce 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 16 ounce can drained  salmon 
 ½ cup shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 325°F. 
 Cook noodles as directed on package then drain. 
 Mix noodles,  cottage cheese ,  sour cream ,  onion ,  garlic ,  Worcestershire sauce ,  red pepper sauce ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Add drained salmon then place salmon mixture in greased casserole and sprinkle with  cheese . 
 Bake for 40 minutes.
