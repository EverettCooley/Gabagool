Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 3 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 1 pound beef  top round steak , cut across grain ¼ inch thick 
 1¼ tsp  salt 
 2  sweet green peppers , seeded, cut in ½ inch thick strips 
 2  sweet potatoes , peeled, quartered lengthwise, sliced crosswise ¼ inch thick 
 1 large  onion , thinly sliced 
 ½ tsp  curry powder 
 ¼ tsp ground  ginger 
 ⅛ tsp  ground red pepper 
 ½ cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Heat 2 tbsp oil in skillet. 
 Season meat with ¼ tsp salt. 
 Sauté 2 minutes each side. 
 Remove, keep warm. 
 Add remaining oil to skillet. 
 Sauté peppers, potatoes, onion 6 minutes. 
 Add curry, ginger, red pepper, remaining salt, sauté 2 minutes. 
 Add water, cover, cook 6 minutes or until potato is tender. 
 Add meat, heat. 
 Serve.
