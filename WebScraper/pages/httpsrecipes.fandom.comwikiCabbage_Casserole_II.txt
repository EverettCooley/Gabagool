Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  cabbage 
 1 lb browned  ground meat 
 1 lb smoked  sausage 
 1  onion 
 1  bell pepper 
 1 raw  egg 
 1 cup  rice  (raw) 
 1 can stewed  tomatoes 
 ½ cup ragu sauce 
 Directions : 
 Brown and drain ground meat. 
 Mix all ingredients together in large casserole dish covered with foil. 
 Bake at 350°F for 2 hours. 
 After 1 hour mix well and bake 1 more hour. 
 Enjoy!
