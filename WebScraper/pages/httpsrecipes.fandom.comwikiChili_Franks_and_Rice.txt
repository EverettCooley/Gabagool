Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup chopped  Onion 
 1 12-ounce package frankfurters, cut into 1-inch pieces 
 1 16-ounce can  chili without beans 
 1 15-ounce can kidney or  pinto beans 
 2 teaspoons  chili powder 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon prepared  mustard 
 5 to 6 drops hot pepper sauce 
 1/3 cup  sour cream 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Combine  Onion  and frankfurters in 10-inch skillet. Cook over medium heat, stirring, for 3 to 4 minutes. 
 Add  chili , beans,  chili powder ,  salt ,  mustard  and  pepper  sauce. 
 Cook, covered, 5 minutes longer or until bubbly hot. Stir in  sour cream . 
 Serve over beds of fluffy  rice .
