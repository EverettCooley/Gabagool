 Description : 
 "This method of preparing  carrots  will have us all eating like Bugs Bunny! Preparation time: 10 minutes. This recipe is from The WEBB Cooks, articles and recipes by Robyn Webb, courtesy of the American Diabetes Association." Original recipe yield: 6 servings/ serving size: 1/2 large  carrot .
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 large  carrots , halved 
 3 tablespoons light soy sauce 
 3 tablespoons  rice vinegar 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 2 teaspoons  sesame oil 
 2 teaspoons minced  fresh ginger  root 
 1/4 teaspoon  Chinese five-spice powder 
 Directions : 
 1 - Add  carrots  to boiling  water  for 5 minutes
2 - Add the  carrots  to the rest of the ingredients and let sit for 45 minutes
3 - Cook on barbecue or grill for 10 minutes and serve.
