 Prep Time: 5 minutes 
 Cook time: 10 minutes 
 Serves: 3 
 
 Description : 
 The taste of a buffalo hot wing in burger form.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  ground chuck 
 Buffalo sauce (equal parts  butter  and  hot sauce ) 
 1 cup  coleslaw 
 ¼ crumbled  blue cheese 
 salt 
 pepper 
 hamburger buns 
 Directions : 
 Heat griddle or fry pan to medium high heat 
 Form burger into 3⅓ lb patties 
 Season patties with salt and pepper. 
 Fry each side of patty to personal tastes 
 Dip each patty in buffalo sauce. 
 Mix crumbled blue cheese with coleslaw 
 Assemble burger with bun, burger, blue cheese coleslaw mixture and top with another bun
