       Potet Lefse     
 Potato lefse
 
 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups (9½ dl)  potatoes , cooked 
 1 tsp.  salt 
 ½ cup (1¼ dl)  whipping cream 
 ½ cup (1¼ dl) Crisco  oil 
 2 t  sugar 
 1½ cups (3½ dl)  flour 
 Directions : 
 Boil and  rice   potatoes , add  salt ,  cream  and  oil  to warm  potatoes . 
 Cool the  potatoes  before adding  flour . 
 Cut down a little on the  cream  and  oil . 
 Mix  sugar  with  flour  and add, kneading as you roll them out with a grooved rolling pin. 
 Bake on takke, round griddle.
