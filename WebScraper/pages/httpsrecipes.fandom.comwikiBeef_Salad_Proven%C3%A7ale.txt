Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  beef  roast, cut into thin strips 
 3 cups cooked  rice , cooled 
 1½ cups sliced  celery 
 1 cup sliced  green onions 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 ½ cup  sour cream 
 ½ teaspoon seasoned  pepper 
 1 teaspoon prepared  horseradish 
 1 teaspoon  Dijon mustard 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 2 large  tomatoes , cut into eighths 
 Directions : 
 Combine roast  beef ,  rice ,  celery  and  onions . 
 Blend  mayonnaise ,  sour cream ,  pepper , horseradish,  mustard  and  salt . 
 Stir into  rice  mixture; chill. 
 Garnish with  tomato  wedges and  cucumber  slices, if desired.
