Description : 
 This is a great vegetarian dish that mixes sweet and savoury. It is best served with  rice  or  couscous . This recipe serves 4.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  onion , sliced 
 1  onion , chopped 
 1  garlic  clove 
 2 tablespoons  peanut oil 
 1 cup diced  butternut squash 
 1 cup seasoned  broth 
 ½ cup  raisins 
 1 teaspoon  ground turmeric 
 1 teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 ½ teaspoon ground  ginger 
 15 oz  garbanzo beans 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Put the  onions  and  garlic  into a large pan with the  peanut oil  and cook for about 8 minutes, stirring constantly. 
 Except for the  garbanzo beans , add the remaining ingredients to the pan. Make sure it is on a high heat to start with and reduce the heat after a couple of minutes. 
 Cover the mixture and cook for about 8 minutes, until the  butternut squash  is tender. 
 Finally, stir in the  garbanzo beans . 
 Serve!
