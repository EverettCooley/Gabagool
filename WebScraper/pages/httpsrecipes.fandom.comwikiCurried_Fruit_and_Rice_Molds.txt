Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup seedless  raisins 
 1/4 cup chopped dried  apricots 
 1 tablespoon  Sugar 
 1/2 cup dry  sherry 
 1 teaspoon  curry powder 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 2 tablespoons melted  butter 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 1/4 cup toasted slivered  almonds 
 Directions : 
 Combine  Raisins ,  apricots ,  Sugar  and  sherry  in large saucepan. 
 Bring to a boil; remove from heat and allow to stand about 10 minutes. 
 Blend  curry  powder and  salt  into  butter . 
 Combine fruit,  rice ,  butter  mixture and  almonds ; toss lightly. 
 Press into greased individual molds; invert onto serving dish.
