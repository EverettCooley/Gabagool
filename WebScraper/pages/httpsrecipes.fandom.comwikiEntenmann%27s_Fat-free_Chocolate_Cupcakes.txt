Description : 
 Source: Waldine van Geffen
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 small box Jell-O Cook and Serve  chocolate pudding powder 
 ½ cup  non-fat dry milk  powder 
 1 tbsp unsweetened Hershey's  cocoa 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 1 cup  self-rising flour 
 4  egg whites , beat with 
 1 pinch  salt  in 1½ qt bowl 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 4 oz  applesauce 
 ¼ tsp  baking soda 
 Directions : 
 In medium mixing bowl combine Jell-O, dry  milk ,  cocoa ,  sugar  and flour. 
 Set aside. 
 With electric mixer, beat alternately into the egg white mixture a cup at a time with the vanilla, applesauce and baking soda, which have been mixed together. 
 Beat 2 minutes after last addition. 
 Divide batter equally between 12 paper-line cupcake wells. 
 Bake at 350°F about 18 – 20 minutes or until tester comes out clean. 
 Cool in pan on wire rack 10 minutes then remove. 
 Enjoy.
