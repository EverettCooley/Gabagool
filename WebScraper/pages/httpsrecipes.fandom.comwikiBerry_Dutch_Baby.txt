Berry Dutch Baby
 
Low Fat
 
Serving Size : 4
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 lg  egg whites 
 2 lg  eggs 
 1 c  all-purpose flour 
 1 c Nonfat  milk 
 2 tb  granulated sugar 
 2 ts  vanilla 
 1/2 ts  salt 
 1 tb  butter  or  margarine 
 3 1/2 c  raspberries ,  strawberries ,  blueberries , or a Combo 
 1/4 ts  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Blend Together The  egg  Whites,  eggs ,  flour ,  milk , sugar ,  vanilla , And  salt  Until Smooth. 
 Melt  butter  InA 10- to 12-Inch Nonstick Ovenproof Frying Pan in a 425 Degree Oven. Tilt The Pan to Coat it Evenly. #PourThe Batter Into The Pan And Bake Until Puffy And Well-Browned (about 25 Minutes). 
 In a Bowl, Combine The  Berries  And  cinnamon . SpoonThe Moisture Onto The Dutch Baby And Sprinkle With 
 powdered sugar . (4 Servings)
 
Enjoy.
