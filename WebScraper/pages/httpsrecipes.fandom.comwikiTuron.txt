 Description : 
 Turon , or the fried banana roll,  is one of the very popular  merienda  (snack) of the Filipinos. It is rolled with paper-thin wrapper ( lumpia  wrapper). After it is rolled, it is coated with  sugar ; then it is deep-fried, so it gives the crunch to the pastry.
 
       Turon     
 Ingredients : 
 12 bananas, peeled and cut into half, lengthwise 
 1  â…ž  cup  brown sugar 
 3 cups cooking  oil 
 24  lumpia  wrappers (spring roll) 
 2 cups  langka  ( jackfruit ) 
 Directions : 
 Roll each  banana  in  sugar . Wrap  banana  and  langka /jackfruit in  lumpia  wrapper. 
 Fry in hot  oil  until golden and crispy. Place the fried banana rolls on a plate (they will stick to paper towels) and add more  sugar  on top, if desired. 
 Serve hot and plain or with other fruits or desserts.
