Ingredients : 
 1 lb dry-roasted large  peanuts 
 4 pieces  star anise 
 2 tsp  five spice powder 
 1 tbsp  salt 
 2 tsp  granulated sugar 
 4 cups  water 
 Directions : 
 Put all ingredients in a large saucepan, and bring mixture to a boil over medium heat. 
 Reduce heat to low, and cook for 30 minutes, uncovered. 
 Remove from heat, strain  peanuts  and reserve liquid. 
 Set  peanuts  aside to cool. 
 Serve  peanuts  with several tablespoons reserved liquid.
