      Bhel Puri     
 Ingredients : 
 puffed rice  or  rice  krispies 
 gram flour   vermicelli 
 ¼ tsp  turmeric powder 
 ¼ tsp  rock salt 
 boiled  potatoes 
 1 tsp  oil 
 puris 
 Date and tamarind chutney : 
 4 oz of  dates 
 1 tsp of  tamarind  paste 
 2 tsp  sugar 
 ½ tsp  cumin  powder 
 ½ tsp  coriander powder 
 Directions : 
 Heat 1 tsp  oil  and add  rock salt  and  turmeric  powder. 
 Add  puffed rice  and on a very slow fire roast them for 5 minutes. 
 Add shev,  potatoes ,  onions  and crush puries into the above mix. 
 Date and tamarind chutney : 
 Take the dates and cook in microwave oven for 5 minutes. 
 Add  tamarind  paste,  Sugar ,  cumin  powder and coriander powder. 
 Blend to a fine paste in a liquidiser. 
 Tips : 
 onions  should be finely chopped. 
 A dash of  lime juice  is recommended.
