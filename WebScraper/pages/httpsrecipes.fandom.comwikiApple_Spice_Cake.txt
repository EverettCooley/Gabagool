Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  all-purpose flour 
 1 tsp ground  cinnamon 
 1 tsp ground  nutmeg 
 1 tsp ground  allspice 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 1 cup  butter  or  margarine , softened 
 2 cups  granulated sugar 
 4  eggs 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 1 tbsp warm  water 
 1 tsp  vanilla extract 
 3  apples , peeled and diced 
 ½ cup  raisins , soaked in warm water 
 confectioners' sugar 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 Butter a 10 inch tube or bundt pan. 
 Blend together flour, spices, and salt. 
 Cream the butter and sugar. 
 Mix in eggs and vanilla. 
 Stir together baking soda and 1 tablespoon warm water, then mix into the sugar mixture. 
 Stir in flour mixture, diced apples, and strained raisins until well blended. 
 Pour batter into prepared pan. 
 Bake for 1 hour, or until a toothpick comes out clean. 
 Cool in pan. 
 Once cool, turn onto plate, and dust with confectioners' sugar.
