 About Dabberlocks : 
 Wikipedia Article  About Dabberlocks on Wikipedia 
 Dabberlocks (Alaria esculenta), also called Winged kelp or Bladderlochs, is an edible seaweed. It is a traditional food along the coasts of the far north Atlantic Ocean.
