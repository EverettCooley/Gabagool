Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound lean ground  Beef 
 2 cups cooked  rice 
 1 12-ounce can whole kernel  corn , drained 
 1 cup finely chopped  Onion 
 1 tablespoon  chili powder 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1/4 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 1 14-1/2- to 16-ounce can  tomatoes  or 2 cups 
 1 cup grated  Cheddar   Cheese 
 Directions : 
 Combine  Beef ,  rice ,  corn ,  Onion ,  chili powder ,  salt ,  pepper ,  garlic powder  and  tomatoes ; mix well. Turn into buttered shallow 2-quart casserole. Cover and bake at 375 degrees 25 minutes. Remove cover and bake 20 minutes longer. Sprinkle with  Cheese  and serve with  corn  chips and raw  Onion  rings, if desired.
