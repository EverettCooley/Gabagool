 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/4 c. real  Mayonnaise 
 3 T. grated  Onion 
 3 T. ketchup 
 3 T. honey 
 3 tsp.  lemon juice 
 3 tsp. good  curry  powder 
 3 drops  Tabasco sauce 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in blender or food processor. 
 Store tightly covered in refrigerator. 
 Serve with assorted veggies - this is especially good with raw  Cauliflower  and steamed  Brussels sprouts .
