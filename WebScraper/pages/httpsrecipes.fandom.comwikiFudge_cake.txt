 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 cup  flour 
 1 1/2 cup  buttermilk 
 1 1/4 cup  Sugar 
 1/2 cup  cocoa 
 1/4 cup diet  margarine 
 2 tbsp diet  margarine 
 2 tbsp  margarine 
 1 tsp  margarine 
 2 large  eggs 
 2 tsp  baking soda 
 1/2 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  vanilla extract 
 8 tsp  powdered sugar 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350í. Spray bundt pan with pam.  cream  margarines and  Sugar . Add  eggs . Combine dry ingredients and add to mixture. Add  buttermilk . Mix well and pour into sprayed bundt pan. Bake 40 minutes at 350í. Cool 15 minutes and then remove from pan. Sprinkle with the  powdered sugar  before serving
