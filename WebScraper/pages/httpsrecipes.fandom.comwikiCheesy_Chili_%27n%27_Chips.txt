 Serves 4 to 6 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  ground beef 
 1 can  tomatoes  (16 oz) 
 1 can  tomato paste  (6 oz) 
 1 tbsp  chili powder 
 1 tbsp minced  onions 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 1½ cups shredded sharp  cheddar  cheese 
 2 cups  corn chips 
 Directions : 
 Brown meat; drain. 
 Stir in tomatoes, tomato paste, and seasonings. 
 Cover. 
 Let simmer 10 minutes. 
 Add 1 cup cheese. 
 Stir until melted. 
 Arrange corn chips on platter. 
 Spoon chili over chips. 
 Top with remaining cheese.
