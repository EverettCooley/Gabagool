 Ingredients : 
 1 (16 ounce) can  black beans , drained 
 14 ounce mild salsa 
 1 teaspoon  chili powder 
 40 ounces thin cut French fries 
 4 ounces  jalapeño pepper  rings 
 16 ounces nacho  Cheese  sauce 
 4 ounces  sour cream 
 4 ounces guacamole 
 8 fresh  cilantro  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Mix  black beans , salsa, and  chili powder . Set aside. 
 Prepare French fries according to package directions. 
 Divide fries among 8 warm plates. 
 Top each with sliced  jalapeño pepper  rings,  black bean  mixture, and nacho  Cheese  sauce. 
 Garnish side of plate with  sour cream  and guacamole. 
 Top each with a  cilantro  leaf.
