Ingredients : 
 1 qt. canned  apple juice 
 5½ cups  sugar 
 5 drops of red  food coloring 
 1 pkg powdered  pectin 
 2 tsp  cinnamon 
 ½ tsp  cloves 
 ⅛ tsp  allspice 
 red  food coloring 
 Directions : 
 Combine juice, sugar and spices in large saucepan; bring to a boil, stirring constantly until sugar is dissolved. 
 Boil for 2 minutes. 
 Add food coloring and pectin; bring to a rolling boil for ½ minute. 
 Use enough  food coloring  to make the jelly deep red. 
 Remove from heat; skim. 
 Pour into hot sterilized decorative jars; seal with paraffin. 
 Label with a Christmas seal and store in a dark, cool place.
