 Description : 
 Stuffed  zucchini 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 32 oz medium  zucchini 
 7 oz minced meat 
 1 cup short  grain   rice , washed 
 32 oz red  tomatoes , peeled and chopped 
 2 tspns  salt 
 a dash of ground  allspice  and black  pepper  (optional) 
 a dash of ground  cinnamon  (optional) 
 1 tbls  shortening  or  butter 
 5 cloves  garlic , crushed with a dash of  salt 
 2 cups  water 
 4 tbls  lemon  juice 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Wash well  zucchini .
Remove stems using a knife.
Hollow out using an  Apple  corer.
Wash  zucchini  inside and out. Drain.
Filling: Mix minced meat with  rice .
Stir in  salt  and spices.
Stuff  zucchini , shake each after filling it so that the filling is well distributed (do not over stuff). 
Arrange in a pot. 
Invert a plate on top.
Strain  tomatoes  using a colander. 
Add 2 cups of  water ,  shortening , and a dash of  salt . 
Place  tomato juice  in a pot over medium heat. 
When it boils, add to  zucchini . 
Cook for 1 hour, or until tender. 
Add crushed  garlic  and  lemon  juice. 
Cook for 5 minutes.
Serve  zucchini  and sauce hot separately or together in a large bowl.
