Description : 
 Contributed by Barb in Ocala, Florida via  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 6 – 8 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  ground beef 
 1  onion , chopped 
 1 – 2 cups  celery , chopped 
 ½ cup uncooked  instant rice 
 1 can  chicken with rice soup 
 1 can  cream of mushroom  soup 
 1 – 4 tbsp  soy sauce 
 ½ – 1 can  chow mein noodles 
 2 tbsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 Directions : 
 Brown ground beef with onion and celery. 
 Add rice, soups, water, soy sauce and Worcestershire sauce. 
 Stir. 
 Pour into a greased baking dish. 
 Top with chow mien noodles. 
 Bake at 350°F for 1½ – 2 hours.
