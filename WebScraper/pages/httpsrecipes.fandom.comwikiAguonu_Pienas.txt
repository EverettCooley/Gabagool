Description : 
 Poppy seed milk
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  poppy seeds 
 1 quart of  water 
 ½ cup  sugar  or honey (more or less to taste) 
 Directions : 
 Pour boiling  water  over  poppy seeds  to cover. 
 Let set for 4 hours. 
 Drain. 
 Process scalded  poppy seeds  in food processor, food mill or grinder. 
 Pour the quart of boiled  water  over the processed  poppy seeds . 
 Add  sugar  or honey and stir until  sugar  dissolves. 
 Chill the  milk . 
 Can be served strained or unstrained.
