Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Keller Estate in Arlington, Texas in 1994.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 box yellow cake mix 
 ½ cup packed  light brown sugar 
 1 teaspoon  cinnamon 
 1 cup ripe  bananas  mashed 
 1 cup water 
 ⅓ cup  vegetable oil 
 3  eggs 
 Caramel Frosting : 
 1 stick  butter 
 ½ cup packed  light brown sugar 
 ½ cup packed  dark brown sugar 
 ¼ cup  milk 
 2 cups  powdered sugar  sifted 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Lightly grease and flour rectangular cake pan. 
 Place cake mix, brown sugar and cinnamon in a mixing bowl. 
 Add bananas, water, oil and eggs then blend on low speed for 1 minute and scrape sides down then increase speed to medium and mix 2 minutes. 
 Pour into pan and bake for 35 minutes. 
 Allow to cool completely then make frosting. 
 Prepare frosting by placing butter and brown sugars in a heavy saucepan over medium heat then stir and cook until mixture comes to a boil. 
 Add milk then stir and bring back to boil. 
 Remove pan from heat and add the powdered sugar and vanilla. 
 Beat with a whisk until smooth then immediately pour over cooled cake. 
 Garnish with toasted pecans.
