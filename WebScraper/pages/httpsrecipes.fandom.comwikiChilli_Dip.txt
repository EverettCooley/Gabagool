 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 10 red chillies, 
 6 cloves  garlic  chopped, 
 1  Onion  chopped, 
 1 tablespoon  tomato   ketchup , 
 1/2 teaspoon  soy sauce , 
 1/2 teaspoon  vinegar , 
 1 tablespoon  oil 
 Directions : 
 Remove the seeds from the chillies. 
 Soak them in lukewarm  water  for half an hour. 
 Put all the ingredients in a mixer and grind to a fine paste. 
 If required, add a little  water . 
 This dip tastes even better if ground using a traditional mortar and pestle.
