Ingredients : 
 2 cups cooked  sorghum 
 1 cup  peanuts  (raw or roasted) 
 2 tbsp  oil 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 ½ cup of  stock 
 Directions : 
 Sauté sorghum in  oil , stirring to separate  grains . 
 Lower heat, add stock and  peanuts , and seasoning to taste. 
 Cover tightly and simmer for 20 minutes.
