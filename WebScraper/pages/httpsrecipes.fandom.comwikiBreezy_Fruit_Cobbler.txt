 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  egg 
 3 tbsp.  milk 
 1/3 cup  shortening 
 1/2 tsp.  salt 
 1 1/2 tsp.  baking powder 
 2 tbsp.  Sugar 
 1 cup  flour 
 1 cup  blueberries 
 5 cup combo, sliced  mangos ,  peaches ,  nectarines  & plums 
 3/4 tsp.  cinnamon 
 3 tbsp.  cornstarch 
 1 cup  Sugar 
 Directions : 
 Mix 1 cup  Sugar ,  cornstarch  and  cinnamon  in 3 qt. saucepan. Stir in  mangos ,  peaches ,  nectarines  & plums. Cook stirring constantly, until mixture thickens and boils for 1 minute. Mix in  blueberries . Pour into ungreased 8" square pan. Mix  flour , 2 tbsp  Sugar ,  baking powder  and  salt . Cut in  shortening . Mix in  milk  and  egg . Drop dough onto hot fruit mixture. Bake at 375' 25â€“30 minutes. Serve warm.
