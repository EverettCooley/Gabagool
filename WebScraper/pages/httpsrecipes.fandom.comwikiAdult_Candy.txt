Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  cinnamon schnapps 
 ¼ cup  peach schnapps 
 2 red  apples 
 basil  or  mint  leaves for garnish 
 Directions : 
 In shallow serving bowl combine schnapps stirring till mixed. 
 Core  apples  then slice in thin wedges and place in bowl mix to coat. 
 This can be done 15 minutes to hours in advance, just keep  apple  slices moist on both sides. 
 Garnish with leaves and serve with toothpicks. 
 The  apples  are gone long before the candy my sister spent hours on. 
 They can also be used to accent other dishes.
