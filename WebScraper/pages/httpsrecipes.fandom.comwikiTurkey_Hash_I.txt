Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup chopped  onion 
 1 each diced cooked  turkey  (no skin) 
 1 ½ cup cooked, diced  potatoes 
 ¼ cup shredded,  American cheese 
 2 tbsp reduced-calorie  margarine 
 1 can  cream of celery  soup 
 ⅔ cup cooked  green peas 
 paprika 
 Directions : 
 Sauté  onion  in  margarine  in non-stick skillet until tender. 
 Add  turkey , soup,  potatoes  and  peas  place mixture in 1-qt. 
 Nonstick casserole top with shredded  cheese  and  paprika  bake at 350°F for 30 minutes.
