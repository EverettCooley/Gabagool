 Description : 
 Oblong rissoles
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 kg minced meat (60%  pork  and 40%  veal ) 
 2  onions 
 ½ tsp  cumin  and  pepper 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 salt  the meat and let stand for 1–2 hours in the cold. 
 Then mix with  cumin ,  pepper , finely chopped  onions  (and some  water ). 
 Let stand for another 2 hours. 
 Form into oblong rissoles and grill.
