Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1¾ cups  graham crackers  crumbs (24 squares, crushed) 
 ½ cup  butter , melted 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1  egg yolk 
 24 ounces  cream cheese 
 2 tablespoons  flour 
 ¼ cup  hazelnut liqueur 
 ¼ cup finely chopped  hazelnuts 
 2  eggs 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Combine crackers and nuts, stir in butter. 
 Press mixture onto bottom and about 2 inches up sides of spring form pan. 
 Set aside. 
 In a large mixing bowl, beat cream cheese, sugar, flour and vanilla. 
 Add eggs and yolk all at once, beating on low speed until combined, stir in liqueur. 
 Pour filling into crust. 
 Place in shallow baking pan in oven. 
 Bake in a 375°F oven for 45 to 50 minutes. 
 Cool on wire rack. 
 Loosen sides of pan, cool 1 hour, cover and chill at least 4 hours.
