Makes 4 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/3 cup  sliced almonds 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 2 cups cooked  rice 
 1  orange , peeled, sliced and quartered 
 1/3 cup flaked  coconut , toasted 
 1/2 teaspoon  lemon peel 
 1/2 teaspoon  orange peel 
 1/4 teaspoon  salt 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  white pepper 
 2 tablespoons  orange juice 
 1 teaspoon  lemon juice 
 1 teaspoon  lime juice 
 Directions : 
 Cook  almonds  in  butter  in large skillet over medium-high heat until lightly browned. 
 Add  rice ,  orange ,  coconut ,  lemon peel ,  orange peel ,  salt ,  pepper ,  orange juice ,  lemon juice  and  lime juice ; stir until thoroughly heated.
