 Description : 
The perfect way to start the day - a big Mexican take on bread, salsa, and real cheese is great for appetizers or side dishes and is toasted to golden perfection.       Mexican Bruschetta     
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup salsa 
 french bread or baguette 
 1 cup cheese (Cheddar, American, Colby, or Monterey Jack only) 
 fresh cilantro 
 Directions : 
 Lightly toast sliced French bread in a broiler. 
 Top with shredded cheese. Broil until melted. 
 Top with salsa and cilantro leaves. Serve hot and plain.
