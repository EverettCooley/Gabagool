Ingredients : 
 ⅓ cup  water 
 ⅓ cup  non-fat dry milk 
 ¼ tsp  vanilla extract 
 dash  nutmeg 
 4  ice  cubes, crushed 
 sweetener  to equal 2 tsp 
 Directions : 
 Put all ingredients in blender at low speed. 
 Serve immediately.
