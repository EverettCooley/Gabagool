Description : 
 Hi, my name is Rice. The following is my delicious and easy  sweet potato  and  carrot  Soup.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2  sweet potatoes  peeled and chopped 
 2 large  carrots  peeled and chopped 
 1 large  white onion  chopped 
 ½ tsp ground  cumin 
 ½ tsp ground  coriander seed 
 1  vegetable stock  cube 
 Directions : 
 Place all ingredients in a large saucepan and pour over 1.25 litres boiling  water . 
 Bring back to the boil and allow to simmer for 20 minutes or until the vegetables are tender. 
 Allow to cool then blend in a food processor or blender. 
 Reheat and enjoy, adding chopped  coriander leaves  if desired.
