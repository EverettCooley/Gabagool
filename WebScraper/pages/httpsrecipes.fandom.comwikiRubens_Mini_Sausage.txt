 Description : 
 Rubens Mini  Sausage  says it all, delicious at any moment of the day ! Recipe is easy and tasty !
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 lb Smoked  Sausage 
 1 Loaf Party  rye bread 
 4 tb  butter 
 1 c Thousand Island Dressing 
 16 oz Saurkraut 
 10 Slices Of  Swiss cheese 
 Directions : 
 Slice  Sausage  into 1/4 inch pieces. 
 Drain the saurkraut. 
 Cut  Cheese  slices into 1/2" x 2-1/2" strips. 
 Melt  butter  in small skillet. 
 Brush  rye bread  with  butter  on both sides. 
 Place on baking sheet. 
 Toast at 400 degrees F for 5 minutes. 
 Turn. 
 Remove from oven and build rubens ending with criss-crossed  Cheese  slices on top. 
 Bake for 5–10 minutes
