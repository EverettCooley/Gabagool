Ingredients : 
 Bars 
 1/2 c. light  Karo syrup 
 1/2 c.  sugar 
 3/4 c.  peanut butter 
 3 c. Special K cereal 
 Frosting 
 3 tbsp.  cocoa 
 1/2 tsp.  vanilla extract 
 2-3 tbsp.  milk 
 2 tbsp.  butter 
 1 1/2 c.  powdered sugar 
 Directions : 
 Mix Karo syrup, sugar and peanut butter in a large saucepan. 
 Heat until boiling around the edges. 
 Remove from heat and Mix in Special K. 
 Put in lightly buttered 9 x 9 inch pan and press until flat. Allow to cool. 
 Mix all frosting ingredients together and beat until smooth. 
 Spread frosting on cooled Special K bars. 
 Notes : 
 Peanut butter frosting: Substitute 1/4 cup peanut butter instead of cocoa.
