 Description : 
 Serves: 3 - 4
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup cooked elbow  macaroni 
 1 can  cream  of  Mushroom  soup with 1/2 
 1/2 can skim  milk 
 1 lg can water-packed  Tuna   (drained) 
 2 cans mixed vegetables 
 1 small can sliced  Mushrooms  raw  (drained) 
 2 shutes  green onions 
 1/4 tsp  celery seed 
 1/4 tsp lemon pepper 
 Tony's creole seasoning (to taste) 
 1 to 1 & 1/2 cups crushed chips (your choice of chips or crackers) 
 1 to 1 & 1/2 cups shredded chedder cheese 
 Directions : 
 Mix altogether except cheese & chips. Pour into 1 1/2 quart casserole dish coated with  cooking spray 
 Bake covered at 350 deg F for 25-30 min 
 Remove, increase temp to 400 deg 
 Sprinkle cheese evenly. Sprinkle chips over cheese evenly 
 $# Bake uncovered for additional 10-15 min to melt cheese
