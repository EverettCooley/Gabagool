Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  light cream  or  half and half 
 3 cups  milk 
 4  eggs , lightly beaten 
 2 tsp  sugar 
 1 cup  Southern Comfort 
 1 cup  bourbon 
 fresh grated  nutmeg 
 Directions : 
 Heat milk and cream in a medium pan over low heat until bubbles appear around the edges. 
 Remove from heat and set aside. 
 Whisk together eggs and sugar in a small bowl until thick and light yellow. 
 Stir in southern Comfort and bourbon. 
 Divide among four 12 oz. mugs. 
 Add about 1 cup hot milk mixture to each mug and sprinkle with nutmeg.
