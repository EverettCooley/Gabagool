Ingredients : 
 2 oz  gin 
 2 oz  lime juice 
 2 - 3 oz  club soda 
 1 tsp  Superfine sugar 
 2 dashes  bitters 
 Directions : 
 This cocktail should be served in an Old-Fashioned Glass.
