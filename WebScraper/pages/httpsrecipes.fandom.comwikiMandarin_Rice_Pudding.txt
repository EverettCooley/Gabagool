Mandarin  rice  Pudding
 Categories: Crockpot, Desserts
 Yield: 6 Servings
 
 2 1/2 c Cooked  rice 
 1 c Undiluted  evaporated milk 
 1/2 c  mandarin orange  Liquid (can) 
 1/2 c  light brown sugar 
 3 tb  butter ; Melted 
 1 ts  vanilla 
 3  eggs ; Beaten 
 1/2 c  Raisins 
 1 c Drained  mandarin orange 
 Sections
 
Combine  rice  With All Ingredients Except  orange 
 Sections. Mix Well. Lightly  butter  Crockpot. Pour InRice Mixture. Cover And Cook on Low For 4 to 6 HoursOr on High For 2 to 3 Hours. Stir During First 30
 Minutes. During Last Half-Hour, Stir in  orange 
 Sections.
 
Note: For Classic  rice  Pudding Use 1/2 Cup EvaporatedMilk And 2 Tsp  vanilla .  Raisins  Are Optional. OmitOrange Liquid And Sections.
 
Enjoy.
