 Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/4 oz. Appleton Estate V/X Jamaica  rum 
 6 oz.  tonic water 
 Directions : 
 Pour  rum  over  ice  in a highball glass. Fill with  tonic water . 
 Squeeze a  Lime  wedge over drink and drop into glass.
