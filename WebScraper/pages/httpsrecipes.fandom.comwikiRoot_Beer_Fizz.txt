Ingredients : 
 2 oz  gin 
 1 oz  lemon juice 
 1 tsp  sugar 
 4 oz  root beer 
 1  maraschino cherries 
 Directions : 
 In a shaker half-filled with  ice cubes , combine the  gin ,  lemon juice , and  sugar . 
 Shake well. 
 Strain into a collins glass almost filled with  ice cubes . 
 Add the root  beer . 
 Stir well. 
 Garnish with the  cherry . 
 Serve in: collins glass.
