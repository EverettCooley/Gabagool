Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 2 tablespoon  flour 
 1 cup  chicken broth 
 3 cups cooked  Chicken  cubes 
 1/4 cup diced cooked  Ham 
 2 tablespoons chopped  pimento 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1/8 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 1  egg , beaten 
 1/4 cup dry  white wine 
 1/2 cup (2 ounces) diced  Swiss cheese 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 Fresh dill sprigs for garnish (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter  in large saucepan; blend in  flour . Add broth and cook, stirring, until thick and smooth. Add  Chicken ,  Ham ,  salt ,  pepper  and  pimento ; heat thoroughly. 
 Mix  sour cream ,  egg  and wine; stir into  Chicken  mixture. Add  Cheese  and cook only until  Cheese  melts. Serve over hot  rice . Garnish with dill, if desired.
