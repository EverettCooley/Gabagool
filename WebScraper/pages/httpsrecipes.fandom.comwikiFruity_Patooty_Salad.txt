Ingredients : 
 sliced fresh California  peach 
 sliced fresh California  nectarine 
 sliced fresh California  plum 
 watermelon  cubes 
 sliced  banana 
 pineapple  chunks 
 apple  wedges 
 honeydew  or  cantaloupe  cubes 
 honey and  lemon juice  dressing (optional) 
 Directions : 
 In a large salad bowl, combine fresh California  peach ,  nectarine  and  plum  slices;  watermelon  cubes; sliced  bananas ;  pineapple  chunks;  apple  wedges; honeydew or  cantaloupe  cubes. 
 If desired, drizzle a light honey and  lemon juice  dressing over the fruit and serve.
