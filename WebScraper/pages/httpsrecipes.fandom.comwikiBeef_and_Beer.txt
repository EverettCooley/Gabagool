 Ingredients : 
 4 lbs Boneless chuck 
 12 mushrooms, cut into chunks 
 salt  &  pepper 
 1 1/2 can  beer 
 2  onions , lg, thinly sliced 
 1 1/2 tbsp  Catsup 
 2  green peppers , lg, sliced 
 1 tbsp Prepared  mustard 
 Directions : 
 salt  &  pepper  meat and brown lightly in heavy skillet. 
Add  onions ,  pepper  and  Mushrooms  and brown lightly. 
Pour  beer  over all, stir in  Catsup  and  mustard . 
Cover tightly and simmer slowly for 2 to 2 ½ hours or until meat is tender.
