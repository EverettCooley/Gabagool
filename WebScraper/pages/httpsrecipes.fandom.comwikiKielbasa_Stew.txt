Ingredients : 
 1 pound  turkey kielbasa , cut into 1-inch pieces 
 2 large stalks  celery , sliced 
 1 large  onion , chopped 
 1 can  baby corn 
 1 can condensed  cream of celery  soup 
 1½ cups  baby carrots  (large ones should be halved) 
 1 small package frozen  peas 
 3 – 4 small  red potatoes , cubed 
 1 cup sliced fresh  mushrooms 
 ½ cup sliced  zucchini 
 ½ teaspoon  thyme 
 cooking  oil 
 Directions : 
 Heat cooking oil in large pan. 
 Sauté kielbasa, onion, and celery. 
 Add soup and gradually stir in 1 cup water. 
 Add remaining vegetables and thyme. 
 Bring to a boil and stir until vegetables are just tender.
