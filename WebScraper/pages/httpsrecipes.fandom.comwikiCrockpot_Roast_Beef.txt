Description : 
 My Mom makes this and it is good.
 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (3 lbs)  sirloin  tip roast 
 ½ cup  flour 
 1 envelope dry  onion soup mix 
 1 envelope brown gravy mix 
 2 cups  ginger ale 
 Ingredients : 
 Coat roast with flour. 
 Place in crock pot. 
 Add mixture of soup mix, gravy mix, remaining flour and ginger ale. 
 Cook on low for 8 – 10 hours or until roast is tender.
