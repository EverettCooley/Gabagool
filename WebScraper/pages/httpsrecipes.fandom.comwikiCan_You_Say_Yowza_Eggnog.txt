Description : 
 Purchased from the Nelson Estate in Tulsa, Oklahoma in 1992. Dated 1966
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound box  confectioner's sugar 
 1 dozen  eggs 
 1 quart extra  dark rum 
 1 pint  peach brandy 
 1 quart  milk 
 1 teaspoon  nutmeg 
 1 teaspoon  allspice 
 Directions : 
 Separate the  eggs . 
 Beat the  confectioner's sugar  and the  egg yolks  well together. 
 Add  rum ,  brandy ,  milk ,  nutmeg  and  allspice . 
 Cover and refrigerate overnight.
