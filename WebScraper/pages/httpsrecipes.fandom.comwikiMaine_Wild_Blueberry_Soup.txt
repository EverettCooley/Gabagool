Description : 
 Serves 4.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4½ cups fresh wild  blueberries 
 1 cup  Pinot Noir 
 ¼ cup  honey 
 vanilla  crème fraîche  for garnish 
 Directions : 
 In a food processor, combine the blueberries,  Pinot Noir , and honey; blend until the mixture becomes smooth, do not strain. 
 Chill the soup before serving. 
 Garnish each soup bowl with  vanilla  crème fraîche.
