pineapple  Cake
 
 1 Box  butter  Recipe Cake Mix 
 1 20 Oz. Can Crushed  pineapple , Drain And Reserve Juice 
 1 3 Oz. Box Instant  vanilla  Pudding 
 1 8 Oz. Container  Cool Whip , Chilled Well 
 Prepare Cake Mix According to Directions. After Cake Cools, Pierce Cake With Fork Several Times. Pour Reserved  pineapple juice  Over Cake. With an Electric Mixer, Beat  Cool Whip  And Box  vanilla  Pudding at Medium Speed Until Set. Stir in Crushed  pineapple . Frost Cake With Icing And Enjoy.
