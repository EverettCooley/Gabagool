Description : 
 Bisquick Heart Smart™ recipe! Weekend brunch for two? Or will it be eggs for dinner in a tasty veggie-packed bake. 
Fresh fruit makes a great accompaniment to this egg bake.
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Prep Time: 10 minutes | Start to Finish: 50 minutes 
 Makes: 2 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup chopped  zucchini 
 ½ cup chopped  tomato  (a large plum tomato is just right for this recipe) 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 2 tablespoons grated  Parmesan cheese 
 ¼ cup  Bisquick  Heart Smart ® mix 
 ½ cup  fat-free (skim) milk 
 ¼ cup fat-free  egg product  or 2  egg whites 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 dash  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 400°F. 
 Spray 8x4-inch loaf pan with cooking spray. 
 Sprinkle zucchini, tomato, onion and cheese in pan. 
 In small bowl, stir remaining ingredients until blended. 
 Pour over vegetables and cheese. 
 Bake uncovered about 33 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean. 
 Cool 5 minutes. 
 High altitude (3500 – 6500 ft): increase Bisquick ® mix to 6 tablespoons.
