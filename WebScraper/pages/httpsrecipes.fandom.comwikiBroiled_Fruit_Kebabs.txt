 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  cantaloupe , 1 honeydew, and 1/2  watermelon , each cut into 24 one-inch squares 
 8 wooden skewers, soaked 30 minutes in  water 
 1/4 cup raw  brown sugar 
 4 to 5 sprigs  mint 
 Directions : 
 Alternating fruits, place squares onto skewers. Heat broiler to high. Line a baking sheet with foil and spray with nonstick  cooking spray . Place skewers on sheet and sprinkle with  brown sugar ; broil until slightly bubbly on one side, and turn to repeat, about 2 minutes per side. Remove and chill 1 hour. Garnish with  mint
