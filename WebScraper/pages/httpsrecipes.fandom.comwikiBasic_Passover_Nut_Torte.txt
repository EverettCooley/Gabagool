Ingredients : 
 6  eggs 
 ½ cup  matzoh meal 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 1 cup  blanched almonds 
 1 cup  sugar 
 ½ tsp  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Blend  egg  yolks and  sugar . 
 Add matzoh meal,  baking powder ,  vanilla , and  almonds . 
 Beat  egg whites  till they hold a stiff peak. 
 Fold whites into the nut- matzoh mixture, pour into a buttered 8 inch spring-form pan. 
 Bake in a pre-heated 350 °F oven for 40–50 minutes. 
 Turn upside down on a plate to cool. 
 Serve plain or with  strawberries  and  whipped cream .
