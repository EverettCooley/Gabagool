Info 
 Cook Time : 30 minutes 
 Serves : 4
 
 Ingredients 
 Low fat cooking spray 
 1 1/2 cups frozen sliced  okra 
 1 cup frozen  corn  kernels 
 1/4 cup lean  ham , chopped 
 1 (14 1/2 oz.) can stewed  tomatoes , undrained 
 1/4 cup shredded fresh  basil 
 Salt  and  pepper , to taste 
 Directions 
 1.Spray a large saucepan wth low fat cooking spray. 
 
2.Add okra, corn, ham, tomatoes and basil then bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 15â€“20 minutes or until okra is tender. 
 
3.Add salt and pepper to taste.
