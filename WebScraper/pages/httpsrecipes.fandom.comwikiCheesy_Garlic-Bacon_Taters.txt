Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group

 Ingredients : 
 1½ to 2 lb.  potatoes  (about 4 medium  potatoes ), peeled, quartered 
 ¼ cup softened  cream cheese   or   mayonnaise 
 4 cloves  garlic , crushed, peeled, minced  or  ¼ tsp  garlic powder 
 ½ cup  half and half   or   whipping cream 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 ½ cup Kraft sharp  cheddar  cheese, grated 
 4 slices  bacon , cooked crisp, crumbled 
 ¼ tsp  paprika 
 Directions : 
 Cover  potatoes  with  water  in large saucepan. 
 Bring to boil; reduce heat to medium. 
 Cook 20 minutes or until tender. 
 Drain. 
 Place  potatoes  in preheated oven for 10 minutes to "dry" them out. 
 Mash or whip  potatoes . 
 Add  cream cheese  or  mayonnaise , minced  garlic  or  garlic powder , half and half or  cream ,  salt  and  pepper ; beat until fluffy. 
 Stir in  cheese ,  bacon  and  paprika . 
 Adjust seasoning. 
 Return to oven if necessary to hold  potatoes . 
 Serve hot.
