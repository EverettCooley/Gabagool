Description : 
 Prepare 1 or 2 days ahead of time.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large package  instant pudding 
 1 large  Cool Whip 
 1 can  cherry pie filling 
 1Â½ sleeves of unsalted soda  crackers  (to make 4 layers) 
 Directions : 
 Line a 9 x 14 pan with soda crackers. 
 Spread half the pudding over the crackers. 
 Once it's set, put another layer of soda crackers, then half the Cool Whip, line with crackers and add the rest of the pudding. 
 Line with crackers, then the rest of the  Cool Whip . 
 Cover with pie filling. 
 Refrigerate 1 or 2 days, then serve.
