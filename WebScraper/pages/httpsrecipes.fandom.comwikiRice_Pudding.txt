 This is an old standby that is still popular. Serve it hot or chilled. Many prefer rice pudding served warm. Try it for a new taste treat.
 
 Info 
 Serves : 3
 
 Ingredients 
 
 1 tbsp.  cornstarch 
 1 1/2 tbsp.  granulated sugar 
 1 beaten  egg 
 1 cup  milk 
 1/2 cup well cooked  rice 
 1/2 tsp.  vanilla 
 Directions 
 1.Blend first three ingredients in saucepan until smooth. Add milk slowly, stirring to mix well.
 
2.Add rice. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until mixture is thickened and comes to a boil.
 
3.Remove from heat, add vanilla, and cool. Sprinkle with cinnamon and nutmeg if desired.
 
 Source 
 Rice Pudding  from Recipes for Better Nutrition During Cancer Treatment by the National Cancer Institute
