 Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 This Y-group is international. Good food from all parts of the world. A place where we can share the wonderful food from all over the world.
 
 Ingredients : 
 7 teaspoons  white pepper 
 7 teaspoons  garlic powder 
 7 teaspoons  onion powder 
 7 teaspoons  ground red pepper 
 7 teaspoons  paprika 
 7 teaspoons  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients. 
 Spoon or scoop contents from the homemade seasoning into a glass or plastic jar and reserve this in the cabinet. Add flavor to  chicken ,  pork , and  pasta . 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Servings:  3 tablespoons or 3 teaspoons
