Description : 
 Contributed by Delma at  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves 4 to 6. 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups frozen  broccoli ,  cauliflower  and  carrot  blend or similar vegetable mixture, thawed 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 1 can (10¾ ounces) condensed  cream of mushroom  soup, regular or low fat 
 1 jar (2 ounces) chopped  pimentos , drained 
 2 cups shredded  American cheese 
 Directions : 
 Spray slow cooker with  cooking spray . 
 Combine vegetables, soup,  pimento ,  onion  and  cheese  in slow cooker. 
 Cover and cook on LOW for 4 to 6 hours. Stir well before serving.
