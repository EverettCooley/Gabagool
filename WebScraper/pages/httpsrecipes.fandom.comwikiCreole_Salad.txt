Creole Salad
 
 1 Head  iceberg lettuce 
 1  tomato , Cut Into Wedges 
 2 Oz.  Roquefort   Cheese , Crumbled 
 1/4 Cup  pecans , Broken Itno Pieces 
 1/4 Cup Worcestershire 
 1 T.  lemon juice 
 1/3 Cup  vegetable oil 
 2 T. Dijon Mustrd With Seeds 
 
Put  lettuce ,  tomato   Cheese  And  pecans  in Large Bowl. in Small Cup, Mix Remaining Ingredients. Pour Over Salad. . Makes 6 ServingsSource: my Old Recipes
