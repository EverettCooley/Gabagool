      Shrikand     
 Ingredients : 
 sugar 
 yogurt 
 2 green  cardamon  pods 
 nutmeg  powder 
 saffron 
 dry fruit (garnish, optional) 
 Directions : 
 Put desired quantity of plain Greek style  yogurt  in muslin cloth and allow to hang for 5-6 hours. 
 Press to make sure excess  water  is drained off. 
 Pour this dry  yogurt (chakka) in a bowl. 
 Add the same almost the same quantity of  sugar . 
 Mix it to form a homogeneous smooth mixture. 
 Add cardamon,  nutmeg  and  saffron . 
 Garnish with dry fruits(chopped).
