Ingredients : 
 I
 
 1 ½ cups  Onion 
 1 cup  green pepper 
 1 jalapeño 
 3 cloves  garlic 
 II
 
 2 Tbl  chili powder 
 2 tsp  cumin 
 ½ tsp  oregano 
 III
 
 4 cups raw chopped  Chicken 
 1 cup  water 
 ½ tsp red pepper 
 12 oz bottle  chili sauce  (I never have that so I add some sweet pickle juice but you could use a little  vinegar  and  Sugar  too) 
 ¼ tsp  black pepper 
 1 Tbl worsteceshire sauce 
 1 Tbl Dijon  mustard 
 14 oz can chopped  tomatoes 
 1 can  chicken broth  (or a couple boullion cubes in  water ) 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Heat 3 T of  oil . And Saute the following for 5 minutes the first ingredients 
 Add and cook two more minutes with the part II ingredients 
 Add the rest 
 Simmer 20 minutes and add one 16 oz can Northern white beans. Heat 5 more minutes and serve.
