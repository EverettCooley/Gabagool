 Description : 
 This red-hot, meaty casserole is made with flour tortillas, ground beef, real cheese, vegetables, fruit juice, avocado, and taco seasoning and packed with refried beans, as it delivers a bold aroma that it relishes. Cheese sauce and hot sauce occasionally work for this, too.
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 flour  tortillas 
 1 ½ cup  tomato juice   or  (1 cup tomato sauce) 
 1 envelope  taco seasoning  mix 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 ½ pound  ground beef 
 16 ounce can  refried beans 
 3 cups shredded  cheddar  cheese, divided 
 1 small  avocado 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 1 ½ cup shredded  lettuce 
 1 cup chopped  tomato 
 cheese sauce, optional 
 hot sauce, optional 
 Directions : 
 Wrap tortillas securely in aluminum foil and bake at 350°F for 15 minutes. 
 Combine  tomato juice  (or tomato sauce), seasoning mix, and  oil  and stir well then set aside. 
 Cook ground  beef  in a large skillet until browned stirring to crumble, then drain well. 
 Stir in beans and ½ cup tomato juice (or tomato sauce) mixture. 
 Bring to boil then cover and reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes stirring, occasionally. Remove from heat. 
 Place ¼ cup  beef  mixture and 2½ tablespoons  cheese  down the center of each tortilla. 
 Roll up tortillas and place seam side down in a lightly greased rectangular baking dish. 
 Pour remaining  tomato juice  (or tomato sauce) mixture over casserole then cover and bake at 350°F for 35 minutes. 
 Uncover and sprinkle with remaining  cheese , then bake an additional 5 minutes or until  cheese  melts. 
 Peel and cube  avocado  and toss with lemon juice. 
 Sprinkle  avocado ,  lettuce  and  tomato  over the casserole. Serve hot and plain or with other side dishes and drizzle with the cheese sauce and/or hot sauce, if desired.
