 Ingredients : 
 4 beaten  eggs 
 1 cup chopped fresh  spinach

 2 tablespoons diced  green pepper 
 2 tablespoons diced  green onion 
 2 teaspoons crumbled  feta  cheese 
 1 tablespoon chopped  Greek olives 
 2 teaspoons grated  cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Spray pan with cooking spray then put pan on medium heat. 
 Place vegetables evenly in pan then pour eggs over all. 
 Let cook until eggs are set. 
 Sprinkle with cheddar and put under broiler for a few minutes.
