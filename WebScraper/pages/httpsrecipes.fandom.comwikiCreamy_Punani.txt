Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz  Kahlúa ®  coffee liqueur 
 3 oz  Bailey's® Irish Cream 
 2 oz  Amaretto   almond liqueur 
 vanilla ice cream 
 Directions : 
 Fill a blender about ¾ full with  vanilla   ice cream  (not solid). 
 Add the  amaretto  and  Kahlúa , followed by the  Bailey's Irish Cream . 
 Blend until smooth, and pour into tall glasses.
