Old country recipe : 
 Ingredients : 
 4  pork chops 
 Tony's seasoning (to taste) 
 2  eggs 
 2 tbls canola oil 
 2 tbls fl our 
 1 tbls bread crumbs 
 1  onion  chopped 
 Directions : 
 Pound the chops with the pounder, add Tony's 
 Blend flour and bread crumbs 
 dredge chops with the beaten  egg , then  flour  and breadcrumbs 
 Brown onions slightly in oil 
 Fry breaded chops and onions in hot oil, at low heat until browned & tender 
 Place chops on blot towel on both sides 
 ===== Serve with cooked vegetables or salad  =====
