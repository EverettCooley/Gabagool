Description 
 Cranberry Juice Drink Concentrate, Sweetened, Canned  by the US Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Serves : 14 
 Ingredients 
 1 cup  cranberry juice  concentrate 
 ⅓ cup  sugar 
 1 x 12-ounce package fresh or frozen  cranberries , rinsed and drained 
 ½ cup  dried cranberries  (about 2 ounces) 
 3 tablespoons orange  marmalade 
 2 tablespoons  orange juice 
 2 teaspoons grated  orange  peel 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  allspice  (optional) 
 Directions 
 Combine cranberry juice concentrate and sugar in medium saucepan. 
 Bring to boil over high heat, stirring until sugar dissolves. 
 Add fresh and dried cranberries and cook until dried berries begin to soften and fresh berries begin to pop, stirring often, about 7 minutes. 
 Remove from heat and stir in orange marmalade, orange juice, orange peel, and allspice. 
 Cool completely – cover and chill until cold, about 2 hours.
