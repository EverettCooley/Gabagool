 INGREDIENTS 
 3 tbsp  cornstarch 
 4 tbsp Cold  water 
 2 cup Boiling  water 
 Directions : 
 1 Gooey glue: mix 3 tb.  cornstarch  dissolved in the 4 tb. cold  water . 
2 Pour into pan with the 2 cups boiling  water , stirring constantly until it becomes clear and thickened. 
3 Let cool and pout into a plastic container
