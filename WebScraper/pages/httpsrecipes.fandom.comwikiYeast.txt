      Dry yeast     A leavening agent that occurs naturally. The most commonly available types of yeast are  fresh  (also called compressed, wet, active fresh or cake yeast),  active dry , and  instant  (also known as rapid-rise). There's also  baker's yeast  (sometimes called baking or bread yeast).
 There are also de-activated forms of yeast, like  brewer's yeast  and  nutritional yeast  which are used in cooking.
