 Prep Time: 25 
 Cook time: 24 mins 
 Serves: 2 or more 
 
 Description : 
 A delicious pizza with gyro meat and pepperoni
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  gyro meat 
 1 oz  pepperoni 
 1 cup  tomato sauce 
 1 x 16-ounce loaf frozen whole wheat bread dough, thawed 
 2 cups shredded  mozzarella  cheese (8 ounces) 
 Directions : 
 On a lightly floured surface, roll  bread  dough into a 14-inch circle. 
 Transfer to a greased 13-inch pizza pan. 
 Build up edges slightly. 
 Prick dough generously with a fork. 
 Bake in a 375Â°F oven for 20 to 25 minutes or until light brown. 
 Spoon tomato sauce over crust. 
 Sprinkle 1 cup of the  mozzarella  cheese over sauce. 
 Put pepperoni and gyro meat on top. 
 Bake about 12 minutes more or until cheese melts and pizza is heated through.
