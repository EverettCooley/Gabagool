 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 Kahlúa ®  coffee liqueur 
 4 scoops  coffee   ice cream 
 Directions : 
 Fill blender with  coffee  ice- cream  and the amount of Kahlúa to your desire. 
 Blend until drinkable, pour into a  beer  mug and garnish with a  cinnamon  stick.
