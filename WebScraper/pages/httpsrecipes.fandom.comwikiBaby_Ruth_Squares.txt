Baby Ruth Squares
 1 cup corn syrup 
1 cup sugar 
1-1/2 cups salted peanuts 
6 cups rice krispies 
1 heaping cup peanut butter, smooth 
 Topping
 1 cup sugar 
1/4 cup milk 
1 cup chocolate chips 
2 Tbsp butter 
 Cook corn syrup and sugar until boil. Remove from heat and stir in 
peanut butter, peanuts and cereal. Press into 9x13 pan. 
 Cook topping ingredients, except chocolate chips to full boil. Remove 
from heat and stir in chocolate chips. Spread on top of cereal 
mixture. 
 Cut into small squares.
