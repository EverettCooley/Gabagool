Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Yield: 6 Servings and 2 cups of cream sauce 
 Ingredients : 
 2½ lb  round steak 
 5 oz  evaporated milk 
 2 tbsp green  tabasco sauce 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 2 cups  all-purpose flour  -- divided 
 2 tsp  paprika 
 ¾ tsp  garlic powder 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp cracked  black pepper 
 vegetable  oil 
 Directions : 
 Trim  steak  and pound, if needed, to ½ inch thick; cut into 6 to 8 pieces. 
 Combine  milk ,  tabasco sauce  and  salt  in a bowl. 
 Measure 1 cup of  flour  into a bowl. 
 Combine remaining  flour ,  paprika ,  garlic powder ,  salt  and  pepper  in another bowl. 
 Dip  steak  into  flour , into  milk  mixture, and then into seasoned  flour . 
 Set aside until all meat is coated. 
 Heat 1 or 2 inches of  oil  in fry pan. 
 Fry meat until both sides are golden brown, about 2 minutes per side. 
 Drain on paper towels. 
 Serve with  cream sauce , mashed  potatoes  and gravy. 
 Cream Sauce : 
 Pour off all but 6 tbsp of fat from fry pan. 
 Add 6 tbsp  flour  onto pan (use any leftover seasoned  flour ) and blend well. 
 Gradually stir in 2½ cups  milk . 
 Cook and stir over medium heat until thickened. 
 Season with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Cover steaks with gravy when served. 
 Enjoy.
