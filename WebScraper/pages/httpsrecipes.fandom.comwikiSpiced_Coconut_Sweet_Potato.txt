Description : 
 This deliciously spicy and heavy Burmese vegetarian dish is very easy to prepare and cook.
 
 Makes 4 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 1  onion , chopped 
 3  garlic  cloves, crushed 
 2 large  chile , finely chopped 
 1 teaspoon  ground turmeric 
 1 teaspoon root  ginger , freshly grated 
 salt  and  black pepper 
 1 tablespoon  soy sauce 
 240 ml (8 oz)  coconut milk 
 360 ml (12 oz)  water 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 450 g (1 lb)  sweet potato , peeled and sliced 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Put all ingredients except the  sweet potatoes  in a large saucepan. 
 Bring to a boil. 
 Stir. 
 Reduce heat and let simmer for 5 minutes. 
 Add  sweet potatoes , stir well and simmer for 20 minutes or until  potatoes  are soft and tender.
