Description : 
 Serve this dish with a tossed green salad drizzled with vinaigrette. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source:  Southern Living  2007 Main Dish Marvels 
 Prep: 10 minutes | Cook: 8 hr 
 Makes 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 (16 oz) cans  Great Northern beans , undrained 
 1 (28 oz) can diced  tomatoes , undrained 
 1 (14 oz) package smoked  turkey sausage , sliced 
 ¾ cup  vegetable broth 
 3 medium  carrots , sliced 
 1 cup frozen chopped  onions , minced 
 2  garlic  cloves, minced 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp dried  thyme 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 1  bay leaf 
 ½ cup fine, dry  breadcrumbs 
 ¼ cup freshly grated  Parmesan cheese 
 Directions : 
 Combine first 11 ingredients in a 5-qt slow cooker. 
 Cover and cook on low 8 hours or until vegetables are tender. 
 Remove and discard bay leaf. 
 Stir in bread crumbs. 
 Sprinkle each serving with Parmesan cheese before serving.
