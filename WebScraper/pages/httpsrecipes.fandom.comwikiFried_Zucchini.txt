 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4  zucchini 
 2 tablespoons  flour 
 3-4 tablespoons  oil 
 vinegar  or  lemon juice 
 1clove minced  garlic 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Peel the  zucchini , cut into thin slices. 
 Dry each slice with a cloth, dredge with  flour  and fry in  oil . 
 When all are fried, arrange on a plate and drizzle  lemon juice  or a little  vinegar  mixed with minced  garlic  and  salt  over them. 
 Serve hot or cold.
