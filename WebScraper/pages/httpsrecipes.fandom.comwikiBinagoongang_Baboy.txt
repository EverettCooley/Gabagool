 Description : 
 Pork  cooked with  Shrimp  paste.
 
       Binagoongang Baboy     
 Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 1/4 cup chopped  garlic 
 1/4 cup chopped  Onion 
 1/4 cup coarsely chopped  tomatoes 
 1 and 1/2 lbs boneless  Pork  shoulder cut into cubes 
 3/4 cup  water 
 1/2 tsp  Sugar 
 4 tbsp bagoong ( Shrimp  paste) 
 Directions : 
 1. Heat  oil  in a large skillet.
 2. Saut√©  garlic ,  Onion  and  tomatoes  until soft.
 3. Add the  Pork  and cook until brown.
 4. Add  water  and simmer for 30 minutes.
 5.Add  Sugar  and bagoong and simmer stirring occasionally for further 15 minutes or until  Pork  is tender.
 6. Serve hot with boiled  rice .
