 Ingredients : 
 1 cake of  yeast 
 warm  water  to dilute 
 pinch of  sugar 
 approximately 1 tablespoon of  flour 
 2 whole  eggs 
 2  egg yolks 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 1 cup cold  half and half 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 cup warm  milk 
 3 ounces of  butter 
 1 tablespoon of  lard 
 7 cups of  flour 
 golden raisins  to taste 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl, mix the yeast cake diluted in warm water, sugar and flour in a separate bowl, beat together the whole eggs, egg yolks sugar, half and half and salt. 
 Set aside. 
 In a separate bowl, mix milk, butter and lard. 
 Add to 7 cups of  flour . 
 Then add  yeast  mixture and then add  egg  and  milk  mixture. 
 Knead well (about 10 minutes) let raise until doubled (about 2 hours). 
 Divide into 6 equal parts (about 1 pound each). 
 Roll out and dot with white  raisins  that have been soaked in  water  and drained (start with ¾ cup white  raisins ). 
 Roll up and braid three strands together and put in greased loaf pan. 
 Then roll up and braid the other three strands and put in greased loaf pan. 
 Let raise again (approximately one hour to an hour and a half). 
 Brush with beaten  egg  and  sugar  before baking at 350°F for about one hour.
