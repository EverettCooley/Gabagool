 Description : 
 From my Aunt Maggie's Collection. This one is marked as a family favorite and is dated 1932.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large white  Onion , minced 
 2 tablespoons  unsalted butter 
 2 cans  cream  of  Mushroom  soup 
 2 cans  cream  of  Chicken  soup 
 ½ cup old English  Cheese , grated 
 1/2 cup  milk 
 1/2 cup  water 
 1 teaspoon  curry  powder 
 1/4 teaspoon dried  thyme 
 1/8 teaspoon dried  basil 
 1/4 teaspoon dried  oregano 
 5 pounds  Chicken , cooked and cubed 
 4 ounce jar pimentos 
 2 teaspoons  salt 
 2 teaspoons freshly ground  black pepper 
 ¼ cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Saute  Onion  in  butter . 
 Add soups,  Cheese ,  milk ,  water  and spices. 
 Fold in  Chicken  and pimentos. 
 Divide casserole into two 13x9 inch casserole dishes. 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste (I like the full measurement in recipe, but you may want less  salt  and/or  pepper ), top with grated # Parmesan cheese  and bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
