Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Victor Estate in Allen, Texas in 1981.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4 slices  bacon  chopped 
 2 medium  white onions  chopped 
 1  green pepper  chopped 
 1 cup thinly sliced  okra 
 1 clove  garlic  chopped 
 12 ounces chopped  ham 
 14 ounce can  tomatoes  chopped 
 1 teaspoon dried  thyme 
 ⅛ teaspoon  cayenne pepper 
 1 cup  long grain rice  uncooked 
 2 cups  water 
 Directions : 
 Render bacon in a dutch oven until brown but not crisp. 
 Stir in onion, pepper and okra then sauté until wilted. 
 Add garlic and ham and continue cooking for 5 minutes. 
 Add tomatoes, thyme, cayenne, rice and water. 
 Bring to a boil over high heat then cover and simmer for 30 minutes.
