       Asparagus     
 Description : 
 Asparagus sauce. Makes 2 cups
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup  butter 
 3 tablespoons  flour 
 1 cup  asparagus  water  [1] . 
 1  lemon juice 
 ⅛ teaspoon  salt 
 6  egg yolks 
 Directions : 
 Melt butter in a pan 
 Add the flour and stir until completely mixed and smooth. Cook until it smells a bit "nutty" 
 Add the asparagus water, lemon juice, salt and egg yolks 
 Stir until it reaches the desired consistency 
 Serve hot. 
 Notes : 
 
 ↑   Asparagus water is the water left over after cooking asparagus. Substitute  celery  water with 1  bouillon  cube and 1 tablespoon  sherry  for the  asparagus  water
