 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 Steamed, chilled, lg.  Shrimp  (enough for 4 to 5 persons) 
 1 container frozen  raspberries , pureed with 3 tbsp.  triple sec 
 black olives 
 tomato 
 kiwi  or  cantaloupe 
 lettuce 
 Fresh  raspberries 
 Directions : 
 Arrange  lettuce  on a small plate. 
 Place 4 to 5  Shrimp , an olive, slice of  kiwi  or  cantaloupe , slice of  tomato  and several fresh  raspberries  on top of  lettuce . 
 Drizzle  raspberry  sauce over each.
