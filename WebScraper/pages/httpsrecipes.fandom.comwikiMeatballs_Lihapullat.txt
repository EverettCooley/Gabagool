 Description : 
 (for four - five persons)
 
       Meatballs Lihapullat     
 Ingredients : 
 500 g minced  Beef 
 1 dl fine dry breadcrumbs or two slices of white bread 
 1 dl  cream 
 1  Onion 
 1 tblsp  oil 
 1  egg 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1/4 tsp  allspice  or  white pepper 
 Gravy:
 
 2 tblsp fat 
 2 tblsp  flour 
 4 dl pan juices 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix breadcrumbs with  cream  in a bowl. Let stand for a while. Finely chop the  Onion  and saut√© in  oil  in a frying pan or microwave oven. Add the  Onion ,  egg , seasonings and meat. Mix until smooth. Wet your hands and shape the mixture into balls. Fry meatballs in hot fat on all sides. Small balls will be done in 3-5 minutes, larger ones 5-8 minutes.
 To make gravy, brown the  flour  lightly in fat. Add the liquid stirring all the time. Season to taste. The gravy can be served separately or poured over the meatballs. Serve with  potatoes  and grated  carrots .  lingonberry  jam and gherkins also go well with the dish.
