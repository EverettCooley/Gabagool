 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 400 g  Pasta 
 2 Tbsp  olive oil 
 2 cloves  garlic 
 1 large Avocados 
 1 Tbsp  lemon juice 
 salt  &  pepper 
 Â½ Cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 2 Tbsp  cream 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Pasta  to Manufacturers instructions.
 Peel & stone Avocados.
 Mash with fork. 
 Mix in  lemon juice  &  salt  &  pepper . 
 Heat  oil  in pan, add crushed  garlic  & then  avocado  mixture. 
 Heat without boiling, stir in  cream  & pour over cooked  Pasta . 
 Sprinkle over  Parmesan cheese .
