 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons fish seasoning 
 1/2 cup  flour  seasoned with a little  sea salt 
 1  egg , beaten 
 olive oil 
 1 clove  garlic , sliced 
 1 small  shallot , finely minced 
 Directions : 
 Season the fish pieces on all sides very well with the fish seasonings. 
 Then dip into the  flour , and then into the beaten  egg , and then back into the  flour . 
 In meantime, either preheat  browning  dish as described above, or use a skillet on the burner. 
 Heat the  oil , and then add the  garlic  and  shallot  and cook just until they start to turn soft. 
 Quickly add the coated fish and cook for 1â€“2 minutes on each side. Garnish and serve.
