Description : 
 Source:  Better Homes and Gardens 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cup  pasta , tri-colored spiral 
 1 cup  shrimp , cooked or 1 can (6½ oz)  tuna , drained and flaked 
 ⅓ cup  green pepper , diced 
 ¼ cup  carrots , sliced 
 ½ cup  zucchini , sliced 
 ⅓ cup  Lea & Perrins white wine Worcestershire   [1] 
 ⅓ cup  mayonnaise 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Cook  pasta  according to package directions. 
 In a mixing bowl, combine  pasta ,  shrimp  or  tuna ,  bell pepper ,  carrots  and  zucchini . 
 Add worcestershire,  mayonnaise ,  salt  and  pepper  and toss lightly to combine. 
 Refrigerate at least 30 minutes before serving. 
 Notes : 
 
 ↑   If you can't find this sauce, you can substitute regular Worcestershire sauce and add a bit of sauterne or white wine.
