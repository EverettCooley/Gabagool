Ingredients : 
 2½ cups  flour 
 3 tsp  soda 
 2 tsp  cinnamon 
 ½ tsp  nutmeg 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 cup  butter 
 2 tsp  vanilla 
 2  eggs 
 1 tsp liquid  sugar substitute 
 4 cup grated  apple 
 1 cup  walnuts 
 Directions : 
 Mix dry ingredients; add  butter ,  eggs ,  sugar substitute , and blend well. 
 Stir in grated  apples  and  nuts . 
 Bake in well-greased 9 x 13 pan at 375°F for 40 – 50 minutes. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Food exchange per serving: ½ fat + 1 starch/bread + ½ fruit
