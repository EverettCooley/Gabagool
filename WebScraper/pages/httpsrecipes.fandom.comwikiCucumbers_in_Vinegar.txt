Description : 
 Wonderful snack for anyone who likes  vinegar ,  onions  and  garlic ! This recipe is only for one  cucumber , adjust quantity as desired. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 white vinegar 
 1  cucumber , sliced 
 water 
 1 small sliced  onion 
 1 – 2 cloves diced  garlic 
 Directions : 
 Put  cucumbers ,  garlic , and  onion  in a bowl. 
 Pour in  vinegar  and  water  until covering  cucumbers , at a 3 – 1 ratio between  vinegar  and  water  (Parts  vinegar  for every part  water ). 
 Allow to marinate overnight before serving.
