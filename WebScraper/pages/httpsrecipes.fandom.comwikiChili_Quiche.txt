Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Clovers Estate in Arlington, Texas in 1989.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup pepper  jack cheese 
 1 cup  cheddar  cheese 
 1 cup  half and half 
 4 ounce can chopped  green chilies 
 1 small jar  pimentos 
 4  eggs 
 1 cup all purpose  baking mix 
 2 teaspoons  hot pepper sauce 
 Directions : 
 Grease pie pan then sprinkle chilies, cheese and pimentos on bottom of pan. 
 Mix eggs and baking mix then add half and half and mix together with electric mixer. 
 Pour over top and bake at 350Â°F for 30 minutes.
