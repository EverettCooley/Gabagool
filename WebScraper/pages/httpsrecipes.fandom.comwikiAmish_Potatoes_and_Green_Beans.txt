 Contributed by  PressureCookerRecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups of  water 
 5 to 6  potatoes , quartered 
 ½ to 1 lb  bacon , cut up, fried, and drained 
 4 cans  green beans 
 Direcitons : 
 Place all in large pressure cooker and pressure cook 10 minutes.
