 Description : 
 A nice tasting sweet  Apple  &  almond  salad for any hour of the dayÂ !
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 x Tart green  Apples , peeled, Cut into 1/2 inch cubes  
3 x  Belgian endives , thinly Sliced crosswise 
2 tbl  almonds , peeled and shelled 
 Dressing: 
4 teaspoons  oil 
1 T  lemon juice 
1 t minced  garlic 
1/4 t  salt 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix  Apple , endives and  almonds  together in a bowl. Mix the dressing and pour on salad. Toss thoroughly and serve. Makes 6 servings.
