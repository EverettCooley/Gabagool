Ingredients : 
 12  eggs  (beaten) 
 2 Lbs  brown sugar 
 4 cups white  granulated sugar 
 1 Tbsp.  vanilla 
 8 Tsp.  baking soda 
 1 Tbsp.  maple syrup  (the Real Stuff) 
 1 lb.  butter  (softened) 
 3 lbs  Peanut butter 
 18 cups  quick oats 
 1 Lb.  chocolate chips 
 1 Lb. M&m's candy 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix together both sugars and butter. 
 Add eggs, peanut butter, oats and baking soda. 
 Mix together well. 
 Add vanilla and maple syrup, mixing well again. 
 Fold in chocolate chips and m&m's 
 Spoon onto ungreased cookie sheets and bake at 350F for 12 minutes 
 Notes : 
 Makes 17 Dozen Cookies.  These are good for bake sales, but the recipe may be cut in half or even quarters. 
 The cookies are very moist and should be removed from rack before they cool completely to prevent crumbling.
