 Description : 
 Two citrus juices give this guacamole twice the zing
 
 Ingredients : 
 3  California avocados , peeled, mashed 
 3  tomatoes , peeled, seeded, diced 
 ¼ Cup  Onion , minced 
 1  lime , juiced and grated rind 
 2 Tbsp  lemon juice 
 ½ tsp  salt  (coarse, if possible) 
 ½ Cup  Mayonnaise 
 1 Cup  sour cream 
 3 dash hot pepper sauce 
 Directions : 
 Mix together all ingredients. 
 Add  parsley  as garnish.
