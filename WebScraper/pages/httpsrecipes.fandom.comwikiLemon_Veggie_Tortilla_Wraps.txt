 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 baking  potato  diced 
 1/2  green pepper  chopped 
 1/2 red pepper chopped 
 1  tomato  chopped 
 1  onion  chopped 
 1 cup  broccoli  cut into pieces 
 1/2  yellow squash  thinly sliced 
 3 tablespoon  olive oil 
 1  lemon 
 1 teaspoon oregeno 
 1 teaspoon  basil 
 1 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350. Put  olive oil  in shallow baking dish. Add all veggies (not  lemon ) and spices, and stir. Cut  lemon  in half. Squeeze 1/2 of the  lemon  over the veggies and cut the other 1/2 into slices and place in the veggie mixture. Place in the oven and cook for 30 minutes to an hour (until veggies are tender and slightly browned). Take out the  lemon  slices. Serve with warmed  flour  tortillas or alone as a side dish.
