Ingredients : 
 1 cup  apple juice 
 1 tbsp  sugar  (to taste) 
 2 cups  heavy cream , whipped 
 dash of  nutmeg 
 1 cup mashed  mangos 
 Directions : 
 Mix  mango  and  apple juice . 
 Add  sugar  to taste. 
 Freeze for 30 minutes. 
 Beat it well then fold in  whipped cream  and  nutmeg . 
 Pour into porcelain petit pots and chill for several hours.
