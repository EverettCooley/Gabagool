Ingredients : 
 1½ oz  gin 
 1 oz  cranberry juice 
 ¼ oz  Maraschino liqueur 
 ¼ oz  lemon juice 
 ¼ oz  lime juice 
 Directions : 
 Mix the ingredients and shake them. 
 Fill the glass with  ice  and strain into a cocktail glass. 
 Garnish with  cranberries .
