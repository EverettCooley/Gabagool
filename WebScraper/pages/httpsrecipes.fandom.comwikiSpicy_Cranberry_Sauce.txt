Description : 
 
 Cranberry sauce is typically served with turkey at Christmas or Thanksgiving. It can also be a good accompaniment to  nutloaf  for a  vegetarian Christmas .
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups of frozen  cranberries 
 2 cups of liquid (can be water or  cranberry juice ) 
 1 cup apple juice 
 dash of lemon juice 
 2 cups of  sugar 
 cinnamon 
 nutmeg 
 allspice 
 star anise 
 cloves 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve 1 cup of sugar in a mixture of the boiling water or juice 
 Add apple and lemon juice 
 Add 2 star anise and 4 cloves in a spice bag or tea strainer 
 Add the cranberries 
 Return to a boil and then reduce to a simmer 
 Add cinnamon, nutmeg, and allspice 
 Taste and add more spices and sugar, if needed 
 Simmer for 15 to 25 minutes, until the cranberries have burst and softened. If necessary, add more boiling water to keep it from getting too thick. 
 Check on the level of spices and sugar again. 
 Chill in a refrigerator. The sauce will thicken when it cools.
