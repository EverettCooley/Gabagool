Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2½ pkg  mochiko  (5 cups of sweet rice flour) 
 2½ cups  brown sugar 
 2 x 12 oz. cans  coconut milk 
 1 can  water 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 1 can tsubushi-an ( azuki bean paste ) 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together. 
 Pour into greased 9 x 13 inch baking pan. 
 Bake and 350°F for 1 hour.
