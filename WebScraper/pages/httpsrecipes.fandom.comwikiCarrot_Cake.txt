Ingredients : 
 2 cups  sugar 
 2 cups  flour 
 3 teaspoons  salt 
 2 teaspoons  cinnamon 
 1 teaspoon  soda 
 1½ cup  vegetable oil 
 4  eggs  (beaten) 
 2 cups finely grated  carrots 
 Directions : 
 Sift  flour ,  salt , soda and  cinnamon . 
 Add  sugar  and  oil . 
 Mix well. 
 Add beaten  eggs  and  carrots . 
 Mix well and bake at 350°F for about 30 minutes
