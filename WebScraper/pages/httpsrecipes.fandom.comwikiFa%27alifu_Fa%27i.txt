Ingredients : 
 2 bunches  green bananas  (peeled) 
 2 cans  coconut milk 
 1 cup chopped  onions 
 Directions : 
 Fill pot with enough  water  to completely cover  bananas . 
 Bring  water  to a boil. 
 Add  bananas  cook until tender. 
 Drain  water  completely when cooked. 
 Mix  coconut milk ,  onions  and  salt  to taste, stir. 
 Add to  bananas . 
 Bring to a boil. 
 bananas  are cooked when the  coconut milk  has changed into a thicker consistency like  spaghetti sauce .
