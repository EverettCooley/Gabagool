Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  oil 
 ¼ cup  lime juice 
 4 tsp  sugar 
 1 tsp  onion powder 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ⅛ tsp  pepper 
 2 heads  romaine lettuce  torn into pieces 
 2 cups  mangos , cubed 
 1 large  avocado  sliced 
 Directions : 
 Blend together  oil ,  lime juice ,  sugar ,  onion  powder,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Toss  lettuce ,  mango  and  avocado . 
 Pour on dressing.
