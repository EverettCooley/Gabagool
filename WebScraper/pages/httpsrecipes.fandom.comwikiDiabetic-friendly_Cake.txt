Ingredients : 
 1 cup  water 
 2 jumbo  eggs 
 1 cup  raisins 
 artificial sweetener  to 1 cup  sugar 
 1 cup unsweetened  applesauce 
 ¾ cup  oil 
 1 tbsp  baking soda 
 ¼ tbsp  cinnamon 
 ½ tbsp  nutmeg 
 1 tbsp  vanilla 
 ½ tbsp  salt 
 1½ cup  flour 
 Directions : 
 Cook  raisins  till soft. 
 Drain  water , +  raisins  to  applesauce  and  eggs , sweetener, cooking  oil . 
 Mix well, blend  baking soda ,  flour  + remaining ingredients. 
 Bake 350°F in 8" x 8" or 9" x 9" pan.
