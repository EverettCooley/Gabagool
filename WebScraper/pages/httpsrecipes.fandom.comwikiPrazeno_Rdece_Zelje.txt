Description : 
 Red cabbage
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 medium  red cabbage 
 4 tablespoons  butter  or  oleo 
 2 tablespoons  flour 
 1 tablespoon  Sugar 
 1 cup hot  water 
 1 teaspoon  caraway seeds 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 1 tablespoon  vinegar 
 Directions : 
 Shred  cabbage . 
 Melt oleo in large skillet. 
 Add  flour  and brown slightly. 
 Add  sugar , shredded  cabbage , hot  water ,  salt ,  pepper , and  caraway seeds . 
 Simmer gently for one hour. 
 Add  vinegar  last 15 minutes of cooking time.
