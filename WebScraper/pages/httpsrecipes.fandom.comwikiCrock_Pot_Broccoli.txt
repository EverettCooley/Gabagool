Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Mclean Estate in Irving, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 20 ounces frozen chopped  broccoli  partially thawed 
 1 can condensed  cream of celery  soup undiluted 
 1½ cups shredded sharp  cheddar  cheese divided 
 ¼ cup chopped white  onion 
 ½ teaspoon  Worcestershire sauce 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 1 cup crushed butter-flavored  crackers 
 2 tablespoons  butter 
 Directions : 
 In large bowl combine  broccoli , soup, 1-cup  cheese ,  onion ,  Worcestershire sauce  and  pepper  then pour into a greased crock-pot. 
 Sprinkle crackers on top and dot with  butter . 
 Cover and cook on high for 3 hours. 
 Sprinkle with remaining  cheese  and cook 15 minutes longer.
