Ingredients : 
 oil  for deep frying 
 2 cups white  flour

 2  capsicums 
 1  onion 
 1  egg 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 salt  to taste 
 black pepper  to taste 
 red  chili powder  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Sieve  flour ,  baking powder ,  chili powder ,  salt  and  pepper  in a bowl. 
 Cut the capsicums and  Onion  into small pieces and add to the  flour . 
 Add  water  to the  flour  mixture and stir with hand or wooden spoon to make a thick better like Paccar. 
 If better is very thick or sticky then add little more  water . Add  Cheese  and stir. 
 Heat  oil  in a deep frying pan or karahi. Drop a tablespoon full in the hot  oil  and fry them like pakora. 
 Serve hot with  ketchup  or chutney.
