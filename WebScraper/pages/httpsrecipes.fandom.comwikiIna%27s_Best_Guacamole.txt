Ingredients : 
 2 ripe fresh  California avocados  – halved and pitted 
 1  tomato  – medium, cored, peeled and seeded 
 ½ cup  red onion  – finely chopped 
 ⅓ cups  cilantro  – fresh, chopped 
 2 tbsp  lime juice 
 ½ tsp ground  cumin 
 ¾ tsp  salt 
 ¾ tsp  jalapeño pepper sauce 
 Directions : 
 Into a medium size bowl, scoop the  avocado  flesh and mash with a fork until not quite smooth. 
 Add remaining ingredients and mix well. 
 Cover tightly with plastic wrap snug against the mixture and chill for an hour or until ready to use.
