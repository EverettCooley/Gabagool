brown sugar  Glaze
 Categories: Cakes
 Yield: 8 Servings
 
 1/2 c  light brown sugar ; Firmly 
 -Packed
 
 1/2 c  butter  or  margarine 
 1/4 c  milk 
 
 Combine All Ingredients in a Heavy Saucepan; Bring to aFull Boil And Cook, Stirring Constantly, 2 Minutes. LetCool to Lukewarm. Yield: 2/3 Cup.
