Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup  clam  meat 
 3½ oz  ground beef 
 1 tbsp  sesame oil 
 ½ cup  soybean sprouts 
 2 oz  bracken fern  (kosari), pre-soaked 
 1 bunch  watercress 
 2 tbsp  soy sauce 
 3 cup raw  rice 
 2  hot red peppers 
 Directions : 
 Remove intestines from clam meat. 
 Rinse in weak-salted  water  and drain. 
 Cut off long, hairy ends of soy  bean sprouts , wash, and drain. 
 Discard tough stems from pre-soaked brackens. 
 Cut off root and leaves from watercress and cut into 1½ inch pieces. 
 Wash  rice  and soak in  water  for 1 hour. 
 Cut  red peppers  in half, remove seeds, and chop. 
 Add  sesame oil  to pan and saute clam meat on high heat. 
 Add ground  beef  to clam meat and saute together. 
 Add 2 cups (400 cc)  water  rice and bring to boil hard. 
 Add soy  bean sprouts  and brackens and boil 10 minutes again, seasoning with soy sauce. 
 Drain  rice  in colander, puree in blender, add chopped  red peppers , and puree together. 
 Add rice mixture to meat. 
 When mixture thickens and binds together, add watercress and cook 1 minute until it is done.
