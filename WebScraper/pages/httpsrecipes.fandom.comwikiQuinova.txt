Tiny seeds of this plant, used as cereal grain, rich in minerals including zinc, copper, iron ,magnesium,protein rich, contains fibre, lysine, omega-3 fatty acids, but not digestion troubling gluten. Regarded as a wonder grain. a resinous compound called seponon need to be removed by long soaking and thorough washing.
