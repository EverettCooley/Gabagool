 In Romanian: Prajitura din brinza de vaca si nuci
 
 Ingredients : 
 14 oz / 400 g  cottage cheese 
 4  eggs 
 Â¾ cup  sugar 
 7 oz / 200 g  ground walnuts 
 4 oz / 100 g  raisins 
 5 oz / 150 g  butter 
 vanilla  or  rum 
 shortening  and  flour  to grease and dust the pan 
 Directions : 
 Grind the  cottage cheese . 
 Mix the  sugar  with the  butter , adding the  eggs  one by one, then the  cheese ,  walnuts ,  raisins ,  vanilla  or  rum . 
 Mix everything well and pour into a buttered and dusted bread pan. 
 Bake at medium heat and remove from the pan when cold.
