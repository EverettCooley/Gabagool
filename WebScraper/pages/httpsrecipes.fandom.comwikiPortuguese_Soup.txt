Ingredients : 
 4 – 5 lbs  chuck roast , cut into cubes 
 1 lbs  chourico , sliced ½ inch thick 
 2 cans B&M  baked beans  (large cans) 
 3 Tbsp  crushed red peppers  (wet, Star brand or substitute) 
 salt  and  pepper 
 small  cabbage 
 2 packages of frozen  kale   or  chopped, fresh (about 2 lbs) 
 3 or 4 large  potatoes , peeled and quartered 
 Directions : 
 In a large pot, add  water  to these ingredients until the water reaches about 2 inches from the top 
 Cook covered for 3 hours 
 Then add chopped  cabbage  and  kale 
 Bring  water  level back to the original line and cook for 2 more hours 
 Add  Potatoes  and cook for another 30 minutes
