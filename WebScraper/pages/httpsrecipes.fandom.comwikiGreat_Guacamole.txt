Ingredients : 
 2 ripe  avocados , peeled, seeded and sliced 
 2 tbsp fresh  lemon juice 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1  shallot , minced 
 Â½ tsp  salt 
 several drops  hot pepper sauce 
 3 tbsp chopped fresh  tomato 
 1 tbsp chopped fresh  cilantro 
 Directions : 
 Cut avocados in half and remove the seeds. 
 Scoop out the pulp and place in a shallow bowl. 
 Drizzle with  lemon juice  and mash with a fork. 
 Add remaining ingredients and mix well. 
 Serve right away.
