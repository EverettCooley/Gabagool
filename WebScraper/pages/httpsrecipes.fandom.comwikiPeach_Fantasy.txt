peach  Fantasy
 
 1 Package Frozen  peaches  (12 to 16 Oz) 
 1/2 Cup  Grenadine 
 1 Carton  sour cream  (16 Oz) 
 1 Quart  vanilla   Ice cream 
 
Soften  peaches  Enough to Blend in Blender With  Grenadine . Add  sour cream  And  Ice cream . Put in Individual Bowls to Serve And Refreeze Until Ready to Serve. Taste is Better Than Homemade  Ice cream  And is Quick And Easy. Serves 6Source: Mrs. Oscar S. Spivey
