 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 12 sliced white  bread 
 410 G pie  Apple 
 1 Tablespoon grated  lemon  rind 
 2 Tablespoons  castor sugar 
 1 Teaspoon  cinnamon 
 1/2 Teaspoon ground  nutmeg 
 60 G  butter 
 Directions : 
 Remove crusts from  bread . 
 Spread combined  Apple , rind,  Sugar  and spices over one half of each slice of  bread . 
 Fold over each slice to form a triangle, secure with toothpicks. spread outside over turnovers with  butter . 
 Bake in 180*C for about 12 minutes or until turnovers are well browned, remove toothpicks.
