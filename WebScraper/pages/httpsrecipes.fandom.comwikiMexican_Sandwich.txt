Ingredients : 
 1 (8 ounce) package  tortillas 
 1 (7⅝ ounce) box  Spanish rice 
 1 (15 ounce) can  refried beans 
 1 (15 ounce) can turkey chili  [1] 
 1 lb boneless  beef  cubes 
 3 cups  mozzarella  cheese 
 Directions : 
 First cook your  rice . 
 Once the  rice  is cooked get a plate out. 
 Put the tortilla on the plate. 
 Put  refried beans  on the tortilla, put another tortilla, put  Spanish rice , add another tortilla,spread  cheese , another tortilla put  beef  cube put another tortilla and put  chili  and  cheese  on top. 
 Put the plate in the oven. 
 Heat the oven 450 °F. 
 Notes : 
 
 ↑   Note: can use regular Hormel  chili  or  turkey , but I like to have less fat.
