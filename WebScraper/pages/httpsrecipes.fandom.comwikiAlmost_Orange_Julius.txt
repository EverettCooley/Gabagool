Almost Orange Julius
 1/3 can frozen orange juice concentrate
1/2 cup milk -- cold
1/2 cup water -- cold
2 tablespoons sugar
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 teaspoon almond extract
6 ice cubes
 Combine all ingredients in blender until smooth.
 Enjoy.
