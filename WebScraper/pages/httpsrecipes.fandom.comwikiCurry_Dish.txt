Ingredients : 
 2 cups of  water 
 1  Onion 
 1 cup of  oatmeal ,  quinoa , or  rice 
 1 red pepper 
 1/2 tsp  curry 
 1  celery , thinly sliced 
 1/2 tsp of salt-free  Spike 
 Directions : 
 Cook  grain , then add spice and vegetables 5 minutes before finished cooking.
