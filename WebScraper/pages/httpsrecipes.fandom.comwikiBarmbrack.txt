Barmbrack
 
Categories: Breads
 
 1 c Hot Strong Tea 
 1/2 c Chopped  dates 
 1/2 c Chopped Mixed Peel 
 1 c Washed  Raisins 
 1 c  brown sugar 
 1  egg , Beaten 
 2 c  all-purpose flour 
 1 ts  baking powder 
 1/4 ts Soda 
 1/4 ts  salt 
 
Pour Tea Over Fruit And  sugar  And Let Stand
 Over-Night. Next Morning Stir in  egg , Then  flour , baking powder , Soda And  salt  Sifted Together. TurnInto Greased Loaf Pan And Bake For 1 1/2 Hours At
 
 300f. Turn Onto Wire Rack to Cool. to Serve, SliceAnd  butter . 
 
Enjoy.
