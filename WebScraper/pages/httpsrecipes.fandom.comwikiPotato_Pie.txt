      Potato Pie     
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups  cottage cheese 
 10 boiled and mashed  potatoes 
 ½ cup  sour cream 
 4  eggs 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ½ cup  scallions , thinly sliced 
 1 cup grated hard  cheese 
 greens 
 Directions : 
 Put the  cheese  through a food mill or sieve to make it smooth. 
 Beat the mashed  potatoes  into the  cottage cheese . 
 Beat in the  sour cream ,  eggs ,  salt  and chopped greens. 
 Stir in scallions and put in a well greased with  oil  mould. 
 Sprinkle the top with grated  cheese . 
 Bake in 425°F until golden brown on top.
