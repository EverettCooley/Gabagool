Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Davies Estate in Malakoff, Texas in 1981.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 ounce  whiskey 
 ⅓ ounce  Bénédictine 
 ⅔ ounce  half-and-half 
 Directions : 
 Place a few cubes of ice in a tumbler then pour in the whiskey and benedictine and stir. 
 Place tip of a bar spoon in liquid and gently pour cream over top to create a layer.
