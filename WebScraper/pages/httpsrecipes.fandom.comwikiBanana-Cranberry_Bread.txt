Ingredients : 
 1-pound recipe (8 slices) : 
 1½ tsp  yeast 
 ¾ tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 ¾ cup multi-grain  oatmeal 
 2¼ cup  bread flour 
 3 tbsp  molasses 
 2 tbsp lite  applesauce 
 ¼ cup  egg substitute 
 ¾ cup mashed  bananas 
 ¾ cup  water 
 ¼ cup  dried cranberries 
 1½ pound recipe (12 slices) : 
 2½ tsp  yeast 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1½ tsp  cinnamon 
 1 cup multi-grain  oatmeal 
 3 cup  bread flour 
 ¼ cup  molasses 
 3 tbsp lite  applesauce 
 ¼ cup  egg substitute 
 1  egg  white 
 1 cup mashed  bananas 
 1 cup  water 
 ½ cup  dried cranberries 
 Directions : 
 Add ingredients in the order suggested by the  bread  machine manufacturer and follow instructions provided in the manual.
