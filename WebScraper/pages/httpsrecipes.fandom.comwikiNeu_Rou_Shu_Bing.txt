Description : 
 Fried  beef  and  potato  cakes
 
 Ingredients : 
 150 g  minced beef 
 300 g  potatoes 
 50 ml  bread crumbs 
 50 ml  flour 
 Seasoning : 
 ½ tbsp  salt 
 ⅓ tbsp  sugar 
 2 tbsp  cornflour 
 1  egg 
 1 tbsp mashed  garlic 
 1 tbsp mashed  shallot 
 some  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Mix  flour  and bread crumb then put in a plate. 
 Peel the  potato , cut into thick slices. 
 Steam  potatoes  for 20 minutes until  potatoes  are tender. 
 Mash  potato  while it is still hot. 
 Marinate  beef  with seasoning and mashed  potato . 
 Mix well and make into a biscuit shape. 
 Coat  potato  cake both side with  flour  and bread crumb. 
 Deep fry  potato  cakes until golden brown, serve.
