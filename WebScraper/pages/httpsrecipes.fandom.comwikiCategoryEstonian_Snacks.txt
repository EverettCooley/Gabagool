 Besides having many meat dish recipes, the Estonian cuisine also contains many snacks recipes. These foods are served between the main meals of the day. Many types of breads and cakes are consumed as snacks, in Estonia. The most famous are:
 
 Estonian Barley Skillet Bread 
 Estonian Fried Smelts Kalavorm 
 Estonian oatmeal cookies 
 Estonian style mincemeat rissole 
 Leavened rye bread 
 Gingerbread
