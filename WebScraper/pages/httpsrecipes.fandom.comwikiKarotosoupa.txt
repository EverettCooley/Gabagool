       Karotosoupa     
 Carrot soup, a nutritious meal for the winter time! Serves 4 persons!
 
 Ingredients : 
 Â½ kg  carrots 
 1 medium  onion 
 parsley 
 2 tsp  butter 
 salt 
 pepper 
 Optionally : 
 1 cube  chicken stock 
 1 tsp  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Peel and grate the  carrots . 
 Put them in a saucepan. 
 Clean and chop the  onion . 
 Put it in the saucepan and boil in a small quantity of  water . 
 When the  carrots  and the  onion  boil, blend them well. 
 Melt  butter  in a clean saucepan, add 5 cups of  water ,  parsley ,  salt ,  pepper  and the blended mash of  carrots . 
 Simmer soup for half an hour. 
 Optionally add  sugar  and a cube of  chicken stock  for extra taste.
