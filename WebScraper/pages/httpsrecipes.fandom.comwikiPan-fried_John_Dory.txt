       Pan-fried John Dory     
 To experience John dory is something you won't soon forget. Mild, sweet, and flavorful, this delicate fish will melt in your mouth and leave you pondering why you didn't try it sooner.
 
 Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time:  15 minutes 
 Yields: 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 large  eggs 
 ¾ teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1 cup  all purpose flour 
 3½ cups fresh  breadcrumbs  made from (crustless)  Italian bread 
 4 x 6-ounce skinless  John dory  fillets 
 ¼ cup extra virgin  olive oil 
 lemon  wedges 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 250°F. 
 In a shallow dish, whisk eggs with ¾ teaspoon salt and ½ teaspoon pepper. 
 Place flour on a plate and place breadcrumbs on another plate. 
 Dredge fish in flour, making sure to shake off excess. 
 Dip fish into seasoned eggs and coat with breadcrumbs. 
 Transfer fillets to baking sheet. 
 Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. 
 Gently place 2 fillets into the oil and cook for 3 minutes on each side. 
 Coating should be golden brown and fillets should be opaque in the center. 
 Transfer fillets to baking sheet and place them in the oven to keep them warm. 
 Repeat until all fish has been cooked. 
 Garnish with lemon wedges and serve.
