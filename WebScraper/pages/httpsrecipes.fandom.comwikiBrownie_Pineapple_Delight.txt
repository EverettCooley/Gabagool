Brownie Pineapple Delight
 1 pkg. chocolate brownie mix 
1 container strawberries 
1 or 2 bananas
8 oz. pkg. cream cheese at room temperature 
1 sm. can crushed pineapples with juice 
Cool Whip (optional)
 Prepare and bake brownie mix as instructed on package. Let cool 
completely. Mix cream cheese and pineapples together, spread over 
cooled brownies. Put sliced strawberries and bananas over top and 
optional Cool Whip. Cut into squares.
