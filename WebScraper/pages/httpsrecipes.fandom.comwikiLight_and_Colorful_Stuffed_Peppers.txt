Ingredients : 
 4 green  bell pepper 
 1 lbs lean  Beef  ground round 
 1  Onion 
 8 oz canned  tomato  sauce 
 1/3 lbs  Mushroom 
 1 tsp dried  oregano 
 1 tsp  thyme  leaves 
 1/4 tsp ground  marjoram 
 1/4 tsp  salt 
 1/4 tsp  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Trim, core and blanch  peppers ; reserve. Cook and stir  Beef  and  Onion  in non-stick skillet until  Beef  is browned and  Onion  is soft. Stir in  tomato  sauce,  Mushrooms , herbs,  salt  and  pepper . Stuff  Beef  mixture in  peppers . Place  peppers  in casserole dish. Pour in enough boiling  water  to cover bottom of casserole dish. Bake in 350 °F. Oven until  peppers  are tender, about 20 minutes.
