Description : 
 Source:  Recipes for Diabetics  by Billie Little and Penny L. Thorup
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  ground beef 
 1 cup  onion , chopped finely 
 1 clove  garlic , chopped finely 
 2 tbsp  chili powder 
 1¼ cup condensed  tomato soup  undiluted 
 2 cup  kidney beans 
 1 tbsp  malt vinegar 
 ¼ tsp  salt  (all right) 
 Directions : 
 Brown  beef  in hot pan, stir in  onion , garlic, and  chili powder . 
 Cook, stirring often until  onion  is tender; add rest of ingredients; bring to a boil. 
 Reduce heat and simmer, uncovered, stirring occasionally, about 15 minutes. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Food exchange per servings: 1 starch/bread + ½ fat exchange + 2 meat + 1 vegetable
