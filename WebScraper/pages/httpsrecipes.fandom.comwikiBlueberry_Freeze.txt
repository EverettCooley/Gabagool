Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  vodka 
 ¼ oz  wildberry schnapps 
 1 oz  cream of coconut 
 2 oz  blueberries 
 1½ oz crushed  pineapples 
 1 scoop vanilla  ice cream 
 8 oz crushed  ice 
 1 tbsp  whipped cream 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients thoroughly and pour into a specialty glass. 
 Top with  whipped cream  and garnish with a teaspoon of  blueberries .
