 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  peanut butter 
 1 cup  powdered sugar 
 1 cup  rice crisps  (such as "Rice Krispies") 
 3 tablespoons melted  butter 
 1 cup semisweet  chocolate chips 
 1 tablespoon  shortening 
 Directions : 
 Mix the peanut butter, powdered sugar, rice crisps, and melted butter. 
 Roll into 12 balls and place on waxed paper until all mixture is used. 
 Melt chocolate chips and shortening together. 
 Dip balls into chocolate mixture and once more set onto wax paper to harden.
