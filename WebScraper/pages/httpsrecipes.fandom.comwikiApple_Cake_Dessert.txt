 Description : 
 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1/4 cup  butter  or  margarine 
 1 cup  Sugar 
 1  egg 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1 Teaspoon  baking soda 
 1 teaspoon  cinnamon 
 1 cup  flour 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 2 Tablespoons HOT  water 
 1/2 cup chopped walnuts 
 2 1/2 cups peeled, cored and diced  Apples 
 Directions : 
 cream  together the  butter  and  Sugar . 
 Next ADD:  egg ,  salt ,  baking soda ,  cinnamon , flour ,  vanilla , Hot  water ,  walnuts , 2 1/2 cups  Apples 
 MIX: until well blended and moist 
 DO NOT OVER MIX! Best to mix this by hand with wooden spoon. 
 POUR: into a greased 9Â inch round cake pan 
 Bake: in preheated 350 F. degree oven for 45 minutes.
