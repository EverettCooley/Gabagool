Description : 
 Old-fashioned flavor and worth the wait!
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  pea   or   navy beans 
 water 
 2 tsp dry  mustard 
 dash of  pepper 
 1 tbsp  salt 
 3  onions , chopped 
 ¼ cup  brown sugar 
 ¼ cup  molasses 
 2 tbsp  sweet pickle  juice 
 ¼ lb  salt pork 
 Directions : 
 Wash and clean the beans. 
 Cover with  water  and soak overnight. 
 Drain. 
 Add 2 cups  water ,  mustard ,  pepper ,  salt ,  onions ,  brown sugar ,  molasses , pickle juice. 
 Boil, covered, for about 1½ hours. (Beans should be wrinkled.) 
 Heat oven to 250 °F. 
 Cut  salt pork  into ½” cubes. 
 Place in 2-quart bean pot. 
 Cover with hot beans and their liquid. 
 Sprinkle with  pepper . 
 Bake 8 hrs. covered, until very tender. 
 When beans are baking for 6 hrs. add about ¾ cup water or just enough to cover. 
 Uncover the pot the last ½ hour.
