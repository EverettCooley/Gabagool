Ingredients : 
 1 cup cooked  string beans 
 1 cup cooked  red beans  or  peas 
 1 cup cooked  Broad beans 
 1/2 cup  vinegar 
 1/2 cup  oil 
 1/2  onion  finely chopped 
 Chopped  mint  Leaves 
 Pinch of  sugar 
 Pinch of  salt 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together. 
 
 
 Notes : 
 Serves 4. 
 Country : Bahamas 
 Course : Salad
