 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 12 oz chilled  champagne 
 12 oz chilled  7-Up 
 8 oz chilled  orange juice 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients and serve in tall wine glasses with 2  ice  cubes in each. 
 Garnish with  orange  slice or  strawberry .
