Description : 
 That  cheese  you might have tasted at grandma's place...here is a recipe to make a fine veggy  cheese , Belgian-style, for any moment of your day ! Enjoy ! 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups low-fat  cottage cheese 
 1½ teaspoons  white wine vinegar  or  champagne vinegar 
 1 small  shallot  — finely chopped 
 1 tablespoon  parsley  — finely chopped 
 1 tablespoon  chervil  — finely chopped 
 salt  and  pepper  — to taste 
 1 tablespoon  hazelnut oil  — optional 
 6 slices good bread—fresh or toasted 
 1 medium  black radish  — thinly sliced 
 vegetable crudités — optional 
 Directions : 
 In blender, blend  cottage cheese  until smooth. 
 Remove to large bowl, add  vinegar ,  shallot ,  parsley , and  chervil . 
 Season with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Add  hazelnut oil  if using. 
 Serve on thick slices of good bread and decorate with the  radish  slices.
