 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 c.  brown sugar , packed 
 1 c.  oil 
 2  eggs 
 1 c.  buttermilk 
 2 tsp.  vanilla 
 3 c.  flour 
 1 tsp. soda 
 1 tsp.  salt 
 3/4 c. chopped  nuts 
 2 c. finely sliced  rhubarb . 
 
 
 Toping : 
 1/2 c.  brown sugar 
 2 tsp.  cinnamon 
 1/2 c. chopped  nuts 
 Directions : 
 Combine  Sugar ,  oil ,  eggs ,  milk  and  vanilla  in mixing bowl; mix well. 
 Sift  flour , soda and  salt  together. 
 Stir into liquid mixture. 
 Blend in  nuts  and  rhubarb . 
 Spoon into muffin tins and sprinkle with topping. 
 Bake at 325 degrees 25â€“30 minutes. 
 Makes 24 or more big muffins.
