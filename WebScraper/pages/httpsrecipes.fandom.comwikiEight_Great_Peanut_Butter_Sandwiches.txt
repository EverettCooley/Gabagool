 Description : 
 These spreads are best cold. They make good fillings for taking to work or school. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1st sandwich : 
 2 tbsp  peanut butter 
 2 tbsp sesame  tahini 
 1 tbsp  sunflower seeds 
 2  figs , chopped 
 2nd sandwich : 
 2 tbsp  peanut butter 
 ¼ cup  celery , chopped 
 2 tbsp  yogurt 
 2 tbsp  peanuts , chopped 
 3rd sandwich : 
 2 tbsp  peanut butter 
 2 tbsp  Cheddar  cheese, grated 
 4th sandwich : 
 ¼ cup  tahini 
 ¼ cup  peanut butter 
 ¼ cup  raisins 
 2 tbsp  milk powder 
 5th sandwich : 
 2 tbsp  peanut butter 
 ½  banana , mashed 
 2 tbsp  non-fat milk powder 
 ½ tsp  vanilla 
 6th sandwich : 
 2 tbsp  peanut butter 
 ¼ cup  alfalfa sprouts 
 1 tbsp  sesame seeds 
 8 to 10 slices  cucumber 
 1 tsp  mayonnaise 
 7th sandwich : 
 2 tbsp  peanut butter 
 2 tbsp  raisins 
 ¼ cup  apple , chopped 
 8th sandwich : 
 8 oz  tofu , mashed 
 ¼ cup  peanut butter 
 1  banana 
 1 tsp  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients for spread 
 Spread on your favourite bread
