 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  light rum 
 ¼ oz  lime juice 
 ½ oz  triple sec 
 ½ oz  cherry brandy 
 Directions : 
 Fill a shaker half full with  ice cubes . 
 Pour all ingredients into shaker and shake well. 
 Strain drink into a cocktail glass, garnish with a  maraschino cherry , and serve.
