 Spiced Eggnog Cheesecake with White Chocolate Garnish from Recipes Wiki—original source of recipe, licensed under the GNU Free Documentation License
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Makes 12 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 Crust : 
 8 boards  graham crackers 
 1 tsp  sugar 
 ¼ tsp each ground  ginger ,  nutmeg  and  cinnamon 
 ¼ cup  unsalted butter , melted 
 Filling : 
 4 pkgs 8 oz each  cream cheese 
 ¾ cup  sugar 
 ¼ cup  flour 
 ¼ tsp each  nutmeg  and  cinnamon 
 pinch ground  cloves 
 1  egg 
 1¾ cups  eggnog 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 8 oz container  sour cream 
 Garnish : 
 6 squares (1 oz each)  white baking chocolate 
 pinch  nutmeg 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 350°F. 
 Crust : 
 Crush crackers in plastic bag. 
 Add sugar and next 3 ingredients. 
 Add butter, knead bag to blend. 
 Press over bottom of 9 inch round spring-form pan. 
 Bake crust 10 minutes. 
 Cool. 
 Filling : 
 Beat cream cheese in bowl until smooth. 
 Mix sugar and next 4 ingredients in bowl. 
 Add to cream cheese, beat until smooth. 
 Beat in egg, eggnog and vanilla, pour into pan. 
 Bake 1 hour. 
 Remove from oven, spread with sour cream. 
 Bake 5 more minutes. 
 Cool in pan. 
 Refrigerate until serving. 
 Garnish : 
 Melt chocolate in bowl over saucepan of simmering water. 
 Add nutmeg. 
 Line loaf pan with foil. 
 Pour chocolate into pan, cool until firm. 
 Lift from pan, make curls. 
 Arrange on top of cake.
