Description : 
 Makes 6 servings and ⅔ cup  salad dressing 
 
 preparation 15 minutes 
 Ingredients : 
 Salad dressing : 
 3 tbsp light  Mayonnaise 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 2 tbsp  red wine vinegar 
 1 tbsp  Dijon mustard 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 dash  hot pepper sauce 
 1 dash  Worcestershire sauce 
 ¼ cup  olive oil 
 2 tbsp  water 
 Salad : 
 8 cups  romaine lettuce  leaves torn 
 1½ cup toasted bread  croutons 
 1 tbsp grated  parmesan cheese 
 Directions : 
 Salad dressing : 
 Whisk together  mayonnaise ,  garlic ,  vinegar ,  mustard ,  lemon juice ,  hot pepper sauce  and  worcestershire sauce . 
 Gradually whisk in  olive oil  and  water  until blended and smooth. 
 Salad : 
 Place torn  lettuce , croutons and  cheese  in large salad bowl. 
 Toss with ⅓ cup  salad dressing  (about ½ of dressing). 
 Refrigerate remaining dressing for a second salad. 
 For single serving of salad, use about 1 cup  lettuce , ¼ cup croutons, 1 tbsp  salad dressing  and 1 tsp  Parmesan cheese .
