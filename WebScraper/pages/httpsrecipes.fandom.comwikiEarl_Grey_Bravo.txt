Premium black tea from Sri Lanka, flavored with oil of bergamot, a citrus fruit. According to legend, this tea is named for Earl Grey, a British Prime Minister, to whom a Chinese noble entrusted its secret recipe for saving his life.
 
 Type - Black Tea 
 Origin - Ceylon 
 Combination - Black Tea+bergamot oil
