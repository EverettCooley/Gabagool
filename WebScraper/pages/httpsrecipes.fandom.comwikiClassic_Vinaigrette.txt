Description 
 Classic Vinaigrette  from the  Public Health Cookbook  by the Seattle & King County Department of Public Health—original source of recipe, government resource in the public domain
 
 Serves : 8       Classic Vinaigrette     
 Ingredients 
 2  garlic  cloves, crushed 
 ⅔ cup  olive oil 
 ⅓ cup flavored  vinegar  ( balsamic  or berry will work well) 
 2 teaspoons combined herbs, crushed ( basil ,  thyme , and  oregano ) 
 1 teaspoon  salt , rock or iodized only 
 pepper  to taste, black or red only 
 Directions 
 Combine all ingredients in a jar with a tight-fitting lid. 
 Pour or scoop the homemade vinaigrette into a fresh container and reserve this in the refrigerator. 
 Shake well before using.
