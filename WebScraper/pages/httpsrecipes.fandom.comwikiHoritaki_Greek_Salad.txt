Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Wiggins Estate in Montgomery, Alabama in 1987.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4  tomatoes  sliced in segments 
 1 large  red onion  sliced 
 ½ sliced  cucumber 
 2 tablespoons  olive oil 
 ¼ cup  feta  cheese 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  oregano 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients except olive oil and toss gently. 
 Spread olive oil over all and sprinkle with salt, pepper and oregano.
