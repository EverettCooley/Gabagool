Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  ground beef 
 1  egg 
 ½ cup  bread crumbs 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 ⅓ cup  milk 
 ½ tsp  thyme 
 1 cup cubed  carrots 
 2 cups cubed  potatoes 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 1 can  cream of mushroom  soup 
 ½ tsp  paprika 
 Directions : 
 Combine first 7 ingredients. 
 Roll into balls. 
 Brown in 1 tbsp margarine. 
 Parboil carrots and potatoes in small amount of water, for 5 minutes. 
 Drain. 
 Combine sour cream, soup and paprika. 
 Place meatballs in casserole dish. 
 Add carrots and potatoes and top with soup mixture. 
 Bake at 375°F for 30 minutes.
