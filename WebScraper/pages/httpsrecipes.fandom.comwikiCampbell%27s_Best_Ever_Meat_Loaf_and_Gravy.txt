Description : 
 I have a ton of  onion  recipes collected plus the ones that I created. I took Campbell's best ever meat loaf and manipulated it because I love  onions .
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Makes 6 to 8 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can  cream of onion  soup 
 2 lbs  ground beef 
 ½ cup fine dry  breadcrumbs 
 1  egg , slightly beaten 
 ⅓ cup finely chopped  onion 
 2 teaspoons  garlic powder  (or to taste) 
 2 tablespoons  Worcestershire sauce  (or to taste) 
 2 tablespoons  black pepper  (or to taste) 
 Optional additions : 
 cayenne pepper 
 cheese 
 a hint of  cinnamon  (goes great with  beef ). 
 Gravy : 
 1 teaspoon  Worcestershire sauce  (or more to taste) optional depending on how thick you like it 
 ¼ cup  milk  or  water 
 Directions : 
 Mix thoroughly ½ can soup,  beef , breadcrumbs,  egg ,  onion ,  Worcestershire sauce  and  black pepper . 
 Shape firmly into loaf (8x4 inch); place in shallow baking pan. 
 Bake at 375°F for 1 hour, 15 minutes. 
 In saucepan, blend remaining soup and  Worcestershire sauce  (or milk or  water ). 
 Heat, stir occasionally. 
 Spoon on top of loaf or serve in gravy dish.
