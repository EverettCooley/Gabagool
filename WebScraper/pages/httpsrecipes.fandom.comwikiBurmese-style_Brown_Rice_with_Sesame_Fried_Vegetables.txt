Description : 
 This is a very flavorful and filling vegetarian dish.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  brown rice 
 1 teaspoon  sea salt 
 2  green onions 
 1  radish 
 1  carrot 
 1 slice  cucumber 
 3 tablespoons cooking  oil 
 1 teaspoon  garlic , finely chopped 
 1 tablespoon sesame  tahini 
 sesame seeds 
 soy sauce 
 lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Wash  rice  thoroughly. 
 Boil in 5 cups of  water  with  sea salt  until soft and tender. 
 While  rice  is cooking, wash vegetables. 
 Slice  onion  finely. 
 Cut  radish  in half. 
 Cut  carrot  diagonally then slice in strips. 
 Slice  cucumber  and cut each slice in four. 
 In a wok or pan, heat  oil  and stir-fry  garlic  until it turns golden brown. 
 Add all the vegetables and stir-fry for 3 minutes. 
 Add  sesame seeds  and stir-fry for 2 more minutes. 
 Turn off heat and set aside. 
 When the  rice  is cooked, drain and add in the fried vegetables and  sesame seeds . 
 Stir the  rice  and vegetables. 
 Add soy sauce and  lemon juice . 
 Serve hot with sesame tahini.
