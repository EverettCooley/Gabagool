      Spice Parisienne     The market name for a complex spice and herb blend, also called Epices Fines. French cooks usually make their own blends, which can vary greatly depending on the individual. In general, Spice Parisienne includes  white pepper ,  allspice ,  mace ,  nutmeg ,  cloves ,  cinnamon ,  bay leaves ,  sage ,  marjoram  and  rosemary . As with all spices, this blend should be stored in a cool, dark place for no more than 6 months.
