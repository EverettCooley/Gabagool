Description : 
 In Romanian: Pasta de brinza de vaca
 
 Ingredients : 
 14 oz / 400 g  cottage cheese 
 7 oz / 200 g  butter 
 ½ cup  sour cream 
 2  eggs 
 ¼ cup  milk 
 4 oz / 100 g  raisins 
 2 oz / 50 g ground  walnuts 
 salt 
 1 cup  confectioner's sugar 
 vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Sieve or grind the  cheese . 
 Beat the  eggs  with the  sugar , add  milk ,  salt ,  vanilla  and set on the range, stirring until it starts to thicken. 
 Remove from heat, add  butter , mix well. 
 When the mixture is almost cold, add  cheese ,  sour cream ,  raisins  and  walnuts . 
 Put this mixture into a cloth, tie tightly and weigh down with something heavy for 5 – 6 hours. 
 Keep refrigerated. 
 Then remove from the cloth and cut like a layer cake.
