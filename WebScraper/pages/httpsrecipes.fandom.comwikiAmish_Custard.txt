      Amish Custard     
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  flour 
 1 stick  butter 
 1 cup chopped  pecans 
 8 oz  cream cheese 
 1 cup  powdered sugar 
 1 cup  Cool Whip 
 instant chocolate pudding 
 Directions : 
 melt   butter , add  flour  and  pecans . 
 Spread  mixture  in bottom of a cake pan. 
 Make  pudding . 
 Mix  cream cheese ,  powdered sugar  and  Cool Whip . 
 Cook crust at 350 °F for 20 minutes. 
 Let cool. 
 Layer mixtures on crust. 
 Top with  Cool Whip .
