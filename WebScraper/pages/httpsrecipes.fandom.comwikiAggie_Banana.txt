Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 scoops vanilla  ice cream 
 1 cup whole  milk 
 2 sliced  bananas 
 2 oz  butterscotch schnapps 
 Directions : 
 Place  bananas  in an uncovered plastic bowl to freeze. 
 Allow frost to form on them. 
 Blend  ice   cream ,  milk , and frozen  bananas  until smooth. 
 Add Schnapps, blend, and pour.
