 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 x  eggs 
 1/8 tsp  basil ,dried—ground 
 1/2 cup  cottage cheese  -- lowfat 
 1/4 cup  green pepper  -- chopped 
 1/4 cup  Cheddar   Cheese  -- shredded 
 2 tbl	 oil  -- sesame 
 1 tbl	 milk ,non-instant—skim 
 Directions : 
 Put  eggs ,  cottage cheese ,  Cheddar   Cheese , powdered skim  milk  and  basil  in blender and mix for 20 seconds. 
 Pour  oil  in skillet and add mixture plus  green peppers . 
 Scramble slowly over very low flame.
