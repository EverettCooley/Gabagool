Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Bailey Estate in Mesquite, Texas in 1989. This one is tried and true. I have made this several times and it is awesome.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  cottage cheese 
 ½ cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 3 tablespoons  mayonnaise 
 2  garlic  cloves minced 
 ¼ teaspoon  hot sauce 
 1 can  artichoke hearts  drained and chopped fine 
 48  Melba rounds 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F then coat a pie plate with nonstick cooking spray. 
 Combine  cottage cheese ,  Parmesan cheese ,  mayonnaise , garlic and hot sauce in a food processor and process until smooth. 
 Transfer mixture to a bowl and stir in artichokes. 
 Spoon mixture into dish and bake for 20 minutes. 
 Serve with Melba rounds.
