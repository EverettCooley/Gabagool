Description : 
 This recipe taken from www.Portuguese-recipes.com 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 and 1/4 cup of  flour 
 2 cups of  Sugar ( you can use less) 
 1/2 tsp. of  salt 
 1/2 tsp. of  baking soda 
 1 tsp. of grated  lemon peel  ( optional) 
 1 tsp. of  vanilla 
 1 cup of  margarine 
 1 carton of(8 oz.)  pineapple  or  mandarin orange   yogurt  or 1 cup of  sour cream 
 3  eggs ( 3/4 of a cup of liquid  eggs ) 
 Greased and floured 12 flute cup or tube pan 
 Directions : 
 Mix at low speed. The beat at medium speed for 3 minutes. 
 Bake at 325 for 60 or 70 minutes. 
 Cool upright for 15 minutes. 
 Remove from pan
