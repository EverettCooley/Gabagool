Description : 
 Always check the ingredients to make sure the product is vegan.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  soya milk 
 1½ cups  water 
 ¼ cup  cashew  nuts 
 1 tablespoon  sea salt 
 ⅛ teaspoon  white pepper 
 1 dash  nutmeg 
 ¼ cup  sesame oil 
 ½ cup  unbleached flour 
 Directions : 
 Blend the  soymilk ,  water , cashews, and seasonings in a blender until smooth. 
 Pour into a double boiler and heat. 
 Meanwhile, heat the  oil  in a small saucepan. 
 Add the  flour  to make a roux. 
 Then whisk it into the heated  soy milk  mixture to make a light, creamy sauce. 
 For a thicker sauce, use more roux.
