Ingredients : 
 1  spaghetti squash 
 1 pack sliced  mushrooms 
 1 med  zucchini  squash 
 2 cup cooked  rice 
 favorite  peppers  to taste 
 garlic  to taste 
 cooking  sherry 
 Directions : 
 Bake  spaghetti squash  at 375Â°F until slightly tender (make sure to puncture skin before baking). 
 "Stir-fry"  mushrooms , diced  zucchini  squash,  peppers  until soft with  sherry . 
 When  spaghetti squash  is done, cut in half, remove seeds and inner fibers. 
 Use a fork to remove meat from  squash . 
 It will come out in strings. 
 Add  spaghetti  meat to stir fry. 
 Add more  sherry  and cook until done. 
 You will probably need a large wok to hold it all.
