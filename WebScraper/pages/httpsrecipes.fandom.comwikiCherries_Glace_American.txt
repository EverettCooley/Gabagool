Ingredients : 
 2 cups  vanilla   ice cream 
 2 cups  cherry  (or  raspberry ) ice 
 1 cup  heavy cream , whipped 
 4 cups fresh,  sweet cherries , pitted 
 2 cups crushed macaroons 
 1/2 cup  blanched almonds , chopped 
 1/2 cup  Grand Marnier  (optional) 
 garnish: stemmed, unpitted  cherries 
 Directions : 
 Combine softened  ice cream  and  cherry  or  raspberry   ice . 
 Add  whipped cream  and stir well. 
 Add pitted  cherries , crushed macaroons, and chopped  almonds . 
 Mix well and add  Grand Marnier (optional). 
 Stir again and serve mounded in a decorative bowl garnished with unpitted, stemmed  cherries .
