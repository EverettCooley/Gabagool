 Ingredients : 
 2 cups raw  brown rice 
 5 cups  water 
 4 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 2 chopped  carrots 
 2 sliced  zucchinis 
 2 spring baby  cabbage  , chopped 
 2 cups  chicken stock  ( look up directions  for preparation) 
 2 cans  kidney  or  pink beans  drained 
 2 tablespoons  tamari  sauce 
 Directions : 
 Use a medium size saucepan and bring  rice  and  water  to a boil over high heat. 
 Once it had boiled, bring the heat down and simmer the  rice , cover it, let simmer during 30 minutes or until you see that the  rice  is soft. 
 In a frying pan, heat the  oil  over medium heat, then add in the  carrots , zucchinis, and stir fry these for 3 â€“ 4 minutes. 
 Mix in the spring baby  cabbage  and cook for 2 minutes more. 
 Next add in the beans,  chicken stock , and the tamari into the frying pan and mix well. 
 Put the  rice  in the dogs bowl and put some of the bean combination over it. 
 Refrigerate any leftovers. 
 Make sure to cover these well. 
 Reheat the beans and  rice  together.
