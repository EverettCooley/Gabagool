Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Gingham Estate in Fort Worth, Texas in 1992.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ pounds uncooked  potatoes 
 ¾ pound fine  whole wheat flour 
 ½ pound  feta  cheese 
 ¼ pound smoked  bacon 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon  butter 
 Directions : 
 Grate the peeled raw  potatoes  and mix with  flour  adding a little water if necessary then add  salt . 
 Roll into balls and rinse with cold  water  then throw into bubbling salted  water  and boil for 20 minutes. 
 When they float to the surface take them out rinse with water and mix with  feta  cheese. 
 Serve with  bacon  cut up into small pieces and mix with  feta   cheese . 
 Fry in  butter .
