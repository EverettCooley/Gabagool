 Description : 
 Always check the ingredients to make sure the product is vegan.
 
 Ingredients : 
 Saltines (20 or so) 
 12 oz.  chocolate chips 
 2 sticks of soy marg 
 1 cup  brown sugar 
 1/2 cup chopped  nuts  (I used  almonds ) 
 Directions : 
 Foil a 9 X 14 (or about that size) baking pan. 
 Place one layer of the saltines on the bottom. 
 Melt marg and  brown sugar  on stove, stirring constantly. 
 Bring to a boil, and boil for 3 minutes. Pour this over the saltines. 
 Bake at 400 degrees for 5 â€“ 7 minutes, until golden brown. 
 Pour  chocolate chips  over it, and place it back in the oven for a minute or so until the chips are melted. 
 Spread  chocolate  with a knife. Sprinkle with  nuts . 
 Let it cool and harden in the fridge overnight.
