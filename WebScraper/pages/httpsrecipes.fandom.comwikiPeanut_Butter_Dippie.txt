Ingredients : 
 1 tsp smooth  Peanut butter 
 1 tsp  mayonnaise 
 a few drops of  milk  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 whip together ingredients 
 If still too thick, add a few drops of  milk . 
 Serve with your choice of fruits or vegetables or crackers
