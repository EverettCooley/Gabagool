Description : 
 Adeline, Juneau, Alaska 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 small bag salad  macaroni  -- ( small seashell ) 
 1 med stack  broccoli , chopped 
 1 small chopped  onion 
 1  garlic  clove, chopped 
 1½ cup  salmon , canned or cooked 
 1 small jar  artichoke hearts —chopped 
 1 small bag  sliced almonds 
 1 tbsp  mustard 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 1 tsp  celery salt 
 ½ tsp  horseradish 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Boil macaroni-when almost done add chopped  broccoli . 
 Drain. 
 Add rest of the ingredients. 
 Garnish with  parsley ,  paprika  and  almonds . 
 Best if flavors blend in refrigerator overnight.
