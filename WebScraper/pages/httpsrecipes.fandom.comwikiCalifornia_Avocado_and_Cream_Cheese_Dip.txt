 Description : 
 The mouthwatering texture of this rich green dip is the perfect base for its fresh, slightly garlicky flavour. A popular favorite for all times of the year.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large  California avocado , seed removed 
 4 oz Cooked  spinach , 100 g - chopped 
 2 oz Low-fat curd  Cheese  - mashed 
 1 clove of  garlic , crushed 
 1 tsp to 2  lemon juice 
 2 spring  onions , finely chopped 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Scoop the flesh out of the  avocado  and blend it thoroughly in the food-processor with the  spinach . 
 Add the mashed curd  Cheese  and liquidize again. Then mix in the  garlic  and  lemon juice  to taste, and stir in the chopped spring  onions . 
 Season to taste with  salt  and  paprika . 
 Chill for a while before serving.
