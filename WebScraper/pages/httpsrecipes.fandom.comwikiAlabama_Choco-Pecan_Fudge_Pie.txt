 Description : 
 This grand American pie is made with real chocolate, coffee, pecans, chocolate wafers, and flour and has a lush aroma with the vanilla content. Serve up a great, luscious treat with other desserts to make it sweet.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 ¼ cup chocolate wafer 
 2 ⅓ cups  butter 
 ¾ cup firmly packed  brown sugar 
 3 large  eggs 
 2 ¾ cups semi-sweet  chocolate morsels , melted 
 2 tsp. instant  coffee  granules/powder 
 1 tsp.  vanilla extract 
 ½ cup  flour 
 1 cup coarsely chopped  pecans 
 Directions : 
 Combine chocolate crumbs and 1 ⅓ cup melted butter. 
 Firmly press the mixture on bottom and sides from the 9-inch pie pan. Bake at 350° F for 6-8 minutes. 
 Cream 1 cup butter; gradually add brown sugar, beating at medium speed until blended. 
 Add eggs, one at a time, beating after each addition. Stir with the melted chocolate and the next 4 ingredients. 
 Pour into the crust. Bake at 375° F for 25 minutes. 
 Cool completely on a wire rack. Garnish with whipped cream, if desired. Serve cold and plain but cut into serving pieces with the dessert knife or the kitchen knife.
