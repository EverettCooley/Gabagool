Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 6/24/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 8 lb  pork shoulder  roast 
 ¼ cup  apple juice 
 ¼ cup  vinegar 
 ½ cup  brown sugar 
 4 tbsp  Dijon mustard 
 2 tbsp  brown sugar 
 6 tbsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 1 tbsp  onion powder 
 2 cups  ketchup 
 1 cup  chili sauce 
 ¼ cup  lemon juice 
 ½ tbsp  cayenne  pepper 
 3 tbsp  salt 
 8 tsp  paprika 
 2 tbsp  garlic powder 
 3 tsp  onion powder 
 2 tbsp  black pepper 
 2½ tsp  thyme 
 2½ tsp  oregano 
 Directions : 
 Combine all and cook on low in crockpot 8 – 10 hours or until meat pulls apart and looks done. 
 Serve on bread.
