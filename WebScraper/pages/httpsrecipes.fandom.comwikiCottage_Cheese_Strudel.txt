 Description : 
 In Romanian: Strudel cu brinza de vaca
 
 Ingredients : 
 strudel dough 
 confectioner's sugar  with  vanilla  flavor 
 Filling : 
 1Â¾ lbs / 750 g  cottage cheese 
 2  eggs 
 2 tablespoons  sugar  (optional) 
 salt 
 3 tablespoons melted  butter  or  lard 
 Directions : 
 Mix the  cheese  with the  eggs ,  salt  and  sugar . 
 Knead with your hands. 
 Arrange the  cheese  mixture at one end of the greased  strudel dough . 
 Cover with the edge of the dough and roll like a jelly roll. 
 Moisten the  strudel dough  with melted  butter  or  lard . 
 You get a long roll which you can cut in 2 or 3, depending on the size of your baking pan. 
 Arrange in the greased pan, roll next to roll, with a little distance among them. 
 Grease the tops with melted  butter  or  lard . Bake until golden brown. 
 Cut into serving size portions while still in the pan. 
 When it is done, cut and sprinkle with  vanilla  flavored  sugar .
