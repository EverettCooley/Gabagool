 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  strong coffee 
 1/2 cup  Sugar  or to taste 
 3 cups mocha-flavored  soymilk 
 2 6-oz. containers  vanilla   soy yogurt 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 1 cup  chocolate chips , optional 
 
 
 Directions : 
 1. Combine  coffee  and  Sugar  in saucepan, and heat over medium heat, stirring often, until  Sugar  dissolves.
 
 2. Combine  soymilk ,  yogurt  and  vanilla extract  in mixing bowl, and beat until smooth. Add  coffee  mixture, and stir well. 3. Freeze mixture according to manufacturerâ€™s directions. About 5 minutes before churning is complete, add  chocolate chips , if using.
