Its very delicious.
 
 Ingredients : 
 One small white  Onion 
 two  garlic  cloves 
 One cup uncooked dried  Christmas lima beans 
 Four and half cups  Chicken  or  vegetable stock 
 Half cup extr virgin  olive oil 
 A quarter cup fresh grated Parmesan 
 Half cup chopped  parsley ,  salt  and peeper to taste 
 Directions : 
 Peel the  Onion  and chop coarsely. Peel the  garlic  cloves and crush with the side of a knife. 
 In a 2-quart saucepan, combine the  Onion ,  garlic  and beans. 
 Cover with two cups of stock and cook over medium-high heat until the beans are very soft and tender, about one hour and twenty-five minutes. 
 When the beans are tender, remove from the heat and spoon into a bowl to cool. 
 Puree the cooled beans in a blender or food processor with the  olive oil  until creamy and smooth. 
 Spoon the mixtureinto a largbowl and fold in the parmeasan and  parsley . 
 Add  salt  and  pepper  to taste. 
 Cover and refrigerate.
