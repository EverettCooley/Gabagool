Ingredients : 
 15  habanero chiles , seeded (20 dried habaneros - rehydrated) 
 1½ cups puréed sweet  onion 
 ½ cup  Ballpark mustard 
 2 cups fresh  mango  purée 
 1 tbsp ground  cumin 
 1 tbsp  curry powder 
 1 tbsp  chili powder 
 1 tsp  salt 
 3 tbsp  brown sugar 
 4 tbsp fresh  lime juice 
 2 tbsp  honey 
 ¼ cup fresh  orange juice 
 1  banana , very ripe 
 Directions : 
 Process everything until smooth. 
 Force through sieve or strainer. 
 Refrigerate.
