 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 pounds of  Chicken  pieces 
 1/4 cup soy sauce 
 1 clove  garlic 
 1 teaspoon grated  ginger 
 1 tsp  Sugar 
 2 tablespoons dry  sherry  (wine can be substituted) 
 1 tablespoon honey 
 salt ,  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Mix all the sauce ingredients. Marinate the  Chicken  in it for a few hours or overnight. Bake uncovered at 350 degrees Fahrenheit until brown and cooked through
Serve with  white rice .
