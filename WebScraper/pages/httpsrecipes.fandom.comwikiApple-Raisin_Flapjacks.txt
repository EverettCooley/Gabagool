Ingredients : 
 3  egg white 
 1 cup  non-fat milk 
 2 cups reduced fat  buttermilk  baking mix 
 1 medium  Granny Smith apple , peeled, cored and coarsely chopped 
 ½ cup  raisins 
 ½ teaspoon  nutmeg 
 Directions : 
 In large bowl whisk whites until foamy; whisk in  milk , then stir in baking mix. 
 Add remaining ingredients; mix just to blend. 
 Heat griddle or heavy skillet over medium heat; coat with vegetable  cooking spray . 
 Portion batter onto griddle with ¼-cup measure. 
 Cook until nicely browned, turning once, about 3 minutes on each side. 
 Serve hot with  maple syrup  or nonfat  yogurt .
