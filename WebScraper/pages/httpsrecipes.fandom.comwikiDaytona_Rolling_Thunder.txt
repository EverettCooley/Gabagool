 Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 oz  Southern Comfort Â®  peach  liqueur 
 1/2 oz  triple sec 
 1/2 oz  Bourbon whiskey 
 1/2 oz  peach  schnapps 
 orange juice 
 grapefruit juice 
 Directions : 
 Pour the  Southern Comfort ,  triple sec ,  Bourbon whiskey  and  peach  schnapps into a cocktail shaker half-filled with crushed  ice . 
 Shake well, and strain into a frosted glass mug with a few  ice cubes . 
 Fill with  orange  and  pineapple juice , to taste, and stir. 
 Garnish with a slice of  orange  centered on the rim. 
 Add a straw, and serve.
