Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 6/17/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Makes 3 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pkg.  tortellini , any flavor 
 2 cup  broccoli  florettes 
 1 pkg.  alfredo sauce 
 1 pkg. grilled  chicken breast  strips 
 Directions : 
 Prepare pasta according to package directions adding broccoli to water during last 3 minutes of pasta cooking time; drain. 
 Heat sauce and chicken breast strips in saucepan on medium heat 5 minutes or until thoroughly heated. 
 Toss all ingredients.
