Ingredients : 
 1 – 2 tbsp  milk 
 ½ tsp  vanilla 
 1 cup  powdered sugar 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 reserved  egg white 
 2 tsp  cinnamon 
 ½ tsp  nutmeg 
 1 cup  sugar 
 8 cups  mangos , peeled and sliced 
 1 cup  crushed corn flakes 
 1  egg , separated, yolk beaten with enough  milk  to equal ⅔ cup 
 1 cup  butter , softened 
 1 tsp  salt 
 2½ cups  flour 
 Directions : 
 Combine  flour  and  salt . 
 Cut in  butter  until crumbly. 
 Stir in  egg  yolk mixture until dough forms a ball. 
 Divide in two. 
 Roll one half on floured surface into 15x10" rectangle. 
 Place on ungreased pan. 
 Sprinkle with crushed  corn  flakes. 
 Layer  mangos  over crumbs. 
 Combine 1 cup  sugar , 1½ tsp  cinnamon  and  nutmeg  and sprinkle over  mangos . 
 Roll out remaining dough and place over  mangos . 
 Beat  egg  white until foamy and brush over top crust. 
 Mix 2 tbsp  sugar  and ½ tsp  cinnamon  and sprinkle over crust. 
 Bake at 350°F for 50 – 60 minutes or until lightly browned. 
 Stir together  powdered sugar ,  vanilla  and  milk  and drizzle over warm crust. 
 Cool and cut into bars of desired size.
