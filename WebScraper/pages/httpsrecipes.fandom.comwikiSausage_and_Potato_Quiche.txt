Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves 6 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups  sausage  (browned/drained/crumbled) 
 1¼ cups frozen shredded  hash brown   potatoes 
 1 cup shredded  Cheddar  cheese 
 5  eggs 
 ½ cup  milk 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅛ teaspoon  pepper 
 grated  Parmesan cheese  and  paprika  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 In a skillet, brown  sausage  and drain. 
 Spoon into an ungreased 10-inch pie pan. 
 Top with  potatoes  and  cheddar  cheese. 
 In a bowl, beat  eggs ,  milk ,  salt  and  pepper ; pour over  cheese . 
 Sprinkle with  Parmesan cheese  and  paprika . 
 Bake uncovered, in a 375°F oven for 30 – 35 minutes or until golden brown.
