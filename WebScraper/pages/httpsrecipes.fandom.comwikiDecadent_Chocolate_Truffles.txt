Description : 
 Makes about 36 truffles. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  unsalted butter , softened 
 2½ cups  confectioners' sugar 
 ½ cup  cocoa 
 ¼ cup  heavy  or  whipping cream 
 1 tablespoon  coffee liqueur  (such as  Kahlúa )  [1] 
 ½ teaspoon  vanilla 
 1 cup  semi-sweet chocolate   chips , very finely chopped 
 Directions : 
 Cream butter in large bowl. 
 Combine confectioner's sugar and cocoa. 
 Add alternately with cream, coffee liqueur and vanilla to butter. 
 Blend well. 
 Chill until firm. 
 Shape into 1-inch balls. 
 Roll in semi-sweet chocolate until coated. 
 Chill until firm. 
 Notes : 
 
 ↑   May substitute 1 tablespoon chocolate extract
