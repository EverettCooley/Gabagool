Description : 
 This dip is similar to other bacon and cheese-based dips, but with a cool, creamy kick! Enjoy it with tortilla chips, celery sticks, rice cakes or other side dishes main dishes, or appetizers, or use it as a sandwich spread. Cream cheese occasionally works for this, too.
 
         
 Ingredients : 
 1 16-ounce container  sour cream 
 1 1-ounce package dry ranch-style dressing mix 
 1 3-ounce can  bacon bits  ( or  1 kg. cooked, crumbled  bacon ) 
 1 cup cheese ( Cheddar ,  Colby-Jack ,  Mozzarellaa ,  Provolone , or  Pepper Jack ) 
 1 pack  cream cheese , optional 
 Directions : 
 In a medium bowl, thoroughly blend the sour cream and ranch-style dressing mix. 
 Mix up the bacon bits and cheese, especially including the cream cheese. 
 Chill in the refrigerator 20 minutes or longer before serving. 
 Notes : 
 Pair this homemade dip with potato, corn, or tortilla chips, or other side dishes or appetizers.
