Ingredients : 
 1 cup chopped  ham 
 1 tsp  Cajun seasoning 
 1 cup smoked  sausage , chopped 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 ½ lb party  shrimp 
 1 chopped  bell pepper 
 1 cup cooked  chicken , chopped 
 1 cup hot Paces  picante sauce 
 ½ tsp  garlic powder 
 flour  tortillas 
 2 cups  rice 
 2 cups  chicken broth 
 1 tsp  sugar 
 ⅛ tsp  black pepper 
 
 Directions : 
 Sauté  onion  and  bell pepper . 
 Add  shrimp ,  ham ,  sausage , and  chicken  and cook over medium heat for 5 minutes. 
 Add  sugar ,  black pepper , Cajun seasoning,  garlic powder , and salsa to  chicken broth . 
 Bring to a boil and add  rice  and cook covered for 20 minutes. 
 Add  rice  to meat mixture. 
 Heat  flour  tortillas and fill mixture. 
 Wrap as you would a soft tortilla.
