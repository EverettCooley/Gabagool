Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 1/2 cups  chicken broth 
 1 1/2 teaspoons  Onion  juice or powder 
 Dash ground  white pepper 
 1 teaspoon  celery salt 
 2 envelopes  unflavored gelatin  (2 tablespoons) 
 1/2 cup  water 
 1 1/2 cups cooked  rice 
 1 tablespoon chopped  pimento 
 1/4 cup sliced stuffed  green olives 
 6 thin slices cooked  Turkey 
 6 thin slices cooked  Ham 
 Salad greens 
 Directions : 
 Bring  chicken broth  to boil. Add  Onion  juice,  pepper  and  celery salt . Soften  gelatin  in  water . Stir into hot broth. Toss together  rice  and  pimento . 
 
 Arrange 3 to 4 slices of in bottom of six individual molds. Spoon in layer of  rice , add slice of  Turkey , another layer of  rice , and top with  Ham . Pour broth over all. Refrigerate until set. Unmold onto salad greens.
