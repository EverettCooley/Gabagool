Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 4 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 6  eggs 
 ½ tsp  pepper 
 1 tbsp minced  chives 
 2 tbsp  butter  or  margarine 
 ½ lb sliced  smoked salmon , cut into strips 
 Directions : 
 Beat the eggs with the pepper until foamy. 
 Stir in the minced chives. 
 Melt the butter in a large skillet and put on med heat. 
 Pour in the egg mixture, add the smoked salmon and cook slowly, stirring until the eggs are soft cooked. 
 Nutritional information : 
 109 calories, 5 gr fat, 16 gr protein, trace carbohydrates, 19 mg cholesterol, 547 mg sodium, trace fiber
