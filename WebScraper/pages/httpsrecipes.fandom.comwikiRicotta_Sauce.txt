Ricotta Sauce
 
 1 Cup  ricotta cheese 
 1/4 Cup  milk 
 2 Sprigs  Italian parsley , Snipped 
 1 T.  Italian seasoning 
 1/4 Cup Grated  Parmesan cheese 
 pepper 
 
Put Ricotta,  milk ,  parsley  And  Italian seasoning  in Small Pan Over Medium Heat. Stir Often With Wooden Spoon. When Well Mixed And Hot, Stir in  Parmesan cheese  And  pepper  to Taste. Continue to Heat And Stir Until Parmesan is Melted. Serve at Once.Source: my Old Recipes
