Description : 
 This recipe taken from www.Portuguese-recipes.com 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup of  water 
 1 cup of  Sugar 
 1 tablespoon of  apple cider vinegar 
 Wax paper 
 Directions : 
 Boil all ingredients until it is thick and  brandy  color. 
 Work small amounts on a cookie sheet to form  twists  and wrap in wax paper. 
 You have to work fast because when it cools it will not be pliable. 
 Be very careful this mixture is hot and can burn you
