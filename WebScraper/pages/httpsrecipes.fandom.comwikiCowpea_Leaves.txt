Description : 
 Quick (30 minutes or less). Serves 2-4.
 
 Ingredients : 
 500 grams of cowpea leaves 
 1 cup(s) of groundnuts 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Boil the leaves until tender. 
 Add groundnuts. 
 Season and serve.
