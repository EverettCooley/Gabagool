 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  raspberries 
 3 cup  blueberries 
 3 tbsp  corn starch 
 3/4 cup  Sugar 
 1/8 tsp  salt 
 1 tbsp  butter 
 Directions : 
 Heat starch,  Sugar  and  salt  on low heat. 
 Add one cup  Berries  only. 
 Cook and stir until boiling. cool 10 min. 
 Add  butter . cool 1 hr. add rest of  Berries  raw to mixture and mix well gently. 
 Pour berry mixture in baked pie shell (your own or store-bought). 
 Serve with  whipped cream  or  vanilla  ice- cream  really light and delicious!
