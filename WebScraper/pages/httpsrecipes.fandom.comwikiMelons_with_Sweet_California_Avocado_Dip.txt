Ingredients : 
 1 package (8 ounces)  cream cheese , softened 
 1 carton (8 ounces)  sour cream 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 1  California avocado , seeded, peeled and mashed 
 assorted fresh (honey dew,  watermelon ,  Crenshaw ,  casaba ,  cantaloupe ), seeded, peeled and cut into bite-sized pieces 
 Directions : 
 Blend all ingredients except in blender or food processor until smooth. 
 Cover dip with plastic wrap; refrigerate 1 hour. 
 To serve, spoon dip into small serving bowl and surround with pieces.
