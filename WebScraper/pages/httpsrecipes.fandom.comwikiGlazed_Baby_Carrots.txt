Glazed Baby  carrots 
 
 1 Envelope Lipton Golden  onion soup mix 
 1 1/2 Cups  water 
 1/2 Cup Firmly Packed  light brown sugar 
 1/4 Cup  butter 
 1 T. Fresh  thyme  Leaves or 1/2 T. Dried 
 4 Cups Baby  carrots 
 
Combine The Soup Mix,  water ,  brown sugar ,  butter  And  thyme  in a 3 Quart Pan And Bring The Pot to a Boil. Add The  carrots  And Simmer Them, Uncovered, Over Medium Low Heat Until They Are Tender But Still Crisp, Stirring Frequently, About 10 Minutes. Serve at Once.Source: Unknown
