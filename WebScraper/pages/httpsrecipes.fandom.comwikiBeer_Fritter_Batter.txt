Description : 
 This is good not only for vegetables, but for seafood and  chicken , as well as for fruit fritters, such as 
 banana ,  pineapple , and  apple .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  flour 
 1  egg 
 1 tbsp  butter , melted 
 1 dash  salt 
 ½ cup  beer 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in a blender or food processor and beat until batter is smooth. 
 Let batter stand, covered, for 4 hours before using. 
 Have vegetables well drained and patted dry if they have been washed. 
 Dip in batter and fry in deep fat, 360°F, until golden.
