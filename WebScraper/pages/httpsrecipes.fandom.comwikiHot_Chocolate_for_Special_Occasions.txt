Hot Chocolate for Special Occasions
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 cups milk 
 1 1/2 t. vanilla 
 1 1/4 cups semi sweet chocolate chips 
 Directions : 
 In the top of a large double boiler over simmering water, or in a heavy duty pan, over low heat, heat milk and vanilla. Add chocolate and continue stirring until chocolate is dissolved. Pour into mugs and serve. Makes about 12 servings.
