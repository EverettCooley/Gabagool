      Beef, Pork and Hominy Stew     
 Ingredients : 
 1½ lb lean  pork ribs , cut into 1-inch pieces 
 1 lb cured  bacon 
 1 lb  flank steak , cut into 1-inch pieces 
 1 lb  beef short ribs , cut between bones 
 3 sweet  Italian sausage  links, cut into 1½-inch lengths 
 2 spicy dried  pork sausage  links, cut into 1½-inch lengths 
 3 quarts  water 
 6  carrots , cut into ½-inch-thick rounds 
 1½ lb  butternut squash , peeled and cut into ¾-inch cubes 
 1 large boiling  potato , peeled and cut into ¾-inch cubes 
 2  red bell peppers , cut into ¾-inch pieces 
 1 tablespoon  paprika 
 3 (15-oz) cans white  hominy , rinsed 
 2 (16- to 19-oz) cans  white beans , rinsed 
 1 tablespoon  salt 
 1 tablespoon  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Stir together meats and  water  in a 12-quart heavy pot and bring to a boil. 
 Reduce heat and simmer, partially covered, stirring occasionally, 3½ hours. 
 Add vegetables and  paprika , then simmer, partially covered, stirring occasionally, 30 minutes, or until vegetables are tender. 
 Add  hominy , beans,  salt , and  pepper  and simmer, stirring occasionally, 15 minutes.
