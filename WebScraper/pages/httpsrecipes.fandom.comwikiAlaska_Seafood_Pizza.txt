Description : 
 Contributed by Trinity, Haines, Alaska 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pack pizza crust mix - (or make your own) 
 ¾ cup  crab  meat 
 ¾ cup tiny  shrimp 
 ½ cup crushed  pineapple 
 5  mushrooms , sliced 
 4¼ oz canned sliced  black olives 
 ¼ cup diced  green pepper 
 1 cup  mozzarella  cheese, grated 
 1 cup white  cheddar  cheese grated 
 Directions : 
 Spread pizza dough on to a pizza pan. 
 Top with crab, shrimp,  pineapple ,  mushrooms ,  green pepper ,  mozzarella  and  cheddar   cheese . 
 Bake at 350 °F for 30 minutes.
