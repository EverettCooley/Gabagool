Ingredients : 
 500g of fresh  tuna  in 4  steaks 
 1  lime , juice only 
 2 tbsp extra virgin  olive oil 
 1 tbspoon of  tarragon , minced 
 1 teaspoon of  balsamic vinegar 
 1 teaspoon of  oregano 
 2 tbsp lowfat plain  yogurt 
 Directions : 
 Prepare a  marinade  with the  olive oil , the  balsamic vinegar , the juice of the  lime , the  oregano  and the minced  tarragon . 
 Marinate the  tuna  steaks for an hour in the refrigerator. 
 Preheat the grill. Grill the  tuna  steaks on high for 2 to 3 minutes on each side. Lower the heat and cook until desired, about 10 minutes. 
 Serve the  tuna  steaks immediately with the  marinade  mixed with the  yogurt  and some  salad  or steamed  vegetables .
