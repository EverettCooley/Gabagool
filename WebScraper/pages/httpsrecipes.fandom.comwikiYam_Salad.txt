 Description : 
This easy, rich salad is made with cooked, real yams, grated onions, mayonnaise, and lettuce leaves and is great as the new accompaniment for any meal.       Yam Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs. cooked  yams , diced 
 3 tsp.  salt 
 1 cup grated  onion , red, white, or yellow only 
 4 tsp.  white pepper 
 5 tbsp.  mayonnaise 
 8 crisp  lettuce  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Place  yams , the grated  onion ,  white pepper ,  salt , and  mayonnaise  in a mixing bowl and mix carefully so  yam  cubes do not break. 
 Arrange on a platter with  lettuce  leaves. Serve cold and plain or with other vegetables, side dishes, or main dishes.
