 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb.  spinach , washed well OR (2 CUPS CHOPPED FRIED  leeks ) 
 1 Tbsp.  butter 
 2  eggs 
 1/2 tsp.  salt 
 1 lb. dry  cottage cheese  (sometimes called pressed  cottage cheese ) 
 Directions : 
 Chop  spinach  and remove stems; simmer in  butter  covered in frying pan; add  salt  and remove from burner. 
 Cool slightly and add  cottage cheese  and  eggs ; stir well. 
 Cool before using.
