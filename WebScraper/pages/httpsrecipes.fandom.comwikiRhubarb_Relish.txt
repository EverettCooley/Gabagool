Description : 
 A tasty recipe from New Brunswick, Canada. It's easy to make and with the best ingredients you get an even finer dimension of tastes ! 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 quart  rhubarb , chopped 
 1 quart  onion , chopped fine 
 1 quart  vinegar 
 2 lbs  brown sugar 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  pepper 
 1 tsp  celery salt 
 1 tsp  cloves 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Mix all together and boil slowly for 1½ hours or until thick. 
 Stir occasionally to keep from burning to bottom of pot. 
 Pour into sterilized jars and seal with wax.
