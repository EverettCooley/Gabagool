 About Strawberry preserves and jam : 
 Strawberry jam is a jam made out of  strawberries , sugar and water, and fruit pectin. Commercial jams often contain chemical preservatives like citric acid. Strawberry preserves may or may not contain pectin, depending on the recipe.
