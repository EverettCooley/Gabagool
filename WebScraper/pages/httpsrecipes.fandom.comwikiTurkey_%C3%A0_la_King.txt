Ingredients : 
 several cups chopped  turkey 
 1 cup chopped  onions 
 4 tbsp  butter 
 8 oz softened  cream cheese 
 2 x 10½ oz cans  cream of mushroom  soup 
 6 oz can broiled in  butter  sliced  mushrooms 
 4 oz jar chopped roasted  pimentos 
 ½ cup chopped  green bell pepper 
 ½ cup chopped  red / orange / yellow sweet peppers 
 2 tbsp dry  sherry 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Cook chopped  onion  in  butter . 
 Blend in  cream cheese , soup, and  salt  and  pepper . 
 Add  turkey  and rest of ingredients (include liquid from  mushrooms ). 
 Bring to simmer and simmer until  peppers  are tender. 
 Serve over hot biscuits or in little cooked pastry cups/shells.
