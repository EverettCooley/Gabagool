Description : 
 Red bean ice dessert
 
 This recipe yields 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 6 cup shaved  ice 
 ¼ cup canned sweet  red beans 
 4 tsp  roasted rice powder  (misu gallu) 
 1 can  fruit cocktail  - (16 oz) 
 flavored fruit syrup to taste 
 colored jelly candies (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Press 1½ cups shaved  ice  into small round bowl. 
 Place upside-down in serving bowl to create a nice mound of  ice . 
 Repeat 3 more times so you have 4 bowls of  ice . 
 Add 1 tablespoon  red beans , 1 teaspoon  rice  powder and ¼ cup fruit cocktail around  ice  in each bowl. 
 Pour flavored syrup on  ice  and sprinkle with jelly candy, if desired. 
 Serve immediately.
