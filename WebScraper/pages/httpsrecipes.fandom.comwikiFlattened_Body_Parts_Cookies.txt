Description : 
 Instead of serving those boring round cookies, serve these!
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 packages refrigerated sugar cookie dough 
 seedless  strawberry jam 
 flour 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to temperature specified on the package. 
 Lightly  flour  a flat surface and roll out cookie dough to about â…“ inch. 
 Trace and cut out miniature hands and feet from a clean stiff cardboard (impact tip: try cutting hands and feet that are missing a finger or toe). 
 Place the stencils on top of the dough and then cut out the shapes using a  butter  knife. 
 Transfer the cookies to a cookie sheet and back according to the directions in the package. 
 When the cookies are done, allow it to cool for a few minutes. 
 Then just before serving, dribble  strawberry  jam on the cookies, particularly on areas where a finger or toe is missing.
