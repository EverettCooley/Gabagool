Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes For Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Yield: 2 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 whole-wheat  pita  breads 
 4 cups washed ready-to-eat  romaine lettuce 
 6 oz peeled cooked medium  shrimp 
 2 tbsp  Caesar salad dressing 
 2 tbsp grated  Parmesan cheese 
 freshly ground  black pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven or toaster oven to 300°F. 
 Place  lettuce  and  shrimp  in a bowl and toss with dressing. 
 Place pita  bread  in oven. 
 Divide salad between 2 plates. 
 Sprinkle Parmesan on top and add  pepper . 
 Serve with warm pita  bread . 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving (½ of recipe):
 
 337 Calories | 12g fat | 172 mg cholesterol | 517 mg sodium | 33g carbs | 2g dietary fiber | 4g sugars | 27g protein 
 Diabetic exchanges: 2 starch | 3 very lean meat | 1½ fat
