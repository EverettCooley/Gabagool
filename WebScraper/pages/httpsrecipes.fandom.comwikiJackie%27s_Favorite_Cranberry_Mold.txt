Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Come Into the Kitchen 
 Serves 8 to 10 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large pkg.  black cherry gelatin 
 2 cups boiling  water 
 1 can whole  cranberry sauce  (1 lb) 
 1 can  pineapple  tidbits, undrained (8 oz) 
 few drops mint extract 
 Â½ cup  pecans , chopped 
 1 pint  sour cream 
 Directions : 
 Combine gelatin and boiling water. 
 Stir until dissolved. 
 Stir in cranberry sauce, breaking up chunks. 
 Stir in undrained pineapple tidbits, mint and pecans. 
 Pour into favorite Jell-O mold. 
 Drop sour cream by teaspoonfuls into gelatin mixture. 
 Cover with plastic wrap and chill. 
 Unmold on platter, garnished with greenery.
