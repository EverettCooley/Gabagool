Ingredients : 
 2 cl  Bailey's Irish Cream 
 2 cl  Kahlúa 
 2 cl  amaretto 
 10 cl  milk  or  cream 
 pushed  ice 
 Directions : 
 Put ice and  milk  /  cream  with Bailey's, kahlúa and  amaretto  in the blender foamy stir and filling into a glass. 
 Decorating with a red cocktail  cherry .
