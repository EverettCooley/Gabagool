Ingredients : 
 16 oz. frozen  French fries  (tater tots may be substituted) 
 3/4 cup  Cheez Whiz , heated 
 1/2 cup real  bacon bits 
 1/3 cup  sour cream 
 2 T. sliced  green onions , optional 
 Directions : 
 Prepare French fries as package directs. 
 Arrange hot, crisp fries on a serving platter. 
 Pour hot cheese over fries. 
 Sprinkle with bacon bits. 
 Garnish with sour cream and green onion 
 Notes : 
 Makes 4 to 6 servings.
