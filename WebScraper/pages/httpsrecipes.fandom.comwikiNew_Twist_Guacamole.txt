Description : 
 Yogurt ,  lemon ,  garlic salt , and  dill  are quickly making a new classic!
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 ripe  Fuerte ,  Pinkerton   or   Hass avocados 
 1 Tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 tsp fresh  dill , chopped 
 Â½ tsp  garlic salt 
 1 tsp medium-hot  salsa 
 1 cup low-fat  yogurt 
 Directions : 
 Cut avocados in half, removed seeds and scoop out meat. 
 Put in a medium bowl and blend by hand with other ingredients until just mixed 
 Refrigerate, covered, for several hours to mold.
