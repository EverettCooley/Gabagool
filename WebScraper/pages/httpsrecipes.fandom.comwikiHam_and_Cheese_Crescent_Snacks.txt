This is a great snack! It's great to use for left overs.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (8 ounces) package refrigerated  crescent dinner rolls 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine , softened 
 1 – 2 teaspoon  yellow mustard 
 1 cup cubed cooked  ham  (, great for left overs) 
 ⅓ cup chopped  onions 
 ⅓ cup chopped  green peppers  (you can use any color you like) 
 1 cup shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Unroll crescent dough onto an ungreased baking sheet, roll dough to a 13x9 rectangle, crimp edges of dough. 
 In a small bowl, combine the  butter  and  mustard , stir til smooth. 
 Spread evenly over dough, top with  ham ,  onion , and  peppers . 
 Sprinkle with  cheese . 
 Bake at 375°F for 18 – 20 minutes.
