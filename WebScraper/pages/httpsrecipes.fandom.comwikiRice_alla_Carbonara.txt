Description : 
 Makes 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ pound cooked  ham , julienned 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 ½ pound sliced  bacon , cooked crisp, diced 
 ⅔ cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 2 tablespoons chopped fresh  parsley 
 ½ teaspoon ground  white pepper 
 ¾ cup  heavy cream 
 Directions : 
 Cook  ham  strips in  butter  and  oil  in large skillet over medium heat until  ham  is slightly browned. 
 Add  rice ,  bacon ,  cheese ,  parsley ,  pepper  and  cream ; stir until mixture is creamy and thoroughly heated.
