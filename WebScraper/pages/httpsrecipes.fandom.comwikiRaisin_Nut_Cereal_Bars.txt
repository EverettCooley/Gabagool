Raisin  Nut Cereal Bars
 Categories: Breads, Desserts, Cookies
 Yield: 24 Bars
 
 1/4 c  margarine  or  butter 
 3 c Miniature Marshmallows 
 1/2 ts Ground  cinnamon 
 5 1/2 c  Raisin  Nut  bran  Cereal 
 
 butter  Square Pan, 9x9x2 Inches. Heat  margarine , Marshmallows AndCinnamon in 3-Quart Saucepan Over Low Heat, Stirring Constantly, UntilSmooth; Remove From Heat. Stir in Cereal Until Thoroughly Coated.Pat Into Pan; Cool. Cut Into 2-1/4 x 1-1/2-Inch Bars. Makes 24 Bars.
