Description : 
 This is a recipe I obtained from an Estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Manes Estate in Venus, Texas in 1981.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups fresh sliced  strawberries  sugared 
 6½ cups chilled  rose wine 
 ¾ cup frozen lemonade concentrate thawed 
 1 bottle  champagne  chilled 
 ice cubes 
 Directions : 
 In a large mixing bowl combine strawberries with 4 cups rose wine. 
 Cover and let stand at room temperature for 1 hour. 
 Just before serving pour this mixture into a punch bowl. 
 Add lemonade concentrate, remaining rose wine, champagne and ice cubes. 
 Mix well.
