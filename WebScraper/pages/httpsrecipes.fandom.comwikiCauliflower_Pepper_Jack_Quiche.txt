Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Clovers Estate in Arlington, Texas in 1989.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  cauliflower 
 ⅓ cup chopped  white onion 
 1 cup grated pepper  jack cheese 
 ⅓ cup chopped  green pepper 
 3  eggs 
 1 cup  milk 
 ½ cup all purpose  baking mix 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Cook cauliflower for 10 minutes then drain and put in pie plate. 
 Sprinkle onion, cheese and pepper over cauliflower. 
 Combine eggs, milk, baking mix, salt and pepper and beat well. 
 Pour egg mixture over cauliflower mixture then bake at 375°F for 45 minutes.
