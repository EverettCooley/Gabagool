Description : 
 Simple dessert for dinner party or romantic dinner.
 
       Chocolate Cheese Fondue     
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  unsalted butter 
 2 tablespoons  cocoa 
 ½ cup  powdered sugar , sifted 
 ⅓ cup shredded  American cheese  or  Swiss cheese  or  Cheddar  cheese or  Gruyere 
 ½ teaspoon  cinnamon 
 ¼ teaspoon  nutmeg 
 ½ teaspoon  vanilla 
 ½ teaspoon  peppermint extract 
 ¼ teaspoon  almond extract 
 ½ cup  milk , as needed 
 1 tablespoon  brandy 
 Serve with : 
 ice cream 
 bite-size pieces of  angel food cake 
 bite-size pieces of  chocolate  cake 
 bite-size pieces of cake 
 graham crackers 
 ladyfingers 
 digestive biscuits 
 Directions : 
 In a saucepan, melt  butter  over low heat. 
 Gradually add  cocoa  and  sugar , and blend thoroughly to make a smooth sauce. 
 Add  cheese , and blend well. 
 Stir in  cinnamon ,  nutmeg ,  vanilla , peppermint, and  almond extract . 
 Gradually add  milk  until fondue is of desired consistency. 
 Add  brandy  (do NOT stir); immediately ignite  brandy . 
 Serve over  ice cream , or use as a dip.
