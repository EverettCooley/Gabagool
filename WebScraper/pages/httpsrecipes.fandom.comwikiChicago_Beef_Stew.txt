 Directions : 
 flour  meat, brown in hot  oil . Add next 10 ingredients ( water  through  tomato juice ). 
 Cover and cook over low heat 2 hrs. 
 Add vegetables and  applesauce , cook 15-20 minute longer, or until meat and vegetables are tender. 
 Can also top with dumplings when vegetables are added, then finish as above. 
 Dumplings: You can use your own recipe here. I make dumplings with Bisquick and  eggs . 
 If I am cooking stewed  Chicken , I will add finely chopped green  Onion  and fresh  rosemary  to them. 
 For the stew, I might add green  Onion  or  chives , depending on how the stew tastes.
