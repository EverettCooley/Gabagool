 Description : 
 In Romanian: Chiftelute de dovlecei.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 medium  zucchini 
 1  egg 
 2 tablespoons  flour 
 1 tablespoon mixed chopped  parsley  and  dill 
 crustless bread soaked in  water  and then squeezed dry 
 2 tablespoons  lard 
 1 lightly fried chopped  onion 
 salt 
 pepper 
 green  lettuce  or  tomatoes 
 Directions : 
 Remove the peel (grate), wash and boil the zuchini. 
 Leave in the colander for half an hour to drain well. 
 Then chop with the wooden chopper. 
 Put in a bowl and mix with the chopped and lightly fried  onion ,  egg ,  flour , soaked and squeezed bread,  salt ,  pepper ,  parsley  and  dill . 
 Take tablespoons of this mixture and fry in hot  lard  on both sides. 
 Place on a plate and serve with green  lettuce  or  tomato  slices arranged around the pancakes.
