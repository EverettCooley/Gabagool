asparagus  Shortcake
 
 
 1 Pkg Refrigerator Biscuits 
 1 Can  asparagus  Spears, Drained 
 1 Can  Cheese  Soup 
 
French-Fried  Onion  Rings
 
 
Preheat Oven to 350?. Arrange Half The Biscuits in Greased 1-QuartCasserole; Layer  asparagus  Over Biscuits. Top With Remaining Biscuits. BakeFor 15 Minutes. Spread With Soup; Top With  Onion  Rings. Bake For 10Minutes Longer or Until bubbly.
 
 
Yield: 4-6 Servings
