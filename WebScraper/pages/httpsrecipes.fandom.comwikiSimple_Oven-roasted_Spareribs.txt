 These are better than anything you can imagine. Serve with cole slaw and oven-browned new potatoes.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Cookbook:  The Fannie Farmer Cookbook  by Marion Cunningham 
 Typed by: Susan <alexanderjamesmom@gmail.com> 
 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 4 lbs  spareribs , in whole racks 
 salt  and freshly ground  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 425Â°F. 
 Season the spareribs with salt and pepper generously on both sides. 
 Place on a roasting rack in a pan and roast for 30 minutes. 
 Turn the spareribs over and continue to roast for another 30 minutes.
