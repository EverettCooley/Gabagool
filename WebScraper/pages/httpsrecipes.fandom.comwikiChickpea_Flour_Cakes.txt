Ingredients : 
 2 cups  chickpea flour 
 ½ cup  yellow split peas 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 ½ cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Add liquid all at once and mix quickly using pastry blender. 
 Dust the rolling pin on the board and knead about 1 cm thickness. 
 Cut cookies into round or shape with a cookie cutter dipped in  flour . 
 Bake for 10 minutes at 450°F. 
 And cool for half hour.
