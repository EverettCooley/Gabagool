Prep Time: 5 minutes | Total Time: 5 minutes 
 Makes 1¼ – 1½ cups of seasoning 
 Ingredients : 
 6 tbsp  salt 
 6 tbsp  garlic granules 
 4 tbsp  oregano 
 2 tbsp  black pepper 
 2 tbsp  turmeric 
 2 tbsp  onion powder 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients together and store in an airtight container for up to 6 months.
