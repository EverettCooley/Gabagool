 Ingredients : 
 2 shots Stolichnaya Razberi ( raspberry   vodka ) 
 ½ shot De Kuyper  triple sec 
 ½ shot  crème de framboise 
 1 shot  lime juice 
 10 fresh  raspberries 
 ¾ shot  sugar syrup 
 Directions : 
 Put 10 fresh  raspberries , 2 shots of Stolichnaya Razberi, 1 shot  lime juice  0.75 shots of  sugar syrup , 0.5 shot of De Kuyper  triple sec , 0.5 shot of  crème de framboise  ( raspberry  flavoured liqueur) 
 Mix altogether in a cocktail shaker with crushed  ice  and strain everything into a tumbler. 
 Garnish with 2  raspberries .
