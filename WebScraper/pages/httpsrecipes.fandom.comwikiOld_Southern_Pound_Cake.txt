Ingredients : 
 3 c.  granulated sugar 
 1/2 lb.  butter 
 1/4 c.  Crisco 
 1 c.  sweet milk 
 5  eggs 
 3 c.  flour 
 1/2 tsp.  baking powder 
 2 tsp.  Lemon extract 
 1 tsp.  vanilla extract 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Cream shortening, sugar, and Crisco. 
 Add eggs. 
 Add flour and baking powder a little at a time, alternating with the milk. 
 Add flavoring. 
 Pour into tube pan (12 cup size) or 2 loaf pans, 5 x 9. 
 Put in cold oven at 350 degrees for 1 hour 10 minutes (tube pan) or 1 hour (loaf pans)
