Ingredients : 
 3 Cups Rice Krispies (3 individual size boxes) 
 3 Cups Cheerios (4 individual size boxes) 
 8 oz. Pretzel sticks, broken in half 
 12 oz. Dry Roasted  Peanuts 
 2 lbs. chocolate chips (about 3 bags) 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl. 
 Melt the chocolate over low heat or in microwave. 
 Pour melted chocolate over the dry mix and stir. 
 Spread mixture flat on wax paper or foil and let harden. 
 When hardened, break apart and store in a container.  Enjoy.
