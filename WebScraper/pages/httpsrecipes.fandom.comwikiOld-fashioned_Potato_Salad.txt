Description : 
 This Ol'fashioned potato salad is for any time any place. Easy to make easy to eat ! Enjoy !
 
 Ingredients : 
 9 cups cubed unpeeled round  red potato  (about 3 lbs) or another potato of your choice 
 ½ cup diced  onion 
 ½ cup diced  celery 
 ½ cup cup diced  radishes 
 ¼ cup sweet or dill  pickle relish  (optional) 
 3 hard-cooked  egg , mashed 
 ⅓ cup  mayonnaise  (or more to make it creamy) 
 2 tbsp chopped fresh  parsley 
 1 tsp  prepared mustard  (I use other mustards at times - i.e  Dijon ) 
 ¾ tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 ¼ tsp  garlic salt 
 Directions : 
 Place  potatoes  in a large pot, cover with  water , and bring to boil. Cook about until tender. 
 Drain and cool; cut into cubes, place in bowl with next 5 ingredients; toss gently. 
 Combine  mayonnaise  and next 5 ingredients; stir well. 
 Pour over  potato  mixture; toss to coat. 
 Cover and chill.
