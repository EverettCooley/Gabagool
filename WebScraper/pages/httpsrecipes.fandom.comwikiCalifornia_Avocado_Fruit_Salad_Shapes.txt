 Ingredients : 
 3 "squares" (½ inch) ripe  California avocados 
 3 "wheels" (½ inch)  bananas 
 3 "triangles" (¼ inch)  apples 
 3  grape  "balls", peeled 
 Directions : 
 In small bowl combine fruit shapes and serve.
