Ingredients : 
 1  hog hoof 
 3 stalks  spring onion 
 1 tbsp minced  ginger 
 80 g  crystal (rock) sugar 
 Sauce : 
 5 tbsp  light soy sauce 
 2 tbsp  yellow wine 
 Â½ tbsp  sesame oil 
 Directions : 
 Remove all hair from hog hoof and boil in hot  water  for 5 minutes. 
 Cut hog hoof into small cube (2"x2"), chop spring  onion  into short stalks. 
 Marinate hog hoof with  ginger , spring  onion , and sauce for 2 hours. 
 Parboil the hog hoof in hot  oil  for 1 minute, pour out the  oil . 
 Add 500ml of  water  and the marinate sauce, cook for 1 hour in low heat. 
 Add crystal  sugar  and keep boiling for 1 hour in low heat. 
 When sauce is boiled almost dry, dish up and serve.
