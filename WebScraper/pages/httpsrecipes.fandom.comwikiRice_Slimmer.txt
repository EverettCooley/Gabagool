Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/4 cup bottled  oil  and  vinegar  dressing 
 1 teaspoon  garlic salt 
 1/2 teaspoon seasoned  pepper 
 1/4 teaspoon  ground red pepper 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice  (cooked in  chicken broth ) 
 1 16-ounce can  green beans , drained 
 1 cup sliced  celery 
 1/4 cup diced  pimento 
 4 hard-cooked  eggs , chopped 
 parsley  sprigs for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Combine dressing,  garlic salt , seasoned  pepper  and red pepper. 
 Pour over  rice  and beans. Cool. 
 Add  rice ,  green beans ,  celery ,  pimento  and  eggs . 
 Toss lightly. Adjust seasonings, if necessary. 
 Chill. 
 Garnish with  parsley  and sliced  eggs , if desired.
