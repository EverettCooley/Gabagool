Description : 
 This recipe comes from Montréal, Québec
 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups sliced sweet  yams  (skin on optional) 
 ⅓ cup organic  maple syrup 
 ¼ tsp  thyme 
 ¼ tsp  black pepper 
 ¾ tsp  sea salt 
 juice and zest of an  orange 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Toss sliced yams with remaining ingredients until evenly coated. 
 Place on a 13 by 9 inch baking pan and bake until soft and golden brown about 45 minutes to 1 hour. 
 Stir occasionally to coat evenly in glaze.
