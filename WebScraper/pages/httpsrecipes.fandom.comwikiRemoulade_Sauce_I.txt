Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Mrs. Anne Neal 
 Makes 1 cup 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅔ cup  catsup 
 2 tbsp prepared  horseradish 
 1 tbsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 1 tbsp  celery , minced 
 1 tbsp  parsley , minced 
 ½ tsp  dry mustard 
 ½ tsp  Tabasco sauce 
 1 tbsp  green onion , minced 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients and chill before serving with fresh boiled shrimp.
