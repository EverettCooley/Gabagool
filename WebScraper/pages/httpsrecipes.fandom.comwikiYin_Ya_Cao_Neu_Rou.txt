Description : 
 Stir-fried  beef  with green  bean sprouts  and  chives 
 
 Ingredients : 
 150 g  beef 
 400 g trimmed green  bean sprouts 
 75 g  chives 
 10 g (3 slices) shredded  ginger 
 Seasoning : 
 1 tbsp light  soy sauce 
 ⅓ tbsp  sugar 
 1 tbsp  corn starch 
 ½ tbsp  water 
 ½ tbsp  oil 
 Sauce : 
 1 tbsp  oyster sauce 
 ⅓ tbsp  salt  and  sugar 
 1 tbsp light  soy sauce 
 3 tbsp  water 
 1 tbsp  corn starch 
 some  pepper  and  sesame oil 
 Directions : 
 Wash  chives , then cut into short sections 
 Wash  beef  and shred, marinate with seasoning for 10 minutes 
 Wash and drain green  bean sprouts 
 Heat wok with 1 tbsp of  oil , add green  bean sprouts  and stir fry 
 Add 1 tbsp of  water  while green  bean sprouts  turn soft 
 Stir fry until cook, dish up and drain dry 
 Heat wok with 3 tbsp of  oil , add shredded  ginger  while  oil  is heating 
 Add marinated  beef  and stir fry for a while, add  chive 
 Stir fry for a while, add in green  bean sprouts , stir well 
 Pour in sauce, keep stirring, dish up when sauce dry, serve
