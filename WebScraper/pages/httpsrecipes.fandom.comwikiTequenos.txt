 Description : 
 Toquenos are Venezuelan cheese-filled pastries. Makes 1 Serving.
 
 Ingredients : 
 
 2 lb  all-purpose flour 
 4 x  egg   yolks 
 1/2 lb  butter  room temperature 
 1 cup  water 
 1/2 tsp  salt 
 1 tbl  Sugar 
 2 lb  farmer's cheese 
 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve the  salt  and  Sugar  in the  water . 
 Mix all ingredients except the  Cheese  together. 
 Knead to form a dough. Let rest. 
 Cut  Cheese  into sticks 
 Stretch the dough with a rolling pin. 
 Cut in strips about . 
 Wrap each strip around a  Cheese  stick 
 Pinch the ends so that the  Cheese  is fully covered 
 Roll each tequeno in  flour , to keep them from sticking. 
 Deep fry the tequenos in very hot  oil , until golden in color. 
 Place on paper towels to absorb any excess  oil .
