Description : 
 Serve as a dessert or instead of jam with a bran muffin.
 
 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 3 oz pkg.  strawberry flavored gelatin 
 1½ cup boiling  water 
 1 cup unsweetened  applesauce 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve sugar-free  gelatin  in boiling  water , refrigerate until lukewarm and then add the  applesauce . 
 Pour into 4 individual dishes and refrigerate until firm. 
 Nutritional information : 
 For ¼ recipe:
 
 35 calories | 7 g carbohydrate | protein and fat negligible | 56 mg sodium 
 ½ fruit exchange
