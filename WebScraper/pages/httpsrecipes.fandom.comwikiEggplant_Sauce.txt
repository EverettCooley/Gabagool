Description : 
 In the Ivory Coast, white and yellow  eggplant  are used in "light sauce" with  plantains  and fish, and in  Eggplant  sauce. Serve with  rice , foutou (mashed  yams  or  plantains ), etc.
 
 Ingredients : 
 100 g (3½ oz)  onions 
 500 g (1 lb) fresh  fish 
 500 g (1 lb) peeled  eggplant 
 8 to 9 peeled  okra 
 200 g (7 oz) seeded  tomato 
 a few  taro leaves  cut into strips 
 10 g (⅓ oz.)  chili pepper 
 cooking  oil 
 Directions : 
 Peel  onions ; chop finely; sauté in a little  oil ;. 
 Add fresh fish of your choice; cook over low heat for 15 minutes;. 
 Add  eggplant ,  okra , seeded  tomato ,a few  taro leaves  and  chili pepper ;. 
 Cover with  water ; cover and let simmer 30 minutes;. 
 Remove the vegetables; drain; crush and return to the pot;. 
 Season with  salt .
