Creamy Italian Dressing
 
 3/4 Cup  ricotta cheese 
 1/2 Cup Skim  milk 
 2 T.  lemon juice 
 1 Envelope Italian  salad dressing  Mix 
 
In Blender Container, Mix  ricotta cheese , Skim  milk ,  lemon juice  And Italian  salad dressing . Mix. Cover And Blend Until Creamy. Transfer to a Storage Container. Cover And Chill. Makes 1 1/4 Cups.Source: my Old Recipes
