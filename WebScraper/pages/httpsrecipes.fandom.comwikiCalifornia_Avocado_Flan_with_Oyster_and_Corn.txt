Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 5  eggs , lightly beaten 
 1 quart  whipping cream 
 4  California avocados , pureed (about 2 lbs.) 
 1 Tbsp  sea salt 
 Â½ tsp freshly ground  white pepper 
 18  oysters , poached 
 Cooked  corn  kernels, as needed for garnish 
 Chopped  cilantro , as needed for garnish 
 thyme  sprigs, as needed for garnish 
 Directions : 
 On low speed (take care not to create foam), mix  eggs ,  cream ,  avocado ,  salt , and  pepper . 
 Divide among 18 four-ounce ramekins. 
 Put ramekins in a shallow baking pan. 
 Pour hot  water  around ramekins half-way up their sides. 
 Cover with foil. 
 Bake at 325 degrees until custard sets, about 25 minutes.
