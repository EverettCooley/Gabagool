Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 c Diced  rhubarb 
 3 lg  Apples ,peeled, cored and Sliced 
 1 c  Sugar 
 1 tb Minced  crystallized ginger 
 1 tb  flour 
 Topping:
 
 1/2 c  flour 
 1/2 c  brown sugar 
 4 tb  butter , cold, cut into Chunks 
 1/2 ts  ground ginger 
 1/2 c  oatmeal 
 Directions : 
 Place  rhubarb ,  Apples , 1 cup  Sugar ,  crystallized ginger , and 2 tablespoons  flour  in a large bowl and toss well. 
 Turn into a greased 9" pie pan. 
 Combine topping ingredients in a bowl and mix with a pastery blender until crumbly. 
 Spread crumb mixture over  rhubarb  and  Apples  and bake at 350F for 45â€“50 minutes, until fruit is tender and topping is browned. 
 Serve warm...
