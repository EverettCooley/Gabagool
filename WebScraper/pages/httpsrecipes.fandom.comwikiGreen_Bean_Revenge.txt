Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 5/27/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cans  green beans , drained 
 1 can sliced  water chestnuts  drained and chopped 
 2 jars  Cheez Whiz 
 1½ cups  cracker  crumbs 
 ½ stick  butter  melted 
 Directions : 
 Place green beans in a greased baking dish and cover with water chestnuts. 
 Heat both jars of cheese whiz in microwave just until they can be poured. 
 Pour cheese over green beans and water chestnuts. 
 Combine cracker crumbs and margarine; sprinkle over casserole. 
 Bake at 350°F for 35 minutes.
