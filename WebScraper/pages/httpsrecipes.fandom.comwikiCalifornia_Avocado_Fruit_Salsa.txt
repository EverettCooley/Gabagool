Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 400g  Pasta 
 2 ripe mangoes, peeled, chopped (may substitute  peaches  or  nectarines ) 
 1 or 2 jalapeño  peppers , chopped finely 
 ½ Cup  cilantro , chopped 
 2  California avocados , chopped 
 1 quart  strawberries , chopped 
 3  kiwi fruit , chopped 
 2  limes  (about 1/3 c. juice) 
 Directions : 
 Mix first 6 ingredients together.
 Squeeze juice from  limes  and add.
 Gently toss.
 Serve over grilled  Chicken , or  rice .
