Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  white chocolate 
 ½ cup  peanut butter 
 2 cups Rice Krispies 
 1½ cup miniature  marshmallows 
 Directions : 
 Slowly melt the  white chocolate . 
 Remove from heat and stir in the  peanut butter . 
 Add the Rice Krispies and marshmallows. 
 Put in an 8 x 8 inch buttered dish. 
 Cut into squares before it gets set. 
 The candy will keep best if stored in the refrigerator.
