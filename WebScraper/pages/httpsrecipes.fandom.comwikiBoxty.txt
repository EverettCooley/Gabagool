 Description : 
 Boxty  ( Potato  Griddle Cake) is a traditional Irish dish. In pre-famine times,  potatoes  were abundant in Ireland and were thus the main source of food. Boxty is usually served to accompany main meals.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 lb raw  Potato , grated 
 1/2 lb mashed  Potato 
 1/2 lb  plain flour 
 milk 
 1  egg , beaten 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 
 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl, mix the grate and mashed  potatoes . Add  salt ,  pepper  and  flour . Add  egg  and enough  milk  to make batter that will drop from a spoon. Scoop tablespoonfuls and drop one by one on a hot griddle or frying pan. Cook for 4 minutes on each side or until golden brown. Serve with  Apple  sauce.
