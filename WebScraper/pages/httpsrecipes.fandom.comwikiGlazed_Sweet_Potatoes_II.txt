Glazed  sweet potatoes 
 
 2 Cans (1 Lb. 8 Oz. Each)  sweet potatoes 
 1/4 Cup  margarine  (1/2 Stick) 
 1 cu Firmly Packed  brown sugar  (dark Brown) 
 2 T. Ground  cinnamon 
 
Drain Liquid From  sweet potatoes . Put  sweet potatoes  in Single Layer in Shallow Glass Baking Dish. Heat  margarine  With  brown sugar  And  cinnamon  in Large Skillet Over Low Heat. Stir Until  sugar  Dissolves. Drizzle Mixture Over  sweet potatoes . Bake at 325 For 30 Minutes. Baste With Glaze to Coat Evenly. Arrange on Heated Platter.
 Serves 8
