 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 cups chopped  mangos  and other fruits 
 1/2 cup  lemon juice 
 1 can (14 oz.)  sweetened condensed milk 
 1 angel food cake (8" or 9") 
 Directions : 
 With serrated knife, cut 2 inch layer off top of cake. Set aside. Carefully hollow out cake leaving 1/2-3/4 inch thick shell. Stir together  lemon juice  and  condensed milk  until thickened. Spoon into cake shell. Spoon fruit over  lemon  mixture. Replace cake top. Cover and chill at least 4 hours. When serving, sprinkle with  powdered sugar  and decorate with more fruit.
