 Description : 
 A  rum  drink
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tsp 151-proof  rum 
 1 oz  light rum 
 1/2 oz Brown  creme de cacao 
 1/2 oz White  creme de menthe 
 1 tbsp  light cream 
 ice 
 chocolate  Syrup 
 Directions : 
 Shake with  ice  and strain into a glass half filled with  ice cubes . Drizzle  chocolate  syrup over the top.
