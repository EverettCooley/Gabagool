 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 cups  water 
 1/4 cup  vegetable oil 
 1/4 cup  applesauce 
 2  eggs 
 2 tsp.  vanilla 
 1/3 cup  Peanut   butter  (crunchy works great) 
 2 1/2 cups  whole wheat flour 
 3/4 cup regular  flour 
 1 cup  cornmeal 
 3/4 cup  Oats 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 400 degrees. 
 Mix liquid ingredients together well. 
 Combine the dry ingredients in a large bowl. 
 Pour the wet mixture into the dry ingredients bowl and mix thoroughly until smooth. 
 Roll into a ball; place ball on floured surface. 
 Roll out to 1/2" thickness. 
 Cut into desired shapes (bone-shaped cookiecutters work great!). 
 Place on ungreased cookie sheets. 
 Bake for 20 minutes; turn off the oven and leave the biscuits in there for one hour to harden.
