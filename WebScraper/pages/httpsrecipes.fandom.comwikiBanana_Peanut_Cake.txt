 Description : 
 banana   Peanut  CAKE
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups  flour 
 1/4 cup  cake flour 
 4 tsp.  baking powder 
 1 tsp.  salt 
 1/2 tsp.  baking soda 
 10 oz.  butter  OR  margarine 
 2 cups  Sugar 
 8 MASHED  bananas 
 1/2 cup  peanuts , coarsely chopped. 
 Directions : 
 Sift the ingredients 
 Blend in 4  eggs  lightly beaten. 
 Fold the dry ingredients above alternately with  bananas  and  peanuts
