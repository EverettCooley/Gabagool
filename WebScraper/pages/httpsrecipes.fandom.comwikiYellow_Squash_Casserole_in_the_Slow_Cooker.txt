 Ingredients : 
 5 cups frozen  yellow squash 
 Â½ cup  butter  melted 
 1 can  cream of chicken  soup 
 2 slices cubed  bread 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 Directions : 
 Place  squash  in slow cooker with  butter  and cook for 1 hour. 
 Add soup as it comes from the can and cook until hot. 
 Add  bread  and sour cream and cook until bubbly.
