 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 small cloves  garlic 
 1/2 tsp.  coarse salt 
 1/4 cup fresh  lime juice  (3 to 4  limes ) 
 3 Tbs. dark  sesame oil 
 2 Tbs.  vegetable oil 
 1 Tbs.  cider vinegar 
 1/4 tsp. Dijon  mustard 
 2 Tbs. chopped fresh  cilantro 
 2 tsp. grated  lime  peel 
 Directions : 
 With mortar and pestle or on cutting board using flat side of chefâ€™s knife, mash  garlic  and  salt  to paste.
 
 In small bowl or glass jar with tight-fitting lid, combine  garlic  paste,  lime juice , both oils,  vinegar  and  mustard . Whisk or shake until well blended. Add  cilantro  and  lime  peel and mix well.
