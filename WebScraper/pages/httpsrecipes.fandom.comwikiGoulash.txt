Description : 
 Best time to make this recipe is when you need to clean out your refrigerator.
 
 Contributed by  SF&SC Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 box of elbow  macaroni 
 1 pound of  ground beef 
 seasoned salt 
 1 can of  tomato sauce 
 1 can of stewed  tomatoes 
 1 can of  corn 
 toss in a few things you need to get rid of 
 Directions : 
 Cook macaroni as directed. 
 While the water is boiling for the macaroni, brown ground beef in a skillet with season salt to taste, add tomato sauce, stewed tomatoes, can of corn, and anything else that is on the verge of spoiling in your fridge. 
 Mix together with cooked and drained macaroni.
