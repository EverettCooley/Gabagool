Makes 48 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups cooked  rice 
 2 3-ounce packages  cream cheese  (room temperature) 
 1/4 cup crumbled bleu  Cheese 
 1/4 cup  sour cream 
 1/2 teaspoon seasoned  salt 
 1/2 teaspoon seasoned  pepper 
 1/8 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 3 to 4 drops hot pepper sauce 
 1/4 cup finely chopped  pecans 
 1 tablespoon chopped  chives 
 8 baked or boiled  Ham  slices (6x4x1/16-inch) or 12 slices (4x4x1/16-inch) 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  cream cheese , bleu  Cheese ,  sour cream ,  salt ,  pepper ,  garlic powder  and  pepper  sauce. 
 Blend until smooth and fluffy (use food processor, blender, or electric mixer). 
 Add  pecans  and  chives ; mix thoroughly. 
 Divide mixture evenly and spread on  Ham  slices. 
 Roll up, forming 6-inch or 4-inch rolls. 
 Cover each  Ham  roll tightly with plastic wrap. 
 Refrigerate until firm. 
 Unwrap and slice in 1-inch pieces.
