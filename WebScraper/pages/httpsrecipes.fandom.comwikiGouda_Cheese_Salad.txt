Ingredients : 
 6 cups  romaine ,  spinach  or  radicchio  greens 
 8 ounces sliced  smoked gouda 
 1½ cups sliced  strawberries 
 ½ cup ready-made toasted  ground pecans  into long strips 
 Strawberry Vinaigrette : 
 ¼ cup seedless  strawberry jam 
 ¾ cup extra virgin  olive oil 
 ¾ cup  red wine , or  strawberry vinegar 
 Directions : 
 Arrange greens on four salad plates. 
 Arrange  cheese  in wedges and  strawberries  alternately on greens. 
 Use 2 ounces of  cheese  and ½ cup  strawberries  per plate. 
 Divide dressing between four plates and garnish with 2 tablespoons of toasted  ground pecans  on top of salads.
