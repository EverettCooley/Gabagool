Chicken  Nuggets
 
 1Â lb of  Chicken  Breasts (the White Meat) - Cut Into Squares 
 1 to 1 1/2 Cups of  bread  Crumbs 
 1/2 Cup of Light Biscuit Mix 
 1/4 Cup of No-Fat  milk  or  water 
 Spray  oil 
 
 1 Plastic Bag - You Can Use a Regular or Zippy - Loc Kind. 
 
Have Your Grown up Pre-Heat The Oven to 350 Degrees. Spray a Cookie SheetWith The Spray  oil . Dirst Put The Biscuit Mix in a Bowl And Then Put TheMilk or  water  in And Stir It. Make Sure That You Stir it Till There Are NoLumps And The Biscuit Mix is Like a Creamy Soup. Put The  bread  Crumbs In The Bag. Shake Them up Really Good so It's Covered With  bread  Crumbs. TakeThem Out of The Bag (even if It's Gross to You) Put Them on The Cookie PanAll Spread Out. Have Your Grownup Put The Pan in The Oven. Let Them CookFor 15 Minutes or Until They Are All Golden Brown. Then You Eat Them (beCareful, The Inside Can be Hot! Hot! Hot!) With a Dip.
