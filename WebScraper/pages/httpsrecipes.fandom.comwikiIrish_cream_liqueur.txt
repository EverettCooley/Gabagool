Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Williams Estate in Tyler, Texas in 1994.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1¼ cup  brandy 
 14 ounce can  sweetened condensed milk 
 1 cup  heavy cream 
 4  eggs 
 2 tablespoons  chocolate flavored syrup 
 2 teaspoons instant  coffee 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 ½ teaspoon  almond extract 
 Directions : 
 In blender container combine all ingredients and blend until smooth. 
 Store tightly covered in refrigerator up to one month. 
 Stir before serving.
