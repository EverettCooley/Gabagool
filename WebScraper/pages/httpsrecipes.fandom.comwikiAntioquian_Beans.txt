      Antioquian Beans     
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  kidney beans 
 4 slices chopped  bacon  or  salt pork 
 2 green  plantains , finely chopped 
 1 tbsp  salt 
 4 small  tomatoes , peeled and chopped 
 2  onions , chopped 
 1  garlic  clove, minced 
 1 tbsp cooking  oil 
 Directions : 
 Wash the beans and let soak overnight. 
 Put beans and  bacon  or  salt pork  in a cooker with  water  to cover. 
 Cook for about an hour. 
 Add the  plantains  and cook. 
 Add the  salt  and mix well. 
 Saute the  tomatoes ,  onions  and  garlic  in the  oil  then add to the beans. 
 Cook beans another half an hour.
