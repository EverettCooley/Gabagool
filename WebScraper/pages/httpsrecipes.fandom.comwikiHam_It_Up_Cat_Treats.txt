Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 2½ to 3 dozen 
 Ingredients : 
 1 jar (2½ ounces) strained  ham  baby food ( or   beef  or  chicken  baby food) 
 ⅝ cup  wheat germ 
 ⅝ cup  non-fat milk powder 
 1  egg , beaten 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray cookie sheet with  vegetable oil spray . 
 Mix baby food,  wheat germ ,  milk  powder, and  egg  in medium bowl. 
 Drop by ½ teaspoonfuls onto prepared baking sheet. 
 Bake 12 to 15 minutes. 
 Remove fromoven and let cool on wire rack. 
 Store baked treats in airtight container or plastic bag and place in refrigerator or freezer.
