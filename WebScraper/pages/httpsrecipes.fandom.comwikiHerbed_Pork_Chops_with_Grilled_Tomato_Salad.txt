Herbed  Pork  Chops With Grilled  tomato  Salad
 
 1 Tbs Chopped  rosemary  Leaves 
 2 Tsp  fennel seeds , Crushed 
 salt  And  pepper 
 
 4 Bone in  Pork  Chops 
 1 Pound  plum tomatoes , Each Cut in Half 
 1 Tbs  olive oil 
 2 Tbs Chopped  parsley  Leaves 
 1 Tsp Grated  lemon peel 
 1  garlic  clove, Crushed 
 
Prepare Outdoor Grill. in Cup, Combine  rosemary ,  fennel seeds , 1/2 Tsp  salt , 1/8 Tsp  pepper , Use to Rub All Over  Pork  Chops. in Bowl, Combine  tomatoes ,  oil , And 1/4 Tsp  salt . Place  Pork  Chops on Hot Grill. Cover Grill And Cook  Pork  Chops 5 minutes. Turn Chops Over And Arrange on 1 Side of The Grill. Place  tomatoes , Cut Side Down, on Other Side of Grill, Set Bowl Aside. Cover Grill And Cook  Pork  Chops Until Browned And Done. Cook  tomatoes  Until Charred. Place Chops on Plate And  tomatoes  on Cutting Board. Cool  tomatoes  Slightly, Cut Into Chunks. Return  tomatoes  And Their Juices to Bowl, Stir in  parsley ,  lemon peel , And  garlic . Serve Chops With  tomato  Salad.
 
This Recipe Makes 4 Servings.*
