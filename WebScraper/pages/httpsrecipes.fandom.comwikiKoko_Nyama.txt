 Description : 
 Also known as Koko a la viande (Koko with meat), this is a greens and  Peanut  dish popular in Cameroon and all over central Africa
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 pounds of Koko, cleaned, stems removed, and cut into very thin strips 
 peanut oil 
 1  Onion , chopped 
 1 pound  Beef  stew meat, cut into cubes 
 4 cups  water 
 1 cup  Peanut  paste 
 salt  and  pepper , to taste 
 Directions : 
 Heat the  oil  in a large pot and stir-fry the  onions  for a few minutes, until they become tender. 
 Add the meat to the pot and cook it, stirring frequently, until done. 
 Pour the  water  over the meat and  onions  and bring to the boil. Stir occasionally until the liquid is cooked into a thin sauce. 
 Reduce heat. Stir in the  Peanut  paste and seasoning until smooth. 
 Add the koko and cover. 
 Simmer over low heat until the greens are tender.
