      Amish macaroni salad     
 Ingredients : 
 ½ lb  macaroni , cooked 
 1½ cups  sugar 
 ½ cup  vinegar 
 ½ cup  flour 
 1 tablespoon  butter , melted 
 ¼ cup  yellow mustard 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 6 hard-boiled  eggs 
 1 cup  celery , chopped 
 1 cup  carrots , shredded 
 1  onion , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Mix  sugar ,  vinegar , flour . 
 Cook to boiling, cook, stir. 
 Cool completely. 
 Add  butter ,  mustardand   mayonnaise  to above mixture. 
 Add to  macaroni . 
 Stir in chopped  eggs ,  celery ,  carrots ,  onions .
