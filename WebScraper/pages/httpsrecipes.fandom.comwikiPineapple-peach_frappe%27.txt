 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup  pineapple , water-packed -Chunks 
 1/2 cup  pineapple juice , unsweetened 
 2 Water-packed  peach  halves -Canned and dice 
 1 cup Low-fat  yogurt , plain -unsweetened 
 1 cup Skim  milk 
 Sugar  subsitute to equal 2 -tablespoons  Sugar 
 Directions : 
 1 Combine  pineapple , juice, and  peaches  in container of an electric blender or food processor; process until pureed. 
2 Add  yogurt ,  milk , and  Sugar  subsititute; continue to process until smooth and thickened. 
3 3.pour into individuals glasses, and serve immediately. 
4 Food exchange per-serving: 1 fruit exchange + 1  milk  exchange; calories: 133; cho: 6 mg; car: 23 gm; pro: 7 gm; fat: 1 gm; sod: 103 mg;
