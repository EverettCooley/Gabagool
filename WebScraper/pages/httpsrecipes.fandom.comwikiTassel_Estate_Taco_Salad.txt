Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Tassel Estate in Wylie, Texas in 1986. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound lean  ground beef 
 1 white  onion 
 15 ounce can  kidney beans 
 Â¼ teaspoon  salt 
 4  tomatoes  chopped 
 1 head  iceberg lettuce  chopped 
 1 can sliced  olives 
 1 large  avocado 
 4 ounces sharp  cheddar  cheese shredded 
 1 small bag  nacho cheese taco chips  crushed 
 8 ounce bottle Catalina dressing 
 Directions : 
 Brown ground beef and onions then drain off fat and add kidney beans with juice and salt. 
 Simmer 10 minutes then drain. 
 When meat and bean mixture has cooled add to lettuce mixture along with dressing and cheese. 
 At the last minute add avocado and chips and mix well.
