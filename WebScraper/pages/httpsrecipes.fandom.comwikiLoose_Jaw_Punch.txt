Loose Jaw Punch
 
 5 Cups  orange juice 
 1 Container (12 Oz) Frozen Limeade Concentrate, Undiluted 
 2 Cups  tequila 
 3/4 Cup  triple sec  or  orange  Flavored Liqueur 
 2  limes  Thinly Sliced 
 
Combine All Ingredients in a Large Pitcher Stirring Until Limeade Concentrate Thaws. Cover And Chill at Least 2 Hours or Overnight. Serve Punch in Glasses Filled Three Quarters Full With  ice  Cubes. Add One to Two Slices of  lime  to Each Glass. Makes 12 Servings. For Nonalcoholic Punch, Omit  tequila  And Liqueur. Just Before Serving, Pour 3 Cups  club soda  or  lime  or  lemon  Flavored Sparkling  water  Into Punch. go Easy With This Punch, or The Fireworks Could Start Too Early.
