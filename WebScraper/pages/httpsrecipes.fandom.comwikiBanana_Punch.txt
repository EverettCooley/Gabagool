This is set to serve 40, however, it does keep for several days. Can be made several days ahead, no problem.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 C.  sugar 
 1 12 Oz. Can Frozen  orange juice , Thawed 
 1 12 Oz. Can  water 
 1 6 Oz. Can Frozen Lemonade, Thawed 
 1 6 Oz. Can  water 
 1 Lg. Can  pineapple juice  (believe it is 46 Ounces or So)Juice of 1  lemon 
 4 Large Crushed  bananas , Smoothed in Blender 
 Directions : 
 Boil Together For 3 minutes: 6 C. water and  sugar 
 Add the remaining ingredients 
 Break it up in 3 Batches.
 To Serve: Add 1 Quart or Gingerale to 1 Batch of Above Mixture RightBefore Servin
