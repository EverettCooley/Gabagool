Smoky Basting Sauce
 
 1 Cup  vinegar 
 1/2 Cup  oil 
 1/2 T.  liquid smoke 
 2 T. Grated  lemon peel 
 1/4 Cup  lemon juice 
 2 T.  brown sugar 
 2  bay leaves 
 black pepper  And  salt  to Taste
 
Mix Ingredients And Bring to Boil. Use to Baste Lean  Beef  or Chops While Cooking.Makes 1 3/4 Cup
