Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 ½ pound  turkey sausage  or other low-fat smoked  sausage , sliced into ½-inch thick slices 
 1 medium  onion , chopped 
 1 medium-size  green bell pepper , chopped 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 6 cups cooked  rice 
 2 x 14½-ounce cans Cajun-style stewed  tomatoes 
 1 x 15-ounce can  red beans , drained and rinsed 
 1 x 15-ounce can  navy beans , drained and rinsed 
 1 teaspoon  oregano 
 ½ teaspoon hot pepper sauce 
 1 cup thinly sliced  green onions 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in large skillet over medium-high heat until hot. 
 Add  sausage ,  onion ,  green pepper  and  garlic . 
 Cook and stir 7 to 10 minutes or until  sausage  is browned and  onion  is tender. 
 Add  rice ,  tomatoes , beans,  oregano  and hot pepper sauce . 
 Cook and stir 2 to 3 minutes more until well blended and thoroughly heated. 
 Sprinkle with  green onions  and serve immediately. 
 Nutrition Information : 
 Per Serving:
