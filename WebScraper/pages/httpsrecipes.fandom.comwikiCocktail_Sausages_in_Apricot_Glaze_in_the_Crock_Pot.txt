Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Inman Estate in Mineral Wells, Texas in 1990. My nieces and nephews love these.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup  apricot preserves 
 ¼ cup  prepared yellow mustard 
 2  scallions  chopped 
 ½ pound precooked mini smoked  sausages  (the ones with cheese in them) 
 Directions : 
 In a crock pot mix together the preserves and mustard then stir in scallions and sausages. 
 Cover and cook for 3 hours. 
 Remove cover and stir to mix then serve immediately with toothpicks.
