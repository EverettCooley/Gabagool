In Romanian: Cornete de parizer
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 bologna slices (not very thin), 
 12, 
 2 boiled  potatoes , 
 2-3 tablespoon  mayo , 
 salt , 
 pepper , 
 lettuce 
 Directions : 
 Roll the bologna slices to form cornucupias. 
 Anchor each with a toothpick and attach an olive to each end of the toothpick. 
 Fill each cornucupia with small cubes of  Potato  which have been mixed with the  mayo ,  salt  and  pepper . Cut the  lettuce  noodle fashion and place on a round platter. 
 Place the cornucupias on the platter with the tips in the center.
