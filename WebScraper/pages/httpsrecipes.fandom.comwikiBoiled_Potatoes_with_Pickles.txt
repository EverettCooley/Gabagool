      Boiled Potatoes With Pickles     
 Ingredients : 
 250 g  potatoes  boiled 
 80 g pickled  cucumbers  ( sauerkraut ) 
 60 g  onion 
 2 chopped  hard-boiled eggs 
 natural  mayonnaise 
 Directions : 
 Cut  potatoes  in small cubes, add finely chopped pickled  cucumbers  chopped egg  onion . 
 Add  salt  to taste and dress with mayo.
