Description : 
 Creamy, spicy, green milkshake.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup vanilla  ice cream 
 2 oz  low fat milk 
 6 oz  avocado 
 1â…“ cups of frozen  strawberries 
 1 tbsp  honey  or  maple syrup 
 1 tsp  nutmeg 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 whipped cream 
 Directions : 
 Cut  avocado  in half. 
 Put  ice cream ,  milk ,  avocado , and frozen  strawberries  into blender. 
 Add  Honey  or  maple syrup ,  nutmeg  and  cinnamon 
 Blend until smooth 
 Pour into 20 oz. serving cup 
 Top with  whipped cream 
 Sprinkle extra  nutmeg  if desired
