 Ingredients : 
 2 cups mashed cooked  sweet potatoes 
 2 cups  flour 
 1 cup  margarine 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Mix together in a dough. 
 Roll out in thin layer and cut into 1½ inch circles. 
 Slash designs with knife of half of each circle. 
 On other half put ½ tsp of either nut,  brown sugar ,  butter  or  margarine . 
 Turn over like ½ moon pies. 
 Crimp edges. 
 Bake at 350°F until brown. 
 Roll in  powdered sugar .
