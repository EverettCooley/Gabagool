Description : 
 In Romanian: Mincare de conopida.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large  cauliflower 
 3 tablespoons  oil 
 1 large  onion 
 ½ teaspoon  flour 
 5 – 6 large  tomatoes 
 ½ teaspoon  sugar 
 1 tablespoon mixed chopped  parsley  and  dill 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Divide the  cauliflower  into flowerets, wash and scald with hot  water . 
 Drain well. 
 Saute the finely chopped  onion  in hot  oil  until softened, add the  flour  and let fry a little. 
 Pour 2 – 3 tablespoons of  water , add  sugar ,  salt  and peeled, seeded and chopped  tomatoes . 
 After a minute or two of boiling, add the  cauliflower ,  parsley  and  dill  and simmer at low temperature, covered. 
 The liquid must be well reduced. 
 You can serve this dish warm or cold.
