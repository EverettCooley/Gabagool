 Description : 
 This is a great, healthy salad for dinners or lunches.
 
 Ingredients : 
 Dressing 
 1 tbsp soy sauce 
 1 tbsp  rice vinegar 
 2 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1/2 tbsp honey or equiv. sweetener 
 1/2 tbsp  ground ginger 
 1/4 - ½ tsp toasted sesame 
 Additional Ingredients 
 1 lbs fresh  green beans , blanched 
 and shocked in cold  water 
 1 med size  jicama , peeled and 
 julienned 
 1  red onion , sliced very thin 
 Directions : 
 Whisk together dressing ingredients. Toss in the other ingredients. Chill at least 1 hour before serving, tossing again after ½ hour (to help marinating process).
