Ingredients
 
  Milk                                           2 cups
 Expresso coffee Powder           2 tsps
 Sugar                                        1 Tbsp or to taste
 Vanilla Ice cream                      2 Tbsps plus scoop
  
 Method
  
 Blend milk, sugar, vanilla ice cream (2 tbsps) and the coffee powder in a blender or mixie.  Top with a scoop of vanilla ice cream. Sprinkle with coffee powder or cocoa over the ice cream for decoration. Serve chilled.
