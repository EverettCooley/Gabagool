 Ingredients : 
 1 shot De Kuyper Blue  curaçao 
 2 shots Stolichnaya  vodka 
 Top up Lemonade 
 garnish: Slice of  lemon  or  orange . 
 Glass: Highball
 
 Directions : 
 Half fill highball glass with cubed  ice . Pour in the De Kuyper Blue  curaçao  and Stolichnaya  vodka . Top up with lemonade and stir. Garnish with a slice of  lemon  or  orange  if desired.
