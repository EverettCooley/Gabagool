Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 2/11/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4 tbsp  butter 
 1  onion 
 1 stalk  celery 
 1 chopped  green pepper 
 1 tbsp meat seasoning 
 1 tbsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 ½ cup  evaporated milk 
 ½ cup  ketchup 
 1½ lb  hamburger 
 ½ lb  pork sausage 
 2  eggs 
 2 cups  bread crumbs 
 2 tbsp minced  garlic 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Melt the  butter  in a saucepan, over medium heat. 
 Add the  onion ,  celery , meat seasoning,  Worcestershire sauce . 
 Cook stirring occasionally and scraping bottom of pan until the mixture starts sticking excessively, about 6 minutes. 
 Stir in the  milk  and  ketchup . 
 Continue cooking for about 1 minute stirring occasionally. 
 Remove from the heat and allow to cool at room temperature. 
 Place the  beef  and  pork sausage  in a large mixing bowl. 
 Add the  eggs , cooked mixture (from saucepan) and  bread  crumbs. 
 Mix by hand, being careful not to over mix. 
 Overmixing will make the meat mushy. 
 Blend no longer than necessary to distribute the ingredients. 
 Place mixture in an ungreased 9x13" baking dish. 
 Shape into a loaf making sure not to touch the sides. 
 Bake uncovered 25 minutes. 
 Raise the heat to 400°F and continue cooking until done, approximately 35 minutes longer.
