 Ingredients : 
 8 mixed  peppers  (red, green, yellow) 
 500 g  minced meat  ( beef ,  pork  or  chicken ) 
 60 g  rice 
 2  onions 
 chopped  parsley 
 1 can of chopped  tomatoes 
 3 tbsp  oil 
 paprika 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Take the seeds out of the  peppers  by cutting a hole on top, wash and dry. 
 Chop finely the  onions . 
 Heat the  oil  in a pan, fry the  onions  for 2–3 minutes, add the minced meat and cook for about 5 minutes, add the  rice  and  tomatoes , season with  salt ,  pepper  and  paprika , cook for another 2 minutes and take off the hob. 
 Stuff each  pepper  with the mixture, arrange in an oven proof deep dish, fill halfway up with  water , cover with foil and cook for about 15-20 minutes in a preheated oven (190°C), uncover and cook for another 15–20 minutes until the  peppers  are cooked. 
 You can serve them on their own or with Greek style yogurt or make white sauce(2tbs flour in 2tbs sunflower oil,add the sauce from the peppers and boil 5-10 minutes.)
