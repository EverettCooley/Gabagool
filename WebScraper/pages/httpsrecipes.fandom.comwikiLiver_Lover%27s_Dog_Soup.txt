 Makes 2 liters of soup 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 1 Â¼ kilo  beef liver , trimmed membranes and chop finely 
 2 liters  Basic Dog Broth 
 salt 
 ground  black pepper 
 2 cups  heavy cream 
 4 tablespoons  all purpose flour 
 4 tablespoons  Italian parsley  chopped 
 kibble 
 Directions : 
 Heat up the  oil  in a saucepan over medium heat. 
 Add in the liver and let it cook for about 5 minutes. 
 Stir all this constantly. 
 Add in the broth,  salt , and  pepper  to taste, and let all this boil. 
 Reduce the heat once it starts boiling and simmer it covered for 30 minutes. 
 Stir in the  heavy cream . 
 This can be mixed with the  flour  if your dog likes heavier soups. 
 Serve it over  rice  or kibble.
