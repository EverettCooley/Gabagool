      Nuts     
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 cup  pecans 
 1 cup  cashews 
 1 cup  walnuts 
 ½ cup whole  almonds 
 ½ cup  butter 
 2  egg whites 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Add  sugar  and  salt  to  egg whites . 
 Beat till stiff peaks form and very stiff and thick. 
 Fold  nuts  into meringue. 
 Melt  butter  in a pan. 
 Stir nut mixture into melted  butter . 
 Bake for at least 30 minutes. 
 Stir every 10 minutes during baking. 
 Remove from pan immediately.
