 Description : 
 This recipe is a huge success! We have it every Sunday morning and it holds us over till dinner time.
 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 package of pizza dough to make one pizza 
 ½ lb  bacon  cooked crisp 
 ½ lb  sausage  crumbled and cooked 
 3  green onions  thinly diced 
 ½  green pepper  thinly sliced and diced 
 4  eggs  scrambled 
 8 oz shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Mix dough according to package. 
 While dough is rising cook  bacon  and  sausage . 
 Slice vegetables. 
 Cook scrambled  eggs  in the same skillet as the meat. 
 Spread dough thinly on pizza pan. 
 Cook for 5 minutes at 450°F remove from oven and add meat,  eggs , veggies, and  cheese . 
 Return to oven until  cheese  is melted. 
 Enjoy!
