Ingredients : 
 4 cups of diced boiled peeled  new potatoes 
 1 cup of roasted and coarsely crushed  peanuts 
 ½ cup  sesame  powder (crushed toasted sesame seeds)  [1] 
 2  lemons , juice of (add to taste) 
 4 – 5  green chilies , diced deseeded if too spicy 
 1 teaspoon  chili powder 
 2 inches fresh  gingerroot , grated 
 2 tablespoons  butter 
 1 teaspoon  cumin 
 ½ cup of chopped  coriander leaves 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Melt the  butter . 
 To the melted  butter  add the  cumin  and when brown turn off the gas. 
 To this add the  chilli powder  first and then all the other ingredients and toss together- you can adjust the amount of  lemon juice  /  salt  and chillies to your taste. 
 Notes : 
 
 ↑   If you do not have ready-made toasted sesame seeds, toast the seeds in a dry pan till golden brown and powder - this can be stored and used whenever you want.
