Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Prep time: 5 minutes | Cook time: 25 to 26 minutes 
 Serves: 4 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb (120 g) boneless, skinless  chicken breasts  (3 - 4 breasts) 
 ½ cup (120 ml) unsalted  pistachios , shelled and coarsely chopped 
 1½ tbsp (22.5 ml) Mrs. Dash Garlic and Herb Seasoning Blend, divided 
 1 tbsp (15 ml)  olive oil 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 375°F. 
 Place pistachios on a plate, add 1 tbsp Mrs. Dash Garlic and Herb Seasoning Blend and mix well. 
 Coat chicken breasts with pistachio mixture. 
 Heat olive oil in large non-stick skillet over medium heat, add chicken breasts and saute 3 - 4 minutes until brown. 
 Turn chicken, sprinkle with remaining Mrs. Dash Garlic and Herb Seasoning Blend and continue sauteing for another 3 - 4 minutes until brown. 
 Place chicken breasts in baking dish and bake in oven at 375°F for 20 minutes.
