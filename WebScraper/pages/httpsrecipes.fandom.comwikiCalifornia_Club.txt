 Description : 
 Summer crowd pleaser!
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 Ripened Avocados 
 2  tomatoes 
 1 Pound Peppered  Bacon 
 1 Pound Sliced deli style smoked  Turkey  breast 
 1 Pound Sliced deli style roast  Beef 
 1 Pound Sliced deli style smoked  Ham 
 1 Pound Sliced deli style  mozzarella   Cheese 
 1 pk. Kaiser or Submarine rolls 
 Miracle Whip or Mayonnaise as desired 
 Directions : 
 Peel and slice avocados
 Slice  tomatoes 
 Shred  lettuce 
 Fry  Bacon 
 Spread Miracle Whip or Mayonnaise on roll. Top with peppered  Bacon ,  Turkey  breast,  Ham ,  mozzarella   Cheese ,  tomatoes  and  lettuce  and  avocado  slices. 
 Serving suggestions: Serve with Soup such as potato -  Ham  chowder or with guacamole.
