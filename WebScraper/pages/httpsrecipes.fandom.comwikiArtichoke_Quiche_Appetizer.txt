Description : 
 Purchased from the Inman Estate in Mineral Wells, Texas in 1990. Dated 1942. I made this for a wedding shower I catered and it is OK, not great but 8 people asked me for the recipe, so somebody is seeing something I’m not.
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can water packed  artichokes 
 3  eggs 
 8 ounces  cream cheese , softened 
 1 cup low-fat  cottage cheese 
 ¾ cup  buttermilk 
 1 cup  Swiss cheese , grated 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  thyme 
 ¼ teaspoon white  pepper 
 ¼ teaspoon  Beau Monde seasoning 
 6 slices lean  bacon  cooked and crumbled 
 Directions : 
 Drain artichokes, cut in quarters. 
 Beat  eggs  then add  cream   cheese ,  cottage cheese  and  buttermilk . 
 Add grated  cheese . 
 Stir in seasonings and crumbled  bacon . 
 Place artichokes in bottom of pie shell; pour  cheese  mixture over artichokes. 
 Bake at 375 °F for 50 minutes.
