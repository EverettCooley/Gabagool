Description : 
 Indulge yourself in this low-fat chocolatey dessert. It's a good source of calcium.
 
 Contributed by Jenn B aka Mom2Sam and Tiny at  World Recipes Y-Group 
 Source: Kraft Kitchens 
 Prep Time: 10 minutes | Total Time: 1 hour 10 minutes 
 Makes: 6 servings, ⅔ cup each 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups thawed  Cool Whip  Lite Whipped Topping 
 2 cups cold  fat-free milk 
 1 pkg. (4-serving size) Jell-O Chocolate Flavor Fat Free Sugar Free  Instant Reduced Calorie Pudding  & Pie Filling 
 Directions : 
 Spoon ⅓ cup of the whipped topping into each of 6 dessert dishes. Using back of spoon, spread whipped topping onto bottom and up side of each dish. 
 Pour milk into medium bowl. Add pudding mix. Beat with wire whisk 2 minutes or until well blended. Spoon evenly into center of whipped topping. 
 Refrigerate 1 hour or until ready to serve. Store leftover desserts in refrigerator. 
 Kraft Kitchen tips : jazz it up! garnish with fresh  berries .
