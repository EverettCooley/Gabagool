 Makes 12 servings of brownie sundae. 
 Ingredients : 
 Brownies : 
 4 oz  semisweet chocolate , chopped 
 ½ cup  unsalted butter 
 1 cup  sugar 
 ¾ tsp  salt 
 ¾ cup  walnuts , chopped 
 3 large  eggs 
 ½ cup mini semisweet  chocolate chips 
 1 cup  all purpose flour 
 1 tsp  vanilla extract 
 3 pints vanilla  ice cream 
 Chocolate Sauce : 
 ¼ cup  unsalted butter 
 4 oz  semisweet chocolate 
 2 tbsp  corn syrup 
 ½ tsp  rum extract 
 pinch  salt 
 Directions : 
 Brownies : 
 Heat oven to 350°F. 
 Line 10x9x2 inch pan with nonstick foil, leaving overhang. 
 In saucepan, melt chocolate and butter until smooth. 
 Remove from heat. 
 Stir in sugar, salt and walnuts. 
 Stir in eggs. 
 Stir in chips, flour and vanilla. 
 Pour into pan. Bake 30 minutes. 
 Sauce : 
 In saucepan, melt butter and chocolate until smooth. 
 Stir in corn syrup, rum and salt. 
 Keep warm. 
 Sundae : 
 Lift brownie from pan. 
 While still warm, cut into 1 inch pieces. 
 Place 4 pieces in dessert bowls. 
 Top with ½ cup ice cream and 2 more pieces. 
 Drizzle with sauce. 
 Serve.
