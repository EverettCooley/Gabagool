Description : 
 Makes 2 dozen cookies, depending on size. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  sugar 
 3 cups sifted  flour 
 1½ tsp grated  lemon rind 
 2 cups  butter , softened 
 2 large  egg yolks 
 Directions : 
 Cream  Sugar  and  butter  in a medium-size bowl. 
 Beat in  eggs , then  flour  and  lemon peel . 
 Knead gently together. 
 Don't overwork the dough, but make sure it's consistent. 
 Chill dough for 3 hours. 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Roll out dough with rolling pin. 
 Cut into shapes and place on greased cookie sheets. 
 Bake until tops get pinkish-brown, about 8 minutes.
