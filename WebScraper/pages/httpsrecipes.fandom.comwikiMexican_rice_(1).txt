 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup long  grain   rice 
 2 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 1 tsp  garlic powder 
 1 1/2 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp ground  cummin 
 ½ ea  tomato , chopped 
 ½ ea medium  Onion , chopped finely 
 1 ea small  carrot , chopped finely 
 ½ ea  green pepper , chopped finely 
 1 1/2 cup boiling  water 
 16 oz can  tomatoes , cut-up 
 1 ea  tomato  sauce, optional 
 Directions : 
 In a skillet, saute  rice  in  oil  until lightly browned. Season with  garlic ,  salt , and  cummin . Add  tomato ,  Onion ,  carrot  and  green pepper . Saute for 1 to 2 minutes. Remove from heat, add  water . Return to heat and simmer for 35 to 40 minutes, covered, or until  water  is absorbed by  rice . Stir in  tomatoes  and  tomato  sauce, if desired, to add color. Makes 4 servings. Each serving = 18 % calories from fat
