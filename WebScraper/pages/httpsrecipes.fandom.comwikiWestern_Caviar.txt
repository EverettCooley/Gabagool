Description : 
 Makes 3 cups 
 Source: Marlboro country Cookbook 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cans (15 oz. each)  black beans  or  black-eyed peas 
 2 cloves  garlic , cut up 
 1 medium  onion , cut up 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  pepper 
 ⅓  parsley 
 ⅓ cup  olive oil 
 3 to 4 Tbsp  red wine vinegar 
 Directions : 
 Rinse and drain beans. 
 Place in a food processor with remaining ingredients. 
 Process with an on and off motion for about 1 minute. 
 Refrigerate overnight to allow flavors to blend. 
 Serve with chips, crackers or toast.
