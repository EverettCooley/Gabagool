Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz Appleton Estate V/X  rum 
 1 oz Jamaican  coffee liqueur 
 1 oz  crème de banane 
 ½ peeled chopped  banana 
 1 oz  single cream 
 1 oz  milk 
 Directions : 
 Blend all ingredients with crushed  ice  and serve in a highball glass. 
 Garnish with a  banana  slice.
