 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4  chicken  thighs, 
 1-2  onions , 
 250 gr.  rice , 
 1 cup  white wine  (or  water ), 
 1-2  green peppers , 
 1-2 spoons Picantina spice, 
 magdanoz ( parsley ), 
 savory , 
 salt , 
 black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Remove bones and chop the  chicken  meat into small pieces. 
 Cut  onions  into big pieces and fry them separately. 
 Add chopped  savory . 
 Mix everything and add  green pepper  (chopped up into not-so-small pieces) and wine. 
 Let it simmer on a gentle "fire". 
 The patty mixture must be boiled.  Add  salt  and  oil  to the  water . 
 When it is ready, strain it and cool it in cold  water . 
 Form it into balls and use it as a garnish for the kavarma.
