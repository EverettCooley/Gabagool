Description : 
 Contributed by Lilly at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Triscuit box 
 3 cups or 24 (2 tbsp salad and 7 crackers) servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (15 oz) can  white beans 
 1 (6 oz) can  tuna  in water, drained well, flaked 
 ¼  red onion , finely chopped 
 2 – 3 sprigs fresh  rosemary , finely chopped 
 2 cups  arugula , finely chopped 
 juice of 1  lemon 
 1 tbsp extra virgin  olive oil , for liberal drizzling 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 ⅛ tsp  pepper 
 Triscuit™ reduced fat  crackers 
 Directions : 
 Drain beans well. 
 Place ½ the beans in a bowl. 
 Mash remaining beans with fork. 
 Combine mashed and whole beans with tuna, onion, rosemary and arugula. 
 Dress salad with lemon juice, olive oil, salt and pepper. 
 Serve with crackers.
