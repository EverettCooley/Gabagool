 Description : 
 A  Catfish  recipe.
 
 Ingredients : 
 Catfish : 
 4 U.S. farm-raised  catfish  fillets 
 ½ teaspoon  garlic salt 
 ½ teaspoon  lemon pepper 
 Mustard-dill sauce : 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 2 tablespoons  all-purpose flour 
 1¼ cups  milk 
 ¼ cup chopped  parsley 
 ¼ cup chopped fresh  dill  or 1 teaspoon dried  dillweed 
 3 tablespoons  Dijon mustard 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Sprinkle  catfish  with  garlic salt  and  lemon pepper . 
 Preheat broiler. 
 Place on the greased rack of an unheated broiler pan. 
 Tuck under any thin edges. 
 Broil 3 inches from the heat for 4 to 6 minutes or until flakes easily with a fork. 
 Mustard-dill sauce : 
 Melt  butter  or  margarine  in a small saucepan. Stir in  flour . 
 Add  milk  all at once. 
 Cook and stir until thickened and bubbly. 
 Cook and stir one minute more. 
 Stir in  parsley ,  dill ,  mustard , and  salt . 
 Makes about 1½ cups sauce.
