Description : 
 Source:  Cookies Naturally  by Shirley Hartung, Kitchener, Ontario 1989
 
 Ingredients : 
 6  green tomatoes , chopped 
 6 tart  apples , chopped 
 1 cup  currants 
 1 cup  raisins 
 3 tsp ground  cinnamon 
 1 tsp ground  cloves 
 ½ tsp  allspice 
 ½ tsp ground  ginger 
 ¼ cup  vinegar 
 1 tbsp grated  orange peel 
 ½ cup unsweetened  orange juice 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in a large, heavy pot. 
 Simmer until thick. 
 Makes about 3 quarts. 
 Freeze  mincemeat  in one-cup portions. 
 Nutritional information : 
 ⅓ cup serving = 44 calories, 1 fruit and vegetable choice .6 grams protein, .2 grams fat, 11.3 grams carbohydrate, 1 gram fiber
