Ingredients : 
 1 lb of meat 
 1 can concentrated  tomato sauce  or well seasoned brown gravy 
 10  cabbage  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Soften the leaves a few minutes in very little  water  over low heat. 
 Fill leaves with stuffing. 
 Repeat same stuffing as for stuffed head only omitting leaves and heart. 
 Fold back two sides, then roll each leaf. 
 Secure with tooth-picks. 
 Arrange in pyrex or other oven dish. 
 Baste with: 1 can concentrated  tomato sauce  or well seasoned brown gravy. 
 Cover the dish. 
 Bake in hot oven (400°F) one hour, then reduce heat to 325°F and cook ½ hour more.
