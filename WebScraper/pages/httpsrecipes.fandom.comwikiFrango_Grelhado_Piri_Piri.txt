Description : 
 This Angolan version for Piri Piri  chicken  is very simple. As  piri piri peppers  are very hard to find, you can substitute other  hot peppers .
 
 Ingredients : 
 3½ lbs  chicken  parts 
 2 tbsp fresh  lemon juice 
 ¼ cup  peanut oil 
 1 finely chopped  jalapeño chile  or ¼ tsp finely minced  habanero chile 
 Directions : 
 Preheat broiler. 
 Wash the  chicken  parts and pat them dry. 
 Put  chicken  pieces in a broiling pan. 
 Mix the  lemon juice ,  peanut oil  and  chile  together. 
 Brush mixture over the  chicken . 
 Place  chicken  under the broiler and cook until done, about 7–10 minutes on each side. 
 Serve.
