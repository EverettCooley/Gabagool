Description : 
 This is a real taste experience. The fresh  cranberries  attack your taste buds with sour, and then the sauce pounds the same taste buds with sweetness.
 
 Ingredients : 
 Pudding : 
 2 cup  cranberry  (1 lb bag, washed) 
 ½ cup  molasses 
 2 tsp  soda  dissolved in ½ cup  water 
 1½ cups  flour 
 dash  salt 
 Sauce : 
 1 cup  sugar 
 ½ cup  butter  only, not  margarine 
 ½ cup  whipping cream 
 vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Mix pudding in order given. 
 Steam 2½ hours. 
 Mix sauce and simmer a few minutes. 
 Serve warm over pudding.
