Description : 
 Yield: 4 cups (serving size: ¼ cup)
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  port  or other sweet  red wine 
 ½ cup  honey 
 6 cups fresh  cranberries 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 tablespoon grated  orange rind 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 ⅛ teaspoon ground  cloves 
 ⅛ teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Combine port and honey in a large saucepan; bring to a boil. 
 Add cranberries; cook 6 minutes or until cranberries begin to pop, stirring occasionally. 
 Stir in sugar and the remaining ingredients; cook 5 minutes or until sugar dissolves. 
 Cover and chill.
