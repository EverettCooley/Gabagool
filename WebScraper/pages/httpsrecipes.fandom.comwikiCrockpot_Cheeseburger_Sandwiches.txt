 Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 6/17/07
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ lb  ground beef 
 1 pkg. processed cheese spread sliced ( Velveeta ) 
 2 tbsp  milk 
 1  green pepper  chopped 
 1  onion , chopped 
 2 cloves  garlic  minced 
 8 sandwich  buns 
 Directions : 
 In a large skillet, brown ground beef. 
 Drain. 
 In crock pot, combine ground beef and all remaining ingredients except buns; mix well. 
 Cover, cook on low setting 6 – 7 hours. 
 To serve; spoon mixture onto buns.
