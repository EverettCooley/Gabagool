 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 clove  garlic , halved 
 3/4 c.  beer 
 2 c. (8 oz.) shredded  Swiss cheese 
 1 c. (4 oz.) shredded sharp  Cheddar   Cheese 
 1 tbsp.  flour 
 Dash hot sauce 
 Directions : 
 Rub inside of pan with  garlic . Add  beer , heat slowly. Coat  Cheese  with  flour  and add to  beer . Stir constantly until  Cheese  is melted. Stir in hot sauce. Dip cubed  French bread  or  bagels  in fondue.
