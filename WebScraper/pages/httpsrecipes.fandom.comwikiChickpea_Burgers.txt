Ingredients : 
 2 large  egg whites   or  ¼ cup dry  breadcrumbs 
 ½ cup or more  sesame seeds 
 ½ tsp.  curry powder 
 1 tbs.  canola oil 
 2  green onions , finely chopped 
 ¾ cup finely chopped  white   or   cremini mushrooms 
 3 large cloves  garlic , minced 
 1 cup cooked  or  canned  chickpeas , drained and ½ cup liquid reserved 
 ⅓ cup  sunflower seeds 
 Directions : 
 In medium skillet, heat  oil  over medium heat. 
 Add  green onions  and  mushrooms  and cook, stirring often, until soft, about 5 minutes. 
 Remove from heat. 
 In food processor or blender, combine  garlic ,  chickpeas  and reserved  chickpea  liquid and process until smooth. 
 Transfer to large bowl and combine with  mushroom  mixture. 
 In food processor or blender, process  sunflower seeds  to make a coarse meal. 
 Add to  chickpea  mixture along with  curry  powder; mix well. 
 Stir in  egg whites  or bread crumbs (mixture will be soft). 
 On a large baking sheet, make 8 beds of  sesame seeds  for patties. 
 Shape burger mixture into 8 patties and place on seeds. 
 Flip to coat both sides generously. 
 Grill patties on a well-oiled vegetable grill or broil 4 inches from heat source, 3 to 4 minutes on each side until browned. 
 Serve right away.
