Desription : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Source:  Diabetic Living Diet 
 Prep Time: 15 minutes | Total Time: 15 minutes 
 Makes 4 cups (8 x ½ cup servings) 
 Ingredients : 
 16 oz  coleslaw  mix, packaged 
 ½ cup  ranch dressing , reduced-calorie 
 ¼ cup  sunflower seeds 
 2 tsp  dill 
 1 tsp  caraway seeds 
 Directions : 
 Toss together all ingredients. 
 Serve immediately or cover and chill up to 24 hours. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 41 Calories | 1.6g Total Fat | 0.2g Saturated Fat | 14mg Sodium | 8g Carbs | 1g Dietary Fiber | 5mg Cholesterol | 1g Protein 
 Exchanges: 1½ vegetables 
 Carb Choices: ½
