 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup cooked  rice 
 3/4 cup lowfat  Swiss cheese 
 1 tbsp  flour 
 1 pack frozen chopped  Broccoli 
 1/2 cup  Mushroom 
 1/4 cup  green onion 
 1 red  bell pepper 
 1 1/2 cup healthy choice cholesterol 
 1 cup skim  milk 
 1/2 tsp hot pepper sauce 
 3/4 tsp  salt 
 1/2 tsp dried  basil 
 1/4 tsp  pepper 
 2 tbsp  Parmesan cheese 
 Directions : 
 In large bowl, combine  rice ,  Swiss cheese ,  flour ,  Broccoli ,  Mushrooms  and  Onion . Place in 10-inch pie plate sprayed with  vegetable oil spray . Top vegetable mixture with red pepper slices. In medium bowl, combine  egg  product,  milk , hot pepper sauce ,  salt ,  basil  and  pepper . Pour  egg  product mixture over vegetable mixture. Sprinkle  Parmesan cheese  on top. Bake in 350 °F. Oven for 55 minutes.
