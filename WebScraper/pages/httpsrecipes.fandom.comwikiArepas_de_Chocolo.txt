      Arepas de Chocolo     
 Ingredients : 
 corn  on the cob 
 Directions : 
 Remove the  corn  from the cob and grind. 
 Knead it until it forms a firm ball. 
 Roast over a low charcoal fire or on a wire rack over the burner of the stove. 
 Serve hot with  butter ,  salt  and  white cheese .
