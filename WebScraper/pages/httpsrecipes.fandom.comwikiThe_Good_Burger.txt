Prep Time:15-20 mins 
 Cook time:15 mins 
 Serves:4 
 
 Description : 
 This is a hamburger with the works that make it a meal unto itself.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs lean  ground beef 
 1 thinly sliced Walla Walla (sweet  onion ) 
 2  avocados  sliced 
 4 slices  American cheese 
 8 slices of cooked  bacon 
 4 x 4" Sesame Hamburger buns (lightly toasted) 
 sliced  vine-ripened tomatoes 
 Directions : 
 Preheat large frying pan. 
 Form the hamburger into 8 oz patties(4 equal patties). 
 Cook until medium to medium well done, cut bacon slices in half and place 4 pieceson each patty with one slice of american cheese. 
 Cover for 1 minute. 
 On lightly toasted buns place a layer of sliced avacado and thinly sliced onion. 
 Place cooked burger on top add sliced tomatoes and top with top-half of bun. 
 Enjoy! 
 Also try adding a fried egg on top of the burger for an extra flavor!
