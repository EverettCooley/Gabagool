 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 ½  California avocado , halved and peeled 
 1 ½ Cups cups  mango  nectar 
 ½ Cup crushed  ice 
 Sugar , to taste 
 Directions : 
 Puree  avocado  with juice and  Sugar ; chill.
 Pour over  ice ; garnish with  mint  leaf.
