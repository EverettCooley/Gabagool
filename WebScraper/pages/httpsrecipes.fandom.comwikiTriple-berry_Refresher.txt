Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source : The Dallas Morning News Recipe Of The Day 
 Yield: 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can (12 fl. oz)  evaporated milk 
 1 container (6 or 8 oz) berry-flavored low-fat  yogurt 
 ½ cup fresh  blueberries 
 ½ cup fresh  raspberries 
 ½ cup fresh  blackberries 
 2 tbsp  honey 
 1 tray ice cubes 
 Directions : 
 Place evaporated milk, yogurt, blueberries, raspberries, strawberries and honey in blender; cover. 
 Blend until smooth. 
 Strain to remove seeds. 
 Serve over ice. 
 Notes : 
 Slightly thawed frozen unsweetened or lightly sweetened berries may be substituted.
