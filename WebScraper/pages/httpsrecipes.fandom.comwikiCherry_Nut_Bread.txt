 Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Marlin Estate in Caddo Mills, Texas in 1995. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups baking mix 
 ½ cup sugar 
 ⅓ cup flour 
 1 teaspoon cinnamon 
 1 egg 
 1 cup milk 
 1 cup candied cherries 
 ¾ cup nuts 
 Directions : 
 Flour 2 loaf pans. 
 Combine baking mix, sugar, flour, cinnamon, egg and milk together until well blended. 
 Stir in cherries then pour into loaf pans and bake 45 minutes at 350°F. 
 Cool completely before removing.
