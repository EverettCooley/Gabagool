 Description : 
 A great guac recipe with lots of flavor. A little rich, but it will go fast!
 
 Ingredients : 
 2  California avocados  - diced 
 2 8oz pkg  cream cheese  - softened 
 ½ Cup  Bacon  bits 
 2 large ripe  tomatoes  - peeled, seeded, diced 
 1 medium  yellow onion  - peeled, diced 
 1 medium green  chile  - diced 
 2 Tbsp  cilantro 
 ¼ Cup  lime juice 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Place ripe, cored  tomatoes  in boiling  water  about 10 seconds. 
 Remove from  water , peel, seed and dice.
 Peel and dice  Onion .
 Dice green  chile .
 Mix tomatoe,  Onion ,  chile ,  cilantro  and 1/8 cup  lime juice  together. 
 Chill while mixing remaining ingredients.
 Peel avocados. Dice and cover with remaining  lime juice .
 Mix softened  cream cheese  and avocados to your desired consistency (smooth or chunky).
 Fold  tomato  mixture into  avocado   cream cheese  mixture. 
 Add the  Bacon  bits and refrigerate for about 30 minutes, until  Bacon  bits are soft.
