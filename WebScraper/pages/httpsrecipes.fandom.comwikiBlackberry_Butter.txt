Description : 
 This is a recipe I obtained from an Estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Brigand Estate in Wills Point, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4 sticks  sweet butter , softened 
 1 cup fresh  blackberries 
 5 tablespoon  powdered sugar 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in a blender, then whip until light. 
 Serve with bread. This is great on pancakes, waffles or muffins. Reserve the homemade, fruit-based butter in the refrigerator.
