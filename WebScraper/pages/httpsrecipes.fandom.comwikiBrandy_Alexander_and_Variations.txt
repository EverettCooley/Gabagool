Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Family Circle, circa 2003 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  brandy 
 2 cups white  crème de cacao 
 2 cups  heavy cream  or  half and half 
 ½ cup cold  water 
 Directions : 
 Combine  brandy , white crème de cacao and  heavy cream  or half and half with cold  water  in large pitcher. 
 Cover. 
 Refrigerate at least 4 hours. 
 Dust each serving with  nutmeg . 
 Variations : 
 Mocha Alexander : Reduce  crème de cacao  to ¾ cup. Add ¾ cup  coffee-flavored liqueur  . Sprinkle with  cocoa  powder. 
 Dreamy Alexander : Reduce  crème de cacao  to ¾ cup. Add ¾ cup  cointreau . Slip half an  orange  slice onto the rim of each glass. 
 Alexander Mint : Opt instead for dark  crème de cacao  and reduce to 1 cup. Add ½ cup white or green  crème de menthe . Garnish with a  chocolate  covered  mint .
