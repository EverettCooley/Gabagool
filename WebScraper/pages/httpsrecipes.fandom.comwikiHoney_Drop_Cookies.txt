Ingredients : 
 ½ cup of  butter 
 the  whites  of 2  eggs , beaten dry 
 ½ cup of  granulated sugar 
 1 cup of  honey 
 3 cups of sifted  flour 
 beaten  yolks  of 2  eggs 
 1 teaspoonful of  soda 
 grated rind of 1  lemon 
 more  flour  if needed 
 3 tablespoonfuls of  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 First mix in the usual manner. 
 Drop the dough by teaspoonfuls onto a buttered baking pan, shape into smooth rounds. 
 Bake in a moderate oven. 
 For change add half a cup or more of cocoanut.
