A  Catfish  recipe.
 
 Serves 10 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb U.S. farm-raised  catfish  fillets 
 ½ teaspoon coarse ground  black pepper 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 1 cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 1 cup light  mayonnaise 
 1 x 14-oz. can  artichokes , drained and quartered 
 3 cloves  garlic , minced 
 Directions : 
 Season both sides of  catfish  fillets with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Lightly  oil  grill before placing  catfish  on it. 
 The should cook about 10 minutes per inch of thickness; turn once during grilling. 
 The is done when it is opaque and flakes when tested with a fork. 
 Remove from grill; cool at least five minutes. 
 Preheat oven to 400°F. 
 Chop either using a food processor and pulsing several times, or mashing in a bowl with a fork. 
 In a large bowl, mix together artichokes,  cheese ,  mayonnaise , and  garlic . 
 Spoon mixture into an oven-safe casserole dish coated with non-stick  cooking spray . 
 Bake for 20 minutes. 
 Broil for 5 additional minutes to brown the top.
