Ingredients : 
 2 cups  sugar 
 2 cups cold  water 
 1 cup  lard 
 1 cup  raisins 
 2 teaspoons ground  cloves 
 2 teaspoons  cinnamon 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon  baking soda 
 3 cups  flour 
 1 cup chopped  nuts 
 1 cup chopped mixed  candied fruit 
 Directions : 
 Put the first seven ingredients in large pot and let come to boil only. 
 Set aside to cool to lukewarm, then add the next four. 
 Mix well and put in greased and floured tube or bundt pan. 
 Bake at 325Â°F about one hour and ten minutes, or until well done. 
 Let cool some and then turn out on wire rack.
