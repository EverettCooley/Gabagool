Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz  Wild Turkey ® 101  bourbon whiskey 
 1½ oz 100 proof  tequila 
 1½ oz 151 proof  rum 
 ½ oz  Tabasco ® sauce 
 1 dash  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Build ingredients with 1 – 2 ice cubes in a highball glass, and serve.
