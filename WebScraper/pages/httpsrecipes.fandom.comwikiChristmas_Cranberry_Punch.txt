Description : 
 Taken from the  American Diabetes Association Holiday cookbook 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups  cranberry juice  cocktail 
 2 cups  orange juice 
 12 oz sugar-free lemon-lime pop 
 whole  cranberries 
 Directions : 
 Combine the  cranberry  and  orange  juices in a punch bowl. 
 Pour the carbonated beverage down the sides of the bowl. 
 Float whole  cranberries  on the top.
