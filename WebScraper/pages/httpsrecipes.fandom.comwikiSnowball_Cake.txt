Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 angel food cake 
 1 can crushed  pineapple  (drained) 
 1 cup  sugar 
 2 large  Cool Whip 
 2 envelopes unflavored  gelatin 
 4 tbsp cold  water 
 1 cup boiling  water 
 1 small can  coconut 
 2 tbsp  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Mix  gelatin  with cold  water . 
 Add boiling  water ,  sugar ,  lemon juice  and  pineapple . 
 Set in refrigerator to thicken (30 minutes). 
 Fold in  Cool Whip . 
 Set aside. 
 Crumble Â½ cake in 13x9 inch pan (bite-size pieces) 
 Spoon half of mixture over cake. 
 Repeat with other half. 
 Frost with  Cool Whip . 
 Sprinkle with  coconut .
