Sabatino Dressing
 
 1/2 cup  oil 
 1/2 cup  red wine vinegar 
 1 cup finely grated  Romano   Cheese 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 oregano  to taste 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix All Ingredients in Screw Top Jar; Shake to Mix Well.
