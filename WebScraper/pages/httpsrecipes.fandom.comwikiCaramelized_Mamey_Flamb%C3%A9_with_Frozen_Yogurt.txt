Description : 
 Yield: 4 servings - 210 calories. Per serving.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ lbs  mamey , ripe 
 1 tbsp  margarine 
 2 tbsp  brown sugar 
 1 tbsp fresh  lime juice 
 2 tbsp unsweetened  orange juice 
 3 tbsp  apricot flavoured brandy 
 1 cup  frozen non-fat vanilla yogurt 
 lime  slices (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Cut  mamey  in half lengthwise; remove seed. 
 Peel and cut lengthwise into 1 inch slices and set aside. 
 Place  margarine  in a large skillet; place over medium heat until  margarine  melts. 
 Add  brown sugar  and  lime juice ; stir well. 
 Add  mamey ; cook 2 minutes. 
 Turn  mamey  slices, and cook an additional 2 minutes. 
 Add  orange juice  and  brandy . 
 Ignite  brandy  mixture with a long match; let flames die down. 
 Arrange  mamey  slices on each of 4 dessert plates; spoon sauce evenly over fruit and top each with ¼ cup frozen  yogurt . 
 Garnish with  lime  slices, if desired. 
 Serve immediately.
