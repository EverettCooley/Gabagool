Description : 
 Simple baked fish
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: My old recipes 
 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  sole , washed and dried 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  pepper 
 ½ cup  parsley , minced 
 1  scallion , green and white part, minced 
 juice of ½ medium  lemon 
 ¼ cup unseasoned  breadcrumbs 
 ½ cup  olive oil 
 Directions : 
 Lightly grease a large shallow baking dish. 
 Place fillets in one layer in dish and salt and pepper them. 
 Spread parsley and scallions over them evenly. 
 Dribble lemon juice over the fish. 
 Now cover with very thin layer of breadcrumbs. 
 You may not use all the ¼ cup crumbs. 
 Slowly pour olive oil over fish, drop by drop so that all is well covered. 
 Bake at 350°F for 20 minutes. 
 Fish is done with it flakes easily with a fork. Avoid overcooking. Test a little sooner than you think it is done.
