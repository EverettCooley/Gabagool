Dole's Holiday Ambrosia
 
 1 Can Chunk  pineapple  in Juice or Syrup (20 Oz) 
 1 Can  mandarin orange  Segments (11 Oz.) 
 1 1/2 Cups Seedless  grapes 
 1 Cup Miniature Marshmallows 
 1 Cup Flaked  coconut 
 1/2 Cup Nuts 
 3/4 Cup  sour cream 
 1 T.  sugar 
 
Drain  pineapple  And  orange  Segments. Mix  pineapple ,  oranges ,  grapes , Marshmallows,  coconuts  And Nuts.Mix  sour cream  And  sugar .Stir Into Fruit Mixture. Chill. Serves 4 to 6Source: my Old Recipes
