       Alaska Sablefish Salad     
 This recipe yields 2 main-dish servings or 4 side-dish servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup steamed or poached  sablefish  cut 1" chunks 
 1  tomato  diced 
 1  avocado  peeled, diced 
 ½ cup diced  cucumber 
 2  green onion  diagonally sliced 
 lettuce 
 Vinaigrette dressing : 
 3 tbsp  oil 
 3 tbsp  wine vinegar 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 ⅛ tsp freshly-ground  black pepper 
 ⅛ tsp crushed  oregano  leaves 
 3 dash bottled  hot pepper sauce 
 Directions : 
 Combine  sablefish ,  tomato ,  avocado ,  cucumber  and  onion . 
 Toss gently with  vinaigrette  dressing. 
 Serve on lettuce-lined platter. 
 Vinaigrette dressing : 
 Combine all ingredients.
