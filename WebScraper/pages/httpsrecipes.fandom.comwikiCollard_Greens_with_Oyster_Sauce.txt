Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Knudson Estate in Cedar Hill, Texas in 1992.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  peanut oil 
 3 cloves  garlic  finely chopped 
 1½ pounds  collard greens  cut into bite size pieces 
 ¼ cup  water 
 ¼ cup  oyster sauce 
 1 tablespoon  sesame seeds 
 Directions : 
 Heat oil in large skillet over high heat and sauté garlic for 10 seconds. 
 Add water and collard greens then cook stirring and tossing constantly for 5 minutes. 
 Stir in oyster sauce immediately before serving and garnish with sesame seeds.
