Makes 12 pieces.
 
 Ingredients : 
 18 oz pkg sugar cookie dough 
 13.5 container cream cheese fruit dip ( i used plain cream cheese) 
 3 cups assorted fruit, well drained (i used strawberries, blackberries, pineapple chunks and mandarin orange slices) 
 caramel sauce 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 325 degrees. 
 Arrange cookie dough on 12 inch pizza pan. 
 Press dough to rim of pan, forming a solid cookie crust. 
 Bake 20 min. or until lightly browned. 
 Cool. 
 Spread cream cheese over cooled crust. 
 Arrange fruit on top. 
 Chill. 
 Drizzle warmed caramel sauce over pizza before serving.
