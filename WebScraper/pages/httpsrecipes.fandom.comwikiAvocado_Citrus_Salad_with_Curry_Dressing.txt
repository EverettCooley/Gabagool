 Ingredients : 
 2 naval  oranges 
 2 ruby red  grapefruits 
 2 heads  Boston lettuce , torn 
 3 large  California avocados , peeled 
 ½ medium  purple onion , sliced and separated into rings 
 Curry dressing : 
 ¾ cup  mayonnaise 
 ⅓ cup reserved citrus  juices 
 1 tablespoon  honey 
 2 teaspoons  curry powder 
 2 drops  hot sauce 
 ⅛ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅛ teaspoon  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Peel and section  oranges  and  grapefruit  over a bowl, reserving juices. 
 Set aside ⅓ cup juices for  curry dressing . 
 Cut  avocados  into thin slices. 
 Arrange  avocado ,  onion ,  orange  sections, and  grapefruit  sections over  lettuce ; drizzle with  curry dressing . 
 Serve immediately. 
 Curry dressing : 
 Process all ingredients in a food processor or  blender  until smooth, stopping to scrape down sides.
