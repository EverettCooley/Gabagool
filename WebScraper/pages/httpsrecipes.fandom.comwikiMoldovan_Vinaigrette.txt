Ingredients : 
 2  beets 
 3 – 4 big  potatoes 
 1  carrot 
 1 can of  green peas  drained 
 2 – 3  pickles 
 1  onion 
 3 tablespoons  oil 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Boil separately the  beet ,  potatoes  and  carrot . 
 Let cool. 
 Cube the  potatoes ,  carrot  and  beet  and then mix with the  peas , chopped  onion  and pickles. 
 Add  salt  and  oil . 
 Mix well and let stand at least an hour to mix the flavors. 
 Arrange in salad bowl.
