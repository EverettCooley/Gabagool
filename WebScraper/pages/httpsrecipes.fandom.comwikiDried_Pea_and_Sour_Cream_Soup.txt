Description : 
 In Romanian: Supa de mazare uscata si smantana
 
 Ingredients : 
 7 oz / 200 g dried  peas 
 2  potatoes 
 1  onion 
 1 teaspoon  butter 
 1 hard boiled  yolk 
 Â½ cup  sour cream 
 1 teaspoon  flour 
 salt 
 chopped  dill 
 cubed toasted  bread  
 Directions : 
 Boil the peas with the vegetables. 
 Strain and sieve only the peas and  potatoes . 
 Thin with some of the boiling liquid and set to boil with the hard boiled  yolk  which you have previously mixed with  butter ,  flour  and  sour cream  and thinned with a little boiling liquid. 
 Mix well, add  salt  and chopped  dill  and let boil for a minute or two. 
 Serve with croutons.
