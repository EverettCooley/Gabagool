Corn-based flatbread that are cooked on a griddle. 
 Similar to mexican tortilla's but thicker. 
 Golden yellow and slightly sweet. 
 Moist even after cooking. 
 Made from whole-wheat flour, fresh corn kernel, water, sugar, alt, ghee, and finely ground corn meal.
