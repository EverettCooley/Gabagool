 Ingredients : 
 3  eggs 
 1 cup  oil 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 1 cup chopped  walnuts  (or another  nut  if you prefer) 
 2 cups  sugar 
 1 tsp  salt 
 2½ cups shredded  zucchini 
 1½ cup shredded  carrot 
 2 tsp  baking powder 
 2 tsp  baking soda 
 3 tsp  cinnamon 
 2½ cups  flour 
 Directions : 
 Beat at  eggs   oil  and  vanilla  together 
 In a separate bowl mix together  sugar ,  salt , baking soda and  cinnamon 
 Add  zucchini ,  carrots ,  nuts  and blend thoroughly 
 Add rest of dry ingredients, mix thoroughly 
 Pour into two 9x5 greased and floured loaf pans. Bake at 350°F for slightly over an hour
