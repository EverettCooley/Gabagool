Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup  beer 
 3 tbsp  oil 
 2 tbsp  parsley 
 4 tsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 1  garlic  clove, minced 
 salt  and  pepper 
 2 lbs large  shrimp , in shells 
 Directions : 
 Combine  beer ,  oil ,  parsley ,  Worcestershire sauce ,  garlic ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Add  shrimp , stir and cover. 
 Marinate for 60 minutes. 
 Drain, reserving marinade. 
 Place  shrimp  on well-greased broiler rack; broil for 4 minutes, 4 – 5 inches from flame. 
 Turn and brush; broil 2 – 4 minutes more or until bright pink.
