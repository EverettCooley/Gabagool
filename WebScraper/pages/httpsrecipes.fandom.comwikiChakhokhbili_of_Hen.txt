Ingredients : 
 1  hen 
 ½ cup  clarified butter 
 4 – 5  tomatoes 
 3  onions 
 1 bunch greens ( coriander  and  basil ) 
 2  laurel-leaf 
 Directions : 
 Cut a thoroughly cleaned and washed hen into pieces, put into a sauce-pan and stew on a small fire with the cover of the pan closed. 
 Add finely shredded  onions  and  butter  or  clarified butter  and fry. 
 Put in a laurel-leaf. 
 Stir from time to time with a spoon till the meat turns red. 
 Pour 1 glass of boiled  water  over and put separately stewed peeled  tomatoes . 
 Boil till the hen meat is ready. 
 Add then ground  pepper , finely shredded 
 pepper  and  salt  to taste.
