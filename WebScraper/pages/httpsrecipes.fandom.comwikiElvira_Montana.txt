Ingredients : 
 ½ ounce  Jägermeister ® 
 ½ ounce  Goldschläger ® cinnamon schnapps 
 ½ ounce Rumple Minze®  peppermint schnapps 
 2 ounces  Bacardi 151 
 1 ounce  coffee liqueur 
 ½ ounce  light cream 
 Directions : 
 Fill saucer with sugar. 
 Moisten 5 ounce old fashioned glass with lime wedge. 
 Dip rim of glass in saucer. 
 Turn glass right-side up, fill with ice,and add ingredients. 
 Stir well and serve.
