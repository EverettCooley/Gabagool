Description 
 From Recipes Wiki—original source of recipe, licensed under the GNU Free Documentation License
 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 1 oz  dark rum 
 1 oz  light rum 
 4 oz  pineapple juice 
 1 oz  cream of coconut 
 ½ cup  peaches ; fresh; peeled (more or less, to taste) 
 1 cup  ice ; crushed 
 Directions 
 Combine all ingredients in blender and blend well.
