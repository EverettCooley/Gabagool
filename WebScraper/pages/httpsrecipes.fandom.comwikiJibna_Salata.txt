 Description : 
 This is a salad with small pieces of  tomatoes ,  cucumber , rocket leaves and some  salt  as per taste.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2  cucumber 
 3  tomatoes 
 rocket leaves (or arugula) 
 2 spring  onions 
 100g  feta   Cheese  / jibna 
 pinch  salt 
 Directions : 
 Chop vegetables and mix together. 
 Add  salt  and toss. 
 Grate  feta  over salad.
