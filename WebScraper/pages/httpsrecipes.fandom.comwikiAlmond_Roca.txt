Ingredients : 
 2⅔ cup  sugar 
 1 lb  butter 
 2 cups slivered  almonds 
 1 large  Hershey bar  (½ lb) 
 Directions : 
 Stir sugar and butter in frying pan until the color of light tea. 
 Pour onto a greased cookie sheet 
 Pour melted chocolate over the top 
 Sprinkle slivered almonds on top of the chocolate 
 Let set and repeat other side
