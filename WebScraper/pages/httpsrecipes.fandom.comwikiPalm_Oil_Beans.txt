Ingredients : 
 1 kg  beans  (approximately 2 lbs) 
 1 glass  palm oil 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Cook the beans until tender. 
 Add the  palm oil  and  salt . 
 Simmer until the  oil  is cooked. 
 Serve.
