Ingredients : 
 8  onions , sliced thin 
 1 stick  margarine 
 salt  and pepper - to taste 
 3 tbsp  flour 
 1 pkg.  onion soup mix 
 5 quarts  water 
 garlic  toast 
 mozzarella  cheese, grated 
 Directions : 
 In a large soup pot saute  onions  in  margarine  until clear. 
 Do not brown!! add  salt  and  pepper  and  flour , til until absorbed. 
 Add soup mix and  water . 
 Cook for 30 minutes. 
 Pour soup over  garlic  toast in crocks or oven proof bowls. 
 Top with grated  cheese  and put in oven for 3 minutes until  cheese  melts. 
 Serve immediately.
