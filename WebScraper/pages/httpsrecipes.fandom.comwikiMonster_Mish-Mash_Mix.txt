Description : 
 Contributed  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 12 cups 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (3.5 ounce) bag microwave  popcorn , popped 
 2 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine , melted 
 2 tablespoons  honey 
 ¼ teaspoon  cinnamon 
 1 cup  raisins  or  peanuts 
 1 cup  candy corn 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 275°F. 
 Place popped corn into a 13 x 9-inch baking pan. 
 Combine butter, honey and cinnamon. 
 Pour over popcorn; stir until evenly coated. 
 Bake for 10 minutes. 
 Spread on foil to cool. 
 Stir in raisins and candy corn. 
 Store in tightly covered container.
