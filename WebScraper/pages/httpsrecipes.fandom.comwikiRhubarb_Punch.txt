Ingredients : 
 6 cups finely chopped fresh  rhubarb 
 6 cups  water 
 1 (12 oz) can frozen lemonade concentrate 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 liter lemon-lime soda, chilled 
 Directions : 
 In large saucepan, combine  rhubarb ,  water , lemonade and  sugar . 
 Cover and cook about 20 minutes. 
 Until  rhubarb  is very soft. 
 Strain to remove pulp. 
 Chill liquid. 
 Just before serving, pour  rhubarb  mixture over  ice cubes  in punch bowl. 
 Carefully pour in chilled soda. 
 Garnish with additional  ice cubes  or an  ice  ring.
