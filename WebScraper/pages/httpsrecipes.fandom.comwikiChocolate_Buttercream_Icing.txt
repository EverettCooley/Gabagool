 Description : 
 In Romanian: crema de unt cu ciocolata
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 oz/250 g  butter , 
 7 oz/200 g  confectioner's sugar , 
 5 oz/150 g  chocolate 
 Directions : 
 Cream the  butter  with the  Sugar . 
 Melt the  chocolate  with 2 tablespoons of  water  and then add to the butter- Sugar  mixture.
