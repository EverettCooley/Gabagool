Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  brown rice 
 1 cup (4 ounces) shredded  Cheddar  or  American cheese 
 2 tablespoons chopped fresh  parsley 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon prepared  mustard 
 1/2 teaspoon  Onion  powder 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1/4 teaspoon  ground red pepper 
 1 cup  milk  (made with nonfat dry  milk ) 
 2  eggs , beaten 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  Cheese ,  parsley ,  salt ,  mustard ,  Onion  powder,  black pepper , red pepper,  milk  and  eggs  in large bowl; mix well. 
 Turn into greased 8x8x2-inch baking dish. Bake at 350 degrees 30 to 35 minutes or until firm near center. Do not overbake.
