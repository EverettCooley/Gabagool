 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  butter , softened 
 1 and 1/2 cups  Sugar 
 2 and 1/2 cups  all-purpose flour 
 3  eggs 
 1 tsp.  vanilla 
 1/2 tsp.  salt 
 1 tsp.  baking powder 
 1 Tbsp.  orange  zest, grated 
 1/3 cup  orange juice 
 1/4 cup  rum 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 cream  together  butter  and  Sugar . 
 Add remaining ingredients and mix well. Grease and  flour  a tube pan. 
 Pour batter into pan and bake at 350 degrees for 45 â€“ 50 minutes. 
 If you don't want to use  rum  you may increase  orange juice  to 1/2 cup and use 1 tsp. of  rum extract .
