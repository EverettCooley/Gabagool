Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 mild or hot  Italian sausage  links 
 2 tbsp  olive  or  salad oil 
 1 large  onion , thinly sliced and separated 
 1 clove  garlic , minced or crushed 
 3 medium sized  green  or  red bell peppers , seeded and cut into strips 
 1 (15 oz) can Italian-style  tomatoes  (plum) 
 1 tbsp each dried  basil , sugar, salt and pepper 
 4 French rolls or  hot dog buns 
 Directions : 
 Cook sausages in large frying pan over medium heat until golden brown. 
 Remove pan and set aside. 
 Add oil, onion, garlic and peppers to drippings. 
 Cook, stirring occasionally until vegetables are soft, about 15 minutes. 
 Stir in tomatoes, breaking up with spoon. 
 Then add basil and sugar. 
 Add sausages and cook 15 minutes. 
 Remove cover, increase heat to medium high and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
 Season to taste with salt and pepper. 
 Place sausages and tomato sauce in bun and enjoy.
