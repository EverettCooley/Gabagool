Description : 
 Makes 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  raisins 
 ½ cup  rolled oats 
 ½ cup  brown sugar 
 ¼ cup  all purpose flour 
 ½ tsp  cinnamon 
 ¼ cup  shortening 
 ½ cup  pecans , chopped 
 6 medium  apples , peeled and cored 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 Directions : 
 In a small mixing bowl pour enough boiling  water  over the  raisins  to cover them. 
 Let stand for 5 minutes, then drain  raisins . 
 For topping, in a medium mixing bowl stir together  rolled oats ,  brown sugar ,  flour , and  cinnamon . 
 Using a pastry blender, cut in  margarine  of  butter  till mixture resembles coarse crumbs. 
 Stir in chopped  pecans . 
 Set topping aside. 
 For filling, in large mixing bowl combine drained  raisins , sliced  apples , and  sugar , then gently toss till combined. 
 Transfer the filling to an ungreased 9" x 9" x 2" baking pan. 
 Sprinkle the topping on the filling. 
 Bake in a 375 °F oven for 30 to 35 minutes or till the  apples  are tender and topping is golden. 
 Serve warm.
