 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds of peeled  Crawfish  tails 
 1  Onion , chopped 
 1  bell pepper , chopped 
 4 slices of stale bread 
 2 tablespoons of  Crawfish  fat(if available) 
 1  egg , beaten 
 3 tablespoons of chopped  parsley 
 3 tablespoons of chopped  green onion  tops 
 salt ,  black pepper  and  cayenne pepper , to taste 
 11/2 cups of bread crumbs 
 Directions : 
 Grind  Crawfish  tails,  onions ,  bell pepper  and bread slices in food processor or grinder. 
 Add  Crawfish  fat,  egg ,  parsley  and  green onion  tops; mix thoroughly; add  salt ,  black pepper  and  cayenne pepper  and mix. 
 Shape into balls the size of a  walnut  and roll into bread crumbs. 
 Bake 20 minutes at 350 degrees
