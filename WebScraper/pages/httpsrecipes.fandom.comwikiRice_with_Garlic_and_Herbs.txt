Ingredients : 
 240 g  long-grain rice , (3 dl) 
 5  water 
 1 tsp  thyme 
 1 tbsp  oregano 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 1 fresh  chives , finely chopped 
 2 cloves  garlic , crushed or minced 
 Directions : 
 Rinse  rice  and discard  water . 
 Bring  rice ,  water ,  thyme ,  oregano  and  salt  to a boil. 
 Let boil covered for 20 – 25 minutes at low heat. 
 Add  chives  and  garlic  before serving.
