 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3  egg  whites 
 2/3 cup  Sugar 
 4 cup total cereal 
 1/2 cup  semisweet chocolate  chips 
 - if desired 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 325 degrees. Lightly grease cookie sheet. Beat  egg  whites in large bowl until foamy. Gradually beat in  Sugar ; continue beating until very stiff and glossy. Fold in cereal and  chocolate chips . Drop mixture by teaspoonfuls 2 inches apart onto cookie sheet. Bake 14 to 16 minutes or until golden brown. Store in airtight container. Makes 3-½ dozen meringues
