Description : 
 Contributed by Rosalie Harrington
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large  eggplant , peel, slice 
 1 tbsp  olive oil 
 4 cloves  garlic , roasted 
 1 cup non-fat  yogurt 
 2 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 tbsp  dill 
 3 tbsp  parsley , minced 
 Â¼ cup  walnuts , chopped 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Paint  eggplant  with  olive oil  and grill. 
 Put in processor. 
 Add  garlic ,  yogurt ,  lemon juice , dill,  parsley ,  nuts ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Process until pureed. 
 Serve in hollowed out head of  cabbage  or on a dish. 
 Can dilute with  chicken broth  to make a soup.
