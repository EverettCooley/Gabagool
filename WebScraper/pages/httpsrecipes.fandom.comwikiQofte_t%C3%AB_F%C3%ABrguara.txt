 Description : 
 Fried meatballs. Serves 4 people
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  ground lamb  or  beef  or  chicken 
   or lamb,beef and pork together (is testier and soft)
 
 ½  onion , finely chopped 
 ⅓ cup plain  breadcrumbs 
 4 tbsp chopped  feta  cheese 
 1  garlic  clove, crushed 
 ¼ cup chopped  mint  (use less, if dried) 
 ½ tsp  cinnamon 
 ½ tsp  oregano 
 salt  and  pepper 
 flour 
 oil 
 Directions : 
 Soak bread in  water  or milk until very soft. 
 Squeeze hard to drain. 
 Combine all ingredients except  flour  and  oil . 
 Let stand covered for 1 hour or 24 hour in the fridge to allow the flavors to blend. 
 Shape into ½-inch thick patties. 
 Dust with  flour  and fry until done at 350°F – 365°F.
