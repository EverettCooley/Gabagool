 Description : 
 Serve your customers this spicy dipping sauce for  Onion  flowers, fried vegetables,  Chicken  wings or use as a topping for grilled meat, poultry, or  egg  dishes.  California avocado  makes almost any dish command a better price.
 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  California avocado  (8 ounces) 
 1 ½ Tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 Tbsp finely chopped  Onion 
 1 clove  garlic , chopped finely 
 ¼ tsp dried  thyme 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 Large pinch ground  black pepper  or to taste 
 Pinch  white pepper  or to taste 
 Pinch  Cayenne  or to taste 
 Directions : 
 Roughly mash  avocado . (Texture should not be smooth.) 
 Fold in remaining ingredients. 
 Chill 1 hour to marry flavors. 
 Best served the day it's made. 
 Serve with fresh or fried vegetables,  Chicken  wings or use as a topping for grilled meat, poultry, or  egg  dishes.
