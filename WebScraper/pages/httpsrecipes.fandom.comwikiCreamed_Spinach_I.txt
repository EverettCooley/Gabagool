Desription : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes For Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Original recipe 
 Makes 2 – 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 9 ounce bag fresh  spinach , washed and chopped fine 
 ¼ cup  butter 
 3 tablespoons  cream cheese , 1½ ounces 
 1 teaspoon  heavy cream 
 4 ounces  cheddar  cheese or Monterey  jack cheese , shredded, ½ cup 
 salt  and  pepper , to taste 
 Directions : 
 Melt the butter in a medium pot; add the cream cheese. 
 Stir until melted. 
 Add the spinach and cook just until wilted. 
 Add the cream, cheddar, and salt and pepper. 
 Gently stir until the cheese melts and coats the spinach; serve at once.
