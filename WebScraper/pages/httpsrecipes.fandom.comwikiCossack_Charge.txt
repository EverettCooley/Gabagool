Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz Stolichnaya®  vodka 
 ½ oz  cognac 
 ½ oz  cherry brandy 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients with cracked  ice  in a shaker or blender and pour into a chilled cocktail glass.
