      Almond Cream     
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup unfiltered  apple juice 
 3½ tbsp  agar flakes 
 1 tbsp  kudzu 
 2 cup almond  amazake  or vanilla  soy milk 
 3 tbsp  maple syrup 
 2 tbsp pure  vanilla extract 
 2 tsp alcohol-free  almond extract 
 2 tbsp  almond butter 
 ¼ tsp  sea salt 
 ¼ tsp ground  cardamom 
 Directions : 
 Pour  apple juice  into a small  saucepan 
 Add  agar flakes , and let sir 10 minutes. 
 Place over medium heat and bring to a slow simmer. 
 Cook 10 minutes to dissolve  agar flakes . 
 Dissolve kudzu in ¼ cup  amazake . 
 Add remaining 1¾ cups  amazake ,  maple syrup ,  vanilla extract ,  almond extract ,  almond butter ,  salt  and  cardamom . 
 Whisk  kudzu  mixture into  apple juice  and cook, 15 minutes. 
 Pour into a shallow dish or pan and bring to room temperature. 
 Chill at least 1 hour.
