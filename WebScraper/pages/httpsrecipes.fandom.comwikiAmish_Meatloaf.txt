      Amish Meatloaf     
 Ingredients : 
 1½ lbs lean  ground beef 
 1 beaten  egg 
 ½ tsp  sage 
 ½ cup  Pet milk 
 2 cups  crackers 
 ¼ cup  onion 
 ½ cup  cheese 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Mix all above together. 
 Pat into an oval loaf in a baking dish. 
 Put the extra shredded  cheese  over top of loaf. 
 Bake at 350 °F for 1 hour. 
 Will freeze well.
