blueberry  And  banana  Pie
 
 1 (9 Inch) Deep Dish Pie Crust 
 1 Can  blueberry  Pie Filling 
 1 (12 Oz.)  Cool Whip 
 3 or 4  bananas 
 
Bake Pie Crust as Directed on Package. Cool. Slice BananasAnd Cover Sides And Bottom of Cooled Pie Crust. Next SpreadCool Whip Over  bananas . Pour  blueberry  Pie Filling Over CoolWhip. Keep Refrigerated. Fast, Easy And Good!
 
Enjoy.
