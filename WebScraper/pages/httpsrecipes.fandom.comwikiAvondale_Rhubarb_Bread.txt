Ingredients : 
 3  eggs 
 1 cup  vegetable oil 
 1¾ cups  brown sugar 
 2 tsp  vanilla 
 2 tsp  baking soda 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 1 tsp  salt 
 2 tsp  cinnamon 
 ¼ tsp  nutmeg 
 ½ tsp  allspice 
 1 cup  whole wheat flour 
 2¼ cups  flour 
 ¾ cup  walnuts 
 2⅓ cups diced  rhubarb 
 Directions : 
 Blend first 4 ingredients together. 
 Sift all dry ingredients to together and add to mixture. 
 Stir in  walnuts  and  rhubarb . 
 Pour into 2  bread  loaf pans or muffin tins. 
 For  bread , bake at 350 degrees for 50 minutes; for muffins, bake at 350 °F for 25 minutes.
