Description : 
 An alcoholic Roy Rogers.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 ounces Bacardi Silver ® 
 2 ounces Peter Heering ® 
 2 ounces Coca-Cola with lime ® 
 
 
 Directions : 
 1.Fill 12 ounce highball 
 glass with ice.
 2.Add ingredients.
 3.Stir well using standard 
 bar spoon and serve.
