 Description : 
 Date and source unknown. This is one of the many clippings in my collections I purchased from a garage sale somewhere along the way. I have made this recipe and it is fabulous. Highly recommend it. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1-1/2-cups hot  paprika 
 1/2-cup dry  mustard 
 1/4-cup  garlic powder 
 1-tablespoon  cayenne pepper 
 2-tablespoons freshly ground  black pepper 
 1-tablespoon  ground ginger 
 1/2-cup honey 
 1/2-cup  red wine vinegar 
 1/4-cup  worcestershire sauce 
 2 cans  beer  (not light) 
 4-Slabs baby back ribs (1-1/4-pounds each) 
 
 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl, sift together  paprika ,  mustard ,  garlic powder ,  Cayenne ,  black pepper  and  ginger . 
 In another bowl combine the honey,  red wine vinegar , and  worcestershire sauce . 
 Slowly stir the liquid ingredients into the dry ingredients, adding as much  beer  as needed to form a thick smooth paste. 
 Rub the spice mixture across all surfaces of the ribs. 
 Place the ribs arch side up in a roasting pan without overlapping. 
 Allow marinating in the refrigerator about 1-hour. 
 Preheat oven to 350. Place the roasting pan on the lower rack of the oven, cooking ribs until tender, about 3 hours. 
 Remove ribs form oven and allow to rest 15 minutes before serving.
