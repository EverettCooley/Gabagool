Ingredients : 
 1 bunch of  coriander leaves 
 1 bunch of  mint  leaves 
 2  green chillies 
 A little seedless  tamarind 
 1 tsp  salt 
 4 tbsp  water 
 Directions : 
 Wash and soak  tamarind  in  water  for 1/2 hour. 
 Clean, pick and wash the coriander and  mint . 
 Separate pulp from the  tamarind . Grind coriander,  mint  and green  chilli  into a fine paste. 
 Add the  tamarind  pulp and  salt . 
 Blend well and serve.
