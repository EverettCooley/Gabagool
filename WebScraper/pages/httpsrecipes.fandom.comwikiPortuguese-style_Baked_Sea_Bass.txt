 Ingredients : 
 3 lb  sea bass  fillets, 1" thick (pollock or halibut may also be used) 
 1 tsp  salt 
 2 tbsp  flour 
 2 medium sliced  onions 
 ¼ cup  olive oil 
 ⅓ cup  white wine vinegar 
 3 cloves  garlic , mashed or minced 
 1 tsp  prepared yellow mustard 
 2 tbsp dried  parsley 
 1 tbsp fresh  lemon juice 
 ¼ cup dry  white wine 
 ¼ tsp  ground coriander 
 Directions : 
 Sprinkle fish with salt; lightly dust with flour. 
 Arrange fish in shallow 8x12" baking dish. 
 Saute onions in olive oil in skillet until limp; place over fish. 
 Combine wine vinegar, garlic, oregano, mustard, parsley, coriander and lemon juice; mix well and pour over fish. 
 Pour wine around fish; bake, uncovered, in 350°F oven for about 45 minutes.
