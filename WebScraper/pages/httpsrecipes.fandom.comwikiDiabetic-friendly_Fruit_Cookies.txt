Description : 
 Batter needs to sit overnight.
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes For Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  flour 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 1 tsp  cinnamon  (optional) 
 1 cup  water 
 1 cup  dates , chopped 
 ½ cup  apples ; peeled and chopped 
 ¾ cup  raisins 
 ½ cup (1 stick)  margarine 
 1 cup  quick oats 
 2  eggs , beaten (or  egg beaters ) 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 1 cup  pecans , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Sift flour and soda, set aside. 
 Cook water, dates, apples and raisins; bring to a boil. 
 Simmer 3 minutes. 
 Remove from heat and add the margarine and stir. 
 Cool mixture and then add eggs, oatmeal and the dry ingredients; add the vanilla and nuts. 
 Cover and refrigerate overnight. 
 Drop on cookie sheets 2 inches apart. 
 Bake in 350°F oven for about 24 minutes. 
 Store in the refrigerator in air tight container.
