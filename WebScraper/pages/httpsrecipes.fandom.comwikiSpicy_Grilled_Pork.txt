Desription : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Prep: 10 min | Cook: 10 min 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tbsp  paprika 
 1 tsp  seasoned salt 
 1 tsp ground  sage , rubbed 
 ½ tsp  cayenne pepper 
 ½ tsp  pepper 
 1 clove  garlic , cut in half 
 4  pork rib chops , about 6 ounces each 
 Directions : 
 Prepare grill or broiler. 
 Combine first 5 ingredients in a small bowl. 
 Rub cut side of garlic over both sides of pork chops. 
 Rub spice mixture into both sides of pork. 
 Grill or broil pork 6 – 7 minutes per side, or until done.
