 Description : 
Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group       Texas Cheese Dip     
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb. block  Velveeta  cheese  or  (1 Â¾ cup cheese, Cheddar, Parmesan, Asiago, Colby, or Monterey Jack) 
 1 can Campbell's  cheddar cheese soup   or  (3 cups cheese sauce) 
 1 can Rotel  tomatoes ,  chiles , and  peppers 
 1 small jar  salsa 
 1 pkg. Jimmy Dean hot  sausage  or other sausage brand, optional 
 Directions : 
 Cut each block of  Velveeta  cheese into small pieces and melt in the microwave; add  cheddar  cheese soup (or cheese sauce), Rotel, salsa, and  sausage . 
 Stir and mix well. 
 Use Tostitos 100%  white corn  restaurant style (Frito Lay) tortilla chips or any side dish or appetizer to dip. Serve plain. 
 Notes : 
 You can only use the chopped tomatoes, chopped red peppers, and crushed black pepper as a new substitute for Rotel to mix up the cheese sauce with salsa and real cheese.
