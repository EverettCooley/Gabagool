Ingredients : 
 one 6-ounce can gluten-free  tomato paste 
 ¼ pound  mozzarella  cheese 
 ¼ teaspoon  garlic powder 
 ⅛ pound cooked  hamburger 
 ¼ teaspoon  oregano 
 quarter toasted pieces of bread 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 400 °F. 
 Cut  cheese  and meat into tiny cubes. 
 Combine with  tomato  paste and seasonings. 
 Spread a teaspoonful on each square of bread. 
 Bake for three to five minutes.
