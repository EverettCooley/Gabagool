 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 serving pieces of fresh * 4 ripe  cooking bananas 
 2 spring  onions 
 1  green pepper  (optional) 
 12 matchbox-sized pieces of  pumpkin 
 2 cups of  coconut cream 
 Directions : 
 1. Put the banana pieces in a pot or baking dish.
 2. Peel the  bananas  and cut the lengthwise. Arrange on top of the board .
 3. Wash and chop the spring  onions  and  green pepper .
Sprinkle on the top of the  bananas 
 4. Wash the  pumpkin , cut into slices and add to the  bananas .
 5. Add  coconut cream .
 6. Cook for about 30 minutes or until the  bananas  are soft.
 7. Serve hot.
