Makes 8 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups diced cooked roast  Beef 
 2 cups sliced  celery 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 3/4 cup chopped green  bell pepper 
 3/4 cup finely chopped  Onion 
 1/3 cup chopped pimientos 
 1 cup  Mayonnaise 
 1/3 cup  milk 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 1 1/2 teaspoons  salt 
 1/2 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1/8 teaspoon hot pepper sauce 
 2/3 cup  rice  cereal crumbs 
 Directions : 
 Combine  Beef ,  celery ,  rice ,  green pepper ,  Onion  and pimientos. 
 Blend  Mayonnaise ,  milk ,  lemon juice ,  salt ,  pepper  and hot sauce. 
 Stir into  Beef  mixture. 
 Turn mixture into buttered 2-quart casserole; top with cereal crumbs. 
 Bake at 425 degrees 30 minutes.
