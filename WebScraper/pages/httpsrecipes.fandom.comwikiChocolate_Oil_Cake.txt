My mom's recipe. Very moist cake!
 Mix together:
3 c flour
2 c sugar
2/3 c cocoa
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
2 tsp baking powder
 Make 3 holes in the mixture.
In the first hole add 2 tsp vinegar
In the second hole add 2 tsp vanilla
In the third hole add 1 c vegetable oil
Pour over top 2 c of cold water.
Mix thoroughly
For Layer Cake or 9X13
 350 until toothpick comes out clean. Sorry don't have a time written 
down on my copy.
