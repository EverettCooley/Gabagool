Description : 
 This is great if you stir in some  crabmeat  (real or  fake ) and cook or a couple minutes before adding the noodles. I also like to add a healthy dose of  garlic . 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp  butter 
 4 tsp  flour 
 1½ cups  skim milk 
 ¼ cup  Parmesan cheese 
 some fresh  pepper  and  nutmeg 
 fettuccine noodles 
 Additional Notes : 
 Melt  butter  in saucepan. 
 Stir  flour  into melted  butter  until smooth. 
 Stir  milk  in and cook down a bit until thickened. 
 Add  Parmesan cheese  and stir in until smooth. 
 Add some fresh  pepper  and  nutmeg . 
 Fold in the noodles.
