Ingredients : 
 4 pieces  tenderloin steak  rolls, approx 1½ lbs 
 Sauce : 
 1 ripped  star fruit , seeded and sliced 
 3 cloves  garlic , chopped 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 1 tablespoon  light brown sugar 
 1 tablespoon  soy sauce 
 1 teaspoon  sea salt 
 2  hot chili pepper  or to your taste (option) 
 ¼ teaspoon  black pepper 
 1 cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Place  steak  rolls in container. 
 Set it aside. 
 Put remaining ingredients in a blender and blend well. 
 Pour sauce over steaks, cover container and refrigerate it overnight or at least 4 hours. 
 Cook  steak  over hot grill to your liking. 
 Serve hot with  rice  or  potato .
