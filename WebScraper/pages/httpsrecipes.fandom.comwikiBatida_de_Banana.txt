Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ oz  cachaça 
 1½ – 2 oz  pineapple juice 
 ¼ – ¾ oz  cream 
 1 dash  banana syrup 
 ½  banana 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients in a blender. 
 Strain into a large highball glass over crushed  ice , and serve.
