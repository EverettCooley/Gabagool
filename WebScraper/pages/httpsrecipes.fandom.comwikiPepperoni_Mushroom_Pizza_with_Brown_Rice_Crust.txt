Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 2½ cups cooked  brown rice 
 ¾ cup (3 ounces) shredded  Cheddar  or  American cheese 
 2  eggs , beaten 
 ½ cup  tomato sauce 
 ¾ teaspoon  Italian seasoning 
 ¼ teaspoon  garlic powder 
 1 cup (4 ounces) shredded  mozzarella  cheese, divided 
 2 ounces  pepperoni , thinly sliced 
 1 x 4-ounce can sliced  mushrooms , drained 
 1 tablespoon dehydrated  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  Cheddar  cheese and  eggs . Press into greased 12-inch pizza pan or 10-inch pie pan. Bake at 400 °F for 7 to 10 minutes. 
 Combine  tomato sauce ,  Italian seasoning  and  garlic powder . Spread over  rice  crust. 
 Layer ½ cup  mozzarella  cheese, pepperoni and  mushrooms . Top with remaining ½ cup  mozzarella  cheese and with  parsley . 
 Bake at 400 °F for 8 to 10 minutes.
