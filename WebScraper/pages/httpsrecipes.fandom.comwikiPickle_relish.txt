 About pickle relish : 
 Sliced and diced  pickles  that are chopped into small bits, flavored with  vinegar ,  salt ,  sugar , and  spices  for a savory-sweet taste, and served as a sauce to complement a food. Relishes are typically prepared to be used as a condiment, similar to a  salsa .
