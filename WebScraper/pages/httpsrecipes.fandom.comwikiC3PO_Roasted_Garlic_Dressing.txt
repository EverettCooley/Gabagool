Description : 
 Always check the ingredients to make sure the product is vegan.
 
 Ingredients : 
 12 cloves  garlic 
 1 cup  olive oil 
 ½ cup  balsamic vinegar 
 ¼ cup  water 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ½ tbsp  pepper 
 1 tbsp  Dijon mustard 
 Directions : 
 Separate (but don't peel) the cloves of  garlic  and roast in an un-oiled pan for 15 minutes at 35°F0, turning occasionally. 
 Set aside and peel when cool. 
 In a blender or food processor, blend everything together until smooth.
