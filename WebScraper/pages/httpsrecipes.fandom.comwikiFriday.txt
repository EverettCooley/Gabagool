Desription : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ oz  white rum 
 ¾ oz  mango syrup 
 ¼  mango 
 ¼ oz  lime juice 
 Directions : 
 Prepare  mango  fruit with  rum  and syrup in a blender with crushed  ice . 
 Strain into a large highball glass, and fill with crushed  ice . 
 Squeeze in a  lime  wedge, and serve.
