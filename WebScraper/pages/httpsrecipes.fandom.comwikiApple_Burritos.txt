Ingredients : 
 8 flavored  tortillas  (about 6 inches)  [1] 
 1  apple , sliced paper-thin 
 cinnamon  to taste 
 sugar  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Mix the  apple  slices with the  cinnamon  and  sugar . 
 Spread the  apples  on the flavored tortillas. 
 Roll up each tortilla burrito-style. 
 Heat the burritos in a microwave oven. 
 Top the  apple  burritos with  ice cream  or  whipped cream .
