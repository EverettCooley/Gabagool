 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 60 - 100 Fresh Palm  nuts 
 2 Large Dried *1Â lb. meat 
 4 Large  tomatoes 
 6 Whole  okra 
 1 Cup Shrimps or 6 Crabs (optional) 
 1 Large  Onion 
 2  red peppers 
 salt  to taste 
 water 
 Boiled  eggs  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Wash the palmnuts, put in a saucepan of  water  to boil 
 Cut the meat into cubes and cook in a saucepan of salted  water  until tender 
 Chop  Onion  and  pepper , add to the cooked meat and simmer slowly 
 When  nuts  are soft, remove from the  water  and pound in a mortar to remove and crush the red outer layer. Remove and put in a large bowl, pour boiling  water  and stir to extract the  oil 
 Strain through a sieve and keep the liquid 
 Repeat 4 and 5 until all the  oil  has been extracted 
 Add the extracted liquid to the meat in the saucepan and cook slowly until most of the  water  has been extracted and all ingredients are soft 
 Peel and add whole soft boiled  eggs , shrimps or crabs as desired 
 Skim of the surplus  oil  and save for the future 
 Serve with foofoo,  rice  or boiled  yam
