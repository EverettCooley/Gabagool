 Description : 
 This spice-based cream is made with real horseradish and used as a dip or to mix up with other dishes. Whip up a red-hot treat with this homemade, spiced cream to make it look satisfying.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  whipping cream 
 ¼ cup prepared horseradish 
 1 ¾ tablespoon  vinegar , white only 
 1 ⅞ tablespoon  salt 
 1 ⅞ tablespoon pepper 
 Directions : 
 Whip the cream and add remaining ingredients. 
 Spoon the homemade, horeseradish-based cream into a fresh container before refrigerating. Serve with other dishes, such as roasted beef, or use as a dip or for mixing up the dishes.
