 Description : 
 Date and Source unknown. This is yummy. I make it on a very regular basis.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 small carton  vanilla   yogurt 
 1/4 cup frozen  orange juice  concentrate, thawed 
 1/8-cup  water 
 2 small  bananas  peeled 
 2 tablespoons  wheat germ 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Put all ingredients in blender. 
 Beat until smooth.
