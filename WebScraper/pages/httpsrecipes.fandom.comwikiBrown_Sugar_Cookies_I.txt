Ingredients : 
 2 cups  brown sugar 
 1 cup melted  shortening 
 3  eggs 
 ¼ cup  milk 
 1 tablespoon  vanilla 
 1 teaspoon  soda 
 flour  to mix stiff 
 
 
 Directions : 
 First mix ingredients in order given. 
 Add just enough  flour  to roll. 
 Cut into shapes as desired. 
 Sprinkle with  brown sugar , and bake in a moderate oven (380°F).
