 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2/3 cup-  Turkey , liver, hamburger 
 1/2 cup  rice 
 1/4 cup  Potato 
 1/4 tsp.garlic powder 
 1/2  carrot 
 1 cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Cut meat into tiny bite size pieces, slice the  Potato , and cut the  carrot . 
 Put all into a pot, add 1/2 cup  water , then  garlic powder . 
 Turn on stove to high, let bowl. 
 Cook the  rice . 
 While cooking add a little of the  water  every so often to the stew. 
 Add  rice  to pot, stir, keep at bowl for 2 more minutes. take from heat. 
 Strain juice into separate pot. Let food cool. 
 Once done, add a little juice.
