Description : 
 Contributed by Jenn B aka Mom2Sam and Tiny at  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups  whole wheat flour 
 ½ cup  dry milk powder 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 2 tbsp  active dry yeast  (2 pkgs) 
 ¼ cup  honey 
 1¼ cups warm  water  (110 to 115°F) 
 1½ cups  bran  or 2 cups  bran cereal 
 1  egg 
 3 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 Directions : 
 Stir together the flour, dry milk powder, and salt. 
 Combine the yeast honey , warm water, and bran in a large mixing bowl. 
 Let the yeast mixture stand 2 minutes. 
 Add the egg, oil, and about half of the flour mixture. 
 Beat until smooth. 
 Add the remaining flour mixture and stir to form a sticky dough. 
 Cover with a damp towel and let rise in a warm place until doubled in bulk, about 1 hour. 
 Stir down and pour into a lightly greased 9 by 5 inch loaf pan. 
 Bake in a 375°F oven for about 40 minutes. 
 Cool in the pan for 5 minutes. 
 Remove and cool thoroughly on a wire rack. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Food value per  1 / 18  of loaf: 
 
 128 calories 
 Exchange exchange values: 1 bread + 1 fat
