Ingredients : 
 2 pack  gelatine , unflavored 
 ½ cup cold  water 
 1 can  eggnog , chilled 
 1 cup  almonds , ground 
 1 can  mandarin oranges , drained 
 Cool Whip  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Sprinkle the two envelopes  gelatine  onto the  water  to soften. 
 Place over low heat, stirring until dissolved. 
 Add  gelatin  to the eggnog, add the  almonds . 
 Pour into an oiled 5-cup mold. 
 Refrigerate until set, about 3 – 4 hours. 
 Unmold and garnish with the  mandarin oranges  and Cool Whip.
