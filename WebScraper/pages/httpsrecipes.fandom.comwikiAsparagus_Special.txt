Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Pryor Estate in Grapevine, Texas in 1996.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 10 ounces  asparagus 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 1 cup creamy  cottage cheese 
 ½ cup  baking mix 
 ¼ cup  butter  melted 
 2  eggs 
 1  tomato  peeled and thinly sliced 
 ¼ cup  Parmesan cheese 
 Directions : 
 Lightly grease a square baking dish. 
 Spread asparagus on bottom of dish. 
 Mix sour cream, cottage cheese, baking mix, butter and eggs with hand mixer for 1 minute. 
 Pour mixture over asparagus then arrange tomatoes on top. 
 Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and bake at 350°F for 30 minutes.
