Description : 
 Contributed by Dorie  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pkg. (16 oz) frozen  hash browns 
 2 cups shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 1 can (10 oz)  cream of chicken  soup 
 1 cup  Miracle Whip 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 ⅛ tsp  pepper 
 2 cups crushed  corn flakes 
 2 tbsp  butter  melted 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 350°F. 
 Mix potatoes, cheese, soup, miracle whip, onion and pepper. 
 Spoon into 9x13 baking dish. 
 Sprinkle with combined crushed corn flakes and butter. 
 Bake 45 – 55 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
