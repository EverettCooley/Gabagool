Description : 
 Baked fish that doesn't taste like fish from Menu and Recipes for the week of 2/25/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lb fish ( tilapia ) 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 1 pkg. Hidden Valley dry  ranch dressing  mix 
 1 can  French fried onions 
 Directions : 
 Mix  sour cream ,  mayo  and ranch mix together. 
 Roll dried fish fillets in mixture then roll in  French fried onions . 
 Bake 20 – 25 minutes at 350°F. 
 The fish is done when it flakes easily.
