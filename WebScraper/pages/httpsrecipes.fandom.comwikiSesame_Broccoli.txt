 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 bunch  Broccoli  -- blanched 
 2 tablespoons  olive oil 
 1/4 cup  rice wine vinegar 
 1/4 cup soy sauce 
 2 tablespoons  sesame oil 
 4 tablespoons  sesame seeds 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Blanch  Broccoli , toss with  olive oil . Place on a baking sheet and bake at 450 for 5 minutes. 
 Toss with a dressing made with the remainder of the ingredients except seeds. 
 Toast seeds and sprinkle on salad. Serve warm or cold.
