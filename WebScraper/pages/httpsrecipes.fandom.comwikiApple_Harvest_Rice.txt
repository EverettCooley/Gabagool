 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup thinly sliced  carrots 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 1 1/4 cups  water 
 3/4 cup  apple juice 
 2 tablespoons  lemon juice 
 2 tablespoons  brown sugar 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 cup uncooked  rice 
 1/2 teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 1/2 cup  raisins 
 1/2 cup sliced  green onions 
 2 cups cored, sliced, unpeeled  Apples 
 1 tablespoon toasted  sesame seed 
 Directions : 
 Cook  carrots  in  butter  until tender crisp, about 5 minutes. 
 Add  water ,  apple juice ,  lemon juice ,  brown sugar ,  salt ,  rice ,  cinnamon  and  raisins . 
 Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer until  rice  is tender and liquid is absorbed, about 15 minutes. 
 Gently stir in  green onions  and  Apples ; heat thoroughly. Turn into serving dish. Top with  sesame seed .
