 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  cabbage , coarsely chopped 
 1 cup shredded  carrots 
 ¾ cup  sugar 
 ¾ cup  cider vinegar 
 1 teaspoon  garlic salt 
 1 teaspoon  celery seeds 
 Directions : 
 Mix  cabbage  and  carrot  well. 
 Thoroughly mix  sugar ,  vinegar ,  garlic salt  and  celery seed . 
 Pour over  cabbage . 
 Mix well. 
 Chill.
