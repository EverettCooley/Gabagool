 Description : 
 An appetizer with delicious taste and good look.
 
 Ingredients : 
 250g  Pasta 
 100g grated  Cheese 
 2 tbsp  ketchup 
 1 tbsp  butter 
 1 tbsp  tomato  paste 
 1  green pepper 
 1 pinch  salt 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Pasta  and put in baking tray when  Pasta  tender. 
 Stir in  butter  and add one tablespoon of  tomato  paste and chopped  green pepper . 
 Add pinch of  salt . 
 Grate  Cheese  on top and add  ketchup . 
 Bake in oven until top crispy.
