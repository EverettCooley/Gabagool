Alabama Cookies
 
 1 Cup  shortening 
 1 Cup  sugar 
 1 Cup  brown sugar  Firmly Packed 
 2  eggs 
 1 Teaspoon  vanilla 
 1 1/2 Cups  flour 
 1 Teaspoon  salt 
 2 Cups Regular  Oats 
 2 Cups Chopped  pecans 
 1 Cup  rice  Crispies 
 1 Cup Flaked  coconut 
 
Cream  shortening , Gradually Add Sugars, Beating Well at Medium SpeedOf Mixer. Add  eggs  And  vanilla , Beating Well. Combine  flour  And  salt ,Add to Creamed Mixture, Mixing Well. Stir in  Oats  And RemainingIngredients. Drop Dough by Heaping Teaspoons Onto Greased CookieSheets. Bake at 325? For 10 to 12 Minutes. Let Cool Slightly OnCookie Sheets, Remove to Wire Rack to Cool Completely. 6 Dozen Cookies.
 
Enjoy.
