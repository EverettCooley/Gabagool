Ingredients : 
 2 c. all-purpose  flour 
 1 tbsp.  baking powder 
 1/2 tsp.  salt 
 1 tbsp.  shortening 
 2/3 c. lukewarm  water 
 Fat for frying 
 Honey  or  sugar  and  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Stir together flour, baking powder and salt. 
 Cut in the shortening until mixture resembles cornmeal. 
 Gradually add the water, stirring with a fork (dough will be crumbly). 
 Turn onto floured surface; knead into a smooth ball. 
 Divide dough in half; let stand 10 minutes. 
 Roll each half into a 12 1/2 x 10 inch rectangle. 
 Cut into 2 1/2 inch squares (do not reroll or patch dough). 
 Fry a few at a time in deep hot fat (425 degrees) until golden. 
 Drain on paper toweling. 
 Serve with honey or roll in sugar-cinnamon mixture. 
 Notes : 
 Makes 40.
