Ingredients : 
 24  pappadums 
 ½ cup boiled  potatoes  , chopped 
 ½ cup  onions  , finely chopped 
 2 tbsp finely chopped raw  mango 
 2 tsp fresh  Garlic Chutney 
 8 tbsp  Khajur Imli Ki Chutney 
 4 tbsp  Green Chutney 
 1 tsp  chaat masala 
 ½ tbsp  lemon juice 
 salt  to taste 
 Garnish : 
 ½ cup  sev 
 2 tbsp chopped  coriander  (dhania) (dhania) 
 Directions : 
 Arrange the pappadums on a serving plate. 
 Top each pappadum with a little potato, onion and raw mango. 
 Top the pappadums with some fresh garlic chutney, khajur imli ki chutney and green chutney. 
 Sprinkle with chaat masala, lemon juice and salt on top. 
 Garnish with the sev and coriander. 
 Serve immediately.
