Ingredients : 
 1 centiliter  Kahlúa 
 1 centiliter  Baileys 
 1½ centiliters  Frangelico 
 4 centiliters hot  coffee 
 Cream  if desired 
 Directions : 
 Build into a suiting glass, with no ice. 
 Add cream on top, if desired. 
 Served directly.
