Description : 
 Always check the ingredients to make sure the product is vegan.
 
 Ingredients : 
 6  garlic  cloves, peeled 
 2 tbsp  olive oil 
 ½ tsp  crushed red pepper 
 2½ cups  broccoli 
 1 cup canned ( white kidney beans ), rinsed 
 2 tbsp  lemon juice 
 baguette  slices, toasted 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 Combine  garlic ,  oil  and red pepper in a small custard cup. 
 Cover tightly with foil. 
 Bake until  garlic  is tender, about 35 minutes. 
 Steam  broccoli  until tender, about 8 minutes. 
 Process with  beans ,  lemon juice  and  oil / garlic  mixture. 
 Spread on bread and enjoy.
