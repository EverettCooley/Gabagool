 Description : 
 curry  is a part of Burmese cuisine.  Ametha Net Aloo Hin  ( Beef  and  Potato   curry ) is one of the more popular meat dishes in Burmese culture.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 750 g  Beef 
 375 g  potatoes 
 2 large  onions 
 5 large cloves  garlic 
 2 teaspoons  ginger , chopped 
 1 teaspoon  ground turmeric 
 1 teaspoon  chili powder 
 8 tablespoons light  sesame oil 
 1/2 teaspoon ground  cumin 
 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander 
 1 1/2 teaspoons  salt 
 2 cups  water 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Cut  Beef  into large squares. Wash and peel  potatoes . Cut into quarters. Put  Onion ,  garlic ,  ginger ,  turmeric  and  chili powder  in a food processor. Process until you get a pasty mixture. In a wok or deep-sided frying pan, heat  oil . Add spicy paste and stir. Cook in low heat for 10 minutes. Add a little  water  if the mixture starts to burn or dry. The spicy paste is cooked when it is golden brown and has  oil  around the edges. Add  cumin  and coriander and stir. Add  Beef  and fry. Stir for 3â€“5 minutes. Add  salt  to taste, 2 cups  water  and  potatoes . Simmer slowly until  Beef  and  potatoes  are tender. Serves 4.
