 Ingredients : 
 1 shot De Kuyper  crème de menthe  (Green) 
 1 shot Stolichnaya  vodka 
 1 shot  white rum 
 Directions : 
 Almost fill a tumbler with crushed  ice . Pour each shot of De Kuyper  crème de menthe , Stolichnaya  vodka  and  white rum  into the tumbler. Stir and serve garnished with a sprig of  mint .
