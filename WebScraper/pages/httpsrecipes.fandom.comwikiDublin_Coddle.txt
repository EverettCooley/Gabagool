 Description : 
 Dublin Coddle  is a typical traditional Irish favorite. It's tasty and easy to make.
 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  Pork  sausages 
 1 lb  Bacon , sliced 
 4  potatoes , peeled and sliced 
 2  onions , sliced 
 2  garlic  cloves 
 2  carrots , sliced 
 fresh herbs, chopped 
 hard cider  or  apple cider 
 fresh chopped  parsley  for garnish 
 
 
 Directions : 
 In a pan, fry  Bacon  until crisp then transfer in a large cooking pot. Brown sausages in  bacon grease  or  vegetable oil  then transfer to pot. Fry briefly  onions  and whole  garlic  until soft. Transfer to pot. Add  potatoes  and  carrots . Place bunch of herbs in the middle of the mixture. Sprinkle  pepper  on top. Cover mixture with  Cider . Cook for 1 1/2 hours over moderate heat. Do not boil. Garnish with fresh chopped  parsley  before serving.
