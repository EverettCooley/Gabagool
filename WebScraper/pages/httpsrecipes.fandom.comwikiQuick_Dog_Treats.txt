 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup  corn  meal 
 6 Tbsp.  oil 
 2/3 cup  water  or meat broth 
 2 cups  whole wheat flour 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients well. Roll out to 1/4" thick. Cut into shapes with cutter. Baste with meat drippings. Bake at 350 degrees F for 35-40 minutes.
