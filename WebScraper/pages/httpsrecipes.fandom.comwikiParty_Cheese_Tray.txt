Party Cheese Tray
 Prep Time: 10 min
Total Time: 10 min
Makes: 12 servings
1 pkg. (10 oz.) CRACKER BARREL Vermont Sharp-White Cheddar Cheese 
12 pitted kalamata olives 
12 pitted green olives 
12 sprigs fresh rosemary (4 inch) 
60 WHEAT THINS Snack Crackers 
1 bunch each red and green grapes 
PLACE cheese in center of serving platter. Thread 1 each kalamata olive
and green olive onto each rosemary sprig. 
INSERT stem ends of sprigs into block of cheese, using a red frilled
toothpick to support each sprig. Cut cheese into slices. 
ARRANGE crackers and grapes around cheese. 
Kraft Kitchens Tips
Size-Wise
Trying to pace your eating at a party? Preview your choices and be
selective instead of taking some of everything.
Food Facts:&nbsp
For best flavor, let cheese stand at room temperature about 30 min.
before serving.
