Description : 
 Yield: 5 servings (166 calories per serving). 
 Ingredients : 
 3 medium baking  potatoes , peeled and sliced 
 ¼ cup (1 oz) shredded low-fat  Swiss  or  cheddar  cheese 
 ¼ cup nonfat  cream cheese  product 
 1 tbsp chopped fresh  chives 
 1 tbsp non-fat  margarine 
 1½ tsp  prepared  or  Dijon mustard 
 ¼ tsp  hot sauce 
 ⅛ tsp  salt 
 1  egg white , lightly beaten 
 1¾ cup coarsely  crushed corn flakes 
 vegetable  cooking spray 
 Directions : 
 Cook  potato  in boiling  water  for 20 minutes, or until tender; drain. 
 Transfer to bowl. 
 Beat  potato  at medium speed until smooth. 
 Add  cheese , and next 6 ingredients. 
 Beat well. 
 Divide mixture into 10 equal portions and shape into balls. 
 Dip in  egg white  and roll in cereal. 
 Place on a baking sheet coated with nonstick spray. 
 Bake at 400°F for 10 – 15 minutes or until crisp and golden.
