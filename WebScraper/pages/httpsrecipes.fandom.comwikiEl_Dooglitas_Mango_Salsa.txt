      El Dooglitas Mango Salsa     
 Prep Time:  25 mins
 Cook time:  n/a
 Serves:  6 – 8 servings
 
 Description : 
 A nice tropical salsa that pairs perfectly with chicken apple sausage, fish and most white meat.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2  mangoes , diced 
 1 cup diced  pineapple 
 1 cup diced  cherry tomatoes 
 1 cup chopped  cilantro 
 1 medium  red onion , finely diced 
 6 tbsp  lime juice  (or 2 freshly squeezed limes) 
 2 tbsp  red wine vinegar  (feel free to use flavored vinegar as well) 
 1 tbsp  honey 
 1  jalapeño pepper  (or more to taste) 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in large bowl 
 Season to taste
