 Description : 
 Lebanese Salad with Zesty Ingredients
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/4 cup garbanzo beans, cooked, drained 
 4 cups boiling  water 
 1 1/4 cups raw  bulgar   Wheat . 
 1/2 cups fresh  Parsley 
 3/4 cups minced fresh  mint 
 3/4 cups scallions 
 3 medium  tomatoes  (chopped) 
 1/2 cup  lemon  juice 
 1/4 olive  oil 
 1-2 tspn  salt 
 lettuce 
 Raw  grapes 
 cabbage  Leaves 
 Taco Shells (Optional) 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Have ready 1/4 cup garbanzo beans, cooked, drained (3/4 cup). Pour 4 cups boiling  water  over 1 1/4 cups  bulgar   Wheat  raw. Let stand covered about 2 hours until light and fluffy. To remove excess  water , shake in a strainer and squeeze with hands.
 Combine: The cooked beans, the  Parsley , the  mint , the scallions, the chopped  tomatoes , the  lemon  juice, the olive  oil  and  salt 
 Chill for at least 1 hour. Serve on raw  grape ,  lettuce ,  cabbage  leaves or tacos.
