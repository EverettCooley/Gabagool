 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 c.  butter  or  margarine 
 1/2 tsp.  salt 
 1/2 c.  brown sugar 
 1 c.  flour 
 1c.  brown sugar 
 1 tsp.  vanilla 
 2  eggs 
 1/2 tsp.  baking powder 
 2 Tbsp.  flour 
 1 1/2 c.  coconut 
 1 c. coarsely chopped  nuts 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix first four ingredients and spread in 8"x12"x2" pan. 
 Bake at 325° for 15 minutes. 
 Beat  eggs  well. Add remaining ingredients. 
 Spread over baked mixture. Bake at 325° for 25 minutes. 
 Cut into bars.
