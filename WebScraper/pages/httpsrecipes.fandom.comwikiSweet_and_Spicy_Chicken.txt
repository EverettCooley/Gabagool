 Description : 
 ketchup ,  tamarind  and fresh spice mix give this thick and rich sambal  curry  a tangy and sweet flavour!
 
       Sweet and Spicy Chicken     
 Ingredients : 
 Chicken  wings 
 tomato   ketchup 
 gelangal - crushed 
 lemon grass  - crushed 
 kaffir lime leaves 
 tamarind juice 
 Sugar 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in pan and fry paste, gelangal and  lemon grass  till fragrant, add  Chicken  wings,  Sugar ,  tamarind juice  and cook. When  Chicken  is done, add  lime  leaves,  tomato   ketchup , cook briefly and it's ready.
