 Ingredients : 
 7 large  potatoes , cubed 
 1  onion , chopped 
 1 tbsp low- salt  chicken base 
 1 tsp  garlic powder 
 1 tsp  onion powder 
 1 tsp Vegit, or low-salt seasoning 
 1 tsp  fruit sugar 
 2 cups  peas , frozen 
 2 tbsp  butter 
 Â½ tsp  Bragg's seasoning , or  sea salt 
 3 cups  water , boiled 
 Directions : 
 Fry the  onions  and  potatoes  in the  butter  until lightly browned. 
 Pour in the hot  water  and add the seasonings. 
 Simmer until the  potatoes  are tender. 
 Add the  Peas  during the last 3 minutes of cooking.
