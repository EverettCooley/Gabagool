 Description : 
 In Romanian: Salata de praz.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3-4  leeks , 
 salt , 
 1 tablespoon  oil , 
 1 tablespoon  vinegar 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Wash the  leeks  and cut into thin rounds. 
 salt  and add  oil  and  vinegar .
