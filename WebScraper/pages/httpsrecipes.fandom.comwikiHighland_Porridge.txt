 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups (750 ml)  water 
 1 1/3 (330 ml) cups large flake  Oats /  corn  flakes/ wheatflakes 
 1/2-cup (125 ml) dry  milk  powder 
 2 tablespoons (30 ml) Barbados  Sugar 
 1-tablespoon (15 ml)  butter 
 1/4 teaspoon (1 ml)  cinnamon 
 1/4-teaspoon (1 ml)  allspice 
 1/4-teaspoon (1 ml)  salt , to taste 
 Handful  raisins  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Bring salted  water  to boil in a large pot. 
 Stir in all the ingredients. 
 Reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
 Serve with  milk  or  cream  and a sprinkling of Barbados.
