Description : 
 This recipe taken from www.Portuguese-recipes.com
 
 Ingredients : 
 10 or so  potatoes  cut into slices 
 6  pork chops 
 1 lb of  linguiça 
 ½ tsp of  salt 
 ½ tsp  black pepper 
 ½ tsp of  onion salt 
 ½ tsp of  garlic salt 
 4 tbsp of chopped  peppers , hot 
 ½ cup of  ketchup 
 ½ cup of  water 
 Directions : 
 You will need a shallow pan (12x18x2). 
 Place the  potatoes  to cover the bottom of the pan. 
 Now sprinkle half of the  salt ,  black pepper ,  garlic salt ,  onion salt  and chopped  pepper  over the  potatoes . 
 Now lay out the  pork chops  on top of that. 
 Pour the  water  and ketchup evenly over the chops. 
 Sprinkle the rest of the spices over the chops. 
 Slice the linguiça thinly and put that on top. 
 Cover the pan with aluminum foil and bake at 350°F for 1 hour. 
 After the hour is up remove the foil and cook uncovered for 15 minutes to brown.
