 Description : 
 Home Cookin' Kentucky Recipes  from the office of Senator Mitch McConnell, public domain government resource—source of original recipe
 
 Serves : 8 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups fresh or frozen  black-eyed peas 
 ¼ pound of  bacon 
 2 small red pepper pods 
 2 cups uncooked regular  rice 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Cover peas with water. 
 Simmer peas, bacon and peppers in a covered pot over low heat for 1 to 1½ hours or until tender. 
 Add rice, cover and cook over low heat, stirring frequently until rice is cooked. 
 Add more water during cooking if necessary. Add salt to desired taste.
