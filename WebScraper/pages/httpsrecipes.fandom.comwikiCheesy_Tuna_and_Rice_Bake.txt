Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 11-ounce can condensed  Cheddar   Cheese  soup/sauce 
 1/2 cup  milk 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 10-ounce package frozen  Peas , thawed 
 1 6-1/2-ounce can  Tuna , drained and flaked 
 1 2.8-ounce can French fried  onions 
 Directions : 
 Blend soup and  milk  in large bowl. 
 Combine with  rice ,  Peas  and  Tuna . 
 Turn into greased 2-quart baking dish. 
 Top with  onions . 
 Bake at 350 degrees 20 to 25 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
