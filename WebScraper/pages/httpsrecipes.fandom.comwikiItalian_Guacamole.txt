Description : 
 Parmesan cheese ,  sun-dried tomatoes  and  pine nuts  turn this guacamole into a family favorite.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2  California avocados , about 1 pound 
 ½ Tbsp  white wine vinegar 
 1 Tbsp shredded fresh  basil  leaves or 1 teaspoon dried 
 3 Tbsp grated  Parmesan cheese 
 2 Tbsp chopped  sun-dried tomato  (packed in  oil ; drained) 
 2 Tbsp toasted  pine nuts 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Coarsely mash (do not puree)  avocados ; stir in  vinegar . 
 Fold in remaining ingredients. 
 Guacamole is best made as close to serving as possible. 
 For short-term storage, seal in an airtight container with a piece of plastic wrap against the surface of the guacamole.
