Description : 
 Sangria with red Burgundy
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: With Love from Darling's Kitchen 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ gallon  burgundy wine 
 1 cup  lemon juice 
 1 cup  orange juice 
 1  orange , thinly sliced 
 1  lemon , thinly sliced 
 3  pineapple  slices, cut into spears 
 1 quart  club soda 
 ¼ cup  sugar  or to taste 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients and stir to blend. 
 Add  ice  cubes and serve. 
 Makes about 1 gallon. 
 Note : 
 If you prepare this earlier in the day and store it in the refrigerator, then add the  club soda  at serving time.
