Ingredients : 
 ½ cup of  molasses 
 ½ cup of  sugar 
 4 ounces of  cheese 
 2 cups of  flour 
 1 teaspoon of  soda 
 2 teaspoons  ginger 
 ½ teaspoon of  salt 
 ¾ cup of  water 
 Directions : 
 First rub the  cheese  and  sugar  together. 
 Add the  molasses . 
 Mix, and sift the dry ingredients, and add them to the  cheese  mixture alternately with the  water . 
 Bake fifteen minutes in small buttered tins. 
 It may be baked in a large pan but must be broken when cooked, not cut.
