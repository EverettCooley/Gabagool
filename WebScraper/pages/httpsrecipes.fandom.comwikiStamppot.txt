Mashed potatoes with various vegetables such as carrot, kale or endive and served with a juicy and mild sausage, meat ball, pork chop or cooked beef.
 The various types of Stamppot are known by their ingredients, i.e. Andijvie stamppot (Endive mash) or Boerenkool stamppot (Kale mash).
