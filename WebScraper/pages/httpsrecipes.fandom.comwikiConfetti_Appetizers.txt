Description : 
 Go nuts! Have a party! And at your next special occasion where an appetizer is on the menu, maybe you'd like to give these a try! (I said, "Go nuts..." but there are no nuts in the recipe) :) 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup shredded  Monterey jack  cheese 
 ¼ cup  salad dressing  (like Miracle Whip) or  mayonnaise 
 2 tablespoons chopped  carrots 
 1 tablespoon chopped  green onions 
 1 tablespoon chopped  red peppers 
 6 miniature  bagels , split and toasted 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients, except mini- bagels . 
 Mix well. 
 Spread on mini- bagel  halves. 
 Broil until  Cheese  is melted. 
 Option: Use 24 party bread slices toasted instead of the mini  bagels .
