Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 milk chocolate  cake mix 
 Filling : 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 1 lb  coconut 
 1 cup  milk 
 23 large  marshmallows 
 Frosting : 
 2 cups  sugar 
 3 tbsp  cocoa 
 ½ cup  butter 
 ½ cup  milk 
 3 tbsp  Karo syrup 
 Directions : 
 Mix cake mix as directed. 
 Bake in sheet pan. 
 Cool. 
 Boil milk and sugar 1 minute and add marshmallows and coconut. 
 Spread filling on top of cake. 
 For frosting, boil sugar, cocoa, butter and milk 2 minutes. 
 Let cool and spread over filling.
