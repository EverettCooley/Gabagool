 Source: Mrs. Mike Hayes       Horseradish Dip     
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 2 tsp.  lemon juice 
 horseradish , grated, to taste 
 Directions : 
 Mix the following ingredients. 
 Serve with hot, fried mushrooms, potato or corn chips, or other side dishes, main dishes, or appetizers. Reserve the spice-based dip in the refrigerator but cover this with the aluminium foil. 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Servings:  5 tablespoons
