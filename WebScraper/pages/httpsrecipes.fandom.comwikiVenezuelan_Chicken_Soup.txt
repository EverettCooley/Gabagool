      Venezuelan Chicken Soup     
 Ingredients : 
 3 litres of  water 
 1  chicken 
 1 lb (454 g) of meat 
 2  ribs 
 2  onions 
 1  pepper 
 1  celery  and  leek 
 3 big  potatoes 
 1 yuca ( cassava ) 
 coriander 
 3  corn 
 Directions : 
 Put three litres of  water  in a pan and place it on fire. 
 When starting to boil, put a  chicken  cut into pieces, meat and 2 ribs. 
 Add 2  onions  (in pieces), 1  pepper ,  celery  and  leek . 
 Let boil for 1 hour at slow fire. 
 Then include  potatoes , yuca, coriander chopped in pieces,  corns  in pieces,  salt  and  pepper  to taste.
