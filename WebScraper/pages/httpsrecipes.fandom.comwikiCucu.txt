Description : 
 Prep Time: 3 minutes | Cook time: 1 to 3 minutes 
 Serves: 2 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups steamed  milk 
 ½ cup  mango juice 
 ¼ cup plain  yogurt 
 ¼ tsp  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Steam the milk for 1 minute. 
 Add the mango juice and cinnamon to the milk. 
 Then add the yogurt and you're done.
