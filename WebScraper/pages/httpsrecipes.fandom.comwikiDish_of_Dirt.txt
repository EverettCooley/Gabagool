Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 14 crushed  Oreos  (about 1 cup of crumbs) 
 1 pint chocolate  ice cream 
 ¼ cup chocolate-flavored syrup 
 10 gummy worms (for garnish) 
 ½ pint prepared  whipped cream  topping (for garnish) 
 Directions : 
 In each of four dessert dishes, place 2 tbsp of cookie crumbs. 
 Top each with ½ cup of ice cream, remaining 2 tbsp of cookie crumbs and 1 tbsp syrup. 
 Garnish with gummy worms and whipped topping.
