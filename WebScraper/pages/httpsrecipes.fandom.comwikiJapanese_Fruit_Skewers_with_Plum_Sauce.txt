Description 
 
 Fruit skewers make a great snack or a side dish to a meal that includes fish. The skewers also make a tasty dessert.
 
 Cook Time : 20 minutes 
 Serves : 4 
 Ingredients 
 8 x 6-inch-long wooden  skewers 
 2 large  bananas , peeled and cut into 1-inch slices 
 1 cup  watermelon  chunks 
 1 cup canned whole pitted  litchi  fruit, drained 
 1 cup fresh  strawberries , hulled 
 ½ cup  plum sauce   [1] 
 Directions 
 Soak eight 6-inch-long skewers in cold water for 30 minutes so they will not burn during cooking. 
 Preheat the broiler or grill. Drain wooden skewers. 
 Alternately thread banana chunks, watermelon chunks, litchis, and strawberries on each skewer. 
 Brush fruit with the plum sauce. 
 Place kebabs on rack in the grill or on a broiler pan. Cook 5 minutes, turning frequently. Serve hot and plain or with other desserts or side dishes. 
 Notes 
 
 ↑   Plum sauce can be found in the Asian foods section of your local grocery store. 
 
 
 Nutritional Information 
 Servings:  2 skewers per plate
