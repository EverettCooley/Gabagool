 Description : 
 Serves: 	perhaps 5 to 6 
 Prep Time: 	10 minutes 
 Occasion: 	Any 
 Effort: 	Easy 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  guava 
 2  apples 
 2  bananas 
 ½ cup  grapes  (can use  raisins ) 
 chopped  almond 
 1 cup boiled  pasta 
 Dressing : 
 6 tbsp  cream 
 2 tbsp  milk 
 1 chopped  green chilli 
 1 tbsp  coriander  chopped 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 pinch  salt 
 1 pinch  black pepper  powder 
 2 tbsp mayonnaise 
 Directions : 
 Mix all dressing ingredients in a bowl. 
 Cut fruits in any shape. 
 Boil and rinse  pasta . 
 Mix all and serve cold garnish with chopped  almonds .
