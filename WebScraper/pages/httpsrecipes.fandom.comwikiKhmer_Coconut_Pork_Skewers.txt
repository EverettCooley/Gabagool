 Description : 
 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 1 stalk  lemon grass , thinly sliced 
 1 large  Onion , chopped 
 5  garlic  Cloves, chopped 
 The grated Zest of 1  lime 
 1/2 teasp  ground turmeric 
 90ml/3fl.oz.  water 
 25g/2oz freshly  grated coconut  or unsweetened Desiccated  coconut 
 1 tbsp  Sugar 
 1/2 teasp  salt  400g/14oz Lean  Pork , cut into 2.5 cm/1-inch cubes 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Place the  lemon grass , shallots,  garlic ,  lime  zest,  turmeric  and  water  in a food processor and process to a paste. 
 Transfer the  lemon grass  paste to a mixing bowl together with the  coconut ,  Sugar  and  salt  and mix well. 
 Add the  Pork  and mix to coat well. 
 Cover with clingfilm and leave to marinate for at least an hour at room temperature. 
 Preheat the grill to hot. 
 Thread the  Pork  cubes onto skewers and cook for 15–20 minutes, turning frequently and basting with the marinade. 
 Serve immediately.Also very good on the barbecue.
