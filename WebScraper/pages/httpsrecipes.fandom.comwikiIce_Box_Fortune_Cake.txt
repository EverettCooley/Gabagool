Eat this cake quickly before your past, present and future start melting together!
 
 Incredients : 
 1 stick (1/2 cup)  butter  (softened) 
 1/2 cup  Brown sugar 
 5 cups of crushed Frosted Flakes 
 1 cup of  Coconut 
 1 cup of  pecans , chopped 
 1 container of  Neapolitan ice cream  (softened) 
 1 cup of  chocolate chips 
 1 cup of miniature  marshmallows 
 1 cup of chopped  maraschino cherries 
 Directions : 
 Mix the butter and sugar until well combined, then stir in the Frosted Flakes, coconut and pecans. 
 Put half of the mixture in a 9 x 13 inch cake pan. Spread the softened Neapolitan ice cream over it. 
 Put the other half of the mixutre on top and press down firmly. 
 Decorate the top with chocolate chips, a row of marshmallows, and a row of chopped cherries. 
 Keep in freezer until ready to serve.
