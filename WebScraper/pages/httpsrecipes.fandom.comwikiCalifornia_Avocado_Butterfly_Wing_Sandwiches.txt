 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 Tbsp mashed ripe  California avocado 
 1 tsp honey 
 1 tsp  orange juice 
 2 slices  Wheat  bread 
 1 Tbsp grated  Cheese 
 4 thin  carrot  sticks, 3-inches long 
 Directions : 
 In small bowl blend  avocado , honey and  orange juice . 
 Spread over one slice of bread. 
 Top with  Cheese  and second slice of bread. 
 Cut into 4 triangles. 
 Place 2 triangles on a plate, arranging to make a butterfly. 
 Place  carrot  sticks on each sandwich to make butterfly feelers.
