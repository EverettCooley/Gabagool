Description : 
 Cartofi cu brinza la cuptor.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs / 1 kg  potatoes 
 7 oz / 200 g  sheep cheese 
 3  eggs 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 4 oz / 100 g  butter  or  lard 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Wash the  potatoes  and boil in their jackets. 
 When they are done, peel and cut into medium thin slices. 
 In a well buttered dish, put one layer of  potatoes , then some sprinkled  cheese , then another layer of  potatoes  and then some more sprinkled  cheese . 
 Repeat this until all  potatoes  and all  cheese  have been layered. 
 Pour the beaten  eggs  mixed with  sour cream  and  salt  on top. 
 Dot with some  butter . 
 Bake for 25 â€“ 30 minutes. 
 Serve hot.
