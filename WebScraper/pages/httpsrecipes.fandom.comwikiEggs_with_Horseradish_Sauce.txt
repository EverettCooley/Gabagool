Ingredients : 
 6  hard-boiled eggs 
 1 tsp  horseradish , ground 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 ½ cup  sour cream 
 salt ,  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Cut  eggs  in halves and place with cut part on a platter. 
 Blend the rest of ingredients together and pour over the  eggs . 
 Leave for about 30 minutes before serving.
