 Description : 
 A really simple, quick, and versatile recipe for busy moms and kids.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  avocado 
 1 can of  Tuna  (drained) 
 Bread (if desired) 
 Crackers (if desired) 
 Tortilla chips (if desired) 
 Directions : 
 Mash the  avocado . 
 Stir the  avocado  and  Tuna  together.
 Use as a dip for chips, vegetables, or crackers
 Make a sandwich using the Avocado- Tuna  Mash like a spread
 Eat it plain
 Make hors'doerves by putting spread on crackers and garnishing with fresh  cilantro ,  parsley , etc.
