Description : 
 Sardine omelettes
 
 Ingredients : 
 400 g  sardines  in  oil 
 4  eggs 
 milk 
 2  onions , chopped 
 2  tomatoes 
 4 small slices white bread 
 4  olives 
 1 tbsp of  vegetable oil 
 Directions : 
 Beat the  eggs  lightly with salt,  milk ,  pepper . 
 Heat  oil  and saut√© the onions until translucent. 
 Cut the  tomatoes  in bits and add these to the onions. 
 Continue stirring for 3 minutes. 
 Add sardines including  oil  from the can and let everything simmer 3 minutes. 
 Add the egg mixture to the sardines. 
 Heat the omelette on moderate heat until the  eggs  set. 
 Divide the omelette amonst the small slices of bread, garnish with  olives  and serve immediately.
