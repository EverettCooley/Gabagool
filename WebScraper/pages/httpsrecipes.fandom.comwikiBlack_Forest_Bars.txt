 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 16 oz  sour cherries , pitted 
 8 oz no- Sugar   chocolate  cake mix 
 2 tbsp  Sugar  replacement 
 Directions : 
 Drain  cherries  very well. 
 Combine cake mix,  cherries  and  Sugar  replacement in mixing bowl. 
 Stir to blend thoroughtly. Spread batter in well-greased 9-inch pan. 
 Bake at 375 °F for 20–25 minutes. Cut into 1 x 1 ½ bars. 
 Food exhancge per serving: ¼ starch/bread exchange.
