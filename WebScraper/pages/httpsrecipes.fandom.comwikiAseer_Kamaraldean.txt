Description : 
 Apricot juice
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 packet  dried apricot paste  sheets 
 1 tablespoon  sugar 
 800 ml (1Â¼ pint)  water 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Tear the  dried apricot  sheets into small pieces. 
 Place in a large bowl and soak in boiling  water  for 24 hours. 
 Transfer the soaked  apricot  into a large saucepan. Add  sugar  and  lemon juice  and heat on medium heat for 5 minutes, stirring constantly. 
 Simmer on very low heat for 3 minutes stirring constantly. Pour into a bowl and leave to cool for 1 hour, then place in a fridge for 1 hour.
