 Ingredients : 
 ¼ lbs  margarine 
 ⅓ cup  cocoa 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 pack  vanilla   sugar 
 eggs 
 1½ cup  almonds , grated 
 Directions : 
 Melt  margarine  with  cocoa . 
 Stir in sugars, and  eggs . 
 Stir in  nuts . 
 Spread.
