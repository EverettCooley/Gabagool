Ingredients : 
 5 slices  bread 
 5 strips  mozzarella 
 5 strips  bacon 
 ½ cups  all-purpose flour 
 2  eggs 
 1 cups  bread crumbs 
 ¼ cups fresh  milk 
 200 ml  oil  (for deep frying) 
 Tartar sauce : 
 5 tbsp  mayonnaise 
 1  shallot  (chopped) 
 1  pickle  (chopped) 
 1 hard-boiled  egg 
 1 tbsp  salt  and  pepper 
 ½ tsp  parsley  (chopped) 
 3 tbsp chicken stock 
 Directions : 
 Mix  eggs , milk  then combine  bacon  and  cheese  for stuffing. 
 Roll the loaf and dip it into the  eggs  and  milk  then roll into the flour then return it again into the  eggs  and  milk . 
 Heat  oil  for deep frying then roll the loaves into the bred crumbs until completely covered with crumbs. 
 Drop carefully and one at a time the loaves in  oil . 
 Deep fry until golden brown then drain  oil  in tissue. 
 Serve it hot and perfect with tartar sauce. 
 Tartar sauce : 
 Just mix all ingredients then chill for 20 minutes then serve. 
 Happy eating!!!
