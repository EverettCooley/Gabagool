Description : 
 An ideal all purpose seasoning for cooking or for general table use.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: My old recipes 
 Makes 1 cup 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  salt 
 1 tsp  paprika 
 1 tsp  black pepper 
 1 tsp  white pepper 
 ¼ tsp  celery salt 
 ½ tsp  garlic salt 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients in small bowl. 
 Stir well with a fork to mix. 
 Transfer to glass jar with tight fitting lid, preferably one fitted with a shaker top; store in a cool dry place.
