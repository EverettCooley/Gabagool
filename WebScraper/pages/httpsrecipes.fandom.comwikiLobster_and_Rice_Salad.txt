 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 1/4 cup vinaigrette dressing 
 2 cups cooked lobster meat cubes 
 1 cup sliced fresh  Mushrooms 
 1/2 cup red pepper strips 
 1/3 cup sliced  ripe olives 
 1/4 cup sliced  green onions 
 1 1/2 teaspoons snipped fresh  basil  leaves 
 1 teaspoon snipped fresh dill 
 1 teaspoon snipped  chives 
 Fresh herbs (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Toss  rice  with dressing in large bowl; cool. Add lobster meat,  Mushrooms ,  pepper , ,  onions  and herbs; toss lightly. Garnish with additional fresh herbs if desired.
