Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ oz  Kahlua ®  coffee liqueur 
 ¼ oz  Amaretto   almond liqueur 
 ¼ oz  Frangelico ®  hazelnut liqueur 
 ¼ oz dark  crème de cacao 
 Directions : 
 Chill with ice. 
 Strain into shot glass.
