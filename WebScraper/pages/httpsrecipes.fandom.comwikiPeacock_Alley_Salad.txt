Peacock Alley Salad
 
 1/2 Cup Syrup From Canned Fruit 
 1 Pkg. Hollandaise Sauce Mix 
 1/3 Cup Mini Marshmallows 
 1 Cup Diced Drained  peaches 
 1/2 Cup Drained  pineapple  Tidbits 
 1/2 Cup Halved  Green Grapes 
 1/4 Cup  maraschino cherries , Drained 
 1/2 Cup Flaked  coconut 
 
Add Syrup to Sauce Mix And Stir Until Smooth. Cook,stirring Constantly Until Sauce Has Thickened. Cool. Fold in Marshmallows, Fruit (be Sure Fruit is Well Drained) And  coconut . Chill For 1 Hour. Serve on Crisp Romaine or  leaf lettuce . Serves 4Source: my Old Recipes
