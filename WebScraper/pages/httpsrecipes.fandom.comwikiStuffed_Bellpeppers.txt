Ingredients : 
 1 pound  ground beef 
 6 â€“ 8 large  bell peppers 
 1 can Rotel  tomatoes  (Italian) 
 1 large  onion  chopped 
 garlic  (to your liking) chopped 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Parmesan cheese 
 bread crumbs  (Italian flavored) 
 Directions : 
 Brown  ground beef , drain fat. 
 Smother chopped  onions ,  garlic , and  bell peppers  with drained  ground beef , until  peppers  are somewhat cooked. 
 Add Rotel  tomatoes ,  Parmesan cheese , and Italian  bread crumbs . 
 Pour all ingredients into large baking pan. 
 Top with more  Parmesan cheese , and bread crumbs. 
 Bake until bread crumbs are light golden brown.
