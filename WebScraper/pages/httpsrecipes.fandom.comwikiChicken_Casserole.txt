Ingredients : 
 8 oz  noodles , cooked 
 2 cups cooked  chicken , cubed 
 2 cups  chicken broth 
 1 cup  milk 
 1 can  mushrooms 
 2 tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  pepper 
 ½ cup  margarine 
 ⅓ cup  flour 
 ⅓ cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 Directions : 
 Melt  margarine , add  flour  and stir. 
 Gradually add  milk  and seasonings and  mushrooms . 
 Combine  chicken , cooked noodles, and prepared sauce. 
 Put in ungreased baking pan and top with  Parmesan cheese .
