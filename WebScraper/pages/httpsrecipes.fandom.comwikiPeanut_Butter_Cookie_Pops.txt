Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  butter  or  margarine 
 ½ cup  peanut butter 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 ½ cup  brown sugar 
 1  egg 
 ½ tsp  vanilla 
 1½ cups  all-purpose flour 
 ¾ tsp  baking soda 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 Wilton dark or light  cocoa 
 flavored candy melts 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 375°F thoroughly cream  butter ,  peanut butter ,  sugar ,  egg , and  vanilla . 
 Toss together dry ingredients; blend into creamed mixture. 
 Shape dough into ball. 
 Roll dough approximately ⅛ inch thick. 
 Dip cutters into  flour  before each use. 
 Bake 10 – 12 minutes. 
 Cool. 
 Dip in  chocolate  or make peanut butter chocolate sandwiches. 
 Makes 4 dozen place one or two wafers of candy melts on warm cookie. 
 Sandwich two cookies together on lollipop stick.
