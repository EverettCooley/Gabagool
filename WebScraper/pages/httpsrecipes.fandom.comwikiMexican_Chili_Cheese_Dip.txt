Mexican  chili   Cheese  Dip
 
 1 (8 Oz.) Pkg.  cream cheese 
 1 Can Hormel  chili  With no Beans (or Home-Made  chili ) 
 1 Sm.  yellow onion  (1/2 C.) 
 3 Green  chile peppers , Chopped Into Rings 
 1 Cup Shredded  Cheddar   Cheese 
 Chips
 
Mash And Break up  cream cheese  Into Bottom of GlassPie Dish With a Fork.
 
Top With  chili , Then a Layer of  onions  And  chili 
 peppers .
 
Sprinkle With Shredded  Cheese . Cover And Refrigerate.
 
When Ready to Serve, Microwave on High For 5 Minutes.Serve With Tortilla Chips, Salsa, And a Side Dish OfSour Cream Garnished With a Sprig of  cilantro  or  parsley .
 
Enjoy.
