Info 
 Cook Time : 
 Serves : 30
 
 Ingredients 
 1 46-ounce can chilled  pineapple juice 
 1½ cups  orange juice 
 2 quarts (1 liter)  club soda 
 1 quart pineapple or orange  sherbet 
 Directions 
 Mix pineapple juice, orange juice, and club soda together. 
 Drop spoonfuls of sherbet into punch, and stir until nearly melted. 
 Source 
 Pineapple Juice, Canned, Unsweetened  by the USDA, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
