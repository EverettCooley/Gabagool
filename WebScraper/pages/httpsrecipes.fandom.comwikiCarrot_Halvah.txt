 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 large  carrots 
 1 tbsp condensed  milk 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 tbsp  khoya 
 1 dozen  cashews 
 1 tbsp  currants 
 Directions : 
 Take the  carrot  along with half the of  Sugar , cook covered on med-low flame for 15 minutes. 
 Add the  condensed milk , khoya, and the rest of the  Sugar  and cook uncovered for 5–10 minutes. 
 Garnish with the cashews and  currants  and serve.
