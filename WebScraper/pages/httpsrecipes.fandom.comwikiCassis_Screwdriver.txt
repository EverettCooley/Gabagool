 Ingredients : 
 0.5 shot De Kuyper  crème de cassis 
 0.5 shot Stolichnaya  vodka 
 dash of De Kuyper  Grenadine 
 Top up  orange juice 
 garnish: Dash of  Grenadine  to float and some  currants  if desired. 
 Glass: Sling
 
 Directions : 
 Build the ingredients over some cubed  ice  in a hurricane glass in the following order: de Kuyper  crème de cassis , Stolichnaya  vodka ,  orange juice . 
 Stir the ingredients together and add a dash of  grenadine  to float on the top. 
 Garnish with  currants  if desired.
