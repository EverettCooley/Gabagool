Description : 
 A  rum  drink
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  light rum 
 1 oz  crème de banane 
 1½ oz  cream 
 1 dash  grenadine 
 crushed  ice 
 Directions : 
 Shake ingredients with crushed  ice  and strain into a glass. 
 Top with a slice of  banana  and sprinkle lightly with  nutmeg .
