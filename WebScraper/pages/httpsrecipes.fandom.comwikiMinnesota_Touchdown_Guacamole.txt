 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 ripe genuine  California avocados 
 3 Tbsp fresh  lemon juice 
 ½ Cup Diced  Onion 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 2 Tbsp Minced  cilantro 
 To taste red pepper sauce 
 Garnish Minnesota  Cheddar   Cheese 
 Directions : 
 1. Cut the avocados in half and remove the seeds. 
2. Scoop out the pulp and place in a bowl. 
3. Drizzle the pulp with  lemon juice  and mash. 
4. Combine with remaining ingredients, mix well and add red pepper sauce to taste. 
5. Sprinkle with  Cheese  and serve with chips.
