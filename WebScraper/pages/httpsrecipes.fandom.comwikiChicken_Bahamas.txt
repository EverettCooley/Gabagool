 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1	x	3-lb.  chicken 
 2	cup	cooked  rice  or  spaghetti 
 3	oz	 cashew   nuts 
 Hot  Pepper Sauce  to taste 
 salt 
 2	x	chopped  onions 
 Directions : 
 Boil a  Chicken  in the usual way. reserve stock. Cut  Chicken  into pieces and arrange on a bed of  rice , or  spaghetti . 
 Prepare a sauce by pounding  cashew   nuts  with some hot pepper sauce  and a pinch of  salt . 
 Fry two  onions  and add to nut mixture, with some of the  chicken stock . Pour over the  Chicken .
