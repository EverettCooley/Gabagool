 Description : 
 Makes: 12 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tub (8 oz.)  Cool Whip  Whipped Topping, thawed 
 1 pt. (2 cups)  vanilla   ice   cream , softened 
 1 pkg. (4-serving size) JELL-O Brand  peach  Flavor  gelatin  (unprepared) 
 4 cups pound cake cubes 
 1/4 cup  raspberry preserves 
 12 small  peach  slices 
 12  raspberries 
 Directions : 
 STIR whipped topping,  ice   cream  and dry  gelatin  in large bowl until well blended. 
 Stir in cake cubes. Spoon into 8-inch square pan. 
 FREEZE 3 hours or until firm. 
 DRIZZLE with  raspberry preserves . Cut into squares. 
 Top each square with 1  peach  slice and 1  raspberry . Store leftover dessert in freezer.
