 Description : 
 Harost balls have been a part of Moroccan culture for centuries. They are traditionally a part of the passover feast in Morocco.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups pitted  dates 
 1/2 cup golden  raisins 
 1/2 cup dark  raisins 
 1/2 cup  walnuts 
 1-2 tablespoons  Zinfandel  wine 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Put the  dates ,  raisins  and  walnuts  in a food blender for a minute or so, until the contents are finely chopped and begin to stick together
 Add the wine and stir until the mixture is of a sticky texture.
 Use a spoon to divide the mixture into small sections and put onto a sheet of wax paper.
 Dampen your hands so the mixture doesn't stick and roll each mound into a ball.
 Put into the refrigerator on the sheet for at least 3 hours before serving.
 This recipe should serve 4.
