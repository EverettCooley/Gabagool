      Beet Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 2  beets  boiled and peeled 
 2 pickled  cucumbers 
 2  garlic  cloves 
 mayonnaise 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 # grate beets and  cucumbers , chop  garlic  cloves finely. 
 # combine all ingredients and stir in  mayonnaise .
