 Prep Time: 15-20 mins 
 Cook time: 5 mins 
 Serves: 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 capsicums 
 3-4 potatoes 
 lemon -1 
 green chilli 2 or as required 
 salt (rock salt or sea salt) (req quantity) 
 coriander leaf 
 tomato sauce 
 Directions : 
 Boil the potatoes for 15-20 mins 
 peel the potatoes and smash it 
 grind green chilly and sea salt 
 mix it along with smashed potatoes 
 add some coriander leaves and drops of lemon to it and mix 
 cut the capsicum in the top so that it looks like a cup 
 now remove the inners of capsicum 
 now fill it with the smashed potato 
 close the cup-like-capsicum with the top that was cut before 
 oven it for 5 mins 
 now it is ready to serve
