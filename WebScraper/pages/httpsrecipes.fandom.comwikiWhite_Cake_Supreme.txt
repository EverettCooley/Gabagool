 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 1/4 cup  cake flour 
 1 tbsp  baking powder 
 1 1/4 cup  Sugar 
 3  egg  white 
 1/2 cup  oil 
 3/4 cup nonfat  milk 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 375 °F. Sift  flour ,  baking powder  and ¾ cup  Sugar . Beat  egg  whites until soft peaks form. Add remaining  Sugar  and beat until stiff peaks form. Make a well in dry ingredients. Add  oil , ½ cup  milk  and flavoring. Beat 150 strokes (or 1 minutes. at medium speed. ) Add remaining  milk . Beat again 150 strokes. Mix 1/3 of  egg  mixture with batter. Then fold batter into remaining  egg  whites. Turn into 2 lightly oiled (or greased with  margarine ) 8-inch layer pans. Bake about 30 minutes. Cool 10 minutes and remove from pans. Note: Instead of using  vanilla  you may substitute 2 teaspoons of grated  lemon  rind.
