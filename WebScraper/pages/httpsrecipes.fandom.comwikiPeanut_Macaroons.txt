Ingredients : 
 1 cup  peanuts , unsalted 
 1  egg white 
 pinch of  salt 
 ¾ cup  superfine sugar 
 ½ teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 wax paper 
 Directions : 
 Brown the peanuts under the broiler. 
 Crush them. 
 Beat the egg white a pinch of salt. 
 Add the sugar and beat again. 
 Then add the peanuts. 
 Put the wax paper on a baking sheet. 
 Grease it a little. 
 Put small mounds of mixture on the paper. 
 Bake at low heat 250°F until the macaroons are golden.
