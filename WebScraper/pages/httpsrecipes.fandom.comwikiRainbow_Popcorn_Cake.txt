Description : 
 Now here’s a snack that’s a sure hit with the young ones!
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 quarts  popped corn 
 ½ lb  gumdrops 
 ½ lb  peanuts 
 1 lb white  marshmallows 
 ½ cup melted  margarine 
 ½ cup  corn oil 
 at least 3 different  food coloring 
 Directions : 
 In a saucepan, combine  marshmallow ,  margarine  and  corn oil . 
 Melt over moderate-high heat. 
 Divide mixture in 3 equal parts and put 2 drops each of the  food coloring . 
 Pour the melted  marshmallow  mixtures over the  popcorn  and gumdrops and mix well. 
 Transfer the  popcorn  and  marshmallow  mix in a cake pan and pat down. 
 Refrigerate for an hour before serving.
