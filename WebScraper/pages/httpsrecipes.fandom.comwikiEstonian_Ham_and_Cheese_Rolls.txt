Description : 
 These rolls are great cocktail fare. Buy good-quality imported  Ham  for them and have it sliced thick rather than thin.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 recipe  yeast  Dough (check out in the snacks category) 
 24 slices  Ham , each 3 inches square 
 1/4 cup freshly grated  Parmesan cheese 
 4 tablespoons  unsalted butter  (melted) 
 2 large  egg  yolks (beat with 1 teaspoon  milk ) 
 Directions : 
 1. On a floured surface, roll out the dough to an 18 x 12 inch rectangle.
 2. Now with the help of sharp knife, cut twenty-four 3-inch squares of this dough bread.
 3. Place one  Ham  slice on each square of dough. Place about 1/2 teaspoon of the Parmesan in the middle of each  Ham  square and drizzle with about 1/2 teaspoon melted  butter .
 4. And then starting at one corner, roll each square into a neat roll.
5. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.  butter  two large baking sheets.
 6. Arrange the rolls, on the baking sheets, spacing well apart, and brush with the  egg  mixture.
 7. Keep them to set for about 20 minutes.
 8. Bake until the rolls are golden brown, about 30 to 40 minutes.
 Serving:
 Serve hot with sauce. Serving will be enough for 8 to 12 people.
