Ingredients : 
 8 cups lightly packed  spinach  leaves 
 1 pint basket California  strawberries , stemmed and halved 
 4 cups assorted  melon  chunks 
 1½ cups  orange  and  grapefruit  segments 
 orange-mint ,  strawberry-ginger  or  balsamic-pepper dressing 
 Orange-mint dressing : 
 1 container (8 ounces) plain non-fat  yogurt 
 3 tablespoons thawed  orange juice  concentrate 
 1 teaspoon  sugar 
 3 tablespoons chopped  mint 
 Strawberry-ginger dressing : 
 1½ cups California  strawberries 
 1½ – 2 tablespoons  honey 
 1½ tablespoons  lime juice 
 2 teaspoon grated fresh  ginger 
 a pinch of  salt 
 Balsamic-pepper dressing : 
 1 container (8 ounces) non-fat  strawberry   yogurt 
 1 tablespoon  balsamic vinegar 
 1 teaspoon  honey 
 ½ – 1 teaspoon coarsely ground  black pepper . 
 Directions : 
 Line platter or four individual plates with  spinach . 
 Top with fruits, dividing equally. 
 Serve with your choice of dressing on the side. 
 Makes 4 servings. 
 Orange-mint dressing : 
 In bowl whisk   yogurt , thawed  orange juice  concentrate and  sugar . 
 Mix in chopped  mint . 
 Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate up to 2 days. 
 Makes about 1 cup. 
 Strawberry-ginger dressing : 
 In blender or food processor puree  strawberries ; transfer to bowl. 
 Mix in  honey ,  lime juice , grated  ginger  and  salt . 
 Use immediately, or cover and refrigerate up to 2 days. 
 Makes about 1 cup. 
 Balsamic-pepper dressing : 
 In bowl whisk  strawberry   yogurt ,  balsamic vinegar ,  honey  and  black pepper . 
 Serve immediately or cover-and refrigerate up to 2 days. 
 Makes about 1 cup.
