chocolate  Glazes
 
Method #1)
 Melt 3 Squares  semisweet chocolate  in Top of double boiler Over Hot But Not Boiling  water . When Melted, Add 3 T. of  corn syrup  And 3 T. Cold Tap  water . Mix. Let Cool For a Few Minutes Before Using.
 
Method #2)
 Melt 4 Squares  semisweet chocolate  in Top of double boiler. as  chocolate  Melts, Add 1/4 Cup  milk , Mix Well, Then Remove From Heat. Spread Over Cake at Once.
 
Method #3)
 Melt 3 Squares  semisweet chocolate  in Top of double boiler. Remove From Heat. Add 1/2 T.  vanilla , 1 T.  margarine , 1/3 Cup  powdered sugar . Stir. Add 1 T. Cold  water . Stir And Use on Cake Immediately. Glaze #3 Will Turn Into Candy After a Few Hours in Refrigerator.Source: my Old Recipes
