      Afterburner     
 Ingredients : 
 1 shot De Kuyper  triple sec 
 2 shots Stolichnaya  Vodka 
 0.5 shot  grapefruit juice 
 garnish: Slice of  strawberry  if desired 
 glass: highball 
 Directions : 
 Shake the ingredients together in a cocktail shaker with  ice . 
 Strain into a cocktail glass. 
 Add slice of  strawberry  to side of cocktail glass if desired.
