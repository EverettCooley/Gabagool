Description : 
 From  Sweet Inspirations  by Ron & Patti Lynch
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 tbsp  cornstarch 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 2 cups  skim milk 
 ½ cup Fudge Sweet 
 2 tbsp  butter 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 Fruit Sweet 
 Directions : 
 In a medium saucepan, combine  cornstarch ,  salt , and ½ cup cold  milk . 
 Blend until smooth. Add 1½ cups  milk , and fudge sweet. 
 Stir constantly over medium heat until mixture thickens and begins to boil. 
 Remove from heat and add  butter  and  vanilla . Pour into serving dishes. 
 For a richer pudding: when you remove from heat, pour a small amount of pudding over 2 beaten  egg yolks , which have been placed in a small bowl. 
 Return entire mixture to saucepan, cook 2 minutes, then remove from heat and add  butter  and  vanilla . 
 Notes : 
 Fruit Sweet is made of  peach  and  pear  juices and  pineapple . Syrup It and Fudge Sweet are available from Wax Orchards R4 - 320 Vashon Island, WA 98070
