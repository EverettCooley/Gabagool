 Description : 
 Domodah
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  vegetable oil 
 1 Lb stew meat (optional) 
 1  Onion , chopped 
 1 clove  garlic , minced (optional) 
 1-2  tomatoes , or  tomato  paste 
 2-3 cups  beef broth  or stock 
 1  Squash  or  Eggplant , cubed (optional) 
 1 hot  chili pepper , cleaned and chopped (optional) 
 juice of 1  lemon  (optional) 
 1-2  Peanut   butter  (natural and unsweetened) 
 1-2 cups  water 
 salt ,  black pepper ,  cayenne pepper  or red pepper (to taste) 
 Directions : 
 Heat the  oil  in a large pot. Add the meat and slightly brown it with  onions .
 Add all remaining ingredients, except  Peanut   butter . Add enough  water  to cover. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for twenty minutes or so.
 Stir in the  Peanut   butter . Continue to simmer on a low heat for ten minutes, stirring often. Adjust seasoning. Simmer till the Domodah is done and  Peanut   butter  leaves a little  oil . 
Serve with boiled  rice .
