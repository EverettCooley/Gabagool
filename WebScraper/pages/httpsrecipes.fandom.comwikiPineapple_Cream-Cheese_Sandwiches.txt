Description : 
 This quick appetizer features cream cheese and pineapple.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lb.  cream cheese , softened 
 1 cup crushed  pineapple , well-drained 
 2 loaves rye bread 
 Directions : 
 Whip the cream cheese with a hand-mixer until light and creamy. 
 Add pineapple and mix well. 
 Decrust the bread and cut into desired shapes. 
 Spread cheese mixture on bread generously 
 Notes : 
 Makes approx. 100 small open-face sandwiches 
 Instead of cutting and decrusting the bread, just spread the pineapple and cream-cheese mixture generously on slices.
