Ingredients : 
 2  chickens 
 3  onions  cut into slides 
 3 crushed cloves of  garlic 
 1 coffee spoonful of  ginger 
 A little bit of  saffron 
 1 soup spoonful of  lemon juice 
 Salt 
 1 glass of  water 
 2  preserved lemons  cut into slides 
 10 preserved  green olives 
 Preparation : 
 Clean and drain the chicken, salt it, leave it for 2 hours, then clean and drain it again. 
 Cook it in a pressure cooker with onions, garlic, saffron, ginger, lemon juice, salt and water, mix and cover, then cook on a middle fire, till the chicken is almost cooked, add lemon slides, olives and cook for 5 mn on a little fire. 
 Present the chicken in complete, watered with sauce and decorated with olives and preserved lemon slides.
