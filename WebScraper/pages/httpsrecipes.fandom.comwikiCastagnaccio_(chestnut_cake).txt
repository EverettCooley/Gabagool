Description : 
 4-6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 500 g [1 lb]  chestnut flour 
 150 g [5 oz]  pine nuts 
 100 g [3 1 oz]  Sultanas 
 10 g [1/2 oz]  butter 
 2 tbsp  oil 
 2 tbsp  Sugar 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Mix the  flour  with a little warm  water  and the  oil ; 
 Add the  Sultanas ,  pine nuts , 1 pinch  salt  and the  Sugar  and combine well. 
 Roll out to 1 cm [1/2 in] thick and place on a baking tray [cookie sheet] greased with the  butter ; 
 Bake at 180oC [350oF / Gas 4] for about 30 minutes.
