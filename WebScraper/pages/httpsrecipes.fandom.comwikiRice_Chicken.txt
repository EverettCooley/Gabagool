 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 one natural  Chicken  or  Chicken  legs 
 one small  Onion 
 garlic 
 laos powder of fresh laos 
 ginger  powder of  fresh ginger 
 good soy sauce (not conimex brand) 
 Chicken  cubes 
 MSG 
 cooking  oil 
 Directions : 
 It is most tasty if the  Chicken  is cut up in small pieces and fried in  oil . 
 Afterwards add the  Onion  the  garlic  and other ingredients. Last but not least the soy sauce and the  Chicken  cube. 
 Add a little bit of  water  and let the  Chicken  cook well done. 
 Serve with naturally  rice , and some vegetables as in chinese  string beans  or amsoi (like  bok choy ). 
 Dont forget to serve with a  pepper .
