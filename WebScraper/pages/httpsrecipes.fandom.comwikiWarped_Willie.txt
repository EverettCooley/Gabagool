      Warped Willie     
 Ingredients : 
 ½ shot  Disaronno amaretto 
 ½ shot  Stolichnaya vodka 
 ½ shot  lime cordial 
 Directions : 
 Layer ingredients into glass in this order: Rose's  lime cordial ,  amaretto , Stolichnaya  vodka .
