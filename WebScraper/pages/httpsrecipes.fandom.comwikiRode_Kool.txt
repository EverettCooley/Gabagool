Description : 
 A nice way to prepare  red cabbage . Can be eaten like this, or in combination with other vegetables.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 head of  red cabbage 
 2 – 3 tbsp  brown sugar 
 ¼  margarine 
 ½ medium  onion  (chopped) 
 2  apples  (cubed and peeled) 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ⅛ tsp  pepper 
 ¼ cup  raisins 
 
 Directions : 
 Cook the  cabbage  with the  margarine  for about 5 minutes. 
 Add the  apples ,  sugarand   onion . 
 Cook for another 3 minutes. 
 Add the rest of the ingredients, cover the pot and lower heat. 
 Cook for about 20 – 25 minutes, and remember to stir occasionally.
