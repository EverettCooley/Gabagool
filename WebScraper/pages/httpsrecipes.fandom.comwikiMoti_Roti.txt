Sour-dough like soft flatbread 
 Made from all-purpose white flour, yogurt, and oil 
 Lightly kneaded and left to ferment and rise 
 Dough is then formed into balls flattened and cooked on a griddle 
 Serve with curries or as a breakfastbread smeared with fruit chutney
