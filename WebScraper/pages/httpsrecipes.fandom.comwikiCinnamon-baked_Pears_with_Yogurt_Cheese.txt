Description : 
 When you think dessert start with something that is nutritious like this high in fiber delicacy, then add spices for flavor and top with something like low fat  yogurt cheese . 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 stick  cinnamon 
 ¼ cup  dates , chopped 
 ¼ cup  raisins 
 ½ tsp  cinnamon  or 1  cinnamon  stick 
 1 tsp  lemon zest 
 2 tbsp  prune  purée 
 2 tbsp  brown sugar 
 4 large  pears 
 ½ cup  water 
 Directions : 
 In a mixing bowl, mash first 7 ingredients until you have a paste. 
 Keeping the stem intact, peel/core  pears , digging out a cavity 1 in. across and 2½ in. deep from the center of the large end. 
 Press equal parts mixture into  pears . 
 Place  pears  standing up in a small baking dish, add  water  and  cinnamon  stick to pan, cover with foil. 
 In a 325°F preheated oven, bake  pears  40 minutes or until tender. 
 Transfer  pears  to individual plates and pour remaining syrup from baking pan on top. 
 Place a dollop of  yogurt cheese  next to each  pear  and serve.
