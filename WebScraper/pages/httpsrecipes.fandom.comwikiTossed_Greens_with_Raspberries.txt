Ingredients : 
 Dressing 
 1/4 c.  olive oil 
 2 tbsp.  raspberry vinegar 
 3 tbsp.  honey 
 1 tbsp. dairy  sour cream 
 1/2 tsp.  prepared mustard 
 Salad 
 1/2 c. fresh or frozen  raspberries 
 3 c.  Boston  or  Bibb lettuce 
 1/4 c. chopped  walnuts . 
 Directions : 
 Combine all dressing ingredients in a small bottle or jar and shake well 
 Wash and dry lettuce; place in large bowl 
 Top with raspberries and walnuts 
 Before serving add dressing and toss to mix.
