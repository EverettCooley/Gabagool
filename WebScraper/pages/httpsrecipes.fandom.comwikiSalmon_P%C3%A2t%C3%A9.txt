Ingredients : 
 220 g can red  salmon 
 125 g packaged  cream cheese 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 salt ,  pepper 
 2 tablespoons  lime  or  lemon juice 
 125 g  butter 
 Directions : 
 Combine drained salmon, softened cream cheese, mayonnaise, salt, pepper and lemon juice in blender or food processor. 
 Add melted butter, blend until smooth. Spoon salmon mixture into serving dish, refrigerate until set.
