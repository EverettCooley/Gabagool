Ingredients : 
 1 vegan garlic wrap 
 1-2  garlic  cloves diced 
 handful of  alfalfa sprouts 
 bunch of thinly sliced  cucumber 
 hummous  or low-fat vegan ranch dressing if you want 
 Directions : 
 Stuff the wrap with the ingredients. 
 The crunchiness of the veggies mixes well with the saucy ingredient.
