Description : 
 A Cholesterol Diet recipe
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cup  flour 
 ¼ cup  espresso  powder 
 3 tbsp  cocoa powder 
 1 tsp  black pepper 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  baking powder 
 ½ lbs  butter  @ 70°F 
 1 cup  dark brown sugar 
 4 large  eggs 
 3 tbsp grated fresh  ginger  or fine chopped candied 
 Directions : 
 Combine dry ingredients well. 
 In separate bowl, cream  butter  and  sugar , add  eggs  one at a time beating well after eachadd  molasses  and mix well,add dry ingredients and fold in until well mixed. 
 Our into greased and floured pans between ½ to ⅔rds full. 
 Bake 350°F about 20 minutes (but do not overcook) until a straw comes out clean.
