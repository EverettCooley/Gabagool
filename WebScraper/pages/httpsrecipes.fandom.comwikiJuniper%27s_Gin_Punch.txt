Description : 
 Contributed by Stephen Ceideburg 
 Ingredients : 
 1  orange 
 2  limes 
 1  water 
 3 cups  gin , chilled 
 2 cups  apricot brandy , chilled 
 2 liter  club soda , chilled 
 12 oz frozen  orange juice 
 -concentrate 
 Directions : 
 Slice the  orange  and  limes  into thin round slices and line a 4 cup round mold with the slices. 
 Carefully fill the mold with cold  water  and place in the freezer. 
 Freeze until solid. 
 Combine  gin ,  brandy ,  club soda ,  orange juice  concentrate and stir to blend in a punch bowl. 
 Unmold the  ice  ring and carefully float it in the punch bowl, fruited side up.
