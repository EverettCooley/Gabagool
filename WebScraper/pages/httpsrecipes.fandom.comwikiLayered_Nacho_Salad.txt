      Layered Nacho Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 2 large ripe  avocados  peeled, pitted 
 2 cup purchased thick  salsa  (mild or medium-hot) or more 
 1 head  red-leaf lettuce  in bite-sized pieces 
 3 cup shredded cooked  chicken 
 3 cup broken  tortilla chips 
 1 can  black beans  (15 ounce) rinsed, drained 
 4  green onions  chopped 
 1 small  green bell pepper  chopped 
 1 cup grated sharp  cheddar  Cheese packed 
 Directions : 
 Place avocados in medium bowl. 
 Add â…“ cup salsa and mash to chunky guacamole consistency. 
 Season with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Layer half of  lettuce  of  chicken , chips,  black beans ,  green onions ,  bell pepper  and  cheese  in large bowl. 
 Top with half of guacamole and half of remaining salsa. 
 Repeat layering with remaining ingredients. 
 Use additional salsa to cover top if necessary.
