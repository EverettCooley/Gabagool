 Description : 
 This is sooo good...you will want to have lots of mashed  potatoes  to go with it.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lb. round  Steak 
 1 T.  flour 
 1 1/2 c.  Beef  bouillon 
 3/4 c.  ketchup 
 3/4 c.  water 
 3 T.  tarragon vinegar 
 1 T. Worchestershire sauce 
 1/4 tsp.  oregano 
 1/4 tsp.  marjoram 
 1/8 tsp.  thyme 
 dash  garlic powder 
 Directions : 
 Cut round  Steak  into serving pieces. 
 Dip meat into  flour ; brown in hot skillet with small amount of  oil . 
 Remove from pan; set aside. 
 Add remaining ingredients to pan, bring to boil, scraping pan and stirring. 
 Return  Steak  to pan (or place in deep casserole and pour juices over). 
 Bake at 350F, 50–60 minutes, or until tender. 
 Check on meat from time to time - add a little  water  in small amounts while baking to prevent meat from burning or drying out, and prevent sauce from becoming too thick. 
 Serves 4 amply.
