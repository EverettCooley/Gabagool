Ingredients : 
 1 (8 oz.) box brownie mix 
 1  egg 
 1 tbsp.  water 
 1 (14 oz.) bag Kraft  caramels 
 1 (5 oz.) can  evaporated milk 
 2 (8 oz.) pkgs.  cream cheese 
 1/2 c.  sugar 
 1 tsp.  vanilla extract 
 2 eggs 
 Directions : 
 Mix and bake the first three ingredients in a greased 9 inch square pan at 350 for 25 minutes. 
 Melt caramels and evaporated milk over low heat. Stir until smooth. 
 Pour 2/3rds of the caramel mixture over top of the baked brownies. 
 Beat cream cheese, sugar and vanilla until well blended. 
 Add eggs one at a time. Blend well after each. 
 Pour over caramel mixture in brownie pan. 
 Bake 40 minutes at 350 F. 
 Loosen cake from rim. 
 Chill. 
 Reheat caramel remainder and drizzle over chilled brownies. 
 Drizzle chocolate sauce or hot fudge sauce over top. 
 
 
 Notes : 
 If you can't find an 8 ounce box of brownie mix, then use smallest box you can find and follow box directions. DO NOT OVERBAKE. 
 This recipe is worth the extra effort and will serve 12 to 16 people.
