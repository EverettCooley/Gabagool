Sauce of Three or Four Cheeses
 
 2 Oz.  mozzarella , Shredded 
 3 T. Grated Parmesan 
 1/2 Cup Ricotta 
 1/4 Cup Crumbled  Gorgonzola  (optional) 
 1/2 Cup  heavy cream 
 2 T.  butter 
 1/4 to 1/2 T. Coarse or Ground  black pepper 
 
In Serving Bowl, Mix All Ingredients Except  butter  And  pepper . Blend Well. Add 1 Lb. Hot Cooked Drained  Pasta ,  butter  And  pepper . Toss. Good on Cooked  fettuccine  of Any Color.Source: my Old Recipes
