Description : 
 Contributed by Lilly at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 About 2 dozen 3½" cookies 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup  butter 
 1⅓ cup firmly packed  light brown sugar 
 2  eggs 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 1 tsp  soda 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 1 cup M&M's  ®   plain chocolate  candies 
 ½ cup chopped  nuts 
 Directions : 
 Beat together  butter  and  sugar  until light and fluffy; blend in  eggs  and  vanilla . 
 Gradually add combined  flour , soda and  salt ; mix well. 
 Stir in candies and nuts. 
 Drop dough by heaping tablespoonfuls onto greased cookie sheet , about 3" apart. 
 Press 3 – 4 additional candies into each cookie, if desired. 
 Bake at 350°F for 10 – 12 minutes or until lightly browned. 
 Cool on cookie sheet about 3 minutes; remove cookies to wire rack to cool thoroughly.
