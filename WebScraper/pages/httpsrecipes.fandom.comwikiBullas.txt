      Bullas     
 Ingredients : 
 2¾ cups  brown sugar 
 ½ tablespoon  salt 
 2 cups + little bit of  water 
 1 cup  margarine 
 2 tablespoon  ginger 
 2 tablespoon  vanilla 
 7½ cups  flour 
 3 tablespoon  baking powder 
 ½ tablespoon  baking soda 
 1 teaspoon  nutmeg 
 flour  for rolling out  bulla 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve  sugar  and  salt  in  water , add  ginger ,  margarine  and  vanilla . 
 Sift together all dry ingredients and combine with liquid- gradually. 
 Dough must be clammy and heavy. 
 Place on floured board and dust with  flour  until dough is manageable. 
 Roll out to a thickness of .75 cm and cut into slices. 
 Bake in a 375°F oven until done 20 – 25 minutes.
