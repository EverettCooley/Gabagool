 Ingredients : 
 3  tomatoes 
 Juice of 2  lemons 
 2 green  chile peppers 
 salt 
 1/2 bundle fresh  green coriander 
 Directions : 
 1) Grill  tomatoes  on low heat; remove from the heat; peel, puree and place in a deep dish. 
 2) Wash the coriander and  chili pepper ; chop them both, then grind, using a pestle and mortar. 
 3) Add this mixture to the  tomatoes , plus the  salt  and  lemon juice . Mix and serve. Serves 4-6 persons.
