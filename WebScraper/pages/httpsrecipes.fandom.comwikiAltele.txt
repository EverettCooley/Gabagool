Ingredients : 
 500 g of diced  beef 
 1 tablespoon  plain flour 
 2 rashers of  bacon 
 1 packet of French  onion soup mix 
 1 x 440 g can of  pineapple  pieces or half a fresh  pineapple 
 1 pinch of ground  ginger  or a small amount of fresh  ginger  - the latter is better 
 Directions : 
 Coat the  beef  with the  plain flour . 
 Brown the  beef  and the  bacon  in a pan. 
 Put the  beef  and  bacon  into a  casserole  dish. 
 Add the rest of the ingredients, including the balance of the  flour . 
 Add  water  and stir to give a smooth consistency. 
 Cook in a moderate oven (180 – 200°C) for 1 hour or until the meat is tender. 
 Garnish with  parsley  and serve with boiled  rice  or mashed  potato .
