 Ingredients : 
 5 c Sifted  flour 
 2 c  Sugar 
 1 c  butter 
 1 c  shortening 
 6  egg   yolks 
 2 tb  sour cream 
 1 1/2 ts  vanilla 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Make as for pie crust; roll out and cut with cookie cutter or cookie
press. Bake 12 to 15 minutes at 350 F.
