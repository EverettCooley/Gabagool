Creamy  Broccoli  Dip
 
 2 Pkg. Processed  garlic   Cheese  (8 oz Each) 
 1 Can Condensed Cream of  Mushroom  Soup (10.5 Oz) 
 1 Stick  butter , Cut Into Bits (4 Oz) 
 1 Small  Onion , Sliced 
 2 T.  worcestershire sauce 
 Dash of hot pepper sauce 
 
 1/2 T.  salt 
 1/4 T. Freshly Ground  pepper 
 1 Pkg Frozen Chopped  Broccoli , Thawed And Drained (10 Oz) 
 
In a Medium Pan, Combine  garlic   Cheese ,  Mushroom  Soup,  butter ,  Onion , Worcestershire, Hot Sauce,  salt  And  pepper . Cook Over Medium Heat, Stirring Until  Cheese  is Melted And Mixture is Well Blended, About 3 to 5 Minutes. Stir  Broccoli  Into  Cheese  Mixture. Transfer to a Fondue Pot For Serving. Serves 8
