 Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 oz  dark rum 
 2 oz  cranberry juice 
 2 oz  pineapple juice 
 1 1/2 oz  orange   curacao 
 1 splash Sour Mix 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients except  orange   curacao  with  ice . Shake well. 
 Pour into a glass and float  curacao  on top. 
 Top with a slice of  pineapple  and a  cherry .
