Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3  onions , sliced 
 6 to 7 large  potatoes , cubed 
 1 can  tomatoes , chopped 
 1 to 1½ Tbsp  salt 
 1 tsp  crushed red pepper 
 ¼ tsp  ginger 
 2  bay leaves 
 1 tsp pepper 
 ¼ tsp minced  garlic 
 ½ tsp  oregano 
 1/4 tsp  ground turmeric 
 ½ small bottle  ketchup 
 1½ to 2 lbs  catfish  fillet 
 3 slices of  bacon 
 5  eggs 
 Directions : 
 Fry  bacon  until crisp and crumble. 
 Put  bacon , ½  bacon drippings , and first 12 ingredients in a large stock pot. 
 Add twice the amount of  water  it takes to cover  potatoes  and  onions . 
 Cook until  onions  are tender. 
 Add fish and bring back to a boil. 
 Crack and add 1  egg  at a time and cook until  eggs  are done.
