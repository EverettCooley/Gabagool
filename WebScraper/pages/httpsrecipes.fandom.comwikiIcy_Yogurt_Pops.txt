 Description : 
 Servings: 6 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup low-fat plain  yogurt 
 ¾ cup frozen  fruit juice    [1] 
 ¾ cup  low-fat  or  skim milk 
 Directions : 
 In a 4 cup glass measure, combine  yogurt , fruit juice concentrate and  milk . 
 Pour into pop molds or use small paper cups and insert a wooden stick in the centre of each. 
 Freeze until firm, about 2 – 3 hours. 
 To serve, peel off paper cups. 
 Notes : 
 
 ↑   You can use pureed fresh fruit or frozen fruits instead juice.
