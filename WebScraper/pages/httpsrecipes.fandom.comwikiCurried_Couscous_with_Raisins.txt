 Description : 
 Always check the ingredients to make sure the product is vegan.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 Cup(s)  vegetable stock 
 1 1/2 Cup(s)  couscous 
 1 minced  Onion 
 1/3 Cup(s)  Raisins 
 1 Tablespoon(s)  margarine 
 1 Teaspoon(s)  curry 
 1/2 Teaspoon(s)  cumin 
 1 Dash(s)  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 In saucepan, bring  vegetable stock  to boil; add  couscous  and remove from heat. Cover and let stand for 5 minutes or until  grains  are tender and liquid is absorbed. 
 Meanwhile, in saucepan or microwave dish cook  Onion  in 1 tbsp  water  over medium heat or microwave at High power for 1.5 minutes or until tender. 
 Fluff  couscous  with fork. Add  Onion ,  Raisins ,  margarine ,  curry  powder,  cumin , and  pepper  to taste; stir with fork.
