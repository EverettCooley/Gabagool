Description : 
 Morogo with  potatoes 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2-3 lbs  spinach  or  Swiss chard  (or similar greens), cleaned and torn into pieces 
 2-3 lbs  potatoes , cleaned, peeled, cut into quarters and soaked in cold  water 
 water  or  vegetable broth 
 Directions : 
 Place the greens in the bottom of a large pot. 
 Put the  potatoes  over the greens. 
 Add 1-2 cups of broth sufficient to fill the pot to a depth of one inch. 
 Bring to a boil, reduce heat and cover. 
 Let cook for 30â€“40 minutes or until  potatoes  are steamed tender. 
 Add  water  as necessary to prevent the pot from becoming completely dry. 
 When the  potatoes  and greens are fully cooked, mash them together with a  potato  masher. 
 Serve with scrambled  eggs .
