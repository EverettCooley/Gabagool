Creamy  orange  Fluff
 
Creamy, Fruity And Refreshing, This Salad is Simple to Make Ahead And Cuts Nicely Into Squares For Ease in Serving.
 
 6 Oz. Pkg.  orange   gelatin 
 2 1/2 C. Boiling  water 
 2 Cans (11 Oz. Each)  mandarin oranges , Drained 
 8 Oz. Can Crushed  pineapple , Undrained 
 6 Oz. Can Frozen  orange juice  Concentrate, Thawed 
 
 Topping:
 
 8 Oz. Pkg.  cream cheese , Softened 
 1 C. Cold  milk 
 3.4. Oz. Pkg. Instant  vanilla  Pudding Mix 
 
 in a Bowl, Dissolve  gelatin  in Boiling  water . Stir in  oranges ,  pineapple  With Juice And  orange juice  Concentrate.
 
Coat a 13x9x2-Inch Dish With Nonstick  cooking spray . Add  gelatin  Mixture. Refrigerate Until Firm.
 
in Mixing Bowl, Beat  cream cheese  Until Light. Gradually Add  milk  And Pudding Mix; Beat Until Smooth. Spread Over  orange  Layer. Chill Until Firm.
 
Yields: 12 to 16 Servings
