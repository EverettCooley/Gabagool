Recipe from United  Soybean  Board
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 cups all purpose  flour 
 1/2 cup yellow  cornmeal 
 1/4 cup  soy flour 
 1/4 cup  Sugar 
 1 tbsp  baking powder 
 1/2 tsp  salt 
 1 cup lite  soymilk 
 2  eggs , beaten 
 1/4 cup  soybean oil 
 Directions : 
 Mix  flour ,  cornmeal ,  soy flour ,  Sugar ,  baking powder  and  salt . 
 Combine  soymilk ,  eggs  and  oil ; add to dry ingredients and mix only enough to moisten. 
 Fill oiled muffins tins. 
 Bake at 400 °F 20 minutes.
