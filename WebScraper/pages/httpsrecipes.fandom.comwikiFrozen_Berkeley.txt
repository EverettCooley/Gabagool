 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 oz  light rum 
 1/2 oz  brandy 
 1 tbsp passion-fruit syrup 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients with 1/2 cup crushed  ice  in an electric blender at a low speed for a short length of time. 
 Pour into a champagne flute and serve.
