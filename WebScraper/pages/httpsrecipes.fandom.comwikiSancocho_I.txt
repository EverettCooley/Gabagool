Description : 
 Panamanian chicken soup
 
 Serves 5 to 6 people. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 stewing  hen  (2½ lbs), cut in serving pieces 
 1 medium  onion , chopped 
 4 tablespoons  cilantro , chopped 
 1 teaspoon dried  oregano  leaves 
 2 tablespoons  green pepper , chopped 
 2 lbs  ñame , peeled and chunked (potatoes can be substituted) 
 2" section of  corn  on the cob for each serving (optional) 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Put  chicken  pieces into a stock pot with 2 quarts of  water . 
 Add  onion ,  cilantro ,  oregano  and  green pepper . 
 Cook for 1 hour. 
 Add  salt  to tasted. 
 Add ñame, cook until ñame is tender. 
 Add  corn  last 15 minutes of cooking. 
 Served with steamed  rice  on the side.
