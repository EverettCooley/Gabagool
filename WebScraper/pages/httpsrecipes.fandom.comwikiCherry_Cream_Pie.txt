cherry  Cream Pie
 
 1 Package (8 Ounces)  cream cheese , Softened 
 1 Can (14 Ounces)  sweetened condensed milk 
 1 /3 Cup  lemon juice 
 1 Teaspoon  vanilla 
 1 (9 Inch) Graham Cracker Piecrust 
 1 Can (21 Ounces)  cherry  Pie Filling 
 .. in Large Bowl of Electric Mixer, Beat Softened  cream cheese  Until Fluffy. Gradually Add  sweetened condensed milk  While Continuing to Beat. Add  lemon juice  And  vanilla  And Beat Until Well Blended. Spread in Piecrust And Chill 2 to 3 Hours Before Covering With  cherry  Pie Filling.
 
Source: Melba's Swap Shop, Sunday Oklahoman Newspaper
