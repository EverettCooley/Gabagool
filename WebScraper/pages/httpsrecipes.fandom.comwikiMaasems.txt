Description : 
 "Fatima's little fingers"
 
 Servings : 6 
 Ingredients : 
 2 medium  hard-boiled eggs  — chopped 
 1 large cooked  chicken breast  — chopped 
 ⅓ cup  parsley  — chopped 
 2 medium  eggs  — beaten 
 2 tablespoons  Parmesan cheese  — grated 
 salt  and  pepper  — to taste 
 9 or 18 wrappers  [1] 
 olive oil  — for frying 
 lemon  wedges — for garnish 
 Directions : 
 In mixing bowl, combine both types of  eggs ,  chicken ,  parsley ,  cheese ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Refrigerate 30 minutes. 
 Wrap mixture in wrappers, sealing edges with  water . 
 Fry in 1 inch  oil  until crisp, turning as needed. 
 Serve garnished with  lemon  wedges. 
 Notes : 
 
 ↑   Wrappers can be: 9 medium egg /  spring roll wrappers  or 3  phyllo  sheets
