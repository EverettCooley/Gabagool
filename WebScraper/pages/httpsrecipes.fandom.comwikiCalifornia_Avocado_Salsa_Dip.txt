 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 Avocados, diced in chunks 
 2  tomatoes , diceds in chunks 
 1 Jar Pace Picante Sauce, Reg size 
 1 Pound grated  Cheddar   Cheese 
 Some fresh  cilantro , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl then refrigerate and serve
 Eat whenever you want!
 You can chill all ingredients before mixing. 
 Or add  garlic  if you like
