Description : 
 Source:  Vegetarian Recipes 
 
 Ingredients : 
 wheat  tortillas 
 shredded  tofu 
 tamari 
 curry powder ,  turmeric ,  paprika ,  cayenne ,  parsley ,  cumin 
 fresh crushed  garlic 
 1 minced  onion 
 spaghetti squash 
 enchilada sauce 
 nutritional yeast  flakes 
 Directions : 
 Fry the garlic and onion with a little of olive oil. 
 Add the tofu, tamari and spices. 
 Fry everything until it is browned a little. 
 Add the spaghetti squash. 
 Continue frying until everything is incorporated to the tofu mix. 
 Heat the tortillas for several seconds. 
 Remove them from flame. 
 Place a tbsp of the mix inside the tortilla and spread it in a line. 
 Sprinkle the nutritional yeast. 
 Add the sauce or pour the sauce on top. 
 Roll it into a burrito, and serve.
