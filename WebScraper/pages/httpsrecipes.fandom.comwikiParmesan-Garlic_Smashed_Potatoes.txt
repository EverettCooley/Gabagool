Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ lbs all-purpose  potatoes , scrubbed and quartered 
 3 tbsp  water 
 3 large cloves  garlic , crushed (or as many as you can stand) 
 ⅓ cup  low-fat milk 
 3 tbsp grated  Parmesan cheese 
 ¾ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 In 2-qt. microwave-safe container, combine potatoes, water and garlic. 
 Cover and microwave on high 12 to 14 minutes or until potatoes are tender. 
 Mash potatoes until smooth. 
 Add milk, cheese and salt, beating until light and fluffy (if mixture becomes too thick, add more milk 1 tbsp at a time).
