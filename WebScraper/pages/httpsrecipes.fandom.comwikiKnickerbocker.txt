Description 
 Knickerbocker  from Jerry Thomas' Bartending Guide or How to Mix Drinks
 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 take one-half a  lime  or small  lemon 
 3 tea-spoonfuls of  raspberry syrup 
 1 wine-glass of  Santa Cruz rum 
 3 dashes of  curaçao 
 ice 
 Directions 
 Squeeze out the juice of the lime or lemon into the glass, add the rind and the other materials. 
 Fill the glass one-third full of fine ice, shake up well, and strain into a cocktail glass. 
 If not sufficiently sweet, add a little more syrup.
