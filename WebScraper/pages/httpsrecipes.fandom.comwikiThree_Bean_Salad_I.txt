Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Gibson Estate in Quinlan, Texas in 1981.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can French-style  green beans  drained 
 1 can  wax beans  drained 
 1 can  red kidney beans  drained 
 ½ cup chopped  green onion 
 ¼ cup snipped  parsley 
 8 ounces bottled  Italian salad dressing 
 1 tablespoon  granulated sugar 
 2 cloves  garlic  crushed 
 Directions : 
 Combine beans, onion and parsley in large bowl. 
 Mix dressing, sugar and garlic and pour over bean mixture and toss well. 
 Cover and refrigerate at least 3 hours, stirring occasionally.
