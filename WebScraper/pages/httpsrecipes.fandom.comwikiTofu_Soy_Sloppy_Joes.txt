Ingredients : 
 1 tbsp  olive oil 
 2 medium cloves  garlic , minced 
 1 medium  green bell pepper , chopped 
 1 small  onion , chopped 
 1 lb  extra-firm tofu , well-drained and crumbled or diced 
 2 tbsp  soy sauce 
 1½ cups  tomato sauce 
 1 tbsp  chili powder 
 ½ tsp ground  cumin 
 burger buns ,  rolls  or  bagels , split and toasted 
 Directions : 
 In large skillet, heat  oil  over medium heat. 
 Add  garlic ,  bell pepper  and  onion  and cook, stirring often, until softened, about 5 minutes. 
 Add  tofu  and soy sauce and cook, stirring often, until  tofu  begins to brown, about 5 minutes. 
 Stir in  tomato  sauce,  chili powder  and  cumin  until well mixed. 
 Cook, stirring, until heated through and flavors are well blended, 1 to 2 minutes. 
 Spoon over toasted buns and serve hot.
