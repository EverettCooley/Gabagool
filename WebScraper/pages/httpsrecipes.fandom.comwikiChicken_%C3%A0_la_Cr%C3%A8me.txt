Description : 
 Makes 8 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups sliced  green onions 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 2 tablespoons  flour 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 1 x 4-ounce can sliced  mushrooms , undrained 
 1 cup  chicken broth 
 ⅓ cup  dry vermouth 
 3 cups cooked  chicken  cubes 
 ⅓ cup chopped  pimentos 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon ground  thyme 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 4 cups hot cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Cook  onions  in  butter  in large skillet over medium heat until tender but not brown. 
 Blend in  flour . 
 Add  sour cream ,  mushrooms  with liquid,  chicken broth  and  vermouth ; cook, stirring constantly, until thickened. 
 Stir in  chicken , pimentos,  salt ,  thyme  and  pepper . Heat thoroughly. 
 Serve over hot  rice .
