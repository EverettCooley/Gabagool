Description : 
 A healthy appetizer consisting of fine prepared, glazed, carots. A way to start your evening !
This requires very basic cooking skills and won't take longer than 15 minutes to make.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb Frozen Belgian  carrots 
 6 tbl  butter  or  margarine 
 2 tbl Honey 
 1/4 tsp To 1/2 t  ground ginger 
 Directions : 
 Place frozen  carrots  in a deep, 1 1/2 quart, heat-resistant, non-metallic casserole. 
 Heat, covered, 2 minutes in Microwave Oven. 
 Add remaining ingredients. 
 Uncover and heat an additional 4 minutes or until  carrots  are tender. 
 Let stand for 1 to 2 minutes before serving.
