 Makes 4 to 6 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 4  sweet potatoes , boiled, peeled and sliced 
 ½ cup  butter 
 4  apples , cored, peeled and sliced 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 ½ cup  brown sugar , packed 
 1 to 2 tsp  cinnamon 
 ½ cup  water 
 ¼ cup  lemon juice 
 ¼ cup  orange juice 
 Directions : 
 Arrange a layer of sliced sweet potatoes in a greased one quart baking dish. 
 Dot with butter; sprinkle with sugars and cinnamon. 
 Arrange a layer of apple slices on top; continue layering until all ingredients are used. 
 Combine water and juices; sprinkle over top. 
 Cover and bake at 400°F for 45 minutes or until apples are tender.
