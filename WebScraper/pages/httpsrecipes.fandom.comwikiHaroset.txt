Ingredients : 
 ½ lbs dried pitted  dates 
 ½ cup dark seeded  raisins 
 4 oz  dried apricots  (unsulphured) 
 1 large navel  orange , peeled, separated 
 2 each  Red Delicious apples , peeled, cored, cut in 8ths. 
 zest of ½  lemon 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 ¼ tsp each  allspice  and  ginger 
 ½ tsp  cardamom 
 ½ cup  apple juice 
 2 tbsp each  dark brown sugar  and  honey 
 1 tbsp fresh  lemon juice 
 ½ cup each shelled  walnuts  and whole  blanched almonds 
 sweet red Passover wine 
 Directions : 
 Put all ingredients except  honey ,  lemon juice  nuts and wine in a 3 quart heavy-bottomed saucepan. 
 Bring to a boil. 
 Reduce heat to simmering. 
 Cover and cook for 10 minutes, stirring often. 
 Mixture will remain thick but moist as fruit exudes liquid as it cooks. 
 Let cool. 
 Transfer mixture to food processor with  honey ,  lemon juice  and nuts. 
 Coarsely chop in 4 or 5 on/off turns (do not overchop). 
 Scrape thick mixture into a bowl. 
 If desired, stir in wine to taste. 
 Store in refrigerator in a tightly closed jar.
