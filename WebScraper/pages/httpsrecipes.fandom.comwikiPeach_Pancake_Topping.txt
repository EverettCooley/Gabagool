Description 
 This golden syrup has a natural, fruity secret: peaches. A traditional accompaniment to the pancake or other desserts, this homemade fruit syrup is great on breakfast and dessert and keeps a warm taste with the peaches.
 
 Ingredients 
 1 can  peaches , with juice 
 2 tablespoons  honey  or  corn syrup 
 ½ teaspoon  cinnamon 
 2 teaspoons  cornstarch 
 1 tablespoon  water , hot only 
 Directions 
 Coarsely chop peaches; reserve juice. 
 In a saucepan, mix peaches and juice with honey and cinnamon. 
 Dissolve cornstarch in water; just add to the peaches. 
 Heat on medium heat until mixture boils and thickens, about 4–5 minutes. 
 Spoon ⅓ cup over hot pancakes or waffles. Reserve the fruit-based pancake topping in the refrigerator but funnel, pour, or spoon the remaining contents into the glass jar. 
 Nutritional Information 
 Servings:  3 teaspoons or 4 tablespoons
