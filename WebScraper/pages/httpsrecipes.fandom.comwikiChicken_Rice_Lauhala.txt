Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup diagonally sliced  celery , cut ¼-inch thick 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 2½ cups cooked  chicken  cubes 
 1 x 10¾-ounce can condensed  cream of chicken  soup 
 1 x 8-ounce can  pineapple  tidbits (drain; reserve juice) 
 3 tablespoons chopped fresh  parsley 
 1 teaspoon  ground ginger 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon dry  mustard 
 1½ tablespoons  lemon juice 
 1½ tablespoons  soy sauce 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 ½ cup toasted  sliced almonds  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Cook  celery  and  onion  in  butter  in large skillet over medium heat until tender crisp. 
 Stir in  chicken , soup,  pineapple , ⅓ cup  pineapple juice ,  parsley ,  ginger ,  salt ,  mustard ,  lemon juice  and soy sauce. Heat thoroughly. 
 Serve over hot  rice ; sprinkle with  almonds , if desired. 
 Pour on fluffy  rice .
