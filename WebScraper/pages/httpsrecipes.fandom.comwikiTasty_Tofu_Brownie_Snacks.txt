 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 Dry Ingredients
 
 1 1/3 cups  cake flour 
 1/2 teaspoon  baking soda 
 1/2 teaspoon  cinnamon 
 
Wet Ingredients
 
 1/4 cup unsweetened  applesauce 
 1 teaspoon  canola oil 
 3/4 cup  granulated sugar 
 1 package Mori-Nu Silken Lite  firm tofu 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 1/3 cup European processed  cocoa powder 
 
Optional Garnish
 
 2 tablespoons chopped  walnuts 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Cut a piece of wax paper to fit in bottom of 8-inch square baking pan. With paper removed, grease bottom and sides of pan with  vegetable shortening . Place wax paper on top of greased pan bottom and grease top of wax paper.
 In food processor fitted with metal chopping blade, process all dry ingredients. Empty into small bowl and set aside.
 Place all wet ingredients in work bowl and process until smooth, scraping the bowl sides occasionally. Add dry mixture all at once. Pulse to blend until dry ingredients are just moistened. Scrape mixture into prepared pan; sprinkle with  nuts . Bake for 22 minutes or until brownies pull away from sides. Let cool for 15 minutes before turning out.
 Makes 12 Brownies
 Per brownie: 121 calories, 3.4 g protein, 25 g carbohydrates, 1 g fat, 0 mg cholesterol, 80 mg sodium
