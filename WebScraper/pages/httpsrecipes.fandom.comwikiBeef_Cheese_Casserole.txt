Beef   Cheese  Casserole
 
 1 Lb. Ground  Beef 
 3 T. A-1  Steak  Sauce 
 1/3 Cup Finely Chopped  Onion 
 1 Can  tomato  Sauce (8 Oz) 
 1 Cup Shredded Mild  Cheddar   Cheese 
 1/2 Cup Uncooked Elbow  macaroni , Cooked And Drained 
 6 Slices  Bacon , Cooked And Crumbled 
 5 Slices  tomato 
 
Cook Meat in Skillet Until Crumbly. Drain Off Fat. Mix in A-1  Steak  Sauce,  Onion ,  tomato  Sauce, 3/4 Cup  Cheese ,  macaroni  And Half of The  Bacon . Spoon Into 6 Cup Baking Dish. Top With  tomato . Bake at 350 For 20 Minutes. Sprinkle With Remaining 1/4  Cheese  And  Bacon . Bake 10 Minutes More. Let Stand 5 Minutes Before Serving.
 Serves 4
