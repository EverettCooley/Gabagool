Description : 
 From my Aunt in West Virginia
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 Filling : 
 3 cups mashed  sweet potatoes 
 1 cup  white sugar 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 ½ cup  butter , melted 
 2  eggs  beaten 
 Topping : 
 1 cup  brown sugar 
 ½ cup  flour 
 ½ cup chopped  pecans 
 ½ cup  butter 
 Directions : 
 Mix the filling ingredients well and place in greased casserole dish. 
 Mix the topping ingredients well and spread over filling. 
 Bake uncovered at 350°F for 30 minutes.
