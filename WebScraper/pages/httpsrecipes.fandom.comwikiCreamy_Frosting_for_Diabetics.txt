 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup non-fat  cottage cheese , sieve 
 ⅛ tsp  salt 
 ½ tbsp diet  margarine , melted 
 ½ cup  Equal  or  sugar twin 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients. 
 Beat until smooth. 
 Spread on cake. 
 Frosts one cake.
