Slow Cooker Chinese Style Country Ribs
 
 1/4 Cup  soy sauce 
 1/4 Cup  orange   marmalade 
 2 T.  Catsup 
 1 clove  garlic , Crushed 
 3 to 4 Lbs. Country Style Ribs 
 
Combine  soy sauce ,  marmalade ,  Catsup  And  garlic . Brush on Both Sides of Ribs. Place in Slow Cooking Pot. Pour Remaining Sauce Over All. Cover And Cook on Low For 8 to 10 Hours. Makes 4 to 6 Servings. These Ribs Are Also Known as Farm Style Ribs. Regular Spareribs May be Substituted.Source: Unknown
