 About Irish cream liqueur : 
 Wikipedia Article  About Irish cream liqueur on Wikipedia 
 Irish cream is a cream liqueur based on  Irish whiskey  and made with cream and other sweeteners.  Bailey's Irish Cream  brand popularized it. It is a popular flavor of coffee creamer and an ice-cream flavor at the Cold Stone creameries.
 Other brands include  Sangster's Rum Cream .
