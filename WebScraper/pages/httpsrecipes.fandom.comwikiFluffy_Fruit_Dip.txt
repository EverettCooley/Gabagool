Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup dairy  sour cream 
 ⅓ cup  apricot  or  peach preserves , or orange  marmalade 
 ¼ cup finely chopped  walnuts 
 2 – 3 tbsp  milk 
 Directions : 
 Combine ingredients and mix well; chill. 
 To serve, insert picks into chilled fruit pieces (canned or fresh) and dunk into chilled dip.
