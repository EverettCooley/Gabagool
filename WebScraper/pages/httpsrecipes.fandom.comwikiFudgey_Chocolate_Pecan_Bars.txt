Ingredients : 
 1 	cup 	 all-purpose flour 
 2/3 	cup 	 Sugar 
 1/2 	cup 	HERSHEY'S  cocoa 
 1/2 	tsp 	 salt 
 3/4 	cup 	(1-1/2 sticks) cold  butter  or  margarine 
 2 		 eggs 
 1 	can 	(14 oz)  sweetened condensed milk  (not  evaporated milk ) 
 1 1/2 	tsp 	Maple flavoring 
 2 	cups 	 pecan  halves or pieces 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 350 F. 
 In large bowl, stir together  flour ,  Sugar ,  cocoa  and  salt ; cut in  butter  until crumbly. 
 Stir in 1 beaten  egg . 
 Press mixture evenly on bottom of 13x9x2-inch baking pan. 
 Bake 25 minutes. 
 Meanwhile, in medium bowl, beat  sweetened condensed milk , remaining 1  egg  and flavoring; stir in  pecan  halves. 
 Pour over prepared crust, distributing  pecan  halves evenly. 
 Return to oven. Bake 25 minutes or until golden. 
 Cool completely in pan on wire rack. 
 Cut into bars. 
 Store tightly covered at room temperature.
