Description : 
 Makes 2 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 ounces smoked country-style link  Sausage 
 1/3 cup chopped  Onion 
 1/4 cup  catsup 
 2 tablespoons  brown sugar  (not packed) 
 1/2 teaspoon prepared  mustard 
 1/4 teaspoon  liquid smoke 
 1/4 teaspoon  salt 
 1/8 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1 1/2 cups cooked  rice 
 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Sausage  in small amount of  water  in covered pan for 10 minutes. Remove  Sausage  and cut into 2-inch pieces. 
 Combine  Onion ,  catsup ,  brown sugar ,  mustard ,  liquid smoke ,  salt ,  pepper  and  rice . Turn into buttered 1-quart casserole. Arrange  Sausage  on top. Cover. Bake 25 to 30 minutes at 350 degrees.
