Desription : 
 Contributed by Alesia at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅓ cup uncooked  minute rice 
 1 can  cream of mushroom  soup 
 ¾ cup can  water 
 4  pork chops 
 Directions : 
 Combine soup and rice. 
 Add water and mix. 
 Pour in casserole dish. 
 Brown pork chops on both sides and place on top of rice mixture. 
 Bake at 350°F for 1 hour.
