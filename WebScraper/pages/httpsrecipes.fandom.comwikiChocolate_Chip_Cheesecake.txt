 Ingredients : 
 2 	x 	pkgs of  cream cheese , softened 
 14 	oz 	can of Eagle Brand  sweetened condensed milk 
 1 	large 	 egg 
 1 	tsp 	 vanilla extract 
 1 	cup 	mini- chocolate chips 
 1 	tsp 	 flour 
 1 	x 	6oz Keebler ® Ready Crust ®  chocolate  Pie Crust 
 chocolate  Glaze (recipe follows) 
 chocolate curls  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°. 
 With mixer, beat  Cheese  until fluffy, gradually beat in Eagle Brand until smooth. 
 Add  egg ,  vanilla ; mix well. 
 Toss chips with  flour ; stir into  Cheese  mixture. 
 Pour into crust. 
 Bake 35 minutes or until center springs back when lightly touched. 
 Cool and top with glaze and curls. 
 Serve chilled. 
 Refrigerate leftovers. 
 Chocolate  Glaze : 
 Melt 1/2 cup mini- chocolate chips  with 1/4 cup  whipping cream ; cook and stir until thickened and smooth. 
 Immediately spread over pie.
