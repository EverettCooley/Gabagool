Ingredients : 
 1 oz.  lime juice 
 1 oz.  orange liqueur 
 1 oz.  vodka 
 Directions : 
 Mix with cracked  ice  and strain into a chilled cocktail glass, or strain into a shot glass and serve as a shooter.
