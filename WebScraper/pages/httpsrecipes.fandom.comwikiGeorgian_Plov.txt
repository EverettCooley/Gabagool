Ingredients : 
 2 cups  rice 
 150 g  raisins 
 150 g  honey 
 a cup boiled  butter 
 Directions : 
 Prepare the plov as Turkmenistanian plov (see the recipe in  plov ). 
 Mix the honey with an equal amount of hot  water  and add the  raisins . 
 Pour this sauce over the plov and cook for 10 minutes.
