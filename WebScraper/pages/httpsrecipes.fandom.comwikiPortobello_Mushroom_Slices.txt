 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Original recipe 
 This recipe yields 1 serving. 
 Ingredients : 
 3 ounces  portobello mushroom  slices (about 8 large slices) 
 1 teaspoon  olive oil 
 1 pinch  salt 
 â…› teaspoon freshly-ground  black pepper 
 1 teaspoon  balsamic vinegar 
 1 teaspoon grated  romano  or  Parmesan cheese 
 Directions : 
 Spray a medium nonstick skillet with nonstick spray coating. 
 Add the mushrooms to the skillet and brush with the oil, using a brush or your finger. 
 Sprinkle with salt (if desired) and pepper. 
 Cook over medium to medium-high heat 6 to 7 minutes or until the mushrooms have exuded their juices, softened and begun to brown. 
 Remove the pan from the heat. 
 Stir in the vinegar and cheese and toss to coat the mushrooms. 
 Serve at once.
