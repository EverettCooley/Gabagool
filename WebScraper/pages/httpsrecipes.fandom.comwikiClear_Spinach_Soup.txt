Description : 
 This recipe yields 4 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ lb fresh  spinach 
 1  scallion 
 1  garlic  clove 
 4 cup  water 
 ½ lb  ground beef 
 1 tsp  soy sauce 
 1 tbsp  salt 
 1 dash freshly-ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Wash the  spinach  thoroughly and trim off the thick stems. 
 Chop the  scallion . 
 Mince the  garlic . 
 Bring the  water  to a boil. 
 Add the meat and bring to a second boil. 
 Skim off the froth. 
 Add the  spinach ,  scallion ,  garlic , soy sauce,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Lower the flame and simmer for 10 minutes. 
 Serve immediately.
