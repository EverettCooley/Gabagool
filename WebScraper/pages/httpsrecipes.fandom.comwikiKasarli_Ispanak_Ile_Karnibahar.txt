Description : 
 Here is celebration of  spinach  and  cauliflower  as a nutritious, light dinner. Also can be used as a side dish with various meat dishes.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 small  cauliflower , washed and separated into small pieces. 
 ½ pound of fresh  spinach 
 1 cup of  milk 
 2 tbs  flour 
 2 tbs  margarine 
 2 chopped  onions 
 1  egg , beaten 
 1 cup of  Kashar   cheese , grated 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Set the oven 375 °F. 
 Boil the cauliflowers for 5-7 minutes in  water  and  salt  and drain. 
 Boil the  spinach  for 5-7 minutes in  water  and  salt  and drain well. 
 Sauté  onion  in  butter , separate into two portions sautee cauliflowers with half of the  onions . 
 Spread them into baking pan or into an earth ware. 
 Sauté  spinach  with the remaining half of  onions  for 6-7 minutes and add  salt  and  pepper . 
 Add flower and  milk , stir them until the mix thickens. 
 Take it off the heat. 
 Add the beaten  egg  slowly and mix. 
 Spread this mix over the cauliflowers in baking pan. 
 Sprinkle grated kashar  cheese  over it. 
 Bake it for 10-15 minutes. 
 Serve it hot.
