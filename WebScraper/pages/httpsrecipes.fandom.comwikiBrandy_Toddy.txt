Description : 
 Brandy Toddy  from How to Mix Drinks by Jerry Thomas—original source of recipe, out of copyright
 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients : 
 1 teaspoon  sugar 
 ½ a wine glass of  water 
 1 do.  brandy 
 1 small lump of  ice 
 Directions : 
 Stir together with a spoon. 
 Variations : 
 For a hot brandy toddy, omit the ice and use boiling water.
