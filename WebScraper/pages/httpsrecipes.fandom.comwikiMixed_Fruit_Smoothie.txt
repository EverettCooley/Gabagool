Info 
 Cook Time : 
 Serves : 
 
 Ingredients 
 1 15-ounce can  fruit cocktail , chilled 
 ½ cup (4 ounces)  milk 
 ½ cup  orange juice 
 6-8  ice  cubes 
 Directions 
 Combine ingredients in blender. 
 Cover and blend on high until smooth. 
 Add more ice cubes for a thicker consistency. 
 Source 
 Fruit Mixed, Canned  by the US Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
