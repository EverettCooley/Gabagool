      Description     
 Ingredients : 
 3 ounces fresh lime juice 
 5 ounces silver  tequila 
 2 1/2 ounces blue  Curacao  liqueur 
 2 ounces agave nectar, or to taste 
 coarse salt for glass rim, optional 
 Directions : 
 Stir together all ingredients except salt. Fill two margarita or rocks glasses 3/4 full with ice, and pour mixed margarita over the ice.
