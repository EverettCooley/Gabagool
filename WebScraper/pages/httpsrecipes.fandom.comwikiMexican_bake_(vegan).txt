 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 x  corn  tortillas 
 x beans 
 x left over  rice 
 x salsa 
 x frozen  corn 
 x frozen vegetables 
 x hot sauce 
 Directions : 
 Put  corn  tortillas in a pyrex pan. Add beans and  rice . Cold  brown rice  is great. beans - any thing you got, black, red,  chile , whatever. Add salsa, frozen  corn , frozen veggies, hot sauce. More  corn  tortillas, more salsa. Bake. Eat
