 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 package  firm tofu , drained and crumbled 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 1 cup green  bell pepper , chopped 
 1 cup  Onion , chopped 
 1 cup fresh  Mushrooms , sliced 
 2 cloves  garlic , chopped 
 1/4 teaspoon  turmeric 
 1/4 to 1/2 cup barbecue sauce (optional) 
 1/4 cup Betty Crocker Bac-Os (optional) 
 salt  and  pepper , to taste 
 Directions : 
 Saut√© the crumbled  tofu  in  oil  until dry. Add remaining ingredients and continue to cook for 5 to 10 minutes.
