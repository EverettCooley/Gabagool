 Ingredients : 
 1 oz. Bacardi  light rum 
 1 oz. Wilderberry Schnapps 
 1/2 oz. Bacardi 151 
 1 oz. Sour mix 
 1 cup  strawberries 
 1  banana 
 Directions : 
 Blend with Crushed  ice . 
 Serve in a large Decorative glass. 
 Garnish with a fresh  strawberry  if available, or an  orange  slice and  cherry .
