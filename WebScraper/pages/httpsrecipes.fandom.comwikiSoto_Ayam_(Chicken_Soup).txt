 Description : 
 A Malaysian style  Chicken  soup
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  onions , chop coarsely 
 3 cloves  garlic , chop finely 
 1Â cm  ginger , chop finely 
 1 tbsp macademia  nuts , chop finely 
 1 tsp ground coriander 
 1 tsp  chile paste 
 500g  Chicken  parts, bite-size pieces 
 6 cups  water 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1/2 tsp ground  white pepper 
 2 cups  rice noodles , cooked 
 2 cups  bean sprouts 
 oil  for frying 
 2  eggs , hard boiled and cut into wedges 
 1/2 fried shallots, for garnishing (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in wok and fry  onions ,  garlic  and  ginger  until  onions  are transparent. Add macademia  nuts , ground coriander and  chile paste . Stir fry for 3 minutes. Add  Chicken  parts and stir fry for 5 minutes. Add  water , bring to boil and simmer for 45 minutes. Remove  Chicken  parts, strip meat from bones, discard skin and bones. Strain stock and return to boil. Add  salt  and  pepper  to taste. Place noodles,  bean sprouts  and  Chicken  meat in bowls and pour soup over. Garnish with fried shallots and  eggs . Serve hot. Serves 4 bowls.
