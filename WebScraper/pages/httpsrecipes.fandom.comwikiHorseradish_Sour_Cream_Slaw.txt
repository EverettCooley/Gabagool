Description : 
 This is a recipe I obtained from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Hager estate in Canton, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  catsrecipes y-group 
 Ingredients : 
 1¼ cups  sour cream 
 ¾ cup  mayonnaise 
 2 tablespoons prepared  horseradish 
 ¼ cup  cider vinegar 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  celery seeds 
 1 small  savoy cabbage  shredded 
 1 small  red cabbage  shredded 
 Directions : 
 Stir together first 7 ingredients in a large bowl. 
 Add  cabbage  and toss well. 
 Chill at least 1 hour before serving.
