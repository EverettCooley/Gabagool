 Description : 
 In Romanian: Bigus
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 medium sour  cabbage , 
 5 oz/150 g smoked  Bacon , cubed, 
 5 oz/150 g hot dogs cut in pieces, 
 5 oz/150 g smoked salami, cubed, 
 2 tablespoons  lard , 
 pepper 
 Directions : 
 Julienne the  cabbage . If it is too sour or too salty, wash it. 
 Fry it in hot  lard , together with small cubes of  Bacon , salami and hot dogs. 
 Fry and keep stirring and adding a tablespoon or two of  water  and  pepper . 
 When everything is fried, put in the oven for half an hour.
