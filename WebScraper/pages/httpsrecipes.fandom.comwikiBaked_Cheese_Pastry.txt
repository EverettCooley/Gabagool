 Ingredients : 
 12 sheets of  phyllo  Pastry 
 450g/1Â lb Bulgarian  white cheese  or  feta 
 Plenty of Melted  butter 
 4  eggs 
 240ml/8fl.oz.  soda water 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Preheat the oven to 180C, 350F, Gas Mark 4 and lightly  butter  a wide deep ovenproof dish. 
 The dish should preferably be the same length and width as a sheets of  phyllo . 
 Place a sheet of  phyllo  pastry in the bottom of the prepared dish, bush with melted  butter  then sprinkle with some of the crumbled  white cheese . 
 Continue the layers in the same way, ending with a layer of buttered pastry. 
 Place the  eggs ,  salt , and  soda water  in a mixing bowl and beat well then slowly pour the mixture over the layered  Cheese /pastry. 
 Set aside for 10 minutes then bake for about 40 minutes until slightly risen and browned. 
 Remove from the oven and set aside to cool for about 30 minutes before serving.
