Description : 
 This recipe came from the Hartman Estate Sale. It was obtained it as part of the purchase of the Family Collection from the Hartman Estate in Arlington, Texas in 1984.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups mashed cooked  sweet potatoes 
 1 cup  granulated sugar 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 2  eggs  beaten 
 2½ teaspoons melted  butter 
 ½ cup  milk 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 Crunch Topping : 
 2½ tbsp  butter 
 1 cup  brown sugar 
 ⅓ cup  plain flour 
 1 cup chopped  pecans 
 Directions : 
 Crunch Topping : 
 Make crunch topping by melting  butter  then adding  brown sugar ,  flour  and pecans and mixing well. 
 Soufflé : 
 Mix all ingredients together and pour into a greased baking dish 
 Then cover with crunch topping and bake at 350 °F for 35 minutes.
