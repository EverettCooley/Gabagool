       Green String Bean Soup     
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  string beans , cut 
 3 lbs spring  chicken , cut up 
 2 tablespoons  lard 
 1 large  onion , chop and sautéd 
 1½ tablespoons  flour 
 1 teaspoon  red peppers 
 1 clove  garlic 
 1 cup  onions , small, minced 
 1 stalk  parsley , chopped fine 
 dill , chopped fine 
 3 quarts  water 
 Directions : 
 Place  lard ,  onion  and  flour  in a pot, saute, add red pepper,  water ,  chicken  and cook. 
 Cook cut up  green beans ,  garlic , and  onion  in 4 ounces  water  separately for a half hour, then add to  chicken  pot and cook for 45 minutes or more. 
 Add  parsley  and dill last.
