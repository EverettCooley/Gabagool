Description : 
 Yoghurt-marinated shrimp
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds  shrimp , shelled 
 Marinade : 
 1 cup low-fat  yoghurt 
 ½ tsp  curry powder ,  cumin , or  allspice 
 ¼ tsp red pepper 
 pinch  black pepper 
 2 cloves  garlic , crushed with 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Combine the marinade ingredients. 
 Add the  shrimp  and turn to coat each piece with the marinade. 
 Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours or longer. 
 Thread onto skewers and grill or oven broil, basting with the marinade.
