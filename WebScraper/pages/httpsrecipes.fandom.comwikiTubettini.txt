 About Tubettini : 
 Tubettini, which comes from the Italian word for "little tube" is a smaller version of the  Tubetti . These tiny pasta tubes are most often used in light soups and broths. If tubettini are not available  orzo ,  pastina  or  risi  can be substituted.
