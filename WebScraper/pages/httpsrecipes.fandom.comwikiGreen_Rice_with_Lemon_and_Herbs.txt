Green  rice  With  lemon  And Herbs
 
 1 Small  Onion , Finely Chopped 
 2 T.  butter 
 1 T.  lemon juice 
 1 Cup Long  grain   rice 
 1 Can  chicken broth  (10.5 Oz) 
 3/4 Cup  water 
 2 T. Finely Chopped  parsley 
 2 T. Finely Chopped  chives 
 salt  And  pepper  to Taste
 
In a Pan, Saut?  Onion  in  butter  Until  Onion  is Transparent. Stir in The Remaining Ingredients, Cover Pan And Simmer  rice  Until Liquid is Absorbed And  rice  is Tender. Serves 6Source: With Love From Darling's Kitchen
