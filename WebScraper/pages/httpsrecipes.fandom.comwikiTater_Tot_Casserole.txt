       Tater Tot Casserole     
 From: Dorie at  http://groups.yahoo.com/group/Momsmenuplan/ 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  hamburger 
 Macaroni and Cheese Dinner (cooked) 
 1 can  evaporated milk 
 1 cup shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 1 can  cream of chicken  soup 
 1 can  cream of mushroom  soup 
 1 can  mushrooms 
 1 pkg.  onion soup mix 
 1 pkg. Tater Tots 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Brown hamburger and onion soup mix. 
 Combine all other ingredients once hamburger is cooked and place in a 9x13 baking dish. 
 Cover with Tater Tots. 
 Cook ½ hour.
