 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1  English muffin , split in half and toasted 
 1  egg , cooked over easy 
 2 slices (about 1 oz.) soy Canadian  Bacon  or  Ham 
 1 slice (3/4 to 1 oz.) soy  Cheddar   Cheese 
 Directions : 
 Layer cooked  egg , Canadian  Bacon  and  Cheese  slice on one half of  English muffin . Top with other muffin half. Eat warm.
 
 Yield: 1 sandwich. Serving size: 1 sandwich. Per serving: 315 calories, 10 g fat (2 g sat fat), 24 g protein (5 g soy protein), 30 g carbohydrate, 765 mg sodium, 213 mg cholesterol, 3 g dietary fiber.
