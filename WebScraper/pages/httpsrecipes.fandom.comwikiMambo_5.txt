 Ingredients : 
 1 shot Stolichnaya®  vodka 
 ½ shot De Kuyper®  triple sec 
 1 shot De Kuyper®  apricot brandy 
 ¼ shot  Campari 
 5 shots  orange juice 
 Directions : 
 Shake with cubed  ice  and strain into an  ice  filled highball.
