 Source:  High Cotton Cookin': A Collection of Southern Country Recipes  by Mary E. Abramson, Marvell Academy Mothers Assn, Marvell, AR 72366,  ISBN 0-918544-14-9 
 
 downloaded from Glen's MM Recipe Archive 
 Yield: 12 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  paprika 
 ½ tsp  dry mustard 
 ¼ tsp  black pepper 
 ¼ tsp  lemon pepper 
 1⅓ cup  oil 
 ¾ cup  honey 
 ¾ cup  cider vinegar 
 Directions : 
 Shake all ingredients, except honey and vinegar, in a quart jar. 
 Stir honey into vinegar until blended. 
 Add to oil mixture and shake. 
 Can be stored in refrigerator for several weeks.
