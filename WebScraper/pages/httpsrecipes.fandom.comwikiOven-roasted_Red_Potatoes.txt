Description : 
 Yield : 4 servings 
 Prep Time : 10 minutes 
 Cook Time : 45 – 60 minutes 
 Equipment needed : 
 Chef's knife 
 Cutting board 
 Rubber  spatula 
 Pepper mill 
 Half sheet pan 
 Parchment paper 
 Pastry brush 
 Ingredients : 
 4  red potatoes 
 2 tablespoons  basil 
 2 tablespoons  Italian seasoning 
 olive oil 
 sea salt 
 fresh ground  pepper 
 fresh  thyme  leaves, minced 
 fresh  parsley  leaves, minced 
 Directions : 
 Wash and scrub potatoes to remove dirt, and then cut into ¾ inch slices, quartering each slice. 
 Place on sheet pan and brush with olive oil, then sprinkle with seasonings. 
 Place in 350°F oven and roast for 45 – 60 minutes or until browned or thoroughly cooked.
