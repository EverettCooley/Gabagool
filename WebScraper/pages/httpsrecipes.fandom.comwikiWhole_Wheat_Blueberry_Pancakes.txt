Ingredients : 
 2 cups  whole wheat flour 
 2 tsp  baking powder 
 2 cups  milk , (low-fat) 
 2  eggs 
 1 tbsp  oil 
 1 cup  blueberries 
 Directions : 
 Mix the first two ingredients together in a medium bowl. 
 Beat in the next three ingredients. 
 Stir in the blueberries. 
 Lightly  oil  a frying pan and spoon batter on tablespoon. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Food exchange per serving: 4 pancakes 3 starch/bread exchanges, 1  milk  exchange; pro: 20 mg; fat: 8 gm; car: 72 gm
