 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  ground beef 
 ⅓ cup  milk 
 one medium  onion , chopped 
 1 tablespoon  chili powder 
 one 8-ounce can gluten-free  tomato  sauce 
 one 16-ounce can  kidney beans 
 Directions : 
 Sauté  beef  and  onion  in a large skillet. 
 Drain excess  oil . 
 Add  tomato  sauce,  milk ,  chili powder  and  kidney beans . 
 Simmer 45 minutes to one hour. 
 Serve hot.
