 Description : 
 A white-hot Malaysian take on main dishes - a cheesy taste of prawns is made divine with butter and vegetables and is great with any appetizer/side dish.
 
         
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  butter 
 500 g. medium size prawns, unshelled 
 2  garlic  cloves, pounded 
 ½ large  onion , sliced or minced, red, white, or yellow only 
 2 tbsp.  sugar , white only 
 salt 
 ¼ cup grated  cheese  (Colby, Swiss, Parmesan, Cheddar, or Monterey Jack) 
 chili leaves, optional 
 hot, white rice 
 curry sauce 
 Directions : 
 Mix  garlic ,  sugar  and  salt  to prawns, mix and set aside for 15 minutes. 
 heat  butter  in frying pan and fry prawns till cooked, about 5-7 minutes. 
 Add  onion  and continue stirring for 2 minutes. 
 Add grated  cheese  and cook for another minute. 
 Serve hot with  rice  and  curry . Garnish with the chili leaves, if desired.
