Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes about 6 dozen. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup crushed  chocolate wafers 
 1 cup finely chopped  nuts  ( pecans ,  walnuts  or  almonds ) 
 1 cup  powdered sugar 
 1½ tablespoons  light corn syrup  or  honey 
 ¼ cup  bourbon  (or  rum  or a flavored liqueur) 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together. 
 Form into small balls and roll in powdered sugar or powdered cocoa. 
 Store covered in an airtight container.
