 Description : 
 Haupia (Hawaiian  coconut  Pudding) is served mostly at a Hawaiian Luau.
 
 Ingredients : 
 12 ounces chilled  coconut milk 
 1 1/2 cups  water 
 6 tablespoons  Sugar 
 6 tablespoons  cornstarch 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in saucepan. Stir over medium heat until thickened. Lower heat and cook for ten minutes, stirring constantly to avoid lumping or burning. Pour into 8x8 inch dish and chill until set. Cut haupia into squares.
Note: May be topped with crushed  pineapple , sliced  peaches  or sliced  mango .
