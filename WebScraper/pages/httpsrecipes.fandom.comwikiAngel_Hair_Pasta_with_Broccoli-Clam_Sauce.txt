Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 4 Servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cans (6.5 oz each) chopped  clams 
 8 oz bottle  clam juice 
 2 cups  water 
 2 tsp chopped  garlic 
 ½ tsp dried  oregano 
 ⅛ tsp  red pepper flakes 
 4 cups  broccoli  flowerets 
 ½ pound  angel hair pasta , broken in half 
 1 tbs  olive oil 
 ⅓ cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 Directions : 
 Drain clams over a skillet, reserve clams. 
 Add  clam juice ,  water ,  garlic ,  oregano ,  red pepper flakes  to skillet. 
 Bring to a boil. 
 Add  broccoli , cook 3 minutes. 
 Add  pasta  and  oil , cook 3 minutes more. 
 Remove from heat. 
 Stir clams into skillet. 
 Cover, let stand 5 minutes or until  pasta  is tender. 
 Stir mixture. 
 Sprinkle with Parmesan.
