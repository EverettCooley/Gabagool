 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 	cup 	 nutritional yeast 
 1/3 	cup 	 flour 
 1/4 	cup 	 oil 
 1 1/2 	cup 	 water 
 1 	tbsp 	Tamari 
 1/2 	tsp 	 garlic powder 
 1/4 	tsp 	 paprika 
 1/4 	tsp 	 basil 
 1/4 	tsp 	 oregano 
 Directions : 
 1 	In a medium pot, combine  yeast  &  flour .
 2 	Place over low heat & stir till lightly toasted.
 3 	Add  oil , stirring to make a thick batter.
 4 	Slowly add the  water , stirring constantly until desired consistency is reached.
 5 	You could use as little as 1 c or as much as 2 c. add remaining ingredients & stir well.
 6 	Yields 2 cups
