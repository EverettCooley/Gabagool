Ingredients : 
 1 oz  Galliano 
 1 oz  Kahlúa 
 beer   or   club soda  to fill 
 Directions : 
 In a frosted  beer  mug, add  Galliano  and  Kahlúa . 
 Fill with  beer  or  club soda .
