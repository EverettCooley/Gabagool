 Ingredients : 
 Rice 
 
 14 oz can low sodium  chicken broth 
 1/2 tsp  salt 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1  bay leaf , crushed 
 2 tsp  chili powder 
 2 tsp  cumin 
 2 tsp  thyme 
 2 cups white  rice , uncooked 
 Salad 
 
 1 small red pepper, diced 
 1  jalapeño pepper , seeded and diced 
 1 oz fresh  cilantro , chopped 
 1 tsp prepared  mustard 
 1 tbsp  red wine vinegar 
 2 tbsp  olive oil 
 2 tsp  chili powder 
 2 tsp hot pepper sauce 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1 tsp ground  black pepper 
 1 tsp  salt 
 2 (15-16 oz.) cans  black beans , drained and rinsed 
 Directions : 
 Combine all the rice ingredients in a small baking dish (about 2-qt.), cover and bake at 350F for 30–35 minutes, until  rice  is cooked, but still moist. 
 Combine all salad ingredients in a medium bowl. 
 Then mix with the  rice . 
 Chill and serve.
