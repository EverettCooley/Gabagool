 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  molasses 
 ¼ cup  Sugar 
 3 tablespoons  butter  (or  oil ) 
 1 tablespoon  milk 
 2 cups  flour 
 ½ teaspoon  baking soda 
 ½ teaspoon  nutmeg 
 ½ teaspoon  cinnamon 
 ½ teaspoon cloves 
 ½ teaspoon  ginger 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 375 °F (190 °C). 
 Heat  molasses  to boiling. 
 Add  Sugar ,  butter , and  milk . 
 In a bowl, sift together  flour , soda, and spices. 
 Add dry ingredients to moist ingredients. 
 Mix all. 
 Drop by teaspoonfuls onto a greased cookie sheet, keeping cookies 1" apart. 
 Bake for 8 minutes.
