 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  peanut butter 
 26  Graham cracker  squares 
 1 cup ripe  bananas 
 Directions : 
 Thoroughly mash banana in bowl and blend in peanut butter. 
 Chill. Spread 2 Tbsp. mixture onto one square graham cracker, cover with second graham cracker square. 
 Keep chilled until ready to eat.
