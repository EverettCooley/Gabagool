Description : 
 Ethnicity - Bengali, East Indian 
 Type of meal - Party, Lunch, Dinner 
 Ingredients : 
 Potato  100gm. 
 Paneer 250gms. 
 ginger  paste 2tbsp. 
 cummin  powder 1tbsp. 
 tomato  puree/curd 2tbsp. 
 turmeric  powder 1tsp. 
 chilli powder  1tsp. 
 Roasted & powdered  garam masala  1tsp. 
 Sugar  to taste. 
 salt  to taste. 
 oil  3tbsp. 
 Onion  (cut in small pieces) 1 
 garlic  2-3 pieces 
 Directions : 
 Cut  Potato  into small cubes, fry in  oil  & keep aside. 
 Fry paneer till pink & keep aside. Fry  Onion  and  garlic  pieces lightly. 
 Add fried  potatoes  and stir for few minutes. 
 Add  ginger  paste &  cummin  seed powder,  chilli powder ,  turmeric  powder,  salt  &  Sugar  by adding  tomato  puree till leaves  oil . 
 Add  water , bring to a boil & add paneer. Cover & cook on slow fire till done. 
 Sprinkle  garam masala  on the top.
