Description : 
 Always check the ingredients to make sure the product is vegan.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups sliced  apple 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 2 tablespoons  water 
 2 teaspoons  lemon juice 
 ⅓ cups  flour 
 ¾ cups  oats 
 ⅓ cups  brown sugar 
 ¼ cups  margarine 
 Directions : 
 Arrange  apples  in greased 9" square baking dish. 
 Sprinkle with  sugar ,  water , and  lemon juice . 
 Blend  flour ,  oats , and  brown sugar . 
 Mix in  margarine  until mixture resembles coarse  oatmeal . 
 Sprinkle mixture over fruit. 
 Bake at 375 °F for 35 minutes or until fruit is tender. 
 Serve with Rice Dream "ice cream".
