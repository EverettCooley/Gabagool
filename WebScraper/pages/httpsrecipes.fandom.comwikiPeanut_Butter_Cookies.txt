Description : 
 Purchased from the Wagner Estate in Wichita Falls, Texas in 1990. Date and source unknown but marked as a family favorite. 
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup creamy  peanut butter 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 2½ teaspoons liquid  sweetener 
 2  eggs 
 1 cup  cake flour 
 ¼ teaspoon  baking soda 
 ½ cup  skimmed milk 
 Directions : 
 Beat  peanut butter ,  butter , sweetener and  eggs  together and mix well. 
 Add  eggs  and beat again, then add  milk  and  flour . 
 Blend well. 
 Drop by teaspoonfuls on well-greased cookie sheet. 
 Bake in 375°F oven for 12 minutes.
