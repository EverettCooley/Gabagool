Ingredients : 
 2 whole  eggs  or 4  yolks  beaten very light 
 1 cup  sugar 
 ¾ cup  butter 
 2 cups  flour 
 2 teaspoons  baking powder 
 ¾ cup  sherry  wine 
 2 teaspoons  cinnamon 
 ½ teaspoon  cloves 
 1 cup chopped  raisins 
 ¾ cup chopped  pecans 
 Directions : 
 First dissolve the cinnamon and cloves in wine 
 Mix together the eggs, sugar, butter, flour, baking powder and dissolved spices 
 To this batter add the chopped  raisins  and  pecans 
 Mix all well 
 Bake in gem rings
