INGREDIENTS
 
 1/3 cup popcorn kernels
 
 2 tablespoons butter (or coconut oil, for vegan)
 
 1 teaspoon dried all herb mixture (for example, a mix of basil, rosemary, savory, tarragon, and thyme)
 
 1 teaspoon nutritional yeast*
 
 Fine popcorn salt (Tip: To make popcorn salt, crush kosher salt using a mortar and pestle)
 
INSTRUCTIONS
 
 Pop the popcorn in an air popper or our Stovetop Popcorn method.
 
 Meanwhile, melt the butter. Add the dried herbs and nutritional yeast; stir to combine. Pour the mixture over the popcorn, and sprinkle with popcorn salt to taste.
