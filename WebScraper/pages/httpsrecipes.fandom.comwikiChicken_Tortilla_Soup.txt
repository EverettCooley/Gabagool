 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 12 	 cups  chicken broth 
 1 	de-boned rotissarie roasted deli  Chicken , chopped 
 1 	chopped  Onion 
 2 (4 	ounce) cans green chilies 
 8-10 	whole peppercorns 
 4 	chopped  garlic  cloves 
 1 	 tomato , peeled and diced 
 1/2 	teaspoon  thyme 
 1/3 	chopped  cilantro 
 1/3 	cup fresh squeezed  lime juice 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Boil broth, add  onions ,  garlic , peppercorns,  Chicken , green chillies,  thyme , and  salt . 
 Simmer for 20 minute. 
 Add  lime juice  and simmer 10 more minutes. Add  cilantro  just before serving. 
 Garnish with  sour cream ,  Monterey jack   Cheese , diced  avocado , and deep fied  corn  tortilla strips, and a squeeze of  lime juice .
