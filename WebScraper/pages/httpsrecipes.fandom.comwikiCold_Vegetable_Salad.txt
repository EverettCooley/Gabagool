 Ingredients : 
 1 can  white corn  (shoe peg), drained
 1/2 c. chopped  celery 
 1  green pepper ,
 chopped
1 can  green peas ,
 drained
1 can baby limas,
 drained
1/2 c. purple  Onion , chopped
 MARINADE:
 1/2 c.  apple cider vinegar 
 3/4 c.  sugar 
 1 tsp.  pepper 
 1/2 c.  salad oil 
 1 tsp.  salt 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix vegetables. Mix marinate ingredients and pour over vegetables. 
Drain to serve. Serve cold. Keep refrigerated. Will keep several days.
