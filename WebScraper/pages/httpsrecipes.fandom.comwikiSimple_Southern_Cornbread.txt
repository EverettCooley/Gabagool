Description : 
 This simple southern cornbread recipe brought to you from the Recipes.eu.com recipe collection 
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup yellow  cornmeal 
 1 cup  flour 
 4 tsp  baking powder 
 ¾ tsp  salt substitute 
 1 whole  egg , beaten 
 1 cup  skim milk 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl, sift the dry ingredients together. 
 Add the beaten egg and skim milk; mix well. 
 Into 12x8-inch pan, which has been sprayed with a nonstick vegetable coating, pour the corn bread dough. 
 Bake at 425°F for 25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. 
 Cut into 12 squares and serve warm.
