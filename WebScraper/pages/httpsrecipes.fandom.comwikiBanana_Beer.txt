Ingredients : 
 5 ripe  bananas , mashed 
 5  oranges , juice from 
 5  lemons , juice from 
 5 cups  sugar 
 7 cups  water 
 Directions : 
 Mix together and freeze. 
 Fill large glass ⅓ full (or more) with frozen mixture and add 7-Up®, Sprite®, ginger ale, etc.
