Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Southern Living 
 Makes about 5½ cups. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pkg. frozen  strawberries , partially thawed (8 oz) 
 5 cups ice 
 ¾ cup  tequila 
 ½ cup frozen limeade concentrate 
 ⅓ cup  powdered sugar 
 3 tbsp  orange liqueur  such as  Grand Marnier 
 lime juice 
 red decorator sugar crystals 
 lime  slices, fresh  strawberries  for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Process first 6 ingredients in a blender until smooth. 
 Dip margarita glass rims in lime juice; dip rims on red sugar crystals, coating well. 
 Pour margarita mixture into glasses. 
 Serve at once. 
 Garnish if desired.
