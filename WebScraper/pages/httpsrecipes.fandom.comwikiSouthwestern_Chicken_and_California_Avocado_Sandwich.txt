 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 12 French rolls, split 
 Curly  leaf lettuce  as needed 
 2  red onions , thinly sliced 
 12 boneless  Chicken  breasts, skinned and grilled 
 4  California avocados , seeded, peeled and diced 
 1 ½ Cups salsa 
 Directions : 
 Assemble sandwich by topping one half of roll as follows:  lettuce ,  Onion ,  Chicken  breast,  avocado  slices, 2 tablespoons of salsa.
 Garnish with fresh fruit. May be served warm or cold.
 Variation
 Marinate  Chicken  breasts in teriyaki sauce for 3–4 hours in refrigerator before grilling.
