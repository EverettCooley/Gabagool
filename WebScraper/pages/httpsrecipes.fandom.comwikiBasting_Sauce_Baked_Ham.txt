 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup packed  brown sugar 
 2 tbsp dijon  mustard 
 Directions : 
 Add 2 Tbls.  mustard  to 1 C.  brown sugar  and mix to paste consistency. To this, slowly add the contents of the can of  beer , and mx thoroughly. Use this to baste your  Ham  throughout the baking time.
