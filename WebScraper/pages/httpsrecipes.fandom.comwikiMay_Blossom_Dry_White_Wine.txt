 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4.5 liters may blossom (white or pink) 
 1.1 kilo  Sugar 
 2  oranges 
 2  lemons 
 1 teaspoon  grape  tannin powder 
 wine yeast 
 1 teaspoon nutrient salts 
 Directions : 
 Put the flowers in a muslin bag and place it into 4 liters  water . Let it stimmered for 15 minutes. Then, squeeze the bag well to extract all the liquid. 
 Dissolve the  Sugar  in the hot liquid and add the juice and zest* of the  oranges  and  lemons . Allow the liquid to cool down to 21C (70F). When cooled, add the tannin, the  wine yeast  and  yeast  nutrient. Cover it well. Stir daily and keep it in a warm place to start fermentation. 
 After three days, strain everything into a jar and top it up to the shoulder with cool  water . Fit a bung and airlock and leave the jar in a slightly cooler place for the fermentation to be completed. Rack it after three months when a sediment has been formed. Again, put it in a cooler place. 
 After another three months rack it again and add crushed Campden tablet to it. Top up the level to the neck of the jar with  water . It is now ready to be bottled.
