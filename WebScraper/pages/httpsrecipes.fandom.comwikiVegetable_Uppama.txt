Description : 
 Ethnicity - South Indian 
 Type of meal - Party, Lunch, Dinner 
 Ingredients : 
 150 gm:  semolina 
 100 ml: Curd 
 250 gm: Diced vegetables 
 2 no:  Onion , sliced 
 1 no: Chopped  tomato 
 ½ tsp: Skinned  urad dal 
 ¼ tsp:  sesame seeds 
 ¼ tsp:  cumin  seeds 
 1 no:  curry leaves 
 3-4 no: Chopped green chillies 
 2 tsp:  garlic  paste 
 1 tsp:  ginger  paste 
 A pinch: Ground cloves 
 A pinch:  turmeric  powder 
 2 tbsp: Chopped coriander 
 1 no:  lemon juice 
 4-6 tbsp:  oil 
 250 ml: Hot  water 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil , fry  dal ,  sesame  and  cumin  seeds for 15 seconds. 
 Add  onions ,  curry leaves , chopped chillies,  ginger ,  garlic ,  turmeric ,  and  salt. Stir and fry for 5 minutes. 
 Add vegetables and  tomato . Fry for 3-4 minutes. Add  semolina . 
 After 2-3 minute, pour in  water  and curd. Cook for 5 minutes until dry. 
 Garnish with coriander and sharpen with  lemon juice . 
 Serve hot with rasam.
