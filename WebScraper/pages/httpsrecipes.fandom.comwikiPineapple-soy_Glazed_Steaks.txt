Description 
 Beef Recipes  by the South Carolina Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Ingredients 
 2 well-trimmed  beef  (ribeye steaks) cut 1 inch thick 
 Glaze 
 ¼ cup  pineapple juice 
 ¼ cup  soy sauce 
 2 tablespoons packed  brown sugar 
 1 teaspoon  cornstarch 
 Directions 
 In 1-cup glass measure, combine glaze ingredients, mix well. 
 Microwave on high 2½ – 3 minutes until thickened, set aside. 
 Place steaks over medium coals, grill uncovered 11-14 mins, turning steaks occasionally and brushing both sized with glaze during the last 5 minutes of grilling. 
 Carve steaks crosswise into thick slices and drizzle with remaining glaze. 
 Serve with grilled  pineapple  if desired.
