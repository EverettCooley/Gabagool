Description 
 Garlic Pita Chips  from the  Public Health Cookbook  by the Seattle & King County Department of Public Health—original source of recipe, government resource in the public domain
 
 Cook Time : 15 minutes 
 Serves : 4 
 Ingredients 
 2  pita breads 
 4 teaspoons  olive  or  vegetable oil 
 1  garlic  clove, crushed 
 Directions 
 Heat the oven to 350° F. Using a small knife or kitchen scissors, cut along the circumference of each pita bread to remove the folded edge. 
 Combine oil and garlic in a bowl. Brush each pita round with the oil and garlic mixture. 
 Stack the rounds and then cut into 8 wedges. 
 Arrange wedges in a single layer on a baking sheet and bake for 8–10 minutes until chips are golden brown. Serve warm and plain or with other side dishes or appetizers
