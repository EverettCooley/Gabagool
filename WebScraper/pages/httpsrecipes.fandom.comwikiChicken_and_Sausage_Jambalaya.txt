Description : 
 Makes 4 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ pound boneless  chicken breast  chunks 
 salt , ground  black pepper , and  paprika 
 2 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 1 cup sliced  green onions 
 1 cup sliced  celery 
 1 cup diced  green peppers 
 ½ pound fully-cooked smoked  sausage , cut into ¼-inch slices 
 1 cup uncooked  rice 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 ¼ teaspoon  ground red pepper 
 2 cups  chicken broth 
 Directions : 
 Season  chicken  with  salt ,  pepper  and  paprika . 
 Brown  chicken  in  oil  in large skillet over high heat; remove  chicken . 
 Add  onions ,  celery  and  peppers  to  oil ; cook 1 minute. 
 Add  chicken ,  sausage ,  rice ,  salt ,  garlic ,  black pepper , red pepper and broth; bring to a boil. 
 Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 20 to 25 minutes, or until  rice  is tender and liquid is absorbed. 
 Fluff with fork.
