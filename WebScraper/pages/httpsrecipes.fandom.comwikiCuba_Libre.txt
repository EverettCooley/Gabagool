 Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz.  dark rum 
 Cola 
 lime  or  lemon  wedge 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Pour  rum  over  ice cubes  in a collins glass. Fill with cola and squeeze  lime  or  lemon  wedge into drink and drop into glass.
