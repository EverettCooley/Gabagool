 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 14 oz can  sweetened condensed milk 
 2 teaspoons  peppermint extract 
 12 drops red food color 
 1 1/2 to 2 16-oz packages confectioners'  Sugar 
 1 1/2 cups  pecan  halves 
 Directions : 
 In large bowl, mix  sweetened condensed milk ,  peppermint extract  and food color. With spoon, stir in 1 1/2 packages confectioners'  Sugar . On cutting board generously sprinkled with confectioners'  Sugar , gradually knead in enough additional  Sugar  so that mixture forms a smooth firm ball that doesn't stick to hands or board. ( You will need alot of sugar for this part.) 
 Pat mixture into 8" X 8" square. With knife, cut into 1 inch squares. #Shape each piece into a ball and with fingers, flattened into a 2 inch patty. Top with  pecan  half. (Optional) Repeat with remaining pieces. (Keep pieces covered with plastic wrap while preparing patties) Let patties dry at least 1 hour.
