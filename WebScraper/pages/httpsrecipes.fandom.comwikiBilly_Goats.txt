Billy Goats
 
Categories: Cookies
 
 1/2 c  butter 
 1 1/2 c  brown sugar 
 3  eggs 
 1 ts  vanilla extract 
 2 c  flour 
 1 ds  salt 
 1/2 ts  baking soda 
 1 c  dates ; Chop 
 1/2 c  walnuts ; Chop 
 
Cream  butter  And  sugar  Until Light. Beat in  eggs , 1 AtA Time, And  vanilla extract . Sift Together  flour ,  salt  And Baking SodaAnd Add to  butter  Mixture. Stir in  dates  And  walnuts . Drop Rounded ts OfBatter Onto Greased Baking Sheet. Bake at 350 Until Light Brown on Top,About
 
 10 Minutes. 
 
Enjoy.
