 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs. ground  Beef , seasoned with  salt ,  pepper  and a little  poultry seasoning 
 1 can  cream  of  Chicken  soup 
 1 can  cream  of  celery  soup 
 1/2 soup can  water 
 1 pkg. frozen  Peas 
 4 large  carrots , sliced 
 5-6 medium  potatoes , peeled and cubed 
 Directions : 
 Roll meat into little balls and brown. 
 Mix soups and  water  in baking dish. 
 Add vegetables and meat. 
 Bake 350 degrees 1 - 1 1/2 hours or until vegetables are tender.
