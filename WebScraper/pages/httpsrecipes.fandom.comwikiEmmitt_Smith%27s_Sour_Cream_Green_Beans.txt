Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Dallas Cowboys Wives' Family cookbook 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds  green beans 
 1 medium  onion , thinly sliced 
 1 tbsp  parsley , chopped 
 2 tbsp melted  butter  or  margarine 
 2 tbsp  flour 
 2 tsp  lemon rind , grated 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 1 cup lite  sour cream 
 1 cup buttered  bread crumbs 
 Directions : 
 Prepare beans as usual; cook in slightly salted water until tender. 
 Drain. Set aside. 
 Sauté onions land parsley in butter. 
 Reduce heat; add flour, lemon rind, salt and pepper. 
 Stir until bubbly. 
 Combine sour cream and beans. Stir well. 
 Pour into a greased baking dish; sprinkle with bread crumbs. 
 Bake at 350°F for 20 minutes.
