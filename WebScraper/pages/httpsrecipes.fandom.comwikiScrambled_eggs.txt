 Description : 
 In Romanian: Oua jumari
 
 Ingredients : 
 For one person: 
 
 2  eggs , 
 2 tablespoons  milk , 
 1 teaspoon  butter  or  lard , 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Beat the  eggs  until foamy, mix with the  milk  and  salt  and pour into the hot  butter  or  lard  in the pan. Keep stirring lightly with the fork. 
 Do not overfry. Serve immediately.
