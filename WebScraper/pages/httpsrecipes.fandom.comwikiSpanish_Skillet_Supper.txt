 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 1 pound boneless, skinless  chicken  breast, cut into 1-inch cubes 
 2 cups hot  water 
 1 package (4.4 ounces)  Spanish rice  and sauce mix 
 2 cups Birds Eye frozen green  peas 
 Â½ teaspoon  crushed pepper flakes 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in large skillet over medium-high heat. 
 Add  chicken ; cook and stir until lightly browned, about 5 minutes. 
 Add hot  water  and  rice  and sauce mix; bring to boil. 
 Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer uncovered, 5 minutes. 
 Stir in  green peas ; increase heat to medium-high. 
 Cover and cook 5 minutes or until  peas  and  rice  are tender. 
 Sprinkle with  red pepper flakes .
