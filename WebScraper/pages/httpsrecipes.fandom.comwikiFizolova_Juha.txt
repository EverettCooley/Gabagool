Description : 
 Bean soup
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup cooked  beans 
 1 liter  water 
 ham bone  with some meat 
 few ribs  celery  and leaves 
 1  bay leaf 
 1 chopped  onion 
 1 medium cubed  potato 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 some  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Cook meat and bone in  water , add beans,  celery ,  bay leaf  and  Onion  and simmer. 
 Add  potatoes  and cook until it is cooked. 
 Season to taste. 
 Top with  parsley .
