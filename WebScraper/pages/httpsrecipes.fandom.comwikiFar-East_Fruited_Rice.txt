Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  butter  or  margarine 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 1 cup cubed cooked  ham 
 1 cup cubed cooked  chicken 
 1 cup drained  pineapple  tidbits 
 1 cup finely sliced  green pepper 
 ½ cup sliced  water chestnuts 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 2 tablespoons  soy sauce 
 ¼ teaspoon  garlic powder 
 1 teaspoon  onion powder 
 Directions : 
 Stir  butter  into hot  rice . 
 Add  ham ,  chicken ,  pineapple ,  green pepper ,  water chestnuts ,  salt ,  black pepper , soy sauce,  garlic powder  and  onion  powder. 
 Turn into buttered 1½-quart casserole. 
 Cover and bake 30 minutes at 350°F.
