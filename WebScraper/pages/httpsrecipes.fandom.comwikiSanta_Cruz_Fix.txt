Description 
 Santa Cruz Fix  from Jeremy Thomas' Bartending Guide or How to Mix Drinks—original source of recipe, out of copyright
 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 1 large teaspoonful of powdered white  sugar 
 2 dashes of  curaçao 
 the juice of a quarter of a  lemon 
 1 wine-glass of  Santa Cruz rum 
 Garnish 
 half a slice of  orange 
 small pieces of  pineapple 
 Directions 
 Take 1 large tea-spoonful of powdered white  sugar  dissolved in a little  water 
 Fill up the glass two-thirds full of shaved ice, stir well, and ornament the top with half a slice of orange and small pieces of pineapple.
