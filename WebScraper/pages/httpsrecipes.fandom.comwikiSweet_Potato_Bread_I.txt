Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Roark Estate in Grand Prairie, Texas in 1984.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  sweet potatoes 
 1¼ cups  sugar 
 3  eggs 
 ⅓ cup  oil 
 2⅓ cups  baking mix 
 2 teaspoons  cinnamon 
 ½ cup  nuts 
 ½ cup  raisins  
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients except raisins and combine until well blended. 
 Stir in raisins then bake in greased loaf pan for 55 minutes at 300°F.
