Ingredients : 
 8 ounces wide  egg noodles 
 1/2 stick  sweet butter , melted 
 1 pound pot-style  cottage cheese  (or the driest  cottage cheese  you can find) 
 3/4 cup  Raisins  (optional) 
 2  eggs 
 1/2 cup  Sugar 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 cinammon and  Sugar  to garnish 
 Directions : 
 Cook and drain noodles according to package directions. Mix noodles with  butter . 
 Beat  eggs  lightly and add  Sugar . Fold in  sour cream  and  cottage cheese . Add noodles and rest of ingredients except garnish. 
 Pour into greased casserole dish. Sprinkle with cinammon and  Sugar . 
 Bake at 350 degrees for about 75 minutes. 
 Serve warm or cold.
