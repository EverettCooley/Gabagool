Ingredients : 
         
 3  lemons , juice and rind 
 8 oz  sugar 
 3 oz  butter 
 3  eggs 
 Directions : 
 Squeeze and grate lemons. 
 In a microwave bowl put lemon juice and rind. 
 Add sugar and butter to above. 
 Cook on High power for 3 minutes. 
 Beat 3 eggs and add to mixture by beating. 
 Cook for 1 minute. 
 Beat. 
 Cook for further 1 minute. 
 Let stand until cool. 
 Spoon into jars.
