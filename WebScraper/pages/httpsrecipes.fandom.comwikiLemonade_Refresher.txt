Description : 
 This is from an Equal cookbook
 
 Makes one 8 ounce serving. 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  lemon juice 
 3 to 4 pkts.  Equal 
 ¾ cup  water 
 lemon  slice 
 Directions : 
 Combine  lemon juice  and equal. 
 Stir in  water . 
 Add  ice  cubes. 
 Garnish with  lemon  slice. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Calories per serving: 3 | traditional calories: 13
 
 Diabetic exchanges: ½ fruit.
