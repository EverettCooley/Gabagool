Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Ampere Estate in Arlington, Texas in 1981. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup  vegetable shortening 
 1½ cups  sugar   or   sugar substitute 
 1  egg 
 ¼ cup  water 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 3 cups uncooked  oatmeal 
 1 cup  flour 
 1 tablespoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  soda 
 1 cup  dates  chopped 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Beat together first 5 ingredients. 
 Sift dry ingredients together. 
 Add oatmeal and combine all together. 
 Stir in dates. 
 Drop by teaspoon on greased cookie sheet. 
 Bake at 350°F for 15 minutes.
