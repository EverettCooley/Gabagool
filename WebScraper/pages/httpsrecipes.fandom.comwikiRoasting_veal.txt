About roasting veal : 
 Regarding  veal , aside from the regular use of  onion  and a bit of  garlic ,  celery  and sausage seasoning seem to have an affinity for veal. If gently braising veal as the pot roast, after the first half hour, then add a few sticks of celery, chopped in stew-size pieces. With the veal gravy,  pasta  is a fine companion, with a sprinkle of  Parmesan cheese .
