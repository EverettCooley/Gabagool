Ingredients : 
 1 cup fine  cornmeal 
 1 cup  flour 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 1 tbs  baking powder 
 1  egg substitute  (Ener-G), prepared 
 1 cup  soymilk 
 ¼ cup  margarine , melted 
 ⅛ tsp  salt 
 dash of  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 400°F. 
 Grease a 9" square baking pan. 
 In a large bowl, mix dry ingredients. 
 In a separate bowl, beat prepared  egg substitute  with  soymilk  and  margarine . 
 Add this to dry ingredients, and stir just until moistened. 
 Bake in prepared pan 20 minutes, or until a knife inserted in center comes out clean.
