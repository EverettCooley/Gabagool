 This recipe is being posted for a request. I have not made it myself, but it was given to me by a friend who has made it and says it is very good. It has always been a favorite at her parties. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 box Club  crackers 
 1 lb thin-sliced  Bacon 
 Directions : 
 Cut  bacon  strips into thirds. 
 Break crackers along perforated lines. 
 Around each cracker, wrap a strip of the  bacon  (around the width). 
 Place on ungreased cookie sheets and bake in 200Â°F oven for 2 hours.
