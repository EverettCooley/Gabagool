Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups diagonally sliced  celery 
 1 cup sliced  onion 
 1 large  green pepper , cut into slivers 
 12 ounces peeled, de-veined raw  shrimp 
 2 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 1 x 16-ounce can fancy mixed  Chinese vegetables 
 1 x 10¾-ounce can condensed  cream of chicken  soup 
 3 tablespoons  soy sauce 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 ⅓ cup sliced  pimentos 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 1 x 3-ounce can  rice noodles   or   chow mein noodles 
 Directions : 
 Cook  celery ,  onion ,  green pepper  and  shrimp  in  oil  until vegetables are tender crisp and  Shrimp  are pink. 
 Add Chinese vegetables, soup, soy sauce,  pepper  and pimientos. Heat until hot through. 
 Serve over beds of fluffy  rice  and sprinkle with noodles.
