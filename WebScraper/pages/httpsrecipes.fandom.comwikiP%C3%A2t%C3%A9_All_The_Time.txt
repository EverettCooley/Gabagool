Description : 
 Always check the ingredients to make sure the product is vegan.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup grounded  sunflower seeds 
 ½ cup  wheat flour 
 ½ cup  yeast 
 1 large  onion 
 2 tablespoons  lemon juice 
 1 grated  potato 
 ½ cup  vegetable oil 
 1½ cups  water 
 1 pinch  basil 
 1 pinch)  thyme 
 1 pinch  sage 
 1 pinch  salt 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients. 
 Pour into pyrex casserole. 
 Cook for 1 hour at 350°F.
