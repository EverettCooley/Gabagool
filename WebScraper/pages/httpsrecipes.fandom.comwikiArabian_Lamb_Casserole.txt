 Description : 
 Serves 4-6
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tbl  oil 
 750 gm lean  lamb , cubed 
 1 pkt  Tomato  &  Vegetable Soup 
 1 cup  water 
 1 tbl dried  mint 
 1/4 cup natural  yoghurt 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  and brown  Lamb . 
 Add soup mix,  water  &  mint . 
 Simmer until  Lamb  is tender, about 30-40mins. 
 Mix  yoghurt  with 1/4 cup of the gravy, spoon over the top of the  Lamb  and serve.
