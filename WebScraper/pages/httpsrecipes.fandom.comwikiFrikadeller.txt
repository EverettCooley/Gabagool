      A batch of frikadeller     
 Prep Time: 
 Cook time: 
 Serves: 
 
 Description : 
 Frikadeller would be the Danish version of meatballs. Often served with potatoes and gravy.
 
 Ingredients : 
 500 g ground veal and pork mix 
 1 onion 
 1 egg 
 3 tbsp flour 
 1 dl (~0.5 cup) milk 
 Some salt and pepper 
 Directions : 
 Mix all the ingredients in a bowl 
 Shape them into round meatballs with a spoon and fry them on a pan
