Description : 
 In Romanian: Anghemaht de gaina
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  hen 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 1 teaspoon  flour 
 Â½  lemon 
 salt 
 1 cup of the  chicken broth  (from boiling the hen) 
 Directions : 
 Boil the  hen  pieces as for  Chicken Pilaf . 
 Fry the  flour  in  butter  until yellow, add a cup of the  chicken broth ,  salt ,  hen  pieces and the peeled and sliced  lemon . 
 Simmer until tender. 
 The sauce must be much thinner than for the  Chicken White Sauce Stew . 
 Therefore, add some more  chicken broth  if necessary.
