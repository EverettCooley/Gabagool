Description : 
 Only 15 minutes to prepare and serves 4 people. Serve with fried  black bean  paste and  tortillas .
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 green  plantains 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 oil 
 Directions : 
 Peel  plantains  and cut into Â½ inch slices. 
 Fry in hot  oil  until they turn golden. 
 Put on a board and flatten the slices with a rolling pin. 
 Sprinkle some  salt  on them and put them back in the pan, frying until crispy. 
 Drain and serve.
