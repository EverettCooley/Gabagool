Description : 
 This is really good, like spaghetti, but has bacon and basil in it. Good served with garlic bread.
 
 Contributed by [ http://groups.yahoo.com/group/catsrecipes/  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Makes 6 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tbsp  olive oil 
 6 slices thick cut smoked  bacon , cut into ¼ inch strips 
 1 medium  onion , thinly sliced 
 15 oz can  tomato purée 
 1 tbsp  red wine vinegar 
 ¼ tsp  red pepper flakes 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 1 pound  bucatini  pasta 
 ½ cup grated  romano  cheese 
 ¼ cup  basil  leaves, coarsely chopped 
 black  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. 
 Heat oil in a skillet. 
 Add bacon and cook until bacon begins to turn brown. 
 Remove bacon and reserve. 
 Add onion and cook in bacon drippings 7 min. 
 Stir in reserved bacon, tomato puree, vinegar, pepper flakes and salt. 
 Simmer 3 minutes. 
 Prepare pasta following package directions. 
 Drain pasta. 
 Toss pasta with sauce. 
 Stir in ¼ cup cheese and the basil. 
 Serve with extra cheese on the side and the black pepper to taste. Garlic bread too.
