Ingredients : 
 1Â lb. plain  yogurt , 
 1 1/2 cups cold  water , 
 1 peeled and finelly choped  cucumber , 
 2 cloves  garlic , pound up, 
 4 tbsp vegetable or  olive oil , 
 1 tsp  salt , 
 2 tbsp fresh  dill , 
 1/2 finelly chopped  walnuts  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl mix  yogurt  and  water  until smooth. 
 Add all other ingredients, stir well. Serve. 
 Makes 6-8 servings. 
 In hot summer days you can add 1 cube  ice  in each serving bowl.
