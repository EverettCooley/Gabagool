      Fish Tolcheniki     
 Ingredients : 
 800 g  fish  fillet (1 3/4 lb) 
 70 g  flour  (2/3 c) 
 80 g  oil  (1/3 c) 
 100 g  Onion  (2/3 c chopped) 
 pepper 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Chop fish fillet finely, add  flour ,  salt ,  oil  and  pepper . 
 Stir thoroughly and shape small balls. 
 Cook in salted boiling  water . 
 Chop  onion  finely and serve with tolcheniki.
