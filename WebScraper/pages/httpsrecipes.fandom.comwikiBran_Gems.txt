Description : 
 These gems are excellent for constipation.
 
 Sufficient for eight gems 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cupful  bran 
 2 tablespoonfuls  Crisco 
 1 tablespoonful  whole wheat flour 
 ½ cupl white  flour 
 ½ cup  milk 
 1 teaspoonful  salt 
 1  egg 
 2 tablespoonfuls  molasses 
 1 teaspoonful  baking powder 
 Directions : 
 First mix crisco thoroughly with  molasses , add  egg  well beaten,  milk ,  salt ,  bran , flours, and  baking powder . 
 Divide into well greased gem pans, and bake in hot oven from eight to ten minutes.
