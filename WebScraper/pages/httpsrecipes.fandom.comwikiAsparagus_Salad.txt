 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  olive oil 
 2 tbsp  white wine 
 2 tbsp  pepper sauce 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 2 bunch  asparagus , cut 2 
 1 large  leek , sliced 
 1 cup  mushrooms , sliced 
 1  bell pepper , sliced thin 
 2 tbsp  Parmesan cheese 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven 425 °F . 
 Combine  olive oil ,  wine ,  pepper sauce  and  salt  in cup. 
 Combine  asparagus ,  leek ,  mushrooms ,  bell pepper , and ½  oil  in large roasting pan; mix well. 
 Roast vegetables 20 minutes. 
 Until tender, stirring occasionally. 
 Remove from oven, toss with remaining  oil  mixture. 
 Serve warm.
