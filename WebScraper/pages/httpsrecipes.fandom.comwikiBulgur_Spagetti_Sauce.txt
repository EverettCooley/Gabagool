Ingredients : 
 water  for sauté (or your favorite, but compatible substitute) 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1 can chopped peeled  tomatoes  with liquid (14 oz) 
 1 can  tomato sauce  (8 oz) 
 1 tbsp  parsley 
 1  bay leaf 
 ½ tsp  oregano 
 ¼ tsp  sugar 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 ⅛ tsp  pepper 
 1 cup cooked  bulgur 
 Directions : 
 In 3 qt pan, sauté  onion  and  garlic . 
 Stir in  tomatoes , sauce, and spices. 
 Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer, uncovered, 20 minutes. 
 Discard  bay leaf , stir in  bulgur  and serve over your favorite  pasta , or  spaghetti squash .
