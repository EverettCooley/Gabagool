Info 
 Cook Time : 
 Serves : 6
 
 Ingredients 
 ½ head (1 pound 8 ounces)  red cabbage , shredded 
 ½  red onion , thinly sliced 
 ¼  onion , thinly sliced 
 4 tablespoons  oil 
 2 tablespoons  vinegar 
 2 tablespoons  sugar 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  black pepper 
 1 ½ cups  dried cranberries 
 Directions 
 1.Toss cabbage and onions together in a large mixing bowl.
 2.Mix oil, vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper together in a small bowl to make a dressing.
 3.Pour dressing over cabbage mixture; toss with cranberries.
 4.Marinate in refrigerator for 1 hour.
 
 Source 
 Cranberries, Sliced, Dried  by the US Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
