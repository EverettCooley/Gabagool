 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup  orange juice 
 1 1/2 cups cubed  mango 
 1 clove  garlic , crushed 
 1 tsp. chopped  cilantro 
 1/2 cup  Sugar 
 1/4 cup  green onion , chopped 
 1/4 cup  vinegar 
 2 tbsp.  lime juice 
 Tobasco sauce 
 worcestershire sauce 
 6 boneless  Chicken  breasts cut in bite size pieces 
 Directions : 
 	Marinate  Chicken  in  worcestershire sauce . Then saute in  olive oil  and set aside. Combine  Sugar ,  green onion ,  vinegar ,  lime juice  and a few drops of tobasco sauce in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly until the  Sugar  dissolves. Add  mango ,  garlic  and  cilantro , stirring gently. Mix in  orange juice . Pour hot mixture over  Chicken . Serve immediately.
