Ingredients : 
 1  Onion , Chopped 
 1/3 C.  water 
 1 Tablespoon  caraway seeds 
 2 C. Frozen Chopped Hash Browns 
 2 C. Frozen Mixed Vegetables (i Used Baby  carrots  And Baby Limas) 
 1-3/4 C.  vegetable broth 
 4 C. or About 1/2 Head of Chopped  cabbage 
 2 15-Oz. Cans  small white beans 
 Ground  black pepper  to Taste
 
 Directions : 
 Add All Ingredients Into Pressure Cooker.Mix Well, Cover, And Cook For
 
 10 Minutes. Allow Pressure to Release Naturally to Allow AdditionalCook Time For  cabbage .
