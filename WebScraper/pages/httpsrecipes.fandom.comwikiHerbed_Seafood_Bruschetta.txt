Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Source:  Diabetic Living Diet 
 Total Time: 25 minutes 
 Servings: 24 
 Ingredients : 
 24 slices  baguette 
 2 tsp  chives 
 2 tsp  basil , fresh 
 2 tsp  lemon juice 
 1 tsp  mint , fresh 
 1 tsp  olive oil 
 1 clove  garlic 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 3 oz  crabmeat , frozen 
 4 oz  shrimp , peeled and cooked 
 ¾ cup  plum tomatoes 
 ¼ cup  onion 
 ¼ tsp  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Arrange bread slices on a baking sheet. 
 Broil 3 to 4 inches from heat for 1 to 2 minutes or until toasted, turning once. 
 Cool on wire racks. 
 In a medium bowl stir together chives, basil, lemon juice, mint, oil, garlic, and salt. 
 Add crabmeat, shrimp, tomatoes, and onion; toss to coat. 
 Arrange toasted bread on a serving platter; spoon seafood mixture on each slice. 
 Sprinkle with pepper. 
 Serve immediately. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving: 
 
 25 Calories | 0g Saturated Fat | 74mg Sodium | 0g Total Fat | 3g Carbs | 0g Dietary Fiber | 13mg Cholesterol | 2g Protein 
 Exchanges: ½ lean meat
