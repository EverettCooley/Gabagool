Ingredients : 
 1 x 9-inch  graham cracker  crust 
 Filling : 
 1 packet Knox unflavored  gelatin 
 3 tbsp  lime juice 
 ½ cup boiling  water 
 9 x 1-gram packets Equal (aspartame)  sugar substitute 
 1 cup  evaporated skim milk 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 juice of 1½  limes 
 2 drop green  food coloring 
 Garnish : 
 lime zest 
 thin  lime  slices 
 Directions : 
 Sprinkle  gelatin  over  lime juice  and let it stand for i minute. 
 Add boiling  water  and sweetener to  gelatin  mixture and stir until  gelatin  is dissolved. 
 Refrigerate about 45 minutes or until slightly thickened. 
 Combine  milk  and  vanilla  and freeze 30 minutes. 
 Remove from freezer and whip at high speed until stiff. 
 Stir  lime juice  and  food coloring  into whipped  milk  (save remaining ½  lime  for garnish). 
 Slowly blend  gelatin  mixture into whipped  milk . 
 Spoon pie filling into crust. Chill until firm. 
 Garnish with  lime  zest and  lime  slices. 
 Cut into 8 even portions and serve 1 portion per serving. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving: 
 
 111 calories | 14 gm. Cholesterol | 4 gm. Protein | 5 gm. Fat | 130 mg. Sodium 
 Food exchanges: 1 bread, 1 fat 
 low-sodium diets: use salt-free crust
