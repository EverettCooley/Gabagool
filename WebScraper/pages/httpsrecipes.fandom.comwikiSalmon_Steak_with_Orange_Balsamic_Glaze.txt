 Description : 
 This recipe comes from a neighbor who served this to me a few years back. I was so impressed with the flavors I demanded the recipe. He gave it to me and it is now a mainstay in your home.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group       Salmon Steak with Orange-Balsamic Glaze     
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  orange juice  concentrate 
 ¼ cup  balsamic vinegar 
 1 tablespoon extra virgin  olive oil 
 2 cloves  garlic , finely chopped 
 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh  rosemary 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅛ teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 4  salmon  steaks 
 vegetable  cooking spray 
 Directions : 
 Place first 7 ingredients in a large, sealable plastic bag and shake well. 
 Add salmon and refrigerate for 1 hour. 
 Remove steaks from marinade and pat dry with a paper towel. 
 Coat the entire grill with cooking spray and heat on high. 
 Grill steaks turning once halfway through until no longer translucent in the center about 4 minutes per side. Serve warm and plain over white rice.
