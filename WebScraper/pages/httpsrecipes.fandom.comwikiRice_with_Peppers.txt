Description : 
 Makes 4 servings. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  red pepper , cut into strips 
 ½ cup  green pepper , cut into strips 
 ½ cup  yellow pepper , cut into strips 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 2 cups cooked  rice  (cooked in  chicken broth ) 
 2 tablespoons chopped fresh  parsley 
 ¼ teaspoon  hot pepper sauce 
 Directions : 
 Cook  red peppers ,  green peppers  and yellow  peppers  in  butter  in large skillet over medium-high heat until tender crisp. 
 Add  rice ,  parsley  and  pepper  sauce; stir until thoroughly heated.
