Ingredients : 
 8 oz fine  noodles   or   angel hair pasta  cut up into 2 inch lengths 
 4 cup finely shredded  green cabbage 
 ½ cup  vegetable broth  for sautéing 
 salt  /  pepper  /  garlic powder  to taste 
 ½ cup fat-free  sour cream   or   plain  yogurt 
 Directions : 
 Cook noodles according to package directions. 
 Drain. 
 Meanwhile, saute the  cabbage  in  vegetable broth  until tender (about 10 minutes). 
 Add cooked noodles and mix well. 
 Add  salt ,  pepper  and  garlic powder  to taste. 
 Take the pan off the heat and add the ff  sour cream  or  yogurt . 
 Serve.
