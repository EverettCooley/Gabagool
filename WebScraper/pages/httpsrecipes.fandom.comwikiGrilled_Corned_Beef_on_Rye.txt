 1 Serving 
 Ingredients : 
 2 slices Jewish  rye bread 
 4 slices deli sliced  corned beef 
 2 slices  Swiss cheese 
 1 tbsp  garlic   mayonnaise  or condiment of choice 
 Directions : 
 Spread  butter  or  margarine  on 1 side of each piece of  bread . 
 With  butter  sides out, layer  cheese , meat, condiment and  cheese  between the 2 slices of  bread . 
 Place on a non-stick cooking surface. 
 Grill to golden brown on each side. 
 Serve immediately.
