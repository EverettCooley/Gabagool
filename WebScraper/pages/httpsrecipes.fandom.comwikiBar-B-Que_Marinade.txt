 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups Kikkoman  soy sauce 
 2 cups  Sugar 
 2 tsp minced  ginger 
 1  Onion , diced 
 1 clove  garlic , crushed 
 black pepper 
 crushed red pepper 
 2-3 lbs  Beef  brisket 
 Directions : 
 Boil  soy sauce ,  Sugar  and  ginger  until  Sugar  is dissolved. Be careful not to let mixture boil over. 
 Remove from heat. While mixture is cooling off, add remaining ingredients. 
 Marinate  Beef  brisket for at least 1–2 hours (longer is better).  Beef  can be substituted with  Chicken  or  Pork .
