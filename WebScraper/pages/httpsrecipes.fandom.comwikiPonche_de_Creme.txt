Description : 
 Traditional Christmas time beverage
 
 Recipe By: Naparima Girl's High School Cookbook 
 Ingredients : 
 6  eggs 
 3 tins  evaporated milk  (large low-fat) 
 1½ tins  condensed milk 
 ½ cup  rum 
 1 teaspoon  Angostura bitters 
 grated  nutmeg 
 zest  of one  lime 
 Directions : 
 Beat eggs with lime peel until light and fluffy 
 Add evaporated milk 
 Sweeten to taste with condensed milk. 
 Add bitters, grated nutmeg, and rum according to taste. 
 Strain 
 serve with crushed ice.
