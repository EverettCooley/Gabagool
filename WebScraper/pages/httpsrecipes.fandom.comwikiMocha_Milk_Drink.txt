Description : 
 Contributed by Ursula Taylor
 
 Yield: 1 serving of 8 oz 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tbsp  syrup 
 1 tsp  instant coffee powder 
 1 cup (8 oz)  skim milk 
 Directions : 
 Measure syrup into a tall glass, add instant  coffee  powder, and pour  milk  on top slowly, stirring vigorously to blend well. 
 If you have a blender, measure ingredients into blender, cover, and mix at low speed for about 30 seconds. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 Calories 100 | cho 14g | pro 9g | fat 1g | fiber 1.1g | sodium 152mg | chol 4mg 
 Food exchanges: 1 skim milk 
 Low sodium diets: omit  salt  from  syrup  recipe.
