Description : 
 Shish kebab
 
 Ingredients : 
 300 g (10 oz) boneless  lamb , cut into 4 cm cubes 
 1 medium  onion , cut into quarters 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 pinch of  black pepper 
 6  cherry tomatoes 
 1  red pepper , cored and sliced 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 1 teaspoon  garam masala 
 juice of 1  lemon 
 Directions : 
 Prepare the marinade by placing  lemon juice ,  olive oil ,  garam masala ,  salt  and  pepper  in a medium bowl, mix well. 
 Next add the  lamb  cubes, cover and leave to marinate for 1 hour. 
 Arrange  lamb  cubes on skewers, alternating with  pepper  slices,  onions  and  tomatoes . 
 Place under a hot grill or over hot coal and cook for 10 â€“ 15 minutes turning once, or twice until meat is cooked.
