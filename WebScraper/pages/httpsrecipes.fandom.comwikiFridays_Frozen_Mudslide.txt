Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz Absolut®  vodka 
 1½ oz  Bailey's® Irish Cream 
 1½ oz  Kahlúa ®  coffee liqueur 
 8  ice  cubes 
 1½ oz  cream 
 1 scoop Haagen-Dazs® vanilla  ice cream 
 2 scoops Haagen-Dazs® chocolate  ice cream 
 Directions : 
 Begin by crushing the 8  ice  cubes in a blender. 
 When  ice  is finely crushed, add the remaining ingredients and blend for 45 seconds on blender's highest setting. 
 Pour into glass and serve immediately.
