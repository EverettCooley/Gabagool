Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup chopped  green bell pepper 
 1 x 2½-ounce can sliced  mushrooms  (drain; reserve liquid) 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 1 x 10¾-ounce can condensed  cream of chicken  soup 
 1½ cups boned, cooked  turkey  or  chicken  (large pieces) 
 1 cup diced cooked  ham 
 2 tablespoons diced  pimento 
 ⅓ cup  milk 
 2 tablespoons dry  sherry  (optional) 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Stir-fry  pepper  and  mushrooms  in  butter  in large skillet over medium heat until tender crisp. 
 Stir in soup,  turkey ,  ham ,  pimento ,  milk  and liquid from  mushrooms ; heat thoroughly. 
 Add  sherry . 
 Serve over hot  rice .
