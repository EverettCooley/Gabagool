Note: This recipe is quick, easy, healthy, and delicious! 
 
 submitted by: Betty Jean Nichols 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups fresh,  sweet cherries 
 1 cup fresh  blueberries 
 1 cup fresh  raspberries  (or other  berries ) 
 ⅓ cup  granulated sugar 
 1 tbsp  all-purpose flour 
 1 tbsp finely chopped  crystallized ginger 
 5 cups soft whole  wheat  bread cubes, divided 
 3 tbsp  butter , melted 
 sweetened  whipped cream  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 375 °F. 
 Lightly grease a 2-quart square baking dish; set aside. 
 Rinse  cherries ; remove stones, cut each  cherry  in half. 
 Rinse  blueberries ; remove any stems. 
 In a large bowl, combine  sugar ,  flour , and  ginger . 
 Add  cherries ,  blueberries , and  raspberries  (or other  berries ). 
 Toss to coat. 
 Add 2 cups of the bread cubes; toss gently until well-combined. 
 Transfer mixture to prepared baking dish. 
 Place remaining 3 cups of bread cubes in a medium bowl. 
 Drizzle with melted  butter ; toss to coat. 
 Sprinkle over cherry-berry mixture. 
 Bake about 30 minutes, or until topping is golden. 
 Serve warm. 
 Garnish each serving with  whipped cream , if desired.
