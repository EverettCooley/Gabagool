Great tasting Dip for vegetables (i.e.  celery ,  carrots ) or grilled meat.
 
       Cucumber Dip     
 Ingredients : 
 2 seedless  cucumber 
 1 bunch  dill 
 1 bunch  mint  leaves 
 21 ounces  yoghurt 
 2 tablespoons olive  oil 
 2 cloves  garlic 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 2 tablespoons  water 
 Sugar 
 Directions : 
 Peel  cucumbers . Use grater to shred  cucumbers . 
 Add  salt  and let sit 15 minutes. 
 Set a few  dill  sprigs aside for garnish. Chop the rest and the  mint  (small). 
 Mince  garlic . 
 Add olive  oil ,  garlic , and  water  to  yoghurt . Blend til smooth. 
 Knead the  cucumber  to remove excess  water . 
 Add  cucumber ,  dill  and  mint  to  yoghurt . blend. 
 Add  salt  and  Sugar  to taste. 
 Garnish with  dill  sprigs.
