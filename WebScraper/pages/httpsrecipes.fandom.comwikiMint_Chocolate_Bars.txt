Directions : 
 Cookie layer 
 1 c.  flour 
 1 c.  sugar 
 1/2 c.  butter , softened 
 4  eggs 
 1 1/2 c.  Hershey's Syrup 
 Mint Cream layer 
 2 c.  confectioners' sugar 
 1/2 c.  butter , softened 
 1 tbsp.  water 
 1/2 tsp.  mint extract 
 3 drops green  Food coloring 
 Chocolate Topping 
 6 tbsp. butter 
 1 cup Hershey's mint chocolate chips 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 350 degrees. Grease rectangular pan 13x9x2 inch. 
 In mixing bowl, beat flour, sugar, butter, eggs and syrup until smooth. 
 Pour into prepared pan. 
 Bake 30 minutes or until top springs back when touched. 
 Cool in pan. 
 Beat until smooth. 
 In a small bowl combine mint cream layer ingredients 
 Spread mint cream over cookie base. 
 Chill. 
 In a small microwave safe bowl, melt butter and mint chocolate chips on high for 1 - 1 1/2 minutes or until chips are just melted 
 Stir until mixture is smooth 
 Pour chocolate topping over dessert. 
 Cover and chill again.
