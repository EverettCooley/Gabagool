Description 
 Soup, Tomato, Canned, Condensed  by the US Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Serves : 32 
 Ingredients 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 1 cup  oil 
 2 teaspoons  salt 
 1 teaspoon  paprika 
 1 can condensed  tomato soup , undiluted 
 1½ cups  vinegar 
 1 large  onion , grated 
 1 teaspoon  Worcestershire sauce 
 ¼ teaspoon  pepper 
 Directions 
 Combine ingredients. 
 Shake or whisk until well blended. 
 Store in a tightly covered container in the refrigerator.
