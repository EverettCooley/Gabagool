 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 500g  flour , 
 1tsp  yeast , 
 150g white sheep's  Cheese , 
 120g  butter , 
 2 tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Make a hole in the  flour  and place 1/2 tsp  salt , the diluted  yeast  and 1/2 a cupful of  water  in it. Knead a medium soft dough (add some more  water  if necessary) and let stand for about on hour. 
 Roll into sheets, ~3-4mm thick cut into squares and add crumbled  Cheese  to each. 
 Fold in two, seal the edges and drop into  salt  boiling  water . 
 Boil for ~5min. 
 Arrange the ears is a dish and pour over melted  butter . 
 Serve hot.
