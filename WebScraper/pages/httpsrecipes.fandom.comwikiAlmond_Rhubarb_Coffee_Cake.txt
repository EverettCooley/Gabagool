 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 c  flour 
 3 ts  baking powder 
 2 tb Suger 
 ds  salt 
 1/3 c  butter  or  margarine 
 1 c  brown sugar , firmly packed 
 1  egg 
 1 c  milk 
 1 ts  vanilla 
 1 1/2 c  rhubarb , finely chopped 
 1/2 c  almonds , sliced/slivered 
 Topping : 
 1/2 c  Sugar 
 1 tb  butter  or  margarine 
 1/2 c  almonds , slivered/sliced 
 Directions : 
 Combine  flour , baging powder,  Sugar  and dash  salt . 
 Cut in  butter  until mixture is grainy and has lumps teh size of  Peas . 
 Add  brown sugar ,  egg ,  milk  and  vanilla . Beat hard until well mixed. 
 Stir in  rhubarb  and 1/2 c  almonds . Pour into greased 9x13" baking pan. 
 Combine topping ingredients and sprinkle over batter. 
 Bake at 350 F. for 30 minutes.
