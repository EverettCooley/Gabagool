Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Appetizers, Southern Style 
 Makes 1 quart 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can frozen limeade concentrate (6 oz) 
 6 oz  tequila 
 2 oz  triple sec 
 ice 
 lemon  slices 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Combine limeade, tequila, and triple sec in blender. Add ice to fill the blender. 
 Blend until smooth. 
 Store in freezer. 
 To serve, rub a lemon slice around the rim of a stemmed glass and dip in salt. 
 Fill with frozen mixture. 
 Can be made ahead of time and frozen.
