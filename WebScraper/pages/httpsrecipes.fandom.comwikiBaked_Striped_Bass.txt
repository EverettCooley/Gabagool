Description : 
 Contributed by Jenn B aka Mom2sam and Tiny at  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Yield: 4 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 4 (6-ounce)  striped bass  fillets (½ inch thick) 
 cooking spray 
 2 tablespoons light  mayonnaise 
 1 teaspoon  white wine 
 Worcestershire sauce 
 ½ teaspoon  Old Bay seasoning 
 ½ teaspoon  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 450°F. 
 Place fish in an 8-inch square baking dish coated with  cooking spray . 
 Combine  mayonnaise  and remaining 3 ingredients, stirring well. 
 Brush  mayonnaise  mixture evenly over fish. 
 Bake, uncovered, at 450°F for 8 to 10 minutes or until fish flakes easily when tested with a fork. 
 Transfer to a serving platter, and serve immediately.
