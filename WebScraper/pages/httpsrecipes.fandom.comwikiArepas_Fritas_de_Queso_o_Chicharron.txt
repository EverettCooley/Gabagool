Description : 
 Fried  corn  cakes from Antioquia with  cheese  or  pork  rind
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x  Basic arepa dough recipe 
 1 cup grated white cheese( Muenster ,  Paisa , or semisoft white cheese will work well), or 1 cup ground pork rind ( chicharrones ) 
 Directions : 
 Knead dough with  cheese  or chicharrones. 
 Shape into 4-inch discs and deep-fry in hot  oil  until crispy. 
 Drain on paper towels and serve hot.
