 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 Unbaked pizza dough shell, 12-inch 
 1 Tbsp  olive oil 
 ½ tsp Dried  basil 
 2/3 Cups Canadian  Bacon , cut in 1/4-inch dice 
 1  California avocado 
 1 Cup  tomato , cut in 1/4-inch dice 
 salt  - as needed 
 ¾ Cup Monterrey  jack cheese , shredded 
 Directions : 
 METHOD
 Brush dough with  olive oil ; sprinkle with  basil .
 Scatter, one at a time,  Bacon ,  avocado  and  tomato  over dough. Lightly sprinkle with  salt .
 Sprinkle  Cheese  over pizza.
 Bake at 500 °F until lightly browned, about 10 minutes.
 Cut into 8 slices.
