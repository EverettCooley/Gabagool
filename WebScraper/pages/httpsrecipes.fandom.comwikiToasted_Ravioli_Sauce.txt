Description : 
 This is a dip we made for our toasted  ravioli  one night, inspired by the great dip by chef Miller. Use as a dip with toasted  ravioli ,  French bread , or as a  pasta  sauce.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (7 ounces) jar  roasted red peppers , rinsed and drained 
 ½ cup  cream cheese 
 2  green onions , chopped 
 1 clove  garlic , chopped 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 2 cups  tomato sauce 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 2 tablespoons Frank's red  hot sauce 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in blender and pulse until smooth. 
 Heat on the stove top until hot.
