Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Recipes and Reminiscences of New Orleans II 
 Makes about 6 (8 oz) jars. 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup  bell pepper , ground 
 6½ cups  sugar 
 ¼ cup  hot red peppers , ground 
 1½ cups  vinegar 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 1 bottle  Certo  (2 packs) 
 Directions : 
 Grind pepper, keep juice. 
 Put juice, sugar, salt and vinegar in kettle. 
 Bring to boil. 
 Boil and stir 5 minutes. 
 Let cool 10 minutes. 
 Add Certo. 
 Pour into jars. 
 After 10 minutes, turn upside down for five minutes to distribute seeds evenly.
