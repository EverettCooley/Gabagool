 Ingredients : 
 1 bunch of  collard greens  (or  kale ) 
 1 tablespoon of  oil 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Rinse leaves. 
 Stripe from stems and coarsely chop leaves. 
 Heat  oil  in a large skillet over medium heat. 
 Add  collards  and  salt . 
 Cook until wilted (about 4 minutes).
