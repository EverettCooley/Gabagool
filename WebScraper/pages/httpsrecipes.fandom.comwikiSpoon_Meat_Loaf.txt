Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Seems Like I Done It This A-Way 
 Serves 8 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  hamburger meat 
 1 large  onion , diced 
 2 tbsp  green pepper , diced 
 4 cups cooked elbow  macaroni 
 1 can  tomato sauce  or  tomatoes 
 1 cup grated  American cheese 
 Directions : 
 Place hamburger in skillet. 
 Add chopped onion and pepper. 
 Brown slowly until partially done. 
 Drain off fat. 
 Place a thin layer of cooked macaroni, well drained, in the bottom of a baking dish or pan; put a layer of meat mixture over macaroni, then a layer of tomatoes or sauce. 
 Sprinkle a layer of grated cheese over this. 
 Repeat layers. 
 Bake at 350Â°F for about 35 to 45 minutes.
