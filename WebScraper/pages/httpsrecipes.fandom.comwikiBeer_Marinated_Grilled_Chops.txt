beer  - Grilled Chops
 
 4 Boneless  Pork  Loin Chops, Trimmed, About 1 Lb. 
 
Marinade:
 
 1/4 C.  soy sauce 
 1 C.  beer , at Room Temperature 
 2 Tbsp.  brown sugar 
 2 Tsp. Grated Fresh  gingerroot 
 
Combine Marinade Ingredients Together Well; PlaceChops in Plastic Bag And Pour Marinade Over, Seal BagAnd Refrigerate 4-24 Hours. Prepare Medium-Hot CoalsIn Grill. Remove Chops From Marinade; Grill Over
 Medium-Hot Coals 7-8 Minutes Per Side, Turning Once.You May Use Chops With Bone Also. Serves 4.
 
Enjoy.
