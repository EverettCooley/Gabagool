Ingredients : 
 1 large can  evaporated milk 
 ¼ lbs  butter 
 4 cup  sugar 
 2 packs  chocolate  pieces (6 oz ea) 
 ½ jar  marshmallow cream 
 Directions : 
 Combine first three ingredients in saucepan and cook to 234°F or until it forms a soft ball in cold  water . 
 Stir often. 
 Remove from heat and fold in remaining ingredients. 
 If desired add chopped  nuts . 
 Pour into greased 8 x 13 inch pan. 
 Refrigerate until firm. 
 Cut into squares.
