Grapefruit Fresh  by the US Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Ingredients 
 ¼ cup  honey 
 ¼ cup frozen limeade concentrate, thawed 
 2 teaspoons  poppy seed , optional 
 1  grapefruit , peeled and sectioned (membranes removed) 
 5 cups cut up fruit ( strawberries ,  cantaloupe ,  honeydew ,  pineapple , etc.) 
 ¼ cup  walnuts , chopped and toasted 
 Directions 
 Mix honey, limeade concentrate, and poppy seed in medium bowl. 
 Carefully toss fruit with honey mixture. 
 Sprinkle with walnuts.
