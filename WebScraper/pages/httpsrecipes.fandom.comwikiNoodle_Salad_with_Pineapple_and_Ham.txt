Description : 
 From:  Salad Recipes 
 
 Serves 6 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup of  cream 
 1 cup of  mayonnaise 
 1 can of  pineapple 
 ¼ cup of  ham 
 ¼ of a package  cream cheese , softened 
 salt  and  pepper 
 1 package of  noodles 
 Preparation : 
 Cook the noodles al dente. 
 Dice the pineapple and ham. 
 Mix all the ingredients.
