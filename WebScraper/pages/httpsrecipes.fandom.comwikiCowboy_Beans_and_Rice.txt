Description : 
 Makes 4 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 1 medium  Onion , chopped 
 1 medium green  bell pepper , chopped 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 2 15- to 16-ounce cans  pinto beans , drained and rinsed 
 3/4 cup barbecue sauce 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in large skillet over medium-high heat until hot. 
 Add  Onion  and  green pepper , cook and stir 3 to 5 minutes or until tender. 
 Add  rice , beans and barbecue sauce. 
 Simmer 5 to 7 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
