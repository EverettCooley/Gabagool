Ingredients : 
 3  tortillas , choose your flavor 
 2 cups  hummus 
 1 cup very thick  salsa 
 1 bunch chopped greens ( romaine ,  spinach ,  arugula ,  fennel , etc.) 
 olive oil 
 juice of 1  lemon 
 Directions : 
 Toss greens with a little  olive oil , then stir in the juice of  lemon . 
 Stir together hummus and salsa or leave separate. 
 Place tortilla on plate. 
 Top with â…“ hummus, salsa, and greens. 
 Repeat twice, making a stack. 
 Garnish with salsa or  radishes  or olives. 
 Cut into 4 pieces.
