 Ingredients : 
 8 cups  cabbage 
 ¼ cup shredded  carrot 
 1  onion 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 ⅛ tsp  pepper 
 ¼ cup  milk 
 1 cup  mayo 
 ¼ cup  buttermilk 
 ¼ cup  vinegar 
 2½ tbsp  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Combine sugar, salt, pepper, milk, mayo, buttermilk, vinegar and lemon jucie in large bowl and beat until smooth. 
 Add cabbage, carrots and onion and mix well.
