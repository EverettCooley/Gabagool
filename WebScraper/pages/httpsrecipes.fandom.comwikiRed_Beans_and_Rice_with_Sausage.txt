Description : 
 Makes 4 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound smoked  sausage , sliced into ½-inch thick slices 
 1 x 14½-ounce can stewed  tomatoes 
 1 x 15- to 16-ounce can  red beans , drained and rinsed 
 ¼ teaspoon  ground red pepper 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Heat large skillet over medium-high heat until hot. 
 Add  sausage ; cook and stir 3 to 5 minutes or until lightly brown. 
 Add  tomatoes ,  red beans  and red pepper. 
 Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer 7 to 10 minutes. 
 Stir in  rice ; cook and stir 2 to 3 minutes more or until thoroughly heated.
