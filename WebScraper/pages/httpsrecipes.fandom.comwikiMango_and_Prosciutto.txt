Ingredients : 
 16 fresh  watercress  sprigs (or  arugula ) 
 16  mango  cubes, 1 inch 
 8  prosciutto  slices, thin, halved 
 Directions : 
 Arrange a watercress sprig on each  mango  cube, wrap with a prosciutto slice. 
 Secure gently with a wooden pick. 
 Cover and chill.
