Description : 
 This is a recipe for vegetarian meal pancakes
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 Dry ingredients : 
 1¼ cups  whole wheat flour 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 2 tsp  baking powder 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 Wet ingredients : 
 1 tbsp  oil 
 1¼ cups  water   or  ½ cup  soymilk  and ¾ cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Sift together the dry ingredients mix together the wet ingredients. 
 Make a hole in the centre of the dry ingredients. 
 Pour the wet mixture into the dry and mix -- only until ingredients are blended. 
 Heat your non-stick griddle to a medium high temperature (cook on one side for 2 minutes, then flip and cook 1 more minute on the other side). 
 Serve hot.
