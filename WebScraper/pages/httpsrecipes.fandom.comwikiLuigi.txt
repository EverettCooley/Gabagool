Ingredients : 
 1½ oz  gin 
 1½ oz  dry vermouth 
 ½ tsp  Cointreau ®  orange liqueur 
 1 tsp  Grenadine  syrup 
 1 oz  mandarin juice 
 Directions : 
 Shake and strain into a cocktail glass. 
 Garnish with a  cherry , and serve.
