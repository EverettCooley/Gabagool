 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 shredded white  cabbage  leaves 
 1 finely chopped  Onion 
 1 tb.  oil 
 1-2 tablespoons  lemon juice 
 3-5 crushed  garlic  cloves 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Sprinkle the  salt  onto the  cabbage  and  Onion  and rub well. Rinse and drain. 
 Mix  oil ,  lemon juice ,  garlic  and  salt  and pour over the cabbage- Onion  mix. Toss well.
