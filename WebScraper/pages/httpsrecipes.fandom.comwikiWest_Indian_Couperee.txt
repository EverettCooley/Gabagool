West Indian Couperee  from Jerry Thomas' Bartending Guide or How to Mix Drink—original source of recipe, out of copyright
 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ pony-glass of  brandy 
 1 pony-glass  Maraschino  or  Curaçao 
 vanilla  ice cream 
 nutmeg 
 Directions : 
 Fill the glass one-third full of vanilla  ice cream . 
 Mix thoroughly, and fill the glass nearly full with plain soda. 
 Grate a little  nutmeg  on top, and serve.
