Chewy  chocolate chip  Brownies
 
 2 c (12 Oz) Semi-Sweet  chocolate chips 
 2/3 c  all-purpose flour 
 ? t  baking powder 
 
 1/3 c  butter  or  margarine , Softened 
 2  eggs 
 ? c  sugar 
 
Heat Oven to 350? F. Spray an 8-Inch Square Baking Pan With Non-StickCooking Spray; Set Aside. Place 1 c of Semi-Sweet  chocolate chips  Into aSmall Microwave-Safe Bowl And Microwave on High Power For 2 Minutes; SetAside. Combine  flour  And  baking powder  in Measuring Cup; Set Aside. CombineButter And  sugar  in a Large Mixing Bowl And Beat on High Speed With AnElectric Mixer Until Fluffy (about 2 Minutes). Add  eggs  to  butter  MixtureAnd Beat For 1 Minute on Medium Speed. Slowly Add Melted  chocolate chips  ToBatter And Beat For 1 Minute on Slow Speed. Slowly Add  flour  Mixture ToBatter on Slow Speed And Beat For 1minute. Stir in Remaining ChocolateChips. Pour Batter Into Prepared Baking Pan And Bake For 35 Minutes. CoolBefore Serving.

Makes: 16 Two-Inch Brownies
