 Description : 
 In Romanian: Coltunasi umpluti cu carne cruda
 
 Ingredients : 
 dumpling dough made from the same quantities and in the same way as for  Cottage Cheese Dumplings ; 
 Filling : 
 10 oz / 300 g sirloin 
 1 big  onion 
 1 tablespoon fat 
 pepper 
 salt 
 2 tablespoons fat for scalding dumplings 
 Directions : 
 Grind the meat, add  salt ,  pepper  and the finely chopped  onion  that was previously fried in a tablespoon of  lard . 
 Mix everything well. 
 Use this as filling for the dumplings. 
 Boil in  salt   water , rinse in warm  water  and scald with hot  lard . 
 Serve hot.
