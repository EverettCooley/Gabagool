Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Herman and Zina Patterson 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  chocolate chips  (6 oz) 
 ½ cup  coconut 
 ½ cup salted  peanuts , chopped 
 1 cup  Rice Krispies 
 Directions : 
 Melt chocolate chips. 
 Remove from heat. 
 Add remaining ingredients and stir until well coated with chocolate. 
 Drop by teaspoonfuls onto waxed paper or greased baking sheet. 
 Let cool to harden.
