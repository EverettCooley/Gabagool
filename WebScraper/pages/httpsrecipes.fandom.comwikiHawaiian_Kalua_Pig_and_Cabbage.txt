Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 – 3 lb  pork butt  (or pork shoulder) 
 3 tbsp  liquid smoke  (or to taste) 
 3 tbsp  Hawaiian  or  kosher salt  (to taste) 
 Directions : 
 Water to fill crockpot ½ way with pork inside. 
 Cook 45 minutes per pound at 300 degrees. 
 Remove pork and shred with fork. 
 Place cut up cabbage in broth and cook on high until tender to liking. 
 Put all together and serve.
