Ingredients : 
 1 cup  pineapple 
 1 cup  apple 
 1 cup  blueberries 
 1 cup  oranges  (without skins) 
 1 cup  strawberries 
 Directions : 
 Put all ingredients into a big bowl. 
 Mix. 
 Enjoy!
