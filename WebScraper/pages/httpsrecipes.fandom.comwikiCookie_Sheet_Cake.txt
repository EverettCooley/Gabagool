Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Servings: 15 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  flour 
 2 cups  sugar 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 stick  margarine 
 ½ cup  oil 
 1 cup  water 
 3 tbsp  cocoa 
 2  eggs 
 ½ cup  buttermilk 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 Icing : 
 3 tbsp  cocoa 
 1 stick  margarine 
 1 lb  confectioner's sugar  — (1 box) 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 6 tbsp  milk 
 ½ cup  nuts 
 Directions : 
 Cake : 
 In large bowl, combine  flour ,  sugar , and  salt . 
 Mix well. 
 In saucepan, add  margarine ,  oil ,  water , and  cocoa . 
 Bring to a boil and add to  flour  mixture; stir well. 
 Add  eggs ,  buttermilk , and  baking soda . 
 Mix well. 
 Pour on ungreased cookie sheet and bake 20 minutes at 350°F. 
 Icing : 
 Mix all ingredients until smooth. 
 Frost cake as soon as it is taken out of oven.
