 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cloves  garlic , crushed 
 3 Tb. Extra-virgin  olive oil 
 1 Ts.  paprika 
 a Ts.  cumin 
 a Ts.  ground ginger 
 Serious pinch of  Cayenne  or ground  chili pepper 
 ˝ lb. Large fresh or frozen  Shrimp  (if frozen, thaw before using) 
 salt 
 2-3 Tb chopped  cilantro  or  parsley 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Sauté the  garlic  in the  oil  until fragrant. 
 Add the spices & stir gently. 
 Add in the  Shrimp , then season with  salt , then add the  cilantro  or  parsley . 
 Fry quickly, stirring constantly, for about 5 minutes or until the  Shrimp  turn pink.
