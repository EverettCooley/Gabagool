 Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 1 serving 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup hot  coffee 
 1 tbsp  crème de cacao 
 1 tbsp  brandy 
 whipping cream 
 ground  cinnamon  or  nutmeg 
 Directions : 
 Pour coffee into a cup or mug. 
 Stir in crème de cacao and brandy. 
 Top with a dollop of whipped cream; sprinkle with cinnamon or nutmeg. 
 Variations : 
 Café Carrie : Follow directions for Café Alexander, substituting 1 tbsp  coffee liqueur  and 1 tbsp  rum  for  crème de cacao  and  brandy . 
 Café Dublinv : Follow above directions, substitute 1 tbsp  Irish whiskey  and 2 tsp  sugar  for crème de cacao and brandy 
 Café Holland : Follow above directions, substitute 2 tbsp  chocolate mint liqueur  for crème de cacao and brandy 
 Café Israel : Follow above directions, substituting 2 tbsp  chocolate flavored syrup  and 2 tbsp  orange liqueur  for crème de cacao and brandy 
 Café Noix : Follow above directions, substituting 2 tbsp  amaretto  or  frangelico  for  crème de cacao  and  brandy .
