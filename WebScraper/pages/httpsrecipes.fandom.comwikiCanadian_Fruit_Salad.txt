Description : 
 This is a great healthy fruit salad like many, but each has its own taste and uniqueness.
 Enjoy this easy to make saladÂ ! 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 navel  oranges 
 2 - 3  apples 
 2  bananas 
 walnuts 
 sweet  cream 
 sugar  or replacement 
 lemon juice 
 1  kiwifruit , optional 
 diced  pineapple , optional 
 Directions : 
 Pare and cut fruits in little pieces. 
 Crack  walnuts  and put them all in a bowl. 
 Add a little bit of sweet  cream ,  sugar  and  lemon juice  depending on taste.
