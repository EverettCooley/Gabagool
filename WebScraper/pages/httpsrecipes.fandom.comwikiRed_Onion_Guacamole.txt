Makes 2 Cups
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 avocados 
 ½ red onion, finely chopped 
 3 tbs lime juice 
 2 tbs chopped cilantro 
 ¼ tsp each salt and hot pepper sauce 
 Directions : 
 Cut avocados in half, remove pits. 
 Scoop out flesh in a bowl, mash avocado flesh. 
 Stir in onion, lime juice, cilantro, salt and hot pepper sauce. 
 Press plastic wrap directly onto surface and refrigerate until ready to use.
