 Ingredients : 
 3 jars baby food —  beef  or  carrots 
 ¼ cup  cream of wheat 
 chicken 
 ¼ cup  dry milk powder 
 Directions : 
 Combine ingredients in bowl and mix well. 
 Roll into small balls and place on well-greased cookie sheet. 
 Flatten slightly with a fork. 
 Bake in preheated 350°F oven for 15 minutes until brown. 
 Cool on wire racks and store in refrigerator. 
 Also freezes well. 
 Another prodigy member made these substituting  cream of wheat  for  wheat germ  and then used tablespoon to make cookie sized drops on plate. 
 Microwaved for 4 minutes on medium high. 
 Suggested keeping an eye on them in microwave and perhaps start out for only 3½ minutes. 
 They do come out soft. 
 Also suggested using  beef /vegetable dinner baby dinner instead of  beef  or  chicken .
