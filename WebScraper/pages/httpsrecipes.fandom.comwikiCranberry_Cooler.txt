      Cranberry Cooler     
 Ingredients : 
 1 shot Disaronno  amaretto 
 1 shot Stolichnaya  vodka 
 3 shots  cranberry juice 
 2 shots  orange juice 
 garnish:  lime  squeeze 
 glass: highball 
 Directions : 
 Build over cubed  ice .
