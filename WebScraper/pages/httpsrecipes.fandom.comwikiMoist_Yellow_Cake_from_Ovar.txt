 Description : 
 this one is very yummy!!! this is very moist and it's eaten with a spoon. it looks like a yellow cake with sweet yellow paste...
 
 Ingredients : 
 18 egg yolks 
 4 egg whites 
 250gr of sugar 
 60gr of wheat flour 
 greaseproof paper 
 Description : 
 Mix the sugar and the egg yolks and beat them for 30 minutes. 
 Mix the flour and the egg white until well beaten ( they are well beaten when you turn the bowl upside down and it doesn't fall). When mixing the egg whites do not use the mixer. l 
 Let cook for almost 30 minutes in a cake tin without hole covered with greaseproof paper at medium temp.
