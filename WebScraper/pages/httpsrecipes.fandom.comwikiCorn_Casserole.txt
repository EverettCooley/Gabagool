Desciption : 
 Corn casserole - universally known as the greatest of the casserole family
 This Recipe Came From an Estate Sale from The Steep Estate in Elmo, Texas in 1989.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  butter , melted 
 15 ounce can whole kernel  corn , undrained 
 15 ounce can creamed  corn 
 8½ ounce box  corn  muffin mix 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together in a casserole dish 
 Bake for 1 hour at 350 °F
