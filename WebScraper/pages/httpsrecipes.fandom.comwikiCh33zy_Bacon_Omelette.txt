 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 10  eggs  (large grade a) 
 1/4 cups grated  Cheddar   Cheese 
 1/4 cup sliced  Muenster   Cheese 
 1/4 cup grated nacho  Cheese 
 1/3 cup (optional)spicy  Cheese 
 3/4 cup  Bacon  (cook to your desired tendency) 
 2 t  butter 
 salt 
 pepper 
 Directions : 
 prepare  Bacon  to desired tenderness, place on paper towel and put another sheet on top of it.crack all  eggs  into a bowl, stir w/ a fork, break  Bacon  into little pieces. add  Bacon  and  Cheese  in with the  eggs , mix thouroughly.greese skillet w/  butter . pour in a little bit of mixture at a time. make one omelette at a time.
