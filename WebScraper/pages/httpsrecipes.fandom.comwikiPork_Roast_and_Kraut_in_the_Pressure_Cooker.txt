Pork, Apples & Sauerkraut for Pressur Cooker
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 lb boneless pork roast 
 Crisco or vegetable oil for browning roast 
 chopped onion 
 2 cups chicken broth 
 1 bag of sauerkraut drained and rinsed 
 Directions : 
 Sprinkle steak seasoning on pork roast. 
 Brown pork in pressure cooker. 
 Add all remaining ingredients. 
 Cook under high pressure for 45 to 50 minutes. 
 Let pressure release naturally. 
 Cut pork and serve the sauerkraut
