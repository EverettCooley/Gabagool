Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  ground chuck 
 1  egg 
 1 cup chopped  bell pepper 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 1 tbsp  salt 
 1 cup  cracker  crumbs 
 1 pkg. brown  gravy  mix 
 1 cup  milk 
 3 – 4 small  potatoes , quartered 
 Directions : 
 Mix together all ingredients, except  potatoes . 
 Shape into a loaf. 
 Place in crock pot. 
 Quarter  potatoes  and arrange at the sides of meat loaf. 
 Cook on high 4 – 5 hours or low 8 – 10 hours.
