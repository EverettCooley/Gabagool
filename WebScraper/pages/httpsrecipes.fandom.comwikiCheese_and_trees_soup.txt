 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 	tbsp 	 butter  or  margarine 
 1 	cup 	Chopped  Onion 
 1 1/3 	lbs 	(4 medium)  potatoes 
 -- cut into Â¾-inch cubes 
 2 1/2 	cup 	 water 
 2 		 Chicken  bouillon cubes 
 10 	oz 	Frozen chopped  Broccoli 
 -- thawed and drained 
 1 1/2 	cup 	Shredded  Cheddar   Cheese 
 salt  and  pepper , to taste 
 Directions : 
 1 	In 2- to 3-quart saucepan over medium heat, melt  butter .
 2 	Add  Onion ; saute 5 minutes.
 3 	Add  potatoes ,  water  and bouillon cubes.
 4 	Bring to boil, reduce heat, cover and cook until  potatoes  are just tender, about 15 minutes.
 5 	Remove 1 cup of the  Potato  cubes with slotted spoon; set aside.
 6 	Pour contents of saucepan into container of electric blender.
 7 	Holding lid down tightly, blend until smooth; return to saucepan.
 8 	Mix in reserved  potatoes  and the  Broccoli .
 9 	Over medium-low heat gradually add  Cheese , stirring until heated through and  Cheese  is completely melted.
 10 	Season with  salt  and  pepper
