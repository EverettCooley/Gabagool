peach  Melba
 
 1 Can  peach  Halves, Drained (16 Oz) 
 1 Pint  vanilla   Ice cream 
 1/4 Cup  raspberry preserves 
 
Place One  peach  Half in Each of 4 Small Serving Dishes. Fill Center With a Scoop of  ice  Cram; Top With 1 T. Preserves. Makes 4 Servings. Tip: to Substitute, Use Frozen  raspberries , Thawed, For The Preserves.Source: Unknown
