Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz Early Times®  Bourbon whiskey 
 1 oz  Jagermeister ®  herbal liqueur 
 2 oz  orange juice 
 6 – 8 oz Coca-Cola® 
 Directions : 
 Pour all ingredients into a highball glass, stir, and serve.
