Ingredients : 
 8 oz  soy cream cheese 
 1 cup grated  soy Cheddar 
 15 oz can vegetarian  refried beans 
 ½ tsp Mexican  chili powder  (or to taste) 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients. 
 Put into a greased baking pan. 
 Bake at 300°F for 20 minutes. 
 Serve with  tortilla chips .
