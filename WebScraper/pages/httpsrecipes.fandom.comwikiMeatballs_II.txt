Ingredients : 
 1 lb ground  goat  meat 
 ½ cup dry  bread crumbs 
 ⅓ cup minced  onion 
 ¼ cup  goat milk 
 1  egg 
 1 tbsp  parsley 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  black pepper 
 ½ tsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 ¼ cup  shortening 
 1 bottle  chili sauce 
 Directions : 
 Mix ground goat meat, crumbs,  onion ,  milk ,  egg , and next 4 seasonings. 
 Gently shape into 1 inch balls. 
 Melt  shortening  in large skillet; brown meatballs. 
 Remove meatballs from skillet; drain. 
 Serve with dips.
