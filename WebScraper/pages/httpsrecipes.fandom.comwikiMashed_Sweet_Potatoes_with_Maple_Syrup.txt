Description : 
 Always check the ingredients to make sure the product is vegan.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2¼ lbs  sweet potatoes , peeled and cut into 1 inch pieces 
 ¼ cup (½ stick)  soy margarine 
 2 tbps  maple syrup 
 ½ tsp ground  cardamom 
 Directions : 
 Steam the  potatoes  until tender, about 20 minutes. 
 Mash them with the other ingredients. 
 Season with  salt  and  pepper  to taste.
