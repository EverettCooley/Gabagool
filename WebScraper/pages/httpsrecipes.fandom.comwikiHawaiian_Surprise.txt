Ingredients : 
 1 (#2) can crushed  pineapple , well drained 
 1/4 c.  lemon juice 
 1 med. container  Cool Whip 
 1 can Eagle Brand  sweetened condensed milk 
 1 c. chopped  pecans 
 2  graham cracker pie crusts 
 Directions : 
 Mix milk, lemon juice, Cool Whip, pineapple and nuts. 
 Pour into pie crusts and refrigerate overnight.
