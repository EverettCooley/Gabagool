Ingredients : 
 2 ripe  California avocados , halved, peeled 
 3  tomatoes , chopped 
 lemon juice 
 4 oz  American cheese , grated 
 15 oz can  chili with beans 
 Directions : 
 Sprinkle  lemon juice  on avocados to retain color. 
 Heat  chili  thoroughly; add  tomatoes . 
 Spoon mixture into avocados and top each with grated choose. 
 Place under broiler briefly until  cheese  melts. 
 Serve with enriched and  skim milk .
