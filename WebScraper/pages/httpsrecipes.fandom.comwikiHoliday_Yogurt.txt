Ingredients : 
 2 cups nonfat plain  yogurt 
 1 teaspoon  cherry  or  strawberry  extract 
 Red  food coloring 
 Sugar-free  chocolate  syrup 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl, add 1/4 tsp  cherry  or  strawberry  extract for every 1/2 cup of plain  yogurt . Use red  food coloring  to color the dish to the desired shade. Place it in decorative bowls and drizzle with sugar-free  chocolate  syrup.
