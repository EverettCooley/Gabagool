Description : 
 Tired of mashed  sweet potatoes  with marshmallows on top? These roasted  sweet potatoes  are awesome, and simple to make.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4  sweet potatoes , peeled and cubed into one-inch pieces. 
 1 tsp  red pepper flakes , or more if you like it hot 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 Tbsp  thyme 
 3 Tbsp  olive oil 
 Directions : 
 Pre-heat the oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit. 
 Mix all ingredients together and place on a cookie sheet. 
 Bake 40 minutes until soft. Longer if you prefer golden brown and crispy on the outside. 
 Surprise your friends and family by showing them how good  sweet potatoes  can be!
