Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Puckett Estate in Irving, Texas in 1990. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups cooked  northern beans 
 1  onion  cut finely in half moons 
 ½ cup  parsley  finely chopped 
 1 teaspoon crushed  pepper 
 2 hard boiled  eggs  sliced 
 1  tomato  diced 
 ¼ cup  tahini 
 ¼ cup  vinegar 
 ¼ cup  lemon juice 
 2 cloves  garlic  minced 
 Directions : 
 Soak beans over night. 
 Bring them to a boil and then on medium heat cook them until soft. 
 In a bowl mix tahini, vinegar, lemon juice and garlic for the sauce. 
 Pour tahini dressing over beans and mix well. 
 Slice onion thinly julienne style in half moon shape. 
 In a bowl knead it with 1 teaspoon salt and rinse. 
 Mix beans with onion and parsley. 
 Decorate with tomatoes and slices of hard boiled eggs.
