      Cheese Sandwiches     
 Ingredients : 
 200 g  cheese 
 2  yolks  hard boiled 
 1 tbsp  mustard 
 50 g  butter 
 Directions : 
 Grind  cheese  finely and mix in  yolks . 
 Spread the mass on a bread slice ⅕ inch thick. 
 Put sandwiches in a well heated oven for 5 – 10 minutes.
