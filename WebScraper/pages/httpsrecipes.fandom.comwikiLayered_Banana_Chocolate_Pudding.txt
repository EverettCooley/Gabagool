Description : 
 Anyone who likes bananas will love this layered pudding treat that gets its start with a convenient boxed pudding mix.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: Taste of Home Test Kitchen Favorites 
 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1¾ cups cold  milk 
 1 (3.9 oz) box  instant chocolate pudding mix 
 24  vanilla wafers 
 2 large firm  bananas 
 1 (8 oz) tub frozen  whipped topping , thawed 
 grated  chocolate , optional 
 Directions : 
 In large mixing bowl, whisk milk and pudding mix 2 minutes; let stand 2 minutes or until soft-set. 
 Arrange ½ of vanilla wafers in 1-qt bowl. 
 Top with ½ of pudding, bananas and whipped topping. 
 Repeat layers. 
 Cover and refrigerate for 3 hours. 
 Garnish with grated chocolate if desired.
