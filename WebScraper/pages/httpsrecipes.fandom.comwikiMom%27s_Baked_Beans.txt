 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups cooked (about 2 cups dry) navy or other white beans 
 1  Onion , chopped 
 1/3-1/2 cup  molasses  (depending on your tastes) 
 1/3 cup natural  ketchup 
 1/4 cup prepared yellow  mustard 
 1/4-1/2 tsp  cayenne pepper 
 cooking liquid from the beans 
 Directions : 
 When your beans are tender, drain and reserve the cooking liquid. 
 
 
 Preheat the oven to 275 F. In a casserole dish or loaf pan, combine all ingredients but the beans and their cooking liquid until well-mixed. Add the beans and enough of the cooking liquid to cover them by about an inch. Cover the dish with a tight-fitting lid or foil, and bake in the oven for 6â€“8 hours, stirring occasionally and adding more liquid as needed, until the beans are aromatic and very soft
