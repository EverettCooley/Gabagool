Ingredients : 
 1 or 2  chocolate  bars (or a bunch of Nesquik) 
 1 can (400 g) lait sucré 
 2 teaspoons  vanilla  (or 1 sachet sucre vanilliné) 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine lait sucré and  chocolate  in a heavy-bottomed pot. 
 Heat over low flame until  chocolate  is completely melted, stirring occasionally. 
 Remove from heat and add in  vanilla  ( nuts ,  Peanut butter , or  mint  candies, too, if desired). 
 Pour into a greased pan. 
 Refrigerate until firm.
