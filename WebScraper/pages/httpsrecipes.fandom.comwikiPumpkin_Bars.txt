Description : 
 Contributed by Michaela at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cup  flour 
 2 teaspoon  soda 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon  cinnamon 
 4  eggs , beaten 
 2 cup  pumpkin 
 2 cup  sugar 
 1 cup  oil 
 1 cup chopped  nuts  (optional) 
 Frosting : 
 6 ounces  cream cheese  (at room temperature) 
 1½ sticks  butter  (at room temperature) 
 2 tbsp  cream  ( milk  ok) 
 2 teaspoon  vanilla 
 3½ cups  powdered sugar 
 Directions : 
 Mix all together. 
 Bake in greased and floured jelly roll pan at 350°F for 25 minutes. 
 Frosting : 
 Cream all together and frost.
