Description 
 Pork Recipes  by the South Carolina Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Ingredients 
 1 pound bulk hot and spicy  sausage 
 4 cups shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 3 cups  Bisquick 
 ½ cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 Directions 
 Heat oven to 350 °F. 
 Mix all ingredients together. Shape into 1" balls. 
 Bake on a lightly greased cookie sheet with sides for 20–25 minutes or until brown. 
 Remove from pan and serve warm.
