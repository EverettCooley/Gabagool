Description : 
 Contributed by   Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves 8 
 Ingredients : 
 4 medium  chicken breasts 
 ½ medium  bell pepper  chopped 
 1  onion  chopped 
 1 x 16 ounce package mild  pork sausage 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 oil 
 2 cups  chicken broth 
 1 cup  rice 
 Directions : 
 Cut the  chicken  in bite size pieces. 
 In a skillet combine the  chicken  with the  bell pepper  and  onion . 
 Add  sausage  and  salt  and cook until golden brown in enough  oil  to keep the mixture from sticking. 
 Add the broth and rice. 
 Cover the pan and cook slowly for 45 minutes.
