Ingredients : 
 1 fluid ounce  gin 
 ½ fluid ounce  sweet vermouth 
 ½ fluid ounce  dry vermouth 
 Directions : 
 Shake with ice 
 Strain into a chilled cocktail glass. 
 Garnish with two crushed  strawberries .
