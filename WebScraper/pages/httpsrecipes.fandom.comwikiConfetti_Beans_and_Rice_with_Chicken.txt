 Description : 
 Makes 4 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 bag Success  brown rice 
 2 tsp Dijon  mustard 
 1/2 cup Caesar salad dressing 
 15 oz can  black beans , rinsed 
 12 oz cooked diced  chicken 
 11 oz can Mexican style  corn , drained 
 4  green onions , thinly sliced 
 4 Large  tomatoes , with seeds and pulp removed 
 Directions : 
 Prepare rice according to package directions. 
 In a bowl, combine mustard and dressing. 
 Toss in rice, beans, chicken, corn and onions. 
 Chill or serve at room temp. 
 Serve in large tomatoes with pulp and seeds removed.
