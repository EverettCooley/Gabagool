Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 Layer 1 : 
 2 cups  flour 
 1 cup melted  butter 
 1 cup chopped  nuts 
 Layer 2 : 
 8 oz soft  cream cheese 
 1 cup  powdered sugar 
 1 cup  Cool Whip 
 Layer 3 : 
 2 small pkg. pistachio  instant pudding  (any flavor pudding can be used) 
 2 tsp  crème de menthe  (optional) 
 3 cups  milk 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 Layer 4 : 
 additional  Cool Whip 
 Directions : 
 Layer 1 : 
 Combine ingredients and pat into 9x13 inch pan. 
 Bake 15 minutes at 350°F; let cool. 
 Layer 2 : 
 Blend ingredients and spread over crust. 
 Layer 3 : 
 Beat ingredients at low speed until thickened. 
 Spread on  cheese  layer and refrigerate 2 hours or overnight. 
 Layer 4 : 
 Spread  cool whip  on top of pudding layer and chill until serving time. 
 Nuts can be sprinkled on top. 
 Enjoy.
