Ingredients : 
 4 tbsp  butter 
 4 tbsp  flour 
 2 cups hot  milk 
 3  egg yolks 
 Â½ cup  cheese , grated 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter  in saucepan. 
 Add in  flour  and stir 3 or 4 minutes until sauce is smooth. 
 Then add  milk , stirring constantly. 
 While still stirring, add in  egg yolks . 
 Remove from heat and fold in  cheese .
