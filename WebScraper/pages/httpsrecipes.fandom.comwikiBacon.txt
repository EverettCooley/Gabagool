      Raw Bacon     
 About Bacon : 
 Bacon  is a popular form of fried ham. Bacon is often served with breakfast meals. It is also used as flavoring in salad and bean dishes, and can be found in sandwiches or on hamburgers.
 Lean bacon is a designation used in reqard to the fat content of a meat product containing 10% or less of fat. 
 
 Storing Bacon : 
 Keep bacon very cold while storing. Don't eat it after 4-5 days.
