 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 half, whole  Wheat  pita pocket 
 1 Tbs  soynut butter 
 1 Tbs  apple butter 
 6  Apple  slices, about 1/4  Apple 
 Directions : 
 Spread  soynut butter  and  apple butter  in pita half. Arrange  Apple  slices inside pita.
 Yield: 1 pita half. Serving size: 1 pita half. 
 Per serving: 231 calories, 6.5 g fat (1 g sat fat), 7 g protein (4 g soy protein), 38 g carbohydrate, 255 mg sodium, 0 mg cholesterol, 1.5 g dietary fiber.
