 Ingredients : 
 ½ lb cooked  fish 
 ½ pint  rice 
 ½ tsp chopped  celery 
 salt  and  pepper 
 1 tsp  curry powder 
 a little grated  nutmeg 
 1 hard boiled  egg 
 Directions : 
 Cook the  rice . 
 Remove the skin and bones from the fish and flake it. 
 Melt the  margarine . 
 Add the fish,  rice , and seasoning and mix thoroughly. 
 Pile neatly on a dish. 
 Garnish with sliced  egg  and chopped  parsley
