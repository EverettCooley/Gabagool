Ingredients : 
 1 cup  barley 
 ⅓ cup  oil 
 ⅓ cup  lemon juice 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 pepper , to taste 
 parsley , to taste 
 2 cups  corn 
 ½  red pepper 
 ½  green pepper 
 ½ cup  green onions 
 Directions : 
 Cook  barley  and drain. 
 Combine with the rest of the ingredients. 
 Mix and chill overnight.
