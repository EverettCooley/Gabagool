 Description : 
 This juicy dessert has only three ingredients - grapes, currant jelly, and sugar! Other flavors of jam may be used instead of currant jelly, if preferred. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 seedless  grapes 
 currant jelly, melted 
 sugar 
 Directions : 
 Dip seedless grapes in melted currant jelly. 
 Roll with sugar to coat.
