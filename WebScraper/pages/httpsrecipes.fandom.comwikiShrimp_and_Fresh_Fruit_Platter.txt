Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Debbie Parker 
 Ingredients : 
 assorted fresh fruits ( melon ,  kiwi ,  pineapple ,  strawberries ,  mandarin oranges ) 
 chilled  shrimp , peeled and deveined 
 Directions : 
 Arrange on a salad plate. 
 Drizzle with  raspberry  dressing (can be purchased) and garnish with  mint  leaves.
