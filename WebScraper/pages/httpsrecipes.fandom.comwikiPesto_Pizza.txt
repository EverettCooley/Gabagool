Ingredients : 
 1 (12 inch) pre-baked pizza crust 
 ½ cup pesto 
 1 ripe tomato, chopped  
 ½ cup green bell pepper, chopped 
 1 (2 ounce) can chopped black olives, drained 
 ½ small red onion, chopped 
 1 (4 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and sliced 
 1 cup crumbled feta cheese 
 Directions : 
 Step 1Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). 
 Step 2Spread pesto on pizza crust. Top with tomatoes, bell peppers, olives, red onions, artichoke hearts and feta cheese. 
 Step 3Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until cheese is melted and browned.
