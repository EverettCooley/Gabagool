 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 300 g of prepared MARGUERITE attieke 
 1 kg of meat ( Veal ,  Lamb , or  Chicken ) 
 2 diced  onions 
 2 sliced  leeks 
 1 spoon of  tomato  concentrate 
 4 sliced  carrots 
 250 g of boiled chick  Peas 
 4  potatoes  cut in four 
 3 sliced courgettes 
 350 ml of  water  or  vegetable stock 
 saffron 
 Directions : 
 Brown the meat in a saucepan, Add the  onions ,  leeks , and the  carrots , Cook for 10 minutes, Pour in the  water  (or the stock), add the  tomato  concentrate, the spices and let cook for 1 hour on low heat, Add the chick  Peas , the courgettes, the  potatoes  and the  saffron , and let simmer for another ½ hour, Serve with the prepared attieke in accompaniment.
