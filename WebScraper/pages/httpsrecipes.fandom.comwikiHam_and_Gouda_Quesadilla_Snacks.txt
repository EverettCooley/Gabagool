Description : 
 This is a tasty and unique quesadilla recipe 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups of shredded  smoked gouda  cheese 
 1 cup of chopped  ham 
 ½ cup of pitted chopped  olives , chopped 
 ¼ cup of minced  red onions 
 ½ cup of Grey Poupon country  dijon mustard 
 8 flour tortillas  (6-7 inch) 
 [[sour cream], if desired (to garnish) 
 chopped  bell peppers , if desired (to garnish) 
 Directions : 
 Mix  cheese ,  ham ,and  onions  in a small bowl. 
 Spread 1 tbsp of  mustard  on each tortilla shell and spread about ⅓ cup of the  gouda  mixture over ½ of the tortilla. 
 Fold the tortilla in half to cover the filling. 
 Heat the filled tortillas is a large non stick skillet over medium heat for 4 minutes or until  cheese  melts, turning once half way through cooking. 
 Cut the quesadillas into 3 wedges and place on serving platter. 
 Garnish with  sour cream  and  peppers .
