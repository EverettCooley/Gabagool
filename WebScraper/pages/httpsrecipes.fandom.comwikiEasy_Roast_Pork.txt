Description : 
 NOTE: You'll need a pressure cooker for this recipe. 
 Ingredients : 
 2¼ lbs  pork loin  for roasting 
 2  garlic  cloves, chopped 
 salt  and  pepper 
 2 tbsp  butter 
 ½ large  carrot , sliced into thin chips 
 2  onions , quartered 
 ¼ cup  white wine 
 1  bouquet garni 
 Directions : 
 Rub crushed  garlic  all over the  pork  loin. 
 Season with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Melt  butter  in the pressure cooker over high heat. 
 Add the  pork  loin and cook to brown the sides. 
 Add the  carrots  and  onions , saute for 2 – 3 minutes. 
 Add the  white wine ,  bouquet garni , then season with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Cover, seal, and cook under pressure for 30 minutes. 
 Arrange on a serving dish and serve with sauteed  potatoes .
