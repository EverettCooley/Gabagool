Description : 
 Nice for a picnic. 
 
 Yield: 16 meat cakes 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ lbs lean  ground beef 
 ½ lbs bulk  Italian sausage 
 ½ cup minced  onion 
 1 tsp  Italian seasoning 
 1 tsp  salt 
 2 tsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 ½ tsp dried  sage 
 1 cup unsweetened  applesauce 
 ½ cup whole wheat  bread crumbs 
 ½ cup unprocessed  bran 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Mix all ingredients together well. 
 Place ⅓ cup (2 oz) mixture in each of 16 muffin cups. 
 Bake at 350°F. 
 Until done, about 1 hour. 
 Remove from muffin tins. 
 Serve hot or cold. 
 Nutrient information : 
 1 meat cake: ½ starch/bread exchange | 2 lean meat exchanges | 150 calories | 12 g protein | 8 g fat | 8 g carbohydrate | 360 mg sodium | 41 mg cholesterol.
