Description : 
 This is a recipe I obtained from an Estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Milligan Estate in Crandall, Texas in 2000.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups thinly sliced or shredded unpeeled  zucchini 
 ½ cup grated  cheese 
 1 cup  baking mix 
 ½ cup chopped  red onion 
 ½ teaspoon  oregano 
 2 teaspoons chopped fresh  parsley 
 ½ teaspoon  seasoned salt 
 1 clove minced  garlic 
 ½ cup  oil 
 4 beaten  eggs 
 Description : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Mix all ingredients and pour into rectangular greased baking dish for 30 minutes. 
 Cut into squares and serve hot.
