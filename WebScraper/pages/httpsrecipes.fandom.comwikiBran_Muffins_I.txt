 Original recipe 
 Makes 18 muffins 
 Ingredients : 
 2 medium  bananas , mashed 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 4 tsp  baking powder 
 2 tsp  vanilla 
 ¾ tsp  cinnamon 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 6 oz plain  yogurt 
 2 cups natural  bran 
 3 each  eggs , beaten 
 2 cup  powdered skim milk, dry 
 4 tsp  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Spray muffin tin with non-stick coating agent. 
 Mix mashed bananas, yogurt and baking soda. 
 Add remaining ingredients and mix well. 
 Pour into prepared muffin tins and bake 15 – 20 minutes. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 Calories 97 | Carbohydrates 15 gm | Fat 6 gm | Protein 6 gm | Sodium 354 mg
