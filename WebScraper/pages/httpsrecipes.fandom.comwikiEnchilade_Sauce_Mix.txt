Enchilada Sauce Mix
 
Mix Together And Store in Airtight Container:
 
 1/2 C.  cornstarch 
 1 (6 Oz.) Pkg. Medium Hot Dry  chili powder 
 1 Tsp.  garlic powder 
 1 1/2 Tbsp.  salt 
 1/2 Tsp.  cumin 
 1/2 Tsp.  oregano 
 1 Tbsp.  paprika
