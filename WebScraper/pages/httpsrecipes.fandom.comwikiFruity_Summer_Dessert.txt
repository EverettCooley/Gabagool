Description : 
 Fresh ruby red strawberries give your taste buds a treat. The juice is sweet and tantalizing. Even the aroma of fresh berries excites the palate and prepares the way for a great adventure in eating. Try this recipe as you partake of a fruit that is luscious, beautiful in color, and heavenly in any menu plan. Enjoy!

 Ingredients : 
 1 pkg. white cake mix 
 ½ cup  strawberry preserves  (not jelly or jam) 
 12  strawberries , washed, hulled and sliced 
 1 – 2 fresh  peaches , sliced thinly 
 1 cup  peach preserves 
 ½ cup  whipped topping 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease and  flour  a 13”x9”x2”pan. 
 Prepare white cake mix per package instructions and bake. 
 Split cake in half horizontally. 
 Place bottom cake layer on serving plate. 
 Spread this layer with  strawberry preserves . 
 Place top layer of cake over  strawberry preserves . 
 Score the top of the cake with knife to mark 24 servings. 
 Alternate fresh strawberries with  peach  slices on alternate blocks. (Quilt design) 
 Heat the  peach  preserves and strain. Brush the fruit with the warmed glaze. 
 Garnish with whipped topping and curled  chocolate  strip.
