 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  chicken broth 
 ½ cup  butter 
 ¼ cup  onion 
 ¼ cup  celery 
 4 oz  mushrooms , drained 
 ⅛ cup  parsley 
 ½ tbsp  sage 
 ½ tbsp  salt 
 ½ tbsp  poultry seasoning 
 ¼ tbsp  pepper 
 6 cup day-old  bread  cubes 
 1  egg 
 1 can  chicken soup 
 2½ cup cubed  chicken 
 Directions : 
 Lightly grease crock pot. 
 Layer stuffing +  chicken  + stuffing +  chicken  cook on low 5 to 6 hours.
