Ingredients : 
 2 cups  sugar 
 1-1/2 cup  water 
 3/4 tsp. pink  food coloring 
 3/4 - 1 tsp.  Rose essence 
 1 c.  Milk 
 ice cubes 
 Instructions : 
 In a heavy bottomed pan, heat sugar and water. Stir well to dissolve the sugar. 
 Bring it to boil. Let it boil for 4 mins on medium flame. 
 Add food colour towards last minute. 
 Let the syrup become thick. If you test between your forefinger and thumb, a small string should form and break – sticky consistency. Not one string consistency.  
 Switch off the flame and add rose essence.  
 Cool down completely and pour to bottle, to store in the fridge. 
 Place 5-6 icecubes in a tumbler. 
 Add 1 cup boiled cooled milk and 3 tblsp of the prepared rose syrup. 
 Mix well and serve immediately.
