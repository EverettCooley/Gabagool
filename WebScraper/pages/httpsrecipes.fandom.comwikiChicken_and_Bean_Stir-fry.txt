       Recipe by Hooked on Heat     
 Ingredients : 
 1  chicken  breast chopped into tiny bite-size pieces 
 2 cups  green beans  (I love having the frozen ones in my freezer all the time) 
 2 stalks of  green onion  sliced into 1-inch pieces 
 3 – 4 stalks of  celery  chopped in bite-size pieces 
 1 cup sliced  red pepper 
 1 tbsp  soy sauce 
 1 cube  chicken  bouillon 
 1 tsp  curry powder 
 ½ tsp red  chili powder 
 ½ tsp crushed  black pepper 
 salt , to taste 
 2 tbsp cooking  oil 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in a wok and add  chicken  pieces. Stir-fry till  chicken  is cooked through and add  chili powder ,  curry  powder, bouillon cube and  black pepper . 
 Stir-fry for a minute and add  celery  and  beans . 
 Cook for a minute or two and add red pepper and  green onion . 
 Add  soy sauce  and  salt  and stir-fry for a few minute till all the flavours blend together. 
 Serve over warm  rice  and enjoy!
