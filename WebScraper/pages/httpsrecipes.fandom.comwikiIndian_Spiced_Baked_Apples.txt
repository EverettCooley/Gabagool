 Description : 
 Served chilled these apples baked in coconut milk would make an unusual and cooling dessert to end a spicy meal. If you prefer you can cook them in the microwave on HIGH for 6-8mins instead.
 
 Prep : 10mins 
 Cook : 25 – 30 mins 
 Serves : 4 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (400g) can  coconut milk 
 100 g / 4 oz  caster sugar 
 4  cardamom  pods, split 
 4  Bramley apples , halved and cored 
 pistachio nuts  and  pomegranate seeds  to serve 
 Directions : 
 Place the coconut milk, sugar and cardamom pods in a pan and gently bring to the boil, stirring until the sugar dissolves. Set to one side to allow the cardamom to infuse the milk. 
 Preheat the oven to 180 °C / Fan 160 °C / 350 °F / Gas Mark 4. Place the apples cut side down in an ovenproof dish, pour over the coconut milk, cover the dish with foil, then ovenbake for 25 – 30 mins or until the apples are tender. Cool then chill. 
 Serve the apples in small bowls with chopped pistachios or pomegranate seeds to decorate.
