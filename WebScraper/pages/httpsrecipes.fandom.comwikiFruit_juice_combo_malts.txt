 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3/4 cup Fruit juice 
 1/3 cup  vanilla  malted  milk  powder 
 1 quart  vanilla   ice   cream 
 Directions : 
 1 ** fruit juice should be a combination such as pineapple-orange- banana . 
2 In a 5 cup blender or food processor, combine juice and malted  milk  powder. 
3 Blend at medium speed, stopping frequently to scrape sides, until malted  milk  powder is dissolved. 
4 Add  ice   cream ; continue blending, stopping frequently to scrape sides, until smooth. 
5 Serve immediately. 
6 Yield: 5 servings
