Ingredients : 
 1 cup  sugar 
 ½ cup  butter 
 2  eggs 
 1 cup mashed  avocado 
 ½ cup  buttermilk 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 1 ½ cups  all purpose flour 
 ½ tsp  nutmeg 
 ½ tsp  cinnamon 
 ½ tsp  allspice 
 ½ cup  raisins 
 ½ cup  walnuts  (broken) 
 Lemon sauce : 
 1 cup  water 
 ⅓ cup  sugar 
 1 tbsp  cornstarch 
 2 tbsp  butter 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 ½ tsp  vanilla extract 
 Directions : 
 Cream sugar and  butter , add the 2  eggs  and  avocado  and mix well. 
 Add the  baking soda  to the  buttermilk  and add to the batter. 
 Add spices to  flour  and add to the batter all at once and mix well. 
 Dust the  raisins  and  walnuts  lightly with  flour  and add to the batter. 
 Pour into well-greased pyrex pan (8”x10”) and bake at 350°F for 35 – 40 minutes (this can be stored of frozen; however, it is delicious served warm with or without a tart  lemon  sauce). 
 Server lemon sauce over the cake. 
 Lemon sauce : 
 Mix sauce ingredients together. 
 Bring to a boil, and boil until clear.
