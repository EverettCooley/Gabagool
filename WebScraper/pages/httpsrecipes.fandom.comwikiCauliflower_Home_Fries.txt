 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 bag frozen  Cauliflower 
 2 slices  Bacon 
 salt ,  pepper ,  paprika 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Cauliflower  till tender-crisp. Cook  Bacon  and crumble. Cut  Cauliflower  into chunks and saute in  butter  in skillet till crisp. Add  salt ,  pepper  and  paprika  while sauteeing. Top with  Bacon  and serve.
