 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  bulghur   Wheat 
 2 cup boiling  water 
 1 cup minced  parsley 
 Pinch of  salt 
 1/2 cup minced  Onion 
 1-2 ripe chopped  tomatoes 
 1/4 cup  olive oil 
 2-3 tsp. dried  mint  or  basil  or 1/4 c. fresh 
 Juice of 1-2  lemons 
 
 
 Directions : 
 In medium large salad bowl, pour boiling  water  over  bulghur . Let sit for 15 minutes to absorb  water , then stir to cool. Toss rest of ingredients into soaked  bulghur , adding  garlic powder  and  pepper  to taste.
 Let sit for at least 1/2 hour to blend flavors. Serve on  lettuce  or use to stuff in pita bread.
 Optional ingredients: Chopped  cucumber ,  zucchini ,  celery ,  green pepper , scallions,  chives ,  Mushrooms , sprouts,  sunflower seeds , chopped hard boiled  eggs , cooked/drained beans, etc.
