 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large  Onion 
 2 tbsp  curry  powder 
 1 dash  olive oil 
 1 cup  Lentils 
 4 cup  water 
 Directions : 
 1 big  Onion  chopped & sauteed with 2 tbsp currie powder (i confess to using Â¼ tsp. extra virgin  olive oil  here). Add a cup of  Lentils  and 4 cups of  water  and cook for 2 hours. Serve over steamed  rice  (i prefer the sticky jasmine  white rice  which i buy in 25 pound bags). I serve it with coleslaw made with ff honey/ mustard  dressing. Yummy!
