Description : 
 Source:  The Joslin Diabetes Gourmet Cookbook  by Bonnie Sanders Polin, Ph.D., and Frances Towner Giedt, ISBN #0-553-08760-6
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  orange 
 2  tangerines 
 ⅓ cup chilled sparkling  mineral water ,  seltzer  or  club soda  (about ⅓ cup) 
 Directions : 
 Juice the oranges, and pour into glasses. 
 Top with mineral water, seltzer or club soda.
