 Ingredients : 
 1 cup of  raisins 
 1 pkg. of regular or  instant vanilla pudding  (the 6 servings size) 
 2 cups of  milk 
 1 cup of thawed  Cool Whip 
 1 tsp of  lemon juice 
 â…› tsp of  cinnamon 
 1 baked 9" pie shell 
 Directions : 
 Pour boiling  water  over the  raisins  to cover. 
 Let them stand for 5 minutes. 
 Then drain well. 
 Prepare pie filling mix by using 2 cups of  milk . 
 Chill thoroughly. 
 Fold in  Cool Whip ,  lemon juice ,  cinnamon , and the  raisins . 
 Spoon into pie shell. 
 Chill for 3 hours.
