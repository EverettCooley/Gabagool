Ingredients : 
 2½ tbsp  margarine 
 2 lbs  carrots , sliced 
 ¼ cup  apple juice 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 2 tbsp  chives 
 ½ cup  almonds , sliced 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Heat  margarine  in a large skillet. 
 Add  carrots  and saute over moderate heat, stirring frequently, until tender-crisp and beginning to turn golden, about 10 to 15 minutes. 
 Add  apple juice  and sauté, stirring until it is all absorbed. 
 Stir in the remaining ingredients, then transfer to a serving container.
