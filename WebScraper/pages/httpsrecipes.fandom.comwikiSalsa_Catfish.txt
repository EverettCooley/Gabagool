 Description : 
 A  Catfish  recipe.
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 U.S. Farm-Raised  Catfish  fillets, 6 to 8 ounces each 
 1/2 teaspoon Freshly ground  white pepper 
 3/4 teaspoon  garlic salt 
 2 cups Favorite salsa or Fresh Garden Relish (see recipe below) 
 Fresh Garden Relish Ingredients 
 4 Medium ripe  tomatoes , chopped 
 1 Green  bell pepper , seeded and chopped 
 1 Yellow  bell pepper , seeded and chopped 
 1/4 cup Snipped fresh  chives 
 1/2 cup Minced  red onions 
 1 Jalape√±o, seeded and minced 
 1 1/2 teaspoons  sea salt 
 1/2 teaspoon Freshly ground  black pepper 
 
 Directions : 
 1. Sprinkle the fillets with the  pepper  and  garlic salt . Prepare a hot fire. 
 2. When ready to grill, place the on an oiled perforated grill rack over the fire. Grill until the is opaque and just beginning to flake when tested with a fork, about 10 minutes per inch of thickness, turning once halfway through the cooking. 
 3. Serve the topped with salsa. Serves 6. 
 4. For Fresh Garden Relish: Combine all ingredients. Cover and chill until ready to serve. Makes 5 to 6 cups.
