Ingredients : 
 1 cup  butter 
 1 cup  olive oil 
 1 head  cabbage 
 3 med  bell peppers 
 1 stalk  celery 
 2 cloves  garlic 
 1 can flat  Anchovies 
 Directions : 
 Saute  butter , oil ,  garlic  and achovie. 
 Cook until anchovie dissolves. 
 Add chopped vegetables. 
 Steam until all vegetables are tender.
