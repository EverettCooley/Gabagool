      Apple Mousse     
 Ingredients : 
 4  apples 
 2  egg whites 
 1 tsp  gelatin 
 sugar 
 a pinch of  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Stew sour  apples  in some  water  until very soft. 
 Then grate through the sieve, add  sugar ,  vanilla ,  gelatin  and  egg whites  to  apples  and whip until it is light and fluffy. 
 Serve in a glass bowl with  milk .
