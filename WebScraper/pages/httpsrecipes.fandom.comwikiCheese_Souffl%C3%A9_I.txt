 Description : 
 Serves:  4 hungry people or 6 as a brunch dish 
 Cook time : 45 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp  butter 
 6 bread slices 
 1½ cup  cheese , grated 
 6  eggs 
 1½ cups  milk 
 ½ tsp dry  mustard 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 250 °F. 
 Spread  butter  on bread & cut into small cubes. 
 In casserole dish, alternate layers of bread cubes and  cheese . 
 In a bowl, beat together  egg ,  milk  and dry  mustard ; pour over bread; stir gently. 
 Let stand at least 10 minutes. 
 Bake 40–45 minutes or till set in center.
