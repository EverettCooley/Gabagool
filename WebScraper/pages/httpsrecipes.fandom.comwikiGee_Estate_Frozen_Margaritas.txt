Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Gee Estate in Quinlan, Texas in 1994.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 6 ounce can frozen limeade concentrate 
 6 ounces  tequila 
 2 ounces  triple sec 
 6 ounces lemon lime soda 
 5 cups crushed  ice 
 lime  slices 
 margarita salt 
 Directions : 
 Pour limeade concentrate,  tequila , tripe sec and soda into blender along with  ice  and blend until desired consistency. 
 Pour into salt rimmed glasses and garnish with  lime  slices.
