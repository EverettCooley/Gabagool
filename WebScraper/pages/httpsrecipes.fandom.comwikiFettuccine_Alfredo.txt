      Fettuccine Alfredo     
 About Fettucini Alfredo : 
 The Fettuccine is enrobed in a rich sauce of butter, grated Parmesan Cheese, heavy cream and plentiful grindings of black pepper. Other noodles may be substituted for the fettuccine.
