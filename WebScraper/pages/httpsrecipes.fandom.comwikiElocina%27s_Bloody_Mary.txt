 Description 
 Serves : 1
 
 Ingredients 
 Ice 
 Jig of  vodka 
 Store-bought  Bloody Mary Mix 
 Dash of steak sauce 
 Dash of  Tabasco sauce 
 fresh-squeezed  lime 
 Garnish Options 
 celery 
 lime 
 salted  rim 
 *  scallion 
 pickled okra 
 green bean 
 a  pepperoncini 
 Directions 
 Place ice in a tall glass. 
 Add all of the ingredients listed above. 
 Variation 
 Substitute  tequila  for the vodka to make a Bloody Maria.
