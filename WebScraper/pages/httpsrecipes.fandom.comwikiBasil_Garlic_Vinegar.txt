Desription : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 wine  or  white vinegar 
 1 cup fresh  basil 
 2 cloves  garlic , peeled 
 optional: a sprig or two of lemon  verbena  or  lemon balm 
 Directions : 
 Rinse and dry basil thoroughly. 
 Place herbs in a quart jar. 
 Pour in vinegar and cap. 
 Allow to sit for about a month before using, occasionally shaking. 
 Then remove herbs and label. 
 Use for dressing, marinades or sauces.
