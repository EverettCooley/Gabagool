 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 Cups Grated green  Bananas 
 1 Cup grated  coconut 
 2.5 cups grated sweet  potatoes 
 1 cup  flour 
 1 tsp.  baking powder 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 1 tsp  Mixed Spice 
 2 cups  Coconut Milk 
 1 1/2 cups  brown sugar 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together. 
 Place one cup mixture into quailed  banana  leaves. 
 Wrap and tie with string or  banana  bark. 
 Put the small parcels into enough boiling  water  to cover, and cook for one hour.
