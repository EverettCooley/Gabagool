Ingredients : 
 2 - 3 bunches fresh  broccoli 
 1½ cups  mayonnaise 
 ¼ - ½ cup  sugar 
 1 cup  raisins  (fresh  grapes  are a good replacement if you can find them) 
 1 tsp  vinegar 
 1 small  red onion  - chopped (optional) 
 1 lb  bacon  - fried 
 Directions : 
 Fry  bacon , crumble into small pieces. Set aside. 
 Cut broccoli florets into bite-size pieces, wash and set aside. 
 Combine  mayonnaise ,  sugar  and  vinegar . Set aside. (Miracle Whip works in place of this) 
 Chop  onion . 
 If  grapes  are used in place of  raisins , cut in half. 
 Combine broccoli,  bacon ,  onion  and  grapes . 
 Stir in  Mayonnaise  mixture or Miracle Whip. Refrigerate until served.
