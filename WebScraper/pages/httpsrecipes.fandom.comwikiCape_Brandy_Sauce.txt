 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 	 tablespoon  butter 
 1 	cup  Sugar 
 1/4 	teaspoon  salt 
 5 	ounces  water 
 1/2 	cup  brandy 
 1 	teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 Directions : 
 In a saucepan, heat the  butter ,  Sugar ,  salt , and  water  for 5 minutes over medium heat. 
 Add the  brandy  and  vanilla extract  and heat an additional minute.
