 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups whole  Wheat  or all purpose  flour 
 1 cup  cornmeal 
 1/4cup  wheat germ 
 2 teaspoon  Beef  bouillon powder 
 1/2 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 Directions : 
 Mix above ingredients in a medium bowl. 
 Add: 1 large  egg , 1 tablespoon cooking  oil  and 1 cup hot  water . 
 Stir well. Roll out on a well-floured surface to 1/2 inch thickness. 
 Place on ungreased cooking sheet. 
 Bake on center rack, (275 F. or 140 C) for about 2 hours until dry and very hard. 
 Let stand overnight to dry thoroughly. 
 Makes 10 big bones and 14 puppy bones.
