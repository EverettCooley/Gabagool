Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. It was obtained when the family collection was purchased from the Burnett Estate in Terrell, Texas in 1981.
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 ounces  cream cheese , softened 
 1  green onion , finely chopped 
 1 can  cream of mushroom  soup 
 1 tablespoon  gelatin , dissolved in 3 tablespoons  water 
 1 can  crab  meat 
 Â½ cup chopped  celery 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 1 teaspoon  lemon juice 
 1 teaspoon  horseradish 
 a few drops  Tabasco pepper sauce 
 a few drops  Worcestershire sauce 
 Directions : 
 Heat soup 
 Dissolve  gelatin  in  water  and add to hot soup. 
 Cool slightly then combine with remaining ingredients. 
 Put in mold. 
 When set, unmold and serve with crackers or toasted  bread  rounds.
