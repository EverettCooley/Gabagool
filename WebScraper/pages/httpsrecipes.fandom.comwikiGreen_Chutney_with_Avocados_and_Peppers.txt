Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Old Newspaper Clipping 
 Ingredients : 
 6  jalapeño peppers , roasted, peeled 
 4  red bell peppers , roasted, peeled 
 3  avocados , peeled 
 2 cups lightly packed  cilantro 
 ¾ cup lightly packed  parsley 
 lime juice  to taste 
 3 cloves  garlic 
 ¾ tsp  garam masala 
 dash  sugar 
 dash  salt 
 Directions : 
 Cut both  peppers  and  avocados  into ¼" dice. 
 In food processor, combine remaining ingredients into smooth paste. 
 Gently stir in  peppers  and  avocados  and serve along on grilled  chicken , fish or with an omelet.
