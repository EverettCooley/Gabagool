      Arepas Rellenas Con Guiso De Carne     
 Ingredients : 
 1 recipe  basic arepa dough 
 2 tbsp  oil 
 ½ lb  ground beef 
 ½ lb  ground pork 
 1 medium  onion  -- chopped 
 1 clove  garlic  -- minced 
 1 small  green chile  -- chopped 
 2 large  tomatoes  peel, seed, chop 
 ½ tsp  achiote powder 
 ½ tsp ground  cumin 
 ¼ tsp  thyme  leaves 
 ¼ tsp  black pepper 
 1 tsp  salt 
 8  pimento -stuffed sliced 
 1 tbsp  capers 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in a heavy skillet over medium heat, 
 Add meat,  onions ,  garlic , green  chile ,  tomato ,  achiote powder ,  cumin ,  thyme ,  black pepper  and  salt 
 Cook for 20 minutes 
 Stir in and  capers . 
 Form arepas dough into small disks. 
 Place spoonful of filling on each dough disk. 
 Wrap dough around to seal 
 Fry arepas until golden brown.
