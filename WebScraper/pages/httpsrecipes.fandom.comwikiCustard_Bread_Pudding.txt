Ingredients : 
 3 cups whole  milk 
 5 whole  eggs 
 ½ cup  granulated sugar 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 2 slices hot buttered toast 
 1 teaspoon  Sugar  mixed with ¼ teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Scale the  milk . 
 In a bowl, lightly beat 5  eggs . 
 Stir in  Sugar  and  salt . 
 Slowly pour in the hot scalded  milk . 
 Stir constantly. 
 Pour this mixture into a well buttered baking dish. 
 Float toast atop the custard, sprinkle with  cinnamon sugar  mixture. 
 Bake in 350 oven for about 25 minutes.
