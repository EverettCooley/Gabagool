 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup tamari soy sauce 
 1 ½ Tbs. plain  rice vinegar 
 ½ tsp. toasted  sesame oil 
 2 tsp.  granulated sugar 
 2 tsp. finely minced shallots 
 Pinch  crushed red peppers 
 1  scallion , thinly sliced for garnish 
 Toasted  sesame seeds  for garnish 
 Directions : 
 1. Combine soy sauce,  vinegar ,  sesame oil ,  Sugar , shallots and  crushed red peppers  in small mixing bowl.
 2. Set aside for 10 minutes, letting flavors mingle and intensify before serving. Place dipping sauce in serving bowl and garnish with sliced scallions and toasted  sesame seeds . Pass with pot stickers.
