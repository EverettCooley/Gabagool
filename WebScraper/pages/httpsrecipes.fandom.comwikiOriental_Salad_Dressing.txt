 Makes 1½ cups 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅓ cup vinegar 
 ¼ cup soy sauce 
 2 tbsp water 
 1 tbsp sesame oil 
 ½ tsp sesame seeds 
 ⅔ cup oil 
 Directions : 
 Mix first 5 ingredients in jar with tight fitting lid. 
 Shake well. 
 Add oil and shake again. 
 Chill until ready to serve.
