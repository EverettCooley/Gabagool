 Ingredients : 
 3 medium  potatoes , peeled and diced 
 1 medium  onion , chopped 
 2 tablespoons  margarine 
 ½ teaspoon  salt  (to taste) 
 ¼ teaspoon  pepper  (to taste) 
 1 tablespoon  milk 
 5 ounces grated  Cheddar  cheese 
 1 pkg.  phyllo  pastry dough 
 1  egg 
 coarse salt 
 Directions : 
 Cook  potatoes  in boiling salted  water  until tender and then drain,soute  onion  until tender too. 
 Mash the  potatoes  add  onions ,  salt ,  pepper ,  cheese , and  milk , blending well. 
 Place thawed phyllo on floured surface slice into 2" wide strips brush with butter and place on heaping tbsp. of potato mixture on phyllo fold phyllo into triangle pkgs 
 Preheat oven to 375 °F 
 Repeat with potato mixture until all is used up brush with 1 egg beaten and sprinkle with coarse salt 
 Bake at 375 °F for 10-15 minutes, or until golden brown. 
 Serve with sour cream and bacon bits{optional}
