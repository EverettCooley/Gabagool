Ingredients : 
 1 8 Oz. cream cheese 
 6 Tbsp. mayonnaise 
 4 Tbsp. catsup 
 ¼ Cup chopped onion 
 ¼ Cup chopped bell pepper 
 1 Can tiny shrimp, drained 
 Procedure : 
 Mix cream cheese and mayo, add catsup, onion and bell pepper, then stir in shrimp and Slap Ya Mama seasoning to taste. Chill for 1 hour before serving this extraordinary treat.
