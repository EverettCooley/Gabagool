Description : 
 The amounts below vary with how many people you are feeding.
 
 Ingredients : 
 fresh  ground beef 
 fresh beans  (roughly one third as much as the ground beef) 
 pinch of  cinnamon 
 garlic salt , to taste 
 salt  and  pepper , to taste 
 1  onion  (chopped, based on about 1 lb of ground beef) 
 whole peeled  tomatoes  (chopped, roughly half as much as the ground beef) 
 green  and  red peppers 
 paprika , to taste 
 shredded  cheese 
 Directions : 
 First cook  ground beef  for about 15 – 20 minutes. 
 Add the beans and simmer for about another 10 – 15 minutes. 
 Add just a pinch of  cinnamon . 
 Stir. 
 Next add the  garlic salt ,  paprika ,  salt , and  pepper . 
 Chop the  tomatoes  and  onions . 
 Stir occasionally. 
 Let it cool for about 10 minutes. 
 Add the shredded  cheese  on top.
