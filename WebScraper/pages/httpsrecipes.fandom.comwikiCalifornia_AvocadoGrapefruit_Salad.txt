 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 Large avocados 
 2 ruby red  grapefruit 
 poppy seed  dressing 
 lettuce  Leaves 
 Directions : 
 Peel and slice avocados 
 Section  grapefruit , removing pulp 
 Toss avocados and  grapefruit  together with  poppy seed  dressing (about 1/2 cup of dressing) 
 Serve on  lettuce  leaves
 grapefruit  should be of a sweet variety such as Ruby Red
