Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 6 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 container Shedd's Country Crock mashed  sweet potatoes 
 ½ cup  raisins 
 ¼ cup  half and half 
 1  egg , beaten 
 2 tbsp packed  brown sugar 
 ½ cup crushed ginger snap cookies 
 2 tbsp Shedd's Spread Country Crock spread, melted 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 375°F. 
 In bowl, combine sweet potatoes, raisins, cream, egg and sugar. 
 In shallow baking dish, spoon in potato mixture, set aside. 
 In bowl, combine cookie crumbs and 2 tbsp Shedd's spread. 
 Sprinkle into potato mixture. 
 Bake 30 minutes or until heated through. 
 Let stand 15 minutes before serving. 
 Goes good with turkey.
