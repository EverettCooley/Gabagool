 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 ripe  avocado 
 1 (10.5 ounce) package soft lite  silken tofu 
 1 chopped  tomato 
 3 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1 small  Onion , chopped 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1/4 teaspoon  pepper 
 1/2 teaspoon  chili powder 
 Hot sauce, to taste 
 Directions : 
 Peel and mash the  avocado  until soft and smooth.
 Add the  tofu  and continue mashing until the  tofu  is fairly smooth. Stir the  avocado / tofu  mix until completely mixed together.
 Add the remaining ingredients and mix well. Adjust seasonings to taste.
 Serve with baked tortilla chips (low in fat!). Use for burritos, taco salads, sandwich filling and many other things.
