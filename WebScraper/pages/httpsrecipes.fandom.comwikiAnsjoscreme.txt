Description : 
 Anchovy paste
 
       Anchovy     
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups soft  butter 
 1½ ounces  anchovy paste 
 4 hard-boiled  eggs 
 Directions : 
 Spread small rounds of  bread  with softened  butter , then with  anchovy paste . 
 Cut narrow strips of the boiled  egg whites  to arrange in petal shapes. 
 Fill center with sieved  yolks .
