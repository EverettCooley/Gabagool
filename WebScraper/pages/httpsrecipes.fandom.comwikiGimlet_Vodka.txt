Ingredients : 
 2 ounces  vodka 
1/2 ounce sweetened  lime juice 
 
 Directions : 
 Half fill cocktail shaker with crushed  ice  and  gin  and  lime juice . Shake well. Strain into cocktail glass; add dash of soda.
