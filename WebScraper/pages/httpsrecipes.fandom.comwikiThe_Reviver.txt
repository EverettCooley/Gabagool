The Reviver
 
 3/4 Cup Fresh  orange juice 
 3/4 Cup  cranberry juice  Cocktail 
 1/4 Cup Fresh  grapefruit juice 
 1/4 Cup Gold  tequila 
 1 T. Plus 1 T. fresh  lime juice 
 Shaved  ice 
 
 2  limes , Slices 
 
Mix First 5 Ingredients in Small Pitcher. Stir Well. Pour Into Balloon Wine Glasses Filled With  ice . Garnish With  lime  Slices And Serve.Serves 2
