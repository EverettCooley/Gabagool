 Yield: 2 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pkg. frozen  baby lima beans 
 2 tbsp  milk 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ⅛ tsp  pepper 
 pat of  butter 
 2 tbsp grated  Parmesan  or  romano  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Thaw beans; place in foil pie plate. 
 Add remaining ingredients. 
 Cover with heavy-duty foil, crimping edges tightly. 
 Grill over hot coals for 15 minutes, turning often.
