 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  peanuts , shells and skins removed, roasted 
 1 cup  rice flour 
 1/2 cup  Sugar 
 Directions : 
 Using a mortar and pestle, break and grind the  peanuts  into small pieces 
 Parch the  rice flour  in a dry skillet, stirring continuously, until it becomes slightly browned. 
 Combine the crushed  peanuts  and  rice flour . Crush and grind them together until they form a powder. Add the  Sugar  and mix well. 
 Using your hands, form the mixture into cubes or pyramids, or press it into a pan and then cut it into squares. 
 Serve immediately, or store in air-tight containers
