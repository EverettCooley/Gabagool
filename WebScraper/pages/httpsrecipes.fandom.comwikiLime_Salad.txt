 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lg. pkg.  lime  Jello 
 1 lg. container  cottage cheese 
 1 lg. can crushed  pineapple , drained 
 1 lg. container  Cool Whip 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Sprinkle Jello over  cottage cheese ; stir until mixed. 
 Let stand until Jello is dissolved, about 30 minutes. Add fruit. 
 Fold in  Cool Whip . Makes 12 servings.  nuts  may be added, if desired.
