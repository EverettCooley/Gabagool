 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  red potatoes , scrubbed, cut into ¼" slices 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 ¼ cup  olive oil 
 2 tbsp fresh  lemon juice  (bottled can be used) 
 1 tsp dried  thyme  leaves  or  2 tbsp chopped fresh 
 romaine lettuce  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Bring  water  to boil in large saucepan over high heat. 
 Add  potato  slices. 
 Reduce heat to medium-low. 
 Simmer, covered, 10 minutes or until  potatoes  are barely tender. 
 Pour into colander; drain well. 
 Sauté  garlic  in  oil  in large deep skillet over medium heat, 1 – 2 minutes. 
 Stir in  lemon juice  and  thyme ; heat through. 
 Add drained  potatoes ; stir lightly to coat  potatoes  with dressing. 
 Season with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Reduce heat to medium-low. 
 Cover and cook 4 – 5 minutes, turning once. 
 Arrange  lettuce  on 6 salad plates; top with hot  potato  mixture. 
 Optional: garnish with  cherry tomatoes  and fresh  thyme .
