 Ingredients : 
 apples  - 450 g (1 lb) stewed and sweetened to taste, 
 hazelnut yogurt - 150 g (5 oz), 
 fruitand nut crunchy bars - 2, 
 apple  slices - dipped in  lemon juice . 
 Directions : 
 Mix the stewed apple and  yogurt  together. 
 Spoon into 4 glasses or individual dishes. 
 Crush fruit and nut bars and divide between dishes. 
 Decorate with  apple  slices.
