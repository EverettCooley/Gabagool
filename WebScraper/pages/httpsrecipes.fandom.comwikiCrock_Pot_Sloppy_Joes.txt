Ingredients : 
 3 lbs. ground beef 
 1 to 2 onions, finely chopped 
 1 green pepper, chopped 
 2 8-oz. cans tomato sauce 
 1 c. water 
 2 pkgs. sloppy joe seasoning mix 
 2 tbsp. brown sugar 
 Directions : 
 Brown beef in skillet. 
 Drain. 
 Place browned beef in crock pot. 
 Add onions, green pepper, tomato sauce, water, seasoning mix and sugar. 
 Stir thoroughly. 
 Cover and cook on low for 8 to 10 hours or on high for 3 to 4 hours.
