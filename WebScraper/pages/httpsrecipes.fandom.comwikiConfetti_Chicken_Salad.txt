 Ingredients : 
 ¼ Cup fresh  lime juice 
 3 Tbsp  olive oil 
 1 clove  garlic , crushed 
 1 tsp  chili powder 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 2 Cups cooked and cubed  Chicken 
 1 red  bell pepper , cut into thin strips 
 1/3 Cups sliced  green onions , including tops 
 2 Tbsp chopped fresh  cilantro  or  parsley 
 2 jalapeño  peppers , stemmed, seeded and minced 
 3 Cups cooked  rice , cooled 
 2  California avocados , seeded, peeled and cut into chunks 
 Directions : 
 Blend  lime juice ,  oil ,  garlic ,  chili powder  and  salt  in large bowl.
 Add  Chicken , red pepper,  onions ,  cilantro  and  hot peppers . 
 Cover and refrigerate 2 to 3 hours. 
 Add  rice  and  avocado  chunks; toss lightly, and serve.
