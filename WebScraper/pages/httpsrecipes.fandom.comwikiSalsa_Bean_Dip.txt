Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: My Old Recipes 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can  refried beans  (17 oz) 
 ¼ cup  salsa  picante, salsa casera or salsa roja (4 oz, Del Monte, etc) 
 1 cup diced  green chilis , drained (4 oz) 
 ⅓ cup shredded  cheddar  or  Jack cheese 
 tortilla chips  (plain flavor) 
 Directions : 
 mix beans, salsa and chilies in skillet. 
 Heat through. Put in serving dish. 
 Top with cheese. 
 Serve with tortillas.
