 Description : 
 In Romanian: Legume asortate in otet
 
 Ingredients : 
 15 green  bell peppers , 
 30 red  bell peppers , 
 2 big  carrots , 
 2 big  celery  roots, 
 1 medium  Cauliflower , 
 2 big  garlic  heads, 
 1Â lb/500 g  Shallots 
 For each 1 qt/1 l  vinegar 
 
 1 1/2 cups  water , 
 1 heaping tablespoon  salt , 
 2 tablespoons  Sugar , 
 1 tablespoon  honey , 
 a few  bay leaves , 
 a few  juniper berries ; 
 Directions : 
 Remove core and seeds from  peppers . 
 Wash and section each  pepper  in 8 parts. 
 Clean, wash and cube the  carrots  and celeries. 
 Clean the  garlic  and  onions  and keep whole. 
 Divide the  Cauliflower  in flowerets. 
 Arrange in a jar, placing assorted layers of everything. 
 Then pour the  vinegar  boiled with the  water  and spices like for the  Peppers in vinegar Romanian Style . 
 Pour the mixture while hot, a little at a time. 
 After it has cooled, tie the jar with cellophane.
