Description : 
 An update of an old family favorite - Alice in Houston 5/07/9
 
 12 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  tvp  chunks 
 2  garlic  cloves 
 1 cup chopped  onions 
 1 cup  carrot  chunks 
 5 tsp  vegetable broth  powder 
 ¼ cup Uncle Ben's  converted rice 
 2 cans Hunt's no-salt  tomatoes 
 3½ cups  water 
 2 cups  pinto beans , cooked 
 pepper  to taste 
 ½ tsp  basil , dried (or 2 tsp fresh) 
 1 can  green beans , cut 
 1 cup  okra , frozen or fresh, cut 
 salt  to taste, optional 
 Directions : 
 Directions: place all ingredients in crockpot. 
 Cover and cook until done, about 6 hours.

 Serving size: 1 cup:
