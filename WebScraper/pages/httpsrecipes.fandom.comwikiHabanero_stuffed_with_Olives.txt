Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Printey Estate in Forney, Texas in 2001.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large jar  pimento -stuffed  green olives 
 3  habanero peppers 
 1 teaspoon Thai  chili paste 
 3 tablespoons minced  garlic 
 Directions : 
 Retain juice from and remove  pimentos . 
 Slice habaneros into thick rings then flatten and stuff into. 
 Add  garlic  and chili paste to juice then return to jar and add juice. 
 Seal, shake and store in refrigerator for up to 1 week.
