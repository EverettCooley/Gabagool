Description : 
 This makes a marvelous "starter". or appetizer.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 ripe  California avocados , halved and "fan" sliced 
 1 pound  mushrooms , sliced 
 1 tbsp  butter , melted 
 4 each slices of  stilton cheese , approx. ¼" thick 
 4 ramekins or glass pyrex dishes. 
 Directions : 
 Sauté the  mushrooms , and divide into the 4 dishes. 
 Place a slice of the stilton on each portion of  mushrooms . 
 Top each piece of stilton with the half of a  California avocado , fan sliced. 
 Brush the  California avocado  with the melted  butter . 
 Microwave only until the stilton has begun to melt, approx. 45–60 seconds.
