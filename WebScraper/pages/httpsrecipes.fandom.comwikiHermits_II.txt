Description : 
 Hermits are a favorite cookie in New England.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅓ cup  butter  or  margarine 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 ½ cup  molasses 
 2  eggs 
 2 cups  flour 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  baking soda 
 2 tsp  baking powder 
 ½ tsp ground  cloves 
 ¼ tsp each:
 mace 
 nutmeg 
 allspice 
 ¾ cup  raisins 
 ½ cup chopped  nuts 
 Directions : 
 Cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy. 
 Add molasses and eggs. Beat well. 
 Sift together dry ingredients; mix with raisins and nuts, then add to molasses mixture. Blend well. 
 Spread evenly in a 12"x8"x2" baking pan. 
 Bake in a 350°F oven for 30 minutes. Cool, then cut into squares.
