Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ oz  kahlúa 
 ½ oz  amaretto  di Saronno® 
 ½ oz  crème de cacao  (white) 
 1 drop  grenadine 
 Directions : 
 Shake well with  ice . 
 Strain into a shot glass. 
 Put a drop of  grenadine  in the centre.
