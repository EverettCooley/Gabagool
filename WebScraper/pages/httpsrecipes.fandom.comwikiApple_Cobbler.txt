Description 
       Apple Cobbler     
 Cook Time : 15 minutes prep, 20 minutes baking 
 Serves : 8
 
 Ingredients 
 Filling 
 6 cups baking  apples , sliced, peeled and cored 
 ⅔ cup  sugar 
 6 tablespoons  water  (divided into 4 and 2) 
 1 tablespoon  cornstarch 
 Topping 
 1 cup  all-purpose flour 
 2 tablespoon  sugar 
 1½ tsp  baking powder 
 ¼ cup  butter 
 1  egg 
 ¼ cup  milk 
 Directions 
 Filling 
 Stir together apples, ⅔ cup sugar and 4 tbsp water in a large sauce-pan. 
 Simmer, covered for 5 minutes or until fruit is tender. 
 Add 2 tablespoons water and 1 tablespoon cornstarch. 
 Cook and stir until thick and bubbly. 
 Topping 
 In a medium bowl stir together flour, 2 tbsp sugar, and baking powder. 
 Cut in butter until mixture resembles large crumbs. 
 Set aside. 
 In a small bowl stir together the egg and milk. 
 Add to flour mixture, just to moisten. 
 Putting It All Together 
 Transfer filling to 2-quart square baking dish. 
 Using a large spoon, drop topping into small mounds on top of the filling. 
 Bake for 20 – 25 minutes in a 400 °F oven (205 °C). 
 Tips 
 Cobblers can be made with peaches, pears, nectarines, apricots, and various types of berries.
