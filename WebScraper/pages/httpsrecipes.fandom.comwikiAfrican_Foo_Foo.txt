 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 8  bananas 
 1 teaspoon of  nutmeg 
 1 teaspoon of  cinnamon 
 1 beaten  egg 
 2 cup of  flour 
 powdered sugar 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mash the  bananas  well in a bowl. Stir in the  nutmeg ,  cinnamon , and  egg . Add 2 cups  flour  and mix well. 
 Let the mixture stand for 10 minutes. Heat a small amount of  oil  in a frying pan. 
 Drop 1 tablespoon at a time of the mixture into the  oil . Fry for 2 to 3 minutes on each side. 
 Drain on paper towels and sprinkle with  powdered sugar . Serve hot
