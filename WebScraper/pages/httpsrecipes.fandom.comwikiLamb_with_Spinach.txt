Description : 
 In Romanian: Miel cu spanac
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb/500 g  lamb 
 3 lbs/1 kg  spinach 
 1 big  onion 
 ½ teaspoon  flour 
 2 tablespoons  lard 
 1 tablespoon  tomato sauce 
 salt 
 lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Fry the meat pieces in  lard , add finely chopped  onion ,  flour , washed, blanched and drained  spinach , a tablespoon of  tomato  sauce, 2 – 3 tablespoons  water  and  salt . 
 Cover the pan and bake until the liquid is reduced. 
 When you serve, add  lemon juice .
