Makes 6 servings. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 1 11-ounce can  corn , drained 
 1/2 cup (2 ounces) shredded  Cheddar  or  Monterey jack   Cheese 
 1/2 cup (2 ounces) shredded  Colby   Cheese 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine , melted 
 1/2 cup Italian breadcrumbs 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 400 degrees. 
 Mix  rice ,  corn  and cheeses in large bowl. 
 Spoon evenly into buttered baking dish. Cover and bake 10 minutes. 
 Blend  butter  and breadcrumbs in small bowl with a fork. Sprinkle evenly over  rice  dish. 
 Return to oven and bake until crumbs are crusty, about 5 minutes.
