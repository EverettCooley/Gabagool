Description : 
 My family enjoys this Sephardic recipe for charoset ("חרוסת" in Hebrew).
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs pitted  dates 
 ½ quart  walnuts   or   almonds 
 2 lbs box of dark  raisins 
 wine  to moisten 
 6  apples , peeled 
 4  oranges  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Grind all the fruits together along with the  nuts . 
 Add enough wind to make a moist paste. 
 Refrigerate until serving 
 Notes : 
 I usually double this recipe as my family enjoys this spread on matzoh in the morning or as a snack during the day.
