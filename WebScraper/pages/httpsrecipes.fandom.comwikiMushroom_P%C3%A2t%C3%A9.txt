Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes For Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  butter 
 6  shallots , finely chopped 
 1 pound  mushrooms , finely diced 
 ½ cup  cream cheese 
 ¼ cup soft whole wheat  breadcrumbs 
 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh  parsley 
 ¼ teaspoon  rosemary 
 ½ teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 1 tablespoon  Dijon mustard 
 2  eggs , lightly beaten 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Melt butter in heavy saucepan. 
 Add shallots and saute 2 minutes. 
 Add rosemary, parsley, salt and pepper. Saute for another 2 minutes. 
 Add mushrooms, cheese, bread crumbs and mustard. Mix well and remove from heat. Adjust seasonings if necessary. 
 Add egg and stir ingredients well. 
 Butter loaf pan and fill with mushroom mixture. 
 Bake at 325 °F for 1½ to 2 hours or until toothpick inserted in middle comes out clean. Allow to cool before unmolding. 
 Nutrition Information : 
 Per serving (10, 2.5 oz-wt.): 
 
 90 calories (50 from fat) | 5g total fat | 3g saturated fat | 3g protein | 7g total carbohydrate (less than 1g dietary fiber, 1g sugars) | 15 mg cholesterol | 90 mg sodium
