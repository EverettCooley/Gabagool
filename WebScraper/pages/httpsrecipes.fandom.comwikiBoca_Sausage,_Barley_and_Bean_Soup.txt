Sit down to a hearty bowl of low fat, cholesterol free bean soup. It's made with Great Northern beans, a good source of dietary fiber, and tomatoes that add a rich source of vitamin C. This recipe is from Kraft
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes For Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Source:  Brand-Name Diabetic Meals in Minutes , a cookbook published by the American Diabetes Association 
 Preparation time: 10 minutes | Total time: 30 min 
 Number of Servings: 6 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x 10 oz pkg frozen Boca  meatless smoked sausage 
 4 cups or 1 qt  water 
 1 x 15½ oz can  Great Northern beans , rinsed and drained  [1] 
 1 x 14½ oz can stewed  tomatoes , with juice 
 1 medium  onion , chopped 
 ½ cup  celery , chopped 
 ½ cup or 2 medium sliced  carrots 
 ½ cup quick-cooking  barley 
 2 tsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 ½ tsp dried  basil  leaves 
 1 dash  hot pepper sauce  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Cut sausage into ¼-inch-thick slices (tip: slice sausage while still partially frozen. Remove sausage from freezer about 10 minutes before slicing or thaw slightly in microwave before slicing). 
 Place sausage and all remaining ingredients except hot pepper sauce in Dutch oven or large saucepan. 
 Bring to boil; cover. 
 Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer 15 minutes or until vegetables are tender. 
 Season to taste with hot pepper sauce. 
 Notes : 
 
 ↑   Substitute  cannellini beans  for Great Northern beans. 
 
 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per 1 cup serving:
 
 230 Calories | 4g Total Fat | 0g Saturated Fat | 0 mg Cholesterol | 650 mg Sodium | 32g Total Carbs | 7g Dietary Fiber | 7g Sugars | 16g Protein 
 Exchanges: 1½ starch | 1 vegetable | 1 meat
