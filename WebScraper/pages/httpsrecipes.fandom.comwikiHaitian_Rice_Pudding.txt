 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 c.  rice  - precooked 
 1/2 c.  brown sugar 
 1 tsp.  cinnamon 
 1/2 tsp.  nutmeg 
 1/2 tsp.  allspice 
 1/2 tsp.  ginger 
 1/2 tsp.  salt  ` 
 1 c.  milk 
 1/2 tsp. cloves 
 1 Tbs.  vanilla 
 1/2 c.  Raisins ,  nuts 
 2  eggs , beaten 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients and bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees. 
 
 DONT FORGET TO BE STICKY!!!!!
