Description : 
 Source:  Simple Recipe  collection
 
 Ingredients : 
 semi-cured cheese 
 1 can of  anchovies  in oil 
 1 tray of  cherry tomatoes 
 extra virgin  olive oil 
 oregano 
 Directions : 
 Place a rolled anchovy on a toothpick along with a thin slice of cheese and a tomato. 
 Spray a dash of oil and sprinkle with oregano.
