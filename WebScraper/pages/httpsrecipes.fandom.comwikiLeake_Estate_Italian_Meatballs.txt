Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Leake Estate in Arlington, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds  Italian sausage 
 1 pound sharp  cheddar  cheese grated 
 1 cup  self rising flour 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 400°F. 
 Take  sausage  out of casing if not bulk style. 
 Crumble  sausage  in a bowl then mix in grated  cheese  and  flour  and shape into 1½ inch meatballs and place on cookie sheets and bake for 18 minutes.
