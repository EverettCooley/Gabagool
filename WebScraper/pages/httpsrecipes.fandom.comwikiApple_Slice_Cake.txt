Ingredients : 
 ⅛ cup frozen  apple juice  concentrate 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 1  egg white 
 1 cup  flour 
 1 tbsp  baking powder 
 1 tbsp  cinnamon 
 1 tbsp  ginger 
 ¼ tbsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Cream  apple juice  concentrate,  sugar  and  egg . 
 Add dry ingredients,  mix , and put into a 9" round non-stick baking dish (if the mixture is not smooth like cake batter, add up to ⅛ cup  water ). 
 Bake 15-20 minutes at 350 °F. 
 When baked and almost cool, slice in half and fill with softened  apple  chunks (microwave  apple  pieces until there is some sauce and then use). 
 Mix ½ cup  icing sugar  with enough  milk  to make icing. 
 Add 3 drops of red  food colouring . 
 Ice the  cake . 
 Recipe can be doubled.
