Crispy Herb  bread 
 
 1 1/2 T.  basil 
 1 T.  rosemary 
 1/2 T.  thyme 
 1 C. (2 Sticks)  butter , Melted 
 1 Pkg. Hot Dog Buns 
 
 Combine First 4 Ingredients And Let Stand Several Hours at Room Temperature. Spread on Buns And Cut Into Strips. Bake at 300 Degrees For 15 to 20 Minutes or Until Crisp.
