 Description : 
 Purchased from the Malin Estate in Caddo Mills, Texas in 1995. Date and source unknown. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 12 ounces  queso blanco   Cheese 
 8 ounces  Pork  chorizo 
 Tortilla chips 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Cook the chorizo and drain off excess fat. 
 Put the chorizo in an oven-proof casserole dish. 
 Grate the  Cheese  over the top and melt in a 350 oven until melted and bubbly. 
 Serve with tortilla chips.
