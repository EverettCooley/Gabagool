Ingredients : 
 Dough : 
 5 cups  flour 
 1 tsp  salt 
 6 tbsp  sugar 
 1 lb  butter 
 ¾ cup  milk 
 1 pkg. (¼ oz)  dry yeast  (or ½ large household  yeast  cake) 
 4  eggs , beaten (save some whites for glaze) 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 Nut mixture : 
 1½ lbs ground nuts ( pecans  or  walnuts ) 
 1½ to 2 cups  sugar  (sweeten to taste) 
 sufficient  milk  to make a spreadable paste 
 Yeast : 
 1 pkg  yeast , dissolved in ¼ cup warm  water 
 ¼ tsp  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Nut mixture : 
 Mix the nut meal and add sufficient  milk  to make a spreadable paste. 
 Yeast : 
 Dissolve  yeast  in  water  and add sugar. 
 Dough : 
 Heat the  milk  and the  butter  together until melted. 
 When mixture is cool, add  yeast  and beaten  eggs ; mix well. 
 Pour this mixture into  flour  and remaining ingredients. 
 Refrigerate dough overnight. 
 Divide dough into 5 equal parts. 
 Roll on floured board. 
 Spread with nut mixture. 
 Then roll like jelly roll. 
 Brush with  egg  whites. 
 Bake at 350°F for ½ hour on lightly greased cookie sheet.
