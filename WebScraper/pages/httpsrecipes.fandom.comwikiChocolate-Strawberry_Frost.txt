Ingredients : 
 1 cup (about 2 large scoops) vanilla fat free frozen  yogurt 
 1 cup frozen  strawberries 
 ½ cup fat free  milk 
 1 packet rich milk chocolate Nestlé® Carnation® Instant Breakfast 
 Directions : 
 Place frozen  yogurt ,  strawberries ,  milk  and Carnation® Instant Breakfast in blender; cover. 
 Blend until smooth.
