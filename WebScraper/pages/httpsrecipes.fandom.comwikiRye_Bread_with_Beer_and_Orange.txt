 Ingredients : 
 Bread Ingredients : 
 1 pack  yeast 
 1 cup medium  rye flour 
 2½ cups  all-purpose flour 
 2 tbsp  wheat germ 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 cup  beer , warmed 
 2 tbsp  molasses 
 2 tsp grated  orange rind 
 2 tbsp  butter , softened 
 ¼ cup warm  water 
 Mix for whipped butter : 
 1 stick  sweet butter 
 1 tbsp  honey 
 2 tbsp  amaretto  or any sweet liqueur of your choice 
 ¼ tsp  almond extract 
 Directions : 
 Add all "bread ingredients" into pan, select white bread and push start. 
 Note: the  beer  gives this hearty  rye  a magnificent taste. 
 Serve this bread with sweetened  whipped butter .
