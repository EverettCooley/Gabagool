Description : 
 Makes: 5 servings, ½ cup each
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups cold of  fat free strawberry milk  Pefered colored pink 
 1 pkg. (4-serving size) Jell-O  lemon  flavor fat free sugar free instant reduced calorie pudding and pie filling 
 1 cup thawed  Cool Whip  FREE whipped topping 
 Directions : 
 Pour pink  milk  into medium bowl. 
 Add dry pudding mix. 
 Beat with wire whisk 2 minutes or until well blended. 
 Stir in whipped topping. 
 Spoon evenly into 5 dessert dishes; cover. 
 Refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving. 
 Store leftover desserts in refrigerator.
