Breakfast Casserole  (Pennsylvania Dutch)
 Categories: Meats, Breakfast, Sausages, CasserolesYield: 6 Servings
 
 6 Large  eggs  2 c  milk 
 1 ts  salt  1 ts Dry  mustard 
 2 Slices White  bread , Cubed 1 lb  Sausage  Browned 
 1 c Sharp/Mild  Cheddar  Shreds * 
 Use Either Sharp or Mild Shredded  Cheddar   Cheese .Beat  eggs , Add  milk ,  salt  And  mustard , Mixing Well. Grease Bottom of 9 x 
 13 Baking Dish. Place in Layer of  bread  Cubes, The  Sausage , Then  Cheese .Pour  egg  Mixture Over The Top. Refrigerate Overnight. Bake at 350 DegreesF. For 45 Minutes. Let Stand About 5 Minutes Before Cutting. Note: For 8People Use 3 Slices of  bread  And 8  eggs . 
 
Enjoy.
