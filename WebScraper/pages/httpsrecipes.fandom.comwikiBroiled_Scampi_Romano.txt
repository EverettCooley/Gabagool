Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Serving Size : 4 
 Ingredients : 
 6 tablespoons dressing Caesar salad dressing 
 2 tablespoons fresh  Parmesan cheese  -- grated 
 1½ pounds large  shrimp  -- peeled and deveined 
 rice   or   angel hair pasta  -- for serving 
 green vegetable, optional 
 Directions : 
 Combine the reserved dressing and the  cheese . 
 Toss the  shrimp  with this marinade, cover, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour. 
 Remove  shrimp  from the marinade and grill or broil 4 inches form a hot fire for 1 to 2 minutes perside, basting with additional marinade when you turn the  shrimp . 
 Cook until the  shrimp  are firm and opaque. 
 Serve with  rice  or  pasta  and a green vegetable. 
 Enjoy.
