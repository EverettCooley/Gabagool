 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/4 cup  margerine  softened 
 2 tsp  evaporated milk (unsweetened) 
 1 tsp  butter  flavoring 
 1 cup Powder  Sugar  replacement 
 Directions : 
 1  cream  together the  margerine ,  milk  and  butter  flavoring until fluffy.stir in the  Sugar  replacement. 
2 Knead until smooth.roll out into marble size balls and either press into mold and unmold onto wax paper,or place balls on wax paper and flatten slightly. 
3 Recipe makes 45 pieces 3 pieces per serving exchange for 3 pieces: 1/3 low fat  milk  calories for 3 pieces: 56 calories?
