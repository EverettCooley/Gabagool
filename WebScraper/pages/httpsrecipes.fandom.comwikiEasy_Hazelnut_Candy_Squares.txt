 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  coating chocolate 
 1/2 cup  Raisins 
 1/2 cup  coconut 
 3/4 cup roasted & chopped Oregon  hazelnuts 
 3/4 cup toasted  rice  cereal 
 Directions : 
 1 Melt  chocolate  on the top of a double boiler over hot tap  water . (Not on the stove!) 
2 Add remaining ingredients and stir to mix well. 
3 Pour into a 9x13-inch metal pan that has no coating in the pan. 
4 Chill until partially set and then score into small squares. 
5 Return to refrigerator until hard. 
6 Take out of the pan and break into scored pieces.
