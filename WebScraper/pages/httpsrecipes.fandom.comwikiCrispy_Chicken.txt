Description : 
 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 chicken  (4 pieces of  chicken  2 lb total) 
 Coating mix : 
 2 cup dry  bread crumbs 
 ⅓ cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 1 tsp  oregano 
 1 tsp  basil 
 ½ tsp  garlic powder 
 Directions : 
 Combine the coating mix ingredients and mix well. 
 Measure out ¾ cup into a plastic bag. 
 Place  chicken  pieces into bag and shake to coat. 
 Place  chicken  pieces in a baking dish with heaviest sections towards the outside of the dish. 
 Cover with waxed paper. 
 Microwave at medium 70% for 18 – 20 minutes or until juices run clear. 
 Allow 5 minutes after cooking time. 
 Variations : 
 Mexican - substitute  chili powder  or  oregano , add ⅛ tsp  cayenne . 
 Cheddar - substitute  cheddar  cheese for Parmesan. 
 Notes : 
 Coating mix keeps well in refrigerator. 
 In microwave cooking, natural cheeses ( Swiss ,  cheddar ,  Parmesan ,  romano ) melt, brown and crisp. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving (each 1  Chicken  piece):
 
 3 protein choices, ½ starch choice 
 8 g carbohydrates | 25 g protein | 10 g fat | 289 calories
