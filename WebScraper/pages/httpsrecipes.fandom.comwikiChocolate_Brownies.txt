chocolate  Brownies
 
 
 200 g  dark chocolate  Chopped 
 250 g  butter 
 1 3/4 Cups  brown sugar 
 4  eggs 
 1 Cup  plain flour 
 1/4 Teaspoon  baking powder 
 1/3 Cup  cocoa powder 
 
Preheat The Oven to 180c. Place The  chocolate  And  butter  in a Saucepan OverLow Heat And Stir Until Smooth. Place The  sugar ,  eggs ,  flour ,  baking powder , cocoa , And  chocolate  Mixture in a Bowl And Mix to Combine. Pour The Mixture IntoA Shallow 20 x 30Â cm Tin Lined With Non Stick Baking Paper. Bake For 50Minutes or Until Set. Cool in The Tin And Cut Into 40 Small Squares. Makes 40Pieces.
