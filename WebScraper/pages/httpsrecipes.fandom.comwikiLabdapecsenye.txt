Description : 
 Hamburgers
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ lb  ground beef 
 ½ lb  ground pork 
 4 slices of  white bread 
 1 tsp  salt 
 2 raw  eggs 
 1 small  onion  finely chopped 
 1 clove of  garlic , mashed 
 2 tbsp of fresh  parsley , minced 
 ½ tsp ground  black pepper 
 ½ cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Saute chopped  onion  in a little fat or  oil . 
 Wet bread with  water , tear into small pieces. 
 In mixing bowl beat  eggs  well. 
 Add meat, bread and the rest of the ingredients and mix lightly with your hands. 
 Form mixture into medium sized balls, about 5 or 6. 
 Flatten slightly. 
 Fry slowly in ½ inch of Crisco,  lard  or  oil  until tender and all sides are nicely browned and some what crispy. 
 Let each side cook before attempting to turn with spatula. 
 Serve hamburgers hot along with a  potato  and vegetable dish, and perhaps Hungarian  cucumber  salad.
