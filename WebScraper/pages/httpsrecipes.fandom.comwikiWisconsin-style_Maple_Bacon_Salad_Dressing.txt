Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 slices  bacon , cut into 1-inch pieces 
 olive oil 
 ⅔ cup chopped  Bermuda onion 
 1 clove  garlic , crushed 
 ½ cup  red wine vinegar 
 ½ cup Wisconsin  maple syrup 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 2 tablespoons minced or 1 tablespoon dried  chives 
 Directions : 
 Stir-fry  bacon  in a large saucepan until extremely crisp. 
 Remove bacon with slotted spoon and drain on paper towels. 
 Pour all of bacon drippings into a glass measuring cup and if necessary, add olive oil to bring the level to ½ cup. 
 Return to saucepan. 
 Add  onions  and garlic and sauté until  onions  are translucent. 
 Remove pan from heat and pour in  vinegar . 
 Add  maple syrup . 
 Over low heat, blend mixture with wire whisk and heat until warm. 
 Whisk in  salt ,  mayonnaise  and chives until smooth and blended. 
 Remove from heat. 
 This dressing is especially good over  spinach  or other green salads.
