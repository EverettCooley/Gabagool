Ingredients : 
 2 x 3 – 3½ lb  chickens  jointed and seasoned to taste 
 1 teaspoon  paprika 
 8 oz  butter  melted 
 2 tablespoons  curry powder 
 pinch of ground  ginger 
 salt  to taste 
 black pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Combine melted  butter ,  curry  powder,  ginger ,  salt  and  paprika . 
 Set  chicken  in shallow roasting pan, top sides down and thoroughly baste on both sides with combined  butter   curry , etc. 
 Then bake at 375°F. 
 For about 20 minutes, basting often with the sauce. 
 Turn all pieces of  chicken  to continue baking for a further 20 minutes. 
 Pour off sauce from roasting pan place in a small saucepan and slice an  onion  and add to sauce, then place over low flame to simmer gently. 
 Set  chicken  on grill to brown to a nice rich color, spooning some of the sauce over it from time to time. 
 Pour rest of sauce over the  chicken  and serve at once.
