 Description : 
       Apple Cinnamon Couscous     
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  apple juice 
 2 tbl  dried apples  diced 
 2 tbl  Raisins 
 1 tsp  cinnamon  or to taste 
 1/2 cup  couscous 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Put  apple juice ,  dried apple ,  Raisins  and  cinnamon  in a saucepan. 
 Bring to a boil. 
 Stir in  couscous , cover, and remove from heat. 
 Let stand 5 minutes. 
 Fluff  couscous  with a fork and serve.
