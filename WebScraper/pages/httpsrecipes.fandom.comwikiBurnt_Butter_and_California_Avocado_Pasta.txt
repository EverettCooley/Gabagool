 Description : 
 A delicious main dish or take to a buffet.
 
 Ingredients : 
 spaghetti  Noodles enough for desired amount of servings 
 1 stick  butter 
 1 clove  garlic 
 1  California avocado  cut and diced 
 Â½ Pound  feta   Cheese  grated 
 All other ingredients are optional (Put in whatever you like and as much as you like - these are my favorites) 
 Bacon  bits 
 Diced  tomatoes 
 Chopped  black olives 
 Chopped  green onions 
 Directions : 
 Cook and drain  Pasta  according to instructions on box or your preference.
 In small pan simmer  butter  and  garlic  until  butter  almost burns. 
 Pour immediately over drained  Pasta  noodles. 
 Gently toss in 1/2 of the  avocado  and  feta   Cheese . 
 Toss in any other ingredients you wish to add.
 Serve and top with remaining  avocado  and  feta   Cheese .
