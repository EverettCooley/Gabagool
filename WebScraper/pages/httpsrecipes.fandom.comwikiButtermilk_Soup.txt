 Description : 
 Not everyone will enjoy this, but if you like the taste of  buttermilk , this is something for you.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 quarts  buttermilk 
 3/4 cup  flour 
 Sugar  or syrup 
 Directions : 
 Mix  flour  with a little  water  until smooth; add 1 pint of the  buttermilk . 
 Let come to a boil, stirring constantly to prevent separating. 
 Add the rest of the  buttermilk , a little at a time. 
 Serve hot with  Sugar  or syrup.
