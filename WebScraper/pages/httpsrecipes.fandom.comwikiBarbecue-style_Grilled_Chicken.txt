Description : 
 Contributed by Dorie at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  chicken  (3-3½ lb) cut into 8 pieces  or  (8 boneless  chicken breasts ) 
 1 cup  ketchup 
 ¼ cup  brown sugar , packed 
 2 tbsp.  white vinegar 
 1 tbsp.  instant minced onion 
 2 tbsp.  Worcestershire sauce 
 1 tsp.  dry  mustard 
 2 tbsp.  prepared mustard 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Coat a 9x13 baking dish with nonstick cooking spray; place the chicken in the dish and bake 25 minutes. 
 Meanwhile in a separate bowl, combine the remaining ingredients. Preheat the grill to medium heat. 
 Coat the chicken with the sauce mixture then grill for 15 – 20 minutes, or until the juices run clear and chicken is no longer pink; turn halfway through grilling. 
 Serve hot and plain over rice, or with any main dish, side dish, or appetizer.
