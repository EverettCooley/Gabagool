Ingredients : 
 2 lbs.  Velveeta  cheese 
 1 sm. jar El Paso picone sauce 
 1 can diced  tomatoes  and  chilies 
 1 lb.  ground beef , cooked & drained 
 Tortilla chips 
 Directions : 
 Mix the cheese, picone sauce and chilies and the hamburger in a crock pot. 
 Heat until cheese is melted and all stirred together. 
 Serve with the tortilla chips.
