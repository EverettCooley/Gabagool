 Glazed Sweet Potatoes  from the Wikibooks Cookbook, original source of recipe, licensed under the GNU Free Documentation License.
 
 Cook Time : 30 minutes 
 Serves : 6 
 Ingredients : 
 Â½ cup (120ml)  maple syrup 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 1 can (40 oz.) [1100g]  sweet potatoes , drained 
 Directions : 
 Bring syrup and butter just to boil in large skillet on medium heat. Reduce heat to low, simmer about 2 minutes. 
 Add sweet potatoes. Cook on low heat 10 minutes, gently turning sweet potatoes frequently to glaze. 
 Garnish with  pecan  halves, if desired.
