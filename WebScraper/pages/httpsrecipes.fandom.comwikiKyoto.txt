      Kyoto     
 Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 oz  gin 
 1 oz  Midori ® melon liqueur 
 ½ oz  dry vermouth 
 ¼ tsp fresh  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Pour all ingredients into a mixing glass half-filled with  ice  cubes. Stir well. 
 Strain into a chilled cocktail glass. 
 Garnish with a melon ball, and serve.
