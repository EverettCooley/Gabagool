 Description : 
 This dish is a great one to prepare when you’ve got leftover tortillas. Kids will love the sweet and spicy taste of tortilla strips.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 dozen  corn  tortillas, several days old 
 1 cup  onions , chopped 
 1/4 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 2 teaspoons  chili powder 
 1 teaspoon  cumin  powder 
 1 1/2 cup  cottage cheese 
 1 1/2 cup crushed  tomatoes 
 1 cup (6 oz) grated jack or  Cheddar   Cheese 
 salt  to taste 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Tear tortillas into 1/2-inch strips. Sauté  onions  in a pan until soft and light brown. Add tortilla,  chili powder ,  garlic ,  cumin  and  salt . Toss until tortilla strips look wilted. Remove from heat and place tortilla mixture in a baking dish. Put  cottage cheese  and  tomatoes  in a blender and puree until smooth. Pour over tortilla strips. Sprinkle with grated  Cheese . Bake at 350 °F for 20 minutes or until  Cheese  becomes slightly browned.
