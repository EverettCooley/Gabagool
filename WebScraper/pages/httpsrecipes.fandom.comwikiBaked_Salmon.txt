Ingredients : 
 1 whole pink  salmon 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 5 cloves  garlic 
 1 tbsp seasoning  salt 
 Directions : 
 spread open the fish 
 sprinkle with seasonning  salt , let stand for 10 minutes. 
 in a small bowl mix  mayo  and minced  garlic 
 spread on seasoned fish baked at 350 until brown
