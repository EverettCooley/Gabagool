Ingredients : 
 1 (15 ounce) can  black beans , rinsed and drained 
 4  taco shells , crushed 
 ½ cup chopped  onions 
 ½ cup chopped  green peppers 
 ⅓ cup minced  cilantro 
 2  egg whites 
 2 tablespoons chopped  jalapeno peppers 
 2 teaspoons  cumin 
 2 teaspoons  chili powder 
 2 tablespoons minced  garlic 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  pepper 
 2 lbs lean  ground beef 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients  except  ground  beef  in a large bowl. 
 Add the  beef  and mix well. 
 Press into a loaf pan coated with  cooking spray , bake at 350°F for 40 – 50 minutes, until done. 
 Cool for 10 minutes before removing from pan.
