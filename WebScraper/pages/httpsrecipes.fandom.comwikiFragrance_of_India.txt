Description : 
 That wonderful aroma that is so familiar with Basmati is due to the natural chemical 2-acetyl-1-pyroline that is found in all rices, but is found in higher concentrations in Basmati.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  basmati rice 
 4 cups  water 
 1 tsp  curry 
 pinch  sea salt 
 Directions : 
 Wash  rice  thoroughly under tap, removing starch. 
 Soak  rice  in cold  water  for 30 minutes, then drain. 
 Bring  water  to boil and stir in  rice , simmering for 5 minutes, drain off  water  and add  curry  and  salt . 
 Let  rice  stand for 5 minutes and fluff gently with fork. 
 Yields 3 cups of cooked  rice .
