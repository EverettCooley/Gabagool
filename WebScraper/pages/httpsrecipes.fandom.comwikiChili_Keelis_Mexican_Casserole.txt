chili  Keelis Mexican Casserole
 
 1 Lb. Lean Ground  Beef 
 1/4 Lb. Diced Mexican  Sausage  (more if You Like) 
 ripe olives  to Taste
 
 1 Large  Onion , Chopped 
 1 clove  garlic , Mashed 
 1 Can (14 Oz) Enchilada Sauce 
 1 Can  tomato  Sauce (8 Oz) 
 thyme  to Taste
 
 1  bay leaf 
 Yellow  Cheese , Grated
 
 6 Tortillas 
 
Saut? Meat,  Onion ,  garlic , And Until Lightly Browned. Add Sauces And Seasonings. in a Large Greased Casserole Make a Layer of Tortillas, Then a Layer of  Cheese , Then a Layer of Meat Mixture. Repeat Until All Ingredients Are Used. Bake at 375 Until bubbly, About 30 Minutes. Serve With  sour cream . Served With a Green Salad, With Light  oil  Dressing, This Makes a Delicious meal. Source: Mrs. Helen B. Montgomery
