 Description : 
 Better Homes and Gardens menu: Chicken Parmesan 202 calories
 
 Ingredients : 
 chicken  breast, (about 2 ¼ pounds total), skinned, boned, and halved 
 ½ of a 10-ounce package frozen chopped  spinach , thawed and drained 
 ⅓ cup  Parmesan cheese , grated 
 ¼ tsp  Italian seasoning , crushed 
 ¼ cup  green onion , sliced 
 1 tbsp  margarine 
 1 tbsp  flour , all-purpose 
 ½ cup  milk , skim 
 1 tbsp  pimento , chopped 
 Directions : 
 In a small mixing bowl combine  cheese  and Italain seasoning. 
 Roll  chicken  pieces in  cheese  mixture to coat lightly; set remaining  cheese  mixture aside. 
 Arrange pieces in an 8x8x2-inch baking dish. 
 In a small saucepan cook  onion  in hot  margarine  till tender. 
 Stir in  flour ; add  milk  all at once. 
 Cook and stir till bubbly; stir in drained  spinach  and  pimento . 
 Spoon  spinach  mixture over  chicken ; sprinkle with remaining  cheese  mixture. 
 Bake, uncovered, in a 350 °F oven for 30 to 35 minutes or till tender. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving: 
 
 202 calories. 
 30g pro. 
 4g carbo. 
 7g fat 
 77 mg chol.
