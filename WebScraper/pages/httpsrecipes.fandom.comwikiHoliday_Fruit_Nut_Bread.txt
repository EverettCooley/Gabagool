Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  butter 
 1 cup  sugar 
 2  eggs , beaten 
 3  bananas , mashed 
 2 cups  flour 
 1 tsp  soda 
 1½ cups  nuts 
 ½ cup  chocolate chips 
 ½ cup  maraschino cherries 
 Description : 
 Cream butter and sugar and add beaten eggs, then mashed bananas. 
 Sift flour, salt and soda together. 
 Stir just to moisten and then stir in nuts, chocolate chips and maraschino cherries. 
 Bake in a greased 9 by 5 by 3 inch loaf pan at 350°F for about 40 minutes.
