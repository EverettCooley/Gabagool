 Contributed by Delma at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 12 ripe  tomatoes 
 9 medium size  onions 
 12  apples 
 3 cups  sugar 
 3 cups  vinegar 
 1 tsp  pepper 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 Â¼ cup  salt 
 1 tsp  mustard 
 1 tsp  ginger 
 1 tsp  cloves 
 Directions : 
 Peel  tomatoes ,  onions , and  apples . 
 Chop or put through food grinder. 
 Boil these ingredients with  vinegar . 
 Add dry ingredients and boil longer, until thick-watch carefully so it doesn't burn. 
 Put in sterilized jars and seal. 
 If you want this to be smooth, can be put through colander.
