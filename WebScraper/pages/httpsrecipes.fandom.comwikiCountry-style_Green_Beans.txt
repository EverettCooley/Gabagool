 Source: The Illustrated Encyclopedia of American Cooking 
 Typed By: Susan 
 Yield: 6 – 8 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ lb.  bacon , diced 
 1 x 14-oz pkg. frozen  green beans 
 2 small  onions , chopped, red, white, or yellow only 
 2 tsp.  salt 
 1 tsp.  sugar , white only 
 ½ tsp.  red pepper 
 1 lb  new potatoes , peeled 
 Directions : 
 Partially cover bacon with water in large saucepan; then boil for 20 minutes. 
 Add beans, onions, salt, sugar and red pepper. 
 Cover; cook for 15 minutes. 
 Add potatoes; cook for about 15 minutes or until potatoes are tender. Serve in a large vegetable dish as well as with other side dishes or appetizers.
