Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 cup white  flour 
 2 cup  graham flour 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1/2 cup  shortening 
 1/4 cup  granulated sugar 
 1 cup  Raisins  or  currants  or both 
 1  egg  slightly beaten 
 1 cup sweet  milk 
 1 cup  buttermilk 
 3/4 cup  molasses 
 Directions : 
 In large bowl: combine dry ingredients, cut in  shortening . 
 Add  Raisins  and/or  currants  & mix well. 
 In small bowl: combine wet ingredients & mix only until  flour  is dampened. 
 Bake in 2 greased loaf pans at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
