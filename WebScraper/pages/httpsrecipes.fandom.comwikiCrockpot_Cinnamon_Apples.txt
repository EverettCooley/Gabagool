Description : 
 Serve these sweet apples alone or over ice cream or pound cake
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source:  Southern Living  2007 Sweet Endings 
 Prep: 16 minutes | Cook: 6 hr 
 Makes 6 – 8 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 6 medium  Granny Smith apples , peeled and cut into eighths 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 ½ cup firmly packed  dark brown sugar 
 ½ cup chopped  walnuts 
 ½ cup  maple syrup 
 ¼ cup sweetened  dried cranberries  (like Craisins) 
 ¼ cup  butter , melted 
 2 tsp ground  cinnamon 
 2 tbsp  water 
 1 tbsp  cornstarch 
 Directions : 
 Combine apples and lemon juice in a 4-qt slow cooker; toss well to coat. 
 Add brown sugar and next 5 ingredients, combining well. 
 Cover and cook on low 3 hours. 
 Stir together water and cornstarch in a small bowl, stir into apples. 
 Cover and cook on low 3 more hours or until apples are tender.
