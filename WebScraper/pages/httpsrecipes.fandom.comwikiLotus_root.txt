 About Lotus root : 
 Wikipedia Article  About Lotus Root on Wikipedia 
 The lotus root's creamy white flesh has the crisp texture of a raw potato and a flavor akin to fresh coconut. Besides the fresh form, it's also available canned, dried and candied. Lotus root is used as a vegetable as well as in sweet dishes.
