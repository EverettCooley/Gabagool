 Description : 
 Dried  nettle leaves , when crushed and steeped in  water , make tea. Fresh  nettle leaves , after being boiled several times, are good  spinach  substitutes.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pint young nettle tops 
 1 oz  butter 
 1 oz  oatmeal 
 10 oz  water , stock or  milk 
 salt  and  pepper 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Wear rubber gloves when you wash young nettle tops in cold  water . Do this several times. Chop nettle tops finely. In a saucepan, melt  butter . Add  oatmeal  and fry until golden brown. Add  water , stock or  milk . Bring mixture to a boil. Stir constantly. Add chopped nettle tops, and  salt  and  pepper  to taste. Bring to a boil then simmer for 45 minutes over low heat. Serves 4.
