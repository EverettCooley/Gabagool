Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Servings: 4 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups long-grain  brown rice 
 1 can  beef consommé  (10 oz) 
 1 can  French onion soup  (10 oz) 
 1 can sliced  mushrooms , not drained (4 oz) 
 1 stick  butter , cut into bits 
 Directions : 
 In a medium size oven baking dish combine  rice ,  beef consommé , French onion soup and  mushrooms . 
 Stir all ingredients together, then dab butter bits on top. 
 Cover and bake at 350°F for 60 minutes. 
 Don't peek! Enjoy.
