Ingredients : 
 1 cup 2%  milk 
 2 tbsp bitter- sweet chocolate  chips 
 1 tsp  brown sugar 
 1 tsp instant  coffee 
 Directions : 
 Heat everything in a small pan, stirring constantly till  chocolate  melts and  milk  is warmed through. 
 Pour in a large cup and enjoy it warm.
