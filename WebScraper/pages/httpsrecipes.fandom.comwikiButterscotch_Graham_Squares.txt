Description 
 Kids Food for Fun  by the South Carolina Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Ingredients 
 12  graham crackers 
 ½ cup (1 stick)  butter 
 ½ cup firmly packed  light brown sugar 
 ½ cup chopped  pecans 
 Directions 
 Preheat oven to 325 °F. 
 Break each graham cracker in half. 
 Place close together on 17¼ x 11½ x 1" non-stick jelly roll pan. 
 Place butter and brown sugar in a glass measuring cup. 
 Cover loosely with plastic wrap. 
 Microwave on high 1 minute, until butter is melted. 
 Remove from microwave. 
 Stir. 
 Let cool at room temperature 2 minutes. 
 Add pecans, mix well. 
 Put a teaspoon of mixture on center of each cracker. 
 Bake 7 minutes on center oven rack. 
 Use pot holder and carefully remove from oven. 
 Let cool 3 minutes. 
 Remove from baking pan with metal turner. 
 Place on wire rack to cool completely.
