 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 medium avacados 
 1 small  Onion 
 2 Tbsp  cider vinegar 
 2 Tbsp  lemon juice 
 ½ tsp tabasco 
 1 jalapeño seeded,finely chopped 
 1 large  tomato  diced 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Peel, and mash  avocado  with a fork. 
 Mix in juices, then  Onion , then  pepper  and  tomato .
 Serve cold, with  corn  chips and favorite beverage
