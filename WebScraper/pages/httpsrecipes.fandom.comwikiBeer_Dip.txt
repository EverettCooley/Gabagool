 Ingredients : 
 1 	pack 	(8 ozs)  cream cheese , soften 
 8 	oz 	Pasteurized process 
 Cheddar   Cheese  with 
 jalapeño pepper , cubed 
 1/3 	cup 	 beer 
 1 	cup 	 black beans , drained/rinsed 
 1/2 	cup 	Sliced scallions 
 1/2 	cup 	Chopped  tomatoes 
 Chips or tortilla crisps 
 Directions : 
 Over low heat, cook  cream cheese , pasteurized  Cheese  and  beer , until melted and smooth. 
Stir in beans, scallions and  tomatoes ; heat through. 
Serve warm or cold as a dip with pretzel chips or tortilla crisps. 
Makes 3 ½ cups.
