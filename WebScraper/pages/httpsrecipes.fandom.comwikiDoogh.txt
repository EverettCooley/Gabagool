Doogh , or dough, is a Persian yogurt drink. Carbonated doogh is a popular soft-drink in Iran.
 
 This recipe was provided by Persiancity.com 
 Original recipe 
 Ingredients : 
 one glass  yogurt 
 2-3 glasses water 
 1 teaspoon dried  mint 
 1 teaspoon dried  pennyroyal  (optional) 
 salt 
 black pepper  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Beat yogurt until it flows smoothly. 
 Add mint, pennyroyal, salt, and black pepper, and mix well. 
 Add water to yogurt gradually and mix as you add. The quantity of water can be adjusted to obtain the desired density. 
 Place in the frig for a few hours. 
 Mix again before drinking.
