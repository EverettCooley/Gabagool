Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 3/04/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 6  lasagna noodles 
 1 pkg.  Italian seasoning 
 2 x 8 oz blocks  mozzarella  cheese 
 1 pkg. shredded  mozzarella 
 1 lb  ricotta cheese 
 1 cup  Parmesan cheese 
 ½ cup  parsley 
 1 tbsp  basil 
 dash  pepper 
 dash  salt 
 olive oil 
 egg 
 Directions : 
 Combine  ricotta cheese ,  egg ,  mozzarella , ½ cup  Parmesan cheese , ½ cup  parsley , dash  black pepper  and 1 tbsp  basil . 
 Mix well with fork and set aside. 
 Spoon small amount of lasagna sauce on bottom of pan. 
 Layer noodles, then dollop lasagna filling about 2" apart; carefully spread out. 
 Top with  mozzarella   cheese  slices, spoon lasagna filling on top of this. 
 Sprinkle ½ of the  Parmesan cheese  mixture on top of this. 
 Repeat process. 
 Cover pan. 
 Cook 45 minutes. 
 Remove foil and cook 10 more minutes. 
 Take out and let sit 10 more minutes before serving.
