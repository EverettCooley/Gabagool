Ingredients : 
 4  green bananas 
 2 chopped  green chilli 
 2  potatoes 
 ¼ tsp ground  cinnamon 
 4 tsp  soyabean oil 
 ¼ tsp ground  cardamon 
 1 ts  cumin  seeds 
 2 tsp  sugar 
 2  onions , minced 
 2 tsp  lemon juice 
 ½ tsp  ground turmeric 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Peel  banana  and cut into ½ inch thick pieces. 
 In a sauce pan add two cups of  water , ½ tsp  salt  and ½ tsp  turmeric . 
 Add  banana , boil for tree minutes. 
 Drain water in 2 tsp of  oil , fry  banana  slices until both sides are brown. 
 Peel and cube  potatoes . 
 Boil in  salt   water  and drain. 
 In a frying pan, heat 2 tsp  oil . 
 Add  cumin  seeds. 
 Then add ground  onion , green  chilli ,  turmeric ,  cinnamon ,  cardamom  and ½ cup of  water . 
 Stir and fry until  oil  separates from spices. 
 Add  potatoes  and fry 2 minutes. 
 Add  banana , salt , sugar ,  lemon juice  and ½ cup of  water . 
 Cover cook over low heat for 5 minutes. 
 Serve hot with  rice  or chapati.
