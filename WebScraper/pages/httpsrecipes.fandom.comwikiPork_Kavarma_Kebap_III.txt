 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 0.800 kg of  Pork  , 
 2  onions , 
 1-2  carrots , 
 1 slice of  celery , 
 5-6  leeks , 
 1 spoonful of  tomato  paste, 
 1/3 cupful of wine , 
 salt , 
 red pepper, 
 pepper , 
 1-2  laurel leaves , 
 2-3 small  hot peppers 
 Directions : 
 Cut the meat into small pieces and brown it together with the thinly sliced  onions ,  carrots  and  celery . 
 Add the  leeks  cut in pieces, the  salt , the  pepper , the red pepper, the  tomato  paste and 1 cupful of hot  water . 
 Let the meat stew for 15–20 minutes on a slow fire and then add the wine, the  bay leaves  and the  hot peppers . 
 The meal is done when it is left in its own juice.
