Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 slices  bacon , diced 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1 cup uncooked  rice 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  ground turmeric 
 ⅛ teaspoon  ground red pepper 
 2 cups  water 
 1 medium fresh  tomato , chopped 
 ½ cup chopped  green bell pepper 
 1 cup diced fully-cooked  ham  (optional) 
 1 cup dried  black beans , cooked and well-seasoned 
 Directions : 
 Cook  bacon  in large skillet over low heat. Pour off fat. Return 1 tablespoon to skillet. 
 Add  onion  and  garlic ; cook over medium heat 2 minutes. 
 Stir in  rice ,  salt ,  turmeric , red pepper and  water . Bring to a boil; reduce heat, cover, and simmer 10 minutes. 
 Add  tomato ,  green pepper  and  ham ; simmer 5 minutes longer, or until  rice  is tender and liquid is absorbed. 
 Serve yellow  rice  in deep bowls and spoon  beans  in center.
