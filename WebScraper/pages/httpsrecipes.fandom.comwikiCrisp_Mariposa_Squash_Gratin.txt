 Description : 
 Always check the ingredients to make sure the product is vegan.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 clove(s)  garlic 
 1 Tablespoon(s) or more,  all-purpose flour 
 1/2 Teaspoon(s)  marjoram 
 1 Teaspoon(s)  salt 
 1 Pinch(s)  black pepper 
 2 1/2 Pound(s) cut into 1/3-in.dice  butternut squash 
 2 Tablespoon(s) extra-virgin  olive oil 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 325F. In a small bowl, combine well eht  garlic ,  flour ,  marjoram ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 In a well oiled, 2-quart (2-L) shallow baking dish, toss  Squash  pieces with  flour  mixture, coating evenly, Drizzle coated  Squash  with 1 tablesboon of the  oil  and toss well. 
 Drizzle the remaining  olive oil  over the mixture, cover dish tightly with foil and cut 3 slits in the foil to allow steam to escape. 
 Bake  Squash  mixture in the upper third of preheated over for 40 minutes; remove foil and toss mixture gently. Increase heat to 400F and bake  Squash  40 minutes longer, or until browned lightly.
