Description 
 Bread Recipes  from the South Carolina Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Ingredients 
 1 cup white self-rising  cornmeal 
 ½ cup  flour  (self-rising) 
 3 tbsp  sugar 
 ½ cup  oil 
 1 cup  milk 
 Directions 
 Pour oil in baking pan to ½ inch high and heat pan until hot. 
 Pour mixture in hot pan. 
 Bake at 350 °F for 20 minutes.
