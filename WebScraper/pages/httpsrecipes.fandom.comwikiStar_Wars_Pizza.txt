 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds pizza dough, one pound for each pizza 
 1 pound mozzarella cheese, grated - 8oz per pizza 
 2 cups pizza sauce - 1 cup per pizza 
 2 - 6oz cans sliced olives 
 2 green bell peppers, sliced and blanched 
 1 head of  Broccoli , cut into florets and blanched 
 1 cup sliced  Mushrooms 
 Directions : 
 Roll out the dough. Spread the pizza sauce on the pizza. Arrange the toppings to look like the character of your choice! See photos for examples and ideas.
