 Description : 
 Makes 8 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound ground  Turkey 
 1 cup packaged stuffing mix 
 4  egg  whites or 1/2 cup  egg substitute 
 1/4 cup  chicken broth 
 1 tablespoon grated  Onion 
 2 tablespoons  parsley  flakes 
 1 teaspoon dried  oregano  leaves 
 1/2 teaspoon  garlic salt 
 Vegetable  cooking spray 
 2 cups  spaghetti  sauce 
 3 cups hot cooked  brown rice 
 Directions : 
 Combine  Turkey , stuffing mix,  egg  whites, broth,  Onion ,  parsley ,  oregano  and  garlic salt  in large bowl; mix lightly. 
 Using about 1/4 cup mixture form into balls. 
 Place balls on shallow baking sheet coated with  cooking spray . 
 Bake at 425 degrees 20 minutes or until done. 
 Heat sauce; add cooked meat balls to sauce. 
 Serve over  brown rice .
