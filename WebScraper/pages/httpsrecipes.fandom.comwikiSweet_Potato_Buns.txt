 Ingredients : 
 4 tbs  butter , melted 
 1 tsp  yeast 
 ½ cup lukewarm  water 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 1 c cooked mashed  sweet potatoes  well drained. 
 ½ cup  milk 
 1 tsp  salt 
 3½ to 4 cups  flour 
 2  eggs , well beaten 
 Directions : 
 Add  butter  to  sweet potatoes , beat to blend. 
 Dissolve  yeast  in lukewarm  water . 
 Heat  milk  to steaming and add to dissolved  yeast . 
 Add  potatoes   eggs ,  salt ,  sugar , and 2 cups of the  flour . 
 Spoon into a large bowl. 
 Cover bowl with a damp cloth, and let rise in warm place until dough is doubled about 1 hr. 
 Sift in remaining  flour . 
 Knead until smooth 10 minutes. 
 Form into rolls about ½ in diameter. 
 Place close together on buttered baking sheet. 
 Cover and let rise again until double. 
 Bake 20-30 minutes until tops are golden at 375°F.
