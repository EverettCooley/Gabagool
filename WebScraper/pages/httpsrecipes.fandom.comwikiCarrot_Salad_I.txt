This Estonian Carrot Salad recipe comes under the category of "Raw Salads". It is an easy meal to create and doesn't need a lot of supplies.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup of  carrots 
 ½ cup of  apples 
 1 table spoon of  sugar 
 1 teaspoon of  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Grate the raw  carrots  and the  apples . 
 Mix them together well. 
 In the end, season it with  sugar . 
 Before serving, garnish it with  parsley .
