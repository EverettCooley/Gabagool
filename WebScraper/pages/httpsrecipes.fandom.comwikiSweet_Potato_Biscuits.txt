Description 
 Sweet Potatoes Canned  by the US Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Ingredients 
 1 cup  flour 
 3 teaspoons  baking powder 
 3 tablespoons  sugar 
 3 tablespoons  margarine  or  butter 
 1 cup mashed  sweet potatoes 
 ½ cup skim  milk 
 Directions 
 Preheat oven to 425 °F. 
 Combine flour, baking powder, and sugar. Cut in margarine. Stir in mashed potatoes. Gradually add milk to form dough. 
 Roll or pat dough to ½ inch thickness on floured board. Cut with biscuit cutter. 
 Bake biscuits on a greased cookie sheet for 12–15 minutes.
