Ingredients : 
 2 cups  flour 
 1½ tsp  baking powder 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  baking soda 
 1  egg 
 ⅛ cup soft  shortening 
 ¾ cup  orange juice 
 thin rind of ½  orange 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 cup  cranberries 
 1 cup  nuts 
 Directions : 
 Sift together  flour ,  baking powder ,  salt  and  baking soda . 
 Mix in blender  egg ,  shortening ,  orange juice ,  orange  rind and  sugar . 
 Add  cranberries  and  nuts , for 2 cycles at "chop" setting. 
 Empty into  flour  mixture. 
 Mix by hand until moistened. 
 Bake in a greased 9x5 pan at 350°F for 50 to 60 minutes.
