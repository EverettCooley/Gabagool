 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 6 x  banana . 
 14 x strips  bacon  cut in halves. 
 Brown Sugar. 
 Honey. 
 Directions : 
 Cut  banana  in 4 crosswise. 
 Prepare Honey and Brown Sugar glaze; heat Honey til warm then mix Brown Sugar. 
 Wrap each banana piece in half of  Bacon  strip. 
 Arrange on skewer; Broil for 5 minutes. 
 Roll in Brown Sugar and Honey glaze. 
 Enjoy
