Ingredients : 
 1 (6 oz) can  peaches  in light syrup or 2 fresh ripe  peaches , sliced and pitted 
 1 cup  heavy cream 
 1 tsp  sugar  or  honey  (optional) 
 popsicle sticks and cups 
 Directions : 
 Whip  cream  in a blender for 30-45 seconds. 
 Add  peaches  and  honey . 
 Whir until smooth. 
 Pour into molds, insert sticks, and freeze.
