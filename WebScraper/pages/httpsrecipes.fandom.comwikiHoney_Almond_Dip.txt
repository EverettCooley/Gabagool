 Description : 
 Made with yogurt, authentic almonds, and honey, this golden dip is great on desserts, pretzels, and fruits, and often delivers a stone-cold aroma that it relishes and preserves.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅔ cup non-fat plain  yogurt 
 3 tbsp. slivered, toasted  almonds , finely chopped 
 2 ½ tbsp.  honey 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together. Spoon contents into the fresh container. Keep cool overnight in the refrigerator. 
 Serve cold with other desserts. Reserve the nut-based dip in the refrigerator. 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Servings:  3 teaspoons
