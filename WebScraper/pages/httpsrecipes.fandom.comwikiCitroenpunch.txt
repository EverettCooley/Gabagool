Ingredients : 
 250gr.  Sugar 
 1/2 liter  water 
 lemon peel 
 juice of 2  lemons 
 1 dl of  rum ,  arak  or  cognac 
 Directions : 
 Bring the  water  with the  lemon peel  slowly to the boil. Add the  Sugar  and let the mass boil until it becomes syrupy.
 Put the syrup through a sieve and add the  rum ,  arak  or  cognac . You can thin with boiling  water  to taste, or add a slice of  lemon .
