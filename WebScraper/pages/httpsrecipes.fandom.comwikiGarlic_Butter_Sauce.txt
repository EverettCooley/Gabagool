 Description : 
This bold butter sauce is made with only 2 ingredients - garlic powder and butter - and delivers a rich, herbaceous flavor.       Butter Garlic Sauce     
 Ingredients : 
 1 pack  butter  buds 
 Â½ cup hot  water 
 4 tsp.  garlic powder 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve  butter  buds in hot  water . Just add the  garlic powder . 
 Stir well. Reserve the homemade, garlic-based butter sauce in the refrigerator. Use on  steak  or hamburgers or brush on  French bread . 
 Notes : 
 Instead of adding hot water to the butter buds, have an entire block of butter and a large saucepan ready to heat and add parsley to the butter-garlic mixture.
