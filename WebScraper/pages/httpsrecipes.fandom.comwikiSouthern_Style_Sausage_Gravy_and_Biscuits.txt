Ingredients : 
 1/2 pkg. mild sausage 
 2 tbsp.  butter 
 2 tbsp.  all purpose flour 
 5 cups  whole milk 
 1 1/2 tsp.  salt 
 1 1/2 tsp.  pepper 
 Buttermilk biscuits 
 Directions : 
 Cook sausage until done. 
 Add butter and flour. 
 Simmer for a few minutes on medium heat stirring frequently. 
 Add milk, salt and pepper. 
 Simmer over medium-highheat stirring constantly until desired thickness. 
 Spoon gravy over biscuits and enjoy!
