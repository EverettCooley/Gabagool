Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Hawkins Estate in Mesquite, Texas in 1994.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  butter 
 ½ pound  chicken livers  cut in half 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅛ teaspoon  white pepper 
 ¼ teaspoon  oregano 
 3  eggs 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅛ teaspoon  pepper 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 ½ cup plain  yogurt  at room temperature 
 1 small  tomato  peeled seeded and chopped 
 1 tablespoon snipped  chives 
 Directions : 
 In medium skillet melt 2 tablespoons butter. 
 Add chicken livers, salt, white pepper and oregano. 
 Saute chicken livers about 5 minutes then cover and keep warm. 
 In small bowl beat eggs, salt and pepper with a whisk. 
 In am omelet pan over medium heat melt 1 tablespoon butter then add egg mixture. 
 Slide spatula around edge of pan carefully lifting egg mixture so eggs will flow underneath. 
 Cook until set then carefully place on warm platter. 
 Stir yogurt until creamy. 
 Arrange cooked chicken livers and chopped tomato over top of omelet. 
 Spoon yogurt on top then sprinkle with chives.
