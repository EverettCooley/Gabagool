Description : 
 Serve with warm  croissants , or  Banana Nut Bread , or softened  cream cheese  and  honey  sandwiches or biscuits. the only cooking time is for the  chicken .
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups rice 
 2 cups  shrimp  cooked and chopped 
 2 cups  pineapple  chunks, drained 
 1½ cups  strawberries , halved 
 1 cup  green grapes 
 1 cup  celery , sliced 
 ½ cup  salad dressing  (may use light "or" fat free) 
 ¾ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  ginger 
 1 dash  pepper 
 lettuce 
 ¼ cup toasted  walnuts 
 Directions : 
 Combine (lightly) all ingredient except the  nuts  and  lettuce . 
 Chill. 
 Serve on  lettuce  leaves. 
 Top with  nuts . 
 Garnish with additional  strawberries  if you like.
