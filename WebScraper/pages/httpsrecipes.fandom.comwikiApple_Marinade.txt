Description : 
         
 This apple-based marinade is perfect for meat, poultry, or seafood. You can make the marinade bolder with additional pepper, or sweeter with honey.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon extra-virgin  olive oil 
 1  Granny Smith apple , cored and cubed 
 ¼ cup chopped  lemongrass  (from 3 stalks) 
 3  garlic  cloves, minced 
 1 piece (1 inch) fresh  ginger , sliced 
 1 fresh red finger  chili , thinly sliced 
 3 whole  star anises 
 ½ cup lukewarm  water 
 ¼ cup  tamari  or regular  soy sauce 
 3 tablespoons  apple juice 
 1 teaspoon  honey , optional 
 1-3 teaspoons  black pepper , optional 
 ​Directions : 
 Heat oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Add the apple, lemongrass, garlic, ginger, chili, and star anise. Cook, stirring, for 5 minutes. 
 Add the remaining ingredients and bring to a simmer. 
 Remove from heat and let cool for 5 minutes before use. 
 Spoon, funnel, or pour into a fresh plastic or glass jar and keep in the refrigerator. 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Servings:  3 cups or 3-5 tablespoons
