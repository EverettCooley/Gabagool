Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Makes 4 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups slivered fresh baby  spinach 
 1 cup grated, peeled and seeded  cucumbers 
 1 cup grated, peeled  carrots 
 1 cup grated red  radishes 
 1 tbsp extra virgin  olive oil 
 1 tbsp  rice vinegar 
 ½ tsp dried  oregano 
 ½ tsp dried  basil 
 ½ cup plain low-fat  yogurt  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 In large bowl, toss  spinach  with  cucumbers ,  carrots  and radishes. 
 Divide salad into 4 individual serving dishes. 
 In small bowl, combine remaining ingredients for dressing with fork. 
 If creamier dressing is desired, mix in  yogurt . 
 Pour into dressing container and serve with salad. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 52 Calories | 4g Fat | 5g Carbs | 2g Fiber | 40mg Sodium
