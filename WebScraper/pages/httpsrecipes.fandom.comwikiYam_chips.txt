 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1	tbl	virgin  olive oil 
 2	tbl	fresh  rosemary 
 2	tbl	fresh  sage  sliced 
 2	tsp	fresh  oregano 
 1	tsp	fresh  lime   thyme 
 1/2	tsp	kosher or  sea salt 
 1	tsp	cracked  pepper 
 1	x	 yam  peeled and cut in chunks 
 1	x	 sweet potato  peeled and cut in chunks 
 Directions : 
 Pour  oil  into a large baking pan and swirl to cover bottom. Arrange herbs over  oil , sprinkle with half the  salt  and  pepper . 
 Arrange  yam  and  sweet potato  chunks on top, sprinkle with remaining  salt  and  pepper  and bake at 350 degrees until soft (35â€“40 minutes). 
 Serves Four to Six
