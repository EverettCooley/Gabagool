Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  bourbon 
 2 tsp  triple sec 
 2 tsp  peach brandy 
 2 tsp  Maraschino liqueur 
 2 tbsp  heavy cream 
 several drops  maraschino cherry  juice 
 Directions : 
 Mix with cracked  ice  in shaker or blender, serve in chilled old-fashioned glass.
