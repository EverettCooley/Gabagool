Description : 
 Baked bean pot. A lovely light dish, goes well with  rice .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tsp  vegetable oil 
 250 g stir-fry vegetables 
 150 g (tinned)  baked beans 
 100 g  corn  (tinned) 
 pepper 
 150 g  pineapple 
 Directions : 
 Heat the  oil  and stir-fry the vegetables. 
 Add the beans and  corn . 
 Add  pepper  to taste. 
 Add the  pineapple  and warm through.
