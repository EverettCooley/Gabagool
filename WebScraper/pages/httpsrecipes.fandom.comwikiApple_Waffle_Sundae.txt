 Description : 
 This fruit-based, divine dessert is made with real ice cream and chocolate sauce in a waffle.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 Belgian  waffles 
 8 tbsp.  stewed Bramley Apples 
 ice cream , any flavor 
 chocolate sauce 
 Directions : 
 Pop the waffles in a toaster for 1 minute to heat through. 
 Top with hot or cold stewed Bramley apples, ice cream, and chocolate sauce. 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Cooking Time:  1 minute
 Preparation Time:  5 minutes
