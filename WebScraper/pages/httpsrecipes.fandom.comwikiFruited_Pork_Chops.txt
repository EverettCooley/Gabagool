 Ingredients : 
 6  pork  chops 
 1½ tsp  oregano  leaves 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 2 tbsp  flour 
 1½ tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  garlic powder 
 1 can  pineapple  chunks, drained with juice reserved 
 2 Tbsp  tomato paste 
 2 Tbsp minced  onion 
 ¼ cup  raisins 
 Directions : 
 Combine spices and rub in to chops. Brown in vegetable oil. 
 Add water to the pineapple juice to make 1 cup. 
 To this add tomato paste and minced onion 
 Pour juice mixture into pressure cooker. 
 Place rack in bottom of cooker. Arrange chops on rack and sprinkle with raisins. 
 Pressure cook for 12 minutes. Remove from heat. Let pressure drop of own accord for 5 minutes then release pressure. 
 Remove meat and add pineapple chunks to juice. Heat through. 
 Serve on rice. Spoon fruit mixture over the meat.
