Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Yield: 1 cup. 
 Ingredients : 
 Spinach salad : 
 ½ lbs  spinach , fresh, torn into bite-sized pieces 
 2 tbsp  almonds , slivered, toasted 
 ¾ cup  cabbage, purple , shredded 
 ¼ cup  raisins 
 Dressing : 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 ¾ tsp  mustard , dry 
 ¾ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  celery seeds 
 1½ tbsp  onion , minced 
 ¼ cup  vinegar 
 ½ cup  oil , vegetable 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients except dressing in a large bowl. 
 Toss and serve with dressing. 
 Dressing : 
 Combine all ingredients in a jar. 
 Cover tightly, and shake vigorously. 
 Chill several hours. 
 Shake dressing again before serving over salad.
