Ingredients : 
 1 x 12- to 14-inch pizza crust 
 8 oz shredded  soy mozzarella 
 Simple seasoned tomato sauce : 
 1 cup commercial  tomato sauce 
 1 tbsp  Italian seasoning 
 salt  and freshly ground  black pepper  to taste 
 Meat topping : 
 1 Tbsp  olive oil 
 4 oz soy “ ground Beef ” 
 2 soy “ sausage ” links, sliced into ½-inch rounds 
 ¼ cup chopped  onion 
 12 slices  soy “pepperoni” 
 6 slices soy  “Canadian bacon” 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 450°F. 
 Prebake fresh pizza crust, if using, for 3 to 4 minutes. 
 Remove from oven, and set aside to cool slightly. 
 Simple seasoned tomato sauce : 
 Combine  tomato sauce ,  Italian seasoning ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Set aside. 
 Meat topping : 
 Heat 1 teaspoon  oil  in large skillet over medium-high heat, and saut8e “ ground beef ” and “ sausage ” links for about 3 minutes, or until hot and slightly crispy. 
 Using a spatula, remove “meat” from pan, add remaining 2 teaspoons  oil  to skillet and sauté  onions  for 3 minutes, or until translucent. 
 Add  simple seasoned tomato sauce , and stir. 
 Remove from heat. 
 Assembly : 
 Spread sauce mixture over pizza crust. 
 Top with “ground beef,” “sausage,”  cheese , “pepperoni” and “Canadian bacon” slices. 
 Bake for 13 to 15 minutes. 
 Remove from oven, and serve.
