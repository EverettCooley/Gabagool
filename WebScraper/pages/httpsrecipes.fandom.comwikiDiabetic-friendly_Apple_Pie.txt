Ingredients : 
 2 pie crusts or use your own favorite 
 8 medium  apples  (tart are best) 
 4 tbsp  butter  or  margarine 
 4 tbsp ground  cinnamon 
 8 packs  sugar substitute  (like Equal) 
 ½ cup  raisins  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Put one pie crust in the bottom of a 9 inch pie plate. 
 Use a fork to prick the bottom crust in several places. 
 Using one of the tablespoons of  butter , dot the bottom crust with small pieces of  butter . 
 Next put on two of the  apples , sliced, peeled and cored. 
 On top of that sprinkle two packets of  sugar substitute  and a tablespoon of  cinnamon . 
 Next sprinkle ⅛ of a cup of  raisins , if you're using them. This completes one layer. 
 Start again with the  butter , then the  apples , etc. 
 Make 4 layers, using all the remaining ingredients. This will give you an over-flowing pie. 
 Place the top crust on pinch the edges and put a vent in the top. 
 Bake at 375°F for 40 – 50 minutes. 
 The pie is done when the crust has turned a nice golden brown.
