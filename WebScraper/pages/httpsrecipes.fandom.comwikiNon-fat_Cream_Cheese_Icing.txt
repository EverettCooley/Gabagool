Ingredients : 
 1 carton of  ricotta 
 1 carton of  cream cheese 
 1 box instant vanilla pudding 
 1 cup or to taste  confectioner's sugar 
 a small amount of of  milk 
 Directions : 
 Beat together until firm and tasty. 
 It tastes very good and gives a thicker consistency similar to buttercream.
