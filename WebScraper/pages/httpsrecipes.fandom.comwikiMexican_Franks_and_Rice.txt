Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup chopped  Onion 
 1 medium  green pepper , cut into 3/4-inch squares 
 1 12-ounce package frankfurters, cut into 1-inch pieces 
 1 15-ounce can  kidney beans 
 1 8-ounce can  tomato  sauce 
 1 tablespoon  chili powder 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 2 cups grated  Cheddar   Cheese 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Onion ,  green pepper  and franks in skillet until vegetables are tender crisp. Stir in beans,  tomato  sauce,  chili powder ,  salt  and  Cheese . Cook until hot and bubbly. Serve over beds of fluffy  rice . Garnish with  corn  chips, if desired.
