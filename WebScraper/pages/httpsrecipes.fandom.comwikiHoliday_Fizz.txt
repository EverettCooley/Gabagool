Description : 
 This won an award for Marilee Syme of Portland in an Automobile Club of Oregon Zero Proof Mixoff contest.
 
 Makes 34 x ½-cup servings 
 Ingredients : 
 3 quarts  seltzer water 
 2 cups  whipping cream 
 2⅓ cup Torani  raspberry syrup   [1] 
 ¾ cup Torani  orgeat  (almond syrup) 
 Directions : 
 Mix seltzer,  cream  and syrups. 
 Serve over  ice . 
 Notes : 
 
 ↑   The syrups can be found in specialty food stores and some  coffee  stores. 
 
 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving: 135 calories (1 percent from protein, 65 percent from carbohydrate, 34 percent from fat), less than 1 gram protein, 22 grams carbohydrate, 5 grams fat, 19 milligrams cholesterol, 74 milligrams sodium. 
 
 Exchanges: 1 fruit, 1 fat.
