Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Fisher Estate in Rockwall, Texas in 1980.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 8 ounces  grape juice 
 8 ounces  apple juice 
 8 ounces  orange juice 
 1 quart  ginger ale 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Mix first 4 ingredients together in a pitcher. 
 Add ice cubes and 9 ounces of the beverage to each glass. 
 Serve immediately.
