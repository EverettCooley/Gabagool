 Ingredients : 
 Half shot Warninks  advocaat 
 Half shot De Kuyper  crème de menthe  (green) 
 1 shot Stolichnaya Red  vodka 
 Top up Lemonade 
 Glass: Highball
 
 Directions : 
 Shake all the ingredients well together (except the lemonade), and pour into a highball glass. Top up with lemonade.
