Description : 
 Cold  tomato  soup is wonderful in the summer. It is full of  California avocados  and seafood. This soup is absolutely delicious!!!
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cans  tomato soup 
 ½ pound tiny  shrimp  [cooked] 
 ½ pound  imitation crab  [flaked] 
 2  California avocados  - peeled and diced 
 1 tsp  lemon juice 
 dash  hot pepper sauce 
 4 finely sliced  green onions 
 Directions : 
 Place soups [diluted] in a bowl - mix well. 
 Gently add additional ingredients and stir to combine. 
 Place in refrigerator for several hours [preferably overnight] to marry ingredients. 
 Serve.
