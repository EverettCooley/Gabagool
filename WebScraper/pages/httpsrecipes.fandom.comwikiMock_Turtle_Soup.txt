Ingredients : 
 2½ quarts of  water 
 2 lbs ground chuck or very lean  ground beef 
 ¼ cup  worcestershire sauce 
 2 medium  onions , diced fine 
 25 ginger snaps (crushed) 
 ½  lemon , seeded and chopped fine 
 2 Tbsp  cider vinegar 
 1 bottle  catsup  (14 oz) 
 2 tsp  salt 
 pepper  to taste 
 3 or 4 hard-boiled  eggs  (chopped) 
 Directions : 
 Brown hamburger in pot. 
 Drain grease from pot. 
 Put all remaining ingredients in a pot except hard-boiled  eggs . 
 Cook at high pressure for 15 minutes. 
 Use natural pressure release. 
 Add chopped  eggs  shortly after soup is done. 
 Serve and enjoy.
