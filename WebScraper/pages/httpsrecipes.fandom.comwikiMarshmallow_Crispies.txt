Ingredients : 
 1 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 40 large  marshmallows  (4 cups mini) 
 4 cup  crisp rice cereal 
 Directions : 
 Grease large saucepan with cooking  oil . 
 Add marshmallows and melt over low heat; watch carefully. 
 Quickly stir in cereal; mix well. 
 With buttered spoon (or spoon dipped in  water ), press into greased 8 inch square pan. 
 Cut into 25 squares. 
 Nutritional information : 
 1 square = 60 calories, 1 fruit exchange 15 grams carbohydrate, trace protein, trace fat, 39 mg sodium
