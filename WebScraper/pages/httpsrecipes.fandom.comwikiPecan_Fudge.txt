 Description : 
 This was in my grandmother's collection. Date and source unknown. It is very easy to make and always gobbled right up. I make triple batches of this at Christmas. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 12 ounces  semi-sweet chocolate  chips 
 12 ounces butterscotch chips 
 2 ounces  unsweetened chocolate 
 2 cans  sweetened condensed milk 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 1 cup finely chopped  pecans 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients and cook over low heat for 5 minutes or until everything is smooth. 
 Pour into rectangular baking pan and chill at least 2 hours.
