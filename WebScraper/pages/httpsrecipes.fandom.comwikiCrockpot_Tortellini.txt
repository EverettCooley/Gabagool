Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 10/14/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  Italian sausage 
 1 chopped  onion 
 26 oz  spaghetti sauce 
 2 cans (4 oz)  mushrooms  drained 
 1 x 14 oz can diced  tomatoes  undrained 
 1 x 15 oz can  tomato sauce 
 1 x 9 oz pkg. refrigerated cheese  tortellini 
 1 cup shredded  mozzarella 
 Directions : 
 Brown ground beef together with onions stirring frequently until beef is browned. 
 Drain thoroughly. 
 Mix all ingredients except tortellini and cheese in crockpot. 
 Cover and cook on low 8 hours. 
 Add tortellini. 
 Stir to combine then sprinkle with cheese. 
 Cover crockpot and cook on low 15 â€“ 20 minutes longer or until cheese is melted.
