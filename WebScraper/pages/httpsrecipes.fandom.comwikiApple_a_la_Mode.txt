 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  dark rum 
 2 oz  apple juice 
 1 tbsp  vanilla   ice   cream 
 Directions : 
 Blend briefly with half a glassful of crushed  ice . 
 Serve in a double cocktail glass with a sprig of  mint .
