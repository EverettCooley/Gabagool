 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 oz  margarine 
 4 oz soft  brown sugar 
 3 oz  golden syrup 
 8 oz  porridge oats 
 2 oz  Sultana 's 
 2 oz chopped  nuts 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to Gas mark 4. 
 Use either a non-stick square or oblong tin about one and a half inches deep. 
 Put  Sugar , Syrup, and  margarine  in a pan and melt gently. 
 Pour into a bowl over  porridge oats , nut's and  Sultanas  and mix well. 
 Put in the tin and flatten top slightly. 
 Bake in oven Gas 4 for 30 minutes. 
 Take out of oven and cut the desired shape either square or oblong and leave in tin to cool. 
 Store in an airtight tin. 
 You can replace the nut's and  Sultanas  with anything else, I use  dried banana  chips,  coconut ,  currants ,  Raisins   walnuts  or any other  nuts  but chopped.
