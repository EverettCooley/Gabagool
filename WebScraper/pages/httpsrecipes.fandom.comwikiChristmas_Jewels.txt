Ingredients : 
 ⅓ cup  shortening 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 ½ cup  molasses 
 1  egg 
 1½ cups  flour 
 ½ tsp  soda 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 1 tbsp  cinnamon 
 1 tbsp  mace 
 1 tbsp  nutmeg 
 ¼ tsp  ginger 
 ¼ tsp  allspice 
 2½ cups mixed  candied fruit 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 320°F. 
 Mix  shortening ,  sugar ,  molasses  and  egg . 
 Stir dry ingredients; blend into  shortening  mixture. 
 Stir in fruit. 
 Drop by teaspoonfuls about 1" apart on lightly greased baking sheet. 
 Bake 12 – 15 minutes.
