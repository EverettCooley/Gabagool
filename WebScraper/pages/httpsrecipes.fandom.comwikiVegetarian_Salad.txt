 Description : 
 Vegetarian Salad
 
 Ingredients : 
 romaine lettuce 
 1 	 avocado 
 3 	 green onions 
 9 	 cherry tomatoes 
 1 	salad 
 Directions : 
 Wash and dry  lettuce  and tear into bite size pieces. 
 Slice the  avocado , green onions  and  tomatoes . 
 Place all ingredients in a large salad bowl and add salad. 
 Toss thoroughly. 
 Serve immediately.
