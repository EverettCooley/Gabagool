 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups fresh or frozen  cranberries , well rinsed and stemmed 
 1 cup  apple cider vinegar 
 1 tsp. ground  allspice 
 1/2 tsp. grated  nutmeg 
 2 tsp. ground  cinnamon 
 1 cup  honey 
 1 tsp.  salt 
 Directions : 
 1. Put  cranberries  in 3-quart saucepan; add  vinegar ,  allspice ,  nutmeg ,  cinnamon  and 1 cup  water . Bring to a boil over medium heat, and cook, uncovered, 10 minutes, or until all  Berries  are soft.
 
 2. Put  cranberries  in blender, and pur√©e until smooth. Mix in  honey  and  salt . Return to pan. Cook over medium-low heat for 15 minutes, or until thick.
 
 3. Remove from heat, cool and store in plastic container. May be kept in refrigerator up to 2 months.
