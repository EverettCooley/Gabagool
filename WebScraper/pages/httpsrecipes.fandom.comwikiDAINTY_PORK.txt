Ingredients : 
 400 g  pork 
 1 tbsp  flour 
 2  eggs 
 40 g  cheese 
 fat 
 garlic 
 pepper 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Cut the  pork  into pieces, pound, add the  salt  and  pepper , sprinkle with minced  garlic , dip in slightly whipped  egg , dredge with  flour  and roast until done. 
 Lay slices of  cheese  on the  pork  pieces and roast in oven.
