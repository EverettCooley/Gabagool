Ingredients : 
 2 – 3  crawfish  tails 
 3 hard cooked  eggs , chopped 
 ½ cup  celery , finely chopped 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 cup  onions , finely chopped 
 1 juice of 1 or 2  limes 
 3 tbsp  mayonnaise 
 hot pepper 
 Directions : 
 Cut up  crawfish  into small pieces and marinate in  lime juice  for ½ hour. 
 Add remaining ingredients and toss lightly. 
 Chill in refrigerator and serve garnished with crisp  lettuce  and  tomato  slices.
