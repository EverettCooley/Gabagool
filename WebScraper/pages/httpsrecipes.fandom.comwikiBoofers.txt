Ingredients : 
 3 cups  mashed potatoes 
 1 tbsp  oil 
 1 medium  onion , chopped 
 ½ cup  tofu , crumbled 
 ¼ cup  parsley , minced 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  black pepper 
 2 tbsp  oil 
 Directions : 
 Sauté the 1 tbsp  oil  and 1 medium chopped  onion . 
 Mix the  tofu ,  parsley ,  salt  and  pepper  into the  potatoes . 
 Shape into 12 patties ½" thick and brown about 4 minutes each side in 2 tbsp  oil .
