Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz 151-proof  rum 
 1 oz  cherry brandy 
 1 oz fresh  lime juice 
 1 tsp  sugar syrup  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients with cracked  ice  in a cocktail shaker. 
 Shake well and strain into chilled old-fashioned glass.
