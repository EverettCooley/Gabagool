Description 
 Cherries, Canned  by the US Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Serves : 8 
 Ingredients 
 2 cans red tart  cherries  with juice 
 1½ cups  sugar 
 ¼ cup  cornstarch 
 2½ cups  oats 
 2 cups  flour 
 1 teaspoon  baking soda 
 1 cup packed  brown sugar 
 Directions 
 Combine sugar and cornstarch in saucepan. 
 Add undrained cherries. 
 Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring constantly, until thickened. Set aside. 
 Mix oats, flour, baking soda, and brown sugar until crumbly. 
 Place half of crumb mixture into 9” x 9” baking pan. 
 Pour cooked cherry mixture over crumbs. 
 Top with other half of crumbs. 
 Bake at 350°F for 30–35 minutes until lightly browned.
