 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 oz of a diabetic mix of  Dream Whip 
 1/2 cup  ice   water 
 1 tbsp  cocoa 
 strawberries  for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Beat all ingredients until soft peaks form. Spoon into dishes. Per serving: 67 calories, 0.7 gms protein, 4 gm fat, 0 cholesterol, 6 gms carbohydrates, 13 mg sodium
