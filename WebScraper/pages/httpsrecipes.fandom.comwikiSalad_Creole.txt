Ingredients : 
 ⅔  pineapple 
 ⅓ a  tomato  per person 
 ½ cup fresh  cream 
 1 tbsp  ketchup 
 1 pinch  salt 
 squeeze of  lime juice 
 chopped  onion 
 Directions : 
 Cut  pineapple  into thin strips and dice  tomato . 
 Combine next 4 ingredients and pour over salad. 
 Serve on  lettuce  leaves and top with a sprinkle of chopped  onions .
