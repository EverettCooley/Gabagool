Info : 
 Cook Time : 40 
 Serves : 6
 
 Ingredients : 
 chicken wings 
 season all 
 black peper 
 cayan peper 
 cooking oil 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 1.Skin chicken.
 
2.Season with season all, salt, table spoon of cayan peper.
 
3.Preheat oil on 350 degree heat for ten minutes.
 
4.Cook chicken for 30 minutes.
