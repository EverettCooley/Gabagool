 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 a whole  Snapper  about 300g 
 a  lemon , 
 1 clove of  garlic , 
 2 shallots, 
 thyme  and  salt . 
 50g sliced  Onion , 
 5g sliced  garlic , 
 50g diced  tomato , 
 10g chopped  parsley  and  chives , 
 10cl  oil , 1 spoonful hot  water , 
 salt ,  pepper , and  chili pepper . 
 150g  christophine , 
 (local potato-like vegetable) au gratin, 
 80g fried  banana , 
 80g mashed giromon ( West Indian pumpkin ), 
 60g of  rice  with  curry . 
 Directions : 
 Take a whole  Snapper  about 300g scaled, gutted and trimmed, left to marinate for one hour with a  lemon , 1 clove of  garlic , 2 shallots,  thyme  and  salt . 
 Grill the  Snapper  and serve accompanied with : “Dog sauce”: 50g sliced  Onion , 5g sliced  garlic , 50g diced  tomato , 10g chopped  parsley  and  chives , 10cl  oil , 1 spoonful hot  water ,  salt ,  pepper , and  chili pepper . 
 And add a choice of vegetables : 150g  christophine , (local potato-like vegetable) au gratin, 80g fried  banana , 80g mashed giromon ( West Indian pumpkin ), 60g of  rice  with  curry .
