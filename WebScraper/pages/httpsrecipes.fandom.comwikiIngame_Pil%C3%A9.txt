 Ingredients : 
 malanga 
 Directions : 
 Peel it, cut it up into medium sized chunks and boil it for 10–15 minutes until soft. 
 Get a small metal bowl and a hand-sized wooden pounder and pound away adding small amounts of  water  until it’s moist enough. 
 To get all the lumps out pound it long enough.
