 Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 shots Blue  curaçao  liqueur 
 1 shot Stock 84®  brandy 
 1 shot  white rum 
 1 shot  Metaxa  brandy 
 2 shots  ouzo   anise liqueur 
 juice of 1  lemons 
 juice of 1  limes 
 8  ice cubes 
 Directions : 
 Mix the Blue  curaçao ,  whisky ,  rum ,  brandy ,  ouzo ,  lemon  and  lime  juices in a blender. 
 Pulse mixture for 2–3 seconds. Add the 8  ice cubes . 
 Pulse for about 20–30 seconds or until desired consistency of  ice  is achieved.
