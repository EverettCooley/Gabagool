Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 boneless chuck roast 
 1 package dry burrito mix 
 3 tablespoons salsa (mild, medium or hot according to taste) 
 ¼ cup  water 
 1 can  refried beans 
 grated  cheese  (Parmesan, Cheddar, Asiago, Colby-Jack, or Swiss) 
 hot sauce, optional 
 Directions : 
 Place chuck roast in a crockpot and add burrito mix and  water . Turn the roast a few times and swish it around to blend in the mix. 
 Add the salsa; cover and cook on low heat for 12 to 14 hours or until the meat is falling apart. 
 Wrap  flour  tortillas in foil and warm in a 325° F oven for 10 to 15 minutes. While tortillas are warming, heat the beans. 
 Place some of the meat, beans, and grated  cheese , on the middle of the tortillas, leaving about ½ inch at the top and the bottom and have the mixture go out to the sides about a couple of inches. Fold as for a burrito. Serve warm and drizzle with the hot sauce.
