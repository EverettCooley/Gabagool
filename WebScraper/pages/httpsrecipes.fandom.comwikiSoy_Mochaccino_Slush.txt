Ingredients : 
 1 cup  ice cubes 
 ¾ cup chocolate  soy milk 
 1 packet cappuccino Nestlé® Carnation® instant breakfast 
 Directions : 
 Place  ice ,  soy milk  and  carnation  Instant Breakfast in blender 
 Cover. Blend until smooth.
