 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  chicken ; cooked, diced 
 ½ lb  velveeta ; cubed 
 1  pinto beans ; drained 
 2½ oz  ripe olives ; sliced 
 ½ cup  salsa 
 2 tbsp  garlic powder 
 ½ cup  milk 
 1  egg 
 1 cup self-rising  cornmeal  mix 
 3 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 2 tbsp  chili powder 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Use for garnish: shredded  lettuce , chopped  onion , chopped  tomato ,  avocado  slices, sour  cream  and salsa. 
 Heat oven to 400 °F. 
 Grease a 2-quart baking dish. 
 In a large bowl combine  chicken ,  cheese ,  beans , salsa,  chili powder ; blend well. 
 Spoon into greased baking dish; set aside. 
 In a med bowl beat  egg . 
 Stir in  milk ,  cornmeal  and  oil ; blend well. 
 Pour evenly over  chicken  mixture. 
 Bake at 400 °F for 30–35 minutes or until golden brown. 
 Serve with toppings as desired.
