Description : 
 Serves: 10 - 12
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  ground pork 
 1 pound  ground turkey 
 1 pound ground  kangaroo  meat ( ground beef  may be substituted) 
 1 pound premium grade, low fat  ground beef 
 1  onion , finely chopped 
 1 cup  parsley , finely chopped 
 1 cup dried  breadcrumbs 
 2  eggs , beaten 
 1 teaspoon  Italian seasoning 
 Directions : 
 Place all ingredients into a very large bowl. 
 Using the hands, mix well, until well combined. 
 With wet hands, form mixture into desired size patties, and place onto a tray ready for refrigerator or freezer. 
 Patties should be flattened at time of making, as they split on the sides if you try to flatten them at the time of cooking. 
 Layer patties using freezer wrap between the layers, and either refrigerate for later use or place into freezer until the special day.
