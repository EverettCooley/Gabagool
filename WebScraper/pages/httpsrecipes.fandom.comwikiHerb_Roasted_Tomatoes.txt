 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 teaspoon  olive oil , divided 
 2 large red  tomatoes , halved 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1 tablespoon fresh  thyme  leaves 
 salt  and  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. Lightly coat a 9 x 13 inch baking pan with half of the  oil . 
 Arrange  tomatoes , cut side up, in pan. Sprinkle  tomatoes  with  garlic ,  thyme ,  salt  and  pepper ; dot with remaining  oil . 
 Bake 45 minutes to 1 hour, until very soft. Serve hot or at room temperature.
