      Aji De Gallina     
 Ingredients : 
 4 lb  chicken 
 ½ cup  oil 
 ½ lb  nuts ,  chopped 
 2 tsp  garlic , ground 
 6  chiles , liquidated 
 4 slice of  bread 
 1 can  evaporated milk 
 4 oz  Parmesan cheese , grated 
 1 large  onion , finely chopped 
 salt , to taste 
 pepper , to taste 
 6  yellow potatoes 
 1 can  black olives 
 3  eggs , hard boiled 
 Directions : 
 Boil the  chicken  in salted  water . 
 Remove from bone and break into bite size pieces. 
 In a saucepan heat the  oil  and fry the  onion ,  garlic  and  chile peppers ,  salt  and  pepper  to taste. 
 Fry until golden and add the bread. 
 Cook slowly for 10 minutes. 
 Add chopped  nuts , grated  cheese  and chopped  chicken . 
 Two or three minutes before serving add the  evaporated milk .
