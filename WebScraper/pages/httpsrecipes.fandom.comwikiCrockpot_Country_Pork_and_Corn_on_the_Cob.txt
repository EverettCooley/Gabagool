 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source:  Southern Living  2007 Main Dish Marvels 
 Make Ahead | Prep: 14 minutes | Cook: 4 hr 
 Makes 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  all-purpose flour 
 1½ tsp  salt , divided 
 1 tsp  pepper , divided 
 6 (5 oz) bone-in center cut  pork chops  (about ½-inch thick) 
 2 – 3 tbsp  olive oil , divided 
 4 large  garlic  cloves, pressed 
 3 ears fresh  corn , cut in half 
 1 (18-oz) bottle honey  BBQ sauce  (like KC Masterpiece Honey BBQ sauce) 
 1 cup  chicken broth 
 Directions : 
 Combine flour, 1 teaspoon salt, and ½ teaspoon pepper in a large zip-top bag; add pork, tossing to coat. 
 Heat 1 tbsp oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. 
 Cook pork in 2 batches, 2 minutes on each side until browned, adding more oil if necessary. 
 Place in a lightly greased 4½-qt slow cooker. 
 Rub remaining 1 tbsp oil and garlic evenly over corn. 
 Sprinkle with remaining ½ tsp each salt and pepper; add to slow cooker. 
 Combine BBQ sauce and broth; add to slow cooker. 
 Cover and cook on  low  4 hours or until meat is tender.
