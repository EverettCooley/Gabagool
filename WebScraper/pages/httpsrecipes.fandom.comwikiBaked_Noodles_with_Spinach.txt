 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 	oz 	Noodles 
 1 1/2 	tsp 	 salt 
 1 1/2 	quart 	 water , boiling 
 8 	oz 	 yogurt 
 1/2 	cup 	 Cheese , cottage 
 10 	oz 	 spinach , frozen, thawed 
 2 	tbsp 	 onions , chopped 
 1/2 	cup 	 Cheese ,  Cheddar , shredded 
 Directions : 
 1 	Gradually add noodles and  salt  to rapidly boiling  water  so that  water  continues to boil; cook, uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender.
 2 	Drain noodles in colander.
 3 	Preheat oven to 400.
 4 	Combine  yogurt  and  cottage cheese ; combine noodles,  spinach  and  Onion  with  cottage cheese  mixture.
 5 	Pour into a 1-quart baking dish; top with  Cheddar   Cheese .
 6 	Cover and bake 20-25 minutes.
 7 	Uncover and bake until  Cheese  is melted and brown
