Ingredients : 
 4 lbs jumbo  shrimp 
 2 tablespoons  lemon juice 
 ½ cup  tomato sauce 
 ½ cup  sherry 
 1 can crushed  tomatoes 
 2  bay leaves 
 2 tablespoons  soy sauce 
 2  green bell peppers , chopped 
 4 cloves of  garlic , chopped 
 2  onions , chopped 
 ½ cup  olive oil 
 ½ teaspoon red pepper 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  cumin 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Season the  shrimp  with  salt ,  pepper , soy sauce and  lemon . 
 Sauté quickly in  olive oil . 
 Remove the  shrimp  and add to the  oil  the  onion ,  garlic ,  pepper ,  bay leaves , crushed  tomatoes ,  tomato  sauce, soy sauce,  cumin , red pepper and  sherry . 
 Cook over medium heat until the vegetables are soft. 
 Add the  shrimp , reduce the heat to low and cook for 15 more minutes. 
 Serve with  white rice .
