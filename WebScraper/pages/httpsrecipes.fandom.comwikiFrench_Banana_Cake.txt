 Description : 
 Purchased from McNew estate in Pasadena, Texas in 1987. Date on card indicates it is a recipe taken from a church potluck. Dated 1944.
2/3-cup  water 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  white vinegar 
 1-teaspoon  baking soda 
 1 box white cake mix with pudding 
 2  eggs 
 1-1/4 cups very ripe mashed  bananas 
 2/3 cup chopped  pecans 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix  water ,  vinegar  and soda. 
 Combine with cake mix and  eggs . 
 Stir until moistened. 
 Scrape bowl with spatula. 
 Add mashed  bananas . 
 Beat with electric mixer 3 minutes at medium speed. 
 Blend in  nuts . 
 Pour into greased and floured 9x13 inch pan. 
 Bake at 350 for 35 minutes
