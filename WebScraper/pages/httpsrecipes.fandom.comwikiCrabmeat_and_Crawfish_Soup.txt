 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  crawfish 
 1 container  crabmeat  (lump or claw) 
 2 cans of cream-style  corn 
 1  onion  chopped fine 
 1 tablespoon  garlic  (chopped or minced) 
 1 pint of  half and half  cream 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Sauté  onions  and  garlic  in 2 tablespoons of  butter . 
 Add  crawfish , simmer for about 15 minutes. 
 Add  corn  and half and half  cream . 
 Cook for about 15–30 minutes on low-medium heat. 
 Add crabmeat simmer for about 20 minutes. 
 Enjoy with crackers.
