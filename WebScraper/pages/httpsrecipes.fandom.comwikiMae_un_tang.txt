Korean "bouillabaisse", an outstandingly delicious hot and spicy soup that contains great chunks of a variety of seasonal fresh fish and other  seafood , stewed with  chile pepper  and  gochujang .
 
 Mae un means spicy; tang is a meat-based soup boiled for a relatively long time. The bouillabaisse-like soup consists of large chunks of any variety of fresh fish, which is stewed with chiles and red pepper paste.
