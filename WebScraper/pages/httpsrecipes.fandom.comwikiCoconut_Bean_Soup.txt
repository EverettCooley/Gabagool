 Description : 
 coconut  Bean Soup
 
 Ingredients : 
 125 ml chopped  Onion 
 3 tsp.  margarine  or  butter , melted 
 125 ml chopped  green pepper 
 1 tsp.  curry  powder 
 1 tsp.  salt 
 1/2 tsp.  pepper 
 250 ml chopped  tomato 
 625 ml canned  kidney beans 
 500 ml  coconut milk 
 750 ml  water 
 125 ml cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Saut√© the  onions  in the  butter 
 Add the  green peppers  and season with  curry  powder,  salt  and  pepper 
 Add the  tomatoes  and simmer for 2 minutes 
 Add  kidney beans ,  coconut milk  and  water  to the mixture and simmer for 10 minutes 
 Add the  rice  and simmer for another 5 minutes
