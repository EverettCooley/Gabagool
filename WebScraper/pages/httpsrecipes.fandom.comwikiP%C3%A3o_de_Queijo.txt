 Description : 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs of polvilho doce 
 4 cups  milk 
 1 cup  oil 
 1 tsp  salt 
 5  eggs 
 1 lb grated  Cheese  ( Cheddar , white or yellow) 
 Directions : 
 Heat  milk  and  oil  and pour on the polvilho and let cool. 
 Add other ingredients and knead the dough. 
 Make small balls (about 1 teaspoonful) and drop them onto ungreased cookie sheet. 
 Bake at 350 °F until golden (about 30 minutes).
