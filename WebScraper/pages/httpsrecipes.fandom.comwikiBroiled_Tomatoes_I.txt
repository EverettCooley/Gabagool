Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 cup stuffing mix, herb-seasoned 
 2 tsp  butter  (or marg.), melted 
 1/2 cup  Cheese , Parmesan, grated 
 mustard , dijon 
 8  tomatoes , halved 
 Directions : 
 Combine stuffing mix,  butter , and  Cheese ; mix well. 
 Spread  mustard  over surface of  tomatoes . 
 Spoon stuffing mixture over  mustard . 
 Broil 4 inches from heat about 7 minutes or until topping is lightly browned.
