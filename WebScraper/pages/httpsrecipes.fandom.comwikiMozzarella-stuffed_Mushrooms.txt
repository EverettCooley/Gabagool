Ingredients : 
 ¼ lb pkg.  mushrooms 
 1 cup  butter  (divided) 
 1 cup  onion , chopped 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1 cup  pepperoni  slices, chopped 
 1 cup shredded  mozzarella  cheese 
 ½ tbsp  Italian seasoning 
 1 cup seasoned  bread crumbs 
 Directions : 
 Clean  mushrooms ; remove and chop stems. 
 Dip  mushroom  caps into 2 tbsp melted  butter . 
 Arrange on baking pan and set aside. 
 Sauté chopped stems,  onion  and  garlic  in remaining 2 tbsp  butter  until tender. 
 Remove from heat. 
 Stir in  pepperoni ,  cheese , seasoning, and  bread  crumbs and mix well. 
 Spoon into  mushroom  caps. 
 Bake at 350°F for 15 minutes or until hot. 
 Serve with  garlic   bread  sticks and a marinara sauce for dipping.
