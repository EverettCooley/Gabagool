Description : 
 Serves 4.
 
 Ingredients : 
 200 g  self-raising flour 
 100 g  caster sugar 
 75 g  butter 
 juice and grated rind of 1  lemon 
 juice and grated rind of 1  orange 
 1  egg , beaten 
 sesame seeds 
 Directions : 
 Sieve the  flour , add the  sugar  and rub in the  butter  until the mixture resembles fine bread crumbs. 
 Add the  lemon  and  orange  rinds bind together with the juice and the beaten  egg  adding a tiny amount of  water . 
 If necessary to make the dough workable. 
 Break the dough into small like balls and roll these into thin  sausage  shapes or shape them into rings. 
 Twist in the middle will convert the circles figures of eight which is the traditional figure of eight. 
 On a plate have a quantity of  sesame seeds  press each shape into them, so that the top is covered. 
 Place well spaced on a greased baking tray and bake at 200°C / 400°F /gas mark 6 reducing heat to 180°C / 350°F / gas 4 for 20 – 25 minutes until golden.
