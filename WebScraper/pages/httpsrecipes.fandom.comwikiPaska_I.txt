 Easter Bread. 6 servings
 
       Easter Bread (Paska)     
 Ingredients : 
 1 cake  or  pkg.  yeast 
 2 cups lukewarm  milk 
 9 cups  flour 
 1 cup  butter , soft 
 1 cup  sugar 
 4  eggs 
 1 tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Crumble  yeast  into bowl; add 1 cup  milk  and 1 cup  flour . 
 Mix and set aside to rise in warm place. 
 Cream  butter ; add  sugar  and  eggs  and mix well. 
 Add remaining  milk . 
 Add to yeast mixture. 
 Knead until dough is smooth and elastic. 
 Set aside in warm place until double in bulk. 
 Form into desired shapes. 
 Allow dough to rise to double in bulk in pans;. 
 Bake at 350Â°F for 30 to 35 minutes. 
 Halfway through baking, brush top with beaten  egg  yolk to which a little  milk  has been added. 
 Tip : 
 If you melt 1  stick   butter  and knead into dough it will keep paska soft.
