Description : 
       Alaska Dungeness Crab Fruit Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 1  dungeness crab  - thawed if necessary 
 2 cups fresh  pineapple  chunks see * note 
 2  apple  or  pear  cut into chunks 
 1  orange  peeled, sliced, and halved 
 lettuce 
 lime  or  lemon  wedges 
 Honey dressing : 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 1 tbdp  lime juice 
 1½ tsp  honey 
 ⅛ tsp ground  ginger 
 Directions : 
 Left off back shell of  crab . 
 Remove and discard  viscera  and gills. 
 Rinse crab thoroughly under cool, running  water . 
 Break off legs; crack along edges. 
 Cut into serving-sized pieces. 
 Break body section into several pieces. 
 Micro-cook or steam until thoroughly heated; cool. 
 Remove meat from body. 
 Combine crab meat from body with fruit. 
 Honey dressing: combine all ingredients. 
 Arrange fruit mixture and cracked crab legs on lettuce-lined platter.
