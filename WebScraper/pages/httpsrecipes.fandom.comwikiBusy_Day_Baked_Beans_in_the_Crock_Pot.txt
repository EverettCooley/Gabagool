Description : 
 This is a recipe I obtained from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Hager estate in Canton, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  catsrecipes y-group 
 Ingredients : 
 5 cans  navy beans  drained 
 1 small  white onion  peeled and chopped 
 1½ cup  maple syrup 
 2 tablespoons yellow  mustard 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 4 slices thick cut  bacon 
 Directions : 
 In a 5-quart crock pot mix together all ingredients except  bacon . 
 Lay  bacon  strips over the bean mixture then cover and cook on low for 14 hours.
