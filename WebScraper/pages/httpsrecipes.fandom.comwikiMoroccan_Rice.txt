This is a great meal for vegetarians. It can be served with bread or salad and thus is a healthy choice!
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup nonfat plain  yogurt 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 2 teaspoon  Sugar 
 1 teaspoon  curry  powder 
 4 cups cooked  basmati rice 
 1/2 cup peeled and chopped  Apple 
 1/3 cup chopped  green onions 
 1/4 cup  Raisins 
 2 tablespoon snipped fresh  chives 
 salt  and  pepper 
 3 cups torn  lettuce 
 Directions : 
 Cook the  rice  well, until it is light and fluffy, and then leave to cool to room temperature. 
 Mix the  yogurt ,  oil ,  Sugar , and  curry  powder together in a medium bowl, beating well. You can use a whisk if necessary. 
 When the mixture is even, add the  rice ,  Apple ,  green onions ,  Raisins  and  chives  and stir well, seasoning to taste with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Let the  rice  marinade for about 30 minutes before serving 
 This recipe serves 4.
