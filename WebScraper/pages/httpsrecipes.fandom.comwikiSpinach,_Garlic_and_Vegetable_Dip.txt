Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Rachael Ray 
 5 cups or 40 (2 tbsp and 5 cracker) servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 (10 oz) pkg chopped frozen  spinach 
 1 (8 oz) pkg  cream cheese , softened 
 2 (5.2 oz) garlic and fine herbs  gournay  cheese 
 ½ cup  heavy cream 
 2 tsp  lemon juice , eyeball it 
 1 tsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 1 tsp  hot sauce , eyeball it 
 ½  red bell pepper , chopped 
 1 (5 oz) can sliced  water chestnuts , drained, chopped 
 4  scallions , chopped 
 1 small  carrot , peeled, finely diced 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 ⅛ tsp  pepper 
 Sociables™ savory  crackers 
 celery  sticks, for dipping 
 Directions : 
 Defrost spinach in microwave. 
 Place defrosted spinach in clean kitchen towel and wring it completely dry. 
 Separate and add to mixing bowl. 
 In food processor, combine cream cheese, gournay cheese and cream; process until very smooth. 
 Add lemon juice, Worcestershire and hot sauce; pulse process to combine. 
 Add smooth cheese blend to spinach and mix in bell pepper, water chestnuts, scallions and carrots with rubber spatula until vegetables are evenly distributed. 
 Season dip with salt and pepper to taste. 
 Transfer to serving dish and surround with crackers and celery sticks.
