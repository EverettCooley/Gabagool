Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Come Into The Kitchen 
 Makes 4 to 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 3½ cups dry  white wine , chilled 
 ½ cup  cointreau 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 ice  cubes 
 1 bottle  club soda , chilled 
 1  orange , sliced 
 1  lemon , sliced 
 2  limes , cut into wedges 
 Directions : 
 Combine wine,  cointreau  and  sugar  until well blended in a glass pitcher. 
 When ready to serve, stir in  ice  and  club soda . 
 Add fruit and serve.
