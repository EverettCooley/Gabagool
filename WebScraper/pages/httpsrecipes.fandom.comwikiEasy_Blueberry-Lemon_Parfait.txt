 Description : 
 Usa Today Recipe Section
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 Cups Fresh or Thawed Frozen  blueberries 
 2 8-Ounce Cartons Non-Fat lemon  yogurt 
 10 Gingersnaps, Crumbled 
 Directions : 
 In Each of Four Parfait Glasses or Tall Wine Glasses, Put 1/2 Cup  blueberries 
 Add 1/2 Cup  yogurt 
 Top with Crumbled Gingersnaps. 
 Serves 4.
 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Per Serving: 147 Calories, 3.7g Protein, 15g Carbohydrates, 2g Fiber, 2.9g Fat (0g Saturated), 160mg Sodium.
