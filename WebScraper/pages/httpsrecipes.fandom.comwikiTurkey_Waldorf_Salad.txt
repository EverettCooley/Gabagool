Description : 
 Makes 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups cooked  rice  (cooked in  chicken broth ), cooled 
 2 cups cooked  turkey breast  strips 
 1½ cups diced unpeeled  apple 
 1 cup sliced  celery 
 ¼ cup chopped  walnuts , toasted 
 ⅓ cup bottled  poppy seed   salad dressing 
 lettuce  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  turkey ,  apple ,  celery ,  walnuts  and  salad dressing  in medium bowl; toss. 
 Serve on  lettuce  leaves.
