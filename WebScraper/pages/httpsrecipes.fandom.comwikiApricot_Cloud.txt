Description : 
       Apricot Cloud     
 This creamy, indulgent dessert is known in England as a “fool.” For best texture, chill before serving. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups  heavy cream 
 2 tablespoons granular  sugar substitute 
 1 cup no-sugar-added  apricot  baby food 
 1 cup no-sugar-added mixed-fruit baby food 
 Directions : 
 With an electric mixer on medium, beat  cream  and  sugar substitute  until medium-firm peaks form. 
 Fold in baby food. Divide among dessert glasses. 
 Chill at least 1 hour before serving.
