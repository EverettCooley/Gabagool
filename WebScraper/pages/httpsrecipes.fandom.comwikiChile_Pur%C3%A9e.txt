Ingredients : 
 2 medium  red chilis , stems and seeds removed 
 5 medium dried  red chiles , stems and seeds removed 
 1  jalapeño pepper , seeds removed, finely chopped 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 2 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 2½ tsp ground  cumin 
 hot water 
 Directions : 
 Soak the dried chiles in hot water for 15 to 20 minutes. 
 Process soaked chile peppers in a blender with a bit of water to make a pureé. 
 Strain out excess liquid.
