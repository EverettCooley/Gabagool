Description : 
 This is a recipe I obtained from an Estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Ellis Estate in Fort Worth, Texas in 1980.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 bunch  beets  peeled and cubed 
 1 cup  red wine 
 ¼ cup  honey 
 2 tablespoons  butter 
 Directions : 
 Place beets in saucepan then add remaining ingredients and enough water to barely cover. 
 Simmer until tender then pour into baking dish and bake at 350°F for 30 minutes.
