 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2  eggs , beaten 
 1 can  cream   corn  (15oz) 
 2/3 cup  milk 
 1 cup cracker crumbs (about 24 
 -crackers) 
 1/2 cup chopped  Onion 
 1/2 cup chopped  green pepper 
 1 cup grated  Cheddar   Cheese 
 1/4 tsp  salt 
 1/8 tsp  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 375 °F. Coat a 2-qt casserole with  vegetable oil spray . In a large mixing bowl, stir together the  eggs ,  corn ,  milk , cracker crumbs,  Onion ,  green pepper  if using,  Cheese ,  salt  and  pepper . Pour into casserole. Bake uncovered, for about 50 minutes, until the  corn  is set in the middle and begins to pull away from the sides of the casserole. Nutritional info per serving: 213 calories; 9g pro, 28g carb, 8g fat (33%)
