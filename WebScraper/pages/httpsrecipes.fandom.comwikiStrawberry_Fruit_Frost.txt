Ingredients : 
 1½ cups California  strawberries , stemmed 
 2 small (or 1½ large), thoroughly ripe  bananas , broken into 1-inch pieces 
 1 cup frozen or canned  peach  slices 
 1 cup  apple juice 
 1 tablespoon  honey 
 1 cup  ice cubes 
 Directions : 
 In container of blender, combine all ingredients except  ice cubes ; blend until smooth. 
 Gradually add  ice cubes  with motor running; blend until smooth. 
 Makes 2 servings (about 2 cups each serving).
