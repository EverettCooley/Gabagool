Ingredients : 
 2 cups  ketchup 
 ½ cup  vinegar 
 ½ cup  honey 
 2 tbsp  soy sauce 
 2 tbsp  brown sugar 
 ½ cup  beer  (may be flat) 
 dash  Tabasco sauce , to taste 
 dash ground  lemon pepper , to taste 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients. 
 Can be used as a marinade, use some of sauce to marinade over night. 
 Then grill and baste with left over sauce.
