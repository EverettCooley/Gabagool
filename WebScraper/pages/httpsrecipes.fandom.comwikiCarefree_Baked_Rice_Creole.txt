Description : 
 Makes 8 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup uncooked  rice 
 2 x 10-ounce packages frozen sliced  okra 
 1 medium  onion , chopped 
 ½ cup chopped  green pepper 
 1 x 14½- to 16-ounce can  tomatoes  (2 cups) 
 2 cups  beef broth  (or  vegetable stock ) 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 Directions : 
 Butter 2½-quart baking dish. Sprinkle half the  rice  in the bottom of dish. 
 Cover with half the  okra , then half the  onion  and  green pepper . 
 Combine  tomatoes , broth,  salt  and  pepper ; heat. 
 Pour 2 cups over mixture in baking dish. Dot with  butter . 
 Repeat layers. 
 Cover and bake at 375°F for about 45 minutes to 1 hour or until  rice  is tender and liquid is absorbed.
