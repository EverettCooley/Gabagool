Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 package (8 oz)  cream cheese , softened 
 shrimp  cocktail sauce 
 chopped  green pepper 
 chopped  green olives 
 1 can  shrimp  or frozen, if preferred 
 Directions : 
 Spread softened cream cheese on plate. 
 Spread with shrimp cocktail sauce. 
 Add green peppers, shrimp and olives. 
 Add more cocktail sauce if desired. 
 Best to eat with crackers or rippled chips. 
 Serve at room temperature.
