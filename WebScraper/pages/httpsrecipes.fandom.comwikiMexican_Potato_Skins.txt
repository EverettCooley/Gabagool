Description : 
       A great twist on a favorite!       Yields:  4-5 servings
 Ingredients : 
 6 medium  russet potatoes 
 ½ lb  ground beef 
 Your favorite meat seasoning 
 4 oz salsa 
 1 tsp chili powder or taco seasoning 
 4 oz grated  cheddar  cheese 
 4 oz  sour cream 
 Directions : 
 Bake  potatoes . russet potatoes 
 Cool and quarter. Scoop out  potato  and place skin side down on a non-stick pan. 
 Brown  ground beef  until done, mix in 4 oz. of salsa & chili powder 
 Scoop seasoned  beef  onto each  potato  skin. 
 Sprinkle cheese onto skins. 
 Bake at 350°F for 15 minutes. 
 Garnish with  sour cream 
 === Add chopped blk olives if desired ===
