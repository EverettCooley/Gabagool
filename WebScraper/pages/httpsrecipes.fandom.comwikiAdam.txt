Ingredients : 
 2 ounces  dark rum 
 1 ounce  lemon juice 
 1 teaspoon  grenadine 
 Directions : 
 In a shaker half filled with ice cubes, combine all of the ingredients. 
 Shake well. 
 Strain into a cocktail glass.
