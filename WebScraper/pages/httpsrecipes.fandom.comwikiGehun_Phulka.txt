The word  Gehun  means  wheat kernel 
 Paper-thin chapatis are made from soaked, finely ground wheat berries that are mixed with whole-wheat  atta  flour and enriched with powdered buttermilk and ghee. 
 The finished product is ultra-thin, reddish-brown in color, soft and pliable.
