 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  oil 
 2  garlic  cloves, diced 
 1  Onion , diced 
 1  carrot , diced 
 1 red  bell pepper , diced 
 1  celery  stick, diced 
 2 tablespoons tamari 
 2 pounds  tofu , mashed 
 4 slices whole  Wheat  bread 
 1 tablespoon  oil 
 1 tablespoon tamari 
 1/2 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 5 tablespoons  nutritional yeast 
 1/2 cup tahini 
 1/2 teaspoon  basil 
 1/2 teaspoon  oregano 
 1/2 teaspoon  turmeric 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in a skillet. Add  garlic ,  Onion ,  pepper ,  carrot  and  celery . Season with the 1 tablespoon tamari. Cook until vegetables are tender. Remove from heat and put into a large mixing bowl. Add mashed  tofu .
 Cut bread into crouton size pieces and quick fry in remaining  oil  with remaining tamari,  garlic powder  and  nutritional yeast . Add to  tofu  mixture. Add remaining ingredients. Mix well. Place in a well oiled casserole dish and press down very well. Bake at 350 degrees F for 35 minutes.
 Allow to cool slightly, then turn out on a plate. Slice and serve. This is also excellent cold.
