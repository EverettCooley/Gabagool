Description : 
 Squash soup
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb cubed,  beef  stew meat 
 1 lb  chicken 
 1 frozen  squash 
 1  boniata 
 9  spinach 
 1  malanga  or  scallops 
 2  onions , sliced 
 3 large  carrots  or some  miso  broth 
 6 medium  potatoes 
 ¼ lb  spaghetti  or noodles 
 3 tsp seasoned  salt 
 2  limes , cut in half 
 2 tsp  thyme 
 2 tsp  parsley 
 ½ cup  scallions 
 salt ,  black pepper , and  hot pepper  or  jalapeños  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Clean the meat with hot  water  and  lemon  and set aside in a bowl. 
 Add seasoning  salt  and set aside for 2 hours. 
 Boil meat in stockpot with 3 quarts of  water  until tender (about 1½ - 2 hrs). 
 Add more additional  water  if necessary and remaining ingredients except noodles. 
 Cook for 20 minutes and add noodles. 
 Then enjoy with a cup of meat stew.
