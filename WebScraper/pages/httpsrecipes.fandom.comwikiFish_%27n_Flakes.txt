Ingredients : 
 2 x 3 oz  fish  fillets 
 ⅛ cup  skimmed milk 
 ⅓ cup crushed  corn flakes 
 ½ tsp  safflower oil 
 season to taste 
 Tartar sauce : 
 ¼ cup low-fat plain  yogurt 
 2 tablespoons  sweet pickle relish 
 Directions : 
 Rinse and pat dry fillets. 
 Dip fillets in  milk  then roll in  corn flakes  let stand briefly so crumbs adhere. 
 Brush the  oil  around the bottom of a 9 x 9 pan. 
 Place fillets in pan, season, and bake (uncovered) at 400°F. 
 For 10 minutes, turning after 5 minutes. 
 Serve immediately. 
 Note: do not try to keep the fillets warm by covering. 
 Tartar sauce : 
 Mix together and spread over fish. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 Calories: 12 | added fat: 0 | cholesterol: 1mg 
 The  corn flakes  values: cholesterol: 43 mg
