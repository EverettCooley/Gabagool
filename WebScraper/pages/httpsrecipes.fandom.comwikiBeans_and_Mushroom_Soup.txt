Ingredients : 
 1 cup white beans 
 3 medium  potatoes 
 1 1/2 cups  Mushrooms 
 1 medium  Onion 
 salt ,  pepper , fresh greens to taste 
 water 
 
 
 Directions : 
 1) Soak beans overnight
 2) Pour the  water  in the pot, bring to a boil, put the soaked beans into the pot and cook on low until the beans are 90% tender
 3) peel and cube the potatoes and add to the soup, keep cooking. In the meantime in the saucepan fry chopped  onions , when they are golden, add  Mushrooms , fry until  Mushrooms  are tender
 4) At the moment when the beans and the potatoes are ready, add the fried onion- Mushroom  mixture to the soup. Mix well, add  salt ,  pepper  & other spices if you feel them to be necessary. Remove from the heat and leave for 2â€“3 minutes to develop flavours.
 Serve with fresh greens and, if desired, with vegan  sour cream
