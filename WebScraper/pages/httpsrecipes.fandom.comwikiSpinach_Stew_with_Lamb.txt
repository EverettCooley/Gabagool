 Description : 
 spinach  Stew with  Lamb 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds lean  Lamb  meat, cubed 
 2 cups  water 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  white pepper 
 4 medium bunches fresh  spinach , washed 
 1 can (14 ounces)  coconut milk 
 4 ounces  butter  or  ghee  (available at ethnic markets) 
 1½ teaspoons  curry  powder 
 1 large  Onion , diced 
 1 large ripe  tomato , diced 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Lamb  in  water  with  salt  and  pepper  until  Lamb  is cooked through, about 10 minutes 
 Put cut  spinach  in medium pot and add  coconut milk 
 Cook over medium heat about 10 minutes 
 Heat  butter ,add  curry  powder,  Onion , and  tomato . 
 Add meat and cook over low heat about 5 minutes. 
 Add reserved  spinach  and  coconut milk  and cook 10 minutes.
