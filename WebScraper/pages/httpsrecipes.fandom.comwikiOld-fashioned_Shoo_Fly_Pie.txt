Description : 
 Purchased from Zimmerman Estate in Terrell, Texas in 1989. This recipe indicates it was received from Ms. Tibbenham’s Sunday School class. Dated 1938. 
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1¼ cup  all-purpose flour , divided 
 ¾ cup  light corn syrup 
 ½ cup  molasses 
 4  eggs 
 ½ teaspoon  baking soda 
 your favorite 9”  pie shell , preferably home made 
 ½ stick  unsalted butter , softened 
 ½ teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  nutmeg 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 In a medium bowl, combine ¼ cup  flour ,  corn syrup ,  molasses ,  eggs  and  baking soda . 
 Mix well and pour into the pie shell. 
 In a small bowl, combine remaining 1 cup  flour ,  butter ,  cinnamon  and  nutmeg . 
 Stir until crumbly then sprinkle evenly over the  corn syrup  mixture. 
 Bake for 55 minutes. 
 Serve warm.
