Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup  orange juice 
 1/4 cup  honey 
 1 pint basket California  strawberries , stemmed and halved 
 1 half-pint basket  raspberries 
 1 half-pint basket  blueberries 
 2  oranges , peeled and cut into sections 
 1 cup  cantaloupe  or  honeydew melon  balls 
 3 tablespoons fresh  mint  leaves 
 Directions : 
 In medium bowl whisk juice and  honey  then add remaining ingredients. 
 Toss gently to combine, chill 1 hour. 
 Spoon salad into 4 individual bowls, dividing equally. Makes 4 servings
