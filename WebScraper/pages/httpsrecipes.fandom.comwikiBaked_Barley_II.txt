 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 	cup 	Cooked & mashed AM beans 
 -- (of choice) 
 1 	cup 	Pre-cooked AM Bits-O- Barley 
 1 	cup 	 soy milk 
 1/2 	cup 	AM Raw  wheat germ 
 2 	tbsp 	AM  soy flour 
 1 	tbsp 	AM Unrefined  vegetable oil 
 1/4 	cup 	 Onion , minced and sauteed 
 3 	tbsp 	Prepared  mustard 
 1/2 	cup 	 Honey 
 1/2 	cup 	 Catsup  of choice 
 1/2 	tsp 	 garlic powder 
 1/2 	tsp 	 Onion  powder 
 1/2 	tsp 	 chili powder 
 sea salt  to taste (optional) 
 Directions : 
 1 	Preheat oven to 350 °F.
 2 	Mix all ingredients together.
 3 	Let stand for 30 minutes.
 4 	Turn into an oiled casserole dish and bake for 40 minutes.
