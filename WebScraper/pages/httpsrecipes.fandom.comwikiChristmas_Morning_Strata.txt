Ingredients : 
 10 	slices	white bread 
 5 	x	 eggs 
 1/2 	quart	 heavy whipping cream 
 3 	cups	diced  Ham 
 2 	cups	shredded  Cheddar   Cheese 
 1 	cup	shredded  Cheddar   Cheese 
 1 	cup	crushed  Potato  chips 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Pre heat oven to 375 degrees. In bowl mix  cream  and  eggs  and set aside. 
 In a caserole dish line with 5 slices of bread. 
 Then sprinkle over the bread the 1/2 the  Ham , half the  Sausage , and half the  Cheese  and 1/2 the  egg  and  cream  mixture. 
 Put the other 5 pieces of bread on top of that. 
 Add on top of that bread the other half of the  Ham ,  Sausage ,  Cheese , and  egg  and  cream  mixture. 
 Season with  salt  and  pepper  to taste then bake for 1 hour at 375 degrees. 
 Then pull out of oven and springle chips on top and broil for 2 minutes until brown. 
 Let stand for 10 minutes and serve.
