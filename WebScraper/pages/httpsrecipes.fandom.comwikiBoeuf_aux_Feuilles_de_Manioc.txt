 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 750 g stew meat 
 2 paquets de feuilles de  manioc 
 3 tablespoons  palm oil 
 piment,  salt 
 1/2 bowl of  Peanut butter 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Cut meat into bite-size pieces. 
 Boil in  water  to remove the juice. 
 Cook  manioc  leaves in another pot. 
 Heat  palm oil . 
 Brown meat at high heat. 
 Turn down heat and continue cooking. 
 Add the feuilles de  manioc  pil√©es, piment, and the remaining meat juice. 
 Let simmer for an hour and a half. 
 Mix the  Peanut butter  with a small amount of  water  and add to mixture. 
 Serve with  manioc .
