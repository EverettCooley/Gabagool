Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Cullen Estate in Allen, Texas in 1984.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1  lime  cut into 8 wedges 
 ¼ cup  coarse salt 
 2 bottles of your favorite  beer  chilled 
 ½ cup frozen concentrate limeade thawed 
 ½ cup chilled  tequila 
 ice cubes 
 Directions : 
 Rub lime wedges around rims of 4 margarita glasses. 
 Dip rims into salt to coat lightly. 
 In medium pitcher combine beer, limeade and tequila. 
 Fill prepared glasses with ice then with margarita mixture. 
 Garnish with remaining lime wedges and serve immediately.
