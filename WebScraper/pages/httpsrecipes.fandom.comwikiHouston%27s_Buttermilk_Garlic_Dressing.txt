 Description : 
 Houston’s is known for their high quality, and service that is par to none. They serve a delicious salad, and here is one of our most requested recipes. Our version of their buttermilk garlic dressing is easy to make, and everyone will wonder if you got some of this salad dressing to go. Try our take on their buttermilk garlic salad dressing.
 
 Secret Restaurant Recipes 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅔ cup  sour cream 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 ¼ tsp crushed  garlic 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  paprika 
 ½ tsp freshly ground  pepper 
 1 tsp  mustard powder 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 ½ cup  buttermilk 
 Directions : 
 Blend all in blender until smooth, flavor is better when it sets for a couple hours and its great used for a dip.
