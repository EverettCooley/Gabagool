Ingredients : 
 1 pint  strawberries 
 2 cups cooked  rhubarb 
 2 tablespoons  lemon juice 
 ⅛ teaspoon  salt 
 1½ cups  sugar 
 ¾ cup  heavy cream 
 Directions : 
 Wash  berries , hull and mash. 
 Press  rhubarb  through sieve, add remaining ingredients, pour into freezing tray of refrigerator and freeze without stirring until firm. 
 When ready to serve, scrape up thin layers of the mixture with an inverted spoon, beat back and forth in the tray until smooth and serve immediately.
