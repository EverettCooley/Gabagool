 Description : 
 Begin a meal with Pe Chan Gyaw (Split-pea), an authentic Burmese recipe.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 5 tablespoons  rice flour 
 500 ml (2 cups)  water 
 100g (1/2 cup) dried yellow split- Peas 
 1/4 teaspoons  salt 
 250 ml (1 cup)  oil  for deep frying 
 Directions : 
 Soak the split- Peas  overnight. The next day, mix the  rice flour  and  water , then add and stir in the split- Peas  and  salt . In a saucepan or wok, heat the  oil  for deep-frying. Using a spoon, scoop into the mixture and drop it in the pan. Drop several scoops and deep-fry them until they turn crisp. Drain on absorbent paper and let cool before serving.
