Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup boiling  water 
 1 (4 oz) sugar-free  Hawaiian pineapple Jell-O 
 ½ cup cold  water  plus  ice  cubes to make 1 cup 
 ½ tsp  vanilla 
 1 (8 oz) carton plain low-fat  yogurt 
 1 cup drained crushed  pineapple 
 1 can drained  mandarin oranges 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve jello in boiling  water . 
 Add cold  water  (remove undissolved ice cubes). 
 Blend in  yogurt , mixing well. 
 Add  vanilla  and fruit. 
 Pour into baked pie shell over 6 sherbet glasses.
