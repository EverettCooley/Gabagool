 Source:  With Love From Darling's Kitchen 
 Serves 6 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds  potatoes , boiled, peeled and sliced 
 1  carrot , grated 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 1 tbsp  vinegar 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 4 tbsp  lemon juice 
 Â¼ cup chopped  chives  or  green onions 
 2 tbsp chopped  parsley 
 2 tbsp prepared  horseradish 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl, toss together first 5 ingredients until nicely mixed. 
 Stir together the next 5 ingredients and add to the  potatoes , tossing and turning until blended. 
 Cover and refrigerate for several hours or overnight. 
 Serve with cold  ham ,  corned beef , and the like.
