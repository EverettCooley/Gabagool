Ingredients : 
 6 medium  potatoes , boiled in jackets until barely fork tender, peeled and cubed 
 ½ bunch  parsley 
 ½ bunch  scallions 
 ½ cups  lemon juice 
 ⅓ cups  oil 
 ¼ cups  simaq  (optional) 
 ½ clove  garlic 
 1½ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Finely chop scallions and  parsley . 
 Add to cubed  potatoes  in large bowl. 
 Mash  garlic  and  salt . 
 Add to  lemon  and  oil . 
 Pour over  potatoes  and mix well. 
 Add simaq if desired and adjust seasoning.
