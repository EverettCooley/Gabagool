Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Barilla 
 4 – 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup extra virgin  olive oil 
 2 cloves peeled  garlic 
 1 tsp red pepper 
 2 pt cut in halves  cherry tomatoes 
 5 leaves  basil 
 salt  to taste 
 1 pkg  angel hair 
 ½ cup  pecorino  dolce 
 Directions : 
 Heat olive oil in skillet. 
 Peel and gently press garlic. 
 Add garlic and pepper. 
 Cut cherry tomatoes in ½ and tear basil into sm pieces. 
 Add cherry tomatoes and basil. 
 Simmer 5 min. 
 Season with salt. 
 Discard garlic cloves. 
 Cook pasta according to directions. 
 Drain and toss with sauce. 
 Garnish with freshly grated pecorino.
