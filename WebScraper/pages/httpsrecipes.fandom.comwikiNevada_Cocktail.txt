 Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 oz  light rum 
 1 oz  grapefruit juice 
 Juice of 1  lime 
 1 dash  bitters 
 4 tsp  powdered sugar 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Shake with  ice  and strain into a glass. Top with a  grapefruit  wedge.
