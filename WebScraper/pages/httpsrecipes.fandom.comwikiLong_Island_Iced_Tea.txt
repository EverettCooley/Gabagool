 Description : 
 The signature ice-cold alcoholic beverage is a great combination of real fruit juice, vodka, gin, triple-sec, tequila, cola, and brewed tea and is great with any meal. This is as cold a glass as it can get, while it also keeps a bold, juicy taste with the alcohol content.
 
         
 Ingredients : 
 ½ shot Stolichnaya Red  vodka 
 ½ shot  gin 
 ½ shot De Kuyper  triple sec 
 ½ shot  white rum 
 ½ shot  tequila 
 1 shot  lemon juice 
 splash of coke 
 ½ cup brewed tea, optional 
 1 teaspoon  caster sugar 
 Directions : 
 Shake all the ingredients except the coke and the brewed tea with  ice  and strain into a highball. 
 Pour the coke and brewed tea on the top. Serve cold and plain. Pair this alcohol-based beverage with any meal, if desired.
