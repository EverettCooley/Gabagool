This is another family recipe. This is also very good. Hope you enjoy.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 (8 ounce) packages  cream cheese  (softened) 
 1 (4 ounce) can crushed  pineapple  (drained) 
 2 tablespoons finely chopped  green peppers 
 1 tablespoon finely chopped  onions 
 1 teaspoon lowery seasoning  salt 
 chopped  nuts 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients very well. 
 Chill mixture for about 20 minutes. 
 Then form into 2 balls and roll into chopped  nuts , and serve.
