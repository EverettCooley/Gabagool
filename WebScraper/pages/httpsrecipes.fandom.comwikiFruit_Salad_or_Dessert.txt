Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 About 8 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (3 oz) pkg.  cream cheese 
 1 cup  salad dressing 
 1 cup crushed  pineapple , drained 
 16 large  marshmallows , quartered 
 Â½ pt.  whipping cream 
 small bottle  maraschino cherries , drained 
 strawberry-flavored gelatin 
 Directions : 
 Whip cream and mix with creamed cheese and dressing. 
 Fold in fruits and marshmallows. 
 Put in pan about 9 x 13 inches. 
 Chill or freeze. 
 When thoroughly firm, pour a layer of strawberry gelatin that has been mixed according to package directions. 
 Be sure gelatin is cool before pouring. 
 Chill until firm. 
 Cut in squares and serve on lettuce for salad or on individual plates for dessert. 
 Enjoy.
