 Description : 
 In Romanian: Rulada cu nuci
 
 Ingredients : 
 Same quantities as for  Rolled Cake with Marmalade or Preserves 
 Filling : 
 8 oz / 250 g  ground walnuts 
 1 cup  milk 
 3 tablespoons  sugar 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla   or  a little  rum 
 Directions : 
 Boil the  milk  with the  sugar  and  walnuts  at low heat, mixing continuously until you get a soft paste. Add  vanilla  or  rum . 
 Use this paste to spread on the dough prepared the same way as for  Rolled Cake with Marmalade or Preserves .
