      Homemade Louisiana Kahlua     
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups  sugar 
 5 cups  water 
 2 ounces instant  coffee 
 a 5th of  vodka  (entire bottle) 
 4 tsp  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Mix  sugar  and  water  and simmer 45 minutes. 
 Then add 2 oz, instant  coffee . 
 Simmer 15 minutes. 
 Let cool. 
 Add  vodka  and  vanilla . 
 Rebottle and let it stand 2 weeks.
