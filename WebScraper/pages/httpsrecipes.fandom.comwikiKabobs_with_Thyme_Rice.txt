Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound boneless  beef  sirloin, cut into 1-inch cubes 
 ¼ cup Italian  salad dressing 
 1 medium  zucchini , cut into chunks 
 1 medium-size  yellow squash , cut into chunks 
 1 medium-size  red onion , cut into chunks 
 1 medium-size  red pepper , cut into squares 
 ½ pound  cherry tomatoes 
 1 cup uncooked  rice 
 2 cups  chicken broth 
 ½ teaspoon dried  thyme  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Combine  beef  in large plastic bag with zippered closing. 
 Add  salad dressing ; marinate 4 to 6 hours in refrigerator. 
 Alternate marinated  beef  with vegetables on skewers. 
 Grill or broil 5 to 7 minutes on each side. 
 Combine  rice , broth and  thyme  in 2- to 3-quart saucepan. Bring to a boil; stir once or twice. 
 Reduce heat; cover and simmer 15 minutes or until  rice  is tender and liquid is absorbed. 
 Serve with kabobs.
