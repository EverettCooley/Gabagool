Description : 
 In Romanian: Sufleu de conopida
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 medium  cauliflower 
 3  eggs 
 ½ tablespoon  flour 
 1 cup  milk 
 2 tablespoons  butter 
 a pinch of  salt 
 Directions : 
 Boil the  cauliflower  in  salt   water , drain and set aside. Fry the  flour  with the  butter , add  milk , stirring continuously, then add  yolks  one by one, stirring at all times. 
 After well cooled,  butter  a mold, arrange the  cauliflower  within and pour the  egg  mixture to which the whipped  egg  whites have also been added, after cooling. 
 Salt  and bake at medium heat. Serve warm.
