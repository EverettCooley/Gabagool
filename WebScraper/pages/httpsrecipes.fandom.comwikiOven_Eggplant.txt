 Description : 
 From "Vegetarian Cooking for Diabetics" 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 medium  eggplant 
 ½ cup  cottage cheese , low fat 
 2 tbsp  onion , chopped 
 1 tsp  bay leaf , ground 
 1 tsp  basil 
 1 tsp  oregano 
 2 tbsp  tomato sauce 
 2 oz  provolone 
 Directions : 
 Cook  eggplant  in boiling  water , covered, for 10 minutes. 
 Drain and cut in half lengthwise. 
 Scoop out insides leaving ½" shell. 
 Mash scooped out insides with  cottage cheese ,  onion ,  bay leaf ,  basil ,  oregano  and  tomato  sauce. 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F stuff  eggplant  halves, place in casserole dish and bake covered for 15 minutes . 
 Add a little  water  to bottom of pan to keep  eggplant  moist. 
 Top with  provolone  cheese. 
 Bake 5 more minutes uncovered. 
 Nutritional informaton : 
 1 serving = 244c, 2 meat, 1 bread, 2 veg protein 10gm, fat 4gm, carbo 10 gm
