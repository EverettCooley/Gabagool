Description 
 Bread Recipes  by the South Carolina Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Ingredients 
 1 pkg. Jiffy cornbread mix 
 2 cups chopped  broccoli , cooked 
 1 cup  cottage cheese 
 4  eggs , beaten 
 1 stick  margarine 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1  onion , finely chopped 
 Directions 
 Mix ingredients together. 
 Pour in shallow baking dish. 
 Bake in preheated 400 °F oven for 25 – 30 minutes. 
 Cut in squares to serve.
