 Description : 
 These are tasty little meatballs that are very easy to prepare. You can serve them with a variety of dipping sauces. Great for cocktails and dinner parties or just having snacks.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs. ground  chicken 
 1 cup fresh  breadcrumbs 
 4  scallions , finely sliced 
 1 tablespoon ground  coriander 
 1 cup chopped fresh  cilantro 
 2 tablespoons fresh  lemon juice 
 oil , for shallow-frying 
 grated cucumber, optional 
 Â¼ cup hot sauce or sweet  chili sauce , optional 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients, except  oil ,  cucumber , and  chile  sauces. 
 Wet hands, and take one tablespoon of ground chicken mixture and form into a meatball; repeat. Freeze the Thai-style meatballs overnight. 
 Shallow-fry the meatball with the oil until they are brown and cooked through. 
 Serve hot and plain or with other dishes. Top with the grated cucumber and lightly drizzle with the hot sauce or sweet chili sauce, if desired.
