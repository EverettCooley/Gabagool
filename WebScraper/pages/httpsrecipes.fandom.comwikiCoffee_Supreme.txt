coffee  Supreme
 
 1/2 Cup  coffee  (8 T) 
 2 Sticks  cinnamon 
 2 T. Chopped  crystallized ginger 
 2 T.  brown sugar 
 3 Cups  water 
 Prepared Whipped Topping
 Ground  cinnamon 
 
Mix  coffee ,  cinnamon  Sticks And  ginger  in Brew Basket of Drip  coffee  Maker. Place  brown sugar  in Empty Pot of  coffee  Maker. Prepare  coffee  With 3 Cups of  water . Garnish With Topping And Sprinkle of  cinnamon .Serves 6
