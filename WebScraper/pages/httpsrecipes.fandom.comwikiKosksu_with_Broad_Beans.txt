 Description : 
 Kosksu is small  pasta  pellets. It is manufactured in Malta.
 
 Ingredients : 
 10 oz. kosksu  [1]  (type of  pasta ) 
 1  onion  chopped 
 5 oz peeled  broad beans 
 3 oz peeled  tomatoes 
 2 oz  tomato paste 
 1  beef  cube 
 2 pint  water 
 Directions : 
 Fry the  onion  in a little  olive oil  till golden brown. 
 Add the chopped  tomatoes  and  tomato  paste. 
 And the  water . 
 Bring to the boil, add the  beef  cube and the kosksu  pasta , and simmer till cooked. 
 Add the broad beans 10 minutes before removing from the heat.
