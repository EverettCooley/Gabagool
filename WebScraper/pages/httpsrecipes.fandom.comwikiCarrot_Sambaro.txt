 Description : 
 carrot  Sambaro
 
 Ingredients : 
 250gm 	organic  carrots 
 5 ml 	 mustard seed 
 2.5 ml 	Fair Trade  turmeric 
 2 	cloves  garlic 
 5 ml 	Fair Trade  chilli powder 
 15 ml 	FAIRTRADE  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Peel the  carrots  and slice them small, and then par boil. 
 Peel and chop the  garlic . 
 In a heavy pan fry the  mustard seed ,  turmeric ,  garlic , and  chilli  for a minute. 
 Add the par-boiled  carrots  and stir them round until they are coated with the spice mixture. 
 Add a little  salt , cover the pan, and simmer gently for 5 minutes.
