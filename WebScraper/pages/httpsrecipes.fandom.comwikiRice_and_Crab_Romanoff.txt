Description : 
 Makes 4 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups hot cooked  rice 
 1/2 cup creamed  cottage cheese 
 1 2-ounce can sliced  Mushrooms , drained 
 2 tablespoons chopped  chives 
 1/4 cup  sour cream 
 1/4 cup  Mayonnaise 
 2 tablespoons grated  Parmesan cheese , divided 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1/8 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1/8 teaspoon  ground red pepper 
 1/2 teaspoon  worcestershire sauce 
 8 ounces crab meat 
 paprika 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  cottage cheese ,  Mushrooms  and chiles in large bowl. 
Blend  sour cream ,  Mayonnaise , 1 tablespoon  Parmesan cheese ,  salt ,  black pepper , red pepper and  worcestershire sauce ; add to  rice  mixture. Fold in crab meat gently. Turn into greased shallow 1-quart baking dish. Sprinkle with remaining 1 tablespoon  Parmesan cheese  and with  paprika . Bake at 350 degrees 25 to 30 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
