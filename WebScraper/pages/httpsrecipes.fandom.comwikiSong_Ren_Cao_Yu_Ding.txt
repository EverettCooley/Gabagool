Description : 
 Stir-fried fish cubes with pinenuts
 
 Ingredients : 
 250 g  fish  ( grouper  or  sole ) 
 1 stalk  celery 
 4 tbsp  pine nuts 
 1  red pepper 
 1 tbsp minced  ginger 
 2 stalks  spring onion 
 1 tbsp  yellow wine 
 Seasoning : 
 ⅓ tbsp  salt 
 1 tbsp  egg white 
 1 tbsp  cornflour 
 1 tbsp  oil 
 some  pepper 
 Sauce : 
 ½ tbsp  cornflour 
 ⅕ tbsp  salt 
 3 tbsp  water 
 some  sesame oil 
 some  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Fry  pine nuts  in low heat  oil  until crispy, dish up for use. 
 Trim the  celery , wash and cut into small stalks. 
 Heat wok with 1 tbsp  oil , stir fry  celery  until cooked, dish up. 
 Remove seeds of  red pepper , chop into fine dices, chop  spring onion . 
 Clean fish and wipe dry, cut into small cubes, marinate with seasoning. 
 Parboil fish cubes in high heat, dish up until cooked. 
 Heat 2 tbsp of  oil , stir fry minced  ginger  and  red pepper . 
 Add fish cubes,  celery  and wine, stir fry and mix well, then add sauce. 
 Add chopped  spring onion , then  pine nuts , stir fry mix it well, dish up.
