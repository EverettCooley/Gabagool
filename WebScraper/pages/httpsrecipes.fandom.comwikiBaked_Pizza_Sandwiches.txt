Description : 
 This recipe came from The Printey Estate sale. It was obtained it as part of the Family Collection from The Printey Estate in Forney, Texas in 2001. 
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound lean  ground beef 
 15 ounce can  tomato sauce 
 15 ounce can  pizza sauce 
 1 teaspoon  oregano  leaves 
 2 cups  biscuit baking mix 
 1 large  egg 
 ⅔ cup  milk 
 8 ounces sliced  mozzarella  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Heat Oven to 400°F. 
 Cook and stir meat in a large skillet until brown then drain off the excess fat. 
 Stir in half of the  tomato  sauce and the  oregano  leaves into the meat mixture 
 Heat to boiling and reduce and simmer uncovered for 10 minutes. 
 While the meat mixture is simmering mix the baking mix,  egg  and  milk . 
 Measure out ¾ cup of the batter and set aside. 
 Spread the remaining batter in a greased baking rectangular baking pan and pour remaining sauce over batter, spreading evenly. 
 Layer 4 slices of the  cheese , the meat mixture, the  mushrooms  and the remaining  cheese  on top of the batter and  tomato  sauce. 
 Spoon the reserved batter on the top of the cheese and bake uncovered for 25 minutes. 
 Cool 5 minutes before cutting into squares.
