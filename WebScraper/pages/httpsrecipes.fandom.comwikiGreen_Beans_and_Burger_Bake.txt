Makes 8 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound lean ground  Beef 
 1 cup chopped  Onion 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 cup (4 ounces) shredded  Cheddar   Cheese , divided 
 1 1/2 teaspoons  garlic salt 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1 16-ounce can cut  green beans , drained 
 3 tablespoons chopped pimientos 
 1 10-3/4-ounce can condensed  cream  of  celery  soup 
 1/4 cup  Mayonnaise 
 1/2 cup  milk 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Beef  and  Onion  in large skillet until meat turns brown, stirring to crumble. Add  rice , 1/2 cup  Cheese ,  garlic salt  and  pepper . 
 Turn into greased shallow 2-1/2-quart baking dish. 
 Top with  green beans  and pimientos. 
 Blend soup,  Mayonnaise ,  milk  and  lemon juice  in small bowl; pour over beans. 
 Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup  Cheese . 
 Bake at 350 degrees 25 to 30 minutes.
