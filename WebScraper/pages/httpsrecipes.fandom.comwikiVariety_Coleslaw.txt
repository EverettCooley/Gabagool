Ingredients : 
 2 cup shredded  cabbage 
 1 medium  carrot , chopped or grated 
 2 tbsp  green pepper , chopped 
 ¼ cup boiled  dressing , diet (a dressing made with artificial sweetener) 
 2 tbsp plain  yogurt 
 choice of one:
 ½  apple , chopped or 
 ⅓ cup drained, unsweetened  pineapple  or 
 2 tbsp  raisins 
 Directions : 
 Combine  cabbage ,  carrot  and  green pepper  in a bowl. 
 Combine dressing with  yogurt . 
 Add  apple  or  pineapple  or  raisins  to  cabbage  mixture. 
 Pour on dressing and mix gently.
