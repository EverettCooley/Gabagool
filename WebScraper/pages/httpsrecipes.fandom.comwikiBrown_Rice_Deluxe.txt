brown rice  Deluxe
 
 1 C. Quick Cooking  brown rice 
 1 (3 1/2 Oz.) Can French Fried  onions 
 2 Cans  Mushroom  Soup 
 1/4 C. Stuffed  green olives , Sliced 
 1/4 C. Freshly Grated Parmesan or  Cheddar   Cheese 
 1 (2 Oz.) Can  Mushroom  Stems & Pieces 
 1/2 C.  milk 
 1/4 Tsp.  pepper 
 Cook  brown rice  According to Package Directions. Drain And Place in a 2Quart Casserole Dish. Add The  onions ,  Mushrooms  And (but Save TheJuice From The  Mushrooms ). Mix All Gently. in Another Bowl, Put The Soup, milk ,  pepper  And Juice From The  Mushrooms ; Set All Aside. When Ready to BakePour The  Mushroom  Mixture Into  rice  Mixture And Mix All Gently. Bake,Uncovered at 350 Degrees For 30 Minutes. Then Sprinkle  Cheese  Over The Top.Bake 10 Minutes More. Serves 6.
