Description : 
 Makes 8 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 2 x 6½-ounce cans  tuna  in water, drained and flaked 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 2 teaspoons seasoned  pepper 
 2 teaspoons  dill weed 
 1 teaspoon seasoned  salt 
 1½ cups sliced  celery 
 1 cup sliced  green onions 
 1 cup cooked  green peas 
 1 x 4-ounce can sliced  mushrooms , drained 
 1 x 4-ounce jar sliced  pimentos , drained 
 ⅓ cup sliced ripe  olives 
 lettuce  leaves 
 quartered hard-cooked  eggs  for garnish (optional) 
 quartered  tomatoes  for garnish (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  tuna ,  sour cream ,  pepper ,  dill weed  and  salt  in large bowl; toss lightly. 
 Cover and marinate 1 to 2 hours in refrigerator. 
 Mix with  celery ,  onions ,  peas ,  mushrooms , pimientos and ; chill. 
 Serve on  lettuce  leaves; garnish with  eggs  and  tomatoes , if desired.
