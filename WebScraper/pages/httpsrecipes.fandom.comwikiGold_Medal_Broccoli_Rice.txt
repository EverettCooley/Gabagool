Description : 
 Makes 10 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup chopped  onion 
 ¾ cup chopped  celery 
 Vegetable  cooking spray 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 x 10-ounce package frozen chopped  broccoli , thawed 
 1 x 10¾-ounce can low-fat  cream of mushroom  soup 
 ¾ cup (6 ounces) grated  Cheddar  cheese, divided 
 ½ cup light  sour cream 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Cook  onion  and  celery  in large skillet coated with vegetable  cooking spray  until tender crisp. 
 Add  rice ,  broccoli , soup, ½ cup  cheese ,  sour cream ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Spoon into 1½- to 2-quart baking dish coated with  cooking spray . 
 Bake at 400°F for 35 minutes. 
 Top with remaining ¼ cup  cheese . Bake 3 minutes longer, or until  cheese  melts.
