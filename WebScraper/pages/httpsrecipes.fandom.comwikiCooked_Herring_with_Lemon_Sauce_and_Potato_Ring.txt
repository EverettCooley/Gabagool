       Cooked Herring with Lemon Sauce and Potato Ring     
 Ingredients : 
 2 lb  Herring 
 water 
 salt 
 Potato  RINGÂ :
 
 1 1/2 lb  potatoes 
 1 Grated or chopped  Onion 
 2 Slices white bread 
 2  eggs 
 1/2 c  milk 
 1/4 c  butter 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 lemon  SAUCE:
 
 2 tb  butter  or  margarine 
 2 tb  flour 
 2 c broth 
 2  egg   yolks 
 1/2 ts  salt  (if broth is not sufficiently salted) 
 Juice of 1  lemon 
 Directions : 
 Peel  potatoes  and cook until soft. Drain and mash or  rice  the  potatoes . Cover bread with  milk  and let stand for a few minutes. Mix together the  potatoes , grated  Onion , bread, and  milk . Season with  salt  and  pepper . Add melted  butter  and beaten  egg   yolks . Beat  egg  whites stiff and fold in.  butter  a ring mold and sprinkle with bread crumbs. Put  Potato  mixture into mold and bake in a moderate oven (375F) until nicely browned. Turn out on serving platter. While  Potato  ring is baking, clean, bone, rinse and fillet  Herring . (Frozen  Herring  fillets may be
used.) Cut fillets in pieces and cook in boiling salted  water  only until tender. Carefully remove and drain  Herring  and place in center of  Potato  ring. Pour  lemon  sauce over  Herring  and serve.
For  lemon  sauce: Melt  butter  or  margarine  and add  flour . Stir until well blended. Add broth slowly, stirring constantly. Beat  egg   yolks  and add  salt . Add to sauce, a little at a time, stirring briskly. Do not boil after  eggs  are added. Add strained  lemon juice .
