 Ingredients : 
 2 shots Stolichnaya  vodka 
 ½ shot de Kuyper  crème de cassis 
 ½ shot fresh  lime juice 
 1 teaspoon  caster sugar 
 4 fresh  strawberries 
 Directions : 
 Muddle  strawberries  in cocktail shaker, then shake the other ingredients with cubed  ice  and strain.
