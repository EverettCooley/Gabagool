Description : 
 Ethnicity - South Indian 
 Type of meal - Party, Lunch, Dinner 
 Ingredients : 
 6 – 8 sticks  celery  chopped 
 ½ cup  green moong  (cherupayar) cooked to ¾ th 
 ¾ cup  grated coconut 
 1 small  onion , chopped 
 2 cloves  garlic 
 1 small piece  ginger  cut into julienne strips 
 6  green chillies  chopped (can alter based on taste) 
 2 – 3  dried red chillies 
 1 stick  curry leaves 
 1 tsp  mustard 
 ½ tsp  cumin 
 1 tbsp  oil 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in a pan. 
 Add  mustard  to it and let it splutter. 
 Just pound the  garlic  and  cumin  together and add it to the  oil  and fry it for few seconds. 
 Add  ginger , chopped  onions , green chillies,  curry leaves , red chillies and fry it for a minute. 
 Add the  celery  and  coconut  and cook it till the  celery  is half cooked. 
 Add the cooked cherupayar and cook it covered for some more until everything is evenly cooked. 
 Add  salt  to taste.
