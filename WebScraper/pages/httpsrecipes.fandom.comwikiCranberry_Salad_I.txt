Ingredients : 
 1 cup fresh  cranberries  chopped 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 2 cups mini  marshmallows 
 ½ cup chopped  pecans 
 ⅓ cup diced unpeeled red  McIntosh apples 
 1 cup  Cool Whip 
 Directions : 
 Blend first three ingredients. 
 Fold in the next three ingredients after blending.
