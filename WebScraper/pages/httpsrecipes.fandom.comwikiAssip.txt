 Description : 
 Assip  is a delicious traditional Kyrgyz dish - palatable sausages made from by-products and  rice . They can be served cold and hot as well.
 
 Ingredients : 
 lamb  heart and lungs 
 chopped  onion 
 pepper 
 salt 
 rice 
 water  (150 g) 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Chop  lamb  heart and lungs finely and combine with finely chopped  onion ,  pepper ,  salt  and  rice . 
 Stuff  lamb  bowels with the  mincemeat  and pour in some  water  (150 g).
