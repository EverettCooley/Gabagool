This recipe comes from an Estate Sale and were obtained when  The Family Collection  from The Wetzel Estate in Plano, Texas was purchased in 1982. Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3  Apples , sliced thinly and unpeeled 
 1 Cup crushed  pineapple , drained (reserve Juice) 
 ½ Teaspoon  cinnamon 
 1½ Teaspoons  granulated sugar 
 ¼ Cup  pineapple juice 
 Directions : 
 Slice  apples  into a baking dish 
 Sprinkle  pineapple  over  apples  and sprinkle with  sugar  and  cinnamon . 
 Pour juice over mixture 
 Bake in microwave for 7 minutes.
