 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup california date 
 1/2 cup  orange juice 
 3 frozen  vanilla   yogurt 
 Directions : 
 Combine  dates  and  orange juice  in blender and puree until  dates  are finely chopped. Add frozen  yogurt ; blend until just combined.
