Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Hart Estate in Sherman, Texas in 1997. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 10 ounce can  apple juice 
 2 quarts  ginger ale 
 1 bottle  light rum 
 juice of 12  limes 
 3 ounce  grenadine 
 Directions : 
 Stir apple juice and ginger ale in punch bowl. 
 Add large block of ice cubes and stir. 
 Add rum, lime juice and grenadine then stir well. 
 Decorate with apples, pineapple rings and cherries.
