 Yield: 8 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 3 lbs  chicken  parts, skin and fat removed 
 1 large  onion , sliced thinly 
 3 tbsp  vinegar 
 3 tbsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 2 tbsp  brown sugar 
 black pepper , to taste, 
 1 tbsp  hot pepper flakes 
 1 tbsp  chili powder 
 1 cup  chicken stock 
 Directions : 
 Place  chicken  in a 13x9x2 inch pan. 
 Arrange  onions  over the top. 
 Mix together ingredients together and pour over  chicken . 
 Baked at 350Â°F for 1 hour or until done. 
 Baste occasionally.
