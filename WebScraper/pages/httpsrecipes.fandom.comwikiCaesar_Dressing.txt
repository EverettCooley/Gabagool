Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cloves (2 teaspoons) minced  garlic 
 3  anchovy  fillets (1 teaspoon) rinsed and minced 
 1 teaspoons  Worcestershire sauce 
 1 teaspoons  Dijon mustard 
 3 tablespoons fresh  lemon juice 
 1 large  egg yolk 
 5 tablespoons extra virgin  olive oil 
 2 tablespoons freshly grated  Parmesan 
 salt  and freshly ground  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Whisk first 6 ingredients together until combined. 
 Whisk in olive oil to form a smooth dressing. 
 Stir in  cheese  and season to taste with salt and  pepper . 
 Chill until ready to use.
