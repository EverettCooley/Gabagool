Ingredients : 
 1 cup hot  water 
 ⅔ cup  oatmeal 
 1 tbsp  raisins 
 ½ tsp  cinnamon 
 1 tbsp  sunflower seeds 
 ¼ cup  milk  or  yogurt  (low-fat) 
 Directions : 
 Mix in a small pan the hot  water  and  oatmeal . 
 Cook gently for 5 – 6 minutes. 
 Add  raisins ,  cinnamon  and  sunflower seeds . 
 Serve with  milk  or  yogurt . 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 Calories: 352 | pro: 15gm | fat: 6gm | car: 57gm 
 Food exchanges: 2 starch/breads  | 1 fruit  | 1 fat  | ½ milk
