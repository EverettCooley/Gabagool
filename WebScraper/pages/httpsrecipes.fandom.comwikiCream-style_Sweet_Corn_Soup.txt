 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can cream-style  corn  (450 grams) 
 4 cups clear  vegetable stock 
 2 tablespoons  cornflour  mixed with ¼ cup  water 
 ½ teaspoon  ajinomoto  powder (optional) 
 ½ teaspoon  soy sauce 
 salt  to taste 
 To serve : 
 Chillies  in  vinegar 
 chilli sauce 
 soy sauce 
 Directions : 
 Boil the stock, add the  cream  style  corn  and mix well. 
 Allow it to come to a boil. 
 Add the  cornflour  paste, Ajinomoto,  soy sauce  and  salt  and simmer for a few minutes. 
 Serve hot with the chillies in  vinegar ,  chilli  sauce and  soy sauce .
