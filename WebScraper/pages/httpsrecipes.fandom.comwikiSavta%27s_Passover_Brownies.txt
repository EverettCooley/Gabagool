Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  cocoa 
 ½ cup  oil 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 2  eggs 
 1 cup  sugar 
 ½ cup  cake meal 
 ½ cup chopped  nuts 
 Directions : 
 Mix above ingredients together. 
 Pour into 8 x 9 inch square pan (greased).
