Description : 
 Serves 2.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x 10-ounce package frozen  strawberries , partially thawed 
 2 tablespoons  tequila 
 1 tablespoon  orange liqueur 
 1 tablespoon freshly-squeezed  lime juice 
 1 cup  ice  cubes 
 Directions : 
 In blender, combine  strawberries ,  tequila ,  orange liqueur , and  lime juice . 
 Mix well. 
 Add  ice  cubes + blend until smooth.
