 Description : 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound ground  Beef 
 1  Onion , chopped 
 1 16-ounce can  chili beans , with liquid 
 1 15-ounce can black  beans , with liquid 
 1 15-ounce can whole kernel  corn , with liquid 
 2 cups  water 
 1 14.5-ounce cans peeled and diced  tomatoes 
 1 14.5 ounce can diced tomatoes with green chiles 
 1 1.25-ounce package  taco seasoning  mix 
 Directions : 
 In a medium skillet, cook the ground  Beef  & onion until browned over medium heat. 
 Drain and set aside. 
 Place the ground  Beef ,  Onion ,  chili beans , black  beans ,  corn ,  water , diced  tomatoes , and  taco seasoning  mix in a slow cooker (or use large pot on top of stove) 
 Mix to blend. 
 Cook on low for eight hours. ( This is really done when you put it together. Can be prepared in large pot and simmered 30 min. then eaten.) 
 Top bowls with  corn  chips, shredded  Cheddar   Cheese  and a dollop of  sour cream .
