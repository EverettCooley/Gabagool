 Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 4 boneless center cut  pork chops  (thick cut) 
 2 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 Seasoning mix : 
 1 teaspoon  cayenne pepper 
 1 teaspoon  ground ginger 
 1 tablespoon  paprika 
 ½ teaspoon ground  cumin 
 ¼ teaspoon  white pepper 
 ¼ teaspoon  black pepper 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon whole  thyme  leaves (dry) 
 ½ teaspoon  chili powder 
 Directions : 
 Combine seasoning mix ingredients; mix well. 
 Dust both sides of pork chops with the seasoning mix and reserve. 
 Heat a large cast iron pan over a medium flame and add the vegetable oil. 
 When the pan is hot, add the  pork chops . 
 Cook the  pork  chops for 10 to 14 minutes or until they have reached an internal temperature of 145°F. 
 Note: chili-dusted  pork chops  are also excellent grilled.
