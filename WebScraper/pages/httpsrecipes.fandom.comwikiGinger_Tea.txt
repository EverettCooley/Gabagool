 Description : 
 A refreshing beverage that is popular in  Ayurvedic  treatment for eliminating toxins and  ama  if taken regularly. You may use fresh  gingerroot  ( adrak ) or  dried ginger  (saunth), I prefer fresh.
 
 Ingredients : 
 One or more cups of  water 
 2 or 3 Slices of  ginger 
 1 tsp. Of  lemon juice  (optional) 
 1 tsp. Of  honey  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Place  water  in pot with  ginger  slices and bring to a boil. 
 It will start to fizz, turn down heat. 
 Simmer for about 5 minutes. until dark yellow. 
 Add optional ingredients if you like.
