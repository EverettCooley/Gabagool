      Chicken Soup with Guacamole     
 Ingredients : 
 2 soup  chickens 
 ½ cup  rice 
 salt  to taste 
 2 medium  avocados 
 1 large  tomato 
 4  scallion , sliced 
 1  serrano chile  ; minced 
 2 tbsp  cilantro ; chopped 
 Directions : 
 Make the  chicken  soup from a well-grown hen with pieces of meat. 
 Peel and slice the avocados, and cut into 1" pieces. 
 Pour soup into an onion-soup pots, and spoon on the uncooked topping.
