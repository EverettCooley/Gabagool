 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 6 medium  zucchini  (for frying: 2 tablespoons  flour , 3-4 tablespoons  oil ) 
 2 tablespoons  oil 
 1 teaspoon  butter 
 1 cup  sour cream  or  yogurt 
 1 teaspoon  flour 
 1 teaspoon chopped dill 
 1  bay leaf 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Peel the  zucchini , cut into thin slices. Dry each slice with a cloth, dredge with  flour  and fry in  oil . 
 Put in a heat resistant dish; pour the  sour cream  mixed with  butter ,  flour  and  salt , sprinkle with chopped dill, add the  bay leaf  and bake for 20 minutes. 
 Serve hot.
