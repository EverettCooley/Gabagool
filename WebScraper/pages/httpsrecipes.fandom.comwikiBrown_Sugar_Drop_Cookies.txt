Ingredients : 
 1 C.  shortening 
 2  eggs 
 1 1/2 Tsp.  baking soda 
 2 C.  brown sugar 
 1/2 C. Warm  water 
 1 Tsp.  salt  (optional) 
 4 C.  flour 
 1 Tsp.  vanilla 
 Ingredients : 
 Mix ingredients together. 
 Drop Cookie Dough Onto Ungreased SheetAnd Make Imprint With Thumb, Spoon on Date Filling (homemade or Canned) And Seal With Dough Cover. 
 Bake And Let Cool on Flat Surface. Fruit Filling Will BeVery Hot. 
 Notes : 
 Any Semi-Solid Fruit Can be Used as a Filling,  strawberry  Jam is Good as is  Apple  or  blueberry . Very Good! Bake at 350 Degrees For 8-10 Minutes.
 
Enjoy.
