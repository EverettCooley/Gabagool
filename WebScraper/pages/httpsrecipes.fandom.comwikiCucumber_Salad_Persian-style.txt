Description : 
 A cool salad with a difference on a hot summers night. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 peeled  cucumbers 
 2½ cups plain  yogurt 
 3  spring onions , finely chopped 
 ¾ cup  raisins 
 ½ cup  walnuts 
 salt  and  pepper 
 1 teaspoon dried  mint 
 Directions : 
 Halve and deseed  cucumbers . 
 Dice  cucumbers  and sprinkle with  salt . 
 Leave to drain 20 – 30 minutes. 
 Rinse  cucumbers , dry and place in a bowl. 
 Mix in  yoghurt  then remaining ingredients. 
 Cover and chill 1 – 2 hours.
