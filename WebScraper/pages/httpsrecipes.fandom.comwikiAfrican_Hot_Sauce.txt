 Ingredients : 
 1 dozen  chile peppers  (i.e., hot  red peppers !) 
 1 small  sweet green pepper  (or  bell pepper ) 
 1 clove  garlic 
 1 medium  onion 
 2 cans  tomato paste 
 4 tablespoons  vinegar 
 1 teaspoon  sugar 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Remove stems and seeds from  peppers  â€” careful! Wear kitchen gloves! Do not touch your eyes or face while handling  hot peppers ! Wash your hands with soap and  water  after you have handled  hot peppers 
 Grind  hot peppers ,  green pepper ,  garlic , and  onion  (or use food processor). 
 Combine all ingredients in sauce pan. 
 Simmer for an hour or two. 
 Add  cayenne pepper  if desired. 
 Put in jars and refrigerate. 
 Serve with everything.
