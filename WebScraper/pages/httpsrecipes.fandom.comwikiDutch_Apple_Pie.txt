Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  flour 
 ½ cup  quick cooking oats 
 ½ tsp  cinnamon 
 5 cups sliced baking  apples 
 sweetener equal to ½ cup  sugar 
 ½ cup unsweetened frozen  apple juice  conconcentrate 
 1 pie crust for a 1 crust pie 
 Topping : 
 ¼ cup  flour 
 ½ cup quick cooking  oats 
 sweetener equal to ½ cup brown sugar 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 1 pack sugar-free  instant vanilla pudding 
 ½ cup  butter  or  margarine 
 Directions : 
 Mix  flour ,  oats ,  cinnamon , and sweetener with a fork. 
 Pour over sliced  apples  and mix well. 
 Pour  apple juice  over top of  apples  and mix well again. 
 Put  apple  mixture in pie crust; set aside. 
 For topping: mix dry ingredients with fork. 
 Add  butter  and cut into dry ingredients with a pastry blender or 2 knives until it resembles coarse crumbs, pour mixture over pie filling and press down evenly over  apples . 
 Bake at 400°F for 45 – 55 minutes. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Exchanges: 2 bread, 3 fat, and 1 fruit exchange. 
 
 251 calories, 12 grams of fat, 283 mg. sodium, 5 mg. cholesterol per serving
