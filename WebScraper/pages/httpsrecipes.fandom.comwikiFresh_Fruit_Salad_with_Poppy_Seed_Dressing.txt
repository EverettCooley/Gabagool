Ingredients : 
 Dressing 
 3/4 c.  sugar 
 1 1/2 tsp.  onion salt 
 1 tsp.  dry mustard 
 1/3 c.  vinegar 
 1 c.  Crisco oil 
 1 tbsp.  poppy seed 
 Salad 
 8 c. cut-up mixed fresh fruit (Oranges, apples, bananas, pears, peaches, strawberries, 
 grapes)
 
 Bibb lettuce 
 Directions : 
 Dressing 
 In a small bowl, combine sugar, salt, and dry mustard. 
 Stir in vinegar. 
 Beat at medium speed while gradually adding Crisco oil. 
 Beat 5 to 10 minutes longer, until thickened. 
 Add poppy seeds. 
 Pour into a screw-top jar. 
 Cover tightly and shake vigorously to blend well. 
 Salad 
 Line a large salad bowl with Bibb lettuce leaves. 
 Fill salad bowl with fruit. 
 Drizzle with poppy seed dressing 
 Notes : 
 6 to 8 servings. 
 Store dressing covered in refrigerator. Shake well before using. 
 Dressing also goes well on any fresh fruit, salads, grapefruit sections, or on lettuce wedges. 
 Makes about 1-2/3 cups dressing. 
 Enjoy.
