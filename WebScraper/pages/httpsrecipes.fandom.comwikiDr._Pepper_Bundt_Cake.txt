Ingredients : 
 1 (18 ounce) box  yellow cake mix 
 1 (3 ounce) box  instant vanilla pudding mix 
 1 (12 ounce) can warm Dr. Pepper cola (save about three tablespoons for the icing) 
 4  eggs 
 3/4 cup  oil  or  butter 
 chopped  pecans  (optional) 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix well, 
 Pour into a bundt cake pan, 
 bake at 350 for 50 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean. 
 While cake is warm glaze with: 
 1 cup of powdered sugar. 
 1/4 teaspoon vanilla and remaining Dr. Pepper.
