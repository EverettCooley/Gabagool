 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup  milk 
 1 cup  coconut milk 
 1/2 tsp grated  nutmeg 
 2  eggs 
 3 tablespoons  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 250 degrees F. Beat the  eggs  and stir in  sugar  and liquids, then  nutmeg . 
 Put into a greased pie dish and stand in a shallow pan with  water . 
 Put pan with dish into the oven. 
 Bake till custard is set (about 45 minutes).
