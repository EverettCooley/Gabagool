 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/3 cup  whole wheat flour 
 1/3 cup  nutritional yeast  flakes 
 1/3 cup  oil 
 1 1/2 cups  water 
 2 tbsp soy sauce 
 ground  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Toast the  flour  in a saucepan until slightly browned and aromatic. Stir in the  nutritional yeast  and  oil  to make a thick paste. Cook on medium heat until it starts to bubble and sizzle, then gradually whisk in the  water  and soy sauce. Cook on medium heat until it reaches a gravy-like consistency. Serve over the
 Lentil  loaf, or any other dish you like.
