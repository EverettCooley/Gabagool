Ingredients : 
 1 cup  water 
 1 tbsp reduced calorie  margarine 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 3 cup chopped  cabbage 
 ⅓ cup  skim  or  1% milk 
 1 cup instant mashed  potato flakes 
 1  green onion , finely chopped 
 Directions : 
 In a medium saucepan, combine  water ,  margarine , and  salt . 
 Bring to boil. 
 Stir in  cabbage ; return to a boil. 
 Reduce heat, cover and simmer 4 – 6 minutes, or until  cabbage  is tender. 
 Remove from heat; stir in  milk  and  potato  flakes with fork. 
 Stir in  onion . 
 Cover and let stand 3 minutes.
