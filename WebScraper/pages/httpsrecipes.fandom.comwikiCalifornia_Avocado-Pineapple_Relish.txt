 Description : 
 pineapple  provides the perfect counterpoint to the lush texture and subtle, nutty flavor of  California avocado  in this versatile relish. Serve it with grilled meat, poultry or layer it on a  Turkey ,  Ham , or  Cheese  sandwich.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ Cup diced  pineapple , fresh or canned 
 ¼ Cup finely chopped  Onion 
 1 ½ Tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 Tbsp chopped, fresh  mint 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 Large pinch  crushed red pepper  flakes 
 1  California avocado  (8 ounces), diced 
 Directions : 
 Combine  pineapple  with next 5 ingredients.
 Gently fold in  avocado .
 Let chill 1 hour to marry flavors.
 Best served the day it's made.
 Serve with grilled meat, poultry or layer it on a  Turkey ,  Ham , or  Cheese  sandwich.
