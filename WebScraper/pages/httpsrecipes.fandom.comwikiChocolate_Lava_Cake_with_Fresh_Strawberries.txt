Description : 
 Chocolate melt lava cake is one of the worlds most sought after desserts. The chocolaty and gooey treat is loved by many and lots of restaurants and resort establishments have their own recipes for you to indulge in. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 50g unsalted  butter 
 2  eggs 
 500g  flour 
 10g  dark chocolate 
 20g  sugar 
 5g  baking powder 
 20g  milk 
 10g  whipping cream Â  
 Method of Preparation : 
 Add in the butter and sugar together and beat until creamy. 
 Add the eggs and mix well 
 Mix the flour, soda and baking powder. 
 Lastly, pour in the milk and cream, and blend. 
 Brush some butter into the cup mould, pipe in the chocolate mixture and bake for 13-15 minutes at 200 degrees Celsius. 
 Once cooked remove the mould and serve on a deep plate with vanilla ice cream and fresh strawberries. 
 Source : 
 Recipe from  Berjaya Waterfront Hotel
