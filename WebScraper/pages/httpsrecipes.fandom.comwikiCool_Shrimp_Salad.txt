 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ Cup Fresh  avocado  (Diced) 
 4 Spring  green onions  (Chopped) 
 ½ Cup Fresh Green  celery  Sliced 
 ½ head  iceberg lettuce 
 ¼ head of  iceberg lettuce 
 ½ Cup Fresh Bay  Shrimp 
 ½ Cup Ranch Dressing 
 1/8 tsp  garlic powder 
 salt  &  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Prepare Vegetables as stated above. Toss.
 Add dressing and avoacdo and toss. 
 Add Bay  Shrimp  and seasoning toss and chill for 10 to 15 minutes before serving. 
 Serve with warm bread as a meal or as a wonderful side dish.
 Enjoy! ! !
