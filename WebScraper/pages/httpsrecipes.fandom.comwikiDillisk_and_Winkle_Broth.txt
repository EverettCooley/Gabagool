Prep Time:  5 minutes
 Cook time:  20 minutes
 Serves:  Serves 4
 
 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 500 g  winkles 
 14 g  dillisk 
 1 clove  garlic 
 125 ml dry  white wine 
 2  spring onions 
 12 g  carrageen  moss 
 2  bay leaves 
 800 ml  fish stock 
 14 g flat leaf  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Cook well-washed winkles in garlic and white wine, save liquid, cool winkles under cold water and shell. 
 Soak carrageen moss and bring to boil with fish stock and simmer for 15-20 minutes. 
 Saut√© chopped spring onions and dillisk in a little olive oil for 4-5 minutes, add winkles, bay leaves and strained stock and wine liquid. 
 Simmer for 15-20 minutes, add parsley and serve.
