Description : 
 This classic Philippine dessert starts with authentic rice, chocolate, milk, and sugar, and has a fresh, high hint of vanilla.
 
       Champorado     
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup glutinous  rice 
 2 ½ cups  water 
 ½ cup unsweetened  cocoa 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 ⅛ tsp.  vanilla extract 
 sweetened condensed milk or evaporated milk , for topping 
 Directions : 
 Wash the rice and drain. 
 Cook rice in a moderate-sized saucepan with water, stirring constantly.. 
 When rice is ready, add the cocoa, sugar and vanilla 
 Serve in bowls with the fresh swirls of sweet condensed milk on top.
