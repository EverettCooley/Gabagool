Ingredients : 
 lettuce  or other greens 
 sliced  hard-boiled eggs 
 sliced  onion  ( red onions  are best) 
 carrots , grated or chopped 
 cold  sweet corn , drained 
 chopped fresh  parsley 
 cucumber , peeled and seeded 
 chopped  green pepper 
 radishes 
 chopped  sardines  or  anchovies 
 slices of  ham  or  chicken 
 avocados 
 cheese  ( roquefort  or bleu recommended) etc, etc. 
 Directions : 
 Wash  lettuce  or other greens well. 
 Tear or cut  lettuce  into manageable pieces. 
 Place in a bowl and add ingredients to your liking. 
 Dress with a vinaigrette or other  salad dressing . 
 Mix and Serve.
