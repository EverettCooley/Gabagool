Description : 
       Arepas     
 10 Servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  corn flour  for arepas 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ½ cup grated  white cheese 
 2 cup cool  water  (or more if needed) 
 Directions : 
 In a large mixing bowl,  whisk  together the  flour ,  salt , and  white cheese . 
 stir  in  water  to make a firm dough. 
 Let it rest for 5 minutes. 
 Divide the  dough  into 10 pieces and form each piece into a ball. 
 Flatten the ball slightly. 
 Preheat the oven to 350 °F. 
 Oil a griddle very lightly and warm it over medium heat. 
 Cook the arepas on the griddle for about 5 minutes on each side. 
 Transfer the arepas to a baking sheet and bake 20 to 25 minutes.
