 Ingredients : 
 1 lbs new  red potatoes 
 1/4 cup  water 
 1/2 tbsp  salt 
 1 small  red onion , thinly sliced 
 1/3 cup sliced  ripe olives 
 1/2 cup chopped  green pepper 
 1  tomato , chopped 
 1/2 cup Italian dressing 
 2 tbsp snipped fresh  parsley 
 1 tbsp  Parmesan cheese 
 Directions : 
 Scrub  potatoes ; cut crosswise into ¼ in. slices. Place in 1 ½ qt. microwave safe casserole. Add  water  and  salt . Cover with lid. Microwave on high for 9–10 minutes. or until  potatoes  are tender; stirring once. Drain and cool. Add  onions ,  green pepper ,  tomato , dressing and  parsley . Mix lightly to coat evenly. Refrigerate until chilled. Stir before serving and sprinkle with  Cheese
