 Ingredients : 
 ½ lb of  macaroni  (or similar  short pasta ) 
 ½ lb or  carrots 
 1 cup of  sweet corn 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 ½ cup  peas 
 1 medium  red onion 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Boil the  pasta  until tender (it needs to be softer than al dente). 
 Separately boil  carrots  and  eggs . 
 Peel the  carrots  and chop into small cubes. 
 Chop the  eggs  into small cubes. 
 Mix in the rest of the ingredients, and adjust  salt  to taste.
