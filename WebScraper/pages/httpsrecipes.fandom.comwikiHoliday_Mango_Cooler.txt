Ingredients : 
 1 cup  pineapple  chunks 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 3 cups  low-fat milk 
 1 cup  mango  chunks 
 Directions : 
 Place all ingredients in blender. 
 Blend, pour over  ice  or blend with crushed  ice  and enjoy. 
 A healthy refresher anytime.
