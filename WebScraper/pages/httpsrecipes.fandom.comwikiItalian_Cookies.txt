Ingredients : 
 2 c.  sugar 
 2 sticks melted  butter 
 15 oz.  ricotta cheese 
 3  eggs 
 1 tsp.  vanilla extract 
 4 c.  all-purpose flour 
 1 tsp.  salt 
 1 tsp.  baking soda 
 1 cup  confectioners' sugar 
 2 T.  milk 
 Directions : 
 Mix sugar, butter, ricotta, eggs and vanilla until combined 
 In a separate bowl, combine flour, salt and baking soda 
 Gradually add flour mixture to ricotta mixture until combined 
 Flour hands, and roll dough into meatball sized balls (the dough will be very moist). 
 Place dough balls 2 in ches apart on ungreased baking sheet 
 Bake at 350 degrees for 10-15 minutes, or until edges are slightly brown (tops will not be brown). 
 While baking, combine confectioner's sugar with milk to make icing 
 Ice warm cookies and top with sprinkles 
 =Notes : 
 Store in an Air-Tight Container When Cool.
