Ingredients : 
 10 oz. cream of Chicken or Mushroom soup, condensed 
 2 c. skim milk 
 6 oz.  Tuna , canned, drained 
 1/4 c.  green pepper , chopped 
 1/4 c. red pepper, chopped 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients in saucepan. 
 Cook over medium heat. Do not allow to boil. 
 Serve over biscuits (cut in halves) or toast points.
