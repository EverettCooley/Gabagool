Description 
 Dessert Recipes  by the South Carolina Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Ingredients 
 1 (1 lb. 2.25-ounce) yellow cake mix with pudding 
 1 (1.4-ounce) box sugar free chocolate instant pudding 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 ¾ cup  vegetable oil 
 ¾ cup  water 
 4 large  eggs 
 1 (8-ounce) carton  sour cream 
 1 (6-ounce) pkg.  semi-sweet chocolate morsels 
 Directions 
 Stir cake mix, pudding, and sugar together. 
 Beat in oil, then water, and then eggs, one at a time. 
 Stir in sour cream. 
 Toss chocolate morsels with a little all-purpose flour. 
 Stir in chocolate morsels. 
 Pour into a prepared tube pan. 
 Bake at 350 °F for 60 minutes.
