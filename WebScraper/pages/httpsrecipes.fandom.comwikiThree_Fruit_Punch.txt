Ingredients 
 2 cups  grapefruit juice 
 1 1/2 cups  orange juice 
 1 1/2 cups  pineapple juice 
 1 cup crushed  pineapple 
 1 cup  sugar 
 2 cups  water 
 Directions 
 1.Mix ingredients and serve over cracked ice. 
 2.Garnish with small fancy sections of all fruits.
