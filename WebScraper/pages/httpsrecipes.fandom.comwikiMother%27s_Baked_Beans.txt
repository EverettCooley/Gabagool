Ingredients : 
 6  Bacon  Slices 
 1 Medium  Onion , Chopped 
 1 Green  bell pepper , Chopped 
 1 Can  Pork  And beans (16 Oz) 
 5 T.  Catsup 
 Directions : 
 Preheat Oven to 400. 
 Fry  Bacon  in Large Skillet Over Medium High Heat Until Crisp, About 7 Minutes. Drain  Bacon  on Paper Towels. 
 Transfer Drippings to Medium Ovenproof Pan. 
 Add  Onion  And  bell pepper  to Drippings And Saut Over Medium Heat Until Soft, About 7 Minutes. 
 Stir in  Pork  And beans; 
 Crumble  Bacon  And Add to beans. 
 Pour  Catsup  Over. Srpead Over Top to Edges. 
 Cover And Bake 30 Minutes. 
 Stir  Catsup  Into beans. 
 Note 
 
 Can be Made 1 Day Ahead; Cover beans And Refrigerate. 
 Serves 4
