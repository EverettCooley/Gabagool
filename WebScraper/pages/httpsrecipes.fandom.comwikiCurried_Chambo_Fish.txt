Ingredients : 
 fish  fillets 
 lemon juice 
 flour 
 1 medium sized  onion  chopped 
 1 tbsp  curry powder 
 1 cup  water 
 1 thinly sliced  carrot  (optional) 
 1 thinly sliced  green pepper  (optional) 
 a small handful  raisins  or  sultanas  (optional) 
 salt  to taste 
 1 tbsp fruit  chutney 
 Directions : 
 Clean,  salt , and sprinkle fillets with a bit of  lemon juice . 
 Roll in  flour  and fry 4 minutes each side in about 2 tbsp  oil . 
 Take fillets out. 
 Put in 1 chopped med sized  onion  and fry till glossy (5 minutes). 
 Mix in 1 tbsp  curry  powder and fry for 2 minutes. 
 Sprinkle about 1 tbs  curry  powder over and add 1 cup  water . 
 Turn heat to low. 
 Add a thinly sliced  carrot , a thinly sliced  green pepper , a small handful raisins or  sultanas  and  salt . 
 Simmer until vegetables are soft 10 minutes. 
 Add 1 tbsp of fruit chutney. 
 Let cool. 
 Put in a serving dish with a lid. 
 Place fillets in and make sure they are covered by gravy. 
 Close lid, and let flavors penetrate. 
 Can be served hot or cold with  rice  or bread. 
 The same sauce can be served over  hard-boiled eggs  or toast.
