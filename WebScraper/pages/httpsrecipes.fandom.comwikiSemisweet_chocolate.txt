 About Semi-sweet chocolate : 
 Wikipedia Article  About Semi-sweet chocolate on Wikipedia 
 Semisweet chocolate is often used for cooking purposes. It is a dark chocolate with high sugar content. An eating or baking chocolate, it is just usually slightly sweeter than bittersweet chocolate. Bittersweet chocolate may be substituted. Store chocolate, well-wrapped in a cool, dry place, for up to 4 months.
