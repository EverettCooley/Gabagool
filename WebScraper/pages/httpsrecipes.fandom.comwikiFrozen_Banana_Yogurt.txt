Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Turner Estate in Dallas, Texas in 1997.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups plain  yogurt 
 1 large  banana 
 Â¼ cup  honey 
 Directions : 
 Puree yogurt and fruit in blender adding honey once the fruit is well blended. 
 Pour mixture into a shallow container and freeze for 30 minutes. 
 Spoon into blender and whip for 1 minute. 
 Pour mixture into paper cups them freeze until firm.
