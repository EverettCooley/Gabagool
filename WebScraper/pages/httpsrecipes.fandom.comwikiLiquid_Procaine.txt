Description : 
 One of multiple variations of  Liquid Cocaine .
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ oz  vodka 
 ½ oz  amaretto 
 ½ oz  Southern Comfort ® 
 ½ oz  Grand Marnier ® 
 ½ oz Pusser’s Painkiller #4 ® 
 ½ oz Pat O'Brien's  Sweet and Sour Mix ® 
 1 tsp 7-Up® 
 Directions : 
 Pour vodka, amaretto, Southern Comfort and Grand Marnier into old fashioned glass with ice. 
 Add Painkiller and sweet and sour mix. 
 Add 7-Up, stir well, and serve.
