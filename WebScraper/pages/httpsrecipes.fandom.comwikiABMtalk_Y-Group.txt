This is a bread list, primarily for bread machine recipes and information, and also for recipes using bread (like bread pudding etc.). Recipes for scones, coffee cakes, muffins, etc. also are welcome. From time to time, there is a challenge ABM recipe that all are invited to take part in. If interested in joining, please send a blank email to ABMTalk-subscribe@yahoogroups.com
