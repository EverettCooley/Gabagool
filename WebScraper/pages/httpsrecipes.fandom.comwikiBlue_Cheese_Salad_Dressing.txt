 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup Sour  cream 
 1/2 cup  milk 
 1 cup Kraft  mayo 
 4 ounces Blue  Cheese , crumbled 
 1/8 teaspoon  Onion  powder 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Use electric mixer to combine all ingredients until smooth. 
 Tightly cover and refrigerate. 
 Use within 10 days
