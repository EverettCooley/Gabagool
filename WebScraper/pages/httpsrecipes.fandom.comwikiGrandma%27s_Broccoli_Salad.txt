Description : 
 Contributed by Carolyn Cinnamon
 
 Ingredients : 
 Salad : 
 1 pound of  bacon  (cooked crisp and crumbled) 
 2 cups  green  and  red grapes 
 6 ounces of sliced  almonds 
 1 bunch  broccoli  cut fine 
 1 cup  raisins 
 ½ cup  green onion  chopped 
 Dressing : 
 1 cup  Miracle Whip 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 1 Tablespoon  vinegar 
 Directions : 
 Mix salad ingredients together 
 Mix dressing ingredients together, and pour over salad, serve chilled.
