 Description : 
 Meatballs in Tomato Sauce
 
 You Will Need: : 
 1 small  onion  cut into thin slices 
 2  garlic  cloves, crushed 
 1 small  onion , grated 
 6  tomatoes , cut into quarters 
 oregano 
 ground  black pepper 
 1 small  pepper  sliced a la julienne 
 2 tablespoon of  oil 
 1  bell pepper  diced 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Mix meat, bread crumbs (or galleta de panader√≠a),  egg , grated  Onion ,  garlic ,  salt ,  oregano , and  black pepper . Mix well. Put two tablespoons of the mixture in the palm of your hands and forming a ball. Use all the mixture to make balls. 
 In a heavy sauce pan heat the  oil  and pan-fry the meat balls at medium temperature turning the balls until they are well cooked and golden brown all over. Add the sliced  Onion  to the pan and sautee. 
 Add the remaining ingredients and add 3 tablespoons of waters, reduce temperature and cover. 
 Stir frequently for 10 minutes, by then all the vegetables would be tender and you should obtain a thick sauce.
