Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Stewart Estate in Quinlan, Texas in 1994. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Makes 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 x 10-ounce packages frozen chopped  spinach , thawed and drained 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 4  eggs , beaten 
 1 x 10¾-ounce can condensed  cream of mushroom  soup 
 dash ground  nutmeg 
 1 teaspoon  onion powder 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 ¼ cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 Directions : 
 Thaw, drain and separate spinach then mix with  rice  in large mixing bowl. 
 Combine  eggs , soup,  onion powder ,  salt  and  pepper . Stir into  spinach  mixture. 
 Turn into buttered shallow 2½-quart baking dish. 
 Sprinkle top with  Parmesan cheese . 
 Bake at 350°F for 30 minutes or until firm. 
 Serve Immediately.
