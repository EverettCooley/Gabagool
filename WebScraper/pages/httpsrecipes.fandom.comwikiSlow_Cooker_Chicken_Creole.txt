 Some of the reviews for this said they used the left over sauce and added to it to make soup. Take Care, Gloria
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes For Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Submitted By: Mary Moon to Allrecipes.Com 
 Total time: 12 Hours 10 Minutes | Hands-on time: 10 Minutes | Cooking time: 12 Hours 
 Serves: 4 
 Ingredients : 
 4 skinless, boneless  chicken breast  halves 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Creole-style seasoning  to taste 
 1 (14.5 oz) can stewed  tomatoes , with liquid 
 1 stalk  celery , diced 
 1  green bell pepper , diced 
 3 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1  onion , diced 
 1 (4 oz) can  mushrooms , drained 
 1 fresh  jalapeno pepper , seeded and chopped 
 Directions : 
 Place  chicken  breasts in slow cooker. 
 Season with  salt , pepper , and Creole-style seasoning to taste. 
 Stir in tomatoes with liquid,  celery ,  bell pepper ,  garlic ,  onion ,  mushrooms , and  jalapeno pepper . 
 Cook on low for 10 to 12 hours, or on high for 5 to 6 hours.
