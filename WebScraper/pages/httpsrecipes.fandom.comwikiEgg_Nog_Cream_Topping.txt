Description : 
 Makes about 3½ cups.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cup Borden  Egg Nog 
 1 pack instant  vanilla  pudding mix (4 serving size) 
 ½ pint  whipping cream 
 Directions : 
 In large bowl,combine 1½ cups canned Borden  Egg Nog , chilled and one 4 serving size package instant  vanilla  flavor pudding mix; blend well. 
 Fold in ½ pint  whipping cream , whipped. Chill. 
 Use as topping for pie,cake or fruit.
