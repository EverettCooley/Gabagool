 Ingredients : 
 4 pounds bone-in  Pork  roast 
 3/4 cup soy sauce 
 1/2 cup dry  sherry 
 1/3 cup honey 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1/2 teaspoon  ground ginger 
 1 tablespoon  cornstarch 
 1 tablespoon  water 
 Directions : 
 1 - Marinate  Pork  in soy sauce, honey,  sherry ,  garlic , and  ginger  for approx 8 hours.
 2 - Cook  Pork  for 1 hour at 375 degrees while marinating, wrap in foil and cook for another 1.5 hours
