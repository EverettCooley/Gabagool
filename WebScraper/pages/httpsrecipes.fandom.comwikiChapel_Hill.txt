Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz  bourbon 
 ½ oz  triple sec 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Shake with  ice  and strain into cocktail glass. 
 Add twist of  orange peel .
