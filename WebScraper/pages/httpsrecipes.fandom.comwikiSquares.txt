 Ingredients : 
 2 cups graham cracker crumbs 
 1 1/3 cups ready-to-use  mincemeat  (I use the kind in a jar, or you can use your own home-made) 
 1 (15 ounce) can  sweetened condensed milk 
 Directions : 
 1. Blend crumbs,  mincemeat  and  condensed milk  together.
 2. Turn into a well-greased 9 x 13-inch pan.
 3. Bake at 350 degrees for about 30 minutes or until lightly browned.
 4. Let cool in pan before cutting.
