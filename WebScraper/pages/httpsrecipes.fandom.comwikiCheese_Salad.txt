Description : 
 A very tasty salad consisting of good  Cheese  of choice. No cooking skills required, just a great taste and a nice day ! Enjoy !
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 ½ lb  cheese , grated 
 celery salt 
 pepper  to taste 
 12  capers 
 lettuce 
 Directions : 
 Mix  mayonnaise  and  cheese  with seasonings. 
 Form into balls with  butter  paddles or 2 spoons. 
 Serve on  lettuce  leaves on individual plates, garnish with  capers . 
 Pass more  mayonnaise  in a separate bowl.
