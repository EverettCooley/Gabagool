Description : 
 In Romanian: Sarlota de cafea
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs / 1 kg  whipped cream 
 11 tablespoons  gelatine 
 4 cups cold black  coffee 
 sugar  to taste 
 1 cup  milk 
 sour cherry  preserves (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Set the black  coffee  and  milk  to boil. 
 Add the  gelatine  and mix well until dissolved. 
 Strain through a dense sieve and let cool. 
 When only tepid, pour into the  whipped cream  with  confectioner's sugar  to taste. 
 Mix well. Pour in cups or glasses and refrigerate. 
 When serving, you can garnish with  whipped cream  or  sour cherry  preserves.
