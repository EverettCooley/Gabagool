 Description : 
 This is an old dish
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 black  radishes 
 2 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 2 tbsp  honey 
 Directions : 
 Peel the  black radish  and soak in cold  water  for 15 to 20 minutes. 
 Dice the black radish and fry in a hot skillet with  vegetable oil , gradually adding the honey.
