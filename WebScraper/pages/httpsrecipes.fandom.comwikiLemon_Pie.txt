Ingredients : 
 1  lemon 
 vanilla extract 
 condensed milk 
 egg yolk 
 real  lemon juice 
 crushed  vanilla wafer  cookies (for crust) 
 Directions : 
 Combine ingredients and pour into crust. 
 To make topping; use  egg whites  and  cream of tartar  and ½ a teaspoon of  sugar  blended on high speed to make meringue or topping. 
 Bake at 350°F.
