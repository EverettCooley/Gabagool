Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  butter 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 1 tbsp extract,  vanilla 
 1 cup  flour 
 ¼ cup  nuts , ground 
 sugar , powdered 
 Directions : 
 Cream together  butter ,  Sugar ,  salt , and  vanilla , then add  flour  and  ground nuts . 
 Chill overnight. 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Form dough by teaspoons into half moon shapes. 
 Bake for 12 – 15 minutes; roll in  powdered sugar  before cooling.
