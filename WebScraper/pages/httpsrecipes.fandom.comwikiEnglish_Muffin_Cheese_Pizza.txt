Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Recipe makes one serving 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ of an  English muffin 
 2 tablespoons low-sodium  tomato sauce 
 ⅛ teaspoon crushed dried  basil 
 ⅛ teaspoon crushed dried  oregano 
 2 tablespoons shredded part-skim  mozzarella  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Preheat broiler. 
 Place English muffin on a small baking sheet. 
 Spread tomato sauce over top of muffin. 
 Sprinkle with basil and oregano. 
 Top with cheese. 
 Broil until cheese is brown and sauce is bubbly. 
 Eat at once. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 Calories: 115 | Calories from fat: 23-percent | Protein: 7g | Total Fat: 3g | Saturated Fat: 1.6g | Carbohydrate: 15g | Dietary Fiber: 1g | Cholesterol: 8mg | Sodium: 212mg 
 Exchanges: 1/2 medium-fat meat, 1 carbohydrate (1 bread/starch)
