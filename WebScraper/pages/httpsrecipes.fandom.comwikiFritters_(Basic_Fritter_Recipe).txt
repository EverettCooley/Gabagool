 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  flour 
 salt  to taste 
 2 tsp.  baking powder 
 2 tbs,  Sugar 
 3/4 tsp  cinnamon 
 1  egg 
 1/2 cup  milk 
 1 tbs melted  butter 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Sift dry ingredients together. 
 Whip  egg . 
 Add  milk  and melted  butter  to it. 
 Add liquids to dry ingredients. Beat batter vigorously. 
 Drop by tablespoons into hot fat. Shallow fry until golden brown. 
 Turn over once. Place on kitchen paper to drain.
 
 Sprinkle with  granulated sugar  .
