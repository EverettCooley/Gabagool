Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: my Old Recipes 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pkg. Kraft Macaroni and Cheese (14.5 oz) 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 2 tbsp  margarine 
 1½ cup  milk 
 2 cans condensed  cream of mushroom  soup 
 2 to 3 cups cooked and cup up  chicken 
 Directions : 
 Make dinner as pkg. directs except using 1½ cups  milk  and the ⅓ cup  margarine  called for in the directions. 
 Sauté  onion  in the 2 tbsp  margarine  until tender. 
 Add to prepared dinner along with remaining ingredients (soup and  chicken ). 
 Mix well and spoon into a 3 quart casserole. 
 Bake at 350°F for 35 to 40 minutes until bubbly. 
 Delicious!
