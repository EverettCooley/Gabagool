Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 8 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 4 to 5 cooked  chicken breast  halves, cut up 
 3 stalks  celery , chopped 
 1 large  onion , chopped 
 1 cup  instant rice 
 1 can  cream of celery  soup 
 1 cup  chicken broth 
 1 tbsp  salt 
 ½ tbsp  pepper 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 1 cup  potato chip  crumbs 
 2 cups  cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients except  potato chips  and  cheese . 
 Place in a greased casserole dish and top with chips and  cheese . 
 Bake in a preheated 350°F oven for 40 to 45 minutes.
