Description : 
 Recipe reprinted with permission from Zespri kiwifruit
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 Zespri Gold ( kiwis ) 
 2  oranges 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 1 small  papaya 
 100 grams seedless black  grapes 
 200 ml ready-made  custard 
 4 tbsp  praline 
 Directions : 
 Halve and squeeze the  oranges , warm the juice and melt the  sugar  in it, then while stirring allow simmering for a few minutes. 
 Peel and halve the  papaya , removing the stone, then cut into small cubes. 
 Wash, halve and finely cube the  grapes . 
 Allow everything to steep in the  orange  liquor. 
 Peel and slice the Zespri Gold. 
 Serve the custard onto plates, drain the marinated fruit, then arrange alternately on skewers with the sliced Zespri Gold, and place on the custard. 
 Serve dusted over with the praline and as desired with lemon- balm  garnish.
