Ingredients : 
 1 ripe  avocado , seeded and diced 
 ½ cup crumbled  feta  cheese 
 2 tsp fresh  lime juice 
 freshly ground  black pepper  to taste 
 ½ cup sliced  scallions 
 2 tbsp soy “ bacon ” bits 
 ¼ cup  sunflower seeds , preferably toasted 
 2 tbsp fat-free  soy mayonnaise 
 16  grape tomatoes 
 1 cup shredded  leaf lettuce  plus half head for garnish 
 4 x 8-inch low-fat plain or flavored flour  tortillas 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to broil. 
 Put diced  avocado  into a mixing bowl, and stir in  feta   cheese ,  lime juice  and  black pepper . 
 Add scallions, “ bacon ” bits,  sunflower seeds  and  soy mayonnaise , stirring gently to combine. 
 Stir in  grape tomatoes  and shredded  lettuce . 
 Spray tortillas lightly with nonstick  cooking spray , and heat in oven until softened, for about 30 seconds. 
 Remove from oven, and place each tortilla on individual plates. 
 To serve, line each tortilla with whole  lettuce  leaf, and mound salad mixture on top.
