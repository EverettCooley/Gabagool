It is called Kontommire aka spinach stew
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  palm oil 
 1 cup  onions , coarsely chopped 
 1/2 tsp  cayenne pepper 
 1 cup  tomatoes 
 1 cup kippered  Herring 
 4 cups fresh  spinach , chopped 
 salt and spice 
 3 eggs 
 2 tablespoons of agushi 
 Any protein of choice: fish, meat 
 Directions : 
 1) Heat the  oil  in a large skillet or heavy pot, then saute  onions  and  pepper  together.
 2) Mash or grind the  tomatoes  and stir them in, along with the remaining ingredients. Cook at a moderate temperature for 15 minutes, or until fish is tender and flaky.
 3) If there is too much  oil  on the surface for your liking, skim some off with a spoon. Serve kentumere with cooked plaintain or  rice .
