Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz jubilee®  peach schnapps 
 1½ oz  sweet and sour mix 
 ½ oz  triple sec 
 1 oz  rum 
 Directions : 
 Combine in blender with  ice  until smooth. 
 Serve in a large cocktail glass. 
 Optional: add ¼ canned  peach  with juice.
