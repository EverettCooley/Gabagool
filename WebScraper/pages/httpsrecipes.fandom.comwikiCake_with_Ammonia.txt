 Description : 
 In Romanian: Prajitura cu amoniac
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 teaspoon  ammonia powder 
 7  yolks 
 ¾ cup  confectioner's sugar 
 ½ cup melted  butter 
 flour  as needed 
 a few sliced  walnuts  or  almonds 
 Directions : 
 Cream the  butter  with the  sugar , adding the  yolks  one by one. 
 Add the ground ammonia and mix until the mixture lightens in color. 
 Add  flour  and knead rapidly to obtain a soft dough. 
 Roll a finger thick sheet and lay on the baking sheet. Wash with a little  egg . 
 Sprinkle sliced  walnuts  or  almonds  on top. Bake, first at low temperature, then increase it. 
 After it is completely cold, cut into desired shapes.
