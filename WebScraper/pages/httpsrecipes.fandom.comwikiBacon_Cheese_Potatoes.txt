Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 6/17/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4 large baking  potatoes  peeled and sliced 
 1  onion  chopped 
 ½ cup  celery 
 ½ cup  green pepper 
 6 slices  bacon  cooked and crumbled 
 ⅔ cup  butter   or   margarine 
 1 cup shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Place potato slices on large piece of aluminum foil. 
 Cover with onion, celery and green pepper. 
 Season with salt, pepper and butter. 
 Sprinkle with bacon and cover with cheese. 
 Seal tightly. 
 Bake at 350°F for 1 hour.
