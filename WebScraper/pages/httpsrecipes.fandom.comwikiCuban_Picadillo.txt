Ingredients : 
 1 lb  ground meat 
 1 large  onion , chopped 
 2 – 3  garlic  cloves, chopped 
 1 small can  tomato sauce 
 ¼ cup dry  white wine 
 pimento -stuffed  olives 
 raisins 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 In a large skillet, brown the  ground meat ,  onions  and  garlic , pouring out any excess fat. 
 Reduce the heat to medium low and add the  tomato sauce  and white wine. 
 While that simmers, chop up the  olives  and add them to the  meat  mixture. 
 Add the  raisins  and adjust the seasonings. 
 Serve over fluffy,  white rice .
