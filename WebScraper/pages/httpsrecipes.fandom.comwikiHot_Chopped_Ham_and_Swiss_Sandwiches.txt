Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Howell Estate in Canton, Texas in 1981.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound thinly sliced cooked  ham 
 8 ounces  Swiss cheese  cut into small cubes 
 ½ cup  butter  softened 
 ¼ cup finely chopped white  onion 
 2 tablespoons  poppy seeds 
 2 teaspoons  prepared mustard 
 10 sandwich buns 
 Directions : 
 Cut  ham  into ½ inch squares. 
 Combine  ham  and all remaining ingredients except buns in large bowl then spoon into buns. 
 Wrap each in foil. 
 Preheat oven to 325°F then bake sandwiches for 25 minutes. 
 Serve hot.
