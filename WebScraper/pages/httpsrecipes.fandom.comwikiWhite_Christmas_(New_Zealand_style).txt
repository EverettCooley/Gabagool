      White Christmas     
 Description : 
 For those non-NZers, Kremelta is the equivalent of the Australian "Copha" which is a white  coconut  based fat. Memories of primary school cooking classes...
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup desiccated  coconut 
 1 cup  rice bubbles 
 ⅓ cup chopped dried apricots 
 ⅓ cup ginger 
 ⅓ cup  raisins 
 1 cup  icing sugar 
 1 cup whole  powdered milk  (or half each with malted milk powder) 
 6 - 7 ounces  Kremelta 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 Directions : 
 Mix dry ingredients in a bowl. 
 Melt Kremelta and pour over dry mix; mix well. 
 Press into a flat, lightly greased, baking tin and cut before too hard. 
 Refrigerate.
