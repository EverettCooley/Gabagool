Cola  Chicken 
 
 4 to 6  Chicken  Breasts ( salt  & Peppered) 
 1/2 Cup Diced  Onion 
 1/2 Cup Diced  bell pepper 
 3/4 Cup  tomato   ketchup 
 1/3 to 1/2 Cup Coke, Diet Coke or Pepsi (i Used Coke) 
 
Spray Crockpot With  cooking spray . Arrange
 Chicken  on Bottom of Crockpot. Mix All Other IngredientsTogether, & Pour on Top of  Chicken . Cook on Low 4 to 5 Hours.
 
Enjoy.
