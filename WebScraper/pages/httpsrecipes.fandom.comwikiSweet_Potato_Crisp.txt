Info 
 Cook Time : 60 minutes 
 Serves : 
 
 Ingredients 
 Base 
 7 cups cooked, mashed  sweet potatoes 
 1 cup  sugar 
 3/4 cup  butter 
 2  eggs , well beaten 
 1/3 cup  milk 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 Topping 
 3/4 butter, melted 
 1 cup light  brown sugar 
 1/2 cup  flour 
 1 cup  pecans , chopped 
 Directions 
 Blend base until creamy.
Mix topping in a bowl.
Pour the sweet potato a large casserole dish.
Sprinkle on topping and bake at 350 degrees until top crisps and hardens.
 
 Tips 
 Other nuts can be used in the topping,  almonds  or  peanuts  serve admirably.
