 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 avocados 
 1 small cup of  concentrated milk  (Peak can or Nestle tube) 
 2 ice cubes 
 For topping
 
 2 spoonfuls of honey 
 2 spoonfuls of roasted  cashew   nuts 
 2 swirls of  whipped cream 
 Directions : 
 Mix the avocados, the  ice  and the  milk  in a blender. 
 Choose nice  ice cream  cups and pour the  avocado  mixture in them. 
 Sprinkle with cashews, honey and finish with  whipped cream .
