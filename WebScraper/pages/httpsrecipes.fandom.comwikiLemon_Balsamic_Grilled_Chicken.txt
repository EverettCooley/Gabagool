 From  glutenfree.com 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb boneless  chicken  breast 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 1  lemon , sliced thin 
 2 teaspoons  balsamic vinegar 
 2 tablespoons  Italian parsley , chopped 
 salt  and  pepper 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Preheat gas grill. 
 Drizzle  chicken  with  olive oil  and season generously with  salt  and freshly ground  black pepper . 
 Cook  chicken  over a medium flame for about 10 minutes. 
 Flip  chicken  and place  lemon  slices over  chicken . 
 Cook  chicken  over lowest heat setting for 30-40 minutes or until  chicken  is cooked through. 
 Remove  chicken  from grill and sprinkle with  Italian parsley  and  balsamic vinegar .
