Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  olive oil 
 ½ cup  wine vinegar 
 1 cup  apricot nectar 
 ¼ cup  brown sugar 
 2 tablespoons  Dijon mustard 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  chili powder 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and heat until boiling over medium heat. 
 Boil for 1 minute, then cool at room temperature, and refrigerate until ready to use. 
 This is a great one to use on pork or chicken. 
 Enjoy
