 Makes 8 servings of bean and onion sauté. 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds mixed  green ,  yellow  and  purple beans , trimmed 
 ¼ pound  prosciutto , chopped 
 1 tbs  olive oil 
 2 medium  onions , thinly sliced 
 ¼ tsp  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Cook beans in boiling water 4 minutes or until tender. Drain. 
 Heat skillet, add prosciutto, saute 3 minutes. 
 Remove, set aside. Reduce heat. 
 Heat oil. Add onions, cook 5 minutes or until light golden. 
 Add beans, prosciutto and black pepper to pan. Heat through.
