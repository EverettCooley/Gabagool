 Ingredients : 
 Salad : 
 4 large  tomatoes , cubed 
 3 mild  green chilies , seeded and minced, or 1  green bell pepper , seeded and minced 
 4  scallions , finely chopped 
 1  English cucumber , diced 
 10 sprigs fresh  Italian parsley , finely chopped 
 5 small  radishes , optional, for garnish 
 8 to 10  black olives , optional, for garnish 
 Dressing : 
 ¼ cup extra virgin  olive oil 
 juice of ½  lemon 
 1 tbsp  white vinegar 
 salt  and  black pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Salad : 
 Mix together the  tomatoes , chilies, scallions,  cucumber  and  parsley  in a large bowl. 
 Dressing : 
 Beat together the  olive oil ,  lemon juice  and  vinegar  in a second bowl. 
 Season to taste with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Dress the salad. 
 Peel the  radishes , if using, in strips, and garnish the salad with  radishes  and  black olives , if desired.
