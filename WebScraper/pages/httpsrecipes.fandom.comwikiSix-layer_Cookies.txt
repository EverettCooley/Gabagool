Description : 
 Contributed by Kim Pallo
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cups of  butter 
 1½ cups of  graham cracker crumbs 
 1 cup  walnuts 
 1 cup  chocolate chips 
 1⅓  coconut 
 1 (14 oz) can of  condensed milk 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 350°F. 
 Put butter in a 13x9 pan for 3 – 5 minutes to melt butter. 
 Remove pan and add remaining ingredients layer by layer in the order listed using the back of a spoon to flatten each layer. 
 Bake for 25 minutes or until edges are brown. 
 Remove from oven and refrigerate for at least 3 hours before cutting.
