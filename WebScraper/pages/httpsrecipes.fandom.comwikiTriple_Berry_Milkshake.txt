Ingredients : 
 6 ice cubes 1 cup of ice 
 5 scoops vanilla ice cream 
 1/2 cup blueberries 
 1/2 cup raspberries 
 1/2 cup blackberries 
 1/2 cup milk 
 extra berries for garnish 
 Instructions : 
 Crush the ice in a blender. Then add the remaining ingredients and blend until smooth. 
 Garnish with additional berries.
