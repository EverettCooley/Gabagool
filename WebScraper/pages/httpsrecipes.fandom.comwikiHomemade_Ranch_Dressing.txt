Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Turner Estate in Dallas, Texas in 1997. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 1 cup  buttermilk 
 4 teaspoons  dry mix 
 Dry mix : 
 1½ tablespoons  salt 
 2 teaspoons  msg 
 2 teaspoons dry  parsley  flakes 
 1 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 1 teaspoon  black pepper 
 ½ teaspoon  onion powder 
 Directions : 
 Mix all dry ingredients and store in an airtight jar. 
 To make dressing combine mayonnaise, buttermilk and 4 teaspoons dry mix together and mix well. 
 Store in refrigerator.
