Ingredients : 
 1 pkg black  raspberry Jell-O 
 1 cup  rhubarb  (frozen or fresh) heated 
 ½ cup cold  water 
 ½ cup  pineapple juice 
 ½ cup chopped  celery 
 ½ cup chopped  nuts 
 Directions : 
 Mix and put in refrigerator until jelled.
