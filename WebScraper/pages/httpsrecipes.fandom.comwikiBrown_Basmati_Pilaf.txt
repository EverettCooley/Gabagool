 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups brown  basmati rice 
 1 tsp.  olive oil 
 1 large  shallot , minced 
 2/3 cup thinly sliced scallions 
 1/2 cup shredded  carrots 
 4 cups boiling  vegetable stock 
 1/2 tsp.  ground turmeric 
 1/4 tsp.  sea salt 
 1/3 cup  cashew  pieces 
 1/4 cup  crystallized ginger 
 Directions : 
 1. Rinse  rice  thoroughly in a wire mesh colander using cold running  water  for at least 2 minutes. Set aside.
 2. Heat  oil  for 30 seconds in a saucepan over medium heat, and add  shallot , scallions,  carrots  and  rice . Saut√© for 3 minutes, stirring constantly. Add stock,  turmeric  and  salt . Bring to a boil, and stir in cashews. Reduce heat to low, cover and cook without stirring for 40 minutes, or until liquid is absorbed and  rice  is tender. Add  ginger , and toss gently. Remove from heat, and serve immediately.
