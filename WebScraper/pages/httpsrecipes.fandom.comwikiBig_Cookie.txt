 Description : 
 This makes 2 soft cookies 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz  oatmeal , dry 
 ⅓ cup  non-fat dry milk 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 2 tbsp  raisins 
 2 tbsp chunky  peanut butter 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 ½  banana , mashed (adds sweetness) 
 2 (maybe 3) tbsp  water 
 Directions : 
 Combine all. 
 Divide into 2 cookies on a sprayed baking sheet. 
 Bake at 350°F for 5 – 8 minutes. 
 Do not over-bake. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per cookie :
 
 Exchanges: 1 bread | ½ milk | 1 protein | 1 fat  | 1 fruit
