Ingredients : 
 3 pounds  California avocados 
 ⅓ cups  lemon juice 
 ⅓ cups finely chopped  onion 
 2 tbsp chopped  garlic 
 1 ½ tsp dried  thyme 
 1 ½ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp coarsely ground  black pepper 
 ½ tsp  white pepper 
 ¼ tsp  cayenne pepper 
 Directions : 
 Roughly mash  avocado  (texture should not be smooth) 
 Fold remaining ingredients into  avocado . 
 Chill 1 hour to marry flavors. 
 Serve ¼ cup dip with  onion  flowers, fried vegetables,  chicken  wings or use as a topping for grilled meat, poultry, fish or  egg  dishes.
