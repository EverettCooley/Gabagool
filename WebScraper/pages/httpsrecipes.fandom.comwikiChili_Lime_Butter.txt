       Chili-Lime Butter     
 Recipes courtesy Rachael Ray.
 
 Episode#: TMSP01, Copyright 2003 Television Food Network, G.P., All Rights Reserved 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Yield: about Â½ cup butter topping 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  lime zest 
 2 tablespoons  chili powder 
 2 tablespoons grill seasoning 
 1 stick  butter 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in a small pot, and melt butter over low heat. 
 Transfer to a small bowl, for basting or spreading. 
 Notes : 
 May Cover the homemade butter with the plastic wrap then reserve this in the freezer after cooling. 
 Serve warm with grilled corn 
 May add 3 red chilis, chopped and 1 teaspoon lime juice to increase flavor
