 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 Chicken , cut up 
 2 tbsp  olive oil 
 1/2 tsp  thyme 
 2 tbsp  lemon juice 
 pepper 
 2  limes , cut into wedges 
 Directions : 
 Place  Chicken  pieces in an oiled baking dish, and mix with  olive oil , thyme , juice and  pepper . Bake at 375 °F for 45 minutes to and hour, or until  Chicken  is golden and tender. Garnish with wedges of  lime  so guests can soak his own  Chicken
