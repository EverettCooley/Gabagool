Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Weight Watchers Shortcut Cookbook 
 4 (1 cup) servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  water 
 1 (3 oz) pkg. low-fat  ramen  noodles, uncooked and crumbled 
 cooking spray 
 1 (24 oz) pkg. frozen  chicken  stir-fry mix (such as Contessa) 
 2 tsp  sugar 
 ¼ tsp  crushed red pepper 
 ⅓ cup unsalted  peanuts , coarsely chopped 
 Directions : 
 Pour water into a large nonstick skillet; bring to a boil. 
 Discard seasoning packet from ramen noodles; add noodles to boiling water. 
 Cook 4 minutes or until noodles are tender; drain. 
 Wipe pan dry with paper towels; coat with cooking spray. 
 Heat pan over medium-high heat. 
 Add stir-fry mix and stir-fry seasoning pkt. 
 To pan. 
 Stir-fry 3 minutes; add sugar and pepper. 
 Cook 1 minute or until thoroughly heated. 
 Stir in noodles, sprinkle with peanuts and serve immediately. 
 Nutritional information : 
 WW Points: 6 
 Exchanges: 1½ starch, 2 veg, 1 very lean meat, 1 high-fat meat
