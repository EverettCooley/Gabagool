Ingredients : 
 1/2 oz  coffee  Liqueur 
 1/2 oz  Irish cream liqueur 
 1/2 oz  sambuca 
 1/2 oz  Grand Marnier 
 1/2 oz  vodka 
 Directions : 
 Pour carefully, in order given, into cordial glass so that each ingredient floats on the preceeding without mixing.
