 Ingredients : 
 2  ice cubes 
 1 measure La Mauny  sugar cane syrup 
 1 measure  Grenadine  cordial 
 1 measure La Mauny  amber rum 
 pineapple  or  guava  juice 
 Directions : 
 Put in a double cocktail glass the ingredients. 
 Fill to half with  pineapple  or  guava  juice. 
 Decorate with a slice of  pineapple  or  star fruit . 
 Serve with a straw.
