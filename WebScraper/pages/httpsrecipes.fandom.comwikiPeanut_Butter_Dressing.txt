Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  peanut butter 
 ⅓ cup  salad oil 
 ¼ cup  vinegar 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 5 teaspoons  sugar 
 ½ teaspoon  prepared mustard 
 ⅛ teaspoon  paprika 
 Directions : 
 Beat all ingredients until smooth. 
 Serve on fruit salads.
