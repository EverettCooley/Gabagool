Description : 
 This recipe comes from Montréal, Québec
 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb (500 g)  eggplants 
 2 tsp  sesame oil 
 2 tsp  salt , or to taste 
 1 tbsp  sesame paste 
 ½ tsp fresh coriander (or  cilantro ), chopped 
 ½ tsp  chives , chopped 
 1 whole  garlic , mashed 
 Directions : 
 Peel the  eggplant  and cut into thin slices. 
 Place in a heat-proof dish and steam for 20 minutes, or until very soft. 
 Mash and let cool. 
 Mix well with the  sesame oil ,  salt , sesame paste,coriander,  chives , and  garlic  and serve.
