Description : 
 Lentil rice
 
 Ingredients : 
 450 g (1 lb)  long-grain rice 
 200 g (7 oz)  red lentils 
 600 ml (1 pint) boiling  water 
 1 tablespoon  salt 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 Directions : 
 Wash  lentils , drain and set aside until required. 
 Wash  rice , drain then place in a medium saucepan and add  lentils . 
 Pour the boiling  water ,  olive oil ,  salt  and give a quick stir. 
 Cover pan and boil for 10 minutes. 
 After 10 minutes give a quick stir then cover and simmer for 10 minutes.
