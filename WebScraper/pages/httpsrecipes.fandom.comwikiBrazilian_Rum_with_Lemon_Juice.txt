Ingredients : 
 2 teaspoons  egg whites 
 2 tablespoons  sugar , superfine 
 2 ounces Brazilian  rum 
 1 ounce  lemon juice 
 4 ice cubes 
 1  lemon  slice 
 Directions : 
 Combine egg whites and  sugar  in a mixing glass. 
 Stir with a bar spoon to dissolve  sugar . 
 Add Brazilian rum,  lemon juice  and ice cubes. 
 Place a bar shaker on top of mixing glass and, grasping glass and shaker firmly together with both hands, shake vigorously 9 or 10 times. 
 Remove shaker, place a strainer on top of glass and pour into a chilled old fashioned glass. 
 With a sharp knife, slit  lemon  slice from peel just to center and use the slit to hold the slice on the rim of the glass.
