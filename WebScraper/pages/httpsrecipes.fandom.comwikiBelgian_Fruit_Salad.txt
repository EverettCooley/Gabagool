 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2  Apples 
 10  grapes 
 2  bananas 
 2 tsp red pepper 
 1 tsp  black pepper 
 2  limes 
 4 strawberrys 
 10 blueberrys 
 Directions : 
 Cut up all fruits and mix them up in a bowl. 
 Add the spices and  limes  to the fruits. 
 Mix until everything is blended. 
 Serve as appetizer or a side dish.
