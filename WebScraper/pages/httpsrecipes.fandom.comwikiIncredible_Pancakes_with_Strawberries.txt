Description : 
 Purchased from the O'Reilly Estate in Fort Worth, Texas in 1988. Dated 1964. These are the most incredible pancakes. I tried them on my husband on his birthday and now it's a dish he requests 2 – 3 times a month. I have also made them with  strawberries  and  blueberries .
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  unsalted butter 
 4  eggs 
 1 cup  milk 
 1 cup  flour 
 2 tablespoons  sugar 
 ½ teaspoon  vanilla 
 1 pint  strawberries , hulled and quartered 
 1 tablespoon  confectioners' sugar 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 425°F. 
 Melt 1 tablespoon  butter  in each of two 9 inch pie plates in oven. 
 Beat  eggs  with  milk ,  flour ,  sugar  and  vanilla . 
 Remove pie plates and pour half of batter into each plate. 
 Scatter with  berries . 
 Bake 20 minutes and sprinkle with  confectioners' sugar .
