 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Get Togethers 
 Serves 6 to 8 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  new red skin potatoes , halved 
 3 to 4 tbsp  olive oil 
 coarse salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 ¼ cup  onion , diced 
 1 tbsp  garlic , minced 
 1 tsp fresh  rosemary , minced 
 ½ tsp fresh  thyme , minced 
 fresh  rosemary  and  thyme  for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients except garnish in a large bowl; mix well to coat potatoes. 
 Pour into a 13 x 9 baking dish sprayed with nonstick vegetable spray. 
 Bake at 425°F for 30 minutes or until potatoes are golden and tender when pierced. 
 Garnish with rosemary and thyme sprigs if desired.
