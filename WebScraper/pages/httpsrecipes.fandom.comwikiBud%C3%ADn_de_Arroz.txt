 Description : 
 rice  custard
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 cups  white rice , pre-cooked, measured after cooking (get our recipe for  white rice 
 3/4 cup  Sugar  or equivalent such as Splenda or Equal 
 1/4 teaspoonful  salt  (or to taste) 
 1 cup whole or skim  milk 
 1/2 cup seedless  Raisins 
 2 tablespoonfuls melted  butter  or  peanut oil 
 2  eggs , lightly beaten 
 Directions : 
 Preheat your oven to 325 degrees. 
 Blend all ingredients, preferably in the order listed. 
 Bake for thirty minutes at 325 degrees. 
 Allow to cool. While still warm, cut into serving-size pieces, about 1 1/2Â inches on each side. 
 May be served warm or refrigerated. 
 If desired, add one ounce of  cognac  to each individual serving, set the  cognac  on fire, and serve "flambe" or "flaming".
