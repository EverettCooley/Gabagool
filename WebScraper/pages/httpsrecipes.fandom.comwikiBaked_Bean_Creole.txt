 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 	large 	 Onion , chopped 
 1 		 garlic  clove, crushed 
 oil  for frying 
 2 		Green  bell peppers , diced 
 3 		 celery  stalks, sliced 
 2 	tbsp 	 flour 
 1 	can 	 tomatoes 
 1 	can 	 tomato  paste 
 1 	pinch 	 basil 
 1 	pinch 	 rosemary 
 2 	cup 	Cooked beans of your choice 
 1/2 	cup 	Breadcrumbs 
 water 
 Directions : 
 1 	Saute  onions  &  garlic  lightly in  oil .
 2 	Add  pepper  &  celery .
 3 	Fry for several minutes til ltender.
 4 	Stir in the  flour  & add  tomatoes .
 5 	Add  tomato  paste & herbs & beans.
 6 	Mix in breadcrumbs.
 7 	Add a little  water  if too dry.
 8 	Spoon into oiled casserole & sprinkle breadcrumbs on top.
 9 	Bake for 30 minutes at 350 °F.
 10 	Serve with  rice  & salsa sauce.
