Description : 
 Contributed by Jeff at  SF&SC Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  red beans  — cooked 
 ½ pound  ham  — diced 
 ½ pound hot link  sausages  — diced 
 3 medium  jalapeno chile  pepper — diced fine 
 1 medium  onion  — diced 
 1 tablespoon  Creole seasoning 
 2 bottles amber  beer 
 ½ cup  celery  — diced 
 ½ cup  red bell pepper  — diced 
 rice 
 Directions : 
 In a crockpot or a heavy 3 quart pan place all the ingredients except the beans to a boil and simmer for an hour or two. 
 Add the beans and simmer for another hour or two. 
 Mash a few of the beans with a spoon to thicken the liquid. 
 Do not use a creole seasoning made with salt. 
 The sausage and ham provide salt, and more may be added at the table. 
 Add additional peppers if desired. 
 Serve with rice. 
 A tablespoon of tomato paste may be added near the end of the cooking time.
