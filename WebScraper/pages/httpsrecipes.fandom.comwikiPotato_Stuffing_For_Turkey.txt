Ingredients : 
 20 	 	 potatoes 
 8 		 onions , chopped 
 salt  &  pepper 
 poultry seasoning 
 Directions : 
 Peel & cut up  potatoes , boil till tender and drain. 
 Add 1 to 1/2 stick of  butter ,  salt  &  pepper  to taste and mash chunky. 
 Add about 1 tbsp of  poultry seasoning  or more if desired. 
 Saute  onions  in  butter  till just turning brown but do not burn. 
 Add to  Potato  mixture and stir well. 
 Taste and adjust seasonings. 
 Stuff a 20-25Â lb  Turkey  and roast  Turkey  as usual. 
 Can make stuffing up to 2 days ahead and keep refrigerated in a bowl till ready to stuff bird.
