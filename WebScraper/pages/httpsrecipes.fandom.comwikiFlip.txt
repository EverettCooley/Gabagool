Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Glahn Estate in Allen, Texas in 1984.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4  egg yolks 
 4 teaspoons  sugar 
 1 teaspoon  nutmeg 
 4 jiggers  whiskey 
 1 quart rich  milk 
 Directions : 
 Beat egg yolks, sugar and nutmeg together then add whiskey and milk. 
 Shake well with crushed ice then strain and serve in stemmed glasses.
