 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 500 g  Grouper  (gutted, skin, head and bones removed, cut into ½ inch cubes) 
 100 g  Onion  (sliced thinly) 
 20 g  garlic  cloves (sliced thinly) 
 2  curry leaves 
 2 pcs  rampe leaf 
 6 – 8 Bilinbi (de-stemmed, cut into halves) 
 10 g  cinnamon 
 6 – 8 Cardamoms 
 2 tsp  fennel  (ground) 
 6 tbsp  chilli powder 
 ½ tsp  turmeric  Powder 
 1 tsp  ginger  (grated) 
 4 Green  chilli 
 2 tbsp  ghee 
 100 ml  coconut milk 
 50 ml  coconut cream 
 ½ ltr  water 
 salt  to season 
 Directions : 
 Fry the  Onion ,  garlic ,  rampe leaf  and  curry leaves  until the  onions  are golden brown. 
 Add in the fish, bilinbi,  cinnamon ,  cardamom ,  fennel ,  chilli powder ,  turmeric  powder,  ginger , green  chilli ,  coconut milk  and  water . 
 Over a medium heat cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add in the  coconut cream , adjust seasoning and remove from heat.
