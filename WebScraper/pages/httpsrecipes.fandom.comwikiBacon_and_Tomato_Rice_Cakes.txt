Makes 4 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 lightly-salted  rice  cakes 
 1 4-ounce container French  Onion  spreadable  Cheese 
 4  tomato  slices 
 1 cup (4 ounces) shredded  mozzarella   Cheese 
 4 slices  Bacon , cooked and crumbled 
 Directions : 
 Place  rice  cakes on baking sheet. 
 Spread French  Onion   Cheese  evenly on each  rice  cake; 
 top with  tomato  slices,  mozzarella   Cheese , and crumbled  Bacon . 
 Broil at 400 degrees 3 to 5 minutes. 
 Serve immediately.
