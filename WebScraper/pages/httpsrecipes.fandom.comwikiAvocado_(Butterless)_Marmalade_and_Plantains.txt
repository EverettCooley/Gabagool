Ingredients : 
 2 ripe large genuine  California avocados 
 4 oz  cream cheese  (softened) 
 2 oz  guava   marmalade  or  guava  preserves 
 1 oz  mango   passion fruit   liquor 
 Directions : 
 In a mixing bowl, chop  avocados  with  cream cheese  until buttery texture and creamy consistency. 
 Add 2 oz  guava   marmalade  or preserves. 
 Mix well with electric mixer or by hand with a whisk. 
 Serve with crispy plaintain sticks or  croissants .
