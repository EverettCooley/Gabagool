Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves 6 
 Ingredients : 
 4 pounds  chicken  pieces 
 ¼ cup  all purpose flour 
 2 tablespoons cooking  oil 
 6 ounces lemonade frozen concentrate, thawed 
 3 tablespoons  brown sugar 
 3 tablespoons  catsup 
 2 tablespoons  water 
 2 tablespoons  cornstarch 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1¼ teaspoons  salt 
 Directions : 
 Combine  flour  with  salt ; coat  chicken  thoroughly. 
 Brown  chicken  pieces in  oil  and drain. 
 Stir together lemonade,  sugar ,  catsup  and  vinegar . 
 Place  chicken  in crockpot and pour lemonade mixture over it. 
 Cook on high 3 to 4 hours (or low 6 to 8 hours). 
 Drain liquid into saucepan; add  cornstarch  and boil till reduced. 
 Pour gravy back over  chicken ; serve over  rice .
