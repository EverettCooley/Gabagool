Description : 
 A cold soup
 
 Ingredients : 
 1.5 l  water 
 300 g  sorrel 
 2  cucumbers 
 120 g  green onion 
 2  eggs 
 3 tbsp  sour cream 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Sort the  sorrel , shred, boil in salted  water  and cool. 
 Add fresh  cucumbers  cut up fine, hard-boiled  eggs ,  green onion  rubbed together with  salt . 
 Before serving season with  sour cream  and sprinkle with minced  dill .
