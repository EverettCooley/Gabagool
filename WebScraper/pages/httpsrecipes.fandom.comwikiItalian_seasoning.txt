Italian seasoning  is a mixture of many herbs, including  basil ,  oregano ,  marjoram ,  thyme ,  rosemary ,  savory , and  sage . Sprinkle on  potatoes  or vegetables, in  soups , and in  sauces  for an  Italian  taste.       Italian Seasoning
