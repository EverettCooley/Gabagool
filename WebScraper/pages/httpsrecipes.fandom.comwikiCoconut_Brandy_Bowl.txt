Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 oz  brandy 
 1½ oz  banana liqueur 
 2 large  coconuts 
 3 – 4 oz crushed  ice 
 Directions : 
 Slice off the eyes of the  coconuts ; drain and save the  milk . 
 Gouge out the meat of the  coconuts ; saving as much  milk  from that as possible. 
 Combine the  brandy ,  banana liqueur , and all the  coconut milk  in a blender with the crushed  ice . 
 Blend at a high speed for 15 seconds. 
 Serve the drink in the two  coconut  shells.
