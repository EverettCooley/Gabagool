Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  ground beef 
 ½ tsp  garlic powder 
 ⅓ cup  tomato juice 
 2 tbsp  chili powder 
 ½ bag  corn chips 
 1 chopped  onion 
 1 can  tomato sauce 
 ¼ tsp  oregano 
 1 can  kidney beans 
 grated  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Brown meat,  onion ,  salt  and  garlic powder . 
 Drain off fat. 
 Add tomato juice,  oregano  and  chili powder . 
 Simmer 10 minutes. 
 Grease a 2 quart casserole. 
 Alternate layers of meat mixture, beans and liquid and crunched  corn chips  ending with chips. 
 Bake covered 20 minutes. 
 Before serving top with grated  cheese . 
 Bake at 350°F.
