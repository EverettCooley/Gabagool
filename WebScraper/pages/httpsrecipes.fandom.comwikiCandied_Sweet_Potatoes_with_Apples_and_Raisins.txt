Description 
 Diana's Candied Sweet Potatoes  from the Wikibooks Cookbook—original recipe, licensed under the GNU Free Documentation License
 
 Cook Time : 60 minutes 
 Ingredients 
 4 medium  sweet potatoes , peeled and sliced 
 4   apples , peeled, cored, and sliced 
 ½ cup  raisins 
 1/2 cup brown sugar 
 1 tsp ginger 
 1 tbsp cinnamon 
 2 tablespoons  honey 
 3 tablespoons  apple juice  or  cider 
 3 tablespoons melted  butter 
 Directions 
 Arrange sliced sweet potatoes, apples, and raisins in a greased 2-quart baking dish. 
 Mix the liquids and butter together. 
 Pour liquid mixture over the dish's contents. 
 Cover and bake at 350°F for 40 to 45 minutes, until sweet potatoes are tender and glazed
