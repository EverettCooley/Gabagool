Ingredients : 
 hearts of palm 
 lettuce 
 stuffed  olives 
 green pepper  rings 
 chopped  parsley 
 paprika 
 French dressing 
 Directions : 
 Cut the heart of palm into lengthwise strips and place on  lettuce . 
 Garnish with olives and  green pepper  rings. 
 Sprinkle top with  parsley  and  paprika . 
 Pour dressing over salad before serving.
