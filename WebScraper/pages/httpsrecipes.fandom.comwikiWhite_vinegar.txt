 About white vinegar : 
 Wikipedia Article  About White vinegar on Wikipedia 
 The so-called "white vinegar" (actually clear) can be made by oxidating a distilled alcohol. Alternatively, it will be nothing more than a solution of acetic acid in water. It is used for culinary as well as cleaning purposes.
