Description : 
 Ethnicity - Marathi, West Indian 
 Type of meal - Party, Lunch, Dinner 
 Ingredients : 
 Tadka : 
 4 – 5 tsp  ground nut oil 
 ½ tsp  mustard seeds 
 ½ tsp  cumin  seeds 
 5 – 6  curry leaves 
 3 chopped  garlic 
 2 – 3 slit  green chillies 
 1 small piece  ginger 
 Paste : 
 toor  / Ahrar  dal 
 ½  grated coconut 
 3 – 4  garlic 
 ½ tsp  cumin  seeds 
 ¼ tsp  turmeric powder 
 ½ tsp  coriander leaves 
 salt  to taste 
 ½ cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Prepare a paste of  coconut ,  garlic  and  cumin  seeds and keep aside. 
 Heat  oil  in pan. 
 Prepare a tadka of  mustard  and  cumin  seeds,  curry leaves , slit green chillies,  garlic  and  ginger . 
 Now add the  coconut  paste to the pan along with  turmeric  powder, and  salt . 
 Sauté for 5 minutes till the tadka turns red. 
 Add pre-boiled  dal  and  water . 
 Wait for one boil. 
 Vaaran is ready. 
 Garnish with coriander and serve with  rice .
