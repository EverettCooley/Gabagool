 Description : 
 Avocado Salad. Canned  peach  slices may be added to the  avocado  salad-in which case use one slice of  tomato  and two to three  peach  slices. This  lemon  Dressing is excellent on a tossed green salad or hearts of  lettuce . It is light and easy to make.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2  tomatoes  cut in 8-inch uniform slices overlapping with 
 2  avocados  cut in thick uniform slices across the  lettuce  in a straight line. 
 Lemon dressing : 
 1 cup  lemon juice  (bottled) 
 1 cup  olive oil 
 1 cup  peach  syrup (from a can or use any fruit syrup) 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp salad herbs 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Cut 1 head  iceberg lettuce  down in ¾-inch round uniform slices. 
 Arrange one slice on each salad plate. 
 Alternate: 2  tomatoes  cut in 8-inch uniform slices overlapping with 2 avocados cut in thick uniform slices across the  lettuce  in a straight line. 
 Spoon 2 tbsp of the following  lemon  dressing over the  avocado  salad. 
 Lemon dressing : 
 Combine  lemon juice  and  olive oil . 
 Shake with  peach  syrup (from a can or use any fruit syrup) and  salt . 
 Add salad herbs and  pepper . 
 Serve as a side salad.
