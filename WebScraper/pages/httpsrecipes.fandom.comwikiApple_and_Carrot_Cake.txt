 Ingredients : 
 2 cup  whole wheat flour  (self-rising) 
 ½ cup  margarine 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 1 cup  walnuts , chopped 
 ⅔ cup  raisins 
 2 cup dessert  apples , peeled cored and grated 
 1 cup  carrot , grated 
 grated peel of 1  orange 
 2 large  eggs , 
 4 tbsp fresh  orange juice 
 Directions : 
 Line the base and grease of a loaf pan with 4 cup capacity. 
 Preheat the oven to 350 °F. 
 Place the  flour  in a bowl and mix well, then cut in the  margarine  and stir in the  cinnamon . 
 Stir in the  walnuts ,  raisins ,  apples  and  carrot . 
 Add the  orange peel ,  eggs  and juice, then beat well until thoroughly combined. 
 Spoon the mixture into the prepared pan, smooth the top and bake for about 1¼ hours, or until the cake is well risen and firm to touch. 
 Turn out on a wire rack.
