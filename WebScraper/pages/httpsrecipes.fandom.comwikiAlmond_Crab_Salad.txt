Description : 
 Makes 4 servings. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups cooked  rice , cooled 
 2 cups (8 ounces)  crab  meat 
 1 cup frozen  peas , thawed 
 1 cup sliced  celery 
 ¼ cup sliced  green onions 
 ⅓ cup  mayonnaise 
 ⅓ cup  sour cream 
 3 tablespoons fresh  lemon juice 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  white pepper 
 lettuce  leaves 
 ½ cup  sliced almonds , toasted 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice , crab meat,  peas  and  green onions  in large mixing bowl. 
 Blend  mayonnaise ,  sour cream ,  lemon juice  and  white pepper . 
 Add to  rice  mixture; toss lightly. 
 Serve on  lettuce  leaves. 
 Garnish with  almonds .
