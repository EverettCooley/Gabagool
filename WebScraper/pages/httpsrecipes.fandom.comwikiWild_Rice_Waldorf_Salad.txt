Ingredients : 
 1 cup  wild rice , cooked 
 1  apple , chopped 
 1 cup seedless  grapes 
 1 cup chopped  celery 
 2 tbsp  mayonnaise 
 1 tbsp plain low-fat  yogurt 
 2 tbsp chopped dry roasted  peanuts 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients except  nuts  in a bowl. 
 Toss to mix. 
 Cover and chill thoroughly, about 3 to 4 hours. 
 Sprinkle the  nuts  on top just before serving. 
 Nutritional information : 
 For ¼ recipe:
 
 157 calories | 20g carbohydrate | 3g protein | 7g fat | 138mg sodium | 193mg potassium | 4mg cholesterol 
 Exchanges: 1 bread | ½ fruit | 1 fat
