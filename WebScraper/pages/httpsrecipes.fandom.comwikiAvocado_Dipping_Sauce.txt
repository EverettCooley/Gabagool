 Ingredients : 
 2  avocado , halved 
 juice of half limoen or  lemon 
 some drops  tabasco 
 pepper 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Scoop the pulp of the  avocados  in a bowl and mash it fine. 
 Add limoen - or  lemon juice  and tabasco. Mix everything well. 
 Add  pepper  and salt and serve with warm toast, warm bread or reepjes  paprika .
