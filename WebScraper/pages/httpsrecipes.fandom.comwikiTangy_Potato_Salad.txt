 Prep Time: 30 min 
 Cook time: 20 min 
 Serves: 4-6 
 
 Description : 
 Boiled Yukon white potatoes with blue cheese and bacon
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 yukon potatoes 
 6 strips crispy bacon 
 1/2 cup crumbled Danish blue cheese 
 6 scallions 
 1/2 cup mayo made with olive oil 
 1 Tbsp Lemon juice 
 1 tsp cayenne pepper.. 
 Directions : 
 Boil potatoes until fork tender and cut into cubes. 
 Mix all ingredients together in large mixing bowl. 
 Refridgerated for 2 hours. 
 Serve and enjoy.
