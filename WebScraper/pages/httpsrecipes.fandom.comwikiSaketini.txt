 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 1.5 oz.  gin 
 0.5 oz.  sake 
 1  green olive 
 ice 
 Directions 
 Stir gin, sake and ice in a mixing glass. 
 Strain into a martini glass. 
 Garnish with a green olive.
