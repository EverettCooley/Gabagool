Ingredients 
 4 cups of chopped  peaches 
 1½ small boxes peach flavored  gelatin 
 1 angel food cake 
 1 large box instant vanilla pudding mix 
 3 cups cold  milk 
 8 oz container of  Cool Whip 
 3 tsp  powdered sugar 
 Directions 
 Put chopped peaches into a 9x13 pan. 
 Pour 1½ boxes dry gelatin mix over peaches. 
 Crumble cake and put on top of fruit. 
 Mix milk and vanilla pudding and pour on top of cake. 
 Put cool whip, sweetened with powdered sugar, over pudding mix. 
 Chill.
