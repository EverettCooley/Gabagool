 Description : 
 In Romanian: Zacusca pentru iarna I
 
 Ingredients : 
 10 young  eggplants 
 15  peppers 
 15  bell peppers 
 8 lbs / 4 kg  tomatoes 
 2 lbs / 1 kg  onions 
 1 pint / 500 ml  oil 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Roast the  eggplants ,  peppers ,  bell peppers , peel and drain. 
 Remove the core and all seeds from the  peppers  and then julienne them. 
 Chop the  eggplants . 
 Saute the chopped  onions  in the  oil  until softened. 
 Add the chopped  eggplants , julienned  peppers  and  bell peppers , as well as the juice from 8 lbs / 4 kg boiled and strained  tomatoes .
