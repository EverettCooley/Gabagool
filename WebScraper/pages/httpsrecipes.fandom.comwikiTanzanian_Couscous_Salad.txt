 Description : 
 Tanzanian  couscous  Salad
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  couscous 
 3 cups hot  water 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 2 cups shredded  carrots 
 1 cup fresh, frozen or canned  corn 
 1 cup chopped  macadamia nuts 
 1 cup chopped fresh  cilantro 
 2 teaspoons fresh crushed  garlic 
 2 teaspoon fresh grated  ginger 
 1/2 cup chopped pitted  dates 
 Directions : 
 Place the  couscous  in a bowl, pour over the boiling  water . 
 Then let stand until cooled to room temperature. 
 Stir in the remaining ingredients and chill.
