 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: Lipton Company 
 Makes about 2Â½ cups 
 Ingredients : 
 1 envelope Lipton  onion recipe soup mix 
 1 jar  apricot preserves  (20 oz) 
 1 cup Wish Bone Sweet n Spicy  French dressing  (8 oz) 
 Directions : 
 In small bowl, blend soup mix, preserves, and dressing. 
 Use as a glaze for chicken, spareribs, kabobs, hamburgers, or frankfurters. 
 Brush on during the last half of cooking. 
 Can be stored covered in refrigerator up to 2 weeks. 
 Variations : 
 Also terrific with bone Lite Sweet 'n spicy French, Russian or Lite Russian Dressing.
