Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 4 servings of beef and vegetable stir fry. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  beef broth 
 ¼ cup  teriyaki sauce 
 ¼ cup  rice vinegar 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 1 tsp Asian dark  sesame oil 
 1 tsp  Chinese 5 spice powder 
 ½ tsp  cinnamon 
 4 tsp  cornstarch 
 2 tsp  vegetable oil 
 1 pound  flank steak , cut against the grain into strips 
 4 cups  broccoli  flowerets 
 1  sweet red pepper , thinly sliced 
 1 small  red onion , thinly sliced 
 8 oz bag  carrot  matchsticks 
 cooked  brown rice 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl, whisk first 8 ingredients. 
 In a skillet, heat 1 tsp oil. 
 Add steak and cook 3 minutes. 
 Remove from pan, set aside. 
 Drain any fat from pan. 
 Return skillet to heat, add broccoli and ½ cup water. 
 Cover and cook 5 minutes or until tender. 
 Remove cover, cook until water has evaporated. 
 Add remaining oil to pan. 
 Add peppers and onion, cook 4 minutes or until tender. 
 Add carrots, cook 1 min. 
 Return beef to skillet. 
 Add sauce, cook 2 minutes or until thickened. 
 Serve with brown rice.
