 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 Tin of fava / fuul beans 
 olive oil 
 lemon juice 
 salt  &  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Mash the beans against the side of the saucepan as you heat them. 
 When hot and thoroughly mashed add  lemon juice ,  olive oil  and seasoning to taste. 
 Eat immediately with bread.
