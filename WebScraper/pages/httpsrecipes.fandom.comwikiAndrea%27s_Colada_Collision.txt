Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz  light rum 
 3 oz La Preferida®  cream of coconut 
 6 oz  pineapple juice 
 1 frozen ripe  banana 
 Directions : 
 Fill blender about ¼ full with  ice . 
 Add all ingredients to blender. 
 Blend to a smooth consistency. 
 Serve in either a hurricane glass or a tall glass or goblet. 
 Garnish with a  pineapple  spear is optional.
