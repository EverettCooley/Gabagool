 Ingredients : 
 A roasted  breadfruit 
 1 tablespoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Take 1 whole roast bread fruit and cut out the 'heart' (uneatable portion in the middle with seed). 
 Cut  breadfruit  into pegs. 
 Pour  oil  in skillet and heat. 
 Fry pegs till golden brown each side. 
 Serve with calloo or  ackee .
