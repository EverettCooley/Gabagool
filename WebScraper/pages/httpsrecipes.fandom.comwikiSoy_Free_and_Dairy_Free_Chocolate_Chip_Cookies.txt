Description : 
 Hi, I adapted this recipe from Weight Watchers and I love these cookies. 
 
 Contributed by  FOODALLERGYKITCHEN Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons of  margarine  (I use Mother's kosher for passover) 
 2 tablespoons of  vegetable oil 
 ½ cup of  brown sugar 
 1 teaspoon of  vanilla extract 
 ⅛ teaspoon of  salt 
 1  egg white  (I use a replacement of 2 tablespoons of cornstarch and 2 tablespoons of water and mix together) 
 ¾ cup  all-purpose flour  (i usually add a bit more flour if the dough is not sticky enough) 
 ¼ teaspoon of  baking soda 
 ½ cup of semi-sweet  chocolate chips  (i use Paskesz kosher chocolate chips) 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 375 °F. 
 Cream together margarine, oil, and brown sugar. 
 Add vanilla, salt and egg white and mix together. 
 Mix together flour and baking soda. 
 Combine wet and dry ingredients. 
 Add chocolate chips and stir. 
 Drop rounded half-teaspoon of dough onto a nonstick baking sheet. 
 Bake for 4 to 6 minutes or until finished, then cool on a wire rack.
