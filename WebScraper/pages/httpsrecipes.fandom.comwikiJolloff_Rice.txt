Ingredients : 
 rice 
 1 can peeled  plum tomatoes 
 3 or 2 small  hot peppers 
 1  big pepper 
 1 long pepper 
 2  onions 
 Directions : 
 First of all you will need to get a blender and pour all the ingredients inside the blender and cut the onions into the blender. 
 Blend the ingredients. 
 After that pour the mixture into a pot. 
 Get some rice and wash it and pour in to pot. 
 After the 30 minutes serve and enjoy.
