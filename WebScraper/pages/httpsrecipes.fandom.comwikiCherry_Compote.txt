Description : 
 In Romanian: Compot de cirese
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs / 1 kg  cherries 
 3 tablespoons  sugar 
 water  to cover 
 a little  vanilla  stick 
 Directions : 
 Wash the  cherries  and place in a pot. 
 Add the  sugar ,  vanilla  and  water  to cover. 
 Let boil for a little while. 
 Serve cold.
