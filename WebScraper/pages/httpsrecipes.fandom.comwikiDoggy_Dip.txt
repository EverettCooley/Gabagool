 Ingredients : 
 3 tbsp  peanut butter 
 2 tbsp  honey 
 1 very ripe  banana   or  a large jar of baby food fruit, any flavor 
 1 x 16 ounce container  vanilla   yogurt 
 1 tbsp  whole wheat flour 
 Directions : 
 Mix the  peanut   butter , honey, and fruit together until well blended. 
 In a separate bowl, combine the  yogurt  and  flour , mix well. 
 Add the fruit mixture to the  yogurt  and blend together. 
 Keep cold in refrigerator. 
 Use this dip to coat or dip biscuits and treats into. 
 Allow treats to chill in refrigerator until coating is set and firm; this prevents big messes!.
