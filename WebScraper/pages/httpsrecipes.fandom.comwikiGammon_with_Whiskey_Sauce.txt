 Description : 
 Try this true Irish meal of broiled  Ham  steaks with brown sauce. Serve and eat it with  Colcannon .
 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3/4 cup  water 
 2 tbl  Onion , chopped 
 2 tablespoons  whiskey 
 2 tablespoons  flour 
 1 tablespoon  demerera sugar 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 4  Ham  steaks 
 seasoning 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Lightly brush  Ham  steaks with melted  butter . Remove fat from the edges. Grill for 8 minutes on each side. For the sauce, fry  onions  in  butter  until cooked. Turn off heat and then gradually stir in  flour . Add  water . Turn on heat. Add  Sugar  and bring to a boil. Simmer for 2 minutes to cook  flour . Add more  water  if sauce looks a little thick. Add  whiskey  and seasoning. Serve  Ham  steaks on platter and drizzle with brown sauce.
