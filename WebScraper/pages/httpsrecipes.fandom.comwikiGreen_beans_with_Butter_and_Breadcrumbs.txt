Description : 
 In Romanian: Fasole verde cu unt si pesmet
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs / 1 kg  green beans 
 3 tablespoons  butter 
 2 tablespoons  bread crumbs 
 2 cloves minced  garlic  (optional) 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Clean, wash and boil the beans in salted  water . 
 Melt the  butter  in a pan and fry the bread crumbs and the minced  garlic . 
 Add the drained beans,  salt , and shake the pan a few times so that the beans are mixed with the bread crumbs and the  butter .
