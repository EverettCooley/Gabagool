Ingredients : 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 2 tsp  baking soda 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 3  eggs , well beaten 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 1 x 8 oz can (or fresh) crushed  pineapple 
 1½ cups chopped  mango 
 ½ cup chopped  walnuts 
 ½ cup  raisins 
 2 cups  flour 
 Directions : 
 Mix  cinnamon ,  baking soda  and  salt . 
 Mix  eggs ,  vanilla  and crushed  pineapple . 
 Mix  mangos ,  walnuts  and  raisins . 
 Combine all ingredients together. 
 Pour into prepared 9"x11" pan and let sit for 20 minutes. 
 Bake at 350°F for 40 – 45 minutes.
