 Description : 
 Makes 8 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked medium  grain   rice 
 1 14-ounce can unsweetened  coconut milk 
 1 cup  sweetened condensed milk 
 1 cup  milk 
 8 ounces  semi-sweet chocolate , chopped 
 2 cups flaked  coconut 
 Toasted  coconut , for garnish 
 shaved chocolate , for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  coconut milk ,  condensed milk  and  milk  in 2-quart saucepan. 
 Cook over medium heat until thick and creamy, 20 to 25 minutes, stirring frequently. 
 Remove from heat. 
 Add  chocolate  and  coconut , stirring until  chocolate  is melted. 
 Garnish with toasted  coconut  and  shaved chocolate , if desired.
