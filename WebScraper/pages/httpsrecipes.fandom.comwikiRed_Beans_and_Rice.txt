Description : 
 Serves: 4 (1 cup servings)
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 ⅛ tsp  cayenne 
 ⅛ tsp  chili powder 
 2 cup  brown rice , cooked 
 1 cup  ham , cooked and diced 
 ⅓ cup  onion , diced 
 ⅛ tsp  cumin 
 2 tsp  Tabasco sauce 
 2 cup  red beans , cooked 
 Directions : 
 In a large pan, saute  garlic  and  onion  with seasonings. 
 Add  rice , beans and  ham ; cook over medium heat. 
 Stir in approximately ¼ cup  water  or liquid from beans. 
 Cook until heated through. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per 1 cup serving:
 
 calories: 306 protein: 19 g fat: 4 g carbohydrates: 48 g fiber: 10.4 g cholesterol: 21 mg sodium: 555 mg (high in sodium) potassium: 731 mg 
 Diabetic exchanges: 3 starch/bread+  1 medium-fat meat
