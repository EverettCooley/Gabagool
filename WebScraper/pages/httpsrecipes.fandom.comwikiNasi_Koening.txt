Description : 
 Spiced yellow rice
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (one) lb  long-grain rice 
 1 (one) can  coconut milk  (dilute with  water ) 
 1 (one) teaspoon  kunir ,  annatto , or masusa (for the color) 
 1 (one)  salam leaf 
 1 (one) pinch greater  galanga 
 1 (one)  pandan leaf 
 1 (one) pinch  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Boil the  rice  together with the  coconut milk , the turmeric,  annatto , or masusa, salam leaf,  laos , pandan leaf,  black pepper  and  salt  (optional). 
 Use a  rice  cooker for this. 
 Take leaves out before serving.
