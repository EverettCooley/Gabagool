 Ingredients : 
 1 shot Disaronno  amaretto 
 ½ shot  Kahlua 
 ¾ shot Stolichnaya  vodka 
 Directions : 
 Layer all ingredients in a glass, disaronno first, then the  kahlua , then the  vodka .
