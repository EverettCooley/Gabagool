 Description : 
 Che Chuoi also known as  bananas  in  coconut milk  is a Vietnamese delicious dessert.
 
       Che Chuoi     
 Ingredients : 
 2 	tablespoons  sesame seeds 
 4 	 bananas 
 500 	ml  coconut milk 
 4 	tablespoons  Sugar 
 1/8 	teaspoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Prepare the  bananas  and cut them, add  Sugar ,  coconut milk  and boil them.
