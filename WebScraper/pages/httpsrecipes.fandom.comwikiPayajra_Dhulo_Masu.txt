Description : 
 Onion ground meat curry
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  ground meat 
 1 cup finely chopped  onions 
 1 inch finely chopped  ginger 
 1 tsp  pepper 
 1 tsp  cumin  powder 
 1 tsp  turmeric 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 2 tbsp  mustard oil 
 ½ cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in a pan and add  turmeric . 
 Mix all ingredients (except the meat and the  water ) and put them in the  oil . 
 Add meat and turn it over well. 
 Fry until it becomes brown. 
 Add the ½ cup of  water  and boil gently for a few minutes.
