 INGREDIENTS : 
 10 cups popped popcorn 
 1 1/4 cups Imperial Sugar Extra Fine Granulated Sugar 
 1 small box strawberry gelatin 
 1/3 cup light corn syrup 
 5 tablespoons salted butter, diced 
 
 
 
 DIRECTIONS : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F.  Prepare a sheet pan with parchment paper. Lay popped popcorn out over pan; set aside. 1 
 In a small pot, heat sugar, gelatin, and corn syrup over medium low heat until smooth and bubbly, about 5-6 minutes.  Add butter and stir until incorporated. 2 
 Pour mixture over popcorn, stirring to coat.  Place in oven and bake for 3-4 minutes.  Remove from oven and stir; return to oven for 4 more minutes.  Remove from oven and allow to set, about 2-3 minutes.  Form into balls, if desired.
