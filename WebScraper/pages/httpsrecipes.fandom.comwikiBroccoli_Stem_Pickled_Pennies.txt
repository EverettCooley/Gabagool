Ingredients : 
 3 or 4  Broccoli  stems 
 2 tsp  salt 
 1 or 2  garlic  cloves 
 1 tbsp  olive oil 
 2 tsp  apple cider vinegar 
 Directions : 
 Peel the skin from the  Broccoli  stems. 
 Thinly slice them diagonally, discarding the tough end. 
 Place the slices and  salt  in a glass jar or covered plastic bowl. Leave in the refrigerator overnight. 
 Mix  garlic ,  oil  and  vinegar . 
 Drain all the salty  water  from the sliced  Broccoli  stems. Add the  oil  mixture and shake well. 
 Return to the refrigerator for several hours or overnight before serving.
