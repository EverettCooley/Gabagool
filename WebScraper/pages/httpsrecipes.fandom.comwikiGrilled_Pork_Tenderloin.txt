Description : 
 This is so easy that I thought it just can't be that good and almost didn't try it but it is excellent. I got the recipe from another group and it was posted by Buttercup.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 4 to 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2  pork tenderloins  (2 pounds each) 
 1 x 8 ounce bottle zesty  Italian salad dressing 
 Directions : 
 In large covered bowl pour salad dressing over tenderloins and marinate for 8 to 12 hours in refrigerator, turning often. 
 Remove tenderloins from dressing and place on grill. 
 Cook until meat thermometer reaches 160 to 170Â°F (about 10 minutes on each side). 
 Slice and serve.
