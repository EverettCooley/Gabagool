This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Henson Estate in Dallas, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups jalapeño  jack cheese  cubed 
 24 square  wonton wrappers 
 3 cups  vegetable oil 
 8 ounces  cream cheese  softened 
 ⅓ cup bottled  sweet and sour sauce 
 Directions : 
 Place 1 cube of  cheese  in middle of wonton wrapper then fold in half to form a triangle sealing edges with  water . 
 Fold remaining 2 edges together and seal. 
 Repeat with remaining  cheese  and wrappers. 
 Heat oil in deep skillet to 375°F. 
 Fry wontons 6 to 8 at a time until golden brown about 4 minutes. 
 Drain on paper towels.
