Ingredients : 
 2½ cups  sugar 
 2 cups  water 
 12 drops  peppermint oil 
 Directions : 
 First boil  water  and  sugar  together to 217°F. 
 Take from fire, add  peppermint oil , and beat until cloudy. 
 Drop from tip of teaspoon on slightly buttered sheets of paper.
