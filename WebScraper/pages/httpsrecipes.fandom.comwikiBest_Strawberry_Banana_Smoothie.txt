 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 large  strawberries , fresh or frozen 
 1/2 c.  milk 
 1 ripe  banana 
 5  ice  cubes 
 1 1/2 tsp  Sugar  (opt.) 
 Directions : 
 Combine  strawberries ,  milk  and  ice . Blend until perfectly smooth. Add  Sugar . Blend for 1 minute.
 
 Makes approximately 32 oz.
