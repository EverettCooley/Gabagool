Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz  light rum 
 ¾ oz  brandy 
 1 tsp  grenadine 
 1 tsp  melon liqueur 
 ¼ tsp  lemon juice 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Shake with  ice  and strain into a glass.
