This chutney is nice and spicey. It adds wonderful flavor to any Indian meal. 
Servings : 6-8 servings
Preparation Time : 10 minutes
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  coriander  (1 bunch) 
 2  serrano chiles 
 2 tablespoons  channa dal  (split dalia) 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon  sugar 
 4 teaspoons  coconut 
 1 teaspoon plain  yogurt 
 1 tablespoon of  tamarind  paste 
 1 cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Remove coriander leaves from 
 Put all the ingredients in a blender and mix. 
 If the ingredients does not mix completely, add a little more  water  (½ cup). 
 Serve with Indian snacks like  Samosa ,  Khaman ,  Dosa  or  Idli .
