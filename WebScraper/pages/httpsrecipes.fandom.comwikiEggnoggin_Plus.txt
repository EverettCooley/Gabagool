Ingredients : 
 1¼ cups  milk , chilled 
 1  egg 
 ¼ tsp  vanilla extract 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 pinch of ground  nutmeg  to decorate 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients, except  nutmeg , in a blender at high speed until frothy. 
 Pour into a glass and sprinkle with ground  nutmeg .
