Description : 
 Bean cake 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1.5 kg of  dry beans  (small white grains) 
 palm oil 
 1 red  hot pepper 
 Â½ litre  water 
 8  leaves of bananas 
 Directions : 
 Chop the  grains  and put them in cold  water  for 1 hour. 
 Wash the beans thoroughly until the skin is completely removed. Drain and crush the seeds. 
 In a terrine, place the crushed bean seeds and add the crushed  hot pepper . Add  oil  and  water  in small quantities, while mixing, in order to obtain a homogeneous dough. 
 Season the dough and then place in the  banana  sheets. 
 Cook in the oven for about 3 hours. 
 Serve hot with green  bananas .
