 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 bananas ,  kiwis ,  oranges ,  grapefruit ,  watermelons ,  pineapple , fresh or in tin and  Apples ,  grapes ,  strawberries 
 old  rum 
 Directions : 
 Take exotic fruits (  bananas ,  kiwis ,  oranges ,  grapefruit ,  watermelons ,  pineapple , fresh or in tin ) and  Apples ,  grapes ,  strawberries . 
 Peel the fruit. 
 Cut it in cubes. 
 Place it in a bowl (with the juice of the fruit). 
 Sprinkle with old  rum . 
 Mix it. 
 Cover the bowl with aluminium paper. 
 Place the bowl in the fridge for one hour. 
 Serve very cold.
