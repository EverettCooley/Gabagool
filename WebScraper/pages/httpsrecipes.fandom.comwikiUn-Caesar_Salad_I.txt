Description : 
 Makes somewhere around 1 cup
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 to 4 cloves  garlic , to taste, chopped 
 4 tbsp fresh  lemon juice 
 1 tbsp  capers  including the brine 
 4 tbsp  chickpeas 
 4 tbsp additional  chickpea  can liquid or cooking liquid 
 2 tbsp  tamari , or to taste - depends on  salt  in  beans 
 additional  capers  for garnish 
 pepper 
 croutons  (optional) 
 romaine lettuce 
 Directions : 
 Blend everything except the last three items until only tiny pieces of  chickpea  are visible. (I used a Braun hand-blender). 
 Toss  romaine lettuce  with mixture. 
 Garnish with the  capers  and fresh ground  black pepper  and croutons.
