Description : 
 Pasta bullets with vegetarian sauce
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 cups  water 
 2 tablespoons  olive oil 
 2 medium  tomatoes  — chopped 
 1 tablespoon  tomato paste 
 1 medium  zucchini  — cubed 
 ¼ pound  string beans  — chopped 
 ½ cup  green peas 
 1 medium  potato  — cubed 
 1 medium  onion  — chopped 
 6 cloves  garlic  — finely chopped 
 2 tablespoons  cilantro  — finely chopped 
 2 teaspoons  mint 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  pepper 
 1 tablespoon  paprika 
 1 pound  pasta bullets 
 Directions : 
 Bring  water  to a boil in a large pan and add all remaining ingredients, except  pasta . 
 Cook over moderate heat for 30 minutes. 
 Rinse  pasta  in cold  water , drain and add to the sauce. 
 Cook for 15 minutes. 
 Serve.
