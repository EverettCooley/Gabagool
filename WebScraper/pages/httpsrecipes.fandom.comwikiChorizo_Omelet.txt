 Ingredients : 
 6-8 thin slices of  chorizo , sliced up 
 3 large  eggs 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅛ teaspoon  pepper , black or red only 
 2 teaspoons fat or  butter  or  butter substitute 
 Directions : 
 Fry the chorizo up in  butter . 
 In a large bowl, add 3  eggs . 
 Beat 3  eggs  with a fork and season with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Beat until the  eggs  can pass through the fork without sticking to it. 
 Melt the fat or  butter  in a hot frying pan. Pour on the  eggs . 
 Stir with a fork, at the same time shaking the pan, until the  eggs  start to set, carefully lifting the edges of the omelet to allow the uncooked portion to run underneath to be cooked. Add the fried chorizo. 
 When the  eggs  are set, fold over and cook until  eggs  are cooked through. Serve warm and plain over white rice.
