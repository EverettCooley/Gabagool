Ingredients : 
 ⅓ cup  rice flour 
 1 cup  whole wheat flour 
 1½ teaspoons  baking powder 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅓ cup  carob  powder (unsweetened!) 
 ¾ cup  honey   or   maple syrup 
 ⅓ cup  oil 
 ¼ cup  apple juice  ( water  can also be used) 
 1 tablespoon  vanilla extract 
 ½ cup  carob chips  (optional) 
 ½ cup chopped  walnuts  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 In a large bowl, combine flours,  baking powder ,  salt  and  carob  powder. 
 Add in the  honey , juice,  oil  and  vanilla  and stir just until combined. It's okay if there are some lumps! 
 Spread into a lightly greased 8x8 inch pan. Bake for 25–30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the middle comes out clean.
