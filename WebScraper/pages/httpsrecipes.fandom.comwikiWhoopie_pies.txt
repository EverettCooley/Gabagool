 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup  cocoa 
 1/2 cup  water  (hot) 
 1 1/2 cup  Sugar 
 2 x  eggs 
 1/2 cup  milk  (sour) 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 1/2 cup  shortening 
 1/2 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp Soda 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 2 3/4 cup  flour 
 FILLING 
 3/4 cup  Sugar  (confectionary) 
 3/4 cup Crisco 
 6 tbsp  marshmallow  Fluff 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 1 Mix together all "cake" ingredients and drop by teaspoon on cookie sheet. 
2 Bake 15 minutes at 350 deg. 
3  cream  together the "filling" ingredients and spread between 2 of the "cakes". 
4 These will freeze well
