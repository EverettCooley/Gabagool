 Description : 
 Makes: 4 servings, about 1 cup each 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup thawed  Cool Whip  whipped topping 
 3 drops green  food coloring 
 1 cup boiling  water 
 1 pkg. (4-serving size) Jell-O brand X-Treme  watermelon  flavor  gelatin 
 2 cups ice cubes 
 1 square Baker's  semi-sweet baking chocolate , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Mix whipped topping and  food coloring ; spoon evenly into 4 dessert dishes to resemble the rind of a  watermelon . 
 Place in freezer while preparing  gelatin . 
 Stir boiling  water  into dry  gelatin  in large bowl at least 2 minutes until completely dissolved. 
 Add  ice  cubes. 
 Stir 5 minutes or until  gelatin  is slightly thickened. 
 Remove any unmelted  ice . 
 Spoon  gelatin  evenly over whipped topping. 
 Insert  chocolate  pieces into  gelatin  to resemble  watermelon seeds . 
 Refrigerate 30 minutes or until  gelatin  is firm.
