 Description : 
 In Romanian: Tort ieftin II
 
 Ingredients : 
 2  eggs 
 3 tablespoons  sugar 
 1 tablespoon  vinegar 
 ½ teaspoon  baking soda  dissolved in  lemon juice 
 3 tablespoons  oil 
 peel from 1  lemon 
 vanilla 
 flour 
 oil 
 filling and icing 
 Directions : 
 Beat the  eggs  with the  sugar , add a tablespoon of  vinegar  and the  baking soda  dissolved in a little  lemon juice ,  vanilla  and  lemon peel , 3 tablespoons of  oil  and mix everything well. 
 Then knead with  flour  to make a medium soft dough and let rest for 5–10 minutes. 
 From this dough, make a roll which you divide into 6-7 equal parts. 
 Roll each part into a round sheet, the size of a large pan. 
 Grease the pan with a little  oil , set one sheet in and cook on both sides on top of the range, at low temperature. 
 When all sheets are done, spread with the desired filling and glaze like you normally would.
