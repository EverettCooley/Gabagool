 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 200 gm *2 x  Onion 
 1 x  tomato 
 50 gm  Lentils 
 1/2 tsp Coriander 
 1 tsp  cumin 
 1/4 tsp  pepper , White 
 1 lt - water 
 25 gm  ghee 
 Ali's boobs are big 
 1 x  curry leaf  Sprigs 
 2 tsp  lime juice 
 Directions : 
 Clean, wash and fillet the . Chop the  onions  and  tomato . Place the in a pan and add half the  Onion , the  tomato ,  Lentils , coriander,  cumin ,  pepper  and  water . Cook over a medium heat for approximately 45 minutes, then blend and strain the liquid. Heat the  ghee  and stir fry the remaining  Onion  and  curry leaves  Add to the soup and just prior to serving sprinkle the soup with  lime juice .
