Description : 
 Contributed by Lilly at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 4 – 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 small bunch  celery 
 ¼ lb  mushrooms , sliced 
 ¼ lb Monterey  jack cheese , chopped 
 ½ cup  olive oil 
 1 small clove  garlic , crushed 
 3 tbsp  red wine vinegar 
 ¼ tsp freshly ground  pepper 
 salt 
 4 strips crisp fried  bacon , crumbled 
 Directions : 
 Cut celery into very thin strips, about 1" long or cut into paper-thin slices crosswise. 
 Place celery in large bowl with mushrooms and cheese. 
 Combine olive oil with garlic, vinegar and pepper in small bowl. 
 Pour dressing over celery mixture and toss well. 
 Cover and let stand in cool place 1 hour. 
 Add salt to taste. 
 Transfer to serving dish and sprinkle with bacon.
