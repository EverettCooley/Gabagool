 Source: Mccormick 
 4 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 4 (½" thick)  pork chops 
 4 tsp McCormick ®  garlic  herb seasoning blend, divided 
 5 tbsp  olive oil , divided 
 8 cups salad  greens 
 1 cup chopped  tomato 
 ¼ cup thinly sliced  red onion 
 2 tbsp white balsamic or  white wine vinegar 
 Directions : 
 Sprinkle 3 tsp seasoning blend over  pork  chops; set aside. 
 Heat 2 tbsp  olive oil  in lg nonstick skillet on med heat. 
 Add  pork  chops; cook 8 minutes or until desired doneness; turning once. 
 Toss salad greens,  tomato  and  onion  in lg bowl. 
 Mix remaining 3 tbsp olive,  vinegar  and remaining seasoning blend. 
 Pour over salad; toss to coat well. 
 Divide salad among 4 servings plates. 
 Top each with 1  pork  chop. 
 Serve immediately.
