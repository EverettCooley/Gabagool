 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4  bell pepper 
 ½ cup of  rice 
 3  potatoes 
 1 spoon dry  mint 
 2 zucchinis 
 ½ cup of  lemon 
 3  tomato 
 ½ spoon  salt 
 ½  Lamb  meat 
 ½ spoon  black pepper 
 1  cilantro 
 1 spoon of  tomato  paste 
 1  green onions 
 ½ cup  olive oil 
 Directions : 
 Slice the meat,  Onion , cilantro and  tomato  into small pieces and mix all with the  rice ,  mint ,  oil  spices and  lemon . Put the mix in the " bell pepper "  potatoes  and  zucchini . Put the stuffed vegetables in a frying pan, to prevent the steam from going out. Preheat the oven at 250 degrees and cook it for 1 hour. Increase the temp to 350 degrees for the  rice  to be well cooked.
