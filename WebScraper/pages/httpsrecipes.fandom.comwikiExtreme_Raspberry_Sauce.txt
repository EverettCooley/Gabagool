Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Trapper Estate in Garland, Texas in 1987.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 12 ounce bag red  raspberries 
 1 cup  ketchup 
 ½ cup  water 
 5 tablespoons  brown sugar 
 1 teaspoon  tabasco  pepper sauce 
 1 teaspoon  lemon juice 
 ¼ teaspoon  liquid smoke 
 ½ teaspoon  dry mustard 
 ½ teaspoon  chili powder 
 ½ teaspoon  garlic salt 
 dash  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients in blender until smooth. 
 Pour in saucepan then simmer over low heat for 20 minutes. 
 Great on poultry, pork or seafood.
