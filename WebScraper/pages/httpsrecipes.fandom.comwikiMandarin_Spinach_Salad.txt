 Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves 4 
 
 Ingredients : 
 Salad : 
 ½ lb  bacon 
 1 (2½ oz) pkg. sliced  almonds 
 1 (10 oz) pkg. fresh  spinach , washed, stems removed, chilled 
 1 (11 oz) can  mandarin oranges , drained and chilled 
 Dressing : 
 1 cup  catsup 
 1 cup  vegetable oil 
 ⅔ cup  vinegar 
 ⅔ cup  sugar 
 1 small grated  onion , optional 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  celery seed 
 ½ tsp  paprika 
 juice of 1  lemon 
 1  garlic  clove, minced 
 pinch of  dry mustard 
 ½ tsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 Directions : 
 Cook bacon until crisp. 
 Drain, crumble and set aside. 
 Sauté almonds in bacon grease over medium heat until lightly brown. 
 Drain. 
 In a large bowl, toss spinach and oranges. 
 Stir in dressing and almonds. 
 Serve immediately. 
 Enjoy.
