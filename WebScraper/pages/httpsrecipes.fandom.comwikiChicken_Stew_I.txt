 Serves 5 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  chicken breasts , cut in 1 inch cubes 
 1 can fat-free  chicken broth 
 1½ cups  potatoes ; peel, cube 
 ½ cup  onion ; chop 
 ½ cup  celery ; slice 
 ½ cup  carrots ; slice thin 
 ½ tsp  paprika 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 ¼ tsp rubbed  sage 
 ¼ tsp dried  thyme 
 3 oz no-salt-added  tomato paste 
 ¼ cup cold  water 
 2 to 3 tbsp  cornstarch 
 Directions : 
 In a slow cooker, combine the first 11 ingredients; cover and cook on high for 4 hours, or all day on low. 
 Mix  water  and  cornstarch  until smooth; stir into stew. 
 Cook on high until thickened.
