      Norwegian Rutabagas     
 Ingredients : 
 1  bouillon cube 
 ¾ cup (1¾ dl) boiling  water 
 2 large  carrots , sliced 
 1 large  leek , sliced, including the green 
 1½ cups (3½ dl)  rutabagas , cubed 
 ¾ lb. (340 g)  medisterpølse  or  bratwurst 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve bouillon in boiling  water . 
 Add all the vegetables and let simmer 15-20 minutes or until vegetables are barely done. 
 Remove skin from  sausage , cut in thick slices, and add to vegetables. 
 Add  mustard ,  salt  and  pepper , and let is simmer covered for a few more minutes. 
 Do not overcook. 
 Serve with  mustard ,  potatoes , and dark bread.
