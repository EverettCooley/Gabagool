      Cucumber Salad-Swedish Style     
 Ingredients : 
 2 large firm  cucumbers 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 6 tablespoons  sugar 
 â…” cup  white vinegar  or  cider vinegar 
 Directions : 
 Wash  cucumbers , dry off, remove the ends. 
 Score the skins with the tines if a fork, repeat all the way around. 
 Slice  cucumbers  very thin. 
 Place in a bowl, sprinkle with  salt ,  sugar . 
 Add  vinegar , and press slightly with back of a spoon. 
 Cover. 
 Refrigerate overnight. 
 Keeps well in a covered jar.
