Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil  or other 
 1  onion , peeled and chopped 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 ½  piment , minced 
 2½ cups  water 
 1  chicken bouillon  cube (Maggi cube) 
 2 small  potatoes , peeled and diced 
 1 ear fresh  corn  kernel 
 ¼ teaspoon dried  thyme 
 2 tablespoons NIDO ( powdered whole milk ) 
 2 tablespoons  potato flakes 
 fresh  parsley , minced 
 Directions : 
 Sauté  onion ,  garlic  and piment in  oil  until soft. 
 Add 2 cups  water , bouillon cube,  potatoes ,  corn  and  thyme . 
 Cook 10–15 minutes. 
 Add NIDO ,  potato flakes , and ½ cup  water , and cook until thickened, about 5 minutes. 
 Add  parsley ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Thin with more  water  if desired.
