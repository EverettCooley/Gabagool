 Description : 
 This bold side dish is made and cooked with garlic and real potatoes to golden-brown perfection. Vegetable lovers always know it as a red-hot treat with any meal.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb.  red potatoes 
 1 tbsp.  olive oil 
 3 medium  garlic  cloves, crushed or minced 
 2 tsp.  salt 
 2 tsp. fresh, ground  black pepper 
 1 cup melted butter 
 Directions : 
 Wash  potatoes  and cut into 1" cubes. 
 Heat  oil  in a non-stick skillet large enough to hold the  potatoes  in one layer. 
 Add  garlic  and sauté for about 1 minute. 
 Add  potatoes  and sauté golden all sides, about 15 minutes. Sprinkle with a little  salt  and cover. Reduce heat and cook gently for about 10 minutes, or until soft, tossing and stirring from time to time. 
 Remove or keep the  garlic  and season with a little more  salt ,  pepper , and butter to taste. Serve hot and plain or with other side dishes or vegetables. 
 Nutritional information :

 13 mg. sodium
