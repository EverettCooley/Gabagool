Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Utley Estate in Mesquite, Texas in 1988. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  macaroni 
 2  eggs 
 ¾ cup  milk 
 1 cup crumbled  feta  cheese 
 ½ cup sliced  black olives 
 1½ tablespoons  butter 
 ½ bunch  dill 
 ½ teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 ½ teaspoon  crushed pepper 
 ½ teaspoon  thyme 
 1 cup grated  Swiss cheese 
 Directions : 
 Cook macaroni for 20 minutes and rinse well. 
 Mix eggs and milk in a bowl and add feta, olives, oil, parsley, thyme and pepper. 
 Add macaroni to this mix and put them in a casserole and put grated cheese on top. 
 Bake macaroni in preheated 375°F oven for 30 minutes.
