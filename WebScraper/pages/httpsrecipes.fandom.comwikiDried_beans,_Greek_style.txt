 Description : 
 In Romanian: Fasole boabe preparata greceste.
 
 Ingredients : 
 12 oz/350 g  green beans  out of pods, 
 a handful of dill, 
 a handful of  Parsley , 
 a handful of  lovage , 
 10-15  green onions , 
 1 teaspoon  tomato  sauce, 
 4 tablespoons  oil , 
 salt , 
 pepper 
 Directions : 
 Wash and boil the beans in  salt   water . Drain. 
 Fry the finely chopped  Onion  in  oil . 
 When the  Onion  is softened, add the chopped dill,  Parsley ,  lovage  and let fry until softened. 
 Add  tomato  sauce,  salt ,  pepper  and the boiled and drained  green beans . 
 Mix and let fry for a few minutes.
