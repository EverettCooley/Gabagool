WKD Original Vodka  is a brand of  alcopop  produced by Beverage Brands. It comes in several flavours or colour.
 
 WKD Original Vodka Iron Brew ("WKD Orange" or "WKD original") which is orange in colour and Irn-Bru flavour. 
 WKD Original Vodka Blue ("WKD Blue") which is blue in colour and blueberry-flavoured 
 WKD Original Vodka Red ("WKD Red") which is red in colour and cranberry flavoured
