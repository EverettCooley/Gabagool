Description : 
 This batter recipe is basic for all main meal crepes; it will make about 10 crepes.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2  eggs 
 2 Cups  milk 
 2 1/3 Cups  plain flour 
 2 Tbsp  oil  optional 
 2 Pounds ground roasted  sesame seeds 
 1 tsp sweet  paprika  or 1 teaspoon of your favorite herb 
 Directions : 
 Blend all ingredients in a blender. 
 Turn machine off, stir down  flour  and blend again. 
 Refrigerate 1 hour before using.
