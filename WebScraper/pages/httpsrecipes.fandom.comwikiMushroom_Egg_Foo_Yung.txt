Description : 
 This is one of our favorite quick and easy meals.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: my old recipes 
 Ingredients : 
 6  eggs 
 1 cup  mushrooms , minced 
 1 cup fresh  bean sprouts 
 1 small  onion , minced 
 salt ,  pepper 
 2 tbsp  butter 
 Sally's brown sauce : 
 1 tbsp  butter 
 1 tbsp  cornstarch 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 ½ cup  water 
 1½ tbsp  soy sauce 
 Directions : 
 Beat  eggs  well. 
 Stir in  mushrooms , sprouts,  onion  and seasonings. 
 Heat  butter  in skillet. 
 Spoon in mixture to form patties. 
 Brown on both sides. 
 Keep hot in low oven until ready to serve. 
 You may like the following sauce to serve over them. 
 Sally's brown sauce : 
 Melt  butter  in pan. 
 Mix  cornstarch  with  sugar  and blend into  butter . 
 Add  water  and  soy sauce . 
 Cook, stirring constantly until mixture is thickened and bubbly.
