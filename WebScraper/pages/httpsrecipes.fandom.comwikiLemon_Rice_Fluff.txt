 Description : 
 Makes 8 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 3-ounce package Cook 'n Serve  lemon  pudding and pie filling mix 
 1/3 plus 1/4 cup  Sugar , divided 
 2 cups  water 
 1  egg , beaten 
 1/2 cup  heavy cream 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 2 cups cooked  rice 
 whipped cream  for garnish (optional) 
 lemon  twist for garnish (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Combine pudding mix, 1/3 cup  Sugar ,  egg  and  water  in medium saucepan. 
 Cook, stirring constantly, over medium heat until thickened, about 10 to 12 minutes. 
 Cool. Whip  cream  with  vanilla  and remaining 1/4 cup  Sugar . 
 Fold  rice  and  whipped cream  into pudding. 
 Pour into serving dishes and chill. 
 Garnish with  whipped cream  and  lemon  twist, if desired.
