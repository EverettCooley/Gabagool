 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 (15 ounce) cans  pinto beans ,drained and rinsed 
 1 cup salsa, divided 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 2 tablespoons chopped fresh  cilantro 
 1 (15 ounce) can  black beans , rinsed and drained 
 1/2 cup chopped  tomatoes 
 7 (8 inch)  flour  tortillas 
 2 cups shredded reduced-fat 
 Cheddar   Cheese 
 1 cup salsa 
 1/2 cup  sour cream 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). 
 In a large bowl, mash  pinto beans . Stir in 3/4 cup salsa and  garlic . 
 In a separate bowl, mix together 1/4 cup salsa,  cilantro ,  black beans  and  tomatoes . 
 Place 1 tortilla in a pie plate or tart dish. Spread 3/4 cup  pinto bean  mixture over tortilla to within 1/2 inch of edge. 
 Top with 1/4 cup  Cheese , and cover with another tortilla. 
 Spread with 2/3 cup  black bean  mixture, and top with 1/4 cup  Cheese . 
 Repeat layering twice. Cover with remaining tortilla, and spread with remaining  pinto bean  mixture and  Cheese . 
 Cover with foil, and bake in preheated oven for about 40 minutes. 
 Cut into wedges, and serve with salsa and  sour cream .
