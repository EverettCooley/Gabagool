 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 2/3 cup extra virgin olive or  flax seed   oil 
 1/3 cup  lemon juice  or  red wine vinegar 
 1 1/2 tsp dried  tarragon 
 1 tsp  maple syrup  or  brown rice syrup 
 1 clove minced  garlic 
 Directions : 
 Place all ingredients in a jar with a tight-fitting lid and shake well to combine. Refrigerate unused portions. 
 
 
 Serves 4-6
