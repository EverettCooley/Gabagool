Ingredients : 
 6 cup  flour 
 1 tsp  cayenne 
 2 tbsp  salt 
 1 tsp  white pepper 
 6 cups  beer 
 12  eggs 
 12  egg yolks 
 2 tbsp  Dijon mustard 
 ⅓ cup  sour cream 
 ⅓ cup  unsalted butter 
 ⅓ cup  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Sift  flour ,  cayenne ,  salt , and  pepper  together. 
 Add beer-mix well. 
 Add  eggs ,  egg  yolks, and Dijon mustard-mix well. 
 Add  sour cream ,  butter , and  parsley . 
 Prepare as for crepes or pancakes. 
 Serve warm, with seafood or as desired.
