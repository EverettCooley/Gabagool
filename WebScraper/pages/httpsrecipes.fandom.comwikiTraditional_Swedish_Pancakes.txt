       Traditional Swedish Pancakes     
 Ingredients : 
 3  eggs 
 2½ cups low-fat  milk 
 1¼ cups  flour 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 3 tablespoons  butter , melted 
 Directions : 
 Beat the  eggs  with half the  milk . 
 Beat in  flour  and  salt . 
 Stir in melted  butter  and remaining  milk . 
 Heat a griddle with  vegetable oil . 
 Pour about ¼ cup batter onto the griddle and cook over medium heat 1 to 2 minutes. 
 Turn the pancakes and cook about ½ minute. 
 Serve immediately.
