 Description : 
 Makes 4 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x 10¾ ounce can condensed  cream of chicken  soup 
 ½ cup grated  cheddar  cheese 
 1½ cups chopped cooked  chicken 
 2 cups cooked  rice 
 ½ cup  sour cream 
 ¼ cup chopped fresh  parsley 
 ¼ cup chopped  pimentos 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon seasoned  pepper 
 ½ of 3-ounce can  rice noodles  or  chow mein noodles 
 Directions : 
 Combine soup and  cheese ; mix well. 
 Add  chicken ,  rice ,  sour cream ,  parsley ,  pimentos ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Pour into buttered shallow 1½-quart casserole. 
 Sprinkle  rice noodles  over casserole. 
 Bake at 350°F for 20 to 25 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
