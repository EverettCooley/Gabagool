 Makes 2 cups 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup cracked  ice 
 2 oz  white rum 
 2 tbsp  coconut cream  (below) 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 Coconut cream : 
 2 cups  cream , heated 
 1 cup  flaked coconut 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients in blender and frappe until smooth and foamy. 
 Serve with straw. 
 Coconut cream : 
 Pour cream over  coconut  and let stand 30 minutes. 
 Strain through fine sieve. 
 Chill.
