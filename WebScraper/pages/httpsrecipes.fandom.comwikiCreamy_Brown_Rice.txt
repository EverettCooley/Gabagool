 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 cup  water 
 1/2 cup 2% lowfat  milk 
 1 tbsp  margarine  or  butter 
 1/4 tsp  salt 
 1 cup  brown rice 
 jam 
 Directions : 
 In medium saucepan, bring  water ,  milk ,  margarine  and  salt  to a boil. 
 Stir in  rice  and bring to a boil again. 
 Cook, covered, 45 to 50 minutes or until liquid is absorbed and  rice  is tender. 
 Spoon into bowls and top with a generous spoonful of  Honey   peach  Topping.
