Info : 
 Cook Time : 
 Serves : 6 to 8
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 quarts butter lettuce or other salad greens 
 1/4 cup lemon juice, freshly squeezed preferred 
 1 tablespoon sugar 
 1/2 teaspoon salt 
 dash white pepper 
 1/4 cup heavy cream 
 Directions : 
 1.Gently wash and dry leaf lettuce. This is a tender lettuce.
 2.Tear lettuce into bite-size pieces.
 3.Place in refrigerator to chill.
 4.Whisk together lemon juice, sugar, salt and pepper.
 5.Whisk in heavy cream, a bit at a time, until creamy.
 6.Drizzle over lettuce leaves. 
 7.Toss gently.
 8.Serve immediately.
 10.Serves 6 to 8.
 12.Note: This salad sings spring and tastes so good after eating winter veggies.
