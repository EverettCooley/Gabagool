Description : 
 Mithai Ka masala  is the Hindi name for a delicious addition to Indian desserts. This very tasty blend can be used over  rice  pudding, tapioca, or burfe.
 
 Recipe makes 1 – 2 cups 
 Ingredients : 
 25  green cardamom  pods 
 ¼ cup  cashew  pieces 
 ¾ cup  pistachios 
 ½ cup  almonds 
 seeds of 1  black cardamom 
 Directions : 
 Grind ingredients together in spice grinder. 
 Store in air-tight container. 
 keep refrigerated , lasts 2 months.
