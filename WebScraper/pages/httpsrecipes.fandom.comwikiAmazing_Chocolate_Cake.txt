Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Morgan Estate in Austin, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2½ cups  flour 
 2 cups  granulated sugar 
 6 tablespoons unsweetened  cocoa powder 
 2 teaspoons  baking soda 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ½ cup cooking  oil 
 2 teaspoons  vanilla 
 2 tablespoons  vinegar 
 2 cups ice cold water 
 Directions : 
 Grease rectangular cake pan. 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 Sift together flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda and salt. 
 Combine oil, vanilla, vinegar and water. 
 Pour over dry ingredients and mix until smooth. 
 Pour mix into pan and bake for 35 minutes.
