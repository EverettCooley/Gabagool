Description : 
 Source: "Cooking Light, Nov/Dec 1994, page 145" Copyright: "© Cooking Light"
 
 Serving Size: 1 cup Yield: 17 cups 
 Ingredients : 
 12 cups  cranberry juice  cocktail 
 4 cups  orange juice 
 1 cup  lemon juice 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 1 tablespoon grated  orange rind 
 12 whole  cloves 
 6  cinnamon  sticks -- (3-inch) 
 Directions : 
 Combine all the ingredients in a large Dutch oven, and bring to a boil. 
 Reduce heat, and simmer for 5 minutes. 
 Strain the juice mixture, and discard spices.
