Contributed by  The Bartending School       Avocado Daiquiri     
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ medium  avocado 
 ¾ oz. fresh  lime juice 
 1 cup crushed  ice 
 1 ½ oz.  light rum 
 1  lime  wedge 
 Directions : 
 Crush  ice  in a blender. 
 Add  rum ,  lime juice , and ¼ medium  avocado  in a blender. 
 Blend until thoroughly mixed and smooth. 
 Pour into glass and garnish with the  lime  wedge. Serve cold. 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Servings:  2 cups per glass or mug
