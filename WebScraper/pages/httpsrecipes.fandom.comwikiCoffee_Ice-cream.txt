Description : 
 In Romanian: Inghetata de cafea
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 qt / 1 l  milk 
 14 oz / 400 g  sugar 
 1.5 cups / 1,5 cups concentrated and strained  coffee 
 10 oz / 300 g  whipped cream 
 vanilla 
 4  yolks  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Boil the  milk  with a little  vanilla  stick. 
 Remove from heat and mix with  sugar  and  coffee . 
 After it has cooled off, add the  whipped cream , mix well and freeze. 
 Serve with a little  whipped cream . 
 If using  egg yolks , follow the  Vanilla Ice-cream  procedure.
