Ingredients : 
 1 ripe  mango  cut in ½" cubes 
 1½ cups  pineapple , coarsely chopped 
 ¼ cup  red onion , sliced paper-thin 
 1  lime , juice only 
 2  jalapenos , skinned, seeded and minced 
 8  basil  leaves, shredded 
 5 shakes  hot sauce 
 Directions : 
 Toss ingredients together gently in a bowl and serve immediately.
