 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup pasteurized American  process cheese  spread 
 1/2 cup  milk 
 1/4 cup  catsup 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 12-ounce package frankfurters, cut into 1-inch pieces 
 1 14-1/2 to 16-ounce can  tomatoes , crushed and drained (2 cups) 
 1/4 cup finely chopped  Onion 
 2 tablespoons chopped fresh  parsley 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine  Cheese  spread,  milk ,  catsup ,  salt  and  pepper  in small saucepan. Heat on low until well blended. 
 
 Combine  Cheese  sauce with  rice , frankfurters,  tomatoes  and  Onion . Turn into buttered shallow 2-quart baking dish. Bake at 350 degrees 25 to 30 minutes, or until heated through. Sprinkle with  parsley .
