Baked  onions 
 
 4 Large  onions 
 1 Oz.  Swiss cheese 
 3/4 Cup  light cream  or  milk 
 salt 
 pepper  to Taste
 
 4 T. Dry  sherry  (optional) 
 
Peel And Slice  onions . Put Them in a Greased Ovenproof Dish. Preheat Oven to 350 First. Grate or Dice The  Swiss cheese . Mix With The Cream or  milk  And if Desired, The  sherry . Pour Over The  onions . Season. Bake For 30 Minutes at 350. Serves 4Source: my Old Recipes
