Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Bivins Estate in Dallas, Texas in 1990. Tried and true. This is fabulous. I make it at least once a month for my neighbor who is a die hard chocolate fanatic.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2½ tablespoons  cocoa 
 1 cup  butter 
 1 cup water 
 2 cups  sugar 
 2 cups  flour 
 ½ cup  buttermilk 
 2  eggs  beaten well 
 1 teaspoon  baking soda 
 1 teaspoon  cinnamon 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 Icing : 
 4¼ cups  powdered sugar 
 ½ cup  butter 
 ⅓ cup  cocoa 
 ⅓ cup  milk 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 ¼ cup  pecans 
 Directions : 
 Put cocoa, butter and water in a large pan and bring to a boil. 
 Let cool slightly. 
 Beat eggs then add buttermilk, sugar and vanilla while beating. 
 Mix dry ingredients. 
 Add everything to large pan with cocoa mixture and mix well. 
 Pour into a sheet cake pan and bake at 400°F for 20 minutes. 
 Icing : 
 For icing beat and soften butter then slowly add remaining ingredients. 
 Add nuts and put icing on cake.
