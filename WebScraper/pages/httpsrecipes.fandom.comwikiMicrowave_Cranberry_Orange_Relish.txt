 Description : 
 Serve in sandwiches or with roast  chicken ,  ham ,  turkey .
 
 Preparation 10 minutes 
 Makes 2¼ cups 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups  cranberries 
 1 small unpeeled  orange , seeded, chopped 
 1 med unpeeled  apple , cored, chopped 
 ⅓ cup  water 
 ¼ cup  granulated sugar 
 ¼ tsp  ground ginger 
 ¼ tsp ground  nutmeg 
 Directions : 
 In food processor or blender container, process  cranberries ,  orange  and  apple  until finely chopped. 
 Place in shallow microwavable casserole. 
 Stir in  water ,  sugar ,  ginger  and  nutmeg . 
 Microwave on high for 3 to 4 minutes; stir. 
 Microwave on medium 50% power for 2-3 minutes. 
 Cool slightly. 
 Cover and refrigerate up to 2 weeks. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per ¼ cup serving:
 
 45 calories | 12g carbohydrate | 0g protein | 0g fat | 2g fibre 
 Exchanges: 1 fruits & vegetables 
 Per 1 tbsp serving:
 
 11 calories | 3g carbohydrate | 1g fibre 1 ++ extra
