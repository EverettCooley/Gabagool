 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Mom Marcon's Recipes 
 Ingredients : 
 egg yolks 
 for each egg yolk, add the following:
 3 Tbsp  vinegar 
 1 Tbsp  butter 
 2 Tbsp  prepared mustard 
 1 tbsp sugar 
 1 tsp salt 
 1 can  evaporated milk 
 Directions : 
 Mix together in small pan and cook over low heat, stirring constantly until thick. 
 Remove from fire, add 1 small can pet milk  [1] 
 Stir well and store in refrigerator.
