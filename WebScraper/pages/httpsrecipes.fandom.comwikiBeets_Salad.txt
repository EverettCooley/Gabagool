 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 medium beets 
 2 cloves minced  garlic 
 1/2 cup finely chopped  walnuts 
 3 table spoons  Mayonnaise 
 1 table spoon  lemon juice 
 salt 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Boil the beets, peel them. Grate the cooled beets and mix with the rest of the ingredients. 
 Let stand for one hour to mix the flavors. 
 Serve with the country style  bread  or as a salad with the main dish.
