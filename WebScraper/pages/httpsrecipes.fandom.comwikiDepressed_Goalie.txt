Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 3½ oz Jim Beam®  Bourbon whiskey 
 2 oz Pepsi® cola 
 2 oz Mountain Dew® citrus soda 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Fill a mason jar with  ice . Pour over the  whiskey  (Jim Beam). 
 Then add the Pepsi and Mountain Dew. Mix if desired.
