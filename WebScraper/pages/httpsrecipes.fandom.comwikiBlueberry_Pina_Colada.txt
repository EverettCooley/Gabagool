Ingredients : 
 2 ounces of rum 
 1/2 cup pineapple 
 2 tablespoons of coconut cream 
 1/4 cup blueberries 
 1 teaspoon of agave 
 1/2 lime, juiced 
 1 cup of ice 
 Garnish: Blueberries and pineapple
 
 Instructions : 
 Fill blender with rum, pineapple, coconut cream, agave nectar, blueberries, lime juice and ice 
 Blend mixture then pour into serving glass
