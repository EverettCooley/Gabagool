Description : 
 Cream of shrimp and cheese. May be used for canapés, finger sandwiches, or dip.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: My old recipes 
 Makes about 1 quart 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pkg.  cream cheese  (8 oz) 
 2 cups cleaned cooked  shrimp 
 1 tsp grated  onion 
 2 drops  Worcestershire 
 3 drops  hot sauce 
 2 tbsp  lemon juice 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ½ cup  whipping cream 
 Directions : 
 Put all ingredients except whipping cream in blender container and blend 30 seconds. 
 Pour blended contents into a bowl. 
 Whip cream and fold into shrimp mixture. 
 Check salt. Add more if needed.
