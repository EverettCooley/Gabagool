Description : 
 This marinade is especially good on  fish  steaks and fillets.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 From the Recipe Ross Newsletter 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  lemon juice 
 4 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 1 tsp  dill weed 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Combine ingredients in a shallow dish.
