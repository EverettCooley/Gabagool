Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Fix it and Forget it Slow Cooker Magic 
 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  fat-free  or  2% milk 
 2 tbsp  honey  or ¼ cup  brown sugar 
 1 tbsp  margarine 
 ¼ tbsp  salt 
 ½ tbsp  salt 
 ½ tbsp ground  cinnamon 
 1 cup dry regular  oats 
 1 cup chopped  apples 
 ½ cup  raisins  (optional) 
 ¼ cup  walnuts , chopped 
 ½ cup fat-free  half and half 
 Directions : 
 Spray inside of slow cooker with  cooking spray . 
 In a mixing bowl, combine all ingredients except half and half. 
 Pour into cooker. 
 Cover and cook on low 6 to 8 hours or overnight. 
 oatmeal  is ready to eat in morning. 
 Stir in half and half just before serving.
