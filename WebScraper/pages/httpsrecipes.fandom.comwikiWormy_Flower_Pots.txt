 Description : 
 This snack-dessert will make kids go “Cool!” and dig in.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 
 2 cups  milk 
 1 pack instant  chocolate  pudding 
 3 1/2 cups  Cool Whip 
 16 oz Oreos 
 10 gummy worms 
 10 plastic flowers (optional) 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix  milk  and  chocolate  pudding in a bowl and beat for 3-5 minutes or until they are well blended. 
 Let it stand for another 5 minutes. Break Oreos in small pieces and set aside. 
 Add the  Cool Whip  and half of the Oreos in the pudding mix. 
 To make the wormy flower pots, put 1 tablespoon of the crushed Oreo in the bottom of a cup. 
 Fill the cup about 3/4 full with the pudding mixture. Sprinkle the top with a bit more Oreo crumbs. 
 Stick a plastic flower in the middle. Place a gummy worm or two on top. Repeat process for the other cups.
