 Ingredients : 
 3 c.  Potatoes , cubed or sliced [Idaho] 
 3  Onions , sliced - divided in half 
 3 lb.  Pork Roast , trimmed 
 8  garlic  cloves, minced 
 1 c.  Water 
 1/2 c.  Ketchup 
 3 T.  Vinegar 
 2 T.  Brown sugar 
 2 T.  Soy sauce 
 2 t.  Dijon mustard 
 1 t.  Pepper 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Arrange potatoes in slow cooker. 
 Place half of onions on top of potatoes. 
 Place pork on top of onions 
 Spread garlic all over. 
 Top w/remaining onions. 
 Whisk together water, ketchup, vinegar, sugar, soy, mustard, pepper; pour over pork. 
 Cover and cook on LOW for 6-8 hours or on HIGH for 3-4 hours. 
 Slice crosswise into thin slices. 
 Notes : 
 Serves: 4
