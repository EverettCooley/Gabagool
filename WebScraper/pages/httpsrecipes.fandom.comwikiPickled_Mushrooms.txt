Ingredients : 
 5 kg edible!  mushrooms 
 1 cup  salt 
 1 leaf of  black currant 
 1 leaf of  horseradish 
 5  garlic  cloves 
 pepper 
 Directions : 
 Sort out the  mushrooms , wash thoroughly, soak for 2 3 days in cold  water  changing it many times and keeping the  mushrooms  in a cold room. 
 Place the soaked  mushrooms  into a prepared wooden or glass vessel in rows, with their stems up, interspersing them with spices and  salt . 
 Put a round wooden cover and a load over it. 
 Leave for a week or two before eating.
