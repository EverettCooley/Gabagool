      Apple Salad     
 8 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 large  apples , diced and pared 
 ½ cup  celery , diced 
 ¼ cup  raisins 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 1 tablespoon  mayonnaise , fat-free 
 8  lettuce  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients except  lettuce . 
 Mix well. 
 Refrigerate until ready to serve. 
 Serve on  lettuce  leaves. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per ¼ cup serving: 
 
 Exchanges: 1 fruit
