 Description : 
 Makes 4 servings
 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup cut fresh or frozen  okra 
 3/4 cup chopped  Onion 
 3/4 cup diced green  pepper 
 2 teaspoons  margarine 
 1 1/2 cups chopped  tomatoes 
 1 1/3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 1/3 cups cooked  Chicken  cubes 
 1 tablespoon chopped fresh  cilantro 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1/8 to 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper 
 3 cups fat-free  chicken broth 
 
 Directions : 
 Cook  okra ,  Onion  and green  pepper  until tender crisp in  margarine  in large saucepan over medium-high heat. Add  tomatoes ,  rice ,  Chicken ,  cilantro ,  garlic , red pepper and broth. Bring to a boil; simmer, covered, 20 to 25 minutes.
