 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 4 overripe  bananas 
 1 teaspoon minced  Scotch bonnet -type chile 
 1 cup  chicken stock 
 2 cups  water 
 1 cup  heavy cream 
 curlicues of  red  and  yellow bell pepper  for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Peel the  bananas  and cut them into pieces. 
 Put the  bananas  and the  chile  through a food mill until pureed. 
 Place the banana- chile  pur√©e,  chicken stock , and  water  in a saucepan and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
 Add the heavy  cream , bring to a boil, and remove from the heat. 
 Serve hot with the curlicues of  red  and  yellow bell pepper  for garnish.
