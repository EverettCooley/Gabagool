Baked  Chicken  on  pecan  Stuffing
 
 1 Small  Onion , Chopped 
 2 Ribs  celery , Chopped 
 1  Apple , Peeled And Diced 
 3 Tbs  butter 
 1 Tsp  salt 
 1/2 Tsp Plus 1/8 Tsp Dried  sage 
 1/2 Tsp  pepper 
 3/4 Pound Stale  Italian bread , Cubed 
 3/4 Cup  chicken broth 
 1  egg , Beaten 
 2/3 Cup Chopped  pecans 
 1/2 Cup  Raisins 
 4  Chicken  Breast Halves 
 1/4 Cup  Apple  Jelly 
 1/8 Tsp  cinnamon 
 
Cook  Onion ,  celery ,  Apple  in  butter  in Skillet 5 minutes. Add 1/2 Tsp Each  salt ,  sage ,  pepper . Mix in Bowl With  bread . Drizzle With  chicken broth ,  egg . Stir in 1/2 Cup Nuts,  Raisins . Spread in 13x9x2 Inch Pan. Top With  Chicken  And Sprinkle With Remaining  salt . Bake in 400 Degree Oven 30 minutes. Melt Jelly, Remaining  sage ,  cinnamon  in Saucepan, Brush on  Chicken . Top With Remaining Nuts. Bake 15 More Minutes. Makes 4 Servings.*
