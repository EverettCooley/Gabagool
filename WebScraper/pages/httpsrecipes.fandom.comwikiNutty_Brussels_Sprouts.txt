Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Source:  Light And Easy Diabetes Cuisine 
 Yield: 4 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb small  Brussels sprouts 
 1 tsp virgin  olive oil 
 8 toasted  hazelnuts  or  almonds , chopped 
 ⅛ tsp ground  cardamom 
 Directions : 
 Wash  Brussels sprouts  and trim off bottoms of stems and loose leaves. 
 Steam sprouts over boiling  water  until tender, 7 to 10 minutes. 
 Remove sprouts to a serving bowl and stir in  olive oil , nuts and  cardamom . 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving (¼ of recipe):
 
 62 Calories | 3g Fat | 22mg Sodium | 3g Protein | 9.1g Carbs | 0mg Cholesterol 
 Diabetic exchanges: 1½ vegetable | ½ fat
