Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Day Estate in Fort Worth, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ pound fresh  snap beans 
 3 tablespoons  bacon drippings 
 5  new potatoes  soaked in water with a little soda then scraped 
 1 small  white onion  chopped 
 1 teaspoon  sugar 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  black pepper 
 1 cup boiling  water 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in a small stew pan then cover with a tight fitting lid and cook over medium heat for 5 minutes. 
 Reduce heat and cook 15 minutes longer.
