Ingredients : 
 1 can  sweetened condensed milk 
 Directions : 
 Get a large pot with a metal rack. 
 Place the can inside the pot on the metal rack. 
 Add  water  to completely cover the can, plus 2 – 3 inches. 
 Simmer for 3 – 4 hours. 
 The can must always be completely covered in  water .  Caution : if the can becomes uncovered there could be an explosion. 
 Let the can cool for 3 – 4 hours. 
 Open the can and enjoy!
