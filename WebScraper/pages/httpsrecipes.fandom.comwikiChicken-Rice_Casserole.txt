Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 2 cups diced cooked  chicken 
 1½ cups 6 ounces shredded  Cheddar  cheese, divided 
 ½ cup sliced  green onions 
 ¾ cup  milk 
 2  eggs , beaten 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  chicken , ¾ cup  cheese  and  onions . 
 Combine  milk ,  eggs  and  salt  in small bowl. 
 Pour  rice  mixture into greased 2-quart baking dish; top with  egg  mixture. Bake at 350 °F for 15 to 20 minutes. 
 Top with remaining ¾ cup  cheese . 
 Bake an additional 5 to 10 minutes or until  cheese  is melted.
