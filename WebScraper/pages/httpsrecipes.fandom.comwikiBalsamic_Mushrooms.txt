Description : 
 Merlot  goes great with these mushrooms.
 
 Serves: 4 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons + 1¼ teaspoons  olive oil 
 1½ cloves  garlic , minced 
 ½ pound fresh  mushrooms , sliced 
 1 tablespoon + 1½ teaspoons  balsamic vinegar 
 1 tablespoon + 1½ teaspoons  white wine  (or  red wine , your choice) 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Saute the  garlic  in  olive oil  for 1 to 2 minutes. 
 Do not brown the garlic. 
 Add  mushrooms  and cook 2 more minutes, stirring occasionally. 
 Stir in  balsamic vinegar  and  wine , cook another 2 minutes. 
 Season to taste with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Calories: 94 | Total Fat: 8.3 g | Cholesterol: 0 mg
