 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 lbs  spinach 
 1 tbsp  butter 
 4 tbsp  cream 
 x  salt  and  pepper 
 x ground  nutmeg  for seasoning 
 Directions : 
 Wash and clean the  spinach . Fill a large pot with  water , then bring to a boil. Add  salt  or  Chicken  bouillon cube to give the liquid a light flavor. Add the  spinach  and boil for 5 minutes. Remove the  spinach  and drain. Heat  butter  in a frying pan, then add the  spinach  and saute. Add half of the  cream  and mix well. The liquid from the boiled  spinach  will come out - let the  spinach  simmer in the liquid for about 8-12 minutes. Remove from heat, then add the remaining  cream  to the  spinach . Season with  salt ,  pepper , and a dash of ground  nutmeg .
