Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Ritchie Estate in Balch Springs, Texas in 1984.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ stick  butter 
 2 cups  baking mix 
 1  egg 
 8 ounce can creamed  corn 
 8 ounce corn whole kernel  corn 
 2 cups  asiago  cheese shredded 
 2 large pickled  jalapenos  diced 
 2 cups shredded sharp  cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Melt butter in square baking dish. 
 Mix baking mix, egg, jalapenos, cheese and corn together then dump into melted butter. 
 Bake at 350°F for 25 minutes.
