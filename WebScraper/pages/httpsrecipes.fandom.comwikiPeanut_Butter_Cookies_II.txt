Description : 
 Yes, these simple ingredients create a dynamite peanut butter cookie! 
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Original recipe 
 30 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup crunchy  peanut butter 
 ½ cup  light brown sugar 
 1  egg 
 ½ teaspoon  baking soda 
 ¼ cup chopped  peanuts 
 Directions : 
 Preheat the oven to 350°F. 
 In a large bowl, combine the peanut butter, sugar, egg, and baking soda until well combined. 
 Stir in the peanuts. 
 Place dough by teaspoonfuls on a nonstick baking sheet and press down with a fork to form ridges. 
 Bake for 12 – 14 minutes or until lightly browned. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per 1 cookie serving: 
 
 Calories 76 (Calories from Fat 45) | Total Fat 5g (Saturated Fat 1g) | Cholesterol 7mg | Sodium 57mg | Total Carbohydrate 6g (Dietary Fiber  1g, Sugars  4g) | Protein 2 g 
 Exchanges: ½ carbohydrate | 1 fat
