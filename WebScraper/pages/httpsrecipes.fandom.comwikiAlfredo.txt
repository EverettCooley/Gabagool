      Alfredo     
 About Alfredo sauce : 
 This is made from heavy cream, butter, grated Parmesan cheese, pepper, parsley and minced garlic and occasionally nutmeg, used with fettuccine pasta to create the rich ' fettucine alfredo ' dish.
 White wine is also now used in this popular sauce created by Alfredo di Lello.
