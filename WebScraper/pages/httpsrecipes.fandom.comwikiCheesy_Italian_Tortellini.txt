Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 6/24/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  ground beef  cooked and drained 
 1 lb  sausage 
 1 (16 oz) jar  spaghetti  or  marinara sauce 
 1 small can  mushrooms 
 2 (9 oz) pkg. refrigerated cheese  tortellini 
 1 cup shredded  mozzarella  cheese 
 ½ cup shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 350°F. 
 Crumble the ground beef and sausage into a large skillet. 
 Cook over medium high heat until browned. 
 Drain. 
 Combine the meats, marinara sauce and mushrooms. 
 Sprinkle cheeses on top. 
 Cover and cook at 350°F for 25 minutes.
