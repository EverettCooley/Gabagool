 Prep Time: 1 hour 
 Cook time: 20 - 30 minutes 
 Serves: 4 
 
 Description : 
 A nutritious, healthy meal featuring turkey seasoned with some simple, yet tasty, ingredients. Serve with a loaf of crusty  ciabatta  bread (or whatever you have on hand) and a good red wine.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb lean  ground turkey  (93/7 blend) 
 5 oz smoked turkey bacon, chopped 
 2  garlic  cloves, crushed 
 1  onion , finely chopped 
 2  carrots , diced 
 2 tbsp  tomato paste 
 1¼ cups  chicken stock 
 8 oz  rigatoni  or  penne  pasta 
 2 tbsp grated  Parmesan cheese 
 salt  and black  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Brown the turkey in a nonstick saucepan, breaking up any large pieces until the meat is well-browned all over. 
 Add the chopped turkey bacon, garlic, onion, carrots, paste, stock and seasoning. Bring to a boil, cover and simmer for 1 hour, until all the vegetables are tender. 
 Preheat the oven to 350 °F. Cook the pasta in a large pot of boiling, salted water according to the package instructions, until al dente. Drain the pasta thoroughly and mix with the turkey sauce. 
 Transfer to a shallow ovenproof dish and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese. Bake for 20 - 30 minutes, until lightly browned on top.
