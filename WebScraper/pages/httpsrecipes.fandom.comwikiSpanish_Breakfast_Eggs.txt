Description : 
 This is a recipe I obtained from an Estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Manes Estate in Venus, Texas in 1981.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4  eggs 
 4  tortillas 
 1 cup Ranchero  salsa 
 4 tablespoons grated sharp  cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Fry tortillas in small amount of oil. 
 Fry eggs sunny side up. 
 Place the tortillas on a plate and add the fried eggs. 
 Pour salsa over the eggs then sprinkle with grated cheese.
