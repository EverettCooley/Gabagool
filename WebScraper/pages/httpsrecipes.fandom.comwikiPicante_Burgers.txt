 Ingredients : 
 1Â lb. ground  Beef 
 1/2 cup Pace Picante Sauce or Chunky Salsa* 
 4 hamburger rolls 
 Nachos 
 Directions : 
 Mix thoroughly  Beef  and picante sauce.  
Shape firmly into 4 patties, 1/2" thick. 
Grill patties 10 minutes. or until done, turning once and brushing often with additional picante sauce. 
Serve on rolls with additional picante sauce and Nachos.
 
 Makes 4 servings 
 Nachos : 
 Top tortilla chips with shredded  Cheddar   Cheese  and microwave until  Cheese  melts. Serve with Pace Chunky Salsa.
 
 Tip: For a unique bold taste, try with Pace Chipotle, Grande  garlic  or  lime  &  garlic  Chunky Salsas.
