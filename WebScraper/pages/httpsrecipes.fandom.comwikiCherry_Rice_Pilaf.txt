Makes 8 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup chopped  Onion 
 1 cup chopped  celery 
 1/2 cup  dried cherries 
 1/2 cup California  walnuts , chopped 
 1 tablespoon chopped fresh  thyme * 
 1 tablespoon chopped fresh  marjoram * 
 1/2 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Onion ,  celery ,  cherries ,  walnuts ,  thyme ,  marjoram  and  pepper  in  butter  in a large non-stick skillet over medium heat 10 minutes, or until vegetables are tender. 
 Add  rice ; cook until thoroughly heated.
