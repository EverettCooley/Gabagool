Description 
 Ginger, Melon and Pear Beverage  from the  Public Health Cookbook  by the Seattle & King County Department of Public Health, public domain government resource
 
 Serves : 2 
 Ingredients 
 Â½  cantaloupe  (or other type of melon, if preferred), seeded 
 2 ripe  pears , seeded and stemmed 
 1 inch fresh  ginger , minced 
 Directions 
 Using a spoon or melon-baller, scoop out the melon then place in a blender or juicer. 
 Coarsely chop pears and place in blender or juicer. 
 Add ginger and blend until smooth or to desired consistency then pour into a glass and serve. 
 Tip 
 If using a blender, you may consider straining the juice in a sieve or filter to remove any excess bits of fruit and ginger for a smoother beverage.
