Ingredients : 
 4  chicken  breasts 
 4 slices  provolone  cheese 
 carrot  sticks 
 spinach 
 1 can flavored diced  tomatoes  (any will do) 
 tablespoon  oil 
 Directions : 
 Put provolone cheese, spinach, and carrot stick on chicken breasts. 
 Roll and secure with toothpicks. 
 Brown in frying pan with oil for 5 minutes. 
 Add can of flavored diced tomatoes. 
 Cover and simmer for 20 minutes. 
 Serve and enjoy.
