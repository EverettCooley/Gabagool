Description : 
       Uunipuuro     
 Oven porridge. Also  whole oats ,  wheat ,  rice ,  buckwheat  or  millet  make excellent porridge.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2½ dl  pearl barley 
 ½ l  water 
 1½ l  milk 
 1 tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Combine all the ingredients in a buttered oven dish. 
 Bake the porridge for 4 – 5 hours at 125°C. 
 Serve the porridge with  milk  or fruit sauce.
