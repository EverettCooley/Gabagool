White Fish on Black-eyed peas (Senegal).  L'Assiette des Assiettes  means "the dish of dishes." In Senegal the term is used to refer to the appetizer of appetizers
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 bed of  lettuce 
 3 Tbs. cooked  Black-Eyed Peas 
 3-inch square piece cooked fish ( Haddock  or  Halibut ) on the  Peas 
 2  tomato  slices 
 3  cucumber  slices 
 1 or 2 pieces  hearts of palm 
 1/2 hard-boiled  egg 
 bottle tomato  catsup 
 1 tsp.  worcestershire sauce 
 1/2 cup  white vinegar 
 3 Tbs.  sugar 
 1/2 cup  onion , chopped finely 
 Directions : 
 Arrange 1 bed of  lettuce . 
 Place 3 Tbs. cooked black-eyed peas in center of plate. 
 Place a 3-inch square piece cooked fish ( Haddock  or  Halibut ) on the  Peas . 
 Surround with: tomato, cucumber, hearts of palm, and egg 
 Mask the fish with the following dressing (enough for 8 portions): 
 Combine tomato catsup,  worcestershire sauce ,  white vinegar ,  Sugar , and  onion 
 Chill thoroughly before serving.
