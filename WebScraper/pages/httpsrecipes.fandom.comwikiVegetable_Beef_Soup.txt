 Ingredients : 
 2 teaspoons cooking  oil 
 1/2Â lb lean ground  Beef 
 1 (14 ounce) can whole  tomatoes , broken up, with juice 
 10 ounces frozen mixed vegetables 
 1 cup  onions , chopped 
 4 cups  water 
 1 tablespoon  Beef  bouillon powder 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1/4 teaspoon  pepper 
 1/4 teaspoon  thyme , ground 
 Directions : 
 1. Heat cooking  oil  over medium heat in large saucepan and add ground  Beef .
 2. Scramble fry until browned and crumbly. You can drain the hamburger and add back to pot, that is up to you.
 3. Add remaining ingredients and stir, bringing to a boil.
 4. Cover and boil slowly, turning down heat as needed.
 5. Simmer about 20 minutes or until vegetables are tender but not too soft.
 6. Serve with crackers, rolls or sandwiches.
