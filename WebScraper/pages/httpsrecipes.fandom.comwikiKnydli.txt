 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups cooked mashed  potatoes 
 1  egg , beaten 
 1/2 cup  flour 
 4 tbsp bread crumbs 
 1 tbsp  butter 
 salt  to taste 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together. Place on floured board and mix to a smooth dough. 
 Add more  flour  if too sticky. Roll out into 2 lengths, 1 inch thick and cut in 1 inch slices. 
 Drop into boiling salted  water . 
 Boil for 1 minute. When knydli rise to the top, strain them. 
 Toss with  butter . Serve immediately. Half a cup dry  cottage cheese  can be added to  potatoes .
