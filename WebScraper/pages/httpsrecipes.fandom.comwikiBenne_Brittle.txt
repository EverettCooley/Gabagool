Description : 
 Benne seeds  (sesame seeds) cannot be procured in many places, but the candy made with it is delicious and very characteristic of Charleston.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cup  sugar 
 ½ tsp  vanilla extract 
 ½ tsp  lemon extract 
 2 cup parched  benne seed 
 Directions : 
 Add the  extracts  to the  sugar . 
 Melt the  sugar  in a saucepan, stirring constantly, as for  Peanut  brittle. 
 When the  sugar  is melted, add the  benne seed , stirring it in quickly. 
 Pour at once onto a marble slab to cool or pour into lightly buttered pans. 
 Mark in one-inch squares while still warm and break along the lines when cold.
