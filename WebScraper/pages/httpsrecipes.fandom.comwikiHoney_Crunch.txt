Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 8 or 9 cups 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  brown sugar 
 ⅓ cup  honey 
 ¼ lb  butter 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 4 cups Quaker Corn Bran 
 1½ cups Golden Grahams 
 1½ cups  Cheerios 
 1½ cups  nuts 
 ½ cup raisins 
 Directions : 
 Combine brown sugar, honey, butter and cinnamon. 
 Cook for a few minutes until syrupy. 
 In baking pan, mix corn bran, golden grahams, cheerios and nuts. 
 Pour syrup over cereal. 
 Stir. 
 Bake at 325°F for 20 to 25 minutes. 
 Stir at least twice. 
 Remove from oven and stir in ½ cup raisins. 
 Pour on waxed paper to cool.
