Description : 
 Servings: 1
 
 Ingredients : 
 200 g cooked  carrots 
 700 g cooked  beet 
 400 g cooked  potatoes 
 200 g roast  pork   or  cooked  beef 
 1 medium salted  herring  ( kipper  can do) 
 1 – 2  pickled cucumbers 
 2  apples 
 6 dl  sour cream 
 salt  according to taste 
 ½ tsp  mustard 
 a little  sugar 
 Garnish : 
 spring onions  to garnish 
 2 – 3 hard-boiled  eggs 
 Directions : 
 Peel the  potatoes ,  carrots  and beets, and core and peel the  apples  and cut them all in small tiny cubes. 
 Fillet the  herring , removing the skin and even the smallest bones and cut in cubes. 
 Mix  sour cream ,  mustard ,  salt  and  sugar  together to make a sauce. 
 Mix all the salad vegetables and meat with the sauce. 
 Keep covered in the fridge for an hour or two, and take out about one hour before serving. 
 Put a nice heap of salad in a dish or a bowl, cover the surface with finely chopped  eggs ,  egg  slices or segments, and decorate with chopped spring  onion .
