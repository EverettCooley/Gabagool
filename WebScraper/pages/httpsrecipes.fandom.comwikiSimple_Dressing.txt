A Simple Dressing
 
 1/2 c  vinegar 
 1/4 c  oil 
 1/2  lemon  -- Juice Of 
 1 sm  Onion  -- Minced 
 1/3 c  ketchup 
 2/3 c  sugar 
 1/2 ts  paprika 
 1 ts (scant)  salt 
 
Beat All Ingredients Together With a Rotary Beater or Wire Beater. or YouCan Put it in a Jar, Cover And Shake Well. This is a Fine Dressing to Useon  lettuce  And Fresh  tomatoes .
