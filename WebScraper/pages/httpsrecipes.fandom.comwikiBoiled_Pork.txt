 Ingredients : 
 1 kg of  pork 
 2 medium  onions 
 1 clove of  garlic 
 1 dl of  palm oil 
 3 ripe  tomatoes 
 1  laurel leaf 
 1 bunch of  spinach  leaves 
 salt 
 chilli pepper 
 Directions : 
 Chop the meat into small pieces and boil it in  water  with a pinch of  salt  until it is cooked. 
 In a separate saucepan, cook the  onion  cut into fine rings, the chopped  garlic , the  tomatoes  (peeled), the  laurel leaf , the  palm oil ,  salt  and  chilli . 
 When it is ready, add the  pork  and a small amount of  water , and cover with the  spinach  leaves. 
 Cook this on a low heat. 
 It is best served with cooked  banana .
