Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: my old recipes 
 makes 1⅓ cups. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  mayonnaise  or  salad dressing 
 ½ cup finely chopped  sweet pickles 
 1½ tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 tbsp grated  onion 
 Directions : 
 Mix  mayonnaise  or  salad dressing , sweet pickles,  lemon juice  and grated  onion . 
 Cover. 
 Chill to mix flavors. 
 Serve as a sauce for baked, broiled or fried fish or on a sandwich.
