 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 Cups of  milk 
 1 large avocados, seeded, peeled and chopped 
 1/4 can of  condensed milk 
 1 stick of  cinnamon 
 1 small pot espresso 
 Directions : 
 In a pot, bring to a boil  milk  with  cinnamon ,  coffee ,  coffee  beans and  condensed milk .
 Blend avocados in a blender for one minute. 
 Pour
 Blend into the pot with the  milk  and bring it up to a boil. 
 Remove  coffee  beans and  cinnamon  sticks before serving.
