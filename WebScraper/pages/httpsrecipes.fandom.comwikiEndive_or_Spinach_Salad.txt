 Description : 
       Endive or Spinach Salad     
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 quart  Endive  or  spinach 
 3 hard-boiled  eggs 
 4 slices  Bacon 
 1/2 cup  Sugar 
 2 tablespoons  flour 
 1  egg , beaten 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1/2 cup  vinegar 
 1 1/2 cups  water 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Top with sliced  eggs . 
 Cover with hot  Bacon  dressing and serve. 
 Brown  Bacon , remove from drippings and crumble. 
 Combine  Sugar  and  flour . 
 Add  egg ,  salt ,  vinegar  and  water  and mix . 
 Pour into  Bacon  drippings and heat stirring constantly. 
 Add crumbled  Bacon .
