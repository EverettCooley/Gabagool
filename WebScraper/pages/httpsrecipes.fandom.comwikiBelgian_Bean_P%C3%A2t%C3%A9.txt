Description : 
 Good tasting  Belgian Bean Pâté  to put on everything you can imagine, taste and quality are yours with this traditional recipe !
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  7-bean and barley 
 ¼ cup cooking liquid from beans 
 1 cup minced fresh  parsley 
 ½ cup  tahini 
 5 tbsp  lemon juice 
 ¼ cup  olive oil 
 1  garlic  clove (juice from) 
 ½ tsp  cumin 
 ½ tsp  onion powder 
 1 dash  cayenne pepper   or  ¼ tsp  chili powder 
 sea salt  (optional) 
 sliced  black olives 
 Directions : 
 Purée the cooked beans, using the liquid as needed to make a smooth purée. 
 Combine the puree with the remaining ingredients except the olives. The mixture should be thick. 
 Chill, covered, for at least 1 hour. 
 Garnish with olives, if desired and serve as an hors d'oeuvre with whole wheat bread or crackers.
