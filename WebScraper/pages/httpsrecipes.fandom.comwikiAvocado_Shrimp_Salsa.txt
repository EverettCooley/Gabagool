 Description : 
 A tasty, very flavorful salsa.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 Genuine  California avocados  sliced into small pieces. 
 2  tomatoes  diced. 
 1 can chopped jalepeno mild-med or hot. 
 6  green onions  chopped. 
 1 bunch of  cilantro  choped 
 1 8oz cans of  tomato  sauce, 2 if you want it thick. 
 1 med  cucumber  diced into about 1/2" pieces. 
 Â½ Pound bay  Shrimp . 
 Directions : 
 Put all the ingredients except the avacados and  Shrimp  into a blender and add  water  to the top and blend till chopped.
 Pour into a bowl, add the avacado and bay  Shrimp  stir and put into the fridge for an hour or so to chill.
 Have plenty of good chips
