 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 4  eggs 
 1 cup  milk 
 1 cup cooked  rice 
 1 14-ounce can  sweetened condensed milk 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 1 tablespoon  sugar  mixed with 1 teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Beat  eggs  in large mixing bowl. Add  milk ,  rice ,  condensed milk  and  vanilla ; mix well. Pour into greased shallow 1-1/2-quart baking dish. Sprinkle sugar-and- cinnamon  mixture over top. 
 Set dish in pan containing 1-inch of hot  water . Bake at 350 degrees 45 minutes, or until knife inserted near center comes out clean. Serve warm or chilled.
