Description : 
 Source:  Favorite Brand Name Light Cooking 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup Heinz  chili sauce 
 ¼ cup  orange marmalade 
 1½ tsp  prepared mustard 
 4 boneless skinless  chicken breast  halves (about 1 lb) 
 1 tbsp  margarine 
 Directions : 
 For glaze, combine  chili sauce ,  marmalade  and  mustard  in small bowl. 
 Lightly flatten  chicken  breasts to uniform thickness. 
 In large skillet, melt  margarine . 
 Add  chicken ; cook until brown on both sides. 
 Pour glaze over  chicken . 
 Simmer, uncovered, 8 minutes, basting occasionally or until  chicken  is tender and no longer pink in center.

 Calories - 219 | cholesterol - 66 mg | fat - 4 g | sodium - 361 mg
