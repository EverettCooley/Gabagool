 Description : 
 Always check the ingredients to make sure the product is vegan.
 
 Ingredients : 
 5-6 small  potatoes , sliced 
 1 tbsp dijon  mustard 
 1 can of baked beans 
 2 tsp  pepper 
 2 cups grated  soy cheese 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 400. Boil or steam the  potatoes  until they can be easily pierced with a fork. 
 In a small bowl, mix together the beans,  mustard  and  pepper . 
 Place half of the cooked  potatoes  in a lightly oiled casserole dish. 
 Now, you'll put this together like a lasagna, with a layer of beans and a layer of  Cheese . 
 Then, repeat with another layer of the remaining  potatoes , the rest of the beans and the rest of the  Cheese . 
 Cover and bake for 35 â€“ 40 minutes.
