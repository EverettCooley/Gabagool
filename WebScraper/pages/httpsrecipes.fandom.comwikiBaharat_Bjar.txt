      Baharat Bjar     
 Description : 
 This recipe is for 2 cups.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅓ cup  black pepper 
 ¼ cup  coriander  powder 
 ¼ scant cup  cinnamon 
 ¼ scant cup cloves 
 ⅓ cup  cumin 
 2 tsp  cardamom , ground 
 ¼ cup  nutmeg , ground 
 ½ cup  paprika , ground 
 ⅓ cup  curry powder 
 ¼ scant cup  dried lime , ground 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients. 
 Store in a tightly sealed jar.
