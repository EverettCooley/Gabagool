This recipe comes from an Estate Sale and were obtained when  The Family Collection  from The Wetzel Estate in Plano, Texas was purchased in 1982. Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 Pound  Cheese  spread 
 1 Pound  Cheddar   cheese 
 8 Ounces  cream cheese 
 4 cloves  garlic , minced 
 ½ Cup chopped  pecans 
 ¼ Cup minced  pecans 
 ½ Teaspoon  paprika 
 Directions : 
 Mix cheeses together 
 Mix in  garlic  and chopped nuts 
 Chill several hours. 
 Roll into balls and roll in minced nuts then sprinkle with paprika.
