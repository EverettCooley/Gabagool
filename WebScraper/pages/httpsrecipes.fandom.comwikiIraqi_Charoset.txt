 Iraqi Charoset  is a dried fruit and nut based paste served during the Jewish celebration of Passover by Jews from Iraq.
 
 Ingredients 
 500 ml Iraqi  dates , chopped and seeded 
 water 
 salt 
 125 ml chopped  walnuts   or   pistachios 
 Directions 
 Boil dates in lightly salted water stirring frequently until it is a thick paste. 
 Mix in finely chopped nuts. 
 A traditional variant is to strain the pulp out of the paste producing a honey-like date syrup. This procedure uses considerably more dates and can take several days to produce sufficient syrup.
