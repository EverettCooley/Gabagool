Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 16 slices  bacon  fried and crumbled 
 2 8 oz. pkg.  cream cheese  softened and cubed 
 4 cup shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 1 cup  half and half 
 2 tbsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 1 tbsp minced  onion 
 ½ tbsp  dry mustard 
 ½ tbsp  salt 
 dash  hot sauce 
 Directions : 
 Put all ingredients in crock pot and cook on low stirring occasionally until  cheese  melts about 1 hour. 
 Taste and adjust seasonings, add  bacon  and keep on low to serve. 
 Serve with cubed  French bread .
