 Ingredients : 
 3 Tbs. fresh  lime juice  (1 large  lime ) 
 2 Tbs.  olive oil 
 1 tsp. ground  cumin 
 15 1/2-oz. can  navy beans , rinsed and drained 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1  jalape√±o pepper , seeded and chopped 
 3/4 cup chopped  cilantro 
 3 to 5 scallions (white and light green parts), finely chopped ( 1/4 cup) 
 Directions : 
 1. In food processor, combine  lime juice ,  oil ,  cumin , beans and  garlic  and pulse 5 or 6 times until blended.
 
 2. Transfer mixture to serving bowl, stir in  pepper ,  cilantro , scallions and  salt  to taste. Serve with crackers or raw vegetables.
