Description : 
 A TNT recipe
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: my old recipes 
 Ingredients : 
 6 thin  pork chops , boned 
 pepper 
 salt 
 ginger  powder 
 garlic powder 
 2 tbsp  soy sauce 
 ½ cup  flour 
 1  egg 
 1½ cups  bread crumbs 
 ½ cup  oil 
 tomato  or  barbecue sauce 
 Directions : 
 Pound meat with tenderizer and sprinkle both side with dash of  pepper ,  salt ,  ginger  and  garlic powder . 
 Sprinkle  pork chops  with  soy sauce  and coat with  flour . 
 Dip chops in beaten  egg , then coat with breadcrumbs. 
 Heat pan over high heat for 1 minutes and pour in a little  oil . 
 When  oil  is heated, turn heat to medium and put chops in. 
 Fry until browned on both sides and  pork  is cooked through. 
 Cut cooked chops into bite size pieces and arrange on hot serving dish. 
 Garnish with  tomato  wedges and shredded  cucumber . 
 Serve with  tomato  or  barbecue sauce .
