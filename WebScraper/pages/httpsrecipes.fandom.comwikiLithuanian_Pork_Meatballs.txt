Ingredients : 
 2 lbs fresh  ground pork 
 2  eggs 
 1 tsp  salt 
 pepper  to taste 
 ¼ tsp  allspice 
 2 medium  onions , chopped 
 ½ cup  cracker crumbs 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients. 
 Form in 1 inch thick, 2½ inches long. 
 Fry until brown. 
 Bake at 350°F for 1 hour.
