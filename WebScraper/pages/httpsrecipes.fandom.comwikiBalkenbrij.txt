Description : 
 This is a very traditional Dutch recipe, which is hardly every made anymore. Please do not read if you have a weak stomach.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pig's head without lower jaw. 
 1/2 pound of fresh, streaky  ham 
 5 or 6 liters of  water 
 salt ,  pepper  to taste 
 1 1/4 kg of  pancake flour 
 some normal  flour 
 Directions : 
 Wash the pig's head 
 Add the ham and salt to taste, and cover with water 
 Cook until tender 
 Take the meat off the bone and mince it together with the ham 
 Season to taste with salt and pepper 
 Add the remaining water to the cooking water, and return to the boil 
 Add the diced meat and return to the boil. 
 Mix in flour until the mixture is stiff.  Add more salt if necessary. 
 Transfer into broad shallow dishes which have been dusted with flour, and leave to cool. 
 To serve, cut the Balkenbrij into slices of about a finger thick, coat with flour, and fry quickly in lard or butter.
