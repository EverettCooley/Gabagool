Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz  Kahlúa ®  coffee liqueur 
 2 oz  coconut liqueur 
 1 pinch  cinnamon 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Mix the  Kahlua  and  coconut  liqueur, poor over crushed  ice , and sprinkle a pinch of  cinnamon  on the top. 
 Voila! You can also put this in a blender if you so desire.
