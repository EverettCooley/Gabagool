Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Cashion Estate in Terrell, Texas in 1996.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  butter 
 2 tablespoons  Dijon mustard 
 6 cups shredded  green cabbage 
 1 teaspoon  celery salt 
 ¼ teaspoon dried  thyme 
 ¼ cup  water 
 1 tablespoon  sesame seeds 
 Directions : 
 In a large skillet melt butter over medium heat then stir in mustard and add cabbage. 
 Sauté for 3 minutes stirring constantly. 
 Sprinkle celery salt and thyme over cabbage then stir to mix. 
 Lower heat and add water then cover and steam for 3 minutes. 
 Stir in sesame seeds and serve immediately.
