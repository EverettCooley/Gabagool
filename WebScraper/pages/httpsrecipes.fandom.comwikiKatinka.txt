 Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 shot De Kuyper®  apricot brandy 
 1½ shots Stolichnaya Red®  vodka 
 Half measure  lime juice 
 Directions : 
 Shake the ingredients well together with  ice  and strain into a cocktail glass half filled with crushed  ice . 
 Decorate with a sprig of fresh  mint .
