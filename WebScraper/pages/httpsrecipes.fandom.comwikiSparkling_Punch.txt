Description : 
 Contributed by Stephen Ceideburg
 
 Makes 8 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 4 Ruby Mist, Red Zinger or other fruity  herbal tea  bags 
 1 licorice tea bag 
 1 cup boiling  water 
 1 tbsp  honey 
 1 quart plain sparkling  water 
 Directions : 
 In a 2-cup heat-resistant measuring cup or other container, brew the tea bags in the boiling  water  for 5 minutes. 
 Gently squeeze and remove tea bags. 
 Stir in  honey . 
 Let cool. 
 Place 2 tablespoons tea in a champagne flute and fill with  sparkling water .
