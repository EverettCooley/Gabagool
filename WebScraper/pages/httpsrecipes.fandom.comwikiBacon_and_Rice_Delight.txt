Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 5 slices  bacon , diced 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 ½ cup sliced  ripe olives 
 ¼ cup  pecans 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 2 cups  cottage cheese 
 ¾ cup grated  Cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Cook  bacon  and  onion  in large skillet until  onion  is golden. Pour off excess drippings. 
 Add  rice ,  pecans ,  salt  and  pepper  to skillet. Continue cooking until thoroughly heated. 
 Remove from heat. Stir in  cottage cheese . 
 Turn into buttered 1½-quart shallow casserole. 
 Top with  Cheddar  cheese. 
 Bake at 375 °F for 15 to 20 minutes.
