 Description : 
 "This is a recipe that a Korean Friend of mine gave to me. I like it, except for all the  ginger !" Original recipe yield: 8 servings.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds coarsely chopped  Chinese cabbage 
 1 tablespoon  salt 
 2 tablespoons chopped  green onion 
 1 clove  garlic , crushed 
 1 tablespoon  chili powder 
 2 teaspoons minced  fresh ginger  root 
 1/2 cup light soy sauce 
 1/2 cup  white wine vinegar 
 2 teaspoons  white sugar 
 1 dash  sesame oil 
 Directions : 
 1 - dash  cabbage  with  salt , let sit for approx 4 hours
 2 - Drain liquids out of  cabbage  and add the  garlic ,  onions ,  chili powder .  vinegar , soy sauce, and  Sugar . Add  sesame oil  before serving.
