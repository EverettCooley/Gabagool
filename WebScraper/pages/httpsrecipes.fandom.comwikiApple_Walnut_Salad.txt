Ingredients : 
 Dressing : 
 2 tbsp  lemon juice 
 2 tbsp  vegetable broth 
 1½ tbsp  vegetable oil 
 ½ tsp  salt   or  1 tsp  soy sauce 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 Salad : 
 1 large  watercress  bunch, tough stems removed 
 1 large  carrot , shredded 
 1  Red Delicious apple , cored and shredded 
 1  Golden Delicious apple , cored and shredded 
 2 tbsp  lemon juice 
 ½ cup  walnuts , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Dressing : 
 In a small bowl, combine all dressing ingredients. 
 Mix well. 
 Salad : 
 Arrange watercress around the outside edge of a large platter. 
 Scatter  carrot  next o the watercress. 
 In a small bowl, toss shredded  apples  with  lemon juice . 
 Spoon  apples  into the center of the platter. 
 Scatter  nuts  on top of the  apples . 
 Pour dressing over the salad and serve immediately.
