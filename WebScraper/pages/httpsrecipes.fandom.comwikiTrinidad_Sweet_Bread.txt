 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  sugar 
 3 tsp  baking powder 
 ¾ cup  water   or   milk 
 1 tsp  vanilla extract 
 ½ cup mixed peel 
 1 cup  raisins 
 1  egg 
 3 cups  flour 
 ½ cup  margarine 
 1  coconut , grated 
 1 tsp  Angostura bitters 
 ½ cup  maraschino cherries 
 ½ cup  currants 
 Directions : 
 Mix  grated coconut  with  sugar  and a little  water . 
 Add  egg  and mix thoroughly. 
 Add essence,  bitters  and  margarine . 
 Sift dry ingredients together and add to  coconut / egg  mixture. 
 Add remaining  water  to make a soft dough, do not knead. 
 Add fruits and mixed peel. 
 Pour in prepared baking pan and bake at 350°F for 50-60 minutes, remove from oven. 
 Mix a little  sugar  and  water  so make a syrup and baste on bread. 
 Return bread to hot oven for 3-4 minutes.
