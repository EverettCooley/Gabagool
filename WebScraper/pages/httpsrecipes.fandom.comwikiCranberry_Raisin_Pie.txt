cranberry   Raisin  Pie
 
 1 c  Raisins 
 3 c  cranberries 
 1 c  sugar 
 2 t  cornstarch 
 1/4 t  salt 
 3/4 c  water 
 1 1/4 t  vanilla 
 1 x Pastry For 2 Crust 9 Inch Pi 
 
Grind or Chop  Raisins  And  cranberries . Put Filling Ingredients,Except  vanilla  Into Saucepan. Cook Over Low Heat About 8 to 10Minutes. Add  vanilla  And Let Cool While Making Pastry. Pour FillingInto Pastry Lined Pie Plate, Cover With Top Crust or Lattice Crust.Make Slits or Air Vents in Top Crust. Bake in 425? Oven For 30Minutes or Until Browned.
 
Enjoy.
