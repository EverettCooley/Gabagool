 Description : 
 In Romanian: Tort de curmale cu frisca
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 oz / 250 g  confectioner's sugar 
 10 oz / 300 g chopped  dates 
 10 oz / 300 g  ground hazelnuts 
 1 tablespoon fine  breadcrumbs 
 10  egg whites 
 1 lb / 500 g  whipped cream 
 vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Beat the  egg whites  with the  sugar  until foamy. 
 Add the  dates  cut lengthwise,  hazelnuts , bread crumbs and  vanilla . 
 Pour the mixture in the cake pan and bake at medium heat. 
 Let cool, then halve crosswise and fill with  whipped cream . 
 Cover all the cake with  whipped cream  and garnish with cut  dates .
