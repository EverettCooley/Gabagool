 Ingredients : 
 ½ lb  ground beef 
 2 tbsp grated  cheese 
 1  carrot , finely grated 
 ½ tsp  garlic powder 
 ½ cup  bread crumbs  (whole wheat is healthiest) 
 1  egg , beaten 
 1 tsp  tomato paste 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Combine all ingredients together; mix thoroughly. 
 Roll into meatballs - whatever size is appropriate for your dog. 
 Place on a cookie sheet sprayed with non-fat  cooking spray . 
 Bake for 15 – 20 minutes, or until they are brown and firm. 
 Cool and store in the fridge or freezer.
