Description : 
 Drizzle over fat-free frozen  yogurt , angel food cake slices or  berries .
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  cocoa 
 ¼ cup hot  coffee  or hot  water 
 14 oz fat-free  sweetened condensed skim milk  (1 can) 
 2 tbsp  corn syrup 
 1 tsp  raspberry vinegar 
 ½ tsp  vanilla extract 
 Directions : 
 In a medium saucepan, combine the  cocoa  and the  coffee  or  water . 
 Beat with a whisk until the  cocoa  is smooth and all the lumps have dissolved. 
 Whisk in the  condensed milk ,  corn syrup  and  raspberry vinegar . 
 Heat over low heat until warm, stirring constantly. Whisk in the  vanilla extract . 
 Alternately, combine all the ingredients except the  vanilla  in a microwave-safe bowl. 
 Heat on medium power (50%) in a microwave oven, stirring frequently until warm. 
 Stir in the  vanilla extract  and serve.
