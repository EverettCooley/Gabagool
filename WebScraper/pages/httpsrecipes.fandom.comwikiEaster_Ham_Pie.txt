Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ lb Italian  ham  cubes 
 1 lb  fresh cheese  ( basket cheese ), no salt 
 15  eggs 
 pinch of  pepper 
 ¼ cup grated  cheese 
 2 tbsp grated  parsley 
 Crust : 
 4 cups  flour 
 4  eggs 
 1 cup  Crisco 
 1 tsp each  salt  and  pepper 
 1 tbsp  baking powder 
 4 – 6 tbsp  water 
 Directions : 
 Dice ham, beat eggs, cheese, then add the ham, pepper. 
 Fill in pan lined with pie crust. 
 Put another pie crust on top. 
 Brush top pie crust with egg whites to make the top look shiny. 
 Crust : 
 Beat eggs, black pepper, grated parsley, grated cheese. 
 Cut each corner, so crust doesn't explode. 
 Bake at 325 °F in 13x9 pan for 1 hour and 20 minutes. 
 Enjoy.
