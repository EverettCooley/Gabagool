Description : 
 A coconut cocktail. Where no palm wine is available, one may substitute 5 ounces  Lady Bligh Coconut Rum ® for a: 'Congorum', 5 ounces vodka for a: 'Chi Chi Congo', or simply omit the palm wine.
This is the: 'Triple Coco Sour' variation.
 
 Ingredients : 
 5 ounces palm wine 
 5 ounces  Coco López ® 
 5 ounces Pat O'Brien's®  Triple sec 
 5 ounces Pat O'Brien's® Sweet and Sour Mix 
 Directions : 
 Fill blender with ice. 
 Add all ingredients and blend well on high. 
 Serve in Hurricane glass.
