 Description : 
 Purchased from the Wells Estate in Canton, Texas in 1987. Date and source unknown.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds ground  Beef 
 2  eggs 
 1 cup quick cooking  rolled oats 
 1 medium white  Onion , grated 
 1 cup  applesauce 
 2 teaspoons  salt 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 12 teaspoon  sage , crumbled 
 1 tablespoon  Steak  sauce 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix ground  Beef  lightly with  eggs ,  rolled oats ,  Onion ,  applesauce ,  salt ,  pepper , and  sage  until well-blended. 
 Pack firmly into a loaf pan. 
 Unmold into a shallow baking pan. 
 Score top in cross-cross patter and brush with  Steak  sauce. 
 Bake in 350 oven for 1 hour and 15 minutes.
