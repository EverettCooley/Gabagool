Asian Glazed Short Ribs
 
 Servings: 4
 Prep Time: 4 minutes
 Cook Time: 4 Hrs, 10 minutes
 
 
 4 lb  Beef  (or  Pork ) Short Ribs 
 1 Env  onion soup mix 
 1/2 c  apricot  Preserves 
 1/2 c  chili sauce 
 1/4 c Firmly Packed  light brown sugar 
 1/4 c  soy sauce 
 2 Tbsp  apple cider vinegar 
 1 Tbsp  corn starch 
 1 c  water 
 
Arrange The Ribs in The Crockpot. Combine RemainingIngredients, Except  cornstarch  And  water .
 Cook, Covered on Low For 8 to 10 Hrs or on High
 For 4 to 6 Hrs, or Until Ribs Are Tender. Remove Ribs to ServingPlatter; Keep Warm. in Small Bowl, Combine  cornstarch  With  water . Stir IntoSauce And Cook, Covered, 10 to 15 minutes or Until Thickened. PourSauce Over Ribs.
