Description : 
 A River of Recipes: Native American Recipes Using Commodity Foods  by the USDA Food Distribution Program on Indian Reservations, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Serves : 8-10 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups washed  dry beans 
 7 cups  water 
 1 medium  onion , chopped 
 1 cup chopped  ham   or  ham bone 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Put beans in water and boil 2 minutes. 
 Cover, remove from heat, and let stand 1 hour. 
 Add chopped onion to beans. 
 Bring to a boil and gently simmer 2 to 2½ hours, or until beans are tender. 
 Add ham or ham bone and cook for 1 hour. 
 Add more water if needed. 
 15 minutes before done, remove 1 cup beans, mash and return to soup (this will thicken soup).
