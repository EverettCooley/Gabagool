Description : 
 This is a recipe I obtained from an Estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Brampton Estate in Heath, Texas in 1988.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  water 
 1½ cups  baby carrots 
 1 teaspoon  honey 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 1½ cups  baby corn  husked 
 1 teaspoon dried  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Bring water to a boil. 
 Add carrots along with honey and salt. 
 Cover and cook 7 minutes. 
 Add baby corn and parsley then cook 5 minutes adding more water if necessary.
