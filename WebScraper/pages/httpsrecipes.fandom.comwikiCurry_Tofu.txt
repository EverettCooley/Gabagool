 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large block  tofu 
 3 small  red peppers 
 3 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 1 bag  curry  powder (or, to taste) 
 cornstarch  - enough for your preferred  curry  texture 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1/2 teaspoon  granulated sugar 
 Dash of  MSG  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Cut drained  tofu  into small pieces. Bring a pan of  water  to boil; boil  tofu  for 5 minutes. Turn off the heat and pour out the  water . Remove the  tofu  and set it aside. Add the  oil  and  peppers  to the pan. Bring the temperature to a very high heat. Stir until you can smell the spiciness you desire. Put the  tofu  back in. Add the  curry  powder and a 1/2 cup  water . Add  salt ,  Sugar  and  MSG . Cook for 3 to 5 minutes. At the end, add enough  cornstarch  for your desired texture.
