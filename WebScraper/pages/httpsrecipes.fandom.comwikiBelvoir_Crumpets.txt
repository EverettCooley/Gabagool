Description : 
 You could also serve this mixture grilled on toast. Makes 8.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 175 g (6 oz)  Stilton cheese , crumbled 
 25g (1 oz) softened  butter 
 1 tbsp  port 
 1 tbsp natural  yogurt 
 25g (1 oz) mixed  nuts , chopped 
 8  crumpets 
 8  walnut  halves 
 chopped fresh  parsley  chopped to garnish 
 Directions : 
 Combine  Cheese  and  butter . Stir in  port ,  yogurt  and  nuts . 
 Toast one side of each  crumpet . 
 Spread the  Cheese  mixture onto the untoasted side of the crumpets and grill until bubbling. 
 Garnish with  walnuts  and  parsley , and serve hot.
