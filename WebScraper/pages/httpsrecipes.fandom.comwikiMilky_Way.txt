Ingredients : 
 3 cl Beefeater®  gin 
 3 cl  amaretto  Di Saronno® liqueur 
 1 cl Greizer®  strawberry liqueur 
 1½ cl Monin®  strawberry syrup 
 3 cl fresh  pineapple juice 
 Directions : 
 Add ingredients to a cocktail shaker, shake, and serve with cut  apple ,  lemon , and pineapple-leaves.
