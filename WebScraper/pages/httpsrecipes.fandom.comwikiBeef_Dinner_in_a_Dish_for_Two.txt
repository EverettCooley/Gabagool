Makes 2 generous servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 ounces lean ground  Beef 
 1/4 cup chopped  Onion 
 1/2 cup cooked, cubed yellow summer  Squash 
 1/2 cup coarsely chopped  green pepper 
 1 cup cooked  rice * 
 1  egg 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1/2 teaspoon  basil 
 1/2 teaspoon  oregano  leaves, crushed 
 1/8 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1/4 cup (1 ounce) grated  Cheddar   Cheese 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Beef  and  Onion  in medium skillet until meat is no longer pink, stirring to crumble. Pour off fat. 
 Add  Squash ,  green pepper ,  rice ,  egg ,  garlic ,  basil ,  oregano  and  black pepper . 
 Spoon into 1-quart casserole. Sprinkle with  Cheese . Bake at 350 degrees 25 minutes, or until heated through.
