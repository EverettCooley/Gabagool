 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 6 ounces  soft tofu 
 1/2 teaspoon  white vinegar 
 1/4 teaspoon  salt 
 1/4 teaspoon fresh  lemon juice 
 1/2 teaspoon  vegetable oil 
 Pinch of  white pepper 
 Directions : 
 Place  tofu  in  water  and bring to a boil. Cook 8 minutes, then drain. Place in linen napkin and squeeze lightly to remove excess  water . Place  tofu  in a bowl and mash until smooth. Add  vinegar ,  salt ,  lemon juice ,  oil  and  pepper . Mix well. The final consistency should be like that of  yogurt . Refrigerate before serving.
