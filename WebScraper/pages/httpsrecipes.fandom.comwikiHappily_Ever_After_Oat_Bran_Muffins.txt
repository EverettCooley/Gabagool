Description : 
 These muffins are great for someone that has  wheat  and dairy allergies.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  oat bran  cereal 
 ¼ cup  brown sugar 
 2 tsp  baking powder 
 1 cup  skim milk  or  soy milk  or  goat milk 
 2  egg whites , slightly beaten 
 ¼ cup  molasses  or honey 
 2 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 Directions : 
 Don't mix these together too much. 
 Bake at 425°F for 10 to 12 minutes.
