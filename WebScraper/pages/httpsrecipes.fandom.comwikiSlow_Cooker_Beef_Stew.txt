 Prep Time: 30 mins 
 Cook time: 8 hours 
 Serves: 4 
 
 Description : 
 Slow cooked beef stew with turnips.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  beef , cubed 
 2 cups of  beef stock 
 8  radishes 
 10  pearl onions 
 2 pounds of  potatoes , chopped 
 1 cup of  turnips , chopped 
 1 cup of  carrots , chopped 
 1 tablespoon  Worcestershire sauce 
 Directions : 
 Coat beef with flour and beef seasoning. 
 Brown beef. 
 Add beef and 2 cups of beef stock to slow cooker. 
 Add turnips, onions, radishes, and worcester sauce. 
 Turn on slow cooker for 8 hours. 
 4 hours before eating put put in potatoes. 
 2 hours before eating put in carrots.
