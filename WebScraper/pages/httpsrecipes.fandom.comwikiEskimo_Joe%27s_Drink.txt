Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½  pineapple 
 1  lemon 
 1 green  apple 
 1  orange 
 1 handful  ice 
 500 ml  raspberry   yogurt 
 3 oz  orange juice 
 8 oz Malibu ®   coconut rum 
 Directions : 
 Add  rum , fruits,  ice , juice, and  yoghurt  to a blender. 
 Ensure the  orange  is peeled and seedless. 
 Cut seeds out of  apple . 
 Blend for 1 minute and you're ready for the eskimo.
