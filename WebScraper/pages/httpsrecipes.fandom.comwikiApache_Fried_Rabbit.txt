      Apache Fried Rabbit     
 Ingredients : 
 swamp or cotton-tail  rabbit 
 bacon  or  pork drippings 
 flour 
 salt 
 water 
 Directions : 
 Dress swamp or cotton-tail  rabbit . 
 Wash, cut up, and cover with  water , and cook until nearly done. 
 Take pieces out of liquid, dust with  flour  and  salt , and fry until brown in a  skillet  of  pork fat .
