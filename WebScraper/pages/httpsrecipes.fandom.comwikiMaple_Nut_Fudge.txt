 Ingredients : 
 2 cups Fancy or Grade A Medium Amber Pure Vermont  maple syrup 
 1/2 cup chopped  nuts 
 1 Tablespoon  butter 
 Directions : 
 Boil syrup to 236 degrees. 
 Remove from heat and place in a pan of cold  water . 
 Cool to room temperature. Stir vigorously until color begins to change, then add  nuts . P 
 our into well-buttered 8" or 10" square pan. Cut fudge into squares while warm.
