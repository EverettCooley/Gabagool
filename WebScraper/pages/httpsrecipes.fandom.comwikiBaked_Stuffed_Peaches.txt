Baked Stuffed  peaches 
 
 6 Ripe  peaches 
 1/2 Cup  sugar 
 4  egg  Yolks 
 1/2 Lb.  almond  Macaroons, Crushed. 
 
Peel And Halve  peaches ; Scoop Out About 1 Teaspoon Of The Center Pulp of Each Half. Mash Pulp; Mix With  sugar ,Crushed Macaroons And  egg  Yolks.
 Place Halves Close Together, Cut Side Up, in GreasedBaking Dish, About 8x12. Spoon Macaroon Mixture IntoCenter of Each. Bake in a Slow Oven (300?) For
 
 30 Minutes, or Until  peaches  Are Tender.
