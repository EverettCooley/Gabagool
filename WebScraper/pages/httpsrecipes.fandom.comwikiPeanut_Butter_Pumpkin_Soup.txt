Ingredients : 
 4 Tbsp  unsalted butter 
 2 cups mashed  sweet potatoes 
 6 cups  chicken stock  (or Maggi equivalent) 
 1 tsp  salt 
 4 cups cooked  pumpkin 
 1 cup  Peanut butter 
 1 tsp  black pepper 
 fresh  chives 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter  in soup pot over medium heat. 
 Stir in  pumpkin ,  sweet potatoes  and  Peanut butter . 
 Add stock,  salt ,  pepper , and stir well until smooth. 
 Reduce heat and simmer to cook for 20 minutes. 
 Before serving, garnish with  chives .
