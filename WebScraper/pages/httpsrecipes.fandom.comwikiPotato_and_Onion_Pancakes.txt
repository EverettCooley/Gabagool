Description : 
 Crispy  Potato  Pancakes with a slight hint of  Onion . Great for lunches 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 large  potatoes 
 1 medium  Onion 
 2 tablespoons  flour 
 1  egg 
 salt  and  pepper 
 oil  (for frying) 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Grate  potatoes  and  Onion  together. 
 Add  flour  and  egg . 
 Add  salt  and  pepper  to taste. 
 Mix well. 
 In fry pan add enough  oil  to coat pan. 
 Place 1 heaping tablespoon of mixture in pan and spread out. 
 Cook until crisp and then turn over. 
 Place on paper towel to remove grease.
