Description 
 A lassi is a traditional Indian drink which is similar to a fruit  smoothie .
 
 Fruits and Veggies Matter Child Recipe Cards  by the US Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, public domain government resource—original source of recipe 
 Cook Time : 10 minutes 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 
 1 small  mango , quartered, pitted, and peeled 
 ¾ cup non-fat plain  yogurt 
 ⅓ cup  orange juice 
 2  ice  cubes 
 Directions 
 Combine all the ingredients in a blender. 
 Blend until smooth and frothy. 
 Variation 
 Try using strawberries, bananas or other fruit in place of the mango next time you make a lassi.
