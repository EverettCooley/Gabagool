Description : 
 In Romanian: Conopida cu unt si pesmet
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 medium  cabbage 
 3 tablespoons  butter 
 2 tablespoons  bread crumbs 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Remove the first outer leaves from the  cabbage , divide in six parts and set to boil in  salt   water . 
 When ready, drain well, remove the core, and place in a heat resistant glass dish that was greased with some  butter . 
 Fry the bread crumbs with the  butter  and pour over the  cabbage . 
 Place in the oven for a few minutes. 
 Before serving, pour some melted  butter  on top.
