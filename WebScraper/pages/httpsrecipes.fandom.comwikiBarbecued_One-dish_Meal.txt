Description : 
 Makes 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound smoked country-style link  sausage 
 ½ cup  water 
 1 x 16-ounce can cut  green beans 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil  or  bacon  drippings 
 ¾ cup  catsup 
 2 tablespoons  brown sugar 
 1 teaspoon  prepared mustard 
 1½ teaspoons  liquid smoke  flavoring 
 2  bouillon  cubes 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Cover and cook  sausage  in  water  about 5 minutes. 
 Remove from  water  and cut into 1-inch pieces. 
 Drain beans, reserving liquid. 
 Cook  onion  in  oil . 
 Add ½ cup bean liquid,  catsup ,  brown sugar ,  mustard , smoke flavoring and bouillon cubes. 
 Simmer 10 minutes. 
 Stir in  rice ; season to taste. 
 Cover and continue simmering 20 minutes.
