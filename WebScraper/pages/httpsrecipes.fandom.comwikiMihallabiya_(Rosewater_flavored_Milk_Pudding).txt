 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 tablespoons ground  rice 
 3 cups  milk 
 1 cup  Sugar 
 1 tablespoon  rosewater 
 2 tablespoons mixed  nuts 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Add ground  rice  to 1 cup of the  milk . Dissolve the  Sugar  in the remaining  milk  and bring to a boil. 
 Add the rice- milk  mixture, lower heat, and cook, stirring occasionally until the pudding thickens like a thin custard. 
 Remove from the heat, stir in the  rosewater  and pour into a shallow serving dish. Sprinkle with the  nuts .
