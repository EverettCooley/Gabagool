Ingredients : 
 Filling : 
 32 oz  cream cheese  (4 x 8 oz packages) 
 6  eggs 
 1 cup  sugar 
 Crust : 
 1 can  chocolate  macaroons 
 ½ cup  almonds 
 4 tsp  butter , (melted) 
 Directions : 
 Combine the filling ingredients. 
 In a food processor, mix the crust ingredients. 
 Shape crust mixture in a spring-form pan then put in  cheese  mixture. 
 Bake at 350 °F for 1 hour until set. 
 Make one day ahead. 
 Can be topped with fresh fruit.
