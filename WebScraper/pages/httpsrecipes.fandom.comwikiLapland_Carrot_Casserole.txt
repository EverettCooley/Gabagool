 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups cooked,  brown rice 
 1 cup skim  milk 
 2 1/2 cups shredded  carrots 
 2  eggs 
 2 teaspoons  brown sugar 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  nutmeg 
 2 tablespoons crushed cereal flakes 
 Directions : 
 Add cooked  rice  to  milk  in saucepan. 
 Simmer uncovered until most of the liquid reduces and mixture is creamy, 5 to 10 minutes. 
 Mix with remaining ingredients, except cereal crumbs, in 3-quart casserole. Sprinkle with crumbs. 
 Bake uncovered in preheated 325-degree oven
 Slightly less than an hour until set. 
 
 FOR THREE 8 x 4-INCH LOAF PANS:
 PREPARE as above. Bake for 55 to 60 minutes.
 
 FOR FIVE OR SIX 5 x 3-INCH MINI-LOAF PANS: 
 PREPARE as above. Bake for 50 to 55 minutes
