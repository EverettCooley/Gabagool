Description : 
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 large  shrimp 
 2 ounces  jack cheese , shredded 
 6 slices thick cut  bacon 
 Directions : 
 Peel shrimp, leaving the shell on the tail segments. 
 Devein. 
 Partially butterfly shrimp. 
 Form cheese into 6 long, thin rolls. 
 Place 1 roll in open part of each shrimp. 
 Place shrimp on bacon and roll up. 
 Run 1 long bamboo skewer through upper portion of all 6shrimp and another through lower portion of all 6 shrimp in order to keep shrimp closed and hold bacon in place. 
 Grill until bacon is cooked and shrimp are no longer translucent. 
 Remove from skewers to serve. 
 Enjoy. 
 Notes : 
 Makes 6 appetizers or 1 main dish serving 
 May be drizzled with melted butter, hot sauce, or sweet chili sauce 
 May substitute other cheese to taste.
