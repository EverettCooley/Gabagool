ANGEL FOOD DOUGHNUTS
-
 
  4-6 lg. eggs
 2 c. sugar
 1 tsp. soda
 2 tsp. baking powder
 Vanilla and salt to taste
 1 c. sour cream
 1 1/2 c. sour milk OR buttermilk
 6 c. flour
 
 Beat eggs until lemon colored; add remaining ingredients
alternately. Use doughnut press and fry in hot lard. Makes 6 dozen.
