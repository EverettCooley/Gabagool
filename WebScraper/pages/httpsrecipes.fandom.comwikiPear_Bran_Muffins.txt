Description : 
 Why not start the day with a delicious and satisfying muffin? It's the perfect breakfast whether you have time to dine or need to grab something on the run! These wholesome, satisfying muffins are filled with tender pieces of pear and spiced with  cinnamon . They freeze well; just heat in the toaster or microwave before serving.
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes For diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 From:  The South Beach Diet Phases 2 and 3  and also  The South Beach Diet Quick & Easy Cookbook 
 Prep Time: 15 Minutes | Cook Time: 20 Minutes 
 Makes 12 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups whole-grain  pastry flour 
 1 cup  wheat bran 
 2 tbsp granular  sugar substitute 
 1¼ tsp ground  cinnamon 
 1¼ tsp  baking soda 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 1¼ cups 1 percent or fat-free  buttermilk 
 2 large  eggs , lightly beaten 
 3 tbsp  canola oil 
 1  Bosc pear , cored and diced into ¼-inch cubes 
 1½ tsp  vanilla extract 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 350°F. 
 Line a muffin tin with paper liners or lightly coat with  cooking spray . 
 Combine  flour ,  bran ,  sugar substitute ,  cinnamon , baking soda, and  salt  in a large mixing bowl. 
 Combine  buttermilk , eggs ,  oil ,  pear , and  vanilla  in another mixing bowl. 
 Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients. 
 Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and mix just to combine; do not overmix. 
 Divide batter evenly into muffin cups. 
 Bake for 20 minutes. 
 Cool and serve. 
 Nutrition Information : 
 Per serving:
 
 130 calories | 5g total fat (0.5g sat) |  5g protein | 20g carbs | 5g fiber | 200 mg sodium
