       Green Bean Medley     
 Ingredients : 
 200 g Iceland frozen  peas 
 180 g Iceland cut  green beans 
 240 g Iceland  broad beans 
 grated rind of 1 small  lemon 
 freshly ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Place the  peas  in a saucepan and cover with boiling  water . Return to boil and cook for 2 – 3 mins. Drain well. 
 Place the  broad beans  in a saucepan and cover with boiling  water . Return to boil and cover for 4 mins until tender 
 Place the cut  green beans  in a saucepan cover with boiling  water . Return to boil and cook for 5 – 6 mins. Drain well 
 Tip all the vegetables into a large bowl. Add the grated  lemon  rind and season with freshly ground  black pepper . 
 Mix together and serve immediately.
