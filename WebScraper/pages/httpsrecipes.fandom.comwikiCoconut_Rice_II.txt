Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  long-grain rice  ( basmati ) 
 2 cups  coconut milk 
 2 cups  water 
 1 small  onion , diced 
 1-inch piece  ginger , grated 
 1 can  kidney beans , drained and rinsed 
 salt  and  pepper 
 1 bunch  scallions 
 Directions : 
 In a medium sauce pot, sweat the  onion  and minced  ginger  in 1 tbsp  olive oil  until fragrant and then add  rice  and saut√© for 1 minute. 
 Add the  water  and  coconut milk  and bring to a boil 
 Season with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Add the drained  kidney beans . Cover and place on a low heat and cook for 20 minutes. 
 Turn the heat off and let sit for 5 minutes. 
 Remove lid and stir in the sliced scallions and check the seasoning.
