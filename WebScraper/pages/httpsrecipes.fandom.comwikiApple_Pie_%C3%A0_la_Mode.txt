Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ oz Captain Morgan® Original  spiced rum 
 ½ oz  Apple schnapps 
 2 oz  apple juice 
 1 oz  cream of coconut 
 1 oz  heavy cream 
 2 tbsp  Apple pie filling 
 8 oz crushed  ice 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients in blender until smooth and creamy. 
 Serve in specialty glass. 
 Garnish with  apple  wedge or  cinnamon  stick.
