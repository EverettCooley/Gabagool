 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 Gallons shredded  iceberg lettuce 
 3 Cups ranch dressing 
 1 Gallon diced  tomato 
 4 ½ Pounds cooked  Turkey , diced 
 12  California avocados  (6 lbs.) 
 48 slices crisply cooked  Bacon 
 48 toast points 
 Directions : 
 Per order - toss 2 cups  lettuce  with 1 tablespoon dressing.
 Arrange in a bed on a serving plate.
 Arrange 1/2 cup  tomato  over  lettuce , leaving a border of  lettuce .
 Repeat sequence with 2/3 cup  Turkey  and 1/2  avocado , diced.
 Drizzle with 1 tablespoon dressing.
 Top with 2 slices  Bacon .
 Garnish with toast points
