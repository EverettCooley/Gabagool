Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4-6 pita bread pieces 
 4 cups  pizza sauce 
 1 can  tomato sauce 
 1 tsp  oregano 
 1 tsp  basil 
 1 tsp  thyme 
 2 tsp crushed  red pepper flakes 
 1 cup  mozzarella 
 1/4 cup  tomato 
 1 cup cooked  Italian sausage 
 2 tbsp  cream cheese 
 Directions : 
 Combine seasonings and sauce, and simmer for 20 minutes. 
 Layer the sauce, cream cheese, cheese, tomatoes, and sauce over the pitas and bake in the oven for 5-10 minutes until the cheese is sizzling and brown.
