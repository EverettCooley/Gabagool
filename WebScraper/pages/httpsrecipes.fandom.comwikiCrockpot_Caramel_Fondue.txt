Description : 
 Keep this fondue warm in the slow cooker for easy dipping. Reheat any leftovers in the microwave, stirring at one-minute intervals until heated through.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source:  Southern Living  2007 Sweet Endings 
 Make Ahead | Prep: 12 minutes | Cook: 3 hr, 30 min 
 Makes 4½ cups or 18 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 (14 oz) packages caramels 
 2 (14 oz) cans  sweetened condensed milk  (like Eagle brand) 
 apple  slices 
 pound cake  squares 
 Directions : 
 Place caramels in a 3-qt slow cooker; stir in condensed milk. 
 Cover and cook on low 3½ hours, stirring occasionally, until caramels melt and mixture is smooth. 
 Serve with apple slices and pound cake squares.
