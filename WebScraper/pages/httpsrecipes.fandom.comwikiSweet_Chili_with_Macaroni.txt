Description : 
 This chili is really good, especially with the macaroni in it, has beef and beans too and I garnished it with sour cream, cheese, and green onions. YUMMY.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Makes 8 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds  ground beef 
 2 large  onions , chopped 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 2 tsp  chili powder 
 2 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp ground  cumin 
 1 tsp  black pepper 
 1 tsp dried  oregano 
 28 oz can whole  tomatoes 
 28 oz can  tomato sauce 
 16 oz can  red kidney beans , drained and rinsed 
 Â½ cup packed  light brown sugar 
 1 pound elbow  macaroni , cooked following package directions 
 Directions : 
 In a pot, brown beef, onion and garlic 10 minutes. 
 Add chili powder, salt, cumin, pepper and oregano. 
 Cook 2 minutes or until fragrant. 
 Drain liquid from tomatoes into pot, chop tomatoes and add. 
 Fill can with water and add to pot along with tomato sauce, beans and brown sugar. 
 Bring to a boil, lower heat and simmer uncovered 35 minutes. 
 Serve over macaroni. 
 Garnish with shredded cheddar cheese, sour cream and chopped green onions.
