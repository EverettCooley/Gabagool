Cream of Vegetable Soup
 
Servings: 1
 
 1 tb  celery 
 salt  &  pepper  to Taste
 
 1 tb  Onion 
 1 c  milk 
 1 tb  butter 
 Worcestorshire Sauce To
-Taste
 
 2 ts  flour 
 1/2 c Any Cooked Vegetable 
 1/4 ts  Chicken  Bouillon 
 
Cook  celery  And  Onion  in  butter  Until Soft. Stir in  flour , Bouillon, SaltAnd  pepper . Add  milk  And Worcestorshire Sauce. Cook Until Thickened Plus 1Minute. Blend Vegetable With Soup Until Smooth. Makes 1 Serving. SuggestedCombinations:  carrots  And  nutmeg ,  Peas  And  mint ,  potatoes  And  dill .
