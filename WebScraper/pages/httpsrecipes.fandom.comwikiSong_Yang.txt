A beautiful white tea from the Zhejiang region of China. It was first produced during the Tang Dynasty (618 - 907). Today Song Yang continues to be harvested and made exclusively by hand. About three thousand leaves go into the making of just one pound of this rare tea.
 
 Type - White Tea 
 Origin - China
