 Ingredients : 
 1 lb lean  ground beef 
 ⅓ – ⅔ cup  quick-cooking oats 
 ½ teaspoon  paprika 
 salt , to taste 
 pepper , to taste 
 cumin , to taste (optional) 
 ½ teaspoon crushed dried  hot peppers , or less 
 3 – 4 tablespoons  tomato sauce 
 1  egg , beaten 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients in a large bowl. 
 Form into 4 patties, brush with  oil  and  grill .
