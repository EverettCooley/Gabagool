 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 stephen ceideburg 
 12 ruby mist, red zinger or 
 -other fruity herbal tea 
 -bags 
 4 cup boiling  water 
 2 cup  apple juice  concentrate 
 1/4 cup freshly squeezed  lemon juice 
 -(1  lemon ) 
 2 cup cold  water 
 1  honey  to taste 
 8 thin slices of  lemon , for 
 -garnish 
 Directions : 
 Steep tea bags in the boiling  water  for 10 minutes. Gently squeeze tea bags and remove. Add  apple juice  concentrate,  lemon juice , cold  water  and  honey . Heat and serve warm in mugs. Garnish with  lemon  slices. Per serving (without  honey ): 119 calories (1 percent from protein, 97 percent from carbohydrate, 2 percent from fat), less than 1 gram protein, 30 grams carbohydrate, less than 1 gram fat, 0 cholesterol, 19 milligrams sodium. Exchanges: 2 fruit. Makes 8 servings naomi kaufman price writing in the portland oregonain"s foodday, 12/22/93
