 Serves : 10 
 Ingredients 
 1 pound  ground beef 
 1 cup  onion , chopped 
 4 cups  water 
 4 cups  tomato juice 
 1 cup  potatoes , diced 
 1 cup  carrots , diced 
 1 cup  celery , chopped 
 2 teaspoons  salt 
 1 small  bay leaf 
 pinch  basil 
 Directions 
 Brown ground beef and onions. 
 Add water and tomato juice and heat to boiling. 
 Add diced potatoes, carrots, celery, salt, bay leaf, and a small pinch of basil. 
 Simmer until vegetables are done (about 30 minutes).
