Description : 
 Contributed by Jenn B aka Mom2sam and Tiny at  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ lb  ground beef 
 5  bacon  strips, diced 
 ½ cup chopped  onions 
 2 cans (16 oz each)  pork  and beans, undrained 
 16 oz can  butter beans , rinsed and drained 
 16 oz can  kidney beans , rinsed and drained 
 ⅓ cup  brown sugar , packed 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 ¼ cup  ketchup 
 ¼ cup  barbecue sauce 
 2 tbsp  molasses 
 2 tbsp  mustard 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 In large skillet or saucepan over medium heat, brown ground  beef ,  bacon  and  onion ; drain well. 
 In large bowl, combine all of the beans. 
 Add the meat mixture and stir well. 
 Add rest of ingredients, mix well. 
 Pour into a greased 2½ qt baking dish. 
 Bake, uncovered, at 350°F for 1 hour.
