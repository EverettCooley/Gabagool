Ingredients : 
 1 can spam luncheon meat, cubed -(12 oz) 
 2 cup shredded  cabbage 
 1 cup shredded  zucchini 
 1  Onion , shredded 
 1  carrot , shredded 
 2  garlic  cloves, minced 
 1 1/2 tbsp chopped fresh  basil  leaves 
 1 tsp dried  oregano 
 1/2 tsp  red pepper flakes 
 1 can diced  tomatoes  (28 oz) 
 1 1/2 cup  instant rice 
 1 tbsp  brown sugar 
 8  bell peppers , cored & seeded 
 Directions : 
 In skillet over medium-high heat, saute spam until lightly browned; remove from skillet. 
 Add  cabbage ,  zucchini ,  Onion ,  carrot ,  garlic ,  basil ,  oregano , and  red pepper flakes  to skillet. Cook over low heat 10 minutes or until vegetables are crisp-tender. 
 Drain juice from  tomatoes ; reserve juice. Add enough  water  to  tomato juice  to make 2 cups. 
 Add juice,  tomatoes , spam,  rice , and  brown sugar  to vegetable mixture. #Cover. Cook 10 minutes. 
 In large saucepan, boil  bell peppers  in  water  5â€“7 minutes or until crisp-tender. Drain. 
 Fill  peppers  with spam mixture
