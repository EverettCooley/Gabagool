Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  bourbon 
 1 oz  sour mix 
 1 oz  orange juice 
 1 dash  grenadine 
 lemon-lime soda 
 Directions : 
 Pour all ingredients over  ice  in highball glass. 
 Fill with lemon- lime  soda and stir. 
 Add dash of  Grenadine , garnish with an  orange  wedge.
