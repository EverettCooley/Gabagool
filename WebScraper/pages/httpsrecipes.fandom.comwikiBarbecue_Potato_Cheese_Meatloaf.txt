 Description : 
 Purchased from the Bivins Estate in Dallas, Texas in 1990. Dated 1947.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups baked beans 
 2 pounds ground  Beef 
 4 slice  bread  crumbled 
 2  eggs  or substitute 
 1-cup barbecue sauce 
 1/4 cups  mustard 
 1/4 cups  ketchup 
 2 cups  potatoes  mashed 
 1-cup  Cheese 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mash up the baked beans. 
 Mix everything together except the  potatoes  and the  Cheese . 
 Add more liquid or  bread  as needed to reach desired consistency. 
 Press mixture into the bottom and around sides of two 9 x 5Â inch loaf pans, leaving a large trough in the center. Reserve about 1-1/2 cups of mixture to cover top with. 
 Fill troughs of each meatloaf with mashed  potatoes . 
 Cut  Cheese  into cubes, or grate, and put this on top of the  potatoes . 
 Cover  potatoes  and  Cheese  with reserved meat mixture. 
 Bake at 350 for 1 hour.
