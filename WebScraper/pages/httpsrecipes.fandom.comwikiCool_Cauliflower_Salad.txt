 Ingredients 
 large head  cauliflower , chipped 
 red onion 
 green bell pepper , seeded and chipped 
 2  scallions , minced 
 1 tbsp fresh  chives , chopped 
 1 cup cooked  basmati rice 
 cup bottled  vinaigrette  dressing 
 Directions 
 Place cauliflower in a steamer basket over boiling water. 
 Cover pan and steam about 3 minutes, until almost tender. Chill. 
 Combine next 5 ingredients in a bowl. 
 Add chilled cauliflower, drizzle with dressing and serve chilled.
