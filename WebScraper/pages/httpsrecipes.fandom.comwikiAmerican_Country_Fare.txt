      American Country Fare     
 Ingredients : 
 1 box deluxe Kraft  Macaroni & Cheese Dinner Mix 
 1 lb  smoked sausage , cut diagonally into 1-inch chunks 
 1½ cups fresh  broccoli  florets 
 1 cup  red peppers  or  green peppers , strips 
 2 tablespoons  green onions , slices 
 ½ teaspoon dried  oregano 
 Directions : 
 Boil  macaroni  for about ten minutes. 
 Drain and then stir in the pouch of  cheese  that comes in the box. 
 Set aside. 
 Cook  sausage  in a large  skillet  over medium heat for 6 minutes. 
 Add vegetables and  oregano . 
 Cook until vegetables are tender. 
 Add  macaroni  and  cheese  and mix lightly.
