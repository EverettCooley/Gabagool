A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ tsp instant  coffee 
 ½ cup  water 
 2 tsp  powdered sugar 
 2 dashes  bitters 
 1 oz  bourbon 
 2 oz  club soda 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve coffee in  water ; stir in  Sugar ,  bitters , and  Bourbon . 
 Add soda and pour over  ice  in old-fashioned glass. 
 Garnish with  orange  slice and  cherry
