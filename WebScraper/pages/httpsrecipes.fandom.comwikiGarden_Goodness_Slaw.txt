Description : 
 "For a different taste and texture, try this slaw made with  Chinese cabbage ." 
 
 Original recipe yield: 4 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups  cabbage , shredded 
 1 small sweet  green pepper , trimmed, seeded, and chopped 
 1 cup  celery , sliced 
 ¼ cup  green onion , chopped 
 1 (2 ounce) jar chopped  pimento , drained 
 ½ teaspoon  dill weed 
 ½ cup Marzetti® slaw dressing 
 Directions : 
 Mix together  cabbage ,  peppers ,  celery ,  onions , and pimentos. 
 Add dill and dressing to salad, chill and serve.
