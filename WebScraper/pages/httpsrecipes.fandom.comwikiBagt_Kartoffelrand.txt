Description : 
 Baked potato ring
 
 Serves 6 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  potatoes  — grated 
 ½ cup melted  butter 
 2  eggs  — beaten 
 1 teaspoon  onion  — grated 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  paprika 
 ½ cup  milk 
 ½ cup sharp  cheddar  cheese — grated 
 Directions : 
 Combine grated raw  potatoes  with melted  butter , beaten  eggs ,  onion ,  salt  and  paprika , and mix well. 
 Place in a well buttered baking dish and pour the  milk  over mixture. 
 Bake 40 minutes in a preheated 350ºF oven. 
 Remove from oven and sprinkle with grated  cheese . 
 Return to oven and bake until  cheese  has melted and browned.
