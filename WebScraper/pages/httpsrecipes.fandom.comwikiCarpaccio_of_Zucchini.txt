      Carpaccio of Zucchini     
 Prep Time: 10 minutes 
 Cook time: 15 minutes 
 Serves: 4-6 
 
 Description : 
 It really can't get much simplier than this! Use the freshest zucchini possible & this will be just delightful!
 
 Ingredients : 
 zucchini 
 salt 
 pepper 
 olive oil 
 lemon 
 parmesan 
 Directions : 
 Use mandolin or slicer, slice zucchini very very thinly - paper thin. 
 Lay out one layer on a plate or plater. 
 Crack of pepper, crack of salt, drizzle olive oil, squeeze of lemon and shave parmesan over the top. 
 Let sit for 15 minutes and serve.
