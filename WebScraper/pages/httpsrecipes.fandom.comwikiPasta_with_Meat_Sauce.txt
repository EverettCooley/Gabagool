Ingredients : 
 1 lb of  spaghetti  or  fettuccine 
 2 lbs of  ground beef 
 8 very ripe  tomatoes  sliced 
 1 small  red onion  diced into small cubes 
 2 tablespoons of  olive oil 
 2  red peppers 
 1 pinch of  oregano 
 black pepper 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Put the  ground beef  in a bowl, mix in the  onion , add a pinch of  oregano , a pinch of  pepper  and a teaspoon of  salt . 
 In a shallow pan heat a tablespoon of  oil . Add the  ground beef  and stir so it cooks uniformly. Add two tablespoons of  water  and the  tomatoes . Let simmer at low heat. Adjust  water  when necessary. 
 When the  meat  is ready let the liquid evaporate to make a thick sauce. Adjust  salt  to taste. Turn off the heat and reserve. 
 Boil the  pasta  according to the instructions in the package. When it is ready top with the  beef  sauce and serve immediately.
