Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 6 ½ slices French or  Italian bread , lightly brushed with  olive oil , rubbed with clove of  garlic  and toasted lightly 
 2 medium ripe avocados, pitted and 1/2 inch diced 
 1/8 tsp seasoning  salt 
 1/8 tsp  garlic powder 
 2 Tbsp diced  tomatoes  with green chiles 
 1 ½ tsp  lime juice 
 Directions : 
 Prepare bread as directed above. 
 In bowl gently toss remaining ingredients, chill and serve with freshly toasted bread. 
 Serves 4 as an appetizer if bread slices are cut in thirds.
