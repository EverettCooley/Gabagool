Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 8 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups frozen  watermelon  chunks 
 ½ cup frozen limeade concentrate 
 ½ cup plain  yogurt 
 ⅓ cup  sour cream 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Process frozen watermelon chunks, frozen limeade concentrate and yogurt, sour cream and sugar until consistency of soft serve ice cream.
