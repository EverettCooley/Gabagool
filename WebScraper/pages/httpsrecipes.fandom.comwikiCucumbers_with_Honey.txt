Description : 
 An old European dish from the Gothic period. Invented by Jogaila, Grand Duke of Lithuania.   
 Later spread to Poland and other eastern European countries.  
 
 Ingredients : 
 4  cucumbers 
 50 g  honey 
 Directions : 
 Cut fresh-salted or fresh  cucumbers  in rings and pour over with honey. 
 Serve with pancakes or  rye bread .
