Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 	cups 	 all-purpose flour 
 2/3 	cup 	 Sugar 
 1/2 	tsp 	 salt 
 3/4 	cup 	(1-1/2 sticks) cold  butter  or  margarine 
 2 		 eggs 
 1 	can 	(14 oz)  sweetened condensed milk  (not  evaporated milk ) 
 1 1/2 	tsp 	Maple flavor 
 2 	cups 	Chopped  nuts 
 1 	cup 	HERSHEY'S  semi-sweet chocolate  Chips 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 350 °F. 
 In large bowl, stir together  flour ,  Sugar  and  salt ; cut in  butter  until crumbly. 
 Stir in 1 beaten  egg . 
 Press mixture evenly onto bottom of ungreased 13x9x2-inch baking pan. 
 Bake 25 minutes. 
 Meanwhile, in medium bowl, beat  sweetened condensed milk , remaining 1  egg  and maple flavor; stir in  nuts . 
 Sprinkle  chocolate chips  evenly over prepared crust. 
 Gently spread nut mixture evenly over  chocolate . 
 Return to oven. 
 Bake 25 minutes or until golden. 
 Cool completely in pan on wire rack. 
 Cut into bars. 
 Store tightly covered at room temperature.
