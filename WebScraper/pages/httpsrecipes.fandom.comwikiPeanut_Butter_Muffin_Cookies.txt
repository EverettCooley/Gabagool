 Prep Time: 5-10 minutes 
 Cook time:10-12 minutes 
 Serves: As many as needed 
 
 Description : 
 Chocolate chip cookies with peanut butter cups in the shape of a mini muffin
 
 Ingredients : 
 chocolate chip cookie dough 
 peanut butter cups 
 mini muffin cooking tin 
 non-stick cooking spray 
 Directions : 
 Spray the muffin tin with the cooking spray. 
 Fill each muffin holder half way with chocolate chip cookie dough. 
 Once the tin is full, place in the oven for 7 – 8 minutes. 
 Pull out the tin and place one peanut butter cup into the middle of the cookie dough in each holder. 
 Put the tin back in the oven for another 2 – 3 minutes to finish baking the cookies and to slightly melt the peanut butter cup. 
 Let the cookie cool before taking them out of the tray. 
 Enjoy!.
