 Ingredients : 
 2 shots Stolichnaya  vodka 
 1 shot Warninks  advocaat 
 ½ shot Stolichnaya  vanilla   vodka 
 ½ shot  bourbon 
 1 bar spoon  sugar syrup 
 garnish: sprinkle of  nutmeg 
 glass: martini 
 Directions : 
 Stir all ingredients with  ice , in a cocktail shaker and strain into a Martini glass.
