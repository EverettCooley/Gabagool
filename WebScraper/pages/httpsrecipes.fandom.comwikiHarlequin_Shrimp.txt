This is a great finger-food appetizer.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 tablespoons extra virgin  olive oil 
 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed  lemon juice 
 salt 
 freshly ground  black pepper 
 24 large cold  water   Shrimp , cooked and de-veined 
 1 large  green pepper , de-seeded and finely diced 
 1 large  red pepper , de-seeded and finely diced 
 1 bunch fresh  chives , snipped 
 fresh dill or fresh  chervil  or fresh  parsley 
 Directions : 
 In a small saucepan, put  olive oil  and  lemon juice . 
 When warm enough (do not let mixture boil), add  red peppers  and seasoning. 
 Saute for 2 â€“ 3 mins. 
 Add in  Shrimp  and toss for a few minutes. 
 Arrange  Shrimp  on platter. Sprinkle with  pepper  and  chives . Place sprigs of fresh herbs all around.
