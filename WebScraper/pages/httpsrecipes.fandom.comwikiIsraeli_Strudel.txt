 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 c.  flour 
 2 stick  butter 
 1 c.  sour cream 
 3 tbsp.  Sugar 
 1 tsp.  vanilla 
 1 tsp.  baking powder 
 -- FILLING: 
 Grated  Apple 
 raspberry  jam 
 3 oz.  coconut 
 6 tbsp.  Sugar 
 1/2 c.  Raisins 
 1/4 c.  walnuts 
 1/2 c.  cocoa powder 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients for dough. 
 Roll out into long wide strips. 
 Spread jam on each strip. 
 Then add grated  Apple ,  coconut ,  Sugar ,  Raisins ,  walnuts  and  cocoa . 
 Close dough strips lengthwise. 
 Place seam side down on sprayed cookie sheet and bake at 350 degrees until golden. 
 Makes 2 strips approximately 12-15Â inches long. 
 Cut into slices.
