Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  ground cooked beef 
 ¼ cup ground  peanuts 
 4 small boiled  potatoes 
 4 cups  beef broth 
 2  plantains  (1 grated) 
 1 teaspoon  onion  (minced) 
 ½ tablespoon  parsley 
 1 large chopped  cabbage  leaf 
 2 tablespoons  oil 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Boil one of the  plantains  in the  beef broth  for 15 minutes. 
 Save the broth. 
 Heat 1 tablespoon of the  oil  and brown the  onion ,  peanuts  and  beef  in it. 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste. 
 Combine the grated  plantain  with the cooked one and the other tablespoon of  oil  and mix well. 
 Shape into small balls of this and put a tablespoon of the meat mixture inside the balls. 
 Cut the  potatoes  into pieces, and add the  potatoes  and  cabbage  to the broth. 
 About 2 minutes before its ready to serve, add the balls to the broth. 
 Sprinkle with  parsley  and serve.
