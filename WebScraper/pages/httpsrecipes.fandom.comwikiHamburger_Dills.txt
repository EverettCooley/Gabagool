Description : 
 Contributed by Delma at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 4 quarts 
 Ingredients : 
 cucumbers  (sliced, enough to fill 4 quart jars) 
 1 slice  onion 
 1 clove  garlic 
 1 sprig of  dill 
 1 pint  water 
 1 quart  vinegar 
 2½ cups  sugar 
 ½ cup  salt 
 1 tsp  alum 
 Directions : 
 Place  onion  slice, clove of  garlic  and  dill  in quart jar. 
 Boil water, vinegar, sugar, salt and alum 
 Slice  cucumbers  (the size of silver dollars) into sterilized jars. 
 Pour boiling liquid over pickles. 
 Add spray of  dill  on top of jar and seal.
