Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  sweet potatoes  (grated) 
 2 – 4 oz (900 g) root  ginger  (grated) 
 ¾ – 1 lb (2 cups / 450 g)  brown sugar 
 1 cup (112 g)  raisins  (optional) 
 2 tsp (10 ml)  vanilla 
 1 tsp (5 ml)  nutmeg 
 2 tbsp (30 ml) melted  margarine 
 4 cups  grated coconut , squeezed to extract the  milk   or  use  evaporated milk 
 Directions : 
 Grease baking tins or pyrex dishes. 
 Preheat oven to 450 °F /225 °C. 
 Wash, peel and grate  potatoes . 
 Grate  coconut . 
 Add four cups, squeeze to extract  milk  or use  evaporated milk . 
 Put grated  potato  into large bowl. 
 Add  sugar ,  vanilla ,  nutmeg ,  raisin  and  ginger . 
 Mix well. 
 Add  milk  and  margarine . 
 Mix well. 
 Put in baking dishes/pans. 
 Put on bottom shelf of oven after 30–45 minutes, then remove to top shelf, lower oven to 350 °F. 
 Cook until brown or a knife inserted comes out clean. 
 About 75–90 minutes.
