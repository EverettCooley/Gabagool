Description : 
 Contributed by Lil at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Old Newspaper Clipping 
 6 Sandwiches 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  butter 
 ¼ cup grated  onion 
 ¼ cup  prepared mustard 
 1 tbsp  poppy seeds 
 6 large buns or onion  rolls 
 shaved cooked  ham 
 Swiss cheese 
 Directions : 
 Cream together  butter ,  onion ,  mustard  and  poppy seeds . 
 Spread on both sides of buns. 
 Put on  ham  and  cheese . 
 Wrap in foil and heat 15 minutes in heated 350°F oven.
