Beer measures : 
 nip  - ⅓  pint . Barley wine was usually bottled in nips. 
 small  - ½  pint 
 large  - 1  pint 
 flagon  - 1  quart 
 pin  - 4½  gallons 
 firkin  - 9  gallons 
 anker  - 10  gallons 
 kilderkin  - 18  gallons 
 barrel  - 36  gallons 
 hogshead  - 54  gallons 
 tun  - 216  gallons 
 Spirit measures : 
 gill  - ¼  pint
