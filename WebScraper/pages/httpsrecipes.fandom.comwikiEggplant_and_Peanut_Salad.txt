Ingredients : 
 2 medium  eggplants , peeled and chopped 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 3 tsp  olive oil 
 juice of one  lemon 
 ¼ cup of  peanuts , finely chopped 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 Tabasco  to taste 
 4 tsp  olive oil 
 Directions : 
 Cover  eggplant  in  water . 
 Cook for 30 minutes. 
 Drain and press to remove excess  water . 
 Mix  eggplant  with remaining ingredients. 
 Sauté in  olive oil  for 5 minutes. 
 Mash together.
