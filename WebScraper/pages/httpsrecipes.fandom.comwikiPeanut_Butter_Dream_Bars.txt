Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: old newspaper clipping 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cup  quick oats , uncooked 
 1½ cups  flour 
 1 cup chopped  peanuts 
 1 cup firmly packed  brown sugar 
 1 tsp  soda 
 ¾ tsp  salt 
 1 cup melted  butter 
 1 (14 oz) can  sweetened condensed milk 
 ⅓ cup  peanut butter 
 1 cup M&M's™ plain candies 
 Directions : 
 Combine oats, flour, peanuts, sugar, soda and salt; mix well. 
 Add butter; mix until dry ingredients are thoroughly moistened and mixture resembles coarse crumbs. 
 Reserve 1½ cups crumb mixture; press remaining mixture onto bottom of greased 15½"x10½" jelly-roll pan. 
 Bake at 375°F for 12 minutes. 
 Combine condensed milk and peanut butter in small bowl, mixing until sell blended. 
 Spread over partially baked crust to within ¼" from edge. 
 Combine reserved crumb mixture and candies; sprinkle evenly over condensed milk mixture, pressing in lightly. 
 Continue baking for 20 – 22 minutes or until golden brown. 
 Cool thoroughly; cut in bars.
