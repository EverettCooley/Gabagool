Description : 
 This is a delicious, healthy cold soup perfect for a hot summer day.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ fresh genuine  California avocado , without skin 
 ¼ cup of fresh  carrot juice  (approx. 1 large  carrot ) 
 ½  cucumber , peeled and cut into small chunks 
 optional: seasoning to taste ( dill , etc.) 
 Directions : 
 Place ingredients into blender in order of recipe and blend till smooth.
