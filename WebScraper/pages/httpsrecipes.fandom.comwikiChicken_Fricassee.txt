       Chicken Fricassee     
 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 1 stewing hen, about 1Â½ kg (3 pounds) 
 lightly salted  water 
 3 tbsp  butter 
 3 tbsp  flour 
 salt 
 1 tsp ground  white pepper 
 2 tsp  curry powder 
 grated rind of 1  orange 
 2 dl  whipping cream 
 1 small bunch  parsley , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Poach the hen in salted  water  about 90 minutes. 
 Cool it. 
 Cut the meat into bite-sized pieces. 
 Melt the  butter  and stir in the  flour . 
 Gradually add the cooking liquid. 
 Season with  salt ,  pepper ,  curry  powder, and  orange  rind. 
 Simmer 5 minutes. 
 Add the  cream  and simmer 5 minutes more. 
 Stir in  parsley . 
 Serve the  chicken  in the sauce with boiled  potatoes ,  peas ,  carrot  cubes, and  cauliflower .
