Description : 
 A Cocktail made with cream, Crème de Menthe and white Creme de cacao. Because it's very sweet, a grasshopper is usually served after dinner. 
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz. green  crème de menthe 
 1 oz.  light cream 
 1 oz. white  crème de cacao 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients with cracked  ice  in a shaker or blender and strain into a chilled cocktail glass. 
 Strain the ingredients into a shot glass to create a Grasshopper Shooter.
