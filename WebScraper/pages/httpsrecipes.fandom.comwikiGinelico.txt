Ingredients : 
 2 oz  gin 
 ½ oz  Frangelico ®  hazelnut liqueur 
 1 pinch  nutmeg 
 Directions : 
 Stir  gin  and  Frangelico   hazelnut liqueur  with  ice , strain into a cold cocktail glass, and garnish with some freshly grated  nutmeg .
