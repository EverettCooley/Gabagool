 Description : 
 Makes 4 to 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 1 medium  onion , chopped 
 1  green pepper , chopped 
 3 cups cooked  brown rice 
 2 x 16-ounce cans Mexican style  pinto beans 
 Â¾ cup hickory smoke  barbecue sauce 
 4 to 6 whole  grain  buns 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in large skillet over medium-high heat. 
 Add  onion  and  green pepper ; cook 2 to 3 minutes. 
 Add  rice ,  beans  and barbecue sauce. Simmer 10 to 15 minutes, until heated through. 
 Serve on buns.
