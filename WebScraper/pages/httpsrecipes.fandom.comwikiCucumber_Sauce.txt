Description : 
 In Romanian: Sos de castraveti
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  lard 
 1 tablespoon  flour 
 1 small  onion 
 2 pickled  cucumbers 
 1 tablespoon  tomato sauce 
 1 tablespoon  vinegar 
 1 teaspoon  sugar 
 2 cups  beef broth  (or  water ) 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Fry the finely chopped  onion  in  lard  with the  flour  until it turns yellow. 
 Pour the broth and add  salt ,  sugar ,  tomato  sauce and thinly sliced pickled  cucumbers . 
 Let everything boil for a few minutes, then add the  vinegar  and let simmer until the sauce thickens somewhat. 
 Serve warm.
