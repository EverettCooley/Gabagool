Description : 
 This tasty dish is a cousin to scalloped ham and potatoes. You could top with cheese if desired. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 center-cut ham slice (about 1-1.5 lbs) 
 1 medium onion, diced 
 2-3 T. flour 
 7 medium potatoes, peeled and sliced 
 1 c. water, milk or soy milk 
 butter or margarine 
 Directions : 
 Cut or shred ham slice into bite-size pieces and place in a casserole dish 
 Sprinkle with diced onion and flour 
 Cover with sliced potatoes 
 Pour milk or water over the top 
 Dot with margarine or butter 
 Cover with saran and microwave on high for 5 minutes 
 Immediately remove saran, cover with foil and bake at 325F for one hour. 
 Remove foil and bake an additional 15 minutes
