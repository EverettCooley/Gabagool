 Description : 
 Makes 8 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1/4 cup chopped  chives 
 1 cup grated  carrots 
 1 1/2 cups cooked  green peas 
 1/4 cup diced pimientos 
 1 cup diced  Cheddar   Cheese 
 1 6-ounce package sliced boiled  Ham , cut into thin strips* 
 1/2 of a 0.7 ounce package dry Italian dressing mix (about 1 tablespoon) 
 3/4 cup  Mayonnaise 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  chives ,  carrots ,  Peas , pimientos,  Cheese  and half of the  Ham  in large mixing bowl. Blend  salad dressing  into  Mayonnaise . Add to  rice  and vegetables and toss lightly. Cool. Arrange on salad greens with remaining  Ham  on top. Garnish with  tomato  wedges, if desired.
