Description : 
 Contributed by   Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: my old recipes 
 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  bacon 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 3 large  eggs 
 16 oz  linguine 
 ⅓ cup  parsley , chopped 
 2 oz  Parmesan cheese , grated (½ cup) 
 ⅛ tbsp  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Sauté  bacon  and  garlic  in skillet until  bacon  is crisp. 
 Crumble and set aside. 
 Beat  eggs  in serving bowl. 
 Cook  linguine  as directed. 
 Drain thoroughly and immediately toss into  eggs  until  eggs  stick to  linguine  strands. 
 Sprinkle with  bacon  and  parsley . 
 Toss until mixed through. 
 Add  cheese  and  pepper . 
 Toss again until mixed. 
 Serve at once.
