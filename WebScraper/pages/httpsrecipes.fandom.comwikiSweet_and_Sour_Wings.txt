Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 lb  wings  (28 pieces or 18 drumettes) 
 1 cup packed  brown sugar 
 ¼ cup  flour 
 ½ cup  water 
 ¼ cup  white vinegar 
 1½ tbsp  ketchup 
 ¼ cup  soy sauce 
 ¼ tbsp  garlic powder 
 1 tbsp  onion flakes 
 ½ tbsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Cook on low 8 or 9 hours.
