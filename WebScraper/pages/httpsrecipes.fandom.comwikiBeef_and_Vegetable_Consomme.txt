Description : 
 Serves 6.
 
 Ingredients : 
 430 g can  beef   consommé 
 1¾ cups water 
 ¾ cup  orange juice 
 2  carrots 
 2  zucchini 
 Directions : 
 Place consommé in pan, add water and strained orange juice, bring to boil. 
 Reduce heat, simmer and uncovered 2 minutes. 
 Cut carrots and zucchini into thin strips. 
 Bring pan of water to boil, add carrots and zucchini, cook, uncovered, 2 minutes 
 Drain, rinse under cold running water. 
 Add vegetables to soup. 
 Refrigerate covered until chilled.
