Description : 
 In Romanian: Marinata pentru vinat
 
 Ingredients : 
 1.5 qts / 1,5 l  water 
 1 qt / 1 l  wine vinegar 
 ½ teaspoon ground  juniper berries 
 ½ teaspoon ground  pepper 
 5 – 6  bay leaves 
 peel from ½  lemon 
 1 tablespoon  salt 
 5 – 6  cloves 
 2 – 3  garlic  cloves 
 Directions : 
 Boil the  water  with the  wine vinegar ,  juniper berries ,  pepper ,  bay leaves , cloves,  garlic , peel from ½  lemon  (in pieces) and a tablespoon of  salt  in a non-reactive pot. 
 Boil everything for 5 minutes. 
 Let cool and pour over the venison placed in a bowl. 
 Keep it like this for 24 hours. 
 The liquid must cover the venison. 
 If not, you have to turn the venison every 5 – 6 hours.
