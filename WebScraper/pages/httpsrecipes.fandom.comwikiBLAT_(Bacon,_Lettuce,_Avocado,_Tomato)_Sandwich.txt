 Description : 
 This wonderful "Mouth Party" is great between  bread  slices or rolled on a tortilla and sliced as a pinwheel "Appeteaser". Also, a small dollop on a cracker works well too. Fine dicing is the key to success.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 Med.  California avocado 
 1 Med.  tomato 
 2  green onions 
 10 Slices Frozen  Bacon 
 ¼ Head of  lettuce 
 ½ Cup  Mayonnaise  (or to taste) 
 8 Slices  bread 
 Optional; Tortillas, Crackers 
 Directions : 
 Finely dice  tomato , and  avocado . 
 Chop  Onion  in small pieces,and shred finely the  lettuce .
 Slice thin strips of frozen  Bacon , and fry the  Bacon  until crisp,and drain off grease. 
 Put all ingredients into mixing bowl and add  Mayonnaise , and stir,until everything is well combined.
 Spread onto  bread  slicesfor sandwiches, or spread thin layer on tortilla and roll up. 
 Slice about 3/4 inch wide, or put 1/2 teaspoon of mixture on each cracker.
