Ingredients very similar to the Greek loukoumades : 
 12 oz  flour 
 8 oz  sugar 
 4  eggs 
 2 tsp  cinnamon 
 1 tsp  mixed spice 
 4 oz  butter 
 ½ tsp  yeast  fat for frying 
 Directions : 
 Coating: 1 and ½ lb  sugar  1 pint  water . 
 Mix the  yeast  with a little  sugar , then add the mixed dry ingredients. 
 Beat the  eggs  and add to the mixture. 
 Knead all together and leave to stand for 1 hour. 
 Roll out and cut into rectangles. 
 Cut 2 slits down the length (1/4 inch from top) 
 Plait 3 strips and press ends firmly into each other 
 Fry in deep fat. 
 When cooked,  DIP  into a syrup ( don't leave in syrup ) made from the 1 and ½ lb  sugar  and  water  boiled together with a little more  cinnamon  for taste.
