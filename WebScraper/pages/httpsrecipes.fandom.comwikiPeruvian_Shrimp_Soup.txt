 Description : 
       Peruvian Shrimp Soup     
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cup  shrimp 
 1 sm  Onion 
 1 clv  garlic 
 2 tbl  tomato  paste 
 8 cup  water 
 1 x  salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 2 tbl  rice 
 3 sm  potatoes 
 2 x  eggs 
 2 tbl  cottage cheese 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Cut  Onion  and  garlic  in pieces. 
 Brown in  butter . 
 Add  tomato  paste and  water . 
 Boil. 
 Add  salt  and  pepper  to taste. 
 And  rice  and  potatoes  cut in 1-inch pieces. 
 Replace boiled away  water  with  milk . 
 Beat  eggs  and add to soup. 
 Add  cottage cheese ,  oregano , and  shrimp . 
 Serve hot.
