 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 bunch fresh  cilantro 
 1/4 cup fresh dill 
 juice from 1/2 a  lemon 
 4  garlic  cloves (more or less to your taste) 
 1/2 cup tahini 
 2 cans garbanzo beans 
 1/4 teaspoon  chili powder 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 The herbs must be fresh. Removing the stems from the  cilantro , blend everything except for garbanzo beans together. Then add the garbanzo beans and blend until smooth. Adjust amounts to taste.
