Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: North Carolina Regiment of Young Marines, page 17 
 Recipe by: YM Rect. Daniel McGill, Camp LeJeune 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (2 l)  Mountain Dew 
 32 oz  ginger ale 
 1 large can  pineapple juice 
 1 pkg.  Kool-Aid  (kind of Kool-Aid makes the color) 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together and freeze to slush.
