Ingredients : 
 2 or 3 large ripe  tomatoes 
 1  onion  finely chopped 
 1 tbsp.  olive oil 
 25 - 50 g.  butter 
 chopped  garlic  to taste (optional) 
 8  eggs 
 salt  and  pepper 
 1 tbsp.  parsley  or  mint  chopped 
 Directions : 
 Peel deseed, and chop the  tomatoes , cook  Onion  in  oil  and a little  butter  until soft, add the  garlic  and  tomatoes  cook all for further 5 mins. 
 Beat the  eggs  lightly with the  salt  and  pepper  and pour them on to the  tomatoes  and  onions  adding the remaining  butter  and  parsley  or  mint , stir continuously over low heat until the  eggs  are barely set, continue to stir once over the heat.
