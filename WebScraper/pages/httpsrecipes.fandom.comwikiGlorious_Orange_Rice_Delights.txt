Description : 
 Makes 10 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 3 cups  milk 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 1 teaspoon grated  orange peel 
 ⅛ teaspoon  salt 
 1 x 3-ounce package  orange  flavor  gelatin 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 1 cup  whipping cream 
 1 x 11-ounce can  mandarin orange  segments, chopped and drained 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  milk ,  sugar ,  butter ,  orange peel  and  salt  in medium saucepan. 
 Bring to a boil. 
 Reduce heat; simmer 25 minutes or until thick and creamy, stirring frequently. 
 Stir in  gelatin  and  vanilla ; cool. 
 Whip  cream . 
 Fold  cream  and  oranges  into  gelatin  mixture. 
 Spoon into 10 individual dessert molds. 
 Chill until firm. 
 Unmold onto dessert plates. 
 Garnish with additional  whipped cream  and  chocolate   jimmies , if desired.
