Ingredients : 
 5 cups  rhubarb , diced 
 4 cups  sugar 
 6 oz  strawberry Jell-O 
 Directions : 
 Mix  rhubarb  and  sugar  together and let stand overnight. 
 In morning, cook mixture until  rhubarb  is soft. 
 Remove from heat and stir in Jell-O until it is dissolved. 
 Jar and seal.
