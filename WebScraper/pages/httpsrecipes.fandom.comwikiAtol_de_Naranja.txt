 Description : 
 Orange custard
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 big  oranges 
 cinnamon  sticks to taste 
 Â½ cup  maicena 
 2 cups  sugar 
 1 tsp  butter 
 Directions : 
 Wash the  oranges , cut them in half and extract carefully the juice. 
 Keep the skin halves and remove partitions.. 
 Mix the juice, the  sugar  and the  maicena  and bring to a boil stirring constantly. 
 When it starts boiling remove from stove and add a teaspoon of  butter . 
 Refill the skin halves with the custard and refrigerate until it sets. 
 Decorate with  mint  leaves or  cherries .
