 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1  Onion , chopped 
 2 or 3  garlic  cloves, minced 
 1 teaspoon minced piment, or to taste 
 2 tablespoons  olive oil  or other  oil 
 1/2 cup small dry  Lentils , washed 
 6+ cups  water 
 1/2 cup  rice , well washed 
 2  Chicken  bouillon cubes 
 1 small can  tomato  paste 
 2-3  tomatoes , cut up 
 2-3 small  zucchini  or other green vegetable 
 curry  powder, if desired 
 salt  and  pepper , to taste 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Saut√©  Onion ,  garlic , piment and  olive oil  in a large pot. 
 Add  Lentils  and 3 C  water . 
 Cook 20 minutes or so. 
 Add  rice , 3 C  water  and bouillon cubes and cook for 20 more minutes. 
 Add  tomato  paste,  tomatoes ,  zucchini  and more  water  if soup is too thick. 
 Cook about 10 more minutes, until the green vegetables are tender. 
 Season to taste.
