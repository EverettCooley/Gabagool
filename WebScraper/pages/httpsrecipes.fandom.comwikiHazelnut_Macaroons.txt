 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3  egg  whites 
 2 cups  powdered sugar 
 2 tsp  lemon juice 
 2 cups finely  ground hazelnuts 
 Directions : 
 1. Beat  egg  whites until very stiff. 
 2. Gradually beat in  Sugar ; continue beating until glossy. 
 3. Add  lemon juice . 
 4. Reserve 3 tbsp. of mixture; into the remainder, fold  ground hazelnuts . 
 5. Drop heaping tsp. on prepared baking sheet. 
 6. Put a small dab of reserved mixture on each and press in a  hazelnut  in the middle. 
 7. Bake at 325 degrees for 20 minutes.
