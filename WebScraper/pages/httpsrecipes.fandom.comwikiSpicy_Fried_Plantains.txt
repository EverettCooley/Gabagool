Description : 
 A sweet and spicy version of the fried banana fritters is deep-fried and added with spices to perfection. Powdered sugar rarely works for this, too.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4-6  plantain   bananas  slightly ripe, peeled and cut into bite-sized cubes 
 ½ tsp  cayenne pepper  or red pepper 
 ½ peeled, grated  fresh ginger  root 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 2 tbsp.  water , lukewarm only 
 1-2 cups  vegetable oil  for frying 
 Directions : 
 Grind together grated  ginger root ,  pepper , and  salt , and then mix with  water . In a glass bowl, toss together the  plantain  cubes and spice mixture. 
 In a deep skillet, heat  oil  up to 350° F. Fry  plantains , turning once, until golden brown on both sides. 
 Drain on absorbent paper, keep in warmed oven until all the  plantains  are fried. Serve immediately.
