Ingredients : 
 400 g tinned  red kidney beans , drained 
 1 tablespoon chopped dried  parsley 
 1 clove  garlic , sliced 
 2 tablespoons  olive oil 
 1 small  onion , chopped 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 Â½ teaspoon  cayenne pepper 
 Directions : 
 Fry the  garlic ,  onion ,  salt  and  pepper  in the  oil , until the  onion  is soft. 
 Add the  parsley  and beans and fry until the bean skins start to split. 
 Remove from the heat. 
 Serve hot.
