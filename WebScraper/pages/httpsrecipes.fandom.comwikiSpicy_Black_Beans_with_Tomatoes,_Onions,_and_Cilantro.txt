 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tbl  butter 
 2 tbl  olive oil 
 1 med  Onion , chopped 
 2 cl  garlic , minced or pressed 
 1 Green  bell pepper , seeded, deribbed, and chopped 
 1 Red  bell pepper , seeded, deribbed, and chopped 
 2 cup  black beans , cooked 
 1/2 tsp  salt 
 6  plum   tomatoes , peeled, seeded, and chopped 
 2 tbl  olive oil 
 1/4 cup  cilantro , freshly chopped, or  parsley 
 1/4 tsp  cayenne pepper , ground 
 5  parsley  sprigs, for garnish 
 Directions : 
 In a large saute pan, heat the  butter  and  olive oil  over medium heat. Add the  Onion ,  garlic , and green and  red peppers , and saute 4â€“6 minutes, until  onions  are translucent and vegetables are tender. 
 Add the  black beans  and  salt  to taste. Add  tomatoes  and  olive oil . Let cook another 5 minutes, until hot throughout. Add  cilantro  and mix well. 
 Add  cayenne pepper  to taste and serve hot, garnished with sprigs of  parsley .
