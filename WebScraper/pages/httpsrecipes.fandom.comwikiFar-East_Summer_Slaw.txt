Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Makes 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x 16 oz bag coleslaw mix or ½ head  cabbage , thinly sliced (about 7 cups) 
 1 cup chopped  radishes 
 ⅓ cup  rice vinegar 
 3 tablespoons  brown sugar 
 2 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 ½ teaspoon  garlic salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  ginger 
 ¼ teaspoon crushed  red pepper 
 Directions : 
 Toss coleslaw mix and radishes. 
 Set aside. 
 Mix other ingredients well. 
 Pour dressing over slaw; toss. 
 Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 Calories 87 | Fat 5g | Carbs 11g | Sodium 101 mg | Fiber 2g
