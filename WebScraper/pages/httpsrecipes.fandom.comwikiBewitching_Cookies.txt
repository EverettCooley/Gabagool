Bewitching Cookies
 Categories: Cookies
 Yield: 72 Servings
 
 3/4 c  shortening 
 1/4 c  unsalted butter 
 1 1/2 c Packed  light brown sugar 
 2 lg  eggs ; Lightly Beaten 
 2 tb Grated  orange peel 
 1/4 c  orange juice 
 1 ts  vanilla 
 1 c  buttermilk 
 3 1/2 c Sifted  all-purpose flour 
 2 ts  baking powder 
 1 ts  baking soda 
 1/4 ts  salt 
 1 c Semisweet  chocolate morsels 
 
Preheat Oven to 350 Degrees. Using Electric Mixer, Cream TogetherShortening,  butter  And  brown sugar  in Large Bowl Until Light And Fluffy.Beat in  eggs ,  orange peel ,  orange juice ,  vanilla  And  buttermilk  UntilWell-Combined. Sift Together  flour ,  baking powder ,  baking soda  And  salt  InSeparate Bowl. Blend Into  butter  Mixture Until Combined. Stir in ChocolateMorsels. Drop Batter by Teaspoonfuls Onto 2 Greased Baking Sheets. Bake 12To 15 Minutes or Until Brown Around Edges And Bottoms. Yield: 6 Dozen.
 
Enjoy.
