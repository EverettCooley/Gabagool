Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 This recipe yields about 1 cup of vinaigrette. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  cider vinegar 
 1 cup  water 
 ½ cup  salad oil 
 ½ cup  catsup 
 ¼ cup  chili sauce 
 ¼ cup  granulated sugar 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ⅛ teaspoon  pepper 
 ¼ teaspoon dry  mustard 
 ⅛ teaspoon  paprika 
 1 small  onion , finely grated 
 Directions : 
 Combine ingredients in a bowl. 
 Beat together with a rotary beater. 
 Store in a covered jar in refrigerator. 
 Shake well before using.
