Description : 
 Fava beans - This way of preparing fava beans, which are commonly eaten as a breakfast food in Sudan. The ingredients tend to be common for the dish but may be varied in their quantities. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x 16-ounce can cooked  fava beans 
 1 large  onion , chopped 
 1 large  tomato , diced 
 1½ tablespoons  olive oil 
 1 teaspoon  cumin  powder 
 ¼ cup  parsley , chopped 
 juice of 2  lemons 
 salt ,  pepper , and  red chili pepper  to taste 
 pita  bread (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Pour the beans into a pot and bring to a boil for about 1 – 2 hours.. Mix them well and add remaining ingredients. 
 Bring to a boil again, then reduce to medium heat and cook for about 5 minutes. 
 This dish is usually eaten with bread (Pita).
