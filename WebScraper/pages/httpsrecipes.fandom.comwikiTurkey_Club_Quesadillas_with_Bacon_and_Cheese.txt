Description : 
 A TNT recipe. I have tried this recipe and its really simple to make and very good, I left out the chile however, we like making cheese quesadillas but this recipe sure has more goodies in it.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Makes 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 8 flour  tortillas 
 4 oz smoked  turkey , sliced 
 1  chipotle  chile in  adobo sauce , finely chopped 
 Â½ cup  sour cream 
 4  plum tomatoes , sliced 
 8 slices cooked  bacon 
 2 cups shredded spicy Monterey  jack cheese 
 1 cup shredded  lettuce 
 Directions : 
 Place 4 tortillas on work surface. 
 Top each with turkey, chile, sour cream, tomatoes, bacon, cheese and lettuce. 
 Place second tortilla on top of each. 
 Place 1 quesadillas in a skillet and heat until browned on one side, flip and cook second side until tortilla is browned and cheese melted. 
 Repeat with remaining quesadillas. 
 Let sit 2 minutes before cutting each into 4 wedges. 
 Serve with guacamole and salsa.
