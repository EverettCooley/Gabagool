 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 400-500 g frying  Beef   Steak 
 1  Onion 
 1 clove of  garlic 
 1 glass of wine 
 3-4 tbsp of fat or  oil 
 1 tbsp  cornflour 
 1/2 cup  beef stock 
 paprika ,  pepper ,  salt 
 Directions : 
 Cut the meat in strips, season with  salt  and  pepper  and leave for half an hour. Then heat the fat in a pan and brown the meat. Take the meat out and put aside, in the same  oil  fry the finely chopped  Onion  for 2â€“3 minutes and add to the meat. Season with  salt ,  pepper ,  paprika  and gently stir to mix the meat and  onions . 
 Add the stock, wine and the chopped  garlic  to the hot  oil  in the pan, bring to the boil, simmer for few minutes to reduce the sauce, then thicken with  cornflour .
