Ingredients : 
 2 cups  skim milk 
 ¼ tsp ground  nutmeg 
 4 large  eggs  or 1 cup  egg substitute 
 1 pinch  salt 
 ¼ cup  light brown sugar , firmly-packed 
 2 cups cooked  rice 
 4  dried figs , large, coarsely-chopped 
 ½ tsp ground  cinnamon 
 ½ tsp  vanilla extract 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Spray a 1½ qt casserole with nonstick  cooking spray . 
 Remove casserole from  water  bath, cook 10 – 15 minutes on rack before serving.
