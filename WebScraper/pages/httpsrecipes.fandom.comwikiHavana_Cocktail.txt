 Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3/4 oz  light rum 
 2 oz  pineapple juice 
 1 tsp  lemon juice 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Shake with  ice  and strain into a glass. Top with a twist of  lemon peel .
