      Sloppy Hot Dogs     
 Contents 
 
 1   Ingredients 
 2   Directions 
 3   Other Links 
 4   See also 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 1/2 cup chopped green  bell pepper 
 1/3 cup chopped  Onion 
 1/2 cup barbecue sauce 
 1 can (8 ounces)  tomato  sauce 
 1 pound hot dogs, cut into 1/4-inch slices 
 8 hot dog or hamburger buns, split 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in 10-inch skillet over medium heat.  
Cook  bell pepper  and  Onion  in  oil , stirring occasionally, until  Onion  is tender. 
Stir in barbecue sauce,  tomato  sauce and hot dogs.  
Simmer uncovered 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.  
Fill buns with hot dog mixture.
 
 Makes 8 servings
