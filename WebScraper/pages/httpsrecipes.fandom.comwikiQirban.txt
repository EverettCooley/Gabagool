 Description : 
 festivity cakes (Prepared for religious celebrations)
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 500g  flour 
 1 tspn  yeast 
 1 tspn  salt 
 200 g  Sugar 
 1/4 cup  orange  blossom  water  (or  rose water ) 
 1 cup  water  or more 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Gradually mix together the  flour ,  yeast ,  salt ,  Sugar ,  orange  blossom  water  and  water  until you get a dough like  bread dough . 
Allow to rest for 2 hours. 
Make cakes the size of a tea saucer. 
Prick the dough with a fork or knitting needle, especially in the center, to prevent it from swelling. 
Bake in the oven on medium heat. 
Serve cold.
