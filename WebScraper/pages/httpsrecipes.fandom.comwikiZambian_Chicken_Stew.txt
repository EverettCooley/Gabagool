 Description : 
 Zambian  chicken  stew.  Serves 4 - 6.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 fryer  chicken , cut up 
 2 tablespoons  oil 
 1  onion , sliced or chopped 
 1 cup  water 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ½ cup unsalted  peanuts  finely ground  or  ⅓ cup  peanut butter 
 Directions : 
 Fry  chicken  pieces in hot  oil  in a heavy stewing pan until well browned. 
 Drain  chicken  and set aside. 
 In same  oil , sauté  onion  until golden brown. 
 Add  water  and  salt  and bring to a boil. 
 Return  chicken  pieces to pan and simmer 20 minutes. 
 Add  ground nuts  or  peanut butter  and continue cooking until  chicken  is tender.
