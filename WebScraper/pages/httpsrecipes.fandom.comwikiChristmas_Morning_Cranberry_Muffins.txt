       Christmas Morning Cranberry Muffins     
 Makes 9 large muffins 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  cranberries 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 1½ cup  flour 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 2 tsp  baking powder 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  cinnamon 
 ¼ tsp  allspice 
 1  egg  beaten 
 ¼ tsp  orange rind 
 ¾ cup  orange juice 
 ⅓ cup  butter   or   margarine  melted 
 ¼ cup  nuts  chopped 
 Directions : 
 Coarsely chop  cranberries , sprinkle with ¼ cup  sugar  and set aside. 
 Preheat oven to 375 °f, prepare pans. 
 Combine  flour , 2nd measure  sugar ,  baking powder ,  salt ,  cinnamon  and  allspice . 
 In another bowl combine  egg ,  orange  rind and juice and melted  butter . 
 Add dry mix to wet mix and stir to moisten. 
 Fold in  cranberry  mixture and  nuts . 
 Spoon into pans and bake for 15 – 20 minutes or until golden.
