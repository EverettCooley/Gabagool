Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 10/29/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 8 frozen  hash brown  patties 
 3 cups shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 1 cup shredded Monterey  jack cheese 
 1 cup fully cooked  ham  diced 
 7  eggs 
 1 cup  milk 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp dry  mustard 
 ½ tsp  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Place hash browns in a single layer in a greased 9x13 baking dish. 
 Sprinkle with cheeses and ham. 
 In a bowl, beat eggs, milk, salt, mustard and pepper. 
 Pour over hash browns. 
 Cover with foil and bake at 350°F for 1 hour. 
 Uncover and bake 15 minutes longer or until edges are golden brown.
