Ingredients : 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 cup  light corn syrup 
 1 cup creamy  peanut butter 
 6 cups oven-toasted  rice cereal 
 1 cup Nestlé® Toll House®  semi-sweet chocolate  morsels 
 1 cup Nestlé® Toll House®  butterscotch flavored morsels 
 Directions : 
 Grease 13x9x2-inch baking pan. 
 Combine  sugar  and  corn syrup  in large, heavy-duty saucepan; bring just to a boil over medium heat. 
 Stirring constantly. 
 Remove from heat; stir in  peanut butter . 
 Stir in cereal. 
 Press into prepared baking pan. 
 Melt  chocolate  and butterscotch morsels in medium, microwave-safe bowl on medium-high (70%) power for 1 minute; stir. 
 Microwave at additional 10 to 20 second intervals, stirring until smooth. 
 Spread over cereal mixture. 
 Refrigerate for 20 minutes or until firm.
