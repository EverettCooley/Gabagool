 Description : 
 One of the most basic and daily used products is  milk , in this case it's Belgian  buttermilk  Soup, a long lasting taste and sweet for every sense. Easy to make and no regrets ! Enjoy !
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tbl  all-purpose flour 
 2 cup  milk 
 1 qt  buttermilk 
 2 x  egg  yolks,beaten 
 brown sugar  to taste 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix  flour  with 1 cup of the  milk  to smooth paste. Bring remaining  milk  to boil. Stir in  flour  mixture. Cook stirring constantly, until thickened and smooth. Gradually stir in  buttermilk . Bring to boil remove from Heat. Place  egg  yolks in soup tureen. Gradually stir hot soup into the  egg  yolks. Serve hot with  brown sugar  on the side.
Makes about 2 quarts.
