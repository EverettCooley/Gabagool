This is also from WP and Deb Murray. Can't be any easier than this..... stuff you already have in your freezer and cabinet. The kids loved these.......
 
 Contributed by  PressureCookerRecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 12 boneless skinless  chicken  tenders 
 2 cups of low sodium  chicken broth 
 1 jar ( 16 oz) medium  salsa 
 Directions : 
 Place all ingredients in pressure cooker bring up to high pressure for 10 minutes do a quick release. 
 After steam has been released open lid and keep heat under the pot and reduce the sauce to thicken it. 
 Serve this over nachos, in burritos or quesadillas with condiments of your choice i.e. cheese, sour cream, chopped green onions etc.
