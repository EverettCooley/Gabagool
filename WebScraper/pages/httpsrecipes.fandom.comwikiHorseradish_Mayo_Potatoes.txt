      Horseradish Mayo Potatoes     
 Ingredients : 
 1 kg  potatoes , use baby  new potatoes 
 Horseradish mayonnaise : 
 1  egg 
 2 teaspoons  white vinegar 
 3½ cups  oil ,  sunflower oil  or  safflower oil  is recommended 
 1½ cups  horseradish  cream, available from most supermarkets 
 Directions : 
 Boil  potatoes  until just tender. 
 Drain and refresh under cold  water . 
 Drain and chill in the refrigerator until ready to mix in the  mayonnaise . 
 Horseradish mayonnaise : 
 Beat  egg  and  vinegar  in a blender. 
 Add  oil  very slowly, beating constantly until thick. 
 Stir in horseradish. 
 Pour  mayonnaise  over the  potatoes .
