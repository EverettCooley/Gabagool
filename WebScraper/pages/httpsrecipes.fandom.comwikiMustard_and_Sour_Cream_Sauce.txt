 Description : 
 In Romanian: Sos de mustar cu smantana
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 2 tablespoons  mustard 
 1 teaspoon  sugar 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Mix the  sour cream  with the  mustard ,  sugar  and  salt . 
 Can be put on vegetables, meat, fish, etc.
