Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Turner Estate in Dallas, Texas in 1997. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups ground  cranberries 
 4 chopped large  apples 
 3 chopped large  oranges 
 1 cup chopped  walnuts 
 1 cup  sugar 
 6 ounces strawberry  gelatin 
 3½ cups boiling water 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together in large bowl then refrigerate until well set.
