      Fruit and Cabbage Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 2  oranges , pared and sectioned 
 2  apples , chopped 
 2 cups shredded  green cabbage 
 1 cup seedless  green grapes 
 ½ cup  whipping cream 
 1 tablespoon  granulated sugar 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ cup  mayonnaise  or  salad dressing 
 Directions : 
 Place  oranges ,  apples ,  cabbage  and  grapes  in bowl. 
 Beat  whipping cream  in chilled bowl until stiff. 
 Fold  whipped cream ,  sugar ,  lemon juice  and  salt  into  mayonnaise . 
 Stir into fruit mixture.
