 Name variations : 
 Hungarian wax pepper 
 About Yellow wax pepper : 
 Yellow wax peppers look similar to the sweet banana peppers, but are much hotter. A small yellow chile pepper that has a mild to hot flavor. It is often mistaken for the  banana pepper , which has a milder flavor.
