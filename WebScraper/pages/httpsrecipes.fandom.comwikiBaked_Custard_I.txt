Ingredients : 
 2½ cup low fat  milk 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 3  eggs 
 3 tbsp  granulated sugar -replacement 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 nutmeg 
 Directions : 
 Combine  milk ,  sugar  replacement,  salt ,  vanilla  and  eggs  in large bowl. 
 Beat to blend well. Pour into six ½ cup individual baking dishes; sprinkle with  nutmeg . 
 Set cups in large baking pan. Add 1 inch of  water  to pan. 
 Bake custard at 350 °F for 45 min. or until knife inserted in center comes out clean.
