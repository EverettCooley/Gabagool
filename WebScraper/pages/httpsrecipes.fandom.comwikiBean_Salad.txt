      Bean Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 1 can 16-oz  green beans 
 1 can 16-oz  kidney beans 
 1 can 16-oz  wax beans 
 ½ cup chopped  green pepper 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 ⅔ cup  vinegar 
 ½ cup  salad oil 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 Directions : 
 Drain the three kinds of beans and place them in a bowl. 
 Add all ingredients but the  onion . 
 Mix well and chill several hours. 
 Add ½ cup  onion  before serving.
