 Ingredients : 
 1 package small white  navy beans 
 1 lb  chouriço 
 red wine vinegar 
 1  onion  chopped 
 1 x 16 oz can  tomato sauce 
 Directions : 
 Soak  beans  overnight. 
 In meanwhile cut chourico in small chunks and marinate in wine overnight. 
 In the morning, rinse the  beans  and out then in a pan big enough to cover them, add the  onion  chourico and cover with  water . 
 Bake at 350 °F for 3–4 hours or until they are cooked. 
 Remove them and add  tomato  sauce or if you prefer, add  ketchup  until it is to your desire look and taste. Put them back in the oven until all the liquid is absorbed, about 45 minutes. 
 Check on them. You can also do this in a crock pot, them put them in the oven to brown.
