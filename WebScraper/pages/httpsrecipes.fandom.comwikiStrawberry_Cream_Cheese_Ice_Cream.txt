Description : 
 Contributed by Lilly at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 8 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (8-oz) pkg  cream cheese , softened 
 1 (7-oz) jar  marshmallow crème 
 1 pint  strawberries , mashed 
 1 cup  whipping cream 
 Directions : 
 Combine  cream cheese  and  marshmallow crème  in a large bowl, mixing until well blended. 
 Add  strawberries  and  whipping cream ; mix well. 
 Pour into 9" square pan. 
 Freeze until almost firm. 
 Coarsely chop mixture; spoon into chilled bowl. 
 Beat with electric mixer until smooth; freeze. 
 Place in refrigerator 20 - 30 minutes before serving.
