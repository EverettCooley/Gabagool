Description : 
 Makes 2¼ cups 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pack (10.5 oz) Kraft Spreadery  cheese snack  with Classic Ranch flavor 
 1 pack (10 oz) chopped  spinach 
 6 slice  bacon , crisply cooked and crumbled 
 ⅓ cup chopped  water chestnuts 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients. 
 Refrigerate. 
 Serve with vegetable dippers or crackers.
