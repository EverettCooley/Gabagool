 Description : 
 '
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  black beans , rinsed 
 1 large  Onion , chopped 
 1 medium-large  garlic  bulb - yup the whole thing used whole 
 2  green peppers , chopped 
 3  bay leaves 
 1/2 cup  olive oil 
 1 tablespoon  white vinegar 
 2  limes ; cut into thin wedges 
 Directions : 
 Place beans in a large pot with enough  water  to cover plus 2 inches and bring to a boil over high heat. 
 When it is boiling, add the  Onion ,  garlic ,  green peppers ,  bay leaves , and  oil . Turn it down to a simmer. It should be done in 1-2 hours, but check to make sure the beans are sufficiently soft. 
 Remove from the heat, add the  vinegar  and let sit 15 minutes. (If you think that you have too much liquid for your liking, use some of it to cook the  rice .) 
 
 
 Serve over  brown rice .
