      Brazilian-style Lobster     
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  lobster  meat 
 4 ripe  tomatoes 
 1 chopped  onion 
 2 crushed cloves of  garlic 
 1 tablespoon  vinegar 
 ½ cup  coconut milk 
 4 tablespoon  olive oil 
 salt  to taste 
 ½ cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Combine in blender diced  tomatoes , chopped  onion ,  garlic ,  vinegar ,  salt  and  water . 
 Beat to make a coarse sauce. 
 Heat the  olive oil  in a sauté pan, pour the sauce and simmer it for 5 minutes. 
 Add more  water  if sauce is too thick. 
 Add lobsters into sauce and cook for about 10 minutes in medium high heat. 
 Pan covered. 
 Add  coconut milk  and simmer for more 5 minutes.
