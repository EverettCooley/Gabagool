Ingredients : 
 2 medium fresh California  peaches , quartered 
 1 cup nonfat  milk 
 1/4 cup frozen orange-pineapple juice concentrate 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 3  ice  cubes, cracked 
 Directions : 
 Combine  peaches ,  milk ,  pineapple  concentrate and  vanilla  in electric blender. 
 Whir until pureed. 
 Add  ice  and whir until smooth and fluffy.
