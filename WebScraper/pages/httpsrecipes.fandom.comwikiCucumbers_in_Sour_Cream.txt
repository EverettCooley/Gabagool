       Cucumbers in Sour Cream     
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 Â½ cup  sour cream 
 1 tablespoon  Sugar

 1 teaspoon  sesame seeds  -- optional

 2  cucumbers  -- sliced thin

 Mix first six ingredients together and pour over the combined  cucumber  and  Onion  slices. Stir gently. Chill before serving.
