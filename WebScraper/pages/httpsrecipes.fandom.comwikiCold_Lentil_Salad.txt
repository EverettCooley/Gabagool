      Cold Lentil Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup any type of  lentils 
 salt  or soy sauce to taste 
 black pepper  to taste 
 2 tbsp  olive oil  (optional) 
 1 tbsp  wine vinegar 
 2 tsp  Dijon mustard 
 2  tomatoes , chopped or canned 
 2 â€“ 3  green onions , chopped 
 2 tbsp  parsley , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Cook  lentils  in  water  for 30 to 45 minutes drain. 
 Blend in other things except for  tomatoes ,  parsley  and  green onions . 
 Chill. 
 Add rest of the stuff. 
 Serve cold with or without hard-boiled eggs.
