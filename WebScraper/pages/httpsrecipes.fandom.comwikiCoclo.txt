Description : 
 Big meatballs
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  ground beef 
 ½ cup  rice 
 1  garlic  head, finely chopped 
 1 medium  egg , beaten 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp ground  bay leaf 
 ⅛ tsp ground  mace 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 ⅛ tsp  thyme 
 2 tbsp  olive oil 
 1 medium  onion , finely chopped 
 ½ bunch  cilantro , tied in a bundle 
 ¾ cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Mix  beef ,  rice ,  garlic ,  egg ,  salt ,  bay leaf ,  mace ,  pepper ,  thyme  and olive  oil  together well. 
 Shape into 2 large meatballs. 
 Put  onion ,  cilantro  and  water  in a pan. 
 Add meatballs. 
 Cover pan and simmer over low heat for 2 hours or a bit more. 
 Remove and discard  cilantro . 
 Serve with  rice  or  couscous .
