Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Olan and Iona Calvert 
 Ingredients : 
 3 tbsp  sugar 
 2 tbsp  flour 
 2 well beaten  eggs 
 1 can (no. 2½) crushed  pineapple , drained and juice reserved 
 1 tbsp  butter 
 1 carton  whipping cream , whipped 
 1 cup chopped  nuts 
 ½ pound small  marshmallows 
 Directions : 
 Mix sugar and flour. 
 Add eggs and pineapple juice. Cook in double boiler until thick. 
 Add butter. Cool. 
 Add whipped cream, nuts and marshmallows. Chill.
