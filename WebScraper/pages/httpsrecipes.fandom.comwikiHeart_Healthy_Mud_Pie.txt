Ingredients : 
 1 1/4 cup graham cracker crumbs 
 1/4 cup unsweetened  cocoa 
 1/3 cup unsweetened  applesauce 
 1/4 cup  Sugar 
 1/4 tsp  cinnamon 
 2 tsp  margarine 
 18 oz nonfat frozen coffee yogurt 
 2 tbsp  chocolate  syrup 
 Directions : 
 In a medium bowl, mix crumbs,  cocoa ,  Sugar ,  cinnamon ,  applesauce  and  margarine  until combined. Press mixture evenly on bottom and side of a 9-inch pie pan. 
 Bake approximately 10 minutes at 350 degrees until firm. Cool and set aside. 
 Refrigerate. Spread softened  yogurt  evenly over pie crust to form filling. 
 Place in freezer. Drizzle with  chocolate  syrup before serving.
