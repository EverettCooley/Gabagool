Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 oz  cranberry juice 
 3 oz  orange juice  or  pineapple juice 
 1 oz  peach schnapps 
 1 oz  vodka 
 Directions : 
 Add several  ice cubes  to a highball glass, pour in all ingredients, and stir briskly. 
 Strain the ingredients into a shot glass to create this popular shooter.
