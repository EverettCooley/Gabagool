Ingredients : 
 2 squares  chocolate 
 ½ cup  butter  or  shortening 
 1 cup  brown sugar 
 ½ teaspoon  soda 
 1  egg 
 2 cups  flour 
 ¾ cup sweet  milk 
 Directions : 
 First melt the  chocolate  and add to the melted  shortening . 
 Add  sugar ,  egg ,  milk , and soda and  flour  sifted together. 
 Batter should be stiff enough to drop from the spoon. 
 Drop by spoonfuls on greased pans, and bake in a moderate oven (350 – 375°F). 
 Frost, if desired.
