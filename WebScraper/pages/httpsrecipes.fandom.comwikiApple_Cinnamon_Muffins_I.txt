Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1  Egg 
 2 cup Bisquick baking mix 
 3/4 cup  Apple , peeled & finely chop 
 1/3 cup  Sugar 
 2/3 cup  Milk 
 2 tbsp  oil , vegetable 
 2 tsp  Cinnamon , ground 
 Directions : 
 Fat grams per serving: approx. cook time: :15 preheat oven to 400 °F. 
 Grease  bottom only of 12 medium muffin cups. 
 Beat   egg  slightly, stir in remaining ingredients just till moistened. 
 Divide  Batter  evenly among cups. 
 Bake till golden brown, 15 to 17 minutes. 
 Makes: 12 muffins
