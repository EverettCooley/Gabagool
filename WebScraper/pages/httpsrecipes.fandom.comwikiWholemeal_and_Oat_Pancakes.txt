 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  whole wheat flour 
 1/4 cup quick cooking  rolled oats 
 a pinch  nutmeg  powder 
 a pinch  cardamom  powder 
 1/4 teaspoon  cinnamon  powder 
 1 1/2 tablespoons  powdered sugar 
 1 teaspoon  oil 
 1/2 cup low fat  milk 
 1/2 teaspoon Eno's fruit  salt 
 a pinch of  salt 
 3 teaspoons low fat  butter  for cooking 
 For serving 
 2 tablespoons  honey 
 1/2 cup  orange  segments 
 Directions : 
 Combine all the ingredients except the fruit  salt  in a bowl with enough  water . 
 Whisk till it is a smooth batter. 
 Heat a non-stick pan and grease it with a little low fat  butter . 
 Add the fruit  salt  to the batter and mix well. 
 Divide into 6 equal portions. 
 Pour a spoonful of the batter on the pan to make 2" diameter pancakes, cooking on both sides with a little  butter  till golden brown. 
 Repeat with the remaining batter to make 5 more pancakes. 
 Serve hot with  honey  and  oranges .
