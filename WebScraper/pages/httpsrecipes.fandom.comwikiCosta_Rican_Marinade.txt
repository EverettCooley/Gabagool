 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 ts Grated  lime  peel 
 1/3 c  lime juice 
 1/4 c  tomato juice 
 1 tb Chopped fresh  cilantro 
 Or  parsley 
 2 ts  vegetable oil 
 1/4 ts  salt 
 1/8 ts red pepper sauce 
 2 Cloves  garlic , minced 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients in shallow nonmetal dish or resealable plastic bag. 
 Add up to 3 pounds  Chicken ,  Pork  or  Beef , turning to coat with marinade. 
 Cover dish or seal bag and refrigerate, turning meat occasionally, at least 8 hours but no longer than 24 hours. #Remove meat from marinade; discard.
