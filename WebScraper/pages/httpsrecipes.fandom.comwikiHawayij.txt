Description : 
 A curry-like, Yemeni spice mixture
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp  black peppercorns 
 1 tbsp  cumin  seeds 
 1 tsp  cardamom  seeds 
 1 tsp  saffron 
 2 tsp  turmeric 
 Directions : 
 Grind the spices together thoroughly, and store them in an airtight jar.
