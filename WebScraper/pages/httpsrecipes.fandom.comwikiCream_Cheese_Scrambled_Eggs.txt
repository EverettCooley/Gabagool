cream cheese  Scrambled  eggs 
 
 1 Pkg.  cream cheese , Softened (3 Oz) 
 2 T. Half And Half Cram 
 8  eggs 
 1/3 Cup Grated  Parmesan cheese 
 12 T.  lemon pepper  Seasoning 
 1/8 T.  salt 
 1/2 Cup Real  Bacon  Bits 
 2 T.  butter 
 
In Small Mixing Bowl, Beat  Cheese  And Cream Until Smooth. Add  eggs ,  Parmesan cheese ,  lemon pepper  Seasoning And  salt . Mix Well. Stir in  Bacon  Bits. in a Large Skillet Melt  butter . Add  egg  Mixture. Cook And Stir Over Medium Heat Until  eggs  Are Completely Set. Makes 6 to 8 Servings.Tip: Microwave And Crumble Your Own  Bacon ; Use 6 to 8 Slices For 1/2 Cup.Source: Simple And Delicious
