Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Original recipe 
 Yields 6 servings, each one equal to ½ cup 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups dry  pinto beans 
 6 cups  water 
 ⅓ cup chopped  onion 
 1 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 1 teaspoon  olive oil 
 1 cup liquid from the cooked beans 
 Directions : 
 Clean the beans. Leave them to soak overnight. Drain. 
 In a medium pan, put soaked beans, water, onion, garlic powder and olive oil. 
 Cook over medium heat for 2½ hours or until beans are tender. 
 Mash 3 cups of cooked beans in large heavy skillet. 
 Add 1 cup bean cooking liquid. 
 Cook over medium heat until thick and the desired consistency. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per ½ cup serving:
 
 Calories 120 | Calories from Fat 10 | Total Fat 1 g | Cholesterol 0 mg | Sodium 200 mg | Carbohydrate 22 g | Dietary Fiber 7 g | Protein 7 g 
 Exchange 1 starch | 1 fat
