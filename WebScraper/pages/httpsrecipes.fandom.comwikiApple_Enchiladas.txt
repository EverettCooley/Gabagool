       Apple Enchilada Dessert     
 Makes 6 large  tortillas ; may be cut in half to serve 12. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (21 ounce) can  apple pie filling 
 6 (8 inch) flour  tortillas 
 1 teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 ⅓ cup  margarine 
 ½ cup  white sugar 
 ½ cup packed  brown sugar 
 ½ cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F (175 °C). 
 Spoon fruit evenly onto all  tortillas , sprinkle with  cinnamon . 
 Roll up  tortillas  and place seam side down on lightly greased 8x8  baking pan . 
 Bring  margarine ,  sugars  and  water  to a boil in a medium sauce pan. 
 Reduce heat and simmer, stirring constantly for 3 minutes. 
 Pour sauce evenly over  tortillas ; sprinkle with extra  cinnamon  on top if desired. 
 Bake in  preheated  oven for 20 minutes.
