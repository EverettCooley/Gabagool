Ingredients : 
 1 stick  butter , melted 
 1 lb box  powdered sugar 
 1 (8 oz) pkg.  cream cheese 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Mix together all ingredients and beat well. 
 This will keep in the refrigerator for weeks.
