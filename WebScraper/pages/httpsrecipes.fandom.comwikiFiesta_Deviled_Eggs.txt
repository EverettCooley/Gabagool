Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Yield: 16 Appetizers 
 Ingredients : 
 8 hard boiled  eggs 
 ½ cup shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 ¼ cup  mayonnaise 
 ¼ cup  salsa 
 2 tablespoons sliced  green onion 
 1 tablespoon  sour cream 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Slice  eggs  in half lengthwise. 
 Remove the yolks and set the whites aside. 
 In a small bowl, mash the yolks with the remaining ingredients. 
 Evenly refill the egg whites. 
 Lightly sprinkle with paprika if desired. 
 Chill until ready to serve.
