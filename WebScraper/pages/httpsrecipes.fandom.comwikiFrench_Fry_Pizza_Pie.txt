Description : 
 Makes one 12-inch pizza
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lbs (about 5 cups) frozen French fried shoestring  potatoes 
 1 cup prepared  pizza sauce  or  spaghetti sauce 
 1 tsp dried  oregano  leaves crumbled 
 ½  green bell pepper , cut into ¼-inch strips 
 2 tbsp sliced  green onions 
 2 cup shredded fat-free  mozzarella  cheese 
 ¼ cup grated fat-free  Parmesan cheese 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 450°F. 
 Coat 12-inch round pizza pan with vegetable  cooking spray . 
 Arrange frozen  potatoes  evenly in one layer in pan. 
 Bake 10 minutes. 
 Remove pan from oven and cover  potatoes  with sauce. 
 Sprinkle with  oregano . 
 Arrange  pepper  strips on sauce; sprinkle with  onions  and top with cheeses. 
 Bake about 15 minutes until top is lightly browned.
