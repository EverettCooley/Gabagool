Asparagus Guacamole
 serves 12
 1 pound fresh asparagus spears, cut into 1-inch lengths (2 cups)
3/4 cup water
2 tablespoons plain nonfat or low-fat yogurt
1 tablespoons lemon juice
1 medium tomato, seeded and chopped (1 cup)
2 tablespoons sliced green onion
1 teaspoon ground cumin
1 clove garlic, minced
� teaspoon dried oregano leaves
� teaspoon salt
� teaspoon cayenne
 Combine asparagus and water in a 2-quart saucepan.
Bring to a boil over medium-high heat.
Cover and reduce heat to medium-low.
Simmer 8 to 10 minutes, or until asparagus is tender.
Rinse with cold water; drain.
Blot asparagus with paper tower to remove excess moisture.
Combine asparagus, yogurt and lemon juice in a food processor or blender.
Process until smooth.
In a medium mixing bowl, combine asparagus mixture and remaining 
ingredients.
Chill, if desired.
Serve with raw vegetables or tortilla chips
