 Ingredients : 
 12 oz silken tofu 
 1/3 cup Pure  maple syrup 
 1/3 cup  unsweetened cocoa powder 
 1 tsp  vanilla extract 
 1 dash Ground  cinnamon  or to taste 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Put all ingredients into a food processor or blender; process until smooth and creamy. 
 Transfer the mixture to a medium bowl or individual dessert cups. 
 Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour. 
 Covered and refrigerated this pudding will keep for 2 days.
