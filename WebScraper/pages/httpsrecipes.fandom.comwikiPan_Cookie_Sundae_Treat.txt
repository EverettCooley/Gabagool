Description : 
 Contributed by Lilly at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 35 Squares 
 Ingredients : 
 2¼ cups  all-purpose flour 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 cup  butter , softened 
 ¾ cup  granulated sugar 
 ¾ cup firmly packed  brown sugar 
 1 tsp  vanilla extract 
 2  eggs 
 1 (12-oz) pkg  semi-sweet chocolate  morsels 
 1 cup chopped  nuts 
 ice cream , any flavor 
 1 (22-oz) bottle  chocolate  or other flavor syrup 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 375°F. 
 In small bowl, combine  flour ,  baking soda  and  salt ; set aside. 
 In large bowl, combine  butter , sugars and  vanilla extract ; beat until creamy. 
 Beat in  eggs . 
 Gradually add  flour  mixture. 
 Stir in  chocolate  morsels and nuts. 
 Spread into 15½"x10½"x1" baking pan. 
 Bake at 375°F for 20-25 minutes. 
 Cool; cut into 2" squares. 
 Top with your favorite  ice cream  and syrup. 
 Serve garnished with  strawberries , if desired.
