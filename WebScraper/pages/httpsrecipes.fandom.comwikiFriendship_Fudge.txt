Friendship Fudge 
 
 Directions : 
 Butter one 8 inch pan and set aside. In a saucepan mix brown sugar, evaporated
milk and salt. Cook over low heat and stir until boiling. Boil for 3 minutes
.. Remove from heat and stir in chocolate chips, peanut butter and vanilla. Mix
until smooth. Pour fudge into pan. Refrigerate 4 hours or until firm. Cut into squares. Share with a friend! and enjoy.
