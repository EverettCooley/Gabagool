 Description : 
 This is an excellent recipe for a cook out or to take to a pot luck. Everyone always likes it. It is simple and very good.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 bag of baby  spinach  leaves 
 1 can of  mandarin oranges 
 2 ripe  California avocados 
 1 pkg. of  sliced almonds 
 1 bottle of  raspberry  Vinaigarette Dressing 
 Directions : 
 Use a large salad bowl. Put in the  spinach  leaves first. Add the  mandarin oranges  on top.
 Slice two ripe  California avocados  lengthwise in strips across the top of the salad. 
 Sprinkle  sliced almonds  across the top.
 Then serve with  raspberry  Vinaigarette Dressing.
 So easy, but so good.
