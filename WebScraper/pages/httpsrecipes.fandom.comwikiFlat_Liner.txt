Description 
 A flat liner is a layered cocktail which is drunk from a shooter glass. Tabasco sauce lies between two layers of spirits forming a line between them.

 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 1 oz  Sambuca 
 1 oz  Tequila 
 1 dash  Tabasco sauce 
 Directions 
 Add one ounce of sambuca to a shot glass. 
 Shake several drops of tabasco sauce onto the surface of the sambuca. 
 Hold an inverted teaspoon in the glass and carefully layer the tequila over the sambuca. 
 Serve.
