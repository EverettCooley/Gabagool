Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Peg Buckingham 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pkg.  caramels 
 8 oz pkg.  cream cheese 
 Directions : 
 Combine caramels and cream cheese. 
 Microwave until warm and creamy. 
 Stir several times during warming. 
 Serve as a dip with fresh unpeeled apple slices spritzed with lemon juice so they stay snowy white.
