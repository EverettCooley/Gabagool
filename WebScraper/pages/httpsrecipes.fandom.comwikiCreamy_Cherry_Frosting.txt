Description : 
 This is one of my favorites. Enjoy.
 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 5½ cups sifted  confectioners' sugar 
 ¾ cup  butter   or  regular  margarine  -- softened 
 6 tbsp  maraschino cherry  juice 
 2 drops red  food coloring 
 ½ tsp  vanilla extract 
 ¼ cup chopped red  maraschino cherries 
 Directions : 
 Combine the sifted confectioners  sugar ,  butter , juice,  food coloring  and  vanilla extract  in a bowl. 
 Beat with an electric mixer at medium speed until smooth and creamy. 
 Add 1 more tbsp of  cherry  juice, if necessary, to make the frosting into a spreading consistency.
