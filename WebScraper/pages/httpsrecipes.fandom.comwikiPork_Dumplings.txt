 Description : 
 These tasty treats make a perfect appetizer or you can serve them as a main dish. For a main dish count on about 15 dumplings per person. Serve with hoisin sauce, hot Chinese-style  mustard  and toasted  sesame seeds ." Original recipe yield: 100 dumplings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 100 (3.5 inch square)  wonton wrappers 
 1¾ pounds  ground pork 
 1 tablespoon minced fresh  ginger  root 
 4 cloves  garlic , minced 
 2 tablespoons thinly sliced  green onion 
 4 tablespoons  soy sauce 
 3 tablespoons  sesame oil 
 1  egg , beaten 
 5 cups finely shredded  Chinese cabbage 
 Directions : 
 Mix the  pork  with  garlic ,  ginger ,soy sauce,  onions ,  sesame oil ,  eggs  and  cabbage . 
 Place 1 tablespoon of filling on the wontons and pinch the edges closed 
 Steam for 15 – 30 minutes.
