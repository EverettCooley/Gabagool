Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Duval Estate in Seven Points, Texas in 1999.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 16 ounces  sour cream 
 1 small jar  corn  relish 
 Directions : 
 Combine  sour cream  and relish together and blend well. 
 Chill at least one hour before serving.
