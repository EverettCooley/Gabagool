Ingredients : 
 30 oz canned  black beans , drained and rinsed 
 2 cup cooked  long-grain white rice , or  brown 
 4  scallions , chopped 
 2 medium  tomatoes , diced 
 1 medium  cucumber , peeled and diced 
 1  jalapeno , seeded and minced 
 2 tbsp  canola oil 
 1  lime , juiced 
 2 tbsp chopped  cilantro 
 2 tsp dried  oregano 
 ½ tsp ground  cumin 
 ½ tsp  black pepper 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 In a large mixing bowl, combine the beans,  rice , scallions,  tomatoes ,  cucumber , and, if desired, jalapeno. 
 Blend in the  oil ,  lime juice ,  cilantro , and seasonings. 
 Chill for 1 hour before serving. 
 Tip : for a slight twist, add 2 roasted, seeded, and chopped  red bell peppers  or try  New Mexico chile .
