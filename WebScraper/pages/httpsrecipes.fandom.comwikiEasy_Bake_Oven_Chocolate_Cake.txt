Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 tablespoons of  flour 
 ¼ teaspoon of  baking powder 
 1 teaspoon of  cocoa 
 ¾ teaspoon of  shortening 
 a pinch of  salt 
 4 teaspoons of  milk 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients well. 
 Bake in easy bake oven for 12 to 14 minutes.
