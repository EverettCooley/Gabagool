Tasty oven baked chicken featuring vegetables, bread crumbs and cheese
 (You can prepare this meal with or without vegetables) 
 Serves: 4 - 6
 Cook Time: 50 - 60 min
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 - 4 large chicken brests skinned & trimmed of fat 
 2  bay leaves 
 2 eggs 
 1 1/2 cups low fat milk 
 1 tbls parsley 
 1 cup  Italian salad dressing 
 1 package dry  onion soup mix 
 3/4 tbls Tony's creole seasoning (or salt & pepper) 
 1 (4.5-ounce) can  mushrooms , drained 
 2 medium  tomatoes , quartered 
 1 can new potatoes (and other vegetables if desires) 
 1 cup plain bread crumbs, crushed French fried onions or crushed croutons (your choice of 1 or any blend) 
 1 1/2 cups shredded chedder cheese 
 non-stick spray 
 Directions : 
 Slice chicken into 1 to 2 inch strips 
 Combine milk, eggs, seasoning & 1/2 of salad dressing in a 1 1/2 qt. bowl 
 Put chicken in bowl and place in fridg. for 1 hour 
 In a greased 13 x 9 x 2-inch baking pan, arrange  chicken ; tuck  bay leaves  around  chicken 
 In a small bowl, combine the  salad dressing ,  onion soup mix  with 1/3 cup of marinade (after chicken is removed) 
 Remve chicken from marinade and dip into bread crumbs coating both sides well 
 Spray baking dish and place chicken spaced evenly into dish 
 Preheat oven to 375 deg 
 Pour mixture over  chicken . Try not to disturb coating 
 Bake uncovered in preheated oven for 45 minutes 
 Combine all vegetables, spread over dish, then sprinkle cheese over entire dish 
 Bake an additional 15 â€“ 20 minutes or until cheese is melted 
 Enjoy.
