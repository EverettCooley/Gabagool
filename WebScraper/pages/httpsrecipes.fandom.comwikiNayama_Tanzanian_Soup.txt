 Nayama Tanzanian Soup
 
 Ingredients :

 1/2 tsp.  cayenne pepper 
 1/2 tsp.  turmeric 
 1/4 tsp. cloves

 Directions : 
 Brown in 2T  oil   curry , Cayenne , Onion , coconut  and cloves  garlic 
 Add  Beef , cut into chunks 
 Add  tomato  paste, tomatoes ,juice  lime  and  salt 
 Cover with  water  and simmer for 2 to 3 hours
