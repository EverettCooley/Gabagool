Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup  butter 
 1 cup  white sugar 
 ¼ cup sweet  milk 
 2 cups  Quaker™ oats 
 1¼ cups  flour 
 2  eggs 
 1 level teaspoon  baking powder 
 1 level teaspoon  salt 
 1 level teaspoon  cinnamon 
 1 level teaspoon  nutmeg 
 ½ teaspoon  cloves 
 1 cup seeded  raisins  cut fine 
 ½ cup  hickory nuts 
 Directions : 
 First drop in buttered baking pan in spoonfuls some distance apart if dough spreads too much add more  flour .
