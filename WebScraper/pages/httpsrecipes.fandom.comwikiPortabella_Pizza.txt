Description 
 Fruit & Vegetable Recipes I  by the South Carolina Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Serves : 4 
 Ingredients 
 1 pound  sausage 
 1 clove  garlic , chopped (optional) 
 1 (14 oz.) jar  pizza sauce 
 12 small  portabello mushrooms 
 ¾ cup grated  mozzarella  cheese, grated 
 Directions 
 Brown sausage and drain fat. 
 Add chopped garlic and pizza sauce. 
 Simmer for 20 minutes. 
 Scrape out a small portion of the gills of the mushrooms (enough to make a small bowl for the filling). 
 Put the sauce in the mushrooms and top with grated mozzarella cheese. 
 Bake 10 minutes in a 400 degree f preheated oven.
