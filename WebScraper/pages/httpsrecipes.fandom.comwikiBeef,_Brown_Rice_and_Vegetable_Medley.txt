Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound boneless  Beef  sirloin  Steak , cut into strips 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1/2 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 1 cup red pepper strips 
 1 cup sliced fresh  Mushrooms 
 1 cup sliced  zucchini 
 1 7-ounce can yellow  corn , drained 
 1/3 cup sliced  green onions 
 3 cups cooked  brown rice 
 Directions : 
 Season  Beef  with  salt  and  pepper ; brown  Beef  with  garlic  in  oil  in large skillet over high heat. 
 Stir in red pepper,  Mushrooms ,  zucchini ,  corn  and  onions ; stir-fry 3 to 4 minutes or until vegetables are tender crisp. 
 Stir in  rice ; heat thoroughly.
