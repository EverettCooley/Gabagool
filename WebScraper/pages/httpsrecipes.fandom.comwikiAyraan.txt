Ingredients : 
 1 pt. laban ( yoghurt ) 
 1 cup cold  water 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Buy  yoghurt  or laban prepared from whole  milk . 
 Lift off the yellow crust of  cream  on the top. 
 Mix the rest of the  yoghurt  with the  water  and  salt  in an electric blender, or beat well together using a wooden spoon. 
 Serve well chilled.
