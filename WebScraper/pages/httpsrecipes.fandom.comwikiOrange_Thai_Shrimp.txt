 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1Â lb. large  Shrimp , cooked, peeled, deveined 
 Sections of 3  oranges , seeded, cut into bite-sized pieces 
 10  mint  leaves, coarsley chopped 
 4 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1/2 tsp.  salt 
 1/2 tsp. dried red  chile flakes 
 Juice of 1  lime 
 1/2 tsp. sauce 
 Peel of 1  orange , cut into thin strips 
 Directions : 
 Combine the  Shrimp  and  oranges  in a bowl and mix well. 
 Stir in the  mint ,  garlic ,  salt  and red  chile flakes . 
 Add the  lime juice , sauce and  orange peel  and mix well. 
 Marinate, covered in the refrigerator for 1 hour or longer, stirring occasionally. 
 Spoon onto a glass platter or into a crystal bowl.
