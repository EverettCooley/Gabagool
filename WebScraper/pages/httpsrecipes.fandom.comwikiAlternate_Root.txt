 Ingredients : 
 1 shot root  beer  schnapps 
 2 oz  orange juice 
 1 glass ice 
 Directions : 
 Mix Schnapps and juice in glass of ice. Place in blender. 
 Blend until smooth. Serve in chilled glass.
