 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 	cup  lime juice 
 1/2 	cup  water 
 1/2 	cup  Sugar 
 2 	medium  nectarines , thinly sliced 
 1 	large firm  banana , thinly sliced 
 1 	pint  blueberries 
 1 	pint fresh  strawberries , sliced 
 1 1/2 	cups  watermelons , balls 
 1 	cup  Green Grapes 
 1 	 kiwi , peeled and chopped 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl, combine the  lime juice ,  water  and  Sugar ; stir until the  Sugar  is dissolved. Add the  nectarines  and the  bananas . Toss to coat. 
 In a 2 Â½-quart glass bow, combine the remaining ingredients. 
 Add the  nectarine  mixture; stir gently. 
 Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour. 
 Serve with a slotted spoon
