 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 slices whole- grain   Raisin  vegan bread (like Ezekiel's) 
 Veganaise Dressing & Sandwich Spread (or similar) 
 1 or 2 vegan  American cheese  slices 
 Apple  slices 
 Directions : 
 Spread vegan bread with Veganaise. (We sometimes put Vegenaise on one slice and  Peanut   butter  on the other.) Add the  Cheese  and  Apple  slices. 
 
 
 I came upon this recipe when money was tight and this was all I had in my fridge for lunches that day. The kids loved them. Now they are grown and ask for them when they come home.
