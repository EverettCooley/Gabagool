 Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 oz  light rum 
 1 tsp 151-proof  rum 
 1/2 oz Coffee-flavored  brandy 
 1 1/2 tsp  light cream 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Shake with  ice  and strain into a glass. Float 151-proof  rum  on top.
