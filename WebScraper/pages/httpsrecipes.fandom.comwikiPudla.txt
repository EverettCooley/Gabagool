Also called  puda 
 A spicy pancake-like bread made from chickpea flour and water, with cumin seeds, turmeric, minced chilies, onions, lime juice, salt, and tomato 
 A few tablespoons of batter are spread on a hot griddle snd cooked until golden brown on each side
