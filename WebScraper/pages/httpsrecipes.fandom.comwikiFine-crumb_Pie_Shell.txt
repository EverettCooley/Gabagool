Ingredients : 
 1¼ cup fine crumbs ( graham cracker ) or 1½ cup dry cereal,  zwieback , etc. 
 3 tbsp  margarine  (melted) 
 1 tbsp  water 
 spices  (optional) 
 sugar replacement  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Combine crumbs with melted  margarine  and  water ; add spices and  sugar replacement , if desired. 
 Spread dough evenly in 8 – 10 inch pie pan, pressing firmly onto sides and bottom. 
 Either chill until set, or bake at 325°F for 8 – 10 minutes. 
 Nutritional information : 
 1 serving with graham cracker = 1 bread, 1 fat, calories: 85 
 1 serving with dry cereal = ½ bread, 1 fat calories: 64 
 1 serving with zwieback = ½ bread, 1 fat calories: 7
