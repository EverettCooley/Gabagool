      Lucque olive     
 About Lucque olive : 
 Wikipedia Article  About Lucque olive on Wikipedia 
 Lucques' originated in the south of France. They are green, of a large size, and elongated. The bone has an arcuated shape. Their flavour is mild and nutty.
