Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Morgan Estate in Austin, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 ripe fresh  watermelon 
 1 pint fresh picked  strawberries 
 ¼ cup fresh squeezed  lemon juice 
 ⅓ cup  granulated sugar 
 Directions : 
 Scoop out the red watermelon flesh and put into a large mixing bowl. 
 Remove seeds and mash watermelon until smooth. 
 Clean and hull the strawberries. 
 Mash strawberries and add to the mixing bowl. 
 Add lemon juice and granulated sugar. 
 Mix well until all ingredients are well combined. 
 Pour into several ice cube trays. 
 Freeze overnight.
