 Description : 
 Ke Jia-style fried  egg 
 
 Ingredients : 
 5  eggs 
 50 g green  bean sprouts 
 1 tbsp  dried shrimp 
 ⅓ red  tomato 
 Seasoning : 
 ½ tbsp  salt 
 2 tbsp  chicken stock 
 dash  sesame 
 1 stalk  spring onion 
 Directions : 
 Wash and soak dry shrimps by warm  water , chop the  spring onion 
 Remove seeds of  tomato , chop into small pieces 
 Boil the green  bean sprouts  with ½ tbsp of  salt , take up for use 
 Whisk the  egg  with ½ tbsp of  salt  and  chicken stock 
 Add chopped  tomato , dry shrimps and green  bean sprouts , mix well 
 Heat the wok with 2 tbsp of  oil , fry  egg  both sizes until golden, dish up 
 Sprinkle sesame and chopped spring  Onion  on surface, serve
