Ingredients : 
 1 ounce  crème de menthe 
 1 ounce  brandy 
 2 scoops vanilla  ice cream 
 Directions : 
 Soften the  ice cream  a little before adding to other ingredients. 
 Mix all ingredients together. 
 Blend until creamy and enjoy.
