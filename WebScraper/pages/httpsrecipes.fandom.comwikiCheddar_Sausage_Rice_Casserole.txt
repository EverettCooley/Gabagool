Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 pound smoked link  Sausage , diced 
 3/4 cup sliced  green onions 
 3/4 cup chopped  celery 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 12-ounce can whole kernel  corn , drained 
 1 cup (4 ounces) shredded  Cheddar   Cheese 
 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Sausage ,  onions  and  celery  in large skillet until vegetables are tender. 
 Pour off all but 1 tablespoon drippings from pan. 
 Stir in  rice  and  corn ; heat thoroughly. 
 Spoon into greased 2-quart baking dish; top with  Cheese . 
 Broil until  Cheese  melts.
