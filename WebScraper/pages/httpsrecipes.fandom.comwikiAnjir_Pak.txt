 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 50 gms  milk  powder 
 250 gms dried  figs 
 250ml.milk 
 50gms  Sugar 
 pinch of  cardamom  powder (optional) 
 2 tbsps nut flakes 
 Directions : 
 Clean and wash dried  figs  properly. Chop them 
 Place  milk  into heavy bottom pan. Bring it to boil. 
 Place chopped  figs  in the  milk . Boil and stir till the  milk  evaporates and  figs  turn soft. 
 Add  Sugar  and heat till  Sugar  melts. 
 Add  milk  powder and heat. Stir well till the pak is done. 
 Add  cardamom  powder and mix well. 
 Garnish with nut flakes if you wish and serve hot.
