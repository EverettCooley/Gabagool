Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Real Simple. 
 Ingredients : 
 6 cups strongly brewed black  coffee 
 ¼ cup  kahlúa 
 ¾ cup  Irish cream liqueur , such as Bailey's 
 Directions : 
 In a large pitcher or bowl, combine the  coffee ,  kahlúa  and Irish cream. 
 Pour into mugs. 
 Note: be sure to brew a batch of straight  coffee  for the designated drivers.
