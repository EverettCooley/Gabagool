Bloody Mary Mix
 
 6 Cups  tomato juice 
 3 T. Prepared Horseradish 
 1 to 2 T.  Tabasco sauce 
 2 T.  worcestershire sauce 
 1/4 Cup  lemon juice 
 salt  And  pepper  to Taste
 vodka 
 celery  Stalks
 
Mix All Ingredients Except  vodka  And  celery  Stalks in a 2 Quart Container. Mix Well And Chill. For Each Serving, Pour 1 1/2 Oz.  vodka  Over Crushed  ice  in a Tall Glass. Add 1 Cup Bloody Mary Mix, Stir Well And Garnish With  celery  Stalk.
