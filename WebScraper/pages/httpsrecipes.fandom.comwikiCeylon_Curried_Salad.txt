Description : 
 Makes 4 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups cooked  rice , cooled 
 ¼ cup diagonally sliced  celery 
 3 tablespoons finely diced  carrots 
 3 tablespoons  green bell pepper  slivers 
 ¼ cup  mayonnaise 
 ¼ cup  sour cream 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  curry powder 
 ¼ teaspoon  lemon juice 
 lettuce  leaves 
 diced  carrots  or  grated coconut  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  celery ,  carrots  and  pepper  in medium bowl. 
 Blend  mayonnaise ,  sour cream ,  salt ,  curry powder  and  lemon juice ; toss with  rice . 
 Serve on  lettuce  leaves; top with diced  carrots  or  grated coconut , if desired.
