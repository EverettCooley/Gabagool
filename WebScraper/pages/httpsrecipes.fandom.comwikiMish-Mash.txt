 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 medium  tomatoes , 
 3  red peppers , 
 1  Onion , 2-3 tbs. of  vegetable oil , 
 200 gr. of  feta   Cheese , 
 3  eggs , 
 chopped  parsley , 
 salt  and  pepper  to season 
 Directions : 
 Cut the ingredients into small pieces. 
 Heat the  oil  in a frying pan, add the  onions , then the  peppers  and the  tomatoes  and cook for about 3–5 minutes. 
 Add the  Cheese  and  eggs  and cook for another 2–3 minutes. 
 Sprinkle with  parsley  and serve.
