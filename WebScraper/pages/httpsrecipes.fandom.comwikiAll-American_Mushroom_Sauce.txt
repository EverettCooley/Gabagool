Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: My old recipes 
 Makes about ¾ cup 
 Ingredients : 
 1 envelope Lipton  Cream of mushroom  Cup-a-Soup 
 ½ cup boiling  water 
 ¼ to ⅓ cup shredded  American cheese   or  use 2 to 3 slices American cheese 
 Directions : 
 In small bowl, blend all ingredients. 
 Serve over hot cooked noodles or pasta or hot cooked vegetables.
