Description : 
 Contributed by Delma at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 12 large  cucumbers 
 10 medium  onions 
 1 head  cabbage 
 1 bunch  celery 
 2  green peppers 
 2  red peppers 
 Â½ cup  salt 
 2 tbsp  celery seed 
 1 tbsp  mustard seed 
 1 tbsp  turmeric 
 4 cups  sugar 
 4 cups  vinegar 
 Directions : 
 Remove seeds from  cucumbers  and grind. 
 Cover with cold  water  and let stand overnight. 
 Grind the remaining vegetables and sprinkle with  salt . 
 Let stand overnight. 
 In morning, drain  cucumbers  and vegetables well. 
 Combine with remaining ingredients. 
 Simmer 20 minutes. 
 Seal in hot sterilized jars.
