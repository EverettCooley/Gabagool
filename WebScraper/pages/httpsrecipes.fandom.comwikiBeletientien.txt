Beletientien  or  atama leaves'  are an annual herb cultivated in delta areas. It smells and taste like  tarragon .  It is typically used fresh or dried in  Nigerian Cuisine . Use dried leaves sparingly as flavor is more intense. Readily available from African food stores.
