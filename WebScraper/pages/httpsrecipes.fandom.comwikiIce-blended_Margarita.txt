       Ice-blended Margarita     
 Margarita  from the Wikibooks Bartending Guide—original source of recipe, licensed under the GNU Free Documentation License
 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 1 ½ oz  Tequila 
 ½ oz orange liqueur ( Cointreau  or  triple sec ) 
 ½ oz  lime juice  plus 1  lime  wedge 
 1½ cups ice cubes 
 Directions 
 In a  blender , combine the ingredients and blend until slushy. 
 Pour into prepared margarita glass and serve with a lime wedge.
