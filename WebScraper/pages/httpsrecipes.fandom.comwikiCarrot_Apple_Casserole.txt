 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 large  carrots , sliced 
 4 large  Apples , peeled,quartered,and sliced 
 5 tablespoons  all-purpose flour 
 1 tablespoon packed  brown sugar 
 1/2 teaspoon ground  nutmeg 
 1 tablespoon  margarine 
 1/2 cup  orange juice 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. Cook  carrots  in large 
 saucepan in boiling  water  for 5 minutes 
 before draining.Layer  carrots  and  Apples  in large 
 casserole. Combine  flour ,  Sugar  and  nutmeg ; 
 sprinkle over top. Dot with  margarine ; pour  orange 
 juice over  flour  mixture. 
 Bake 30 minutes or until  carrots  are tender.
