Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 10/29/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  butter 
 1  garlic  clove chopped 
 ½  onion  chopped 
 1 lb  ham  chopped 
 ½ cup  flour 
 3 cups  chicken broth 
 4 cups peeled diced  potatoes 
 2 tsp  black pepper 
 2 cups  heavy cream   or   half and half 
 12 oz  Velveeta  cubed 
 1 cup shredded  cheddar 
 Directions : 
 Melt butter and sauté onion and garlic until clear over medium heat. 
 Add ham, sauté stirring frequently. 
 Add flour and stir until well blended. 
 Add chicken broth and stir over heat until thickened and bubbly. 
 Add potatoes and bring to a simmer. 
 Reduce heat to medium low, cover and simmer until potatoes are tender, about 20 minutes. 
 Add pepper to taste, half and half and cheese. 
 Stir until cheese is melted.
