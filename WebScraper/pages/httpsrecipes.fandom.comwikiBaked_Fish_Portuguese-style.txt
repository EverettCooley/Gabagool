Description : 
 This recipe is from www.Portuguese-recipes.com
 
 Serves 4. 
 Ingredients : 
 2 large  onions , chopped 
 2 stalks  celery , chopped 
 2  tomatoes , chopped 
 ¼ cup of  butter 
 ½ cup of  tomato juice 
 2 tbsp  lemon juice 
 3 – 4 cloves  garlic , crushed 
 1  bay leaf 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 1 – 2 lbs  fish  fillets of your choice 
 Directions : 
 Sauté  onions ,  celery  and  tomatoes  in  butter  until tender. 
 Add all juices,  garlic  and  bay leaf . 
 Cook this for 3 minutes over medium heat. 
 Place fillets in greased baking dish. 
 Season. 
 Pour sauce over the fish. 
 Bake at 350°F about 20 minutes, until fish is done or opaque.
