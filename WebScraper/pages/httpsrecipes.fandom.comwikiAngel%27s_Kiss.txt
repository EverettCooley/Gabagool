Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ oz  crème de cacao  (white) 
 ¼ oz  sloe gin 
 ¼ oz  brandy 
 ¼ oz  light cream 
 Directions : 
 Pour carefully, in order given, into cordial glass so that each ingredient floats on the preceding without mixing.
