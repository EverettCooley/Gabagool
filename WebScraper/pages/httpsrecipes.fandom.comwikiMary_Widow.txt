Ingredients : 
 2 oz  gin 
 1 oz  dry vermouth 
 1 tsp  Pernod 
 1 tsp  Bénédictine 
 2 dashes  Orange bitters 
 Directions : 
 Shake all the ingredients in a shaker with  ice  and strain into a cocktail glass. 
 Garnish with  lemon .
