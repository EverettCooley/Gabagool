About biscuit mix : 
 Biscuit mixes  are commercial products which contain the basic ingredients, such as  flour ,  baking powder ,  salt , and sometimes fat like  butter  or  oil , which would normally be used in baking in general and biscuit making in particular.
 One popular brand is  Bisquick .
