Description 
 Green bean casserole was invented in 1955 by the Campbell Soup Company. It has since become a Thanksgiving favorite in the United States.
 There are many basic recipes for green bean casserole. Here is one example:
 
 Green bean casserole  from the Wikibooks Cookbook, source of original article licensed under the GNU Free Documentation License. 
 Cook Time : 30 minutes 
 Serves : 4 
 Ingredients 
 8 cups cooked  green beans 
 1 cup  milk 
 2⅔ cups (canned)  French fried onions , divided into 2 equal portions 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 2 x 10¾-ounce cans  cream of mushroom  soup 
 2 teaspoons  soy sauce 
 Directions 
 Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). 
 Stir beans, milk, ½ of onions, pepper, soup, and soy sauce in a 3-quart casserole. 
 Bake until hot, about 25 minutes. 
 Sprinkle top with rest of onions. 
 Bake another 5 minutes. 
 Tips 
 Cream of mushroom soup may be regular or reduced sodium. 
 If you love cheese, substitute ½ cup shredded cheese for the soy sauce. 
 ½ cup of chopped red bell peppers will make it very colorful.
