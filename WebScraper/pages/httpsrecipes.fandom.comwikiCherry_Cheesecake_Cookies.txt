Description : 
 Contributed by  Lindas Busy Kitchen Desserts Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 box yellow cake mix or butter cake mix 
 ⅓ cup  oil 
 2  eggs 
 1 can cherry pie filling (21 oz) 
 Directions : 
 Mix cake mix with the oil and eggs. 
 Line cookie sheet with parchment paper and drop cookie dough by tablespoons onto it. 
 Use a small teaspoon and make a little dent in each cookie and drop a cherry into each dent. 
 Bake at 350 °F for about 10 minutes.
