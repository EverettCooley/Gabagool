Description 
 Citrus Mint Crush  from the  Public Health Cookbook  by the Seattle & King County Department of Public Health—original source of recipe, public domain government resource
 
 Serves : 4 
 Ingredients 
 1¼ cups cold  water 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 ½ cup tightly packed  mint  leaves 
 1 tablespoon grated  orange zest  (no pith) 
 ¾ cup fresh squeezed  orange juice 
 ¾ cup fresh squeezed  lemon juice 
 2 cups lemon-flavored sparkling water 
 crushed  ice 
 mint sprigs for garnish (optional) 
 Directions 
 Add water and sugar in a medium saucepan and cook over medium-high heat until sugar completely dissolves, stirring constantly. 
 Remove from heat and let cool completely. 
 Coarsely chop mint leaves and set aside. 
 Add the cooled sugar-water to a medium sized bowl then stir in mint, orange zest, orange juice and lemon juice. 
 Cover bowl and refrigerate until chilled through. 
 Pour mixture through a fine mesh strainer into a large pitcher. 
 Discard remaining orange zest and mint leaves. 
 Stir in sparkling water then serve over crushed ice. 
 Garnish with a mint sprig.
