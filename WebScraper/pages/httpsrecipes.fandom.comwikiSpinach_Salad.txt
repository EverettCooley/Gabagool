Ingredients : 
 6 cups fresh  spinach  leaves, washed, stemmed and torn 
 1¼ cups (1 medium) unpeeled, chopped red  apple 
 ¼ cup  raisins 
 2 tbsp sliced  green onion 
 Dressing : 
 3 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 2 tbsp  cider vinegar 
 2 tsp chopped  chutney 
 ¾ tsp  curry powder 
 ½ tsp  dry mustard 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Salad : 
 Combine  spinach ,  apple ,  raisins  and  green onion  in large bowl; refrigerate for 5 minutes. 
 Dressing : 
 Combine  vegetable oil ,  vinegar , chutney,  curry  powder,  mustard  and  salt  in small bowl. 
 Pour over salad; toss well to coat.
