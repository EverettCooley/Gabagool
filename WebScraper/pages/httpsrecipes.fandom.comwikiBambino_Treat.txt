 Ingredients : 
 (A) 1 tablespoon urad dal 
 1 tablespoon arhar dal 
 1 teaspoon jeera 
 1 teaspoon mustard 
 2 full red chillies(dried) 
 1+1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder 
 3/4 teaspoon haldi 
 1+1/2 teaspoons salt 
 2 green chillies(chopped) 
 few curry leaves 
 1 pinch Hing 
 3+1/2 tablespoon groundnut oil 
 
 
 (B) Bambino Samaiya Packet (3/4 packet) 
 3/4 cup groundnuts 
 1 potato 
 1 Lemon 
 Coriander Leaves 
 Water in container 
 
 
 Directions : 
 =====1. Boil water with 2 tablespoons of oil in a
container . Add aboout 3 tablespoons of
salt to the boiling water.
 2. Add Bambino from the packet and stir a
little.
 3. Boil the bambino for about 2 mins only(till the bambino becomes just slightly soft).
 4. Drain the water using a holed kitchen
container ( Steel Channi with big holes).
 5. Put all the bambino left in the chhanni
(without the water) in a Parat(utensil   
reqd. for kneeding flour).
 5a. Add lemon juice to it and mix it.
 6. Add oil in a Kadhai
 7. Deep Fry the small chopped pieces of   
potato and keep in a bowl.
 8. Fry the groundnuts and keep in a bowl.
 9. Add the ingradients under (A) above for
frying(as Tadka).
 10. Add the Bambino boiled above , in the
kadhai.
 11. Mix the tadka and bambino in the kadhai
well.
(Care has to be taken that the bambino
does not get mashed up.)
 12. Add the fried potatoes and groundnuts 
in kadhai and mix .
 13. Garnish with coriander leaves cut into
small pieces.
14. Serve hot
