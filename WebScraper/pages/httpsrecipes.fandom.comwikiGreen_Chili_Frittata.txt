Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Come Into the Kitchen 
 Serves 12 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  flour 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 10  eggs , lightly beaten 
 ¼ pound  butter , melted and cooled 
 2 cups small curd  cottage cheese 
 1 pound  jack cheese , grated 
 3 cans (4 oz. each) diced  green chilies 
 salt  to taste 
 Description : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 Butter a 9 x 13 inch baking dish. 
 Mix flour and baking powder. 
 Add eggs and butter; blend well. 
 Blend in remaining ingredients. 
 Put mixture in prepared dish and bake 35 to 45 minutes or until set. 
 Cut into squares. 
 Serve hot.
