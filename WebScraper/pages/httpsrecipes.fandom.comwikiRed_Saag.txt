Description : 
 Often called red spinach because of the earthy spinach flavor. 
 Similar to amaranth, but has dark red leaves and light-red stems. 
 Instructions : 
 Mix red saag curries into plain rice turning it a pretty pink color. 
 To cook cut off the lower tough stems, then wash and chop it coarsley. 
 It can also be pureed and cooked with spices, or stewed with meats
