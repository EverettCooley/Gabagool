 Ingredients : 
 1 whole  chicken 
 water 
 6  chicken bouillon  cubes 
 4 stalks  celery  (with leaves) 
 1 large  onion , quartered 
 pepper 
 1 (8 ounce) package  angel hair pasta , broken into 1-inch pieces 
 3  carrots , sliced 
 Directions : 
 Wash  chicken  and place in large soup pot. 
 Add  water  up to the top of the  chicken . 
 Add bouillon cubes,  celery  stalks,  onion  and  pepper  (to taste). 
 Bring to boil, cover and let simmer for 3 hours. 
 Be sure not to burn the bottom. 
 Remove vegetables and  chicken . 
 Break up the  chicken  meat into small pieces with a fork and put back into the soup. 
 Continue to lightly simmer the soup while you prepare the  pasta  in a separate pan, then add  pasta  to soup. 
 While cooking the  pasta , boil the  carrots  in a pan for about 30 minutes and add to the soup. 
 Serve!
