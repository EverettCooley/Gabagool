A blend of sweet and hot spices 
 Dhan  means rice and  sak  is the dal and lamb that go with it 
 Masala is Made of cardamom, cloves, black pepper, coriander, red chilies, ginger, nutmeg, star anise, and ground cinnamon 
 To make dhansak, lamb is cooked in the thick sauce with the masala and cooked into the dal
