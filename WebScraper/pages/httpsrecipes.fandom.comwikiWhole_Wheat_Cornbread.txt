Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup  cornmeal 
 1 cup  whole wheat flour 
 4 tbsp  baking powder 
 ¾ tbsp  salt 
 ¼ cup  honey  (or use  molasses  it's good too!) 
 2 tbsp  applesauce 
 ¾ cup  soy milk ,  milk  or  water 
 1  egg  (or ¼ cup  egg substitute ) beaten 
 ½ cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Sift together  cornmeal ,  flour ,  baking powder  and  salt . 
 Add remaining ingredients; mix well with a spoon. 
 Pour into a 9" round teflon baking dish (or spray a 9" round glass baking dish with " oil  &  flour " non-stick spray). 
 Bake at 350°F for 25 minutes, or until golden brown.
