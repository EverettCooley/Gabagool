Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Love Estate in Sachse, Texas in 1984.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups fresh  blueberries 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 1½ cups  flour  sifted 
 1 teaspoon  sugar 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ½ cup salad  oil 
 2 tablespoon  milk 
 Streusel : 
 ¾ cup  sugar 
 2 tablespoon  flour 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 2 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 Directions : 
 Sprinkle ¼ cup of sugar over berries then set aside. 
 Mix flour, sugar and salt. 
 Combine salad oil and milk in a small bowl and whip with fork then pour over flour mixture. 
 Mix with fork until moist then turn into greased square cake pan. 
 Press dough into pan building up edges about one inch deep. 
 Spread berries over the dough. 
 Mix sugar, flour and salt together then cut in butter until crumbly and sprinkle over fruit. 
 Bake at 425°F for 45 minutes.
