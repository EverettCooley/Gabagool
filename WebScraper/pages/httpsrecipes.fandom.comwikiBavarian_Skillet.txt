Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound smoked country-style link  Sausage 
 1 cup chopped  Onion 
 1 10-3/4-ounce can condensed  cream  of  celery  soup 
 1 16-ounce can cut  green beans 
 2 tablespoons prepared  mustard 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Sausage  in covered pan in 1/2 cup  water  about 5 minutes. 
 Remove from  water  and cut into 1-inch pieces. Set aside. 
 Cook  Onion  in greased skillet until tender but not brown. 
 Stir in soup,  green beans  with their liquid and  mustard . 
 Season to taste. 
 Simmer about 10 minutes to blend flavors. 
 Serve over fluffy  rice .
