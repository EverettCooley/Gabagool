 Description : 
 From my Aunt Maggie's Collection, (date on card says 1897).
 
 Ingredients : 
 1-cup brown  sugar 
 2 tablespoons unsalted  butter 
 2 cups hot  water 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Prepare in a large heavy saucepan with a tight fitting lid. Make the following batter and drop by the spoonful into the hot sauce. 
 ½ cup granulated  sugar 
 ½ cup whole  milk 
 1-cup  flour 
 ½ cup  dates  or 1-cup  raisins 
 2 tablespoons  unsalted butter 
 1 tablespoons  cocoa 
 1-teaspoon  cinnamon 
 2 teaspoons  baking powder 
 Bake on top of stove, covered tightly, for 35 minutes. Serve hot or cold. Both are good.
