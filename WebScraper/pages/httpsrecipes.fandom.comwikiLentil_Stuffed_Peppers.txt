 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2/3 c  red lentils 
 1Â inch piece  ginger ,=grated 
 4 tb  vegetable oil 
 1 tb Ground coriander 
 4 md Green  bell peppers 
 1 1/4 c  water 
 1 ts  cumin  seeds 
 salt  &  pepper 
 2  onions , chopped 
 2 tb Chopped  cilantro 
 2 Green chilies 
 Directions : 
 Rinse  Lentils  & soak for 30 minutes. 
 Heat half  oil  in skillet. 
 Add  peppers  & cook for 3 to 5 minutes till golden brown. Drain & cool. 
 Add remaining  oil  to pan. 
 Cook  cumin  till they begin to pop. 
 Add  onions  & chilies & cook for 8 minutes. 
 Stir in  ginger  & coriander. 
 Drain  Lentils  add to the pan with  water . 
 Stir well & cover. 
 Cook for 15 to 20 minutes until lthe liquid has evaporated. 
 Stir in  salt  &  pepper . 
 Add  cilantro . 
 Cut tops off  peppers  & remove seeds. 
 Stuff with the  Lentils  & replace the tops. 
 Stand in a baking dish. 
 Bake at 350F for 15 minutes.
