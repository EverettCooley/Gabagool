Description : 
A fresh take on a grilled cheese, this bagel sandwich is packed with real cheese and tomatoes.         Bagel Sandwich     
 Ingredients : 
 5 fresh  bagels 
 5  tomato  slices 
 5 cheese slices  cheese 
 basil  leaves, optional 
 Directions : 
 Cut each bagel in half and arrange tomatoes on one side and cheese on the other. 
 Place on the cooking sheet and broil in an oven until  cheese  is melted. 
 Remove from the oven and put the  bagel  sandwich together.
