Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds  strawberries , hulled and halved 
 1  mango , pitted, peeled and diced 
 2  kiwis , peeled and diced 
 ¼ cup  sugar  or to taste 
 1 cup  white grape juice 
 3 tbsp  lime juice 
 ½ cup vanilla  yogurt 
 Directions : 
 Dice 1 cup strawberries. 
 Combine in a bowl with mango and kiwi. 
 Reserve and refrigerate ½ cup. 
 Puree remaining halved strawberries with the sugar in food processor until smooth. 
 Pour into a bowl, stir in grape and lime juice and remaining diced fruit. 
 Refrigerate 2 hours. 
 To serve, ladle gazpacho into bowls. 
 Top with yogurt, then reserved fruit.
