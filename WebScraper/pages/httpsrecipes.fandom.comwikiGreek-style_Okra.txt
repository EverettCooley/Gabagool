Description : 
 In Romanian: Bame preparate greceste.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb / 500 g young  okra 
 2 - 3 tablespoons  oil 
 5 - 6  tomatoes 
 1  onion 
 1 tablespoon  vinegar 
 ½ teaspoon  sugar 
 1 teaspoon mixed chopped  parsley  and  dill 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Clean the okra, wash and keep in water with salt and vinegar for 30–40 minutes. 
 In the meantime, finely chop the onion and fry in oil until yellow. 
 Add the peeled, seeded and chopped tomatoes, then the okra, vinegar, sugar and salt. Mix gently and simmer, covered, at low heat. 
 When almost done, add the chopped parsley and dill. 
 You may serve this dish warm; however, it is much tastier when cold.
