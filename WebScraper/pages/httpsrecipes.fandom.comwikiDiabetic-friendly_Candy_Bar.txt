Contributed by Jenn B aka Mom2Sam and Tiny at  Healthy Recipes For Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 packets or 1 tsp  Sweet 'n Low 
 ⅓ cup  powdered milk 
 ⅓ cup  peanut butter 
 ⅓ cup  oats 
 ½ tsp  vanilla 
 1½ tsp  water 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients. 
 Shape into bar. 
 Wrap in wax paper. 
 Refrigerate until firm.
