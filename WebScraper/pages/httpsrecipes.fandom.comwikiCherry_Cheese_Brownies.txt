Ingredients : 
 1 pkg. Betty Crocker Fudge Brownie Mix 
 2 tsp. vanilla 
 1 egg 
 1 can sweetened condensed milk 
 1 (8 oz.) cream cheese, softened 
 1 can cherry pie filling 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 350 F. 
 Prepare brownie mix as directed except bake 20 minutes. 
 Beat egg, milk, vanilla and cream cheese in large bowl until smooth. 
 Pour over baked brownie layer. 
 Bake until topping is set, 25 minutes. 
 Cool 2 hours. 
 Spread cherry pie filling. 
 Cut into 2 inch squares. 
 Refrigerate.
