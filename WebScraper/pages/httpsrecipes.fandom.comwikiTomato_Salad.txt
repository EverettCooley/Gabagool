Description : 
 You have to like tomatoes, but this is a good way to use up summer garden vegetables.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: Newspaper Clipping 
 Ingredients : 
 tomatoes 
 bell peppers 
 sweet  pickles  or  relish 
 mayonnaise 
 Ritz crackers 
 Directions : 
 Layer chopped tomatoes (fairly thick layer) 
 Layer chopped bell peppers (thin to medium thick layer) 
 Layer sweet pickles or relish (thin or medium thick) 
 Top with a layer of mayonnaise 
 finally add a layer of finely crushed Ritz crackers. 
 Cover and leave in refrigerator overnight.
