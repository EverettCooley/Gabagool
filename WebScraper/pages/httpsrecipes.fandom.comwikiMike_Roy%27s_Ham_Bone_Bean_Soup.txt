Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Come Into The Kitchen 
 Serves 8 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups dry  navy beans 
 3 quarts  water 
 ham bone 
 1 large  onion , chopped 
 1 cup  celery , chopped 
 1 clove  garlic , chopped 
 1 cup  tomato sauce   or  2 large  tomatoes , peeled and chopped 
 ¼ tbsp  oregano 
 ¼ tbsp  thyme 
 ¼ tbsp  sweet basil 
 1  bay leaf  (optional) 
 3 whole  cloves 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Wash beans, cover with  water  and let stand overnight. 
 Drain beans, cover with  water  again. 
 Bring to a rolling boil and boil one minute. 
 Remove from heat and drain. 
 Add the 3 quarts of  water  to the beans along with the  ham  bone and simmer 2 hours. 
 Add the remaining ingredients and simmer another hour. 
 Be careful with  salt  because of the  ham  bone.
