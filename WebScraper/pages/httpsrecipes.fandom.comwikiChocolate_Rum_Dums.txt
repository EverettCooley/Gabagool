Ingredients : 
 8½ oz Pkg  chocolate  wafers crushed 
 1 cup  pecans , chopped 
 1 cup xx  sugar 
 ¼ cup  light corn syrup 
 ¼ cup  dark rum 
 Directions : 
 Combine crushed wafer,  pecans  and  sugar , syrup and  rum . 
 Mix well. 
 Shape by teaspooful into round balls. 
 Let stand 10 minutes. 
 Roll in extra xx  sugar .
