Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1 teaspoon  curry powder 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 1 cup chopped  carrots 
 1 cup chopped  zucchini 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 ½ cup  water 
 3 cups cooked  rice  (cooked in  chicken broth ) 
 ½ cup  raisins , plumped 
 ½ cup toasted chopped  pecans 
 2 tablespoons  orange marmalade 
 ¼ cup  sour cream 
 Directions : 
 Heat  garlic  and  curry  in  oil  in large skillet. 
 Add  carrots ,  zucchini ,  salt ,  pepper ,  cinnamon  and  water . 
 Cover; cook 10 minutes, or until vegetables are tender crisp. 
 Stir in  rice ,  Raisins ,  pecans ,  marmalade  and  sour cream ; heat thoroughly.
