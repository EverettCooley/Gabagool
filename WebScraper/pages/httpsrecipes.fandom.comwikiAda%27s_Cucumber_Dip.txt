 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1  cucumber  (thinly sliced) 
 1  Onion , small (thinly sliced) 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1/2 tsp  garlic powder 
 1 tsp  lemon juice 
 1/2 tsp  marjoram 
 8 oz lo-calories  yogurt 
 Directions : 
 Peel and thinly slice  cucumber  and  Onion . Sprinkle with  salt . Allow to r combine  garlic powder ,  lemon juice ,  marjoram , and  yogurt ; mix thoroughly. Fold in sliced  cucumber  and  Onion . Chill. Yield: 2 cups 1 serving = 1  milk  calories: 10
