 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group

 Serves 10 – 12 
 Ingredients : 
 5 lbs  russet potatoes 
 1 pint  sour cream 
 1 can  cream of chicken  soup 
 12 oz grated sharp  cheddar  cheese 
 ½ cup  butter , melted 
 3 tbsp chopped  chives 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Bake  potatoes  about 1 hour at 350°F or until fork inserted comes out easily. 
 Cool. Remove skin. Discard skins. 
 Grate  potatoes . 
 Mix grated  potatoes  with remaining ingredients. 
 Stir until well blended. 
 Put in 2 quart casserole or any size that they will fit in. 
 Bake at 350°F for 30 to 40 minutes or until  cheese  is melted and mixture is bubbly around the edges.
