Description : 
 Contributed by  PressureCookerRecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Oscar Gomez, Tucson, Arizona in  Southern Living , November 2000 
 Yield: 8 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 sliced  purple onion 
 2 teaspoons  olive oil 
 3 cups  Mexican Meat Mix 
 ½ cup  sour cream 
 16-ounce  Italian bread  shell 
 onion 
 1 (8-ounce) package shredded Mexican  cheese  blend 
 ½ cup  lettuce 
 ½ cup  cherry tomatoes , halved 
 2 tablespoons chopped fresh  cilantro 
 Directions : 
 Sauté onion in olive oil over medium heat until tender. 
 Stir together  Mexican Meat Mix  and sour cream, spread evenly over the Italian bread shell. 
 Top with onion and shredded Mexican cheese blend. 
 Place on a lightly greased baking sheet. 
 Bake at 450°F for 15 minutes. 
 Remove from oven; top with lettuce; cherry tomatoes and chopped fresh cilantro.
