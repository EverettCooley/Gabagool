 Description : 
 Makes 1 1/2 cups.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pint California  strawberries , washed and stemmed 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 1/2 cup  orange  honey 
 Directions : 
 In blender, whirl  Berries  until smooth. 
 Measure 2 cups puree into saucepan. 
 Add remaining ingredients. 
 Bring to boil, then simmer 20 to 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
 Cool (This is a  butter  that will pour.)
