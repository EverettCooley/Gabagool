Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 6 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups (uncooked)  corkscrew macaroni 
 ½ cup plain low fat  yogurt 
 ⅓ cup reduced calorie creamy Italian salad dressing 
 1 tbsp grated  Parmesan cheese 
 1 small clove  garlic , minced (optional) 
 2 cups mixed shredded  cabbage  and  carrots 
 1 medium  cucumber , chopped 
 1 medium  tomato , seeded and chopped 
 lettuce  leaves (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Cook macaroni according to package directions. 
 Drain, rinse under cold running water. 
 Drain well. 
 In a small bowl stir together yogurt, salad dressing, Parmesan cheese and garlic. 
 In a large bowl toss together macaroni salad dressing mixture, cabbage and carrots, cucumber and tomato. 
 Cover; quick chill in the freezer for 10 minutes. 
 Serve atop lettuce if desired. 
 Nutrition information : 
 Per serving: 131 cal., 3 g Fat, 2 mg Chol., 155 mg Sodium
