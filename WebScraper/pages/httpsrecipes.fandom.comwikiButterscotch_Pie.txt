My mother obtained from her knitting group in 1952. She said it had been around for years at that point. She is guessing that this is a recipe from the 1920s.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup unsalted  butter 
 1 cup  water 
 2  egg   yolks 
 1 cup brown  sugar 
 2-1/2 tablespoons  cornstarch 
 1-1/2 cups  milk 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Brown  butter  in pan. Add  Sugar  and cook together, stirring constantly, until  Sugar  is melted. 
 Add  water ; stir slowly and cook until  Sugar  is again dissolved. 
 Combine  cornstarch ,  egg   yolks  and  milk , and add to  Sugar  mixture. 
 Cook until thickened. Pour in baked pie shell and top with  cream .
