Description : 
 Contributed by Lil at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Old Newspaper Clipping 
 Ingredients : 
 4 large  eggs 
 2 tbsp  milk 
 ½ tsp ground  cinnamon 
 8 slices cinnamon  bread 
 apricot jam  or your choice 
 1 tbsp  butter 
 maple syrup 
 Directions : 
 Beat  eggs  with  milk  and  cinnamon . 
 Make 4 sandwiches with  apricot jam . 
 Dip sandwiches into  egg  mixture, turning once until all moisture is absorbed by  bread . 
 Cook sandwiches on buttered skillet or griddle over low heat until browned on both sides. 
 Serve hot with  maple syrup . 
 Variations : 
 Substitute  cottage cheese  for jam: mix ½ cup  cottage cheese  with 1 tbsp  sugar . 
 Substitute fresh fruit for jam: arrange thin slices of fresh  pear ,  peaches ,  nectarines  or  bananas  on  bread  and sprinkle with  cinnamon  or spread with a little  apricot jam .
