Description : 
 Contributed by Dorie  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  rice 
 1 can  cream of mushroom  soup 
 ½ soup can  water 
 1 pkg dry  onion soup mix 
 ½  chicken  cut up or 4  chicken breast  halves 
 Directions : 
 Grease baking dish. 
 Place rice in dish. 
 Combine soup, water and soup mix and pour over rice. 
 Place chicken on top and gently press down into liquid (does not have to be totally covered). 
 Cover and bake at 350°F for 1 hour or until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed.
