Ingredients : 
 1 (pound) or (16 ounces) 45 dag  Bacon 
 1 head  Broccoli 
 Onion , chop fine, (to your taste) 
 1 1/2 (pounds) or (24 ounces) 68 dag whole, red seedless  grapes , (wash,drain, chill) 
 1 1/2 cups ( 12 ounces) sliced or  slivered almonds ,add these just before serving 
 DRESSING; 1 cup mayonnaise 
 1/4 cup  Sugar 
 2 Tablespoons of  vinegar 
 Blend the dressing ingredients together and put in a covered container in the refrigerator overnight. 
 Directions : 
 The night before, fry the  Bacon  until crisp, drain off the fat and crumble the  Bacon  and put in a covered bowl in the refrigerator. 
 Chop  Onion  and put in covered container also. 
 Next, clean and wash the  Broccoli  and fruit and again put them separately in refrigerator. 
 When ready to serve, put dressing over all ingredients in a bowl and now put in the  almonds . Toss lightly and serve.
