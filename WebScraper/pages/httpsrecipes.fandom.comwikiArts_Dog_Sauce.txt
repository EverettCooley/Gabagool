Ingredients : 
 1 	lbs 	ground  Beef 
 70 	oz 	 ketchup 
 2 	cups 	 water 
 2 	med 	 onions  chopped 
 1 1/2 	tsp 	 garlic  ground 
 1 	tsp 	 cinnamon 
 1 	tsp 	cyenne  pepper 
 14 	drops 	 Tabasco sauce 
 4 	Tbls 	whochester sauce 
 Directions : 
 Fry  onions  and ground  Beef  till done. 
Add all other ingredients and simmer for 2-3 hours enjoy great hot dog sauce.
