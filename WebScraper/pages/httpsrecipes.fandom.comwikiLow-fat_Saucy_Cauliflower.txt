 Ingredients : 
 1 head  cauliflower , steamed 
 ½ cup fat-free  mayonnaise 
 ½ cup fat free  sour cream 
 2 teaspoons  Dijon mustard 
 ½ cup fat-free  cheddar  cheese 
 ½ teaspoon  garlic salt 
 2 sprigs  cilantro , for garnish (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Heat the oven to 375°F. 
 Use Pam to spray an 8x8 oven dish. 
 Put the  cauliflower  in the dish. 
 Mix remaining ingredients and pour over the  cauliflower . 
 Bake at 375°F for 15 minutes, or until heated through.
