 Description : 
 Broccoli Soup. Serves 6 persons
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 700 g  broccoli 
 1 medium  onion 
 3 small  potatoes 
 4 tablespoons  white wine 
 3 cups  chicken broth 
 2 tablespoons  butter 
 1 litre  milk 
 3 tablespoons  cream 
 salt 
 pepper 
 Directions : 
 Wash and clean  broccoli ,  onion  and  potatoes . 
 Melt the  butter  in a large saucepan and saute the  broccoli , the  onion  and the  potatoes  for 7 â€“ 10 minutes. 
 Add the  chicken broth , the wine,  salt  and  pepper  and let simmer for 20 minutes. 
 Place the soup in a mixer and mash it. 
 Put it back in the saucepan, add the mink and let it boil for 6 minutes. 
 Serve hot with some  cream  on top and some  pepper .
