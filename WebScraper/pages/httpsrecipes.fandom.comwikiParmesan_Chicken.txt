Description : 
 Contributed by Linda in Tennessee via  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 6  chicken breasts  halves, skinless, about 2 pounds 
 2 tablespoons  butter 
 ½ cup  Parmesan cheese  grated 
 ¼ cup  bread crumbs 
 1 teaspoon  oregano  dried 
 1 teaspoon  parsley  flakes 
 ¼ teaspoon  paprika 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 400°F. 
 Spray 15x10x1 inch baking pan with no-stick  cooking spray . 
 Dip  chicken  in  butter ; coat with combined remaining ingredients. 
 Place in prepared pan. 
 Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until tender. 
 Variations : 
 Spicy: Substitute ⅛ to ¼ teaspoon  ground red pepper  for  black pepper .
