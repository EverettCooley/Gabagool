 Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Prep time: 15 minutes | cook 40 minutes | stand 20 minutes | total 1 hour 15 minutes 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  flour 
 ½ cup unsweetened  cocoa 
 2 teaspoons  baking powder 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ½ cup  soy milk 
 ¼ cup unsweetened  applesauce 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 ¾ cup  brown sugar 
 1¾ cups hot  water 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Spritz 8x8 pan with cooking spray. 
 Combine flour, baking powder, salt, and ¼ cup cocoa. 
 Blend in apple sauce, vanilla, and milk. 
 Spoon batter into pan. 
 Combine brown sugar, ¼ cup cocoa, and hot water. 
 Stir to blend. 
 Pour over batter in pan. 
 Bake for 40 minutes. 
 Remove and cool in pan. 
 You may cover with your favorite topping, or plain, but spoon chocolate sauce that has formed on the bottom, over the top of the brownie.
