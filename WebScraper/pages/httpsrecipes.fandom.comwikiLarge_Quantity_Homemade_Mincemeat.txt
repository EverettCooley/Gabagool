Description : 
 From my Aunt Lois' collection. The card was very ragged so I'm assuming it was well used. Date unknown.
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 makes 12 quarts 
 Ingredients : 
 4 pounds lean  beef  - boil and grind 
 8 pounds peeled, chopped  apples 
 1 pound  suet  - grind 
 4 pounds  raisins 
 1 pound  brown sugar 
 1 quart cooking  molasses 
 2 quarts sweet  cider 
 1 tablespoon each -  salt ,  pepper ,  allspice ,  cloves ,  nutmeg 
 4 tablespoons  cinnamon 
 1 quart sweet  wine  - or concord  grape juice 
 Directions : 
 Cook all together in large kettle until  Apples  and  raisins  are cooked medium temperature, careful of sticking. 
 Put into quart jars and seal. 
 If any jars don't seal make a pie!
