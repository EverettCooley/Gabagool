Description : 
 The only part in this recipe (Piyaz - Turkish Black-eyed pea salad) that is time consuming is the peas. If you haven't got the time to boil the peas just buy canned peas (3 cans should do it).
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ large  red onion , grated or ground by hand with a bit of  salt  sprinkled on the  onion 
 2 cups  black-eyed peas , soaked for 24hrs in salty  water  and boiled until done in the same  water  for soaking and drain well 
 4  roma tomatoes , diced 
 ⅓ cup  cilantro 
 2 tablespoons  dill weed  (dry or fresh what ever is available) 
 4 freshly squeezed  lemons 
 3 tablespoons  red wine vinegar 
 salt  and black  pepper 
 1 teaspoon ground  cumin 
 1½ teaspoons  sumac 
 4-6 finely chopped  garlic 
 Directions : 
 Toss all the ingredients together. 
 Marinate for a few hours or 24hrs and serve. 
 Great with barbequed  salmon .
