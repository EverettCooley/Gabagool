Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  water 
 ½ cup  granulated sugar 
 ¼ cup  corn syrup 
 2 x medium candy canes (any flavour ) 
 1 liter non-fat  vanilla  frozen  yogurt  or  ice cream 
 1 oz  bittersweet chocolate  grated 
 2 tbsp  cocoa powder 
 2 tbsp  evaporated milk 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 pinch of  salt 
 Directions : 
 Bring  water ,  sugar  and  corn syrup  to a boil in a small saucepan. 
 Reduce heat and simmer for 12 minutes. 
 Meanwhile seal candy canes in a ziplock plastic bag and crush candy with a small mallet. 
 Place frozen  yogurt  or  ice cream  in food processor fitted with small blade. 
 Add smashed candy canes and pulse to mix. 
 Whisk grated  chocolate  and  cocoa powder  into  corn syrup  mixture, whisk in  evaporated milk ,  vanilla  and  salt . 
 Serve over frozen  yogurt  or  ice cream . Served over frozen  yogurt  is a nice low fat treat.
