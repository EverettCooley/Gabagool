 Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 oz. Bacardi Carta Blanca (Light-Dry)  rum 
 1/2 oz.  lemon  or  lime juice 
 1/2 oz.  Grenadine 
 4 1/2 oz.  cranberry juice 
 1 oz.  pineapple juice 
 Directions : 
 Pour over  ice  into tall glass.

 The Bartending School
