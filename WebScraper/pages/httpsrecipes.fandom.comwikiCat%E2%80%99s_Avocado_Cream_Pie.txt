 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 oz  butter 
 1 Tbsp Honey 
 14 oz  sweetened condensed milk 
 1 ½ Cups Graham cracker crumbs 
 8 oz  cream cheese 
 2  California avocados 
 ¼ Cup  lime juice 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 1/8 tsp  sea salt 
 ½ Cup  coconut , toasted - as garnish 
 Directions : 
 METHOD
 Preheat oven to 325 °F.
 To make crust, melt  butter  in a small saucepan, over low heat. Stir in honey. Add graham cracker crumbs and mix well. Pat crumbs evenly onto bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch pie plate. Bake pie shell for 12 to 15 minutes. Remove from oven and cool.
 In a medium bowl, combine  condensed milk  and  cream cheese ; blend well.
 Mash avocados with  lime juice  and add to  milk  mixture.
 Add  vanilla  and  salt , and beat until smooth. Turn into cooled crust.
 Garnish with  coconut , cover and chill for 2 to 6 hours before serving.
