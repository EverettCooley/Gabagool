Description : 
 A TNT recipe from Dinah Shore's Kitchen
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: my old recipes 
 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs lean  ground round 
 1  egg 
 1 tbsp  dry or English mustard 
 2 tbsp  Worcestershire 
 1 tbsp  salt  or to taste 
 ¼ tbsp  pepper 
 1 cup  chicken broth  or  consommé 
 2 medium  onions , thinly sliced 
 Directions : 
 Mix meat,  eggs , seasoning thoroughly. 
 Add consommé gradually, blending well. 
 Shape into patties. 
 Brown quickly in fat and finish cooking over low heat. 
 Remove hamburgers from pan and keep hot. 
 Add  onions  to fat in pan. 
 Sauté over low heat until brown and serve on the side.
