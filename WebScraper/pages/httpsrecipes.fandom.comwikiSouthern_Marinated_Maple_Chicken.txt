Description : 
 This is a recipe I obtained from an Estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Manes Estate in Venus, Texas in 1981.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 6  chicken breasts 
 ½ cup  maple syrup 
 ½ cup  corn oil 
 1 tablespoon  garlic 
 1 teaspoon  ginger 
 1 can  tomato soup 
 ½ teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients except chicken in large mixing bowl. 
 Put chicken in large plastic bag and pour marinade over top. 
 Refrigerate overnight. 
 Grill and serve.
