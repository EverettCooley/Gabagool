Ingredients : 
 4 cups dry  samp  (broken hominy, slightly crushed) 
 1 Lb dry  cowpeas  (black-eyed peas) or any similar beans 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Combine samp and cowpeas in a large glass bowl. Add cold  water  sufficient to cover. Cover, and let stand overnight. Drain and rinse before cooking. 
 Place the soaked samp and cowpeas in a large pot and cover the mixture with cold  water . Bring to a boil. Let boil for 10 minutes. Reduce heat. Simmer on low heat for 1-2 hours, until all is tender and the  water  is mostly absorbed. Add additional  water  during cooking if needed. 
 Season with  salt . Serve hot.
