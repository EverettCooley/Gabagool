Description : 
 Good with whipped topping. Not suitable for diabetic, carbohydrate information missing.
 
 Cut 16 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1¼ cup  flour 
 ¾ cup  granulated sugar 
 ¼ cup  cocoa 
 ¼ cup  margarine , softened 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 2 large  egg whites  (1 large egg works) 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 1 cup hot  water 
 2 tsp  confectioner's sugar  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Measure ingredients into mixing bowl in the order given, beat until smooth. 
 Pour into 9x9 inch pan that has been sprayed with no-stick cooking spray or use wax paper to line the pan. 
 Bake about 25 – 30 minutes or until an inserted wooden pick comes out clean. 
 Serve warm or cool. 
 If cool, sift icing/confectioner"s sugar over cake. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 110 calories | 0mg cholesterol | 169mg sodium | 3g fat
