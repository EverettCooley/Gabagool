 Directions : 
 In a bowl combine seafood,  mayonnaise ,  catsup ,  lemon juice , Worcestershire, petit pois,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Peel  avocados ; divide lengthwise and sprinkle with a dash of  lemon juice . 
 Fill stuffing in that cavity. 
 Garnish with chopped  chives  and  red pepper .
