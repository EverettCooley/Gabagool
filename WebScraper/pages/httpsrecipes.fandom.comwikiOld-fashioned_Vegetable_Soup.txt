Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Cookbook: Caring & Cooking for the Hyper-Active Child by Mary Jane Finsand 
 Typed By: Susan alexanderjamesmom@gmail.com 
 Yield: 2½ qt. (2.5 l) 
 Ingredients : 
 2 qt.  beef stock  (will post later) 2 l 
 ½ cup  lima beans  60 ml 
 1 cup  carrots  (diced) 250 ml 
 ½ cup  potatoes  (diced) 125 ml 
 ½ cup  celery  (chopped) 125 ml 
 ½ cup  peas  125 ml 
 ½ cup  corn  60 ml 
 ¼ cup  green beans  (cut) 60 ml 
 ¼ cup  rutabagas  (diced) 60 ml 
 1 cup  noodles  (broken and cooked) 250 ml 
 ¼ cup  cabbage  (shredded) 60 ml 
 ¼ cup  onions  (chopped) 60 ml 
 garlic powder 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Combine beef stock and lima beans in soup kettle. 
 Bring to boil; reduce heat and simmer 30 minutes. 
 Add remaining ingredients. 
 Simmer 30 minutes or until vegetables and noodles are tender.
