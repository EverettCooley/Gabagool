Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Williams Estate in Tyler, Texas in 1994.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 layer raw  potatoes  sliced 
 1 layer white  onions 
 1 layer  bacon  strips uncooked 
 1 layer  celery  sliced 
 1 layer  carrot 
 1 layer small meatballs uncooked 
 1 layer  cheese  slices 
 3 tablespoons  rice  uncooked 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 1 can  cream of chicken  soup 
 1 cup  milk 
 Directions : 
 Butter sides of baking dish and layer ingredients in layers. 
 Mix soup and  milk  and pour over all. 
 Bake at 350Â°F for 90 minutes.
