Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 oz  pineapple juice 
 2 scoops vanilla  ice cream 
 ¾ oz  coconut rum 
 ¾ oz  dark rum 
 2 splashes  orange juice 
 ¾ oz  cream 
 2 ice cubes 
 Directions : 
 Place all ingredients in a blender. 
 Mix for 1 minute or until smooth. 
 Serve immediately.
