 Serves : 3 
 Ingredients 
 1 cup  evaporated milk 
 1 ripe  banana 
 1 teaspoon  lemon juice 
 2 cups  ice 
 1 tablespoon  honey  or  sugar 
 nutmeg , optional 
 Directions 
 Mix evaporated milk, banana, lemon juice, and honey together in blender on high speed. 
 Add ice gradually; process until slushy. 
 Sprinkle with nutmeg, if desired.
