 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 18 Oreo cookies, finely chopped 
 ¼ cup  butter , melted 
 3 (8 oz)  cream cheese , softened 
 1 (14 oz) Eagle brand  sweetened condensed milk 
 3  eggs 
 2 tsp  vanilla 
 1 cup mini  chocolate chips 
 1 tsp  flour 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 300°F. Combine crumbs and  butter . 
 Pat firmly in 9 inch spring form pan. 
 In large bowl, beat  cream cheese  until fluffy. 
 Add eagle brand  milk , beat until smooth. 
 Add  eggs  and  vanilla . 
 Beat well. 
 In small bowl, toss together ½ cup chips with  flour  to coat. 
 Stir into  cream cheese  mixture. 
 Pour into pan. 
 Sprinkle remaining chips on top. 
 Bake 1 hour or until springs back when touched. 
 Cool then chill. 
 Remove side of pan. 
 Keep refrigerated (serves large group).
