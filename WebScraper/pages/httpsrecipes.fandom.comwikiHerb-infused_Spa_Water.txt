Ingredients : 
 6 cups chilled still spring or mineral  water 
 12 thin slices  cucumber 
 4 thin slices  lemon 
 4 sprigs (each 2 in. long) fresh  mint , slightly crushed 
 2 sprigs (each 2 in. long) fresh  rosemary , slightly crushed 
 Directions : 
 In a 2- to 2½-quart pitcher, combine above ingredients * serve, or cover and chill at least 2 hours or up to 8 hours. 
 Add  ice  cubes just before serving. 
 Makes about 1½ quarts; about 4 servings.
