Annie's  sherry  Bars
 
 4 oz  baking chocolate 
 1 c  margarine 
 4  eggs 
 2 c  sugar 
 1 c  flour 
 1/4 ts  salt 
 1 ts  vanilla 
 Filling:
 
 1/2 c  butter 
 4 c  powdered sugar 
 1/4 c Half-And-Half Cream 
 1/4 c  sherry 
 Topping:
 
 12 oz  chocolate chips 
 3 tb Half-And-Half 
 4 tb  butter 
 
First Layer: Melt  chocolate  And  margarine  Together. Add  eggs ,  sugar , flour ,  salt ,  vanilla . Spread This Mixture in a Jelly Roll Pan And Bake
 
 20â€“25 minutes. Refrigerate Until Cool. 
 
Filling: Cream  butter  And  powdered sugar . Slowly Add Half-And-Half And The  sherry . Mixture Should be Light And Fluffy. Spread on Cool Base AndRefrigerate Until Cold. Topping: Melt  chocolate chips  And Add Half-And-HalfAnd  butter . When Filling is Cold, Spread on Top And Keep in RefrigeratorUntil Serving Time. Cut Into Squares.
 
Enjoy.
