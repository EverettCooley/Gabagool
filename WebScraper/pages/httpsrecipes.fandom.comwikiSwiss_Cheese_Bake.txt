Ingredients : 
 4 cups  croutons 
 2  green tomatoes , sliced thinly 
 1 ripe  tomato , sliced thinly 
 ½ lb.  Swiss cheese , shredded 
 2  eggs 
 1½ cups  milk 
 ¾ tsp.  salt 
 ½ tsp.  paprika 
 ½ tsp. dry  mustard 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 Arrange croutons in a 9" pie plate 
 Top croutons with  tomato  slices; sprinkle with  Swiss cheese . 
 In bowl, with fork or wire whisk, beat remaining ingredients 
 Pour over  cheese . 
 Bake 40 mins. or until puffy and brown. Serve immediately.
