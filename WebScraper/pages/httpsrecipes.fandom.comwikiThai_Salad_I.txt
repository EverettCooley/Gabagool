 Prep Time: 
 Cook time: 
 Serves: 
 
 Ingredients : 
 cooked  beef  (hot or cold) 2 thick steaks (sirloin or rib-eye is best) cooked until rare or some rare roasted topside/silverside 
 mint  leaves – a large handful, roughly chopped 
 coriander  – a small bunch, roughly chopped 
 chillies  – add as many as you like (the hotter the better!) chop finely 
 3 or 4  [spring onions]], sliced quite finely on the diagonal 
 2 or 3 cloves  garlic , peeled and sliced very finely 
 Dressing : 
 juice of 1 lime 
 1-2 tbsp fish sauce 
 freshly ground black pepper 
 a sprinkling of sugar (to taste) 
 Directions : 
 Cut the cooked beef into thin strips or chunks (as you prefer). 
 Mix together lime juice and fish sauce with some pepper and sugar. 
 Combine all the herbs, garlic, spring onions, chillies and beef in a bowl and mix well. 
 Pour over the dressing and mix again. 
 Serve with  sticky rice  and fresh vegetables: cucumber, green beans and cabbage or Chinese leaf.
