 Description : 
 Because this recipe uses  California avocados ...it's definitely nutritious!
 
 Ingredients : 
 Wonton wrappers 
 1  avocado 
 White  American cheese 
 Directions : 
 Cut the  Cheese  in long tiny slices and wrap it in the wonton wrapper
 To seal the edges wet your fingers with  water  and press down, and deep fry.
 Smash the  avocado  and use it like a dip.
