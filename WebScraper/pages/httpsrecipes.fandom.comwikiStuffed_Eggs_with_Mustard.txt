Description : 
 In Romanian: Oua umplute cu mustar
 
 Ingredients : 
 6  eggs 
 2 tablespoon  oil 
 1 tablespoon  mustard 
 1 tablespoon minced  dill 
 1 tablespoon  sugar 
 salt 
 6  olives 
 Directions : 
 Hard boil the  eggs , shell and let cool. 
 Cut in two lengthwise. 
 Remove the  yolks  and mix them with the  oil  poured drop by drop. 
 Add the  salt ,  sugar ,  mustard  and the chopped  dill . 
 Fill each white half with this paste and garnish each with half an olive.
