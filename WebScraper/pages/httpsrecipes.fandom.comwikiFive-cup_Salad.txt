Description : 
 Here is an easy salad recipe you can prepare for your family or take to your next family get-together.
 
 Contributed by Paula in California at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 1 cup miniature  marshmallows 
 1 cup  coconut 
 1 cup  pineapple 
 1 cup  mandarin oranges 
 Directions : 
 Mix together and refrigerate for 12 hours.
