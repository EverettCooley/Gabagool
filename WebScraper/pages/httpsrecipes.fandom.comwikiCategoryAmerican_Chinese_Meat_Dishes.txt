 American Chinese cuisine uses meats combined with all kind of special ingredients, vegetables and fruits. Chop suey is such a dish made in a juicy brown sauce. Chaw mein is a dish made with meat and served with stir-fried noodles and vegetables. As a seasoning one can always add some spicy soy sauce.
