Ingredients : 
 1/2 oz.  coffee  liqueur 
 1/2 oz.  crème de banane 
 1/2 oz. Irish  cream 
 Directions : 
 Shake with  ice  and strain into shot glass.	
This shooter recipe can also be prepared as a layered shot by putting the  coffee  liqueur in first, followed by the  creme de banane  and topped off with the Irish  cream .
