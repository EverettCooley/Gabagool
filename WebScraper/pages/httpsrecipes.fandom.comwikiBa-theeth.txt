Date crumble. An Arab favourite, this dish is from the UAE and is served regularly with gah-wa (Arabian  coffee ).
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups  wheat flour 
 2¾ cups she - ripe sticky  dates 
 1 teaspoon  cardamom 
 1¼ cups toasted  sesame seeds 
 ¾ cup  samen 
 Directions : 
 Brown the  flour  in a skillet, stirring to avoid burning. 
 Stone the  dates  and pull them apart with your hand. 
 Place in a large mixing bowl and sprinkle the  cardamom  and  sesame seeds  over. 
 Gradually add the samen ( oil ) and browned  flour , a little at a time, working the ingredients in with the fingertips. 
 When all is well blended, the mixture should resemble large biscuit crumbs. 
 Store in an airtight container and serve on a fou-alla. 
 The mixture will keep for a week.
