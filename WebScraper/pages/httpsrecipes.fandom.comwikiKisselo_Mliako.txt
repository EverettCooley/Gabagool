Ingredients : 
 2  courgettes  (cut in thin slices) 
 frying  oil 
 50 â€“ 80 g of plain white  flour 
 500 g natural live or Greek style  yoghurt 
 2  garlic  cloves (crushed) 
 chopped fresh  dill 
 Directions : 
 This dish is best prepared at least few hours in advance. 
 After cutting the courgettes into slices season with  salt  and leave aside for 10 minutes, then dip each slice into the  flour  and fry in a pan. 
 You can also use a deep-fat fryer but the courgettes may become a bit oily. 
 Leave to cool. 
 Mix the crushed  garlic  and chopped dill with the yoghurt. 
 In a dish arrange a layer yoghurt followed by a layer of fried courgettes. 
 Cool in a fridge for few hours or just serve.
