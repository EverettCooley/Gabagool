Relish refers to any savory-sweet  preserve  of vegetables or fruits-flavored with  vinegar ,  salt ,  sugar , and spices-prepared for use as a  condiment . The most common types are  pickle relishes , sometimes still referred to by the old-fashioned term  piccalilli . Sweet green pickle relishes tend to have a sweeter flavor than more tangy green pickle relishes.
