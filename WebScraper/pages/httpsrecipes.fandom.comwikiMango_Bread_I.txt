Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 2 loaves (7⅜ by 2¼) or 1 large loaf 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  flour 
 2 tsp  baking soda 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 ¾ to 1 cup  sugar 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 ½ cup  raisins 
 ¼ cup  walnuts 
 ¾ cup  vegetable oil 
 3  eggs 
 2 cups chopped ripe  mangoes 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Combine dry ingredients in a large bowl. 
 Make a well and add remaining ingredients. 
 Mix. 
 Pour into greased and floured loaf pans. 
 Bake at 325°F degrees for 1 hour.
