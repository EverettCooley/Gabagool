 Prep Time: 3 minutes 
 Cook time:  1 minute 
 Serves:  1 
 
 Description : 
 This is a great meal to make when you have no time and you're cooking for 1-2 people.  It's bachelor food at its best!
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 flour  tortilla 
 1 scoop  cottage cheese 
 ½ sliced  avocado 
 ½ sliced  tomato 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Slice up the Avocado and Tomato 
 Place the tortilla in a fry pan on the stove and warm up both sides 
 Add the Avocado and Tomato 
 Sprinkle salt and pepper to taste 
 Fold it up and fry both sides 
 Take the wrap off the stove, open it up and add a dollop of cottage cheese 
 Serve and enjoy!
