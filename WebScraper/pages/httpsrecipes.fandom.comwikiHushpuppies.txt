Description : 
 Contributed by Sylvia at  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes about 40 
 Ingredients : 
 Dry ingredients : 
 2 cups white  self-rising cornmeal  mix 
 ½ cup  self-rising all purpose flour 
 2 tsp  black pepper 
 ½ tsp  garlic powder 
 Wet ingredients : 
 ⅓ cup  buttermilk 
 1 large  egg 
 6 tbsp  cooking oil 
 ⅓ cup finely chopped  sweet peppers 
 ⅓ cup  onions 
 Directions : 
 Mix the dry ingredients. 
 Then mix the wet ingredients. 
 Mix wet and dry ingredients together (adding extra milk if needed). 
 Drop about 1 heaping tbsp in to 350°F frying shortening and fry till golden brown. 
 As soon as I dip them out I salt them and cover with a dry cloth towel. 
 Note : 
 You can make them ahead of time. 
 Drop them on a cookie sheet with wax paper and freeze then put in to a zip lock bag. 
 It will take a little longer to cook.
