      Island Snapper Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 red snapper , broiled 
 cucumber , julienned 
 minced  shallot 
 Dressing : 
 olive oil 
 lemon juice 
 rice vinegar 
 fresh ginger  root, grated 
 black pepper 
 minced  cilantro 
 Directions : 
 Combine small pieces of broiled fresh red  snapper  with some julienned  cucumber  and minced shallots. 
 Add a snappy dressing made of  olive oil ,  lemon juice  and  rice vinegar , season with fresh grated  ginger ,  black pepper  and minced  cilantro . 
 To serve surround a serving with diced fresh  mango  and  papaya  tossed in the vinaigrette.
