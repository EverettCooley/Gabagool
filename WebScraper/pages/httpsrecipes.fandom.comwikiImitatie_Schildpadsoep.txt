Description : 
 This imitation turtle soup does not contain any meat. However, it is an imitation of  Schildpadsoep  (Turtle Soup) which does contain meat, but not turtle meat. It is therefore an imitation of an imitation.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1Â¼ liter  water  that was used to cook  black beans 
 45 g  butter 
 carrot 
 chopped  onion 
 parsley 
 celery 
 thyme 
 bay leaf 
 4  peppercorns 
 45 g  flour 
 soy sauce 
 Directions : 
 Add all the herbs to the bean  water  and leave to simmer for about 20 minutes. 
 In the  butter , add the chopped  onion ,  carrot ,  parsley  and  celery  and leave to simmer in the  butter  for about 10 minutes. 
 Add this to the bean  water . 
 Bring to the boil and add the  flour  to thicken the soup. 
 Leave to boil for another 10 minutes. 
 Put to the sieve and add soy to taste.
