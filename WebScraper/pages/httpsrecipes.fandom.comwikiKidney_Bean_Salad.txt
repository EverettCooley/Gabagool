 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  kidney beans 
 2  onions , chopped (or  chives ) 
 ⅓ cup  mayonnaise 
 ½  cucumber , sliced 
 ½ tsp strong  mustard 
 ¾ cup  celery  chopped 
 ½ tsp each  salt  and  pepper 
 4 hard boiled  eggs 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients except for  kidney beans  and hard boiled  eggs  with  mayonnaise . 
 Then when mixed well, add  beans  and  eggs . 
 Mix well. 
 Cover, chill overnight.
