 Ingredients : 
 6  eggs 
 2  onions 
 140 g of peeled  tomatoes 
 150 g of  tomato concentrate 
 300 g of bon gari . 
 Directions : 
 Gently fry the  tomatoes ,  onions , and the  tomato  concentrate in a little  oil 
 Add 2 beaten  eggs 
 Dampen the bongari with a half-glass of  water  while stirring vigorously 
 Pour the prepared sauce on the dampened  gari  and blend together 
 Make an omelet separately,
