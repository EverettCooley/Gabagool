Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 16 servings of fruit smoothie no bake cheesecake. 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups  graham cracker  crumbs 
 ¼ cup  butter , melted 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 4 pkgs (8 oz each)  neufchatel  cheese (I used  cream cheese ), softened 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 12 oz pkg frozen mixed  berries , thawed, drained 
 8 oz tub  Cool Whip , thawed 
 Directions : 
 Line 13x9 inch baking pan with foil, extending foil over sides of pan. 
 Mix graham crumbs, butter and 2 tbs sugar, press onto bottom of pan. 
 Refrigerate. 
 Beat cheese and ½ cup sugar in bowl until blended. 
 Smash berries, stir into cheese mixture. 
 Stir in 2 cups of Cool Whip. 
 Spoon over crust, cover. 
 Refrigerate 4 hours or until firm. 
 Use foil to remove cheesecake from pan before cutting into pieces to serve. 
 Top with remaining cool whip. 
 Garnish with whole berries and a mint sprig.
