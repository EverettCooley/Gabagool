Description : 
 Tired of the same ole boring fruit dips...This one is sure to tickle your taste buds. Go ahead and get tropical! 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup of chopped ripe mangoes (peeled and seeded) 
 1 cup kraft  marshmallow creme 
 1 (8 ounces) package  cream cheese 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Puree  mango  in a food processor. 
 Then add  marshmallow   cream  and  cream cheese  to the food processor and blend well. 
 Serve with different fruits for example:  pineapple ,  kiwi ,  oranges ,  strawberries ,  cantaloupe ,  grapes . 
 Enjoy!
