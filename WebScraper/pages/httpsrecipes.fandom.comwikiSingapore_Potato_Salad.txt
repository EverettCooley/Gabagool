      Singapore Potato Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 1 kg  potatoes 
 3  spring onions 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 6 tbsp  milk 
 2 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  pepper 
 handful of  mint  and  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Chop spring  onion  stems, and discard  onion  bulbs; wash and chop  parsley  and  mint . 
 Dilute  mayonnaise  with  milk  and mix well. 
 In a large saucepan, heat  water . 
 Boil  potatoes . 
 Remove from flame and let it cool. 
 Peel the skin and slice. 
 Transfer into a serving bowl while still warm. 
 Add  salt ,  pepper  and chopped spring  onions . 
 Mix well. 
 Add thinned  mayonnaise  and mix ensuring that each slice is coated with  mayonnaise . 
 Be careful not to break the  potato  slices. 
 Add half the  mint  and  parsley  and toss gently. 
 Garnish with the remaining half.
