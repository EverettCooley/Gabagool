Blt Salad
 
 1 1/2 Quarts Torn  lettuce 
 1/2 Cup  green onion  Slices 
 1 1/2 Cup Shredded Sharp  Cheddar   Cheese 
 1 1/2 cu  cherry tomato  Halves 
 1 Cup  Mayonnaise 
 6 Crisply Cooked  Bacon  Slices, Crumbled 
 
Mix  lettuce  And  onions . Toss Lightly. Chill. Add Remaining Ingredients. Mix Lightly And Serve at Once. Serves 6 to 8Source: my Old Recipes
