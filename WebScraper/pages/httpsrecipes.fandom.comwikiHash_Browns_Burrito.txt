Description : 
 Cooking Time:  10 minutes 
 Preparation Time:  5 minutes 
 Serves: 2 
 Per Serving
 
 Calories: 513 
 Fat: 30 grams 
 Carbohydrates (includes fiber): 53 grams 
 Protein: 7 grams 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons oil 
 1/4 cup onion, diced 
 1/4 cup bell pepper, diced 
 1 cup frozen hash browns 
 2 large tortillas 
 1/2 cup vegan cheddar cheese, grated 
 Ketchup, to serve 
 Hot sauce, to serve 
 Directions : 
 Heat a heavy cast iron pan (large enough for a tortilla) to medium-high. Add oil, onion, pepper and hash browns. Brown on 1 side, turn and brown the other. Slide mixture onto a plate. 
 Place the tortilla in the pan and spin it around in the rest of the oil for a few seconds then flip it over. 
 Sprinkle the cheese evenly over it add the potato mixture. 
 Add ketchup and hot sauce, to taste. 
 Slide onto a plate, and roll it up.
