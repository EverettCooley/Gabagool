 Description : 
 A low-fat but tasty version of a typically calorie-laden favorite.
 
 Contributed by Jenn B aka Mom2Sam and Tiny at  World Recipes Y-Group 
 Serves 6 
 Ingredients : 
 6 x 7-inch flour  tortillas  (made without  lard ) 
 6 ounces low-fat Monterey  jack cheese , planed 
 6 ounces  smoked turkey  slices (98% fat-free) 
 36  green grapes , halved lengthwise 
 fresh  cilantro  sprigs 
 ½ teaspoon ground  cumin 
 1 tablespoon fresh  lime juice 
 coarse salt 
 Directions : 
 Place tortillas on work surface. 
 Arrange  cheese ,  turkey ,  grapes  and  cilantro  over half of each tortilla. 
 Sprinkle with  cumin . 
 Fold tortilla over filling. 
 Preheat oven to 200°F. 
 Heat large nonstick skillet over medium heat. 
 Cook quesadillas 1 at a time until golden brown, about 3 minutes. Turn. 
 Brush cooked top with  lime juice  and sprinkle with  coarse salt . 
 Cook until golden brown, about 3 minutes. 
 Keep warm in oven. 
 Repeat with remaining quesadillas. 
 Serve. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per Serving: 
 
 Calories, 200 | Fat, 8g | Sodium, 606Mg | Cholesterol, 32Mg
