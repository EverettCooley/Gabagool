Description : 
 Use as a stuffing for roast  duck  or  pork  chops or as a side dish. Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup chopped  dried apricots 
 ½ cup boiling  chicken broth 
 2 cups sliced  celery 
 1 cup sliced  green onions 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 ½ cup chopped  pecans 
 1½ teaspoons  poultry seasoning 
 salt  and ground  black pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Soak  apricots  in broth; set aside. 
 Cook  celery  and  onions  in  butter  in large skillet until tender crisp. 
 Add  rice ,  pecans ,  poultry seasoning ,  salt ,  pepper ,  apricots  and broth; mix well. 
 If using as a side dish, bake in baking dish at 350 °F for 20 minutes.
