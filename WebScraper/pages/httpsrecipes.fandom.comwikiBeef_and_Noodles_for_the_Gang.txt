Beef  And Noodles For The Gang
 
 1 Pound Small  egg noodles 
 2 Pounds Lean Ground  Beef 
 2 Cans  tomato  Sauce (6 Oz.) 
 1 T.  worcestershire sauce 
 salt  to Taste
 
 1 Cup Cream Style  cottage cheese 
 1 Pkg.  cream cheese , Softened (8 Oz) 
 1 Cup  sour cream 
 1 Bunch  green onions , Chopped With Some of The Green Tops 
 2 T.  butter , Melted 
 
Cook Noodle,rinse Under Hot  water . Brown Meat Well. Add  tomato  Sauce, Worcestershire And  salt . Mix Together The  cottage cheese ,  cream cheese ,  sour cream  And  onions . Melt  butter . Grease Two 2 Quart Casseroles. Place 1/4 of The Noodles in Each, Pour a Little Melted  butter  Over The Noodles Land Add Half The  Cheese  Mixture to Each Casserole. Add Remaining Noodles, More  butter  And Top With Meat Mixture. Bake at 350 For 30 to 45 Minutes. Can be Frozen. Serves 8 to 10
