Description : 
 Pita bread,  California avocado ,  tuna , lots of veggies and melted  cheese  sandwich.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups of your favorite  tuna  salad 
 6 pieces of  pita  bread (sliced in half) 
 3  California avocados  mashed 
 mayo  to taste 
 1 to 2 carton(s)  alfalfa sprouts 
 3 large  tomatoes  (sliced) 
 ½ pound sliced fresh  mushrooms 
 3 cups grated  jack cheese 
 a few shakes of  spike  spice 
 Directions : 
 Spread pita bread with  mayo , then  avocado , and then  tuna  salad. 
 Top that with sprouts, a  tomato  slice or two, a handful of  mushrooms , cover with  cheese  and sprinkle with  spike . 
 Bake 375°F until  cheese  is melted (5–10 minutes).
