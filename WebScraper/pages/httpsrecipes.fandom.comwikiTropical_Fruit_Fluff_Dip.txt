Ingredients : 
 1 c.  sour cream 
 1/4 c. flaked  coconut 
 2 tbsp. chopped  walnuts 
 2 tbsp.  apricot preserves 
 Slices of fresh fruit, such as  bananas ,  apples ,  pears , etc. 
 Directions : 
 In bowl blend all ingredients except fruit slices. 
 Add small amount of milk to make desired consistency if needed. 
 Dip fruit in as dessert or appetizer.
