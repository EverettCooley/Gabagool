Ingredients : 
 1½ lbs ground  goat  meat 
 2 tsp  salt 
 2  eggs , slightly beaten 
 margarine 
 1¼ cups  beef bouillon  (mix 2 cubes with 2 cups  water ; take out 1¼ cups 
 1½ cups soft  bread crumbs 
 1  onion , chopped 
 2 tbsp  steak sauce , i.e. 2 tbsp  worcestershire , or A-1 
 ½ cup  sour cream 
 Directions : 
 Have the butcher grind the meat or you can purchase a hand grinder. 
 Combine meat, crumbs,  salt ,  onion ,  steak  sauce and  eggs . 
 Mix lightly and shape into 1½ balls. 
 Brown meat balls in hot  margarine . 
 Add bouillon and simmer covered for 20 – 25 minutes. 
 Add ½ cup  sour cream  and blend lightly.
