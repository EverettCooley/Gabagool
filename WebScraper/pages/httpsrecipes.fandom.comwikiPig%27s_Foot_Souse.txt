Ingredients : 
 4 lbs  pig's feet , cut into small pieces 
 4 cloves  garlic , minced 
 ½ tsp  black pepper  chopped 
 hot pepper  to taste 
 1 large  onion , thinly sliced 
 juice of 2 or 3  limes 
 1 tsp  sugar 
 2 – 3 tsp  salt 
 2 large  cucumbers , peeled 
 sliced  watercress  or chopped  parsley  for garnishing 
 Directions : 
 Cook pig's feet in  water  and  salt  until tender, or pressure cook. 
 Drain well and rinse in cold  water . 
 Combine in a large bowl, 4 cups of  water ,  lime ,  sugar ,  black pepper ,  hot pepper  and  garlic . 
 Add  meat ,  cucumber  and  onion ; marinate for a few hours in the refrigerator. 
 Add  salt  and  pepper , garnish with watercress.
