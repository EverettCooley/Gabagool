Chicken   Mushroom  Sandwiches
 
 2 Cups Cooked  Chicken , Finely Chopped 
 2/3 Cup  almonds , Toasted, Finely Chopped 
 2 T. Instant Minced  Onion 
 2 Cans (3 Oz. Each)  Mushrooms  Drained And Chopped 
 1 Cup  celery , Diced 
 1/2 t 1 T.  salt 
 1/4 T.  pepper 
 1 T.  curry  Powder 
 1 Cup  Mayonnaise 
 Wheat   bread  Rounds, Light And Dark
 
Combine All Ingredients. Mix Well. Spread on  Wheat   bread  Rounds. Makes 3 to 4 Cups. Can do Ahead.Source: Appetizers, Southern Style
