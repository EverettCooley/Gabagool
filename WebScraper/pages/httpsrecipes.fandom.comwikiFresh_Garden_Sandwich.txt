 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ a  California avocado 
 2 slices of your favorite bread 
 4 thin slices of  mozzarella   Cheese 
 Sprouts 
 4 very thin slices of roma  tomato 
 4 very thin slices of  cucumber 
 salt  and  pepper 
 ½ tsp of  margarine  or  butter 
 Directions : 
 First toast your favorite bread and lightly  butter  or  margarine  them
 Slice half an  avocado  and place on one slice of bread
 Place four thin roma  tomato  slices (or two regular  tomato  slices) on top of the  avocado 
 salt  and  pepper  the  tomato  slices
 Add two slices of the  Cheese 
 Add the four thin slices of  cucumber 
 Add the sprouts
 Add with the last 2 slices of  Cheese 
 Top with the other slice of bread
