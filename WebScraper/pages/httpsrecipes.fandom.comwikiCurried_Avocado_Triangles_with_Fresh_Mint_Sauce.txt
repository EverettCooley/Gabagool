 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1  California avocado 
 1 tsp  curry  powder 
 Pinch  salt 
 24 wonton wraps (dough squares) 
 vegetable oil  as needed for deep frying 
 Fresh  mint  Sauce 
 Fresh  mint  Sauce 
 2 ½ Cups (lightly packed) fresh  mint  leaves 
 1 large  shallot , coarsely chopped 
 ¼ Cup  lime juice 
 3 Tbsp  lemon juice 
 3 Tbsp  water 
 1 Tbsp  Sugar 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Mash  avocado , stir in  curry  powder and  salt .
 Put 1 generous teaspoon  avocado  mixture in one corner of wrap.
 Brush edge with  water . Fold over in a triangle, press to seal. Repeat with remaining wraps.
 Deep fry 4 triangles at a time until golden, about 3 minutes, keep warm until all are cooked.
 Serve 6 per serving with 1/4 cup Fresh  mint  Sauce

 In a food processor or blender, puree all ingredients, reserve
