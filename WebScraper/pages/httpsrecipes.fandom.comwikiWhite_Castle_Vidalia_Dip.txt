Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 14  White Castle  hamburgers, chopped fine, buns set aside 
 2 cups chopped Vidalia  onions 
 1 cups  mayonnaise 
 2 cups shredded  Swiss cheese 
 1 cup crumbled  bacon 
 crushed  crackers  (topping) 
 Directions : 
 Mix all above ingredients (except buns and crackers). 
 Bake in casserole dish at 350 °F for 30-35 minutes. 
 Let stand for 10 minutes. 
 Put open buns under broiler until toasted. 
 Serve dip on buns or crackers.
