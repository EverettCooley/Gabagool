Description : 
 Fava bean soup
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups of  fava beans , washed 
 6 cups of  water 
 1 tap  cumin 
 2 cloves of  garlic , crushed 
 ¼ cup  olive oil 
 ¼ cup  lemon juice 
 2 tablespoon  parsley , finely chopped 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Soak the fava beans over night in the 6 cups of  water . 
 Drain the beans but reserve the  water  and set aside. 
 Remove the skins from the beans and put them in a saucepan. 
 Measure the reserved soaking  water  and add enough to make six cups. 
 Add this to the beans. 
 Bring to a boil. 
 Cover and lower heat to simmer 1 hour, until the beans are tender. 
 Puree the beans in a blender. 
 Return the bean puree to the saucepan, add remaining ingredients with exception of the  parsley . 
 Bring to a boil, lower heat and cook for another 5 minutes.
