 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  whole wheat flour 
 1 cup regular  flour 
 1 cup white  cornmeal 
 3 tbsp  olive oil 
 3  eggs  (beaten) 
 1/2 cup juice from boiling  Chicken  giblets 
 4-5 fresh  garlic  cloves 
 1 pound  Chicken  giblets and hearts (or  Chicken  liver) 
 Directions : 
 Place  olive oil , fresh  eggs , chicken stock , fresh peeled  garlic  cloves, and boiled  Chicken  gizzards in the blender. 
 Blend until well mix, a little chunky not quite smooth. 
 Then mix in the flours and  cornmeal . Place on greased cookie sheets. 
 Bake 20 minutes in a 450 degree oven. Store in the fridge or freeze if you will not use in over four days. 
 They even like them straight out the freezer! 
 The  garlic  is good for their heart and is a natural flea deterrent. 
 The  olive oil  and  eggs  are good for a healthy coat.
