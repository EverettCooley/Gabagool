 Description : 
 A tangy combination of authentic cheese and garlic powder is made with pecans and parsley and is great on crackers, pretzels, meat, and poultry, as it delivers a sharper aroma that it relishes then offers to the cheese and nuts.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 ¾ lb. fresh cheese (Mozzarella, Cheddar, or Swiss) 
 1 lb.  processed cheese  (Colby, American, or Monterey Jack) 
 3 oz.  cream cheese , softened 
 1 ½ cup finely chopped  pecans 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 3 tablespoons  garlic powder 
 5 cloves garlic, minced, optional 
 chopped, fresh  parsley 
 1 teaspoon chili powder 
 Directions : 
 Shred the cheese, minced garlic (optional), and  processed cheese  into a fresh, large bowl. Mix with  cream cheese . Add pecans,  salt , and  garlic powder  then stir. 
 Divide into three mounds. Roll into a log about 2 inches in diameter. 
 Roll each log with  chili powder ,  parsley , and  pecans . Refrigerate or freeze several hours before serving. 
 Slice 2 inches thick and serve with crackers, if desired.
