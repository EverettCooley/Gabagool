 Description : 
 Pineapple juice and light rum are mixed with club soda for extra powerful taste. Lime or lemon zest sometimes works for this for garnishing, too.
 
         
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz.  light rum 
 1 oz.  pineapple juice 
 1 teaspoon pineapple syrup, optional 
 ½ tsp.  powdered sugar  or white sugar 
 club soda 
 ice 
 sliced lemon or lime zest, optional 
 Directions : 
 Pour pineapple juice, shake with  ice , and strain into glass ¼ full of  ice cubes . 
 Fill with  club soda , pineapple syrup (optional), and light rum and stir gently. Pour into beer mugs or other glasses. 
 Serve cold and plain or with other fruits or appetizers. Top with the lemon zest and/or pineapple syrup, if desired.
