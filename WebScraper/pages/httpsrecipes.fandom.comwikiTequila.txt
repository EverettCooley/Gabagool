 About tequila : 
 Wikipedia Article  About Tequila on Wikipedia 
 Tequila is a strong, distilled, alcoholic beverage made primarily in the area surrounding Tequila, a town in the western Mexican state of Jalisco, 50 km from Guadalajara. It is made from the agave plant (also called Maguey), similar to the lily, which is native to Mexico.
