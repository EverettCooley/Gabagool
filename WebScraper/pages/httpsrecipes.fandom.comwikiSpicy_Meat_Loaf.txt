 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 Tbsp.  olive oil 
 1 sweet  Onion , chopped 
 4 cloves  garlic , minced 
 3 pounds lean ground  Beef 
 3 tsp.  salt 
 3 tsp. fresh ground  pepper 
 2  eggs , beaten 
 1 cup V-8 vegetable juice 
 3/4 cup fresh chopped  parsley 
 2 Tbsp. hot sauce 
 1/3 cup  ketchup 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Heat  oil  in pan and saute  Onion  for about 3 minutes. 
 Add  garlic  and saute for 1 minute. Put  Onion  and  garlic  in large mixing bowl and add ground  Beef ,  salt  and  pepper ,  eggs , vegetable juice,  parsley  and hot sauce. 
 Mix well and mold mixture into a greased loaf pan (about 12 x 8Â inches). Top with  ketchup  and bake for one hour.
