Description : 
 Purchased from the Colvin Estate in Little Rock, Arkansas in 1989. Dated 1934. This recipe indicates this was a Blue Ribbon Winner in the Arkansas State Bake-off in 1949.
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup firmly packed  brown sugar 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 ½ cup  margarine  or  butter , softened 
 ½ cup  shortening 
 1½ tsp  vanilla 
 1  egg 
 1¾ cups  all purpose flour 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 1 cup  semi-sweet chocolate  chips 
 ½ cup chopped  nuts  or shelled 
 sunflower seeds  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 375°F. 
 In large bowl, combine  brown sugar ,  sugar ,  margarine  and  shortening ; beat until light and fluffy. 
 Add  vanilla  and  egg ; blend well. 
 Stir in  flour ,  baking soda , and  salt ; mix well. 
 Stir in  chocolate chips  and  nuts . 
 Spread in ungreased 13 x 9-inch pan. 
 Bake for 15 to 25 minutes or until light golden brown. 
 Cool completely. 
 Cut into bars.
