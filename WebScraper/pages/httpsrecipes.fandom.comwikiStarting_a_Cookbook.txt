 Individual users can collect and display all their recipes on their user page.
 
 Creating Your Cookbook 
 Create an account . 
 On your user page, put {{Cookbook}}, squiggles and all. (Your user page can be accessed by clicking "User: Username " over at the top right anytime you are logged in.) 
 Add recipes .  Whenever you write a recipe, be sure you're signed in.
