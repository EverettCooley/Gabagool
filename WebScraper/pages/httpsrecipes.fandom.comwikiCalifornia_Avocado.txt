 Description : 
 These golden brown beer-battered fritters make a quick and easy hors d'oeuvre. Guests always remark that they've never had cooked avocados and how good they taste.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 Cup  flour 
 1  egg 
 1 can  beer 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 jalape√±o, finely minced 
 2 avocados, diced 
 1 Pound small raw  Shrimp , cleaned & peeled 
 Directions : 
 Mix first 5 ingredients to form batter. 
 Fold in  avocado  and  Shrimp . 
 Drop heaping tablespoons of mixture into hot  oil . 
 Fry approximately 2 minutes until golden brown. 
 Drain and serve hot.
