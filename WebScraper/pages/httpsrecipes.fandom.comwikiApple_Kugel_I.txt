 Description : 
 "Kugel" is a noodle dish that adapts very well to apples. If making for the lunchbox, prepare the day before and refrigerate. Cut into serving-size squares.
 
 Serves 6 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups uncooked  egg noodles 
 8 ounces reduced-fat  cream cheese 
 ½ cup  brown sugar 
 1 pound low-fat  cottage cheese 
 1  egg 
 2  egg whites 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 ½ teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  nutmeg 
 2  Rome Beauty apples 
 Directions : 
 Cook egg noodles according to package directions until just tender; drain. 
 Meanwhile, in large bowl with electric mixer, beat cream cheese and brown sugar until well blended. 
 Add cottage cheese, egg, egg whites, vanilla, cinnamon and nutmeg, beating until combined. 
 Heat oven to 350 °F. 
 Lightly grease 8-or 9-inch square pan. 
 Core, quarter and thinly slice apples; stir apple slices and cooked noodles into cheese mixture. 
 Spread mixture in pan; bake 45 minutes or until kugel is golden and firm. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 Protein: 20g | Fat: 13g | Carbohydrate: 57g | Fiber: 4g | Sodium: 458mg | Cholesterol: 103mg | Calories: 429
