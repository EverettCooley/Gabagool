Description : 
 Humble cabbage becomes coleslaw when mixed with salad dressing and other tasty ingredients.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  salad dressing  ( Miracle Whip  for example) 
 1 tablespoon  vinegar 
 1 tablespoon  sugar 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 dash  pepper 
 2½ cups shredded  cabbage 
 ¼ cup chopped  bell pepper 
 Steps : 
 Combine the salad dressing, vinegar, sugar, salt and pepper. 
 Chill. 
 Add cabbage and bell pepper to a salad bowl. 
 Add chilled salad dressing mixture. 
 Stir dressing lightly into cabbage mixture. 
 Makes about 2 cups of coleslaw. 
 Things You'll Need : 
 Measuring cups and spoons 
 Mixing bowls 
 Knife 
 Cutting board
