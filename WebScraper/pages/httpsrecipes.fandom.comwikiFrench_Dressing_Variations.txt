Ingredients : 
 ½ cup low-calorie  French dressing 
 Avocado French dressing : 
 2 tbsp  avocado , mashed 
 Cheese French dressing : 
 2 tbsp  blue cheese , mashed  or  2 tbsp  roquefort  cheese, mashed 
 Egg French dressing : 
 1 hard-cooked  egg yolk  crumbled and combined with a dash  hot pepper sauce 
 Lemon French dressing : 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 Soy sauce French dressing : 
 1 tbsp  soy sauce 
 Directions : 
 Start with ½ cup low-calorie French dressing. 
 Add the appropriate extras to get the variation you want. 
 Allow to mellow for several hours. 
 Nutritional information : 
 ½ cup = 100 calories
 
 Avocado dressing: 1 fat exchange 
 Cheese dressing: ¼ meat exchange 
 Egg dressing: 1 meat exchange 
 Lemon dressing: free 
 Soy sauce dressing: free (I used  salt -free)
