Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Preparation Time: 1:00 
 Yield: 4 – 6 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 5 medium  apples , peeled and sliced 
 ½ cup  walnuts , chopped 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 ¼ tsp ground  cloves 
 Topping : 
 3 tsp  margarine , non-dairy, softened 
 ¼ cup  flour , whole-grain wheat 
 3 tsp  brown sugar 
 4 tsp  wheat germ 
 ½ tsp  cinnamon 
 ½ tsp  nutmeg 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Combine the first four ingredients in a mixing bowl, and mix together until the spices coat the apples more or less evenly. 
 Grease an 8 x 8-inch or 9 x 9-inch baking pan and arrange the apples in it. 
 Cut the margarine into thin slices and place in a mixing bowl. 
 Add the remaining topping ingredients and cream together with a fork until a crumbly mixture is formed. 
 Sprinkle evenly over the apples and bake for 50 to 60 minutes, or until the apples are done and the crust is browned.
