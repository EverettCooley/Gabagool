Ingredients : 
 ½ kg sliced peeled  potatoes 
 2 small  onions 
 1 teaspoon  garlic  (finely chopped) 
 ½ cup  cream 
 1 table spoon chopped  chives 
 Directions : 
 1.	In an oven dish make a layer of sliced potatoes.
 2.	Now add on it chopped  Onion ,and  garlic .
 3.	And add  cream  to it. Now make another layer of potatoes on it.
 4.	Now cover it with a piece of foil and bake for almost an hour, then topped with grated  Cheese  and grilled until golden brown.
 5.	The  Potato  gratin is accompanied by various 'raw salads'.
