 Wikipedia Article  About Cachucha pepper on Wikipedia 
 Ají dulce (from South American Spanish ají, "chilli" + Spanish dulce, "sweet") is any of a variety of sweet peppers found in Latin America and the Caribbean. It is most widely known in Venezuela where it refers to a specific native variety of Capsicum chinense related to the habañero, but with a much milder, smoky flavour.
