Ingredients : 
 4 Servings 
 3 Tablespoons  olive oil  1 Cup  long-grain rice 
 2 Cups Low Sodium  chicken broth  or  water 
 1 Tablespoon  butter 
 salt  And  pepper , to Taste*
 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in Pressure Cooker. Stir in  rice , And Then Add Broth or  water .
 
Close Lid, Bring up to High Pressure, Then Lower Heat And Cook 8Minutes. Release Pressure, Open Lid, Add  butter , Plus  salt  And PepperTo Taste. Mix Thoroughly. Serve at Once While Hot.
