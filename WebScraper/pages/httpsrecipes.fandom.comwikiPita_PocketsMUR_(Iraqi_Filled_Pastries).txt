 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 SAUCE
 
 1/2	cup	Canned chick  Peas , drained 
 1/4	cup	 water 
 1	tbl	 sesame seeds 
 1	x	clove  garlic 
 1/2	tsp	 hot pepper  Sauce 
 FILLING
 
 1/2	cup	Chopped  cucumber 
 1/2	cup	 yellow squash , julienned 
 1/4	cup	Sliced  radishes 
 1/4	cup	Sliced  green onions 
 1	x	Pita breads (6Â inch) 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Sauce: Mix all ingredients in a blender until smooth. 
 Mix the vegetables together in a small bowl. 
 Cut pita breads in half to form pockets. 
 Spoon vegetable mixture into each pocket. Top with chick pea sauce.
