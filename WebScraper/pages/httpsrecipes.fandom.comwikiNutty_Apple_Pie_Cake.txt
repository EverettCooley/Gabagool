Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup  sugar 
 ⅓ cup soft  butter 
 1  egg 
 1 cup  flour 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 1 tsp  nutmeg 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 ¼ cup warm  water 
 2 cups pared diced  apples 
 ½ cup chopped  nuts 
 Directions : 
 Cream sugar and butter. 
 Add egg and beat until fluffy. 
 Mix baking soda and warm water. 
 Add to creamed mixture the flour, cinnamon, nutmeg and baking soda. 
 Beat until smooth and fluffy. 
 Fold in apples and nuts. 
 Put in well greased 9 inch pie pan. 
 Bake at 350°F degrees for 35 – 40 minutes.
