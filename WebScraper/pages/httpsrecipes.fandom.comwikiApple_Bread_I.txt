Ingredients : 
 1 pound recipe (8 slices) : 
 1 tsp  yeast 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 1 tbsp nonfat dry  milk 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 ¾ tsp  nutmeg 
 2½ cup  bread flour 
 2 tbsp lite  applesauce 
 ⅔ cup  water 
 ½ cup  dried apples , chopped 
 1½ pound recipe (12 slices) : 
 1½ tsp  yeast 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 2 tbsp nonfat dry  milk 
 2 tsp  cinnamon 
 1 tsp  nutmeg 
 3⅓ cup  bread flour 
 3 tbsp lite  applesauce 
 1 cup  water 
 ¾ cup  dried apples , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Add ingredients in the order suggested by the  bread  machine manufacturer and follow baking instructions provided in the manual.
