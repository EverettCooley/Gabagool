       Serbian Garlic Sauce     
 Ingredients : 
 6 cloves of  garlic 
 1 tbsp  olive oil 
 1 pt.  mayonnaise  with starch 
 A pinch of  white pepper 
 A few drops of  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Sauté the  garlic  in the  olive oil  until golden brown; remove and cool. Add  garlic  to  mayonnaise  and purée in a blender. 
 Season with  pepper  and  lemon juice
