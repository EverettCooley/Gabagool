Ingredients : 
 ⅓ cup  honey 
 ⅓ cup  brown sugar 
 ½ cup  butter   or   margarine 
 ½ tsp  vanilla 
 ¼ tsp  almond extract 
 2  eggs 
 ½ cup  currants   or   raisins 
 ⅓ cup  golden raisins 
 ½ cup  almonds , chopped 
 2½ cups  whole wheat flour 
 2 tsp  baking powder 
 1 tsp ground  cardamom 
 ¼ tsp ground  cloves 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Cream together  butter  or  margarine , honey, and  brown sugar . 
 Add  eggs  and  extracts ; mix well. 
 Stir in  currants ,  raisins , and  almonds . 
 Add dry ingredients; mix well. 
 Drop by tablespoon onto greased cookie sheets. 
 Bake 15 minutes.
