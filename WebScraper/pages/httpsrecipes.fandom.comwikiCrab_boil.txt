Crab boil usually refers to a spice mixture that is used to flavor the water in which crabs or other shellfish are boiled. Homemade crab boil recipes call for abundant amounts of  hot sauce ,  cayenne pepper ,  salt ,  bay leaf ,  lemon , and  garlic .  Mustard seeds ,  coriander  seeds, and  allspice  are popular extra options. Many people will start with a commercial crab boil product and then supplement it with extra pepper. The leading commercial product is  Zatarain's  which comes in two forms.
