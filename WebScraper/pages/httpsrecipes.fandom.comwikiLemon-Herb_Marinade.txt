 Description : 
 Try this marinade with meat,  poultry , and  fish . This fruit-based barbecue sauce is made sour with the real lemons and used for grilling and basting.
 
         
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 From the Recipe Ross Newsletter 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  lemon juice 
 1 ¾ teaspoon lemon zest, optional 
 ¼ cup  olive oil 
 2 tbsp. finely chopped  parsley 
 ½ tsp.  salt 
 ½ tsp. ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Combine ingredients in a shallow dish. 
 Use as a barbecue sauce and for grilling and/or basting. Reserve the fruit and herb-based marinade in the refrigerator. 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Servings:  4 teaspoons or 3 tablespoons
