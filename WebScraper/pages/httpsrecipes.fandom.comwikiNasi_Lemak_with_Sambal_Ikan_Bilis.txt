 Description : 
 This is a typical Malay breakfast dish.  coconut   rice  is best served at room temperature when the fragrance of the  coconut   rice  can be best appreciated. Many different dishes maybe served with it but the most crucial is the chillie sauce. Deep Fried  turmeric  goes very well with it.
 
       Nasi Lemak with Sambal Ikan Bilis     
 Ingredients : 
 For  rice : 
 
 coconut milk 
 pandan leaves 
 salt 
 For Sambal Ikan Bilis: 
 
 Peeled ikan bilis ( Anchovies ) 
 onions 
 dried chillies 
 Sugar 
 salt 
 tamarind 
 tomatoes 
 Directions : 
 Cook  rice  with  coconut milk  with a bunch of  pandan leaves  and a pinch of  salt . Leave to cool. 
 To make chillie paste, remove seeds from dried chillies, soak in hot  water  and grrind still smooth, add  onions . Fry mixture in hot  oil  till  oil  seeps through, season with  sea salt  and  Sugar . Put aside. 
 Fry chopped  onions  in hot  oil  till soft, add  Anchovies  and fry till fragrant. Add  tamarind juice ,  tomatoes , some of the chllie paste and season with  salt  and  Sugar . 
 Serve the  rice  cold sambal ikan bilis,  cucumber , scrambled  eggs  and chillie paste.
