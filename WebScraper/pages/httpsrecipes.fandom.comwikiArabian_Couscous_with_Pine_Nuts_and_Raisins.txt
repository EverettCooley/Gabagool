       Arabian Couscous with Pine Nuts and Raisins     
 Pine nuts  are among the more notable supposed aphrodisiacs, and this is a delicious way to showcase them.
To make it even more sensual, as well as colorful, add some diced  avocado . This is very tasty dish that comes together very quickly.
 
 Serves 3. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tbsp  margarine 
 ½ small  onion  minced 
 ½ medium  carrot  diced 
 1 cup  chicken stock 
 ¼ cup dark  raisins  plumped in hot  water  5 – 10 minutes 
 ½ cup  couscous 
 2 tbsp  pine nuts  toasted 
 juice of ½  lemon 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Heat  butter  in a saucepan. 
 Add  onion  and  carrot ; saute until  onions  are translucent, about 5 minutes. 
 Add stock and  raisins , raise heat to high and bring liquid to a boil. 
 Add  couscous , stir briefly, cover and remove from heat. 
 Let stand 5 minutes. 
 Stir in  pine nuts  and  lemon juice . 
 Season with  salt  and  pepper .
