Description : 
 Source: my adaptations from the cookbook that came with the machine
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  apple juice 
 ¾ cup  water 
 2 tbsp  honey 
 3 tbsp  orange juice  concentrate 
 1½ tbsp  salt 
 2½ cups  whole wheat flour 
 ¼ cup wheat  gluten 
 ¾ cup  quick oats 
 3 tbsp  yeast 
 Directions : 
 Mix in the order recommended by your breadmaker. 
 This uses the fresh-milk white/wheat setting on the Black and Decker.
