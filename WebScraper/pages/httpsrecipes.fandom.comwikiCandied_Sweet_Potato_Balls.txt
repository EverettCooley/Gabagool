 Ingredients : 
 4 – 6 large  sweet potatoes 
 1 oz.  butter  ( salt )  margarine 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 ¼ cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Boil  sweet potatoes  in salted  water . 
 Cool. 
 Scoop the cooked  potatoes  into balls; arrange in a greased shallow baking dish so that there will be only one layer. 
 Make syrup with the  sugar  and  water . 
 Pour the syrup over the  potatoes .
