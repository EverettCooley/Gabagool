 Description : 
 Singkamas is the filipino term for  jicama . It's a white succulent fleshy root crop, usually a good summer snack. It can also be made to a very simple refreshing salad.
 
       Singkamas     
 Ingredients : 
 singkamas ( jicama ) 
 white vinegar 
 salt  (asin) 
 Directions : 
 Peel  jicama . Slice peeled  jicama  into thin slices. Add the  vinegar  and sprinkle with  salt . Refrigerate and serve cold.
