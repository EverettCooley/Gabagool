Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Gibson Estate in Quinlan, Texas in 1981.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 4 ounces  cream cheese  softened 
 ⅓ cup fresh  dill  chopped 
 2 tablespoons  green onion  minced 
 ¼ teaspoon  lemon rind  grated 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 1 tablespoon prepared  horseradish 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 In food processor combine sour cream, cream cheese, dill, green onion, lemon rind, lemon juice and horseradish and blend until smooth. 
 Stir in salt and pepper.
