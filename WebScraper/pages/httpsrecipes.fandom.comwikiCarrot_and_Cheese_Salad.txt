Description : 
 Serves: 4 – 6
 
 Ingredients : 
 4  carrots , washed, peeled and grated 
 2 cups  cheddar  or  gruyère  cheese, grated 
 1 cup  sultanas 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together and season with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Refrigerate until served.
