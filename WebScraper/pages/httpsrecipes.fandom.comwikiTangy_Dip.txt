 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 Onion  - 1 medium 
 garlic  - 3-4 flakes 
 Mayonnaise  - 2 tbsp 
 Cheese  cubes - 6 
 Directions : 
 Blend all the ingredients in a mixie. 
 Add a little  yogurt  if the dip is too thick. 
 Chill in the refrigerator. 
 Serve with deep fried pieces of pitta bread. 
 (Take the regular arabic flat bread and cut it in a bit size pieces. Deep fry it and drain on kitchen napkin.)
