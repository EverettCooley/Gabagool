Description 
 A River of Recipes: Native American Recipes Using Commodity Foods  by the USDA Food Distribution Program on Indian Reservations, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Ingredients 
 1½ cups  whole wheat flour 
 2½ cups  all-purpose flour 
 ½ cup  instant nonfat dry milk 
 1 tablespoon  baking powder 
 ½ cup  vegetable oil 
 1¼ cup warm  water 
 Directions 
 Combine flours, nonfat dry milk powder, and baking powder in mixing bowl. 
 Add vegetable oil and mix well with fingertips. 
 Add water slowly to mixture using one hand for mixing to work into dough. 
 Divide dough into 10 medium sized balls and lay out on a clean cloth. Roll out each ball until round, flat and thin. Cook on moderately hot griddle, turning each side until lightly browned.
