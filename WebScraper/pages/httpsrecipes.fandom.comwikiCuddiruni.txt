      Cuddiruni     
 
 
 Description : 
 Sicilian cuddiruni or cudduruni pizza, Are stuffed with onions (or sometimes other vegetables such as potatoes or broccoli), anchovies, olives, cheese, mortadella: the rolled pizza dough is folded in two over the stuffing and the edge is braided, prior to frying.
