Apple  Crumb Cake (with  cinnamon )
 Makes: 12 Servings
 
Crumb Topping
 
 3/4 Cup  flour 
 1/3 Cup Graham Cracker Crumbs 
 1/4 Cup  brown sugar 
 1/3 Cup  butter 
 Cake
 
 1 1/2 Sticks  butter 
 1 1/4 Cups  brown sugar 
 2 Tsp  vanilla 
 1 1/2 Tsp  cinnamon 
 1 Tsp Each  baking powder  And  baking soda 
 1/2 Tsp  salt 
 3  eggs 
 2 1/4 Cups  flour 
 1 1/2 Cups  sour cream 
 3  Golden Delicious apples , Thinly Sliced 
 
Heat Oven to 350 Degrees. Line a Baking Pan With Foil. Topping: Mix Cracker Crumbs,  flour  And  sugar  in a Bowl. Add  butter  And Rub in With Fingers Until Crumbs Form. Cake: in Bowl, Beat First 7 Ingredients Until Creamy. Beat in  eggs  Until Blended. Add  flour  Alternately With  sour cream , Beating Until Smooth. Spoon 1/2 Into Pan, Cover With 1/2 The  Apples , The Remaining Batter, Then Remaining  Apples . Crumble Topping Over  Apples . Bake 45 minutes. or Until Done. Cool in Pan. Lift Cake From Pan. Cut in Squares. Serve Warm or Cool. Dust With Confectioners  sugar .*
