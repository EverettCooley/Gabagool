Baked Creamed  potatoes 
 Yield: 6 Servings
 
 4 c  potatoes , Diced 
 salt  And  pepper 
 
 1 t Fresh  dill , Minced 
 1 c  heavy cream 
 1 c  milk 
 
Good With Fish.
 Place  potatoes  in Shallow Baking Dish; Sprinkle With  salt ,  pepper , AndDill. Combine Cream And  milk ; Pour Over  potatoes . Bake 350 f, 30 Mins.
 
Enjoy.
