 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 5 cl Malibu 
 5 cl Baileys 
 3 drops of  Grenadine  Syrup 
 2 El fine Cocos powder 
 13 cl  pineapple juice 
 and pushed  ice 
 Directions : 
 All liqueurs, the  pineapple juice , a couple of  ice  cubes and the 2 soup-spoon fine Cocos powder into a blender and shortly foamy stirring. 
 Pouring into a glass and decorating with a  cherry  and a  pineapple  corner.
