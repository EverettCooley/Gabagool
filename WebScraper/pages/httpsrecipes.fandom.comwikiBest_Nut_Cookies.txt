Ingredients : 
 4  eggs 
 1 cup  Sugar 
 11/2 cups  pastry flour 
 1 cup chopped  walnuts 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1/2 teaspoon  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 First put together like Superior Nut Cake, and put on an oiled and floured pan with a pastry bag or a spoon. 
 Separate the whites from the yolks of the  eggs , beat the yolks stiff. 
 Beat in the  Sugar , a little at a time, and beat till very stiff. 
 Beat in the  vanilla . 
 Add the  salt  to the whites, and beat till stiff and dry. 
 Pour the beaten yolk mixture onto the whites; sift over it some  flour , and sprinkle over it some  nuts , and fold them in. 
 Sift on more  flour  and sprinkle on more  nuts , continuing thus till all the  flour  and  nuts  are folded in.
