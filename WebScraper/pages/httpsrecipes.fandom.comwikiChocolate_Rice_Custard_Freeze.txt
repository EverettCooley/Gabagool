 Description : 
 Makes 8 servings. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 cups cooked  rice 
 1 cup  Sugar 
 4 4-ounce squares  unsweetened chocolate , melted 
 1/8 teaspoon  salt 
 3 cups  milk 
 2  eggs , beaten 
 2 cups  heavy cream 
 1 tablespoon  vanilla extract 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  Sugar ,  chocolate ,  salt ,  milk  and  eggs  in large saucepan. 
 Cook over low heat until mixture just begins to bubble, stirring constantly; cool. Add  cream  and  vanilla . 
 Prepare  ice   cream  freezer according to manufacturer's directions. 
 Pour mixture into freezer can; freeze as directed.
