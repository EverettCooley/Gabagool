Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 6/10/07
 
 Contributed by Dorie at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  egg noodles 
 1 lb ground  sausage  cooked 
 1 can  cream of chicken  soup 
 2 cups shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 1 can diced  tomatoes 
 ½ cup  salsa 
 1 can  mushrooms 
 salt  and  pepper 
 garlic salt  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Cook noodles and set aside. 
 Brown sausage and set aside. 
 Combine soups and 1 cup cheese. 
 Stir in noodles and sausage, tomatoes, and salsa, mixing well. 
 Pour into 9x13 baking dish. 
 Top with 1 cup cheese. 
 Bake at 450°F for 20 minutes or until browned.
