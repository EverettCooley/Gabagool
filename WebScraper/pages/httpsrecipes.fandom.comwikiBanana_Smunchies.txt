Ingredients : 
 2 cups  peanut butter 
 2 cups ripe  bananas 
 52  graham cracker  squares 
 Directions : 
 My suggestion: mash one  banana  into a measuring cup. 
 Measure an equal amount of  peanut butter . 
 Mash very ripe  bananas  into a smooth paste. 
 Mix in  peanut butter . 
 Chill well. 
 Drop 2 tbsp onto one square graham cracker, cover with second graham cracker square. 
 Freeze until ready to use. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving: 
 
 188 calories | 20g carbohydrate | 7g protein | 11g fat | 118mg sodium 
 Exchanges: 1 starch | 1 medium-fat meat | 1 fat
