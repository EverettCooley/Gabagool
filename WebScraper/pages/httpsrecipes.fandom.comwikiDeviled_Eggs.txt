 Makes 16 halves 
 Ingredients : 
 8 hard boiled  eggs 
 3 tbsp  mayonnaise 
 2 tsp sweet  pickle  juice 
 Â¼ cup finely chopped  ham

 Directions : 
 Slice eggs in half lengthwise; remove yolks and mash. 
 Stir in remaining ingredients. 
 Fill whites with mixture using a spoon or pastry tube. 
 Cover and chill until ready to serve.
