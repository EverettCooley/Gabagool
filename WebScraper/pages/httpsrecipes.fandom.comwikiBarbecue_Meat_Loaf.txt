Description : 
 We had this for dinner last night—from cooking light—and thought it was wonderful. Next time I might add a touch of  cayenne  :) for a little extra zing.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Yield: 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ pounds  ground beef , extra lean 
 ½ cup dry  breadcrumbs  (I only had Progresso Italian and used them) 
 ⅓ cup your favorite  barbecue sauce , divided 
 1 tablespoon  prepared mustard 
 1½ teaspoons  chili powder 
 1 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 ½ teaspoon  salt  (I left that out—there's enough in the  bread crumbs , BBQ sauce for us) 
 ½ teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 2 large  egg whites  (I used 1 whole  egg ) 
 cooking spray 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Combine  beef , breadcrumbs, and  onion , 1 tablespoon barbecue sauce, and remaining ingredients except  cooking spray  in a large bowl. 
 Shape meat mixture into a loaf shape on a broiler pan coated with  cooking spray . 
 Spread remaining barbecue sauce over top of meat loaf. 
 Bake for 1 hour or until a thermometer registers 160°. 
 Let stand 10 minutes. 
 Slice.
