 Description : 
 Makes 12 tablespoons 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  lemon juice 
 ¼ cup  cider vinegar 
 ¼ cup unsweetened  apple juice 
 ½ tbsp  oregano 
 ½ tbsp  dry mustard 
 ½ tbsp  onion powder 
 ½ tbsp  basil 
 ⅛ tbsp  thyme 
 ⅛ tbsp  rosemary 
 chopped  garlic  cloves to taste 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients. 
 Chill for at least an hour or two to allow herbs to blend. 
 Remove garlic clove pieces before serving. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving (2 tablespoons):
 
 Calories: 9 | fat: 0 gm, 0% fat
