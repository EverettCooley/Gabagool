Description : 
 Sangria made with white wine
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: With Love From Darling's Kitchen 
 Makes about 1Â½ quarts or 12 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 bottle dry  white wine  ( chablis ,  chenin blanc ) 
 1 cup  ginger ale 
 1 cup  orange juice 
 2 tbsp  lemon juice 
 2 tbsp  orange liqueur 
 peach  slices,  orange  slices,  strawberries 
 Directions : 
 In a large pitcher or punch bowl, stir together first 5 ingredients. 
 Float fresh fruit on top and refrigerate for several hours. 
 About 10 minutes before serving, chill with about 16  ice  cubes.
