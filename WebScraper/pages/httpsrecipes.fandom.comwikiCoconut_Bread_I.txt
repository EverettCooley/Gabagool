      Coconut Bread     
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups  flour 
 ⅓ cup  sugar 
 2 tsp  baking powder 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 1  egg , beaten 
 1 cup  coconut milk 
 ½ cup  margarine , melted 
 1 tsp  vanilla extract 
 2 cups unsweetened shredded  coconut 
 1 cup  raisins , optional 
 Directions : 
 Sift dry ingredients into a mixing bowl. 
 Blend in  egg ,  milk ,  margarine  &  vanilla . 
 Mix in  coconut  &  Raisins  if used. 
 Knead very lightly. 
 Shape into 2 loaves & pat into 2 oiled medium loaf pans. 
 Bake at 350f for 40–45 minutes.
