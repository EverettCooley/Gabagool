Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups (8-ounces)  firm tofu , drained and finely chopped 
 ½ cup chopped  celery 
 ½ cup sliced  radishes 
 ⅓ cup sliced  green onions 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 2 tablespoons diet  margarine 
 2 cups cooked  rice 
 1¼ cups (8-ounces) grated  Cheddar  cheese, divided 
 1 teaspoon  salt  (optional) 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Cook  tofu ,  celery ,  radishes ,  onion  and  garlic  in  margarine  in large skillet until vegetables are tender crisp. 
 Add  rice  1 cup cheese,  salt  and  pepper . Cover; cook over medium heat 10 minutes or until heated through, stirring occasionally. 
 Sprinkle with remaining ¼ cup cheese. 
 Cover; cook 3 minutes longer, or until cheese is melted.
