Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup uncooked  rice 
 2 ounces seedless  raisins  (⅓ cup) 
 1 ounce  dried apples , chopped (⅓ cup) 
 1 tablespoon instant minced  onion 
 1 tablespoon  chicken bouillon  granules 
 1 teaspoon  curry powder 
 2 cups  water 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  raisins ,  apples ,  onion , bouillon and  curry . 
 Add  water  and  butter . Bring to a boil; stir once or twice. 
 Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 15 minutes (25 minutes for  parboiled rice ), or until  rice  is tender and liquid is absorbed. 
 Fluff with fork.
