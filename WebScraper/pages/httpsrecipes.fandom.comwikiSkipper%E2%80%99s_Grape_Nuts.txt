 Description : 
 Purchased from the Van Sickle Estate in Mabank, Texas in 1990. Date and source unknown. Marked as “Paw Paw’s favorite snack”. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups  graham flour 
 1/2-cup  wheat germ 
 1-cup  brown sugar 
 2 cups  buttermilk 
 1-teaspoon  baking soda 
 1 pinch  salt 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in large mixing bowl. 
 Beat until smooth. 
 Spread dough onto 2 large greased baking sheets. 
 Bake 25-30 minutes. 
 When still warm, break into chunks and whirl briefly in blender, about 1 cup at a time. 
 Crisp in 250 oven for 20-30 minutes. 
 Store in airtight container.
