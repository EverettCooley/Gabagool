Ingredients : 
 2 teaspoons  cumin  seed 
 1 teaspoon  cardamom 
 ½ teaspoon  allspice 
 1 teaspoon  fenugreek seed 
 1 teaspoon  coriander seed 
 8  cloves 
 1 teaspoon  black peppercorns 
 20 crumbled  dried red peppers 
 ½ teaspoon dried  ginger 
 ½ teaspoon  cinnamon 
 1 teaspoon  turmeric 
 3 tablespoons sweet or hot  paprika 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Put all the ingredients up to the  salt  in a frying pan and heat for 2 minutes, stirring constantly. 
 Add the  salt  and grind the spices in a spice grinder.
