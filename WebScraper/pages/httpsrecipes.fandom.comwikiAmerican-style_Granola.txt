      American-style Granola     
 Ingredients : 
 ½ oz  sunflower oil 
 ½ oz runny  honey 
 6 oz  rolled oats 
 1 oz whole wheat flakes 
 1 oz  sesame seeds 
 1 oz  sunflower seeds 
 1 oz mixed chopped  nuts 
 tsp ground  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Preheat the oven to 160 °C / 325 °F / gas mark 3. 
 Combine the  oil  and  honey  and mix with the other ingredients. 
 Spread this out evenly on a non-stick baking tray and  bake  for about 30 minutes, turning once.
