Description : 
 This is easy and delicious as a dressing on  chicken  or salads or as a dip for assorted raw vegetables. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup plain low-fat  yogurt 
 ½ tsp  curry powder 
 ⅛ tsp ground  ginger 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 dash of  cayenne pepper 
 Directions : 
 Blend all ingredients until smooth. 
 Chill in a covered container at least 1 hour before serving. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Food exchange per serving: up to 2 tablespoons may be considered "free." Low-sodium diets: omit  salt .
