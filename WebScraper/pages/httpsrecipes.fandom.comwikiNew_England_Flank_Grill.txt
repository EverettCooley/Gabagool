 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2Â lbs flank  Steak 
 3/4 cup  maple syrup 
 2 tablespoons soy sauce 
 1/4 cup  vinegar 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 fresh ground  pepper 
 Directions : 
 1. combine  maple syrup , soy sauce,  vinegar ,  garlic , and  pepper  in a bowl.
 2. place flank  Steak  in a plastic bag with marinade.
 3. refrigerate for 2 hours or overnight.
 4. strain marinade into a sauce pan and cook on low heat.
 5. meanwhile, heat the grill to medium-high.
 6. place  Steak  on grill and cook 5 minutes per side.
 7. cut into thin slices against the  grain .
 8. serve with the heated marinade.
