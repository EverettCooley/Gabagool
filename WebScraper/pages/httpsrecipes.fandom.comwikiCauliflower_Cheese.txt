 Ingredients : 
 1 large   cauliflower , trimmed 
 300 ml  milk 
 3 tbsp  plain flour 
 50 g (2 oz)  butter 
 100 g (3 oz)  cheddar  cheese, grated 
 salt  and  black pepper 
 a pinch  mustard  powder 
 a pinch grated  nutmeg 
 25 g (1 oz) fresh  breadcrumbs 
 Directions : 
 Cook the  cauliflower  in boiling salted  water  for 10-15 minutes until tender. Drain and place in a flameproof dish. 
 Meanwhile, place the  milk ,  flour  and  butter  in a saucepan. Heat, stirring continuously until the sauce thickens, boils and is smooth. Cook for a further 2 minutes. 
 Add about three-quarters of the grated  cheese  to the sauce. Season with  salt  and  pepper  to taste. 
 Add the  mustard  and  nutmeg . Stir and cook for another minute. 
 Pour the sauce over the  cauliflower . Mix the remaining  cheese  and breadcrumbs together, sprinkle over the sauce. Grill until golden. 
 Serve immediately.
