 head lettuce

 1 clove  garlic , sliced 
 freshly ground  pepper 
 2 tbsp  salad oil 
 1 tbsp minced  parsley 
 1 tbsp  wine vinegar 
 1 tsp  lime juice 
 Directions : 
 Wash, drain, and thoroughly dry the  lettuce . 
 Rub a salad bowl with  garlic  and add the other ingredients to the bowl. 
 Mix well with salad spoon. 
 Crisscross salad fork and spoon in salad bowl to keep  lettuce  from dressing until ready to serve. 
 With hands, tear  lettuce  leaves and place over top of salad fork and spoon. 
 Just before serving, toss thoroughly.
