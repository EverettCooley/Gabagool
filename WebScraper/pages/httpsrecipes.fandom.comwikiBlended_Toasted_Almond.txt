 Ingredients : 
 1 shot Stolichnaya  vodka 
 ½ shot Disaronno  amaretto 
 ½ shot  Kahlua 
 2 shots single  cream 
 1 scoop vanilla  ice cream 
 Directions : 
 Blend all ingredients with crushed  ice  until smooth.
