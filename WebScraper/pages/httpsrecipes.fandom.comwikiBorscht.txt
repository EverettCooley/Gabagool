      Borscht     
 Name variations : 
 borshch 
 borsch 
 About borscht : 
 A Ukrainian soup made with fresh beets. It can be prepared using an assortment of vegetables, or with meat and meat stock, or a combination of both. It should always be garnished with a dollop of sour cream.
