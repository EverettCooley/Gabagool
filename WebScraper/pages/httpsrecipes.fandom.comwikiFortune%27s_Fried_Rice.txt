A good breakfast together with bacon, eggs, or ham. Enjoy it, and start your day with Fortune's Fried Rice!
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup cooked  rice 
 1/2 tsp  margarine 
 1 tbsp sesame seeds 
 1 stalk  celery  chopped 
 1/4 tsp chicken broth 
 1/2  onion  chopped 
 1 clove  garlic  chopped 
 2 sprigs  parsley  chopped 
 Directions : 
 Heat pan and melt  margarine . Cook  garlic  until medium brown, then cook sesame seeds until brown. 
 Add onions, celery and chicken broth, mix well and then add rice. Mix all ingredients thoroughly. 
 Fry  rice  until medium fried 
 Put the rice into a molding cup. 
 Top with chopped parsley and serve hot.
