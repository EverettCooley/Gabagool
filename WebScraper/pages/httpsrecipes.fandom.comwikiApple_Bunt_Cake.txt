Apple  Bunt Cake
 
 1 c  butter 
 2 c  sugar 
 3  eggs 
 3 c  flour 
 1/2 ts  baking soda 
 1/2 ts  salt 
 1 ts  cinnamon 
 1/4 ts  mace 
 1 ts  vanilla 
 3 c  Apples ; Chopped 
 2 c  pecans 
 
Bake in Bunt Tube Pan at 325 Degrees For 1 1/2 Hours.
