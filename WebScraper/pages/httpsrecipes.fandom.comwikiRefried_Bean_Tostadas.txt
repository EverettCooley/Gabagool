Description 
 Beans, Refried Canned  by the US Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Serves : 10 
 Ingredients 
 1 (15½ ounce) can  refried beans 
 ¼ cup chopped  onion 
 1 package (1¼ ounce)  taco seasoning  mix 
 1 (10-count) package  tostada shells , warmed 
 2 cups shredded  lettuce 
 ½ cup shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 ⅓ cup sliced ripe (optional) 
 2 medium ripe  avocados , pitted, peeled and thinly sliced 
 Taco sauce  for accompaniment 
 Directions 
 Combine beans, onion and seasoning mix in medium saucepan. Cook, stirring frequently, for 4 to 5 minutes or until heated through. 
 Spread ¼ cup bean mixture over each shell. 
 Top with lettuce, cheese, olives, avocado, and taco sauce.
