 Ingredients : 
 2Â lbs.  Beef 
 1 small  Onion 
 4 clove  garlic 
 1 tsp. ground  jeera  ( cumin ) 
 5 tsp.  curry  powder (mild) 
 3 Tbsp.  oil 
 1 Tbsp.  tomato  paste 
 4  potatoes 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Cut meat into small pieces and wash. Cut  onions  and  garlic  and grind together. Put  oil  in a pressure cooker and heat to a medium temperature. 
 Add the grinded  Onion  an  garlic  to the pressure cooker and let it fry for a few minutes. Add  curry  powder and  jeera  to the pressure cooker. Keep stirring and let it fry for approximately 3 to 4 minutes. 
 Now add the meat. Add  salt . Cover the pressure cooker loosely and cook until meat is well fried. 
 Stir periodically. Add enough  water  to cover the meat. 
 Let it pressure for 5 to 7 minutes depending on the tenderness of the meat. 
 Add the  potatoes  and cook untill done. This dish can be served with  rice  or roti
