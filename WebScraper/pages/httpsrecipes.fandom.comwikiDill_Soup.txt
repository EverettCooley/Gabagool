 Description : 
 An easy Hungarian soup
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 Tbs  lard  or  butter 
 1 Tbs  flour 
 3 hard boiled  eggs  diced 
 1  egg yolk 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 1 bunch of  dill 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Heat a sauce pan and roux with  lard  and  flour . Add  dill , 4 cups  water , and a pinch of  salt  then boil for a few minutes. Mix  yolk  and  sour cream  and put in a tureen with hard boiled  eggs . Ladle soup in tureen a little at 
a time while stirring constantly to avoid curdling. Heat but do not boil and serve.
