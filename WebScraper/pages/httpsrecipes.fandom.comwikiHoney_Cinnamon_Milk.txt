Ingredients : 
 1 cup fat-free  milk 
 1  cinnamon  stick (3 inches) 
 Dash ground  nutmeg 
 Dash ground  allspice 
 1-1/2 teaspoons  honey 
 Instructions : 
 In a small saucepan, combine the milk, cinnamon stick, nutmeg and allspice. 
 Cook and stir over medium heat until heated through; whisk in honey. 
 Serve warm in a mug; garnish with cinnamon stick.
