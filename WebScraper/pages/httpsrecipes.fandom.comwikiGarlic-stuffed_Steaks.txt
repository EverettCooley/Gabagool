Description : 
 Contributed by   Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Marlboro Country Cookbook 
 Makes 4 to 8 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 8 boneless  rib eye   steaks , 8 to 10 oz each 
 12 cloves  garlic , peeled and cut in quarters lengthwise 
 ¼ cup  olive oil 
 ½ tbsp  salt 
 ½ tbsp  black pepper 
 ½ tbsp  cayenne pepper 
 1 cup crumbled  blue cheese 
 ½ cup  butter  or  margarine 
 Directions : 
 With a sharp pointed knife, make horizontal slits in the sides of each  steak . 
 Stuff slivers of  garlic  into slits; brush steaks with  olive oil  and season with  salt ,  pepper  and  cayenne pepper . 
 Place steaks on grill 4 to 5 inches from medium hot coals. 
 Grill 5 to 7 minutes per side. 
 Soften  cheese  and  butter  to room temperature. 
 Add ½ tbsp each  salt ,  pepper  and  cayenne pepper . 
 Stir to blend. 
 Remove steaks from grill; top with  cheese ,  butter  and return to grill for a few minutes. 
 Serve at once.
