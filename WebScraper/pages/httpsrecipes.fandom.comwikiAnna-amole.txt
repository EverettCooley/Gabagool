 Description : 
 It's an awesome guac recipe that has been thoroughly enjoyed by many  avocado  enthusiasts.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 ripe  California avocados 
 4 Tbsp  Mayonnaise 
 1 2 in. sweet  Onion , chopped 
 4  garlic  cloves, minced 
 4 Tbsp fresh  cilantro , cut finely with scissors 
 1  chile de arbol , dried and finely crushed 
 1 tsp  cumin  powder 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  black pepper ,  lime juice 
 Directions : 
 Chill  avocados , then mash with a  Potato  masher leaving a good amount of chunks. 
 Stir all ingredients together and spatula them flat into a bowl.
 Sprinkle with 1-TBS. of  lime juice  to keep from discoloring. 
 Chill for about an hour. 
 Turn into a serving bowl, garnish with  cilantro ,  parsley  and .
