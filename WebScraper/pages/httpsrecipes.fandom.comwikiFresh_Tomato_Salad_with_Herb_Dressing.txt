 garlic  Bread 
 4 slices  sourdough bread

 Herb Dressing 
 1/2 cup extra virgin  olive oil 
 2 Tbs.  red wine vinegar , optional 
 1 small clove fresh  garlic , crushed 
 2 Tbs. finely chopped fresh  parsley 
 2 Tbs. chopped fresh  chives 
 2 Tbs. finely chopped fresh  basil 
 salt  and freshly ground  black pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 1. To make the Salad: Core large  tomatoes , cut in half and slice. Set aside.
 
 2. To make the  garlic  Bread: Brush the bread on both sides with  olive oil , and toast the slices. After toasting, rub the bread lightly with the  garlic , and set aside.
 
 3. To make the Herb Dressing: Whisk together the  olive oil  and  wine vinegar , if using, with the  garlic , fresh herbs,  salt  and  pepper .
 
 4. To serve, toss the  tomatoes  in the Herb Dressing. Place the  garlic  toast on individual serving plates, and arrange the  tomato  slices in a stack on top of the bread. Drizzle the Herb Dressing over the  tomatoes  and around the plate, and sprinkle with fresh herb leaves as a garnish.
