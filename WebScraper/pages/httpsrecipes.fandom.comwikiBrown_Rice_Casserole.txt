brown rice  Casserole
 
 2 C.  brown rice 
 1/4 C.  pumpkin  Seed 
 2 Tbsp.  butter 
 1 C. Chopped  Onion 
 2 Tbsp.  vegetable oil 
 1/2 Lb.  Pork   Sausage 
 1/2 C.  chicken stock 
 1/2 Tsp. Seasoned Vegetable  salt 
 Cook  brown rice  According to Directions, Using Only 2 Cups  water . Saut?  pumpkin  Seeds in  butter  Until Crisp. Cook  Pork   Sausage  in Small Pan, Breaking Apart With Fork, Until Tender And Lightly Browned. Saut?  onions  Until Tender-Crisp. Combine All Ingredients in Buttered Casserole, Reserving a Few  pumpkin  Seeds For The Top. Bake Uncovered at 350 Degrees For 20 Minutes.
 
Enjoy.
