Description : 
 Makes 6 to 8 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 ¼ cup  all-purpose flour 
 3 cups  milk 
 2  eggs , slightly beaten 
 2 cups cooked  rice 
 2 teaspoons grated  orange peel 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 ½ cup  whipped topping 
 1 prepared  graham cracker  crust 
 1 x 11-ounce can  mandarin oranges , drained 
 2  kiwi  fruits, sliced 
 1  banana , sliced 
 Directions : 
 Combine  sugar  and  flour  in 2-quart saucepan; gradually stir in  milk . 
 Cook, stirring constantly, over medium heat until thick and bubbly, about 10 minutes. 
 Reduce heat; cook, stirring, an additional 2 minutes. 
 Remove from heat. 
 Gradually stir in 1 cup hot mixture into  eggs . 
 Return  egg  mixture to saucepan; bring to a slow boil. 
 Cook, stirring, 2 minutes. 
 Remove from heat. 
 Stir in cooked  rice ,  orange peel  and  vanilla . 
 Cover and chill in refrigerator. 
 Fold in whipped topping; pour into crust. 
 Just before serving, garnish with  oranges ,  kiwi fruit  and  banana .
