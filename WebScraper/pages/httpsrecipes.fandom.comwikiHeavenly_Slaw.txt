Heavenly Slaw 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 young cabbage 
 1/2 cup cream 
 1/2 cup sugar 
 1/2 cup cider vinegar 
 1 t. salt 
 Directions : 
 Discard outer leaves of cabbage. Shred the rest fine. Beat cream, sugar, vinegar and salt together thoroughly until the dressing is like whipped cream. Mix with cabbage just before servings
