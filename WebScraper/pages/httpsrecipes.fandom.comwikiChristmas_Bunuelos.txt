Ingredients : 
 1 tsp  cinnamon , ground 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 4 cup  flour 
 Â¼ cup  oil 
 1 tsp  salt 
 2 tsp  sugar 
 1 cup  water 
 1 each  sugar  and  cinnamon  mixture 
 Directions : 
 Sift all dry ingredients together. 
 Slowly add  water  and a little  oil . 
 Turn onto a lightly floured board and knead until dough is smooth and elastic. 
 Divide into about 40 small balls; roll out into approximately 4" to 6" circles. 
 Fry in very hot  oil  (hot and deep) until delicately browned on both sides. 
 Drain on paper towels and sprinkle with  cinnamon  and  sugar  mixed.
