Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ oz Tennessee  whiskey 
 ½ tsp  sugar 
 1 – 2 dashes  bitters 
 chilled  champagne 
 Directions : 
 In collins glass, combine the Tennessee  whiskey ,  bitters , and  sugar , stirring until  sugar  is dissolved. 
 Fill with  champagne  and add a slice of  peach .
