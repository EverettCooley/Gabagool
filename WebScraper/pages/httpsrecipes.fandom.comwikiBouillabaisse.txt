       Bouillabaisse     
 Contributed by  PressureCookerRecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 tablespoons  olive oil 
 2  onions , chopped 
 2 cloves  garlic 
 2 tablespoons  parsley , chopped 
 1 can  tomatoes , drained and chopped 
 1  bay leaf 
 1 teaspoon  thyme 
 ¼ teaspoon  saffron , crushed (optional) 
 4 cups water 
 1 pound hearty  fish  fillets, cut in chunks 
 1 pound  lobster  tail, cut in chunks 
 12 oz  scallops 
 ¼ lb  shrimp , shelled 
 6  clams  in shells 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Heat oil in pressure cooker pot and sauté onion. 
 Add garlic, parsley, tomatoes, bay leaf, thyme, water, salt and pepper to taste. 
 Close lid and bring to high pressure and cook for 5 minutes. 
 Release pressure and open lid. 
 Add fish and seafood, then stir. 
 Close lid and bring back up to high pressure and cook for 3 minutes. 
 Release pressure, open lid and serve.
