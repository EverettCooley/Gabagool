 Ingredients : 
 1/4 pound  red beans  ( kidney beans ) 
 1/4 pound  hominy , ( posole ) 
 small  Onion , chopped 
 small tomatoe, chopped 
 Directions : 
 Boil beans and the  maize  together until cooked. 
 Fry  onions  and  tomatoes  together in hot  oil . 
 Combine all ingredients. 
 Season to taste.
