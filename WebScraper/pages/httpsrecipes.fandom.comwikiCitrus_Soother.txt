Description 
 Juice, Grapefruit, Canned Unsweetened  by the US Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource
 
 Serves : 4 
 Ingredients 
 1 cup  grapefruit juice 
 3 cups  orange juice 
 ¼ cup  honey 
 ⅛ teaspoon  cinnamon  (optional) 
 Directions 
 Heat in a medium saucepan the grapefruit juice, orange juice, honey, and cinnamon just until warm. 
 Note 
 Honey is not recommended for children under 1 year of age.
