 Ingredients : 
 1/4 cup  heavy cream 
 1 teaspoon.  Sugar 
 2 1/2 oz.  rye bread  (crumbed) 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 Directions : 
 1.	Beat cold  cream  together with  Sugar  until fluffy. 
 2.	Slice and toast rye-bread in some  vegetable oil , and grate it. 
 3.	Arrange  cream  in cups, a layer of  cream , then a layer of bread, and repeat.
 Serving:
 Serve with fruits decorated on cups. Serve for 2 people.
