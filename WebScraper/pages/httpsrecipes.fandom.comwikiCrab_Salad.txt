      Crab Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp  lemon juice 
 2 tbsp  mayonnaise 
 2 tbsp natural  yogurt 
 450 g (1 lb)  crab  meat 
 1 diced  cucumber 
 4 skinned and cubed  tomatoes 
 110 g (4 oz)  pasta shells , cooked 
 1 shredded  lettuce 
 cucumber  and  lemon  slices to garnish 
 Directions : 
 Mix together the  lemon juice ,  mayonnaise  and  yogurt . 
 Combine the dressing with the remaining ingredients except the  lettuce . 
 Serve the crab salad on a bed of shredded  lettuce , garnished with  cucumber  and  lemon  slices, accompanied with brown bread and  butter .
