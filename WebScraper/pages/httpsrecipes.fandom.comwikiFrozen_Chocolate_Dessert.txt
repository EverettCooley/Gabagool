Ingredients : 
 1 lb. pkg. Hydrox/Oreo chocolate sandwich cookies 
 1/2 c. melted  butter 
 1/2 gallon vanilla  ice cream 
 1 lg. can  evaporated milk 
 1 c.  sugar 
 1/2 c. butter 
 2 sq. semi sweet  chocolate 
 1 pt. whipping  cream 
 1/2 c. chopped  walnuts  or  pecans 
 Directions : 
 Mix crushed Hydrox chocolate cookies with 1/2 cup melted butter. 
 Put in two 9" x 13" pans. Freeze. 
 Divide 1/2 gallon of ice cream (softened a little) in half. (I slice the ice cream into slices.) 
 Spread ice cream all over crust evenly. 
 Return to freezer. 
 Combine evaporated milk, sugar, butter, and chocolate squares in saucepan and cook until thick. Use medium to low heat as burns easily if not stirred. 
 Cool, spread over frozen ice cream and return to freezer. 
 Whip 1 pint whipping cream with a little sugar and spread over the frozen chocolate mixture. Top with chopped walnuts and freeze. 
 Notes : 
 Makes two 9x13 inch pans of dessert 
 Nice to make ahead when having large crowd 
 Can be kept 1 month in freezer. Enjoy.
