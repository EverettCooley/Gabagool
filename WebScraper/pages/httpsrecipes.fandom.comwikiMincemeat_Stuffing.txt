Ingredients : 
 16 oz  mincemeat , fresh or canned 
 4 cups  breadcrumbs   or  1 loaf white bread torn into pieces 
 1 small  onion , chopped 
 1  apple , cubbed 
 1 stalk  celery , sliced 
 1 cup  dried cranberries , I use Crazons by Ocean Spray 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 450 °F. 
 Mix all ingredients together in a large baking pan or a mixing bowl. 
 Either bake stuffing alone or in a bird (If in a bird, add the gizzards to the mix). 
 Bake for 1 hour or until done at 450 °F.
