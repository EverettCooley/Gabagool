 Fried carp
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 â€“ 2 sazan ( carp ) or  bream 
 50 g of  butter 
 50 g of dried and finely ground  breadcrumbs 
 2 tablespoons of  dill 
 a cup of  oil 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 The cleaned fish is cut in pieces, salted and peppered, dipped in melted  butter  and rolled in dried and finely ground breadcrumbs. 
 It is fried in heated  oil  being continuously turned over. 
 The ready fish is laid out in a flat plate, next to it fried  potatoes  and  lemon  sections are put; all this is strewed with cut dill. 
 According to this recipe other kinds of fish can be fried.
