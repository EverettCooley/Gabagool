Ingredients : 
 2 cups  Flour 
 1/2 cup cooking  oil 
 1  Egg 
 2 tablespoons  Sugar 
 1/2 teaspoon  baking powder 
 Directions : 
 To prepare this  Almond  Fingers Recipe, first rub cooking  oil  into  flour , add  Sugar  and  baking powder . Make into stiff paste with  egg . Roll out and cut into fingers. Chop 1 cup  Almonds  and mix with 1/2 cup sifted  Sugar , and white 1  egg . Spread on fingers and bake quickly a light brown color.
