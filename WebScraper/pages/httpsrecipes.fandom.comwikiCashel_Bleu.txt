       Cashel Bleu     
 About Cashel Bleu : 
 This creamy yet crumbly blue cheese from Ireland has a tangy but mellow flavor. It's cheaper than Stilton but not quite as good. Substitutes include  Stilton ,  Gorgonzola  or  Roquefort
