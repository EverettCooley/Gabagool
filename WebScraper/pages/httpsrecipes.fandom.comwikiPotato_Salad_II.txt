 In Romanian: Salata de cartofi II
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb / 500 g  potatoes 
 2  onions 
 1  pickle 
 1 hard boiled  egg 
 2 â€“ 3 tablespoons  oil 
 vinegar  to taste 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Wash, boil, peel and let cool the  potatoes . 
 Then slice very thinly and mix with  salt , thinly sliced  onions , pickle rounds,  oil  and  vinegar . 
 Place in salad bowl and garnish with rounds of hard boiled  egg . 
 You can also add some  tomato  slices.
