Description : 
 Makes 8 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  chicken  or  turkey  cubes 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 2 cups diced  celery 
 ⅓ cup chopped  nuts 
 ¼ cup minced  onion 
 ¼ cup chopped  dill pickles 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 1 cup (4 ounces) shredded  Cheddar  cheese 
 1 cup crushed  rice cereal  or  corn flakes 
 Directions : 
 Combine  chicken ,  celery ,  nuts ,  onion , pickles,  salt ,  pepper  and  mayonnaise . 
 Turn into greased shallow 2½-quart baking dish. 
 Mix  cheese  and cereal; sprinkle over salad mixture. 
 Bake at 350°F for 30 minutes.
