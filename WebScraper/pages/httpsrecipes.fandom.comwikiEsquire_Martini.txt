 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 shots Stolichnaya Red  vodka 
 1 1/2 shots Stolichnaya Razberi  vodka 
 1/2 shot  Parfait Amour 
 garnish: Rose petal 
 Glass: Martini
 
 Directions : 
 Stir all the ingredients and strain into a chilled martini glass.
