Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/4 cup  cocoa 
 1/4 cup  butter  or  shortening 
 1/4 cup  applesauce 
 2 tbsp sweet*10 liquid 
 2 tbsp  vanilla 
 2  eggs , beaten, or substitute 
 1 cup  flour 
 1/2 tsp  salt 
 1 1/2 tsp  baking soda 
 1/4 cup  nuts , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Add  vanilla , sweetener,  eggs . Stir until well-blended and add the dry ingredients. Mix until well-blended, stir in chopped  nuts , pour into a greased 8" square pan. Level batter smoothly. Bake at 325 °F for 20 minutes. Cut into 16 squares. 
 calories: 82 exchanges: ½ bread, 1 fat nutritive values: 2 g prot; 7 g carb; 5 g fat
