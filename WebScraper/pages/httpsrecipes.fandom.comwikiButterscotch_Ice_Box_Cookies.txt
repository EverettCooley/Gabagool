Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  butter  or  margarine 
 1 cup  brown sugar 
 1  egg , slightly beaten 
 ½ teaspoon  vanilla 
 2 cups  flour 
 ½ teaspoon  cream of tartar 
 ½ teaspoon  baking soda 
 ½ cup  nut  meats broken in pieces 
 Directions : 
 First mix ½ cup  butter  or  margarine  until creamy. 
 Add 1 cup  brown sugar  and when well blended. 
 Add 1  egg , slightly beaten and mix well. 
 Add ½ teaspoon  vanilla . 
 Sift together 2 cups  flour , ½ teaspoon  cream of tartar  and ½ teaspoon soda and add to first mixture. 
 Mix well. 
 Add ½ cup nut meats broken in pieces. 
 Pack into a small bread pan and leave in the refrigerator over night. 
 When wanted, cut in thin slices and bake 8 minutes in a hot oven or at 400°F. 
 Substitutions : 
 White sugar  may replace the  brown sugar . 
 Other flavors may be used in place of  vanilla . 
 Nuts  may be omitted or replaced by  coconut  or  Raisins
