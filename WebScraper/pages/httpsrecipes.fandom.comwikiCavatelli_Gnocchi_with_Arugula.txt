 Prep Time: 20 minutes 
 Cook time: 
 Serves: 4 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  cavatelli 
 1 pounds  arugula , washed and chopped 
 1 cup warm or hot  tomato sauce 
 Freshly grated  pecorino  cheese 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Bring a large pot of water to a rolling boil, salt abundantly, and add the pasta. 
 Cook for 2 minutes and add the arugula. 
 Drain when the pasta is al dente and toss with the tomato sauce and pecorino cheese. 
 Serve.
