Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ oz  Myers's Original Dark Rum 
 ½ oz  curaçao 
 2 oz  orange juice 
 2 oz  heavy cream 
 1 oz  cream of coconut 
 8 oz crushed ice 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients in blender until smooth and creamy. 
 Serve in a specialty glass. 
 Rim glass with crushed toasted  coconut  and  orange  slice.
