Ingredients : 
 1 boiled  chicken  shredded 
 2 sliced  green peppers 
 1 sliced  mango 
 peanuts  (to taste) 
 Sauce : 
 ¼ cup  pineapple juice 
 ¼ cup  soy sauce 
 2 tbs  vinegar 
 1 tbsp  ginger 
 2 tbsp  flour 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Mix the sauce ingredients in a bowl. 
 Add sauce to the shredded  chicken  and let marinate at least one hour. 
 Stir fry the marinated  chicken  with 2 sliced  green peppers  add  water  if too dry. 
 Then add 1 sliced  mango  and  peanuts . 
 Cook only long enough for the  mango  to get hot. 
 Serve over a streaming bed of  rice .
