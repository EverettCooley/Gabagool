 Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 3/25/07
 
         
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x 10-oz. canned, refrigerated pizza crust dough 
 ⅓ lb. thinly sliced roast  beef   or  (1 ¾ lb. cooked, leftover beef) 
 2 medium  green onions , sliced 
 1 x 4 oz. can  mushrooms  pieces and stems, drained 
 1 cup shredded cheese, with  Mozzarella  at ½ cup, and Cheddar or Colby-Jack at ½ cup 
 Dijon mustard , optional 
 Directions : 
 Heat and oven to 350° F. Lightly grease the cookie sheet. 
 Unroll pizza crust on cookie sheet; gently stretch to form a 12" square. 
 Layer the beef, onions, mushrooms, and cheeses on half the crust to exactly 1" of one edge. 
 Fold crust over filling; fold the edge up and seal with fork. Cut slits on top. 
 Bake 20 – 25 minutes or until crust is golden brown and filling is hot. 
 Cool 5 minutes before cutting. Serve with Dijon mustard, if desired.
