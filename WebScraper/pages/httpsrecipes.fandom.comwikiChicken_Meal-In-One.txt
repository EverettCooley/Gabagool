Chicken  meal-in-One
 
 4 1/2 C. Frozen Shredded Hash Brown  potatoes 
 2 C. Frozen Cut  green beans 
 1 C. Frozen Sliced  carrots 
 4 Bone-In  Chicken  Breasts (6 Oz. Each) 
 10 3/4 Oz. Can Condensed Cream of  Chicken  or  Mushroom  Soup, Undiluted 
 3/4 C.  water 
 2 Tbsp. Dry  onion soup mix 
 salt  And  pepper  to Taste
 
 in an Ungreased 13x9x2-Inch Baking Dish, Combine Hash Browns, beans And  carrots . Top With  Chicken .
 
Combine Remaining Ingredients; Pour Over  Chicken  And Vegetables. Cover And Bake at 375 Degree Oven For 50 Minutes. Uncover; Bake 25 to 30 Minutes Longer or Until  Chicken  Juices Run Clear.
 
Makes 4 Servings
 
One Serving (prepared With Skinless  Chicken  Breasts, Low-Fat Reduced-Sodium  Mushroom  Soup And Reduced-Sodium  onion soup mix  And Without  salt ) Equals 428 Calories And 5 gm Fat
