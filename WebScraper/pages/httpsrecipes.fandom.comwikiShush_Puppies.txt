 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  cornmeal 
 1 cup  wheat flour 
 1⅔ cup  cheddar  cheese 
 3 tsp  garlic powder 
 4 tbsp  oil 
 ‡ cup  powdered milk 
 1⅛ cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 400°F. 
 Combine all dry ingredients in a bowl. 
 Add  oil  and gradually add  water , stirring to mix well. 
 Dough should form a sticky ball. 
 Using a tablespoon, scoop and form a small oval shape biscuit. 
 Place on a greased cookie sheet and bake for 20 minutes.
