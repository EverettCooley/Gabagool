Ingredients : 
 1 medium  onion  chopped 
 1 medium  green pepper  chopped 
 3 cups cooked  brown rice 
 2 x 16 oz cans Mexican-style  pinto beans 
 ¾ cup  barbeque sauce 
 buns, rolls,  corn  bread - what ever you'd like to put underneath 
 Directions : 
 Saute  onions  and  green peppers  until tender crisp. 
 Add  rice , beans, and barbeque sauce. 
 Simmer 10 – 15 minutes until heated through. 
 Serve over your choice of rolls/bread.
