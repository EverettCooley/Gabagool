 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups  dark rum 
 4  eggs 
 1 x 415ml (14½ fl. oz) can  condensed milk 
 Angostura bitters 
 grated  nutmeg  (to taste) 
 1 tsp  lime juice 
 1 tsp  vanilla extract 
 Directions : 
 Combine the  rum ,  milk ,  eggs , essence and  lime juice  in a blender. 
 Sprinkle on a few drops of  Angostura bitters . 
 Add grated  nutmeg . 
 Serve chilled.
