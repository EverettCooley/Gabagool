Ingredients : 
 2 oz  rum 
 2 oz blue  curaçao 
 ½ cup  pineapple juice 
 ½ cup pina colada mix 
 1 medium  mango , peeled and sliced 
 1  banana , peeled and sliced 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients with  ice  in blender and serve.
