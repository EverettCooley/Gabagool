Description : 
 Lebanese bean and grain soup
 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group This Y-group is international. Good food from all parts of the world. A place where we can share the wonderful food from all over the world. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  chickpeas  - cooked 
 1 cup  black beans  - cooked 
 ½ cup  lentils 
 9 cups  chicken stock 
 1 tablespoon  salt 
 ½ cup  bulgur 
 ¼ cup  rice 
 1 large  onion  - sliced 
 ¼ cup  olive oil 
 Directions : 
 Bring  lentils  to a boil in the stock. 
 Add  bulgur  and  rice , simmer for 30 minutes. 
 Saute  onions  in  oil  until well browned. 
 Add  chickpea , beans,  onions  and their  oil  to the  lentils . 
 Cover and simmer for 20 more minutes. 
 Season with  salt  and serve.
