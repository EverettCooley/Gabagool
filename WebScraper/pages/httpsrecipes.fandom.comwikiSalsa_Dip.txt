 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3  tomatoes , 
 2 teaspoon chillies soaked in  vinegar , 
 1 small  Onion  finely chopped, 
 1/2 teaspoon  chilli powder , 
 1  capsicum , 
 2 pinches  ajwain , 
 1 tablespoon  oil , 
 1/2 teaspoon  Sugar , 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Put the  tomatoes  in hot  water  for about 10 minutes. 
 Remove the skin and chop. 
 Pierce the  capsicum  with a fork and hold over the flame until the skin blackens. 
 Remove from heat, rub off and discard the burnt skin and chop finely . 
 Heat the  oil  and fry the  Onion  for 1/2 a minute. 
 Add the remaining ingredients and cook for 3 to 4 minutes. 
 Serve with salad or  Potato  chips.
