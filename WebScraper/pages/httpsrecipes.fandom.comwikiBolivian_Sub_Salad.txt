 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  rigatoni   Pasta , cooked and cooled 
 1 lb  Turkey   Ham 
 1 lb  Cheddar   Cheese 
 2 	 tomatoes , diced 
 1 	 Onion , diced 
 1 	 green pepper , diced 
 2 	dill pickles, diced 
 1 (8 	ounce) bottle Italian dressing 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together except dressing. 
 Add dressing to taste.
