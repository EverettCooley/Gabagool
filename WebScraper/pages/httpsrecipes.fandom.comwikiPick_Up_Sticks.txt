Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 12 long, thin pretzel sticks (about 4 inches long) 
 ¼ ripe  California avocado , seeded, peeled and cubed 
 2 slices nonfat  American cheese , cut into 1 inch squares 
 ½ red  Apple , cored and cubed 
 12  Red Grapes 
 Directions : 
 Using pretzel stick as skewer, thread on  grape ,  Cheese  square,  Apple  cube and  California avocado  cube.
 Repeat process making twelve "Pick Up Sticks"
 Serve to kids and let them pick up their snack enjoying fruit and sticks. 
 Hint: Use wooden skewer to poke holes in  avocado ,  Apple  and  grape .
