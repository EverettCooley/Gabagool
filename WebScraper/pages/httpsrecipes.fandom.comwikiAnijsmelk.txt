Description : 
 Aniseed milk. This winter drink is great on a cold day, with or without an open fire. It is also a wonderful drink to send you off to sleep.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 liter of  milk 
 1Â½ tsp  aniseed  powder, or whole  aniseed . 
 4 tsp  sugar 
 Directions : 
 When using whole  aniseed , tie the  aniseed  in a thin cloth and leave in the warm  milk  for a while. 
 Add the  sugar , remove the seed, and boil the  milk . 
 When using  aniseed  powder, simply boil the  milk  and add the  aniseed  powder to taste.
