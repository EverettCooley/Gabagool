      American Shepherd's Pie     
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb lean  ground beef 
 ½ cup chopped  onions 
 ½ cup chopped  celery 
 1 (10 ounces) can condensed  vegetable soup 
 ½ teaspoon dried  thyme 
 3 cups  mashed potatoes 
 1 cup shredded  colby  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F and spray a 2 qt baking dish with nonstick spray. 
 In large  skillet  over medium-high heat, brown  ground beef ,  onion  and  celery  until juices run clear. 
 Drain. 
 Pour mixture into baking dish with vegetable soup and  thyme ; stir well. 
 Spread mashed  potatoes  on top. 
 Bake 20 minutes. 
 Sprinkle shredded  cheese  on top and bake 5 minutes more.
