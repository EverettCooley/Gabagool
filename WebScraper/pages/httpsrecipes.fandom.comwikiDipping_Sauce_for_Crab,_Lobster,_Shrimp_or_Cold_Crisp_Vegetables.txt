 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Come Into The Kitchen 
 Makes 2 cups 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 ½ cup  sour cream 
 2 tbsp  wine vinegar 
 ½ tbsp  dry mustard 
 1 tbsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 2 tbsp  onion , grated 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 3  eggs , hard cooked and chopped 
 3 tbsp  black olives , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Blend all ingredients. 
 Chill for 2 hours before serving.
