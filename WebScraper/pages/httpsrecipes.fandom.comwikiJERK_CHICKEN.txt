 Ingredients : 
 6Â lb roast  Chicken 
 1 tbsp.  salt 
 2 tsp.  garlic powder 
 1 tsp.  paprika 
 1 tsp.  soy sauce 
 1 small  Onion 
 3 tbsp. busha browne's jerk seasoning 
 Directions : 
 The first step, is to rub the  Chicken  with  salt . 
 Grater the  Onion  and then rub the grated  Onion  all over the  Chicken . 
 Add the  paprika  and  garlic powder  to the  Chicken . 
 Rub the Jerk seasoning all over the  Chicken  and let the  Chicken  marinate for at least 2 hours. 
 Place in an over at 350 degrees fahrenheit and let it roast for 1 to 11/2 hours.
