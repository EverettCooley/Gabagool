Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  apricot nectar  or other natural fruit purée 
 3 tablespoons  walnut  or  hazelnut oil 
 1 tablespoon  Pommerey-style mustard  with the seeds 
 2 tablespoons boiling  water 
 Directions : 
 Place the  apricot  nectar,  oil , and  mustard  in a blender or mini food processor. 
 Blend at high speed for 10 seconds. 
 Season, add the boiling  water  then blend to emulsify the dressing.
