 Description : 
 Always check the ingredients to make sure the product is vegan.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 Cup Vegan  margarine * 
 
 3/4 Cup Demerera or  brown sugar 
 
 Equivalent of 2  eggs  Using  egg  Replacer 
 
 2 Cups  flour 
 
 1 Tsp  baking powder 
 
 1/2 Cup  soy milk 
 
 2 Ripe  bananas  Mashed 
 Directions : 
 cream   margarine  and  Sugar  very well. Add  egg  replacer mixture, and beat well again.Sift dry ingredients together and add to creamed mixture alternately with the  soy milk . Fold in mashed  bananas .
 
 Pour into a greased loaf pan, and bake at 350Â° for 1 hr.
