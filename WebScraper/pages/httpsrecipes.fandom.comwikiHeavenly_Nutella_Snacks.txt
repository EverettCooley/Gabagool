I just saw this made on Essence of Emeril and I nearly went crazy with desire....not for Emeril, but for these heavenly treats.
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 tablespoons  unsalted butter 
 1 (13 ounces) jar  chocolate-hazelnut spread 
 6 ounces  semi-sweet chocolate  chips 
 1 (12 ounces) box  crispy rice cereal 
 2½ cups  powdered sugar 
 ¾ cup unsweetened  cocoa 
 Directions : 
 In a saucepan, melt the  butter ,  chocolate-hazelnut spread , and  chocolate chips  over medium-low heat, stirring occasionally. 
 Remove from the heat. 
 In a large bowl place the cereal and pour the melted  chocolate  mixture over the top. 
 Gently stir well to coat evenly. 
 Sprinkle with the  powdered sugar  and  cocoa , and toss to coat. 
 Store in an airtight container.
