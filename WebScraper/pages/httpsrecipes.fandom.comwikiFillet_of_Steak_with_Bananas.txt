 Description : 
 Fillet of  Steak  with  bananas 
 
 Ingredients : 
 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Cut the  Steak  into slices 1-inch thick. Trim into neat rounds and beat. 
 Rub with  salt  and brush with  oil . Allow to stand 1 hour. 
 Fry both sides rapidly in hot fat. Cook 5 to 7 minutes altogether. 
 Cut  bananas  in halves lengthwise, then across in two. 
 Fry for 2 to 3 minutes by the side of the meat. 
 Serve  Steak  on rounds of fried bread
