Ingredients : 
 230g  butter 
 120g  cocoa  powder 
 400g  sugar 
 2 tsp  vanilla extract 
 a pinch of  salt 
 6  eggs 
 120g  flour 
 Directions : 
 Melt the butter on low heat. 
 In a bowl, beat the eggs and add the sugar, vanilla and salt. 
 Add the melted butter 
 Add the cocoa and flour, and beat until fully blended 
 Pour into a greased or parchment lined pan. 
 Bake for 15 to 20 minutes at 180degrees. 
 Allow to cool before cutting into squares.
