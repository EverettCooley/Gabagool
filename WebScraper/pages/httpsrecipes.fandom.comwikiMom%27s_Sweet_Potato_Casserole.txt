Ingredients : 
 6 medium  sweet potatoes 
 2  eggs , beaten 
 ¼ cup  brown sugar 
 ¼ cup  orange juice 
 6 tbsp  butter , melted 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp ground  cinnamon 
 ¼ tsp ground  cloves 
 ⅔ cup  pecan  halves 
 Syrup : 
 ¼ cup  brown sugar 
 2 tbsp  orange juice 
 2 tbsp  butter 
 Directions : 
 Prehat oven to 375°F. 
 Peel and boil sweet potatoes until soft; cool slightly. 
 Mash potatoes until creamy. 
 Add  eggs , brown sugar,  orange juice ,  butter ,  cinnamon  and cloves and mix well. 
 Place in a lightly greased 2-quart casserole and smooth the top. 
 Set aside. 
 Syrup : 
 In a small saucepan over medium heat, warm  brown sugar ,  orange juice  and  butter  until melted. 
 Arrange pecans on top of  sweet potatoes  and drizzle with syrup. 
 Bake for 35 minutes,. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving: 324 cal; 4g pro, 37g carb, 19g fat (51%)
