 In Romanian: Varza de Bruxelles cu smantana.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1Â¾ lbs / 750 g  Brussels sprouts 
 2 tablespoons  butter 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Remove the outer yellow leaves from the sprouts. 
 Wash them well and boil at high temperature, in  salt   water , for approximately 20 minutes. 
 When done, drain well, then place in a pan with melted  butter  at slow temperature. 
 Cover and simmer for 10 minutes, shaking the pan every now and then. 
 Pour the  sour cream  and simmer a few minutes more. 
 Serve hot.
