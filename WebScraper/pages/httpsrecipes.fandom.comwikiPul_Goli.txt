Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Long Estate in Irving, Texas in 1987.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ pounds boneless  sirloin 
 1 tablespoon toasted  sesame seeds 
 ¼ cup  soy sauce 
 2 tablespoons  sesame oil 
 2 tablespoons  sugar 
 1  green onion  finely chopped 
 2 cloves  garlic  minced 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Thinly slice the meat. 
 Combine sesame seeds, soy sauce, sesame oil, sugar, green onion, garlic and black pepper. 
 Add meat to marinade and refrigerate for 2 hours. 
 Stir fry meat in wok for 10 minutes. 
 Serve over hot rice.
