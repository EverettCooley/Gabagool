Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup no-fat/low fat  sour cream 
 ¼ cup no-fat/low fat  mayonnaise 
 1 tsp  spicey mustard 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 pinch  red pepper flakes 
 Directions : 
 Mix the above ingredients in the top of a double boiler (adding  lemon juice  last and slowly). 
 Heat thoroughly about 15 minutes or until completely mixed. 
 You can control the taste by experimenting with different types of  mustard . 
 More  pepper  flakes will give the dish a rewardingly healthful twang.
