 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup roasted & chopped Oregon  hazelnuts 
 1 pack  semi-sweet chocolate  pieces 
 1/2 cup  heavy cream , whipped 
 1 tbsp  Sugar 
 1/4 tsp  rum  flavoring 
 Directions : 
 1 Melt  chocolate  in double boiler or small bowl over hot  water . 
2 If  chocolate  should stiffen, stir in 1 to 2 tablespoons  vegetable shortening  until smooth. 
3 Whip  cream , add  Sugar  and flavoring. 
4 Fold in slightly cooled  chocolate . 
5 Chill until mixture is firm enough to form into balls, then roll in finely chopped  hazelnuts . 
6 Chill until firm. 
7 Store in refrigerator.
