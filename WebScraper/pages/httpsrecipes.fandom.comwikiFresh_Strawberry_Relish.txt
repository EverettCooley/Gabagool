 Description : 
 Makes 4 servings (about 1 3/4 cups).
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp.  balsamic vinegar 
 2 tbsp.  orange juice 
 1 tbsp. Dijon-style  mustard 
 1 tbsp.  honey 
 1/2 tsp. grated  orange peel 
 1/2 tsp.  red pepper flakes 
 1 pint basket California  strawberries , stemmed and sliced 
 3 tablespoons  raisins 
 3 tablespoons chopped  walnuts 
 Directions : 
 In medium bowl measure all ingredients except  strawberries ,  raisins  and  walnuts . 
 Whisk to blend thoroughly. Add remaining ingredients; toss. 
 Serve with baked or grilled fish or  Chicken .
