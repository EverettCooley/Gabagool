Link: https://recipes.fandom.com/wiki/Black_forest_torte

A dessert that is created by layering kirsch-scented chocolate cake, sour cherries and kirsch-laced whipped cream. A generous coating of whipped cream garnished with chocolate curls and cherries completes the cake.
