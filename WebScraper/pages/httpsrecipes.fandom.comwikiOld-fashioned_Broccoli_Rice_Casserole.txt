Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup chopped  onion 
 ¾ cup chopped  celery 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 x 10-ounce package frozen chopped  broccoli , thawed 
 1 x 10¾-ounce can condensed  cream of chicken  soup 
 4 ounces pasteurized process  American cheese  spread 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 ⅛  hot pepper sauce 
 ½ cup (2 ounces) grated  cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Cook onion and celery in butter in large skillet until tender crisp. 
 Add rice, broccoli, chicken soup, cheese spread, salt, pepper and hot pepper sauce. 
 Spoon into buttered 1½ to 2-quart baking dish. 
 Bake at 350°F for 35 minutes, or until hot and bubbly. 
 Top with grated cheese. 
 Bake 3 minutes longer, or until cheese melts.
