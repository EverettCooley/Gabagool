Description : 
 Contributed by Paula in California via  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 (3 oz) pkgs.  strawberry gelatin 
 1 cup flaked  coconut 
 1 cup chopped  pecans 
 ¾ cup  Eagle brand milk 
 ½ tsp  vanilla 
 red  decorator's sugar 
 Directions : 
 Mix gelatin, coconut and pecans. 
 Stir in vanilla and milk. 
 Mix well. 
 Chill in refrigerator for 1 hour. 
 Shape into small balls or into the shape of strawberries. 
 Roll in red sugar.
