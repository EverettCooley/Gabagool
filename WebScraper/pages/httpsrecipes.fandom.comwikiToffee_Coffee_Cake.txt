Ingredients : 
 1 c.  brown sugar 
 1/2 c.  white sugar 
 1  egg 
 1 tsp.  vanilla extract 
 5 Heath bars 
 2 c.  flour 
 1/4 lb.  butter 
 1 tsp.  baking soda 
 1 c.  buttermilk 
 1/2 c. chopped nuts 
 Directions : 
 Mix sugars, flour and butter like a pie crust and save out 1/2 cup crumbs. 
 Add eggs, soda, vanilla and buttermilk to the remainder; beat well. 
 Pour into a greased 9 x 13 inch pan. 
 Sprinkle 1/2 cup crumbs on top. 
 Crush 5 Heath bars and spread over crumbs. 
 Sprinkle with chopped nuts. 
 Bake 25-30 minutes at 350 degrees.
