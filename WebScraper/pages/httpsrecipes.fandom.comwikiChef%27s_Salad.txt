Chef's Salad
 
 1  garlic  clove, Halved 
 1 Large Head Romaine or  iceberg lettuce 
 1/4 Lb. Cooked  Chicken  or  Turkey  Meat 
 1 Pkg. Sliced  Swiss cheese  (8 Oz) 
 1 Pkg. Sliced Cooked  Ham  (6 Oz) 
 2 Medium  tomatoes 
 2 Hard Cooked  eggs 
 French Dressing, Russian Dressing or Thousand Island Dressing
 
Rub Large Shallow Bowl Well With Laved  garlic . Discard Both Pieces. Into Salad Bowl. Tear  romaine lettuce  Into Bite Size Pieces. With Knife, Cut  Chicken  or  Turkey ,  Cheese  And  Ham  Into Thin Trips. Cut  tomatoes  Into Wedges. Peel And Then Quarter The Hard Cooked  eggs . on  lettuce , Arrange  Chicken ,  Cheese ,  Ham ,  tomato  And  egg .Just Before Serving, Toss Together With Dressing. Serves 6
