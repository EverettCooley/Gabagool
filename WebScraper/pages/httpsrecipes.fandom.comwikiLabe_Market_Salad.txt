Ingredients : 
 tomatoes 
 hard boiled  eggs 
 cooked  potatoes 
 cucumber 
 onion 
 grated  carrot 
 lettuce 
 1 – 2 tbsp  mayo 
 juice from one  citron 
 Directions : 
 Wash  lettuce , hand tear. 
 Chop and add the rest of ingredients. 
 Squeeze in juice from one  citron , 1 – 2 tbsp  mayo , eat with bread.
