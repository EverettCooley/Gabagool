 Source:  Grandma's Casseroles , page 17 
 Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 35 minutes | Stand Time: 10 minutes 
 Makes 8 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 10 to 12 slices  Italian bread , about ½ inch thick 
 ⅔ cup (about 5 ounces) sharp  cheddar  light cold pack cheese food, softened 
 1⅓ cups French's ®  French fried onions 
 1 package (10 ounces) frozen chopped  broccoli , thawed and drained 
 ½ cup (2 ounces) shredded  Swiss cheese 
 5  eggs 
 3 cups  milk 
 2 tablespoons French's ® Zesty Deli  mustard 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  white pepper 
 Directions : 
 Grease 3-quart baking dish. 
 Spread bread slices with cheddar cheese. 
 Arrange slices in a single layer in bottom of prepared baking dish, pressing to fit. 
 Layer French fried onions, broccoli and Swiss cheese over bread. 
 Beat together eggs, milk, mustard, salt and pepper in medium bowl until well blended. 
 Pour egg mixture over layers. 
 Let stand 10 minutes. 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Bake 35 minutes or until knife inserted into center comes out clean (cover loosely with foil near end of baking if top becomes too brown). 
 Cool on wire rack 10 minutes. 
 Cut into squares to serve.
