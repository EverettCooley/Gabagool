Description 
 Apple Juice, Canned Unsweetened  by the US Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Ingredients 
 ¼ cup  apple juice 
 ¼ cup  oil 
 3 tablespoons  vinegar 
 2 tablespoons  lemon juice 
 1 tablespoon  sugar 
 Paprika ,  salt , and  pepper  (optional) 
 Directions 
 Combine apple juice, oil, vinegar, lemon juice, sugar, and spices (if desired) in a covered jar. Shake to mix well. 
 Serve over fresh greens or  pasta salad .
