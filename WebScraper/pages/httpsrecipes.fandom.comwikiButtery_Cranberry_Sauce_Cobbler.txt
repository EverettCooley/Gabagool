Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Hoyt Estate in Allen, Texas in 1989.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 box Butter Recipe golden cake mix 
 1 cup  rolled oats 
 2 tablespoons  margarine 
 2 whole  eggs 
 ⅓ cup water 
 3 tablespoon  margarine 
 16 ounce can jellied  cranberry sauce 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 375°F. 
 Prepare a rectangular cake pan with cooking spray and flour then set aside. 
 Combine half cup cake mix, oats and two tablespoons margarine in a mixing bowl. 
 Mix until crumbly then set aside. 
 Combine remaining cake mix, eggs, water and remaining margarine. 
 Spread into bottom of prepared pan. 
 In another mixing bowl mix cranberry sauce until smooth then spread over base in pan. 
 Sprinkle with reserved topping and bake for 35 minutes.
