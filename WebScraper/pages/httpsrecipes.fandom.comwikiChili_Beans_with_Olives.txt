Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Joanne Albert 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large  onion , chopped 
 1 tbsp  oil 
 1 can (16 oz) light  red kidney beans 
 1 can (16 oz) dark  red kidney beans 
 1 can (4 oz) chopped  black olives 
 1 can (8 oz)  tomato  sauce 
 ¾ cup  water 
 3 tbsp packaged  chili  mix 
 1 tbsp  Parmesan cheese 
 Directions : 
 Sauté  onion  in  oil  until limp. 
 Add all remaining ingredients, and mix well. 
 Simmer until bubbly and hot. 
 Add extra  chili powder  to taste. 
 A small can of whole kernel  corn  can be added for variety.
