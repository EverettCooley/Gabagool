      Peruvian Chicken Pasta Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  Miracle Whip  salad dressing 
 ¼ cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 2 tsp  milk 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 1½ cup  chicken , cooked and chopped 
 1 cup  corkscrew noodles , cooked and drained 
 1 cup  tomato , chopped 
 1 cup  green pepper , chopped 
 ¼ cup  onion , coarsely chopped 
 lettuce 
 Directions : 
 Combine  salad dressing ,  cheese ,  milk  and  salt ; mix well. 
 Add all remaining ingredients except  lettuce ; mix lightly. 
 Chill several hours or overnight. 
 Add additional  salad dressing  just before serving, if desired. 
 Serve on  lettuce  covered platter.
