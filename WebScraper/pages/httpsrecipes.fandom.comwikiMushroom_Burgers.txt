Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 Portobello mushroom  caps 
 oil 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Brush caps with oil, and season with salt and pepper. 
 Grill or broil 4 min. per side. 
 Serve on a bun with burger fixings.
