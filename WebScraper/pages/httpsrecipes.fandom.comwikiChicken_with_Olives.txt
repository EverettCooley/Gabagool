Ingredients : 
 200 g  green olives 
 1  lemon  - freshly squeezed juice 
 2 – 3 tablespoons  olive oil 
 2  onions  — sliced 
 1 teaspoon  cooking salt 
 1 teaspoon  pepper 
 ¼ teaspoon  ginger 
 1 tablespoon  paprika 
 1 kg  chicken breasts 
 1  onion  — finely chopped 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in a large sauce pan. 
 In it add ¾th cup  water  gradually. 
 Chop finely one  onion  and slice the other 2. 
 Grate the  ginger . 
 Add  onion  slices, sprinkle with spices. 
 Lay  chicken  on top. 
 Steam over low heat for 20 minutes. 
 Add finely chopped  onion  and, and steam for another 5 minutes. 
 Just before serving squeeze on  lemon .
