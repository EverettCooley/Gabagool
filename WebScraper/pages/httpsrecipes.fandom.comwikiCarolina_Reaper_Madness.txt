The  Carolina Reaper Madness chip  was created by Paqui. It is the hottest chip in the world and only one single chip comes per package.
 The chip is named after its key ingredient, the  Carolina Reaper Pepper , which the Guinness Book of World Records has named the world's spiciest pepper, hitting an average of 1,569,300 heat units on the Scoville Scale.
 The tortilla chip also contains traces of ghost peppers and chipotle seasoning, Mashable reports.
 The chip was available for a limited time online starting in late September 2020 and then at select stores in the United States beginning Oct. 1 2020.
