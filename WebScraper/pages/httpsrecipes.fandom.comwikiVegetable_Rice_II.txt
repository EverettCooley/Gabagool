 Ingredients : 
 2 cups cooked Curried  rice 
 1 cup halved  Mushrooms 
 l/2 cup melted  margarine 
 1  green pepper , sliced 
 1  Onion , sliced 
 1 tablespoon  tomato  paste 
 1 large  tomato , quartered 
 salt  and  cayenne pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 In a large skillet, fry  onions  in  margarine  for 5 minutes. Add remaining ingredients, except  rice , and cook for another 25 minutes. Stir in cooked curried  rice  and serve at once.
 Serves 4 to 6
Cooking time: 45 minutes
