 Description : 
 Vegetarian Jumbalya
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 	 bags boil-in-the-bag  rice 
 2 	quarts  water 
 14 	ounces crushed  tomatoes  or  tomato  sauce 
 2 	cups frozen  corn  (niblets) 
 1 (14 	ounce) can  kidney beans , drained and rinsed 
 1/2 	 Onion 
 crushed  garlic 
 1 	tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 seasoning 
 Directions : 
 Boil  rice  as per boxed instructions. 
 drain and set aside. 
 Sweat  Onion  in pan. 
 Add remaining ingredients. 
 Add  rice . 
 Return to boil.
