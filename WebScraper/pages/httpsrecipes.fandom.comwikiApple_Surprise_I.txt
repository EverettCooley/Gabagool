Ingredients : 
 1  apple 
 raisins 
 peanut butter 
 Directions : 
 Cut apple in half. 
 Carefully, cut out the core of the apple. 
 Spread peanut butter where the core used to be and over surface of apple. 
 Sprinkle raisins over the peanut butter.
