Description : 
 Shearing Cake
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  self-rising flour 
 8 oz  butter 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 2 tsp  baking powder 
 12 oz  sugar 
 zest  of one  lemon 
 ½ pint of  milk 
 2 beaten  eggs 
 Directions : 
 Sift the flour into a mixing bowl, and then rub in the butter. 
 Add the other dry ingredients, and then stir in the milk and beaten eggs. 
 Mix well. 
 Pour batter into a greased cake tin or loaf pan, and bake at 325° for thirty minutes. 
 Reduce the oven temperature to 300 °F, and bake for another two hours.
