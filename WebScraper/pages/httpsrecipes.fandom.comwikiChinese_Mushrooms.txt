Ingredients : 
 15 large fresh  mushrooms , stems removed 
 ½ pound  ground pork 
 ¼ slice Chinese  salted turnip  ( chung choi ), rinsed and chopped 
 1 tablespoon  soy sauce 
 3 tablespoons finely chopped canned  water chestnuts 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  white sugar 
 Directions : 
 Remove  mushroom  heads from stems and thoroughly wash. 
 Mix the  pork ,  turnip , soy sauce,  water chestnuts ,  salt , and  sugar  in a large bowl. 
 Fill the  mushroom  caps with the stuffing. 
 Steam the  mushrooms  for approx. 30 minutes, then serve.
