 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 one  Chicken  cut into serving pieces 
 a little  oil 
 kaukau 
 bunch of  green onions 
 pumpkin  tips 
 cobs of  corn 
 2  coconuts  for  cream 
 salt  to taste 
 curry  powder to taste 
 Directions : 
 Place  Chicken  in the pot with a little  oil  if necessary. 
 Chop kaukau and place on top of  Chicken . 
 Coarsely chop  green onion  and add next. 
 Coarsely chop  pumpkin  tips and add next. 
 Peel and break  corn  to place on top of greens. 
 Squeeze  coconut milk  over the meat and vegetables to cover. 
 Cover and bring to a boil. 
 Cook gently for 30â€“40 minutes. 
 Add  salt  and  curry  powder. 
 Serve as a meal.
