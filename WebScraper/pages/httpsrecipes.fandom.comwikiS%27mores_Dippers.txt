Description : 
 Of course you can make s'mores without a campfire. Go and transform an ice cream cone into a classic. This is a frozen dessert guests of all ages would definitely enjoy.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 Chocolate or Vanilla Ice Cream Cones 
 1/2 cup marshmallow creme 
 1 teaspoon cream or milk 
 2 tablespoons graham cracker crumbs 
 1 tablespoon red, white and blue cookie sprinkles 
 Directions : 
 In a small microwaveable bowl, combine marshmallow and cream or milk. 
 Microwave on high for about 20 seconds or until it starts to puff. 
 Stir until all ingredients are well blended. 
 Set this aside. 
 In another small bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs and colored sprinkles. 
 Dip the ice cream cones into the marshmallow mixture then into the graham cracker mixture. 
 Serve immediately
