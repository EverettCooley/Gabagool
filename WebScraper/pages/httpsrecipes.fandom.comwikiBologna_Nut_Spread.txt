Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Gibson Estate in Quinlan, Texas in 1981.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  bologna 
 ½ pound blanched  peanuts 
 1 small jar  dill pickle   relish 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 Directions : 
 Grind bologna and peanuts then add relish and mix well. 
 Add enough mayonnaise to moisten.
