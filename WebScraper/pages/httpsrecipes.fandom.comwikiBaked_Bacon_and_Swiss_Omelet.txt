Desription : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Davis Estate in Greenville, Texas in 1994.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 12 slices  bacon  cooked and crumbled 
 1 cup shredded  Swiss cheese 
 ⅓ cup  onion  minced 
 4  eggs 
 2 cups  half and half 
 1 tablespoon  green pepper  diced 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  sugar 
 ⅛ teaspoon ground  cayenne pepper 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 425°F. 
 In a square baking dish place bacon then top with cheese. 
 Combine remaining ingredients and pour over cheese and bacon. 
 Bake for 15 minutes then reduce temperature to 300°F and bake 30 minutes longer.
