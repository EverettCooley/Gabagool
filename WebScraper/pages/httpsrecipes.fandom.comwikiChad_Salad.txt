 Description : 
 A lovely tangy salad dish from Chad. Used as a party dish or dessert this makes 1 to 2 portions.
 
 Ingredients : 
 150 gm Fair Trade long  grain   brown rice 
 1 	small  cucumber 
 2 	FAIRTRADE  bananas 
 50 gm 	FAIRTRADE  Raisins 
 15 ml 	chopped  almonds 
 60 ml 	 olive oil 
 20 ml 	FAIRTRADE  lemon juice 
 zest of 1 FAIRTRADE  lemon 
 5 ml 	 salt 
 2.5 ml Fair trade ground coriander 
 2.5 ml Fair trade ground  cumin 
 2.5 ml Fair trade  cayenne pepper 
 5 ml 	Organic FAIRTRADE runny honey 
 Directions : 
 Cook the  rice  in the usual way, rinse and allow to cool. 
 Slice the  cucumber . 
 Peel and slice the  bananas . 
 Put the  rice , cucmber,  bananas ,  Raisins  and  almonds  in a salad bowl and mix gently but well. 
 Dressing 
 Mix together the  lemon juice ,  lemon  zest,  salt , coriander,  cumin ,  Cayenne  and honey. 
 Pour the dressing over the salad and mix well. 
 Chill for 30 minutes in the refrigerator and then serve.
