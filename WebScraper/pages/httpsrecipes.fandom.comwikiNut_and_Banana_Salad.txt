Description : 
 Salad with  nuts  and  bananas . 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  egg 
 1 cup  sugar 
 2 tbsp  flour 
 ¼ cup  vinegar 
 ¾ cup  water 
 2 tbsp  butter 
 8  bananas 
 1 cup finely chopped  nuts 
 lettuce 
 Directions : 
 Beat  eggs  lightly in top of double boiler; stir in  sugar  and  flour . 
 Add  vinegar  and  water ; cook stirring until thickened. 
 Stir in  butter ; cool. 
 Peel  bananas ; dip into cooked dressing. 
 Roll into  nuts ; arrange on lettuce-lined salad plates.
