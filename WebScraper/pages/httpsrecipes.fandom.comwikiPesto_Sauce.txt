Description 
       Pasta with pesto sauce     
 Farmers' Market Recipes  by the Commonwealth of Massachusetts Department of Agricultural Resources, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Pesto Recipes large collection of Pesto recipes. Different styles and difficulties 
 Cook Time : About 5–10 minutes 
 Ingredients 
 Medium bunch of  basil 
 4 – 6 tablespoons  olive oil 
 2  garlic  cloves, skinned and crushed 
 1 ounce  pine nuts  or  walnuts 
 1 ounce grated  Parmesan cheese 
 Directions 
 Mix all the ingredients, except the cheese, in a blender or food processor and mix until smooth. 
 Stir in the cheese and add more oil if a thinner consistency is desired. 
 Serve over  pasta .
