Description : 
 These shortbread-style cookies always mean Christmas at our house.
 
 Makes about 4 dozen cookies 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 cup  butter 
 3 oz  cream cheese 
 1  egg yolk 
 2½ cups  flour 
 1 cup finely chopped  walnuts  or  pecans 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 1 tsp grated  lemon peel 
 1 bag of  semi-sweet chocolate  bits 
 Non-pareils 
 Directions : 
 Cream butter, cream cheese and sugar 
 Beat in egg yolk 
 Stir in flour, nuts, salt and lemon peel 
 Refrigerate dough at least 2 hours 
 Shape dough into "logs" -- roughly 3 inches by ¾ inches 
 Place on ungreased cookie sheets 
 Bake at 325° F until lightly browned 
 Cool 
 Melt chocolate bits in a double boiler 
 Dip one end of each bar into melted chocolate, and then into the non-pareils. Place on wax paper to set chocolate
