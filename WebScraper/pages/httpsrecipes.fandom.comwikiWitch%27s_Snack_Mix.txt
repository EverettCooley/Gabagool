Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup blood drops ( red hot candies ) 
 1 cup owl eyes ( peanuts ) 
 1 cup chicken toenails (candy  corn ) 
 1 cup colored flies (M & Ms) 
 1 cup cat's claws ( sunflower seeds ) 
 1 cup dead ants ( raisins ) 
 1 cup chicken gizzards (shoestring  potatoes ) 
 1 cup bat bones (small  pretzel  sticks) 
 1 cup ghost noses (mini  marshmallows ) 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients and serve in a plastic pumpkin.
