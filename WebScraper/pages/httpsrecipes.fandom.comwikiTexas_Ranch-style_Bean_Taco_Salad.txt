 Ingredients : 
 1 head  lettuce 
 1 large  white onion 
 16 ounce can  ranch-style beans 
 1 package  corn chips 
 2  avocados  ripe but not too soft 
 2  tomatoes 
 1 pound grated  cheddar cheese 
 1 bottle  Catalina dressing 
 1 pound  ground beef 
 2 tablespoons  lime juice 
 Directions : 
 Saut√© ground beef then drain well and set aside to cool. 
 Tear lettuce into pieces. 
 Dice tomatoes and onion. 
 Grate cheese. 
 Drain beans. 
 Break corn chips into smaller pieces. 
 Chop avocado and sprinkle with lemon juice lightly tossing to coat. 
 Toss together all ingredients except corn chips. 
 Top with chips just before serving.
