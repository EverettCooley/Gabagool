Ingredients : 
 ½ cup of  olive oil 
 ½ cup of  red wine vinegar 
 ½ cup of small diced  onions 
 ¼ cup of diced  parsley 
 Adjust the following to personal flavor
 
 1 spoonful of fine diced  garlic 
 1 spoonful of  salt 
 1 spoonful of  black pepper 
 ½ spoonful of dried  oregano 
 ¼ spoonful of  garlic powder 
 Directions : 
 Combine all the ingredients. 
 Mix them all well. 
 Let them all rest for 2 – 3 hours. 
 Chimichurri tastes great on asado!.
