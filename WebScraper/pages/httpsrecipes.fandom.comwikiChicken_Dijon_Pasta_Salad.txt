Ingredients : 
 4 oz  rotini , uncooked 
 8 oz plain low-fat  yogurt 
 ⅓ cup  wheat germ 
 3 tbsp  white wine vinegar 
 1 tbsp  Dijon mustard 
 ⅛ tsp  black pepper 
 1 cup  chicken breast , cooked and diced 
 ¾ cup  broccoli  flowerets, diced 
 ½ cup  tomato , chopped and seeded 
 ⅓ cup  red onion , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Cook  pasta  according to package directions. 
 In medium bowl, combine  yogurt ,  wheat germ ,  vinegar ,  mustard  and  pepper ; mix well. 
 Add  pasta  and remaining ingredients: toss to coat. 
 Serve immediately or chill before serving. 
 Sprinkle with additional  wheat germ  before serving. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per ¼ salad: 
 
 Calories: 260 | cho: 35mg | car: 34g | pro: 22g 
 Food exchanges: 1 lean-meat | 1 vegetable | 1 starch
