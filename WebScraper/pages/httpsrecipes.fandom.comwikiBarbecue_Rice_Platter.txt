Description : 
 Serve with barbecued  chicken  breast, sliced  pork  or  beef . Makes 4 servings. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 ⅓ cup chopped  onion 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 ½ cup  barbecue sauce 
 ¼ cup sliced  green onion  tops 
 1 teaspoon chopped  cilantro 
 ½ teaspoon  garlic powder 
 ¼ teaspoon  liquid smoke 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in large skillet over medium-high heat. Add  onion ; cook 2 to 3 minutes or until translucent. 
 Add  rice , barbecue sauce,  green onion  tops,  cilantro ,  garlic powder  and  liquid smoke . 
 Cook until thoroughly heated.
