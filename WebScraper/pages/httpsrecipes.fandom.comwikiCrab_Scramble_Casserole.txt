Desription : 
 A nice light supper.
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Original recipe 
 Submitted by: jkbgraysail via dLife 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  canola oil  or  butter , melted 
 12  eggs 
 ½ cup  fat-free (skim) milk 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  white pepper 
 1½ teaspoons chopped fresh or ½ teaspoon dried  dill  weed 
 1 cup chopped cooked  crabmeat  or  imitation crabmeat 
 1 package (8 ounces) reduced-fat cream cheese ( Neufchatel ), cut into ½-inch cubes 
 2 medium  green onions , sliced (2 tablespoons) 
 paprika 
 Directions : 
 Pour oil into square baking dish, 8x8x2 inches; tilt dish to coat bottom. 
 In a large bowl, beat eggs, milk, salt, white pepper and dill weed with fork or wire whisk until well mixed. 
 Stir in crabmeat, cream cheese and onions. 
 Pour into baking dish. 
 Cover and refrigerate minimum 4 hours, but no longer than 24 hours. 
 Heat oven to 350°F. 
 Sprinkle paprika over egg mixture. 
 Bake uncovered 45 to 50 minutes or until center is set. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 Calories: 205 | Carbohydrates: 4g | Protein: 16g | Total Fat: 14g | Saturated: 6g | Cholesterol: 350mg | Fiber: 0g | Sodium: 590mg 
 Dietary Exchange: 2 Medium-Fat Meat | 1 Fat
