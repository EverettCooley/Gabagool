 Makes 1½ cups 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  green pepper  chunks (1 medium), ribs and seeds removed 
 ½ cup sliced  red onion 
 ½ cup fresh squeezed  lime juice 
 1 to 3 pickled  jalapeno peppers , halved and seeded, stems removed (use according to taste. jalapenos are hot) 
 1 clove  garlic 
 ¼ tbsp  salt , optional 
 Directions : 
 Mix  green pepper ,  onion ,  lime juice , jalapeno  peppers ,  garlic  and  salt  in blender or food processor. 
 Puree until finely chopped but not smooth. 
 If using blender, it may be necessary to puree sauce in 2 batches.
