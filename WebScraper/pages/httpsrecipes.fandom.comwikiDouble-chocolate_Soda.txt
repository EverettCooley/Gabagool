Description : 
 Some call this drink a  chocolate  phosphate. 
 
 Contributed by Ursula Taylor 
 Ingredients : 
 2 to 3  ice cubes , cracked into small pieces 
 2 tbsp  chocolate syrup 
 180 TO 200 ml   chilled  club soda . 
 
 Directions : 
 Place  ice cubes  in a tall beverage glass. 
 Measure  chocolate  syrup on top of  ice . 
 Add  club soda  slowly; stir vigorously with a long-handled beverage spoon to blend. 
 Serve at once. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 Calories 22 | cho 3 g | pro 1 g | fat 1 g | fiber 0.2 g | sodium 109 mg | chol 0 mg. 
 Food exchanges: 1 serving may be considered "free". 
 Low sodium diets: omit  salt  from  chocolate syrup  recipe.
