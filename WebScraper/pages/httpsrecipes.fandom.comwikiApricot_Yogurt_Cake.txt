Description : 
 This is a recipe I obtained from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Crawford Estate in Irving, Texas in 1982. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup apricot  yogurt 
 2½ cups  flour 
 3 teaspoons  baking powder 
 ¼ cup  oil 
 ¾ cup  granulated sugar 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 2  eggs 
 1 cup  apricot preserves 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together except preserves and beat well until smooth. 
 Pour into greased round cake pan and bake for 45 minutes. 
 Heat preserves in saucepan stirring constantly until almost boiling. 
 Remove from heat and cool 2 minutes then spread over cake and cool before serving.
