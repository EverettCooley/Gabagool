Description : 
 This Caesar salad can be made in 10 minutes! It's a combination of Chicken of the Sea Premium Skinless & Boneless Salmon, crisp romaine lettuce and your favorite Caesar dressing.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (7.1-oz) pouch Chicken of the Sea Premium Skinless and Boneless Pink  Salmon 
 1 (5-oz) pkg  romaine lettuce  (about 4 cups) 
 2 cups seasoned  croutons 
 ½ cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 ¾ cup favorite  Caesar dressing 
 Directions : 
 In large mixing bowl, toss lettuce with dressing. 
 Fold in chicken of the sea salmon and parmesan cheese. 
 Evenly divide salad onto four salad plates; top with croutons. 
 Notes : 
 Preparation Time: 10 minutes 
 Makes 4 servings 
 May substitute approximately 2 (6-oz) cans of drained salmon for one pouch. 
 To reduce sodium:
 substitute 8 oz. of fresh salmon that has been poached or baked 
 create your own croutons from old bread 
 use low-sodium salad dressing, or make  your own .
