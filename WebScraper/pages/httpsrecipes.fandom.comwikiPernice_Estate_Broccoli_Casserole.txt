Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Pernice Estate in Mesquite, Texas in 1988.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 boxes frozen  broccoli  chopped, cooked and drained 
 ½ cup  butter 
 4 tablespoons  flour 
 4  chicken bouillon  cubes 
 2 cups  milk 
 Dressing : 
 ½ bag stuffing crumbs 
 ½ cup boiling  water 
 2 tablespoons  butter 
 nuts 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter  then add crumbled bouillon cubes. 
 Add  flour  with  milk  and cook until thick. 
 Dressing : 
 Put crumbs into a bowl then melt  butter  in  water  and pour over crumbs. 
 Add a few nuts and toss with a fork. 
 Put the  broccoli  in the bottom of a buttered dish. 
 Pour cream sauce over. 
 Spoon on stuffing. 
 Sprinkle with more nuts. 
 Bake uncovered at 350°F for 30 minutes.
