 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (3 	ounce) package  cream cheese , softened 
 1/4 	cup  lemon  curd or tangerine curd or  lime  curd 
 1 	tablespoon fat-free half-and-half 
 Fruit : 
 1 	small  honeydew melon , peeled, sliced 
 2 	cups  cantaloupes , peeled, sliced 
 3 	 kiwi fruits , peeled, sliced thin 
 1 	cup fresh  strawberries , hulled and halved 
 lemon peel  (optional) 
 lime  peel (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Combine all dressing ingredients in small bowl with wire whisk. 
 Cover; refrigerate 1t least 30 minutes. 
 Meanwhile, just before serving, arrange fruit on serving platter. 
 Serve with dressing; garnish with  lemon peel  or  lime  peel, if desired.
