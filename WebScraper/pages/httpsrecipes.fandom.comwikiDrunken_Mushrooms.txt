 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds fresh  Mushrooms , washed and quartered 
 6 - 8 cloves fresh  garlic , minced 
 1 sweet  red onion , minced 
 3 tbsp extra virgin  olive oil 
 1 cup fine, dry  red wine 
 1 tbsp soy sauce or Bragg's Liquid Aminos 
 1/2 tbsp each dried  thyme , dried  rosemary  and dried  bouquet garni 
 2 tsp  sea salt 
 dash of freshly ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Put all the  Mushrooms  in a deep casserole dish. Mix together the wine,  olive oil  and all of the herbs. Add the  garlic  and  Onion  and stir well. Pour over the  Mushrooms  and toss to coat. Cover with a tight fitting lid or foil and bake for 25â€“35 minutes at 450 F. Serve hot. 
 
 Serves 4-6
