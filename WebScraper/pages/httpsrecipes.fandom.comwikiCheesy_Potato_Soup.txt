 Ingredients : 
 6 medium  potatoes , peeled, cubed 
 1  onion , very finely chopped 
 2 cans  chicken broth  (14 oz each) 
 Â½ tsp  white pepper 
 1 pkg (8 oz) shredded  American cheese 
 1 cup  half and half 
 Directions : 
 In slow cooker, combine potatoes, onion, chicken broth and white pepper. 
 Cover and cook on low for 8 to 10 hours. 
 With potato masher, mash potatoes in slow cooker. 
 About 1 hour before serving, stir in cheese and cream and cook for 1 more hour.
