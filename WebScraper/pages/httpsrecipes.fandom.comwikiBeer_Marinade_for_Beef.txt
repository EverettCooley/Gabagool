 Ingredients : 
 2 	cup 	 beer  (12 oz or 10 oz cans) 
 2 	tsp 	 salt 
 1/2 	cup 	 olive oil 
 1 	tsp 	Ground  cayenne pepper 
 1 	tbsp 	 wine vinegar 
 1 	tbsp 	Prepared horseradish 
 1 	tsp 	 Onion  powder 
 2 	tbsp 	 lemon juice 
 1 	tsp 	 garlic powder 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together and use as a marinade. 
Then use as a basting sauce for the meat while it cooks.
