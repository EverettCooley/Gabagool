Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Decker Estate in Elmo, Texas in 1999.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4  eggs  beaten 
 ¾ cup  brown sugar 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ¾ cup  light corn syrup 
 ¾ cup  molasses 
 1 tablespoon  butter , melted 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 1½ cups  pecan  halves 
 2 unbaked  pie crusts 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 400°F. 
 Mix eggs, sugar, salt, corn syrup, molasses, butter and vanilla. 
 Add pecans and pour into prepared pie shells. 
 Place in oven and immediately reduce heat to 350°F. 
 Bake for 45 minutes then cool before serving.
