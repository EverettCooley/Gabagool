This is a traditional Sudanese recipe which i learned from Sudanese friends. Its a great alternative to traditional cakes. the sound of the ingredients might put you off, but you have to taste it to believe how nice it is.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups coarse  semolina 
 1 1/2 cups  caster sugar 
 500 ml natural  yoghurt 
 1/2 cup desiccated  coconut 
 3 tablespoons  margarine  or  butter 
 1 teaspoon  baking powder 
 2 cups boiling  water 
 1 teaspoon of some delicious 
 lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 cream  together  semolina ,  margarine 
 mix in  yoghurt , 1 cup  Sugar ,  coconut , and  baking soda . 
 pour mix into deep baking tray. 
 cook for 20 minutes at 180 degrees Celsius. 
 when cool dissolve 1/2 cup  caster sugar  in two cups boiling  water  and  lemon juice . 
 boil until slightly thickened. 
 pour over cooled cake. 
 allow to cool.
