Makes 4 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 1 cup cubed  Ham  (about 3 ounces) 
 1 medium-size green  bell pepper , chopped 
 1/2 cup thinly sliced  green onions 
 1 4- to 6-ounce package  Chicken  flavored seasoned  rice  mix, prepared according to package directions 
 1 15- to 16-ounce can black-eyed  Peas , drained and rinsed 
 1/2 teaspoon hot pepper sauce 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in large skillet over medium-high heat until hot. Add  Ham ,  green pepper  and  onions . Cook and stir 3 to 5 minutes, or until  Ham  is beginning to brown and  Onion  is tender. 
 Stir in  rice , black-eyed  Peas  and hot pepper sauce . Cook 2 to 3 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
