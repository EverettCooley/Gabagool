Description : 
 Know locally as "pan".
 
 Ingredients : 
 4½ cups of  flour 
 1½ cups of  water 
 1 tablespoon of  dry yeast 
 1 teaspoon of  salt 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve the  yeast  in the  water  at a room temperature 80 °F. 
 Add the  flour . 
 Knead for 10 minutes until the mix is thick and sticky. 
 Let the mix rise for 1 hour and 45 minutes 
 Work the mix with your fingers, push it down. 
 Let it rise for another 45 minutes. 
 Form rolls of  bread , slice a hole along the top. 
 Cook at 450 – 480 °F for 20 minutes or until ready.
