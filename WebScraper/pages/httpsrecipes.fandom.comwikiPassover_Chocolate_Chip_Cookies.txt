Ingredients : 
 1 cup  matzo meal 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 1 cup  farfel 
 6 oz  chocolate chips  for Passover 
 1 cup chopped  nuts 
 2  eggs 
 ½ cup Mother's  margarine  melted 
 Directions : 
 chocolate  chip cookies combine matzo meal,  sugar , farfel,  chocolate chips ,  nuts . 
 Beat  eggs  with melted margarine, pour over dry ingredients and mix well. 
 Drop by teaspoon onto cookie sheet 1" apart. 
 Bake at 350 °F for 20 min.
