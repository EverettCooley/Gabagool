Description : 
 Always check the ingredients to make sure the product is vegan.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 head  romaine lettuce 
 ⅓ cup  Nayonaise 
 ¼ cup  olive oil 
 2 cloves  garlic  (crushed) 
 2 tsp whole grain  Dijon mustard 
 1 – 2 tsp  lemon juice  or juice of ½ a  lemon 
 1 – 2 tbsp  maple syrup 
 3 – 4 drops vegan Worcestershire sauce  [1] 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients and blend until smooth and creamy. 
 Wash  lettuce  and tear into bite sized pieces. 
 Pour dressing over  lettuce  and toss. 
 Add croutons if desired. 
 Notes : 
 
 ↑   Traditional  Worcestershire sauce  is not vegan, it contains anchovies
