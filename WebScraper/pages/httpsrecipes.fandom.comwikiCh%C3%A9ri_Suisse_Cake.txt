Description : 
 Chéri Suisse cake is a chocolate cake made with Cheri Suisse, which has a chocolate-cherry flavor
 
 Ingredients : 
 2½ cups  flour 
 3  eggs 
 1 teaspoon  baking powder 
 ½ cup  Chéri Suisse  liqueur 
 1 cup melted  semi-sweet chocolate 
 1 jar of  maraschino cherries 
 1 stick  butter , softened 
 1 cup  dark brown sugar 
 2 teaspoons  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 In a large bowl mix the flour and baking powder and in another bowl mix the eggs, sugar, butter, Cheri Suisse, cherries, chocolate and vanilla. 
 Add this to the dry mixture and blend well. 
 Pour batter into two 9-inch greased cake pans and bake 35 to 40 minutes.
