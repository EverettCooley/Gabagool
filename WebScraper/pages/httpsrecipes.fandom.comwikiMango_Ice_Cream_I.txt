      Mango Ice Cream     
 Ingredients : 
 1 cans  condensed milk 
 12 oz  whipped cream  ( Cool Whip ) 
 1 can  mango  pulp (Alphonso) 
 Directions : 
 Mix all of the pulp,  condensed milk  and  whipped cream  in a bowl. 
 Put in the freezer for about 8 hours.
