 Description : 
       Pancakes Ohukaiset     
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 l  milk  or 2 1/2 dl  cream  and 2 1/2 dl  water  or mineral  water 
 1 1/2 dl  flour 
 2  eggs 
 c. 1 tsp  salt 
 
 
 Directions : 
 1. Mix the  flour  and the  milk . Add the  salt  and beat in the  eggs . Let the batter stand for a minute before frying. 
 2. Fry pancakes on a hot pan, greased with  butter  or  margarine . 
 3. Serve with sweet jam.
 To make a sheet pancake from the same batter, pour into a greased baking pan or frying pan and bake in the oven at 225Â° C until golden brown.
