      Malai Kurma     
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups malai (thick top  cream  of  milk ) 
 2  onions  finely chopped 
 1  tomato  finely chopped 
 10  flakes of garlic 
 1" piece  ginger  peeled 
 5  green chillies 
 1 tbsp  coriander leaves , finely chopped 
 10 - 12  cashew  nuts 
 Â¼ tsp  turmeric powder 
 salt  to taste 
 3 tsp  ghee 
 Directions : 
 Make a smooth paste of  ginger  -  garlic . 
 Break the  cashew   nuts  into small bits. 
 Heat  ghee , fry the  cashew   nuts , drain, and keep aside. 
 Add the chopped  onions , and fry for 2 minutes. 
 Then add the ground paste,  salt , green chillies,  turmeric  powder. 
 Add  tomatoes , cook till  oil  separates from gravy. 
 Add  cream , stir and cook. 
 Simmer for 2 minutes. 
 Garnish with chopped coriander and fried  cashew   nuts . 
 Serve hot with roti or naan.
