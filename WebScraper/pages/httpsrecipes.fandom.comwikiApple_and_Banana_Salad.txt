 Description : 
 Apple  and  banana  Salad
 
 Ingredients : 
 1x  egg , separated 
 2/3cup	 Sugar 
 2tbl	 flour 
 2tbl	 butter 
 1cup	 water 
 1tsp	 vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Beat yolk of  egg , Add  Sugar ,  flour , and  butter  and mix thoroughly. 
 Put in saucepan and put on stove. 
 Add  water  and stir constantly until it boils. 
 Let this cool completely. 
 Add  vanilla  and gently fold in  egg  white which has been stiffly beaten. 
 Pour sauce over diced  Apples  and  bananas .
