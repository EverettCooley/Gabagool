 Ingredients : 
 2 cups shredded  green cabbage 
 2 cups shredded  red cabbage 
 2 cups peeled, shredded  carrots 
 2 cups shredded  celery root   or   daikon 
 1 cup diced  red onion 
 1 cup dried  currants   or   raisins 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp freshly ground  pepper 
 Dressing : 
 1 cup soy or regular  mayonnaise 
 3 tbsp fresh  lemon juice 
 2 tsp prepared white  horseradish 
 2 tsp  rice vinegar   or   balsamic vinegar 
 1½ tsp  dijon mustard 
 1 small clove  garlic , minced 
 Directions : 
 Dressing : 
 In medium bowl. mix all ingredients until well combined. 
 Slaw : 
 In large bowl, combine both cabbages,  carrots ,  celery root ,  red onion  and  currants . 
 Add dressing and toss to coat. 
 Season with  salt  and freshly ground  pepper  to taste and serve.
