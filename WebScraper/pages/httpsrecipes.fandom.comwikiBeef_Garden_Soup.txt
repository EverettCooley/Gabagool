 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound lean ground  Beef  or ground  Turkey  breast meat 
 1 medium  Onion , cut into 1-inch pieces 
 3 medium  carrots , sliced 
 2 ribs  celery , thinly sliced 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 3 cups low-sodium vegetable juice cocktail 
 1 14-1/2-ounce can no  salt  stewed  tomatoes 
 1 6-ounce can no  salt   tomato  paste 
 3  Beef  bouillon cubes or 3 teaspoons bouillon granules 
 3 cups  water 
 2 15-ounce cans  kidney beans , drained and rinsed 
 1 teaspoon  chili powder 
 1/2 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 3 cups cooked  rice , cooked without  salt  or fat 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Beef ,  Onion ,  carrots ,  celery  and  garlic  over medium-high heat in 4-quart Dutch oven until  Beef  is brown, about 5 to 7 minutes. Add vegetable juice cocktail,  tomatoes ,  tomato  paste, bouillon cubes,  water , beans,  chili powder  and  pepper . Bring soup to a boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer 10 minutes. Stir in  rice ; heat thoroughly.
