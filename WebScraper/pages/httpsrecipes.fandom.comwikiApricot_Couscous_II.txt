 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 cup  water 
 2 oz  Apricots , dried, finely 
 -chopped 
 1 tsp  Margarine , nondairy 
 1/3 tsp  Cardamom 
 1 cup  Couscous 
 Directions : 
 1 Combine  water ,  Apricots ,  Margarine  and  Cardamom  in 1 quart casserole dish.
 2 Cover.
 3 Microwave on high for 4 minutes, or until  water  reaches full boil.
 4 Add  couscous .
 5 Cover.
 6 Let stand 5 minutes or until liquid is absorbed.
 7 Serves 5
