 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 bunch of  Chinese cabbage 
 1/2 medium chopped  Onion 
 some dhania or  mint  or  parsley 
 Miti dressing 
 1/2 cup lolo 
 2 tbsp  lemon juice 
 salt 
 a little  chilli 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Wash the  cabbage  very well and shake dry in a cloth, shred the  cabbage  one way and cross cut it to make little diamonds. 
 Put in a bowl and add  Onion . Just before the meal mix well with miti dressing.
