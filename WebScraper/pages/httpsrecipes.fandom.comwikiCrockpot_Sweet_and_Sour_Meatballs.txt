Ingredients : 
 Frozen mini meatballs 
 Two jars of your favorite sweet and sour sauce 
 Directions : 
 Spray crockpot with non-stick cooking spray. 
 Pour frozen mini meatballs into crockpot. 
 Pour sweet and sour sauce over meatballs and stir until meatballs are evenly coated. 
 Cook on low 6 to 8 hours, or until done. 
 Serve with brightly colored toothpicks.
