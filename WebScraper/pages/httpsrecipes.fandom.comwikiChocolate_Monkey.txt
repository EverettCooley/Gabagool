chocolate  Monkey
 
 1/2 Cup  chocolate   milk 
 1 Small  banana  Cut Up 
 1 Tsp.  vanilla 
 2  ice  Cubes 
 
Put All But  ice  Cubes in Blender; Blend Till Smooth; Add  ice  Cubes ThroughThe Hole in The Lid of Blender One by One; Ready When  ice  is Melted;Serve Immediately
