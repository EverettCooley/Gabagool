Ingredients : 
 1 large  sweet onion 
 ½ cup  skim milk 
 ¾ cup plain dried  breadcrumbs 
 1 tsp  chili powder  (optional) 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 1 tbsp vegetable  oil 
 1 tsp vegetable  oil  (more) 
 Directions : 
 Cut  onion  into ¼ inch slices and separate rings. 
 In 9 x 13 pan combine  onion  and  milk . 
 Let stand 30 minutes turning frequently. 
 On wax paper combine next 3 ingredients. 
 Turn  onion  rings into bread crumbs to coat. 
 Spray cookie sheet and place  onion  rings on pan. 
 Drizzle evenly with  vegetable oil . 
 Bake 450°F for 5 minutes on each side. 
 Nutritional information : 
 1 serving = 1 fat, ½ vegetable, 1 bread, 5 optional calories
