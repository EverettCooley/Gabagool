Pasta  Salad al Pesto
 
 
 1 Pound Medium Sized  Pasta , Such as  penne ,  ziti  or  rotini , Cooked in a  spaghetti  Cooker Until Firm But Tender And Drained. Plunge Into Cold  water  Until Chilled. 
 1/2 Cup  sour cream 
 1/2 Cup Cream 
 2 T. Dried  sweet basil  flakes 
 1/2 Cup Grated  Parmesan cheese 
 2 cloves  garlic , Minced 
 4 T.  pine nuts 
 
Place Cooked  Pasta  in a Large Bowl. Combine The Remaining Ingredients in a Food Processor And Blend Until  pine nuts  Are Pureed. Pour Dressing Over Cooked  Pasta  And Toss to Blend. Refrigerate Until Serving Time. Can be Prepared Earlier in The Day And Stored in The Refrigerator. Source: With Love From Darling's Kitchen
