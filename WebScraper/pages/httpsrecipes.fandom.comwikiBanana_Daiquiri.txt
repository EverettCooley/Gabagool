Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  rum 
 ¾ oz  crème de banane 
 splash of  sour mix 
 dash of  simple syrup 
 1  banana 
 Directions : 
 Blend all with a scoop of  ice  until smooth. 
 Garnish with fresh fruit.
