Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 lbs  white chocolate  coating 
 14 oz  sweetened condensed milk 
 1 tsp  vinegar 
 1/4 tsp oil base butter rum flavoring 
 1 1/2 cups roasted & chopped Oregon  hazelnuts 
 Directions : 
 1 Melt  chocolate  in top of double boiler over hot  water . 
2 When melted, add  sweetened condensed milk ; stir, add  vinegar , flavoring and  nuts . 
3 Stir to blend. 
4 Pour into parchment-lined, 9-inch square pan. 
5 Smooth and chill about 2 hours. 
6 Turn onto cutting board, peel off paper and cut into squares. 
7 Refrigerate in tightly covered container.
