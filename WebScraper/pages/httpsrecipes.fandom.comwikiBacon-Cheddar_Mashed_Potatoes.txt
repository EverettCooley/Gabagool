Description : 
 A fair combination of real bacon and mashed potatoes is made with real cheese and eaten as a side dish. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1-1/2 lbs.  potatoes , unpeeled and quartered 
 1/2 cup  salad dressing  (eg. ranch) 
 4 slices  bacon , crisply cooked and crumbled 
 1/4 tsp.  garlic powder 
 1/4 tsp.  salt 
 1/8 tsp.  pepper 
 1/2 cup shredded  Cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Cover potatoes with water in a large saucepan. Bring to a boil; reduce to medium heat. 
 Cook 20 minutes or until tender. 
 Drain and mash potatoes. Add ranch dressing, bacon, garlic powder, salt, and pepper 
 Beat until fluffy. 
 Stir in cheese.
