Ingredients : 
 1 cup  non-fat milk 
 1 ripe  banana 
 1 pack Swiss Miss diet  cocoa  mix 
 Directions : 
 Take one cup nonfat  milk , add a ripe (or overripe)  banana  (or one-half  banana ) and one packet of Swiss Miss diet  hot cocoa mix . 
 Blend in blender. 
 Drink slowly.
