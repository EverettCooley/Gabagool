Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup diced, cooked  chicken 
 1 cup diced  celery 
 1 tbsp  mayonnaise 
 1 tbsp finely chopped  pickles 
 1 cup chopped  hard-boiled egg 
 Directions : 
 Mix well and serve. 
 You may also use tuna fish or ham in place of the chicken.
