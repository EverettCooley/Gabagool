 Makes enough to serve 8. 
 Ingredients : 
 
 20 ounces whole frozen  strawberries , partially thawed 
 12 ounces frozen pink lemonade concentrate 
 ice cubes 
 chilled  club soda 
 Directions : 
 Put berries and lemonade concentrate in blender. 
 Blend until smooth. 
 Add ½ cup puree to a glass. 
 Add ice cubes. 
 Add ¼ cup club soda. 
 Stir gently. 
 Serve.
