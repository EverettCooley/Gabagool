Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Hoyt Estate in Allen, Texas in 1989.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 7 ounces  shell pasta  cooked 
 1 cup  carrots  sliced 
 1 cup  bell peppers  chopped 
 1 can dark  red kidney beans  drained 
 1 can  garbanzo beans  drained 
 1 can diced  tomatoes  drained 
 ½ cup  Parmesan cheese 
 ⅔ cup  Italian salad dressing 
 Directions : 
 In mixing bowl combine cooked pasta, carrots, bell peppers, beans, tomatoes, parmesan cheese and salad dressing. 
 Mix well then chill several hours before serving.
