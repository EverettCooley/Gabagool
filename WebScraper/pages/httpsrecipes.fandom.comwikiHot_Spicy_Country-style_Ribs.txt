Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Morgan Estate in Austin, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4 pounds country-style  pork ribs 
 1 large  white onion  chopped 
 2  garlic  cloves minced 
 1 tablespoons  cooking oil 
 1½ cups  ketchup 
 ⅓ cup  cider vinegar 
 ¼ cup  molasses 
 1 tablespoons bottled  hot pepper sauce 
 2 teaspoon ground  red pepper 
 1 teaspoon  chili powder 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  dry mustard 
 Directions : 
 In a large saucepan cook ribs covered in enough boiling salted water to cover for 1 hour then drain well. 
 Meanwhile in a medium saucepan cook onion and garlic in hot cooking oil until onion is tender or limp but not brown then stir in remaining ingredients. 
 Bring mixture to boiling then reduce heat and simmer uncovered for 15 minutes stirring occasionally. 
 Grill ribs over slow coals for 45 minutes turning every 15 minutes. 
 Brush frequently with sauce.
