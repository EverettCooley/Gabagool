Ingredients : 
 8 oz  blueberry   yogurt 
 8 oz  vanilla   yogurt 
 3/4 cup  blueberries , fresh or frozen 
 2 tbsp grape nuts, granola or other crispy cereal 
 sliced or  slivered almonds 
 Directions : 
 Stir together the yogurt. 
 Add most of the  blueberries  and stir gently. 
 Spoon into dessert dishes. 
 Sprinkle with cereal and  sliced almonds , and garnish with a few  blueberries . 
 Recipe from  sneakykitchen.com
