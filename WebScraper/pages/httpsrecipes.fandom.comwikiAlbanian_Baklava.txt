Ingredients : 
 Â½ kg (about 18 ounces) of ready made rolled dough 
 200 g (about 7 ounces) of pressed  walnuts 
 3 cups of  sugar 
 2 cups of  water 
 Directions : 
 Spread the rolled filo each at the time and brush with melted butter, every 10 filo sprinkle with walnuts, covering with another layer of filo. 
 Cut the baklava in equal squares or diamond shapes. 
 Cover the baklava with melted  butter  and leave it to bake in a hot oven. Afterwards, leave it to cool. 
 Prepare the syrup with  water  and the  Sugar , using the amounts given above. 
 Let the syrup boil for 5 minutes. 
 Before removing the syrup from the heat, add the juice of half a  lemon . 
 Let the syrup cool for about 15/20 minutes, then pour it over baklava.
