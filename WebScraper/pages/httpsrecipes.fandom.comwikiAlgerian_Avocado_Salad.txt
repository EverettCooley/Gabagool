 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2  avocados ; halved 
 1 Red  Onion ; cut in slices 
 1  tomato ; cut in slices 
 salt ; to taste 
 1  lime ; cut in half 
 Directions : 
 1) Cut  avocado  in halves. 
 2) In a big bowl. Place sliced  tomatoes ,  red onions , and  lettuce . 
 3) Sprinkle with  lime  juice,  salt  and  pepper . Toss. Add  avocado  on top.
