Description : 
 This is a recipe I obtained from an Estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Manes Estate in Venus, Texas in 1981.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  mayonnaise 
 ½ cup  chili sauce 
 3 hard cooked  eggs 
 2  dill pickles  chopped 
 ⅓ cup  celery  chopped 
 1  green pepper  minced 
 1 small  white onion  chopped 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together and stir until well combined. 
 Chill at least two hours before using.
