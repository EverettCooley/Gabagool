Contributed by Jenn B aka Mom2Sam and Tiny at  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds  sweet potatoes , cooked 
 2 tablespoons  margarine , melted 
 ½ teaspoons ground  cinnamon 
 16  apricot  halves, dried 
 orange  slices, fresh 
 Directions : 
 Arrange the sweet potatoes in a shallow baking dish. 
 Combine the margarine and cinnamon. 
 Pour over the potatoes. 
 Arrange the apricot halves on top. 
 Cover the dish and bake in a 425°F oven for about 15 minutes. 
 Add the orange slices and serve. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Calories: 185; Carbohydrate: 18 gm; Protein: 3 grams; Fat: 7 grams 
 Exchanges: 1 Bread/Starch; 1 Fruit; 1 Fat
