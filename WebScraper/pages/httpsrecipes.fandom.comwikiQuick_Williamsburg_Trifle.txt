Ingredients : 
 1 Box Yellow Cake Mix 
 2 Lg. Boxes  vanilla  Jell-O Pudding & Pie Filling (notInstant) 
 2 (6-10 Oz.) Boxes Frozen  strawberries 
 Cool Whip 
 Sliced  bananas 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Prepare Cake According to Directions on Box; Cool. 
 Prepare Pudding According to Directions on Box; Cool. 
 Thaw  strawberries . 
 Assemble Approximately 4 Hours Before Serving. 
 Break Cake Into Bite Size Pieces. Place Half of Cake InDeep Dish. 
 Cover With 1 Box  strawberries . 
 Cover With Half of The Pudding. 
 Repeat Layers. 
 Cover And Chill. 
 Just Before Serving, Top With Dollops of  Cool Whip  AndSprinkle With  almonds .
