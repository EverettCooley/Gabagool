 Ingredients : 
 800 g of meat (preferably  beef ) 
 120 g of  butter 
 120 g  red onions 
 40 g of boiled  tomato sauce  (or blanched  tomatoes ) 
 80 g of  carrots 
 20 g of  parsley 
 red pepper 
 Directions : 
 Slice the red onions, carrots and chop the parsley. 
 Cut the meat into small pieces. 
 Heat the  butter  in the pan and add meat and vegetables. 
 Pour  water  into the stew and let it boil. 
 Take the foam of the top, add  salt  and  pepper  and blanched  tomatoes  (or tomato sauce). 
 Fry it gently, until meat is cooked. 
 In the end add red pepper and stir it in. 
 Always serve with plenty of sauce.
