Ingredients : 
 Assorted fresh vegetables 
 2 pkgs. Pillsbury crescent rolls 
 2 (8 oz.) pkgs.  cream cheese 
 1 c. Miracle Whip, or  mayonnaise 
 1 pkg. Hidden Valley ranch dressing mix 
 Directions : 
 Unroll crescent dough and press into jelly roll pan or large cookie sheet (approximately 10 x 15). 
 Bake 10 minutes at 400 degrees. 
 Let cool. 
 Mix cream cheese and mayo. 
 Add powdered dressing. 
 Mix well. 
 Spread on top of baked crust. 
 Cut assorted veggies into small pieces. Use a variety of mixed colors (carrots, celery, broccoli, purple onion, tomato, zucchini, cauliflower, or radishes). 
 Spread over cream cheese mixture and press into it. 
 Chill and cut into squares.
