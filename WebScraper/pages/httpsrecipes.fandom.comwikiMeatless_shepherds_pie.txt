 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup uncooked am  Lentils 
 2 cup  water 
 1  bay leaf 
 1/2 cup am  bulgur wheat 
 1 cup boiling  water 
 1/2 tsp  garlic powder 
 1/4 tsp  sea salt  (optional) 
 1/2 cup  mozzarella   Cheese , grated 
 16 oz canned  tomatoes , drained 
 1 med  Onion , finely chopped 
 1 1/2 cup am  Potato  flakes 
 2 cup boiling  water 
 1/4 tsp  sea salt  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Lentils  with  bay leaf  about 15 minutes or until tender, drain. Remove  bay leaf . While cooking  Lentils , pour boiling  water  over  bulgur wheat ,  garlic powder  and  salt , set aside for 10 minutes. Preheat oven to 350 °F. Chop  Onion  and  tomatoes , grate  Cheese . Layer in the bottom of an oiled 8" x 8" oven dish in the following order:  bulgur wheat ,  Lentils ,  mozzarella   Cheese ,  Onion , and  tomatoes . Mix  Potato  flakes,  water  and  salt  and layer on top of the  tomatoes . Bake for 30 minutes.
