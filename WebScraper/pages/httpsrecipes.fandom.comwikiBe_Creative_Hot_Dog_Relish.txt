Ingredients : 
 Adjust and change to suit your particular tastes: 
 
 onion 
 green tomatoes 
 green  /  red sweet peppers  chopped 
 salt 
 sugar 
 1 tsp  celery seed 
 1 tsp  mustard seed 
 Directions : 
 A combination of the above ingredients, finely chopped. 
 Store in refrigerator in a covered jar. 
 Also good on polish  sausage ,  bratwurst , and  smoked sausages .
