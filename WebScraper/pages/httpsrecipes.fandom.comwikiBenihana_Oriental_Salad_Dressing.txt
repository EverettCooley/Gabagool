From Benihana of Tokyo in Los Angeles.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: my old recipes 
 Makes 1¼ cups 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  soy oil 
 2 tbsp  white vinegar 
 1 tbsp  tomato paste 
 1 tbsp  soy sauce 
 1 tbsp  msg  (optional) 
 ½ tbsp  salt 
 ½ tbsp  ginger 
 ½ cup sliced  celery 
 ¼ cup coarsely chopped  onion 
 Directions : 
 Mix  oil ,  vinegar ,  tomato  paste,  soy sauce ,  msg ,  salt ,  ginger ,  celery  and  onion  in blender container. 
 Blend at medium speed 3 to 4 seconds until  celery  is finely grated.
