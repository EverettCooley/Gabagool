Description : 
 I got a new cookbook from my sister-in-law — Jack Daniel's Cookbook, The Spirit of Tennessee. I'm going to share one.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Makes 20 – 25 Meatballs. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound ground cooked  ham 
 ½ pound  ground fresh pork 
 1½ cups soft  bread crumbs  (about 3 slices) 
 2 tablespoons Jack Daniel's  whiskey , divided 
 2 tablespoons  water 
 1  egg 
 ¼ cup minced  celery 
 2 tablespoons minced  onion 
 ½ teaspoon dry  mustard 
 ¼ teaspoon  pepper 
 ⅛ teaspoon ground  cloves 
 ⅓ cup firmly packed  brown sugar 
 1 tablespoon  prepared mustard 
 ½ tablespoon  vinegar 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Combine  ham ,  pork , crumbs, 1 tablespoon Jack Daniel's  whiskey ,  water ,  egg ,  celery ,  onion , dry  mustard ,  pepper  and cloves. 
 Shape mixture into meatballs (1 tablespoon each). 
 Arrange on baking sheet;bake for 15 minutes or until fully cooked. 
 Make sauce of  brown sugar ,  prepared mustard ,  vinegar  and remaining 1 tablespoon Jack Daniel's  whiskey . 
 Stir over low heat until hot. 
 Use toothpicks to dip cooked meatballs in sauce.
