Description 
 Serves : 9 
 Ingredients 
 3 cups reconstituted  cranberry juice  concentrate 
 1½ teaspoons grated  orange rind 
 1½ cups  orange juice 
 Thin  orange  slices for garnish (optional) 
 Directions 
 Combine reconstituted cranberry juice, orange juice, and orange rind. 
 Freeze in ice cube tray until firm. 
 Spoon frozen mixture into a bowl and beat at low speed, with an electric mixer, until smooth. 
 Spoon into individual dessert cups. 
 Freeze again until ready to serve.
