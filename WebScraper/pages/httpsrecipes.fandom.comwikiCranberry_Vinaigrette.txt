Description : 
 This yummy salad dressing is wonderful on a green salad with blue cheese and toasted nuts.
Prep Time: 5 minutes
Total Time: 5 minutes
 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅓ cup  olive oil 
 ½ cup fresh or frozen  cranberries 
 1 tbsp  wine  or  cider vinegar 
 1 tbsp water 
 salt ,  pepper , and  sweetener  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Put all ingredients in blender and process until smooth. 
 Store in refrigerator.
