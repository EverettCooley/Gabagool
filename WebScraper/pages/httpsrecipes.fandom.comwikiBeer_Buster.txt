 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 	oz 	100-Proof  vodka 
 Cold  beer 
 2 	drops 	 Tabasco sauce 
 Directions : 
 Pour  vodka  into a highball glass and fill with ice-cold  beer . 
Add  Tabasco sauce , stir lightly, and serve.
