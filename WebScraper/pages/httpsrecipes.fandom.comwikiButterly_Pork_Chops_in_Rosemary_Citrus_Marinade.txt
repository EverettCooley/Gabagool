Description : 
 This is a recipe I obtained from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Hager estate in Canton, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  catsrecipes y-group 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  orange juice 
 ¼ cup  lemon juice 
 ¼ cup  lime juice 
 2 tablespoons  oil 
 2 teaspoons crushed  rosemary 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground white  pepper 
 4 large butterfly  pork  chops 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients except  pork  and mix well. 
 Pour over  pork  and refrigerate 8 hours before grilling.
