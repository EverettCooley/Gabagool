 The Austrian snacks seem to resume to sandwiches and dishes that contain one of the many types of sausages, along with mustard. One of the traditional snacks is Bosna or Bosner, which is a breaded spicy bratwurst that resembles of the American hot-dog. This food is part of the menu in every Austrian fast-food restaurant. Other snacks which are enjoyed by the Austrians are:
 
 Geselchtes 
 Heurigenplatte 
 Liptauer 
 Quargel 
 Saumeise 
 Saure Blunzen 
 Schmalzbrot 
 Schweinebraten 
 Surbraten 
 Verhackerts
