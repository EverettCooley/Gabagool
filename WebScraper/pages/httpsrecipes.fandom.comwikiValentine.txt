 Ingredients : 
 2 oz banana liqueur 
 1 oz  vodka 
 3 oz  cranberry juice 
 ice cubes 
 Directions : 
 Shake banana liqueur,  vodka , and  cranberry juice  with ice 
 Strain into a red wine glass.
