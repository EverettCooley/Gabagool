 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 kilogram of  Beef 
 1  Onion , chopped very fine 
 1/2 cup  tomato juice 
 1/2 teaspoon mixed spices 
 cooking  oil 
 salt  and  pepper 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mince the  Beef  coarsely. Place meat,  Onion , mixed spices and seasoning over low flame, stirring often. 
 When the juice has been reabsorbed, add  tomato juice  and cook until the juices are substantially reduced. 
 Add cooking  oil  and cook for 5 minutes.
