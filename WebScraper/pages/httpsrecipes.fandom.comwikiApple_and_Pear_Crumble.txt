 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 medium  Granny Smith apples , peeled, cored and thinly sliced 
 3 medium  Bosc pears , cored and thinly sliced 
 1/4 cup pure  maple syrup 
 1 tsp.  vanilla extract 
 1/2 tsp. ground  cinnamon 
 1/4 tsp. ground  nutmeg 
 1/4 tsp. ground  allspice 
 Nondairy  vanilla  frozen dessert or frozen  yogurt  for serving (optional) 
 Topping 
 1/2 cup whole- Wheat   pastry flour 
 1/2 cup  wheat germ 
 1/3 cup finely chopped  almonds 
 2 Tbs. soy  margarine , melted 
 2 Tbs.  light brown sugar 
 1/4 tsp. ground  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 Lightly coat 13 x 9-inch baking pan with  vegetable oil   cooking spray . 
 In large bowl, mix together  Apples ,  Pears ,  maple syrup ,  vanilla ,  cinnamon ,  nutmeg  and  allspice  until well combined. 
 Pour into prepared baking pan, spreading evenly. 
 Make topping: 
 In small bowl, combine all ingredients and mix well. 
 Sprinkle evenly over fruit mixture. 
 Bake until fruits are soft and topping is golden, 40 to 45 minutes. 
 Let cool on a wire rack until just warm and serve as is or with frozen dessert if desired.
