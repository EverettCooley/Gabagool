Picante  pinto beans  With  Bacon 
 
Ingredients:
 
 1 Cup Pace? Picante Sauce 
 1/4 Cup  ketchup  or Barbecue Sauce 
 1/4 Cup Packed  brown sugar 
 1 Tsp. Ground  cumin 
 4 Slices  Bacon , Cooked And Crumbled 
 2 Cans (about 15 Oz. Each)  pinto beans , Rinsed And DrainedSliced Jalape?o  pepper 
 
Directions:
 
Mix Picante Sauce,  ketchup ,  sugar ,  cumin ,  Bacon  And beans in Saucepan.Heat Through. Garnish With Jalape?o  pepper .
 
Serves 6.
