 A TNT recipe
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: my old recipes 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tbsp dry  spaghetti sauce  mix 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 1 tbsp finely chopped  green pepper 
 Directions : 
 Stir  spaghetti  sauce mix into  sour cream  with finely chopped  green pepper . 
 Chill. 
 Serve as dip for crisp veggies or  corn  chips.
