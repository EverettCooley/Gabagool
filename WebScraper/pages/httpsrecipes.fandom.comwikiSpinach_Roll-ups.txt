Ingredients : 
 16 oz softened  cream cheese 
 ¼ cup  mayonnaise 
 ¼ cup  sour cream 
 1 packet dry ranch dressing mix 
 4 – 5 slices crisp-cooked, crumbled  bacon 
 2 (10 oz) pkg frozen  spinach , thawed and drained as dry as possible 
 8 large flour  tortillas 
 Directions : 
 Combine everything but tortillas. 
 Spread over tortillas all the way to the edges. 
 Don't layer too thickly or the filling will leak out the ends. 
 Roll up tightly wrap in plastic wrap. 
 Chill for at least an hour. 
 Slice into 1" pieces.
