Description : 
 In Romanian: Salata de cartofi III
 
 Ingredients : 
 14 oz / 400 g  potatoes 
 2  onions 
 1  herring 
 2 oz / 50 g  black olives 
 2 tablespoons  oil 
 1 tablespoon  vinegar 
 salt  (if needed) 
 Directions : 
 Wash, boil, peel and let cool the  potatoes . 
 Then slice and mix with the boned, desalted and thinly sliced  herring , with the olives, chopped  onions ,  oil  and  vinegar .
