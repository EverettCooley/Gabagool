Description : 
 Hunky Soup
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  beef chuck , in one piece 
 1-2 soup bones with marrow 
 5 qts cold  water 
 3 tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  black peppercorns 
 6 whole  carrots , cleaned 
 3 ribs of  celery , cut in half 
 3 medium  onions  washed but unpeeled 
 3  potatoes , peeled and halved 
 Noodle's - your preference (we use wide egg noodles) 
 Directions : 
 Sear meat in pot. 
 (this gives greater flavor and color). 
 Add soup bone(s) 
 Cover with  water . 
 Let it come to a boil and skim the foam off. 
 Add  salt ,  peppercorns , and let simmer for 1 hour. 
 Add the  carrots ,  celery ,  and  onions . 
 Cover and simmer slowly for about 2 hours. 
 Add potatoes last hour of cooking. 
 Boil the noodles to your taste. Keep separate from the soup. 
 Bring the noodles and the "soup" together at the table. 
 Heinz Ketchup to taste!  :)
