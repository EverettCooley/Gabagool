Description : 
 Among the many kebab dishes, Seafood kebab remains one of the most loved dishes all around the globe. Commonly served in restaurants and resorts close to the sea, with the likes of  Berjaya Hotel Colombo , it is a succulent and juicy dish that will certainly tantalize oneâ€™s taste buds. Find below an easy recipe to prepare this dish from the comfort of your home.
 
 Ingredients : 
 cuttlefish,  300 g. 
 prawns, 300 g. 
 crabs  300 g. 
 green bell pepper  20 g. 
 3 tablespoons  chili powder 
 crushed  garlic  20 g. 
 lemongrass  20 g. 
 ginger , 20 g. 
 coriander powder, 10 g. 
 cumin, 10 g. 
 3 tablespoons  lime juice 
 4 teaspoons black  pepper 
 jaggery,  20 g. 
 2 cups  onions,  red or white only 
 coriander, 10 g. 
 2 teaspoons salt 
 Directions : 
 Make a paste with the crushed  garlic ,  lemongrass ,  ginger , black  pepper ,  salt , and lime juice. 
 Marinate the seafood with the paste and leave for 15-30 minutes before skewering the pieces. Grill the kebabs one by one. 
 Stir with the  jaggery  and cook for 10 minutes. Add  salt , red  pepper , and  lemongrass  to taste. 
 Glaze the sauce over the cooked kebabs or serve it separately but remember to serve the kebabs hot!
