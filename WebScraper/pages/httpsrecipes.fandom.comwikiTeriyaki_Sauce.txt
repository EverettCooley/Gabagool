 Description : 
 Homemade  teriyaki sauce . Pour over  beef ,  poultry  or  shrimp .
Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: my old recipes 
 Makes 1 cup       Teriyaki Sauce     
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  soy sauce 
 ⅓ cup  oil 
 2 tbsp.  light corn syrup 
 2 tbsp.  white wine vinegar 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1 tbsp.  ginger 
 1 tbsp. dry  mustard 
 1 teaspoon sake, optional 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients in a small mixing bowl. Pour 
 Refrigerate covered for at least 8 hours. 
 Use as a dip or marinade. Reserve the remaining teriyaki sauce in the refrigerator.
