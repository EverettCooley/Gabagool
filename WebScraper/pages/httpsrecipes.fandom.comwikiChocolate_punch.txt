 Description : 
 Recipe by: spoonbread &  strawberry  wine - isbn 0-385-47270-6 
 Yield: 12 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 4 1-oz sq  semisweet chocolate 
 1/2 cup  Sugar 
 2 cup Hot  water 
 2 quart  milk 
 1 1/2 tsp  vanilla extract 
 1 quart vanilla  ice cream 
 1 quart  club soda 
 1/2 pint  heavy cream , whipped 
 Ground  cinnamon 
 
 
 Directions : 
 In a large saucepan combine the  chocolate  and  Sugar  with the hot  water . 
 Bring to a boil, stirring for 2 mins. 
 Add  milk , and continue heating. 
 When hot, beat in  vanilla  with a rotary  egg  beater or whisk. 
 Remove from heat. 
 Chill, then pour into a punch bowl over  ice cream . 
 For sparkle, add  club soda . 
 Top with  whipped cream  and dust with  cinnamon .
