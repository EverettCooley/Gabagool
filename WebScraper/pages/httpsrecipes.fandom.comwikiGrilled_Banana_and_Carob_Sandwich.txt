 Description : 
 This colorful recipe has two ingredients:  carob and banana. A brand-new classic, it is great for breakfast or dessert,
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 small  banana 
 small handful of  carob chips   or  (other vegan  chocolate ) 
 vegan  margarine 
 2 slices of vegan bread 
 Directions : 
 Cut the banana into slices and arrange on one slice of vegan bread. 
 Sprinkle carob over bananas and top with the second slice of vegan bread. 
 Heat a skillet over medium heat. 
 Add about a teaspoon of the margarine to the pan and wait for it to melt. 
 Once the margarine is melted, put the sandwich into the pan. 
 Fry both sides until golden brown and the carob is melted.
