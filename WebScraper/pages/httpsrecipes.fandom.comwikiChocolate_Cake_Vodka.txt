 Ingredients : 
 ½ oz  Frangelico   hazelnut liqueur 
 ½ oz  vodka 
 Directions : 
 Add  vodka  and  frangelico   hazelnut liqueur  to a shaker with  ice . 
 Shake. 
 Garnish with a sugar-coated  lemon . 
 Shoot the drink, like you would  tequila .
