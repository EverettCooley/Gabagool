 Description : 
 Another great salad from North Africa! Extremely healthy and easy to prepare, this has to one of the best.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2Â lb raw beetroot 
 1  red onion , finely chopped 
 2  garlic  cloves, minced 
 4 ripe  tomatoes 
 1 tablespoon chopped fresh  cilantro 
 1 tablespoon chopped fresh  parsley 
 1  black olives ; to garnish 
 2 tablespoons  balsamic vinegar 
 1/2 cup  olive oil 
 1/2 teaspoon harissa 
 salt 
 freshly ground  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Firstly, trim and cook the beetroot. It should be placed in boiling salted  water  over a medium-high heat for 1 hour. When it's done, remove the skins in cold  water . 
 Slice the beetroot and put it in a medium bowl with the  Onion  and  garlic . 
 Put the  vinegar ,  olive oil , harissa,  salt  and  pepper  in a bowl and stir vigorously. When the mixture is even, pour half of the dressing over the beetroot and toss. 
 Slice or dice the  tomatoes  and put them in a bowl with the rest of the dressing and mix gently. Serve this to one side of the beetroot and sprinkle the salad with chopped  cilantro  and  parsley , add the  black olives  and serve. 
 This recipe serves 4.
