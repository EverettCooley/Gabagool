Description : 
 Fried lamb burgers
 
 Ingredients : 
 500 g (1 lb 2oz)  minced lamb 
 1 bunch  parsley , finely chopped (stem discarded) 
 1  garlic  clove, finely chopped 
 1 large  onion , finely chopped 
 2  eggs , beaten 
 100 g (4 oz)  plain flour 
 1 tablespoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon  baking powder 
 3 teaspoons  turmeric 
 2 teaspoons ground  cumin 
 1 tablespoons  garam masala 
 700 ml (1¼ pint)  water 
 100 ml (3½ oz)  oil , for frying 
 Directions : 
 Place chopped  parsley  in a large bowl and add chopped  onions ,  garlic ,  salt ,  cumin ,  turmeric ,  garam masala ,  baking powder  and  flour  then add meat and mix. 
 Beat the  eggs  in a medium mixing bowl. 
 Pour the beaten  eggs  to the mixture, add the  water  and mix well. Cover and set aside for 5 minutes. 
 Place the  oil  in a frying pan and heat for 2–3 minutes on medium heat. Using a tablespoon scoop from the mixture and fry, making round shapes, frying 2 – 3 minutes on each side until golden brown and slightly crisp.
