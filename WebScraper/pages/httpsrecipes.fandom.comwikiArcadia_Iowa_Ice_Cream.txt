ARCADIA IOWA ICE CREAM 
 5 eggs 
2 1/2 cup sugar 
Beat until of cake batter consistency 
 Add: 2 tall cans Carnation Milk 
2 cartons half and half (or 1 carton whipped cream) 
1 1/2 T. vanilla 
 Put in freezer, and add enough milk to fill container 3/4 full.
 continue as per your ice cream freezer directions
