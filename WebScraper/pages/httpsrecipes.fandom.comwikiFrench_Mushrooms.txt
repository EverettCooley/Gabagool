French  Mushrooms 
 
 1/2 Lb. Small  Mushroom  Caps 
 Juice of 1/2  lemon 
 salt 
 pepper 
 
 1 T.  olive oil 
 1 T. Prepared  mustard 
 1 T.  parsley , Chopped 
 
Place All Ingredient But The  mustard  And  parsley  in Pan. Cover And Cook Over High Heat For 10 Minutes. Shake Often. Uncover The Pan And Set Aside to Cool. When Cold, Remove The  Mushrooms  From The Juice And Blend in The  mustard . Pour The Marinade Over The  Mushrooms , Garnish With Fresh  parsley , Serve Cold.
