Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 ½ cup sliced fresh  mushrooms 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 2 cups hot cooked  rice 
 2 cups cooked  chicken  breast cubes 
 2 cups chopped fresh  broccoli , steamed 
 1 x 10¾-ounce can condensed  cream of mushroom  soup 
 ½ cup (2 ounces) shredded  Cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Cook  onion  and  mushrooms  in  butter  in large skillet until tender. 
 Stir in  rice ,  chicken ,  broccoli  and condensed soup. 
 Pour into buttered shallow 1½-quart baking dish; top with  cheese . 
 Bake at 350°F for 20 to 25 minutes.
