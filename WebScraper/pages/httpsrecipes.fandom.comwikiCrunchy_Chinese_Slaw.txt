Description : 
 "This colorful salad is made with fresh  Chinese cabbage ,  broccoli , red  radishes  tossed with slaw dressing and garnished with  Chinese noodles ." 
 
 Original recipe yield: 2 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  Chinese cabbage  (napa or savoy), shredded 
 1 cup  broccoli , broken into small florets 
 ½ cup canned  water chestnuts , drained 
 ½ cup red  radishes , thinly sliced 
 ¼ cup  green onions , chopped 
 1 cup  chinese noodles , divided 
 ¼ teaspoon  ginger 
 ½ cup Marzetti® slaw dressing 
 Directions : 
 Add the  cabbage ,  broccoli ,  chestnuts ,  radishes , and  onions  together in a bowl. 
 Cover with the slaw dressing mixed with  ginger  and serve.
