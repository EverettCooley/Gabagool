Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 oz Malibu ®   coconut rum 
 3 tbsp  sugar 
 milk 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Add Malibu ®   rum ,  sugar  and your desired amount of  ice  to a blender. 
 Fill with  milk , and blend until smooth with a frothy head. 
 Serve in glasses immediately.
