      Apples with Wine Sauce     
 Ingredients : 
 2 kg big  apples 
 200 g  sugar 
 Sauce : 
 1 bottle dry  white wine 
 12  yolks 
 200 g  sugar 
 vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Peel  apples , remove the core through the hollow of stem. 
 Fill this hollow with  sugar  and bake in a preheated oven until done (you are able to cut with fork). 
 Sauce:. 
 Beat  yolks  with  sugar ,  vanilla  and add hot wine. 
 (warning* do not double recipe unless you have a really big bowl). 
 Put the bowl with sauce in a pan with hot  water  and whip until dense. 
 Serve  apples  and the sauce cooled down.
