 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pint basket California  strawberries , stemmed 
 2 medium  bananas , peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces 
 3 cups  ice  cubes, divided 
 1/2 cup nonfat  strawberry  or plain  yogurt 
 1/2 cup  orange juice 
 2 tablespoons  honey 
 
 
 Directions : 
 In a blender combine all ingredients except whole berry; blend smooth. Pour into stemmed glass. Garnish with whole berry.
