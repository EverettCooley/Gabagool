Description : 
 This is a variation of the  Pepper Jack   Potato  Omelet. I perfected this recipe in 1990. It is a mainstay and served at least once a week. 
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 large  new potatoes , peeled and quartered 
 ½ pound  pepper   jack cheese , grated 
 2  eggs , beaten 
 2 tablespoons unsalted  butter 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 2 teaspoons freshly ground  black pepper 
 2 teaspoons  mustard 
 1 teaspoon  cayenne pepper 
 1 fresh  jalapeño , finely chopped 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  horseradish 
 Directions : 
 Boil and mash  potatoes . 
 Mix  potatoes ,  cheese ,  eggs  and  butter  together. 
 Stir in  salt ,  pepper ,  mustard ,  cayenne pepper , jalapeño and horseradish. 
 Pour into a buttered dish and bake at 350 °F for 15 minutes.
