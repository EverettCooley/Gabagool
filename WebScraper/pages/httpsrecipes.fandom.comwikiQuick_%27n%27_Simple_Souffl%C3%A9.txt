Descripton : 
 Try with fruit  yogurt . Makes 2 large or 4 small servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tbsp  oil  (or non-stick spray) 
 3  eggs , well beaten  or   egg replacer 
 ½ cup  milk  or  milk  substitute 
 1 tbsp  honey 
 ½ tsp  vanilla extract 
 ½ cup am multi blend  flour 
 2 tsp non- alum   baking powder 
 ¼ tsp  nutmeg 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 450 °F. 
 Oil a 9" x 9" cake pan. 
 Mix liquid ingredients together. 
 Mix dry ingredients together. 
 Combine mixtures. 
 Pour batter into pan and bake 15 minutes. 
 Serve while hot with  honey  or syrup and/or fruit.
