Ingredients : 
 5 lbs  all-purpose flour 
 1 qt  milk 
 1 cup liquid  butter  ( ghee ) 
 5 tbsp  poppy seeds 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 Directions : 
 Combine dry ingredients, and add wet ingredients. 
 Roll into balls and bake until golden brown.
