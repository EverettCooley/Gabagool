 Description : 
 Spanish flag cod
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ kilo of  salt cod  (not dogfish) 
 tomato sauce  made with ½ kilo of  tomatoes 
 Mayonnaise : 
 2  eggs 
 1 cup  oil 
 Directions : 
 Soak the cod for 12 hours changing the water after 6 hours. 
 Bring some water to a boil and add the cod for 2 minutes. 
 Make a tomato sauce and mayonnaise. 
 Arrange a plate with tomato sauce on one side, cod in the middle and the mayonnaise on the other side. 
 Serve with boiled potatoes, boiled onions or white rice.
