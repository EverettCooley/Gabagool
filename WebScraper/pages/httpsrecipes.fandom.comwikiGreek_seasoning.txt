 About Greek seasoning : 
 Greek seasoning is a blend of spices which is often used in cooking (though probably not in Greece!). The exact mixture varies from cook to cook and brand to brand for the commercial brands, but typically includes:
 
 oregano 
 basil 
 thyme 
 mint 
 garlic 
 onion 
 parsley
