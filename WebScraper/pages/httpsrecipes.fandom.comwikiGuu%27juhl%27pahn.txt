구절판
Nine-sectioned lacquerware dish filled with mixtures of meat, seasoned vegetables, eggs, and buckwheat pancakes or thinly sliced pickled radish in the middle.
 Diners wrap the ingredients to their taste in the pancakes or pickled radish.
