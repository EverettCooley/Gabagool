Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: My old recipes 
 Makes about 1 cup 
 Ingredients : 
 2 envelopes Lipton  cream of chicken  or  cream of mushroom  Cup-a-Soup 
 1 cup boiling water 
 Directions : 
 In bowl, blend 2 envelopes Lipton  cream of chicken  or  cream of mushroom  Cup-a-Soup with 1 cup boiling water. 
 Serve over hot cooked noodles, rice, beef, fish, or poultry. 
 Variation : 
 Add 1 of the following: 
 
 2 tbsp  white wine ,  or 
 1 tbsp  sherry   or 
 2 tsp  parmesan cheese   or 
 ½ tsp  dill weed   or 
 1 hard cooked  egg , chopped and ½ tsp  parsley .
