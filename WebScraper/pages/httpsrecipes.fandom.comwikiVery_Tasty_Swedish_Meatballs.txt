      Very Tasty Swedish Meatballs     
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  breadcrumbs 
 ⅔ cup  milk 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 ½ cup finely chopped  onions 
 1½ lbs  ground beef 
 3  eggs , slightly beaten 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon fresh ground  pepper 
 1 teaspoon  nutmeg 
 1 teaspoon  paprika 
 3 tablespoons  butter 
 3 tablespoons  flour 
 1 tablespoon  beef broth 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 Directions : 
 Melt 1 tbsp  butter  in skillet and sauté  onion  over low. 
 Mix bread crumbs,  ground beef  and  onions . 
 Add  eggs ,  salt ,  pepper ,  nutmeg , and  paprika . 
 Shape the meat into small balls, dusting hands with  flour  frequently. 
 Melt the remaining 3 tbsp of  butter  in a large skillet over medium heat. 
 Add meat balls and fry . 
 Stir in 3 tbsp of  flour  and stir well until blended. 
 Add  beef broth  and a bit of  pepper . 
 Cook, stirring constantly until thickened. 
 Cook about 5 minutes to get rid of the raw flour taste. 
 Stir in  sour cream  a little at a time. 
 Return meat balls to pan. Cover pan and simmer for 5-10 minutes. 
 Season to taste and serve immediately.
