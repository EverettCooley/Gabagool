Description : 
 These make great party snacks. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup sliced  green onions 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 1 cup shredded  monterey jack  cheese 
 1 cup shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 1 (4 ounces) can  mushroom  stems and pieces, drained 
 ½ cup chopped  ripe olives 
 ½ cup chopped  pimento -stuffed  olives 
 1 loaf  rye cocktail bread 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl, combine  green onions ,  mayonnaise , cheeses,  mushrooms  and  olives . 
 Spread on bread slices and place on ungreased baking sheets. 
 Bake at 350 °F for 8–10 minutes or until bubbly.
