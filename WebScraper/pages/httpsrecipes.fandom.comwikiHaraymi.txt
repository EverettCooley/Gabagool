 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 pieces of fish 
 ½ spoon  salt 
 ½ chopped  garlic 
 ½ spoon kammon hoot 
 lemon juice 
 ½ spoon  chilli 
 Directions : 
 Marinate the fish in  lemon ,  salt , kammon and  garlic . Put some  oil  in pan heat it up until it sizzles then put  onions ,  garlic  and heat till coloured. Add 3 spoon  tomato  paste and  tomato juice . Leave for 5 minutes then add  salt , kammon and  water . After 10 minutes. Add the marinated fish to the mixture and heat up for 15 minutes.
