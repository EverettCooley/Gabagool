 Recipe by: YM Rect. Jofin Parker, Camp LeJeune 
 Ingredients : 
 1 gallon  apple cider 
 1 cup  light brown sugar 
 6 oz can frozen  lemonade 
 6 oz can frozen  orange juice 
 12 whole  cloves 
 6 whole  allspice 
 1 tsp ground  nutmeg 
 1 â€“ 4  cinnamon  sticks 
 Directions : 
 In large crockpot, combine cider, sugar, lemonade and orange juice. 
 Tie cloves and allspice into a small piece of cloth or empty tea bag and add to cider. 
 Add nutmeg and cinnamon sticks. 
 Simmer gently until heated thoroughly. 
 Do not boil. 
 Remove spice bag and discard. 
 Serve.
