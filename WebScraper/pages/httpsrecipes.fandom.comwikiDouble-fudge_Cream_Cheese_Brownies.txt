Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  butter 
 4 (1 oz) squares  unsweetened chocolate 
 2 cups  sugar 
 1½ cups  flour 
 4  eggs , slightly beaten 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 1 tsp  salt 
 2 tsp  vanilla 
 1 cup  semi-sweet chocolate  chips 
 Filling : 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 2 tsp  butter , softened 
 3 oz pkg.  cream cheese , softened 
 1  egg 
 1 tbsp  flour 
 ½ tsp  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 350°F. 
 In 2 quart saucepan combine 1 cup butter and unsweetened chocolate. 
 Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until melted (4 – 6 minutes). 
 Stir in remaining ingredients, except chocolate chips. 
 Fold in chocolate chips. 
 Spread half of batter into greased 9 x 13 inch pan. 
 In a small bowl combine the filling. 
 Filling : 
 Spread filling over brownie mixture. 
 Spoon remaining batter over cream cheese (batter will not entirely cover cream cheese mixture) 
 Bake 30 – 35 minutes or until brownies pull away from sides of pan.
