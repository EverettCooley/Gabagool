Description : 
 Pani means  water 
 Puri is small ping-pong ball sized, deep fried, puffed breads, also called gol gappa or puchkas 
 The mix is a blend of flavored  water 
 Ingredients : 
 powdered coriander 
 cumin 
 salt 
 citric acid 
 cumin 
 chilies 
 Directions : 
 The puffs are stuffed with  chickpeas  and filled with  water .
