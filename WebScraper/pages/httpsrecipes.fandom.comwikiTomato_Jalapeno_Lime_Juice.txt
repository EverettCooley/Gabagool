Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 	cups 	 tomato juice , chilled 
 1/2 	cup 	crushed  ice 
 1 		jalapeno, seeded and chopped 
 juice of  lime 
 Directions : 
 Place the  ice  in a blender. 
Add the jalapeno and blend. 
Add the  tomato juice  and  lime juice . 
Blend until combined.
