Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz  bourbon 
 3 oz  club soda 
 2 tsp  water 
 1 tsp  superfine sugar 
 ½ oz tawny  port 
 ⅛ tsp grated  nutmeg 
 ⅛ tsp grated  cinnamon 
 garnish:  lemon  twist 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve the  Sugar  in a  water  and  Bourbon  in a highball glass. 
 Fill the glass with  ice  and add the  club soda . 
 Float the  port  on top. 
 Garnish with a  lemon  twist and dust with  nutmeg  and  cinnamon .
