Description : 
 Recipe by: Art Ginsburg (Mr. Food) 
 
 preparation time: 0:20 
 Ingredients : 
 pasta 
 1 tbsp  Italian seasoning 
 2 clove  garlic , crushed 
 ½ tsp red pepper, crushed 
 1 x 8 oz can  tomato sauce 
 ¼ cup non-fat  Parmesan cheese 
 1 x 15 oz can stewed  tomatoes 
 2 cups any combination vegetables ( celery ,  onions ,  red  and  green bell peppers ,  carrots , or  spinach ) 
 ½ cup  peas 
 1 cup non-fat  mozzarella  cheese, shredded 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven at 400°F. 
 In a pot of boiling  water , cook  pasta  until just underdone; drain. 
 Place  pasta  in a 9"x13" baking dish that has been sprayed with  cooking spray ; toss with  Italian seasoning ,  garlic ,  crushed red pepper ,  tomato  sauce, and  Parmesan cheese . 
 Pour stewed  tomatoes  over the top, then layer remaining ingredients, ending with the  mozzarella  cheese. 
 Bake 25 to 30 minutes.
