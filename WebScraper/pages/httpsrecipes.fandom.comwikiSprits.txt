Description : 
 Sprits  is a typically Dutch cookie that usually is served with a cup of  coffee .
 
 Ingredients : 
 250 grams of  butter  or  margarine 
 150 grams of  Sugar 
 2 satchels of  vanilla sugar 
 2  egg whites 
 3 tblsp  milk 
 375 grams  flour 
 Directions : 
 Cream the soft butter with the sugar and vanilla sugar 
 Beat the egg whites until stiff and fold them into the butter mixture, together with the milk. 
 Finally blend in the flour. 
 Put the dough into a piping bag and pipe the dough into waves of 6 or 7 cms length onto a greased baking sheet. 
 Bake the sprits in the middle of a preheated oven at 180C until golden.
