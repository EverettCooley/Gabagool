Description : 
 Contributed by Lilly at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 8 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tsp instant  coffee  granules 
 ¼ cup hot  water 
 1 (7-oz) jar  marshmallow crème 
 2 cups  whipping cream 
 ¼ cup  hazelnut liqueur 
 ¼ cup chopped toasted  hazelnuts 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve  coffee  in  water . 
 Add  coffee  to  marshmallow crème  in lg bowl, mixing until well blended. 
 Add  whipping cream  and liqueur; mix well. 
 Pour into 9" sq pan; freeze until almost firm. 
 Coarsely chop mixture; spoon into chilled bowl. 
 Add  hazelnuts . 
 Beat with electric mixer until smooth; freeze.
