Serve over  pound cake  or  Jell-O .
 
 Dessert Recipes  by the South Carolina Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe 
 Ingredients 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 heaping tbsp Adluh®  flour 
 3  eggs 
 1 can  evaporated milk 
 1 can  water 
 1 tsp.  vanilla 
 Directions 
 Mix sugar and flour. 
 Stir in eggs and beat well. 
 Add milk and water. 
 Stir. 
 Cook in double boiler, stirring constantly. Custard is done when it coats spoon. Do not overcook. 
 Add vanilla.
