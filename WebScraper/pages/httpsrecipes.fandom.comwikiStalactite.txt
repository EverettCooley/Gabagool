Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1⅛ oz  sambuca 
 ¼ oz  Irish cream liqueur 
 ¼ oz  black raspberry liqueur 
 Directions : 
 Pour  sambuca  into cordial glass. Float Irish  cream  on top of  sambuca . 
 Carefully pour black  raspberry liqueur , drop by drop, as top layer. 
 The  raspberry liqueur  will pull the Irish  cream  through the  sambuca  and will settle on the bottom.
