Description : 
 Makes 8 servings.
 NOTE: Can be prepared ahead and refrigerated or frozen. If frozen, thaw in refrigerator overnight before baking.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups cooked  rice 
 2 cups  sour cream 
 2 teaspoons dried  oregano 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 Â½ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1 (4 ounce) can chopped  green chilis 
 8 ounces (2 cups) shredded  Cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl, combine  rice ,  sour cream  and seasonings. 
 Fold in chopped green chiles and  cheese . 
 Pour mixture into a 9 x 13-inch baking dish which has been sprayed with nonstick spray. 
 Bake for 20 to 30 minutes, until heated through.
