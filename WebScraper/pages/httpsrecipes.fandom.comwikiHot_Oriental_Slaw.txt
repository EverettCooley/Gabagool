Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves: 4 
 Ingredients : 
 1 small head  cabbage  (about 1 pound) 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 1 tbsp  cornstarch 
 2 tbsp  vinegar 
 1 tsp  soy sauce 
 1 tbsp grated  gingerroot  or 1 ts ground ginger 
 1 clove  garlic , finely chopped 
 1 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 2  green onions  (with tops),thinly sliced (about 2 tablespoons) 
 1 medium  carrot , shredded 
 1 medium  bell pepper , chopped (about 1 cup) 
 Instructions : 
 Prepare shredded cabbage as directed. 
 Mix sugar, cornstarch, vinegar, soy sauce, gingerroot and garlic; reserve. 
 Heat wok or 12-inch skillet until hot. 
 Add oil and tilt wok to coat side. 
 Add cabbage, onions, carrot and bell pepper. 
 Stir-fry 3 minutes. 
 Stir in vinegar mixture. 
 Cook and stir about 10 seconds or until thickened.
