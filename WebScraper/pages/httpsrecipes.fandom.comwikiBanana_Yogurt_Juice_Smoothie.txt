Description : 
 A great fruit smoothie
 
 Ingredients : 
 2  bananas , peeled, wrapped in plastic wrap & frozen 
 1/2 c.  Apple  or  orange juice 
 1 c. plain  yogurt 
 1 tbsp.  Honey 
 2 or 3  ice  cubes 
 Directions : 
 Blend all ingredients at high speed in blender. Pour into glasses and serve cold.
