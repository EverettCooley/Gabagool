Description : 
 Chicken in Soya Sauce
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  chicken , cut into bite size pieces 
 5  shallots 
 3 cloves  garlic 
 2 cm  ginger 
 4 tbsp thick  soy sauce 
 1 large  onion , sliced into rings 
 1  lime 
 2 tsp  turmeric powder 
 salt  to taste 
 for garnish:  red pepper  (julienne),  coriander leaves  (chopped) 
 Directions : 
 Marinate  chicken  with  salt  and  turmeric  powder. 
 Grind,  shallots ,  garlic  and  ginger . 
 Heat  oil  in wok and fry  chicken  pieces until crispy. 
 Set aside. 
 In 2 tbsp of  oil , fry ground  shallots ,  garlic  and  ginger  until fragrant. 
 Add  soy sauce  and  chicken  pieces and mix well. 
 Add  onion  rings and queeze  lime juice  just before removing from heat. 
 If you want more gravy, add  water  and bring to boil. 
 Garnish with red pepper and  coriander leaves . 
 Serve hot with  rice .
