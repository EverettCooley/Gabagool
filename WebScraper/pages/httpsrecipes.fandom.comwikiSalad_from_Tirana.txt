 Ingredients : 
 1  romaine lettuce 
 1 bunch of  green onions , chopped 
 2 hard-boiled  eggs , sliced 
 salt  and  pepper 
 olive oil 
 lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Carefully wash the  romaine lettuce  and chop it up into pieces. 
 Combine  lettuce , chopped  green onion  and  egg  slices in a salad bowl. 
 Add  salt  and  pepper  to taste. 
 Right before serving, add  olive oil  and  lemon juice  to taste.
