Description : 
 Bun  is also known as  rice sticks ,  rice vermicelli  and is described as a noodle salad, which can be served as a snack.
 
       Bun     
 Ingredients : 
 cucumber 
 rice vermicelli 
 lettuce  leaves 
 bean sprouts 
 basil 
 coriander 
 mint 
 oregano 
 lime juice 
 nuoc 
 Directions : 
 Prepare the  cucumber , cut it, add some  rice sticks  and cook them for 4 minutes. 
 Toss  bean sprouts ,  cucumber  herbs, the noodles and  lime juice .
