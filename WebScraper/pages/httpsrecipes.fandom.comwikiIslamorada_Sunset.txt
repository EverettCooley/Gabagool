 Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 oz.  Conch  Republic Islamorada  light rum 
 1 1/2 oz.  cranberry juice 
 1 1/2 oz.  orange juice 
 Directions : 
 Fill a goblet size glass with crushed  ice . Add all ingredients and shake well. Garnish with a slice of  orange .
