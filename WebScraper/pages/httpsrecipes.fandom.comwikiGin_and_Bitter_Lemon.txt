      Description     
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 oz  gin 
 1/2 oz  lemon juice 
 1/2 tsp  Superfine sugar 
 4 oz  tonic water 
 Directions : 
 Pour into a shaker some  ice  cubes, add the  gin ,  lemon juice  and  Sugar . After shaking, strain into a highball glass and add more  ice  cubes. Top with the  tonic water .
