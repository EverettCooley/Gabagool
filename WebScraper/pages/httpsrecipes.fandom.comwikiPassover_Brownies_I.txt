Ingredients : 
 ½ lbs  butter   or   shortening 
 2  semi-sweet baking chocolate  bars 
 1 cup chopped  nuts 
 4  eggs 
 1½ cup  matzo cake meal 
 2 cup  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Melt together  shortening  and  chocolate  over hot  water . 
 Beat  eggs , add  sugar  gradually and beat until thick. 
 Add  chocolate  and  shortening  and blend well. 
 Fold in cake meal and  nuts . 
 Pour into a greased 8" square pan. 
 Bake at 350 °F for 30 to 45 min. 
 Cool. 
 Cut into squares.
