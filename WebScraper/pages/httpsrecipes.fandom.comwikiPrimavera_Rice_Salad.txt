Description : 
 Makes 4 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups cooked  rice  (cooked in  chicken broth ) 
 1 x 9-ounce package frozen  artichoke hearts , thawed 
 ½ cup quartered  cherry tomatoes 
 ⅓ cup sliced  celery 
 ⅓ cup cubed  green pepper 
 ¼ cup sliced  ripe olives 
 3 tablespoons Italian dressing 
 4  red cabbage  or  radicchio  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  artichoke  hearts,  tomatoes ,  celery ,  green pepper , and dressing in large bowl. 
 Serve in  cabbage  leaves.
