Description : 
 Contributed by: Mary Ann Lee
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 envelope  unflavored gelatin 
 ⅓ cup  water 
 1 x 8 oz carton dairy  sour cream 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 1 tbsp fresh  lemon juice 
 ½ tsp  vanilla extract 
 ½ tsp  almond extract 
 2 cups coarsely chopped fresh pitted  cherries , divided 
 1 cup  heavy whipping cream 
 3 tbsp  amaretto  liqueur 
 1 cup coarsely crumbled  amaretto  cookies 
 24 whole pitted  cherries 
 fresh  mint  leaves for garnish 
 Directions : 
 In small saucepan, combine  gelatin  and  water . 
 Let stand for 5 minutes. 
 Cook and stir over low heat until  gelatin  is dissolved. 
 Remove from heat. 
 In blender container or food processor bowl, combine cooked  gelatin , sour cream ,  sugar ,  lemon juice  and 1 cup of  cherries . 
 Cover and blend or process until pureed. 
 Pour into a large bowl; set aside. 
 Beat  cream  and  amaretto  liqueur until soft peaks form. 
 Remove ½ cup of  whipped cream  for topping; cover and refrigerate. 
 Gently fold remaining  whipped cream  into  cherry  mixture. 
 Fold in another cup of cut up cherries and the crumbled  amaretto  cookies. 
 Divide mixture among 8 individual serving dishes or parfait glasses. 
 Cover and chill at least 2 hours or until serving time. 
 Before serving, top each with a dollop of remaining  whipped cream  and 3 whole  cherries  garnish with fresh  mint  leaves.
