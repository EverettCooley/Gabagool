Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 6 servings of savory broccoli bread pudding. 
 Ingredients : 
 6 cups 1 inch cubes  Italian bread 
 10 oz pkg  broccoli  florets, coarsely chopped 
 4 oz sliced  ham , coarsely chopped 
 12 oz jar roasted  red peppers , drained and coarsely chopped 
 4  scallions , sliced 
 1 cup shredded Italian  cheese  blend 
 6 large  eggs 
 2 cups  milk 
 ¼ tsp each dried  thyme ,  salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Coat a shallow baking dish with nonstick spray. 
 Spread ½ the bread cubes over bottom. 
 Cover with ½ the broccoli, ham, peppers, scallions and cheese. 
 Repeat layers once. 
 Whisk egg, milk, thyme, salt and pepper to blend. 
 Pour over top. 
 Cover with foil, refrigerate 2 hours. 
 Heat oven to 350°F. 
 Bake covered 60 minutes. 
 Uncover bake 5 minutes or until done. 
 Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
