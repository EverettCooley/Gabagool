Description : 
 I obtained this recipe from an estate sale when I purchased the family collection from the Monsey Estate in Crandall, Texas in 1991.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ teaspoons unflavored  gelatin 
 ¼ cup cool water 
 1 cup raw  cashews  well rinsed 
 1 tablespoon  nutritional yeast  flakes 
 2 teaspoons  honey 
 1½ teaspoons  onion powder 
 1½ teaspoons  salt 
 ½ teaspoon dried sweet  basil 
 ½ teaspoon ground  coriander 
 ½ teaspoon  garlic powder 
 1 cup boiling water 
 ¼ cup fresh  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Place gelatin in blender with cool water and let soak while preparing other ingredients. 
 To blender add rest of ingredients except boiling water and lemon juice. 
 Place lid on blender with insert removed then turn on blender and immediately begin to pour boiling water in steady stream through opening in lid. 
 After all water has been added continue to blend for 4 minutes. 
 Briefly blend in lemon juice. 
 Pour into jar with tight-fitting lid. 
 Refrigerate several hours until set.
