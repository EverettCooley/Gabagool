Ingredients : 
 4 Tblsp  Horseradish 
 3 Tblsp  Balsamic vinegar 
 3 tsp.  Dijon mustard 
 2/3 c.  Olive oil 
 4 c.  Red cabbage , shredded 
 6 oz. roast  beef , cut into strips 
 2/3 c.  Anise 
 1/2   Red onion , thinly sliced 
 1/3 c.  Roquefort  cheese , crumbled 
 Pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Whisk together horseradish, vinegar, and mustard 
 Slowly add oil till blended. 
 Cook cabbage till just wilted; transfer to bowl & cool 
 Mix in beef, anise, onion, cheese, pepper & dressing
