Ingredients
 Recipe makes 12 Servings
 1 Lb H‑E‑B Mi Comida Barbacoa
 1 Central Market Italian Pizza Crust
 1 cup(s) tomato basil pasta sauce
 1 Oz Goya Chipotle Peppers in Adobo
 1 cup(s) cilantro
 1 1/2 cup(s) queso asadero, shredded
 1 Large Hass avocado
 1 small white onion, finely diced
 1 medium lime, cut in wedges
 Instructions
 1
 Preheat oven to 425°F.
 2
 Heat barbacoa according to package directions.
 3
 Heat pasta sauce and chipotle in a small saucepan.
 4
 Spread 1/4 cup sauce on each pizza crust. Cover with barbcoa and cheese.
 5
 Bake 15 to 20 minutes or until cheese is slightly browned and bubbly.
 6
 Remove from oven and top with cilantro, onion, avocado, and a squeeze of lime.
