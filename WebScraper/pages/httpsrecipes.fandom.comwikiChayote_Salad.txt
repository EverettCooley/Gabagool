 Ingredients : 
 6  chayotes  (also referred to as meletons or mirletons) 
 ½ tsp  mustard 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  black pepper 
 ¼ cup  oil 
 Directions : 
 Boil and cube chayotes. 
 Mix the rest of ingredients and pour over cubed  chayote . 
 Place in refrigerator for about 1 hour. 
 Serve over  lettuce .
