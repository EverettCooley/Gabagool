Ingredients : 
 1 cup (cream-style)  corn 
 2  spring onions 
 8 – 10  beans 
 1 small  carrot 
 3 – 4  mushrooms 
 a pinch  ajinomoto 
 ½ tsp  white pepper 
 2 tbsp  cornflour 
 2 tsp  sugar 
 1 lb  butter 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Cut  mushrooms ,  carrot , beans and  spring onions  with greens finely. 
 Heat  butter  in a pan, add  onions , stir a while then add the  corn  and a cup of  water . 
 Add ajinomoto,  sugar ,  salt ,  pepper . 
 Mix  cornflour  with half a cup of  water  and bring to a boil. 
 Just before serving put  onion  greens,  carrot ,  mushrooms  and beans. 
 Serve hot.
