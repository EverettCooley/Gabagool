Ingredients : 
 1 large  cucumber , peeled and sliced 
 1 small  onion , thinly sliced 
 1 cup of  yogurt  or  milk  with  vinegar 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together and let marinate for at least one hour.
