 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 oz  light rum 
 1 oz  orange juice 
 1 tbsp  lime juice 
 1 tsp  Sugar 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients with 1/2 cup shaved  ice  in an electric blender at a low speed. 
 Pour into a champagne flute and serve.
