      Cheese Finger Food     
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  egg 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon  Sugar 
 1 kg  flour  (2.2 pounds) 
 1 cup  vegetable oil 
 1 kg very firm  feta   Cheese 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix the  flour ,  oil ,  egg ,  salt  and  Sugar  in a bowl. While kneading, add a tablespoon or two of  water . 
 Cut the  Cheese  into strips into a finger-like shape 
 Form it into balls slightly larger than your fist and roll them 
 Enfold the  Cheese  "fingers" with the dough strips
