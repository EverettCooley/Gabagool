Description : 
 Among the many Japanese food preparation methods, Teppanyaki is certainly one of the most
ancient and unique ones! Teppanyaki is a method of cooking food that uses a hot iron griddle to cook food quicker and easier and also the cooking method gives the food a distinct flavour.  If you have a griddle at home, you can try this popular and exciting cooking style from the comfort of your home.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1lb sliced  beef  for Yakiniku 
 1  pepper 
 1  zucchini 
 ¼  Kabocha squash 
 1  onion 
 7  Shiitake mushrooms 
 oil  of your choice 
 Yakiniku Sauce      
 2 tablespoon of  sugar 
 ¼ cup of  soy sauce 
 2  chilli peppers  (dried) 
 2 tablespoons of  mirin 
 1 tablespoon of grated  ginger 
 1 tablespoon of grated  garlic 
 ½ tablespoon of  sesame oil 
 ½ tablespoon of ground  sesame seeds 
 
 
 Instructions : 
 Yakiniku Sauce 
 Slice the dried chillis and add them into a pot along with all the ingredients except the sesame oil. 
 Cook the mixture thoroughly until it comes to a boil. 
 Add in the sesame and continuously stir for 3 minutes. 
 Main dish 
 Cut the onions, squash, zucchini and peppers into half-inch slices 
 Cut off the stems from the mushrooms 
 Heat the oil on the griddle at high heat. 
 Place the vegetables on the griddle, followed by the sliced meat with the yakiniku sauce 
 Grill until cooked through 
 Serve hot.
