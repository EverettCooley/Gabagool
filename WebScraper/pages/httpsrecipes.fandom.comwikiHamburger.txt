      Hamburger     
 About hamburger : 
 A hamburger, or simply burger, consists of a cooked patty of the  ground beef  sandwiched between two bread halves, usually in the form of a  hamburger bun . The meat can be mixed with various flavorings including finely chopped onions and herbs. 
 The term is also used to refer to ground, shredded or finely chopped  beef .
