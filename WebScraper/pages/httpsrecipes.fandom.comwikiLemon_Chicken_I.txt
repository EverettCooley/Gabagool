Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 4 Servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 4  chicken  breasts halves 
 pepper  to taste 
 2  lemons 
 1 teaspoon  tarragon 
 Directions : 
 Remove all visible fat from  chicken . 
 Place each breast in the center of a 12 inch square piece of aluminum foil. 
 Fold the sides of the foil up so the  lemon juice  won't run out. 
 Halve the  lemons  and squeeze the juice of ½  lemon  over each  chicken  breast. 
 Sprinkle each with ¼ teaspoon  tarragon  and  pepper . 
 Fold the foil together and seal well to secure the  chicken  inside. 
 Place the wrapped  chicken  in a shallow baking dish and bake at 350f for about 45 minutes.
