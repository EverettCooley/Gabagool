Ingredients : 
 1 (6 oz can) frozen  lemonade  concentrate, thawed 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 1 cup snipped pitted dried  prunes 
 2 tbsp  flour 
 pinch  salt 
 ¼ cup chopped  walnuts 
 ½ cup soft  shortening 
 ½ cup uncooked  rolled oats 
 1 cup sifted  all purpose flour 
 ½ cup packed  brown sugar 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 To lemonade concentrate, add enough  water  to make 1 cup. 
 Simmer with  prunes  in saucepan until  prunes  are soft, about 5 minutes. 
 Add  granulated sugar , 2 tbsp  flour ,  salt  and  nuts . 
 Cook, stir until mixture is thick, about 8 minutes. Cool. 
 Meanwhile,  cream   shortening , add  oats , 1 cup  flour ,  brown sugar  and mix until crumbly. 
 Pat ½ of this mixture in the bottom of a 8 x 8 inch pan. 
 Top with the cooled  prune  mixture and then the remaining crumbs. 
 Bake about 30 minutes. 
 Cut into bars.
