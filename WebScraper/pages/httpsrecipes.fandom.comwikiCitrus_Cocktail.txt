Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Golan Estate in Allen, Texas in 1984.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ ounce  rum 
 1½ ounce white  crème de cacao 
 1 ounce  grapefruit juice 
 1 ounce  orange juice 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients and shake well. 
 Serve over crushed ice.
