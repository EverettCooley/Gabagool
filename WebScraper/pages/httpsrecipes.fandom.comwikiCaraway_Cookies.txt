Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup  butter  or  shortening 
 1 cup  Sugar 
 1  egg 
 2 cups  flour 
 2 teaspoons  baking powder 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 3/4 cup  milk 
 1-1/2 tablespoons  caraway seeds 
 
 
 Directions : 
 First  cream  the  butter  or  shortening  with the  Sugar 
 Add beaten  egg . Mix and sift the  flour ,  baking powder , and  salt , and add alternately with the  milk  to the first mixture. 
 Add  caraway seeds . 
 Toss on lightly floured board. 
 Roll out about one-half inch thick and cut in fancy shapes. 
 Place on greased baking sheet and bake in moderate oven (380-390 degrees F.) Twelve to fifteen minutes.
