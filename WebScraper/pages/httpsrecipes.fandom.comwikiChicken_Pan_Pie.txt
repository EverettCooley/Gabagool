Description : 
 Makes 8 servings. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 medium cooking  apples , cored and diced 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 1 cup chopped  celery 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 3 cups cooked  chicken  or  turkey  cubes 
 2 cups cooked  rice 
 1 teaspoon  marjoram 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 2 x 10¾ ounce cans condensed  cream of chicken  soup 
 2 cups (8 ounces) shredded  Cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Apples ,  onion  and  celery  in  butter  in large skillet until tender. 
 Add  chicken ,  rice ,  marjoram ,  pepper  and soup. 
 Mix thoroughly.Turn into greased individual casseroles or shallow 2-quart baking dish. 
 Cover and bake at 350°F 20 to 30 minutes. Remove cover; sprinkle with  Cheese . 
 Continue baking an additional 5 minutes
