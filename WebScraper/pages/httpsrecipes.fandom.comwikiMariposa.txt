 Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  light rum 
 1/2 oz  brandy 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 oz  orange juice 
 1 dash  Grenadine 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Shake well with  ice  and strain into a glass.
