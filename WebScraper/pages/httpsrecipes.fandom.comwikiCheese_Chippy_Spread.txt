This recipe came from an estate sale. It was obtained when the family collection was purchased from the Burnett Estate in Terrell, Texas in 1981.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ Pound  cream cheese 
 2 Tablespoons  milk 
 1 Teaspoon  Tabasco sauce 
 2 Tablespoons grated white  onion 
 1 Teaspoon  salt 
 2 Teaspoons freshly ground  black pepper 
 ½ Cup finely chopped dried  beef 
 Directions : 
 Let  cream cheese  stand at room temperature until soft enough to beat 
 Add  milk  to make a smooth paste and beat in  onion  and seasonings. 
 Add dried  beef , stirring to make the mixture a smooth paste. 
 Pack paste firmly into mold 
 Chill until ready to serve.
