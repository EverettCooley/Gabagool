Description : 
 Contributed by Catsrecipes Y-Group
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup dried  navy beans 
 ¼ cup dried  chickpeas 
 ¼ cup dried  kidney beans 
 ¼ cup dried  pinto beans 
 ½ pound hot  Italian sausage  or  ground beef 
 8 cups  water 
 2 cups  tomato sauce 
 5 cups sliced vegetables of choice, such as  cabbage ,  green beans ,  onions ,  green pepper ,  carrots  or  yellow squash 
 ½ cup small  pasta 
 2 tsp  salt 
 Seasoning packet : 
 2 tsp dried  basil 
 2 tsp dried  oregano 
 2 tablespoons  beef  or  vegetable bouillon  granules 
 ½ tsp  garlic powder 
 ¼ tsp dried  rosemary 
 1 tsp ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Cover beans with water and soak overnight. 
 Drain beans and rinse. 
 Sauté  sausage  or ground  beef  in a large pan, add beans,  water ,  tomato sauce  and the seasoning packet, and bring to simmering. 
 Cook, covered for one hour. 
 Add sliced vegetables. 
 Next, add  pasta  and  salt . 
 Cook, covered for 20 more minutes until vegetables are tender.
