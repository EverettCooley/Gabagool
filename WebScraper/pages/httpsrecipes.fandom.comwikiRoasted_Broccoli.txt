Description : 
 This is a really easy recipe for crispy Broccoli. Serving with cheese is my favorite.
 
 Ingredients : 
 Broccoli 
 Salt  /  pepper 
 Olive oil 
 Utensils : 
 Parchment paper 
 Baking Sheet 
 Bowl 
 Directions : 
 Set your oven to 400 degrees 
 Chop the broccoli into bite-size pieces and put into a bowl that fits the amount of broccoli you have chopped 
 Drizzle 2-3 Tablespoons of olive oil with 2 pinches of salt and pepper into the bowl then toss the broccoli 
 Cut a sheet of parchment to fit the size of your pan and place your broccoli in one layer on the baking sheet 
 Bake for at least 15 min. or until you can see decent browning on all the pieces 
 Serve by themselves or on top of any dish.
