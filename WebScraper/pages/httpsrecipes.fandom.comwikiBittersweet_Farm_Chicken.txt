Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Timmons Estate in Sachse, Texas in 1997. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  all purpose flour 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 4 pound frying  chicken  cut into serving pieces 
 8 tablespoons  butter  divided 
 ¼ cup  lemon juice 
 ¼ cup  orange flavored liqueur 
 ¼ cup  honey 
 grated peel of 1  orange 
 1 tablespoon  soy sauce 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Combine flour, salt and pepper in large sealable bag. 
 Add chicken then shake to coat completely with flour mixture shaking off excess. 
 Melt 4 tablespoons butter in large baking pan in oven. 
 Remove pan from oven then roll chicken in butter to evenly coat. 
 Arrange chicken skin side down in single layer in pan. 
 Bake chicken 30 minutes. 
 Meanwhile melt remaining butter in small saucepan over medium heat. 
 Stir in lemon juice, liqueur, honey, orange peel and soy sauce. 
 Remove chicken from oven and turn pieces over. 
 Pour honey mixture over chicken. 
 Continue baking basting occasionally with pan drippings for 30 minutes.
