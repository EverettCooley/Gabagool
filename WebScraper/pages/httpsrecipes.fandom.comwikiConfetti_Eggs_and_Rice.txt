Description : 
 Makes 8 servings.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 12  eggs , slightly beaten 
 2 cups cooked  rice 
 1 medium  Onion , chopped 
 1 cup shredded  carrots 
 1 cup shredded  zucchini 
 1 cup sliced  Mushrooms 
 1 cup (4 ounces) shredded  Cheddar   Cheese 
 1 cup (4 ounces) shredded  Monterey jack   Cheese 
 1 cup  light cream 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon ground black pep 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine  eggs ,  rice ,  Onion ,  carrots ,  zucchini ,  Mushrooms ,  Cheese ,  cream ,  salt  and  pepper  in bowl; mix well. 
 Transfer mixture to buttered 9x13-inch baking pan. Bake at 400 degrees 20 to 25 minutes or until  eggs  are set and top is golden.
