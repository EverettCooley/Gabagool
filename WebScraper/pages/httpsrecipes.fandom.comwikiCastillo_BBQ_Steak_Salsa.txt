Description : 
 An excellent condiment for BBQ steaks.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 large  tomatoes 
 1 large white  sweet onion 
 1  lemon 
 1 cup fresh chopped  cilantro 
 1  garlic  clove 
 1 tsp  salt 
 pepper 
 Directions : 
 Dice  tomatoes  and  onions  chop up  cilantro  finely crush  garlic  in  garlic  press combine all of the above ingredients. 
 In a bowl squeeze the juice of one  lemon  on vegetables add  salt  and  pepper  toss all ingredients together and let marinate for at least one hour.
