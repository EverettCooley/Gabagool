      Mint-Coriander Chutney by Hooked on Heat     
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups fresh chopped  coriander leaves 
 1 cup chopped  mint  leaves 
 2 – 3  green chilis 
 salt , to taste 
 ½ tsp red  chili powder 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 tsp chopped  ginger 
 Directions : 
 Add all the ingredients in a mixer with a little  water  and grind to a fine paste. 
 Store in an air-tight container in the fridge.
