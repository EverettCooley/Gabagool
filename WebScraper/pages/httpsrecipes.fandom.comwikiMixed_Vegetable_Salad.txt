Description : 
 Contributed by Jenn B aka Mom2Sam and Tiny via  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 8 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup distilled  white vinegar 
 ½ cup light  olive oil 
 2 teaspoons  Italian seasoning 
 ½ teaspoon dried  oregano 
 ½ teaspoon dried  basil 
 ½ teaspoon dried  rosemary 
 ½ teaspoon  salt  and  pepper 
 ½ clove  garlic , minced 
 ½ teaspoon packed fresh  basil  leaves 
 ½ teaspoon dried  marjoram 
 8 cups mixed fresh  vegetables 
 Directions : 
 Whisk together the vinegar, olive oil, Italian seasoning, oregano, rosemary, salt and pepper, garlic, basil and marjoram. 
 Pour dressing over vegetables, toss and chill for at least 4 hours. 
 Drain by serving with a slotted spoon.
