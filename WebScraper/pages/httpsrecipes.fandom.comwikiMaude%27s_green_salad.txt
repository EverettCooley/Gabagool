 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 head  iceberg lettuce 
 1/2 head  Boston lettuce 
 1/2 head  chicory 
 1/2 lbs  spinach 
 1/2 head  romaine lettuce 
 5 tbsp lo-calories italtan dressing 
 1 tbsp  Parmesan cheese 
 Directions : 
 Rinse and crisp the salad greens. Break  iceberg lettuce  into bite-size pieces. Carefully pat iceberg dry on towel. Place in large plastic bag. Add 1 tb italian dressing. Shake lightly until all leaves are covered. Place in strip on medium platter or plate. Repeat with each green. Sprinkle lightly with  Parmesan cheese .
