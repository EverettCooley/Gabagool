Ingredients : 
 l large  egg  or 2 small  eggs 
 10 tbsp  flour 
 5 tbsp  tapioca starch 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 1½ tsp  sugar 
 and love 
 Directions : 
 Break  egg  into bowl and beat well. 
 Grease frying pan and pour the  egg  into the pan. 
 Cook until to become gold. 
 Mix other ingredients until well blended. 
 Grease the pan and pour ingredients into the pan (you can add  green onion ,  sweet corn  or other chopped vegetable if you want). 
 Cook until done and become gold. 
 Place  egg  on the cooked  flour  mixture and roll it up. 
 Cut into 1½ – 2 inch slices.
