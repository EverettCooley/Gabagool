 About Cambozola : 
 This  German   blue cheese , also called Cambazola, derives its name from  Camembert  and  Gorgonzola  cheese as it combines their tastes. It is made of cow's milk and cream, moist, rich creamy and mellow tasting, goes well with salads, sauces,  sausages , meats, appetizers.
