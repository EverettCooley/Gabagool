Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 pound smoked country-style link  Sausage 
 1/2 cup  water 
 1 cup chopped  Onion 
 2 15-1/2-ounce cans kidney or  pinto beans 
 1/3 cup firmly packed  brown sugar 
 1/2 cup  catsup 
 2 tablespoons  vinegar 
 2 teaspoons prepared  mustard 
 2 teaspoons  garlic salt 
 2 teaspoons  chili powder 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Place links in saucepan. Add  water ; cover and simmer 5 minutes. Remove from  water  and cut into thin slices. 
 Combine  Sausage  and  Onion ; in large skillet; cook until  Onion  is transparent. 
 Stir in beans,  brown sugar ,  catsup ,  vinegar ,  mustard ,  garlic salt  and  chili powder ; simmer 10 minutes. Serve over beds of  rice .
