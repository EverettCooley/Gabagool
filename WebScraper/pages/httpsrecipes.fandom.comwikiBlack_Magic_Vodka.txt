 Ingredients : 
 1½ shots Stolichnaya Red  vodka 
 ½ shot  kahlúa 
 dash  lemon juice 
 garnish: slice of  lemon 
 Glass: Tumbler
 
 Directions : 
 Shake all ingredients with crushed  ice  and strain into glass. 
 Garnish with a slice of  lemon .
