Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup + 2 tbsp  orange  flavored drink mix, sugar-free 
 ¼ cup  lemonade  flavored-drink mix, sugar-free 
 ¾ cup  iced tea  mix, sugar-free 
 1 tsp ground  cloves 
 2 tbsp ground  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients, stirring until blended. 
 Store mixture in an airtight container. 
 For each serving, place 1 teaspoon mix in a cup. 
 Add 1 cup hot  water , stirring well. 
 Serve hot. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 Calories: 1 | car: trace | pro: trace | fat: trace | sod: 1 mg 
 Food exchanges: free
