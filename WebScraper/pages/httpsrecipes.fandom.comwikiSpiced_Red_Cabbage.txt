      Spiced Red Cabbage     
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  oil 
 2-pound head  red cabbage , chopped 
 1 small  onion , thinly sliced 
 1 small  apple , cored and chopped 
 ½ cup  raisins 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  cloves 
 ⅛ teaspoon ground  allspice 
 1 tablespoon  white wine vinegar 
 2 teaspoons  sugar 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Heat the  oil  in oven or large saucepan. 
 Add  cabbage ,  onion ,  apple ,  raisins , cloves and  allspice . 
 Stir to coat. Cover. 
 Cook over moderate heat, stirring about 1 hour. 
 Stir in  vinegar ,  sugar  and  salt . 
 Cook for 30 minutes.
