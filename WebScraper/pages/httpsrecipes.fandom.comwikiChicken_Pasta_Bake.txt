Chicken   Pasta  Bake
 
This Easy  Chicken  Dish is Quick And Leftovers Are Scrumptious!
 
 4 Cups Italian-Style  Cheese  (set Aside a Handful to Top Casserole) 
 2 Cups  Chicken , Cooked And Cubed 
 1 Jar Italian-Style  Pasta  Sauce 
 1  green pepper  Chopped 
 1 red pepper Chopped 
 8 Ounces Elbow  macaroni  (cooked) 
 1 Teaspoon  garlic powder 
 1 Teaspoon  Onion  Power 
 
Combine All Ingredients in a Bowl. Spray an 11- x 13-Inch Glass Dish With Non-Stick Spray And Slowly Pour Ingredients Into Dish. Bake at 350?f For 30 Minutes. Sprinkle Casserole With Remaining  Cheese  And Continue to Cook Until  Cheese  is bubbly And Brown.
