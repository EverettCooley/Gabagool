Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Ritchie Estate in Balch Springs, Texas in 1984.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  graham crackers 
 2 pounds  peanut butter 
 1 pound  powdered sugar 
 2 tablespoons  butter  softened 
 Directions : 
 Break crackers into large pieces in bowl then add remaining ingredients and beat for 5 minutes. 
 Form into logs then roll in chopped nuts or powdered sugar. 
 Chill and slice.
