Description : 
 This is really yummy, I have tried it several times, a pretty dish, we all like it.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 Bean layer : 
 2 cans 16 oz each  refried beans 
 2 tbsp  lime juice 
 2 tsp  chili powder 
 1 tsp each minced  garlic , ground  cumin , and  hot pepper sauce 
 Guacamole layer : 
 2  avocados 
 1 tbsp each  lime juice  and chopped  cilantro 
 1 tsp each minced  garlic  and  salt 
 ¾ cup  sour cream 
 ⅔ cup shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 Salsa layer : 
 1 cup chopped  tomato 
 ¼ cup sliced  scallions 
 ¼ cup chopped  cilantro 
 1 tbsp  lime juice 
 ½ tsp minced  garlic 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Bean layer : 
 Mix ingredients in a bowl. 
 Mound in center of a large serving plate. 
 Shape into a flat circle. 
 Guacamole layer : 
 Halve avocados, scoop flesh into a bowl. 
 Mash with lime juice, cilantro, garlic and salt until almost smooth. 
 Spread over bean layer. 
 Top with sour cream, sprinkle with cheese. 
 Salsa layer : 
 Mix ingredients in a bowl. 
 Drain juices from salsa and spoon over cheese. 
 Serve with tortilla chips.
