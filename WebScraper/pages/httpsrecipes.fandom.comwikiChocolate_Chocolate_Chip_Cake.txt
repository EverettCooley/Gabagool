 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pkg  chocolate  cake mix 
 1 12oz semi  sweet chocolate  chips 
 1 lg pkg cook and serve  chocolate  pudding 
 Directions : 
 Grease 9x11 pan preheat oven 350. 
 Use a large pot. 
 Make pudding following directions on box. 
 Once pudding is made remove from heat add cake mix stir well put in cake pan top with  chocolate chips . 
 Bake at 350 for 25 minutes.
