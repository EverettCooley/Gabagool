Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  gin 
 1 oz  kirsch 
 ½ oz  cherry brandy 
 1 oz  lemon juice 
 ½ tsp  superfine sugar 
 Directions : 
 Shake all the ingredients in a shaker with  ice  and strain into a cocktail glass.
