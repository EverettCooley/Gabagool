Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Source:  Better Homes and Gardens 
 Prep Time: 15 minutes 
 Servings: 4 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup(s)  ketchup 
 2 tablespoon  sugar substitute , heat-stable 
 1 tablespoon  tapioca 
 1 tablespoon  vinegar 
 1 teaspoon  Worcestershire sauce 
 ¼ teaspoon  cinnamon , ground 
 ¼ teaspoon  crushed red pepper 
 2 pounds  turkey , thighs 
 brown rice 
 Directions : 
 In a 3½- or 4-quart slow cooker combine ketchup, sugar substitute, tapioca, vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, cinnamon, and red pepper. 
 Place turkey thighs meaty side down on ketchup mixture. 
 Cover and cook on low-heat setting for 10 to 12 hours or high-heat setting for 5 to 6 hours. 
 Transfer turkey to a serving dish. 
 Pour cooking juices into a small bowl; skim off fat. 
 Serve turkey with cooking juices and, if desired, hot cooked rice.
