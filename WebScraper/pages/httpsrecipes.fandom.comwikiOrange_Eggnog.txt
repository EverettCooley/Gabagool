Desciption : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale from the Steep Estate in Elmo, Texas in 1989.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups  heavy cream 
 2 cups  milk 
 5 tablespoons  honey 
 6  eggs , separated 
 1 cup  orange juice 
 Grated rind of one  orange 
 nutmeg 
 Directions : 
 Beat  egg  yolks and  honey  together. 
 Add one cup cream and  milk  and continue beating. 
 Add  orange juice  slowly to prevent curdling. 
 Whip remaining cream then fold into  orange  mixture. 
 Beat the  egg  whites until soft peaks form 
 Then add two tablespoons  honey  and beat again until  honey  is mixed with  egg  whites 
 Then fold into eggnog and garnish with grated  orange  rind and  nutmeg .
