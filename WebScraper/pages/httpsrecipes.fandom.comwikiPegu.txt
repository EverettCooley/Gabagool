      Description     
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 oz  gin 
 1/2 oz  Cointreau ®  orange liqueur 
 1/2 oz  lime juice 
 2 dashes Angostura®  bitters 
 Directions : 
 Stir with cracked  ice  and strain into a chilled cocktail glass. Garnish with a  lime  twist.
