 This recipe serves 4.

 Directions : 
 Steam the  carrots  with the  sugar ,  milk ,  cardamom , and  lemon peel  for about 15 minutes, until the  carrots  are tender. 
 Pour int a sieve, pressing firmly on the  carrots  to remove all the liquid. 
 Reserve the liquid. 
 Melt the  butter  in a saucepan and stir in the  flour . 
 Add the  carrots , stirring to coat with he  flour  mixture, and toss for 3 or 4 minutes. 
 Pour the cooking liquid over the  carrots , mix well and cook or about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
 Pour into individual serving dishes and garnish with  nuts  and  raisins .
