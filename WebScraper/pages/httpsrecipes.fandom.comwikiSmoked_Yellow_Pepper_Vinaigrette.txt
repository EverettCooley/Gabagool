Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 This recipe yields about 1 cup of vinaigrette. 
 Ingredients : 
 2  yellow bell peppers , cold smoked, peeled, and seeded 
 ½ canned  chipotle 
 ¼ medium  red onion , coarsely chopped 
 3 tablespoons fresh  lime juice 
 ¼ cup  olive oil 
 1 teaspoon  sugar 
 salt 
 freshly ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 In a blender, combine the  peppers , chipotle,  onion , and  lime  juice and blend. 
 While the blender is still running, slowly add the olive oil until emulsified. 
 Add the  sugar  and season with  salt  and pepper to taste.
