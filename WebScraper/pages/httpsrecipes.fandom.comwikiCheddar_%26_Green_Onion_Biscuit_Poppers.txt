 Ingredients : 
 2 C  Bisquick 
 2/3 C  milk 
 1/2 C leftover shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 4 medium  green onions , sliced ( including greens ) 
 1/4 C fresh baby  spinach  leaves, cut into small strips 
 2 Tbsp  margarine  or  butter , melted 
 Salsa ( to serve; optional ) 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 450-degrees F. Spray a cookie sheet with nonstick cooking spray. 
 In a bowl, stir the Bisquick mix, milk, cheese and onions until a soft dough forms. 
 Drop dough by rounded teaspoonfuls onto the cookie sheet; 
 Bake seven to nine minutes or until golden brown. 
 Brush melted margarine or butter over the warm biscuits; serve with salsa if desired. 
 Notes : 
 Leftovers can be frozen if tightly wrapped; warm three to four poppers at a time in the microwave on high for up to thirty seconds.
