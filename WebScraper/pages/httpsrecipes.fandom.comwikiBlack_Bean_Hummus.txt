Ingredients : 
 16 oz  black beans 
 1 tbl Tahini 
 3 tbl  olive oil 
 1  lime  juiced 
 2  garlic  clove, sliced 
 salt  &  pepper 
 1 tsp Ground  cumin 
 Directions : 
 In a food processor, combine all the ingredients & process till smooth. 
    * Cover & refrigerate till ready to use. Bring to room temperature before serving.
