Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz Bacardi  coco rum 
 ½ oz  Rose's lime juice 
 1½ oz sprite 
 Directions : 
 Shake all ingredients with  ice . 
 Strain into chilled martini glass.
