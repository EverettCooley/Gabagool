Description : 
 This is a delicious easy appetizer. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 slices  bacon , cooked 
 8 ounces grated  cheese 
 ½ teaspoon  garlic powder 
 1 teaspoon  sugar 
 2 tablespoons  onions , finely chopped 
 4 tablespoons  Miracle Whip ™ 
 ½ teaspoon  celery salt 
 miniature party  rye  rounds 
 Directions : 
 Fry  bacon  and crumble fine. 
 Add remaining ingredients except  rye bread . 
 Mix well. 
 Spread on party  rye . 
 Broil until hot.
