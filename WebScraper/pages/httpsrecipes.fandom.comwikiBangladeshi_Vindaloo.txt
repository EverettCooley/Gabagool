Ingredients : 
 1 lb  meat 
 2 tbsp  vinegar 
 1 tsp  coriander  paste 
 ½ tsp  cumin  paste 
 1 tsp  turmeric 
 ½ tsp  mustard 
 2 tsp  chili powder 
 ½ tsp  black pepper 
 ¼ cup diced  onions 
 1 tsp diced  garlic 
 3  green chile 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ¼ cup  oil 
 Directions : 
 In hot  oil , stir fry the  onions  and  garlic . 
 Add the spices and  vinegar  and stir fry for 5 minutes in medium-high heat. 
 Add the meat and cooked covered in medium heat until meat is tender. 
 You might wish to evaporate excess  water .
