Description : 
 Yields 24 pieces 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large  cucumber 
 ½ cup of  sushi rice  (see recipe) 
 ¼ cup lump  crabmeat  or salad  shrimp 
 1 tbsp  wakame  seaweed, soaked and drained 
 1 tbsp pickled red  ginger  ( beni shoga ) 
 Directions : 
 Wash a  cucumber  and cut off the ends. 
 Slice the  cucumber  lengthwise and pry it open. 
 Next, pry it open  do not break through the rind underneath . 
 Scoop out the seeds and fill with ingredients that have been mixed together. 
 Close  cucumber  snugly and cut into half-inch rounds and enjoy!.
