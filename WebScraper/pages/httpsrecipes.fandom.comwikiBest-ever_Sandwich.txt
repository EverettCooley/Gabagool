Ingredients : 
 1  bagel  (any flavor) 
 3 tablespoons of  hummus 
 sliced  red  or  green pepper  (or both) 
 fresh  spinach  leaves 
 sliced  cucumber 
 sesame seeds  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Slice  bagel  in half. 
 Slice vegetables on both sides of  bagel . 
 Spread  sesame seeds  on hummus. 
 Place sliced vegetables on top of hummus. 
 Close the  bagel  and enjoy!
