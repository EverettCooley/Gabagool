 Description : 
 Try this for acne'
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 medium size  Apple  (grated) 
 
 5 tablespoons of  Honey 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix the grated  Apple  and mix with  Honey  to make a mask. Smooth over skin then let sit for 10 minutes, rinse off with cool  water .
