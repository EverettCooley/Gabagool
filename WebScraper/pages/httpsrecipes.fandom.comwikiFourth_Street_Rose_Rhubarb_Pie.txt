Ingredients : 
 2  eggs 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 ¼ cup  flour 
 2 cups frozen  strawberries 
 1 pie shell 
 2 cups frozen  rhubarb 
 Topping : 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 ¼ cup  shortening 
 ¼ cup  butter 
 1 pn  salt 
 3 tbsp liquid  honey 
 ¾ cup  flour 
 Directions : 
 Pre heat oven 425 °F 
 Whisk  eggs  in large bowl. 
 Whisk  sugar ,  flour  until smooth. 
 Set aside. 
 Cut  rhubarb , fold into  egg  mixture. 
 Put in pie shell. 
 Bake 15 minutes, remove and reduce heat to 350 °F. 
 Apply topping and bake for 40 minutes.
