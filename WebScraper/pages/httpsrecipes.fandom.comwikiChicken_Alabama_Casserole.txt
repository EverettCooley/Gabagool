Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  chicken breasts 
 1 stick  oleo 
 1 cup chopped  green pepper 
 1 cup chopped  celery 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 1 can  cream of mushroom  soup 
 1 large can sliced  mushrooms 
 1 pkg, (4½ oz)  noodles 
 salt  to taste 
 ½ lb  Velveeta  cheese  or  8 oz  Cheez Whiz 
 1 small jar stuffed  olives , sliced 
 Directions : 
 Cook  chicken  until tender. 
 Save broth for cooking noodles. 
 Cool  chicken , bone, skin and cut in bite size pieces. 
 Cook  onion ,  celery , and  pepper  in  butter . 
 Add  cheese ; stir until melted. 
 Add,  mushrooms  and  chicken . 
 Blend gently with  mushroom  soup adding a little  chicken broth  if needed. 
 Boil noodles in broth; drain. 
 Add to sauce, fold gently. 
 Pour into 9 x 15-inch greased casserole. 
 Top with ritz cracker crumbs or  potato  chip crumbs. 
 Dot with  butter . 
 Bake 45 minutes at 300°F.
