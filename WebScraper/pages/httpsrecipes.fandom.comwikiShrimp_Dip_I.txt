Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Original recipe 
 Makes about 3 cups / Serves 9 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ pound  shrimp 
 ½  onion , quartered 
 1 clove  garlic 
 ½ teaspoon  cayenne pepper 
 8 ounces  Neufchatel  cheese 
 4 ounces  sour cream 
 1 tablespoon organic  Dijon mustard 
 dash of  Tabasco sauce 
 1 tablespoon minced  garlic 
 1 teaspoon  white pepper 
 1 teaspoon  Worcestershire sauce 
 1 teaspoon  sea salt 
 1 teaspoon  cayenne pepper 
 2 tablespoons unsweetened  ketchup 
 ½ cup  green onions , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Boil shrimp with onion, garlic and ½ teaspoon cayenne. 
 Peel and devein shrimp. 
 Coarsely chop shrimp and set aside. 
 Combine rest of ingredients (except for green onions) in a mixing bowl or food processor. 
 Mix well; add shrimp and green onions. 
 Allow to chill at least one hour before serving. 
 Serve with whole grain breads, crackers, and plenty of vegetables to lower the overall percentage of fat calories of the entire meal. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving ⅓ cup (83 g-wt.): 
 
 180 calories (110 from fat) | 12g total fat | 8g saturated fat | 10g protein | 8g total carbohydrate (0g dietary fiber, 5g sugar) | 80mg cholesterol | 540mg sodium
