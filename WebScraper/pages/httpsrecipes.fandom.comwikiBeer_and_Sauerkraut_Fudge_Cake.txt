 Ingredients : 
 2/3 	cup 	 butter 
 1 1/2 	cup 	 Sugar 
 3 		 eggs 
 1 	tsp 	 vanilla 
 1/2 	cup 	 cocoa 
 2 1/4 	cup 	Sifted  flour 
 1 	tsp 	 baking powder 
 1 	tsp 	Soda 
 1 	cup 	 beer 
 2/3 	cup 	 sauerkraut 
 1 	cup 	 Raisins 
 1 	cup 	Chopped  nuts 
 Directions : 
 cream   butter  and  Sugar  until light. 
Add  eggs , one at a time, beating well after each addition. 
Blend in  vanilla . 
Sift  cocoa ,  flour ,  baking powder , soda and  salt  together. 
Add to creamed mixture alternately with  beer , beginning and ending with dry ingredients. 
Stir in  sauerkraut . 
 Raisins  and  nuts  are optional. 
Turn into two 8 or 9 inch greased and floured cake pans. 
Bake at 350 °F for 35 minutes. 
Cool and frost as desired.
