 Ingredients : 
 1 pound ground  Beef 
 1 package (1.31 ounces) McCormick Sloppy Joe Seasoning, divided 
 1 teaspoon McCormick Minced  Onion 
 1/2 teaspoon McCormick  Italian seasoning 
 6 ounces  tomato  paste 
 1 1/2 cups  water 
 4 6-inch submarine rolls 
 shredded  mozzarella   Cheese 
 Directions : 
 Combine ground  Beef  and 1 tablespoon of Sloppy Joe Seasoning, minced  Onion  and  Italian seasoning .  
Shape into 1 1/2-inch meatballs. 
Brown meatballs 10 minutes or until done; drain.  
Blend remaining Seasoning,  tomato  paste and  water ; pour over meatballs.  
Reduce heat and cook 5 minutes. 
Place meatballs into sub rolls.  
Spoon sauce over meatballs; sprinkle with  Cheese . Serve.
 
 Side dishes:  Potato  wedges;  corn  on the cob 
 Makes 4 subs
