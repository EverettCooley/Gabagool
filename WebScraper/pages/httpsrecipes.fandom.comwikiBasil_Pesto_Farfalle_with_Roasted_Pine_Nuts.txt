Description : 
 This is a wonderful mixture of  basil ,  garlic , and  Parmesan cheese .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 box  farfalle  pasta (bowtie) 
 4 cups shredded  Parmesan cheese 
 2 packages fresh  basil 
 2 tablespoons minced  garlic 
 ½ cup  olive oil 
 1 package  pine nuts 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Boil  farfalle  according to instructions. 
 Al dente works great. 
 Meanwhile: add 2 cups  parmesan cheese , 2 packages fresh  basil ,  garlic ,  salt , and ½-package  pine nuts  in food processor. 
 Slowly pour the  olive oil  while blending all ingredients together. 
 Set aside. 
 Toast the remaining  pine nuts  in a splash of  olive oil  until golden brown. 
 Set aside. 
 Rinse  pasta . 
 Stir in the  cheese / basil  mixture. 
 Blend in the remaining 2 cups Parmesan and the toasted  pine nuts . 
 Serve warm or cold.
