 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 8 oz  cream cheese , softened 
 2 cups sharp  Cheddar   Cheese , shredded, room temperature 
 1/2 cup chopped stuffed  green olives 
 2 tbsp minced  parsley 
 1 tbsp minced  green onion 
 1/4 tsp hot pepper sauce 
 1/2 cup finely chopped, roasted Oregon  hazelnuts 
 Directions : 
 1 In a large bowl,  cream  together the  cream  and sharp cheeses. 
2 Stir in,  parsley ,  green onion  and hot pepper sauce  until well mixed. 
3 Shape into a ball and roll in the chopped  hazelnuts . 
4 Cover and chill 1 to 24 hours. 
5 Serve with crackers.
