Ingredients : 
 4 medium tart  apples , pared and cut into chunks 
 ¼ cup light  margarine , melted 
 ½ cup  brown sugar 
 ¼ cup  whole wheat flour 
 ¾ cup quick cooking oats 
 1 tsp ground  cinnamon 
 ½ tsp ground  nutmeg 
 vanilla  ice milk  or frozen low-fat  yogurt 
 Directions : 
 Arrange  apple  chunks in 8x8x2-inch mirowave dish. 
 In separate bowl, mix  margarine ,  quick oats ,  brown sugar ,  flour ,  cinnamon , and  nutmeg ; blend thoroughly. 
 Sprinkle mixture over  apples . 
 Microwave on high setting for 10 to 12 minutes, until  apples  are tender. 
 Top with vanilla  ice milk  or frozen low-fat  yogurt , if desired. 
 Nutritional information : 
 5 grams fat per serving
