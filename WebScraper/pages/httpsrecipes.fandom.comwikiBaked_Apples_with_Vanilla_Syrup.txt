Ingredients : 
 8 – 10 equal-sized  apples 
 ⅓ cupful  walnut 
 ¾ cupful  sugar 
 vanilla 
 cinnamon  according to taste 
 50 g  butter 
 Directions : 
 Peel and carefully hollow the  apples . 
 Prepare a stuffing from the beaten  butter , ¾ cupful of  sugar , crushed  walnut  kernels and  cinnamon . 
 Stuff the  apples  and place in a lined dish, pour over 1 – 2 spoonfuls of  water  and bake in a moderate oven. 
 Serve cold, sweetened with syrup made from cupful of  sugar , 1 cupful of  water  and  vanilla . 
 The same recipe may be used for  quinces  and  pears .
