 Ingredients : 
 1 pound round or flank  steak 
 ½ teaspoon  sugar 
 1 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 1 teaspoon  ground ginger 
 1 teaspoon  paprika 
 1 teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 chilli powder 
 2 teaspoons  onion salt 
 ½ cup  peanuts , finely crushed 
 vegetable oil , for brushing 
 Directions : 
 Trim the  steak  of any fat and cut into 1-inch wide strips. 
 Place in a bowl or a shallow dish. 
 Mix the  sugar ,  garlic powder , spices and  onion salt  together in a small bowl. 
 Add the crushed  peanuts . 
 Add this mixture to the  steak , mixing well so that the spices are worked into the meat. 
 Thread the  steak  on to six sticks, pushing the meat close together. 
 Place in a shallow dish, cover loosely with foil and let marinate in a cool place for a few hours. 
 Preheat a barbecue grill. 
 Brush the meat with a little  oil  and then cook over a moderate heat for about 15 minutes, until evenly brown.
