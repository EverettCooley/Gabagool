Ingredients : 
 1½ lbs  ground beef 
 2 lbs  ground pork 
 6 chopped  onions 
 6  garlic  cloves 
 2 cups sliced  sweet peppers 
 5 cups chopped  celery 
 2 large cans  tomatoes 
 2 cans  tomato paste 
 2 cans  tomato soup 
 3 cups  mushrooms  with liquid 
 2 cups sliced stuffed  olives 
 2 cans  pimentos , chopped 
 1 bunch chopped  parsley 
 5 cups grated  American cheese 
 1½ lb wide  egg noodles 
 4 tbsp  oil 
 1 tbsp ground  pepper 
 3  bay leaves 
 salt  to taste 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Sauté  beef  and  pork  in  oil  in a large skillet. 
 When it has cooked awhile, add  garlic  cloves, thinly sliced  onions , and  peppers . 
 Fry a little and then add remaining ingredients, except noodles and  cheese  and simmer a while. 
 Cook noodles in boiling  water  and drain. 
 Grease a large pan. 
 Add the meat mixture to the noodles and place in pan in layers, sprinkling the grated  cheese  on the layers and on top. 
 Bake in slow oven, 275°F for two hours.
