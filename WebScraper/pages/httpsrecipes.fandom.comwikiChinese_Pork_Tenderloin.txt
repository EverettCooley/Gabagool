Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: a Southern Collection...Then And Now 
 Ingredients : 
 2  pork tenderloins 
 ½ cup  soy sauce 
 ½ cup  chicken broth 
 1 cup  honey 
 ½ cup  sherry 
 2  garlic  cloves, crushed 
 1 tsp dried  ginger 
 1 tsp  dry mustard 
 1 tsp  paprika 
 Directions : 
 Put  pork  tenderloin in a glass baking dish. 
 Mix together all other ingredients and simmer over medium heat until ingredients are blended. 
 Let cool slightly and then pour over tenderloins. 
 Marinate in refrigerator for about 3 hours, turning every 30 minutes. 
 Place dish in oven as is and bake uncovered in a preheated 350 oven for about 1½ hours. 
 Time may vary. 
 Baste every 15 to 20 minutes and continue to bake until sauce becomes thick, dark and forms a crust. 
 This is a good dish to serve company and makes great leftovers. 
 Serve with whipped  potatoes  or  rice . 
 Sauce can also be used on spare ribs that have been broiler 4 minutes on each side before adding the sauce. 
 The aroma is mouth watering.
