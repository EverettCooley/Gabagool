Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 2 chipotle chiles en adobo, finely minced 
 1/2 cup  corn  kernels 
 1 tablespoon minced  cilantro 
 1/2 cup grated sharp  Cheddar   Cheese 
 Directions : 
 In large bowl, combine  rice , chiles,  corn  and  cilantro ; mix well. 
 Serve topped with  Cheese .
