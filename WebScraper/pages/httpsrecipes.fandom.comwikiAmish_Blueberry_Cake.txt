      Amish Blueberry Cake     
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup  sugar 
 ¼ cup  vegetable oil 
 1  egg 
 ½ cup  milk 
 2 cups  flour 
 2 tsp  baking powder 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 2 cups  blueberries , well drained 
 Topping : 
 ¼ cup  butter 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 ⅓ cup  flour 
 ½ tsp  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Crumble over cake batter. 
 Cream together  sugar ,  oil  and  egg  until  lemon  colored. 
 Stir in  milk . 
 Sift together  flour ,  baking powder  and  salt  and stir. 
 Gently fold in  blueberries . 
 Spread batter into greased and floured square pan. 
 sprinkle  with topping. 
 Bake for 45 – 50 minutes at 375 °F.
