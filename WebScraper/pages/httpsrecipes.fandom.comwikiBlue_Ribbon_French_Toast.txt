Blue Ribbon French Toast
 
 2  eggs 
 1 c  flour 
 1 c  milk 
 1 1/2 ts  baking powder 
 1 ts  vanilla 
 1 ts  cinnamon 
 8 Slices Firm  bread , White Or- Wheat   bread 
 1/2 ts  salt 
 
Combine All Ingredients Except For The  bread . Blend Well. Pour Into a Shallow Dish or Pan. Place  bread  Slices Into The Dish. Turn to Coat  bread  on Both Sides. Cook on Griddle or in a Skillet, in  oil , margarine  or  butter , Until Golden, Turn And Cook Until Second Side is Golden. Serve With Syrup OrConfectioners  sugar .
 
Enjoy.
