Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Davidson Estate in Malakoff, Texas in 1988.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon whole  allspice 
 1  cinnamon  stick 
 3 cups  apple cider 
 1 cup  orange juice 
 ½  lemon  sliced 
 1 teaspoon  honey 
 ¼ cup  brandy 
 Directions : 
 Tie allspice in a piece of cheesecloth. 
 Place in medium saucepan along with cinnamon, cider, juice, lemon slices and honey. 
 Bring to a boil then reduce heat and simmer covered 5 minutes. 
 Remove spice bag then stir in brandy.
