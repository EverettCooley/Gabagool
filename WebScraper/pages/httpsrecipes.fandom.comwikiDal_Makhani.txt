Description : 
 Ethnicity - Punjabi, North Indian 
 Type of meal - Party, Lunch, Dinner 
 Ingredients : 
 Ingredients Black  urad dal  whole 100 gms. 
 Rajmah 25 gms. 
 cumin  seeds 1 tsp. 
 Chopped  garlic  1 tbsp. 
 Chopped  ginger  1 tbsp. 
 garam masala  powder 1 tsp. 
 Fresh  cream  ½ cup 
 Red  chili powder  1 tbsp. 
 butter  50 gms. 
 Chopped  tomato  ½ cup 
 Chopped  Onion  1 cup 
 salt  As per taste 
 oil  1 tbsp. 
 Directions : 
 Pick, wash and soak black urad whole and rajmah overnight in 5 cups of  water . 
 Cook the soaked  dal  and rajmah in 5 cups of  water  with  salt , red  chili powder  and chopped  ginger  till  dal  and rajmah are cooked/soft. 
 Heat  oil  and  butter  in a thick bottomed pan. Add  cumin  seeds, let it crackle. Add chopped  onions  and cook till golden brown in color. 
 Add chopped  ginger ,  garlic  and chopped  tomatoes . Sauté till  tomatoes  are well mashed and fat starts to leave the  masala . Add boiled  dal  and  rajma  to this. Correct seasoning. 
 Add  garam masala  Powder and simmer at very slow flame for 15 minutes. ( Slower it is cooked better the taste and the consistency of the  dal . 
 Add fresh  cream  and let it simmer for 5 minutes. 
 Serve hot with Paratha
