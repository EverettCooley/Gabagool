 Ingredients : 
 3  eggs , beaten 
 ¾ cup  matzo cake meal 
 1½ cup  water 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 Filling : 
 1 lbs dry  cottage cheese 
 1 tbsp thick  cream 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 1  egg 
 ½ tsp  sugar 
 Directions : 
 To the beaten  eggs , add the  salt , alternately a little of the cake meal and  water , mixing thoroughly. 
 Pour into a small hot greased frying pan only enough batter to make a very thin pancake, tipping pan from side to side. 
 Toss out on a towel, fried side up. 
 Make a number of sheets this way. 
 Place a heaping tablespoon of  cheese  mixture on each sheet, fold sides of sheet in over mixture to form an envelope, and fry again on both sides until brown. 
 Filling : 
 Press  cheese  through a sieve, add remaining ingredients and beat until smooth.
