Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 7  tea  bags 
 1 small can frozen  lemonade 
 1 small can frozen limeade 
 1 cup  sugar 
 fresh  mint 
 1 quart  water 
 Directions : 
 Bring 1 quart  water  to a boil. 
 Add 7 individual tea bags (the minute the  water  comes to a boil remove from heat and put in tea bags). 
 Set aside to steep for 7 â€“ 8 minutes, remove tea bags. 
 Fill a one gallon container half full of cold  water . 
 To cold  water  add cans of  lemon  and limeade. 
 Mix thoroughly, add  sugar  to taste (about 1 cup). 
 Stir until dissolved. 
 Taste. 
 Add  mint .
