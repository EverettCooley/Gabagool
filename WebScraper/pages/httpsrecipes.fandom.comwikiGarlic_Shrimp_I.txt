Description : 
 Shrimp  in a garlicky sauce is a classic and a classically simple Spanish tapa. It is usually cooked and presented in individual shallow earthenware casseroles, but it can just as easily be made in a larger casserole or skillet. Offer crusty  bread  alongside. 
 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 Original recipe 
 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 8 ounces uncooked large  shrimp , peeled, de-veined 
 1 teaspoon  coarse salt 
 Â¼ cup  olive oil 
 1 tablespoon chopped  garlic 
 1 small  bay leaf 
 1-inch piece dried red  chili pepper , seeded 
 1 tablespoon minced fresh  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Place  shrimp  in bowl; Sprinkle with  coarse salt  and toss. 
 Let stand 15 minutes. 
 Heat  oil  in medium skillet over high heat. Add  garlic ,  bay leaf  and dried  pepper  and stir 1 minute. 
 Add  shrimp ; Stir until just cooked through, about 3 minutes. 
 Transfer to serving dish. Sprinkle with  parsley .
