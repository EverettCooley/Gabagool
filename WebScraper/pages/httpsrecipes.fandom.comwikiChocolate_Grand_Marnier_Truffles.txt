 Ingredients : 
 7 tablespoons unsalted  butter 
 1Â½ cups  heavy cream 
 zest of 1  orange  peeled off in 1 long strip, if possible 
 1 pound grated Valrhona  bittersweet chocolate 
 4 tablespoons  Grand Marnier 
 2 teaspoons pure  orange extract 
 cocoa powder 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter  in heavy saucepan; add  cream  and  orange  zest. 
 Heat until it just starts to boil. 
 Remove  orange  zest and whisk in  chocolate . 
 Then whisk in  Grand Marnier  and  orange  extract. 
 Refrigerate, stirring occasionally so that the mixture firms uniformly. 
 When ready to make  truffles , remove mixture from refrigerator. 
 Let stand 10 minutes to soften slightly. 
 Scoop mixture by teaspoons and roll in  cocoa . 
 Refrigerate until ready to serve.
