 Ingredients : 
 1 pound ground  Beef  or  Turkey 
 1 can  kidney beans 
 1 can  pinto beans 
 1 can yellow  corn , drained 
 1 can  hominy  (white or yellow), drained 
 1 can Mexican  tomatoes 
 1 package Taco mix 
 1 package Ranch dressing mix 
 Directions : 
 Brown ground  Beef  or  Turkey ; drain. Add remaining ingredients; blend well. Heat through or put ingredients in a crockpot and cook on HIGH for 2 to 3 hours.
 Serve over tortilla chips with shredded  Cheese ,  sour cream , guacamole, etc.
