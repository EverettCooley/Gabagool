 Description : 
 This red-hot, sweet sauce is made with real fruit, sugar, spices, and fruit juice and keeps a tangy aroma with the berries to help maintain its sweet consistency.
 
         
 Ingredients : 
 13 oz. package of fresh  cranberries , rinsed, sorted and trimmed 
 1 ⅜ cup raspberries, optional 
 2 ⅜ cups  cranberry juice , cold only 
 1 ⅞ cup  sugar , brown only 
 2 ¼ teaspoons  allspice 
 2 ¼ teaspoons  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 In a large, non-aluminum saucepan, bring all ingredients to a boil. Then turn down to medium heat and cook for 10 minutes. 
 Remove from the heat, cool, and refrigerate until ready to serve. 
 Use as a dip. Reserve the fruit-based sauce in the refrigerator.
