Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 This recipe yields about 1 cup of vinaigrette. 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons fresh  lime juice 
 1 tablespoon  vinegar 
 ½ teaspoon  sugar 
 ½ teaspoon dry  mustard 
 2 teaspoons  dill seed , coarsely ground 
 1 tablespoon  mayonnaise 
 ⅓ cup  olive oil 
 salt  and freshly ground  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Blend together all ingredients except  olive oil , add  oil  in a steady stream, whisking or blending until thoroughly combined. 
 Add  salt  and pepper to taste.
