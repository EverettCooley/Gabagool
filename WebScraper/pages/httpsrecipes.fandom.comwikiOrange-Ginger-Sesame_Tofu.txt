Cook time:80 min 
 
 Description : 
 This is a vegetarian version of  Orange-Ginger-Sesame Chicken .
 
 Ingredients : 
 the sauce from the  Orange-Ginger-Sesame Chicken  recipe 
 flour 
 your favorite deep-frying  batter 
 oil with a high  smoking point  appropriate for deep frying 
 firm or extra-firm  tofu  cut into two (2) cm³ cubes 
 sesame seeds 
 sesame oil 
 Directions : 
 Roll the tofu cubes in flour, subsequently dip them in batter, and  deep fry  them in batches.  They may be refrigerated or frozen until ready for use. 
 Sauté  the deep-fried tofu cubes in a skillet with the sesame oil until desired crispiness is achieved. 
 Pour an appropriate quantity of sauce over the cubes.  Turn until coated and the sauce has thickened and adheres well. 
 Sprinkle with sesame seeds.
