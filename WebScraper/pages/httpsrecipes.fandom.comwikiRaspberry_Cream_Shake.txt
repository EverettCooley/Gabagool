 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup cold skim  milk 
 1 pack (4-serving size) jell-o 
 -brand  raspberry  flavor 
 -sugar-free  gelatin 
 1 cup  vanilla   ice   milk  * 
 1 cup crushed  ice 
 1/2 cup  raspberries 
 Directions : 
 * recipe calls for  ice   milk  but diabetics would be better served to use a sf frozen dairy dessert instead. : pour  milk  into blender container. Add remaining ingredients; cover. Blend at high speed 30 seconds or until smooth. Makes 3 ½ cups or 3 servings. : nutritional information per serving: calories - 120, protien - 7 gm., fat - 2 gm., carbohydrates - 18 gm., cholesterol - 10 mg., sodium - 160 mg. Diabetic exchange: nonfat  milk  - ½, starch/bread - :
