Description : 
 Vietnamese  beef  salad can be served at lunch.
 
       Beef Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 400 grams lean  beef mince 
 2 crushed cloves  garlic 
 1 cm piece grated  ginger 
 teaspoon  five spice  powder 
 2 tablespoons  Hoisin sauce 
 2 tablespoons low sodium  soy sauce 
 1 to 2 finely chopped seeded hot  red chiles 
 4 large  lettuce  leaves 
 1 medium julienned Lebanese  cucumber 
 1 medium julienned  carrot 
 Directions : 
 Mix fried  beef  with  garlic ,  ginger ,  five spice  powder, hoisin, soy sauce and red pepper. 
 Prepare a garnishment with slice red  capsicum , julienne Lebanese  cucumber ,  carrot ,  celery  strips and spring  onions .
