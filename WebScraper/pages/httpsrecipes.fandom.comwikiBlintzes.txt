Description : 
 Looks delicious and smells amazing!!! You can also put  apples  in instead of an  onion ... great idea. Blintzes can be served with  butter ,  sour cream ,  herring , black or  red caviar , fillet of  sturgeon ,  lox , and  salmon .
 
 Servings: 2 – 3 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  flour 
 3  eggs 
 3 cups of  milk  (or  water ) 
 0.5 teaspoon  salt 
 2 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 0.5  onion 
 Directions : 
 Mix  eggs  with 3 cups of  milk  or  water . 
 Add  salt  and  flour  and mix thoroughly. 
 The dough can be drained so that there are no  flour  lumps in it. 
 Pour  vegetable oil  into a saucer. 
 Clean an  onion  and cut it into 2 parts; take one part with a fork and dip it in  oil . 
 Use it every time for greasing the pan with  vegetable oil . 
 Heat the pan. Grease it. 
 Evenly pour thin layer of batter. 
 Cook until light brown, about 2 – 3 minutes on each side.
