 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  banana 
 4 ounces  yogurt  (plain or fruit) 
 2 oz.  water 
 Directions : 
 Add ingredients to food processor or blender and mix until smooth. Pour into small (3 oz.) paper cups or  ice  cube tray and freeze. 
 
 Remove cups before serving.
