Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup fresh  snow peas , trimmed and halved 
 1 cup sliced fresh  mushrooms 
 ½ medium red  bell pepper , cut into julienne strips 
 2 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 3 cups cooked  rice , cooked in  chicken broth 
 1½ tablespoons  rice vinegar * 
 1 teaspoon  sugar 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1 tablespoon  sesame seed , toasted 
 Directions : 
 Cook vegetables in hot  oil  over medium-high heat in wok or large skillet for 2 minutes. 
 Stir in  rice ,  vinegar ,  sugar  and  pepper . 
 Serve warm or at room temperature. 
 Sprinkle with  sesame seed .
