Ingredients : 
 6 cups fresh fruit, ( raspberries ,  grapes ,  blueberries ,  strawberries ) 
 juice of 2  limes 
 3 tbsp berry Libby's® Kerns® all nectar 
 2 teaspoons  mint 
 Directions : 
 COMBINE fruit in large bowl. 
 Add  lime juice , nectar and  mint ; toss gently to coat. 
 Refrigerate until ready to serve.
