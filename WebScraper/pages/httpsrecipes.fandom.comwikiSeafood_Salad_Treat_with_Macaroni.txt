Ingredients : 
 2 cans  crabmeat 
 12  crab leg sticks 
 2 lbs  shrimp 
 3 trays  lobster 
 2 tsp  Old Bay seasoning 
 2 tbsp  mustard 
 1 box  macaroni 
 2 tsp  mustard 
 4 sticks chopped  celery 
 2 large  onions , chopped 
 pastrami  to taste 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 salad dressing  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Cook  macaroni , drain and cool. 
 Mix  macaroni  together in a large bowl and add the ingredients listed. 
 Season to taste.
