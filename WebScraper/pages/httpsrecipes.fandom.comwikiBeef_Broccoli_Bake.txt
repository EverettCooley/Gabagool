Ingredients : 
 1 pound lean ground  Beef 
 1/2 cup chopped  Onion 
 2 cups cooked  rice  (cooked in  beef broth ) 
 1 10-ounce package frozen chopped  Broccoli , thawed, drained 
 1 cup (4 ounces) shredded  Cheddar   Cheese , divided 
 3/4 teaspoon  salt 
 1/2 teaspoon crushed  thyme  leaves 
 1/2 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1 8-ounce can  tomato  sauce 
 1 teaspoon  worcestershire sauce 
 Directions : 
 Brown  Beef  and  Onion  in large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. 
 Stir in  rice ,  Broccoli , 1/2 cup  Cheese ,  salt ,  thyme ,  pepper ,  tomato  sauce and  worcestershire sauce . 
 Turn into buttered 2-quart baking dish; sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup  Cheese . 
 Bake at 350 degrees 20 to 25 minutes.
