 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup  Peanut   butter 
 1/2 cup  honey 
 1 cup  wheat germ 
 Shredded  coconut  or: 
 Chopped  nuts 
 Directions : 
 1 In bowl, blend  Peanut   butter ,  honey  and  wheat germ . 
2 Roll into small balls Roll balls in  coconut  or  nuts . 
3 Refrigerate. 
4 Eat when chilled and hardened.
