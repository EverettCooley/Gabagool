Ingredients : 
 1 tsp  water 
 ¾ cup seasoned  rice wine vinegar 
 ½ cup  lime juice 
 ¼ cup  soy sauce 
 3  garlic  cloves, chopped finely 
 1½ tsp  salt 
 1-2 Tbs. chopped cilantro 
 1¼ cups  vegetable oil 
 ¼ cup Asian  sesame oil  (toasted) 
 6 pounds  California avocados 
 4½ pounds ahi  tuna 
 Directions : 
 Whisk together  vinegar ,  lime juice ,  soy sauce ,  garlic  and  salt ; whisk in  oil . 
 Reserve. 
 Mix cilantro with ½ diced  avocado  with 3 ounces coarsely chopped  tuna ;. 
 Gently fold in 2 tablespoons reserved dressing.
