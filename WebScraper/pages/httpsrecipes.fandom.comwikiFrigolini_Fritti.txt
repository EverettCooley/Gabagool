Description : 
 Italian fried strawberries
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound ripe  strawberries  and cleaned 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 ½ cup sweet  liqueur  (any) 
 ⅓ cup  all-purpose flour 
 1  egg 
 1  egg white 
 2 tablespoons soft  unsalted butter 
 milk 
 ¼ cup  brandy 
 powdered sugar 
 Directions : 
 Mix the sugar with the liqueur, and add the berries. 
 Mix well, and let sit for about one hour. 
 In a bowl, mix together the flour and butter. 
 Add the egg, and brandy. 
 Add enough milk to make a smooth batter. 
 Beat the egg white until stiff, and then fold this into the batter. 
 Heat the oil to 375°F. 
 Dip the berries into the batter, and fry until golden. 
 Drain on paper towels, sprinkle with powdered sugar, and serve.
