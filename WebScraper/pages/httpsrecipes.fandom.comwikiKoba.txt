 Ingredients : 
 3  bananas 
 3 tbsp  rice flour 
 1 tbsp  honey 
 1 tbsp  ground peanut 
 1 tsp  vanilla extract 
 3  banana  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Mash  bananas , and add  rice flour , honey,  peanuts  and  vanilla . 
 Stir until you obtain a soft dough, almost liquefied (you can add  milk  or  water  to obtain the right consistency). 
 Cut  banana  leaf in 2 parts and wrap 1tbs. 
 Of the dough inside each leaf.

 Slice to serve.
