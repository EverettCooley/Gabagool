Espresso Royale
 
 1/2 Cup  cognac 
 1/4 Cup  sugar 
 3 Cups Hot Brewed Espresso  coffee 
 
Bring  cognac  And  sugar  to Boil in Chafing Dish or Pan. Ignite. Ladle Hot  coffee  Into Flaming  cognac  Slowly And Carefully. Blend Well Until Flame is Extinguished. Serve in Demitasse Cups. Serves 8Source: my Old Recipes
