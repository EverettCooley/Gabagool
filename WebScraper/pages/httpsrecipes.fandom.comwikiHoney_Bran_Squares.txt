Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  honey 
 ¼ cup  butter  or  margarine 
 4 cup mini  marshmallows 
 6 cup  bran cereal 
 1 cup  peanuts 
 Directions : 
 Blend honey and  butter  in large saucepan; stir in marshmallows. 
 Cook and stir over medium-high heat until marshmallows are melted. 
 Mix cereal and  nuts ; stir in  marshmallow  mixture until well-coated. 
 Press into lightly greased 13 x 9 x 2-inch pan. 
 Cut into squares.
