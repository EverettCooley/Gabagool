Ingredients : 
 1 box  banana  Libby's® Kerns® all nectar 
 1  banana 
 1 cup Taster's Choice® 100% pure instant  coffee 
 ½ cup  heavy whipping cream 
 2 teaspoon  granulated sugar 
 ½ teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 3  coffee   ice cream 
 Directions : 
 Place nectar,  banana ,  coffee ,  vanilla extract ,  cream  and  sugar  in blender; cover. 
 Blend until smooth and frothy. 
 Pour into three tall glasses; add one scoop of  ice cream  to each glass.
