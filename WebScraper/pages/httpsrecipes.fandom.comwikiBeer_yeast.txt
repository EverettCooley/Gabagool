 About Beer yeast : 
 Wikipedia Article  About Beer yeast on Wikipedia 
 Beer yeast (also known as brewers yeast or brewing yeast) can mean any live yeast used in brewing. It can also mean yeast obtained as a by-product of brewing, dried and killed, and used as nutritional yeast.
