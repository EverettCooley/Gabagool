 Description : 
 Makes 2 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 3/4 cups nonfat dry  milk 
 1 cup cooked  brown rice 
 1/2 cup confectioners  Sugar 
 1/2 cup smooth  Peanut   butter 
 1/4 cup  honey 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine  milk ,  Sugar ,  Peanut   butter ,  honey  and  rice  in large bowl; mix well. Mixture will be stiff
