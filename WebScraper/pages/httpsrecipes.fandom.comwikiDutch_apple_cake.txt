 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  flour 
 5 tbsp  Sugar 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 2 tbsp low fat  margarine 
 2  egg  whites, slightly beaten 
 1/4 cup skim  milk 
 1/2 tsp  vanilla extract 
 4 cup sliced peeled  Apples 
 1 tsp  cinnamon , ground 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven 375 °F. Grease and  flour  a 9 in. tart or pie pan. In a mixing bowl, combine  flour , 2 t  Sugar  and  baking powder . With a pastry blender or 2 knives, cut in 1 ½ t  margarine . Add  egg  whites,  milk  and  vanilla ; beat until smooth. Batter will be stiff. Spoon into prepared pan and spread batter to fit pan. Arrange  Apple  slices, overlapping, in 2 concentric circles to cover batter. In a small bowl, combine 3 t  Sugar  and  cinnamon ; sprinkle over  Apple  slices. Dot with ½ t  margarine . Bake 30 minutes. cool 10 minutes before serving. calories: 183; fat: 2 g.
