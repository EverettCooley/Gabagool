 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 16 oz french cut  string beans 
 3 tbsp dehydrated  Onion 
 1 small jar pimentos, sliced 
 8 oz can cut  Mushrooms 
 1 med  cucumber , diced 
 1/2 small  Cauliflower  in small pieces 
 marinade 
 1/4 cup  champagne vinegar 
 ts  MSG  (optional) 
 1/4 tsp  garlic powder 
 3 drop liquid artificial sweetener 
 1/4 cup  water 
 pn  pepper  to taste 
 1/4 tsp seasoned  salt 
 Directions : 
 Place vegetables in non metal container. Make marinade - pour over vegetables - refrigerate over night. Ww handout 1970 == courtesy of dale & gail shipp, columbia md. ==
