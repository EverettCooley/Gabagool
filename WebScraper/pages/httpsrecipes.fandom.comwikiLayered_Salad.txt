Description : 
 This is a recipe I obtained from an Estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Stewart Estate in Terrell, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 head  iceberg lettuce 
 ½ cup  bell pepper 
 ½ cup  green onions 
 1 package frozen green  peas  uncooked but thawed 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 10 slices  bacon  cooked 
 1 cup shredded  Swiss cheese 
 Directions : 
 Layer in order and refrigerate.
