 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 juice of ½  lemon 
 1½ tsp  amaretto 
 1½ oz  Bourbon   or   blended whiskey 
 Directions : 
 Pour into old-fashioned glass over  ice cubes  and stir.
