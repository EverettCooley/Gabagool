Ingredients : 
 1½ ounces  light rum 
 1½ teaspoons  triple sec 
 1 tablespoon  lime juice 
 1 teaspoon  sugar 
 1  egg white 
 1  mint  Sprig 
 Directions : 
 Combine and shake all ingredients, except  mint , with ice 
 Strain into an old fashioned glass over ice cubes. 
 Add the sprig of  mint  and serve.
