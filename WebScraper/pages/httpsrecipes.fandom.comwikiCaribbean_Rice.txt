Description : 
 Serve with grilled or broiled  shrimp . Makes 4 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups hot cooked  rice 
 1 x 11-ounce  mandarin oranges , drained, coarsely chopped 
 1 x 8-ounce can crushed  pineapple , drained 
 ½ cup chopped  red pepper 
 ½ cup  slivered almonds , toasted 
 ½ cup unsweetened  grated coconut , toasted 
 ⅓ cup sliced  green onions 
 2 tablespoons  mango chutney 
 ¼ teaspoon  ground ginger 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  mandarin oranges ,  pineapple , red pepper,  almonds ,  coconut ,  green onions , chutney and  ginger  in large skillet over medium-high heat. 
 Stir and cook until ingredients are blended and thoroughly heated.
