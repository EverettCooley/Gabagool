 Description : 
 grape tomato  Salad
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cup  grape tomatoes 
 1 1/2 cups chopped  cucumber 
 2 tbsp chopped  green onions 
 1/4 cup  balsamic vinegar 
 1 tsp  Sugar 
 2 tsp  olive oil 
 1/4 tsp  black pepper 
 1/8 tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Place  tomatoes ,  cucumber  and  green onions  in a medium bowl - toss gently to combine. 
 Combine  balsamic vinegar ,  Sugar ,  olive oil ,  pepper , and  salt  in a small bowl and stir with a whisk. 
 Drizzle vinaigrette over salad; toss gently to coat.
