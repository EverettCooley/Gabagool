      Korean-style Grilled Chicken     
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  sesame seeds 
 ¼ cup  corn oil 
 ¼ cup  soy sauce 
 ¼ cup  dark corn syrup  ( Karo ) 
 1 small  onion , sliced 
 1 clove  garlic , crushed 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 ¼ tsp  ginger , ground 
 1 broiler-fryer  chicken  (cut-into pieces) 
 Directions : 
 In a shallow baking dish stir together the first eight ingredients. 
 Add  chicken , turning to coat. 
 Cover and refrigerate, turning once, at least three hours. 
 Grill over low coals, turning and basting frequently, about 50 minutes.
