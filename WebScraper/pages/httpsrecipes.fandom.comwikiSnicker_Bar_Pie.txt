Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Mclean Estate in Irving, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 Crust : 
 1½ cups  graham cracker  crumbs 
 1 tablespoon  sugar 
 6 tablespoons  unsalted butter  melted 
 Fudge layer : 
 6 tablespoons  flour 
 ½ teaspoon  baking powder 
 ⅛ teaspoon  salt 
 ½ cup  unsalted butter  cut in pieces 
 4 ounces  semisweet chocolate  chopped 
 1 ounce  unsweetened chocolate  chopped 
 ½ cup  granulated sugar 
 1 large  egg 
 1 large  egg yolk 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 8 miniature Snickers bars 
 Cream cheese layer : 
 10 ounces  cream cheese  at room temperature 
 ⅓ cup  granulated sugar 
 1 large  egg 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 Directions : 
 Crust : 
 Heat oven to 350°F. 
 Combine ingredients and press into a buttered glass pie pan and bake 5 minutes. 
 Cook on rack. 
 Fudge layer : 
 Sift  flour ,  baking powder  and  salt  into medium bowl. 
 Combine butter and chocolates in a double boiler and stir until melted and smooth. 
 Cool slightly then using an electric mixer beat  sugar ,  egg  and egg yolk in a medium bowl until slightly thickened. 
 Add  vanilla  and cooled  chocolate  mixture and mix until well blended. 
 Add dry ingredients and mix until just combined. 
 Pour into crust and bake 17 minutes then cool on rack for 10 minutes. 
 Cut up snicker bars and place on fudge layer. 
 Cream cheese layer : 
 With an electric mixer combine  cheese  and  sugar  then add  egg  and vanilla and beat until smooth. 
 Spread mixture over snicker's and bake for 15 minutes then cool on rack. 
 Melt 2 ounce  milk chocolate  and 2 tbsp  whipping cream  to drizzle on top. 
 Refrigerate and serve chilled.
