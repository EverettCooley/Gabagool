Ingredients : 
 ½ oz  lemon juice 
 2 oz  orange juice 
 2 oz  pineapple juice 
 1½ oz  rum 
 1 oz  coconut flavor rum 
 ½ oz  cherry 
 ½ oz  grenadine 
 Directions : 
 Shake with cracked  ice . 
 Serve in a 12 oz glass. 
 Garnish with a  cherry  and ½ slice  orange  in a tooth pick. 
 Serve in: hurricane glass.
