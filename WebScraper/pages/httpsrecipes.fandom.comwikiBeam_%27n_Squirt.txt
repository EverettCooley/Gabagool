 Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 oz Jim Beam®  Bourbon whiskey 
 4 oz Squirt® citrus soda 
 Directions : 
 Pour both ingredients over 6  ice  cubes in a highball glass. Stir, and serve.
