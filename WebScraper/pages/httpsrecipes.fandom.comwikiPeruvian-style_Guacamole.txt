      Peruvian-style Guacamole     
 Ingredients : 
 2  avocados 
 ¼ cup finely chopped  onions 
 ½ cup ají amarillo or rocoto  hot sauce 
 2 tbsp  water 
 1 tbsp  white vinegar 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients and serve as it is.
