Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅓ oz  Bailey's® Irish Cream 
 ⅓ oz  Kahlúa ®  coffee liqueur 
 ⅓ oz  Frangelico ®  hazelnut liqueur 
 Directions : 
 This drink is best when shaken and strained, but the proper way is to layer the drink. 
 Kahlúa  is poured first, then Bailey's is next, and  Frangelico  is layered on top.
