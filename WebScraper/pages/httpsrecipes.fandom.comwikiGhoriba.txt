 Description : 
 An authenic Moroccan bisuit, ghoriba with  almonds  are a gorgeous treat for everyone in the family.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 7 beaten  eggs 
 250g  granulated sugar 
 1 sachet of chemical  yeast 
 1 zest of  lemon 
 1Â kg chopped  almonds 
 200g fine  semolina 
 orange blossom water 
 icing sugar 
 Directions : 
 Mix, the  eggs ,  Sugar ,  yeast  and the  lemon  zest. Add the chopped  almonds  and  semolina  gradually, and stir in. Keep manipulating it until it becomes a soft dough. 
 Dampen your hands with a touch of  orange blossom water  and take a piece of dough. Roll it into a ball, and then flatten it slightly. Put the balls on a plate and sprinkle with  icing sugar . 
 Heat for 20 to 25 minutes in an oven that is set to 350F. 
 This recipe makes about 60 biscuits.
