Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves 8 
 Ingredients : 
 1  egg 
 1½ lbs lean  ground beef 
 8 oz  Italian sausage , casings removed 
 1 cup Italian seasoned  bread crumbs 
 8 oz  tomato sauce , divided 
 1 cup finely chopped  onion 
 ½ cup finely chopped  green pepper 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Beat  egg  lightly in a large bowl, add  beef ,  sausage ,  bread  crumbs,  onion ,  peppers  and ¾ cup of the  tomato sauce . 
 Mix together and form into a heart shape in an ungreased baking pan. 
 Bake uncovered for 60 minutes. 
 Spoon remaining  tomato  sauce over the top and bake an additional 15minutes or until no longer pink in the center. 
 Let stand 1 minutes and remove to serving platter.
