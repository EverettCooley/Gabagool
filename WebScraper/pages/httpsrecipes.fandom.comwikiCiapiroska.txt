 Ingredients : 
 2 shots Stolichnaya Red  vodka 
 1 whole  lime 
 4 teaspoons  caster sugar 
 Glass: Tumbler
 
 Directions : 
 Muddle the  Sugar  and  limes  in the bottom of a tumbler, add crushed  ice  and  vodka .
