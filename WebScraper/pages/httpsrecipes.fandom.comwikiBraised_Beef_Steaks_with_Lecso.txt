      Braised Beef Steaks with Lecso     
 Ingredients : 
 4  steaks  of about 180 grams each 
 30 grams  oil 
 80 grams  onions 
 80 grams lean  bacon 
 1½ cups  beef   stock  or  water 
 1 tablespoon  tomato paste 
 1 teaspoon  paprika 
 1 crushed  garlic  clove 
 ½ cup  white wine 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Trim and pound steaks,  salt  and  pepper  them, brown quickly on both sides in hot fat and set aside. 
 Sauté  bacon  and diced  onions  in the same fat, add  white wine ,  tomato paste ,  garlic ,  paprika  and stock and let simmer until tender. 
 Shortly before the end of the cooking time, add lecso and simmer for another 5 minutes. 
 Season to taste.
