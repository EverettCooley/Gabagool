 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 750 g  carrots , peeled and cut into chunks 
 2  garlic  cloves, crushed 
 1/4 cup  olive oil 
 2 tablespoons  red wine vinegar 
 1 teaspoon  paprika 
 1 pinch  chili powder 
 1 teaspoon  cumin 
 1 teaspoon coriander 
 fresh coriander, to serve 
 mint  sprigs, to serve 
 Directions : 
 Place  carrots  and  garlic  in saucepan with salted  water . Bring to boil and cook until tender. Drain. 
 Mash roughly and stir in remaining ingredients, season to taste and serve.
