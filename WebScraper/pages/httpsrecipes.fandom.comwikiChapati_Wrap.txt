 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 whole  wheat  chapati-unleavened vegan bread like a tortlla 
 1 tablespoon humus 
 1/2 cup cookd  brown rice 
 1/2 cup cooked  black beans 
 1/2 small  red onion  sliced 
 1/2 small  tomato  chopped 
 1 teaspoon  olive oil 
 1 teaspoon  balsamic vinegar 
 few sprigs fresh  cilantro 
 Directions : 
 In a small skillet saute the  tomato  and  Onion  in the  olive oil  and  vinegar . Only for a short time so the  Onion  stays just a little bit crisp. Lay out the chapati and start to layer: 1. humus - 2.  rice  - 3.beans - 4.onion and  tomato  mixture - 5.cilantro. 
 
 
 Roll up and nuke for 2 minutes.
