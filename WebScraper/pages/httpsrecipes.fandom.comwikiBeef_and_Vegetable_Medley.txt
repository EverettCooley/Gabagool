Makes 4 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3/4 pound boneless  Beef  sirloin  Steak 
 3/4 teaspoon  salt 
 3/4 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 1 medium red pepper, cut into strips 
 1 cup (about 4 ounces) sliced fresh  Mushrooms 
 1 cup sliced  zucchini 
 1 cup sliced  yellow squash 
 1/3 cup sliced  green onions 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1/2 teaspoon dried  rosemary  leaves 
 1/2 teaspoon dried  thyme  leaves 
 3 cups hot cooked  brown rice 
 Directions : 
 Partially freeze  Steak ; slice across the  grain  into 1/8-inch strips. 
 Season  Beef  with  salt  and  black pepper . Heat  oil  in large skillet over medium-high heat until hot. 
 Brown meat quickly in  oil . Add red pepper,  Mushrooms ,  zucchini ,  yellow squash ,  onions ,  garlic ,  rosemary  and  thyme ; stir-fry 3 to 4 minutes or until vegetables are tender crisp. 
 Stir in  rice ; heat thoroughly.
