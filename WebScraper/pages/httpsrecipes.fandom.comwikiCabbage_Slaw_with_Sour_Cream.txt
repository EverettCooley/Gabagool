CABBAGE SLAW AND SOUR CREAM* 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup Chilled Sour Cream 
 1 cup Lemon Juice or Pineapple Cider 
 1/2 cup Honey 
 3 cups Shredded cabbage 
 1 tsp. Paprika 
 1 tsp. Celery Salt 
 1 tsp. Salt 
 Directions : 
 Beat cream until thick. Add lemon juice and honey and salt slowly. Beat
again for 3 or 4 minutes. Add paprika, celery salt to cabbage. Add sour
cream dressing and mix lightly.
