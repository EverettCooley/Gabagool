Description : 
 An international combination of authentic pork and Dijon mustard is made with onions, Italian salad dressing, and black pepper and cooked to natural perfection. You can make the pork chops bolder with herbs and spices based in France.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4  pork loin chops , ½ thick 
 1 medium  onion , yellow or white only 
 3 tbsp.  Dijon-style mustard 
 2 tbsp. low-calorie  Italian salad dressing 
 ¼ tbsp. black  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Trim excess fat from chops with the kitchen knife. 
 Spray a 10-inch skillet with nonstick spray coating. 
 Brown chops on both sides in a hot skillet. Remove chops. 
 Add  onions ; cook and stir over medium heat 3 minutes. 
 Push  onions  aside; return chops to the skillet. 
 Combine  mustard , dressing and  pepper ; then spread over chops. 
 Cover; cook over medium-low heat 15 minutes or till  meat  is tender. Serve hot and plain with rice.
