Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup uncooked  brown rice 
 1/4 teaspoon  salt 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 1/2 cup seedless  raisins  or dried  cranberries 
 1 teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 2 1/4 cups  water * 
 milk  or  cream  (optional) 
 honey or  brown sugar  (optional) 
 fresh fruit or toasted  nuts  (optional) 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  salt ,  butter ,  raisins  and  cinnamon  with  water  2- to 3-quart saucepan. Bring to a boil; stir once or twice. 
 Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 45 to 55 minutes, or until  rice  is tender and liquid is absorbed. Fluff with fork. Serve with  milk  or  cream , honey or  brown sugar , and fresh fruit or  nuts , if desired.
