Description : 
 Always check the ingredients to make sure the product is vegan.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup hot, cooked  cornmeal 
 ½ cup  water 
 ¼ cup  coconut 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Blend all ingredients till smooth. 
 Use on bread and vegetables, but note that it does not melt well.
