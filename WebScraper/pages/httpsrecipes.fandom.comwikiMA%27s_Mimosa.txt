      Mimosa     
 Description 
 For larger quantities pour into a pretty punch bowl, stir gently, and serve.
 
 Contributed by user  MA 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 Orange juice 
 Champagne 
 Directions 
 Pour equal amounts of orange juice and Champagne into a Champagne flute. 
 Stir gently.
