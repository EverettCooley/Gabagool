      Beef Round Eye Sandwiches     
 Ingredients : 
 1 piece  Beef round eye 
 4 cup  water 
 1 cup  vinegar 
 2 teaspoon  salt 
 2 cloves of  garlic  crushed 
 1 lb  tomatoes 
 2  onions 
 1  green pepper 
 Directions : 
 Put the  beef  in a pressure cooker with the  water ,  vinegar ,  salt  and  garlic . 
 Cook in medium-heat for about 50 minutes after the regulator starts to blow steam. 
 While the  beef  is cooling down mix together diced  tomatoes  (Â¼-inch cubes), diced  onions , diced  green pepper , 1 teaspoon of  salt  and group  pepper . 
 Finely slice cooked  beef . 
 In a glass casserole, alternates layers of sliced  beef  with layers of vinaigrette and refrigerate.
