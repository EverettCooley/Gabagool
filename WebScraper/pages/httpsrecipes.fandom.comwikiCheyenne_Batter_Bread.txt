      Cheyenne Batter Bread     
 Ingredients : 
 1 qt  milk  or  water 
 2 cup Yellow or white  cornmeal 
 3 x  eggs , separated 
 4 tbl Melted  butter 
 1 1/2 tsp  salt 
 1/2 tsp  pepper 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. 
 Bring  milk  to a boil in a large saucepan over medium heat. 
 Gradually stir in  cornmeal  and cook, stirring. 
 Beat in  egg   yolks ,  butter  and seasonings. 
 In a separate bowl, beat  egg  whites . 
 Fold whites into  corn  mixture and pour into a 2-quart baking dish. 
 Bake for 20 to 30 minutes .
