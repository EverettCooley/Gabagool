Description : 
 Similar to a sake bomb.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 ounce  sake 
 1 ounce Gold  tequila 
 1 ounce  orange juice 
 1 ounce  tomato juice 
 18 ounces  Pilsner Urquell 
 Directions : 
 Pour sake,tequila,orange juice, and tomato into 4 ounce shot glass. 
 Pour pilsner into 22 ounce beer mug. 
 Drop shot glass in mug and slam down beer when glass reaches bottom.
