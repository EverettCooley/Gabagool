 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cup  cabbage , thinly sliced 
 1 green  Apple , peeled cored 
 -diced 
 1/4 cup  Onion , diced 
 2 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 chicken-flavor bouillon cube 
 1/8 tsp  allspice 
 1 dash  pepper 
 3/4 cup  water 
 2 pack sweet 'n low 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients except  water  and sweet "n low in large saucepan. Add  water . Cover and simmer 20 minutes, or until  cabbage  is tender. Stir in sweet "n low. Food exchange per serving: 1 vegetable exchange + ¼ fruit exchange? serving size (½ cup): calories: 35; protein 1gm; car: 8gm; fat: trace protein: 1gm; sod: 175mg; cooking for the health of it by carl becker, r.d.
