Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 15-ounce cans three bean salad 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 15-ounce can  kidney beans , drained and rinsed 
 1 6-ounce can marinated  artichoke  hearts, drained 
 1 teaspoon cracked  black pepper 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 lettuce  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Drain cans of three bean salad, reserving liquid from 1 can; discard remainder. 
Combine beans, reserved liquid,  rice ,  kidney beans ,  artichoke  hearts,  pepper  and  salt  in large bowl. Toss and chill. Serve over  lettuce  leaves.
