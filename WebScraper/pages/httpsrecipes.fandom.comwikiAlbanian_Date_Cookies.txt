 date cookies : 
 Ingredients : 
  powder 
 
 1 tbsp.  baking soda 
 2 tbsp.  Sugar 
 6 c.  flour 
 1 box  dates , pitted, or  raisins 
 walnuts , ground 
 SYRUP: 
 2 c.  Sugar 
 4 c.  water 
 1 tsp.  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Boil until syrup forms. 
 Combine  oil ,  orange juice ,  vanilla . 
 Combine dry ingredients and add slowly to liquid, mixing thoroughly. 
 Dough should be soft and moist. 
 If necessary add more  flour . 
 Form dough into balls. 
 Press date or  raisins  into the middle and roll. 
 Place on cookie sheet. 
 Bake 20–25 minutes at 350 degrees. 
 Dip cool cookies into warm syrup. 
 Press top of cookie into  ground nuts , and let dry.
