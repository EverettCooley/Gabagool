Ingredients : 
 1 oz  gin 
 1/2 oz sweet  sherry 
 1/2 oz dry  sherry 
 2 oz red  grape juice 
 Directions : 
 Use a double-cocktail glass and fill it with broken  ice . Add and shake the ingredients. Garnish with sprig of  mint  and serve with straw.
