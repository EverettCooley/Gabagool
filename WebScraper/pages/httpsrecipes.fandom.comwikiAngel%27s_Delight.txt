Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ tsp  grenadine 
 1½ tsp  triple sec 
 1½ tsp  sloe gin 
 1½ tsp  light cream 
 Directions : 
 Pour carefully, in order given, into cordial glass so that each ingredient floats on the preceding without mixing.
