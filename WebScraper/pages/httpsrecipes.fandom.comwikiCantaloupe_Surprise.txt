Ingredients : 
 1  cantaloupe 
 10  grapes 
 2 tbsp  maple syrup 
 1  banana 
 4  strawberries 
 Directions : 
 Slice a  cantaloupe  in half with jagged cuts. 
 Fill with  grapes ,  bananas , and  strawberries . 
 Add  maple syrup  on top.
