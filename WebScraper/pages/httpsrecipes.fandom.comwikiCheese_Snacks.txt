Description : 
 A quick snack and a good appetizer.This recipe is dated 1965.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cloves  garlic , finely chopped 
 1⅔ cups  all-purpose flour 
 ½ cup  butter 
 1⅓ cups grated sharp  cheddar  cheese 
 1½ cups grated  Parmesan cheese 
 1 teaspoon hot  chile peppers 
 2 – 6 tablespoons whole  milk  (depends on the  flour ) 
 ½ cup  Rice Krispies  (or  Grape-nuts ) 
 Directions : 
 Combine all together and add  milk  gradually to form a ball. If it doesn't take all the 6 tbsp, that's okay humidity is always a factor with  flour . Add in the  Rice Krispies , (or  Grape-nuts ) 
 Form all into a log. 
 Wrap and chill or freeze. 
 Slice and bake on a greased cookie sheet 375°F for 7 minutes — watch as these will brown quickly.
