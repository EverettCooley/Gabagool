 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cup mini shredded  Wheat  cereal 
 1/2 cup  water 
 1  butter  buds mix 
 1 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 1 sweet'n low  granulated sugar 
 1 tsp low sodium soy sauce 
 3/4 tsp  chili powder 
 1/4 tsp  cayenne pepper 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 300 °F. 
 Spray 9 x 11-inch jelly roll pan with nonstick  cooking spray . 
 Place shredded  Wheat  in baking pan. 
 Combine remaining ingredients and mix well. 
 Pour over cereal and toss gently to cover all surfaces. 
 Arrange in a single layer. 
 Bake approximately 40 minutes, or until dry, stirring every 10 minutes. 
 Cool and store in airtight container.
