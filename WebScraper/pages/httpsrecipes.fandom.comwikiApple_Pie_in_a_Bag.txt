Description : 
 Contributed by Delma at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 cups  Golden Delicious apples , peeled, cored, and thinly sliced 
 1 tablespoon  orange juice 
 ½ teaspoon  cinnamon 
 ⅔ cup  all-purpose flour 
 ⅓ cup  sugar 
 1 x 9-inch  pie shell 
 6 tablespoons (¾ stick)  butter  or  margarine 
 1 teaspoon grated  orange zest 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 400 °F. 
 Toss apples with orange juice, cinnamon, and 2 tablespoons each flour and sugar. 
 Fill pastry shell with apple mixture, mounding in the center. 
 In small bowl, combine remaining flour and sugar; with pastry blender or fork, cut in butter until crumbly. 
 Stir in orange zest. 
 Gather mixture together to make form a soft dough. 
 Flatten small pieces of dough between fingers arrange on top of apple filling. (Dough will not completely cover filling) 
 Wrap pie in a 30- by 15-inch piece of parchment paper, folding parchment over top of pie making sure it does not touch pie; staple ends of parchment to seal. 
 Set on rack in center of oven bake for 1 hour and 10 minutes. 
 Carefully open parchment and allow steam to escape. 
 Remove pie from parchment cool on wire rack.
