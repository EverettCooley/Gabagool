Ingredients : 
 mango  slices for garnish 
 ¼ cup  nuts , chopped 
 1 cup  mango , peeled and chopped 
 1¾ cups thawed  non-dairy whipped topping 
 ½ cup plain  yogurt 
 ⅓ cup cold  water 
 1 cup boiling  water 
 1 package (4 servings)  mango gelatin 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve  gelatin  in boiling  water . 
 Add cold  water . 
 Chill until slightly set. 
 Blend  yogurt  into whipped topping and then into  gelatin . 
 Stir in  mangos  and  nuts . 
 Pour into 8"x 4" loaf pan. 
 Chill until firm. 
 Unmold and garnish with  mango  slices.
