 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 oz Pkg.  orange   gelatin 
 1 cup Boiling  water 
 1 cup  pineapple juice  and  water 
 1 tbsp Grated  orange  rind 
 1 1/3 cup Crushed  pineapple , drained 
 1 cup  carrots , raw, grated 
 GARNISHES 
 Marshmallows 
 carrot  strips 
 Directions : 
 1 Dissolve  gelatin  in boiling  water . 
2 Add  pineapple juice / water  mixture and  orange  rind. 
3 Chill until slightly thickened. 
4 Then fold in  pineapple  and  carrots . 
5 Pour into 6-8 individual round molds. 
6 Chill until firm. 
7 Unmold on crisp  lettuce . 
8 Add  carrot  strips to form ears, a large  marshmallow  for the head, and a half a  marshmallow  for the tail. 
9 Serve plain or with  Mayonnaise , if desired
