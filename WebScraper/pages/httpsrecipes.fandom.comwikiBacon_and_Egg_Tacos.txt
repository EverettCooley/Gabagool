Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Steep Estate in Elmo, Texas in 1989.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 6  eggs 
 ¼ cup crumbled cooked  bacon 
 2 tablespoons  butter 
 3 slices  American cheese  diced 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  pepper 
 6 flour  tortillas  warmed 
 salsa 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl beat the  eggs  then add  bacon . 
 Melt  butter  in a skillet over medium heat and add  egg  mixture then cook and stir until  eggs  are completely set. 
 Stir in the  cheese ,  salt  and  pepper  then spoon ¼ cup down the center of each tortilla and fold sides over filling. 
 Serve with salsa.
