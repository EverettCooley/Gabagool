Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup sliced  green onions 
 1 cup sliced  celery 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 x 11-ounce can  mandarin oranges , drained 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 1 teaspoon ground  ginger 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 Â¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Cook  onions  and  celery  in  butter  until tender crisp. 
 Add  rice ,  oranges ,  lemon juice ,  ginger ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Heat thoroughly, tossing lightly with fork. 
 If desired, press firmly into individual custard cups or molds. 
 Unmold onto serving dish with baked  Cornish game hens .
