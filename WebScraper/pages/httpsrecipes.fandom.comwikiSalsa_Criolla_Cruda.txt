 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2  tomatoes , chopped 
 2 Sticks  celery , chopped 
 2 Medium  onions , chopped 
 1 Green  capsicum  ( sweet pepper ), deseeded and chopped 
 1  garlic  clove, finely chopped 
 120ml/4fl.oz.  olive oil 
 120ml/4fl.oz.  white wine vinegar 
 60ml/2fl.oz.  water 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Place all the ingredients in a medium sized mixing bowl and mix well to thoroughly blend. 
 Chill until ready to serve.
