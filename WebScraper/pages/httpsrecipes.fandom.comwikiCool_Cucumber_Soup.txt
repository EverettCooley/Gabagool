This recipe came from the Hartman Estate Sale. It was obtained it as part of the purchase of the Family Collection from the Hartman Estate in Arlington, Texas in 1984.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 Large  cucumbers 
 1/4 Cup  walnuts 
 1  fennel  or  celery  Stalk 
 3 Cups Plain  yogurt 
 1 Tablespoon  olive oil 
 Chopped  dill  for garnishment 
 Directions : 
 Peel  cucumbers  then blend in food processor with all remaining ingredients except  oil . 
 Add  oil  slowly while blending. 
 Chill for 2 hours and garnish with  dill .
