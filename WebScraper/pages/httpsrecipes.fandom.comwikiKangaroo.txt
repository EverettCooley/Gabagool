 Ingredients : 
 1 shot Stolichnaya  vodka 
 0.5 shot Noilly Prat Dry 
 Large dash  lime juice 
 
 Directions : 
 Shake the Noilly Prat and Stolichnaya together and pour into a tumbler filled with  ice  cubes. Add a generous dash of freshly squeezed  lime juice . Garnish with a wedge of  lime .
