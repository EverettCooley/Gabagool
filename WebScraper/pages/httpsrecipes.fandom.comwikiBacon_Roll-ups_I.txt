Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Yields 50 
 Ingredients : 
 6 oz  cream cheese  with  chives 
 25 slices mixed grain sandwich  bread 
 1 tbsp  milk 
 25 slices  bacon  cut in halves 
 Directions : 
 Combine milk and cheese. 
 Stir to spreading consistency. 
 Spread about 2 teaspoons on each side of bread, thin cut in 2 pieces. 
 Roll bread and wrap with bacon. 
 Place on broiler pan. 
 Bake at 350Â°F for 30 minutes or until bacon browns. 
 These freeze well. 
 Thaw before cooking. 
 Enjoy.
