Ingredients : 
 5 cup low-fat reduced sodium chicken broth 
 1½ cup  onion 
 3 cup fresh  kale 
 3 cup  sweet potato 
 1 cup smoked  turkey  breast 
 fresh herbs 
 8 oz low fat Wisconsin  Muenster  cheese 
 Directions : 
 In stock pot heat ½ cup stock and  onion ; simmer, covered, 5 minutes. 
 Add  kale ,  sweet potatoes ,  turkey  and remaining 4½ cups  chicken broth 
 Return to a simmer and cook 35 to 40 minutes or until  kale  and  potatoes  are tender. 
 Adjust seasonings, if desired. 
 To serve, ladle 1 cup hot soup into crock and top with a slice of  Muenster . Serve hot.
