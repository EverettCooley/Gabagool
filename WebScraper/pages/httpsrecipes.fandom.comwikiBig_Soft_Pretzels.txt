 Ingredients :

 1  egg , beaten

 Directions : 
 1 Lightly grease 2 large baking sheets. 
2 In a large bowl, dissolve  yeast  in lukewarm  water . 
3 Blend in 2  eggs  and  butter . 
4 Add 5 cups bread mix. 
5 Stir well. 
6 Add additional bread mix to make a soft, but not too sticky dough. 
7 Knead about 5 minutes until dough is smooth.

9 Form into pretzel shapes. 
10 For pretzel sticks, cut dough into 5 to 6 inch lengths. 
11 Place on prepared baking sheets.

14 Makes 12 to 15 large pretzels.
