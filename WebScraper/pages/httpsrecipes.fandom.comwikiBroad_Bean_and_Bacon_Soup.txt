      Broad Bean and Bacon Soup     
 Ingredients : 
 225 g (8 oz)  broad beans  - shelled 
 225 g (8 oz)  peas  - shelled 
 1 large  onion  - chopped 
 450 ml  milk 
 300 ml  vegetable stock 
 2 rashers back  bacon  - rinded, grilled and chopped 
 Directions : 
 Simmer the beans,  peas ,  onions ,  milk  and stock together for 20 minutes, until the vegetables are tender. 
 Liquidise some or all of the soup if you like. 
 Reheat gently if liquidised. 
 Serve garnished with the  bacon .
