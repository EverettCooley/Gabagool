 Description : 
 A  rum  drink
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 3/4 oz  light rum 
 1 tbsp  lime juice 
 1 1/2 tsp Green  creme de menthe 
 1/2 tsp  powdered sugar 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Shake gently with  ice  and strain into a glass. Top with a twist of  lime  peel.
