      Super Pizza topped with, Olive Oil, Sliced Campari Tomatoes, Red Onions, Sliced Chickedn Breast, Garlic, Oregano, Chopped Kale, Asiago Cheese.     
 Prep Time: 20 minutes 
 Cook time: 25 minutes  
 Serves: 4 – 6 
 
 Description : 
 This is a pizza dough that has incredible texture and taste while packing a healthy punch.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2½ cups  whole wheat flour 
 1 pkg dry  yeast 
 ¼ cup  flax seeds 
 ½ cup  sunflower seeds 
 ¼ cup granulated  garlic 
 ¼ cup  olive oil  (separated) 
 2 tsp  sea salt 
 ¼ cup  oregano   or   Italian seasoning 
 1 cup very warm water 
 1 tbsp  honey 
 cornmeal  for dusting pan (coarse works best) 
 Directions : 
 Add flour, yeast, seeds, spices, garlic, salt and mix together in a large bowl 
 Mix water, honey and 1 tbsp olive oil together and pour into dry mixture 
 mix in mixer until forms into a ball of dough 
 Coat large bowl with olive oil and place ball of dough inside, cover with plastic wrap 
 Allow to rise until doubled in bulk (about 20 minutes) 
 Lightly coat pizza pan with olive oil and dust with cornmeal 
 After dough has doubled spread on pizza pan and pinch up sides 
 bake in 450°F oven until it becomes firm (holds moisture from toppings better if par-baked) 
 top with healthy topping and bake for another 15 – 20 minutes
