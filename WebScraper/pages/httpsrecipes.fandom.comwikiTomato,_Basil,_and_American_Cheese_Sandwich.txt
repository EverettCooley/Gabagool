Ingredients : 
 2 slices fresh  white bread 
 2 slices fresh ripe  tomatoes 
 3 – 4 fresh  basil  leaves 
 1 – 2 tablespoon  mayonnaise 
 2 slices  American cheese 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Spread  mayo  on one side of both slices of bread place  basil  leaves on  mayo  then  tomato  and  Cheese  on one slice. 
 Season with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Place slice of bread on top and slice in half side to side or corner to corner& enjoy.
