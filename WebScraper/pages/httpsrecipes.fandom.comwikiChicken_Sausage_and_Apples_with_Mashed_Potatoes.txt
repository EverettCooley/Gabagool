Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 4 Servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 10 oz pkg precooked  chicken sausage  with  apple , slice ½ inch thick 
 1 tsp  unsalted butter 
 1  red onion , sliced 
 1  Golden Delicious apple , peeled and sliced 
 ½ cup  apple cider 
 ½ cup bottled  chutney , chopped 
 mashed  potatoes 
 Directions : 
 Sauté  sausage  in skillet until golden brown. 
 Remove to a plate, keep warm. 
 Heat  butter  in skillet. 
 Add  onion , saute until slightly softened. 
 Add  apple , cook until slightly softened. 
 Stir in  cider , chutney and  sausage , cook 10 minutes, or until  onion  is softened and sauce is slightly thickened. 
 Serve with mashed  potatoes .
