 Ingredients : 
 2 small sectioned  oranges 
 1 large sectioned  grapefruit 
 1 cubed red  Apple 
 2 cups  pineapple  chunks 
 1 1/2 cups chopped  mango 
 1/2 cup chopped  dates 
 1/2 cup plain  yogurt 
 2 tbsp.  lime juice 
 1 tbsp.  honey 
 1/2 tsp. grated  lime  peel 
 Directions : 
 Toss  oranges  with  grapefruit ,  Apple ,  pineapple ,  mango  and  dates . 
 Spoon into 6 fruit bowls. 
 Dressing : 
 combine remaining ingredients in blender. 
 Whir until smooth. 
 Spoon over salads to serve.
