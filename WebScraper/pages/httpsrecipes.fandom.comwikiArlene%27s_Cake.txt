ARLENE'S CAKE
 1 yellow cake mix
2 boxes instant vanilla pudding
2 cans crushed pineapple
8 oz. pkg. cream cheese
2 cups. milk
Cookie sheet with sides
Cool Whip
 Mix cake mix per package, pour in cookie sheet and bake for 15 minutes. After cake is completely cooled, mix pudding, pineapple, cream cheese and milk together
and place on top of cake. Refrigerate. When ready to serve put Cool Whip on top.
