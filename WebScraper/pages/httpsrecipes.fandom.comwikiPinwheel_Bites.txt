Pinwheel Bites
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 Slice whole wheat bread, Crust Removed 
 1 Tbsp. Miracle Whip Dressing 
 3 Slices Oscar Mayer Thin Sliced Oven Roasted  Turkey  Breast 
 1 Kraft Single 
 Directions : 
 Flatten bread With Rolling Pin or Drinking Glass. 
 Spread bread Slice Evenly With Dressing. 
 Top With Turkey And Singles; 
 Roll up Tightly. 
 Cut Crosswise Into 4 Pinwheels. 
 Stack Pinwheels on Top of Each Other And Wrap in Plastic Wrap. 
 
 
 Notes : 
 Prep Time: 5 minutes 
 Total Time: 5 minutes 
 Makes: 1 Serving 
 Save 4g of Fat And 20 Calories Per Serving By Preparing With Miracle Whip Light Dressing And Kraft 2% milk Singles.
