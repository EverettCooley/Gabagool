 Description : 
 Serves 6 to 8 people 
 Ingredients : 
 1Â½ kg of  lamb  meat cut into pieces 
 2 kg of  artichoke 
 2 kg of  green peas ( with fresh beans) 
 1 teaspoon of  ginger 
 1 pinch of  saffron 
 1 clove of  garlic 
 3 tablespoons of oil 
 the  rind  of a preserved  lemon 
 and  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Pour oil in the pressure cooker, shake the pieces of meat into it 
 Salt and add ginger and saffron, mix to make the meat impregnate 
 Add water and close the pressure cooker 
 Clean the artichoke, plunge it in water with lemon and  vinegar , cook it in hot oil and salted  water  with the sauce of the meat 
 Cook the green peas for 10mn in water then add some of the meat sauce.
