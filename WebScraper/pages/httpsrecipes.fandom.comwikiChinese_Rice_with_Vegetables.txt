Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup uncooked  rice 
 2 cups  water 
 2 tablespoons  soy sauce 
 2 teaspoons  vegetable oil 
 2 cups any combination of:
 fresh or frozen  peas 
 frozen mixed vegetables 
 julienned  carrots 
 sliced  mushrooms 
 chopped  green pepper 
 shredded  Chinese cabbage 
 broccoli  florets 
 sliced  green beans 
 ground  black pepper 
 salt  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  water , soy sauce,  oil  and vegetables in 2- to 3-quart saucepan. 
 Bring to a boil. Stir once or twice. 
 Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 15 minutes. 
 Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 5 minutes, or until  rice  and vegetables are tender and liquid is absorbed. 
 Season to taste.
