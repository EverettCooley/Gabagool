Description : 
 Contributed by Lilly at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Oreo  ®  Pie Crust Package 
 10 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2½ cups cold  milk 
 2 (4 serving) pkg Jell-O  ®  Oreo  ®  Flavor Instant pudding and pie filling 
 1 (8-oz) tub  Cool Whip   ®  whipped topping, thawed, divided 
 1 (6-oz) Oreo  ®  pie crust  [1] 
 5 Oreo  ®   chocolate  sandwich cookies, halved 
 Directions : 
 Pour  milk  into a large bowl. 
 Add dry pudding mixes. 
 Beat with wire whisk 2 minutes or until well blended. 
 Gently stir in ½ of whipped topping. 
 Pour into pie crust. 
 Refrigerate 4 hr or until set. 
 Garnish with remaining whipped topping and cookie halves just before serving. 
 Store leftover pie in refrigerator. 
 Notes : 
 
 ↑   Substitute: graham pie crust for Oreo  ®  pie crust.
