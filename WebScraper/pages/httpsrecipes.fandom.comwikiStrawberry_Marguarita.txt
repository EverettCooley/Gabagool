 Ingredients : 
 Dash of  Grenadine 
 Blend with crushed  ice 
 Serve in a Poco or decorative glass 
 Garnish with a bit of  whipped cream , a  lime  wedge, & fresh  strawberry  or  cherry 
 Directions : 
 Mix with Crushed  ice , Serve in a  Sugar  frosted Decorative glass. 
 Garnish with a  lime  wedge & fresh  strawberry  if available.
