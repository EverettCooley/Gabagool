 Directions : 
 Cook  rice  in a pressure pan with enough  water , till soft. 
 Drain and mash. 
 Heat  milk  to a thick and creamy consistency. 
 Add  condensed milk , mashed  rice ,  sugar ,  almond  powder,  cardamom  powder and  saffron . 
 Cook till creamy. 
 Serve hot with puris.
