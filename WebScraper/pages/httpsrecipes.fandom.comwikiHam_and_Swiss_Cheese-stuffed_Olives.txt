 Cookbook: Caring & Cooking for the Hyper-Active Child 
 Author: Mary Jane Finsand 
 Typed By: Susan alexanderjamesmom@gmail.com 
 Ingredients : 
 pitted  olives 
 2 tbsp sweet  butter  30 ml 
 ½ cup white  Swiss cheese  (grated) 125 ml 
 ½ cup  ham  (cooked and grated) 125 ml 
 Directions : 
 Cream together butter and cheese. 
 Stir in ham. 
 Stuff into well-drained, pitted olives (ripe or green), using pastry tube or tip of spoon. 
 Other Stuffing Suggestions : 
 walnut  halves 
 pecan  halves 
 water chestnuts 
 small whole  shrimp 
 wiener  sticks 
 mushrooms 
 zucchini 
 small  cauliflowerettes
