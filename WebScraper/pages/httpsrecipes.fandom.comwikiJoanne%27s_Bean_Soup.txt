Description : 
 Contributed by  Pressurecookerrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  milk 
 1  onion , diced 
 1  carrot , diced 
 6 small  ham hocks 
 1 lb  navy beans 
 Directions : 
 In a 6 quart pressure cooker, place above ingredients with approximately 2½ quarts of water. 
 Pressure 45 minutes.
