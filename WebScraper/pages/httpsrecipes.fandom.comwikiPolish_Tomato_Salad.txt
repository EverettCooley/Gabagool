      Polish Tomato Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 tomatoes 
 spring onions 
 an  onion 
 vinaigrette 
 Zubrowka  (bison herb  vodka ) 
 Directions : 
 Slice or quarter  tomatoes  and mix with thinly sliced spring  onion  and very thinly sliced  onion  to taste. 
 Mix with vinaigrette and a shot of Zubrowka. 
 It is the Zubrowka which gives the  tomatoes  that special twang!.
