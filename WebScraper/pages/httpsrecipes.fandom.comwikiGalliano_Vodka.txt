Ingredients : 
 2 1/2 cups  vodka 
 3 teaspoons  pure vanilla extract 
 1/2 teaspoon pure  anise extract 
 1 tablespoon  lime juice 
 4 drops yellow food color 
 2 cups  granulated sugar 
 Directions : 
 Make a syrup of 2 cups  Sugar  and 2/3 cup  water . Bring to a boil and let cool; then add ingredients listed above. No waiting to use.
