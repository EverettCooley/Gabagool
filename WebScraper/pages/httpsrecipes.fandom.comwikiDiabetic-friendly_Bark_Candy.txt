Ingredients : 
 1 lbs  milkcote   or  whitecote chocolate 
 1 cup crunchy cereal 
 watermelon seeds 
 any other crunchy food 
 Directions : 
 In the top of a double boiler, melt coating over hot, not boiling,  water . 
 Add cereal and seeds. 
 Blend together thoroughly, then pour onto waxed paper and spread thinly. 
 Let stand until firm. 
 Break into pieces.
