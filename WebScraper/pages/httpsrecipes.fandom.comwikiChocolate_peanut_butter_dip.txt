 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 oz Pkg  cream cheese , softened 
 1/3 cup Creamy  Peanut   butter 
 1 dash Ground  cinnamon 
 1/4 cup  chocolate  syrup 
 2 tbsp  milk 
 Assorted cut-up fruit 
 Assorted cookies 
 Pretzels 
 Directions : 
 1 From: sesame street mag. 
2 Parents guide 
3 this one is so easy to make! your kids will be able to fix and dip into it within 5 minutes of coming home. 
4 Use an assortment of dippers...fruit, cookies, pretzels. 
5 In large bowl with electric mixer on medium, beat  cream cheese ,  Peanut   butter , and  cinnamon  until smooth. 
6 Gradually beat in  chocolate  syrup and  milk  until well blended. 
7 Serve with fruit, cookies, or pretzels. 
8 prep time: 5 minutes
