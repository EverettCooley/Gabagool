 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 	cup 	Finely  Grated Carrots 
 3/4 	cup 	 granulated sugar 
 1/2 	cup 	 canola oil 
 1 	cup 	Boiling  water 
 1 1/2 	cup 	Wholewheat  flour 
 1/2 	cup 	 unsweetened cocoa powder 
 1 	tsp 	 cinnamon 
 1 1/2 	tsp 	 baking powder 
 1/2 	tsp 	 salt 
 Directions : 
 1 	Pre-heat oven to 350 °F.
 2 	In a large bowl, combine  carrots ,  Sugar  &  oil .
 3 	Pour  water  over the mixture.
 4 	In a separate bowl, combine the rest of the ingredients.
 5 	Add to the  carrot  mixture & mix well.
 6 	Pour into a non-stick or lightly oiled 8" square pan.
 7 	Bake for 35 minutes.
