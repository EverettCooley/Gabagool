Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ finely chopped  ginger root 
 ¼ cup chopped  onion 
 ¼ cup chopped  carrot 
 2 tablespoon(s) chopped  celery 
 ½ cup(s)  soy sauce 
 1 cup  peanut oil 
 ½ cup  white vinegar 
 Directions : 
 Blend together onion, carrot and celery until very fine. 
 Add other ingredients, including freshly ground black pepper if desired. 
 Shake well before use. 
 Best made a day ahead for flavors to meld.
