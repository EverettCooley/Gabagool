Ingredients : 
 500 g raw dry  groundnuts 
 100 g  butter 
 2 spoons  tomato paste 
 2 small  onions 
 2 teaspoons mixed spices to taste 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Shell groundnuts, clean and grind into  flour . 
 Put the other ingredients into a saucepan with 500cc of  water  and bring it to the boil. 
 Simmer for 30 minutes. 
 Serve with injera.
