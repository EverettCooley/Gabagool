Description : 
 Meatballs.
Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: my old recipes 
 Serves 6 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  ground beef 
 1  egg 
 ⅓ cup  Parmesan cheese 
 ½ cup  bread crumbs 
 1 tbsp  oregano 
 2 drops  garlic oil 
 1  onion , chopped fine 
 2 tbsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredient together and form into balls. 
 Brown in hot  oil .
