Description : 
 This can also be tossed in a bowl and served as a mixed salad.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Makes 4 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pkgs (3 oz each)  ramen  noodles 
 8 oz thickly sliced roast  beef , cut in narrow strips 
 2 cups each shredded  lettuce  and  bean sprouts 
 2  cucumbers , sliced 
 1  red bell pepper , sliced 
 ½ cup chopped unsalted  peanuts 
 1 cup torn  mint  leaves 
 ¾ cup Thai  peanut sauce  mixed with 3 tbsp water 
 Directions : 
 Soak noodles in a bowl of warm water to cover 10 minutes. 
 Arrange next 6 ingredients and ¾ cup mint leaves on plates, leaving space in center for noodles. 
 Put noodles in a colander under running warm water. 
 Break apart with fingers until softened. 
 Drain, toss with ¼ cup peanut sauce and rest of mint. 
 Add to plates and serve with rest of sauce.
