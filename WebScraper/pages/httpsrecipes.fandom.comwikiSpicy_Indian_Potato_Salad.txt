 Ingredients : 
 3 – 4 medium sized  potatoes , boiled (I like using both, the white and  red potatoes . Its combination is taste is just wonderful.) 
 1 tbsp extra virgin  olive oil 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 ½ cup chopped  coriander leaves 
 1 tbsp fresh  lemon juice 
 1 tsp  chaat masala 
 Directions : 
 Peel and slice  potatoes  into a salad bowl. Add remaining ingredients and toss. 
 Serve warm or at room temperature. 
 Spicy Notes : 
 Chaat masala  is a blend of multiple seasonings, including  cumin , coriander,  black salt ,  ginger , and the tart and tangy  dried mango powder  ( amchoor ). It is easily available at any Indian grocery store.
