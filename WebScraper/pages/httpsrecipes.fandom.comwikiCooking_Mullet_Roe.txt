Ingredients : 
 mullet roe 
 cornmeal 
 salt and pepper to taste 
 peanut oil for frying 
 Directions : 
 Salt and pepper the mullet roe lightly, being careful not rupture the casings - roll the mullet roe in corn meal and fry in hot peanut oil until golden brown and crispy on the outside. 
 Serve as a finger food - with hot sauce or catsup.
