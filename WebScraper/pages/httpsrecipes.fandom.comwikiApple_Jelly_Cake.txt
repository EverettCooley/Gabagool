Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the O'Connor Estate in Lubbock, Texas in 1991.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 12 box butter  cake mix 
 8 tablespoons  sour cream 
 30  eggs 
 5 cups  apple jelly 
 11 cups  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Prepare cake mix as you normally would but add in the sour cream and mix well. 
 Spray 2 big cake pans with cooking spray and bake for 30 minutes. 
 Meanwhile in a large bowl mix sugar and jelly together until well blended and set aside. 
 When cake is done take it out of the oven and let cool completely. 
 Put icing on the first layer then pour remaining icing on top of cake and spread or serve as two cakes.
