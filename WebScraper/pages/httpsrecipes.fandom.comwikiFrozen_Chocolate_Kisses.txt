 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  heavy cream , whipped 
 6 oz soft  cream cheese 
 2 T  sour cream 
 1/8 cup  cream  & 1/8 cup  water 
 instant  chocolate  pudding 
 Directions : 
 Whip 1 cup  cream , set aside. 
 In separate bowl,  cream   cream cheese , add  sour cream  and  cream  and  water . 
 Mix well. Stir in pudding powder and blend well. 
 Fold into the  whipped cream . 
 Using a pastry bag with a star tip, pipe hershey size kisses onto a sheet pan covered with wax paper. 
 Place in freezer til solid, then store in freezer.
