Ingredients : 
 1Pkg  firm tofu 
 2  celery  Stalks 
 1 Sprig  green onion 
 1/2 Tsp Dehydrated  Onion 
 1/2 Tsp Dehydrated  garlic 
 1/2 Tsp  salt 
 1/2 Tsp  turmeric 
 Fresh Ground  black pepper 
 2/3 Cup Nayonaise 
 Directions : 
 Chop vegetables into small pieces. 
 Crumble tofu into a bowl and add all other ingredients. 
 Mix well, cover and chill for at least 2 hours before serving.
