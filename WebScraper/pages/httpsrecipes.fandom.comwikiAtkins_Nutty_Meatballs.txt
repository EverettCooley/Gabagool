 Description : 
 A favorite from the Atkins recipe archives. Weve only changed the method: these are baked instead of sauteed.
 
       Description     
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound ground  Beef  (85% lean) 
 3 tablespoons  sour cream 
 1 tablespoon diced  Onion 
 1/2 cup finely chopped  walnuts 
 1  garlic  clove, pushed through a press 
 1 1/2 teaspoons  salt 
 Directions : 
 1. Heat oven to 400 °F. In a medium bowl, mix  Beef ,  sour cream ,  Onion ,  walnuts ,  garlic , and  salt . Form into 24 small meatballs; place on a baking sheet. Bake 15 minutes, until nicely browned.
