coffee  Frosted
 
 2 Cups Brewed  coffee , Chilled 
 1 Pint  vanilla , coffee  or  chocolate   Ice cream 
 
Mix  coffee  And  Ice cream  in Bowl And Beat With hand Beater Until Blended And Thick or Use a Shaker or Glass Jar And Shake Until Blended, or Blend in Electric Blender. Serve at Once in Tall Glasses With  ice . Serve 3 to 4Source: my Old Recipes
