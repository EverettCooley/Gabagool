Description : 
 Makes 6 servings (generous 1 cup each).
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 2 cups sliced  zucchini  (about ¾ pound) 
 ½ cup uncooked  rice 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  sage 
 ¼ teaspoon  thyme 
 1 quart (4 cups)  chicken broth 
 2 tablespoons grated  Parmesan cheese 
 Directions : 
 Cook  onion  and  garlic  in  butter  in Dutch oven or large saucepan until soft but not brown. 
 Add  butter ,  zucchini ,  rice ,  pepper ,  sage ,  thyme  and broth; bring to a boil. Stir once or twice. 
 Lower heat; simmer 15 to 20 minutes or until  rice  is tender. 
 Ladle into bowls; sprinkle with  cheese .
