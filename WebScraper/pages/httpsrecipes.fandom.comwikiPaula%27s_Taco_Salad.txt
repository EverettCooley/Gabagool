Description : 
 Here is an easy salad recipe you can prepare for your family or take to your next family get-together.
 
 Contributed by Paula in California at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ½  onion , chopped 
 3  tomatoes 
 1 head  lettuce 
 4 oz  cheese , grated 
 8 oz  Thousand Island dressing 
 Taco sauce  to taste 
 1 bag  tortilla chips 
 1 large  avocado , opt. 
 1 lb  ground beef 
 1 can  kidney beans 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Chop onion, tomatoes, and lettuce. 
 Toss with cheese, dressing and taco sauce. 
 Crunch and add chips. 
 Brown meat, add kidney beans, salt and simmer 10 minutes and mix into cold salad. 
 Serve immediately (or prepare ahead of time and add cheese and chips right before serving).
