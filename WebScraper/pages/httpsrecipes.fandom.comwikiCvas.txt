Description : 
 In Romanian: Bautura sanatoasa (Cvas)
 
 Ingredients : 
 12 qts / 12 l  water 
 2 lbs / 1 kg  rye bread 
 1 oz / 25 g  yeast 
 1 lb / 500 g  sugar 
 12  raisins 
 12  rice  grains 
 peel from half a  lemon 
 Directions : 
 Dry the thinly sliced  bread  in the oven. 
 Boil the  water , add the  sugar  and pour on top of the dried  bread  that was placed on the bottom of a 15 qt glass jar. 
 Add the  lemon peel , cover with a napkin and let stand for 4 – 5 hours. 
 Strain through a dense sieve, add the  yeast  dissolved in a little  water , mix, cover with a napkin and let stand another 4 – 5 hours until the drink starts to foam. 
 Strain a second time and pour in bottles. 
 Leave two inches empty at the top. 
 To each bottle add a  raisin  and a  rice  grain. 
 Cap with a boiled cork and tie it with a string. 
 Do this for all bottles. 
 Keep the bottles horizontal in a cool place. 
 In 2 – 3 days, the cvas is ready. 
 Do not stir the bottle before uncorking.
