Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Yield: About 4½ cups. Ten servings 
 Preparation Time: 0:05 
 Ingredients : 
 5  English toffee -flavored candy bars 1⅛ oz each; crushed 
 1½ tbsp  amaretto   or  other  almond-flavored liqueur 
 3 tbsp flaked  coconut , toasted 
 12 oz frozen whipped topping, thawed 
 Directions : 
 Gently fold first 3 ingredients into whipped topping.
