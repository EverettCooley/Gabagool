Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  rum 
 ½ oz 151-proof  rum 
 ½ oz  vodka 
 ½ oz  tequila 
 ½ oz  triple sec 
 1 oz  amaretto 
 2 oz  pineapple juice 
 1 oz  orange juice 
 1 oz  cranberry juice 
 crushed  ice 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients with  ice  and pour into a hurricane glass. 
 Top with a  pineapple  ring, slice of  orange , and  cherry  speared on a paper umbrella.
