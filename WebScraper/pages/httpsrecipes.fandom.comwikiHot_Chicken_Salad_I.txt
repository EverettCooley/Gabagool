Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  chicken , cooked 
 1 cup  rice , cooked 
 1 cup  celery 
 ¾ cup  mayonnaise 
 1 cup  mushrooms , sliced 
 1 tsp chopped  onion 
 1 tsp  lemon juice 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 can  cream of chicken  soup, undiluted 
 1 cup  water chestnuts 
 Topping : 
 ½ stick  margarine 
 ½ cup  almonds 
 1 cup crushed  corn flakes 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients next day put in casserole. 
 In separate pan melt margarine, and stir in crushed corn flakes and slivered almonds. 
 Spoon over casserole. 
 Bake for 35 minutes, uncovered, in 350°F degree oven.
