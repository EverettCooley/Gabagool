 Description : 
 Try it! You can make it in the microwave too, just remember to stir every minute." Original recipe yield: 20 servings.
 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 tablespoons unsweetened  cocoa 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 1 (14 ounce) can  sweetened condensed milk 
 
 
 Directions : 
 1 - Add  cocoa ,  butter ,  milk  to pan. Cook for approx 10 minutes. Form into small balls and chill.
