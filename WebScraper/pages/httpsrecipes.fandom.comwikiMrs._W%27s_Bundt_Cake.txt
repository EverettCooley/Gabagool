Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1¼ cups  butter 
 2¼ cups  sugar 
 7  eggs 
 2½ cups  flour 
 2½ tsp  vanilla  or  lemon extract 
 Directions : 
 Grease a 12-cup bundt pan and coat with sugar. 
 Cream shortening and sugar; add unbeaten eggs, one at a time beating well after each. 
 Add flour all at once and beat well. 
 Add vanilla. 
 Bake in the bundt pan at 325°F for 1 hour or until done. 
 Cool at least 10 minutes. 
 Then turn upside down on a cake plate. 
 This cake is very good served with ice cream or fresh fruit or both.
