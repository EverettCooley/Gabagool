 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 100 grams  white chocolate 
 1 can  sweetened condensed milk 
 2 tsp  gelatine 
 2 tbsp hot  water 
 500 ml  cream 
 Directions : 
 1. Melt  white chocolate  in double saucepan. Set aside. 2. Whip  cream  till thick and starting to form very soft peaks. 3. Into  cream  mix gently stir in  sweetened condensed milk . Set aside 4. Sprinkle the  gelatine  over the hot  water , whisk until the  gelatine  has disolved. 5. Quickly add  gelatine  & a small amount of the  cream / condensed milk  mixture to the melted  chocolate . Stir until well combined. (you may need to reapply heat to ensure it maintains smooth, creamy texture) 6. Gently fold the cooled  chocolate  mixture into the remaining  cream  &  condensed milk . 7. Place into serving glasses and refrigerate till firm
