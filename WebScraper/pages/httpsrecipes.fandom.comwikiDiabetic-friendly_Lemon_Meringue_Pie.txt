Ingredients : 
 1½ cups  water 
 4 packs Equal (or 1 tbsp sweetener) 
 4½ tsp  cornstarch 
 2  eggs 
 ⅓ cup  lemon juice 
 Meringue : 
 4  egg whites 
 4 pack Equal 
 1½ tsp  cream of tartar 
 Directions : 
 Cook all ingredients in double boiler until thickened. 
 Pour into pie shell. 
 Top with meringue which has been beaten stiff. 
 Bake 450°F for 15 minutes or until golden brown.
