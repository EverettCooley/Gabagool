Description : 
 Makes 6½ cups
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cup uncooked  quick oatmeal 
 1 cup chopped  peanuts  (no skins) 
 ½ cup  Grape-Nuts 
 ½ cup  bran 
 ¼ cup granulated  sugar substitute 
 ⅓ cup  vegetable oil 
 ½ cup  wheat germ 
 ½ cup  raisins 
 Directions : 
 Spread the  oatmeal  on a cookie sheet and heat in a 350°F oven for 10 minutes. 
 Combine all but the  wheat germ  and  raisins . 
 Bake on an ungreased cookie sheet or pan for 20 minutes, stirring once to brown evenly. 
 Allow mixture to toast in the oven. 
 Add  wheat germ  and  raisins . 
 Refrigerate in glass jars or plastic containers. 
 Nutritional information : 
 ¼ cup serving = 140 calories, 1 starch/bread, 1 fat exchange 15 grams carbohydrate, 5 grams protein, 7 grams fat, 57 mg sodium
