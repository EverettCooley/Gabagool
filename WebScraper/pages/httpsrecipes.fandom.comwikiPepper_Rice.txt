Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 teaspoons  vegetable oil 
 1 teaspoon  hot chili oil 
 ½ cup diced  red pepper 
 ½ cup diced  yellow pepper 
 ½ cup diced  green pepper 
 2 to 3 cloves  garlic , minced 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 ½ teaspoon seasoned  salt 
 Directions : 
 Heat oils in large skillet; add  peppers  and  garlic . Cook until tender. 
 Stir in  rice  and  salt . Cook 3 minutes, stirring constantly, until thoroughly heated.
