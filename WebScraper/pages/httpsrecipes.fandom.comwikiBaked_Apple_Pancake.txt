      Baked Apple Pancake     
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  butter 
 3 tablespoons  sugar 
 ½ teaspoon  cinnamon 
 ½ teaspoon  baking powder 
 ½ cup  milk 
 1  egg 
 1 teaspoon  salad oil 
 1  apple  -- pared and sliced 
 ½ cup pancake mix 
 Directions : 
 Melt butter and combine with apple, sugar, and cinnamon. 
 Heat together. 
 Combine pancake mix, 1 tablespoon sugar, baking powder, egg, milk, and oil. 
 Pour this mixture over the apple mixture. 
 Bake for 12 minutes. 
 Serve with powdered sugar or syrup.
