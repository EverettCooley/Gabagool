 An easy Crock Pot cassoulet, with  chicken  and  Great Northern beans .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 1 large  onion , finely chopped 
 4 boneless skinless  chicken  thighs, chopped 
 ¼ pound cooked smoked  sausage , diced 
 3 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1 teaspoon dried  thyme  leaves 
 ½ teaspoon  black pepper 
 4 tablespoons  tomato paste 
 2 tablespoons  water 
 3 cans (about 15 ounces each)  great Northern beans , rinsed and drained 
 3 tablespoons minced fresh  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Heat  olive oil  in large skillet over medium heat. 
 Add  onion  to hot  oil ; cook and stir until  onion  is tender, about 4 minutes. 
 Stir in  chicken ,  sausage ,  garlic ,  thyme , and  pepper . 
 Cook 5 to 8 minutes, or until  chicken  and  sausage  are browned.
