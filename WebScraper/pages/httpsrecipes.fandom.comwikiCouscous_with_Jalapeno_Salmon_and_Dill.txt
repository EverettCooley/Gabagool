 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  couscous 
 2 cups boiling  water 
 6 ounce can dry pack  smoked salmon 
 6 ounces  heavy cream 
 2 tablespoons fresh  dill 
 2  jalapenos  sliced paper thin 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Place couscous and boiling water in a bowl. 
 Cover and set aside for 5 minutes. 
 With a fork flake the salmon into bite-sized pieces. 
 In a small saucepan heat the cream. 
 Add dill, jalapenos salt and pepper. 
 Fluff the couscous and place in serving bowl. 
 Toss with salmon. 
 Drizzle cream sauce over top. 
 Serve immediately.
