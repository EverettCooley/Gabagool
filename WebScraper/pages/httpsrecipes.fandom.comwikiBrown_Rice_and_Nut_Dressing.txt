Description : 
 Serve with  chicken  breasts or as a side dish. Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup uncooked  brown rice 
 2½ cups  chicken broth 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 1 cup chopped  celery 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1½ tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 1 teaspoon seasoned  salt 
 dash ground  black pepper 
 1 cup chopped  walnuts 
 6 baked  chicken  breast halves 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice  and  chicken broth  in 2- to 3-quart saucepan. Heat to boiling. Stir. 
 Reduce heat, cover and simmer 45 minutes. 
 Cook  onion ,  celery  and  garlic  in  butter  until tender; stir into cooked  rice . 
 Stir in  salt ,  pepper  and  nuts . 
 Serve with  chicken  breasts or as a side dish.
