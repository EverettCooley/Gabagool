Ingredients : 
 1½ cups chopped  ham 
 1 cup  flour 
 2 tbsp chopped  onion 
 1  egg 
 1 tsp  curry powder 
 1 tbsp chopped  sweet pepper 
 1 cup mashed  mango 
 1 cup finely chopped  celery 
 ½ cup  milk 
 Directions : 
 Beat  egg  slightly. 
 Mix  ham ,  flour  and  curry  powder. 
 Stir in  milk . 
 Add  egg ,  celery ,  pepper ,  onion  and  mango . 
 Shape in a loaf. 
 Bake in a flat pan for 1 hour at 325°F.
