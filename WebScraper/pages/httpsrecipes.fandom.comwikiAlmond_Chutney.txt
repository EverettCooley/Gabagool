      Almond Chutney     
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  almonds 
 4 to 6  green chillies 
 garlic / ginger  - small piece 
 coriander  leaves 
 small piece of  tamarind 
 salt  for taste 
 Seasoning : 
 oil  as required 
 1 tsp  mustard seeds 
 1 tsp  jeera 
 4 to 6  curry leaves 
 Directions : 
 Grind all the ingredients finely. 
 Heat the  oil  for seasoning. 
 Add the  mustard ,  jeera seeds ,  curry leaves . 
 Add to the  chutney , and mix well.
