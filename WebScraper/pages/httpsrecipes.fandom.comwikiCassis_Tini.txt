 Ingredients : 
 2 shots Stolichnaya  vodka 
 0.5 shots De Kuyper  crème de cassis 
 2.5 shots  cranberry juice 
 garnish: Stemmed  cherry ,  blueberries  or  red currants  if desired. 
 Glass: Martini 
 Directions : 
 Shake all the ingredients together with  ice  and strain into a cocktail glass. Garnish with a stemmed  sherry ,  blueberries  or a sprig of  red currants  if desired.
