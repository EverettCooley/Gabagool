 Description : 
 In Romanian: Mamaliga cu lapte la cuptor
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 qt  milk /1 l  milk , 
 corn  meal as needed to make a rather soft polenta 
 5 oz/150 g grated Parmesan, 
 5 oz/150 g cubed  Swiss cheese , 
 6  eggs , 
 salt , 
 1 cup  sour cream , 
 a little  butter 
 a little more grated Parmesan 
 Directions : 
 Make a rather soft polenta from the  milk  and  corn  meal. Set aside from heat and mix in the grated Parmesan,  Swiss cheese  and  eggs . Sprinkle some  salt . 
 butter  a heat resistant glass dish and place the polenta inside. Pour the  sour cream  and sprinkle some grated Parmesan on top. Place in the oven to brown it slightly.
