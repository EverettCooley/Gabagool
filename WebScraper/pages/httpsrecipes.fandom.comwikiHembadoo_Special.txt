Ingredients : 
 4 cl  gin 
 4 cl Bacardi  gold or brown 
 4 cl  vodka 
 1 cl  grenadine  syrup 
 12 cl  pineapple juice 
 crushed  ice 
 Directions : 
 Mixing  gin ,  rum  and vodka in a glass half filling with pushed  ice , then add a little bit  grenadine  syrup and fill it on with the  pineapple juice . 
 Decorating the glass with a  orange  plate.
