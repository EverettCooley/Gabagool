 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/6 cup fai 
 1 1/3 cup  mint 
 1/4 cup Mien  cilantro 
 1/2 cup  chives  chopped 
 1/6 cup  lemon basil 
 2 cups chopped  cilantro 
 1/3 cup  lime  leaves 
 1/2 white  Onion 
 3 stalks  lemon grass  sliced 
 10 chiles arbol minced 
 1 head  garlic 
 8 pounds boneless  Beef  chuck, cooked and chopped 
 2  limes  cut in half 
 salt  to taste 
 1 1/2 teaspoon red pepper powder 
 1 1/2 teaspoon  MSG  (optional) 
 2 teaspoons  black pepper 
 3 1/2 teaspoons sauce 
 1/4 cup  rice  powder 
 6 cups  bean sprouts 
 Directions : 
 Fai,  mint ,  chives ,  lemon basil , Chopped  cilantro ,  lime  leaves put in the same bowl. 
 In a separate bowl add sliced  Onion ,  lemongrass , chiles and  garlic  minced. 
 Put Chopped meat in large bowl. 
 Squeeze  lime  into meat add  salt , red pepper,  MSG , Sprinkle  black pepper . 
 Add  Onion   garlic  mixture. 
 Mx well 
 Add sauce. 
 Mix well. 
 Add  rice  powder. 
 Mix well . 
 Add  bean sprouts 
 Mix well 
 Add  cilantro  Mixture 
 Mix well 
 Eat with  lettuce  and  rice
