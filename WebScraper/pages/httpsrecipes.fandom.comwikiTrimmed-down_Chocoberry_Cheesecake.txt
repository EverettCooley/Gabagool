 Ingredients : 
 Graham bottom : 
 ½ cup  graham cracker  crumbs 
 1 tbsp  butter  or  margarine 
 Cheesecake : 
 1 cup non-fat  cottage cheese 
 1 pack  Neufchatel   cheese 
 1 cup  sugar 
 ⅓ cup Hershey's  cocoa 
 1 pack frozen  strawberries  in syrup 
 ⅓ cup frozen  egg substitute 
 strawberries 
 whipped topping 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 325 °F. 
 Prepare  Graham bottom 
 Graham bottom : 
 Stir together graham cracker crumbs and melted  butter  or  margarine 
 press onto bottom of 8-inch springform pan. 
 Cheesecake : 
 In food processor, place  cottage cheese ; process until smooth 
 Add  Neufchatel   cheese ,  sugar ,  cocoa  and  strawberries ; process until smooth. 
 Stir in  egg substitute . 
 Pour gently onto graham mixture in pan. 
 Bake 55 to 60 minutes or just until almost set in center. 
 Cool completely in pan on wire rack. 
 Refrigerate; serve cold, topped with whipped topping and  strawberries .
