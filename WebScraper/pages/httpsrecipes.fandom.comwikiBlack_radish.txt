 About Black radish : 
 Latin name: Raphanus Sativus Niger = black radish
 The Black Radish is a biennal vegetable and much larger than the red radish. Originally from the Central Asia
and it's cultivated mostly in Japan but on European continent as well. Can be used fresh and raw. Used to calm the coughing in combination with bee  honey .
 It has a rough black skin with hot-flavored white flesh, is round or irregularly pear shaped,[9] and grows to around 10 cm (4 in) in diameter.
