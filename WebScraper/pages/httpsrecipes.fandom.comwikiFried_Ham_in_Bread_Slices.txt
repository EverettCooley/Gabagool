      Fried Ham in Bread Slices     
 Ingredients : 
 ham 
 bread 
 chopped greens (dill,  parsley ) 
 mustard 
 Directions : 
 Spread  mustard  on every  ham  slice and fry in  butter , then out every slice on fried piece of bread and sprinkle with chopped greens.
