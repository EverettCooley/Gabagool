 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup chopped  onions 
 3/4 cup yellow  corn  meal 
 2 tsp  chili powder 
 1 cup evaporated skim  milk 
 1/4 cup  water 
 16 oz whole kernel  corn , canned, d 
 16 oz  black beans , canned, rinse/d 
 16 oz canned stewed  tomatoes 
 4 oz canned diced mild green chil 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 degrees lightly spray an 8" square baking pan with non stick spray. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients,. Mix well, place in prepared pan. Bake uncovered 45 minutes. Nutrition (per serving): 293 calories, total fat 1 g (4% of calories)
