Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (12 oz) pkg  chocolate chips 
 1 (12 oz) pkg  butterscotch pieces 
 3 cups  cashew  nuts 
 Directions : 
 Melt chips. 
 Add cashews. 
 Drop by teaspoonsful onto wax paper and let cool. 
 Store with wax paper between each layer so they won't stick together.
