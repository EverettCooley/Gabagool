 Description : 
 Akoho misy Sakamalao  ( Chicken  with  garlic  and  ginger ) - Serves 4 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2-inch piece  fresh ginger , grated 
 6  garlic  cloves, crushed 
 salt 
 4 Large  Chicken  Joints, halved 
 2 Tbs.  oil 
 Directions : 
 In a small bowl, mix together the  ginger ,  garlic  and  salt , then rub the mixture all over the  Chicken  pieces. 
 Heat the  oil  in a large frying pan, add the seasoned  Chicken  and cook gently over a low heat for about 40 minutes, turning from time to time, until the  Chicken  is cooked through. Serve hot.
