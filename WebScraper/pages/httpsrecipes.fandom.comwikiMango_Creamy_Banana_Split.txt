Ingredients : 
 1  banana , split lengthwise 
 3 scoops  cottage cheese 
 1  mango , peeled and sliced 
 6 slices  papaya 
 1 tbsp  granola 
 3 oz  cream cheese 
 1 tbsp  lime juice 
 3 tbsp  honey 
 ¾ cup whipped  heavy cream 
 Directions : 
 Place 3 scoops of creamed  cottage cheese  on  banana  halves sliced lengthwise. 
 Top with  mango  and  papaya  slices. 
 Sprinkle with 1 tbsp granola. 
 Serve with dressing by blending 3 oz  cream cheese , 1 tbsp  lime juice , 3 tbsp honey and ¾ cup whipped  heavy cream .
