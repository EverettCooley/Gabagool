Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Unknown 
 Ingredients : 
 1 fifth  whiskey 
 1 fifth  sauterne 
 1 fifth  burgundy 
 1 fifth  sherry 
 1 fifth  champagne 
 1 fifth  soda water 
 oranges 
 lemons 
 Directions : 
 Mix and chill the first four ingredients overnight. 
 At serving time, add chilled champagne and soda water. 
 Stir. 
 Into each glass, place a slice of orange and a slice of lemon. 
 Makes 40 servings note: one of two servings is all you need per person. 
 Not suggested for the young or the week at heart. 
 One of the pleasant traditions of the old army.
