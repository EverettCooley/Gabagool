 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 oz. Grand Mariner 
 1 1/2 oz.  Galliano 
 Splash of  cream 
 Splash of  orange juice 
 Dash of  Grenadine 
 Directions : 
 Shake over  ice . Strain into a chilled flute, or over fresh  ice  in a highball glass.
