Description : 
 Makes 2 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 2½ cups peeled, ripe  mango  chunks 
 ½ cup vanilla  yogurt 
 1 cup  skim milk 
 ½  banana , peeled 
 2 tablespoons fresh  lime  or  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Freeze  mango  chunks 1 - 1½ hours, or until firm. 
 Just before serving, ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth. 
 Pour into chilled glasses and serve immediately. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 260 calories | 2 grams fat
