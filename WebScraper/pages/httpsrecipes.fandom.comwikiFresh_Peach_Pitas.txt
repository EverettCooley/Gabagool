Ingredients : 
 1 large or 2 small thinly sliced fresh  peaches 
 1 uncut pita bread pieces 
 1 cup low fat or nonfat  cottage cheese 
 1/4 cup chopped fresh  mint  leaves 
 12 thin slices of  cucumber  or red  bell pepper 
 Directions : 
 Warm pita bread and cut them in half. 
 Fill each with  cottage cheese ,  mint ,  peaches  and  cucumber  slices. 
 Makes 2 servings.
