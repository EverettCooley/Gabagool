Ingredients : 
 2 cups  corn flour 
 ½ cup white grated  cheese 
 1 tablespoon  oil 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Mix together all ingredients and form into medium sized rounds ½ inch thick. 
 Fry in  oil  on both sides until slightly brown.
