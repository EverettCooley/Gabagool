 About bamboo salt : 
Bamboo salt is made by roasting  sea salt  in bamboo cylinders plugged with the yellow mud.  The salt absorbs minerals from the bamboo and mud, which in turn leach the salt of impurities. Look for plastic bags of it in Korean markets. If you will not find bamboo salt, then the sea salt is a suitable substitute.       Bamboo salt
