Description : 
 Purchased from the Colvin Estate in Little Rock, Arkansas in 1989. Notation on card indicates this is a weekly meal in the Colvin House. Dated 1964.
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 3 pounds  beef  stew  meat 
 ¼ cup  water 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 2 cans diced  tomatoes  with green chilies 
 3 cloves minced  garlic 
 1 cup chopped  white onion 
 3 tablespoons  flour 
 ½ teaspoon  cumin 
 ½ teaspoon  oregano 
 1 teaspoon  chili powder 
 1 diced  bell pepper 
 Directions : 
 Place stew meat,  water ,  salt  and  pepper  in crackpot. 
 Turn heat to high and let simmer for 1½ hours. 
 Drain juice from  tomatoes  into measuring cup. 
 Add  tomatoes ,  garlic  and  onions  to crackpot. 
 Stir and let simmer on high for 30 minutes. 
 Add  cumin ,  oregano , and  chili powder . 
 To crock-pot and stir. 
 Blend juice and enough  water  to equal 1½ cups liquid and  flour  stir into meat and veggie mixture. 
 Let cook on low for 3-4 hours until sauce is nice and thick (if you like runnier gravy three hours is good).
