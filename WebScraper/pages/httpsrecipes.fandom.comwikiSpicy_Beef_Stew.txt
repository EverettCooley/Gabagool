       Vietnamese Spicy Beef Stew     
 Ingredients : 
 3 tbsp  oil 
 4  star anise 
 2 medium  onions 
 1 inch  cinnamon  stick 
 10  green onions 
 ½ tsp whole  peppercorns 
 1 stalk  lemon grass 
 sugar 
 2 lbs stewing  beef 
 5 cups  water 
 ⅓ cup  yellow bean sauce 
 1 tsp  chili powder 
 Directions : 
 Make a mixture with all the ingredients and boil them in the  oil .
