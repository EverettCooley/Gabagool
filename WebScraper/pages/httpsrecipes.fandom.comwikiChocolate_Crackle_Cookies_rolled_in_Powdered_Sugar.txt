chocolate  Crackle Cookies
 
 3 1/2 oz  semisweet chocolate  Baking Bar, Broken Up 
 1 Stick  unsalted butter , Cut in Small Pieces 
 1 Cup Packed  light brown sugar 
 1 Extra Large  egg , Lightly Beaten 
 1 Tsp  vanilla 
 1 1/3 Cups Self Rising  flour 
 1/2 Tsp  baking soda 
 2 Tbs Confectioners  sugar 
 
Heat Oven to 400 Degrees. Microwave  chocolate ,  butter  And  sugar  in Bowl, Until Melted And Smooth. Stir in  egg  And  vanilla , Then  flour  And  baking soda  Until Blended. Cover And Refrigerate 20 minutes. or Until Firm. Put Confectioners  sugar  in a Shallow Dish. Roll Dough Into 24  walnut  Size Balls, Then Roll in Confectioners  sugar  to Coat. Place on Ungreased Baking Sheets. Bake 10 minutes. or Until Just Set. Cool on Baking Sheet.
 
This Recipe Makes 24  chocolate  Crackle Cookies.*
