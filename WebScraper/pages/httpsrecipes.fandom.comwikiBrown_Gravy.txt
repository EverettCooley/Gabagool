Ingredients : 
 2 1/2 tbsp.  margarine  or  butter 
 1/4 cup  all-purpose flour 
 1 1/2 cups  vegetable broth 
 2 tbsp.  soy sauce 
 1/4 tsp. freshly ground  black pepper 
 1/4 cup chopped fresh  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Melt butter in small saucepan over medium-low heat. 
 Add flour and whisk together about 30 seconds; then add broth slowly while whisking. 
 Cook over medium heat for 2 minutes. Add soy sauce and pepper. 
 Cook until gravy achieves desired thickness. 
 Pour into a gravy boat or bowl and stir in parsley. Serve warm.
