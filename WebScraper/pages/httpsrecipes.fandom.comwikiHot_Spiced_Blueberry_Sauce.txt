Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the McLean Estate in Irving, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  blueberries 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 ½ teaspoon  cinnamon 
 ¼ teaspoon  nutmeg 
 Directions : 
 Combine blueberries, sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg and bring to boiling point. 
 Boil 5 minutes stirring occasionally.
