Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Howard Estate in Mesquite, Texas in 1983.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4 ounces  gin 
 4 ounces  vodka 
 4 ounces  triple sec 
 4 ounces  white rum 
 4 ounces  tequila 
 4 ounces  sweet and sour mix 
 8 ounces  orange juice 
 lemon  wedges 
 Directions : 
 In blender combine gin, vodka, triple sec, rum, tequila, mix and orange juice. 
 Blend until well combined then pour into 4 highball glasses filled with ice. 
 Garnish with a lemon wedge.
