       Cheese     
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/4	cup	whipped  cream cheese 
 1/4	cup	 Peanut butter 
 2	tbl	 carrot , Shredded 
 Directions : 
 Blend the  cream cheese  and  peanut butter  together and mix well. 
 Add the shredded  carrot , mixing well. 
 Serve on toasted  raisin bread  or toasted  bagels .
