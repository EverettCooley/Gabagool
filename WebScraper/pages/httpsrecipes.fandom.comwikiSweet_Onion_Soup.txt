Description 
 Sweet Onion Soup  from the  Public Health Cookbook  -- original source of recipe, taken from government resource in the public domain
 
 Cook Time : 50 minutes 
 Serves : 8 
 Ingredients 
 low fat  cooking spray 
 1½ pounds  sweet onion  such as  walla walla  or  Vidalia , thinly sliced 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1 teaspoon  sugar 
 ⅓ cup  all-purpose flour 
 5 cups low-sodium  vegetable broth 
 1 cup dry  white wine 
 1½ teaspoons dried  sage 
 1  bay leaf 
 Directions 
 Spray a large saucepan with low fat cooking spray and heat over medium heat. 
 Add onions and garlic and cook, covered, for about 10 minutes. 
 Reduce heat to medium-low then stir in sugar. 
 Cook for about 15 minutes or until onions start to caramelize. 
 Stir in the flour and continue to cook for 2 minutes. 
 Add the broth, wine, sage and bay leaf. 
 Heat to boiling then reduce heat to medium-low and simmer, covered, for 30 minutes. 
 Notes 
 Top with 1 tablespoon of sour cream and a sprinkling of chives if desired.
