Description : 
 Fried green plantain
 
 6 – 8 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 3 green  plantains 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 oil 
 Directions : 
 Peel  plantains  and cut into ½ inch slices. 
 Fry in hot  oil  until golden. 
 Place on cutting board and smash the slices with a glass bottle or rolling pin. 
 Sprinkle with  salt  and return to pan to fry again. 
 Fry until crispy. 
 Drain on paper towels and serve immediately.  Ketchup  is optional!
