Description : 
 Also called Fank.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups of  flour 
 1½ cups  milk 
 2 pkg  yeast 
 4 heaping tbsp  sugar 
 a dash of  salt 
 4 tbsp  butter 
 8  egg yolks 
 Directions : 
 Boil  milk , add  butter  and  sugar , and then cool. 
 Add  yeast  and  milk  and beat  egg  yolks in well with beater. 
 Add this mixture to  flour . 
 Let rise for 2 hours. 
 Roll out lightly on floured board to ⅜ inch thickness, and cut out round circles. 
 Lighly cover with cloth and let rise again about ½ hour. 
 Fry in hot Crisco  oil  ( vegetable oil ) or  lard . 
 Bismarcks will turn nice brown on both sides after being flipped over. 
 Bismarcks should drain for a few seconds on paper toweling. 
 If you want jelly Bismarcks, add jelly after they are fried. 
 Use a pastry bag to add jelly. 
 You can also sprinkle  powdered sugar  on top of Bismarcks.
