 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 1 cup chopped  celery 
 1/4 cup chopped pimientos 
 1 tablespoon grated  Onion 
 3 hard-cooked  eggs , chopped 
 1/4 cup chopped nut meats 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 1/4 cup crumbled bleu  Cheese 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 1/2 teaspoon seasoned  salt  (optional) 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  celery , pimientos,  Onion ,  eggs ,  nuts ,  salt  and  pepper ; toss lightly. Blend  sour cream  with  Cheese , juice and seasoned  salt . Fold into  rice  mixture. Turn into mold and chill thoroughly. Unmold before serving.
