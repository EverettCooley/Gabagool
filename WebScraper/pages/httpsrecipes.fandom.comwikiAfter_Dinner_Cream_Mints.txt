After Dinner Cream  mints 
 
 1 (8 Oz.) Pkg.  cream cheese 
 vanilla ,  mint  or  butter  Flavoring to Taste
 food coloring 
 
 2 Lbs.  powdered sugar 
 granulated sugar 
 Soften  Cheese , Mix With Flavoring And Enough Coloring to Tint Desired Shade. Add  powdered sugar , Gradually, Mixing Well. Roll Into 1/2 to 1 Inch Balls; Roll in  granulated sugar . Press Into Mold or Press Flat With Thumb.  mints  May be Frozen And Used as Needed. Place in Container With Waxed Paper Between Layers; Cover Tightly. Makes 200  mints .
