Ingredients : 
 4 tbsp extra virgin  olive oil 
 1 tbsp vegetable spread  or   butter 
 1 tbsp  garlic  (minced) 
 1¼ tsp  salt 
 1 tsp fresh ground  pepper 
 1¼ tsp  paprika 
 ½ cup chopped  green onion 
 ½ cup chopped  cilantro 
 1 lb medium  shrimp  (peeled) 
 1 cup  white short grain rice 
 2 cups  water 
 Directions : 
 In a 2-qt saucepan, bring 2 cups of  water  and ¼ tsp  salt  to a boil. 
 Stir in 1 cup of  rice . 
 Cover. 
 Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes or until all the waster is absorbed. 
 Pour contents into serving dish. 
 In a bowl combine  olive oil , vegetable spread,  garlic ,  salt ,  pepper ,  paprika . 
 Set stove at low heat place mixture in a 10" pan and heat till warm. 
 Add shrimps and cook for 4-5 minutes. 
 Add  green onion , and  cilantro ; mix and cover for 3 minutes. 
 Serve on top of  rice .
