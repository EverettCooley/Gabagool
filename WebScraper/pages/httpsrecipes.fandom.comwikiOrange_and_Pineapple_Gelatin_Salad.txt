Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Tassel Estate in Wylie, Texas in 1986. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 7 ounces lemon lime  soda 
 ½ pound miniature  marshmallows 
 3 ounce package  orange gelatin 
 6 ounces softened  cream cheese 
 20 ounce can crushed  pineapple  undrained 
 ¾ cup chopped  walnuts 
 1 cup  heavy whipping cream  whipped 
 ⅔ cup  salad dressing 
 Directions : 
 Combine soda and marshmallows in sauce pan. 
 Cook over medium heat until marshmallows are melted. 
 Add gelatin and stir until dissolved then add hot mixture to cheese and beat until smooth. 
 Chill for 30 minutes then fold in whipped cream and salad dressing. 
 Blend in pineapple and nuts then pour into lightly oiled mold then chill until well set.
