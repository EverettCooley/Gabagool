 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked mashed  sweet potato 
 3 tablespoons  butter 
 1 cup  milk 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  black pepper 
 2  eggs  (separate  yolk , and whites) 
 marshmallows  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Add seasonings,  butter , and  milk , to well mashed  sweet potatoes . 
 When thoroughly blended stir in beaten  egg   yolks , then add beaten  egg  whites. 
 Pour into a dish and bake in an oven until firm, about 300-350 °F, 30 minutes. 
 When it is almost cooked, add marshmallows on the top.
