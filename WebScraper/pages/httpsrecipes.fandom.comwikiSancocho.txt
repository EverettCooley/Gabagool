Ingredients : 
 1 pound of  meat 
 2  ribs 
 1  chicken 
 2  onions 
 1  pimento 
 3  potatoes 
 1  yuca 
 2  plantains 
 3 ears of  corns 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Put three litres of  water  in a pan and place it on fire. 
 When starting to boil, put a  chicken  cut into pieces 
 1 pound of meat (with bone) and 2 ribs. 
 Add  onions  (in pieces),  pepper  ( pimento ),  celery  and  leek . 
 Let boil for 1 hour at slow fire. 
 Add 3 big  potatoes  (peeled),  yuca ,  plantains  cut in pieces,  corn  in pieces,  salt  and  pepper  to taste.
