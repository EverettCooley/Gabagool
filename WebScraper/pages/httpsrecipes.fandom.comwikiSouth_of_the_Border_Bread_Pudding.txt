Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  orange juice 
 ¾ cup packed  brown sugar 
 ½ tsp  cinnamon 
 ¾ cup  raisins 
 5 cups lightly packed 1-inch cubes  French bread  (about 6 oz) 
 6 oz Monterey  jack cheese , shredded (about 2½ cups) 
 ½ cup toasted  almonds 
 lightly sweetened  whipped cream ,  light cream , or  ice cream 
 Directions : 
 In a small saucepan, combine the  orange juice ,  sugar ,  cinnamon , and raisins. 
 Bring to a boil, stirring. 
 Pour over the  bread  and toss to mix. 
 Add 2 cups of the  cheese  and the  almonds , and toss. 
 Turn into a buttered 1½ quart baking dish, and sprinkle the remaining  cheese  on top. 
 Bake in a preheated 375°F oven 15-20 minutes, until the pudding is heated through and the  cheese  is melted. 
 Serve warm, topped with cream or  ice cream , if desired.
