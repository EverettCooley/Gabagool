Apple  Crisp Pie
 
 1 1/2 Cups Cold Whole  milk 
 1 Box  vanilla  Instant Pudding And Pie Filling Mix (3.4 Oz) 
 1 1/2 T. Ground  cinnamon 
 1 Premade Graham Cracker Piecrust (keebler, Etc.) 
 1 Container(21 Oz) Sliced  Apple  Pie Filling or Topping (comstock More Fruit) 
 2 Crunchy Granola Bars 1.5 Oz. Each, Such as Nature Valley 
 
Combine Mil, Pudding Mix And 1 T. of The  cinnamon  in Large Bowl. Beat For 2 Minutes or Until Well Blended And Beginning to Thicken. Pour Into Graham Cracker Piecrust. Refrigerate For 10 Minutes. Stir  Apple  Pie Filling With Remaining 1/2 T. of The  cinnamon  in Medium Bowl to Blend; Spread Over Pudding. Cover Pie; Refrigerate Until Ready to Serve. Enclose Granola Bars in Resealable Plastic Bag. Using a Rolling Pin, Crush Bars Into Coarse Crumbs. Sprinkle Pie With Granola Crumbs And Serve. Serves 6
