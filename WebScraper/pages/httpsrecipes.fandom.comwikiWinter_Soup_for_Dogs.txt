 Ingredients : 
 ½ kilo  chicken   gizzards , hearts, or  beef heart  chopped 
 1 cup  whole grain  hulled or  barley 
 any vegetables available such as:  carrots ,  garlic , baby  cabbage ,  cabbage  etc 
 ½ teaspoon  vegetable broth  powder 
 ½ liter  water 
 kibble 
 Directions : 
 Put the meat, vegetables,  barley , and broth powder into a large pot. 
 Add in the  water  and bring it to a boil. 
 Reduce the heat once it has started boiling and simmer covered during 30 minutes. 
 Serve over kibble.
