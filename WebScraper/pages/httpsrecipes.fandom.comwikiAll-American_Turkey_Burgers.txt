Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Yield: 3 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  ground turkey 
 Â½ medium  onion , finely chopped or minced, red, white, or yellow only 
 1 stalk  celery , finely chopped 
 1 teaspoon dried  thyme 
 1 tablespoon  barbecue sauce , homemade or purchased 
 1 teaspoon  hot sauce 
 2 teaspoons  Worcestershire sauce 
 1 teaspoon salt 
 1 teaspoon pepper, black only 
 Directions : 
 Preheat an electric tabletop grill. 
 Simply combine all ingredients and form the mixture into three burgers. 
 Toss them onto the grill and cook for five minutes or so, and serve. 
 Notes : 
 The carb count will vary a bit depending on what barbecue sauce you use, but should be around 4 grams of carbs and 1 gram of fiber, for a total of 3 grams of usable carbs and 27 grams of protein.
