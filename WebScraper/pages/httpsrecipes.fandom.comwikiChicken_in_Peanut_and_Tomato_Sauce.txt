Ingredients : 
 peanut oil 
 1  chicken , cut into bite-sized pieces 
 2  onions , cut up 
 2 or 3 cloves of  garlic , minced 
 1 cup  tomato paste 
 1 cup  peanut butter 
 1 cup  water 
 red pepper ,  black pepper ,  salt 
 Directions : 
 Heat a couple spoonfuls of  oil  in a deep pot. 
 Add the  chicken  and fry it on both sides until it is browned. 
 Remove the  chicken  and set aside. 
 Fry the  onions  and  garlic  in the same pot. 
 Stir in  tomato  paste. 
 Reduce heat and simmer for a few minutes. 
 Return  chicken  to pot. 
 Stir in  peanut   butter  and add enough  water  to make the mixture of  tomato paste  and  peanut butter  into a smooth sauce. 
 Add red pepper,  black pepper  and salt to taste. 
 Serve with  fufu  or  Baton de Manioc .
