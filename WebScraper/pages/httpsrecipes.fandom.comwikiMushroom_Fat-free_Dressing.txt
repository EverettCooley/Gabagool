Description : 
 Source:  Eating Smart  by Jeanne Jones
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lbs fresh  mushrooms 
 ¾ cup fat-free  dressing 
 ½ cup  red wine 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp freshly ground  black pepper 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 2  garlic  cloves 
 2 tsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 1 tbsp  Dijon mustard 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Combine  mushrooms  and dressing in a skillet and cook until  mushrooms  are just tender, about 5 minutes. 
 Dressing combine  vinegar  and  salt ; stir until  salt  is completely dissolved. 
 Add  pepper ,  sugar ,  garlic , Worcestershire, Dijon and  lemon juice ; mix well. 
 Add  water  and mix well again. 
 Refrigerate to store.
