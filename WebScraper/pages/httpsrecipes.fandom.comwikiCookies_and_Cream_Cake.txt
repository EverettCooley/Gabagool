Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Yield: 6 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pk cake mix, white 
 10 cookies, whole 
 1¼ cups  water 
 ¾ cup  shortening 
 ⅓ cup  oil 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 3  egg whites 
 1 cup  Oreos , crushed 
 Frosting : 
 3 cups  sugar (powdered) 
 2  egg whites 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 350°F. 
 Grease and flour 2 round cake pans. 
 In large bowl, combine all cake ingredients except cookies. 
 Mix at low speed until moistened. 
 Beat 2 minutes at high speed. 
 Stir in crushed cookies. 
 Bake at 350°F for 25 – 35 minutes or until it tests done. 
 Cool layers. 
 Frosting : 
 In small bowl combine ½ cup of the  powdered sugar ,  shortening ,  vanilla  and  egg whites . Blend well. 
 Beat in remaining  sugar  until frosting is smooth. 
 Fill and frost cake. 
 Arrange whole cookies on end and on top of frosted cake. 
 Enjoy.
