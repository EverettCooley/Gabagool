 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (12 ounce) bag frozen  raspberries  (do not thaw) 
 1 cup drained  soft tofu 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 3 tablespoons  granulated sugar , divided 
 Directions : 
 In food processor combine  raspberries  straight from the freezer,  tofu ,  vanilla extract , and 1 tablespoon of the  Sugar . Process until pureed and well blended, scraping down the sides if needed. Taste and add additional  Sugar  if needed.
 Serve immediately in stemmed glasses or dessert dishes.
