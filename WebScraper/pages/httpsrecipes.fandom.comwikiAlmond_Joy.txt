Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ ounce  Frangelico 
 ½ ounce  Malibu rum 
 ¼ ounce  crème de cacao 
 splash  cream 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients over ice in shaker, shake, pour in shot glass.
