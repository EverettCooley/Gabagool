Description : 
 This is a really easy sauce that can be used as a dip for grilled meat,or veges and sometime people use it as a dressing for a green salad..or you can use it anyway you like.. I hope you like it...
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 tablespoons  tahini 
 1 teaspoon  vinegar 
 3 teaspoons  lemon juice 
 buttermilk 
 salt 
 pepper 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients together and when it comes to the  buttermilk  just poor out of the carton and whisk it up until it becomes smooth but make sure its not thick it should be a little runny but coats your spoon.
