 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (8 	ounce) package  cream cheese 
 1 (16 	ounce) jar of your favorite salsa (Do not use fresh salsa, it needs to be the cooked kind in a jar.) 
 Directions : 
 Put  cream cheese  in a low sided bowl. Pour salsa on top of  Cheese . Allow to come to room temperature. 
 Serve with tortilla chips.
