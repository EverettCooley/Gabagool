Description : 
 In Romanian: crema de castane
 
 Ingredients : 
 10 oz / 300 g  butter 
 10 oz / 300 g  confectioner's sugar 
 1 lb / 500 g  chestnuts 
 1 tablespoon  rum 
 Directions : 
 Cream the  butter  with the  sugar  and  rum . 
 Make an X into the  chestnut  peel, and set to boil. 
 Start with cold  water . 
 Let boil for just a few minutes. 
 Then peel the  chestnuts , grind and then sieve them. 
 Put the  chestnut  puree in a bowl and gradually add the  butter  mixture. 
 Mix well.
