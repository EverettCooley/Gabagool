 Description : 
 Sweet Maragwe
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 Lb dry  red beans  or  kidney beans , soaked in  water  overnight 
 1-2 cups of  coconut milk 
 2-4 tbsp  Sugar  to taste 
 Â¼ tsp ground  cardamom  (or a small piece of stick  cinnamon ) 
 1 tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Rinse and drain soaked beans. Place beans in a large cooking pot and add fresh  water  to cover. Bring to a fast boil and cook for ten minutes. Reduce heat and simmer until beans are nearly tender. Add all remaining ingredients. Cover and cook until beans are tender, stirring occasionally. Savor after lunch or dinner.
