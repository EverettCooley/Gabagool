Ingredients : 
 1 cup  strawberries  cut in quarters 
 ½ cup  confectioners' sugar 
 1 cup  heavy cream 
 3 – 4 tbsp  port  or  madeira  wine 
 Directions : 
 Sprinkle  sugar  over  strawberries  cut in quarters in a bowl. 
 Beat:  cream  with  madeira  wine and ¼ cup sugar until stiff. 
 Fold  whipped cream  into  strawberries . 
 Pile high in footed sherbet glasses. 
 Garnish each with one whole  strawberry .
