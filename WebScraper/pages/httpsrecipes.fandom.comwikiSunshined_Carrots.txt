Makes 4 servings
 
 
    2 cups water                                                    
   1 16-ounce bag baby carrots                                     
   2 tablespoons orange juice                                      
   1/4 cup orange marmalade                                        
   1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg                                      
 
 Pour water into pressure cooker. Set carrots atop rack or trivet in
cooker. Lock lid and bring to full pressure. Lower heat and maintain
pressure. Cook for 2 minutes. Quick-release pressure. 
 In a microwave-safe bowl, combine juice and marmalade. Stir in
nutmeg. Microwave on medium until hot, 1-3 minutes. Transfer carrots
to a serving bowl. Drizzle orange sauce over carrots and toss to coat.
