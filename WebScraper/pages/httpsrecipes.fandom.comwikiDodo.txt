 Description : 
 Fried Plantains. Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 ripe  plantains 
 vegetable oil  for frying 
 salt  to taste (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in pan. Peel  plantains  and slice crosswise at an angle. 
 If desired, sprinkle with  salt  to taste. 
 Fry  plantains , working in batches and turning as needed, until golden brown. 
 Drain on paper towels. 
 Serve hot or warm.
