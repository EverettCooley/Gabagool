Description : 
 Bite into this mixture with  Onion ,  garlic , and a threesome of hot sauces - and it’ll bite you back!
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 ripe  California avocados 
 ½ cup  tofu 
 ½ small  onion , minced 
 1  garlic  cloves, minced 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 tsp  worcestershire sauce 
 5 drops  hot pepper sauce 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp hot  salsa 
 Directions : 
 Whirl  tofu  in blender until smooth. 
 Mash avocados. 
 Add  tofu  and other ingredients to mashed avocados. 
 Mix well. 
 Press a piece of plastic wrap on the surface of the dip to prevent darkening. 
 Chill and serve.
