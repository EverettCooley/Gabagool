Ingredients : 
 3Â lbs 	Ground  Beef 
 8 	oz bag 	Sea Shell Noodles 
 1 	pkg 	pepperoni 
 1 	lg 	 bell pepper 
 1 	lg 	 Onion 
 1 	sm can 	 Mushrooms  (Pieces & Stems) 
 6 	cups 	Shredded  Cheddar   Cheese 
 6 	cups 	Shredded Mozzerella  Cheese 
 Directions : 
 Boil noodles and set aside. 
 In small bowl cut up the  bell pepper ,  Onion ,  Mushrooms , and one side of the pepperoni, mix in skillet and add ground  Beef . 
 Brown ground  Beef  with the above ingredients and drain. 
 Drain Noodles and get a large baking dish. 
 Put a layer of noodles, add a layer of meat mixture, and add a layer of  Cheddar  and mozzerella cheeses. 
 Continue layering until all noodles, meat, and chesse is gone. 
 Top with remaining pepperoni. 
 Put in a 400 degree oven for about 20 minutes, or until  Cheese  is melted and golden brown.
