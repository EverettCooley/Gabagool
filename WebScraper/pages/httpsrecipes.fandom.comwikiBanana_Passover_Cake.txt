Ingredients : 
 3  eggs  separated 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 cup  bananas , mashed 
 ¾ cup cake meal 
 ¼ cup  potato flour 
 ½ cup  nuts , chopped fine 
 Directions : 
 Beat  egg  whites until stiff. 
 Mix  sugar  and yolks until thick and creamy. 
 Add other ingredients.
