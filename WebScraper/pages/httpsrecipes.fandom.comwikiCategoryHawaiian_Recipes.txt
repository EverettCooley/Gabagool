The Hawaiian cooking is famous due to the unique flavors its recipes exhibit and the expertise of the chefs in cooking the food items. The Hawaiian recipes make use of vegetables, rice, and white and read meat and also include seafoods. The food makes use of the local spices and herbs cultivated in the region and spices brought in by different traditions.
