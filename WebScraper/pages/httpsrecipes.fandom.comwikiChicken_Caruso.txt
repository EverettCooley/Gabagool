Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound boned and skinned  chicken  breast strips 
 ½ teaspoon  garlic salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 2 cups sliced  celery 
 1 x 15½-ounce jar  spaghetti sauce  (2 cups) 
 1 teaspoon  Italian seasoning 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 Grated  Parmesan cheese  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Season  Chicken  with  garlic salt  and  pepper . 
 Cook in  butter  in large skillet about 2 minutes. 
 Add  celery ; continue cooking until  celery  is tender crisp. 
 Stir in  spaghetti sauce  and  Italian seasoning ; cover and simmer 10 minutes. 
 Serve over hot  rice . 
 Sprinkle with  cheese , if desired.
