 Ingredients : 
 1 large ripe  avocado , peeled,pitted,and chopped 
 2 teaspoons fresh  lime juice 
 2Â lbs large  Shrimp , peeled and deveined 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1/2 cup fresh  lime juice 
 1/4 cup  honey 
 1/2 teaspoon ground  cumin 
 1/2 cup chopped fresh  cilantro 
 2 cans  black beans , rinsed and drained 
 1 medium  red onion , chopped 
 1 can Mexican-style  corn , drained 
 1 cup  refried beans 
 8 12-inch  flour  tortillas 
 Directions : 
 1. Toss  avocado  with 2 tablespoons fresh  lime juice .
2. Heat small amount of  oil  in a large skillet, add  Shrimp  and  garlic , and saute until  Shrimp  tuen pink.
3. Let cool.
4. Combine the 1/2 cup fresh  lime juice ,  honey ,  cumin , and  cilantro .
5. Add  black beans ,  Onion ,  corn  and  Shrimp  to the dressing and toss.
6. Spread  refried beans  on bottom 3/4 of tortilla.
7. Top with  Shrimp  and vegetable mixture.
8. Roll up from bottom, like a jelly roll.
9. Slice in half and serve!
