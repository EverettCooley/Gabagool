Ingredients : 
 2 c. cold  skim milk 
 1 (1.6 oz.) pkg. sugar free instant vanilla pudding 
 1 (8 oz.) carton plain lowfat  yogurt 
 1 c. crushed ice 
 1  Banana 
 1  Peach 
 1 cup  Strawberries 
 Pulp of 1/2  orange 
 Directions : 
 Blend all ingredients well. 
 Notes : 
 Makes 1 quart. 
 Per 1/2 cup serving: calcium 139 mg. calories 99; protein 4 gm. cholesterol 1.5 mg. fat 0.3 gm.
