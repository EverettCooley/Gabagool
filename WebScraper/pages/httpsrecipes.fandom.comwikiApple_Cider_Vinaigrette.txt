Apple Cider Vinaigrette
 1 1/2 cups vegetable oil
2/3 cup cider vinegar
2/3 cup firmly packed light brown sugar
2 t. celery salt
1 1/2 t. dry mustard
3 T. coarsely chopped onion
 Process all ingredients in a blender or food processor until smooth. Package vinaigrette in an airtight container and chill. Makes about 2 cups vinaigrette.
Directions for gift card, if giving as a gift: Store in refrigerator up to 1 week. Stir dressing well before drizzling desired amount over fresh fruit or spinach salad.
