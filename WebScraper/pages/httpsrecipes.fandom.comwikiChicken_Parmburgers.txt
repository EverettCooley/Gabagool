Ingredients : 
 1 pound ground chicken or turkey 
 1/3 cup Parmesan cheese, grated 
 1/3 cup seasoned dried bread crumbs 
 1 teaspoon garlic, minced 
 pizza sauce 
 mozzarella cheese, shredded 
 Directions : 
 Mix ground chicken or turkey, Parmesan cheese, bread crumbs and minced garlic 
 Shape into 4 pattties. 
 Cook in a little oil in a skillet, turning until cooked through. 
 Top with pizza sauce and shredded mozzarella cheese. 
 Cover, cook 1 min. to melt cheese. 
 Serve on toasted buns or English muffins. 
 Makes 4 servings
