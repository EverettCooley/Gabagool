Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Fuqua Estate in Richardson, Texas in 1994.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ ounce  gin 
 1 ounce  orange juice 
 1 ounce  lemon juice 
 ½ teaspoon  grenadine 
 Directions : 
 In a shaker half filled with ice cubes combine all ingredients. 
 Shake well then strain into a cocktail glass.
