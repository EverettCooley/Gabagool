Description : 
 Supposed to be 6 servings at 53  cal, 1g fat a serving.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cup chopped fresh  basil  leaves 
 1 cup chopped  flat leaf parsley 
 ¼ cup toasted  breadcrumbs 
 2 cloves minced  garlic 
 3 tbsp  light miso paste 
 ¼ - ⅓ cup  water 
 2 tbsp grated  Parmesan  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 In food processor, combine everything except  water . 
 Pulse til finely minced. 
 With machine running, slowly add  water  until it reaches desired consistency. 
 Serve with  pasta  or whatever your heart desires!.
