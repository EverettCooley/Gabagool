 Description : 
 Purchased from the Early Estate in Corpus Christi, Texas in 1992. Dated 1978.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups applejacks cereal 
 2 cups  pecan  halves 
 1 cup whole  blanched almonds 
 1-cup  chow mein noodles 
 1/2-cup  Sugar 
 1-tablespoon ground  cinnamon 
 1/4-teaspoon  salt 
 1  egg  white 
 
 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl combine the cereal,  nuts  and  chow mein noodles . 
 In a small bowl combine the  Sugar ,  cinnamon  and  salt . 
 Stir in  egg  white. 
 Pour over the cereal mixture and toss to coat. 
 Transfer to a greased baking pan. 
 Bake uncovered at 300 for 45 minutes, stirring every 15 minutes.
