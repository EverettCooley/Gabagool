Description : 
 Serve with any  beef  or  veal  dish. Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅓ cup sliced  almonds 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 3 cups cooked  rice  (cooked in  beef broth ) 
 3 tablespoons chopped fresh  parsley 
 3 tablespoons snipped  chives 
 ¼ cup dry  white wine 
 Directions : 
 Sauté  almonds  in  butter  in large skillet over medium heat until golden brown. 
 Add  rice ,  parsley ,  chives  and wine. 
 Heat thoroughly, tossing lightly.
