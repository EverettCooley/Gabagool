Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x 8-ounce package frozen cooked  shrimp , thawed 
 1 x 10¾-ounce can condensed  cream of mushroom  soup 
 2 tablespoons dry  sherry 
 2 cups coarsely chopped cooked  chicken 
 ½ teaspoon  onion salt 
 ½ teaspoon  celery salt 
 ¼ cup diced  pimentos 
 1 cup drained, cooked  green peas 
 ½ cup  sour cream 
 3 cups  yellow rice 
 Directions : 
 Drain  Shrimp  on paper towels to remove excess  water . Set aside. 
 Combine soup and  sherry . 
 Stir in  chicken ,  shrimp ,  onion salt ,  celery salt , pimientos and  peas . 
 Heat thoroughly. Fold in  sour cream . 
 Serve over fluffy yellow  rice .
