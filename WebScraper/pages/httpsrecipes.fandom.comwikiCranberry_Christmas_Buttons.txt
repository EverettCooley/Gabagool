Description : 
 These cookies are very festive and have a great  holiday  flavor. Purchase  cranberries  the day after Thanksgiving when they go on sale, and these cookies will be an inexpensive holiday favorite.
 
 Ingredients : 
 Dough : 
 ⅔ cup  margarine 
 2½ cups  flour 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 1  egg 
 1 tbsp  milk 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 Topping : 
 1 cup  cranberries 
 ¼ cup  orange marmalade 
 1 tbsp  honey 
 Glaze : 
 1 cup  powdered sugar 
 ¼ tsp  vanilla 
 1 tbsp  milk 
 Directions : 
 Dough : 
 In mixing bowl combine ingredients. 
 Stir until batter is smooth. 
 Chill for 3 hours. 
 Topping : 
 In saucepan, boil  cranberries ,  marmalade , and honey. 
 Once  cranberries  pop, continue to let the mixture simmer for 10 minutes. 
 Set aside. 
 Glaze : 
 Combine  powdered sugar ,  vanilla  and  milk  in mixing bowl. 
 Set aside at room temperature. 
 Preparation : 
 Remove dough from refrigerator. 
 Divide the dough into thirds and roll out each third on lightly floured surface. 
 Taking a small round cookie cutter (approx. 1½ inches wide) cut out dough. 
 Place on cookie sheet. 
 Top each  button  with 1 tsp  cranberry  filling. 
 Cook in 350°F oven for 10 minutes, or until done. 
 Set cookies aside to cool. 
 Drizzle with  powdered sugar  glaze.
