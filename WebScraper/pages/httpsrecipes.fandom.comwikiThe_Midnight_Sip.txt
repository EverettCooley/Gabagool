 Cook Time: 5 min. 
 Ready In: 5 min. 
 Serves 1 
 
 Description : 
 This warm milk flavored with honey, vanilla, and cinnamon will put you right to bed.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  milk 
 1 teaspoon  honey 
 2 drops  vanilla extract 
 1 pinch ground  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Pour milk into a microwave safe mug and place into microwave. Cook on High until the milk is very hot and begins to foam, about 3 minutes. Stir in honey and vanilla, then sprinkle with cinnamon before serving.

 Amount Per Serving Calories: 152 | Total Fat: 4.8g | Cholesterol: 20mg
