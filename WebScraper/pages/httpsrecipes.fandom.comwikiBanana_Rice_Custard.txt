 Description : 
 Makes 8 servings. 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 medium  bananas , fully ripened 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 3  eggs 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 3/4 cup  Sugar 
 1 1/2 teaspoons grated  lemon peel 
 2 cups  milk 
 nutmeg , sliced  bananas  and  whipped cream  for garnish (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Mash  bananas  with fork; stir in  lemon juice . 
 Blend with  rice  in 2-quart baking dish. 
 Beat  eggs ; add  salt ,  Sugar ,  lemon peel  and  milk ; stir into banana- rice  mixture. 
 Sprinkle with  nutmeg . 
 Place baking dish in larger pan; half-filled with hot  water . 
 Bake in 325 degree oven for 1 hour. 
 Chill thoroughly, at least four hours before serving. 
 Serve with  whipped cream  and sliced  bananas  for garnish, if desired.
