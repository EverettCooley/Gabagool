A nice way to start off your Holiday parties! 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 (8 ounces) cans refrigerated crescent dinner rolls 
 ¾ cup shredded  mozzarella  cheese 
 ¾ cup grated  romano cheese 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 1 clove crushed  garlic , chopped finely 
 1 (14 ounces) can  artichoke hearts , drained and chopped 
 1 (4 ounces) can  green chilies 
 1 teaspoon  Italian seasoning 
 Garnish : 
 grated  Parmesan cheese 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients except crescents. 
 Unroll the triangles and cut in half so you have smaller triangles. 
 You`ll be cutting from the wide side through the narrow point. 
 Place 1 ounce (2 tablespoons) of filling into each crescent. 
 Roll wide side up to thin side. 
 Spinkle tops with  Parmesan grated cheese . 
 Bake at 375°F for 20 minutes.
