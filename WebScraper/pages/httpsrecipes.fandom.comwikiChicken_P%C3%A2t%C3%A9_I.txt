Description : 
 Yield: 1¾ cups 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups  chicken breast , cooked, minced 
 8 oz  Neufchatel  cheese, softened 
 3 tbsp chopped  onion 
 2 tbsp dry  sherry 
 2 tbsp  mayonnaise  (diet) 
 2 tsp  lemon juice 
 ¼ tsp  hot sauce 
 ⅛ tsp ground  nutmeg 
 1 dash  paprika 
 Directions : 
 Combine  chicken  (which has been finely chopped),  Neufchatel ,  onion ,  sherry ,  mayonnaise ,  lemon juice , hot sauce, and  nutmeg  in container of electric blender; process until smooth. 
 Transfer mixture to a 2 cup mold coated with  cooking spray  (PAM). 
 Cover, and chill overnight. 
 Unmold onto a serving plate. 
 Sprinkle with  paprika . 
 Garnish with  parsley  sprigs, if desired. 
 Serve with Melba toast rounds or unsalted crackers. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving (1 tbsp): 
 
 28 calories | 2.8 g fat | .3 g carbohydrates | 0 g fiber | 10 g cholesterol | 30 mg sodium | 27 mg potassium
