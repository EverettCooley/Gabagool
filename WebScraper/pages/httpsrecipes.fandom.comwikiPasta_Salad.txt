Ingredients : 
 500 g  pasta spirals 
 2 tablespoons  olive oil 
 200 g baby  mushrooms , sliced 
 3 sticks  celery , sliced 
 1 large  red capsicum  ( bell pepper ) 
 1 large  green capsicum  ( bell pepper ) 
 1 can  corn  kernels, drained 
 2 tablespoons fresh  parsley , chopped 
 1 bottle (250ml) Caesar  salad dressing 
 Directions : 
 Add the  pasta  to a saucepan of boiling  water  and boil, uncovered, until the  pasta  is just tender. 
 Drain off the hot  water  and rinse under cold  water . 
 Allow to drain again until cool. 
 Add the rest of the ingredients and mix well but gently.
