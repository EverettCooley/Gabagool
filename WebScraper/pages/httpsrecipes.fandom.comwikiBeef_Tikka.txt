 Description : 
 A monumental high-quality combination of beef and real fruit is made with spices and yogurt, enriched with herbs, and grilled to red-hot perfection, and is a skewer-based dish eaten with rice.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ kilo cubes of  beef 
 2 tbsp.  unripe papaya , ground 
 ½ tsp.  ginger  paste 
 ½ tsp.  garlic  paste 
 salt , according to taste 
 2 tbsp.  yogurt 
 1 tsp.  chili powder   or  (1 tbsp. chopped  green chilies ) 
 Directions : 
 Mix together all the spices,  yogurt , and  papaya  and coat the  beef  cubes with it. 
 Leave to marinate for 5 – 6 hours, or preferably overnight. 
 Skewer the coated/marinated beef with the barbecue stick one by one. Then grill over charcoal. Serve hot and plain or with rice. 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Servings:  2 skewers per plate
