 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 3/4 cup  soy milk 
 2 1/2 tbsp  agar agar  (flakes or powdered) 
 2 1/2 tbsp light  oil 
 4 tbsp finely ground raw cashews 
 2 tbsp  nutritional yeast  flakes 
 2 tsp  apple cider vinegar 
 1 tsp tahini 
 1 tsp  sea salt 
 1/2 tsp dijon  mustard 
 dash of  paprika 
 Directions : 
 Boil  water  and  agar-agar  together and simmer 5 minutes, stirring frequently. Place all ingredients in a blender and process until smooth. Pour immediately into a lightly oiled dish. Chill and slice. Keep refrigerated.
