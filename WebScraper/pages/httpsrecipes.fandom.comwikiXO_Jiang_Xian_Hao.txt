Description : 
 Oyster in XO sauce
 
 Ingredients : 
 10 fresh  oysters 
 ½  green pepper 
 ½  red pepper 
 1 tbsp minced  garlic 
 1 stalks chopped  spring onion 
 5 thin slices  ginger 
 2 – 3 stalks  leaf vegetable  
 Seasoning : 
 1 tbsp  ginger  sauce 
 2 tbsp  light soy sauce 
 1 tbsp  cornflour 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 some  pepper 
 some  sesame oil 
 Sauce : 
 3 tbsp  satay paste 
 1 tbsp  oyster sauce 
 ½ tbsp  chile oil 
 4 tbsp  water 
 1 tbsp  light soy sauce 
 ½ tbsp  sugar 
 ½ tbsp  cornflour 
 Directions : 
 Wash oysters well, marinate with seasoning, stir well. 
 Blanch the vegetable with  salt  and  oil  in hot  water , put on dish. 
 Chopped green and  red pepper , stir fry with 1 tbsp of  oil . 
 Add minced  garlic  and  ginger , then oysters, stir fry well. 
 Pour in sauce, fry and sauté until oysters are done and sauce less. 
 Sprinkle chopped spring  onion , dish up over the vegetable, serve.
