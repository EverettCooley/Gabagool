 Ingredients : 
 6 cl  white rum 
 6 cl  pineapple juice 
 6 cl  orange juice 
 1 cl  grenadine  syrup 
 ice  cubes 
 Directions : 
 Mix  pineapple juice  with  orange juice  and the  white rum  and fill it into a glass. 
 Adding then slowly the  grenadine  sirup and then the  ice  cubes. 
 Decorating with a cocktail  cherry  and a piece of  orange .
