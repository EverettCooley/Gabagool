Description : 
 This recipe is only 1% fat
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 medium  russet potatoes 
 ¼ cup  non fat milk 
 ½ cup non-fat  sour cream 
 ⅛ cup  parsley 
 1 tsp  onion powder 
 ½ tsp  garlic powder 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Boil  potatoes  until soft, drain, cube and mash in a large bowl. 
 Add all ingredients and mash together until well mixed.
