Description : 
 Inherited from the Davidson Estate in Terrell, Texas in 1987. This recipe indicates it came from the back of a  rolled oats  container and is dated 1924.
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅓ cup  granulated sugar 
 ⅓ cup  brown sugar 
 ⅓ cup butter-flavored  vegetable shortening  (recipe revamped to include  butter  flavor  shortening ) 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 2  eggs 
 2 large ripe  bananas 
 ¼ cup sweetened bakers  coconut 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 2¼ teaspoons  baking powder 
 1¾ cup  all-purpose flour 
 ½ cup  rolled oats 
 ¾ cup chopped  walnuts 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 cream  sugars and  shortening . 
 Add  eggs ,  vanilla , and mashed  bananas . 
 Stir in remaining dry ingredients,  coconut , and  walnuts . 
 Pour into well-greased loaf pan. 
 Bake 1 hour, or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
