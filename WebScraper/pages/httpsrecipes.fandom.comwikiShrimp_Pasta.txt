Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 12 oz package  rotini  pasta 
 1 cup fresh grated  Parmesan cheese 
 4 cloves pressed  garlic 
 ¾ cup  olive oil 
 1-2 cups  shrimp  (½ lb.) 
 2 tsp dried  parsley   or  ½ cup fresh parsley 
 Directions : 
 Heat olive oil over low heat. 
 Add garlic and sauté lightly. 
 Remove from heat and add parsley. 
 Cook and drain pasta. 
 Toss hot pasta with the oil, garlic, and parsley mixture. 
 Add shrimp and toss. 
 Lastly add the Parmesan cheese and toss. 
 Serve immediately. Enjoy.
