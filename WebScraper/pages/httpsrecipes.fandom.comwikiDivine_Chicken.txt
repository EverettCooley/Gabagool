Easy roasted chicken recipe that makes its own sauce
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs bone-in skin-on  chicken  breasts 
 ¼ cup good  olive oil 
 4 teaspoons of fresh  thyme 
 1 tablespoon of  red pepper flakes 
 8 large  shallots , peeled and halved diagonally 
 juice and  zest  of 2  lemons 
 ¼ cup  dry vermouth   or  cooking  sherry 
 kosher salt  and freshly ground  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 Combine all ingredients save chicken. 
 Pour oven chicken breasts in ovenware container and rub into chicken. 
 Season chicken with additional salt and pepper. 
 Pop into over for 25 minutes at 350 °F or until shallots are tender and chicken is just pink inside. 
 Turn broiler up and broil chicken until skin is golden. 
 Serve with roasted potatoes, risotto, or, my favorite, cauliflower puree.
