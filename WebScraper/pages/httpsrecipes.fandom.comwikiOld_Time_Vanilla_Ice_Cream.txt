Ingredients : 
 2  eggs 
 1 can  sweetened condensed milk 
 1/4 cup  white sugar 
 1/4 cup  brown sugar 
 1 pint  heavy whipping cream 
 1 pint  half-and-half 
 1/4 tsp.  salt 
 2-1/2 tsp.  vanilla extract 
 Directions : 
 In a large mixing bowl whisk eggs 
 Add 1 can of sweetened condensed milk and whisk together until thoroughly mixed. 
 Then add white and brown sugars, and again mix thoroughly. Don't pack the brown sugar into the measuring cup; let it stay loose just as it comes out of the box. 
 Add heavy whipping cream, half and half, salt and vanilla extract. 
 Put in a sauce pan and simmer for about 15 minutes, stirring constantly. 
 Let cool a few hours in the fridge 
 Pour into the ice cream freezer and freeze according to the directions 
 Notes : 
 You can add fresh fruit about halfway through the churning process if you like.
