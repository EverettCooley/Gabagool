 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Come Into the Kitchen 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups fresh berries ( blackberry ,  blueberry ,  elderberry ,  huckleberry ,  juniper  berry) 
 Sliced and scraped peeled of one  lemon 
 Pinch of  tarragon   or   cloves 
 3 cups  vodka   or  2 cups vodka and 1 cup  brandy   or  sweet  white wine 
 1 cup  sugar syrup 
 Directions : 
 Lightly crush berries with a fork. 
 Add to vodka with lemon peel and cloves. 
 Steep 3 months. Strain. 
 Crush the berries through the filters to squeeze out all the juices. 
 Add sugar syrup to taste. 
 Mature 4 to 6 weeks.
