Description : 
 Individual mini-meat loaves. Easy, moist & delicious, and the leftovers make good sandwiches. Great for picky eaters as you can add to or leave things out of each. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ lb  ground beef 
 2 tablespoons instant  onion flakes 
 ½ teaspoon Watkins Meat Magic flavor enhancer 
 ½ cup  breadcrumbs 
 1  egg , beaten 
 2 ounces  tomato sauce  (optional) 
 2 ounces shredded  cheese  (½ cup) (optional) 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅛ teaspoon  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Preheat the oven to 450°F. 
 Combine all ingredients in a large bowl and mix well. 
 Generously fill each section of the muffin tin with  meat . 
 Bake for 15 – 20 minutes and serve.
