Ingredients : 
 4½ oz coarsely chopped mixed  dried fruit , such as  raisins ,  apricots ,  cherries ,  prunes  or  cranberries 
 1 cup  orange juice 
 2 tsp  sugar 
 1 tbsp dried  orange peel 
 4 x 3 oz baked  sweet potatoes 
 Directions : 
 In a small non-aluminum saucepan, combine all ingredients except  potatoes  and bring to a boil. 
 Lower heat and simmer, covered, for 5 minutes. 
 Uncover and simmer until liquid is slightly syrupy, 10-15 minutes. 
 With fork, blossom the  potatoes  and spoon ¼ of the fruit mixture evenly over each. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Serving provides: 2 fruit, 1 bread, 7 cal. Each serving equals 203 cal, 0 gm fat, 3 gm protein, 30 gm carbo.
