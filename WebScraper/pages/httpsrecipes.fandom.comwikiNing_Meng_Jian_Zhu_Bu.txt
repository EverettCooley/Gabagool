Description : 
 Fried  pork  fillet with  lemon juice 
 
 Ingredients : 
 250 g lean  pork 
 1 tbsp mashed  garlic 
 Seasoning : 
 1 tbsp  light soy sauce 
 ½ tbsp  sugar 
 ⅓ tbsp  salt 
 ½  egg 
 1 tbsp  cornflour 
 some  pepper  and  sesame oil 
 Sauce : 
 250 ml  water 
 1 tbsp tomato  ketchup 
 2 tbsp fresh  lemon juice 
 2 tbsp mashed  lemon peel 
 3 tbsp  sugar 
 ⅓ tbsp  cornflour 
 Directions : 
 Cut lean  pork  into thick slices, hit lightly by a flat hammer. 
 Slice 3 slashes on the white tissues on fillet, prevent rolling. 
 Marinate  pork  fillet with seasoning for 20 minutes. 
 Fry  pork  fillets both sides until they turn slightly yellow and fully cooked. 
 Heat wok with 1 tbsp of  oil , fry the mashed  garlic , then add sauce. 
 Add cooked  pork  fillet during sauce is boiled, stir well, dish up and serve.
