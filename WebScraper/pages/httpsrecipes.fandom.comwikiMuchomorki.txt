      Muchomorkie     
 Ingredients : 
 8  hard-boiled eggs , shelled 
 4 small  tomatoes , halved length-wise 
 salt  and  pepper 
 mayonnaise 
 lettuce 
 Directions : 
 Cut ends off the  eggs . 
 Stand upright on a plate lined with  lettuce  to create a  mushroom  stem. 
 Slice  tomatoes  in half lengthwise, hollow out a bit of the centers and place on the  eggs  to form ( mushroom ) caps (if your  tomatoes  are too big just trim the edges back). 
 Dot the flybanes' ( mushroom ) caps with mayonnaise.
