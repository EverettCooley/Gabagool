Ingredients : 
 1 cup  quinoa flour 
 ½ teaspoon  ammonia 
 ¼ pound  shortening 
 1 cup warm  water 
 1 pinch  salt 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 2 cups  rice flour 
 2  eggs 
 ½ teaspoon  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Sift  quinoa flour , add ammonia,  cinnamon , and all the ingredients alternating liquids and solids, until you get a soft dough. 
 Roll out thin and cut into desired shapes. 
 Bake in a medium heat (350°F) in oven. 
 Let them rest and sprinkle some powdered  sugar  on top of each cookie.
