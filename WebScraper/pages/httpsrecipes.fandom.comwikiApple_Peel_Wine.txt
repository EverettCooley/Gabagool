 Description : 
 Purchased from the Rosenthal Estate in Montgomery, Alabama in 1989. Dated 1936. Very different, but very good.
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 gallon  water 
 3  yeast  cakes 
 5 pounds  sugar 
 1 quart  orange  juice 
 juice of 3 medium  lemons 
 peeling from 12 tart  apples 
 Directions : 
 heat the water to 110 degrees. : 
 Dissolve  yeast  cakes in the warm water 
 Stir in  sugar ,  orange  juice,  lemon  juice and  apple  peeling. 
 Remove from heat; pour into a large jug but do not fill completely to top. 
 Do not tighten lid on jug. 
 Let peeling and juices stand for 9 days. 
 Strain and sweeten to taste.
