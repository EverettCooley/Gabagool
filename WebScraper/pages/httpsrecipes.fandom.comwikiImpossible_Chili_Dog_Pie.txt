Ingredients : 
 6 Hot Dogs, Sliced 
 1/3 C. Chopped  onions , if Desired 
 1 1/2 C.  milk 
 3/4 C. Biscuit Mix 
 3  eggs 
 1 1/2 C. Shredded  Cheese  (about 6 Oz.) 
 1 Can of Hormel  chili 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Grease 9 x 13 Inch Pan. 
 Spread Hot Dogs And  onions . 
 Pour  chili  Over That And Top With as Much  Cheese  as You Like. 
 in a Bowl, Mix  milk , Biscuit Mix, And  eggs  Together. Beat Until Smooth. 
 Pour Into Pan Over The Hot Dog And  chili  . 
 Sprinkle With  Cheese . 
 Bake at 400 Degrees Until Knife Inserted Into Center Comes Out Clean, About 30 to 45 Minutes. 
 Cool 5 Minutes.
