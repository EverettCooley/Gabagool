In Medium Bowl, mix: 
 
 2 T.  Mayonnaise 
 2 T.  sour cream 
 1 1/2 T.  cider vinegar 
 1/2 T.  sugar 
 1/8 T.  salt 
 1/8 T.  pepper . 
 Add:
 
 1/2 Small Head  green cabbage , shredded 
 1/2 Cup Thinly Sliced  green onion 
 1 T.  celery seed . 
 Toss Until Well Coated And Serve. Serves 4
