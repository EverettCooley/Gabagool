Stir up The Berry Best Batch of  banana  Muffins You've Ever Made!
 
Prep: 15 minutes - Ready In: 40 minutes
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2/3 Cup  sugar 
 1/2 Cup  oil 
 2  eggs 
 2/3 Cup Mashed Ripe  bananas 
 1 Teaspoon  vanilla 
 1 2/3 Cups Gold Medal  all-purpose flour 
 1 Teaspoon  baking soda 
 1/2 Teaspoon  salt 
 3/4 Cup Finely Chopped Fresh  strawberries 
 
Directions:
 
 Heat Oven to 375 °F. Line 12 Muffin Cups With Paper Baking Cups. in Medium Bowl, Combine  sugar ,  oil  And  eggs ; Blend Well. Stir in  bananas  And  vanilla . 
 Lightly Spoon  flour  Into Measuring Cup; Level Off. Add  flour ,  baking soda  And  salt  to  sugar  Mixture; Stir Just Until Combined. 
 Stir in  strawberries . Spoon Batter Evenly Into Paper-Lined Muffin Cups. 
 Bake at 375 °F. For 17 to 21 Minutes or Until Toothpick Inserted in Center Comes Out Clean. Immediately Remove From Muffin Cups. Serve Warm or Cool. 
 
Makes 12 Muffins
