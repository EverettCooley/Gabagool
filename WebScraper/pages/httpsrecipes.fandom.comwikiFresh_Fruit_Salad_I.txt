Description : 
 Contributed by Lilly at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 4 – 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pint  blueberries 
 ½ lb large bing  cherries , halved, pitted 
 4 large  peaches , sliced 
 ¼ cup  Grand Marnier 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 1 pint fresh  raspberries 
 vanilla  yogurt , optional 
 Directions : 
 Toss blueberries, cherries and peaches with Grand Marnier and sugar. 
 Put fruit in clear glass bowl and sprinkle raspberries over everything. 
 Serve plain or with chilled vanilla yogurt.
