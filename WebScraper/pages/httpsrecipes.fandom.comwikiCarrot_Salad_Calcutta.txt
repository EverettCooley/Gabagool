Ingredients : 
 1 (15 oz.) can  tomato  sauce 
 1 c  oil 
 1 c  sugar 
 ¾ c  vinegar 
 1 tsp dry  mustard 
 ½ tsp  pepper 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 2 lbs.  carrots 
 1  green pepper 
 1  onion 
 Directions : 
 Cut  green pepper  and  onion  into thin slices. 
 Peel and thinly slice  carrots . 
 Cook  carrots  briefly in  water . 
 Blend  tomato  sauce,  oil ,  sugar ,  vinegar , and seasonings in a bowl. 
 Add vegetables. Refrigerate overnight.
