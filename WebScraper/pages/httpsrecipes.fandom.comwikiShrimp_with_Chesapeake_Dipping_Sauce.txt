Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 4 servings of shrimp with dipping sauce. 
 Ingredients : 
 12 oz  beer   or  1½ cups water 
 2 tbsp  pickling spice 
 1½ pounds  shrimp , in the shell 
 Dipping sauce : 
 ⅓ cup  mayonnaise 
 3 tbsp each  ketchup , minced  dill pickle  and  onion 
 2 tbsp white  horseradish 
 Directions : 
 Bring beer and pickling spice to a boil in saucepan. 
 Cover and boil 5 minutes. 
 Mix dipping sauce ingredients in a bowl and leave at room temp while cooking shrimp. 
 Add shrimp to saucepan, return to a boil, cover reduce heat and simmer until shrimp turn pink and are barely opaque at center. 
 Drain and serve immediately with the dipping sauce.
