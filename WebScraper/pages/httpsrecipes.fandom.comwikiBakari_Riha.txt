 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 800 gram Mutton with bone (bite size pieces) 
 225 gram  Onion , thinly sliced 
 20 gram  garlic , sliced 
 1 each  cherry  red pepper, sliced 
 Few  curry leaves 
 3 pieces Raan’baa leaf 
 50 ml  coconut oil 
 5 tbsp. Coriander powder 
 200 ml  coconut cream 
 400 ml  coconut milk 
 1 litre  water 
 salt  to season 
 Directions : 
 Heat the  oil . Sauté sliced  Onion , sliced  garlic ,  cherry pepper  and Raan’baa leaf till golden brown. Add mutton, coriander powder and  salt . 
 Sauté and then add  coconut milk  and  water . Simmer till mutton is soft. 
 Finish with  coconut cream  and adjust seasoning.
