Ingredients : 
 4 oz uncooked  vermicelli 
 1 medium  red bell pepper  cut into 2 x ¼" strips 
 1 small  mango , chopped 
 1 medium  tomato , chopped 
 2 tbsp fresh  cilantro , chopped 
 1 tbsp  peanut oil 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp ground  cardamom 
 ¼ cup cocktail  peanuts , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Cook and drain  vermicelli  per package directions. 
 Rinse with cold  water  and drain. 
 Toss  vermicelli  and remaining ingredients except  peanuts . 
 Sprinkle with  peanuts .
