Description : 
 Makes 6 to 8 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 cup (4 ounces) shredded  Swiss cheese 
 1 medium  tomato , coarsely chopped 
 1 small  zucchini , coarsely chopped 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 2 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 1 tablespoon  cider vinegar 
 1 tablespoon  water 
 1 teaspoon  marjoram  leaves, crushed 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 ¼ teaspoon dry  mustard 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  cheese ,  tomato ,  zucchini ,  onion  and  garlic  in large bowl. 
 Blend  oil ,  vinegar ,  marjoram ,  salt ,  pepper  and  mustard . 
 Toss  rice  mixture with dressing. 
 Spoon into 2-quart baking dish; bake at 350 °F for 20 to 25 minutes, or until hot.
