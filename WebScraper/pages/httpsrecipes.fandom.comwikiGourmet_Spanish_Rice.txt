Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 2 tablespoons  butter ,  margarine , or  oil 
 2 cups drained canned  tomatoes , chopped 
 1 tart cooking  apple , peeled, cored, and coarsely chopped 
 ⅓ cup  sliced almonds 
 2 teaspoons  chili powder 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  garlic powder 
 3 cups cooked  rice  (cooked in  beef broth ) 
 Directions : 
 Cook  onion  in  butter  in large skillet until soft but not brown. 
 Stir in  tomatoes ,  apple ,  almonds ,  chili powder ,  salt  and  garlic powder ; cook 2 to 4 minutes. 
 Add  rice  and cook, stirring, until thoroughly heated.
