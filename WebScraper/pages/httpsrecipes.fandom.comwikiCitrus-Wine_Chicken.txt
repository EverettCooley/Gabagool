Ingredients : 
 1 frying  chicken , skinned and cut up (less calories when skinned) 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 ¼ tsp  onion powder 
 ¼ tbsp  paprika 
 1 tbsp  oleo , diet 
 1 cup  orange juice 
 1 cup  white wine 
 1 tsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 1 clove  garlic  minced 
 2 tbsp  cornstarch 
 ¼ cup  water 
 3 packets  Equal 
 Directions : 
 Lightly sprinkle  chicken  pieces with  salt ,  pepper ,  onion  powder and  paprika . 
 Place  chicken  in non stick shallow casserole. 
 Melt oleo. 
 Brush  chicken  pieces. 
 Bake at 375°F for 40 minutes. 
 Combine juice, wine,  Worcestershire sauce  and  garlic . 
 Pour over  chicken . 
 Cover and bake 20 minutes or until tender. 
 Remove  chicken . 
 Combine  cornstarch  and  water . 
 Add to pan juices. 
 Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until thickened. 
 Remove from heat. 
 Stir in Equal. 
 Serve sauce over  chicken .
