Ingredients : 
 ⅔ cup  Karo, Blue Label 
 ⅔ cup  sugar 
 ½ cup  Mazola 
 ½ cup  milk 
 2  eggs 
 1 cup  raisins 
 ½ cup chopped  dates 
 3 teaspoons  baking powder 
 3¼ cups  flour 
 ¼ cup Argo or Kingsford's  cornstarch 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon  clove 
 1 teaspoon  cinnamon 
 1 teaspoon  nutmeg 
 Directions : 
 First stir dry ingredients together and add fruit. 
 Mix  Karo ,  sugar , Mazola,  milk  and  egg  and beat in  flour  mixture. 
 Drop by teaspoonfuls (an inch apart) onto a pan oiled with Mazola, and bake in a moderate oven, 350°F, about fifteen minutes.
