Ingredients : 
 chunky natural  peanut butter 
 sandwich bread 
 raisins 
 banana  slices 
 jelly 
 marshmallow  fluff 
 apple  slices 
 Directions : 
 Pair chunky, natural  peanut   butter  and sandwich bread with any or all of the following (my son's favorite is  peanut   butter  with  raisins  and  banana  slices): jelly,  marshmallow  fluff,  raisins , frozen  banana  slices, and  apple  slices. 
 Combine ingredients creatively and spread on bread.
