Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  Myers's Original Dark Rum 
 1 dash  port 
 1 dash  orange curaçao 
 ¾ oz  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Stir ingredients with crushed  ice . 
 Strain into sugar-rimmed cocktail glass. 
 Garnish with  lemon  twist.
