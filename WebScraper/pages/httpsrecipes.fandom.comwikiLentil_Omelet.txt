 Ingredients : 
 3  eggs 
 1 teacupful  lentils 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ½  pepper 
 Directions : 
 To 1 teacupful of boiled  lentils  take 3 well-beaten  eggs , and  pepper  and  salt  to taste. 
 Add 1 dessert spoonful of  water  to each  egg , and mix the  lentils  and  eggs  smooth. 
 Fry the mixture as an omelet in boiling  butter .
