 Ingredients : 
 2Â lbs boneless  Chicken  breast 
 2 cups  pineapple juice 
 2 cups  pineapple  tidbits 
 2 cups rasins 
 2 cups  cashew  halves 
 2 cups  mayo 
 1 cup honey 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 2 tsp  black pepper 
 2 tsp  salt 
 2 cloves fresh  garlic 
 1/4 tsp cayanne  pepper  optinal 
 Directions : 
 Cook 11/2 c.  pineapple juice  and 1/4 c. honey and  garlic  to a boil. Remove from heat and cool. Combine  Chicken  and  pineapple juice  mixture.Place in a tupperware boil or large sandwich bags and refrigerate over night. Drain liquid off  Chicken  and either bake or grill  Chicken  until cooked but not dry. Cube  Chicken  and put in large mixing bowl. Add  pineapple ,rasins and  cashew  halves.Mix together. Add  salt  and  pepper  and cayanne  pepper  mix. In mixing bowl add  mayo  1/2 cup pineapple juice and remaining honey and whisk until well blended. I have also added marshmellow  cream  to make it extra special for parties. Pour dressing mixture over  Chicken  and mix well chill for about 2hours. This can either be served on a bed of mixed greens or served on crosiants.
