Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz  bourbon 
 1½ oz  cranberry juice 
 ½ oz  lime juice 
 ½ oz  grenadine 
 1 tsp  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Shake all ingredients with cracked  ice . 
 Strain into cocktail glass or over fresh cracked  ice  in old-fashioned glass. 
 Garnish with a half-slice of  orange .
