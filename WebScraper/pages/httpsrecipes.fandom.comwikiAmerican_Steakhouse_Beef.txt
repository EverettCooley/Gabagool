      American Steakhouse Beef     
 Ingredients : 
 1 (1.5 lbs) trimmed lean  boneless beef  top  sirloin steaks 
 ⅓ cup low sodium  soy sauce 
 ⅓ cup unsweetened  pineapple juice 
 ⅓ cup dry  sherry 
 ¼ cup  cider vinegar 
 Directions : 
 Place steak in large, shallow dish. 
 Combine  soy sauce  and remaining ingredients; pour over steak. 
 Cover and chill 4 hours. 
 Drain, discarding marinade. 
 grill  over 400 – 500 °F for 10 to 15 minutes on each side. 
 To serve, slice across the grain into thin slices.
