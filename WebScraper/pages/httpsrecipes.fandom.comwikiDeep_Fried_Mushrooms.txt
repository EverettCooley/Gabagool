Ingredients : 
 1 lb. First Choice  mushrooms 
 3/4 cup.  all-purpose flour 
 3/4 cup.  club soda 
 1/2 tsp.  garlic salt 
 Cooking oil for deep frying 
 Directions : 
 Wash mushrooms and dry thoroughly on towel. 
 Small mushrooms may be left whole; large ones should be cut into 1/4 inch slices. 
 Stir flour, club soda and garlic salt together until smooth. 
 Dip mushrooms in batter and fry in 400 degree oil for about 5 minutes or until golden brown. Oil should be deep enough for mushrooms to float freely. 
 Drain on paper towels.
