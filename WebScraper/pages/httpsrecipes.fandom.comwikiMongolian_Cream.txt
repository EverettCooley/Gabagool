Description : 
 This imperial dessert starts with gelatin and is made with real fruit, sour cream, and whipping cream. Fruit lovers simply know it as a divine treat on any occasion.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 ⅜ cup  whipping cream 
 4 tablespoons  water , warm only, if desired 
 1 ¼ cup  sugar , brown only 
 2 envelopes plain  gelatin 
 1 ⅜ cup  sour cream 
 ½ teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 2 ⅜ cups mixed fruits (kiwifruit, pineapple, strawberry, starfruit, blueberry, papaya, mango, and orange) 
 Directions : 
 Mix together cream, sugar, water (optional), and gelatin in a saucepan and heat gently until the gelatin is thoroughly dissolved. 
 Cool until slightly thickened. Fold or mix with the sour cream and flavor with vanilla. 
 Whisk until mixture is quite smooth. 
 Pour mixture into a serving bowl or 3 cup metal mold. 
 If you want to make individual servings, pour into six small ½ cup individual molds. 
 Cover and chill until set, at least 4 hours or above. 
 Unmold by dipping the container in hot water until edges start to melt. 
 Invert the mold onto a serving dish and surround with fruit. Serve cold and plain or with other desserts.
