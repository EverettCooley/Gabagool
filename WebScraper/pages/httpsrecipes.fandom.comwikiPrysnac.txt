      Prysnac     
 Ingredients : 
 10 oz  broccoli , frozen chopped 
 6 x  eggs , unbeaten 
 24 oz  cottage cheese , small curd 
 6 tbl  flour 
 8 oz  American cheese , diced 
 Â¼ cup  butter , melted 
 2 x  scallion , chopped 
 salt , to taste 
 Directions : 
 Quickly thaw frozen  broccoli  by placing in a colander ar holding under hot running  water . 
 Separate the pieces with fork; drain well. 
 Combine  broccoli  with all remaining ingredients in a large bowl and beat until well blended. 
 Pour into greased crockpot. 
 Cover and cook on high 1 hour, then on low 2 to 3 hours.
