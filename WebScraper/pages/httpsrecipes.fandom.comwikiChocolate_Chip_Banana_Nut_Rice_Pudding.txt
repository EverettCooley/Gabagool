Description : 
 Makes 8 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  medium-grain rice 
 2 cups  milk 
 1 x 14-ounce can  sweetened condensed milk 
 2 medium  bananas , sliced 
 ½ cup  semi-sweet chocolate  chips 
 ½ cup chopped  pecans 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  milk  and  sweetened condensed milk  in 2-quart saucepan. 
 Cook over medium heat until thick and creamy, 20 to 25 minutes, stirring frequently. 
 Remove from heat. 
 Alternate layers of  bananas ,  chocolate chips ,  pecans  and  rice  pudding in parfait glasses. 
 Garnish with  chocolate chips  and  pecans .
