Description : 
 This nutrient-dense, fiber-rich side dish comes together quickly, so it saves you time in the kitchen. This Asian-inspired creation tastes great with grilled meat or seafood. Ordinary broccoli springs to life with this garlicky lemon-soy dressing. Serve it with any Asian-flavored dish or even a simple grilled chicken or shrimp skewer.
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Source:  The South Beach Diet Quick & Easy Cookbook  Phase 1 
 Prep time: 10 minutes | Start to finish: 8 minutes 
 Serves 2 (1-cup servings) 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ tbsp + ½ tsp  canola oil 
 ½ (1½-pound) head  broccoli , cut into florets (3 cups) 
 1 tbsp low-sodium  soy sauce 
 1  garlic  clove, minced 
 1 tsp fresh  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Heat oil in a large wok or high-sided skillet over medium-high heat. 
 Add broccoli and stir-fry for 2 minutes. 
 Add soy sauce, garlic, and lemon juice; continue to stir-fry until broccoli is crisp-tender, about 5 minutes. 
 Serve hot. 
 Nutritional information : 
 80 Calories | 5g Total fat (0g Saturated fat) | 4g Protein | 7g Carbs | 3g Fiber | 300mg Sodium
