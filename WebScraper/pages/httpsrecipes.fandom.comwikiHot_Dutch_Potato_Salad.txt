Description : 
 A potato salad that is eaten warm. You can add some meat like  chicken ,  bacon  or  ham  if you like a little more taste. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  leaf lettuce , shredded 
 1 small  onion , chopped 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 6 slices  bacon 
 ¼ cup  vinegar 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 ½ cup  water 
 2 tbsp  flour 
 1 tbsp  mustard 
 1  egg 
 4 medium  potatoes , cooked, mashed 
 2  eggs , hard cooked, sliced 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl combine  lettuce ,  onion ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Dice  bacon  and fry. 
 Reserve 3 tbsp  bacon  drippings. 
 Blend  vinegar ,  sugar . 
 water ,  flour ,  mustard  and  egg  together; add to  bacon  drippings and cook until thickened. 
 Add hot mashed potatoes to  lettuce  and pour over hot dressing. 
 Add hard cooked  eggs  and toss. 
 Diced  ham  or  chicken  may be added.
