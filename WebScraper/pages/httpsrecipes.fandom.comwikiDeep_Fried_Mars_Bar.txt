Deep Fried Mars Bar. 
 Take one Mars Bar
 Remove wrapper.
 Dip in batter.
 Drop in hot oil until golden brown.
 Take out and drain.
 Allow to cool slightly then it is ready to serve.
 Feel arteries harden.
