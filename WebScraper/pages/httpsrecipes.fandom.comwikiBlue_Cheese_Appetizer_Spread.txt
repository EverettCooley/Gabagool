Description : 
 I got a new cookbook from my sister-in-law — Jack Daniel's Cookbook, The Spirit of Tennessee. I'm going to share one.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Makes 1 cup. 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  butter , softened 
 ½ cup  blue cheese , softened 
 3 tablespoons Jack Daniel's  whiskey 
 ¼ cup  walnuts , finely chopped 
 Directions : 
 Combine  butter ,  cheese  and Jack Daniel's  whiskey  in a food processor and blend until smooth. 
 Remove from processor; stir in  walnuts . 
 Cover and chill for several hours or overnight. 
 Serve with crackers.
