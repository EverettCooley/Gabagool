Ingredients : 
 5 â€“ 6 slices whole grain  bread , cut into cubes 
 6 tbsp extra virgin  olive oil 
 4 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1 tbsp dried  basil 
 2 tsp dried  tarragon 
 2 tsp dried  oregano 
 Directions : 
 In a large frying pan, heat the  olive oil . 
 Add the bread cubes and toss constantly, on high heat, for five minutes, until the bread has absorbed most of the  oil . 
 Turn heat to low, add the  garlic  and herbs, and fry for 25 minutes, tossing occasionally, until the croutons are browned and crunchy.
