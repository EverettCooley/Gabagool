Description : 
 This recipe came from The Printey Estate sale. It was obtained it as part of the Family Collection from The Printey Estate in Forney, Texas in 2001. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups cooked or canned white  cannellini beans , drained and rinsed 
 ½ cup chopped  red onion 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 ½ cup chopped fresh  parsley 
 ½ cup  olive oil 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 2 cans water-packed  tuna , drained 
 Directions : 
 Mix beans,  onion ,  garlic  and  parsley  with the  olive oil 
 Season with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Arrange  tuna  on top, mixing lightly without mashing 
 Allow to stand for 1 hour to allow flavors to blend.
