Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 6 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  snow peas , strings removed 
 1 large  red bell pepper , cored, seeded, thinly sliced 
 8 oz can sliced  water chestnuts , drained 
 2 large  scallions , trimmed and sliced 
 1 tbs  light soy sauce 
 1 tbs  hoisin sauce 
 1 tbs  sesame oil 
 1 tbs  rice wine vinegar 
 Â½ cup fried  rice noodles  or  crispy chow mein noodles 
 Directions : 
 Bring a pot of water to a boil. 
 Salt and add snow peas. 
 Cook 3 min. Drain. 
 In a bowl, combine peas, red pepper, chestnuts and scallions. 
 Allow to cool. 
 In a 2nd bowl, whisk soy sauce, hoisin, sesame oil and vinegar. 
 Toss with vegetables. 
 Serve at room temp or cold. 
 Garnish with noodles and sliced scallions just before serving.
