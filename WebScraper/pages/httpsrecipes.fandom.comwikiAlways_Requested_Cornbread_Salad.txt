 Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: The Great Flavors of Texas 
 Serves 12 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pan cooked  cornbread , crumbled 
 1½ cups  mayonnaise 
 2 cups  celery , sliced 
 1  green peppers , seeded and chopped 
 ¾ cup  green onion , chopped 
 1 jar  green olives  and  pimentos , drained (5 oz), rinsed and chopped 
 ¾ cup  pecans , toasted and chopped 
 2 large  tomatoes , chopped 
 1 tbsp  sage 
 pepper  to taste 
 10 slices  bacon , fried crisp and crumbled 
 1  jalapeño pepper , seeded and chopped 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl, combine all ingredients. 
 Refrigerate 3 to 4 hours before serving. 
 Wonderful!
