Description : 
 This crab dish is well worth the effort!
 
 Ingredients : 
 2  dungeness crabs , cleaned 
 2  onions , one whole and one minced 
 1  carrot 
 ¼ lb fresh  mushrooms , thinly sliced 
 ¼ cup  butter 
 Worcestershire sauce 
 2 tablespoons  all-purpose flour 
 1 cup 2%  low-fat milk 
 salt ,  pepper  to taste 
 2 tablespoons old  cheddar  cheese, grated 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Prepare crabs, clean them and put them in a large pot. 
 Place one whole  onion  and 1  carrot  into a pot of salted  water  (sprinkle of  salt  only!) boil for 20 minutes. 
 Shells will change colour when done. 
 Remove from pot and cool. 
 Remove meat from shells. 
 In a large frying pan, saute  mushrooms  and  onions  until  onions  are tender. 
 Add crabmeat, Worcerstershire sauce, and toss until well blended. 
 In a bowl, mix  flour  and  milk  until smooth and creamy. 
 Add to crab mixture and stir constantly for about 5 minutes over medium heat until well blended. 
 Add  salt  and  pepper  and mix well. 
 Fill 2 shells with crab mixture. 
 Sprinkle the grated  cheese  on top. 
 Bake until  cheese  melts (about 4 minutes).
