 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pkt  yeast 
 1 cup Warm  water 
 1  egg 
 1/2 cup Honey 
 1 tbl Coriander 
 1/2 tsp  cinnamon 
 1/4 tsp Cloves 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 cup  milk , scalded 
 1/4 cup  butter , melted 
 5 cup  flour 
 Directions : 
 Separate bowl combine the  egg , honey, coriander,  cinnamon , cloves and  salt ; Mix well. Add the  yeast ,  milk , and  butter . Beat in 1 1/2 C  flour . Let rise until double, then punch down, knead for 1-2 minutes. Shape and place in a buttered loaf pan. Let rise for 1 hour. Bake in 300 oven for 50â€“60 minutes. 
 Makes 1 large loaf
