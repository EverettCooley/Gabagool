 Description : 
 Serves 4.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup long  grain   rice 
 1/3 cup  sugar 
 1/4 tsp.  salt 
 pinch  nutmeg 
 2 cups  milk 
 2/3 cup  water 
 1 tablespoon unsalted  butter 
 ½ tsp.  vanilla  (optional) 
 1/3 cup  raisins 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 300 F. Lightly grease a 1 1/2 quart baking dish 
 Mix everything except  Raisins  and place in baking dish. 
 Bake uncovered about 1 hour. 
 Stir in  Raisins , dust top lightly with  cinnamon , reduce heat to 250 F. 
 And bake for another 30 minutes.
