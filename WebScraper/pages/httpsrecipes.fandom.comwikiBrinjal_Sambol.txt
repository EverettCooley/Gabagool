Description : 
 Sri Lankan eggplant
 
 Ingredients : 
 4  eggplants 
 75 g  onion 
 1  chile , fresh 
 250 ml  oil 
 Â½ tsp  salt 
 juice of 1  lime 
 Directions : 
 Wash the  eggplant , then cut into fairly small dice. 
 Chop the  onion  and  chile . 
 Heat the  oil  and fry the  eggplant  until browned. 
 Remove and drain. 
 Place in a dish, add the  onion ,  chile ,  salt  and  lime juice  and mix well.
