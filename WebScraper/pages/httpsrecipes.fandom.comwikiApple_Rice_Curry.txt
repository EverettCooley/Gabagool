Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  raisins 
 ½ cup boiling  water 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 ½ teaspoon  curry powder 
 ⅛ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 2 tart cooking  apples , peeled, cored and chopped 
 Directions : 
 Add  water  to  raisins ; set aside. 
 Cook  onion ,  curry  powder and  pepper  in  butter  in large skillet until  onion  is soft but not brown. 
 Add  rice  and  apples ; heat thoroughly. 
 Drain  raisins  and stir into  rice .
