 About Rose petals : 
 Roses are edible flowers and have a strong perfume flavor. From ancient times they have appeared at feasting tables as bouquets and as food. The flavor of roses is varied as the colors. The sweet, tart or spicy petals are easy to use and popular additions to any kitchen.
 They are used to make rose water, jams and syrups. Pesticide-free rose petals must be purchase because regular roses generally contain large amounts of pesticide.
