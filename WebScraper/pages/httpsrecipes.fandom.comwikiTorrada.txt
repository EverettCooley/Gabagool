Description : 
 Toast Bread. In Brazil you can find savory and sweet toast in the supermarkets. Home-made toast is delicious and very easy to prepare. 
 
 Ingredientes / Ingredients : 
 
 
 Portuguese 
 
 English 
 
 
 
 1 pão francês 
 
 
 1  baguette 
 
 Modo de fazer / Directions : 
 
 
 Portuguese 
 
 English 
 
 
 
 Corte o pão em fatias. 
 Coloque as fatias numa assadeira. 
 Asse em forno quente (180-200 graus) por uns 10 minutos ou até dourar. 
 
 
 Cut the bread into slices. 
 Put the slices on a baking tray. 
 Bake in preheated oven (330-350°F) for about 10 minutes or until golden brown.
