Ingredients : 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1½ cup  water 
 ¼ cup  potato starch 
 3  yolks 
 1 tbsp  margarine 
 ¼ cup  lemon juice 
 1 tsp  lemon extract , optional 
 6  egg whites 
 ⅓ cup  sugar 
 1 tbsp  potato starch 
 Directions : 
 Whip  egg whites  with  sugar  (⅓ cup) and  potato starch  until very stiff. 
 Boil 1 minute. 
 Add  margarine , juice, and  lemon extract . 
 Mix until smooth.
