Description : 
 Thyme  pastries
 
 Ingredients : 
 2½ cups plain  flour 
 1 tsp  yeast  dissolved in ¼ cup of  water . 
 a generous ½ cup tepid  water 
 1 tbsp  salt 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 6 tbsp  thyme 
 ½ cup  olive oil 
 Directions : 
 Dilute the  rice  in a bit of cold  water . 
 Boil the  milk  and add the  rice . 
 Stir until the mixture thickens. 
 Pour in cups and allow to cool. 
 Sprinkle  pistachios  on the surface before serving.
