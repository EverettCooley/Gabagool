Description : 
 A classic Filipino dessert, a caramel custard made of  egg yolk ,  sugar ,  milk  and  vanilla extract . The experience is a basic custard, with a very nice caramel syrup poured over and under. 
 
       Leche Flan     
 Ingredients : 
 12  egg yolks 
 1 can  evaporated milk 
 1 can  condensed milk 
 brown sugar  for a syrup 
 Directions : 
 Mix  eggs  and  milks  together. 
 Make brown syrup of the  sugar  and hot  water  in a flan pan. 
 Add  eggs  mixture and steam in a wok for 30-40 minutes. 
 Cool and invert on plate.
