Description : 
 Plantain  omelet
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 ripe (not overripe)  plantains , sliced thinly 
 ½ diced  onion 
 ½ cup diced  ham  or cooking  ham 
 6  eggs , beaten, with  salt  added to your taste 
 ½ cup  sausage  or Spanish  sausage , shredded 
 ⅓ cup sweet  peas 
 frying  oil 
 Directions : 
 Sauté the  plantain  slices in very hot frying  oil . 
 Use enough  oil  to cover the slices. 
 Remove the  plantain  slices when they are golden and soak off the excess  oil . 
 Sauté your choice of  ham  and sausages lightly, remove and add to the  plantains . 
 Add the sweet  peas  and the beaten  eggs . 
 Use an omelet frying pan if possible. 
 Add ⅓ cup frying  oil . 
 Place the ingredients in the pan and cook over medium heat for several minutes. 
 The sweet smell of the frying  plantains  will be replaced by a typical omelet smell when the mixture is ready to be turned over. 
 Do so carefully. 
 Let cook for another two to three minutes. 
 Serve immediately.
