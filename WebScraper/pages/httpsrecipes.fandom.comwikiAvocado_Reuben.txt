Description : 
 This is a colorful sandwich. Pink and yellow and green. It's pretty
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 slices of whole wheat vegan  bread 
 Nayonaise  and  ketchup  mixed together 
 1 ripe  California avocado 
 1 cup of  sauerkraut 
 Directions : 
 Toast 8 slices of vegan  bread . 
 Paint 4 slices with Russian dressing (nayo and  ketchup  mix) and top with sauerkraut. 
 Reheat these 4 slices. 
 Meanwhile, peel and slice the avocado and distribute on the remaining 4 slices. 
 Put one sauerkraut vegan  bread  on top of each avocado  bread  and slice each  sandwich  in half.
