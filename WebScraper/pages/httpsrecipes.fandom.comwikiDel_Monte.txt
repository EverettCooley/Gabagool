 Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 oz. Appleton White Jamaica  rum 
 3/4 oz.  lemon juice 
 1/4 oz.  Grenadine 
 Directions : 
 Shake with cracked  ice  and strain into chilled cocktail glass. Garnish with a  maraschino cherry .
