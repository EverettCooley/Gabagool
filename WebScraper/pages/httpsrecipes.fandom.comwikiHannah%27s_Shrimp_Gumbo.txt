Ingredients : 
 2 lbs fresh  shrimp , deveined 
 4 cups sliced smoke  sausage 
 3 cups sautéed young  okra 
 4  green onions , chopped 
 1  celery  stalk, thinly sliced 
 2 large, fresh  tomatoes ,chopped 
 1  red pepper , chopped 
 1 small  green pepper , chopped 
 3 tbsp  roux  — made from ¼ cup  vegetable oil  and ¼ cup  flour 
 red pepper 
 salt  and  pepper 
 vinegar  to taste 
 hot sauce  optional 
 2 cups uncooked  white  or  brown rice  (prefer white) 
 Directions : 
 Use roux to make watery soup based. 
 Pour in all ingredients and simmer for about one hour this is the very easy way.
