Description : 
 Sufficient for one hundred snaps.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cupfuls  molasses 
 1 cupful  brown sugar 
 1 cupful  Crisco 
 2 teaspoonfuls  baking soda 
 2 teaspoonfuls powdered  ginger 
 1 teaspoonful powdered  mace 
 1 teaspoonful  salt 
 2 tablespoonfuls boiling  water 
 flour  (enough to make a stiff paste) 
 Directions : 
 Cream Crisco and  sugar  together 
 Add  molasses , spices,  salt , soda mixed with boiling  water  and sufficient  flour  to make stiff paste. 
 Roll out thin, cut with small cutter, lay on Criscoed tins and bake in hot oven from five to seven minutes.
