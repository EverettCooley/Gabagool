Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4  carrots , peeled 
 4  celery  sticks 
 1  red  or  green pepper 
 1  cucumber , cleaned 
 1 lb (500 g)  Cheddar , sliced ½ inch (1¼ cm) thick 
 12 assorted  crackers 
 4 tbs (60 ml)  peanut butter 
 1 red  apple  with peel, seeded and thinly sliced 
 4 individual-size  apple juice  boxes 
 Directions : 
 Cut the carrots, celery, pepper and cucumber into sticks. Using a knife or cheese-cutters, cut out of the Cheddar slices amusing Halloween shapes, like pumpkins, ghost, crescent moon... Cut out 12 shapes total, as close together as possible to use maximum amount of cheese. 
 Spread peanut butter on crackers and top each one with one or two slices of apple and a cheese cutout. Decorate cheese cutouts with eyes and mouths, using apple peel, raisins or other pieces of fruit. 
 Serve immediately along with vegetable sticks and apple juice.
