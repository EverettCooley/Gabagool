      Palak Bhaaji     
 Ingredients : 
 2 – 3 bunches palak ( spinach ) 
 a pinch of  asafoetida 
 60 g  garlic  cloves, chopped finely 
 60 g  oil 
 100 g  onions , chopped 
 6 – 8  red chilies 
 5 g  chili powder 
 3 g  turmeric 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Clean the  spinach  and chop them finely. 
 Set aside. 
 Heat  oil  in a pan and fry the red chilies, chopped  onions  and chopped  garlic  until golden in color. 
 Then add  chili powder ,  turmeric ,  asafoetida  and fry for few minutes. 
 Now, add chopped  spinach  and fry for few more minutes. 
 Add  salt , cover the pan and cook until the  spinach  gets tender. 
 Serve hot with chapaties.
