Description : 
 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 700 g [1Â lb 6 oz]  Beef  round or rump steaks thinly sliced 
 300 g [10 oz] tinned [canned] whole or diced  tomatoes 
 4 tbsp  olive oil 
 2 cloves  garlic 
 1 tbsp  oregano 
 salt 
 freshly ground  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Heat the  oil  in a large frying pan and gently fry the  garlic  for 3 minutes. 
 Add the  tomatoes ,  salt ,  pepper  and  oregano  and cook for a further 10 minutes. 
 Add the meat and leave it to cook in the sauce over a low heat for about 15 minutes, or until tender.
