Concession  chili 
 
 4 Pounds Hamburger Meat 
 1 Large  Onion , Chopped 
 4 Cans (28 oz Each)  tomatoes 
 1 Can (12 Oz)  tomato  Paste 
 4 Pkg. (1.62 Oz. Each) Lawry's  chili seasoning  Mix 
 3/4 T.  garlic  Slat 
 2 T.  brown sugar 
 salt  And  pepper  to Taste
 
Brown Hamburger Meat And Drain Well. Add  Onion  And  tomatoes  Which Have Been Put Through a Food Processor. Add  tomato  Paste And  chili seasoning  Which Has Been Liquefied in  water . Add  garlic  And  brown sugar . Put Ingredients in a Large Round Roaster And Fill With  water . Let  chili  Boil Slowly For 3 Hours. Add  water  if it Boils Down Before That Time. Stir Often And Cook Slowly so That it Does Not Stick to The Bottom of Pan. You Can Add  salt  And  pepper  to Taste. Freezes Well. Source: Dine With The Angels
