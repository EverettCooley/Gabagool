Info 
 Cook Time : 
 Serves : 
 
 Ingredients 
 4 TBS  butter 
 1  egg 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 1/4 tsp  baking soda 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 cup  flour 
 1/2 tsp  baking powder 
 1/8 tsp  salt 
 3 large  apples , peeled, cored, cut evenly in crescent slices 
 Directions 
 Cream together butter and sugar. Add egg and beat. 
 Sift together flour, cinnamon, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Beat thoroughly. 
 Stir apples into batter which will be thick and hard to stir. Turn into a buttered 9 inch pie pan and spread smooth. 
 Bake in 350 degree oven for one hour. Cool and cut into wedges. Very delicious when served with vanilla ice cream. 
 Source 
 Apple Torte  by the New Jersey Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
