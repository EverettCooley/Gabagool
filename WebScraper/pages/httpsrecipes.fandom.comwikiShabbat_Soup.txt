Ingredients : 
 1 large  turkey  neck cut into pieces 
 3  carrots 
 3  zucchini 
 3  celery  sticks  or   celery root 
 2 small  onions 
 1 – 2 small  parsnips 
 a piece of  pumpkin  (optional) 
 parsley ,  dill 
 salt ,  pepper ,  chicken  soup powder to taste 
 Directions : 
 Place  turkey  neck in large soup pot. 
 Cover it with  water . 
 Boil for 15 – 20 minutes. 
 Scrap and discard any froth. 
 Add vegetables and spices. 
 Bring it to a boil and simmer on medium heat for 1 hour. 
 Add  pumpkin ,  parsley  and  dill  and cook for another ½ hour. 
 When cool, strain soup and add back cut-up vegetables (pieces of  carrot ,  zucchini ,  onion   parsnip ). 
 Serve with noodles and/or soup  nuts .
