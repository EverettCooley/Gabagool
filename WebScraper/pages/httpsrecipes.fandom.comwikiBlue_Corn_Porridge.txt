Description 
 A River of Recipes: Native American Recipes Using Commodity Foods  by the USDA Using Commodity Foods, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Serves : 8 
 Ingredients 
 4 cups  water 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1¼ cups roasted  blue  (or yellow)  cornmeal 
 Directions 
 Mix cornmeal and salt. 
 Bring water to boil. 
 Introduce cornmeal to boiling water and mix well. 
 Continue to beat slowly – stirring for about five minutes or until smooth and thick.
