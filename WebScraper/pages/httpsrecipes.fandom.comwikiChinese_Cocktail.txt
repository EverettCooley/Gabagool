 Description : 
 A  rum  drink
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 oz Jamaica  rum 
 1 tsp  triple sec 
 1 tbsp  Grenadine 
 1 dash  bitters 
 1 tsp Maraschino 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Shake well with  ice  and strain into a glass.
