Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 2/11/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pkg. meatballs (thawed) 
 1 loaf  Italian  or  French bread  broken into pieces 
 1 8 oz pkg.  cream cheese  softened 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 1 tbsp  Italian seasoning 
 ½ tbsp  pepper 
 2 cups shredded  mozzarella  cheese, divided 
 1 x 28 oz jar  spaghetti sauce 
 1 cup  water 
 2  garlic  cloves, diced 
 Directions : 
 Place  bread , broken into pieces in an ungreased 9x13 baking dish. 
 Combine  cream cheese ,  mayonnaise ,  Italian seasoning  and  pepper . 
 Spread over the pieces of  bread . 
 Sprinkle with ½ cup of the  mozzarella   cheese . 
 Combine sauce,  water  and  garlic . 
 Add meatballs. 
 Pour over the  cheese  mixture. 
 Sprinkle with the remaining  cheese . 
 Bake uncovered at 350°F for 30 minutes or until heated through.
