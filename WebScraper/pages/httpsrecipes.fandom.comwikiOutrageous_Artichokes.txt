 Ingredients : 
 4  artichokes , medium size 
 Â¼ cup  butter , melted 
 celery salt 
 2 tablespoons  lemon juice , concentrated 
 2 cups  water 
 Directions : 
 Wash, clean and trim artichokes. 
 Pour melted  butter  inside of  artichoke  leaves then sprinkle with  celery salt . 
 Mix lemon juice with  water  and pour inside pressure cooker. 
 Place steamer basket inside pressure cooker and add artichokes. 
 Close lid, bring to high pressure, lower heat on stove and pressure cook for 3 minutes. 
 Use cold- water  release method to release pressure according to manufacturer's directions. 
 Open lid and serve immediately while hot.
