 About Toddy palm seeds : 
 Toddy Palm Seed from Chaokoh. Toddy Palm or 'Look Dtahn' in Thai, is clear and crunchy. Serve with ice and syrup or top your favorite ice cream or even by itself. Ingredients: Toddy palm seed, water, sugar, calcium, chloride, citric acid. Net Weight: 10.00 oz. Product of Thailand.
