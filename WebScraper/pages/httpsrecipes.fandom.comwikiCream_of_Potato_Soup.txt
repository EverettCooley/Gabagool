Description 
 Serves : 4
 
 Ingredients 
 ½  onion , chopped 
 2 slices of  ham , diced 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 1 cup  instant non-fat dry milk  + 3 cups  water  (or 3 cups  non-fat milk ) 
 1 (15½ ounce) can  corn 
 ¾ cup instant  potato flakes 
 Directions 
 Brown onion and ham in butter or margarine for 5 minutes over low heat. 
 Add milk and corn. 
 Simmer 5 minutes and add potato flakes.
