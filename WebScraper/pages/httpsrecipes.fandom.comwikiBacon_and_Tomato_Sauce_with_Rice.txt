Makes 6 to 8 servings (3 cups sauce)
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup chopped  celery 
 1/2 cup chopped  Onion 
 1/4 cup finely minced  Bacon 
 2 tablespoons  flour 
 2 cups chopped canned  tomatoes 
 1 teaspoon  Sugar 
 1 cup  chicken broth 
 4 cups hot cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Combine  celery ,  Onion  and  Bacon  in large skillet; cook until  Bacon  is crisp. 
 Add  flour  and cook 1 minute. 
 Blend in  tomatoes ,  Sugar  and broth. 
 Continue cooking, stirring constantly, until thick and bubbly. 
 Serve over hot  rice .
