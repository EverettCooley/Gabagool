 Description : 
 A vegetable salad suitable for all kind of dishes.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 beetroot 
 2  carrots 
 3  tomatoes 
 1  green pepper 
 lettuce 
 4 spring  onions 
 1 tbsp  vinegar 
 1  lemon 
 1 tbsp  sesame oil 
 pinch  salt 
 Directions : 
 Chop vegetables and mix together. 
 Sprinkle with  vinegar ,  lemon juice  and  sesame oil . 
 Add  salt  and toss.
