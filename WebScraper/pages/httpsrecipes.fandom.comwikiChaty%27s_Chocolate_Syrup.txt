 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 6 ounces  semi-sweet chocolate 
 1/2 cup  evaporated milk 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Melt the  chocolate  in the top of a double boiler, over hot but not boiling  water . 
 Gradually stir in the  evaporated milk  and if a thinner sauce is desired, 1/4 cup of  water . 
 Stir over the hot  water  until well blended and smooth
