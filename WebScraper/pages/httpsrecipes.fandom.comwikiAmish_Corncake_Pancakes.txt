      Amish Corncake Pancakes     
 Ingredients : 
 1⅓ cup  buttermilk  at room temperature 
 1 teaspoon  baking powder 
 ¾ cup  yellow cornmeal 
 ¼  flour 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon  sugar 
 1  egg 
 1 tablespoon  corn oil 
 Directions : 
 Whisk together  buttermilk  and  baking soda  let sit for 5 minutes. 
 Beat  egg ,  stir  in  oil , add dry ingredients. 
 Add  buttermilk   mixture . 
 On hot  skillet  drop heaping tbsp of batter. 
 Serve hot with  syrup .
