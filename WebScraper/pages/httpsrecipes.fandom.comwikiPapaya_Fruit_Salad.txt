Ingredients : 
 fresh  papaya , peeled, seeds removed and cut into bite-sized pieces. 
 mint  leaves, chopped and crashed 
 grated coconut 
 honey 
 Directions : 
 In a glass bowl, combine the  papaya  pieces and crashed  mint  leaves. 
 Add some  sugar   water  and stir gently. 
 Cover the fruit salad and allow it to stand for 30 minutes before serving. 
 Top with  grated coconut  before serving. 
 Eat
