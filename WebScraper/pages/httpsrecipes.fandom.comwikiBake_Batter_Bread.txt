Description : 
 Here's an easy bread to make, especially for novice bakers. No kneading is involved for this homey bread slightly coarse textured yeast bread.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2½ cups  flour 
 2 tablespoons  sugar 
 1 package  active dry yeast , 2¼ teaspoons 
 1 cup  milk 
 2 tablespoons  shortening 
 1  egg 
 Directions : 
 Combine  dry ingredients in a large bowl. 
 Heat  milk and shortening together over low heat until warm. The shortening does not need to melt. 
 Add  egg to flour mixture. 
  Stir  in milk mixture. 
  Beat  very hard for 300 strokes or use a heavy-duty mixer. The batter should leave the sides of the bowl. 
 Scrape  batter down from side of bowl and cover. 
 Let  rise in a warm place (80 °F to 85 °F) until double, about 45 minutes. 
  Grease  a 9 x 5-inch loaf pan. 
 Stir  batter down and pour into greased loaf pan. 
 Let  rise in a warm place until doubled in size, about 45 minutes. 
 Preheat  oven to 350 °F. 
 Place  bread into oven and bake about 40 minutes. The bread will sound hollow when tapped when it is finished baking. 
  Remove  bread from pan and let cool on baking rack. 
  Makes  about 18 slices. 
 Things You'll Need : 
 measuring cups and spoons 
 mixing bowls 
 spoon 
 mixer, optional 
 loaf pan 
 cooling rack 
 Sources and Citations : 
 Breads, Cakes and Pies in Family Meals: A Guide for Consumers, USDA, 1979.
