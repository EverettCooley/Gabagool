 Description : 
 This is my version of  avocado  salad. Since my family likes highly seasoned foods I came up with this idea so that guest could have their salad to their own liking.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3  avocados 
 1 Cup  pineapple  cubes 
 1 Cup  grapes , cut in halve 
 2  oranges , peeled and cut into pieces 
 1/3 Cups French dressing 
 lettuce  leaves 
 Fresh  mint  for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Cut  avocados  into halves lengthwise and scoop out pulp and dice.
 Save shells.
 Combine in a bowl  avocado ,  pineapple ,  grapes ,  oranges  and French dressing.
 Marinate for 20 minutes.
 Fill  avocado  shells with mixture and place on place on a plate on  lettuce  leaf. 
 Garnish with  mint  leaves
