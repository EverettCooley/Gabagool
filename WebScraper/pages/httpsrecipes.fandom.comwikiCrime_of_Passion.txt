 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  dark rum 
 1 oz passion-fruit juice 
 2 tbsp  vanilla   ice cream 
 1/3 oz  raspberry  syrup 
 3 oz  cream  soda 
 Directions : 
 Blend briefly. Pour into a wine glass and add soda. Garnish with a slice of  orange  and a  cherry .
