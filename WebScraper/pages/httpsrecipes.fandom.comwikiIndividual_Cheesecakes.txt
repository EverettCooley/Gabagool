Description 
 A speedy cheesecake is great for the single person.
 
 Individual Cheese Pies  from Recipes for Better Nutrition During Cancer Treatment by the National Cancer Institute, public domain government resource—original source of recipe 
 Cook Time : About 30 minutes 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 1 tbsp  ricotta cheese 
 1 tbsp  applesauce  (puréed),  peaches , or drained crushed  pineapple 
 2 tsp  sugar 
 sprinkle of  cinnamon 
 one 3-inch sugar cookie (store-bought) 
 Directions 
 Blend cheese, fruit, sugar, and cinnamon. 
 Spoon over a sugar cookie, turned upside down so the sugar side is on the bottom next to the cookie sheet or foil. 
 Bake at 350°F for 15 minutes (the cookie softens as it absorbs the liquid from the fruit-cheese mixture (for a softer treat, lower the oven to 325°F).
