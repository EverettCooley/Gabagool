Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup sliced fresh  Mushrooms  (about 2 ounces) 
 1 cup  asparagus  spears, cut into 1-inch pieces 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 3 cups cooked  brown rice 
 3/4 cup  walnuts , chopped and toasted 
 1/4 cup sliced  green onions 
 1 tablespoon grated  lemon peel 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1/2 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Saute  Mushrooms ,  asparagus  and  garlic  in  oil  in large skillet over medium-high heat until tender, about 1 to 2 minutes. 
 Add  rice ,  walnuts ,  green onions ,  lemon peel ,  salt  and  pepper ; heat thoroughly.
