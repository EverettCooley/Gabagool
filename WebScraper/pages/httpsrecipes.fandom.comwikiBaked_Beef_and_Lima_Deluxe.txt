 Description : 
 I obtained this recipe from a garage sale in Quinlan, Texas in 1988. Date and source unknown. It is very good. I make it on a very regular basis. Always gobbled right up. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound ground  Beef 
 1 medium white  Onion , chopped 
 15 ounce can  tomato  sauce with  tomato  bits 
 1/2-cup  brown sugar 
 1-1/2 teaspoons  salt 
 Â½ teaspoon freshly ground black  pepper 
 2 large cans green lima beans, drained 
 2 slices  Bacon , cut in fourths 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Cook ground  Beef  and  Onion  in skillet until  Beef  is browned. 
 Meanwhile, combine  tomato  sauce,  brown sugar ,  salt  and  pepper  in casserole. 
 Stir in ground  Beef  and limas. 
 Top with  Bacon  strips. 
 Bake in 350 oven for 90 minutes.
