rice  Almondine
 
 1 Cup Uncooked Long  grain   rice 
 2 1/2 Cup  vegetable broth 
 1 T.  margarine 
 1 T.  salt 
 1/3 Cup Sliced Unblanched  almonds 
 2 T.  margarine 
 salt 
 white pepper 
 
Mix  rice ,  vegetable broth , 1 T.  margarine  And  salt  in a Medium Pan. Bring to Boil. Stir With Fork. Lower Heat. Cover Pan. Simmer 20 Minutes or Until Liquid is Absorbed And  rice  is Tender. While  rice  Cooks, Brown  almonds  in The 2 T.  margarine  in Small Skillet. Stir  almonds  Into  rice  And Season With  salt  And  pepper . Spoon Onto Heated Serving Platter. Serves 6
