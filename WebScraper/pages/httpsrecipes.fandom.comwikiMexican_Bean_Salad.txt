Description : 
 Contributed by Barb in Ocala, Florida via  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can  ranch-style beans 
 ½ head of  lettuce  (shredded) 
 1 lb shredded  longhorn cheese 
 3 oz  French dressing 
 Fritos 
 1  tomato 
 1 small  onion , chopped 
 3 oz Catalina dressing (or more) 
 ½ pkg.  Fritos , crushed 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients except Fritos. 
 Cover and let stand overnight or a few hours in refrigerator or for a few hours. 
 Just before serving, add crushed Fritos and mix.
