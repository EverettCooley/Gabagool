 Description : 
 The chips can be fried together with  eggs  or fresh . 
 
 Ingredients : 
 cassava  (round shaped chips) 
 salt 
 oil 
 
 Directions : 
 Soak the chips overnight, dry them and  salt  to taste.  
Fry until brown.  
Serve with or  eggs .
