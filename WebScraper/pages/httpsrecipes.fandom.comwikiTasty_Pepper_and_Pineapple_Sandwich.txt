Ingredients : 
 2 pieces of yummy whole grain vegan  bread 
 vegan  mustard 
 Cajun seasoning  (Zatarains and Tony's are both good) 
 ¼  green bell pepper 
 ¼  yellow bell pepper 
 ¼  red bell pepper 
 ¼  orange bell pepper 
 drained  pineapple  chunks 
 ½  tomato 
 lettuce 
 Directions : 
 First slice the  bell peppers  into thin strips, vertically. 
 Thinly slice the  tomato  while you're still wielding the knife, and then put it away! you could hurt someone! sprinkle Cajun seasoning on  peppers . 
 Cover vegan bread with  mustard , put down a layer of  bell pepper , making sure that the different colors are evenly distributed. 
 Then put on some  lettuce ,  tomato , and  pineapple  and maybe even some  soy cheese  if you want some extra yumminess. 
 Pineapple  is also  very  good on pizza with  peppers , try it!.
