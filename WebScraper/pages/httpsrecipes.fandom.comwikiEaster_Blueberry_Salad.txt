Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large pkg. black cherry Jell-O 
 2 cups boiling water 
 14½ to 15 oz can can  blueberries 
 15 oz can crushed  pineapple 
 ½ cup chopped  walnuts 
 1 pint pkg.  Dream Whip  (one pkg.) 
 Directions : 
 Mix jell-o with boiling water. 
 Drain blueberries and add juice to Jell-O. 
 Cool. 
 Set aside ¾ cup Jell-O and juice; add blueberries, pineapple and nuts to Jell-O. 
 Refrigerate until well set. 
 Mix dream whip as directed on box, then slowly add the cooled ¾ cup Jell-O and juice to dream whip. 
 Continue beating until lavender in color; top salad. 
 Enjoy.
