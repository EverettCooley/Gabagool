Ingredients : 
 
 4  Chicken  breasts 
 1  green pepper , chopped 
 16 oz.  tomato  sauce 
 1 tsp  cumin 
 1/2 tsp  salt 
 1/2 tsp  pepper 
 1 dash  cayenne pepper 
 Directions : 
 Spray crockpot with a light  cooking spray . 
 Arrange  Chicken  breasts in the bottom of the pot. 
 Mix the rest of the ingredients in a bowl and pour over the  Chicken . Try to coat all of the  Chicken  with the mixture. 
 Cover and cook on low for 6â€“8 hours. 
 Serve on a bed of  white rice .
