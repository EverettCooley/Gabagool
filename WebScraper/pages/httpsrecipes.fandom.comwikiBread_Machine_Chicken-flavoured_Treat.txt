 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  chicken stock 
 1 cup bread or  all-purpose flour 
 2 cup  whole wheat flour 
 ¼ cup  non-fat dry milk  powder 
 1½ teaspoon  yeast 
 Directions : 
 Use dough cycle. 
 Roll dough to ½" thickness. 
 Cut with cookie cutters or a pizza cutter. 
 Place on a greased baking sheet. 
 Cover with plastic wrap and let rise in warm place about one hour. 
 Bake at 325°F for one hour. 
 When all are baked, turn off oven and leave overnight. 
 Store in airtight container.
