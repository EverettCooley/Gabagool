Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Love Estate in Sachse, Texas in 1984.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4 ounce package  strawberry gelatin 
 ¾ cup boiling water 
 ½ cup cold water 
 ice cubes 
 1½ cups diced fresh  fruit 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve gelatin in boiling water. 
 Combine cold water and ice cubes to make 1 cup then add to gelatin stirring until thickened. 
 Add fruit and chill for 10 minutes. 
 Place foil baking cups in muffin pans or use small individual molds. 
 Spoon gelatin mixture into cups or molds filling each about ⅔ full. 
 Chill until firm about 2 hours. 
 Peel away foil cups carefully or dip molds in warm water for 5 seconds to unmold.
