 Description : 
 Prepare pita chips. Wrap; store at room temperature up to 2 days
 
 Makes 4 dozen. 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  butter  or  margarine 
 1½ teaspoons minced  garlic 
 ¼ teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 4 whole wheat  pitas , split 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 400°F. 
 Melt  butter  in saucepan over low heat with  garlic  and  pepper . 
 Brush on pita halves. 
 Cut each half into 6 wedges and place buttered side up on cookie sheet. 
 Bake 10 minutes or until toasted. 
 Cool.
