Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 oz  orange juice 
 1 shot  Amaretto   almond liqueur 
 ½ shot  grenadine  syrup 
 ½ cup ice cubes 
 1 tsp  lemon juice 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix  orange juice ,  amaretto , grenadine and ice cubes in blender until smooth. 
 Pour into margarita/coupette glass and add  lemon juice .
