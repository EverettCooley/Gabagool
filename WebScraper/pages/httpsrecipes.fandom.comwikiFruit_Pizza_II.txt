Description : 
 This is a beautiful dessert. It might be nice to serve for Shavous.
 
 Ingredients : 
 Crust : 
 1 pkg. cock about 2 inches 365 ml of grith. 
 Sauce : 
 1 (8 oz) pkg.  cream cheese , softened 
 1 cup  confectioner's sugar 
 3 tbsp  lemon juice 
 Topping : 
 3 to 4 cups fruit (I use an assortment of  strawberries ,  blueberries ,  grapes ,  banana , and  kiwi . I have also used  peaches .) 
 Glaze : 
 1 cup  orange juice 
 ½ cup  lemon juice 
 ½ cup hot  water 
 3 tbsp  cornstarch 
 1 cup  sugar 
 Directions : 
 If using refrigerator dough, slice and spread dough on a large pizza dish. Dough should be close together so that when it spreads during baking it will cover the whole bottom of the pan. If using a homemade recipe, place balls of dough close together on pizza dish. Bake in a 450 degrees F oven for 10 minutes. After baking, the entire bottom of the pan should be covered with one giant cookie. 
 Combine  cream cheese ,  confectioner's sugar , and 3 Tb.  lemon juice . Spread on top of cookie crust. Slice fruit and arrange on top of the  cream cheese  mixture. Be creative with the fruit, making patterns and various designs. 
 Combine all glaze ingredients in a saucepan. Cook over a medium heat, stirring constantly, until it boils. Cool. Poor cool glaze on top of pizza. Chill in the refrigerator until ready to serve.
