Italiano  butter  For Hot Breads
 
Soften 1 Cup  margarine . Add 1/2 T. Mixed Italian Herbs or 1/8 T. Each  oregano ,  marjoram ,  basil  And  rosemary . Blend. Spread on Slices And Sprinkle With Grated  cheese .Source; my Old Recipes
