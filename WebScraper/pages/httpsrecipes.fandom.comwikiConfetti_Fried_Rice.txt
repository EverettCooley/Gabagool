Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 1 3/4 cups chopped fresh  Mushrooms 
 3/4 cup thinly sliced  carrots 
 3/4 cup sliced  green onions 
 3 cups cooked  rice , chilled 
 1 cup 3 ounces cooked  Turkey  breast, sliced into strips 
 2 tablespoons low sodium soy sauce 
 1 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in large skillet over medium-high heat until hot. 
 Add  Mushrooms ,  carrots  and  onions . 
 Saute until  Mushrooms  are lightly browned. 
 Stir in  rice ,  Turkey , soy sauce,  garlic powder  and  pepper . 
 Cook until thoroughly heated, about 5 minutes
