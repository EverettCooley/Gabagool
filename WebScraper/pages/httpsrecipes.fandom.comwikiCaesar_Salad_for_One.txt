      Ceasar Salad     
 Description : 
 This recipe is for 1 serving.
 
 Ingredients : 
 80 g  iceberg lettuce 
 80 g  romaine lettuce 
 80 g basic  Caesar dressing 
 30 g  Parmesan cheese , grated 
 Parmesan , shaved 
 10 g crispy  bacon bits 
 20 g crispy bread  croutons 
 Directions : 
 Mix the salad with the dressing. 
 Garnish with the croutons and shaved Parmesan. 
 NOTE: The salad can be served with various accompaniment:  smoked salmon , grilled  chicken  supreme,  Parma ham , grilled  prawns  or  scallops . 
 Various garnishes can be used: Roasted  pecan  nuts,  focaccia  bread croutons,  sun-dried tomato  bread croutons.
