Description 
 Balaklava Nectar  from  How to Mix Drinks  by Jerry Thomas—original source of recipe, out of copyright
 
 Serves:  15 
 Ingredients 
 rind of ½ a  lemon 
 2 tablespoons crushed  sugar 
 ½ a small  cucumber , thinly sliced, peel on 
 2 bottles of  soda water 
 2 bottles of  claret 
 1 bottle  champagne 
 
 
 Directions 
 Thinly peel the rind of half a lemon, shred it fine and put it in a punch-bowl. 
 Add the sugar, lemon juice and cucumber. 
 Toss up the mixture several times and then add the soda water, claret and champagne. 
 Stir together and serve.
