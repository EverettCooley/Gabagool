Ingredients : 
 2 shots of  apple juice 
 1 shot of  vodka 
 whipped cream 
 cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Combine  apple juice  and  vodka  in a strainer filled with  ice . 
 Shake and pour into a lowball glass. 
 Top off with whip  cream  and sprinkle  cinnamon  on top.
