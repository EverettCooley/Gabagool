 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients : 
 1.5 oz (45 ml)  light rum 
 0.5 oz (15 ml)  Grand Marnier 
 0.5 oz (15 ml)  lime juice 
 1 tsp (5 ml)  orgeat syrup 
 Directions : 
 The ingredients are mixed together with cracked ice in a shaker or blender and strained into a chilled cocktail glass.
