      Chili Rice Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 1 pkt Uncle Ben's  fast cooking Spanish rice 
 2 cup light  red kidney beans , well drained 
 2 cup  green pepper , chopped 
 1 cup  onion , finely shopped 
 1 cup  salad oil 
 3 tbsp  vinegar 
 1 tbsp  chili powder 
 Â½ tsp  hot pepper sauce , ( tabasco ) 
 Directions : 
 Prepare  rice  according to the instructions on the package, omitting the  margarine . 
 Cool slightly. 
 Add  beans ,  green peppers  and  onions . 
 Combine  salad oil ,  vinegar ,  chili powder , and hot pepper sauce . 
 Stir into the  rice  mixture. 
 Chill well before serving.
