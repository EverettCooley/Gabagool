Ingredients : 
 1 large bunch  broccoli 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 apple cider vinegar  to taste 
 1 tbsp  water  or sugar-free lemon-line soda 
 sweetener  if desired 
 Directions : 
 Clean and trim boroccoli. 
 Cut stalks and floweretes into about 3 inch "trees" 
 Simmer gently about 12 minutes covered with  water  (I prefer to steam). 
 Drain. 
 Mix  lemon juice , liquid, and sweetener. 
 Trickle over  broccoli . 
 Heat the  lemon  mixture to keep the vegetable warm.
