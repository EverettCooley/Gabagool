 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 30 gombos 
 2 boots of sheets of Keleng keleng 
 100 g of crushed groundnuts 
 2  onions 
 red  palm oil 
 salt ,  pepper  and  chilli  to taste 
 Directions : 
 First stage: 
 
 Finely cut the gombos. 
 Chop the  onions . 
 clean the sheets of keleng keleng. 
 Second stage: 
 
 saut√©  onions  in red  palm oil . 
 add the paste of crushed groundnuts and  water . 
 Mix regularly to prevent stickiness 
 Add the  gombo  and the sheets. 
 Season with  salt ,  pepper  and  chilli  to taste 
 Let simmer for 20 minutes. 
 Third stage:
 
 Bring slightly salted  water  to the boil 
 Add the  cornstarch  and mix vigorously to obtain desired consistency. 
 Alternatives: Add a few pieces of fish or dried  Beef .
