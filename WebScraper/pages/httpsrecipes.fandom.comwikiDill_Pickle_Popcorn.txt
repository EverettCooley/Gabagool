Ingredients : 
 1 tbsp  sea salt 
 1 1/2 tsp dried  dill 
 1/2 tsp  coriander 
 3/4 tsp  garlic powder 
 1/2 tsp light  mustard seed 
 1/2 tsp  onion powder 
 1 tsp  dill seed 
 1/2 tsp  citric acid 
 popcorn 
 
 
 Instructions : 
 Mix all spices together in a bowl, or put all spices into a spice grinder and pulse to a powder. 
 Sprinkle over popcorn sprayed lightly with cooking spray or tossed with butter. If using microwave popcorn, just open bag, close, and shake. 
 Enjoy!
