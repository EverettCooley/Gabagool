Ingredients : 
 1 tbsp  butter 
 dash of  nutmeg 
 green, medium-sized  papaya 
 boiling salted  water 
 Directions : 
 Peel and slice one very green, medium sized  papaya . 
 There should be about two cups of fruit. 
 Drop it into rapidly boiling salted  water . 
 Simmer for about one-half hour, or until fruit is tender. 
 Drain well. An excellent side dish to stews. 
 Add: 1 Tbsp  butter , dash of  nutmeg .
