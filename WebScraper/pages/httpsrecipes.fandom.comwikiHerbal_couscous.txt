 Description : 
 Always check the ingredients to make sure the product is vegan.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 Cup(s)  couscous 
 1 1/2 Cup(s)  water 
 1 scallions 
 1 Dash(s)  olive oil 
 1 Pinch(s)  black pepper 
 1 Pinch(s)  salt 
 1 Tablespoon(s)  basil 
 3 Tablespoon(s) chopped  parsley 
 4 Tablespoon(s) soy sauce 
 1 Pinch(s)  thyme 
 1 Cup(s)  pine nuts 
 1 green  bell pepper 
 Directions : 
 Roast  couscous  in a skillet in  olive oil  over medium heat.
 
 Boil  water  and add to  couscous .
 Gently stir once.
 Set aside for 5 minutes.
 
 Sautee scallions and  bell pepper  in  olive oil .
 Add  salt  and  pepper  to taste.
 Mix in  basil , parsely, soy sauce, and  thyme .
 
 Fluff  couscous  with fork and add the finely chopped scallions/  bell pepper  mixture.
 
 Serve with  pine nuts  sprinkled over the top.
