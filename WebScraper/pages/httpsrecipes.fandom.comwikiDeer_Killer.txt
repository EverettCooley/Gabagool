Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Makes enough for at least 10 people. 
 Ingredients : 
 2 shots  Absolut® vodka 
 1 pint  Firewater ®  cinnamon schnapps 
 4 tbsp  Tabasco ® sauce 
 1 splash  butterscotch schnapps 
 10 oz  Wild Turkey ®  Bourbon whiskey 
 6 oz  Jack Daniel's ® Tennessee  whiskey 
 30 shots  Jagermeister ®  herbal liqueur 
 Directions : 
 Mix all drinks in a blender, You can add 8 cubes of  ice  to chill the drink and give it more of a body. 
 Add the hot melting  butter  (or the schnapps) right before you drink.
