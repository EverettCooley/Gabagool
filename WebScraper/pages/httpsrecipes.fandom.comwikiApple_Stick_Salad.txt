 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup  white sugar 
 1/2 cup  distilled vinegar 
 3 tart  Apples , cored and julienned 
 2/3 cup chopped  Onion 
 1/2 cup chopped  dill  pickle 
 Directions : 
 In a small bowl, combine  Sugar  and  vinegar . 
 In a medium bowl, mix together  Apples ,  Onion , and pickle. 
 Toss with  vinegar  mixture. 
 Refrigerate until thoroughly chilled.
