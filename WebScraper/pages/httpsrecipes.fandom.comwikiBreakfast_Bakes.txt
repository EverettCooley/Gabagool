 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 cups  flour 
 1 teasp.  baking powder 
 1/2 teasp.  salt 
 2 teasps.  Sugar 
 1 tablesp.  butter 
 1/4 cup  water 
 oil  for deep-frying 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix all above ingredients and form small balls and flatten 
 Heat up  oil  for deep-frying and fry bakes, a few at a time, until light brown
