 We all want to save. : 
 Tasty is not synonymous with expensive. Here is a list of delicious recipes that won't diminish your bank account.
 Top 3 cost effective foods: 
 
 
 Rice , oh the possibilities! Fry it, steamed it or turn it into a wonderful pudding for dessert! 
 Canned tomato sauce , dress it up and pour on top of a juicy chicken breast or better yet use it as a dip for cheesy bread sticks. 
 Vegetables , shop seasonally for the best deals and explore the plethora of ways to serve as a healthy side dish.
