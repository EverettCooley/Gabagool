Description : 
 Boiled meat
 
 Ingredients : 
 500 g  mutton  or  beef 
 500 g (18 oz)  turnips  or  carrots 
 1  chili pepper 
 15  black peppercorns 
 a few springs  dill 
 2  bay leaves 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Cut meat into 150 – 200g (6 – 7 oz) pieces and put in a large pot of boiling  water . 
 Add  turnips ,  salt , peppercorns,  bay leaves ,  dill ,  chili pepper  and cook at a low boil for 1 to 1½ hours. 
 Remove meat and vegetables, cut into slices and serve.
