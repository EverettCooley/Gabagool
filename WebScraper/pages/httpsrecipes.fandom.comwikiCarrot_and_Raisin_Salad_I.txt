Ingredients : 
 3 cup  carrot , shredded 
 1/2 cup low-calorie dressing 
 1/3 cup seedless  raisins  (1 ½-oz box) 
 1/4 tsp  salt 
 Sugar  substitute equivalent to 6 ts  Sugar 
 lettuce  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients, except  lettuce , thoroughly. 
 Cover bowl. 
 Chill 2 hours or longer before serving. 
 Serve on crisp  lettuce . 
 Nutritional information : 
 food exchange per serving: 2 vegetable exchanges; calories: 47; cho: 9g; pro: 1g: sod: 153 mg; cho: 10 mg; 
 
 low-sodium diets: omit  salt  and prepare the low-calorie cooked dressing without  salt .
