Frozen  Peanut butter  Cups
 
 1 8oz Tub ff  Cool Whip 
 6 t  Peanut butter  (peter Pan Whipped) 
 2 t  chocolate  Syrup 
 
Thoroughly Combine  Cool Whip ,  Peanut butter  And  chocolate  Syrup. Spoon Into Mini Muffin Tins. Freeze.
 
Makes 24 Mini-Muffins
 
 1 Point Per Muffin 
 
 
Makes 12 Muffins
 
 2 Points Each 
 
 
Makes 6 Muffins
 
 4 Points Each 
 
 
Note: The Points For This Recipe Are Figured Using Peter Pan Whipped  Peanut butter . it Has Less Calories Because It's Whipped. You Need to Refigure The Points if Using Another  Peanut butter .
