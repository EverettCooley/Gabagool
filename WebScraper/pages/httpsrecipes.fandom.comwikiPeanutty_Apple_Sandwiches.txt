      Peanutty Apple Sandwiches     
 Contents 
 
 1   Ingredients 
 2   Directions 
 3   Other Links 
 4   See also 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3/4 cup crunchy  Peanut   butter 
 1/4 cup  apricot  preserves 
 1/2 teaspoon ground  mustard 
 8 slices  raisin bread 
 2 medium unpeeled or peeled eating  Apples , thinly sliced 
 lettuce  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Stir together  Peanut   butter , preserves and  mustard ; spread on 1 side of 4 slices bread. 
Place  Apple  slices on  Peanut   butter  mixture; top with  lettuce  leaves and remaining 4 slices bread.  
Cut sandwiches in half or fourths if desired.
 
 Makes 4 sandwiches
