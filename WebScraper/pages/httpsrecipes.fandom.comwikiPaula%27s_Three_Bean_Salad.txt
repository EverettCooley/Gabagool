Description : 
 Here is an easy salad recipe you can prepare for your family or take to your next family get-together.
 
 Contributed by Paula in California at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can  green beans 
 1 can  kidney beans 
 1 can  wax beans 
 ¾ cup  sugar 
 ½ cup  vinegar 
 ½ cup  vegetable oil 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Drain beans. 
 Heat remaining ingredients in a saucepan until heated through. 
 Pour over beans and refrigerate before serving.
