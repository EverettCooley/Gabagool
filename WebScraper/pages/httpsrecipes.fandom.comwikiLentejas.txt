 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups of  Lentils 
 1/2 cup of chopped  celery 
 1 small  red onion  cut into four quarters 
 1 teaspoon of chopped coriander/ cilantro 
 2 large  potatoes , cut into small cubes 
 1 tablespoon of  oil 
 1/2Â lb of gem  Squash  (auyama), cut into small cubes 
 1/2 teaspoon of mashed  garlic 
 1 pinch of  oregano 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 In a pot heat the  oil , add  oregano ,  Onion ,  garlic ,  celery  and herbs. Stir. 
 Add the  potatoes  and gem  Squash . 
 Stir to incorporate all the ingredients. 
 Sautee at medium heat for 2 minutes. 
 Add the  Lentils  (without the  water ) and stir until the  Lentils  are very tender. 
 Add 2 cups of  water  and simmer at low heat until it reaches a creamy consistency. Adjust  salt  to taste.
