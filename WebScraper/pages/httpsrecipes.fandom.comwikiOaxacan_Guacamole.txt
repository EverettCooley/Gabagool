Ingredients : 
 2 very ripe avocados 
 1/4 cup coarsely chopped onion 
 1 clove garlic, minced 
 1 to 3 serrano or jalapeno chiles, coarsely chopped 
 1/4 cup fresh lime juice (or more, to taste) 
 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro 
 1/2 teaspoon salt (or more, to taste) 
 Directions : 
 Peel, pit and coarsely chop the avocados. Combine the onion, garlic and chiles in a blender or food processor and process until finely chopped. Add the avocados and process into a puree. Add lime juice, cilantro and salt and process just to blend. Taste for seasoning, adding lime juice or salt as needed.
