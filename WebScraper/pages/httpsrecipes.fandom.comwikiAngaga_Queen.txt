 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cl  tequila 
 4 cl Maraschino 
 Grenadine  Syrup 
 sugar syrup 
 lime  and  orange juice 
 Directions : 
 Give  tequila , Maraschino,  lime juice  and  sugar syrup  in a glass with pushed  ice , fill the  orange juice  on and give a couple of splashes  Grenadine  syrup and stirring easily. 
 Decorating with a cocktail  cherry .
