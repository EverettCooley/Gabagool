Description : 
 Makes 4 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  top round  or  sirloin steak , sliced into ⅛-inch thick slices 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 ½ cup chopped  green onions 
 1 x 4-to 6¼-ounce package  long grain  and  wild rice  mix, prepared according to package directions 
 1 x 7-ounce can whole kernel  corn , drained 
 1 x 16-ounce can stewed  tomatoes , undrained 
 Directions : 
 Brown  beef  in  oil  in large skillet over medium-high heat. 
 Add  onions ; cook 1 minute longer. 
 Add  rice ,  corn  and  tomatoes . 
 Cook until thoroughly heated, about 2 to 3 minutes.
