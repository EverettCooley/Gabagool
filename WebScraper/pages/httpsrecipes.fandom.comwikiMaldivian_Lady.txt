 Ingredients : 
 3 cl Bacardi ( white rum ) 
 2 cl  apricot brandy 
 a little bit  Grenadine  syrup 
 7 cl  orange juice 
 7 cl  pineapple juice 
 Directions : 
 Mix the Bacardy with  apricot brandy , shaken it, give pushed  ice  in the glass, adding the alcohol and then admitting a couple of splashes  Grenadine  syrup. 
 At last filling up carefully with the  orange  and  pineapple juice . 
 Decorating with a small piece of  pineapple  and a green cocktail  cherry .
