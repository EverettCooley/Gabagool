Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Gibson Estate in Quinlan, Texas in 1981.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 seedless  cucumber  peeled seeded and chopped fine 
 8 ounces soft mild  goat cheese  at room temperature 
 1½ teaspoons freshly grated  lemon zest 
 1½ teaspoons fresh  lemon juice 
 2 teaspoons finely chopped fresh  dill 
 2 tablespoons finely chopped  red onion 
 ½ teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 In a food processor puree ½ cup cucumber with goat cheese, zest and lemon juice until almost smooth. 
 In a bowl stir together cucumber mixture, remaining cucumber, dill, 1-tablespoon onion and pepper. 
 Sprinkle spread with remaining tablespoon onion. 
 Serve spread with toasts or crackers.
