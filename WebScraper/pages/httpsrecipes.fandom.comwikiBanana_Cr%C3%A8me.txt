 Ingredients : 
 3 to 4 ripe  bananas 
 2  eggs 
 20 g  sugar 
 2 dl  condensed milk 
 lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Peel  bananas  and mash with a fork. 
 Stir together  egg yolk  and  sugar . 
 Stir  milk  till it gets thick. 
 Mix all together. 
 Cook it For 20 mins 
 Add lemon juice
