 Ingredients : 
 1 dozen hard boiled  eggs  with shells removed 
 ⅛ cup Sue Bee  honey 
 ⅓ cup  salad dressing 
 1 tsp  onion powder 
 ½ tsp  garlic powder ,  salt  and  pepper 
 1  jalapeno pepper , seeded and diced 
 chili powder 
 Directions : 
 Blend  egg  yolks, honey and dressing until smooth. 
 Mix in seasonings and jalapeno, except for  chili powder . 
 Put spoonful of mixture in each  egg  white center and sprinkle with  chili powder .
