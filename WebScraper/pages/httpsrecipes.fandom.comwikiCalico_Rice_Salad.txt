Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 cup thinly sliced  celery 
 1 medium  tomato , diced 
 ½ cup diced  green pepper 
 ½ cup finely chopped  onion 
 ½ cup (2 ounces) diced sharp  cheddar  cheese 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise  or  salad dressing 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon seasoned  pepper 
 lettuce  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  celery ,  tomato ,  green pepper ,  onion  and  cheese  in large bowl. 
 Chill. 
 When ready to serve, add  mayonnaise ,  salt  and seasoned  pepper ; toss lightly. 
 Serve on  lettuce  leaves. 
 Or, for a festive touch, pack salad firmly into 6-cup ring mold. 
 Unmold on  lettuce  leaves.
