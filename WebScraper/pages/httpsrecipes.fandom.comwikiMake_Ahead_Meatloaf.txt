Make Ahead Meatloaf
 
 1 1/2 Lbs. Ground  Beef 
 4 1/2 T. A-1  Steak  Sauce 
 1 Cup Soft Whole  Wheat   bread  Crumbs (2 Slices 
 1 Medium  Onion , Finely Chopped 
 1/4 Cup Chopped  parsley 
 1  egg 
 1/4 Cup  milk 
 parsley  Noodles (recipe Follows)
 zucchini  Saut? (recipe Follows)
 
Mix  Beef  Lightly With A-1  Steak  Sauce,  bread  Crumbs,  Onion ,  parsley ,  egg  And  milk  in Large Bowl. Pack Mixture Into a 5 Cup Ring Mold. Invert Onto a Jelly Roll Pan. Bake at 375 For 50 Minutes or Until Golden Brown. Cool on Pan.. Put on an Oven to Table Platter.Cover With Foil. Chill. Reheat, Covered in 375 Oven For 30 Minutes or Until Heated Through. Spoon  parsley  Noodles Around Ring And Fill Center With  zucchini  Sauce.
 parsley  Noodles:
 Cook 1 Pkg. Noodles (1 Lb.) Following Directions on Label, in Large Kettle. Drain. Add 1/4 Cup Chopped  parsley  And 2 T. Softened  margarine ; Toss Until Coated. zucchini  Saut?: Heat 2 T.  oil  in Skillet. Saut? 3 Large  zucchini , Sliced For 2 Minutes. Add 1/4 Cup  water , 1 T. Mixed Italian Herbs, Crumbled, 1/2 T.  salt , And 1/4 T.  pepper . Cover And Steam For 10 Minutes or Until Crisply Tender.Serves 6
