Description : 
 This refreshing, cold,  yogurt  and  cucumber  salad is served with spicy dishes like  Kebab , as well as everyday. In Turkish cuisine it is primarily served in the summer months.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 Large  cucumber 
 1 clove of  garlic 
 3 cups of  yogurt 
 3 tbs olive  oil 
 1/2 cup  water 
 2 tbs fresh  dill  or 1 tbs dried  dill  flakes 
 2 tbs fresh  mint  or 1 tbs dried  mint  flakes 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Chop cucumber in small cubes. 
 In a separate bowl mix yogurt, garlic, water, dill, mint, oil, and salt. 
 Stir until mixture is consistency of thick soup. 
 Add cucumbers and mix. 
 Cover and place the bowl in refrigerator. 
 Serve very cold in small individual bowls.
