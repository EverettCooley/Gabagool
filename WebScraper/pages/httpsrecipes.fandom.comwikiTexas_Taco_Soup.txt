Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds  ground beef 
 1 envelope  taco seasoning 
 1 envelope  ranch dip 
 1 can whole kernel  corn 
 1 can   ranch-style beans 
 1 can stewed  tomato 
 1 medium  red onion 
 1 can  pinto beans 
 1 can  tomatoes  with  green chilies 
 Directions : 
 Brown ground beef and drain well. 
 Combine all ingredients and simmer for 30 minute over medium heat. 
 Serve with chips and shredded cheese.
