Santa fe Stew
 
 
 1 1/2 Lbs. Ground  Beef 
 1 Pkg.  taco seasoning 
 1 Pkg. Ranch-Style Dry Dressing Mix 
 15 Oz. Can Whole Kernel  corn  With Liquid 
 15 Oz. Can  kidney beans  With Liquid 
 2 15-Oz. Cans Stewed  tomatoes  With Liquid 
 2 15-Oz. Cans  pinto beans  With Liquid 
 4 Oz. Can Diced  tomatoes  And Green Chilies 
 
 Cook  Beef  Until Brown. Drain Well if Necessary.
 
Add Both Packages of Seasonings And Mix Well. Add  corn ,  kidney beans , Stewed  tomatoes ,  pinto beans , And  tomatoes  And Green Chilies; Mix Well. Simmer 25 Minutes.
 
if You Want it Really Hot, Use 2 Cans  tomatoes  And Green Chilies.
