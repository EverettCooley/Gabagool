 Description : 
 Bread pudding
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup of softened  butter 
 4 cups of bread cut in small cubes (better if  French bread ) 
 8 cups of  evaporated milk 
 2  eggs 
 1 teaspoon of  baking powder 
 ½ teaspoon of  salt 
 ¼ cup of  raisins 
 4  cinnamon  sticks 
 10  cloves 
 Caramel : 
 1 cup of  water 
 1 cup of  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Stir the  eggs  until the  yolk  and the white are well mixed. 
 Reserve. 
 Break the  cinnamon  sticks into 1-inch pieces and put in the  milk . 
 Put the  milk  to boil at low heat, turn off the heat when it is about to break a boil. 
 Mix all the ingredients and pour in the baking pan. 
 Bake for 45 minutes. 
 In the meantime make a light caramel mixing the  sugar  and  water  and putting on the fire at low heat until half the  water  evaporates. 
 Reserve the caramel. 
 Pinch the cake with a clean knife. 
 If the knife comes out clean, take out of the oven. 
 Let cool before taking out of the pan. 
 Serve the cake covered with the caramel. 
 Use as dessert or to accompany hot  cocoa  or  coffee .
