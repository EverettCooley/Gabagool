 Ingredients :

 12 hamburger buns

 12 large  tomato  slices 
 12  cheese  slices 
 2 cups  Simple Guacamole  (recipe follows) 
 2 cups  Hass Avocado Salsa  (recipe follows)

 4  California avocados , mashed
