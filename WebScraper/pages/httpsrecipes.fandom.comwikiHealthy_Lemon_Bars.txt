Description : 
 Source:  Best-tasting Healthy All-time Favorites 
 
 preparation time: 1:00 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  flour 
 ½ cup  butter  or  margarine 
 ¼ cup  brown sugar 
 3  eggs , well beaten 
 2 cup  brown sugar 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 1 cup  coconut , finely shredded 
 ½ cup  raisins 
 1 cup  walnuts , optional 
 2 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 tsp grated  lemon rind 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 350°F. 
 Mix  flour ,  butter  and ¼ cup  Sugar  thoroughly. 
 Press firmly into bottom of lightly greased oblong pan 13"x 9½". 
 Bake 10 minutes. 
 Mix  eggs , 2 cups  Sugar  and  salt  together. 
 Stir in rest of ingredients, spread over the partially baked bottom layer. 
 Bake about 25 minutes. Top will be lightly brown.
