Ingredients : 
 1  cabbage  head 
 1/2 small  onion 
 2 Tbl. white  vinegar 
 5 Tbl. vegetable oil 
 1 cup Cocoa Puffs cereal (generic is OK!) 
 1 cup Honey Smax cereal (generic is OK!) 
 salt  and  pepper , to taste 
 Directions : 
 Chop up the cabbage into slaw. Crush the cereal so it's kind of like junk food granola. Mix it all together, with the oil and stuff. Refrigerate. 
 Good for about three days.
