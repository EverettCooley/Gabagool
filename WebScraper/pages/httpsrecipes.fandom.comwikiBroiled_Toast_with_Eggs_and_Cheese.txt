 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1  egg , 
 100 grams Bulgarian  white cheese , 
 2 slices bread 
 Directions : 
 Beat the  egg  and then put the crushed  white cheese  and mix it well. 
 Pour the bread slices with the mixture and broil above only.
