Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. It was obtained when the family collection was purchased from the Burnett Estate in Terrell, Texas in 1981.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 Cups pancake mix 
 3 Cups  cream of wheat 
 4 Cups  milk 
 6  eggs 
 12 Teaspoons  vegetable oil 
 Vegetable  cooking spray 
 Directions : 
 Spray griddle with  cooking spray . 
 In a bowl, combine pancake mix and cereal. 
 Blend  milk ,  egg  and  vegetable oil  then stir into cereal mixture. 
 Pour desired amount of batter onto hot griddle for each pancake. 
 Cook until bubbly then turn and cook until golden brown. 
 Repeat with remaining batter.
