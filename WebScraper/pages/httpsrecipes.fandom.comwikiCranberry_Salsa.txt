This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Mclean Estate in Irving, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 quart fresh  cranberries 
 juice of 1  lemon 
 1 large white  onion  chopped 
 10  jalapeno peppers 
 5 cloves  garlic  minced 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Combine  cranberries ,  lemon juice ,  onion ,  peppers  and  garlic  and cook until  cranberries  are tender and falling apart then place in food processor and pulse with short bursts until mixture is coarsely pur√©ed. 
 Season with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Let cool and refrigerate at least four hours before serving.
