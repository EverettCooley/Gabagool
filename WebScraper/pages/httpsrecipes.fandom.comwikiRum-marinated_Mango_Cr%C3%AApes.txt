 Ingredients : 
 Crêpes : 
 ½ cup  flour 
 ¼ cup  milk 
 2 large  eggs 
 1½ tbsp melted  butter 
 ½ tsp  sugar 
 Filling : 
 2 ripe  mangos , peeled and cut in ¾" pieces 
 2 tbsp fresh  lemon juice 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 ⅓ cup  dark rum 
 1 tbsp melted  butter 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl toss together the  mangos ,  lemon juice ,  sugar  and  rum . 
 Set aside. 
 Blend all ingredients for crêpes until smooth. 
 Cover with plastic wrap and let stand 1 hour. 
 Make 12 crêpes in a 6 – 7 inch crêpe pan. 
 To serve: fold crêpes in quarters and put on a baking sheet. 
 Brush tops with the melted  butter  and bake in 400' preheated oven for 10 minutes or until browned and slightly crispy. 
 Stuff each crêpe with about ¼ cup  mango  mixture, transfer to a platter and dust with  powdered sugar .
