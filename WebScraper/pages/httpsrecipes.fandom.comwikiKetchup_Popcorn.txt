Ingredients : 
 3 tbsp homemade  ketchup 
 2 tbsp  butter 
 1 tbsp  granulated sugar 
 8 cups plain  popcorn 
 salt 
 Instructions : 
 Mix homemade ketchup with butter and granulated sugar in a small saucepan over medium heat 
 Whisk until sugar dissolves. 
 Drizzle over 8 cups popped plain popcorn on a baking sheet. 
 Toss until coated. 
 Sprinkle with salt. 
 Bake at 300F, stirring occasionally, until popcorn is dry and crisp, 12 to 15 min.
