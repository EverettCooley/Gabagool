Apple  Cake Dessert
 
 1 C.  sugar 
 1/4 Tsp.  salt 
 1 Tsp.  baking soda 
 1 C.  flour 
 1/2 C.  whole wheat flour 
 1/2 C.  shortening 
 2  eggs 
 1/2 C.  milk 
 1 1/4 Tsp.  vanilla 
 4 C.  Apples , Sliced 
 
Topping:
 
 3 Tbsp. Melted  butter 
 3/4 C.  brown sugar 
 3 Tbsp.  flour 
 1/4 Tsp.  cinnamon 
 3/4 C.  walnuts , Chopped 
 
Cream  shortening ,  sugar ,  eggs ,  milk , And  vanilla 
 Together. Sift Together Remaining Dry Ingredients; AddTo Cream Mixture And Mix Well. Fold in 4 Cups SlicedApples And Pour Into 9x13 Inch Greased Pan. Smooth
 Evenly And Spread With Topping. Bake at 350 DegreesFor 45-50 Minutes. Top With  whipped cream ; Great
 Served Warm!
 
Enjoy.
