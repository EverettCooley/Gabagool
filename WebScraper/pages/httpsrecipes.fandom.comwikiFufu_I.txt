Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  white yams 
 water  to cover 
 2 tbsp  butter 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Place the unpeeled  yams  in a large pot. 
 Cover with cold  water  and bring to a boil. 
 Let boil for 15 â€“ 30 minutes till  yams  are cooked through. 
 Drain  yams , cool somewhat then peel. 
 Place into a large bowl with the  butter ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Mash with a potato masher until very smooth. 
 Place into a serving bowl. 
 Wet your hands with  water , form into a ball and serve.
