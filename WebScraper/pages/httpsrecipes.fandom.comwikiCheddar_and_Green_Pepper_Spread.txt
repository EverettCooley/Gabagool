Description : 
 Contributed by Jenn B aka Mom2Sam and Tiny at  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less. 
 Accompaniment: Saltine crackers 
 Makes about 2 cups 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ pound extra-sharp yellow  cheddar , grated coarse 
 ¼ pound extra-sharp white  cheddar , grated coarse 
 1  green bell pepper , cut into very thin 1-inch-long strips 
 ½ cup thinly sliced  scallions 
 ½ cup bottled  mayonnaise 
 1½ teaspoons  Dijon-style mustard 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl toss together the cheddars, the  bell pepper , and the  scallions 
 Add the  mayonnaise , the  mustard , and  black pepper  to taste, and combine the mixture well. 
 The spread keeps, covered and chilled, for 1 week. 
 Serve the spread with crackers.
