Ingredients : 
 1 pack Alba or Weight Watchers  chocolate  drink 
 2 tbsp  coffee 
 1 tbsp  peanut butter 
 2 tbsp  raisins 
 2 tbsp  oatmeal 
 Directions : 
 Mix all together; place on plastic wrap. 
 Shape into a log. 
 Freeze. 
 Cut in slices. 
 Nutritional information : 
 WW exchanges: 1  milk , 1 fruit, 1 bread, 1 protein, 1 fat
