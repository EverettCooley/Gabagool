 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 8 oz  ziti  (or any other fancy  tubular pasta ) 
 1 jar (about 6-8 oz.) roasted  red peppers 
 2  Italian sausage  links, about 4 oz 
 2  garlic  cloves, finely chopped 
 2 tsp fresh  thyme   or  ½ tsp dried  thyme 
 1 large  tomato , peeled, seeded and finely chopped 
 1 tbsp  red wine vinegar 
 ⅛ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Squeeze sausages out of casings, break meat into small pieces. 
 Sauté over medium high heat until browned, about 3 minutes. 
 Remove pan from heat, stir in  garlic  and  thyme . 
 Add  pasta  to 3 qt boiling  water  with 1½ tsp  salt . Cook until al dente. 
 While the  pasta  is cooking, drain and cut  peppers  in thin strips. Reserve juice. 
 Return skillet with meat to heat. 
 Add  peppers ,  tomato ,  vinegar , and ⅛ tsp  salt . 
 Add a small amount of  pepper  juice. 
 Simmer until this thickens and is reduced by about ½ - takes 5–7 minutes. 
 Drain  pasta , return it to the pot, add sauce. 
 Cover the pot and let  pasta  stand for 5 minutes to allow flavors to blend. 
 Serve topped with grated  romano  or  Parmesan cheese .
