 Description : 
 In Romanian: Cotlete de porc la cuptor
 
 Ingredients : 
 Pork  chops (in a whole rack), 
 salt , 
 a little  lard 
 Directions : 
 Use the cleaver to cut the backbones,  salt  and place the whole meat piece in the roasting pan that was greased with a little  lard . 
 Add 2-3 tablespoons of  water . Leave in the oven for 1 1/2 to 2 hours, basting every now and then with the liquid in the pan. 
 When ready, slice and serve with French fries or mashed  potatoes  or sauted  cabbage . 
 It may also be served cold, sliced thinly.
