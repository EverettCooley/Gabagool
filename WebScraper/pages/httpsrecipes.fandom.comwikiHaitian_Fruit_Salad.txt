 Ingredients : 
 2  oranges 
 4 slices  pineapple  diced 
 2 1/2  bananas 
 ½ cup  raspberries 
 ½ cup  melon  balls 
 24 roasted  nuts 
 ½ cup  strawberries 
 ¼ cup  pineapple juice 
 ¼ cup  lime juice 
 1 cup  sweetened condensed milk  beaten with 2  eggs 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients. Mix the following ingredients together and pour over the fruit:  pineapple juice ,  lime juice ,  sweetened condensed milk  beaten with 2  eggs . 
 Top with  grated coconut  (optional) 
 it serves 4.
