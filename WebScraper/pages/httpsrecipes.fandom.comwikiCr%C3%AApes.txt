Ingredients : 
 100 g  flour 
 100 ml mineral  water 
 50 g  oil 
 25 g  Sugar 
 10 g  salt 
 Directions : 
 Mix the dry ingredients with wooden spoon adding a little liquid at a time, until all is used up. 
 The mixture should resemble thin  sour cream . 
 You may add some  flour  or mineral  water  to adjust the consistency. 
 Take a pancake pan and heat it. Grease it lightly with  oil . 
 Place one tablespoon of the pancake mixture in the hot pan, shaking the pan so that the bottom is covered in a thick layer. 
 When it starts  browning , turn onto the other side. 
 Fill the crepes with preserves,  marmalade ,  Cheese  or meat.
