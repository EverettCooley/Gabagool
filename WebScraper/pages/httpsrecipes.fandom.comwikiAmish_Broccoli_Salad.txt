 Ingredients : 
 1 lg. head  Broccoli , broken into small flowerettes 
 1 med.  red Onion  or about 1/2 c. chopped  red Onion 
 8 to 10 slices  Bacon 
 1 c.  Mayonnaise 
 1/4 to 1/2 c.  Sugar 
 2 to 3 tbsp.  vinegar 
 1/2 c.  raisins 
 1/2 c.  nuts  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Cut  Bacon  into bits and fry crisp. 
 Mix together  Mayonnaise ,  Sugar ,  vinegar ,  Bacon  and  raisins . 
 Pour over  Broccoli  and  onions . 
 Mix well. 
 Let stand at least 1 hour or more before serving.
