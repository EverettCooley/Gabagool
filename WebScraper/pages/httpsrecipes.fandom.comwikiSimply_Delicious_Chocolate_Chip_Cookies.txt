Description : 
 These can be made in a toaster oven.
 
 Contributed by Jenn B aka Mom2Sam and Tiny at  World Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 6 tbsp softened salted  butter 
 ¾ cup  artificial sweetener 
 ½ tsp  molasses 
 1 raw  egg 
 1 cup vanilla-flavored whey  protein powder 
 ¼ tsp  table salt 
 ½ tsp  baking soda 
 ½ tsp  cinnamon 
 ½ cup dry  rolled oats 
 ¾ cup  semi-sweet chocolate  mini morsels 
 ½ cup raw chip  coconut 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Using a hand mixer, combine the  butter , artificial sweetener,  molasses , and  egg ; beat until creamy and fluffy. 
 Add protein powder,  salt ,  baking soda , and  cinnamon . 
 Continue mixing until well mixed. 
 Using low speed, stir in  oats ,  chocolate morsels , and  coconut . 
 Coat cookie sheet with non-stick spray. 
 Drop heaping teaspoons of cookie batter onto cookie sheet. 
 Bake for 6 – 8 minutes at 350°F or until golden brown.
