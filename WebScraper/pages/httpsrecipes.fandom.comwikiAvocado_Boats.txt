 Description : 
 Always check the ingredients to make sure the product is vegan.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2  avocado 
 1/2 ripe, finely chopped  tomato 
 1/3 finely chopped  cucumber 
 1/4 Teaspoon(s)  garlic powder 
 1 Teaspoon(s)  lemon juice 
 1 Pinch(s)  cayenne pepper 
 1 Pinch(s) ( salt  to taste)  salt 
 Directions : 
 Carefully cut  avocados  in half lengthwise and remove seed. 
 Remove  avocado  pulp from shell and mash in a bowl. 
 Add other ingredients and mix well. Put mixture back into  avocado  shells. 
 Serve alone or with chips or raw  carrot  and  celery  sticks.
