Ingredients : 
 6 unpeeled  Apples 
 1 can crushed  pineapple , drained 
 1 lg. container  Cool Whip 
 2 T.  Sugar 
 1/2 c.  nuts 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Dice  Apples . 
 Add rest of ingredients, mixing well. 
 Top with chopped  nuts .
