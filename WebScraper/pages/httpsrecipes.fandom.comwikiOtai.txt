Description : 
         Peace Corps workers pouring 'otai - 2nd Homestay; Holonga, Vava'u.     Prep time: 10 Minutes 
 Serves: About 10-20 Cups 
 Ingredients : 
 fruits:  mango , shredded  pineapple ,  watermelon 
 siale  water 
 half and half  (carton box) 
 sugar 
 Directions : 
 Cut up mango into little squares, pour the shredded pineapple into the pot after, cut up watermelon into little squares and then pour everything into pot. 
 Even pour the half and half into the pot too. 
 Pour a cup of water into the pot and then add a spoon of sugar into the pot.
