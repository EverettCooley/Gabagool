 About Spring salad mix : 
 Mesclun (also called salad mix and gourmet salad mix) is simply a mixture of small, young salad greens, herbs and edible flowers. The mix varies depending on the source, but among those greens commonly included are  arugula ,  dandelion ,  frisée ,  mizuna ,  oak leaf ,  mâche ,  radicchio , and  sorrel . Choose mesclun with crisp leaves and no sign of wilting. Refrigerate in a plastic bag for up to 5 days. Wash and blot dry just before using.
