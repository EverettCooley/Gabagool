cherry  Mash Candy
 
 Ingredients
 
 2 Cups  sugar 
 2/3 Cup  evaporated milk 
 1/2 Cup  margarine 
 12 Regular Sized Marshmallows 
 Dash  salt 
 
 6 oz Package of  cherry  Chips 
 1 Tsp  vanilla 
 12 oz Package of Semisweet  chocolate chips 
 3/4 Cup Chunky  Peanut butter 
 8 oz Package Salted  peanuts ,chopped (or  pecans ) 
 
Preparation
 
Combine The First 5 Ingredients And Warm Over Medium Heat.Bring to a Boil And Boil For 5 Minutes.
 Remove From Heat And Add  cherry  Chips And  vanilla .Stir Until Chips Are Melted And Pour Into a Buttered 9 Inch by 13 Inch Pan.in a double boiler,melt The  chocolate chips  And The  Peanut butter .Add The Nuts And Spread Over  cherry  Mixture.
 Chill.
 Cut Into Bars.
