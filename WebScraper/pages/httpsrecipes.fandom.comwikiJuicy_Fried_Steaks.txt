Ingredients : 
 2 lbs (900 g)  beef   steak 
 1  lime   or  ¼ (50 ml) cup  vinegar 
 1 tsp  season  all 5ml 
 2 tbsp (30 ml)  soy sauce   or  Lea & Perrins  Worcestershire sauce 
 2 – 3 tblsps (15 ml)  meat tenderizer 
 1 large  onion  (sliced) 
 ½ small  sweet pepper  (sliced) 
 2 tbsp  tomato paste  diluted with ½ cup (125 ml)  water 
 2 tsps (10 ml)  vegetable oil 
 Directions : 
 Clean  steak  with  lime juice   or   vinegar . 
 Rub all over with  meat tenderizer . 
 Put in a plastic bag and put in bottom of refrigerator. Leave overnight or at least for four to six hours before using. 
 When needed remove from plastic bag. Season with soy sauce or Lea & Perrins and season all. 
 Heat frying pan and add fat. 
 Fry on each side for about 10 minutes or use grill. 
 Add sliced  onions  and  sweet peppers . 
 If necessary, add ½ cup  water . Simmer for 5 minutes. 
 To make gravy add 1 tbsp diluted  tomato paste .
