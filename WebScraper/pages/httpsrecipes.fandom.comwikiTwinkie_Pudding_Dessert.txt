Ingredients : 
 1 box Twinkies 
 1 box vanilla pudding mix 
 1 cup  milk 
 1 can  cherry  pie filling 
 1 large tub  cool whip 
 Directions : 
 Cut Twinkies in half, layer in bottom of pan. 
 Mix pudding according to directions. Pour over Twinkies. 
 Add cherry pie filling 
 Finish with cool whip. 
 Refrigerate before serving.
