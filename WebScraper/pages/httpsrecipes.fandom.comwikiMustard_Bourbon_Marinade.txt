Description : 
 This marinade is good for  beef  and  pork ribs ,  chicken  and  sausages .
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 From the Recipe Ross Newsletter 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  Dijon mustard 
 ¼ cup  bourbon 
 ¼ cup  soy sauce 
 ½ cup  brown sugar 
 2 tsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 Directions : 
 Combine ingredients in a shallow dish.
