 About sherry vinegar : 
 Sherry vinegar, produced in Spain, is made from Sherry. It tends to grow correspondingly expensive. This wine vinegar has the rich, nutty flavor of a fortified wine. It will get used for several purposes, such as in marinades for grilling and in salads.
