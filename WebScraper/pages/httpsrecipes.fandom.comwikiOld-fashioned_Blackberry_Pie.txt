Description : 
 Purchased from Tibbenham Estate in Anna, Texas in 1997. Dated 1962. Indicates this was Sara Beth's favorite pie.
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  unsalted butter , softened 
 1½ cups  cake flour 
 ½ cup  granulated sugar 
 1½ cups  buttermilk  at room temperature 
 1½ teaspoons  baking powder 
 1 teaspoon  cinnamon 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 4 cups fresh  blackberries 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter  in a glass square baking dish. 
 Combine  flour ,  sugar ,  baking powder ,  cinnamon  and  salt . 
 Add  buttermilk  and stir until moistened. 
 Spoon the blackberries over the batter. 
 Do not stir. 
 Bake in 325°F oven for 45 minutes. 
 Serve warm with  whipped cream .
