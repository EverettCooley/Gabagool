 Ingredients : 
 1 box spanish quick  brown rice 
 4  eggs 
 4 oz chopped  green chilis 
 1 cup  water 
 1 cup grated  cheese  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Cook contents of box according to package directions. 
 When the  rice  is done, add the remaining ingredients except the  cheese  and stir. 
 Sprinkle grated  cheese  on top and bake for 30-35 minutes at 325 °F.
