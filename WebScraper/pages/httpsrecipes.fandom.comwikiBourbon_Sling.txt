Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz  Bourbon 
 1 oz  lemon juice 
 1 tsp  Superfine sugar 
 one tsp  water 
 Garnish:  lemon  Twist 
 Directions : 
 Shake all the ingredients in a shaker with  ice  and strain into a highball glass over  ice . 
 Garnish with  lemon  twist.
