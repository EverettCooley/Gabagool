 Source: Diabetic Living Diet Online 
 Prep Time: 15 minutes | Rest Time: 2 hours | Total Time: 15 minutes 
 Servings: 1 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup soft  bread crumbs 
 2 tbsp  fat-free milk 
 1 can  white kidney beans 
 ¼ cup  sour cream , light or fat-free 
 Pine nuts : 
 3 tbsp  pine nuts 
 ¼ tsp salt-free garlic and herb seasoning blend or other salt-free seasoning blend 
 ⅛ tsp  cayenne pepper 
 2 tsp fresh  oregano 
 assorted vegetable dippers (such as peeled  baby carrots ,  zucchini  slices,  pea pods , yellow  summer squash  sticks, and/or red  sweet pepper  strips) 
 Directions : 
 In a small bowl, combine bread crumbs and milk. 
 Cover and let stand for 5 minutes. 
 Meanwhile, in a blender or food processor, combine beans, sour cream, the 3 tablespoons pine nuts, seasoning blend, and cayenne pepper. 
 Cover and blend or process until nearly smooth. 
 Add bread crumb mixture. 
 Cover and blend or process until smooth. 
 Stir in snipped or dried oregano or basil. 
 Cover and chill for 2 to 24 hours to blend flavors. 
 If desired, sprinkle with additional pine nuts and garnish with basil or oregano leaves. 
 Serve with assorted vegetable dippers. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 40 Calories | 1g Total Fat | 0g Saturated Fat | 70mg Sodium | 1mg Cholesterol | 7g Carbs | 2g Dietary Fiber | 3g Protein 
 Exchanges: ½ starch | ½ carb choice
