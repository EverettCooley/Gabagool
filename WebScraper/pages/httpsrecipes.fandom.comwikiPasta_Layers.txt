 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 20 circles of  Pasta 
 2 tablespoons of  olive oil 
 1 tablespoon of  leeks  finely diced 
 1Â lb of ground  Pork  or  Beef 
 1 teaspoon of  oregano 
 6  tomatoes  cut into quarters 
 Ground  black pepper 
 1 cup of  white wine 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Boil  Pasta  adding some  salt  to the  water , reserve. 
 Sautee all the ingredients. 
 Add the wine and  water  adjusting when necessary until the meat is tender. 
 Adjust  salt  to taste. 
 Arrange a layer of  Pasta  in the plate. 
 Put some of the meat mixture on top and another layer on top. 
 Arrange 5 layers per serving. 
 Serve hot.
