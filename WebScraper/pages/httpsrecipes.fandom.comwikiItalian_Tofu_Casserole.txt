 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  tofu 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 1 teaspoon  Onion  powder 
 1 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 1 tablespoon  parsley 
 2 teaspoons  oregano 
 1 teaspoon  basil 
 1 quart  spaghetti  sauce 
 1/2 pound noodles 
 1 cup  Cheddar   Cheese , grated 
 Directions : 
 Drain and mash  tofu .
 Heat  oil  in skillet. Add  tofu  and all spices. Mix well and stir until  tofu  stops steaming, about 10 minutes.
 Add 1 cup  spaghetti  sauce and simmer 5 minutes.
 Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
 Boil noodles until tender, but not soft. In a 2 quart baking dish, layer half the noodles, 1 1/2 cups  spaghetti  sauce, half the  tofu  mixture and half the  Cheese . Repeat, using the  Cheese  last. Bake for about 30 minutes or until  Cheese  melts and sauce bubbles.
 Serves 8.
