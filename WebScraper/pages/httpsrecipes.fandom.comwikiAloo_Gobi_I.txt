Description : 
 Potatoes with cauliflower. This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Smyth Estate in Plano, Texas in 1990. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 teaspoon  canola oil 
 1  white onion  chopped 
 4 cloves  garlic  minced 
 10 slices fresh  ginger  minced 
 ¼ cup  almonds  chopped 
 1 teaspoon  turmeric 
 1 teaspoon  coriander 
 1 teaspoon  sea salt 
 ½ teaspoon  pepper 
 ½ teaspoon  chili powder 
 1 cup  tomato purée 
 2 large  potatoes  cubed 
 2 cups  cauliflower  florets 
 1 cup water 
 2 teaspoons  garam masala 
 Directions : 
 Saute onions, garlic, and ginger in skillet. 
 Add almonds and all spices except garam masala and saute for 3 minutes. 
 Add tomato puree, potatoes, and cauliflower with water then simmer for 20 minutes. 
 Add garam masala and serve immediately.
