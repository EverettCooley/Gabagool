 Serves : 4 
 Ingredients :

 Directions : 
 Soak the shrimp in cold water for half an hour, drain, and cover with cold water. 
 Add the cayenne pepper and soy sauce; boil slowly, about one hour. 
 Mix the rice flour with cold water to a smooth paste, and stir until it thickens. 
 Serve with rice.
