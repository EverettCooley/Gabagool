Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 16 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups  graham cracker  crumbs 
 ¼ cup  butter , melted 
 1¼ cups  sugar 
 4 pkgs (8 oz each)  cream cheese , softened 
 2 tsp  vanilla 
 16 oz container  sour cream 
 4  eggs 
 2 cups mixed  berries 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 325°F. 
 Line baking pan with foil, extending over sides of pan. 
 Mix crumbs, butter and 2 tbs sugar, press onto bottom of pan. 
 Beat cream cheese, 1 cup of the remaining sugar and 1 tsp vanilla in bowl until blended. 
 Add 1 cup sour cream, mix well. 
 Add eggs, beating until blended. 
 Pour over crust. 
 Bake 40 minutes or until center is almost set. 
 Mix remaining sour cream, 2 tbsp sugar and 1 tsp vanilla until blended. 
 Spread over cheesecake. 
 Bake an additional 10 minutes. Cool. 
 Cover, refrigerate 3 hours. 
 Lift cheesecake from pan. 
 Cut into 16 pieces. 
 Top with mixed berries just before serving.
