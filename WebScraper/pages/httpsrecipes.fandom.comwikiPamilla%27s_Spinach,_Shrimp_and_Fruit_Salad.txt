Description : 
 A light and refreshing salad perfect for a summer meal. Tossed with a fresh citrus  salad dressing .
 
 Ingredients : 
 Salad : 
 1 genuine  California avocado 
 lemon juice 
 1 6oz bag of fresh baby  spinach 
 1 11oz can of drained  mandarin oranges 
 1 ½ cups seedless  red grapes 
 1 ½ cups cooked  shrimp 
 2 stalks chopped  green onions 
 Salad dressing : 
 ¾ cup  olive  or macadamia nut oil 
 ¼ cup  orange juice 
 3 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 tbsp  white vinegar 
 ⅓ cups  sugar 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  paprika 
 1½ tsp grated  onions 
 Directions : 
 Salad dressing : 
 Make ahead of time and chill. 
 Mix in a cruet; olive or  macadamia nut   oil ,  orange juice ,  lemon juice ,  white vinegar ,  sugar ,  salt ,  paprika , grated  onions . 
 Cover and shake vigorously. 
 Refrigerate. 
 Shake again before use. 
 Salad : 
 Dice  avocado  into a small bowl. 
 Cover with  lemon juice  and set aside. 
 Mix fresh baby  spinach ,  mandarin oranges , seedless  red grapes , cooked  shrimp , and chopped  green onion  in a large bowl. 
 Drain  avocado  and add to salad. 
 Toss together. 
 Take 2 or 3 paper towels and run them under cold  water . 
 Wring them out and place over the top of salad. 
 Cover tightly with plastic wrap and refrigerate until ready to serve.
