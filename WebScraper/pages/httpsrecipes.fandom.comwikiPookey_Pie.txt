 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 5 big potatoes-thick sliced 
 1 can progresgo's  Chicken  with  Barley  soup 
 1 can  chicken broth 
 4 tblsp  butter 
 2 tblsp  flour 
 1/2 cup  milk 
 2 tblsp cut into squares butter-top of  potatoes 
 Directions : 
 1.-slice washed  potatoes  and soak in  water  then preheat oven at 350 degrees. 2.-over low heat in med. sauce pan melt 4-tblsp of  butter ,after  butter  is melted, add (sprinkle) flour , whisk in  milk . whisk until lumps are gone and smooth. 3.-add  Chicken  with  Barley  and cook until boils. 4.-add  chicken broth , cook until boils still whisking. 5.-coat buttom of glass (6x9) pan and add  potatoes  by layers once pan is almost filled add soup mixture. 6.-2-tblsp cut the  butter  into squares and top  potatoes  then cover with foil. let cook for an hour, then uncover and add rest of mixture and let cook an half hour. garnish as desired
