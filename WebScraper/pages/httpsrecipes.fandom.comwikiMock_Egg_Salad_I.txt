Description : 
 From: "Choice Cooking", Canadian Diabetes Assoc.
 
 Ingredients : 
 19 oz can  chickpeas , drained 
 ¼ cup  Tangy Boiled Dressing 
 ¼ cup chopped  celery 
 ½ tsp  basil 
 ¼ tsp  curry powder 
 ¼ tsp  garlic salt 
 Directions : 
 Coarsely mash chick  peas . 
 Combine with dressing and other ingredients. 
 Mix until well blended. 
 Food processor may be used but a coarse texture is more like a real  egg  salad.
