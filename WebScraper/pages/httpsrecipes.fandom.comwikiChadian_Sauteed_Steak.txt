 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 	 medium  onions , chopped coarsely 
 1 	tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 1 1/2 	teaspoons  tomato  sauce 
 1 1/2 	teaspoons  worcestershire sauce 
 1 1/2 	tablespoons sweet  red wine 
 salt 
 pepper 
 1Â lb  Steak , cut into slices, well pounded 
 Directions : 
 Saute the  onions  in the  oil  until light brown over medium heat. 
 Add the sauces, wine,  salt  and  pepper  to taste. 
 Simmer for 2 minutes, stirring constantly. 
 Season meat with  salt  and  pepper  and place in the saute pan with the other ingredients and saute on both sides. 
 Once meat is cooked, serve at once.
