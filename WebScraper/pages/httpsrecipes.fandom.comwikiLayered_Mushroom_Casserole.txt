Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes For Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Makes 9 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups sliced  mushrooms 
 1 cup fat-free  cottage cheese 
 ¼ cup  green onion , chopped 
 ½ teaspoon dried  rosemary 
 1 clove  garlic , finely chopped 
 8 slices whole grain  bread 
 1½ cups  skim milk 
 1 cup fat-free cholesterol-free  egg substitute 
 ½ cup shredded  cheddar  or Monterey  jack cheese 
 Directions : 
 Spray square pan, 9x9x2 inches with nonstick cooking spray. 
 Combine mushrooms, cottage cheese, green onions, rosemary and garlic and mix well. 
 Place 4 slices of bread in the bottom of the pan. 
 Cover the bread with mushroom mixture and top with the remaining slices of bread. 
 Beat milk and egg substitute and pour over the casserole and sprinkle with cheese. 
 Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 45 to 50 minutes or until mixture is set and top is browned. 
 Let stand 10 minutes and cut into 9 pieces. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 Calories 162 | Fat 4 g | Carbs 21 g | Sodium 362 mg | Fiber 3 g
