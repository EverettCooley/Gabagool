 About Candlenut : 
 Wikipedia Article  About Candlenut on Wikipedia 
 Candlenuts, similar in shape to hazelnuts, are small, white, waxy nuts-called buah keras in Malaysia and Indonesia-used primarily as a thickening agent for spice pastes. Blanched almonds or unsalted macadamia or Brazil nuts may be substituted. 
 A round, cream-colored nut with an oily consistency used to add texture and a faint flavor to many dishes. Substitute  macadamia nuts  or raw  cashews .
