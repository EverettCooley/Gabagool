Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 kg  chicken  - cut up into serving sizes 
 1 tbsp cooking  wine 
 1 tsp  sea salt 
 1 tsp  sugar 
 4 tsp  soy sauce 
 4  green onions  - cut into 1-inch pieces 
 4 slices  ginger root 
 2 small  egg yolks 
 4 tbsp  cornstarch 
 oil  for frying 
 Directions : 
 Mix the cooking wine, salt, sugar, soy sauce, green onions, and ginger in a bowl. 
 Add in the chicken, mix well and marinate for at least 1 hour. 
 Before frying, you may remove the ginger and green onions if preferred. 
 Add the egg yolks and mix. 
 Add the cornstarch, mix well. 
 Deep fry in oil (medium heat) for about 6 â€“ 7 minutes or until cooked and the outside is crispy. 
 Serve.
