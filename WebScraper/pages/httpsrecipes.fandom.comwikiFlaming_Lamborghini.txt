Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  Kahlúa 
 1 oz  amaretto  Di Saronno® 
 1 oz  vodka 
 ½ oz green  Chartreuse 
 1 oz blue  curaçao 
 ½ oz  milk 
 Directions : 
 In a cocktail glass, mix kahlúa,  amaretto , and  vodka . 
 Float the chartreuse. 
 In a liqueur glass, put the blue curaçao and float the  milk . 
 Light the cocktail glass and drink quickly through a straw. 
 When it's almost gone, pour the blue curaçao and  milk  into the cocktail glass and suck until all gone.
