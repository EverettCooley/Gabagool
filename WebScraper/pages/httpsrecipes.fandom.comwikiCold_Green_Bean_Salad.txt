Info 
 Cook Time : 
 Serves : 8
 
 Ingredients 
 2 (15.5 ounce) cans  green beans , drained 
 1  red onion , sliced in rings 
 1 (16 ounce) bottle lowfat Italian-style salad dressing 
 Directions 
 1.Place beans in a serving dish. Toss with onions and salad dressing.
 2.Cover with plastic wrap, and chill for 1 hour.
