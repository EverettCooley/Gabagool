 Description : 
 Makes a 1½ lb loaf 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cup  bread flour 
 1 cup  whole wheat flour 
 1 packet / 2¼ tsp  yeast 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tbsp  powdered skim milk  or  soy milk  (optional, but I think it helps) 
 1½ cup hot  water 
 Directions : 
 Follow usual procedure for breadmaker. 
 Nutritional information : 
 I think this works out to 70 calories, 0.5 g fat per ounce.
