Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Puckett Estate in Irving, Texas in 1990. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup plain  yogurt 
 ½ cup  vegetable oil 
 ½ cup  butter 
 2  eggs 
 2 teaspoons  baking powder 
 3 cups  flour 
 1 cup crumbled  feta 
 1 bunch  dill  chopped finely 
 ¼ cup  black olives  pitted and sliced 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon  pepper flakes 
 poppy  and  sesame seeds 
 Directions : 
 Except for one egg yolk and seeds mix all the ingredients well. 
 Using your hands make small balls of dough and place them on a greased baking sheet. 
 Brush them with egg yolk and sprinkle seeds on top. 
 Bake in a preheated oven at 350°F for 45 minutes.
