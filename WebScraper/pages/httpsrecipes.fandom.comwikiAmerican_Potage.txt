American Potage
 1/4 cup oil
1/2 lb. beef chuck, ground
1/4 cup cubed carrot
1 cup diced potatoes
1 can condensed onion soup, undiluted
grated Parmesan cheese
 About 1/2 hour before dinner, in pan, in oil, brown beef and add carrot, potatoes, soup, 1 soup can water. Simmer covered, 15 minutes or until vegetables are tender. Top with cheese. Serves 4
