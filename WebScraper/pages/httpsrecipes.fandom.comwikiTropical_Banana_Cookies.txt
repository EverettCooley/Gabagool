Tropical Banana Cookies
 1 box banana cake mix
1/4 c. veg. oil
1 egg
3/4 c. mashed ripe banana (2 medium bananas)
pecan halves if desired
 Preheat oven to 350.
 Combine all ingredients except pecan halves and mix well. Drop from a 
teaspoon onto an ungreased cookie sheet. If desired, top each cookie 
with a pecan half. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes until golden brown. Cool 
on cookie sheet for about 1 minute then remove to rack to finish 
cooling. 
 Makes 4 dozen cookies
