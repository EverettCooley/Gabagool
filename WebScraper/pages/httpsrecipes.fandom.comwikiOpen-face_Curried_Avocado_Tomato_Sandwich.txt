Description : 
 Hot (or mild) curried  avocado  with cool  tomatoes 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 ripe  California avocados 
 2 Tbsp  curry powder  (or to taste) 
 Â½ tsp  lime juice 
 dash  salt 
 dash  white pepper 
 2 medium  tomatoes 
 2 slices whole grain  bread  (preferably home baked or bakery) 
 Directions : 
 Toast bread slices. 
 Mash avocados. 
 Stir in  lime juice ,  salt ,  pepper , and  curry powder  to taste  (be sure to use enough  curry  to provide a tangy zip!) 
 Finely chop  tomatoes . 
 Spread  avocado  mixture thickly and evenly on toasted bread. 
 Cover with a thick layer of chopped  tomatoes . 
 Eat with knife and fork!
