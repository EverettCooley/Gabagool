Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 canned  black-eyed peas , rinsed 
 plum tomatoes 
 red onion , sliced 
 baby salad  greens 
 Dijon  vinaigrette 
 crisp  bacon , crumbled 
 pecans , toasted 
 Directions : 
 Toss rinsed black eyed peas, cut up plum tomatoes, sliced red onion and baby salad greens with Dijon vinaigrette. 
 Sprinkle with crumbled crisp bacon and toasted pecans.
