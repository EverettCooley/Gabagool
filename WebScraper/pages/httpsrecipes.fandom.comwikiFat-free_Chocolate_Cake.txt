Ingredients : 
 mazola no stick  cooking spray 
 1¼ cups  flour 
 ½ cup unsweetened  cocoa 
 ¼ cup Argo or Kingsford's  cornstarch 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 ½ tsp  baking soda 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 1¼ cup  sugar 
 1 cup  water 
 3  egg whites 
 ½ cup  Karo  light or  dark corn syrup 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Spray 9-inch square baking pan with  cooking spray . 
 In large bowl combine  flour ,  cocoa ,  cornstarch ,  baking powder ,  baking soda  and  salt . 
 In medium bowl, using a fork or wire whisk, mix  sugar  and  water . 
 Blend in  egg  whites and  corn syrup . 
 Gradually stir into dry ingredients until smooth. 
 Pour into prepared pan. 
 Bake 35 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. 
 Cool in pan on wire rack.
