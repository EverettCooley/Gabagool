Description : 
 An exciting, cool and refreshing side salad that goes great with grilled meat. Fantastic for get-togethers.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cans whole  corn 
 2 large  tomatoes , diced 
 1 large sweet  onions , cut into thin strips 
 4  green onions , chopped 
 1 bunch  cilantro  leaves, minced into tiny strips 
 2  limes , juiced 
 â…“ cup  rice vinegar 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl, combine  corn ,  tomatoes , sweet  onion ,  green onion , and  cilantro . 
 Squeeze  lime juice  over mixture, and mix in. 
 Stir in  rice vinegar  to taste; the amount you use will depend on the sweetness of the  corn , and the acidity of the  lime . 
 Season with  kosher salt . 
 Cover, and chill for 45 minutes to an hour. 
 Stir before serving.
