 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 6 or more large ripe  tomatoes , sliced fairly thick 
 1 pound whole or part-skim  milk   mozzarella , sliced the same thickness as the  tomatoes 
 A handful of fresh  basil  leaves, rinsed, patted dry, and minced 
 1/4 cup  olive oil 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Alternate the  tomato  and  mozzarella  slices on a large platter. 
 Sprinkle with the  basil  and drizzle with  olive oil . 
 Prepare in advance, then season lightly with  salt  and  pepper  just before serving.
