Bran cereal is usually made from wheat bran, but some are made from oat bran. The outer layers of the grain of cereals such as wheat, removed during the process of milling and used as a source of dietary fiber.  They are typically ready-to-eat, cold breakfast cereals.
 A few well known brands include:
 
 Bran Buds 
 Raisin Bran 
 All-Bran
