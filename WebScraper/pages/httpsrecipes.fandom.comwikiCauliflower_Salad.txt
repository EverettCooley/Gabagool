 Description : 
 In Romanian: Salata de conopida.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 medium  Cauliflower , 
 2 tablespoons  oil , 
 2 tablespoons  vinegar , 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Wash the  Cauliflower , divide in flowerets and boil in  salt   water . 
 Drain and place in salad bowl. Pour a dressing made of  oil ,  vinegar  and  salt  on top.
