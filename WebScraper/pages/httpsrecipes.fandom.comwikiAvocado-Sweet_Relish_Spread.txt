Description : 
 Yield: 24 Servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 3  California avocados  (1½ pounds) 
 ½ cup chopped  parsley 
 ⅓ cup finely chopped  red onion 
 ⅓ cup prepared sweet pickle  relish 
 Directions : 
 Roughly mash avocados (1½ pounds). 
 Stir in ½ cup chopped  parsley , ⅓ cup finely chopped  red onion  and ⅓ cup prepared sweet pickle relish. 
 Chill 1 hour to marry flavors. 
 Serve 2 tablespoons on  ham ,  cheese ,  turkey  and  chicken  sandwiches or alone on a  bagel .
