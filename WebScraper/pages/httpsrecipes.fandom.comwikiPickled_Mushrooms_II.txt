      Commercial pickled mushrooms     
 Ingredients : 
 4 pounds small  mushrooms 
 4 cups boiling  water 
 1½ tablespoons  salt 
 Marinade : 
 1¾ cups  water 
 15  peppercorns 
 2  bay leaves 
 2½ tablespoons  salt 
 ¾ cup  sugar 
 ¾ cup  vinegar 
 Directions : 
 Cut the  mushroom  stems off even with the caps. 
 Cook over medium heat in boiling  water  with  salt  until they sink to the bottom, about 10 to 15 minutes. 
 Remove  mushroom  caps; place in small sterilised jars. 
 Marinade : 
 Boil  water  with peppercorns and  bay leaves  for 30 minutes. 
 Add  salt  and  sugar . 
 Stir until dissolved. 
 Add  vinegar , bring to boiling. 
 Pour hot marinade over  mushroom  caps. 
 Close the jars. 
 Keep refrigerated 2 or 3 days before serving.
