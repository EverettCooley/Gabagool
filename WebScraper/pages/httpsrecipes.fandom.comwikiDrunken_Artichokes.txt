Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Simms Estate in Duncanville, Texas in 1987.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 8  artichokes 
 8 ounces  sambuca 
 8 ounces dry  white wine 
 2 tablespoons  olive oil 
 4 cloves  garlic  minced 
 2 small  white onions  minced 
 Â½ teaspoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Wash artichokes and spread leaves and place side by side in heavy large saucepan. 
 Mix remaining ingredients and pour over artichokes. 
 Cover saucepan tightly and cook over low flame for 45 minutes. 
 Serve artichokes with pan juices spooned over them.
