Description : 
 Atkanchay is a true Kyrgyz  tea . It differs from others as it is combined with  milk  (1:1) and brought to boil. This national tea is served with flatcakes.
 
 Ingredients : 
 500 g  milk 
 25 g  tea 
 25 g  butter 
 150 g  sour cream 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Brew strong tea and pour in  milk  (1:1), bring to boil, then add  butter ,  salt ,  sour cream , stir and bring to boil again.
