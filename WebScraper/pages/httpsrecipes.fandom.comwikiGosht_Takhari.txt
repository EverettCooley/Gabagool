Beef Curry - Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ pounds boneless  round steak 
 2 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 1 cup chopped  celery 
 1 cup chopped  green peppers 
 1 tablespoon  flour 
 1 tablespoon  curry powder 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  ground ginger 
 ½ teaspoon  turmeric 
 ¼ teaspoon  paprika 
 ¼ teaspoon  ground black pepper 
 ¼ cup  raisins 
 1½ cups boiling  beef broth 
 ¼ cup  yogurt 
 4 cup hot cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Cut  steak  into 1-inch pieces. 
 Heat  oil  in large skillet. 
 Add meat and cook over high heat until brown. 
 Lower heat; add  onion ,  celery  and  green peppers ; continue cooking until vegetables are tender crisp. 
 Blend  flour ,  curry powder ,  salt ,  ginger ,  turmeric ,  paprika  and  black pepper ; sprinkle over meat and vegetables. 
 Stir well. 
 Add  raisins  and broth. 
 Bring to a boil; reduce heat, cover, and simmer 1 hour, or until meat is tender. 
 Adjust seasonings, if necessary. 
 Just before serving stir in  yogurt . 
 Serve over beds of fluffy  rice .
