 Description : 
 breadfruit  should be cooked as it begins to become ripe. Fully ripened, mushy,  breadfruit  is not as good. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups cubed, roasted  breadfruit 
 1 stalk  celery , chopped 
 1 small  sweet pepper , chopped 
 1 small  Onion , chopped 
 2 Tbsps.  palm oil 
 1 can mixed vegetables, drained 
 1 tsp.  salt 
 1 head  lettuce , separate leaves 
 Directions : 
 Combine  breadfruit  with  celery ,  sweet pepper , and  Onion . 
 Moisten with  palm oil , and toss in mixed vegetables. 
 Chill for 10 minutes. 
 Serve on a bed of  lettuce
