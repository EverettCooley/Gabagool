 Ingredients 
 2¼ cups  sifted   cake flour 
 1½ cups  sugar 
 3 teaspoons  baking powder 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ½ cup soft  shortening 
 1 cup  milk 
 1½ teaspoons  vanilla 
 2  eggs 
 Directions 
 Heat oven to 350 °F. 
 Grease and flour two 8 or 9-inch round layer pans. 
 Sift together flour, sugar, baking soda and salt into mixing bowl. 
 Add shortening, ⅔ cup milk and vanilla. 
 Beat vigorously for 2 minutes (300 strokes). 
 You can use an electric mixer if you scrape the sides down often. 
 Use medium speed. 
 Add another ⅓ cup milk and eggs. 
 Beat two minutes. 
 Pour into prepared pan. 
 Bake for  30  to  35  minutes. 
 Cool. 
 Finish with  quick fudge icing  or store bought chocolate frosting.
