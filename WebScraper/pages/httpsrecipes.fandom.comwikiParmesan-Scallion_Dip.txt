Description : 
 I've tried this one time and it's absolutely wonderful.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup whipped  cottage cheese , with  chives 
 5 tablespoons grated  Parmesan cheese 
 4  scallions , including greens,chopped fine 
 1 tablespoon prepared  horseradish 
 1 dash  cayenne pepper 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in a small bowl or blender; mix well. 
 Taste and add more cayenne if desired. 
 Cover and chill at least 2 hours before serving.
