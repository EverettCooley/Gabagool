Description 
 Here is a natural fiber snack or cereal.
 Granola I  from Recipes for Better Nutrition During Cancer Treatment by the National Cancer Institute, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Cook Time : 25 minutes to bake 
 Ingredients 
 
 1½ cups quick  oatmeal 
 ½ cup regular  wheat germ 
 ½ cup  coconut 
 ½ tsp.  salt 
 ½ cup chopped  nuts 
 ⅔ cup  sweetened condensed milk 
 Directions 
 Measure oatmeal, wheat germ, coconut, salt, and nuts into mixing bowl, stirring to blend. Add oil and mix thoroughly. 
 Pour in condensed milk and blend well. 
 Sprinkle a handful of wheat germ on a cookie sheet and gently spread mixture on top. 
 Bake in 325 °F oven about 25 minutes. 
 Check mix as it bakes-after the first 10 minutes, mix will begin to brown. Stir it on cookie sheet every 10 minutes until it is as brown as you like. 
 Cool on pan; store in covered container in refrigerator. 
 Variations 
 Granola II : Omit milk. Add ½ cup  honey . Bake as above. 
 Chocolate chip : Put hot mixture in bowl. Stir in ½ cup of  chocolate chips . 
 Raisin:  Stir in 1 cup of  raisins  after the mixture cools.
