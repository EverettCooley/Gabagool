Description : 
 makes 1/2 cup 
 Ingredients : 
 5 cloves garlic, peeled 
 ¾ cup mayonnaise 
 1½ tbsp. fresh lemon juice 
 2 chipotle peppers in adobo 
 Salt and pepper to taste 
 Directions : 
 Cook garlic in a dry skillet over medium-high heat until cloves are golden with some black spots, about 9 minutes. 
 Transfer to a food processor along with mayonnaise, lemon juice, and peppers, and process until combined; season with salt and pepper.
