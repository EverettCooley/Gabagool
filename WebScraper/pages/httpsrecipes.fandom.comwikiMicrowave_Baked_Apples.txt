 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 medium (about 6 to 8 oz. each)  Criterion apples 
 4 tablespoons favorite fruit preserves 
 2 tablespoons  apple juice 
 Directions : 
 1.) Pare top third of  Apple ; core. Arrange  Apples  in microwave-safe baking dish; fill centers with preserves. Pour juice in bottom of dish; cover with vented plastic wrap or lid. Microcook at HIGH (100%) 4-5 minutes or until  Apples  are tender.
