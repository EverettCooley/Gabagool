 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups  Berries  of your choice or any combination thereof 
 1/2 cup raw  brown sugar  or to taste 
 4 tbsp  unbleached flour  or  whole wheat flour 
 pinch of  salt 
 1 unbaked double pie crust 
 Directions : 
 Pile  Berries  into  pastry shell . 
 Mix together the  flour  and  Sugar  and sprinkle over the  Berries . 
 Position top crust over  Berries , crimp edges, trim off excess dough. 
 Cut a few slashes in the top with a sharp knife. 
 Bake on the bottom rack until golden brown, about 30â€“35 minutes. 
 Serve warm with a big scoop of non-dairy ice cream.
