Ingredients : 
 1½ cups sifted  flour 
 1 cup  sugar 
 ⅔ cup  chocolate chips 
 1 cup chopped  dates 
 ½ cup chopped  green cherries 
 3  eggs 
 ½ teaspoon  baking powder 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 2 cups  pecans , chopped 
 ½ cup  red cherries , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Sift  flour ;  baking powder  and  salt  together: stir in  chocolate chips ,  pecans ,  dates  and  cherries  until well coated with  flour . 
 In large bowl beat  eggs . 
 Gradually beat in  sugar . 
 Fold in  flour  mixture and pour into greased loaf pan. 
 Bake about 1½ hours or until well done at 325°F.
