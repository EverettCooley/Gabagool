Ingredients : 
 Â¼ cup  butter 
 12 oz  beer 
 1 large  onion , thinly sliced 
 1 lbs  wieners , all-meat 
 10  buns, wiener 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter  in saucepan; add  beer  and  onion . 
 Cover and cook over low heat 30 minutes. 
 Transfer saucepan to grill. 
 Heat wieners on grill. 
 While they grill, use tongs to dip them into  beer  sauce four or five times at 5-minute intervals. 
 Serve wieners in buns; spoon cooked  onion  and sauce over wieners.
