Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 10/14/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pkg (24 oz) frozen  hash browns 
 2 cups fully cooked  ham 
 ¾ cup shredded  cheddar  cheese, divided 
 1  onion  chopped 
 2 cups  sour cream 
 1 (10¾ oz) can  cheddar cheese soup 
 1 (10¾ oz) can  cream of potato  soup 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl, combine potatoes, ham, ½ cup cheese and onion. 
 In another bowl, combine sour cream, soups and pepper; add potato mixture and mix well. 
 Transfer to a greased 3 qt. baking dish. 
 Sprinkle with remaining cheese. 
 Bake uncovered 60 – 65 minutes or until bubbly and potatoes are tender. 
 Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
