 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Gonzales Estate in Crandall, Texas in 1980.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  wheat berries 
 1 cup vanilla  soy milk 
 2 tablespoons unsweetened  applesauce 
 1 tablespoon  egg replacer  mixed with 6 tablespoons water 
 Â½ teaspoon  salt 
 4 teaspoons  baking powder 
 1 tablespoon  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Blend berries and soy milk until smooth. 
 Mix remaining ingredients then add to blender and combine. 
 Pour batter onto hot waffle iron and cook according to manufacturer's instructions.
