Ingredients : 
 300 grams  Pork , sliced 
 100 grams  green beans 
 1  onions , sliced 
 2  tomato , chopped 
 2 red-hot  chilli , chopped 
 2 cloves  garlic , crushed 
 2 tbsp soy sauce 
 1 tbsp  vinegar 
 2 tbsp  tomato  puree 
 sprinkle of  Sugar 
 dash of fish sauce 
 1 pack mini tortillas 
 Directions : 
 In a pan, heat  garlic ,  Onion , and  chilli . 
 Add  Pork  chops, and vegetables. 
 Add a little bit of  water  and let it simmer. 
 Add the Soy sauce,  vinegar , dash of Fish sauce, and sprinkle some  Sugar . 
 The dish is done. Wrap it with microwaved Tortillas (usually takes about 10 sec. to heat), and you will have your Filipino-style Fajitas! Best when dish is served cold with the hot tortillas
