Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  shortening 
 ½ cup  dark brown sugar 
 3 large  eggs 
 1 tsp  vanilla extract 
 1 cup  whole wheat flour 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 ½ cup  oatmeal 
 ½ cup chopped  walnuts 
 ½ cup  raisins  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Mix  shortening  and  sugar  in large bowl until creamy. 
 Add  eggs  and  vanilla  and beat until smooth. 
 Mix  flour ,  salt ,  baking powder  and  cinnamon  in a small bowl. 
 Stir dry ingredients into the liquid mix. 
 Stir in  oatmeal ,  nuts  and  raisins . 
 Spread in 13 x 9 inch pan bake 350°F for 15 – 20 minutes, until crust begins to brown.  Do not overbake . 
 Cool completely. 
 Invert on board and cut into 10 – 12 squares. 
 One square makes one breakfast serving.
