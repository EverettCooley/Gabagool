Ingredients : 
 2 pt blueberries 
 1/2 lb lg bing cherries, halved, pitted 
 4 lg peaches, sliced 
 1/4 c Grand Marnier 
 1/4 c sugar 
 1 pt fresh raspberries 
 Vanilla yogurt, optional 
 Preparation : 
 Toss blueberries, cherries & peaches with Grand Marnier & sugar. Put fruit in clear glass bowl & sprinkle raspberries over everything. Serve plain or with chilled vanilla yogurt.
 4-6 servings
