 Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 oz  light rum 
 3 tbsp  coconut milk 
 5 tbsp Crushed  pineapple 
 2 cubs  ice 
 Directions : 
 Blend at high speed until smooth. Strain into a glass and serve with a straw. Top with a  pineapple  wedge and a  cherry .
