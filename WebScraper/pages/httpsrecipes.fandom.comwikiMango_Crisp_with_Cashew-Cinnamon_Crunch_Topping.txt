 Description : 
 mango  Crisp with Cashew- cinnamon  Crunch Topping
 
 Ingredients : 
 2	 cups peeled and sliced  mango 
 1	 large  lemon , juiced 
 1	cup chopped cashews 
 1	cup  flour 
 ½	cup (1 stick)  butter  or  margarine , room temperature 
 1½ 	teaspoons ground  cinnamon 
 ½	cup packed  brown sugar 
 whipped cream  or  vanilla bean   ice   cream 
 Directions : 
 Put sliced mangoes in bowl and squeeze fresh  lemon juice  over them; 
 Mix with a fork to combine 
 Combine cashews,  flour ,  butter ,  cinnamon  and  brown sugar  and mix well to make topping 
 Top fruit with the mixture and bake in preheated oven 15 to 20 minutes 
 Serve warm with  whipped cream  or  ice   cream .
