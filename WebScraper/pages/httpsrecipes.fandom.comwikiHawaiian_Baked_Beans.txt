Ingredients : 
 2 cans vegetarian beans in  tomato  sauce 
 1 can  pineapple  chunks, drained 
 1-2 tbsp.  light brown sugar 
 1 1/2 tsp. mild  mustard 
 1/4 tsp.  salt 
 dash ground cloves 
 Directions : 
 Combine ingredients, pour into 1 quart casserole. 
 Bake, uncovered in 375 degree oven for 50 to 55 minutes or until beans are hot, stirring occasionally. 
 Makes 4 to 6 servings.
