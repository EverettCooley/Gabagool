 Description : 
 Makes approx. 20 cookies.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups rice 2 packages Reg. Flavor oatmeal (mixed w/milk) 
 1/4 cup molasses 
 1 cup carrots 
 1/3 cup spinach 
 1 1/4 cup flour 
 1/2 tbsp brown gravy mix 
 4 tbsp applesauce 
 1/2 tbsp vegetable oil 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350degrees Stir Ingredients, but adding flour gradually. 
 Drop on cookie sheet using tsp. 
 Bake 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
