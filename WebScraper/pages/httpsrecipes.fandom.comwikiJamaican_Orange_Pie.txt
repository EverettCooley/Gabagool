 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups  orange 's 
 1 cup  milk 
 3/4 cups  Sugar 
 1/2 cup  yeast 
 2 cups dried  pineapple juice 
 1 cup  dried bananas 
 1 cup dried strawberrys 
 Directions : 
 Slice  oranges  into bite size pieces put the  oranges  in to a bowl. 
 Put 1 Cup of  milk  in the Bowl. 
 Put the Dried Strawberrys,  dried bananas , Dried  pineapple juice ,  yeast 
 Bake for 35 Minutes. 
 And if not done put in for extra 25 minutes.
