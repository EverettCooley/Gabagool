Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 several dashes  Tabasco sauce  (optional) 
 several dashes  Worcestershire sauce 
 4 oz beef  consommé  or  beef bouillon 
 ½ tsp grated  horseradish  (optional) 
 1½ – 2 oz  vodka 
 1 tsp  lemon juice 
 pinch  celery salt  or  celery seed 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients with  ice  cubes in a chilled double old fashioned glass. 
 Strain and pour into a shot glass for a bull shot "shot".
