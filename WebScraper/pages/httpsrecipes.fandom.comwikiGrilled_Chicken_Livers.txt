Description : 
 Purchased from the Montgomery estate in Quinlan, Texas in 1980. Date on card says 1932 
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 box  chicken livers 
 1 pound  bacon 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Cut livers in half and wash. 
 Pat dry. 
 Cut  bacon  strips in half. 
 Salt  and  pepper  livers. 
 Wrap each liver with  bacon  and hold in place with wooden toothpick. 
 Grill until  bacon  and liver are done.
