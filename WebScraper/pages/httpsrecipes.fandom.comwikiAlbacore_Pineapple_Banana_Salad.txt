Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Hager Estate in Canton, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 3 ripe  bananas  diced 
 ½ cup diced canned  pineapple 
 1½ cups canned  albacore tuna 
 ¼ cup diced  celery 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 tablespoon chopped  pickle 
 mayonnaise  to moisten 
 Directions : 
 Mix  bananas  and  pineapple  together then add flaked  albacore  and fold in remaining ingredients.
