Description : 
 
 Contributed by  Indiancuisineandculture Y-Group 
 
 Makes 8 servings 
 Preparation time: 15 minutes 
 Cooking time: 1 hour 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 3 tablespoons  Dijon mustard 
 ½ cup  maple syrup 
 5 medium  sweet potatoes  (about 3 pounds), peeled, halved lengthwise, cut into ½-inch pieces 
 ¼ teaspoon  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 350°F. 
 Melt butter or margarine in microwave; stir in mustard, then syrup. 
 Place potatoes in a roasting pan coated with cooking spray. 
 Pour mustard sauce and pepper over potatoes, toss to coat and spread evenly. 
 Bake 1 hour, stirring occasionally, until potatoes are tender.
