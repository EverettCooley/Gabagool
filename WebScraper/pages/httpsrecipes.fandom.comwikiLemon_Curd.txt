       Lemon Curd     
 For an alternative recipe containing only  lemons ,  eggs ,  sugar  and  butter  - check out  Lemon Curd (classic) .
 
 Ingredients : 
 3  eggs 
 250 g  skim milk 
 6 leaves  gelatin 
 250 g low fat  yogurt 
 juice of 2  lemons 
 1 tbl  sugar 
 liquid  sweetener  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Split the  eggs  and beat egg yolk til foamy. 
 Bring  milk  to a boil, and add the egg yolk while stirring til it's thickened. 
 Take curd of stove and stir in the prepared  gelatine . 
 Stir for a while. 
 Leave to cool for 15 minutes * beat egg whites and  sugar  til very stiff. 
 Stir  yoghurt  and lemon juice in curd and add the stiff egg whites. 
 Add liquid sweetener to taste. 
 Stirring as less as possible.
