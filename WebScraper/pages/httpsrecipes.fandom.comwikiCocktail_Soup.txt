Description : 
 This recipe will make 48 to 60 ounces of punch; serve in highball glasses.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  Cisco Red® 
 ½ cup 190 proof  Everclear ® 
 ½ cup  Southern Comfort  Reserve® 
 1 quarter  Mountain Dew® 
 1 quarter cup  tonic water 
 ½ cup  Coco López ® 
 1 cup  orgeat syrup 
 1 cup  gin 
 1 quarter cup  Angostura Bitters ® 
 1 quarter cup  sour mix 
 1 cup to 12 ounces  sherbet 
 Directions : 
 Pour Sherbet into blender,secure lid tightly,and blend on high until liquid. 
 Add all other ingredients and blend again. 
 Pour liquid into pitcher,or pour directly into cups from blender. 
 Note : 
 If refrigerating overnight,pour into pitcher and cover rim with Saran Wrap®.
