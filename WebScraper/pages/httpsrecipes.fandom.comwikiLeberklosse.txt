       Leberklosse     
 Liver dumpling soup 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ lb  liver sausage 
 1  egg white  or  egg yolk 
 ½ cup  cracker crumbs 
 1 tablespoon chopped  parsley  or  chives 
 1 tablespoon  catsup  (optional) 
 3 cups  beef stock 
 ½ teaspoon  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Shape mixture into 1 inch meatballs. 
 Drop into gently simmering stock. 
 Simmer 5 – 6 minutes. 
 Add  parsley  for garnish.
