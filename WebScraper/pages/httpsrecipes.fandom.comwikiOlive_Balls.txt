Ingredients : 
 1 c. shredded  Cheddar  cheese (about 4 oz.) 
 1/4 c.  butter , softened 
 1/4 tsp.  Worcestershire sauce 
 1 c.  Bisquick  baking mix 
 1 jar (5 oz.) pimento-stuffed  olives 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 400 degrees. 
 Mix cheese, butter and Worcestershire sauce 
 Mix in baking mix until dough forms (work with hands if necessary). 
 Pat olives completely dry on paper towel. 
 Shape 1 teaspoon dough around each olive. 
 Bake on lightly greased cookie sheet until light golden brown, about 10 minutes. 
 Notes : 
 Makes about 45 appetizers.
