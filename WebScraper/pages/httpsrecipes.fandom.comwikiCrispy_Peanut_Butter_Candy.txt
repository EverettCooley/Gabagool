Really delicious. Fools your palate and seems like  marshmallow  instead of angel food cake." 
 
 Original recipe yield: 1 - 9x13 pan. 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  butter 
 2 cups confectioners'  sugar 
 2 teaspoons  vanilla extract 
 4  egg yolks 
 1 (12 ounce) container frozen  whipped topping , thawed 
 1 (9 inch) angel food cake 
 6 (2.1 ounce) bars chocolate-covered crispy  peanut butter  flavored candy bar 
 ½ cup  pecan  halves 
 Directions : 
 Mix together  butter ,  sugar , and  eggs . 
 Cut angel cake into tiny pieces. 
 Crumble the cookie bars. 
 Cover cake with mixture, chill and serve.
