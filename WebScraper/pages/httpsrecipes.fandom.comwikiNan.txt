 Ingredients : 
 6 cups  maida  (white flour) 
 6 tsp  milk powder 
 1 cup  evaporated milk 
 6 – 7 tsp  sugar 
 200 g  oil  (you can also use  margarine ) 
 2 tsp  baking powder 
 3 tsp  yeast 
 1  egg 
 Directions : 
 Mix all the ingredients until it is fine dough. 
 Cover with a wet cloth and let it sit at room temperature for 3 hours. 
 Then divide it evenly into 12 parts. 
 Roll out into flat round shape and make sure it is as thick as a pizza's crust. 
 Bake in oven at 350 °F and take it out when it is light brownish color.
