Makes 8 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 4- to 6-ounce package Spanish seasoned  rice  mix 
 1 pound ground  Beef 
 1 medium  Onion , chopped 
 1 1-ounce packet  taco seasoning  mix 
 6  corn  tortillas, divided 
 2 14-1/2-ounce cans Mexican-style stewed  tomatoes , divided 
 2 cups (8-ounces) shredded Mexican-style  Cheese  blend, divided 
 chopped fresh  cilantro 
 Directions : 
 Prepare  rice  according to package directions. 
 Heat large skillet over medium-high heat until hot. 
 Add  Beef ,  Onion , and contents of seasoning packet. Cook 5 to 7 minutes, breaking up  Beef  with a spoon or until  Beef  is brown and  Onion  is tender. 
 Remove from heat; drain fat. 
 Tear three  corn  tortillas into bite-size pieces and place on bottom of a non-metallic 13x9x2-inch dish. 
 Add half of  Beef  mixture, spreading evenly. Top with half of  rice  mixture, half of the  tomatoes  and half of the  Cheese . 
 Repeat layers; except  Cheese . 
 Cover and bake in 400 degree oven for 15 minutes. 
 Uncover; top with remaining  Cheese  and bake 10 minutes more or until  Cheese  is melted and golden brown. 
 To serve, garnish with  cilantro .
