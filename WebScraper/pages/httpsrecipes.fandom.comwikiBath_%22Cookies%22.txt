Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Yield 24 cookies / 12 baths 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups finely ground  sea salt 
 ½ cup  baking soda 
 ½ cup  cornstarch 
 2 tbsp light  oil 
 1 tsp Vitamin E  oil 
 2  eggs 
 5 to 6 drops  essential oil 
 Directions : 
 Bake at 350 degree for 10 – 12 minutes. 
 Do not over bake. 
 Use 1 – 2 cookies per bath.
