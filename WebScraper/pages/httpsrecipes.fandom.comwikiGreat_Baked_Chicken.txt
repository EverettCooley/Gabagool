Description : 
 Contributed by Linda in Tennessee at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  oil 
 1 tablespoon  honey 
 1 tablespoon  lime juice 
 ¼ teaspoon  paprika 
 seasoned salt  to taste 
 4  chicken breasts  halves 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients (except  chicken ) in a saucepan and warm just to melt  honey . 
 Arrange  chicken  breast-side-up in a square baking dish or pan and bake uncovered at 400°F about 35 – 40 minutes, basting pieces without turning them, 3 or 4 times during baking. 
 Immediately upon removing from oven, seal baking dish or pan tightly in foil and let stand 15 to 20 minutes before serving.
