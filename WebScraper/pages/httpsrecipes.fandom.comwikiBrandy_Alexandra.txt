 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  brandy 
 ½ oz  crème de cacao 
 1 scoop vanilla  ice cream 
 1 scoop  ice 
 powdered  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Mix all the ingredients in blender, garnished with  cinnamon  powder.
