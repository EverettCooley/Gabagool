banana  Split Cake Dessert
 
Mix:
 
 2 C. Graham Cracker Crumbs 
 1/2 C. Melted  butter 
 
Press Into a 9x13 Inch Pan. Bake at 350 Degrees For 7Minutes.
 
Beat Together:
 
 1 C. Soft  butter 
 2 Lbs. Confectioners'  sugar 
 1  egg 
 
Spread Over Cooled Crust.
 
 5  bananas 
 
Cut Lengthwise, Dip in  lemon juice  And Pat Dry. PlaceOn Above Mixture.
 
Spread:
 
 1 (large) Can of Crushed  pineapple  Over  bananas . 
 
Whip:
 
 1 C.  whipping cream 
 
Spread Over Top.
 
Sprinkle on Top:
 
 1/2 C. Chopped  walnuts 
 Drained Red  maraschino cherries 
 
Refrigerate Several Hours Until Well Chilled. Delicious!
 
Enjoy.
