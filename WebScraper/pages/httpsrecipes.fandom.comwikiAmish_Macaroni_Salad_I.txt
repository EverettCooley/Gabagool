      Amish Macaroni Salad II     
 Ingredients : 
 Salad : 
 1 lb  macaroni 
 ½ cup finely chopped  celery 
 ½ cup finely grated  carrots 
 ¼ cup finely chopped  onions 
 ¼ cup chopped  green peppers 
 6 hard-boiled  eggs 
 paprika , for  garnish 
 Dressing : 
 2 cups  Miracle Whip 
 ¼ cup  vinegar 
 ¾ cup  sugar 
 2 tablespoons  prepared mustard 
 Directions : 
 Cook  macaroni , drain and cool. 
 Whisk together the dressing ingredients. 
 Combine the  macaroni ,  celery ,  carrots ,  onions , and remaining  eggs . 
 Fold the dressing into the  macaroni  salad mixture. 
 Place into your serving bowl and garnish with the reserved slice  egg  and  paprika . 
 Let sit overnight.
