Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  brown sugar 
 2 teaspoons  soy sauce 
 1 tablespoon  sesame oil  (optional) 
 ¼ cup  vegetable oil 
 3 tablespoons  rice vinegar 
 1 (8 ounce) package dried  rice noodles 
 1 head  iceberg lettuce  - rinsed, dried, and chopped 
 4 boneless  chicken  breast halves, cooked and shredded 
 3  green onions , chopped 
 1 tablespoon  sesame seeds , toasted 
 Directions : 
 add 2 tablespoons of  oil  to a hot  skillet . Add  noodles  and cook for 3 –5 minutes 
 Add the  lettuce ,  chicken ,  onions , and  sesame seeds  together. Allow to cool for about 15 minutes. 
 Add the dressing (made by mixing  brown sugar ,  soy sauce ,  sesame oil ,  salad oil , and  rice vinegar ) right before serving
