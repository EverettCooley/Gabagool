 Cook Time : 
 Serves : 
 Ingredients 
 1 container of  plain  or vanilla flavored  yogurt  (8 ounces) 
 granola  cereal 
 3 or 4 of the following fruits:  apple  chunks,  peach  slices,  blueberries ,  kiwifruit ,  strawberries ,  banana  slices,  grapes 
 2 paper cups or small glasses (8 ounces) 
 Directions 
 Put 2 spoonfuls of yogurt into each paper cup or glass. 
 Sprinkle a spoonful of granola over the yogurt. 
 Put 2 spoonfuls of fruit over the granola. 
 Put 2 spoonfuls of yogurt over fruit. 
 Sprinkle a little granola on top.
