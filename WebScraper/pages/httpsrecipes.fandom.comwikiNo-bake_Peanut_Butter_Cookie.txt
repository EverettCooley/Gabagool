Description : 
 Contributed by Paula in California at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  milk 
 ½ cup  butter 
 2 cups  sugar 
 ⅔ cup  peanut butter 
 3 tablespoon  cocoa 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 3 cups  rolled oats 
 1 cup  coconut  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Mix first 3 ingredients in a saucepan. 
 Cook over medium heat until mixture comes to a rolling boil. Cook 1 minute longer. 
 Remove from heat and add peanut butter, vanilla, cocoa, rolled oats and coconut. 
 Mix thoroughly. 
 Drop from teaspoon onto wax paper. Allow to set until cool at room temperature.
