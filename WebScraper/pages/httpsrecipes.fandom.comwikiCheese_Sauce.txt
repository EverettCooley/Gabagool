 Description : 
       Cheese Sauce     
 Ingredients : 
 3 tbsp.  butter 
 3 tbsp.  flour 
 1 ½ cup  milk 
 1 cup grated/fresh  cheese 
 ½ tbsp.  salt 
 ¼ tbsp.  paprika 
 ¼ tbsp.  cayenne 
 ½ tbsp.  dry mustard 
 Directions : 
 In a pan, melt butter. Stir in flour. 
 Slowly stir in milk. 
 When the sauce is smooth, reduce heat and mix with the grated/fresh cheese. 
 Season the sauce with spices. 
 Stir until the cheese is melted.
