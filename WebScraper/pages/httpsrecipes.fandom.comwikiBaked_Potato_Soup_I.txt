From Menu and Recipes Week 3/11/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  bacon 
 ⅔ cup   margarine 
 ⅔ cup   flour 
 7 cup   milk 
 4 large  baked potatoes , peeled and cubed 
 4  green onions 
 1½ cup  shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 1 cup   sour cream 
 1 tbsp  salt 
 1 tbsp  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Cook  bacon . Drain, crumble and set aside. 
 In Dutch oven or stock pot, melt  margarine  over medium heat. 
 Whisk in  flour  until smooth. 
 Gradually, stir in  milk , whisking constantly, until thickened. 
 Stir in  potatoes  and  onions . 
 Bring to a boil, stirring frequently. 
 Reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes. 
 Mix in  bacon ,  cheese ,  sour cream ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Continue cooking stirring frequently, until  cheese  is melted. Add  milk  if too thick.
