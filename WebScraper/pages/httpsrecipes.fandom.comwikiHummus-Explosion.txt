Ingredients : 
 1 big  eggplant 
 4 – 5 cloves  garlic 
 juice of 3  lemons  or  limes , I use both 
 1 can of  garbanzo beans , rinsed well 
 1 or 2 cans of  black beans 
 5 – 8  sun-dried tomatoes , in  olive oil 
 3 tablespoon  tahini 
 3 tablespoon creamy  peanut butter 
 salt ,  pepper ,  parsley ,  hot sauce  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together in a food processor. (This makes quite a bit so you might have to do it in batches.) 
 Season with spices to taste. If the consistency is too thick, add lemonade, yes lemonade, till desired consistency. 
 Serve with toasted pita slices, small cut up veggies and vegan crackers. Also makes a great sandwich or roll-up.
