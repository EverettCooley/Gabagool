 Ingredients : 
 1 can baby  Peas 
 1-2 teaspoon  Mayonnaise 
 1/2 medium  Onion , diced very small 
 Directions : 
 1. Just mix all ingredients together and let chill for at least an hour before serving.
 2. Add more or less  mayo  to taste.
