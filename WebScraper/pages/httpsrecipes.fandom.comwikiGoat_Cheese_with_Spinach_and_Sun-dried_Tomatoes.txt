Ingredients : 
 1 cup  romaine lettuce  (1.9 grams of carbs) 
 ½ cup  spinach  (1.2 grams of carbs) 
 1  radish , diced (0.2 grams of carbs) 
 4 tbsp  sun-dried tomatoes  (1.5 grams of carbs) 
 3 tbsp  olive oil 
 1 tbsp  balsamic vinegar  (0.9 grams of carbs) 
 ¼ pound  goat cheese  (0.6 grams of carbs) 
 Directions : 
 Slice  goat cheese , brush with  oil  and broil until golden. 
 Serve on top of salad with diced  radish  and  sun-dried tomatoes . 
 Drizzle with  oil  and  vinegar .
