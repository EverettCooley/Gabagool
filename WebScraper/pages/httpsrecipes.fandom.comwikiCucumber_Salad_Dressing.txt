 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2	oz	 tofu , Firm drained 
 1	cup	 cucumber  coarsely chopped and unpeeled 
 2	tbl	fresh  lemon juice 
 1	tbl	 vegetable oil 
 1/2	tsp	 salt 
 1/4	tsp	Dark  sesame oil 
 1/8	tsp	 ground red pepper 
 Directions : 
 Place all ingredients in a food processor; process until smooth.
