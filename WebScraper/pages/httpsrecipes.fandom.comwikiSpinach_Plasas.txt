 Ingredients : 
 2 packages frozen chopped  spinach 
 ½ pound smoked  fish , flaked 
 1 large  onion , chopped 
 ¼ cup  groundnut  paste ( peanut butter ) 
 3 cups  water 
 1½ cups  palm oil 
 2  hot peppers  or 1 tsp.  cayenne pepper 
 1 pound meat, stewing or chuck roast 
 Directions : 
 Cut meat. 
 Put in saucepan with two cups of  water ,  salt ,  onion  and  pepper . 
 Bring to boil and add  palm oil . 
 Continue cooking partly covered. 
 Cook for 1½ hours over medium heat. 
 Add  spinach  (previously thawed and drained), flaked  fish  and  groundnut paste  mixed with  water . 
 Stir, cover and simmer for 10 minutes. 
 Serve with steamed  rice .
