 Description : 
 This is a healthy recipe that can be served either as a snack or main course. Itâ€™s a great vegetarian dish.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 Main ingredients:
 
 1/2 cup cooked  rice 
 1/2 cup  rice  vermicellis, soaked in hot  water  until soft 
 1/2 cup  egg noodles , cooked al dente 
 1/2 cup boiled  potatoes , sliced 
 1/4 cup  fried bean curd , sliced thinly 
 1 hard boiled  egg , chopped 
 1/3 cup  cabbage , shredded 
 1/3 cup  green papaya , shredded 
 1/3 cup  cucumber , shredded 
 1/3 cup  carrots , shredded 
 1/3 cup  Onion , thinly sliced 
 Dressing:
 
 2 tablespoons  lime juice 
 8 tablespoons  tamarind juice 
 4 tablespoons  fish sauce 
 4 tablespoons  chili oil 
 2 tablespoons  gram flour 
 2 tablespoons dried prawns, pounded 
 2 tablespoons dried  chili powder 
 Garnish:
 
 a handful fried shallots 
 a handful  coriander leaves 
 Directions : 
 Put  rice , vermicellis,  egg noodles  and  potatoes  in a large bowl. Add ingredients for dressing. Mix well. Adjust seasoning if desired. Add in the rest of salad ingredients and mix well. Garnish with shallots and  coriander leaves . Serves 4 people.
