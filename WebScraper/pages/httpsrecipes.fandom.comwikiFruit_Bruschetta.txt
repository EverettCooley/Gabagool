      Fruit Bruschetta     
 Description : 
 A fruity twist on bruschetta.   Balsamic vinegar, basil leaves, and goat cheese perfectly complement the sweetness of the fruit.
 
 Ingredients : 
 6-12  baguettes , cut into bite-sized shapes 
 10-20  strawberries , diced 
 3-6  peaches , diced 
 2-4 tablespoons raspberry white  balsamic vinegar 
 3-6 tablespoons  honey , optional 
 1-2 cup fresh  basil  leaves, cut into small pieces 
 6-12 ounces cranberry  goat cheese   or  (1 pack  cream cheese ) 
 Directions : 
 Preheat an oven to 350° F. Place bread on a baking sheet and bake for 4-5 minutes, or until toasted. 
 In a moderate-sized bowl, combine strawberries, peaches, honey (optional), and balsamic vinegar. 
 Spread goat cheese onto each slice of bread. 
 With a slotted spoon, place the fruit mixture on top of the cheese.  
 Garnish with basil
