Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  wild rice , uncooked 
 1 cup  water 
 1  green onion 
 1 small  garlic  clove, minced 
 1 tsp  vegetable oil 
 ¼ cup  pine nuts  or any chopped nut 
 ½ tsp dried  thyme 
 Directions : 
 Cook the  wild rice  in the  water  until tender, about 1 hour. 
 Add more  water  if needed. 
 Meanwhile, saute the  onion ,  garlic ,  nuts  and  thyme  in the  oil . 
 Add the  wild rice . 
 Simmer for 10 minutes to blend the flavors. 
 Cool enough to stuff into quail or one  Cornish hen  (or serve with  chicken ). 
 Nutritional information : 
 ½ recipe - 182 calories, 1 bread exchange, 2 fat 15 grams carbohydrate, 4 grams protein, 11 grams fat 138 mg sodium, 114 mg potassium, 0 cholesterol
