Ingredients : 
 4  lamb shoulder  or  blade chops , ¾" thick 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp dried  rosemary 
 1 tsp  Dijon mustard 
 1 tbsp  white vinegar 
 ⅓ cup  mango jam 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients except chops. 
 Cook slowly, stirring until melted. 
 Barbeque chops brushing with glaze while cooking.
