Ingredients : 
 dash of  light rum 
 sparkling  water 
 ½ cup  mango  purée 
 Directions : 
 In a tall glasses, combine mango and rum. Stir together. 
 Fill each glass with sparkling water. 
 Serve with an iced-tea spoon.
