 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 6 fresh jalapenos (or as many as desired), split down the middle, seeds removed (so they're left as 'jackets') 
 1 package Philly  cream  cheese- or other brand 
 Directions : 
 Use aluminum foil to line cookie sheet 
 Using spoon, fill jalapeno 'jackets' with  cream cheese 
 Arrange jalapenos on baking sheet in single rows 
 Bake for about 15 mins or until  peppers  are cooked and  Cheese  is crusted and browned.
