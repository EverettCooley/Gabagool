 Description : 
 This Maroccan sweet bread is ideal as a snack. It is suitable for vegetarians and is absolutely gorgeous. Enjoy!
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 teaspoon honey 
 360ml  water  or 1 cup + 1/3 cup + 2Tablespoons 
 15g dry active  yeast 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 450g strong white  flour  or 3 1/2 cups 
 Directions : 
 Mix the  water , honey and  yeast  together in a small bowl stand for 10 minutes.
 Sieve the  flour  and  salt  into a large mixing bowl. Make a hole in the middle and pour in the  yeast  mixture. Mix until you have a firm dough.
 Take the dough out of the bowl and  flour  the work surface. Knead it for 10 minutes, until it begins to firm up.
 flour  a baking tray in preparation for baking.
 Shape the dough into a loaf and place it on the baking tray. Leave it in a cool, dark place for 30 minutes, by which time it should have doubled in size.
 Preheat the oven to 375F.
 Put the loaf in the oven and bake for about 45 minutes.
 Serve when it has cooled.
