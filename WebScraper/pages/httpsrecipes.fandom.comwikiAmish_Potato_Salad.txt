Ingredients : 
 1 cup raw  bacon 
 1  onion , chopped 
 3 tbsp  flour 
 1½ cups  water 
 ⅔ cup  vinegar 
 ½ cup  parsley , minced 
 ⅔ cup  sugar 
 2 tsp  celery seed 
 6 cups almost done  red potatoes , sliced 
 Directions : 
 Sauté  onion  and  bacon  together, then add  flour  and cook together for one or two minutes. 
 Mix  water ,  vinegar ,  parsley ,  sugar  and  celery seed  and add to  bacon  mixture. 
 Pour over almost done  potatoes  and bake for 45 minutes in a 375 °F oven.
