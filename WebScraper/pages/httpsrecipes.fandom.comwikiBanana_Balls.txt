 Ingredients : 
 4 big  banana 
 1 cup  cream of rice 
 3 cups  coconut  powder 
 1 pinch  cardamom 
 Â½ cup  powdered sugar 
 Directions : 
 Slice  bananas  into small pieces. 
 Put them into a pot and melt them. 
 Stir often. 
 Add a little  water  if  bananas  stick to pot. 
 When  bananas  have completely melted, add coconut powder and  cream of rice . 
 Continue cooking for 10 minutes. 
 Stir well and scrape bottom of pan. 
 Then add  powdered sugar  and  cardamom . 
 Let mixture cool down. 
 Make  banana  balls and roll them in coconut powder.
