Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 1 tablespoon  flour 
 2  onions 
 1 teaspoon  sugar 
 1 tablespoon  tomato sauce 
 1 â€“ 2 tablespoons  vinegar  or  lemon juice 
 2 cups  beef broth  or  water 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Chop the  onions  and fry in  butter  until yellowish. 
 Add the  flour , fry a little and then add the broth, stirring continuously. 
 Add  salt ,  sugar  and  tomato  sauce and let boil for 10 minutes. 
 Then add the  lemon juice  and simmer until thickened. 
 To be served with  beef  or  pork .
