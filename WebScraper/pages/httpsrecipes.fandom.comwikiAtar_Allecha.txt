Description : 
 Atar Allecha is a spiced  green pea  purée.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅓ cup  onion , chopped 
 20 ml  garlic , finely chopped 
 1 tbsp  corn oil 
 1 cup  split green peas , soaked, cooked, drained, mas 
 ½ tsp  ground turmeric 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 3 tsp hot  green pepper , finely 
 1 cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Soak  Peas  for one hour. Cook for ½ hour. Drain. Mash. Set aside. 
 In dry pan over moderate low heat, stir fry the  Onion  and  garlic  for 2 minutes. 
 Add  oil  and stir fry one minute more. 
 Add the mashed  peas , turmeric,  salt , and  chile . Mix well. 
 Add the  water  and cook 3 – 4 minutes longer to reduce the mixture to a thick, green, well spiced purée. 
 Serve with warm  Injera .
