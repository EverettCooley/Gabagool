Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 1 cup 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  blackberries  and/or  strawberries 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 1 tbs  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Simmer, covered, 1 pound blackberries, ¼ cup sugar and 1 tbs lemon juice for 12 minutes. 
 Press through a strainer to remove seeds. 
 Serve chilled over ice cream or plain cake.
