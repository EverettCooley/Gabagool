Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  rice vinegar 
 1 tbsp  honey 
 2 tsp  white miso 
 ½ tsp dry  mustard 
 ½ tsp dried  oregano 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 ¼ cup  safflower  or other mild  vegetable oil 
 ¼ cup coarsely chopped  onion 
 1 medium clove  garlic , minced 
 1½ cups fresh  basil  leaves 
 ¼ cup  silken tofu 
 Directions : 
 In blender or food processor, combine  onion  and  garlic  and pulse on and off until finely chopped. 
 Add remaining ingredients along with ¼ cup  water ; blend until smooth. 
 If it’s thick, add more  water  until mixture reaches desired consistency.
