 Directions : 
 Beat the  yolks  with the  sugar , gradually add the  flour , then  rum  and bread crumbs. 
 After everything has been mixed well, add the peeled and grated  apples , mix some more and then add the whipped  egg whites . 
 Pour the mixture into a buttered and dusted (with bread crumbs) pan and bake at medium heat. 
 When done, put on a platter and pour some syrup over it while still hot.
