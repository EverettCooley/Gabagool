Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 stick  butter 
 1½ cup  graham cracker  crumbs 
 6 ounce  semisweet chocolate  chips 
 1½ cup  coconut 
 1 cup  pecans , chopped 
 1 can (12 ounce)  sweetened condensed milk 
 Directions : 
 First layer - melt butter in a 9 x 12-inch baking pan, and sprinkle with graham cracker crumbs. 
 Press down to form crust. 
 Second layer - chocolate chips, third layer - coconut, fourth layer - pecans. 
 Pour milk on top. 
 Bake at 325°F for 20 – 25 minutes. 
 Cool and cut into squares.
