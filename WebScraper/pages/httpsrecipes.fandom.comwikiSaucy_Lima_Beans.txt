 Description : 
 Easy  lima beans  from the crockpot, made with canned  lima beans  or  butter beans ,  tomatoes ,  bacon , and smoked  sausage . 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cans  butter beans  or  lima beans , drained 
 1 can  tomatoes 
 1  onion  sliced 
 1 green or  red bell pepper  sliced 
 1 (14 oz.) bottle  ketchup 
 1 cup  brown sugar 
 12 slices  bacon , fried, drained, and crumbled 
 1 pound  kielbasa , sliced 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together in crockpot 
 Cover and cook on low 4 to 5 hours, or on high for 2Â½ hours.
