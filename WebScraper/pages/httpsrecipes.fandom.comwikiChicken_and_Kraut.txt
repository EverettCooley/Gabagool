Description : 
 Contributed by  PressureCookerRecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4  chicken breasts , boneless and skinless (Can also make this with Polish sausage or country-style ribs instead of chicken.) 
 1 (32 oz) jar  kraut 
 1 can (16 oz) whole  potatoes 
 2 tbsp  brown sugar 
 1 cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Brown chicken breasts in small amount of oil (or PAM if desired) in 4 quart pressure cooker or saucepan. 
 Remove chicken and place ½ jar of kraut in pan. 
 Add water and ½ brown sugar. 
 Add chicken and potatoes, rest of kraut and brown sugar. 
 Bring up to pressure and cook for 20 minutes under high pressure.
