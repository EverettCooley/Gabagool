 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 c. hot cooked  corn  meal  mush 
 pinch "malaguette",  oregano  or  marjoram 
 1/4 c. chopped  Onion 
 6 cloves 
 1 c. chopped  pepper 
 1/4 c. grated  Cheese 
 2 c. chopped  tomatoes 
 1/2 c.  milk  (optional) 
 1 Tbs.  salt 
 1/2 c. chopped  parsley 
 3 Tbs.  oil 
 Directions : 
 Gently fry  Onion  and  pepper  in  oil . 
 Add  tomatoes  and spices and simmer 10 minutes. 
 Pour cooked  mush  into this mixture. 
 Stir and pour in buttered pan or oven dish. 
 Sprinkle with  Cheese . 
 Bake about 20 minutes in hot oven.(450 degrees)
