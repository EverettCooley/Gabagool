 Ingredients : 
 1 tbsp red  palm oil 
 ½  onion , chopped 
 3 cloves of  garlic , chopped 
 1 piment, minced 
 6 big  okra  ( gombo ), sliced 
 1 maggi  chicken bouillon  cube 
 2½ cups  water 
 ½ cup cooked  rice 
 ½ cup cooked  lentils 
 1 tsp crushed dried  okra 
 2  tomatoes , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Sauté  onion  and  garlic  in  oil  until soft. 
 Add piment,  gombo ,  water  and Maggi. 
 Cook until  gombo  is soft. 
 Add remaining ingredients and cook 10–15 minutes. 
 Season to taste.
