Ingredients : 
 4  chicken  breast halves, boneless 
 1 teaspoon  cumin  seeds 
 1 teaspoon  fennel seeds 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 2 tablespoons  ghee  or  butter 
 1 cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Put  water ,  ghee , and spices in a deep pan. 
 Place  chicken  in  water . 
 water  should just cover  chicken . 
 Add more  water  to keep that level. 
 Keep pan covered. 
 Simmer for 45 minutes or to desired doneness. 
 Cut in strips or cubes. 
 Use in any recipe that calls for cooked  chicken  such as curries and cold salads.
