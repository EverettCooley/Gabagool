 Ingredients : 
 5 tablespoon sesame  tahini 
 2 tablespoon  tamari 
 5 tablespoon  water 
 2 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 2 slices vegan bread 
 ¼ cup  zucchini 
 1  green onion  ( scallion ) 
 1 teaspoon  olive oil 
 1 teaspoon spicy brown vegan  mustard 
 1 teaspoon  balsamic vinegar 
 Directions : 
 Istanbul Dressing : 
 Blend tahini, tamari,  water  and  lemon juice . I'm not sure about the amount of  lemon juice . Use less and see if you need more. 
 Sandwich : 
 Chop  zucchini  into thin rings. 
 Chop a whole  green onion  (stem and all). 
 Sauté  zucchini  and  onion  in  olive oil . 
 Add  balsamic vinegar . After approx. 5 minutes, put on your bread. 
 Spread vegan bread with Istanbul dressing and spicy brown  mustard
