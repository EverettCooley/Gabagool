In Romanian: Plachie de crap
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs / 1 kg  carp 
 4 tablespoons  oil 
 5 – 6 big  onions 
 1 tablespoon  tomato sauce 
 2 tablespoons  flour 
 2 – 3  peppercorns 
 2 – 3  juniper berries 
 1 cup  wine 
 a few  lemon  slices 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Clean and wash the fish, cut into pieces,  salt  and let sit for 15 – 20 minutes. 
 Then dry each piece with a clean cloth, dredge with  flour  and fry in hot  oil  on both sides. 
 Fry the chopped  onion  in the  oil  that remained from the frying. 
 When the  onion  starts to turn yellow, add the  tomato  sauce, wine,  salt ,  pepper ,  juniper berries ,  lemon  slices and pieces. 
 Place in the oven at low temperature and let it bake until the liquid is reduced. 
 You may serve it warm or cold.
