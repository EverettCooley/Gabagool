Description : 
 Contributed by Lilly at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tub ready to spread type  lemon  frosting 
 1 cup frozen  whipped topping , thawed 
 1 tbsp grated  lemon peel 
 Directions : 
 In a small bowl, beat frosting at medium speed 1 minute. 
 Add whipped topping: beat until light and fluffy. 
 Stir in  lemon peel . 
 Frost cake.
