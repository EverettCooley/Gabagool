 Makes 1¾ cup. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup imitation  sour cream  or low-fat  yogurt 
 ½ cup drained crushed  pineapple 
 ¼ cup finely chopped  pecans 
 1 tbsp  maraschino cherry  juice 
 ⅛ tbsp  powdered ginger  or ½ tbsp chopped fresh  ginger 
 assorted fresh  fruit 
 Directions : 
 Stir together the  sour cream , crushed  pineapple ,  pecans ,  cherry juice  and  ginger . 
 Spoon into a serving bowl and chill. 
 To serve, place bowl on a chop plate and surround with fresh fruits such as whole  strawberries , large  grapes ,  pineapple  spears, and slices of fresh  peach . 
 Use the fruit as dippers or you can provide toothpicks to pick up the fruit for dunking. 
 Notes : 
 Make this recipe as directed and then freeze for a delicious and refreshing dessert.
