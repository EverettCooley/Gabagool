 Ingredients : 
 1 oz white  creme de cacao 
 1 oz  Galliano ®  herbal liqueur 
 3/4 oz  vodka 
 2 oz  milk 
 1 tsp  Grenadine  syrup 
 2 tbsp  vanilla   ice   cream 
 1 1/2 oz  whipped cream 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients (except  cream ) briefly with half a glassful of crushed  ice  in a collins glass. 
 Top with  whipped cream . 
 Serve with a  cherry .
