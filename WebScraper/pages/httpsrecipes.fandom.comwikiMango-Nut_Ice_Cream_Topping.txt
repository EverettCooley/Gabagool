Ingredients : 
 1½ quarts  mango  chunks 
 3½ cups  sugar 
 1 quart roasted  peanuts  or  pecans 
 ¼ cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Bring  mangos  and  water  to a boil in heavy pan. 
 Simmer for 10 – 15 minutes until thickened and has smooth consistency, stirring frequently. 
 Add  nuts  and cool. 
 Serve over  ice cream .
