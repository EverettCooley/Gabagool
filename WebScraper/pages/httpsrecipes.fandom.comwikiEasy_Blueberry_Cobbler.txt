Ingredients : 
 3 cups fresh  blueberries 
 vegetable  cooking spray 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1  egg , beaten ( egg substitute  may be used) 
 1 cup  all-purpose flour 
 sugar substitute  to equal 1½ cups  sugar 
 ¼ cup + 2 tbsp reduced-calorie  margarine , melted 
 Directions : 
 Place  blueberries  in bottom of a 10 x 6 x 2 baking dish coated with  cooking spray ; sprinkle with  lemon juice . 
 Combine  egg ,  flour , and  sugar substitute ; stir until mixture resembles coarse meal. 
 Sprinkle over  blueberries . 
 Drizzle melted  margarine  over top. 
 Bake at 375°F for 30 minutes. 
 Serve warm.
