Description : 
 A great way to give the kids veggies they need.	This recipe does not specify to use the lower sodium cream of celery soup but it is a better choice. Take care, Gloria
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes For Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Source: SparkPeople user SMERSNICK 
 Number of Servings: 8 
 Ingredients : 
 2 bunches  broccoli , diced 
 1 cup  asparagus , diced 
 1 cup fresh  spinach 
 1 slice each  Swiss  and  provolone  cheeses 
 2 slices  mozzarella  cheese 
 1 can  cream of celery  soup 
 1 cup  non-fat milk 
 1 tbsp  garlic powder 
 salt  to taste 
 2 tbsp grated  Parmesan  for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Place first three ingredients in a bowl: broccoli, asparagus and spinach. 
 Shred the Swiss, provolone and mozzarella cheese slices and add to bowl. 
 Add cream of celery soup, milk, garlic powder and salt to taste. 
 Mix gently and pour into a large casserole dish. 
 Bake covered at 350°F for approx 35 – 40 minutes. 
 Add Parmesan for garnish before plating.
