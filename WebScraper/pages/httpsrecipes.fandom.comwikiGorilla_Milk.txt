Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  light rum 
 ½ oz  coffee liqueur 
 ½ oz  Irish cream liqueur 
 ½ oz  crème de banane 
 1 oz  light cream 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Shake with  ice  and pour into a hurricane glass ⅔ full of  ice . 
 Top with a slice  banana .
