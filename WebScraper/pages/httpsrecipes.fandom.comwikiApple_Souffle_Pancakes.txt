Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup  oat bran 
 1/2 cup  flour  ( Wheat ,  Rye ,  Buckwheat  or  rice ) 
 2 tbsp ground  flax seed  (optional) 
 1 tbsp  baking powder 
 2 tbsp  Sugar 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 1/4 tsp  nutmeg 
 2 small  Apples , tart or crisp, cored and finely sliced or diced 
 1 cup  milk , low-fat or skim 
 2 tbsp  milk  (only if you add  flax seed ) 
 2  egg  yolks 
 1 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 4  egg  whites 
 Directions : 
 Mix dry ingredients together. 
 Combine all wet ingredients except  egg  whites, including the  Apple . 
 Beat  egg  whites until stiff. Combine wet and dry ingredients, stirring only until barely mixed. 
 Gently fold in the beaten  egg  whites. Keep on folding until no large chunks of  egg  whites can be seen. 
 Heat a non-stick griddle or frying pan until a drop of  water  skitters on it. 
 Spray lightly with  cooking spray , or wipe on a little  oil  with a paper towel. 
 Scoop out large spoonfuls of batter and instantly spread slightly on the skillet. 
 Cook over medium heat to give  Apples  a chance to cook through. 
 When browned, turn and brown other side. 
 Serve with syrup and/or  butter , or for a healthier dish, with  applesauce .
