Ingredients : 
 1 cup  nut milk  ( I use Califia unsweetened almond milk) 
 1 cup  water 
 1 scoop vanilla  protein powder  (I use Clean Program) 
 2 medjool  dates , pitted (optional) 
 1 cup riced  cauliflower , frozen 
 2 ounces  coconut  meat, frozen (I use inner-eco fresh young coconut) 
 1 tablespoon  peanut butter  (this is where that buttery flavor comes from!) 
 1 teaspoon pure  vanilla extract 
 pinch of pink salt 
 2 handfuls of ice cubes 
 Instructions : 
 Blend all ingredients until smooth and then sip, sip, sip!
