Description : 
 The sauce is fabulous!
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lbs large  shrimp 
 2 tbsp extra virgin  olive oil 
 2 tbsp freshly squeezed  lemon juice 
 1 tsp  Tabasco sauce 
 1 tsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 ½ tsp  cayenne pepper 
 2 tsp  salt 
 2 tsp freshly ground  black pepper 
 ¼ lbs  unsalted butter 
 Directions : 
 Rinse  shrimp  in cold  water  and place in 9 by 13 inch glass baking dish (single layer). 
 Drizzle unpeeled  shrimp  with  oil ,  lemon juice ,  tabasco  and  Worcestershire sauce . 
 Cover generously with  cayenne ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Broil approximately 4 minutes. 
 Turn and continue broiling until shell separates from  shrimp , approximately 5 minutes depending on distance from broiler. 
 Remove  shrimp . 
 Stir sauce and add more  butter  or  lemon juice  if needed. 
 Serve immediately with lots of good  French bread .
