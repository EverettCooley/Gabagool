Recipe by Freedawgnight/Kallie Inc. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  ground turkey  or  beef 
 1 jar  cheese  sauce 
 1 jar hot  salsa 
 assorted spices including but not limited to:  garlic  and  onion powder ,  paprika , red pepper,  black pepper ,  salt . 
 1 dozen flour  tortillas 
 Directions : 
 After Campfire has burned down and you have good coals to cook with, the hotter the better, heat large well seasoned cast-iron skillet. 
 Cook meat, draining off the fat continuously 
 When meat is minutes from being done—add spices and mix together. Then salsa and mix together, and finally  Cheese  sauce, mix everything together. 
 Cover for a couple of minutes—keep your eye on it 
 Warm tortillas on grille flipping from side to side and roll camping burritos the perfect size to suit your liking.
