 Ingredients : 
 500 	 g  minced meat 
 2 	large  onions , finely chopped 
 4 	 potatoes , finely chopped 
 1 	teaspoon minced  garlic 
 1 	teaspoon minced  ginger 
 1 	teaspoon  black pepper 
 4 	tablespoons HP Steak sauce 
 2 	tablespoons  tomato   ketchup 
 1 	tablespoon  worcestershire sauce 
 2 	tablespoons  oil 
 puff pastry 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  and put in all the ingrediants except for sauces.Keep cookimg for about half an hour. This mixture needs to be quite dry.Now add all the sauces and mix. Leave it to cool. 
 Roll  puff pastry  and put the mixture in it. You can make it into rolls or any shape and bake in oven till the pastry is cooked.
