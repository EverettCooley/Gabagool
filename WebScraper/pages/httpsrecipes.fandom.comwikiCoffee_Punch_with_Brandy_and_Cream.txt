coffee  Punch With  brandy  And Cream
 
 8 Cups  strong coffee , Chilled 
 1 1/2 Cups  milk 
 sugar  to Taste (about 3 to 4 T)
 
 2 T.  vanilla 
 1/4 Cup  brandy 
 1/4 Cup  Kahlua  Liqueur 
 24  coffee   ice  Cubes (recipe Follows) 
 1/2 Gallon Mocha  Ice cream 
 1 Cup Cream, Whipped 
 
Combine  coffee ,  milk ,  sugar  And  vanilla  And Refrigerate Until Ready to Serve. Pour Mixture Into a Punch Bowl And Stir in  brandy ,  Kahlua  And  coffee   ice . Float  Ice cream  And  whipped cream  on Top. Sprinkle With  cinnamon  And Powdered Instant  coffee . Makes Bout 24 Servings.To Make  coffee   ice : Fill  ice  Cube Tray With Freshly Brewed  coffee  And Freeze Until Firm. Remove From Freezer at Serving Time.Source: With Love From Darling's Kitchen
