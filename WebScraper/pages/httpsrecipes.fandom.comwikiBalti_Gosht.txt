 Description : 
 Spice mixture for an Indian stir-fry in a spicy sauce. 
 Ingredients : 
 Meat 
 Seafood 
 Vegetables 
 Spices 
 Chilies 
 Chopped  tomatoes 
 Instructions : 
 Meat, seafood, and vegies are fried with spices, chopped  tomatoes , and chilies, and  vinegar . 
 Then simmered in  water  until tender 
 Origin : 
 from the northern most region of Pakistan, called Baltistan. 
 Buying Guide : 
 Look for pre-packaged Spice Mix
