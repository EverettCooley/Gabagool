Description : 
 A strong cocktail that includes the first mass produced soft drink in the United States of America, Moxie, in it's original flavor.
 
 Ingredients : 
 12 ounces Original Flavor  Moxie ® 
 12 ounces  Crown Royal ® 
 ½ ounce  brandy 
 ½ ounce Pat O'Brien's  Sweet and Sour Mix ® 
 Directions : 
 Pre-chill 25 ounce brandy snifter. 
 Shake all ingredients well. 
 Strain and serve.
