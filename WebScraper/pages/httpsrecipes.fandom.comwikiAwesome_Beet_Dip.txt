Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  green onion 
 1 pound  beets , peeled and quartered 
 1 bunch  watercress , stemmed and chopped 
 6 ounces  cream cheese , softened 
 2 teaspoons fresh  lemon juice 
 1 teaspoon prepared  horseradish , drained 
 1 teaspoon dried  dill 
 Directions : 
 In a food processor, process  onions  and beets until finely chopped. 
 Add the watercress and process 5 seconds. 
 Add  cream cheese , lemon juice, horseradish and  dill  and process until well blended. 
 Spoon into container and refrigerate until ready to use. 
 Serve with raw vegetables.
