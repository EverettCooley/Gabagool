Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 racks of  ribs 
 Dry rub : 
 2½ tbsp  black pepper 
 2½ tbsp  paprika 
 1¼ tsp  ground red pepper 
 1¼ tsp  garlic powder 
 1½ tsp  celery salt 
 ¾ tsp  dry mustard 
 BBQ sauce : 
 1 cup  catsup 
 ¼ cup  soy sauce 
 2 tbsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 ¾ tsp  garlic powder 
 ¼ cup apple  cider vinegar 
 ¼ cup  apple juice 
 ¾ tsp medium-sized  onion  finely grated 
 ¼ medium-sized  Golden Delicious apple  grated 
 2 tsp grated  green bell pepper 
 Directions : 
 Dry rub : 
 Mix together ingredients for dry rub. 
 Place on ribs coating both sides. 
 Wrap in foil for 8 – 10 hours. 
 BBQ sauce : 
 Combine all ingredients and bring to a boil. 
 Add onion apple and green peppers simmer, uncovered to the desired thickness. 
 Ribs : 
 To smoke ribs 5½ hours at 180 – 200 °F baste with apple juice during cooking. 
 Baste with the sauce during the last 15 minutes. 
 Enjoy.
