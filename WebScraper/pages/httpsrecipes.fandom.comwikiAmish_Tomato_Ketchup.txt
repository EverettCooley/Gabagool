      tomato     
 Ingredients : 
 3 quarts  tomato juice 
 1 pint  apple cider vinegar 
 4 – 5 cups  sugar 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  pepper 
 3 drops  clove oil 
 5 drops  cinnamon oil 
 4 tablespoons ground dry  mustard 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients. 
 Boil for 2½ hours. 
 Pour into jars.
