Potato   Cheese  Chips
 
 1 T.  margarine 
 1 Pkg (9 Oz)  Potato  Chips 
 2 T. Grated  Parmesan cheese  or Sharp  American cheese 
 
Preheat Skillet to 300. Put in  butter , Fill With  Potato  Chips. Sprinkle With Grated  Cheese . Cover Skillet For 5 to 10 Minutes. Uncover . Stir And Toss Chips. Reduce Skillet to Lowest Temperature. Keep Warm. Let Guests Help Themselves.Source: my Old Recipes
