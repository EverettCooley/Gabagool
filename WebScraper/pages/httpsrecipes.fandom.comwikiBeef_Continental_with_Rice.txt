Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 pounds round  Steak , thinly sliced 
 1/2 cup seasoned  flour 
 3 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 1 cup chopped  Onion 
 1/2 cup dry  red wine 
 1 1/2 cups  beef broth 
 1 4-ounce can sliced  Mushrooms  and liquid 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 1-3/8-ounce package  onion soup mix 
 Directions : 
 Tenderize  Steak . 
 Cut into 1-inch strips. 
 Coat with seasoned  flour . 
 Brown in  oil . 
 Add  Onion  and cook 2 to 3 minutes longer. 
 Stir in wine, broth and  Mushrooms  with liquid. 
 Bring to a boil. 
 Cover, reduce heat and simmer 20 to 30 minutes or until tender. 
 Serve over beds of hot  rice . Garnish with  tomato  wedges and fresh  parsley .
