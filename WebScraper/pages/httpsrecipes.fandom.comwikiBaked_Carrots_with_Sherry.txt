Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group  and also   Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Original recipe 
 Ready in: 30 – 60 minutes 
 Serves/Makes: 6 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups grated  carrots 
 2 tablespoons light  margarine , melted 
 2 tablespoons dry  sherry 
 1 tablespoon fresh  lemon juice 
 1 tablespoon chopped  chives 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Combine all ingredients except chives, stirring well. 
 Put in a 1 quart casserole dish and sprinkle with chives. 
 Bake, covered, for 30 minutes.
