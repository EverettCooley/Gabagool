Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz  bourbon 
 1 oz  sloe gin 
 garnish:  cherry 
 glassware: cocktail glass 
 Directions : 
 Shake all the ingredients in a shaker with  ice  and strain into a cocktail glass. 
 Garnish with a  cherry .
