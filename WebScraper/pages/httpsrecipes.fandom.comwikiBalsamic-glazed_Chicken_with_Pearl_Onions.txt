Description : 
 Recipe from  http://www.sneakykitchen.com 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2  chicken breasts , skinless with bone in, halved 
 ½ lb frozen  pearl  or small white  onions , thawed 
 1  green  or  red bell pepper 
 2  garlic  cloves, minced 
 1 tbsp fresh  rosemary , chopped, or 1 tsp dried 
 ⅓ cup  balsamic vinegar 
 1 tbsp  light brown sugar 
 2 tsp  olive oil 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  black pepper , freshly ground 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 400°F. 
 Cut  bell pepper  into 1" pieces. 
 In shallow roasting pan, arrange  chicken ,  onions  and  bell pepper . 
 Sprinkle  garlic  and  rosemary  on top. 
 In a small bowl, whisk together  balsamic vinegar ,  sugar ,  olive oil ,  salt  and  pepper , and pour over the  chicken  and vegetables. 
 Bake uncovered 40 minutes, or until the  chicken  is cooked through.
