Ingredients : 
 1½ lbs lean  ground beef 
 3 slices soft white  bread , torn into very tiny pieces 
 1 cup  V-8 juice 
 1 medium  onion , finely chopped 
 2 tablespoons chopped  parsley 
 1  egg , beaten 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  pepper 
 1 package Lipton  onion soup mix  (do not substitute) 
 1 – 2 cloves minced  garlic 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F 
 Mix ingredients thoroughly, press into small roasting pan or loaf pan. 
 Bake uncovered for 1½ hours.
