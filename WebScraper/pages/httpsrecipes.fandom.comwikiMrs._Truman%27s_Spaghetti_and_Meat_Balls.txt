This recipe came from the kitchen of Bess Truman and is one of President Truman's favorite recipes. Mrs. Truman had a well-equipped kitchen and often prepared breakfast or lunch for her family. The Trumans also employed several African American women who cooked for the family.
 
 Mrs. Truman's Recipes  from the Harry S. Truman National Historic Site, public domain government resource—original source of recipe 
 Ingredients 
 
 spaghetti 
 hamburger 
 onion 
 Sauce 
 1½ cup  water 
 1 x 16 oz canned  tomato 
 1 cup  tomato paste 
 3 tablespoon  oil 
 1 chopped  onion 
 1 clove  garlic 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 Directions 
 Cook spaghetti in salt water then blanche and strain well. 
 Put aside. 
 Mix sauce ingredients together in pot and simmer for 30 minutes. 
 Brown hamburger, onion and garlic before mixing together; cook for 30 minutes. 
 Stir often to keep from burning.
