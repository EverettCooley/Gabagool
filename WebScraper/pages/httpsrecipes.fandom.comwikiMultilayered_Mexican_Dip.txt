 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can  refried beans  (14 oz) 
 1 1/4 cup  sour cream 
 1/4 tsp  cumin ,Ground 
 1/4 tsp Tabasco 
 1/3 cup  Onion ,Finely Chopped 
 1 tsp  lime juice 
 1/4 tsp  red pepper flakes 
 1/3 cup  green onions 
 1/2 cup  black olives ,Sliced 
 2  tomatoes ,Chopped 
 2 cup  Cheddar   Cheese ,Shredded 
 Directions : 
 In bowl, blend together beans, 1/4 cup of  sour cream ,  cumin  and hot pepper sauce . 
 Spread evenly on tray 12" in diameter and at least 1 1/2 deep. 
 In bowl, mix remaining  sour cream , chopped  Onion ,  lime juice  and  red pepper flakes ; spread over  refried bean  layer. 
 Garnish with concentric rings of  green onions  on outside,  black olives ,  tomatoes  and  Cheese  in centre. 
 Cover and refrigerate till serving time. Tortilla wedges may be used as dippers.
