 Description : 
 Makes 8 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 medium  onion , chopped 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 1 x 10½-ounce can condensed  cheddar cheese soup 
 1 x 6-ounce roll pasteurized  process cheese  food with  garlic 
 1 x 8-ounce can  mushroom  stems and pieces 
 1 x 10-ounce package frozen chopped  broccoli , thawed and drained 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 1 x 3-ounce can  French fried onions 
 Directions : 
 Cook  onion  in  butter  until soft but not brown. 
 Add soup and  cheese . Cook over low heat, stirring, until  cheese  melts. 
 Add  mushrooms  with liquid,  broccoli  and  rice . 
 Turn into shallow casserole; top with  French fried onions . 
 Bake at 350°F for 15 to 20 minutes, or until hot and bubbly.
