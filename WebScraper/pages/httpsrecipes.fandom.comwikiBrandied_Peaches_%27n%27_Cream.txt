 Ingredients : 
 3/4 oz  peach  schnapps 
 1/2 oz  peach   brandy 
 1 1/2 oz  cream of coconut 
 1 scoop  vanilla   ice   cream 
 8 oz crushed  ice 
 Directions : 
 Mix well in a blender until smooth. Serve in a specialty glass. 
 Garnish with a  cinnamon  stick or fresh  peach  wedge.
