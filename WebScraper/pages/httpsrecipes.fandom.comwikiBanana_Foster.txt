Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 scoops vanilla  ice cream 
 1½ oz  spiced rum 
 ½ oz  banana liqueur 
 1  banana 
 Directions : 
 Mix until smooth and pour into a large brandy snifter. 
 Sprinkle  cinnamon  on top.
