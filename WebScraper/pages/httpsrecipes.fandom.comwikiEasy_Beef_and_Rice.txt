Description : 
 Makes 4 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ pound lean  ground beef 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 2 cups cooked  rice 
 1 x 10¾-ounce can condensed  cream of mushroom  soup 
 1 x 8-ounce can stewed  tomatoes , undrained, chopped 
 1 x 7-ounce can whole kernel  corn 
 ½ teaspoon  garlic powder 
 ½ teaspoon  chili powder 
 1 cup (4-ounces) shredded  Cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Brown  beef  and  onion  in large non-stick skillet over medium-high heat. 
 Stir in  rice ,  mushroom  soup,  tomatoes ,  corn ,  garlic powder  and  chili powder . 
 Pour mixture into buttered 2-quart baking dish; bake at 350°F for 20 minutes. 
 Top with  cheese ; bake an additional 5 to 10 minutes.
