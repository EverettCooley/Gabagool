       Curry Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 Curry Dressing 
 2  eggs  -- hard cooked 
 16 ounces  herring  in wine sauce—drained, chopped 
 1 cup  ham  -- chopped 
 ½ cup  cucumber  -- diced 
 2 cups  rice  -- cooked 
 crisp  lettuce  leaves 
 fennel 
 Directions : 
 Whip  cream  until soft peaks form. 
 Add remaining ingredients.
