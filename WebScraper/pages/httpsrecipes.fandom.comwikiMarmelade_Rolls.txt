Description : 
 In Romanian: Rulouri cu marmelada
 
 Ingredients : 
 5 oz / 150 g well drained  cottage cheese 
 5 oz / 150 g  butter 
 5 oz / 150 g  flour 
 2 tablespoons  confectioner's sugar 
 salt 
 Â¼ teaspoon  baking soda 
 marmalade 
 egg  for washing 
 granulated sugar 
 Directions : 
 Mix together the  cheese ,  sugar ,  salt  and  baking soda . 
 Then add  flour  and knead well. 
 Roll into a sheet the thickness as for noodles. 
 Cut into medium sized rectangles, spread marmalade on them and shape each rectangle into a roll. 
 Wash with  egg , sprinkle a little  granulated sugar  and bake. 
 They may also be filled with  cottage cheese  mixed with 1  egg  and a little  sugar. .
