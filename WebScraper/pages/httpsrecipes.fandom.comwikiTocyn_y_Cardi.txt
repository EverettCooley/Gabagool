Description : 
 Fried Oat Cakes
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tbsp  rolled oats 
 2 tbsp of  milk 
 pinch of  salt 
 1 tbsp  flour 
 1 tbsp  baking powder 
 bacon fat 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl, combine all of the dry ingredients; stir in the milk. 
 Using your hands, form batter into small cake-sized patties; fry in bacon fat, turning once to brown evenly. 
 The cakes go well with strips of crisp bacon.
