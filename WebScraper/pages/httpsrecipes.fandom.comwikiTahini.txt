 About tahini : 
 Tahini is used in Greek cooking. It is a thick paste made of ground  sesame seed . It's used to flavor various dishes such as  Hummus  and  Baba Ghanoush . It may be used as a spread, sauce, garnish, etc. Also known as 'Tehina' and 'Tahina'.
