banana - orange  Smoothie
 
 1  banana , Peeled 
 1 Large  orange , Peeled And Seeded 
 2 Cups  vanilla -Flavored  soy milk 
 1 Teaspoon  ground ginger 
 
Directions
 
Place  banana ,  orange ,  soy milk , And  ginger  in an Electric Blender. ProcessUntil Ingredients Are Blended And Smooth.
