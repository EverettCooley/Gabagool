Ingredients : 
 1 (8 oz.) pkg.  cream cheese 
 1/4 c.  ketchup 
 1 1/2 tbsp.  milk 
 1 tbsp.  French dressing 
 2 tsp. minced  onion 
 Directions : 
 Let cream cheese get soft and combine all ingredients. 
 Mix with mixer until smooth. 
 Notes : 
 It's best if it sits overnight before serving.
