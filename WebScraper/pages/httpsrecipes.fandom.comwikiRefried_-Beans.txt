 Ingredients : 
 2 Cups Cooked  pinto beans  (including liquid) 
 1/4 Cup  shortening 
 salt 
 white pepper 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Heat  shortening  on high in a large skillet. When  shortening  is hot, slowly add beans.
Allow beans to boil for approximately 5 minutes, do not stir.
When the beans have warmed completely, begin to mash with a spatula or spoon.
Continue to mash the beans until desired consistency is achieved.
 Add  salt  and  white pepper  to taste.
