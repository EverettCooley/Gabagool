 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 Lb.s  cornstarch 
 3 3/4 cup  water 
 Directions : 
 1 Warning: non-edible green  food coloring  add  food coloring  to the  water  to desired color, stir into the  cornstarch . 
2 When the  cornstarch  is dissolved, drop the whole batch onto a tray for the child to play with. 
3 This is almost a liquid, so makes a fun change from other doughs. 
4 Clean up is the easiest of all doughs, as when it dries it is simply swept up. 
5 Any that is left can be plaed in a clean glass jar and let dry. 
6 It can then be reconstituted by simply adding  water
