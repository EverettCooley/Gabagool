Desription : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Gee Estate in Quinlan, Texas in 1994.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  white sauce mix 
 1 cup cool water 
 ½ cup grated  Swiss cheese 
 Mix : 
 2 cups  instant dry milk 
 1 cup  unbleached flour 
 2 teaspoons  salt 
 1 cup  butter 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl combine dry milk, flour and salt then mix well. 
 With a pastry blender cut in butter until mixture resembles fine crumbs. 
 Combine mix and water in small sauce pan and cook over low heat until smooth. 
 Season with pepper, herbs and spices of your choice. 
 Stir in cheese and stir until cheese is melted.
