 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 or 3 avocados 
 1 medium Vadelia  Onion 
 1 can of whole kernel  corn 
 salt  7  pepper 
 mayo 
 Directions : 
 Peel and cut the avocados in squares. Cut the  Onion  in thin slices.Drain the  corn . Mix all ingredients, add  mayo  (about half a cup) and  salt  &  pepper . You can add a couple of drops of  lime juice  to avoid the  avocado  to turn black. Enjoy!
