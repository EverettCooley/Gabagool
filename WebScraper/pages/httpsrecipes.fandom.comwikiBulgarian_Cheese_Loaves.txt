 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 kg/2.2 lb Self Raising  flour 
 450g/1 lb Bulgarian  white cheese  or  feta , crumbled 
 480ml/16fl.oz. Plain  yoghurt 
 3  eggs 
 butter 
 Directions : 
 Preheat the oven to 200C, 400F, Gas Mark 6 and lightly  oil  2 or 3 baking trays. Place the  flour ,  yoghurt ,  eggs  and crumbled  Cheese  in a large mixing bowl and mix to a stiff dough. Knead lightly. 
 Divide the mixture into 8 portions, shape each portion into a ball then place on the baking tray and slightly flatten each ball with the palm of your hand. 
 Make a hole in the middle of each loaf with your finger and place a knob of  butter  inside. Bake for 30–35 minutes or until the loaves and browned and sound hollow when tapped on the base. Cool on a wire tray.
