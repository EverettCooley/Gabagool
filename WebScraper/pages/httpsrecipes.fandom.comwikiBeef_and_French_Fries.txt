 Description : 
 Vietnamese  Beef  and French Fries is a meat recipe.
 
       Vietnamese Beef and French Fries     
 Ingredients : 
 8 tbsp.  vegetable oil 
 2  potatoes 
 1  Onion 
 2  garlic 
 10 oz.  Steak 
 2 tsp.  cornstarch 
 3 tsp.  water 
 1 tsp. Nuoc Mam 
 Directions : 
 Fry the  potatoes  in the  oil , then fry the  Onion  and  garlic  in the  oil . Add the  Beef . Make a mixture with  cornstarch ,  water ,  Sugar  and Nuoc Mam sauce. Add the mixture to the  Beef , then add the  potatoes .
