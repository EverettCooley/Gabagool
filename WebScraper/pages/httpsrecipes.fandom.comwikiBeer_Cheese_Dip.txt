      Beer Cheese Dip     
 Ingredients : 
 3 lb sharp  Cheddar  cheese, shredded 
 1 medium  onion , finely minced 
 ½ tsp  Tabasco sauce 
 2 tbsp cream-style prepared  horseradish 
 ¼ tsp  crushed red pepper  flakes 
 2 cloves  garlic , finely minced 
 1 bottle flat  beer 
 Directions : 
 Combine  cheese ,  onion ,  Tabasco sauce , horseradish,  red pepper flakes  and  garlic . 
 Add enough  beer  until the consistency of a  cheese  dip.
