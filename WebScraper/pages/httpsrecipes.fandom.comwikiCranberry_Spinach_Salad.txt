Ingredients : 
 6 oz pkg spinach 
 1/2 cup sweetened dried cranberries 
 Red onion slices 
 Dressing 
 4 slices bacon cooked crisp and crumbled 
 1/2 cup honey 
 1/2 cup lime juice 
 2 tbs Dijon mustard 
 Method : 
 Wash spinach. 
 Divide among 4 plates. 
 Top each with 2 tbs cranberries and onion slices. 
 Dressing 
 Combine dressing ingredients in a bowl. 
 Heat in microwave on high for 1 min. or until warm. 
 Pour dressing over salad and serve with bread sticks.
