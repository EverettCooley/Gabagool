 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 unbaked deep-dish pie shell 
 1 lb lean ground  Beef 
 1/4 teaspo  garlic powder 
 1/4 cup  all-purpose flour 
 1/3 cup chopped dill pickles 
 1/3 cup dill pickle juice 
 1/3 cup NESTLÉ®  carnation ®  evaporated milk 
 8 oz  American cheese 
 1  mustard  and ketchup 
 Directions : 
 1 PREHEAT oven to 425 °F. 
2 COOK  Beef  and  garlic powder  in large skillet, stirring frequently, until  Beef  is no longer pink. Sprinkle with  flour ; stir. Stir in pickles, pickle juice,  evaporated milk  and half of  Cheese . Spoon into pie shell. 
3 BAKE for 20 minutes; top with remaining  Cheese . Bake for 5 minutes or until  Cheese  is melted. Cool on wire rack for 5 minutes. Serve with  mustard  and ketchup, if desired.
