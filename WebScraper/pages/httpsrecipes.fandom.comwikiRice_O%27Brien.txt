Ingredients : 
 ½ cup sliced  green onions 
 ½ cup diced  green bell pepper 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 3 cups cooked  rice  (cooked in  chicken broth ) 
 3 tablespoons diced  pimentos 
 ¼ cup sliced pitted ripe  olives  (optional) 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Cook  onions  and  green pepper  in  butter  until tender crisp. 
 Add  rice , pimientos, and  salt . 
 Heat, tossing lightly with a fork to keep  rice  light and fluffy.
