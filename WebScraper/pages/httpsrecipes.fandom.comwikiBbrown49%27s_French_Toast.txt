 Cook Time : 5 Minutes 
 Serves : One

 1 tablespoon  orange juice

 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil  (to prep stainless pan) 
 Directions : 
 Heat oil in pan. 
 Scramble egg, milk, juice and sugar. 
 Dip both sides of bread into mix and fry over medium heat for 2 minutes or until done. 
 Repeat with second piece of bread. 
 The orange juice gives the "toast" a delightful flavor. 
 Enjoy!
