                Foster&-39;s Home for Imaginary Friends Se4 - Ep05 Infernal Slumber - Screen 07     
 Ingredients : 
 2 cup  almond milk , unsweetened 
 1 tablespoon  vanilla   protein powder 
 1 cup  raspberries 
 1 medium  banana 
 1/8 cup  almond butter 
 1 cup Greek  yogurt , plain 
 1/8 cup  honey 
 Instructions : 
 Halve all ingredients and mix well in high-powered blender, making two servings at a time. More milk can be added for a thinner consistency, or ice can be added for a thicker consistency if desired.
