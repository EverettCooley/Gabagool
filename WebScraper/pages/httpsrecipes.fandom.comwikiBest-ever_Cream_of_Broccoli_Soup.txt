Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Kristine Marumoto, Salt Lake City, UT 
 Makes 4 to 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  onion , finely chopped 
 4 tbsp  butter 
 4 tbsp  flour 
 3 cups  water 
 3 tsp  chicken bouillon  granules 
 10 oz pkg. frozen chopped  broccoli 
 1 cup  half and half 
 1  bay leaf 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Sauté onion in butter until tender; blend in flour. 
 Add water, bouillon, broccoli, half and half and bay leaf. 
 Simmer until broccoli is thawed; bring to a boil. 
 Before serving, salt and pepper to taste. 
 Remove bay leaf.
