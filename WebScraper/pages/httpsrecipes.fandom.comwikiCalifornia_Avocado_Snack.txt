 Ingredients : 
 1 Slice of bread (How ever many you want)

 Directions : 
 In a bowl mix  avocado ,  lemon juice ,  garlic .
 Spread  avocado  mix on bread.
 Place  bean sprouts  on bread,  tomato  slice and a slice of  Cheese  on top.
 Preheat oven at 300.
 Let cook for 20 mins until  Cheese  is melted. 
 Will make more than one snack
