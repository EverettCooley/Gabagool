 Ingredients : 
  8 servings
 
 4 cups  flour and extra for kneading 
 2 TBSP  yeast 
 2 TBSP  sugar 
 1 tbsp  salt 
 2 TBSP  Olive oil 
 1/2 teaspoons  white pepper 
 4 cups For the sauce and topping 
 4 cups Pureed Tomato 
 4 cups Oregano, basil or rosemary 
 4 cups Garlic and oil 
 4 cups Chopped carrots 
 4 cups Black and green Olive 
 4 cups Bell pepper red and green 
 4 cups Chili pepper 
 4 cups Tomato sliced 
 4 cups Sausage of choice 
 4 cups Shredded chicken parts 
 4 cups Salt, black pepper 
 4 cups Onions, spring onions 
 Steps : 
 In warm water mix sugar and yeast. Rest for 30minuts and then add your oil, salt, flour and some white pepper. 
 Add for 10 mins and coat with oil and allow to prove for 1hour. 
 Make your sauce by heating oil. Add garlic and well chopped onions. Add Oregano then Tomato, sugar and little salt and black pepper and allow to cook until liquid evaporates from the sauce. All to cool down. 
 Chop, Slice and shred your veggies as needed 
 After one hour take off dough. Cut and shape and put into baking Sheet or pizza pan. Poke holes on the dough. This helps air to aid the cooking process. 
 Coat with Tomato sauce. Add your sausage, chicken and other veggies afterwards. 
 Grate your choice cheese ontop and allow to stand for 15-30minutes. 
 Then grill in the oven for 15-25mins.
