 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon vegetable  oil 
 1 pound  Beef  sirloin  Steak , cut into 1/4-inch thick slices 
 1 1Â lb 5-oz package frozen stir-fry vegetables with sauce packet 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 6 8 to 10-inch  flour  tortillas, warmed 
 Directions : 
 Heat 1 tablespoon  oil  in large skillet until hot. Add  Beef , cook 2 to 3 minutes until no longer pink. Add stir-fry vegetable mix; prepare according to package directions. Stir in  rice . Spoon about 1 cup  rice  mixture into each tortilla, wrap up and serve immediately.
