Ingredients : 
 2  eggs 
 ⅓ cup hot  molasses 
 1 cup  pastry flour 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅔ cup fine shredded  coconut 
 Directions : 
 First break the  eggs  into mixing bowl. 
 Set the bowl in hot  water , and beat the  eggs  with an  egg  beater till stiff. 
 Fold into the beaten  eggs  the hot  molasses . 
 Fold in the  flour  and shredded coconut. 
 Put on an oiled and floured pan with a spoon or pastry bag, and bake.
