Best Meatloaf Ever!!
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  ground beef 
 1 Tbsp  ketchup 
 3 cloves  garlic  (finely chopped) 
 1 tsp  olive oil 
 1  egg 
 2 cups Italian-style  breadcrumbs 
 ½ cup  Parmesan cheese 
 salt  and  pepper , to taste 
 Directions : 
 Pre-heat oven to 350 °F. 
 Place all ingredients in a mixing bowl. 
 By hand, mix all ingredients until well blended. 
 Shape meatloaf into desired shape, drizzle  oil  over top and rub by hand. 
 Wrap in foil and place on a baking sheet. 
 Bake in oven for 1 hour. 
 Raise temperature to 375 °F and unwrap meatloaf. 
 Bake for another 15 minutes for crust to form.
