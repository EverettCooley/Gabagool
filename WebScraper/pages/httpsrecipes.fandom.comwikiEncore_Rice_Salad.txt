 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice , cooled 
 1 small tart red  Apple , cored and chopped 
 1/2 cup seedless green or  Red Grapes , halved 
 1/2 cup chopped  pecans 
 1/2 cup chopped  celery 
 1/4 cup sliced  green onions 
 3/4 cup  Mayonnaise 
 1 tablespoon  Sugar 
 2 teaspoons  lemon juice 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1/2 teaspoon  ground ginger 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 lettuce  leaves 
 grape  clusters for garnish (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  Apple ,  grapes ,  pecans ,  celery  and  onions  in large bowl. 
 Blend  Mayonnaise ,  Sugar ,  lemon juice ,  salt ,  ginger  and  pepper ; 
 Toss with  rice  mixture. Chill. 
 Serve on  lettuce  leaves; garnish with  grapes , if desired.
