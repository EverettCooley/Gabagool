 Ingredients : 
 1  egg white 
 1 cup  sugar 
 ¼ cup  brown sugar 
 ¼ teaspoon  cream of tartar 
 ⅓ cup  water 
 salt  -- pinch 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Combine ingredients in double boiler on low heat. 
 Beat with electric mixer for 4 minutes. 
 Spread on cool cake. 
 Enjoy.
