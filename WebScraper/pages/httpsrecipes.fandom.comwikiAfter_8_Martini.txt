      After 8 Martini     
 Ingredients : 
 2 shots Stolichnaya  vodka 
 ½ shot De Kuyper white  crème de menthe 
 1 shot De Kuyper white  crème de cacao 
 garnish:  chocolate powder  rim 
 glass: martini 
 Directions : 
 Stir with cubed  ice  and strain.
