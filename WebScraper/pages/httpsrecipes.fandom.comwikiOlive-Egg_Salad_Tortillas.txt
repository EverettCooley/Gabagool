Description : 
 Wrap an-out-of-the-ordinary egg salad in a garlic tortilla, and you've got a tasty meal in ten minutes flat. It's a good idea to keep hard-cooked eggs in the fridge as meal builders or high protein, low-carb snacks.
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Original recipe 
 Recipe makes four servings 
 Ingredients : 
 6 hard-cooked  eggs , finely chopped, minced, or grated 
 ¼ cup  mayonnaise 
 2 tablespoons extra-virgin  olive oil 
 2 tablespoons chopped  black olives 
 1 tablespoon chopped  sun-dried tomatoes  in oil, drained 
 ⅛ teaspoon  red pepper flakes 
 salt  and  pepper 
 8 garlic and herb  tortillas 
 1 cup  arugula  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Mix eggs, mayonnaise, olive oil, black olives, sun-dried tomatoes and red pepper flakes. 
 Season to taste with salt and pepper. 
 Warm tortillas between damp paper towels in microwave. 
 Spread egg mixture on tortillas, leaving a ½-inch border. 
 Top with arugula leaves. 
 Roll up tortillas. Serve plain or with other vegetables. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 Carbohydrates: 26 grams | Carbs: 8g | Fiber: 18g | Protein: 20g | Fat: 30.5g | Calories: 405
