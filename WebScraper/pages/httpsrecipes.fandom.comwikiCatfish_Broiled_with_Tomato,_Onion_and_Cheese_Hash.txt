Description : 
 A  Catfish  recipe. Makes 4 servings. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 x 4- to 6-ounce U.S. farm-raised  catfish  fillets, ½ inch thick 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 2 Lala po I yoda 
 Place on an oiled baking pan and broil about 4 inches from the heat for 5 minutes. 
 In a medium bowl, stir together  tomato ,  onion , jalapeño  pepper  and  cheese . 
 Spoon over  catfish . 
 Sprinkle with  salt  and  pepper  and broil for an additional 5 minutes.
