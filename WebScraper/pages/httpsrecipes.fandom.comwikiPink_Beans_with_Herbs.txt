Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: my old recipes 
 Serves 8 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups dried  pink beans 
 ¼ cup  olive oil 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 4 tbsp  butter 
 4½ tbsp  lemon juice 
 2 tbsp dried  oregano 
 2 tbsp dried  rosemary 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Wash  beans . 
 Soak overnight in  water  to cover. 
 Discard any  beans  that float to the top. 
 Next day, put  beans  in a pot and simmer in their soaking  water  for 2 to 2½ hours or until tender. 
 When cooked, drain off any excess liquid. 
 Preheat oven to 325 °F. 
 In skillet, heat  oil  and sauté  onions  in it until soft. 
 Add  onions  and all other ingredients to the  beans  and mix well. 
 Put bean mixture in a buttered ovenproof dish and bake for 15 to 20 minutes. 
 Serve hot.
