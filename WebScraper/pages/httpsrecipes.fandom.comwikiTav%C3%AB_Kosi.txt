 3.1   Yogurt sauce 
 
 
 
 
 Description : 
 Tavë kosi  is a  national dish  in  Albania . It is a simple dish of baked  lamb  and  rice , served with a  yogurt  sauce.
 
 Serves 4 people 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ lbs  lamb leg  or  shoulder 
 4 tablespoons  butter  (½ stick) 
 2 tablespoons  rice 
 salt ,  pepper 
 Yogurt sauce : 
 1 tablespoon  flour 
 4 tablespoons  butter  (½ stick) 
 2 lbs  yogurt 
 5  eggs 
 salt ,  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Cut meat in 4 serving pieces, sprinkle each piece with  salt  and  pepper , and bake in a moderately-heated oven with half the  butter , sprinkling the meat with its gravy now and then. 
 When meat is half-baked, add  rice ; remove the baking pan from the oven and leave it aside while you prepare the  yogurt  sauce:. 
 Yogurt sauce : 
 Sauté  flour  in  butter  until mixed thoroughly. 
 Mix  yogurt  with  salt ,  pepper  and  eggs  until a uniform mixture is obtained, and finally stir in the  flour . 
 Put the sauce mixture in the baking pan; stir it with the meat pieces and bake at 375°F for about 45 minutes. 
 Serve hot.
