 Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: My old recipes 
 Makes 1 ⅔ cup 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can  apple pie filling  (21 to 22 oz.) 
 ½ cup  apple juice , cold only 
 1 pack  red cinnamon candies 
 ¼ tsp.  nutmeg 
 1 tsp. brown sugar 
 1 tablespoon cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 In a small pan, over medium heat, mix all ingredients especially including the cinnamon. 
 Cook until the candies have dissolved and sauce has come to a boil. 
 Remove from heat. 
 Cool and serve over ice cream, any fruit, or other desserts. Reserve the remaining apple topping in the refrigerator.
