Ingredients : 
 4  matzohs 
 3  eggs , beaten 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ½ cup  honey 
 ¼ cup  oil 
 2 tbsp  horseradish 
 ½ cup chopped  walnuts 
 2  apples , chopped 
 ½ cup  raisins 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Break matzohs in to pieces, soak in  water  and drain. 
 Combine  eggs ,  salt ,  honey ,  oil ,  cinnamon  and  horseradish  and add to matzoh. 
 Mix in nuts,  apples  and  raisins . 
 Place in greased 8 inch square baking dish and bake at 350 °F for 35 minutes.
