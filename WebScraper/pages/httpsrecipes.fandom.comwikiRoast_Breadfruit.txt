 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 Whole  Breadfruit 
 Directions : 
 Put whole  breadfruit  on grill. 
 Cook on all sides till brownish black. 
 Use skewer to check if cooked. 
 Take  breadfruit  off grill and use a pot holder to hold and peel off skin. 
 Cut in half. 
 Cut out the 'heart'(uneatable portion in the middle with seed). 
 Cut  breadfruit  into pegs. 
 Serve with calloo or  ackee .
