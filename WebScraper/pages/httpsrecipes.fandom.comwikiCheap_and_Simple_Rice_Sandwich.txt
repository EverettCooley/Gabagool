Ingredients : 
 1/3 cup of  rice  (minute  rice  is quickest) 
 1 piece of vegan bread, toasted 
 non-hydrogenated vegan  margarine , as needed 
 lots of  garlic salt 
 Directions : 
 Cook  rice  as usual. 
 Toast the 1 piece of vegan bread and spread the  margarine  on it. 
 Add rice. 
 Then put as much  garlic salt  as you please and enjoy.
