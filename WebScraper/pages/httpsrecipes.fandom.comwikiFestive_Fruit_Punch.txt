Description : 
 Source:  Special Celebrations and Parties Cookbook , by Betty Wedman, M.S., R.D.
 
 Ingredients : 
 46 oz can  pineapple juice , not sweetened 
 10 oz frozen  raspberries , thawed 
 4 cup sugar-free lemonade 
 4 cup sugar-free lemon-lime drink 
 lemon  slices 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients together and serve in a nice punch bowl.
