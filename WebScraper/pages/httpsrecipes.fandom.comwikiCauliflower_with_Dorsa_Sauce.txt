 Ingredients : 
 8 oz chopped Italian  tomatoes 
 2 large cloves  garlic 
 4 Tbs  olive oil 
 1 tsp each of  paprika ,  salt  and  black pepper 
 1 tsp harissa 
 1 medium sized  Cauliflower  trimmed of its leaves and cut into florets 
 Directions : 
 1) Boil the  Cauliflower  in salted  water  until it is cooked but still crisp. This should take about 7â€“8 minutes. 
 2) Meanwhile, cook the finely chopped  garlic  in  oil  in a separate pan for a minute or so until it starts to turn golden. 
 3) Add the  tomatoes ,  salt ,  pepper ,  paprika  and harissa and mix together. Turn the heat down and simmer the sauce for 10 minutes.
 4) Drain the  Cauliflower  and add it to the sauce, turning it over and allowing it to heat through for 5 minutes or so. If the sauce becomes too thick, add a little of the  water  in which the  Cauliflower  was cooked.
 5) Serve with boiled  rice , sprinkled with a little  parsley .
