 Prep Time: 10 minutes 
 Cook time: 12-14 minutes 
 Serves: 12 small cookies 
 
 Description : 
 Tasty vegan oatmeal-peanut butter chocolate chip cookies
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  oats 
 ¼ cup + 2 tablespoons  flour  (unpacked) 
 ¼ teaspoon  baking soda 
 ⅛ teaspoon  salt 
 2 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 ¼ cup (packed) golden  brown sugar 
 2 tablespoons granulated  sugar 
 2 tablespoons  applesauce 
 ½ teaspoon  vanilla 
 ¼ cup natural type  peanut butter 
 ¼ cup  peanuts  (salted is fine) 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 375 °F. 
 Grind oats in processor. 
 Add flour, baking soda and salt and pulse to mix. 
 Stir oil and both sugars together in a medium bowl. It’s okay to do this with a spoon – no need to pull out the electric mixer. 
 Beat in applesauce, peanut butter and vanilla. 
 Add flour mixture and stir until blended. 
 Stir in peanuts and chocolate chips. 
 Drop onto prepared cookie sheet. 
 Bake until edges are golden brown, about 12-14 minutes. 
 Cool on sheets 5 minutes. 
 Transfer to racks; cool completely. 
 Cookies will firm up as they cool.
