 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 HERSHEY'S SPECIAL  dark chocolate  Bar (7 oz), broken into pieces 
 1/3 cup  milk 
 1 1/3 cups Miniature marshmallows 
 1 cup (1/2 pt) cold  whipping cream , divided 
 8 Single serve graham cracker crusts 
 2 tsp  powdered sugar 
 1/4 tsp  vanilla extract 
 Directions : 
 1 In small saucepan over very low heat, melt  chocolate  with  milk , stirring constantly, until smooth. 
2 Add marshmallows; stir until smooth. Remove from heat; cool completely. In small bowl, beat 2/3 cup  whipping cream  until stiff; fold into  chocolate  mixture. 
3 Divide evenly into crusts. Cover; refrigerate until firm. 
4 Just before serving, beat remaining 1/3 cup  whipping cream ,  powdered sugar  and  vanilla  until stiff; serve on tarts as garnish. Refrigerate leftovers. 
5 Recipe from Hershey's Kitchens
