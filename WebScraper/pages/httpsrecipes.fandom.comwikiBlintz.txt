Link: https://recipes.fandom.com/wiki/Blintz

A tender, ultrathin pancake that can be made with any number of flours. The blintz is rolled to enclose a sweet or savory filling including cottage or ricotta cheese, fruit or meat mixtures. It is then sauteed until golden brown and served with sour cream.
