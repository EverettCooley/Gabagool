 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 litre chickens -, runder - or vegetable soup 
 1 medium  Onion , grofgehakt 
 1 large  leek , well washed and grofgehakt, plus what chopped green 
 2 medium  carrots , crossed out, in discs 
 some dried red chilipepertjes 
 125 g  Peanut   Cheese  with bits note, kindest zekfgemaakte 
 salt  towards taste 
 black pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Do soup,  Onion ,  leek  and  carrots  in a heavy pan and bring to cook on high fire. This leaves halfly covered approximately 30 minutes appetites on low fire. Mash the soup. Return him in the pan and bring him rapidly to cook. Mix in a bowl half of the soup with the  Peanut   Cheese . Mix this by the rest of the soup in the pan. Still 30 minutes appetites leave the soup covered. Joint  chile   peppers  and leave minutes still 10 appetites. Remove  chile   peppers . Bring the soup on taste with  salt  and  pepper .
