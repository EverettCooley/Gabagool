Ingredients : 
 Base dip : 
 1 cup  cottage cheese , low-fat 
 ½ cup plain  yogurt , low-fat 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 ¼ cup  green onion , chopped 
 1 pinch  pepper 
 Extras (optional) : 
 onion soup mix 
 parsley 
 basil 
 artichoke 
 dill 
 shrimp 
 crab 
 curry  as desired 
 Directions : 
 In blender, process  cottage cheese  with  lemon juice  until blended. 
 Add other ingredients. 
 Process just until mixed. 
 Refrigerate four hours, or overnight. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per 1 tsp serving: 
 
 10 calories | 0 fat | 1mg cholesterol | 96mg sodium
