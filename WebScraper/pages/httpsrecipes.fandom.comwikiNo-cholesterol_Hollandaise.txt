Description : 
 Prep time: 5 minutes 
 Cooking time: 10 minutes 
 Yield: 1 cup 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  Edensoy   or  any Eden food beverage, original flavor 
 2 tbsp Eden  safflower oil 
 ¼ tsp lima  sea salt 
 1 tbsp Eden  kudzu   or   arrowroot , dissolved in 2 tbsp water 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 paprika  or ground  nutmeg , for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Combine Edensoy, oil, salt, and dissolved kudzu or arrowroot. 
 Simmer about 3 – 5 minutes, stirring constantly with wire whisk until mixture becomes thick. 
 Add lemon juice. 
 Pour hot over food and garnish with paprika or ground nutmeg. 
 Delicious over cooked vegetables, fish, tempeh, tofu, or seafood. No eggs!
