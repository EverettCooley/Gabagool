 Description : 
 Its very delicious and good for health.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 ripe  avocado 
 juice of 1  lime 
 1 large ripe  tomato , cut into wedges 
 lettuce  leaves 
 Dressing : 
 ¼ cup  vinegar 
 ¼ cup  olive oil 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Prepare it as a normal salad. 
 Serve it in a salad bowl.
