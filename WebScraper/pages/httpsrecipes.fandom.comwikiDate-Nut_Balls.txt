My favorite Christmas Cookie. My aunt used to make them for me at Christmas.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Makes about 4 dozen depending on the size of the balls 
 Ingredients : 
 8 ounces of whole pitted  dates , chopped (I use Aldi's chopped dates) 
 ¾ cup  sugar 
 ½ cup  butter  or regular  margarine 
 2½ cups  Rice Krispies 
 1 cups  nuts , finely chopped (I leave out, I don't like nuts) 
 coconut  to roll balls in 
 Directions : 
 Combine the chopped dates, sugar and butter in a medium saucepan. 
 Bring to a boil. 
 Cook, stirring constantly, for one minute. 
 Stir in the cereal and nuts. 
 Cool mixture until able to shape into 1 inch balls. 
 Roll the date balls in coconut.
