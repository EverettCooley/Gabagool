 Ingredients : 
 4 to 6 tablespoons  unsalted butter , room temperature 
 1/4 cup coarsely chopped fresh herbs (such as flat-leaf  parsley ,  oregano ,  marjoram  and  basil ) 
 1/2 cup freshly grated  Parmesan cheese 
 coarse salt  and freshly ground  pepper 
 24-inch French or  Italian bread , split lengthwise 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350. In a small bowl combine  butter , herbs and  Cheese . Stir to combine. Season with  salt  and  pepper . Spread evenly over cut sides of bread. Wrap bread in aluminum foil. Bake until warm all the way through and  Cheese  melts, about 15 minutes. Unwrap and slice. Serve immediately.
