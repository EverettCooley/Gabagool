Description : 
 Biscuits with the special  aniseed  taste.
 
 Ingredients : 
 6  eggs 
 100 g  sugar  (caster) 
 100 g  flour , plain 
 3 tsp  aniseed 
 Directions : 
 Separate  eggs . 
 Beat  egg   yolks  until thick and lemon-colored. 
 Beat  egg white  until stiff and combine the two mixtures. 
 Gradually beat in the  sugar , mix lightly. 
 Sift the  flour  and add; then stir in the  aniseed . 
 Drop from the tip of a teaspoon, about 3 cm apart, on a greased baking sheet. 
 Put pans into freezer for 2 hours. 
 Bake at 150 °C for 12 minutes.
