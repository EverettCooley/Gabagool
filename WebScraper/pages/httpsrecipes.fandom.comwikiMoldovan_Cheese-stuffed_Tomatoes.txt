Ingredients : 
 ½ lb/250 g cow  cheese 
 ½ lb/250 g  feta  cheese 
 4 oz/100 g  butter 
 1 clove minced  garlic 
 3 tablespoon minced  dill 
 5 or more big  tomatoes 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Drain the  cheese  well and mix with the  butter ,  garlic  and  salt . 
 Choose firm and round  tomatoes , cut a slice on top and remove the inside with all the seeds. 
 Fill with the  cheese  mixture, and then spread  dill  on top. 
 Refrigerate.
