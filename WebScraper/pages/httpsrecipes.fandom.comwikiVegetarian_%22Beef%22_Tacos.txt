 Description : 
 Vegetarian Tacos can be prepared very fast.
 
       Vegetarian Taco Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb morningstar farms grillers burger style recipe crumbles vegetarian ground  Beef 
 1 	package  taco seasoning  mix 
 1/4 	cup  water 
 1 	cup cooked  kidney beans 
 1 	cup frozen  corn  kernels 
 1 (15 	ounces) can diced  tomatoes  and green chilies 
 1 	head  romaine lettuce  or green  leaf lettuce  or red  leaf lettuce 
 1/2 	cup shredded  Jalapeno Jack   Cheese 
 1 	package taco shells 
 Directions : 
 Prepare  tomatoes  and taco, add Prime Grillers Crumbles and the  corn  and bring the mixture to a heat.
