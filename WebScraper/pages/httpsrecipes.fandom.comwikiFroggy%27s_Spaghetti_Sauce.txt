Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  hamburger  (any type) 
 1 large  onion 
 1 large  green pepper 
 1 large  red pepper 
 1 large  orange pepper 
 1 large  yellow pepper 
 2 cans  mushrooms 
 2 cans Ragu  spaghetti sauce 
 1 envelope of McCormick's spaghetti seasoning 
 3 tbsp  garlic powder 
 1 small can  black olives 
 Directions : 
 Brown the hamburger, onion, peppers and mushrooms in skillet. 
 When brown transfer to sauce pan, draining excess grease. 
 Then add other ingredients and let simmer for 20 minutes.
