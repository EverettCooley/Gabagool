Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source:  Southern Living  2007 Slow Cooked Sides 
 Prep: 5 minutes | Cook: 9 hr 
 Makes 10 – 12 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 smoked  turkey wing  (about 1¼ pound) 
 2 (14 oz) cans seasoned  chicken broth 
 2 (1 lb) packages chopped fresh  collard greens 
 5  green onions , chopped 
 1  green bell pepper , seeded and coarsely chopped 
 ¾ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  black pepper 
 pepper sauce 
 Directions : 
 Remove skin and meat from turkey wing, discarding skin and bone. 
 Coarsely chop meat. 
 Combine chopped turkey and next 6 ingredients in a 6-qt slow cooker. 
 Cover and cook on low 9 hours or until greens are tender. 
 Serve with pepper sauce.
