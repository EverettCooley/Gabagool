Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  flour 
 1 teaspoon  baking powder 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 2 tablespoons  shortening  or  butter 
 1  egg 
 2 tablespoons  milk 
 3 cups cut-up  rhubarb 
 1 package  strawberry jello 
 1 cup  sugar 
 ½ cup  flour 
 ⅓ cup melted  butter 
 Directions : 
 Mix 1 cup flour, 1 teaspoon baking powder and ½ teaspoon salt. 
 Cut in the 2 tablespoons shortening or butter. 
 Beat the egg and milk together and add to make batter for the crust. 
 Press into bottom of 9-inch square pan. 
 Spread rhubarb over dough. 
 Sprinkle jello (dry) over rhubarb. 
 Mix sugar, flour and melted butter for topping and sprinkle on top. 
 Bake 40 – 45 minuets at 350°F to 375°F.
