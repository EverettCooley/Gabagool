Description : 
 Cauliflower fritters
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  cauliflower 
 3  eggs 
 1 tablespoon  baking powder 
 4 cloves  garlic 
 4 tablespoons  flour 
 salt 
 oil  for frying 
 Directions : 
 Boil the cauliflower in salted water. 
 When the tender, pureed it. 
 Then you add the eggs and the chopped garlic, and mix well. 
 After you add the flour sifted with baking powder is bat a bit. 
 Fry the fritters in hot oil one spoonful at a time. 
 Remove and place on absorbent paper.
