Description : 
 Crème brulée
 
 Ingredients : 
 32  egg yolks 
 20 oz  sugar 
 1 tsp  vanilla  liquid 
 4  vanilla beans 
 3 quarts  heavy whipping cream 
 1 tbsp pure  corn starch 
 ½ cup  water 
 8 ripe mashed  California avocados  (marinated in a  Pernod ) 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl place the following ingredients:  egg yolks ,  sugar ,  vanilla extract . This mixture needs to be cooked in a  bain-marie  for 15 minutes. 
 In a large pot place the following ingredients:  vanilla beans , heavy whipping- cream ,  corn starch  dissolved in  water , diced avocados 
 Bring mixed ingredients to a boil, cool on side and after it cools combine together the two mixtures.
