 Description : 
 Got a frozen beer mug? This ice-cold fruit punch is made and mixed with real fruit juice and lemon-lime soda and is great for breakfast, dinner, or lunch, or as the new accompaniment to any meal. Ginger ale and cinnamon rarely work for this, too.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  pineapple juice , 500 ml. 
 2 cups  grapefruit juice , 500 ml. 
 4 cups  orange juice , 1 liter 
 4 cups  water , 1 liter, cold only 
 2 pints  ginger ale  or 7-Up 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients in a large mug or bowl, except water and 7-Up or  ginger ale . 
 Add  water , mix, and put to chill. 
 When ready to serve, add 7-Up or  ginger ale . Pour or scoop the soda-based fruit punch into a beer mug with ice cubes. 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Servings:  28 glasses or 2-3 scoops per mug
