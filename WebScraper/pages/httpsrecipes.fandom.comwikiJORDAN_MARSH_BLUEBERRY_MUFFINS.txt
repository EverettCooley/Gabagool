 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 c.  butter 
 2 c.  flour 
 1 c.  Sugar 
 2 c.  blueberries 
 2  eggs 
 1/2 c.  milk 
 2 tsp.  baking powder 
 1 tsp.  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 cream   butter  and  Sugar , add  eggs  one at a time. 
 Add dry ingredients alternately with  milk . 
 Lightly sprinkle with  Sugar  before baking. 
 Bake at 350 degrees for 20 to 30 minutes. 
 Fill cupcake 1/2 to 3/4 full.
