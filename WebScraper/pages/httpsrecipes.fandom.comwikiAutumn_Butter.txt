Description : 
 Got a hot, toasted, pre-sliced bread used for breakfast and dessert? This flavored butter is made and mixed with only 3 ingredients - brown sugar, pumpkin pie spice, and whipping cream - and is great with bread and other desserts.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup firmly packed  brown sugar 
 1 tsp.  pumpkin pie spice 
 ¼ cup  whipping cream 
 1 cup  butter , softened 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients until well blended. 
 Spread onto your favorite muffins, quick bread, or sweet crackers, or drop a dollop onto morning pancakes. Reserve the remaining sugar-based butter in the refrigerator.
