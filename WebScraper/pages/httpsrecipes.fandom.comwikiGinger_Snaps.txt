Ingredients : 
 1½ teaspoonfuls  baking powder 
 ½ cupful  lard 
 ½ cupful  butter 
 1 large cupful  brown sugar 
 1 cupful  water 
 1 tablespoonful  ginger 
 1 teaspoonful  cinnamon 
 1 teaspoonful  cloves 
 1 quart  flour 
 Directions : 
 Rub to a smooth paste the  lard ,  butter , and  sugar 
 Rub it into the  flour  and powder sifted together. 
 Mix into a firm dough with the  water  and  spices . 
 Roll out the dough thin on a floured board, cut out with a round biscuit-cutter 
 Bake on greased pan, in a hot, steady oven, eight minutes.
