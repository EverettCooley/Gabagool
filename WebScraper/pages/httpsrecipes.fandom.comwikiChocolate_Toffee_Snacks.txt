 Description : 
 These are sinfully delicious and so easy!! These are a delicious and amazing blend of candy and cookie! 
 
 Ingredients : 
 24  chocolate   graham crackers  (2½ x 2½ each) 
 ½ cup  butter  (no substitutes!) 
 ½ cup firmly packed  brown sugar 
 ⅓ cup toasted  slivered almonds 
 ¼ cup chopped  maraschino cherries 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 350°F. 
 Place graham crackers close together on large cookie sheet. 
 Mix  butter  and  brown sugar  in a 1 quart saucepan. 
 Stir over low heat until  butter  and  sugar  are melted together. 
 Stir in  almonds  and  cherries . 
 Spoon mixture over crackers. 
 Bake 8 minutes. 
 Cool about 5 minutes. 
 Remove from pan and cool completely.
