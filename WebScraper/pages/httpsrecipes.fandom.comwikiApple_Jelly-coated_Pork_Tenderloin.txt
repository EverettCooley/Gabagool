Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Gonzales Estate in Crandall, Texas in 1980.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  apple jelly 
 2 tablespoons  lemon juice 
 ½ tablespoon  pumpkin pie spice 
 vegetable  cooking spray 
 1 pound  pork tenderloin  trimmed 
 2 tart red cooking  apples  unpeeled cored and thinly sliced 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 375 °F then in small bowl combine apple jelly, lemon juice and pumpkin pie spice and blend well. 
 Spray roasting rack with non- stick cooking spray then place in shallow pan and place pork tenderloin on rack and brush with half of the apple jelly mixture then roast at 375 °F for 30 minutes and allow to stand for 10 minutes. 
 Place apple slices on broiler pan and brush with remaining apple jelly mixture then broil 5-6 inches from heat for about 5 minutes then slice tenderloin and serve with cooked apple slices.
