 Ingredients : 
 500 g  bitter gourd  (thinly sliced, soaked in salted  water ) 
 50 g semi-dried  tuna  (cleaned, cut into pieces) 
 100 g  onions  (sliced thinly) 
 4  garlic  cloves]] (sliced thinly) 
 6  curry leaves 
 2 pieces  rampe leaf 
 50 g  coconut  (grated) 
 10 g  chilli powder 
 45 ml  coconut oil 
 salt  to season 
 Directions : 
 Fry the  onions ,  garlic ,  curry leaves  and  rampe leaf  until  onions  are golden brown. 
 Add in the  bitter gourd ,  tuna ,  coconut ,  chilli powder  and seasoning. 
 Braise stirring until the  bitter gourd  is crisp.
