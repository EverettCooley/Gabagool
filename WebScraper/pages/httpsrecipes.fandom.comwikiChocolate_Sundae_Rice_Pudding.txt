 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups cooked  rice 
 2 cups  milk 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 1 cup  marshmallow   cream 
 1/2 cup  chocolate  syrup 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  milk  and  salt  in saucepan and simmer 20 to 25 minutes or until thick and creamy, stirring occasionally. 
 Remove from heat. Add  vanilla ; cool. 
 Fold in 3/4-cup  marshmallow   cream . 
 Spoon into dessert dishes and top with remaining 1/4 cup  marshmallow   cream  and  chocolate  syrup.
