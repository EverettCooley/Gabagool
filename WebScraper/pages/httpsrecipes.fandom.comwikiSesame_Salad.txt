 Description : 
 Toss with  Tarragon Dressing .
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 heads of  butter   lettuce , washed and torn into bite-sized pieces 
 1/4 cup toasted  sesame seeds 
 1/4 cup sliced  water chestnuts 
 1/4 cup Almonsen Sprinkle 
 tarragon  Dressing (see below) 
 Directions : 
 Toss all ingredients together with just enough dressing to coat all. Serve immediately.
