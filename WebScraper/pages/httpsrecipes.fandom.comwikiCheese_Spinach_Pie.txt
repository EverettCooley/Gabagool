This is a simple one-dish meal, a cousin to  Quiche Lorraine . It is just the thing needed for a night when you don't want to fuss. Since the pie can be frozen after baking, you can make this in advance to eat on nights when you don't have time to cook.
 
 Cheese Spinach Pie  from Recipes for Better Nutrition During Cancer Treatment by the National Cancer Institute, public domain government resource—original source of recipe 
 Cook Time : About 45 minutes 
 Serves : 4 
 Ingredients 
 ⅓ cup chopped  onion 
 1 tbsp  margarine 
 ¼ lb sliced  cheese  ( Swiss  or  Muenster ) 
 1 cup cooked, chopped  spinach  (drained) 
 3 large  eggs 
 ⅓½ cup of  milk 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 dash  pepper 
 9-inch  pie shell 
 Directions 
 Cook onion in margarine until tender; cool. 
 Lay slices of cheese over pie dough, follow with spinach, then onions. 
 Beat eggs, adding enough milk to make 1 cup. 
 Add seasonings and pour over ingredients in the pie shell. 
 Bake in 400° oven about 35 minutes, or until a knife comes out clean. Serve piping hot. 
 Variations 
 Substitute cooked, chopped broccoli, green beans, zucchini, or peas for spinach.
