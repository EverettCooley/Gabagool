Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup of Thousand Island  dressing 
 24 slices of cocktail  rye bread 
 ½ lb of thinly sliced  corned beef 
 1½ cups of chopped  sauerkraut , well drained 
 4 oz of sliced  Swiss cheese 
 Directions : 
 Heat the oven to 400°F. 
 Spread ½ teaspoon of the Thousand Island dressing on each slice of the  rye bread . 
 Top each with a slice of  corned beef  and 1 tablespoon of  sauerkraut . 
 Cut the  cheese  slices to fit the  bread  and place over the  sauerkraut . 
 Place on ungreased cookie sheet and bake for 10 minutes or until  cheese  is melted. 
 Serve immediately
