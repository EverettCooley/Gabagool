Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Chung Estate in Arlington, Texas in 1987.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound fresh  green beans  cut into 1 inch pieces 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 1 large  yellow onion  halved lengthwise and thinly sliced 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Cook  green beans  in boiling  water  for 10 to 12 minutes or steam for 2 to 3 minutes until barely fork tender then drain well. 
 Heat  oil  in a large skillet and sauté  onion  until golden then stir in  green beans , salt  and  pepper .
