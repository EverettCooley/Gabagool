Ingredients : 
 3 1/2 to 4 Lb.  Beef  Brisket Or Bottom Round Roast 
 1 Tsp.  nutmeg 
 1 Tsp.  cinnamon 
 1/2 Tsp.  ginger 
 2 Tsp.  salt 
 1/8 Tsp.  pepper 
 2 Tbsp.  shortening 
 2 Medium  onions , Sliced 
 1/8 Tsp.  garlic powder 
 3/4 C.  brown sugar 
 1/2 C.  red wine vinegar 
 1/2 C.  water 
 4  bay leaves 
 Directions : 
 Combine  nutmeg ,  cinnamon ,  ginger ,  salt ,  pepper  AndGarlic Powder And Rub Into The Meat. Heat Cooker And AddShortening. Brown Meat Well on All Sides. Add  Onion , BrownSugar Dissolved in Wine,  water  And  bay leaves . Close CoverSecurely. Place Pressure Regulator on Vent Pipe And Cook 40Minutes With The Pressure Regulator Rocking Slowly. LetPressure Drop of Its Own Accord.
