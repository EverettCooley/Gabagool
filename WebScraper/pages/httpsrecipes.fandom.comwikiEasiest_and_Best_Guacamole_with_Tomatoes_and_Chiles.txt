Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: With Love from Darling's Kitchen 
 Makes about 3 cups. 
 Ingredients : 
 4  avocados , mashed 
 1 small  onion , finely chopped 
 1 can diced  green chiles  (4 oz) 
 1  tomato , chopped 
 4 tbsp  lemon juice 
 salt  to taste 
 1  tabasco   pepper , very finely minced (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Combine all the ingredients and stir until blended. 
 Place mixture in a bowl and cover securely with plastic wrap. 
 Refrigerate. 
 As  avocados  have a tendency to darken, serve soon after preparing. 
 Serve as a dip with fresh vegetables or  corn  chips. Note: fresh vegetable can include  cucumber  slices,  carrot  sticks,  celery ,  j√≠cama ,  zucchini , etc.
