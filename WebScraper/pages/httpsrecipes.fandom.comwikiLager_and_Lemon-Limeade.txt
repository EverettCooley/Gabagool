 Description : 
 Similar to the classic lager, this tangy alcohol-based beverage is made and mixed with lemonade and real fruit juice and keeps a stone-cold aroma with the alcohol content. Lemon zest occasionally replaces the lemon wedge and works for this for garnishing, too.
 
         
 Ingredients : 
 6 cans lager, chilled 
 3 cups lemonade, chilled 
 6 tablespoons sweetened lime juice or syrup 
 lime wedge 
 Directions : 
 Stir together all ingredients. 
 Add ice, if desired, and serve in a beer mug or a highball with a floating lime wedge as a garnish. 
 Directions : 
 Servings:  6 glasses
