 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  water 
 1 cube  Chicken  or  Beef  boillion (dog's favorite) 
 4 small pieces of  Chicken  or  Beef 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve boillion in  water  and tear meat into smaller pieces about 1/2 inch squares. 
 Freeze for about 12 hours or until solid.
