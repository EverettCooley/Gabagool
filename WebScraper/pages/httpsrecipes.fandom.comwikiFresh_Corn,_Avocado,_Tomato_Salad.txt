 About : 
 This recipe is great for warm Spring nights or to bring to a neighbor's BBQ. It is a quick and easy recipe that you will use time and time again.
 Servings: 4 (side dish)
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 pieces of  corn  (uncooked) 
 1/2 red  onion 
 1  avocado 
 1 1/2 cups  cherry tomatoes 
 1/2 tsp.  mustard 
 1 clove  garlic 
 3 tbsp.  balsamic vinegar 
 1/2 cup  olive oil 
 salt  &  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Shuck the fresh corn (cut it off of the rind) and put it in a bowl. Chop up the red onion, cut the avocado into cubes, and chop the cherry tomatoes in half. Put everything into the bowl. 
 To make the dressing you cut up 1 clove of garlic very finely. Mix the garlic with the mustard and the balsamic vinegar. Whisk in the olive oil and the salt and pepper. 
 Mix altogether and serve!
