Description : 
 This recipe came from the Hartman Estate Sale. It was obtained it as part of the purchase of the Family Collection from the Hartman Estate in Arlington, Texas in 1984.
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup  shortening 
 1 cup  light brown sugar 
 2  eggs 
 3 tablespoons  milk 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 2 cups  flour  sifted 
 ¼ teaspoon  baking powder 
 ¾ teaspoon  baking soda 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 2 cups  oatmeal 
 1½ cups  dates  cut up 
 ¾ cup chopped  nuts 
 Directions : 
 Cream  shortening  then add  sugar  gradually and cream until fluffy. 
 Blend in  eggs ,  milk  and  vanilla . 
 Add dry ingredients then drop from teaspoonfuls on greased cookie sheet. 
 Bake at 375°F for 10 minutes.
