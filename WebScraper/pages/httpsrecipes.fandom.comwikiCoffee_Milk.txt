Ingredients : 
 2 quarts cold 2% or whole  milk 
 1/2 cup ground  coffee 
 2 cups cold  water 
 1 cup  granulated sugar 
 Instructions : 
 Brew coffee with water 
 Combine coffee and sugar in a saucepan 
 Simmer until reduced by half: about 30 minutes 
 Refrigerate until cold 
 In a large pitcher, mix milk and coffee syrup until blended. 
 
 
 Notes : 
 May substitute 1 cup Eclipse coffee syrup instead of making your own 
 Coffee syrup may be stored in refrigerator for up to 2 weeks
