 Ingredients : 
 without kernels, green or black or both 
 some tablespoons of  capers 
 soaked sundried  tomatoes  (you could do with  tomato /paste too) 
 1 clove of  garlic  (or more) 
 rind and juice of one  lemon  (green  lemon  tastes better) 
 virgin  olive oil  of a good quality 
 fresh  thyme 
 fresh  basil 
 fresh grounded  black pepper  and/ or even a red  chili pepper 
 salt  to taste (not necessary) 
 Challe or slices of vegan bread 
 Directions : 
 Put the,  capers , soaked  tomatoes ,  garlic , rind and juice of  lemon  in food processor and mix, but not too much, you should still be able to see small pieces of . Add, while mixing in the food processor some  olive oil , until the mixture is smooth enough to use as a spread. Then add  thyme  and/or  basil  (fresh tastes so much better) and  black pepper , and perhaps the  chilli pepper . Probabely you do not need any additional  salt .
