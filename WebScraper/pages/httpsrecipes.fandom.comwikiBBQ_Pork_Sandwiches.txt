Bbq  Pork  Sandwiches
 
 1 (14 Ounce) Can  beef broth 
 3 Pounds Boneless  Pork  Ribs 
 1 (18 Ounce) Bottle Barbecue Sauce, Your FavoritePour Can of  beef broth  Into Slow Cooker And Add Ribs. Cook on High For 4Hours or Until Meat Shreds Easily. Preheat Oven to 350?. Remove Meat FromCooker And Shred With 2 Forks. it Will Seem That it is Not Going to Work,But it Will. in a Dutch Oven or Skillet, Stir Meat And Bbq Sauce Together.Heat in Oven For 30 Minutes or Until Hot. Serve on Buns With Your FavoriteColeslaw And  Potato  Chips. Makes 8 Servings.
