Ingredients : 
 1 cup uncooked  brown rice 
 1 1/2 cup  water 
 1 can 12 oz chopped  tomatoes , keep the juice 
 1/2 pkg  taco seasoning 
 2 tsp  fruit sugar 
 1 cup  corn 
 2 tbsp  olive oil 
 1  Onion , finely chopped 
 1/2 tsp  garlic powder 
 1/2 tsp  Onion  powder 
 Directions : 
 Add the  water  to a large pot and bring to a boil. Add all the ingredients, except the  corn . Reduce to a simmer, cover and cook until  water  has been absorbed into  rice . Average cooking time is 60 minutes. Let stand for 10 minutes and fluff with fork. Recipe reprinted with permission from HealthRecipes.com
