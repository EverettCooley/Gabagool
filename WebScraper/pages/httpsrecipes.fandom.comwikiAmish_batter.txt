A mixture usually consisting of flour mixed with liquid ingredients and other dry ingredients, which vary depending on the recipe. The liquid ingredients may include  eggs , water, or  milk  and other dry ingredients may include  salt ,  baking powder  or  baking soda , and  sugar . Batter is usually liquid enough to be poured, unlike dough, which is much thicker and must kneaded and formed into a shape by hand.
