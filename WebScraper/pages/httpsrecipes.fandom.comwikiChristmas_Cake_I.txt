Description : 
 This recipe dates from the first quarter of last century. I make just half of this recipe and I put in a very small teaspoon of soda.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups good  butter 
 1¾ cups  brown sugar 
 16  eggs 
 4 lbs  raisins 
 4 lbs  currants 
 1 lbs  mixed peel  cut fine 
 2 lbs  flour 
 1 lbs  almonds , blanched cut fine 
 2  nutmeg , grated same of  mace 
 ½ pint  brandy 
 1 tbsp  lemon essence 
 Directions : 
 Cream  butter  and  sugar  well, add the beaten  yolks , then the stiffly beaten  egg whites . 
 Put all fruits and  nuts  together, rub with some of the 2 pounds of  flour . 
 Beat well, then add  nuts  and  brandy . 
 Bake slowly.
