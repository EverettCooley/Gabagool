Ingredients : 
 1  red cabbage , head, cored, quartered 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 1  apple , peeled, cored, chopped 
 1  onion , chopped 
 2  bacon  slices, chopped 
 1  garlic  clove, chopped 
 ½ cup  red wine vinegar 
 1 cup  honey 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon freshly cracked  black pepper 
 ½  potato , peeled, grated fine 
 Directions : 
 Shred  cabbage  through a food processor outfitted with a fine metal blade. 
 Heat  butter  in a large sauté pan over medium-high heat. 
 Stir in  apple ,  onion ,  bacon , and  garlic . 
 Cook until  onion  is translucent, about 6 minutes. 
 Stir in  cabbage ,  vinegar , honey,  salt  and  black pepper . 
 Simmer, stirring frequently, for 20 minutes. 
 Stir in the  potato  and cook until all of the liquid has been absorbed, about 5 minutes.
