Info- POO 
 Cook Time : 10 muinutes
 Serves : 10
 
 Ingredients 
 1-1/2 pounds fresh  green beans , trimmed and rinsed 
 3 tablespoons  olive oil 
 1 large  shallots  (or one small  onion ,) peeled, halved and thinly sliced 
 2.5 tablespoons  pine nuts 
 1 cups  cherry tomatoes , halved 
 
 
 Directions 
 1.Bring a large frying pan of water to a boil. Add the beans and cook for about 2 minutes.
 
2.Drain beans and immediately immerse in cold water to instantly stop cooking. Drain in a colandar then gently pat dry.
 
3.Heat oil in a medium skillet over medium heat. Add shallots and saut√© until golden brown. Add pine nuts and cook for about 3 minutes.
 
4.In a medium sized serving bowl, add the beans, tomatoes, shallots and pine nuts then gently toss until combined then serve.
 
 Source 
 Green Beans, Tomatoes and Pine Nuts  from the  Public Health Cookbook  -- original source of recipe, public domain government resource
