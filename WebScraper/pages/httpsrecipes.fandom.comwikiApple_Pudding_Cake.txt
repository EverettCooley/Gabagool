Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Stinson Estate in Fort Worth, Texas in 1992
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup  honey 
 1  egg 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 1 cup  flour 
 4 teaspoons  baking powder 
 1 teaspoon  cinnamon 
 ½ teaspoon  allspice 
 1 teaspoon  nutmeg 
 ¼ cup  butter  softened 
 2 cups chopped  apples 
 ¾ cup chopped  nuts 
 ½ cup  raisins  or chopped  dates 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 In a large mixing bowl combine all ingredients except apples, nuts and raisins or dates. 
 Beat at low speed for 1 minute then beat at high speed for 3 minutes. 
 Stir in apples, nuts and raisins or dates then pour into greased square cake pan. 
 Bake for 30 minutes.
