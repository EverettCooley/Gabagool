Ingredients : 
 2 oz  gin 
 ½ oz white  curaçao  liqueur 
 ½  egg white 
 1 oz fresh  lemon juice 
 1 tsp  orgeat syrup 
 Directions : 
 Mix the ingredients, shake them and strain into a cocktail glass and serve.
