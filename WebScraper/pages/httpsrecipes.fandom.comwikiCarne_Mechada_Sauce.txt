 Description : 
       CARNE MECHADA SAUCE     
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 ea  green peppers , chopped 
 1 T  olive oil 
 1 ea Pimentoes, small can 
 2 ea  onions , chopped 
 2 ea Cans  tomatoes , 12 oz. 
 1 T Worchesterchire 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Simmer shredded meat with above sauce, add 1/2 t  thyme , 1/4 t  pepper , 
 3  bay leaves , 1 t  oregano , 1 t worchesterchire, 1/2 t  cumin ,  salt  to
taste.
