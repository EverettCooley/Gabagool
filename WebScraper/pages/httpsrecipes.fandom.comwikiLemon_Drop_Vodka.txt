 Ingredients : 
 1/2 shot Stolichnaya Red  vodka 
 1/2 shot De Kuyper  triple sec 
 1/2 shot Freshly squeezed  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Shake all the ingredients together and fine strain into a chilled glass. Garnish with thin slice of  lemon  coated in  Sugar .
