Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 (8 oz) pkgs.  cream cheese , softened 
 1 (24 oz) jar stuffed  olives , chopped 
 2 tbsp  mayonnaise 
 3 to 4 tbsp  milk , to desired consistency 
 3 tbsp bottled  lemon juice 
 3 (long) loaves sandwich thin  bread 
 Directions : 
 Mix first 5 ingredients until well blended. 
 Trim bread and make sandwiches and cut into desired shapes (4 pieces per sandwich). 
 Can use party-rye bread, approximately 5 loaves, but rye bread dries much faster.
