 Prep Time: 10 minutes 
 Cook time: 50 minutes (inc rice cooking time) 
 Serves: 6 
 
 Description : 
 Winter comfort food of whole grain rice, chard, beef & yogurt
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup brown rice, cooked 
 1 pound ground sirloin, browned 
 1 large shallot, small diced 
 1 clove garlic, minced 
 1 pkg swiss chard, cleaned & chopped 
 1 pkg grape tomatoes, halved (optional) 
 salt & pepper, to taste 
 plain yogurt, for serving 
 Directions : 
 Cook rice per package instructions, set aside 
 brown sirloin, remove from pan & remove all fat, except 1 tablespoon 
 add diced shallot & 1 clove garlic, saute until opaque 
 add swiss chard, cover & let steam 1-2 minutes 
 add browned sirloin 
 toss with grape tomatoes, if using. 
 salt & pepper to taste. 
 serve over cooked rice, topped with plain yogurt
