Description : 
 Contributed by Dorie at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 6  potatoes 
 6  eggs  boiled 
 1  onion 
 ½  green pepper 
 1 cup  Miracle Whip 
 2 tbsp  mustard 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  black pepper 
 2 stalks  celery 
 ¼ cup  ranch 
 ½ tsp  celery salt 
 ½ cup  pickle relish 
 Directions : 
 Bring potatoes and eggs to a boil until done. 
 Set them in cold water to cool. 
 Chop onion, green pepper and celery. 
 Peel potatoes and eggs, rinse. 
 Cut potatoes into small bite size pieces. 
 Save the yolks from 3 eggs. 
 Chop remainder of eggs into pieces. 
 Mash the 3 yolks and add the dressing, mustard, sugar salt and pepper. 
 Blend until mostly smooth in texture. 
 Put all ingredients into large bowl and mix together.
