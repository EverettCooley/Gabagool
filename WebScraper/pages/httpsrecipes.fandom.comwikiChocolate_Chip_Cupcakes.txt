Ingredients : 
 1 box  cake mix 
 1 bag  chocolate chips 
 Directions : 
 Mix cake mix as instructed on box. 
 Line muffin pans with cupcake papers. 
 Pour batter in the cups half full put the chips on top of each cupcake. 
 Put them in to bake for the amount of time shown on the box of mix. 
 Let cool and frost with  vanilla  icing and Christmas candies found in the baking aisle in the grocery.
