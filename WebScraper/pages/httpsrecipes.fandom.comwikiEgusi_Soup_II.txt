 Description : 
 Melon seed soup. Serves five to six
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 pounds  goat meat 
 1 cup dried  crayfish 
 2 cups  seed , ground 
 1 teaspoon dried red pepper, ground or crushed 
 2 tablespoons  salt 
 1 medium  onion , chopped 
 3 tablespoons  red palm oil 
 bitterleaf , chopped (optionally,  spinach  or  kale ) 
 7 cups  water 
 Directions : 
 Cut meat in small pieces and place in skillet. 
 Combine with  water ,  onion ,  salt  and boil for 15 to 20 minutes. 
 Add ground seed and remaining ingredients, stirring as added. 
 Cook for 10 more minutes, and serve.
