Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x 19-ounce can  chili without beans 
 1 x 16-ounce can  kidney beans 
 2 cups cooked  rice 
 1 large  head lettuce , shredded 
 3 medium  tomatoes , diced 
 1 medium  onion , thinly sliced 
 Â½ cup  French dressing 
 1 cup (4 ounces) grated  cheese ( Monterey jack  or  cheddar ) 
 1 x 6-ounce package  corn chips 
 1 to 2  avocados 
 taco sauce  or  salsa  picante (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Mix  chili ,  beans  and  rice . 
 Heat. 
 Combine  lettuce ,  tomatoes ,  onion  and French dressing. 
 To serve, arrange  lettuce  mixture on plates. 
 Top with chili- rice  mixture. 
 Sprinkle with  cheese  and  corn chips . 
 Garnish with  avocado  wedges. 
 Serve with taco sauce or salsa picante, if desired.
