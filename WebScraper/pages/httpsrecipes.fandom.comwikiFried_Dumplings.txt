 Directions : 
 Mix the  cheese  with the yolks, add the  flour ,  salt  and whipped  egg whites . 
 Take tablespoonfuls and shape longish and flat dumplings. 
 Roll them in  flour  and fry in hot  lard . 
 Serve powdered with  confectioner's sugar  and  sour cream  (optional).
