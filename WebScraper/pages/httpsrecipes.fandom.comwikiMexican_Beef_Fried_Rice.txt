Description : 
 Makes 4 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound lean ground  Beef 
 1 medium red or green  bell pepper , cut into 1/2-inch pieces 
 1 large clove  garlic , crushed 
 1 tablespoon  chili powder 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 3 cups cooked  rice , cooled 
 1 cup frozen  Peas , defrosted 
 3/4 cup prepared  milk  or medium salsa 
 Directions : 
 Brown  Beef ,  pepper  and  garlic  in large nonstick skillet over medium heat 8 to 10 minutes or until  Beef  is no longer pink, breaking up into 3/4-inch crumbles. Pour off drippings. Season  Beef  with  chili powder  and  salt . Add  rice ; mix well. Cook 2 minutes or until  rice  is hot, stirring occasionally. Stir in  Peas  and salsa; heat through.
