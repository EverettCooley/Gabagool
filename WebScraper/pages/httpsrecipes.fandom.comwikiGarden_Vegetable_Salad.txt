Makes 8 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice  (cooked in  chicken broth ), cooled 
 1½ cups sliced  celery 
 1 cup sliced  green onions 
 ½ cup sliced  radishes 
 ⅓ cup  mayonnaise 
 ⅓ cup  sour cream 
 1½ tablespoons  tarragon vinegar 
 1 teaspoon prepared  mustard 
 1 teaspoon dried  dill weed 
 lettuce  leaves (optional) 
 cherry tomatoes  for garnish (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  celery ,  green onions  and  radishes . 
 Blend  mayonnaise ,  sour cream ,  vinegar ,  mustard  and  dill weed . 
 Add to  rice  mixture; toss lightly. 
 Serve on  lettuce  leaves and garnish with  cherry tomatoes , if desired.
