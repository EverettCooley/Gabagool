Description 
 Fruit & Vegetable Recipes I  by the South Carolina Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Serves : 2 
 Ingredients 
 1  kiwifruit , peeled and sliced 
 1  banana , peeled and sliced 
 1  papaya 
 ½ cup  orange juice 
 crushed  ice 
 kiwifruit slices for garnish 
 Directions 
 Place all ingredients in a blender and blend until frothy. 
 Serve in tall glasses over ice. 
 Garnish with kiwifruit and banana slices on long sticks.
