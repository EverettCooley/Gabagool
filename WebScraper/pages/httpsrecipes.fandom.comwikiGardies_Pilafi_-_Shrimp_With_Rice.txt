Ingredients : 
 1 med. or lg. onion, chopped 
 1/2 c. olive oil 
 1/2 or 1 lb. shrimp 
 1 1/2 c. canned tomatoes 
 3/4 or 1 c. uncooked rice 
 1/2 tsp. dried oregano leaves 
 Salt and pepper to your own taste 
 1 1/2 c. water 
 Directions : 
 Saute onion in olive oil until golden brown. 
 Combine all other ingredients, cover and simmer until water is absorbed. 
 Makes 6 servings.
