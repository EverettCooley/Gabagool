Description : 
 A  rum  drink
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  light rum 
 1 oz blue  curacao 
 2 oz  pineapple juice 
 1 oz  cream of coconut 
 1 cup crushed  ice 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in blender at high speed. 
 Pour into a glass and top with a slice of  pineapple  and a  cherry .
