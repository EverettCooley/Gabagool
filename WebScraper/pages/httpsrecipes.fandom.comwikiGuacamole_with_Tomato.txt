Info 
 Creamy green guacamole is a common after-school snack in many Latin American countries.
 
 Fruits and Veggies Matter Child Recipe Cards  by the US Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, public domain government resource—original source of recipe 
 Cook Time : 15 minutes 
 Serves : 4 
 Ingredients 
 
 2 ripe  avocados 
 ½ cup chopped  tomato 
 3 tbsp chopped  onion 
 juice of half a  lime 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 Directions 
 Cut each avocado in half lengthwise around the seed in the middle of the avocado. 
 Scoop the seed out of the middle with a spoon. 
 Use the spoon to scoop the avocado flesh into a bowl. 
 Mash the avocado with a fork until smooth. 
 Mix in the remaining ingredients until well combined. 
 Tips 
 Avocados must be ripe before they can be used for guacamole. A ripe avocado is black and slightly soft when touched.
