      Asian-American Confetti     
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup imitation  crabmeat , flaked 
 1 cup grated  carrots 
 ¼ cup peeled quartered thinly sliced  cucumbers 
 ¼ cup thinly sliced  scallions  ( green onions ) 
 ½ cup  peanuts  (preferably unsalted) 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 1 tablespoon  soy sauce 
 1 teaspoon  rice vinegar 
 1 tablespoon yellow  curry powder 
 1 teaspoon  granulated sugar 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients. 
 May be eaten as is, but tastes best chilled overnight.
