 Prep Time:  1 to 2 minutes
 Cook time:  20 seconds
 Serves:  1 person
 
 Description : 
 This recipe is quick to make and enjoyable. It did not come from Canada but tastes it too. PlusÂ : You only have to microwave it.
 
 It is a drink too* 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup of 1% lowfat milk 
 1 1/2 tablespoons of Hersey's syrup 
 2 tablespoons of Coffee Mate French Vanilla 
 1 tablespoon of Country Kitchen Maple Syrup 
 Directions : 
 Get a normal drinking glass and a measuring cup. 
 Get your Ingredients. 
 Measure 1 cup of lowfat milk and put it in the drinking glass. 
 Measure 1 1/2 tablespoons of Hersey's Syrup and put it in the drinking glass. 
 Measure 1 tablespoon of Country Kitchen Maple Syrup and put it in the cup. 
 Microwave your drink for 20 seconds 
 It's done! 
 Warning : 
 Might make a person have a short energy boost in the morning and sleepy at night.
