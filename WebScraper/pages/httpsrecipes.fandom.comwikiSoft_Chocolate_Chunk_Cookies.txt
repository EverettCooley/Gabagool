Description : 
 These cookies are really yummy, we liked them, a glass of cold milk goes good with them.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Makes 46 soft chocolate chunk cookies. 
 Ingredients : 
 2 sticks  butter , softened 
 1½ cup  brown sugar 
 2 tsp  vanilla 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 3 large  eggs 
 2¼ cups  flour 
 2½ cups  dark chocolate  chips 
 1 cup coarsely chopped  walnuts 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 400°F. 
 Beat butter, sugar, vanilla, baking soda and salt until creamy. 
 Beat in eggs until fluffy. 
 Beat in flour until blended. 
 Stir in chocolate and nuts. 
 Drop rounded tablespoonfuls on ungreased baking sheets. 
 Bake 8 minutes or until golden. 
 Cool cookies.
