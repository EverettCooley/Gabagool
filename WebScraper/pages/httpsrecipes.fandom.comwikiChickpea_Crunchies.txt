 Description : 
 Chickpea  Crunchies
 
 Ingredients : 
 2	cup	cooked  Chickpeas  drained well and blotted dry 
 1/2	tsp	 salt  (if you used canned beans reduce or eliminate the  salt ) 
 1/2	tsp	freshly ground  black pepper 
 1/2	tsp	ground  cumin 
 1/2	tsp	ground coriander 
 1/4	tsp	 cayenne pepper 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 400 degrees F 
 Toss the  Chickpeas  with seasonings and spread on a nonstick baking sheet and bake for 30-40 (or 1 hour) minutes or until golden brown and crisp. 
 Makes about 1 1/4 cups
