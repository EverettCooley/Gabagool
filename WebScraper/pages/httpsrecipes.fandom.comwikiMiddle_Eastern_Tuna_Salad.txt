 Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes For Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Yield: 4 servings (about 3½ ounces tuna) 
 From: The Diabetes Food and Nutrition Bible 
 Ingredients : 
 2 (7 oz) cans water-packed  tuna , drained 
 1 large  cucumber , peeled and diced 
 1 small  red onion , diced 
 3 medium  tomatoes , seeded and diced 
 ½ cup cooked canned  chickpeas 
 1 tbsp  olive oil 
 2 tbsp fresh  lemon juice 
 ½ tsp ground  cumin 
 ¼ tsp  ground red pepper 
 2 tsp toasted  sesame seeds 
 Directions : 
 In a large salad bowl, combine all ingredients. 
 Cover and chill for ½ hour. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Nutrition per Serving (3½ ounces tuna):
 
 213 calories | 5g fat | 25mg cholesterol | 344mg sodium | 15g carbs | 4g dietary fiber | 7g sugars | 26g protein 
 Diabetic Exchanges: ½ starch | 2 vegetable | 3 very lean meat | ½ fat
