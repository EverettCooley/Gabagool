 Ingredients : 
 4 boneless  Pork  chops 
 1/2 cup plain  yogurt 
 1 cup tortilla chips, crushed 
 1 1/2 teaspoons  chili powder 
 Directions : 
 1. Stir the  chili powder  into the  yogurt .
 2. Brush on chops.
 3. Press chops into chip mixture.
 4. Arrange chops on baking sheet.
 5. Spray with  cooking spray .
 6. Bake on bottom rack at 400* for 30 minutes, turning twice.
