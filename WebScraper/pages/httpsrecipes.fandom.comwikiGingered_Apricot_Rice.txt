Description : 
 Makes 6-8 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup sliced  green onions 
 1 cup sliced  celery 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 2 x 8¾-ounce cans  apricot  halves, drained and quartered 
 1 teaspoon  ground ginger 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Cook  onions  and  celery  in  butter  in large skillet over medium heat until tender crisp. 
 Add  rice ,  apricots ,  ginger ,  salt ,  pepper  and  lemon juice ; heat thoroughly, tossing lightly with fork. 
 Press firmly into individual custard cups or molds; let stand 3 minutes. 
 Unmold on serving dish.
