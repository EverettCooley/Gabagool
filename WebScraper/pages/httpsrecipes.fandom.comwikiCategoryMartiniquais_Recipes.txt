Martinique cuisine has a large of local recipes such as:
 
 Chicken fritters with coconut and curry made with chicken breast, salt, curry, flour, butter, fresh cream, bananas, tomato sauce, cacahuetes, onion, parsley, shredded coconut and oil. 
 Migan de fruits a pain made with salted conch and moure, vinegar, thyme, parsley, onion, green citrus and salt. 
 A delicious local dessert is Bananas Flambéed made with bananas, butter, sugar, canella powder and a glass of rum. Bananas Flambéed are serve on flames.
