Description 
 Cucumber and Cantaloupe Salad  from the  Public Domain Cookbook  by the Seattle & King County Department of Public Health—original source of recipe, government resource in the public domain
 
 Serves : 4 
 Ingredients 
 1 medium sized  cucumber 
 3 cups  cantaloupe , cubed 
 2 tablespoons  lemonade  or limeade concentrate, undiluted 
 1 tablespoon  water 
 1 teaspoon  vegetable oil 
 ½ teaspoon  chili powder 
 Directions 
 Cut cucumber in half lengthwise then slice each half crosswise into ¼" pieces. 
 Combine cucumber and cantaloupe in a medium sized bowl. 
 In a small bowl, combine juice concentrate, water, oil and chili powder and mix well then pour over cucumber mixture and toss well. 
 Cover and chill completely before serving.
