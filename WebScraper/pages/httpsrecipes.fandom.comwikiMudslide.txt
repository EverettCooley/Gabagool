Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  Kahlúa 
 1 oz  vodka 
 1 oz  Bailey's Irish Cream 
 2 scoops  ice cream 
 Oreo cookie 
 Directions : 
 Break up cookie and blend well. 
 Serve in a Large decorative glass. 
 Garnish with  whipped cream .
