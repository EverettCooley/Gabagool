Ingredients : 
 1 cup planters dry roasted salted peanuts 
 1 tbs bulls eye bbq sauce 
 Directions : 
 Preheat grill to medium heat. 
 Stack 2 large sheets of heavy duty foil, fold up sides. 
 Lightly spray foil with cooking spray. 
 Spread peanuts in single layer in foil and place on grill. 
 Grill 6 to 8 min. 
 Or until peanuts are lightly toasted, stirring often. 
 
 
 Notes 
 For additional zing---toss peanuts with bbq sauce before grilling.
