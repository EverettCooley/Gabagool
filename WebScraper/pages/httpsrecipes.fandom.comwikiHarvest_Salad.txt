 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 bunch  spinach , rinsed and torn into bite−size pieces 
 1/2 cup dried  cranberries 
 1/2 cup crumbled blue  Cheese 
 2  tomatoes , chopped 
 1  avocado  − peeled, pitted and diced 
 1/2  red onion , thinly sliced 
 2 tablespoons red  raspberry  jam (with seeds) 
 2 tablespoons  red wine vinegar 
 1/3 cup  walnut oil 
 freshly ground  black pepper  to taste 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl, toss together the  spinach ,  cranberries , blue  Cheese , tomatoes ,  avocado  and  red onion . Set aside.In a small bowl, whisk together jam,  vinegar ,  walnut oil ,  pepper  and  salt . 
 Taste, and adjust amounts to suit your liking. Pour over the salad and toss to coat.
