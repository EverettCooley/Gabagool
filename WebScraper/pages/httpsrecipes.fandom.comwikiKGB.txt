Ingredients : 
 1/2 oz.  coffee  liqueur 
 1/2 oz.  Galliano 
 1/2 oz. Irish  cream 
 Directions : 
 Pour ingredients into a shooter glass. Carefully float one ingredient over the other in this order -  coffee  liqueur -  Galliano  -  Irish cream liqueur  to for layers.
