Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Steep Estate in Elmo, Texas in 1989.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 small  onion  chopped 
 1 clove  garlic  chopped 
 2 cans  tomato sauce 
 1 can  water 
 1  beef bouillon  cube 
 1 pinch  salt 
 1 pinch  marjoram 
 1 pinch  cumin 
 1 pinch  oregano 
 2  bay leaves 
 24  tortillas 
 3 cups grated  cheddar  cheese 
 2 cups  sour cream 
 salsa 
 Directions : 
 Fry  onion  and  garlic  in  oil  then add  tomato sauce , spices,  water  and bouillon cube then simmer for 15 minutes. 
 Fry tortillas then dip in sauce and put in layers in casserole starting with tortillas, sauce,  cheese  and  sour cream . 
 Repeat layers and bake at 350Â°F for 30 minutes.
