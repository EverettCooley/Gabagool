Ingredients : 
 1 cup  Butter Buds "Mayo" 
 4 tbsp hot  water 
 2 tsp  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Mix " mayo " with hot  water  and  lemon juice , stir well. 
 Note: pour over  broccoli  or  Eggs Benedict  for a great taste.
