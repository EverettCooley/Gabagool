Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  peanut butter 
 ¼ cup  shortening 
 ½ cup packed  brown sugar 
 ¼ cup  granulated sugar 
 1  egg 
 1 tbsp  vanilla 
 1 cup  all-purpose flour 
 1 tbsp  baking soda 
 ½ tbsp  salt 
 1 cup 100%  bran  cereal 
 1 cup shredded  carrots 
 ½ cup chopped  peanuts  or  sunflower seeds 
 Directions : 
 Cream  peanut butter ,  shortening , sugars,  egg  and  vanilla  until light and fluffy. 
 Mix  flour ,  baking soda  and  salt ; blend into creamed mixture. 
 Stir in cereal,  carrots  and  peanuts . 
 Form into 1-inch balls; place on baking sheet, about 2 inches apart. 
 Flatten with fork. 
 Bake at 350°F (180°C) for 10 to 12 minutes or until lightly browned. 
 Cool on racks. 
 Store in airtight container.
