Ingredients : 
 1 frozen  banana 
 1 cup  water 
 1 T.  maple syrup 
 optional add-ins 
 dates 
 vanilla extract 
 chia or flax seeds 
 nut butter 
 cinnamon or nutmeg 
 sea salt 
 Instructions : 
 Add bananas, maple syrup, water and any optional ingredients to a high-powered blender and blend for one minute. 
 Serve immediately and enjoy.
