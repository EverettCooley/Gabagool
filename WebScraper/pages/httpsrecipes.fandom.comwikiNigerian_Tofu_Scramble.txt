Description : 
 Serve piping hot over "buttered"  English muffins .
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ lb very fresh  tofu , mashed (waterpacked type, medium or firm) 
 ¼ cup  nutritional yeast  flakes 
 Ener-G  egg substitute  for 3  eggs  (dry) (optional) 
 ¼ cup  soymilk  (approx) 
 8 oz  soy sausage , crumbled or sliced (optional) 
 6 oz  soy cheeze , sliced or shredded (optional) 
 1 cup chopped vegetables (your choice) 
 salt  and  pepper 
 English muffins , sliced and toasted 
 margarine  or  oil 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl, mix mashed  tofu ,  nutritional yeast , Ener-G, and  soymilk . 
 Heat 1 tbsp  margarine  in a large, heavy frying pan. 
 Add  soy sausage , and saute` until golden. 
 Add  tofu  mixture, and cook, stirring constantly, until mixture thickens somewhat, and resembles scrambled  eggs . 
 Add cheeze and stir until mixed/melted. 
 Add  salt  and  pepper  to taste.
