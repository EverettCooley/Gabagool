Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Yield: 2 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup chunky  applesauce ; unsweetened 
 ¼ cup  honey 
 ¼ cup  raisins 
 4 tsp  lemon juice 
 1¼ tsp  curry powder 
 1 tsp  celery salt 
 1 tbsp  tarragon vinegar 
 2 tbsp  dark brown sugar 
 Directions : 
 Mix the  applesauce  and hone, blending well, and add the rains. 
 Blendin the  lemon juice  and  vinegar , mixing well. 
 Add all of the other ingredients, blending well. 
 Cover and chill. 
 Makes about 1¾ cups of dip suggested dippers: pound cake cubes, dried fruit,  steak ,  apricots ,  melon ,  Italian bread  chunks, croutons. 
 Enjoy.
