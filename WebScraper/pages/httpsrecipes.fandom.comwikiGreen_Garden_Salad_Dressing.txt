Description : 
 Serve with tossed green salad, cucumber-tomato salad or grilled or poached fish and chicken or kebabs.
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Source: www.cookpedia.com newsletter 
 Servings: 8 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup plain  yogurt , fat-free 
 1 cup  spinach  leaves 
 ¼ cup fresh  parsley  leaves 
 2 tbsp  Tabasco  brand green pepper sauce 
 ¼ tsp  salt , optional 
 Directions : 
 In food processor or blender combine all ingredients; blend until smooth.
