 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs.  coconut 
 2 lbs.  Sugar 
 1/4 burnt  Sugar  or  molasses 
 grated  lemons 
 Directions : 
 Grate the  coconut . Carefully brown the  Sugar  but control for over burning. 
 Then carefully combine  coconut  and burnt  Sugar  to a little bit of  water . 
 Next, add 2 lbs. of  Sugar  and cook. When the mixture is just about done, add  lemon  and mix. 
 Remove from heat. Next, pound the mixture well for five minutes. Flatten on to buttered surface. 
 Cut out into square or diagonal shapes. Let harden.
