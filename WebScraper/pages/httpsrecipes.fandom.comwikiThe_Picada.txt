 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 Ham 
 Salami 
 Gruyere  or  Swiss cheese 
 Cheddar 
 Spanish  green olives 
 Potato  chips 
 peanuts 
 Directions : 
 Slice the various meats and cheeses into bite-sized cubes. 
 Place all ingredients on small separate dishes or on one large dish with separate sections and serve. 
 Be sure to place plenty of toothpicks.
