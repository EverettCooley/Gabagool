 2.2   Coconut filling 
 
 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 Coconut filling : 
 1½ pkg or 200 g shredded  coconut 
 2½ tbsp  sugar 
 ½ tsp  vanilla 
 red  food coloring 
 Dough : 
 3 – 4 cups  flour 
 1  egg 
 1½ cups  water 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 ½ tbsp  oil 
 1 tbsp Ferminpan  yeast 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Dough : 
 Beat  eggs , add  water ,  sugar ,  oil ,  salt  and  yeast  to 4 cups of  flour . 
 Mix  flour  with liquid and add more  flour  while kneading. 
 Let rise for 20 minutes. 
 Punch down let rise again for 20 minutes. 
 Coconut filling : 
 Mix the  coconut ,  sugar ,  food coloring  and 4 tbsp  water  in a pot. 
 Boil until dry. 
 Roll out the dough and add coconut filling on top. 
 Spread evenly over dough. 
 Starting at one end, roll up the dough into roll. 
 Leave to rise for 3¼ hours. 
 Bake at 350°F for ½ hour until light brown. 
 Take out of oven and paint  pancake syrup  on top.
