 Description : 
 Makes 8 to 10 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups canned peeled whole  tomatoes , chopped 
 2 to 3 cups cooked  Chicken  cubes 
 1 10-ounce package frozen  green peas 
 1 10-ounce package frozen cut  okra 
 12 to 15 whole small  onions 
 1 cup sliced  celery 
 1 cup sliced  carrots 
 3/4 cup uncooked  rice 
 2 tablespoons  Sugar  (optional) 
 1/2 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 2 quarts (8 cups)  chicken stock 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine  tomatoes ,  Chicken ,  Peas ,  okra ,  onions ,  celery ,  carrots ,  rice ,  Sugar ,  pepper  and stock in large saucepan or Dutch oven. Simmer about 20 minutes or until  rice  and vegetables are tender.
