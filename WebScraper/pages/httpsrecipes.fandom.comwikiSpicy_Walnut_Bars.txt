Description : 
 These bars are truly delicious, they are nutty, spicy and very moist. And the cream cheese frosting is so yummy. We sure liked them.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Makes 30 bars 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  flour 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 1½ tsp  cinnamon 
 ½ tsp ground  nutmeg 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  white pepper 
 ½ cup  unsalted butter , softened 
 1½ cups packed  light brown sugar 
 2  eggs 
 2 tsp  vanilla 
 ¾ cup  sour cream 
 1½ cups chopped butter toffee flavored  walnuts 
 1 can cream cheese frosting 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 350°F. Coat a jelly roll pan with cooking spray. Line with wax paper, coat paper with spray. 
 Combine flour, baking powder, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt and pepper in a bowl. 
 Beat butter and sugar in bowl until combined. 
 Beat in eggs and vanilla. 
 Beat in flour mixture alternately with sour cream. 
 Fold in walnuts. 
 Spread in pan. 
 Bake 30 minutes or until done. 
 Let cool in pan. Turn out, remove paper. 
 Turn upside down onto serving platter. Spread with frosting, cut into bars. 
 Top with chopped walnuts.
