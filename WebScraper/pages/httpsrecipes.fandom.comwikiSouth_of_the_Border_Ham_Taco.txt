Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 – 3 cups fresh chopped  ham 
 1 cup Pace  salsa 
 1 cup chopped  onions 
 ½ cup  jalapenos  (or to taste) 
 tortillas 
 Ingredients : 
 Coat ham in salsa, simmer in skillet with onions until salsa cooks into ham, and onions are golden brown. 
 Drain jalapenos, then add to ham. 
 Stir together, then steam tortilla until soft, then add fixings and enjoy!.
