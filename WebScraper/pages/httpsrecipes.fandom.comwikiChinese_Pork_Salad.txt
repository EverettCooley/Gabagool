Description 
         
 Ingredients 
 1 pound  pork  strips (loin eye, stir-fry, or fillet) 
 ½ cup Oriental stir-fry sauce  or  (4 ¾ tablespoons oyster sauce) 
 ½  red onion , peeled and thinly sliced 
 2 x 10-oz. packages frozen  pea pods , thawed and drained 
 1 x 8-oz. can  mandarin oranges , drained 
 1 x 3-oz. can  chow mein noodles 
 Directions 
 Marinate pork strips in sauce for 30 minutes. 
 In a large, non-stick skillet, stir-fry pork and onion over medium-high heat for about 4–5 minutes. 
 In a large bowl, toss all ingredients together. Serve warm and plain or with other side dishes.
