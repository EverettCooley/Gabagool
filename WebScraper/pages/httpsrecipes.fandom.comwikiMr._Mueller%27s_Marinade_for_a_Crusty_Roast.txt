Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source:  Come into the Kitchen 
 Ingredients : 
 4 oz  barbecue sauce 
 4 oz  Italian dressing 
 1 oz  soy sauce 
 ⅓ pkg  dry chili mix 
 Directions : 
 Marinate roast in this mixture for several hour. 
 Now char the roast on all sides, about 12 to 15 minutes per side. 
 Insert meat thermometer into roast. 
 Roast in 400°F oven until desired doneness.
