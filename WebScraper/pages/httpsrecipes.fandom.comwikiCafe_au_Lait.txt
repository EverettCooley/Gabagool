Cafe au Lait
 
 1 Quart  milk 
 1 Pint  coffee 
 3/4 Cup  sugar 
 
Place  milk  in Pan. Bring to Boil But do Not Boil. Add  coffee  And  sugar . Serve Hot. Makes 6 CupsSource: my Old Recipes
