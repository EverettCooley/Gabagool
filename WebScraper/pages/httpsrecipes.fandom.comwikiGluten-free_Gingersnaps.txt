Description : 
 This is a special recipe made without gluten. 
 
 Makes 40 cookies. 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  margarine 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 2 tbsp  molasses 
 1  egg 
 ½ cup  soy flour 
 ¼ cup  cornstarch 
 ¼ cup  potato starch flour 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 ¼ tsp  ginger 
 ½ tsp  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 Sift dry ingredients together. 
 Cream  margarine  and  sugar  together thoroughly. 
 Beat in  egg  and  molasses . 
 Add  flour  mixture. 
 Mix well. 
 Drop rounded teaspoons of dough onto greased cookie sheet. 
 Bake for 10 to 12 minutes. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving (2 cookies): 
 
 Exchanges: 1 fruit/vegetable | 1 fat
