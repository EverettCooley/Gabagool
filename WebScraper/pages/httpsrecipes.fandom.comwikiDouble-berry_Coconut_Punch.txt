Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Williams Estate in Tyler, Texas in 1994.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ice ring (see below) 
 2 packets frozen  strawberries  in syrup thawed 
 15 ounces  cream of coconut 
 48 ounces  cranberry juice  cocktail chilled 
 2 cups  light rum 
 32 ounces  club soda  chilled 
 Directions : 
 Prepare  ice  ring in advance. 
 In blender puree  strawberries  and  cream of coconut  until smooth. 
 In large punch bowl combine  strawberry  mix, cranberry juice  and  rum . 
 Just before serving add  club soda  and ice ring. 
 Ice ring : 
 Fill ring mold with  water  to within 1 inch of top rim; freeze. 
 Arrange  strawberries ,  cranberries  and  mint  leaves and lime slices on top of  ice . 
 Carefully pour a small amount of cold water over fruit; freeze.
