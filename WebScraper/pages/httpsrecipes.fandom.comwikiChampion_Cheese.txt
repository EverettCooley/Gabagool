 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup grated  cheese , room temperature 
 3 tbsp vegetable  oil 
 3 tsp  applesauce 
 ½ cup cooked  veggies  (whatever kind you prefer) 
 1 clove  garlic , crushed 
 1 cup  whole wheat flour 
 non-fat milk 
 Directions : 
 Mix  cheese ,  oil  and  applesauce  together. 
 Add veggies,  garlic , and  flour . 
 Combine thoroughly. 
 Add just enough  milk  to help form a ball. 
 Cover and chill for one hour. 
 Roll onto a floured surface and cut into shapes. 
 Bake in a preheated 375 °F oven for 15 minutes or until golden brown. Let cool.
