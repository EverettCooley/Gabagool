Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz  light rum 
 ½ oz  brandy 
 ½ oz  gin 
 1½ oz  orange juice 
 ½ oz  lemon juice 
 1½ tsp  orgeat  syrup 
 1 oz sweet  sherry 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Shake all ingredients except  sherry  gently. 
 Strain into a glass half filled with  ice  cubes. 
 Pour  sherry  over the top.
