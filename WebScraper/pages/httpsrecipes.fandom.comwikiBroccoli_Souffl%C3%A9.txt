Description : 
 A unique approach to a great veggie side dish.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pkgs. frozen chopped  broccoli  (2 pounds) 
 1 can  cream of celery  soup (undiluted) 
 1 cup real  mayonnaise 
 2 to 3 tbsp. grated  onion 
 2  eggs , beaten 
 1 cup grated  Cheddar  cheese 
 Ritz crackers 
 1 stick melted  butter  or  margarine , ½ cup 
 Directions : 
 Cook  broccoli  until tender; drain and let cool. 
 Mix soup,  mayonnaise ,  onion ,  egg , and  cheese ; stir into the cooled  broccoli . 
 Spoon mixture into a lightly greased 3½- to 5-quart slow cooker. 
 Mix 1 stack Ritz or buttery crackers (crushed) with  margarine  or  butter .
