Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Yield: 12 – 14 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  sugar 
 1½ cups  vegetable oil 
 3  eggs 
 2 cups  apples ; peeled, shredded 
 3 cups  all purpose flour 
 2 tsp  baking soda 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp ground  cinnamon 
 ½ tsp ground  cloves 
 1 pkg dried pitted  prunes ; 12 oz pkg, chopped, to make 2 cups 
 1 cup  nuts ; chopped 
 Directions : 
 Mix  sugar ,  oil  and  eggs  in large bowl; beat for 2 minutes. 
 Blend in  apples . 
 Combine dry ingredients; gradually beat into  egg  mixture. 
 Stir in  prunes  and nuts. 
 Spoon into a greased and floured tube pan. 
 Bake at 325°F for 1 hour and 20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. 
 Cool in pan 15 minutes; invert onto wire rack to cool. 
 Just before serving, dust cake with confectioners'  sugar .
