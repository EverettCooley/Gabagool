 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 small  Cauliflower 
 Half an  Onion  finely chopped 
 Half an  Onion  chopped in large chunks 
 Half a green  pepper  finely chopped 
 2 Tablespoons  tomato  puree 
 2Â inches grated root  ginger 
 4 cloves  garlic  sliced 
 2 teaspoons  curry  powder 
 1 teaspoon  cumin  powder 
 1 teaspoon  chilli powder 
 1 teaspoon  turmeric 
 1 Tablespoon  worcestershire sauce 
 1  tomato  quartered 
 5 tablespoons chopped  coriander leaves 
 Vegetable  oil 
 Directions : 
 Boil the  Cauliflower  in  water  with a little  salt  and  turmeric  added until cooked. 
 Fry the finely chopped  Onion  until translucent in vegetable  oil . 
 Add the  curry  powder and  cumin  powder and sliced  garlic  and grated  ginger  and stir fry for 2 minutes. 
 Add the  pepper ,  tomato  puree, chunks of  Onion ,  tomato  and  worcestershire sauce  and stir fry for 2 minutes. 
 Add the  Cauliflower , mix in with the sauce and serve immediately topped with the coriander
