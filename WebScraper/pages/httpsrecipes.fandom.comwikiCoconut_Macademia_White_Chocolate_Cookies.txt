Description : 
 Makes 5 dozen cookies 
 Ingredients : 
 1¼ cups  butter , softened 
 1 cup packed  brown sugar 
 ½ cup dairy  sour cream 
 1  egg 
 2 tbsp  vanilla 
 1½ cups  all-purpose flour 
 1 tbsp  baking soda 
 1 tbsp  salt 
 1½ cup old fashioned or  quick oats  uncooked 
 1 pack vanilla  milk chips  (10 oz) 
 1 cup flaked  coconut 
 1 can  macadamia nuts , coarsely chopped (3½ oz) 
 Directions : 
 Beat  butter  and  brown sugar  in large bowl until light and fluffy. 
 Blend in  sour cream ,  egg  and  vanilla . 
 Combine  flour ,  baking soda  and  salt . 
 Add to  butter  mixture; mix well. 
 Stir in  oats ,  vanilla   milk  chips,  coconut  and  nuts . 
 Drop rounded teaspoonfuls of dough, 2" apart, onto ungreased cookie sheets. 
 Bake at 375°F for 10 – 12 minutes or until edges are lightly browned. 
 Cool on cookie sheet 1 minute. 
 Remove to cooling rack; cool completely.
