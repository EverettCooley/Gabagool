Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: old newspaper clipping 
 8 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound cake 
 1 pkg  instant pudding 
 1¾ cup  milk 
 1 tsp instant  coffee 
 8 oz  whipped topping 
 nuts  or mini  chocolate chips , if desired 
 Directions : 
 Line loaf pan with wax paper so that several inches hang over sides. 
 Slice pound cake in ¼" – ½" slices; line bottom and sides of pan with cake slices. 
 Pour milk into mixing bowl, add pudding mix and instant coffee. 
 Mix 2 min and pout into cake-lined pan. 
 Top with nuts or mini chocolate chips. 
 Chill for 2 hours. 
 Lift cake out of pan; slice and serve.
