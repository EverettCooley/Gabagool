Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes for the week of 2/25/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cup  beef broth 
 4 cup  water 
 1 pkg.  tortellini 
 1 lb  meatballs 
 1 cup  salsa 
 3 cups frozen  vegetables 
 Directions : 
 Bring broth and  water  to a boil in large saucepan. 
 Add frozen  tortellini  and cook for 10 minutes. 
 Add meatballs and salsa, then bring to a boil and cook for 15 minutes. 
 Stir in frozen vegetables and cook until desired tenderness.
