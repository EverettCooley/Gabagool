Description : 
 Contributed by Delma at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 Sauce : 
 ½  bell pepper 
 ½  white onion 
 butter  or  margarine  (enough to sauté) 
 1 lb  ground beef 
 oregano ,  garlic powder ,  salt  and  pepper  (to taste) 
 1 cup  mushrooms 
 1 large jar  pizza sauce 
 Topping : 
 2 cups shredded  mozzarella  cheese 
 Parmesan cheese  (as desired) 
 Crust : 
 1 cup of  milk 
 1 cup of  Bisquick 
 1 tablespoon of  oil 
 1  egg 
 Directions : 
 Sauté peppers and onions in butter or margarine, then add hamburger and brown. 
 Drain. 
 Stir in pizza sauce, adding salt, pepper, oregano and garlic powder to taste. 
 Pour sauce mixture into pyrex pie plate. 
 Top with mushrooms and cheese. 
 Crust : 
 Mix all crust ingredients together until smooth. 
 Pour over sauce mixture and sprinkle top with Parmesan cheese. 
 Bake 30 minutes at 375°F.
