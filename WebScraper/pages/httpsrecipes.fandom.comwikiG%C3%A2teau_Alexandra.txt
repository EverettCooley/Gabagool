Description : 
 Time : 1 hour 
 Serves 6 
 Ingredients : 
 80 g  cornstarch 
 20 g  flour 
 120 g  powdered sugar 
 75 g  butter 
 75 g ground  almonds 
 100 g  chocolate 
 4  eggs 
 1 packet of  vanilla sugar 
 1 tsp of  baking powder 
 1 to 2 tbsp of  rum 
 ½ jar of  apricot preserves 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl, mix the cornstarch, flour, sugars, baking powder and almonds with beaten eggs, melted butter, melted chocolate in 2 – 3 spoonsful of water and the rum. 
 Work the mixture into a smooth dough. 
 Put into a cake pan and bake in a medium over for about 45 minutes. 
 Remove the warm cake from the pan, and once cooled, glaze with the apricot preserves.
