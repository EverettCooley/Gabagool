Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  honey 
 ⅓ cup  oil 
 1 cup  sesame seeds 
 1 cup  pecans , chopped 
 1 cup  almonds , chopped 
 2 cups matzoh  farfel 
 ½ tsp  cinnamon , more or less 
 1 cup  raisins 
 ½ cup  grated coconut 
 peel of 1  orange , grated 
 ¼ cup orange  marmalade 
 Directions : 
 In a large saucepan, heat the  honey  with the  oil . 
 Add the  sesame seeds ,  pecans  and the  almonds  and cook in the  honey  mixture, over medium heat fro 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
 Add the farfel,  raisins ,  cinnamon ,  coconut ,  orange peel  and the  marmalade , and cool over medium heat for 20 minutes, stirring frequently. 
 Spread the granule on a greased cookie sheet and cool completely. 
 Store in an airtight container. 
 Note: after Pesach you can replace the farfel with atonal in the recipe.
