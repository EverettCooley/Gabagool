Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Love Estate in Sachse, Texas in 1984.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 ripe  avocados 
 juice of 1  lemon 
 juice of 1  lime 
 ½ cup chopped  pine nuts 
 1 ripe  tomato  diced and drained on paper towels 
 2  scallions  green and white parts thinly sliced 
 ½ clove  garlic  peeled and crushed 
 ¼ teaspoon crushed  coriander seed 
 ¼ teaspoon  hot pepper sauce 
 salt and freshly ground  pepper  to taste 
 lime  wedges for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Cut avocados in half and remove seeds. 
 Carefully scoop out the flesh being careful not to damage skins. 
 Combine avocado flesh and the remaining ingredients in a bowl and mash with fork. 
 Spoon avocado mixture into empty avocado skins. 
 Garnish with lime wedges.
