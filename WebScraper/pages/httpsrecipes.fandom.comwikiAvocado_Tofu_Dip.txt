 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 ripe  avocados 
 1/2 pound  tofu 
 2 tablespoons picante sauce (Pace) 
 1/4 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 Directions : 
 Blend  avocado  and  tofu  in blender until smooth. In bowl, mix  avocado  mixture, picante sauce and  garlic powder .
