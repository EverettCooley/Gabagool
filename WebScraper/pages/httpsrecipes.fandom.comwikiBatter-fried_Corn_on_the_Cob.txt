Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves 12 
 Ingredients : 
 oil  for deep frying 
 ¾ cup  cornmeal 
 ½ cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 ⅓ cup  flour 
 ½ – ¾ tsp  garlic salt 
 ¾ cup  milk 
 2 tbsp  oil 
 1  egg 
 2 pkg. Green Giant Nibblers Frozen  Corn -on-the-Cob, thawed 
 1 cup  cornflake crumbs 
 Directions : 
 In deep fat fryer or heavy saucepan, heat oil to 375°F. 
 In a medium-sized bowl combine cornmeal, Parmesan cheese, flour, garlic salt, milk, oil, and egg; mix well. 
 Dip corn-on-the-cob ears into cornmeal batter to coat; roll in cornflake crumbs. 
 Fry in hot oil 2 – 3 minutes or until lightly golden brown. 
 Drain on paper towels. 
 Serve immediately.
