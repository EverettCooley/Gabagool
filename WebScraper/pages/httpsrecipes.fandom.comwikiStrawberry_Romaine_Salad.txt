Ingredients : 
 salad 
 1 large head of  romaine lettuce 
 1 pt.  strawberries 
 3  green onions  sliced 
 1/2 c.  walnuts 
 dressing 
 1 c.  mayonnaise 
 1/4 c.  vinegar 
 2/3 c.  sugar 
 1/2 c.  milk 
 2 Tbsp.  poppy seeds 
 Directions : 
 Mix dressing ingredients and refrigerate overnight. 
 Combine salad ingredients 
 Toss with dressing when ready to serve. Enjoy.
