ORANGE-CHOCOLATE FUDGE CANDY 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups sugar 
1/2 cup water 
1/2 cup orange juice 
12 oz. pkg. real semi sweet chocolate pieces 
1 T. grated orange peel 
2 cups coarsely chopped walnuts 
 
 Directions : 
 In 3 qt. saucepan, combine sugar, water and juice. Put on medium heat, 
 stir until sugar dissolves. Cool syrup, while stirring, until 234 deggrees, softball 
stage. (Test with candy thermometer.) Remove from heat. 
 
 Add chocolate, stir until well blended. Stir in orange peel and walnuts. 
 Line a small Pyrex pan with foil. Spread candy onto foil. 
 Chill until set. Turn out of pan. Peel off foil. 
 Cut into squares or bars. Keep covered in cool, dry place.
