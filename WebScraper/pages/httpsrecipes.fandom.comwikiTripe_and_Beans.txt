 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 can  baked 
 1 chopped  onion  and  beans 
 1 pound  tripe 
 1 sprig  thyme 
 2 stalks  scallions 
 ¼ teaspoon  black pepper 
 1 ounce  margarine , 
 6  pimiento grains 
 1 tablespoon  flour 
 5 cups water 
 water  to mix 
 Directions : 
 Cut tripe into 1 inch pieces 
 Add tripe to  water  with half the season and cook till tender 
 Make spinner (small dumplings) 
 Add the rest of the seasoning along with the  margarine  and cook for 10 minutes 
 Mix the tablespoon  flour  with ¼ cup  water  till smooth 
 Add and cool for 5 minutes.
