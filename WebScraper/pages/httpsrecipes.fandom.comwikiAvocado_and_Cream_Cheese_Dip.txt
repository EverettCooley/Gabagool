 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 	large 	 avocado , stoned 
 100 	grams 	Cooked  spinach , chopped 
 50 	grams 	Low-fat curd  Cheese , mashed 
 1 		 garlic  clove, crushed 
 1 	tsp 	 lemon juice  (or more) 
 2 		Spring  onions 
 -- finely chopped 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 1 	Serves 2-3
 2 	the mouthwatering texture of this rich green dip is the perfect base for its fresh, slightly garlicky flavour.
 3 	A popular favourite for all times of the year.
 4 	scoop the flesh out of the  avocado  and blend it thoroughly in the food-processor with the  spinach .
 5 	Add the mashed curd  Cheese  and liquidize again.
 6 	Then mix in the  garlic  and  lemon juice  to taste, and stir in the chopped spring  onions .
 7 	Season to taste with  salt  and  paprika .
 8 	Chill for a while before serving.
