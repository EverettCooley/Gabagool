 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/4 cup  apricot  jam 
 1/2 cup flaked  almonds 
 2 sheet  puff pastry 
 Directions : 
 Combine  apricot  jam and  almonds , mix well. 
 Cut each pastry sheet into quarters. 
 Divide  apricot  mixture evenly between pastry squares. 
 Lightly brush edges with  water . Fold in half forming a triangle. 
 Press down lightly to seal. 
 Place on an ungreased oven tray and brush with  milk . 
 Bake at 200*C for approx. 15â€“20 minutes or until pastry is golden brown. 
 Dust with  icing sugar  and serve warm with  cream , icecream or custard.
