 Serves: 4 
 
 Description : 
 Cachumbar is a salad relish most commonly served with Indian curries. There many different versions; this one will leave your mouth feeling cool and fresh after a spicy meal. Cachumbar makes a fine accompaniment to fresh crab, lobster and shellfish dishes.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 ripe  tomatoes 
 2 chopped  scallions 
 Â¼ tsp.  sugar 
 3 tbsp. chopped  cilantro 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Remove the cores from the bottom of the tomatoes with a sharp paring knife. 
 Halve the tomatoes, remove the seeds and dice the flesh. 
 Combine the tomatoes with the scallions, sugar, salt and chopped cilantro. Serve at room temperature.
