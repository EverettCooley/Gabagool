Ingredients : 
 4 cl  gin 
 4 cl  whiskey 
 Grenadine  syrup 
 orange juice 
 Angostura bitters 
 Directions : 
 Give  gin  and  whiskey  in a glass with pushed  ice , give 2 or 3 splashes admit  Angostura Bitters . 
 Filling up with  orange juice  and decorating with a piece of  pineapple  slice.
