 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2  mangos , peeled and cubed 
 1  avocado , cubed 
 3 cloves of  garlic , sliced 
 ¼ Cup of  Onion , chopped 
 ¼ Cup of  cilantro , chopped 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 Add the juice of 1/2  lemon 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together and refrigerate
 Serve chilled with pita bread
