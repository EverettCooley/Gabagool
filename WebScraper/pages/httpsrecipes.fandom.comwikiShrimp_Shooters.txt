Description : 
 This is a recipe I obtained from an Estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Manes Estate in Venus, Texas in 1981.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound cooked peeled  shrimp 
 1 jar Ranchero  salsa 
 2  avocados  peeled cut and diced 
 pico de gallo 
 Directions : 
 Mix together shrimp and salsa. 
 Spoon mixture into individual small plastic shot glasses. 
 Garnish with pico and a slice of avocado. 
 Toss into mouth like a shot.
