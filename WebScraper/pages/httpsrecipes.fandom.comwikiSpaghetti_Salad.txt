      Spaghetti Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 16 ounces  spaghetti  or  pasta 
 8 ounces fat-free  Italian salad dressing 
 8 ounces fat-free  Catalina dressing 
 2  tomatoes , seeded and chopped 
 1  red bell pepper  or  green bell pepper , chopped 
 1 small  onion , diced 
 1 (6 ounces) jar  mushrooms , sliced 
 1  avocado , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Cook and drain  pasta  or  spaghetti . 
 Mix all ingredients. 
 Chill overnight. 
 Serve with  bacon  bits and  sunflower seeds .
