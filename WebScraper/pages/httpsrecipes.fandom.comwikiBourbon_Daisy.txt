Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz  bourbon 
 1 oz  lemon juice 
 ½ tsp  grenadine 
 ½ tsp  superfine sugar 
 garnish:  orange  slice and  cherry 
 Directions : 
 Shake all the ingredients in a shaker with  ice  cubes and strain into an old-fashioned glass over crushed  ice . 
 Garnish with the fruits and serve with a straw.
