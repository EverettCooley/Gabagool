 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 quart popped popcorn  
 1 cup chow mein noodles  
 12 oz butterscotch pieces 
 1/3 cup creamy peanut butter 
 Directions : 
 Combine popped  popcorn  and  chow mein noodles  in large bowl. 
 Melt butterscotch pieces over hot, not boiling  water . 
 Stirring constantly until smooth, about 3 to 5 minutes. 
 Blend in  peanut butter . 
 Pour mixture over the  popcorn ; stir to coat well. 
 Drop by heaping tablespoon onto wax paper- lined pan. 
 When firm, store in tightly covered container. 
 Makes 2Microwave directions; pop microwave  popcorn  according to package directions. 
 Mix with  chow mein noodles  in large bowl. 
 In a 4-cup glass measure, combine butterscotch pieces and  Peanut   butter . 
 Microwave on high 2 minutes or until soft. 
 Stir until smooth. 
 Pour over  popcorn  stir to coat well. 
 Shape as above directions
