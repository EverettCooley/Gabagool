Description : 
 Kazakh noodles
 
 Ingredients : 
 130 g  beef  or  mutton  (brisket) 
 64 g  vegetable oil 
 120 g  onion 
 3 g  garlic 
 1 g  ground red pepper 
 240 g  wheat flour 
 6  eggs 
 50 g  water 
 1 g  salt 
 Directions : 
 Prepare thick dough and let it sit for 30 to 40 minutes. 
 Roll out the dough into small sheets 1. 
 5 mm thick, dry them a little, roll them up and slice them into thin strips (noodles). 
 Cut meat into 23 mm cubes, fry in  vegetable oil , add  salt ,  pepper  and bouillon and cook until done. 
 Boil noodles, then serve them with meat, and sprinkle the mixture with greens.
