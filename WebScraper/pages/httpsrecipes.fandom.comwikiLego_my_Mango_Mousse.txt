Ingredients : 
 3 cups  heavy cream 
 ½ cup  powdered sugar 
 shaved  chocolate 
 16 ripe firm  mangos 
 Directions : 
 Cut  mangos  into pieces but do not peel. 
 Simmer in a small amount of  water  25 – 30 minutes, until tender. 
 Force through a fine sieve. 
 Cool. 
 Fold in stiffly  whipped cream . 
 Add  sugar  and turn into individual dessert dishes. 
 Garnish with shaved  chocolate . 
 Chill.
