 Description : 
 Made with Jello
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 packages(4 serving size each) or 1 package (8 serving size) Jello  lemon  flavor 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 2 Cups boiling  water 
 1 can (15 oz.)  tomato  sauce 
 2 Tbsp  vinegar 
 ½ tsp hot pepper sauce 
 2 Cups chopped  California avocado 
 ½ Cup finely chopped  celery 
 ¼ Cup sliced scallions 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve  gelatin  and  salt  in boiling  water .
 Stir in  tomato  sauce,  vinegar  and hot pepper sauce . 
 Chill until thickened. 
 Fold in  California avocado ,  celery  and scallions. 
 Pour into 5-cup mold. 
 Chill until firm, about 4 hours. Unmold. 
 Garnish as desired.
 Prep. Time: 20 minutes.
 Chill Time: 4 1/2 hours
