Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 cup  grated coconut 
 1 cup  Sugar 
 1 1/2 oz  margarine 
 1/2 cup dried fruit or candied peel 
 1/4 teasp  salt 
 2 1/2 cups  flour  ( Baker's  flour  ) 
 1 tablesp  yeast 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Put 1 cup of warm  water  in a bowl and sprinkle the  yeast  in, add 1 tablesp of the  Sugar  and 2 tablesps of the flour- Stir well with a for and leave to rise 
 In another bowl mix  grated coconut ,  Sugar ,  salt  and softened  margarine 
 Add  yeast  mixture,  flour  and dried fruit - mix well 
 Grease a bowl lightly with  margarine , place dough in and turn once, then cover bowl with a dish towel and let dough rise for 1 hour 
 After one hour, knead dough again, then divide into two equal parts and put into two greased baking tins 
 Cover with towel again and leave to rise until nearly double in size 
 Preheat oven to 350 degr. and bake loaves until light brown ( about 30 mins). Test for done- ness by inserting toothpick in centre of bread- if it comes back out clean, bread is done 
 Remove bread from oven, turn out onto a rack and brush with thick syrup to glaze ( see Sweetbread recipe above)- Cool.
