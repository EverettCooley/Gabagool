Description : 
 Ethnicity - South Indian 
 Type of meal - Party, Lunch, Dinner 
 Ingredients : 
 250 gm:  dried apricots 
 150 gm:  Sugar 
 1/4cup:  almonds , blanched and roasted 
 1 tbsp:  lemon juice 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Wash the  apricots  and soak in  water  to come upto about 2" above, for 4-5 hours. 
 De-seed the  apricots , chop and place in a pan along with the liquid and the  Sugar . 
 Cook over medium heat, till  Sugar  dissolves and the mixture becomes pulpy and blended. 
 Transfer into a serving dish. 
 Serve hot or cold accompanied by  cream  or ice- cream .
