 A surprising balance of flavors will please busy cooks and their families. 
 
 Tuna Broccoli Casserole  from Recipes for Better Nutrition During Cancer Treatment by the National Cancer Institute 
 Serves : 5 
 Ingredients 
 
 2 packages (10 oz) frozen  broccoli , whole or chopped 
 2 cans (7 oz) water-packed  tuna  broken into small pieces 
 1 can (10 oz)  cream of mushroom  soup diluted with ½ cup of  milk 
 1 cup grated  cheddar  or  American cheese 
 ½ cup plain  bread crumbs 
 2 tbsp melted  margarine 
 Directions 
 Cook broccoli according to package directions, drain, and place in shallow 2-quart casserole. 
 Add tuna and cover with diluted mushroom soup. 
 Sprinkle with cheese. 
 Add bread crumbs to melted butter and sprinkle over casserole. 
 Bake at 350°F for 20 minutes.
