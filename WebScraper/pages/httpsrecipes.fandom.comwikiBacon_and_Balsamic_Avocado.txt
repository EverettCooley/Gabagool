 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 ripe Genuine  California avocado 
 1 Tbsp of  olive oil 
 1 to 2 Tablespoon of good quality  balsamic vinegar 
 Rocket leaves 
 6 rashes of streaky  Bacon 
 Directions : 
 Stone  avocado  and half
 Chop the  Bacon  into bite sized pieces
 Wash the rocket leaves
 Fry the  Bacon  and  olive oil  in a frying pan until crispy - add the  balsamic vinegar  making sure all the  Bacon  is coated and the excess  vinegar  is warmed through. Cover top and center of stoned  avocado  with the mixture and garnish with rocket leaves.
