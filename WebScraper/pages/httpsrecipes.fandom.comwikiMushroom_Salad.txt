      Mushroom Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 250 g  mushrooms 
 2  capsicum 
 4  spring onions 
 4 sprigs  parsley 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 ¼ cup  vinaigrette 
 Directions : 
 Wash  mushrooms ,  capsicum , spring  onions  and  parsley  thoroughly. 
 Slice  mushrooms  finely. 
 De-seed  capsicum . 
 Chop  capsicum , spring  onions  and  parsley  finely. 
 In a small bowl, mix  parsley , vinaigrette,  salt  and  pepper  thoroughly. 
 In a salad bowl, place  mushroom ,  capsicum  and spring  onions . 
 Add dressing and toss well.
