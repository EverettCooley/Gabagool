Description : 
 Contributed by Dorie at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup hot cooked  rice 
 ½ cup shredded  cheddar  cheese divided 
 1 tbsp  butter 
 ⅛ tsp  curry powder 
 ⅓ cup  bread crumbs 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Grease baking dish. 
 Toss rice with half of the cheese. 
 Spoon into baking dish; top with remaining cheese. 
 Bake 10 – 15 minutes or until cheese melts. 
 Meanwhile, melt butter; stir in curry. 
 Add bread crumbs and brown lightly remove rice from oven; sprinkle with curried crumbs.
