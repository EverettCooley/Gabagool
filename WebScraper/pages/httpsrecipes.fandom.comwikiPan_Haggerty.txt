 Description : 
 
 Ingredients : 
 butter  - 25g (1 oz), 
 vegetable oil  - 1 tbsp 
 potatoes  - 450g (1Â lb) peeled and thinly sliced, 
 onions  - 2 medium thinly sliced, 
 Cheddar  or  Lancashire   Cheese  - 110g (4 oz) grated. 
 Directions : 
 1. Heat  butter  and  oil  in a large heavy-based frying pan. Remove the pan from the heat and put in layers of  potatoes ,  onions  and grated  Cheese  ending with a top layer of  Cheese .
 2. Cover and cook the vegetables gently for about 30 minutes or until the  potatoes  and  onions  are almost cooked.
 3. Uncover and brown the top of the dish under a hot grill. Serve straight from the pan.
