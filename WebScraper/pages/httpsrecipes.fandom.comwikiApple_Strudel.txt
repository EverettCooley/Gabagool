 Description : 
 In Romanian: Strudel cu mere
 
 Ingredients : 
 strudel dough 
 Filling : 
 2 lbs / 1 kg  apples 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 3 tablespoons melted  butter  or  lard 
 shortening  for greasing the pan and the dough 
 confectioner's sugar 
 Directions : 
 Peel the  apples , grate on the vegetable grater or slice very thinly. 
 On the table there is the  strudel dough  ready to be used. 
 Moisten with melted  butter  or  lard . 
 Arrange the  apples  at one end (long end), sprinkle with  sugar  and cinnamon, cover with the edge of the dough and roll like a jelly roll using the table cloth as an aid. 
 You get a long roll which you can cut in 2 or 3, depending on the size of your baking pan. 
 Arrange in the greased pan, roll next to roll, with a little distance among them. 
 Grease the tops with melted  butter  or  lard . Bake until golden brown. 
 Cut into serving size portions while still in the pan. 
 Sprinkle with  confectioner's sugar
