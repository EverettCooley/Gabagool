Black Forest  cherry  Crisp
 
 1 Can  cherry  Pie Filling 
 1 cn Crushed  pineapple ; 8 1/2 0z 
 1  chocolate  Cake Mix 
 1 c  pecans ; Chopped 
 1  margarine 
 whipping cream ; or Coolwhip
 maraschino cherries ;
-Optional
 
Preparation Time: 0:35
 Spread  cherry  Pie Filling in Bottom of Ungreased 9x13Baking Dish. Cover Evenly With Crushed  pineapple . SprinkleDry Cake Mix Over  pineapple . Sprinkle Chopped  pecans  OverTop. Spread Thinly Sliced  margarine  Evenly Over  pecans .Bake in Preheated 350 Degree Oven For 25-30 Minutes. CoolCompletely Before Serving. When Cool, Cover With  whipped cream  And Garnish With  maraschino cherries .
 
Enjoy.
