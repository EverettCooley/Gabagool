      Green Bourbon Martini     
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 oz Stoli® Razberi  vodka 
 1/2 oz  tequila  Rose®  strawberry   cream  liqueur 
 Directions : 
 Mandi Candy is a tasty  strawberry / raspberry  flavored shot drink. It was invented by bartender Mandi Z, Uncle Paulie's, Glen Ellyn, Illinois. 
 Serve at room temperature or double-chilled, depending on taste.
