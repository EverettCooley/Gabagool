 Description : 
 This bold poultry-based dish is similar to the original roast chicken. A sour, fresh combination of lemon and chicken is made and packed with real herbs, enhanced with yogurt, and roasted to red-hot perfection. You can cook this with other herbs in the chicken on other occasions.
 
 Ingredients : 
 herb  lemon  rub 
 1 tbsp. chopped  rosemary ,  sage , and  thyme  leaves, stem reserved 
 2 tbsp.  salt , iodized or rock only 
 2 tsp.  lemon  zest 
 4 tablespoons Italian seasong, optional 
 1 tbsp.  lemon juice , cold only 
 ¼ cup plain  yogurt 
 6 pounds roasting  chicken , with the skin removed and reserved if desired 
 garlic -flavored  cooking spray 
 Directions : 
 Heat an oven to 350° F. Line a roasting pan with the aluminium foil. 
 Grease a rack that fits in a pan. Put multiple ingredients, especially including Italian seasoning, in a large, fresh bowl and stir until blended. 
 Fill the  chicken 's body cavity with the reserved herb stems and  lemon juice . 
 Tie legs together and tuck wing tips under the back. Rub the herb mixture all over the chicken. Place on a rack in a pan and roast, spraying every 30 minutes, with  garlic   cooking spray  for 2 hours or until cooked through. 
 Transfer to a cutting board. Untie the legs; just let chicken rest 10 minutes before carving. 
 Then transfer chicken to a platter. Serve hot and plain over white rice or with other main dishes.
