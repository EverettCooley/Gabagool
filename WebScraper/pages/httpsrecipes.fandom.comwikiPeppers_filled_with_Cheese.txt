Description : 
 In Romanian: Ardei grasi umpluti cu brinza
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb / 500 g  cheese 
 4 oz / 100 g  butter 
 1 tablespoon minced  dill 
 a few big  bell peppers 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Wash, dry and core the  peppers . 
 Remove all seeds. 
 Drain the  cheese  well and mix with the  butter  and  salt . 
 Fill with the  cheese  mixture, then spread a little  dill  on top. 
 Refrigerate.
