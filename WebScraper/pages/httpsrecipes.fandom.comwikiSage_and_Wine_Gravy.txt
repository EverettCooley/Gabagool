 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/3 cup  whole wheat flour 
 1/3 cup  nutritional yeast 
 1/3 cup  olive oil  + 2 tsp toasted  sesame oil 
 1/2 cup dry  red wine 
 1/4 soy sauce or liquid aminos 
 1 1/4 cups veggie stock or  water 
 1/2 tbsp  sage 
 1 tsp ground  black pepper 
 1/2 tsp each ground  rosemary  and  thyme 
 Directions : 
 In a dry saucepan, heat the  whole wheat flour  over medium, stirring frequently to prevent burning. Once it begins to toast and becomes aromatic, quickly stir in the  nutritional yeast  and  oil . Let this mixture cook for a couple of minutes, while you whisk it. Blend the veggie stock/ water  with the soy sauce and wine, and then add this mixture slowly to the pan in a thin stream, whisking constantly. Add the spices and cook over medium heat until thickened. 
 
 Makes about 2 cups of gravy.
