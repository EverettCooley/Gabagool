Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 pounds boneless round  Steak , cut into 1-inch squares 
 2 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 1 cup chopped  Onion 
 1 cup  beef broth 
 1/4 cup barbecue sauce 
 1/2 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 1/8 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1 cup sliced  celery 
 1 10-ounce package frozen cut  green beans 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Cook meat in  vegetable oil  in 10-inch skillet over medium-high heat until brown, about 10 minutes. 
 Add  Onion ; cook 3 minutes or until tender crisp. Stir in broth, barbecue sauce,  garlic powder  and  pepper . 
 Cover and simmer 30 to 40 minutes or until meat is tender. Add  celery  and  green beans . 
 Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer until vegetables are tender, about 10 minutes. 
 Serve over beds of fluffy  rice .
