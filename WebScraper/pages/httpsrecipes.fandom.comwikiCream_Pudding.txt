 Description : 
       Cream Pudding     
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups  milk 
 1/4 teaspoon  salt 
 1/2 cup  cream  of  Wheat 
 1/2 cup  Sugar 
 4  egg yolks 
 4  egg whites 
 2 tablespoons  Sugar 
 1/2 teaspoon  vanilla 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Beat  egg whites . 
 Add 2 tbsp  Sugar  and beat. 
 Heat  milk  and  salt  to a full boil stirring. 
 Beat  Sugar  and  egg yolks  and add to hot mixture. 
 Cook for 3 minutes . 
 Fold hot mixture into meringue and add  vanilla . 
 Pour into serving dishes and cool. 
 Serve with custard sauce or fruit sauce.
