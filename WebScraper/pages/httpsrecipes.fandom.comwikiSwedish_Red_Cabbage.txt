      Swedish Red Cabbage     
 Ingredients : 
 1 large  red cabbage 
 1  apple , cored 
 1  onion 
 ¼ cup  lemon juice 
 2 tbsp  honey 
 1 tbsp  vinegar 
 2 tbsp  water 
 ¼ cup  red wine   or   sherry 
 1 cup  green peas 
 sea salt 
 black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Shred  cabbage , slice  apple  and grate the  onion . 
 Heat  honey  in a heavy pot, add  cabbage , stirring to coat, cover and simmer 10 minutes. 
 Stir in rest of ingredients except  peas , simmer again for 1 to 1½ hours, stir occasionally. 
 Stir in  peas  just before serving. 
 This mixture will reduce to ½ the original size.
