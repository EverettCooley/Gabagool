Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup finely chopped  onion 
 2 tablespoons  olive oil  or  vegetable oil 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice  (cooked in  chicken broth ) 
 ¼ cup roasted  cashew  nuts 
 1 teaspoon  curry powder 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Cook  onion  in  oil  in large skillet over medium heat until tender but not brown. 
 Add  rice ,  cashew  nuts,  curry powder  and  salt ; toss lightly.
