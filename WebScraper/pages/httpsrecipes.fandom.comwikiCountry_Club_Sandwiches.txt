Serves One.
 
 4 Hard Cooked  eggs  -- Chopped 
 1/2 Cup  celery  -- Finely Chopped 
 1 Tbsp  parsley  -- Chopped 
 1/2 Tbsp  pimento  -- Chopped 
 1/4 Tsp  salt 
 1/8 Tsp  pepper 
 1/3 Cup  Mayonnaise 
 12 Slices Toast Slices 
 8 Slices Crisp  Bacon 
 2  tomatoes  -- Sliced 
 4  lettuce  Leaves 
 
Combine  eggs ,  celery ,  parsley , pimentos,  salt  And  pepper . Gently Stir InMayonnaise And Blend Well.
 Spread 4 Slices Toast With  egg  Salad; Top With Another Slice of Toast.Cover Second Slice With Two Slices of  tomato , Two Strips of  Bacon  AndLettuce Leaf. Cut Into Four Triangles.
 
Enjoy.
