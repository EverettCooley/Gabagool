Description : 
 In Romanian: Gris cu fructe in forma de sarlota
 
 Ingredients : 
 25 oz / 750 g  water 
 1 lb / 500 g fruit ( sour cherries  or  raspberries ,  strawberries ,  wild strawberries ,  gooseberries ) 
 3 – 4 tablespoons  sugar 
 5 oz / 150 g  cream of wheat 
 vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Boil the fruits. 
 After coming to a few boils, remove from heat, let cool, then sift fruits and liquid. 
 Boil, adding  sugar ,  vanilla  and  cream of wheat , adding this last a little at a time, like a rain, stirring continuously until it thickens like a  cream . 
 Remove from heat and beat until foamy. 
 Then pour into a  charlotte  or pudding mold which was previously moistened with cold  water . 
 Refrigerate for 2 – 3 hours. 
 Before serving, turn onto a plate and garnish with some raw fruit.
