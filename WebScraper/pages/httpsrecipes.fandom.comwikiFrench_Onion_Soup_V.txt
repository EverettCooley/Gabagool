 Description : 
 Cooking, Canadian Diabetes Association
 
 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tbsp  butter 
 1 large  onion , sliced thin 
 2 tsp  flour 
 ¼ cup dry  white wine 
 5 cup  water 
 4 tsp  beef bouillon , or cubes 
 freshly ground  pepper 
 4 slice  French bread 
 1 cup  Swiss cheese , shredded 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter  in large saucepan. 
 Add sliced  onion , cover, cook over low heat about 5 minutes until  onion  is tender. 
 Remove lid, increase heat to high. 
 Cook and stir until  onions  are golden. 
 Reduce heat, stir in  flour  and wine. 
 Simmer 1 minute. 
 Add  water  and bouillon cubes. 
 Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes. 
 Season to taste with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Place bread slices in 325°F oven 5 minutes to dry. 
 Pour soup into 4 heated ovenproof bowls. 
 Place a slice of bread on top of each; allow bread to soak up soup, then cover with ¼ cup  cheese  per bowl. 
 Bake at 325°F for 25 – 30 minutes until  cheese  is lightly browned. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Each 1 cup serving:
 
 1 protein choice, 1½ starch, 1½ fat, 20 g carbohydrate, 11 g protein, 11 g fat (223 calories) choice
