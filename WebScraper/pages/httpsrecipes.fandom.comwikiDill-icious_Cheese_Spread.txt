Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Original recipe 
 Serves 2 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup grated  cheese  ( cheddar ,  jack ,  havarti ,  Swiss ) 
 ½ cup  cottage cheese 
 ½ teaspoon  dill weed 
 cucumber  slices 
 red or green leaf  lettuce , or  salad  mix 
 Directions : 
 Mix the grated cheese, cottage cheese and dill weed together. 
 Refrigerate 30 minutes before serving. 
 Serve with cucumber slices and greens on your favorite fresh bread. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving (145 g-wt.): 
 
 160 calories (90 from fat) | 10g total fat | 6g saturated fat | 14g protein | 3g total carbohydrate (0g dietary fiber, 3g sugar) | 30mg cholesterol | 410mg sodium
