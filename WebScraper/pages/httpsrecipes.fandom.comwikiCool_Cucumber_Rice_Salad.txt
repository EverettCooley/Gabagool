 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice , cooled 
 1 medium  cucumber , peeled and chopped 
 1/2 cup chopped  green pepper 
 1/2 cup chopped  radishes 
 1/3 cup Italian  salad dressing 
 1/2 teaspoon dry  mustard 
 1/8 teaspoon ground  white pepper 
 lettuce  leaves 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  cucumber ,  pepper  and  radishes  in large bowl. Blend  salad dressing ,  mustard  and  pepper ; toss with  rice  mixture. Chill. Serve on  lettuce  leaves.
