 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 medium green  bell pepper 
 2 large leaves  romaine lettuce 
 1/4 of a small  Onion 
 1 teaspoon  olive oil  or your favorite  oil 
 Any really spice hot sauce 
 1/4 cup  water 
 garlic powder 
 Directions : 
 Destem and deseed the  pepper , and cut into strips. Chop the  Onion  into smallish pieces. Heat the  oil  in a pan on med. Heat and add veggies. Sprinkle with  garlic powder  and add some hot sauce to your taste. (I like it HOT) Saute for a few minutes then add  water  and cook till  water  turns kind of bubbly. Put half of the veggie mix in a  lettuce  leaf and the other half in the other leaf. Keep wrap together with a tooth pick or just hold it shut.
