 4 cod steaks 
 1-2 beaten  eggs 
 oil  for frying 
 Directions : 
 Season the matzo meal or cracker crumbs with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Dip each cod  Steak  into beaten  egg , then coat with the meal or crumbs, shaking off excess.
