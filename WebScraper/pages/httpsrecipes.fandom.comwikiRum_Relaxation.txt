Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz  light rum 
 1 oz  pineapple juice 
 ½ oz  grenadine 
 lemon-lime soda 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Pour all ingredients except lemon-lime soda over  ice  and shake well. 
 Pour into a hurricane glass and fill with lemon-lime soda. 
 Top with an  orange  slice and a  cherry .
