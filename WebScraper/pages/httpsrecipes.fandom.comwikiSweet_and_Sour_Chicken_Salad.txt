Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes For Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Source:  Flavorful Seasons  Cookbook from ADA. 
 Prep time 15 minutes | Cook time 15 minutes 
 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ lb boneless skinless  chicken breasts , cooked and cut into 2-inch cubes 
 1 cup  celery , diagonally sliced 
 ⅔ cup  snow peas , trimmed 
 ⅓ cup  red bell peppers , seeded and diced 
 ⅔ cup  apples , unpeeled and diced (we use  Gala ) 
 2 tablespoons  vinegar 
 2 tablespoons  canola oil 
 2 tablespoons  sugar 
 ¾ teaspoon  paprika 
 ¾ teaspoon  celery seeds 
 salt  and  pepper , to taste 
 Directions : 
 Combine all salad ingredients. 
 Whisk together the dressing ingredients. 
 Pour the dressing over the salad and serve on a bed of greens.
