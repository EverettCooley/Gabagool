Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 large  eggs 
 ¼ cup  milk 
 ¼ tsp  hot pepper sauce 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 8 slices white  bread 
 2 tbsp creamy  mustard  blend 
 12 thin slices  American cheese 
 8 thin slices  ham 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 425°F. 
 Line a baking sheet with foil. 
 Whisk first 4 ingredients in a shallow bowl to blend. 
 Spread 4 slices bread with mustard. 
 Top each with 3 slices cheese, then remaining bread. 
 Dip both sides in egg mixture and place on pan. 
 Bake 10 minutes, turning sandwiches over once, until browned on both sides. 
 About 2 minutes before sandwiches are done, heat a skillet. 
 Cook ham 1 minute each or until lightly browned.
