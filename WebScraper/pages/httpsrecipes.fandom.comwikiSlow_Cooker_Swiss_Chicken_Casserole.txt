Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves 6 
 Ingredients : 
 6 boneless skinless  chicken breasts 
 6 slices  Swiss cheese 
 1 can  cream of mushroom  soup, undiluted 
 ¼ cup  milk 
 2 cups herb  stuffing mix 
 ½ cup  margarine  or  butter , melted 
 Directions : 
 Spray slow cooker with cooking spray. 
 Arrange chicken breasts in slow cooker. 
 Top with cheese, layering cheese if needed. 
 Combine soup and milk; stir well. 
 Spoon over cheese; sprinkle with stuffing mix. 
 Drizzle melted margarine over stuffing mix. 
 Cook on low 8 to 10 hours or on high 4 to 6 hours.
