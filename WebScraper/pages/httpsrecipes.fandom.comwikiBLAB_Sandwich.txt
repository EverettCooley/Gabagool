 Description : 
 Bacon-Lettuce-Avocado-Blue  Cheese  sandwich that's heaven for those who love rich, buttery, tangy flavors!
 
 Ingredients : 
 Â½  California avocado , sliced 
 2 to 3 slices cooked  Bacon 
 2 to 3 tablespoons crumbled blue  Cheese 
 1  romaine lettuce  leaf 
 1 to 2 tablespoons  Mayonnaise 
 2 slices of toasted  Wheat   bread 
 Directions : 
 Spread  mayo  on each slice of  bread .
 Break up and place  Bacon  slices on one slice of  bread .
 Sprinkle blue  Cheese  over  Bacon  (if  Bacon  isn't hot enough for  Cheese  to melt, put open faced sandwich in microwave for about 20 seconds)
 Put  avocado  slices on top of  Cheese / Bacon .
 Put  lettuce  on top of that.
 Top with second slice of  bread .
 Cut in half and enjoy!
