Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Puckett Estate in Irving, Texas in 1990. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups chopped and pitted  black olives 
 ¾ cup  canola oil 
 1 cup plain  yogurt 
 2 cups  flour 
 3  eggs 
 ½ cup chopped fresh  mint 
 1 bunch  green onions  chopped finely 
 1 tablespoon  red pepper flakes 
 1 tablespoon  black seeds 
 1½ teaspoons  salt 
 1 teaspoon  baking powder 
 Directions : 
 Beat eggs then add yogurt and oil and mix well. 
 Add olives, green onion, mint, red pepper flakes, salt and seeds to mixture and mix. 
 Add baking powder and flour. 
 Mixture will be runny. 
 Pour in a greased rectangular baking dish and bake at 375°F for 45 minutes. 
 Let it sit for at least 15 minutes before you cut and serve.
