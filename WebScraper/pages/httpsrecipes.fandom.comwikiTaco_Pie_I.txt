Description : 
 Each serving provides: 3 p, 1 b, 20 c
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 canned biscuits 
 1 lbs  ground turkey 
 1 small  onion , chopped 
 salt  and  pepper 
 1 tsp  beef bouillon  granules 
 ¾ cup  water 
 1 6 oz can  tomato paste 
 1 pack  taco seasoning 
 8 oz  mozzarella  cheese, shredded 
 Directions : 
 Press biscuits in pie pan. 
 Saute meat, onion ,  salt ,  pepper , taco seasonings,  water ,  tomato paste , and granules. 
 Bring to boil and reduce heat to low for 10 minutes. 
 Put ½ meat mixture on top of biscuits, then ½  cheese , then repeat. 
 Bake at 400 °F. 
 For 15 minutes.
