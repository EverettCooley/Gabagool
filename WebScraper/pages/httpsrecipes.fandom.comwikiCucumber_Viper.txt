Description : 
 From  Jonathan, Adobo Mexican Grill, Chicago 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 slices  cucumber  ½ inches thick 
 2 slices  lime 
 1½ oz Partida Blanco  tequila 
 1 oz  lime juice 
 ¼ oz  Gran Torres  orange liqueur 
 ¼ oz Partida  agave nectar  or 1  sugar  cube 
 Directions : 
 Muddle the first four ingredients. 
 Add the rest and top with ice. 
 Shake and strain into martini or over rocks in old fashion glass. 
 Dust with chili piquin.
