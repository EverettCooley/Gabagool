 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 For Batter: 
 2 cups  all-purpose flour , sifted 
 teaspoon  baking powder 
 salt  and  pepper , to taste 
 2  eggs , beaten 
 1 cup  milk  or  water 
 2 tablespoons cooking  oil 
 
 
 Main Ingredient: 
 2 cups of  bananas 
 oil  for deep frying 
 Directions : 
 Prepare batter by mixing all ingredients in a bowl. Add a bit more liquid if necessary. Batter should be thick enough to cling to a spoon without dripping. 
 Pour  oil  into deep skillet or pot and heat to 375 °F (190 °C). 
 Stir the  bananas  into the batter. 
 Carefully spoon the batter into the  oil . Fry until golden brown, turning a few times.
