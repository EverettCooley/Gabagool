NEW ORLEANS BURGERS
 1 pound lean ground beef 
1/4 cup thinly sliced green onions 
1-1/2 tsp. Lawry's � Garlic Salt 
1/4 tsp. dried basil leaves, crushed 
1/2 tsp. dried thyme leaves, crushed 
1/2 tsp. hot pepper sauce 
4 onion rolls, halved and lightly toasted 
 
Directions 
In medium bowl, combine all ingredients except rolls. Shape into 4 
patties.
 Grill or broil to desired doneness. Serve on rolls and top, if desired, 
with grilled onions.
