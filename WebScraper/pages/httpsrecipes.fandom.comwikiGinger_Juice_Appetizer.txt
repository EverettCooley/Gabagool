 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 kg of  ginger 
 1 litrr of  milk 
 1 kg of  Sugar , 
 250g green  lemon 
 Directions : 
 Wash the  ginger  and then scrape it to remove the skin. 
 In a large container mix the  ginger  with 4 litres of  water . Let rest for 12 hours 
 Mix the preparation well, and then filter thoroughly to remove any excess fibres. 
 Add the  milk  and the juice of  lemons . 
 Mix well and sweeten to your taste. 
 Bottle and keep fresh. 
 Optional: Serve with fried ripe  plantain .
