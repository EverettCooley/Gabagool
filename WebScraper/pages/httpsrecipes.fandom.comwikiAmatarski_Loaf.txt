Ingredients : 
 850 g pork 
 250 g cheese 
 250 smoked bones ham 
 spices, salt 
 Directions : 
 Cut the shoulder  pork  into pieces, pound slightly, add the  salt  and  pepper . 
 Slice the  cheese , place the slices evenly on pounded  pork  and cover with pieces of smoked bones  ham . 
 Shape it into a roll, tie up and fry in the oven until done. 
 Cool the loaf and put it under a press for 3 to 4 hours. 
 Before serving slice the loaf.
