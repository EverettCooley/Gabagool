Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 6 servings of steak pizzaiola. 
 Ingredients : 
 3 tbsp  olive oil 
 3  bell peppers , cut into thin strips 
 1  onion , cut into thick slices 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  pepper 
 3 cloves  garlic , chopped 
 3 cups chunky  marinara sauce 
 ½ tsp  sugar 
 ½ tsp  Italian seasoning 
 ¼ tsp  red pepper flakes 
 1½ pounds boneless  sirloin steak 
 ¾ pound  linguine  pasta 
 2 cups shredded  mozzarella  cheese 
 ¼ cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 1 loaf crusty  Italian bread 
 12 oz bag Caesar salad with dressing 
 Directions : 
 Heat broiler. 
 Bring a pot of salted water to boiling. 
 Heat 2 tbsp oil in skillet. 
 Add peppers and onion, saute until tender. 
 Season with ½ tsp salt and ⅛ tsp pepper. Set aside. 
 Heat remaining oil in saucepan. 
 Add garlic, cook until softened. 
 Add marinara, ¼ tsp salt, sugar, Italian seasoning and pepper flakes, cook 5 minutes. 
 Season the steak on both sides with remaining salt and pepper. 
 Broil steak until done. 
 Let rest 5 minutes. 
 Slice steak across the grain. 
 Lower oven temp to 400°F. 
 Cook the linguine following package directions. Drain. 
 Place the meat in a glass baking dish. 
 Spoon 1½ cups sauce over the meat. 
 Arrange half the onions and peppers over the sauce, sprinkle with the mozzarella. 
 Bake until cheese melts and is heated through. 
 To serve : 
 Toss pasta with remaining pepper mixture and remaining sauce, place on platter. 
 Lift steak slices out of dish and place over the top of pasta. 
 Serve with Parmesan, bread and the salad.
