Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Scroggins Estate in Royse City, Texas in 1992.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 ¼ cup  vinegar 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 4 cups sliced  cucumbers 
 1 small  red onion 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl combine mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar and salt. 
 Add cucumbers and onions then stir to coat. 
 Cover and refrigerate 2 hours.
