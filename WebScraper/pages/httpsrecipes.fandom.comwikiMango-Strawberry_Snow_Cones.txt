Description : 
 this recipeis sweet fun and easy anyone can make it!
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 mangoes, peeled and chopped 
 1 pint strawberries, hulled and sliced 
 1 lime juiced, plus wedges for garnish 
 8 cups ice 
 Mint leaves, for garnish 
 Preparation : 
 Put the mangoes and strawberries into a blender or food processor. 
 Squeeze in the lime juice and puree. 
 Crush ice in a food processor or blender until it is very fine, like snow. 
 Pile the crushed ice into dessert dishes; pour the fruit puree over until you can't see any more white. 
 Garnish with lime wedges and mint; serve immediately
