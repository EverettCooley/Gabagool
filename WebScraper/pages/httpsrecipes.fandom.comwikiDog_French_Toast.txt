 Ingredients : 
 6 tablespoons  non-salted butter

 8  eggs

 1 teaspoon  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 In a large frying pan, melt the  butter  over medium heat. 
 Add in the bread cut in cubes and cook, toss the bread cubes around to make sure it browns evenly. 
 In a small bowl, beat the  eggs  with the  cinnamon . 
 Put the  egg  mixture over the bread and fry until the bread is brown.
