If you want a twist on the classic salad and love  avocados  and  spinach , this is the recipe for you!
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 package of ready pack baby  spinach 
 ½ package of  cherry tomatoes 
 1 medium  cucumber 
 1 x 4 oz package of crumbled  feta  (Athenos tomato basil or peppercorn is best) 
 1 or 2  Hass avocados 
 1 or 2 thin slices of  red onion  (quartered) 
 ¼ cup chopped  walnuts 
 Paul Newman's Parmesan-roasted garlic  dressing  (to taste) 
 lengthwise slices of  red pepper  (optional) 
 fresh cracked  pepper  (to taste) 
 Directions : 
 Place spinach in salad bowl. 
 Halve  cherry tomatoes  and place in salad bowl. 
 With a vegetable peeler, peel  cucumber  lengthwise in a striped pattern (skin/ flesh/ skin/ flesh etc.) 
 Cut  cucumber  in half (lengthwise), slice  cucumber  width-wise as thick as you desire. 
 chop   avocado  into cubes and place in bowl. 
 Add red  onion ,  red pepper  strips (optional),  feta , and  walnuts . 
 Add  salad dressing  and cracked  pepper  to taste. 
 Toss until ingredients are well mixed.
