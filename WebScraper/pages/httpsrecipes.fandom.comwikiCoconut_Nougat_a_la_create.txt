 Ingredients : 
 600g grated  Coconut 
 600g  Sugar 
 200ml  Vanilla essence Â  
 1 pinch Mali-Melo Colouring 
 200g  Condensed milk 
 2 tsp  Nutmeg 
 Directions : 
 Add in all the ingredients except the food colouring and condensed milk into a cooking pot, preferably made out of iron, and bring to a boil on the stove. 
 Let the mixture boil until the coconut is thoroughly cooked. 
 Once cooked empty the cooked mixture into 3 bowls 2 for the colours and one to have naturally coloured nougat. 
 Once the yellow and red coloured mixture is ready, place the coconut nougat 
 back on the flames and add condensed milk]for each of the coloured nougats (make sure that you cook it up to 10 minutes till it becomes sticky) 
 
 Follow the same procedures for the other two mixtures as well. 
 Use a spoon and scoop the mixtures on a baking tray. 
 Allow them to cool for 30 minutes. 
 Garnish with some mint leaves and serve the nougats on a banana leaf and enjoy.
