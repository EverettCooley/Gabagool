Basic  beer   bread 
 
 3 c  flour 
 3 3/4 ts  baking powder 
 2 1/4 ts  salt 
 1 cn  beer 
 1 tb  Honey 
 
Grease Loaf Pan. Combine  flour ,  baking powder ,  salt ,  beer , And  Honey  InLarge Bowl, Stir Together Until Well Mixed. Bake in Preheated 350 f OvenFor 45 Minutes. Turn on Rack And Cool.
 
Enjoy.
