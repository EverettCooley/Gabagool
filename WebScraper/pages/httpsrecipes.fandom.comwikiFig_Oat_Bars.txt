Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Fisher Estate in Rockwall, Texas in 1980.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups  flour 
 ½ teaspoon  soda 
 ¼ teaspoon  cinnamon 
 ½ cup  butter  softened 
 ½ cup  honey 
 ¼ cup  milk 
 2  egg whites 
 2 cups  oat bran  cereal 
 1 cup chopped  figs 
 Directions : 
 Stir together flour, soda and cinnamon then set aside. 
 In large mixing bowl beat together butter, honey, milk and egg whites. 
 Add flour mixture stirring until combined. 
 Mix cereal and figs then drop by tablespoon onto lightly greased baking sheet. 
 Flatten slightly with bottom of floured glass then bake at 375°F for 10 minutes.
