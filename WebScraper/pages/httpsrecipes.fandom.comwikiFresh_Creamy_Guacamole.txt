 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 avocados 
 1 roma  tomato

 1/8 tsp  chili powder  or cayanne  pepper 
 2 Tbsp  sour cream 
 Possible additions: 
 2 Tbsp Chopped  green onion 
 lime juice  ( a few splashes) 
 Chopped  hot pepper 
 1 Tbsp Salsa (as an optional addition) 
 Directions : 
 Chop  tomato  and mince  garlic .
 Mix mashed avocados and the rest of the ingredients. Add any or all of the additions, if desired. 
 Chill, serve, and eat.
