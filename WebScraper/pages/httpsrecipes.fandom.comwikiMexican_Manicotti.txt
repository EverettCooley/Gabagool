 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb lean ground  Beef 
 1 can 16 oz  refried beans 
 2 1/2 tbs.  chili powder 
 1 1/2 tbs. dried  oregano 
 1 pkg  manicotti  shells 8 oz 
 2 1/2 Cups  water 
 1 jar (16 oz) picante sauce 
 2 Cups (16 oz)  sour cream 
 1 Cup (4 oz) shredded  Monterey jack  or Mexican 
 style  Cheese 
 1/4 cup sliced  green onions 
 sliced  ripe olives  (optional) 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl combine the UNCOOKED  Beef , beans,  chili powder , &  oregano . 
 Spoon into UNCOOKED  manicotti  shells, arrange in a greased 13"X9"X2" baking dish. 
 Combine  water  & picante sauce, pour over shells. cover & refrigerate overnight. 
 Remove from refrigerator 30 minutes before baking. Cover & bake @ 350 degrees for 1 hour. 
 Uncover, spoon  sour cream  over the top, sprinkle with  Cheese ,  Onion  & if desired. 
 Bake 5–10 minutes longer or until  Cheese  is melted, yield 8 servings
