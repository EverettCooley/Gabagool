Velvet  chocolate  Glaze
 
 1 oz  unsweetened chocolate 
 -Squares
 
 2 tb  butter 
 2 tb  light corn syrup 
 1 c  powdered sugar 
 1 tb  milk 
 1 ts  vanilla 
 
Melt  chocolate  Over Warm  water . Remove From Heat; Blend InButter And Syrup. Stir in  powdered sugar ,  milk  AndVanilla; Mix Well.
 
Spread Over Brownies, Cakes, Etc.
 
Enjoy.
