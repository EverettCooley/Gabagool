Ingredients : 
 1 oz  Grand Marnier ®  orange liqueur 
 1 oz Tanqueray®  gin 
 juice of 1/4  lemons 
 pineapple juice 
 ginger ale 
 Directions : 
 Mix over  ice  in a highball glass and fill the rest of the glass with equal parts  pineapple juice  and  ginger ale .
