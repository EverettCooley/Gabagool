 About Curuba : 
 Wikipedia Article  About Curuba on Wikipedia 
 Banana passionfruits ("curuba" in Spanish) are the fruits of several plants in the genus Passiflora, and are therefore related to the  passion fruit . They look somewhat like a straight, small banana with rounded ends.
 A yellow, oval shaped passion fruit with bright yellow skin and orange pulp. Pulp is edible and very tasty, though generally not as esteemed as the purple passion fruit.
