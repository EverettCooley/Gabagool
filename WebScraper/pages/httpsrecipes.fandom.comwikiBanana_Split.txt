      Banana Split     A dessert made of a banana cut in half length-wise and placed in an individual-size bowl. It is topped with 3 scoops of ice cream (traditionally chocolate, vanilla, and strawberry) over which sweet syrups are poured (usually chocolate, butterscotch, and marshmallow). It is then topped with rosettes of whipped cream and a maraschino cherry.
