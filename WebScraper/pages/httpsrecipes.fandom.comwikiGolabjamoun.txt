 Description : 
 sweet potato  Cakes. Easy and tasty! Preparation and cooking time quick - 40 minutes or less. 
 Makes 5-6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 p½ cup of  milk 
 500 grams of  sweet potatoes 
 ½ cup of wheat  flour 
 50 – 100 grams of  sugar 
 2 sticks  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Wash and peel  sweet potatoes , boil in  milk  and mash. 
 Add ground  cinnamon  and  flour . 
 Knead into dough and shape into small balls. 
 Fry at moderate temperature.
