Ingredients : 
 1 large bunch of  broccoli , rough chopped 
 1 x 2-inch knob of  ginger , minced 
 2 cloves of  garlic , minced 
 3 tablespoons  olive  or  sesame oil 
 3 tablespoons  oyster sauce 
 2 tablespoons  fish sauce 
 2 tablespoons  chicken broth  or water 
 2 teaspoons dry  sherry  or  mirin 
 juice of Â½ a  lemon 
 chopped  green onions 
 2 tablespoons toasted  sesame seeds 
 Directions : 
 Heat oil in wok over medium high. 
 Add ginger and garlic, stir 10 seconds. 
 Add broccoli and cook until dark green, about 6-10 minutes. 
 Add sauce and cook 2 minutes-add more chicken broth a tablespoon at a time if it reduces too quickly. 
 Plate broccoli. 
 Squeeze lemon juice over broccoli and top with a sprinkling of green onions and sesame seeds. 
 Enjoy!
