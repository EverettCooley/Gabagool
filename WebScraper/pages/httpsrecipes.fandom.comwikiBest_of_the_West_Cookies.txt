Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 4 dozen cookies 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  butter 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 cup  brown sugar 
 1  egg 
 1 cup  oil 
 1 cup  rolled oats 
 1 cup  corn flakes  — crushed 
 ½ cup  coconut  — shredded 
 ½ cup  pecans  — chopped 
 3½ cups  all-purpose flour 
 1 teaspoon  baking soda 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven 325°F. 
 Cream together  butter  and sugars until light and fluffy. 
 Add  egg , mixing well, then  salad oil , mixing well. 
 Add  oats , cornflake, coconut , and nuts, stirring well. 
 Then add  flour , soda, and  salt . 
 Place parchment paper on sheet pan, scoop cookies to be size of  walnuts , place onpan. 
 Flatten with a fork dipped in  water . 
 Bake for 12 minutes, or until lightly golden brown. 
 Allow to cool for a few minutes before removing from pan. 
 Enjoy.
