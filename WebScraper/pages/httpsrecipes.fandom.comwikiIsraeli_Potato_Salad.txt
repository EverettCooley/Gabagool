Ingredients : 
 6 large  potatoes 
 2 hard boiled  eggs 
 2 small  onions 
 3 pickled  cucumbers 
 ¾ cup  mayonnaise 
 1 tbsp  mustard 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  black pepper 
 1 tbsp  vinegar 
 1 tsp  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Place unpeeled  potatoes  and  eggs  in pot of  water , boil for 30 minutes. 
 Set  eggs  aside. 
 While still warm, peel  potatoes . 
 Set aside to cool. 
 Slice  potatoes  into ½ inch cubes. 
 Chop  onions  and add to  potatoes , cube  cucumbers . 
 Add to potato- onion  mixture. 
 Mix  mayonnaise ,  mustard ,  salt ,  pepper ,  vinegar  and  sugar  together; add to  potato  mixture. 
 Mix together. 
 Chill. 
 Then add hard boiled  eggs  to salad and serve.
