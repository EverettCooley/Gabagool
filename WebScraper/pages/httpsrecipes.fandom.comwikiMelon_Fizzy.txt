 Description : 
A sparkling cold combination of watermelon, cantaloupe, or honeydew melon and a ginger ale is a remarkable sweet favorite made as a beverage. Keep the ginger ale freezing to serve up a divine treat with other desserts or fruits.         
 Ingredients : 
 1 whole honeydew, cantaloupe, or watermelon 
 1 liter ginger ale 
 Directions : 
 Cut or scoop the watermelon, honeydew, or cantaloupe into cubes or balls. 
 Pour fizzy ginger ale over for a sparkling appetizer.
