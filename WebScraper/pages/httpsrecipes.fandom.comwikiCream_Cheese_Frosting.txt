 Description : 
 This rich frosting has divine secrets: cream cheese, butter, vanilla, and powdered sugar. But you can use the homemade frosting on any dessert, while it also keeps a stone-cold, sugary aroma with the vanilla content.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  cream cheese , softened 
 ¼ cup butter  or  (½ stick Parkay  margarine , softened) 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla  extract 
 4 cups  powdered sugar , sifted 
 Directions : 
 Beat  cream cheese ,  margarine , and  vanilla  until fluffy. 
 Add the sifted  powdered sugar  ½-cup at a time until all is used. 
 If frosting becomes too stiff, then thin with a few drops of  water . Reserve the cream cheese frosting in the refrigerator but spoon or scoop this into a fresh container. 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Servings:  5 cups or 3-6 tablespoons
