Description : 
 Contributed by Jenn Mom2sam & Tiny at  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can (16 ounces)  black-eyed peas , drained 
 3  green onions , chopped with tops 
 ½ cup  sour cream 
 1 tsp  garlic salt 
 ½ cup  salsa 
 4 slices  bacon , cooked and crumbled 
 Directions : 
 Reserving ⅓ cup, place peas in a blender. Process until smooth. 
 Blend in onions, sour cream and garlic salt. 
 Transfer to a bowl and stir in reserved peas and bacon. 
 Serve with  tortilla chips , bagel chips or  corn chips .
