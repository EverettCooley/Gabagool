Description 
 This is one of Bess Truman's favorite dishes. Bess Truman had a well-equipped kitchen and often prepared breakfast or lunch for her family. The Trumans also employed several African American women who cooked for the family. 
 
 Mrs. Truman's Recipes  from the Harry S. Truman National Historic Site, public domain government resource 
 Ingredients 
 
 3 tablespoons cooking  oil 
 diced up  cabbage 
 Directions 
 Line pan with cooking oil. 
 Place cabbage in pan. Fry until tender and brown.
