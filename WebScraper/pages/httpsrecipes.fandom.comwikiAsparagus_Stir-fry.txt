Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp  oil 
 2 bunches  asparagus , cut into generous bite sized lengths 
 5 small  courgettes , in chunky slices 
 1 cup adzuki or crunchy combo  sprouts 
 Directions : 
 The sprouts in this wonderfully easy stir-fry give it a lovely nutty flavour and texture! use flavoured  oil  if you like. 
 Heat the  oil  in a non-stick pan, or wok. 
 Sauté the  asparagus  and courgettes for 3–4 minutes or until barely tender, toss in the sprouts just before serving.
