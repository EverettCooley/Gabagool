 Ingredients : 
 2 cup mashed  banana 
 1 tbsp  butter 
 6 tbsp  carob  powder 
 2 tsp  vanilla 
 3 tbsp  unbleached flour 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Blend thoroughly and spread on cool cake. 
 Sprinkle with chopped  pecans . 
 The frosting contains  carob , which is a safe (almost tastes like)  chocolate  substitute.
