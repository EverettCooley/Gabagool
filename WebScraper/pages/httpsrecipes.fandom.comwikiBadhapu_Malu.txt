Description : 
 Sri Lankan fried fish
 
 Ingredients : 
 450 g  fish  steaks 
 25 g  tamarind 
 50 ml  water , warm 
 ½ tsp  turmeric 
 ½ tsp  chile powder 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  pepper , black 
 ½ lt  oil 
 Directions : 
 Cut the fish steaks into six pieces. 
 Soak the  tamarind  in  water , remove the seeds and add to the together with the  turmeric ,  chile powder ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Leave to marinade for 10 minutes. 
 Heat the  oil  and fry until it turns brown. 
 Remove and drain well.
