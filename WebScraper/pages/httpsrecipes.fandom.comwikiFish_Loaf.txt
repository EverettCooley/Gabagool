 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1-1/2 lb (minced) 675g 
 1 cup  breadcrumbs 
 1 medium  onion  (grated) 
 1 small  sweet pepper  (chopped) 
 1 tblsp  lemon juice  or  vinegar  (to clean fish) 15ml 
 1 tsp  salt 5ml 
 1/2 tsp  black pepper  2ml 
 1/2 cup  Evaporated Milk  or fresh  milk  125ml 
 1 tblsp  margarine  15ml 
 2  cabbage  leaves 
 2 hard boiled  eggs 
 Directions : 
 Clean fish with  lemon juice  or  vinegar . 
 Boil fish-remove bones. (Mince) 
 In a mixing bowl-add fish, 1/2 bread crumbs,  milk ,  sweet pepper ,  Onion  and  salt . 
 Mix to a soft consistency. 
 Grease loaf tin-line with  cabbage  leaves. Add 1/2 mixture, put boiled eggs-Cover with remaining mixture. 
 Cover with bread crumbs and dot with  margarine  or strips of  Bacon . 
 Bake at 350 F for 30–35 minutes.
