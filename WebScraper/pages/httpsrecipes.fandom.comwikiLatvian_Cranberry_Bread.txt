 Ingredients : 
 2 cup  all-purpose flour  Sifted 
 3/4 cup  Sugar 
 3 1/2 tsp  baking powder 
 1/2 tsp  baking soda 
 1/2 tsp  salt 
 1  egg 
 2 tbl Grated  orange  Rind 
 1/2 cup  orange juice 
 3 tbl Melted  butter 
 2 tbl Hot  water 
 1 1/2 cup  cranberries  Halved 
 3/4 cup Chopped  nuts 
 Directions : 
 1. Sift  flour ,  Sugar ,  baking powder ,  baking soda  and  salt  into a large bowl.
 2. Beat  egg  lightly in a small bowl, stir in  orange  rind and juice,  butter  and hot  water . Add to dry 
ingredients; mix thoroughly. Fold in  cranberries  and  nuts . Turn batter into a well-greased 9x5x3-inch loaf pan.
 3.Bake at 350F oven for 50 minutes or until cake tester inserted in center comes out clean. Remove 
from pan; cool on wire rack.
