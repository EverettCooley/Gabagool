Ingredients : 
 1 bottle of  catsup 
 1 bottle of  chile sauce 
 ½ cup  brown sugar 
 1 Tbsp. prepared  mustard 
 1 tsp  celery salt 
 1 tsp  chili powder 
 ¼ cup  vinegar   or   lemon juice 
 salt  and  pepper 
 ¼ lb  oleo  (1 stick) 
 1 medium  onion  chopped fine 
 1 or 2 Tbsp  liquid smoke 
 Directions : 
 Combine ingredients (except the oleo, onion and liquid smoke) and put on stove and let simmer 
 In another pan combine the oleo and onion, let simmer until onion becomes almost mush 
 Strain onion and oleo into first mixture 
 Spoon to work it through. 
 Add Liquid smoke to taste
