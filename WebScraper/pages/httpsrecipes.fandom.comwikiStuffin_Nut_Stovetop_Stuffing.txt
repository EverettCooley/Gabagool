Description : 
 Serves 2 – 4
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 cups whole grain bread cubes 
 2 tbsp  olive oil 
 1  onion , finely chopped 
 2 stalks  celery , finely chopped 
 2 large  carrots , grated 
 2 cups  mushrooms , sliced 
 ½ cup  raisins 
 ½ cup  slivered almonds 
 1 cup boiling  water 
 1 tbsp veggie broth powder  or   soy sauce   OR   Braggs Liquid Aminos 
 2 tsp dried  sage 
 1 tsp  rosemary 
 ½ tsp  thyme 
 sea salt  and freshly ground  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 In a large pot, heat the  olive oil . 
 Add all the veggies, the  raisins  and the  almonds  and sauté until veggies are tender. 
 Stir in the herbs,  salt  and  pepper , and mix well. 
 Add the bread cubes and mix. 
 Add the veggie broth powder or soy sauce or Braggs to the boiling  water , and then pour into the bread mixture. 
 Stir well until bread absorbs the liquid, reduce heat to the lowest setting, cover and let steam for about 10 minutes. 
 Don't let it burn! serve with a flavorful vegan gravy.
