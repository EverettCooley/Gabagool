       Fenugreek leaves     
 About Fenugreek leaves : 
 The young leaves  and  sprouts  of fenugreek are eaten as greens, and the fresh or dried leaves are used to flavour other dishes. The dried leaves (called kasturi methi) have a bitter taste and a characteristically strong smell.
