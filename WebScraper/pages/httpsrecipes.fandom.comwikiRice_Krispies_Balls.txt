Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Henson Estate in Dallas, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 3 tablespoons  oleo 
 1 cup  sugar 
 2  eggs 
 1 pound cut  dates 
 1 cup chopped  nuts 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 3 cups  Rice Krispies 
 flaked  coconut 
 Directions : 
 Stir oleo,  sugar ,  eggs  and  dates  together in iron skillet over low heat and cook 20 minutes stirring constantly then take off fire and stir in  vanilla , chopped nuts and  Rice Krispies . 
 Shape into balls and roll in  coconut .
