Ingredients : 
 4 slices  tomato 
 1  basil  leaf 
 2 slices  red bell pepper 
 1 tbsp balsamic vinaigrette  salad dressing 
 1 tbsp zesty  honey mustard 
 2 slices toasted  rye bread 
 2 pieces  lettuce 
 Directions : 
 Put all the ingredients together to form a sandwich. 
 Eat sandwich 
 Repeat if desired.
