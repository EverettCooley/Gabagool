Description : 
 Makes 2 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ pound boneless beef  sirloin steak , cut ½-inch thick 
 1 teaspoon  vegetable oil 
 ⅛ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅛ teaspoon ground  white pepper 
 ½ cup sliced fresh  mushrooms 
 ½ cup  snow peas 
 ½ cup diagonally sliced  celery 
 ¼ cup sliced  green onions 
 ¼ cup  red pepper  strips 
 1 teaspoon minced fresh  ginger 
 1 teaspoon  cornstarch 
 1 tablespoon  soy sauce 
 1 tablespoon  water 
 1 cup hot cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Cut  beef  diagonally across grain into ⅛-inch slices. 
 Heat  oil  in wok or medium skillet over medium-high heat. 
 Add  beef ,  salt ,  pepper  and stir-fry until  beef  is browned. 
 Add  mushrooms ,  snow peas ,  celery ,  onions  and  pepper  strips; stir-fry until vegetables are tender crisp. 
 Blend  ginger ,  cornstarch , soy sauce and  water  in small bowl; add to  beef  and cook, stirring, until sauce is thickened. 
 Serve over  rice .
