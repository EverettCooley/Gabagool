 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 c. dried  Lentils , prerinsed 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 2  carrots , shredded 
 3 c.  chicken broth 
 2  bay leaves 
 Pinch dried  thyme 
 salt  or  garlic salt  &  pepper  to taste 
 1 c. diced cooked  Chicken  or  Turkey 
 1 c. chopped  Onion 
 4 ribs  celery , shredded 
 2 tbsp. chopped fresh  parsley 
 3 c.  water 
 1/2 tsp. ground  cumin 
 Pinch  turmeric  or  curry  powder, optional 
 2 tbsp.  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients, except  lemon juice  and poultry. 
 In a large soup pot. 
 Cover and simmer for 50â€“60 minutes or until  Lentils  are tender. 
 Stir in  lemon juice  and  Chicken  or  Turkey  and heat through. 
 Soup will be thick and may be thinned by adding additional  water . 
 Serves 4 generous meal sized portions.
