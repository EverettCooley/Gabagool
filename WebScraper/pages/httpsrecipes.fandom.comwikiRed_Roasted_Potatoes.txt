 Description : 
 This red-hot potato dish is mixed with olive oil and herbs and cooked to steamy perfection to help maintain its golden-brown aroma. Italian seasoning sometimes works for this for garnishing, too.
 
 Ingredients : 
 6  red potatoes 
 2-3 tbsp.  olive oil 
 2 tbsp. dry  parsley 
 1  onion , chopped, grated, or minced; red, white, or yellow only 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1 tablespoon  salt , iodized or rock only 
 1 tablespoon  pepper , black only 
 garlic powder 
 Directions : 
 Scrub  potatoes  well and quarter them. 
 Toss (or rub) with the remaining ingredients and bake at 375° F until tender (about 45–60 minutes). Serve hot and plain or with other main dishes, side dishes, or appetizers.
