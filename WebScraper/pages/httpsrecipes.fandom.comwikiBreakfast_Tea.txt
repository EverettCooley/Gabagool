 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups of  water 
 3 Tablespoons  Sugar 
 2 tea bags or 2 tsp. of loose tea 
 1 Tablespoon  ground ginger  (or a tsp. ground  cardamom ) 
 a 170g can of unsweetened  evaporated milk 
 Directions : 
 Put the  water ,  Sugar  and tea into a teapot and bring to a boil over high heat. 
 Boil for 2â€“3 minutes. 
 Add the  ground ginger  (or  cardamom ) and the canned  milk . 
 Bring again to a boil. 
 Then immediately remove from the heat (before it rises and comes out of the teapot). 
 Strain into cups or a thermos and enjoy piping hot.
