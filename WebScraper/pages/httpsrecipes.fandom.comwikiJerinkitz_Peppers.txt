Ingredients : 
 1  peck  of  bell peppers 
 6 qts  water 
 3 qts  white vinegar 
 8 tbsp  sugar 
 6 tbsp  salt 
 1½ cups  olive oil 
 Directions : 
 Use 1 peck  bell peppers . 
 Remove seeds and cut  peppers  in half. 
 Bring to a boil 6 qts  water , 3 qts  white vinegar , 8 tbsp  Sugar , 6 tbsp   salt  and 1½ cups  olive oil . 
 Drop  peppers  into liquid and boil a few minutes, but do not overcook. 
 Pack in jars, pour boiling liquid over them and seal.
