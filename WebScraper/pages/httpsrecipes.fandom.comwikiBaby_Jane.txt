 Ingredients : 
 1/2 oz  vodka 
 1/2 oz DeKuyper® Buttershots liqueur 
 1/2 oz Bailey's® Irish  cream 
 1/2 oz  gin 
 2 scoops  vanilla   ice   cream 
 Directions : 
 Blend with  ice  and serve with a  cherry  on top.
