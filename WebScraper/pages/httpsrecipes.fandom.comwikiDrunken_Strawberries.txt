Ingredients : 
 1½ pints  strawberries , hulled and halved 
 ¼ cup artificial  sweetener 
 2 tablespoons  rum  flavoring 
 1 teaspoon pure  vanilla extract 
 1 teaspoon fresh  lemon juice 
 3 cups fat-free  ice cream  (optional) 
 6 slices  angel food cake  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Stir together first five ingredients in a medium bowl. 
 Cover and refrigerate overnight. 
 Serve chilled in bowls, or with  ice cream  or  cake .
