 Ingredients : 
 16 medium  oranges 
 Sugar  to taste 
 1 teaspoon  orange blossom water 
 water  (or seltzer) and  ice cubes 
 sprigs of fresh  mint , to garnish 
 Directions : 
 1) Squeeze the juice from the  oranges  and sweeten to taste with the  Sugar . 
 2) Add the  orange blossom water  and mix well. 
 3) Serve diluted with  ice  cold  water  and garnished with  mint .
