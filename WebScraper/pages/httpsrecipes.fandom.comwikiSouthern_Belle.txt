Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1¼ oz  Tennessee whiskey 
 8 oz  pineapple juice 
 ¾ oz  triple sec 
 2 oz  orange juice 
 1 splash  grenadine 
 Directions : 
 Combine Tennessee  whiskey ,  triple sec , and juices in tall glass with  ice . 
 Top with  Grenadine  and stir once.
