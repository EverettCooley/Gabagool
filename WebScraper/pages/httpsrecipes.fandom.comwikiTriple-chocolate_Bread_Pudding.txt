Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Trisha Pape 
 Makes 8 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups day old  French bread , torn into pieces 
 8 squares  semisweet chocolate  (1 oz each), cut into ½ inch pieces 
 ¼ cup  butter , melted 
 4  eggs 
 2 cups  chocolate milk 
 1 cup  sugar 
 ½ cup  cocoa 
 ⅛ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  vanilla extract 
 powdered sugar 
 whipped cream ,  chocolate shavings , fresh  strawberries 
 Directions : 
 Combine bread, chocolate and butter in a large bowl; toss gently. 
 Divide mixture evenly among eight 8 oz ramekins. 
 Beat eggs; gradually add milk and next 4 ingredients, mixing well. 
 Pour egg mixture evenly over bread mixture in ramekins. 
 Let stand 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
 Place ramekins in a large shallow baking pan. 
 Pour hot water into pan to a depth of 1 inch. 
 Bake uncovered, at 350°F for 40 to 45 minutes or until set. 
 Sprinkle with powdered sugar. 
 Garnish, if desired.
