Makes 6 servings. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 2  eggs , slightly beaten 
 1 1/2 cups (6-ounces) grated pasteurized process  American cheese , divided 
 1 small  Onion , cut into 1/8-inch pieces 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 1 14-1/2- to 16-ounce can  tomatoes , drained (2 cups) 
 1 6-ounce can  tomato  paste 
 1/2 teaspoon  oregano  leaves 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1/8 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 5 ounces fully-cooked  Ham , cut into 1/2-inch pieces (1 cup) 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  eggs  and 3/4 cup  Cheese . 
 Press evenly with spoon into well-buttered 12-inch pizza pan to form crust. 
 Bake 20 minutes at 450 degrees. Remove crust from oven. 
 Heat  oil  in a medium-size saucepan. 
 Add  Onion  and cook over medium heat until soft but not brown. 
 Add  tomatoes  to  Onion ; break up with a spoon. 
 Stir in  tomato  paste,  oregano ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Cook over medium heat about 7 minutes, or until bubbly. 
 Pour sauce into crust. 
 Sprinkle with  Ham  and remaining 3/4 cup  Cheese . 
 Bake 10 minutes longer or until  Cheese  is melted.
