Makes 4 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 4  Catfish  fillets 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine , melted 
 2 tablespoons  lemon juice , divided 
 1 teaspoon  dill weed , divided 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1 small  zucchini , cut into ribbon-like strips* 
 1  carrot , cut into ribbon-like strips* 
 1 tablespoon  Mayonnaise 
 1 4 to 7-ounce package  rice  pilaf mix, prepared according to package directions 
 Directions : 
 Brush  Catfish  with  butter  and sprinkle with 1 tablespoon  lemon juice , 1/2 teaspoon  dill weed , and  salt . 
 Broil fillets 8 to 10 minutes or until fish flakes easily with fork. 
 Stir  zucchini ,  carrot ,  Mayonnaise , remaining 1 tablespoon  lemon juice , and remaining 1/2 teaspoon  dill weed  into hot  rice . 
 Cover and let stand 5 minutes. 
 Serve vegetable  rice  with broiled  Catfish .
