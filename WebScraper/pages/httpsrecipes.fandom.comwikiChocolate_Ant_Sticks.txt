chocolate  Ant Sticks
 
 12 Ounces  chocolate chips  or  white chocolate chips 
 24 8 Inch Pretzel Rods 
 chocolate  or Colored Sprinkles
 
In a Heatproof Bowl Melt The  chocolate  Over a Pot of Simmering  water . Dunk ThePretzel Rods Halfway Into The  chocolate . Immediately Roll  chocolate -Coated Pretzel Rods in Sprinkles. Transfer to Waxed Paper-Lined Sheet Pan And Allow to Cool Until Hardened.
 
Yield: 24  chocolate -Coated Pretzel Rods.
