Ingredients : 
 ½ cup of  butter 
 grated yellow rind of 1  lemon   or  1 teaspoonful of  vanilla extract 
 ⅓ cup of  sugar  (granulated) 
 1  egg  beaten light (not separated) 
 1¼ cups of  flour 
 Directions : 
 First cream the  butter ; gradually beat in the  sugar , the  egg , flavoring and  flour . 
 Drop with a teaspoon onto a buttered baking sheet or, preferably, turn the mixture into a pastry bag with star tube (half inch) attached and dispose on the baking sheets in rings or figures the shape of the letter s; dredge with  granulated sugar  and bake in a quick oven. 
 These containing no liquid but  egg , are rather rich and are for occasional not daily use.
