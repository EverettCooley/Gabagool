 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 Slaw 
 1 small head  green cabbage , halved and cored 
 1/2 head  red cabbage , cored 
 3 yellow  bell peppers , seeded and cored 
 - 
 Blue  Cheese  Dressing 
 1/2 cup  olive oil 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1/2 tsp.  salt 
 1/2 tsp. freshly ground  black pepper 
 2 1/2 to 3 Tbs.  white wine vinegar 
 2 oz. blue  Cheese , crumbled 
 Directions : 
 1. To make Slaw: Slice cabbages into fine shreds. Place in large bowl. Slice  peppers  very thinly lengthwise, and add to bowl. 
 
 2. To make Blue  Cheese  Dressing: Place all ingredients in quart jar, screw on lid and shake vigorously. Pour half over slaw, and toss. Refrigerate remainder for future use.
