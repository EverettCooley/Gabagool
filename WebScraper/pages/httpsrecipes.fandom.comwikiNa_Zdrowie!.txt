Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 oz Frozen Belvedere Cytrus  vodka  served in a frozen shot glass. 
 1 paper thin wheel of  lemon 
 1/2 dipped in ground espresso 
 1/2 in  Superfine sugar 
 Directions : 
 Eat the  lemon  wheel and shoot the  vodka .
