 Ingredients : 
 Topping 
 1 1/3 cup  flour 
 pinch of  salt 
 3/4 cup  brown sugar 
 6 tablespoons  unsalted butter , cubed at room temperature 
 Filling 
 3 medium  Apples , peeled, cored, cut into 1" x 1/2" slices 
 1/4 cup  brown sugar 
 1 tablespoon  flour 
 1/4 teaspoon  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Crumble:
 
 Combine flour and brown sugarÂ in a large bowl and mix well. 
 Cut in the butter a little at a time, until the mixture resembles coarse meal. 
 Filling:
 
 Place the apples in a large bowl, sprinkle with brown sugar/flour/cinnamon mixture and stir gently to mix. 
 
Baking Directions:
 
 Grease a shallow 9" pie plate 
 Spoon the fruit mixture into the bottom and sprinkle the crumble mixture on top. 
 Bake at 350 for 50 minutes, until the topping is browned and the fruit mixture bubbling. 
 Serve with  whipped cream  or  ice cream .
