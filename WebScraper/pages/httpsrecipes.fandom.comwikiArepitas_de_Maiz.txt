Ingredients : 
 1 cup of  corn flour 
 ¼ cup of  oil 
 2 tablespoons of  milk 
 1 teaspoons of  sugar 
 ½ teaspoons of  salt 
 1  egg 
 Directions : 
 Mix all the ingredients except the  oil . 
 In a shallow frying pan heat the  oil . 
 Pour the mix one spoonful at a time into pan, making small cakes. 
 Fry the cakes till golden brown on both sides. 
 Serve immediately.
