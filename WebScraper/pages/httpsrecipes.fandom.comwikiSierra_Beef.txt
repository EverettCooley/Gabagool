 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 16 	ounces 90% lean ground  Beef  (or  Turkey ) 
 1 1/2 	cups finely chopped  celery  1 (10 3/4 	ounce) can low-fat  cream  of  Mushroom  soup 
1 1/4 	cups  water  
2 	cups Minute  rice  
1 	teaspoon dried  parsley  flakes 
1/2 	cup nonfat  sour cream 
 
 Directions : 
 Spray large skillet with butter-flavored  cooking spray , saute meat and  celery  for 8 to 10 minutes. 
 Stir in soup and  water . 
 Add uncooked  rice  and  parsley  flakes. 
 Mix well to combine. 
 Lower heat, cover and simmer for 6 to 8 minutes or until  rice  is tender, stirring occasionally. 
 Stir in  sour cream . 
 Continue simmering for 2 to 3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
