Description : 
 For that harvest / Autumn flavor.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Makes 32 bars. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (6-ounce) package diced  dried mixed fruit  bits 
 1 cup chopped  banana  (about 2 medium) 
 ⅔ cup  orange juice   [1] 
 1½ teaspoons  apple pie spice  or ground  cinnamon , divided use 
 1¾ cups  whole wheat flour  or  all-purpose flour 
 1½ cups  oats  (quick or old fashioned, uncooked) 
 ½ pound (2 sticks)  margarine  or  butter , softened 
 1 cup firmly packed  brown sugar 
 ½ cup chopped  nuts 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 375°F. 
 In medium bowl, combine dried fruit, banana, orange juice and 1 teaspoon of apple pie spice; set aside. 
 In medium bowl, combine flour, oats and remaining ½ teaspoon apple pie spice; mix well. 
 In large bowl, beat margarine and sugar together with electric mixer until creamy. 
 Add oat mixture; beat until crumbly. 
 Reserve ¾ cup mixture for topping. 
 Press remaining oat mixture onto bottom of 13 x 9-inch baking pan. 
 Bake 13 to 15 minutes or until light golden brown. 
 Spread fruit evenly over crust to within ¼-inch of edge. 
 Add nuts to reserved oat mixture; mix well. 
 Sprinkle evenly over fruit; pat down lightly.
 Bake 16 to 20 minutes or until golden brown. 
 Cool completely; cut into bars. 
 Store loosely covered. 
 Notes : 
 
 ↑   If using old fashioned oats, decrease orange juice to ½ cup.
