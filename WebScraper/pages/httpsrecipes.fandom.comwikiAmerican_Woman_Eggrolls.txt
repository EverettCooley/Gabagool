Description : 
 The guys sure love this recipe. Enjoy!
 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  ground beef  (sirloin preferably) 
 2 packages  egg roll wraps 
 2 medium  onions 
 1 cup  celery 
 1 large  bell pepper  (red, yellow or green) 
 2  jalapeno peppers  (optional) 
 salt  and  pepper  (to taste) 
 seasoned salt  (to taste) 
 garlic powder  or salt (to taste) 
 hot sauce  (green or red) optional 
 vegetable oil  or  butter 
 Directions : 
 Heat up vegetable oil in deep fryer or large saucepan. 
 Begin by using vegetable oil or butter in frying pan. 
 Add onion, celery and all peppers, cooking until al dente. 
 Add beef and seasonings to taste. 
 If desired, add hot sauce. 
 Saut√© until brown, then set aside. 
 Add a cup of water and begin to make egg rolls. 
 Take egg roll wrap and moisten the sides with water, add a large teaspoon of meat to the center and seal. 
 Cook until golden, turning to cook all sides. 
 Repeat until finished. 
 Serve with  Sweet and Sour Sauce  for dipping. 
 Serve with cold beer.
