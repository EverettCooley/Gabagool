 Ingredients 
 3 packages (8 oz.)  cream cheese 
 6 tablespoons  milk 
 2 tablespoons  Worcestershire sauce 
 3/4 cup  crab  meat 
 Directions 
 Soften cream cheese to room temperature. Mix all ingredients. 
 Bake 15 minutes at 350 degrees. Serve with  chips  or  crackers .
