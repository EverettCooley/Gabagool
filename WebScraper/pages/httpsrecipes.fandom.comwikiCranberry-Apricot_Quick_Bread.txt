Description : 
 Contributed by Jenn B aka Mom2Sam and Tiny via  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 1 loaf 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  margarine 
 ¾ cup  sugar 
 2  eggs 
 3 cups  flour 
 1 tbsp  baking powder 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  baking soda 
 1 cup  orange juice 
 1 tsp grated  orange rind 
 1 cup coarse chopped  cranberries 
 ½ cup chopped  dried apricots 
 Sweet orange spread : 
 1 tbsp  10x sugar 
 1 tsp  orange rind 
 Directions : 
 Beat margarine and sugar until fluffy. 
 Add eggs, one at a time, mixing well. 
 Add combined dry ingredients alternately with juice, mixing well after each addition. 
 Stir in cranberries, apricots and orange rind. 
 Pour into greased and floured 9x5 inch loaf pan. 
 Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour and 15 minutes. 
 Cool 5 minutes, remove from pan. 
 Serve with sweet orange spread. 
 Sweet orange spread : 
 Mix orange rind and 10x sugar well. 
 Chill.
