Description : 
 Yield: 24 Servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 3  California avocados  (1½ pounds) 
 ⅓ cup finely chopped  onion 
 ⅓ cup  lemon juice 
 ¼ cup prepared horseradish (or to taste, depending on the strength of the horseradish) 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Roughly mash avocados. 
 Stir in finely chopped  onion ,  lemon juice , prepared horseradish and  salt . 
 Chill 1 hour to marry flavors. 
 Serve 2 tablespoons on roast  beef  and corned  beef  sandwiches or alone on a  bagel  or with smoked salmon.
