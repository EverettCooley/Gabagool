 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 shots Stolichnaya  vodka 
 1 1/2 shots De Kuyper  triple sec 
 1 1/2 shots Freshly squeezed  lemon juice 
 garnish:  orange  wedge 
 Glass: Martini
 
 Directions : 
 Shake all ingredients with  ice  and strain into glass.
