Cherry Cream Sensation
 Very good and so easy to make.
 10 ice cream sandwiches 
1 can cherry pie filling 
1 (12 oz.) container frozen whipped topping, thawed 
Chocolate sprinkles, optional 
 Arrange ice cream sandwiches in a single layer in 9x13 pan. Top with 
pie filling, whipped topping and chocolate sprinkles if desired. Cover 
and freeze for at least 4 hours. May be frozen up to 2 months. Thaw for 
10 mins. before serving.
