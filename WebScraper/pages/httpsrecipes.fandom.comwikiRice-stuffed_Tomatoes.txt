Description : 
 Makes 4 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x 10-ounce package frozen chopped  spinach , thawed and undrained 
 2 cups hot cooked  rice , cooked in  chicken broth 
 ½ cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 ½ teaspoon  seasoned salt 
 ½ teaspoon  garlic powder 
 ½ teaspoon dried crushed  basil  leaves 
 4 large  tomatoes 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 
 Directions : 
 Purée  spinach  in food processor or blender. 
 Blend  spinach ,  rice ,  cheese ,  salt ,  garlic powder  and  basil . 
 Slice tops of  tomatoes . 
 Scoop out pulp of  tomatoes . 
 Spoon  rice  mixture into  tomatoes . 
 Place  tomatoes  in shallow baking dish. 
 Drizzle with  olive oil . 
 Bake at 400°F 15 to 20 minutes.
