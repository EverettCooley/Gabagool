 Description : 
 This versatile Moroccan dessert can be served hot or cold and is suitable for vegetarians.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 125g  butter 
 125g  caster sugar 
 4  eggs 
 1 teaspoon  baking powder 
 125g plain white  flour 
 1 teaspoon  cinnamon 
 1 teaspoon  nutmeg 
 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves 
 120ml  milk 
 1/2 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 75g  dates 
 50g roughly chopped  walnuts 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Preheat the oven to 325F and grease an 8Â inch cake tin.
 Mix the  butter  and  Sugar  together in a large bowl until blended and fluffy.
 Beat the  eggs  in a separate small bowl before adding to the  butter  and  Sugar  and mixing well.
 In another large bowl, mix the  baking powder ,  flour ,  cinnamon ,  nutmeg , and cloves.
 Mix the contents of the two bowls together and beat well. Add the  milk  and  vanilla  and beat again to make sure the mixture is smooth and even.
 Add the chopped  dates  and  walnuts  at this point, and stir again to ensure they are evenly distributed.
 When happy that there are no lumps, put the mixture into the prepared cake tin and bake for 30 minutes.
 This recipe makes one cake that serves about 8.
