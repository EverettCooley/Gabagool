Ingredients : 
 3  avocados 
 1 cup sliced  strawberries 
 1 cup seedless  grapes 
 1 cup fresh or drained canned  pineapple  tidbits 
 1 tsp  lemon juice 
 pinch of  salt 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 ½ cup  sour cream 
 ½ tsp  ginger 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Cut avocados into halves lengthwise. 
 Cut  avocado  balls with ball cutter. 
 Reserve shells. 
 Combine  avocado  balls,  berries ,  grapes  and  pineapple . 
 Add  lemon juice , dash of  salt  and  sugar . 
 Mix lightly. 
 Spoon into  avocado  shell. 
 Mix  cream , giner and ½ tsp  salt . 
 Spoon over fruit.
