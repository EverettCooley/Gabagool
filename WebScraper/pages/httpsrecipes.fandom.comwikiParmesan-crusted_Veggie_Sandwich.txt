Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  Vegan butter 
 ¼ cup soy  Parmesan cheese 
 salt  (to taste) 
 pepper  (to taste) 
 8 thick sliced firm vegan bread 
 1½ tablespoon  soy mayo 
 1½ tablespoon vegan  Dijon mustard 
 2  avocados , sliced 
 1 medium  tomato , sliced thinly 
 1 small  red onion , sliced 
 1½ cup  alfalfa sprouts 
 Vegan butter : 
 ¾ cup  soft tofu 
 2 tbsp  olive oil 
 pinch  salt 
 dash  turmeric 
 Directions : 
 Blend together the "butter", parmesan,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Spread a portion of the mixture on one side each slice of bread. 
 Grill vegan bread slices in batches on a non-stick frying pan for about 3 minutes, until crisp and deep brown. 
 When done, set aside. 
 In a small bowl, blend the  mayo  and  mustard . 
 On a piece of toast, spread a layer of the  mayo  mixture,  avocado ,  tomatoes ,  onions , and sprouts. 
 Place another toast slice on top and enjoy this incredibly delicious sandwich!! yum!.
