Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 1 to 2 teaspoons  curry powder 
 1 teaspoon seasoned  pepper 
 1 cup sliced  celery 
 2 medium  apples , peeled, cored, and sliced 
 Â¼ cup sliced  almonds 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter ; stir in  curry  powder and  pepper . 
 Add  celery ,  apples  and  almonds ; cook until tender crisp. 
 Stir in  rice  and heat thoroughly.
