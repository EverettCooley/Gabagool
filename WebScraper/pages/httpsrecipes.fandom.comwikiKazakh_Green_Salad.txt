 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 One or two fresh  cucumbers 
 One or two salted or pickled  cucumbers 
 Two or three  tomatoes 
 A handful of  black olives 
 100-150 g of  white cheese 
 A few shredded  lettuce  leaves 
 A splash or two of  olive oil 
 A splash of  balsamic vinegar 
 A large pinch of dried  oregano  or  marjoram 
 A little  salt 
 Directions : 
 Chop the  tomatoes ,  cucumbers  and  Cheese  into cubes. Mix all the ingredients together and you have an authentic Kazakh-Greek salad with modifications.
