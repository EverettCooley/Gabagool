Description : 
 Wow  bread  in a pressure cooker!!! I never would have thought of that. 
 
 Contributed by  Pressurecookerrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1  egg 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 1 cup  flour 
 ½ tsp  soda 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 1 cup  buttermilk 
 1 cup  nuts ,  dates  and/or  raisins 
 4 cups  water 
 Directions : 
 In pressure cooker with rack. 
 Combine  egg  and  sugar . 
 Sift  flour , soda and baking powder. 
 Add  buttermilk  alternately with sifted dry ingredients. 
 Mix well. 
 Add nuts and stuff. 
 Turn into buttered bowl and cover with wax paper. 
 Place bowl in cooker on rack with  water . 
 Place cover on cooker. 
 Steam without pressure weight for 15 minutes, then cook at cook position for 25 minutes, allow to cool naturally.
