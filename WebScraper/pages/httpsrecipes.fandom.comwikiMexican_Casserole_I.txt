Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  ground beef 
 1 large  onion , chopped 
 1 (8 oz)  taco sauce 
 1 (10 oz)  enchilada sauce 
 1 can  cream of chicken  soup 
 1 can  cream of mushroom  soup 
 soft corn  tortillas 
 1 (12 oz)  cheddar  cheese, shredded 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Sauté  ground beef . 
 Drain grease. 
 In a large bowl or pan, combine  ground beef ,  onion , sauces, and soups. 
 Line a greased casserole dish with crumbled (torn in pieces) soft  corn  tortillas. 
 Pour in meat mixture. 
 Bake for 20 minutes. 
 Top with  cheese  and bake 10 minutes more.
