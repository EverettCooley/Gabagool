      Cinnamon Swirl Raisin Bread     
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 cups  milk 
 1/4 cup  granulated sugar 
 2 teaspoons  salt 
 1 cup of unseasoned mashed  potatoes 
 1/2 cup warm  water 
 2 packages  instant yeast 
 7 1/2 cups  all-purpose flour 
 1 1/2 cups  Raisins 
 FILLING 
 1/2 cup  granulated sugar 
 2 teaspoons  cinnamon 
 1/2 cup of melted  butter 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Scald the  milk  in saucepan. 
 Add  Sugar ,  salt ,  butter  and mashed  potatoes . 
 Cool. 
 Add this to  milk  mixture, add  Raisins  and  flour . 
 Let it rise for 1 1/2 hours. 
 Divide dough into 2 loaves. 
 Roll out on floured surface and spread half of the filling on each roll. 
 Roll up and pinch edges together . 
 Place in 2 greased loaf pan, set in a warm place to rise for an hour. 
 Bake in loaf pans at 350* for 45–55 minutes. 
 Brush top with melted  butter  or  margarine . 
 Let cool in pans 10–15 minutes .
