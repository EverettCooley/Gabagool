banana  Cupcakes
 
 2 Cups Bake it All Mix 
 1  egg 
 1/3 Cup  granulated sugar 
 1/3 Cup  milk 
 1  banana —Peel/Slice 
 2 Tablespoons  butter  or  margarine —Melted 
 2 Tablespoons  brown sugar —Packed 
 
Break  egg  Into Bowl, Beat And Stir in  sugar  And  milk . Add MixGradually. Stir to Combine Thoroughly. Rub Muffin Cups Well WithShortening or Use Paper Liners in Muffin Pan. Drop About Half The BatterBy Spoonfuls Into Muffin Cups. Place 2 or 3 Slices of  banana  on Top Of The Batter And Pour Melted  butter  Over The Fruit. Sprinkle Brown SugarOver The  banana  Slices in Each Cup. Drop Remaining Batter by SpoonfulsInto Muffin Cups. Bake in a 400 F. Oven About 15 Minutes.
