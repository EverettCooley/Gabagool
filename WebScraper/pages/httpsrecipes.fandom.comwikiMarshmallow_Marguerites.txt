Ingredients : 
 any variety of thin, unsweetened  cracker 
 butter 
 marshmallow 
 Directions : 
 First take any variety of thin, unsweetened cracker, spread lightly with  butter  and set in a baking pan. 
 On the center of each cracker dispose a  marshmallow , with a bit of  butter  above, - the  butter  may be omitted. 
 Put the pan into a hot oven until the  marshmallow  is softened and browned a little. 
 Then serve at once.
