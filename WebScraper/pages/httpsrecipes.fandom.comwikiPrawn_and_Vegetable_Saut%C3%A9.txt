Description : 
 Invoking a red-hot, bold cominbation of real seafood and vegetables with the rich coconut milk, this glorious, guilt-free dish is great with other vegetables.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb. medium-sized, veined  prawns 
 1 cup cubed  pineapple 
 3-4 cups julienne  carrots ,  jalapenos ,  napa cabbage  etc. 
 2 cups rich  coconut milk 
 Directions : 
 Turn up the heat to medium/low and add the vegetables and  pineapple  to the pan. 
 A minute later, add the  shrimp  and the  coconut milk . 
 Adjust to desired thickness with  cornstarch  and either ladle over  rice  or serve in separate bowls.
