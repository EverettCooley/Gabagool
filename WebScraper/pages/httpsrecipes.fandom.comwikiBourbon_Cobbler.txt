Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 2½ oz  Bourbon 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 2 tsp  grapefruit juice 
 1½ tsp  almond extract 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in mixing glass, pour over  ice  into old-fashioned glass. 
 Decorate with  peach  slice.
