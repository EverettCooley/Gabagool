Description : 
 This  chocolate  Cake Has a  cherry  Pie Filling Between The 2 Round Cake Layers And a  chocolate  Sauce on Top And on Bottom Layer, Garnished With  white chocolate  and  chocolate curls .
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Makes 12 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 18¼ oz Pkg  chocolate  cake mix 
 1 cup semi-sweet  chocolate chips 
 14 oz can  sweetened condensed milk 
 1 Tbsp  milk 
 21 oz can  cherry  pie filling 
 ½ tsp  almond Extract 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 350 °F. 
 Prepare and bake cake mix as package directs for two 9 inch round layers. 
 Remove from pans, cool. 
 In saucepan, melt  chocolate chips  with  condensed milk . Cook and stir until slightly thickened, about 5 minutes. 
 Stir in  milk . Cool 10 minutes. 
 Combine  cherry  pie filling with  almond extract . 
 Place 1 cake layer on serving plate, top side up 
 Remove crust from top of cake to within ½ inch of edge, top with half of the  chocolate  mixture, then the  cherries . 
 Top with second cake layer and remaining  chocolate  mixture. 
 Garnish with  white chocolate  and  chocolate curls .
