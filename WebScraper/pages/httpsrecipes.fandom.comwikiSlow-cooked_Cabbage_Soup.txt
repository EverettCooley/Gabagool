Directions :

 Ingredient : 
 1 lb extra lean  ground beef 
 1 medium  onion , chopped 
 2 cups  tomato juice 
 1 cup  water 
 1 pkg  coleslaw blend  (16 oz) 
 1 can chunky salsa  tomato sauce  (15 oz) 
 1 can  corn , drained (11 oz) 
 1 tsp dried  Italian seasoning 
 3 tbsp light  sour cream , if desired 
 Directions : 
 In large nonstick skillet, cook ground beef and onion over medium high heat for 5 to 7 minutes or until beef is thoroughly cooked, stirring frequently. 
 Drain. 
 Place beef mixture in 3Â½ or 4 quart slow cooker. 
 Add all remaining ingredients except sour cream; stir to combine. 
 Cover; cook on low setting for 6 to 8 hours. 
 Serve with sour cream.
