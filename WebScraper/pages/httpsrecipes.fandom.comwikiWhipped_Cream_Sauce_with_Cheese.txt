Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 1½ cups 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup grated  swiss cheese 
 2 tbsp  prepared mustard 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 ½ cup  heavy cream , whipped 
 Directions : 
 Fold the cheese, mustard and lemon juice into the whipped cream. 
 Spread on cooked asparagus, broccoli, spinach or cauliflower and put under the broiler for about 1 minute until the cheese is melted.
