Description 
 Sauce Recipes  by the South Carolina Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Yield: 1 cup. 
 Ingredients 
 ½ stick  margarine 
 1 - 2 tsp  vinegar 
 1 Tbsp  mayonnaise 
 hot pepper sauce , to taste 
 4 - 5 Tbsp prepared  mustard 
 dash  salt 
 1 Tbsp [ketchup]] 
 dash  pepper 
 2 - 4 Tbsp  dark brown sugar 
 granulated  garlic , to taste 
 Directions 
 Melt butter in pan. Add mayonnaise and stir until liquefied. 
 Add mustard, ketchup and sugar, stirring until dissolved. 
 Add vinegar, hot pepper sauce, salt, pepper and garlic. Heat thoroughly.
