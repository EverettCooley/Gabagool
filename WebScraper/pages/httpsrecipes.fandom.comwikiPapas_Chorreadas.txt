      Papas Chorreadas     
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb of small or medium  red potatoes 
 2 medium  tomatoes 
 1 cup of  milk 
 a bunch of  cilantro 
 a bunch  scallions 
 white cheese 
 table salt 
 Directions : 
 Peel the  papas  partially leaving some skin on. 
 Boil the  papas  until tender. 
 Chop  onions ,  cilantro , and  tomato  into fine small pieces. 
 Shred about Â½ cup of  white cheese . 
 Stir fry the  onions   cilantro  and  tomato  in a frying pan. 
 Add the  milk ,  cheese , and  salt  to taste. 
 Turn heat to low, and let it simmer until it thickens.
