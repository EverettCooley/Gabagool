      Matzo meal     
 Matzo meal  is ground  matzo , generally available in two textures fine and medium. Matzo meal is used in a variety of foods including  gefilte fish ,  matzo balls  and pancakes. It's also used to thicken soups and for breading foods to be fried.
 For baking, the finer grind of matzo meal is used, this is often called " matzo cake meal ".
