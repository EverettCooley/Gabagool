 Description : 
       orange juice     
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cup fresh  orange juice 
 1 cup  water 
 1 cup  cranberry juice  (presweetened) 
 1/2 cup  pineapple juice 
 1 tbl  lemon juice 
 2 dsh red  food coloring  optional 
 mint  leaves 
 
 
 Directions : 
 In 2-qt. casserole combine the liquids; cover. 
 Microwave at High 10 to 12 minutes, or until boiling. 
 Reduce power to 50% (Medium). 
 Simmer 5 minutes. 
 Serve hot, garnished with  mint .
