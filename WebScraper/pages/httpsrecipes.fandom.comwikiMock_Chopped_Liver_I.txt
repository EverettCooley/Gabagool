Ingredients : 
 1½ tbsp  safflower oil 
 1½ cups  onions , chopped 
 1 cup toasted  cashews 
 1 cup  string beans , steamed, cut into 1" lengths 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 salt  and  pepper , to taste 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in a small skillet. 
 Saute  onions  slowly over moderately low heat. 
 Stir frequently until nicely browned. 
 Combine  onions  with rest of ingredients in a food processor and process until smooth. 
 Store in a jar until needed. 
 Bring to room temperature before serving.
