 Description : 
 A unique and delicious main dish cutlet that always gets a rave!
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 Tbsp  butter 
 4 Tbsp  flour 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 1/8 tsp  black pepper 
 1 ½ Cups  milk 
 2 Cups diced California avacados 
 2 Cups cooked crab meat 
 3  eggs , beaten 
 Fine dried bread crumbs 
 oil  for frying 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter  and blend in  flour . 
 Add  salt ,  pepper  and  milk ; cook until thickened, stirring constantly. 
 Add avacado and crab meat; mix well. Cool mixture. 
 Shape into 8 cutlets and roll in crumbs, then in  egg  and again in crumbs. 
 In a large skillet, heat  oil  and brown cutlets on both sides. Serve immediately.
