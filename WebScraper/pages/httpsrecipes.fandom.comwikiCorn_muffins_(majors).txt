 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup  corn  meal 
 1 cup  flour 
 1 tbsp  baking powder 
 1 tsp  salt 
 3/4 cup  milk 
 1 tbsp  butter  or  margarine , melted 
 Directions : 
 Mix dry ingredients. Add lightly beaten  egg ,  milk  and  butter . Bake in muffins tins lined with cupcake papers for 20 minutes at 400f *be careful not to overbeat these (or any muffins) aor they will have air tunnels and pointed tops.
