 Ingredients : 
 ¼  melon  ~ cubed 
 2  apples  ~ cubed 
 2  bananas  ~ sliced 
 5  oranges  ~ peeled, seeded and chopped 
 ¾ cup  orange juice 
 ¼ cup  lemon juice 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 ½ tsp  cinnamon 
 whipped cream 
 Directions : 
 Mix all the ingredients. 
 Chill before serving and serve with whipped  cream .
