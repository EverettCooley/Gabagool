Ingredients : 
 1¼ cups  rice flour 
 2 teaspoons  baking powder 
 1 teaspoon  soda 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1¼ cup  buttermilk  or  sour cream 
 two  eggs 
 ¼ cup  olive oil 
 Directions : 
 Add the  vegetable oil  to  buttermilk . 
 In large bowl, mix together all dry ingredients. 
 Add the  buttermilk / oil  mixture and  eggs . 
 Mix and cook on hot griddle.
