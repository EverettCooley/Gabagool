Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz  cachaça 
 2 oz  tropical fruit  purée 
 1 oz  sweetened condensed milk 
 1 oz  simple syrup 
 Directions : 
 Shake all ingredients with  ice  and strain into an old-fashioned glass. 
 Garnish with a  lime  wheel, and serve.
