Description : 
 Sweet-tart and crunchy, this unusual slaw is enlivened by a combination of  cabbage , sugared  cranberries  and thin orange slices.
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Submitted by Winna to ediets.com 
 Prep Time: 30 Minutes | Cook Time: 0 Minutes | Total Time: 60 Minutes 
 Serves 10 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup halved  cranberries 
 ¼ cup  white sugar   [1] 
 2  oranges , peeled and thinly sliced 
 1  red onion , thinly sliced 
 4 cups shredded  cabbage 
 1 large  apple  - peeled, cored and chopped 
 ¼ cup  mayonnaise   [2] 
 Directions : 
 In a small bowl, toss together the  cranberries  and  sugar . 
 Cover and refrigerate. 
 In a large bowl, toss together the  oranges ,  onion ,  cabbage ,  apple  and  mayonnaise  until evenly coated. 
 When ready to serve add  cranberry  mixture and toss again. 
 Notes : 
 
 ↑   Leave out or use your favorite sweetener such as Splenda. 
 
 ↑   Use low fat or non fat  mayo . 
 
 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 110.11 Calories | 4.6g Total Fat | 16.19g Total Carbs | .99g Protein
