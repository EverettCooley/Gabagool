      Potato Daal     
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  moong dal 
 3 (medium)  potatoes  (cut into medium pieces) 
 1 cup  onion  (cut into small pieces) 
 2 – 3  flakes of garlic 
 2 – 3  green chillies 
 ½ tsp  chilli powder 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Cook the  moong dal  with 2 cups of  water ,  potatoes , a little  salt , and  garlic . 
 In a pan heat  oil , add the  onions  and fry for a few minutes. 
 Now add the green chillies,  tomatoes  and  chilli powder . 
 After a few minutes, add the mashed  moong dal  and a little  water  if required. 
 Garnish with fresh  coriander leaves . 
 Goes well with rotis.
