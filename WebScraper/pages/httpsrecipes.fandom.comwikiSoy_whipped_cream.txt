3/4 C.  soy milk 
 2 Tbs.  Honey 
 1 Tbs  lemon juice  (or  Vinegar ) 
 1/4-1/2 Tsp  vanilla 
 Pinch of  salt  (optional) 
 2 cups  oil 
 
 
 Blend first four Ingredients on high for 5-10 seconds. 
 Slowly dribble in  oil 
 Blending for an additional full minute after all the  oil  has been added. 
 Put into a covered container and chill..
