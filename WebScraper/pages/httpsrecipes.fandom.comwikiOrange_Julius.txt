Description : 
 Source: August 1979 issue of Weight Watchers Magazine
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  orange juice 
 ⅓ cup  non-fat dry milk 
 3 tbsp  sugar 
 ¼ tbsp  vanilla 
 4 to 6  ice cubes 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in blender. 
 Process until thick and frothy.
