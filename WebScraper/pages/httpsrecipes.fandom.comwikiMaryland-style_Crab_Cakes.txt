Description : 
 Purchased from the Wantier Estate in Balch Springs, Texas in 1988.
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  crab  meat 
 ¼ cup  mayonnaise 
 2 tablespoons minced  parsley 
 1 teaspoon  Old Bay seasoning 
 ½ cup soft  bread crumbs 
 2 large  eggs , beaten 
 8 tablespoons  butter 
 Directions : 
 Gently blend all ingredients except  butter . 
 Shape into 8 crab cakes and refrigerate for 30 minutes. 
 Melt  butter  in a large skillet and saute crab cakes until golden brown on all sides for 5 – 7 minutes.
