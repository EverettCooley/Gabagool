 Ingredients : 
 4  pork chops 
 1 teaspoon  vegetable oil 
 1 cup  orange juice 
 ½ cup  salsa 
 2 tablespoons  brown sugar 
 ¼ teaspoon  cumin 
 ¼ teaspoon  ginger 
 salt  to taste 
 fresh  cilantro 
 orange  slices 
 Directions : 
 Brown pork chops in hot oil. 
 Add orange juice, salsa, brown sugar, cumin, ginger and salt. 
 Cover and simmer until chops are cooked through. 
 Reduce the sauce by cooking uncovered. 
 Serve with rice and top with cilantro; garnish with orange slices.
