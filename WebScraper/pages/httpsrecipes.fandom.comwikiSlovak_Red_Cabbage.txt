Ingredients : 
 4 slices  bacon , finely chopped 
 4  green onions , finely chopped 
 2  celery  ribs, finely chopped 
 1  red cabbage , cored, finely chopped 
 2  oranges  - juice only 
 ¼ cup  apple cider vinegar 
 3 tbsp  brown sugar 
 ½ tsp  salt  (or to taste) 
 black pepper , freshly ground 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Cook  bacon  in large skillet over medium heat until crisp and fat is rendered. 
 Add  onions , stirring frequently, until very soft, about 5 minutes. 
 Add  cabbage  in four batches, mixing thoroughly after each. 
 Stir in  orange juice . 
 Reduce heat to low; cook, stirring often, until  cabbage  is wilted, about 15 minutes. 
 Stir in  vinegar  and  sugar . 
 Cook, stirring often, until very tender, about 15 minutes. 
 Adjust seasoning.
