 Ingredients : 
 1 1/4Â lbs Hamburger 
 10 oz Can  cream  of  Mushroom  soup 
 1  egg 
 Chopped  Onion , to taste 
 1 tbsp  worcestershire sauce 
 3/4 cup Grated yellow  Cheese 
 salt  and  pepper , to taste 
 1 Loave, white or brown bread 
 Directions : 
 1 Mix together all ingredients, except bread. 
2 Cut the crusts off very fresh bread slices. 
3  butter  1 side only; with buttered side down, line muffin tins with bread slices, making sure you don"t break the bread at the bottom. 
4 Fill each muffin cup with a good tablespoon of hamburger filling. 
5 Bake at 350f for 30 mins. 
6 They can be frozen, then reheated, or can be served cold. 
7 I use a lean ground  Beef
