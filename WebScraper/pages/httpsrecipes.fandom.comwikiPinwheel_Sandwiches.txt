Description : 
 These are great for showers and other parties!
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 x 8oz pkgs of  cream cheese  (softened) 
 1 pkg of Hidden Valley  ranch salad dressing  mix (original ranch) 
 1 large pkg of flour  tortillas 
 ham ,  turkey ,  cheese  (all sliced thin at the deli) 
 1 x 4 oz jar  pimento  (diced) 
 1 x 4 oz can  green chiles  (chopped) 
 Â½ cup sliced  black olives 
 Directions : 
 Mix  cream cheese  and dressing mix. 
 Spread on tortillas. 
 Next place some pimentos, chiles, and over the  cream cheese  layer. 
 Top with  ham ,  turkey , and  cheese . 
 Roll up tightly. 
 Cut each roll into about a 3-4 inch segment, depending on how big your tortillas are.
