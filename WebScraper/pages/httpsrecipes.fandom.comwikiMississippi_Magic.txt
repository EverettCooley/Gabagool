Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  Bourbon whiskey 
 1 oz  Southern Comfort ®  peach  liqueur 
 ½ oz  dry vermouth 
 1 oz  mandarin juice 
 1 oz  pineapple juice 
 ¼ oz  lime juice 
 1 oz  peach  purée 
 Directions : 
 Blend briefly in a wine goblet filled with crushed  ice , and serve with straws.
