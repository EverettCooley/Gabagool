Description : 
 Stuffed grape leaves
 
 Ingredients : 
       Sarma vo Lozov List     
 1 lb.  beef  and  pork , ground together 
 2  onions 
 1  egg 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  pepper 
 ¼ cup  rice 
 washed  grape leaves 
 a spring of  dill 
 2 tbsp hot  oil 
 2 tbsp  flour 
 1 tsp  paprika 
 ½ cup hot  water 
 Directions : 
 Fry  onions  in hot  corn oil . 
 Add  meat  and fry until  meat  is well done. 
 Cool mixture slightly and then add  egg ,  rice , seasoning and a spring of  dill  chopped fine. 
 Mix together thoroughly. 
 Place 1 tbsp of mixture in a  grape leaf  and wrap up in sarma fashion. 
 Place sarma in deep pan, put 2 tbsp hot  oil , put 2 tbsp  flour  and fry together. 
 Remove from fire, add 1 tsp  paprika , ½ cup hot  water . 
 Blend and pour over sarmas. 
 Many prefer to serve it with  sour cream .
