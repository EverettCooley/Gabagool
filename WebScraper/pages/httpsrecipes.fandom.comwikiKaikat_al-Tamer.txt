 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 cup white  flour . 
 2 cups  dates  without seeds. 
 1 cup of hot  milk . 
 2  eggs . 
 3/4 cup  Sugar . 
 1 pack melted  butter  (113g) 
 1 teaspoon  sodium bicarbonate . 
 1 teaspoon  cinnamon  powder. 
 1 cup  walnuts . 
 dates  Sauce* 
 1 cup  dates . 
 2 cup hot  water . 
 Directions : 
 Pour  milk  over  dates  and mix it by an electronic mixer. - Blend  eggs  with  butter  and  Sugar  by a blender. - Mix well and add  walnuts . - Pour mixture in a greased cake pan and let it stand for about 15 minuets. - Bake in a moderate heat over for about 45 - 60 minuets. - After baking, let the cake stands for about few minutes till it cools down. - Prepar the sauce by processing  dates  with  water  until smooth batter, then strain. - Pour the sauce over the cake and garnish with  almonds .
