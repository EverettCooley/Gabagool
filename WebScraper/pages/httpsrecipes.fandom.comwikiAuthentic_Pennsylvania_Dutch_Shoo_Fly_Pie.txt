Authentic Pennsylvania Dutch Shoo Fly Pie
 
Serves 8
 
 1/2 c Baking  molasses 
 1/2 ts  baking soda 
 3/4 c  flour 
 2 tb  butter 
 1/8 ts  ground ginger 
 1/2 ts  cinnamon 
 1 ea Pastry For 9 Inch Pie 
 1 ea  egg  Yolk 
 3/4 c Boiling  water 
 1/2 c  brown sugar 
 1/8 ts  nutmeg 
 1/8 ts Ground Cloves 
 1/4 ts  salt 
 
Dissolve Soda in Boiling  water  And Add to  egg  And  molasses . Set Aside.Stir Dry Ingredients Together, Mixing Well. Cut in  shortening  UntilMixture Looks Like Coarse Crumbs. Pour  molasses  Into Pie Shell. SprinkleCrumbs Evenly Over Top. do Not Stir. Bake at 450 Degrees For 15 MinutesThen Reduce Heat to 350 Degrees And Bake 20 Minutes Longer. Cool AndServe.
