 About Amorini : 
 Wikipedia Article  About Amorini on Wikipedia 
 Amoretti or Amorini is an Italian term used to describe the small, winged babies who accompanied Cupid in the classicizing art of the Renaissance and late. These tiny pasta shapes are usually served in a broth or very light soup. Also called: Putti.
