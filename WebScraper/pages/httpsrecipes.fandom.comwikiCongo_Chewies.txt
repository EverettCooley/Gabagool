 Description : 
 Congo Chewies are similar to Congo bars 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2/3 cup  margarine , melted 
 2 cups  brown sugar 
 3  eggs 
 3 cups  flour 
 2-3 tsp  baking powder 
 1/2 tsp  salt 
 6 oz.  semi-sweet chocolate  chips 
 Directions : 
 Combine melted  margarine  and  brown sugar . Mix in the rest of ingredients, Pour into a greased 9X13 pan. Bake at 350 for 25-30 minutes. Cool, cut into 2" squares.
