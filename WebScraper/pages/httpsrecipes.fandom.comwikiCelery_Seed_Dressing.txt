Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 This recipe yields about 1¾ cups of vinaigrette. 
 Dressing is better if made day before. 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup  sugar 
 ⅓ cup  vinegar 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon dry  mustard 
 1 teaspoon grated  onion 
 1 cup  salad oil 
 1 tablespoon  celery seed 
 Directions : 
 Combine first five ingredients. 
 Mix well. 
 Add  oil  slowly. 
 Beat with electric mixer until thick. 
 Stir in  celery seed . 
 Refrigerate.
