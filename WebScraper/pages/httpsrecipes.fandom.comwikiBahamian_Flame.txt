 Wiki.com/Bahamian_Flame Bahamian Flame  from Recipes Wiki—original source of recipe, licensed under the GNU Free Documentation License
 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 1½ fluid ounces  coconut rum 
 ½ teaspoon  Bacardi 151 rum 
 Directions 
 Pour the coconut rum in your favourite shot glass. 
 Carefully, pour the 151 proof rum on top. 
 Ignite and serve immediately.
