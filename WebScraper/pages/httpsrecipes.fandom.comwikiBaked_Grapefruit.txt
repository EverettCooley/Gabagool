Baked  grapefruit 
 
Ruby Red Grapfruit
 
 2 T.  sugar  And 1/2 T.  cinnamon  Mixture Per  grapefruit  HalfFresh Fruit or  mint  Leaf For Garnish 
 
 Slice  grapefruit  in Half. Cut Between And Loosen  grapefruit  Segments. Sprinkle With  cinnamon - sugar  Mixture.
 
Bake at 200 Degrees Until  grapefruit  is Warm. Remove And Garnish Each Slice With Fresh Fruit or  mint  Leaf.
