 About Flowering kale : 
 Wikipedia Article  About Flowering Kale on Wikipedia 
 A variety called flowering kale is grown mainly for its ornamental leaves, which are brilliant white or violet on the interior. Most plants sold as "ornamental cabbage" are in fact  kales .
 Ornamental kale is as edible as any other variety, provided it has not been treated with pesticides or other harmful chemicals.
 When uncooked, standard Kale is a popular garnish.
