cp  Cheese  Casserole
 Serves 6
 
 8 Slices (crustless)  bread  Cut Into Squares or Cubes 
 2 C. Grated  Cheese  (old  Cheddar  Was Nice, But a Mixture Would Work - YourFavorite/S) 
 1/4 t  salt 
 1 c Chopped  Ham 
 1 t  parsley  (i Used 1 Tsp. Dried) 
 4  eggs 
 1 c  evaporated milk 
 1 c  light cream  or  milk 
 paprika  to Dust Top
 
Lightly Grease Crock Pot.
 Alternate Layers of  bread ,  Cheese  And  Ham .
 Beat Balance. Pour Over.
 Sprinkle With  paprika .
 Cover. Cook @ Low 3 - 4 Hr.
