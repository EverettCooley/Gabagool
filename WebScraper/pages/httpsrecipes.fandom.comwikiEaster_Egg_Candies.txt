Description : 
 Contributed by Eddie Rickenbacker at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  confectioners' sugar 
 2 tbsp  water 
 1 tbsp  light corn syrup 
 1 tbsp softened  butter 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  almond extract 
 1 pinch  cream of tartar 
 1⅓ cups flaked  coconut 
 Chocolate coating : 
 8 squares  semi-sweet chocolate 
 Directions : 
 Combine sugar, water, corn syrup, butter and vanilla. 
 Add the salt, almond extract and cream of tartar. 
 This mixture will be crumbly. 
 Add the coconut. 
 Shape into eggs (whatever size you want). 
 Melt the chocolate, in a double boiler, over low heat. 
 Cool a little and then frost the eggs.
