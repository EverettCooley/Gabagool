Ingredients : 
 500 g  potatoes 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 1 tablespoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Wash and brush the  potatoes  thoroughly. 
 butter  a pan and place the  potatoes  in the oven dish. 
 Sprinkle the  potatoes  with  salt , bake in the oven. 
 During baking you may brush the  potatoes  with melted  butter . 
 Serve hot with  butter , with salted  herring ,  chive  or spring  onion  dressing or with  buttermilk .
