 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3  California avocados  (1-1/2 pounds), diced 
 1 Tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 Tbsp Dijon  mustard 
 ½ Cup prepared vinaigrette 
 1 ¾ Cups smoked  Turkey  or  Chicken , diced 
 ¾ Cup  dried cherries  or  cranberries 
 12 Cups mixed lettuces 
 6 baked pizza (bread) shells (about 6 inches in diameter) 
 Directions : 
 Gently toss  avocado  with  lemon juice ; reserve. 
 Whisk  mustard  into vinaigrette.
 Toss  Turkey , dried fruit, and mixed lettuces with vinaigrette.
 Gently fold in reserved  avocado .
 Divide among 6 warmed bread shells.
