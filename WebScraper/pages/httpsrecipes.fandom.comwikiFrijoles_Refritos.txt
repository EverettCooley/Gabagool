This is a very basic, traditional recipe for  refried beans , to which other spices can be added to taste.
 
 Ingredients : 
 16 oz. dried  pinto beans 
 1/2 medium-sized  Onion 
 1 large  celery  stalk 
 1 T  salt 
 Directions : 
 Rinse beans well. Put in a pot and cover with  water . Quarter  Onion , chop  celery , and add both to pot, along with  salt . Simmer beans until soft, about two hours. Heat half an inch of  oil  in a large skillet over medium heat. Add beans to skillet, leaving  water  in the pot. Lower heat and mash beans. Add some  water  from pot. Simmer beans, stirring occasionally, until beans have reached desired consistency.
