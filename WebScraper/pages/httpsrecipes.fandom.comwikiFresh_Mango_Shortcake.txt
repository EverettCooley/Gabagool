Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Hall Estate in Terrell, Texas in 1994.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  orange juice 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 1 teaspoon grated  lemon peel 
 1 ripe  mango 
 4 slices  pound cake 
 1 pint vanilla  ice cream 
 ¼ cup chopped  macadamia nuts 
 Directions : 
 In small saucepan combine orange juice, sugar and lemon peel then bring to a boil over medium heat then reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes. 
 Peel and cube mangoes then stir diced mango into syrup and set aside. 
 Cut cake slices in halves diagonally. 
 On each plate place one cake triangle and top with one scoop of ice cream. 
 Place second cake triangle on each plate leaning against the ice cream. 
 Spoon about ¼ cup of the mango sauce over ice cream and sprinkle with nuts.
