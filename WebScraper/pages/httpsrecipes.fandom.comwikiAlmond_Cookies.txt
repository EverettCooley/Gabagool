Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup  Butter 
 1  Egg 
 1/3 cup  Almonds , blanched and finely chopped 
 1/2 cup  Sugar 
 1/2 tablespoon  Cinnamon 
 1/2 tablespoon each 
 clove ,  Nutmeg 
 
 Grated rind 1/2  Lemon 
 2 tablespoons  Brandy 
 2 cups  Flour 
 Directions : 
 To prepare this  Almond  Cookies Recipe, first  cream  the  butter , add  egg  well beaten,  Almonds ,  Sugar ,  Brandy , and spices mixed and sifted with  flour . Roll mixture to one-fourth inch in thickness, shape with a round cutter first dipped in  flour , and bake slowly in an  Oven
