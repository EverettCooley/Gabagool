Description : 
 This is a recipe I obtained from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Hager estate in Canton, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  catsrecipes y-group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cans sliced  asparagus 
 1 can  cream of celery  soup 
 2 hard cooked  eggs  thinly sliced 
 1 cup grated  cheddar  cheese 
 Â½ cup coarsely crushed  saltines 
 1 teaspoon  butter 
 Directions : 
 Place drained  asparagus  in lightly buttered crock pot. 
 Combine soup and  cheese . 
 Top  asparagus  with sliced  eggs , soup mixture then cracker crumbs. 
 Dot with  butter . 
 Cover and cook on low for 6 hours.
