 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 8 average breams 
 Groundnut   oil 
 5 red  hot peppers 
 150 g of  onions 
 3  garlic  cloves 
 150 g of fresh  tomatoes 
 30G  celery 
 50 g  parsley 
 20 g  ginger 
 30 g djanssan 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Wash, empty and trim the breams. 
 Crush all the ingredients ( peppers ,  onions ,  garlic ,  tomatoes ,  celery ) 
 Heat some  Groundnut   oil  in a casserole and fry the crushed ingredients for 20 minutes. 
 Season the breams and coat them with  Groundnut   oil . 
 braise on a barbecue; basting every side with the fried, crushed ingredients. 
 Serve hot and decorate with  parsley  leaves.
