 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cubes  Beef  or  Chicken  bouillon 
 Chicken  pieces 
 2 cups  water 
 Directions : 
 Disolve the bouillon in the  water . 
 Spoon some bouillon into an  ice  cube tray and put some  Chicken  pieces in it. 
 Repeat until bouillon is used up.Freeze for about 12 hours.
