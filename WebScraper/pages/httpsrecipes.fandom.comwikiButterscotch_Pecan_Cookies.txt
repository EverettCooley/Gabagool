Butterscotch  pecan  Cookies
 
 1 Pkg.  butter  Recipe Cake Mix 
 3/4 Oz. Pkg. Instant Butterscotch Pudding Mix 
 1/4 C.  flour 
 3/4 C.  vegetable oil 
 1  egg 
 1 C. Chopped  pecans 
 
 in a Mixing Bowl, Combine Cake Mix, Pudding Mix,  flour ,  oil  And  egg . Mix Well. Stir in  pecans  (the Dough Will be Crumbly). Roll Tablespoonfuls Into Balls; Place 2-Inches Apart on Greased Baking Sheets. Bake at 350 Degrees For 10 to 12 Minutes or Until Golden Brown.
 
Cool For 2 Minutes; Remove From Pans to Wire Racks.
 
Makes 4 Dozen
