Ingredients : 
 2 c.  flour 
 2 tbsp.  sugar 
 2 tsp.  baking powder 
 1/2 tsp.  baking soda 
 1/4 tsp.  salt  (may omit) 
 1  egg  (or 1/4 c. egg beaters) 
 1 c.  sour milk  (do not use buttermilk) 
 2 tbsp.  oil 
 Directions : 
 Sour skim milk by using 1 1/3 to 1 1/2 teaspoons vinegar or lemon juice in cup and filling with milk. Let stand a few minutes before using. 
 Blend moist ingredients with dry. 
 Stir just until mixed. 
 Bake at 400 degrees for 15 to 20 minutes.
