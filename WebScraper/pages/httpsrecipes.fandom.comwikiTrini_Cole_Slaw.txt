 Description : 
 Serves: 	accordingly 
 Prep Time: 	7-10 minutes 
 Occasion: 	Any 
 Effort: 	Easy 
 Ingredients : 
 1 medium  carrot  (scraped,finely grated) 
 ¼ small  onion  (paste) 
 ½ medium  cabbage  (finely cut) 
 a little  water  (to form a thick paste) 
 2 tbsp  mayonnaise 
 pinch  sugar 
 Directions : 
 In a medium sized bowl place grated  carrots ,  cabbage  and  onion  paste. 
 Add mayonnaise,  sugar  and  water . 
 Mix well and refrigerate for 10 minutes or until desired.
