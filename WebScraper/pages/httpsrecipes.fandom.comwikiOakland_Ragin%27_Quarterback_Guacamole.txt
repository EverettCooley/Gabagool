 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 ripe genuine California avocados seeded, peeled and coarsely mashed 
 1 Tbsp fresh lemon juice 
 2 garlic cloves finely chopped 
 ½ tsp salt 
 ½ Cup black beans, rinsed and drained 
 3 Tbsp chopped red onion 
 3 Tbsp chopped tomato 
 1 or 2 pickled jalapenos, chopped 
 Directions : 
 In medium bowl, combine avocados, lemon juice, garlic and salt. 
 Stir in beans, onion, tomato and jalapeno. 
 Serve with tortilla strips.
