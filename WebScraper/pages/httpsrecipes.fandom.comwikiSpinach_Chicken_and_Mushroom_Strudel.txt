 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 spinach  - 500 gms 
 Mushrooms  100 g,  oil  50 ml 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 nutmeg  to taste 
 Onion  50 g,  garlic  - 25 g 
 celery  - 25 g 
 Strudel leaves - 1 pkt 
 egg  - 1 no 
 Chicken  - 1 no. 
 Directions : 
 Debone the  Chicken  into small pieces 
 Clean and wash  spinach  thoroughly, blanc and chop. Chop the  Mushrooms . 
 Chop  Onion ,  garlic  and  celery . 
 Heat  oil  in a pan and saute  Onion ,  garlic  and  celery , add the  Chicken  and saute. 
 Add the chopped  Mushroom  and  spinach , saute till it is semidry. Season with  salt ,  pepper  and grated  nutmeg . 
 Open up the strudel leaves onto a work table, spread the mixture on one side of the strudel leaves and roll. 
 Keep the roll on a greased baking tray, brush with beaten  eggs . 
 Bake in a moderate over for 15 mts. 
 allow to stand for sometime. Serve hot cut into slices.
