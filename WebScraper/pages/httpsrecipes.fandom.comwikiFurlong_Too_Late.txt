Ingredients : 
 2 ounces  light rum 
 4 ounces  ginger beer 
 1  lemon  twist 
 Directions : 
 Pour the  rum  and  ginger beer  into a highball glass almost filled with ice cubes. 
 Stir well. 
 Garnish with the  lemon  twist.
