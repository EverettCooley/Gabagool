Description : 
 This sweet alcoholic beverage adds 3 additional ingredients - apple juice, lemon zest, and apple slices - to traditional  Lambanog .

 Directions :

 Strain into a chilled cocktail glass. 
 Top with additional grated lemon zest and/or apple slices, if desired.
