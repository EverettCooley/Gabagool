Chocolate Pizza Pie
 3/4 c. butter or margarine
1/2 c. confectionary sugar
1 1/2 c. flour
 FILLING:
10 oz. chocolate chips
1/4 c. whipping cream
8 oz. cream cheese
 GLAZE:
1/4 c. sugar
1 T. cornstarch
1/2 c. pineapple juice
1/2 tsp. lemon juice
 Mix and press in pizza pan. Bake at 300, for 15-20 minutes. 
 Melt chocolate chips and whipping cream in microwave. 
 Beat cream cheese into mixture, and spread on top of crust. 
 Place cut up fruit on top (strawberries, peaches, grapes, etc. anything.) 
 Mix and drizzle on top.
