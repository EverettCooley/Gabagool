Description : 
 Spicey  California Avocado  sour  cream cheese  dip for  corn chips ,  crackers , or toppings because this recipe uses  California avocados ...it's definitely nutritious!
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  California avocado 
 2 to 3 tablespoons grated  Monterey jack  jalapeño pepper cheese 
 1 tbsp  sour cream 
 Directions : 
 Cut up and mash the inside of a ripe soft  California avocado , and discard seed and peel. 
 Put this in a small bowl. 
 Add 2 to 3 tablespoons of graded  Monterey jack  jalapeño pepper cheese add 1 tablespoon of  sour cream . 
 Mix well with a fork. 
 Use this to dip  corn chips  or put on top of salad or tacos or add to anything else. 
 Takes about 5 minutes to make.
