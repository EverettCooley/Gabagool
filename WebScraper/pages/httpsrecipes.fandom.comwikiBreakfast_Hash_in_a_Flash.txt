Description : 
 Contributed by  PressureCookerRecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 6 small  potatoes , shredded 
 6  eggs , beaten 
 1 cup shredded  American cheese 
 1 cup  ham , diced 
 1 cup diced ham,  bacon  or  sausage 
 Directions : 
 Spray cooker with cooking spray. Preheat. 
 Rinse shredded potatoes, squeeze moisture out of potatoes, and place in warmed pressure cooker to brown while preparing other ingredients. 
 Beat eggs. 
 Shred cheese and chop meat. 
 Break up potatoes and add Â¼-cup water. 
 Stir in remaining ingredients. Toss to mix lightly. 
 Cover and bring to full pressure. 
 Remove from heat at once and quick-release pressure. 
 Serve at once. 
 Use a food processor for quicker preparation of ingredients.
