Ingredients : 
 2 cups  long-grain rice 
 2 bottles  coconut milk 
 5 cups  water 
 salt 
 parsley , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Rinse the  rice  in  water  and drain. 
 In a large sauce pan, bring the water, coconut milk and salt to a boil. 
 Add the  rice  into the boiling pan. 
 Cook in high-medium heat (pan partially covered) until  rice  is dry and becomes tender. 
 Sprinkle  parsley  and remove pan from heat.
