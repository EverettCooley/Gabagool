 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/4 cup soy sauce 
 1 tbsp dijon  mustard 
 1 tsp  brown sugar 
 1 tsp  rice vinegar 
 1 tsp  green onion 
 Directions : 
 Thoroughly blend the above ingredients to dissolve the  Sugar . Serve as a dipping sauce for STEAMED .
