Description : 
 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 6 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups quartered  radishes 
 2  yellow squashes  and/or  zucchini , cut lengthwise and sliced 
 1 tbsp. chopped  garlic 
 2 tbsp.  butter , melted 
 1 tbsp chopped  oregano 
 chili powder, optional 
 parsley, optional 
 Directions : 
 In a fresh saucepan, cook  radishes ,  squash  and  garlic  in  butter  8 minutes or until tender. 
 Add  oregano . Serve hot and plain or with other dishes. Top with the chili powder and garnish with parsley, if desired.
