 In the Native American cuisine soups are generally served as a starter to the main dish. Most appreciated are vegetable soups made with beans, squash, tomatoes or potatoes. In the end these soups are being garnished with sour cream in order to increase their flavors. Also very appreciated in the Native American cuisine nuts soup made in a delicious way.
