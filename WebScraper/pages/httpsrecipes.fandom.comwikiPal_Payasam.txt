Description : 
 Ethnicity - South Indian 
 Type of meal - Party, Lunch, Dinner 
 Ingredients : 
 3 litres:  milk 
 700 gm:  Sugar 
 180 gm: Dried red  rice 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Boil the  milk . 
 Add  Sugar  and mix well. 
 When it boils again, add the washed  rice  in it. Simmer in medium flame. 
 Cook till the  rice  is done. 
 Serve hot.
