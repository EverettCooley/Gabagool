Ingredients : 
 2 cups cored, diced  apple 
 1 cup diagonally sliced  celery  ( ½-inch pieces) 
 ½ cup diced  red onion 
 ½ cup  walnut  halves (2 oz) 
 1 tbsp minced fresh  dill 
 1 tbsp minced fresh  cilantro 
 1 tbsp minced fresh  mint 
 1 cup creamy  Lemon Tahini Dressing 
 2 cups peeled, halved, seeded and sliced  cucumber  ( ½-inch pieces) 
 ⅓ cup dry  arame  ( ½ oz) 
 Directions : 
 In shallow bowl, combine  arame  and 1½ cups warm  water . 
 Let soak 15 minutes. 
 Drain and rinse quickly under cold running  water . 
 Set aside. 
 In large bowl, combine  cucumber ,  apple ,  celery ,  onion ,  walnuts ,  dill ,  cilantro  and  mint . 
 Season with pinch of  salt . 
 Add dressing and toss to coat. 
 Add  arame  and toss again (if you are not serving raita right away, leave the  arame  out and add it when ready to serve, as it will turn the dressing a slightly dark color).
