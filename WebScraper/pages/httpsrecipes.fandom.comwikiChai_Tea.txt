CHAI TEA* 
 
 
 Start to **f**inish:* 15 minutes 
 
 
 Ingredients* 
 1/2 cup water
 1 black tea bag, such as orange pekoe, English breakfast, Lapsang Souchong,
or Darjeeling
 One 3-inch piece stick cinnamon
 2 cups lactose-free milk
 2 tablespoons raw sugar or honey
 1 teaspoon vanilla
 1/8 teaspoon ground ginger
 
 
 Directions* 
 1. In a small saucepan combine water, tea bag and cinnamon stick.
Bring to boiling. Remove from heat. Cover and let stand 5 minutes. Remove
tea bag and cinnamon stick; discard.
 
 2. Stir the milk, sugar, vanilla, ginger and cardamom into the tea.
Cook and stir over medium heat until mixture is heated through (do not
boil).
 
 3. To serve, pour hot mixture into warm mugs. Makes 2 servings.
