 Directions : 
 Place apple cider, sugar, cinnamon sticks, lemon slices, allspice and cloves in a large saucepan and heat over medium high heat for 20 minutes. 
 Add pineapple / orange juice and red wine and heat thoroughly. 
 Remove lemon slices, cinnamon sticks, cloves and allspice before serving. 
 Serve warm.
