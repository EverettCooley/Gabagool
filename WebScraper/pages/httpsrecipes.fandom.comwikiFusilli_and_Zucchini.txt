Description : 
 Contributed by  Enzomartinelli 
 
 Makes 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 400g of  pasta  ( fusilli  shape) 
 4  zucchini 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 1 small  onion , minced 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1 spoon fresh  mint , minced 
 ½ cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 ¼ cup  milk 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  white pepper 
 50 g of  pine nuts 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter  in large skillet over medium-low heat. 
 Add  onion , cook 2 minutes. 
 Add  garlic ; cook 1 minute longer. 
 Stir in  zucchini  and  mint ; cook 4 minutes longer. 
 Stir cooked  rice  into  zucchini  mixture. 
 Add  cheese , milk,  pepper  and  pepper  sauce.
