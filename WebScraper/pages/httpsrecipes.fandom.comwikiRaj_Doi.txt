Ingredients : 
 115 g / 1 cup seedless black and green  grapes 
 225 g canned  mandarin  segments 
 2  oranges  peeled and segmented 
 1 balls from  honeydew melon 
 ½ balls from  watermelon 
 1  mango  peeled and sliced 
 1 juice of  lemon 
 ½ tsp  sugar 
 ¼ tsp freshly ground  cumin  seeds 
 salt  and freshly ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Place all the fruit in a large serving bowl and add the  lemon juice . 
 Toss gently to prevent breaking up the fruit. 
 Mix the remaining ingredients and sprinkle over the fruit. 
 Gently toss once more, chill thoroughly and serve.
