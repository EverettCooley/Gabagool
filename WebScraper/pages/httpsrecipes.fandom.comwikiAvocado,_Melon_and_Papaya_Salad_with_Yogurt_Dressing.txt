Description : 
 Because this recipe uses  California avocados ...it's definitely nutritious!
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tbsp plain  yogurt 
 1 tbsp  mayonnaise 
 ¼ tsp  lemon juice 
 ¼ tsp  sugar 
 ½ oz diced  California avocado 
 ¾ oz  melon  balls (cut in half) 
 ½ oz  papaya  balls (cut in half) 
 Directions : 
 In a small bowl, blend the first 4 ingredients with a whisk. 
 Cover and refrigerate until ready to use. 
 Spoon over the fruit mixture before serving, either as a snack or dessert.
