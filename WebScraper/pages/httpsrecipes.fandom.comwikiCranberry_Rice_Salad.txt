 Description : 
 Makes 10 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1/2 cup sweetened dried  cranberries 
 2  celery  stalks, chopped 
 1/2 cup salted cashews 
 1/4 cup  balsamic vinegar 
 
 
 Directions : 
 In medium bowl, combine  rice ,  cranberries ,  celery , cashews and  vinegar ; toss well. Serve cold.
