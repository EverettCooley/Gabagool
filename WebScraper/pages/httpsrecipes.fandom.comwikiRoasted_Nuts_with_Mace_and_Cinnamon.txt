Description : 
 Nuts (pistachios or pecans) roasted with a mixture of mace, cinnamon, sugar and salt. 
 
 10 servings 2½ cups 
 10 minutes 5 mins prep 
 Ingredients : 
 2½ cups  pistachios , shelled or  pecans 
 1 teaspoon ground  mace 
 2 teaspoons  cinnamon 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon  brown sugar 
 Directions : 
 In a large skillet, heat the nuts over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until they turn golden borwn (5 minutes). 
 In a separate bowl, mix the remaining ingredients, add the mixture to the nuts, and mix well to coat. 
 Let dry on a baking sheet (can be dried by roasting in oven to speed up process).
