Description : 
 Makes 20 falafel balls, or 4 servings
 
 Source:  Vegetarian Cooking for Diabetics 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cup  garbanzo beans , cooked 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 ½ cup  onion , chopped 
 2 tbsp whole wheat pastry  flour 
 ¼ cup  wheat germ 
 ¼ cup  parsley 
 ¼ cup  sesame seed 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 ¼ tsp  garlic powder 
 2 tbsp  oil 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 350°F. 
 In a food processor or blender grind garbanzo beans,  lemon  and  onion . 
 Remove and add  flour ,  wheat germ ,  parsley ,  sesame seed ,  pepper  and  garlic powder . 
 Form into 20 falafel balls. 
 Heat  oil  in a large baking dish, then add falafel and bake 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
 Description : 
 1 serving of 5 falafel = 434 calories; 3 meat, 2 bread, 1 fat pro 20gm, fat 9gm, carbo 56gm
