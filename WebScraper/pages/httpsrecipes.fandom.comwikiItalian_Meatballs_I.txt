Description : 
 This recipe makes a heaping amount of meatballs, so use some now, refrigerate some for later, and freeze some for next week. These meatballs can be used for pasta or meatball soup. Enjoy!
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  ground beef 
 1 pound  ground pork 
 1 pound  ground veal 
 1½ cup grated Italian cheese ( Parmigiano  or  Romano ) 
 1½ cup Italian  breadcrumbs 
 3  eggs 
 3 tablespoons  parsley 
 bit of  olive oil 
 Method : 
 Mix the ground meat with cheese, bread crumbs, eggs, and parsley. 
 Mix with your hands for best results! 
 Roll meat mixture into 2-inch meatballs. 
 Cook in pan over high heat in a drizzle of olive oil. 
 Cut through center of biggest one to make sure they are complete.
