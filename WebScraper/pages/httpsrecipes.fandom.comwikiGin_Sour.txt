Description 
 Gin Sour  from Jerry Thomas' Bartending Guide or How to Mix Drinks, out of copyright
 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 1 large tea-spoonful of white  sugar  dissolved in a little  Seltzer  or Apollinaris  water 
 2 or 3 dashes of  lemon juice 
 1 wine-glass of  Hollands  or Old Tom  gin 
 Garnish 
 orange  or  pineapple  and  berries 
 Directions 
 Fill the glass full of shaved ice, shake up, and strain into a claret glass. 
 Dress the top with orange, or pineapple and berries.
