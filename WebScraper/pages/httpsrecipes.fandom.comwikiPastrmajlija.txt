Macedonian pizza
 
 Original recipe 
 Ingredients : 
 1 kg of  flour 
 600 g  chicken   meat 
 tsp of  pepper 
 1  yeast 
 Directions : 
 Soak the  yeast  in one cup of luke warm water, one spoon flour and little bit sugar and leave it for 15 minutes to rise. When the yeast is ready, add it into  flour  and prepare the dough adding a luke warm water as needed. 
 Divide the dough into 4 equal pieces. 
 Use rolling tin to make each of these pieces thin and flat. (preferable round) 
 Chop the  meat  in small pieces. 
 Put the  meat  on top of each piece of  dough  and spread it equally. 
 Put the pepper on the meat also. 
 Put the pastrmajlii pieces (prepared like pizza) in the oven. 
 Bake it on temperature of 250Â°C. 
 Serve it hot.
