Ingredients : 
 4 lbs  ground pork , coarsely ground 
 1 lb  ground beef 
 1 tbsp  salt 
 pepper 
 ½ tbsp  allspice 
 ¼ tsp  garlic powder 
 ½ tsp  msg  (optional) 
 ½ cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Mix and let stand for several hours before stuffing into hog casings (rinsed well in cold  water ). 
 Can be eaten fresh, cooked in  water  or pan-fried.
