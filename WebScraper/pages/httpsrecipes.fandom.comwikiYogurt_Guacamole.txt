Description : 
 Dill  and plain  yogurt  provide a delicate flavor-base for this chunky. Note: To store Guacamole, place a piece of plastic wrap directly on the surface of the guacamole and refrigerate
 
 Ingredients : 
 15 ripe medium  California avocados , seeded and peeled 
 ¼ cup fresh  lemon juice 
 3 tbsp fresh  dill , chopped 
 1½ tbsp  garlic salt 
 ½ cup medium-hot  salsa 
 3 cups  yogurt 
 Directions : 
 In a large mixing bowl, coarsely mash avocados, leaving some chunks. 
 Add remaining ingredients and mix to blend.
