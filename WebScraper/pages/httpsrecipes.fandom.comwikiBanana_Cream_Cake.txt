Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Hellmann Estate in Lewisville, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅔ cup  shortening 
 1½ cups  sugar 
 2  eggs 
 1 cup mashed  bananas 
 2 tablespoons  milk 
 2 cups  flour 
 2 teaspoons  baking powder 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 ½ cup chopped  pecans 
 powdered sugar 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients except powdered sugar well and pour into greased tube pan. 
 Bake at 375°F for 25 minutes then sprinkle with powdered sugar.
