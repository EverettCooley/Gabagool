 Ingredients : 
 2 shots Stolichnaya Red  vodka 
 1/4 shot Tio Pepe (Dry  sherry ) 
 2 dashes Angostura 
 4 shots  tomato juice 
 1/2 shot  lemon juice 
 6 drops  Tabasco sauce 
 1/2 shot  worcestershire sauce 
 1/2 teaspoon Horseradish sauce 
 1 pinch  celery salt 
 1 pinch  pepper 
 1/2 teaspoon  caster sugar 
 garnish: Ground  pepper  and  celery  stick 
 Glass: Highball
 
 Directions : 
 Shake and strain
