Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Leake Estate in Arlington, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 3 mashed overripe  bananas 
 1⅛ cup  sugar 
 ½ cup fresh  orange juice 
 2  eggs 
 ¼ cup softened  unsalted butter 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 2 cups  flour 
 2 teaspoons  baking powder 
 ½ teaspoon  baking soda 
 ⅛ teaspoon  salt 
 1 cup small  chocolate chips 
 1 cup chopped  walnuts 
 Directions : 
 Beat  bananas ,  sugar ,  orange juice ,  eggs ,  butter  and  vanilla  well. 
 Add dry ingredients one at a time mixing well. 
 Pour into 2 greased loaf pans and bake at 350°F for 50 minutes.
