Description : 
 Goulash 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  beef chuck 
 1 tsp  salt 
 2  onions , white or  yellow 
 2 tbsp  lard  or  shortening 
 2 tbsp imported sweet  paprika 
 2  bay leaves 
 1 qt.  water 
 4 peeled and diced  potatoes 
 ¼ tsp  black pepper 
 Egg dumpling batter : 
 1  egg 
 6 tbsp  flour 
 ⅛ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Cut  beef  into 1 inch squares, add ½ tsp  salt . 
 Chop  onions  and brown in  shortening , add  beef  and  paprika . 
 Let  beef  simmer in its own juice along with  salt  and  paprika  for 1 hour on low heat. 
 Add  water , diced  potatoes  and remaining  salt . 
 Cover and simmer until  potatoes  are done and meat is tender. 
 Egg dumpling batter : 
 Add  flour  to unbeaten  egg  and  salt . 
 Mix well. 
 Let stand for ½ hour for  flour  to mellow. 
 Drop by teaspoonful into goulash. 
 Cover and simmer 5 minutes after dumplings rise to surface. 
 Serve hot with dollops of  sour cream .
