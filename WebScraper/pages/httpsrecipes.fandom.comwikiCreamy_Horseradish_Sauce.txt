Creamy Horseradish Sauce
 
 2 T. Minced  Onion 
 2 T.  margarine 
 2 T.  flour 
 1 Cup  light cream 
 1 to 2 T. Prepared Horseradish 
 
Saut?  Onion  in  margarine  Until Soft. Stir in  flour . Add Cream Slowly.Cook Over Medium Heat. Stir Constantly Until Thickened And bubbly. Stir in Horseradish. Makes 1 CupSource:my Old Recipes
