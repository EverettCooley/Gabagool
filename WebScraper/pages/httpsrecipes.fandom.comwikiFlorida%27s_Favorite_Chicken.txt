Description 
 Juice, Grapefruit, Canned, Unsweetened  by the US Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource
 
 Serves : 2 
 Ingredients 
 2 skinless, boneless  chicken  breast halves 
 2 tablespoons  chicken broth 
 2 tablespoons chopped  onion 
 1 tablespoon  cornstarch 
 1 tablespoon  water 
 1 cup  grapefruit juice 
 2 tablespoons chopped  walnuts 
 Directions 
 In skillet, brown chicken breasts in broth until lightly browned, 5 minutes each side. 
 Chop into bite-sized pieces and set aside. 
 In same skillet, lightly cook onion until transparent. 
 Mix cornstarch and water and add to onion. 
 Slowly add grapefruit juice and stir constantly until mixture starts to thicken. 
 Mix in chicken and walnuts. 
 Simmer until heated through.
