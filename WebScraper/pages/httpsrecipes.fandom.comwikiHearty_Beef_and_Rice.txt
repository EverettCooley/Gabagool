Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound ground  Beef 
 1/2 cup chopped  Onion 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 10-3/4-ounce can condensed  cream  of  Mushroom  soup 
 1 8-ounce can stewed  tomatoes , undrained, chopped 
 1 7-ounce can whole kernel  corn 
 1/2 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 1/2 teaspoon  chili powder 
 1 cup (4 ounces) shredded  Cheddar  or  American cheese 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Beef  and  Onion  in large skillet over medium-high heat. 
 Stir in  rice , soup,  tomatoes ,  corn ,  garlic powder  and  chili powder . 
 Pour mixture into greased 2-quart baking dish; bake at 350 degrees 20 minutes. 
 Top with  Cheese ; bake an additional 5 to 10 minutes.
