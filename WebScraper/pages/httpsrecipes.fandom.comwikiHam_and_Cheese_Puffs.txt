Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: South Idaho Press, Tuesday, February 20, 2007 
 Typed by: Susan Godfrey 
 Yield: 8 appetizers or 4 main dish servings 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ x 17.3 oz pkg. of  puff pastry  sheets (1 sheet) 
 8 oz Bryan Classic Deli honey  ham  slices 
 4 oz sliced  Swiss cheese 
 1 x 8-oz tub chive and onion flavored  cream cheese 
 ½ x 15-oz can  asparagus  spears, drained 
 Directions : 
 Thaw pastry sheet 30 minutes. 
 Preheat oven to 375°F. 
 Spray large baking sheet with non-stick cooking spray. 
 On a lightly floured surface, roll pastry sheet to 12 inches by 16 inches. 
 Cut into 16 (2x6-inch) strips. 
 Place 3 slices of ham on small plate. 
 Place ½ oz Swiss cheese in center of ham slice and spread 1 t of cream cheese down the center with a knife. 
 Place one asparagus spear in the center of ham and then top with another pastry strip. 
 Seal ends so cheese will not leak out. 
 Repeat until pastry strips are all used. 
 Bake 15 to 20 minutes or until pastry strips are golden.
