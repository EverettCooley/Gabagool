Info 
 Cook Time : 
 Serves : 2
 
 Ingredients 
 2 ripe  pears 
 The juice of 1 medium sized  lime 
 3  kiwi  fruits 
 2-3  ice  cubes 
 Directions 
 Core and stem the pears, then coarsely chop and add to a blender. 
 Add the lime juice. 
 Peel the kiwi then coarsely chop and add to blender. 
 Add ice, cover blender and blend until desired consistency. 
 Pour into a glass and garnish with thin-sliced pear wedges.
