 Description :

 Ingredients : 
 Salad 
 2 bunches  arugula , washed, dried and stems trimmed 
 1 head  Bibb lettuce , rinsed and torn 
 Juice of 1/2  lemon 
 1 ripe  Pear , thinly sliced 
 8 oz. Maytag or other blue  Cheese , crumbled 
 
 
 
 Dressing 
 1 small  shallot , minced 
 2 Tbs.  white wine vinegar 
 1/4 cup  apricot  all-fruit spread 
 1/3 cup extra virgin  olive oil 
 salt  and freshly ground  black pepper , to taste 
 Directions : 
 1. To make Salad: Combine  arugula  and  lettuce  in a salad bowl. Squeeze some  lemon juice  over  Pear  slices to keep them from  browning . Arrange  Pear  slices on top of  lettuce  and  arugula . Top with blue  Cheese  crumbles.
 2. To make Dressing: Combine  shallot ,  vinegar  and  apricot  spread in a bowl. Stream in  oil  while whisking briskly. Add to salad, season with  salt  and  black pepper  and serve.
