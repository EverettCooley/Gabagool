From Menu and Recipes Week 3/04/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  sausage 
 2 tbsp  olive oil 
 3  garlic  cloves 
 4½ tbsp  oregano 
 1  onion 
 2½ tbsp  basil 
 3½ tbsp  parsley 
 2½ tbsp  garlic powder 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 2 tbsp  Cajun seasoning 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 1 x 28 oz can  tomato sauce  + ½ can  water 
 2 x 6 oz  tomato paste  + 2 cans  water 
 1 x 16 oz diced  tomatoes 
 2 cans  mushrooms 
 Directions : 
 Chop 3  garlic  cloves, add  garlic  to  olive oil  and cook until  garlic  bubbles. 
 Add the  onion , turn to medium low. 
 Simmer on low heat about 5 minutes. 
 While simmering, add other ingredients and mix well. 
 Stir every couple minutes. 
 Do this for about 10 minutes. 
 Cover and simmer an additional 10 minutes.
