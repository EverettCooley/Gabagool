 Prep Time: 3 mins 
 Cook time: n/a 
 Serves: 2 
 
 Description : 
 Do you need a fast snack for your kids? Here's is my save all recipe I use.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 c Cherrios 
 1/2 c Chex Mix 
 1/2 c Peanuts 
 1/2 c Popcorn 
 1/4 c Maple Syrup 
 2 TB Butter 
 Directions : 
 Take the first 4 ingredients and mix them in a microwave safe bowl 
 Melt the butter separately 
 Add the maple syrup and melded butter to the mix 
 Mix 
 Place into baggys and make your kids happy
