 Prep Time: 10 minutes 
 Cook time: 30 minutes 
 Serves: 4-6 
 
 Description : 
 Just like Grandma used to make.
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 medium  potatoes  diced 
 1 medium  onion  diced 
 1 x 14 oz can  evaporated milk 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Cover potatoes and onions with water in large pot 
 Boil uncovered until potatoes are fork tender 
 Add milk, salt and pepper to taste and simmer until slightly thickened 
 Serve hot, especially good with a slice of buttered cornbread
