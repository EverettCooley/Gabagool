Description : 
 
 A crockpot  shrimp  creole recipe with  shrimp  and  tomatoes . 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups diced  celery 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 ¾ cup chopped  bell pepper 
 1 can (8 ounces)  tomato sauce 
 1 large can (28 oz) whole  tomatoes 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  pepper 
 4 to 6 drops  Tabasco sauce , or a dash or two of ground  cayenne , to taste 
 1 to 1½ pounds medium  shrimp , de-veined and shelled 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in slow cooker except  shrimp . 
 Cook 3 to 4 hours on high or 6 to 8 hours on low. 
 Add  shrimp  last hour of cooking. 
 Serve over hot boiled  rice .
