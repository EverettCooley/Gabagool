Description : 
 To use in place of canned cream soups in casseroles or as a base for your
own soups. It is much lower in fat and salt than the canned versions. The trick
is to have it made up ready to use! Variations will serve two.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups Powdered nonfat  milk 
 3/4 cup  cornstarch 
 1/4 cup (or less) instant  chicken boullion  (or Vegetable if you prefer) 
 2 tablespoons Dried  onion flakes 
 1 teaspoon  basil  leaves 
 1 teaspoon  thyme  leaves 
 1/2 teaspoon  pepper 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients, mixing well. 
 Store in an airtight container until ready to use.
