Description : 
 Think traditional Southern cooking is always fattening? Here's a recipe for country fried steak with all of the flavor and very little    
fat. Good fixins!
 
 Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 25 minutes 
 Makes 3 to 5 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound lean  round steak , cut into 4 thin slices 
 1 tsp Tony's creole seasoning 
 38 soda  crackers  (1 sleeve), crushed 
 ¾ cup + 2 tablespoons  all-purpose flour  (divided in half) 
 1 (8-ounce) container  egg substitute 
 2 cups  fat-free milk  (divided in half) 
 2 tablespoons virgin olive  oil 
 ¼ cup chopped  Italian parsley  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Flatten steaks to ¼-inch thickness using a meat mallet or rolling pin 
 Sprinkle evenly with ¼ teaspoon Tony's 
 1/4 tsp red pepper 
 Combine crushed crackers, ¾ cup flour, and red pepper 
 Stir together egg substitute and ½ cup milk 
 Dip steaks into egg mixture; dredge in cracker mixture, pressing lightly into cracker mixture 
 Cook steaks in hot oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat 5 to 7 minutes on each side or until golden brown 
 Remove steaks from skillet; drain on paper towels and keep warm 
 Whisk together remaining 1½ cups milk, remaining 2 tablespoons flour, and remaining Tony's 
 Pour mixture into reserved drippings in skillet; cook over medium- high heat, whisking constantly, 10 to 12 minutes or until thickened 
 Serve gravy over steaks 
 Sprinkle with parsley, if desired 
 Cooking tip: if you like a thicker gravy add more flour or reduce the amount of milk 
 Serve with side of favorite vegetables and bread 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving (⅙ recipe):
 
 Calories 288 (35% from fat) | fat 11.1g (sat 2.1g) | protein 24.0g | carb 23.2g | fiber 0.8g | chol 39mg | iron 3.8mg | sodium 435mg
