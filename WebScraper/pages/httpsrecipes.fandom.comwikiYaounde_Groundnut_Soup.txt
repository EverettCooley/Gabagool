 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  ground nuts , roasted and shelled 
 4 cups consommé 
 1 cup  milk 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Pound the  nuts  to break them into pieces. 
 Simmer with the consommé for 30 minutes or until  nuts  are tender. 
 Force the  peanuts  through a sieve. 
 Add  milk  and seasoning to the sieved  nuts  and pour on the remaining stock. 
 Reheat for a further 10 minutes or until done.
