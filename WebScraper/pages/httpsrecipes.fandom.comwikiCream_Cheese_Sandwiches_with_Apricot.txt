Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (8 oz)  cream cheese 
 ½ cup  walnuts , chopped 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 6 slices of stale soft  bread 
 Batter : 
 4  eggs , beaten 
 ¾ cup  milk 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 pinch of  nutmeg 
 3 tbsp  butter 
 Sauce : 
 1 cup  apricot preserves 
 ⅓ cup  orange juice 
 1 cup sliced  banana  or  blueberries 
 Directions : 
 Mix cream cheese, walnuts and 1 teaspoon vanilla and spread on 6 slices of bread. 
 Put 2 slices together, filling side in. 
 Beat batter ingredients until well mixed. 
 Dip sandwiches in batter, coating both sides. 
 Melt butter in skillet and fry sandwiches until golden brown on both sides. 
 Warm apricot preserves with orange juice and beat to mix. 
 Sprinkle fruit over sandwiches and pour sauce over all.
