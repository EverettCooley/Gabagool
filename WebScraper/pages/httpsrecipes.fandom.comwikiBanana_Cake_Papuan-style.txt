Banana Cakes : 
 ½ cup of  sugar 
 2  eggs 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 3 medium size  bananas  (mashed) 
 1 teaspoon  bicarbonate of soda 
 1½ cups of  self-raising flour 
 ⅓ cup of  milk  - well enough to give it the right texture, wet, but not runny. 
 Directions : 
 Pre-heat oven to 180 °C (gas/electric). 
 Beat  butter ,  sugar  and  vanilla extract  until creamy. 
 Gradually beat in  eggs  and add mashed  bananas . 
 Mix  milk  and  bicarbonate of soda  and fold into  banana  mixture with  flour . 
 Pour batter into a round greased 20 cm deep-sided cake pan. 
 Bake for 45–51minutes in a hot oven.
