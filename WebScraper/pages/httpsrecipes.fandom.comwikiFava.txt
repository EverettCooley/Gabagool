Description : 
 Fava  -  broad bean  purée
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups dried  fava beans 
 3 cups  water 
 3 cloves  garlic  (mashed) 
 2 teaspoons  salt 
 2 teaspoons  sugar 
 ½ cup  olive oil 
 ½ cup  lemon juice 
 and  parsley  to garnish 
 Directions : 
 Soak dried fava beans overnight then boil in 3 cups of  water  until mushy. 
 Place fava beans and all the rest of the ingredients in a blender (or mix well by hand) and pour into two deep platters. 
 When set, cut into squares and serve with and  parsley  as garnish.
