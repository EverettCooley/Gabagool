Ingredients : 
 2½ lbs  chicken , cut up 
 ¼ cup  lime juice 
 2½ tbsp  honey 
 1 tsp  dry mustard 
 ½ tsp  black pepper 
 ¼ tsp  paprika 
 1 tbsp  chili powder 
 pinch of red pepper 
 1 large clove  garlic 
 2 tbsp  olive oil 
 1 tsp  salt 
 2 tbsp  basil 
 Directions : 
 Arrange  chicken  skin side down in shallow baking dish. 
 Combine remaining ingredients in small bowl. 
 Pour over  chicken . Cover and refrigerate 4 hours. 
 Bake at 350°F uncovered 30 minutes. 
 Turn and bake 30 more minutes, basting. 
 Broil until brown. 
 Sprinkle with bread crumbs.
