Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 12  chicken  pieces (about 2½ pounds) 
 salt  and ground  black pepper 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 1 clove  garlic , crushed 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 ¼ cup  catsup 
 ⅓ cup orange  marmalade 
 2 tablespoons  soy sauce 
 1 cup  chicken broth 
 2 teaspoons dry  mustard 
 1  green bell pepper , cut into thin strips 
 4 cups hot cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Season  chicken  with  salt  and  pepper . Place in lightly greased baking pan; bake 30 minutes at 350 degrees. 
 Meanwhile, cook  onion  and  garlic  in  butter  until tender. Add  catsup ,  marmalade , soy sauce, broth and  mustard . Simmer 10 to 15 minutes. 
 Spoon sauce over  chicken . Return to oven and bake 20 minutes. 
 Stir in  green pepper  and cook 5 minutes longer. 
 Serve  chicken  and sauce over fluffy  rice
