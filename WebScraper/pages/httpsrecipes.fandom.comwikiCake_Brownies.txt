Description 
 These cake  brownies  are somewhat less dense and almost cake-like compared to brownies made using store-bought cake mixes.
 
 Cake Brownies  from the Wikibooks Cookbook—original source of recipe, licensed under the GNU Free Documentation License 
 Cook Time : 30 minutes 
 Serves : About 20 
 Ingredients 
 2  eggs 
 1 cup (2.4 dl)  sugar 
 ½ cup (120 g)  margarine 
 4 tablespoons  cocoa 
 ¾ cup (180 g/1.8 dl)  flour 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  baking powder 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 ½ cup (120 g/1.2 dl) chopped  walnuts  or other nuts (optional) 
 Directions 
 Beat eggs until light. 
 Add sugar, melted margarine and cocoa. Blend. 
 Add flour, salt, baking powder, vanilla, and nuts. Mix well. 
 Pour into a 9" x 9" (23 cm x 23 cm) greased pan. Bake for 20 minutes at 350°F / 175°C.
