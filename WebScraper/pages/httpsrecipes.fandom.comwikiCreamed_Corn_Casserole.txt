Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can whole kernel  corn , drained 
 1 can creamed  corn 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 1 cup shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 2  eggs 
 ¼ cup melted  butter 
 1 pkg corn muffin mix (save some for topping) 
 Directions : 
 Combine all above in a greased casserole. 
 Bake at 350°F for 30 minutes. 
 I put all of the cornbread mix in with everything else. 
 Enjoy.
