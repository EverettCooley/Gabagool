 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 	fresh  peach , peeled and sliced 
 1 	 nectarine , peeled and sliced 
 2 	cups sliced  strawberries 
 2 (6 	ounce) cartons  strawberry yogurt 
 1 	tablespoon  Sugar 
 2 	tablespoons fresh  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 In food processor or blender, combine  peach , nectrine and  strawberry  slices with  yogurt  and  Sugar . 
 Process until smooth. Stir in  lemon juice . 
 Cover and chill until ready to serve.
