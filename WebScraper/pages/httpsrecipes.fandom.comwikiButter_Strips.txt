 Description : 
       Butter Strips     
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 100 g  butter  or  margarine 
 50 g  Sugar 
 1  egg 
 5 g  vanilla extract 
 3 g  salt 
 500 g  flour 
 125 g  chocolate chips 
 125 g  nuts , chopped 
 Directions : 
 cream  together  butter ,  Sugar ,  egg ,  vanilla , and  salt  . 
 Blend in remaining ingredients. 
 Divide dough into 4 equal parts, and shape into 12" rolls. 
 Place 3" apart on ungreased cookie sheets, and flatten. 
 Bake at 350F for 15â€“20 minutes . 
 Cool for a few minutes and cut into 1" diagonals.
