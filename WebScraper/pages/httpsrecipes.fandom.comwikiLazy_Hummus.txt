Ingredients : 
 1 x 14 oz can  garbanzo beans 
 bunch of  parsley 
 2 – 3  green onions 
 3 – 4 cloves  garlic 
 2 tbsp  tahini  (more if you like) 
 juice of one  lemon 
 teaspoon  salt 
 dash of  cayenne 
 Directions : 
 Place  parsley ,  green onions , and  garlic  into food processor and chop well. 
 Put garbanzos into mixture, add the rest of ingredients, and puree. 
 Taste to decide if spices need adjusting and mix accordingly. 
 Serve on pitas (delicious with falafel and tabouli) or with fresh veggies.
