Ingredients : 
 sweet potatoes 
 salt 
 oil 
 Directions : 
 Wash and peel your  sweet potatoes . 
 Slice thinly. 
 Soak in  water  to remove starch (maybe 1 to 2 hours). 
 Dry slices on paper. 
 Deep fry until crisp. 
 Drain excess  oil  on paper towel. 
 Sprinkle with  salt .
