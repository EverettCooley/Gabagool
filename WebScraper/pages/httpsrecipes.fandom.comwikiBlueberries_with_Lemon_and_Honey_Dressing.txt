Info 
 Cook Time : 
 
 
 Serves : 4
 
 Ingredients 
 2 cups fresh  blueberries , washed 
 1 cup plain  yogurt 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 2 tablespoons  honey 
 
 
 Directions 
 1.Place 1/2 cup  blueberries  in 4 bowls.
 
2.Combine yogurt, lemon juice and honey. 
 
3.Serve about 1/4 cup dressing over each bowl of berries.
