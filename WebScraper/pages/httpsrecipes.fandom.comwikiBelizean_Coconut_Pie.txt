Ingredients : 
 1.5  coconut  (grated) 
 3  eggs  (beaten) 
 1 tsp  nutmeg  (grated) 5 ml 
 1 tsp  lemon  or  vanilla essence  5 ml 
 1 small can  evaporated milk  (¾ cup) 200 ml 
 ¾ tin  condensed milk  (to taste) 
 Directions : 
 Use. already made frozen pie shell 
 Remove outer brown skin of  coconut . 
 (or use half-green  coconut ). 
 Grate  coconut . 
 Preheat oven to 450°F / 230°C. 
 Beat  eggs  until frothy. 
 Add essence,  nutmeg  and  evaporated milk . 
 Mix well. 
 Sweeten to taste with  condensed milk . 
 Add  grated coconut . 
 Mix well. 
 Put mixture in pie shell. 
 Bake at 450°F / 230°C for 15 – 20 minutes. 
 Lower oven to 350°F / 180°C. 
 Bake until filling is firm when touched. 
 Filling should not shake.
