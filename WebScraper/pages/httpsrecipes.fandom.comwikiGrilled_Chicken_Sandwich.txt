 Prep Time: 15 minutes 
 Cook time:40 minutes 
 Serves:4 
 
 Description : 
 Excellent quick meal with lots of flavor!
 
 Contributed by Lorrie Bunes, Coeur d'Alene 
 Ingredients : 
 4 boneless skinless  chicken breasts 
 4 crusty  sourdough  rolls 
 1 cup  Italian salad dressing  divide into 2 x ½ cup dishes 
 8 slices of thick smoked  bacon  cooked till just crisp 
 2 ripe  avocados 
 1 large  red onion 
 bunch of fresh  cilantro 
 sea salt  and fresh ground  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Place chicken breast between 2 sheets of waxed paper pound chicken. 
 Sprinkle salt and pepper on both sides. 
 Slice red onion into ¼ in thick slices. 
 Place chicken on heated BBQ grill low to medium heat grill approx 20 minutes each side brush each side with Italian dressing while grilling. 
 Grill onion during last 5 minutes turning 1x. 
 Place rolls on grill sliced in half just to warm. 
 Slice avocado into halves then slice each halve into slices. 
 Brush rolls with remaining dressing. 
 Place chicken breast on roll, add 2 slices of bacon, grilled onion, avocado, salt and pepper, cilantro. 
 Serve with pasta salad and fresh fruit.
