Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 shot  Kahlúa 
 1 shot white  creme de cacao 
 milk 
 Crushed  ice 
 Directions : 
 In shaker place crushed  ice , each shot of  Liquor , and fill half way with  milk . 
 Shake and pour into small glass. 
 Taste like  chocolate milk !
