Ingredients : 
 1 large or 2 small thinly sliced fresh California  nectarines 
 2 split  bagels 
 6 tbsp  cream cheese  (light or fat-free) 
 12 large  basil  leaves 
 cracked  black pepper  and/or red  chile  flakes 
 Directions : 
 Toast  bagels  and spread with  cream cheese  (1-1/2 tablespoons per  bagel  half). 
 Top with  basil  leaves and  nectarines  slices. 
 Sprinkle with  pepper  and/or chili flakes.
