Description : 
 This is good with  turkey ,  ham  or  pork .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  water 
 1 cup  cranberry sauce 
 1  apple , peeled and sliced 
 1 tbsp  margarine 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 dash ground  cinnamon 
 1½ cups  instant rice 
 Directions : 
 Combine  water ,  cranberry sauce ,  Apple ,  margarine ,  salt  and  cinnamon 
 Bring to full boil 
 Stir in  rice 
 Cover and remove 
 Let stand 5 minutes 
 Fluff with fork
