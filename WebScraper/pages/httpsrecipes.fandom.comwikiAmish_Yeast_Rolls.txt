      Amish Yeast Rolls     
 Ingredients : 
 7 cupss  flour 
 2 pkgs.  quick yeast 
 ½ – ¾ cup  sugar 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 2 sticks  butter 
 1 cup [sour cream]] 
 ½ to 1 cup  milk 
 Directions : 
 Heat until  butter  melts. 
 Mix wet to dry ingredients. 
 Put in warm place and let rise about 20 minutes. 
 Shape in desired shapes. 
 Let rise again, 20 minutes or until doubled. 
 Brush tops with melted  butter . 
 Bake 20 minutes.
