Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Williams Estate in Tyler, Texas in 1994.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 12  green sweet peppers 
 12  red bell pepper 
 12 large  green tomatoes 
 2 medium white  onions 
 1 quart  white vinegar 
 2 teaspoons  sugar 
 1 teaspoon  celery seed 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 4 tablespoons  flour 
 1 cup  prepared mustard 
 1 quart  salad dressing 
 Directions : 
 Grind  peppers ,  tomatoes  and  onions  in food chopper then drain all moisture. 
 Add remaining ingredients and boil for 10 minutes. 
 Remove and add  salad dressing .
