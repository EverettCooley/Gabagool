Description : 
 These cookies are triangle shaped, one end of them is dipped in  chocolate  and has silver  dragees  for decorations.
 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 This recipe makes 64 cookies. 
 Ingredients : 
 Dough : 
 1 cup  unsalted butter 
 1 cup  confectioner's sugar 
 1 tbsp  vanilla 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 2 cups  flour 
 Icing : 
 1 cup  semisweet chocolate 
 2 tbsp  water 
 1 tbsp  unsalted butter 
 Decoration : 
 silver  dragées 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 325°F degrees. 
 Line a jelly roll pan with foil. 
 Dough : 
 In a bowl, beat first 4 ingredients until blended. 
 Stir in  flour  until blended. 
 Press over bottom of ungreased pan. 
 Score dough lengthwise in 4 equal strips, then crosswise in 8 strips to make 32 rectangles. 
 Cut each rectangle in half diagonally, prick each triangle twice. 
 Bake 18 minutes or until shortbread looks dry and pale gold. 
 Cool in pan. 
 Recut cookies while warm. 
 Cool in pan. 
 Icing : 
 Stir  chocolate ,  water  and  butter  in a saucepan over low heat until  chocolate  and  butter  melt. 
 Coat a wire cooling rack with  cooking spray . 
 Dip wide edge of each cookie in  chocolate . 
 Place on rack. 
 Decoration : 
 Add  dragées  while wet, let  chocolate  set.
