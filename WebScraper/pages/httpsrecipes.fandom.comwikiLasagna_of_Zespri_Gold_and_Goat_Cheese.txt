Ingredients : 
 4 Zespri Gold ( kiwis ) 
 Fresh goat  Cheese  in roller of ± 20 cm long 
 Ingredients for sauce: 
 
 Juice of 1  lemon 
 1 tbsp of liquid  honey 
 1 tbsp of  olive oil 
 Zest of 1  lemon 
 1 tsp of freshly chopped  chilli pepper 
 Pepper  and  salt 
 1 tbsp of roughly chopped  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Peel the kiwis and cut them in thin slices of ±½ cm thick. 
 Slice the  cheese  roll as well. 
 Achieve a small tower while superimposing kiwi slices and slices of goat  cheese  successively (begin and finish with a slice of kiwi. 
 Mix all ingredients for the sauce. 
 Serve the  lasagne  towers on plates and pour the sauce on top. 
 Preparation time:  approx. 20 minutes Recipe and photo reprinted with permission from ZESPRI  kiwifruit
