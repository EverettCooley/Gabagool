Ingredients : 
 1½ cup  flour 
 ¼ cup  granulated fructose  or other  sugar  replacement 
 ½ cup unsweetened  cocoa 
 1½ tsp  baking soda 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 cup  low-fat milk  (2%) 
 ⅔ cup liquid  shortening 
 2  eggs 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Grease and  flour  a 13 x 9" cake pan. 
 Combine all ingredients in a mixing bowl and beat just until blended. 
 Pour into prepared pan and bake for 40 – 45 minutes. 
 Let cool.
