Description 
 
 Frappe Coffee  from the Wikibooks Cookbook—original source of recipe, licensed under the GNU Free Documentation License
 
 Cook Time : 10 minutes 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 2 spoons of instant  coffee 
 tall glass of cold  water 
 1-3 spoonfuls of  sugar  (optional) 
 2-3 ice cubes (optional) 
 milk  (optional;  condensed milk  such as the Greek nou-nou works best) 
 Directions 
 Put coffee and sugar in a shaker or tall glass and add cold water as high as it covers the mixture. 
 Shake or stir with mixer until the mixture becomes foamy. 
 Add cold water to fill the glass, ice cubes and as much milk (preferably evaporated or condensed milk) as you like. 
 Drink with a straw. 
 Types 
 Ratio Sugar:Coffee — 2:1 Frappé glykos (Sweet Frappé) 
 Ratio Sugar:Coffee — 1:1 Frappé metrios (Medium or Moderate Frappé) 
 No sugar Frappé sketos 
 Notes, Tips and Variations= 
 You can also add Baileys or a ball of ice cream. 
 By including the  ice  when shaking or stirring the coffee, it allows the foam to become more aerated and frothier. Once this has been done, you can then top up with water and/or milk.
