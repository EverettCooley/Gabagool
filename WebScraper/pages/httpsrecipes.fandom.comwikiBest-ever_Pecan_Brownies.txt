Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4  eggs 
 2 cups  sugar 
 1 cup melted  butter 
 1 cup  flour 
 ½ cup  cocoa 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 ½ cup chopped  pecans 
 Directions : 
 Beat  eggs ,  sugar  and  butter . 
 Add  flour ,  cocoa ,  vanilla  and  pecans ; mix. 
 Bake at 350°F in greased 9x13 inch pan for 35 minutes. 
 Enjoy.
