This dish is high in vitamin A, a good source of vitamin C, cholesterol free, and almost sodium-free.
 
 Info 
 Cook Time : About 1 hour, 15 minutes 
 Serves : 4
 
 Ingredients 
 2 1/2 cups fresh  winter squash , cut into 1/2 inch slices 
 1 1/2 cups cooking  apples , pared and cut into 1/2 inch slices 
 2 teaspoons  cinnamon 
 1 teaspoon  nutmeg 
 Directions 
 1.Alternate layers of  squash  and apples in an 8"x 8" pan, ending with apples on top layer. Sprinkle spices over top layer. 
 
2.Cover pan with aluminum foil and bake in 350 degree F oven for approximately 45–60 minutes until squash is tender. 
 
3.Remove foil and bake another 10–15 minutes to remove any excess liquid that might have accumulated. Cool slightly before serving.
 
 Source 
 Farmers' Market Recipes  from the Commonwealth of Massachusetts Department of Agricultural Resources, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
