Description : 
 Always check the ingredients to make sure the product is vegan.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½  soy sauce  or  Bragg's 
 ¼ cup  rice vinegar 
 ¼ cup  lemon juice 
 ¼  water 
 2 tbsp toasted  sesame oil 
 1 tsp fresh grated  ginger 
 Directions : 
 Put all ingredients in a jar and shake well.
