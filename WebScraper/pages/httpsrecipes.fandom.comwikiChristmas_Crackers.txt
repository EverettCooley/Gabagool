Ingredients : 
 12 oz painted  pinto beans  (prepared according to package instructions) 
 ¾ cup  rice  of your choice (prepared according to package instructions) 
 2 cups  salsa  (your choice of fire) 
 2 bunches  scallions , chopped 
 1 pkg  sun-dried tomatoes , chopped finely 
 1 tsp  olive oil 
 parsley 
 yoghurt 
 Directions : 
 Sauté scallions and  tomatoes  in  oil . 
 Mix beans and  rice  with salsa and add sautéed vegetables in a casserole dish. 
 Heat (oven at 350°F for 20 minutes or microwave on High for 5 minutes - until heated through), sprinkle with chopped  parsley  (or whatever green herb you wish). 
 Serve hot with yogourt on the side. 
 This contains complete protein, is vegetarian, high fibre, low fat and accompanies the season well. 
 For even lower fat, sauté veggies in a pan coated with vegetable spray.
