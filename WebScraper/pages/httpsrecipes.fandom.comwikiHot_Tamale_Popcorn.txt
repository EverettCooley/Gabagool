Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons butter 
 3 tablespoons corn syrup 
 5.5 oz box Hot Tamales (or 3 Alarm Hot Tamales for extra heat!) 
 1 cup mini marshmallows 
 2 tablespoonsÂ  Imperial Sugar Extra Fine Granulated Sugar 
 1/2 teaspoon baking soda 
 6 cups popped popcorn 
 Instructions : 
 Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Place popcorn in a large bowl, set aside.1 
 In a medium size non-stick pan, melt butter over medium-high heat. Add corn syrup, Hot Tamales, mini marshmallows and sugar.2 
 Cook, stirring occasionally, until Hot Tamales have melted completely. This should take about 8 minutes. The mixture will be very sticky. Remove from heat and stir in baking soda.3 
 Pour the mixture over the popcorn, and stir to completely coat the popcorn. Pour popcorn out onto parchment paper and let cool for 10 minutes.4 
 Serve immediately or store in an airtight container for up to 3 days.
