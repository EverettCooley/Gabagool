 Description : 
 This salad is a refreshing summer salad that goes well with a light  Pasta  and wine for quick dinner on those lazy days of summer. Fruits paired with  avocado  can change depending on what's available in your area.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 avocados, peeled,pitted and cubed 
 1  mango , seeded, peeled anc cubed 
 2 Cups fresh or canned  pineapple  chunks 
 11 oz can mandarine  oranges , drained 
 ½ Cup fat free plain  yogurt 
 2 Tbsp honey 
 1 Tbsp  powdered sugar 
 ½ tsp  lime  zest 
 2 Tbsp  lime juice 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl, combine  mango , avocado , pineapple , and mandarine  oranges . 
 In a small bowl, using a whisk, stir together honey,  yogurt ,  lime juice ,  lime  zest, and  powdered sugar  until combined. 
 Add this dressing to fruits in large bowl and gently fold until fruit mixture is coated. 
 Garnish with  lime  peel curls if desired.
