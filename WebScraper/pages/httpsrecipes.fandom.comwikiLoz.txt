Ingredients : 
 8 ounces  ground almonds 
 4 ounces  icing sugar 
 4 â€“ 6 tablespoons  orange blossom water 
 4 ounces  pistachio nuts , peeled and finely chopped 
 1 ounce  caster sugar 
 4 ounces extra  icing sugar 
 4 ounces extra  pistachio nuts , peeled 
 Directions : 
 Combine the finely chopped  pistachio nuts  and  caster sugar , and set aside. 
 Combine the  ground almonds  and  icing sugar  with enough orange-blossom  water  to form a stiff paste. 
 Knead until smooth and allow to rest for about 10 minutes. 
 Shape the paste into small balls the size of a  walnut , and using a teaspoon handle, make a small hole in each ball and and fill it with the combined  pistachio nuts  and  caster sugar ; close the hole over the filling and reshape to a round shape. 
 Roll the balls in  icing sugar  and place in small paper cups or on a serving plate. 
 Decorate the top of each ball with a peeled  pistachio nut . 
 Serve with  coffee .
