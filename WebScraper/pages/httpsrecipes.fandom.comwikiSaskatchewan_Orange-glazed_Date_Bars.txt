Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Yield: 3-4 dozen cookies 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups sifted  all-purpose flour 
 ¾ teaspoon  baking soda 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ¾ cup lightly packed  brown sugar 
 1¼ cups (8 ounces) chopped pitted  dates 
 ½ cup  water 
 ½ cup soft  butter 
 3  eggs , slightly beaten 
 ½ cup  milk 
 ½ cup  orange juice 
 1 cup chopped  pecans 
 orange glaze  (see below) 
 Orange glaze : 
 2 tablespoons soft  butter 
 1½ cups sifted  confectioners' sugar 
 1½ teaspoons grated  orange peel 
 2½ tablespoons  orange juice 
 Directions : 
 Sift flour, baking soda, and salt together. 
 Combine brown sugar, dates, and water in a large saucepan. 
 Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until dates soften. 
 Remove from heat and stir in butter. 
 Add eggs and mix well. 
 Add dry ingredients to date mixture; stir until blended. 
 Add milk and orange juice gradually, mixing well. 
 Stir in nuts. 
 Bake at 350°F about 30 minutes. 
 Cool completely in pan on wire rack. 
 Spread glaze over top and cut into bars. 
 Orange glaze : 
 Combine butter, confectioners' sugar, grated orange peel, and orange juice. 
 Beat until smooth.
