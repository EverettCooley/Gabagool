 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 16 ounces  tempeh 
 1 large  Onion , thinly sliced 
 2 cups prepared barbecue sauce 
 Directions : 
 Cut the  tempeh  into chunks. Steam over boiling  water  for 15 minutes. Place the  tempeh  and sliced  Onion  in a deep dish and pour the barbecue sauce over it. Marinate in the refrigerator for at least one hour. Place on outdoor grill and cook until browned and heated through. Baste frequently with barbecue sauce. Can be cooked in a 350Â° F oven, covered for 30 minutes.
 
 Yield: 4 servings.
 
 Per serving: 435 calories; 20 grams protein; 12 grams fat (38%); 60 grams carbohydrate; 13 grams fat; 950 mg sodium.
