Description : 
 This is a great dish for those who love that southern spicy taste.
 
       Chili Chicken     
 Serves 4 to 6. 
 Ingredients : 
 6 boneless  chicken breast  halves, cut in 1-inch pieces 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 1 cup chopped  bell pepper 
 2  garlic  cloves 
 2 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 2 cans Mexican stewed  tomatoes  (approx. 15 ounces each) 
 1 can  chili beans 
 ⅔ cup  picante sauce 
 1 teaspoon  chili powder 
 1 teaspoon  cumin 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Sauté  chicken ,  onion ,  pepper ,  garlic  in  vegetable oil  until vegetables are wilted. 
 Transfer to slow cooker; add remaining ingredients. 
 Cover and cook on  low  for 4 to 6 hours. 
 Serve with  rice .
