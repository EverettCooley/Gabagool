 From Rival Crock-Pot cookbook, date unknown 
 Servings: 6 
 Ingredients : 
 1 package package  macaroni , cooked and drained 
 2 tablespoons  oil 
 1½ pounds  ground beef , browned and drained 
 salt  and  pepper 
 1 large  onion , chopped 
 2 each stalks  celery , chopped 
 1 can  tomato paste 
 ¾ cup  water 
 2 tablespoons  sherry 
 1 can  tomato soup 
 grated  cheddar  or  Parmesan cheese 
 1 teaspoon leaf  oregano 
 Directions : 
 Toss cooked noodles in oil. 
 Place in greased crock-pot. 
 Add all remaining ingredients, except grated cheese. 
 Stir thoroughly. 
 Sprinkle cheese over top. 
 Cover and cook on low 4 to 8 hours. (High: 2 to 3 hours).
