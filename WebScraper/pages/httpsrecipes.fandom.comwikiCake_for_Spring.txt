 Description : 
 In Romanian: Chec de primavara
 
 Ingredients : 
 Same quantities as for  Economical Cake 
 Directions : 
 Proceed as for  Economical Cake . 
 Before adding the  flour , add ½ cup cold  milk , ¼ cup  rum , grated peel from 1  lemon . 
 Then add the  flour  and the whipped  egg  whites. Divide the mixture into 2 parts. 
 Pour the first part (slightly more than half), in a bread pan. 
 To the second part, add  food coloring  (red or green). 
 Mix well and pour gently over the middle of the batter already in the pan. Bake.
