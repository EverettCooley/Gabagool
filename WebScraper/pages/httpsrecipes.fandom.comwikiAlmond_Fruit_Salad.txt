Description : 
 Makes 4 servings. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups cooked white or  brown rice , cooled to room temperature 
 1  orange , peeled, quartered, and sliced 
 1/2 cup seedless  red grapes , halved 
 1 medium  kiwi fruit , pared, quartered and sliced 
 1/3 cup  slivered almonds , toasted 
 2 tablespoons  orange juice 
 1 tablespoon soy sauce 
 2 teaspoons  vegetable oil 
 1/4 teaspoon minced  fresh ginger 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  orange ,  grapes ,  kiwi fruit  and  almonds  in large bowl. 
 Blend  orange juice , soy sauce,  oil  and  ginger  in small bowl; pour over  rice  mixture. 
 Toss lightly.
