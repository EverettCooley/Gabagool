Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 2 scoops Blue Bell® vanilla  ice cream 
 1 can Coca-Cola® 
 2 oz Maker's Mark®  Bourbon whiskey 
 Directions : 
 Scoop two large scoops of  vanilla  ice- cream  into frosted  beer  mug. 
 Next, add 2 ounces maker's mark. 
 Then, pour in coke. 
 Gently stir and enjoy.
