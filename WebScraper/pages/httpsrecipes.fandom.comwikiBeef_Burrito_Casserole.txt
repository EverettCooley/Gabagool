Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Elmquist Estate in Heath, Texas in 1981.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can  refried beans 
 1 cup all purpose  baking mix 
 ¼ cup water 
 1 pound  ground round  browned 
 16 ounce jar medium  salsa 
 1½ cup shredded pepper  jack cheese 
 sliced  green  or  black olives 
 Directions : 
 Mix together first 3 ingredients and put in bottom of greased rectangular pan. 
 Layer top with hamburger, salsa, cheese and olives. 
 Bake at 375°F for 30 minutes. 
 Serve with lettuce and sour cream.
