Description : 
 Shrimp ,  leek , and  pepper  sauce score a touchdown for this delicious guacamole!
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 ripe genuine  California avocados , seeded, peeled and coarsely mashed 
 1 tsp grated lime  peel 
 1 tbsp fresh  lime juice 
 10 drops  red pepper sauce 
 Â½ tsp coarse  garlic salt 
 4 oz cooked, shelled and de-veined small  shrimp 
 2 tbsp chopped fresh  dill  leaves 
 1 medium  leek  (white to light green part only), finely chopped 
 Directions : 
 In medium bowl, combine avocados,  lime  peel and juice, red pepper sauce and  garlic salt . 
 Coarsely chop  shrimp . 
 Stir in  shrimp ,  dill  and  leek . 
 Serve with  tortilla chips  or cut vegetables.
