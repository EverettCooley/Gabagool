Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Servings:  Makes 4 
 Ingredients : 
 4  limes 
 2  oranges 
 5 cloves  garlic , thinly sliced 
 1 tbsp  canola oil 
 1 tbsp  light brown sugar 
 1 tsp  salt 
 4 thick cut bone in  pork chops 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 Instructions : 
 Zest for ½ cup zest. 
 Juice limes for ½ cup. 
 Zest orange for 2 tbs zest, then juice oranges for ½ cup. 
 In glass dish, mix together lime zest and juice, orange zest and juice, garlic, oil, brown sugar and ½ tsp salt. 
 Add pork chops and turn to coat. 
 Cover, refrigerate 6 hours. 
 Heat grill. 
 Remove pork from marinade and sprinkle with remaining salt and pepper. 
 Grill pork chops for 12 minutes turning, or until done.
