 Description : 
 Vietnamese Fresh  Chicken  with  lemon grass  and  cashew   nuts  is a meat recipe. 
 
       Fresh Chicken with Lemon Grass and Cashew Nuts     
 Ingredients : 
 vegetable oil 
 1/2 cup roasted, unsalted  cashew   nuts 
 2 small dried chillies 
 1 tbsp. chopped  lemon grass 
 1 clove  garlic , chopped 
 2 shallots, cut in quarters 
 1 lb. lean, corn-fed  Chicken , sliced 
 1/2 tsp.  Sugar 
 1 tbsp. oyster sauce 
 1 tbsp. Nuoc Mam sauce or light soy sauce 
 3 tbsp.  chicken stock  or  water 
 Directions : 
 Prepare the chilies in some  oil , prepare the  garlic , add the  Chicken  slices,  Sugar , Nuoc Mam sauces.
Add the other ingredients.
