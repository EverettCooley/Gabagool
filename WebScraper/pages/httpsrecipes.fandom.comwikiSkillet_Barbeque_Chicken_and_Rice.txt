 Ingredietns : 
 1 lb boneless skinless  chicken breasts  cut into strips 
 2 tbsp  oil 
 1Â½ cups  barbeque sauce 
 1 cup  water 
 1 (11 oz) can  corn , drained 
 1  green pepper  chopped 
 2 cups  Minute rice , uncooked 
 Directions : 
 Brown chicken in hot oil in large skillet on medium-high heat. 
 Stir in barbeque sauce, water, corn and pepper; bring to a boil. 
 Reduce heat; cover and simmer 5 minutes. 
 Stir in rice, cover. 
 Remove from heat. 
 Let stand 5 minutes.
