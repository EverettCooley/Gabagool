Description 
 Home Cookin' Kentucky Recipes  by the Office of Senator Mitch McConnell, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Cook Time : About 45 minutes 
 Ingredients 
 ½ cup  margarine , melted 
 1 cup  sugar 
 ½ cup  flour 
 2  eggs , slightly 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 ¾ cup chopped nuts ( pecans  or  walnuts ) 
 ¾ cup  chocolate chips 
 Directions 
 Mix in order given. 
 Pour into unbaked 9 inch pie shell. 
 Bake at 350°F for 30 minutes or until middle is set.
