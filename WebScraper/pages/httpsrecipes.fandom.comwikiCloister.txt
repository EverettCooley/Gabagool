Ingredients : 
 2 oz  gin 
 ½ oz yellow  Chartreuse ® 
 ½ oz fresh  grapefruit juice 
 1 tsp fresh  lemon juice 
 ½ tsp  superfine sugar 
 Directions : 
 Fill a cocktail shaker with all the ingredients and  ice , shake and strain intro a cocktail glass.
