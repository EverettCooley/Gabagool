 Description : 
 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 2 med  acorn squash 
 1  apple , chopped 
 ½ cup  cranberries , fresh or frozen, chopped 
 1  orange , peeled and chopped 
 ½ tsp ground  cinnamon 
 2 tsp  honey   or  equal  sweetener 
 Directions : 
 Cut the  squash  in half and remove the seeds. 
 Combine the remaining ingredients, except the honey or sweetener. Fill the  squash  with the mixture. 
 Drizzle the honey or sweetener over the  squash . Place in a baking pan. 
 Cover with aluminum foil or a lid and bake for 25 minutes in a 400 °F oven. 
 Remove the foil and continue baking until the  squash  is tender, about 20 minutes. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 calories: 125

 protein: 2 
 fat: 1

 potassium: 608 
 cholesterol: 0
