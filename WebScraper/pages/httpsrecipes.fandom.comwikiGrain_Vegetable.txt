 Description : 
 '
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 	cup 	Raw  cashew   nuts 
 1 	 Quick  brown rice 
 --(Vegetable & Herb), cooked 
 1 	cup 	Cooked 7  grain  Cereal 
 2 	cup 	 soy milk 
 1 	large 	 Onion , chopped 
 3/4 	cup 	Wheatgerm 
 4 	tbsp 	 canola oil 
 2 	tbsp 	Soy sauce 
 1 	tsp 	 sea salt  (optional) 
 4 	tbsp 	Minced  parsley 
 1/2 	tsp 	 thyme 
 1/2 	tsp 	Ground  celery seed 
 1/2 	tsp 	Ground  sage 
 1 	tsp 	 Onion  powder 
 1 	tsp 	 garlic powder 
 Directions : 
 1 	Chop the  nuts  fine, or run through a food grinder.
 2 	Add the remaining ingredients and mix well.
 3 	Place in a baking dish and bake at 350 °F.
 4 	Uncovered for 1 hour or until done.
 5 	Serve with favorite gravy.
