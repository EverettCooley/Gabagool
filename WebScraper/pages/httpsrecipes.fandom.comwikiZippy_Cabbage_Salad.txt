 Description : 
 This salad resembles a coleslaw however it does not 
 have the heavy traditional sweet mayonaise. It is light and refreshing and very good for you.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ a Med. size head of  green cabbage 
 2  tomatoes  diced 
 2  California avocados  diced 1/2 " chunks 
 4 to 8 thin slices of fresh jalepeno diced 
 1 to 2 sprigs  cilantro  chopped (optional) 
 ½ whole juice of 1  lemon 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Slice  cabbage  finely ( like coleslaw )
 Chop  tomato ,  avocado , jalepeno, and  cilantro 
 Add all of these to a large bowl and squirt  lemon juice  on the mixture
 Add  salt  and  pepper  to taste, mix thoroughly
 Best if eaten immediately
