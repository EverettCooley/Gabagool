Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup uncooked  rice 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  white pepper 
 1 cup  water 
 ½ cup dry  white wine 
 ½ cup  apple juice 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 ⅔ cup chopped, cored  apple 
 ½ cup  walnuts , chopped 
 1⅓ pounds  pork tenderloin , roasted and sliced 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  pepper ,  water , wine,  apple juice  and  butter  in 2½- to 3-quart saucepan. 
 Bring to a boil; stir once or twice. 
 Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 15 minutes, or until  rice  is tender and liquid is absorbed. 
 Add  apple  and  walnuts . 
 Fluff with fork. 
 Serve with roasted  pork  tenderloin slices.
