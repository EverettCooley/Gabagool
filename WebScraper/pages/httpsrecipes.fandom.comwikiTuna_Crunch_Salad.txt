Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 6 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x 6-ounce  tuna 
 ¼ cup chopped  green pepper 
 1 tbsp minced  onion 
 1 cup crisp  cabbage , shredded 
 ½ cup  salad dressing 
 2 tbsp  vinegar 
 2 cups  potato chips 
 Directions : 
 Flake  tuna ; add  pepper ,  onion ,  cabbage  and mix well. 
 Mix  salad dressing  and  vinegar  together and fold in just before serving. 
 Add  potato chips  and toss lightly. 
 Serve on crisp  lettuce .
