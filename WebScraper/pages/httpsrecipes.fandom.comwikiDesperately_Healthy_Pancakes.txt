Ingredients : 
 Part 1 : 
 ½ cup  soy flour 
 ½ cup  whole wheat flour 
 ½ cup unbleached  all-purpose flour 
 2 tbsp  wheat germ 
 2 tbsp  powdered milk 
 2 tbsp unprocessed  bran 
 1 tbsp  baking powder 
 ½ tsp  baking soda 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 grated peel of 1  orange 
 Part 2 : 
 2  eggs , separated 
 ½ cup plain  yogurt 
 ¼ cup  oil 
 1 tbsp  molasses 
 1 tbsp  honey 
 1½ cup  orange juice 
 Directions : 
 Combine the first 10 ingredients. 
 Reserve the  egg whites  and add the  egg yolks  and the remaining ingredients to the  flour  mixture in a food processor, blend well. 
 Beat the  egg whites  until stiff; fold into the pancake mixture. 
 Cook on a 300 °F griddle, for 2½ minutes on one side and 1 minute on the other. 
 Makes about 36 x 3-inch cakes.
