 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  strawberries  or  blackberries 
 1 cup  orange juice 
 2 tablespoon  instant tapioca 
 1/4 teaspoon  cinnamon 
 1/2 cup  Sugar 
 Juice of 1/2  lemon  and zest 
 1 cup  buttermilk 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Blend 2 cups washed and hulled  strawberries  in processor or blender. Heat  orange juice  in saucepan. Place blended  strawberries  in strainer over  orange juice  and push through. Add tapioca,  cinnamon , and  Sugar . Let it thicken a little over heat. Add  lemon juice  and  lemon  zest from other half of  lemon . Add  buttermilk  and heat for 1 minute. Refrigerate 8 hours. Serve in chilled bowls.
