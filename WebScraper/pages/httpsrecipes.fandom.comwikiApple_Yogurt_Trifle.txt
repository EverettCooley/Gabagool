Ingredients : 
 1  Granny Smith apple , cored and finely chopped 
 2 x 8-ounce containers low-fat cherry  yogurt 
 10 tablespoons  Grape-nuts   cereal 
 Directions : 
 Evenly divide half of the chopped  apple  pieces among four parfait dishes or tall glasses. 
 Divide  yogurt  from one 8-ounce container among dishes. 
 Add 2 tablespoons grape-nuts to each trifle, then top with layers of remaining  yogurt , chopped  apple  and a sprinkle of grape-nuts on top. 
 Refrigerate at least 15 â€“ 20 minutes before serving to allow cereal to soften slightly.
