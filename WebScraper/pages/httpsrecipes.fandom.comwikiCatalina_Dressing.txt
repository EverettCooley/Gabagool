Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  ketchup 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 ½ cup  red wine vinegar 
 ½ cup  onion , grated 
 1 teaspoon  paprika 
 ½ teaspoon  Worcestershire sauce 
 1 cup  salad oil 
 salt 
 pepper 
 Directions : 
 In a blender, blend together the ketchup, sugar, vinegar, onion, paprika and Worcestershire 
 With the blender running, slowly add the oil. 
 Check the seasonings. 
 Cover and refrigerate until needed.
