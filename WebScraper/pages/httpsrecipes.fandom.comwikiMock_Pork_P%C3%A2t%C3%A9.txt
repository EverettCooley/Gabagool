Description : 
 Vegetarian Creton
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 oz  vegetarian ground chicken 
 8 oz  vegetarian sausage 
 2 cup  water 
 2 tbsp  margarine 
 1  onion , chopped fine 
 1  potato , peeled, diced or 
 ¼ to ½ cup  bread crumbs 
 17  allspice  balls 
 ½ tsp  cinnamon 
 ½ tsp  nutmeg 
 ¼ tsp ground clove 
 2  garlic  cloves 
 salt  and  pepper  ( pepper balls  optional) 
 Directions : 
 Place "meats" and  onion  in pot with 2 cup  water . 
 Simmer for about 1 hour. 
 Add  margarine ,  salt ,  pepper , clove,  garlic ,  allspice  balls. 
 Boil  potato  until done. 
 Mash finely and mix into ‘ meat ’ or if not using potato add bread crumb the last 15 minutes of cooking time. 
 After cooking time drain off creton liquid mixture in colander. 
 Put all ingredients into food processor and process to a paste. 
 Shape into a small loaf, and chill to set in fridge for 24 hrs.
