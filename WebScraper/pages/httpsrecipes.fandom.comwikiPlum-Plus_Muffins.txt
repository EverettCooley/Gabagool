Info 
 Plums, Canned, Purple  by the US Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Serves : 12 
 Ingredients 
 Non-stick  cooking spray 
 14 ounce (1 box) oat bran muffin mix 
 ¾ cup  apple juice 
 1 cup  plums , drained and finely chopped 
 ¾ cup  raisins 
 Directions 
 Preheat oven to 425 °F. 
 Lightly coat about 12 muffin cups with nonstick spray. 
 In medium bowl, combine muffin mix and juice until just moistened. 
 Stir in plums and raisins. 
 Spoon batter into muffin cups, full. 
 Bake 14 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. 
 Remove muffins from pan and cool on wire rack.
