Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 8 to 10 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ to 2 cups  all-purpose flour 
 1½ tsp  salt 
 2 tsp dried  thyme  leaves 
 2 tsp  paprika 
 1  egg , beaten 
 ⅓ cup  milk 
 2 tbsp  lemon juice 
 2 to 3 lbs  broiler/fryers , cut up 
 vegetable oil 
 Description : 
 Combine  flour ,  salt ,  thyme ,  paprika . 
 Combine  egg , milk ,  lemon juice . 
 Roll  chicken  in  flour  mixture, dip into  egg  mixture, then back in  flour  again. 
 Refrigerate at least 30 minutes. 
 Heat ½ inch of  oil  to 375°F in electric skillet. 
 Put  chicken  in skillet and fry until golden brown on both sides. 
 Reduce heat to 250°F, cover and continue to cook for 25 minutes. 
 Drain on paper towels. 
 Enjoy.
