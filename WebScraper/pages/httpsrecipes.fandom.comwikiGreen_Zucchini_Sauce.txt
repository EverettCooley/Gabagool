Ingredients : 
 1 medium  zucchini 
 10 fl oz oz fat free plain  yoghurt 
 1 clove  garlic  (or more) 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 2 tbsp dried  basil 
 salt  and fresh ground  black pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Just liquidize the whole lot and serve. 
 It is also great on baked  potatoes .
