Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Ritchie Estate in Balch Springs, Texas in 1984. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds processed  American cheese 
 2 medium  white onions  chopped 
 2 tablespoons  butter 
 2 cans  chili with beans 
 1 teaspoon  chili powder 
 1 dash  tabasco sauce 
 Directions : 
 Melt  cheese  in top of double boiler. 
 Brown chopped  onions  in  butteror   margarine ; add to  cheese  with other ingredients. 
 Blend well. 
 Serve hot.
