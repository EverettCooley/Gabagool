Description : 
 This recipe taken from www.Portuguese-recipes.com 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/3 lb. of linguica 
 2 tbsp. of  butter 
 1/2 cup of chopped  Onion 
 1/2 lb. of chopped  Swiss cheese 
 3  eggs , beaten 
 2 cups of  cream 
 1/2 tsp. of  salt 
 dash of  cayenne pepper 
 1- 9 inch. pie shell 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 450. 
 Sauté  Onion  in the  butter  for 5 minutes. 
 Add ground linguica, sauté additional 5 minutes, set aside. 
 Bake the pie shell for 5 minutes, remove it from the oven and sprinkle linguica mix in it add chopped Swiss. 
 Mix together the  eggs ,  cream ,  salt  and  cayenne pepper . Strain into the pie shell. Bake 10 minutes at 450.Reduce heat to 350 and bake until firm. This should take around 20 minutes.
