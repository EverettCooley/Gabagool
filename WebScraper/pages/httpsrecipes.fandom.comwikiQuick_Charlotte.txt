Description : 
 In Romanian: Sarlota la iuteala
 
 Ingredients : 
 7 oz / 210  ladyfingers   or   butter  biscuits 
 ½ lb / 250 g  whipped cream 
 7 oz / 200 g ground  walnuts   or   hazelnuts 
 ½ lb / 250 g  milk  sweetened with 6 teaspoons of  sugar 
 2 cups  rum 
 some fruits from  preserves 
 Directions : 
 Mix the  milk  with the  sugar  and  rum . 
 Soak the  ladyfingers  in it. 
 Arrange the  ladyfingers  side by side on a platter, spread  walnuts  on top, then a layer of  whipped cream , then another layer of  ladyfingers ,  walnuts  and  whipped cream . 
 Garnish with fruit from preserves.
