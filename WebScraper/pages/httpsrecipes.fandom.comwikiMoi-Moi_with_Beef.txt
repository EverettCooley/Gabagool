Ingredients : 
 2 cups  black-eyed peas 
 Â½ can  corned beef 
 1  onion 
 2 fresh  tomatoes 
 salt  to taste 
 pepper  to taste 
 3x4" aluminum foil bag 
 2 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 Directions : 
 Soak peas in 3 cups of  water  for 1 hour. 
 Wash off the black eye and membrane covering. 
 Blend fine in blender with  onions  and  tomatoes . 
 Add  salt , if desired. 
 Mix with  vegetable oil  in bowl. 
 Mixture should be fairly consistency. 
 Add the corned  beef  in bits. 
 Mixture should be put in aluminum foil bag and sealed. 
 Place in large pot with 2 cups of boiling  water . 
 Allow to cook for 45 minutes to 1 hour. 
 Add  water  to pot, if needed. 
 The moi-moi comes out solid when it cools.
