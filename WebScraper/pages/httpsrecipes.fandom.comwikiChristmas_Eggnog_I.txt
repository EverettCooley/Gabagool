Description : 
 This is a holiday favourite at my house.
 
 submitted by: Andrew Brownrigg 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  milk 
 1 large  egg 
 1 tbsp  white sugar 
 1 dash  nutmeg 
 1 tsp  rum extract 
 Directions : 
 Place all ingredients in a mixer or blender and mix well, until  nutmeg  distributed throughout. 
 Add  sugar  as needed, until desired flavour is reached. 
 You can also substitute the  rum extract  with real  rum  in which case you will have to substitute the 1 tsp of extract with 1 oz of  rum . 
 Try different  extracts  as well. 
 You can eve try add some  maraschino cherry  juice for sweetness and colour.
