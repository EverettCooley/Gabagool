Description : 
 Contributed by  E: catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Prep time: 15 min. 
 Refrigerating time: 4 hours 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pkg. (8-serving size)  or  2 pkgs. (4-serving size) Jell-O Grape  or  Orange Flavor Gelatin Dessert 
 4 cups (1 qt.) cold prepared Kool-Aid Grape  or  Orange Flavor Soft Drink Mix 
 Directions : 
 Prepare gelatin as directed on pkg. 
 Refrigerate 4 hours or until firm 
 Break gelatin into small flakes with fork. 
 Spoon about ½ cup gelatin into each of 8 tall glasses. 
 Pour ½ cup cold soft drink over gelatin in each glass. 
 Serve immediately with a straw to sip gelatin pieces & soft drink. 
 Variations : 
 Two Color Goo : 
 Prepare grape & orange flavor gelatin as directed on pkg. 
 Refrigerate and break into flakes as directed. 
 Layer ¼ cup orange (or grape) gelatin flakes, ¼ cup grape (or orange) gelatin flakes in each glass. 
 Pour cold orange soft drink over gelatin in each glass. 
 Fizzy Goblin Goo : 
 Just before serving, prepare Kool-Aid as directed, substituting cold seltzer for water.
