 The Omani people are real good at preparing snacks like raised pancakes containing white flour, brown flour, baking powder, eggs, sugar, salt, milk, cooking oil, butter, and honey. Except this, they also cook flat pancakes, using brown flour, water, cardamom, and salt; cinnamon rolls with white flour, yeast, milk, sugar, salt, eggs, and butter or margarine; raised potato doughnuts containing powdered milk, butter, nutmeg or cinnamon, salt, yeast, sugar, potatoes, eggs, water, and white flour; delicious croissant rolls consisting of white flour, butter, sugar, yeast, salt, eggs, vegetable oil, cream cheese, and water. Of course, zucchini bread should also be mentioned; it is prepared from ingredients like white flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, salt, eggs, sugar, vegetable oil, vanilla, zucchini, raisins, and nuts.
