Ingredients : 
 Steak, any kind will work, but best if  New York cut 
 Olive oil 
 Worcestershire sauce 
 Vinegar 
 Soy sauce 
 Montreal stark or chicken seasoning 
 Tomato and basil seasoning 
 Salt 
 Pepper 
 Directions : 
 Mix everything in a medium to large bowl (except for the steak) and stir until well blended 
 Add the steaks in to the bowl and make sure they are completely covered cover bowl with tin foil, plastic wrap, or if the bow use that and put it in the fridge for 30 minutes to make sure the marinade get in the stake 
 Once you have done that take out the stakes use a non-stick spray or pan and put the steaks on and set the stove setting to 8 and every 8 minutes check it and flip it over or how every you like to cook steak put if you do it my may do that 3 times the after that let it cook for 4 minutes then take it off
