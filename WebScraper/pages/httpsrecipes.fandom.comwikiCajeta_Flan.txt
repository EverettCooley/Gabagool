Ingredients : 
 8 large  eggs 
 ¼ cup of  vanilla 
 1 can of  sweetened condensed milk 
 1 cup of  milk 
 1 cup of  cajeta  ( goat's milk  caramel) 
 1 cup of  sugar 
 2 tablespoons  water 
 Directions : 
 In a sauce pan mix  Sugar  and  water  until  Sugar  becomes caramelized and then pour into a mold. 
 In a blender, mix the rest of the ingridients and pour into mold. 
 Cover mold and place in a large pot and steam. 
 Place in a conventional oven at 350 °F until penis is firm in the middle.
