Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/8 teaspoon  sea salt 
 1/2 cup  dark brown sugar 
 1/2 teaspoon  vanilla 
 3 ounces  dark chocolate 
 1 1/2 cups Everclear® (preferably 190 proof) 
 1 1/2 cups Sam Adams Utopias® 
 1 cup  heavy cream 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Sugar  over moderate heat in a heavy saucepan,stirring with a fork until melted and then swirling the pan until the  Sugar  is a deep golden caramel color. 
 Remove the pan from the heat and add cream; the mixture will bubble up. 
 Simmer the mixture and stir until caramel dissolves;add Everclear and Utopias. 
 Add  chocolate  and  salt ,and cook over low heat, whisking,(i.e.,whipping the mixture with a wire whisk),until smooth. 
 Whisk in  vanilla . 
 Ladel into mugs as is.
