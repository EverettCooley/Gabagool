Ingredients : 
 1 cup (100 grams)  cashew   nuts , broken 
 ½ cup (50 grams)  sugar 
 ¼ teaspoon tinned  milk powder  /  condensed milk 
 Directions : 
 Soak the  cashew   nuts  in warm  water  for about 30 minutes and wash them 2 to 3 times. 
 Drain all the  water  out and blend the  cashew  nuts to a fine paste. 
 Keep aside. 
 Dissolve the  sugar  in ½ cup of  water  and prepare a syrup of one string consistency. 
 Add the  cashew  paste to the syrup and cook over a medium flame, stirring continuously till the mixture leaves the sides of the pan (approx. 5 to 7 minutes). 
 Transfer to a plate and cool. 
 Cut in diamond shape pieces. 
 Store in refrigerator.
