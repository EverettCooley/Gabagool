 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 	cup 	Fresh  corn  kernels 
 1 	large 	 Onion 
 3/4 	cup 	 tomatoes , diced 
 1/4 	cup 	Red  bell pepper , diced 
 1 	tbsp 	 cilantro , fresh, chopped 
 1 	small 	 jalapeño pepper , finely 
 -minced 
 1 	tbsp 	 seasoned rice vinegar 
 1 	tbsp 	fresh  lime juice 
 Freshly ground  black pepper 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 1 	Bring 6 cups of  water  to a boil.
 2 	Add the  corn .
 3 	Allow the  water  to return to a boil and blanch the  corn  for 1 minute, or just until tender.
 4 	Drain and refresh under cold running  water .
 5 	Drain well and set aside.
 6 	peel the  orange  including the white pith and the membrane between segments.
 7 	Cut each segment into quarters.
 8 	in a large bowl, combine the  corn ,  orange  pieces,  tomato , red  bell pepper ,  cilantro , and jalapeño.
 9 	Pour the  vinegar  andlime juice over the vegetables and toss well.
 10 	Season to taste with  pepper  and  salt .
 11 	Serve at room temperature.
