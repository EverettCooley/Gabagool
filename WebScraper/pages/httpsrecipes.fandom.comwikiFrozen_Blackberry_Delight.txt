Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Turner Estate in Dallas, Texas in 1997.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups fresh  blackberries 
 â…” cup  granulated sugar 
 2 cups  whipping cream 
 4 drops  almond extract 
 fresh mint and additional  blackberries 
 Directions : 
 In a blender or food processor process blackberries until smooth. 
 Transfer to a bowl and add sugar then allow to stand for 1 hour. 
 In a mixing bowl beat cream and extract until soft peaks form then fold into berry mixture. 
 Pour into freezer safe bowl that has been lined with plastic wrap then cover and freeze overnight. 
 Turn out onto a serving plate about 1 hour before serving then return to the freezer. 
 Before serving garnish with mint and blackberries then cut into wedges.
