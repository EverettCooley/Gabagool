Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: old newspaper clipping 
 6 – 8 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  butter  or  margarine 
 1 cup  sugar 
 3  eggs 
 ¾ cup  milk 
 2 cups  blueberries 
 1½ cups  graham cracker  crumbs 
 1 cup  all-purpose flour 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 Directions : 
 Cream butter; beat in sugar. 
 Beat in eggs, 1 at a time. 
 Stir in milk. 
 Combine blueberries, graham cracker crumbs, flour, baking powder and baking soda. 
 Add all at once; mix until well blended. 
 Pour into greased and floured 8" spring-form pan. 
 Bake at 375°F for 50 – 60 minutes or until cake test done and top is richly browned. 
 Cut into wedges; serve warm or cold topped with sweetened whipped cream.
