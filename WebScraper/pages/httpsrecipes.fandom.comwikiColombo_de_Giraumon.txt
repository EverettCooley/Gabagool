Description : 
 Spicy pumpkin
 
 Source: Troth Wells  The World in Your Kitchen: Vegetarian Recipes . 
 Ingredients : 
 1  onion , finely sliced 
 1  garlic  clove, crushed 
 2 tsp  curry powder 
 3  cloves , crushed 
 ½ red  chile , finely chopped 
 1 lbs  pumpkin , cut in 1" cubes 
 2  tomatoes , chopped 
 1 tbsp  sultanas 
 ¼ tsp  sugar , optional 
 1  lemon , juiced 
 2 tbsp  oil 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 First, heat the  oil  in a heavy pan and then add the  onion . 
 Sauté until it is transparent and then add the  garlic  and cook that also. 
 Next put in the  curry powder , ground cloves, the chopped  tomatoes ,  sultanas  and  sugar . 
 Sprinkle the  lemon juice  over. 
 Cover, and cook very gently for 30 – 40 minutes or until  pumpkin  is tender, stirring frequently to ensure that it does not catch. 
 Season and serve to accompany main dishes and  rice .
