Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2  chicken breasts , skinned, boned and sliced into one inch strips 
 ¼ tsp minced  garlic 
 ¼ tsp  pepper  (white, if available) 
 ½ cup low fat, reduced sodium  cream of chicken  soup 
 1 cup sliced  mushrooms 
 1 cup plain  yogurt  or fat-free  sour cream 
 2 tbsp  sherry 
 ¼ cup fat free  Parmesan cheese 
 Directions : 
 Place  chicken  pieces in greased casserole. 
 Combine  garlic ,  yogurt  or sour cream, soup,  pepper ,  sherry  and  mushrooms . 
 Pour over  chicken . 
 Sprinkle  cheese  over top. 
 Microwave, covered, on high for ten minutes or until  chicken  is no longer pink. 
 Serve with noodles,  rice  or  bulgur . 
 Note : 
 This recipe can also be baked in a traditional oven at 400°F for 25 minutes.
