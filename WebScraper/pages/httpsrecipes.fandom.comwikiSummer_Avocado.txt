Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 Summer Fruit 
 2  Red Delicious apples , cut into 1/2 cubes 
 1 tsp  lemon juice 
 2 Genuine  California avocados , cut into 1/2 inch cubes 
 2  kiwi fruits , sliced 
 1  banana  sliced 
 1 Pint  strawberries , sliced 
 1 Pint  blueberries 
 5 Tbsp  honey 
 ¼ tsp  mace  (East Indian spice similar to  nutmeg ) 
 Waffles 
 2 Cups  flour 
 3 tsp  baking powder 
 1 tsp  salt 
 2 Tbsp  Sugar 
 1 ¾ Cups  milk 
 3  eggs  separated 
 4 Tbsp melted  butter  (slightly cooled) 
 Directions : 
 Summer Fruit
 Place  Apples  in a bowl with  lemon juice  to keep from  browning . 
 Add all other ingredients. 
 Gently mix fruit salad topping. 
 Serve on top of hot waffles, with or without liquid syrup.
 Waffles
 Sift  flour ,  baking powder ,  Sugar , and  salt . 
 Add  egg yolks  and  milk . 
 Beat with a wire whisk until mixed. 
 Slowly mix in melted  butter . 
 Beat  egg whites  until stiff. 
 Fold into  flour  mixture. 
 Pour into hot waffle iron. 
 Note: You may substitute homemade waffles with frozen waffles.
 Serving Suggestion: Serve with  avocado  and fruit salad topping.
