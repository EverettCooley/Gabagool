Ingredients : 
 1⅓ teaspoons  yeast 
 3 cups vegan bread or all purpose  flour 
 ⅓ cup  wheat bran  (or substitute  flour ) 
 ⅓ cup mashed cooked  sweet potato  or  yams 
 1½ tablespoons grated  orange zest 
 ¾ teaspoons  salt 
 1½ tablespoons  applesauce 
 ¾ cup  water 
 Directions : 
 On basic bread cycle: add ingredients to machine according to manufacturer's directions. 
 Makes a nice, soft sandwich loaf.
