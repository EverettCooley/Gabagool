Ingrediants : 
 Cale 
 1 box devils food cake mix 
 1 1/4 cups buttermilk 
 1/2 cup oil 
 3 large eggs 
 1 tbs orange extract 
 1 cup mini semisweet chocolate chips 
 Frosting 
 4 sticks butter, softened 
 2 jars 7 oz each marshmallow cream 
 2 cups confectioners sugar 
 2 tsp vanilla 
 1/4 cup orange zest 
 2 tbs orange juice 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 degrees. 
 Beat first 5 ingredients in a bowl until blended, then beat on medium speed for 2 min. # Stir in chips. Pour into 2 round cake pans coated with nonstick spray. 
 Bake for 32 minutes or until done. Cool in pans for 10 minutes. Invert. 
 Beat butter in a bowl until creamy. 
 Beat in marshmallow cream. 
 When blended, beat in confectioners sugar, vanilla, zest and juice. Beat for 4 minutes or until fluffy. 
 Place 1 cake layer on a serving plate. Spread with 1 cup frosting. 
 Top with remaining cake layer. Spread top and sides with remaining frosting. 
 Decorate with cut orange slices. 
 MAKES 16 SERVINGS OF CHOCOLATE ORANGE BLOSSOM CAKE.
