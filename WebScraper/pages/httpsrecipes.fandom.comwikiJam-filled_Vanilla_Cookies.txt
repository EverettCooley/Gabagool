Ingredients : 
 2 cups  margarine  (please use Earth Balance!) 
 1 cup  sugar 
 2 tbsp  soy milk , mixed with 1 tbsp  cornstarch  or  tapioca flour 
 2 tsp  vanilla 
 6 cups  flour  sifted with ½ tsp  salt 
 jam  for filling 
 Directions : 
 Cream the  margarine  and  sugar  together until smooth. 
 Add the  soymilk /starch mixture, and  vanilla , and beat until well mixed. 
 Using a wooden spoon, stir in the  flour / salt  mixture until combined. 
 Roll walnut-sized balls and place on a baking sheet. 
 Make an indentation in the center of each ball with your thumb. 
 Fill these with ½ tsp of jam each. 
 Bake for 10 minutes, or until they are just turning golden. 
 Cool.
