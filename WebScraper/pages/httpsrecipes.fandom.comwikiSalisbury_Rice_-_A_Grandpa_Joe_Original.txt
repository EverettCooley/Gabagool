Ingredients: : 
 1 cup  long-grain rice 
 3 cups  water 
 1 24 oz Banquet Salisbury Steak Diner 
 1 cup  onion , chopped fine 
 1/2 cup  bell pepper , chopped fine 
 1/4 cup  peanut oil 
 1/2 tsp  beef boullion granules 
 1 16 oz can small  green peas 
 
 
 Directions: : 
 Let Salisbury Steak come to room temperature. 
 Cut the steaks into fine pieces. 
 Drain peas and put liquid in 2 qt pot along with rice, water and bouillon granules and bring to a rolling boil. 
 Cover, turn off heat and leave pot on hot burner. 
 Meanwhile put oil in skillet over medium heat. 
 Chop onion & pepper and saute. 
 Add peas, Salisbury steak and its gravy. 
 Check to see if rice has absorbed all liquid. If not, bring to a rolling boil again and let set covered for about 15 minutes. 
 Combine rice with contents of skillet. and serve. 
 
 
 Notes : 
 Makes 8 servings. 
 Local Sav-A-Lot has Banquet Salisbury Steak dinner for 89 cents. Pkg is 24 oz and has 6 patties plus delicious brown gravy. 
 I took my recipe to covered dish and several ladies had seconds. 
 Enjoy
