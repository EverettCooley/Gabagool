Ingredients : 
 3 bananas 
 2 tbs water 
 1 tbs bittersweet chocolate pieces 
 Directions : 
 Peel bananas and cut into 1 inch pieces. 
 Place banana slices on wax paper lined rimmed baking sheet. 
 Cover and freeze for 2 hours or until frozen. 
 Process bananas, water, and chocolate in a food processor until nearly smooth. 
 Pour into glasses. Garnish with Mint Leaves. 
 MAKES 2 CUPS OF BANANA CHOCOLATE SORBET.*
