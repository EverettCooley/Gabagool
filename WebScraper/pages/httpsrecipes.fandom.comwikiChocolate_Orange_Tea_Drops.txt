 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  all-purpose flour 
 1/2 tsp  salt 
 1/2 cup  butter  or  margarine  (we recommend LAND O LAKES®  butter ) 
 1/2 cup  granulated sugar 
 3 oz  cream cheese 
 1 large  egg 
 1 tsp grated  orange peel 
 1 cup  vanilla extract 
 1 cup NESTLÉ® TOLL HOUSE®  semi-sweet chocolate  Morsels 
 Directions : 
 1 PREHEAT oven to 350 °F. Lightly grease baking sheets. 
2 COMBINE  flour  and  salt  in small bowl. Beat  butter ,  Sugar ,  cream cheese ,  egg ,  orange peel  and  vanilla extract  in small mixer bowl until creamy. Gradually beat in  flour  mixture. Stir in morsels. Drop by rounded tablespoon onto prepared baking sheets. 
3 BAKE for 12 to 14 minutes or until light golden brown. Cool on baking sheets for 2 minutes; remove to wire racks to cool completely.
