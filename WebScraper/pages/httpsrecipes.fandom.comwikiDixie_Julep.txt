 Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tsp  powdered sugar 
 2 1/2 oz  Bourbon 
 Directions : 
 Put  Sugar  and  Bourbon  into collins glass. 
 Fill with crushed  ice  and stir gently until glass is frosted. 
 Decorate with sprigs of  mint . Serve with straws.
