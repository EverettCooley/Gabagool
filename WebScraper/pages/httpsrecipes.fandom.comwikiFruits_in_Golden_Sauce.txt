Description : 
 Unlike most desserts, this one's low in fat and refined sugar. It contains the natural fruit sugar. 
 
 Preparation Time : 10 minutes | Cooking Time : 20 minutes 
 Fills 1¼ cups 
 Ingredients : 
 For the fruits : 
 400 g ripe  bananas 
 400 g  strawberries  or 200 g  peaches 
 200 g  pineapple 
 200 g  figs 
 For the sauce : 
 1 teacup pulp of Alphonso  mango 
 2 tablespoons fresh low-fat  yogurt 
 Directions : 
 For the fruits, slice  banana  and halve the  strawberries . 
 if you are suing  peaches  and  pineapple , slice them. Halve the  figs . 
 For the sauce, mix the  mango  pulp and  yoghurt  very well. 
 To proceed, pour the sauce over the fruits and keep in the refrigerator until you wish to serve.
