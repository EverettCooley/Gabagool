Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  margarine  or  butter 
 25  marshmallows  (or 3 cups mini) 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 5 cups  Rice Krispies 
 Directions : 
 Melt  margarine  in a medium heavy saucepan. 
 Add marshmallows and cook over low heat until melted. 
 Remove from heat, stir in  vanilla  and krispies. 
 Moisten spoon with  water  and press mixture into greased 9" square pan. 
 Let set 30 minutes, cut in 24 pieces. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Each serving 1 square:
 
 54 calories 9 g carbohydrate, 2 g fat 
 Diabetic choices: 1 fruit choice + ½ fats & oils
