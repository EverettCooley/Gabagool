 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons of  miso  Paste 
 half box of  tofu 
 1 head (substitute: small dried or small piece of fillet) 
 a few pieces of Bonito 
 half sheet of dried seaweed or  sushi nori 
 half  green onion 
 Directions : 
 Cut seaweed and  tofu  into square pieces, and chop  green onion  into small pieces 
 Boil a saucepan of  water  (about 3 1/2 cups) with head. 
 Add  miso  Paste and stir it until it is totally mixed with the boiling  water 
 Finally, throw in the rest ingredients (seaweed,  tofu ,  green onion  and Bonito) and boil it for a few seconds
