Ingredients : 
 3 cups of  brown short grain rice 
 hummus (you can make your own - below) 
 100gr grated vegan  cheddar  cheese 
 3 cloves  garlic 
 1  leek 
 2  spring onions  or 1  onion 
 1  zucchini 
 ½  green pepper 
 1  carrot 
 1 cup  tomato purée 
 1 teaspoon  fenugreek 
 1 teaspoon vegan  white wine vinegar 
 ground  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Start off by boiling the  rice  add  rice  to hummus and grated  cheese . 
 (to make hummus, blend 1 can chick  peas  with 150ml of tahini and the juice of two  lemons ). 
 Sauce : 
 Heat about 1 tablespoon of  olive oil  in pan. 
 Fry  garlic   onions  and  leeks  gently and add the  fenugreek . 
 Add  carrot  and  green pepper  and finally the  zucchini . 
 Cook for about 10 mins. 
 Add  tomato  puree  vinegar  and ground  black pepper  and leave to simmer for another 10 mins. 
 Pre-heat oven to gas mark 3. 
 butter  a medium sized loaf tin. 
 Pour in half of the  rice  mixture then top with the sauce mixture. 
 Finally finish off with the rest of the  rice  mixture and grated  cheese  on top. 
 Cook in the oven for around 30 mins.
