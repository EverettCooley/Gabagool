Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 oz  gin 
 1/2 oz fresh  lime juice 
 3/4 oz  raspberry liqueur 
 ginger ale 
 Directions : 
 Use a highball glass. Fill it with  ice  cubes, pour the  gin ,  lime juice  and raspberry liqueur,  ginger ale . Garnish with  lime  and serve.
