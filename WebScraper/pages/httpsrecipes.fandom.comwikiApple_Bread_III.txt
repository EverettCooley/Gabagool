Ingredients : 
 3 cups  flour 
 2 cups  sugar 
 1 teaspoon  cinnamon 
 2  eggs 
 2 teaspoons  vanilla 
 1¼ cups  oil 
 2  apples , chopped 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 cup chopped  nuts  (optional) 
 1 teaspoon  baking soda 
 Preparation : 
 Preheat oven to 300 °F. 
 In a large bowl, mix together all ingredients until well blended. 
 Pour batter into 2 greased loaf pans. 
 Bake for about 1 hour.
