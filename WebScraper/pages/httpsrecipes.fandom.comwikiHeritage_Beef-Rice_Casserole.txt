Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 1 1/2 pounds  Beef  chuck, cut into 1-inch cubes 
 1/2 cup chopped  Onion 
 1/2 cup  tomato  catsup 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 cup  cottage cheese 
 1/2 cup  sour cream 
 1/2 cup chopped sweet pickle (optional) 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1/2 cup grated  Cheddar   Cheese 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter  in large skillet. Add  Beef  chuck and cook until brown, about 10 minutes. 
 Add  Onion , cover and simmer for 20 minutes. 
 Stir in catsup and  rice . 
 Remove from heat and blend in  cottage cheese ,  sour cream , pickle,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Turn into buttered 2-quart casserole. Sprinkle with grated  Cheese . 
 Bake at 350 degrees 25 minutes.
