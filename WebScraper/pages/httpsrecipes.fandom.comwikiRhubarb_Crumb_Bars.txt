Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 Top and bottom "crust"
 
 1 cup  flour 
 3/4 cup  oatmeal  (uncooked) 
 1 cup  brown sugar  (packed) 
 1/2 cup  butter , (melted) 
 Filling
 
 3/4 cup  Sugar 
 1-1/2 Tbls  flour 
 1/4 teas  nutmeg 
 1 Tbls  butter  (softened) 
 1  egg  (beaten) 
 2 cups  rhubarb  (cut into 1/2" pieces) 
 Directions : 
 Mix  flour ,  oatmeal ,  brown sugar  and  butter  until crumbly. 
 Press 1/2 into greased 9" square pan. 
 Add  rhubarb . 
 Beat  egg . 
 Blend  Sugar ,  flour ,  nutmeg  and  butter . 
 Add beaten  egg , beat until smooth. 
 Pour over  rhubarb . 
 Top with other half crumb mixture, press mixture down lightly. 
 Bake at 350 degrees for 20â€“25 minutes.
