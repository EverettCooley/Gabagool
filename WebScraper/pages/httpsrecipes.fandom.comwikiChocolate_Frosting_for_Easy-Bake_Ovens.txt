Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  powdered sugar 
 ½ cup  cocoa 
 3 tablespoons  instant non-fat milk 
 6 tablespoons  shortening 
 Directions : 
 Combine  sugar ,  cocoa , and  milk powder . 
 Cut in  shortening . 
 To use: Combine ⅓ cup of mix to ¾ teaspoon  water . 
 Mix well and frost cake. 
 Enjoy.
