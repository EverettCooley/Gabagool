Description : 
 Notation on card says it was out of the Recipes from Old Virginia cookbook of 1828 from my Aunt Maggie's Collection.
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound cooked and peeled  sweet potatoes 
 1 cup  sugar 
 ½ cup  butter , melted 
 6  egg yolks , beaten 
 1 tablespoon granted  lemon rind 
 ¼ teaspoon  mace 
 1 cup  orange juice 
 6  egg  whites 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Mash the  sweet potatoes . 
 Add 1 cup of the  sugar  and the melted  butter . 
 Combine with beaten  egg yolks ,  lemon  rind,  mace , and  orange juice . 
 Beat the whites until stiff and fold into  sweet potato  mixture. 
 Pour into a well greased baking dish. 
 Sprinkle with ¼ cup  sugar  and bake in a 350°F oven for 1 hour.
