white chocolate   cranberry  Squares
 
 2 1/2 Cups All Purpose  flour 
 2/3 Cup  granulated sugar 
 1 Cup  butter 
 2  eggs , Beaten 
 1 Can (300 Ml)  sweetened condensed milk 
 1 Cup  white chocolate chips 
 1 Cup Dried  cranberries 
 1 Pkg (200 g) Sweetened Shredded  coconut 
 1/2 Cup Semi-Sweet or Premium Dark  chocolate chips , OptionalCombine in Food Processor or Large Bowl,  flour  And  sugar .Pulse or Cut in  butter  to Form Fine Crumbs. Press Firmly Into 
 13 x 9Â inch Baking Pan. 
 Bake in 350 Oven About 12 Minutes, Until Lightly Browned. MixTogether in Large Bowl,  eggs  And Sweetened  milk . Stir in WhiteChocolate Chips,  cranberries  And  coconut . Pour Over Crust,Bake 18 to 22 Minutes, Until Set in Centre. Melt Semi-SweetChocolate Chips, Drizzle Over Top.Makes About 48.
