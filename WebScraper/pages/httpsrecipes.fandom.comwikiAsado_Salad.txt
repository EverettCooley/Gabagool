 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 White  onions 
 carrots 
 Fresh  basil 
 olive oil 
 salt 
 pepper 
 lemon juice 
 Grated  Cheese 
 Directions : 
 Put 1-2 unpeeled  onions  near the asado, they should slowly cook. Turn them often so they turn brown all around 
 Grate the  carrots 
 When the  onions  are brown and soft, move them from the fire, peel them under  water , then dice them into small pieces. 
 Mix the  onions  and the  carrots , add  salt  and  pepper . 
 Add a few ripped  basil  leaves (tear them with your hands) 
 Sprinkle  olive oil  and a little  lemon juice . 
 Add the grated  Cheese  to flavor 
 Mix the salad.
