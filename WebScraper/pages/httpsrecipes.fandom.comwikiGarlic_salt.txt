About garlic salt : 
 Garlic salt is a seasoned salt used as the food seasoning made of a mixture of dried, ground garlic and table salt with an anti-caking agent. In its most basic form, it is made by combining 3 parts salt and 1 part garlic powder by volume, or 6 parts salt and 1 part garlic powder by weight.
