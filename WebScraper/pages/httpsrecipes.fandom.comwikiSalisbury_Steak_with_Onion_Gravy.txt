Salisbury  Steak  With  Onion  Gravy
 
 1 Can Condensed  Onion  Soup, Undiluted (10.5 Oz) 
 1 1/2 Lb. Ground  Beef 
 1/2 Cup Fine Dry Breadcrumbs 
 1  egg , Slightly Beaten 
 1/4 T.  salt 
 Dash  pepper 
 
 1 T.  flour 
 1/4 Cup Catsup[ 
 1 T. Worcestershire 
 1/2 T. Prepared  mustard 
 
Mix 1/3 Cup of Sour With  Beef ,  bread  Crumbs,  egg ,  salt  And  pepper . Shape Into 6 Oval Patties. in Large Skillet, Cook Patties Until Browned on Both Sides. Drain or Spoon Off Fat. in Medium Bowl, Gradually Blend Together The Remaining Soup And  flour  Until Smooth. Stir in The  Catsup ,  water , Worcestershire And  mustard . Pour Mixture Over Patties in Pan. Stir to Loosen Any Brown Bits. Cover. Cook Over Low Heat 20 Minutes or Until Meat is Cooked Through. Stir Occasionally. Serve Patties Topped With Gravy.
 Serves 6
