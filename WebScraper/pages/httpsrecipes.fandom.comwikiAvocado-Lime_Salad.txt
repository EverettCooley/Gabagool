Description : 
This savory, avocado-based salad is made with real vegetables, fruit juice, herbs, and a high hint of cumin for rich zest. Make the salad more appetizing by adding to the salad with other herbs and spices.       Avocado-Lime Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 3 tbsp. fresh  lime juice 
 4 tsp.  salt 
 4 small  tomatoes , cut into ¾-inch cubes (2 ½ cups) 
 2 ripe medium  avocados , cut into ¾-inch dice (1 ½ cup) 
 3  scallions  (white and light green parts), thinly sliced 
 1 large clove  garlic , minced 
 1 tbsp.  olive oil  or  vegetable oil 
 1 ¼ tsp. ground  cumin 
 1 large head  Boston lettuce , torn into large pieces (8 cups) 
 1 medium  cucumber , peeled, quartered lengthwise, seeded and thinly sliced diagonally (1 cup) 
 2 tbsp. coarsely chopped  cilantro 
 Directions : 
 In a medium, nonmetallic bowl, whisk together 2 tablespoons lime juice and 2 teaspoons salt. 
 Add tomatoes, avocados, scallions and garlic. 
 Using a rubber spatula, gently fold to combine. Season to taste with pepper. 
 In a large bowl, whisk together oil, cumin, the remaining tablespoon lime juice, and 2 teaspoons salt. 
 Add lettuce and toss. 
 Season to taste with pepper. 
 Divide lettuce among any plates. 
 Top each with mound of avocado mixture. 
 Top with cucumber slices, sprinkle with cilantro and serve.
