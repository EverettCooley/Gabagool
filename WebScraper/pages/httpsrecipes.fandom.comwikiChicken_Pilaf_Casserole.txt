Ingredients : 
 4 Tbls. butter 
 4 Tbls. flour 
 1 3/4 cup milk 
 1 can (10 3/4 oz.) chicken broth 
 salt and pepper to taste 
 1 lb. chicken, cooked & cut into bite size pieces 
 1 cup cooked long-grain rice 
 1/4 lb. mushrooms, sliced 
 1 green pepper, chopped 
 1 red pepper, chopped 
 1 Tbls. chopped parsley 
 Directions : 
 Melt butter in saucepan. 
 Add flour and cook one minute. 
 Gradually stir in milk and broth. Bring to a boil and simmer for 2 minutes. 
 Add salt and pepper. 
 Fold in chicken, rice, mushrooms, peppers and parsley. 
 Turn into a 2 qt. casserole pan. 
 Cover and bake for 45 minutes at 350 degrees.
