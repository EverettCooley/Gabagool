Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes For Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Makes 14 slices, each one about ½ inch thick. 
 Ingredients : 
 Dry ingredients : 
 1½ cups  whole wheat flour 
 ½ cup unsweetened shredded  coconut 
 2 teaspoons  baking powder 
 ½ teaspoon  baking soda 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 Wet ingredients : 
 1 cup mashed  banana 
 3 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 2 tablespoons liquid  honey 
 Directions : 
 Mix together  whole wheat flour , shredded  coconut ,  baking powder ,  baking soda  and ½ teaspoon  salt , in a bowl, set aside. 
 Combine mashed  banana ,  vegetable oil  and  honey . 
 Stir the dry ingredients into the wet ones,until just combined. 
 Do not over mix. 
 Batter will be rough, lumpy and stiff. 
 Spread the batter evenly in a lightly greased loaf pan. 
 Bake about 45 – 50 minutes at 350°F. 
 Turn out of pan and cool completely on a wire rack. 
 Note : 
 After making this,let it cool,wrap and let stand over night before cutting,to allow the flavors to blend. To store longer than 2 days, store in fridge.
 
 Nutritional information : 
 carbs: 16 | fat : 4 | protein: 2
