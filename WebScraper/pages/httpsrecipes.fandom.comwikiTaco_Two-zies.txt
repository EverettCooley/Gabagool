      Taco Two-zies     
 Ingredients : 
 10 Mission™ soft taco size flour  tortillas  warmed 
 10 Mission™  taco shells 
 1 lb  ground beef 
 ⅔ cup  water 
 1 can  refried beans  warmed 
 Toppings : 
 Pace™ salsa to taste, shredded  lettuce 
 shredded  cheese 
 diced  tomatoes 
 2 pkg. Lawry's™ Taco Spices and seasonings 
 Directions : 
 Follow directions on 1st package of Lawry's™ Taco Spices to prepare meat. 
 Add 2nd package of seasonings to  refried beans . 
 Spread warmed beans across each warmed tortilla. 
 Place a taco shell in center of each tortilla and fold around to seal. 
 Fill taco shell with seasoned meat and toppings.
