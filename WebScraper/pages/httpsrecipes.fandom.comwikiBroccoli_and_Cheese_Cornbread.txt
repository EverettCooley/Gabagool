Description : 
 Contributed  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 boxes Jiffy  cornbread  mix 
 4  eggs 
 1 (10 oz)  cottage cheese 
 1½ sticks  butter 
 1 (10 oz) frozen chopped  broccoli 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Melt butter in a 9 x 13 inch baking dish. 
 Thaw frozen broccoli under water. 
 While butter is melting, mix everything in a large mixing bowl. 
 When butter is melted, pour into batter and stir well. 
 Then pour batter into the 9 x 13 inch greased baking dish and cook 40 – 60 minutes.
