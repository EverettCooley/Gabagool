 Description : 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 x  Chicken  breasts, skinned 
 3 cup  chicken stock  (up to 4) 
 2 x  carrots 
 1 x  celery  rib (up to 2) 
 1/4 lb  raisins 
 1 can  corn  kernels 
 1/4 lb Cooked  green peas 
 DRESSING:
 
 1 cup  Mayonnaise 
 1/4 cup  chicken stock 
 TOPPING:
 
 2 cup Straw  potatoes 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Chicken  in stock. 
 Chill 
 Drain  Chicken , saving 1/4 cup stock 
 Shred  Chicken 
 Shred  carrots 
 Dice  celery 
 Stir together the saved stock and  mayo  to make the dressing 
 Combine all the salad ingrdients but  potatoes 
 Add dressing to the salad and stir to combine
