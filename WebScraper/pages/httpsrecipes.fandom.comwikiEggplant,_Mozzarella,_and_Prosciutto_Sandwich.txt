Ingredients : 
 Breaded and Fried Eggplant Cutlets 
 grated  mozzarella 
 sesame seed  roll 
 3 slices of  prosciutto 
 half of a canned / preserved roasted  red pepper 
 Directions : 
 Place several slices of eggplant on a dinner plate and sprinkle with grated mozzarella. Microwave until the cheese has melted. Place on the sesame seed roll, layer prosciutto slices, and top with the flattened roasted red pepper.
