 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 Bread slice 
 1/2 tsp  butter 
 1 large  egg 
 salt 
 pepper 
 Directions : 
 1 You will need: frying pan with lid,  water  glass, measuring spoons and a pancake lifter. 
2 Heat the frying pan to medium. 
3 Using the  water  glass turned upside down, press to cut a round piece out of the center of the bread. 
4 Place both the bread slice and the round piece of bread in the frying pan. 
5 Brown 1 side, turn heat to medium-low. 
6 Turn the bread slice and the round piece of the bread over. 
7 Melt the  butter  or  margarine  in the hole in the bread slice. 
8 Break the  egg  into the hole. 
9 Sprinkle with  salt  and  pepper . 
10 Put the lid on. 
11 Cook slowly until the white of the  egg  is set. 
12 Remove the  egg  and toast with the lifter to the plate. 
13 Garnish with the toasted round piece and fresh fruit and  Cheese .
