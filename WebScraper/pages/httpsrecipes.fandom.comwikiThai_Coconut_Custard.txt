       Thai Coconut Custard     
 This Thai delicacy is made with only 4 ingredients: coconut milk, eggs, palm sugar, and salt.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 ½ cup  coconut milk 
 6  eggs , 
 ¾ cup  palm sugar 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Beat everything together. 
 Cook, stirring, in a double boiler until it resembles soft scrambled eggs. 
 Pour into a small casserole dish. 
 Bake at 350° F for 30 minutes, then brown under the broiler.
