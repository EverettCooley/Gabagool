 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2  romaine lettuce 
 2 big  Steak , Jersey or Israeli  tomatoes 
 4 sm.  cucumbers 
 1  green pepper 
 2 to 3 tbsp. chopped  parsley 
 2 scallions 
 -- SAUCE: 
 3 tbsp.  olive oil 
 1 1/2 squeezed  lemon 
 salt  and  pepper 
 1 big squeezed  garlic  clove 
 Directions : 
 Cut all vegetables to small pieces. 
 Chop  parsley  and sprinkle over the tossed vegetables. 
 Stir sauce ingredients and pour on vegetables in the last minute. 
 Enjoy!
