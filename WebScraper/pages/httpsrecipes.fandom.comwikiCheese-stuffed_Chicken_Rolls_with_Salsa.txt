Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Servings: 4 
 Ingredients : 
 4  chicken breast  halves without skin—boneless 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 3 oz Monterey  jack cheese 
 1 tbsp  butter  — melted 
 ½ cup  salsa 
 2 tbsp  cilantro  — minced, optional 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 375°F (190°C). 
 Grease a 9 inch (230 mm) square pan or appropriate baking dish. 
 Flatten each breast half to ¼ inch (7 mm) thickness. 
 Sprinkle with  salt . 
 Divide  cheese  evenly on each breast. 
 Roll chicken around  cheese , folding in sides. 
 Brush each roll with the melted butter. 
 Place seam side down in dish. 
 Bake uncovered for about 30 minutes. 
 Serve the rolls with the salsa poured on top. 
 Garnish with the  cilantro  if desired.
