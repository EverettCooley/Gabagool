Description : 
 As to why these are called "Amerikaantjes" (Little Americans), that is unknown. They may have been called after the inhabitants of the USA, or they may have been called after that small place in the province of Brabant that is called America.
 
 Ingredients : 
 75 g  butter  or  margarine 
 100 g  sugar 
 1  egg 
 400 g  flour 
 1 satchel  vanilla sugar 
 1 satchel  baking powder 
 1 pinch of  salt 
 ⅛ liter  milk 
 Garnish : 
 white icing ( rum  or  lemon  icing) 
 Directions : 
 Cream the  butter  and add the  egg ,  sugar  and  vanilla   sugar . 
 Mix the  flour  with the  salt  and  baking powder . 
 Alternate mixing the  flour  and the  milk  in with the creamed  butter . 
 Grease a baking sheet and put a dollop of batter on the sheet, leaving plenty of distance between them. 
 Bake the Amerikaantjes in the middle of the pre-heated oven at 200 °C. 
 Once they've cooled spread the icing on the bottom.
