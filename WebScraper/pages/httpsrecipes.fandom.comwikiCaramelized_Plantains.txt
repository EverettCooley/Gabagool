 Description : 
 One of the best Moroccan desserts around - full of sweetness, the  plantains  give you energy and are really easy to make.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 ripe  plantains 
 1 tablespoon  margarine  or  oil 
 1 tablespoon  Sugar 
 Directions : 
 Peel the ripe  plantains  and cut them into rough pieces, not too small.
 Heat the  oil  on a medium heat and, when hot, add the  plantain  slices and cook for 5 minutes. They should be lightly browned and caramelized. 
 Add the  Sugar  to the slices and allow the  Sugar  to caramelize. When it has done this, remove the  plantains  from the pan.
 Give the  plantains  a few minutes to cool down. Serve warm.
