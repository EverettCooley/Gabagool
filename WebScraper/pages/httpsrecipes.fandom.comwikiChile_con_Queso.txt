Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Original recipe 
 Makes 8 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 4 ounces (120 g) reduced-fat  processed cheese , cut into 1-inch (2.5 cm) cubes 
 ⅓ cup (24 g) store-bought chunky  salsa 
 1 x 16-ounce (480 g) can fat-free  refried beans 
 ¼ cup (34 g) well-drained canned chopped  green chiles 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients and put in a microwave-safe dish. 
 Cook for 2 minutes. 
 Stir. 
 Continue to cook for another 4 minutes, stirring once, until cheese is melted and dip is warmed through. 
 Serve hot with fat-free or reduced-fat  tortilla chips . 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving (dip only): 
 
 102 calories (32% calories from fat) | 5 g protein | 3 g total fat (2.0 g saturated fat) | 10 g carbohydrate | 3 g dietary fiber | 10 mg cholesterol | 530 mg sodium
