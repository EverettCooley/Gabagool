 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup quick cook  Oats , uncooked 
 3 tbsp skimmed  milk 
 1 1/2 lbs ground  Beef 
 1/2 cup shredded  carrots 
 1/4 cup sliced  green onions 
 1/4 tsp  salt 
 1/4 tsp rubbed  sage 
 1/4 tsp  pepper 
 1 x  egg  white 
 1/4 cup catsup 
 Directions : 
 Combine  Oats  and  milk  in a large bowl; let stand 10 minutes. Add remaining ingredients and mix well. Shape into a loaf 8 x 5 x 2 inches. Place on a rack, coated with  cooking spray ; place in a shallow baking dish. Brush with catsup and lay two or three  Bacon  strips on top. Bake at 350 deg f. for 1 hour or until cooked through
