Ingredients : 
 1 pint fresh  raspberries 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Rinse  raspberries , in four batches, in cool running  water  and drain. 
 Lay  berries  into a bowl lined with a dry paper towel, and shake bowl gently to blot up excess  water . 
 Place one layer of  berries  into bowl and cover with ¼ of the  sugar . 
 Repeat process till all  berries  are moistened and sugared. 
 Refrigerate. 
 Berries  will keep, without molding, for 4 – 5 days.
