Ingredients : 
 1  pizza crust 
 Barbeque sauce  (enough to spread over the crust) 
 2 tbsps extra virgin olive oil 
 crushed  red pepper  (to taste) 
 1 whole  red onion 
 1 lb of  chicken breast , sliced into strips 
 1-1/2 cups shredded  monterey jack ,  cheddar,  and  provolone 
 Spice rub for chicken 
 Amounts depend on personal preference 
 salt 
 pepper 
 oregano 
 parsley 
 Directions : 
 Prepare crust 
 Add 2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil to the sauce, along with some crushed red pepper and spread over the crust 
 Slice up a whole red onion into rings add on top of the sauce. 
 Roll the strips of chicken in a dry rub of salt, pepper, oregano, and parsley. 
 Add on top of the onions. 
 Top off with shredded cheese mixture. 
 Bake at 375 °F, for approximately 20 minutes or desired melty goodness, enjoy.
