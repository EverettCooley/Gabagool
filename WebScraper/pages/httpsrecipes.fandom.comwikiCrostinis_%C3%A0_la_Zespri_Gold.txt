Description : 
 Preparation time: about 10 minutes.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 Zespri Gold®  kiwis 
 1 small  papaya 
 50 grams  lychees 
 2 tsp  lemon juice 
 3 tbsp  maple syrup 
 1  ciabatta 
 about 100 g buttermilk  cottage cheese 
 Directions : 
 Peel the  papaya  and  lychees , removing the kernels; peel the Zespri Gold® kiwi and cut all into very small cubes. 
 Mix the fruit with the  lemon juice  and  maple syrup . 
 Slice the  ciabatta , spread with the  cottage cheese  and arrange the fruit mixture on it, then serve garnished with Zespri Gold® kiwi slices as desired.
