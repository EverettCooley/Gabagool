Description : 
 Toddler Snack - 12-24 months 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  all purpose flour 
 2 cups  light brown sugar 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 1 tsp ground  cinnamon 
 4 oz  cream cheese , softened 
 1 med ripe  California avocado , seeded, peeled and mashed 
 ½ cup  vegetable oil 
 ⅓ cup  milk 
 2  eggs 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 1 cup grated  carrot 
 1 cup  raisins  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 In medium bowl, combine  flour ,  brown sugar  and  cinnamon ; set aside. 
 In large mixer bowl beat  cream cheese  and  avocado  until smooth. 
 Add  oil ,  milk ,  eggs  and  vanilla , beating until smooth. 
 Add dry ingredients, mixing until blended. 
 Stir in  carrots , and  raisins  if desired. 
 Pour into lightly greased and floured 9x11-inch  baking dish . 
 Bake in preheated oven. 
 Set at 350°F for 40 minutes.
