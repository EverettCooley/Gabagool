 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 ripe  breadfruit 
 1 bunch seasoning 
 1 blade  celery 
 1 red & 1 green  sweet pepper 
 4 tbsp. Grated  Cheese 
 1 tablespoon  margarine 
 1 ½ tsp.  salt 
 1 large onion  
 1 oz chives  
 1/2 cup evap. milk  
 Directions : 
 Cut  breadfruit  into quarters. Half cook  breadfruit  in slated  water . Remove from  water  and cut away flesh from skin, taking care not to damage the skins. Cut out and discard centres. Put flesh back in the pot and cook for 5 minutes. 
 Wash and chop seasoning. Lightly saute in  butter  or  margarine . Crush  breadfruit , add seasoning and return to skins. Sprinkle on grated  Cheese . 
 Bake until brown spots appear. Remove from oven and serve hot.
