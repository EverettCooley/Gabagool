These are yummy, they are oatmeal cookies with an apple filling and half dipped in chocolate, for a prettier presentation dip half the cookies in milk chocolate and the other half of the cookies in white chocolate, I did.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Makes 8 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 8 oz pkg  cream cheese , softened 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 2 tbsp  apple juice 
 2 tbsp  apple jelly 
 ¼ tsp ground  cinnamon 
 2 pkgs (8.6 oz each) soft baked oatmeal cookies 
 6 oz  milk chocolate 
 2 tsp  vegetable oil 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl, combine cream cheese, sugar, juice, jelly and cinnamon. 
 Beat until smooth. 
 Spread 2½ tbsp mixture over flat side of one cookie. 
 Sandwich with flat side of second cookie. 
 Repeat with all the cookies and filling, for a total of 8 sandwiches. 
 Place on wax paper lined baking sheet and freeze 3 hours or until firm. 
 Combine chocolate and oil in a microwave bowl. 
 Microwave on high 1 minute. 
 Stir until smooth, returning to microwave if needed. 
 Remove sandwiches from freezer. 
 Half dip in chocolate. 
 Return to sheet, return sheet to freezer. 
 Freeze 15 minutes. 
 Serve cold with a cold glass of milk.
