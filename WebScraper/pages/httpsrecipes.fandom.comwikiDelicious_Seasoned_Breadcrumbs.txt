 Ingredients : 
 bread 
 1 tbsp  parsley  flakes 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  oregano 
 ¼ tsp  basil 
 ¼ tsp  onion powder 
 3 tbsp grated  Parmesan cheese  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Heat bread in a 300°F oven until dry and crisp. 
 Crush into crumbs in a blender or with rolling pin. 
 For each cup of crumbs, add 1 tbsp parsley flakes, ¼ tsp each salt, oregano, basil and onion powder. 
 For hearty Italian flavor, add 3 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese. 
 Substitute garlic powder for onion powder for variety. 
 Mix well. 
 Store in a tightly covered container. 
 Cheese mixture must be stored in refrigerator. 
 Shake well or stir thoroughly before using.
