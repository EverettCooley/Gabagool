 Description : 
 A quick and easy salad native to Morocco. Perfect if you have a busy lifestyle but want to eat healthily.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3  blood oranges , peeled 
 3  oranges , peeled 
 1/2 cup thinly sliced  green onion 
 1/4 cup imported italian  green olives 
 2 tablespoons  wine vinegar 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 1/2 teaspoon dried  oregano 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  cumin 
 lettuce  leaves 
 2 tablespoons snipped fresh  mint 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix the  blood oranges ,  oranges ,  green onion , and in a dish. The  oranges  can be diced but are traditionally split into segments. Add the  vinegar ,  olive oil ,  oregano .  cumin ,  salt  and  pepper .
 Toss the salad gently and leave it to marinate for 45 minutes. It should be covered and at room temperature.
 Use the  lettuce  leaves and the  mint  to serve.
 This recipe serves 6.
