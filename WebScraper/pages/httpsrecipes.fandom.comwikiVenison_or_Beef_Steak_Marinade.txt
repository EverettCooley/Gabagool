DH makes this and I copied his recipe as close as I can since he won't tell me what is in it. I use this all the time for venison steaks and chops and sometimes for beef but we don't eat that as often. by Tara
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 For 2 – 3  steaks 
 5 min 5 min prep 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  water 
 1 tablespoon  steak sauce  (HP or A1) 
 1 teaspoon  Worcestershire sauce 
 3 tablespoons  soy sauce 
 1 tablespoon  oil 
 1 teaspoon  vinegar  (white or cider) 
 ½ teaspoon  oregano 
 1 pinch  dry mustard 
 1 teaspoon  onion powder 
 1 – 2 clove minced  garlic 
 1 teaspoon fresh ground  black pepper 
 1 dash  chili peppers  (optional) 
 3 – 5 fresh  basil  leaves, if i have them (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Mix all together and pour over your steaks. 
 Marinate covered in the fridge for minimum of 4 hours or overnight. 
 Broil or cook on the grill.
