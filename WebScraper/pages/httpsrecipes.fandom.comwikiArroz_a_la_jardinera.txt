 Description : 
 Arroz a la jardinera
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 medium sized  Onion , peeled and diced 
 1 large red pepper, diced 
 1/4 teaspoon crushed  garlic 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 1 teaspoon anniato  oil  ( Achiote ) 
 3 or 4  tomatoes , diced 
 1 handful of  string beans , diced 
 1 or 2  carrots , diced 
 2 cups diced  pumpkin 
 1Â lb uncooked  rice 
 3 cups  water 
 2 tablespoons cooking  oil 
 1/4 teaspoon  salt  or to taste 
 Directions : 
 Saute the  Onion ,  pepper  and  garlic  on the  olive oil . 
 Add the  water  and cooking  oil . Bring to a boil. 
 Add the remaining ingredients and simmer uncovered until the  water  evaporates. 
 Blend with a ladle, moving the  rice  from the bottom to the top and viceversa. 
 Cover and cook over low heat until the  rice  is tender. Do not overcook.
