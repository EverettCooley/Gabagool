 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pkt	(10-oz) frozen  Peas , cooked and drained 
 1/4 cup  water 
 1 1/2 tsp  chicken broth 
 1/4 tsp  ginger 
 1/2 tsp  Sugar 
 1 tbl  oil 
 1 can	(5-oz)  water chestnuts , sliced 
 1 can (4-oz)  Mushrooms , sliced 
 1 1/2	tsp  cornstarch 
 1 tbl Soy sauce 
 Directions : 
 Heat all ingredients except  cornstarch  and soy sauce. Stir in  cornstarch  and soy sauce until thick.
