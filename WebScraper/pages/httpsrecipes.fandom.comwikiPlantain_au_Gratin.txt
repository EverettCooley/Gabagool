Ingredients : 
 4 ripe  plantains 
 4 tablespoons  oil 
 a little bit of  white sauce 
 1 oz  butter 
 2 teaspoons  flour 
 a large glass of  milk 
 salt 
 pepper 
 3 oz grated  Swiss cheese 
 Directions : 
 Peel the  plantains ,cut them in two parts in lengthways ,then into slices . 
 In the frying pan ,fry them in small quantities with  oil . 
 Prepare the white sauce with the  butter  and the  flour  well mixed, the  milk , the  salt  and the  pepper . 
 Place in successive layers in a "gratin" dish the  plantain  and the white sauceÂ ; finish with the white sauce. 
 Powder with the grated  Swiss cheese .
