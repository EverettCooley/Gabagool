 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons cooking  oil 
 4 cloves crushed  garlic 
 1 teaspoon  ginger , cut in strips 
 Â½ cup white  onion , chopped 
 1 stewing  chicken, cut in to pieces 
 1 tablespoon  fish sauce 
 10 cups  rice   water 
 1 small  green papaya , sliced into small pieces 
 1 cup  sili leaves (chilli pepper leaves) 
 1 teaspoon  salt and pepper 
 Directions : 
 Fry the  garlic  in hot cooking  oil  until brown. 
 Add the  ginger  and chopped  onions . 
 Cook until soft. 
 Drop in pieces of  chicken . 
 Season with fish sauce. 
 Cover and let simmer 5 minutes. 
 Add the  rice   water  and simmer until  chicken  is tender. 
 Add the sliced  papaya  and cook until  papaya  is tender. 
 Just before removing from the fire, season with  salt  and pepper. 
 Add the leaves. 
 Serve hot.
