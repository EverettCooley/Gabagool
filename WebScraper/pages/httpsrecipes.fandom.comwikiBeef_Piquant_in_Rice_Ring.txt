Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 pounds tenderized round  Steak , cut into 1-inch strips 
 2 tablespoons  chili powder 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1/2 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 1 cup cooked  green peas 
 2 tablespoons chopped fresh  parsley 
 1/2 teaspoon  Onion  powder 
 1 1/2 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 1 8-ounce can  tomato  sauce 
 Directions : 
 Season  Beef  strips with  chili powder ,  salt  and  garlic powder ; let stand about 30 minutes. This allows for absorption of spice flavors. 
 Combine  rice ,  Peas ,  parsley  and  Onion  powder; toss lightly. 
 Pack into 1-1/2-quart ring mold. Cover and place mold in pan of hot  water  until serving time. 
 Cook  Steak  in  oil  in large skillet over medium heat about 10 minutes. 
 Add  tomato  sauce; cover and cook 10 minutes longer. 
 Unmold  rice  ring on serving platter; fill ring with meat mixture.
