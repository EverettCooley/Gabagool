Description : 
 This is great to serve as a dip with fresh veggies. Excellent dressing for green or mixed veggie salad 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2  avocados , pureed 
 1⅓ cups  sour cream  (lite is ok) 
 1½ teaspoons  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  chervil 
 1 dash  onion powder 
 1 dash  Tabasco sauce 
 4 teaspoons fresh  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Combine all the ingredients and  chill  for 30 minutes.
