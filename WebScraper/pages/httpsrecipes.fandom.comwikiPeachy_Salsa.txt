 Description : 
 Always check the ingredients to make sure the product is vegan.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/3 Cup(s) highly packed  cilantro 
 2 Cup(s) peeled and diced  peaches 
 2 Cup(s) diced  tomato 
 1 Teaspoon(s)  lemon juice 
 1 Pinch(s)  black pepper 
 1/4 Teaspoon(s)  salt 
 1/2 Teaspoon(s) crushed  garlic 
 1 Teaspoon(s)  olive oil 
 2 Teaspoon(s)  balsamic vinegar 
 1 Dash(s)  cayenne pepper 
 Directions : 
 Place all ingredients in a blender or a food processor & process for a few seconds.
 
 Serve immediately over hot,  fresh pasta  or refirgerate until ready to serve.
