Description : 
 Homemade sausage is easy to make, especially this recipe, as almost all the ingredients are found in the home pantry. Try making some homemade sausage and serve it with biscuits and cream gravy.
 
 Source:  Pork in Family Meals; A Guide for Consumers  1982, USDA Bulletin 160 
 Serves 6. 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ pounds  ground pork 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  pepper 
 1 teaspoon  sage 
 Steps : 
 Mix all ingredients thoroughly. 
 Form into 12 patties, about ½-inch thick. 
 Cook in heavy skillet until well browned. 
 Turn, cook on other side until browned and center is done. 
 Drain off fat.
