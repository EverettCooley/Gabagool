Description : 
 
 Green bean salad with tuna
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tins  tuna  fish 
 2  tomatoes , peeled and finely cut 
 1  onion , finely chopped 
 1 kg fresh  green beans 
 olive oil 
 vinegar 
 salt 
 pepper 
 dill , finely cut 
 Directions : 
 Boil the  green beans  and place them in a platter. 
 Place on top of them the  tomatoes , the  onions  and the dill. 
 Sprinkle with the desired portion of  olive oil  and  vinegar  and finely add  salt  and  pepper  for taste.
