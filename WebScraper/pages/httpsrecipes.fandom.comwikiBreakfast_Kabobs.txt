 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  water 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 2  Golden Delicious apples 
 1 pound reduce-fat kielbasa or breakfast sausages 
 1 bunch fresh  sage  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 400 °F. Lightly coat baking sheet with  vegetable oil   cooking spray . In small bowl, combine  water  and  lemon juice . Core  Apples  and cut each lengthwise into quarters; cut each quarter into 4 wedges. Soak slices in  lemon   water  1 minute; drain. 
 Cut kielbasa diagonally into 24 ½-inch-thick oval slices. On wooden or metal skewers, alternately thread 4 slices or  Apple  and 3 slices kielbasa, beginning and ending with  Apple . (Kielbasa should be threaded through skin side-not cut side.) 
 Roast kabobs, turning occasionally, 15 to 20 minutes or until  Apple  slices soften and kielbasa browns. Set on bed of  sage  leaves, if desired, and serve.
