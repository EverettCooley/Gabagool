Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound shelled raw  peanuts 
 ¼ cup  peanut oil 
 1 tablespoon  chili powder 
 ¾ teaspoon  paprika 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  red pepper 
 ½ teaspoon  onion powder 
 Directions : 
 Place peanuts in shallow roasting pan. 
 Pour peanut oil over peanuts then stir to coat. 
 Roast at 350°F for 35 minutes stirring twice. 
 Drain well. 
 Combine remaining ingredients then roll peanuts in spices coating well.
