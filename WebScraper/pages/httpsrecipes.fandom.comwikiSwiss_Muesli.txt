Description : 
 This is a great healthy breakfast that you can make in large quantities to last the week.
 
 Ingredients : 
 170 g / 6 oz  rolled oats 
 170 g / 6 oz  raisins  or  sultanas 
 110 g / 4 oz  dried apricots , roughly chopped 
 110 g / 4 oz  hazelnuts , split in half and roasted 
 350 ml / 12 fl oz fresh  apple juice 
 2  apples  ( Coxes  or Granny Smith apple|Granny Smiths) peeled and coarsely grated 
 1 small tub Greek or natural  yoghurt 
 4 ripe  apricots , roughly chopped 
 clear  honey  to serve 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl mix together the oats, raisins, apricots and hazelnuts. 
 Pour over the apple juice and leave for 10 â€“ 15 minutes to allow the oats and dried fruit to soak up the juice. 
 Divide the muesli between 6 glass bowls and top with the grated apple. 
 Spoon on a dollop of yoghurt and drizzle over some honey. 
 Serve immediately.
