Mississippi Mud
 
 1 Jigger  Southern Comfort 
 1 Jigger  coffee  Liqueur 
 2 Scoops  vanilla   Ice cream 
 
Blend  Ice cream ,  Southern Comfort , And  coffee  Liqueur Until Thick. Spoon Into Cocktail Glass. Garnish With  shaved chocolate . Serves 1Source: my Old Recipes
