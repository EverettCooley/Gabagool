Ingredients : 
 2 boneless  sirloin  strip steak, about 10 oz. ea. 
 8 tbsp.  butter 
 1 c. sliced  green onions , including tops 
 1 lb.  mushrooms , cleaned and trimmed, whole or sliced 
 1/2 c.  red wine 
 1/2 c. canned  beef broth  or  consommé 
 1/4 to 1/2 tsp.  salt 
 1/2 tsp. coarsely ground  black pepper 
 2 tbsp.  lemon juice 
 2 tbsp. minced  chives 
 Directions : 
 In large skillet, melt 2 tbsp butter. 
 Sear steak on one side for 2-3 minutes 
 Flip steak, reduce heat, cover, and cook to desired doneness 
 Remove steak to a warm platter and cover loosely with foil to keep warm 
 In same skillet, add butter, onions and mushrooms 
 Cook a few minutes until softened 
 Add remaining ingredients and scrape pan to bring up any bits of beef left by the steak 
 Bring to a boil, and simmer until broth reduces 
 Cut steak into strips and return to the skillet 
 Mix with sauce to coat before serving 
 Notes : 
 Serves 4 
 You may substitute canned button mushrooms for the fresh, if you wish. 
 You may substitute chopped shallots for the green onions
