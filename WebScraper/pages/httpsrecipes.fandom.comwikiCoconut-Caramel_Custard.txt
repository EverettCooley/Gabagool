 Description : 
 This recipe takes about 50 minutes to cook.
 
       Coconut-Caramel Custard     
 Ingredients : 
 Caramel
 
 1/4 	cup  Sugar 
 1/4 	cup hot  water 
 Custard
 
 1 	cup  coconut milk 
 1 	cup  milk 
 1/4 	cup  Sugar 
 4 	 eggs 
 1 	teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 Directions : 
 Prepare the caramel and the custard, combine them and cook them for 30 minutes.
