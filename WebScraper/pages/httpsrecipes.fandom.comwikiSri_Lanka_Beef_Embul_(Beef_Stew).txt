 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 450	gm	 Beef 
 25	gm	 tamarind 
 75	ml	- water 
 1/2	tsp	 chile powder 
 1/2	tsp	 pepper , White 
 1	tsp	 salt 
 1/2	tsp	 turmeric 
 2	x	cg  cinnamon  stick 
 2	x	cg  lemon grass 
 Directions : 
 Wash and cut  Beef  into 2Â cm cubes. Mix  tamarind  with  water  and remove seeds. Place  tamarind water  in a pan, add  chile powder ,  pepper  powder,  salt ,  turmeric ,  cinnamon  stick and  lemon grass  and mix well. Add  Beef  cubes and simmer over a medium heat until the meat is tender and the gravy has thickened. Remove the  cinnamon  stick before serving.
