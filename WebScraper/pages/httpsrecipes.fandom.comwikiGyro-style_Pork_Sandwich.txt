Description : 
 The recommended wines are: Fumé Blanc, Beaujolais, or Chianti. 
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Original recipe 
 Prep: 15 minutes | Marinate: 1 hour | Cook: 10 minutes 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  olive oil 
 1 tbsp  prepared mustard 
 ½ cup  lemon juice 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1 tsp dried  oregano 
 1 lb boneless  pork loin , cut crosswise into thin slices, then into 5 x ½ inch strips 
 1 cup plain low-fat  yogurt 
 1  cucumber , peeled and chopped 
 ½ tsp  garlic , crushed 
 ½ tsp  dill 
 2  pita  loaves, halved 
 1 small  red onion , peeled and thinly sliced 
 Directions : 
 Combine first 5 ingredients in a bowl. 
 Place pork slices in a shallow dish and pour marinade over. 
 Cover and marinate in refrigerator 1-8 hours. 
 Combine yogurt, cucumber, crushed garlic and dill in another bowl. 
 Cover and refrigerate. 
 Preheat oven to 450°F. 
 Drain marinade from pork slices and place pork in a single layer in a shallow pan. 
 Roast about 10 minutes, until crisp. 
 Open each pita half to form a pocket. 
 Distribute pork among each half. 
 Top each sandwich with some chilled yogurt mixture and sliced onions.
