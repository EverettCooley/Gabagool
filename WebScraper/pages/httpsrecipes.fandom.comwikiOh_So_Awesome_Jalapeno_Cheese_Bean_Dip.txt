Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  salad oil 
 15 ounce can  refried beans 
 1 teaspoon  water 
 1 tablespoon diced  onion 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  garlic salt 
 ½ cup shredded  cheese 
 2 minced  jalapenos 
 Directions : 
 In a saucepan heat  oil  then add  beans  and  water . 
 Stir in  onions ,  salt  and  garlic salt  then add  cheese  and allow to melt then sprinkle with chilies. 
 Heat until  beans  are slightly thickened then place in dip bowl and serve with tortilla chips.
