 Description : 
 Calabasitas  is a vegetable dish made quickly and easily in the microwave.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2  green peppers , cut in thin strips 
 4 small  zucchini , thinly sliced 
 ½ Pound fresh  Mushrooms , sliced 
 1 Tbsp  butter  or  margarine 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 1 Cup shredded  Swiss cheese 
 4  green onions , chopped finely (including tops) 
 2 Cups shredded  Swiss cheese 
 1 firm  tomato , peeled and diced 
 1 tsp  chili powder 
 2 ripe  California avocados , peeled, pitted and diced 
 Directions : 
 Combine  peppers ,  zucchini ,  Mushrooms  and  butter  in 3 quart microwave safe casserole dish. Place in microwave oven and cook on High for 5 – 7 minutes, covered with wax paper. Vegetables should be lightly cooked, but still crisp. Add  salt  to vegetables and toss to mix well.
 In a separate bowl, toss  onions  with 2 of the cups of  Swiss cheese ,  tomato ,  chili powder  and  California avocados . Add this mixture to the hot vegetables and toss to mix well.
 Sprinkle with 1 cup of shredded  Swiss cheese  just before serving. 
 Serve Calabasitas as a first course warm salad, or if desired as an entree over  rice , or as a taco filling.
