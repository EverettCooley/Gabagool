Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Yield: 72 Servings (6 Dozen) 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  butter ; softened 
 ¾ cup  sugar 
 2 cups  Bisquick  baking mix 
 ½ cup nuts; chopped 
 ½ tsp  almond extract 
 confectioners' sugar 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 375°F 
 Cream  butter  and  sugar . 
 Stir in baking mix,nuts and  almond extract . 
 Shape dough by level teaspoonfuls into balls. 
 Place on ungreased baking sheet. 
 Bake 10-12 minutes. 
 Cool slightly before removing from baking sheet. 
 While warm, roll each ball in  confectioners' sugar . 
 Enjoy.
