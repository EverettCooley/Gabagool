Ingredients : 
 ½ cup natural  peanut butter 
 ½ cup vegan  margarine 
 2-3 cups  icing sugar 
 dash of  sea salt 
 2-3 tbsp  soy milk 
 Directions : 
 Place all ingredients in a bowl and beat until smooth. 
 Chill for a half an hour before icing the cake (and make sure you cake is completely cooled). 
 Should make enough for two round 8” or 9” layers or one 9”x13” sheet cake.
