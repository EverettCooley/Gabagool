 Ingredients : 
 24 x ¼ inch (0.5 cm) French bread  baguette  slices 
 6 tbsp melted  butter 
 4 oz (140g) chopped  pecans 
 1 tbsp  peanut oil 
 salt  to taste 
 4 oz (140g)  cream cheese , room temperature 
 1  apple , cut into 24 slices 
 8 oz (280g) chilled  brie  cheese trimmed, sliced into 24 pieces 
 24  pecan  halves 
 Directions : 
 Place baguette slices on cookie sheet and brush with melted butter. 
 Bake 8 minutes or until golden brown in 400 °F (250 °C) oven remove and allow to cool. 
 Blend pecans and oil in processor or blender to smooth paste. Season with salt. 
 Spread cream cheese on croutons, top with apple slices then, brie slices. 
 Spread pecan butter over top of each one. 
 Top with pecan half. 
 Place back on cookie sheet and Bake until cheese melts slightly or for 1 minute.
