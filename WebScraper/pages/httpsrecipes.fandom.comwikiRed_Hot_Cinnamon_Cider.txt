Description : 
 Makes 8 (½ cup) servings. 
 Recipe from Pillsbury's Children's Choice Cookbook. 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups  apple juice  or  cider  (1 qt.) 
 ¼ cup  cinnamon  red hot candies 
 Directions : 
 In medium saucepan, combine juice or cider and candies; bring to a boil. 
 Reduce heat and simmer 4 minutes or until candies dissolve. 
 Serve hot or cold. 
 If desired, garnish with  apple  wedge or  cinnamon  stick stirrer.
