Description : 
 Contributed by Lil at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Easy Everyday Cooking 
 4 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 (8 oz) skinless, boneless  chicken breasts 
 ⅓ cup  honey 
 ¼ cup  lemon juice 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 4  kaiser rolls 
 4  lettuce  leaves 
 4  tomato  slices 
 Honey sauce : 
 4 tbsp  honey 
 4 tbsp  mayonnaise 
 Directions : 
 Heat grill. 
 Cut  chicken  breasts in ½; pound to ¼" thickness. 
 Mix  honey ,  lemon juice  and  garlic  in shallow baking dish. 
 Reserve about ⅓ of marinade. 
 Add  chicken  to baking dish; turn to coat. 
 Discard marinade in dish. 
 Grill  chicken , basting with reserved marinade, 5 minutes per side. 
 Honey sauce : 
 Blend  honey  and  mayonnaise  in small bowl. Combine well. 
 Split rolls into halves. 
 Top 4 bottom halves with  lettuce ,  tomato  and  chicken . 
 Drizzle  honey  sauce over  chicken ; place tops on  chicken . 
 Serve immediately. 
 Notes : 
 For zestier flavor, add 1 – 2 tbsp brown or  Dijon mustard  to  honey  sauce. Double or triple  honey  sauce to keep on hand. It's delicious on  ham  or  turkey  sandwiches or as a dipping sauce.
