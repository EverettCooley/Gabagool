This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Bailey Estate in Mesquite, Texas in 1989.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 16 ounces  cream cheese  softened 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 4 ounce package  buttermilk -style  salad dressing 
 ¼ cup chopped  green onions 
 10 slices  bacon  cooked and crumbled 
 Directions : 
 Beat  cream cheese  until smooth then blend in  mayonnaise  and stir in salad dressing mix, chopped  green onions  and crumbled  bacon . 
 Cover and chill at least 3 hours. 
 Shape into a ball and serve with crackers.
