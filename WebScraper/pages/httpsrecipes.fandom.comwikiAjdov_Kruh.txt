Description : 
 Buckwheat bread
 
 Ingredients : 
 15 dag  wheat flour 
 1 cooked  potato , mushed (optional) 
 50 dag  buckwheat flour 
 1  cake yeast , melted in lukewarm  water 
 salt  to taste 
 4 dl lukewarm  water 
 Directions : 
 Mix both flours, then make a hole in the center and pour in  yeast  dissolved in lukewarm  water . 
 Cover with  flour  and let it rise. 
 Make dough with salted lukewarm  water . 
 Dough should slowly rise. 
 If you add mashed  potato , bread will be more succulent. 
 Knead and make a loaf. 
 Sprinkle with  flour  and wait until it rise again. 
 Bake.
