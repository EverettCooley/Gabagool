Description : 
 In Romanian: Blinele de cartofi
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb / 500 g  potatoes 
 ½ cup  flour 
 1 oz / 25 g  yeast 
 2 tablespoons  oil   or   lard 
 ¼ cup  milk 
 1 tablespoon  sugar 
 salt 
 sour cream 
 Directions : 
 Peel and wash the  potatoes . 
 Grate small. 
 Place in a bowl, add  sugar ,  salt ,  yeast  mixed with  milk  and  flour . 
 Mix everything well, cover and place in a warm place. 
 After it has risen, take tablespoonfuls and fry on both sides in hot  lard  or  oil . 
 Serve hot with  sour cream .
