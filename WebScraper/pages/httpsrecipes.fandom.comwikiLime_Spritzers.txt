Lime Spritzers
 Cut half a lime into thin slices. Juice the other half and pour into a cocktail shaker. Add 2 t. of sugar and 2 slices of lime and mash gently. Add 3 oz. of white rum and 1 cup of ice and shake. Pour the mixture into 2 glasses filled with ice and the remaining lime slices. Top off with club soda and serve.
Serves 2
