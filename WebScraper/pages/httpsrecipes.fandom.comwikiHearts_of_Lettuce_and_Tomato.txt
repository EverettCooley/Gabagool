Hearts of  lettuce  And  tomato 
 
 1 Lb. Firm Head  iceberg lettuce 
 3  tomatoes 
 3/4 Cup  olive oil 
 1/4 Cup  tarragon vinegar 
 1 T. Dried  basil , Crumbled 
 1/2 T. sugar 
 1/2 T.  salt 
 1/4 T.  pepper 
 
Remove Coarse Outer Leaves And Core All Firm  head lettuce . Wash And Drain Well. Wrap in Paper Towels And Chill Until Ready to Use. Cut  lettuce  Into 6 Wedges. Cut Each  tomato  in 6 Wedges. on Individual Salad Plates, Arrange 1  lettuce  Wedge And 3  tomato  Wedges. Mix  oil ,  vinegar , Dried  basil ,  sugar ,  salt  And  pepper . Pour Over Salad And Serve.
 Serves 6
