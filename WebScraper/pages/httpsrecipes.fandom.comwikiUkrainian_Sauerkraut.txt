      Ukrainian Sauerkraut     
 Ingredients : 
 1 large head of  white cabbage 
 2 large  carrots 
 ½ cup  salt 
 cranberries ,  raisins ,  caraway seeds  - optional 
 Directions : 
 Shred  cabbage  finely, grate  carrots . 
 Mix all the ingredients together and squeeze a little on the table. 
 Put in a wood container and leave at room temperature. 
 After 2 – 3 days the  cabbage  begins to "bubble" - at this point it must be refrigerated. 
 It will be ready to eat in two days after that.
