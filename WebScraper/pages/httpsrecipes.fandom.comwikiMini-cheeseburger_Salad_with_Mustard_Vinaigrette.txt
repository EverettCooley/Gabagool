Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  turkey ,  beef  or  pork, ground 
 ½ cup  onion , finely chopped 
 1 tbsp  Worcestershire 
 2 tsp  seasoning  of choice 
 8 oz  cheddar , sharp, garlic, herb, horseradish 
 1 cup  pickles , drained 
 3 tbsp  Dijon mustard 
 4 tbsp  cider vinegar 
 ⅔ cup  olive oil 
 Directions : 
 Combine meat, onion, Worcestershire, seasoning. 
 Form in mini patties and drizzle with olive oil. 
 Grill or sauté till almost done 
 Add slices of cheese and broil till melted. 
 Arrange salad greens on platter. 
 Toss with dressing and top with mini burgers and pickles and drizzle mustard over all. 
 Enjoy.
