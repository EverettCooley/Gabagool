fig   bread 
 
 1/2 c  figs 
 1/2 c  Raisins 
 2 tb  shortening 
 1 c  Honey 
 1 ea  egg 
 2 1/2 c  flour 
 1/2 ts  salt 
 1 ts  baking powder 
 1/2 ts  baking soda 
 3/4 c Sweet  milk 
 1/4 c  sour milk 
 1 c Nuts 
 
Cut  Raisins  And  figs  in Small Pieces. Cream  shortening  And HoneyWell, Add Beaten  egg . Sift Dry Ingredients Together. Reserve a LittleTo  flour  Fruits And Nuts. Add Alternately With  milk , Add Nuts AndFruit. Bake in Moderate Oven 325 F. 1 Hour And 10 Minutes.
 
Enjoy.
