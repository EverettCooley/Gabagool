 Ingredients : 
 1 cup white  grape juice 
 1 x 24-oz. pkg. frozen  mango  chunks 
 1  cinnamon  stick 
 1½ cups lite  silken tofu 
 1 Tbsp  egg replacer 
 1½ cups light brown  muscovado sugar 
 2 Tbsp  lime juice 
 1 tsp  ground coriander 
 2 Tbsp  cornstarch 
 1 tsp  rose water 
 1 tsp ground  nutmeg  for garnish 
 ½ cup shelled  pistachios  for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 375 °F. 
 Combine  grape juice , mangoes and  cinnamon  stick in a saucepan, and heat over medium heat, stirring frequently, for 5 minutes. Remove from heat, and set aside. 
 Put  silken tofu  in food processor, and blend until smooth, for about 30 seconds. Add  egg  replacer,  Sugar  and  lime juice , and process. Add coriander,  cornstarch  and  rose water . Pour into a 2-quart baking dish, and sprinkle generously with  nutmeg  and pistachios. 
 Bake, uncovered, on middle rack, for 30 minutes. Remove from oven, and set aside to cool. Cover, and refrigerate for several hours or overnight before serving.
