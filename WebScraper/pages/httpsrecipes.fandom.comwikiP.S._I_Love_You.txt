Ingredients : 
 1/2 shot Baileys® irish  cream 
 1/2 shot  Grand Marnier 
 Splash  crème de menthe  (clear) 
 Directions : 
 Mix the Baileys,  Grand Marnier , and single  cream  together with some  ice  in a shaker. 
 Shake thoroughly, and pour into a cocktail glass. 
 Pour a splash (about 1/3 shot) through the mixture to the bottmo of the glass. 
 Shake  nutmeg  over the top of the cocktail, and Bob's your Uncle.
