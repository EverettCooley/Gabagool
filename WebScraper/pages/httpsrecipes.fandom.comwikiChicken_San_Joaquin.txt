Description : 
 Makes 4 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups sliced  celery , including tops 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 2 large  apples , cored and chopped 
 2 cups cooked  chicken  cubes 
 1 x 10¾-ounce can condensed  cream of chicken  soup 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 ¼ teaspoon  poultry seasoning 
 1 cup  milk 
 1 cup (4 ounces) shredded  Cheddar  cheese 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Cook  celery  and  onion  in  butter  in large skillet over medium heat until tender crisp. 
 Stir in  apples ,  chicken  cubes, soup,  pepper ,  poultry seasoning  and  milk . 
 Cook over medium heat, stirring often, about 5 minutes. Sprinkle with cheese. 
 Serve  chicken  and sauce over hot  rice .
