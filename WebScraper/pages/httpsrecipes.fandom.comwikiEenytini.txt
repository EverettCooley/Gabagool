Ingredients : 
 1/2 oz Blue  curacao  liqueur 
 1/2 oz  gin 
 1 oz 7-Up® soda 
 1/2 oz  cranberry juice 
 Directions : 
 Mix all the ingredients into a cocktail glass. Garnish with a  cherry .
