Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Original recipe  submitted by: cfish3241 at dLife 
 Source:  Quick and Easy Low-Carb Cooking  by Nancy Hughes 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x 10-ounce frozen bag chopped  spinach , thaw and squeeze dry 
 ½ cup low-fat  cottage cheese  (If possible, use small curd cottage cheese) 
 1 cup  egg substitute 
 1 teaspoon dried  oregano  leaves 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅛ teaspoon  cayenne 
 1 x 14-ounce can  artichoke hearts , drain and chop 
 3 medium  yellow onions , chop 
 1 cup shredded reduced fat  cheddar  cheese 
 cooking spray 
 Directions : 
 Preheat the broiler. 
 Mix the spinach, cottage cheese, egg substitute, oregano, salt, and cayenne in a bowl. 
 Add in the artichokes. 
 Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. 
 Spray with cooking spray and cook the onions for 6 minutes, stir often. 
 Add in the artichoke mixture and decrease the heat to low. 
 Cover and cook for about 15 minutes. 
 Evenly spread the cheese on top and broil 1 to 2 minutes. 
 Remove from the broiler and let sit for 5 minutes before serving. 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Per serving:
 
 Calories: 134 | Carbohydrates: 12g | Protein: 14g | Total Fat: 5g | Saturated Fat: 2g | Cholesterol: 14mg | Fiber: 3g | Sodium: 654mg 
 Dietary Exchange: 2 vegetable | 1 medium fat meat
