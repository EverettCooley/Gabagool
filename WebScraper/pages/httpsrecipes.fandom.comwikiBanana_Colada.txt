Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz  light rum 
 ½  banana 
 1 scoop vanilla  ice cream 
 1 tsp crushed  pineapples 
 1½ oz pina colada mix 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in a blender except for the  ice  and blend. 
 Then add  ice  until the drink begins to thicken then serve.
