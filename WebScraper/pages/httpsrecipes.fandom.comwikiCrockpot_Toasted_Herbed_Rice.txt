Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source:  Southern Living  2007 Slow Cooked Sides 
 Prep: 7 minutes | Cook: 2 hr 
 Makes 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 3 tbsp  butter 
 1¾ cups uncooked  converted long-grained rice  (like Uncle Ben's) 
 2 (14 oz) cans  chicken broth 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 6  green onions , chopped 
 1 tsp dried  basil 
 ⅓ cup  pine nuts , toasted 
 garnish: fresh  basil  sprig 
 Directions : 
 Melt butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat; add rice, and saute 4 minutes or until golden brown. 
 Combine sauteed rice, broth, and next 3 ingredients in a 4-qt slow cooker. 
 Cover and cook on  high  2 hours or until liquid is absorbed and rice is tender. 
 Stir in pine nuts. 
 Garnish if desired.
