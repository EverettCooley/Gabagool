Ingredients : 
 1 banana 
 1/2 cup orange juice 
 1/2 frozen pineapple 
 1/4 cup plain greek yogurt 
 half a fresh mango 
 1 scoop Burt’s Bees™ Protein + Healthy Radiance – Vanilla 
 3 ice cubes 
 Instructions : 
 Add everything to the blender, blend until smooth.
