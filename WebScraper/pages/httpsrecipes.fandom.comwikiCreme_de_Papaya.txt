Description : 
 Papaya cream
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 ripe  papaya 
 1 scoop of vanilla  ice cream 
 1 tbsp  Crème de cassis 
 1 sprig of  mint  for decoration 
 Directions : 
 Peel papaya and remove seeds. 
 Cut into small pieces. 
 Place in blender with ice cream and crème de cassis. 
 Mix well until smooth. 
 Pour into a small bowl and garnish with mint.
