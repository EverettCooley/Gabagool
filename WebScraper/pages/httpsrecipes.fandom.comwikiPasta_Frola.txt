Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups of  flour 
 1 t  baking powder 
 150 grams of  butter 
 1 cup of  Sugar 
 one  egg 
 one  yolk 
 1 Tsp  milk 
 One can of  quince Â or sweet potato jam  or other fruit desired. 
 Directions : 
 Beat the  butter ,  Sugar  and  eggs . 
 Pour the  flour  and  baking powder . 
 Knead together and the cake mixture is ready. 
 Lay in in a baking pan, add (sweet potato jam), and cut strips of pastry. 
 And in a criss cross pattern, decorate the top of the cake. 
 Bake for 35 minutes in an oven set for 375 F
