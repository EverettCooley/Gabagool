Description : 
 Stir-fried sliced  pork  with  orange  and sesame
 
 Ingredients : 
 300 g  pork 
 1  orange 
 1 tbsp  white sesame 
 Sauce : 
 1 tbsp  light soy sauce 
 1 tbsp  orange wine 
 3 tbsp  orange juice 
 1 tbsp  orange rind jam 
 Seasoning : 
 1 tbsp  light soy sauce 
 Â½ tbsp  sugar 
 a little  pepper 
 1 tbsp  orange wine 
 Directions : 
 Clean and slice the  orange , onto dish. 
 Slice the  pork  into think pieces, marinate 15 minutes with seasoning. 
 Dust the  cornflour  fully around the sliced  pork . 
 Heat the wok with plenty of  oil , deeply fry the sliced  pork , dish up. 
 Heat the wok with few  oil , add deep fried  pork  and sauce, stir fry. 
 Dish up the fried  pork  onto the  orange , then dust the sesame on.
