Ingredients : 
 4 large  russet potatoes  with skin 
 1 cup  onion  sliced 
 1½ tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  garlic salt 
 2 tsp  paprika 
 2 tbsp  all purpose flour 
 2 tsp  sugar 
 4 tbsp  margarine 
 1 lbs  Swiss cheese , grated 
 Directions : 
 Peel  potatoes  and slice into ⅛ inch slices. 
 Layer a buttered casserole dish with ¼ of  potatoes  spread evenly in dish. 
 Sprinkle  potatoes  with ¼ of  onions . 
 Combine in a small bowl  salt ,  garlic salt ,  sugar ,  paprika , and  flour . 
 Blend well. 
 Sprinkle 2½ teaspoons of this mixture evenly over first layer. 
 Dot with 1 tbsp  butter  cut into pieces. 
 Continue the procedure for 3 more layers. 
 Pour  beer  over casserole and top with grated  cheese . 
 Bake at 350°F oven for 1 hour.
