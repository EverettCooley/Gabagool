 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅕ oz liqueur 
 ⅕ oz  grenadine  syrup 
 ⅕ oz blue  curaçao  liqueur 
 ⅕ oz  Amaretto   almond liqueur 
 ⅕ oz  Bailey's® Irish Cream 
 Directions : 
 Pour each ingredient slowly down the side of a shot glass in order, they will settle nicely layered. 
 Shoot.
