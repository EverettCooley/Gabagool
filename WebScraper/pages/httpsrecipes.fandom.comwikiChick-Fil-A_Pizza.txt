Ingredients : 
 for the crust: 
 ½ cup lukewarm water 
 ½ tsp active dry yeast 
 ¼ tsp granulated sugar 
 1½ heaping cups ap flour 
 ½ tsp salt 
 for the toppings: 
 12 pieces of Chick-Fil-A chicken nuggets 
 1 container (party size) of Chick-Fil-A sauce 
 1 order of medium french fries 
 ½ cup shredded mozzarella cheese 
 Instructions : 
 Preheat oven to 500 F. Spray baking spray on large baking sheet. Mix together yeast, water, & sugar & let sit for about 10 minutes until foamy. Add in remaining dough ingredients & mix until dough begins to form. Knead dough for 4 minutes & then roll out into shape of pizza. 
 Once dough is rolled out, spread Chick-Fil-A sauce onto dough leaving about 1 inch around the exterior for crust. 
 Top with nuggets, fries, & cheese. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until crust is golden brown & cheese is bubbling. Serve & enjoy!
