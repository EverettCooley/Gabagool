Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Irwin Estate in Jacksonville, Texas in 1987.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cans whole kernel  corn 
 1 cup  Swiss cheese  grated 
 1  egg white 
 5 ounces  evaporated milk 
 ½ teaspoon granulated  onion 
 ⅛ teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 1½ tablespoons  flour 
 ¼ cup dry  bread crumbs 
 1 tablespoon  unsalted butter 
 Directions : 
 Combine first 7 ingredients. 
 Combine crumbs and melted butter then sprinkle with paprika. 
 Combine corn mixture with flour mixture and bake at 350°F for 30 minutes.
