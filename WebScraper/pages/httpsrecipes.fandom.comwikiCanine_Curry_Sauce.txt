 Makes 1½ cups curry sauce. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  chicken stock  (see  directions  for preparation) 
 1 tablespoon  cornstarch  dissolved in 2 tablespoons  water 
 ½ cup plain  yogurt 
 1 teaspoon  cumin , ground 
 ½ teaspoon  coriander , ground 
 ½ teaspoon  turmeric 
 Directions : 
 Use a small pot and mix the chicken stock and the cornstarch in it. 
 Bring this to a boil over medium heat and cook, constantly stirring until the mixture thickens. 
 Take the pot off the heat and mix in the yogurt, cumin, coriander, and turmeric.
