Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Source:  1,001 Recipes For People With Diabetes  by Sue Spitler 
 Yield: 8 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅔ pkg (12oz size)  rice noodles 
 2 cups  snow peas , steamed 
 2 cups sliced  red bell pepper 
 2 medium  oranges , peeled, cut into segments 
 1 cup sliced  mushrooms 
 ½ cup fresh, or canned,  bean sprouts , rinsed and drained 
 ⅓ cup  orange juice 
 2 tbsp  olive oil 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 ½ tsp  five-spice powder 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Place noodles in large bowl; pour boiling water over to cover. 
 Let stand until noodles separate and are tender, about 10 minutes; drain. 
 Cool. 
 Combine noodles, snow peas, bell pepper, oranges, mushrooms, and bean sprouts in large bowl. 
 Combine orange juice and remaining ingredients; pour over noodle mixture and toss. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving (⅛ of recipe):
 
 198 Calories | 3.7g Fat | 0mg Cholesterol | 72mg Sodium | 5.9g Protein | 36.9g Carbs 
 Diabetic Exchanges: ½ vegetable | 2 bread/starch | ½ fat
