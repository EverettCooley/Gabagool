A tasty banana shake is a rich potassium source.
 
 Info 
 Cook Time : About 10 minutes 
 Serves : 1
 
 Ingredients 
 
 1 whole ripe  banana , sliced 
 1 cup  milk 
 vanilla  (few drops) 
 Directions 
 Measure into blender and blend at high speed until smooth
