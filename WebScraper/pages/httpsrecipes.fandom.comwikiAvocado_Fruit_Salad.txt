 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 A few fresh avocadoes 
 juice of one  lemon 
 chopped roasted  peanuts 
 Sugar  or  Honey 
 Directions : 
 Peel and remove seeds from the  avocado  and cut into bite-sized pieces. 
 Put in a glass bowl. Add the  lemon juice , some  Sugar   water  and stir gently. 
 Cover the  avocado  salad and allow it to stand for a half hour before serving or refrigerate after one hour. 
 Top with the chopped roasted  Peanut  and serve immediately.
