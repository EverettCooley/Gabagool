 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 kilo fish (washed with  salt  or  vinegar  or  lemon ) 
 3-4 green chilies 
 1 cup of fresh dhuniya leaves ( cilantro  /  coriander leaves ) 
 1 tsp  garlic  paste 
 salt  according to taste 
 1/4 tsp haldi powder (Tumeric) 
 2 tbs  tamarind water 
 2 tbs  yogurt 
 1 large  tomato 
 1 medium size  Onion 
 1 tsp  methi  ( fenugreek ) 
 Directions : 
 Grind all the spices together except  Onion  and  methi . Fry the  Onion  in  oil . 
 Add the grinded spices and  methi . Add the pieces of fish and leave on very low heat. Cover and cook till soft. Add a little  water  if necessary. 
 DO NOT COOK THE FISH FOR A LONG TIME OTHERWISE IT WILL BECOME HARD
