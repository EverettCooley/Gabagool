Description : 
 Milk food
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 liters  milk 
 300 ml  cake flour 
 5 ml  salt 
 15 ml  margarine  or  butter 
 cinnamon sugar 
 Directions : 
 Sift the  flour  and  salt  together and rub in  margarine / butter . 
 Bring  milk  to the boil. 
 Add  flour  mixture to  milk  and beat. 
 Lumps will form but keep stirring well!. 
 When lumpy, cover with lid and simmer for 5 â€“ 8 minutes, until cooked. 
 Serve hot with  cinnamon sugar .
