Ingredients : 
 1/2 teaspoon  butter 
 1 teaspoon  onion 
 2 large  egg 
 Splash of  milk 
 2 tablespoons red  enchilada sauce 
 Shredded  mozzarella  cheese 
 3 corn  tortillas 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Melt butter in small pan and saute the onion. 
 Meanwhile, beat eggs with milk (milk makes the egg fluffy) and red enchilada sauce. 
 Pour egg mixture into pan with sauteed onions 
 Add cheese (to your taste) and scramble together. 
 Warm tortillas in microwave. 
 Divide egg mixture into each warm tortilla and roll up.
