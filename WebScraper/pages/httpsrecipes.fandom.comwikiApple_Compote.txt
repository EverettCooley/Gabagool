 Description : 
 In Romanian: Compot de mere
 
 Ingredients : 
 5-6 tart  Apples , 
 10 oz/300 g  Sugar , 
 3 cups  water , 
 a little  cinnamon , 
 2-3 cloves 
 Directions : 
 Peel, seed and quarter the  Apples . 
 Let sit in cold  water . 
 Set the  water  with the  Sugar ,  cinnamon  and cloves to boil. 
 When it starts boiling, add the  Apples , cover and simmer until the  Apples  are soft. 
 Serve cold
