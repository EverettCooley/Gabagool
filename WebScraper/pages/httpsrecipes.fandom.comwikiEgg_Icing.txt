Description : 
 In Romanian: Glazura de ou
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 oz/250 g  sugar 
 2  egg whites 
 juice from 1  lemon  (or  orange ) 
 Directions : 
 Mix the  confectioner's sugar  with the  egg  whites and the juice from 1  lemon  or 1  orange . 
 Stir for almost 1 hour until thick. 
 Cover the cake with this paste.
