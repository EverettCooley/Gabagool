Description : 
 In Romanian: Jeleu de ciocolata
 
 Ingredients : 
 10 oz / 300 g  chocolate 
 10 tablespoons  milk 
 ½ qt / ½ l  sour cream 
 5 oz / 150 g  confectioner's sugar 
 vanilla 
 8 envelopes  gelatine 
 oil 
 Directions : 
 Put the warmed  milk ,  vanilla  and  chocolate  in a pot. 
 When the  chocolate  has melted;. 
 Pour everything over the  sugar  beaten with the  sour cream  and mix well. 
 Then add the dissolved and strained  gelatine . 
 Grease a mold with  oil , fill it with the mixture and refrigerate. 
 Before serving, briefly place the mold in hot  water  and turn onto a plate.
