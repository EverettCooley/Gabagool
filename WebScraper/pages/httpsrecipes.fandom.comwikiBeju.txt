 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 250g/8oz fresh  cassava 
 100g/4oz  caster sugar 
 250g./8oz  grated coconut 
 Directions : 
 Peel and grate the  cassava . 
 Squeeze out excess starch and moisture. 
 Mix in the  Sugar  and  grated coconut . 
 Bake in rings on a grease baking sheet at 275F/140C. 
 When set, turn over the biscuit and continue baking until firm and brown. 
 Cool and store in an air tight containers.
