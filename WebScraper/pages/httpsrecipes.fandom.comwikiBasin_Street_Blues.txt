Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz  bourbon 
 1 oz  triple sec 
 1 oz  lemon juice 
 1 – 2 drops blue  food coloring 
 ½ cup  ice 
 Directions : 
 Shake with cracked  ice . 
 Strain into cocktail glass.
