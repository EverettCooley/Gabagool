Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 5 c. sliced fresh or frozen  rhubarb 
 2 Tbls.  flour 
 1/2 c.  Raisins 
 3/4 c. packed  brown sugar 
 1 tsp. ground  cinnamon 
 1/4 tsp.  salt 
 1/2 c.  flour 
 1/3 c.  quick-cooking oats 
 1/3 c.  butter  or  margarine 
 vanilla   ice cream , optional 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rhubarb  & 2 Tbls. of  flour ; place in greased 8-in. square pan. 
 Sprinkle with  Raisins . 
 Combine  brown sugar ,  cinnamon , &  salt ; sprinkle over  Raisins . 
 Combine  Oats  & remaining  flour ; cut in  butter  until crumbly. 
 Sprinkle over top. 
 Bake at 375 deg. F for 40â€“45 minutes or until golden brown. 
 Serve warm with  ice cream  if desired.
