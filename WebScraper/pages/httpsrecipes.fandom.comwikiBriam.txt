Description : 
 Baked vegetables
 
 
 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 4 medium sized  courgettes 
 4 medium  aubergines 
 2  onions 
 4 – 6  potatoes 
 2  green peppers 
 4 – 6  tomatoes 
 parsley 
 olive oil 
 salt 
 pepper 
 Directions : 
 Clean and chop the  aubergines , the courgettes, the  peppers  and the  potatoes  in pieces. 
 Melt the  tomatoes  and chop the  onions . 
 Add all the above in a deep pan, add  salt ,  pepper ,  parsley  and  olive oil  and bake for about 1½ hours in medium oven (120°C).
