Description : 
 The key ingredients in this recipe are the cake mix, cream cheese, pudding, and bananas. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  yellow cake mix  prepared in a 13-x-9 pan 
 8 oz.  cream cheese , room temperature 
 1 pk. (3 ounces)  instant vanilla pudding 
 2 cups  milk 
 3 teaspoons  brown sugar 
 4  bananas 
 2 cups all-purpose  cream 
 1 cups chopped  nuts  (peanuts, almonds, or pistachios) 
 Directions : 
 Beat cream cheese until creamy. 
 Gradually add milk and brown sugar. 
 Add pudding, beating until well mixed. 
 Pour over cooled cake. 
 Slice the bananas over cake. 
 Cover with cream and top with nuts.
