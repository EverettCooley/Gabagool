 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 6  eggs 
 1 cup cooked  rice 
 ⅓ cup  milk 
 ½ cup sliced  green onions 
 ½ cup chopped  green pepper 
 1 tablespoon chopped fresh  parsley 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 ¾ cup grated  American cheese 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter  in large, heavy skillet, or chafing dish. 
 Beat  eggs  until frothy. 
 Add  rice ,  milk ,  onions ,  green pepper ,  parsley ,  salt  and  black pepper . 
 Pour mixture into skillet. Cook, stirring constantly, until  eggs  are of desired doneness. 
 Serve on hot plates topped with cheese.
