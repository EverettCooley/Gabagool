 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 ripe  breadfruit 
 8 oz. salted  Cod 
 1 lb salted  Pork 
 1  garlic  clove, 
 thyme , 
 parsley , 
 chives 
 3 green  lemons 
 3,6 oz rough  salt . 
 Cod  mix : 
 
 3 tablespoons  oil , 
 1 tablespoon  vinegar , 
 1  hot pepper , 
 salt , 
 pepper , 
 3  Shallots , 
 2  chives 
 Directions : 
 Use 100g rough  salt  to  salt  in successive layers the fat parts of the  Pork . 
 Sprinkle with a little  water , cover and keep in a cool place for 48 hours at least. 
 On the day of the " migan ", unsalt the  Pork  during a few hours, then cook it in one litre of  water  with the  thyme ,  parsley ,  chives , one  lemon juice  and a mashed  garlic  clove, during 45 minutes. 
 Peel the  breadfruit , remove the heart, cut it into small pieces and cook it into salted  water  with a minced  Onion  during about 40 minutes. When the cooking is over, mash the  breadfruit .
