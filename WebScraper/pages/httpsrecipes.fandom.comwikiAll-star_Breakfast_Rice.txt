Description : 
 1 serving
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 1 tablespoon  brown sugar 
 ⅔ cup  milk 
 1 cup cooked  rice 
 fresh  blueberries ,  raspberries  or  strawberries 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  milk ,  brown sugar  and  cinnamon  in small saucepan. 
 Cook over medium heat until thick and creamy, about 10 minutes. 
 Spoon into serving bowl, let cool 3 minutes. 
 Top with fresh  berries .
