Ingredients : 
 4 oz. Mr & Mrs® T Piña Colada Mix 
 1/2 oz. Rose’s® Cocktail Infusions Blue Raspberry Mix 
 1-1/2 oz. rum 
 3 pineapple chunks 
 1 cup ice 
 Optional: Pineapple wedge, pineapple leaf, raspberry, and strawberry for garnish 
 Instructions : 
 Add 1 cup of ice to a blender with 4 oz. of Mr & Mrs T Piña Colada Mix, 1/2 oz. of Rose’s Cocktail Infusions Blue Raspberry Mix, 3 pineapple chunks, and 1-1/2 oz. rum. Blend for 10 seconds or until smooth. Pour into pineapple-rimmed piña colada glass, and garnish with a pineapple wedge, pineapple leaf, raspberry, or strawberry, as desired.
 Makes 1 Serving
