 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  mashed potatoes 
 ½ chopped up  parsley 
 salt 
 pepper 
 1  egg yolk 
 1 tablespoon  non–salted butter 
 ½ teaspoon  paprika 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 200°C. 
 And grease baking dish. 
 Put the mashed  potatoes ,  parsley ,  salt  and  pepper  at desired taste in a mixing bowl. 
 Stir until all is mixed well. 
 Add in  egg yolk  and mix. 
 Shape dough into small balls and place these in the baking bowl. 
 Put a small amount of  butter  on the top of each  potato  ball, and dust lightly with  paprika . 
 Bake for about 10 minutes until lightly browned.
