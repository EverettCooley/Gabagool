Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Steep Estate in Elmo, Texas in 1989.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 14 ounces  artichokes , canned, water packed 
 3  eggs 
 8 ounces  Neufchatel  cheese softened 
 1 cup low fat  cottage cheese 
 ¾ cup  buttermilk 
 1 cup low-fat  white cheese  cubed 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  thyme 
 ¼ teaspoon  white pepper 
 ¼ teaspoon  Beau Monde seasoning 
 6 slices lean  bacon  cooked and crumbled 
 Directions : 
 Drain artichokes then cut in quarters. 
 Beat  eggs  then add cheeses and buttermilk then stir in seasonings and  bacon . 
 Place artichokes in bottom of pie shell and pour  cheese  mixture over artichokes then bake at 375°F for 1 hour.
