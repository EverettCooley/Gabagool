       Cream of Dried Pea Soup     
 In Romanian: Supa de mazare uscata (crema)
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 qts / 3 l  water 
 Â½ lb / 250 g dried  peas 
 1  parsley root 
 1  carrot 
 1  onion 
 1 teaspoon  sugar 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  oil 
 1 teaspoon chopped  dill 
 salt 
 cubed toasted  bread 
 Directions : 
 Pick over and wash the peas and pre-soak in tepid  water  the preceding night. 
 Then drain this  water  and set to boil in fresh tepid  water . 
 The last time, add, in addition to the peas, the  onion ,  carrot , and  parsley root . 
 Let boil, covered, at low temperature. 
 When the peas are tender, remove the  carrot  and  parsley root , strain, then sieve the peas with the  onion . 
 Thin with a little of the strained liquid. 
 Set the soup to boil again, with a tablespoon of  butter  or  oil  and  salt . 
 After coming to a boil, add the chopped  parsley . 
 Serve with croutons.
