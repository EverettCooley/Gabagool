Description : 
 In Romanian: Snitel de miel
 
 Ingredients : 
 leg of lamb  (in 6 pieces) 
 1  egg 
 2 tablespoons  milk 
 2 tablespoon  lard 
 1 tablespoon  oil 
 2 tablespoons  flour 
 bread crumbs 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Cut the leg into finger thick slices and flattened with the wooden hammer. 
 Salt and let sit for 10 â€“ 15 minutes. 
 Beat the  egg  and mix with  milk . 
 Dredge the meat with the  egg  mixture, then  flour , then  egg  again and finally breadcrumbs. 
 Fry in  lard  mixed with  oil  at low heat. 
 Serve with french fries and green  lettuce  or pickles.
