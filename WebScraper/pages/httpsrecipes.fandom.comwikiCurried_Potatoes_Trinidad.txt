 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 tsp  curry  powder 
 1 small  Onion , cut into small pieces 
 4 cloves  garlic , minced or crushed 
 2 medium sized  potatoes 
 Directions : 
 Use sharp knife to slice  potatoes  in wedges. 
 Use a large pan to heat the spices in the  oil  on medium heat but do not burn. Add  Onion  and  garlic  and cook 2 minutes. 
 Cook  potatoes  for 2 additional minutes. Add the  water  and cook  potatoes  until tender on medium heat.
