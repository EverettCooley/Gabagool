 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/4 Paneer cubed 
 1/2 cup  basmati rice 
 2 tbs.  Raisins 
 1/2 tsp. whole  coriander seeds 
 1 tbs. fresh grated  ginger 
 1/4 red  bell pepper  (optional) 
 1 tbs.  cilantro 
 1 tsp. coarsley ground  black pepper 
 1/4 tsp.  salt 
 Directions : 
 Rinse  rice  three or more times until  water  is almost clear. 
 Boil  water , add  rice  and  salt . 
 Simmer for 15-20 minutes. 
 Remove from heat let stand for 5 minutes to firm. 
 Saute  ginger  until golden 
 Add coriander and bell peppper 
 Place in dish fluff  rice  and add remaining ingredients. 
 Garnish with  cilantro  and serve.
