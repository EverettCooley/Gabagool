Ingredients : 
 6 ounces can frozen concentrated  limeade 
 6 ounces  tequila 
 6 ounces  beer 
 Directions : 
 Combine ingredients in blender. 
 Add a couple of ice cubes and blend briefly. 
 Allow to set for a few minutes. 
 Pour contents over ice in  salt -rimmed glass.
