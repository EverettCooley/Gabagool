Description : 
 This recipe taken from www.Portuguese-recipes.com
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup of  flour 
 ½ cup of  bran , any kind 
 1 tbsp of  sugar 
 2 tsp of  baking powder 
 ½ tsp of  baking soda 
 ½ tsp of ground  cinnamon 
 ¾ cup of  orange juice 
 2 tbsp of  vegetable oil 
 ½ cup of  pumpkin 
 1  egg 
 ⅓ cup of  dark  or  golden raisins 
 1 tbsp of  wheat germ 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 400°F. 
 Combine all of the above ingredients hold out the  wheat germ  for now. 
 Mix well in a bowl use a spoon no mixer here please. 
 Spoon into greased muffin tins. Sprinkle the tops with the  wheat germ . 
 Bake for 10 to 15 minutes. 
 Nutritional information : 
 1 muffin = 148 calories
 
 Exchanges: 1 bread, 1 fruit, 1 fat 
 25 g carbohydrate | 3 g protein | 4 g fat | 123 mg sodium | 189 mg potassium | 34 mg cholesterol
