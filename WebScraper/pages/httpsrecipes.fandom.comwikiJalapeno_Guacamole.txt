 Description : 
 Genuine Recipe! Extra hot, but you won't be able to resist.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 Fresh  California avocados 
 6 Jalapenos 
 Â½  lime 
 chile  Piquin  peppers  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Boil Jalapenos in a bowl of  water  for 10 minutes in the microwave.
 Drain.
 Using two forks, shred the Jalapenos.
 Stir together with  avocado  until well mixed.
 Optional: Add crushed  chile  piquin to taste.
 Squeeze juice of 1/2  lime  over top.
 Refrigerate overnight before serving.
