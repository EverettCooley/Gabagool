Description : 
 Pork  and  poultry  are good candidates for this marinade.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 From the Recipe Ross Newsletter 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  soy sauce 
 ¼ cup dry  sherry 
 2 tbsp  sesame oil 
 1 crushed  garlic  clove 
 1 tbsp grated  ginger  root 
 Directions : 
 Combine ingredients in a shallow dish.
