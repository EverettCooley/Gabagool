 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 cups sliced  carrots 
 1 large  Onion , diced 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 1/4 cup  heavy cream 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1/2 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1 1/2 cups Grated  Cheese  (Swiss, Munster  or  processed cheese  food) 
 1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 degrees F 
 Bring a large pot of salted  water  to a boil. 
 Add  carrots  and cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes. 
 Drain and place in a 2 quart casserole dish. Stir in  Onion ,  butter ,  cream ,  salt ,  pepper  and  Cheese ; mix well. 
 Sprinkle bread crumbs over the top. 
 Bake in preheated oven for 50 minutes
