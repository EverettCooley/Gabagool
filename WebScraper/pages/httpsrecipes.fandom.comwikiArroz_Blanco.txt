 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups of  rice 
 6 cups  water 
 5 tablespoons of  oil 
 1 teaspoon of  salt 
 Directions : 
 Heat up 3 spoons of  oil , add the  salt  and  water . 
 Bring to a boil and then add the  rice , stirring regularly to avoid excessive sticking. 
 When all the  water  has evaporated cover with a tight fitting lid and allow to simmer at very low heat. #Wait 15 minutes and remove lid, stir, add the remaining  oil  and cover again. 
 In 5 minutes the  rice  should be firm but tender inside. 
 If necessary, cover and leave another 5 minutes on very low heat.
