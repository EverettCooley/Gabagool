Description : 
 Contributed by  Pressurecookerrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  pork chops 
 2 tbsp  prepared mustard 
 Â½ cup  apple juice  (could use  chicken broth  if you wish instead of juice) 
 1 tsp  olive oil 
 1 medium  onion  sliced 
 2 medium  apples  sliced 
 Directions : 
 Spread both sides of each  pork  chop or the roast with  mustard . 
 Sprinkle with seasonings. 
 Heat  oil  in bottom of pan, and then brown the meat on both sides and also put in the  onion  slices. 
 When meat has browned, add the  apple  slices and juice (or broth). 
 Close lid and bring to pressure. 
 Cook for 10 minutes. 
 Release pressure according to your pressure cooker. 
 Optional: add 1 cup of  water  or  chicken broth .
