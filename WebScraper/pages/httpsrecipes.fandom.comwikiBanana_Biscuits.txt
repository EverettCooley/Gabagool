 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 small ripe  bananas , peeled and mashed 
 1 cup lite  soymilk  or other  milk  alternative 
 2 Tablespoons  oil 
 4 1/4 cups unbleached white  flour 
 1 Tablespoon  baking powder 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Mix the mashed  bananas ,  soymilk , and  oil  together in a large bowl. Add the  flour  and  baking powder  and stir well. 
 Place dough on a floured surface and knead for 3 minutes. 
 Using a rolling pin, roll dough to a 1/2-inch thickness. 
 Cut into 2-inch-wide circles using a cutter or tin can. 
 Place biscuits on a lightly oiled cookie sheet, bake 20 minutes or until browned. Serve warm.
