Vegetable Party Spread
 
 8 Oz.  cream cheese , Softened 
 1/3 C. Finely Grated  carrot 
 2 Tbsp. Finely Chopped  celery 
 2 Tbsp. Minced  Onion 
 2 Tbsp. Finely Chopped Green  bell pepper 
 2 Tbsp. Finely Chopped  cucumber 
 1 T.  lemon juice 
 1 T.  Mayonnaise 
 1/8 T.  salt 
 1/8 T.  white pepper 
 
 Beat All Ingredients at Medium Speed in Mixer Bowl Until Smooth. Chill, Covered, For 1 Hour. Serve at Room Temperature on  bread  or Crackers.
 
Makes 16 Servings
