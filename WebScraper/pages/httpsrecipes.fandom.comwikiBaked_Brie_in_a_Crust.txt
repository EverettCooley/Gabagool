Ingredients : 
 1 	 	Cressent rolls, 4 rolls 
 1 		Small round of  Brie 
 2 	tbsp 	 butter 
 Honey   mustard  or chopped  nuts  or  Apple  slices 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven according to direction on the back of the crescent rolls. 
 Dived dough into two rectangles rather than four triangles lay side by side and press edges together so you have a square. 
 Lay  Brie  on top of square and gather up edges so that you completely encase the  Cheese . 
 Turn over so the gathered part is on the bottom and place on a baking sheet. 
 Bake according to direction on the rolls. 
 If you are using the  nuts ; while the  Brie  is baking, toast the  nuts  in  butter  either in a fry pan or in the microwave. 
 Remove  Brie  from the oven and let set about 5 minutes. 
 Place  Brie  on serving tray and top with toasted  nuts , surround with slices of firm fruit such a as  Apples  and  Pears .
