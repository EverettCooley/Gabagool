Description : 
 Contributed by Jenn b aka Mom2sam and Tiny at  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup cooked, chopped  chicken 
 3 cups  chicken stock  (can use  chicken bouillon  and  water  here) 
 1 cup  coconut milk 
 chopped  onion , amount depends on your  onion  taste buds 
 2 tbsp gluten-free  margarine 
 2 – 3 tbsp  tapioca starch 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Sauté  onion  in  margarine , add  flour . 
 Stir until bubbly, add  chicken , cook for one minute. 
 Add broth and  milk , cook until hot throughout (don't boil) and serve. 
 Can add  garlic  and other spices such as  thyme  and  basil .
