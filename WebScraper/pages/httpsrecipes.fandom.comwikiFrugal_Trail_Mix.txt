Ingredients : 
 2 cups  granola  cereal 
 1 cup  peanuts 
 1 cup  dried apple  bits 
 1½ cups yogurt-covered  raisins 
 ⅔ cup chopped  dates 
 1 cup  dried banana  bits 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients and store in an airtight container. 
 A small serving of this goes a long way!
