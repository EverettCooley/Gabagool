Description : 
 This recipe taken from www.Portuguese-recipes.com
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb. of navy  white pea beans 
 ¾ to 1 lb. of  linguiça 
 ¼ lb. of  bacon  (cut up small) 
 16 oz. can of  tomatoes 
 2 large  onions 
 ½ teaspoon of  baking powder 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste. 
 Directions : 
 Soak  beans  overnight. 
 Pre boil them the next day. for around 30 minutes. 
 Chop  onions  and linguica into small pieces. Chop  tomatoes . After the  beans  are pre-boiled drain and save the  water . 
 Mix in large bowl the  beans , linguica,  onions ,  tomatoes ,  salt ,  pepper , and  bacon . 
 Save a couple of strips of  Bacon  for the top later on. 
 Put all this in a heavy metal pot or bean pot. Pour enough of the reserved bean  water  to cover the mixture. 
 Bake in a 350 °F oven, covered, for 1¾ hours covered and then ½ hour uncovered. 
 Add more bean  water  if it dries out.
