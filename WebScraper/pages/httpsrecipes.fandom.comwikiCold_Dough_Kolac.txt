 Ingredients : 
 8 cups  flour 
 12  egg yolks 
 1 tsp.  salt 
 2  cake yeast  (2 ounces) 
 1 1/2Â lbs.  shortening ( butter  or  margarine ) 
 1 pint  sour cream 
 Directions : 
 Sift  flour . Crumble in  yeast  and add  yolks ,  salt  and  sour cream . Knead until smooth. Put in refrigerator over night. Roll out on lightly floured and sugared board. Cut into squares, fill with any desired filling. Fold over twice, cut small slits along one side. Bake 30 mnutes in 325-F oven.
