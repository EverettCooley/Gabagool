Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  sweet vermouth 
 1½ oz  gin 
 juice of ½  limes 
 1 tsp  Maraschino liqueur 
 Directions : 
 Shake the ingredients with  ice , strain into a cocktail glass, and serve.
