Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 7/29/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  butter 
 2 tbsp  prepared horseradish mustard  (we use regular  yellow mustard ) 
 2 tbsp chopped  onion 
 1 tsp  poppy seeds  (optional) 
 1 tsp  dill seed  (optional) 
 8 slices  Swiss cheese 
 8 sliced cooked  ham  (I use deli ham) 
 8  hamburger buns 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 250°F. 
 Combine butter, mustard, onions, poppy seeds and dill seeds. 
 Spread insides of buns with this mixture. 
 Place a slice of cheese and a slice of ham inside each bun. 
 Wrap the buns in foil and place in preheated oven. 
 Bake 15 – 20 minutes until the cheese is melted.
