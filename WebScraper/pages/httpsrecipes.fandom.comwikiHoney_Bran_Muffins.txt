Description : 
 Bran Flakes: Fortified Ready to Eat Cereal  by the US Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Serves : 12 
 Ingredients : 
 1¾ cups  flour 
 1 tablespoon  baking powder 
 1½ cups  low-fat milk 
 ½ cup  honey 
 1  egg 
 3 tablespoons  oil 
 3 cups  bran flakes 
 ½ cup  raisins  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 400°F. 
 Mix together the flour and baking powder. Set aside. 
 In a large mixing bowl, beat together the milk, honey, egg, and oil. 
 Add to dry ingredients along with bran flakes and raisins (optional). Mix together  only  until moistened. 
 Portion into twelve muffin cups lightly coated with cooking spray or lined with muffin papers. 
 Bake 25 minutes until lightly browned. 
 Serve warm.
