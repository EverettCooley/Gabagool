Jiffy Cake  pineapple  Dessert
 
 1 Sm. White Jiffy Cake Mix 
 1 (3 Oz.) Pkg. Instant  vanilla  Pudding Mix 
 1 (3 Oz.) Pkg. Instant  coconut  Pudding Mix 
 1 (8 Oz.) Pkg.  cream cheese 
 3 C. Cold  milk 
 1 Can Crushed  pineapple  (drained) 
 1 Lg.  Cool Whip 
 Nuts And  cherries  (optional)
 
Mix The Cake Mix According to The Directions on TheBox And Bake it Until Done in 9 x 13 Greased Cake Pan.Cool Cake Completely. Beat Pudding,  cream cheese  AndMilk Until Well Mixed. Put This Layer on The CooledCake. Sprinkle The Crushed  pineapple  on The PuddingLayer. Frost With 1 Large Container of  Cool Whip .
 Sprinkle Nuts or  cherries  on Top. Refrigerate. Note: iUsed One Regular Yellow Cake Mix And Dessert Gets AsHigh as Pan.
 
Enjoy.
