Ingredients : 
 ¼ lb saltine  crackers 
 1 can  asparagus , cut 
 4½ oz  pimentos 
 1 can  mushroom  soup 
 1 cup  Cheddar  cheese, grated 
 3 hard-boiled  eggs , optional 
 Directions : 
 Casserole layer ingredients as follows:
 
 ½ crackers, crumbled ½  asparagus , ½  pimentos  (in strips), ½  mushroom  soup mixed with  pimento  juice, ½ grated  cheese . 
 Repeat the layers using other half of ingredients. 
 Top with  cheese  or crumbled  potato chips . 
 Heat in moderate oven (375 °F). 
 Until bubbly and topping has browned.
