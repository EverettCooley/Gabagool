 Ingredients : 
 1 	cup 	 mustard  powder 
 1/2 c  beer  or ale (flat or fresh) 
 1/4 	cup 	Cold  water 
 Directions : 
 Whisk the  mustard  together with the cold  water  in a medium sized bowl & set aside for 10 minutes. 
Stir in the  beer  & mix thoroughly. 
Serve immediately or store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to six months. 
This  mustard  makes an excellent addition to salad dressings.
