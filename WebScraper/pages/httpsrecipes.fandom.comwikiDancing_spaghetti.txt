Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cup  water 
 1 tbsp  baking soda 
 3 tbsp  vinegar 
 Long  spaghetti  noodles 
 Tall glass 
 Directions : 
 In the tall glass put the  water  and  baking soda  and stir. 
 Break the noodles into 1" pieces, set aside. 
 Stir in the  vinegar  and add the noodles last. 
 It"s also fun to add some  food coloring  to the  water .
