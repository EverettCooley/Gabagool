Vidalia  Onion  Pie
 
 8 Slices  Bacon 
 1 Large  Onion 
 3 T.  flour 
 2  eggs , Beaten 
 1 Cup  milk 
 salt  And  pepper 
 
 1/4 Cup Grated  Swiss cheese 
 1/4 Cup Grated  Parmesan cheese 
 1 Pie Shell, Unbaked 
 
Fry  Bacon  And Saut?  Onion  in  Bacon  Drippings. Crumble  Bacon  And Mix With Saut?ed  Onion . Sprinkle  flour  Over Mixture. Beat  eggs  And Add  milk . Stir in  Bacon  And  Onion . Season With  salt  And  pepper . Sprinkle Cheeses Over Bottom of Pie Shell. Cover With  milk  Mixture. Bake at 350 For 1 Hour. Serves 4 to 5
 Source: Mrs. Warren C. Grice
