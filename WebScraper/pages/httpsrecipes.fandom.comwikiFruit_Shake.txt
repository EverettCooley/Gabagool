Description : 
 Posted on Diabetic Board of nvn by Patricia (Trish) McKenna, ID pmckenna. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  non-fat milk  (liquid) 
 2 tbsp  instant non-fat milk powder 
 2 ripe  bananas ,  pears  or  peaches 
 ¼ tsp  cinnamon 
 1 tbsp  apple juice  concentrate 
 Directions : 
 Cut up fruit and remove seeds if any. 
 Place ingredients for desired drink in blender. 
 Blend 30 to 45 seconds.
