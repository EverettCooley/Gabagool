Ingredients : 
 1 c.  butter 
 2 c.  powdered sugar 
 9 tbsp.  unsweetened chocolate  or 3 (1 oz.) squares melted and cooled 
 5  egg yolks 
 1/2 tsp.  peppermint extract 
 2 tsp.  vanilla extract 
 1 c. vanilla wafer cookie crumbs 
 18 cupcake liners 
 whipped cream (optional) 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Beat butter and sugar. 
 Add the chocolate then egg yolks. Beat. 
 Add vanilla and peppermint flavor. Mix well. 
 Line bottom of cupcake holders with cookie crumbs. 
 Place large spoonful of mixture in each. 
 Place in freezer 45 minutes or 1 hour before serving. 
 May be topped with whipped cream. Enjoy.
