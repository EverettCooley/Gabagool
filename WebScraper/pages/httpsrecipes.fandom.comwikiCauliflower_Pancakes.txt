Description : 
 Broccoli may be substituted for cauliflower or a mix may be used. We have been making these since 1955 and love them. I hope you will too. Enjoy!
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 head cauliflower 
 1 egg or egg substitute 
 3 tbsp matzo meal 
 salt & pepper, to taste 
 1/3 cup oil (vegetable works best but olive or nut could be used) 
 Directions : 
 Boil cauliflower in unsalted water until just tender. 
 Mash and add remaining ingredients, except oil. 
 Drop from the tip of a large spoon into hot oil. 
 Brown on both sides. 
 
Serves 4
