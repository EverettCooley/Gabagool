Description : 
 This recipe was taken from Cathy's Menu and recipes for the week of 2/11/07
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 Pizza crust (store bought) 
 2 Cup cooked shredded  chicken  breast 
 1 Cup  honey  barbeque sauce, divided 
 2 Tsp  butter 
 1 Medium red or purple  onion , cut in half and sliced into thin pieces 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 2 Cup shredded  mozzarella   cheese 
 1 Cup shredded  Gouda   cheese 
 Directions : 
 Prepare crust according to package directions. 
 Heat oven to 450 °F. 
 Coat  chicken  pieces with 2 Tsp  butter  in sauté pan over medium heat. 
 Sauté  onions  and  garlic  in  butter , stirring occasionally, until  onions  are brown and caramelized (about 15 minutes). 
 Remove from heat and stir in  chicken . 
 Spread remaining Barbeque sauce over pizza dough. 
 Top with half of the cheeses. 
 Spread on  chicken  mixture. 
 Top with remaining cheeses. 
 Bake 15-18 minutes until crust is lightly browned and  cheese  completely melted. 
 Let pizza sit 5 minutes before slicing.
