Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves 6 
 Source: Mrs. William Cain 
 Ingredients : 
 6 boneless  chicken breasts 
 6 slices thin  ham 
 6 slices  Swiss cheese 
 12 strips  bacon 
 tarragon 
 parsley 
 Directions : 
 Pound chicken breasts until ¼ inch thick. 
 Top each piece with 1 slice of ham and 1 slice of cheese. 
 Fold chicken over ham and cheese. 
 Wrap each bundle with 2 strips of bacon. 
 Place in shallow baking dish and sprinkle with tarragon. 
 Bake in preheat oven at 350°F for 40 to 45 minute or until chicken is cooked and bacon is browned. 
 Garnish with parsley.
