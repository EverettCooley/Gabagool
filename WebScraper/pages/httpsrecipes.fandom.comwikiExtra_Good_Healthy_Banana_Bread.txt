Ingredients : 
 1  egg 
 ½ cup packed  light brown sugar 
 ½ cup  buttermilk 
 3 tbsp  applesauce 
 2 tbsp  water 
 ¾ cup  flour 
 ½ cup  whole wheat flour 
 ¾ cup ground  flaxseed 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 ⅛ tsp  salt 
 1 cup ripe  banana , pureed 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 In large bowl, lightly beat 1  egg . 
 To beaten  egg , add ingredients 2-5, and beat gently until smooth. 
 In medium bowl, combine  flour ,  wheat flour ,  baking powder ,  baking soda  and  salt . 
 Add dry ingredients to  egg  mixture. 
 Stir only until combined. 
 Mash ripe  banana  (the riper the better) in bowl with fork, till well pureed. 
 Add pureed  banana  to mix and stir, just until mixed. 
 Pour into greased 8x8 pan. 
 Bake until knife inserted in center comes out clean.
