Description : 
 A  Long Island Iced Tea  with Limoncello in it. Black Duck Cranberry Liqueur ® replaces Coca-Cola in the "Long Beach Half and Half" variation. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ oz  vodka 
 ½ oz  gin 
 ½ oz  light rum 
 ½ oz  triple sec 
 1 ounce Pat O'Brien's®  Sweet and Sour Mix 
 1 ounce  Coca-Cola  ® 
 4 ounces  Limoncello  Luxardo ® 
 Directions : 
 Fill 8 ounce highball glass with ice. 
 Add ingredients. 
 Stir well using standard bar spoon and serve.
