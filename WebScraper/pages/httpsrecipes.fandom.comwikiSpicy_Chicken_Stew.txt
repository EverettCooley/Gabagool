Description : 
 Contributed by  Pressurecookerrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 skinless, boneless  chicken  breasts (cut into pieces) 
 2 slices thick  bacon  (cut into pieces) 
 1 large  onion  (sliced up) 
 3 cloves of  garlic  (chopped) 
 1 can white  corn 
 1 can diced  tomatoes  (14.5 oz.) 
 3  carrots  (chopped) 
 1 cup  salsa 
 1 cup  chicken broth 
 1 cup  white wine 
 cumin 
 chili powder 
 cayenne  powder 
 1  bay leaf 
 olive oil 
 kosher salt 
 pepper 
 parsley  (dried) 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Brown  bacon ,  chicken ,  onion  and  garlic  in  olive oil . 
 Add  pepper  to taste. 
 Combine the following in electric pressure cooker:  corn ,  tomatoes ,  carrots , salsa, broth, wine and seasonings. 
 Suggest using about Â½ tsp of  pepper  and  parsley  and 2 tsp  salt . 
 Cook on high pressure for 8 minutes. 
 For a complete meal serve with  pasta  or alone.
