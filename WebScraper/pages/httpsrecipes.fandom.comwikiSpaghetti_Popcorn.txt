Ingredients : 
 3 c 
 popped popcorn
 
 3 Tbsp 
 butter or margarine, melted
 
 1 tsp 
 chili powder
 
 1 pkg 
 spaghetti sauce mix (i use lawry's)
 
 1 tsp 
 granulated sugar
 
 1/2 tsp 
 garlic salt
 
 1/4 tsp 
 cayenne pepper
 
 How to Make Spaghetti Popcorn  : 
 Step-by-Step 
 
 In a small glass jar, combine all ingredients , except the popped corn and the butter. Place the dry mixture in tight fitting jar. 
 Melt margarine or butter in a small microwave measuring cup. Add 1 Tablespoon of the dry mixture from the jar into the margarine and stir until mixed well. Drizzle the mixture over the popped popcorn. Oh so good!
