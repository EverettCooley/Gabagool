Ingredients : 
 6 Oz. Semisweet  baking chocolate 
 1 Oz.  unsweetened chocolate , Melted 
 2 to 3 T.  coffee liqueur  Flavored Liqueur 
 1/2 cup  heavy cream 
 Directions : 
 Mix  chocolate  And 2 T. of Liqueur in Top of double boiler Placed Over Simmering  water . 
 When  chocolate  Has Melted, Gradually Stir in Cream. 
 
 
 Notes : 
 For a Thinner Sauce, Add Another T. of Liqueur. 
 Serve Hot Over Ice cream, Pound Cake or Fruit. 
 Makes 3/4 CupSource:
