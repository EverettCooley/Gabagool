Ingredients : 
 1 cup  flour 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 1 tbsp  sugar  replacement 
 ¼ cup  margarine , cold 
 1  egg 
 ¼ cup  evaporated milk  -freeze the rest 
 Choose one of the following to mix in:
 
 8 chopped  dried apple  halves 
 8 chopped  dried apricot  halves 
 ¼ cup  dried cranberries , chopped 
 8 chopped  dates 
 1 tbsp  lemon peel 
 1½ tbsp  orange peel , grated 
 8 chopped  dried peaches  halves 
 4 tbsp  raisins 
 Directions : 
 Sift  flour ,  baking powder ,  salt  and  Sugar  replacement. 
 Cut in cold  margarine  as for pie crust. 
 Beat  egg  and  evaporated milk  together thoroughly; add into  flour  mixture. #Knead gently on lightly floured board. 
 Divide the dough in half; roll each half into a circle. Cut into quarters. 
 Place on lightly greased cookie sheet. 
 Brush tops with  milk . 
 Bake at 450°F for 15 minutes or until done
