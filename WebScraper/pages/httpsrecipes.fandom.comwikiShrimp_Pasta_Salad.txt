Description : 
 Sorry it took me so long, but here's the recipe as promised. :) Although, it's not a pressure cooker recipe. It serves 8 – 10 people as a side dish. :)
 
 Contributed by Lori at  PressureCookerRecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  shrimp  (I use a 12 oz package of the small frozen shrimp) 
 1 cup diced  celery 
 4 hard-boiled  eggs , chopped (reserve small amount for topping salad) 
 ¼ cup sliced  green onions  (use onion and tops) 
 ¼ cup chopped  dill pickle 
 1 cup  mayo  or  Miracle Whip 
 2 Tbsp  chili sauce 
 2 tsp prepared  horseradish 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 x 8 oz box  small shell pasta 
 Directions : 
 Cook pasta and set aside. 
 Combine shrimp, celery, eggs, green onions, and dill pickle in a large bowl. 
 Blend mayo, chili sauce, horseradish, and salt. 
 Add mayo mixture to shrimp mixture; toss to coat. 
 Add cooked pasta; toss to coat. 
 Sprinkle the top with reserved chopped eggs. 
 Refrigerate and enjoy! :)
