 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz  vodka 
 ½ oz  Midori ® melon liqueur 
 Directions : 
 Shake  vodka  and  Midori  with  ice  and strain, or pour on the rocks into a large or double-shot glass. 
 Serve.
