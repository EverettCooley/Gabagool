 Description : 
 Serves 4.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4  eggs 
 25g.  butter 
 Anchovies 
 Capers 
 4 slices square  bread 
 2 tbsp. milk 
 Salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Toast the slices of bread in a small pan, melt the  butter  meanwhile. 
 Stir the  eggs  in the  milk  till combined, add the  salt  and  pepper  and add to the melted  butter , stirring with a wooden spoon all the time, when ready put a little on each peace of toast. 
 Add the  Anchovies  and  capers  or you can just fry an  egg  on each slice and serve like the picture with the rest.
