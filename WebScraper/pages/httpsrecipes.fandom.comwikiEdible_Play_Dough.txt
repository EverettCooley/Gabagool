Description : 
 Thought I'd send this in just in case anyone has kids that might enjoy this.
 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 Yield: 2½ to 3 cups 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  peanut butter 
 1 cup  corn syrup 
 1¼ cup  confectioner's sugar 
 1¼ cup dry  milk powder 
 Directions : 
 Combine  peanut butter  and  corn syrup  in large bowl; mix until smooth. 
 Mix  confectioner's sugar  and dry  milk  in small bowl. 
 Add  confectioner's sugar  mixture to  peanut butter  mixture; mix well. 
 May add enough additional;  confectioner's sugar  to make of consistency of play dough. 
 Let kids create yummy sculptures and then gobble them up.
