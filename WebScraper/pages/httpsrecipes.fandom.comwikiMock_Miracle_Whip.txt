 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 This Y-group is international. Good food from all parts of the world. A place where we can share the wonderful food from all over the world.
 
 Yield 3¼ cups 
 Ingredients : 
 1  egg  plus enough  water  to make ⅔ cup 
 ¾ cup  vegetable oil 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 2 teaspoons  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  dry mustard 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 ¼ teaspoon  paprika 
 1 cup  water 
 ¾ cup  flour 
 ½ cup  white vinegar 
 Directions : 
 Process  flour  and  water  in blender just until smooth. 
 Pour into saucepan. 
 Add  white vinegar  let sit while you combine the other ingredients in the blender. 
 Put  egg  plus enough  water  to make ⅔ cup in blender container. 
 Add  vegetable oil ,  sugar ,  salt , dry  mustard ,  lemon juice  and  paprika  whirl in blender until smooth. 
 Heat  flour  mixture in saucepan until thickened. 
 Pour hot mixture into blender and blend until smooth again.
