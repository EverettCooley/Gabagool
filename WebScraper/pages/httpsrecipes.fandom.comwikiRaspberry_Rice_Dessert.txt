Description : 
 Makes 8 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x 3-ounce package  raspberry  flavored  gelatin  dessert 
 1 cup boiling  water 
 ½ cup cold  water 
 2 cups cold cooked  rice 
 1½ cups  whipping cream , whipped 
 ⅓ cup  sliced almonds , toasted 
 1½ cups fresh  raspberries , divided 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve  gelatin  in boiling  water  in medium bowl; add cold  water . 
 Place bowl in  ice   water  and stir until  gelatin  is the consistency of unbeaten  egg  whites; stir in  rice . 
 Fold in  whipped cream  until smooth. 
 Lightly fold in  almonds  and 1 cup  raspberries . 
 Continue to stir gently (over  ice ) until thickened. 
 Pour into dessert dishes. 
 Chill until ready to use. 
 Garnish with remaining ½ cup  raspberries .
