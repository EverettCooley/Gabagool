Description : 
 Serves 4. 
 Ingredients : 
 4 large  apples 
 ⅛ tsp  cinnamon 
 1 tbsp  butter 
 ½ cup  skim milk 
 ⅓ cup  sugar 
 ⅛ tsp  salt 
 ½ cup  rolled oats 
 Directions : 
 Cut  apples  into slices. 
 Combine  sugar ,  cinnamon , and  salt . 
 Place half the  apples  in a nonstick baking dish and sprinkle with half the  sugar  mixture. 
 Dot with half the  butter . 
 Sprinkle half the  oats  over all. 
 Arrange another series of layers. 
 Then add  milk . 
 Cover and bake 350 °F for 45 minutes. 
 Remove the cover and bake 15 minutes more. 
 Serve hot or cold.
