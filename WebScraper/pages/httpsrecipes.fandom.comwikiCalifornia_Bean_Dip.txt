Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Original recipe 
 Prep Time: 15 Minutes | Ready In: 8 Hours 15 Minutes 
 Yield: 4 cups / 32 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 (11 ounce) cans  white corn , drained 
 2 (15 ounce) cans  black beans , rinsed and drained 
 ½ cup  Italian-style salad dressing 
 1 cup  ranch-style salad dressing 
 1 small  onion , chopped 
 1 teaspoon  hot pepper sauce  
 2 teaspoons chopped fresh  cilantro 
 1 teaspoon  chili powder 
 ½ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 In a medium bowl, thoroughly mix white corn, black beans, Italian- style salad dressing, ranch-style salad dressing, onion, hot pepper sauce, cilantro, chili powder and ground black pepper. 
 Chill in the refrigerator 8 hours, or overnight, before serving.

 Per Serving: 
 
 Calories: 99 | Total Fat: 5.9g | Cholesterol: 2mg | Sodium: 259mg | Total Carbs: 9.4g | Dietary Fiber: 2.2g | Protein: 2.3g
