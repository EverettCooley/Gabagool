Description : 
 Contributed by  Pressurecookerrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 lbs  chicken , cut into serving pieces 
 3 medium  tomatoes , sliced 
 ¼ cup  parsley , chopped 
 1¼ cup  chicken broth or stock 
 1 tablespoon fresh  rosemary 
 1 tablespoon fresh  sage , chopped 
 hot, cooked brown or  white rice 
 parsley , chopped 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Heat pressure cooker, add  butter  and brown  chicken . 
 Remove  chicken  and sauté  onions  until golden brown. 
 Add  tomatoes , parsley ,  chicken stock ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Add  chicken  and herbs. 
 Close lid and bring to high pressure, then cook for 15 minutes. 
 Release pressure, remove lid and serve with either brown or  rice  white.
