Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  Parmesan cheese , grated 
 2 tsp  paprika 
 ½ tsp  bacon bits 
 ground  white pepper  to taste 
 12 oz baking  potatoes , scrub 
 Directions : 
 Cut  potatoes  into 3" x ½" wedges. 
 Preheat oven to 375°F. 
 Spray nonstick baking pan with non-stick spray. 
 Combine all ingredients except  potatoes . 
 Toss  potatoes  in mixture to coat thoroughly (suggestion to put in gallon size plastic bag, press out air, seal, and toss). 
 Place  potatoes  on prepared baking sheet. 
 Bake 10 – 12 minutes, turning over after 5 minutes. 
 Nutritional information : 
 per serving: 92 calories | 4g protein | 2g fat (19.5%) | 15g carbohydrate | 116mg sodium | 4mg cholesterol
