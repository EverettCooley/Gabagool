 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 lettuce , cleaned and ripped into bite sized pieces 
 tomatoes , cut into slices or wedges 
 cucumbers , peeled, seeded and sliced 
 sweet pepper  - washed, seeded and sliced into thin strips 
 Onion- peeled and sliced into very thin rings ( or cut up small ) 
 stuffed , sliced thinly 
 fresh  pineapple  pieces 
 some  capers  ( and any other ingredients you feel to add, like  black olives , canned and drained pickled  artichoke ,  Anchovies , a little grated  Cheese  or channa  Peas  from a can, drained- etc) 
 For dressing:
 
 Extra virgin  olive oil 
 salt- a little 
 pepper- a little 
 sugar- a little 
 balsamic vinegar  ( Other vinegars also work, but this one is absolutely deliciousÂ !) 
 Directions : 
 Mix the dressing ingredients ingredients and pour over salad. 
 You can serve this as a small side dish salad or as a large salad instead of a full meal.
