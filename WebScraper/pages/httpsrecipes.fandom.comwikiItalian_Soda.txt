 Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz. Bacardi Limon  rum 
 1/2 oz. Absolut Mandrin  vodka 
 1/4 oz. Monin  mint syrup 
 4 oz. 7UP or Sprite 
 Directions : 
 Fill a highball glass with  ice . 
 Add  rum ,  vodka , and  mint syrup . 
 Fill glass with 7UP or Sprite. Stir. 
 Garnish with  lime  and  orange  slices and  mint  sprig.
