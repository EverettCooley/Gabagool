Description : 
 This is a great quick healthy dinner filled with protein, vegetables and some brown starch. 
 
 Preparation time is about 20 minutes. 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs of cubed lean  flank steak 
 1 cup of sliced  green peppers 
 1 cup of sliced  onions 
 1 cup of sliced  mushrooms 
 1 cup of whole  green beans 
 salt  to taste 
 pepper  to taste 
 2 cloves of  garlic 
 2 – 3 tablespoons of  soy sauce 
 Directions : 
 Rub the steak with salt, pepper, and soy sauce. 
 Fry in a hot wok or frying pan for 3 – 4 minutes or until pink. 
 Add all the vegetables, 2 cloves of minced garlic, 2 – 3 more tablespoons of soy sauce, salt and pepper to taste, and cook over high heat until beef is brown and vegetables are cooked but still slightly crunchy. 
 Serve over brown rice.
