 Description : 
 Always check the ingredients to make sure the product is vegan.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 Cans  asparagus  Tips 
 
 2 Cups  soy milk 
 
 1 Tbsp Vegan  margarine * 
 
 1 Tbsp  flour 
 
 salt  and  pepper  to Taste 
 Directions : 
 In a medium sauce pan melt the  margarine . Once melted, add the  flour  and whisk together. Continue to whisk while slowly adding all the  soy milk . When the mixture is free of lumps add the  asparagus  juice and tips. Blend everything together until the  asparagus  is pureed. Add  salt  and  pepper  to taste.
