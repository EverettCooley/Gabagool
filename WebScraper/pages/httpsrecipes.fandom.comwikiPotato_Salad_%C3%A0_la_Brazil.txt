      Potato Salad à la Brazil     
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  white potatoes 
 1 lb  carrots 
 2 hard boiled  eggs 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 1 can  green peas 
 salt  with  garlic 
 chopped  parsley 
 1 green  apple 
 ½ cup seedless  raisins 
 Directions : 
 Cook the  potatoes  and  carrots  for 5 minutes with  salt  and  garlic . 
 Cut  potatoes  into 1-inch cubes and  carrots  into ½-inch cubes. 
 Peel the green  apple , remove the core and dice  apple  into ¼-inch cubes. 
 Combine the diced  potatoes ,  carrots , sliced  eggs , drained  green peas , diced  apple ,  raisins . 
 Add the  mayonnaise  while stirring the salad with a wood spoon. 
 Decorate with the chopped  parsley  and refrigerate.
