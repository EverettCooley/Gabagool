Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  biscuit mix  (2 cups reduced fat Bisquick) 
 1 cup  buttermilk  (1 cup  low-fat milk ) 
 1 cup mashed  banana 
 2 large  eggs 
 ½ tsp ground  cinnamon 
 3 tbs  butter 
 ¾ cup  maple syrup  (¾ cup lite syrup) 
 ¼ cup  peanut butter  (¼ cup reduced fat Skippy peanut butter crunchy style) 
 strawberry syrup  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Whisk together the first five ingredients in a large bowl, just until dry ingredients are moistened. 
 Melt one tablespoon  butter  on a hot griddle and pour about ¼ of the batter for each pancake onto the griddle. 
 Cook pancakes until tops are covered with bubbles and the edges look done;turn and cook other side of pancakes. 
 Repeat steps until pancake batter is finished. 
 Whisk together the  maple syrup  and  peanut butter  until smooth. 
 Serve pancakes with  peanut butter  mixture and  strawberry  syrup.
