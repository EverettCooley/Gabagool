Description 
 Bananas are grown in over 100 different countries throughout the world, and their sweet taste is very popular with children in Africa.
 
 Ingredients 
 1 tbsp.  butter  or  margarine 
 1 tbsp. firmly packed  brown sugar 
 ¼ tsp. ground  cinnamon 
 cooking spray 
 2 large  bananas , medium-ripe 
 ¼ cup chopped, unsalted  peanuts 
 Directions 
 Preheat and oven to 350º F. In a small bowl, melt butter in the microwave, about 45 seconds. 
 Stir in brown sugar and cinnamon until well blended. 
 Coat a 9 x 9-inch baking pan with cooking spray. 
 Peel bananas; cut each banana lengthwise in half with the kitchen knife. Place cut-side down in a baking pan. 
 Brush with some brown-sugar mixture; then sprinkle with chopped peanuts. 
 Bake 20 minutes, or until bananas are lightly browned. Serve warm and plain, or with other fruits or desserts. 
 Variation 
 Try using a different variety of banana such as a red banana.
