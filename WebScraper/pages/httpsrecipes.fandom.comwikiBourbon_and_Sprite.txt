Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 4 oz Henry McKenna®  Bourbon whiskey 
 7 oz Sprite® soda 
 Directions : 
 Pour the  bourbon  into a highball glass with some  ice  cubes. 
 Fill glass with sprite or 7-up, and serve.
