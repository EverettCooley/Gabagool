Ingredients : 
 1 oz  hops 
 a cake of  yeast 
 1 lbs  molasses 
 Directions : 
 Combine in a large saucepan; bring to a boil and stir constantly. 
 Allow to cool and ferment before bottling in a crock pot or stone jug, (it should only take a few days).
