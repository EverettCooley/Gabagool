Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: my old recipes 
 71 calories per serving 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups  chicken broth 
 1½ tbsp minced fresh  oregano  or ½ tbsp dried 
 2  eggs 
 1 tbsp grated  romano  cheese 
 1 tbsp minced  parsley 
 2 tbsp  lemon juice 
 pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 In medium pan, bring broth and  oregano  to a boil; whisk  eggs ,  cheese ,  parsley  and  lemon juice . 
 Whisk lightly into broth. 
 Add  pepper  and serve.
