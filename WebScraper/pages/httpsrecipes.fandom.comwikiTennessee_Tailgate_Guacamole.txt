Description : 
 Blue cheese ,  bell peppers , and  pepper sauce  make this a spicy delight!
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 ripe genuine  California avocados , seeded, peeled and coarsely mashed 
 1 tbsp fresh  lime juice 
 10 drops  red pepper sauce 
 ½ tsp coarse  garlic salt 
 ¼ cup crumbled  blue cheese 
 3 tbsp finely chopped  red bell pepper 
 2 tbsp chopped fresh  Italian parsley  leaves 
 Directions : 
 In medium bowl combine avocados,  lime juice , red pepper sauce and  garlic salt . 
 Stir in  blue cheese ,  bell pepper  and  parsley . 
 Serve with tortilla chips or cut vegetables.
