Description : 
 Contributed by Jenn B aka Mom2Sam and Tiny at  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Makes 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 4 skinless, boneless  chicken breasts 
 4 slices part-skim  mozzarella  cheese 
 1  egg white , slightly beaten 
 2 tbsp  flour 
 1 tbsp grated  Parmesan cheese 
 1 tsp snipped  parsley 
 ¼ tsp  paprika 
 dash  pepper 
 non-stick  spray coating 
 Directions : 
 Rinse chicken: pat dry. 
 Use a sharp knife to cut a small pocket in the thickest side of each chicken breast. 
 Insert a mozzarella slice in each pocket. 
 In a small mixing bowl beat egg white slightly. 
 In another bowl stir together flour, Parmesan cheese, parsley, paprika and pepper. 
 Dip chicken in egg white. 
 Then, coat with flour mixture. 
 Spray a baking dish with nonstick coating. 
 Arrange chicken in dish. 
 Bake in a 375°F oven for 20 to 25 minutes or till chicken is tender and no longer pink. 
 Nutritional information : 
 179 Calories | 4g Fat | 3g Carbs | 30g Protein | 129mg Sodium | 0g Fiber 
 Exchanges 3½ lean meat
