Barbecued Meat Patties
 
 1 1/2 Pounds Ground  Beef 
 3/4 Cup Quick Cooking  Oats 
 1/2 Cup  milk 
 3 T.  Onion , Chopped 
 1 1/2 T.  salt 
 1/4 T.  pepper 
 1 T.  worcestershire sauce 
 2 T.  vinegar 
 2 T.  sugar 
 1 Cup  Catsup 
 1/2 Cup  water 
 6 T.  Onion , Chopped 
 
Combine First 6 Ingredients, Mix Together And Let Stand a Little While. Make Patties And Brown on Both Sides. Combine Last 6 Ingredients And Add to Pan. Let Simmer Slowly 20 Minutes. May be Served as a Main Dish For on a Bun. Serves 4 to 6Source: Come Into The Kitchen
