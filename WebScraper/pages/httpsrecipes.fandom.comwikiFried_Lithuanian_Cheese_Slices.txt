Ingredients : 
 1  egg 
 4 tbsp  milk 
 salt 
 4 tbsp  flour 
 cheese  slices, ¼ inch thick 
 bread crumbs 
 Directions : 
 Beat  egg ,  milk  and  flour  and  salt . 
 Dip  cheese  slices in batter - then bread crumbs. 
 Fry in hot fat and serve with  sour cream  or sprinkled  sugar . 
 Variation : 
 Omit  sugar  - place dot of  butter  on each slice. 
 Bake at 300°F for 30 minutes. 
 Serve immediately on  rye bread .
