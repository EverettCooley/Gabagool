Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 large  potatoes , peeled and diced 
 1  onion , diced, 
 3  celery , diced 
 2 cups  water 
 ½ tsp.  dill weed 
 ½ tsp. Mrs. Dash 
 2 tbsp.  chives , finely chopped 
 1 cup  non-fat milk 
 3 tbsp.  flour 
 Directions : 
 Place potatoes, onion, celery and water in a large saucepan. 
 Add dill, Mrs. Dash and chives; simmer until potatoes are tender, about 30 minutes. 
 Place milk and flour in a pint jar. Shake vigorously to mix thoroughly. 
 Add to the potato mixture; simmer 5 – 10 minutes, or until thickened. 
 Notes : 
 Add ¼ tsp ground  pepper , 1 tsp  basil  leaves and/or ½ tsp  garlic powder  in addition to or instead of other herbs. 
 Blend part of soup mixture in blender if desired.
