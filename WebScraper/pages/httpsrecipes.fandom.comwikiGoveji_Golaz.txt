Description : 
 Beef goulash
 
 Ingredients : 
 60 dag  beef shank 
 2 tablespoons  lard 
 2 large  onions , chopped 
 salt  to taste 
 caraway seeds  or  marjoram  to taste 
 1 tablespoon sweet  paprika 
 2 tablespoon  flour 
 1 cup hot  water 
 pepper  to taste 
 2 dl black  wine 
 1 spoon  mustard 
 Directions : 
 Cut  beef  into 2 cm cubes. 
 Melt  lard , add  onions , and cook until transparent. 
 Add  beef  cubes and cook until brown and juice is absorbed. 
 Add spices,  salt  and  paprika . 
 Sprinkle on the  flour  and keep mixing until brown, but not burned. 
 Add hot  water  enough to cover meat and simmer until tender. 
 Add the  pepper . 
 More wine may be added during cooking if necessary.
