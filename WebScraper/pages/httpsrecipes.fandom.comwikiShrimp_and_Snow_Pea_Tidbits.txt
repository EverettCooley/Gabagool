Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes For diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Source:  The Best Diabetes Cookbook 
 Yield: 4 to 6 servings or makes 16 hors d'oeuvres 
 Ingredients : 
 16  snow peas 
 2 tsp  vegetable oil 
 1 tsp crushed  garlic 
 1 tablespoon chopped fresh  parsley 
 16 medium  shrimp , peeled, de-veined, tail left on 
 Directions : 
 Steam or microwave  snow peas  until barely tender-crisp. 
 Rinse with cold  water . Drain and set aside. 
 In a non-stick skillet, heat  oil ; Sauté  garlic ,  parsley  and shrimp just until  shrimp  turn pink, 3 to 5 minutes. 
 Wrap each  snow pea  around a  shrimp ; Fasten with toothpick. 
 Serve warm or cold. 
 Nutrition information : 
 Per Serving:
 
 39 Calories | 1g Carbs | 0g Fiber | 4g Protein | 2g Fat | 27 mg Sodium | 28 mg Cholesterol 
 Diabetic exchanges: ½ Lean Meat
