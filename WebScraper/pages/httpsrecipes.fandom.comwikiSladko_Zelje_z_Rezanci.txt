Description : 
 Cabbage and noodles
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 medium sized  cabbage , shredded 
 4 tablespoons  oleo 
 1 medium  onion , chopped 
 25 dag  noodles , thin or broad 
 caraway seeds  to taste 
 1 teaspoon  salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Cook noodles in pot of  water  to which  salt  is added. 
 Drain and set aside. 
 Shred the  cabbage  as for cole slaw. 
 Now place oleo in deep skillet and add the chopped  onion . 
 Saute over low flame until  onions  are cooked to a golden yellow, but not brown. 
 Add the shredded  cabbage ,  caraway seeds ,  salt  and  pepper ; cook until  cabbage  is wilted. 
 Add the drained cooked noodles and mix gently. 
 Place on a platter and sprinkle buttered bread crumbs over the  cabbage  and noodles and serve. 
 You may also sprinkle  sugar  over the  cabbage  and noodles if you wish.
