Description : 
 Vietnamese  chicken  salad can be prepared very easy and quickly.
 
       Vietnamese Chicken Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 chicken breasts 
 lime juice 
 Sauce : 
 rice vinegar 
 sugar 
 serrano chilies 
 cloves  garlic 
 red onion 
 bean sprouts 
 napa cabbage 
 cilantro 
 black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Prepare the  chicken . 
 Prepare a mixture with  lime juice , sauce,  rice vinegar ,  sugar ,  onions ,  chile peppers  and  garlic . 
 Prepare the sprouts and add the mixture of  chicken ,  bean sprouts ,  cabbage  and  cilantro .
