Ingredients : 
 2 cans white or yellow  corn  (or 1 of each) 
 ½  Spanish onion , diced 
 1  red pepper , diced 
 ¼ pound  snow peas , trimmed 
 ¼ cup  oil 
 1½ – 2 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 salt , to taste 
 pepper , to taste 
 Directions : 
 Drain  corn  and pour into a bowl. 
 Mix with  onion ,  red pepper , and  snow peas . 
 Combine  oil ,  lemon juice ,  salt , and  pepper . 
 Pour over  corn  mixture.
