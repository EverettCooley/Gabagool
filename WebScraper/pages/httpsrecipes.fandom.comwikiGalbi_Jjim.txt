Description : 
 Beef short rib in sweet soy sauce
 
       Galbi jjim)     
 Ingredients : 
 4.5 lbs gal bi ( beef short ribs ) 
 1  carrot , peeled, cut into ¾ inch thick 
 10  chestnuts , peeled 
 3 cups  water 
 2 cups  soy sauce 
 1.5 cups  sugar 
 ½  onion 
 1 head  garlic 
 1 piece  ginger  (½ inch) 
 1 tbsp whole  black pepper 
 2  green onions 
 1 tbsp chung ju ( sake  or  red wine ) 
 1 tsp ground  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Wash ribs, put in a cold  water  for 1 hour. 
 Drain, put in a big pot with  water , soy sauce,  sugar ,  onion ,  garlic ,  ginger ,  peppers  and chung ju. 
 Stir to melt the  sugar . 
 Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, cover the lid, cook for 2 – 3 hours until the meat falls from the bone. 
 Add  chestnuts  and  carrots  10 – 15 minutes before done.
