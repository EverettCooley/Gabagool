Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Pryor estate in Grapevine, Texas in 1996.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound dry  navy beans 
 ¼ pound  salt pork  cut into chunks 
 ¼ cup  brown sugar 
 1 teaspoon dry  mustard 
 2 teaspoons chopped  onion 
 ½ cup  dark molasses 
 1½ teaspoons  salt 
 Directions : 
 Soak  beans  overnight. bring to a boil in same  water  and simmer until tender. Drain reserving liquid. 
 Preheat oven to 275 °F. 
 Place beans and  salt pork  in casserole. 
 Combine cooking liquid,  brown sugar , mustard ,  onion ,  molasses  and  salt . 
 Pour over  beans  then cover and bake 4 hours.
