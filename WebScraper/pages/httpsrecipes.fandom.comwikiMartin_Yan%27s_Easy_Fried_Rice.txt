Fried Rice : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 teaspoons  vegetable oil 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 2 eggs 
 1/2 cup of peas 
 1/2 cup thinly sliced  carrots 
 1/2 cup sliced  green onions 
 3 cups cooked  rice , chilled 
 2 tablespoons soy sauce 
 1 teaspoon  sesame oil 
 1/8 teaspoon ground  white pepper 
 Directions : 
 Place a wok or wide frying pan over high heat until hot. Add  oil  swirling to coat sides. Add  garlic . Cook, stirring, until fragrant, about 15 seconds. Add   peas ,  carrots  and  onions ; stir 1 minute. Add  rice ; stir to separate  grains  with back of spoon. Make a hole in the rice and add beaten eggs. Stir the eggs around with chop sticks until cooked. Mix well with rice. Stir in soy sauce,  sesame oil  and  pepper ; cook until thoroughly heated.
