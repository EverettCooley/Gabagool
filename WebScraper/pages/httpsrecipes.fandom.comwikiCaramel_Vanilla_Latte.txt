 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3/4 cup hot  water 
 1/4 cup hot  milk 
 2 teaspo  vanilla  Roast TASTER'S CHOICE Instant  coffee 
 1 tables caramel flavored sundae syrup 
 1 teaspo  dark brown sugar 
 1 tables  whipped cream  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 1 COMBINE  water ,  milk  and Taster's Choice in large mug; stir until  coffee  is dissolved. Stir in caramel syrup and  Sugar . Top with  whipped cream . Serve immediately.
