Ingredients : 
 3  cinnamon  sticks 
 2 teaspoons whole  cloves 
 ½ teaspoon ground  nutmeg 
 ½ gallon  apple cider 
 1 cup  sugar 
 2 cups  orange juice 
 ½ cup  lemon juice 
 1 cup  brandy , any fruit flavor 
 Directions : 
 Tie  cinnamon , cloves and  nutmeg  in cheesecloth. 
 Simmer  cider  and  sugar  with spices for about 15 minutes. 
 Remove bag of seasonings. 
 Add  orange juice ,  lemon juice  and  brandy . 
 Heat to bubbling and serve.
