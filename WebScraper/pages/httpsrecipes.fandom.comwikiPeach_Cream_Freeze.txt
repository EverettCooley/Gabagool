peach  Cream Freeze
 
 1 (22 Oz.) Can  peach  Pie Filling 
 1 (15 Oz.) Can Eagle Brand  milk 
 1 Sm. Can Crushed  pineapple , Drained 
 1/4 C.  lemon juice 
 1/4 Tsp.  almond extract 
 1 Sm.  Cool Whip 
 
Mix All Together And Freeze.
