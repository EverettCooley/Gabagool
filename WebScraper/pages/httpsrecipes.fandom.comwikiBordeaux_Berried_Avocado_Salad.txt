Ingredients : 
 1 x 5-ounce package Ready Pac Bordeaux  Salad mix 
 2 ripe  California avocados , peeled, pitted, cubed 
 1 Pint fresh California  strawberries , hulled, halved lengthwise 
 ⅓ cups  avocado ,  grape seed oil  or  canola oil 
 ⅓ cups  red wine vinegar 
 coarse salt  and freshly ground  black pepper  to taste 
 1 x 4-5 ounce log herbed soft  chèvre , sliced into discs 
 ¼ Cup toasted California  almonds , coarsely chopped 
 Directions : 
 Gently toss together the bordeaux salad mix, cubed avocados and  strawberries  with the  oil ,  vinegar ,  salt  and  pepper  in a glass salad bowl. 
 Stud with the chèvre and sprinkle with the  almonds . 
 Serve cool.
