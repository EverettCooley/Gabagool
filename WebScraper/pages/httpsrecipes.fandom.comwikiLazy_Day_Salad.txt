 Ingredients : 
 20 oz can crushed  pineapple  (with Juice) 
 16 oz  fruit cocktail  (drained) 
 2 x 8 oz  mandarin oranges  (drained) 
 1 package instant  vanilla  or  lemon  pudding mix 
 1 small to large container  Cool Whip  (to taste) 
 3 cups miniature  marshmallows  (optional) 
 coconut , to taste (optional) 
 ½ cup  pecans  (optional) 
 ½ cup flaked  coconut  - coloured is nice (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Mix the fruit 
 Add pudding mix 
 Add  Cool Whip  and mix well 
 You can add the miniature marshmallows and  coconut  at this point 
 Sprinkle with:  pecans  and  coconut 
 Store in Regfrigerator
