 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 500 grammes white fish without edge, in bits 
 4 cups  water 
 2  tomatoes 
 1  Onion , peeled 
 4 brushwoods  parsley 
 2  peppers , in bits 
 2 tea spoons salts 
 1 tea spoon dried  thyme 
 Directions : 
 Reel the fish clean and puts together the fish with the  water  in the pan. 
 Tailor the  tomatoes ,  Onion  and  parsley  in small bits and joint them with the bits  pepper  to the fish.
 
 Joint salts and  thyme  and rudder everything by each other. 
 Bring the soup to cook and lets everything 20 minutes softly cook.
