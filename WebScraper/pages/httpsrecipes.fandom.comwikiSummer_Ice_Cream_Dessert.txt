 Filling : 
 2 qt vanilla  ice cream 
 2 qt lemon or lime, or lemon-lime-raspberry swirl  sherbet 
 Topping : 
 4 tbsp  lemon juice  or 3 tbsp lemon juice concentrate 
 1 cup  sugar 
 2 tbsp  butter 
 2 beaten  eggs 
 Directions : 
 Mix together first 3 ingredients until crumbly and press into 9 x 13 x 2 inch pan. 
 Soften the ice cream and sherbet and stir together. 
 Spread mixture over the crumbs and freeze. 
 Combine the topping ingredients and cook over low heat until dissolved. 
 Cook until thick, remove from heat and cool. 
 Spread topping over frosting ice cream and freeze until serving. 
 Enjoy.
