Description 
 Kulfi is a type of ice cream from India. It is usually made with condensed milk, flavored with cardamom and topped with chopped almonds and pistachios.
 
 by user  Bhadani  ( talk ) 
 Cook Time : 30 minutes 
 Serves : 4 
 Ingredients 
 1 liter of creamy  milk 
 10  cardamom  seeds (may leave out if not readily available) 
 10-15 strands of  saffron , crushed, soaked in 1 tsp. warm milk (may leave if not available easily) 
 sugar  to taste 
 ¼ cup chopped and thinly sliced  almonds 
 ¼ cup of chopped and thinly sliced  pistachios 
 Directions 
 Put cardamom in the milk and bring to boil. 
 Reduce the heat and continue to stir till reduced by around one-third – care should be taken that the milk particles does not get stuck to the pan as it will spoil the taste. 
 Remove cardamoms from milk by using a strainer. 
 Add the copped almonds, pistachios and crushed saffron. 
 Pour the mixtures in small containers or cups. 
 Freeze until firm. 
 Remove from the refrigerator about 5 minutes before serving. 
 Tips 
 If cardamom seeds or saffron are unavailable, you may add the chocolate powder of your choice before putting the cups into freezer.
