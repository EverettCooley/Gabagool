 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 large  Pears , halved 
 25g/1oz Chopped  walnuts 
 2 tbsp  brown sugar 
 25g/1oz Soften  butter 
 Â½ teasp Ground  cinnamon 
 For the syrup 
 225g/8oz  granulated sugar 
 240ml/8fl.oz.  water 
 1 teasp  vanilla extract 
 Directions : 
 Preheat the oven to 180C, 350F, Gas Mark 5 and lightly grease a shallow ovenproof dish. Halve the  Pears  lengthways, remove the cores and place in one layer, cut sides up in the ovenproof dish. 
 Place the  walnuts ,  brown sugar ,  butter  and  cinnamon  in a small mixing bowl and mix thoroughly. 
 Divide the mixture between the  Pear  halves, sprinkle over 1-2 tablespoons of  water  and bake for 15 to 20 minutes. 
 Meanwhile, place all the ingredients for the syrup in a saucepan, bring to the boil, stirring then continue to cook for about 10 minutes or until the mixture is thick and syrupy. 
 When the  Pears  are cooked, remove from the oven and pour the hot syrup over the top. 
 Set aside to cool. Serve cold.
