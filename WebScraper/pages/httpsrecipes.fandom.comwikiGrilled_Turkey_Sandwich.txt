INGREDIENTS: : 
 8 pieces of white bread 
 8 slices of turkey 
 1 cup of cranberry sauce 
 1 cup of stuffing 
 1-1/2 cups of mashed potatoes 
 4 lettuce leaves 
 6 pieces of thick cut bacon, cut in half 
 2 large tomatoes, sliced 
 2 cups gravy-warmed 
 For the Herb Mayonnaise:
 
 1 cup real mayonnaise 
 1 tablespoon finely chopped rosemary 
 1 tablespoon finely chopped thyme 
 DIRECTIONS: : 
 Make Herb mayonnaise: mix all ingredients in a bowl and set aside. 
 Place herb mayonnaise on one side of all 8 pieces of bread. 
 Take one piece of bread, herb side down and layer the ingredients: 1 Tablespoon cranberry sauce, 1 Tablespoon stuffing, 2 Tablespoons mashed potatoes, 2 pieces of turkey, 3 pieces of bacon, 1 piece of lettuce, and 2 tomatoes.Â  Repeat same steps with remaining 3 slices of bread. 
 Add the second piece of bread on top with herb mayonnaise side up. 
 Heat a large skillet on medium heat and add two of the sandwiches, grill until bread is golden brown on one side and carefully flip to grill the other side, (about 3-4 minutes a side). 
 Remove sandwiches and repeat with remaining sandwiches, serve hot with gravy for dipping.
