 Ingredients : 
 2 cups cooked  rice 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ½ cup  milk 
 2 tbsp melted  butter 
 2 tbsp  pimento , chopped fine 
 1 tbsp  parsley 
 1 cup  American  or  Swiss cheese , diced 
 pepper  and  paprika  to taste 
 1 tbsp  onion , grated 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients well and use to stuff  peppers  or  tomatoes . 
 Bake about 30 minutes in medium oven.
