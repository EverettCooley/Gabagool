 Ingredients : 
 4 oz (½ cup)  sugar  125 g 
 2 oz (¼ cup)  butter  or  margarine  50-60 ml 
 1 tsp  vanilla  5 ml 
 3 tsps  rum   or   brandy  45 ml 
 ½ cup warm  water   or   milk  125 ml 
 Directions : 
 Cream  sugar  and  margarine . 
 Add warm liquid. Mix well. 
 Add  rum  or  brandy  and  vanilla extract . 
 Put in a container. 
 Serve over black-fruit cake.
