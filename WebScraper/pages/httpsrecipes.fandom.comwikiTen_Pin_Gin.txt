Ingredients : 
 1½ oz London dry  gin 
 1¼ oz  Mandarine Napoleon ®   orange liqueur 
 1 oz  dry vermouth 
 ½ oz  lime juice 
 1 tsp  egg white 
 1 oz  sugar syrup 
 Directions : 
 Mix the ingredients and serve with  ice .
