Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 about 1½ pounds fresh  green beans , cleaned, 1½ inch pieces 
 about ½ pound  mushrooms , cleaned and sliced 
 1  onion , sliced 
 5 or 10 cloves  garlic , minced or smashed 
 2 teaspoons dried  basil 
 1 teaspoon dried  marjoram 
 generous amount freshly ground  black pepper 
 ½ cup  red wine 
 1 x 32 ounce can crushed  tomatoes 
 1 pound  mostaccioli  pasta 
 Directions : 
 Put  red wine  in large skillet or dutch oven add vegetables and herbs, simmer for 10 to 15 minutes. 
 Add canned  tomatoes , (or fresh sauce if you have it) let it simmer away for 30 minutes or until you are ready for it. 
 Cook  pasta  in unsalted, unoiled  water  combine the two.
