Description 
 Fake Shakes  from Recipes for Better Nutrition During Cancer Treatment by the National Cancer Institute, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 Make imitation milkshakes for people who cannot drink milk or eat ice cream. 
 
 Cook Time : About 10 minutes 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 
 1 frozen citrus fruit juice bar (2½ oz)  or  2 bars (1¾ oz) same flavor 
 ½ cup chilled  soy milk  / nutritional supplement 
 ¼ tsp  vanilla 
 Directions 
 Remove citrus bar from freezer and allow to thaw slightly (about 5–10 minutes until soft). 
 Break bar into pieces. 
 Place in blender. 
 Add other ingredients and blend at low speed for 10 seconds. 
 Variations 
 Double Citrus : Add 1 tbsp. frozen orange juice concentrate and 1 tbsp. sugar to the lemon or orange flavor Fake Shake before blending. This version has 192 calories and 3 grams of protein per cup.
