Serves : 4
 
 Ingredients 
 2  sweet potatoes 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 ½ teaspoon  paprika  or  cinnamon 
 dash of  salt 
 Directions 
 Preheat oven to 400 °F. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil, and spray foil well with cooking spray. 
 Wash sweet potatoes, and slice lengthwise into sticks. 
 In a large bowl, mix oil and paprika or cinnamon. 
 Add potato sticks and stir to coat. 
 Place sweet potatoes on prepared baking sheet and lightly sprinkle with salt. 
 Bake for 40 minutes or until sweet potatoes are tender. 
 Source 
 Sweet Potatoes, Fresh  by the US Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
