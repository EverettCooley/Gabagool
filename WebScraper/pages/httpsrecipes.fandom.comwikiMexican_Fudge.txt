 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/4 cup  water 
 1 cup  white sugar 
 1 large can  carnation   milk 
 2 more cups of  Sugar 
 1 cup chopped  nuts 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Boil 1 cup  water  and 1 cup  Sugar  till amber color, add can of  carnation   milk , the extra 2 cups  Sugar  and boil until sample forms lumps in cold  water . 
 Add  nuts  and beat. Put in buttered pan or drop on waxed paper.
