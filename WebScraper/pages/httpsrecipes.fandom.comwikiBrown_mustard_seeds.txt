 About Brown mustard seeds : 
 A variety of mustard seed that is smaller and has a much stronger flavor than white mustard seeds. Brown mustard seeds will be sold whole and used as a seasoning, ground into a powder, or used in the production of a variety of European and Asian prepared mustards. If stored in a cool dark place, then brown mustard seeds will remain usable for a year or more.
