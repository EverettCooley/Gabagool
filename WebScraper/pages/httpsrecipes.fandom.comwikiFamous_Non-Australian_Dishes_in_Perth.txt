No matter how different a taste you crave, Australia never ceases to amaze you with its huge variety of food options and cuisines.
 Especially when it comes to non-Australian foods, Australia has eateries like the Indian restaurant in Perth that serves authentic food of that particular non-Australian origin. Let's find out about some of the most popular non-Australian food items in Perth here:
 
         
 Tacos 
 
Tacos are known to be traditional Mexican foods where small hand-sized tortilla made with corn or wheat flour and are topped with meat and vegetable fillings. This delicious Mexico food is not only extremely popular in various cities of Australia such as Perth, but is also available in different varieties such as tacos made with fish or chicken or beef or chorizo etc. Providing you with a full-meal satisfaction, tacos are quick snacks that are as popular as famous other non-Australian foods like Indian food in Perth. 
 Chicken Cacciatore 
 
         
 Chicken Cacciatore is a flavorsome Italian cuisine that is loved by the people of Perth as well as Australia just the way they love Indian authentic cuisines from the   best Indian restaurant in Perth. Chicken cacciatore is simple yet delicious Italian comfort food where braised chicken or rabbit meat is cooked in a rustic and rich sauce made with Italian herbs, onions, bell peppers, tomatoes, wine and other ingredients. This classic Italian cuisine is best served with rice or pasta. Apart from Chicken Cacciatore, some other extremely popular Italian food in Perth are pizza, spaghetti, pasta, ravioli etc. 
 Tandoori Chicken 
 
         
 When it comes to famous Indian food in Perth, the list is never-ending. From veg items like Malai Kofta, Palak Paneer, Carrot Halwa to non-veg items like Biriyani, Mutton Roghan Josh, Butter chicken is craved by the people of Australia. Among the variety of authentic Indian food served by the best Indian restaurant in Perth ,  Tandoori Chicken is something people can't get over from. Known for its authentic spicy flavor and delicious taste, Tandoori Chicken comprises of slowly cooked chicken chunks that come in the form of kababs.
 Ma Po Tofu 
 
           
 Just like the Indian restaurant in Perth ,  there are many Chinese restaurants in Perth because of the popularity of different Chinese cuisines like Sweet Prok Bun, Dumplings, Sweet and Sour Prok, Peking Duck, Wontons, etc. But when it comes to the Chinese dish Ma Po Tofu where tofu is cooked and served in a spicy sauce along with some minced beef, the popularity is of different levels. This tongue-numbing authentic Chinese dish is best enjoyed along with rice.
