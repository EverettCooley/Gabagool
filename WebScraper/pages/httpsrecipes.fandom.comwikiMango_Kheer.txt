 Description : 
       Mango Kheer     
 Ingredients : 
 1 kg ripe mangoes 
 200 gms  Sugar 
 1 liter  milk 
 2 tbsp. chopped  nuts 
 Directions : 
 Peel and extract  mango  pulp from the ripe mangoes and sieve it. 
 In a deep pan, cook  mango  pulp with equal quantity of  water . 
 Prepare  sugar syrup  (boil  Sugar  with little  water  and boil until it is slightly thickened) and mix it with  mango  mixture while continuously stirring. 
 Add  milk  to it. Mix well and keep it to boil till mixture thickens. Decorate it with  nuts  and serve. 
 Serves: 4
 Preparation time: 35 minutes
