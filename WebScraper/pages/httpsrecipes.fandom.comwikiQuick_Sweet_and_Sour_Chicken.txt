Description : 
 Makes 2 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ x 10- to 12-ounce package frozen breaded  chicken  chunks 
 1 x 8¾-ounce can  apricot  halves in syrup, drained 
 1 x 8-ounce package frozen  sugar snap peas 
 2 tablespoons bottled sweet and sour sauce 
 1 teaspoon  soy sauce 
 1 cup hot cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Bake  chicken  chunks according to package directions. 
 Meanwhile, heat  apricots ,  peas , sweet and sour sauce and soy sauce in medium saucepan over medium heat until  peas  are tender crisp. 
 Stir  chicken  into  apricot  mixture. 
 erve  chicken  mixture over  rice .
