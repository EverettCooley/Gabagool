Description : 
 Prep: 10 minutes 
 Makes 4 servings 
       Country Ham Sandwiches     
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup Kraft  mayo : real or light  mayonnaise   or  substitute  Miracle Whip  or Miracle Whip Light  salad dressing  for  mayo 
 ½ tsp  garlic powder 
 ½ tsp  black pepper 
 8 slices  whole wheat bread 
 lettuce  (optional) 
 tomato  slices (optional) 
 1 pkg. (6 oz) Oscar Mayer smoked  ham 
 8  Kraft singles 
 Directions : 
 Mix  mayo  and seasonings. 
 Spread bread slices with  mayo  mixture. 
 Layer 4 of the bread slices each with  lettuce ,  tomato ,  ham  and 2 singles. 
 Top with remaining bread slices.
