Description : 
 Purchased from the Young Estate in Sparks, Nevada in 1990. The notation on card says this was "Paw-Paw's favorite dessert". Dated 1916.
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cups stale  breadcrumbs 
 2 cups scalded  milk 
 1 ounce bitter  chocolate 
 1 cup  sugar 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  vanilla 
 1 slightly beaten  egg 
 ⅓ cup  sugar 
 ½ teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 Vanilla Sauce : 
 1 tablespoon unsalted  butter 
 2 tablespoons  flour 
 2 cups boiling  water 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Soak bread in 1¾ cup  milk  for thirty minutes. 
 Melt  chocolate  over hot  water . Gradually add  sugar  and remaining ¼ cup  milk  to make smooth paste. 
 Add to bread with  salt ,  vanilla , and  egg  slightly beaten. 
 Place in well buttered pudding dish and bake ½ hour until set. 
 Serve with  vanilla  sauce which is made by melting the  butter  and  flour  and stir until it bubbles. 
 Add the boiling  water  and  sugar . Boil until smooth and thoroughly cooked. 
 Add flavoring, strain and serve hot.
