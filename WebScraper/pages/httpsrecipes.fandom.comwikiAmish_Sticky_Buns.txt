      AMISH Sticky Buns     
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  Yeast 
 1 tablespoon  Sugar 
 1 cup  water 
 1 cup  Milk 
 6 tablespoons  Butter 
 1/2 cup  Sugar 
 1 teaspoon  Salt 
 7 cups  Flour 
 3  eggs , Beaten 
 Syrup 
 3 cups  brown sugar 
 3/4 cup  Butter 
 6 tablespoons  Syrup 
 Topping 
 brown sugar 
 Cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve  Sugar  in warm  water . 
 Scald  milk , then add  Butter ,  Sugar  and  Salt . 
 Cool. 
 Add two cups  Flour . 
 Add  yeast  and  eggs . 
 Stir in the remainder of the  flour  . 
 Let rise until double. 
 Boil  all  Syrup  ingredients for 2 minutes. 
 When dough is doubled, punch down and divide into two parts. 
 Roll each part into rectangle, on a well floured board or table. 
 Sprinkle with  brown sugar  and  Cinnamon . 
 Roll up tightly. 
 Cut into 1" slices, and lay slices on top of the syrup in the pans. 
 Bake  at 375 for 15 minutes.
