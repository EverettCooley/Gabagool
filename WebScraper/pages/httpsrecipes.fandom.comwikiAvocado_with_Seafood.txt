 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/4 cup  lemon juice 
 1/2 tsp.  mace 
 1 pint  Mayonnaise 
 1 lb. crab or lobster meat. 
 1/2 lb  Shrimp , clean and deveined. 
 2 to 3  lettuce  heads 
 fresh avocadoes, peeled and halved 
 Directions : 
 In a 1 quart-bowl, blend the  lemon juice  and  mace  together. Stir slowly and add add the  Mayonnaise  by the spoonsfull. Stir until you get a consistent, thick mixture. 
 Mix the crab or lobster meat and shimp together. Marinate slightly with a little of the dressing immediately before serving 
 Arrange the  lettuce  leaves to form cups on plates. 
 Place the  avocado  halves, peeled, in center of cup. 
 Fill Iightly with seafood blended with dressing.
