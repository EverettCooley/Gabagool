 Wiki.com/Bahama_Breeze Bahama Breeze  from Recipes Wiki—original source of recipe, licensed under the GNU Free Documentation License
 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 1 oz  dark rum 
 ½ oz  banana liqueur 
 ½ oz  apricot liqueur 
 ½ oz  coconut rum 
 ¼ oz  grenadine 
 ¼ oz  honey 
 ½ oz  lemon juice 
 1 oz  orange juice 
 1 oz  pineapple juice 
 Directions 
 Blend well with a couple ice cubes. 
 Pour over ice in tall glass. 
 Garnish with orange, cherry, etc.
