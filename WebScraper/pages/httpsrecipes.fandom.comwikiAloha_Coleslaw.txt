Ingredients 
 1 medium head  green cabbage , thinly sliced 
 1 large  carrot , shredded 
 1 (20-ounce) can  pineapple , drained 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 Directions 
 In a large bowl, stir together cabbage, carrot, pineapple, mayonnaise, and salt and let chill for at least an hour before serving.
