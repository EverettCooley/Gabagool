 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lb  bananas 
 1 lb  Raisins 
 2 oz  garlic 
 2 oz red chillies 
 4 quarts brown  vinegar 
 1 lb  brown sugar 
 6 oz  salt 
 8 oz  ground ginger 
 
 
 Directions : 
 1. Chop finely the  bananas ,  Raisins ,  garlic  and chillies.
 2. Boil with  vinegar  until cooked.
 3. Add  Sugar ,  salt  and  ginger , boil up again for 10 minutes, them bottle.
