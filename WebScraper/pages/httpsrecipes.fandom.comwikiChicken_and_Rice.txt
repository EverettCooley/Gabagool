 Info : 
 Serves:  2
 
 Ingredients : 
 Rice and vegetables : 
 Rice 
 Fine sliced  carrot 
 1 small red pepper 
 1 small  leek 
 6 fish sticks 
 2  eggs 
 Chicken and vegetables : 
 Chicken 
 1 sliced  onion 
 1 courgette 
 Peas 
 1 red and 1 green peppers finely cut 
 Methods : 
 Rice and vegetables : 
 Cook all vegetables in sesame oil. 
 Fry rice and vegetables together in sesame oil. 
 Season with  salt  and  MSG . 
 Chicken and vegetables : 
 Slice chicken into pieces. 
 Fry chicken pieces in oil for 5 minutes with garlic, salt, black pepper, white pepper. 
 Add vegetables to the chicken with a little water. 
 Cook until the vegetables are done. 
 Serving : 
 Serve rice on a plate with chicken and add vegetables over the top.
