 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/3 cup Mild flavored honey 
 1/2 cup Creamy or chunky style - Peanut   butter 
 1/2 cup Nonfat dry  milk 
 Directions : 
 1 Combine honey and  Peanut   butter  in bowl. 
2 Gradually stir in dry  milk  until thoroughly blended. 
3 Shape into narrow roll. 
4 Wrap in waxed paper and chill until firm. 
5 Cut into bite-sized pieces and wrap in plastic wrap or waxed paper. 
6 sounds like valerie"s recipe except with nonfat dry  milk  substituted for the  wheat germ .
