      Zuppa Inglese     Literally translated as English soup,' this Italian dish is, in fact, a refrigerated dessert similar to the British favorite. It's made with  rum -sprinkled slices of  sponge cake  layered with a rich  custard  or  whipped cream  (or both) and  candied fruit  or toasted  almonds  (or both).
