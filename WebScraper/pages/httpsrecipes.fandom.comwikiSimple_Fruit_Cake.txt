Ingredients : 
 1 pkg.  yellow cake mix  with pudding in mix 
 4 c. fresh fruit 
 1 c.  sugar 
 1 pt. whipping  cream  or  Half-and-half 
 Directions : 
 Spray cake pan with Pam. 
 Add dry cake mix to pan 
 Add fruit 
 Add sugar 
 Pour cream over all. 
 Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour. 
 Notes : 
 I use my frozen apples. They work great!
