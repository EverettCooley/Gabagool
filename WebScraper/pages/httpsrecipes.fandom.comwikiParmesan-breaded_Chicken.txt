 Description : 
 Serves four
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup seasoned  bread crumbs 
 ¼ cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 ½ tsp  oregano 
 ¼ tsp  rosemary 
 ¼ tsp  basil 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 4  chicken breasts , skinned 
 ½ cup  buttermilk 
 1 each  oil  for coating pan 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Combine spices, bread crumbs and  cheese  in a shallow bowl. 
 Dip  chicken  pieces in the  buttermilk , then in the crumb mixture. 
 Lightly coat a baking pan with  oil . 
 Place  chicken , bone side down, on the pan. 
 Bake, covered, for 25 minutes or until done. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving: 
 
 277 calories | 23 % fat | 7 gm fat.
