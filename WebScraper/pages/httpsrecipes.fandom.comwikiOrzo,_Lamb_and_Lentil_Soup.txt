 Ingredients : 
 2 Tablespoons  olive oil 
 1 Pound  Lamb  Or  Beef  -- cubed 
 salt  And  pepper  -- to taste 
 8 Cups  chicken broth 
 1 Cup  Lentils 
 1 Medium  Onion  -- chopped 
 1 Small  carrot  -- chopped 
 2 Cloves  garlic  -- chopped 
 2 Tablespoons  butter 
 1/2 Teaspoon  cumin 
 1/2 Teaspoon  cinnamon 
 1 Cup  orzo 
 Directions : 
 1) Saute  Lamb  or  Beef  in  oil . Season with  salt  or  pepper . 
 2) Add half the broth, cover and simmer for 30 minutes. 
 3) Stir in  Lentils , and remaining broth, simmer for 15 minutes. 
 4)Meanwhile saute  Onion ,  carrot , and  garlic  in  butter  until soft. sprinkle with  cumin  and  cinnamon . 
 5) Add them and  orzo  to the pot. cover, keeping lid ajar and simmer for 10 minutes or  Lamb  and  Lentils  are tender and  Pasta  is al dente. 
 6) Season more if necessary.
