Description : 
 Makes 12 muffins
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 package  Apple Muffin Mix 
 1  egg 
 ¾ cup  milk 
 ¼ cup  vegetable oil 
 Directions : 
 Preheat the oven to 400 °F. 
 Grease 12 muffin tins. 
 Place the apple muffin mix in a large bowl and add the egg, milk and oil. 
 Stir the ingredients until they are just blended. 
 Do not overmix. 
 Spoon the batter into greased muffin pans, filling ¾ full. 
 Bake for 15 – 18 minutes or until golden brown.
