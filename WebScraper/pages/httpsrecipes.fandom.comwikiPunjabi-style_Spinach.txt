Description : 
 Ethnicity - Punjabi, North Indian 
 Type of meal - Party, Lunch, Dinner 
 Ingredients : 
 500 g  mustard greens  (or  spinach ) 
 2 cm piece of  fresh ginger 
 2 hot  green chillies 
 2 cloves  garlic  (finely chopped) 
 2 tsp  salt 
 2 tbsp  cornmeal 
 2 tbsp  ghee 
 Directions : 
 Wash, trim, and finely chop greens. 
 Put 2 cups  water  into a large pot with the  salt  and the greens. 
 Cover and cook about 15 minutes until tender. 
 Remove from heat. 
 Drain excess liquid into a jug. 
 Mash greens thoroughly in the pot then stir in  cornflour . 
 Return to heat with half a cup of reserved liquid. 
 Simmer 10 minutes, stirring frequently. 
 Set aside. 
 In another pan heat  ghee  and lightly fry  ginger ,  garlic , and chillies for 3-5 minutes. 
 Add contents of pan into the pot of greens and mix well. 
 Serve hot and enjoy!!.
