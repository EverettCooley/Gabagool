 Ingredients : 
 1 shot Stolichnaya Red  ®   vodka 
 1 shot Stolichnaya Vanil  ®   vodka 
 1½ shots  cream of coconut 
 ¼ shot  sugar syrup 
 6 drops  orange bitters  (if desired) 
 garnish: fresh  strawberries 
 glass: martini 
 Directions : 
 Muddle ingredients in cocktail shaker. 
 Add  ice , then shake and strain into a glass.
