 3 Tbs  Mayonnaise 
 2 Tbs Finely Diced  red onion 
 1  celery  Rib, Chopped 
 3 Slices Fully Cooked  Bacon , Crumbled 
 1 Tbs Dijon  mustard 
 lettuce  Leaves
