Ingredients : 
 Pastry : 
 4 cups of  plain flour 
 1/4 stick of  butter 
 salt 
 warm  water 
 Filling : 
 1 lb of  minced veal 
 1/2 lb of  minced beef 
 4  onions 
 1/4 stick of  butter 
 2  egg yolks 
 salt  and  pepper . 
 Directions : 
 Mix  flour , melted  butter ,  salt  and warm  water  to prepare the pastry. 
 Cut the pastry in four equal pieces. 
 To prepare the filling mix the meet, melted  butter , chopped  onions ,  egg  yolks,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Roll the pastry out on the table until very thin and leave for few minutes to dry. 
 Cover the pastry thinly with melted  butter . 
 Put the filling at the edge of one side of the pastry and roll it up to create  Sausage  roll like pastry. 
 Take the round dish and create the spiral with the pastry starting from the middle of the dish. 
 Before completely baked pour either the  double cream  or  butter  melted in warm  water . 
 Cook time 40-43 minutes.
