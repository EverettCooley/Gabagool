Description : 
 Delicious  onion  soup Belgian style ! Together with the  Parmesan cheese  and  French bread  it's one of the most made soups in Belgium ! Enjoy !
 
 Makes 4 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 2 large  red onions , sliced 
 6 tbsp  butter 
 3 cup  chicken stock 
 1 bottle  beer 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 4 thick slices  French bread 
 grated  Parmesan cheese 
 Directions : 
 In a large saucepan, saute  onion  in  butter  until soft. 
 Add  chicken stock ,  beer ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Cover and simmer about 30 minutes. 
 Adjust seasonings. 
 Broil  bread  on one side about 3 inches from heat. 
 Turn, sprinkle generously with  Parmesan cheese , and broil 1–2 minutes more, until lightly browned. 
 Pour soup into bowls. 
 Add cheese-covered  bread .
