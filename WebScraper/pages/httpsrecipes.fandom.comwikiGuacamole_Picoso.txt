 Description : 
 Spiced up with serrano chilis!
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 medium size  California avocados  (not very ripe) 
 6 medium serrano pepers 
 Â½ Cup  milk 
 salt  as needed 
 Directions : 
 Cut in pieces the  California avocados  and put in a blender/food processor
 Cut serrano pepers in half and add to avocados together with  salt  to taste
 Add about 1/4 cup  milk .
 Blend together until smooth. 
 If the guacamole is too thick, add the rest of the  milk  to desired consistency.
