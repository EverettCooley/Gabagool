      Jack Daniel's Crab and Corn Chowder     
 Ingredients : 
 4½ tbsp  flour 
 ½ cup chopped  celery 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 3 tbsp  butter 
 1 cup diced  red potatoes 
 ⅔ cup fresh  corn 
 ½ tbsp  crab base  (or 1  chicken bouillon  cube) 
 1½ cup  water 
 1 cup  cream 
 ¾ cup  Dungeness crab  meat 
 1 pinch  thyme 
 1 pinch  salt 
 1 pinch freshly-ground  black pepper 
 ½ tsp  Old Bay seasoning 
 1 tbsp minced fresh  parsley 
 2 tbsp  Jack Daniel's   whiskey 
 Directions : 
 Precook  potatoes , drain, cut and set aside. 
 Sweat  onions ,  celery  and  corn  with  butter , add  flour  and  thyme  and cook 5 to 10 minutes. 
 Add  water  and crab base, old bay seasoning,  parsley  and ½ cup of the crab meat. 
 Add  heavy cream  and bring to 180°F. 
 Add  potatoes  and  salt  and  pepper  to taste. 
 Add Jack Daniel's and remainder of crab meat just before serving.
