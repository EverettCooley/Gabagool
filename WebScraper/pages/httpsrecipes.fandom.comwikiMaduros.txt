 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 maduros (sweet  platano   bananas ) 
 3 cups of  milk 
 2 sticks of  cinnamon 
 2 cups of  Sugar 
 1 teaspoon of vainilla 
 Directions : 
 Peal the maduros and put them in a bowl. Add the  milk  and then boil them for a few minutes until they are tender. Then put in the  cinnamon ,  Sugar , and vanilla. Keep on boiling it all for five minutes. Next put them in a bowl and cook them in the oven at 350 degrees for 30 minutes until they are browned.
