Description : 
 This is an excellent accompaniment to opened-faced  sandwiches , served at a tea or receptions. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Serves 4 people. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x 16-oz can tropical fruit salad, drained (Del Monte)  or  2 cups fresh  fruits  may be used 
 1 cup shredded  coconut 
 1 cup miniature  marshmallows 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 Directions : 
 Toss ingredients together gently. 
 Refrigerate, covered, for several hours or overnight.
