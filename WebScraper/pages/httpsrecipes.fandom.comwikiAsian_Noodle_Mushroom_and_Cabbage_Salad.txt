      Description     
 Ingredients : 
 12 large dried  shiitake mushrooms 
 1 tablespoon  peanut oil 
 3 cups thinly sliced  napa cabbage 
 1 tablespoon minced peeled fresh  ginger 
 1 tablespoon minced  garlic 
 14  green onions ; 12 halved lengthwise and cut on diagonal into 2-inch lengths, 2 chopped 
 3 tablespoons  soy sauce 
 1 x 1-pound package fresh thin  Chinese egg noodles   or  one 12-ounce package dried  Chinese egg noodles 
 â…“ cup oriental  sesame oil 
 2 tablespoons fresh  lemon juice 
 1 tablespoon unseasoned  rice vinegar 
 2 teaspoons  sugar 
 3 hard-boiled  eggs ; 2 thinly sliced, 1 chopped for garnish 
 1 cup chopped fresh  cilantro 
 Directions : 
 Boil  mushrooms ,  cabbage ,  ginger ,  garlic , and  onions  for approx 3 minutes. 
 Boil noodles until tender add in sesame  oil ,  sugar ,  eggs , and  cilantro . 
 Add  soy sauce . 
 Chill, toss well and serve.
