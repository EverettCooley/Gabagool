 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs creamed  cottage cheese 
 1 lb dry  farmer's cheese 
 ½ pint  sour cream 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together well. 
 Place in cloth bag sewn into long triangle. 
 Let hand 6 – 8 hours to drip. 
 Place between 2 boards and weigh down with heavy stone. 
 Let set 24 hours. 
 Remove bag slowly. 
 Place on flat plate and cover loosely with plastic wrap and let stand 24 hours in refrigerator. 
 If more dryness is desired place in 200 °F oven for about 1 hour. 
 Serve slices with  butter .
