 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 	 cup imitation crabmeat, flaked 
 1 	cup grated  carrots 
 1/4 	cup peeled quartered thinly sliced  cucumbers 
 1/4 	cup thinly sliced scallions ( green onions ) 
 1/2 	cup  peanuts  (preferably unsalted) 
 1/2 	cup  Mayonnaise 
 1 	tablespoon soy sauce 
 1 	teaspoon  rice vinegar 
 1 	tablespoon yellow  curry  powder 
 1 	teaspoon  granulated sugar 
 1/4 	teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients. 
 May be eaten as is, but tastes best chilled overnight.
