 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ Cup Dijon  mustard 
 3 Cups vinaigrette 
 12  California avocados 
 1/3 Cups  lemon juice 
 3 Gallons mixed lettuces 
 3 ½ quarts smoked  Turkey  or  Chicken , cut in strips 
 3 Cups  dried cherries  or  cranberries 
 24 (6-inch) prepared bread shells 
 Directions : 
 Whisk  mustard  into vinaigrette; reserve.
 Just before service, cut  avocado  in 1/4-inch dice; toss gently with  lemon juice . Reserve.
 Per Order:
 Toss 2 cups  lettuce , 1/2 cup (slightly mounded)  Turkey  or  Chicken , and 2 tablespoons dried fruit with 2 tablespoons reserved vinaigrette. Fold in 1/2 cup  avocado . Mound on top of one warmed bread shell.
