Ingredients : 
 1 kg  cabbage 
 300 g  minced pork  or  beef 
 1  carrot 
 1  onion 
 2 tbsp  tomato paste 
 2 tbsp  rice 
 1 tbsp  flour 
 2 tbsp  fat 
 salt
