Ingredients : 
 2 cups  flour 
 1 cup boiled mashed  pumpkin 
 a little ripe  pawpaw  (mashed) 
 1  egg 
 sugar  to taste 
 butter ,  oil , etc (small amount) 
 Directions : 
 Rub in a little  butter  or  oil  in the  flour . 
 Add all other ingredients. 
 Mix with  water  or  milk  to thin. 
 Rub  oil  onto the frying pan and cook.
