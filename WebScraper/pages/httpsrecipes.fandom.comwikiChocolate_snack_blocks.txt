 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 Envelopes  unflavored gelatin 
 3/4 cup Cold  water 
 1 cup Boiling  water 
 1/3 cup  granulated sugar 
 2 cup (12 Ounces) Semi-Sweet 
 - chocolate  Mini Chips 
 Directions : 
 1 Yield: about 6 dozen squares 
2 if you kids like the fruit  gelatin  snacks, they"ll love these  chocolate  snacks. 
3 in a blender, sprinkle the  gelatin  over the cold  water  and let stand for 5 minutes. 
4 Add the boiling  water  and  Sugar , cover and blend on low speed until the  gelatin  is completely dissolved, about 2 minutes. 
5 Continue to blend, gradually adding small amounts of the  chocolate chips  until the chips are melted and the mixture is smooth. 
6 Pour into an 8- or 9-inch square pan. 
7 Refrigerate until firm and cut into 1-inch squares or shapes with cookie cutters
