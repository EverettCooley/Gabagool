Ingredients : 
 2 cups fresh cut-from-the-cob  corn  kernels 
 8 oz fresh  Chinese pea pods , sliced into thirds 
 3 tbsp diced  pimento , drained 
 2 tbsp  margarine 
 dash of  salt 
 dash of  pepper 
 1/3 cup roasted and chopped Oregon  hazelnuts 
 Directions : 
 In a saucepan, melt  margarine . 
 Add  corn ,  salt  and  pepper , and cook for 4 minutes. 
 Add  pimento  and pea pods and continue to cook for 4 to 6 minutes. 
 Add  hazelnuts  and serve.
