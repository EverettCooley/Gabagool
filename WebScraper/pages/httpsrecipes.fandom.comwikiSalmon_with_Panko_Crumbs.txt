Ingredients : 
         
 2 pounds of  salmon  filet (serves 6 people) 
 Â¼ teaspoon  paprika 
 6 tbsp virgin  olive oil 
 1 cup of  panko  bread crumbs 
 salt  and  pepper 
 1 tsp  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Brush salmon with olive oil and season with salt, pepper and paprika on both sides 
 Let sit for ten minutes 
 While sitting,season panko crumbs on a separate bowl with salt and paprika to taste 
 Place salmon (skin side down) on a greased baking dish and sprinkle the top with seasoned panko crumbs 
 Bake for 10 minutes or until salmon is flaky, drizzle with lemon juice if desired
