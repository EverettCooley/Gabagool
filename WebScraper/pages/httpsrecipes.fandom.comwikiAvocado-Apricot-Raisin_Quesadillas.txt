 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 Slices of  Bacon , well-cooked 
 1 Small  California avocado 
 4 Fresh  apricots , peeled and mashed 
 1 oz  Raisins , chopped 
 1 oz  apple juice , if needed 
 4 oz finely shredded  jack cheese 
 4 Small  flour  tortillas 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 300 degrees.
 Mash ripe  California avocado  in small bowl.
 Finely mince  Bacon  and add to  avocado  mixture.
 In a separate bowl, mix the peeled and mashed  apricot  with the chopped  Raisins .
 If the  apricot  mixture is too thick, thin with  apple juice .
 Spread approximately one teaspoon of  avocado  mixture over one-half of the tortilla.
 Spread approximately one teaspoon  apricot  mixture over the other half of tortilla.
 Fold torilla over and place on sheet pan.
 Place in oven and heat until  Cheese  is melted. Remove from oven and cool.
 Slice into four pieces and serve.
 One serving is two pieces.
