 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1	x	4-lb.  duck 
 1	x	tin  pineapple  slices 
 2	tbl	 oil 
 2	tbl	 soy sauce 
 1	tbl	 rum 
 1/2	cup	 water 
 2	tbl	 brown sugar 
 1	pch	 salt 
 Directions : 
 Clean and roast the  Duck . Cool, remove bones and slice. Drain  pineapple  slices and dice. 
 Place  Duck  and  pineapple  in alternate layers in a casserole. 
 Add the  pineapple juice  mixed with  oil ,  soy sauce ,  rum ,  water ,  Sugar  and  salt . 
 Bake in moderate oven for 1 hour. Serve with  rice  and green salad.
