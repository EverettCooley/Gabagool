 Description : 
 Makes 4 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 1/3 cups skim  milk 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 Low-calorie  sugar substitute  to equal 2 tablespoons  Sugar 
 1 ripe  banana 
 1/4 cup  whipping cream , whipped 
 2  kiwi  fruit, sliced, for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Cook  rice  and  milk  in 2-quart saucepan over medium heat until thick and creamy, 5 to 8 minutes, stirring frequently. 
 Remove from heat; cool. Stir in  vanilla  and  sugar substitute . 
 Just before serving, mash  banana ; fold  banana  and  whipped cream  into pudding. 
 Garnish with  kiwi fruit  slices. 
 
 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Makes 4 servings 
 per serving: calories-195, protein-4.6g, fat-6g, carbohydrate-31g sodium-49 mg and cholesterol-22 mg 
 exchanges ½ starch/bread, 1 fruit, ½ skim milk and 1 fat 
 from the Rice Council of America
