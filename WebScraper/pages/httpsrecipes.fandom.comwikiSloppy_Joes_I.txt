Ingredients : 
 3 lbs  ground beef 
 1 cup  onion , minced 
 2 clove  garlic , minced 
 1½ cups  ketchup 
 1 cup  green pepper 
 ½ dash  water 
 4 tbsp  brown sugar 
 4 tbsp  prepared mustard 
 4 tbsp  vinegar 
 3 tsp  chili powder 
 12  hamburger buns 
 Directions : 
 In a large skillet, brown ground beef, onion and garlic. Cook until meat is brown and onion is tender 
 Drain off fat 
 Combine remaining ingredients in a crock pot 
 Stir in meat mixture. 
 Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3 – 4 hours. 
 Spoon onto hamburger buns. Enjoy!
