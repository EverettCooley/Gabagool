 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  extra-firm tofu 
 1/2 cup  orange juice 
 2 tablespoons  honey 
 1/4 cup  granulated sugar  or  fructose 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 1 ripe  banana 
 Directions : 
 Blend together in food processor or blender.
 
 1 1/4 cups  rolled oats 
 1/2 cup  Raisins 
 1 tablespoon  baking powder 
 1 teaspoon  baking soda 
 1 cup  whole wheat flour 
 Mix together.
 Mix wet ingredients with dry. Spoon into muffin cups and bake at 375 degrees F for 25 minutes.
