 Description : 
 purchased from the Zimmerman Estate in Terrell, Texas in 1989 
 Date on card says 1944
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  navy beans 
 2 teaspoons  salt 
 3 tablespoons  brown sugar 
 ¼ cup  molasses 
 1  bay leaf 
 ½ teaspoon dry  mustard 
 ¼ cup chopped white  Onion 
 1 cup boiling  water 
 ½ pound  salt   Pork  (I have also used  Bacon  and it works extremely well) 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Wash beans. 
 Cover with  water  and soak overnight. 
 Drain well. 
 Cover with a large amount of boiling salted  water  and boil slowly for 1 hour. Drain well. 
 Combine  salt ,  Sugar ,  molasses ,  bay leaf ,  mustard ,  Onion  and  water  and add to beans. 
 Pour into bean pot. Score rind of  Pork  and press into beans, leaving the rind exposed. 
 Cover beans with additional boiling  water  and bake in 300 degree oven for 4 hours. 
 Remove cover for last hour of baking
