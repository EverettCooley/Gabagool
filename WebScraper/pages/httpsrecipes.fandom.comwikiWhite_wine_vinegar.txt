White wine vinegar refers to vinegar that is produced from  white wine  and is a popular ingredient for the preparation of various  salad dressings . White wine vinegar is usually a bit harsher and has fewer flavors than red wine varieties. The quality and flavor of white wine vinegar vary widely between brands.
