Description 
 Cheesy Hamburger Casserole  from Recipes for Better Nutrition During Cancer Treatment by the National Cancer Institute, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 Quickly prepared, this casserole may disappear just as fast at the table! Serves 4
 
 Ingredients 
 1 cup  macaroni , uncooked 
 ½ lb ground meat ( beef ,  veal ) 
 ½ small  onion , chopped 
 ¾ cup  tomato sauce  or chopped  tomatoes 
 ½ can (10 oz) cheddar cheese soup 
 Directions 
 Cook macaroni until slightly tender. Drain, set aside. 
 Brown ground meat and onions in small skillet. Add tomatoes and simmer 10 minutes. 
 Oil a 1-quart casserole, and spoon in 1/3 of meat mixture. Add cooked macaroni, then the remainder of meat mixture. Spread cheese soup over all (may be frozen unbaked). Cover the casserole tightly and bake at 400° until bubbly
