Ingredients : 
 4 large  eggs 
 3 tbsp  flour 
 1 cup  Sugar 
 3 tbsp  cornstarch 
 3 packet 8 oz cream Cheese 
 2 tsp vaniila extract 
 1/2 cup  butter  - 1 stick softened 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 1/4 cup  cinnamon  and  Sugar 
 Directions : 
 Grease the pan with  shortening . 
 Make a mixture of  cinnamon  and  Sugar  and coat the pan. 
 Set Aside. Beat  eggs  in large bowl. 
 Blend in  Sugar ,  flour  and  cornstarch . 
 Add remaining ingredients blending well after each addition. 
 Blend until smooth. Pour into 9-inch springform pan. 
 Pan will be very full. Bake for 1 hour at 325F. 
 Turn oven off and leave cake to cool with oven door open for another hour. 
 Remove and refrigerate. 
 
Note: Leave the cake iin the pan for 4 Hrs, or overnight. Put your favorite topping on, and serve.
