Description : 
 Here is an easy salad recipe you can prepare for your family or take to your next family get-together.
 
 Contributed by Paula in California at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 6 cups shredded  cabbage 
 2 cups shredded  carrots 
 8  bacon  strips, cooked and crumbled 
 12  green onions , thinly sliced 
 ½ cup  vinegar 
 ⅓ cup  vegetable oil 
 ⅓ cup  sugar 
 1 tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Combine cabbage, carrots, bacon, and onion. In a jar mix vinegar, 
 sugar, salt, and shake well. Just before serving, pour dressing
over cabbage mixture and toss.
