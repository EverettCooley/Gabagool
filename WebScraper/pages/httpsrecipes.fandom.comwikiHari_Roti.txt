A light greenish-brown flatbread 
 Made from whole-wheat chapati flour 
 Boiled and mashed with green peas, salt, lime juice, chopped mint, or coriander leaves 
 Oil, ghee, and water are added to the dough 
 The dough is rolled into 5 inch. circles and cooke on a griddle
