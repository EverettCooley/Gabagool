Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2  eggs , well beaten 
 ⅛ tsp  salt 
 1½ to 2 cups  peanut butter 
 4 to 5 cups  powdered sugar 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 1 Hershey®  chocolate  bar 
 1 (6 oz) pkg.  chocolate chips 
 Directions : 
 Mix the  eggs ,  salt ,  peanut butter , sugar and  vanilla  in order listed. 
 Form dough into  egg  shapes. 
 Melt the  chocolate  bar and the  chocolate chips  in a double boiler. 
 Dip  egg  shapes into  chocolate  mixture. 
 Arrange on waxed paper until set.
