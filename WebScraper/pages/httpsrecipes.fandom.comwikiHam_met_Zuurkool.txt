Description : 
 Ham and sauerkraut. A quick and easy dish that is filling and perfect for a winter lunch or if you add  potatoes  a light evening meal.
 
 Ingredients : 
 250 g  sauerkraut 
 1 small  apple 
 1½ tbsp  sour cream 
 100 g lean  ham  in one piece 
 ½ tbsp of  mustard 
 Directions : 
 Preheat the oven to 200c. 
 Wash and drain the  sauerkraut . 
 Peel the  apple  and cut it in two. 
 Slice one half and dice the other. 
 Mix the  sauerkraut  with the  apple  and the  sour cream . 
 Put the  ham  on foil and brush with  mustard . 
 Spread the  sauerkraut  over one half of the  ham . 
 Fold over the  ham  and stick together with a toothpick. 
 Cover with the sliced  apple , wrap in the foil and place in the oven for 15 minutes.
