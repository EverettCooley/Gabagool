Ingredients : 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 2 stalks  celery , diced fine 
 2 small  Anaheim chiles 
 1 cup chopped  onions 
 1 (14.5ounces) can diced  tomatoes 
 1 (8 ounces) can  tomato sauce 
 1 (6 ounces) can  tomato paste 
 2 cans non-fat  beef broth  (or  vegetable stock ) 
 1½ tablespoons  chili powder 
 1 teaspoon ground  ginger 
 salt  and  pepper 
 2 packages Boca   vegetarian ground beef 
 ½  poblano chiles 
 2 cans  pinto beans 
 Directions : 
 Mix the ingredients and boil them for 2 hours.
