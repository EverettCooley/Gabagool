Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  flour 
 ½ lb  currants 
 ½ lb  raisins 
 ¼ lb mixed peel 
 ¾ lb  brown sugar 
 2 tbs  mixed spice  ( nutmeg  and  cinnamon 
 1 tsp rind of one  lemon 
 ½ lb  butter 
 1 ts  bicarbonate of soda 
 1 bottle Guinness  or   stout 
 4  eggs 
 Directions : 
 Sift the  flour  and cut in the  butter . 
 Add the fruit, spice, peel, etc. 
 Beat  eggs . 
 Heat Guinness or other  stout  and pour it over the bicarbonateof soda. 
 Pour  stout  and  eggs  over dry ingredients. 
 Mix well and beat for 15 minutes. 
 Bake in a well-greased 9 by 13 pan in a moderate oven, 350 °F, for 2½ - 3 hours. 
 Keep for a week before cutting. 
 Enjoy.
