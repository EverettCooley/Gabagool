Ingredients : 
 1 cup  onion  and  bell pepper , mixed 
 1 cup  butter 
 2 oz jar sliced  pimentos 
 2 cans refrigerated  biscuits 
 5 slices crisp fried  bacon , crumbled 
 1 tbsp  Parmesan cheese 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter , stir in  onions  and  bell pepper . 
 Saute until wilted then stir in  pimentos . 
 Cut biscuits in quarters, place in bundt pan. 
 Pour melted  butter ,  onions ,  bell peppers  and  pimentos  over biscuits. 
 Stir to coat well, season with  garlic powder  and  cayenne pepper  to taste. 
 Sprinkle crumbled  bacon  bits and  Parmesan cheese  over biscuits and bake at temperature on biscuit can until golden brown.
