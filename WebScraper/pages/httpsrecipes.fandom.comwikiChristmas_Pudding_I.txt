Ingredients : 
 1 cup grated  carrot 
 1 cup grated  potato 
 1¼ cups soft  breadcrumbs 
 1 cup  raisins 
 1 cup  currants 
 1 cup  brown sugar 
 ½ cup  margarine 
 2 tbsp  sour milk 
 ½ tsp  baking soda 
 ½ cup  flour 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 ⅛ tsp  nutmeg 
 ½ tsp  allspice 
 For serving : 
 ½ cup  rum  or  bourbon  heated slightly before serving 
 2 cup  zabaione sauce 
 holly sprigs to garnish (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Mix in order given. 
 Turn into well greased heat proof bowl; cover and steam about 3 hours. 
 Takes a medium sized bowl. 
 Serve with  brown sugar  sauce, or custard, or whatever you like.
