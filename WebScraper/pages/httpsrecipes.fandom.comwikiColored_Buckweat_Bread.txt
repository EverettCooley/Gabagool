Ingredients : 
 50 dag  buckwheat flour 
 20 dag white  flour 
 5 dl boiling salted  water 
 5 dag  lard 
 1 teaspoon grouned  anise 
 4 dag  yeast 
 White dough : 
 30 dag white  flour 
 2 dag  yeast 
 sal and  pepper  to taste 
 2 dl  milk 
 Directions : 
 Pour hot salted  water  on  buckwheat flour . 
 Cool a little, then add a little raised  yeast . 
 Add melted  lard  and  anise . 
 Knead the dough well and add the white four. 
 Let it rise. 
 Roll out. 
 Then make the white dough. 
 Use hot  milk  and add the ingredients. 
 After the rising roll out the dough. 
 You can grease it with melted  lard  or  oil  or crackings. 
 Put the  buckwheat  plate on the white dough plate and roll them. 
 Lay the roll in a greased baking tin and wait, until it rise again. 
 Bake the bread about 40 minutes in a moderate hot oven. 
 Cut cooled bread to slices.
