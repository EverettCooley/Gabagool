Ingredients : 
 1 cup #1  bulgur 
 1 small can  tomato  sauce - about 4-6 ozs 
 1/2  tomato  sauce can worth of  water 
 salt 
 paprika 
 juice of 1.5  lemons 
 chopped  parsley  and  chives 
 Directions : 
 Mix  bulgur ,  tomato  sauce and  water . 
 Wait until liquid is absorbed. 
 Add the rest of the ingredients to taste
