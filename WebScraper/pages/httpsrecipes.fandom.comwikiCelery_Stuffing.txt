Description : 
 This is a recipe I obtained from an Estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Stewart Estate in Terrell, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 8 ounce package herb seasoned  stuffing  mix 
 1 teaspoon ground  sage 
 1 cup chopped  celery 
 ½ cup chopped  white onion 
 ¼ cup  butter 
 1¼ cup  chicken broth 
 Directions : 
 Combine stuffing mix, sage, celery and onion. 
 Add butter to broth the heat through and add to stuffing. 
 Toss lightly.
