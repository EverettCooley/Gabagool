Ingredients : 
 2 balls of  Edam 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 ¼ cup  beer 
 2 tsp  chives 
 Directions : 
 Cut a circle from top of each  cheese , about ½-inch in from sides. 
 Scallop the edges for an elegant presentation, discard removed wax. 
 Scoop out  cheese , leaving approximately a ½-inch shell combine  cheese ,  sour cream ,  beer , and  chives  in a food processor. 
 Process until smooth. 
 Spoon back into shells. 
 Cover and chill for 2 – 96 hours. 
 Serve slightly chilled or at room temperature.
