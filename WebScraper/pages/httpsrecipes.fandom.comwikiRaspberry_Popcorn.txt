Ingredients : 
 500 g Keelings  raspberries 
 100 g  popcorn  kernels 
 5 tbsp  coconut oil  (divided into 2 & 3 tbsp) 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 Instructions : 
 Heat a large pan on a medium to high heat, add 3 tablespoons of the coconut oil and cover the pan with a lid. 
 Add the kernels to the pan and continually shake the pan so that they don't stick or burn. 
 Once all of the popcorn has popped, place them into a large bowl. 
 In a bowl grind the raspberries. 
 Heat the remaining coconut oil in a pan along with the sugar so it dissolves. Remove from the heat and add the raspberries, ensuring that you mix this all together. 
 Pour the mix over the popcorn until it is fully coated.
