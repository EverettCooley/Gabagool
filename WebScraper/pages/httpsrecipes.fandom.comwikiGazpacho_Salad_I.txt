Description : 
 A salad to go with the paella
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups  plum tomatoes , chopped 
 1  cucumber , seeded, peeled and diced 
 ¼ cup chopped  onion 
 ⅓ cup fresh  cilantro , chopped 
 1  jalapeno pepper , seeded and diced (I usually leave this out) 
 ¼ cup  olive oil 
 ¼ cup  red wine vinegar 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients and mix well. 
 Allow salad to sit at room temperature for about an hour to allow flavors to blend.
