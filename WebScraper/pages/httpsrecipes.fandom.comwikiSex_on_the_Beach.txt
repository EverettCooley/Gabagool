       Sex on the Beach     
 Sex on the Beach  from the Wikibooks Bartending Guide—original source of recipe, licensed under the GNU Free Documentation License
 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 1½ oz (40 ml)  vodka 
 ¾ oz (20 ml)  peach schnapps 
 1½ oz (40 ml)  cranberry juice 
 1½ oz (40 ml)  orange juice 
 Directions 
 Fill a highball glass with ice. 
 Add all ingredients. 
 Stir. 
 Serve.
