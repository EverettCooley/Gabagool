 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 medium size  California avocado  (Peeled and Mashed) 
 1 Tbsp  flour 
 1 Tbsp  butter 
 1 Cup liquid hot  milk 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Parmesan cheese  or  Cheddar  or mozarella 
 thyme  and  parsley 
 Breadcrumbs 
 One  egg  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter  in a pan and stir in the  flour .
 Pour the hot  milk  over the mixture till it thickens.
 Add a beaten  egg  if you wish.
 Put in your mashed  California avocado .
 Put also a cupful of grated  Cheese  and beaten  egg .
 Place in a baking tray.
 Top with breadcrumbs and grated  Cheese .
 Cook in oven for 25 minutes.
 Can be served cold with toast bread.
 Can be served with a green salad or  tomato  chutney.
