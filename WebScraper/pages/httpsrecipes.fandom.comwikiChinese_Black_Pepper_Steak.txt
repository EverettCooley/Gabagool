 Ingredients : 
 750 g  eye fillet  (in one piece) 
 2  onions , sliced 
 Â½ tsp  sesame oil 
 2 Tbsp dry  sherry 
 2 tsp  sugar 
 2 tsp grated fresh  ginger 
 1 Tbsp dark  soy sauce 
 2 Tbsp whole  black peppercorns , crushed 
 Directions : 
 Cut  steak  into thin slices and combine with  onions , sesame  oil ,  sherry ,  sugar ,  ginger  and  soy sauce  in bowl. 
 Cover, and let marinate several hours or refrigerate overnight. 
 Stir peppercorns through  steak  mixture. 
 Stir-fry  steak  and  onions  in a wok over high heat until  steak  is cooked to your liking.
