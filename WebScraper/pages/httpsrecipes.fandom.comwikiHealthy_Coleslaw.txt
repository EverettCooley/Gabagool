Desciption : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Source:  The Diabetes Food and Nutrition Bible 
 Yield: 8 servings (½ cup each) 
 Ingredients : 
 Dressing : 
 1 cup non-fat plain  yogurt 
 ¼ cup apple  cider vinegar 
 1 tbsp  honey 
 Slaw : 
 1 lb (1 small head)  green cabbage , shredded 
 ½ cup shredded  carrot 
 1 tbsp  poppy seeds 
 ¼ cup  raisins  or  currants 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl, combine the dressing ingredients. 
 Add the cabbage, carrots, poppy seeds, and raisins. 
 Mix well until the dressing completely coats the cabbage. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per (½ cup) serving:
 
 58 Calories, 1g Fat | 1mg Cholesterol | 36mg Sodium | 12g Carbs | 2g Dietary Fiber | 9g Sugars | 3g Protein 
 Diabetic Exchanges: 1 Carbohydrate
