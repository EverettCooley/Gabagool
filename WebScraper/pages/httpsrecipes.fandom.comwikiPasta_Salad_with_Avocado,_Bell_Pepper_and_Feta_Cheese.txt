Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 36 oz (1kg)  penne  (or other short, tube  Pasta ) 
 2 cups vinaigrette 
 3 cups diced red  bell pepper 
 3 cups diced green  bell pepper 
 3 cups  feta cheese 
 2 cups chopped  red onion 
 1 ½ cups  black olives  pitted, coarsely chopped 
 1 ½ Tbsp chopped  garlic 
 ¾ cup chopped  parsley 
 4 pounds  California avocados 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Pasta  in salted, boiling  water  until just tender, about 10 minutes; drain well. 
 While still warm, toss with vinaigrette; cool. 
 Fold in remaining ingredients. 
 Plate 1-1/2 cups as an entrée salad or 1/2 cup as a side dish.
