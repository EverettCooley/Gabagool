Ingredients : 
 1 cup  butter 
 2 cups  sugar 
 1 1/2 cup  self-rising flour 
 1 1/2 cup  water 
 2-13 oz can of sliced  peaches  in heavy syrup 
 
 
 Instructions : 
 Melt butter. 
 Mix all ingredients together except for peaches. 
 Pour into 13x9x2 inch dish. 
 Pour peaches over batter. Do not stir. 
 Bake 1 hour at 350 degrees, or until golden brown.
