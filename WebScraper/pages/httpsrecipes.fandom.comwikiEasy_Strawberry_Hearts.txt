      Easy Strawberry Hearts     
 Ingredients : 
 fresh  strawberries , washed 
 knife 
 Directions : 
 Pinch the leaves off a strawberry. 
 Cut the strawberry down the middle, from top to bottom. 
 Now the halves easily slice into heart-shaped pieces. Trim as needed. Serve with  Heart-shaped Strawberry Pancakes .
