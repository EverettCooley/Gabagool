 Description : 
 '
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 	pack 	Crescent rolls 
 2 	pack 	(8-oz)  cream cheese 
 1 	cup 	 Mayonnaise 
 1 	pack 	Hidden valley Ranch Dressing 
 Mix 
 3/4 	cup 	vegetables or choose your favorites 
 Broccoli ,  Cauliflower ,  tomatoes 
 Onion ,  peppers , and  carrots 
 Directions : 
 1 	Sprinkle grated  Cheddar  and/or  mozzarella   Cheese  if you'd like.
 2 	Cover the bottom of a cookie sheet with rolls to make a crust.
 3 	Bake at 350 degrees for 8-10 minutes. or until light brown.
 4 	Cool 10 minutes.
 5 	Mix ingredients (except vegetables and  Cheese ) until smooth.
 6 	Spread over cooled crust.
 7 	Finely chop vegetables and spread on  cream cheese  topping.
 8 	Sprinkle with  Cheese .
 9 	If you want to reduce the fat content, low-fat  mayo  and  sour cream  can be used.
