Ingredients : 
 zest of 1  lemon 
 1½ oz cleaned  ginger , thinly sliced 
 1 tbsp  granulated sugar 
 ¾ tsp  cayenne 
 ¾ tsp  salt 
 1 tbsp fresh  lemon juice 
 ¼ cup unseasoned  rice vinegar 
 ⅓ cup  soy sauce 
 2 tbsp  canola oil 
 2 tbsp pure  sesame oil 
 Directions : 
 In a food processor (or with mortar and pestle), combine  lemon  zest,  ginger ,  sugar ,  cayenne , and  salt  and process to a smooth puree; add  lemon juice ,  rice vinegar  and soy sauce. 
 Blend well. 
 Slowly add  canola oil  and  sesame oil  until well combined.
