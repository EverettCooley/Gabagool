Ingredients : 
 Golden Delicious apples 
 Granny Smith apples 
 Red Delicious apples 
 Choice of assorted cheeses:  Cheddar ,  Blue ,  Brie ,  Edam , or  Swiss 
 Cooked  ham , cut in large julienne strips 
 Directions : 
 Arrange  apples  and cheeses on attractive  cheese  board or platter. 
 Set out  apple  cutters, if desired, and  cheese  knives. 
 To serve, cut  apples  in wedges and serve with choice of  cheese .
