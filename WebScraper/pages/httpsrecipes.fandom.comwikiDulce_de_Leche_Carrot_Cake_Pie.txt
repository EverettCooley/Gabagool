      Dulce de Leche Carrot Cake Pie     
 Description : 
 Who says that carrotsÂ are just for the main courses? You can make a frozen delight out of carrots. Turn your ordinary carrot into a luscious cake topped with ice cream, caramel apples and crunchy pecans.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups carrot cake Ice Cream, softened 
 16-ounce prepared graham cracker crust 
 3 large crisp, red-skinned  apples 
 2 tablespoons fat free caramel sundae syrup 
 1 1/2 teaspoons  lemon juice 
 1/4 cup roasted and salted  pecan  halves 
 Directions : 
 Sprinkle sliced apples with lemon juice to keep white 
 Take the softened ice cream and spread it evenly on the pie crust. 
 Cover this and freeze for at least four hours or so. 
 Core and slice the apples, and dip in lemon juice to retain color 
 Arrange apples on pie 
 Drizzle with caramel sundae syrup 
 Sprinkle with pecans
