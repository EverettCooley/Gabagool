Ingredients : 
 6 oz  white  or  bittersweet chocolate 
 2 cups prepared  whipped cream  (or  Dream Whip ) 
 1 tsp grated  lime rind 
 1 tbsp  lime juice 
 ½ cup  powdered sugar 
 1 peeled and puréed  mango 
 1 (250 g) pkg.  cream cheese 
 Directions : 
 In an electric mixer, at medium speed,  cream cheese  with  mango ,  powdered sugar ,  lime juice  and grated  lime rind  until smooth. 
 Fold in 2 cups prepared  Dream Whip . 
 Spoon into  chocolate  cups and refrigerate. 
 Chocolate cups : 
 Melt  white  or  bittersweet chocolate . 
 Using a small spoon, lightly coat the inside of 8 foil-lined baking cups. 
 Freeze for 10 minutes. 
 Recoat any thin spots and freeze 1 hour.
