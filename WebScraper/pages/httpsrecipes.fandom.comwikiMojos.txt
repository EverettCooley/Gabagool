Description : 
 Deep-fried potatoes
 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group This Y-group is international. Good food from all parts of the world. A place where we can share the wonderful food from all over the world. 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  flour 
 1 teaspoon  garlic salt 
 ¾ teaspoon  pepper 
 ¼ teaspoon  celery salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 4 medium  potatoes , unpeeled but scrubbed 
 1  egg , beaten 
 vegetable oil  (for frying) 
 ranch dressing  or  sour cream , optional 
 Directions : 
 Mix  flour  and spices in shallow dish. 
 Cut  potatoes  into ½-inch thick wedges. 
 Dip  potato  slices into the beaten  egg , then into  flour  mixture. 
 Deep fry in hot  oil  (about 375ºF) until golden brown, about 5 to 7 minutes. 
 Serve with dressing or  sour cream .
