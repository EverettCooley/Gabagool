 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 3 oz  light rum 
 3 tbsp  coconut milk 
 5 tbsp crushed  pineapple 
 2 cubes  ice

 Directions 
 Blend at high speed until smooth. 
 Strain into a glass and serve with a straw. 
 Top with a pineapple wedge and a cherry.
