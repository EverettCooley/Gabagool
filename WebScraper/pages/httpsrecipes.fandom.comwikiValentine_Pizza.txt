 Ingredients : 
 Ready made pizza, the kind that comes already with the tomato sauce 
 red peppers  or roasted  red peppers 
 cheese 
 Directions : 
 Start with the ready made pizza. 
 You Than Add Your Own Toppings.  red peppers  or even roasted  red peppers  are a nice touch. Cut them into heart shapes using large cookie heart shaped cutters. 
 You then broil them a few minutes if you are adding  cheese , otherwise just serve them cold.
