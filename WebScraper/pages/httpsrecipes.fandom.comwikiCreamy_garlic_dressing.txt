 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup evaporated skim  milk 
 2 tbsp fresh  lemon juice 
 2 large  garlic  cloves, minced 
 1/4 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp dried  dill weed 
 1 tsp  apple juice  concentrate 
 -frozen 
 1/4 tsp  paprika 
 1/4 tsp  white pepper 
 2 dash  sesame oil 
 ds red( Cayenne )  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Place all ingredients in a blender and process until smooth. Chill before serving. Food exchange per serving: free; calories: 11; cho: 0 mg; car: 2g; sod: 50 mg; fat: 0g; makes about ¾ cup; 12 servings (1-tb each)
