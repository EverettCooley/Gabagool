 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup  butter  or  margarine 
 4 lbs  Halibut 
 1 1/2 tsp  garlic powder 
 1/2 tsp  dill 
 1 1/2 cup Shredded  Cheddar   Cheese 
 almonds  (for garnish) 
 Directions : 
 1. Take a 13x9x2" baking dish. 
 2. Melt ½ cup  butter  in the dish. 
 3. Put the  Halibut  on top of the melted  butter , sprinkle with  garlic powder  and  dill . 
 4. Spread 1½ cups shredded  Cheese  on top of the  Halibut . 
 5. Garnish with  nuts , chopped or silvered. 
 6. Bake at 350 °F for 45 minutes or until can be separated easily with a fork. 
 7. Serves Margie, juneau, Alaska
