 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 10 oz biscuit 
 5 tsp  margarine 
 5 tsp  brown sugar 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 1/2 cup  Raisins 
 2 tbsp  Raisins 
 5 tsp  powdered sugar 
 2 tsp  water 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 400 °F. Spray 8x8x2 inch baking pan; set aside. Separate biscuits; using fingers, flatten each into 3' circle. Brush each circle with an equal amount of the  margarine , sprinkle each with ¼ t  brown sugar  and an equal amount of  cinnamon , then top each with 1T  Raisins . Roll each circle jelly-roll fashion and arrange in two rows, seam side down, in sprayed pan. Bake until rolls are puffed and golden brown, 8-12 minutes. Transfer pan to wire rack and lets rolls cool in pan.
