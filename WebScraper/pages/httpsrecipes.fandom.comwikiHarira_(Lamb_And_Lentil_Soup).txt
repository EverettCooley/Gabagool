 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 175g (6oz)  red lentils 
 100g (4oz) boneless  Lamb , cut into 2.5 cm(1 inch) cubes 
 400g (14oz) can chopped  tomatoes 
 1 large  Onion , roughly chopped 
 1 tablespoon mixed Arabic spices 
 1 small green  chilli , finely chopped 
 3 tablespoons coriander, finely chopped 
 ½ teaspoon * saffron 
 2 teaspoons  salt 
 1.2litre (2 pints) boiling  water 
 Directions : 
 Wash and rinse  Lentils , then soak in plenty of  water  for 30 minutes then strain. 
 Pour 1.2 litre (2pints) boiling  water  into a large saucepan and place on medium heat. Add the  Lentils ,  onions  and  Lamb . Boil for 25 minutes, stirring from time to time. 
 Next add the chopped  tomatoes , green  chilli  and spices to the pan, cover and simmer for 30 minutes. 
 Add the coriander,  saffron ,  salt  and  pepper , mix, cover and simmer on very low heat for a further 5 minutes.
