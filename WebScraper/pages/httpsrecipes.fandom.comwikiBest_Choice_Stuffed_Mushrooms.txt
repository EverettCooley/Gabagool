Ingredients : 
 ½ Cup Best Choice Italian-style dried bread crumbs 
 ½ Cup Best Choice grated parmesan cheese 
 2 Garlic cloves, peeled and minced 
 2 Tbsp chopped fresh Italian parsley leaves 
 1 Tbsp chopped fresh mint leaves 
 Best Choice salt and pepper to taste 
 ⅓ Cup Best Choice extra-virgin olive oil 
 28 Large (2 ½ inch diameter) white mushrooms, stemmed 
 Procedure : 
 Preheat the oven to 400’f. Stir the breadcrumbs, parmesan cheese, garlic, parsley, mint, salt, pepper and 2 tbsp of the olive oil in a medium bowl to blend. Drizzle a heavy large baking sheet with about 1 tbsp olive oil, to coat. Spoon the filling into the mushroom cavities and arrange on the baking sheet, cavity side up. Drizzle remaining oil over the filling in each mushroom. Bake until the mushrooms are tender and the filling is heated through and golden on top, about 25 minutes. Serve warm.
