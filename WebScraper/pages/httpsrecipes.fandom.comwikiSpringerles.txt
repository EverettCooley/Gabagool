Ingredients : 
       Springerles     
 1 pound  powdered sugar 
 4  eggs 
 2 knife-points of cleaned  potash 
 rind  of 1 lemon 
 1 pound of fine  flour 
 Directions : 
 Stir very thoroughly together 1 pound  powdered sugar  and 4  eggs . 
 Next, add 2 knife-points of cleaned  potash , and the grated rind of 1  lemon . 
 When well mixed add 1 pound of fine  flour . 
 Roll out the dough Â¼ inch thick, and be careful to  flour  the moulds well before pressing out the cakes. 
 Remove carefully from the moulds and lay on a board covered with anise-seed, figure-side up. 
 Stand aside for 12 hours in a cold place, then place in pans and bake in a hot oven. 
 
 
 Notes : 
 Work the dough as cold as possible.
