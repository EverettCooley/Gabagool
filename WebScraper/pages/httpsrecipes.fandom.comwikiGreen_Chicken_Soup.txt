 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup chopped  Broccoli  (can also add chopped  cabbage  or  spinach , but may need extra broth) 
 1/2 tsp  basil 
 1 tsp  parsley 
 2-3 cans Fat Free  chicken broth 
 1pkg Sweet Sue Cubed  Chicken  Breast 
 1 medium size  Onion  (cubed) 
 3 stalks  celery  (chopped) 
 2 small  zucchini  (sliced or cubed) 
 1 can  green beans 
 minced  garlic  or  garlic powder  (to taste) 
 
 
 Directions : 
 In a saucepan, heat  chicken broth ,  basil , and  garlic . Add all the vegetables and cook over medium heat until  onions  are transparent . Add 1 packet of cubed  Chicken  breast ( may also substitute canned  Chicken  or leftover baked  Chicken ). Simmer for 15 to 20 minutes, until  Chicken  is completely heated.
