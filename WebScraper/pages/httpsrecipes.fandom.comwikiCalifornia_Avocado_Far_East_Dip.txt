 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large  yellow onion , thinly sliced 
 1 ½ Tbsp  vegetable oil 
 3 Tbsp  ginger , peeled and minced 
 3 Serrano chiles, minced 
 3 Tbsp  cilantro , minced 
 2 Tbsp  sesame oil 
 1 ½ Tbsp  lime juice , fresh 
 3 large, ripe  California avocados , seeded, peeled and mashed 
 salt , to taste 
 Fried wontons 
 Directions : 
 Cook the  Onion  in  vegetable oil  over medium heat until  Onion  is golden brown. 
 In a food processor work bowl fitted with the steel blade, process the  Onion ,  ginger ,  chile ,  cilantro ,  sesame oil  and  lime juice  until the mixture is finely minced. Do not over process.
 Remove the  Onion  mixture from the bowl and combine it with the mashed  California avocado . Season with  salt .
 Serve immediately with fried wontons.
