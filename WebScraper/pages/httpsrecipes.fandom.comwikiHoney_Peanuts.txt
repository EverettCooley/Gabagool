 Description : 
 The sweet alternative to the unsalted peanuts - this honey-based peanut recipe has a golden secret: honey. But you can serve the sweet peanuts with other desserts or snacks if you want, while it then keeps a succulent aroma with the honey.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  water 
 2 cups honey or  sugar 
 4 cups raw  peanuts , shells and skins removed 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 300° F. 
 Combine  water  and honey (or  sugar ) in a saucepan. Bring to the boil, and stir until honey (or  sugar ) is dissolved, to make a syrup. 
 Add  peanuts . Continue to cook over medium heat, stirring regularly, until  peanuts  are evenly coated, and no syrup is left in pan. 
 Spread  nuts  out on baking sheets. Bake at 300° F for about 30 minutes to 1 hour until honey (or  sugar ) is completely dried. 
 Gently stir the  peanuts  a few times while baking. 
 Remove from oven and place on paper towels, let cool, and store in airtight containers.
