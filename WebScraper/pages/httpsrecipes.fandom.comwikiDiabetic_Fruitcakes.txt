 Description : 
 Purchased from the Wagner Estate in Wichita Falls, Texas in 1990. Date and source unknown.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  water 
 2 cups  raisins 
 1 cup unsweetened  applesauce 
 2  eggs 
 2 tablespoons liquid sweetener 
 3/4 cup cooking  oil 
 1-teaspoon  baking soda 
 2 cups  cake flour 
 1-1/4 teaspoon  cinnamon 
 1/2-teaspoon  nutmeg 
 1-teaspoon  vanilla 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Cook  raisins  in  water  until  water  is gone. 
 Add the  applesauce ,  eggs , sweetener and  oil  and mix well. 
 Blend in the other ingredients, which have been sifted together. 
 Add  vanilla . 
 Pour into greased and floured pans, preferably loaf pans. 
 Bake in 350 oven for 1 hour.
