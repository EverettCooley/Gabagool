The temperature tolerances on the Avocado page are listed as Celsius, when in fact they should be labeled Farenheit.  The numbers are correct.  For example, the entry reads that the minimum temperature for Mexicola avocado is 18 degrees Celsius, when it should read 18 degrees Farenheit.
