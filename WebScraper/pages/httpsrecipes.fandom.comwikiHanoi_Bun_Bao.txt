 Description : 
 Street food. Stir-fried beef, marinated in spices, and served hot over rice noodles.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1-11/2 lbs.  Beef  flank (can use  Chicken  or  Pork ) cut into thin slices and pounded. 
 Marinate with:
 
 ½ thumb  ginger  julienned 
 3 cloves of  garlic 
 2 tablespoons  water 
 ½ teaspoon  Sugar  ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 2 tablespoons vegetable  oil 
 2 tablespoons soy sauce 
 ½ lb. Dry roasted  peanuts  smashed in mortar and pestle  bean sprouts  - 1 lb.- blanched in hot  water 
 ½  head lettuce  chopped 
 herbs -  basil ,  cilantro ,  mint  - ½ cut each 
 2 tablespoons  oil  in frying pan 
 
 
 Directions : 
 add 2 cloves of chopped  garlic . 
 Add  Beef  and fry. 
 Serve  Beef  and  rice noodles , on top put :herbs  lettuce   bean sprouts  meat  peanuts  fried  onions
