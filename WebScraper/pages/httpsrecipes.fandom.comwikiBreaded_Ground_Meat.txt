 Description : 
 In Romanian: Snitel din carne tocata
 
 Ingredients : 
 1¼ lb / 600 g  ground sirloin 
 2 tablespoons  butter   or   lard 
 ½ cup  breadcrumbs 
 salt 
 pepper 
 Directions : 
 Mix the ground meat with  salt  and  pepper . 
 From this paste, shape 6 flat patties about ½ inch thick. 
 Dredge with bread crumbs and fry in hot  shortening . 
 Serve with French fries, mashed  potatoes  or boiled vegetables with melted  butter  on top of them.
