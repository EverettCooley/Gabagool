Ingredients : 
 200 g  red beans 
 200 g  onion 
 30 g  cilantro 
 salt 
 pepper 
 parsley 
 Directions : 
 Soak beans for two hours. 
 Cook in  water  until done, strain. 
 Add lightly fried chopped  Onion ,  cilantro , chopped  parsley ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Stew on low heat.
