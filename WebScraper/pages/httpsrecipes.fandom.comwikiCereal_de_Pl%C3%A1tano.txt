plantain  Cereal
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 large, green  plantains 
 3 cups  water 
 1/4 teaspoon  salt  (or to taste) 
 2 teaspoons  Sugar  or  sugar substitute 
 fresh or skim  milk 
 Directions : 
 Peel the  plantains . Grate very coarsely with a hand grater or in a food processor. 
 Save any liquid that may result from grating. 
 In a medium saucepan, stir together the  water ,  salt  and  Sugar  and bring to a boil. 
 Stir in the grated  plantain  and any juice. 
 Reduce heat and cook at a simmer until it is almost smooth, about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
