 2  eggs 
 3 tablespoons  flour 
 2 tablespoons  butter

 Directions : 
 Mix the  Cheese  with the yolks, add the  flour ,  salt  and whipped  egg  whites. 
 Make a flattened roll, about two fingers thick. 
 Spray a little  flour  on top and then cut in 1 1/2" thick pieces. 
 Place these dumplings in boiling  salt   water  and boil until they rise to the surface. 
 Remove with a slotted spoon, place on a plate and put some melted  butter  on them. 
 Serve hot with or without  sour cream .
