 Description : 
 In Romanian: Covrigei I
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 oz/250 g  flour , 
 5 oz/150 g  butter , 
 7 oz/200 g  confectioner's sugar , 
 2  eggs , 
 1/4 cup wine, 
 1/4 cup  rum , 
 vanilla , 
 vanilla  flavored  confectioner's sugar 
 Directions : 
 cream  the  butter  with the  Sugar , adding the  eggs  one by one, then  vanilla , wine and  rum . 
 Knead with the  flour  to get a medium soft dough out of which you shape pretzels. 
 Place on a baking sheet. 
 Bake at medium heat and sprinkle with  vanilla  flavored  confectioner's sugar  while still hot.
