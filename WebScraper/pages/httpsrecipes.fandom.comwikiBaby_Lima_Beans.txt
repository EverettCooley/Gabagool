 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  baby lima beans 
 4 cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Soak beans by bringing to a boil, then remove from heat and soak for 1 to 4 hours (best if left for at least 4 hours); or soak overnight. 
 Combine beans and  water  in a heavy kettle, bring to a boil; simmer for 1 hour, until tender.
