Ingredients : 
 6 slices  Bacon , cut into small pieces 
 3 1-lb. cans baked beans 
 1 8-oz. can  tomato  sauce 
 1 cup chopped  Onion 
 1/2 cup catsup 
 1/4 cup  brown sugar 
 2 tbs. prepared  mustard 
 1 tsp.  salt 
 5 drops bottled hot pepper sauce 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Bacon  until crisp. 
 Drain. 
 Combine with beans. 
 Add  tomato  sauce,  onion , catsup,  brown sugar ,  mustard ,  salt , and hot pepper sauce . 
 Preheat oven to 300. 
 Bake uncovered in beanpot for 4-1/2 hours. 
 Some like it hot! Adjust that hot sauce to your liking.
