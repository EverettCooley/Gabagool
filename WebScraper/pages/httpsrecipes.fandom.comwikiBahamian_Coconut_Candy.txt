Description : 
 This is a coconut candy.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups grated  coconut 
 1½ cups of  sugar 
 ½ cup of  water 
 2 tsp  vanilla extract 
 1 tsp  cinnamon  powder 
 ¼ tsp  baking powder 
 Directions : 
 Bring  water  to a boil and add  coconut . 
 Cook for 5 – 10 minutes and add  sugar . 
 Stir constantly and cook for additional 25 minutes until  coconut  hardens. 
 Remove from heat and add remaining ingredients. 
 Place spoonfuls on a plate or wax sheet and allow to cool and harden.
