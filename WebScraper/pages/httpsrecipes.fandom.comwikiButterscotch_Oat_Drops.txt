 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 c.  butter  or  margarine 
 1/2 c.  Sugar 
 1 c.  brown sugar , packed 
 2  eggs 
 1 tsp.  vanilla 
 2 c.  flour 
 2 c. rolled  Oats 
 1 tsp.  baking soda 
 1 tsp.  baking powder 
 1 tsp.  salt 
 2 c. butterscotch chips 
 1 c. chopped  nuts (optional) 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine  butter  and both sugars in bowl.  cream  well. 
 Beat in  eggs  1 at a time. Add  vanilla . Add remaining ingredients. 
 Mix and drop by spoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheet. 
 Bake at 350F for 8 to 10 minutes. Makes 6 dozen.
