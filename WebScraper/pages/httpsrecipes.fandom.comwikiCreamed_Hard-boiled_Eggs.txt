Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Dimmer estate in Rockwall, Texas in 1994.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 6 hard-boiled  eggs 
 3 tablespoons  butter 
 1 cup  milk 
 3 tablespoons  flour 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon  pepper 
 1 teaspoon  rosemary 
 1 teaspoon  thyme 
 Directions : 
 Peel eggs and slice into ¼ inch thick rings then melt butter in skillet and add milk. 
 Gradually stir in flour and cook, stirring constant for 5 minutes. 
 Add eggs then season with salt, pepper, rosemary and thyme. 
 Serve on hot fresh biscuits.
