Description : 
 Makes 36 cookies.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup diabetic  orange marmalade 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 ½ cup  shortening 
 2 cup  flour 
 1  egg 
 1 cup chopped  dates 
 ½ tsp  cinnamon 
 1 cup  nuts 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together well. 
 Drop by teaspoon on greased sheets. 
 Bake 8 – 10 minutes in a 350°F preheated oven.
