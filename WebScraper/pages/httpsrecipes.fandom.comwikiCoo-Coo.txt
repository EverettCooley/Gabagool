 Description : 
 From the Barbados
 
 Ingredients : 
 12  okras 
 6 cups  water 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tbsp Grace  hot pepper sauce 
 2 cups Grace  cornmeal 
 2 tbsp Grace  soft margarine 
 Directions : 
 Wash  okras , cut off stems, slice into rings and boil with half the  water  and  salt  and grace hot pepper sauce  for about 10 minutes. 
 Add the rest of  water  to the sifted  cornmeal  and mix well. 
 Remove saucepan from heat and stir in the grace  cornmeal  mixture stirring ingredients with a wooden spatula over medium heat. 
 Return saucepan to stove and cook mixture stirring ingredients with a wooden spatula over medium heat. 
 When mixture becomes stiff and smooth, and moves away from saucepan cleanly at the bottom, it is finished. 
 Put into a dish immediately and spread liberally with  butter .
