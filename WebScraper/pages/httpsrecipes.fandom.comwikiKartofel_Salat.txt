      Kartofel Salat     
 Ingredients : 
 3 or 4 large peeled boiled  red potatoes 
 2 bunches  green onions  chopped (the leaves and all) 
 2 medium  tomatoes  cut into small pieces seeds, juice and all 
 crushed  hard-boiled eggs , to taste 
 Miracle Whip , to taste 
 sour cream , to taste 
 Directions : 
 Cut cold boiled  potatoes  into Â½" cubes. 
 Add chopped  green onions , fresh  tomatoes , crushed hard-boiled  eggs  and miracle whip and  sour cream  to taste. 
 Mix until completely coated. 
 Cover and let sit in the fridge 3 or 4 hours to allow the flavors to blend. 
 Serve with hot or cold meats.
