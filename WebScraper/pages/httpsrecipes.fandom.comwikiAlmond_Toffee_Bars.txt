almond  Toffee Bars
 Categories: Cookies
 Yield: 24 Servings
 
 1 1/2 c  flour 
 1/2 c  powdered sugar 
 3/4 c Cold  butter 
 1 cn  sweetened condensed milk 
 1  egg ; Beaten 
 1 ts  almond extract 
 6 Heath Bars; Cut in Pieces 
 1 c  slivered almonds 
 
Preparation Time: 0:25
 Combine  flour  And  sugar  in Medium Bowl; Cut in  butter  Until Crumbly.Press Firmly Into Bottom of 9 x 13 Baking Pan. Bake 15 Minutes. CombineSweetened  condensed milk ,  egg , And  almond extract  in Large Bowl. Mix Well.Stir in Toffee Pieces And  almonds . Spread Evenly Over Baked Crust.Bake at 350 25 Minutes or Until Golden Brown. Cool. Cut Into Bars. StoreCovered in Refrigerator.
 
Enjoy.
