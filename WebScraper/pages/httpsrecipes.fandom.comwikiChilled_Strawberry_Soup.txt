Description 
 Fruit & Vegetable Recipes II  by the South Carolina Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Serves : 4-6 
 Ingredients 
 2 cups crushed  strawberries 
 ¾ cup  ginger ale 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 Directions 
 Mix berries, sugar, and ginger ale. 
 Refrigerate.
