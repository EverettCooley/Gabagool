Description : 
 Make this once yourself, and you'll never turn to the bottled stuff again. You'll save fat and sodium, to boot. The recipe can easily be halved. 
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Original recipe 
 Number of Servings: 6 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup reduced-fat  mayonnaise 
 2 tbsp finely diced peeled  cucumber 
 1 tbsp finely diced  dill pickles 
 1 tbsp minced  green onion 
 1 tsp  capers 
 Directions : 
 In a small bowl, combine all ingredients. 
 Refrigerate 10 minutes to allow flavors to blend. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per 2 Tbsp serving:
 
 68 Calories | 60 Calories From Fat | 7g Total Fat | 1g Saturated Fat | 7mg Cholesterol | 233mg Sodium | 2g Total Carbs | 0g Dietary Fiber | 0g Sugars | 0g Protein 
 Exchanges Per Serving: 1½ fat
