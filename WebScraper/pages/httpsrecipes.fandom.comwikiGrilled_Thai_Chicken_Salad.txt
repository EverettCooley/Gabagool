Ingredients : 
 6  chicken breasts , boned and skinned 
 8 cup  romaine lettuce , shredded 
 1 bunch  green onion , white part chopped 
 ½ cup  red onion , diced 
 ½ cup  cilantro , snipped 
 ½ cup  rice wine vinegar 
 ¼ cup extra virgin  olive oil 
 2½ tbsp  soy sauce 
 ½ tbsp  crushed red pepper flakes 
 Directions : 
 Preheat broiler and brush rack lightly with  oil . 
 Place  chicken  breasts on rack. 
 Broil 3 inches from heat source for 6 – 7 minutes or until cooked through, turning breasts over after 4 minutes. 
 Meanwhile, combine  lettuce ,  green onion ,  red onion  and  cilantro  in large bowl; toss to mix well. 
 To make dressing combine  vinegar ,  olive oil , soy sauce and  crushed red pepper  and mix thoroughly. 
 To serve, place greens on plates. 
 Slice  chicken  and arrange on greens. 
 Drizzled each serving with dressing. 
 Nutritional information : 
 200 calories.
