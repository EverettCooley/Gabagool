Description : 
 Contributed by Lil at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Weight Watchers? Annual Recipes For Success 2007 
 6 (1 Sandwich) Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (1 lb)  pork tenderloin , trimmed, cut in 4 pieces 
 1 (14½ oz) can diced  tomatoes , undrained 
 ½ cup finely chopped  onion 
 ¼ cup finely chopped  celery 
 ¼ cup  barbecue sauce 
 1 tsp  garlic powder 
 1 tsp ground  cumin 
 6 (1.6 oz) light wheat  hamburger buns , toasted 
 Directions : 
 Combine 1st 7 ingredients in medium saucepan over medium-high heat; bring to boil. 
 Cover, reduce heat and simmer 55 minutes. 
 Remove from heat. 
 Remove  pork  from sauce; shred  pork . 
 Return  pork  to sauce. 
 Place about ⅓ cup  pork  mixture on bottom ½ of each bun; top with top halves of buns. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving: 
 
 200 cal (18% cal from fat) | 20.7g protein | 4g fat | 0.9g sat fat | 29.8g carb | 6.3g fiber | 42 mg chol | 2.9 mg iron | 405 mg sod | 71 mg calcium  | 4 WW points
