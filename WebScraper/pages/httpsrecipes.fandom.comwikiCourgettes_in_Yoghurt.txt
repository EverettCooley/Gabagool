 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 Courgettes ( zucchini ), thinly sliced 
 salt 
 50g/2oz  plain flour 
 oil  for shallow frying 
 460ml/16fl.oz. Thick Natural  yoghurt 
 2  garlic  cloves, crushed 
 1 tbsp Freshly chopped  dill 
 Directions : 
 Place the courgettes in a colander, sprinkle with  salt  and leave in the sink or over a plate for 10 minutes. 
 Dry the  courgette  slices on kitchen paper then coat with  flour  on all sides. 
 Heat the  oil  in a large frying pan until very hot then add the  courgette  slices and fry on both sides until golden brown. Drain on kitchen paper. 
 Place the  garlic ,  dill  and  yoghurt  in a mixing bowl and mix well. 
 Place the  yoghurt  in a wide shallow serving dish and top with the drained courgettes. Chill in the refrigerator for 2 hours before serving.
