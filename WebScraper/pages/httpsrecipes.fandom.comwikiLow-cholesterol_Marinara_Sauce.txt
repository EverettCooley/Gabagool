Ingredients : 
 1 lbs  top round steak , ground 
 2 cup canned  plum tomatoes 
 2  garlic  cloves, minced 
 2 tsp fresh  oregano 
 ½ tsp  red pepper flakes 
 ground  pepper  to taste 
 water 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 2 tsp  tomato paste 
 3 tsp fresh  basil 
 pinch  sugar 
 2 tsp  olive oil  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Put about ½ inch of  water  in frying pan. 
 Add  beef  and saute until it loses its pink color, add  tomatoes  and juice,  onion ,  garlic  and  tomato  paste, stir well, simmer 5 minutes. 
 Add  oregano ,  basil ,  red pepper flakes ,  sugar  and  pepper . 
 Cover and simmer 10 to 15 minutes, until thickened slightly or to degree of thickness desired. 
 Add  olive oil  if desired for flavor, but it is not necessary.
