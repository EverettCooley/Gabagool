 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 peanut oil 
 1  Chicken , cut into bite-sized or serving-sized pieces 
 2  onions , cut up 
 1  garlic  clove, minced 
 1 cup  tomato  sauce 
 1 cup  Peanut   butter 
 3 cups  water 
 cayenne pepper ,  black pepper ,  salt 
 
 
 Directions : 
 In a deep pot, heat a couple spoonfuls of  oil . 
 Add the pieces of  Chicken  and fry it on both sides until it is nearly done. Remove the  Chicken  and set aside. 
 Fry the  onions  and  garlic  in the same pot. Add the  tomato  sauce, reduce heat and simmer for a few minutes 
 Return the  Chicken  to pot. Add the  Peanut   butter  and stir in enough  water  (add if necessary) to make a smooth sauce. 
 Add spices to taste. 
 Simmer on low heat until  Chicken  is done. 
 Serve with boiled  rice  or platains
