Ingredients : 
 8 oz  Pet Lite milk 
 4 pack  sugar substitute 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 add one of the following
 2  peach , peeled & diced 
 1  banana , mashed 
 4 slice  pineapple  with juice 
 8 oz  orange juice 
 2 cups  strawberries , crushed 
 Directions : 
 Mix all together and put in freezer of refrigerator until hardened. 
 Remove - break up - put in blender and whip until creamy. 
 Serve. 
 Can also be made in one of the small electric machines, or one of those that goes in freezer. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Calories: 75 calories. 
 Exchanges: ½  milk  and ½ fruit
