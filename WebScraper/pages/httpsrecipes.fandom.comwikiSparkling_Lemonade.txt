Description : 
 Source: 365 Easy Low-Calorie Recipes by Sylvia Schur and Vivian Schulte, R.D., Ph.D., copyright 1990, ISBN #0-06-016309-7
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 each  lemons 
 4 packs granulated  sugar substitute 
 1 quart  club soda  or seltzer 
 Directions : 
 Juice the lemons, then add the sweetener and club soda and stir.
