 Description : 
 Nice lunch, especially for little kids, or easy, make ahead, crowd pleasing appetizer.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (3 ounces) package  cream cheese , softened 
 1 teaspoon  worcestershire sauce 
 1/2 teaspoon  chives , chopped 
 1/4 teaspoon  mustard  powder 
 6 slices boiled  Ham  (thin slices) 
 10 ounces  rye bread , rounds 
 Directions : 
 1. Blend  Cheese ,  worcestershire sauce ,  chives , and  mustard , and spread mixture over  Ham  slices.
2. Roll up jelly roll-style, and chill for an hour or two.
3. Cut into 1/2" to 3/4" slices and serve with  rye bread .
