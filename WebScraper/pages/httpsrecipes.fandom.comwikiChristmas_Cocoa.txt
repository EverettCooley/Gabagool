       Christmas Cocoa     
 Christmas Cocoa  from Recipes Wiki—source of original recipe, licensed under the GNU Free Documentation License
 
 Cook Time : 90 seconds 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients : 
 6 oz  milk 
 Hershey's  chocolate 
 1 package raspberry  cocoa 
 ¾ oz  Chambord  liquor 
 ¾ oz  Godiva liqueur 
 whipped cream 
 Directions : 
 Heat  milk  in microwave for 1½ minutes. 
 Stir in  cocoa  mix and add  liquor . 
 Place whip  cream  on top. 
 Now you can enjoy the holidays.
