Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 ⅔ cup  water 
 ⅔ cup unsweetened  pineapple juice 
 ⅔ cup  lemon juice 
 2 tbsp fresh or bottled  lime juice 
 2 unbeaten  egg whites 
 4 cups finely crushed  ice 
 maraschino cherries 
 Directions : 
 Cook,  sugar  and  water  5 minutes; chill. 
 Shake all ingredients except  cherries  in jar until light and frothy. 
 Top with a  cherry . 
 Serve immediately.
