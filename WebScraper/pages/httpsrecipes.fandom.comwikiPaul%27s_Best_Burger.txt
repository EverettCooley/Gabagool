Description : 
 A great smooth burger!
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ sliced ripe  California avocado 
 1  egg 
 ¼ pound high-quality, low-fat  ground beef 
 fresh  kaiser roll 
 lettuce 
 fresh  tomato 
 "lite"  salt 
 ground  black pepper 
 PAM spray 
 Directions : 
 Before cooking prepare  avocado ,  lettuce  and  tomato  slices. 
 Sprinkle  avocado  with  lemon juice  so it won't discolor. 
 Keep refrigerated until burger and  egg  are cooked spray grill or frying pan with "pam" type spray cook ground  beef  patty (burger) to preferred colorness in separate pan start cooking scrambled  egg  toast kaiser roll (if preferred) when burger and  egg  are done, put burger on kaiser roll and top burger with scrambled  egg  (as much as you can fit), "lite"  salt  and  pepper  to preference 
 Top again with  California avocado ,  tomato  and  lettuce  and optionally, a dab of salsa sauce or a dab of  salad dressing  tastes good. Go for it! it is good!
