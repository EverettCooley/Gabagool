Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 6  eggs , hardboiled, cut in chunks 
 1/4 C  mango  chutney, large pieces minced 
 3 tablespoon  Mayonnaise 
 1 tablespoon  curry  powder 
 1 tablespoon  cilantro , minced 
 2 teaspoon Dijon  mustard 
 1  Granny Smith apple , peeled, cored, diced 
 salt , hot sauce to taste 
 2 tablespoon  red onion , minced 
 cucumber  slices 
 Directions : 
 Mix egss,  Apples , and  cucumber  smoothly. 
 Now make a dressing by combining chutney,  Mayonnaise ,  curry  powder, and  mustard . 
 Mix this dressing is salad with soft hands. 
 Season to taste.
