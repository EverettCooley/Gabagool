Description : 
 Here's a thrifty way to use up leftover cooked chicken or a bit of the holiday turkey by making Chicken Ã  la King. Serve this over buttered toast points, rice, biscuits or baked puff pastry.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup frozen  peas 
 2 tablespoons finely chopped  onion 
 1/4 cup chopped  bell pepper 
 1/3 cup boiling water 
 2/3 cup  flour 
 1 cup cold  milk 
 2 cups  chicken broth 
 2 teaspoons  salt 
 Pepper , to taste 
 1/2 teaspoon  poultry seasoning 
 2 cups cooked  chicken  or  turkey , diced 
 4 ounces  mushroom  stems and pieces, drained and chopped 
 1 tablespoon chopped  pimento 
 Directions : 
 Cook peas, onion, and green bell pepper in boiling water, covered, for 5 minutes. 
 Drain and save the liquid. 
 Blend flour with the milk. 
 Combine vegetable cooking liquid, broth, and seasonings; slowly stir into the flour mixture. 
 Heat the mixture until it comes to a boil while stirring constantly. 
 Boil 1 minute. 
 Add chicken and the cooked vegetables, mushrooms and pimento. 
 Heat thoroughly and serve. 
 Serves 6. 
 Things You'll Need : 
 Measuring cups and spoons 
 Saucepans 
 Cooking spoon 
 Strainer or colander 
 Sources and Citations : 
 Family Fare a Guide to Good Nutrition, #1, USDA
