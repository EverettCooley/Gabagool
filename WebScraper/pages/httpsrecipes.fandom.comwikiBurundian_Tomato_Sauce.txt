Ingredients : 
 a few spoonfuls of  oil  for frying ( palm oil  is most authentic) 
 2  onions , finely chopped 
 1 small can of  tomato paste 
 2 - 3  tomatoes , chopped and mashed 
 bay leaf 
 grated  nutmeg  (to taste) 
 salt ,  black pepper , red or  cayenne pepper  (to taste) 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in a frying pan. Fry  onions  for a few minutes. 
 Stir in all other ingredients. Add  water  as necessary to make a smooth sauce. 
 Season to taste. Stir. 
 Bring to a slow boil, reduce heat, simmer for ten minutes. 
 
 
 Notes 
 Serve with grilled or roasted  Chicken , or meat, or boiled  cassava  ( manioc ) tubers,  plantains  or  yams . 
 A few  okra  or a small  Eggplant  ( aubergine ) could also be included in this sauce. Minced  garlic  might also be added.
