 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large can or 450 grams baked beans 
 3 tablespoons grated  Cheese 
 4 tablespoons  tomato   ketchup 
 1/2 teaspoon  chilli powder 
 3 teaspoons  chilli  sauce 
 salt  to taste 
 To serve:  Potato  wafers or with salted biscuits 
 Directions : 
 Put the contents of the can in a liquidiser. 
 To this, add grated  Cheese ,  tomato   ketchup ,  chilli powder ,  chilli  sauce and  salt . 
 Boil for 5 minutes. If the mixture is too thick, add  water . 
 For serving, take out the dip in a small bowl. 
 Put the bowl in the centre of a serving dish, surround with  Potato  wafers or salted biscuits and serve.
