Ingredients : 
 3 large  mangoes 
 Â½ lb  lychees , canned / seeds removed 
 2 tbsp  confectioner's sugar  (powdered) 
 4 tbsp  lemon juice 
 12 large  ice  cubes 
 Directions : 
 Slice mangoes in half. Remove the seed, then scoop out the meat. 
 Drain the  lychees . 
 Add all ingredients in a blender. Puree to obtain a thick fruit shake. 
 Add a little  Grenadine  syrup, if desired. 
 Pour in tall cocktail glasses and serve immediately.
