Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 10/14/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  stew meat , browned 
 1  onion 
 1 can  carrots 
 4  potatoes , diced 
 2 stalks  celery 
 2 cans  beef broth 
 ¼ cup  flour 
 1 tbsp  thyme 
 1 tbsp  salt 
 1 tsp  pepper 
 1  garlic  clove 
 1 tsp  paprika 
 1 tbsp  Italian seasoning 
 ½ tsp  onion powder 
 ¼ tbsp  garlic powder 
 1 tsp  parsley 
 ¼ tbsp  oregano 
 2 cans  mushrooms 
 ¼ tsp  basil 
 2 cans  cream of mushroom  soup 
 1 tbsp  brown sugar 
 1 tbsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 2 cans  tomato sauce 
 Directions : 
 Combine all and cook in crockpot on low 8 – 10 hours.
