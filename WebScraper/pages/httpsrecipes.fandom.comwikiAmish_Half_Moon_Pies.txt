Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 6 half moon pies. 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  dried apples 
 2 cups cold  water 
 ⅛ tsp  salt 
 2 cups  sugar 
 1  orange , juice and grated rind 
 1 tbsp  cinnamon 
 1 (double crust)  pie pastry 
 Directions : 
 Cook the  dried apples  in a saucepan with  water  and the salt. 
 When  apples  are soft and pulpy. 
 Add the  sugar , orange  and  cinnamon . 
 Simmer until  water  is cooked away. 
 Roll pastry out and cut in 8 inch circles. 
 Place generous portions of the mixture on half of the pastry rounds; fold the pastry over and pinch the edges tightly together. 
 Bake in 450 °F oven for 10 minutes. 
 Reduce heat to 350 °F and bake about 35to 40 minutes longer or until golden brown.
