 Ingredients : 
 4 large baking  potatoes 
 3 tbsp  butter 
 ½ tsp  onion powder 
 salt 
 pepper  to taste 
 milk 
 1½ cups chopped  mushrooms 
 ⅓ cup finely chopped  nuts 
 ¼ cup shredded sharp  cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Wrap potatoes in foil. 
 Bake at 425°F for 55 minutes or until soft. 
 Slice off top of each and carefully scoop out potato. 
 Reserve skins. 
 Mash potato with 2 tbsp of the butter. 
 Add onion powder, salt, pepper and enough milk to moisten. 
 Beat until fluffy. 
 Melt remaining 1 tbsp butter in small skillet. 
 Add mushrooms. 
 Saute about 5 minutes. 
 Stir mushrooms and nuts into mashed potatoes. 
 Heap mixture in potato shells. 
 Arrange on a baking sheet and sprinkle with cheese. 
 Bake at 400°F for 15 minutes or until golden brown.
