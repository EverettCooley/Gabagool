Description : 
 Flat bread with fat
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 kg or 7½ cups  flour 
 2 cups  water 
 2 teaspoons  salt 
 40 g (1½ oz)  yeast 
 1 cup cubed animal fat 
 Directions : 
 Fry fat, drain, and cool. 
 Prepare dough as in flat bread recipe above working in pieces of fat evenly throughout the dough. 
 Divide dough into 40 g (1½ oz) pieces and roll into balls.
