 Prep Time:  20 Min
 Cook Time:  6 Hours
 Yields:  6 - 8 Servings
 
 Description : 
 
A multi-bean, beef and bacon dish that is good anytime of the year.
 
 Ingredients : 
 
 1 pound lean  ground beef 
 6 slices  bacon , diced 
 ¼ cup chopped  onion 
 2 cans (approx 16 ounces each)  baked beans , drain but reserve liquid 
 1 can (15 ounces)  red kidney beans , drained 
 1 can (15 ounces)  lima beans , drained 
 ¼ cup  molasses 
 ½ to ¾ cup  brown sugar 
 ¼ cup  ketchup 
 2 tsp  mustard 
 
 Directions : 
 
 Drain the juice in the baked beans and reserve to use as needed to thin mixture throughout cooking. 
 Brown ground beef with bacon and onion. 
 Put all ingredients in slow cooker. 
 Cover and cook on high for 1 hour. 
 Reduce to  low  and cook 3 to 5 hours.
