Description : 
 All in together, throw it in the oven and leave it  pork  and apple - traditional flavours but tastes so good together. 
 
 Serves 4 
       Apple and Pork Chop Bake     
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups root vegetables -  potato ,  carrot  and  parsnip 
 2 large  pork  shoulder chops  or  4  pork  loin chops 
 2  apples 
 1 cup  apple juice 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Cover the chops with the  apple juice  in a plastic bag and marinate for a couple of hours if possible preheat oven to 170 °C. 
 Layer the bottom of the roasting dish with a single layer of cut vegetables and  apple  (cut into small chunks/rings) if using shoulder chops, cut them in half (chops of 2 halves!) layer the chops atop the vegetables. 
 Pour over the  apple juice  marinade put a couple of the  apple  rings on top of the chops season it all with  salt  and  pepper  cook for around ½ to ¾ hour until  pork  is cooked - time will depend on type of oven and thickness of chops.
