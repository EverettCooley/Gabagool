 Ingredients : 
 2 	 	 Bacon 
 1 		small  Onion 
 2 	can 	baked beans 
 1/2 	cup 	root  beer 
 1/4 	cup 	barbecue sauce 
 1/2 	tsp 	dry  mustard 
 hot pepper sauce 
 fresh ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Bacon  with  Onion  in a medium saucepan until  Bacon  is brown and crisp. 
Add remaining ingredients. 
Heat to a boil, reduce heat and simmer, stirring often, until slightly thickened, 20 minutes.
