 Description : 
 Occasion: 	Any 
 Effort: 	Easy 
 Ingredients : 
 Peas  - Matr 1 pao 
 Onion  1 brown about 1/4 cup 
 Gariic 1 tsp 
 Chilly Powder 1 tsp 
 salt  1 tspHaldi 1/2 tsp 
 tomatoes  blended 2 
 oil  1/4 cup 
 coriander leaves 
 Green chillies 3-4 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  add the  onions  with all above  masala  and fry. 
 When  masala  done add a little  water  and Matr. 
 When Matr half done add blended  tomatoes   coriander leaves , green chillies and put on dam. 
 If needed add little  water  for gravy.
