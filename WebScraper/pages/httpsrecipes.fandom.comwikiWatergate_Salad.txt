Watergate Salad : 
 1 package Jello Pistachio Instant Pudding (4 serving size) 
 1 can crushed pineapple in juice (20 ounces) 
 1 cup miniature marshmallows 
 16oz cup chopped pecans 
 1 container of Cool Whip (8oz) - thawed 
 Directions : 
 Stir pudding mix, pineapple (drained), marshmallows and nuts in large bowl until well blended. Gently stir in Cool Whip.
 Refrigerate 1 hour or until ready to serve. Alternatively, freeze for 3-4 hours and serve frozen.
