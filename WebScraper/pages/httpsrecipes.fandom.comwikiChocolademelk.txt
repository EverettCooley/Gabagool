 Description : 
 This typical Dutch winter drink goes down well on dark winter nights, of after you've been  ice  skating!
 
 Ingredients : 
 Per cup:
 
 1 teasp.  cocoa powder 
 3 teasp.  Sugar 
 boiling  milk 
 whipped cream 
 Directions : 
 Mix the cocoapowder and the  Sugar . Mix in a small amount of cold  milk  to make a paste.
 Boil the remaining  milk  and pour it into the paste.
 Top with  whipped cream
