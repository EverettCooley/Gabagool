Description : 
 Cream dish
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 kilo  milk 
 250 g  sugar 
 80 g  rice flour 
 1  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Mix in a bowl the  rice flour  and the  sugar  and add slowly the  milk , taking care that the  rice flour  dissolves completely. 
 Pour the mixture in a non-stick saucepan and place it over the heat, stirring it continuously with a wooden spoon until it thickens. 
 As soon as it starts to thicken, add the  vanilla . 
 Serve the  cream  in a bowl and let it cool off.
