Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  matzo meal 
 ¾ tsp  salt 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 ¾ cup cold  water 
 3  eggs 
 Directions : 
 Combine matzo meal,  salt  and  sugar . 
 Separate the  eggs . 
 Beat  yolks  slightly and combine with the  water . 
 Add the liquid to the dry ingredients. 
 Allow to stand for 15-20 minutes. 
 Beat the  egg  whites until stiff. 
 Fold them into the matzo-meal mixture. 
 Drop by tablespoons onto a hot, well-greased frying pan or griddle and brown on both sides. 
 Makes 10-12 latkes.
