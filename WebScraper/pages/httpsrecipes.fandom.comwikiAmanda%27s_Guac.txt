Description : 
 It's an awesome guac recipe that has been thoroughly enjoyed by many  avocado  enthusiasts.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 medium-sized ripe  tomatoes 
 4  Hass avocados 
 1 small  purple onion 
 1 tbsp  cilantro 
 1 small  California chile  (or other  pepper  depending upon your heat preference) 
 1 small  lime  (not  lemon ) 
 tabasco sauce 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 In one bowl add diced tomatoes and  onion ; chopped  cilantro ,  lime juice ,and  California chile  pepper. 
 Mix well. 
 In another bowl halve and pit  avocado  and them scoop out  avocado  pulp into bowl. 
 Using a fork mask until creamy with some chunks. 
 Add  tomato  mixture to  avocado  mix well and  tabasco sauce ,  salt  and  pepper  to taste. 
 Serve with  tortilla  chips and enjoy!.
