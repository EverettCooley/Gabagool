Description : 
 Soon There will be more  zucchini  than you can use before spoiling, so I like to share this great recipe. You can substitute  egg  plant or
 yellow squash  for the  zucchini , they all taste great.
 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group This Y-group is international. Good food from all parts of the world. A place where we can share the wonderful food from all over the world. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 medium  zucchini  peeled and cubed 
 1 small  onion  chopped 
 1 cup drained  mushrooms  (optional) 
 1 can  cream of mushroom  or  chicken soup 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 ½ stick  butter 
 4 slices toast cubed 
 1 cup  cheese  cubed 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F 
 Peel and cube medium-sized zucchini and  onion  and place in saucepan. 
 Cover with  water  and bring to boil. 
 Cook till tender. 
 Melt  butter  in casserole dish. 
 Make sure  butter  has covered dish. 
 Cube toast and stir into  butter , remove buttered toast cubes. 
 Mix all other ingredients in a bowl, mix well and pour in ½ the toast cubes, mix gently and pour into casserole dish. 
 Place the rest of the toast cubes over the top. 
 Place in oven and cook till bubbly, approximately 25 – 30 minutes. 
 May place slices of  cheese  on top and let bake another 5 minutes to melt. 
 Also may add a little  cayenne pepper , or other herbs to taste.
