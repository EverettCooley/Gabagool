Chinese pickle is a small yellow fruit that is shaped like a cucumber. It has a crisp texture, and a mild sweet flavor. Typically it is preserved in honey and spices and sometimes soy sauce. Primarily used as a condiment. Also known as tea melon, sweet tea pickle and pickle. 
 
 Other Chinese pickles : 
 Chinese pickles includes a number of different preserved vegetables, namely:
 
 Chung choy 
 preserved Sichuan mustard greens 
 preserved Sichuan kohlrabi 
 preserved turnip root 
 snow pickle (also called "red-in-snow") 
 winter pickle (salted cabbage) 
 Zha cai
