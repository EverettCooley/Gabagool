Ingredients : 
 1/2 oz butterscotch schnapps 
 1/4 oz Bailey's® Irish  cream 
 1/4 oz DeKuyper® Sour  Apple  Pucker schnapps 
 Directions : 
 Pour the butterscotch schnapps into a shot glass. 
 Layer the Bailey's Irish  cream  on top, and finally add the DeKuyper Sour  Apple  Pucker schnapps. Serve.
