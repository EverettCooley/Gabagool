Description : 
 Serve tahini dip with fresh bread, warm pitta bread or as a dressing for salads, grills, or even in sandwiches.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 tablespoons  tahini  paste 
 2 cloves  garlic , crushed with  salt 
 juice of 2  lemons 
 4 tablespoons  olive oil 
 1 teaspoon ground  cumin  seeds 
 cold  water  to thin 
 chopped  parsley  for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Combine all the ingredients, beating hard. 
 This is best done in a food processor. 
 Add enough cold  water  to give a pouring consistency.
