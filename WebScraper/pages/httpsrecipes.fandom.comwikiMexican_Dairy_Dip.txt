Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves 10. 
 Ingredients : 
 Sauce : 
 1 pack  cream cheese  spread 
 1 can (14 ounces)  refried beans 
 2 teaspoons  chili powder 
 ½ teaspoon  cumin 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  hot pepper 
 Layer 2 : 
 1 cup  cottage cheese 
 1 large  avocado , peeled and chopped 
 2 tablespoons diced  green chilis 
 2 tablespoons  onion 
 1  garlic  clove, minced 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 Layer 3 : 
 2 cups  sour cream 
 1  tomato , chopped 
 ½ cup chopped  green onions 
 ½ cup sliced  black olives 
 Directions : 
 Sauce : 
 Blend all sauce ingredients. 
 Spread in bottom of spring form pan. 
 Chill one hour. 
 Layer 2 : 
 In food processor or blender, blend first two  Layer 2 ingredients  until smooth. 
 Add remaining ingredients, blend well. 
 Spread over first layer. 
 Chill one hour. 
 Layer 3 : 
 Spread  sour cream  over Layer 2. 
 Sprinkle with remaining ingredients. 
 Chill one hour. 
 Unmold and serve with taco chips or crackers.
