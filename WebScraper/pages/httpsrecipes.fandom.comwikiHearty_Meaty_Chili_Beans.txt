 Description : 
 a hearty and delicious chili that the biggest of meat lover will enjoy!
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb.  ground hamburger 
 1 package  bacon 
 1 package  sausage  of choice 
 1  yellow onion 
 1 small can  tomato sauce 
 1 can diced  tomatoes 
 1 can each of  pinto beans , dark  red kidney beans  and light  red kidney beans 
 2 tablespoons  taco seasoning 
 1 tablespoon  chili powder 
 1 tsp  garlic powder 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Dice up bacon and cook all 3 meats together. 
 Add chopped onion to meat and cook 3 minutes longer. 
 Add in beans (undrained) and seasonings. 
 Simmer on low heat for about an hour to allow all flavors to combine, 
 Add salt and pepper to taste.
