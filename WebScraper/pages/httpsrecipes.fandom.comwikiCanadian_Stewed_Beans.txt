Description : 
 Mm Mm Mm is what I said when I tasted this mysterious recipe of gods ! It's from a Canadian friend who loves to cook day and night, vegetarian love I'd say. Enjoy !
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups of  beans 
 1  onion 
 salt , bit of grassed  cheese  (fine) 
 1 small  turnip 
 1  carrot 
 3  potatoes 
 Directions : 
 Boil  beans ,  salt ,  cheese  and  onion  until  beans  are almost cooked. 
 Cut  turnip  and  carrot  into small pieces. 
 Cook well, cut  potatoes  small and put in about 15 minutes before you take  beans  off the stove. 
 Serve with fresh bread or fresh rolls.
