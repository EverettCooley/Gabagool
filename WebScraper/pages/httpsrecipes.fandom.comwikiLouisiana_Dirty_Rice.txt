Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 ounces chicken  gizzards 
 1 ounce  chicken livers 
 4 ounces  ground beef 
 2 ounces  ground pork 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 4 cloves  garlic , finely chopped 
 ½ cup chopped  celery 
 ¼ cup sliced  green onions 
 2 tablespoons chopped fresh  parsley 
 1 teaspoon  Worcestershire sauce 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 salt 
 ground  black  and red pepper 
 1½ tablespoons  lemon juice 
 1½ teaspoons  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 ¼ teaspoon  ground red pepper 
 2 slices  white bread 
 ½ cup  milk 
 paprika 
 Directions : 
 Put gizzards and livers in separate saucepans, cover with  water , and simmer until tender. 
 Chop fine and put in separate containers. 
 Cook ground  beef  and  pork  in  oil  until well done. 
 Add gizzards and simmer about 15 minutes. 
 Add  onion ,  garlic ,  celery  and  green onions ; cook until tender. 
 Add livers,  parsley  and  Worcestershire sauce . 
 Cook about 15 minutes longer. 
 Combine with cooked  rice . 
 Season to taste with  salt ,  black pepper  and red pepper.
