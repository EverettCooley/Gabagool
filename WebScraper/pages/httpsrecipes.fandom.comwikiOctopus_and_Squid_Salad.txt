Description : 
 Serves 4
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 kg very small  octopus  and  squid 
 4 tablespoons  olive oil 
 2 tablespoons finely chopped  parsley   mint  and  basil 
 1 tablespoon chopped  garlic 
 juice of 1 small  lemon 
 salt  and  pepper 
 2 small  chile peppers  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Clean the fish well and rinse under running water or buy ready baked clean fish. 
 Put in a pan and cover with cold water bring to the boil,lower the flame and simmer until the fish is tender. 
 Let the water cool before draining the fish into a salad bowl. 
 Season with all the other ingredients and leave in the fridge for a few hours mixing from time to time, this way the fish will absorb all the flavours.
