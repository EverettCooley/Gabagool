 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  honey 
 ¼ cup prepared stir-fry sauce 
 ¼ to ½ teaspoon  crushed red pepper  flakes 
 4 teaspoons  peanut  or  vegetable oil 
 2 cups small  broccoli  florets 
 2 cups small  mushrooms 
 1 small  onion , cut into wedges and separated into 1-inch strips 
 1 medium  carrot , cut diagonally into thin slices 
 Directions : 
 Combine  honey , stir-fry sauce and  pepper  flakes in small bowl; set aside. 
 Heat  oil  in wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. 
 Add vegetables; cook and stir 2 to 3 minutes or until tender. 
 Add  honey  mixture; cook and stir about 1 minute or until vegetables are glazed and sauce is bubbly. 
 Serve vegetables over noodles or steamed  rice , if desired.
