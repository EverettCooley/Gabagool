Salsa salad featuring Genuine  California avocados .
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 large ripe Genuine  California avocados , peeled and cubed 
 3 medium  tomatoes ,seeded chopped 
 1 Cup chopped sweet red pepper 
 1 small  Onion , chopped 
 1 to2 jalapeños  peppers , seeded and chopped 
 2 Tbsp minced fresh  parsley 
 ¼ Cup  lime juice 
 torilla chips 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl, combine the first eight ingredients, mix well. 
 Serve immediately with chips.
