 Ingredients : 
 200gr S&S  gari 
 150gr S&S  cornmeal 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 300ml  water  or  stock 
 500ml  water  or stock 
 Directions : 
 Place salt and pepper in heavy or non-stick saucepan and add gari, cornmeal, and sir until smooth. 
 Add stock, salt and pepper and cook over medium heat, stirring with a wooden spoon, for about 10 to 15 minutes, gradually the fufu will thicken and when very stiff it will form a thick mass then remove from heat. 
 Using wet hands mold the mash into flat, round or oval shapes.
