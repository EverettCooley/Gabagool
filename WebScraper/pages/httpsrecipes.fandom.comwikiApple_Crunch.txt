Description 
 Corn Flakes Fortified Ready to Eat Cereal  by the US Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Serves : 6 
 Ingredients 
 1 tablespoon  flour 
 ⅓ cup  sugar 
 1 teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 4 cups peeled, sliced tart  apples 
 2 cups  corn flakes , crushed to fine crumbs 
 ¼ cup  flour 
 ¼ cup firmly packed  brown sugar 
 2 tablespoons  margarine  or  butter , softened 
 Directions 
 Combine flour, sugar, and cinnamon. Toss with apples. 
 Place apple mixture in 2-cup casserole. 
 Mix the crushed corn flakes, flour, and brown sugar. 
 Blend with margarine until crumbly. 
 Sprinkle corn flake mixture over apples. 
 Bake at 400 °F for 30 minutes until apples are tender. 
 Serve warm, topped with vanilla frozen yogurt.
