Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  ground beef 
 1 medium  onion , chopped 
 1 tablespoon  chili powder 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 x 15½-ounce can chopped  tomatoes 
 1 x 8-ounce can whole kernel  corn , drained 
 1 x 4-ounce can chopped  green chiles 
 ½ teaspoon  garlic salt 
 1 cup (4 ounces) shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Cook  beef ,  onion , and  chili powder  in 10-inch skillet over medium-high heat until  beef  is browned, stirring to crumble. 
 Drain; return to skillet. 
 Add  rice ,  tomatoes ,  corn , chiles, and  garlic salt . 
 Continue to cook until thoroughly heated. 
 Sprinkle with  cheese  before serving.
