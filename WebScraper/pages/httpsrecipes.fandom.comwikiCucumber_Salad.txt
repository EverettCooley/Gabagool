Ingredients : 
 4 medium  cucumbers , peeled in strips 
 2cups cold flaked ( Halibut , bass etc.) 
 1/2 cup  celery , cut in 1/2-inch pieces and 
 1/2 cup  sour cream 
 4 Tbs.  lemon juice 
 2 Tbs.  Sugar 
 1/2 tsp.  salt 
 Directions : 
 Cut each  cucumber  strip in half lengthwise. Cut out soft centers with a  grapefruit  knife. Chop and set aside. 
 Cut a strip from bottom of  cucumber  half so that it will rest firmly on the plate. 
 Place the greens in a 2-quart bowl. 
 Blend the  sour cream ,  lemon juice ,  Sugar  and  salt  together. Stir well until there is a creamy consistency. 
 Place the  cucumber  boats on serving plates. Heap the green mixture to fill the  cucumbers 
 Add the seasoning to hold the salad together.
