 Description : 
 Serve with bread and tamia.
 
 Ingredients : 
 500g fuul musri (greenish-brownish-coloured beans) 
 1 tbsp  salt 
 2  dates  (optional) 
 1/2 cup  sesame seed   oil 
 1  tomato  (optional) 
 1  Onion  (optional) 
 1  green pepper  (optional) 
 50g jibna ( feta   Cheese ) 
 1 tsp shottar -  chili powder  (optional) 
 2  eggs  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Soak beans in  water  for at least 3-hours. 
 Drain beans and put in  water  and cook in pressure cooker until soft. 
 Pour into a bowl and lightly mash with an empty Pepsi bottle. 
 Add chopped  tomato ,  Onion ,  green pepper , fried  egg ,  salt , jibna,  oil  and shottar if desired.
