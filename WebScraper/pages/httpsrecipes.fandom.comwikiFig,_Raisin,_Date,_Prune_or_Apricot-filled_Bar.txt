 2.1   Dried fruit filling 
 
 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  sugar 
 ½ cup  shortening 
 1  egg 
 ½ cup  milk 
 3½ cups  flour 
 3 teaspoons  baking powder 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 Dried fruit filling : 
 1½ cups dried  raisins ,  dates ,  figs ,  prune ,  apricots , or other dried fruit 
 ½ cup  water 
 additional  sweetener  to taste, if desired 
 2 tablespoons  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 400°F. 
 First mix ingredients in order given, sifting the  flour  with the  baking powder  and  salt  before adding it. 
 Roll thin, cut, and put in greased pans. 
 Chill for one hour. 
 While dough is chilling, make the filling. 
 Dried fruit filling : 
 Cook slowly on the stovetop, stirring constantly, until thickened. 
 Allow to cool. 
 After dough is chilled and filling is cooled, roll dough out on a piece of parchment or silicone baking sheet. 
 Cut the dough into long strips about 3 inches wide using a dull knife. 
 Place the filling in a strip along the center lengthwise, then fold both sides over to cover the filling. 
 Roll the log over so that the seam side is down. 
 Bake 8 to 10 minutes, until just golden around the edges. 
 Let cool, then cut into 1 inch bars.
