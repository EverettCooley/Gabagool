French for 'in the Greek style'. The term describes vegetables cooked in a mixture of  oil  and  vinegar , or  lemon juice , with seasoning added. Also food prepared 'In the Greek style' and is used to describe a dish of mixed vegetables cooked in  olive oil , with  lemon juice  and  coriander seed .
