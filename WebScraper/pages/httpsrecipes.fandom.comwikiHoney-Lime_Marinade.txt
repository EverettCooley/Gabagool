Description : 
This juicy marinade starts with real honey and fruit juice, always delivers a tangy, rich aroma that it relishes, and is just great for meat, poultry, and seafood. With thick and rich flavor from the fruit juice, every teaspoon is savory, herbaceous fun!       Honey-Lime Marinade     
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil 
 ¼ cup lime juice, just including the zest from the lime 
 2 tablespoons honey 
 1 teaspoon kosher salt 
 1 teaspoon black pepper 
 1 ½ teaspoon cayenne 
 chopped cilantro, optional 
 Directions : 
 Whisk all ingredients together, especially including cilantro. If you like a stickier glaze, mix 2 more tbsp. honey with 1 more tbsp. lime juice. 
 Pour or funnel contents from the homemade, fruit-based marinade into a fresh glass or plastic jar/bottle. Use on meat, poultry, or seafood.
