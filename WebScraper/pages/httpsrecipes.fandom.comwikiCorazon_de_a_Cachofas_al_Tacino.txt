Description : 
 Hearts of artichokes wrapped in bacon
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: My old recipes 
 Serves 6 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pkg. ( 9 oz each) frozen  artichoke hearts 
 8 slices smoked  bacon 
 Directions : 
 Cook artichoke hearts as directed on package. 
 Drain and cool. 
 Cut bacon slices in 3 pieces each. 
 Wrap an artichoke heart in slice of bacon, stretching bacon so that it will cover artichoke completely. 
 Secure with pick. 
 Broil until bacon is crisp. 
 Serve hot. 
 This same method may be applied to  chicken livers ,  water chestnuts , large stuffed  olives , marinated  shrimp ,  oysters , and  pickled onions .
