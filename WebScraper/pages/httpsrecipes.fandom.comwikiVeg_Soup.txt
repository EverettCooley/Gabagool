Veg Soup  is a vegetarian soup with  tofu , with lower Cholesterol, healthier. 
 
       Veg Soup     
 Ingredients : 
 olive oil 
 1 large sweet  onion 
 1  green bell pepper 
 1 x 15 oz can of  corn 
 1 x 15 oz can of  chili beans 
 1 x 15 oz can of  green beans 
 1 x 15 oz can of  navy beans 
 1 x 15 oz can of  black beans 
 1 x 15 oz can of  black-eyed peas 
 1 x 15 oz can of diced  tomatoes 
 1 x 8 oz can of  tomato paste 
 8¾ cup  barley 
 ¾ cup  brown rice 
 extra-firm tofu 
 Directions : 
 Prepare the vegetables, prepare the liquid and cover the vegetables, boil the mixture, add  rice  and  barley . 
 Let it cook, covered, for 30 minutes, then add spices and  tofu , boil the soup and after several minutes it is ready to be served.
