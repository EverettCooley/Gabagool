Ingredients : 
 12 cup salted  water 
 6 med  sweet potatoes  peeled/diced, about 3 lbs 
 1 lbs  carrots 
 ½ cup pitted  prunes , halved 
 1 cup  orange juice 
 ¼ cup  brown sugar 
 ½ tsp  cinnamon 
 2 tbsp  butter   or   margarine 
 20 oz can  pineapple  chunks 
 1 cup  mandarin orange  piece drained. 
 Directions : 
 In a large pot bring  water  to a boil. 
 Add  potatoes  and  carrots  and simmer; uncovered about 15 minutes or until tender. 
 Drain and mash. 
 Place in greased 6 quart casserole with the  prunes . 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 Combine  orange juice ,  brown sugar  and  cinnamon  pour over the  sweet potatoes . 
 Bake, covered for 30 minutes. 
 Uncover and taste. 
 If the tzimmes tastes sweet enough, dot with  margarine  or  butter  and bake, uncovered 15 minutes more. 
 Otherwise add  pineapple  and  oranges , then dot with  butter  or  margarine  and bake an additional 15 minutes.
