 Description : 
       Quinoa     
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 480ml  water 
 175g  quinoa 
 Directions : 
 Place the  quinoa  in a large sieve and wash. 
 Drain well and place in a saucepan together with the  water . 
 Boil and reduce the heat, cover and simmer for 15 minutes. 
 Serve hot or cold.
