Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  bourbon whiskey 
 ½ oz  Campari ® bitters 
 ½ oz sweet  sherry 
 ½ oz  Rum Tree® liqueur 
 4 oz  lemonade 
 Directions : 
 Pour into an ice-filled highball glass, and serve.
