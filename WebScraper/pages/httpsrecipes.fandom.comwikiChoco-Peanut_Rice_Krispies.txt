 Description : 
 Jazz up that  rice  Krispies to make it more appealing to your kids, because  milk  isn't the only thing you can add to it!
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  Sugar 
 2 cups  corn syrup 
 1 1/2 cups  Peanut   butter 
 8 cups  rice  krispies 
 1/2 cup butterscotch pieces 
 1/2 cup  semi-sweet chocolate  chips 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix  Sugar  and syrup in a saucepan and bring to a boil. Add  Peanut   butter  and stir until smooth. Pour the  Peanut   butter  and syrup mixture over cereal. Mix quickly and thoroughly. Transfer in a greased pan and pat compact. Sprinkle the top with butterscotch and  chocolate chips . Use a spoon to lightly press down butterscotch and  chocolate  into the cereal. Cover and refrigerate for at least an hour. When ready to serve, cut into small bars.
