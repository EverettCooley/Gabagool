      Spicy Herring Potato Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 6 – 10 spicy  herring  fillets 
 6 – 8 boiled  potatoes , peeled and cut into slices 
 2 – 3  red onions , chopped 
 4 tablespoons  mayonnaise 
 4 tablespoons  sour cream  /  crème fraîche 
 4 tablespoons  coarsely grained mustard 
 1 tablespoon  syrup 
 juice of ½  lemon 
 red onion  and  cucumber  slices for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Cut the  herring  fillets into 1 cm pieces. 
 Stir together the  mayonnaise ,  sour cream  and  mustard . 
 Add the syrup and lemon juice. 
 Add the  potato  slices, herring and  red onion . 
 Combine carefully with a spoon.
