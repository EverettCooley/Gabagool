Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ oz  bourbon 
 ½ oz  amaretto 
 ½ oz  Southern Comfort 
 ¼ oz  sloe gin 
 1 splash  triple sec 
 1 splash  orange juice 
 1 splash  pineapple juice 
 Directions : 
 Pour all ingredients over  ice  into parfait or hurricane glass and stir gently. 
 Garnish with an  orange  slice.
