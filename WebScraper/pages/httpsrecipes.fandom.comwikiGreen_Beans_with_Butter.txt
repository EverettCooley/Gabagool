Description : 
 In Romanian: Fasole verde cu unt
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs / 1 kg  green beans 
 2 tablespoons  butter 
 salt 
 Cream of Wheat Croquettes 
 Directions : 
 Clean, wash and boil the beans in  salt   water . 
 Melt the  butter  in a pan, add the drained beans and the  salt  and shake the pan a few times so that the beans are mixed with the  butter . 
 Serve hot with  Cream of Wheat Croquettes .
