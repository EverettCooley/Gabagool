Ingredients : 
 6 cups sliced fresh  strawberries 
 2/3 cup white  sugar 
 1 cup  water 
 4 tablespoons  strawberry flavored gelatin 
 4 tablespoons  cornstarch 
 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust 
 Directions : 
 Mix together sugar, water, gelatin and cornstarch in a medium large saucepan; 
 Boil for a minute. Remove from heat 
 Fold in sliced strawberries, toss until well coated. 
 Pour into graham cracker crust; chill until well set. Â  
 Serve topped with whipped cream. 
 Notes : 
 Sift cornstarch into pan so you don't get white lumps in the glaze
