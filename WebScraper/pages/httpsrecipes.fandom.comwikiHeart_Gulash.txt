Description : 
 In Romanian: Gulas de inima
 
 Ingredients : 
 1¾ lbs / 750 g  beef heart 
 ½ tablespoon  flour 
 1  onion 
 2 – 3 tablespoons  lard 
 butter  or  oil 
 1 tablespoon  tomato paste 
 pepper ,  salt 
 Directions : 
 Wash and cube the heart, fry slightly in a pan with  shortening  and finely chopped  onion , add  flour , mix well and cover with warm  water . 
 Cover and simmer for ½ hour. 
 Add  tomato  paste,  salt ,  pepper  and let simmer until the sauce thickens. 
 Serve with hot mashed or boiled  potatoes .
