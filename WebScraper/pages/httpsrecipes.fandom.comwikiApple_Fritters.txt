Description : 
 This is a typical New Year's Eve treat. Usually there will be a bowl of both  Oliebollen  and Appelbeignets on the table. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 kg hard  apples 
 3 tbsp  sugar 
 cinnamon 
 200 g  plain flour 
 ¼ liter  milk 
 2  eggs 
 2 tsp  baking powder 
 Directions : 
 Core the  apples  and peel them. 
 Cut into slices and leave them overnight in the mixture of  sugar  and  cinnamon . 
 Beat the  eggs  with the  milk  and add  flour  and  baking powder . 
 The batter shouldn't be too thin, it should stick to the  apple  slices. 
 Dip the  apple  slices in the batter, let the excess drip off and fry in hot  oil  at 180 °C until golden brown. 
 Leave to drain and serve with a dusting of  icing sugar .
