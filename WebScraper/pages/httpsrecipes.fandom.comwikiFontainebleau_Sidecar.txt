Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz  bourbon whiskey 
 ½ oz  triple sec 
 juice of ½  lemons 
 1 tsp  Grand Marnier ® orange liqueur 
 Directions : 
 Mix or shake all ingredients, except  Grand Marnier , with cracked  ice  in a shaker. 
 Pour or strain into large chilled wine glass or snifter. 
 Float  Grand Marnier  on top.
