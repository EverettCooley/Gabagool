Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Love Estate in Sachse, Texas in 1984.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  cottage cheese 
 4 slices toasted  bread 
 1  peach  pitted and cut into 8 wedges 
 2 teaspoons  granulated sugar 
 1 teaspoon  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Spread cottage cheese on the slices of toast. 
 Place 2 wedges of peach on each and sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon. 
 Place under a preheated broiler for 3 minutes. 
 Serve immediately.
