Description : 
         
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups sliced  peaches 
 2 cups  oat bran 
 ½ cup  oat flakes 
 ½ cup whole-wheat  pastry flour 
 ¼ cup  slivered almonds 
 ½ cup  honey  or  maple syrup 
 ½ cup  sesame   tahini 
 1 teaspoon  almond extract 
 Directions : 
 Place peaches in a square casserole dish. 
 Combine the next four ingredients. 
 In a separate bowl, mix the honey or maple syrup, sesame tahini, and almond extract together. 
 Work both mixtures together until crumbly. 
 Spread evenly over peaches and bake at 375° F for 35 minutes or until golden.
