Ingredients : 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 1 tsp  vinegar 
 1 tbsp chopped  mint 
 1 pinch  sugar 
 sliced  cucumbers 
 Directions : 
 Mix together  sour cream ,  vinegar ,  mint  and  sugar . 
 Pour this over the sliced  cucumbers . 
 Marinate for an hour before serving.
