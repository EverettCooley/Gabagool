Description : 
 Contributed by Susan, Juneau, Alaska 
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 hard boiled  eggs 
 6½ oz canned  salmon 
 5 tbsp  tartar sauce 
 ¼ tsp  dill , dried 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 ⅛ tsp  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Hard boil and cool  eggs . 
 Split lengthwise, scoop out center, mash and mix with salmon, tartar sauce,  dill ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Fill  eggs  back up and chill.
