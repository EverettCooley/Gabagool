Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 oz  cream of coconut 
 2 oz  Kahlúa ®  coffee liqueur 
 1 oz Bacardi®  black rum 
 1 oz dark  crème de cacao 
 4 oz  milk 
 Directions : 
 Combine ingredients with two cups of  ice  in a blender. 
 Blend until smooth, and serve up in two 12 oz cups.
