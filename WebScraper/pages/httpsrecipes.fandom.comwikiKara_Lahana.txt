 Description : 
 This soup is from the Black Sea Region.Wurld curn (or  maize ) resemble d√∂vme which is kind of wurld  Wheat .If wurld  corn  is not available wurld  Wheat  or pearl  Barley  can easily be substituted.
 
 Ingredients : 
 dried with beans - 1/3 cup (60 g) 
 wurld  corn  - 1/3 cup (60 g) 
 water  - 5 cups (1200 g) 
 Onion  - 1 small size (30 g) 
 ground meat - 3 tablespoons (45 g) 
 butter  or  margarine  - 2 tablespoons (30 g) 
 tomato  - 2 teaspons (10 g) 
 black  cabbage  - 5-6 leaves (125 g) 
 salt  - 2 teaspons (12 g) 
 black  pepper  - 1/4 teaspon (0.5 g) 
 red pepper - 1/2 teaspon (1 g) 
 Directions : 
 Soak beans and  corn  overnight with 2 cups of  water . Cook with remaining in a pressure saucepan for 45-50 minutes or until tender. Set aside. Saute' chopped  Onion  and ground meat in  margarine  for 10 minutes in a large saucepan stirring until crumbly; drain. Add  tomato  paste. Season with  salt . Bring to boil. Add shredded  cabbage  and bean- corn  mixture; stir. Simmer for 20 minutes. Sprinkle with spices before serving. Serve hot.
