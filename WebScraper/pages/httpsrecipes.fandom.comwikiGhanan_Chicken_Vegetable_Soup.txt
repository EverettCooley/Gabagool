 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 Pounds  Chicken , cubed 
 1 Cup White beans 
 1 Large  Onion , chopped 
 1 Medium  bell pepper , chopped 
 2 Quarts  chicken broth 
 1/2 Teaspoon  pepper 
 1 Tablespoon  salt 
 1/2 Teaspoon  crushed red pepper 
 2 Cans  tomatoes , chopped 
 1 Large  Eggplant , cubed 
 Directions : 
 Combine,  Chicken ,  Peas ,  onions ,  peppers , broth,  salt ,  pepper  and red pepper in a pot. 
 Simmer for 20 minutes. 
 Add  tomatoes  and  Eggplant . Simmer for 15 minutes. Serve.
