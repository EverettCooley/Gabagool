Description : 
 Tirana fergese with veal cutlets
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  veal cutlets 
 ½ lb salted ricotta or  cottage cheese  or Greek F eta cheese 
 1 tbsp  flour 
 ¼ lb (1 stick)  butter 
 3 tbsp virgin  olive oil 
 onion , one medium-sized 
 3  cloves garlic finely choped 
 add  salt ,  black pepper , and  chili pepper  to your taste 
 Directions : 
 First, cut the  veal  cutlets into small pieces and then dice the  onion . 
 In a saucepan, preheat the  olive oil  and sauté the meat and  onion , for 15 minutes. 
 In another saucepan, melt the  butter  and then add  flour ,saute till lightly brown, add ricotta,or cottage or  feta  cheese, finely choped garlic, and  black pepper ,  salt  and  chili pepper  to taste. 
 Mix all the ingredients together (adding the sautéed meat and  onions ) in the saucepan and place in a preheated 350°F oven for 15 minutes. 
 Take out and serve immediately.
