Description : 
 Sauce for fish and seafood
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cloves  garlic , crushed 
 ½ cup  green onions , including tops, snipped 
 4 tbsp  parsley , finely chopped 
 1 tsp ground  cumin 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 4 tbsp  vinegar 
 4 tbsp  water 
 Directions : 
 Combine, then grind or process all ingredients into a paste. 
 Chill. 
 Serve over fish or other seafood.
