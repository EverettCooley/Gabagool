Ingredients : 
 1 jar or bottle larger than 32 ounces that can be tightly sealed 
 1 large piece of  ginger root  (1 ounce or more) that can be peeled and crushed 
 ⅓ cup  lime juice 
 peel of 3 small  limes   or  1  lemon 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 3¾ cups boiling  water 
 ¼ tbsp  yeast 
 ¼ cup lukewarm  water 
 Directions : 
 Combine the crushed  ginger ,  lime  peel, juice and  sugar  in the jar. 
 Pour in the boiling  water . 
 Cover loosely and let cool to room temperature. 
 Dissolve the  yeast  in the lukewarm  water  and add to the mixture. 
 Seal the jar as tightly as possible and let stand at room temperature overnight. 
 Chill, strain and serve.
