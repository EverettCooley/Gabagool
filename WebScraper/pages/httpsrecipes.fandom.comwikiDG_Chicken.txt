 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1  Chicken 
 4  plantains 
 4 tomates; 
 2  leeks 
 3  sweet peppers 
 3  carrots 
 1  garlic  clove 
 ginger ; 
 parsley 
 celery 
 palm oil 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Cut the  Chicken  into small pieces and season to taste. 
 Fry the  Chicken  in  palm oil 
 Meanwhile, peel and chop the  plantains  and fry them. 
 Cut all the vegetables into small pieces and then wash and drain. (Make sure you remove the seeds from the chillies) 
 Put all the chopped vegetables in a pan, together with the fried  Chicken . Add  garlic ,  ginger  and a cup of  water  and place under medium heat. 
 Simmer for 10 minutes. 
 Add the fried  plantains  and simmer until no  water  remains in the pan. 
 Add some  oil  and let cook for a further 2â€“3 minutes. 
 Serve hot.
