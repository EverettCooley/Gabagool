Easy Kahlua Cake
 1 pkg. dark chocolate cake mix
1 small pkg. chocolate instant pudding mix
4 eggs
3/4 cup oil
1/2 cup Kahlua
1 container sour cream (16 oz)
1 pkg. semisweet chocolate chips (12 oz)
 Preheat oven to 350. Mix all ingredients together. Pour into a well greased floured or sprayed Bundt pan. Bake 65 minutes or until toothpick inserted near center comes out clean. Cool. Invert to remove. Sprinkle with powdered sugar if desired.
