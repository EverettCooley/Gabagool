Ingredients : 
 500 g (1 lb) peeled, seeded  pawpaw 
 1⅓ cups  cream 
 ¼ cup  kirsch  liqueur 
 4  egg whites 
 60 g (2 oz)  caster sugar 
 Directions : 
 Purée the  pawpaw  in a blender or a food processor with the liqueur; chill. 
 Whip the  egg  whites until stiff, gradually adding the  caster sugar . 
 Chill, then whip the  cream  until a thick consistency is obtained. 
 Combine all the ingredients and serve in a glass bowl and decorate with the fruit.
