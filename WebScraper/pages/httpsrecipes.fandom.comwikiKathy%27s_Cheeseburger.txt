Prep Time:  5 min
 Cook time:  15 min
 Serves:  4
 
 Description : 
 I have never had anyone tell me they didn't love my burger
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds  ground sirloin 
 1 envelope Lipton  onion soup mix 
 2 Tbsp A1  steak sauce 
 1 tsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 2 tsp  Dijon mustard 
 shredded  colby  /  jack cheese 
 4 slices of  American cheese 
 Directions : 
 Mix first 5 ingredients together in a bowl and then divide into quarters to make 4 hamburger patties 
 Cook the hamburger patties in a large covered frying pan with Â½ inch of water in the bottom on a medium heat 
 Flip the burgers half way through cooking 
 When the burgers are cooked to how you like your burgers done top with a mound of the shredded cheese & then a slice of American cheese 
 Serve on large hamburger buns with the toppings of your choice.
