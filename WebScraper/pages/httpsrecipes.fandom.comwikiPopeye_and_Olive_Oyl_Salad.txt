Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 Salad : 
 4 cups clean torn  spinach  leaves 
 2 cups sliced  mushrooms 
 2 cups  cherry tomatoes 
 1 cup  alfalfa sprouts 
 ¼ cup chopped  green onions 
 Dressing : 
 ¾ cup  olive oil 
 ¼ cup  tarragon vinegar 
 1 tsp  honey 
 ¼ tsp  dry mustard 
 ¼ tsp  paprika 
 2 tbsp  water 
 ⅛ tsp freshly ground  pepper 
 Garnish : 
 2 hard boiled  eggs , chopped 
 ⅓ cup sliced  black olives 
 Directions : 
 In salad bowl, combine spinach leaves, mushrooms, tomatoes, sprouts and green onions. 
 In jar, combine dressing ingredients. 
 Shake well to blend. 
 Toss salad to combine and serve on individual plates, drizzled with dressing to taste and garnished with chopped eggs and black olives.
