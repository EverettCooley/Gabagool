Broiled  Steak 
 
This is a  Steak  Dip For Charring in an Electric Oven.
 
 1 Thick  Steak 
 1/3 Cup  oil 
 1 T.  paprika 
 1 T.  sugar 
 1 1/2 T.  soy sauce 
 As Desired:  garlic , Cracked  pepper  or Any Herbs You May Wish.
 
Blend  oil  And Seasonings. Dip  Steak . Broil as Close to Element as Possible, About 6 Minutes on Each Side. When Steaks Are Sealed And Charred, Place  Steak  in Middle of The Oven And Reduce Heat to 350 And Roast 12 to 15 Minutes or Rare, 15 to 25 Minutes For Medium And About 30-35 Minutes For Well Done. Serves 1
