Description : 
 Serves 4 
 Cooking time: 50 minutes 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds fresh  perch ,  butterfish , or  trout , cleaned 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 juice of ½  lemon 
 ½ cup cooking  oil 
 1 teaspoon  powdered ginger 
 Directions : 
 Season fish with  lemon juice ,  ginger , and  salt . 
 Arrange fish on a shallow greased baking tray and brush with  oil . 
 Bake in a preheated oven at 300°F until fish is brown and desired crispness is obtained. 
 Cook and serve with gravy or  hot pepper sauce .
