Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Duval Estate in Seven Points, Texas in 1999.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅓ cup plain  yogurt 
 ½ cup low-fat  milk 
 ½ ripe  banana  peeled 
 2 tablespoons  pineapple juice  concentrate undiluted 
 Directions : 
 In blender whirl all ingredients together 30 seconds to 1 minute until smooth and frothy.
