Ingredients : 
 1 Bream per person 
 thyme, 2 sprigs 
 Garlic 1 clove 
 salt 
 pepper 
 a little olive oil 
 flour for dusting 
 Preparation : 
 Clean and scale each fish, snip off fins. 
 slash each side. 
 season with salt and pepper. 
 slice garlic 
 place fish, garlic, thyme and a dash of oil in a plastic bag, leave to marinade in refrigerator for 1 hour 
 remove from bag dust with flour and shallow fry in hot oil until golden brown 
 serve
