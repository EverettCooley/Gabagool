 Ingredients : 
 1 can whole  asparagus , drained, reserve liquid 
 1 can  cream of mushroom  soup 
 1 small  onion  chopped 
 3 cloves of  garlic  chopped 
 grated  American cheese  to taste 
 salt ,  cayenne , and  black pepper  to taste 
 6 hard boiled  eggs 
 Zatarain's liquid crab boil to taste 
 12  pimento -stuffed  green olives , halved 
 5  green onions , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Mix  asparagus  liquid,  cream  of  mushroom ,  onion ,  garlic ,  cheese , spices, crab boil and  eggs  together in a bowl. 
 Cut asparagus in halves and arrange in a casserole dish. 
 Pour mixture over top of asparagus and bake for 30 minutes.
