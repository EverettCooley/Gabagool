      Arepas Fritas     
 Ingredients : 
 1  arepa  dough (made by mixing pre-cooked white corn flour, salt, and water) 
 1 c Ground  Pork  rind 
 1 c Grated white cheese ( Muenster  or  Paisa  work well for this) 
 Directions : 
 Knead  dough with  Cheese  or  Chicharrones . 
 Shape into a discs and deep-fry in hot  oil . 
 Drain  on paper towels and serve hot.
