Ingredients : 
 1 shot  gin 
 6 oz Mountain Dew® citrus soda 
 1 splash Sprite® soda 
 1  lime  wedge 
 Directions : 
 Use a highball glass and fill it with  ice . Add the ingredients and garnish with  lime .
