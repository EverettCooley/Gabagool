Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Poe Estate in Justin, Texas in 1987.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds  ground beef 
 16 ounce package Stove Top  stuffing  mix 
 1 cup  water 
 2  eggs  beaten 
 ¼ cup  barbecue sauce 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients except barbecue sauce well then shape into two oval loves. 
 Place side by side in rectangular baking dish and top with barbecue sauce. 
 Bake at 375°F for 35 minutes.
