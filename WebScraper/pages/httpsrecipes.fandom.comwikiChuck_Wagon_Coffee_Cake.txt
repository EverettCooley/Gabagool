      Chuck Wagon Coffee Cake     
 Ingredients : 
 2½ cups  all-purpose flour 
 2 cups  brown sugar 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅔ cup  butter  or  lard 
 2 teaspoons  baking powder 
 ½ teaspoon  cinnamon 
 ½ teaspoon  nutmeg 
 ½ teaspoon  baking soda 
 1 cup  sour milk   [1] 
 2  eggs , well beaten 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 400°F. 
 Mix  flour ,  sugar ,  salt  and  butter  in a bowl to make fine crumbs. 
 Remove ½ cup of the crumbs for the cake topping. 
 To the remaining mixture, add the remaining dry ingredients. 
 Add beaten  eggs  to the  sour milk . 
 Add to dry mixture and beat until very smooth. 
 Pour into a well-greased 8 x 12 x 2-inch biscuit pan, then sprinkle the crumbs on top. 
 Bake for 20 to 30 minutes. 
 Serve hot from the pan. 
 Nuts  may be added to the topping, if desired.
 
 Notes : 
 
 ↑   Make  sour milk  by adding 1 tablespoon  vinegar  to 1 cup of  milk .
