Ingredients : 
 ⅓ cup  vegetable oil 
 2 pkts  brown sugar substitute 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 1  egg 
 1 cup wheat flake cereal 
 ½ cup A-P  flour 
 ¼ cup  whole wheat flour 
 ½ tsp  baking powder 
 2 tbsp  wheat germ 
 ½ cup  raisins , chopped 
 ½ tsp  baking soda 
 1 tbsp  water 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients in bowl. 
 Last adding the soda dissolved in  water . 
 Drop by teaspoon on ungreased cookie sheet. 
 Bake in 350°F. 
 Oven for 8 to 12 minutes.
