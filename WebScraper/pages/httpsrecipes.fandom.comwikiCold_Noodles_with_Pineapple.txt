 Ingredients : 
 10 oz  somen   or   hiyamugi  noodles 
 ½ fresh  pineapple , chopped  [1] 
 Sauce : 
 4 tbsp  soy sauce 
 1 cup  coconut cream , boiled and cooled 
 juice of 1  lime 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 2 tbsp garlic oil  [2] 
 Garnishes : 
 3  green onions , chopped 
 2 tsp  red pepper flakes 
 2 tbsp  cilantro  leaves, chopped 
 2 tbsp  ginger , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Boil the noodles for 2 or 3  minutes  in  water  until  al dente . 
 Plunge into cold  water  and then drain. 
 Spread noodles on a large platter and sprinkle with the chopped  pineapple . 
 Mix the sauce ingredients and pour over the noodles. 
 Sprinkle with the  green onion ,  red pepper flakes ,  cilantro  and  ginger . 
 Notes : 
 
 ↑   Substitute  pineapple  tidbits for fresh if you want. You will need a large can. 
 
 ↑   Garlic oil can be made by frying chopped  garlic  in  oil  until golden. Use, as a guideline, 1 part  garlic  to 4 parts  oil . Do not strain the  garlic .
