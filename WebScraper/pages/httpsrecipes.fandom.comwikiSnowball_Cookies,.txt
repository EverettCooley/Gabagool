Snowball Cookies,  #1
 
 2 c  confectioner's sugar 
 6 c Crispy  rice  Cereal 
 1 c  Raisins 
 1 c  pecans ; Chopped 
 4 c Miniature Marshmallows 
 1/4 c  butter  or  margarine 
 1 ts  vanilla extract 
 1/2 ts  almond extract 
 
Place Confectioners  sugar  in a Large Bowl. in Another Large Bowl,Combine Cereal,  Raisins  And  pecans . in a Medium Saucepan, CombineMarshmallows And  butter . Stirring Constantly, Cook Over Medium Heat UntilSmooth. Remove From Heat; Stir in  extracts . Pour  marshmallow  Mixture OverCereal Mixture; Stir Until Well Coated.
 Using Greased Hands, Shape Mixture Into 1 - 1/2 Inch Balls. Coat WithSugar. Yield About 6 - 1/2 Dozen Cookies.
 
Enjoy.
