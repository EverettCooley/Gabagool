Description 
 Serves : 6
 
 Ingredients 
 1 pound  ground beef 
 2 peeled and chopped  carrots 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 ½ cup chopped  celery 
 1 can  condensed vegetable soup 
 1/2 cup frozen  green beans 
 1/2 cup frozen  corn 
 3 cups  mashed potatoes 
 1 cup shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 Directions 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 Lightly oil a 2-quart baking dish. 
 In a large skillet, over medium heat, brown ground beef, celery, carrots and onion until meat is no longer pink. 
 Drain off fat and discard. 
 Mix in condensed vegetable soup along with beans and corn and spread in baking dish. 
 Top with mashed potatoes. 
 Bake for 20 minutes to heat through. 
 Top with shredded cheese and bake 5 minutes more until cheese has melted.
