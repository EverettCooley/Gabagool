Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 c.  peanut butter 
 2 lbs. chocolate almond bark 
 2 c. dry roasted  peanuts , chopped 
 2 c. Rice Krispies 
 1 tsp.  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Melt almond bark and peanut butter. 
 Add peanuts, Rice Krispies, and vanilla. 
 Mix and drop into paper candy cups. The mixture will be soft at first, but 
 the candy hardens as it sits.
