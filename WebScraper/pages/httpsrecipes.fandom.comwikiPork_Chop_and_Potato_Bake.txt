Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Wee 10/21/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 6  pork chops 
 1 can  cream of celery  or  mushroom soup 
 ½ cup  milk 
 ½ cup  sour cream 
 24 oz  hash browns 
 1 can  French fried onions 
 salt  to taste 
 pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Brown pork chops. 
 Combine soup, milk, sour cream, salt and pepper. 
 Stir in ½ cup of the cheese, potatoes, and ½ can onions. 
 Spoon mixture into 9x13 baking dish. 
 Arrange pork chops on top. 
 Bake, covered at 350°F for 40 minutes. 
 Top with remaining cheese and onions. 
 Bake uncovered 5 minutes longer.
