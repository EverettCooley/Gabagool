Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 bread dough 
 sugar 
 Directions : 
 You take bread dough and if you have a stamper you make a hole in the biscuit. 
 Then you stick the doughnut on a skillet for 5 minutes. 
 Then take it out and put some sugar on it. 
 Then you just eat it. Remember to leave about 3 or 4 for your mom and dad. They taste good. Enjoy.
