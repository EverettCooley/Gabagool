Desription : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound fresh  green beans 
 1 tsp herb  vinegar 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅛ teaspoon ground  pepper 
 ¼ cup fresh  basil , thinly sliced 
 Directions : 
 Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. 
 Cook green beans for 5 to 6 minutes until just tender. 
 Drain well. 
 In the same pot over medium heat, melt the butter. 
 Add green beans, herb vinegar, salt and pepper; stir to coat. 
 Cook for 1 minute, then remove from heat. 
 Toss with basil and serve immediately.
