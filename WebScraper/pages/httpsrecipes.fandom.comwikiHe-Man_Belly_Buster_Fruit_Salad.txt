Description : 
 How do you handle a hungry man? 
 
 Recipe reprinted with permission from HealthRecipes.com 
 Ingredients : 
 1  mango 
 1  avocado 
 1  banana 
 1  papaya 
 1  orange 
 ⅓  pineapple 
 ¼ cup of  raisins 
 2  kiwis 
 2  bananas 
 shredded fresh  coconut 
 Directions : 
 Cut fruit into good-sized pieces, except  avocado , which is cut in small pieces. 
 Blend  banana ,  orange  and  Raisins  until smooth. 
 Mix well with fruit and sprinkle with fresh shredded  coconut .
