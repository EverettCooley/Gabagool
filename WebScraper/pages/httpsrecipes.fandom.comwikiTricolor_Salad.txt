Ingredients : 
 5 oz. organic  baby greens 
 1  Bermuda onion , julienned 
 1 large  portabello mushroom , thinly sliced 
 1 cup  red ,  green  and  yellow peppers , julienned 
 1 medium  tomato , diced 
 1  carrot , thinly sliced 
 Salad dressing : 
 1 tsp  Dijon mustard 
 1 tbsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 hot chile pepper sauce  to taste 
 1 tsp  sea salt 
 1 tsp fresh ground  pepper 
 1 tsp dried  basil 
 1 tsp dried  rosemary 
 1 tbsp  balsamic vinegar 
 1 tbsp  apple cider  or  malt vinegar 
 4 tbsp extra virgin  olive oil 
 3 cloves  garlic , finely sliced 
 Directions : 
 Combine ingredients for  salad dressing , mix well and chill. 
 Combine salad ingredients, add dressing and toss
