 Description : 
 Purchased from the Weir Estate in Canton, Texas in 1990. Date on card says 1944.
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 medium potatoes, cleaned and sliced/shredded  or  (2lbs. frozen hash browns) 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 2 cups grated  Swiss cheese 
 1 pint  heavy whipping cream 
 1 stick  butter , melted 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350° F. 
 Layer potatoes in a 9 x 13-inch baking pan. 
 Sprinkle with salt and pepper 
 Cover with the grated cheese and pour cream evenly over the top. 
 Drizzle with melted butter. 
 Bake for 1 ½ hours.
