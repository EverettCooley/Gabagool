Berry Delight Cake
 8 ounces cream cheese
8 ounces cool whip Lite
1 cup powdered sugar
2 pints strawberries, wash and slice
1 package strawberry glaze
1 package white cake mix
 Prepare cake mix as directed. Spray a 9 x 13 pan and bake until
light brown. Combine cream cheese, powdered sugar, and cool whip.
Spread over cooled cake. Mix berries and glaze. Spread over cream
cheese mixture.
