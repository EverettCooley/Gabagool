Apple Cinnamon Wine
 1 bottle dry white wine
3 medium Granny Smith apples, peeled, cored and finely chopped (about 4 1/2 cups)
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 t. ground cinnamon
 In a 2 quart container, combine wine, apples, sugar and cinnamon; stir until sugar dissolves. Cover and refrigerate 1 month to allow flavors to blend. Use cheesecloth to strain wine, reserving 1/2 cu apples for and apple-cinnamon cheese ball if desired. Serve wine chilled.Makes 3 cups.
