These scones are really good, they are like biscuits, and they go great with butter on them. They are a cinch to bake and make.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 1/4 cups  flour 
 2/3 cup  sugar 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 3/4 tsp  salt 
 6 tbs unsalted  butter , cut into cubes 
 1 tbs  lemon juice  plus enough  milk  to make 1 cup 
 1 cup  blueberries 
 1 tsp grated  lemon peel 
 1  egg , beaten 
 1 egg white 
 2 tbs sugar 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 400 degrees. Spread a sheet of parchment paper on countertop. 
 Combine lemon juice and milk 
 Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt in a bowl. 
 Add butter, mix until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. 
 Stir lemon juice milk mixture into dough, along with blueberries, lemon peel and egg until combined. 
 Pat half of the dough into a 7 inch circle on one end of parchment paper. Repeat, with remaining dough, spacing 2 inches apart. 
 Brush with egg white, sprinkle with sugar. 
 Cut halfway down but not through each circle, 6 wedges for each round. 
 Slide paper with dough onto baking sheet. 
 Bake 18 min. or until golden brown. Cool slightly. Pull apart at cut lines. 
 Serve warm or at room temp. 
 MAKES 12 SCONES.
