 Description : 
 Serves 4.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 kg thick  flank 
 500g grated  cheese 
 chopped  parsley 
 200g  minced pork 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Slit the flank with a sharp knife (like making a pocket ). 
 Mix all the ingredients well. 
 Stuff the flank with this mixture and sew it up. 
 Roast the flank in a medium to hot oven for 2hours or steam for 1 hour 20 minutes. 
 Serve with thick sauce.
