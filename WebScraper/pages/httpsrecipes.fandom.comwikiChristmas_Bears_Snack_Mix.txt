Ingredients : 
 2 	cup 	Bear-shaped chocolate graham snacks 
 2 	cup 	Mini oreos with red frosting 
 4 	cup 	Popped  popcorn 
 2 	cup 	Red, white & green candy coated chocolates 
 1 	cup 	Red & green gummi bears 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients in a large bowl. 
 Store in plastic bags or covered container. 
 Note: can change this by changing the chocolate graham bears and mini oreos for any small cookies or snacks desired
