Ingredients : 
 1½ kg whole  chicken 
 200 g  onion  - minced 
 100 g  garlic  - minced 
 100 g  ginger  - minced 
 50 g  lemon grass  - crushed 
 ½ liter  coconut milk 
 200 grams  dry chile  - minced 
 seasoning 
 Directions : 
 Boil minced ingredients,  lemon grass  and  coconut milk . 
 Add in seasoning and adjust. 
 Put in  chicken  inside boiling sauce. 
 Then place  chicken  on a baking tray. 
 Pour over the sauce on top of the  chicken  and grill in oven or under salamander until cook. 
 Keep the sauce and boil it again and adjust the seasoning. 
 Pour the sauce on the  chicken  again before serving.
