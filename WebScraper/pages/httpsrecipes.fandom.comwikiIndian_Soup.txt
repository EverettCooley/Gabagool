Ingredients : 
 2 pounds Kosher cut "round  steak " 
 3 pounds  potatoes , peeled and chunked 
 2 pounds  yellow onions , peeled and chunked 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 2 or 3 quarts of  water 
 Directions : 
 Boil meat and  onions  together til meat is tender; then add  potatoes . 
 Cook till  potatoes  are done. 
 Serve with fry bread.
