Ingredients : 
 2 lbs (about 2 large)  seedless cucumbers 
 1 tbsp  salt 
 Salsa : 
 1 lbs  plum tomatoes , peeled, seeded, and chopped 
 ¼ cup thinly sliced  scallion 
 1 large  garlic  clove, minced 
 1 pickled  jalapeño pepper , seeded and minced 
 1 tbsp  white wine vinegar 
 ¼ tbsp  sugar 
 Directions : 
 With a sharp knife cut cucumbers lengthwise into ⅛ inch thick "noodles" about ½ inch wide. 
 In a bowl toss with salt and let stand for 10 minutes. 
 Stir together the salsa ingredients. 
 Serve together.
