Description : 
 Cook Time : 20 minutes 
 Serves : 4 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp  butter 
 4  chicken breasts 
 1 can  cream of chicken  soup 
 Â¼ cup  water 
 2 tsp  lemon juice 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced or powder 
 4 cups hot cooked  capellini  or  thin spaghetti 
 Directions : 
 Heat butter in skillet. 
 Add chicken and cook until browned. 
 Add soup, water, lemon juice and garlic. 
 Heat to a boil. 
 Cover and cook over low heat 5 minutes or until done. 
 Serve over pasta.
