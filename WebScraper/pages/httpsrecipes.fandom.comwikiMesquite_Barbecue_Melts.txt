      Mesquite Barbecue Melts     
 Contents 
 
 1   Ingredients 
 2   Directions 
 3   Other Links 
 4   See also 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 package (about 1 pounds) HORMEL ® ALWAYS TENDER ® Mesquite Barbecue flavored center cut  Pork  loin filet 
 1 medium  red onion , cut into thin rings 
 1 large red  bell pepper , cut into thin rings 
 6 Kaiser rolls with  sesame seeds , split and toasted 
 6 (1-ounce) slices Sharp  Cheddar   Cheese 
 Directions : 
 Prepare  Pork  as directed on package. 
Meanwhile, in large nonstick skillet, sauté  Onion  until softened and slightly golden; about 8 minutes. 
Once roast has reached an internal temperature of 160 °F. remove from oven and let stand for 5–10 minutes before slicing. 
Slice filet into 1/4-inch thick slices.  
Top bottom half of toasted Kaiser rolls with,  Cheese , followed by the sliced filet, sliced red pepper,  onions  and remaining roll halves. 
If desired, serve with  dill  pickle spears.
 
 Makes 6 servings
