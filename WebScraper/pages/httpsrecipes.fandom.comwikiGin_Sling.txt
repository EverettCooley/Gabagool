Descriptoin 
 Gin Sling  from How to Mix Drinks by Jerry Thomas—original source of recipe, out of copyright
 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 1 teaspoon  sugar 
 ½ a wine glass of  water 
 1 do.  gin 
 1 small lump of  ice 
 nutmeg 
 Directions 
 In a small bar glass, stir the first four ingredients together with a spoon. 
 Grate a little nutmeg on the top of the drink.
