Ingredients : 
 4 slice high fiber  bread , stale 
 1 cup low fat  cottage cheese 
 2  onions , minced 
 13¼ oz can  evaporated skim milk 
 3  eggs 
 1 tbsp  parsley , minced 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 2 tbsp  bacon bits , optional 
 Directions : 
 Line 10" pie pan with bread. 
 Spread with  cottage cheese  and  onions . 
 Combine remaining ingredients except  bacon bits , in blender. 
 Cover; blend until smooth. 
 Pour over  onion  layer. 
 Sprinkle with  bacon  bits. 
 Bake at 425°F for 35 – 40 minutes. 
 Cut into 8 wedges to serve. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 Calories 129.9 | carbohydrate 14.0g | fat 2.7g
