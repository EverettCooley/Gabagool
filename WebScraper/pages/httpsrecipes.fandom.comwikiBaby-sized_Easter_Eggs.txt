Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  sweetened condensed milk 
 2 lb  powdered sugar ; approximately 
 ½ lb  butter 
 1½ tsp  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Mix  sugar ,  condensed milk ,  butter , and  vanilla  together (the amount of  sugar  depends on how much can be worked into the mixture). 
 When well mixed, form into 100 balls (or tiny  egg  shapes). 
 You can dip these into  chocolate  coating or colored coating. 
 Note: these can be made into candy Easter  egg  centers or just used as a regular creamy center for making bon bons! note: you may also add flavoring or coloring to the above recipe! enjoy.
