 Description : 
 Serve with Salad, pitta bread and hummous. This is a wonderful Middle Eastern-style dish.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 bag  spinach 
 ⅓ cup of  onion 
 vegetable oil 
 1 cup  tomato sauce 
 1 pint of  mushroom 
 ¼ tsp  black pepper 
 2 tsp  cayenne  (red pepper) 
 Directions : 
 Cover the bottom of a large pan with a thin layer of  vegetable oil . 
 Brown a minced clove of  garlic  and ⅓ cup  onion . 
 Add: 1 cup  tomato sauce  (or tomatoes). 
 Chop 1 pint of fresh  mushrooms  and add to sauce. 
 Season with:  black pepper , and  cayenne  (red pepper). 
 You may add more  cayenne  for a really hot taste. 
 Simmer for 25 minutes. 
 Chop  spinach  and add to sauce. 
 Cook until  spinach  is done.
