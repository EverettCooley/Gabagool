      Amish Yum-a-Setta     
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds  hamburger 
 salt  and  pepper , to taste 
 2 tablespoons  brown sugar 
 Â¼ cup chopped  onion 
 1 can  tomato soup 
 1 can  cream of chicken  soup 
 1 package  egg noodles 
 1 package  processed cheese 
 Directions : 
 Brown  hamburger  with  salt ,  pepper ,  brown sugar  and  onion . 
 Add  tomato soup . 
 Cook  egg noodles 
 Drain. 
 Add  cream of chicken  soup. 
 Layer hamburger mixture and noodle mixture in a casserole with  processed cheese . 
 Bake for 30 minutes.
