Description : 
 This is a recipe my catering friend perfected in 1991. I love this recipe and serve it on special occasions. 
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 large  salmon  filets, skinned 
 2 cloves  garlic 
 4 tablespoons softened  cream cheese 
 4 stalks of fresh  cilantro , chopped 
 1 teaspoon  kosher salt 
 Directions : 
 Peel and mince the  garlic . 
 Mince the  cilantro . 
 Thoroughly mix the  garlic ,  cilantro , and  cream cheese , and then set aside. 
 Butterfly the salmon filets. 
 Spread the  cream cheese  mixture into the salmon, and fold it back up. 
 Place on hot grill and cook for about 6 minutes on each side. 
 Sprinkle with  salt  during cooking.
