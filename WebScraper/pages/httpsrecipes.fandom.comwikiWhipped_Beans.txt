 Description : 
 In Romanian: Fasole boabe batuta.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb / 500 g  dried beans 
 3 qts / 3 l  water 
 3 large  onions 
 3 - 4 tablespoons  oil 
 Â½ teaspoon  paprika 
 minced  garlic  (optional) 
 salt 
 pickles 
 Directions : 
 Pick, wash and pre-soak the  beans  the night before. The next day, set to boil, let bubble for a minute, then drain the  water . 
 Replace the  water  with some warm  water . Let it come to a boil again and then again, drain the  water . Repeat this procedure of changing the  water  three times. 
 Let the  beans  boil in the third  water  until done. Drain and sieve. Fry the finely chopped  onion  until golden and add the  paprika . 
 Place the sieved  beans  in a bowl and pour all the  shortening  remaining from frying the  onion  over them. Then beat with a spoon until creamy. If the  cream  seems too thick, it may be thinned with a little liquid from the boiling of  beans . 
 Add  salt  and minced  garlic . Place the whipped  beans  on plates. Sprinkle fried  onions  on top and garnish all around the plate with pickled  peppers ,  cucumbers  or other pickles.
