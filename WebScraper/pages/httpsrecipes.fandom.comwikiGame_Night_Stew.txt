Description : 
 Contributed by  PressureCookerRecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  ground beef 
 3 – 4  carrots , sliced 
 2 – 3 ribs  celery , sliced 
 6  potatoes , peeled, cubed 
 1 envelope  onion soup mix 
 1  onion , chopped 
 2 x 10-ounce cans  beef broth 
 water  to cover 
 Directions : 
 In pressure cooker, brown ground beef until crumbly. 
 Add remaining ingredients and water to cover. 
 Lock lid and bring to full pressure. 
 Adjust heat to maintain pressure and cook for 8 minutes. 
 Quick-release pressure. 
 Serve with hot buttered bread and be on your way!.
