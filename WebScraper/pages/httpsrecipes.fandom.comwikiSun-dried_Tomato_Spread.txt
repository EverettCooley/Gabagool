Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Source:  The Diabetes Food and Nutrition Bible 
 Yield: 16 servings (2 tablespoons each) 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup fat-free  cream cheese 
 ½ cup fat-free  mayonnaise 
 3 tbsp  fat-free milk 
 ½ cup low-fat  sour cream 
 ½ cup rehydrated  sun-dried tomatoes , sliced 
 2 tsp minced  chives 
 1 tsp minced  thyme 
 2 tsp minced  basil 
 salt  and  pepper , to taste 
 Directions : 
 In a food processor, blend all ingredients until smooth but thick. 
 Serve with crackers or raw vegetables of choice. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving (2 tablespoons):
 
 34 Calories | 1g Fat | 5mg Cholesterol | 149mg Sodium | 4g Carbs | 0g Dietary Fiber | 2g Sugars | 3g Protein 
 Diabetic Exchanges: ½ carbohydrate
