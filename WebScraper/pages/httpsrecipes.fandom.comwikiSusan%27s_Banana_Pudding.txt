Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp  cornstarch 
 salt  to taste 
 ⅓ cup  granulated sugar 
 2 cups  skim milk 
 ⅓ cup  egg whites , (about 6 whites or ⅓ cup  egg substitute ) 
 1 tsp pure  vanilla extract 
 1 tsp unsalted vegetable oil  margarine , melted 
 4 drop yellow  food coloring , opt. 
 2 medium  bananas 
 3  vanilla wafers , crumbled, if desired 
 Directions : 
 Combine the  cornstarch ,  salt  and  sugar  in a small mixing bowl and mix well. 
 Combine  milk ,  egg  whites,  vanilla ,  margarine  and  food coloring  in another 6- to 8-cup container. 
 Whisk to blend. 
 Add dry ingredients and whisk all the ingredients together thoroughly. 
 Cook in microwave on high (100 percent power) for 6 to 8 minutes or until thick, stirring after every minute. 
 To chill, place wax paper directly on pudding; refrigerate. 
 To assemble dish, slice  bananas  on bottom of serving dish. 
 Add chilled pudding and garnish with crumbled  vanilla  wafers. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving: 
 
 calories 148 | fat 1 g | cholesterol 2 mg | sodium 97 mg | percent calories from fat 6%.
