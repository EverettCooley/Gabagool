 Murmura Namkeen Recipe  : 
 
 Prep Time: 10 min        Cook Time: 15 minutes          Serves: 4 ----
 
 
 
 
 Spicy puffed rice is a very popular Indian tea time snack. It is also known as Kurmura Chivda in some parts of the Country. 
 Peanuts and roasted nuts or chickpeas are the essential part along with puffed rice in this chivda. 
       Spicy & Crispy Puffed Rice-Murmura Namkeen     
 Main ingredients:  Puffed Rice and peanuts 
 Cuisine:  Indian 
 Course:  Snacks 
 Level of Cooking:  Easy
 
 
 
 Recipe Source:   Spicy Veg Recipes 
 Ingredients: : 
 1 cup Puffed Rice 
 oil to fry 
 A pinch Asafoetida 
 ½ teaspoon Turmeric powder 
 1 Green chillies chopped or to taste 
 10-12 Curry leaves 
 ¼ cup Roasted chana dal        
 Salt to taste 
 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder (optional) 
 1/2 cup Peanuts (with skin) 
 ½ teaspoon chat masala 
 Preparation:  : 
 Heat the oil in a deep pan to fry Peanuts over medium heat.  
 Put peanuts in the oil, fry on a low flame till they turn light brown then take them out.  
 Method: : 
 Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat. 
 Add asafoetida, green chillies and curry leaves and sauté till the curry leaves become crisp. 
 Add roasted chana dal and sauté for a minute. 
 Add turmeric powder, red chilli powder and salt and sauté for another minute. 
 Add puffed rice and mix well. 
 Add fried peanuts and chat masala and mix well so that all the mixture gets evenly coated with spices on low flame. 
 Our spicy crispy Murmura Chiwda is ready to serve. 
 Store in airtight container once it cools. 
 Note: : 
 There is no hard rule for the measurements in this recipe. 
 You can use ingredients according to your preference; Feel free to plus or minus the ingredients of your choice. 
 You can add a little sugar if you want or you can avoid sugar or adjust the quantity as per your taste. 
 You can also add cashews, almonds and raisins as per your taste.
