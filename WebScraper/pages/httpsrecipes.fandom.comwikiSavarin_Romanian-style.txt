Ingredients : 
 5 tablespoons  sugar 
 5  eggs 
 5 tablespoons fine  bread crumbs 
 grated peel from 1  lemon 
 juice from Â½  lemon 
 whipped cream 
 Syrup : 
 5 oz / 150 g  sugar 
 1 cup  water 
 1 tablespoon  rum 
 Directions : 
 cream  the  sugar  with the  yolks , add, mixing continuously,  lemon peel  and juice, bread crumbs and finally whipped  egg whites . 
 Pour the mixture into savarin molds (little bundt pans, with hole in the middle). 
 If you don't have this just use normal muffin tray. 
 Bake at medium heat. 
 Then pour a syrup made from  sugar ,  water  and  rum  all over them. 
 When they are cold, fill the middle hole with  whipped cream . 
 You may garnish with  sour cherries  or other fruits from preserves, on top of the  whipped cream .
