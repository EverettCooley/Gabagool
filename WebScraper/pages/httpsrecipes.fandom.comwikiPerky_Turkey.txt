Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 2 cups diced cooked  turkey 
 ½ cup sliced  celery 
 1 x 2 ounce jar diced  pimentos , drained 
 ⅓ cup  Dijon mustard  dressing 
 lettuce  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  turkey ,  celery , pimentos and dressing in large bowl. 
 Serve over  lettuce  leaves.
