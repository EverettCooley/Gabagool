 Description : 
 A  rum  drink
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz.  light rum 
 5 oz.  pineapple juice 
 1/2 oz.  blackberry   brandy 
 Directions : 
 Mix  rum  and  pineapple juice  with cracked  ice  in a shaker or blender and pour into a chilled highball glass. 
 Top off with  blackberry   brandy .
