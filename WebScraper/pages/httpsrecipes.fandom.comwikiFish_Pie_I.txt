Ingredients : 
 1 cup  onions , chopped 
 ½ tsp  pepper 
 2 tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  dry mustard 
 2 oz  butter  or  margarine 
 3 cups cold steamed  fish , cleaned from bones and flaked. 
 1 x 8 oz can  tomato sauce 
 2½ cup  mashed potatoes 
 2  eggs , beaten lightly 
 2 tbsp  water 
 Directions : 
 Sauté the onions, salt, pepper and mustard in butter or margarine. 
 Add fish,  tomato sauce , ½ cup of  mashed potatoes  and one beaten  egg . 
 Pack in a buttered 10-inch deep pie dish. 
 Spread 2 cups  mashed potatoes  over top. 
 Brush with beaten  egg  beaten thinned with  water . 
 Bake at 350°F for 30 minutes or until golden brown on top. 
 Cut pie in 8 wedges and serve.
