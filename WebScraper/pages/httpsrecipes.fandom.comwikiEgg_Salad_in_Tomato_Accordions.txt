Ingredients : 
 6 hard-boiled,  eggs , chopped 
 ½ cup finely chopped  celery 
 ⅓ cup diced  green pepper  ( capsicum ) 
 finely chopped  onions  to taste 
 2 to 3 tbsp  prepared mustard 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ¼ cup  salad dressing  or  mayonnaise 
 6 medium  tomatoes 
 dash of  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Combine  egg ,  celery ,  green pepper ,  onions ,  mustard ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Add  salad dressing . 
 Mix well and chill. 
 Turn  tomato  stem end down. 
 Without cutting to bottom, cut down, making 6 slices. 
 Sprinkle cut surface with  salt . 
 Fill between slices with  egg  salad. 
 Arrange on crisp salad greens. 
 Garnish with  parsley .
