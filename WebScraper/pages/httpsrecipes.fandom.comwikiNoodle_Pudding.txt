Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb pkg. broad  noodles 
 ¼ lb  butter  or  margarine 
 1 lb  cottage cheese   or  ¾ lb  cottage  and ¼ lb  creamed 
 4  eggs 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 ¾ cup  milk 
 ¾ tsp  salt 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 ¼ tsp  cinnamon 
 Topping : 
 ⅓ cup  corn flakes 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 ¼ tsp  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Boil noodles according to directions; drain well. 
 Add butter. 
 Add cheese; mix well. 
 Add eggs, one at a time, mix well after each egg. 
 Finally add remaining ingredients, blend well. 
 Pour into well buttered large pyrex dish or casserole, 9 x 13 inch. 
 Crush corn flakes and mix with sugar and cinnamon. 
 Sprinkle top of pudding. 
 Bake at 350°F for 1 hour. 
 Can be served as a dessert with strawberries or crushed pineapple and sour cream.
