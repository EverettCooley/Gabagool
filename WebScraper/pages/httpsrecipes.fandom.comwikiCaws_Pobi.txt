Description : 
 Welsh Rarebit
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 oz grated  cheddar  cheese 
 3 tbsp  milk 
 dash of  mustard 
 1  tomato 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 bread slices 
 Directions : 
 Blend the grated cheese, milk, mustard, salt and pepper. 
 Spread this evenly (and thickly) over the bread slices. 
 Place on a flat cooking pan, and broil until bubbly and starting to brown. 
 Slice the tomato thinly, and place one slice on each piece of bread.
