 Description : 
 This is wonderful for entertaining or cut the recipe in half for a smaller group. Its fast and easy. You can also buy 1 pound of  Shrimp , steam and decorate the edge of the bowl with them.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 (8 ounces) packages Philadelphia  cream cheese 
 2 (10 ounces) cans  cream  of  Shrimp  soup 
 3 (7.5 ounces) cans deveined medium  Shrimp 
 Directions : 
 1. Soften  cream cheese .
2. Beat together with  cream  of  Shrimp  Soup until smooth.
3. Thoroughly clean  Shrimp  (rinse in cold  water  and make sure all veins are removed).
4. Stir in  Shrimp .
5. Refrigerate.
6. Serve with  Potato  chips, fancy crackers, veggies, your choice, a combo of all works great.
