Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz Myer's or  spiced rum 
 1 oz  coconut rum 
 ½ oz  crème de banane 
 splash of  orange juice  and  pineapple juice 
 Directions : 
 Blend and serve in hurricane or tall glass, garnish with a flag.
