Description : 
 Purchased from Vega Estate in Longview, Texas in 1990. Dated 1952.
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can  cream of celery  soup 
 1 cup  milk 
 1 can  water  packed  tuna , drained and flaked 
 1 cup uncooked elbow  macaroni 
 3 tablespoons  mustard 
 1 tablespoon  sour cream 
 1 cup fresh  peas 
 ½ cup chopped  yellow onion 
 1 cup grated  Swiss cheese 
 Directions : 
 Whisk soup and  milk  in sauce pan until blended. 
 Add remaining ingredients except ¼ cup  cheese . 
 Cover and refrigerate overnight. 
 Bake in 350°F oven for 30 minutes. 
 Sprinkle remaining  cheese  on top and place back in oven for 1-2 minutes while  cheese  melts.
