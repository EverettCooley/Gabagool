      English Breakfast     
 Description : 
 Diabetics do not omit the Baked Beans, as these greatly lower the  G.I.  of the meal.
 
 Serves:  1 
 Ingredients : 
 2  eggs 
 3 slices  Canadian bacon 
 2  sausage  links, brown and serve 
 1  roma tomato , halved lengthwise 
 2 ounces fresh  mushrooms , quartered 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 1 small tin sugar free  baked beans 
 salt  and  pepper , to taste 
 Directions : 
 Heat the baked beans in a saucepan. 
 Heat the butter in a large skillet. 
 Saute the mushrooms on medium-high heat until tender, season to taste with salt and pepper. 
 Remove from pan and keep warm. 
 In same skillet, place the tomatoes, bacon and sausage links. 
 Cook until heated through and browned. 
 Turn to cook both sides of tomato and meats. 
 Sprinkle tomatoes with salt and pepper. 
 Meanwhile, fry or scramble eggs in the remaining butter in a small skillet. 
 Arrange all on dinner plate and eat. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per Serving: 
 
 592 Calories | 48g Fat | 33g Protein | 9g Carbohydrate | 2g Dietary Fiber | 7g Net Carbs
