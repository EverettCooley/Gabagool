 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 small  grapefruit , peeled 
 3  oranges , peeled 
 2/3 c.  wine vinegar 
 1/2 tsp.  salt 
 1/3 c.  Sugar 
 1 medium  cucumber , thinly sliced 
 sunflower seed(or any  nuts  of your choice) 
 2 avocados, seeded, peeled, and sliced 
 1 sm.  Onion , thinly sliced and separated into rings 
 lettuce 
 orange juice 
 Directions : 
 Section  grapefruit  and  oranges  over a small bowl to catch juices; reserve juices. 
 In a large bowl combine citrus sections,  cucumber ,  Onion , and  avocado  slices. 
 Measure reserved fruit juices; add  orange juice  to make 1 cup. 
 Combine juice mixture,  vinegar ,  Sugar ,  salt , and 1/4 teaspoon  pepper ; pour over fruit. 
 Cover and marinate in refrigerator for 2 to 3 hours. 
 At serving time, mix in  nuts  and stir until coated. 
 Then use a slotted spoon to remove fruit from marinade. 
 Arrange on lettuce-lined plates. Drizzle some of the marinade on top of each salad.
