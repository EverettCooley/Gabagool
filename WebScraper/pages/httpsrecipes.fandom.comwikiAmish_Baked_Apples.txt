      Amish Baked Apples     
 Ingredients : 
 8 to 10  apples ,  cored ,  peeled  and halved 
 ¾ cup  white sugar 
 ¾ cup  brown sugar 
 ½ cup  flour 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 2 tsp  butter , melted 
 1 cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Place  apples  in greased dish. 
 Mix remaining ingredients in pan and bring to a boil. 
 Simmer, stirring until thick. 
 Pour  syrup  over  apples  and bake for 35 to 40 minutes.
