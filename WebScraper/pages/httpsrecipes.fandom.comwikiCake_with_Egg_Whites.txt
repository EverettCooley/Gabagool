 Description : 
 In Romanian: Chec din albusuri
 
 Ingredients : 
 7 - 8  egg whites 
 10 oz / 300 g  sugar 
 5 oz / 150 g  butter   or   margarine 
 5 oz / 150 g  flour 
 peel from 1  lemon 
 4 oz / 100 g sliced  walnuts 
 butter  and  flour  to grease and dust the pan 
 Directions : 
 Whip the  egg whites  with the  sugar . Gradually add the melted and still warm  butter  ( margarine ), stirring continuously. 
 Then add the  lemon peel  and  flour . 
 Place half of the  walnuts  on the bottom of a buttered and dusted bread pan. 
 Then pour the batter and the remaining  walnuts  on top. Bake, first at low heat and then increase it.
