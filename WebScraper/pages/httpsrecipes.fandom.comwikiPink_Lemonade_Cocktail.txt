Description : 
 Southern Living 2007 Holiday Dinners 
 
 Prep: 5 minutes 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Makes 6 – 8 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (12-oz) can frozen pink  lemonade  concentrate, thawed 
 3 (12-oz) bottles  beer  (not dark), chilled 
 ¾ cup  vodka , chilled 
 ice 
 garnishes: fresh  cranberries , citrus slices 
 Directions : 
 Stir together first 3 ingredients. 
 Serve over ice. 
 Garnish, if desired.
