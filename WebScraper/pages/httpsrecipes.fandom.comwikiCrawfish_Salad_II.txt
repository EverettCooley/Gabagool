 Description : 
 Similar to chicken salad
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds of  crawfish  (peeled) 
 2 ribs of  celery  (chopped) 
 1  onion  (chopped) 
 2 tablespoons of  butter 
 mayonnaise 
 prepared mustard 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Sauté  onions  and  celery  in  butter . 
 Add  crawfish . 
 Sauté about 10 minutes on medium heat. 
 Pour into food processor, grind ingredients. 
 Remove from processor-put in large mixing bowl 
 Add  mayo  and  mustard . 
 You may add pickle relish if you like.
