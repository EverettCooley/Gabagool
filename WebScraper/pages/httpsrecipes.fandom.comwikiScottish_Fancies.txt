Ingredients : 
 1  egg 
 1 cup  rolled oats 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 ⅓ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅔ tablespoon melted  butter 
 ¼ teaspoon  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 First beat  egg  until light, add gradually  sugar , and then stir in remaining ingredients. 
 Drop mixture by teaspoonfuls on a thoroughly greased inverted dripping-pan one inch apart. 
 Spread into circular shape with a case knife first dipped in cold  water . 
 Bake in a moderate oven until delicately browned. 
 Varitaions : 
 To give variety use ⅔ cup  rolled oats  and fill cup with shredded  coconut .
