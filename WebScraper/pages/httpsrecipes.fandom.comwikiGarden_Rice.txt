Description : 
 Makes 8 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 1 x 10-ounce package frozen  peas  and  carrots , thawed 
 1 x 10-ounce package frozen  broccoli , thawed 
 1 x 8¾-ounce can  corn , drained 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1  tomato , chopped 
 1 teaspoon  garlic salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in large skillet. Sauté  onion  until crisp-tender. 
 Add  broccoli ,  peas  and  carrots  and  corn . Stir; heat thoroughly. 
 Add  rice ,  tomato ,  garlic salt  and  pepper , stir until well blended.
