Ingredients : 
 1 cup of  gem squash  cut into small cubes 
 2 lbs of old hen or  chicken 
 1 cup of  potatoes  cut into small cubes 
 1 cube of  chicken stock 
 6 cups of  water 
 1 spoons of  oil 
 ½ cup of  noodles 
 1 spoon of chopped  parsley 
 ½ teaspoon of mashed  garlic 
 ¼ cup of chopped  celery 
 Directions : 
 Heat up the  oil  in a pot, add the  chicken ,  garlic ,  salt  and herbs. 
 Add the  water  and bring to a boil. 
 Leave at medium heat until  chicken  or hen is tender, adjust  water  when necessary minutes. 
 Add the  potatoes ,  celery  and  squash  and five minutes later add the noodles. 
 Stir regularly to avoid excessive sticking, adjust  water  when necessary. 
 Adjust  salt  to taste. 
 When the noodles are tender turn off the heat.
