 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (2 lbs) can  pineapples , drained,reserving juice and slightly salted 
 2 teaspoons  Sugar 
 2 tablespoons  cornstarch 
 1 package mini marshmallows 
 1/2 cup chopped  nuts 
 1/2 lb grated  Cheddar   Cheese 
 Directions : 
 Heat the reserved  pineapple juice . Add  Sugar  and  cornstarch . Thicken and cool.
Add marshmallows and  nuts . Sprinkle  Cheese  on top.
