A tasty way to serve  tofu . Ideal for a supper party.
 
 Ingredients : 
 Fritters : 
 50 g  self-raising flour , (2 oz) 
 100 ml  water , (4 fl. oz) 
 salt  and freshly ground  pepper 
 vegetable oil  for frying 
 1 x 285 g  tofu , cut into chunks 
 Mixed Berry Salsa : 
 2 tbsp  caster sugar 
 2 tbsp  red wine vinegar 
 300 g mixed  berries , e.g.  red currants ,  black currants ,  blackberries ,  raspberries ,  blueberries  (10z) 
 2 shallots, finely diced 
 Directions : 
 Make the salsa. 
 Place the  vinegar  and  sugar  in a pan and gently heat to dissolve the  sugar . 
 Add the  berries  and shallots and poach gently for 10 minutes until softened. 
 Allow to cool. 
 Make the batter, place the  flour  in a bowl and gradually whisk in the  water . 
 Heat the  oil  in a deep pan until hot. 
 Dip the  tofu  in the batter and deep fry for 1 – 2 minutes until the batter is crispy. 
 Serve immediately with the salsa.
