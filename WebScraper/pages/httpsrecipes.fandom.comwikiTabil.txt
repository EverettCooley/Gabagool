A Tunisian blend of spices consisting of caraway seeds, chiles or chile powder, coriander, and garlic. In Tunisia, the word "tabil" also is the Arabic term for "coriander" however, this term is most often used to refer to the blend of spices used in the preparation of rice, couscous and meat dishes.
