Description : 
 4 servings, 60 minutes preparation
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 Head  green cabbage 
 1  green pepper 
 salt 
 2 c Cubed  potatoes 
 Zafrig 
 Directions : 
 Select 1 firm head green cabbage and 1 green pepper. 
 Cut cabbage into large cubes. 
 Put cubes into large soup pot and barely cover with water 
 Halve the green pepper and put on top of cabbage. 
 Add salt to taste; boil until tender. 
 Add 2 cups cubed potatoes. 
 Cook until potatoes are tender. 
 Pour Zafrig in and simmer for 10 minutes.
