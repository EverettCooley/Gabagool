Description : 
 In Romanian: Sufleu de fasole verde
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs / 1 kg  green beans 
 3 tablespoons  butter 
 3  eggs 
 2 tablespoons  flour 
 1 cup  milk 
 3 tablespoons grated or crushed sheep's  cheese 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Clean, wash and set the  green beans  to boil with some  salt . 
 When done, drain and keep aside. Put the  butter  to melt in a pan, add  flour , fry slightly and then add the  milk , stirring continuously. 
 Remove from heat and add the yolks one by one. 
 When almost cold, add the whipped  egg  whites mixed with the  cheese . 
 To this mixture add the  green beans . 
 Mix well and place everything in a buttered mold. 
 Bake at medium heat. Serve warm
