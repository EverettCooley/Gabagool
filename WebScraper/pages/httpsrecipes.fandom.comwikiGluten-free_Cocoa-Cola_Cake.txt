Ingredients : 
 2 cups  sugar 
 1 cup cola (not diet) 
 2 cups  gluten-free flour mix 
 1½ cups gluten free miniature  marshmallows 
 1½ teaspoon  xanthan gum 
 ½ cup  buttermilk 
 ½ cup  vegetable oil 
 1 teaspoon  baking soda 
 ½ cup  butter  or  margarine 
 2  eggs , slightly beaten 
 ⅓ cup Hershey's  cocoa 
 1 teaspoon gluten free  vanilla extract 
 Gluten-free flour mix : 
 6 parts  white rice flour 
 2 parts  potato starch  (not  potato flour ) 
 1 part  tapioca starch  (also called  tapioca flour ) 
 Directions : 
 Preheat the oven to 350 °F, and grease a 13 x 9 x 2-inch rectangular pan. 
 In a large mixing bowl combine the  sugar ,  flour , and  xanthan gum . 
 Set aside. 
 In a medium saucepan combine the  oil   butter ,  cocoa , and cola. 
 Bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat, stirring constantly. 
 Add the  chocolate  mixture to the  sugar  mixture. 
 Beat until smooth. 
 Stir in the marshmallows. 
 Add the  buttermilk ,  baking soda ,  eggs , and  vanilla ; blend well. 
 Pour the batter into the greased pan. 
 Bake 40–45 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. 
 Gluten-free flour mix : 
 Mix the ingredients together thoroughly.
