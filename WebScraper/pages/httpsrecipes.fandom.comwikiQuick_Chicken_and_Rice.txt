Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup uncooked  rice 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 2 cups  chicken broth 
 1 cup sliced fresh  mushrooms 
 1 cup cooked  chicken  cubes 
 ½ cup (2 ounces) shredded  Cheddar  cheese 
 ⅛ teaspoon  paprika 
 1/16 teaspoon  ground red pepper 
 Directions : 
 Cook  rice  in  butter  in large skillet over medium heat 1 minute. 
 Add broth and bring to a boil. 
 Stir once or twice, lower heat, cover, and simmer 15 minutes or until  rice  is tender and liquid is absorbed. 
 Remove from heat. 
 Stir in  mushrooms ,  chicken ,  cheese ,  paprika  and  pepper . 
 Cover and let stand 10 minutes.
