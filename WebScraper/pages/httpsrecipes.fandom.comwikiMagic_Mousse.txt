 10 (Â½ cup) servings 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups boiling  water 
 1 (8-serving) pkg  cherry flavored gelatin 
 1 (16-oz) tub whipped topping, thawed 
 Directions : 
 Stir boiling  water  into dry  gelatin ; mix in med bowl at least 2 minutes, until completely dissolved. 
 Add whipped topping to hot  gelatin ; stir with wire whisk until whipped topping is completely melted and mixture is well blended. 
 Mixture will still be thin. 
 Pour into 6-cup mold, sprayed with  cooking spray . 
 Refrigerate 8 hr or overnight, until firm. 
 Unmold onto serving plate just before serving. 
 Store leftover dessert in refrigerator. 
 Tip : 
 If whipped topping is still frozen, just follow recipe as directed, substituting frozen whipped topping for thawed, making sure to stir  gelatin  mixture until whipped topping is completely melted before pouring into mold. Different  gelatin  flavors may be used.
