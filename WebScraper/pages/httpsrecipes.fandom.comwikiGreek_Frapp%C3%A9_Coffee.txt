Description : 
Frappé drinking is a must in Greece. If you are planning to visit Greece on the summer do not hesitate to order in a cafeteria a frappé. To order it, just ask for a frappé glykos or metrios or sketos (sweet or medium or with no  sugar ) me gala or xwris gala (with  milk  or without  milk ).       Greek Frappé Coffee     
 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2-3 tsp. instant  coffee 
 1 ¾ cup cold  water 
 1 tsp.  sugar , white only 
 2 cups  milk 
 2  ice  cubes 
 Directions : 
 Add the  coffee ,  sugar  and a little  water  (4 – 5 spoons) into the shaker. Shake until thick and foamy. 
 Strain the  coffee  into a tall glass and add the rest of the  water , the  ice  cubes, and  milk . 
 Greek frappé  coffee  is drunk with a straw, which is also used to mix the  coffee  and  sugar  as it stands on the bottom of the glass. Serve cold and plain or with other desserts.
