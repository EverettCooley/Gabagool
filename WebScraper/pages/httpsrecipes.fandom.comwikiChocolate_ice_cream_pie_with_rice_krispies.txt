 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 PIE FILLING: 
 
 1 Jar  chocolate  fudge sauce 
 1 quart  ice  cream- vanilla  or whatever 
 1/4 cup  Peanut butter 
 CRUST: 
 
 3 tbsp  margarine , melted or -softened 
 CRUST INGREDIENTS-CONTINUED: 
 
 1 tbsp  corn syrup 
 2 1/2 cup  rice  krispie cereal 
 Directions : 
 For pie: allow  ice cream  to soften. 
 Spread evenly over the crust. 
 Put in freeze until  ice cream  has solidified. 
 Mix together the  Peanut butter  and the  chocolate  sauce. 
 Pour over top of  ice cream  and return pie to freezer. 
 Remove pie from freezer about ten minutes before serving. 
 Garnish before serving. 
 For crust: mix together the cereal and syrup. 
 Add melted  butter . 
 With your hands, press this mixture into a pie plate, forming a crust. 
 Chill well.
