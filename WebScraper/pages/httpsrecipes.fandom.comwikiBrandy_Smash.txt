Description 
 Brandy Smash  from Jeremy Thomas' Bartending Guide or How to Mix Drinks, out of copyright
 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 Take 1 teaspoonful of  white sugar 
 2 tablespoonfuls of  water 
 3 or 4 sprigs of tender  mint 
 1 wineglass full of  brandy 
 Directions 
 Press the mint in the sugar and water to extract the flavor, add the brandy, and fill the glass two-thirds full of shaved ice. 
 Stir thoroughly, and ornament with a half a slice of orange, and a few fresh sprigs of mint. 
 Serve with a straw.
