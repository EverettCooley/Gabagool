Description : 
 Makes 12 x 4-inch pancakes
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  whole wheat flour 
 ½ cup unbleached white  flour 
 1 tsp  sugar 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 ½ tsp  baking soda 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 1  egg 
 1 cup  buttermilk   or   sour milk  (1 cup lowfat  milk  + 1 tablespoon  vinegar ) 
 2 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 Directions : 
 Stir together the flours,  sugar ,  baking powder ,  baking soda  and  salt . 
 Beat the  egg ,  milk  and  oil  together. 
 Add the liquids to the  flour  mixture and stir just until blended. 
 Pour the batter onto a greased hot griddle. 
 Serve with fresh fruit slices, unsweetened  applesauce  or low-calorie syrup. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Two pancakes = calories: 114 | carbohydrates: 18  | protein: 4  | fat: 3  | sodium: 246  | potassium: 1200  | cholesterol: 47  | exchange value: 1 bread exchange + ½ fat exchange
