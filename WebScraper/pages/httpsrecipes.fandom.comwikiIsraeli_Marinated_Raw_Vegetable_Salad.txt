Ingredients : 
 2 large  carrots 
 2 large  Kirby cucumbers 
 1 large  red pepper 
 1 large  green pepper 
 1 medium  onion 
 5  broccoli  flowerets 
 5  cauliflowerets 
 ¾ cup  oil , vegetable or olive 
 ½ cup  vinegar 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ⅛ tsp  red pepper flakes 
 Directions : 
 Slice first 5 vegetables. 
 Break in flowerets. 
 Combine next 5 ingredients and pour over vegetables. 
 Put into jar or container and refrigerate overnight. 
 Mix several times.
