Ingredients : 
 3 lbs  chicken , in quarters/pieces 
 1 tbsp  prepared mustard 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 1 cup  bread crumbs , freshly made 
 1 tbsp  butter , softened 
 1 tbsp  vinegar 
 ¼ tsp  cayenne , or to taste 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 375°F. 
 Wipe the  chicken  with a paper towel. 
 Mix the=  butter ,  mustard ,  vinegar ,  salt  and  cayenne , and rub the mixture all over the skin side of the  chicken . 
 Place the  chicken , skin side up, in a shallow roasting pan. 
 Sprinkle evenly with the crumbs and bake for about 40 minutes, or until meat thermometer registers 170°F in the breast meat, 185°F in the dark meat.
