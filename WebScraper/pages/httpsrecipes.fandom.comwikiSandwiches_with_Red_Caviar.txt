      Sandwiches with Red Caviar     
 Ingredients : 
 150 g  red caviar 
 20 g  butter 
 300 g  bread 
 greens 
 Directions : 
 Slice bread finely. 
 Cut out crusts from bread. 
 Put a small hip of  caviar  and a decorated piece of  butter  on a bread slice. 
 Decorate with  greens .
