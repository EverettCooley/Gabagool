About smoked sausage : 
       Smoked sausage     
 A sausage that has been cured with the use of aromatic wood, which is burned to produce smoke. The smoke gives the sausage a distinctive flavor and extends the storage life of the product. There are varieties of smoked sausage that are uncooked, such as the  Polish sausage , and there are varieties that are fully cooked, such as  Kielbasa .
