 About Chili seasoning : 
 Chili seasoning is a blend of herbs and spices used to make the chili, a spicy stew. Some traditional herbs and spices which simply go into this mix are:
 
 chili powder 
 red pepper 
 onion 
 garlic 
 white sugar 
 cumin 
 parsley 
 salt 
 basil
