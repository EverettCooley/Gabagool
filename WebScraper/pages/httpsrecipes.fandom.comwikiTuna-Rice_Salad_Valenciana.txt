Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups cooked  rice 
 1 cup sliced  celery 
 ¼ cup sliced  stuffed olives 
 ¼ cup chopped  dill pickles 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅛ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1 x 6½-ounce can  tuna  in water, drained and flaked 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 2 tablespoons  chili sauce 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 2 tablespoons  capers  for garnish (optional) 
 sliced  stuffed olives  for garnish (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  celery ,  olives , pickles,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Add  tuna  to  rice  mixture; toss lightly. 
 Cool. 
 Spoon into serving dishes. 
 Blend  mayonnaise ,  chili sauce  and  lemon juice ; pour over individual salads. 
 Garnish with  capers  and sliced , if desired.
