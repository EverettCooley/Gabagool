Pedernales River  chili 
 
 1 T.  oil 
 1 Medium  Onion , Chopped 
 1 T. Minced  garlic 
 2 Lb. Lean Ground  Beef 
 3 T.  chili powder  or to Taste 
 1 T. Ground  cumin 
 1 T. Dried  oregano 
 1/2  salt 
 3 T. hot pepper sauce  or to Taste 
 2 Cans  tomatoes  (16 Oz. Each) Undrained, Broken Up 
 2 Cans  red kidney beans  (16 oz Each) 
 
Heat  oil  in Large Kettle Over Medium Heat. Add  Onion  And  garlic . Cook 2 Minutes or Until  Onion  is Tender. Crumble Meat Into Pot And Cook 5 to 6 Minutes or Until it Looses Its Pink Color. Stirring 2 to 3 Times. Add Remaining Ingredients. Bring to Boil. Reduce Heat. Simmer Uncovered About 50 Minutes Until Slightly Thickened And Flavor Develops. Makes 8 Cups.
 Source: President Lyndon Johnson, During His Term in The White House, 1963-1969Source: my Old Recipes
