 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 cups cooked  rice 
 1/2 cup shredded  Cheddar  cheese 
 1/4 cup sliced green onions 
 1/4 cup finely chopped  ripe olives 
 1/4 cup Italian-style bread crumbs 
 1/4 teaspoon  salt 
 1  egg , beaten 
 1 teaspoon  lemon juice 
 1/8 teaspoon hot pepper sauce 
 paprika  for garnish 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  Cheddar  cheese, onions, olives, bread crumbs,  salt ,  egg   lemon juice  and  pepper  sauce in large mixing bowl. Shape mixture into 1-inch balls; place on well-greased baking sheet. Bake at 375 degrees 10 to 15 minutes. Serve warm
