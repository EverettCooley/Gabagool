Description 
 Fruit & Vegetable Recipes I  by the South Carolina Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Serves : 4 to 6 
 Ingredients 
 2  kiwifruit , pared and sliced 
 1 fresh  pear , cored and cut into chunks 
 1  orange , peeled and sectioned 
 1 teaspoon grated  orange peel 
 1  banana , peeled and sliced 
 1 cup  red grapes , halved and seeded if necessary 
 ½ cup  ginger ale 
 Directions 
 Combine all ingredients except ginger ale; spoon into fruit dishes or stemmed goblets. 
 Pour ginger ale over mixture.
