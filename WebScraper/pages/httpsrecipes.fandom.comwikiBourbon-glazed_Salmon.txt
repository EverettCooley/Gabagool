Description : 
 Cook Time : 4 minutes each side 
 Serves : 2 
 Ingredients : 
 4 x 6 ounce  salmon  fillets 
 Marinade ingredients : 
 3 tbsp  brown sugar 
 3 tbsp  bourbon 
 2 tbsp  soy sauce 
 1 tbsp grated fresh  ginger 
 1 tbsp  lime juice 
 3  garlic  cloves, minced 
 ¼ tsp ground  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Mix the marinade ingredients following in a big ziploc bag. 
 Add salmon fillets to marinade. 
 Marinate for 1 – 2 hours. 
 Coat skillet with cooking spray, cook on medium high heat for ~ 4 minutes each side or until done how you like it. 
 Drizzle remaining sauce over fish - sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds if you'd like.
