 Description : 
       Swedish Beef Roast     
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 	tablespoon  butter 
 5 	lbs boneless  Beef  chuck roast or rump roast 
 4 	 Anchovies , chopped 
 1 	 Onion , minced 
 2 	teaspoons  salt 
 1 	 bay leaf 
 15 	peppercorns 
 1 	tablespoon  brown sugar 
 1 	tablespoon  vinegar 
 1 	tablespoon  whiskey 
 1 	cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter  in a kettle. 
 Add  Beef  roast and brown well on all sides. 
 Add  Anchovies ,  Onion ,  salt ,  bay leaf , peppercorns,  Sugar   vinegar ,  whiskey  and  water . 
 Cover tightly and cook 2-1/2 hours . 
 Serve roast with the pan juice.
