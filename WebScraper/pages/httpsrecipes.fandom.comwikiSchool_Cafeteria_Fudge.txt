School Cafeteria Fudge
 
 yes, there is no chocolate in this recepie, just the cocoa
 
 2 cups granulated sugar
 1/2 cup milk
 1/2 cup (1 stick) butter
 1/4 cup cocoa
 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
 1/2 cup peanut butter
 2 cups quick oats
 
 Boil sugar, milk, butter and cocoa 6 minutes, stirring
 constantly.
 Remove from heat, add vanilla extract and peanut butter and mix
 well.
 Stir in oats and mix thoroughly.
 Pour into a buttered 8-inch pan and let cool.
 Cut into squares.
