Ingredients : 
 2 parts  green  or  wax beans 
 1 part diced  potatoes 
 1 part chopped  onion 
 4 slices of  bacon 
 a pint of  cream  or  sour cream 
 Directions : 
 Take 2 parts sliced green or  wax beans  and 1 part diced  potatoes  and chopped  onion . 
 Cover and boil in  water  until soft. 
 Fry 4 slices of  bacon  and add  bacon  bits and fat to soup. 
 Add about a pint of  cream  and simmer (or  sour cream ).
