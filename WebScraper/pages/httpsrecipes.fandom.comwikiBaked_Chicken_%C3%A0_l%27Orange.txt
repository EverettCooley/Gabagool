 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: My old recipes 
 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 4 large single  chicken breasts , skin removed 
 1 can diet  Shasta orange soda  (12 oz) 
 1 cup chopped  celery 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  white pepper 
 Directions : 
 Arrange chicken in shallow baking pan or casserole. 
 Pour on diet Shasta. 
 Sprinkle remaining ingredients over top. 
 Cover and bake at 350°F for 50 minutes. 
 Uncover and bake until tender, about 20 minutes more.
