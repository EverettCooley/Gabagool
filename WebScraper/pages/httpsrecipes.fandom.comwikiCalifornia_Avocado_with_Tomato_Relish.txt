 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 Cups diced  tomato 
 1  scallion 
 ¼ Cup  parsley , chopped 
 3 Tbsp  green pepper , minced 
 2 Tbsp  wine vinegar 
 1 ½ Tbsp  salad oil 
 1 Tbsp  olive oil 
 ¼ tsp  Tabasco sauce 
 ¼ tsp  chili powder 
 ¼ tsp  basil 
 2 ripe avocados 
 shredded  lettuce 
 Directions : 
 Mix together all ingredients but the  avocado  and  lettuce . 
 Let stand at room temperature for one hour. 
 Refrigerate. To serve, place theshredded  lettuce  on a plate. 
 Place  avocado  on  lettuce  and spoon  tomato  mixture over.
