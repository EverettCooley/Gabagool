      All American Sandwich     
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ lb  American cheese , cubed 
 1 (7 ounces) can of flaked  tuna 
 2 tablespoons  sweet pickles  or  dill pickles , diced 
 2 tablespoons  onions , chopped 
 3 hard-boiled  egg , chopped 
 2 tablespoons  green peppers , chopped 
 2 tablespoons  stuffed olives , chopped 
 ½ cup Miracle Whip or  mayonnaise 
 6  hotdog buns 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients except buns, mix lightly and spoon into  hotdog buns . 
 Wrap in tinfoil and bake at 250 °F for 30 minutes.
