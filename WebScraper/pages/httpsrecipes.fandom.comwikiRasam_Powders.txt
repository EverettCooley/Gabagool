Rasam  means  essence 
 It is a close relative of Sambhar, but thinner and served as a spicy soup 
 Rasam powder is made of chili, tamarind, asafoetida, cumin seeds, black peppercorns, dry-roasted mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, and dried curry leaves, mixed with a small amount of lentils 
 To make blend the powder with water and simmer about 20 minutes and some chopped tomatoes
