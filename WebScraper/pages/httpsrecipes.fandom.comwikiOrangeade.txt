 Description : 
 In Romanian: Oranjada
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 big  oranges , 
 2  lemons , 
 15 lumps or teaspoons  sugar , 
 1 qt/1 l  water 
 Directions : 
 Squeeze the  lemons  and  oranges . 
 Strain the juice through  Cheese  cloth. 
 Dissolve the  Sugar  in  water , add the  lemon  and  orange  juices, pour in a bottle with one piece of  orange peel  and refrigerate. 
 Serve cold after a few hours.
