 Description : 
 Docono is a sweet dish served hot or cold.
 
 Ingredients : 
 semolina 
 sweetened milk 
 ripe  bananas 
 vanilla extract 
 cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Cook the  semolina  in sweetened  milk  with sliced  bananas . 
 After cooking, flavor with  vanilla  and  cinnamon  powder. 
 Serve either hot or cold.
