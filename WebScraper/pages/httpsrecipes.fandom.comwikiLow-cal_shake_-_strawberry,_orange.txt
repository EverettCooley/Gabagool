 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pint  strawberries  (1 pint) 
 1 each  orange , peeled 
 16 oz  vanilla   yogurt 
 1 cup skimmed  milk 
 6 each  ice  cubes 
 Directions : 
 In a blender, mix all ingredients until frothy. Makes 4 servings. Each serving = 167 calories and 2 grams fat
