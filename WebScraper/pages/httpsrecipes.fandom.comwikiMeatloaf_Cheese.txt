Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 slices homemade  bread 
 ½ cup  milk 
 ¼ to ½ medium  onion , diced 
 2  eggs 
 1 lb  hamburger 
 ½ cup diced  white cheese 
 ¼ to ½ tsp  salt 
 2 tsp  sesame seeds 
 Directions : 
 Soak  bread  in  milk . 
 Chop  onion . 
 Beat  eggs . 
 Mix all ingredients well. 
 Shape into a loaf and place in a greased loaf pan. 
 Bake at 350°F for 1 hour.
