Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  butter 
 ½ cup  cream cheese 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1  egg 
 2 cups  flour 
 2 tbsp culinary  lavender 
 lavender fruit  jam 
 walnuts  or  pecans 
 Directions : 
 Mix  butter  and  cream cheese  together in mixer until blended well. 
 cream  in  sugar . 
 Mix in  egg ,  flour  and  lavender  until well blended together. 
 Pat dough onto lightly greased jelly roll pan (15½ x 10½). 
 Bake at 350°F for 20 minutes or until inserted toothpick comes out clean. 
 Do not brown top. 
 Spread top of warm cookie base with  lavender  fruit jam and sprinkle with  nuts . 
 Cut into bars while warm.
