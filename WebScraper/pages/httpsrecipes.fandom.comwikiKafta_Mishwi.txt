Description : 
 Grilled Kabab Fingers
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb finely  minced lean meat 
 2 tablespoons chopped fresh  parsley 
 1 grated medium  onion 
 2 tbsp  shortening   or   butter 
 1 tsp  salt 
 Â½ tsp  ground sweet pepper 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Blend meat,  parsley , grated  onion ,  salt , and  pepper . 
 Divide meat mixture into equal pieces as big as a walnut. 
 Make from each piece a finger shape piece. 
 Meanwhile prepare charcoal. 
 Thread meat. 
 Grill over hot coals. 
 Turning several times until tender and browned, about 8 minutes. 
 Serve hot with salad or french fries. 
 Note: if you use bamboo skewers, soak in  water  for 1 hour before using to keep the exposed portions from burning.
