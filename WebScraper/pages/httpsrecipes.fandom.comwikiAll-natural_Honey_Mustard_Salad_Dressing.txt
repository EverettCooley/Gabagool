Description : 
 This easy-to-make salad dressing has a sweet and slightly tangy flavor. Experiment by trying different vinegars and mustards. A mild honey such as clover or orange blossom works best in this recipe. Toss dressing with salad greens or serve as a dipping sauce with chicken, fried mozzarella or potatoes.
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Makes 2⅔ cups 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups expeller pressed canola or safflower  mayonnaise 
 ½ cup extra virgin  olive oil 
 ½ cup  honey 
 ¼ cup apple  cider vinegar 
 2 tablespoons  Dijon mustard 
 2 tablespoons finely chopped  shallot 
 1 teaspoon dried  parsley 
 sea salt , to taste 
 ground  pepper , to taste 
 Directions : 
 Whisk the mayonnaise with the olive oil to combine. 
 Whisk in remaining ingredients. 
 Taste and adjust seasoning. 
 Serve at once, or chill in a covered container for up to 5 days. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving (2 Tbsp / 35 g-wt.): 
 
 190 calories (160 from fat) | 18g total fat | 2g saturated fat | 0g protein | 7g total carbohydrate (0g dietary fiber, 7g sugar) | 5mg cholesterol | 160mg sodium
