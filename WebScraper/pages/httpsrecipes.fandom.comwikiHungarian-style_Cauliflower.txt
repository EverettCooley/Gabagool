Ingredients : 
 a whole head of  cauliflower 
 ¼ lb  butter 
 1 cup of  breadcrumbs , medium brown 
 salted  water 
 ⅛ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Cook a whole head of  cauliflower  in a pot of salted  water  till tender. 
 Drain and place whole head on a serving dish. 
 In a saute pan melt ¼ lb  butter  and medium brown 1 cup of bread crumbs, season with ⅛ tsp.  salt . Stir and toss crumbs. 
 Do not let it burn or it will be bitter. 
 Spoon  butter  and crumb mixture over the top of  cauliflower  and serve.
