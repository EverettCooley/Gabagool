Ingredients : 
 4 cups diced (¼")  papaya  or  cantaloupe 
 2 cups chopped sweet  onion 
 ¼ cup fresh  lime juice 
 ¾ tsp  chipotle  in  adobo sauce  (or to taste) 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients an hour or two before serving to marry flavors.
