 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 Solution A: 
 1 ½ cups warm  water   2 cups elmers white glue             food coloring 
 
                 Mix together until -completely dissolved 
 Solution B: 
 2 teaspoons borax       1 1/3 cups warm  water 
                  Mix together until—completely dissolved 
 Directions : 
 1 Pour solution an into solution b.  
2 Do not mix! lift gak out and knead into shape.  
3 Here are some tips: borax can be found in the laundry detergent section of a grocery store- such as "20 mule team" if you are going to half the recipe...  
4 Use only half of solution a and mix the whole amount of solution b.  
5 The results will be the same.  
6 This is important..this is the only way you can half the recipe.  
7 Do not double the recipe and do not reuse solution b (the borax and  water ), use only elmer"s glue.
