Description 
 This is a high-protein snack of good quality. The spread keeps wellin refrigerator but is difficult to spread when cold. 
 
 Makes ⅓ cup. 
 Peanut Butter Snack Spread  from Recipes for Better Nutrition by the National Cancer Institute, public domain government resource—original source of recipe 
 
 
 Ingredients 
 
 1 tbsp  instant dry milk 
 1 tsp water 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 1 tbsp  honey 
 3 heaping tbsp creamy  peanut butter 
 Directions 
 Combine dry milk, water, and vanilla, stirring to moisten. 
 Add honey and peanut butter, stirring slowly until liquid begins to blend with peanut butter. 
 Spread between graham crackers or milk lunch crackers. 
 Variations 
 The spread can also be formed into balls, chilled, and eaten as candy. 
 For a molasses taffy flavor, substitute molasses for honey.
