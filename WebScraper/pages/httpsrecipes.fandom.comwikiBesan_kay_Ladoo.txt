 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups  gram flour 
 1 cup  ghee 
 2 cups  Sugar 
 1 tsp. powder  cardamom 
 1\2 cup chopped  almonds  and pistachios 
 Silver leaves to decorate, (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Heat  ghee  in a heavy based saucepan. 
 Add the  gram flour  and fry it on a low heat stirring constantly, until light brown. Cool slightly. 
 Add  Sugar  and  cardamom , mix well. Add chopped  almonds . 
 Shape into ladoos, decorate with silver leaves and serve.
