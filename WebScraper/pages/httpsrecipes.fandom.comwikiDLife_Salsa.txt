Description : 
 A tasty and quick recipe...absolutely delicious! 
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Original recipe 
 Submitted by: admin via dLife 
 Servings : 4 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  corn , yellow 
 1 cup  black beans , canned 
 ½ cup chopped  red bell or sweet peppers , raw 
 ½ cup chopped  celery , raw 
 ½ cup chopped  green bell or sweet peppers , raw 
 ¼ cup  lime juice , raw 
 1 / 16  cup  olive oil 
 ¼ cup coriander ( cilantro ) leaves, raw 
 ¼ cup chopped  onions , raw 
 Directions : 
 Drain black beans and place in a bowl. 
 Add corn, onions, peppers, and celery. 
 Whisk together olive oil, lime juice and cilantro. 
 Pour over salsa and chill. 
 Serve over red pepper shell with lime wedge.
