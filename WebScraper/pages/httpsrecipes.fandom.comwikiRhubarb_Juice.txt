Ingredients : 
 6 qt.  rhubarb  juice 
 1 qt.  pineapple juice 
 1 can  Hawaiian Punch 
 3½ cups  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Boil  rhubarb  (cover with  water ) when mushy strain to get juice. 
 Put juice back in kettle and add other ingredients. 
 Bring to a boil. 
 Boil for 5 – 10 minutes. 
 Pour in warm jars right from stove and seal
