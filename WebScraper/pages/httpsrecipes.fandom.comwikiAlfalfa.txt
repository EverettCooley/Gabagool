 About Alfalfa sprouts : 
 Alfalfa sprouts are the shoots of the alfalfa plant (Medicago sativa), harvested before they become the full-grown plant. Because they are so small, the sprouts normally contain a concentrated amount of certain vitamins and minerals such as calcium, vitamin K, and vitamin C.
