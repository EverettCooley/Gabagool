 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 tablespoons  lime juice 
 2 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 1 tablespoon fresh chopped  cilantro 
 1/2 teaspoon  cumin 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 3 cups cooked  rice , cooled 
 3/4 pound grilled  Turkey  breast, cut into 2-inch strips (about 2 cups) 
 1 medium-size red pepper, cut into strips 
 1 cup julienne  jicama  strips* 
 1/2 cup sliced  green onions 
 Directions : 
 Combine  lime juice ,  oil ,  cilantro ,  cumin  and  salt  in large bowl. Add  rice ,  Turkey ,  pepper ,  jicama  and  green onions ; toss. Serve chilled.
