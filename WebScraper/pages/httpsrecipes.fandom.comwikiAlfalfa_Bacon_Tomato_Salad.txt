Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Hager Estate in Canton, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups  alfalfa sprouts 
 2 cups  tomatoes  diced small 
 1 cup chopped cooked  bacon 
 Dressing : 
 3 cups  mayonnaise 
 ¼ cup  prepared mustard 
 ¼ cup plain  yogurt 
 1½ teaspoons  horseradish 
 1½ teaspoons  honey 
 Directions : 
 Prepare dressing by combining and stirring all ingredients until well blended. 
 Combine sprouts,  tomatoes  and  bacon  and serve with dressing poured over.
