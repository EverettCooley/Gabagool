Mary Frances'  Chicken 
 
 8  Chicken  Breasts, Skinned, Boned And Halved 
 1/2 Cup  margarine 
 1/2 Cup  French bread  Crumbs 
 1/4 T. Each  salt ,  garlic salt ,  Onion  Powder,  sage ,  marjoram ,  pepper  And  paprika 
 2 T.  parsley , Chopped 
 
Arrange  Chicken  Breasts in Long, Shallow Baking Dish. Combine Remaining Ingredients, Except  paprika . Place on Top of  Chicken . Sprinkle  paprika  on Top. Bake at 300 For 1 1/2 Hours. This May be Prepared a Day Ahead. if So, Bake an Additional 10 Minutes.Source: Mary Frances Singleton
