Description : 
 EXPLOSIVE
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  butter 
 1½ cups  sugar 
 4  eggs 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 2 tsp  baking soda 
 4 cups  flour 
 1½ cups diced  mangos 
 2 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 cup chopped  nuts 
 Directions : 
 Cream together  butter  and  sugar . 
 Add  eggs . 
 Stir in dry ingredients,  mangos  and  lemon juice . 
 Pour into 2 x 8" loaf pans. 
 Bake at 350°F for 1 hour or until loaf tests done. 
 Cool in pans 10 minutes. 
 Remove from pans and cool thoroughly. 
 For best results, wrap in foil and serve the following day.
