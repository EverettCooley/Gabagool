Ingredients : 
 1 large can whole  tomatoes , diced 
 1 medium  onion , chopped 
 2 ounces diced  green chiles 
 1½ teaspoons  garlic powder 
 4 teaspoons  chili powder 
 ¼ teaspoon  oregano 
 ¼ teaspoon  basil 
 1 pound  tofu 
 1 cup  cheddar  cheese 
 1 cup  mozzarella  cheese 
 2 ounces  green chiles , diced (optional) 
 black olives  (optional) 
 8 to 12 whole wheat  tortillas 
 Directions : 
 In saucepan mix  tomatoes ,  onions , chiles and spices. 
 Simmer 15 minutes. 
 Rinse, drain and crumble  tofu . 
 In a bowl, mix together  tofu ,  cheese  and chiles. 
 Place this  tofu  mixture in each tortilla. 
 Roll tightly and place in baking dish, seam side down. 
 Cover with sauce. 
 Sprinkle with  cheese  and sliced . 
 Bake 20 to 30 minutes at 350 °F.
