Blushing  Onion  Soup
 
 1/4 Cup  butter  or  margarine  (1/2 Stick) 
 4 Cups Thinly Sliced  Onion 
 1 clove  garlic , Finely Chopped 
 1 Can  tomato juice  (46 Oz) 
 1 Cup  water 
 2 T.  Beef  Flavored Instant Bouillon Granules or 2 Bouillon Cubes 
 2 T. Dried  parsley  flakes 
 8 Slices  French bread 
 2 Cups Shredded  mozzarella   Cheese 
 
In Large Kettle, Over Medium Heat, Melt  butter . Cook  onions  And  garlic  in Hot  butter  Until Tender, About 10 Minutes. Stir in  tomato juice ,  water , Bouillon And  parsley . Bring to Boil. Reduce Heat to Low. Simmer Uncovered 20 to 30 Minutes. Stir Occasionally. to Serve, Ladle Soup in 8 Ovenproof Soup Bowls. Top With Brad Slices And Sprinkle Generously With  Cheese . Broil 2 to 3 Minutes or Until  Cheese  Melts. Serve at Once. Serves 8
