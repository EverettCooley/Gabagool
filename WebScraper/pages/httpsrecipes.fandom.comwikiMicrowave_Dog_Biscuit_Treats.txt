 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  whole wheat flour 
 3/4 cup dry  milk 
 1/4 cup  cornmeal 
 1/3 cup  shortening 
 1 tablespoon bouillon granules 
 1/2 cup  plain flour 
 1/2 cup  quick-cooking oats 
 1 teaspoon  Sugar 
 1  egg  slightly beaten 
 1/2 cup hot  water 
 Directions : 
 Combine  flour ,  cornmeal ,  milk ,  Oats  and  Sugar . Cut in  shortening . Add  egg , bouillon and hot  water . Knead 5 minutes.
 
 Roll 1/2-inch thick and cut out into favorite shapes. Microwave at 50% power for 5 to 10 minutes, rotating plate. These donâ€™t last long, so make a bunch.
