Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup uncooked  rice 
 1 cup  orange juice 
 1 cup  chicken broth 
 1 teaspoon  salt  (optional) 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  white pepper 
 ¼ cup flaked or shredded  coconut 
 1 tablespoon unsalted polyunsaturated  margarine 
 2 cups peeled and diced  papaya  or  mangos 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice , juice, broth,  salt  and  pepper  in medium saucepan. Bring to a boil; stir once or twice. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 15 minutes, or until  rice  is tender and liquid is absorbed. 
 Stir in  coconut  and  margarine . Cover; let stand 10 minutes. 
 Fold in  papaya .
