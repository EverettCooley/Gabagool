resh Herb Vinaigrette
 
 1/4 Cup  red wine vinegar 
 1 Tbs Dijon  mustard 
 3/4 Tsp  salt 
 1/8 Tsp  pepper 
 1/2 Cup  olive oil 
 1/2 Cup Fresh  basil ,  dill , or  cilantro  Leaves, Finely Chopped 
 
in Bowl, Mix  vinegar , Dijon,  salt  And  pepper  Until Mixed. Whisk in  oil  Until Blended. Whisk in Herb of Your Choice or All of Them Together. Mix Well Before Serving. Great on Salads, Meats And Veggies. Makes 1 Cup.*
