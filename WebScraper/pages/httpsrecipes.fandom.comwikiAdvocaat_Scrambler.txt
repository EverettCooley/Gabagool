Scrambled eggs  with  Advocaat  and  Sausage  in them.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 large eggs 
 2 tablespoons whole milk 
 1 teaspoon butter oil 
 1/4 teaspoon Sea salt 
 4 ounces De Kuyper AdvocaatÂ® 
 1 whole breakfast sausage 
 no rice required! 
 Directions : 
 1.Mix eggs,milk,and Advocaat in measuring cup;beat until foamy.
 2.Set eye on 4.Place skillet on eye,and pour butter oil into skillet,
 then eggs;make sure skillet is completely buttered before adding eggs.
 3.Crumble and cook sausage in separate skillet on 4 until brown.
 4.Pour sausage and drippings over eggs.
 5.As eggs begin to whiten around edges,begin to rake gradually toward 
 center of skillet with fork,eventually folding eggs in on themselves,
 covering sausage,as in any other omelette;do not overcook eggs.
 6.Salt and serve.
