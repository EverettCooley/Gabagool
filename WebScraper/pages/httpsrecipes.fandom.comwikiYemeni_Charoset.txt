Description : 
 Makes 12 balls. Each ball: 75 calories; 2 mg sodium
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup pitted  dates , chopped 
 ½ dried  figs , chopped 
 ⅓ cup Passover concord  grape  wine 
 3 tbsp  sesame seeds 
 1 tsp  ginger 
 pinch  coriander 
 1 small  red chile , minced  or  pinch  cayenne pepper 
 2 tbsp  matzo meal 
 Directions : 
 Combine  dates ,  figs  and wine in large bowl. 
 Add  sesame seeds ,  ginger , coriander,  chile  and matzo meal and blend thoroughly. 
 Roll into 1-inch balls or serve in bowl.
