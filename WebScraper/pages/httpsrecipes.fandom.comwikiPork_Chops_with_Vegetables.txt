Ingredients : 
 4  pork chops 
 Season to taste with Tony's Creole seasoning 
 3 tbsp melted  butter 
 1 cup sliced  carrots 
 1 cup sliced  green beans 
 3 large sliced  potatoes 
 1/3 tsp  basil 
 1 can  cream of mushroom  soup 
 Directions : 
 Brown 4  pork  chops & potatoes in 2 tbsp melted  butter  with seasoning. 
 Add sliced  carrots ,  green beans ,  basil  and  cream  of  mushroom  soup. 
 Blend with 1/2 cup  water . 
 Simmer covered for 45 minutes. 
 Serve with  wild rice  (may simmer  rice  with chops).
