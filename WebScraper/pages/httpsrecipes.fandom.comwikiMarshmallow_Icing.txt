marshmallow  Icing
 Yield: 6 Servings
 
 1 1/2 c  sugar 
 1/8 ts  cream of tartar 
 8 Diced Marshmallows 
 Or 1 1/2 Tbsp  marshmallow 
-Cream
 
 3/4 c Boiling  water 
 1  egg  White, Stiffly Beaten 
 1 ts  vanilla 
 Few  grains   salt 
 
Combine  sugar ,  water ,  cream of tartar , And  salt . Boil to Soft BallStage (236 F). Add Marshmallows to  egg  White. Add Syrup Slowly,Beating Constantly, Until Thick And Creamy. Add Flavoring.
