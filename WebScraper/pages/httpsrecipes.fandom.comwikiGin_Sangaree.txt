Ingredients : 
 2 oz  gin 
 2 oz  club soda 
 1 tsp  Superfine sugar 
 2 tsp  water 
 1/2 oz Tawny  port 
 Directions : 
 Shake the ingredients and serve the cocktail in a Highball Glass.
