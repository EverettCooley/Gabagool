      Chicken rissoles     
 Description : 
 Just when you were wondering where North Korea figured in all this…
 This recipe is for 4 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 450 g / 1 lb minced  chicken breasts 
 2 cloves  garlic 
 3 cm / 1 in chunk of  ginger 
 2  spring onions 
 1 tbsp dry-roasted  sesame seeds 
 1 tbsp  sesame oil 
 1 tbsp  soy sauce 
 0.5 tsp  chilli powder 
 oil  for frying 
 Directions : 
 Smash the  garlic  and grate the  ginger . Slice the spring  Onion  finely. 
 Thoroughly mix everything except the  oil  for frying and make it into 4–6 flattish rissoles. 
 Fry in hot  oil  till they are brown on both sides.
