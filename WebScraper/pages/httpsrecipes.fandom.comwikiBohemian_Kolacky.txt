Bohemian Kolacky
 
Categories: Cookies
 
 2 Sticks  margarine 
 8 oz  cream cheese 
 2 c  flour 
 Preserves or Jam
 powdered sugar 
 
Allow  margarine  And  cream cheese  to Come to Room Temperature. Mix UntilReally Creamy; Add  flour  And Blend Thoroughly. Make Balls (size of a SmallWalnut). Place on Slightly Greased Cookie Sheet. Flatten With a Glass CoverWith a Damp Cloth. Press an Indentation in Center And Fill With Preserves.Bake 350 For 18-20 minutes. Remove From Sheet And Dust With  powdered sugar .
 
Enjoy.
