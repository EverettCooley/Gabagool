 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 cups  flour 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1/2 tsp  baking soda 
 1 cup firmly packed  brown sugar 
 3/4 cup quick cooking  oatmeal 
 1/2 cup  shortening 
 2 cans  cherry  pie filling 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Sift  flour ,  salt  and  baking powder  into a bowl. Blend in  brown sugar  and  oatmeal . 
 Cut in  shortening  until particles are size of small  Peas . Press 1/2 of mixture into bottom of ungreased 9"x13" pan. 
 Spread the cans of  cherry  pie filling. 
 Cover with remaining  oatmeal  mixture. Press down gently with a spoon or your fingers. 350F, 25â€“30 minutes. 
 Serve warm with whipped topping or  ice   cream .
