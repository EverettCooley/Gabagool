 About Coconut oil : 
 Wikipedia Article  About Coconut oil on Wikipedia 
 Coconut oil , also known as coconut butter, is a fat consisting of over 90 percent saturated fat extracted from coconuts and used in baking as a cooking oil. Hydrogenated or partially-hydrogenated coconut oil is often used in non-dairy creamers, and snack foods.
