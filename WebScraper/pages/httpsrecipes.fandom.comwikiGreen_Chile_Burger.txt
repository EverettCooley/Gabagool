 Prep Time: 30 minutes 
 Cook time: varies 
 Serves: 4 
 
 Description : 
 A unique burger originating from the U.S. State of New Mexico, incorporating their state vegetable the  New Mexico chile , spicy and flavorful.
 Canned Hatch diced green chile peppers can be found in numerous supermarkets, if they can not be found the  California chile  aka the "Anaheim pepper" can be a decent substitute. But, New Mexico chile is recommended.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds  ground beef 
 2 tablespoons  kosher salt 
 1/2 tablespoon  black pepper 
 4 ounces of  New Mexico green chile  peppers 
 Bun of your choice 
 Optional : Cheese (Green Chile Cheeseburger), either  American cheese ,  Jack cheese , or  Queso fresco . 
 Optional : Toppings;  lettuce , sliced  tomato , diced onion,  New Mexico red chile  sauce, barbecue sauce, etc. 
 Directions : 
 Dice the New Mexico chile peppers, or buy them pre-diced, you can choose to boil them with various flavorings. 
 Ball the meat into multiple "slider sized" patties and begin cooking. 
 Once you flip the burgers place New Mexico chile peppers on top of the already cooked side of the burger. You can also choose to simply add the peppers when the burger is done. 
 Place the burger and chile into the bun, and add toppings including optional cheese and enjoy.
