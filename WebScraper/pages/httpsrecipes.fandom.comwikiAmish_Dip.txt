      Amish Dip     
 Ingredients : 
 8 ounces  cream cheese 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 1 (6.5 ounces) can  tuna 
 ½ cup  olives ,  pitted , chopped 
 ½ cup  walnuts  or  almonds , chopped 
 2 tablespoons  lemon juice 
 black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Mix all the ingredients, adding more or less  mayonnaise . 
 Serve on dark heavy  bread  as a sandwich.
