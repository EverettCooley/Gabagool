 Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz  light rum 
 1/2 oz  triple sec 
 1 tsp  raspberry  Syrup 
 1 tsp  lemon juice 
 1 tsp  orange juice 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Shake with  ice  and strain into a glass. Top with a slice of  pineapple  and a  lemon  wedge.
