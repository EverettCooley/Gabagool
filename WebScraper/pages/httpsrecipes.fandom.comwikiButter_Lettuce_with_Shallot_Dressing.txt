Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: my old recipes 
 Serves 6 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ lb  butter lettuce  (2 small heads) 
 2 tbsp  wine vinegar 
 1 tbsp  Dijon mustard 
 2 tbsp minced  shallots 
 ¼ tbsp  salt 
 7 tbsp  oil 
 red-leaf lettuce  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Arrange 3 to 4 red  leaf lettuce  leaves on each of 6 plates. 
 Separate  butter   lettuce  into individual leaves and distribute on each plate. 
 Mix  vinegar ,  mustard ,  shallots ,  salt  and  oil . 
 Blend well. 
 Spoon dressing equally over each salad.
