Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: my Old Recipes 
 Serves 6 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup melted  butter 
 1 medium  onion , chopped 
 1 lb  potatoes , cooked and coarsely mashed 
 1 lb  cabbage ,  kale  or other greens, cooked 
 3 tbsp toasted  bread crumbs 
 salt 
 pepper 
 additional  butter  or drippings 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 400°F. 
 Grease a 2 quart baking dish. 
 In medium skillet over medium heat, heat  butter . 
 Add  onion . 
 Cook about 5 minutes. 
 In large bowl, mix  potatoes  and greens. 
 Stir in  onions  and  bread  crumbs. 
 Season with  salt  and  pepper  to taste. 
 Transfer mixture to baking dish. 
 Cover with foil. Bake 45 minutes. 
 To serve, spoon into serving dish and drizzle with additional melted  butter  if desired. 
 Note : 
 If  sausage  is desired, add 1 lb bulk  pork sausage , crumbled and browned to above ingredients.
