Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Barger Estate in Mckinney, Texas in 1997.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 package precooked  pork link sausage 
 1 package refrigerator  butter  flake rolls 
 Directions : 
 Cut each  sausage  in thirds. 
 Peel two thin sections off a  butter  flake roll and wrap around a small piece of  sausage . 
 Continue until all sausage is wrapped and place on an ungreased cookie sheet. 
 Bake at 450Â°F for 7 to 8 minutes. 
 Serve hot.
