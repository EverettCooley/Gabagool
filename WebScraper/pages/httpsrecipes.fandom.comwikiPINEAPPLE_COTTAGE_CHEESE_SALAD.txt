pineapple   cottage cheese  SALAD 
 1 c. crushed  pineapple  with juice
1/2 c. hot  water 
1 c. miniature marshmallows
1 (3 oz.) pkg.  lemon  Jello
1 c.  cottage cheese 
1 c.  whipped cream  or whipped topping
Chopped nuts (optional)
 Bring  water  and  pineapple  to a boil. Add Jello. Stir to dissolve. Add 
marshmallows. Stir to mix. Chill until partly set. Add the cottage 
 Cheese  and  whipped cream . Chill.
NOTE: May be put in oiled mold or bowl. Any flavor Jello may be used.
