Ingredients : 
 2  eggs 
 1 tsp  vinegar 
 ½ lb  hazelnuts , toasted and ground 
 ½ cup  dates  cut up 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 1 cup  corn flakes 
 Directions : 
 Beat whites of  eggs  until stiff, add  vinegar  and  sugar  and beat well; add rest of ingredients and drop on a slightly greased cookie sheet. 
 Bake at 325°F degrees for 15 minutes. 
 Keep in a jar, as they improve with age.
