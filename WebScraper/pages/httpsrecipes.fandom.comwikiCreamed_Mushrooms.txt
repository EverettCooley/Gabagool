Creamed  mushrooms 
 
 1 Pound  mushrooms 
 flour 
 
 2 Tablespoons  butter 
 1 Cup Rich  milk  -- or Cream 
 salt 
 pepper 
 
Wash The  mushrooms , Skin The Caps, And Cut The  mushrooms  in Half or Into Several Pieces if They Are Large, Slicing Down Through Cap And Stem. Lightly  flour  The Sliced  mushrooms . Melt The Fat in a Heavy Skillet, Add The Floured  mushrooms , And Brown Delicately Over Moderate Heat. as Liquid Cooks Out of The  mushrooms , Pour it Off And Save it to Add Later. When The  mushrooms  Are Well Browned,
 Pour in The  milk  or Cream, Add The  mushroom   liquor ,  salt , And  pepper , And StirUntil The Sauce is The Right Consistency. Serve on Slices of Crisp Toast.
 
When i Have These, i Like to Have Them With a Nice Broiled or Grilled  steak  And Forget The Toast.
 
Enjoy.
