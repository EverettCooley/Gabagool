      Pineapple Upside-down Cake     
 Description : 
 A yummy dessert cocktail!
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz vanilla  vodka 
 2 oz  pineapple juice 
 1 dash  grenadine  syrup 
 Preparation : 
 Shake vanilla vodka and pineapple juice with ice. 
 Strain into Martini glass. 
 Add a dash of grenadine. Grenadine will sink to bottom and add a sweet ending. 
 Garnish with a cherry
