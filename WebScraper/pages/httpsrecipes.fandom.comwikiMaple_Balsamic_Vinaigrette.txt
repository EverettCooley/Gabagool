Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 This recipe yields about 1 cup of vinaigrette. 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  olive oil 
 2 tablespoons  balsamic vinegar 
 2 teaspoons  maple syrup 
 2 teaspoons  grainy mustard 
 salt  and  pepper , to taste 
 Directions : 
 In a small bowl, whisk  oil ,  vinegar , syrup,  mustard ,  salt  and pepper until well-blended. 
 Alternatively, combine ingredients in a small jar, secure the lid, and shake until well-blended.
