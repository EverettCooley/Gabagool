Ingredients : 
 a handful  podina 
 a handful  coriander leaves 
 a handful  curry leaves 
 green chilli  as required 
 1 cup  cucumber  (discard the skin) 
 a pinch  zeera  powder 
 a pinch  ajwan  powder 
 a pinch  pepper  powder 
 salt  as required 
 a spoonful fresh  cream 
 a spoonful  thick curd 
 Directions : 
 Grind the ingredients together without the curd and  cream . 
 After grinding mix the chutney with  cream  and curd. 
 This chutney can be ground and stored in the refrigerator but  cream  and curd should only be used while serving.
