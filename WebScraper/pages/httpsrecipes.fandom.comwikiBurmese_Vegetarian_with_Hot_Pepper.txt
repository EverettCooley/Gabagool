Description : 
 A great Burmese recipe for vegetarians. This recipe serves 2 people.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 tablespoons  sunflower oil  (or  vegetable broth ) 
 1½ cups  red bell pepper , sliced 
 ½ cup  green bell pepper , sliced 
 1½ cups  snow peas 
 1½ cups  bok choy , sliced 
 2 tablespoons baby  leeks , sliced 
 1 cup  carrots , thinly sliced 
 1  garlic  clove, sliced 
 ⅛ teaspoon  fresh red chile , chopped 
 4 teaspoon  tamari 
 Directions : 
 In a large wok or saucepan, keat  oil  over high heat. 
 Toss in all the ingredients and stir-fry for 2–3 minutes, or until the vegetables become crunchy. 
 Remove from the heat and serve with  rice .
