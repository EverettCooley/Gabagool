This good ol'fashioned recipe will make many happy ! Requires basic cooking skills, enjoy it ! 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 lbs. Moose, cut in small pieces 
 ¼ lb  butter 
 6 cups  water 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 1 onion 
 2  carrots 
 2 parsnips 
 1 small  turnip 
 10  potatoes 
 Directions : 
 Brown moose meat in hot  butter , add  water ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Let simmer, adding chopped  Onion  after about an hour of cooking. 
 Cook for another hour. Then cut up and add vegetables. 
 Cook for 30 minutes or until vegetables are tender. 
 Make dumplings if you wish.
