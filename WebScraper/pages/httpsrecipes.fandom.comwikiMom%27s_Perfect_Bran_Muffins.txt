Info : 
 Cook Time : 30 min 
 Serves : Makes about 30 large muffins
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 large  eggs 
 1 quart  buttermilk 
 5 cups white  flour 
 2 cups  sugar 
 5 tsp  baking soda 
 2 tsp  salt 
 1 cup  vegetable oil 
 1 x 20 oz package of  Raisin Bran 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Beat the eggs in a large bowl. 
 Add flour, Sugar, Soda, salt. 
 Mix in oil and Raisin Bran. 
 Cover bowl and allow batter to set for 24 hours. 
 Stir, spoon into paper cups in muffin tins (Â¾ full). 
 Bake about 20 to 30 minutes depending on size of  muffins .
