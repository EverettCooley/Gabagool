 Ingredients : 
 Chick Pea  flour  	11 oz 
 safflower oil  	2 tsp 
 red pepper flakes  	1 tsp 
 black pepper , ground 	1 tsp 
 cumin  Powder 	1/2 tsp 
 curry  Powder 	1/2 tsp 
 white sugar  	2 tbsp 
 kosher salt  	2 tsp 
 cayenne pepper , ground 	1/4 tsp 
 water  	10 fl oz 
 Simplot IQF  Cauliflower  	32 oz 
 Directions : 
 Place all ingredients for the batter in a mixing bowl and whisk thoroughly to remove any lumps. Batter should be smooth and pancake like. 
 Cover the bowl and let rest for 30 minutes in a warm place. 
 For each order, dip 4 oz. of the Simplot  Cauliflower  into the batter. 
 Allow the excess batter to drain from the  Cauliflower  and place in a preheated 375 °F fryer. 
 Fry for 7 to 10 minutes or until golden brown color is achieved. 
 Serving Suggestion 
 
 Serve with  mango  Chutney or  yogurt   cucumber  Sauce.
