 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 none 
 Directions : 
 Soak ½ cup  almonds  for about 12 hours then rinse. Soak in boiling  water  for about 30 sec then immerse in cold  water . Then squeeze them out of their husks (blanching) Put  almonds , ½ cup  water  and about 2 tbsp of sweetener ( maple syrup ) in and blend away. Add another 1 ½ cup of  water  and continue to blend. (optional) filter off gritty  almond  pulp, you can use it for a great facial scrub by adding it to a liquid soap. Store in the fridge, it keeps for about 3 days.(but ours has lasted for about 5+days)
