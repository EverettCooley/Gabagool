 Description : 
 I believe I am the original poster of this recipe. My mom handed this recipe down to me several years ago and it has recently become very popular with busy moms all over the Internet. Try using Chinese  ketchup  instead of regular. Serve with steamed  white rice  like jasmine or basmati and/or  Chinese noodles ." Original recipe yield: 6 servings.
