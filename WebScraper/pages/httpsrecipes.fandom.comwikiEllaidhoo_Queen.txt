 Ingredients : 
 4 cl  red wine 
 2 cl  crème de cassis 
 2 cl Bacardi ( white rum ) 
 12 cl  pineapple juice 
 Directions : 
 Pour the strongly shaken  red wine ,  crème de cassis  and Bacardi into a glass half-filled with crushed  ice . 
 Decorate the glass with a piece of  pineapple  and a green cocktail  cherry .
