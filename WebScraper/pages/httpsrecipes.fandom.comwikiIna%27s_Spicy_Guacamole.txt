Ingredients : 
 8 fresh  California avocados  – ripe 
 ¼ cup  onion  – finely chopped 
 1  tomato  – large, diced 
 ½ cup  green chile  – chopped 
 2  jalapeno peppers  – minced 
 1  garlic  clove – minced 
 1 tsp  cumin 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Cut the avocados and remove pits. 
 Scoop out the flesh and mash with a fork until coarsely mixed. 
 Stir in remaining ingredients. 
 Serve immediately or cover with plastic wrap snugly against the mixture and chill.
