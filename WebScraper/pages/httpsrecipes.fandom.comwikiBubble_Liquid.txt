 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 Sue Woodward 
 1/4 cup Liquid detergent, preferably Dawn 
 1/2 cup - water 
 1 tsp  Sugar  -OR 
 3 tbsp Glycerine, (can be purchased -in most pharmacies) 
 Directions : 
 1 Mix all ingredients in a flat container such as a dishpan or baking pan. 
2 You can make bubbles with purchased wands, but it"s much more fun to make your own bubble blowers from items you find around the house. 
3 For example, poke the bottom out of a paper cup and dip the end in the mixture; also try a slotted spoon, a plastic berry basket, and drinking straws
