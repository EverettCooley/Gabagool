Desription : 
 Serve this robust dish alongside grilled steak or pork.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Makes 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2½ pounds medium  asparagus , trimmed 
 2 tablespoons extra-virgin  olive oil 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  black pepper 
 2 ounces  feta  (preferably French), crumbled (½ cup) 
 Directions : 
 Put oven rack in lower third of oven and preheat oven to 500°F. 
 Toss asparagus with oil, salt, and pepper in a large shallow baking pan and arrange in 1 layer. 
 Roast, shaking pan once about halfway through roasting, until asparagus is just tender when pierced with a fork, 8 to 14 minutes total. 
 Serve asparagus sprinkled with cheese. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 80 calories | 7 g fat (2 g saturated fat; 79 % calories from fat) | 3 g carbohydrates | 8 mg cholesterol | 301 mg sodium | 4 g protein | 2 g fiber.
