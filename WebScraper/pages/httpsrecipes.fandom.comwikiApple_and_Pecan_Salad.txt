Apple  &  pecan  Salad
 
 5  Apples 
 1 C. Chopped  pecans 
 Chop  Apples  And  pecans  (do Not Peel  Apples ). Add Dressing Mix And Serve.Dressing:
 
 1 C.  sugar 
 2 Tbsp.  flour 
 3/4 C.  water 
 1  egg , Beaten 
 2 Tbsp.  vinegar 
 Mix  sugar ,  flour ,  water  And  egg . Cook Until Sauce Thickens. Add  vinegar . Pour Over  Apples  And  pecans  And Mix Well. Will Keep in Refrigerator Several Days.
 
This is Good. Enjoy.
