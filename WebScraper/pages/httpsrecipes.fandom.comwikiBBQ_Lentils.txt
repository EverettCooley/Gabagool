Cook Time : 10 minutes 
 Serves : 8
 
 Description 
 Bring something different to your next potluck. Try these BBQ lentils instead of baked beans. Lentils are small, flat legumes used in many Indian and Middle Eastern dishes. Look for them next to other dry beans in the grocery store.
 
 Ingredients 
 
 12 oz  barbeque sauce 
 3½ cups  water 
 1 lb dry brown lentils 
 2  green peppers , diced 
 2  red peppers , diced 
 2 small  onions , diced 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 Directions 
 Combine all ingredients in a slow cooker. 
 Cover and cook on low heat for 6–8 hours. 
 Variations 
 Save time and bake this recipe. Sauté red peppers, green peppers, and garlic for 5 minutes. Add lentils and stir for another 5 minutes. Mix lentils, peppers, garlic, barbeque sauce, and 1 cup water in a glass baking dish. Bake at 375 °F for 60 minutes.
