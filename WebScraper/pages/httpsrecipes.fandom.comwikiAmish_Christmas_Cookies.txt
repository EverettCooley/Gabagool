Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  butter 
 1 cup  brown sugar 
 1 cup light  molasses 
 1  egg , beaten 
 4 cups  flour 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 1 tsp ground  cloves 
 ½ tsp  nutmeg 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 Grease cookie sheets. 
 Cream butter and sugar. 
 Blend in molasses and eggs. 
 Sift dry ingredients together and stir into molasses-egg mixture. 
 Roll out dough on lightly floured board and cut into shapes with cookie cutters. 
 Bake for 10 to 12 minutes.
