 Description : 
 This red-hot, bold is made with only 2 ingredients - garlic and chili. Serve up a burning, tangy treat with other appetizers or side dishes. Make this chutney tastier by adding herbs and vegetables.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3-4 cloves  garlic   or  (3 teaspoons  garlic  paste) 
 3 whole  red chiles 
 salt , according to taste 
 1 teaspoon black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Grind all the above ingredients together with a little  water  to liquefy. Pour the contents into a fresh saucepan by heating. 
 Spoon or scoop the spice-based chutney into a small bowl. Pair this herb-based chutney with other appetizers, side dishes, or main dishes.
