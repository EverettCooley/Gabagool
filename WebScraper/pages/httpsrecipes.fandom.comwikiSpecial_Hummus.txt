Description : 
 Serve with pita wedges and vegetables. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can (16 oz)  garbanzo beans  ( chickpeas ) 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 2 tablespoon  tahini 
 2 to 4 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 2 cloves  garlic , crushed 
 ½ teaspoon  cumin  (more to liking) 
 1 teaspoon  tamari  (strong soy sauce)  or  ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Drain  garbanzo beans , saving liquid. 
 Place  garbanzo beans  and other ingredients in blender or food processor. 
 Blend until smooth, adding 1 –  2 tablespoon of saved liquid if necessary. 
 Thin to desired consistency. 
 Best if refrigerated several hours or overnight to allow flavors to blend.
