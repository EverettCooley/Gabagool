red potato  Salad
 
 7 Cooked Medium  red potatoes , Cubed 
 1/4 Cup  sour cream 
 1/4 Cup  Mayonnaise 
 1/4 Cup Shredded  Cheddar   Cheese 
 2 T. Chopped  red onion 
 1  Bacon  Strip,cooked And Crumbled 
 1 1/2 T. Snipped  chives 
 salt  And  pepper  to Taste
 
In a Bowl, Combine All of The Ingredients. Mix Well. Refrigerate Until Serving. Serves 4 to 6Source: Taste of Home
