 Ingredients : 
 1  cabbage , shred in thin silvers 
 1 cup  celery , cut diagonally 
 ½ cup  green peppers , in thin strips 
 ½ cup  tomatoes , in tiny wedges 
 1 cup  pineapple , in ½-inch dice 
 1 tsp salad  herbs 
 1 cup  yogurt 
 2 tablespoons  sour cream 
 2 to 3 tablespoons  milk 
 1 tsp  salt  or to taste 
 freshly ground  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Place the shredded  cabbage  in a 2-quart bowl. 
 Add the greens and set aside. 
 Combine the  yogurt ,  sour cream  and  milk . 
 Beat together until there is a consistency of  heavy cream . 
 Add enough dressing to hold the salad together.
