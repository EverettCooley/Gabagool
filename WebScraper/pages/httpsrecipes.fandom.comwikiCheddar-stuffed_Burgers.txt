Description : 
 At a reduced 341 calories per hamburger, this recipe deserves some attention. Leave out the cheese and you'll save even more calories, and fat! This recipe may look difficult at first because of the long list of ingredients, but really it's quite simple!
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes For Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Original recipe 
 Makes 4 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 3 tablespoons  ketchup 
 3 tablespoons  Worcestershire sauce 
 2 tablespoons fresh  parsley , chopped 
 1½ teaspoon  chili powder 
 1 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 ¾ teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  minced dried onion 
 ½ teaspoon ground  pepper 
 ¼ teaspoon  hot pepper sauce 
 1½ lbs lean  ground turkey 
 1 ounce low-fat  cheddar  cheese, cut into four pieces 
 4  hamburger buns 
 romaine lettuce  leaves, torn into pieces 
 8 thin  tomato  slices 
 Directions : 
 Prepare grill or broiler. 
 Combine ketchup and next 8 ingredients. 
 Stir half of ketchup mixture into turkey and combine well. 
 Divide into 8 equal patties and place 1 piece of cheese on 4 of them. 
 Top with remaining patties, pinching edges to seal. 
 Grill or broil, turning once, until cooked through, brushing remaining sauce over burgers during the last 2 minutes of cooking time. 
 Serve on buns with lettuce and tomato. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 341 calories | 45g protein | 4g fat | 106mg cholesterol | 30g carbohydrates | 1,072mg sodium (check the labels of the sauce ingredients, cut back if you're on a salt-restricted diet) | 2g fiber
