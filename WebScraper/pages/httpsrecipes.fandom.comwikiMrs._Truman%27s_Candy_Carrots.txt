Description 
 This is one of Bess Truman's favorite dishes. Bess Truman had a well-equipped kitchen and often prepared breakfast or lunch for her family. The Trumans also employed several African American women who cooked for the family.

 Ingredients 
 2 to 3 tablespoons  oleo 
 2 or 3  carrots 
 2 to 3 tablespoons  brown sugar 
 Directions 
 Melt oleo in pan. 
 Slice carrots. 
 Put carrots in oleo and cook until almost tender. 
 Add brown sugar. 
 Cook until tender and golden brown. 
 Variation 
 For candy sweet potatoes, cook sweet potatoes the same way as carrots you may want to use a little more oleo. 
 Butter or another brand of margarine can be used in place of the oleo.
