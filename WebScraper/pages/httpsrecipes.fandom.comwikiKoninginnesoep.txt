Description : 
 This is more commonly known as Potage àla Reine. The name translates to "Queen's Soup".
 
 Ingredients : 
 1¼ liter  chicken stock 
 50 g  butter 
 50 g  flour 
 1  egg yolk 
 4 tbsp  cream  or  milk 
 Directions : 
 Mix the  butter  and  flour  and bring to the boil. 
 Slowly add the warm stock and bring to the boil. 
 Let the soup boil for another 10 minutes. 
 Beat the  egg  with the  cream  or  milk  and slowly pour in some hot soup until the  egg  is thoroughly heated through. 
 Then pour in the rest of the soup.
