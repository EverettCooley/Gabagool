Ingredients : 
 1 stick  margarine  ½ cup / 125 ml 
 4 medium  onions 
 ½ cup  flour  56 g 
 1 cup low-fat  evaporated milk  or fresh  milk  250 ml 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 2 – 3 cups left over  chicken 
 1  egg yolks 
 1½ cups  breadcrumbs  375 g 
 ¼ lb  cheese  grated 1 cup / 112 g 
 Directions : 
 Heat frying pan add ½ stick  margarine  sauté the  onions  until tender. 
 (do not burn) remove from heat. 
 Add  flour  and  milk  gradually to make a thick sauce. 
 Season with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Add chopped  chicken , and  egg yolk . 
 Combine all ingredients. 
 Grease pie dish and set aside. 
 Mix bread crumbs with the other ½ stick of  margarine . 
 Press this mixture in the bottom and side of a 9" pie dish. 
 Fill with  chicken  and  onion  mixture. 
 Garnish top with grated  cheese . 
 Bake for about 30 minutes in moderate oven 350°F / 180°C.
