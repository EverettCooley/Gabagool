 Ingredients : 
 1 lb fresh  yellow  or  green beans 
 1 small carton  table cream  15% 
 6 large  potatoes  (diced) 
 salt 
 1 lb  sausage  (must get from your Slovak or Hungarian butcher) 
 1 tbsp  lard 
 1 tbsp  flour 
 1 tbsp  paprika 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 ½ tsp  garlic powder 
 1 small ladle  white vinegar 
 Directions : 
 Fill large dutch kettle with 2 quarts  water . 
 Add  sausage  (cut in 3 pieces) and beans (cut up). 
 Bring to boil lower to medium and cook for about one half hour. 
 Add diced  potatoes  and  salt  and cook another half hour. 
 In small saucepan melt  lard , add  flour ,  paprika ,  pepper  and  garlic . 
 Mix well and cook over medium heat for about 4-5 minutes. 
 Add this mixture to soup and mix well. 
 Add  vinegar  and simmer for 5 minutes. 
 In  cream  carton mix 1 tbsp  flour  and pinch of  baking soda . 
 Add to soup and bring to boil simmer 5 minutes and serve.
