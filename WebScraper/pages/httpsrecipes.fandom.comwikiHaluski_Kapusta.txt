Description : 
 Noodles and cabbage
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ lb  butter 
 2 chopped  onions 
 1 small head of  cabbage  chopped 
 salt  and  pepper  and  garlic powder 
 noodles 
 Directions : 
 Melt ½ lb  butter  in pot fry 2 chopped  onions . 
 Add 1 small head of  cabbage  chopped. 
 Fry till browned add  salt  and  pepper  and  garlic powder . 
 Make a double batch of noodles mix into fried  cabbage  cook on low till hot.
