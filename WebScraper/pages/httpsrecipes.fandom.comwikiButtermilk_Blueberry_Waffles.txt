Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Ritchie Estate in Balch Springs, Texas in 1984.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups  instant oatmeal 
 1½ cups  all-purpose flour  sifted 
 2 teaspoons  baking powder 
 2 tablespoons  sugar 
 2 cups  buttermilk 
 3  eggs 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 ⅓ cup  oil 
 2 cups  blueberries 
 Directions : 
 Mix together oatmeal, flour, baking powder and sugar. 
 Beat buttermilk, eggs, vanilla and oil. 
 Add liquid to dry ingredients and stir until moistened. 
 Fold in blueberries and spoon into preheated, lightly oiled waffle iron.
