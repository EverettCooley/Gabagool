Ingredients : 
 1 cup  almond milk 
 1  banana 
 ½ cup frozen mixed berries 
 ½ cup fresh  cranberries 
 Instructions : 
 Blend almond milk, banana, mixed berries, and cranberries in a blender until smooth 
 Refrigerate until chilled, at least 1 hour.
