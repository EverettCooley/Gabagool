 Recipe reprinted with permission from healthrecipes.com
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½  seedless cucumber , finely diced (or 3  dill cucumbers ) 
 1 ripe and juicy red  tomato , diced 
 1 tsp  Spanish onion , finely chopped 
 ¼ tsp  onion powder 
 ¼ tsp  garlic powder 
 ½ tsp  fruit sugar 
 Braggs All Purpose Seasoning , or  sea salt  to taste (optional) 
 1 tbsp good health  mayonnaise 
 Directions : 
 Mix the ingredients together, and enjoy.
