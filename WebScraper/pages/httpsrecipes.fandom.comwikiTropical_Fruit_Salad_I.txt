Description : 
 Contributed by Lilly at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 20 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 Salad : 
 2  cantaloupes  cut in small pieces or balls 
 1  honeydew melon , cut in small pieces or balls 
 2  pineapples , cubed 
 3  bananas , peeled, sliced crosswise 
 4 New Zealand  star fruits , sliced crosswise  [1] 
 1 lb seedless  grapes 
 strawberries , hulled 
 kumquats   [2] 
 kiwi  fruits, peeled, sliced  [3] 
 Sauce : 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 ¼ cup grated fresh  coconut 
 ¼ cup  apricot preserves 
 2 tbsp dry  white wine 
 ½ cup chopped  macadamia nuts 
 Directions : 
 Combine all fruits in giant clam-shell or large bowl. 
 In another bowl, combine sauce ingredients; stir. 
 Serve sauce separately in hollowed-out coconut shell or bowl. 
 Notes : 
 
 ↑   Star fruits can be purchased fresh in season at specialty groceries. 
 
 ↑   Kumquats can be purchased fresh at specialty groceries or canned in gourmet shops. 
 
 ↑   Kiwi fruits are available fresh at groceries almost year round.
