Description : 
       Cream of Potatoes Soup     
 In Romanian: Supa de cartofi (crema)
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 qts / 2 l  water 
 1 lb / 500 g  potatoes 
 1  onion 
 1 cup  sour cream  or  yogurt 
 1 teaspoon  flour 
 1 tablespoon chopped  parsley 
 1 teaspoon  butter 
 salt 
 toasted bread 
 Directions : 
 Boil the cubed  potatoes  with the finely chopped  onion . 
 When they are done, strain. 
 Sieve the  potatoes  and thin with some of the boiling liquid. 
 Add  salt  and set to boil. 
 When the soup starts to boil, add the  sour cream  or  yogurt  well mixed with the  flour . 
 Let boil for a minute or two, add the chopped  parsley  and  butter . 
 Serve with toast.
