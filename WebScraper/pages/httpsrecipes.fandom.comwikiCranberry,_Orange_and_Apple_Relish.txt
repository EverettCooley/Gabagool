Description : 
 Contributed by  Pressurecookerrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Kuhn Rikon Switzerland Web-Site 
 15 minutes 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large  Granny Smith apple , peeled and diced 
 3 cups (1 pkg) fresh  cranberries 
 ¼ cup  orange juice 
 1 cup  granulated sugar 
 ½ tsp ground  cinnamon 
 zest of 1 whole  orange 
 Directions : 
 Place all the ingredients in a 4-qt or larger cooker and stir well to blend. 
 Close the lid and bring pressure to the first red ring (8psi). 
 Adjust heat to stabilize and cook 5 minutes. 
 Allow the pressure to subside naturally, off heat. 
 Serve room temperature.
