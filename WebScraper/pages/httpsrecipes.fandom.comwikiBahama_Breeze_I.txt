Ingredients : 
 1 oz  dark rum 
 ½ oz  banana liqueur 
 ½ oz  apricot liqueur 
 ½ oz  coconut rum 
 ¼ oz  grenadine 
 ¼ oz  honey 
 ½ oz  lemon juice 
 1 oz  orange juice 
 1 oz  pineapple juice 
 Directions : 
 Blend well with a couple ice cubes. Pour over ice in tall glass. Garnish with  orange ,  cherry , etc. 
 Got this recipe from the bartender at our hotel on Elbow Cay years ago.
