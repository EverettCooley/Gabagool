Description : 
 Makes 12 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ quarts cooked  rice , cooled 
 1 cup minced  onion 
 1 cup sliced  celery 
 ¼ cup crisp  bacon bits 
 1 cup  sweet pickle relish 
 ½ cup diced  pimentos 
 6  hard-cooked eggs , chopped 
 1 tablespoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 2 cups  mayonnaise 
 2 teaspoons  prepared mustard 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  onion ,  celery ,  bacon  bits, relish, pimientos,  eggs ,  salt ,  pepper ,  mayonnaise  and  mustard ; blend thoroughly. 
 Serve on  lettuce  leaves. 
 Garnish with  cherry tomatoes .
