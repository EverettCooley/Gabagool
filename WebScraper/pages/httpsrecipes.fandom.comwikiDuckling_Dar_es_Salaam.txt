Ingredients : 
 1 cup  onions  finely chopped 
 1 cup  tomatoes  in small dice 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  curry powder  (optional) 
 ½ tsp  crushed red pepper  (optional) 
 4 tbsp  oil  or  margarine 
 6 lb  duckling , cut up into about 12 pieces 
 2 quarts  water 
 slightly green  bananas  or  plantains  (allow ½  banana  per portion or 4 large  bananas ) 
 Directions : 
 Take the onion, tomato, salt, curry powder and crushed red pepper and fry in oil until soft. 
 Add the duckling and water. 
 Cover and simmer for 30 minutes or bake at 350°F until duckling is tender. 
 Slightly green  bananas  or  plantains  may be simmered with the duckling or they may be prepared separately and served as a side vegetable.
