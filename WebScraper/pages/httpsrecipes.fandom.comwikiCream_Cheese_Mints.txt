Description 
 Cream Cheese Mints  from the Wikibooks Cookbook—original source of recipe, licensed under the GNU Free Documentation License
 
 Cook Time : 20 minutes 
 Serves : 50 
 Ingredients 
 4 oz / 125 g softened  cream cheese 
 3½ cups / 875 ml  icing sugar 
 ⅛ teaspoon / 0.5 ml  peppermint extract 
 ¾ cup / 175 ml  granulated sugar 
 Directions 
 Combine cream cheese, icing sugar and peppermint flavouring in large bowl. 
 Stir until mixed. 
 Roll dough into small balls. 
 Roll balls in bowl of icing sugar. 
 Press small balls with bottom of glass until flat. Chill. 
 Store in airtight container.
