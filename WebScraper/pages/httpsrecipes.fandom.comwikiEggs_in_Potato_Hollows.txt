 Source: Marlboro Country Cookbook 
 Makes 8 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups cold  mashed potatoes 
 ½ cup crumbled crisp  bacon  or diced  ham 
 ¼ cup minced or grated  onion 
 ¼ cup chopped fresh herbs, optional ( tarragon ,  dill ,  parsley , etc.) 
 ½ tsp  black pepper 
 bacon fat  or  butter 
 ½ cup dry  bread crumbs 
 8  eggs 
 1 cup shredded cheese ( cheddar ,  jack , etc.) 
 Directions : 
 Combine  mashed potatoes ,  bacon ,  onion , herbs and  pepper , stirring until well mixed. 
 Grease a shallow baking pan with  bacon fat . 
 Sprinkle with  bread  crumbs. 
 Spoon 8 mounds of  potato  mixture onto pan. 
 With back of spoon, make deep depressions large enough to contain  eggs  in the  potatoes . 
 Bake in a 450 oven for 10 minutes or until  potatoes  are hot. 
 Reduce heat to 350°F. 
 Drop  eggs  into  potato  hollows. 
 Bake for 20 minutes or until  eggs  are set. 
 Sprinkle with  cheese . 
 Return to oven for a few minutes until  cheese  melts and  eggs  are done.
