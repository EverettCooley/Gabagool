Description : 
 Lithuanian coffee cake
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cakes  yeast , softened in ¼ c. lukewarm  water  sweetened with ¼ tsp.  sugar 
 1 cup  milk 
 1 stick  butter 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 tsp  salt 
 2  eggs 
 5 cups  flour 
 2 tsp  vanilla 
 1 cup  golden raisins 
 Directions : 
 Soften  yeast  in  water  and  sugar ; set aside. 
 Heat  milk  below boiling, add  butter ,  sugar  and  salt . 
 Cool and add  eggs , 1 at a time, beating in bowl. 
 Add  flour , softened  yeast  and  vanilla . 
 Wait 15 minutes and knead dough until dough becomes satiny. 
 Place dough in a greased bowl in a warm place. 
 After it doubles in bulk (about 2 hours) punch down. 
 Add 1 cup  golden raisins  and knead to distribute them. 
 Divide in half and form 2 loaves. 
 Allow to rise 1 hour. 
 Bake 350°F for about 45 minutes.
