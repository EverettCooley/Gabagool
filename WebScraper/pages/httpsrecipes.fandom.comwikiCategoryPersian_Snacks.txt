 There are several Persian snacks worth mentioning. 
 
 GERDU is a popular snack in Iran – it consists of peeled walnuts which are boiled extensively in water. 
 After the thin walnut peel is removed they areserved salted. 
 TCHAGALEH BADOUM is a snack consisting of almonds, also served with salt. 
 Roasted corn, or BALAL, is yet another popular snack in Iran. 
 Corn on the cob is roasted on a charcoal fire in the traditional way – however, Iranians also salt the corn by dipping it into salted water after it is roasted.
