 Description : 
 Serves 6.
 
 
       Fried Rice Fiesta     
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4	x	 radishes , trimmed, sliced 
 1	tsp	 rice wine vinegar 
 3	x	cloves  garlic , minced 
 1	tsp	 sesame oil 
 4	x	Scallions 
 4	oz	 Mushrooms , thinly sliced 
 3	cup	Cooked  brown rice 
 3	oz	Frozen  Peas , defrosted 
 2	oz	 water chestnuts , sliced 
 1	tbl	Soy sauce 
 Directions : 
 Cook  rice . 
 Combine  radishes  with  rice wine vinegar  in a small bowl and reserve. 
 Place  garlic  and  sesame oil  in a saucepan. 
 Add scallions and  Mushrooms  and saute until tender. 
 Add  brown rice ,  radishes  and remaining ingredients. 
 Combine. 
 Serve hot.
