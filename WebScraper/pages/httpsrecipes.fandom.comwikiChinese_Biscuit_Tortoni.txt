 Description : 
 Bursting with flavor!  almond ,  rum , and  chocolate  in a smooth, creamy base. Great finale for a dinner party or cookout. To serve, spoon the biscuit tortoni into individual old-fashioned  ice   cream  dishes or custard cups. " Original recipe yield: 8 servings.
 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup chopped  blanched almonds 
 5 ounces amarettini cookies 
 3 tablespoons  dark rum 
 3/4 cup  semisweet chocolate  chips 
 1 cup  heavy cream , chilled 
 3 tablespoons  brandy 
 1 quart  vanilla   ice   cream , softened
