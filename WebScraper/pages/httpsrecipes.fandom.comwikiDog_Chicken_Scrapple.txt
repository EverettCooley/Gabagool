 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  cornmeal 
 ½ cup  oatmeal 
 1 ½ cups  chicken stock  ( look up directions  for preparation) 
 1 cup boiled  chicken , cut up into small pieces 
 ¼ cup thinly chopped baby  cabbage 
 ½ teaspoon ground  thyme 
 salt 
 ground  black pepper 
 vegetable oil  or  butter  for frying 
 Directions : 
 Use a pot and combine the  cornmeal ,  oatmeal  and  chicken stock . 
 Cook these things in boiling  water  for approximately 30 minutes. 
 Add in the  chicken , baby  cabbage ,  thyme ,  salt , and  pepper  to taste, stir all this well. 
 This mixture should be quite thick. 
 Grease a bread pan and pour in the  cornmeal  combination. 
 Smooth the top with a spatula, then, cover it with a plastic wrap, and put it in the refrigerator overnight. 
 Cut the  chicken  scrapple into slices. 
 Over a frying pan or skillet, heat up the  oil  or  butter  over medium heat. 
 Next, add in the scrapple, and fry slices on both sides until scrapples are golden brown.
