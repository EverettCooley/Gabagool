Description : 
 These almost fatless biscotti-style cookies are idea for dipping in your .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup chopped  cherries 
 1 cup  pistachios 
 ⅓ cup  rum 
 4  egg whites  and 2  eggs 
 2 tsp  vanilla extract 
 1 tsp  almond extract 
 1 tbs  orange zest 
 ⅔ cups  sugar 
 1¾  plain flour 
 1 cup  whole wheat flour 
 2 tsp  cinnamon 
 2 tsp  baking powder 
 1 cup unprocessed  wheat bran 
 Directions : 
 In a saucepan simmer  cherries  and  rum  over medium heat. 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Stir and cover to let set for 10 minutes, uncover and cool for 10 minutes. 
 Cover a baking sheet with foil and coat with non-stick cooking spray. 
 Sift all-purpose  flour , whole wheat flour,  baking powder ,  sugar , and  cinnamon . 
 Stir in wheat  bran . 
 In a mixing bowl, whisk  egg  whites,  egg ,  orange  zest,  vanilla  and  almond   extracts  until smooth. 
 whisk in  cherry  and  rum  mixture. 
 Stir in dry ingredients until well combined. 
 Turn dough onto floured work surface and divide in half. 
 Flour each half and shape to a log. 
 Place logs onto baking sheet and bake until light brown and puffed approx. 20 minutes. 
 Cool 10 min. or up to several hours. 
 Transfer logs to work surface and cut into biscotti-style pieces. 
 Put back in oven for 12 minutes. turn and brown on both sides 6 minutes. each. 
 Let cool and store in container or serve warm.
