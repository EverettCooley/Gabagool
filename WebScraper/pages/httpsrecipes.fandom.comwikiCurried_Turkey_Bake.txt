Description : 
 Makes 2 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup cooked  rice 
 ¾ cup diced cooked  turkey  or  chicken 
 ½ cup chopped tart cooking  apple 
 ¼ cup sliced  celery 
 2 tablespoons  raisins 
 2 tablespoons finely chopped  onion 
 ½ cup medium  white sauce 
 1 tablespoon  sherry 
 ½ teaspoon  curry  powder 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt  (optional) 
 1/16 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 flaked  coconut  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  turkey ,  apple ,  celery ,  raisins  and  onion  in medium mixing bowl. 
 Blend white sauce,  sherry ,  curry ,  salt  and  pepper  in small bowl. 
 Turn into buttered, shallow 1-quart baking dish. 
 Add to  rice  mixture. 
 Bake, uncovered, at 350 °F for 25 to 30 minutes, or until heated through. 
 Sprinkle with  coconut .
