Ingredients : 
 1 medium  onion 
 1  celery  stalk, chop fine 
 1 medium  carrot , chop fine 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 4½ cups  water 
 1 can  tomato paste 
 1 tsp  chili powder 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  paprika 
 ½ tsp  black pepper 
 ½ tsp  oregano 
 ½ tsp  cumin 
 ¼ tsp  allspice , ground 
 ¼ tsp  cloves 
 ¼ tsp  nutmeg 
 1  bay leaf 
 ⅓ cup  Bulgar wheat 
 Directions : 
 In a heavy saucepan, sauté  onion ,  celery , and  carrot  (I use  balsamic vinegar ). 
 Stir in  garlic  and cook another minute. 
 Add  water ,  tomato  paste and spices. 
 Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer 30 minutes. 
 Add  Bulgar wheat  and cook another 30 minutes. 
 Add more  water  if necessary. 
 Remove  bay leaf  before serving. 
 Serving suggestions: on  spaghetti  topped with  chili  and ff  cheddar  cheese,  onions  and / or  pinto beans . 
 You could add other vegetables such as  zucchini ,  peppers  or  onions , and top with  yogurt . 
 Purists demand oyster crackers.
