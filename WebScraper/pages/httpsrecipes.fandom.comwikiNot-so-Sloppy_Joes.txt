Description : 
 Contributed by Lil at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Reynolds™ 
 Ingredients : 
 1 Reynolds™ slow cooker liner 
 2 lb  ground beef , cooked, drained 
 1 large  onion , chopped 
 1 medium  green bell pepper , chopped 
 2 cups  ketchup 
 1 cup  barbecue sauce 
 1 – 2 tbsp each  vinegar  and  Worcestershire sauce , optional 
 1 tbsp  prepared mustard 
 buns or rolls 
 Directions : 
 Open slow cooker liner and place inside 5 – 6-qt slow cooker bowl. 
 Place  beef ,  onion  and  bell pepper  in lined-slow cooker in even layer. 
 Combine remaining ingredients in medium bowl. 
 Pour over  beef  and vegetables. 
 Place lid on slow cooker. 
 Cook on low 7 – 8 hours or on high 4 – 5 hours, until vegetables are tender. 
 Carefully remove lit to allow steam to escape. 
 Gently stir Not-so-Sloppy Joes; serve directly from lined-slow cooker. 
 Do not lift or transport liner with food inside. 
 Cool slow cooker completely; remove liner and toss.
