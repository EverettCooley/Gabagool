California Quiche
 
 1 Deep Dish Pie Crust Shell 
 3/4 Pound Hot Italian  Sausage 
 3 Lightly Beaten  eggs 
 1 3/4 Cup  milk 
 2 Cups Shredded Monterey  jack cheese  (8 Oz) 
 1 Can Old el Paso Chopped Green Chilies (1/4 Cup, or 4 Oz. Can) 
 
Prebake Unpricked Pie Crust on Preheated Cookie Sheet in 450 Oven For 6 Minutes, According to Directions. Remove Pie Curst From Oven. Reduce Oven Temperature to 325. Crumble  Sausage , Removing From Casing if Needed. Cook  Sausage  in Skillet Until Brown, Breaking up Meat With a Fork. Drain Well. in Mixing Bowl, Combine  eggs ,  milk , Sausage ,  Cheese  And Chilies. Turn Into Partially Baked  pastry shell . Bake in 325 Oven on Preheated Cookie Sheet For 45 Minutes or Until Knife Inserted Off Center Comes Out Clean. Let Stand About 10 Minutes Before Serving. Serves 6
