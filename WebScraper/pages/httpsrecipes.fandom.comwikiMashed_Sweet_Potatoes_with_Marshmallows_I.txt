Description : 
 Rating difficulty: easy with an electric mixer. 
 Time: 15 minutes preparation, 30 minutes baking. 
 Precision: approximate measurement ok. 
 Ingredients : 
 2¼ lbs  sweet potatoes  (canned or precooked) 
 2 cup  milk 
 3 tbsp  brown sugar 
 3 tbsp melted  butter 
 ½ tbsp  salt 
 dash  pepper 
 dash  cinnamon 
 ¼ cup  raisins 
 marshmallows 
 Directions : 
 Mash  sweet potatoes  by putting through a sieve or beating in an electric beater or using a  potato  masher or food mill. 
 Add  milk  gradually (if you add it too fast it splashes and is hard to mix in). 
 Mix in remaining ingredients. 
 Put in buttered casserole. 
 Heat through (bake in slow oven (250°F) for 20–30 minutes, or in microwave). 
 Cover top with marshmallows. 
 Return to oven until marshmallows are slightly melted and a bit brown (if the oven is too hot, the marshmallows will disappear!) this step can- not be done in the microwave.
