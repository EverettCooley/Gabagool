Description : 
       Salata po Polsku     
 Serves 6 
 Ingredients : 
 2 crisp  iceberg lettuce 
 1 tablespoons chopped  dill 
 1 teaspoon  lemon juice 
 150 ml  sour cream 
 salt  and  sugar 
 2  eggs , hard-boiled and diced 
 Directions : 
 Separate leaves of the  lettuce  and was and dry it thoroughly. 
 Tear into bite-size pieces and arrange in a salad bowl. 
 Meanwhile make the dressing by add  sour cream , chopped  dill ,  lemon juice ,  salt  and  sugar . 
 Pour over salad and toss well. 
 Sprinkle with diced  eggs  and serve.
