Description : 
 "Batida de coco" is also a popular drink in Brazil.
 
 Ingredientes / Ingredients : 
 
 
 Portuguese 
 
 English 
 
 
 
 1 latinha de leite de coco 
 4 colheres de açúcar 
 2 doses de cachaça 
 8 pedras de gelo 
 
 
 1 small can of  coconut milk  (165 ml) 
 4 tablespoons of  sugar 
 2 shots of  sugar cane spirits 
 8 ice cubes 
 
 Modo de fazer / Directions : 
 
 
 Portuguese 
 
 English 
 
 
 
 Bata o leite de coco, o açúcar e a cachaça no liquidificador. 
 Coloque a mistura numa jarra. 
 Adicione oito pedras de gelo e sirva. 
 
 
 Mix the coconut milk, the sugar and the sugar cane spirits in the blender. 
 Put the mixture in a jug. 
 Add eight ice cubes and serve it.
