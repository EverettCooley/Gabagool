 Ingredients : 
 4 lbs back  ribs 
 2 cups  hoisin sauce 
 4 Tbsp  catsup 
 4 Tbsp  honey 
 4 Tbsp  soy sauce 
 ⅔ cup orange  marmalade 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 Parboil ribs for 15 minutes in boiling  water  to cover. 
 Drain and put in shallow foil-lined baking pan. 
 Mix remaining ingredients and pour half the mixture over the ribs. Reserve remainder to use when serving ribs. 
 Bake 45 to 60 minutes, basting with sauce in pan every 15 minutes.
