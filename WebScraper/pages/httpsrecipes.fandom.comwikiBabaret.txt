Ingredients : 
 2 oz  bourbon 
 1 oz  dry vermouth 
 2 tsp  blackberry brandy 
 2 tsp  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Shake the ingredients and pour them into a cocktail glass. 
 Garnish with  lemon peel .
