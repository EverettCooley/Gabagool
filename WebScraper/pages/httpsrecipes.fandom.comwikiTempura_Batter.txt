Ingredients : 
 12 oz  beer 
 1 ½ cups  flour 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine ingredients in a medium sized bowl. 
 Allow mixture to stand for one hour before using. 
 Dip raw vegetables,  chicken ,  fish  or cut into bite-sized pieces and fry in two inches of hot  oil  until golden brown. 
 Best if used cold.
