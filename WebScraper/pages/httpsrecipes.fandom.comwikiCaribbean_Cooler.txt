Description : 
 This won an award for Robert Parsons of the Benson Hotel, Portland, in an Automobile Club of Oregon Zero Proof Mixoff contest.
 
 Contributed by Stephen Ceideburg 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp Torani  orgeat  (almond syrup) 
 2 tbsp fresh  lemon juice 
 6 drops  bitters 
 4 shakes ground  nutmeg 
 Â½ cup fresh  orange juice 
 1 crushed  ice 
 1  lime  slice, optional garnish 
 Directions : 
 Into a cocktail shaker or similar container, put syrup,  lemon juice ,  bitters ,  nutmeg ,  orange juice  and crushed  ice . 
 Shake and strain into chilled chimney glasses, filled with  ice cubes . 
 Garnish with  lime  slices, if desired.

 Exchanges: 2 fruit. Makes 1 serving
