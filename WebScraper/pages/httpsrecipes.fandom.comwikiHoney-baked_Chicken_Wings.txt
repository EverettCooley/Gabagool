Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source:  Come Into the Kitchen 
 Serves 6 to 8 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds  chicken wings 
 ½ cup melted  butter  or  margarine 
 ½ cup  honey 
 ¼ cup  prepared mustard 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  curry powder 
 Directions : 
 Discard tip of wing. 
 Cut remaining wing in 2 pieces. 
 Place chicken pieces in shallow pan. 
 Combine all ingredients and pour over chicken wings. Bake at 350°F for 1 hour or until done, basting every 15 minutes, until chicken is tender and nicely browned.
