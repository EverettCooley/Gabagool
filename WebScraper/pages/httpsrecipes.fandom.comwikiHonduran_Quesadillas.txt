Ingredients : 
 1½ cups  margarine 
 3 cups  white sugar 
 4 cups sifted  all-purpose flour 
 6 cups  rice flour 
 3 tablespoon  baking powder 
 2 cups warm  milk 
 6 large  eggs 
 3½ cups  Parmesan cheese 
 ½ cup  white sugar 
 ¼ cup  all-purpose flour 
 ½ cup  sesame seeds 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). 
 Lightly grease and  flour  a medium glass baking dish. 
 In a bowl, beat together  margarine  and 3 cups  sugar  until fluffy. 
 Alternately beat in 2 cups  all-purpose flour ,  rice flour ,  baking powder , and  milk . 
 Slowly beat in the  eggs  and  Parmesan cheese . 
 Transfer to the prepared baking dish. 
 In a small bowl, mix the ½ cup  sugar , ¼ cup  all-purpose flour , and  sesame seeds . 
 Sprinkle evenly over the batter in the cake pan. 
 Bake 20 minutes in the preheated oven, until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. 
 Cool completely, and cut into squares.
