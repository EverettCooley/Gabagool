 Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Steep Estate in Elmo, Texas in 1989.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 12 hard-boiled  eggs 
 ⅔ cup  mayonnaise 
 ⅓ cup finely shredded smoked  cheddar  cheese 
 1 teaspoon  lemon juice 
 ¼ teaspoon seasoned  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  cumin 
 ⅛ teaspoon ground  cayenne 
 Directions : 
 Cut  eggs  in half. 
 Remove yolks and set whites aside. 
 Mash yolks well in medium bowl then beat in remaining ingredients until fluffy. 
 Using a pastry bag pipe yolk mixture back into whites then cover and chill.
