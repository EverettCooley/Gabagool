Description : 
 Milk with cardamom.  Cardamom  helps the digestive system and is a spice greatly relied on in this country. Boiled with  milk ,  cardamom  helps to relax the body and is thought to calm the stomach, thus offering a good night's rest. Generally this drink is served hot in large English teacups or small  coffee  cups.
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 cups  fresh milk 
 sugar  to taste 
 1 teaspoon ground  cardamom 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in a medium pot and bring to a rapid boil, stirring constantly. 
 Boil for one or two minutes. 
 Remove from heat, transfer to a vacuum flask and keep warm until ready to drink.
