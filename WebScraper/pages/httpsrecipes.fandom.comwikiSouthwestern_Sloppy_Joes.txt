Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Original recipe 
 Source:  Diabetic Cooking  Magazine 
 Submitted by: dlife 
 9 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound lean  ground beef  round 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 ¼ cup chopped  celery 
 ¼ cup  water 
 1 can (10 ounces) diced  tomatoes  and green chilies, undrained 
 1 can (8 ounces) no-salt-added  tomato sauce 
 4 teaspoons  brown sugar 
 ½ teaspoon ground  cumin 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 9 whole wheat  hamburger buns 
 Directions : 
 Heat large nonstick skillet over high heat. 
 Add beef, onion, celery and water. 
 Reduce heat to medium. 
 Cook and stir 5 minutes or until meat is no longer pink. 
 Drain fat. 
 Stir in tomatoes and green chilies, tomato sauce, brown sugar, cumin and salt; bring to a boil over high heat. 
 Reduce heat; simmer 20 minutes or until mixture thickens. 
 Serve on whole wheat buns. 
 Garnish as desired. 
 Green chilies add some heat to these sloppy joes, but if you prefer them plain, just add an equal amount of tomatoes without chilies. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving (1 bun with ⅓ cup meat mixture, without garnish):
