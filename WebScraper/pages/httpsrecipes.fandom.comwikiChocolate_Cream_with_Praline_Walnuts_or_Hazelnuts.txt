Description : 
 In Romanian: crema de ciocolata cu nuci sau alune pralinate
 
 Ingredients : 
 4  eggs 
 8 oz / 250 g  confectioner's sugar 
 10 oz / 300 g  butter 
 3 tablespoons  chocolate  or  cocoa 
 4 â€“ 5 tablespoons  Praline Walnut or Hazelnut Cream 
 2 tablespoons  milk 
 Directions : 
 Place in the  eggs  in a pan which is standing into a larger pan full of boiling  water , on medium heat. 
 Beat the  eggs  with the  sugar  until a  cream  is formed. 
 Remove from heat and leave until the  cream  is cool. 
 Add the  butter  in thin slices and beat well to make the paste very creamy. 
 Melt the  chocolate , on slow heat, with two tablespoons of warm  milk  or  water . 
 Add this to the  cream . 
 Then add the praline  hazelnuts  or  walnuts , mix, pour into cups and refrigerate.
