 Description : 
 1 Serving
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lb Lean sirloin  Steak , 1/2 inch thick 
 1/3 cup  olive oil 
 1 cup  onions , coarsely chopped 
 1 tsp  garlic , finely chopped 
 6 med  tomatoes , peel,seed,chop OR 
 2 cup Canned  tomatoes , drained and chopped 
 1/2 tsp Ground  cumin  seeds 
 1 tsp  salt 
 
 Directions : 
 Boil the  Steak  under a preheated broiler for 5 minuted on each side, 
 Using a knife, cut or pull the meat into pieces approximately 1/2 in (125 mm) long and 1/4 in (50  onions  and  garlic . 
 Cook for about 5 minutes 
 . Add the  tomatoes ,  cumin  and  salt  and reduce the heat to low. 
 Cook uncovered for about 30 minutes, 
 Add the strips of  Beef  and mix them well with the sauce.
