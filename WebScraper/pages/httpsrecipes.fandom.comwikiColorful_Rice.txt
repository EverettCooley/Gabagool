Makes 2 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/4 cup sliced  green onions 
 2 teaspoons  butter  or  margarine 
 1 cup cooked  rice 
 2 tablespoons sliced pimientos 
 salt  and ground  black pepper  to taste 
 1 tablespoon  sherry 
 1 2-ounce can sliced  Mushrooms , drained 
 2 tablespoons  sliced almonds , if desired 
 Directions : 
 Cook  onions  in  butter  until tender. 
 Add  rice , pimientos,  salt ,  pepper ,  sherry ,  Mushrooms  and  almonds ; cook until heated through. 
 Toss lightly. 
 Spoon into buttered custard cups, pressing to mold. 
 Unmold onto plates.
