Ingredients : 
 2  mangos , peeled and sliced 
 ½ cup shredded  coconut 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 ½ tsp  almond extract 
 2 tbsp  milk 
 1¼ cups  flour 
 ⅓ cup  sugar 
 ½ cup  butter 
 Directions : 
 Beat  butter  and  sugar  until light and fluffy. 
 Add  flour ,  milk  and  almond extract . 
 Beat at low speed until well blended. 
 Press dough onto bottom and sides of 10" tart pan with removable bottom. 
 Prick bottom with fork and bake at 400°F for 13 – 20 minutes until light golden brown. 
 Cool completely. 
 Stir together the  sour cream  and  sugar . 
 Spread over crust. 
 Sprinkle with  coconut . 
 Arrange  mangos  on top.
