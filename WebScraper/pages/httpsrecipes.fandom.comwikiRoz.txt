Description : 
 Plain white rice
 
 Ingredients : 
 450 g (1 lb)  long-grain rice 
 400 ml (14 fl oz) boiling  water 
 2 teaspoons  salt 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 2  cardamom  pods 
 Directions : 
 Wash  rice , drain and place in a medium saucepan. Add 400 ml (14 fl oz) of boiling  water . 
 Add  olive oil ,  salt ,  cardamom  and cover pan. Boil on high heat for 10 minutes, until the  water  has evaporated. 
 Lift the lid and give a quick stir, then place lid back on and simmer for 10 minutes on very low heat.
