Description : 
 A garden fresh  avocado  sandwich.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 slices of a thick grainy  bread 
 4 or 5 slices of ripe Genuine  California avocado 
 red onion  slices 
 fresh garden  tomato  slices 
 homemade  mayonnaise 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Spread a generous portion of mayonnaise on each slice of whole grain bread. 
 Layer  avocado ,  onion  and  tomato . 
 Liberally apply  salt  and  pepper  to  tomato . 
 Ready to serve!
