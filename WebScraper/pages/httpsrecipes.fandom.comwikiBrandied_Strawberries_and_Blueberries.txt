Contributed by Lilly at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 4 - 6 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 qt vanilla  ice cream 
 ½ cup  brandy 
 1 pint fresh  strawberries , hulled, sliced 
 ½ pint fresh  blueberries 
 orange-flavored liqueur 
 Directions : 
 In a medium bowl, beat  ice cream  and  brandy  with electric mixer on low speed until slightly softened. 
 Increase speed to med and beat until smooth. 
 Place mixture in freezer container. 
 Cover and freeze 8 hr or overnight; mixture will not freeze solid. 
 Chill 4 - 6 brandy snifters in freezer. 
 To serve: spoon  ice cream  mixture into chilled snifters. 
 Top with  strawberries  and  blueberries  and drizzle with  orange-flavored liqueur .
