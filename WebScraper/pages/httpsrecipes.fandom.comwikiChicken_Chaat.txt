Description : 
 Ethnicity - Punjabi, North Indian 
 Type of meal - Party, Lunch, Dinner 
 Ingredients : 
 500 g boneless  chicken 
 Â¾ cup chopped  onion 
 1 tsp  garam masala  powder 
 1 tbsp chopped  green coriander 
 1 tsp  dried mango powder 
 1 tsp chopped  green chile 
 2 tsp  lemon juice 
 1 tsp  red chile powder 
 1 tbsp  oil 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Clean and cut the  chicken  into 1 inch cubes. 
 Boil  chicken  pieces in salted boiling  water  for 1 minute. 
 Drain excess  water  and chill  chicken  in a refrigerator. 
 Mix chopped  onions ,  garam masala  powder, dry  mango  powder, chopped green chilies, red  chili powder ,  salt , chopped  green coriander ,  lemon juice  and  oil  with cooked  chicken  pieces. 
 Arrange in a serving platter or bowl. 
 Decorate with  tomato  slices or a  tomato  rose.
