Ingredients : 
 1 cup  butter 
 1½ cups  sugar 
 3  egg yolks 
 1 tbsp  vanilla 
 2¾ cups  flour 
 1 tsp  soda 
 ⅛ tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 ½ tsp  cloves 
 2 tbsp  sour milk 
 4½ cups  nuts 
 1 lb chopped  dates 
 Directions : 
 Cream  butter  and  sugar . 
 Add  egg yolks  and  vanilla . 
 Beat at medium speed 2 or 3 minutes. 
 Sift dry ingredients and add to above. 
 Add  sour milk . 
 Blend by hand. 
 Add  nuts  and  dates . 
 Drop by ½ teaspoons on cookie sheet. 
 Bake at 325 degrees for 10 to 15 minutes. 
 You may refrigerate dough and use as née.ded.
