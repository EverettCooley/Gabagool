Ingredients : 
 200 grams  pumpkin  peeled 
 180 grams lobster meat 
 15 ml  Peanut ,  oil 
 50 ml coconnut  cream 
 20 grams  garlic  chopped 
 5 ml  rice vinegar 
 3 grams coriander chopped 
 6 grams  salt 
 200 ml  Pork  stock 
 Directions : 
 Hot  oil , fried lobster meat with  pumpkin , garlic . Put in stock,coconnut  cream , simmer 20mins. Blend and seasoning. Garnish with coriander.
