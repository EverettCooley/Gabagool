 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 24 c.  dates  or  Raisins , (24 to 2 1/2) 
 2  eggs 
 1/4 cup  vegetable oil 
 2 cups  flour , self rising 
 1/2 cup chopped  nuts 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 2 tsp liquid sweetener 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 
 
 Directions : 
 If using all purpose  flour , add 1/2 teaspoon  salt  and 1 teaspoon  baking powder . 
 Use 350 degree oven. Put cut  dates  or  Raisins  into bowl; sprinkle with  baking soda  and cover with 1 cup boiling  water . Let stand until cool.
