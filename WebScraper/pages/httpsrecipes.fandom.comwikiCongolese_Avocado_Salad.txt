 Description : 
 This is a rare recipe for Congolese  avocado  Salad.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3-4 avocados, with 1/2  avocado  per person 
 For the filling:
 
 1/4 cup shelled shrimps 
 1/4 cup  Tuna  (tinned) 
 1/4 cup cold salmon flaked (fresh or tinned) 
 1/4 cup mixed vegetable salad diced (of your choice) 
 Directions : 
 Wash and cut  avocado  in half lengthwise and remove the stone. 
Blend selected filling with sufficient salad  cream  or French dressing to moisten the mixture. Fill centre of  avocado  and decorate with  paprika   pepper . 
Serve chilled on  lettuce  leaves.
