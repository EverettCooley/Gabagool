Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Yield: 4 Servings         
 Ingredients : 
 8 large, cleaned  shrimp 
 4 tbsp. lime or  lemon juice 
 ½ cup  butter 
 9 tbsp. chopped  parsley 
 3 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1 cup  breadcrumbs 
 2 tsp.  paprika 
 hot sauce, optional 
 Directions : 
 Butterfly  shrimp  only and do not cut all the way. Spread and flatten. 
 Place shrimps in individual baking dishes. 
 Sprinkle with a portion of  lemon juice . 
 Combine  butter , 5 tbsp.  parsley , and  garlic  in a fresh, large bowl. 
 Place some of the mixture on each  shrimp . 
 Combine crumbs, the remaining  parsley , and  paprika  and place some on each  shrimp . Bake at 400° F for 15 minutes. 
 Garnish with lemon wedges. Serve with rice, noodles, or mashed potatoes. Drizzle with the hot sauce, if desired.
