Description : 
 The sauce can also be served as a dessert sauce for pound cake,  bread  pudding, pancakes, angel food cake or  ice cream .
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 Pancake mix (or your favourite  pancake recipe ) 
 Strawberry sauce : 
 1 Package (16 ounces) frozen  strawberries , thawed (the kind with juice) 
 2 Teaspoons  cornstarch 
 Directions : 
 Follow the instructions on the package mix package or use your favourite recipe. 
 Serve, topped with strawberry sauce 
 Strawberry sauce : 
 In a medium saucepan, combine juice From  strawberries  and  cornstarch . 
 Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture thickens. 
 Cook 2 minutes longer. 
 Add  strawberries  and heat to serving temperature. Do not overcook the  strawberries .
