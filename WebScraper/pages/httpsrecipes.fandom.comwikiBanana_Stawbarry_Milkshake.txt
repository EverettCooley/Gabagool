 3.1   Milkshake Videos 
 
 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 bananas cut in slices 
 2 cup  ice cream  of stawberry shortcake 
 2 cup  ice 
 3/4 cup  milk . 
 Directions : 
 Put sliced bananas in blender. 
 Put  ice   cream  in blender. 
 Put  ice  in blender. 
 Put  milk  in blender. 
 Grind Ingredients then blend on high then low 
 serve in milkshakes glasses.
