Ingredients : 
 1 bag  popcorn 
 mini round cookies 
 chocolate icing 
 chocolate kisses 
 star  sprinkles 
 Directions : 
 Unwrap kisses and set aside 
 Spread icing on the bottom of each kiss and place on a cookie 
 Mix with popcorn in a bowl 
 Sprinkle with stars
