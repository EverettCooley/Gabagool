time it takes: 10 minutes
 
 Ingredients : 
 One  tomato 
 One  cucumber 
 Half an  onion 
 One  lemon 
 Salt 
 Directions : 
 Dice the vegetables; mix 
 Cut the lemon in half; squeeze 
 Salt to taste
