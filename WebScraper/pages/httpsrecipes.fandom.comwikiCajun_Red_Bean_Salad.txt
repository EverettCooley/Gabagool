Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x 4.5 to 8-ounce package Cajun  rice  mix or jambalaya mix, prepared according to package directions 
 1 x 15-ounce can dark  red kidney beans , drained and rinsed 
 1 cup thinly sliced  green onions 
 1  celery  rib, chopped 
 1 medium-size  red bell pepper , julienned 
 1 medium-size  green bell pepper , julienned 
 ¼ cup  cider vinegar 
 2 tablespoons  olive oil 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1 teaspoon  chili powder 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  ground red pepper 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  beans ,  onions ,  celery , red pepper and  green pepper  in large bowl. 
 Combine  vinegar ,  oil ,  garlic ,  chili powder ,  salt  and red pepper in small bowl. 
 Whisk until well blended. 
 Pour over salad. 
 Refrigerate 1 hour. 
 Serve cold or at room temperature.
