 Ingredients : 
 4 pounds  beef  or pork  spareribs 
 ¼ cup  olive oil 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 4 tablespoons  butter 
 1 medium  white onion  chopped 
 5  garlic  cloves minced 
 2 large ripe  tomatoes  chopped 
 6 ounce can  tomato paste 
 ¼ teaspoon  Tabasco sauce 
 2 teaspoons  Worcestershire sauce 
 ½ cup  brown sugar 
 1 heaping tablespoon  Dijon mustard 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon  cayenne pepper 
 ½ cup  red wine 
 1  lemon juiced 
 Directions : 
 Start charcoal or wood or fire up your gas grill. 
 Rub the slab of ribs all over with the olive oil then sprinkle liberally with salt and pepper and rub it in. 
 Cover and set aside. 
 In a large skillet over medium heat melt butter. 
 When it begins to bubble add onion and garlic and sauté 3 minutes. 
 Add all remaining ingredients then stir and turn heat to medium low and cook 30 minutes. 
 When grill is ready put ribs bone side down in center of grill. 
 Cover grill and open vents enough to make sure there is air flow then grill ribs for 1 hour turning twice during cooking. 
 Baste every 10 minutes with sauce.
