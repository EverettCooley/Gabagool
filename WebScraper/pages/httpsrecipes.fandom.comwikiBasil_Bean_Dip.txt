Desription : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cloves  garlic , peeled 
 1 x 19 oz can  white beans , drained and rinsed 
 ¼ cup  olive oil 
 4 tsp  wine  or herb  vinegar 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 ⅛ tsp  cayenne pepper 
 ¼ cup fresh  basil , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Blanch garlic for 2 minutes in boiling water. 
 Cool. 
 Mash garlic, beans, oil, vinegar, salt and pepper until smooth. 
 Stir in fresh, chopped basil. 
 Chill.
