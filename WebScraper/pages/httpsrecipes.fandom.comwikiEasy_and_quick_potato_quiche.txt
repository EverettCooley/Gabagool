 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 mashed  Potato  -- enough to 
 line a med 
 1 cup mixed vegetables—diced 
 and cooked 
 1/2  Onion  -- chopped. 
 1/2 cup  corn  -- cooked 
 1/4 cup  Peas  -- cooked 
 1/4 cup  green beans  -- cooked 
 pn  garlic ,  Cayenne ,  basil ,  sage 
 to taste 
 1 1/2 cup  cottage cheese , 1% fat 
 3  egg  whites—or equivalent 
 Directions : 
 Line a quiche dish with mashed  Potato . Mix all vegetables and herbs and spices together with the  Cheese  and  egg  whites. Spread over the  Potato  base. Bake in moderate oven for 35 to 40 minutes until the filling has set...
