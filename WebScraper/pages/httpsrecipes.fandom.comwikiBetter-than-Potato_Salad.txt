      Better-than-Potato Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 3½ cups Uncle Ben's  Converted brand rice 
 2 qt  water 
 1½ tbsp  salt 
 1¾ qt  mayonnaise 
 3 cups  onions , finely chopped 
 ¼ cup  prepared mustard 
 2 tsp  salt 
 1 qt  cucumber , pared and diced 
 2 cups  radishes , sliced 
 Directions : 
 Cook  rice  in  water  with  salt  according to package directions. 
 Cool. 
 Combine  mayonnaise ,  onion ,  mustard , and  salt . 
 Stir into  rice . 
 Cover and thoroughly chill. 
 Stir in  cucumber  and  radishes  just before serving.
