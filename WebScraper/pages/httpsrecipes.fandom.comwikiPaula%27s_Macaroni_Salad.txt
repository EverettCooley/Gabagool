 Description : 
 Here is an easy salad recipe you can prepare for your family or take to your next family get-together.
 
 Contributed by Paula in California at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb salad  macaroni 
 12 medium  green onions , sliced 
 4 hard-boiled  eggs , chopped 
 1 cup thinly sliced  celery 
 12 slices crisply cooked  bacon , crumbled 
 1 cup  dill pickles , chopped 
 1½ cups  mayonnaise 
 1 tbsp  mustard 
 1 tbsp  horseradish 
 1 tbsp dill pickle juice 
 Salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Cook macaroni according to package directions and drain well. Rinse and drain again. 
 Place macaroni into large bowl. Add onion, eggs, celery, and pickle. 
 Blend together the mayonnaise, horseradish, mustard, and pickle juice. 
 Stir into macaroni mixture. 
 Season to taste with salt and pepper. 
 Cover and chill 4 – 6 hours.
