 Ingredients : 
 6 thin  Pork  chops 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 1 cup  apple juice 
 1 cup  water 
 1 cup uncooked  rice 
 1 package dry  onion soup mix 
 1 red  Apple , cored and chopped 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in large skillet with lid over medium-high heat until hot. 
 Add  Pork  chops turning to brown all sides. 
 Sprinkle  onion soup mix  over chops. 
 Add  apple juice ,  water ,  rice  and  Apple . 
 Bring mixture to a boil; stir. Make sure  rice  is under  Pork  chops. 
 Reduce heat; cover and simmer 15 minutes or until liquid is absorbed and  Pork  chops are done.
