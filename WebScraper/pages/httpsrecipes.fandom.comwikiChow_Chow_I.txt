Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 quart  cabbage , chopped 
 3 medium  onion , chopped 
 3 medium  green bell peppers , chopped 
 3 medium  red bell peppers , chopped 
 1 pint  green tomatoes , chopped 
 2 tbsp  salt 
 1¼ cups  sugar 
 1 tbsp  mustard powder 
 ¾ tsp  turmeric 
 ¾ tsp ground  ginger 
 1 tbsp  mustard seed 
 1½ tsp  pickling spice 
 1½ pint  vinegar 
 Directions : 
 Mix all vegetables with the salt. 
 Let stand overnight. 
 Drain and rinse. 
 Tie pickling spices in a bag. 
 Add sugar and spices to vinegar. 
 Simmer 20 minutes. 
 Add all other ingredients and simmer until hot and well seasoned. 
 Remove spice bag. 
 Pack and seal. 
 Process in boiling water bath for 10 minutes.
