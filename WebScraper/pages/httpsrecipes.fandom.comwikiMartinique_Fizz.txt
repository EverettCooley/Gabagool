 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cl.  tequila 
 2 cl.  crème de cacao 
 2 cl.  lime juice 
 Top with  ginger ale 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Blend ingredients with crushed  ice  - pour into a tall glass and top with  ginger ale .
