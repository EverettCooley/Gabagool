Ingredients : 
 ½ cup low-calorie  Italian dressing 
 Anchovy Italian dressing : 
 1 oz  anchovy  fillets, ground 
 Bacon Italian dressing : 
 1 tbsp  bacos 
 Parmesan Italian dressing : 
 1 tbsp  Parmesan cheese 
 Tomato Italian dressing : 
 1 tbsp  tomato purée 
 Wine Italian dressing : 
 1 tbsp dry  white  or  red wine 
 Directions : 
 Start with ½ cup low-calorie Italian dressing. 
 Add the appropriate extras to get the variation you want. 
 Allow to mellow for several hours. 
 Nutritional information : 
 ½ cup = 24 calories
 
 Anchovy dressing: 1 meat exchange 
 Bacon dressing: ½ fat exchange 
 Parmesan dressing: ⅛ meat exchange 
 Tomato dressing: none 
 Wine dressing: none
