Description : 
 Contributed by Lil at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: old newspaper clipping 
 1 serving 
 Ingredients : 
 4 fresh  mint  sprigs 
 4 ice cubes 
 1 large ripe  peach , peeled, pitted, quartered 
 juice of 1  lime 
 1 tsp  sugar 
 1½ oz  light  or  dark rum 
 Directions : 
 Crush 2 mint sprigs and rub them all over inside surface of tall glass. 
 Discard mint. 
 Freeze glass 1 – 2 hours. 
 Finely chop leaves of 1 mint sprig. 
 In food processor or blender, crush ice with peach, chopped mint, lime juice and sugar. 
 Add rum and process 1 second. 
 Garnish with remaining mint sprig.
