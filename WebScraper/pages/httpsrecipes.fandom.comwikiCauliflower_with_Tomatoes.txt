Ingredients : 
 2 cups  Cauliflower  florets, cooked 
 6  plum tomatoes , split 
 1 tsp  olive oil 
 2  green onions 
 1/4 cup  green olives 
 juice of a  lemon 
 1 tsp  oregano 
 1 tsp tabasco 
 pinch of  nutmeg 
 fresh ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Preheat a nonstick sauté pan. 
 Add the  olive oil  and  green onion  and sauté for a minute. 
 Add the  Cauliflower ,  tomatoes ,  oregano ,  lemon , tabasco,  nutmeg , and  black pepper . 
 Cook for 2 minutes and serve. 
 
 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Calories 35, Protein 1g, Fat 1g, Cholesterol 7 mg, Fiber 2g, Sodium 65 mg 
 
From the cookbook of George Hirsch "Living It Up"
