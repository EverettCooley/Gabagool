 Hot pepper oil, or chile oil, is a commercial product made by infusing hot red chiles in vegetable or sesame oil, yielding a red, fragrant, spicy seasoning. 
 Bits of fiery hot chilies in vegetable oil used with abandon in many regions of China. Serve at the table to add life to any dish. Very hot.
