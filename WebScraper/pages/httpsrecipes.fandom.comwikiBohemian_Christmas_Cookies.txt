Ingredients : 
 yolks of 2 hard cooked  eggs 
 ⅓ cup  butter  or  shortening 
 ⅓ cup  sugar 
 yolk of 1  egg 
 1 tablespoon  milk 
 flour  to stiffen for rolling 
 3 tablespoons finely chopped  blanched almonds 
 Directions : 
 First put the hard cooked yolks of  eggs  through a ricer or sieve and  cream  with the  butter  or  shortening . 
 Add the  Sugar ,  cream  again, then stir in the uncooked  egg  yolk, the  milk , and sifted  flour . 
 The dough should be stiff enough to roll. 
 Cut into small round shapes with cookie cutters, brush these with beaten  egg  white and sprinkle with finely chopped  almonds . 
 Bake in a slow oven (300°F).
