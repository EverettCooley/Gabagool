Lemon Bonbon cookies 
 1 egg
1/2 c. shortening
1/3 c. butter, softened
1 tsp. vanilla
1 pkg. lemon cake mix
1 can lemon frosting
Pecan halves
 Beat eggs, shortening, butter, vanilla and about 1/2 of the cake mix 
until smooth. If dough is too dry, add 1-1/2 tsp water. Shape dough 
into 1-1/4 inch balls. Place on ungreased cookie sheet. 
 Bake at 350F for 8-10 minutes. Cool slightly before removing from 
baking sheet. 
 Frost cooled cookies. Top each frosted cookie with a pecan half.
