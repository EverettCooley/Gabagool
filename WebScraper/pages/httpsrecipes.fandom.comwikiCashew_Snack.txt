Description : 
 Contributed  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Prep: 5 minutes 
 Cook: 20 minutes 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ lb raw  cashews 
 ¾ cup water 
 1 cup + 5 tbsp  sugar 
 2¼ cups  vegetable oil 
 Directions : 
 Place first 3 ingredients in a sauce pan. 
 Bring to a boil, lower heat, and simmer for 8 – 10 minutes. 
 Drain. 
 Heat oil. 
 Deep fry the cashews for 8 – 10 minutes, or until they turn golden brown. 
 Remove with a strainer and drain on paper towels. 
 Allow to cool before serving.
