Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Ritchie Estate in Balch Springs, Texas in 1984.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 head  lettuce 
 1  avocado 
 juice of 1  lemon 
 3 cups  cantaloupe  balls 
 1 cup  pineapple  cubes 
 1 cup fresh  blueberries 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 8 ounces  American cheese 
 2 tablespoons  honey 
 2 tablespoons sliced  almonds 
 Directions : 
 Arrange lettuce in bowl. 
 Cut peeled avocado in strips then sprinkle with lemon juice. 
 Combine fruit and add to bowl. 
 Garnish with avocado and cheese strips. 
 Combine mayonnaise, cheese, honey and almonds and sprinkle over salad.
