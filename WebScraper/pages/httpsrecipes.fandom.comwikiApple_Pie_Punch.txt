Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Wadley Estate in Allen, Texas in 1990.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  apple cider 
 4 ounces  spiced rum 
 2 ounces  cinnamon schnapps 
 ice cubes 
 1 red  apple  sliced for garnish 
 cinnamon  sticks for garnish 
 Directions : 
 In a pitcher combine apple cider and schnapps. 
 Serve punch over ice cubes and garnish with red apple slices and cinnamon sticks.
