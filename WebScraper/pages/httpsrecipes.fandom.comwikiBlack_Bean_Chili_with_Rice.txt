Makes 4 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 pound lean ground  Beef 
 1 1/3 cups cooked  black beans 
 1/2 cup diced  green pepper 
 1/2 cup chopped  Onion 
 1/2 cup diced  carrots 
 2 teaspoons  chili powder 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1  bay leaf 
 1/8 to 1/4 teaspoon  ground red pepper 
 1 1/2 cups vegetable juice or  tomato juice 
 1 1/3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Cook beef in 2- to 3-quart saucepan over medium heat, stirring to crumble. 
 Add beans, green pepper, onion, carrots, chili powder, garlic, bay leaf, red pepper and vegetable juice. 
 Bring to a boil; simmer, uncovered, 20 to 25 minutes. 
 Remove bay leaf. Serve over hot rice.
