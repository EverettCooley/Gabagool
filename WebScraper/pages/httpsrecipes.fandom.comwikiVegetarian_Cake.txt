 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  semolina 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 cup curds 
 1 teaspoon  baking soda 
 4 tablespoons melted  oil 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 4  cardamons  (powdered) 
 4 tablespoons chopped  nuts 
 10-15  raisins 
 Directions : 
 Mix  semolina ,  sugar  and curd. Stir well. 
 Add  raisins ,  nuts , powdered  cardamom  and  vanilla extract . 
 Pre-heat oven to 300 °F. 
 Grease a medium size pan with  butter . 
 Pour in the mixture and bake for 30–45 minutes.
