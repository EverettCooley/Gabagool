pumpkin  Pudding
 
 3  eggs 
 16 Oz. Can  pumpkin 
 3/4 Cup Loosely Packed  brown sugar 
 1 1/2 Cups  milk  or  light cream  (or a 13 Oz. Can  evaporated milk ) 
 1 T. Ground  cinnamon 
 1/2 T. Grated  fresh ginger 
 1/4 T. Ground Cloves 
 Pinch  salt 
 
Preheat Oven to 350. Beat  eggs ; Add All Other Ingredients. Mix Together, Beating With a hand Beater or Using a Food Processor or Blender. Put Mixture in an Oiled 9 Inch Baking Dish (round Dish) And Bake 45 Minutes or Until a Knife Inserted in The Center Comes Out Clean Note: if You Serve This as a Vegetable, You May Want to Reduce The Amount of  sugar  to 1/2 Cup. Also, if Serving as a Dessert, Try Baking it in Oiled Custard Cups; You Will Need 7 to 8, Depending on How High You Fill Them. Serve Accompanied With  Turkey ,  Chicken  or  Pork . Serves 6 to 8
