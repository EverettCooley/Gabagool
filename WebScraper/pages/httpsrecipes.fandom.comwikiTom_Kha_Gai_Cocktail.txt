 Ingredients : 
 30ml  White rum 
 15ml  Crème de menthe  white 
 7ml   Lime juice 
 15ml  syrup 
 15ml  Coconut cream 
 2 Fresh  chilies 
 3 small  galangal  roots 
 1  Kaffir leaf 
 2 leaves sweet  basil 
 Directions : 
 Pour white rum, creme de menthe, lime juice, galangal root, and syrup into a cocktail shaker with ice. 
 Shake well 
 Add chili and kaffir leaves, adn shake again. 
 Pour the liquid ingredients into the glass 
 Garnish with Coconut cream, fresh Chili and sweet basil 
 Notes : 
 Recipe originally included on page for  Skillet Chicken with Orzo .
