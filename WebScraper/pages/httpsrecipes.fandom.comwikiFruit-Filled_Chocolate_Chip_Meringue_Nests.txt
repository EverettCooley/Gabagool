Ingredients : 
 Meringues : 
 4 large  egg whites 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  cream of tartar 
 1 cup  granulated sugar 
 1 package Nestlé® toll house®  semi-sweet chocolate  morsels 
 3 cups fresh fruit 
 Chocolate sauce : 
 1 box Nestlé® Carnation®  evaporated milk 
 1 package Nestlé® Toll House®  semi-sweet chocolate  morsels 
 2 tbsp  granulated sugar 
 2 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 ⅛ teaspoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 300 °F. 
 Lightly grease baking sheets. 
 Beat  egg  whites,  salt  and  cream of tartar  in large mixer bowl until soft peaks form. 
 Gradually add  sugar ; beat until  sugar  is dissolved. 
 Gently fold in morsels. 
 Spread meringue into ten 3-inch nests with deep wells about 2 inches apart on prepared baking sheets. 
 Bake for 35 to 45 minutes or until meringues are dry and crisp. 
 Cool on baking sheets for 5 minutes; remove to wire racks to cool completely. 
 Heat  evaporated milk  to boiling in medium, heavy-duty saucepan. 
 Stir in morsels. 
 Cook, stirring constantly, until mixture is slightly thickened and smooth. 
 Remove from heat; stir in  sugar ,  vanilla extract  and  salt . 
 Drizzle meringues with  chocolate  sauce and fill with fruit (whole  blackberries ,  blueberries  or  raspberries , sliced  kiwi ,  peaches  or  strawberries ). 
 Serve immediately.
