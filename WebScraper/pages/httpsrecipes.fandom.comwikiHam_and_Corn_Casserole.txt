Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 tbsp reduced-calorie  margarine 
 3 tbsp  flour 
 1½ cups  skim milk 
 ½ tsp  dry mustard 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 ⅛ tsp  pepper 
 ¼ tsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 1 (20 oz.) can whole kernel  corn , drained 
 ¼ cup shopped  onion 
 ¼ cup shopped  green pepper 
 2 cups cubed lean cooked  ham 
 1 cup  breadcrumbs 
 1 cup grated low-fat processed  American cheese 
 Directions : 
 Make white sauce of  margarine ,  flour  and  milk  stir in  mustard ,  salt   pepper , and  Worcestershire sauce , add  corn ,  onion ,  green pepper  and  ham . 
 Pour mixture into non-stick casserole top with breadcrumbs and  cheese . 
 Bake at 375°F for 25 minutes or until bubbly.
