 Ingredients : 
 4  bananas 
 1 to 2 tsp chopped  peanuts 
 Caramel sauce : 
 ½ cup  white sugar 
 1 cup  light brown sugar 
 3 tbsp  evaporated milk 
 2 tbsp  butter 
 2 oz  white rum 
 Directions : 
 Caramel sauce : 
 In a small skillet, heat both sugars, evaporated milk, and butter. 
 Cook until sugar caramelizes (i.e., melts and starts to turn brown). 
 Stir in 2 oz white rum and cool. 
 Assembly : 
 Slice 4 bananas. 
 Arrange ½ banana per person in compote dish. 
 Spoon 3 to 4 tbsp caramel sauce over bananas. 
 Sprinkle with 1 to 2 tsp chopped peanuts.
