 Ingredients : 
 1 (15 ounce) can  black beans , drained and rinsed 
 3/4 cup  beef broth 
 1/2 cup thick & chunky salsa 
 1/2 teaspoon  cumin 
 2 tablespoons  sour cream 
 1 tablespoon sliced  green onions 
 Directions : 
 1. In a food processor with a metal blade or a blender container, combine the beans, broth, salsa, and  cumin . Process 1 minute, or until smooth.
 2. Heat bean mixture in a medium saucepan over medium heat, until thoroughly heated.
 3. To serve, ladle soup into 2 individual bowls. Spoon 1 tablespoon of  sour cream  on top of each; swirl gently. Sprinkle with  green onions .
