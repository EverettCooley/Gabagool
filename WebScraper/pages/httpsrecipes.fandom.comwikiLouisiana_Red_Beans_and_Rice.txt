Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Gingham Estate in Fort Worth, Texas in 1992.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 package dry  kidney beans 
 1 pound smoked  sausage 
 2 smoked  ham hocks 
 1 small white  onion 
 ½ small  green pepper 
 1 teaspoon  onion salt 
 1 teaspoon  garlic salt 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 1  celery  stalk 
 1 tablespoon  parsley 
 ½ cup  ketchup 
 1 large  jalapeno  chopped 
 Directions : 
 Rinse beans several times to remove all debris then put in large pot and add enough  water  to cover beans then bring to boil and reduce heat then cover and let simmer for one hour. 
 Add  onion  and garlic salt and stir well then add  parsley ,  onion ,  green pepper ,  celery ,  sausage  and  ham  hocks then continue simmering for 3 hours stirring occasionally to prevent sticking. 
 When beans are done add ketchup then simmer for 5 minutes longer. 
 Pour over cooked  rice  and serve.
