Serves: 6
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups water
 2 cups uncooked white rice
 8 American Cheese singles
 
 Directions : 
 1. Bring water to boil. 
 2. Stir in rice and cheese slices.
 3. Cook and stir until cheese is melted; cover. 
 4. Remove from heat. Let stand 5 minutes. 
 5. Stir.
 6. The cheesy rice is ready! Enjoy!
