Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  Crisco 
 2 cups  brown sugar 
 2  eggs 
 1 cup cold  coffee 
 1 teaspoon  cinnamon 
 1 teaspoon  nutmeg 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 1 level teaspoon  baking powder 
 1 level teaspoon  baking soda 
 2 cups  flour 
 2 cups  quick rolled oats 
 2 cups  raisins 
 2 cups chopped  nuts  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Blend the first nine ingredients together and then add the last four. 
 Drop on cookie sheet by the spoonful. 
 Bake at 350°F until no imprint can be seen (approx 8 – 10 minutes).
