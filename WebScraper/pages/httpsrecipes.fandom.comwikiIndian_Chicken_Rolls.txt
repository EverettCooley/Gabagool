      Indian Chicken Rolls     
 Ingredients = : 
 1 cup cooked  chicken 
 1  onion , chopped 
 1 small  tomato , chopped 
 1 tsp  ginger   garlic  paste 
 1 tsp  garam masala 
 2  eggs 
 a few  tortillas  or  chapathis 
 salt  and  chili powder  to taste 
 Directions : 
 In a pan heat  oil  and fry the  onions . 
 Also add  ginger   garlic  paste and fry for 3-4 minutes adding a few drops of  water . 
 Then add  tomatoes , cooked  chicken . 
 Fry well, add  garam masala ,  salt ,  chili powder  and fry this till done. 
 Now in a bowl, beat the  eggs  and season with  salt . 
 Heat 1 tsp of  oil , add 2 tbsp of the  egg  and cover it with a tortilla. 
 When the  egg  gets cooked, flip the tortilla and fill it with a little of the  chicken  mix. 
 On top of the  chicken  you can add a few chopped  onions ,  cilantro  and a bit of  lime . 
 Roll it up and serve hot with ketchup.
