 Ingredients : 
 4 cl  white rum 
 1 cl  grenadine  syrup 
 7 cl  pineapple juice 
 7 cl  orange juice 
 ice  cubes 
 Directions : 
 pineapple juice  mixing with  grenadine  syrup and  white rum  and pouring into a glass with 2-3  ice  cubes. 
 Adding then slowly the  orange juice . 
 Decorating with a red cocktail  cherry  and a piece of  pineapple .
