Description : 
 In Romanian: Chiftelute din heringi sau scrumbii sarate
 
 Ingredients : 
 2  herrings 
 1  egg 
 2 crustless slices of  white bread 
 1  onion 
 1 tablespoon  parsley  and  dill 
 2 tablespoon  oil 
 bread crumbs 
 Directions : 
 Wash the fish well, slit lengthwise and keep them in tepid  water  for 5 â€“ 6 hours. 
 Take out of the  water , remove all skin and bones. 
 Then grind together with the raw  onion  and the bread soaked in  milk  or  water  and squeezed of excess liquid. 
 Mix this with the  egg ,  parsley  and dill and make pancakes. 
 Cover in bread crumbs and fry in hot  oil . 
 Serve hot.
