 This is an easy salad to put together, it will complement other things you might serve with an Italian dinner such as crusty  Italian bread  or  Pinot Grigio .
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 	 large firm  tomatoes  (sliced thin) 
 1/2Â lb  mozzarella   Cheese  (sliced) 
 1/3 	cup fresh  basil  leaves (torn into small shreds) 
 2 	tablespoons  capers 
 1/4 	cup  olive oil 
 kosher salt 
 Directions : 
 On a large platter arrange  tomato  slices and Mozarella slices (alternating the slices). 
 Sprinkle with  basil  and  capers . 
 Season with  salt . 
 Drizzle with  olive oil . 
 Serve.
