Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Turner Estate in Dallas, Texas in 1997. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup  dry mix 
 1 pound lean  ground beef 
 3 cans crushed  tomatoes 
 1 cup fresh  mushrooms 
 1 cup grated  zucchini 
 1 cup grated  carrots 
 ½ cup chopped  celery 
 1 white  onion  chopped 
 Dry mix : 
 2 cup dry minced  onion 
 3 tablespoons  oregano 
 2 tablespoons dried  green pepper  flakes 
 4 teaspoons dried  basil 
 1 tablespoon dry minced  garlic 
 4 teaspoons  salt 
 4 tablespoons dry  parsley 
 2 teaspoon  rosemary 
 2 teaspoons  thyme 
 2 teaspoons  marjoram 
 Directions : 
 Combine all dry mix ingredients and stir until well combined. 
 Brown ground beef in skillet then drain. 
 Add remaining ingredients and simmer over low heat for two hours.
