Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  mustard 
 1 teaspoon  lemon juice 
 1½ teaspoon  white wine vinegar 
 2 tablespoons fresh  tarragon  chopped 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 ¾ cup baby  shrimp 
 ½ cup  sour cream 
 Directions : 
 Mix  mustard ,  lemon juice ,  vinegar  and  tarragon  then blend in the  mayonnaise . 
 Add  shrimp  and  sour cream  and blend well. 
 Cover and chill.
