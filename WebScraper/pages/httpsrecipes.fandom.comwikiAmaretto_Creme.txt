Description : 
 An occasional drink has helped boost many a lagging appetite.
 
 Amaretto Creme  from Recipes for Better Treatment During Cancer Prevention by the National Cancer Institute, public domain government resource—original source of recipe 
 Cook Time : About 5 minutes 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients : 
       Amaretto creme     
 ½ cup chilled  half-and-half 
 2 tbsp vanilla  ice cream 
 1 tbsp  amaretto  cordial 
 Directions : 
 Mix until smooth. 
 Serve in stemmed glass. 
 Variations : 
 Butterscotch Brandy Creme : Omit the Amaretto; add 2 tbsp butterscotch sauce and 1 tbsp brandy instead
