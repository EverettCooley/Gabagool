      Polish Cabbage Rolls     
 Ingredients : 
 1  green cabbage 
 1 lb  ground beef ; brown 
 ¼ cup uncooked  rice 
 1  egg ; beaten 
 1  onion ; minced 
 6 red potatoes, peeled & quartered 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp black  pepper 
 ¼ cup  cider vinegar 
 16 oz. can chopped tomatoes 
 16 oz. jar saurkraut 
 bay leaves 
 Directions : 
 Mix  ground beef , parboiled rice,  egg ,chopped  onion ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Mix together large can chopped tomatoes, large jar saurkraut drained & rinsed. 
 Drop  cabbage  in boiling  water  for 5 to 10 minutes. 
 Remove 10 large leaves. 
 Chop remaining  cabbage . 
 Place in bottom of crockpot with tomatoes & saurkraut. 
 Place 2 to 4 tbsp of meat mixture in center of each leaf. 
 Roll up and poke finger in both sides to hold cabbage roll. 
 Place open side down into crockpot. 
 Add peeled & quartered red potatoes. 
 Cover and cook on low for 8 to 10 hours. 
 If adding bay leaves, remove before serving.
