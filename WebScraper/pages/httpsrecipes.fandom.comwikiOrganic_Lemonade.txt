      Organic lemonade     
 Ingredients : 
 2Â½ cups natural  lemon juice  (organic of course) 
 2 cups hot water 
 800 g  sugar  (raw) 
 2 tablespoons  lemon zest 
 Directions : 
 Boiling water with sugar until it can stand the drop of the mixture as a knot over the nail (five minutes). 
 Add the zest and the lemon. 
 Remove from heat and put the lemon juice. 
 Location in bottles in the fridge and when I want to drink lemonade, put 2 fingers in a glass and fill with cold water.
