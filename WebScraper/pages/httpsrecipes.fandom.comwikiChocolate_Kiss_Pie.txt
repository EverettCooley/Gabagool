Description : 
 This is a recipe I obtained from an Estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Fisher Estate in Irving, Texas in 1997.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 12 ounce package  chocolate  kisses 
 2 ounces  milk 
 8 ounce package  cream cheese  softened 
 12 ounce carton frozen  whipped topping  thawed 
 1 chocolate ready made  pie crust 
 Directions : 
 Remove foil wrappers and melt kisses over medium heat in the milk. 
 When melted add cream cheese and stir until completely melted. 
 Rapidly stir in whipped topping with the heat off. 
 Pour into crust and refrigerate until set.
