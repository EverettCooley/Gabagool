Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 1 cup chopped  green bell pepper 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 2 tablespoons  bacon drippings  or  vegetable oil 
 1 x 15-ounce can  pinto beans  or  pinto beans  seasoned with  jalapeno peppers 
 1 x 8-ounce can  tomato sauce 
 Â½ teaspoon  salt 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Cook  onion ,  pepper  and  garlic  in drippings in large skillet over medium heat until tender crisp. 
 Stir in beans,  tomato  sauce and  salt ; simmer 10 minutes. 
 Serve over hot  rice .
