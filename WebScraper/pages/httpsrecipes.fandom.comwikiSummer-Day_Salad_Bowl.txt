Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked cold  rice 
 1 cup cooked  ham  in ½ inch cubes 
 1 cup cooked  chicken  in ½-inch cubes 
 ½ cup  celery  in ¼-inch pieces 
 2  pimentos  (canned) in ¼-inch pieces 
 1 cup  pineapple  or  melon  or both 
 2 tbsp shredded  coconut 
 2 tbsp chopped  peanuts 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 3 tbsp frozen  orange juice  concentrate, undiluted. 
 1 to 2 heads  lettuce 
 1 tsp chopped  peanuts 
 watercress  or  parsley  sprigs 
 Directions : 
 In a 2-quart bowl combine cooked cold  rice , cubed  ham , cubed  chicken ,  celery , pimentos,  pineapple  or melon or both, shredded  coconut , chopped  peanuts  and  salt . 
 Blend  mayonnaise  with  orange juice  concentrate. 
 Combine  orange   mayonnaise  with the mixture. 
 Line wooden salad bowls using the  lettuce . Divide mixture among the bowls. 
 Garnish top of salad with 1 to 2 tbsp  orange   mayonnaise . 
 Sprinkle chopped  peanuts  on the  mayonnaise . 
 Decorate with  watercress  or  parsley  sprigs.
