Description : 
 A great burger with a twist! 
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Original recipe 
 Source: dLife 
 6 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 16 ounces ground 90% lean  turkey  or  beef 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 ½ cup chopped  green bell pepper 
 1 (10¾-ounce) can Healthy Request  tomato soup 
 2 teaspoons  chili seasoning 
 1 teaspoon dried  parsley  flakes 
 ¼ teaspoon  black pepper 
 6 reduced-calorie  hamburger buns 
 Directions : 
 Sauté the brown meat, onion, and green pepper in a large skillet sprayed with olive oil-flavored cooking spray. 
 Mix in the tomato soup, chili seasoning, parsley flakes, and black pepper. 
 Then lower the heat and let simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
 Spoon ⅓ of a cup of the meat mixture between each bun and serve!. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per Serving: 
224 calories | 8g fat | 16g protein | 22g carbohydrate | 385mg sodium | 11mg calcium | 2g fiber 
 
 Exchanges: 2 meat | 1½ starch
