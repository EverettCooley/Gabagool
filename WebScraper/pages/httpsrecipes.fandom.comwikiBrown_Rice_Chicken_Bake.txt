Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  brown rice 
 1 x 10-ounce package frozen  green peas 
 2 cups cooked  chicken  breast cubes 
 ½ cup reduced calorie  mayonnaise 
 ⅓ cup  slivered almonds , toasted (optional) 
 2 teaspoons  soy sauce 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 ¼ teaspoon  garlic powder 
 ¼ teaspoon dried  tarragon  leaves 
 Vegetable  cooking spray 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  peas ,  chicken ,  mayonnaise ,  almonds ,  soy sauce ,  pepper ,  garlic powder  and  tarragon  in bowl. 
 Transfer to 3-quart baking dish coated with  cooking spray . 
 Cover and bake at 350 °F for 15 to 20 minutes.
