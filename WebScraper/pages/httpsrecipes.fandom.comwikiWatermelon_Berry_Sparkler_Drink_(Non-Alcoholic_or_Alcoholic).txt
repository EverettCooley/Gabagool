 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 16 glasses of watermelon berry sparkler drink. 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  watermelon , cubed and seeded 
 6 oz container of red  raspberries 
 3 tbsp  sugar 
 4 oz cold  champagne  or dry  sparkling wine  or  club soda  or  seltzer 
 Directions : 
 Purée watermelon and raspberries in a blender. 
 Scrape mixture through a fine sieve into a bowl, discard seeds. 
 Stir sugar into purée. 
 Pour 2 tbs puree into each glass and top with champagne or wine. 
 Non-alcoholic version : 
 Use club soda or seltzer instead.
