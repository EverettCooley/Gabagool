 Description : 
 Bo Luc Lac Salad is a Vietnamese salad, which can be served cool.
 
       Bo Luc Lac Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 sirloin steaks, cubed 
 garlic 
 sauce 
 Sugar 
 salt 
 olive oil 
 Onion 
 rice vinegar 
 black pepper 
 olive oil 
 watercress 
 Directions : 
 Prepare the ingredients. Then mix  garlic , sauce,  Sugar ,  salt ,  Beef ,  oil ,  black pepper ,  vinegar  and marinate  Onion .
