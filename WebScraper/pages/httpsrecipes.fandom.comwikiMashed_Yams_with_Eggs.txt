Description : 
 Cooking time: 45 minutes 
 Serves 2 to 4 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups mashed  yams , or mashed white  potatoes 
 2 tablespoons grated  onions 
 Â¾ cup melted  palm oil 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 1 ripe  tomato , peeled and diced (optional) 
 6  hard-boiled eggs 
 Directions : 
 Boil  yams  or  potatoes , then mashed smoothly with a fork; or, prepare instant mashed  potatoes  using directions on package, but using  water  instead of  milk . 
 Prepare gravy in a saucepan by flying  onions  and seasoning in  palm oil . 
 Add  tomatoes  if desired. 
 Remove from heat. 
 Mash 2  egg  yolks and stir into gravy. 
 Stir gravy into mashed  yams  and mix well until color is even. 
 Empty into a bowl and decorate with remaining whole  eggs .
