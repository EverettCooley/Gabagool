 Description : 
 '
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lbs 	 tofu , crumbled 
 2 	tsp 	Chinese 5 spice powder 
 1/4 	tsp 	 cayenne pepper 
 2 	tbsp 	 Onion , dried 
 1/4 	cup 	 bell pepper , chopped 
 4 	tsp 	 mustard , prepared 
 1 	tbsp 	Soy sauce 
 Directions : 
 1 Combine filling ingredients in a mixing bowl.
 2 Spread 1 ½ tablespoons puree on each  corn  husk "boat."
 3 Cook for 20 minutes. or until husks have softened.
 4 Season to taste with  sea salt  and  lime juice .
 5 Serve!
