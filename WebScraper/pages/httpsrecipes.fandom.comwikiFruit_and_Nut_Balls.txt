Description : 
 Serves 12
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  dates , pitted 
 ½ cup  raisins , dark or golden 
 2 tbsp  cocoa   or   carob  powder 
 ½ cup  walnuts 
 ½ cup  sunflower seeds 
 walnuts , ground 
 Directions : 
 Grind together  dates ,  raisins ,  cocoa  (or  carob  powder),  walnuts  and  sunflower seeds  in a food processor or meat grinder. 
 Press the mixture into 24 small balls. 
 Roll in the  ground nuts . 
 Store in an airtight container. 
 Nutritional information : 
 two balls = carbohydrates: 10 protein: 2 fat: 6 sodium: 2 potassium: 104 cholesterol: 0 
 
 exchange value: 1 fruit + 1 fat
