 Description : 
 Purchased from Bowers Estate in Fort Worth, Texas in 1994. Dated 1951.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4  English muffins 
 8 slices Canadian  Bacon 
 8  eggs 
 2 avocados 
 1-cup  Mayonnaise 
 2 tablespoons  lime  juice 
 Hot sauce to taste 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Toast  English muffins . 
 Place 2 halves on each plate. 
 Lightly saut√©  Bacon  and top each muffin half with a slice. 
 Poach  eggs  and top each slice of  Bacon  with 1  egg . 
 Mash avocados then whip in  Mayonnaise , limejuice, and hot sauce with a wire whisk until smooth. 
 Pour over  eggs  Benedict.
