 Ingredients : 
 1 shot Stolichnaya  vodka 
 1 shot De Kuyper  crème de cassis 
 Top up with Lemonade 
 Directions : 
 Place  ice  in highball glass. 
 Pour the Stolichnaya and  crème de cassis  in a glass and mix together. 
 Top up with lemonade (we suggest using 7-Up or Sprite to give a tangy lime-y flavour) and garnish with some  raspberries .
