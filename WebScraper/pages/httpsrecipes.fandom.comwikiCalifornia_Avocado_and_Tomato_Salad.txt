 Description : 
 Its a mixture of avocados and  tomatoes  with a light dressing. Increase the  avocado  and  tomatoes  ratio as you wish!
 
 Ingredients : 
 2  California avocados  cubed 
 2 medium size  tomatoes  chopped(not too small) 
 3  green onions  chopped 
 6 slices  Bacon  fried and crumbled optional (I omit it because of a couple of vegetarians in the family) 
 Salad greens 
 3 Tbsp  vegetable oil 
 1 Tbsp  vinegar 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 few drops of tobasco sauce (just to add a little bite) 
 Directions : 
 Mix  oil , salt ,  pepper  and tobasco sauce if using.
 Toss into salad add crumpled  Bacon  if using.
 Refrigerate for 2 hours to develop flavor and you can serve it on salad greens if you wish. 
 Enjoy.
