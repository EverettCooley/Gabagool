 About rice vermicelli : 
 Wikipedia Article  About Rice vermicelli on Wikipedia 
 The rice vermicelli are thin noodles made from real rice, sometimes also known as the rice noodles or rice sticks. They are a part of several Asian cuisines, where they are often eaten as part of a soup dish, stir-fry, or salad. Rice vermicelli are particularly prominent in the cuisines of China and Southeast Asia, many of which normally feature a notable Chinese culinary influence.
