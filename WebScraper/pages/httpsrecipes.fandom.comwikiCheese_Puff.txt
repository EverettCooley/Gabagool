Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: I Love Recipes 
 Serves 6 
 Ingredients : 
 35  Ritz Crackers , coarsely crushed 
 1 pkg. sharp  cheddar  cheese, grated (8 oz) 
 1 can sliced  mushrooms , drained (4 oz) 
 4  eggs , slightly beaten 
 2 cups  milk 
 1 tbsp  onion powder 
 1 tbsp  parsley  flakes 
 ¼ tbsp  poultry seasoning 
 Directions : 
 In greased 6 cup soufflé dish. 
 Combine crackers, cheese and mushrooms. 
 In medium bowl, with mixer at medium speed, beat together eggs, milk, onion powder, parsley and seasoning. 
 Pour over mixture in soufflé dish. 
 Bake at 350°F for 1 hour and 10 minutes or until puffed and lightly browned.
