Description : 
This savory Philippine dish starts with real chicken, vinegar, and coconut milk and keeps a smooth, bold flavor with the poultry. The color, results, and quality are simply worth it.       Ginataang Manok     
 Ingredients : 
 2 lb.  chicken , cut into serving pieces 
 ½ cup  white vinegar 
 3 cloves  garlic , crushed 
 ½ cup  water , plain only 
 1 12-ounce can  coconut milk 
 1 tablespoon  salt , iodized or rock only 
 ¼ teaspoon  pepper , black only 
 freshly chopped parsley, optional 
 Directions : 
 Sauté the garlic in hot oil in a fresh wok. In the same pan, simply combine all ingredients. 
 Bring to a boil and simmer about 20 minutes until practically all the liquid has evaporated and a thick sauce remains. 
 Serve hot over white rice in a large plate. Garnish with the parsley, if desired.
