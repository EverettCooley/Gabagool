Description : 
 Contributed by Delma at  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Yields 8 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 16 slices firm-textured, day old  bread 
 1¾ cups  milk 
 8 ounces mixed  dried fruit , cut into small pieces 
 ½ cup chopped  nuts 
 1 medium  apple , cored and diced 
 ¼ cup  butter   or   margarine , melted 
 ⅓ cup firmly packed  brown sugar 
 1  egg , slightly beaten 
 1 teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  nutmeg 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  cloves 
 Directions : 
 Tear  bread , with crusts, into 1- to 2-inch pieces. 
 Place into crockpot. 
 Pour  milk  over  bread ; let soak 30 minutes. 
 Stir in dried fruit, nuts and  apple . 
 Combine remaining ingredients in a small bowl. 
 Pour over  bread  mixture. 
 Stir well to blend. 
 Cover and cook on low for 3½ to 4 hours or until skewer inserted in center comes out clean.
