Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 Cups diced  Chicken  or  Turkey 
 ¼ Cup California  sherry 
 1 ½ Cups diced  celery 
 ½ Cup slivered toasted  almonds 
 1  pimento  chopped 
 ½ Cup real  Mayonnaise 
 2 Tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 Tbsp grated  lemon  rind 
 1 tsp minced  Onion 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  black pepper 
 3  avocados  peeled, cut lengthwise 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 450 Degrees.
 Heat the following until very hot.
 In a saucepan heat the  Chicken  and  sherry .
 Add  celery ,  almonds ,  pimento  and the combined  Mayonnaise ,  lemon juice , rind,  Onion ,  salt  and  pepper , blending thoroughly. 
 Fill  avocados . 
 Place in a pan, bake in very hot oven until lightly browned. 
 Serve immediately. 
 Baking time: About 15 minutes.
