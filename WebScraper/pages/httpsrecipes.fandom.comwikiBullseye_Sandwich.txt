 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 slice White bread 
 1 tsp Dijon  mustard  ( opt) 
 2 slice ( about 2 oz) sharp 
 Cheddar   Cheese 
 unsalted butter , softened 
 2 tbsp  unsalted butter 
 1  egg 
 salt 
 Freshly ground  pepper 
 1 slice  tomato 
 1 slice Peeled  cucumber 
 Directions : 
 1 Spread both sides of the bread with  mustard , if desired. 
2 Arrange the  Cheese  on one slice and top with the other. 
3 With a 1-Â½-inch cookie cutter ( or reasonable facsimilie) cut a circle out of the middle of the sandwich. 
4 Spread softened  butter  on the outsides of both the sandwich and the cut out circle. 
5 In a skillet, melt the 2 t of  butter  over medium heat. 
6 Arrange the sandwich and the cutout circle in the pan. 
7 Break an  egg  into the cutout hols and season lightly with  salt  and  pepper . 
8 Cook until the bottom of the  egg  has set. 
9 Turn the sandwich and  egg  and the circle, and continue to cook until the  egg  has reached the desired degree of doneness. 
10 The  Cheese  should be melted and the bread toasted a golden brown. 
11 To serve; carefully turn out the  egg  filled sandwich onto a warm plate. 
12 Place the toasted circle next to it and top with the  tomato  and  cucumber  slices. 
13 Season with  salt  and  pepper  and serve immediately.
