Description : 
 This is great summer drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pint Haagen Dasz mango (or peach)  sorbet 
 milk 
 3 shots Bacardi  Rum Limón 
 1½ shots good quality  dark rum 
 3 dollops  whipped cream 
 Directions : 
 Empty a softened pint of the sorbet into a shaker or other tall mixer. 
 Stir in milk by hand with a spoon until it has the consistency of a very thick shake (do not use a blender). 
 Mix in 3 shots of the Rum Limon. 
 Pour into three large glasses (water tumblers will do). 
 Gently pour a half shot of dark rum down the side of each glass. 
 Put a dollop of whipped cream on top and garnish with you favorite fruit wedge. 
 tick a straw in and enjoy!
