 Description : 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can  sweetened condensed milk 
 same measure of  passion fruit  juice 
 3  egg  whites 
 1 envelope of  unflavored gelatin 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Blend  condensed milk  and  passion fruit  juice. 
 Dissolve  gelatin  in a cup of  water  (sprinkle it on  water , stir and let it stand for a minute). 
 Beat  egg  whites until stiff and fold into mixture. 
 Add  gelatin  and wet a ring mold and pour the mousse into it. 
 Refrigerate for 6 hours. 
 Unmold and serve with a cr√®me anglaise.
