Ingredients : 
 1⅓ cups  all-purpose flour 
 2 tsp  baking powder 
 2 tsp  sugar 
 2 tsp  salt 
 2  eggs , separated 
 ½ cup  milk 
 ½ tsp finely chopped  lemon zest 
 ½ tsp chopped, fresh  thyme  or pinch dried 
 3 cloves  garlic , chopped finely 
 pinch  cayenne pepper 
 2 genuine  California avocados , diced ¼ inch 
 vegetable oil  as needed for deep frying 
 Creole mustard-apricot sauce 
 Creole mustard-apricot sauce : 
 ¾ Cup + 2 tablespoons  apricot jam 
 ½ Cup  Creole mustard  or other  whole-grain mustard 
 2 Tbsp  water 
 Directions : 
 Mix dry ingredients, reserve. 
 Beat  egg yolks  until light, whisk in  milk ,  lemon  zest,  thyme ,  garlic , and  cayenne . 
 Stir in dry ingredients until just mixed. 
 Gently fold in  avocado , reserve. 
 Whip  egg whites  until stiff, fold into  avocado  mixture. 
 Deep fry 6 generous tablespoons at a time until golden, about 3 minutes. 
 Keep warm until all batter is used. 
 Serve 3 per serving with ¼ cup  Creole mustard-apricot sauce . 
 Creole mustard-apricot sauce : 
 Thoroughly mix  apricot jam ,  mustard  and  water , reserve.
