Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/3 cup chopped  Onion 
 1/3 cup chopped  celery 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 3 cups cooked  brown rice 
 1/4 cup  pine nuts , toasted 
 1 tablespoon chopped fresh  parsley 
 3/4 teaspoon  poultry seasoning 
 1/4 teaspoon  salt 
 1/8 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1 cup  chicken broth 
 1  egg , beaten 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Onion  and  celery  in  butter  until tender in small skillet over medium heat. 
 Combine with  rice ,  nuts ,  parsley ,  poultry seasoning ,  salt ,  pepper , broth and  egg  in buttered 2-1/2-quart baking dish. Bake at 350 degrees 25 to 30 minutes.
