Description : 
 Makes 5 cups or 5 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups cold  skim milk 
 1 pack (4-serving size) Jell-O brand  strawberry flavor sugar-free gelatin 
 1 carton (8 oz.) plain low-fat  yogurt 
 1 cup crushed  ice 
 1 large  banana , cut into chunks 
 Directions : 
 Pour  milk  into blender container. 
 Add remaining ingredients; cover. 
 Blend at high speed 30 seconds or until smooth. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving: 
 
 calories: 100 | protein: 7 g | fat: 1 g | carbohydrates: 14 g | cholesterol: 5 mg | sodium: 130 mg 
 Diabetic exchanges: nonfat milk: ½ | fruit: ½
