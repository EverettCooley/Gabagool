Description : 
 Use in place of grated Parmesan or Romano cheeses.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  blanched almonds , finely ground 
 1 cup engevita  yeast  (powdered) 
 3/4 tsp  sea salt 
 1/2 tsp  Onion  powder 
 1/2 tsp  garlic powder 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in a container with a tight-fitting lid and shake vigorously. 
 Store in your fridge for up to a month.
