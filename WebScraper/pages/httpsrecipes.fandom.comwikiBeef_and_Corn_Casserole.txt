Description : 
 Makes 4 servings. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  ground beef  or  turkey 
 1  onion , chopped 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 x 11-ounce can  corn  kernels with  red  and  green peppers , drained 
 1 cup chunky  salsa 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1½ cups (6 ounces) shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 tortilla chips  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Brown  beef  and  onions  in large skillet over medium-high heat. 
 Stir in  rice ,  corn , salsa and  salt ; stir until thoroughly heated. 
 Top with  cheese . 
 Serve over tortilla chips.
