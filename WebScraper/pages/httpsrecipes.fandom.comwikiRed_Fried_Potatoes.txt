Ingredients : 
 1 and 1/2Â lbs. of small  red potatoes  (washed) 
 3 tbsp. of  butter 
 3 tbsp. of  olive oil 
 1/2 tsp. of  salt 
 pepper, to taste 
 1 tbsp. of finely chopped  parsley 
 Directions : 
 In a heavy skillet over moderate heat, melt the butter and olive oil. 
 When the foaming decreases add the potatoes. 
 Turning frequently cook until tender and golden brown. 
 Season with salt and a little pepper. 
 Sprinkle with parsley and serve.
