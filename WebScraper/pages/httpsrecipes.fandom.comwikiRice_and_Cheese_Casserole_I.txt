Description : 
 Serves 6 (¾ cup servings)
 
 Ingredients : 
 2½ cups cooked  brown rice 
 3  green onions , chopped 
 1 cup low-fat  cottage cheese 
 1 tsp  dill weed 
 ¼ cup freshly grated  Parmesan cheese 
 ½ cup  low-fat milk 
 ½ tsp  Dijon-style mustard 
 non-stick vegetable spray 
 Directions : 
 Combine all but the last ingredient in a mixing bowl. 
 Pour into a casserole dish coated with nonstick vegetable spray. 
 Bake in a preheated 350°F oven for 15 to 20 minutes. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per ¾ cup serving: 
 
 calories: 225 | protein: 14 g | fat: 4 g | carbohydrates: 34 g | fiber: 2.5 g | cholesterol: 10 mg | sodium: 328 mg | potassium: 218 mg 
 Diabetic exchanges: 1 ½ starch/bread + ½ lean meat + 1 low-fat milk
