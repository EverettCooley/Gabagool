Description : 
 May be used as a basting sauce for barbecue meat or as a condiment when serving grilled  pork ,  beef  or  chicken .
 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group This Y-group is international. Good food from all parts of the world. A place where we can share the wonderful food from all over the world. 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅔ cup yellow  prepared mustard 
 ½ cup  white sugar 
 ¼ cup  light brown sugar 
 1 cup  cider vinegar 
 2 tbsp  chili powder 
 1 tsp  black pepper 
 1 tsp  white pepper 
 ¼ tsp  cayenne pepper 
 4 – 5 drops  Tabasco sauce 
 ½ tsp  soy sauce 
 2 tbsp  butter 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients except soy sauce and  butter  in saucepan and simmer 10 minutes. 
 Remove from heat. 
 Stir in soy sauce and  butter .
