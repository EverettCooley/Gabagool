 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2/3 cup  Flour 
 3 tbsp  baking powder 
 1/2 tbsp  Salt 
 4  Egg  whites 
 1/2 cup  Sugar 
 1/2 cup  brown sugar 
 3 tbsp  Vanilla 
 3 cup  Apples , cut fairly small 
 skins on is fine. 
 Directions : 
 Beat   eggs , add  Sugar  and  vanilla  and beat well. Add dry ingredients and  Mix . Dump in  Apples  and  Stir  until evenly distributed.  Bake  in a deep baking dish, 45 minutes at 350. Serve with custard
