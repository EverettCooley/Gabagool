 About Pickling spice : 
 Pickling spice is a mixture of whole and broken spices, seeds, and herbs used to flavour during the pickling process.
The spices and seeds most typically used include:
 
 Mustard seed 
 Coriander 
 Bay leaves 
 Dill seed 
 Fenugreek 
 Cinnamon 
 Ginger 
 Allspice 
 red pepper 
 black pepper 
 Cloves
