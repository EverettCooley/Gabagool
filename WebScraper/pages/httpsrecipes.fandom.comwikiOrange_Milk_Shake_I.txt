Description : 
 Posted on Diabetic Board of nvn by Patricia (Trish) McKenna, ID pmckenna. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  orange juice 
 ¼ tsp  vanilla extract 
 ¼ cup  instant non-fat dry milk 
 1 cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Place ingredients for desired drink in blender. 
 Blend 30 to 45 seconds.
