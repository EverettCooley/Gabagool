 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 cup  beet  liquid, drained of 1 
 -can of beets 
 3/4 cup  tomato juice 
 1/4 tsp  Onion  powder 
 1/4 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp fresh  lemon juice 
 1/4 cup plain low-fat  yogurt 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients except  yogurt . Chill 2–3 hours in a covered jar. Serve in cocktail glasses or small glass bowls, topping each with 1 tablespoon  yogurt . 4 servings (yield: 2 cups) 1 serving: ½ cup nutritive values per serving: 6 gm. Carbohydrates; 2 gm.
