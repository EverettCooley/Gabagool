Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ stick of  butter 
 ¼ cup dry cat or dog food 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 1 – 2 tbsp  milk  or  water 
 1 tbsp  flour  if needed for thickening 
 Directions : 
 Put everything in a food processor and mix well until desired thickness. 
 Spread on pet's favorite cake or cookies. 
 This is for occasional treating only. 
 Store in airtight container in refrigerator.
