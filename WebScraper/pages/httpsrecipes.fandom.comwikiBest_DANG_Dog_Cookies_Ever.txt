 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  whole wheat flour 
 1 tablespoon  baking powder 
 1 cup natural  Peanut   butter 
 1 cup skim  milk 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
 
 
 Combine  flour  and  baking powder  in one bowl, and  Peanut   butter  and  milk  in another. Add wet ingredients to dry and mix well. Roll dough out on a lightly floured surface and knead. Roll out to 1/4Â inch thick and cut out shapes with a cookie cutter. Place on greased baking sheet and bake for 20 minutes or until lightly browned.
 
 
 Cool in rack and store in an airtight container.
