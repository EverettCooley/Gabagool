 Ingredients : 
 2 c.  flour 
 2 c.  Sugar 
 2 c. grated  Cheese  (white) 
 1 c.  milk 
 4  eggs 
 1/2Â lb.  butter , melted 
 2 tsp.  baking powder 
 sesame seeds  for top 
 Directions : 
 Mix  Sugar ,  egg  yolks and 1/3 cup of  milk . Add  Cheese  little by little alternating with the rest of  milk . 
 Mix  flour ,  baking powder  and  butter . Beat  egg  whites until stiff and fold into other mixture. Put in buttered and floured pie dish. Sprinkle on  sesame seeds . 
 Bake for approximately 25 minutes at 325 degrees.
