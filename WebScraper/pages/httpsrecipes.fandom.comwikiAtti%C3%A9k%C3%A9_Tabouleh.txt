 Description : 
 Tabouleh made using cassava couscous
 
 Attiéké 
 Ingredients : 
 Salad : 
 1 pack (300g) of cooked attieke ( cassava   couscous ) 
 1  cucumber , chopped 
 2 small  tomatoes , chopped 
 1 bunch  green onions , (4) sliced 
 ½  green pepper  sliced 
 Dressing : 
 1 fresh  lime juice 
 1 ½ Tbsp extra virgin  olive oil 
 1 tablespoon  pepper 
 2 teaspoons  salt , or to taste 
 Garnish : 
 ½  red pepper  sliced 
 ½  yellow pepper  sliced 
 ½ cup fresh chopped  mint 
 2 cups fresh chopped  parsley 
 1 clove  garlic , minced (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Mix the salad ingredients, including the attieke (cooked) in a medium bowl. 
 Mix the dressing ingredients together and stir into the salad mixture 
 Garnish 
 Serve chilled or at room temperature
