Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Watson Estate in Greenville, Texas in 1987.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 trays ice 
 ¼ liter  vodka 
 ½ liter sour apple mix 
 ¼ teaspoon  sweet vermouth 
 1  Granny Smith apple  cored and sliced 
 Directions : 
 In medium pitcher combine ice, vodka, sour apple mix and vermouth. 
 Stir thoroughly then strain into a martini glass and garnish with apple slice.
