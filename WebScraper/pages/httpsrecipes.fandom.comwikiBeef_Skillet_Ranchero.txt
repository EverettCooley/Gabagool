Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound lean ground  Beef 
 1 cup chopped  Onion 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 10-3/4-ounce can condensed  cream  of  Mushroom  soup 
 1/4 to 1/2 cup chopped green chiles 
 1/2 cup  beef broth  or  water 
 1 1/2 cups (6 ounces) shredded  Cheddar   Cheese 
 1 cup crushed  corn  chips 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Beef ,  Onion  and  garlic  in large skillet over medium heat until lightly browned. 
 Stir in  rice , soup, chiles and broth; heat thoroughly. 
 Stir in  Cheese  and top with  corn  chips just before serving.
