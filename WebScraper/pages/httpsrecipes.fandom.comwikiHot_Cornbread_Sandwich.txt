Hot  Cornbread  Sandwich
 
 2 8-Oz. Pkgs.  corn  Muffin Mix 
 2  eggs , Beaten 
 2/3 C.  milk 
 12 Slices  American cheese 
 6 Slices Deli  Ham 
 
 Preheat Oven to 400 Degrees.
 
in Bowl, Combine Muffin Mix,  eggs  And  milk  And Mix Well. Pour Half of Mixture Into Greased 11x7-Inch Baking Dish. Carefully Place 6 Slices of  Cheese , Then  Ham  Slices And Remaining  Cheese  Slices on Top of  Ham . Spoon Remaining  Cornbread  Batter Over Top of  Cheese . Bake For 25 Mintues or Until  Cornbread  is Gold Brown. Cut Into Squres.
