Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Gingham Estate in Fort Worth, Texas in 1992.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds  cauliflower  cut into bite sized pieces 
 ¼ cup  yogurt 
 1 cup  chickpea flour 
 ¼ teaspoon  baking powder 
 ½ teaspoon  cumin  seed 
 ¼ teaspoon red  chili powder 
 ¼ cup  water 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 oil  for deep frying 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl mix all ingredients except  cauliflower  then dip cauliflower in the batter covering completely. 
 Heat  oil  in a wok over medium heat and when hot add  cauliflower  pieces and fry  cauliflower  until brown. 
 Remove and drain on paper towels.
