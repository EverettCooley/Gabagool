Ingredients : 
 2 – 3  dove  breasts 
 1 bottle  Italian dressing 
 1 – 1½  jalapeño pepper , ½ per dove 
 2 – 3 slices  bacon , 1 slice per dove 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Salt and pepper each bird and fill cavity with ½ a jalapeño. 
 Wrap with strip of  bacon  and use a toothpick to secure. 
 Place on charcoal grill and baste with Italian dressing. 
 Grill slowly and continue basting with dressing until birds are done.
