Ingredients : 
 3/4 c.  oatmeal 
 3/4 c.  powdered milk 
 1/2 c.  peanut butter , chunky 
 1/2 c.  honey 
 1 tsp.  vanilla extract 
 1/4 c.  raisins 
 Instructions : 
 Mix the dry ingredients with the raisins in a small or medium bowl. 
 Mix in the remaining ingredients, combining all together thoroughly. 
 Make into 1 inch balls. 
 Refrigerate. 
 Notes : 
 Yields 2 dozen.
