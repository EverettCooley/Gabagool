 Description : 
 Purchased from a garage sale in Mineral Wells, Texas in 1994. Date and source unknown. These are tried and true and very good. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups  buttermilk  biscuit mix 
 1-1/2 cups grated sharp  Cheddar   Cheese 
 1-teaspoon  celery salt 
 ¾ cup  milk 
 ¾ cup halved fresh  cranberries 
 1-tablespoon  Sugar 
 
 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl combine biscuit mix,  Cheese  and  celery salt . 
 Add  milk  and stir until dough cleans the bowl. 
 Fold in  cranberries  and  Sugar . 
 Knead dough a few times on a heavily floured surface until a smooth ball. 
 Roll out dough to 3/4-inch thickness and cut with a cookie cutter dipped in  flour  into 2-inch rounds. 
 Place rounds onto a greased cookie sheet. 
 Bake at 450 for 12 minutes.
