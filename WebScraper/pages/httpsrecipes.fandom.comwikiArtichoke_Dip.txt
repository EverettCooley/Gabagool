Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Kathy Myrick 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cans  artichoke hearts  (9¼ oz) 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 1 cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 ⅛ tsp  garlic powder 
 juice from 1  lemon 
 cayenne pepper 
 Directions : 
 Drain artichoke hearts and chop. 
 Set aside. 
 Mix together the mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, garlic powder and lemon juice. 
 Mix in the chopped artichoke hearts. 
 Put mixture in a buttered casserole and sprinkle top with cayenne pepper. 
 Bake at 350°F for 30 to 40 minutes.
