 Ingredients : 
 2 	cup 	Sharp  Cheddar   Cheese  shredded 
 1 	tsp 	 worcestershire sauce 
 1/2 	tsp 	Dry  mustard 
 1 	small 	clove  garlic , minced 
 1/2 	cup 	 butter  (to 2/3 cup) 
 Assorted crackers or  rye bread  crumbs 
 Directions : 
 Have cheeses at room temperature. 
Combine cheeses, worcestershire,  mustard , and  garlic . 
Beat in enough  beer  to make of spreading consistency. 
Serve at room tempearture with crackers or  Rye  rounds.
