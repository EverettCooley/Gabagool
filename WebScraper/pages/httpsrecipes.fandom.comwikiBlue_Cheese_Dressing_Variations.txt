Ingredients : 
 ½ cup low-calorie blue cheese dressing 
 Anchovy blue cheese dressing : 
 1 oz  anchovy , mashed 
 Bacon blue cheese dressing : 
 1 tbsp  bacos , ground 
 Chive blue cheese dressing : 
 2 tbsp  chives , chopped allow to mellow for several hours 
 Herb blue cheese dressing : 
 1 tsp  parsley , ground 
 1 tsp  chives , ground 
 1 tsp  marjoram , ground 
 Directions : 
 Start with ½ cup low-calorie blue cheese dressing. 
 Add the appropriate extras to get the variation you want. 
 Allow to mellow for several hours. 
 Nutritional information : 
 ½ cup = 56 calories
 
 Anchovy dressing: 1 meat exchange 
 Bacon dressing: ½ fat exchange 
 Chive dressing: none 
 Herb dressing: none
