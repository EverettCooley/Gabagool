chocolate   pecan  Pie
 
 1 Refrigerated Pie Crust 
 2  eggs 
 1/2 Cup  flour 
 1/4 Cup  sugar 
 1/2 Cup  unsalted butter , Melted 
 1 Tsp  vanilla 
 1 Cup  pecan  Halves, Coarsely Chopped 
 2 Cups  milk   chocolate chips 
 12  pecan  Halves For Garnish 
 
Heat Oven to 325 Degrees. Fit The Piecrust Into a 9 Inch Pie Plate, Crimp Edges. Refrigerate Until Ready to Fill. in a Large Bowl, Beat  eggs , Beat in  flour  And Then  sugar . Continue to Beat For 1 minutes. Stir in  butter  And  vanilla  Until Combined. Fold in Chopped  pecans  And 1 Cup  chocolate chips . Pour Into Piecrust. Bake 55 minutes. or Until Edges Are Golden. Remove From Oven And Cool. Place Remaining  chocolate chips  in a Small Microwave Glass Bowl. Microwave on High For 1 minutes. Stir. Microwave For an Additional 30 Seconds. Stir Until Smooth. Spread Melted  chocolate  Over Top of Pie. Garnish With 12  pecan  Halves. Allow to Cool Completely. Makes 10 Servings.*
