Description : 
 Yield : 16 x ¼ cup servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  apples , chopped 
 1 cup  bananas , sliced 
 8 oz  pineapple  chunks, unsweetened, drained 
 ½ cup  raisins 
 ½ cup  celery , chopped 
 ⅓ cup  mayo , reduced calories 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 16  lettuce  leaves, optional 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients except  lettuce  leaves. 
 Toss gently to coat, and chill 1 to 2 hours. 
 Serve on  lettuce  leaves, if desired. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Each serving may be exchanged for 1 fruit.
