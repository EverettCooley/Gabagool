Ingredients : 
 ½ ounce  bittersweet chocolate 
 6  dried apricots 
 ¼ tablespoon chopped  pistachios 
 Directions : 
 Microwave the  chocolate  in a microwavable bowl on high for 2 minutes, stirring after 1 minute. 
 Stir until completely melted. 
 Dip the  apricots  halfway into the  chocolate . 
 Let the excess  chocolate  drip off. 
 Place the  apricots  onto waxed paper. 
 Sprinkle the pistachios over the chocolate-covered portions of the  apricots . 
 Place in the refrigerator until the  chocolate  is set.
