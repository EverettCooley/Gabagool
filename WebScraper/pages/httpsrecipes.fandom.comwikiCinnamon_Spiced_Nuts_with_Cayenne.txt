cinnamon  Spiced Nuts With  Cayenne 
 
 1 1/2 T.  sugar 
 1 T.  salt 
 3/4 T.  cayenne pepper 
 1/2 T. Ground  cinnamon 
 1/2 T. Ground  allspice 
 1/4 T. Ground Cloves 
 2 T.  canola oil 
 4 Cups Unsalted Raw Nuts Such as  walnut  Halves And Whole  almonds 
 
Preheat Oven to 350. Mix First 6 Ingredient in Large Bowl.Mix in  oil , Then Nuts. Toss to Coat Evenly. Spread Nuts on Rimmed Baking Sheet. Roast Until Nuts Are Golden, Stirring Occasionally, About 20 Minutes. Cool Nuts on Sheet. Can be Made 3 Days Ahead And Stored Airtight at Room Temperature. Source: Unknown
