Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 oz  tequila 
 2 oz  Pisang Ambon ® liqueur 
 1 oz Rose's®  lime juice 
 Directions : 
 Mix well in a blender with  ice  and serve in a highball glass that has been rimmed with  jimmies . 
 Garnish with a day-glo  orange  circus  Peanut .
