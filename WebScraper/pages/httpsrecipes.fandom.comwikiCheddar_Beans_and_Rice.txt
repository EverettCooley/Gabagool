Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup chopped  Onion 
 1 cup sliced  celery 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1 tablespoon polyunsaturated  margarine 
 1 cup dried  black beans , cooked and well seasoned 
 3 cups hot cooked brown or  white rice 
 2 cups shredded  Cheddar   Cheese 
 3 tablespoons chopped fresh  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Onion ,  celery  and  garlic  in  margarine  until tender. 
 Stir in beans; heat thoroughly. 
 Serve bean mixture over fluffy  rice . 
 Top with  Cheese .
