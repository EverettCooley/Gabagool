Ingredients : 
 8  flying fish  fillets 
 3  limes 
 1 tbsp  salt 
 3 tbsp Barbadian seasoning 
 1 large  onion  sliced 
 6 cloves  garlic  minced 
 1 green  bell pepper  cut in julienne strips 
 1 oz fresh  thyme 
 1 oz fresh  marjoram 
 1 tsp  parsley  chopped 
 1 large  tomato  chopped 
 1 tsp  lime juice 
 2 cups  water 
 2 tsp hot pepper sauce 
 2 tsp  curry powder 
 3 tbsp  margarine 
 seasoned  salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Rub the fish with the juice of the  limes  and the  salt  and leave to stand for 10 minutes. 
 Rinse the fish and pat dry with paper towels, then rub in the Barbadian seasoning. 
 Roll each up fish (like a sausage) and secure with a toothpick.
