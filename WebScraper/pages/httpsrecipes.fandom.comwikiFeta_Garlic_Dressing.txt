Ingredients : 
 1 cup  olive oil 
 salt 
 freshly ground  pepper 
 2 – 3 tsp  vinegar 
 1½ cup grated  feta 
 2 – 3  garlic  cloves, crushed 
 1 cup  milk  or  buttermilk 
 1 tsp dried  dill weed 
 Directions : 
 In a blender or food processor, whirl all ingredients except the  milk  for one minute. 
 With the blender running, slowly pour in  milk . 
 As soon as the dressing thickens, turn off the blender or the dressing will separate and become runny. 
 It should be thick and creamy. 
 Chill at least 30 minutes.
