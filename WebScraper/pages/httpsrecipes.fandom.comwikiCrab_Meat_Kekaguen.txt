 Ingredients : 
 1 bag  artificial crab meat , chopped 
 1 small  onion , chopped 
 2 – 4  hot peppers  (can be eliminated if desired) 
 2 – 3  lemons , squeezed 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Placed crab  meat ,  onion , and  lemon juice  in a medium size bowl and thoroughly mixed by hand. 
 Add  salt  and  pepper  to desired taste. 
 Add  hot peppers  if desired.
