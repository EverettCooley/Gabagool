Ingredients : 
 1 Tbsp vegetable oil 
 ½ Cup finely chopped onion 
 ½ Cup finely chopped celery 
 ¼ Cup finely chopped green pepper 
 1 Cup Heinz tomato ketchup 
 1 ½ Lb. lean ground beef 
 1 Cup soft, fresh bread crumbs 
 1 Large egg, slightly beaten 
 ½ Tsp. salt 
 ¼ Tsp. pepper 
 Procedure : 
 Preheat oven to 350°f. Heat oil in a large skillet set over medium-high heat. Cook onion, celery and pepper until softened, about 5 minutes. Combine 1 half of the sauce with ground beef, bread crumbs, egg, salt and pepper. Mix gently. Put mixture into a lightly greased meatloaf pan. Bake for 1 hour or until instant-read thermometer reaches 165°f. Rest meatloaf for 5 minutes. Reheat the reserved sauce mixture. Slice meatloaf and serve with sauce.
