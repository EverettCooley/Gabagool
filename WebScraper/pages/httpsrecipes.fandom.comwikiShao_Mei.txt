 Description : 
 Chinese dumplings
 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group This Y-group is international. Good food from all parts of the world. A place where we can share the wonderful food from all over the world. 
 Ingredients : 
 Filling : 
 1½ pounds lean  pork 
 ¾ pound medium  shrimp 
 2 ounces  black mushrooms , soaked in  water 
 Seasoning : 
 ½ tablespoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  white pepper 
 ½ teaspoon  sugar 
 3 tablespoons  sesame seed oil 
 Directions : 
 Cut  pork  and  black mushrooms  into small pieces. 
 Squeeze  mushrooms  until dry. 
 Mix  shrimp  in mixer until it begins to get sticky. 
 Take out, mix  pork  the same way for about three minutes. 
 Then add  shrimp  and  mushrooms , and mix and add  salt  and  pepper  and  sugar . 
 Mix and finish with  sesame seed   oil . 
 Wrap 1½ teaspoons of filling in each wrapper. 
 Steam for 10 minutes and serve.]]
