 Description : 
 This is guacamole for people who love the taste of  avocado . It's "pure" and simple.
 
 Because this recipe uses  California avocados ...it's definitely nutritious!
 
 Ingredients : 
 As many ripe avocados as you wish 
 Many  garlic  cloves 
 lemon juice 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Mash as many avocados as you wish. 
 Add many crushed  garlic  cloves. 
 Add  lemon juice 
 salt  to taste.
