Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: unknown. 
 Makes about 16 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pkg. frozen  strawberries  in  sugar , thawed (15 oz) 
 2 tbsp grated  lime peel 
 juice of 1  lime 
 1 bottle  Pinot Noir 
 1 bottle  Sauternes 
 1 bottle dry  champagne 
 Directions : 
 Combine  strawberries ,  lime  peel and  lime juice  in pan. 
 Simmer together 10 minutes. 
 Put through food mill or sieve. 
 Cool. 
 Pour fruit mixture over block of  ice  in punch bowl add wines just before serving. 
 Garnish with whole  strawberries  or  lime  slices if desired.
