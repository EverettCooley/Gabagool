Ingredients : 
 1 cupful  molasses 
 1 cupful  sugar 
 1 cupful  butter  or  lard 
 1 tablespoonful  ginger 
 1 tablespoonful  soda 
 ½ cupful of  sour milk 
 2  eggs 
 Directions : 
 First mix all dry ingredients in separate bowl and add to  eggs ,  milk ,  molasses , and  lard . 
 Bake at 350°F for 10 minutes.
