Description : 
 I love this stuff!!!! I think I could wipe the whole bowl of this. It is just the best stuff
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 head  napa cabbage 
 2 pkg.  ramen noodles , broken into pieces. discard seasoning pkt. 
 1 stick  butter 
 ¼ cup  oil 
 ½ cup packed  brown sugar 
 ¼ cup  vinegar  (I like  apple cider vinegar ) 
 2 heaping tbsp  soy sauce 
 black pepper  to taste (½ tsp) 
 1 cup roasted  sunflower seeds  (I like honey roasted) 
 ½ cup  almond  slices or slivers 
 2 bunches  green onion , chopped small 
 Directions : 
 Brown noodles in butter. 
 Set aside. 
 Mix oil, brown sugar, soy sauce and pepper in small saucepan. 
 Boil for 2 minutes. 
 Cool cut the napa cabbage up in bite size pieces, add the nuts and onions. 
 Add the noodles. 
 Just before serving add the oil, sugar mixture.
