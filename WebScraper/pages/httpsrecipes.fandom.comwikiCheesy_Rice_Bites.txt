Makes 48 bars.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 8-ounce package  cream cheese , softened 
 3 ounces bleu  Cheese , crumbed (3/4 cup) 
 2/3 cup pitted  ripe olives , divided 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 2  eggs , beaten 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1/3 cup finely chopped  walnuts 
 2 tablespoons chopped  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Blend cheeses. 
 Chop 1/3 cupÂ ; stir into  Cheese  mixture. 
 Add  rice ,  eggs  and  salt . 
 Spread into buttered 13x9-inch baking pan. 
 Top  rice  mixture with  nuts ,  parsley  and remaining 1/3 cup, sliced. 
 Bake at 350 degrees 20 minutes, or until set. 
 Chill one hour. 
 Cut into 1-1/2 by 1-inch bars.
