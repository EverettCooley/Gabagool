Description : 
 Contributed by Linda in Tennessee via  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 4  chicken breast  fillets, halved lengthwise 
 cracked  black pepper 
 1½ tablespoons  olive oil , plus extra  oil  for sauce 
 2 tablespoons  capers 
 1 teaspoon  chile flakes 
 1 tablespoon finely grated  lemon rind 
 2 cloves  garlic , sliced 
 2 tablespoons  lemon juice 
 ½ cup chopped  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Heat the  oil  in a frying pan over medium heat. 
 Sprinkle the  chicken  with the pepper and add to the pan. 
 Cook for 5 minutes each side or until browned. 
 Add the extra  oil ,  capers ,  chile flakes ,  lemon  rind and  garlic , cook for 1 minute, then add the  lemon juice  and  parsley . 
 Serve with a rocket ( arugula ) salad.
