Ingredients : 
 1½ kg  green cabbage 
 ½ l  water 
 salt  (to taste) 
 2 teaspoon of  lemon juice  or  vinegar 
 sugar  (to taste) 
 Directions : 
 Clean and shred the  cabbage , put it into a little boiling  water  and cook until soft. 
 Now add  water  if necessary. 
 And season with the  lemon juice  or  vinegar ,  salt  and  sugar . 
 Serve as an accompaniment to  lamb  or cured meat.
