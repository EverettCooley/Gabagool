Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz  rum 
 1 oz  blackberry brandy 
 1 oz  banana liqueur 
 1⅕ oz  lime juice 
 ½ oz  grenadine  syrup 
 crushed  ice 
 Directions : 
 Mix in blender until  ice  is crushed into slushy form. 
 Serve while frozen.
