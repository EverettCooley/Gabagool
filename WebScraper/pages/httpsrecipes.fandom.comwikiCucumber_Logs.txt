 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 	  cucumbers 
 1 (15 	ounce) can  Chickpeas , rinsed 
 1 	tablespoon extra virgin  olive oil 
 1 	tablespoon  water 
 1/3 	cup  lemon juice , fresh 
 1 	teaspoon  salt 
 1/4 	cup  black olives , chopped small 
 Directions : 
 Cut off the ends of the  cucumbers . Cut each  cucumber  crosswise into 6 rounds and turn the pieces so they're flat. Gently scoop out some of the flesh from the top of each  cucumber  round, making a cup. 
 In a food processor or blender, make the hummus by combining the  Chickpeas ,  oil ,  water ,  lemon juice  and  salt . Puree, adding more  water  if needed for creaminess. Stir in  black olives . Spoon the mix into the hollowed out part of each  cucumber  round. Arrange on a serving tray.
