Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x 11-ounce package Mexican  rice  mix, prepared, cooled 
 1 x 15-ounce can  pinto beans , drained 
 1 cup (4 ounces) shredded  Cheddar  cheese 
 Â¼ cup bottled Italian  salad dressing 
 lettuce  leaves or crisp corn  tortilla  shells (cup shaped) 
 sour cream  for garnish 
 avocado  chunks for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  beans  and  cheese  in large mixing bowl. 
 Pour dressing over  rice  mixture; toss lightly. 
 Serve on  lettuce  leaves or crisp tortilla shells; garnish with  sour cream  and  avocado .
