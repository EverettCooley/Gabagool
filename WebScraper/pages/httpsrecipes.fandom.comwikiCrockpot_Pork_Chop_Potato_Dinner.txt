Description : 
 Tender chops cook on a bed of creamy potatoes in this all-in-one meal.  It's a snap to assemble.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: the Taste of Home Slow Cooker Recipes 
 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 6 bone-in  pork chops  (½ inch thick) 
 1 tbsp.  vegetable oil 
 30 oz pkg. frozen shredded  hash brown  potatoes, thawed 
 1½ cups (6 oz) shredded  cheddar  cheese, divided 
 10¾ oz can condensed  cream of celery  soup, undiluted 
 ½ cup  milk 
 ½ cup  sour cream 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ⅛ tsp  pepper 
 1 can (2.8 oz)  French-fried onions , divided 
 Directions : 
 In a large skillet, brown chops in oil on both sides; set aside and keep warm. 
 In a bowl, combine the potatoes, 1 cup of the cheese, soup, milk, sour cream, salt and pepper. 
 Stir in half the onions. 
 Transfer to a greased 5-quart slow cooker; top with pork chops. 
 Cover and cook on high for 2½ to 3 hours or until meat juices run clear. 
 Sprinkle with remaining cheese and onions. 
 Cover and cook 10 minutes longer or until cheese is melted.
