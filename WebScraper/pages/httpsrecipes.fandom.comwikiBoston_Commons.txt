 Description : 
 A  rum  drink
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz  light rum 
 Juice of 1/2  lemon 
 1 tsp  powdered sugar 
 2 oz  club soda 
 club soda 
 Cracked  ice 
 Directions : 
 Into a glass pour  lemon juice ,  Sugar , and 2 oz  club soda . 
 Stir well. Fill glass with cracked  ice . 
 Add  rum . Fill with  club soda  and stir well. 
 Add a spiral of  orange peel  on top.
