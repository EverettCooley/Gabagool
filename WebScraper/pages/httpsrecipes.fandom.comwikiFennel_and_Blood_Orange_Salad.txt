      fresh fennel     
 Prep Time: 5 
 Cook time: 10 
 Serves: 6 
 
 Description : 
 The secret to fennel is knowing how to use it & what to pair it with. Fennel can be a bit tough and fibrous with an anise flavor. It is also light, crunchy and refreshing found in many Mediterranean recipes.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 bulb of  fennel , cut in ½ and remove core 
 2 – 3  blood oranges 
 salt  and  pepper 
 3 glugs of extra virgin  olive oil 
 Directions : 
 Slice fennel as this as possible (use a food slicer or mandolin if available). 
 Peel and supreme the oranges. 
 Save the extra pulp and squeeze the remaining juice into the bowl. 
 Mix sliced fennel, juice from the pulp and oranges - season with salt and pepper. 
 Drizzle with olive oil and toss. 
 Let stand for 10 minutes then serve.
