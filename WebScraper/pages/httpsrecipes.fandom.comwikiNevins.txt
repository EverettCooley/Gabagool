 Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 oz  Bourbon 
 1 1/2 tsp Apricot-flavored  brandy 
 1 tbsp  grapefruit juice 
 1 1/2 tsp  lemon juice 
 1 dash  bitters 
 Directions : 
 Shake with  ice  and strain into cocktail glass.
