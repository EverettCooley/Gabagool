 Prep Time: 20 minutes 
 Cook time: 10 minutes 
 Serves: 2-3 
 
 Description : 
 Yummy easy pancakes.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup flour 
 1 tsp salt 
 1 tsp baking soda 
 2 eggs 
 3/4 cup plain or vanilla yogurt 
 1/2 cup milk 
 2 tbsp melted butter 
 Directions : 
 Mix together dry ingredients 
 Mix egg yolks, yogurt, milk and butter and add to dry ingredients 
 Whip egg whites and add to mixture 
 Spoon portions onto lightly oiled skillet until cooked to a medium brown. 
 Serve with jam, maple syrup or peanut butter and ENJOY!
