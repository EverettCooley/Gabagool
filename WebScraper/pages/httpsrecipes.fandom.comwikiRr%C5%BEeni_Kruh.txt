Description : 
 Rye bread
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  milk 
 2 tablespoons  sugar 
 1  cake yeast 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 cup white  flour 
 2 cups  rye flour 
 1 cup  whole wheat flour 
 1 tablespoon melted  butter 
 Directions : 
 Melt  yeast  in 8 tablespoons of warm  milk , stir in the  sugar ,  salt  and add the rest of  milk . 
 Gradually stir in the white and  wheat flour  and add the melted  shortening  . 
 Mix and gradually add the  rye flour  and blend well. Place in 1 big or 2 small greased pans. 
 Let rise in warm place until dough is double in bulk. Bake for 1 hour.
