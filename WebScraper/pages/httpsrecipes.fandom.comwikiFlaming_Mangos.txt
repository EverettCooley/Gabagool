Ingredients : 
 vanilla  ice cream 
 ¼ cup  brandy 
 1 tsp  lemon juice 
 4  mangos , peeled and sliced 
 ½ cup  water 
 3 tbsp  sugar 
 ¼ cup  mango jam 
 Directions : 
 Simmer jam,  sugar  and  water  until mixture is syrupy, about 5 minutes. 
 Add  mangos  and cook over low heat 3 minutes. 
 Stir in  lemon juice . 
 Heat  brandy  until just warm. 
 Ignite and pour flaming over  mangos . 
 Stir and spoon over  ice   cream .
