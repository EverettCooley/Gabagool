Ingredients 
 chicken 
 salt 
 pepper 
 2 medium  onions , sliced 
 1 cup  ketchup 
 rice 
 Directions 
 Boil chicken. Remove from stock and debone. Return to stock. 
 Add salt, pepper, 2 sliced medium onions and 1 cup ketchup. Simmer until thickened. 
 Serve over rice.
