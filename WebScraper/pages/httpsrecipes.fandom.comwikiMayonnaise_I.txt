Description : 
 In Romanian: Maioneza
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 hard boiled  yolk 
 1 raw  yolk 
 1 teaspoon  mustard 
 1 teaspoon  lemon juice 
 2 cups  oil 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Sieve the hard boiled  yolk  and place in a bowl. 
 Mix with a little  oil . 
 Then add the raw  yolk  and keep mixing while adding a little  oil  at a time. 
 After all the  oil  is used up, add  salt ,  mustard  and  lemon juice . 
 From the beginning to the end, stir in the same direction. 
 You may add a tablespoon of  sour cream .
