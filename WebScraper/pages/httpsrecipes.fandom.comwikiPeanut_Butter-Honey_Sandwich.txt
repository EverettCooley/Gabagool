Info 
 Serves : 1
 
 Ingredients 
 2 slices of  bread 
 2 tablespoons of  peanut butter 
 1 tablespoon of  honey 
 Sprinkle of  cinnamon 
 Directions 
 1. Mix peanut butter and honey in a cup.
 
2. Spread peanut butter and honey mix on one slice of bread. Sprinkle peanut butter and honey slice with cinnamon.
 
3. Sandwich the 2 pieces of bread together.
