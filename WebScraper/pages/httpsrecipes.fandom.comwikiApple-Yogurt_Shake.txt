Ingredients : 
 3  Apples , peeled 
 1 cup  apple juice 
 1 cup  milk 
 1 cup plain  yogurt 
 3 tbsp  honey 
 Directions : 
 Cut  Apples  into quarters. 
 Remove the seeds and cut into small pieces. 
 Put all ingredients in a blender and puree. 
 Pour into glasses and serve chilled.
