Southern Spoon Bread 
 3 cups milk
1 teaspoon salt
1 Tablespoon sugar
1 1/4 cups corn meal
3 eggs
1 Tablespoon baking powder
2 Tablespoons cold water
2 Tablespoons butter
 Preheat oven to 400�F. In pan, heat milk, salt and sugar to a moderate
temperature. Add corn meal and cook as mush (about 5 minutes). Beat
together eggs, baking powder, water and butter. Add to corn meal
mixture. Pour into buttered 1 1/2 quart baking dish. Bake for 25 to 30
minutes.
