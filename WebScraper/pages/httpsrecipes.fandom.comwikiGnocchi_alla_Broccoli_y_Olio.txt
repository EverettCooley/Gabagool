Description : 
 This is an awesome recipe for gnocchi!!!
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 package fresh  gnocchi 
 1 head of fresh  broccoli  cut into small florets 
 1 medium  onion  chopped 
 3 cloves of  garlic  minced 
 6 tablespoons  olive oil 
 pecorino romano  (about half cup) 
 Directions : 
 heat olive oil over medium heat in a very large skillet. 
 Start water to boil in large pot. 
 Steam broccoli until almost tender. 
 Add chopped onion and saute until translucent. 
 Add minced garlic and saute about 1 minute. 
 Add steamed broccoli and saute 5 minutes. 
 Add cooked gnocchi (cooked according to package directions). 
 Remove from heat and add pecorino romano to taste (about a half cup is what I use) and fresh ground black pepper. 
 Add extra olive oil if needed and saute until just heated through, about 1 minute. 
 Pass additional romano at serving, if desired.
