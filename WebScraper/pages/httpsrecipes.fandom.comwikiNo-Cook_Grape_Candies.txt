Description 
 Grape Juice, Canned, Unsweetened  by the US Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Serves : 36 
 Ingredients 
 ¼ cup  butter 
 1 pound  confectioner's sugar 
 ½ cup finely chopped  walnuts 
 ½ cup  grape juice 
 4 ounces  semi-sweet chocolate  pieces 
 2 tablespoons  butter 
 Directions 
 In a bowl, combine butter, confectioner’s sugar, walnuts, and grape juice. 
 Work into a smooth ball with hands. 
 Shape into 36 1-inch balls, set aside. 
 Melt chocolate pieces and butter together. 
 Dip balls into melted mixture and place on waxed paper. Chill.
