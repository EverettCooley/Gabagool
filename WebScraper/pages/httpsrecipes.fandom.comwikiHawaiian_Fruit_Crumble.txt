Ingredients : 
 2 cups thinly sliced  apples 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 1 cup crushed  pineapple , drained 
 2 cups  cranberry sauce 
 1 cup quick cooking  oats 
 ¾ cup packed  brown sugar 
 ½ cup  all-purpose flour 
 ½ teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 salt  to taste 
 ⅛ cup  butter 
 Directions : 
 Toss  apple  slices with  lemon juice . 
 Place in a 10 x 6 x 1½ inch baking dish. 
 Spoon  pineapple  evenly over  apples , then cover with  cranberry sauce . 
 Mix  oats ,  brown sugar ,  flour ,  cinnamon , and  salt . 
 Cut in  butter  or  margarine  till crumbly. Sprinkle over fruit. 
 Bake at 350 °F (175 °C) for 30 minutes, or till  apples  are tender. Serve warm.
