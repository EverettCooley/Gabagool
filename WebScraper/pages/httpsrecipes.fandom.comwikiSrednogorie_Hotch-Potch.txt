Ingredients : 
 1 kg  veal 
 2 cupfuls  mushrooms 
 100 g  okra 
 2 – 3  onions 
 2 – 3  tomatoes 
 3 – 4  peppers 
 ½ cupful  rice 
 1 – 2  potatoes 
 half a cupful  white wine 
 1 tsp  paprika 
 pepper 
 parsley 
 ¾ cupful  sunflower oil 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Fry the meat into large pieces in  oil  and some  water , take out. 
 Brown the finely chopped  onions ,  tomatoes  and  paprika  in the same  oil . 
 Replace the meat, add the wine and some hot  water , bring to the boil and  salt . 
 Add the  mushrooms  and after some 10min the  peppers  sliced into strips and the cleaned  okra . 
 When tender, add the remaining  oil , bake. 
 Sprinkle with finely chopped  parsley  and  pepper  before serving.
