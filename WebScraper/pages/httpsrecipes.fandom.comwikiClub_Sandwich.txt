Description : 
 Serves 6 people.
 
 Ingredients : 
 mayonnaise  or  salad dressing 
 18 slices  white bread , toasted 
 12  lettuce  leaves 
 6 slices cooked  chicken  or  turkey 
 18 slices  tomato , about 3 med. 
 12 slices  bacon , crisply fried 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Spread  mayonnaise  over 1 side of each slice of toast. 
 Place 1  lettuce  leaf and 1 slice  chicken  on each of 6 toast slices. 
 Cover with second slice of toast. 
 Top with  lettuce  leaf, 3 slices  tomato  and 2 slices  bacon . 
 Sprinkle with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Cover with third slice of toast; secure with wooden picks. 
 To serve, cut diagonally into triangles.
