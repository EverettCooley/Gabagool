 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  brown rice , cooled to room temperature 
 1 cup cooked  pinto beans 
 1 cup chopped fresh  tomato 
 1 cup cooked  Chicken  cubes 
 1 cup (4 ounces) shredded  Cheddar  or  American cheese 
 1 tablespoon chopped fresh  parsley 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1/3 cup prepared Italian dressing 
 lettuce  leaves (optional) 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice , beans,  tomato ,  Chicken ,  Cheese ,  parsley ,  salt  and  pepper  in large bowl. Pour dressing over  rice  mixture; toss lightly. Serve on  lettuce  leaves, if desired.
