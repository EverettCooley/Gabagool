Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 8 large  potatoes  sliced (Yukon Gold or  red potatoes  work Best) 
 2 large Spanish or Vidalia  onions  sliced 
 olive oil 
 Vegan  margarine 
 salt 
 pepper 
 Aluminum Foil 
 Non Stick  cooking spray 
 Directions : 
 Tear off two fairly large sheets of aluminum foil (approx. 36" each), and lay one horizontally with the other one on top going vertically. 
 Spray both sheets very well with cooking spray. 
 Place half of the sliced potatoes in the center of the two sheets of foil. 
 Put half of the sliced onions on top of the potatoes. 
 Drizzle olive oil over the potatoes and onions, and dot with little pieces of margarine. 
 Sprinkle salt and pepper to taste. 
 Make a second layer by repeating the above directions.
