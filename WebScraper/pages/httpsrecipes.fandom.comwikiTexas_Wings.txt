Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 Sauce : 
 1 cup  apple juice 
 ⅓ cup  lime juice 
 ¾ cup  Louisiana Hot Sauce 
 1 teaspoon  Worcestershire sauce 
 1 teaspoon  celery seed 
 1 teaspoon  white pepper 
 ½ teaspoon  Tabasco sauce 
 1 teaspoon  water 
 1 teaspoon  cornstarch 
 Chicken wings : 
 20  chicken  drummettes 
 2 cups  all-purpose flour 
 2 teaspoons  salt 
 2 teaspoons  black pepper 
 1 teaspoon  paprika 
 Directions : 
 Combine all of the sauce ingredients except  water  and  cornstarch  in a pan and bring to a boil. 
 Combine  water  and  cornstarch  in a separate bowl and then stir into boiling liquid. 
 Cook for 5 minutes, stirring constantly, set aside and keep warm. 
 Combine the  flour  with the  salt ,  pepper  and  paprika , and mix well. 
 Dust the  chicken  drummettes in the seasoned  flour  and deep fry until golden brown. 
 Place fried drummettes in a bowl and ladle the sauce over them. toss to coat. 
 Remove the  chicken  from sauce and serve with your favorite dressing.
