beer   bread  on The Light Side
 
 ~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~* 
 
Ingredients:
 
 3 Cups  self-rising flour 
 2 Tbsp  sugar 
 1 Can Lite  beer , Room Temperature 
 1  egg , Beaten With 1 Tbsp  water 
 
 Directions:
 Mix  flour ,  sugar , And  beer . Pour Mixture Into a NonstickBread Pan Which Has Been Coated With Nonstick Spray. LetSit 15 Minutes to Rise. Brush Top With Egg/Water Mixture.Bake at 375 Degrees For About an Hour.
