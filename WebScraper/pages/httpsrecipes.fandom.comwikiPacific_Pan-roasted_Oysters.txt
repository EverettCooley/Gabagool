Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Ritchie Estate in Balch Springs, Texas in 1984.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup fresh medium size  oysters 
 3  eggs  slightly beaten 
 ½ cup  flour 
 ½ cup fine  bread crumbs 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ cup  vegetable oil 
 4 teaspoons  water 
 2 teaspoons freshly ground  black pepper 
 Cocktail sauce : 
 ½ cup  catsup 
 1 tablespoon  horseradish 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 1 tablespoon  Worcestershire sauce 
 Directions : 
 Heat oil in large cast iron or heavy skillet over medium heat. 
 Mix flour, salt and pepper. 
 Mix eggs and water. 
 Dip each oyster in flour then egg water mixture then bread crumbs before putting in skillet. 
 Cook approximately 3 minutes per side. 
 Oysters should be medium brown color. 
 Turn only once. 
 Serve hot with cocktail sauce.
