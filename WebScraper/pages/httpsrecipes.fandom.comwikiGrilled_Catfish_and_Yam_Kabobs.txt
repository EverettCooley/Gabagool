 Description : 
 A  Catfish  recipe.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 tbsp  orange juice 
 2 tbsp  honey 
 1 tbsp  margarine 
 1½ lb U.S. farm-raised  catfish  fillets, cut into pieces 
 1½  sweet potatoes , peeled and sliced into circles 
 ½ lb  portabello mushrooms  cut into fourths 
 1  red onion , cut into eighths 
 additional vegetables optional 
 Directions : 
 In a medium bowl, mix together  orange juice , honey and  margarine . 
 Add  sweet potatoes ,  mushrooms ,  onions , and  catfish . 
 Marinate for 30 minutes. 
 Skewer the marinated ingredients on metal skewers. 
 Grill for 15 to 20 minutes or until vegetables are tender when pierced with skewer and  catfish  is opaque in color.
