Granola Sundae
 
 1 C.  vanilla yogurt 
 1 C. Granola Mix 
 2 C. Sliced  bananas 
 1 Pint  strawberries , Sliced 
 
 Layer  yogurt , Granola And Fruit in 4 Stemmed Glasses or Bowls. Refrigerate Until Ready to Serve.
