Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup sliced  celery 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 1 x 10¾-ounce can condensed  cream of mushroom  soup 
 ½ cup  sour cream 
 ¼ cup dry  white wine 
 2 tablespoons chopped  chives 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon seasoned  pepper 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 2 cups cooked  chicken  cubes 
 1 cup shredded sharp  Cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Cook  celery  in  butter  in large skillet or saucepan until tender but not brown. 
 Add soup,  sour cream , wine,  chives ,  salt  and  pepper ; heat. Stir in  rice  and  chicken . 
 Turn into greased shallow 2-quart baking dish. Sprinkle with cheese. 
 Bake at 350°F for 20 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
