 Description : 
 Cabbage Soup with Pork 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ lb lean  pork , cut into small pieces 
 1 tbsp  butter 
 4 cups  beef broth /bouillon 
 1 can  tomato soup 
 4 cups shredded  cabbage  (approx 1 lb) 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 2 tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  paprika 
 1  bay leaf 
 pepper  to taste 
 1 tbsp  sherry 
 sour cream 
 Directions : 
 Brown pork in butter. 
 Add remaining ingredients, except sour cream. Cover and simmer for approximately 30 minutes, stirring often. 
 Serve with a spoonful of sour cream.
