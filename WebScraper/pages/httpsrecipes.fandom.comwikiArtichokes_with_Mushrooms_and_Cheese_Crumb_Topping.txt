Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: With Love From Darling's Kitchen 
 Serves 6. 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ pound  mushrooms , sliced 
 1  onion , minced 
 2  shallots , minced 
 4 clove  garlic , minced 
 3 tbsp  butter 
 ¼ cup  cream 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 jar marinated  artichoke hearts , drained (15 oz) 
 Cheese crumb topping : 
 2 tbsp fresh  bread crumbs 
 2 tbsp grated  Parmesan cheese 
 1 tbsp melted  butter 
 Directions : 
 Sauté together first 5 ingredients until  mushrooms  are softened. 
 Add the cream and  lemon juice  and cook for 2 minute. 
 In a large oval porcelain baker, stir together  mushroom  mixture and artichokes and sprinkle top with  cheese  crumb topping. 
 Bake in a 350°F oven for 25 minutes or until crumbs are browned. 
 Cheese crumb topping : 
 Toss together until blended  bread crumbs , grated  Parmesan cheese  and melted  butter .
