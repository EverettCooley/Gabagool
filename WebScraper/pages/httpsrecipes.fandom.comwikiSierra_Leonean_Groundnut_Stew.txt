Total Time :  3 hours and 25 minutes 
 Prep:  3 hours 
 Cooking:  25 minutes 
 Serves:  4 
 
 
 Description : 
 Rice is the staple and is combined with different stews. The stew is usually veggies and some kind of meat. 
 such as:  Cassava leaves, ground nut, and spinach just to name a few. This is a traditional dish from Sierra Leone. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound stewing meat 
 2 large  tomatoes , diced 
 1/2 cup  oil 
 1/4 cup  Groundnut  paste ( Peanut   butter ) 
 1/2 tsp. "Mrs. Dash" seasoning 
 1 large  Onion , chopped or sliced 
 1 large  pepper , finely chopped 
 1/2 tsp.  cayenne pepper , optional 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Season meat. Let it absorb for three hours. Brown meat in  oil . Add some  water  and simmer until tender. Remove and set aside. In the same  oil , sauté  pepper  and  onions . Add  tomatoes  and stir briskly. Mix  Groundnut  paste with 1/2 cup  water  to form a thin paste and add to stew. Stir, add meat,  salt  to taste and let simmer 15 minutes over low heat. Serve with  rice , boiled  yam ,  cassava  and green vegetable.
