Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Pinion Estate in Mesquite, Texas in 1994.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2½ cups  all-purpose flour 
 2½ teaspoons  baking powder 
 ½ teaspoon  baking soda 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅓ cup  vegetable shortening 
 1¼ cups  buttermilk 
 ½ cup shredded sharp  cheddar  cheese 
 2 tablespoons chopped fresh  chives 
 2 tablespoons  butter  melted 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 450°F. 
 In large bowl combine  flour ,  baking powder , baking soda  and  salt . 
 Cut in  shortening  with two knives then stir in buttermilk,  cheese  and  chives  just until blended. 
 Drop dough by heaping tablespoons 1 inch apart on ungreased large baking or cookie sheet. 
 Brush tops of biscuits with melted  butter . 
 Bake 12 minutes and serve warm.
