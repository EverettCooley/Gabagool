Description : 
 This recipe comes from Montréal, Québec
 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  ground beef 
 ½  onion , minced 
 1  egg , beaten 
 1 sleeve of  saltines , crushed 
 1 teaspoon each dried  basil ,  garlic powder  and  seasoning salt 
 2 cups  heavy whipping cream 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 1 cup  Parmesan cheese , grated or shredded 
 Directions : 
 In large bowl, mix together beef, onion, egg, saltines and seasonings. 
 Roll into 1-inch balls. 
 Fry in skillet or bake in oven until cooked through (can also be frozen until ready to use) 
 In heavy-bottomed saucepan, combine whipping cream and butter and heat until just before boiling. 
 Add Parmesan and whisk 5 – 7 minutes until thickened. 
 Pour over meatballs and serve. 
 This is also great over pasta.
