 Description : 
 Makes 6 generous servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 lbs.  carrots 
 4 tbsp.  butter 
 1/3 c. dry  white wine 
 1/2 tsp.  nutmeg 
 2/3 c. white  Raisins 
 3 tbsp.  brown sugar 
 Directions : 
 Peel and slice  carrots  into 1/4 inch round slices. 
 Place in pan with  butter , wine and  nutmeg . 
 Cover and cook until tender. 
 Stir in  Raisins  and  brown sugar . 
 Cook until  Raisins  are plump and  carrots  are glazed.
