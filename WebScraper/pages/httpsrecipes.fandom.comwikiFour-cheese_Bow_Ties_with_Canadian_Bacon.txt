Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Cannon Estate in Arlington, Texas in 1992.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 8 ounces  bow ties  uncooked 
 1 cup  half and half 
 ½ cup crumbled  blue cheese 
 ½ cup grated  Swiss cheese 
 ½ cup grated  romano cheese 
 ¼ cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 ⅓ cup  Canadian bacon  strips 
 2 tablespoons chopped fresh  basil 
 Directions : 
 Cook pasta according to package directions. 
 While pasta is cooking heat half and half in 2 quart saucepan over medium heat but do not boil. 
 Add all cheeses then reduce heat stirring constantly until sauce is smooth. 
 Remove from heat then pour over well drained pasta and toss. 
 Sprinkle with additional Parmesan cheese, Canadian bacon and basil. 
 Serve immediately.
