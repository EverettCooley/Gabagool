      Vichy water     
 Vichy water  is a naturally sparkling mineral water from the springs located in and around central France's well-known spa city of Vichy. This famous potable is the water that is supposed to be used to prepare  Vichy carrots .
 The term is sometimes also used to describe sparkling mineral water that resembles true Vichy water.
