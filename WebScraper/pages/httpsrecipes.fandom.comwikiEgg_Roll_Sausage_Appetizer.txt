Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. It was obtained when the family collection was purchased from the Burnett Estate in Terrell, Texas in 1981.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 Cups cooked crumbled spicy  sausage 
 1½ Cups grated  Cheddar   cheese 
 1½ Cups Monterey  jack cheese 
 1 Cup prepared Ranch dressing 
 ½ Cup chopped  tomatoes 
 1 Package egg roll wrappers, cut in quarters 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 Blot dry  sausage  then combine  sausage  with cheeses, dressing and  tomatoes . 
 Lightly spray muffin tin then press 1 wrapper in each cup. 
 Spray with  oil  then bake 5 minutes. 
 Remove from tin and place on baking sheet. 
 Fill with  sausage  mixture and bake 5 minutes.
