 Ingredients : 
 1 large tin of each (825g cans),  apricot  halves,  peach  slices, whole peeled  plums 
 ⅔ cup of  brown sugar 
 1 cup red  maraschino cherries  from grocery 
 1 cup of brandy of  Grand Marnier 
 ¼ cup of blanched  almonds 
 Directions : 
 Drain fruit and reserve 2 cups of liquid. 
 Combine this juice in pan with sugar and cherries, bring to boil and stir until sugar dissolved. 
 Cool. 
 Mix liquid with fruit, add brandy and refrigerate in air tight Tupperware bowl---let set 1 week sprinkle with almonds at serving.
