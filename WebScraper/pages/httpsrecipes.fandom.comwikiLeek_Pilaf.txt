 Description : 
 In Romanian: Pilaf de praz.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4-5  leeks , 
 2  onions , 
 1 cup  rice , 
 2 cups  water , 
 3 tablespoons  oil , 
 1 tablespoon  tomato  sauce, 
 salt , 
 pepper 
 Directions : 
 Lightly fry the  onions  in  oil , then add the washed  rice  and lightly fry it. 
 Add the cut, washed and blanched  leeks ,  salt ,  pepper ,  tomato  sauce and  water . 
 Set to boil and at the first bubble, cover the pot and bake until done.
