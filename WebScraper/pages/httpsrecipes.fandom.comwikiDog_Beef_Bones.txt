Description : 
 My homemade dog treats.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 teaspoons  soy sauce 
 ½ pound  beef , cut 
 ⅓ cup  flour 
 1 tablespoon  salt 
 ½ tablespoon  chicken broth 
 Preparation : 
 In a mixing bowl, combine soy sauce, flour, salt, chicken broth and beef. 
 Mix the ingredients together. 
 Knead the mixture into bones. 
 Bake at 355°F until ready. 
 Let cool.
