Jun is basically anything fried in an  egg  batter.
 
 Ingredients : 
 200g (a little less than a half a pound) ground  Beef  or  Pork 
 A large slab of  tofu 
 2  eggs 
 2 tblsp. chopped  green onions 
 1 tblsp. crushed  garlic 
 1/2 tsp.  salt 
 1/2 tsp.  pepper 
 1 tsp.  sesame oil 
 vegetable oil 
 flour 
 Directions : 
 Smash the  tofu  in a cotton cloth. 
 Mix the  green onions ,  garlic ,  salt ,  pepper ,  tofu  and  sesame oil  with the meat. 
 Roll meat into about 3 cm (1 1/4Â inch) balls. 
 Sprinkle  flour  over the balls and dip them into the stirred  eggs . 
 Put a little  vegetable oil  on a heated pan and fry the meatballs to taste.
