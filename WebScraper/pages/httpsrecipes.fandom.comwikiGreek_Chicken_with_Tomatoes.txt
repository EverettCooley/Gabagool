This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Wiggins Estate in Montgomery, Alabama in 1987.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pound  chicken 
 1 large  onion  sliced 
 1 quart liquid  tomato paste 
 Â½ cup  olive oil 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 2 teaspoons  sugar 
 1 can cooked  okra  beans 
 Directions : 
 Heat oil in a large pot over medium high heat. 
 Place cleaned chicken in pot and turn to brown all sides evenly. 
 Add tomato juice then add the same amount of water and the sugar. 
 Let boil for one hour then stir in okra and serve.
