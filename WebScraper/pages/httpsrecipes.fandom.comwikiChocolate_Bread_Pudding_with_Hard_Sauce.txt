 Description : 
 Purchased from the Colvin Estate in Little Rock, Arkansas in 1989. Dated 1918. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 slices bread 
 2 cups  milk 
 2  eggs 
 2 squares  chocolate 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 Hard Sauce:
 
 1 cup confectioners'  Sugar 
 3 tablespoons unsalted  butter 
 1/8 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 Â¼ teaspoon ground  nutmeg 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Break bread into small pieces. Heat 2 cups  milk  and soak bread in  milk  for 1/2 hour. 
 Beat 2  eggs  and melt  chocolate . Add to bread. Mix in  Sugar  and  vanilla . 
 Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes. 
 Make sauce by mixing confectioners'  Sugar  with 3 tablespoons  butter ,  salt  and  vanilla . 
 Place in small dish and top with  nutmeg . When ready to serve pour sauce over bread pudding.
