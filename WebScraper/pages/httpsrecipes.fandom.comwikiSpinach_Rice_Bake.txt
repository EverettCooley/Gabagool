 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 5 cups chopped fresh  spinach , cooked 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 ½ teaspoon  garlic powder 
 ½ teaspoon dry  basil 
 ½ teaspoon  thyme 
 3  eggs , beaten 
 ½ cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 Directions : 
 Cook  onion  in  butter  in large skillet until tender. 
 Stir in  spinach ,  rice ,  garlic powder ,  basil ,  thyme  and  eggs . 
 Turn into greased 2-quart baking dish; sprinkle with  Parmesan cheese . 
 Bake at 375 °F for 20 minutes.
