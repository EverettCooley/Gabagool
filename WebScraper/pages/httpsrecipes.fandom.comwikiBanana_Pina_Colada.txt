Ingredients : 
 3/4 oz. Coconut Rum 
 3/4 oz. Banana Rum 
 1 1/2 oz. Cream of Coconut 
 4 1/2 oz. Pineapple Juice 
 1 Small Banana 
 Ice 
 Garnish: Pineapple Slice/Maraschino Cherry
 
 Instructions : 
 Add ice, coconut rum, banana rum, pineapple juice, cream of coconut and one small banana to blender. Combine well until smooth. 
 Pour mix into glass and garnish with pineapple and cherry.
