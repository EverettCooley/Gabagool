Ingredients : 
 1/4 	cup 	 butter 
 3/4 	cup 	 sour cream 
 1 	cup 	 white sugar 
 1 	tsp 	 vanilla 
 1/4 	tsp 	 salt 
 1/2 	cup 	 nuts 
 1 	cup 	 brown sugar 
 2 	tbsp 	White Syrup 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter . 
 Add  brown sugar  and heat to boiling. 
 Add  white sugar ,  sour cream  and  salt . 
 Cook to 236 degrees. 
 Cool. 
 Add  vanilla  and  nuts  then beat until thick. 
 Pour into buttered pan.
