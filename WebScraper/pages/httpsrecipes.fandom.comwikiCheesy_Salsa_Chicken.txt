Description : 
 10 minute cheesy salsa chicken
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Makes 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp  all purpose flour 
 ¼ tsp each  salt  and ground  pepper 
 4 skinned boned  chicken breast  halves, tenderloins removed 
 2 tsp  oil 
 1 cup  salsa 
 ½ cup drained canned  corn 
 ¼ cup Mexican blend shredded  cheese 
 garnish - chopped  cilantro 
 Directions : 
 Mix flour, salt and pepper on a sheet of wax paper. 
 Add chicken, turn to coat. Shake off excess. 
 Heat oil in a skillet. 
 Add chicken and cook for 6 minutes, turning once until golden and done. 
 Top breasts with salsa, corn and cheese. Cover and cook for 1 minute or until cheese melts. 
 Sprinkle with cilantro.
