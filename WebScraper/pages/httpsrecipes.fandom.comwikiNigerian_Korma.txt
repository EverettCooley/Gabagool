 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 Tbs  vegetable oil 
 1 large  Onion , peeled and minced 
 1 - 3 tsps  curry  paste (to taste) 
 3 Tbs  water 
 1 lb mixed vegetables, chopped 
 ( potatoes ,  carrots ,  Cauliflower ,  Peas , etc) 
 
 1 & 1/2 cups  coconut milk  or soy creamer 
 salt  and  pepper 
 1/4 cup  slivered almonds 
 Directions : 
 In a large frying pan, heat  oil  over medium heat. 
 Add minced  Onion , and stir-cook until golden. 
 Dissolve  curry  paste in  water , and add to pan. 
 Cook 1 minute. 
 Add vegetables, and stir gently. 
 Add  coconut milk  or creamer, and stir gently. 
 Cover, and simmer for 10 – 15 minutes, or until 
 vegetables are tender. 
 Season with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Sprinkle with  almonds , and serve with  rice  or 
 bread
