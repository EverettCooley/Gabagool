Description : 
 These are well adapted for children's luncheons, and are much enjoyed by the convalescent, taken with a glass of  milk .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup fine  oatmeal 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 cup  rolled oats 
 ⅛ teaspoon  soda 
 2 cups  flour 
 ¼ cup  butter  or  lard 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 ½ cup hot  water 
 Directions : 
 First mix first six ingredients. 
 Melt  shortening  in  water  and add to first mixture. 
 Toss on a floured board, pat, and roll as thinly as possible. 
 Shape with a cutter, or with a sharp knife cut in strips. 
 Bake on a buttered sheet in a slow oven.
