 Ingredients : 
 1 pkg  soup beans 
 2  ham shanks 
 1  onion 
 1  carrot 
 2 tablespoons  olive oil 
 Â¼ cup  red wine 
 1 cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Brown chopped  onion  in  olive oil  add chopped  carrot . 
 Add  water  and wine and shanks cook on high pressure for 36 minutes. 
 While the shanks are cooking boil soup beans (mixed beans)for two minutes and let soak until the shanks are done. 
 Take hocks out of cooker and clean. 
 Drain beans and add to pressure cooker in juice left over from hocks add  pork  and enough water to cover beans add 2 tbsp of  olive oil . 
 Cook on high pressure for 12 minutes. 
 Add  salt  and  pepper  to taste.
