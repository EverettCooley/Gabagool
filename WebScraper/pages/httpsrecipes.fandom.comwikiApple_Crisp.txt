Description : 
 When you don't have the time or ingredients to deal with a fickle pie crust, this old-fashioned apple crisp recipe will rescue you from a dessert disaster. It takes mere moments to throw together, and the variations are endless. Add your personal touch of caramel, nuts, chocolate, or your favorite spices to make this basic apple crisp recipe really special. The buttery, sweet topping will make you wonder why you ever want to mess with a soggy crust again!
 
 Ingredients : 
 Apple Filling : 
 5 lbs tart  apples 
 1/2 cup - 3/4 cup of  sugar 
 2 tsp ground  cinnamon 
 1 tsp ground  nutmeg 
 Topping : 
 1 1/2 cups  flour 
 3/4 cup  sugar 
 3/4 light  brown sugar 
 1/2 tsp  salt 
 1 cup  oatmeal 
 1/2 lb cold unsalted  butter 
 Directions : 
 Preheat the oven to 150 °F. 
 Peel, core, and cut the apples into wedges and toss with sugar and spices. Mix in any other additional ingredients like nuts or chocolate at this time. Pout into a buttered dish. 
 To make the topping, combine flour, sugars, salt, oatmeal, and cubes of butter in a bowl. Mix on low speed until the mixture is crumbly. Scatter evenly over the apples. 
 Place the crisp on a shoot pan and bake for 1 hour until the top is brown and the apples bubbly.
