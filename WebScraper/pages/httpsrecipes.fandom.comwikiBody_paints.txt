 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cup Non-irritating baby shampoo 
 Directions : 
 1 Warning: non-edible powdered tempra paints divide the shampoo into two or more portions (depending on number of children who will playing). 
2 Stir in enough tempra paints to make a medium-density color. 
3 Outside: the children can smooth this onto their bodies, lathering to make shapes and designs. 
4 This comes off easily by running through the sprinklers
