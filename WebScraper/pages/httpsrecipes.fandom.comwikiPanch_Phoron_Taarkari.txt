Description : 
 Ethnicity - Assamee, North East Indian 
 Type of meal - Party, Lunch, Dinner 
 Ingredients : 
 150 no: Gym  pumpkin , dims 
 2 no: Large  potatoes , diced 
 1 no: Large brirtjal, diced 
 2 no: Large dry chillies, slit 
 2 tbsps: Cooking  oil 
 ½ tsp:  fenugreek seeds 
 ½ tsp: Aniseeds 
 ½ tsp:  mustard seeds 
 ½ tsp:  cumin  seeds 
 2 no: Bay Levels 
 2 no: Green chillies, chopped 
 1 tsp:  turmeric  power 
 1 tsp:  milk 
 ½ tsp:  Sugar 
 salt  to taste 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Heat the  oil  and add all the spices. 
 Stir-fry the spices, add the cut vegetables and mix well. 
 Add the green chillies,  Sugar ,  salt ,  turmeric  and mix thoroughly. 
 Pour enough  water  and add  milk  to cook the vegetables. 
 Simmer till vegetables are tender and the  water  is absorbed. 
 Serve hot with luchies (pooris) or  rice .
