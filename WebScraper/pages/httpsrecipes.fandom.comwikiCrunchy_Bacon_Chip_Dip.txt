Crunchy  Bacon  Chip Dip
 
 ? c Softened  cream cheese 
 
 2 t  Catsup 
 1 t Prepared  mustard 
 
Dash of  ground ginger 
 ? c  sour cream 
 ? c Chopped Cooked  Bacon 
 
 Potato  Chips or Crackers
 
 
Combine  cream cheese ,  Catsup ,  mustard ,  ginger ,  sour cream  And  Bacon . BlendWell. Serve With Chips or Crackers.
 
 
Yield: About 1 Cup
