Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  oil 
 1 cup  orange marmalade 
 1 cup  water 
 ½ cup  walnuts , chopped 
 peel of 1  orange , grated 
 2 cups  cake flour 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 ½ tsp ground  cloves 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Combine first 5 ingredients in bowl. 
 Sift  flour  and add dry ingredients to  flour . 
 Mix together and pour into greased 9-inch square pan. 
 Bake for 35 to 40 minutes.
