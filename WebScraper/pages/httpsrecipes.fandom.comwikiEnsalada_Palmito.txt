 Optional : 
 1 large vine ripened  tomato , chopped 
 1  red  or  yellow sweet pepper , chopped 
 1 recipe  Mayonnaise  (see below) 
 Directions : 
 Cut the heart of palm into small bite sized pieces and pile on  lettuce  lined plates. 
 Sparingly, squeeze  lime juice  through a strainer to catch the pulp and seeds. 
 Add a tiny dash of  salt  and fresh  pepper . 
 The optional ingredients can be added together or individually. 
 Mayonnaise  can be substituted for the  lime ,  salt , and  pepper  as is common in restaurants.
