Description : 
 Sufficient for forty-six wafers.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cupful  sugar 
 5 tablespoonfuls  Crisco 
 2 cupfuls  flour 
 ¼ cake  chocolate 
 2  eggs 
 ¼ teaspoonful  baking soda 
 ½ teaspoonful  vanilla extract 
 ¼ teaspoonful  salt 
 Directions : 
 First cream Crisco and  sugar  together. 
 Add  chocolate  melted,  eggs  well beaten,  vanilla extract ,  flour ,  salt , and soda. 
 Mix and turn out on to floured baking board. 
 Roll out thin, and cut with small cutter. 
 Lay on Criscoed tin and bake from seven to ten minutes in moderate oven.
