 Ingredients : 
 2 oz  vodka 
 6 oz  orange juice 
 1 tsp  Sugar 
 1  egg yolk 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients with 1/2 cup crushed  ice  in an electric blender. 
 Blend at a low speed for a short length of time. 
 Pour into a collins glass and serve.
