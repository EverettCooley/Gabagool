Ingredients : 
 2 cups  sugar 
 2 tsp  dry mustard 
 6  green mangos , chopped 
 2  onions , finely chopped 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 8 oz fresh  mint , minced 
 ½ cup  golden raisins 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 2 cups  vinegar 
 Directions : 
 Bring  sugar ,  vinegar  and  mustard  to a simmer. 
 Remove from heat. 
 Place  mangos ,  onions ,  garlic  and  mint  in a bowl and pour hot  vinegar  mixture over. 
 Add  raisins  and  salt . 
 Stir well. 
 Pack into hot sterile jars and process in boiling  water  bath for 10 minutes.
