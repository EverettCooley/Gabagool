Description : 
 A  Catfish  recipe.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  canola oil 
 1 pound U.S. farm-raised  catfish  fillets, cut into 1-in pieces 
 ½ teaspoon  garlic powder 
 ½ teaspoon  Italian seasoning 
 1 x 32 oz carton creamy  tomato soup 
 1 x 6 oz bag pre-washed baby  spinach  (about 4 packed cups) 
 1 cup dried  rotini  pasta 
 ¼ cup  Parmesan cheese 
 Directions : 
 Heat the  oil  in a large saucepan or dutch oven over medium-high heat. 
 Add the  catfish ,  garlic powder , and  Italian seasoning . 
 Cook, stirring frequently, for about 3 minutes. 
 Stir in the  tomato  soup and  spinach  (it may seem like a lot of  spinach , but it will quickly cook down). 
 Bring to boil. 
 Add the  rotini  and cook at a low boil, uncovered, stirring occasionally, until  pasta  is done (15 to 20 minutes). 
 Serve in individual bowls and top with  Parmesan cheese .
