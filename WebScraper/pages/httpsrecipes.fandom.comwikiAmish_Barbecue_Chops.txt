      Amish Barbecue Chops     
 Ingredients : 
 8  pork chops 
 1 cup  ketchup 
 1 can  celery soup 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 1 or 2 tbsp  soy sauce 
 Directions : 
 Mix the  ketchup ,  celery soup , chopped  onion  and sauce together. 
 Pour over the  pork  chops in a buttered  casserole  dish. 
 Bake at 275 °F for 2 hours.
