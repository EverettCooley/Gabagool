 Ingredients : 
 3 large ripe  tomatoes , stemmed and quartered

 1 large  Onion , peeled and quartered 
 2 Tbs.  olive oil 
 2 Tbs.  cider vinegar 
 1 tsp. crushed  garlic 
 salt  and freshly ground  black pepper  to taste 
 Chopped scallions for garnish 
 Diced  green peppers  for garnish 
 bread  cubes for garnish 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine  tomatoes ,  cucumber ,  green peppers ,  Onion ,  olive oil ,  vinegar ,  garlic ,  salt  and  pepper  in food processor, and pulse until mixture is smooth. Pour soup into serving bowls, and garnish with scallions,  green peppers  and  bread  cubes.
