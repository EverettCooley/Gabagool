 Description : 
 This recipe taken from www.Portuguese-recipes.com 
 
 Ingredients : 
 8-slices of bread, cut into pieces 
 2- cups of  milk 
 1 and 1/2 cups of  Sugar 
 2- eggs 
 1/2 cup of  Raisins 
 1-teaspoons of  cinnamon 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together. 
 butter  a baking dish and add ingredients to it. 
 Dot the top with pats of  butter . 
 Bake for an hour in a 350 degree oven.
