 Description :

 Ingredients : 
 3 tbsp  butter 
 1 cup finely minced  onion 
 8 oz  cream cheese 
 1 lbs  cottage cheese 
 1 cup  ground almonds  and  walnuts  combined 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 lots of  black pepper 
 ½ tsp  dill 
 2 tsp  prepared mustard 
 2 to 3 tsp fresh  lime  or  lemon juice 
 2 cup grated  cheddar 
 Topping : 
 1 cup  ricotta 
 a few  walnuts  halves, whole  almonds  and whole or chopped  olives 
 radishes ,  cucumber  slices and  parsley  sprigs 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 325 °F. 
 Grease a standard loaf pan with 2 tablespoons of the  butter . 
 Saute  onions  in the remaining  butter  until soft, about 4 minutes. 
 Combine the  cream cheese ,  cottage cheese ,  ground nuts ,  salt ,  pepper ,  dill ,  mustard ,  lime juice , and  cheddar  in a food processor until uniform. 
 Spoon into the prepared pan and bake 1 hour. 
 Allow to cool completely in pan then chill for at least several hours before turning onto a serving platter. 
 To decorate, spread ricotta cheese all over as if frosting a cake. 
 Place garnish over cake as desired. 
 Serve with dark bread or crackers. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving: 481 Cal; 24g pro, 9g carb, 40g fat(73%), 2g fiber, 97 mg chol, 731 mg sodium
