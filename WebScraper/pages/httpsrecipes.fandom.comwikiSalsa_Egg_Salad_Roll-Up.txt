Ingredients : 
 1  egg 
 salsa 
 tortilla 
 Directions : 
 Hard boil 1  egg . 
 Then finely chop the hard-boiled  egg  and mix with 2 tablespoons salsa. 
 Spread onto medium-sized  flour  tortilla and roll up jellyroll style. 
 Wrap tightly with plastic wrap and refrigerate.
