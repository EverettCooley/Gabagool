 Source:  Jenny Craig Diabetes Cookbook  1997 
 Yield 12 servings, serving size 1 ounce 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 1 tablespoon  soy sauce 
 â…› teaspoon ground  ginger 
 1 clove  garlic , crushed 
 1 (16 ounce) package frozen un-breaded  fish  fillets, thawed 
 2 tablespoons minced  green onions 
 Directions : 
 Combine first four ingredients in bottom of broiling pan. 
 Place fillets in pan, and turn to coat in mixture. 
 Cover and marinate in refrigerator for at least 1 hour. 
 Broil fillets in marinade 8 to 10 minutes or until fish flakes easily when tested with fork. 
 Sprinkle with  green onions  before serving. 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Exchanges: 1 Lean Meat
