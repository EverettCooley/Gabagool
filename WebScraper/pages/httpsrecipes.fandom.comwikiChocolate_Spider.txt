 Ingredients : 
 1Â½ cups  semisweet chocolate  morsels 
 5 oz can  chow mein noodles 
 1 cup salted  peanuts 
 large pkg M&M's or other similar round colored candy 
 Directions : 
 Place  chocolate morsels  in top of double boiler; bring  water  to a boil. 
 Reduce heat to low. 
 Cook until  chocolate  melts (this can be done in the microwave on lowest possible setting, a few seconds at a time, checking frequently). 
 Add noodles and  peanuts , stirring well. 
 Drop  chocolate  mix by teaspoonfuls onto greased baking sheets. 
 Press in two candies for spider eyes. 
 Refrigerate 8 hours or overnight. 
 Keep chilled until ready to serve.
