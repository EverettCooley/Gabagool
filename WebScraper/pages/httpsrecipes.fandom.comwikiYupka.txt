Description : 
 Pancakes
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 kg or 7½ cups  flour 
 300 g (11 oz)  mutton  or  beef 
 2  onions 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 salt ,  pepper 
 250 g (9 oz)  vegetable oil  for frying 
 Directions : 
 Mince the meat in meat-grinder and fry in  oil . 
 Make stiff dough out of  flour ,  salt , and  water , roll into tortilla-shaped bread but even thinner. 
 Rub frying pan with  butter  and fry dough on both sides. 
 Remove and fry second bread on one side. 
 Flip over, spread out a thin layer of minced meat on the the side that has been fried. 
 Layer with first bread and spread out another layer of filling and top with uncooked dough. 
 Turn the entire stack over, repeat with filling and layers of dough. 
 Make stack about 10 – 12 high. 
 Cook on very low heat. 
 To serve fold each layer with meat filling like an envelope.
