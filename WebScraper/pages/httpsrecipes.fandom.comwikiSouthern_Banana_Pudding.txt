 Description : 
 Purchased from the Miller Estate in Wills Point, Texas in 1992. Dated 1908. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4  eggs 
 2 large cans  evaporated milk 
 1 cup  sugar 
 8  bananas 
 1 box  vanilla  wafers 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Heat  milk  (scalding) in medium saucepan. 
 Using a large uncrackable bowl, put 1/2 box of wafers and slice 1/2 of  bananas . 
 Beat  eggs  until frothy. Pour some hot  milk  into  eggs . 
 Pour back into pan, stirring constantly until mixture begins to thicken. Remove from heat. 
 Pour 1/2 mixture over  bananas , covering all. Add remaining wafers and slice remaining  bananas . 
 Pour remaining  milk  mixture over all  bananas . Cool and cover with plastic wrap. Refrigerate. 
 Serve plain or top with  Cool Whip .
