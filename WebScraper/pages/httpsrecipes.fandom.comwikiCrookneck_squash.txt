       Crookneck squash     
 About crookneck squash : 
 Crookneck squash are summer squash with yellow skin and pale flesh with a slightly sweet flavor. They have a bulbous flower end and a slender, curving stem end. Choose squash that are heavy for their size and do not have cracks or bruises. Store in the refrigerator for up to 4 days.
