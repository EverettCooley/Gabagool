Description : 
 Makes 9 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 2½ cups  rice flour 
 1 teaspoon  ginger 
 ½ teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 ½ teaspoon  nutmeg 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  baking soda 
 ½ teaspoon  xanthan gum 
 ¾ cup  molasses 
 ½ cup unsweetened  applesauce 
 ¼ cup  honey 
 ¼ cup  water 
 1  egg 
 Directions : 
 Sift  rice flour ,  ginger ,  cinnamon ,  nutmeg ,  salt ,  baking soda  and  xanthan gum  into large bowl. 
 Combine  molasses ,  applesauce , honey,  water  and  oil  in small saucepan; bring to a boil. 
 Stir boiling  molasses  mixture into  flour  mixture, mixing well, let stand 5 minutes. 
 Stir in  egg . 
 Pour into lightly greased 9-inch square baking pan. 
 Smooth top with spatula or back of wet spoon. 
 Bake at 350°F for 25 minutes or until wooden pick inserted near center comes out clean. 
 Cool 20 minutes; serve warm or completely cooled.
