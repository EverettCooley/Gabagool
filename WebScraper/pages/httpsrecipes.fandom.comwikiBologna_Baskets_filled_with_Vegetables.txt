Description : 
 In Romanian: Cupe de parizer umplute cu legume
 
 Ingredients : 
 7 oz / 200 g  bologna  (6 slices) 
 ½ tablespoon  lard 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 ½ lb / 250 g  peas 
 1  hard-boiled egg 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Leave the casings on the bologna slices and fry them in  lard  just on one side. 
 The edges will turn up forming a basket. 
 Each such basket is filled with one tablespoon of boiled and buttered  peas . 
 Place one slice of  egg  on top. 
 The baskets can also be filled with sliced boiled  carrots  or very thin boiled  potato  slices.
