Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz Appleton Special  Jamaica rum 
 ¼ oz blue  curacao 
 2 oz  cream of coconut 
 2 oz  pineapple juice 
 8 oz crushed  ice 
 Directions : 
 Mix well in blender until smooth. 
 Serve in specialty glass. 
 Garnish with  pineapple  fruit flag.
