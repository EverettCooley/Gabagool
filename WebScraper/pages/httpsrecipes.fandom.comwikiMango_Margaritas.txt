Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Wadley Estate in Allen, Texas in 1990.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 ounces  tequila 
 1 ounce  orange flavored liqueur 
 1 ounce puréed ripe  mango 
 2 tablespoons quick dissolving  sugar 
 1 tablespoon  lime juice 
 1 cup ice cubes 
 Directions : 
 In a blender container combine tequila, liqueur, mango purée, sugar and lime juice. 
 Add the ice then cover securely and blend until smooth. 
 Serve in chilled glasses rimed with salt.
