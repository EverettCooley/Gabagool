Ingredients : 
 1-2 lb. beef  London broil  or  flank steak 
 OR 
 
 3 lbs.  chicken  legs & thighs 
 Marinade 
 1/3 c. Kikkoman natural brew  soy sauce 
 1/4 c.  sherry  (cooking sherry is fine) or  wine 
 1 tbsp. dried, crushed  red pepper flakes 
 1 tsp.  Italian seasoning  (marjoram, thyme, rosemary, savory, sage, oregano & basil) 
 1/8 tsp. each  onion  and   garlic powder 
 Peanut-Pepper Sauce 
 1/2 c.  milk 
 1/2 c.  chicken broth 
 4 tbsp.  peanut butter  (natural if available) 
 1 tsp. Kikkoman soy sauce 
 1-2 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine all marinade ingredients 
 Marinate meat, turning occasionally for 1-5 hours. 
 Prepare gas or charcoal grill to medium-high heat. 
 Cook beef on open grill or chicken on foil, until done. Use the marinade as a basting liquid. 
 Combine peanut pepper sauce ingredients in a saucepan over medium-low heat. 
 Cook, stirring frequently, until thickened. 
 Slice beef into thin strips 
 Serve meat with peanut sauce. 
 Notes : 
 Variation: Cut a 1-2 pound flank steak into thin strips. Marinate 1-5 hours. Meanwhile soak in warm water 20-30 bamboo skewers. Thread meat onto skewers (zig-zag) and grill.
