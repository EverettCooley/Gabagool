Barbecue Hash
 From Larry K Hooks 
 This is a traditional southern style barbecue hash.
 
 INGREDIENTS : 
 4 pounds boston butt 
 1 pound of shredded squirrel 
 2 pounds beef 
 1 1/2 pounds pork liver 
 2 large onions 
 1 bottle ketchup 
 1 stick of butter or margarine 
 2 tablespoons hot pepper sauce 
 3 tablespoons vinegar 
 salt & pepper to taste 
 PREPARATION : 
 Boil meat until it falls apart easily. 
 Grind meat finely. 
 Mix together all ingredients and serve over rice.
