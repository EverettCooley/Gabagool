 Description : 
 Serves 4.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 barbecued  chicken 
 ½ cup  coconut , shredded 
 1 (440g) can unsweetened  pineapple  slices, drained 
 4  spring onions , chopped 
 Curried mayonnaise : 
 1 tsp  curry powder 
 ¼ tsp  five spice powder 
 1 clove  garlic , crushed 
 1 tsp grated fresh  ginger 
 ¾ cup  mayonnaise 
 ¼ cup  coconut milk 
 Directions : 
 Curried mayonnaise : 
 Combine all ingredients thoroughly. 
 Chicken : 
 Toast  coconut  on oven tray in moderate oven for about 5 minutes. 
 Cut  chicken  and  pineapple  into chunks; place in bowl, mix in  coconut , shallots and  mayonnaise . 
 Refrigerate 1 hour before serving.
