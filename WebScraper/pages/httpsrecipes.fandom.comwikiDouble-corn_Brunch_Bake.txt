Description : 
 An easy to make side dish or baked brunch dish.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ tsp  vegetable oil 
 ½ cup chopped  onions 
 17 oz whole kernel  corn 
 ¾ cup  cornmeal 
 2  eggs 
 4  egg whites 
 ½ cup  non-fat milk 
 ¼ cup  flour 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  baking powder 
 bottled  salsa ,  taco sauce ,  chili sauce  or  tomato sauce 
 Directions : 
 In medium saucepan over medium heat, cook  onion  in  oil , stirring occasionally, until tender, 4 to 5 minutes. 
 Meanwhile, drain  corn  reserving liquid. 
 Add  water  to liquid to make 1 cup. 
 Add liquid mixture and  cornmeal  to  onions . 
 Cook and stir until mixture boils. 
 Remove from heat. 
 Thoroughly blend in  milk ,  eggs , and  corn . 
 In small bowl, stir together  flour ,  baking powder  and  salt . 
 Stir into  cornmeal  mixture. 
 Pour into greased 9 x 9 x 2-inch baking pan. 
 Bake in preheated 350°F. 
 Oven until knife inserted near center comes out clean, 25 to 30 minutes. 
 Top with salsa to taste. 
 To serve, cut into squares. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving): 212 calories | total fat 4 g (15% of calories)
