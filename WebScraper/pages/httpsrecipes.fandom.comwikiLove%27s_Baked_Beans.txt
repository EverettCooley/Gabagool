Ingredients : 
 5 Lb. Canned  Pork  And beans 
 2  bell peppers , Chopped 
 1 Lb.  brown sugar 
 1 Bottle  Catsup  (14 Oz) 
 liquid smoke  to Taste (about 1 T.) 
 8 Slices  Bacon 
 Directions : 
 Mix All Ingredients Except  Bacon  in Large Casserole or Baking Dish. 
 Top With  Bacon  Strips; 
 Bake at 325 For 2 1/4 Hours. 
 Serves 20
