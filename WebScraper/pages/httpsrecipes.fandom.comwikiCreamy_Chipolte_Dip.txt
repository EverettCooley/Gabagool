Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Original recipe 
 Makes about 1 cup 
 Ingredients : 
 8 ounces  Neufchatel  cheese, softened 
 ¼ cup  chipotle peppers  in  adobo sauce , roughly chopped (note: for a spicier dip, use more peppers; for a less spicy dip, use more sauce.) 
 ¼ cup  cilantro , chopped 
 1 thick  green onion  or two thin ones, chopped 
 Directions : 
 In a food processor, combine the cheese with the peppers and adobo sauce until thoroughly combined. 
 This will probably require scraping down the food processor bowl sides a few times. 
 Pulse in the cilantro and green onion until combined. 
 Serve with tortilla chips. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving (2 oz-wt.): 
 
 110 calories (80 from fat) | 9g total fat | 6g saturated fat | 0g dietary fiber | 4g protein | 3g carbohydrate | 3mg cholesterol | 160mg sodium
