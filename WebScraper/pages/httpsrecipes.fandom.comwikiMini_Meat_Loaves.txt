Mini Meat Loaves
 Prep: 10 minutes, Cook: 30 minutes.
 A.. 1-1/2 Lbs. Lean Ground  Beef 
 B.. 2  eggs , Beaten
 C.. 2 Small  onions , Finely Chopped
 D.. 2 Cups Fresh Breadcrumbs
 E.. 2 Tbs. Grated Parmesan or  Romano   Cheese 
 F.. 2 Tbs. Fresh  parsley , Finely Chopped
 G..  salt  And  pepper , to Taste
 Preheat Oven or Toaster Oven to 350?f. Combine All Ingredients in a Bowl, Mixing Well. Spoon Equally Into a 6-Cup Muffin Pan. Bake For 25-30 Minutes, or Until Thoroughly Cooked.
 
This Recipe Serves 6 People. Due to The Nature of This Recipe, it Adjusts The Number of Servings in Multiples of 3 Only. The Instructions Describe How to Prepare The Ingredients For One Pan. There Are Enough Ingredients to Prepare 2 Pans.
