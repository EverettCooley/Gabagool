Description : 
 This recipe taken from www.Portuguese-recipes.com 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large can of crushed  pineapple  ( drained) 
 1 large can of  mandarin oranges  ( drained) 
 1 - Pkg. of  orange  Jello 
 1- 41/2 oz.  Cool Whip 
 1- 8 oz.  sour cream 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix  pineapple ,  mandarin oranges  and Jello. Refrigerate overnight. 
 Before serving mix in  Cool Whip  and  sour cream .
