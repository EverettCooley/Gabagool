 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cl Malibu 
 3 cl Blue  curacao 
 3 cl  white rum 
 1-2 El Cocos powders 
 15 cl  pineapple juice 
 Directions : 
 Put 1-2 cut soup spoons of fine Cocos powder, all liqueurs which  pineapple juice  and a couple of  ice  cubes give into the blender, foamy stir and pour in a high glass. 
 Decorating with a peace of  pineapple  slice and one of a red cocktail  cherry .
