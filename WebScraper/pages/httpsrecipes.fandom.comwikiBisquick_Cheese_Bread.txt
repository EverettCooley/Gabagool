Description : 
 My mother used to bake this and now I bake it for my family.
 
 Cook Time : 60 minutes baking time 
 Serves : 12 to 16 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large  egg 
 1½ cups  milk 
 3¾ cups  Bisquick  baking mix 
 1 cup shredded  Cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 Spray a 9 x 5-inch loaf pan with nonstick cooking spray. 
 Beat egg on low speed of mixer. 
 Add remaining ingredients and beat ½ minute medium speed until blended. 
 Pour batter into prepared loaf pan. 
 Bake about 1 hour or until golden brown. 
 Cool on wire baking rack. 
 Serve slightly warm with lots of fresh, sweet butter.
