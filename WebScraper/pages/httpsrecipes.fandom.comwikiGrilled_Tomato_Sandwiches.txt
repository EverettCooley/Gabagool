Description 
 Fruit & Vegetable Recipes II  by the South Carolina Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Cook Time : 
 Serves : 4 
 Ingredients 
 8 slices thick  bread 
 ¼ cup  butter 
 8 slices  Mozzarella  cheese] 
 3 medium  tomatoes , thinly sliced 
 1 tablespoon  basil 
 12 cooked  bacon  slices 
 Directions 
 Spread one side of each of the slices of bread with butter; turn buttered side of four slices over and top with a slice of cheese. 
 Layer tomato slices evenly over the cheese. Sprinkle marjoram over tomato slices. Top with bacon and remaining cheese slices. 
 Place remaining bread slices, buttered side up on top of cheese slices. 
 Place a large skillet over medium heat until hot. Cook sandwiches until golden, turning once.
