Ingredients : 
 1 pkg jiffy  corn  muffin mix 
 1 small jar  pimentos 
 1 large  egg  (well beaten) 
 1 med  Onion  (chopped) 
 1 small  bell pepper  (chopped) 
 1 can creamed  corn  (15 oz) 
 1 cartn  sour cream  (8 oz) 
 1/4 cup  vegetable oil 
 1 pkg  Cheddar   Cheese  (8 oz) 
 Directions : 
 Mix all the ingredients except cheddar cheese together and put half of the mixture in a 1 quart size, greased, baking dish. 
 Top with half of the grated  Cheddar   Cheese  and pour the remaining mixture in. 
 Top with other half of grated  Cheddar   Cheese  and bake at 375 degrees for about half an hour or until done (when knife inserted in middle comes out clean)
