Description 
 Serves : 1
 
 Ingredients 
 1  apple 
 applesauce 
 Directions 
 Cut the top of the apple off as if you were getting ready to clean out a pumpkin. 
 Scoop out the apple leaving nothing inside. 
 Fill the apple with applesauce (alternatives include natural yoghurt, apple juice or even syrup). 
 Put the top back on and let the kids open it up to see what is inside.
