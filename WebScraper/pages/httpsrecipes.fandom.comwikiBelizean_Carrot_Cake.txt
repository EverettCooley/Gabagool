Ingredients : 
 1 cup  sugar  225 g 
 ½ cup  vegetable oil 
 margarine  112 g 
 2  eggs 
 2 cups  flour  450 g 
 2 tbsp  baking powder  10 ml 
 ½ tsp  baking soda  2 ml 
 1 tsp  cinnamon  5 ml 
 1 tsp grated  orange peel  5 ml 
 2 cups grated  carrots  450 g 
 1 cup  raisins  (optional) 225 g 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F /180°C. 
 Prepare a small cake tin (8" x 2"). 
 Grease and  flour  tin. 
 cream  fat and  sugar  until well mixed. 
 Beat  eggs . 
 Add to fat and  sugar  mixture. 
 Mix well. 
 Add dry ingredients to  egg  mixture (fold in). 
 Add grated  orange peel  and grated  carrots . 
 Put batter in greased pan. 
 Bake at 350°F /180°C for 35 – 40 minutes.
