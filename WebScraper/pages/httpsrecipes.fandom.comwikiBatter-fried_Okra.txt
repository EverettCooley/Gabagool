Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Servings: 8 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup  cornstarch 
 ¼ cup  flour 
 1 teaspoon  baking powder 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  pepper 
 ½ cup  water 
 1  egg , slightly beaten 
 1 quart (about)  Mazola™ oil  (or brand of your choice) 
 4 cups fresh  okra , trimmed 
 Directions : 
 In medium bowl combine corn starch, flour, baking powder, salt and pepper. 
 Sir in water and egg until smooth. 
 Pour oil into large skillet, filling no more than ⅓ full. 
 Heat oil over medium heat to 375°F. 
 Dip okra into batter (stir batter occasionally); fry a few pieces at a time, turning once, 2 to 3 minutes or until golden brown and crisp. 
 Drain on paper towels.
