Ingredients : 
 8  apples 
 2 cupful  30% cream  or  sour cream 
 1 cupful  castor sugar 
 2 tbsp crumbled  nuts 
 2 g  citric acid 
 Directions : 
 Remove the skin and scoop the cores out of the  apples , boil them in acidified and sugared  water  until done. 
 Beat the  cream  or  sour cream  together with  castor sugar , add half of the crumbled  nuts . 
 Fill the hollowed  apples  with the prepared mixture, then dust them with the remaining  nuts  and decorate with slices of  apples .
