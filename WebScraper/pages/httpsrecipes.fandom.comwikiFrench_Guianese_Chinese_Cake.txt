Ingredients : 
 400 g  black eye beans 
 3 cups  brown sugar 
 red  food coloring 
 3 tbsp  vanilla 
 2  eggs 
 pastry 
 Directions : 
 Soak black eye beans overnight. 
 Pressure beans with water for 10 minutes. 
 Drain off most of the water and put in a blender or food processor. 
 Put in a pot with  brown sugar  and cook until it becomes thick. 
 Add a few drops of  food coloring  to obtain a dark color. 
 Add  vanilla  and let it cool. 
 Mean while, make the pastry. 
 Roll flat and cut into 5 inches circles. 
 Use two circles. 
 Then put a tablespoon of bean filling in your circles and bring the sides up together and twist to close, making a round ball. 
 Continue until all are done. 
 Put in pan, brush with beaten  eggs . 
 Then using your finger put a drop of  food coloring  in the middle of the Dhinese cake. 
 Bake until brown at 400Â°F.
