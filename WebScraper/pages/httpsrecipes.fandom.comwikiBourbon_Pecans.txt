Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Love Estate in Sachse, Texas in 1984.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  white sugar 
 ¼ teaspoon  nutmeg 
 ¼ teaspoon  cinnamon 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅓ cup  bourbon 
 ⅓ cup water 
 2 cups  pecan  halves 
 Directions : 
 Cook first six ingredients together until mixture reaches soft ball stage. 
 Stir in pecan halves and spread out on wax paper to cool.
