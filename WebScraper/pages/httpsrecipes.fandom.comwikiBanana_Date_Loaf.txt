 Ingredients : 
 3/4 cup  Sugar 
 1/2 cup  vegetable shortening 
 1 cup mashed  banana 
 1 tbsp.  lemon juice 
 1 3/4 cups all purpose  flour 
 1 tsp.  baking soda 
 1/2 tsp.  salt 
 1 cup cut up  dates 
 1 cup chopped  nuts 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 F. 
 cream   Sugar  and  shortening  thoroughly. 
 Add  banana  and  lemon juice  and mix well. 
 Sift  flour , soda and  salt . 
 Add dry ingredients,  dates  and  nuts  to creamed mixture, stirring just until all  flour  is moistened. Spread in a well-greased 9 x 5 x 3Â inch.loaf pan. 
 Bake for 60 minutes.
