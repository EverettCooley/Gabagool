Ingredients : 
 1 measure  gin 
 ½ measure  dry vermouth 
 3 dashes blue  curaçao  liqueur 
 3 dashes  grenadine  syrup 
 fill with  orange juice 
 ice  cubes 
 Directions : 
 Use a glass and fill it with  ice  cubes. 
 Pour over some  gin  and then  dry vermouth . 
 Fill the glass with  orange juice . 
 Serve with a straw.
