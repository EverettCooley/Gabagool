 About melon seeds : 
 Melon seeds can be obtained from most  melons  by separating them from the melon flesh, washing, then drying them. They can then be ground, or roasted, or roasted and then ground.
 Some popular melon seeds include  pumpkin seeds  and  watermelon seeds .
