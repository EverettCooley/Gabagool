 fine herbs 
 About Fines herbes : 
 Wikipedia Article  About Fines Herbes on Wikipedia 
 Fines herbes is a culinary staple in French cuisine. It is a mixture of herbs consisting of  chervil ,  tarragon , and  chives . Sometimes  parsley  is included as well. Because they quickly lose their flavor, fines herbs should be added to a cooked mixture shortly before serving.
