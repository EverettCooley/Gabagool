Description : 
 Makes about sixteen delicious, filling, vegan, nutritious ounces.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 frozen  banana  (freezing it makes the drink super cold without diluting it with  ice ) 
 ½ fresh  papaya 
 10 to 12  raspberries  (fresh or frozen) 
 ½ cup  water  or  fruit juice 
 1 tbsp toasted  wheat germ  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Puree in blender 30 – 45 seconds.
