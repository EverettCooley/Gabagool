Ingredients : 
 Filling 
 3 lbs. ground  beef 
 2 grated  onions 
 1 stick  celery , diced 
 1/2  green pepper , diced 
 Pinch of  salt 
 Pinch of  black pepper 
 1 tsp.  thyme 
 1  hot pepper , diced 
 Pastry 
 4 1/2 c.  flour 
 1/2 c.  butter 
 1/2 c.  shortening 
 1/2-1/4 c.  water 
 1 pinch salt 
 2  eggs 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Brown ground beef, 
 Add rest of the filling ingredients. 
 Place salt, flour, butter and shortening in a bowl. 
 Blend ingredients finely. 
 Add water to dough. 
 Use a rolling pin to flatten the dough, and cut out circles with a glass. 
 Place small amount of meat mixture on one side of dough. 
 Cover with other side and seal. 
 Beat two eggs and brush on patties. 
 Bake at 350 degrees.
