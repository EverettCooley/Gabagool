Ingredients : 
 1/2 	head  cabbage 
 1 	whole white  Onion  or 2 whole  yellow onions 
 3 	cloves  garlic 
 16 	ounces  tomato  sauce, more if you wish 
 Directions : 
 Cut up your  cabbage  into small pieces. 
 dice the  onions  (preferably in long thin pieces). 
 grade your  garlic  on a  Cheese  grader into the crockpot. 
 fill crockpot 3/4 of the way with  water . 
 cook on high for 3â€“4 hours. 
 now add your  tomato  sauce (and more  water , if needed). 
 add any seasonings you like for flavor. 
 i use  pepper ,  salt ,  garlic salt , and  Italian seasoning  cook on low for 2 more hours or until you have reached your desired consistency. 
 eat your heart out.
