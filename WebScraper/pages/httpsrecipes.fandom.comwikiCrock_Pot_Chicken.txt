Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves 4. 
 Ingredients : 
 1  chicken ; or cut up  [1] 
 2  carrots ; sliced thinly 
 2 medium  onions ; sliced thinly 
 2  celery  stalks; chopped 
 1½ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  black pepper 
 16 oz  chicken broth ; or use  bouillon  in 2 cups  water 
 1 tsp  basil ; crushed 
 2½ tbsp  Italian seasonings  mixture 
 Directions : 
 In a slow cooker, put in half of the  chicken broth . 
 Place half of the carrots,  celery  and  onions  in the bottom. 
 Place the  chicken  (back side down)in the slow cooker. 
 Add remaining broth, vegetables,  salt  and  pepper , basil and Italian seasonings (if used). 
 Cover. 
 Cook on low heat for 7 10 hours. 
 Notes : 
 
 ↑   Make sure it will fit in your crock pot.
