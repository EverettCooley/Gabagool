Description : 
 Rice ,  papaya , and  coconut milk  might seem like an odd combination. The mixture is, however, a delicious one. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  rice 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 2 cups  coconut milk 
 1  papaya , peeled, seeded and cut into small pieces (about 1 cup diced) 
 Directions : 
 Place the  rice , ¼ cup  water ,  salt ,  cinnamon , and  coconut milk  in a medium-size pot and bring to a boil. 
 Cover, reduce heat, and cook for 20 minutes. 
 Fluff the  rice  with a fork and let stand for 10 minutes. 
 Mash half the  papaya  and mix it and the  papaya  cubes with the  rice . 
 Serve hot.
