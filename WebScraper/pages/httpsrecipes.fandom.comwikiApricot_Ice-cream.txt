Description : 
 In Romanian: Inghetata de caise
 
 Ingredients : 
 8  yolks 
 7 oz / 210 g  sugar 
 1 qt / 1 l  milk 
 1 cup  whipped cream 
 Â½ lb / 250 g  apricots 
 Directions : 
 Beat the  sugar  with  yolks  until creamy, add the  milk  and then cooled off and simmer, stirring continuously, until thickened (it should not come to a boil). 
 Let the mixture cool off and set to freeze. 
 When almost frozen, add 1 cup  whipped cream  and ripe, clean, crushed  apricots . 
 Mix well and freeze.
