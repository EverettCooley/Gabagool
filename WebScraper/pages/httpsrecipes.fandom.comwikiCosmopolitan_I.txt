      cosmopolitan     
 Ingredients : 
 1.5 Shots Stolichnaya Red  vodka 
 0.75 Shot de Kuyper  triple sec 
 2.5 Shots  cranberry juice 
 0.5 Shot  lime juice 
 1 Teaspoon  caster sugar 
 Garnish : 
 Zest a piece of  orange peel  over the surface of the drink. 
 glass: martini 
 Directions : 
 Shake and strain into a chilled martini glass.
