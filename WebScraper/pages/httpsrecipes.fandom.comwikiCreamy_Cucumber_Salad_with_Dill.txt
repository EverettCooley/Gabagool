Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 medium  cucumbers , sliced 
 1 medium  sweet onion , thinly sliced 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 ½ cup  white wine vinegar 
 5 tbsp fresh  dill , chopped 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 dash  pepper 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl make alternating layers of cucumbers and sliced onion, sprinkling with chopped dill. 
 Combine sour cream, sugar, white wine vinegar, salt and pepper. 
 Pour over cucumbers. 
 Let chill at least 4 hours.
