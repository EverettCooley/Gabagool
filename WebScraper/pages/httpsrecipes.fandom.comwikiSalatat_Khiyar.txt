 Ingredients : 
 1 large  cucumber , peeled, halved lengthwise, seeded, thinly sliced 
 1/2 green  bell pepper , cored, seeded and cut in half lengthwise 
 1/3 cup pitted and coarsely chopped imported  green olives 
 4 large fresh  mint  leaves, finely chopped 
 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh coriander ( cilantro ) leaves 
 1/2 teaspoon  paprika 
 1/4 cup extra virgin olive  oil 
 3 1/2 teaspoons  white wine vinegar 
 salt  and freshly ground black  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 1. Toss the  cucumber  in a salad bowl with the green  pepper , , and  mint .
 2. Add the coriander,  paprika , olive  oil , and  vinegar , season with  salt  and  pepper , toss again, and serve.
