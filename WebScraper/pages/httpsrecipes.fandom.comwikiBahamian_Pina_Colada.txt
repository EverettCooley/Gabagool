 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 1 ounce  coconut rum 
 1 ounce  white rum 
 3 ounces  pineapple juice 
 crushed  ice 
 Garnish 
 pineapple  wedge 
 cherry 
 Directions 
 In a blender, combine ingredients and blend. 
 Garnish with a pineapple wedge and a cherry. 
 Variation 
 You can also buy a Pina Colada mix and add the coconut rum to it.
