Applesauce Cake Roll
 SERVINGS:10
CATEGORY:Dessert
METHOD:Baked
PREP:25 min.
COOK: 10 min.
TOTAL: 35 min
 This old-fashioned roll is pretty for parties and great for
gifts, but it's also a real treat for a coffee break.
 INGREDIENTS
.. 4 eggs
.. 1 cup all-purpose flour
.. 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
.. 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
.. 3/4 cup sugar
.. 1/2 cup applesauce.
 FILLING:
.. 1 package (8 ounces) cream cheese, softened
.. 3 tablespoons milk
.. 1/4 cup confectioners' sugar
.. 1/2 cup raisins
.. Additional confectioners' sugar, optional
 DIRECTIONS
 In a mixing bowl, beat eggs.
Combine flour, cinnamon and cloves.
Add to eggs along with sugar; mix well.
Add applesauce.
Line a 15-in. x 10-in. x 1-in. pan with waxed paper, and grease
and flour the paper.
Pour batter into pan.
Bake at 400� for 15 minutes.
Turn out onto a linen towel dusted with confectioners' sugar.
Peel off paper and roll cake up in towel.
Let cool.
Meanwhile, for filling, beat cream cheese, milk and sugar in a
small mixing bowl.
Stir in raisins.
Unroll cake; spread filling to within 1 in. of edges.
Roll up again.
Dust with confectioners' sugar if desired.
Refrigerate until serving time.
Cut into 1-in. slices.
Yield: 10 servings.
