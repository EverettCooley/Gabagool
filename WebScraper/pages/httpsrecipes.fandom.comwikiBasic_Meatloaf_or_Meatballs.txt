Description 
 Here is a reliable, mildly flavored recipe which can be adapted to your needs.
 Basic Meatloaf or Meatballs  from Recipes for Better Nutrition During Cancer Treatment by the National Cancer Institute, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 Cook Time : 30 minutes—simmered meatballs, 45 minutes—simmered meatloaf, 45 minutes baked meatballs, 1 hour 15 minutes baked meatloaf 
 Serves : 4
 
 Ingredients 
 2 tbsp dry  bread  or  cracker  crumbs 
 1 tbsp  water 
 ½ lb  ground beef  or  veal 
 1  egg 
 ¼ tsp. minced  onion 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 1 tbsp  oil  or  margarine 
 2 slices  onion 
 Directions 
 Combine crumbs and water in small mixing bowl. 
 Add meat, minced onion, egg, and seasonings. 
 Mix until well blended. 
 Form into patties, 1-inch meatballs, or a loaf. 
 Brown in oil or margarine in skillet, turn to brown both sides. 
 Add sliced onion, lower heat, cover and simmer for at least 15 minutes, 30 minutes for meatloaf. 
 You also can bake at 350 °F. 
 For meatballs, bake 30 minutes, turning after 15 minutes. 
 For meatloaf, bake 1 hour. 
 Can be frozen raw or cooked.
