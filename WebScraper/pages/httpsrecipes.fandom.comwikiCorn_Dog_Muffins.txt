corn  Dog Muffins
 
 2 Pkgs. (8 1/2 Oz. Each)  corn  Bread/Muffin Mix 
 2 Tbsp.  brown sugar 
 2  eggs 
 1 C.  milk 
 11 Oz. Can Whole Kernel  corn , Drained 
 5 Hot Dogs, Chopped 
 
 in a Bowl, Combine  corn   bread  Mix And  brown sugar . Combine  eggs  And  milk ; Stir Into Dry Ingredients Until Moistened. Stir in  corn  And Hot Dogs (batter Will be Thin).
 
Fill Greased or Paper-Lined Muffin Cups Three-Fourths Full. Bake at 400 Degrees For 14 to 18 Minutes or Until Golden Brown. Serve Immediately or Refrigerate.
 
Makes 1 1/2 Dozen
