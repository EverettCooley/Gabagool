       Basil Cherry Tomatoes     
 Serve these pretty little, zesty  tomatoes  on a leaf of  lettuce  for a nice presentation. The  basil  gives them a lovely taste.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 pints  cherry tomatoes 
 ½ cup fresh  basil , chopped 
 1½ teaspoons  olive oil 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅛ teaspoon  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Cut  tomatoes  in half; place in bowl. 
 Add  basil ,  oil ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Mix well, cover and chill.
