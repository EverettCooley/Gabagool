Ingredients : 
 3 cups  milk 
 cornmeal 
 salt 
 ½ lb / 250 g  sheep's cheese  (or other) 
 7 oz / 200 g  butter 
 3  eggs , beaten 
 Directions : 
 Prepare a quick  Mamaliga , replacing the  water  with  milk . 
 Add 4 oz / 100 g crumbled  sheep's cheese , 4 oz / 100 g  butter  and  salt  if needed. 
 Let cool a little, and then mix with three beaten  eggs . 
 Grease a pan or baking sheet with some  butter , place half of the polenta in, spread the rest of the  cheese  and small pieces of  butter  on top, and then cover with the other half of polenta. 
 Put some more  butter  on top of that and bake for 20 – 30 minutes. 
 Serve hot with pickled vegetables.
