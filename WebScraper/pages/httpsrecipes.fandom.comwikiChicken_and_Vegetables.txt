 Description🍥 : 
 Ingredients🌽 : 
 butter  for  browning 
 1  Chicken  cut into serving pieces 
 1  coconut   cream 
 enough vegetables to serve 6 people: tapioca,  yam ,  banana ,  pumpkin , kauhau,  onions 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Brown the  Chicken  in a little  butter  in a heavy frying pan. 
 Add peeled, washed and chopped vegetables to frying pan. 
 Cover with  coconut cream . 
 Add a little  salt , chopped  onions  and any seasoning to taste. 
 Cover and cook slowly until vegetables are tender, about half an hour.
