Ingredients : 
 Pizza dough 
 olive oil 
 dried  oregano 
 Directions : 
 Roll out pizza dough to form a rectangle and spread out on a baking sheet. 
 Spread  olive oil  and sprinkle dried  oregano  evenly all over the dough 
 bake till dough is cooked through and turns slightly brown on top.
