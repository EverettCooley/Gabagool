 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2  avocados 
 2 teaspoons of  vinegar 
 2 teaspoons of  olive oil 
 1/2 teaspoon of  salt 
 Directions : 
 Peel and cut the  avocados  into small cubes. 
 Mix the  salt ,  vinegar  and  oil  in a bottle and shake. 
 Pour over the  avocados  and serve.
