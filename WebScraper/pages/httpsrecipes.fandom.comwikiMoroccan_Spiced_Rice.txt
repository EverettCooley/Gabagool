Description : 
 Serve with grilled flank  steak . Makes 4 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 2 teaspoons grated  lemon peel 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 ¼ teaspoon  curry powder 
 ¼ teaspoon  turmeric 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 ¾ cup chopped  dates  or  raisins 
 ½ cup  slivered almonds , toasted 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in large skillet over medium-high heat. Add  onion ,  garlic ,  lemon peel ,  salt ,  pepper ,  curry powder ,  turmeric  and  cinnamon . Cook 2 to 3 minutes or until  onion  is tender. 
 Stir in  rice ,  dates  and  almonds . Stir well to blend; cook until thoroughly heated.
