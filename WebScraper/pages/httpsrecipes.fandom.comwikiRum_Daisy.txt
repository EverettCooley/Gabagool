      Rum Daisy     
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz  rum 
 juice of ½  lemon 
 1 tsp  raspberry syrup  or  grenadine 
 ½ tsp  powdered sugar 
 fill cracked  ice 
 Directions : 
 Mix the ingredients.
