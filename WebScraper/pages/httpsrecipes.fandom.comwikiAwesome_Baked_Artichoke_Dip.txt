Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  mozzarella  cheese, part skim  milk , shredded 
 1 cup  Parmesan cheese , grated 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 1 teaspoon  parsley  chopped 
 1 teaspoon  garlic , minced 
 1 tablespoon  chives  chopped 
 16 ounce can  artichoke  hearts, chopped 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in a 1 quart casserole dish. 
 Bake at 325 °F for 25 Minutes until top is golden and cheese is bubbly. 
 Serve with sturdy crackers.
