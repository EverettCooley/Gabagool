Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Pryor estate in Grapevine, Texas in 1996.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  flour 
 ¼ teaspoon  baking soda 
 2 tablespoons  granulated sugar 
 ¼ teaspoon  cinnamon 
 ⅛ teaspoon  ginger 
 ⅛ teaspoon  nutmeg 
 1  egg  well-beaten 
 1¼ cup  milk 
 2 tablespoons melted  shortening 
 ½ cup canned  pumpkin 
 Directions : 
 Combine  flour , soda,  sugar  and spices. 
 Combine  egg ,  shortening ,  pumpkin  and  milk  then add to  flour  mixture beating until smooth. 
 Bake on hot lightly greased griddle turning only once. 
 Serve hot with butter and syrup or  powdered sugar .
