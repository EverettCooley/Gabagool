 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 5 lbs. trimmed  Lamb  meat 
 5 clove  garlic  ground 
 1/2 tablespoon  black pepper 
 1/2 tablespoon  white pepper 
 1/2 lb. medium size  Onion  (ground) 
 2 tablespoons  olive oil 
 1 tablespoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Cut the trimmed meat in 2 1/4 X 1 1/4 inch cubes and place in a mixing bowl. Add 2 tablespoons  olive oil  and cover the meat, let it marinate for 15 minutes in refrigerator. 
 Mix the ground  garlic ,  black pepper ,  white pepper ,  salt  and add to the meat. Mix until all pieces are well coated. Add the ground  Onion  to the mixture of meat and spices, and marinate for at least 2 hours. Keep in refrigerator (DO NOT FREEZE) 
 After two hours, put the meat on skewer with vegetables and grill over high flame on a pre-heated grill.
