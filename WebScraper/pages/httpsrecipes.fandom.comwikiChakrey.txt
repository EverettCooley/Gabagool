 Description : 
 Chakrey
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups of  couscous 
 pat of  butter  (optional) 
 dash of  salt  (optional) 
 1 cup  evaporated milk 
 2 cups  vanilla   yogurt 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 ½ cup  Sugar 
 ½ tsp  vanilla extract 
 dash of  nutmeg  (optional) 
 Raisins  or  mint  garnish (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Prepare the  couscous  as normal. Bring four and one half cups of  water  to boil in a large saucepan. Add  couscous ,  butter , and  salt . Stir and cover. Remove from heat. Leave covered for 10 minutes. Allow  couscous  to cool.
 Combine all other ingredients. Stir  yogurt  mixture into  couscous . Add more  Sugar , to taste. Garnish as desired. Serve warm or chilled.
