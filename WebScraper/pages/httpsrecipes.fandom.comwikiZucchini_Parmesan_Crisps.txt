A great tasting, healthy snack or a good side dish.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb. zucchini or squash (about 2 medium sized) 
 1/4 cup shredded parmesan cheese 
 1/4 cup bread crumbs or (croutons, french fried onions, etc.) 
 1 tbl. olive oil 
 3/4 tsp. Tony's Creole seasoning or (salt & pepper) 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 400 deg. Line 2 baking sheets with foil & spray lightly with veg. spray. 
 Slice zucchini into 1/4 inch thick rounds. Toss rounds with oil, coating well. 
 In a wide bowl or plate, combine bread crumbs or (croutons, crushed fried onions, etc.) cheese, & seasoning, then blend. 
 Place rounds in mixture, coating both sides of each round. The mixture will not completely cover each round but will put a light coating on each one 
 Place rounds flat on baking sheet, sprinkle remaining bread crumbs over rounds. 
 Place in oven and bake for 22 - 27 min. until golden brown. 
 Remove from oven and let cool for a few min. before serving.
