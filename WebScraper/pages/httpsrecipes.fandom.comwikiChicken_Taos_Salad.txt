 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 2 cups diced cooked  Chicken  breast meat 
 1 15-ounce can  kidney beans , drained and rinsed 
 1 11-ounce can  corn , drained 
 1 medium-size red  bell pepper , chopped 
 1/2 cup thinly sliced  green onions 
 1/4 cup  vegetable oil 
 2 tablespoons fresh  lime juice 
 1 tablespoon  cider vinegar 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1 tablespoon  brown sugar 
 1 teaspoon  chili powder 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1/2 teaspoon  cumin 
 1/4 teaspoon hot pepper sauce 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  Chicken , beans,  corn , red pepper and  green onions  in large bowl. Whisk together  oil ,  lime juice ,  vinegar ,  garlic ,  brown sugar ,  chili powder ,  salt ,  cumin  and hot pepper sauce  in small bowl. Pour over salad; toss to coat. Serve immediately
