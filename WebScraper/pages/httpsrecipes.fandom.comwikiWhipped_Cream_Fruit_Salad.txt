Ingredients:
Fruits chopped - mangoes, bananas, apples, plums, papaya, cherries, grapes, chikoos, pears etc.
Whipped cream
 
  Method:
 
 Put some chopped fruits in a serving dish . Add spoonfuls of whipped cream. Again add fruits. Repeat with whipped cream. Chill N serve.
