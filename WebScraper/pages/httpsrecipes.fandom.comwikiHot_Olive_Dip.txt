 Description : 
       Hot Olive Dip     
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup 	dark, pitted 
 1/2 cup 	grated  mozzarella   Cheese 
 1/2 cup 	 yogurt 
 1/4 cup 	 evaporated milk 
 1/2 cup 	chopped  pecans 
 3/4 cup 	Aji Hot Sauce 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients in a blender and refrigerate until served.
