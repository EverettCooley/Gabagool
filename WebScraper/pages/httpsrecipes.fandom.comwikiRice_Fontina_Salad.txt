Description : 
 Makes 8 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups cooked  rice , cooled 
 1 cup (4 ounces) grated  fontina   or   Swiss cheese 
 ½ cup chopped  celery 
 12  cherry tomatoes , halved 
 1 small  zucchini , cut lengthwise into thin strips 1-inch long 
 ½ cup sliced pitted ripe * ¼ cup chopped  onion 
 ¼ teaspoon crushed  basil  leaves 
 ¼ teaspoon crushed  oregano  leaves 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 ⅛ teaspoon  garlic powder 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 ½ cup plain  yogurt 
 1 teaspoon  white wine vinegar 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  cheese ,  celery ,  tomatoes ,  zucchini , ,  onion ,  basil ,  oregano ,  pepper  and  garlic powder  in large bowl. 
 Blend  mayonnaise ,  yogurt ,  vinegar  and  salt ; toss with  rice  mixture. 
 Chill. 
 Serve on salad greens, if desired. 
 One-fourth cup of the dressing can be set aside to use as garnish.
