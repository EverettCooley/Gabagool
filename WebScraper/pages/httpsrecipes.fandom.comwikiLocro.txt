Description : 
 Potato soup. This is one of the most delicious Ecuador recipes.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 tablespoons (¼ cup)  butter 
 1 teaspoon sweet  paprika 
 1 medium  onion , finely chopped 
 4 pounds  potatoes , peeled and sliced 
 1 cup each  milk  and  light cream 
 ½ pound  munster cheese , grated 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 In a large, heavy saucepan heat the  butter  and stir in the  paprika . Add the  Onion  and saute over moderate heat until the  Onion  is softened. 
 Pour in 4 cups  water , bring to a boil, and the  potatoes , and simmer over low heat, uncovered, stirring occasionally. 
 When the  potatoes  are almost done, add the  milk  and  cream  and continue to cook, stirring from time to time, until the  potatoes  begin to disintegrate. 
 Stir the  Cheese  into the  potatoes , season to taste with  salt , and serve immediately.  Avocado  slices are sometimes served with the Locro, on separate plates but to be eaten at the same time.
