 Description : 
       Peanut Cookies     
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 Cookies:
 
 1 1/2 	cups  flour 
 1 1/2 	teaspoons  baking powder 
 1 	teaspoon  baking soda 
 1 	cup  brown sugar , firmly packed 
 1 	cup  unsalted butter , softened 
 1 	 egg 
 1 	cup  rolled oats 
 1 	cup flaked  coconut 
 1 	cup salted  peanuts 
 1/2 	cup  corn  flakes, finely crushed 
 Drizzle Icing:
 
 2 	tablespoons  unsalted butter 
 1 	cup confectioners'  Sugar 
 1 	tablespoon  water , hot 
 1 	teaspoon fresh  lemon juice 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Cookies: Combine  flour ,  baking powder ,  baking soda , and set aside.  cream   butter  and  Sugar .
Add  egg  and beat well.
Add all dry ingredients, blending after each addition.
Drop by rounded teaspoon on ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake at 350 degrees F. for 12-15 minutes.
Drizzle Icing Melt  butter .
Mix in confection  Sugar , hot  water , and fresh  lemon juice . Beat until smooth and drizzle over cooled cookies.
(Add additional hot  water  if mixture is too thick.)
