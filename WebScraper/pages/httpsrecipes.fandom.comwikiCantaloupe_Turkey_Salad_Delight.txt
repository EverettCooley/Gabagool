Ingredients : 
 3/4 lb smoked  Turkey  breast cut into 1/4-inch cubes 
 3/4 lb  cantaloupe  cut into cubes or balls 
 1 cup chopped  celery 
 1/2 cup thinly sliced  green onion 
 2 tbsp chutney chopped 
 1 tbsp  olive oil 
 1 tbsp  white wine vinegar 
 1/2 tbsp  sesame oil 
 1/2 tbsp dijon-style  mustard 
 1/2 tbsp reduced-sodium soy sauce 
 1/8 tsp  red pepper flakes 
 1 clove minced  garlic 
 Directions : 
 In large bowl combine  Turkey ,  cantaloupe ,  celery  and  green onions . 
 In small bowl whisk chutney,  oil ,  vinegar ,  sesame oil ,  mustard , soy sauce,  red pepper flakes  and  garlic . 
 Combine dressing with  Turkey / cantaloupe  mixture. Mix well. 
 Cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour. 
 
Recipe by The National  Turkey  Federation.
