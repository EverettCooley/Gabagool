Ingredients : 
 3 oz  white rum 
 1 oz  Cointreau 
 1½ oz  coconut cream ; such as  Coco López 
 3 tbsp crushed  strawberries 
 3 tbsp crushed  pineapple 
 2 cups crushed  ice 
 2 whole  strawberries ; for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Combine all the ingredients except the garnish in a blender, and process at high speed until mixed, 1 minute. 
 Pour into 2 tall glasses. Garnish with whole  strawberries .
