 About Bleu de Bresse : 
 Wikipedia Article  About Bleu de Bresse on Wikipedia 
 Blue Bresse Cheese was first made in the Bresse region of France. It is made from a double cream cheese derived from the milk of cows, it has a firm but edible outer coating that is characteristically white in color.
