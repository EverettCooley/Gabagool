Ingredients : 
 1  apple  (firm) 
 Â¼ cup  orange juice 
 1 tsp  lemon juice 
 1 tbsp  raisins 
 1 tbsp  celery 
 2 tbsp  applesauce 
 1  lettuce  leaf (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Slice off top of  apple ; remove core. 
 Prick outside with sharp fork. 
 Place  apple  in tall narrow bowl. 
 Combine  orange  and  lemon juice ; pour over  apple . 
 (add extra  water  if  apple  is not covered). 
 Marinate in refrigerator 4-5 hours. 
 Combine  raisins ,  celery , and  applesauce . 
 Allow to mellow at room temperature 2 hours. 
 Chill thoroughly. 
 Drain  apple . 
 Cut  apple  into 8 sections, slicing almost to the bottom. 
 Fill with  applesauce  mixture. 
 Place on crisp  lettuce  leaf. 
 Nutritional information : 
 1 serving = 2 fruit | calories = 54
