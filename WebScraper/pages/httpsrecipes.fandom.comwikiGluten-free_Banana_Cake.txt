Ingredients : 
 7  eggs , separated 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 cup  potato starch flour 
 2 to 3 mashed  bananas 
 1 cup  ground walnuts 
 fresh  strawberries , sliced 
 ground walnuts  for garnish 
 Directions : 
 beat whites until stiff and set aside. 
 in separate bowl, beat yolks and  sugar  together. 
 add  potato starch  slowly, beating to a smooth consistency. 
 add mashed  bananas  and groundnuts. 
 gently fold mixture into whites. 
 grease bottom but not sides of tube pan. 
 pour batter into pan and bake at 350 °F for 45 minutes. 
 turn pan upside down and let cool. 
 garnish with  strawberries  and  ground walnuts .
