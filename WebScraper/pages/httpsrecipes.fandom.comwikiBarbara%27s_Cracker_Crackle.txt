 Ingredients : 
 1 	stick 	 margarine  or  butter 
 3/4 	cup 	 Sugar 
 1 	Sleeve 	Saltine Crackers or other Favorite Crackers 
 Directions : 
 Place crackers on a Cookie Sheet side by side. 
Bring to a boil  margarine  or  butter  add  Sugar  and boil for 2 minutes. 
Pour over crackers. 
Place in oven and bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes. 
Let cool eat and enjoy.
