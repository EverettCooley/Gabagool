About Pizza spice : 
 Pizza spice is any of varying blends of usually dried Italian spices used on pizzas. These can include, but are not restricted to:
 
 oregano 
 basil 
 fennel seed , crushed 
 dried  thyme 
 garlic powder  or  salt 
 cayenne 
 chili pepper flakes 
 rosemary
