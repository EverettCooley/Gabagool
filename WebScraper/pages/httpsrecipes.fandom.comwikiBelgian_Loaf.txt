Description : 
 A pleasure to eat and make type of small  bread . It has a sweet long lasting taste and you might want to add a small layer of  Honey  on top. Try it with tea or  milk  ! 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 cup  raisins 
 1 cup  milk 
 ¼ lb  shortening 
 1  egg 
 1 cup + 1 cup  flour 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  baking powder 
 ½ tsp  baking soda 
 
 
 Directions : 
 In saucepan put first 4 ingredients and bring to a boil, cool. 
 Then stir in 1  egg , then the dry ingredients except 1 cup of the flour. 
 Mix in the 2nd. cup of  flour . 
 Bake at 350°F for 45 minutes.
