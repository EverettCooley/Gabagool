      Fruit salad     
 Description : 
 The abundance of fresh fruit in Israel, ripe from the fields and orchards, makes fruit salads a natural choice. After a good meal, nothing is more refreshing than this cool dessert.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3  oranges 
 2  apples 
 lemon juice 
 nuts 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 2 fresh  peaches 
 grapes ,  melons ,  pears , or 1 can fruit cocktail 
 Directions : 
 Cut the  apples  and  oranges  into small pieces. 
 Add  lemon juice  and  sugar . 
 Add the rest of the ingredients a short time before serving.
