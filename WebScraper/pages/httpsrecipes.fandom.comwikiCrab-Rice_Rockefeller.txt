Makes 8 appetizer servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup chopped  Onion 
 2 teaspoons  margarine 
 1 10-ounce package frozen chopped  spinach , drained well 
 1/2 teaspoon seasoned  salt 
 1/2 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 3 cups hot cooked  brown rice 
 1/2 cup reduced calorie  Mayonnaise 
 Vegetable  cooking spray 
 1 6-ounce package frozen lump or white crab meat, thawed 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 1 tablespoon chopped fresh  parsley 
 1 tablespoon Worchestershire sauce 
 2 tablespoons grated  Parmesan cheese 
 1  lemon , cut into wedges (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Onion  in  margarine  in large skillet over medium-high heat until  Onion  is tender. 
 Add  spinach ,  salt  and  pepper ; heat thoroughly. 
 Stir in  rice  and  Mayonnaise . 
 Spoon approximately 1/2 cup  rice  mixture into individual baking shells or ramekins coated with  cooking spray . 
 Toss crab meat with  lemon juice , 
 Top each ramekin with crab meat. 
 Sprinkle with  parsley  and  Parmesan cheese . 
 Place shells on baking sheet and broil 4 inches from heat source for 2 to 3 minutes. 
 Squeeze  lemon  wedges over hot mixture before serving, if desired.
