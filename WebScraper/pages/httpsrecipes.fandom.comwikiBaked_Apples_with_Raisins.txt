Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Prep Time: 15 Minutes 
 Ingredients : 
 2  apples 
 1 tbsp  raisins 
 orange juice 
 pinch ground  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Peel and core the apples. 
 Coat with the orange juice on the outer surface. 
 Stuff with the raisins. 
 Sprinkle on the cinnamon. 
 Place in pyrex baking cups. 
 Bake in a 350°F oven for 25 – 30 minutes, or until fork pierces surface easily. 
 Serve warm or cold. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 Calories: 74 | Protein: 0 g | Sodium: 0 mg | Cholesterol: 0 mg | Carbohydrates: 10 g 
 Exchanges: 1½ fruit
