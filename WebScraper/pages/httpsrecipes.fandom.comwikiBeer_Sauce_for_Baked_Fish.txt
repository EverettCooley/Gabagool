 Ingredients : 
 1 	cup 	Medium white sauce 
 1 	cup 	Grated  Cheddar   Cheese 
 2 	tsp 	 worcestershire sauce 
 1 	tsp 	 paprika 
 1 	tsp 	 mustard 
 1 	cup 	 beer 
 1 	tbsp 	 capers 
 Directions : 
 Combine white sauce,  Cheese ,  worcestershire sauce ,  paprika  and  mustard . 
Stir over low heat until  Cheese  melts. 
Add  beer  slowly while stirring; add  capers . 
Serve hot with baked fish fillets from the files of al  rice , north pole Alaska.
