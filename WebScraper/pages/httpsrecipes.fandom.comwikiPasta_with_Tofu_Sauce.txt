Ingredients : 
 2 pounds fresh chopped  tomatoes 
 ½ cup fresh  basil , chopped 
 8  garlic  cloves, chopped or pressed 
 ½ pound  tofu , chopped 
 ¼ cup (or less) extra virgin  olive oil 
 1 pound  pasta 
 cayenne  or other  hot pepper , to taste 
 ¼ cup  Parmesan  or  romano  cheese, grated 
 Directions : 
 Place chopped  tomatoes ,  garlic ,  basil  and  tofu  in large bowl. 
 Add  olive oil  and  hot pepper . 
 Toss. 
 Marinate for a least 2 hours (but preferably 4 hours). 
 Cook  pasta  al dente. 
 Drain the  pasta  and toss it with the grated  cheese . 
 Then toss the  pasta  with the  tomato  mixture, or place  tomato  mixture on top.
