Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 9 hard-cooked  eggs , chopped 
 ½ cup sliced  green onions 
 1 cup sliced  celery 
 ¼ cup chopped canned  green chiles 
 1 cup grated  cheddar   cheese 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 ¼ cup  picante sauce  (optional) 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon seasoned  pepper 
 salad  greens 
 1 large fresh  tomato , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  eggs ,  onions ,  celery , chiles and  cheese . 
 Blend  sour cream ,  lemon juice , picante sauce,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Pour over  rice  mixture; mix well. 
 Chill. 
 Serve on salad greens and top with  tomato .
