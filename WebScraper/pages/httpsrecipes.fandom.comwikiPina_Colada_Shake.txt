Description : 
 This recipe is from an Equal cookbook
 
 Makes approximately one 12 oz Serving. 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  skim milk 
 3 pack  Equal 
 3  ice  cubes 
 2 tbsp drained crushed  pineapple  (packed in own juice) 
 1 tsp  rum 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in blender. 
 Blend at high speed until smooth. 
 Serve immediately. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Calories per serving: 89 | traditional calories: 368 
 Diabetic exchanges: ½  milk  and ½ fruit.
