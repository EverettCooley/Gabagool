Description : 
 This is a sweet appetizer or dessert you can make in less than 30 minutes. It's full of energy and will be remembered for the next time. Below the ingredients which you can easily buy or already have. Instead of  pecans  you can choose other type of  nuts .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can  sweetened condensed milk 
 1 cup  granulated sugar 
 4 tablespoons  butter 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Cook  milk  and  Sugar  over low heat until it shows signs of sugaring and is caramel color; stir constantly. 
 Just before removing from heat, add  butter  and  nuts . 
 Pour into greased pan and cut into squares.
