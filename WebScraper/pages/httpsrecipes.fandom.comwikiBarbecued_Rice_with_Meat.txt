Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 1/2 cups julienne strips of cooked  Beef 
 1/3 cup minced  Onion 
 2 4-ounce cans sliced  Mushrooms , drained (reserve liquid) 
 1 3/4 cups  water 
 1 cup uncooked  rice 
 1/4 cup  butter  or  margarine 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 2 teaspoons prepared  mustard 
 1/2 cup  chili sauce 
 1 cup diced  celery 
 Directions : 
 Cook meat,  Onion ,  Mushrooms  and  rice  in  butter  until  rice  is golden. 
 Add  salt ,  pepper ,  mustard ,  chili sauce ,  celery  and  Mushroom  liquid combined with  water  to make 2 cups liquid. 
 Heat to boiling. Cover, lower heat; cook 20 to 30 minutes, or until liquid is absorbed. 
 Fluff with fork; serve at once.
