      Royal Gin Fizz     
 Ingredients : 
 50 ml  gin 
 25 ml fresh  lemon juice 
 12.5 ml  sugar syrup 
 1  egg white  (optional) 
 Champagne 
 Directions : 
 Shake the ingredients and garnish with  lemon .
