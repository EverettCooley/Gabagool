 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 baking  Potato 
 1 cup guacamole 
 1 cup  tomato 
 1/4 cup  green onion 
 1 cup plain lowfat  yogurt 
 1 cup  Cheddar   Cheese 
 1/4 cup  Bacon  bits 
 1/4 cup  butter 
 
 
 Directions : 
 1 Bake  potatoes , one per person, split and fluff with fork. 
2 Each person can select his or her own topping. 
3 TOPPING VARIATIONS:- Combine lowfat  cottage cheese , chopped green and red  bell peppers  and sliced  green onions . 
4 - Kabobs of grilled meat or poultry and vegetables of your choice. 
5 - Combine plain lowfat  yogurt , minced  Onion  and  parsley ,  white wine vinegar  and a dash of  paprika .
