 Ingredients : 
 2 cups self-rising  flour 
 pinch of  salt 
 1 tablespoon  granulated sugar 
 3 tablespoons  butter 
 2-3 tablespoons dried  currants ,  Sultanas , or  raisins 
 1-1/2 cups  milk 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 425 °F. 
 Lightly grease baking sheets. 
 Sift flour, salt, and sugar into a bowl. 
 Rub in the butter quickly and lightly. 
 Add fruit. 
 Mix in milk, a little at a time. 
 With floured hands knead lightly to a soft dough, adding a little more  milk  if necessary. 
 Roll out evenly but lightly about 1/4 inch thick on a floured board.Cut out scones. 
 Place on baking sheets and bake 12–15 minutes. 
 Note : 
 Self-rising flour makes lighter scones and biscuits, but it's possible to substitute 2 cups all-purpose bleached flour, 3 teaspoons baking powder, and 1 teaspoon salt.
