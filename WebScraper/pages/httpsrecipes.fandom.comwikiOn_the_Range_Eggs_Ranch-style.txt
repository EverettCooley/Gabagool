Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Butler Estate in Irving, Texas in 1988.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 12 corn  tortillas 
 12  eggs 
 1 tablespoon  unsalted butter 
 Mexican Tomato Sauce : 
 2 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 1 small  white onion  finely chopped 
 1 clove  garlic  finely chopped 
 2 large  tomatoes  peeled seeded and chopped 
 ½ teaspoon  granulated sugar 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 2 tablespoons finely chopped  jalapeno peppers 
 Directions : 
 Heat vegetable oil in a heavy skillet over moderate heat and fry tortillas until limp. 
 Drain on paper towels and place two tortillas side by side on each plate. 
 Fry eggs in butter for 2 minutes. 
 Place 2 eggs on top of each tortilla and top with tomato sauce. 
 To make sauce heat oil in a saucepan and fry onion and garlic for 3 minutes. 
 Add remaining ingredients and cook over low heat for 20 minutes stirring occasionally.
