Description : 
 This was enjoyed by many at a Super Bowl Party (Football)
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups uncooked  spiral pasta 
 1 cup unsweetened  pineapple  chunks save the juice) 
 1 (8 ounce) carton peach  yogurt 
 2 tablespoons  sour cream 
 1½ cups  cantaloupe , cut into cubes 
 1 cup seedless  red grapes , cut in halves 
 1½ cups fresh  strawberries , sliced 
 Directions : 
 Cook  pasta  according to package directions, then rinse, drain and cool well. 
 Drain the  pineapple , well, and reserve 2 tablespoons of the juice. 
 Set the juice aside for the dressing. 
 Combine the  yogurt ,  sour cream , and the  pineapple juice  for the dressing. 
 Combine the other ingredients  pasta ,  pineapple  chunks, cantaloupe,  grapes  and  strawberries . 
 Now add the dressing and toss gently. 
 Chill and serve cold!
