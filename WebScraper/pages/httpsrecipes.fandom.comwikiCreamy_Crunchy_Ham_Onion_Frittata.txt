Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Steep Estate in Elmo, Texas in 1989.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  margarine 
 5  eggs  slightly beaten 
 ¼ cup  milk 
 8 ounce jar  Cheez Whiz 
 1 cup chopped cooked  potatoes 
 1 cup chopped  ham 
 1 can  French fried onions 
 Directions : 
 Melt  margarine  in ovenproof skillet over low heat. 
 Gradually add eggs and  milk  to  cheese  spread and mix well then stir in  potatoes ,  ham  and ½ of  onions . 
 Pour into skillet and bake at 350°F for 20 minutes. 
 Top with remaining  onions  and continue baking 5 minutes.
