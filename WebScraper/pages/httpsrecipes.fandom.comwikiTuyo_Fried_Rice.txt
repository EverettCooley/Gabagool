 Description : 
This fried rice recipe combines two Pinoy favorites:  sinangag  and dried fish! Add salty notes to your fried rice by adding dried fish. Make fried rice even more flavorful by cooking the grains in coconut milk.       Tuyo Fried Rice     
 Ingredients : 
 5 pieces  tuyo , cooked 
 2 tablespoons vegetable oil 
 2 cloves garlic, minced 
 3 cups cooked rice, white only 
 sprigs of spring onions, sliced 
 ½ cup coconut milk, optional 
 Directions : 
 Remove the scales and fillet the  tuyo  (dried fish). Set aside. 
 In a medium-sized wok, heat the oil and cook garlic until golden brown. Add the rice and sauté until the rice is just coated with oil. 
 Add the  tuyo  fillets and coconut milk (only if desired) and mix together slowly, making sure not to mash the rice. 
 Add spring onions before serving. Pair this seafood-based rice dish with any main dish. 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Servings:  4
