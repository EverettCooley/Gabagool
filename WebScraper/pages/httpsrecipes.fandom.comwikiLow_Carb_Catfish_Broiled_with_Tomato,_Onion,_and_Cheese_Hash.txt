Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Yield: 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 6 oz  catfish  fillets, ½ inch thick 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 2 tsp  olive oil 
 1 tsp  hot sauce 
 2 medium  tomatoes , chopped 
 1 medium  onion , finely chopped 
 1 tsp minced fresh  jalapeño pepper , seeds discarded 
 ½ cup coarsely grated 2% sharp  cheddar  cheese 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp cracked  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Preheat broiler. 
 In a small bowl, stir together lemon juice, olive oil, and hot sauce. 
 Brush both sides of catfish fillets. 
 Place on an oiled baking pan and broil about 4 inches from the heat for 5 minutes. 
 In medium bowl, stir together tomato, onion, jalapeño pepper, and cheese. 
 Spoon over catfish. 
 Sprinkle with salt and pepper and broil for an additional 5 minutes. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 280 calories, 16 g fat, 27 g protein, 7 g carbohydrates, 1g fiber, 77 mg cholesterol, 503 mg sodium
