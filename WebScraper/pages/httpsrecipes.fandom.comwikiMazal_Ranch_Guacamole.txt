 Description : 
 Great with fajitas.
 
 Ingredients : 
 Genuine  California avocados 
 onions (preferably walla walla), diced, to taste 
 Half  lime  per  avocado 
 chili powder , to taste 
 Pinch  cumin ,  salt ,  pepper 
 Roma  tomatoes (diced), to taste 
 cilantro (diced), to taste 
 Jalapenos(diced), to taste (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Cut up  avocado  and mash with fork in small bowl.
 Add all the ingredients and mix well.
