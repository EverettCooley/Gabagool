Ingredients : 
 Cake 
 3/4 c. frozen  strawberries , thawed & drained, save liquid 
 1/2 c. strawberry liquid from above 
 1 c. Wesson  oil 
 1 pkg. Duncan Hines white  cake mix 
 1 sm. box strawberry  Jello 
 4  eggs 
 1 tbsp.  vanilla 
 Icing 
 1 sm. pkg.  cream cheese 
 3/4 stick  butter 
 1 box  10X sugar 
 remaining strawberry liquid 
 Directions : 
 Beat together all cake ingredients in mixer bowl for 5 minutes. 
 Pour into a greased and floured tube pan. 
 Bake for 1 hour at 350 degrees. 
 Cool cake 
 Cream together the cream cheese and butter. 
 Add sugar until the desired consistency. (I used all of the box.) 
 Add strawberry juice to flavor a bit. 
 Frost cooled cake 
 Notes : 
 Keep cake in refrigerator.
