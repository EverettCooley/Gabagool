Description : 
 A tangy way to add zazz to a salad or steak!
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ tsp  white wine vinegar 
 2 tbsp fresh  lime juice 
 1 cup diced  mango 
 1 tbsp  honey 
 ½ tsp freshly ground black  pepper 
 ⅛ tsp ground  cayenne pepper 
 ⅛ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Blend all ingredients until smooth. Enjoy!
