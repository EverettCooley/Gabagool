Description 
 The  B-57  is a layered shot and part of the B-50 series.
 
 B-57  from the Wikibooks Bartending Guide—original source of introduction and ingredients, licensed under the GNU Free Documentation License 
 Bartending/Techniques  from the Wikibooks Bartending Guide—original source of directions, licensed under the GNU Free Documentation License 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 1 part  Kahlúa 
 1 part  triple sec 
 1 part  Sambuca 
 Directions 
 Pour ingredients over the back of a spoon and let them flow down the side of a straight sided glass.
