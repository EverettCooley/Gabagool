cherry   chocolate  Cream Parfaits
 Another Very Good One
 
 Recipe Rating:
 
Prep Time: 15 minutes
 Total Time: 2 hr 15 minutes
 Makes: 4 Servings
 
 
 
 1 Pkg. (4-Serving Size) Jell-O Brand  cherry  Flavor  gelatin 
 1 Cup Boiling  water 
 1 Cup Cold  water 
 6 Oreo  chocolate  Sandwich Cookies, Divided 
 1/2 Cup Thawed  Cool Whip  Whipped Topping 
 
 
Dissolve  gelatin  in Boiling  water ; Stir in Cold  water . Pour Into 8-Inch Square Pan. Refrigerate 2 Hours or Until Firm.Chop Coarsely 2 of The Cookies. Add to Whipped Topping; Stir Gently Until Well Blended. Set Aside. Cut  gelatin  Into Cubes; Spoon Evenly Into 4 Parfait Glasses. Top Evenly With The Whipped Topping Mixture.Serve Immediately. Or, Cover And Refrigerate Until Ready to Serve. Top Each Parfait With 1 of The Remaining Cookies Just Before Serving.
 
 
 Kraft Kitchens Tips
 
 Special Extra
 Garnish With  maraschino cherries  Just Before Serving.
