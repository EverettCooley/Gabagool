 Description : 
Keep the chocolate and the gelatin boiling to pour into molds. This rich gelatin-based dessert is made with real chocolate and vanilla to help maintain its sweet consistency.       Chocolate Jelly     
 Ingredients : 
 75 g.  chocolate  powder  or  (7 tablespoons chocolate syrup) 
 500 ml.  milk , regular or reduced-fat/skimmed only 
 3 tbsp.  sugar , brown only 
 10 ml.  vanilla  extract 
 12 g.  gelatin 
 Directions : 
 Pour over  gelatin  cold boiled  water  (1 part  gelatin ; 9 parts  water ). 
 Leave for 40 â€“ 60 minutes and then warm up on a very low heat; do not bring to boil. 
 Strain carefully and boil the  milk . 
 Dissolve  chocolate ,  sugar , and  milk ; add  vanilla  and  gelatin . 
 Stir carefully and pour into molds. Cool down, then serve plain with other desserts. 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Servings:  4
