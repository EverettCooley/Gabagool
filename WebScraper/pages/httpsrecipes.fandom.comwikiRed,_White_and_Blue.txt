 Ingredients : 
 ⅓ oz blue  curaçao 
 ⅓ oz  grenadine 
 ⅓ oz  peach schnapps 
 Directions : 
 Pour the  grenadine . 
 Next float the  peach schnapps , then the blue  curaçao .
