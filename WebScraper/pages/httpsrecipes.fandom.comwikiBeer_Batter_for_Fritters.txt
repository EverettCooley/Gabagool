Ingredients : 
 1 cup  all-purpose flour 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 1  egg 
 1 cup  beer 
 ¼ cup  vegetable oil 
 Directions : 
 Mix  flour  and  baking powder . 
 Add remaining ingredients and beat until smooth. 
 To fry: dip chunks of food into batter. 
 Fry in 3 – 4 inches of fat at 375°F until golden brown. 
 Drain on paper toweling. 
 Sprinkle fruit fritters with  confectioner's sugar  or top with a sweet sauce.
