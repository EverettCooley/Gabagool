Description : 
 Makes 3 cups or 3 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup cold  skim milk 
 1 pack (4-serving size) Jell-O brand  cherry  flavor sugar-free  gelatin 
 1 cup crushed  ice 
 1 large  banana , cut into chunks 
 Directions : 
 Pour  milk  into blender container. 
 Add remaining ingredients; cover. 
 Blend at high speed 30 seconds or until smooth. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving: calories - 80, protein - 5 gm., fat - 0, carbohydrates - 14 gm., cholesterol - 0 mg., sodium - 130 mg. 
 
 Diabetic exchange: nonfat  milk  - ½, fruit - ½
