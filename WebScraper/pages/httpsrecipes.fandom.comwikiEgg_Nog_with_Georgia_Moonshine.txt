NOTE: For this recipe, you'll need a mixer and 3 mixing bowls. (2 MEDIUM 1 LARGE) 
 Ingredients : 
 6  egg 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 cup Georgia  moonshine 
 1 pint of  whipping cream 
 1 cup  milk 
 Directions : 
 Separate  eggs : Yolks in large mixing bowl, whites in medium mixing bowl. 
 Place  whipping cream  in medium mixing bowl. 
 Beat whites until stiff, adding ⅓ cup of  sugar  then set aside. 
 Beat cream until stiff, adding ⅓ cup of  sugar  set aside. 
 Beat york until light and thickening adding ⅓ cup of  sugar  slowly. 
 Add 1 cup of Georgia Moonshine SLOWLY while beating constantly. 
 To the yolks, mix in the beaten  egg whites . 
 To this mixture mix in the  whipped cream . 
 To this final mixture add 1 cup of sweet  milk  and mix well.
