 Ingredietns : 
 3 tablespoons cooking  oil 
 ½ cup chopped  green onions 
 1 cup  brown rice 
 3 cups  beef broth 
 ½ teaspoon  thyme  leaves 
 ½ teaspoon  marjoram 
 Directions : 
 Place 3 tablespoons cooking  oil  in a 3 quart saucepan over medium heat. 
 Add chopped  green onions  and  brown rice . 
 Cook until  rice  is lightly browned, stirring occasionally. 
 Stir in  beef broth ,  thyme  and  marjoram . 
 Heat to boiling, reduce heat, cover and cook 45 minutes or until  rice  is tender and all liquid is absorbed.
