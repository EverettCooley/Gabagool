 Description : 
 Yield: 1½ pound loaf 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups  bread flour 
 ⅓ cup  soy flour 
 3 tbs  sugar 
 1½ tsp  salt 
 1½ tbs  margarine 
 ⅓ cup  oatmeal 
 ¾ cup  soymilk  (plain or  vanilla ) 
 ¾ cup  water 
 1½ tsp  dry yeast 
 Directions : 
 Add ingredients to bread machine according to manufacturer's directions. 
 All ingredients should be at room temperature for best results. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving (1 slice /  1 / 16  loaf):
 
 129 calories | 4g protein (1g soy protein) | 24g carbohydrate | 2g total fat (0.3g sat fat) | 233mg sodium | 0mg cholesterol | 0.75g dietary fiber
