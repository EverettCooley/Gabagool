Description : 
 This recipe is from an Equal cookbook
 
 Makes approximately one 12 oz Serving. 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  skim milk 
 3 pack  Equal 
 3  ice  cubes 
 1½ tsp instant  coffee 
 1 tsp  cocoa 
 1 tsp  brandy extract 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in blender. 
 Blend at high speed until smooth. 
 Serve immediately. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Calories per serving: 75 | traditional calories: 39
 
 Diabetic exchanges: ½ nonfat milk or ½ milk if low-fat  milk  is used.
