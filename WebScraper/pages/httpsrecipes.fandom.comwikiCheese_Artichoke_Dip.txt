Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 ounces  cream cheese  softened 
 12 ounces  mozzarella  cheese shredded 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 1 cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 1 white  onion  finely chopped 
 2 cloves  garlic  finely chopped 
 2 small jars marinated  artichoke hearts  drained well 
 2 bags  pita  bread 
 Directions : 
 Tear artichokes apart with your fingers. 
 Cut up pita  bread  into chip size triangles then separate and bake on a cookie sheet until crispy for 5 minutes. 
 Combine remaining ingredients and mash then bake in uncovered casserole dish for 30 minutes at 350Â°F. 
 Serve warm.
