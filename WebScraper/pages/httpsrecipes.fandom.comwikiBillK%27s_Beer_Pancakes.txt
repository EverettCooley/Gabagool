Description 
 Contributed by user --  BillK  ( talk ) 
 
 Cook Time: Variable. 
 Ingredients 
 3 cups sifted  flour 
 3 tsp  baking powder 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 2  eggs  separated 
 1 cup  beer 
 1½ cups  milk 
 ⅓ cup melted  butter 
 Directions 
 Sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar 2. 
 Beat egg yolks with a fork. 
 Stir in beer, milk, butter and add to dry ingredients. 
 Beat until smooth. 
 Beat egg whites until stiff. 
 Fold into batter. 
 Cook on a hot griddle until bubbles.
