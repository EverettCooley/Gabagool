      Meat loaf     Serves: 4 - 5 with  vegetables & or potatoes
 Ingredients
 
 ⅔ cup dried  breadcrumbs , soaked in 
 1 cup  milk 
 1½ lbs  ground beef 
 2  eggs , slightly beaten 
 1 cup chopped  onions 
 1 chopped bell pepper 
 1 1/2 tsp Tony's creole seasoning 
 1 tsp liquid smoke 
 1 pkg onion soup mix 
 1 tsp A1 steak sauce 
 ½ teaspoon  sage 
 Glaze : 
 3 tablespoons  brown sugar 
 ¼ cup  ketchup 
 Directions : 
 Saute' onions and peppers 
 Combine all meatloaf ingredients. 
 Place on shallow pan or in loaf pan ( coat pan with non-stick spray) 
 Top with 3/4 of glaze. 
 Bake at 350°F for 50 minutes 
 Top with remaining glaze, bake 10-15 min more (or until middle is not pink)
