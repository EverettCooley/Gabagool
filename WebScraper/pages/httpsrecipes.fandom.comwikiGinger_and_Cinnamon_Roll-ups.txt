Ingredients : 
 1 10x15  puff pastry 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 1/2 tbsp  ginger 
 1/2 tbsp cinnamon 
 egg  wash 
 2 cups powdered sugar 
 water 
 Directions : 
 Cut  puff pastry  into 5x5 squares brush with  egg  wash sprinkle each square with  ginger  and cinnamon 
 roll up tight cut each in half 
 place on a sheet pan bake at 350 °F for 10 minutes 
 turn pan around bake another 10 min. 
 Cool on rack 
 mix  water  with powder sugar just till it becomes a glaze, pour over each roll up.
