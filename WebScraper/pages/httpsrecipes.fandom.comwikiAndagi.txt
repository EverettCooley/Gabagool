Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 2½ to 3 dozen 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups  flour 
 1 cup  sugar 
 3 tbsp  baking powder  (heaping) 
 1 tsp  salt 
 3 – 4 large  eggs 
 ¾ cup  evaporated milk 
 3 tbsp  oil 
 Directions : 
 Sift dry ingredients together. 
 Combine eggs and milk; add to dry ingredients. 
 Mix thoroughly. 
 Drop by teaspoonfuls into 375 °F oil; cook until brown.
