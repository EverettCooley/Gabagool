Ingredients : 
 16 	tbsp 	 margarine 
 2/3 	cups 	 brown sugar 
 2 	unit 	 eggs 
 2 	tsp 	 baking soda 
 1 	tsp 	 salt 
 2 	tsp 	Ground Cloves 
 2 	tsp 	 cinnamon 
 1 	tsp 	 nutmeg 
 1/2 	cup 	 apple juice 
 4 	cups 	 flour 
 2 	cups 	Chopped  Apples 
 2 	cups 	 Raisins 
 2 	cups 	Chopped  nuts 
 Directions : 
 cream  together  margarine ,  brown sugar  and  eggs . 
Add baking sodam spices and  apple juice  and mix well. 
Gradually add  flour , mixing well after each addition. 
Fold in  Apples ,  Raisins  and  nuts . 
Drop by heaping teasponnful onto lightly greased cookie sheet. 
Bake at 375 degrees for 10â€“12 minutes.
