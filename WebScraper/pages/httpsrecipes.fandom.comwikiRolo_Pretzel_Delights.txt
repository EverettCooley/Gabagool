This is an extremely simple yet tasty recipe. All you need to makes these are pretzels and Rolo Chewy Caramel in Milk Chocolate pices.
 
 Procedure : 
 Heat oven to 350â€™f. Line cookie sheets with parchment paper or foil. Place one pretzel for each pretzel treat desired on prepared sheet. Top each pretzel with one unwrapped Rolo chewy Caramel in milk chocolate. Bake 3-5 minutes or until caramel piece begins to soften, but not melt. Remove from oven; top with either pecan half or additional pretzel. Cool completely. Bag of Rolo makes approximately 7 dozen treats.
