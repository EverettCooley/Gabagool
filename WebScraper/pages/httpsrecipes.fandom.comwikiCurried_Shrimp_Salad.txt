 Description : 
 Crunchy  jicama  and spicy  curry  powder dress up simple  Shrimp  salad. If you cant find  jicama , you may substitute  celery .
 
       Description     
 Ingredients : 
 1/3 cup  Mayonnaise 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 2 teaspoons  curry  Powder]] 
 1 1/4 pounds cooked medium  Shrimp , cut into 1/2 pieces 
 1/2  Jicama , peeled and diced in 1/3" cubes (about 1 cup) 
 salt  and  Pepper 
 Directions : 
 1. In a large bowl, mix  Mayonnaise ,  lemon juice , and  curry  powder until smooth. Add  Shrimp  and  jicama ; toss to coat evenly with dressing. Season to taste with  salt  and  pepper .
