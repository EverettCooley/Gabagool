Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/3 cup  flour 
 1 tablespoon  chili powder 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1/4 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1 1/2 pounds boneless lean  Beef  round  Steak , sliced into thin strips 
 1/4 cup  vegetable oil 
 1 cup chopped  Onion 
 1/3 cup sliced pimentos 
 2 1/2 cups  beef broth 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Blend  flour ,  chili powder ,  salt ,  garlic powder  and  black pepper . 
 Dredge meat in seasoned  flour ; brown in  oil  in large saucepan or Dutch oven. 
 Add  Onion  and saute lightly. 
 Stir in pimientos and broth. 
 Cover and simmer 30 minutes or until meat is tender. 
 Remove from heat; stir in  sour cream . 
 Top each serving with a mound of fluffy  rice .
