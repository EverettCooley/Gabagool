 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 8 	boneless, skinless  Chicken  breast halves 
 2 	tbsp	 butter 
 2 	tbsp	 oil 
 2 	cans	10-3/4 oz.  cream  of  Chicken  soup 
 1 	cup	 milk 
 1 	can	16-oz. crushed  pineapple 
 1 	cup	chopped  macadamia nuts 
 1/2 	tsp	 paprika 
 Directions : 
 Easy to prepare for Christmas Eve, this recipe I created has a uniquely different, delicious taste sensation using  Chicken  breasts,  pineapple  and  macadamia nuts . 
 Usually accompany this with steamed  rice , a green salad topped with  cherry tomatoes  and hot crusty rolls; a wonderful Christmas Eve meal that everyone enjoys. 
 In a skillet brown  Chicken  in  butter  and  oil . 
 Place in a singe layer in a casserole. 
 Dilute condensed soup with  milk ; pour over  Chicken . 
 Spread  pineapple  and  macadamia nuts  over casserole. 
 Sprinkle with  paprika . Cover tightly and bake in a preheated 350 degree oven for 45â€“50 minutes.
