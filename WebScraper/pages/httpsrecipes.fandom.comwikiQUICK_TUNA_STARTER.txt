 Description : 
 Quick  Tuna  starter. Decorate with  lemon  slices.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tin  Tuna , drained and flaked 
 3 to 4  bananas , sliced 
 lettuce  leaves 
 15ml thick white  Onion  soup powder (or to taste) 
 100ml  mayonnaise 
 Directions : 
 Mix  Mayonnaise  and soup powder with  bananas  and tuna. 
 Arrange  lettuce  leaves in cocktail shells or glasses. 
 Spoon mixture onto  lettuce  leaves. 
 Sprinkle with  paprika  and decorate with  lemon  slices.
