 Description : 
 Purchased from the Van Sickle Estate in Mabank, Texas in 1990. Notation on paper says this is served at family picnics. Dated 1928
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 ounce package  cream   Cheese , softened 
 2 cups cold  milk 
 1 small box  chocolate  instant pudding and pie filling 
 21 ounce can  cherry  pie filling 
 1 tablespoon  cherry   Liquor 
 1/2 cup  chocolate  wafer cookie crumbs 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Beat  cream   Cheese  with 1/2 cup  milk  at low speed until smooth. Add pudding mix and remaining  milk . 
 Beat until smooth, 1 to 2 minutes. Mix  cherry  pie filling and  Liquor . 
 Reserve a few  cherries  for garnish, if desired. 
 Spoon 1/2 of pudding mixture evenly into individual dessert dishes. 
 Sprinkle with cookie crumbs. Top with pie filling, then with remaining pudding mixture. 
 Refrigerate until ready to serve. Garnish with reserved  cherries  and additional cookie crumbs.
