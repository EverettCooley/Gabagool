 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 	cup 	Cooked elbow  macaroni 
 1 	cup 	Mashed  tofu 
 3 	cup 	 Cheesy Gravy 
 Directions : 
 1 	Preheat oven to 350 °F.
 2 	Combine  tofu  &  macaroni .
 3 	Place in a baking dish.
 4 	Pour gravy over the mixture & bake 20 minutes.
