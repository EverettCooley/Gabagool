Ingredients : 
 4  tomatoes ,seeded and quartered 
 ½  red onion , sliced 
 1  sweet green pepper , sliced 
 8  radishes 
 2  lettuce  hearts 
 4 ribs  celery 
 1 x 6½ oz can  tuna 
 8  anchovy  fillets 
 2 hard-boiled  eggs , quartered 
 8  ripe olives 
 Salad dressing : 
 2 tbsp  wine vinegar  or  lemon juice 
 6 tbsp extra virgin  olive oil 
 salt  and ground  black pepper 
 fresh  basil  leaves, coarsely chopped 
 Directions : 
 Combine the prepared vegetables in a salad bow, arranging the  tuna  olives, and quartered  eggs  on top. 
 Mix the  Salad dressing  and sprinkle over the salad.
