Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
Yield: 6 Servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 slices  bacon , crisp-cooked and crumbled 
 ½ cup finely diced, cooked country  ham 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 1 tsp minced, fresh  ginger root 
 7 cups shredded  green cabbage 
 ½ cup  heavy cream 
 Directions : 
 In a large pot, layer in order:  bacon ,  ham ,  onion , ginger , and  cabbage . 
 Pour cream over all. 
 Bring to a simmer. 
 Reduce heat to medium. 
 Cook 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until  cabbage  is soft and liquid is reduced to a thick sauce. 
 Enjoy.
