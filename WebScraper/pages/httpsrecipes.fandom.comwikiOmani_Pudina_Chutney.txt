Ingredients : 
 a bunch of  pudina , cleaned and washed 
 green chillies  according to taste 
 raw mango  (small) - only if available (optional: can squeeze a few drops of  lemon  in absence of raw  mango ) 
 one pinch of  red chile powder 
 salt  to taste 
 one small  onion 
 Directions : 
 Chop  onion , pudina, green chillies and raw  mango  into the mixer jar. 
 Add  salt  and a few drops of  water . 
 Grind till it makes a fine paste. 
 If adding  lemon , do it after you finish grinding.
