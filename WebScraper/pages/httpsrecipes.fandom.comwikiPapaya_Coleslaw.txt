Ingredients : 
 1  papaya  chopped 
 lime juice 
 6 cups shredded  cabbage 
 1 large can drained  pineapple  chunks 
 1 cup  red grapes 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 1 tablespoon  sugar 
 1 teaspoon  lemon juice 
 chopped  walnuts 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Combine fruits with  lime juice  and  cabbage . 
 In separte bowl mix  sour cream ,  sugar ,  lemon juice  and  salt . 
 Mix well. 
 Pour  sour cream  mixture over  cabbage  mixture. 
 Toss until well coated. 
 Cover, chill. 
 Add  nuts  before serving.
