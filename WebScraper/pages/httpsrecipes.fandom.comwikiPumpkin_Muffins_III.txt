 Description : 
 These freeze well.
 
 Makes 12 muffins. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  pumpkin 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 2  eggs  or  egg substitute 
 1 tsp  vanilla extract 
 6 tbsp  flour 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 4 tbsp  raisins 
 ½ cup  carrots , grated 
 ⅔ cup  powdered milk 
 10 pack  Sweet 'n Low 
 2 tsp  pumpkin pie spice 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients. 
 Spray muffin tin with pam. 
 Divide equally. 
 Bake at 350°F. 
 For 25 minutes. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Each serving - of six (6) muffins, yes 6 muffins
 
 Weight Watchers exchanges: 1 bread, 1 fruit, 1  milk , 1 protein and 1 ½ vegetables. 
 This is a no fat recipe! you may substitute  Egg Beaters  for the  eggs  and Equal for the Sweet 'n Low.
