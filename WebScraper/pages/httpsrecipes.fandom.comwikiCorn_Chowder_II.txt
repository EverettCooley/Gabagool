corn  Chowder
 
 3 Medium  potatoes , Diced 
 1 Small  Onion , Grated 
 1 Cup  water 
 1/4 Cup  butter 
 1 Can (1 Lb.) Whole Kernel  corn , Undrained 
 3 Cups  milk 
 salt  And  pepper  to Taste
 Chopped  chives , Optional
 
In a Pan, Combine  potatoes ,  Onion ,  water  And  butter . Bring to a Boil. Cover, Simmer For 10 to 15 Minutes, or Until  potatoes  Are Tender. Add  corn  And  milk . Simmer For 15 Minutes Longer. be Careful Chowder Does Not Boil. Season With  salt  And  pepper . if Desired, Garnish Each Serving With  chives . Serves 8
 Source: Come Into The Kitchen
