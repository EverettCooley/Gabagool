Potato Pizza
 2 pkg. frozen French fries (12 oz each)
2 pkg. mozzarella cheese, sliced (6 oz)
1 can tomato sauce (8 oz)
1 cup cut up salami
1 cup cut up pepperoni
1 cup cooked pork sausage
1/4 t. oregano
 Arrange fries in single layer in jelly roll pan. Lay cheese slices over potatoes, then spread with tomato sauce. Place meat evenly over top. Sprinkle with oregano. Bake in 450 oven for 15 to 20 minutes. Cut in squares and serve at once.
Serves 6 to 8
