Description : 
 A recipe from Malaga, Spain. The recipe is good for pork ribs too.
 
 Ingredients : 
 leg of lamb 
 soy sauce 
 miel de caña  ( molasses ) 
 Málaga wine 
 Directions : 
 A leg of lamb, is marinated in a mixture of equal parts soy sauce, molasses and wine of malaga for about 24 hours. 
 Then drained, dried and placed in the oven and roasting. 
 Basting with the drained marinate.
