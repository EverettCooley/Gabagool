Description : 
 An interesting variation of the traditional nanaimo bar. 
Makes: 16 bars 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 oz  chocolate, semisweet , 3 squares 
 ½ cup  butter 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 1 each  egg 
 2 cup graham wafer crumbs 
 1 cup  coconut 
 ½ cup  nuts , chopped 
 Filling : 
 2 tbsp  custard powder , Bird's is the most common brand 
 ¼ cup  milk 
 2 tbsp  butter , softened 
 ½ cup  peanut butter , smooth 
 2 cup  sugar , icing, or confectioner -sifted 
 Icing : 
 5 oz  chocolate, semisweet  -chopped 
 1 tbsp  butter 
 Directions : 
 Base : 
 Melt  chocolate  and  butter  in microwave on high power 2 minutes. 
 Mix in  sugar ,  vanilla  and  egg . 
 Add crumbs,  coconut  and  nuts . 
 Press into 9 inch square pan. 
 Chill. 
 Filling : 
 Using electric mixer, beat together all ingredients. 
 Spread over base; chill. 
 Icing : 
 Melt  chocolate  with  butter  in saucepan on medium power 3 to 4 minutes. 
 Spread over filling.
