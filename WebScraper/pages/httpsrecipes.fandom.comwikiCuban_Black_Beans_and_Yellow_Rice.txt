Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 slices  Bacon , diced 
 1 cup chopped  Onion 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1 cup uncooked  rice 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1/4 teaspoon  ground turmeric 
 1/8 teaspoon  ground red pepper 
 2 cups  water 
 1 medium fresh  tomato , chopped 
 1/2 cup chopped  green pepper 
 1 cup diced fully-cooked  Ham 
 1 cup dried  black beans , cooked and well-seasoned 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Bacon  in large skillet over low heat. Pour off fat. Return 1 tablespoon to skillet. 
 Add  Onion  and  garlic ; cook over medium heat 2 minutes. 
 Stir in  rice ,  salt ,  turmeric , red pepper and  water . 
 Bring to a boil; reduce heat, cover, and simmer 10 minutes. 
 Add  tomato ,  pepper  and  Ham ; simmer 5 minutes longer, or until  rice  is tender and liquid is absorbed. 
 Serve yellow  rice  in deep bowls and spoon beans in center.
