Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Legmen Estate in Jacksonville, Texas in 1991.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 prepared pizza crust 
 ½ cup  barbecue sauce 
 1 package grilled  chicken  strips 
 2 cups shredded pepper  jack cheese 
 1 large  bell pepper  sliced 
 1 large  white onion  sliced 
 Directions : 
 Spread pizza crust with barbecue sauce. 
 Top with chicken strips, cheese, green pepper and onion. 
 Place on cookie sheet and bake at 450°F for 10 minutes.
