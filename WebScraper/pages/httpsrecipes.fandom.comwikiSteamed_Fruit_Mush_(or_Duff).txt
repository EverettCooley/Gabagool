Steamed Fruit  mush  (or Duff)
 
 4 c Fruit 
 2 c  sugar 
 1 ts  lemon juice 
 1 tb  butter 
 2 c  flour 
 4 ts  baking powder 
 1 ts  salt 
 3/4 c  milk 
 For  blueberry   mush , Use  blueberries . For  raspberry   mush , UseRaspberries. For  blackberry   mush , Use  blackberries . For  cherry  Duff,Use  cherries . For  peach  Duff, Use  peaches . 
 
Sift  flour ,  baking powder  And  salt  Together, And Work in The  butter .Add The  milk  And Mix Thoroughly. Combine  sugar , Fruit And LemonJuice. Mix With First Batter. Pour Into a Buttered Mold, CoverTightly And Steam For 45 Minutes. Serve With Cream.
 
Source: Pennsylvania Dutch
