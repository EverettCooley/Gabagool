Description : 
 For a special occasion, a birthday, or for a heart healthy Valentine's Day!
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes For Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 From: www.naturemade.com 
 Serves 12 
 Ingredients : 
 Crust : 
 ¾ cup  graham cracker  crumbs 
 2 tbsp melted  butter 
 2 tbsp Splenda 
 Filling : 
 8 oz 1%  cottage cheese 
 8 oz light tub style  cream cheese , room temperature 
 8 oz fat-free  cream cheese , room temperature 
 1 cup light  sour cream 
 1 cup  Splenda  (sucralose) 
 2 large  eggs 
 3 large  egg whites 
 2 tbsp  tequila 
 2 tbsp  triple sec 
 2 tbsp fresh  lime juice 
 Directions : 
 Preheat the oven to 350°F. 
 Spray a 9 inch round cake pan with nonstick  cooking spray . 
 Then wrap pan tightly with aluminum foil (you need foil so  water  doesn't get in). 
 Mix the cracker crumbs,  butter , and Splenda and pat it into the bottom of the prepared 9 inch round pan. 
 Bake the crust for 10 minutes. 
 Cool it completely. 
 Place the pan in another 9 x 13 inch pan with 2-3 inch sides. 
 Lace the  cottage cheese  in the blender and puree it. 
 Spoon the mixture into a mixing bowl and add the  cream cheeses . 
 Beat with an electric mixer until it's creamy. 
 Add sour cream and Splenda and beat until smooth. 
 Beat in the eggs and  egg  whites one at a time. 
 Stir in  Don Julio ,  triple sec , and  lime juice . 
 Pour the batter into the crust. 
 Pour hot  water  into the outer baking dish so that it goes halfway up the sides of the cake pan. 
 Bake in  water  bath for 55 minutes or until it is set in the center. 
 Chill and serve. 
 Nutritional information : 
 170 calories | 6g fat | 14g carbs 
 Smiles from your guests: countless!
