Asparagus - Ham  Rolls
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 Cans  asparagus  Spears 
 6 Thin Slices  Ham 
 4 t  butter 
 4 t  flour 
 t  salt 
 pepper  to Taste 
 2 c  milk 
 lb  Cheese , Grated 
 6 Slices Toast 
 Directions : 
 Drain  asparagus  Spears 
 Divide Spears Equally Among  Ham  Slices. Roll Ham around  asparagus 
 Place Rolls, Seam Side Down, on Lightly Greased BakingSheet. Bake at 325F Until heated through. 
 Melt  butter  in Medium Saucepan. Blend in  flour ,  salt ,  pepper  and  milk . Simmer, stirring constantly, until sauce is thickened. Stir in  Cheese . 
 Place  asparagus  rolls on toast. 
 Top with  Cheese  Sauce. 
 Yield: 6 Servings
