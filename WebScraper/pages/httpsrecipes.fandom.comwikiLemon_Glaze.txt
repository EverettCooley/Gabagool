 Ingredients : 
 1 Â¾ cup  confectioner's sugar 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine , softened 
 2 tablespoons fresh squeezed  lemon juice , cold only 
 grated lemon zest 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl, gradually blend a small amount of  sugar  into softened  butter . 
 Add  lemon juice  and the remaining  sugar ; stir to blend well. 
 Top with the grated lemon zest. Reserve the fruit-based glaze in the refrigerator but spoon or scoop this into a fresh container. 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Servings:  3 teaspoons or 4 tablespoons per dessert
