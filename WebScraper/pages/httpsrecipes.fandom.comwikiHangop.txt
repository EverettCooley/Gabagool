Description : 
 The name of this dessert comes from the fact that it is left to hang (up) overnight.
 
 Contributed by  CorineJ  14:02, 23 Mar 2006 (PST) 
 Ingredients : 
 3 liters of  buttermilk 
 some  demerara sugar 
 2 or 3  Dutch rusks 
 cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Put a cotton tea towel which has been rinsed in cold  water  and wrung out in a colander. 
 Place the colander in a washing up bowl and pour in the  buttermilk . 
 Take up the corners of the tea towel, tie it closed and hang it up over the bowl. 
 Leave to hang overnight. 
 The next day, beat the thick mass that is left in the cloth with the  sugar  and  cinnamon , and sprinkle the crushed rusks over.
