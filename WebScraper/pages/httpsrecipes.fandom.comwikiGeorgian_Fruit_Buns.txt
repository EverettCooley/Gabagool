Ingredients : 
 6 tbsp  baking powder 
 2 tsp  cinnamon  ground 
 6¼ cups wheat  all-purpose flour 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  allspice 
 12 g  brown sugar 
 ½ cup  karo  white syrup 
 2 cups  raisins 
 2 cups  cream   or  half and half 
 ½ cup  cream cheese 
 4 cups  apples  diced ¼" 
 ⅓ cup  puréed prunes 
 ⅓ cup  apple sauce 
 ⅓ cup puréed  peaches 
 ⅓ cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Mix the dry ingredients together in a large mixing bowl. 
 Pour the syrup the purees and the  water  over the dry mixture. 
 Add the  cream cheese  in chunks. 
 Mix by hand using your hands by working your hands under the dry ingredients from the sides. 
 Use a folding motion and mix until all ingredients are thoroughly blended together. 
 Add the diced  apples  and the  raisins . 
 Mix them into the dough by the same folding method. 
 Add the  cream  or half and half and fold until completely blended. 
 Make 24 round balls of the dough. 
 Place on a well greased baking sheet slightly squash the top of each loaf and bake for 50 – 55 minutes in pre-heated 300°F oven.
