Description : 
 Date sweet
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  plain flour 
 75 ml (3fl oz)  oil 
 3  blanched almonds 
 1 teaspoon ground  pistachios 
 500 g (1 lb 2oz) pitted  dates 
 1 tablespoon ground  cardamom 
 Directions : 
 Heat the  oil  in a medium sized saucepan for 2 â€“ 3 minutes on medium heat, then fry the  almonds  until slightly brown. Remove  almonds  and set aside until required. 
 Now add the  flour  to the  oil , stirring constantly until  flour  becomes reddish brown in colour. 
 Add  dates , and keep stirring continuously for about a minute. 
 Turn of heat and mix very well until mixture is soft, then add the ground  cardamom  and mix.
