Description : 
 Cornflour gruel
 
 Ingredients : 
 225 g / 8 oz ogi (wet  cornflour ) 
 600 ml / 1 pt  water 
 Directions : 
 Bring the  water  to the boil, mix the ogi to a smooth paste with a little warm  water  in a pot, pour on the boiling  water  until thickens and changes color. 
 Stir and cook for 3 minutes sweeten to taste and serve hot with  akara .
