Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Baxter Estate in Heath, Texas in 1984. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound peeled  artichokes 
 1 pound  fresh fava beans 
 1 white  onion  finely chopped 
 ½ bunch  green onion  finely chopped 
 ½ bunch fresh  mint  leaves finely chopped 
 juice of 2  lemons 
 ⅓ cup  olive oil 
 1 bunch  dill  chopped 
 1 teaspoon  sugar 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 cup hot water 
 Directions : 
 Heat oil in a broad pot. 
 Stir in onion and green onion and allow them to soften. 
 Add fava beans, mint and half of the dill then stir for 4 minutes. 
 Add lemon juice, sugar and hot water. 
 Place artichokes in the pot pointy sides facing up. 
 Seal the pot with aluminum foil and place the lid on. 
 Cook on medium for 30 minutes. 
 Once you turn it off sprinkle with salt. 
 Let artichokes cool in the pot. 
 Place artichokes on serving plate and with a spoon stuff them with fava beans. 
 Sprinkle the rest of dill on top.
