 Description : 
       Chive-Cheese Butter     
 Ingredients : 
 1/4 lb Soft  butter  or  margarine 
 1/4 lb  Roquefort   Cheese 
 2 TSP Frozen chopped  chives 
 Directions : 
 Blend  butter ,  Cheese , and frozen chopped  chives  thoroughly. 
 Shape into a roll on waxed paper. 
 Refrigerate until ready to use.
