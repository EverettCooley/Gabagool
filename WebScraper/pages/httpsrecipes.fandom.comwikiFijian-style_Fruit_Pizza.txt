 Ingredients : 
 1 cup unsweetened  apple butter 
 1  kiwi fruit , peeled and sliced 
 6 large  strawberries , sliced 
 1  apple  or  pear , peeled, cored, and thinly sliced 
 ¼ teaspoon  cinnamon 
 pita 
 Directions : 
 Spread  apple butter  over pita bread. 
 Arrange slices of fruit on top of  apple butter . 
 Sprinkle with  cinnamon . 
 Serve as is or heat in 350°F oven for 15 minutes and serve warm.
