Burger Vegetable Soup
 
 1 Pound Ground Chuck 
 1 Cup Chopped  Onion 
 1 Can Whole  tomatoes  - (28 Oz) -- Chopped 
 3 Cups Diced  potatoes 
 1 Can Cut  green beans  - (16 Oz) 
 2 Teaspoons  chili powder 
 2 Dashes  cayenne pepper  Sauce - (to 3 Dashes) 
 2 Cans Condensed  Beef  Bouillon - (10 1/2 oz Ea) 
 1 Cup Chopped  celery 
 1 Cup Sliced  carrots 
 1 Teaspoon  salt 
 1 Teaspoon  worcestershire sauce 
 Brown Meat With  Onion  And  celery ; Drain Off Fat. Stir in Remaining Ingredients And Add 1 or 2 Cups  water . Cover And Cook on Low For 8-10 Hours.
