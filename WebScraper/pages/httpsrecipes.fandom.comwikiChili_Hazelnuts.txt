Ingredients : 
 2 cup Oregon  hazelnuts 
 1/5 cup  butter  or  margarine 
 3/4 tsp  chili powder 
 das  salt 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter  in sauce pan; stir in  chili powder ,  salt  and  hazelnuts . 
 Turn into shallow baking pan. 
 Bake in a 275 degree oven for 20 minutes.
