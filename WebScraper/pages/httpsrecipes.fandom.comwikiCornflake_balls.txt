 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  Peanut   butter 
 1/2 cup Honey 
 3 1/2 cup Cornflakes 
 1 large Bowl and a wooden spoon 
 Paper plates and a marker 
 sesame seeds  or  wheat germ 
 -or finely  ground nuts 
 Directions : 
 1 This snack is a real treat for children. 
2 They can eat it warm or cold. 
3 help the children measure the  Peanut   butter  and honey into a small pot. 
4 Stir constantly over medium heat until blended. 
5 Remove from heat. 
6 microwave directions: the children can measure the  Peanut   butter  and honey into a large microwave bowl. 
7 Microwave on high for 1 minute. 
8 Remove from micro. 
9 Let the children stir til smooth. 
10 help the children measure and add 3 Â½ cups cornflakes. 
11 They can take turns stirring the mixture with a spoon. 
12 if everyone is hungry and can"t wait, given them a paper plate and spoon and let them eat while its warm. 
13 to shape into balls, chill the dough for 30 minutes in the refrigerator. 
14 After the dough is stiff, put a portion on each child"s plate. 
15 They can pinch off small pieces, roll them into balls, and them roll the balls in  sesame seeds ,  wheat germ , or finely  ground nuts . 
16 store these snacks in the refrigerator on the children"s plates.
