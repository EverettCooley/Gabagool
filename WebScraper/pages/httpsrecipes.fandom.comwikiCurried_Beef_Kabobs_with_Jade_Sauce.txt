Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source:   Southern Living  2007 Cookoff Winner Party Starter category 
 Prep: 15 minutes + 10 minutes for sauce | Soak: 30 minutes | Grill: 6 minutes 
 Makes 12 servings and ⅔ cup of sauce 
 Ingredients : 
 12 x 6-inch  skewers 
 2 tbsp  peanut oil 
 1¼ tsp  garam masala 
 1¼ tsp roasted  garlic  and  sea salt 
 ¾ tsp  curry powder 
 ½ tsp  peppercorn  melange 
 ¼ tsp  cumin 
 1½ lb  flat iron steak  cut into 1-inch pieces 
 jade sauce 
 ½ cup sweetened flaked  coconut , toasted 
 garnish: fresh  cilantro  sprigs, fresh  mint  sprigs 
 Jade sauce : 
 ½ cup chopped fresh  cilantro 
 ¼ cup chopped fresh  mint 
 2 tbsp chopped  green onions 
 ¼ cup extra-virgin  olive oil 
 1 tsp grated  lime rind 
 1 tbsp fresh  lime juice 
 2 tsp seeded and minced  jalapeno pepper 
 2 tsp  honey 
 1 tsp minced  garlic 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Soak wooden skewers in water 30 minutes. 
 Preheat grill to 350°F to 400°F (medium high). 
 Whisk together peanut oil and next 5 ingredients in a large bowl until blended. 
 Add steak pieces, and toss to coat. 
 Thread beef onto skewers. 
 Grill beef, covered with grill lid, over medium-high heat 3 minutes each side. 
 Place skewers on serving platter, and drizzle with jade sauce. 
 Sprinkle with toasted coconut. 
 Garnish, if desired. 
 Jade sauce : 
 Process all ingredients in food processor 10 seconds or until thoroughly blended, stopping to scrape sides as needed.
