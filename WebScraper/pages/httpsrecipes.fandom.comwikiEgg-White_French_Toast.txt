Ingredients : 
 2  egg whites 
 ½ cup 2%  low-fat milk 
 ½ tsp  vanilla extract 
 ⅛ tsp  cinnamon 
 8 slices bread 
 Suggested toppings : 
 Apricot-Pineapple Syrup 
 Raspberry Syrup 
 Strawberry Syrup 
 Directions : 
 In medium bowl beat  egg  whites well. 
 Add  milk ,  vanilla  and  cinnamon  to  egg  whites and continue to beat. 
 Dip bread slices into batter and coat on both sides. 
 Cook one of the following ways:a) place dipped bread in greased and heated skillet. 
 Cook by turning a few times to brown. 
 B) place dipped bread on greased or oiled baking sheet. 
 Then broil in the oven (watching closely), turning only once (approximately 2–3 minutes each side when placed 5 inches from broiler top). 
 Serve French toast hot with syrup, sauce or topping!
