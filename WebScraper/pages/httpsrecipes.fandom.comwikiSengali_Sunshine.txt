 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 California (thick-skinned)  orange 
 2 cups  orange juice 
 6 oz.  white rum 
 2 oz.  curaçao 
 4 oz  light cream 
 1 cup crushed  ice 
 orange  slices (garnish) 
 fresh  strawberries  (garnish) 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Peel 1 CALIFORNIA (thick-skinned)  orange  rind very thinly and close to the edge, avoiding the white pulp, and chop finely. Place the rind in the blender with: 2 cups  orange juice , 6 oz.  white rum , 2 oz.  curaçao , 4 oz.  light cream  and 1 cup CRUSHED  ice . Blend until thoroughly smooth and thick. Pour into 4 ounce glasses. Garnish with  orange  SLICES and FRESH  strawberries .
