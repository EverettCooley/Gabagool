 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  rye flour 
 ½ cup  vegetable oil 
 ⅔ cup warm  water 
 ½ cup white  flour 
 ¼ cup  cornmeal 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients well. 
 I usually add about ¼ tsp either  vanilla  or  mint  flavor. 
 Roll out to ¼" thick. 
 Cut into shapes (i usually use about a 3 – 4" bone-shape cutter). 
 Bake on lightly greased cookie sheet for 30 minutes at 350°F.
