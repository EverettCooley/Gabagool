 Description : 
 Mercimek Salatasi -  Lentil  Salad
 
 Ingredients : 
 3/4 cup  Lentils 
 2 cups hot  water 
 1 bunch (approx one dozen)  green onions 
 1/2 cup chopped  Parsley 
 salt ,  pepper 
 1/2 cup  vinegar  and  salt  (1/4 cup of each) 
 ripe olives 
 Directions : 
 Soak  Lentils  overnight then wash and boil in 2 cups of hot  water  until they are quite soft but not mushy. Drain  Lentils  and place them in a bowl, add  green onions  cut into small pieces and all other ingredients. Mix well, garnish with  ripe olives .
