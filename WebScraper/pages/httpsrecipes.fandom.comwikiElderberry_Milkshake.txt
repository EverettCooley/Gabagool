Ingredients  : 
 500 milliliters elderberry juice 
 400 milliliters milk 
 100 milliliterslemon  buttermilk 
 ice cubes 
 lemon zest  garnish  (to taste) 
 Preparation steps : 
 1.
 Puree the elderberry juice, milk, lemon buttermilk, and the sugar in a blender. 
 2.
 Pour the shake into chilled glasses. Garnish with the lemon zest and serve immediately.
