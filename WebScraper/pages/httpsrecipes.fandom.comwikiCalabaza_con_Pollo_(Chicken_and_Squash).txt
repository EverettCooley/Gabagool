 Ingredients : 
 1  Chicken , cut in small pieces 
 3 T  oil 
 2 Medium Mexican  Squash , diced 
 1/2 t  cumin  seed, mashed 
 1-2 cloves  garlic , mashed 
 1/4 t  black pepper 
 1  Onion , chopped 
 1/2 C  tomato  sauce (or 1 C stewed  tomato ) 
 1 Small  bell pepper , sliced 
 1 1/2 C  water 
 16 ounces of Frozen Corn or Fresh if available 
 1 tsp of tomato flavored Chicken Buillion 
 Directions : 
 Brown  Chicken  in  oil  cooking thoroughly.
Drain excess  oil  and stir in  Squash .
Add all remaining ingredients and stir well.
Cook for 25-35 minutes. over low heat.
Substitution:
You can substitute 4  Chicken  breasts for the  Chicken . If there is too much broth boil for additional 6 minutes uncovered.
