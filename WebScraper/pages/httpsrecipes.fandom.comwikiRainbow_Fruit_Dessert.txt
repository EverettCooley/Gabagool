Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups seedless  Green Grapes 
 2  nectarines  sliced 
 1  kiwi  peeled & sliced 
 1/3 cup  orange juice 
 2 tbsp.  lemon juice 
 1 1/2 tbsp.  honey 
 1/4 tsp.  ground ginger 
 dash of  nutmeg 
 1 large  mango  diced 
 Directions : 
 Layer fruit in glass serving bowl. Just before serving pour on Honey- orange  Sauce. For sauce combine all other ingredients in bowl and mix well.
