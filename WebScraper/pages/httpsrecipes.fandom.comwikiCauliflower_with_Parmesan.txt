Description : 
 In Romanian: Conopida cu parmezan.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large  cauliflower 
 3 tablespoons  butter 
 3 – 4 tablespoons grated  Parmesan  (or other  cheese ) 
 1 tablespoon  bread crumbs 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Wash the  cauliflower , divide into flowerets and start to boil in salted  water . 
 Drain, place into a heat-resistant dish and sprinkle with Parmesan. 
 Then sprinkle with bread crumbs and pour melted  butter . 
 Bake for 15 – 20 minutes. 
 Serve hot.
