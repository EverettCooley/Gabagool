Description : 
 From my Aunt Barbara’s Collection. Her notation says this was Uncle Bob’s most requested bean recipe. Dated 1919.
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups small dry  red beans  or dry  kidney beans 
 1 cup uncooked  rice 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 2 tablespoons  sake  or dry wine 
 1 tablespoon toasted  black sesame seeds 
 Directions : 
 Rinse beans, cover with cold  water  and let soak 4 hours or overnight. 
 Drain, cover with fresh  water  plus  salt . 
 Boil, reduce heat and simmer until tender about 1½ hours. 
 Drain but retain liquid. 
 Prepare  rice  by using bean liquid plus enough  water  to make 1½ cups liquid. 
 Cover and bring to boil. 
 Add beans and bring to boil again, reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes. 
 Add  sake . 
 Mix gently and remove from heat and let sit until  sake  is absorbed. 
 Cool.
