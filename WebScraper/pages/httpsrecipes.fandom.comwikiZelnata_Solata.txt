Desription : 
 Slovenian cold slaw
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups shredded  cabbage 
 2 tablespoons minced  onion 
 3 tablespoons  cider vinegar 
 4 tablespoons  salad oil 
 1 to 2  potatoes , sliced 
 1½ teaspons  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  caraway seeds 
 Directions : 
 Pour  oil  and  vinegar  over the  cabbage . 
 Add  potatoes ,  salt ,  Onion ,  caraway seeds , toss and serve.
