Ingredients : 
 ½ kg  mango 
 ½ tin  Milkmaid 
 ½ kg  curd 
 100 g  paneer 
 1 tsp  mango  essence 
 Directions : 
 Put the curd in a muslin cloth and allow to hang for 10 minutes. 
 Press to make sure the excess  water  is removed. 
 Peel off skin of the  mango  and take out the pulp. 
 Mash the paneer till smooth and creamy. 
 Blend  mango  pulp,curd, with Milkmaid and  mango  essence in a blender to make it smooth. 
 Chill till almost frozen.
