 Description : 
 In Romanian: Pesmeti cu amoniac
 
 Ingredients : 
 2  eggs 
 1 teaspoon ammonia powder 
 1/2 cup  Sugar 
 1/2 cup  oil 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 flour  as needed 
 Directions : 
 Cream the  sugar  with the yolks, add the  oil , mix, then add the ammonia powder and mix again. 
 At the end add the  vanilla . 
 Knead well with  flour  to obtain a soft dough. 
 Cover with a cloth and let sit for 1 hour. 
 Then roll a sheet about 1/2Â inch thick and cut into various shapes with cookie cutters. 
 Bake until golden. 
 They are better after a day or two.
