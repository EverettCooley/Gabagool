Description : 
 Lobster Soup is, of course, the king of soups. It is for special occasions and when you need an outstanding dish to start your menu. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  rock lobster  tails 
 1½ quarts  water 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 cup  onions 
 4 oz  butter 
 2 medium  tomatoes , cut into small wedges 
 1 clove  garlic , mashed 
 1 tsp salad herbs 
 ½ tsp  nutmeg 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  pepper 
 1 piece  lemon peel 
 1 cup dry  white wine 
 ½ cup  light cream 
 Directions : 
 In a 2-quart saucepan, simmer until tender the rock lobster tails in 1½ quarts  water  and 1 tsp  salt . 
 Reserve  water . 
 In a 3-quart saucepan, sauté the  onions  in  butter  until soft but not brown. 
 Add  tomatoes , mashed  garlic , salad herbs,  nutmeg ,  salt ,  pepper  and  lemon peel . 
 Simmer for 15 minutes. 
 Add 1 cup dry  white wine . 
 Heat until it bubbles. 
 Add 1 quart  water  from rock lobster. 
 Cook for 5 minutes longer. 
 Put mixture through food mill or sieve. 
 Add: the lobster meat chopped finely and  light cream . 
 Correct the seasoning. 
 Serve in soup cups.
