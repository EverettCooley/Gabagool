 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can gorton's clams, drained 
 1 tsp chopped  chives 
 1 cup  cottage cheese 
 1/4 tsp  salt 
 1/8 tsp  thyme 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients; mix well. Serve as a dip for raw vegetables. Makes approximately 1-1/3 cups
