Description : 
 In Romanian: Iahnie de cartofi.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs / 1 kg  potatoes 
 2  onions 
 5 tablespoons  oil 
 1 teaspoon  paprika 
 2 tablespoons  tomato sauce 
 1 teaspoon  flour 
 1  bay leaf 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Peel the  potatoes , wash and cut in medium sized pieces. 
 Lightly fry in a pan with hot  oil , adding the finely chopped  onions . 
 When the  onions  starts to turn yellow, sprinkle the  flour , mix with the  potatoes , adding  tomato  sauce,  paprika ,  salt  and  bay leaf . 
 Pour  water  to cover and simmer until done. 
 There should be very little liquid left.
