 Ingredients : 
 9 strips  bacon 
 3 cups  half and half 
 4 large  eggs 
 5 slices  bread , cut into small bite size pieces 
 4 Â½ teaspoons fresh grated  Parmesan cheese 
 Directions : 
 In frying pan, fry the  bacon  over medium heat until these are crispy. 
 Drain the  oil  out of the  bacon  onto a paper towel and crush. 
 Put this on the side. 
 In a pot, heat up the half and half over medium heat until it is simmering. 
 Separate the  egg  whites from the yokes and poach the  egg  whites until they are cooked. 
 Put the bread into a deep bowl, and take the  eggs  out of the pot and put them on top of the bread. 
 Sprinkle the top with  bacon  and  cheese . 
 Pour the poaching liquid over the  eggs  and bread and serve right away.
