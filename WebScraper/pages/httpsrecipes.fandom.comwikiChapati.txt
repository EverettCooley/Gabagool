      Chapati     
 The daily bread of India 
 An unleavened pancakelike bread usually made from a simple mixture of whole-wheat atta flour and water. The dough is rolled into thin rounds and baked on a griddle for one minute. 
 Store-bought  chapati  should be reheated and wrapped in a towel to keep warm while eating.
