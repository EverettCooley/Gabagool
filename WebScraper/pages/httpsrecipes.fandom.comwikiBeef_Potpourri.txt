Makes 8 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 pounds lean ground  Beef 
 1 cup sliced  carrots 
 1 cup sliced  Onion 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 16-ounce can  bean sprouts , drained 
 1 1/2 cups sliced  celery 
 1 10-3/4-ounce can condensed  cream  of  Mushroom  soup 
 1 4-ounce can sliced  Mushrooms , drained 
 1/4 teaspoon  garlic salt 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 3 tablespoons  worcestershire sauce 
 2 fresh  tomatoes , sliced 
 1 cup 4 ounces shredded  Cheddar   Cheese 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Beef ,  carrots  and  Onion  in large oven-proof skillet until meat and vegetables are tender but not brown. 
 Stir in  rice ,  bean sprouts ,  celery , soup,  Mushrooms ,  salt ,  pepper  and  worcestershire sauce . 
 Arrange  tomato  slices over  rice  mixture; top with  Cheese . 
 Bake at 350 degrees 15 to 20 minutes
