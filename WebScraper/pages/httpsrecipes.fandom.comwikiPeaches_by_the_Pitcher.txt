Description : 
 Contributed by Lil at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: old newspaper clipping 
 about 2 quarts 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups peeled, sliced ripe, juicy  peaches 
 1 peach pit 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 2 tbsp  lime juice 
 1 (1 liter) bottle chilled  seltzer  water 
 1 (25.4 liter) bottle sparkling  white grape juice , chilled 
 fresh  raspberries , optional 
 Directions : 
 In bowl, toss peaches with sugar and lime juice. 
 Mash peaches with back of spoon or transfer to food processor or blender and purée 
 Place peaches in tall pitcher. 
 Add reserved pit for flavor. 
 Slowly add seltzer water and sparkling white grape juice. 
 Stir with long-handled spoon. 
 Pour into stemmed glasses, using spoon to distribute pulp evenly. 
 Garnish each wine glass with a few raspberries, if desired.
