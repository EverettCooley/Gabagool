Description : 
 Good with vegetables ( mushrooms ,  onions ,  bell peppers ,  squash , etc.). Also good with  shrimp , fish, crab and  crawfish  tails.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  flour 
 1 cup  beer 
 1  egg 
 1 tbsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 1 tbsp  mustard 
 2 cup  potato  chips, crushed 
 Directions : 
 Pour  beer  in  flour  until thin mixture. 
 Add 1 beaten  egg , Worcestershire, and  mustard . 
 Dip vegetable or fish in batter; then roll through crushed  potato  chips. 
 Drop into deep fryer at 300 °F. 
 Cook 3 to 4 minutes until light brown.
