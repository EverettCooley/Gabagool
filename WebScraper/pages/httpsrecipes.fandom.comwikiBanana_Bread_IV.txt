Description : 
 Recipe By: Public domain recipes converted from meal Master format
 
 Yields: 10 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  flour 
 1 cup  sugar 
 ⅓ cup  oil 
 2  eggs 
 1 teaspoon  baking soda 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 4  bananas 
 Directions : 
 Mix together the  oil  and  eggs . 
 Combine dry ingredients in a separate bowl. 
 Mash  bananas  and add alternately with dry ingredients to the  oil  and  egg  mixture. 
 Pour into greased loaf pan and bake at 350°F for 50 minutes.
