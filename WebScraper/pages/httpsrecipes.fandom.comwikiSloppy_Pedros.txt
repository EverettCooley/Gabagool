Description : 
 A TNT recipe
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ lb  ground beef 
 1 clove  garlic , crushed 
 1 can  kidney beans , drained 
 ½ cup  water 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 1 medium  onion , chopped 
 2 tsp  chili powder 
 1 can  tomato soup 
 4  hamburger buns , split and toasted 
 Directions : 
 Brown beef; drain. 
 Add onion, garlic and chili powder; cook 5 minutes. 
 Stir in remaining ingredients. 
 Simmer 10 minutes. 
 Serve on buns.
