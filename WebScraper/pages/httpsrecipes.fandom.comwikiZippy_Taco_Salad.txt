Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  ground beef 
 1 (15 oz) can  kidney beans , drained 
 ½ cup  picante sauce 
 ½ lb  Velveeta  pasteurized process cheese spread, cubed 
 tortilla chips 
 3 cups shredded  lettuce 
 3 cups chopped  tomatoes 
 Directions : 
 Brown meat; drain. 
 Stir in beans and sauce. 
 Cover; simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
 Add cheese spread; stir until melted. 
 For each serving; top chips with meat mixture, lettuce and tomato.
