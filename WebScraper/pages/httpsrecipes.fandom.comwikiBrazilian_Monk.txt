Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  Frangelico ®  hazelnut liqueur 
 1 oz  Kahlúa ®  coffee liqueur 
 1 oz dark  crème de cacao 
 ½ oz dry  sherry 
 4 tbsp vanilla ice cream 
 Directions : 
 Pour into a collins glass and blend briefly. 
 Garnish with a  mint  leaf and  cherry , and sprinkle with grated  chocolate .
