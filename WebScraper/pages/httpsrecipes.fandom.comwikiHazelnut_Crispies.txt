 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2  egg  whites 
 1/2 tsp  vanilla 
 3/4 cup shredded  coconut 
 2 cup  corn  flakes 
 1/2 cup chopped filberts 
 3/4 cup  Sugar 
 Directions : 
 1 Beat  egg  whites and  vanilla  until foamy, gradually add  Sugar  while beating; continue to beat until stiff and glossy. 
2 Fold in  corn  flakes,  nuts  and  coconut . 
3 Drop by level tbsp. onto well greased baking sheet. 
4 Bake at 350 degrees for 12 minutes. 
5 Remove immediately to cool on rack.
