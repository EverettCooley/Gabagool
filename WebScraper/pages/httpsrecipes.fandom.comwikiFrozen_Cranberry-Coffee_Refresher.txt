Description 
 Cranberry-Apple Juice, Canned, Unsweetened  by the US Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Cook Time : About 5 minutes 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 ¾ cup  cranberry-apple juice 
 1 tablespoon cold  coffee 
 1 scoop (⅓ cup) vanilla  ice milk 
 1 cup crushed  ice 
 ground  cinnamon  for garnish 
 Directions 
 Briskly stir all ingredients in a tall glass until blended. 
 Garnish with ground cinnamon.
