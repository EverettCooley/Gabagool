      Chicken Liver     Chicken liver is the largest organ of the chicken. Livers are available fresh in supermarkets and butcher shops or frozen. Look for liver that is bright in color, and has a fresh, clean smell. Chicken livers are high in protein and iron and more mildly flavored than beef or pork. It can be prepared in numerous ways.
