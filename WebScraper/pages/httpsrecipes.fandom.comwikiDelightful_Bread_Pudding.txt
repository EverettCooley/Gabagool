 Description : 
 Purchased from Taylor Estate in Mineral Wells Texas in 1984. Dated 1922
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  light brown sugar 
 4 slices bread, buttered on both sides 
 3 whole  eggs 
 2 cups whole  milk 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla  extract 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  cinnamon 
 ½ cup chopped  dates  (or can substitute  raisins ) 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Put  Sugar  in top of double boiler. 
 Cut buttered bread into one inch cubes and put on top of  Sugar . 
 Mix  eggs ,  milk ,  salt  and  vanilla  together and pour over cubes. 
 Do not stir. 
 Add chopped  dates  on top. 
 Sprinkle with  cinnamon  and  Sugar . 
 Cover tightly. 
 Cook over boiling  water  until firm, approximately 1-1/2 hours. 
 Bread will rise to top leaving the sauce on the bottom
