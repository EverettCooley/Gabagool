Ingredients : 
 2 heads of  romaine lettuce 
 4  roma tomatoes 
 1  onion 
 red wine vinegar 
 extra virgin  olive oil 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Thoroughly wash the  lettuce  and chopped into somewhat small pieces and place in a large salad bowl. 
 Slice the  tomatoes  and  onion  into very thin rings and place on top of the  lettuce . 
 Immediately before serving the salad add the dressing. 
 In this order add a few sprinkles of  salt , drizzle a small amount of  vinegar , then drizzle the  olive oil . 
 Do not over do it with the  oil  and  vinegar . 
 Just enough to very lightly glaze the salad's ingredients when mixed. 
 Mix the salad together and serve immediately.
