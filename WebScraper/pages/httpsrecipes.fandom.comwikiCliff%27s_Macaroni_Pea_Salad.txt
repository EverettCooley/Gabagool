      Cliff's Macaroni Pea Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 2 cup cooked  macaroni 
 1 cup frozen  peas 
 ¾ cup chopped  celery 
 ½ cup 97% fat-free  ham 
 ¾ cup Kraft® reduced fat  cheddar  cheese shredded 
 2 x hard-boiled  eggs  chopped 
 ¼ cup  sweet pickle relish 
 ½ cup fat-free  ranch salad dressing 
 ½ cup non-fat  mayonnaise 
 1 tsp  dry mustard 
 ¼ tsp  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Combine first 6 ingredients. 
 In a small bowl, combine remaining ingredients. 
 Add to  macaroni  mixture and toss to coat. 
 Refrigerate until ready to serve. 
 Chopped hard boiled  egg  can be added.
