 Description : 
 Prawns are a succulent and delicious seafood item that is loved and savoured by many all
around the globe. When cooked right they are tender and juicy you can enjoy them at almost all popular restaurants they are one of the most ideal food items. No matter where you try them or how you cook them, prawns taste amazing.
 
 Ingredients : 
 60gm  avocado 
 120gm  prawns 
 20gm  onion 
 1/2  lemon 
 60gm lolla rosa  lettuce 
 30gm frisse lettuce 
 10gm lettuce 
 20gm  Olive oil 
 20gm yellow  mustard 
 10gm  salt 
 10gm  pepper 
 1 Tbsp  vinegar 
 Directions : 
 In a large saucepan, over medium-high heat brings 1 cup of water to a simmer. 
 Add prawns onions and lemon wedges. 
 Reduce the heat and let it simmer until the prawns are cooked through. 
 Once its cooked drain and put them aside. 
 Cut avocado into slices. 
 In a small bowl, whisk vinegar, mustard, salt and pepper together 
 Add olive oil, and whisk or shake until emulsified 
 Mix the lettuce with the vinaigrette and toss to coat 
 Place the avocadoes and prawns on a plate and drizzle a little bit of vinaigrette over them. 
 Place the tossed lettuce next to the avocadoes and prawns and serve.
