 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  sunflower seeds  or  nuts 
 ½ cup  coconut 
 ½ cup  wheat germ 
 ½ cup  powdered milk 
 Mix:
 
 2/3 cup honey 
 2/3 cup  vegetable oil 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Stir until thoroughly coated. 
 Bake 20–25 minutes, stirring every 3–5 minutes until uniformly golden. 
 Do not overbake. 
 Press down to make it chunky.
