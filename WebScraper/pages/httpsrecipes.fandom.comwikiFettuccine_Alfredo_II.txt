Description : 
 Fettuccine Alfredo from Ristorante Alfredo Alla Scrofa, in Rome. This is the true version of  fettuccine  al fredo. No cream. Just  butter  and soft moist grated  Parmesan cheese .
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: my old recipes 
 Serves 4 to 6 
 Ingredients : 
 home made  fettuccine  noodles 
 1 cup  butter  or  margarine , softened 
 grated  Parmesan cheese 
 fresh ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Drop  fettuccine  in boiling salted  water  and cook until  fettuccine  rises to the surface, about 2 minutes. 
 Quickly drain and put in warm bowl container half the softened  butter . 
 Top with lumps of remaining  butter  and ⅓ to ½ cup  cheese . 
 Toss lightly, using fork and spoon, about 2 minutes until  fettuccine  is well coated and a creamy sauce is formed. 
 Serve sprinkled with  pepper  and more grated  cheese .
