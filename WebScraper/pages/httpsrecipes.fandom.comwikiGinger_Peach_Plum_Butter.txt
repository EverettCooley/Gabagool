 Description : 
 Makes 1 cup
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup diced fresh  peaches 
 ¾ cup diced fresh  plums 
 2 tbsp  water 
 ⅓ cup Equal  sugar substitute 
 ½ tsp  ground ginger 
 Directions : 
 Small batch preserves cook quickly without scorching in a microwave. 
 In medium sized microwaveable bowl, combine  peaches , plums and  water . 
 Microwave at high for 6 – 10 minutes, stirring frequently, or until fruit mixture is very thick. 
 Stir in equal and  ginger . 
 Spoon jam into a hot clean jar. 
 Cover and refrigerate for up to 1 month or freeze up to 3 months. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per 1 tbsp: 
 
 10 calories | 0.1g protein | 0.1g fat | 2.5g carbohydrate
