Ingredients : 
 2 bottles  beer 
 3  leeks  chopped 
 2  onions , chopped 
 3 sprigs  parsley 
 1 stalk  celery 
 1  bay leaf 
 6 tbsp  butter 
 3 tbsp  flour 
 2 lbs  shrimp 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Simmer  beer  with  leeks ,  onions ,  parsley ,  bay leaf  and  celery  15 minutes. 
 Strain out vegetables. 
 Melt  butter ; blend in  flour . 
 Add  beer  slowly, stirring constantly over low heat until thickened. 
 Add  shrimp ; simmer 5 minutes until turns pink; season to taste with  salt  and  pepper .
