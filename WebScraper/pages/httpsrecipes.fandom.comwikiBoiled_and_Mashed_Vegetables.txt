Ingredients : 
 washed, peeled, and cut into serving-sized pieces:
 sweet  cassava  tubers (also called  manioc , or yuca) 
 plantains 
 red potatoes  or white  potatoes 
 sweet potatoes 
 taro  (also called  cocoyam ) 
 yams 
 salt 
 Mashed vegetables : 
 beans with one of the vegetables listed above 
 beans and  corn  ( maize ) 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Heat  water  and  salt  (to taste) to boiling in a saucepan. 
 Add vegetables. 
 Cook until tender. 
 Serve with any main dish. 
 Mashed vegetables : 
 Heat  water  and  salt  (to taste) to boiling in a saucepan. 
 Add all ingredients. 
 Cook until tender. 
 Drain  water  from pan, and mash (like mashed  potatoes  or  refried beans ). 
 Season with any or all (or none) of the following: fried  onion ,  palm oil ,  cayenne pepper  or red pepper, hot sauce, ground dried  shrimp , dried fish, salted fish, or smoked fish.
