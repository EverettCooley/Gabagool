Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large  onion , chopped 
 1 pound of  sausage  links, skin removed 
 1 (4 ounces) can chopped  green chilis 
 2 (16 ounces) cans  refried beans 
 1 cup shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 1 cup shredded Monterey  jack cheese 
 1 teaspoon  chili powder  or to taste 
 Directions : 
 Crumble  sausage  in a large heavy skillet and cook just until the fat starts to gather in the pan. 
 Add  onion  and cook until  onion  is transparent; Drain. 
 Add remaining ingredients and cook until all is hot and the cheeses are melted. 
 Serve hot with tortilla chips or corn chips.
