Ingredients : 
 ¼ to ½ pound fresh  spinach 
 1 small  onion , thinly sliced 
 ½ cup thinly sliced  celery 
 ¼ cup sliced  radishes 
 3 hard-cooked  eggs , sliced 
 1½ cups cooked  rice , cooled 
 ¾ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 bottled  French dressing 
 Directions : 
 Wash  spinach  thoroughly. 
 Break off stems and measure 1 quart. 
 If leaves are large, tear into bite-sized pieces. 
 Shake  spinach  in towel to dry well. 
 Arrange attractively with other vegetables,  eggs , and  rice  in salad bowl. 
 Chill. 
 When ready to serve, season, add dressing and toss lightly.
