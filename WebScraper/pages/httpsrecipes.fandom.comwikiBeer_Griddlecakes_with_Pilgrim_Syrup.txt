 Ingredients : 
 1 3/4 	cup 	 all-purpose flour 
 1 1/2 	tsp 	 baking powder 
 1/2 	tsp 	 baking soda 
 1/2 	tsp 	 salt 
 1 		 egg 
 3 	tbsp 	 oil 
 1 	tbsp 	 molasses 
 1 		Bottle  beer 
 PILGRIM SYRUP 
 1 	cup 	Packed  brown sugar 
 1/2 	cup 	 beer 
 1 	tbsp 	 butter  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 For batter, mix dry ingredients. 
Beat  egg  with  oil  and  molasses . 
Add to dry ingredients along with  beer . 
Stir lightly, just until blended. 
Batter with be slightly lumpy and somewhat thick. 
Using about 2 tbs batter for each griddlecake, spoon batter onto hot and very lightly greased griddle. 
Spread with back of spoon to 3 ½ to 4 inches in diameter. 
Cook until browned, turning once. 
For syrup, combine ingredients in saucepan and boil for minutes. 
Makes 2/3 cups. 
Makes 20 griddlecakes, or 4-5 servings.
