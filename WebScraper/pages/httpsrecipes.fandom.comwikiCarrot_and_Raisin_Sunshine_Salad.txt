Description 
 This salad is colorful and provides kids with many important nutrients, including vitamin A. It makes a good snack, side dish, or dessert.
 
 Encourage Your Kids to Eat More Fruits and and Veggies  by the US Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, public domain government resource—original source of recipe 
 Serves : 4 to 6 
 Ingredients 
 1 pound  carrots  (5-6) peeled and shredded 
 ½ cup  raisins 
 1 carton (8oz) low-fat vanilla  yogurt 
 4 to 6  iceberg lettuce  leaves 
 Directions 
 Mix all ingredients together except lettuce leaves in a mixing bowl. 
 Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 15 minutes. 
 Toss again before serving. Serve on lettuce leaves.
