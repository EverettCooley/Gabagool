Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz  apple schnapps 
 1 oz  cream of coconut 
 1 oz  half-and-half 
 1 slice  apple 
 1  cherry 
 Directions : 
 Mix schnapps,  cream of coconut , half-and-half, and 2 cups of crushed  ice  in an electric blender at a high speed. 
 Pour contents into a collins glass. 
 Decorate with an  apple  slice and a  cherry . 
 Serve with a straw.
