Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp  olive oil 
 1 medium  onion  ~ chopped 
 2 cloves  garlic 
 1 tsp  cumin 
 ½ tsp  cinnamon 
 1 cup sugar 
 ½ tsp  cayenne 
 1 can  tomato  ~ diced 
 2 medium  carrots  ~ chopped 
 2 stalks  celery  ~ chopped 
 1 cup  cauliflower  ~ chopped 
 1 medium  red bell pepper  ~ chopped 
 1 medium  turnip  ~ chopped 
 1 cup  chicken broth 
 1 pound  chicken  breast ~ strips 
 1 medium  zucchini  ~ dials 
 1 cup  water 
 1 cup whole wheat  couscous 
 3 tbsp  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Saute in  oil ,  onion  and  garlic  for 5 mintues. 
 Stir in seasonings and sugar. 
 Add  tomatoes ,  carrots , celery,  cauliflower ,  pepper ,  turnip  and broth. 
 Boil, reduce heat and simmer 15 minutes. 
 Add  chicken  and  zucchini , 10 more minutes. 
 Cook couscous as directed on package, fluff with fork, stir into mixture and serve.
