Description : 
 In Romanian: Snitel din carne de porc
 
 Ingredients : 
 1¾ lbs / 750 g  leg meat 
 1 teaspoon  oil 
 2  eggs 
 2 – 3 tablespoons  lard 
 flour 
 bread crumbs 
 pepper 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Cut the meat in medium sized pieces, about a finger thick. 
 Lightly pound with the wooden hammer,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Dredge each  pork  slice with the  eggs  beaten with a teaspoon of  oil ,  flour ,  egg  again and finally, bread crumbs. 
 Fry on both sides in hot  lard . 
 Serve with French fries, sautéed vegetables or a salad.
