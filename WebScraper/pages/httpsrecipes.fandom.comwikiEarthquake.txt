Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ oz Appleton Estate 12 year old  Jamaica rum 
 ½ oz Seagram's extra  dry gin 
 ¼ oz  Anisette 
 Directions : 
 Shake with shaved or crushed  ice . 
 Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
