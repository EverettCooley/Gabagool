 Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz  Bourbon 
 1/2 oz  Benedictine 
 Glassware:  brandy  Snifter Glass 
 Directions : 
 Pour the  Bourbon  and  Benedictine  into a  brandy  snifter and stir.
