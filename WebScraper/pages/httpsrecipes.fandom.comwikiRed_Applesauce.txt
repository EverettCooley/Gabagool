Description : 
 This is a recipe I obtained from an Estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Manes Estate in Venus, Texas in 1981.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups  applesauce 
 1 cup  7-Up 
 1 small box  strawberry gelatin 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve gelatin in 7-Up then combine all ingredients in saucepan and bring to a light boil. 
 Boil for 3 minutes stirring constantly. 
 Chill and serve.
