Description : 
 Chickpeas and linguiça
 
 This recipe taken from www.Portuguese-recipes.com 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ lb of  chickpeas 
 ¼ lb of  linguiça , cut into 1 inch pieces 
 1 medium  onion , chopped 
 1½ tsp of  salt 
 1 tbsp of  vinegar 
 Directions : 
 Wash the  peas  and soak overnight in cold  water  enough to cover the  peas . 
 Drain the  peas  and pour them in a pan, cover with  water , add the linguiça along with the rest of the ingredients. 
 Bring all of this to a boil, reduce to low heat and cook for 3 hours.
