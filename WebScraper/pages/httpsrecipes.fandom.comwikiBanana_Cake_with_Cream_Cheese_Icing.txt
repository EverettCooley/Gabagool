 Description : 
 A fruity tasting cake with  cream cheese  icing as the finishing touch !
 Enjoy ! 
 
 Ingredients : 
 Cake:
 
 2-cups  all-purpose flour 
 1 1/2-tsp  baking powder 
 1-tsp  baking soda 
 1/2-tsp  salt 
 1 1/2-cups  granulated sugar 
 1/2-cup  margarine 
 1/4-cup  buttermilk  or  sour milk 
 1-cup mashed ripe  bananas  (3-4  bananas ) 
 2- eggs 
 1-tsp  vanilla 
 1/4-cup  buttermilk  or  sour milk 
 Frosting:
 
 1-package (250g each)  cream cheese 
 1/4-cup  margarine 
 1 1/2 cups  icing sugar , sifted 
 Directions : 
 Cake:
 Combine  flour ,  baking powder ,  baking soda , and  salt  in a large mixer bowl
Add  Sugar ,  margarine , 1/4-cup  buttermilk , and  banana 
Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes
Add  eggs ,  vanilla , and 1/4-cup  buttermilk 
Beat on medium speed for 1 minute
Spread batter evenly in 2 greased 9" round cake pans
Bake at 350* for 30 t0 40 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean
Cool 10 minutes, then remove from pans and cool completely
 Frosting:
Beat  cream cheese  and  margarine  together on medium speed of electric mixer
Add  icing sugar , beating until smooth
Frost cake layers
