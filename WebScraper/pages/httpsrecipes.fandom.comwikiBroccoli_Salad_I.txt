 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 Fresh  Broccoli  (l head) 
 1/2 Pack of Shredded  Cheddar   Cheese 
 1/2 Pack of  sunflower seeds  (Shucked) 
 4 oz.  Raisins 
 1/4 of Purple  Onion  (minced fine) 
 1 cup  Mayonnaise 
 2 Tablespoon Honey 
 2 Tablespoon  vinegar 
 Directions : 
 Cut  Broccoli  into small flowerettes and peel stalk of  Broccoli  and cut into small pieces. Add seeds,  Raisins   Onion  and  Cheese  to  Broccoli . Mix  Mayonnaise , honey and  vinegar . Add dressing mix to  Broccoli  mix.
