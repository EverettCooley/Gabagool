 About Sierra Leone bologi : 
 Wikipedia Article  About Sierra Leone Bologi on Wikipedia 
 Crassocephalum is a genus the common names of whose members include ragleaf, thickhead, and bologi. Several species are raised as leaf vegetables and used for medicine, especially in West Africa.
 It is a shade tolerant perennial vine grown especially in Sierra Leone, often on trellises. Its  spinach -like leaves are often eaten steamed.
