 Description : 
 2 racks of med ribs,13 rump steaks. 13 hours, 30 minutes cooking time.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 	 bottle  soy sauce 
 1/2 	bottle  Teriyaki Sauce 
 1/2 	bottle  Worcestershire Sauce 
 1 	tablespoon  vinegar 
 garlic , fresh but  garlic powder  and  salt  works the same 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Always add  vinegar  first then add in everything. 
 Pour half the bottles of all the liquids in the bottom of a long plastic dish and add  garlic  "except  vinegar  only use about 1 cap full". 
 Its used to take the salty taste of the soy out then add ur meat i use a rack of ribs then pour. 
 The rest of the liquids in on top of the meat. 
 Take a fork and stab the meat so the liquds soak in the meat and bone then put a lid on it and shove it in the fridge and let it marinade for a good 12 hours.
