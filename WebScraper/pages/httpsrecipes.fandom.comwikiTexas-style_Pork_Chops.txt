Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Original recipe 
 Ready in: 30 – 60 minutes 
 Serves/Makes: 6 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup seasoned  breadcrumbs 
 flour, for dredging 
 3 tablespoons  chili powder 
 ½ teaspoon  seasoned salt 
 1 teaspoon black pepper 
 1 large  egg 
 ¼ cup regular or  fat-free milk 
 6 bone-in  pork rib chops  (about 7 ounces or 2 pounds each, ¾ inch thick) 
 hot sauce, optional 
 Directions : 
 In a shallow bowl, combine the bread crumbs, chili powder, black pepper powder, and the seasoned salt. 
 In another shallow bowl, combine the egg and milk. Dip the chops in egg mixture after dredging with flour, then coat with crumbs. 
 Transfer to a 15" x 10" x 1" baking pan coated with the nonstick cooking spray. 
 Bake at 350°F for 20 – 25 minutes or until a meat thermometer reads 160° F. Serve hot and plain with rice. Drizzle with the hot sauce, if desired. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving (1 pork chop):
 
 299 calories | 12 g fat (4 g saturated fat | 120 mg cholesterol | 448 mg sodium | 12 g carbohydrate | 1 g fiber | 34 g protein. 
 Diabetic Exchanges: 4 lean meat | 1 starch.
