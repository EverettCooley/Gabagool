Violet Lawlor's Baked  ziti 
 
 1 Lb.  ziti , Cooked And Drained (large Tubular  macaroni ) 
 1 Container  ricotta cheese  (15 Oz) 
 8 Oz.  mozzarella   Cheese , Shredded 
 1  egg , Slightly Beaten 
 1 T.  salt , or to Taste 
 3 1/2 Cups  tomato  Sauce, Divided(preferably Homemade) 
 1/4 Cup Grated  Parmesan cheese 
 
Mix  ziti , Ricotta,  mozzarella ,  egg ,  pepper ,  salt  And 11/2 Cups Sauce. in 2 1/2 Quart Shallow Baking Dish, Spread 1/2 Cup  tomato  Sauce, Then  macaroni  Mixture. Top With Remaining 1 1/2 Cups  tomato  Sauce; Sprinkle With  Parmesan cheese . Bake in Preheated 375 Oven For 30 Minutes or Until Sauce is bubbly And Top Lightly Browned. Let Stand 10 Minutes Before Cutting. Serves 8
