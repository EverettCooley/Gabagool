Description : 
 This recipe comes from an Estate Sale and were obtained when  The Family Collection  from The Wetzel Estate in Plano, Texas was purchased in 1982. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 Cups shredded  cabbage 
 1½ Cups miniature marshmallows 
 1 Cup chopped  apple 
 ½ Cup  raisins 
 ¼ Cup  peanuts 
 Salad  dressing 
 Directions : 
 Combine  cabbage , marshmallows,  apples ,  raisin , nuts and enough salad dressing to moisten 
 Toss lightly and chill for 1 hour.
