 Description : 
 Purchased from a garage sale in 1988. Date and source unknown.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds dried  Swedish brown beans  (Iâ€™m not really sure what these are) 
 2 teaspoons  salt 
 1 pound lean  salt   Pork , cut in cubes 
 1-cup  Dark molasses 
 1 stick  cinnamon 
 1/4-teaspoon ground  nutmeg 
 1 medium white  Onion , sliced 
 Directions : 
 Wash beans and soak overnight in cold  water  to cover. 
 Add  salt  and  salt   Pork  and simmer for 2 hours. 
 Stir in remaining ingredients. 
 Place in deep bean pot. 
 Bake covered in 325 oven for 5 hours, adding more  water  if beans become too dry.
