Description : 
 I have used this same thing on  chickens  too as well  Guinea hens . All came out well as did the  quail  I used it with.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4  Cornish game hens  (optional rinse hens and pat dry) 
 4  tangerines , peeled, seeded, and chopped 
 1 cup fresh squeezed  orange juice 
 ¼ cup  Zinfandel  wine 
 1 tsp or  salt  to taste 
 4 tbsp  butter 
 ¼ cup minced  onion 
 ¼ cup  mushrooms  chopped 
 4 tbsp  pecans  chopped 
 1 tsp  soy sauce 
 1 clove  garlic  minced 
 1 tbsp  honey 
 Directions : 
 Mix wine, soy sauce,  salt ,  honey  and  orange juice  together. 
 Rub 1 tbsp  butter  inside each hen. 
 Sprinkle the cavity with  salt . 
 Mix the  onion ,  garlic ,  pecans ,  mushrooms , and tangerines together. 
 Stuff each hen with this mixture. 
 Heat the liquid mixture until it begins to boil. 
 Turn off and let cool for 5 minutes. 
 Pour 2 tbsp of this into each hen. 
 Preheat oven to 325°F then bake hens for 45 minutes. 
 Be sure to baste every 10 minutes with the liquid mixture. 
 Test birds. If not done. Cook until done.
