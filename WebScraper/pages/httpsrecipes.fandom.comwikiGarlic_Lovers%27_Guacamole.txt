Description : 
 A Spicy variation of Guacamole for  garlic  lovers.
 
 Ingredients : 
 avocado 
 1  garlic  - medium to large clove crushed (per  avocado ) 
 dash of  cayenne pepper 
 Nature's Seasoning (to taste) 
 Directions : 
 Smash  avocado (s). 
 Mix in  garlic . 
 Add  cayenne pepper  and Nature's Seasoning and serve.
