 Description : 
 Melting  brie  with the best baked  apples  you can make is an enjoyable gourmet snack.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 large  Granny Smith apples 
 2 tbsp  unsalted butter 
 1 large wheel  brie , unripe 
 1 medium  onion 
 ½ cup  raisins 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 ¼ cup  honey 
 ⅓ cup  sliced almonds  -- lightly toasted 
 ½ cup  grapes  -- sliced in half 
 1 loaf crusty  bread  -- sliced 
 Directions : 
 Peel, quarter and slice  apples . 
 Sprinkle the apples with  lemon juice  to prevent discoloring; set aside. 
 Melt  butter  over medium heat in a large saucepan. 
 Chop the onion. 
 Add  onions  and sauté until lightly brown and caramelized. 
 Remove from pan to a plate; set aside. 
 Add  honey  and  sugar  to pan and stir until it has liquified. 
 Add  apples  and continue stirring until  apples  are almost cooked. 
 Add  grapes  and  raisins . 
 Continue cooking until  sugar  has caramelized. 
 Pour the  apple  mixture into a 2 quart ceramic souffle dish or other ovenproof dish. 
 Sprinkle with the toasted  almonds . 
 Cut  brie  into 1" chunks and spread evenly over the  apple  mixture. 
 Bake uncovered at 400°F for 12–15 minutes or until  cheese  has melted and turned lightly golden.
