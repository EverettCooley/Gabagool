Ingredients : 
 1 3/4 cups  cream 
 2 tablespoons  buttermilk 
 Directions : 
 Stir cream and buttermilk in a pint jar. Cover and shake. 
 Let stand at room temperature, uncovered, 6 hours to overnight, then chill well. 
 When ready to serve sour cream, beat to medium-stiff peaks - not stiff. Whipped mixture should double in volume. 
 Serve immediately 
 Notes : 
 Makes a scant 4 cups, whipped.
