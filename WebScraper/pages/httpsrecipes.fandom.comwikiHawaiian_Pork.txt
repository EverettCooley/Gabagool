 Description : 
 This is a good casserole for taking to a thanksgiving potluck,something different than  Turkey .also very tasty.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups crushed  pineapple 
 3 medium  sweet potatoes 
 2 tablespoons  brown sugar 
 4  Pork  steaks (cut in half) 
 salt  and  pepper 
 4 slices  Bacon 
 Directions : 
 Place  pineapple  in a large baking dish. Peel and slice  sweet potatoes . Layer over  pineapple ,and sprinkle with the  brown sugar . Sprinkle  salt &  pepper  over  Pork  steaks& place over the  sweet potatoes . Arrange  Bacon  strips on top of meat. Cover casserole with foil,and bake at 350 degrees for about one hour,or till  sweet potatoes  and meat are tender. Remove cover,and turn temperature to 450 degrees,for the last 10 minutes of cooking. Till meat and  Bacon  are browned.
