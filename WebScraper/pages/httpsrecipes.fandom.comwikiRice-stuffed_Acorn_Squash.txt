 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3  acorn squash 
 2 bn  green onions , white part 
 -only, sliced 
 1 small  green pepper , seed and dice 
 2 tbsp  olive oil 
 3 cup cooked  rice 
 1/2 cup chopped  pecans 
 salt  and  pepper 
 1/2 cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 Directions : 
 Pierce the squaash, and microwave on high (100% power) for 8–10 minutes or until soft. Allow to cool, then cut in half lengthwise, cutting a thin horizontal slice off the bottom of each half to allow it to sit flat. Scoop out the seeds. Saute the  green onions  and  green pepper  in the  oil  until soft, then stir in the  rice , stirring well to coat. Add the  nuts  and saeson with  salt  and  pepper  to taste. Add the  Cheese . Pile the  rice  mixture into the  Squash  cavity and wrap each  Squash  in foil or plastic wrap ad freeze. To serve: un wrap as many halves as you need. Microwave on high in 3-minute increments until heated through. To heat in oven, set  Squash  halves in baking dish, cover with foil and bake at 350 °F for about 25 minutes, until heated through. Nutritional info per serving: 308 calories; 39% calories from fat
