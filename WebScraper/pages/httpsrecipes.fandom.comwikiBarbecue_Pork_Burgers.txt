Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 4 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ pound  ground pork 
 ½ cup barbecue sauce 
 1 tbsp  Dijon mustard 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 2 oz sliced  Monterey jack  or  muenster  cheese 
 4 hamburger buns, split, grilled or toasted 
 1½ cups coleslaw 
 Directions : 
 Prepare grill. 
 In a bowl, mix pork, ¼ cup bbq sauce, mustard, salt and pepper. 
 Divide mixture into 4 patties. 
 Grill burgers until browned and cooked through, brushing them with the remaining bbq sauce. 
 Place cheese on burgers during last 2 min. 
 Of cooking time, with grill covered, to melt cheese. 
 Place each burger on bottom of each hamburger bun, top with coleslaw. 
 Serve with potato chips and pickles.
