Ingredients : 
 4 T.  margarine 
 1/4 cup  flour 
 1-1/2 cups  milk 
 1 cup grated  Cheddar  or  Swiss cheese 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Melt margarine in pan. 
 Stir in flour and cook for a few minutes. 
 Slowly add milk, stirring constantly. 
 Cook until mixture thickens. 
 Add sour cream and cheese and stir until melted.
