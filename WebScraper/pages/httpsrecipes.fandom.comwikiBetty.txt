Baked puddings made of layers of sugared and spiced fruit and buttered bread crumbs.
 As in  Apple Brown . A baked pudding with sweetened fruit and buttered bread crumbs on the top. Similar to bread pudding, made with bread cubes. It is often associated with autumn when apples are in season but can be made with summer fruits, too.
