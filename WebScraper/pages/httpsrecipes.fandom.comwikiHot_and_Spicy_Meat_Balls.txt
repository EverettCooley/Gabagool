Description : 
 Contributed by Jenn B aka Mom2Sam and Tiny at  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Yields: about 3 dozen meat balls 
 Ingredients : 
 Sauce : 
 ¾ cup of  ketchup 
 ½ cup of  water 
 ¼ cup of  cider vinegar 
 2 tablespoons of  brown sugar 
 1 tablespoon of minced  onion 
 2 teaspoons of  Worcestershire sauce 
 1½ teaspoons of  salt 
 1 teaspoon of  dry mustard 
 ¼ teaspoon of  pepper 
 3 drops of  Tabasco sauce 
 a pinch of  cayenne pepper  (or to taste) 
 Meat balls : 
 ¾ lb of  ground beef 
 ¾ cup of fine  breadcrumbs 
 ¾ teaspoon of  salt 
 ½ teaspoon of  pepper 
 1½ tablespoons of minced  onion 
 ½ teaspoon of  horseradish 
 3 drops of  Tabasco sauce 
 2  eggs , beaten 
 1 tablespoon of  butter  or  margarine 
 Directions : 
 Mix the sauce ingredients together in bowl and set aside. 
 Mix the meat ball ingredients and shape into ¾ inch balls. 
 Melt  butter  or  margarine  in a skillet and brown evenly 
 Once the meat balls are browned, drain the excess grease and pour the sauce over meat balls and cover. 
 Cook 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
 Keep hot until ready to serve.
