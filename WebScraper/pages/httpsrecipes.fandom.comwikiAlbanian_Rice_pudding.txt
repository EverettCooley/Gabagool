 Description : 
 Rice  pudding is a dessert enjoyed by people of different cultures all over the world. It is made by combining  rice  with a sweetener and other ingredients often including  milk . Those who prize  rice  pudding often regard it as a comfort food. Others find some of the varieties to be unpleasantly bland and glutinous.
 
 Ingredients : 
 One quart of  milk 
 1/2 teacup of  rice 
 2 teaspoons of  Sugar 
 1/2 of a  nutmeg  grated 
 a small piece of  butter , size of hickory-nut. 
 Directions : 
 Pick and wash the  rice ; add all the ingredients. 
 Stir all well together, and put in a slack oven one and half to two hours. 
 When done pour it in a pudding dish, and serve when cold. 
 If baked in an oven, take off the brown skin before it is poured in the pudding-dish, and replace it on the Sop of the pudding as before.
