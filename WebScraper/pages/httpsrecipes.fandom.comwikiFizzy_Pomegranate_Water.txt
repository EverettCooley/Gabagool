Prep Time:  0 minutes.
 Cook time:  0 minutes.
 Serves:  4
 
 
 Description : 
 This is a great drink to quench your thirst! Fizzy Pomegranate Water is sweet and bubbly!
 
 Ingredients : 
 16 Ice Cubes 
 4 Cups Seltzer Water 
 0.25 Cup Pomegranate Juice 
 4 Lime Wedges 
 Directions : 
 Fill 4 Cups with 4 ice cubes each. 
 Add 1 Tablespoon of pomegranate juice. 
 Fill the Remainder of the glasses with seltzer water. 
 Garnish with lime wedges (and straws) and serve.
