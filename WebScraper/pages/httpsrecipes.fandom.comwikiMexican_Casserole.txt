Description : 
 From my Aunt Lois' collection. Date and source unknown. She indicates on the card that this was a family favorite. 
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds  ground beef 
 2 cans  cream of mushroom  soup 
 ½ cup  water 
 2 small cans  green chilies 
 1 can hot  enchilada sauce 
 1 white  onion , chopped fine 
 1  green bell pepper , chopped fine 
 dash of  garlic salt 
 1 pound grated  cheddar  cheese 
 1 pound  tortillas , quartered 
 Directions : 
 Brown  meat  with everything except  cheese  and tortillas. 
 Layer  meat ,  cheese , tortillas, beginning with  meat  on bottom of large dish or pan. 
 Cook at 350°F for 30 – 45 minutes.
