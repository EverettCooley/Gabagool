Ingredients 
 2 cups boiling  water  to melt Jell-O in a big pot 
 3 boxes orange Jell-O 
 3 boxes strawberry Jell-O 
 3 boxes sugar-free pineapple Jell-O 
 1 can undiluted frozen  orange juice 
 1 can frozen  strawberries  in sugar 
 4 cup  rum 
 2 cup  coconut rum 
 Directions 
 Blend the OJ and strawberries before mixing with other ingredients.
