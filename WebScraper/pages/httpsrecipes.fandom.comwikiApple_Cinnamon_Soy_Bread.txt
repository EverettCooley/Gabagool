Description : 
 Yield: 1½ pound loaf (16 slices) 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups  bread flour 
 ⅓ cup  soy flour 
 3 tbsp  sugar 
 1½ tsp  cinnamon 
 1½ tsp  salt 
 1½ tbsp  margarine 
 ⅔ cup  oatmeal 
 1 cup chopped  apple , skin left on 
 ¾ cup vanilla  soymilk 
 ½ cup  apple juice 
 2 tsp  yeast 
 Directions : 
 Add ingredients to  bread  machine according to manufacturer's directions. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per slice: 
 
 152 calories | 2g fat | 0.3g sat fat | 5g protein | 28g Carbohydrate | 254mg Sodium | 0mg Cholesterol
