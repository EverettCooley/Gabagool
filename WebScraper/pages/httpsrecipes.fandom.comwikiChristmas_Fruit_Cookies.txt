Description : 
 Makes 6 dozen cookies. 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  shortening 
 ½ cup  brown sugar , firmly packed 
 2  eggs 
 ½ tsp  vanilla 
 ½ tsp  soda 
 1½ tbsp  buttermilk 
 1 cup  flour 
 ¼ tsp  allspice 
 ¼ tsp  nutmeg 
 ¼ tsp  cinnamon 
 ¼ cup  bourbon 
 ½ lbs  white raisins 
 ½ lbs  candied cherries 
 ½ lbs  candied pineapple 
 ½ cup  flour 
 1½ cup  pecans , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Cream  shortening  and  sugar ; beat in  eggs  and add  vanilla . 
 Dissolve soda in  buttermilk . 
 Combine 1 cup  flour  and spices; add to creamed mixture alternately with  buttermilk  and  bourbon . 
 Beat well after each addition. 
 Coat fruit with ½ cup  flour ; stir fruit and  nuts  into batter. 
 Drop by teaspoonfuls onto greased baking sheets and bake at 350 °F for 10-15 minutes.
