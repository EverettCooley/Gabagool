Bacon and Tomato Bread
 Ingredients for a 2-lb loaf:
1 1/3 cups water
1/3 cup snipped dried tomatoes (oil-packed)
4 teaspoons olive oil or cooking oil
4 cups bread flour
1/3 cup cooked bacon pieces
4 teaspoons sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon dried basil, crushed
1 teaspoon active dry yeast or bread machine yeast
 Directions:
Select the loaf size. Add the ingredients to the machine according to 
manufacturer's directions. Select the basic white bread cycle.
