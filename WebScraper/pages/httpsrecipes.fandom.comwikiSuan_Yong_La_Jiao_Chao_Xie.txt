Description : 
 Fried crab with mashed  garlic  and  red pepper 
 
 Ingredients : 
 600 g (2)  crabs 
 ½  onion 
 1 tbsp mashed  garlic 
 2 tbsp mashed  garlic pepper paste 
 ½ tbsp  satay paste 
 2 whisked  eggs 
 Sauce : 
 2 tbsp  tomato sauce 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 ¼ tbsp  salt 
 ⅓ tbsp  cornflour 
 4 tbsp  water 
 Directions : 
 Cut crabs into pieces, wash and remove dirt and innards, crush the shell. 
 Mix crabs with ½ tbsp  cornflour , parboil in plenty of hot  oil , dish up. 
 Fry  onion , mashed  garlic  with few  oil , add satay paste, stir well. 
 Mashed  garlic pepper paste  then crabs, stir fry for 1 minute, add sauce. 
 Add whisked  egg , stir fry until cook, dish up.
