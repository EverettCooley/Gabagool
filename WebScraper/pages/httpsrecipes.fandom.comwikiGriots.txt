 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds  Pork , cut in cubes 
 1 cup chopped  onions 
 1/4 cup  chives , chopped 
 1/2 cup  lime juice 
 1/4 cup  water 
 pinch of  thyme 
 1/4 teaspoon  salt 
 1/4 teaspoon  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Put all ingredients in bowl and allow to marinate a few hours. 
 Drain. 
 Heat some  oil , brown meat, then add marinade and simmer over low heat 30 minutes covered. 
 Remove lid and increase heat to eliminate any liquid. 
 Serve hot.
