Description : 
 Purchased from the Zimmerman Estate in Terrell, Texas in 1967. Another tried and true extraordinary delight. 
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  unsalted butter 
 ½  zucchini , cut into small strips 
 1 tablespoon finely chopped  shallots 
 1 teaspoon finely chopped  parsley 
 2 cups  heavy cream 
 juice of 1  lemon 
 1 teaspoon  kosher salt 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 4 ounces cubed  smoked sockeye 
 1 ounce grated  Parmesan cheese 
 8 ounces  fettuccine  pasta, cooked 
 Directions : 
 Sauté  butter ,  zucchini , shallots and  parsley  until tender. 
 Add  cream ,  lemon juice ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Let sauce reduce until thick in consistency. 
 Add smoked sockeye and  Parmesan cheese . 
 Pour over  fettuccine  and serve immediately.
