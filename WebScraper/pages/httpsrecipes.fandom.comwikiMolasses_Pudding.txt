 Description : 
 A very nice type of Pudding, prepared in canadian style. Requires basic cooking skills and when ready the result will "speak" for itself ! 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 ½ cups  flour 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 ½ tsp  salt  ( omit if  salt   Pork  is used) 
 1 tsp  mixed spice 
 ½ cup  butter  or 1 cup  suet  (cut fine) 
 or ½  salt   Pork  ( cut fine) 
 1 cup  molasses 
 1 cup  milk  or  water 
 1 cup  raisins 
 1 cup  currants  (optional) 
 1/3 cup peel (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Sift  flour , soda,  salt , and spice. 
 Add  molasses  and  milk  ( or  water ). 
 Add fruit coated with  flour  and  butter . 
 Mix well. Steam for 2 ½ hours boiling constantly. 
 Serve with a sauce.
