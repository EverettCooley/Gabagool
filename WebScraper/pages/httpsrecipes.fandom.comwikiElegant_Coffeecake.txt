Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Barger Estate in Mckinney, Texas in 1997.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2½ cups  flour 
 ¾ cup  brown sugar 
 1 cup  granulated sugar 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon  nutmeg 
 ¾ cup  salad oil 
 1 tablespoon  cinnamon 
 1 cup  pecans  or  walnuts 
 1  egg  beaten 
 1 cup  buttermilk 
 1 teaspoon  soda 
 Directions : 
 Mix  flour ,  brown sugar ,  granulated sugar ,  salt  and  nutmeg  then add salad  oil  and mix well. 
 Remove ½ cup of this mixture then add to it the  cinnamon  and nuts and save for the topping. 
 To original mixture add  egg ,  buttermilk  and soda then pour into a cake pan and top with frosting mixture. 
 Cut through with a knife so frosting can seep into cake. 
 Bake 35 minutes at 375°F.
