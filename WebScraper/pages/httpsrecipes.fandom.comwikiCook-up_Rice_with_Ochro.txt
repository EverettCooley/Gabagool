 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs.  Beef , 
 1 pkg. frozen  ochro , 
 1  Onion , 
 2 stalks  green onions , 
 2 1/2 cups  converted rice , 
 5 cups  water , 
 1 oxo  Beef  cube, 
 2 Tbsp.  oil , 
 1 Tbsp. marmite, 
 4 Tbsp.butter, 
 1/2 pkg. frozen  coconut  crean, 
 2  potatoes , 
 plantains  or  cassava 
 Directions : 
 Cut  Beef  into 1 inch cube, and stir-fry in a pan, adding  onions  and  green onions . 
 Add 2 cups  water  and cook meat until tender. 
 Add remaining ingredients and cook over slow heat until  rice  is tender and dry.
