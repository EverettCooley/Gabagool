Dutch Oven Barbecued  Beef  Sandwiches
 2 pounds  Beef  stew meat
2 cups  water 
4 cups  cabbage  -- shredded
1/2 cup barbecue sauce
1/2 cup  Catsup 
1/3 cup  worcestershire sauce 
1 tablespoon prepared horseradish
1 tablespoon prepared  mustard 
10 hamburger buns
In a covered Dutch oven or saucepan, simmer  Beef  in  water  for 1 1/2 hours or
until tender. Drain cooking liquid, reserving 3/4 cup. Cool  Beef ; shred and
return to Dutch oven. Add  cabbage , barbecue sauce,  Catsup , Worcestershire
sauce, horseradish,  mustard  and the reserved cooking liquid. Cover and
simmer for 1 hour. Serve on buns.
