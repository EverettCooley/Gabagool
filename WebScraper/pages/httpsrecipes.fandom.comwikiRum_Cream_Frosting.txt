Ingredients : 
 1½ cups  heavy cream 
 2  eggs  — beaten 
 ⅓ cup  sugar 
 1 teaspoon  rum 
 ¼ cup melted  butter 
 Directions : 
 Whip cream until stiff, add the eggs, sugar and rum. 
 Continue beating until thick. 
 Allow melted butter to cool somewhat, then stir into other ingredients and mix well.
