Description : 
 Here is an easy salad recipe you can prepare for your family or take to your next family get-together. Keep an open mind about this Broccoli Salad recipe and give it a try. It is
delicious! 
 
 Contributed by Paula in California at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 bunch  broccoli , cut in tiny flowers 
 1 cup  raisins 
 ¼ cup  red onion 
 ¼ cup  pecans  (or  walnuts , or  sunflower seeds ) 
 ¼ cup  bacon  bits 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 ¼ cup  vinegar 
 Directions : 
 Mix mayonnaise, sugar and vinegar. 
 Add bacon bits and other ingredients and mix. 
 Chill for a few hours.
