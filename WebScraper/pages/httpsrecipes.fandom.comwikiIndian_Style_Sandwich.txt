 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 3-4  potatoes 
 1  Onion 
 ginger  (an inch thick cut into small pieces) 
 coriander leaves  ( 
 half a green  chile  sliced about a couple of mm thick 
 Directions : 
 Boil  potatoes , mash them, sautee  Onion . After it becomes golden brown, add  ginger ,  coriander leaves  ( cilantro ) and green  chile . Add a little bit of  cayenne pepper  if desired, use a bunch of  coriander leaves  coarsely chopped. cook for a while. Put this mixture between vegan bread slices and grill just like a grilled  Cheese  sandwich
