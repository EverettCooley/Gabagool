Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Hansen Estate in Crandall, Texas in 1994.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups fresh  blueberries 
 2 tablespoons  flour 
 1 lemon  cake mix 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 4 large  eggs 
 Directions : 
 Grease and flour tube pan. 
 Sprinkle flour over blueberries. 
 Combine dry cake mix and sour cream. 
 Add eggs one at a time beating at low speed then beat at medium speed until smooth. 
 Fold in blueberries then bake at 350°F for 45 minutes. 
 Cool on rack for 15 minutes then remove from pan.
