Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 36 triple layer cookie bars 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups  graham cracker  crumbs 
 ½ cup  butter 
 7 oz pkg flaked  coconut 
 14 oz can  sweetened condensed milk 
 12 oz bag  semi-sweet chocolate  chips 
 ½ cup creamy  peanut butter 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 350°F. 
 In baking pan, melt butter in oven. 
 Sprinkle crumbs over butter. 
 Top with coconut then milk. 
 Bake 25 minutes or until lightly browned. 
 In a bowl, melt chips with peanut butter in microwave 15 seconds or until melted. 
 Spread over coconut layer. 
 Cool 30 minutes. 
 Chill. 
 Cut into bars and place on serving plates. 
 Garnish with toasted coconut.
