Description : 
 I serve this with  rice . If you are eating it with  potato  latkes, I would not use  rice . Good luck on your project! Etta 
 
 Reformatted by Elaine Radi 
 Ingredients : 
 1  chicken , cut up into 8 pieces 
 ¼ cup  apricot jam 
 ¼ cup  ketchup 
 ½ cup  Coca-Cola 
 1 tsp  garlic powder 
 2 tsp  soy sauce 
 3 tbsp onion soup powder 
 Directions : 
 Put  chicken  in casserole dish. 
 Put jam and  ketchup  in bowl. 
 Mix well with spoon. 
 Add coca cola, garlic powder ,soy sauce, and  onion soup mix . 
 Mix with spoon. 
 Pour mixture over  chicken . 
 Cover casserole dish. 
 Bake at 350 °F for 1½ hours. 
 Remove cover and bake  chicken  uncovered for another ½ hour.
