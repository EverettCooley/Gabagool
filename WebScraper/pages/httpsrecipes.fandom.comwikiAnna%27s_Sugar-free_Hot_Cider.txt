Description : 
 This is yummy......and smells so wonderful!!
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 (64 oz) bottles  cranberry/peach juice 
 8  cinnamon  sticks 
 1½ tsp whole  cloves 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ½ cup  Splenda 
 Directions : 
 Pour the juices in a pot and bring to a boil. 
 Meanwhile, make pouch for the spices using cheese cloth (or a new hand wipe), place the spices in center of cloth and secure with cooking twine (or a rubber band). 
 When the juices are boiling, drop in the pouch. 
 Turn off heat and steep for about 10 minutes. 
 Serve hot.
