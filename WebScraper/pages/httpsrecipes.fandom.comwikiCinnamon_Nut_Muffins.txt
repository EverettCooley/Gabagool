Description : 
 Contributed by Jenn B aka Mom2sam and Tiny at  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 1½ dozen 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  sugar 
 2 cups  flour 
 3 tsp  cinnamon 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 2 tsp  baking powder 
 1 cup  milk 
 ½ cup  vegetable oil 
 2  eggs , beaten 
 1 cup  pecans , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Sift together  sugar ,  flour ,  cinnamon ,  salt  and  baking powder . 
 Combine  milk ,  vegetable oil  and  eggs , mixing well. 
 Add to  flour  mixture. 
 Add  pecans . 
 Fill greased (or lined) muffin tins and bake 15 to 20 minutes at 425°F.
