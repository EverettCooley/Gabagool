Ranch House Slaw
 
 2 T.  Mayonnaise 
 2 T.  sour cream 
 1 1/2 T.  cider vinegar 
 1/2 T.  sugar 
 1/8 T.  salt 
 1/8 T.  pepper 
 1/2 Small Head  green cabbage , Shredded (3 Cups) 
 1/2 Cup Thinly Sliced  green onion 
 1 T.  celery seed 
 
In Bowl, Mix  Mayonnaise ,  sour cream ,  cider vinegar ,  sugar ,  salt  And  pepper . Add  cabbage ,  green onions  And  celery seed .Makes 3 Cups
