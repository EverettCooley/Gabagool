Kohl Slaw
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 head cabbage, chopped fine 
 1/2 cup vinegar 
 1/2 cup brown sugar 
 1/4 cup butter or 1/2 lb. salt pork 
 Salt 
 Pepper 
 Directions : 
 Put vinegar, sugar and butter in a good sized pot. 
 Add cabbage, salt and pepper. 
 Cook on top of stove about 45 minutes or until cabbage is tender.
