 Prep Time: 5-10 min 
 Cook time: 25 min 
 Serves: 4 
 
 Description : 
 Baked Salmon topped with mayonaise and spices.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 salmon fillets (palm-sized), skinned, deboned 
 4 cups (approx.) Mayonaise 
 Seaweed Furukake 
 Olive (or Vegetable) Oil 
 1/4 cup Lemon Juice 
 1/4 cup Garlic chopped 
 Wasabi (powder/paste) 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 degrees 
 Rinse Salmon and coat with lemon juice 
 Combine Mayonaise, Garlic and Wasabi (to taste) and mix in a bowl. Set aside. 
 Lightly coat a baking pan with olive oil. 
 Place lemon soaked salmon on pan. 
 Generously smother mayonaise mix over the salmon. 
 Sprinkle a hearty amount of Furukake on the mayonaise mix. 
 Bake for approx 20-30 minutes. Use a fork to check salmon if done.
