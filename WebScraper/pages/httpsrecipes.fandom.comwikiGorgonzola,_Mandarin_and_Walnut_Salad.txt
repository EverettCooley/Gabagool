Ingredients : 
 5 ounces mixed salad  greens  or  romaine lettuce 
 1 cup toasted  walnuts 
 1 cup crumbled  gorgonzola 
 11 ounces  mandarin orange  sections, drained. 
 2 cups  Raspberry Vinaigrette  dressing 
 1 dash salad seasoning 
 8  grape tomatoes 
 Directions : 
 Divide  field greens  into four servings on chilled plates. 
 Sprinkle with  walnuts ,  gorgonzola  cheese, and the drained  orange  slices. 
 Add seasonings to taste. 
 Top with dressing. 
 Garnish with the  grape tomatoes .
