The Renowned 24 Hour Salad
 
 1 Can  pineapple  Chunks, Drained (20 Oz) 
 1 Can  mandarin orange  Sections, Drained (11 Oz) 
 1 Cup Shredded  coconut 
 1 Cup Miniature Marshmallows 
 1 Cup Toasted Chopped Nuts 
 8  maraschino cherries , Halved 
 1 Cup  sour cream 
 
Mix All Ingredients Together And Refrigerate For 24 Hours Before Serving.Serves 6 to 8
