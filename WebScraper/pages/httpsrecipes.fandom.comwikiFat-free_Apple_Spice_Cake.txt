Ingredients : 
 2 cups white unbleached  all purpose flour 
 1¼ cups  sugar 
 ¼ cup fruit purée ( oil ,  butter ,  margarine  substitute) 
 1 tsp ground  cinnamon 
 1 tsp ground  cloves 
 1 tsp  nutmeg 
 6  egg whites 
 ½ cup boiling  water 
 2 cups  apples , finely chopped 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 2 tsp  baking soda 
 1 tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Spray 13x9 baking pan with fat free spray, like pam. 
 Heat oven to 350°F. 
 Mix together  flour ,  sugar , soda,  salt  and spices and set aside. 
 Mix together the fruit puree fat substitute,  egg whites ,  vanilla  and boiling  water . 
 Add dry ingredients and mix with electric mixer on medium power for 2 minutes. 
 Stir in finely chopped  apples . 
 Pour into 13x9 baking pan and back for 40 – 45 minutes. 
 Check for doneness at 40 minutes and add time if necessary. 
 Top should spring back when touched. 
 Dust with  confectioners' sugar  and serve warm.
