Ingredients : 
 Meat balls : 
 2½ pounds  hamburger 
 2  eggs , slightly beaten 
 2 cups  cracker crumbs 
 ½ pounds  ground lean pork 
 1 cup  milk 
 ½ teaspoon  lemon rind 
 ¼ teaspoon  nutmeg 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  pepper 
 Sauce : 
 3 cups thinly sliced  onions 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 2 tablespoons  flour 
 1 can condensed  beef bouillon 
 blend in ½ cup  dry vermouth 
 2 ounces  vodka 
 Directions : 
 Meat balls : 
 Mix well and let stand for 1 hour, then form into small balls. 
 Sauce : 
 Brown in a skillet in drippings along with 3 cups thinly sliced  onions  for about 15 minutes. 
 Remove from pan. 
 To the drippings, add  sour cream  and thicken with  flour  and  beef bouillon ; cook until blended, then add  onions  and meat balls. 
 Blend in  dry vermouth  and  vodka .
