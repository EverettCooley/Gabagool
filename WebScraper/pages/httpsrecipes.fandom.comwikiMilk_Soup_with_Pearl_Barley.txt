Ingredients : 
 1 liter  water 
 200 gm  pearl barley 
 2 liter  milk 
 2 tablespoon  butter 
 salt  (to taste) 
 Directions : 
 Wash the  barley  and soak about 2-4 hours. 
 Put into a pot together with the soak  water  and bring to a boil. 
 Cook at a low temperature. 
 When the  barley  is soft add the  butter  and  milk . 
 Season with  salt  and then for few minutes. 
 Serve as a first dish or with jam as a dessert.
