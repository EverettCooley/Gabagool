Description : 
 Makes 6½ cups
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  milk  (don't use skim!) 
 2 cups  beer 
 16 oz  Cheez Whiz 
 ⅓ cup cooked, crumbled  bacon  or 2 oz bits 
 ½ cup  flour 
 ¼ cup  butter , melted 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Combine  milk ,  beer  and  cheese  spread in medium saucepan. 
 Stir in  bacon . 
 Combine  flour  and  butter  to form into a ball. 
 Add  butter  mixture to warm  milk  mixture. 
 Cook over low heat, stirring constantly until  butter  mixture dissolves and soup is thickened. 
 Season with  salt  and serve.
