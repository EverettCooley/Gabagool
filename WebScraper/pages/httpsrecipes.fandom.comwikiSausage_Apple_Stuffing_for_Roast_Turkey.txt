 Ingredients : 
 ¼ lbs bulk  pork sausage 
 ⅓ cup chopped  onion 
 ⅓ cup chopped  celery 
 6 cups dry bread cubes 
 1 tbsp finely chopped fresh  parsley 
 1½ cups chopped peeled  apples 
 ¾ tsp Accent® flavor enhancer 
 ¾ tsp  salt 
 1½ tsp  poultry seasoning 
 ⅛ tsp  pepper 
 3 tbsp  butter , melted 
 ⅓ cup  water 
 ½ cup  raisins 
 Directions : 
 In small skillet, brown  pork   sausage  with  onion  and  celery . Do not drain. 
 In large bowl, combine bread,cubes,  parsley , accent flavor enhancer,  salt ,  poultry seasoning ,  pepper  and  butter , mix well. 
 Stir in  water ,  apples ,  raisins  and  sausage  mixture including drippings. 
 Prepare  turkey  as directed. 
 Just before roasting, spoon stuffing into  turkey ; do not pack tightly. 
 Continue as directed for stuffed  turkey . 
 Stuffing will fill 18 lb  turkey .
