Ingredients : 
 5 scoops French vanilla  ice cream   or  eggnog  ice cream 
 ¼ cup  brandy 
 ¼ cup  milk 
 2 tablespoons  Grand Marnier  (orange-flavored liqueur) (optional) 
 ¼ teaspoon grated  nutmeg 
 Directions : 
 In blender, combine  ice cream ,  brandy ,  milk ,  Grand Marnier , if desired, and ⅛ teaspoon  nutmeg  and blend until mixture is smooth and frothy. 
 Pour into 4 glasses and sprinkle with remaining  nutmeg .
