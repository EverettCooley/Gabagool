All My Popcorn...Soap Opera Snack
 4 quarts popped popcorn
1/3 cup margarine, melted
1/2 t. each garlic and onion salt
2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
 Empty popped corn into an ovenproof mixing bowl. Add butter, salts, and cheese (a small amount at a time) and toss. Put in a 325 oven for 5 minutes to 10 minutes until cheese is melted. Stir gently once or twice before serving.
