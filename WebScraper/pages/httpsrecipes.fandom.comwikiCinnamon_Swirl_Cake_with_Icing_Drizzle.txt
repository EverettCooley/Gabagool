Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 9 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 Batter : 
 1½ cups  flour 
 2 envelopes rapid rise  yeast 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 ⅔ cup  milk , very warm 
 ¼ cup  butter , melted 
 1  egg 
 Cinnamon topping : 
 3 tbsp  butter , softened 
 ¾ cup  dark brown sugar 
 1½ tsp  cinnamon 
 Icing : 
 1 cup  powdered sugar 
 2 tbsp  milk 
 1 tbsp  butter , melted 
 ½ tsp  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Mix batter ingredients in a pre-sprayed 8x8 inch baking dish, let rest 10 min. 
 Combine cinnamon mixture in a bowl. 
 Top batter with cinnamon topping, poke topping into batter. 
 Bake by placing in a cold oven, set temp to 350°F. Bake 30 min. or until lightly browned and firm in center. Cool 10 minutes. 
 Combine icing ingredients and drizzle over warm cake.
