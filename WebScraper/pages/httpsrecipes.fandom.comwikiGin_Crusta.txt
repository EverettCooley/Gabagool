Ingredients : 
 5 oz  London dry gin 
 30 drops gomme syrup 
 10 drops Angostura®  bitters 
 10 drops Blue  curacao  liqueur 
 Directions : 
 Shake with  ice  and strain into a chilled glass rimmed with  lemon juice  and  Sugar . Garnish with a twist of a  lemon peel .
