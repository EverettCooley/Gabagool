 Ingredients : 
 several  chile peppers  (i.e., hot  red peppers !), cleaned and finely chopped 
 juice of one  lemon 
 a few cloves of  garlic , minced 
 several pieces of  parsley , minced (optional) 
 a few tablespoons of cooking  oil 
 a pinch of  salt 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients by hand or with a blender, food processor. 
 Cook in a hot skillet for a few minutes. 
 Store in a glass jar in the refrigerator. 
 Serve with everything.
