Description : 
 Contributed by Dorie at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 large  tortillas 
 ⅓ cup  butter  softened 
 6 tbsp  Parmesan cheese 
 4 oz  provolone  cheese, sliced 
 4 oz  salami , sliced 
 4 oz  ham , thinly sliced 
 4 oz can  mushrooms  drained 
 4 oz shredded  Mozzarella  cheese 
 marinara  or  pizza sauce  heated (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 400°F. 
 Lightly spread one side of each tortilla with butter. 
 Sprinkle with 1 Tbsp Parmesan. 
 Arrange ¼ each of provolone, salami and ham over over center of tortillas leaving a border around the edges. Sprinkle with ¼ of the mozzarella. 
 Fold up wrap fashion. 
 Place seam side down on baking sheet. Brush with remaining butter; sprinkle with remaining Parmesan. 
 Bake 12 – 15 minutes or until golden brown. Slice each stromboli in half on the diagonal. 
 Serve with marinara or pizza sauce, if desired.
