Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 3 dozen 
 Ingrediants : 
 14 oz pkg flaked  coconut 
 ⅔ cup  sugar 
 6 tbsp  flour 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 4  egg whites 
 1 tsp  almond extract 
 8 squares  semisweet baking chocolate , melted 
 Method : 
 Mix first 4 ingredients in bowl. 
 Stir in egg whites and almond extract until blended. 
 Drop by tablespoonsful onto greased and floured cookie sheets. 
 Bake at 325°F for 20 minutes or until edges of cookies are golden brown. 
 Cool. 
 Dip cookies halfway into melted chocolate. 
 Let stand at room temp on wax paper lined tray until chocolate is firm.
