Description : 
 Tamarind juice
 
 Ingredients : 
 250 g (9 oz) dry  tamarind 
 1.2 litre (2 pints)  water 
 3 tablespoons  lemon juice 
 400 g (14 oz)  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Break up the dry  tamarind  into small pieces, place in a large bowl, cover with  water  and soak for 12 hours or over night. 
 Pour the  tamarind  with its  water  in a large saucepan and boil for 5 minutes, removing any froth. 
 Pour through sieve and discard stones. 
 Return to saucepan and add  Sugar . Mix on low heat until mixture begins to thicken. 
 Add  lemon juice  and boil together for 5 minutes stirring occasionally. 
 Pour the liquid through a sieve, then leave to cool for 1 hour. Place in fridge for 1 hour.
