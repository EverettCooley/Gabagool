 Ingredients : 
 25 ml Vladivar®  vodka 
 50 ml  cranberry juice 
 50 ml  mango  juice 
 50 ml  orange juice 
 2 tbsp  ice cream 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients with four ice cubes in a blender for 20 – 30 seconds. 
 Pour into a highball glass or gibralter glass, and serve.
