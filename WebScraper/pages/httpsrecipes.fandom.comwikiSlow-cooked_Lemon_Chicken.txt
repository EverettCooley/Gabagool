Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 6  chicken breast  halves, skinned, but bone in 
 1 tbsp dried  oregano 
 ½ tbsp seasoned  salt 
 ¼ tbsp  pepper 
 2 tbsp  butter 
 ¼ cup  water 
 3 tbsp  lemon juice 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1 tbsp  bouillon  granules 
 Directions : 
 Rub meat with spices. 
 Brown in  butter . 
 Put in slow cooker. 
 Use the following to deglaze the pan. 
 Add to slow cooker. 
 Cook on low 4 - 5 hours. 
 Sprinkle with  parsley  and serve with gravy.
