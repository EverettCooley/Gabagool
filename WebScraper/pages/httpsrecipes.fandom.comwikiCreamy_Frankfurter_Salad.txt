 Description : 
 Makes 8 servings (6 cups).
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice , cooled 
 1 12-ounce package frankfurters, sliced 
 1 1/2 cups sliced  celery 
 3/4 cup chopped  Onion 
 1/2 cup chopped pimientos 
 1 11-ounce can condensed  Cheddar   Cheese  soup 
 1/4 cup  Mayonnaise 
 1 tablespoon prepared  mustard 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 3 hard-cooked  eggs , sliced 
 Salad greens (optional) 
 Sliced hard-cooked  eggs  for garnish (optional) 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice , frankfurters,  celery ,  Onion  and pimientos in large bowl. 
 
 Blend soup,  Mayonnaise ,  mustard  and  pepper ; stir into  rice  mixture. Chill. Serve on salad greens, if desired; garnish with  egg  slices, if desired.
