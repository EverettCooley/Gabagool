Description : 
 Makes 2 to 3 servings.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅓ cup chopped  onion 
 1 teaspoon  butter  or  margarine 
 1 cup cooked  rice 
 ⅔ cup  tuna , drained and flaked 
 7 tablespoons (⅓ of 10¾-ounce can) condensed  cream of celery  soup 
 2 teaspoons  lemon juice 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅛ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 ⅓ cup (1.3 ounces) shredded  Cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Cook  onion  in  butter  in small skillet until tender but not brown. 
 Stir in  rice ,  tuna , soup,  lemon juice ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Turn into greased shallow 6-inch baking dish or individual casseroles. 
 Bake at 350°F for 20 minutes or until thoroughly heated. 
 Top with cheese and bake 5 minutes longer.
