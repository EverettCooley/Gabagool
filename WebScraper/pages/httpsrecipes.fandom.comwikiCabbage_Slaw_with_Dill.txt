Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 6-8 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 small head  red cabbage , thinly sliced 
 1 small head  green cabbage , thinly sliced 
 6  scallions , thinly sliced 
 ½ cup  sour cream 
 ¼ cup  mayonnaise 
 1 small clove  garlic , minced or pressed 
 1 tsp dried  dill  weed 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 tbsp  white wine vinegar 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  sugar 
 ⅛ tsp  white pepper 
 ¼ cup salad  oil 
 chopped  parsley , for garnish 
 Preparation : 
 In large bowl, combine cabbages and scallions. 
 In med bow, mix until smooth, sour cream, mayonnaise, garlic, dill, lemon juice, vinegar, salt, sugar and pepper. 
 Using whisk or fork, gradually beat in oil until well combined. 
 Mix dressing lightly with cabbage mixture. 
 Cover and refrigerate 1-2 hr to blend flavors. 
 Serve sprinkled with parsley.
