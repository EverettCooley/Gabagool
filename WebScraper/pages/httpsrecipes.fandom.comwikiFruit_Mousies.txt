Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 For each mousie:
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 half of a  pear 
 1  grape  cut in half 
 3  raisins 
 1 long  banana  slice 
 2  strawberries  cut in half 
 toothpicks 
 Directions : 
 Take the half  pear  and put it flat side down on a plate. 
 Take the half  grapes  and two toothpicks and put the  grapes  on the mouse for ears. 
 Then take three  raisins  and stick them in for the eyes and the nose. 
 Use the  strawberries  for the feet. 
 Take the long  banana  slice for the tail. 
 Attach all the parts with toothpicks.
