Description : 
 Beets are cooked with a sweet-sour sauce in the crockpot. 
 
 4 to 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  granulated sugar 
 2 tablespoons  flour 
 ¼ cup  water 
 ¼ cup  white vinegar 
 2 x (16 oz) cans whole  beets , drained 
 Directions : 
 Mix  sugar  and  flour  and Stir in the  water  and  vinegar . 
 Place beets in slow cooker 
 Stir in  vinegar  and  sugar  mixture. 
 Cover and cook on high for 3 to 4 hours.
