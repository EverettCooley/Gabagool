 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  shortening 
 3 tbsp  honey 
 4  eggs 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 1 cup  whole wheat flour 
 ¼ cup  carob  powder 
 ½ tsp  baking powder 
 Directions : 
 cream   shortening  and  honey  together thoroughly. 
 Add remaining ingredients. 
 Beat well. 
 Bake in a greased cookie sheet (10x15") for 25 minutes at 350°F. 
 Cool completely.
