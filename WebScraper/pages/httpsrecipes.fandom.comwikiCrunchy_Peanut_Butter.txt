Description : 
 Making your own peanut butter will take more time, but the quality and results are worth it. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups salted  peanuts 
 2 Tbsp.  butter 
 Directions : 
 Measure 1 ½ cup peanuts and put aside. 
 Empty remaining peanuts into a blender's container. 
 Cover and blend on low speed until the peanuts are chopped. 
 Add butter, cover and blend on low speed for 15 seconds. 
 Turn off the blender and scrape down sides with a rubber spatula. 
 Cover and blend on low speed for 5 seconds. 
 Repeat this process 3 or 4 times until the peanut butter starts to get smooth. 
 Cover and blend on the highest speed for 1 minute. 
 Add the remaining peanuts, cover and blend on moderate speed for 3 – 5 seconds. 
 Refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving.
