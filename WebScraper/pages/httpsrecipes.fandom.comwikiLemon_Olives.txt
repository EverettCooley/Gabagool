Description : 
 This appetizer is authentically Moroccan. Lemon-flavored olives are gorgeous with a unique tang of their own.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups mixed olives 
 3 tablespoons chopped fresh  cilantro 
 3 tablespoons chopped  parsley 
 ½ clove  garlic , minced 
 ¼ teaspoon  cayenne pepper 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  cumin 
 2 tablespoons  olive oil 
 1 cup fresh  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Drain olives, rinse in colander, and dry with paper towels. 
 Mix all ingredients, put into a jar or other glass container with a lid, and store in the refrigerator. 
 Refrigerate for 3-5 days to let flavors blend. 
 Take out 1 hour before serving so you can serve marinated olives at room temperature. Yum!
