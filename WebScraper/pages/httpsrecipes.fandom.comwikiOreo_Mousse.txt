Ingredients : 
 1 qt. quality vanilla  ice cream 
 1 (8 oz.) container Cool Whip type  whipped topping 
 12 oreo cookies, crushed 
 Directions : 
 Allow ice cream to soften slightly. 
 Mix with whipped topping. 
 Fold in cookies. 
 Freeze mixture in square pan. 
 About 20 minutes before serving, cut dessert into squares and allow to thaw.
