 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 head fresh  lettuce  (local or imported) 
 2 stalks  celery  (chopped small) 
 1 large firm  tomatoes  (cut small) 
 1  cucumber  (cut small) 
 1 medium  carrot  (chopped small) 
 vinegar  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Tear  lettuce  leaves. Prepare other vegetables. 
 Put into a bowl. Toss. 
 Add  vinegar / oil  or any of your favourte dressing.
