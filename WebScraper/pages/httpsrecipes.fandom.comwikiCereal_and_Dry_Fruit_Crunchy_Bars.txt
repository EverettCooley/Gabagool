 Ingredients : 
 1 cup (250 ml)  Barbados sugar 
 1 cup (250 ml)  vegetable oil 
 1 whole  egg 
 1  egg white 
 1 teaspoon (5 ml)  vanilla extract 
 1½ cups (375 ml)  corn flakes , crushed 
 1½ cups (375 ml)  rolled oats 
 ½ cup (125 ml)  sesame seeds 
 ¼ cup (60 ml)  soy flour 
 1 tablespoon (15 ml)  cinnamon 
 1 teaspoon (5 ml)  allspice 
 1 teaspoon (5 ml)  nutmeg 
 1 cup (250 ml) of  raisins 
 ½ cup (125 ml) chopped  dried figs 
 1 cup (250ml) shredded  coconut 
 1 cup (250 ml) chopped  nuts 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C). 
 Grease a 9" x 13" pan. 
 In a large bowl, beat together Barbados  sugar ,  vegetable oil , whole  egg ,  egg  white, and  vanilla extract . 
 Add  corn flakes ,  rolled oats ,  sesame seeds ,  soy flour ,  cinnamon ,  allspice  and  nutmeg . 
 Stir in  raisins ,  figs  (or  chocolate chips ),  coconut  and chopped  nuts . 
 Spread mixture evenly over pan and press down lightly. 
 Bake for 20–25 minutes, or until crisp around the edges. 
 Allow to cool completely before cutting into bars.
