 Description : 
 Colourful, tempting and tasty.
 
 Ingredients : 
 10 small  tomatoes , 
 15 grams crumbled paneer or khoya 
 2 teacups mixed boiled vegetables (french beans,  carrots  and  Peas ) 
 1 chopped  Onion , 
 2 chopped green chillies, 
 2 tablespoons chopped cashewnuts, 
 2 tablespoons  ghee , 
 salt  to taste 
 -- For gravy: 
 2 grated  onions , 
 3 tablespoons  tomato   ketchup , 
 1/2 teaspoon  chilli powder , 
 2 tablespoons  ghee , 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 For  tomatoes : 
 Scoop the  tomatoes . Keep aside the pulp for the gravy. 
 Add the  ghee  and fry the  Onion  for 1 minute. 
 Add the green chillies and fry again for ½ minute. 
 Add the cashewnuts and fry again for ½ minute. 
 Add the vegetables, paneer and  salt  and cook for a few minutes. 
 Fill the  tomatoes  with the mixture. 
 For gravy: 
 Heat the  ghee  and fry the  Onion  till golden in colour. 
 Add the  tomato  pulp,  tomato   ketchup ,  chilli powder  and  salt  and boil for 1 minute. 
 For baking grease a flat baking tray and arrange the  tomatoes  over it. 
 Pour the gravy all over and bake in a hot oven at 400oF for 15 to 20 minutes. 
 Serve hot. If you like, decorate with  Potato  wafers.
