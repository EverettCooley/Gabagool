Leftover Roast  Beef  Casserole
 
This is a Fantastic Recipe For Using up Leftovers And Requires Almost Zero Prep.
 
Ingredients
 
Leftover Roast  Beef , Sliced Thin
 
 2 Cans Sliced  potatoes  or Fresh Thinly Sliced  potatoes 
 1 Lb. Monterey  jack cheese , Shredded 
 1 Can Cream of  Mushroom  Soup 
 1 Small Can  Mushrooms , Drained 
 
Directions
 
In a Casserole Dish, Put a Layer of  potatoes , a Layer of Roast  Beef , Then a Layer of  Cheese , Layering Until You End up With The  Cheese  on Top. Mix  Mushrooms  And  Mushroom  Soup And Pour Over The Top. Bake For 15-20 Minutes at 350 Degrees.
