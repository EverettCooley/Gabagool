Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 12 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tube pizza crust dough 
 2 tbsp  olive oil 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1  onion , minced 
 1½ cups  marinara sauce 
 1 tsp  Italian seasoning 
 1 bunch  broccoli rabe , cut into pieces 
 ½ tsp  hot pepper flakes 
 ¾ cup  ricotta  cheese 
 3 tbsp  Parmesan cheese 
 2 cups shredded  mozzarella  cheese 
 1 cup shredded  fontina 
 ½ cup sliced  pepperoni 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 425°F. 
 Coat baking pan with nonstick spray. 
 Roll out dough and fit into pan. 
 Bake 12 minutes. 
 Remove and let pan cool slightly. 
 Heat 1 tbsp oil in skillet. 
 Add garlic and onion and cook 3 minutes. 
 Stir in marinara and ½ tsp seasoning, heat 2 more minutes. Set aside. 
 In a second skillet, heat remaining oil and broccoli and hot pepper flakes. 
 Cook 10 minutes or until tender. Set aside. 
 Stir together ricotta, parmesan and remaining seasoning. 
 Spread marinara sauce over pizza crust. 
 Drop spoonfuls of ricotta mixture over half the crust, sprinkle broccoli on top. 
 Top with ⅓ cup mozzarella and all the fontina. 
 Sprinkle ⅓ cup mozzarella on top of the other half of pizza. 
 Top with half the pepperoni, then remaining mozzarella and remaining pepperoni on top of that. 
 Bake 17 minutes or until cheese is bubbling. 
 Serve with ranch dressing or hot sauce.
