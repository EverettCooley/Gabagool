Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Steep Estate in Elmo, Texas in 1989.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cans whole berry  cranberry sauce 
 2 cups boiling  water 
 2 small packages  strawberry gelatin 
 2 tablespoons  lemon juice 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 2 cups diced  apples 
 ½ cup chopped  walnuts 
 Directions : 
 Melt  cranberry sauce  over medium heat then drain reserving liquid and berries. 
 Mix together  cranberry  liquid, boiling  water  and  gelatin  and stir until  gelatin  is dissolved. 
 Add  lemon juice  and  salt . 
 Chill until mixture mounds slightly on a spoon then add  mayonnaise  and beat until smooth. 
 Fold in  cranberries ,  apple  and nuts then pour into 2-quart mold. 
 Chill overnight.
