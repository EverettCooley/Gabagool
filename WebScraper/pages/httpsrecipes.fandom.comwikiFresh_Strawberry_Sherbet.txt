 Ingredients : 
 1/3 cup  apple juice  concentrate or: fruit and berry concentrate 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1/2 cup  water 
 5 cup fresh  strawberries 
 Directions : 
 Wash and stem  Berries . In a blender or food processor combine concentrate,  water  and  lemon juice . Add half the  Berries . Cover and blend til smooth.
