       Baked Acorn Squash     
 This warm vegetable-based dish uses lots of butter, honey, and black pepper cooked to steamy perfection.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 medium-sized  acorn squash 
 8 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 8 teaspoons  honey 
 salt, to taste 
 fresh ground black  pepper , to season 
 Directions : 
 Slice the squash in half crosswise and scoop out the pulp and seeds. Trim the bottoms. 
 Place 2 teaspoons honey in the hollow of each squash. 
 Add 2 tablespoons butter or margarine to each and a twist or two of salt and fresh ground pepper. 
 Repeat steps 2 and 3 for the squash. 
 Place squash in a large, shallow baking pan and bake, uncovered, at 350° F, for about 2 ½ hours.
