Description : 
 Genuine  California avocado  and roasted red pepper with seasoned  cream cheese  on a flavorful  bagel .
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ half Genuine  California avocado 
 ½ roasted  red pepper 
 cream cheese  seasoned with  Spike  seasoning 
 Asiago  Cheese  bagel  (or your favorite  bagel . Sun  dried tomato  is also good) 
 Optional chopped  green olives  or sliced  cucumbers 
 Directions : 
 Roast your  pepper  in the oven under the broiler on all sides until blackened. 
 Remove to a paper sack to cool. 
 Peel. 
 You could substitute bottled  peppers . 
 Mash  cream cheese  with fork in a plate. 
 Sprinkle on  spike  seasoning to taste (sparingly at first) and blend well. 
 Spread  cream cheese  mixture onto sliced  bagel  halves. 
 Spread  avocado  half on bottom half of  bagel  and press down with fork to mash. 
 Top with roasted  pepper  half and other half of  bagel .
