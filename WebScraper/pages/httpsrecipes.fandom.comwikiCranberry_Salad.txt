Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can (8 oz) crushed  pineapple  in own juice 
 1 can (16 oz) Ocean Spray whole  cranberry sauce 
 1 pkg. (3 oz)  raspberry gelatin 
 ¼ cup chopped  nuts 
 ¼ cup chopped  celery 
 Directions : 
 Drain pineapple, save juice, add water to make 1¼ cup liquid. 
 In saucepan bring liquid to boil and take off of heat. 
 Stir in gelatin until dissolved. 
 Break up cranberry sauce with fork and stir into gelatin mixture. 
 Cool until slightly thick and add celery, nuts and pineapple. 
 Pour into mold and chill.
