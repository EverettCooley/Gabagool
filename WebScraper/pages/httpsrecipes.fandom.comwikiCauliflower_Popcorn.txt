Ingredients : 
 1 large head  cauliflower , broken into small florets 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil   
 ½ teaspoon  garlic salt​​​​​​ 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). 
 Whisk olive oil and garlic salt together in a large bowl 
 Add cauliflower and toss to coat completely. 
 Spread cauliflower out onto a baking sheet. 
 Bake in the preheated oven until golden and tender, 15 to 18 minutes.
