Bee's Knees Bars
 1 cup (2 sticks) butter or margarine, softened 
1/3 cup honey 
1 teaspoon vanilla or almond extract 
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour 
1 1/2 cups quick-cooking rolled oats
1/2 teaspoon salt 
1 (6 ounce) package butterscotch chips
1/2 cup slivered or sliced almonds 
2 tablespoons honey, or more if needed
 Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
 Beat butter, the 1/3 cup honey and extract until smooth.
 Blend in flour, oats and salt. Stir in butterscotch chips.
 Press dough into ungreased 9 x 13 x 2-inch pan (bars will be thin.) 
Sprinkle almonds over top and pat into dough. Brush with 2 
tablespoons honey. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes until golden brown.
 Cool completely before cutting into bars.
 Makes 3 dozen.
