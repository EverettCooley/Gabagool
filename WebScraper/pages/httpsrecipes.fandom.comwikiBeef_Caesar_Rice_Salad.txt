Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  parmesan cheese 
 3 tablespoons  butter , softened 
 2 teaspoons  lemon juice 
 1 tablespoon  Dijon mustard 
 1 teaspoon grated  lemon peel 
 1½ teaspoons  anchovy paste 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 ⅛ teaspoon  hot pepper sauce 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 1 pound  sirloin steak , grilled and sliced 
 1 cup finely chopped  spinach 
 ½ cup  walnuts 
 ½ cup sliced  black olives 
 spinach  leaves or  romaine lettuce 
 Directions : 
 Combine  cheese ,  butter ,  lemon juice ,  mustard ,  lemon peel , anchovy paste,  garlic  and  hot pepper sauce  in large bowl. 
 Add  rice ,  steak ,  spinach ,  walnuts  and to  cheese  mixture. 
 Serve over  spinach  leaves or romaine leaves.
