Ingredients : 
 asparagus  spears 
 Paul Newman  salad dressing 
 Directions : 
 Allow 4 spears of fresh  asparagus  per serving. 
 Dip  asparagus  in boiling  water  and drain immediately. 
 Pour Paul Newman (or similar)  salad dressing  over  asparagus , cover, and marinate overnight. 
 Serve on  lettuce  leaves.
