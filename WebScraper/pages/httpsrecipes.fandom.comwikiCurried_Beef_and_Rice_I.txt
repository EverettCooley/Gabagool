Makes 6 to 8 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 pounds lean ground  Beef 
 2 cups sliced  onions 
 1 cup uncooked  rice 
 1 4-ounce can sliced  Mushrooms , drained 
 2 to 3 teaspoons  curry  powder 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 21/2 cups  beef broth 
 1 tablespoon  worcestershire sauce 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 Chopped fresh  parsley  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Beef  and  onions  in Dutch oven or large skillet over medium heat until meat is no longer pink. Add  rice  and continue cooking until meat has browned. 
 Add  Mushrooms ,  curry  powder,  pepper , broth and  worcestershire sauce ; heat to boiling. Stir well, reduce heat, cover, and simmer 20 minutes or until  rice  is tender and liquid is absorbed. 
 Serve hot and top with  sour cream . Sprinkle with  parsley , if desired.
