 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 A few pounds of  Beef , cut into bite-sized pieces 
 Onion , sweet  green pepper ,  tomatoes  (optional); cut into bite-sized pieces 
 Marinade:
 
 oil 
 vinegar  (or  cider vinegar ) 
 tomato , chopped 
 Onion , finely chopped 
 garlic , minced 
 fresh ginger , minced 
 cayenne pepper  or red pepper 
 salt 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Use a mortar and pestle to grind together all of the marinade ingredients. 
 In a glass bowl mix the meat and marinade, thoroughly covering the meat. 
 Allow the meat to marinate for several hours if possible. 
 Place the meat (and vegetables, if desired) on skewers. 
 Cook the skewered meat under the oven broiler. 
 Baste with the remaining marinade, making sure the marinade is fully cooked after the last basting.
