      Three Bean Burrito     
 Ingredients : 
 8 Mission™ burrito-size flour  tortillas 
 16 oz can  kidney beans  drained 
 16 oz can  pinto beans  drained 
 1½ cups hot cooked  long-grain rice 
 ¾ cup fat-free  black bean  dip 
 3 tbsp fresh  cilantro  chopped 
 1 cup Mission™  salsa 
 ½ cup reduced-fat sharp  cheddar  cheese shredded 
 (optional)  lime  wedges 
 Directions : 
 Combine  kidney beans ,  pinto beans ,  black bean  dip,  rice  and  cilantro  in a medium bowl *stir well. 
 Warm  flour  tortillas according to package directions. 
 Spread ½ cup bean mixture over each tortilla; top each with 1 tablespoon  cheese  and 1 tablespoon of salsa; roll up. 
 Spoon 1 tablespoon salsa over each burrito. 
 Garnish with  lime  wedges, if desired.
