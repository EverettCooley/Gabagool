Ingredients : 
 500 ml (2 cups)  water 
 300 g (10 oz.) well-washed  long-grain rice 
 1 litre (4 cups)  milk 
 75 g (5 tbsp)  sugar 
 tablespoon of  cinnamon 
 candied fruit 
 Directions : 
 Boil 500 ml (2 cups)  water ; add 300 g (10 oz) well-washed long-grain rice]]; cook for 5 minutes; drain. 
 Meanwhile, in another pot, bring 1 litre (4 cups)  milk  to the boiling point; add the drained  rice  and cook for 12 to 15 minutes. 
 Remove from the heat; sprinkle with 75 g (5 tbsp)  sugar  and a generous tablespoon of  cinnamon . 
 Arrange in a dome shape in a deep platter. 
 Garnish with  candied fruit .
