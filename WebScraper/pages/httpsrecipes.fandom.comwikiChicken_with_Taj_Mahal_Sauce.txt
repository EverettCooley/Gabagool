 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 c.  orange  juice 
 1/2 c.  brown sugar , packed 
 1/2 c.  Sugar 
 1 tsap.  curry  powder 
 2 T.  sherry 
 8 whole  Chicken  breasts 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine first 6 ingredients in large saucepan. Bring to boil. 
 Add  Chicken . 
 Simmer 10 minutes per side. Remove to large baking dish. 
 Bake at 350F., 1 hr., basting occasionally with remaining sauce. 
 Makes 8 servings.
