Ingredients : 
 2 x 8 oz pkgs.  cream cheese  softened 
 1 x 0.4 oz pkg.  ranch salad dressing  mix 
 ½ cup minced  sweet red pepper 
 ½ cup minced  celery 
 ¼ cup sliced  green onions 
 ¼ cup sliced stuffed  olives 
 4 x 10" flour  tortillas 
 Directions : 
 In a mixing bowl, beat  cream cheese  and dressing mix until smooth. 
 Add  red pepper ,  celery ,  onions  and olives; mix well. 
 Spread about ¾ cup on each tortilla. 
 Roll up tightly; wrap in plastic wrap. 
 Refrigerate for at least 2 hours. 
 Slice into ½" pieces.
