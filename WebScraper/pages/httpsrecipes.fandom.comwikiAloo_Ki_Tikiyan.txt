 Ingredients : 
 4 big  potatoes 
 1  onion , finely chopped 
 5 – 6  green chile , chopped 
 ½ cup  cilantro  leaves, chopped 
 4 teaspoons  masala  or 2 teaspoons red  chili powder 
 1 teaspoon  onion seed  (kalongi) 
 1 teaspoon  jeera  powder ( cumin  powder) 
 1 teaspoon fresh grated  ginger  (optional) 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice  (optional) 
 salt 
 Batter : 
 1 cup white  flour 
 1 teaspoon  sea salt 
 2 tablespoons  oil 
 1 pinch  baking soda 
 water , for making the batter 
 Directions : 
 Peel and boil the  potatoes  until soft. 
 Mash the  potatoes  and add the  masala  or spices,  onions ,  cilantro ,  chiles  and add  ginger  and  lemon juice  if desired. 
 Form the mixture into small patties. 
 Mix the white  flour  with the  salt ,  baking soda ,  oil  and  water  to make a thick batter. 
 Heat  oil  in a pan. 
 Dip patties into the batter and fry at least 5 minutes on each side, until golden brown. 
 Serve once ready
