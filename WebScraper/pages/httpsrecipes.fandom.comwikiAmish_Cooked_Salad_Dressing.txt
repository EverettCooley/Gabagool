      Amish Cooked Salad Dressing     
 Ingredients : 
 1  egg 
 1 cup + 1 tablespoon  water 
 ⅔ cup  all purpose flour 
 2 teaspoons  dry mustard 
 ⅔ cup  sugar 
 ½ cup white  vinegar 
 ¾ cup  vegetable oil 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 1 tablespoon minced dry onion 
 Directions : 
 In a small  bowl , beat the  egg  with 1 tbsp of the  water . 
 Set aside. 
 Whisk together the  flour ,  mustard , and  sugar . 
 Then whisk in the remaining 1 cup  water ,  vinegar ,  oil  and  lemon juice . 
 Heat over medium heat for 1 minute, whisking continuously. 
 Add the  egg  mixture to the  flour  mixture and continue to cook for 1 minute, stirring. 
 Remove from heat, cover, and cool completely. 
 Store in a covered jar in the refrigerator.
