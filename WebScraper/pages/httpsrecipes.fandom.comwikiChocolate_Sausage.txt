Description : 
 When I was young I loved this recipe! And I must admit that I still love it a lot! It's an easy no-baking sweet that every kid will enjoy both to make and to eat! 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2/3 cup  white sugar 
 1  egg yolk 
 1  egg 
 1 tablespoon  butter , melted 
 1 1/8 cups  unsweetened cocoa powder 
 5 ounces crispy butter cookies, crushed 
 1/3 cup  ground almonds 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Beat together sugar, eggs, butter, cocoa, crushed cookies and almonds until well combined. 
 Shape into sausage shapes. 
 Freeze for 3 â€“4 hours and serve. 
 Notes : 
 If the dough is too hard to shape you can add 1 or 2 tablespoons of milk or melted butter. 
 This recipe contains raw eggs 
 Original recipe yield: 1 - 13 inch sausage.
