Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Wadley Estate in Allen, Texas in 1990.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups freshly brewed black  coffee 
 1¼ ounce  tequila 
 ¼ ounce  Kahlúa 
 2 teaspoons  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Pour 1 cup of the hot coffee into each of two footed coffee glasses. 
 Divide the tequila, Kahlúa and sugar evenly among the glasses and stir until the sugar is completely dissolved. 
 Serve immediately.
