 Description : 
 In Romanian: Fasole verde cu rosii.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 3/4 lbs/750 g  green beans , 
 1 lb/500 g  tomatoes , 
 1  Onion , 
 3 tablespoons  oil , 
 1 teaspoon mixed chopped  parsley  and dill, 
 1/2 teaspoon  Sugar , 
 salt , 
 1 teaspoon  flour 
 Directions : 
 Clean the beans, wash and put in hot  water . After a few minutes of boiling, drain. 
 Fry the finely chopped  Onion  in  oil  with the  flour  and then add a little of the liquid in which the beans boiled. 
 Add the  tomato  sauce made from 1 lb/500 g  tomatoes  or the peeled, seeded and chopped  tomatoes , chopped  parsley  and dill,  Sugar ,  salt  and the well drained beans. 
 Let boil for a few minutes and then bake until the liquid is reduced. 
 It can be served hot or cold.
