Ingredients : 
 1 cup cooked and drained  white beans 
 2 tbsp chopped fresh  parsley 
 1 tbsp snipped  chives 
 1 tbsp chopped fresh  dill  (or 1 tsp dried  dill ) 
 ¼ tbsp  salt  (can eliminate this if using canned beans) 
 ⅛ tbsp  pepper 
 2 oz non-fat  cream cheese 
 1 tbsp fresh  lemon juice 
 8 slice favorite  bread  (recipe recommends dark  rye ) 
 1  cucumber , peeled and thinly sliced 
 lettuce 
 sprouts 
 Directions : 
 Combine first 8 ingredients in a food processor and process until smooth. 
 Taste for seasonings and add more herbs or  lemon juice  if necessary. 
 Spread mixture on bread, top with  cucumber ,  lettuce  and sprouts. 
 Put sandwiches together and slice.
