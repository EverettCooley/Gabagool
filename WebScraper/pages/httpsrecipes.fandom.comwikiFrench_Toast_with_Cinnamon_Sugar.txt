 Ingredients : 
 thick slices of white  bread  or any bread you prefer 
 6  eggs  (large are good) 
 enough  milk  to fill your bowl half full (1%, 2%, whole or  skim milk ) 
 sugar  to taste 
 1 tablespoon  vanilla 
 real  maple syrup 
 butter  or  cooking spray  for griddle 
 Directions : 
 Preheat griddle pan. 
 Whisk  eggs ,  milk ,  sugar  and  vanilla  together until you get a golden yellow mixture. 
 Add sliced bread one at a time and soak until completely covered but not falling apart. 
 Place each slice on the buttered or cooking sprayed griddle and cook until golden on each side. 
 As each slice comes off the griddle, sprinkle with a mixture of  cinnamon  and  sugar . 
 Serve with warm  maple syrup .
