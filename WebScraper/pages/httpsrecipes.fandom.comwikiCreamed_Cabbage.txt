Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Yield: 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 small head of  green cabbage  or 6 cups shredded 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ½ stick  butter  or  margarine 
 2 or 3 tsp  lemon juice 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 1  egg 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 Directions : 
 Coarse shred cabbage to make 6 cups. 
 Melt butter in large pan over medium-low heat. 
 Add cabbage and cook and stir about ½ hour or until wilted and tender. 
 Stir in salt, sugar, and lemon juice. 
 In separate bowl mix egg and sour cream. 
 Add mixture to cabbage and stir and cook until thickened. 
 Season with salt and pepper to taste.
