Ingredients : 
 1 cup + 1 tbsp  flour 
 ¾ cup baking  cocoa 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  baking soda 
 ¼ tsp  baking powder 
 5  ground almonds 
 1 cup real  butter 
 3 oz low-fat  cream cheese 
 2 cups  white sugar 
 3  eggs 
 3 tbsp mini- chocolate chips 
 2 tsp  vanilla extract 
 Directions : 
 In a large mixing bowl cream  butter ,  cream cheese ,  sugar , until light and fluffy. 
 Next add  eggs ,  vanilla extract . 
 Set aside in a separate bowl combine  flour ,  cocoa ,  almonds ,  baking soda ,  baking powder ,  salt 
 Then add to  butter  mixture mix well, then fold in  chocolate chips  spread mixture into a greased 13x9-inch rectangle baking dish. 
 Bake about 28 – 34 minutes at 325°F degree oven. 
 Remove and allow to cool.
