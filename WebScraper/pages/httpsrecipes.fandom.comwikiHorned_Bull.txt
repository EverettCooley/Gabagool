Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Wagner Estate in Greenville, Texas in 1984. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ ounces  tequila 
 3 ounces  orange juice 
 1 ounce lemonade 
 ½ ounce  grenadine 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in a blender with ice and blend until firm. 
 Garnish with orange wedge and cherry.
