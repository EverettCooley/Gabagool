Description : 
 Garfish balls. This recipe is an "as much as you want" main course. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 garfish  filets 
 mashed  potatoes 
 green onion  tops 
 garlic powder 
 tasso 
 chopped  onions 
 lard  or cooking  oil  ( peanut ) 
 Directions : 
 Take garfish filets and measure equal amount of cooked mashed  potatoes . 
 Grind filets and tasso together. 
 Coarse grind meat grinder is best. 
 If you use food processor, be careful not to over grind. 
 Tasso amount should be Â½ oz per pound of fish. 
 Increase will add more smoke flavor and seasoning, so adjust to your taste. 
 Add minimal amount of Cajun experience seasoning mix. 
 Tasso adds a lot of  salt  and  cayenne . 
 Mix in to  potatoes , add veggies and shape into balls. 
 Roll in  cornmeal . 
 Deep fry in  lard  for real Cajun taste, or  peanut oil  for les Americains, delicate children, Yankees, etc.
