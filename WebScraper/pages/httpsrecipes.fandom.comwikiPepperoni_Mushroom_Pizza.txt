 Description : 
 This white-hot appetizer for pizza is made divine with pepperoni and mushrooms. Keep the pizza dough warm by getting crisp and serve up a savory treat with other side dishes.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  egg , beaten 
 ½ cup (2 ounces) shredded  Cheddar  cheese 
 ½ to ¾ cup  tomato sauce 
 ¼ teaspoon  basil  leaves 
 ¼ teaspoon  garlic powder 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  oregano 
 1 tablespoon grated  Parmesan cheese 
 1 cup (4 ounces) shredded  mozzarella  cheese, divided 
 2 ounces  pepperoni , thinly sliced 
 ½ cup sliced fresh  mushrooms 
 1 tablespoon chopped fresh  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Combine the beaten  egg  and  Cheddar  cheese. Press into the buttered 12-inch pizza pan or 10-inch pie pan. Bake at 400 °F for 4 minutes. 
 Combine  tomato sauce ,  basil ,  garlic powder  and  oregano . Pour on the tomato sauce mixture by spreading. Sprinkle with  Parmesan cheese . 
 Layer ½ cup  mozzarella  cheese,  pepperoni  and  mushrooms . Top with remaining ½ cup  mozzarella  cheese and  parsley . 
 Bake at 400 °F for 8 to 10 minutes. Serve warm and plain but cut into serving pieces with the pizza cutter, the cleaver, or the chef's knife.
