 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1-2 tablespoons  Sugar 
 2 tablespoons  vinegar 
 2 tablespoons  worcestershire sauce 
 1/4 cup  chili sauce 
 6 slices  Bacon , cooked and crumbled 
 2 tablespoon cooking  oil 
 3 cups chopped  bok choy 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine first six ingredients in a wok or skillet; simmer 5 minutes. 
 Add  bok choy ; stir fry until crisp tender. Serve, with more  chili sauce  on the side.
