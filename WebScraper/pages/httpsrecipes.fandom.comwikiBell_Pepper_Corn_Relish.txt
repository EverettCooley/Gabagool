Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Pryor estate in Grapevine, Texas in 1996.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 5 ears fresh  corn 
 1 cup chopped  cabbage 
 ⅓ cup chopped  onion 
 ⅓ cup chopped  green pepper 
 ⅓ cup chopped  sweet red pepper 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 1½ teaspoon ground  mustard 
 ¾ teaspoon  celery seed 
 ¾ teaspoon  mustard seed 
 ¾ teaspoon  turmeric 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 cup  white vinegar 
 ¼ cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Cook  corn  5 minutes in boiling  water  then drain and cut kernels from cob. 
 Combine  corn  with remaining ingredients in large pan then bring to boil, cover, reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes. 
 Chill well.
