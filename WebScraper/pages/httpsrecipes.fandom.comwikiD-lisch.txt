 Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 oz.  rum 
 1/2 oz.  Grenadine 
 juice of 1/2  lime 
 Schweppes  ginger ale 
 Directions : 
 Pour ingredients over  ice  and stir. 
 Top off with  ginger ale . 
 Garnish with  cherry . Makes 8 oz. glass.
