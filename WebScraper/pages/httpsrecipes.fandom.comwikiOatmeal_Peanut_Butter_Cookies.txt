 Description : 
 Makes about 5 dozen
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  peanut butter 
 ⅓ cup  butter , softened 
 ½ cup packed  brown sugar 
 ½ cup  granulated sugar 
 2  eggs 
 2 tsp  vanilla 
 2¾ cup  quick-cooking oats 
 ⅔ cup  all-purpose flour 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 1 cup  raisins 
 Directions : 
 In bowl, beat together  peanut   butter ,  butter  and sugars until light and fluffy; beat in  eggs , one at a time, and  vanilla . 
 Combine  oats ,  flour ,  baking soda  and  salt ; using wooden spoon, gradually stir into bowl. 
 Stir in  raisins . 
 Roll heaping teaspoonfuls (5 ml) of batter into balls. 
 Place on lightly greased baking sheets and flatten with palm of hand. 
 Bake in 375°F (109°C) oven for 10 minutes or until golden. 
 Let cool on rack. 
 Can be stored in airtight container for up to 5 days. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per cookie: 
 
 about 65 calories | 2g protein | 3g fat | 9g carbohydrate
