 Description : 
 Makes 3 to 4 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 medium tart cooking  apple , cored and chopped (about 1 cup) 
 ½ cup sliced  celery 
 ⅓ cup sliced  green onion 
 3 tablespoons seedless  raisins 
 2 teaspoons  butter  or  margarine 
 1½ cups cooked  rice 
 ½ teaspoon  curry  powder 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon seasoned  pepper 
 1 x 6½-ounce can  tuna , drained and flaked 
 ¼ cup  mayonnaise 
 1 teaspoon  lemon juice 
 cherry tomatoes , for garnish (optional) 
 fresh  parsley  sprigs, for garnish (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Cook  apple ,  celery ,  onion  and  raisins  in  butter  in large skillet until tender crisp. 
 Stir in  rice ,  curry  powder,  salt  and  pepper ; heat thoroughly. 
 Transfer to serving platter. 
 Arrange  tuna  on top of  rice  mixture. 
 Blend  mayonnaise  and  lemon juice  in small bowl; spoon over  tuna . 
 Garnish with  tomatoes  and  parsley , if desired.
