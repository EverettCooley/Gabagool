Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  Crisco 
 2 sticks  butter 
 2¾ cups  sugar 
 6  eggs 
 3¼ cups  flour 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 ¼ cup  milk 
 ¾ cup undrained, crushed  pineapple 
 1 cup  coconut 
 1 cup  pecans 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Cream Crisco, butter, and sugar. 
 Add eggs; mix well. 
 Combine dry ingredients add with the milk to sugar mixture. 
 Stir in pineapple, coconut, pecans, and vanilla; mix well. 
 Pour into a greased and floured tube pan. 
 Bake at 325 °F for 1½ hours. 
 Enjoy.
