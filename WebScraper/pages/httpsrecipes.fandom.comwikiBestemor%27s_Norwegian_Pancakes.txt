 Description : 
       Bestemor's Norwegian Pancakes     
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 	 eggs 
 2 	cups  milk 
 2 	tablespoons melted  butter 
 1 	cup  flour 
 Directions : 
 Beat  eggs . 
 Add  milk  and melted buuter and mix . 
 Slowly mix in  flour . 
 Add batter to form a layer on pan. 
 Flip pancake over and cook. 
 Spread  butter  on pancake and spread  maple syrup .
