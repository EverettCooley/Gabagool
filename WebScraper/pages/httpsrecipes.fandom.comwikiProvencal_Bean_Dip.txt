 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 small clove  garlic , peeled 
 19-oz. can  cannellini beans , reserve 1/3 cup liquid, rinse and drain 
 1/4 cup fresh  basil  leaves or 1 1/2 tsp. dried  basil 
 1 Tbs.  rosemary  infused  olive oil  or extra-virgin  olive oil  plus 1/4 tsp. dried  rosemary 
 1/4 tsp.  salt 
 1/4 tsp. freshly ground  black pepper 
 Fresh vegetables and  breadsticks 
 Directions : 
 In food processor, mince  garlic . Add beans,  basil ,  oil ,  salt  and  pepper ; process until well blended. Cover; chill at least 30 minutes or up to 8 hours. Serve dip at room temperature with crudit√©s and bread sticks.
