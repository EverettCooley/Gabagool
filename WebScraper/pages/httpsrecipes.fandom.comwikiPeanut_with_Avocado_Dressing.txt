 Description : 
 Serves 2
 
 Ingredients : 
 2  avocadoes , ripe but firm 
 2 tbsp shelled  peanuts 
 ½ tsp  paprika 
 ½ tsp  cinnamon 
 chilli powder  and  salt , to taste 
 fresh  chives , to garnish 
 Directions : 
 Chop the  avocado  into cubes. 
 Place  peanuts  and spices in food processor and grind into a fine mixture. 
 Sprinkle mixture over  avocado  with finely chopped  chives . 
 Chill and serve.
