Ingredients : 
 4 large  potatoes 
 2 tablespoons of  butter 
 3 tablespoons of  bacon  chips 
 2 tablespoons of  parsley  finely chopped 
 salt 
 Aluminum foil 
 Directions : 
 Wash the  potatoes  very thoroughly and wrap in aluminum foil, put in the oven 20 minutes or until they are cooked. 
 Unwrap the  potatoes , make a cut along the length of it, put a little  butter  and sprinkle with  salt . 
 Decorate with  cheese  and  bacon  chips and put in the oven for some minutes before serving.
