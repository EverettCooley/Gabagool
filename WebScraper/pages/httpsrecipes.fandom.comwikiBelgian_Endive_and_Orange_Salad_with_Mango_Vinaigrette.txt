 Description : 
 
 The  mango  vinaigrette is also good as a marinade with broiled or baked  chicken , or brushed on a bruschetta for a different first course or hors d'oeuvre.
 
 Ingredients : 
 Mango Vinaigrette : 
 1½ tsp  white wine vinegar 
 2 tbsp fresh  lime juice 
 1 cup diced  mango 
 1 tbsp  honey 
 ½ tsp freshly ground  black pepper 
 ⅛ tsp ground  cayenne pepper 
 ⅛ tsp  salt 
 Salad : 
 1 head purple  kale , washed 
 4  Belgian endive , washed 
 2 large seedless  oranges , peel and section 
 ½ cup  pecan  halves 
 Directions : 
 Mango Vinaigrette : 
 Place all the vinaigrette ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth. 
 Let stand at room temperature while you prepare the salad. 
 Salad : 
 Place a couple of leaves of  kale  on each of 4 individual salad plates. 
 Equally divide the  Endive  and  oranges  on each plate, sprinkle  pecans  on top, and add a spoonful of  mango  vinaigrette.
