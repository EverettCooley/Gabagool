Link: https://recipes.fandom.com/wiki/Apple_Martini


 Ingredients : 
 1 oz Absolut  vodka 
 1 oz DeKuyper  Sour Apple Pucker 
 1 oz  apple juice 
 Directions : 
 Pour all ingredients into a shaker. 
 Shake well and strain into a cocktail glass.
