Description : 
 This is a good dip for artichokes. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: My old recipes 
 Makes 4 cups 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  mayonnaise 
 2 cups  sour cream 
 ¼ cup fresh  lemon juice 
 2½ tsp finely grated  lemon peel 
 2 tsp white  horseradish 
 2 tsp  Dijon mustard 
 1 tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients in large bowl and blend. 
 Cover and refrigerate. 
 Adjust seasoning before serving.
