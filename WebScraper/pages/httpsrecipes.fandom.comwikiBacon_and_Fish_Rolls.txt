 Ingredients : 
 8 back slices of  bacon 
 8 small fillets of  fish  ( banga mary ,  butterfish  or  mullet ) 
 salt  and  pepper 
 lemon juice 
 sprigs of  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Remove  bacon  rind and flatten the slices with the back of a knife. 
 Put a fish fillet on each slice, add  salt  and  pepper  and a squeeze of  lemon juice . 
 Roll up and secure with a toothpick. 
 Place on a greased pan and bake in a moderate oven for 20 â€“ 30 minutes. 
 Serve with  egg  sauce. 
 Garnish with  parsley .
