Ingredients : 
 1 cup  cranberries  — washed and halved 
 1¼ cup  sugar  — divided 
 1 cup  flour 
 ¼ cup  cornstarch 
 1 tbsp  baking powder 
 ½ tbsp  baking soda 
 ½ tbsp  salt 
 ⅔ cup  buttermilk 
 2  egg whites  — lightly beaten 
 1 tbsp  orange peel 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Line 13 x 9" pan with foil; spray with nonstick spray. 
 Combine cranberries with 1 cup  sugar , flour,  cornstarch ,  baking powder , baking soda and  salt . 
 Mix well and make well in center. 
 Add  milk ,  egg whites  and  orange  peel; stir until smooth. 
 Spread in prepared foil-lined pan. 
 Bake 20 – 25 minutes. 
 Cool in pan for 5 min; cut into 12 squares.
