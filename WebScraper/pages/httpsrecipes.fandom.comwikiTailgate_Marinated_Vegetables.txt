Ingredients : 
 2 cups whole kernel  corn , drained 
 2 cups  green beans , drained 
 2 cups  red kidney beans , drained and rinsed 
 ½ cup  celery , chopped 
 ½ cup  carrots , thinly sliced 
 ½ cup  yellow squash , thinly sliced 
 ½ cup  zucchini , thinly sliced 
 ½ cup  broccoli  florets 
 ½ cup  cauliflower 
 6  pickled okra , sliced 
 1 (4 ounce) jar  pimentos 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Dressing : 
 1 cup  vinegar 
 1 cup  vegetable oil 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Place vegetables in large bowl. 
 Mix well. 
 add dressing and refrigerate overnight. 
 Place in red, green and/or yellow  peppers . 
 Serve with fried  chicken , sliced  tomatoes  and  onions  and your favorite beverage for a great tailgate party.
