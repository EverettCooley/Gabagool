 Ingredients : 
 1 shot De Kuyper  triple sec 
 1 shot Stolichnaya  vodka 
 1.5 shots  tequila 
 Top up  orange juice 
 Directions : 
 Half fill highball glass with cubed  ice . Shake together in a cocktail shaker the De Kuyper  triple sec , Stolichnaya  vodka  and  tequila . Pour into the highball glass and top up with  orange juice . Garnish with a slice of  orange  if desired.
