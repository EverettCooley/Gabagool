Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Lynch Estate in Garland, Texas in 1994.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 8 large white  onions  chopped 
 5  sweet red peppers  chopped 
 5  green peppers  chopped 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 quart  white vinegar 
 4 teaspoons  salt 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients and cook until slightly thickened (about 45 minutes), stirring occasionally. 
 Pack into jars and seal. 
 Store in refrigerator.
