 Prep Time: 15 min 
 Cook time: 10-15 min 
 Serves: 4-6 
 
 Description : 
 An easy, healthy, surprisingly flavorful dish from simple on-hand ingredients.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  chicken broth 
 1 head  broccoli , chopped into ¾ inch pieces 
 1 roasted red pepper, diced 
 10 oz  penne  or  rigatoni , cooked 
 3 cloves  garlic , minced 
 2 oz  Parmesan cheese , shredded 
 ¼ tsp (or to taste)  red pepper flakes 
 Directions : 
 Heat the chicken broth in a large skillet to a simmer 
 Add the garlic and broccoli, simmer about 6-8 minutes, or till broccoli is bright green and crisp-tender 
 Add the roasted red peppers, cooked pasta and red pepper flakes, and stir well 
 Heat another two minutes till pasta is warmed through 
 Sprinkle Parmesan over the top of the pasta and serve.
