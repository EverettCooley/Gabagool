 Prep Time: 10 minutes 
 Cook time: 8 hours 
 Serves: 4-6 
 
 Description : 
 Fast and simple crock pot recipe to make an excellent roast.
 
 Ingredients : 
 beef  roast (one that will fit in crock pot — I use 3#) 
 8 oz  Italian dressing 
 ½ cup water 
 Directions : 
 Put the thawed roast in the crock pot. Place fat side up (if there's a fat side.) 
 Put about ½ cup water in the bottom of the crock pot. 
 Pour 8 oz Italian dressing over the roast. 
 Turn crock pot onto Low setting. 
 Let it roast 8 hours.
