      Amish Apple Cake     
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup chopped  pecans 
 2½ cups finely chopped  apples 
 ½ cup  butter , softened 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1  egg 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 ½ tsp  nutmeg 
 ½ tsp  vanilla 
 1 cup  all purpose flour 
 Caramel sauce : 
 ½ cup  butter 
 1 cup  brown sugar 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 ½ cup  evaporated milk 
 Directions : 
 Cream the  butter . 
 Add the  sugar  and beat until fluffy. 
 Add the  egg  and beat until well blended. 
 Mix in the soda,  salt ,  cinnamon  and  nutmeg . 
 Add the  flour  and stir. 
 Stir in the  apples  and  nuts . 
 Pour into an oiled cake pan and bake in an oven for 30 minutes. 
 Serve with hot  Caramel sauce . 
 Caramel sauce : 
 Melt the  butter ,  brown sugar  and  salt . 
 Bring to a boil. 
 Remove from heat and whisk in  vanilla  and  milk . 
 Serve warm sauce over cake.
