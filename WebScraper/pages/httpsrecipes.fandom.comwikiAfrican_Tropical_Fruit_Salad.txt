      African Tropical Fruit Salad     
 Description : 
 This traditional African fruit salad is made with lime juice and brown sugar, giving it a unique flavor.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups of ½-inch cubed fresh or canned  pineapple 
 2 medium  oranges , peeled and divided into sections 
 2 medium  mangoes , peeled and diced 
 2  bananas , cut in ½ inch slices 
 2 tablespoons  lime juice 
 2 tablespoons  dark brown sugar 
 flaked  coconut 
 Directions : 
 Combine fruits in a bowl. 
 Combine  lime juice and sugar, and add to fruits; toss to coat. 
 Cover and chill at least 1 hour. 
 Sprinkle with coconut before serving.
