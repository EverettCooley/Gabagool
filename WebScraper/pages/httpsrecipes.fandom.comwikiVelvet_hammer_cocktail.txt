      Velvet hammer     A  velvet hammer cocktail  is a  rich, creamy cocktail made with  Cointreau  or  triple sec ,  Tia Maria ,  heavy cream  and sometimes  Brandy . The mixture is shaken with ice and strained into a cocktail glass. The result is smooth but potent.
