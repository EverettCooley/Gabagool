Description : 
 Polish poppyseed cookies
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ lb  butter 
 2¾ cups  flour 
 ⅔ cup  confectioners' sugar 
 2 teaspoons  baking powder 
 2  egg yolks 
 1  egg 
 3 tablespoons  sour cream 
 3 tablespoons  poppyseeds 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients and mix well. 
 Knead dough for a few minutes and refrigerate for 15 minutes. 
 Roll out dough ⅛ inch thick. 
 Use cooking cutters to cut dough and place on a greased cookie sheet. 
 Bake for 10 minutes at 375°F degrees.
