      Leeks     
 Ingredients : 
 1 bunch  leeks 
 water  to cover 
 salt  to taste 
 butter , melted or whipped 
 Directions : 
 Cut off green top and rinse to remoe sand. 
 Cook in salted boiling  water  until tender. 
 Drain, serve with  butter .
