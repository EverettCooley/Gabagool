Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: my old recipes 
 Serves 4 to 6 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs lean  ground beef 
 2 tbsp  oil 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 1 can  tomatoes  (16 oz) 
 1 tbsp  Tabasco sauce 
 ¼ tbsp  cayenne pepper 
 1 tbsp  salt 
 1½ tbsp fiery  chili powder 
 ½ tbsp ground  cumin 
 ½ tbsp ground  oregano 
 ½ tbsp  paprika 
 ½ tbsp ground  cloves 
 ½ tbsp ground  allspice 
 1 tbsp  brown sugar 
 1  bay leaf , crushed 
 ½ cup dry  red wine 
 1 can (29 oz)  kidney beans , drained 
 Directions : 
 Pour  oil  in skillet. 
 Add meat and brown with  garlic . 
 Transfer meat to large pot. 
 Add remaining ingredients except wine and beans. 
 Simmer for 1 hour. 
 Add  water  occasionally if needed. 
 Add wine land beans, simmer for 15 minutes more.
