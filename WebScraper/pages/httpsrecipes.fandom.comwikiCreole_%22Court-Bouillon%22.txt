Ingredients : 
 21.6 oz to 28.8 oz of fish 
 1  onion , sliced 
 2  tomatoes , peeled

 Â¼ litre of  water 
 Marinade : 
 salt

 2  limes

 Directions : 
 Keep the fish marinated for one hour. 
 Cook the sliced  onion  and the peeled  tomatoes  in  oil . 
 When all is brown, add a bowl of  water  and a  garlic  clove, boil it and dip the into it. 
 Simmer for 10 to 20 minutes (depends on the thickness of the fish). 
 Serve hot with the juice from the cooking accompanied with the juice of the  lime  and  hot pepper  if you wish.
