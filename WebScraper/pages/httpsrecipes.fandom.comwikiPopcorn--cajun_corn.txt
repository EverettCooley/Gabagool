Ingredients : 
 1/4 	cup 	Unpopped  popcorn  (8 cups Popped) 
 4 	tbsp 	 butter 
 1 	tsp 	 paprika 
 1 	tsp 	 lemon pepper 
 1/2 	tsp 	 Onion  powder 
 1/2 	tsp 	 garlic powder 
 1/2 	tsp 	Ground  cayenne pepper 
 Directions : 
 Pop  popcorn . 
Melt  butter  and add the spices. 
Drizzle over the unseasoned  popcorn  and toss until well blended.
