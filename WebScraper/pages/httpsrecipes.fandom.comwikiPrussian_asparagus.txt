 About Prussian asparagus : 
 Wikipedia Article  About Prussian Asparagus on Wikipedia 
 Ornithogalum pyrenaicum, also called Prussian asparagus, Pyrenees star of Bethlehem or spiked star of Bethlehem, is a plant whose young shoots may be eaten as a vegetable, similar to asparagus.
