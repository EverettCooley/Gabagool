Description : 
 This fresh steak dish is made with mushroom and onion soups and real beef broth, baked to a flaming hot aroma, and served with rice, noodles, or mashed potatoes.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cans  cream of mushroom  soup 
 ½ package dry  onion soup mix 
 1 large  round steak 
 ½ cup  beef broth 
 Directions : 
 Preheat an oven to 300° F. 
 In a medium-sized bowl, mix together the cream of mushroom soup, onion soup mix, and  beef broth . 
 Place round  steak  in a casserole dish and pour soup mixture over the  steak . 
 Bake for 3 hours. Serve with  rice , noodles, or mashed  potatoes .
