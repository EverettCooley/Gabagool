Ingredients : 
 1 oz  gin 
 ½ oz  brandy 
 ½ oz  sweet vermouth 
 ½ oz  dry vermouth 
 1 dash  bitters 
 lemon peel 
 Directions : 
 Shake all the ingredients into a shaker and strain into a cocktail glass. 
 Garnish with  lemon peel . 
 Can be served with  ice .
