      Vanilla Cream Shooter     
 Ingredients : 
 ½ shot Disaronno  amaretto 
 ½ shot  Amarula 
 ½ shot Stolichnaya vanilla  vodka 
 Directions : 
 Layer all ingredients in a glass, Disaronno first, then the Amarula, then the  vodka .
