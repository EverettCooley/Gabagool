 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 coconut  meat - 1 dry  coconut 
 garlic  - 2 cloves 
 1/2 tsp  salt 
 cilantro  - 1/4 cup 
 lime juice  - 2 tbsp 
 Directions : 
 Roast the  coconut  meat over a low flame until golden brown and smells toasted. Peel off the hard brown outer coating, then grate finely in a food processor. 
 Finely crush the  garlic  with the  salt  until paste is formed. Add smashed  garlic ,  cilantro  and  lime juice  and blend well.
