Description : 
 Makes 8 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 dash of  salt 
 3½ cups  milk 
 1 cup flaked  coconut 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 toasted flaked  coconut  for garnish (optional) 
 Meringue : 
 1  egg white 
 ¾ cup  sugar 
 ½ teaspoon  instant coffee powder 
 ¼ teaspoon  cream of tartar 
 2 teaspoons  rum extract 
 ¼ cup boiling  water 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  sugar ,  butter ,  salt  and  milk  in large saucepan. 
 Cook over low heat until thick and creamy, about 30 to 40 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
 Stir in  coconut  and  vanilla . 
 Chill. 
 Spoon into dessert dishes; top with meringue. 
 Garnish with additional  coconut , if desired. 
 Meringue : 
 Combine  egg  white,  sugar ,  coffee  powder,  cream of tartar  and  rum extract  in small deep bowl; mix well. 
 Pour in boiling  water ; beat at high speed until meringue stands in stiff peaks.
