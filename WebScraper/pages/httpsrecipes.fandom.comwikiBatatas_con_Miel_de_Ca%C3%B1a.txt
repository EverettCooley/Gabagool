Description : 
 A speciality of Malaga, Spain. Sweet potatoes with molasses.
 
 Ingredients : 
 sweet potatoes 
 miel de cana  ( molasses  and  syrup  mix) 
 water 
 Directions : 
 Cut peeled potatoes after into small pieces and boil in molasses with a little water,. 
 Keep stirring to prevent from catching on the bottom of the pan. 
 Eaten cold or sometimes flavoured with lemon rind or cinnamon, or both at once.
