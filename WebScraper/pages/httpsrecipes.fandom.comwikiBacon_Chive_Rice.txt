Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 slices  bacon , diced 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 ¼ cup chopped  chives 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅛ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1 tablespoon  worcestershire sauce  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Cook  bacon  until crisp in large skillet. Drain fat. 
 Add  rice ,  chives ,  salt ,  pepper  and  worcestershire sauce . Mix well and heat thoroughly.
