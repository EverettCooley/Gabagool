Ingredients : 
 3/4 Cup  all-purpose flour 
 1/2 Cup No-Calorie Sweetener 
 1/2 Cup  unsweetened cocoa powder 
 1/4 Teaspoon  baking soda 
 1/2 Cup Evaporated Fat-Free  milk 
 1/3 Cup  butter , Melted 
 1/4 Cup Cholesterol-Free  egg substitute 
 1/4 Cup  Honey 
 1 Teaspoon  vanilla 
 1/2 (15-1/2-Ounce) Can Pitted Tart  red cherries , drained And Halved 
 Directions : 
 Preheat Oven to 350?f. Grease 11x7-inch baking pan; set Aside. 
 Stir together  flour , sweetener,  cocoa powder  and  baking soda  in large mixing bowl. Add  milk ,  butter ,  egg substitute ,  honey  and  vanilla . Stir until just mixed. Pour into prepared pan. 
 Sprinkle  cherries  over top of  chocolate  mixture. Bake 13 to 15 Minutes or until wooden pick inserted into center comes out clean. Cool. Cut into 12 equal-size brownies.
