Description : 
 Makes a thick sheet in a biscuit pan.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup New Orleans  molasses 
 1 cup boiling  water 
 2 cups Gold Medal  flour , heaped 
 Â½ cup  butter 
 1  egg 
 1 teaspoon  soda 
 1 teaspoon  ginger 
 2 tablespoons  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Stir  butter  and  sugar  together. 
 Rub it into the  flour  till fine. 
 Add  molasses  and yolk of  egg  and beat well. 
 Add the boiling  water  and white of  egg  beaten stiff.
