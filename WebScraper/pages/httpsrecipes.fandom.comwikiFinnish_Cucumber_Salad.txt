 Description : 
       Finnish Cucumber Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 5	sm	 cucumbers  -- peeled and	Sliced 
 salt 
 4	tbl	Dillâ€”chopped 
 1	x	 garlic  clove -- crushed 
 1	cup	 sour cream 
 ds  Sugar 
 2	tbl	Fresh  tomato  paste 
 2	tbl	 wine vinegar 
 1/4	cup	 olive oil 
 Fresh ground  pepper 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Sprinkle some  salt  over the sliced  cucumbers , leave for half an hour. Use a strainer to strain out the liquid. Remove the sliced  cucumbers  into a bowl.
Mix  cream , dill,  garlic ,  pepper  and  Sugar . Add the  tomato  paste,  vinegar  and  oil  while mixing. Pour the dressing over the  cucumbers  and chill well.
