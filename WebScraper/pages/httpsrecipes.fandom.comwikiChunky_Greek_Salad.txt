Description : 
 This was ok, but not terribly exciting. The original recipe called for ¼ cup olive oil and ¼ teaspoon oregano. I used Caesar dressing to give it a bit more flavor.
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Original recipe 
 Makes about 8 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 medium  cucumber , chopped, 7 ounces 
 2 cups diced  tomato , about 12 ounces 
 ¼ cup  red onion , chopped, 1¼ ounces 
 6 ounces  feta  cheese, crumbled 
 ¼ cup  Caesar salad dressing  
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients and chill well before serving. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per Serving: 
 
 104 Calories | 8g Fat | 4g Protein | 4g Carbohydrate | 1g Dietary Fiber | 3g Net Carbs
