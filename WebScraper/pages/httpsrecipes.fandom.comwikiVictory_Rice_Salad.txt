Description : 
 Makes 8 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup  sour cream 
 ⅓ cup  Italian salad dressing 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 ½ teaspoon  sugar 
 ⅓ cup finely chopped  onion 
 1 x 1-pound can cut  green beans , drained 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 ⅓ cup crumbled cooked  bacon 
 3 small  tomatoes , quartered 
 Directions : 
 Blend  sour cream , dressing,  salt ,  pepper  and  sugar . 
 Pour over  onion ,  green beans ,  rice  and  bacon ; toss lightly. 
 Chill. 
 Spoon into salad bowl; garnish with  tomato  wedges.
