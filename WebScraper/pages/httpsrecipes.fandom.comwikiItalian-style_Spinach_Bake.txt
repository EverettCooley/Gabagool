Description : 
 An international combination of real spinach, ground beef, cheese, and herbs is made and cooked with vegetables, spices, rice, milk, and eggs and baked to fine perfection, as it is great for breakfast, dinner, or lunch and keeps a golden taste with the vegetables, cheese, and meat.
 
         
 Ingredients : 
 ½ pound lean  ground beef 
 1 cup chopped  onion , red, yellow, or white only 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 x 10-ounce package frozen chopped  spinach , thawed 
 1 cup (4 ounces) shredded Monterey  jack cheese  or Provolone 
 ¾ cup  milk 
 4 large  eggs , beaten 
 ½ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1 x 15-ounce can  tomato sauce   or  (1 pack spaghetti sauce) 
 ½ teaspoon  garlic powder 
 ½ teaspoon  basil  leaves 
 ½ teaspoon  oregano  leaves 
 ½ teaspoon seasoned  pepper 
 ½ cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 3 tablespoons Italian seasoning, reserving 1 tablespoon for garnishing if desired 
 Directions : 
 Cook  beef ,  onion  and  garlic  in large skillet over medium heat until meat is no longer pink. 
 Add  rice ,  spinach  and  Monterey jack   cheese ; mix well. 
 Combine  milk ,  eggs ,  pepper , and 2 tablespoons Italian seasoning (optional); stir into  rice  mixture. 
 Turn into a greased shallow 2-quart baking dish. 
 Bake at 350° F for 30 minutes or until set. 
 Meanwhile, blend  tomato  sauce (or spaghetti sauce),  garlic powder ,  basil ,  oregano  and seasoned  pepper  in a small saucepan. Simmer about 5 minutes. 
 Cut the cooked  spinach  mixture into squares and serve topped with  tomato  sauce,  Parmesan cheese , and the remaining Italian seasoning (optional).
