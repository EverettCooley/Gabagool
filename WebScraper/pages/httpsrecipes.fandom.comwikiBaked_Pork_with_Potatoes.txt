 Description : 
 Serve 4.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 kg  pork  (chops or leg) 
 100g  onions 
 600ml  Beef stock 
 4 cloves chopped  garlic 
 1 kg  potatoes 
 5Og cooking  fat 
 salt  and  pepper 
 1 tblsp.dried  basil 
 Directions : 
 Cut the meat into four portions 
 Peel the  potatoes  and  onions , and cut into thick slices. 
 Prepare a well greased baking dish. 
 Put a layer of  potatoes  and  onions , arrange the meat season,sprinkle the crushed  garlic , cover with the remaining  potatoes  and  onions . 
 Add stock and fat then bake for at least 2 hours. 
 On low heat Check the stock frequently, adding more if required.
