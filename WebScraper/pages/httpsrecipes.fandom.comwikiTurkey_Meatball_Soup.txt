Description : 
 This recipe comes from a nurse friend. She brought this to me a few years ago when I had minor surgery. This was so comforting. I now make this for friends who need a pot full of love and comfort when times are tough.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  ground turkey 
 1  egg 
 ¼ cup chopped  onion 
 ½ teaspoon  garlic salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 1 cube  beef bouillon  cube 
 1½ cups water 
 1 can condensed  cream of mushroom  soup 
 4  carrots  coarsely chopped 
 2 stalks  celery  chopped 
 1 onion  chopped 
 1 can whole kernel  corn  drained 
 Directions : 
 Mix together ground turkey, egg, onion, garlic salt and pepper then form into meatballs. 
 Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat then brown meatballs in oil. 
 Transfer meatballs to a paper towel to drain. 
 In a soup pot boil water and dissolve bouillon cube in the boiling water. 
 Stir in the undiluted mushroom soup. 
 Add carrots, celery, onion and corn. 
 Add meatballs to the soup and bring to a boil. 
 Simmer for 20 minutes then serve.
