Ingredients : 
 6 ripe  plantains 
 juice from three  oranges 
 2 tbsp of  rum 
 ¾ cup bee's  honey 
 1 tsp of powder of  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Peel the  plantains  and cut them in small pieces. 
 Place them in a Pyrex bowl and cover them with the  orange juice ,  rum , and bee  honey . 
 Sprnkle the  cinnamon  on top. 
 Bake in a pre-heated oven at 350°F for approximately 30 minutes.
