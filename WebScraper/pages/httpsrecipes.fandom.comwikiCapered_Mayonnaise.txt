Serve on sliced ripe  tomatoes  or other veggies, use as a dip for tortilla chips or raw vegetables, or spread a small amount on  French bread  to serve with your favorite  Pasta  dish.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 med  garlic  cloves 
 3 tbsp salad * 2 tbsp  capers , drained 
 1/4 tsp  Sugar 
 2 tsp  vinegar , balsamic, wine or  apple cider 
 1/4 cup low-fat  Mayonnaise  or  salad dressing  such as miracle whip 
 1 tbsp snipped  parsley  or 1-1/2 tsp dried  parsley  flakes 
 dash  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Place  garlic ,  capers ,  Sugar  and  vinegar  in a food processor. Puree, scraping down sides several times. 
 Add  Mayonnaise , and continue to puree and scrape sides down into blade. 
 Add the snipped  parsley  and  black pepper  to taste. Stir to mix.
