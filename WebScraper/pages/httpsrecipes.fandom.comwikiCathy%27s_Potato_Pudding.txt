 Description : 
 From my Aunt Lois's massive collection. Date on note paper says 1918. It has 3 blue stars on it. According to my Aunt Barbara, anything in my Aunt Lois's collection with blue stars on it meant it was a "keeper". I make this on a regular basis and it is very good.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 large  potatoes 
 3  eggs , separated 
 1 cup  granulated sugar 
 Â¼ cup  all-purpose flour 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 cup  heavy cream 
 3 tablespoons fresh  lemon  juice 
 1 teaspoon fresh grated  lemon  zest 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Boil, mash and cool the  potatoes . 
 Mix with  egg   yolks . When well blended, add  egg  whites, which have been well beaten and combined with  Sugar )  flour ,  salt ,  cream ,  lemon  juice and grated lemon rind. 
 Bake in a buttered dish in a 350 oven for 30 minutes. 
 Serve with  Sugar  and  cream . 
 Note 
 
 I have added fresh  Berries  to the top and it's really good that way as well.
