 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1lb  Chicken  pieces cut very small 
 1  Onion , chopped 
 1  carrot , chopped 
 Couple of large  tomatoes 
 1/2lb Spring  cabbage 
 1 tbsp	 mixed spice  and gravy  browning . 
 UGALI 
 1lb  maize   flour 
 Directions : 
 Fry the  Chicken  pieces with chopped  Onion  and a tablespoon of gravy  browning . Simmer for 15 minutes with a pint of  water . Then add the chopped  cabbage , the  carrot  and the  mixed spice . 
 Simmer ten more minutes and add the  tomato . The meat is now ready. 
 For the Ugali, boil a pint of  water  and pour in the  maize   flour . Stir as it cooks into a stiff cake. #Serve into portions and pour the  Chicken  over.
