 Description : 
 Makes 4 servings 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2¾ cups  skim milk 
 ¼ tsp ground  cinnamon 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 ⅓ cup  unsweetened cocoa powder 
 3 tbsp  cornstarch 
 ⅓ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 In saucepan, heat 2 cups  milk  and the  cinnamon  until hot. 
 In another pan, mix  sugar ,  cocoa ,  cornstarch  and  salt . 
 Blend in remaining ¾ cup of  milk ; add hot  milk . 
 Over medium low-heat, stir until boiling. 
 Over low heat, cook 1 minutes stirring constantly. 
 Pour into dishes, chill. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 183 calories | 1 g fat
