Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 each  egg  white, room temperature 
 2 tsp  granulated sugar 
 1 each serving non-stick spray(pam) 
 1 tsp  powdered sugar , divided 
 1/8 tsp  white vinegar 
 1/8 tsp  vanilla extract 
 1/2 cup fresh  strawberries  or  kiwi 
 1 tbsp whipped topping 
 Directions : 
 Use a shallow rectangular baking dish, approximately 6 by 8 inches. Spray with non-stick  butter  flavored  cooking spray . Line with wax paper cut to fit. Spray again with pam, dust lightly with ½ of the  powdered sugar . Set aside. Whip  egg  white until firm peaks form. Add  Sugar ,  vinegar  and  vanilla  and whip until stiff peaks form, about 30 seconds to 1 minute more. Spread evenly in baking dish, and cook in microwave oven for 1 ½ to 2 minutes on high. The meringue will stretch and spread while baking, but will shrink back to proper size while cooling, so don"t panic! cool meringue, and turn out onto wax paper that has been lightly dusted with the remaining  powdered sugar . Spread with the tablespoon of whipped topping, top with the fruit and gently roll up. Garnish with small piece of fruit and  mint  leaves
