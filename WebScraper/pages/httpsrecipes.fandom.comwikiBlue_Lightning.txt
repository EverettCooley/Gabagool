Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅓ oz Blue  curaçao  liqueur 
 ⅓ oz  Southern Comfort ® peach liqueur 
 ⅓ oz  Wild Turkey ®  Bourbon whiskey 
 5 oz  lime  mix 
 Directions : 
 Pour the blue  curaçao ,  Southern Comfort  and  Wild Turkey  into a highball glass almost filled with  ice  cubes. 
 Fill with prepared  lime  mix, and serve.
