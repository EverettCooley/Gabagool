      Amish Biscuits     
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  all purpose flour 
 ½ teaspoon  baking soda 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 2 teaspoons  baking powder 
 2  eggs , beaten 
 1 cup  Amish starter 
 ¼ cup  vegetable oil 
 ¼ cup  butter , melted 
 Directions : 
 Combine  flour ,  baking soda ,  salt  and  baking powder . 
 In a separate bowl combine  eggs ,  oil . 
 Mix well. 
 Add starter to dry ingredients and mix. 
 Transfer to a lightly floured surface and roll. 
 Cut out biscuits and place on a lightly grease  cookie sheet . 
 brush  top of biscuits with melted  butter . 
 Cover and let rise 30 minutes. 
 Bake in a  preheated  350 °F oven for 15 – 20 minutes.
