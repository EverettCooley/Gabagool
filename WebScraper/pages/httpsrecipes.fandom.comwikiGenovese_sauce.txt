Also known as  pesto , this green sauce made of fresh basil leaves, parsley, pine nuts, extra virgin olive oil, garlic and Parmesan or romano cheese. It may be used as sauce as well as a seasoning to other dishes, or as part of topping for appetizers. There are several variations; notably in Sicily,  tomato is added and basil omitted .
