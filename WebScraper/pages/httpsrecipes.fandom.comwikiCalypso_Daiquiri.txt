 Ingredients : 
 1 1/4 oz. Myer's  Jamaican rum 
 2 1/2 oz. Sour mix 
 1/2 oz. Half & Half 
 1  banana 
 1 tsp.  vanilla extract 
 Directions : 
 Blend with  ice  and serve in a hurricane glass.
