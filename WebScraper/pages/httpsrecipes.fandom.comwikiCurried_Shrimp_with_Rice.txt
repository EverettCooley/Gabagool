Makes 8 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 2  Pears , peeled, cored and sliced 
 1/2 cup sliced green  onion 
 1/2 cup  raisins 
 1/4 cup  light cream 
 1 teaspoon  curry powder 
 1/4 teaspoon  salt 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 3 cups cooked  rice , cooked in  chicken broth 
 1 pound cooked medium  shrimp 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Pears ,  green onion  and  Raisins  in  butter  in large skillet over medium-high heat. 
 Stir in  light cream ,  curry  powder,  salt  and  pepper ; cook 1 minute. 
 Add  rice  and  Shrimp ; heat thoroughly.
