 Makes 8 servings of Sicilian swordfish. 
 Ingredients : 
 3 tbsp  olive oil 
 1 medium  onion , thinly sliced 
 4  anchovy  fillets, drained and chopped 
 28 oz can whole  tomatoes  in purée, drained and broken up 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  black pepper 
 ½ tsp dried  oregano 
 ¼ tsp crushed  red pepper flakes 
 4  swordfish  steaks 
 grated rind of 1  lemon 
 Directions : 
 In skillet, heat oil. 
 Add onion, saute 5 minutes. 
 Stir in anchovy, cook 5 minutes. 
 Add tomatoes, ¾ tsp salt, ⅛ tsp pepper, oregano and pepper flakes, simmer uncovered 10 minutes. 
 Heat oven to 350°F. 
 Grease a baking dish. 
 Season swordfish with the remaining salt and pepper. 
 Place fish in the baking dish. 
 Spoon the tomato sauce over the swordfish. 
 Bake 15 minutes or until the fish flakes. 
 Garnish with the grated lemon rind.
