Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups cubed cooked  chicken 
 1 x 5- to 6-ounce package chicken-seasoned  rice  mix, prepared as directed 
 1 medium  red onion , chopped 
 1 x 2½ ounce can sliced  black olives , drained 
 1 package (4 ounces) seasoned  feta  cheese, crumbled 
 ½ cup prepared  Caesar salad dressing 
 3 x 8-inch  pita  bread rounds, halved 
 Directions : 
 Combine  chicken ,  rice ,  onion ,  olives ,  cheese  and dressing in large bowl; toss to blend. 
 Fill each pita with 1 cup  rice  mixture. 
 Wrap up and serve immediately.
