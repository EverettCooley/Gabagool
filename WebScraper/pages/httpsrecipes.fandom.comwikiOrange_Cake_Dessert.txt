orange  Dessert Cake
 
 1 C.  flour 
 1 C.  sugar 
 1 Tsp. Soda 
 1/2 Tsp.  vanilla 
 1 Sm. Can  mandarin oranges 
 1/2 Tsp.  salt 
 1/3 C.  brown sugar 
 1/3 C. Nuts 
 
Combine All Ingredients except for brown sugar and nuts, Beat Well.  Pour Batter in Ungreased 8x8 Inch
 Pan. Sprinkle With brown  sugar  And nuts. . Bake in 350 Degree Oven For
 
 30-40 Minutes.Serve Warm With IceCream or Whipped Topping
