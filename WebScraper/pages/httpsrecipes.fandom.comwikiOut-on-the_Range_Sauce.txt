Description : 
 Recipe by Arleen Kaptur. See her article on  Perk-up Sauces and Marinades ;
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x 16-oz can  pork and beans  in  tomato sauce 
 1 x 8-oz can  tomato sauce 
 ½ cup  water 
 1 x 1¼ oz envelope  chili seasoning  mix 
 1 tsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 Directions : 
 In blender, combine the ingredients. 
 Cover and blend until smooth. 
 Use to baste steaks, hamburgers, and pork chops during the last 5 minutes of barbecue time. 
 Feels like you're out there on that 'lone prairie!
