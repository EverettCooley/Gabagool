 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 5 	ounces  chive  &  Onion   cream cheese , softened 
 2 	tablespoons  sour cream 
 1/4 lb crabmeat, drained & flaked 
 2 	tablespoons  Parmesan cheese , shredded 
 2 	teaspoons  Parmesan cheese , shredded 
 1 	tablespoon  green onions , chopped, only the green parts 
 1 	teaspoon  green onions , chopped, only the green parts 
 2 	tablespoons  water chestnuts , chopped fine 
 2 	teaspoons dry  white wine  (optional) 
 3/4 	teaspoon prepared horseradish 
 2 	tablespoons chopped fresh  parsley 
 1/8 	teaspoon hot pepper sauce  (optional) 
 1 3/4 	tablespoons  sliced almonds 
 paprika , for sprinkling 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 Mix all ingredients, except  almonds , until well blended. 
 Spoon into a buttered 9" pie plate or quiche dish and sprinkle with  almonds . 
 Bake 25–30 minutes ot until lightly browned. 
 Sprinkle with  paprika . 
 Serve with crackers,  bagel  chips or crispy pita triangles.
