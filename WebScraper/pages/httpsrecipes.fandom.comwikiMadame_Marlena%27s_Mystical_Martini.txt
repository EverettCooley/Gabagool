Madame Marlena is famous at bridge for these fabulous fruity concoctions. Mix up a batch to enhance the effects of the already potent Martini spread, or any time you need a little pick-me-up. Enjoy!
 Makes 1 serving.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup of  ice cubes 
 1 ounces of citrus-flavor vodka 
 1 ounces of  strawberry liqueur 
 Lemon  peel twist 
 1  strawberry 
 Directions : 
 Drop the ice cubs in a martini shaker. 
 Add the vodka and liqueur; shake vigorously. 
 Strain into the martini glass. 
 Add lemon peel and strawberry.
