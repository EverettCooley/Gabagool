Description : 
 Recipe does not include the sweet peppers in the ingredients list but does mention in the instructions... Use or leave out... your choice! Use low or non-fat dressing, cheeses, etc. as well as healthy/low carb pasta! Take care, Gloria
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Source: www.bhg.com 
 Start to Finish: 20 minutes 
 6 main-dish servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  shell pasta  or elbow  macaroni 
 1 lb frozen cooked  shrimp , thawed or 1 lb cooked shrimp 
 ⅓ cup bottled creamy onion or Caesar  salad dressing 
 2 tbsp snipped fresh  dill  (optional) 
 salt  and freshly ground  black pepper 
 1 x 6 oz package baby  spinach 
 4 oz  chèvre , sliced, or  feta  cheese, crumbled 
 Directions : 
 Prepare pasta according to package directions; drain. 
 In an extra-large bowl combine pasta, shrimp, and chopped sweet peppers. 
 Drizzle with the salad dressing; sprinkle with dill. 
 Toss to coat. 
 Season to taste with salt and pepper. 
 Divide spinach among 6 shallow bowls or plates. 
 Top with shrimp mixture and cheese.
