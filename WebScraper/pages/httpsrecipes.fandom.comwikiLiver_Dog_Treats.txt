 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound raw liver (any kind) blended into a paste 
 1  egg 
 1cup  flour 
 1/2 cup  corn  meal (or  flour ) 
 1/4 teaspoon  oregano 
 1/4 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 Directions : 
 Mix well and bake at 350 for 1/2 hour for chewy treats, longer for crisper treats.
