 Ingredients : 
 cheese  sticks 
 grapes 
 apples 
 wooden picks 
 Directions : 
 Unwrap cheese sticks and place on cutting board. Cut each cheese stick into 6 equal pieces. 
 Carefully cut apple in half. Remove core. Cut into cubes. 
 Put wooden picks through a grape, then cheese cube, then apple cube. Place kabobs on plate to serve or stick in a whole apple.
