 Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Wiggins Estate in Montgomery, Alabama in 1987.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2  mangos  peeled seeded and cubed 
 2 cups fresh  blueberries 
 ¼ cup frozen piña colada mix thawed 
 1 tablespoon  dark rum 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl combine mango cubes, blueberries, piña colada mix and rum. 
 Into 4 martini glasses or dessert dishes spoon fruit mixture dividing evenly. 
 Garnish with blueberries, mango slices and thin lime slices.
