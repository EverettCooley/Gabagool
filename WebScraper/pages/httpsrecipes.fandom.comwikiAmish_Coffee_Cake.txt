      Amish coffee cake     
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  light brown sugar 
 2 cups  flour 
 ¾ cup  shortening 
 1  egg 
 2 tsp  vanilla 
 1 cup hot  coffee 
 1 tsp  soda 
 Directions : 
 Mix  sugar ,  flour  and  shortening . 
 Take out 1 cup for topping. 
 Dissolve soda in hot  coffee , add to the  flour  mixture add  egg  and  vanilla . 
 Spread on sheet pan and sprinkle on topping. 
 Bake at 325 – 350 °F for 30 minutes. 
 Sprinkle with  powdered sugar  after baked.
