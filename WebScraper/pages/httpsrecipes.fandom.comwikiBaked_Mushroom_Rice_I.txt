Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 ½ cup sliced  green onions 
 1 cup uncooked  rice 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon seasoned  pepper 
 1 x 2½-ounce jar sliced  mushrooms  with liquid 
 ¼ cup chopped  pimentos 
 1½ cups  chicken broth 
 ¼ cup dry  sherry 
 Directions : 
 Cook  onions  in  butter  until soft. 
 Add  rice ,  salt ,  pepper ,  mushrooms , pimientos, broth and  sherry . 
 Bring to a boil; stir. Pour into buttered 2-quart baking dish. Cover with tight-fitting lid or foil. 
 Bake at 375°F for 25 to 30 minutes or until  rice  is tender and liquid is absorbed. Fluff with fork.
