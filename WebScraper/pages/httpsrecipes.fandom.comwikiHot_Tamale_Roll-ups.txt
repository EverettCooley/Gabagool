Description : 
 Rolled up tamales in flour  tortillas .
 
 Ingredients : 
 10 flour  tortillas 
 1 can  tamales  or 10 fresh ones 
 1  onion 
 1 can  refried beans 
 1 small can sliced  black olives 
 1 package of  taco seasoning 
 1 can  enchilada sauce 
 1 package shredded  mozzarella  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Cook hamburger meat,  onions  and add  taco seasoning  until meat is browned. 
 Drain warm tortillas lay tortilla one at a time on flat surface. 
 Add one tsp  refried beans  top with one tamale add small amount of  black olives  and top with 1 tbsp  cheese  roll up and repeat process until complete. 
 Add each to a buttered 9x13 casserole dish top with enchilada sauce then spread extra  cheese  on top. 
 Bake 350°F for 25 – 30 minutes and serve.
