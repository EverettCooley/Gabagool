Ingredients : 
 olive oil 
 1 small  onion , sliced thin 
 1 can  plum tomatoes 
 12  olives  pitted and cut in pieces 
 1 tbsp  capers 
 25 g  mushrooms 
 1  anchovy , minced 
 1 slice of  habanero pepper 
 salt 
 pepper 
 oregano 
 1 lb  spaghetti 
 Parmesan cheese 
 Directions : 
 In large skillet, heat  oil ; add  onion . 
 Saut√© until golden. 
 Add  tomatoes  and let cook for a few minutes. 
 Add other ingredients and cook until done. 
 Cook the spaghetti al dente. 
 Drain and transfer to large serving bowl. 
 Toss the spaghetti with sauce. 
 Serve and add grated Parmesan.
