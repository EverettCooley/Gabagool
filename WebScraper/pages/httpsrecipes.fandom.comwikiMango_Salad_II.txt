Ingredients : 
 1 cup boiling  water 
 1 package  lemon gelatin 
 1½ packages  unflavored gelatine 
 1 large can  mangos 
 8 ounces  cream cheese 
 fresh  fruit  of your choice 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve  lemon gelatin  and  unflavored gelatine  in boiling  water . 
 Blend mangoes and  cream cheese  in blender. 
 Mix all together and place in ring mold. 
 Unmold and fill center with fresh fruit. 
 Note: you may serve with a side bowl of dressing consisting of ½  mayonnaise  and ½  sour cream .
