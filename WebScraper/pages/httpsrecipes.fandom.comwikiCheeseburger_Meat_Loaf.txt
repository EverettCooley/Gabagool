Makes 8 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 pounds lean ground  Beef 
 2 cups cooked  rice 
 1/4 cup finely chopped  Onion 
 1 1/2 teaspoons  garlic salt 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1/3 cup  milk 
 2  eggs , beaten 
 6 slices pasteurized process  American cheese 
 1/2 cup barbecue sauce 
 Directions : 
 Combine meat,  rice ,  Onion ,  garlic salt ,  pepper ,  milk  and  eggs  in large bowl; mix thoroughly. 
 Press half of mixture into greased 9x5-inch loaf pan; top with  Cheese . 
 Spread remaining meat mixture over  Cheese , being certain  Cheese  is completely covered. 
 Spoon barbecue sauce on top. 
 Bake at 350 degrees 45 to 50 minutes, or until meat is done. Let stand 5 minutes before slicing and serving.
