 Ingredients : 
 1 lb loaf : 
 ½ tbsp  active dry yeast 
 3 tbsp  dry milk 
 2¼ cup  bread flour 
 ¼ tsp  cloves 
 ½ tsp  cinnamon 
 ½ tbsp  coriander 
 ⅓ tsp  salt 
 2¼ tbsp  oil 
 3 tbsp  honey 
 ⅔ cup  water 
 1½ lb loaf : 
 2 tsp  active dry yeast 
 ¼ cup  dry milk 
 3 cup  bread flour 
 ⅓ tsp  cloves 
 ¾ tsp  cinnamon 
 2 tsp  coriander 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 3 tbsp  oil 
 ¼ cup  honey 
 1 cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Load ingredients into bread machine pan according to manufacturer's directions. 
 Bake on light crust setting. 
 Delayed timer okay.
