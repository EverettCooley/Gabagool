Description 
 Dessert Recipes  by the South Carolina Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Ingredients 
 Cake 
 3  eggs 
 1½ cups  salad oil 
 2 cups  sugar 
 3 cups  all-purpose flour 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon  baking soda 
 1 teaspoon  cinnamon 
 2 teaspoons  vanilla 
 3 cups finely chopped  apples 
 1½ cups chopped  pecans 
 Topping 
 1 cup  brown sugar 
 ¼ cup  milk 
 1 stick  margarine  or real  butter 
 Directions 
 Cake 
 Mix eggs, salad oil and sugar and blend well. 
 Add flour, salt, soda, and cinnamon and mix well. 
 Add vanilla, chopped apples and nuts. 
 Put into greased 8 or 9-inch tube or bundt pan. 
 Bake at 350 °F for one hour. 
 While cake is still hot, pour  topping  (below) over it in the pan. 
 Topping 
 Combine all ingredients and cook for 2½ minutes. 
 Pour hot topping from pan. Let set until cold; when completely cold, remove cake with topping from pan.
