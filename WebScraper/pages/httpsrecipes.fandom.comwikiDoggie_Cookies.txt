 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2cups  all-purpose flour 
 1 1/4 Shredded  Cheddar   Cheese 
 6 Finely choped green beens 
 1 Grated  Apple 
 1/2cup Veggie  oil 
 7tbWater with boilian 
 1/2 Baccon bits 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven ot 400. 
 Add all the ingredients into a bowl. 
 Roll out doe(1/4 inch thick and cut out with a cookie cutter. 
 Bast them with some extra boilian and place them into the oven.Let them cook for about 10–15 minutes or until  Cheese  looks oranger. 
 After cooking bast them again and let them cool. 
 Store in a plastic bag or fridge
