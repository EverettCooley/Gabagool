 Description : 
 Caramelized  Chicken  Wings with an  orange  Sauce is a very delicious meat dish recipe.
 
       Caramelized Chicken Wings with an Orange Sauce     
 Ingredients : 
 8  Chicken  wings 
 salt  and  pepper 
 2 tbsp.  sesame oil 
 4 tbsp. clear honey 
 4 tbsp.  vegetable oil 
 2 tbsp.  Superfine sugar 
 shredded peel and juice of 1  orange 
 Directions : 
 Prepare the  Chicken . Mix the  sesame oil  and honey. Add the  Sugar  to the pan and heat. Add the  orange juice  and reserved pan juices. Place the  Chicken  wings, pour the caramel sauce over them.
