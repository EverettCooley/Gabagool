 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup fat free  milk 
 1 container (8 oz.) fat free  blueberry   yogurt 
 1 packet no  Sugar  added  vanilla  crème nestlÉ®  carnation ® instant breakfast 
 1/2 cup frozen unsweetened  blueberries 
 1/2  banana 
 Directions : 
 PLACE  milk ,  yogurt ,  carnation  Instant Breakfast,  blueberries  and  banana  in blender; cover. 
 Blend until smooth. NOTE: For added variety, blend in any flavor of fat free  yogurt  or unsweetened  Berries . 
 For a frostier drink, omit  yogurt  and blend in 1 cup (about 2 large scoops) of your favorite fat free frozen  yogurt .
