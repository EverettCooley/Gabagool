Description : 
 This recipe is a classic and whenever it's made, it doesn't stick around for long. This zarbo stelle pasta salad is a great all-year-round salad and is especially good to have at a barbecue.
 
 Ingredients : 
 Pasta Salad : 
 ½ cup  sultanas 
 ¼ cup toasted  pine nuts 
 ¼ cup  sesame seeds 
 3 tablespoons  poppy seeds 
 1  red capsicum , seeds removed, finely diced 
 1  green capsicum , seeds removed, finely diced 
 ½ cup chopped  parsley 
 1½ cups  stelle  pasta 
 Dressing : 
 3 tablespoons  raspberry vinegar 
 ⅓ cup  olive oil 
 ½ teaspoon ground  cumin 
 ½ teaspoon  curry powder 
 ½ teaspoon  turmeric 
 Directions : 
 Pasta salad : 
 Cook the stelle pasta in boiling, salted water for 7 to 10 minutes, stirring occasionally until al dente. 
 Add sultanas, nuts, seeds, capsicum and parsley to the cooked pasta and toss well. 
 Add the dressing and combine well. 
 Dressing : 
 Combine all and stir together well.
