Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 flour  tortillas 
 Thousand Island  salad dressing 
 deli  coleslaw 
 corned beef 
 Swiss cheese 
 Directions : 
 Spread burrito size flour tortillas with thousand island dressing. 
 Top with deli coleslaw and thinly sliced corned beef and Swiss cheese. 
 Roll up, cut diagonally in half. 
 You can make as many servings as you want.
