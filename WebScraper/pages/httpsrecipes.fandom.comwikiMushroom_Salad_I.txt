Description : 
 When the British hostesses served prawn cocktails in 1980s, the Estonian hostesses had to settle for the non-prawn versions. One of them is  mushroom  salad.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup of pickled  mushrooms 
 ½ cup of grated  cheese 
 ½ cup  apples  (cubed) 
 ½ cup  sour cream 
 1 tablespoon  mustard 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 table spoon  sugar 
 1 tablespoon  dill 
 Directions : 
 Mix  mushrooms , grated  cheese , cubed  apples  together well. 
 Now topped with a dollop of  sour cream  3. 
 In the end season with hot  mustard  and  salt  and  sugar . 
 For the garnish, use some  dill .
