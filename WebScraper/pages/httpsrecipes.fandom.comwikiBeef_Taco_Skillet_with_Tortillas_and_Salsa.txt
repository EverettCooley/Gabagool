Description : 
 This is a very easy and fast meal and is very yummy too. I used corn tortillas and the Cheddar cheese but sharp. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Makes 4 servings of beef taco skillet. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  ground beef 
 10¾ oz can  tomato  soup 
 ½ cup  salsa 
 ½ cup  water 
 6 flour or corn  tortillas , cut into pieces 
 ½ cup shredded  cheddar  cheese or Mexican cheese blend 
 ½ cup  sour cream 
 Directions : 
 Cook beef in skillet until well browned. 
 Pour off fat. 
 Stir in soup, sour cream, salsa, water, tortillas. 
 Heat to a boil. 
 Reduce heat and cook 5 minutes. 
 Stir. 
 Top with cheese. 
 Garnish with chopped green onions.
