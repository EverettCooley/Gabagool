Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup  butter  melted 
 1½ cups  sugar 
 ¾ cup  milk 
 5 cups  flour 
 2 teaspoons  baking powder 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 full teaspoon  ginger 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together. 
 Roll thin in sheets and mark with a grooved roller. 
 Sprinkle with  sugar  and bake in moderate oven.
