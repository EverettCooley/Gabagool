Ingredients : 
 8 fresh crawfish 
 8 fresh crayfish 
 100 G of mussels, shelled 
 100 G of shelled clams 
 100 G of octopus 
 100 G of fresh squids 
 5 G of fresh marjoram 
 5 G of fresh parsley 
 Court-bouillon 
 1 carrot 
 1 celery 
 1 onion 
 2 bay leaves 
 Dressing 
 250 G of olive oil 
 20 G of water 
 1 fresh lemon juice 
 Salt 
 Directions : 
 Prepare the court-bouillon in salted water, with the carrot, celery, onion and bay leaves. 
 Cook the seafood in the court-bouillon prepared earlier. 
 Drain and season with the dressing, chopped parsley and marjoram.
