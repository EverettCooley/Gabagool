 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/3 cup crisco chilled 
 1 cup  flour  (sifted) 
 1/4 tsp  salt 
 2 tbsp  water  (may need up tp 4 t) 
 Directions : 
 Either by hand or in a processor cut  shortening  into the  flour  and  salt  until fine crumbs. Add  water  1t at a time until a ball forms. Wrap in plastic and chill for one hour. Roll out to fit a 9" pie pan.bake at 425 °F for 10-12 minutes or leave unbaked if oie recipe calls for unbaked shell.
