Description : 
 Contributed by Jenn B aka Mom2Sam and Tiny at  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  grated coconut 
 1 cup diced  pineapple 
 1 cup seeded  tangerine  segments 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 Directions : 
 Combine ingredients and serve on  cabbage  leaves or  lettuce  leaves.
