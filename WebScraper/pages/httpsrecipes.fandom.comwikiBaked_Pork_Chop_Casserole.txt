This recipe comes from an Estate Sale and were obtained when  The Family Collection  from The Wetzel Estate in Plano, Texas was purchased in 1982.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4  Pork  chops 
 1½ Cups chopped white  onions 
 ½ Cup chopped  green pepper 
 ¼ Cup  margarine 
 1 clove  garlic , crushed 
 1 Pound can pork and beans 
 ½ Cup  chili sauce 
 1 Tablespoon  brown sugar 
 1 Tablespoon  Worcestershire sauce 
 1 Tablespoon prepared  mustard 
 1 Teaspoon  salt 
 1 Teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Season  pork  chops with  salt  and  pepper 
 Brown well in a skillet then place in a baking dish. 
 Sauté  onions  and  peppers  in margarine 
 Add  garlic , beans,  chili sauce ,  brown sugar  and  mustard . 
 Mix well and pour over chops. 
 Bake at 350 °F for 1 hour.
