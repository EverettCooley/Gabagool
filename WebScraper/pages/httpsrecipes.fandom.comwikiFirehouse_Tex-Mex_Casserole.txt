 Ingredients : 
 3 cups Minute  brown rice 
 1 1/2 cups hot  water 
 1Â lb lean ground  Beef 
 1/2 cup barbecue sauce 
 19 ounces canned stewed  tomatoes 
 14 ounces  refried beans 
 1 1/2 tablespoons  chili powder 
 1/2 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 1/2 cup grated  Cheddar   Cheese 
 2  green onions , thinly sliced 
 Directions : 
 1. Heat oven to 350F degrees.
2. Place  rice  in a greased 3-quart casserole dish.
3. Add hot  water ; cover and let stand for 5 minutes.
4. Meanwhile, brown the meat in a large frying pan.
5. Add barbecue sauce,  tomatoes , beans, and  chili powder .
6. Mix well and then bring to a boil.
7. Spoon the meat mixture over the  rice .
8. Sprinkle with  Cheddar   Cheese .
9. Cover and bake for 30 minutes.
10. Garnish with  green onions .
