 Prep Time: 
 Cook time: 35 minutes 
 Serves: 
 
 Description : 
 BBQ Sauce. A sauce to serve with the meat at a braai (BBQ). Homemade is always better.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 or 4 cloves of  garlic , crushed 
 2 medium  onions , finely chopped 
 green sweet pepper , finely chopped 
 50 ml brown  vinegar   [1] 
 15 ml cooking  oil 
 1 tin  tomatoes 
 1 tablespoon  tomato paste 
 30 ml  Worcestershire sauce 
 2 ml  tabasco sauce 
 50 ml  brown sugar 
 5 ml  French mustard 
 30 ml fruit  chutney  (peach chutney is best - South Africans would use Mrs. Ball's Original Chutney. could also be replaced with peach jam) 
 Directions : 
 Heat the oil in a heavy saucepan 
 ry the onions, garlic and sweet pepper until the onions are translucent. 
 Add the vinegar and the remaining ingredients and simmer for 30 minutes.
