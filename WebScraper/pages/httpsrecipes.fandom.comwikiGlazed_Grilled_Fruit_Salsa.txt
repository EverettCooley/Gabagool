Wonderful with barbecued Pork, poultry or fish.
 Contributed by Roxanne Chan for the BBQ Recipe Contest 
 Ingredients : 
 1  banana , cut into chunks 
 1 cup fresh  pineapple  chunks 
 1 cup  papaya  chunks 
 ¼ cup  jalapeño jelly , melted 
 1 tbsp  lime juice 
 1 small  green onion , minced 
 2 tbsp minced  crystallized ginger 
 1 tbsp snipped  mint 
 1 tbsp snipped  cilantro 
 2 tbsp diced  red bell pepper 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 Directions : 
 Prepare the grill to medium high heat. 
 Skewer the fruit chunks on 12 inch skewers. 
 Combine the melted jelly and  lime juice . 
 Grill the fruit, turning and basting till glazed about 6 – 8 minutes. 
 Dice the fruit and combine with the rest of the ingredients plus any remaining basting sauce.
