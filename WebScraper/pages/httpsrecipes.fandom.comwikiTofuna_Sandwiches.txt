Ingredients : 
 1 large stalk  celery , finely chopped 
 1  scallion  (white and light green part), finely chopped (optional) 
 4 regular or 6 mini-size  pita  breads, warmed and cut crosswise in half 
 ⅓ to ½ cup  tofu mayonnaise 
 8 oz pkg. baked marinated  tofu 
 Directions : 
 In medium bowl, crumble  tofu . 
 Add  mayonnaise ,  celery  and  scallion  if using and stir until well combined. 
 Stuff mixture into warmed pita bread halves and serve.
