Ingredients : 
 1½ shot  melon liqueur 
 1 shot  lime   vodka 
 1 shot Absolut  vodka 
 1 shot  triple sec 
 1½ shot  Blueberry schnapps 
 1 splash of  lime juice 
 1 splash of 7-Up 
 Directions : 
 Pour ingredients in a highball glass over  ice , and stir.
