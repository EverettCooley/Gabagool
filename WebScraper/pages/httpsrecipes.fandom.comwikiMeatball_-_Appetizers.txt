 Description : 
 This is a wonderful little appetizer we serve after Christmas Midnight Mass in our house. 
 
 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb minced  Ham 
 1 lb ground  Pork 
 2  eggs , well beaten 
 1 cup  milk 
 2 cups breadcrumbs 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 Sauce 
 
 1 1/2 cups  brown sugar  (packed) 
 3/4 cup  white vinegar 
 3/4 cup  water 
 1 tablespoon dry  mustard 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Meatballs: Mix all ingredients and form into 1" meatballs. 
 Sauce: Mix well and pour over meatballs. 
 Bake at 325 F. 
 for 1 hour. 
 Baste frequently.
