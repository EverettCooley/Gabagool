Ingredients : 
 2 large cans whole  tomatoes 
 2  onions 
 1 or 2  green peppers 
 4 or 5  cucumbers  (peeled and seeded) 
 ⅓ cup  lemon juice 
 ⅓ cup  vinegar  (optional) 
 ⅓ cup  oil  (optional) 
 4 cloves of  garlic 
 salt ,  pepper ,  thyme ,  oregano ,  dill ,  parsley ,  chives ,  tarragon ,  coriander 
 Directions : 
 Chop everything that's choppable, not including your fingers. 
 Combine all ingredients and season to taste. 
 Chill. 
 For a smoother soup, you can grate the vegetables with a hand grater. 
 For an even smoother soup, blend all ingredients in a blender or food processor.
