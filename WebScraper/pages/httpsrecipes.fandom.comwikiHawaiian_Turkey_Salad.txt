Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  brown rice 
 2 cups cooked  turkey  cubes 
 1 x 8-ounce can  pineapple  chunks in juice (drain; reserve 1 tablespoon juice) 
 1 x 8-ounce can sliced  water chestnuts , drained 
 1 medium unpeeled  apple , cored and chopped 
 ⅓ cup chopped  macadamia nuts 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ½ cup (4 ounces) plain  yogurt 
 lettuce  leaves 
 ¼ cup shredded  coconut , toasted, for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  turkey ,  pineapple ,  water chestnuts ,  apple ,  nuts  and  salt  in large bowl. 
 Toss lightly with  yogurt  and 1 tablespoon  pineapple juice . 
 Serve on  lettuce  leaves; garnish with  coconut .
