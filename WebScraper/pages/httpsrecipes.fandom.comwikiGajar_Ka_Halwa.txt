      Gajar Ka Halwa     
 Description : 
 This is a sweet, colourful and luscious pudding which originated on the subcontinent of India. This dessert has a soft, creamy and custardy texture with a distinct flavour which will certainly make you crave for more.
 Serves: 10
 Preparation time: 45–50 minutes
 
 Ingredients : 
 5 cups  milk 
 1 tin  sweetened condensed milk 
 3/4 kg  carrots 
 1-2 tbsp  Sugar 
 2 tsp  ghee  or  butter 
 50g  nuts / raisins 
 Directions : 
 Grate the carrots, add to the milk and bring to a boil. 
 Cook on slow fire stirring occasionally, till milk dries up. 
 Add condensed milk and sugar. 
 Cook on slow fire till dry (for about 25 to 30 minutes) stirring occasionally. 
 Add ghee and cook for another 10 minutes. 
 Garnish with  nuts ,  raisins  and serve hot.
