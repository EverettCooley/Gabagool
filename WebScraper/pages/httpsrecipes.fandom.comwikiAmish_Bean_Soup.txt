      Amish Bean Soup     
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  navy beans 
 3 quarts  water 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 pinch  baking soda 
 ½ cup shredded  carrot 
 1 cup chopped  celery 
 2 medium  onions ,  chopped 
 2  bay leaves 
 1½ pounds  ham  meat 
 1 cup mashed boiled  potato 
 ½ teaspoon  black pepper 
 ½ teaspoon dried  thyme , optional 
 2 tablespoons  sauce 
 ¼ cup chopped fresh  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Combine the beans,  water ,  salt  and  baking soda . 
 Bring to a boil, cover, and  simmer  for 5 minutes. 
 Remove from the heat, skim off foam, re-cover, and let stand for 2 hour. 
 Add the remaining ingredients except the  parsley  and simmer, covered, for about 1½ to 2 hours. 
 Remove the  ham bone . 
 Add the  parsley  just before serving.
