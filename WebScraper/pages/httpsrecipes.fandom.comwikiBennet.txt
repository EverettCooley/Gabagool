Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz  gin 
 ½ oz  lime juice 
 ½ tsp  superfine sugar 
 dash  orange bitters 
 lime  twist 
 Directions : 
 Using a shaker filled with  ice , combine all ingredients. 
 Shake well. 
 Strain into a chilled cocktail glass. 
 Garnish with  lime  twist.
