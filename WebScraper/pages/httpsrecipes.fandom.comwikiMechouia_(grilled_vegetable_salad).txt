 Ingredients : 
 2 large sweet  red peppers 
 2 large  onions 
 4 medium  tomatoes 
 1 small  hot pepper 
 3 T. fresh  lemon juice 
 3 T.  olive oil 
 1 t  oregano 
 1 7 oz. can of  Tuna 
 2 oz crumbled  feta   Cheese 
 2 hard boiled  eggs , chopped 
 salt  &  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Grill the  peppers ,  tomatoes  and  onions  in a hot oven approximately 400 degrees. 
 Turn them once during this process. 
 Grill them until they are soft. 
 Remove from oven and cool. 
 Remove the seeds from the  peppers . 
 Dice all of the cooked vegetables into small pieces. 
 Place the vegetables on a flat serving platter. 
 Stir in gently the  lemon juice ,  olive oil ,  oregano  and the  salt  and  pepper . 
 Best way to eat:Spread the  Tuna  and hard boiled  eggs  over top.
