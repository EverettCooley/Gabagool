Biscochos  ( sugar  Cookies)
 Categories: Cookies
 Yield: 52 Servings
 
 4  eggs 
 4 1/2 c  flour 
 1 c  sugar 
 4 ts  baking powder 
 1/2 c  oil 
 Cinnamon/Sugar
 Or  sesame seeds 
 
Beat  eggs . Add  sugar  And  oil . Beat Well. Add  flour  And  baking powder  aLittle at a Time Until it Forms a Smooth Dough. Let Dough Rest About 15Minutes (until Not Too Sticky) Roll Portions of Dough Into Pencil Logs. CutWith a Knife And Close to Make a Circles.
 
Gash 4 or 5 Times With a Knife. Roll Into Cinnamon/Sugar Mixture, IfDesired, or Paint With Beaten  egg  And Sprinkle With  sesame seeds . Bake At
 
 350 Degrees For 15 - 20 Minutes on a Lightly Greased Cookie Sheet. AfterThey Cool, Return to The Oven (which Has Been Shut Off) And Let Crisp a FewMinutes. Store in a Glass Jar With a Lid. This Recipe Can Also be Used ToRoll Out Dough And Cut Out Shapes. 
 
Enjoy.
