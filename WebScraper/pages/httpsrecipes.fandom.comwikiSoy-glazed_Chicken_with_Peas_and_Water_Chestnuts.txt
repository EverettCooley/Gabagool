Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 6 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  soy sauce 
 ¼ cup  dark brown sugar 
 ½ tsp  ground ginger 
 2 tbsp  rice vinegar 
 2 tbsp dark Asian  sesame oil 
 2 tsp  cornstarch 
 4 pounds  chicken , cut up 
 3 oz  snow peas , strings removed 
 8 oz can sliced  water chestnuts , drained 
 Directions : 
 Whisk first 6 ingredients in saucepan, cook until glaze thickens, 2 minutes. 
 Place  chicken , skin side up, on rack in baking pan. 
 Brush with some of the glaze. 
 Bake in 350 degree oven 45 minutes. 
 Brushing with glaze every 10 minutes. 
 Stop glazing 5 minutes before end. 
 Broil  chicken  5 minutes to crisp skin. 
 Fix  couscous  following package directions, with  water , add  peas  and  water chestnuts . 
 Put on platter with  chicken .
