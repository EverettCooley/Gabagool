 Description : 
 From my Aunt Maggie's Collection. My mother says she made this quite often and it was loved by all. Dated 1921. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/4 teaspoon dried  mustard 
 2  eggs 
 1/2 pint of  milk 
 12 ounces grated  Cheese 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Put all the ingredients in to a bowl and stir. 
 Pour into a greaseproof oven dish and put in the oven at 400 for 20 minutes. 
 Cool and serve at room temperature.
