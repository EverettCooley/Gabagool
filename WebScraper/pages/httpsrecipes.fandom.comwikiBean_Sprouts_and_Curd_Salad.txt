Description : 
 This is a Burmese salad that’s easy and quick to prepare.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups  bean sprouts 
 1 cup  beancurd , sliced 
 1 tablespoon  oil  for salad (extra for frying curd) 
 2 tablespoons  shrimp powder 
 2 teaspoons  fish sauce 
 1 teaspoon or less roasted  chili powder 
 1 large  lime 
 ½ cup  fried onion 
 Directions : 
 Trim sprouts and boil for 5 minutes or until cooked. 
 Slice curd into ½-inch strips and fry strips in a wok until crisp. 
 In a bowl, put sprouts,  shrimp  powder, 1 tablespoon  oil , fish sauce and  chili powder . 
 Squeeze  lime  over mixture and mix well. 
 Add  salt , sauce or  lime  as desired. 
 Add curds and fried  onion . 
 Mix lightly and then serve.
