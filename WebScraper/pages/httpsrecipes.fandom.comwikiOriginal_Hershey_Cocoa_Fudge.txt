Ingredients : 
 2/3 c.  cocoa 
 3 c.  sugar 
 1/8 tsp.  salt 
 1-1/2 c.  milk  or 1 c.  evaporated milk  + 1/2 c.  water 
 1/4 c.  butter 
 1 tsp.  vanilla 
 1/2 c. nuts (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Combine cocoa, sugar and salt in large saucepan. 
 Add milk (and water) gradually. 
 Mix well, stirring continuously. 
 Boil until temperature reaches 232 degrees on candy thermometer; mixture should be at the soft ball stage. 
 Remove from heat, add vanilla and butter, but do not stir. 
 Cool until 110 degrees at room temperature. 
 Beat until it loses some of the gloss. 
 Pour in greased pan or dish.
