       Cream of Spinach Soup     
 In Romanian: Supa de spanac (crema)
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 qts / 3 l  water 
 1 lb / 500 g  spinach 
 1  lemon  slice (with peel) 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 1 teaspoon  flour 
 Â½ cup  sour cream 
 salt 
 French Toast 
 Directions : 
 Wash and pick over the  spinach . 
 Set to boil with the  water . 
 After boiling for a little while, remove from heat and sieve everything. 
 Set to boil again, adding the  lemon ,  salt  and  butter . 
 Let boil for another minute or two, then add the  sour cream  mixed with  flour . 
 Serve with  French Toast .
