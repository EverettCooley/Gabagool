 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 	large	White  Onion  (about 2 cups sliced) 
 1 	cup	 white vinegar 
 1/3 	cup	 water 
 1/2 	teaspoon	 Sugar 
 1 	sprinkle 	 salt  to taste 
 3 		Jalapeno  chile  (fresh) 
 Directions : 
 Handle fresh  chile  with care, slice jalapenos very thinly. 
 Combine  vinegar ,  water ,  Sugar , and  salt , whisk until  Sugar  and  salt  have dissolved.
 
 Add  onions  and jalapenos to pickling mixture, store in refrigerator for 2â€“3 days, stirring occasionally. 
 onions  will keep for several weeks in the refrigerator.
 
 These  onions  are served as table condiments for grilled  Beef  dishes. You may increase the heat by adding more jalapenos.
