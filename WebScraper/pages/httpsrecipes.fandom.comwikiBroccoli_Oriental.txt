 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 bunch  Broccoli , trimmed 
 4  carrots , in strips 
 2  onions , slivered 
 1 cup waterchestnuts, canned, sliced 
 2 teaspoons  soy sauce 
 Directions : 
 Saute  onions , add the rest. Cover and cook 10-15 minutes, adding more  water  if needed.
