Description : 
 This divine fruit dish is made and mixed with real flour, sugar, butter, cloves, and cinnamon, and is good for dessert, as it keeps a blissful aroma with the fruit and spices.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 kilo (2 ¼ pounds) fresh  apricots  (approximately) 
 1 ¼ cup  plain flour 
 1 ¼ cups  sugar , brown or white only 
 185 grams (6 ounces)  butter 
 1 pinch ground  cloves 
 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Preheat an oven to 375 °F. 
 Slice apricots in half and discard the stone. 
 Cover the bottom of a shallow, buttered oven-proof dish with two layers of apricots. 
 Place flour, granulated sugar, butter, cloves and ground cinnamon into a food processor and blend until it is like bread crumbs. 
 Spread mixture on top of apricots. 
 Bake in a preheated 375 °F oven for approximately 50 minutes or until lightly browned on top.
