Ingredients : 
 3 cups  lager beer  or  ale 
 3 cups  flour 
 2 tbsp  paprika 
 6  garlic , large cloves chopped finely 
 3 pounds  California avocados 
 vegetable oil  - as needed for deep frying 
 salt  - as needed 
 3 cups  salsa  of choice 
 Directions : 
 To make  beer  batter, whisk together  beer ,  flour ,  paprika  and  garlic  until well blended. 
 Let stand at least 2 hours. 
 Per order : 
 Peel and cut half an  avocado  into 6 wedges. 
 Dredge each wedge in reserved  beer  batter. 
 Deep fry in 375°F  oil  until golden brown, about 3 minutes. 
 Drain well; season with  salt . 
 Serve with ¼ cup salsa.
