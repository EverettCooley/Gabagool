Description : 
 Contributed by  SF&SC Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 large  Granny Smith apples 
 1½ cups  sugar 
 2 teaspoons  cinnamon 
 2 sticks  butter , melted 
 2 cans  crescent rolls 
 1 can Mountain Dew 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 Peel and cut each apple into 8 pieces. 
 Mix sugar, cinnamon, and melted butter together. 
 Roll 1 piece of apple in each crescent roll, the more doughy side up. 
 Place in a 9x13 inch pan. 
 Pour sugar mixture over top. 
 Pour Mountain Dew over top of sugar mixture. 
 Bake for 40 minutes or until brown.
