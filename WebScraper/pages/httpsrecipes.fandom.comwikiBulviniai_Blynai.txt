Description : 
 Potato pancakes
 
 Ingredients : 
 7  potatoes , medium 
 1  onion , medium 
 3  eggs 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 dash  pepper 
 4 tbsp  flour 
 1 tsp  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Peel and grate  potatoes , drain excess  water . 
 Add grated  onion ,  eggs ,  salt ,  pepper  and  flour . 
 Mix until consistency of thick batter. 
 Add ⅛ to ¼ cup of  milk  if too thick, beat until mixture look like a waffle batter. 
 Add 1 tsp of  lemon juice  last. 
 Fry in  vegetable oil  or  lard , until golden brown on both sides. 
 Drain on paper towels. 
 Serve with  sour cream  or your favorite topping.
