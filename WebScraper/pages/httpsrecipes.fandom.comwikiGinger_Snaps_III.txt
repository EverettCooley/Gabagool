Ingredients : 
 1 cup  molasses 
 1 teaspoon  soda 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 ½ cup  butter 
 1 tablespoon  ginger 
 3 cups of Gold Medal  flour  to roll very thin 
 Directions : 
 Mix  molasses ,  sugar ,  ginger  and  butter 
 Stir over the fire until the  butter  is melted, then stir in quickly  flour  in which has been sifted the pulverized soda. 
 Knead the dough until it becomes smooth and set on  ice , over night if possible. 
 Roll as thin as pasteboard and bake in a quick oven.
