Ingredients : 
 3 cups  flour 
 4 teaspoons  baking powder 
 2  eggs 
 1 cup thick  cream 
 1 cup  Sugar 
 3/4 cup shredded cocoanut 
 pinch of  salt 
 
 
 Directions : 
 First sifted together  flour ,  baking powder  and  salt . 
 Beat  eggs . 
 Add gradually  Sugar , cocoanut,  cream ,  flour ,  baking powder  and  salt  sifted together. 
 Let chill a little. 
 Toss on floured pastry board. 
 Roll 1/4Â inch thick. Cut with a small round cutter. 
 Bake on buttered sheet of paper in moderate oven for about 25 minutes.
