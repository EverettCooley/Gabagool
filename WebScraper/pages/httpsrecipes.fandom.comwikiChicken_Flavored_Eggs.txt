 Cook time: 2 minutes 
 Serves: 1 serving 
 
 Description : 
 This is a delicious recipe for sunny-side up eggs that well... taste surprisingly like chicken! If you like Rotisserie Chicken, you will love this simple but yummy breakfast recipe!
 
 Ingredients : 
 2  eggs 
 ½ teaspoon  garlic salt   or  ½ teaspoon of mixed  garlic powder  and  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon of  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Carefully crack eggs and pour them into a bowl. 
 Mix the garlic salt or the garlic powder-salt mix (whichever you are using) with the sugar. 
 Pour the eggs into a frying pan and turn the fire on. 
 Before the egg whites becomes white, gently pour the powder mixture over the eggs. 
 When done cooking, you may serve. It is suggested that you serve with  bacon  and  toast . 
 Enjoy!
