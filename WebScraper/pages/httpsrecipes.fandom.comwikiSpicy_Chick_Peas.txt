Description : 
 A good fall back meal using ingredients that many pantries will often have lying in a dusty corner, but are also cheap to pick up.
 
 Ingredients : 
 250 g  chickpeas 
 2 teaspoons  Dijon mustard 
 salt  and  pepper , to taste 
 Directions : 
 Cook the chick peas until soft 
 Strain chick peas and place in serving bowl 
 Add mustard and stir through 
 Pepper and salt to taste
