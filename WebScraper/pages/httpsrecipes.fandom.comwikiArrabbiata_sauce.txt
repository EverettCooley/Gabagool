About arrabbiata sauce : 
 Literally, "Angry Sauce" in Italian, due to the 'peperoncino', the hot red pepper used in it. This sauce is made with tomatoes, garlic, parsley, and green and red hot chili peppers and goes well with penne, linguine, and spaghetti.
