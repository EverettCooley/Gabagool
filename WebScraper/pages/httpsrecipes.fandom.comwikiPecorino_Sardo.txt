 About Pecorino Sardo : 
 Pecorino Sardo, or Fior de Sardo, is an Italian sheep's milk cheese, It is one of the most popular aged forms of Pecorino.  It is sometimes referred to as Pecorino Tuscano, or simply Pecorino.  Varieties include  Pecorino Romano  and  Pecorino Toscano ; the latter is used as a table cheese.
