Description : 
 Tasty microwavable enchiladas! 
 
 Contributed by Jenn B aka Mom2Sam and Tiny at  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup chopped fresh  tomato 
 ¼ cup chopped  onion 
 ¼ cup chopped  green bell pepper 
 1½ cups frozen or fresh whole-kernel  corn 
 2 teaspoons reduced-calorie  margarine 
 4 flour or corn  tortillas 
 ⅓ cup shredded Kraft reduced-fat  cheddar  cheese 
 ¼ cup Land o' Lakes no-fat  sour cream 
 Directions : 
 Mix together the tomato, onion, green pepper, corn, and margarine in a 4-cup glass measuring cup. 
 Then, microwave on medium power for 4 to 5 minutes. 
 Put about ½ a cup of the veggie mixture onto each tortilla and sprinkle 1½ tablespoons cheddar cheese on top. 
 Microwave the tortillas on medium for 2 to 3 minutes, and then top each one with 1 tablespoon of the sour cream. 
 Roll up and serve! 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 209 calories | 5 g fat | 8 g protein | 33 g carbohydrate | 285 mg sodium | 99 mg calcium | 2 g fiber 
 Exchanges: 2 starch | ½ vegetable | ½ meat
