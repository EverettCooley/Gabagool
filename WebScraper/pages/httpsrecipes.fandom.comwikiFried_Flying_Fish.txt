Ingredients : 
 6 ready boned  flying fish 
 2  limes 
 Seasoned Breadcrumbs 
 1 â€“ 2 well beaten  eggs 
 oil  for frying (cholesterol free  oil  such as mazola or  canola oil ) 
 2 tbsp ready-made bottled Bajan seasoning (Delish or Lotties Bajan Seasoning) 
 1 tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Marinate fish in  lime  and  salt  for about 15 minutes. 
 Wash off and drain well, then place seasoning in the grooves left after fish has been boned. 
 Dip fish in breadcrumbs first, then  egg , then back in breadcrumbs and fry in hot  oil  one minute on each side, then place on paper towels to absorb  oil  then place in dish.
