Description : 
 Contributed by Delma  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  chicken breast , sliced 
 2 cups  coconut milk  or  light cream 
 2 tablespoons  green curry paste 
 1 cup sweet  peas , frozen 
 1  green chile , cut lengthwise 
 1  red chile , cut lengthwise 
 2 tablespoons  nampla 
 2 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 3 or 4 leaves of  basil 
 Directions : 
 In a saucepan, heat oil over medium heat. 
 Stir-fry green curry paste for 1 minute. 
 Add chicken and  nampla . 
 Stir fry until chicken is done. 
 Add coconut milk and bring to a boil. 
 Add sweet peas and red and green chile. 
 Stir a few times. 
 Remove from heat. 
 Garnish with basil leaves.
