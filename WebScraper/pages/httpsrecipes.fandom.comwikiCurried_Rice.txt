Ingredients : 
 1 tbsp  fat  15 ml 
 1 small  onion  chopped 
 6 tsp (2 tbsp)  curry powder  30 ml 
 3 cups  rice  750 g 
 5 cups  water  1 l 
 1 tsp  salt  5 ml 
 Â½ tsp  black pepper  2 ml 
 Directions : 
 Heat fat in a large sauce pan. 
 Add  onion  and  curry  powder. 
 Cook until  onion  is tender, stirring often. 
 Add clean  rice  to  onion  mixture. 
 Stir together. 
 Add  water  and all other seasonings. 
 Cover pot. 
 Boil rapidly for 5 minutes, reduce to low heat, and cook until  water  is absorbed. 
 Put simmer ring under  rice  to prevent burning. 
 Add  water  as needed to make grains tender. 
 Serve with any  meat  dish.
