 Description : 
 Yield: 1 heaping cup 
 Serving size: ¼ cup 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (12 oz) package  soft tofu , drained 
 1 tsp minced  garlic 
 ¼ cup  Parmesan cheese , grated 
 ¼ cup  Romano  cheese, grated 
 1 Tbsp  soybean oil 
 1½ tsp dried  basil 
 1 Tbsp dried  parsley 
 ¼ tsp  black pepper 
 1 tsp  onion powder 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in food processor or blender. Blend until creamy on high, about 1½ minutes. 
 Heat sauce in microwave or stove top and serve over hot  pasta . 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving: 
 136 calories 
 9 g fat (3 g sat fat) 
 9 g protein (6 g soy protein) 
 4 g carbohydrate 
 195 mg sodium 
 12 mg cholesterol 
 0 g dietary fiber
