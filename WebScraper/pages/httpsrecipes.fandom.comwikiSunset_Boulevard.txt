 Ingredients : 
 2 shots Stolichnaya  vodka 
 1 shot  peach  Schnapps 
 3 shots  cranberry juice 
 1 shot  orange juice 
 Directions : 
 Fill the glass with cubed  ice . Pour in the  peach  Schnapps, Stolichnaya  vodka  and  cranberry juice  and give it a stir. Slowly add the  orange juice  to create a layered effect.
