Description 
 Chai Spice Tea  from the  Public Health Cookbook  by the Seattle & King County Department of Public Health—original source of recipe, public domain government resource
 
 Cook Time : About 35 minutes 
 Serves : 4 
 Ingredients 
 2 cups  water 
 4 tea bags, black  tea 
 2 cups  2% milk 
 2 tablespoons  honey 
 2 slices fresh  ginger , cut into 1½ inch slices 
 ½ teaspoon ground  cardamom 
 ½ teaspoon ground  nutmeg 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 ¼ teaspoon  allspice 
 Directions 
 In a saucepan, bring water to a boil. 
 Add tea bags, reduce heat and simmer for 2 minutes. 
 Remove tea bags. 
 Add remaining ingredients to tea and bring to a boil. 
 Reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes. 
 Strain and serve hot.
