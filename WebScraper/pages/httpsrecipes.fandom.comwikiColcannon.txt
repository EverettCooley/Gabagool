Description : 
 Ireland is rich in culinary delights and colcannon is one of the popular main dishes in the country.
 
 Ingredients : 
 250 g (½ lb)  potatoes , peeled and diced 
 250 g (½ lb)  green cabbage , shredded 
 5 tablespoons  milk 
 1 large  leek , chopped 
 pinch of  nutmeg  (best if freshly grated) 
 1 tablespoon melted  butter 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Cook  cabbage  in  water  with  salt  for 10 minutes. Drain. 
 Set aside  cabbage . 
 In another saucepan, bring  milk  to a boil. 
 Add  potato  and  leek . 
 Simmer over low heat until vegetables are cooked. 
 Remove from heat. 
 Add  nutmeg ,  pepper  and  salt . Mash. 
 Add  cabbage  to the mashed  potato  and  leek  and mix well. 
 Place mashed vegetable in a serving dish. 
 Drizzle melted  butter  on top. 
 Serve hot.
