Mushroom  Marinade California
 
 1 Pound Small  Mushrooms 
 3/4 Cup  salad oil 
 1/3 Cup  red wine vinegar 
 2 T.  lemon juice 
 3 T.  chives , Chopped 
 1 T.  tarragon 
 1 Small clove  garlic , Chopped 
 1 T.  salt 
 1/2 T.  sugar 
 
Combine All Ingredients Except  Mushrooms . Pour Marinade Over The  Mushrooms  And Marinate Several Hours, Turning Occasionally. Drain And Serve.Source: Come Into The Kitchen
