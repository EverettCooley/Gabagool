Description : 
 Bo Luc Lac  is also known as Vietnamese Shaking  Beef .
 
       Bo Luc Lac     
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 lb lean sirloin steaks 
 6 cloves  garlic 
 1 teaspoon fish sauce 
 1/2 teaspoon  Sugar 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 3 teaspoons  olive oil 
 1  Onion 
 1 tablespoon rice vinegar 
 1 dash  pepper 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 2 cups watercress leaves 
 Directions : 
 Mix the ingredients, put the mixture in  oil  and heat it.
