      After Dark Martini     
 Ingredients : 
 2 shots Stolichnaya  vodka 
 ½ shot De Kuyper  crème de menthe 
 1 shot De Kuyper white  crème de cacao 
 ½ shot  Noilly Prat  dry 
 garnish:  chocolate  rim 
 glass: martini 
 Directions : 
 Stir all ingredients with  ice  and strain into glass.
