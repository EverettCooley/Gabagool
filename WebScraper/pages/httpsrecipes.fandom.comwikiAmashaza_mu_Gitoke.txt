Description : 
 Peas with cooking bananas
 
 Ingredients : 
 250 g  peas 
 10  bananas  ( plantains  also work well for a less sweet taste) 
 2  onions 
 2  tomatoes 
 salt 
 pili pili 
 3 tablespoons  palm oil 
 Directions : 
 Soak dried  peas  and boil for half an hour. 
 Add the  bananas  and cook until tender. 
 In a separate pan heat up the  oil , add the  onions  and the  tomatoes  and fry for 5 minutes. 
 Mix it with the  peas  and the  bananas . 
 Serve warm.
