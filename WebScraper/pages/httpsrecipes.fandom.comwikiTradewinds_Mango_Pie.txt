Ingredients : 
 1 large  mango  cut in chunks 
 1 can  evaporated milk 
 1 x 8 oz container frozen  whipped topping 
 2 prepared  graham cracker pie shells 
 1 x 6 oz can frozen limeade 
 Directions : 
 Mix limeade,  mango ,  evaporated milk  and whipped topping. 
 Pour into baked or graham cracker pie shells. 
 Refrigerate until firm. 
 May be frozen.
