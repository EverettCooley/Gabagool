Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  peanut butter 
 ¼ cup  light cream 
 1 cup  brown sugar 
 ½ cup  coconut , optional 
 ½ cup chopped  peanuts , optional 
 Directions : 
 Blend the  peanut butter , cream, and  brown sugar . 
 Cook and stir to a boil. 
 Spread over warm cake. 
 Broil 4 inches from heat for 2-3 minutes. 
 Enjoy.
