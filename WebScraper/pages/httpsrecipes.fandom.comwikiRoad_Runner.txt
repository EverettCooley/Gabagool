      Road Runner     
 Ingredients : 
 2 shots Stolichnaya  vodka 
 1 shot Disaronno  amaretto 
 1 shot  coconut cream 
 1 shot  Single cream 
 1 scoop  vanilla   ice cream 
 Directions : 
 Blend all ingredients with crushed  ice  until smooth.
