 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: My old recipes 
 Serves 4 to 6 
 Ingredients : 
 8  eggs 
 ½ cup  light cream 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 dash  cayenne pepper 
 1 cup grated  Swiss cheese 
 2 tbsp  margarine 
 snipped  chives  or  parsley 
 Directions : 
 With beater, beat eggs, cream, salt and cayenne in top of double boiler until well mixed. 
 Stir in ¾ cup Swiss cheese and butter. 
 Cook over gently boiling water, stirring occasionally, 12 to 15 minutes or until eggs are set but still creamy. 
 Serve eggs sprinkle with rest of cheese and the chives or parsley.
