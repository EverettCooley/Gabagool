Ingredients : 
 5 medium red potatoes, peeled and sliced 
 ¼ cup olive oil 
 1 cup chopped onion 
 ¼ cup chopped green pepper 
 3 cloves garlic, minced 
 4 cups frozen chopped broccoli 
 12 eggs, beaten 
 ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese 
 ½ cup water 
 1 t. dried basil 
 ½ t. salt 
 ½ t. pepper 
 1½ cups shredded jack cheese 
 Directions : 
 Saute potato in oil in a large skillet 10 minutes. 
 Add onion, green pepper and garlic; saute until tender. 
 Add broccoli; cover and cook 5 minutes. 
 Arrange mixture in an 11 x 7 inch baking dish. 
 Combine eggs and next 5 ingredients. 
 Beat until blended. 
 Pour over potato mixture. 
 Sprinkle with jack cheese. 
 Bake, uncovered at 350 for 25 to 30 minutes or until set. 
 Makes 8 servings.
