Description : 
 Commonly known as jaljira 
 jeera  means  cumin 
 Tan colored powder mix used in making a tangy, salty, and spicy cooling drink. 
 Ingredients : 
 green mango powder 
 ground  cumin 
 citric acid 
 salt 
 caraway 
 black pepper 
 chili powder 
 cloves 
 asafoetida 
 Directions : 
 Mix all the ingredients together. 
 Keep in an air-tight container.
