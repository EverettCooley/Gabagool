Description 
 Try a chewy, delightful bar, great with tea or for a snack for the children with milk.
 Granola Bars  from Recipes for Better Nutrition During Cancer Treatment by the National Cancer Institute, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Cook Time : About 50 minutes total 
 Serves : 18 
 Ingredients 
 ¾ cup quick cooking  oatmeal 
 ½ cup  Granola II 
 ½ cup  coconut 
 ½ cup  brown sugar  (packed) 
 ¼ cup melted  margarine 
 1  egg 
 ¼ tsp  vanilla extract 
 1 tbsp  honey 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 ¼ cup  flour 
 Directions 
 Measure oatmeal, granola, coconut, and brown sugar together in deep bowl. Mix well. 
 Pour melted margarine over all and blend thoroughly. 
 Beat the egg, extract, honey, and salt together. 
 Pour this over dry ingredients, stirring to blend. 
 Add flour, stirring until smoothly mixed. 
 Press mixture onto greased, floured 11x7-inch shallow baking pan or cookie sheet. 
 Bake at 325 °F for 35 minutes. 
 Cool slightly, cut into bars, and remove from the pan while warm.
