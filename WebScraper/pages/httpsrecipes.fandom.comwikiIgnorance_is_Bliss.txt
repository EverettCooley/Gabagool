 Ingredients : 
 0.5 shot Stolichnaya Ohranj  vodka 
 0.5 shot  Campari 
 0.5 shot  apple juice 
 0.5 shot  passion fruit  syrup 
 Directions : 
 Shake the liquid ingredients together and strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with a squeeze of  lime .
