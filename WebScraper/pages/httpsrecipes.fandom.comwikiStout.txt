 About Stout : 
 Wikipedia Article  About Stout on Wikipedia 
 Stout and Porter are dark beers made using roasted malts or roast barley. There are a number of variations including Baltic Porter, Sweet Stout and Imperial Stout.
 Stout is a dark beer, sometimes black in colour. The most famous variety of stout is Guinness, originally from Ireland but now brewed in various countries around the world. It has a full taste, with distinct bitterness, and is known for its creamy head of foam.
