Ingredients : 
 2 cups of fruit (cut or sliced thin) ( apples ,  bananas ,  peaches ,  apricots ,  pears  and  plums  work great) 
 200 g of sweet  cream 
 6 tablespoons of  sugar  (white) 
 2 beaten  egg  whites 
 vanilla  (to taste) 
 lemon juice  (to taste) 
 Directions : 
 Beat the  cream ,  vanilla  and ⅓ of the  sugar  in a bowl. 
 The  cream  should become light and fluffy. 
 In another bowl beat the  egg  whites, add ⅓ of the  sugar . 
 The whites should become stiff. 
 Spread whites onto the  cream  with a mixer. 
 In another bowl add the fruit, ⅓ cup of  sugar  and a little  lemon juice . 
 Mix the fruit well. 
 Now place the fruit in cups or bowls and cover with the  cream /whites mixture.
