 Ingredients : 
 2 shots Stolichnaya Red  vodka 
 ¾ shot De Kuyper  crème de cassis 
 1 shot fresh  lime juice 
 ½ shot  lemon juice 
 3 teaspoons  caster sugar 
 Glass: Martini
 
 Directions : 
 Shake with cubed  ice  and strain into a martini glass.
