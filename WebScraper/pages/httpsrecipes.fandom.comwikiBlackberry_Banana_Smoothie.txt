 Description : 
 This ice-cold, rich smoothie is made with real fruit, enriched with milk, and blended to sweet perfection. You can simply make this with other fruits and yogurt.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 ½ cup  blackberries 
 2 sliced  bananas 
 1 ¾ cup  milk 
 1 teaspoon  sugar 
 Directions : 
 In a blender, mix blackberries, sugar, and milk. 
 Then blend in the sliced banana until smooth. 
 Add ice, pour into drinking glasses, and serve cold. 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Servings:  2 cups per glass
