 Description : 
 '
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup light unbleached  Sugar 
 2/3 cup boiling  water 
 6 Tbsp.  soymilk  powder 
 5 Tbsp. GeniSoy® Ultra-XT Protein Powder or GeniSoy® Ultra-XT  vanilla  Shake 
 1 Tbsp. melted soy  margarine 
 1 teaspoon ground  cumin 
 1/2 teaspoon ground  cardamom 
 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 2  bay leaves 
 1-inch piece  cinnamon  stick 
 Directions : 
 Combine all the ingredients in a blender, and process until the  Sugar  is dissolved and the mixture is thick 
 Pour into a clean jar, cover, and refrigerate 
 The  milk  thickens when chilled
