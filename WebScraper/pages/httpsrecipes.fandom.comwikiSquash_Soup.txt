Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp  butter  ( margarine  or  oil ) 
 2 tbsp chopped  onion 
 ½ tsp  ginger 
 1 tbsp  flour 
 2 cups prepared (boiled)  squash  or  pumpkin 
 2 cups  chicken bouillon  (use Maggi  chicken  cube) 
 2 cups  milk 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Sauté  butter ,  onion  and  ginger . 
 Stir in  flour . 
 Add  squash  and cook 5 minutes. 
 Gradually add  chicken stock  and  milk . 
 Add  salt  to taste. 
 Simmer for at least 5 more minutes.
