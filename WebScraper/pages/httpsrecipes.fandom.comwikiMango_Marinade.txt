Ingredients : 
 4  shallots 
 2" piece peeled  ginger 
 1 clove  garlic 
 2 tbsp  lime juice 
 ½ tsp ground  coriander seed 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 ⅛ tsp  pepper 
 ⅓ cup  olive oil 
 1 medium  mango , chopped 
 Directions : 
 In a food processor,process  garlic , shallots +  ginger . 
 Add  mango  and puree 30 seconds. 
 Add  lime juice ,  coriander seed ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Then process, adding  oil  in a thin stream while processor is running.
