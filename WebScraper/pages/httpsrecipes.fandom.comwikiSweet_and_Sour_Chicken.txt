 Description : 
 You can substitute  chicken  with  pork  or fish. A delicious meal served with  rice .
 
 Ingredients : 
 Group A : 
 2  chicken breasts 
 2 tsp  light soy sauce 
 2 tsp  sherry 
 1 cm fresh  ginger , crushed 
 salt  to taste 
 Group B : 
 2  eggs , beaten 
 1 tbsp  flour 
 1 tbsp  cornflour 
 salt  to taste 
 Group C : 
 ½ cup  water 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 ¼ cup  vinegar 
 1 tbsp  cornflour 
 1 tsp  soy sauce 
 1 tbsp tomato  ketchup 
 1 small  tomato , cut into wedges 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 1 tsp [hot sauce] 
 Garnish : 
 cucumber 
 sliced thinly  red pepper , julienned 
 Directions : 
 Marinate  chicken  with Group A ingredients for 30 minutes. 
 Coat  chicken  breasts with ingredients from Group B and deep fry in hot  oil  until golden brown and  chicken  is no longer pink. 
 Cut  chicken  breasts into bite size pieces. 
 Set aside. 
 To make sauce, mix ingredients from Group C in a pan and bring to boil on slow fire. 
 Season with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Pour over  chicken  breasts and garnish with  cucumber  and  red pepper . 
 Serve hot.
