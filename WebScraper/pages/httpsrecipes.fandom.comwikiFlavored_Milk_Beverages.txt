 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group

 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  milk  + one of the following flavorings: 
 Berry : 
 ½ cup fresh  berries 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 1 tbsp  lemon  or  orange juice , or ½ tbsp sweetened fruit 
 Coffee : 
 1 tbsp instant  coffee 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 Peanut butter : 
 1 to 2 tbsp  peanut butter 
 Strawberry : 
 1 to 2 tbsp  strawberry preserves 
 Vanilla : 
 ½ tbsp  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 In blender, mix 1 cup  milk  with one above choices.
