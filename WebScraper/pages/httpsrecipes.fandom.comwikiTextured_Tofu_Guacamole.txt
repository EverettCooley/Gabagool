Description : 
 Score some extra protein points blending  tofu  with  red onion  and green chilis.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4  avocados 
 10½ oz  tofu 
 ¼ medium  red onion , chopped 
 1½ tbsp canned  green chilis , diced 
 1 tbsp  mayonnaise  or plain  yogurt 
 2  garlic  cloves, pressed 
 1 tsp  Tabasco sauce 
 1 tsp  salt 
 optional garnish of sliced  red pepper 
 Directions : 
 Drain excess  water  from  tofu . 
 Combine with  mayonnaise  or  yogurt , mashing  tofu  with a fork. 
 Peel and seed avocados. 
 Combine all ingredients in a large bowl. Serve immediately.
