Fantasy Freeze Pie
 
 1 Pkg.  cream cheese , Softened (8 Oz) 
 1 Jar  marshmallow  Creme (7 Oz) 
 1 Cup  lime ,  orange  or  raspberry  Sherbet (softened) 
 2 Cups Thawed Whipped Topping ( Cool Whip , Etc.) 
 1/2 Cup  slivered almonds , Toasted Chopped 
 1 Deep Dish Pie Crush Shell, Baked 
 
Mix  cream cheese  And  marshmallow  Creme. Mix With Mixer Until Well Blended. Add Sherbet; Mix Well. Fold in Whipped Topping And  almonds . Pour Into Pie Shell. Freeze Until Firm. Serves 6 to 8Source: my Old Recipes
