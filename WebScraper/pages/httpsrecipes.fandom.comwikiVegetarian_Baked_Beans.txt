Ingredients : 
 250 g dried beans, soaked overnight 
 2  bay leaves 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 4 tablespoons  oil 
 250 g  Onion , chopped 
 3 dried chilies, deseeded 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 chopped  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Rinse the beans and put them in a pan with double the amount of  water . 
 Add 1 clove of  garlic  and the  bay leaves . 
 Bring to the boil, turn down the heat and simmer until done. 
 Drain the beans, but keep part of the cooking liquid. 
 Heat the  oil  in a heavy pan and bake the  onions  until soft. 
 Add the cooked, drained beans and the remaining  garlic . 
 Bake the beans for 5 minutes, adding cooking liquid if necessary. 
 Mix in the  parsley .
