Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 3/4 pound  Beef  sirloin  Steak , cut into 1-inch thick slices 
 1 medium  Onion , chopped 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 3 cups cooked  brown rice 
 1/2 cup  slivered almonds , toasted* 
 1 teaspoon fresh grated  ginger 
 1 teaspoon  curry  powder 
 3/4 teaspoon  salt 
 2 medium baking  Apples , coarsely chopped 
 1/4 cup  apple cider  or  apple juice 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in large skillet over medium-high heat until hot. Add  Beef ,  Onion  and  garlic ; cook, stirring constantly, 5 to 7 minutes or until  Beef  is no longer pink. 
 Add  rice ,  almonds ,  ginger ,  curry  powder and  salt . 
 Cook and stir 3 minutes or until flavors are well blended. 
 Just before serving, stir in  Apples  and  apple cider .
