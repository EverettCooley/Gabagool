Ingredients : 
 1 hamburger bun, low calorie 
 1 hamburger patty (morningstar fat free) 
 1 oz  red onion —sliced 
 1/2 oz roma  tomato —sliced 
 3/4 oz  romaine lettuce 
 1 1/2 tsp ketchup 
 1 1/2 tsp nonfat  Mayonnaise 
 1 1/2 tbsp bac'n pieces 
 Directions : 
 Put hamburger patty and  Onion  on grill express. Grill for 1 to 1 ½ minutes. 
 Pile on  bun  spread with mayonnaise, ketchup. 
 Add  lettuce  and sprinkle  Bacon  pieces over all
