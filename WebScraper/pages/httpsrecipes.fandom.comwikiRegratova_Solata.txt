Description : 
 Dandelion salad. Slovenians pick the dandelion every spring. The plants must be very young. Older leaves are bitter.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups  dandelion  shoots or young leaves. pick them in early spring or late autumn 
 2 medium  potatoes , cooked., peeled and sliced, or ½ cup cooked  kidney beans 
 3 tablespoons  apple vinegar 
 4 tablespoons  bacon , finely diced 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  caraway seeds 
 2 hard cooked  eggs , sliced 
 Directions : 
 Clean and wash  dandelion . 
 Put warm  potatoes  on it. 
 Melt  bacon  and mix with  vinegar . 
 Bring it to a boil and then pour over  potato  and  dandelion  to soften them a bit. 
 Add other ingredients. 
 Toss and garnish with sliced hard cooked  eggs . 
 Use as salad or as light dish. 
 Serve immediately.
