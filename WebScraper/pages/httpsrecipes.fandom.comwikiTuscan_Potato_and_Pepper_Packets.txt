Description : 
 Foil packet side dishes are the perfect way to cook a whole meal on the grill. Keep your kitchen cool with this potato, pepper and onion trio.
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 © 2007 Weight Watchers International, Inc. © 2007 WeightWatchers.com, Inc. 
 Preparation Time: 15 min | Cooking Time: 25 min 
 Servings: 4 (1 packet each) 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound uncooked  red potatoes , scrubbed and thinly sliced (about 3 cups) 
 1 medium  yellow pepper , cut into thin strips (about 2 cups) 
 1 medium  red onion , thinly sliced (1 cup) 
 1 tbsp  olive oil 
 2 medium  garlic  cloves, minced 
 1 tbsp  rosemary , fresh, chopped, or less to taste  [1] 
 ½ tsp table  salt 
 ½ tsp  black pepper , freshly ground 
 Directions : 
 Heat outdoor grill. 
 Tear off four 12-inch long pieces of aluminum foil. 
 In a large bowl, combine potatoes, pepper strips, onion, oil, garlic, rosemary, salt and black pepper until mixed and coated. 
 Spread about 1½ cups of vegetable mixture in a fairly even layer near 1 end of each piece of foil. 
 Fold foil over to form packets; fold edges over to form a tight seal. 
 Grill packets, turning a few times, until potatoes are tender, about 20 to 25 minutes. 
 Notes : 
 
 ↑   Rosemary imparts a strong flavor to this recipe. If you're not a big rosemary fan, cut back on the amount.
