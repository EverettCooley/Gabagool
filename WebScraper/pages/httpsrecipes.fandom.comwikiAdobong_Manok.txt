 Adobong Manok is one of the best Filipino dish.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 kg  chicken  legs 
 2 Tbsp  soy sauce 
 1 tsp black  peppercorn 
 2 Tbsp maggi  oyster sauce 
 3 Tbsp  vinegar 
 1 cup  water 
 1 Tbsp  vegetable oil 
 1 head  garlic , crushed
