 Ingredients : 
 4 pounds ripe  grapes 
 10 whole  cloves 
 12  allspice 
 1 stick of  cinnamon 
 gingerroot 
 Â½ cup  vinegar 
 sugar 
 Directions : 
 Wash and stem the grapes, then mash, tie the spices in a bag and add to the mashed grapes. 
 Cook until the grapes are soft and discard the spice bag. 
 Press the grape through a sieve and measure the pulp. 
 Add the vinegar and 1 cup sugar to each 2 cups pulp. 
 Cook and stir until the butter is thick. 
 Pour into hot jars and process for 10 minutes in a boiling water bath.
