Description : 
 Makes 24 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  self raising flour 
 ¼ tsp  mixed spice 
 ⅓ cup  butter 
 ⅓ cup  caster sugar 
 2 tbsp  sultanas 
 2 tbsp  currants 
 1 tbsp  mixed peel 
 1  egg , beaten 
 ½ cup  milk 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F). 
 Sift flour and spice. 
 Rub butter into flour with fingertips. 
 Add sugar, fruit and peel. 
 Mix with egg and sufficient milk to form a stiff dough. 
 Place spoonfuls of mixture on greased oven tray (cookie sheet). 
 Bake at 200°C until golden brown, 10 – 15 minutes. 
 Cool on wire rack.
