Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup  butter 
 ⅓ cup  cocoa 
 1  egg 
 1 cup flaked  coconut 
 2 tbsp instant vanilla pudding mix 
 2 cup  confectioners' sugar 
 3 square  semi-sweet chocolate 
 ¼ cup  granulated sugar 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 2 cup  graham cracker  crumbs 
 ½ cup chopped  walnuts 
 3 tbsp  milk 
 1 tbsp  butter 
 Directions : 
 Melt ½ cup butter in saucepan. 
 Blend in granulated sugar, cocoa, vanilla, egg, crumbs, coconut, and nuts. 
 Press mixture into 9-inch square pan. 
 Cream ¼ cup butter until light and add dry pudding mix, milk, and confectioners' sugar, blending well. 
 Spread over mixture and chill thoroughly. 
 Melt chocolate with 1 tablespoon butter, stirring to blend. 
 Spread over chilled mixture. 
 Chill, then cut into bars. Yummy!
