Ingredients : 
 2 oz  apricot kernel oil 
 1 oz  sunflower oil 
 1 oz  jojoba oil 
 1 oz  rice bran  or  oat bran 
 6 drops essential oils of  lavender  or  rosemary 
 Directions : 
 Heat plant oils together. 
 Mix in  bran  and essential oils.
