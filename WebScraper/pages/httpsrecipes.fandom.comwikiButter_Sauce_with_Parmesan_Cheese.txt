butter  Sauce With  Parmesan cheese 
 
 1/2 Cup Grated  Parmesan cheese 
 1/4 Cup  butter , Room Temperature 
 2 T. Cream 
 
Blend  Cheese  And Softened  butter  Together Thoroughly And Stir in The Cram. Good as a Topping For  Pasta  And as a Dip, as Well as Over Veggies.Makes 3/4 Cup
