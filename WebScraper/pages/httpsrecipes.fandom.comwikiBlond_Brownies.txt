Ingredients : 
 8 oz (1)pkg white cake mix 
 -no- Sugar 
 2  eggs 
 2 tbsp granulated  brown sugar 
 -replacement 
 2 tbsp  water 
 1/4 cup  mints   chocolate chips 
 1/4 cup  peanut butter chips 
 Directions : 
 Combine cake mix,  eggs ,  brown sugar  replacement and  water  in mixing bowl. Beat in medium speed until well blended and thickened. Fold in chips. Pour batter into two, greased and papered 8-in pans. Bake at 375 °F for 12 to 15 minutes or until brownies test done. Cut into 2-in squares. 
 Food exchange per serving: 1 bread exchange + ½ fat exchange per squares. calories: 1 square: 56;
