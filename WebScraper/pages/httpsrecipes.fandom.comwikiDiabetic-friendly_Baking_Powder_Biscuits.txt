Ingredients : 
 2 cups  flour 
 3 tbsp  baking powder 
 1 tsp  salt 
 4 tbsp  shortening 
 ¾ cup  skim milk 
 Directions : 
 Mix  flour ,  baking powder , and  salt  together. 
 Cut in the  shortening  until it is evenly mixed with the dry ingredients. 
 Then add,  milk , mixing well. 
 Then knead for a ½ minute and roll out to about 1½ inch in thickness. 
 Cut with a biscuit cutter. 
 Place the biscuits 1½ inches apart on a greased baking sheet. 
 The brush with the  milk  and bake at 450°F oven degrees 10 – 12 minutes. 
 Nutritional information : 
 One biscuit may be exchanged for: 1  bread  exchange or 1 fat exchange.
