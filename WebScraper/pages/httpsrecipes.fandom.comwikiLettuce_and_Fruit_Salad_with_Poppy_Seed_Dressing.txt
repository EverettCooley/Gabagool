 Description : 
 Yield: 2 cups
 
 Ingredients : 
 Fruit salad : 
 3 cans  mandarin orange , drained (11 oz ea.) 
 3  grapefruit , pink, peeled, seeded and sectioned 
 6  bibb lettuce  heads, torn in bite size pieces 
 Poppy seed dressing : 
 ¼ to ½ cup  onion , chopped 
 ¾ cup  tarragon vinegar 
 2 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 2½ tbsp  poppy seeds 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp dry  mustard 
 ¾ cup  oil , vegetable 
 Directions : 
 Combine  mandarin oranges ,  grapefruit  sections, and  lettuce  in a large salad bowl; toss lightly. 
 Serve with  poppy seed  dressing. 
 Poppy seed dressing : 
 Combine first 7 ingredients in container of electric blender; blend well. 
 Slowly add ¾ cup  vegetable oil , continuing to blend until thick. 
 Pour into a jar with a tight-fitting lid, and chill. 
 Shake well before serving. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Food exchange per serving: 1 fruit exchange + 1 fat exchange
