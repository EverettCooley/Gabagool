 Prep Time:15 
 Cook time:30 
 Serves:10 people 
 
 Description : 
 it is a great sauce for parties (this is for you spice fanatics)
 
 Ingredients : 
 10  habaneros 
 5 tsp of  red pepper flakes  (dried) 
 5 cans  tomato sauce 
 5 tbsp  hot sauce 
 7  jalapenos 
 and 3  chili peppers  (the whole thing) 
 Directions : 
 get a bowl 
 put the tomato sauce in 
 chop up the jalapenos 
 put in the pepper flakes 
 drop in the hot sauce 
 stir 
 add the chili peppers 
 add the haberneros 
 stir 
 add the jalepenos 
 stir 
 enjoy
