Ingredients : 
 11 oz. pizza dough 
 1 Tbsp. olive oil 
 3/4 cup vodka sauce 
 8 oz. mozzarella, sliced 
 1/4 cup pesto 
 Instructions : 
 Preheat oven to 400Â°F. 
 Spread out pizza dough onto a pizza stone or pizza pan. Spread olive oil over crust and cook 8 minutes with just the crust. 
 Remove from oven and top with vodka sauce and sliced mozzarella and cook an additional 8-10 minutes. 
 Swirl with pesto sauce and serve warm!
