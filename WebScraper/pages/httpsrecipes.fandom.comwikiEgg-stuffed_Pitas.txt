Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Love Estate in Sachse, Texas in 1984.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  butter 
 6  eggs  beaten 
 1 medium  tomato  chopped 
 2  scallions  green and white parts finely chopped 
 ½ cup crumbled  feta cheese 
 ½ teaspoon dried  oregano 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  pepper 
 6  pita  breads 
 Directions : 
 Melt butter in a large skillet over moderate heat. 
 Combine eggs, tomato, scallions, feta, oregano, salt and pepper. 
 Pour into skillet and cook until set. 
 Cut pockets in the pita breads with a serrated knife. 
 Fill breads with egg mixture.
