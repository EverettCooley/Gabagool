      Chocolate Cake     
 Ingredients : 
 175 g  chocolate 
 90 g  butter 
 4 tbsp  sugar 
 3  egg whites 
 45 g chopped  peanuts  (optional) 
 45 g  breadcrumbs 
 Glazing : 
 175g  heavy cream 
 4 tbsp  rum 
 Directions : 
 Melt  chocolate  and  butter  in double boiler. 
 Add  sugar , beaten egg-whites, a half  nuts  and bread crumbs and mix thoroughly. 
 Grease the baking mold with  butter , sprinkle with bread crumbs and  nuts , pour in  chocolate  batter and bake in a preheated oven 190°C (or 375°F) for 25 minutes. 
 Cool down and pour over  cream  whipped with  rum .
