Description : 
 Makes 4 servings (1 1/4 cups each).
 
 May substitute 12 packets Equal® sweetener. 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups plain nonfat  yogurt 
 1 medium size ripe  banana , peeled, sliced 
 1/2 cup Equal® Spoonful* 
 3 tablespoons  lemon juice 
 2 cups frozen unsweetened whole  strawberries 
 1 cup frozen unsweetened red  raspberries 
 Directions : 
 Combine  yogurt ,  banana , Equal® and  lemon juice  in blender container; cover. Blend until smooth. Add half of frozen fruit. Blend until smooth. Repeat with remaining frozen fruit. 
 Serve immediately in tall glasses.
