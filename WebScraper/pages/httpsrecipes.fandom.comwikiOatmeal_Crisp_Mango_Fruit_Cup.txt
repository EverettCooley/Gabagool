 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup  water 
 3/4 cup honey 
 1/3 cup  vegetable oil 
 1 tsp.  vanilla 
 4 cups  old-fashioned oats 
 1 tsp.  cinnamon 
 1 cup  golden raisins 
 1 cup  bananas , sliced 
 1 cup  Apples , sliced 
 1 cup  grapes , red, seedless 
 1 cup  mango , peeled and diced 
 1/2 tsp.  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Pre-heat oven to 350'. In a small saucepan, bring to boil 1/2 cup  water . Add honey,  oil  and  vanilla ; mix well and remove from heat. In a large bowl, combine  Oats  and  cinnamon , then gradually stir in honey mixture until  Oats  are well coated. Place oat mixture in ungreased 15x10x2 inch baking dish and bake for 15 minutes. Remove from oven and stir well. Reduce heat to 275' and return to oven baking 1 to 11/2 hours or until toasted and crisp, stirring every 15 minutes. Cool, toss with  Raisins  and set aside. Combine  bananas ,  Apples   grapes  and  mangos . Toss with  cinnamon . Divide evenly between 8 pudding cups and top with  oatmeal  crisp
