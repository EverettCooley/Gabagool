 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 Tbsp LAND O LAKES  butter 
 1 large  Onion , cut in half and sliced 
 3  California avocados , peeled and sliced 
 1 (3-ounce) package  cream cheese , softened 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp freshly ground  pepper 
 1 12-inch) pizza crust 
 1 small red  bell pepper , thinly sliced 
 1 small yellow  bell pepper , thinly sliced 
 ½ Cup sliced  Kalamata  * 1 Cup shredded  provolone   Cheese 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter  in a large skillet over medium heat.
 Add sliced  Onion , and cook, stirring occasionally, 20 minutes or until  Onion  is caramel colored.
 Process 1 sliced  avocado  and next 3 ingredients in a food processor until smooth, stopping to scrape down sides; spread over pizza crust. 
 Top with  Onion , remaining  avocado ,  bell peppers , and  Cheese . 
 Bake at 400° for 15 minutes.
