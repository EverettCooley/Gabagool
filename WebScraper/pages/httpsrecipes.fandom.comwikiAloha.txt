Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ oz  Myers's Original Dark Rum 
 ¼ oz  dry vermouth 
 ¼ oz  cognac 
 ¼ oz  gin 
 ½ oz  lime juice 
 1 oz  soda water 
 Directions : 
 Shake with ice cubes and strain into large cocktail glass. 
 Garnish with  lime  wheel.
