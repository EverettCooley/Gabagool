Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves: 4 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  ground beef 
 1 cup  water 
 1 can (16 oz) undrained stewed  tomatoes 
 1 cup uncooked  pasta  (elbow  macaroni ) 
 1 envelope vegetable soup mix 
 Directions : 
 Brown  beef  and drain. 
 Stir in remaining ingredients. 
 Bring to a boil then simmer covered, stirring occasionally, for 20 minutes or until pasta is done.
