Instant Green Enchilada Sauce
 
 5 Canned Whole Green Chilies, Drained 
 1 Can Green Enchilada Sauce (10 Oz), Without  vinegar  (use Old el Paso Green Enchilada Sauce For This) 
 1 Canned Whole Jalapeño or 3 t Chopped 
 1 clove  garlic , Chopped 
 salt 
 
 1/4 Cup Chopped  scallion  For Flavor 
 
Rinse Drained Canned Chilies Briefly in Tepid Running  water  to Reduce Their Preserved Taste. Using Electric Blender or Food Processor, Puree All Ingredients Except  salt , Including  scallion .  salt  Lightly And Taste. The Sauce Will be Quite Hot And Will Turn Hotter as it Stands. Don't Rush to Test Your Courage With Another Canned Jalapeño. Although This Sauce Can be Used For Cooking as Is, It's Flavor Matures if You Transfer it to Pan And Heat to a Simmer For 2 to 3 Minutes. The Finished Sauce Stores Well in Refrigerator Closely Covered With Saran But it is Not so Long Lived as Red  chili sauce . Makes 1 1/2 Cups.
