Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Steep Estate in Elmo, Texas in 1989.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds lean  ground round 
 2 tablespoons  Worcestershire sauce 
 ½ cup finely chopped  red onion 
 1  egg 
 5 ounces  hot jalapeño pepper sauce 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 6 slices pepper  jack cheese  OR  Muenster  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients except  cheese . 
 Form into 6 patties and grill to desired doneness. 
 Top with  cheese  and cook 1 minute longer to allow  cheese  to melt. 
 Place on buns and top with your favorite condiments.
