      Apple pie spice     
 About Apple pie spice : 
 Apple pie spice is a blend of spices commonly used in apple pies, but it can also be used in other recipes. The combination consists of some of the following spices:
 
 cinnamon 
 nutmeg 
 allspice 
 cardamom
