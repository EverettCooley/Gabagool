Ingredients : 
 1 ripe medium  avocado 
 2 ripe large  avocados 
 4 cups  heavy cream 
 1 large ripe  tomato 
 2 ripe  tomatillos 
 1 small  sweet onion 
 1 clove roasted  garlic 
 1  yellow bell pepper 
 3 tbsp  olive oil 
 Â½ cup finely grated pepper  jack cheese 
 sea salt 
 pepper 
 Directions : 
 In a blender mix 4 cups of  cream  and 2 large avocados, blend until smooth. 
 In a large frying pan add 2 tbs. 
 olive oil , heat on medium for 6 to 8 minutes. 
 Add small sweet  onion  (medium diced) and roasted  garlic  clove, stir around in  oil  until caramelized (golden brown). 
 Then add  cream  and  avocado  mixture, stir and reduce mixture until you reach desired thickness. 
 Add a pinch or two of  sea salt  and  pepper  (to taste). 
 Then lower temperature to low and stir in pepper jack  cheese  until smooth and creamy. 
 In a separate frying pan add 1 tbs. 
 olive oil , heat on medium for 6 to 8 minutes. 
 Then add  tomato , tomatillos, yellow  bell pepper  (medium diced) stir and cook just briefly to warm the inside of the vegetables (1 to 3 minutes). 
 Dice medium  avocado , then pour sauce mixture over your favorite  pasta  and sprinkle with warm vegetables and  avocado ! grate fresh  parmesan cheese  on top if desired and enjoy!!!
