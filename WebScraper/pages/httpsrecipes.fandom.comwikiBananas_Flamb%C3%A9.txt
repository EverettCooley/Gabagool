Ingredients : 
 6  bananas 
 2 oz  butter 
 4 oz  castor sugar 
 powdered  cinnamon 
 1 glass of old  rum 
 Directions : 
 Peel  bananas  and cut them in two lengthwise parts. 
 Put them in the frying-pan, in melted warm  butter , make brown each side of them and sprinkle them with  sugar  and cinnamon. 
 When they are caramelized, put them with preheated  rum . 
 And then, "flamb√© " them when serving them.
