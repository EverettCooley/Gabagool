 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 bananas  or any other fruit 
 Skim  milk 
 honey (optional for added sweetness) 
 Directions : 
 Before hand, peel and freeze  bananas  in a plastic bag. When you are ready, put 8 oz. of slim  milk  in a blender; Add  bananas  (1 is enough for a smoothie, 2 for a shake); Blend until smooth; Pour into a cup and enjoy! You can use any type of fresh fruit:  strawberries ,  Apples , or  oranges , the possibilities are endless!
