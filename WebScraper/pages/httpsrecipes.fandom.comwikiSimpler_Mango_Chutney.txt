Description : 
 It's delicious served with vegetable fritters (pakora), chips or crudités
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 – 4 tbsp chopped  cilantro  (or  mint ) 
 2 – 3 chopped, seeded  jalapenos 
 juice of one  lime 
 salt  to taste 
 2  green mangoes , peeled and chopped 
 sugar  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Place all ingredients in blender until smooth!
