 Description : 
 Morning, Lunch or Dinner  avocado  on toast is a treat.
 
 Because this recipe uses  California avocados ...it's definitely nutritious!
 
 Ingredients : 
 bread  of any type 
 butter , for spread on toast 
 Spoonful of a California avacado 
 Pinch of  salt 
 Directions : 
 Toast  bread , med 
 Spread  butter  over  bread 
 Scoop out a spoonful of  avocado , spread over  bread 
 Add a pinch of  salt  for taste
