Ingredients : 
 ¼ lb drip grind  coffee 
 1  egg 
 1 quart rapidly boiling  water 
 Directions : 
 Measure ¼ lb drip grind  coffee  in a 2-quart glass or enamel saucepan. 
 Stir in 1  egg , slightly beaten and enough  water  to mix thoroughly (about ½ cup). 
 Pour 1 quart rapidly boiling  water  over the  coffee  and stir up. 
 Bring slowly to a boil, stirring occasionally. 
 Remove from heat, cover, and allow to stand for 3 to 4 minutes. 
 Strain through fine sieve or cheese cloth into hot serving pot. 
 Pour in small demitasse cups. 
 Serve black.
