Description : 
 Contributed by Catsrecipes Y-Group
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  pinto beans 
 ¼ cup  northern beans 
 ¼ cup  black beans 
 ¼ cup  lentils 
 ½ pound  Italian sausage , crumbled 
 8 cups of  water 
 2 cups of  tomato  or  spaghetti sauce 
 Seasoning packet : 
 2 tablespoons dried  basil 
 1 tablespoons  chili powder 
 1 tsp ground  cumin 
 ½ tsp  garlic powder 
 2 tablespoons veggie  bouillon  granules 
 Directions : 
 Cover beans with  water , and soak overnight. 
 Drain beans. 
 Brown crumbled  sausage  in large pan. 
 Add beans,  water  and tomato or spaghetti sauce and seasoning packet. 
 Stir well, and bring to simmering, cover and cook for an hour and ½ until beans are tender. 
 Stir occasionally.
