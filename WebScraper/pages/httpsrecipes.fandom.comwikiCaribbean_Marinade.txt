Description : 
 This marinade is yummy on  pork ,  poultry  and  fish .
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 From the Recipe Ross Newsletter 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  lime juice 
 ¼ cup  orange juice 
 2 tbsp  cider vinegar 
 1 crushed  garlic  clove 
 ½ tsp ground  allspice 
 ½ tsp crushed  thyme 
 ¼ tsp  cayenne pepper 
 Directions : 
 Combine ingredients in a shallow dish.
