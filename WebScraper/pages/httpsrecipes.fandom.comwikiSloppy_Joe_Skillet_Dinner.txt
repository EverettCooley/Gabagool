Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 8/5/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  ground beef 
 1 (15 oz) can  chili 
 1 cup  onion  chopped 
 2 cups  Fritos  slightly crushed 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 1 cup shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 In a skillet, brown onion and hamburger. 
 Drain off fat. 
 Stir in chili and pepper. 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 I a greased casserole dish, alternate layers of chili mixture, Fritos and cheese. 
 Cover and bake until heated through about 10 minutes.
