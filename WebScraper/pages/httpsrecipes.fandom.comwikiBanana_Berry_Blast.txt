Ingredients : 
 1 large  banana , peeled, sliced and frozen 
 ¾ cup  berries , frozen or fresh,  strawberries ,  raspberries  or  blueberries  recommended 
 ¾ cup vanilla frozen  yogurt , low-fat or fat-free 
 12 oz can or bottle of  ginger ale , regular or diet 
 Directions : 
 Place all the ingredients in a blender or food processor. 
 Cover and blend at highest speed until smooth. 
 Notes : 
 Note: if you use fresh  berries , try to use a frozen  banana , and if you use a fresh  banana , go for frozen  berries . Otherwise your drink will be too thin. 
 Both fruits being frozen will give you a satisfyingly thick drink.
