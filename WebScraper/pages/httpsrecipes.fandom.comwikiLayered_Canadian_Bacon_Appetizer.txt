 Description : 
 A solid but suprisingly light appetizer to start with, especially the  Bacon  is a delight for the senses.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (8-ounce) package  cream cheese  softened 
 1 tablespoon refrigerated pesto sauce 
 1/2 cup pizza sauce 
 1 1/2 cups shredded  iceberg lettuce 
 1/2 cup Canadian  Bacon  sliced and chopped 
 1/2 cup shredded  mozzarella   Cheese 
 Directions : 
 In bowl, combine  cream cheese  and pesto. Spread mixture onto serving platter to about 1/4-inch thickness. Top with pizza sauce,  lettuce , Canadian  Bacon  and  Cheese . Serve with tortilla chips, crackers, or toasted pita bread wedges for dipping.
