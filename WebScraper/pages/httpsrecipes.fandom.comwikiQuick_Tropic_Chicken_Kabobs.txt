 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  chicken  chunks 
 ½ cup Italian  salad dressing 
 1 tbsp.  curry powder 
 ⅓ cup  honey 
 3 cups  plum  chunks 
 2 cups  mango  chunks 
 12 bamboo skewers 
 Directions : 
 Marinate  chicken  in  salad dressing ,  curry  and  honey  at least 3 hours. 
 Thread on bamboo skewers alternately with  mango  and  plum . 
 Brush with marinade. 
 Broil or barbecue, basting until done.
