 Ingredients : 
 2 cup  Broccoli , cut Â¾" pieces 
 1/2 cup  Onion , chopped 
 1/4 tsp  lemon pepper 
 1/4 tsp  thyme , dried & crushed 
 1/4 tsp cooking  oil 
 1 cl  garlic , minced 
 4  Chicken  breast halves 
 -boned skinless(1 1b total) 
 1 cup halved  cherry tomatoes 
 Directions : 
 Spray a cold large skillet with nonstick coating. Preheat skillet over medium heat.
Add  Broccoli ,  Onion ,  lemon pepper , and  thyme  to skillet. Cook and stir for 3 to 4 minutes or till vegetables are crisp-tender. Remove each vegetable from the skillet when crisp/tender; keep warm.
Add  oil  and  garlic  to hot skillet. 
Rinse  Chicken ; pat dry. Add to skillet. Cook  Chicken  over medium-high heat about 10 minutes or till  Chicken  is tender and not longer pink, turning once.
Add  cherry tomatoes  and vegetables. Cover and cook 1 to 2 minute or till heated through. 
Makes 4 servings. 
Food exhange per serving: 3 lean meat exhanges + 1 vegetable exchange + 1 fat exchange
