Description : 
 Rice salad. Htamin Thouk is an popular food in Myanmar. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 rice 
 prawn powder 
 pepper oil 
 lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Get some rice. 
 Mix oil and pepper and boil it. 
 If you have prawn powder, you can smear it on the rice. 
 Mix the pepper oil with the rice. 
 Squeeze the lemon and mix it with rice. 
 There you go, a rice salad!
