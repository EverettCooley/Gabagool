Ingredients : 
 1 box German Chocolate cake mix 
 1 cup chopped  pecans  ( optional ) 
 1 8 oz  cream cheese  softened 
 1 stick  butter  softened 
 1 box  powdered sugar 
 1 cup  shredded coconut 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 and grease a 9 x 13 cake pan. 
 Prepare cake mix according to package directions and add nuts if using. 
 Pour into prepared pan 
 Blend cream cheese and butter till smooth. 
 Add powdered sugar and coconut. (This mixture will be very thick) 
 Drop by teaspoon on top of unbaked cake til mixture is all used up. 
 Bake for 40 - 45 minutes. 
 Notes : 
 The cake batter rises and the cream cheese mixture settles on the 
bottom.
