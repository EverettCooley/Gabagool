Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 4 dozen 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup [{Crisco]] 
 ½ cup  granulated sugar 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 1 tsp  vanilla extract 
 ½ cup  brown sugar 
 1  egg 
 2 cups  all-purpose flour 
 1 tsp  cream of tartar 
 1 cup  coconut 
 Directions : 
 Mix Crisco and sugar until creamy; add egg. 
 Sift all dry ingredients together. 
 Add to Crisco mixture. 
 Roll dough into small balls and dip into water, then into brown sugar. 
 Put on cookie sheet. 
 Bake at 375 °F for 15 minutes.
