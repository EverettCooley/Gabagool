Description : 
 Makes 8 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 2 cups (about 8 ounces) sliced fresh  Mushrooms 
 1 small  Onion , minced 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 3 cups cooked  brown rice 
 1 cup (6 ounces) diced  Ham 
 1/2 cup chopped  walnuts  (optional) 
 1/4 cup chopped fresh  parsley 
 1 tablespoon  white wine vinegar 
 1 tablespoon Dijon  mustard 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in Dutch oven or large saucepan over medium-low heat until hot. 
 Add  Mushrooms ,  Onion  and  garlic ; cook until tender. 
 Stir in  rice ,  Ham ,  walnuts ,  parsley ,  vinegar ,  mustard  and  pepper ; cook, stirring, until thoroughly heated.
