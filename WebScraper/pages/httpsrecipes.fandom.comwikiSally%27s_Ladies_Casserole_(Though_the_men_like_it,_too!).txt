 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  ground beef 
 ½ lb  ground pork 
 2  onions , finely cut 
 20 oz can  tomato soup   [1] 
 3 oz pkg.  cream cheese 
 butter 
 ¼ cup chopped  almonds 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 1½ tbsp  Worcestershire 
 2 tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 ½ lb wide  noodles 
 ½ lb  mushrooms 
 ½ cup crushed  cornflakes 
 Directions : 
 Cook beef and pork and onions in 2 tbsp butter until golden brown. 
 Add tomato soup, crumbled cream cheese, sugar, Worcestershire, salt and pepper. 
 Simmer 5 to 10 minutes until thick. 
 Have noodles ready cooked and in a large greased casserole. 
 Pour meat mixture over noodles. 
 Brown mushrooms in 1 tbsp butter and spread on top. 
 Sprinkle crushed cornflakes on top. 
 Bake at 350°F for 30 minute. 
 Five or ten minutes before serving, sprinkle chopped almonds over the top and let them toast slightly. 
 Notes : 
 
 ↑   I don't know if the tomato soup called for in this recipe is condensed or diluted. I'd probably guess condensed.
