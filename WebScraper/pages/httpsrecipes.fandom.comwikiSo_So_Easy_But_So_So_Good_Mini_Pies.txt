Description : 
 These are so easy and good.
 
 Contributed by  Lindas Busy Kitchen Desserts Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large tube of Grands flaky biscuits 
 1 stick  butter  melted 
 1 small jar pineapple preserves — can use strawberry if you prefer 
 cinnamon 
 granulated sugar 
 non-stick  cooking spray 
 8 ounce block of  cream cheese  sliced into 8 strips 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Spray a 9 x 13 cookie sheet with non stick spray. Sprinkle a little flour on a sheet of waxed paper and roll out each biscuit until flat — about  1 / 16  inch thick. 
 Spread a coat of butter on flattened circle,put one slice of cream cheese and a teaspoon of preserves in middle and sprinkle with cinnamon,roll up jelly roll style and place on cookie sheet — continue until all biscuits are filled and rolled. 
 Brush each with remaining butter — sprinkle with sugar and top with a sprinkle of cinnamon. 
 Bake until turning golden brown — take spatula and loosen each from pan where as they will not stick as they cool. 
 Serve with a dollop of Cool Whip or can be reheated in microwave and a scoop of ice cream put on top.
