Description : 
 Despite the name, this concoction contains no actual Dr. Pepper®. Does it taste like Dr. Pepper? Decide for yourself?
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ of a jig of  amaretto 
 ¼ jig of 151 proof  rum 
 ½ a glass of  beer 
 Directions : 
 Pour the beer into a glass. 
 Next, fill a shot glass with the amaretto and rum. 
 Use a match to light the shot on fire. 
 Drop the shot into the beer.
