Description : 
 The best bean dip ever tasted!
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: Cook 'em Horns 
 Serves 15 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can  refried beans  (15 oz) 
 4 oz  cream cheese , softened 
 12 oz  sour cream 
 1 bunch  green onions , finely chopped 
 ½ (12 oz) can oregano  salsa 
 1 packet  taco seasoning  (1.25 oz) 
 ½ can diced green  chile peppers  (4 oz can) 
 dash  oregano 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 1 cup grated  Monterey jack ,  longhorn  or  cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients except grated cheese in ovenproof dish. 
 Sprinkle top with grated cheese. 
 Bake 1 hour at 300°F.
