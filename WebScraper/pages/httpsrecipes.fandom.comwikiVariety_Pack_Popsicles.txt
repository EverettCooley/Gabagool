Ingredients : 
 1 (6 oz) can frozen  orange juice  concentrate, softened  or  use  grape juice   or   cranberry juice 
 1 (6 oz) can  water 
 1 pint vanilla  ice cream , softened  or  2 containers of plain  yogurt 
 popsicle sticks and cups 
 Directions : 
 Whir in a blender. 
 Pour into molds, insert sticks, and freeze.
