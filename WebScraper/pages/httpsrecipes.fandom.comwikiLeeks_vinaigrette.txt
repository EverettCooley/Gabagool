 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 8 med size  leeks , trimmed well 
 -washed 
 1 tbsp balsamic vinaigrette 
 Directions : 
 Split the trimmed  leeks  lengthwise, leaving the white root ends whole. Make certain all sand is rinsed away. Bundle  leeks  and tie with string. Drop into saucepan of boiling  water  and simmer, covered, about 12 minutes, until just tender. Serve immediately with vinaigrette, or place in iced  water  to stop cooking, and serve chilled. Food exchanges per serving: 1 vegtable exchange + 1 fat exchange; calories: 76; cho: omg; car: 8g; pro: 1g; sod: 46mg; fat: 5g;
