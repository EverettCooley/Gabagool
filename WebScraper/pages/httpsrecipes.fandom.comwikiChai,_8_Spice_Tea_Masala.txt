      8 Spice Tea Masala     
 Description : 
 This is my favorite recipe. If you like the authentic taste of Indian restaurant tea this is it.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ tsp  black peppercorns 
 ¼ teaspoons  carom seeds 
 1 tsp dried  ginger  or 2 – 3 slices of fresh  gingerroot 
 1 tsp ground  cinnamon 
 1  black cardamom pod 
 seeds from 25  green cardamom pods 
 4  cloves 
 ½ tsp  fennel seeds 
 1 – 2 teaspoons of loose  tea  or a tea bag (any kind of tea will work, I like  black  or  green tea . 
 1 cup of  water 
 1 cup of  milk 
 Directions : 
 First grind ingredients (don’t grind fresh  ginger ) 
 In a pot bring  water  to a boil. 
 Add spices (and fresh  ginger  slices if using) and simmer for 5 minutes. 
 Add the tea. 
 Boil for 1 minute then add  milk  and boil once more. 
 Strain into a cup 
 Serve with honey or  sugar  to taste.
