 Description : 
 Cook Time : 5 minutes 
 Serves : 32 
 Ingredients : 
 2 loaves  Texas toast  (or very thickly sliced bread) 
 2 pints  milk 
 12  eggs 
 1 box  brown sugar 
 Directions : 
 Beat eggs in a bowl 
 Pour in milk and stir 
 Add brown sugar 
 Stir until mixture is creamy 
 Dip Texas toast in mixture 
 Drop unto a hot buttered griddle. 
 Flip from side to side 
 Brown to desired color 
 Remove from griddle promptly.
