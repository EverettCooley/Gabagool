Description : 
 This recipe uses edible papaya seeds which add a peppery taste.
 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 Makes 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 4 roast  chicken breast  halves, boned and sliced 
 Slaw : 
 ¼ cup  mayonnaise 
 ¼ cup  orange juice 
 2 tsp  white vinegar 
 ½ tsp each  salt  and  pepper 
 1  papaya , seeds reserved, sliced 
 4 cups  coleslaw mix 
 1  red bell pepper , thinly sliced 
 ½ cup coarsely chopped  cilantro 
 Mojo sauce : 
 1 cup  cilantro  leaves 
 ⅓ cup  orange juice 
 2 tbs  olive oil 
 1 clove  garlic 
 ¼ tsp each  salt ,  paprika ,  cumin 
 Directions : 
 Slaw : 
 Whisk first 5 ingredients in a bowl. 
 Add remaining ingredients, toss to mix and coat. 
 Mojo sauce : 
 Blend ingredients in a blender until cilantro is very finely chopped. 
 Spoon sauce over chicken. 
 Serve with the slaw, sprinkled with papaya seeds.
