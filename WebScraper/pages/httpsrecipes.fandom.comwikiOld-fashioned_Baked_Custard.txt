Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Original recipe 
 Recipe makes 3 cups (6 servings) 
 Ingredients : 
 3 large  eggs , slightly beaten, or ¾-cup  egg substitute 
 2 tablespoons  sugar 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅛ teaspoon ground  nutmeg 
 2 cups  fat-free milk 
 ½ teaspoon pure  vanilla extract 
 pinch of ground  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 325°F. 
 In a large bowl, combine eggs, sugar, salt and nutmeg. 
 Slowly stir in milk and vanilla. 
 Pour ½ cup custard into each of six 5-ounce custard cups. 
 Sprinkle with cinnamon. 
 Set filled custard cups in a shallow pan. 
 Pour about 1 inch of hot water in the pan around custard cups. 
 Bake on center rack of oven 35 minutes, or until a knife inserted comes out clean. 
 Serve hot, warm or chilled.
