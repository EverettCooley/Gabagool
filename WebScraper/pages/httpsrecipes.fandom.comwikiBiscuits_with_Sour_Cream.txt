Description : 
 In Romanian: Pesmeti cu smantana
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup  sugar 
 3  yolks 
 2 whole  eggs 
 ½ teaspoon  baking soda 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 ½ cup  butter  or  lard 
 vanilla 
 flour  as needed 
 Directions : 
 Beat the  sugar  with the yolks and whole  eggs , add  vanilla  and  sour cream ,  baking soda  and  butter  and mix well. 
 Add  flour  and knead a rather soft dough. 
 Let rest for 10 – 15 minutes. 
 Then roll into a ½ thick sheet. 
 Cut into shapes using cookie cutters, place on the baking sheet and bake at medium heat until golden. 
 These biscuits are tenderer after 2 – 3 days.
