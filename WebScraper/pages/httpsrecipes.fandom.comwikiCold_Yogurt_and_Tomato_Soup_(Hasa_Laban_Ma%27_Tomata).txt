 Description : 
 This is a different and delicious recipe to make for your whole family. When you have to go get the kids from school, run some errands, and go grocery shopping, who has the time to cook? With this recipe, just whip it up before you leave and it's ready when your ready. I would highly recommend this dish to all busy moms.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cup  tomato juice 
 2 cup unsweetened  yogurt 
 1/4 cup  lemon juice 
 2 tbl  olive oil 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 1/4 tsp  chilli powder 
 2 tbl finely chopped fresh  coriander leaves  ( cilantro ) 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Thoroughly mix all ingredients except the  coriander leaves . Chill in a refrigerator for at least two hours. 
 Place in serving bowls and garnish with the chopped  coriander leaves . 
 Serves 6 to 8.
