 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 1/4 cups  rye flour 
 1 cup of grated  Swiss cheese 
 1 Cup of Grated  Ham 
 1/3 Cup of  vegetable oil 
 1  egg 
 1/2 Teaspoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Combine the  flour ,  Cheese ,  Ham  and  salt  in a bowl, then add the  egg  and the  oil , mixing thoroughly. Roll the dough out to about 1/2Â inch and cut biscuits with a dogbone cookie cutter or use any other shape. Place biscuits onto an ungreased cookie sheet. Bake for15-25 mins, depending on the size of the biscuits. Store in the refrigerator in an airtight container.
