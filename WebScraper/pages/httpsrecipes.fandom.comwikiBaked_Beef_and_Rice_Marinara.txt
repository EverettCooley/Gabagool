Makes 4 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound lean ground  Beef 
 3/4 cup sliced fresh  Mushrooms 
 1/2 cup chopped  Onion 
 1/2 cup chopped  celery 
 1/2 cup diced  green pepper 
 2 cups cooked  rice 
 3/4 teaspoon ground  oregano 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1/2 teaspoon  basil 
 1/2 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 1 (15-ounce) can  tomato  sauce 
 3 slices  American cheese 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Beef ,  Mushrooms ,  Onion ,  celery  and  green pepper  over medium-high heat in large skillet until meat is no longer pink and vegetables are crisp tender, stirring frequently. 
 Combine meat mixture with  rice ,  oregano ,  salt ,  basil ,  garlic  and  tomato  sauce in buttered 2-quart baking dish; arrange  Cheese  slices on top. 
 Bake at 350 degrees 20 to 25 minutes.
