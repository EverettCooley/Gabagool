 Oh so good!
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source:  http://www.mccormick.com 
 Makes 8 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 3 tbsp  butter 
 ½ cup  light brown sugar 
 1 can (21 oz)  apple  pie filling 
 2 tsp ground  cinnamon 
 1½ tsp pure  vanilla extract 
 1 can (12 oz) refrigerated  biscuits 
 ½ cups chopped  nuts 
 Directions : 
 In a saucepan, melt butter, stir in brown sugar, apple pie filling, cinnamon and vanilla. 
 Spread half the apple mixture in a 2 quart shallow baking dish. 
 Separate biscuits ; cut each into quarters. 
 Arrange pieces, points up over mixture. 
 Spoon remaining mixture over biscuits and sprinkle with nuts. 
 Bake at 350°F for 35 to 40 minutes. 
 Let stand for 5 minutes. 
 Serve warm.
