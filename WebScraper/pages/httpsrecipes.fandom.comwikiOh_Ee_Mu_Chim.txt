Description : 
 Cucumber salad
 
       Oh ee mu chim     
 Ingredients : 
 8 oz  English cucumber  or seeded  cucumber 
 3 tablespoons  rice vinegar 
 3 tablespoons  sugar 
 1 tablespoon  soy sauce 
 2 teaspoons  sesame seeds 
 1½ teaspoons minced  garlic 
 ½ teaspoon go choo ga roo (Korean  chili powder ) 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Half the  cucumber  and slice very thinly. 
 Put all ingredients in a bowl, add  cucumber , mix. 
 Serve cold.
