 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 legs of goat, cut into 1" pieces, cleaned well 
 1 c. Bar-B-Q sauce (optional) 
 2 qt. boiling  water 
 1 Tbs.  rum 
 4 Tbs.  lemon juice 
 2 Tbs.  butter 
 2 Tbs.  oil 
 1 Tbs.  vinegar 
 salt ,  hot pepper ,  Onion ,  garlic ,  parsley  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Mix  water  and  lemon juice  together. Before seasoning the meat, pour the boiling  water  and  lemon juice  mixture over the goat. This helps remove the strong smell of the meat. Let it stand for 2 or 3 minutes. Drain and discard the  water  completely and pour seasoning,  vinegar ,  rum ,  butter ,  oil , Bar-B- Q sauce over the meat. Marinade for 3 or more hours. 
 Put meat and marinade in a saucepan and cook until the meat is tender. (Amount of time depends on piece of meat). Then put the goat on a grill and baste with marinade.
