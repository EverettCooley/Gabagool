 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 oz  light rum 
 1 tsp 151 proof  rum 
 1 tbsp passion-fruit syrup 
 1 oz  orange juice 
 1 oz  lime juice 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients with 1/2 cup crushed  ice  in an electric blender. 
 Blend at a low speed for a short length of time. 
 Pour into an old-fashioned glass and serve.
