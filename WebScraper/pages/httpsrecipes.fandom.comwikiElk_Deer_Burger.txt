Description : 
 A simple but delicious recipe, good meat and good ingredients makes this a feast !
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb ground elk or  deer  meat 
 1  egg 
 ½ cup  rolled oats 
 1 tbsp Montreal Steak spice 
 2 cloves of chopped  garlic 
 ½ cup  salsa  or  BBQ sauce 
 ½ cup of fresh chopped  dill 
 Directions : 
 Set barbeque to high heat. 
 Mix ingredients together and shape into four or five patties. 
 Place patties on grill and sear each side. 
 Reduce heat to low until thoroughly cooked. 
 Serve with your favourite condiments.
