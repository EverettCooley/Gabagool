 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 	  Chicken  bouillon cubes or  vegetable stock 
 1 (14 1/2 	ounce) can stewed  tomatoes 
 1 	cup  carrots , chopped 
 2 	cups  onions , chopped 
 1 	cup scallions, sliced 
 3/4 	cup creamy  Peanut   butter 
 1 	cup  milk 
 1/4 	cup instant mashed  potatoes 
 1 	dash  chili powder  or  Cayenne , and  salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Boil 4 cups of  water  in a 3-qt saucepan and dissolve  Chicken  cubes. 
 Add  tomatoes ,  carrots ,  onions  and scallions. 
 Reduce heat and simmer for 5â€“6 minutes until  carrots  and tender. 
 Mix in  Peanut   butter  and stir until well blended, add  milk , instant  potatoes  and seasonings. 
 Simmer until sauce thickens.
