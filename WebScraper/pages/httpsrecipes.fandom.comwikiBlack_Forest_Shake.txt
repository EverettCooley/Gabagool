 Ingredients : 
 vodka 
 chocolate  syrup 
 cherry brandy 
 milk 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients;  chocolate  syrup,  cherry brandy ,  vodka , and  milk  with  ice  cubes very well in a blender. 
 Pour into an attractive low glass, and serve.
