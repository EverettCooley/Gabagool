Description : 
 Source:  Lean and Luscious and Meatless, Volume 3  in the series, by Bobbie Hinman and Millie Snyder
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup low-fat  cottage cheese 
 1 cup canned crushed  pineapple , sugar-free, drained slightly 
 1 tbsp  honey 
 ½ tsp  vanilla extract 
 ⅛ tsp  coconut extract 
 ⅕ tsp  lemon extract 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients together in a blender.
