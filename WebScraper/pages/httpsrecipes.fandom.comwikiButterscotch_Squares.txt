Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Fisher Estate in Rockwall, Texas in 1980.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  butter 
 2 cups  brown sugar 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 ½ cup  walnuts  chopped 
 2 medium  eggs 
 1½ cups  all purpose flour 
 2 teaspoon  baking powder 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Cook butter and brown sugar together until smooth. 
 Cool to lukewarm. 
 Add eggs and beat well. 
 Add flour, baking powder, vanilla and walnuts then spread in rectangular pan which has been lightly greased. 
 Bake for 30 minutes. 
 Cut in squares.
