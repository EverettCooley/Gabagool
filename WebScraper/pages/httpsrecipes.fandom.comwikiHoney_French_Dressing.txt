 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  salad oil 
 1/2 cup honey 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1/3 cup  chili sauce 
 1/2 cup  red wine vinegar 
 1/2 cup finely chopped white  Onion 
 1 tablespoon  worcestershire sauce 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients and put in tight-fitting covered jar. 
 Shake vigorously.
