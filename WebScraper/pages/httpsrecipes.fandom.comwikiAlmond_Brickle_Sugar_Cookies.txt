almond  Brickle  sugar  Cookies
 Categories: Cookies
 Yield: 48 Servings
 
 2 1/2 c  all-purpose flour 
 1 c  sugar 
 1 c  unsalted butter ; Softened 
 1 lg  egg 
 1 ts  baking soda 
 1 ts  vanilla 
 6 oz  almond  Brickle Bits 
 
Preheat Oven to 350f. in Large Mixer Bowl Combine All Ingredients ExceptAlmond Brickle Bits. Beat at Medium Speed, Scraping Bowl Often, Until WellMixed (2 to 3 Minutes). Stir in  almond  Brickle Bits. Shape RoundedTeaspoonsfuls of Dough Into 1-Inch Balls, Place 2 Inches Apart on GreasedCookie Sheets. Flatten to 1/4-Inch Thickness With Bottom of Glass Dipped InSugar. Bake For 8 to 11 Minutes, or Until Edges Are Very Lightly Browned.
