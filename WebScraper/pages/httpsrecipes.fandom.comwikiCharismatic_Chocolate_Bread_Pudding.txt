 Description : 
 Received from church pot luck in 1984. The lady who gave it to me said that her grandmother used to make it for her as a child, so it is at least 70 years old and another mainstay at our supper club and family gatherings. Yummy.
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 slices of day old bread crumbled 
 4 cups scalded  milk 
 ¼ cup unsalted  butter , melted 
 1 cup granulated  sugar 
 4 slightly beaten  eggs 
 ¾ teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 Sauce:
 
 ¼ cup  dark corn syrup 
 ¼ cup canned evaporated  milk 
 1 cup  chocolate chips 
 Topping:
 
 4  egg  whites 
 ½ teaspoon  cream of tartar 
 8 tablespoons  sugar 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Prepare the pudding by putting bread into a buttered baking dish. 
 Blend  milk ,  butter ,  Sugar ,  eggs ,  salt , and  vanilla  until well combined. 
 Pour over bread. Bake at 350 for 45–60 minutes until pudding is done (solid). 
 Make the sauce by melting  chocolate chips  with  corn syrup  and  evaporated milk . 
 Pour over cooled pudding. 
 Prepare topping by beating  egg  whites,  cream of tartar ,  Sugar , and  vanilla  until stiff peaks form. 
 Pour over warm  chocolate , making peaks. 
 Brown at 350 until golden brown peaks form.
