Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  peanut butter 
 1 teaspoon minced  green onion 
 ½ teaspoon  soy sauce 
 ¼ teaspoon  sesame oil 
 dash  hot red pepper sauce 
 Directions : 
 Combine  peanut butter ,  green onion ,  soy sauce ,  sesame oil  and  hot red pepper sauce .
