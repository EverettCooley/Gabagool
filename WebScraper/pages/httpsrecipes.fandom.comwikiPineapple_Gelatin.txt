 Description : 
This ice-cold gelatin is made with real fruit and cinnamon and is great for breakfast even when paired with other desserts or fruits, as it delivers a colorful aroma that it relishes.       Pineapple Gelatin     
 Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp. unflavored  gelatin 
 ½ cup cold  water 
 ½ tsp.  lemon juice 
 ¼ tsp.  cinnamon 
 ¼ tsp.  orange extract , optional 
 20 oz. can unsweetened, crushed  pineapple 
 Directions : 
 Sprinkle  gelatin  over cold  water  in a small bowl and allow 5 minutes to soften. 
 Dissolve over hot  water . Mix remaining ingredients in blender until smooth. 
 Add  gelatin  mixture and whip until blended. 
 Pour into 3 cups mold and refrigerate until set. 
 Garnish with whipped topping. Serve cold with other desserts, fruits, or side dishes.
