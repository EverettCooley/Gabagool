Description : 
 Makes 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 1 small  onion , chopped 
 2 x 14½-ounce cans Italian-style stewed  tomatoes 
 1 teaspoon dried  oregano 
 1 pound medium  shrimp , peeled and deveined 
 1 x 6-ounce package florentine  rice  mix, prepared according to package directions 
 ½ cup crumbled  feta  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in large skillet over medium-high heat until hot. 
 Add  Onion  and saute 2 to 3 minutes or until tender. 
 Add  tomatoes  and  oregano . Simmer until slightly thickened, stirring occasionally, about 5 minutes. 
 Add  Shrimp ; cook stirring constantly, about 3 minutes or until  Shrimp  are pink. 
 Combine  rice  and  Cheese . 
 To serve spoon  Shrimp  mixture over  rice .
