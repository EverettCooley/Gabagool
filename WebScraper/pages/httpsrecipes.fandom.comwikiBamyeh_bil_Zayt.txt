Description : 
 Okra in oil.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ lbs  okra  fresh,frozen, or canned; trim stem end and wash. 
 32 oz  tomatoes , washed and sliced 
 1 cup finely chopped  onion 
 2 heads  garlic , peeled 
 1 tsp  salt  (optional) 
 ½ cup  vegetable oil  or  olive oil 
 1 cup fresh, finely chopped  coriander  - washed 
 1 tbsp ground dried  coriander 
 4 cloves  garlic , crushed 
 1  fresh red  or  green chile 
 1 cup  water 
 a dash of ground  allspice 
 a dash of  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Fry the  okra  in  oil . 
 Remove and put on paper towels. 
 Fry  onions  in  oil  until transparent. 
 Add the dried coriander and  garlic . Stir for 2 minutes until scented. 
 Add the  tomatoes . Cook over medium heat for another 2 minutes. 
 Add  okra ,  salt ,  sugar ,  allspice ,  chile , and  water . 
 Bring to a boil over medium heat and cook for 25 minutes or until the  okra  is tender. 
 Add fresh coriander and boil for 2 minutes. 
 Remove  chile  and serve cold.
