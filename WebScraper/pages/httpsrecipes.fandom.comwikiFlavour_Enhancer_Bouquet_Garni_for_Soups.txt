 Ingredients : 
 green part of fresh  leeks , as required 
 1 sprig fresh  rosemary 
 1 sprig fresh  thyme 
 3 stalks fresh  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Tie all the herbs together with a string. 
 Then, hang the herbs into the soup or any other dish which is being slow-cooked. 
 Tie the loose end of the string to the handle of the pan. 
 Note: alternatively, you can tie the herbs in a muslin cloth. 
 Enjoy the extra awesome burst of flavour this will provide to your soup!
