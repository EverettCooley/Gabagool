Description : 
 Makes 4 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 1 cup uncooked  rice 
 1 teaspoon  curry powder 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1¾ cups  water 
 1  apple , cored, coarsely chopped 
 ¼ cup  slivered almonds , toasted 
 2 tablespoons chopped  cilantro 
 Directions : 
 Cook  onion  in  butter  in large saucepan over medium heat until tender. 
 Add  rice ,  curry powder  and  salt ; cook 2 minutes. 
 Stir in  water . Bring to a boil; reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer 15 minutes or until  rice  is tender and  water  is absorbed. Let stand 5 minutes. 
 Add  apple ,  almonds  and  cilantro  to  rice  mixture; mix lightly.
