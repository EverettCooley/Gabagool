Description : 
 Cook Time : No Time 
 Serves : unlimited
 
 Ingredients : 
 extra virgin  olive oil , possibly Italian. 
 anchovies  (conserved salted fillets ) 
 capers  (small or big) 
 lemon  or  lime juice 
 Directions : 
 If you are so lucky to own a blender, pour the oil in it. 
 Add more than 3 fillets if you like it salty. 
 Add 5 big or 12 small capers. 
 Squeeze half a lemon or a lime. 
 Remember to push the start button. 
 Serve on: any salad, cooked vegetables, meats or place it in the middle of the table as dip! 
 Give a couple of seconds to alloy and start eating. 
 My way : 
 I do take a bowl and pour the oil in it. 
 Than i start cutting all the things I'm mentioning above, slowly. 
 Of course tiny bits. 
 After several hours, my appetite is at maximum. 
 I'll start eating without giving the time to alloy.
