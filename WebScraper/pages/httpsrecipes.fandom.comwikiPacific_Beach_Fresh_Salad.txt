Ingredients : 
 mixed salad  greens 
 sliced  celery 
 sliced  radishes 
 sliced  jícama 
 carrot , shaved 
 red onion , thinly sliced 
 whole  pecans 
 mandarin orange  wedges 
 Dressing : 
 ½ cup extra virgin  olive oil 
 ¼ cup  rice vinegar 
 ¼ cup  raspberry vinegar 
 3 tbsp  tequila 
 Directions : 
 Start with a large wooden salad bowl filled with mixed greens; we recommend mixing fresh  spinach ,  butter   lettuce , red  leaf lettuce , and hearts of romaine. 
 Add sliced  celery ,  radishes , and  jicama  for crunch. 
 We also added  carrot  shavings for color and thinly sliced  red onion  for pop. 
 Add whole  pecans  and  mandarin orange  wedges to the greens. 
 Now add the  salad dressing , and toss like mad! mix the dressing. 
 Don't add to the salad until just before serving. 
 As a final touch, add some good quality large size croutons and fresh milled  black pepper  and rush to the table! Delicious.
