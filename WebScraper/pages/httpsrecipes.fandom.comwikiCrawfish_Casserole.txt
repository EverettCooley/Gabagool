Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Leslie Bridge, director hospitality services, Dauterive Hospital 
 Serves: 10 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  butter 
 2 pounds  crawfish , or  lobster 
 ⅜ cup  flour 
 1½ teaspoons  dry mustard 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 3 cups fat-free  half and half 
 ¼ cup Butter Buds 
 1 cup  potato flakes 
 1 cup chopped  seasoning 
 chopped  green onions 
 minced fresh  parsley 
 Seasoning for crawfish : 
 garlic powder 
 onion powder 
 seasoning salt 
 cayenne  (very small amount) 
 white pepper 
 Directions : 
 Season crawfish and set aside. 
 Saute chopped seasoning in butter. 
 Stir in dry mustard. 
 Add fat free half and half, stirring until hot. 
 Stir in 1 cup potato flakes. 
 Stir in seasoning salt to taste. 
 Stir crawfish into cream mixture along with green onions. 
 Pour into baking pan and bake for 20 – 25 minutes at 350°F until bubbly and delicately browned. 
 Garnish with minced parsley and serve.
