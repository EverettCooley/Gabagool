Description : 
 A cake with pieces of  Apples  and spiced with  cinnamon .
 
 Serves six 
 Ingredients : 
 2 medium  apples 
 1½ cups  flour 
 3½ tsp  baking powder 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 6 tbsp  granulated sugar , divided 
 ¼ cup  margarine  or  butter 
 1  egg , well beaten 
 ¾ cup  milk 
 ½ tsp ground  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Peel and cut  apples  into eighths (wedges). 
 Sift together  flour ,  baking powder  and  salt  with 4 tablespoons of the  sugar . 
 Cut in  butter / margarine . 
 Combine  egg  and  milk  and add to  flour  mixture. 
 Turn batter into greased 8 inch square cake pan. 
 Press  apple  wedges partly into batter. 
 Combine remaining 2 tbsp  sugar  and  cinnamon , sprinkle over  apple . 
 Bake at 425 °F for 25 to 30 minutes.
