Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Original recipe 
 Yields 7 servings, each one equal to 1 cup 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  ground turkey 
 1 can (15½ ounces) dark  red kidney beans 
 2 cans whole  tomatoes 
 ½ teaspoon minced  garlic 
 1 tablespoon  all-purpose flour 
 2 tablespoons  instant chopped onions 
 1½ teaspoons  chili powder 
 ½ teaspoon  ground red pepper 
 ½ teaspoon ground  cumin 
 Directions : 
 Brown ground turkey in medium skillet over medium high heat. 
 Drain and rinse turkey in colander. 
 Add beans, tomatoes and garlic. 
 In a small bowl, combine the flour with the spices. 
 Add the mixture to the pan. 
 Reduce heat and simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 Calories 190 | Calories from Fat 50 | Total Fat 6 g | Cholesterol 50 mg | Sodium 150 mg | Carbohydrate 18 g | Dietary Fiber 7 g | Protein 17 g 
 Exchanges: 1 starch | 1 lean meat
