Ingredients : 
 2 Tbsp  chutney 
 1/2 tsp grated  orange  peel 
 2 Tbsp fresh  orange juice 
 2 1/2 cups shredded  cabbage 
 1/2 cup shredded  carrot 
 1 cup coarsely fresh  pineapple  or one 8 oz pineapple tidbits 
 1/4 cup  raisins 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl, combine chutney, orange peel, and orange juice. 
 Mix well. Add shredded cabbage and carrot, pineapple, and raisins; toss to mix. Serve immediately or cover and chill until serving time.
