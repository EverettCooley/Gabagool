 Description : 
 This macaroni-based dish is made with real, ground beef and cheese, enriched with herbs, spices, and tomato soup, and baked to monumental or savory, red-hot perfection.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  macaroni , raw 
 ½ lbs. ground  Beef 
 ½ tsp.  garlic powder 
 4 tsp.  chili powder , reserve 3 teaspoons for sprinkling 
 1 pinch  salt 
 1 pinch  pepper , freshly ground 
 1 can  tomato  soup  or  (1 cup tomato sauce, dissolved or mixed with 1 cup water) 
 1 oz.  cheese  (Cheddar, Colby, Swiss, Mozzarella, Parmesan, or Manchego) 
 Directions : 
 Cook  macaroni  about 7 minutes. Brown meat in a large saucepan and drain off all fat. Add  garlic ,  chili powder ,  salt , and  pepper . 
 Mix well and cook on low about 5 minutes. Add cooked  macaroni  and  tomato  soup  or  (tomato sauce mixed with water). Heat thoroughly. Just before serving, add grated  xheese . 
 Cover and heat until  cheese  melts. Serve hot and plain or with other dishes. Sprinkle with the chili powder, if desired.
