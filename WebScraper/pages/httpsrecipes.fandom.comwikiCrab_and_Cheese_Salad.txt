Ingredients : 
 1 pack (6oz) frozen  crabmeat  or 1 can (6oz) crab, drained 
 ½ cup (2oz)  Swiss cheese  shredded 
 ¼ cup chopped  onion 
 ¼ cup finely chopped  celery 
 ¼ cup finely chopped  green bell pepper 
 2  hard-boiled eggs 
 ¼ cup  mayonnaise 
 ¼ cup  French dressing  or  cocktail sauce 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 If using frozen crab, drain about half the liquid off. 
 Combine all ingredients; chill and serve.
