Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 slices day-old  bread , cut ½ inch pieces 
 1 cup hot  water 
 1 cup packed  brown sugar 
 4  eggs , lightly beaten 
 2 cups warm  milk 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 ½ teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 ½ teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 ⅛ teaspoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Place  bread  in a greased 2-qt baking dish. 
 Combine  water  and  brown sugar ;pour over  bread . 
 Combine remaining ingredients; pour over  bread . 
 Bake at 350 °F for 50-60 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean. 
 Serve warm or cold. 
 Enjoy.
