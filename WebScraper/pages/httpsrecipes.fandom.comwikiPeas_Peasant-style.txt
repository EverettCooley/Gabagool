Description : 
 In Romanian: Mazare pregatita taraneste.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs / 1 kg  peas 
 2 tablespoons  oil 
 4 – 5  tomatoes 
 1 tablespoon chopped  parsley  and  dill 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Put the  peas  and peeled, seeded and sliced  tomatoes  in a pan with  oil . 
 salt  and simmer, covered, shaking the pan every now and then. 
 Add 1 – 2 tablespoons of  water  when needed. 
 When the  peas  are done, add the chopped  dill  and  parsley  and simmer a few more minutes. 
 Serve hot or cold.
