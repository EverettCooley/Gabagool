Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Lowery Estate in Arlington, Texas in 1984.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 small package lemon  gelatin 
 ¾ cup boiling water 
 1 cup concord  grape juice 
 1¾ cup frozen  whipped topping  thawed 
 grated  lemon peel 
 Directions : 
 Lightly oil 4 dessert glasses. 
 In a bowl dissolve gelatin in boiling water then stir in grape juice and chill until slightly thickened. 
 Gently fold in whipped topping then pour into glasses. 
 Chill until set then garnish with lemon peel and serve.
