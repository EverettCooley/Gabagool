 Description : 
       Gobi Shorba     
 Ingredients : 
 8 oz/1/4 kg  Cauliflower  flowerets cut into cubes 
 1 quart/liter  milk 
 10 cashews, finely chopped 
 1 tsp.  butter 
 2 cups  water 
 1 tsp.  Sugar 
 salt  to taste 
 Freshly ground  pepper  to taste 
 A pinch of roasted and ground  cumin  as the garnish 
 Directions : 
 Mix  Cauliflower  flowerets and cashews in  water  and  milk  and boil on low flame for 15-20 minutes. Remove from flame, let the mixture cool for sometime and then blend the mixture.
Add  salt ,  Sugar  and  pepper  to the blended mixture and again boil the mixture. Stir in the  butter  near boiling point.
Serve immediately garnished with  coriander leaves  and roasted  cumin .
 Serves: 4-6
 Preparation time: 40-45 minutes
