Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Turner Estate in Dallas, Texas in 1997.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  sugar 
 ¼ stick  butter 
 4  egg yolks 
 1 cup concord  grape jelly 
 2 teaspoons  vanilla 
 1  pie shell 
 4  egg whites 
 Directions : 
 Cream butter and sugar then add well beaten egg yolks, jelly and vanilla. 
 Fold in beaten egg whites then fold into pie shell. 
 Bake at 350°F for 10 minutes then reduce heat to 300°F and cook 30 minutes longer.
