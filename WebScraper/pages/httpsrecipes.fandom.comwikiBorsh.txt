Ingredients : 
 500 g  beef  ( or  chicken ) 
 50 g  butter 
 1 small  cabbage 
 4 medium size  potatoes 
 1 small  beet 
 onion 
 tomatoes 
 carrot 
 bell pepper 
 garlic 
 parsley 
 Directions : 
 Put  Beef  into the pan with  water , add some  bay leaves ,  oregano  and boil until the meat is ready. While waiting prepare all the vegetables: cut  potato ,  cabbage ,  carrot ,  bell pepper ,  tomatoes ,  garlic . Also chop the  onion  and grate the  beet . 
 Then fry the  onion  (use  butter ) on the frying pan with  tomatoes ,  carrots ,  bell pepper  and  beet . 
 When the broth is ready boil  potato  in it. After that add the  cabbage  you chopped before and let it boil for 5 minutes. Then take all the vegetables you fried on the frying pan and put them into the bouillon. Cook borsh for 2 more minutes. 
 At the end add chopped  garlic  and  parsley .  salt  and  pepper  to your liking. You can serve borsh with  sour cream .
