 Wikipedia Article  About Muscadine jelly on Wikipedia 
 Though mostly used fresh,  muscadines  have also been locally used in making home-brewed wine, juice, and jelly. They have also been used for making commercial fine wines and port wines dating back to the 16th Century in and around St. Augustine, Florida.
