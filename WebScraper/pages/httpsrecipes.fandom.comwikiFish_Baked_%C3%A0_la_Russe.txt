      Fish Baked à la Russe     
 Ingredients : 
 300 g fish ( pike ,  cod ,  catfish ,  perch ) 
 300 g  potatoes  boiled. 
 Sour cream sauce : 
 300 g  sour cream 
 20 g  flour 
 15 g  oil 
 60 g  cheese 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Grease a pan with fat, put sliced  potatoes  around and fish slices in the center,  salt . 
 Mix  sour cream  with  oil  and  flour  thoroughly and pour over. 
 Sprinkle with  cheese  and bake in the oven for 30 – 40 minutes.
