 Description : 
 This recipe taken from www.Portuguese-recipes.com 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pkg. of pistachio instant pudding 
 1- 20oz can of crushed  pineapple  and juice 
 1 cup of miniature marshmallows 
 1- 9 oz carton of  Cool Whip 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients and chill. When pudding sets the dish is ready. 
 You can add  cherries ,  nuts  or  coconut  if you desire 
 Good with the meal or as a dessert.
