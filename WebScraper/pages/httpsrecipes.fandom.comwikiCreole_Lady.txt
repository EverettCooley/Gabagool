Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz  bourbon 
 1½ oz  madeira 
 1 tsp  grenadine 
 Directions : 
 Stir with  ice  and strain into cocktail glass. 
 Serve with one green and one  red cherry .
