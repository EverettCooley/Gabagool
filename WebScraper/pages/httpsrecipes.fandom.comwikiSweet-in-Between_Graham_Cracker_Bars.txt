Ingredients : 
 Bars 
 
 32 (2 1/2 x 2 1/2 inch)  graham cracker  squares 
 1 c. graham cracker crumbs 
 1/2 c.  sugar 
 1/2 c. firmly packed  brown sugar 
 1/2 c.  butter 
 1/2 c.  milk 
 1 c. (6 oz.) miniature semi-sweet  chocolate chips 
 Frosting 
 
 1/4 c. butter 
 2 tbsp. brown sugar 
 1 1/2 c.  powdered sugar , sifted 
 2 to 3 tbsp. milk 
 1/2 tsp.  vanilla extract 
 Directions : 
 Grease bottom only of 13x9 inch pan. 
 Arrange 15 graham cracker squares to cover bottom of prepared pan. (Cut to fit.) 
 In medium saucepan, combine graham cracker crumbs, sugar, 1/2 cup brown sugar, 1/2 cup butter and 1/2 cup milk. 
 Bring to boil over medium heat; boil 5 minutes, stirring constantly until mixture thickens. 
 Remove from heat; spread hot mixture evenly over graham crackers in pan. 
 Sprinkle with chocolate chips; press lightly into filling. 
 Arrange remaining graham cracker squares over filling. 
 In small saucepan, melt 1/4 cup butter; remove from heat. 
 Add 2 tablespoons brown sugar; stir until well blended. 
 Add remaining frosting ingredients, adding enough milk to have the desired consistency; blend until smooth. 
 Spread over bars. 
 Refrigerate until firm. 
 Cut into bars. 
 Notes : 
 Keep refrigerated. 
 Makes 48 bars.
