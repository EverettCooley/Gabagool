 Description : 
 carvi  cream  (prepared on the occasion of a birth)
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  Sugar  * 1 cup  rice  powder 
 2 tbls carvi powder 
 1/2 tbls  aniseed  powder 
 1/2 tbls  cinnamon  powder 
 roughly chopped  nuts , pine kernels,  almonds , grated  coconut 
 Directions : 
 Bring 2.5 liters of  water  to a boil, add the  rice  powder diluted in a bit of cold  water . Add the  Sugar  and spices. Stir with a wooden spoon until the mixture thickens. Pour in cups. Serve cold, covered with the  nuts  and  coconut .
