 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 pounds lean ground  Beef 
 2/3 cup soft breadcrumbs 
 1/2 cup finely chopped  Onion 
 1  egg 
 1 tablespoon  worcestershire sauce 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  black pepper , divided 
 1 cup  beef broth 
 2 tablespoons  flour 
 1 teaspoon  paprika 
 1/8 teapoon  garlic powder 
 1 10-3/4-ounce can condensed  cream  of  Mushroom  soup 
 1/3 cup dry  sherry 
 1/2 cup  sour cream 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Combine  Beef , breadcrumbs,  Onion ,  egg ,  worcestershire sauce ,  salt  and 1/8 teaspoon  pepper . Form into 18 meat balls (about 1-1/2Â inches in diameter). Cook meat balls over medium heat in large skillet until brown, about 20 minutes. Discard fat. Blend broth with  flour . Add broth mixture,  paprika ,  garlic powder , soup and  sherry  to skillet. Simmer 10 minutes, or until thickened, stirring occasionally. Stir in  sour cream ; heat through, but do not boil. Serve over beds of fluffy  rice .
