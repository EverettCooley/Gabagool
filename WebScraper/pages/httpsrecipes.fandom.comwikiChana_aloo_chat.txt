India is home to the largest population of vegetarians in the world and has some of the world most decadent vegetarian dishes one could ever taste.  Chana Aloo Chat one of such dishes you can make from the comfort of your home.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup boiled  chickpeas 
 2  potatoes , diced 
 1  onion , chopped 
 1  tomato , chopped 
 1  cucumber , chopped 
 2 chilli padi, sliced 
 20g fresh  coriander 
 50g papdis 
 1 Tbsp green  chutney 
 1 Tbsp sweet chutney 
 1 Tbsp  yogurt 
 50g chat  masala 
 10g  Chili powder 
 10g  Cumin  powder 
 1 Tbsp  Lemon juice 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Put the boiled chickpeas in a mixing bowl.  Chop the potatoes 
 Boil the chopped potatoes, onions, tomatoes, cucumber, fresh coriander, and chopped chillies, and mix them all together adding them to the bowl. 
 Add the papdis, chat masala, chilli powder, cumin powder, green chutney, sweet chutney, yoghurt and salt and blend 
 well.
 
 Add in the blended mixture to the bowl, mix the ingredients and plate the mixture, garnish with fresh papdis and 
 coriander and serve with sliced cucumber on the sides
