Ingredients : 
 3 cup hot cooked  oatmeal 
 ¼ cup  light brown sugar 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 1 medium to large  apple , at room temp-cut into small chunks 
 ⅓ cup  raisins 
 2 tbsp  walnuts , or  pecans , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Stir  brown sugar  and  cinnamon  into cooked  oatmeal . 
 Add  apple  chunks,  raisins , and chopped  nuts . 
 Serve hot!. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving: 3 g total fat | ⅖ g sat fat | 171 cals | 4 g fiber | 7 mg sodium
