Description : 
 In Romanian: Pesmeciori cuiburi
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup  sugar 
 5 oz / 150 g  butter  or  margarine 
 2  eggs 
 ½ teaspoon  baking soda 
 1 teaspoon  lemon juice  (or  vinegar ) 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 flour  as needed 
 1 tablespoon  vanilla  flavored  confectioner's sugar 
 oil 
 Directions : 
 Cream the  eggs  with the  sugar . 
 Add the  vanilla , softened  butter  ( margarine ) and the  baking soda  dissolved in the  lemon juice  or  vinegar . 
 Knead a very stiff dough and then grind it through the meat grinder. 
 Arrange the little sticks that come out of the grinding machine in small piles, nest fashion. 
 Place on the previously oiled baking sheet and bake at medium heat. 
 When still hot, sprinkle with  vanilla  flavored  confectioner's sugar .
