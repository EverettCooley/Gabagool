Ingredients : 
 24 C microwave popcorn 3 bags popped and kernels removed 
 2 sticks of butter 
 16 oz bag of mini marshmallows 
 1 C brown sugar 
 Instructions : 
 Microwave butter, marshmallows, and brown sugar for 2 1/2 min. 
 Take out and stir. 
 Microwave again for 1 minute. 
 Repeat until all is melted. 
 When done pour over popcorn and mix. YUM!
