Description : 
 Purchased from Zimmerman Estate in Terrell, Texas in 1989. This recipe indicates it is a church pot luck favorite. Dated 1936. I have made it and it is very good. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups fresh  green beans , snapped and string removed 
 2 tablespoons  butter 
 Â½ cup  sliced almonds 
 Directions : 
 Cook the  green beans  in boiling salted  water  until tender but still lightly crisp. 
 Drain well. Melt the  butter  in a medium skillet. 
 Add the  almonds  and toast them slightly over low heat. 
 Add the beans to the  butter  and  almonds  and toss to coat.
