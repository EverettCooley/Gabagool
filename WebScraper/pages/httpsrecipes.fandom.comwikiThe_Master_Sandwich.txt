 Description : 
 This sandwich was invented on a rock climbing trip to smith rock, OR, by Laura Matsen, Alice Meganck, and Sean Cooney. It is extremely tasty, as each of the ingredients compliments the others, and the  cucumbers  give it that needed crunch
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large  California avocado 
 1 medium sized fresh  tomato

 Directions : 
 Spread goat  Cheese  onto one or both slices of bread (depends how much you like)
 Slice and layer remaining ingredients ( California avocado ,  tomato ,  cucumber )
 EAT!
