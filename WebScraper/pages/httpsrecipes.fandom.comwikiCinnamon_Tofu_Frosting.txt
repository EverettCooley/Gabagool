 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  firm tofu  (about 2 ounces) 
 2 tbsp soy  margarine 
 2 tsp  vanilla 
 3 tbsp  vanilla   soymilk 
 1½ tsp  cinnamon 
 2 cup  powdered sugar  or more as needed 
 
 
 Directions : 
 In a medium bowl, mix  tofu ,  margarine ,  soymilk  and  vanilla  with electric mixer. 
 Add  cinnamon  and half the  powdered sugar  slowly and mix well. 
 Add remaining  sugar  and mix until good spreading consistency. 
 Add more  powdered sugar  if necessary. 
 Makes about 1 cup frosting. 
 Serving ideas : spread on  cinnamon  rolls with soy and  tofu .
