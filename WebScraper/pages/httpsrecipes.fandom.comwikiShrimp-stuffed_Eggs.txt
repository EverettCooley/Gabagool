 Description : 
 A nice looking appetizer that consists of hardboiled  eggs , stuffed with shrimp.
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 hard-boiled  eggs 
 4 ounces cleaned cooked small  shrimp 
 1 tablespoon  mayonnaise  or  sour cream 
 1 teaspoon tomato  ketchup 
 Â½ tablespoon  brandy  or  whiskey  or dry  sherry 
 salt  and  pepper 
 1 tablespoon chopped  chives 
 Directions : 
 Chop the  shrimp , except for 12, which will be used for garnish. 
 Half the  eggs  remove the  yolks ; mix them with the  shrimp  and the other ingredients. Check the seasoning. 
 Fill the  eggs  with the mix and garnish each one with a  shrimp . 
 Serve on a  lettuce  lined platter/plate.
