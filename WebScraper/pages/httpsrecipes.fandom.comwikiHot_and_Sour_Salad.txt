 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  snow peas , trimmed and steamed until tender 
 2 cups  pea shoots 
 1 cup  bean sprouts 
 1 cup  carrots , julienned 
 1 cup  baby corn , steamed until tender and halved lengthwise 
 1 tablespoon  umeboshi  paste 
 pinch  sea salt 
 ¼ – ½ teaspoon dried  chili flakes 
 3 tablespoons  sesame seeds , toasted 
 1 teaspoon  soy sauce  ( shoyu ) 
 2 teaspoon  sesame oil 
 3 tablespoons  peanut oil  ( or   olive oil ) 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl, combine the snow  peas , pea shoots,  bean sprouts ,  carrots  and  corn . 
 Mix the remaining ingredients in a blender until smooth. 
 Thoroughly mix the dressing through the vegetables and serve immediately.
