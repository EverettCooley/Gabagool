 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pint basket California  strawberries , stemmed and halved 
 3 tablespoons  Sugar 
 1 tablespoon  heavy cream 
 1 tablespoon  Grand Marnier 
 1 tablespoon  brandy 
 1/2 teaspoon cracked  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 In bowl combine ingredients, toss gently. Cover and refrigerate 1 hour. Serve over  vanilla   ice cream  in stemmed glasses. Yield: 4 servings
