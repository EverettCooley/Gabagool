Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 slices  Bacon , diced 
 1 cup chopped  Onion 
 1 cup chopped  celery 
 1 cup chopped green  bell pepper 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1 pound lean ground  Beef 
 1 10-3/4-ounce can condensed  cream  of  Mushroom  soup 
 1 4-ounce can sliced  Mushrooms  (drain; reserve liquid) 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 2  Beef  bouillon cubes 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Bacon  in 10-inch skillet over medium heat 2 minutes. 
 Add  Onion ,  celery ,  green pepper ,  garlic  and  Beef ; cook until meat and vegetables are tender but not brown. 
 Stir frequently to crumble meat. Add soup,  Mushrooms ,  rice ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Stir in bouillon cubes dissolved in  Mushroom  liquid. 
 Simmer gently for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
