 Description : 
 8 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 	mangoes, cubes 
 1/2 	 pineapple , fresh,cubed 
 1/2 	cup  lemon juice 
 1 	cup  apricot  nectar or  orange juice 
 lettuce 
 strawberries , for garnish 
 Directions : 
 In a 2-quart bowl: Add the  pineapple  to the  mango . 
 Blend the juices separately and then pour over the  mango . 
 Serve on  lettuce  cups either individually or in a bowl. 
 Garnish with fresh  strawberries .
