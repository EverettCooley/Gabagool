Description : 
 Alexander  from the Wikibooks Bartending Guide—original source of recipe, licensed under the GNU Free Documentation License
 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ shot  gin   or   brandy 
 ½ shot  heavy cream  (or  half-and-half ) 
 ½ shot white  crème de cacao 
 ice 
 Garnish : 
 Nutmeg 
 Directions : 
 Combine ingredients over ice. 
 Shake and strain into glass. 
 Sprinkle nutmeg on top. 
 Warning : 
 Do not attempt to combine the Gin and Brandy Alexander into a single drink.
