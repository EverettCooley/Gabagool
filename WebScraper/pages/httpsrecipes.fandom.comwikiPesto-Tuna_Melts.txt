      Pesto-Tuna Melts     
 Contents 
 
 1   Ingredients 
 2   Directions 
 3   Other Links 
 4   See also 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 slices  Italian bread , 1 inch thick 
 1/2 cup pesto 
 2 medium  tomatoes , thinly sliced 
 2 cans (6 ounces each)  Tuna , drained 
 4 slices  mozzarella   Cheese  (about 6 ounces), cut diagonally in half 
 Freshly ground  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Set oven control to broil. 
Place bread on ungreased cookie sheet.  
Broil with tops about 4 inches from heat until golden brown; turn and broil until golden brown. 
Spread bread slices with pesto.  
Top with  tomato ,  Tuna  and  Cheese  slices.  
Broil about 1 minute or just until  Cheese  begins to melt.  
Sprinkle with  pepper .
 
 Makes 4 sandwiches
