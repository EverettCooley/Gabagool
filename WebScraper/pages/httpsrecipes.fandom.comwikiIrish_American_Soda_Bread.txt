      Irish American Soda Bread     
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups  unbleached flour  (all-purpose) 
 1 tablespoon  baking powder 
 1/4 teaspoon  baking soda 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1/2 cup Splenda  sugar substitute 
 1 cup golden  raisins  or black  raisins 
 1/4 cup  egg  Beaters  egg substitute 
 1 3/4 cups  buttermilk  or soured  milk 
 4 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine , melted 
 Directions : 
 Whisk together  flour , baking powder , baking soda , splenda and  raisins . 
 In separate bowl whisk together  egg  beater, buttermilk  and melted  butter . 
 Quickly stir wet ingredients into dry ingred. 
 Pour batter into greased 9x5" loaf pan. 
 Bake bread in preheated 375 °F oven 45 minutes to an hour.
