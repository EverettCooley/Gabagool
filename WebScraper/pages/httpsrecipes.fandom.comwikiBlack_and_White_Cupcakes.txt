 Description : 
 Arrange these imaginative  chocolate  and  vanilla  frosted treats on a pretty platter or tiered cake stand for a fun dessert-they’re perfect for kids and adults! To make 1 dramatic "black eyed Susan" cupcake, stir yellow  food coloring  into about ¼ cup  vanilla  frosting until it’s tinted bright yellow; use a star tip to pope on petals, then finish with a  chocolate  center. EP: 1hour 30 minutes plus cooling. BAKE: about 25 minutes 1hour 30 minutes plus cooling. BAKE: about 25 minutes. Makes 24 cupcakes.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 decorating bags 
 2 couplers 
 2 writing tips (each ¼-inch opening) 
 2 star tips (each ¼-inch opening) 
 2 small basketweave tips (each 1/8-inch opening) 
 1 recipe Basic  butter  Cake (Refer below) 
 1 recipe  vanilla  Butter- cream  Frosting(Refer below) 
 2 ounces  unsweetened chocolate , melted and cooled 
 1 ounce  semisweet chocolate , melted and cooled 
 Directions : 
 Prepare Basic  butter  Cake or  chocolate  Cake as recipe directs for cupcakes. Bake and cool as directed. 
 Prepare  vanilla  Butter- cream  Frosting as recipe directs. 
 Remove 1 1/2 cups frosting and set aside. 
 To frosting remaining in bowl, beat in melted unsweetened and semisweet chocolates until blended. (You’ll have slightly more  chocolate  frosting.) 
 Spoon  vanilla  frosting into decorating bag fitted with a coupler. 
 Spoon  chocolate  frosting into another decorating bag fitted with another coupler. 
 Using  vanilla  and  chocolate  frostings with suggested decorating tips, and small spatula, decorate tops of cupcakes as desired with black and white designs.
