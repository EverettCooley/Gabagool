Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 large pkgs  butterscotch chips 
 1 pkg Spanish  peanuts 
 4 cups  chow mein noodles 
 Directions : 
 Melt butterscotch chips in double boiler. 
 Stir in peanuts and chow mein noodles. 
 Drop by spoonfuls onto wax paper. 
 Let set until cool.
