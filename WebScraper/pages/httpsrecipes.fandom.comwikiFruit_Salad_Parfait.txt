Descripton : 
 Cook Time : About 15 minutes 
 Serves : 2 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup low-fat vanilla  yogurt 
 ½ cup sliced  peaches ,  apples ,  pears , or other fruits 
 ½ cup  strawberries ,  blueberries , or other berries 
 granola  or  graham crackers  for topping, optional 
 Directions : 
 Spoon ¼ cup yogurt into each glass. 
 Add ½ cup fruit. Top with ¼ cup yogurt. 
 Top with granola or crushed graham crackers, if desired.
