Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound bag of fresh  spinach 
 1 tbsp  bacon grease  or  oil 
 1 tbsp finely chopped  onion 
 1 small clove  garlic  finely chopped 
 1 cup  heavy cream  - ½ and ½ or 18% cream 
 ½ tsp  salt  or to taste 
 ½ tsp  sugar 
 1 tsp  nutmeg 
 1 tbsp  flour 
 Directions : 
 Boil down the spinach to make 2 cups. 
 Drain in colander leave it sitting. 
 In a pan, sauté the onion in garlic in the bacon grease fora few minutes. 
 Then put  spinach  back in pan amd add the remaining ingredients mix all together and stir for about 1 hour with wire whisk. 
 It takes a while to make this dish, but it is soooooooo good!!.
