Makes 4 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound boneless  Pork 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 1 4- to 6-ounce package chicken-flavored  rice  mix, prepared to package directions 
 1 6-ounce package frozen snow  Peas 
 1 8-ounce can  pineapple  tidbits, drained 
 1/4 cup  slivered almonds , toasted 
 1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon  red pepper flakes 
 2 tablespoons soy sauce 
 Directions : 
 Slice  Pork  into 1/8-inch strips. 
 Heat  oil  in large skillet or wok over medium-high heat until hot. 
 Add  Pork  and stir fry until lightly browned. 
 Stir in  rice , snow  Peas ,  pineapple ,  almonds ,  pepper  and soy sauce. Cook 1 to 2 minutes or until thoroughly heated
