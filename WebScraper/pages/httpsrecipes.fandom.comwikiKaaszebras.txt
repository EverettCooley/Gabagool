Description : 
 "Cheese zebras". The Dutch version of the finger sandwich. Great for tea parties.
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 tablespoons  butter 
 4  egg yolks , boiled 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 1 dash  pepper 
 3 drops  Worcestershire sauce 
 2 tablespoons grated  parmesan cheese  or  gouda  cheese 
 4 thin slices dark  pumpernickel bread 
 2 teaspoons  Dijon mustard 
 Directions : 
 Beat  butter  til soft. 
 Add  egg  yolks,  salt ,  pepper , Worcestershire,  cheese , and  mustard  mix til smooth. 
 Spread thick on slice of bread as thick as the bread. 
 Top with bread and add another layer of spread, add bread on top. 
 Wrap in aluminum foil and chill at least 2½ hours. 
 Cut into ladyfinger sized strips. 
 Serve.
