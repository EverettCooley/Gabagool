Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 ounce package  cream cheese , softened 
 1 cup plain  yogurt 
 2 tablespoons  milk 
 10 ounce package frozen chopped  spinach , thawed and drained 
 2 hard boiled  eggs , finely chopped 
 1 clove  garlic , finely chopped 
 1 dash  paprika 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Combine  cream cheese ,  yogurt  and  milk  mixing until well blended. 
 Stir in  spinach ,  eggs ,  garlic ,  paprika  and season to taste with  salt  and pepper.
