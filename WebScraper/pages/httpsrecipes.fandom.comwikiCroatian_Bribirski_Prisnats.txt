4 servings, 60 minutes preparation
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 Slice  Ham 
 1/2 lb  Bacon ; cubed 
 1 1/2 lb  jack cheese ; cubed or Cottage Cheese, fresh Ricotta Cheese (16oz package) 
 2 bn  green onions  -- cut into small pieces 
 12  eggs 
 1 Cake of  yeast 
 1/2 c  butter  (skip the butter - the Bacon has grease) 
 1/4 c Warm  water 
 salt  (1 Tblspoon) 
 pepper  (just a little bit) 
 flour  (3 Cups) 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve  yeast  in warm  water  with 1 tablespoon  flour ; stir and set aside to rise. 
 Fry  Bacon  in for 2 minutes - let cool. 
 Add  Ham  and the  green onions ; set aside to cool. 
 Beat  eggs  and add enough  flour  to make a soft batter. 
 Add the raised  yeast  and mix. 
 Add  Ham ,  Bacon  and  onions  and mix. 
 Add  cheese ,  salt  and  pepper  to taste. 
 Put into a large bowl to rise for about 2 or 3 hours. 
 Bake at 375 F for about 1 hour or until top is nice and brown.
