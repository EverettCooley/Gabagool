BHUMI BATTAKH ( Duck ) MUGHLAI
 1  Duck  cut in joints
5-6 red chilies
2 teaspoons  pepper 
6 pods  garlic 
 salt  to taste
a few  poppy seeds .
Cook the meat with ground  masala  and  salt  and keep aside. When it is dry, 
fry the pieces
and arrange in a flat dish. Garnish it with fresh  mint  leaves,  tomato  slices 
and chipped
 potatoes .
