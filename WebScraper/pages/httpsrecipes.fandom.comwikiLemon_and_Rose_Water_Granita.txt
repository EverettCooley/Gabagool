 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  honey 
 ½ cup fresh  lemon juice 
 1 tsp.  rose water  (found in Middle Eastern or gourmet markets) 
 rose petals , washed, for garnish 
 Directions : 
 In a small saucepan, combine honey and 1½ cups  water . Heat until honey is completely dissolved, 2 to 3 minutes (do not boil). Remove from heat and cool completely. Add  lemon juice  and  rose water . 
 Place in an 8" x 8" glass or stainless steel baking dish and freeze until completely frozen, about 3 hours. With the tines of a fork, scrape the granita, creating  ice  shavings. Return to freezer for another 2 to 4 hours. Scrape again. 
 Either serve and sprinkle with  rose petals , or return to freezer in a covered container. Scrape again when ready to serve.
