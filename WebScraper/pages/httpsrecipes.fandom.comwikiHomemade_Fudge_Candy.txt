HOMEMADE FUDGE CANDY
 
 3 c. sugar
1/4 tsp. salt
3 tbsp. Hershey's cocoa
1 1/2 c. milk
 On high heat - bring to boil. Drop small amount of fudge in cold water. When it forms a hard ball, add: 1 tsp. vanilla
