 Description : 
 In Romanian: Unt cu raci
 
 Ingredients : 
 7 oz/200 g  butter , 
 25  Crayfish , 
 1 teaspoon  tomato  paste, 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Boil the  Crayfish  in  salt   water . 
 When they are boiled and cooled, get the meaty parts, grind or chop them fine and then mix with  butter ,  salt  and a teaspoon  tomato  paste. 
 Mix until creamy. Refrigerate until serving.
