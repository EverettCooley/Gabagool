Description : 
 Yields 8 dozen cookies
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups  butter 
 2 cups  brown sugar 
 2  eggs 
 4 cups  flour 
 2 tsp  baking soda 
 2 tsp  vanilla 
 2 tsp  salt 
 ½ cup shredded  coconut 
 1 cup chopped  pecans 
 1 cup chopped  maraschino cherries 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl, cream the butter; add the sugar gradually and mix well. 
 Add the eggs and vanilla and mix well again. 
 Sift together the flour, baking soda and salt. 
 Add this to the sugar/eggs and blend well. 
 Add the pecans, coconut and cherries. 
 Mix until blended well. 
 Drop teaspoonfuls on a greased cookie sheet. 
 Bake at 375°F for about 10 minutes.
