Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ can pink lemonade 
 6 - 10 oz Malibu ®   coconut rum 
 1½ oz  whipped cream 
 Directions : 
 Crush a handful or two of  ice  into a blender, add the frozen pink lemonade and Malibu ®   rum , and blend for 30 seconds or until smooth-like consistency. 
 Add in your desired amount of  whipped cream  and blend further until smooth. 
 Serve.
