 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/3 cup  unbleached flour 
 1/3 cup  nutritional yeast  flakes 
 1/3 cup light, natural  oil 
 1 1/2 cups unflavoured  soy milk 
 sea salt  and freshly ground  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 In a small saucepan, stir together the  flour ,  yeast  and  oil  until it forms a paste (roux), and let it sizzle for a few minutes. Gradually whisk in the  soy milk  and cook over low heat until thickened. Add  sea salt  and  pepper  to taste. Serve over noodles,  potatoes , or whatever you like.
