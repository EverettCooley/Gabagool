Description : 
 Serve with toasted pita bread triangles, whole-wheat crackers, homemade tortilla chips, or cut up fresh veggies. Or try it on baked  potato  in place of  sour cream .
 
 Makes 1 cup 
 Ingredients : 
 8 oz plain, low-fat  yogurt 
 ¼ tsp  garlic powder 
 ⅛ tsp  dill weed 
 ¼ tsp  oregano 
 ¼ tsp  basil 
 ½ tsp  chives 
 ½ tsp dried  parsley 
 ½ tsp  marjoram 
 dash of  salt 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in a medium-sized mixing bowl. 
 Chill for a couple of hours before serving. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per ¼ cup: 
 
 32 cals | 1 mg chol | 0 fat | 123 mg sodium | 0 dietary fiber
