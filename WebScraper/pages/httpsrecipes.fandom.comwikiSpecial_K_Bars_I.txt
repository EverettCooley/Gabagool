Description : 
 Contributed by Paula in California at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  light karo syrup 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 ¾ cup  peanut butter 
 3 cups  Special K cereal 
 Topping : 
 1 cup  butterscotch chips 
 1 cup  chocolate chips 
 Directions : 
 Mix  karo  syrup,  sugar , and  peanut butter  in a large saucepan. 
 Heat until boiling. 
 Mix in Special K. 
 Press mixture into buttered 9x9-in. baking dish. 
 Cool and cut into squares. 
 Topping : 
 Melt chips together and spread over bars. 
 If too thick to spread, add a little  milk  until desired consistency.
