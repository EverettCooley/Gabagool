Ingredients : 
 1  onion , grated 
 3 tablespoons  oil  or  margarine 
 2  carrots , finely chopped 
 3  celery  stalks, finely chopped 
 1 tablespoon  flour 
 1 tablespoon  tomato sauce 
 5 cups meat  stock 
 1 pound  cabbage , finely chopped 
 2 tablespoons  vinegar 
 1 pound  potatoes , diced 
 2  beet  roots, diced 
 salt 
 pepper 
 4 tablespoons  cream 
 Directions : 
 Saut√©  onion  lightly in  oil . 
 Add  carrots  and  celery  and continue to saute. 
 Add  flour  and continue to saute. 
 Add  tomato  sauce and a few tablespoons stock slowly, stirring continuously. 
 Cook over low heat until the sauce is ready. 
 Boil the remaining stock and add  cabbage . 
 Add  vinegar  and cook for 15 minutes, then add  potatoes  and  beet  roots and cook for another 5 minutes. 
 Season with  salt  and  pepper  and serve hot, covered with a tablespoon of  cream  for each dish.
