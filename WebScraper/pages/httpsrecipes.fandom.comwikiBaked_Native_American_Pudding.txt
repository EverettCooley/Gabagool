      Baked Native American Pudding     
 Ingredients : 
 1/4 cup  Sugar 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 1/2 tsp  ground ginger root 
 1/4 tsp  salt 
 1/4 tsp  nutmeg 
 4 cup  milk 
 1/2 cup yellow  cornmeal 
 1/2 cup  raisins 
 1/2 cup  pancake syrup 
 2 tbl  margarine , softened 
 2 x  eggs , beaten 
 Directions : 
 Pre-heat Dutch oven. 
 Mix together  Sugar ,  cinnamon ,  ginger ,  salt , and  nutmeg . 
 Blend well and set aside. 
 Heat  milk  in medium pot. 
 Stir in  cornmeal . 
 Cook stirring constantlly for 20min. 
 Stir in  Sugar  mixture and remaining ingredients. 
 Pour into large greased pot and bake in Dutch oven 1 hour 
 Serve warm.
