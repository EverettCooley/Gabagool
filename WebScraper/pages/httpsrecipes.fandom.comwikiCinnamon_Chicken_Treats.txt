 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup of  flour 
 1 cup OF  chicken broth 
 3/4 cup of  oatmeal 
 1/4  corn starch 
 1/4 cup of  oil 
 1 table spoon of  baking powder 
 2 table spoons of  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Mix the ingredients together and cook for 20 minutes at 350 degrees
