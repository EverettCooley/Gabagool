Ingredients : 
 2½ cups  flour 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 1½ cup  sour cream 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 1 – 2 cups  powdered sugar 
 Directions : 
 Mix carefully the ingredients except  powdered sugar . 
 Prepare a large skillet with  oil  heated to hot, 2 inches deep, for deep frying. 
 Drop with large spoon small dollops. 
 Cook each side after about 3 – 4 minutes on each side. 
 Drain on paper towel but roll in  confectioners' sugar  before completely cool.
