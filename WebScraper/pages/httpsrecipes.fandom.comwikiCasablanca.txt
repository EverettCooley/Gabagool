      Description     
 Ingredients : 
 3 parts  white rum 
 1 part  cherry  liqueur 
 1 part  triple sec 
 1 part  lime juice 
 1  lime  slice 
 Directions : 
 Shake all the ingredients and serve the cocktail with  ice .
