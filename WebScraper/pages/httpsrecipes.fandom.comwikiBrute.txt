Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  Amaretto   almond liqueur 
 1 oz dark  crème de cacao 
 3 tbsp vanilla ice cream 
 Directions : 
 Blend briefly with half a glassful of crushed  ice  in a collins glass. 
 Garnish with a  cherry  and serve with straws.
