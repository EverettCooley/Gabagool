Description : 
 Source:  Butter Buster - the Cookbook  by Pam Mycoskie
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 oz  egg beaters  99%  egg 
 Directions : 
 In an 8" skillet, pour  egg beaters , or substitute. 
 Cover tightly; cook over low heat 10 min or until just set. 
 Remove from heat; let stand, covered for 10 min. 
 Remove from skillet and cool completely. 
 Chop and use in your favorite  egg  salad recipe or sprinkle over salads or casseroles.
