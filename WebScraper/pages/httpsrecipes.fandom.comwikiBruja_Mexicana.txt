 Ingredients : 
 1/2 part  Strega ®  herbal liqueur 
 1/2 part Agavero®  tequila  liqueur 
 fill with  soda water 
 sugar syrup 
 crushed  ice 
 Sugar 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix  Strega  and  tequila  in a blender the crushed  ice . 
 Moist the rim of the glass with  lemon  and decorate with  Sugar . 
 Add a little  sugar syrup . Pour in and fill with soda.
