Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ lbs  ground beef  or  pork 
 1 clove  garlic , crushed 
 1 medium  onion , minced 
 5 dried  mushroom , soaked and sliced 
 1 x 10 oz pkg. chop suey vegetables 
 1 pkg.  lumpia wrappers 
 2 stalks  green onion , chopped 
 1½ tsp  salt 
 ⅛ tsp  shoyu  (soy sauce) 
 1  egg , beaten 
 oil  for deep frying 
 Directions : 
 Brown ground beef, stir in garlic, onion and mushroom; saute slightly. 
 Fold in chop suey vegetables and green onions. 
 Add seasonings and cook for about 2 minutes. 
 Drain and cool. 
 To make lumpia: place 2 tbsp filling on a lumpia wrapper. 
 Fold neatly like an envelope. 
 Moisten with beaten egg to seal corners and top. 
 Deep fry lumpia until golden brown. 
 Serve with dipping sauce.
