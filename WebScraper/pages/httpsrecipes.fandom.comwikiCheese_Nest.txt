 Prep Time: 10
 Cook time: 15
 Serves: 4
 
 Description :

 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup green  peas 
 8 cloves  garlic 
 ½ inch  ginger 
 1 tsp  mustard seeds 
 1 tsp  cumin  seeds 
 1 tsp  corn flour 
 2  potatoes  boiled and mashed 
 4  green chilis 
 vermicelli 
 cheese 
 salt  to taste 
 [[oil] to free 
 Directions : 
 Cook green peas in a pan until they are soft. 
 Grind green chilis, garlic, ginger, cumin seeds and add salt 
 In another pan heat 2 tsp oil, put 1 tsp mustard seeds, add the ground paste, add green peas, add mashed potatoes, and mix well. and keep aside. 
 In a small bowl take ¾ water and mix 2 tsp of corn flour 
 In a dish take vermicelli and crush it to make small strips. 
 Now make small bowls of the vegetable preparation which we have made. 
 Roll the balls in the corn flour water lightly. 
 Roll the balls in vermicille so that the ball is covered full with vermicelli. 
 Heat oil in a pan to deep fry the balls. 
 When all the balls are fried, cut the balls in two pieces, so that they will look like nest. 
 Now with small piece of cheese prepare egg like shape and put the cheese eggs on top of the next. 
 Serve with green chutney and sauce.
