 Description : 
 Marzipan in English is  almond  paste; traditionally made with  almonds ,  sugar  and  egg whites , it has a very strong  almond  flavor; it is now sold ready-made; used in several pastries and desserts.
 
 Ingredients : 
 150 g  almonds 
 1  egg white 
 150 g  powdered sugar 
 Directions : 
 Dry the  almonds  and then crush them as fine as possible. 
 Combine the  almonds  with the  egg  white and mix into a uniform paste along with gradually adding the  sugar . 
 Mix it until it attains a consistent elasticity. 
 Form the dough into decorations for cakes or sweets.
