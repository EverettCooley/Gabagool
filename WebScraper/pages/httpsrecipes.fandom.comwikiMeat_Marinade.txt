Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  Cookie's Bar-B-Q Sauce 
 2 cups  water 
 ½ cup  lemon juice 
 ½ cup dark  vinegar 
 1 cup  corn oil 
 3 tbsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 2 tbsp  yellow mustard 
 1 tsp  pepper 
 1 tsp  garlic salt 
 ½ tsp Durkee's Smoke  salt 
 1 large  onion , cut into wedges 
 Directions : 
 Combine everything. 
 Submerge meat for 24 hours. 
 Cover and refrigerate. 
 Drain and broil meat; basting with Cookie's Bar-B-Q Sauce.
