 Ingredients : 
 1 	  egg , separated 
 1/2 	cup  milk 
 1/4 	teaspoon  salt 
 1 	tablespoon  butter , melted 
 1/2 	cup  flour , sifted 
 1 	pint raw Maine clams, shucked 
 oil  (for frying) 
 Directions : 
 1. Beat  egg white  until stiff.
 2. Add  milk ,  salt , and melted  butter  to  egg yolk  and beat together.
 3. Add  flour  and stir.
 4. Fold into stiffly beaten  egg white .
 5. Drain clams and dip each into batter and fry in deep fat at 375F until golden.
 6. Drain on paper towels.
