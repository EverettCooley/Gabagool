Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Southern Living 
 Makes 10 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 2 medium  onions , chopped 
 1 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 1 lb  ground beef 
 1 lb  ground pork 
 6 tsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 1 can (28 oz) crushed  tomatoes 
 ¼ cup  prepared mustard 
 ¼ cup  white vinegar 
 ¾ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  pepper 
 hot cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Saute onions in hot oil in a dutch oven over medium heat until tender. 
 Add ground beef and pork and cook, stirring until meat crumbles and is no longer pink; drain. 
 Stir in worcestershire, and next 3 ingredients, and bring to a boil; cook over low heat, stirring often, 45 minutes. 
 Add salt and pepper. 
 Serve with hot cooked rice.
