 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 9 oz  chocolate  wafer cookies 
 4 oz  milk chocolate  candy melts 
 Directions : 
 1 Use a serrated knife to carefully cut 18 of the cookies into quarters. 
2 Save remaining cookies for another use. 
3 For each bat, place 2 cookie quarters ¼" apart on waxed paper. 
4 Repeat with remaining quarters. 
5 Melt candy melts. 
6 Drop about ½ teaspoon of melted candy at center of each bat, connecting cookies. 
7 Use a toothpick to smooth melted candy into a uniform circle. 
8 Cool completely before removing from waxed paper.
