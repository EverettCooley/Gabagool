 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 Green  plantain 
 Fresh  lime 
 Boiling salted  water 
 Directions : 
 Peel the  plantain  and cut off and discard both ends. 
 Cut into sections, rub with the  lime  and cook in the boiling salted  water  for about 30 minutes, until tender.
