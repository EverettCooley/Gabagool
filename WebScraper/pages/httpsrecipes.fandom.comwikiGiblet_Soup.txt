 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 qts/2 l  water 
 1 qt/1 l  sauerkraut  juice 
 giblets and wings from one hen/ Turkey / Goose  or mixed 
 1  carrot 
 1  parsley root 
 1 big  Onion 
 1/2  celery root 
 chopped  parsley  and dill 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Boil the giblets and wings. 
 Remove foam and add  salt . 
 Add the finely chopped vegetables. 
 When the meat is tender, add the  sauerkraut  juice that was boiled separately, the  parsley  and dill and let boil for a few more minutes.
