 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 sweet potatoes , cooked, peeled, sliced 
 cucumbers , cut into quarters, seeded, salted and allowed to drain 
 nasturtium  blossoms 
 Mayonnaise 
 quartered hard-boiled  eggs 
 tomatoes , peeled, seeded and sliced 
 Directions : 
 Arrange all ingredients artistically on a serving platter. 
 Just before serving, pour over them a few tablespoonfuls of Vinaigrette sauce.
