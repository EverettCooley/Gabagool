Desciption : 
 This Recipe Came From an Estate Sale from The Steep Estate in Elmo, Texas in 1989.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound lean ground  sausage 
 1 package Taco Mix 
 2 cans  cheddar   cheese  soup 
 1/2 Cup  milk 
 Directions : 
 Cook  sausage  and drain thoroughly. 
 Stir in taco mix. 
 Add soup and milk 
 Cook over medium for 10 minutes. 
 Serve with chips.
