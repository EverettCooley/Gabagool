Description : 
 This is a recipe I obtained from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Crawford Estate in Irving, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  shortening 
 1 cup  butter 
 3 cups  granulated sugar 
 5  eggs 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  baking powder 
 5 tablespoons  cocoa 
 3 cups  flour 
 1 cup  milk 
 1 cup  black walnuts 
 Glaze : 
 2 cups  powdered sugar 
 3 teaspoons hot  coffee 
 1 teaspoon  almond extract 
 Directions : 
 Cream shortening, butter, sugar, eggs and vanilla. 
 Sift together salt, baking powder, cocoa and flour. 
 Add alternately with milk then add nuts and bake at 325°F for 1½ hours. 
 Cover after 10 minutes and cool completely before serving. 
 Immediately before serving drizzle glaze over top. 
 For glaze combine all ingredients and whisk until smooth.
