Ingredients : 
 3 cups  cauliflower , broken into florettes 
 6 cups artificially flavored Pareve  chicken stock  (hot) 
 3 tablespoons  butter 
 3 tablespoons  flour 
 ½ cup  cream  (or 1 cup  half and half ) 
 3  egg yolks  (can use 1 less) 
 ½ cup chopped  onions 
 Directions : 
 Cook  onions  in  butter  until soft. 
 Stir in  flour  and cook 2 minutes. 
 Stir in hot broth and bring to a boil, stirring. 
 Add  cauliflower  and simmer 30 minutes. 
 Stir together  egg yolks  and  cream  and pour into hot soup. 
 Bring almost to a boil. 
 Serve.
