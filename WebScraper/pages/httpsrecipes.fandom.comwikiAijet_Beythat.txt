Description : 
 Egg kebab
 
 Ingredients : 
 8  hard-cooked eggs , shelled 
 ¼ cup  butter 
 ⅓ teaspoon  salt 
 ¾ teaspoon  paprika 
 ¾ teaspoon  white pepper 
 ¾ teaspoon  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Prick whites of  eggs  all over so they won't split as they are heated through. 
 Sauté  eggs  in  butter  over low heat, turning, until light brown. 
 Mix seasonings. 
 Sprinkle over  eggs .
