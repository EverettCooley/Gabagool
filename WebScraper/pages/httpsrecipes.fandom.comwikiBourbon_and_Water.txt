Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz  Bourbon 
 4 oz  water 
 Directions : 
 Pour  bourbon  and  water  into old-fashioned glass. 
 Add  ice  and a twist of  lemon peel , if desired, and stir.
