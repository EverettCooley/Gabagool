Ingredients : 
 6 T.  butter 
 1/2 c. fresh  cranberries 
 2 T.  sugar 
 2 T.  honey 
 1/2 t.  vanilla extract 
 1/2 t.  salt 
 12 cups popcorn (popped) 
 1 c. chopped toasted  almonds 
 Instructions : 
 Melt butter in a saucepan with cranberries, sugar, honey, vanilla extract and salt 
 Cook until the cranberries pop, about 3 minutes. 
 Toss with popcorn and toasted almonds. 
 Spread on a baking sheet and bake at 325 degrees F, stirring occasionally, until dry, about 8 minutes.
