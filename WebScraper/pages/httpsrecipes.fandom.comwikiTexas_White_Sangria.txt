Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Southern Living 
 Makes 7½ cups 
 Ingredients : 
 1⅓ cups  water 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 4  cinnamon  sticks (3 inches each) 
 1 cup fresh  mint  leaves, divided 
 1 bottle dry  white wine  (750 ml) 
 2  lemons , sliced 
 2  oranges , sliced 
 2  peaches , peeled and sliced 
 2 cups  club soda , chilled 
 Directions : 
 Bring first 3 ingredients and ½ cup mint leaves to a boil in a pan over medium heat. 
 Reduce heat, and simmer 5 minutes. 
 Remove from heat and cool. 
 Cover and let stand 8 hours, if desired. 
 Remove cinnamon sticks and mint leaves with a slotted spoon. 
 Combine sugar mixture, remaining ½ cup mint leaves, wine and next 3 ingredients in a large pitcher; chill overnight, if desired. 
 Stir in club soda just before serving. 
 Serve over ice.
