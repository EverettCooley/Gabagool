Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Williams Estate in Tyler, Texas in 1994.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4 medium  potatoes 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 3 tablespoons melted  butter 
 3 tablespoons chopped fresh  chives 
 4 tablespoons grated sharp  cheddar  cheese 
 1Â½ tablespoons  Parmesan cheese 
 Directions : 
 Scrub and rinse  potatoes  then cut into thin slices but not all the way through. 
 Put  potatoes  in baking dish and fan slightly. 
 Sprinkle with  salt  and sprinkle melted  butter  over them then sprinkle chives over top and bake at 425 for 50 minutes. 
 Remove from oven and sprinkle with cheeses. 
 Place back in oven for 15 minutes to allow cheeses to melt.
