Description : 
 Artichoke  with meat
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 12  artichoke  heads 
 14 oz  minced meat 
 1 medium  onion , finely chopped 
 ½ cup fried  pine nuts 
 2 tbsp  butter  for frying meat and  onion 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ¼ cup  lemon juice 
 1 cup finely chopped boiled  carrots  (optional) 
 a dash of ground  pepper 
 a dash of ground  allspice 
 a dash of  flour 
 2 cups  water  for cooking artichokes 
 Directions : 
 Cover  artichoke  heads with  water ,  flour  and  lemon  juice. 
 boil  for 10 minutes. 
 Remove and fry in some  butter . 
 Place aside. 
 Fry  onion  until soft. 
 Stir in meat,  salt , and  spice . 
 Cook on low heat for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
 Remove meat from heat. 
 Stir in  carrots . 
 Distribute meat mixture over prepared artichokes. 
 Place in a tray. 
 Add  water . 
 Cover and cook over moderate heat for 15 minutes or until tender. 
 Serve artichokes hot, garnished with  pine nuts  and accompanied by cooked  rice ,  radishes  and  lemon .
