Ingredients : 
 2 to 3 cups (one to one-and-one-half pounds) dried  cowpeas  (black-eyed peas]]), or  kidney beans , or similar 
 1 to 2 cups  maize  ( corn ) - fresh (off the cob), canned, frozen or dried 
 a few small pieces of dried or smoked or ground  dried shrimp  (optional) 
 Â½ cup  palm oil  (or any  oil ) 
 cayenne pepper  or red pepper 
 black pepper 
 small piece of  potash , or dash of  baking soda , or  salt  (to taste) 
 Directions : 
 If using dried cowpeas (or beans) or dried  corn . Clean and soak in  water  for a few hours or overnight, as needed. 
 Rinse and drain. 
 Cook cowpeas in  water  in a large part until nearly tender (about an hour). 
 Add  corn  and other ingredients. Cook until cowpeas and  maize  begin to disintegrate and form a paste. 
 Season to taste. 
 Serve as a main dish or side dish.
