 Ingredients : 
 2 shots Stolichnaya  vodka 
 0.5 shots de Kuyper  crème de cassis 
 2.5 shots  grapefruit juice 
 garnish: blackcurrants 
 glass: martini 
 Directions : 
 Shake the ingredients together in a cocktail shaker with  ice . 
 Strain into a cocktail glass.
