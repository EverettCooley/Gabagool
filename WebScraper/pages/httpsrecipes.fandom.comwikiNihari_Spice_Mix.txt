 Description : 
 A spice blend for lamb shank stew or brains 
 Ingredients : 
 ground almonds 
 cashews 
 kewra (screwpine) 
 ginger 
 garlic 
 other spices 
 Directions : 
 Whole shanks or nallis are cooked in a moghul-influenced creamy sauce. 
 Origin : 
 From Lucknow in Uttar Pradesh
