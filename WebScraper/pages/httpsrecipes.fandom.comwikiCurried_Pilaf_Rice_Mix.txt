 Description : 
 Makes 4  rice  mixes. Each mix makes 6 servings. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 Rice mix : 
 4 cups uncooked long-grain  white rice 
 1⅓ cups seedless  raisins 
 1⅓ cups chopped  dried apples 
 1⅓ tablespoons instant minced  onion 
 1 tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon  curry powder 
 Pilaf : 
 one packet of  rice mix 
 1¾ cups  water 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Rice mix : 
 Combine all ingredients in large bowl; mix well. 
 Place 2 cups  rice  mix in each of 4 air-tight containers or zip-top plastic bags. 
 Pilaf : 
 Combine 1  rice mix , 1¾ cups  water , and 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine  in 2- to 3-quart saucepan. 
 Bring to a boil; stir once or twice. Reduce heat; cover and simmer 15 minutes, or until  rice  is tender and liquid is absorbed. 
 Fluff with fork.
