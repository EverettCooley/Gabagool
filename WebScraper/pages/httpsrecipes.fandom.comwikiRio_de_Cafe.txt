Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves 4 to 6 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  chocolate syrup 
 dash  cinnamon 
 dash  salt 
 2 tbsp instant  coffee  (don't use the cheap stuff!) 
 1¾ cup hot  water , divided 
 2 cups hot  milk 
 ½ tsp  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 In a medium pan, combine chocolate syrup, cinnamon, salt, coffee, vanilla and ¼ cup hot water warm over medium heat until heated through, stirring often. 
 Add milk and 1½ cups hot water. 
 Stir often until heated through. 
 Beat with a hand mixer until foamy. 
 Serve at once.
