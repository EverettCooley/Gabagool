 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 5 each  tomatoes , chopped 
 2 each  celery , chopped 
 2 each  carrots , chopped 
 1 each  Onion , medium, chopped 
 8 each  green onion , chopped 
 1 pack equal 
 1 tsp  basil 
 1/4 tsp  garlic powder 
 1/2 tsp  salt 
 1/2 tsp  pepper 
 1/2 tsp  oregano 
 1 tbsp vegetable or  salad oil 
 1 lbs  spaghetti 
 Directions : 
 Put the vegetables in a pot and cover tightly. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, for 10 minutes. add seasonings. Cover pot, cook medium-low heat for 5 minutes. Add  oil  and simmer for 30 minutes. or until  carrots  are tender. Cook  spaghetti , drain. Toss with the sauce. calories: 150; fat: ½g
