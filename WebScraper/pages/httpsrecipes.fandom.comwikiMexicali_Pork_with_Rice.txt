Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound boneless  Pork , cut into thin strips 
 2 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 1 cup chopped  Onion 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1 6-ounce can  tomato  paste 
 1 15-ounce can  pinto beans , drained 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1/2 teaspoon ground  cumin 
 1/2 teaspoon  oregano 
 1 1/2 teaspoons  chili powder 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Brown  Pork  in  oil  in large skillet over medium heat. Add  Onion  and  garlic ; saute lightly. Stir in  tomato  paste and 1 cup  water . Cover and simmer 30 minutes. Add beans,  salt ,  cumin ,  oregano  and  chili powder ; simmer 15 minutes longer. Serve over hot  rice .
