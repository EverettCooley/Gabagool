Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 2½ oz  light rum 
 1 oz  Jamaica rum 
 ½ oz 151-proof  rum 
 ½ oz  apricot-flavored brandy 
 1 oz unsweetened  pineapple juice 
 juice of 1  lime 
 juice of 1 small  orange 
 2 tsp  powdered sugar 
 1 oz  passion fruit  syrup 
 crushed  ice 
 Directions : 
 Blend all ingredients and ½ cup crushed  ice  at low speed for 2 minutes. 
 Strain into a frosted glass. 
 Top with a stick of  pineapple  and a  cherry . 
 Carefully float 151-proof  rum  on top. 
 Add a sprig of fresh  mint  dipped in  powdered sugar  and serve with a straw.
