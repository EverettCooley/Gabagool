 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cup  watermelon , cubed, seeded 
 1/3 cup  granulated sugar 
 1/2 cup  water 
 2 tbsp  lime juice , fresh 
 Directions : 
 1 Servings: 4 
2 in a small saucepan, combine  Sugar  and  water ; bring to a boil and cook until  Sugar  is dissolved, about 1 - 2 minutes. 
3 Or, place  Sugar  and  water  in a 2-cup glass measure. 
4 Microwave at high for 2 - 3 minutes or until liquid comes to a boil, stirring once to dissolve the  Sugar . 
5 Let cool. 
6 Puree the  watermelon  in a food processor or blender until smooth; place in a bowl along with  sugar syrup  and  lime juice . 
7 Pour into an 8-inch square baking dish. 
8 Freeze until nearly solid, about 2 hours. 
9 Scoop mixture into a food processor or blender and beat until mixture is smooth, but not melted. 
10 Spoon into dishes and serve immediately. 
11 Serves 4. 
12 from the gazette, 91/07/24.
