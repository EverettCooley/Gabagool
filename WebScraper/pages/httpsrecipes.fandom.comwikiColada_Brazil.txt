Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz  cachaça 
 ¾ oz  white rum 
 2 oz  pineapple juice 
 ¾ oz  coconut cream 
 ¾ oz  cream 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients together with crushed  ice  in a shaker or blender. 
 Pour into a large highball glass over crushed  ice , and serve.
