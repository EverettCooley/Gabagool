 Description : 
 Fresh meat stew. Both north and south in Zambia, this would be served with  cornmeal   mush , called Nshima.  Both in the north and south of Zambia, this would be served with  cornmeal   mush , called Nshima.
 
 Serves 4 - 6. 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ pounds  beef , cut in cubes 
 2 cups  water 
 3  onions , sliced 
 1  tomato , peeled and sliced 
 ¼ to ½ cup  peanut oil 
 2  chile peppers   or  ½ - 1 tablespoon  crushed red pepper 
 Directions : 
 In a heavy stewing pan, combine meat,  water , and  onions . 
 Simmer 30 minutes. 
 Add  tomato ,  oil , and  chile peppers . 
 Continue simmering until meat is tender, 30 to 45 minutes more. 
 Variations : 
 This same stew may be made with  chicken ,  lamb , or . 
 Eliminate the  chilli pepper  for a southern Zambian version.
