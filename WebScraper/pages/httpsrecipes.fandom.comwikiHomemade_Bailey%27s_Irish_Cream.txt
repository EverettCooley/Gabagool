 Description : 
 This recipe is for all of you who enjoy Bailey's. This recipe tastes just as good, if not even better. Try it and let me know what you think. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 whole  eggs 
 1 can  sweetened condensed milk 
 1 tsp  vanilla extract 
 1 tbsp instant  coffee 
 1½ cup  Irish whiskey   [1] 
 Directions : 
 Whip  eggs  in blender. 
 Add  milk  and  vanilla . 
 Liquefy  coffee  with ½ cup of the  whiskey  and then blender all ingredients together. 
 Notes : 
 
 ↑   The whiskey must be Irish for flavor
