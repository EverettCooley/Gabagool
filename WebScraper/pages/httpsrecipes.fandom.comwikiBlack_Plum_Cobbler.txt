Ingredients : 
 2 pounds black plums, halved, each half cut in 4 wedges 
 1/4 cup + 1 tbs sugar 
 7.75 oz bag cinnamon swirl complete biscuit mix 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 425 degrees. 
 Put fruit in baking dish on a foil lined rimmed baking sheet. 
 Add 1/4 cup sugar and 2 tbs water, toss to mix and coat. Cover tightly with foil. 
 Bake 25 min. or until hot and juices bubble. 
 Stir biscuit mix and 1/2 cup water until a soft dough forms. 
 Drop by spoonfuls in 6 evenly spaced mounds on hot fruit. Sprinkle with 1 tbs sugar. #Bake 20 min. or until biscuits are golden brown. 
 Makes 6 servings.
