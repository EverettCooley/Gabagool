 Description : 
 Makes 4 servings. 
 
 
 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 to 12 ounces  Beef  round  Steak , cubed 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 4 1/2 cups  beef broth 
 1 tablespoon  soy sauce 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1/2 cup uncooked  rice 
 1 14-ounce can chop suey vegetables 
 1  egg , beaten 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Brown meat in  oil  over high heat. Add broth,  soy sauce  and  pepper . Bring to a boil. Lower heat, cover, and simmer 30 minutes. Stir in  rice . Replace cover and simmer 15 minutes, or until  rice  is tender. Rinse and drain vegetables. Add to meat. Remove from heat and slowly stir in  egg .
