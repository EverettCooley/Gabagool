Ingredients : 
 ⅔ cup  water 
 ⅓ cup  bulghur wheat 
 2 tbsp  cashews 
 ½ tsp  vanilla 
 ½ cup  milk  or  yogurt  (low-fat) 
 1  banana , sliced 
 Directions : 
 Bring to a boil in a small saucepan  water  and  bulghur wheat . 
 Simmer, covered until liquid absorbed. 
 Blend to a fine powder in a blender cashews. 
 Add to bulgur and blend again. 
 Pour  vanilla  and  milk  or yogurt over  bulghur wheat  and cook 5 minutes more. 
 Add the sliced banana. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Food exchange per serving: 2½ starchs/bread + 1 fruit + 2 fat + ½ milk
 
 calories: 387; pro: 10gm; fat: 3gm; car: 71gm;
