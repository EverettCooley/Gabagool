Description : 
 These ribs are so simple to prepare... just brown them, then combine with the sauce ingredients in your slow cooker.  Let the cook all day for falling-off-the-bone tenderness.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 From:  Taste of Home Slow Cooker Recipes . 
 2 to 3 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ to 1 cup  white vinegar 
 ½ cup  ketchup 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 2 tbsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 1  garlic  clove, minced 
 1 tsp  ground mustard 
 1 tsp  paprika 
 ½ to 1 tsp  salt 
 ⅛ tsp  pepper 
 2 lbs  pork spareribs 
 1 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 Directions : 
 Combine vinegar, ketchup, sugar, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, mustard, paprika, salt and pepper in a slow cooker. 
 Cut ribs into serving-size pieces; brown in a skillet in the hot oil. 
 Transfer to slow cooker. 
 Cover and cook on low or about 4 to 6 hours until tender or all day for fall-of-the-bone tenderness.
