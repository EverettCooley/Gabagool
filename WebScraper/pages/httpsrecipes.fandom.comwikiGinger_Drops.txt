Ingredients : 
 1 cup  light brown sugar 
 ⅔ cup  butter 
 ½ cup cold  water 
 1  egg 
 ⅔ cup  molasses 
 1 large tablespoon  ginger 
 1 large teaspoon  baking soda 
 enough Gold Medal  flour  to make thick batter 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together, adjusting the amount of flour to make a thick batter. 
 Drop by teaspoonfuls on a well greased pan, bake in moderate oven. 
 These are very nice if properly made.
