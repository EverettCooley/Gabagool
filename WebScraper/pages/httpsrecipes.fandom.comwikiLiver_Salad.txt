Ingredients : 
 200 g boiled  liver 
 3  potatoes 
 1  pickled cucumber 
 1.5  onion 
 salad seasoning 
 greens 
 Directions : 
 Separately boil the liver and  potatoes  and dice finely. 
 Cut the pickled  cucumbers  and  onions  into shoestrings. 
 Mix the ingredients, add the salad seasoning. 
 Heap the salad in a bowl, decorate with greens.
