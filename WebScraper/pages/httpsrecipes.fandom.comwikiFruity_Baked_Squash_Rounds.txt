Description 
 Fruity Baked Squash Rounds  from the  Public Health Cookbook  -- original source of recipe, public domain government resource
 
 Serves : 8 
 Ingredients 
 1 large  acorn squash , cut into ½ inch rings, seeds removed 
 ½ cup  orange juice 
 3 tablespoons  brown sugar 
 ⅛ teaspoon  nutmeg 
 ¼ teaspoon  cinnamon 
 Non-stick  cooking spray 
 Fruit Filling 
 whole  cranberry sauce  or  blueberry  sauce.  [1] 
 Directions 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 Spray a 13" X 9" baking dish with non-stick cooking spray. 
 Arrange squash rounds in baking dish. 
 Combine orange juice, brown sugar, nutmeg and cinnamon. Drizzle over squash. 
 Bake 45 minutes. 
 Spoon whole cranberry sauce or blueberry sauce into center of squash rounds. 
 Notes 
 
 ↑   To make blueberry sauce, heat one cup frozen  blueberries  with 2 teaspoons  sugar  in a covered microwave bowl for 2 minutes. Stir.
