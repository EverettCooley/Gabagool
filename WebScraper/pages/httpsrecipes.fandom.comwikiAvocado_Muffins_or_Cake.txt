Description : 
 Can be either a delicious moist muffin or cake, depending on the pan that it is baked in.
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup  sugar 
 1 stick  butter 
 2  eggs 
 1 cup mashed genuine  California avocado 
 ½ cup  buttermilk  or ½ cup  milk  + 1 tsp  lemon juice 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 1 ½ cups  flour 
 ½ tsp each:  nutmeg ,  cinnamon  and  allspice 
 ½ cup  raisins 
 ½ cup  walnuts , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together and pour into a  greased  8" x 10" pan, or 12 muffin pan. 
 Bake at 350°F for 35–40 minutes.
