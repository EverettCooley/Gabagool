 Ingredients : 
 1 kg  pork  meat (of thigh) 
 4 â€“ 5  onion  pieces 
 300 g ground meat (for small meat balls) 
 tomatoes 
 700 g  mushrooms 
 300 g dry smoked meat into small pieces 
 salt ,  pepper , spices, bukovec 
 some  wine ,  cheese ,  cream 
 Directions : 
 First do the meatballs where you include a piece of ground  onion salt ,  pepper  and spices. Then cut the  pork  into small pieces and fry it on  oil , add the ground  onion  and fry it together. After they redden you add the  tomatoes , the  mushrooms , the smoked meat and the small meat balls, add spices,  salt , red pepper, bukovec and  pepper  into the mixture. 
 On the other hand, dissolve a spoon full of  flour  into a glass of  water  (the one in which the  mushrooms  have been) and also add a glass of wine. The whole mixture is baked (roasted) in the oven in a baking dish made of clay. 
 By the end of the baking you can add  cheese  and  cream  according to your own taste.
