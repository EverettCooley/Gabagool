Cheese   Raisin  LOAF
 
                   Ingredients
           Quantity
                  bread flour 
           1000 grams
                 INSTANT  dry yeast 
           10 grams
                  butter 
           45 grams
                  refined sugar 
           150 grams
                  bread  improver
           4 grams
                 IODIZED  salt 
           10 grams
                  egg  YELLOW POWDER COLOR
           0.16 grams
                  butter 
           for brushing
                  Raisins 
           135 grams
                  Cheese , grated
 
                 PROCEDURE
 1. Combine all ingredients except  Raisins . Knead for 8 minutes or until 
 
 gluten is fully developed.
 
  2. Rest for one hour or until double in bulk.
 3. Cut the dough into 200 grams portions.
 4. Flatten each dough and sprinkle 15 grams  Raisins . Roll into a cylinder. 
 
 Make 1/2Â inch thick cuts 80% through on one side of the dough. Turn each 
cut sideways alternately. Tuck all the edges.
 
  5. Place in greased loaf pan and proof for 1 to 2 hours or until it 
 
 reaches 85% of height of the pan.
 
  6. Brush with  egg  wash (1  egg  white + 1 tbsp.  evaporated milk ).
 7. Bake at preheated 350?F oven for 25 to 30 minutes or until golden 
 
 brown.
 
  8. After cooling, brush with  butter  and top with grated  Cheese .
