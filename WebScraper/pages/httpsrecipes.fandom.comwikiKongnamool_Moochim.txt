 Description : 
 Soy  bean sprouts  salad
 
       Kongnamool moochim     
 Ingredients : 
 ½ lb  soy bean sprouts   [1] 
 1 tbsp  soy sauce 
 2 tbsp  sesame oil 
 1 tbsp Korean  chili powder 
 2 tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp minced  garlic 
 sesame seeds 
 1 cup  water 
 1 tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Wash soy  bean sprouts  (take out the thin tail part if you'd like). 
 Put it in a pot, add 1 cup  water  and  salt . 
 Cook for 15-20 seconds until soften. 
 Drain well, or squeeze slightly. 
 In a bowl, add soy  bean sprouts  and put soy sauce,  sesame oil ,  chili powder ,  salt ,  garlic  and  sesame seeds . 
 Mix well with hands. 
 Notes : 
 
 ↑   You can substitute to mung  bean sprouts .
