Ingredients : 
 1 cup  skim milk 
 1 envelope  unflavored gelatin 
 ½ cup  honey  or  sugar 
 1  egg white 
 1¼ cups  apricot nectar  or canned  pineapple juice 
 popsicle sticks and cups 
 Directions : 
 Pour  milk  into blender and add  gelatin . 
 Let soften for one minute before adding the rest of the ingredients to whip. 
 Pour into molds, insert sticks, and freeze.
