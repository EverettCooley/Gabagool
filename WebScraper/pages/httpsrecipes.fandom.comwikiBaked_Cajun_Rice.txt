Ingredients : 
 1 lb  ground meat  or boneless,skinless chicken 
 1 large  onion , chopped 
 1  bell pepper , chopped 
 3  celery  stalks, chopped 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1 bunch  green onion  tops, chopped 
 1½ cups  rice 
 3 cups  water 
 2 teaspoons  Cajun seasoning 
 2  tomatoes , chopped 
 1 can (6 oz.)  tomato paste  (or sauce for lighter tomato taste) 
 Directions : 
 Preheat your oven to 350°F. 
 Cook the meat,  onion ,  celery ,  bell pepper , and  garlic  in an iron skillet until the meat is done and the vegetables are cooked. 
 Drain the grease. 
 Place the meat mixture in 13"x9"x2" casserole. 
 Add remaining ingredients. 
 Mix thoroughly. 
 Cover (foil is fine) and bake 45 minutes to an hour or until  rice  is tender.
