 Description : 
 From my Aunt Maggieâ€™s Collection. This was marked as a family favorite. Dated 1944.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/4-cup warm  water 
 1 packet  dry yeast 
 1-cup  cottage cheese , warm 
 2 tablespoons  Sugar 
 1  egg 
 1 tablespoon minced  Onion 
 1-tablespoon  butter 
 2 teaspoons  dill weed 
 1-teaspoon  salt 
 1/4-teaspoon  baking soda 
 2-1/2 cups  flour 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Place all ingredients together and mix well. 
 Let rise in warm place for 45 minutes. 
 Knead down into a ball. 
 Place in well greased round casseroles. 
 Let rise again and bake at 350 for 35 minutes.
