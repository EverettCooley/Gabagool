Jelly Glaze
 
Mix And Beat Until Smooth:
 
 1/4 Cup  Ham  Juices From Pan Dripping or  Ham  Stock 
 1 Cup  currant jelly ,  orange   marmalade ,  cranberry  Jelly,  apricot  Preserves or  pineapple  Preserves 
 1 T. Dry  mustard 
 Dash  cinnamon 
 Dash Ground Cloves
 
Spread on  Ham  The Last 30 Minutes of Baking.
