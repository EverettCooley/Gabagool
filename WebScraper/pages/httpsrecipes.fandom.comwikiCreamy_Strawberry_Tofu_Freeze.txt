Description : 
 Makes 5 cups.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (12 ounce) box Mori-Nu  silken tofu  (extra firm Lite) 
 ½ cup chilled  orange juice 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 ¼ cup  granulated sugar 
 1½ pounds whole frozen  strawberries , partially thawed 
 Directions : 
 Place  tofu  in blender or food processor. Gradually add  orange juice  and  vanilla extract , pulsing until smooth. 
 Add  Sugar  and a few  berries  and blend. 
 Add and process remaining  berries , stopping occasionally to push mixture toward blades. Process until very smooth. 
 Serve immediately as a  milkshake , breakfast drink or soft, custard-like  dessert .
