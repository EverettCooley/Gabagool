Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 cup  flour , sifted 
 3 tsp  baking powder 
 1/2 tsp  salt 
 1  egg , well beaten 
 1/2 cup  milk , or skim 
 1 tsp sucaryl solution 
 3 tsp  margarine , melted 
 2/3 cup  blueberries , fresh  or  2/3 cup canned  blueberries -well drained 
 Directions : 
 Mix together sifted  flour ,  baking powder , and  salt . 
 Add well-beaten  egg ,  milk , and sucaryl solution. * sucaryl * is an artificial  Sugar  sweetener.. 
 When partly blended, add melted  margarine . Fold in  Berries . 
 Then pour into a greased muffin pan and bake at 400 degree"s for 25 minutes... 
 It is evenly mixed with the dry ingredients. 
 Then add,  milk , mixing well. 
 Bake 400 °F for 25 minutes.
