Description : 
 Potato curry
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  potatoes 
 mango 
 2 tsp  salt 
 2 tbsp  curry powder 
 2 tbsp  oil 
 2 cloves  garlic 
 1 cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Burn crushed  garlic  in  oil , remove  garlic  and fry  curry  powder for 5 minutes. 
 Add sliced  potatoes ,  mango ,  water  and  salt . 
 Cover and simmer until vegetables are tender. Serve with roti or  rice .
