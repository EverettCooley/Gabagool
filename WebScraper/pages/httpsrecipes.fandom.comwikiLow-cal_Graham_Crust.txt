Ingredients : 
 8 crushed  graham crackers  (2½ in squares) 
 3 tbsp melted  margarine 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Combine ingredients in 9 inch pie tin and mix well with a fork. 
 Press crumbs evenly around the edges and on the bottom of the pie tin. 
 Bake at 350°F for 6 minutes. 
 Cool an fill as desired. 
 Cut into 8 even portions. 
 Nutritive information : 
 For 1 portion (⅛ of recipe):
 
 calories 78 | cho 8 gm | pro 1 gm | fat 5 gm | NA 100 mg 
 food exchanges per serving: ½ bread, 1 fat 
 low sodium diets: use salt-free  margarine
