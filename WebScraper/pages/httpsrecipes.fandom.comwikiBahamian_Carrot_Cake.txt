Ingredients : 
 1 lb  carrots  grated 
 2 cups  granulated sugar 
 2 cups  oil 
 6  eggs 
 3 cups  flour 
 1 tsp  clove  powder 
 1 tsp  ginger 
 ½ tsp  pimiento 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 2 tsp  baking powder 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 1 cup  walnuts 
 1 cup  raisins 
 1 tablespoon  vanilla 
 2 tsp  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Pre-heat oven to 350°F. 
 And grease 10 inch baking pan. 
 Mix all dry ingredients and sift. 
 Beat  eggs , add  oil , then add to dry mixture. 
 Mix well, pour in baking pan and bake for about 1 hour.
