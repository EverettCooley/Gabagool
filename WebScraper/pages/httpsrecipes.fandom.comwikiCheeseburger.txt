A  cheeseburger  is a traditional sandwich that consists of ground meat made into a patty, cooked, topped with a slice of cheese, and placed between two halves of a bun. The ground meat is typically  ground beef , and is produced by grinding and blending together various cuts of beef including meat from the shoulder, brisket, and loin. A cheeseburger can also be made with a variety of ground meats other than beef, such as lamb, pork, turkey, chicken, and buffalo.
