Description 
 Fruit and Vegetable Recipes I  by the South Carolina Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Ingredients 
 PAM 
 3 cups  blueberries  –- fresh or frozen 
 1 x 15½ oz crushed  pineapple  in its own juice 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 box yellow cake mix 
 ¼ cup  butter 
 1 cup chopped  nuts 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 Directions 
 Spray 9 x 13 casserole dish with pam. 
 Mix blueberries, pineapple and sugar. 
 Put in casserole dish. 
 Top with 1 box yellow cake mix. 
 Melt ¼ cup butter and sprinkle over top. 
 Put 1 cup chopped nuts on this. 
 Sprinkle ¼ cup sugar over mixture. 
 Bake 350 °F for approximately 40 minutes.
