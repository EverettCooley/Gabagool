      American Potato Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 4 lbs  potatoes 
 ⅓ cup  red wine vinegar 
 1 tablespoon  sugar 
 ½ cup finely chopped  onions 
 1½ teaspoons  salt 
 fresh ground  pepper 
 1 cup diced  celery 
 1 cup diced  green peppers 
 1 tablespoon  celery seeds 
 1½ cups  mayonnaise 
 2 hard-boiled  eggs , sliced 
 ⅛ teaspoon hot  paprika 
 Directions : 
 In a large pot, cover the  potatoes  with a generous amount of cold salted  water . 
 Bring to a boil, cover and cook for 30 minutes or until tender. 
 Drain and peel the hot  potatoes . 
 Cut into 1 inch cubes. 
 Mix the  vinegar  and  sugar  and then pour over the hot  potatoes . 
 Add the  onion  and season with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Set aside for 1 hour. 
 Toss the  celery ,  green pepper  and  celery seeds  into the  potatoes . 
 Mix in the  mayonnaise . 
 Correct the seasoning. 
 Decorate the top with the sliced  eggs  and sprinkle with the  paprika .
