 Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 oz. Cabana Boy Citrus  rum 
 1 1/2 oz.  grapefruit juice 
 splash of fresh  lime juice 
 splash of  Grenadine 
 2 tsp.  brandy 
 Directions : 
 Blend first 4 ingredients with 1/2 cup crushed  ice  until smooth. 
 Pour into glass and float  brandy  on top. Garnish with a  lemon  and  lime .
