 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2	med	 Crawfish  tails 
 1	x	 Onion , finely chopped 
 1 1/2	oz	 Tomato Paste 
 Thyme 
 Salt  and  pepper 
 1/4	cup	 Water 
 2	tbl	 Butter 
 Directions : 
 Boil  Crawfish  talis about 25 minutes. Cool. Remove shells and chop meat into small pieces. Melt  butter  in a skillet; add  Crawfish ,  Onion  nd  thyme ; let brown, stirring occasionally. Add  tomato  paste,  salt ,  pepper  and  water . 
 Cook 5 minutes longer. Serve hot with  lemon  slices. Good served with  white rice .
