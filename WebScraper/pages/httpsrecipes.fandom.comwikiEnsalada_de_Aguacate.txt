Description : 
 Cuban avocado salad
 
 Ingredients : 
 watercress   or   lettuce  leaves, the platter, optional 
 2 large Florida  avocados   or  4  Haas  peeled, halved lengthwise and sliced 
 1 small  onion , sliced vertically 
 salt , to taste 
 fresh ground  black pepper 
 Â½ cup pure Spanish  olive oil   or  extra virgin olive oil 
 3 tbsp  white vinegar   or  fresh  lemon juice 
 1 tsp  parsley , finely chopped 
 Directions : 
 Lined with the greens, arrange the  avocado  slices, place the  onions  on top, and sprinkle with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Whisk together the  oil  and  vinegar  and drizzle over the salad. 
 Sprinkle with the  parsley  and serve.
