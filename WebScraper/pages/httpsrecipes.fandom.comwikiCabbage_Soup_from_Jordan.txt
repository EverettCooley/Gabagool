 Description : 
 Makes about 13 cups.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 very large pot, this makes a lot of soup 
 1 small head  cabbage 
 1 bunch  celery , chopped 
 3 cloves  garlic , minced 
 3  yellow onions , chopped 
 1 package Lipton  onion soup mix  (very important) 
 2 large cans V-8 vegetable juice 
 2 cans  water 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Bottled hot sauce to taste 
 Directions : 
 Cook  cabbage ,  celery ,  garlic , and  onions  in a large soup pot on low for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
 Add dry soup mix, juice, and  water . 
 Cover and cook about 2 hours. 
 Season to taste with  salt ,  pepper , and hot sauce.
