Description : 
 This pizza is really good, we all love pizza here, this pizza has cheeses and  onions  and  chicken  on it, and a  honey  Dijon dressing too.
We used ranch dressing, hot sauce, and  red pepper flakes  for garnishes.
 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 Makes 8 slices. 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds pizza dough 
 1 cup honey Dijon  salad dressing 
 ½ pound shredded  cheddar ,  mozzarella  cheese blend 
 ½ pound shredded cooked  chicken 
 2 tbsp grated  Parmesan cheese 
 1  red onion , thinly sliced in rings 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 450°F. 
 Coat pizza pan with  cooking spray . 
 Pat out pizza dough onto pan. 
 Spread dressing over dough, leaving ½ inch border around edge. 
 Sprinkle with half of  cheese  blend. 
 Scatter  chicken  over top. 
 Sprinkle with remaining  cheese  blend and the Parmesan. 
 Top with  onion  rings. 
 Bake for 20 minutes or until top is bubbly and bottom is browned. 
 Let stand 10 minutes before slicing.
