Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Butler Estate in Irving, Texas in 1988.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 head  romaine lettuce  torn shredded 
 1  green bell pepper  seeded and diced 
 1  red bell pepper  seeded and diced 
 ½ cup  dried cranberries 
 ½ cup shelled  pistachios 
 ¼ cup extra virgin  olive oil 
 3 tablespoons  red wine vinegar 
 1 teaspoon  spicy mustard 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Combine romaine, peppers, cranberries and pistachios in a large bowl. 
 Whisk together the remaining ingredients and toss with lettuce mixture.
