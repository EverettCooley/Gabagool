Description : 
 Makes 4 x ½ cup servings. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pint  oysters  with liquor 
 1 tbsp  butter 
 2 cups  low-fat milk 
 ⅛ tsp  cayenne pepper 
 Directions : 
 Combine the oysters,  oyster liquor , and  butter  in a skillet. 
 Cook until the edges of the oysters curl. 
 Add the  milk . Heat just to boiling. 
 Sprinkle with the  cayenne pepper , ladle into hot bowls. Garnish with chopped fresh  parsley  if desired. 
 Nutrition : 
 Per serving: 
 
 calories - 154 | carbohydrates - 10 g | protein - 14 g | fat ~7 g | sodium - 184 mg | potassium - 337 mg | cholesterol - 65 mg 
 Exchanges = 1 lean meat, 1 milk, 1 fat
