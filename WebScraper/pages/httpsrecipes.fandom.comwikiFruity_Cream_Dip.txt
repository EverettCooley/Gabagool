Description 
Made with authentic fruits and cream cheese, this colorful dip is great on desserts and pretzels.        Fruity Cream Dip     
 Ingredients 
 1 (8 oz.) pkg.  cream cheese , softened 
 1 small ripe  banana , mashed 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  nutmeg 
 ¼ cup crushed  pineapple , well drained 
 ¼ cup crushed  strawberries , well drained 
 apple  wedges,  grapes , strawberries, or other assorted fruits 
 Directions 
 Mix together first 3 ingredients. Stir until smooth. 
 Add crushed pineapple and crushed strawberries. Stir until well blended. 
 Spoon contents into the large bowl. Cover and chill 1 hour or overnight.
