Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 7/1/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pkg. frozen chopped  broccoli , thawed 
 1 pkg. smoked  turkey  breast chopped 
 1 cup shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 1  tomato  chopped 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 2 cans pizza crust dough 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients except dough. 
 Roll out dough. 
 Cut each crust in half diagonally. 
 Spoon ¾ cup of the filling on each crust half. 
 Fold over; crimp edges closed with fork. 
 Place on greased cookie sheet. 
 Bake at 425°F for 12 – 15 minutes or until golden brown.
