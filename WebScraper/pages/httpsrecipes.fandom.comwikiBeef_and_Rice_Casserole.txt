 Description : 
 Beef and Rice Casserole from: Uncle Ben's Rice
 Serves: 4 
 Prep Time: 45 
Meal Occasion: Side Dish, Main Dish, Lunch, Dinner 
Cuisine Type: American 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup UNCLE BEN'SR ORIGINAL CONVERTED (tm) Brand Rice 
 1 lb ground meat 
 1 can chopped tomatoes undrained 
 1/2 bag frozen vegetable mix cooked 
 1 cup cheddar cheese shredded 
 2 tsp. garlic powder 
 8 oz. sour cream 
 salt and pepper to taste 
 Directions : 
 Cook rice according to package instructions. 
 While rice is cooking, season ground meat with garlic powder, salt and pepper to taste. Brown ground meat in a large non-stick skillet. 
 Add vegetables, tomatoes and sour cream to ground beef and cook until just heated through. 
 Combine with cooked rice and mix thoroughly. Transfer to a casserole dish and top with cheese. 
 Place in a preheated 350 degree oven until warmed through and cheese melts, about 20 minutes. Serve warm. 
 Tips & Notes : 
 Can use any kind of cheese you prefer. Recommended: Monterey jack, cheddar or mozzarella.
