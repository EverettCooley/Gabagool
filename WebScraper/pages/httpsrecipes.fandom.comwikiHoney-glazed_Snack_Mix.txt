Ingredients : 
 8 cups Crispix cereal 
 3 cups miniature pretzels 
 2 cups  pecan  halves 
 ⅔ cup  butter  or  margarine 
 ½ cup  honey  
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl, combine the cereal, pretzels and  pecans ; set aside. 
 In a small saucepan, melt  butter ; stir in honey until well blended. 
 Pour over cereal mixture and stir to coat. 
 Spread into (2) greased 15 x 10 x 1- inch baking pans. 
 Bake at 350°F for 12–15 minutes or until mixture is lightly glazed, stirring occasionally. 
 Cool in pan for 3 minutes; remove from pan and spread on waxed paper to cool completely. 
 Store in an airtight container.
