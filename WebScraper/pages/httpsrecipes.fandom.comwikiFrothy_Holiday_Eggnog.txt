Description : 
 Makes 4 one half cup servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  low-fat milk 
 ½ cup Simply Eggs ( egg substitute ) 
 4 tbsp  sugar 
 1 tbsp  rum  or  brandy , optional 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 ⅛ tsp grated  nutmeg 
 whipped topping , optional 
 Directions : 
 Place  milk ,  eggs ,  sugar ,  rum  or  brandy ,  vanilla  and  nutmeg  in a blender or food processor. 
 Blend until frothy. 
 Pour eggnog into 2 tall glasses and if desired, top with whipped topping. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving without optional ingredients: 
 
 145 calories | 5 rams fat | 32 mg cholesterol | 124 mg sodium
