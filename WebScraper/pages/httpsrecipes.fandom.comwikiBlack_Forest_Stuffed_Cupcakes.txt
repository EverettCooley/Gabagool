Black Forest Stuffed Cupcakes
 1 pkg. (2-layer size) chocolate cake mix 
1 pkg. (8 oz.) Cream Cheese, softened 
1 egg 
2 Tbsp. sugar 
1 can (20 oz.) cherry pie filling, divided 
1-1/2 cups thawed Cool whip
 Preheat oven to 350F. Prepare cake batter as directed on package; set 
aside. Mix cream cheese, egg and sugar until well blended. 
 Remove 3/4 cup of the pie filling for garnish; set aside. Spoon 2 
Tbsp. of the cake batter into each of 24 paper-lined medium muffin 
cups. Top each with 1 Tbsp. each of the cream cheese mixture and 
remaining pie filling. Cover evenly with remaining cake batter. 
 bake 20 to 25 min. or until toothpick inserted in centers comes out 
clean. Cool 5 min.; remove from pans to wire racks. Cool completely. 
Top cupcakes with whipped topping and remaining pie filling just 
before serving.
