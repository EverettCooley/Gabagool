 Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz  Bourbon 
 1/2 oz  sweet vermouth 
 1/2 oz  Benedictine 
 2 dashes  bitters 
 Garnish:  lemon  Twist 
 Directions : 
 Shake all the ingredients in a shaker with  ice  and strain into a cocktail glass. 
 Garnish with a  lemon  twist.
