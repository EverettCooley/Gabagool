 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz  vodka 
 lemon  wedge 
 sugar 
 Directions : 
 Chill  vodka  and strain into shot glass. 
 Dip  lemon  wedge in  sugar . 
 To drink, shoot the  vodka  and immediately draw on the  lemon .
