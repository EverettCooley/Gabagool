Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 1 loaf 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  margarine 
 1 cup  sugar 
 2  eggs 
 1 tsp  vinegar 
 3  bananas 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 ½ tsp  baking soda 
 2 cups  flour 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ¼ cup  water 
 ¼ cup  milk 
 Directions : 
 Cream together  butter ,  sugar ,  eggs ,  vinegar  and  bananas . 
 Add dry ingredients alternately with water/milk mixture. 
 Bake at 350°F for 45 – 50 minutes.
