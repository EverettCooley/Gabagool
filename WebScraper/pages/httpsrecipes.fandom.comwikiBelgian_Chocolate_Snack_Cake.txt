 Description : 
 Very tasty  chocolate  Snack Cake straight from Belgium ! Easy to prepare !
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 1/3 c  Sugar  1 2/3 c  cocoa 
 2 1/4 c  water 
 2/3 c Veg.  oil 
 3 lg  eggs 
 2 tb  sour cream 
 2 ts  vanilla 
 2 1/3 c  flour 
 3 tb  cornstarch 
 2 1/2 ts Soda 
 1/2 ts  salt  -----glaze----- 
 1/2 c  heavy cream 
 8 oz  semisweet chocolate ; chopped 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 325. Grease 2 9" round cake pans. 
 Sift  Sugar  and  cocoa  together in large bowl. 
 Whisk in  water , then  oil ,  eggs ,  sour cream  and vainlla. 
 Whisk in dry ingredients until smooth. 
 Pour into pans and bake 40-45 minutes. 
 Until firm in center. 
 Cool in pans on racks 10 minutes. 
 Invert and remove pans; cool completely. 
 GLAZE: Heat  cream  to boiling. Remove from heat and add  chocolate  and stir until smooth. 
 Pour evenly over cakes.
