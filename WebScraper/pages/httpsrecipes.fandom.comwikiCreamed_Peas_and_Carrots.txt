 Description : 
 Serves 6
 
       Creamed Peas and Carrots     
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 10 ounces  Peas 
 10 ounces  carrots 
 3 tablespoons  flour 
 3 tablespoons  butter 
 salt 
 Sugar 
 parsley 
 Directions : 
 Scrape and cut  carrots  in small pieces; boil with fresh  green peas  (shelled). Miz  butter  and  flour  in saucepan over heat; and  vegetable stock . Season with  Sugar  and  salt . Add vegetables and warm well. When served, sprinkle with chopped  parsley .
