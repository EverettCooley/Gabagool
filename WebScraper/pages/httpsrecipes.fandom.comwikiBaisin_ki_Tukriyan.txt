Ingredients : 
 500 g baisin ( gram flour ) 
 250 g  icing sugar 
 250 g  ghee 
 100 g  blanched almond , roughly chopped 
 100 g  cashew  nut, roughly chopped 
 50 g unsalted  pistachio nuts , roughly chopped 
 seeds from 8  green cardamoms 
 ½ tsp ground  cardamom 
 4 – 5 silver leaves (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Heat  ghee  in a heavy based pan over low heat. 
 Add  cardamom  seeds and baisin. 
 Stirring all the time, fry these for about 1\2 an hour or until it is brown in color. 
 Add the  sugar ,  almond ,  cashew   nuts , pistachio and ground  cardamom . 
 Mix well and stirring frequently cook for 10 more minutes. 
 Spread evenly in a tray and leave it to cool completely for several hours or preferably overnight. 
 Cut into squares or diamonds. 
 Decorate with silver leaf and chopped  nuts , if you wish. 
 Store in an air tight container. 
 Serve with green tea.
