Ingredients : 
 2½ oz  gin 
 1½ oz  lemon juice 
 1 tsp  superfine sugar 
 4 oz  club soda 
 ½ oz  crème de cassis 
 Directions : 
 Shake the ingredients with  ice  in a shaker. 
 Add  gin ,  lemon juice  and  sugar . 
 After shaking, strain into a collins glass and fill with  ice  cubes. 
 Add the  club soda . 
 Stir well. 
 Drop the  crème de cassis  into the center of the drink.
