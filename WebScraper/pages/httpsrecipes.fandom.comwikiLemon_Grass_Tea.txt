 Ingredients : 
 1 cup chopped  lemon grass  leaves 
 2 cups boiling  water 
 sugar  (optional) 
 milk  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Place the  lemon grass  leaves in a teapot and pour the boiling  water  over them. 
 Steep for about 5 minutes. 
 Serve with  sugar  and  milk , if desired.
