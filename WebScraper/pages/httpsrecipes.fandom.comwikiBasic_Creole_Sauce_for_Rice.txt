 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 or 2onions 
 1/4 lb.lean diced  Bacon 
 2  tomatoes , peeled and seeded and diced 
 2 c.watercress 
 2 c.water 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Slice  onions  very thin and sauté them in  butter  or  oil  with  tomatoes  and  Bacon . 
 Wash, dry and strip leaves of watercress and add into pan. 
 When the watercress is wilted, add  water ,  salt  and  pepper  and allow to simmer 15 minutes.
