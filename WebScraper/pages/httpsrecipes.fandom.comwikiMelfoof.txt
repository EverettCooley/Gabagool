 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups  almond 
 1 cup  Sugar 
 1 tbsp ground  cardamon 
 2 tbsp  rose water  or  orange blossom water 
 1 package  fillo  pastry 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Grind the  almonds  and mix together with all the ingredients. The mixture should be moist. 
 Cut the  fillo  in 3 parts and cover with a damp towel. Take one piece of  fillo  and fold it about 2Â inches from the bottom up. Insert a pencil in the fold. Put one tablespoon of the mixture and roll. Push the 2 sides to the middle and slide the pencil out. Bake in a preheated over at 350 degrees for 10 minutes, until light golden in color.
