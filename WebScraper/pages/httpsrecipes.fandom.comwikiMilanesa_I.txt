Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  beef  ( round steak , loin, etc.) 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 garlic  (optional) 
 2  eggs 
 Â½ c  milk 
 flour 
 fine  bread crumbs 
 oil  for frying 
 Directions : 
 Cut the meat in thin slices and pound each side. 
 Season. 
 Beat the  eggs  and add the  milk . 
 Dip the meat first in the  flour , then the  egg  mixture, and then the bread crumbs. 
 Heat enough  oil  in a frying pan to cover the meat. 
 Fry until brown, turn and brown the other side (only about 10 minutes are needed for thinly cut meat). 
 Remove and keep hot while frying the remaining pieces.
