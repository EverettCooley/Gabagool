Ingredients : 
 1½ cups boiling  water 
 1 cup  dates , chopped 
 1¼ cups  sugar 
 1  egg 
 1 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 2 tsp  vanilla extract 
 2 cups  all-purpose flour 
 2 tsp  baking soda 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  baking powder 
 1 cup chopped  walnuts 
 1 cup Hershey's Mini Chips  semi-sweet chocolate 
 Directions : 
 Pour  water  over  dates  in medium bowl; let stand 15 minutes. 
 Heat oven to 350°F. 
 Beat  sugar ,  egg ,  oil  and  vanilla  in large bowl on high speed of mixer 3 minutes. 
 Stir together  flour ,  baking soda ,  salt  and  baking powder ; add alternately with  dates  to  egg  mixture, beating until blended. 
 Stir in  walnuts  and small  chocolate chips . 
 Pour about 1¼ cups batter into each prepared pan. 
 Bake 40 to 50 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean. 
 Cool 10 minutes; remove from pans to wire racks. 
 Cool completely. 
 Wrap and store overnight before slicing.
