Description : 
 The international alternative to the brown sauce - you can make this with cream, garlic powder, and onion soup while serving as a dip and/or with other chicken-fried dishes.
 
 Ingredients : 
 Gravy : 
 1 tbsp.  Worcestershire sauce 
 3 tbsp.  butter 
 2 tbsp.  onion soup mix 
 ¼ tsp.  thyme 
 ¼ tsp.  garlic powder 
 1 recipe  white sauce  (below) 
 White sauce : 
 1 tbsp.  cornstarch 
 2 tbsp.  flour 
 1 tsp.  lemon pepper 
 1 cup regular/non-fat  milk 
 Directions : 
 Gravy : 
 Mix all ingredients and heat until the soup mix is well dissolved and  onions  are limp from the soup mix. 
 If sauce becomes too thick, add a little regular or  non-fat milk . 
 White sauce : 
 Place  flour  in a small saucepan and cook until toasted, but not browned. Stir constantly. 
 Add  cornstarch ,  lemon pepper  and  milk . 
 Continue cooking, and stirring until thickened. 
 For the main mixture : 
 Pour the cooked gravy on the white sauce mixture. 
 Stir the combined, cooked gravy and white sauce while cooking. Serve warm, then pair this homemade gravy with other main dishes.
