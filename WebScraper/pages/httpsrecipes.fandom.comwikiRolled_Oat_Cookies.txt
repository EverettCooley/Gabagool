Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 5 tablespoons  solid vegetable fat 
 2 teaspoons  molasses 
 ⅔ cup seedless  raisins 
 1 cup  rolled oats 
 ¾ cup  flour 
 1  egg 
 a few grains of  salt 
 Directions : 
 First add the sprinkle of  salt  and the  molasses  to the fat, and work together in a bowl. 
 Add the  sugar  gradually, and  cream  the mixture until white and very light. 
 Add the well beaten  egg  and mix. 
 Add the  raisins , then the  oats , and lastly the  flour . 
 Mix lightly, and press off from the side of a tablespoon onto an oiled baking pan, leaving a little space between, and bake in a medium slow oven, to a delicate brown.
