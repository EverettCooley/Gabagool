Ingredients : 
 2 cups  black-eyed peas 
 3 teaspoons ground  coriander 
 3  garlic  cloves; minced 
 3 tablespoons fresh  ginger ; grated 
 Â½  red pepper  chopped or  cayenne  pepper to taste 
 2 tbsp olive  oil 
 1 tbsp  vinegar 
 1  salt 
 1 tsp Hungarian  paprika 
 Directions : 
 Soak peas. Put in a pot with  water  to cover, plus 1 inch and add coriander,  garlic ,  ginger  and red pepper. 
 Cook for a little over 1 hour or until still a little chewy. 
 While the  peas  are cooking, prepare a dressing by mixing together  oil ,  vinegar ,  salt  and  paprika . 
 Use 2:1 oil- vinegar  ratio and add seasonings according to taste. 
 Add to  peas  as soon as you remove them from the heat. 
 Allow to cool to room temperature and adjust seasoning then serve.
