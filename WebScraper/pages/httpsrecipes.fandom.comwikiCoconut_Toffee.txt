 Directions : 
 Crack open the  coconut , take off the skins, and cut very fine. 
 Put into a saucepan and add the  Sugar  and about four tablespoons of  water . 
 Bring to a boil and then add the  lime juice . 
 Cook until brown, stirring all the time. 
 Turn off and divide into pieces and allow to cool.
