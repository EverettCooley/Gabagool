 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 to 3  cucumbers , large 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  Sugar  replacement 
 1/4 cup  vinegar 
 1/8 tsp fresh ground  pepper 
 ( i also add a little fresh 
 garlic  to this) 
 Directions : 
 Score  cucumbers  with tines of fork. Cut into very thin slices. Sprinkle with  salt . Chill 2 hours; drain well. Sprinkle with  Sugar  replacement; add  vinegar  and  pepper . Marinate 30 minetes or more before serving. Food exchange per serving: negligible calories: negligible
