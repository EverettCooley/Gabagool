 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 shallots, trimmed and sliced OR 2  green onions , sliced thin 
 2 cloves  garlic , peeled and crushed 
 finely grated rind and juice of one  lemon 
 1/3 cup  flax seed   oil 
 2 tbsp crushed  thyme  leaves 
 2 tbsp chopped fresh  parsley 
 sea salt  to taste 
 finely ground  black pepper  to taste 
 2 cups thickly sliced  Mushrooms 
 pinch of  paprika 
 Directions : 
 Put the shallots or  green onions  in a bowl with the  garlic ,  lemon  rind and juice,  oil , herbs,  salt  and  pepper . Beat well to mix. 
 
 Add the  Mushrooms  and toss in the dressing until thoroughly coated. Cover and leave to marinate for a minimum of 2 hours in the fridge. The  Mushrooms  seem to "pickle" in this marinade, becoming very soft and flavourful.
 
 Serves 4
