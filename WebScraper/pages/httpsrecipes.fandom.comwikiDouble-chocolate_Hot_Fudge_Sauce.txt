 Makes 3 cups 
 Ingredients : 
 6 oz  milk chocolate 
 4 oz  unsweetened chocolate 
 1 lb  caramels 
 ½ cup undiluted  evaporated milk 
 ½ cup  strong coffee 
 1 tbsp  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Break  chocolate  into crock pot. 
 Add  caramels ,  milk  and  coffee . 
 Cover and cook 45 minutes on high or until melted. 
 Watch carefully so mixture doesn't burn. 
 Add  vanilla  and stir to blend thoroughly. 
 Well wrapped in refrigerator, it will keep indefinitely.
