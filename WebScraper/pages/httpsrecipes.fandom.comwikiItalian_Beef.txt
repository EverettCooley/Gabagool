 Ingredients : 
 4 – 5 lb  roast , cut into pieces 
 2 – 3  beef bouillon  cubes 
 1 tsp  garlic salt 
 2 tbsp  Italian dressing 
 ½ tsp  garlic powder 
 1 pkg.  onion soup mix 
 1 tsp  basil 
 1 tsp  oregano 
 1 tsp  Italian seasoning 
 1 tsp  celery salt 
 1 tsp  red pepper 
 Directions : 
 Water to cover beef. 
 Cook on low in crockpot 6 – 8 hours.
