 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 Chickpeas 
 vegan  mustard 
 garlic salt 
 whole  Wheat  vegan bread 
 optional: sprouts,  lettuce ,  tomatoes 
 Directions : 
 Mash  Chickpeas  in a bowl, add  garlic salt  (to taste) and  mustard  (to taste) and spread on sandwich. Add  tomatoes , sprouts,  lettuce , etc.
