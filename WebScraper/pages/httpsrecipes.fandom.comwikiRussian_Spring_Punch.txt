 Ingredients : 
 2 shots Stolichnaya red  vodka 
 ½ shot de Kuyper  crème de cassis 
 topped with  champagne 
 1 shot fresh  lemon juice 
 2 teaspoons  caster sugar 
 Directions : 
 Shake the ingredients with cubed  ice , strain into an  ice  filled highball - top with champagne.
