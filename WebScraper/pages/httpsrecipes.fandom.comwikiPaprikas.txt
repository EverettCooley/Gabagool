Description : 
       Paprikas     
 A Serbian goulash in a spicy broth that can be served with  cornbread  or over mashed  potatoes . A dry  red wine  such as  Chianti  or  Merlot  goes well with this dish. 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x 16 oz can recipe cut  tomatoes 
 1 x 8 oz can  tomato sauce 
 1 x 5" stick  pepperoni , sliced 
 4 Kosher  hot dogs , sliced 
 1  green bell pepper , strips 
 ½  white onion , sliced 
 2 cups  water 
 ½ teaspoon Hungarian  paprika 
 1 dash  cayenne pepper 
 1½ teaspoons  chili powder 
 Directions : 
 Add all ingredients to an eight quart stockpot. 
 Stir and bring to a boil, then cover and simmer for one hour, stirring occasionally.
