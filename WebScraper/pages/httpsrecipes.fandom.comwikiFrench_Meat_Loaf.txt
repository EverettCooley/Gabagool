Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes For Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Original recipe 
 Servings: 4 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb very lean  ground beef 
 ¼ cup low-fat Sweet n Spicy  French dressing 
 ¾ oz  wheat germ 
 ½ cup finely chopped  onions 
 2  egg whites 
 ⅛ tsp  pepper 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Combine all ingredients, shape into a loaf and place on a rack in a shallow baking pan. 
 Bake, uncovered, 1 hour. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving: 
 
 23g protein | 15g fat | 7g carb | 214mg sodium | 70mg chol. | 270 calories.
