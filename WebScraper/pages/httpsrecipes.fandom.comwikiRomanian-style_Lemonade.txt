 Description : 
 In Romanian: Limonada
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 big  lemons  or 4 small 
 15 teaspoons  sugar 
 1 l  water 
 Directions : 
 Squeeze the  lemons  and strain the juice through cheese cloth. 
 Dissolve the  Sugar  in  water , add the  lemon juice  and pour in a bottle with a little piece of  lemon peel . 
 Refrigerate for a few hours. Serve cold, adding a thin  lemon  slice to each glass.
