This is an adaptation of a Frugal Gourmet (Jeff Smith) recipe. It is
delicious the second and third day.

 Ingredients : 
 1 (2 1/2 to 3Â lb) head cabbage 
 2 bell peppers 
 2 small sweet onions 
 1/4 cup sugar 
 Dressing : 
 2 tbl sugar 
 2 tsp celery seed 
 2 tsp dry mustard 
 2 tsp salt 
 2/3 cup vinegar 
 2 tbl olive oil 
 1/2 cup salad oil 
 
 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl, shred cabbage; slice peppers into thin strips. Cut
onions in halves and slice very thin. Toss vegetables with 1/4 cup of
sugar; let stand while preparing the dressing. Combine sugar, celery
seed, dry mustard, and salt; add vinegar. Bring these ingredients to
a boil; cook 1 minute and let cool. When cool, beat in the oil. 
Place dressing on vegetable and refrigerate until ready to serve. 
Yields 15 + servings.
