 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 8 1/4 oz can  pineapple , well drained 
 1/2 cup shredded  carrot 
 1/2 lb  ricotta cheese 
 1/2 cup plain  yogurt 
 1 tsp  lemon juice 
 4 1/4 oz cans small  Shrimp , drained 
 2 hard cooked  eggs , chopped 
 1/2 cup roasted & chopped Oregon  hazelnuts 
 1 recipe Appetizer  cream  Puffs 
 Directions : 
 1 Combine first five ingredients. 
2 Fold in  Shrimp ,  eggs  and  nuts . 
3 Cover and chill. 
4 Just before serving, spoon a rounded tablespoon of filling into each  cream  puff. 
5 *Appetizer  cream  Puffs: In a saucepan, melt 1/2 cup  butter  or  margarine  in 1 cup boiling  water . 
6 Add 1 cup  all-purpose flour  and 1/4 teaspoon  salt  all at once; stir vigorously. 
7 Cook and stir until mixture forms a ball that does not separate. 
8 Remove from heat; cool slightly. 
9 Add 4  eggs , one at a time, beating until smooth after each. 
10 Drop level tablespoons of dough 3 inches apart on greased baking sheet. 
11 Bake at 400 for 25 minutes. 
12 Cool on rack. 
13 Cut off the top third of each puff. 
14 Remove any soft dough from inside.
