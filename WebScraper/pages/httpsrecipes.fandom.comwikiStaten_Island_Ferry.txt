Desription 
 A  Staten Island Ferry  is a variation on the  Piña Colada  that does not require coconut cream, and thus is more easily mixed in bars that lack the specialty ingredients and blenders that a Piña Colada would require.
 
 Staten Island Ferry  from the Wikibooks Bartending Guide—original source of recipe, licensed under the GNU Free Documentation License 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 Malibu rum 
 pineapple juice 
 Directions 
 Mix equal parts Malibu rum and pineapple juice over ice.
