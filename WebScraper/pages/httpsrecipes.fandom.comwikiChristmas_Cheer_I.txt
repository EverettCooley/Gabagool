Ingredients : 
 4 cups Libby's ® Juicy Juice ®  apple juice 
 3 cups  pineapple juice 
 2 cups  cranberry juice 
 1  lemon 
 1  cinnamon  stick 
 ¼ tsp ground  nutmeg 
 Directions : 
 In a large pot, combine  apple juice ,  pineapple juice ,  cranberry juice ,  lemon peel ,  cinnamon  stick and  nutmeg . 
 Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes. 
 Strain and serve warm. 
 Peel  lemon  with a vegetable peeler, making sure not to get any of the pith (white part). 
 Save fruit for another use. 
 Cut peel into ½-inch slices.
