SERVINGS 	4-6 persons
 
 Ingredients : 
 50 dag lima beans 
 60 dag finely chopped white  onions 
 4 dl  oil 
 1 tablespoon powdered mild  pepper  (originally hot) 
 smoked ribs or  Sausage  to taste 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Soak the beans, then place them into a convenient container. 
 Pour  water  and cook. After boiling drain hot  water  and pour over the beans fresh  water . 
 Add smoked meat. Continue to cook. Take care that the beans do not overcook. 
 Place  oil  in another pot and add  Onion . Simmer until  Onion  becomes glassy, but it must not turn brown. 
 Mix in red pepper,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Add the beans. Place the dish in oven and bake until it is well-baked.
