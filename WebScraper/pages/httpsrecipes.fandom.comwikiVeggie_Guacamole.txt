Description : 
 Veggie Guacamole  from the  Public Health Cookbook  -- original source of recipe, government resource in the public domain
 
 Cook Time : 15 minutes 
 Serves : 4 
 Ingredients : 
 2 ripe  avocados , peeled and pitted 
 juice of 1  lemon 
 1 large  tomato , seeded and diced 
 1 cup canned  black beans , drained 
 1 cup cooked  corn  (canned, fresh, or frozen) 
 ½ teaspoon minced  garlic 
 1 tablespoon  green onion , chopped 
 1 teaspoon  jalapeño peppers , chopped (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Mash avocados in a bowl. 
 Add lemon juice and salt, then mix in the remaining ingredients.
