 Ingredients : 
 3 tablespoons  olive oil 
 1 medium  Serrano pepper  chopped 
 1 medium  yellow onion , chopped 
 1 tablespoon fresh minced  garlic 
 1 teaspoon fresh  thyme 
 1 teaspoon fresh  rosemary 
 1 teaspoon fresh  cilantro 
 2 pounds cubed fresh  Lamb 
 1 cup plain  yogurt 
 2 tablespoons fresh  lime juice 
 1/4 teaspoon  Cayenne 
 Fresh ground  pepper  to taste 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in a saucepan. 
 Add  pepper  and  Onion  and fry gently until lightly golden. 
 Add  garlic ,  thyme ,  rosemary , and  cilantro  and fry gently 2 minutes, stirring constantly. 
 Add  Lamb  and fry until brown, stirring frequently. 
 In small bowl, mix  yogurt ,  lime juice ,  Cayenne  and  black pepper . 
 Stir  yogurt  mixture into  Lamb  mixture. 
 Cover and cook over low heat for 45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
