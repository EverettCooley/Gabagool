Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 6 cups chopped  rhubarb 
 1 large pkg.  raspberry   gelatin 
 1/2 cup  orange juice 
 2 teaspoons  vanilla 
 2/3 cup  flour 
 1/3 cup  oatmeal 
 1/2 cup  brown sugar 
 1 teaspoon  cinnamon 
 2 tablespoons  margarine 
 2 tablespoons  orange juice 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rhubarb ,  gelatin ,  vanilla , and 1/2 cup  orange juice  in a 9-inch square baking dish. 
 Blend remaining ingredients in a small mixing bowl until crumbly. 
 Sprinkle crumbs over  rhubarb  mixture. 
 Bake uncovered for 45 minutes at 350 degrees, just until bubbly.
