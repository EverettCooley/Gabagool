Description : 
 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 6  Chicken  breasts 
 6  bananas 
 1 cup  canola oil 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Wash  Chicken  breasts and dip them in  oil . 
 Mash  bananas . 
 Arrange  Chicken  on a baking sheet and cover each piece with  banana . 
 You can garnish this with almost any spice imaginableâ€”popular ethnic ones are  paprika , coriander, poppyseeds, or  cinnamon . 
 Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes or until  Chicken  reaches internal temperature of 165 degrees.
