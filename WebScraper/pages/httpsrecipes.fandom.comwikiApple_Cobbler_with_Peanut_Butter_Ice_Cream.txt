 Ingredients : 
 Cobbler Filling 
 6  Apples , cut into 1/2-inch dice 
 2 tsp.  granulated sugar 
 1/2 tsp. ground  cinnamon 
 1/4 tsp. ground  cardamom 
 Cobbler Topping 
 2 cups  all-purpose flour 
 1 cup  brown sugar 
 1 cup  granulated sugar 
 Pinch  kosher salt 
 3/4 cup  quick-cooking oats 
 1/2Â lb. cold  unsalted butter , cut into 1/2-inch dice 
 Directions : 
 1. Preheat oven to 350F. Lightly  butter  ovenproof baking dish.
 2. To make filling: Combine filling ingredients in mixing bowl, and toss together. Place into baking dish.
 3. To make topping: Combine dry ingredients in mixing bowl. By hand and using sharp knives, cut  butter  into dry ingredients, and work the mixture until  butter  is size of large  Peas . Pack topping over diced
 Apples .
 4. Bake 20 to 30 minutes or until golden brown. Remove from oven, and serve hot or at room temperature
