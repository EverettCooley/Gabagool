Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Turner Estate in Dallas, Texas in 1997. Also listed as part of the Rankin Estate in Dallas, Texas in 1988. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound cooked cubed  pork 
 2 large cans crushed  tomatoes 
 ½ cup  chili seasoning mix 
 6 cups cooked  kidney beans 
 1 cup  salsa 
 1 large white  onion  chopped 
 Chili seasoning mix : 
 ⅔ cup  flour 
 3½ cups minced dry  onions 
 1¼ cup  chili powder 
 3 tablespoons  salt 
 2 tablespoons  cumin 
 ½ cup dried minced  garlic 
 3 tablespoons  red pepper flakes 
 3 tablespoons  oregano 
 Directions : 
 Combine all mix ingredients and store in a cool dry place. 
 Combine all ingredients in the crock pot and cook on low for 6 hours.
