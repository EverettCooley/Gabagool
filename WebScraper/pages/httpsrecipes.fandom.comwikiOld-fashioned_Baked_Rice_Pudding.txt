Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup uncooked  rice 
 ⅓ cup  sugar 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 5 cups  milk , scalded 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 ⅓ cup  raisins 
 Directions : 
 Measure  rice ,  sugar  and  salt  into 2-quart baking dish. 
 Add  milk  and  vanilla ; stir. 
 Dot with  butter . 
 Bake at 325°F for 2 hours, stirring at 30 minute intervals. 
 Add  raisins  at last stirring. 
 Bake 30 minutes more without stirring.
