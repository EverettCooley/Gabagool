 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup  butter 
 1/2 cup  Sugar 
 1  egg 
 1 cup  flour 
 2 teaspoons  baking powder 
 2 tablespoons  cocoa 
 1 cup  peanuts 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 375 °F (190 °C). 
 cream   butter  and  Sugar . 
 Add  egg  and beat well. 
 Mix in sifted dry ingredients. 
 Add  peanuts . 
 Put on greased cookie sheets by the spoonfuls. 
 Bake 20 minutes.
