 Ingredients : 
 2 shots De Kuyper  cranberry liqueur 
 1 shot Stolichnaya  vodka 
 3 shots  apple juice  (clear) 
 garnish: a wedge of  lime  and/or fresh  cranberries 
 glass: martini 
 Directions : 
 Shake all the ingredients together in a cocktail shaker with  ice . 
 Strain into a martini glass. 
 Add a wedge of  lime  and/or fresh  cranberries  if desired.
