Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group  and also  Healthy Recipes For Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Original recipe 
 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 6 cups mixed greens, torn into pieces ( red leaf ,  Boston  or  romaine lettuce ,  radicchio ,  arugula , etc.) 
 â…“ cup mixed fresh herbs, chopped ( parsley ,  thyme ,  basil ,  chives ,  cilantro ,  dill ,  tarragon , etc.) 
 4 red  radishes , thinly sliced 
 12  cherry tomatoes 
 16  walnut  halves 
 1 tbsp organic  balsamic vinegar 
 2 tbsp organic extra virgin  olive oil 
 freshly ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Toss the greens, herbs, and radishes with the oil and toss again with the vinegar. 
 Arrange the cherry tomatoes and walnuts. 
 Dust lightly with pepper and serve.
