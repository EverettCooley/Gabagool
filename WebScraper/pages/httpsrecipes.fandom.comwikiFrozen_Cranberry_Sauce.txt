Frozen  cranberry sauce 
 
 2 Cups Fresh or Frozen  cranberries 
 1 Small  Onion 
 1/2 Cup  sugar 
 3/4 Cup  sour cream 
 2 T. Grated Horseradish 
 
Chop  cranberries  And  Onion  in Food Processor. Mix Together With Remaining Ingredients. Freeze. Serve When Just Thawed, But Still Icy. This is a National Public Radio Recipe. it is Wonderful With  Turkey  or Roast  Beef . Don't Say no Until You've Tried It.Source: Rosalie Bier
