 Pipino is the Filipino term for  cucumber .
 
       Pipino Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 1 large  cucumber 
 2 tbsp  vinegar 
 â…› tsp  salt 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 1 small  tomato 
 sweet red pepper 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl, combine  vinegar ,  salt ,  sugar ,  tomato  and  sweet red pepper . 
 Set aside. 
 Peel  cucumber , then slice thinly crosswise. 
 Pour into the dressing mixture and refrigerate, then serve.
