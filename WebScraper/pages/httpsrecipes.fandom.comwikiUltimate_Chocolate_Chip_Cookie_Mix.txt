 Description : 
 A professional combination of chocolate, baking soda, flour, salt, and sugar is packed in jars by selling. You can also make this with real vanilla for light sweetness.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 ¼ cup flour 
 1 cup sugar, brown or white only 
 ½ teaspoon baking soda 
 ¼ teaspoon salt, iodized or rock only 
 3 cups chocolate morsels, with 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate, 1 cup white chocolate, and 1 cup milk chocolate separated 
 Directions : 
 For selling : 
 Layer all ingredients in order, as listed above in a 1-quart clear container with a tight-fitting lid. 
 Pack each layer before adding another by lightly tapping the bottom of the container on the countertop. Seal; store in a cool dry place. Sell per jar, if desired. 
 To bake : 
 Beat ½ cup softened butter at medium speed with a mixer until creamy. Add 1 large egg and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, beating until blended. 
 Pour the contents from the ultimate chocolate chip cookie mix into a large bowl and stir with a spoon. Drop by tablespoonfuls onto baking sheets. Bake at 350° F for 9 to 10 minutes or until golden brown. 
 Cool on the baking sheets for 1 minute. 
 Transfer to the wire racks; cool completely. Serve cold and plain or with ice cream or other dessert-based dips.
