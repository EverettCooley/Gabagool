Ingredients : 
 1 pint  strawberries ; fresh 
 1 lb  rhubarb ; chopped 
 1½ cups  sugar 
 3 tbsp  tapioca 
 2 tbsp  butter 
 unbaked pie shell 
 Directions : 
 Half the  strawberries . 
 Mix all ingredients except  butter . 
 Pour into unbaked pie shell; dot with  butter . 
 Bake at 350 °F for 45 – 55 minutes.
