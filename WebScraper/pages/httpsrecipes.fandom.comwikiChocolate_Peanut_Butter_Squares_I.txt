Description : 
 This recipe is really yummy if you like peanuts and chocolate together and this is really simple to make.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Makes 4 dozen squares. 
 Ingredients : 
 2 packages (10 oz each)  peanut butter chips 
 ⅓ cup  unsalted butter , cut up 
 2 cans (14 oz each)  sweetened condensed milk 
 2 cups finely ground honey  graham cracker  crumbs 
 ½ cup unsalted  peanuts , finely chopped + 1 cup coarsely chopped 
 12 oz pkg semisweet  chocolate chips 
 Directions : 
 Line baking pan with foil. 
 Combine peanut butter chips and butter in bowl. 
 Microwave on 50% power for 30 seconds. Stir. 
 Return to microwave, heat on high 1 minute and 30 seconds, stirring every 30 seconds. 
 Whisk in milk. 
 Stir in crumbs and ½ cup peanuts. 
 Pat the mixture into the pan. 
 Refrigerate until firm. 
 Microwave chocolate chips in a bowl on high 1 minute. 
 Stir until smooth. Microwave more if needed. 
 Spread over top of peanut mixture. 
 Sprinkle with remaining peanuts. 
 Let stand at room temp 1 hour. 
 Cut into 48 squares.
