 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 ½ cup  sour cream 
 ½ cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 ¾ cup shredded  mozzarella  cheese divided 
 2 teaspoons  Dijon mustard 
 ½ teaspoon  white pepper 
 14 ounce can  artichoke hearts  drained and coarsely chopped 
 1 cup coarsely chopped  spinach  leaves 
 ½ cup finely chopped  red onion 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl combine  mayonnaise ,  sour cream , Parmesan, ½ cup  mozzarella ,  mustard  and  pepper . 
 Stir in  artichoke  hearts,  spinach  and  onion  then transfer mixture to casserole dish and cover and chill at least 3 hours. 
 Bake at 350°F for 30 minutes. 
 Top with remaining  mozzarella  and bake 5 minutes longer.
