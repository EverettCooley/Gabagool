 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3	x	Green  plantain 
 1	x	Ripe but firm  plantain 
 2	tbl	 Butter 
 Salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Boil  plantains . when soft, pound them in a large mortar. 
 Dip pestle in cold  water  in between pounding to prevent sticking. when smooth, mix with  butter ,  salt  and  pepper . keep warm. 
 I like this with almost any meat as a substitute for  rice  or  potatoes .
