Description : 
 My daughter LOVED these when she was little. Healthy sweet snacks you can feel good about. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  peanut butter 
 ½ cup  raisins 
 ½ cup  dry milk 
 ¼ cup  sesame seeds 
 ¼ cup  honey 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together, and roll into one inch balls. 
 Chill.
