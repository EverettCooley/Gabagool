       Quick and Easy Sloppy Joes     
 Prep: 10 minutes | Total: 25 minutes 
 Makes 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb lean  ground beef 
 1 cup chopped  onions 
 ¾ cup A1 Original  steak sauce 
 ¼ cup  water 
 1 tsp  chili powder 
 4  hamburger buns  or  kaiser rolls  or  English muffins , split, toasted 
 Directions : 
 Cook and stir meat and  onions  in large skillet until browned; drain. 
 Stir in  steak  sauce,  water  and  chili powder ; bring to boil. 
 Reduce heat; simmer 10 to 12 minutes. 
 Spoon meat mixture into rolls to serve.
