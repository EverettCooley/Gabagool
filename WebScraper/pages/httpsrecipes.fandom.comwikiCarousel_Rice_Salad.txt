Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice , cooled 
 1 cup grated  carrots 
 1 cup sliced  celery 
 ⅓ cup sweet pickle  relish 
 ¾ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅛ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 lettuce  leaves 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 ½ teaspoon  sweet basil 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  carrots ,  celery , pickle relish,  salt  and  pepper  in large bowl. 
 Toss lightly; chill. 
 Serve on  lettuce  leaves. 
 Blend  basil  into  sour cream ; spoon over salad.
