Ingredients : 
 12 oz  okra , fresh or frozen 
 2 med  green peppers , diced 
 2 cup  celery , diced 
 4 oz  onion , diced 
 4 cloves  garlic , pressed 
 salt  and  pepper , to taste 
 ½ cup  water 
 1 lb roasted  lamb , in bite size cubes 
 ¼ tsp  thyme 
 paprika 
 grated  lemon rind 
 Directions : 
 Cook  okra  until liquid evaporated in skillet or Microwave dish, stirring occasionally for 5 minutes. 
 Add  green pepper ,  celery ,  Onion ,  garlic ,  salt  and  pepper  to taste. 
 Cook for 10 minutes. 
 Stir to prevent vegetables from sticking. 
 Add  water ,  Lamb  and  thyme  and simmer, covered, over Low heat for 20 minutes until heated through. 
 Sprinkle with  paprika  and garnish with  lemon  rind.
