Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 800 g  Pork 
 1 kg  potatoes 
 400 g  turnips 
 400 g  carrots 
 2  onions 
 salt  (to taste) 
 Directions : 
 1.	Boil the  Pork ,  potatoes , sliced  turnips ,  carrots  and  onions  in a little  water . (you can remove the  Pork  pieces from the stock)
 2.	Now add  salt  and cook it for few more minutes so that  salt  get absorb in all vegetables.
 3.	Serve with the stock or use the stock as a sauce. 
 4.	Green salad as accompaniment.
