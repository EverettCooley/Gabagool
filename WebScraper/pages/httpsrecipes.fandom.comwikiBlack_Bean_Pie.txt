Ingredients : 
 ½ teaspoon  chili powder 
 3 tablespoons  oil 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 ½ cup  pepper 
 2 tablesppons chopped  jalapeño pepper  (optional) 
 1 can  black beans , drained and rinsed 
 ½ cup  salsa 
 3 flour  tortillas 
 grated  colby - jack  or  cheddar 
 1 pkg. all-ready pie crusts 
 Directions : 
 Spread mixture on bottom crust. 
 Top with  flour  tortilla. 
 Spread ⅓ of bean mixture over tortilla. 
 Top with layer of shredded  cheese . 
 Repeat layers—tortilla, ⅓ of bean mixture, and layer of shredded  cheese —to make three layers in all. 
 Cover top layer of  cheese  mixture with remaining pie crust. 
 Cut slits in crust to vent. 
 Bake for 40 – 50 minutes in 350°F oven. 
 Let stand five minutes before serving.
