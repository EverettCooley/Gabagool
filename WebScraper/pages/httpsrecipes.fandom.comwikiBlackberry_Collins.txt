      Description     
 Ingredients : 
 50ml  gin 
 25ml fresh  lemon juice 
 12.5ml  sugar syrup 
 8-10  blackberries 
 soda water 
 Directions : 
 Fill a glass with the ingredients, shake them and serve with  ice .
