Ingredients : 
 6 green  plantain 
 2 pints  vegetable oil  or  shortening 
 Directions : 
 Peel  plantain . 
 Slice thinly at an angle lengthwise into dip size chips. 
 Heat  oil  and fry slices until crisp. 
 Lightly  salt .
