Description : 
 Makes about 1¼ cups
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup quick or old fashioned  rolled oats . 
 1¼ cups  flour , divided 
 ½ cup packed  brown sugar 
 ½ teaspoon each  baking powder  and  salt 
 1 cup cutter of  margarine , softened 
 1  egg , beaten 
 4  Golden Delicious apples , pared, cored and sliced into 16ths. 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 ⅛ to ¼ teaspoon ground  nutmeg 
 Crumb topping : 
 ⅓ cup  rolled oats 
 ⅓ cup  flour 
 ⅓ cup  sugar 
 ¼ cup softened  butter  or  margarine 
 Directions : 
 Combine  oats , 1 cup  flour ,  brown sugar ,  baking powder  and  salt . 
 Cut in  butter  until crumbly. 
 Stir in beaten  egg . 
 Spread mixture into 15 x 10 x 1 inch pan. 
 Gently toss  apples , with  sugar , ¼ cup  flour  and  nutmeg . 
 Arrange on crust. 
 Sprinkle crumb topping in diagonal pattern over  apples . 
 Bake at 350°F for 30 – 35 minutes or until  apples  are tender. 
 Cut into bars or squares. 
 Crumb topping : 
 Combine  rolled oats ,  flour  and  sugar . 
 Cut in softened  butter  or  margarine  until crumbly.
