Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ oz  light rum 
 ½ oz  Southern Comfort 
 ½ oz  amaretto 
 2 oz sour mix 
 2 oz  orange juice 
 1 splash  grenadine 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Combine and shake well with  ice . 
 Pour into a glass and top with a slice of  orange .
