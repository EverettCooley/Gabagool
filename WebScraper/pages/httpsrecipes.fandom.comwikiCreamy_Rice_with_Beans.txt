Creamy  rice  With beans
 
 1/2 Cup Uncooked Converted Style  rice 
 1 Can  red kidney beans  (8 Oz), Rinsed And Well Drained 
 1/3 Cup  sour cream 
 1/2 Cup Shredded Sharp  Cheddar   Cheese , Divided 
 
In Medium Pan, Prepare The  rice  as Package Directs. Stir in The  kidney beans ,  sour cream  And All But 1 T. of The  Cheddar   Cheese . Reduce Heat. Cook, Stirring Until Heated Through. Top With Remaining 1 T.  Cheese . Serves 4Source: my Old Recipes
