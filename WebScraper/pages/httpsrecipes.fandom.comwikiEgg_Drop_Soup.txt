Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Best of Bridge 
 Serves 6 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cans  chicken broth  (10 oz each) 
 1  egg , slightly beaten 
 2 tbsp chopped  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Heat broth just to boiling. 
 Blend in beaten  egg  very slowly,stirring constantly, just until  egg  cooks and separates into strings. 
 Remove from heat and cool slightly. 
 Ladle into bowls and sprinkle lightly with  parsley .
