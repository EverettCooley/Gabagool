      Swedish Bean Bake     
 Ingredients : 
 2 cans baked beans 
 1 	large  Apple , chopped 
 1/4 	cup  Raisins 
 1/2 	cup chopped  onions 
 4 	slices cooked  Bacon , crumbled, or, 1-2 cup chopped boiled  Ham 
 3/4 	cup  brown sugar 
 1/4 	cup sweet pickle relish 
 1 	tablespoon prepared  mustard 
 3/4 	cup  catsup 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients, mix well. 
 Put into a 2-quart casserole. 
 Bake covered at 250 degrees about 1 1/2 hours.
