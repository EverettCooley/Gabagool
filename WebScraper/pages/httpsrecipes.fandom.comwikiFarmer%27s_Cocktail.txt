Ingredients : 
 2 oz  gin 
 1 oz  dry vermouth 
 1 oz  sweet vermouth 
 2 dashes  bitters 
 Directions : 
 Shake the ingredients and serve them into a cocktail glass. 
 Garnish with  lemon  twist.
