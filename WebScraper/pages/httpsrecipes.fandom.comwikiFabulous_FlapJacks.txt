Ingredients : 
 2 cups stone ground  whole wheat flour 
 3 tsp  baking soda 
 1/2 tsp  salt 
 1 tbsp  molasses 
 1/4 cup  sucanat 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Mix all dry ingredients together in a bowl. 
 Mix in  water  to desired consistency. The thicker the batter the higher the pancake. 
 Fry in non-stick pan until golden brown.
