Apple   bran  Muffins
 
Serving Size : 12
 
Microwave
 
Preparation Method
 
 1 Cup  brown sugar 
 3 Teaspoons  baking soda 
 3/4 Cup  butter 
 3 Teaspoons  cinnamon 
 1/2 Cup  molasses 
 1 Teaspoon  nutmeg 
 2  eggs 
 1 Teaspoon  salt 
 2 Cups  flour 
 1 Pound Can  Apple  Sauce 
 1 1/2 Cups 100%  bran  Cereal 
 
Cream  sugar  And  butter . Beat in  eggs  And  molasses . Add Dry Ingredients,Then  applesauce . Will Look Curdled. Use 2 Paper Liners Per Muffin AndFill 1/2-Full. Last up to 4 Weeks.
 Cook on Medium-High (4 Muffins - 2-1/2 Minutes; 6 Muffins - 3-1/2Minutes.)
