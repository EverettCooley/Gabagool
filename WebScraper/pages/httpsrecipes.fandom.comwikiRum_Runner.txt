      Rum Runner     
 Ingredients : 
 ⅞ oz  blackberry brandy 
 ⅞ oz  banana liqueur 
 ¾ oz 151  rum 
 ⅝ oz  grenadine 
 1½ oz  lime juice 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Fill glass with ice, put ice in blender. 
 Add remaining ingredients. 
 Blend until smooth. 
 Pour back into original glass and garnish with mini umbrella.
