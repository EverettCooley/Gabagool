      Chile Cheese Burritos.jpg     
 Ingredients : 
 6 Mission™ fat-free fajita-size flour  tortillas 
 2 cups fat-free  chile 
 ¾ cup Mission™ garden-style  salsa 
 1½ cups fat-free  cheddar  cheese shredded 
 6 tbsp fat-free  sour cream  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Prepare a medium-hot grill and lightly spray with nonfat  cooking spray . 
 Lightly spray a small saucepan with nonfat  cooking spray ; add  chile  and salsa to pan and place on grill. 
 Cook  chile , stirring constantly, 5–10 minutes until thoroughly heated; remove from heat and keep warm. 
 Place tortillas on grill and cook 1–3 minutes, turning 2-3 times, just until soft and warm. Remove tortillas from grill; top each tortilla *with  chile , 1/4 cup  Cheese  and 1 tablespoon  sour cream , if desired. 
 Roll tortilla up and serve with additional salsa, or wrap in foil and grill 1–2 minutes, until  Cheese  is melted.
