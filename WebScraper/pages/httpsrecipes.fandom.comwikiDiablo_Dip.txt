 Description : 
This bold, red-hot dip is made and mixed with tomato sauce, fruit juice, and garlic and is sure to relish a tangy aroma with the condiments. It is easy to pair with other side dishes or appetizers to make it look delicious.       Diablo Dip     
 Ingredients : 
 8 ounce canned  tomato  sauce 
 ½ teaspoon  lemon juice 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 ⅛ teaspoon  black pepper 
 ⅛ teaspoon  hot pepper sauce  to taste 
 cooked, ground beef, optional 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients, especially including cooked, ground beef, in a small bowl. 
 Serve with  Sombrero artichokes , nachos, potato chips, or other appetizers, vegetables, or side dishes.
