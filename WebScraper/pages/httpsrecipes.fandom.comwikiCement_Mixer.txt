Ingredients : 
 1 ounce Bailey's Irish  cream 
 1 dash Rose's  lime juice 
 Directions : 
 Pour Bailey's into shot glass. 
 Float  lime juice  and stir till curdled (about 10 Sec.). 
 Pour into a large shot glass and serve.
