Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 Â½ cup chopped  onion 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1 cup uncooked  rice 
 2 cups  chicken broth 
 Directions : 
 Melt  margarine  in 3-quart saucepan over medium heat. 
 Add  onion  and  garlic ; cook until  onion  is tender. 
 Add  rice  and broth. Bring to a boil; stir. 
 Reduce heat; cover and simmer 15 minutes or until  rice  is tender and liquid is absorbed. Fluff with fork.
