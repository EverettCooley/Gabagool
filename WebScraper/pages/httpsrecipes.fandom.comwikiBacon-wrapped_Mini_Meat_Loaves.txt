Description : 
 Purchased from Bowers Estate in Fort Worth, Texas in 1994. Dated 1941. This is a very good recipe. I made it about once a month and it never gets old.
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ pounds lean  ground beef 
 1 cup sharp  cheddar  cheese, shredded 
 1 large  egg 
 ¼ cup dry  breadcrumbs 
 ¼ cup  white onion , chopped 
 ¼ cup fresh  lemon juice 
 ¼ cup  green bell pepper , chopped 
 ½ cup  water 
 ½ teaspoons instant  beef bouillon 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 6 thin slices  bacon , each cut in half 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 350°F. 
 Mix all ingredients together except  bacon . 
 Shape the mixture into 6 small loaves. 
 Crisscross 2 of the half slices of  bacon  across each loaf tucking the ends under each loaf. 
 Place loaves on a rack in a shallow baking pan and bake uncovered for 50 minutes. 
 Let stand for 5 to 10 minutes before serving.
