Description : 
 Serves: 2-3
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ lbs thick  beef round  or  sirloin steak 
 ⅓ cup  flour 
 3 tbsp  oil 
 1 tsp Tony's seasoning 
 3 medium  onions , sliced 
 10 oz can  beef broth 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 tsp minced  garlic  
 Directions : 
 Cut meat into serving-size pieces 
 Add Tony's 
 Pound flour into steak with meat tenderizer 
 In large skillet, brown steak in oil and top with onion slices 
 In medium bowl, combine remaining ingredients 
 Pour over steak, bring to a boil and reduce heat to simmer 
 Cover and cook slowly for 1 hour (or until tender) 
 Check steak while cooking and add a little water if needed 
 === Serve with potatoes or vegetables ===
