Ingredients : 
 2-3 lba  ground beef 
 1 cup  oatmeal  (can also use crushed saltine crackers) 
 ¼ cup  milk  (can add more if needed) 
 2  eggs 
 1 medium  onion , chopped up 
 ¼ cup  tomato   ketchup 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Put everything into one bowl, mix well using hands (this always worked better than a spoon) 
 Put into pan and bake at 350 °F for about an hour. 
 Drain grease off and serve.
