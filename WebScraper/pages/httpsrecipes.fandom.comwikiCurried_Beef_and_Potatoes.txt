Ingredients : 
 ¾ lbs beef  top round steak 
 ½ cup  beef broth 
 ¼ tsp Tony's seasoning 
 ¾ cup chopped  green  or  red pepper 
 1/3 tsp  curry powder 
 3 small  potatoes , halved and thinly sliced (½ lb total) 
 2 tsp  cornstarch 
 ¾ cup chopped  onion 
 1 tbsp cooking  oil 
 1 can stewed  tomatoes , Partially drained choped coarse 
 Directions : 
 Partially freeze  meat 
 Thinly slice across the grain into bite sized strips. Set aside 
 Cook sliced  potatoes  in boiling  water  about 5 minutes or till tender. Drain and set aside 
 Meanwhile, for sauce, stir together  beef broth ,  cornstarch , and Tony's. Set aside 
 Spray a wok or large skillet with PAM 
 Heat over medium-high heat 
 Add  onion  and stir fry about 2 minutes 
 Add green or red pepper and stir fry about 2 minutes more or till vegetables are crisp-tender 
 Remove from wok, set aside 
 Add  oil  to hot wok 
 Add  beef  and  curry  powder 
 Stir fry 2 – 3 minutes or till  beef  is tender, set aside 
 Stir sauce and add to wok 
 Cook and stir till thickened and bubbly 
 Stir in all ingredients 
 Cook and stir all ingredients for 1 minute or till heated through 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 251 calories | 23 g protein | 21 g carbohydrates | 8 g fat | 53 mg cholesterol | 414 mg sodium | 714 mg potassium
