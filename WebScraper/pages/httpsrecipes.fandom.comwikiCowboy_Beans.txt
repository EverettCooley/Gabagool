Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup chopped  Onion 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil  or  Bacon  drippings 
 3 cups drained cooked  pinto beans 
 1 smoked  Ham  hock 
 1 6-ounce can  tomato  paste 
 1 4-ounce can diced green chiles 
 1 teaspoon  Sugar 
 1 teaspoon  chili powder 
 2 cups  water 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Onion  in  oil  in large saucepan or Dutch oven over medium heat until  Onion  is transparent. 
 Add beans,  Ham  hock,  tomato  paste, chiles,  Sugar ,  chili powder  and  water . 
 Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer, uncovered, 45 minutes. 
 Remove  Ham  hock; cut meat off bone and dice. 
 Return meat to beans. 
 Serve beans over  rice .
