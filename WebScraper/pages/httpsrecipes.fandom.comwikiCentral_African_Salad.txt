 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 150 gm long  grain   brown rice 
 1 small  cucumber 
 2  bananas 
 50 gm  raisins 
 15 ml chopped  almonds 
 60 ml  olive oil 
 20 ml  lemon juice 
 zest of 1  lemon 
 5 ml  salt 
 2.5 ml ground coriander 
 2.5 ml ground  cumin 
 2.5 ml  cayenne pepper 
 5 ml Organic runny honey 
 Directions : 
 Cook the  rice  in the usual way, rinse and allow to cool. 
 Slice the  cucumber . 
 Peel and slice the  bananas . 
 Put the  rice , cucmber,  bananas ,  raisins  and  almonds  in a salad bowl and mix gently but well. 
 Dressing
 
 Mix together the  lemon juice ,  lemon  zest,  salt , coriander,  cumin ,  Cayenne  and honey.
