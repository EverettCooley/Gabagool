      Amish Chicken Dressing     
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup finely chopped  onion 
 ½ cup finely chopped  celery 
 ½ cup finely chopped  carrots 
 1 cup diced  potatoes 
 ½ cup  margarine 
 1 cup  chicken broth 
 1 large loaf dried  bread  cut into cubes. 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ⅛ tsp  pepper 
 1 cup of  milk 
 2 cups of finely chopped chicken 
 Directions : 
 Cook until  vegetables  are tender. 
 Add  bread  cubes,  salt , pepper ,  milk , 1  chicken broth  and chicken to vegetable mixture and mix. 
 Bake 35 – 45 minutes at 350 °F.
