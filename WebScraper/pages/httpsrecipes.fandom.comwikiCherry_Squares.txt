cherry  Squares
 
 1 Can  cherry  Pie Filling or Any Fruit 
 2 C.  sugar 
 1 C.  butter  (2 Sticks) 
 4  eggs 
 3 C.  flour , Sifted 
 1 1/2 Tsp.  baking powder 
 1 Tsp.  almond extract 
 1 Tsp.  vanilla extract 
 
Beat  sugar  And  butter  Until Creamy. Add  eggs , FlourAnd Extract Alternately. Beat Until Smooth. Spread 3/4Batter in Greased 9x13 Pan. Spread Can of  cherry . DotRemaining Batter And Spread. Bake in 350 Degree F.
 Oven For 45 Minutes. Cool And Cut Into Squares. IfPyrex Dish is Used, Bake at 435 Degrees F. For 45
 Minutes. Top With Fruit. Chill And Serve.
 
Enjoy.
