Ingredients : 
 1 medium  onion , chopped 
 1 stalk  celery , chopped 
 3 tbsp  margarine 
 ¾ lbs lean  ground beef 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 1 medium  cabbage , shredded 
 2  apples , sliced thin 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 In skillet, saute  onion  and  celery  in  margarine  2 minutes. 
 Add  beef  and  salt  and stir another 2 minutes. 
 Spread half the  cabbage  in 2 qt baking dish and cover with half the  apples  and all the meat mixture. 
 Add remaining  cabbage  and  apple  slices. 
 Cover and bake 1 hour. 
 Nice with mashed  potatoes  or noodles. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Food exchange per serving: 1 lean-meat exchange + 1 vegetable exchange + 1 fat exchange if that + 1 fruit exchange
