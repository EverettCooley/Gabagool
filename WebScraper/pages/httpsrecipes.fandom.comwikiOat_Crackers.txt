      Oat Crackers     
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups  rolled oats 
 1 cup  flour 
 ½ cup  wheat germ 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ⅓ cup  oil 
 ⅔ cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Mix dry and wet ingredients separately. 
 Then combine them together. 
 Roll out dough ⅛" thick onto an oiled cookie sheet. 
 Score and bake at 325°F for 25 to 30 minutes.
