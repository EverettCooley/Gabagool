Ingredients : 
 8  plantain  bananas (matoke) 
 juice of 1  lemon 
 1 tablespoon  ghee  or  butter 
 2  onions  sliced 
 ½ bunch  coriander leaves 
 1 whole  chile 
 2 cups  beef stock 
 Directions : 
 Peel the  bananas  and soak in lukewarm  water  with juice of one  lemon  for 2 minutes. 
 Melt  ghee / butter  in a large saucepan. 
 Fry  onions ,  chile  and coriander in  ghee  for 3 minutes. 
 Add  bananas  and cover with 2 cups  beef stock . 
 Simmer for 30 – 35 minutes.
