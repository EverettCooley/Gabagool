Ingredients : 
 1 lb.  kidney beans 
 2 medium  onions , peeled & thinly sliced 
 ½ - ¾ lb.  salt  belly of  Pork , cut into 1 inch cubes 
 4 tbsps.  soy sauce 
 2 level tsps. dry  mustard 
 1 level tsp.  salt 
 good shake of  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Wash beans, and cover with  water  overnight. 
 Simmer or pressure cook until tender. 
 Drain, and save the liquor. Heat oven to 300 degrees F. 
 Place beans,  Pork  and  onions  and other seasonings in 2 quart fire proof dish. 
 Add enough liquor to cover and bake for 2 hours until tender.
