Description : 
 This is the Dutch equivalent of eggnog and literally translated means "lawyer". This traditional Dutch beverage is always served during the Christmas season. Serve in a lovely glass and provide the guest with a small spoon for consumption of the beverage.
 
 Ingredients : 
 10  egg yolks 
 pinch of  salt 
 275 grams finely  granulated sugar 
 1Â½ tsp  vanilla extract 
 400 ml  brandy  (brandewijn) 
 Directions : 
 Beat the yolks,  salt  and  sugar  until the mix is creamy and thick. 
 Very slowly pour in the  brandy , but keep on beating the mixture. 
 Pour the mix into a heavy saucepan and heat gently, whisking constantly until the  advocaat  is warm (not hot) and thick. 
 Remove from the heat and pour into a clean bottle. 
 The  advocaat  should be thick enough to scoop with a spoon to eat. 
 It usually is served with  whipped cream . 
 Despite its appearance, advocaat is viewed as a beverage.
