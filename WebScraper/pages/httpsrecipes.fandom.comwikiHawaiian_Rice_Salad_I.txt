Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice , cooled 
 1 20-ounce can  pineapple  tidbits in juice 
 1/4 cup flaked  coconut 
 1/4 cup sliced toasted  almonds 
 1/4 cup chopped  maraschino cherry 
 1/4 cup  sour cream 
 2 teaspoons  Sugar 
 1/4 teaspoon  salt 
 lettuce  leaves 
 1/4 cup sliced toasted  almonds  for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Drain  pineapple , reserving 1/4 cup juice. 
 Combine  rice ,  pineapple ,  coconut ,  almonds  and  cherries  in mixing bowl. 
 Blend  sour cream ,  Sugar ,  salt  and reserved  pineapple juice . 
 Pour over  rice  mixture; toss lightly. 
 Serve on  lettuce  leaves, if desired. Garnish with  almonds .
