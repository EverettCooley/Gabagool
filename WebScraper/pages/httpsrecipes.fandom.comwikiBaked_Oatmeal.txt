 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups  oatmeal 
 ¼ cup  milk powder 
 1  banana , mashed 
 1½ cup hot  water 
 cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Preheat over to 350°F. 
 In a small casserole dish, mix ingredients (except cinnamon) in order listed. 
 Bake 20 minutes. 
 Variation : 
 Replace  banana  with 2 tbsp  raisins  and ¼ cup  coconut  sprinkle with  cinnamon , bake 20 minutes. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving: 
 
 Calories: 418 | pro: 14g | fat: 16g | car: 61g 
 Food exchanges: 2 starch/bread | 1 milk  | 1 fat | 1 fruit
