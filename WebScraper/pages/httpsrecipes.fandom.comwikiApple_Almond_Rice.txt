Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup uncooked  rice 
 ⅓ cup chopped  dried apples 
 ⅓ cup  slivered almonds , toasted 
 1 tablespoon  parsley  flakes 
 1 tablespoon  chicken bouillon  granules 
 ⅔ cup  apple juice 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 1½ cups  water 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  apples ,  almonds ,  parsley , bouillon granules,  apple juice ,  butter  and  water  in 2- to 3-quart saucepan. Bring to a boil; stir once or twice. 
 Lower heat to simmer; cover with tight-fitting lid. Cook 15 minutes or until liquid is absorbed. 
 Fluff with fork.
