 Description : 
 4 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 7-1/2-ounce can salmon, drained 
 4  green onions , trimmed and sliced diagonally 
 2 tablespoons reduced-calorie soy sauce 
 1 red pepper, trimmed and sliced 
 1 cup snow  Peas 
 1 cup sliced  Mushrooms 
 2 cups cooked  rice 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Flake salmon; set aside. 
 
 Spray non-stick pan or wok with  cooking spray  and heat over medium heat. Add  onions , soy sauce and  pepper ; toss. Cover and cook 2 minutes. Add salmon,  Peas  and  Mushrooms ; toss. Cover and cook 1 minute. Serve over  rice .
