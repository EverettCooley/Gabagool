 Description : 
 A creamier version of our all time favorite, loaded with fresh  red onion  and roma  tomatoes  and a hint of something different.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 Large Genuine  California avocados 
 3 oz finely diced  red onion 
 1 roma  tomato , finely diced 
 1 Tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 Tbsp Ranch dressing (1T  sour cream  and 1/2 t ranch dressing mix can be substituted) 
 Sprinkle of  garlic powder ,  salt , and  pepper  to taste. 
 Directions : 
 Peel and pit avocados and place in large bowl. 
 With a  Potato  masher or fork, mash until a near creamy substance. 
 Add remaining ingredients and blend well. 
 Cover and chill for 2â€“3 hours
 Serve with tortilla chips, salsa, and  sour cream .
