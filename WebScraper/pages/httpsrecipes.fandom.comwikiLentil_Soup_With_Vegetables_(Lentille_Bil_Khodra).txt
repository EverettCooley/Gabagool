 Ingredients : 
 1 Tablespoon  olive oil 
 1 Pound  Beef  -- cubed 
 1 Small  Onion  -- finely chopped 
 6 Cloves  garlic  -- finely chopped 
 1/2 Teaspoon  salt 
 1/2 Teaspoon  pepper 
 1/2 Teaspoon  cumin 
 1/2 Teaspoon Carawayâ€”ground 
 1/4 Teaspoon  Cayenne 
 1/4 Teaspoon  turmeric 
 1 Large  tomato  -- finely chopped 
 2 Large  carrot  -- finely chopped 
 1 Large  Potato  -- cubed 
 4 Stalks  celery  -- chopped 
 4 Sprigs  parsley  -- chopped 
 3 Cups  water 
 1 Pound  Lentils 
 2 Tablespoons  tomato  Paste 
 Directions : 
 1) Heat  oil  in pan, add  Beef ,  Onion  and  garlic . Saute for 5 minutes. 
 2) Add  salt ,  pepper ,  cumin , caraway,  Cayenne , and  turmeric . Cook for 5 minutes. 
 3) Add  tomato ,  carrots ,  Potato ,  celery ,  parsley  and  water . Bring to a boil and simmer for 15 minutes. 
 4) Add  Lentils  and  tomato  paste, simmer for 1 hour. 
 5) Serve over  rice  or coucous or with bread.
