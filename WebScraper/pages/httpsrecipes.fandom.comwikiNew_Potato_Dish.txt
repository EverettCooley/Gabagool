Description : 
 In Romanian: Mincare de cartofi noi.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1¾ lbs / 750 g  potatoes 
 1 large  onion 
 1 lb / 500 g  tomatoes   or  2 tablespoons  tomato sauce 
 1 teaspoon chopped  parsley  and  dill 
 salt 
 2 tablespoons  oil 
 ½ teaspoon  flour 
 Directions : 
 Fry the chopped  onion  with the  flour  in  oil . 
 Add the peeled, seeded and cut  tomatoes  and  salt . 
 Put the peeled  potatoes  in this sauce, sprinkle the  parsley  and  dill  and simmer at low heat until done.
