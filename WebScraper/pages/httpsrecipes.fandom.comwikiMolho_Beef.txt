Description : 
 This recipe taken from www.Portuguese-recipes.com 
 
 Ingredients : 
 Sandwich steaks or cubed  Steak  ( enough to serve) 
 Liver, optional but adds to the dish 
 3 large  onions 
 1 lb of small  potatoes  or cut up large ones 
 1 lb. of  carrots  cut up in large pieces 
 1 can of stewed  tomatoes 
 1 teaspoon of soy sauce 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 1/4 Tsp. of  garlic powder 
 1/4 tsp. of  allspice 
 Portuguese  allspice 
 Directions : 
 This dish is done in layers. 
 You can layer it by putting the same ingredients in layers or by dividing the ingredients and making several thin layers of each component. 
 Put the  potatoes  and  carrots  in a large pan and cover with  water . 
 Start to cook these. 
 Brown steaks and liver in a separate pan. 
 When they are brown, place over  potatoes  and  carrots . 
 In the pan you used for the steaks brown the  Onion  and place atop of the meats. Add the  tomatoes  over the  Onion . 
 Add a teaspoon of  salt , 1/4 tsp. of  pepper  and a 1/4 of a teaspoon of  garlic powder . 
 Cover and simmer until the  potatoes  and  carrots  are done, about 11/2 to 2 hours.
