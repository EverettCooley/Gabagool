Description : 
 Dressing for spinach salads, from The Bicycle Shop, Santa Monica
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: my old recipes 
 Makes 2½ cups 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  red wine vinegar 
 dash  tarragon 
 1 tbsp  Dijon mustard 
 2 cups  peanut oil 
 salt ,  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Bring  vinegar  and  tarragon  to boil. 
 Cool. 
 Blend  mustard  and cooled vinegar/tarragon mixture in deep bowl. 
 Stir well. 
 Gradually add  oil  beating constantly until well mixed. 
 Season to taste with  salt  and  pepper .
