Panera  bread  Balsamic Dipping  oil 
 
 1cup Aged  balsamic vinegar 
 3cups Extra-Virgin  olive oil 
 1/4teaspoon  garlic , Minced 
 1/4cup Pecorino or  pepper , to Taste 
 
In a Mixing Bowl, Combine All Ingredients And Whip Thoroughly Until AllIngredients Are Fully Incorporated. Stored in an Airtight Container AndRefrigerated, This Mixture Should Last at Least 6 Months.Use as a Dipping Sauce For Any Panera  bread .
