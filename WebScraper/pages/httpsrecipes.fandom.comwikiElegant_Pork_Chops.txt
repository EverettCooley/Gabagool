Ingredients : 
 2 rib  Pork  chops 
 1 med  Onion  sliced 
 2 tbsp.  flour 
 1/2 can  tomato  paste 
 1/4 cup  red wine 
 1 cup  chicken broth 
 1/4 tsp granulated  garlic 
 1/4 tsp orgeano or  basil 
 Directions : 
 salt  &  pepper   Pork  chops & sprinkle with  flour . 
 Brown chops with  butter  in frying pan. Remove chops & set aside. 
 Add  onions  with  butter  &  flour . Fry until transluscent. 
 Add  tomato  paste. Stir well. Add  red wine  & stir until reduced by half. 
 Return chops to sauce & add spices. Simmer. The longer you simmer, the more tender the chops. 
 Serve over fresh  linguine . Garnish with  Parmesan cheese .
