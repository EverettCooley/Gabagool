Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: my old recipes. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 bottle  chablis , chilled 
 1 split (6 oz)  champagne , chilled 
 1 sliced  lemon , slightly squeezed 
 ice cubes 
 strawberries 
 Directions : 
 Mix  chablis ,  champagne ,  lemon  and  ice  cubes. 
 Pour into large wine glasses filled with  ice . 
 Garnish with a  strawberry .
