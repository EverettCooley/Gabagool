 Directions : 
For potato lovers! When it comes to real vegetables, just serve up a red-hot treat with other main dishes, side dishes, or appetizers. This is one savory vegetable dish the family will not forget.       Skillet-fried Ranch Potatoes     
 Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp.  olive oil 
 4 cups medium  red potatoes , diced 
 Â¼ cup chopped  onion 
 1 envelope ranch dip mix  or  (5 tablespoons ranch dressing) 
 Directions : 
 Heat oil in a large skillet over moderate-high heat. 
 Slowly add potatoes and onions to the hot oil. Cover the pan with a lid. 
 Turn diced potatoes with a spatula every 2 to 3 minutes or until golden brown. 
 After 6 minutes of cooking, stir-fry the ranch dip mix (or the ranch dressing) into the potatoes. 
 Cook another 6 to 8 minutes, turning potatoes every 2 to 3 minutes. Serve hot and plain or with any side dish, main dish, or appetizer. 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Servings:  4 to 6
