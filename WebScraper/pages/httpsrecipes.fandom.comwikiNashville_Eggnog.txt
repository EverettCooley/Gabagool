Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 32 oz prepared dairy  eggnog 
 6 oz  Bourbon 
 3 oz  brandy 
 3 oz  Jamaica rum 
 Directions : 
 Combine in large punch bowl and serve. 
 Sprinkle  nutmeg  on top of each serving.
