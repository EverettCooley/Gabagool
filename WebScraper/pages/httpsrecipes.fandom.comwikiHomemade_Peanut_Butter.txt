 Description : 
 Purchased from the Wells Estate in Canton, Texas in 1987. Notation on card indicates this came from a 1940's cookbook. Exact source and date unknown.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup salted cocktail  peanuts 
 1 tablespoon  peanut oil 
 Â½ teaspoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Place ingredients in an electric blender. 
 Blend until mixture becomes paste-like. 
 It may be necessary to add more  peanut oil . 
 The ingredients must be blended for several minutes. 
 Store in tightly covered container. 
 Homemade  Peanut   butter  will separate on standing. 
 Stir before using.
