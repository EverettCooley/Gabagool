Ingredients : 
 8  Eggs 
 1 pound  Sugar 
 1 scant pound  Flour 
 1 teaspoonful  baking powder 
 pinch of  salt 
 1 tablespoonful anise-seed 
 Directions : 
 Beat  eggs  and  Sugar  together three minutes 
 Then add  anise ,  flour  mixed with powder 
 Roll out thin. 
 Cut in fancy shapes and bake on flat tins in quick oven.
