Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 12/2/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4 boneless  pork chops 
 2 tbsp  honey 
 2 tbsp spicy  mustard 
 ¼ cup  bread crumbs 
 ¼ cup crushed butter  crackers 
 2 tbsp  butter 
 Directions : 
 Pound pork chops to about ⅛" thickness, between waxed paper. 
 In a small bowl, combine honey and mustard; mix well. 
 In a dish combine bread crumbs and cracker crumbs. 
 With a pastry brush, generously coat both sides of pork chops with the honey -mustard sauce. 
 Coat in crumbs. 
 In a heavy skillet, melt butter over medium-high heat. 
 Cook pork 4 minutes per side until crisp and lightly browned.
