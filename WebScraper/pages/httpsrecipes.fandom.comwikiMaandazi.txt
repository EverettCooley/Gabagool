 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 ¼ cup  flour 
 2 Tbsp.  Sugar 
 1 teaspoon  baking powder 
 1 teaspoon ground  cardamom 
 Pinch of  salt 
 1  egg 
 ¼ cup  milk 
 oil  for frying 
 Directions : 
 Mix together dry ingredients and mix in  egg  and  milk  until well mixed. 
 Roll out as for a jellyroll to about ¾" thickness. 
 Cut using a doughnut cutter with center ring removed or a glass turned upside down. 
 Fry in hot (365 °F)  oil  until golden brown. 
 Makes about 1 dozen
