Onion   Cheese  Points
 
 6 Slices White  bread , Crusts Removed. The Best  bread  to Use is The Soft, Spongy Unsung Loaf. Roll Slices Flat With a Rolling Pin And Cut on The Diagonal Into Triangles. 
 4 T. Melted  butter 
 4 T. Grated  Parmesan cheese 
 3/4 Cup Grated  Swiss cheese 
 1/3 Cup Finely Chopped  green onions 
 
Brush  bread  With Melted  butter  on Both Sides. Bake in a 350 Oven For 10 to 12 Minutes or Until  bread  is Crisp And Just Beginning to Color. Toss Together The Remaining Ingredients And Sprinkle Mixture on The Brad. When Ready to Serve, Bake in a 350 Oven For About 5 Minutes or Until  Cheese  is Melted. Serve at Once. Serves 6
 Source: With Love From Darling's Kitchen
