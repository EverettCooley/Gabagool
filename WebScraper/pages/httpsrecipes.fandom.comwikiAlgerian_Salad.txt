 Description : 
 A wonderful salad loaded with flavor
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 sweet  red peppers , seeded and chopped fine 
 4 medium ripe  tomatoes , chopped 
 3/4 cup sliced  cucumber 
 2 small  onions , sliced thin 
 1/2 cup  black olives , pitted and halved 
 6 anchovy fillets, chopped 
 2 hard boiled  eggs , quartered 
 1 tsp chopped fresh  basil  or  cilantro 
 3 tbsp olive  oil 
 1 tbsp  vinegar 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 1) Place all ingredients into a large salad bowl and toss gently.
 2) May be served with pita bread.
