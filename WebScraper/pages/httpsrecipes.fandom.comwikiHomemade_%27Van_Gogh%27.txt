 Prep Time: 2 minutes 
 Cook time: N/A 
 Serves: As much as you'd like 
 
 Description : 
 A home made 'Van Gogh' style alcohol with coffee and vanilla flavoring.
 
 Ingredients : 
 Vodka  (it can be lower quality as you will flavor it) 
 Vanilla seeds 
 Coffee  seeds 
 Simple syrup  (sugar and water... google it!) 
 Directions : 
 Put vodka, vanilla and coffee beans into a jar with a lid. 
 Allow to sit for 2 – 3 days 
 Strain vodka extracting coffee beans and vanilla 
 Add simple syrup 
 Allow to sit for 2 – 3 days 
 Put in a martini, coffee, whatever! 
 Get drunk.
