cherries  Jubilee
 
 2 Lbs. Pitted Bing  cherries 
 1 C.  sugar 
 1/8 Tsp.  salt 
 2 Tbsp.  cornstarch 
 1 1/2 C.  water 
 2 Pts.  vanilla   Ice cream 
 1/4 C.  brandy 
 Square Toasted  bread  Cubes
 
Combine The  sugar ,  salt ,  cornstarch  And  water . Add ThePitted  cherries  And Cook Until Thickened, Stirring
 Constantly. Soak  bread  Squares in  brandy . Scoop VeryHard  vanilla   Ice cream  Into Individual Dessert Cups.Pour Jubilee Mixture Over  Ice cream , Place BrandiedBread on Top And Ignite And Serve.
 
Enjoy.
