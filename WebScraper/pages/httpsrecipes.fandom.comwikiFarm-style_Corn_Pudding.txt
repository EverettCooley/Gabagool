From my collection. This was a magazine clipping but I'm not sure which magazine or when I cut it out. This is very good. Another tried and true keeper.
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cup cream-style  corn 
 1 can whole kernel  corn 
 1 pickled  jalapeño pepper , diced 
 2 teaspoons  pimentos , diced 
 1 cup medium fine dry  bread crumbs 
 1 cup  milk 
 2 tablespoon chopped  green pepper 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  pepper 
 4 ounces sliced  cheddar  cheese cut into 1½" squares 
 3 slices  bacon  cut in 1" strips 
 Directions : 
 Combine  corn , jalapeño, pimentos, bread crumbs,  milk ,  green pepper ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Pour into a buttered baking dish. 
 Arrange alternate pieces of  cheese  and  bacon  across top in checkerboard fashion. 
 Bake at 325°F for one hour.
