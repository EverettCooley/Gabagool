strawberry   vanilla  Shortcake
 
 12 oz Pkg Pound Cake, Cut Into 12 Slices 
 1 1/2 Cups  milk 
 Small Pkg  vanilla  Instant Pudding
 
 1 1/2 Cups Thawed  Cool Whip  ( whipped cream ) 
 3 Cups Sliced  strawberries 
 1 1/2 Cups Thawed  strawberry   Cool Whip 
 
Cut Each Cake Slice Diagonally in Half to Form 2 Triangles, Place 1 Cake Piece on Each of 12 Dessert Plates. Pour  milk  Into Bowl. Add Pudding Mix, Beat 2 minutes. or Until Blended. Stir in Regular Whipping Topping. Spoon 2 Tbs of The  strawberries  Over Each Cake Piece, Top With 1/4 Cup of Pudding Mixture And a Second Cake Piece. Top With Remaining  strawberries  And  strawberry  Whipped Topping. Serve Immediatley. Makes 12 Servings.*
