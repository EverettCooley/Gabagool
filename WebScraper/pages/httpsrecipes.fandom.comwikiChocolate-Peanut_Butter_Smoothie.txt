 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  chocolate   soy milk , chilled 
 1 small  banana , sliced and frozen 
 2 Tbs. smooth natural  Peanut   butter 
 1/2 cup crushed  ice  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 In blender, combine  soy milk ,  banana  and  Peanut   butter . Blend until smooth and creamy. With blender running, add  ice  if using and blend until almost smooth. Serve right away.
