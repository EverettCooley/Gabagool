Description : 
 A nice smoothie.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 or 3 cups  strawberries 
 ½ tsp  almond extract 
 ½ tsp  orange extract 
 2 quarts frozen  lemonade 
 orange juice  (optional) 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Mix in a blender and serve immediately.
