Description : 
 This recipe taken from  http://www.portuguese-recipes.com/Apple%20Crisp%20Recipe.htm 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups sliced peeled  Granny Smith apples 
 2/3 cup  dark brown sugar 
 1/2 cup  flour 
 1/2 cup  Oats 
 3/4 teaspoon  cinnamon 
 3/4 teaspoon  nutmeg 
 1/3 cup softened  butter 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 375 degrees. 
 Grease an 8x8x2 pan and place  Granny Smith apple  slices in the bottom of the pan. 
 Mix the remaining ingredients together and sprinkle evenly over the top of the  Apples . 
 Bake for 30 minutes.
