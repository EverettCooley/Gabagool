This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Henry Estate in Dallas, Texas in 1980.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ pounds  Fuji apples  peeled halved and cored 
 ½ cup minced  green onions 
 2 tablespoons minced  jalapenos 
 ½ cup fresh  lemon juice 
 2 tablespoons  honey 
 4 teaspoons finely grated  lemon peel 
 Directions : 
 Coarsely grate  apples  into strainer set over large bowl. 
 Press and turn  apples  to drain off excess juices. 
 Transfer  apples  to medium bowl then stir in  green onions  and jalapenos. 
 Whisk  lemon juice ,  honey  and  lemon peel  in small bowl to blend then mix into  apples  with salt and  pepper . 
 Cover and chill until ready to use.
