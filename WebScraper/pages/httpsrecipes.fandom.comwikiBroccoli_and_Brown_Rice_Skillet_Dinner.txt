Broccoli  And  brown rice  Skillet Dinner
 
 1 Skinless, Boneless  Chicken  Breast, Diced 
 1/2 Tsp. Minced  garlic 
 1 1/2 C. No-Added- salt   chicken broth 
 1 Small Head of  Broccoli , Broken Into Florets 
 1 red pepper, Cut Into Thin Strips 
 1  yellow squash , Sliced Thin 
 1/4 Tsp.  black pepper 
 1 1/2 C. Instant  brown rice , Dry 
 1 Oz. Freshly Grated  Parmesan cheese 
 In a Deep Skillet, Heat  Chicken ,  garlic ,
 Broth,  Broccoli , red pepper And  Squash  to Boiling.Stir in  pepper  And  brown rice ,
 And Reduce Heat to a Simmer.
 Simmer Uncovered For 5 Minutes.
 Sprinkle  Parmesan cheese  on Top And Serve.
