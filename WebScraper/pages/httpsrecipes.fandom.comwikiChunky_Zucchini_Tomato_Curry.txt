Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Source:  Diabetes Cookbook for Dummies 
 Yield: 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp  olive oil 
 1 medium  red onion , finely diced 
 2 tsp grated fresh  ginger 
 4 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1 tsp  ground coriander 
 2 tsp  curry powder 
 1 cup canned crushed  tomatoes 
 1 lb  zucchini , quartered lengthwise and large diced 
 Directions : 
 Heat the olive oil in a large nonstick skillet. 
 Saute the onion, ginger, and garlic for about 5 minutes, or until the onions are translucent. 
 Add the coriander and curry powder. 
 Continue cooking 1 minute. 
 Stir in the tomatoes and zucchini. 
 Simmer approximately 10 minutes, or until the zucchini is tender.
