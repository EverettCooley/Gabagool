Ingredients : 
 1 cup plain low-fat  yogurt 
 1 pack  chocolate pudding mix , low-fat (4) 
 2 medium  bananas , peeled 
 ½ tsp  lemon juice 
 ½ oz  pistachios , coarsely chopped 
 ¼ cup thawed dairy  whipped topping 
 4 Bing  cherries , fresh  [1] 
 Directions : 
 In blender,process  yogurt  on high speed for 30 seconds, add pudding mix and process 1 minute, scraping down sides of container as necessary. 
 Cut each  banana  into 6 diagonal slices and sprinkle with  lemon juice . 
 Arrange 2  banana  quarters into each of 4 goblets or dessert dishes, top with ¼ pudding mixture. 
 Sprinkle each portion with ¼ of the pistachios, then top each with 1 tablespoon whipped topping and 1  cherry . 
 Notes : 
 
 ↑   If Bing  cherries  are not available, use 4  maraschino cherries .
