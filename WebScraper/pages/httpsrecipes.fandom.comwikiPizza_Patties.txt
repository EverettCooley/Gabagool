Ingredients : 
 29 oz can red kidney beans, rinsed 
 1 egg 
 ¼ cup dried bread crumbs 
 1½ tsp Italian seasoning 
 marinara sauce 
 Parmesan cheese, grated 
 Directions : 
 Mash rinsed red kidney beans from a 29 oz can. 
 Mix with a egg, ¼ cup dried bread crumbs and 1½ tsp Italian seasoning 
 Make 4 patties. 
 Pan fry until hot and crusty. 
 Top with heated marinara sauce and grated Parmesan cheese.
