 Ingredients : 
 1  Chicken  breast...cooked. 
 Cut into bite size pieces 
 2 Avocados....peeled, pitted and smoothed in blender. 
 8 oz of low fat or no fat  sour cream . 
 Chopped  garlic ....as much as you like. 
 2 oz chopped  black olives . 
 Directions : 
 Saute  garlic  in very little  oil . 
 Add blended avocados,  sour cream ,  black olives  and  Chicken . 
 Heat over low heat
 Serve over cooked  penne .
