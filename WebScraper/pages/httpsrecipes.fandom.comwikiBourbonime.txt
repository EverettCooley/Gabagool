Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz  bourbon whiskey 
 7 oz  Coca-Cola ® 
 ¼  lime 
 Directions : 
 Pour the  bourbon whiskey  into a highball glass half-filled with  ice  cubes. 
 Add the juice of the  lime  and fill with coke. 
 Garnish with a  lime  wedge and serve.
