Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Lynch Estate in Garland, Texas in 1994.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup grated  horseradish 
 ½ cup  white vinegar 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Wash and peel horseradish roots and grate or chop in blender. 
 Combine ingredients and pack into jars. 
 Seal tightly. 
 Store in refrigerator.
