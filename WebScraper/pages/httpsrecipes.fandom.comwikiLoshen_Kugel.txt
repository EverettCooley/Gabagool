Description : 
 Noodle pudding. Calories per serving: 225
 
 Ingredients : 
 2  eggs 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 ¼ tsp ground  nutmeg 
 2½ cup cooked broad  noodles 
 1 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 1 cup unsweetened  apple juice 
 ½ cup  raisins 
 ¼ cup chopped  walnuts  or  pecans 
 Directions : 
 Beat the  eggs  and  sugar  until fluffy. 
 Add the remaining other ingredients, except the  nuts . 
 Pour into a well oiled 2 qt casserole dish or an 8 inch baking pan. 
 Sprinkle on the  nuts . 
 Bake in a 350°F oven for 40 to 50 minutes or until browned.
