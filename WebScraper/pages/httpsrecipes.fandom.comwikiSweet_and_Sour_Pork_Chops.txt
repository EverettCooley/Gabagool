Sweet And Sour  Pork  Chops
 
 6  Pork  Chops 
 1 Tbsp Dijon  mustard 
 1 c  water 
 1/2 c  ketchup 
 3 Tbsp  brown sugar 
 4 Tbsp  white vinegar 
 1 Tbsp Worchestershire Sauce 
 salt  And  pepper  to Taste
 
Pre-Heat Oven to 350. Place  Pork  Chops in a Baking Dish. Mix AllIngredients in a Bowl And Pour Over  Pork  Chops. Cover With Foil AndSlice Holes in It. Place in Oven And Cook For 1-1/2 Hrs.
