About ground turkey : 
 A product consisting of ground white or dark  turkey meat , or a combination of both. It contains approximately 65 to 75 percent less fat than  ground beef . It can be used to make sausages and patties and can be used as a substitute for ground beef in any recipe calling for ground beef. Ground turkey is available fresh or frozen, in patties, 1 pound packages, and in bulk.
