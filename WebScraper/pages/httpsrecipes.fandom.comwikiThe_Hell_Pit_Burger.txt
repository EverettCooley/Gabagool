 Prep Time: 5-10 Minutes 
 Cook time: 5-10 Minutes 
 Serves: 1 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 lb. beef 
 1 tbs. horse radish 
 1 sliced habrenero pepper 
 2 sliced jalepenos 
 1 tbs. beer (or spicy brown) mustard 
 1 cup thickly sliced lettuce 
 1 slice pepperjack cheese 
 1 cup chopped onion 
 1 tsp. chili powder ( to be added to beef before cooking) 
 1 seasame seeded bun 
 1 16 oz. milk. 
 Directions : 
 Knead 1 tsp. of chili powder into beef. 
 Cook beef for 5-10 minutes, or until well done. 
 Toast bun. 
 Spread 1 tsp. horse radish on top half of bun. 
 Spread 1 tbs. mustard on bottom half of bun. 
 Place 1 slice pepperjack cheese ontop of bottom half of bun. 
 Place cooked beef ontop of bottom half. 
 Place jalepenos, habreneros, onions, and lettuce ontop of beef. 
 Have the milk at-the-ready, because you're gonna need it! --  Kingatemu  10:01, February 22, 2010 (UTC)
