Description : 
 You can place this mixture in either a Kong or a clean, sterilized marrow bone.

 Ingredients : 
 1 cup of regular dry dog food or biscuits. 
 ½ cup  peanut butter . 
 Kong or marrow bone. 
 Directions : 
 Take 1 cup of their regular dry dog food or biscuits. 
 Grind in a food processor or with a  meat tenderizer . 
 Take the resulting treat bits and add ½ cup  Peanut   butter . 
 Place mixture in a Kong or marrow bone. In the summer you can freeze it for ½ hour to get it nice and cool for your pups.
