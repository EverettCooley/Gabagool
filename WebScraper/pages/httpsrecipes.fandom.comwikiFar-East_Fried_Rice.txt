Ingredients : 
 8 oz  tofu  or  tempeh , steamed for 10 minutes and cooled 
 1 tbsp  oil 
 ½ cup  brown rice 
 1½ cup  water 
 2 tbsp  sesame seeds 
 ¼ cup  onions , sliced 
 ½ cup  celery , sliced 
 4  spinach  leaves, shredded 
 1  egg  (optional) 
 4 tsp  tamari 
 ¼ cup  green onions , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Cut the steamed  tofu  or  tempeh  into julienne strips. 
 Simmer for 45 minutes until liquid is absorbed. 
 Heat oil in wok or skillet. 
 Simmer rice in water for 45 minutes until liquid is absorbed. 
 Add sesame seeds and brown lightly. 
 Add and brown the  tofu  /  tempeh . 
 Stir in the onions, celery and spinach. 
 Saute a few minutes, add the cooked  rice . 
 For extra protein you can can make well in center of pan and drop in an egg. 
 Cook a little, then mix  egg  with  rice  and vegetables mix all ingredients together then add tamari. 
 Sprinkle green onions on top to serve. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving: 
 
 pro: 14gm | fat: 13gm | car: 36gm 
 Food exchanges 4 meats + 2 starch/breads + 2 fats
