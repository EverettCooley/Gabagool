Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 2½ cups 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup raspberry  sorbet 
 ½ cup lemonade 
 1 cup frozen unsweetened  strawberries , not thawed 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 6 ice cubes 
 Directions : 
 Whirl sorbet and lemonade in a blender to combine. 
 Add strawberries and sugar. 
 With blender running, add ice cubes, one at a time, until mixture is thick and smooth. 
 Pour into glasses and garnish with whole raspberries.
