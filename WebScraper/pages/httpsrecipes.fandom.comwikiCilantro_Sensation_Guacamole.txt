 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 5 large ripe Hass  California avocados 
 1 medium  garlic  clove, pressed 
 1 Tbsp  lemon  juice, freshly squeezed 
 1 Pound fresh  cilantro , chopped 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ¼ Cup sour  cream 
 ¼ Cup  Mayonnaise 
 ¼ tsp  Tabasco sauce 
 Directions : 
 Mash ripe avocados with pastry blender or fork. 
 Add remaining ingredients and mix. 
 Serve garnished with additional  cilantro  if desired.
