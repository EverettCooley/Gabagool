Honey  And Dijon Marinade For Vegetables
 
 1/4 Cup  olive oil 
 1 Tablespoon Tops  Honey 
 2 Tablespoons  balsamic vinegar 
 1/2 Teaspoon  Tabasco 
 2 Tablespoons Tops Dijon  mustard 
 Fresh Ground  pepper  to Taste
 
Combine All The Marinade Ingredients in a Medium Bowl And Blend Well.Select The Vegetables You Wish to Marinate.
 Cut Into Appropriate Sized Pieces For Grilling.
 Add The Vegetables to The Marinade And Cover For One Hour.Remove The Vegetables And Grill.
 
Makes 1 Cup
