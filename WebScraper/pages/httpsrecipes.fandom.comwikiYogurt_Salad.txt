 Ingredients : 
 1 medium  carrot  cut into cubes 
 2. 1 medium  Potato  cut into cubes 
 3. 1/4 cup shelled  Peas 
 4. 1/4 cup shredded French beans 
 5. 1/2 cup fresh  yogurt  (curd) 
 6. 1 tablespoon of each  mint  and corainder leaves (finely chopped) 
 7. 1 teaspoon  chilli powder 
 8. 1 teaspoon  pepper  powder 
 9. 1/2 teaspoon roasted  cumin  seed powder 
 10. 1/2 teaspoon  garam masala 
 11.  salt  to taste 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Boil all the vegetables. Beat the  yogurt  (curd) and add vegetables, along with the rest of the ingredients. Garnish with  mint  and  coriander leaves . Chill and serve
