 Ingredients : 
 1 shot Stolichnaya  vodka 
 0.5 shot Noilly Prat  sweet vermouth 
 1 teaspoon De Kuyper  Grenadine 
 Squeeze of  lime 
 Juice of 1 medium sized  orange 
 Directions : 
 Pour all the ingredients into a cocktail shaker and shake. Strain into the glass, then garnish with a slice of  orange  and a  maraschino cherry .
