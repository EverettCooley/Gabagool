 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4  eggs —room temp 
 1/4 cup  ricotta cheese 
 1/4 teaspoon  cream  of 
 tartar
 
 Parmesan cheese —little or a lot - whatever 
 Directions : 
 Set oven to 300. Separate CAREFULLY your  eggs .
Beat your whites until stiff peaks form, add  cream  of
tartar, keep beating till really stiff. Mix yolks, and cheeses together, add a little of your white to the yolk mixture . Slowly fold in your yolk mixture, trying not to break the whites down, streaky is okay. Pour in 6 globs on a greasy cookie sheet or in a greased muffin top pan. Bake for 35-40.
