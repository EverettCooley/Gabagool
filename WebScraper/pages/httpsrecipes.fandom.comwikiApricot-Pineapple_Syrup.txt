Ingredients : 
 1 cup  dried apricots 
 1 cup  water 
 1 cup unsweetened  Pineapple juice 
 1 cup  water 
 Directions : 
 In small saucepan covered with lid, boil then simmer first two ingredients for approximately 25 minutes. 
 Remove from stove and allow mixture to cool. 
 Whip ingredients in blender or processor (mixture will be a thick pur√©e). 
 Add  pineapple juice  and  water  to mixture and whip again. 
 Return syrup to saucepan and heat on low to desired temperature. 
 Serve apricot- pineapple  syrup warm. 
 Make-ahead Tip : 
 Place syrup in tightly covered container and store in refrigerator up to one month. To serve, reheat on low to desired temperature.
