Ingredients : 
 1 package Sugar cookie dough 
 marshmallow creme 
 any kind of fruit you like 
 Directions : 
 Roll out the cookie dough till it fits a cookie sheet then put in the oven til golden brown. Remove from oven and let cool. 
 Cover with marshmallow creme 
 Top with fruit 
 Refrigerate until ready to serve.
