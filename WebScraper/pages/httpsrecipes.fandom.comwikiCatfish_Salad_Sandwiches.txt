Description : 
 A  Catfish  recipe. 
 
 Serves 6 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup low-fat  ricotta cheese 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 1 ½ cups chopped cooked  catfish  (about ½ pound) 
 ¼ cup finely chopped  celery 
 2 tablespoons finely shredded  carrot 
 2 tablespoons low-fat  ranch salad dressing 
 salt  and  pepper , to taste 
 6 whole wheat  hamburger buns 
 6  tomato  slices 
 1½ cups shredded  lettuce 
 Directions : 
 Place  cheese  and  lemon juice  in a blender. 
 Cover and blend for about 30 seconds or until smooth. 
 Mix  cheese  mixture, fish,  celery , dressing,  salt , and  pepper . 
 Place ½ cup mixture on each bun bottom, and add  tomato  and  lettuce . 
 Top with bun top.
