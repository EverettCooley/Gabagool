 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 kg,  green onions  (chopped) - 15-20 pcs, 
 rice  - 1  coffee  cup, 
 parsley  (chopped) - 1 table spoon, 
 Hogweed (chopped) - 1 table spoon, 
 sunflower oil  - 1  coffee  cup, 
 black pepper  - a tea spoon, 
 salt  - for a taste 
 Directions : 
 Cut the meat into small pieces. 
 Mix the pieces with the  onions , the  rice , the  black pepper , the  parsley  and hogweed, the  oil  and  salt . #Put the mixture in oiled paper and wrap it as a parcel. 
 Put the parcel paper ends downwards in a small baking dish, pour a finger of  water  and bake it at a moderate temperature in an oven for 2–3 hours. 
 Serve with lattuce salad.
