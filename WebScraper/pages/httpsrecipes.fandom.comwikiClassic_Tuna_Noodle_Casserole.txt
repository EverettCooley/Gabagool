Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can (10 3/4 oz) condensed cream of celery soup 
 1/2 cup  milk 
 1 cup cooked  peas 
 2 tbsp chopped  pimiento  (optional) 
 2 cans (about 6oz)  tuna , drained and flaked 
 2 cups hot cooked medium  egg noodles 
 2 tbsp dry  bread crumbs 
 1 tbsp  butter  or  margarine , melted 
 Directions : 
 Mix soup, milk, peas, pimiento (if you wish so), tuna and noodles in a casserole dish. Bake this for 20 minutes at 400Â°F. Once done, stir and set aside. 
 Take the bread crumbs and mix it with butter. Sprinkle this on top of the casserole. Bake again for 5 minutes or until it is hot.
