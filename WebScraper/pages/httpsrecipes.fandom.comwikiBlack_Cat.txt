 Ingredients : 
 1 shot De Kuyper  cherry brandy 
 1 shot Stolichnaya  vodka 
 2 shots Cola 
 2 shots  cranberry juice 
 Glass: Highball
 
 Directions : 
 Pour ingredients into highball glass with some cubed  ice  and stir well.
