Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves 6 
 Ingredients : 
 6  New York strip steaks 
 Marinade : 
 1 cup  honey 
 2 cups  molasses 
 1 cup  bourbon 
 2 tbsp  crushed red pepper 
 1 tbsp ground  ginger 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 Directions : 
 Blend all marinade ingredients thoroughly. 
 Add the steaks and marinate 2 hours in refrigerator. 
 When ready to cook, remove steaks and pat dry with paper towels. 
 Grill to desired doneness. 
 Enjoy.
