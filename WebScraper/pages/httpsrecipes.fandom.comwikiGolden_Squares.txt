Ingredients : 
 2 c. firmly packed  graham cracker  crumbs 
 6 oz. pkg. semi-sweet  chocolate pieces 
 1/2 c. coarsely chopped nut meats 
 1 can  sweetened condensed milk 
 Directions : 
 Mix together graham cracker crumbs, chocolate pieces and nut meats. 
 Blend in condensed milk. 
 Pour into 8 x 8 x 2 inch square pan which has been greased and bottom lined with waxed paper and greased again. 
 Bake in moderate oven (350 degrees) until top is golden brown, about 35 minutes. 
 Remove immediately; remove waxed paper. 
 Cool. 
 Cut into 2 inch squares.
