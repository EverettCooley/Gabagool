      Banana Ice Cream     
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups whole  milk 
 1 tbsp  custard powder 
 2  egg yolks 
 ½ cup sweetened  condensed milk 
 ¾ cup  evaporated milk 
 3  bananas  pureed 
 Chocolate Sauce 
 Waffle Cones 
 Directions : 
 Mix  custard powder  to a paste with a little of the  milk . 
 Bring remainder of  milk  to boil and stir in paste and beaten  egg  yolks. 
 Cook over low fire for 3 minutes. 
 Stir until cool, then mix in condensed and  evaporated milk  and  banana  puree. 
 Set to freeze. 
 When very firm pack in container lined with wax paper; leave edge to overhang. 
 Cover. 
 Store in freezer.
