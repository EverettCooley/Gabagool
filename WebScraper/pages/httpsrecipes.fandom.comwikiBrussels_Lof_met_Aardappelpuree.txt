Description : 
 Chicory with mashed potatoes. This lovely dish gets the best of the  chicory  taste, and makes a lovely fresh and crunchy dish 
-- CorineJ  13:00, 23 Mar 2006 (PST)
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ kg of  chicory 
 1½ kg freshly boiled  potatoes 
 2 or more  hard-boiled eggs 
 Directions : 
 Clean and chop the  chicory  and wash several times in clean  water . 
 Leave to drain, then put in a clean cloth, pick up the 4 corners and swing to get all the  water  out. 
 Make mashed  potatoes  the usual way. 
 Put a layer of mashed  potatoes  and a layer of  chicory  in a dish, make sure the top layer is mashed  potato . 
 Put the halved hard boiled  eggs  on top and serve as quickly as possible.
