Mom's House Salad
 
 3 T. Extra Virgin  olive oil 
 1 T.  balsamic vinegar 
 1/2 T. Dijon  mustard 
 1/2 T. Pressed Fresh  garlic 
 2 Dashes of  worcestershire sauce 
 Generous Pinch of  salt 
 
 6 Cups  lettuce , Torn  butter   lettuce  or  arugula , Mixed Greens or Baby  spinach 
 1/4 Cup Toasted  walnuts , Optional 
 1/2  avocado , Skinned And Sliced or Cubed, Optional 
 1 Medium  tomato , Sliced, Optional 
 
Combine The  olive oil , Balsamic,  mustard ,  garlic ,  worcestershire sauce  And  salt . Whisk Until Emulsified. Pour The Dressing Into a Serving Bowl, Add The Rest of The Ingredients, Toss Until The Dressing Coats The  lettuce  And Serve.Source: The Last Minute Party Girl
