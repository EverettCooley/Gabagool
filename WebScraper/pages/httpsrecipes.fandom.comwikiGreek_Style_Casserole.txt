Ingredients : 
 6-8 med.  potatoes 
 2 Tbsp.  butter 
 1 lg. can Campbell's tomato soup 
 salt  And  pepper , to Taste 
 2 lbs.  ground beef 
 1  onion 
 1/2 c.  water 
 White Sauce 
 2 c.  milk 
 3 Tbsp.  butter 
 1/2 c.  velveeta  cheese 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Boil potatoes in skins until almost tender. 
 Peel And Slice. 
 While potatoes are cooking, brown meat. 
 Then drain hamburger 
 Add butter and onion to Hamburger And Slightly Brown onion. 
 Add tomato soup and water to Meat And Simmer 5 Minutes. 
 Melt butter in Small Saucepan Then Mix in flour Gradually Over Low Heat. 
 After Mixed Well, slowly add milk while stirring. 
 Cook Until Thick. 
 Remove From heat and add cheese. 
 Put Into Greased 13 x 9 Pan as Follows: Layer of potatoes, meat, then sauce. Repeat layers. 
 Then Last Put All Remaining Sauce And Top With cheese. 
 Bake in Preheated Oven at 350 Degrees Until Top Lightly Browned, Approximately 35-40 Minutes.
