 Ingredients : 
 ½ Cup of diced  avocado 
 ½ Cup of  red lettuce 
 ½ Cup of  Boston green lettuce 
 ¼ Cup of diced  Granny Smith apple 
 ¼ Cup of smoked cooked  Turkey 
 ¼ Cup of  green onions 
 ¼ Cup of sliced  carrots 
 1 boiled  egg , sliced 
 Kraft ranch dressing to taste and top with fresh black ground  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Wash  lettuce  thoroughly combined all ingredients in bowl and serve.
