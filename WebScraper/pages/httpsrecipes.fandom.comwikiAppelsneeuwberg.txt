Description : 
 "Apple Snow Mountain". A lovely warm dessert.
 
 Submitted by  CorineJ  13:41, 23 Mar 2006 (PST) 
 Ingredients : 
 8 large sweet  apples 
 4 tbsp  honey  or  jam 
 30 g  butter 
 Â¼ liter  custard 
 50gr shredded  coconut 
 2  egg whites 
 50 g  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Wash the  apples , core them, but don't peel them. 
 Fill the cores with the  honey  or jam and  butter  and part of the shredded  coconut . 
 Place in an oven dish, with a little  water  and leave in the middle of a medium warm oven until soft. 
 Pour the warm custard over the  apples  and cover with the beaten  egg  whites. 
 Sprinkle the cocos and  sugar  over the top.
