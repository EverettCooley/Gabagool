Description 
 Skillet Granola  from the  Public Health Cookbook  -- original source of recipe, public domain government resource
 
 Ingredients 
 ⅓ cup  vegetable oil 
 ⅓ cup  brown sugar 
 1 tablespoon  water 
 4 cups dry, quick-cooking  oatmeal 
 1 cup  raisins 
 Directions 
 Stir oil, brown sugar and water together in a cool, large skillet. 
 Stir in oatmeal and mix until coated with sugar and oil. 
 Heat over medium heat. Stir until oatmeal is slightly brown. Stir in raisins. 
 Cool and store in a large jar with a tight fitting lid.
