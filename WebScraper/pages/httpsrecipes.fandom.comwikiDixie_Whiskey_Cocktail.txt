Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ tsp  powdered sugar 
 1 dash  bitters 
 ¼ tsp  triple sec 
 ½ tsp  crème de menthe  (white) 
 2 oz  bourbon 
 Directions : 
 Shake with  ice  and strain into cocktail glass.
