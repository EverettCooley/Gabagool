 Ethnicity - South Indian 
 Type of meal - Party, Lunch, Dinner 
 Ingredients : 
 100 gm: Ash gourd 
 100 gm:  pumpkin 
 4 no: Green chillies (split the edge) 
 1 tbs: Red  gram dal  (soaked in the  water  for about 6 hours) 
 Â½ no:  grated coconut  (Squeeze out 1/4cup: of the  milk  from the  coconut  without adding  water . Take one more cup: of  milk  from the  coconut ) 
 1 tbs:  coconut oil 
 1sprig:  curry leaves 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Remove the covering of the ash gourd and  pumpkin  and cut it into small pieces. 
 Cook the vegetables and red  gram dal  in  water . Add enough  salt  and the green chillies. 
 When it is done, add 1cup  coconut milk  and boil. 
 When it thickens well, add 1/4cup  coconut milk . Bring to a boil and remove from fire. 
 Add 1tbs  coconut oil  and the  curry leaves  and mix well.
