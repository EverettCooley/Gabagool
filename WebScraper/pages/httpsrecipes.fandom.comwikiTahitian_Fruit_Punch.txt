Ingredients : 
 1 (46 ounces) can  pineapple juice , chilled 
 1 (46 ounces) can  apple juice , chilled 
 2 quarts carbonated lemon-lime beverage, chilled 
 1 pint lemon  sherbet  or lime sherbet 
 Directions : 
 In punch bowl, stir together juices and carbonated beverage. 
 Spoon sherbet into bowl. 
 Serve immediately.
