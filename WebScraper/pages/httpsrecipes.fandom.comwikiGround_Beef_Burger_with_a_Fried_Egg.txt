Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Makes about 16 sandwiches 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lb lean  ground beef 
 ½  onion , chopped 
 1 can  tomato sauce 
 ¼ cup  ketchup 
 2 tbsp  yellow mustard 
 2 tbsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 1 tbsp  white vinegar 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 1 egg 
 Directions : 
 In pan, cook beef and onion over medium-high heat until beef is no longer pink and onions are translucent. 
 Drain fat. 
 Add remaining ingredients except salt and pepper; cook over medium-low heat 20 to 30 minutes. 
 Add salt and pepper. 
 Fry an egg in a sauce pan. 
 Serve on hamburger buns with pickle slices, lettuce, tomato, and top with a fried egg. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 96 Calories | 3g Fat | 4g Carbs | 0.5g Fiber | 222mg Sodium
