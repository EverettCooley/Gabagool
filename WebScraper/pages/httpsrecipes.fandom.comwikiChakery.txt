Ingredients : 
 1 pint vanille 
 8 ounces of  sour cream 
 12 ounces of  evaporated milk 
 can of crushed  pineapples 
 dash of  nutmeg 
 splash of  vanilla flavoring 
 couscous 
 Directions : 
 Mix all the ingredients except the  couscous  together to make the sauce. 
 Cook the  couscous  separately with  water . 
 Serve chakery sauce over  couscous  and enjoy!.
