Description : 
 In Romanian: Telina cu maioneza
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 big  celery roots 
 5 oz / 150 g  hot dogs 
 salt 
 mayo 
 pepper 
 tomatoes  (or  pickled peppers ) 
 Directions : 
 Peel and wash the  celery  roots and then grate them. 
 salt  them and let them sit in a sieve for a half hour. 
 Then mix them with the  mayo , the peeled and julienned hot dogs,  pepper ,  salt . 
 Place on a plate and garnish with  tomatoes  or red pickled  peppers .
