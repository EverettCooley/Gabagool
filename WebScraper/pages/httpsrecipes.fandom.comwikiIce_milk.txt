      Ice Milk     
 About ice milk : 
 Ice milk  or iced milk is a frozen dessert with less than 10 percent milk fat and the same sweetener content as ice cream. Ice milk is typically priced lower than ice cream and is typically sold as a generic product.
 Products containing less milk fat but higher sweetener content are sold as  sherbet , and products with no milk fat or dairy analogues are sold as  sorbet . Products which use nonfat or low-fat yogurt or dairy analogues are sold as  frozen yogurt .
