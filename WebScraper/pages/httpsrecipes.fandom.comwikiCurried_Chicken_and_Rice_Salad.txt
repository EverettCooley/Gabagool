 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice , cooled 
 2 1/2 to 3 cups cooked  Chicken , cut into thin strips 
 1 tart red  Apple , cored and chopped 
 1/2 cup seedless  Raisins 
 1/2 cup sliced  celery 
 3/4 cup  Mayonnaise 
 1 teaspoon  curry  powder 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1/2 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 lettuce  leaves 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  Chicken ,  Apple ,  Raisins  and  celery  in large bowl. 
 
 Blend  Mayonnaise ,  curry ,  salt  and  pepper ; toss lightly with  rice  mixture. Chill. Serve on  lettuce  leaves.
