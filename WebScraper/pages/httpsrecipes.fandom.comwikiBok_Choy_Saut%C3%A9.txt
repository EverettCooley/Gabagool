 Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes For Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 From: Light And Easy Diabetes Cuisine 
 Yield: 4 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  bok choy 
 1 tsp virgin  olive oil 
 1  garlic  clove, minced 
 ½ tsp crystalline  fructose 
 1 tsp low-sodium  soy sauce 
 1 tsp  sesame oil 
 Directions : 
 Rinse  bok choy  in cold  water . 
 Cut stalks diagonally in 1-inch pieces and leaves into thin shreds. 
 In a large non-stick skillet, heat  oil . 
 Add the stems first and sauté, tossing, about 2 minutes. 
 Then add  garlic , leaves, fructose ,  soy sauce  and  sesame oil , and continue cooking over high heat 2 minutes more. 
 Nutrition information : 
 Per serving (¼ of recipe):
 
 40 Calories | 2g Fat | 59mg Sodium | 1g Protein | 5g Carbs | 0mg Cholesterol 
 Diabetic Exchanges: 1 vegetable | ½ fat
