Fruit Cocktail Salad
 
 2 Cans (16 Oz. Each) Fruit Cocktail in Juice, Undrained 
 20 Oz. Can Unsweetened  pineapple  Tidbits, Drained 
 11 Oz. Can  mandarin oranges , Drained 
 1 Tbsp.  lemon juice 
 1 Pkg. (1 Oz.) Instant  sugar -Free  vanilla  Pudding Mix 
 2 Med. Firm  bananas , Sliced 
 
 in a Bowl, Combine The Canned Fruit And  lemon juice . Sprinkle With Pudding Mix. Stir Gently For 1 Minute or Until Mixture is Thickened. Fold in  bananas .
 
Refrigerate Until Serving.
 
Makes 12 Servings
 
One Serving Equals 88 Calories, 0 Cholesterol & a Trace of Fat.
