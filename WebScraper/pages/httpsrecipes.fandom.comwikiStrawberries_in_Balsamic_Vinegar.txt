 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1-pint basket California  strawberries , stemmed and halved 
 2 tablespoons  balsamic vinegar 
 2 tablespoons  Sugar 
 
 Directions : 
 In bowl combine ingredients, toss gently. Cover and refrigerate 1 hour. Serve in stemmed glasses. Yields: 4 servings
