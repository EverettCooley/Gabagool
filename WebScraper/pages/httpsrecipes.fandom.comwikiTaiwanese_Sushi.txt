Ingredients : 
 5 bowls steamed  rice 
 5 sheets dried seaweed or  sushi nori 
 2  eggs 
 1 pickled  daikon 
 shredded fried seasoned  pork  - you can put as much as you want 
 seasoning for  rice 
 3 tablespoons  white sugar 
 2 tablespoon  rice vinegar 
 ¾ teaspoon  salt 
 Seasoning for eggs : 
 ¼ teaspoon  rice wine 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ tablespoon  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Cut the pickled  daikon  into thin slices beat the  eggs  with the ingredients for  eggs  and then fry the  eggs  in a round shape. 
 Cut the  eggs  into thin slices. 
 Mix the ingredients for  rice  and put it into microwave for a few second to dissolve the  sugar  into liquid. 
 Then mix the liquid into the steamed  rice . Make sure that it is evenly mixed. 
 Cover the dried seaweed with  rice . 
 Then top it with sliced pickled  daikon , sliced  eggs  and some shredded fried seasoned  pork  (you can put as many toppings as you want as long as the dried seaweed does not explode) 
 Roll up the dried seaweed and cut it into pieces.
