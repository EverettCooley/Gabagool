In Romanian: Mamaliguta la cuptor
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups  milk 
 cornmeal 
 salt 
 ½ lb / 250 g  sheep's cheese  (or other) 
 7 oz / 200 g  butter 
 Directions : 
 Prepare a quick polenta, replacing the  water  with  milk . Add 4 oz/100 g crumbled sheep  Cheese , 4 oz/100 g  butter  and  salt  if needed. 
 Let cool a little, then mix with three beat  eggs . 
 Grease a pan or baking sheet with some  butter , place half of the polenta in, spread the rest of the  Cheese  and small pieces of  butter  on top, then cover with the other half of polenta. 
 Put some more  butter  on top of that and bake for 20 – 30 minutes. Serve hot.
