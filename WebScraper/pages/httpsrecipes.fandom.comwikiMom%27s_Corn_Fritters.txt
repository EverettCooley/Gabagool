Description : 
 I perfected this recipe in the early 1980s when I was a newlywed. My husband and I both like spice, so I added some  peppers  and  onions .
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  corn bread  mix 
 Â½ cup warm  milk 
 1 small can whole kernel  corn 
 1 small can creamed-style  corn 
 2 pickled  jalapeno peppers , chopped 
 1 small white  onion , finely chopped 
 1 cup sharp  cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together and mix with a fork until mixed but not mushy. 
 Dough will be stiff. Drop in hot  oil  in spoonfuls and cook for about 2 minutes.
