 Description : 
 In Romanian: Salata de fasole verde.
 
 Ingredients : 
 28 oz/750 g  green beans , 
 2 tablespoons  oil , 
 2 tablespoons  vinegar , 
 salt , 
 2 minced  garlic  cloves (to taste), 
 parsley 
 Directions : 
 Clean, wash and boil the beans. 
 Drain, place in salad bowl and mix with  oil ,  vinegar ,  salt  and  garlic  to which 2-3 from the beans boiling liquid has been added. 
 Garnish with a few sprigs of  parsley .
