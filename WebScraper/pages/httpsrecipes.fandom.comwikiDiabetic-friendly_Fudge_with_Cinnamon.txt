Ingredients : 
 1 envelope  gelatin 
 ¼ cup  water 
 1 square unsweetened  chocolate 
 ⅛ tsp  cinnamon 
 ¾ tsp liquid food sweetener 
 ¼ cup  water 
 ½ cup  evaporated milk 
 ½ tsp  vanilla 
 ¼ cup chopped  nuts 
 Directions : 
 Soften  gelatin  in ¼ cup  water  for 5 minutes. 
 Melt  chocolate  with  cinnamon  and sweetener; add  milk  and  water  slowly. 
 Add  gelatin . 
 Stir until dissolved. 
 Remove from fire. 
 Add  vanilla , cool. 
 When mixture begins to thicken, add  nuts . 
 Turn into cold pan. 
 When firm cut into pieces.
