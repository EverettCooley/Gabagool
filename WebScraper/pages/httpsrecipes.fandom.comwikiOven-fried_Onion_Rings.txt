Description : 
 Light and tasty treats are particularly sweet with Vidalia onions, but they are good with whatever is available.
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes For Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Source:  The Healthy HomeStyle Cookbook 
 Number of Servings: 4 
 Ingredients : 
 1  vidalia onion , thinly sliced ea 
 1 tbsp reduced-calorie  corn oil 
 2 tbsp  cornmeal 
 2 tbsp fine  breadcrumbs 
 1 tbsp  Parmesan cheese 
 ⅛ tsp  paprika 
 Directions : 
 Peel onion; slice into ¼ inch rings. 
 Sprinkle with oil and toss to coat. 
 Mix dry ingredients, sprinkle over onion rings, and toss to coat evenly. 
 Place on a nonstick baking sheet. 
 Bake at 400°F for 20 minutes or until lightly browned. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per ½ cup (2 oz) serving
 
 Exchanges: 2 Vegetable | 1 Fat 
 Calories 93 (Calories from fat 45) | Total fat 5g (Saturated fat 1g) | Cholesterol 3 mg | Sodium 104 mg | Total Carbohydrate 9 g (Dietary fiber 1g) | Protein 3 g
