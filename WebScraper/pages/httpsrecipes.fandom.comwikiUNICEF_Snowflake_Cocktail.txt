Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 white sugar  crystals 
 2 cups store bought  eggnog , chilled 
 ½ cup  brandy , (Christian Brothers) 
 ½ cup  amaretto  di Saronno 
 ½ cup  vanilla   ice cream 
 1 tbsp ground  nutmeg 
 pumpkin pie spice , for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Rim martini glasses with  sugar  crystals. 
 Combine eggnog,  brandy ,  amaretto ,  ice  cram, and  nutmeg  in a blender; blend until smooth. 
 Pour into glasses. 
 Garnish each cocktail with a pinch of  pumpkin pie spice .
