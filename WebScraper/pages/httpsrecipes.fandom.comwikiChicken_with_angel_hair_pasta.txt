Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4  Chicken  breast—boned and 
 Skinned 
 1/4 Bread crumbs, seasoned 
 2  eggs 
 1 clove  garlic  -- crushed 
 1 tbsp  butter 
 1 tbsp  olive oil 
 1 lbs  angel hair pasta  -- cooked 
 As directed 
 1 Jar good  Pasta  sauce 
 mozzarella   Cheese 
 Cheddar   Cheese 
 Colby   Cheese 
 Directions : 
 1 Pound  Chicken  breasts between plastic until ¼ inch thick. 
2 Heat  butter  and  oil  in large frying pan. 
3 Dip  Chicken  first in scrambled  egg  and then in bread crumbs.melt  butter  in hot frying pan and add  olive oil  and crushed  garlic . 
4 Heat for just a minute and add  Chicken  breasts. 
5 Fry until browned and cooked. 
6 Make nests of cooked  Pasta . 
7 Put some sauce on  Pasta . 
8 Place one cooked  Chicken  breast on and put a small amount of suace on top of that. 
9 Sprinkle the cheeses on the  Chicken  breast and put under broiler until  Cheese  melts
