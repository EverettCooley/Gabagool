 Ingredients : 
 2 oz Barbados  rum 
 1 oz  Jamaica rum 
 ½ oz  apricot brandy 
 ½ oz  falernum  or  sugar 
 2 dashes  angostura bitters 
 juice of 2  lemons  or  limes 
 Directions : 
 Mix well and chill.
