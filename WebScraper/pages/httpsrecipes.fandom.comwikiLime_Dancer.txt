Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 oz  orange juice 
 1 oz  bourbon 
 1 oz  lime cordial 
 1 splash  lime juice 
 1 oz  melon liqueur 
 Directions : 
 Shake ingredients hard together and strain into shot glasses to share.
