       Amish Onion Patties     
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3/4 cup  Self-rising flour 
 1 tablespoon  sugar 
 1 tablespoon  cornmeal  or  panko  bread crumbs or both 
 2 teaspoons  baking powder 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 3/4 cup  milk  or  buttermilk 
 2 1/2 cups  onions ,  chopped 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix together the ingredients and add the milk. 
 Add onions and mix thoroughly. 
 Drop the batter into hot deep fat. 
 Turn them and flatten them into patties after 3 minutes. 
 Fry about 3â€“4 minutes. 
 Drain on paper towels.
