Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source :  Better Homes And Gardens 
 Makes 5 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ pounds lean  ground beef 
 1 x 15-ounce can Italian-style  tomato sauce 
 1 x 4-ounce can sliced  mushrooms , drained 
 ¼ cup sliced pitted ripe  olives  (optional) 
 1 to 1½ cups shredded  mozzarella  cheese (4 to 6 ounces) 
 1 x 10-ounce package refrigerated  biscuits  (10 biscuits) 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 400°F. 
 In a large skillet cook beef until brown. 
 Drain off fat. 
 Stir in tomato sauce, mushrooms, and olives (if using). 
 Heat through. 
 Transfer mixture to a 2-quart rectangular baking dish. 
 Sprinkle with cheese. 
 Flatten each biscuit with your hands. 
 Arrange the biscuits on top of cheese. 
 Bake for 15 to 17 minutes or until biscuits are golden.
