Ingredients : 
 3 Cups  pineapple  Tidbits, Frozen And Thawed or Canned (reserve Juice) 
 3 Tablespoons  butter 
 4 Tablespoons  brown sugar 
 3 Tablespoons  dark rum  (or More if Desired) 
 1 Teaspoon  allspice 
 1 Teaspoon  nutmeg 
 1 Teaspoon  cinnamon 
 1 Teaspoon  ginger 
 Directions : 
 In Pressure Cooker Melt butter 
 Then Add sugar and Stir. 
 Add The pineapple, pineapple juice, spices and rum. 
 Close Pressure Cooker Lid, Bring to High Pressure Then Lower Heat and Cook For 3 Minutes. 
 Release Pressures (cold water OrAutomatic, According to Manufacturer's Directions), 
 Open Lid And Stir. 
 Notes : 
 Excellent Served Over Pound Cake, Angel Food Cake, Ice cream 
 Or serve by Itself and top with whipped cream
