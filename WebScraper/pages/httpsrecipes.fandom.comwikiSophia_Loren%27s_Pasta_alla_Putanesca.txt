Sophia Loren's  Pasta  Alla Putanesca
 
 1/2 Cup  oil 
 1 Small  Onion , Chopped 
 3  garlic  cloves 
 1 Can  plum tomatoes , Drained And Quartered (14 Oz) 
 3  Anchovies , Minced 
 1 T. Drained  capers 
 1/2 Cup Pitted Italian  ripe olives 
 1/2 T. Crushed  red pepper flakes 
 1/8 T.  salt 
 1/8 T.  oregano 
 1 Lb.  Pasta  (use Favorite Kind) 
 3 T. Grated  Parmesan cheese 
 
In Large Skillet, Heat  oil ; Add  Onion  And  garlic . Saut? Until Golden. Remove  garlic ; Add  tomatoes ,  Anchovies  And  capers . Cook Covered Over Medium Heat 15 Minutes. Add ,  pepper  flakes,  salt  And  oregano . Cook 5 Minutes. Cook  Pasta  According to Package Directions For al Dente. Drain And Transfer to Large Serving Bowl. Toss  Pasta  With Sauce. Add  Cheese  And Toss Again. Serve at Once.
 Serves 4
