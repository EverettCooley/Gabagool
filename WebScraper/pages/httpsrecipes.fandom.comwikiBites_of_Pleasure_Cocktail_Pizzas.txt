Description : 
 Yield: 48 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 2½ cups  cheddar  cheese, grated 
 1½ cups chopped  ripe olives 
 2 x 8 oz loaves party  rye bread 
 butter  or  margarine , to cover  rye  slices 
 ¾ cup  green onions , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Take party  rye  slices and  butter  each slice. 
 Put the slices on a cookie sheet and toast lightly under broiler. 
 Mix the other ingredients in a bowl till well blended. 
 Spread this mixture over the toasted  bread . 
 Place it under broiler for 2 and ½ minutes which should be about inches away from heat.
