Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 6 servings of Thai salad with pork. 
 Ingredients : 
 Dressing : 
 ⅓ cup smooth  peanut butter 
 3 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 3 tbsp  lemon juice 
 2 tbsp  soy sauce 
 2 tsp  sugar 
 ½ tbsp  red pepper flakes 
 ¼ tsp  garlic powder 
 ¼ tsp ground  ginger 
 Pork and salad : 
 6 boneless thin cut  pork chops 
 2 tbsp  soy sauce 
 8 cups shredded  lettuce 
 1 cup  bean sprouts 
 1  cucumber , peeled, thinly sliced 
 2  red bell peppers , thinly sliced 
 3  scallions , thinly sliced 
 Directions : 
 Heat outdoor grill. 
 Dressing : 
 Whisk all ingredients in a bowl until smooth. 
 Whisk in ⅓ cup warm water. 
 Cover and set aside. 
 Pork and salad : 
 Brush chops with soy sauce. 
 Grill 4 min. 
 Turn and grill until done. 
 Set aside and keep warm. 
 In a bowl, toss lettuce, sprouts, cucumber, red pepper, and scallions with ¾ cups dressing. 
 Place 2 cups of salad on each plate. 
 Thinly slice pork, fan slices over each salad. 
 Drizzle each with 1 tbsp dressing.
