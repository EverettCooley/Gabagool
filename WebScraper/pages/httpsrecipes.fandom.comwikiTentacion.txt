Description : 
 "Temptation" — candied plantain
 
 4 – 5 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 3 large ripe  plantains 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 ½ cup  water 
 1 tablespoon  oil 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 ½ teaspoon  vanilla 
 dash of  cinnamon , optional 
 Directions : 
 Hit the  plantains  on all sides with a wooden spoon to soften. 
 Peel and cut into 5 pieces each. 
 In a large skillet, melt  butter  and  oil  on high heat; add  plantains . 
 Fry until brown. Combine  Sugar ,  vanilla ,  cinnamon  and  water . 
 Reduce heat to low and pour liquid over  plantains . 
 Continue cooking until the liquid thickens, about 15 minutes.
