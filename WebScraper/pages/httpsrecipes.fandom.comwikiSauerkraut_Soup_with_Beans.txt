Ingredients : 
 2.5 liter  water 
 500 g  sauerkraut 
 100 g  broad beans 
 500 g  pork 
 1  onion 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Wash the dried beans and soak in cold  water  for 7-9 hours. 
 Put in a pot together with the soak  water , add the cleaned  meat  and bring to a boil. 
 Skim off the scum. 
 Add the  sauerkraut , chopped  onion  and  salt . 
 Cook at a low temperature. 
 Remove from heat when it goes consistent. 
 Serve it hot. 
 With the  pork  you may serve grated raw  carrots .
