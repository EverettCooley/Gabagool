 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons fresh  lemon juice 
 1/2 teaspoon Dijon  mustard 
 1/4 cup  olive oil 
 3 crisp red  Apples , cut into 1/4" thick julienne 
 2 tart green  Apples  cut into 1/4" thick julienne 
 4 large  celery  ribs, cut into 1/4−inch−thick julienne 
 1/4 cup chopped fresh  parsley 
 1/3 cup dry−roasted  peanuts , coarsely chopped 
 Directions : 
 Whisk together  lemon juice  and  mustard  in a large bowl. 
 Add  oil  in a stream, whisking until emulsified. 
 Add  Apples ,  celery , and  parsley ,tossing to coat. Just before serving, sprinkle with  peanuts .
