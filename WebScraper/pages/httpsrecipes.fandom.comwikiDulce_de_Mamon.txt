Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 kilo of  Mamon fruit 
 800 gr.  Sugar 
 a spoonful of  lime juice . 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Peel, remove the seed and cut to pieces. 
 Place fruit pieces mixed with lime and sugar into a pot and let it rest for half an hour. 
 Bring mixture to boil (keep stirring!) and simmer (stirring!) until right consistency. 
 Put in clean (confiture) jars, close them firm immediately and put them upside down until cooled down 
 Instead of 4: Put in a bowl, let cool down. Enjoy!
