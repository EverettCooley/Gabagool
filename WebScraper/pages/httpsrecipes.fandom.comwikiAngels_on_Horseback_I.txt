Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 12 oysters in shells 
 pepper 
 12 slices bacon 
 1 lemon — thinly sliced 
 Directions : 
 Place 6 oysters in round glass casserole. 
 Heat, uncovered, in microwave 45 to 60 seconds on high, or just until shells open slightly. Do not cook oysters. 
 Repeat with other 6 oysters. 
 Remove oysters from shells. 
 Season with pepper; place 6 slices of bacon on roast rack, set in glass dish. 
 Cover with paper towel; cook 2 minutes on high. 
 Repeat with rest of bacon. 
 Wrap a slice of bacon around each oyster; secure with toothpick. 
 Place on rack, cover and cook 3 to 4 minutes on high or until oysters are cooked. 
 Rotate dish ½ turn, ½ way through cooking time. 
 Serve with lemon slices.
