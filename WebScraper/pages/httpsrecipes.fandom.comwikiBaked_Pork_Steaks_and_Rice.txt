Baked  Pork  Steaks And  rice 
 
 2 T.  margarine 
 1 1/2 Lb.  Pork , Cut Into 6 Steaks (or 6 Steaks Fillets of  Pork ) 
 1 1/2 T.  salt 
 1/4 T.  pepper 
 3/4 Cup  rice 
 2 Pints Boiling  chicken broth 
 1 Medium  Onion , Chopped 
 
Heat  margarine  in a Pan And Brown The  Pork  Fillets. Remove From Pan And Season With  salt  And  pepper . Add  rice  to The Pan And Cook, Stirring Constantly, Until it is Browned. Add Broth And  Onion  And Put Into a Large Casserole. Stand  Pork  on Top And Cover. Put Into a 350 Oven And Bake For 40 Minutes. Serves 6
