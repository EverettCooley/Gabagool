Description : 
 Makes about 6 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  ground lamb 
 1 cup fine  bulgar  (fine  wheat ) 
 1 medium  onion 
 ¼ tsp  cinnamon 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 4 oz  cream cheese 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Cut  onion  and puree in blender. 
 Add small amount of  water  to make blending easier. 
 Place meat into mixing bowl and add all ingredients. 
 Drain  water  from  bulgar  and squeeze out excess  water  and add to mix. 
 When finished mixing, take out about a large meatball size of mix and shape into a football(about 4" long and 2" wide) poke finger into one end and widen ¾ way in place  cream cheese  into hole(about 2" by 1" chunk) close top and reshape to look like a football. 
 Bake in oven at 350°F for 45 minutes.
