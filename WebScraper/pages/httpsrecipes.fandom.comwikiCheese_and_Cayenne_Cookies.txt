Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup less 2 tablespoons  flour 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅛ - ¼ teaspoon  cayenne  or to your liking 
 1 stick of  butter 
 2 cups grated extra sharp  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Mix butter and grated cheese. 
 Combine dry ingredients, and add to the cheese mixture. 
 Roll into log 2 inch in diameter. 
 Wrap in wax paper and chill overnight. 
 Slice as thin as possible. 
 Place on non stick cookie sheet, or foil, and place a halved pecan on top. 
 Bake 375 for 14-15 minutes or until light brown. 
 If using foil, cool, and peel off. 
 Store in covered container, refrigerated.
