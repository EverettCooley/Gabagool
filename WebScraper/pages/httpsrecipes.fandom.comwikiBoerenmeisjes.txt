Description : 
 A "drink" similar to  Boerenjongens , although this is made with  apricots  and  sherry  or  white wine . The name translates to "Farming Lassies".
 
 Ingredients : 
 400 g  apricots 
 400 g  Sugar 
 lemon peel  or  cinnamon  stick 
 50 g  raisins 
 1 liter  sherry  or  white wine 
 Directions : 
 Wash the  apricots  and leave to drain. 
 Put them in a stoppered bottle and add the  lemon peel  and  raisins . 
 Boil the  sugar  with a little  water , leave to cool and mix with the  sherry  or  white wine . 
 Pour onto the  apricots  and leave to stand for 2 months. 
 With  white wine  it doesn't keep as long as with  sherry , so should be drunk sooner.
