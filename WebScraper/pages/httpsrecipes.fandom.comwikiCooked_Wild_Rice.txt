Cooked  wild rice 
 
Serves 2.
 
 1/3 c  wild rice 
 1 ts  butter ; Unsalted 
 1 c  water 
 1 Dash  salt 
 1  Bacon  Strip; Thick, Lean 
 1 Dash  white pepper ; Freshly 
 Ground
 
Put The  wild rice  And  water  in a Small Sauce Pan. Bring The  water  to aBoil, Then Reduce The Heat to a Bare Simmer, Cover And Cook Until The RiceIs Tender, 1 to 1 1/4 Hours. While The  rice  is Cooking, Saute The BaconUntil Golden Brown And Crisp, Then Drain on a Paper Towel. Cut The BaconCrosswise Into Thin Julienne Strips And Set Aside. When The  rice  is Done,Gently Stir in The  butter  With a Fork Until Melted; Season to Taste WithSalt And  pepper . Just Before Serving, Toss The  rice  With The BaconStrips.
 Makes 1 Cup.
 
Enjoy.
