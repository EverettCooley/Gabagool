chocolate  Mallow Pie
 
 8 Oz. Pkg.  cream cheese , Softened 
 2 C. Cold  milk , Divided 
 1 Pkg. (3.9 Oz) Instant  chocolate  Pudding Mix 
 1 1/2 C. Miniature Marshmallows 
 9-Inch Graham Cracker Crust 
 
 in a Mixing Bowl, Beat  cream cheese  And 1/2 Cup of The  milk  Until Smooth. Add Pudding Mix And Remaining  milk ; Mix Well. Fold in Marshmallows. Pour Into Crust. Refrigerate Until Serving.
 
Makes 6 to 8 Servings
