Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves 4. 
 Ingredients : 
 juice of 6 medium  lemons 
 3 tablespoons concentrated fruit  sweetener , found at health food stores 
 4 cups cold  water 
 ice cubes 
 4  mint  sprigs 
 Directions : 
 In a large pitcher, combine the  lemon juice , concentrated fruit sweetener and cold  water . 
 Stir until thoroughly mixed. 
 More  lemon juice  can be added to taste. 
 Refrigerate the mixture for 1 hour, or until chilled. 
 Pour the lemonade into tall  ice  filled glasses, garnish with the  mint , and serve immediately.
