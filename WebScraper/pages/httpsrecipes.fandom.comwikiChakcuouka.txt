Description : 
 A Tunisian cooked  tomato  and  pepper  salad
 
 This recipe yields 4 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 2 medium  red bell peppers 
 olive oil spray  as needed 
 2 teaspoon  olive oil 
 Â½ medium  yellow onion  sliced (1 cup) 
 2 medium  garlic  cloves crushed 
 2 ripe medium  tomatoes  cut into wedges 
 1 teaspoon  sugar 
 salt  to taste 
 Freshly-ground  black pepper  to taste 
 4  pita  breads 
 Directions : 
 Preheat broiler. 
 Line a baking tray with aluminum foil and place red  bell peppers  on tray. 
 Spray  peppers  with  olive oil spray . 
 Broil 5 minutes per side. 
 Heat  oil  in a medium-sized nonstick frying pan on medium-high heat. 
 Add  onion  and  garlic  and saute 5 minutes. 
 Remove  peppers  from broiler, and core, seed, and slice them. 
 Add  peppers  and  tomatoes  to frying pan. 
 Simmer 15 minutes. 
 If mixture is watery, cook a few minutes more. 
 Sprinkle with  sugar  and toss well. 
 Add  salt  and  pepper  to taste. 
 Place pita in broiler, which should still be warm from cooking  peppers , for 2 minutes before serving. 
 Spoon warm salad into a serving bowl or on individual plates with  couscous  salad and serve with warm pita bread.
