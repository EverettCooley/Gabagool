Makes 15 to 18 latkes (4 servings).
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups cooked white or  brown rice 
 1 cup (4 ounces) shredded  Cheddar   Cheese 
 1/2 cup finely chopped  Onion 
 3 tablespoons  all-purpose flour 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 3  eggs , separated 
 1/8 teaspoon  cream of tartar 
 2 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 sour cream  (optional) 
 applesauce  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  Cheese ,  Onion ,  flour ,  salt ,  pepper  and  egg yolks  in medium bowl. 
 Beat  egg  whites with  cream of tartar  in small bowl until stiff but not dry. #Fold beaten  egg  whites into  rice  mixture. 
 Heat  oil  in large skillet over medium-high heat. 
 Spoon 2 to 3 tablespoons batter into skillet for each latke; push batter into diamond shape using spatula. 
 Cook latkes, turning once, until golden brown on both sides. 
 Serve warm with  sour cream  and  applesauce , if desired.
