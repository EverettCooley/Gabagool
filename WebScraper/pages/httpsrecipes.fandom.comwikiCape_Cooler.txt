 Ingredients : 
 1 shot Stolichnaya  vodka 
 2.5 shots  cranberry juice 
 1 shot Soda or  tonic water 
 garnish: Slice of  lime 
 Glass: Highball
 
 Directions : 
 Fill the glass with cubed  ice . Pour the Stolichnaya  vodka  and the  cranberry juice  into the glass and give it a stir. Top up with soda or  tonic water  (as preferred). Garnish with slice of  lime .
