 Description : 
 Serve 4.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb dried  broad beans 
 2 tsp chopped  parsley 
 1  garlic  crushed 
 1  chili pepper 
 a dash of  tabasco sauce 
 1 tbsp of mixed fresh  mint , finely chopped 
 2 tbsp  olive oil 
 Directions : 
 Soak beans overnight replace  water . 
 Add  salt  to taste bring to boil & simmer until beans are soft mash beans and place them in a serving dish. 
 Pour  olive oil  on top. 
 Add other ingredients to taste.
