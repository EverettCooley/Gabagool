Prep Time:  
 Cook time:  
 Serves: 8 
 
 Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  graham cracker  pie crust 
 2 squares 1 oz each  semi sweet baking chocolate 
 14 oz can  sweetened condensed milk 
 Â¼ cup creamy  peanut butter 
 8 oz tub frozen  whipped topping , thawed 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl, melt chocolate in microwave for 15 seconds or until melted. 
 Add milk and peanut butter to melted chocolate and mix well. 
 Fold in whipped topping. 
 Spoon into crust. 
 Freeze 6 hours. 
 Garnish with chocolate curls and chopped peanuts. 
 Cut into slices and place on serving plates. 
 Freeze leftovers.
