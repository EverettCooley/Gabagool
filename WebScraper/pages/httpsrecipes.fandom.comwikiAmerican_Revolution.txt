 Prep Time: 5-10 minutes 
 Cook time: 0 
 Serves: 1 
 
 Description : 
 The  American Revolution  is a cocktail that combines  gin  (the quintessential British liquor) with  bourbon  (the quintessential liquor of the USA). It is a strong cocktail that is meant to mellow as its ice melts.
 
 Ingredients : 
 Bourbon , 1 ½ oz 
 Gin , 1 ½ oz 
 Bitters , 2 dashes 
 Orange peel 
 Sour  Cherries 
 Ice 
 Directions : 
 In a mixing glass, muddle orange peel with 1 oz of the gin. 
 Add remainder of gin, the bourbon, bitters, and ice. 
 Stir 30 seconds (until the aromatics of the liquors are awakened). 
 Strain into old fashioned glass and fill with ice. 
 Garnish  with sour cherries.
