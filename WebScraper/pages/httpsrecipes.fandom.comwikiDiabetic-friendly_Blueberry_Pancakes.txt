Moist and delicious high fiber, low carb blueberry pancakes. I developed this recipe because I was tired of the dry, funny tasting low carb recipes out there. I also include bran for high fiber, which we need more of on a low carb diet! Please try, and enjoy!
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes For Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Prep. Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 30 minutes 
 Serves/Yields: 12 pancakes 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup of  soy flour 
 ⅓ cup unprocessed  bran  (optional) 
 1 cup whole milk  ricotta  cheese 
 3  eggs 
 ⅓ cup of  heavy cream 
 ¼ tsp  cinnamon 
 ⅓ cup of  Splenda 
 1 tps  baking powder 
 ⅛ tsp  nutmeg 
 1 tsp pure  vanilla extract 
 ½ cup  blueberries 
 PAM  cooking spray 
 Directions : 
 Combine soy flour, bran, ricotta, eggs, cream, Splenda, baking powder, cinnamon, nutmeg, and vanilla. 
 Whisk or use an electric mixer until combined. 
 Fold in blueberries. 
 Spray pan with PAM, and cook like normal pancakes. 
 They are ready to be flipped when you see bubbles! serve with butter and lots of sugar free maple syrup! enjoy!. 
 Nutritional Information : 
 About 15 grams of carbs in entire recipe
