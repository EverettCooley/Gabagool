Ingredients : 
 6 grated  carrots 
 1½ cups  green beans , steamed 
 1½ cups shredded  white cabbage 
 1 grated  cucumber 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 ½ teaspoon  black pepper 
 3 tablespoons  Barbados sugar 
 3 tablespoons  cider vinegar 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 1 teaspoon  ground turmeric 
 Directions : 
 Combine  carrots , steamed  green beans , white  cabbage  and  cucumber  in a glass bowl. 
 Combine  garlic ,  black pepper ,  Barbados sugar ,  cider vinegar ,  lemon juice  and  turmeric . 
 Toss vegetables in this dressing. 
 Chill, covered for 4 hours.
