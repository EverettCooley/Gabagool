Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz  gin 
 1 oz white  crème de cacao 
 1 oz  cream 
 Directions : 
 Using a shaker filled with ice, combine all ingredients. 
 Shake well. 
 Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
