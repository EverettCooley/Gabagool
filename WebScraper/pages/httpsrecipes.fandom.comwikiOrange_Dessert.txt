 Description : 
 orange  Dessert
 
 Ingredients : 
 6	x	 oranges , sliced in rounds and seeded 
 6	lrg	pitted  dates , chopped 
 2	tbl	 slivered almonds , toasted 
 Juice of 1/2  lemon 
 1	tsp	 cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Combine  oranges ,  dates  and  almonds  in a serving dish. 
 Gently stirin  lemon juice  and chill for an hour. 
 Just before serving, sprinkle with  cinnamon .
