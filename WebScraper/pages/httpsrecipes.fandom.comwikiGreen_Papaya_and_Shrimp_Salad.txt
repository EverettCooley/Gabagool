Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp equal parts - fresh  basil ,  cilantro  and  mint , chopped 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 2 tbsp  fish sauce 
 2  limes , juice only 
 3  garlic  cloves, minced 
 1 tbsp. fresh ground  black pepper 
 3 fresh  jalapeno peppers , chopped 
 1½ lbs  green papaya , shredded 
 ¾ lb  shrimp , cleaned, steamed and chilled 
 2 tbsp roasted  peanuts , chopped 
 2  tomatoes , firm and diced 
 Directions : 
 Mix fresh herbs, combine  sugar , fish sauce,  lime juice ,  garlic ,  black pepper  and chiles until  sugar  is dissolved. 
 Toss with remaining ingredients and serve with fresh tortillas.
