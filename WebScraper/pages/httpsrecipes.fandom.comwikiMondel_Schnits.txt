Description : 
 Almond slices
 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2  eggs 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 ¾ cup  flour 
 ½ tsp  baking powder 
 ¾ cup  almond , sliced 
 2 tbsp  water ,  ice 
 ½ tsp  lemon extract   or  ½  lemon , juice of 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 Directions : 
 The  almonds  need not be blanched, just sliced fine. 
 Sift  flour  and baking powder together, then set aside. 
 Cream the  eggs  and sugar thoroughly. 
 Add the extract and  water  and beat hard. 
 Add the sliced nuts and  flour , beating lightly until thoroughly mixed. 
 Pour the batter into a greased 9 by 13 inch pan, sprinkle  sugar  over the top and bake at 350°F until brown. 
 Allow the cake to cool in pan before cutting in slices.
