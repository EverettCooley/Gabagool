Description : 
       Cream of Carrots Soup     
 In Romanian: Supa de morcovi (crema)
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 qts / 3 l  water 
 1 lb / 500 g  carrots 
 1  onion 
 ½ teaspoon  sugar 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 ½ cup  sour cream 
 salt 
 cubed toasted bread 
 Directions : 
 Julienne the  carrots  and  onion  and set to boil until the  carrots  are tender. 
 Strain, then sieve the  carrots  and  onion . 
 Add the puree obtained this way to the strained liquid and set to boil again, with the  sugar ,  salt  and  butter . 
 Let boil for a few minutes and then add  sour cream  when serving. 
 Serve with croutons.
