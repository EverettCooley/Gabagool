 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup uncooked  basmati rice 
 2 cups veggie stock or  water 
 2 tbsp  olive oil 
 1/2 cup  golden raisins 
 1/2 cup  slivered almonds 
 1  Onion , chopped fine 
 2 tbsp fresh  cilantro , minced 
 2 tsp tumeric 
 1 tsp  curry  powder 
 Directions : 
 In a medium saucepan, heat the  oil . Throw in the  onions ,  Raisins ,  almonds , tumeric and  curry  powder and saut√© for a few minutes, until the  onions  are tender. Turn heat up to high and quickly add the  rice  and  water , stirring well. Bring to a boil, cover and reduce the heat to low. Cook for 10 minutes, or until  rice  is tender and all liquid is absorbed (don't peek until 10 minutes is up, though!). Remove from heat and let rest for another 10 minutes. Take of lid and fluff up with a fork. Lightly stir in the minced  cilantro , and serve.
