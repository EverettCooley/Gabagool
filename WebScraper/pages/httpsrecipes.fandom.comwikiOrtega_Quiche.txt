 Description : 
 This recipe freezes well & can be re-heated in the microwave.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 c melted  butter 
 1/2 c  flour 
 1 tsp  salt 
 10  eggs 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 8 oz chopped Ortega  peppers 
 1 pint  cottage cheese  (Farmers) 
 1 lb  jack cheese , grated 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix  eggs  in bowl (break  yolks ). Add all ingredients in order given. Mix well. 
 Pour into greased shallow pans. 
 Bake at 400° for 15 minutes, reduce heat to 350° and bake for 30 minutes. Cut into squares and serve.
