 Ingredients : 
 1 bunch watercress, chopped 
 6  tomatoes , diced 
 2  cucumbers , diced 
 1  Onion , diced 
 1/4 pound  feta 
 3 tablespoons  olive oil  (or to taste) 
 1 bunch  parsley 
 Directions : 
 1) Combine the watercress,  tomatoes ,  cucumbers  and  Onion  in a bowl.
 2) Mix together the  feta ,  oil , and  parsley . Sprinkle this mixture over the salad.
