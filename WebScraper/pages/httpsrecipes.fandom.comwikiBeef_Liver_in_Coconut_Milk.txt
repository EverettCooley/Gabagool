 Description : 
 Also known locally as Kalio Hati in Indonesia. Instead of using beef, it can also be substituted with chicken.
 
 Ingredients : 
 500 cc  coconut milk 
 1  turmeric leaf , torn and knotted 
 1 stalk  lemon grass , bruised (white part only) 
 3  kaffir lime leaves 
 1 – 2 pieces  asam gelugur 
 500 g  beef liver , cut into 3 cm cubes 
 Spice (ground) : 
 150 g  red chilies 
 1 tablespoon chopped  galangal 
 1 teaspoon chopped  ginger 
 1½ teaspoons chopped  turmeric 
 8  shallots 
 2 teaspoons  cilantro , roasted 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Use a small pot to boil the coconut milk with the ground spice and other ingredients except the liver. 
 Cook until the gravy has thickened. 
 Add the liver and simmer over low heat until the gravy is thick and oily. 
 Remove the leaves before serving it hot with white rice.
