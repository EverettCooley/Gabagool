Made from Dry-roasted chickpea flour, wheat flour,millet flour, mixed with spices and fresh herbs, salt, ghee, yogurt, and water. 
 Nutty in flavor and spicy, soft in texture and flat 
 Excellent for lunch as it stays fresh outside the refrigerator and is very tasty even cold
