 Description : 
 These are more like a dense cake than commercial granola bars. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅓ cup  vegetable oil 
 ⅓ cup  brown sugar  -- packed 
 ¼ cup  molasses 
 6 oz frozen  orange juice  concentrate 
 2 cups  flour 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 1 tsp ground  cinnamon 
 ½ tsp  ground ginger 
 ½ cup  rolled oats 
 ½ cup  raisins  -- chopped 
 ½ cup  dried apricots  -- chopped 
 ½ cup  sunflower seeds 
 ½ cup  wheat germ 
 2 tbsp  sesame seeds 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 Cream the  oil ,  brown sugar , and  molasses  together in a bowl. 
 Add the defrosted unsweetened  orange juice  concentrate. 
 Combine the  flour ,  baking soda ,  cinnamon  and  ginger . 
 Blend into the creamed mixture. 
 Stir in the  oats ,  raisins ,  apricots ,  sunflower seeds ,  wheat germ , and  sesame seeds . This makes a very stiff batter. 
 Spread in a greased 9 x 13 pan. 
 Bake about 20–30 minutes or until light brown. 
 Cut into bars. 
 Nutritional information : 
 ½ recipe = 140 calories, 1 starch + ½ fruit + 1 fat exchange 3 grams protein, 22 grams carbohydrate, 5 grams fat, 48 mg sodium
