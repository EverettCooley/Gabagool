Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can  cheddar cheese soup 
 ½ cup  milk 
 dash of  pepper 
 1 bag frozen  broccoli , cooked 
 1 can  French fried onions 
 Directions : 
 In a 2 qt casserole dish mix soup, milk, pepper and broccoli. 
 Bake at 350°F for 25 minutes. Stir. 
 Sprinkle onions over broccoli mixture. 
 Bake 5 more minutes or until onions are golden.
