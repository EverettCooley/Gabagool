 About Indian bitter melon : 
 Wikipedia Article  About Indian bitter melon on Wikipedia 
 In Indian ayurvedic medicine, it is popularly seen as a "plant-insulin" and is thus highly recommended for diabetics. It is called ampalaya and prepared into a dish in the Philippines. Even in western medicine due to its high nutrition content it is advised that patients take this vegetable regularly.
