Cafe Diable
 
 2 T.  sugar 
 5 Oz.  dark rum  or  brandy 
 2  orange   twists 
 10 Whole Cloves 
 2 Pieces Stick  cinnamon  (1 Inch Each) 
 5 Cups  strong coffee 
 
Put All Ingredients Except  coffee  in Chafing Dish. Heat Gently. Stir Constantly. Ignite And Let Burn One Minute. Slowly Add  coffee . Ladle Into Cups.Serves 4 to 6
