Ingredients : 
 6 small baking  potatoes 
 ½ cup  onion , chopped 
 1 tbsp unsalted  margarine 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 ¼ cup  skimmed milk , hot 
 1 tsp  dill weed 
 ⅛ tsp  white pepper 
 1 tsp  lemon peel , grated 
 1 tbsp  parsley 
 paprika 
 Cheese lemon herb potatoes : 
 ½ oz  cheddar  cheese, for each potato 
 Directions : 
 Bake  potatoes  until done. 
 Cool. 
 Sauté  onions  and  garlic  in unsalted  margarine . 
 Scoop out  potatoes  and mash. 
 Beat in  onion  mixture, hot skimmed  milk ,  lemon  and  pepper . 
 Mix in dill and  parsley . 
 Refill skins. 
 Sprinkle with  paprika . 
 Bake at 400°F for 30 minutes. 
 Cheese lemon herb potatoes : 
 Add ½ oz grated  cheddar  cheese on top of each  potato  before baking the final time. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Food exchange per serving: 1 starch/bread + ¼ fat (calculated without the cheese)
