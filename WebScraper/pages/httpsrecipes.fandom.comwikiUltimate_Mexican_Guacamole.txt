 Ingredients : 
 6 very ripe, peeled  avocados 
 3 fresh  garlic  cloves 
 1 tsp  salt 
 2 chopped  tomatoes 
 2 chopped jalapeno  peppers 
 2 chopped habanero  peppers 
 1 medium  red onion  - diced 
 4 tsp corona  beer  or to taste 
 1 tsp  lime juice 
 1 tsp nacallo extract 
 1 cup grated Monterey  jack cheese 
 Directions : 
 Mash avocados, combine all other ingredients, except for nacallo. 
 Extract and half of  lime juice . 
 Chill for 2 hours. 
 Add remaining  lime 
 Juice and nacallo extract before serving.
