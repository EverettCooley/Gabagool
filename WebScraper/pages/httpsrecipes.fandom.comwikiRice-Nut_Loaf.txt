 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  brown rice 
 2 cups (8 ounces) shredded sharp  cheddar  cheese 
 4  eggs , lightly beaten 
 1 medium  onion , chopped 
 1 cup shredded  carrots 
 ½ cup Italian-style  breadcrumbs 
 ¼ cup chopped  walnuts 
 ¼ cup chopped  sunflower kernels 
 ¼ cup  sesame seeds 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1 x 16-ounce jar  spaghetti sauce  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  cheese ,  eggs ,  onion ,  carrots , breadcrumbs,  walnuts , sunflower kernels,  sesame seeds ,  salt  and  pepper ; pack into greased 9-inch loaf pan. 
 Bake at 350°F for 50 to 60 minutes until firm. 
 Let cool in pan 10 minutes; unmold and slice. 
 Serve with heated  spaghetti sauce , if desired. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving: 
 
 444 calories | 20.2g Protein | 25.9g Fat | 33.6g Carbohydrate | 2.5g Dietary fiber | 187mg Cholesterol | 546 mg Sodium
