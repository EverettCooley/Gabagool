 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 Coffee Cake:
 
 1 1/2 cups  flour 
 1/4 cup  sugar 
 1 tbsp  baking powder 
 1 tsp.  cinnamon 
 1/4 tsp.  salt 
 1 1/2 cups fresh cut  mango  (for substitutes you can use  peaches  or  blueberries , but I highly recommend mangos) 
 1  egg 
 1/2 c.  milk 
 1/4 cup.  butter , melted 
 Topping:
 
 3/4 cup  brown sugar 
 1 tbsp  flour 
 1/4 cup coarsely chopped  walnuts ) 
 Directions : 
 
 
 In a large bowl, combine the first five ingredients ( flour ,  sugar ,  baking powder ,  cinnamon  and  salt ). Gently fold in the  mango . Set aside. 
 In a small bowl, whisk together the  egg ,  milk  and melted  butter . Add this to the flour mixture and stir until well combined. 
 Spread batter into a greased 8 x 8 inch  baking pan .  
 Combine topping ingredients ( brown sugar ,  flour  and  walnuts ) and sprinkle over batter. 
 Bake in oven at 425°F for 20 to 25 minutes, or until the top is lightly golden brown.
