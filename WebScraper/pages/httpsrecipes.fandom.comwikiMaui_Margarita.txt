Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: The Last Minute Party Girl 
 Ingredients : 
 3 to 4 oz limeade (frozen concentrate mixed with  water ) 
 2 oz good to great  tequila , more if you're a serious party girl! 
 ½ oz  Triple sec  or  Grand Marnier  (more if you add extra  tequila ) 
 ½  lime , cut into wedge 
 margarita salt  (optional) 
 crushed  ice 
 ½ oz  cranberry juice 
 Directions : 
 Combine the limeade,  tequila  and  triple sec . 
 Squeeze the juice from the  lime  wedges into the mix and throw one squeezed wedge into the batch. 
 Moisten the rims of your glasses, then dip them into a plate of  margarita salt . 
 Serve in the  salt  rimmed glass over crushed  ice  and with a floater of  cranberry juice . Pour gently so its color doesn't blend with the rest of the margarita.
