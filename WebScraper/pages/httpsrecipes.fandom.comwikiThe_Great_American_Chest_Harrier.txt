Description : 
 Coffee that:'puts hair on one's chest',whence the name.
 1 ounce of this,(minus the Kefir),when shaken and strained 
 with 1/2 ounce  Brandy  and 1 ounce Kirshwasser®
 into an old fashioned glass with ice,is called a:'Black Cat.'
 
 Ingredients : 
 12 cups Luxco® Golden Grain Alcohol® 
 12 cups Kopi Luwak 
 12 cups Kefir grains 
 
 
 Directions : 
 1.Pour Golden Grain into lower 
 chamber of a siphon coffee brewer.
 2.Dump Kefir grains into upper chamber.
 3.Dump Kopi Lumak onto Kefir.
 4.Allow brewing process to complete itself.
 5.Drink unsweetened while hot.
