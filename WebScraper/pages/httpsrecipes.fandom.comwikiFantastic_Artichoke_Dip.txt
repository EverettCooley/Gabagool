Fantastic  artichoke  Dip
 
 8 oz  cream cheese 
 12 oz Shredded  mozzarella 
 1 c  Mayonnaise 
 1 c Grated Parmesan 
 1  Onion ; Finely Chopped 
 2 cl  garlic ; Finely Chopped 
 2 Jars Marinated  artichoke  Hea 
 2 of Pita  bread 
 
Drain Artichokes Well And Tear Them Apart With Your Fingers.Cut up Pita  bread  Into Chip Size Triangles, Separate And Bake on a CookieSheet Until Crispy (approx 5 Minutes).
 Combine All Other Ingredients And Mash.
 Bake in a Uncovered Casserole Dish For Approx 30 Minutes @ 350 or UntilBubbly.
 Serve Hot Out of The Oven And Scoop up Dip With Pita  bread .
 
Enjoy.
