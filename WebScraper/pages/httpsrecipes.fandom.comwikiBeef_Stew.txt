Description : 
 Here's an easy to prepare hearty meat dish.
 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 lbs  Steak 
 1/2 cup  pearl barley 
 8  potatoes , peeled 
 3 parsnips 
 3  carrots , diced 
 beef stock 
 seasoning 
 mixed herbs 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Place meat in a pot and cover with water. Bring to a boil. 
 Add diced  carrots , parsnips, herbs and seasoning. 
 Simmer over moderate low heat for 20 minutes. 
 Add peeled whole  potatoes  and  beef stock . 
 Simmer on low heat for 1 hour. Stir occasionally.
