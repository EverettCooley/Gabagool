Description : 
 In Romanian: Glazura de rom
 
 Ingredients : 
 15 oz / 420 g  sugar 
 ½ cup  water 
 2 tablespoons  rum 
 juice from ½  lemon 
 Directions : 
 Set the  water  to boil with the  sugar ; when thickened as for sherbets, add  rum  and  lemon juice . 
 Remove from heat, stir well and when it lightens in color, quickly cover the cake with it.
