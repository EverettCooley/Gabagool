Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 oz Bombay Sapphire®  gin 
 1/2 oz  Cointreau ®  orange liqueur 
 juice of 1/2  lemons 
 Directions : 
 Mix the ingredients into a shaker, shake and strain into a cocktail glass. Garnish with  lemon  and serve.
