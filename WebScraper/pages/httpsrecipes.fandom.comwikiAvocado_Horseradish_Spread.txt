 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 California  Avocados 
 1/3 Cups Chopped  Onion 
 1/3 Cups  lemon juice 
 ¼ Cup  Horseradish  (or to taste) 
 ½ tsp  Salt 
 Directions : 
 METHOD
 Mash  Avocados .  Stir  in finely chopped  Onion ,  lemon juice , prepared horseradish, and  salt .
 Chill 1 hour to marry flavors.
