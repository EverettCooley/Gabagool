Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup sliced  green onions 
 1 8-ounce can sliced  Mushrooms , drained 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 2 tablespoons chopped fresh  parsley 
 1 teaspoon  basil 
 1/2 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 1/2 teaspoon ground  nutmeg 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Cook  onions  and  Mushrooms  in  butter  5 minutes. Add  rice ,  parsley ,  basil ,  garlic powder ,  nutmeg ,  salt  and  pepper . Toss lightly. 
 Turn into greased 1-quart ring mold or pan. Bake, covered, at 350 degrees 20 minutes, or stuff neck and body cavity of 4 to 5-pound  Turkey . 
 Truss  Turkey  and season with  salt  and  pepper ; bake
