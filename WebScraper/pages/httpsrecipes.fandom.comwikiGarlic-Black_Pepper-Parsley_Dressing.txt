 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  vinegar 
 2 tbsp  olive oil 
 2 tsp  garlic powder 
 ½ tsp  black pepper 
 parsley 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients and pour the dressing on boiled  broccoli  or  cauliflower  salad. 
 Reserve the dressing in the refrigerator and use as a dip or marinade.
