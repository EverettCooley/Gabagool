Ingredients : 
 ½ cup lukewarm  water 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1  egg yolk 
 4 to 5 cups  flour 
 1 tbsp  anise seed 
 ½ cup  butter 
 3  eggs 
 2 tbsp  light corn syrup 
 and cheese 
 Directions : 
 Sprinkle the  yeast  over the lukewarm  water ; stir until the  yeast  is dissolved. 
 Beat the  eggs  in a large mixing bowl with a fork until slightly beaten; add  sugar ,  yeast  mixture,  salt ,  anise seed ,  butter , and 2 cups of  flour . 
 Beat with electric beater until well beaten and smooth. 
 Stir in additional  flour  to make a soft dough. 
 Turn out onto a well floured pastry board and knead until dough is soft and elastic. 
 Do not add too much  flour . 
 Place in a greased bowl and cover with a damp warm cloth. 
 Allow to rise until double in bulk. 
 Punch down the dough and make a round loaf, smooth on top with the folded edges on the bottom of the loaf. 
 Place in a deep round cake pan. 
 Beat the  egg yolk  with the  corn syrup  and brush the top with a pastry brush. 
 Bake in a preheated oven at 350°F for 15 minutes or until done.
