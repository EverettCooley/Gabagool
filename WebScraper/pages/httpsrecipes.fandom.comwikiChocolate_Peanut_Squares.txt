Chocolate Peanut Squares
 1 Cup Margarine
6 1-oz. squares of semi-sweet chocolate
1 cup Coconut
1 1/2 cup graham cracker crumbs
1/2 cup chopped, unsalted peanuts
2 8-oz. pkgs.Cream Cheese, softened
1 cup sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
 1. Microwave 3/4 cup margarine & 2 oz. chocolate squares on high for 
1-2 min. or until melted, stirring every 30 sec. 
 2. Stir in crumbs, coconut, & peanuts. Press onto bottom of 13 x 9 
in. baking pan. Chill 30 min. 
 3. Mix cream cheese, sugar, & vanilla until well blended. Spread over 
crust; chill 30 min. 
 4. Microwave remaining margarine & chocolate squares on high 1-2 min. 
or until melted, stirring every 30 sec. 
 5. Spread margarine & chocolate mixture over cream cheese, sugar, and 
vanilla layer. Chill; cut into squares
