chocolate chip   Cheese  Cake Dessert
 Squares
 
 2 (16 Oz.) Pillsbury Refrigerator  chocolate chip 
 Cookie Rolls
 
 2 (8 Oz.) Pkgs.  cream cheese , Softened 
 2/3 C.  sugar 
 2  eggs 
 1 1/2 Tsp.  vanilla 
 
Heat And Grease 9x13 Inch Pan. Slice Well ChilledDough Into 1/4 Inch Slices And Spread in Bottom Of
 Prepared Pan.
 
Combine  cream cheese ,  sugar ,  eggs  And  vanilla  WithElectric Mixer. Spread  cream cheese  Mixture Over
 Cookie Dough. Place Remaining Roll of Dough SlicesOver  cream cheese  Mixture. Bake at 375 Degrees For
 
 30-35 Minutes Until Crust is Golden Brown. Chill 
 Completely. Cut Into Squares. Refrigerate Leftovers.
 
Enjoy.
