 Ingredients : 
 250 g  octopus  arms (well cleaned, sliced thinly) 
 20 g  dried chilli 
 100 g  onion 
 20  garlic  cloves 
 1 tsp  cumin 
 2  curry leaves 
 2 tsp  black peppercorns 
 2 pieces  rampe leaf 
 60 ml  coconut oil 
 salt  to season 
 Directions : 
 Fry half the  onion  and  garlic  together with the  curry leaves  and  rampe leaf  until  onions  are golden brown. 
 Blend the rest of the spice mixture to a smooth thick paste. 
 Add the  octopus  and the spice paste and seasoning. 
 Braise over a low heat for 20 minutes.
