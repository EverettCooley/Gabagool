Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Elmquist Estate in Heath, Texas in 1981.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 16 slices  bread 
 8 slices  ham 
 8 slices  cheddar  cheese 
 2 teaspoons  onion powder 
 6  eggs  well beaten 
 3 cups  milk 
 ½ teaspoon  dry mustard 
 ½ teaspoon  tarragon 
 2 teaspoons  paprika 
 crushed  potato chips 
 ½ cup melted  butter 
 Directions : 
 Place bread, ham, cheese sandwich wise in a pan big enough to hold the sandwiches. 
 Mix remaining ingredients except potato chips and butter beat well and pour over sandwich. 
 Cover with crushed potato chips then cover and refrigerate overnight. 
 Remove from refrigerator 1 hour before placing in oven. 
 Pour ½ cup melted butter over top then bake at 350°F for 1 hour. 
 Serve hot.
