 Note from the cookbook: This is wonderful! 
 Makes 40 squares. 
 Ingredients : 
 2 jars marinated  artichoke hearts  (6 oz. each) 
 1 small  onion , finely chopped 
 4  eggs 
 ¼ cup dry  breadcrumbs 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 ⅛ tsp  pepper 
 ¼ tsp  oregano 
 ⅛ tsp  Tabasco sauce 
 2 cups  cheddar  cheese, shredded 
 2 tbsp  parsley , minced 
 Directions : 
 Drain artichokes, saving marinade form one jar. 
 In marinade, sauté onion. 
 Cut up artichokes. 
 Beat eggs and add crumbs and seasoning. Stir in remaining ingredients. 
 Turn into a greased 7 x 11 inch pan. Bake at 325°F for 30 minutes. 
 Let cool a little before cutting into squares. Serve hot or cold.
