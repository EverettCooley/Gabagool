 Ingredients : 
 2  orange  halves - shells only 
 2  oranges  - segmented 
 250 gm cooked prawns 
 1  kiwi  - peeled and sliced 
 2 tbsp chopped  nuts 
 4 tbsp chopped herbs 
 Dressing
 
 2 tbsp  yoghurt 
 2 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 tbsp honey 
 1/2 tsp  mustard 
 1 tbsp  oil 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Mushroom  rolls
 
 4 slices bread - crusts removed 
 1 tbsp softened  butter 
 1/4 tsp  lemon  rind 
 1 tsp chopped herbs 
 pepper 
 2 tbsp finely chopped  Mushrooms  â€“ fried 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine  orange  segments with prawns,  kiwi ,  nuts  and herbs.
Season. Whisk together dressing ingredients. Mix with prawns and fruit.
Spoon into  orange  shells. Chill. Flatten bread slices with a rolling pin.
Beat  butter , rind, herbs and  pepper  together. Spread over bread and scatter  Mushrooms . Roll up bread. Chill well before serving
