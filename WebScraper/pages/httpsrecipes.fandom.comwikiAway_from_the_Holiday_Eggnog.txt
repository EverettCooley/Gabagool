Description : 
 Always check the ingredients to make sure the product is vegan.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2½ cups  silken tofu 
 2 cups  soy milk 
 1 tbsp  vanilla extract 
 ¾ cup  sugar 
 2 tbsp  maple syrup 
 ¼ tsp  turmeric 
 ½ tsp  nutmeg 
 1 cup  rum  or  brandy 
 Directions : 
 Blend it all together in a food processor or blender and chill.
