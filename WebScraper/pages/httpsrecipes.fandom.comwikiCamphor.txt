Kacha karpoor 
 Made from a steam distillation of the aromatic leaves and wood of cinnamomum camphora, an evergreen tree that grows in China and India 
 Pure, edible camphor is a crystalline compound that resembles coarse salt 
 Sold wrapped in paper in small boxes, jars, or packets 
 Has strong cardamom like aroma 
 Used in desserts and sweets 
 Look for Swad brands in 3 ounce jars 
 Inedible camphor is toxic and is used for puja, and should not be consumed
