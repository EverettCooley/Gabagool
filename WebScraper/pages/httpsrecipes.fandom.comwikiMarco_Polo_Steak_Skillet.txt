Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 1 1/2 pounds  Beef  round  Steak , cut into thin strips 
 1 medium green  bell pepper , cut into 3/4-inch squares 
 1 tablespoon  cornstarch 
 2 tablespoons soy sauce 
 1 1/2 cups  beef broth 
 1 teaspoon  Sugar 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1 medium  tomato , cut into 3/4-inch pieces 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in large skillet. Add  Beef ; cook over high heat until brown, about 5 minutes, stirring often. Add  green pepper  and continue to cook until soft but not brown, about 3 minutes. 
 Dissolve  cornstarch  in soy sauce; stir into skillet. Add broth,  Sugar  and  salt . Cook, stirring often, until clear and thickened. Add  tomato ; heat through. Serve over fluffy  rice .
