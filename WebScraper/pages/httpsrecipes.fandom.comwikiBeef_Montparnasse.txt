Description : 
 Contributed by  Pressurecookerrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds  round steak , cut in serving size pieces 
 2 tablespoons  shortening 
 2  bacon  slices — crumbled 
 6 small  white onions 
 6  new potatoes  — peeled 
 2 cans  mushrooms  with liquid — 4 ounces each 
 ½ cup dry  white wine 
 1 tablespoon minced  parsley 
 2 teaspoons  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  pepper 
 1  bay leaf 
 Directions : 
 Heat pressure cooker. 
 Add  shortening ; brown  steak . 
 Add remaining ingredients. 
 Close cover securely. 
 Place pressure regulator on vent pipe. 
 Cook for 15 minutes. 
 Let pressure drop of its own accord. 
 Remove  bay leaf . 
 Thicken gravy, if desired.
