Ingredients : 
 1½ cups  papaya  or  guava  or  apricot nectar . 
 4 tbsp  cornstarch 
 4 tbsp  lemon juice 
 4 tbsp  sugar 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve  cornstarch  in  lemon juice . 
 Add to the nectar and cook until thick. 
 Add 2 cups diced fresh fruit such as  papaya ,  pineapple ,  melons ,  oranges . 
 Cool to room temperature. 
 Spread with: 1 cup  heavy cream  whipped with 4 tbsp  sugar .
