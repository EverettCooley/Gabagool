 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 24 ounces  cream cheese  softened 
 Â½ pound  smoked salmon 
 juice of 2  limes 
 12  scallions  sliced including green tops 
 3 tablespoons chopped fresh  dill 
 Directions : 
 Place cream cheese and salmon in the bowl of a blender or food processor and process until just combined. 
 Add the lime juice, scallions and fresh dill and pulse 5 times. 
 Serve with cucumber rounds or crackers.
