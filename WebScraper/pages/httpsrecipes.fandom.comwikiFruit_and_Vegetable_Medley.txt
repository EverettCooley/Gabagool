Description : 
 Fruit & Vegetable Medley  from the  Public Health Cookbook  by the Seattle & King County Department of Public Health—original source of recipe, public domain government resource
 
 Serves : 6 
 Ingredients : 
 non-fat  cooking spray 
 2¼ cups medium  carrots , peeled and thinly sliced 
 2¼ cups  parsnips , peeled and thinly sliced 
 2 medium ripe  pears , peeled and chopped 
 ¾ cups  orange juice 
 ⅓ cup dried chopped  dates 
 1 teaspoon grated fresh  ginger 
 2 tablespoons  brown sugar 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 Directions : 
 Spray a large skillet with cooking spray. 
 Add carrots, parsnips, pears, orange juice, dates and ginger. 
 Stir to combine. 
 Bring to boiling then reduce heat to simmer, uncovered, for about 7 minutes or until vegetables are crisp-tender. 
 Add sugar and butter and stir to coat vegetable mixture. 
 Cook for an additional 2–3 minutes then serve as a side dish.
