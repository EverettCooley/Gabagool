 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cup whole  Wheat  bread 
 1/2 cup dried fruit 
 1 1/2 cup skim  milk 
 1/2 cup healthy choice cholesterol 
 1/4 cup  Sugar 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 1/4 tsp  nutmeg 
 Directions : 
 In 1 ½ quart casserole sprayed with  vegetable oil spray , combine bread and fruit. 
 In small bowl, combine  milk ,  egg  product,  Sugar ,  vanilla  and  nutmeg . 
 Pour  milk  mixture over bread. Bake in 350 °F. 
 Oven for 60 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean. 
 Note: 4 slices of bread makes 4 cups cubed.
