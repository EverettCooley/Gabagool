 Description : 
 24 Select Potato Chips are the house potato chip brand for Store 24, a New England convenience store chain owned by Tedeschi Food Shops, Inc. The chips retail for fifty-nine cents a bag.
 Snack website  taquitos.net  describes the chips as " Lay's -type regular potato chips". The chips were deemed "not too great" overall.
