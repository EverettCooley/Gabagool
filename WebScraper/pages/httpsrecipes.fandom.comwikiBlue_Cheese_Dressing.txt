Blue  Cheese  Dressing
 
 1 Cup Chef's  salad dressing  (recipe Follows) 
 1/4 Cup Crumbled Blue  Cheese  (1 Oz) 
 
In Small Bowl, Mix Chef's  salad dressing  Mix And Blue  Cheese . Refrigerate 30 Minutes Before Serving. Makes About 1 1/4 Cup.Chef's  salad dressing  Mix:
 
 2 1/2 Cups  Mayonnaise 
 1 1/2 Cups Fresh  buttermilk 
 2 T.  vinegar 
 2 T.  sugar 
 1/2 T. Worcestershire 
 1/4t. Dry  mustard 
 1/2 T.  salt 
 1/8 T.  garlic powder 
 
In Medium Bowl, Mix All Ingredients. Stir With Whisk Until Thoroughly Blended. Pour Into a 1 Quart Container With a Tight Fitting Cover. Label With Date And Contents. Store in Refrigerator. Use Within 4 Weeks.Source: my Old Recipes
