 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 5  Apple 
 1 tsp  Cinnamon 
 1 tbsp  Butter 
 1 cup  Sugar 
 1  Egg 
 1/2 cup  Flour 
 1/2 tsp  baking powder 
 1 pinch Of  Salt 
 Directions : 
 1 Peel  Apples  and dice,  Sprinkle  with  cinnamon  and 1/3 cup of the  Sugar . 
2  cream   butter  and 2/3 cup  Sugar . 
3  Beat   egg  and add to  butter  mixture. 
4 Sift  flour ,  baking powder  and  salt . 
5 Add to mixture. 
6 Pour over  Apples  and bake 10 minutes at 425 - then 20 minutes at 350. 
7  Batter  will be thick - just drop and spread as best as you can. 
8 recipe from marilla ransdell, submitted by donna ransdell.
