Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 l  sour milk 
 1  egg 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  Sugar 
 2 tbsp  oil  or fat 
 1 tsp  caraway seeds 
 ½ gl  Barley  meal 
 ¾ gl  wheat flour  (middling) 
 ½ tsp  baking soda 
 Directions : 
 1.	Mix  egg  with  salt  and  Sugar .
 2.	Now add  sour milk ,  oil ,  caraway seeds  and meal mixed with  baking soda . 
 3.	Knead the mixture to make light and tender dough.
 4.	Put the mixture in a buttered cake tin and bake. 
 Serving:
 Serve with  butter , honey or cowberry jam.
