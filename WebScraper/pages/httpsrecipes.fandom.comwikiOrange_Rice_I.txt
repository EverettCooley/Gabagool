Ingredients : 
 1 cup brown  basmati rice 
 1 to 1½ cups  water 
 ⅔ cup  orange juice 
 1  cinnamon  stick 
 6 whole  cloves 
 ¼ tsp ground  ginger 
 1 tbsp  golden raisins 
 Directions : 
 Soak  rice  for 30 minutes to 2 hours before cooking; drain. 
 In a medium-size saucepan, combine remaining ingredients. Bring to a boil; reduce heat. 
 Add drained  rice . Cook, partially covered, until liquid is absorbed about 15 minutes. 
 Remove cloves and  cinnamon  stick. Fluff with a fork and serve. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Food exchange per serving: 1 starch/bread exchange + 1 fruit exchange
