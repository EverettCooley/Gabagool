Desription : 
 This is divine on baked potatoes and broccoli. Substitute sour cream for the buttermilk to make dip for raw vegetables. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: Hope Parks 
 Makes 2 cups 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 1½ tsp dried  onion flakes 
 1 tsp  Accent 
 ¼ tsp  garlic 
 1 cup  buttermilk 
 1 tbsp dried  parsley 
 1 scant teaspoon  onion salt 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together and store in refrigerator until ready to use. 
 May prepare ahead.
