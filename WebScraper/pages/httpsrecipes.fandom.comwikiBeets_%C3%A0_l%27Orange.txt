Ingredients : 
 3 cups sliced cooked  beets 
 ⅓ cup  sugar 
 1 tablespoon  potato starch 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ¼ cup  beet  juice 
 ½ cup  orange juice 
 1 tsp grated  orange rind 
 1 tbsp  vegetable shortening 
 Directions : 
 Drain beets and save liquid. 
 Combine  sugar ,  potato starch  and  salt  in a saucepan. 
 Stir in  beet  juice, cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until very thick. 
 Add  orange juice , rind, vegetable  shortening  and sliced beets. 
 Mix until beets are well coated. 
 Heat thoroughly and serve.
