Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 x 8 oz cans of  crescent dinner rolls 
 1 x 16 oz can of  refried beans 
 1 cup of  sour cream 
 2 tablespoons of  taco seasoning  mix 
 6 oz shredded  Cheddar  cheese 
 ½ cup chopped  green onion 
 ½ cup chopped  green bell pepper 
 1 cup chopped  tomatoes 
 ½ cup sliced  ripe olives 
 salsa 
 Directions : 
 Heat Oven to 375°F. 
 Unroll the crescent dough into 4 long rectangles. 
 Place cross-wise in ungreased 15x10x1 inch pan; Press over the bottom and up the sides to form a crust. Firmly press perforations together to seal. 
 Bake at 375°F for 14 to 19 minutes or until golden brown and allow to cool completely. 
 Spread the  refried beans  over the crust to within about ½ inch of the edges. 
 In a small bowl, combine  sour cream  and  taco seasoning  and mix well. 
 Spread sour cream mixture over the  refried beans . 
 Sprinkle with shredded  cheese ,  onions ,  peppers ,  tomatoes  and . 
 Cover and refrigerate 1 hour. 
 Cut into squares and serve with salsa.
