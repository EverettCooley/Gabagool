Ingredients : 
 1 lb. ground  Beef 
 1 c. (4 oz.) shredded  Cheddar   Cheese 
 1 c. (4 oz.) shredded  Monterey jack   Cheese 
 1 can (4 oz.) chopped green chilies, drained 
 1/2 c. bottled green taco or enchilada sauce 
 2 large  eggs , beaten 
 tortilla chips (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Cook and stir  Beef  in large skillet over med. high heat until  Beef  loses pink color. 
 Pour off drippings. Stir in cheeses, chilies, taco sauce and  eggs . 
 Transfer mixture to 8x8" baking pan. 
 Bake at 350 F. 35 ot 40 mins. or until knife inserted in center comes out clean and top is golden brown. 
 Cool in pan 15 mins.Cut into 36 squares. 
 Serve with tortilla chips. Makes 36 appetizers.
