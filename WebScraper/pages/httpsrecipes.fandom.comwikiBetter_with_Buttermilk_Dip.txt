Ingredients : 
 1 cup  buttermilk 
 2 cups  mayonnaise 
 1 cup  sour cream  or  yogurt 
 1 envelope ranch style dressing 
 Directions : 
 mix ingredients stir together and chill. 
 Serve with your choice of fruits or vegetables or crackers 
 Variations : 
 For use as  salad dressing , substitute a second cup of  buttermilk  for the  sour cream  or  yogurt .
