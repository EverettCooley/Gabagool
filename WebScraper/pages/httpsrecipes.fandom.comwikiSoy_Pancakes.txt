 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  soy flour 
 2 3/4 cups  all-purpose flour 
 3 Tbs  baking powder 
 3 Tbs  Sugar 
 1 1/2 tsp  salt 
 3  eggs 
 3 cups  soymilk 
 6 Tbs  soybean oil 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together until moistened. Add extra  soymilk  for thinner pancakes. Spray griddle with a non-stick  cooking spray . Preheat to 350 °F. Pour 1/2 cup of batter on griddle for each pancake.
 
 Yield: 16 servings. Serving Size: 1 pancake. Per serving: 188 calories, 7 g protein (2 g soy protein), 23 g carbohydrate, 7.5 g total fat (1.3 g sat fat), 441 g sodium, 41 mg cholesterol, 1 g dietary fiber.
