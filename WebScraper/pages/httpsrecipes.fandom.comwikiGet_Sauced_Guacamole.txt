 Description : 
 Worcestershire and Tabasco sauces spice up this creamy version.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 large  California avocados 
 1 large  tomato , chopped 
 ½ Cup  Onion , chopped 
 1 tsp  lemon juice 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 1/8 tsp  pepper 
 ½ tsp  worcestershire sauce 
 1/8 tsp  Tabasco sauce 
 ½ Cup  sour cream 
 1 Tbsp  Mayonnaise 
 Directions : 
 Peel and remove pit from  avocado . Mash with fork. 
 In small bowl combine all ingredients. 
 Bury pit in Guacamole until time to serve.
