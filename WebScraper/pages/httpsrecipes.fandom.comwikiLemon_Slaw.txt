      Lemon Slaw     
 Ingredients : 
 6 cup finely shredded  cabbage 
 2 medium  carrots , finely shredded 
 1 small  onion , finely chopped 
 1 small  green pepper , finely chopped 
 ¾ cup  oil 
 ⅓ cup  vinegar 
 ⅓ cup  water 
 1 cup  sugar 
 ¼ tsp  mustard seed 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  celery seed 
 1 pkt (3-oz)  lemon   gelatin 
 Directions : 
 Combine all vegetables and toss with  oil . 
 Combine remaining ingredients except  gelatin . 
 Boil. 
 Add  lemon   gelatin . 
 Stir to dissolve. 
 Pour over slaw and toss well. 
 Cover and chill overnight or at least 6 hours.
