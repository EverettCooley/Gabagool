Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 white  cake mix  with pudding 
 ¼ cup  vegetable oil 
 3  eggs 
 1 can (8.5 oz)  cream of coconut   [1] 
 8 ounces  sour cream 
 flaked  coconut 
 Cream cheese frosting : 
 8 oz  cream cheese , softened 
 2 tablespoons  milk 
 1 (16 oz) package  powdered sugar 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Beat first 5 ingredients together. 
 Pour into greased and floured 9x13 pan. 
 Bake at 350°F for 40 to 50 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean. 
 Cook cake in pan. 
 Spread  cream cheese  frosting over cake; sprinkle with  coconut . 
 Cream cheese frosting : 
 Beat All Ingredients Together.
 
 Notes : 
 
 ↑   You can find  cream of coconut  in the liquor aisle of your grocery store. The one I use is called  Coco López . It's in the mixer section.
