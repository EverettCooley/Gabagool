 Ingredients : 
 2 pound boneless pieces of  chicken 
 red  chili paste 
 garlic  paste 
 crushed  pineapple juice 
 5  green onions 
 soy sauce 
 Hawaiian salt 
 seasonal spices (cayenne, ginger powder, galangal, and curry powder) 
 hot sauce, optional 
 butter, optional 
 Directions : 
 Prepare and mix the chicken with soy sauce and pineapple juice. The chicken is soaked in the juices, which were already heated inside the skillet. 
 Add seasonal spices and salt according to preference and introduce garlic paste and red chili pepper to the mixture. 
 Keep boiling till the chicken becomes tender. 
 Serve hot with rice. Drizzle with the hot sauce and top with the butter, if desired.
