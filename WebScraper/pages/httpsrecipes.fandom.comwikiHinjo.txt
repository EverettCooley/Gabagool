Description : 
 Hinjo  (Burmese Sour Soup) is best served with  rice .
 
 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 2 teaspoons  sesame oil 
 1  onion , finely sliced, lengthwise 
 2 cloves  garlic , crushed 
 ¼ teaspoon  ground turmeric 
 3  green tomatoes , chopped 
 1 cup  spinach   or  other  greens , torn 
 5 – 6 cups  rhubarb   or   fish stock ,  or  boiling  water 
 ¼ teaspoon dried  shrimp paste 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in a saucepan until very hot. 
 Then fry the  onion ,  garlic  and  turmeric  for 30 seconds. 
 Add in the  tomatoes  and  spinach . 
 Stir for another 30 seconds then add the stock or  water . 
 Let the mixture boil. 
 Add the  shrimp paste , cover the pan and let it simmer over low heat until vegetables are soft. 
 Add  salt  to taste.
