Spare Ribs with Sauerkraut
 Makes 4 servings
 
 
    2 pounds pork spareribs, cut into serving pieces                
   1 tablespoon vegetable oil                                      
   Salt and pepper                                                 
   1 quart sauerkraut                                              
   1 tablespoon brown sugar                                        
   1-1/2 cups water                                                
 
 Heat oil in cooker. Add ribs and brown evenly. Season with salt and
pepper. Pour sauerkraut over ribs and sprinkle with brown sugar. Add
water. Lock lid and bring to full pressure. Adjust heat to maintain
pressure and cook for 15 minutes. Quick-release pressure.
