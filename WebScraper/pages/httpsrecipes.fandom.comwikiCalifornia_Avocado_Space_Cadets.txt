 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 envelopes  unflavored gelatin 
 1 Cup chilled  apple juice 
 3 Cups boiling  apple juice 
 1 medium  California avocado , peeled, seeded and diced 
 Â½ Cup chopped  dried apricots ,  peaches  or whole  golden raisins 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl place the  unflavored gelatin . 
 Pour in the chilled  apple juice  and stir until dissolved. 
 Let stand for 2 minutes. 
 Add the boiling  apple juice  and stir well to combine. 
 Place in refrigerator for 30 to 45 minutes, or until mixture thickens to the consistency of beaten  egg  white. 
 Stir in the  avocado  and dried fruit.
 Spray a 13 x 9-inch dish with aerosol  cooking spray . 
 Pour in  gelatin  mixture. Cover and chill until firm. 
 To serve, cut into bitesized squares, or kids can cut out shapes with cookie cutters. 
 Transport in plastic container for bag lunches.
 Preparation time: 15 minutes
