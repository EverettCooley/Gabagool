 Description : 
 One serving may be exchanged for: ½ fruit exchange.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups  cranberries 
 1½ cups boiling  water 
 ½ cup  orange juice 
 1 teaspoon sucaryl solution 
 For spiced cranberry sauce : 
 2 tbsp  lemon juice 
 ⅛ tsp  nutmeg 
 ⅛ tsp  cloves 
 ⅛ tsp  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Cook  cranberries  in boiling  water  until skins are broken. 
 Then add the remaining ingredients. 
 Then chill in refrigerator.
