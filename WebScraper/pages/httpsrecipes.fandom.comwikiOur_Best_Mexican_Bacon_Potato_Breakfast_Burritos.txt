Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Butler Estate in Irving, Texas in 1988.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  unsalted butter 
 1 medium  potato  finely diced 
 1  red bell pepper  seeded and finely diced 
 4 slices  bacon  cooked and crumbled 
 8  eggs  lightly beaten 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 4 flour  tortillas 
 4 ounces grated sharp  cheddar  cheese 
 ½ cup chunky  salsa 
 2  scallions  finely chopped 
 Directions : 
 Melt butter in a skillet over moderate heat. 
 Sauté potato and pepper until tender about 5 minutes. 
 Add bacon and eggs then cook stirring frequently until set. 
 Divide egg mixture between the tortillas and top with cheese, salsa and scallions. 
 Roll up tucking the ends under.
