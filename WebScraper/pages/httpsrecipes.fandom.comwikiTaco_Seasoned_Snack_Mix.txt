Ingredients : 
 1 package (6 ounces) Pepperidge Farm® Goldfish® Xtra​ Cheddar + Pretzel Mix 
 3 cups salted  popcorn 
 2 cups oven toasted corn or rice  breakfast cereal 
 2 tablespoons  taco seasoning 
 3 tablespoons  butter , melted 
 1/2 teaspoon grated  lime zest 
 Instructions : 
 Heat the oven to 300°F. 
 While the oven is heating, stir the pretzel mix, popcorn, cereal and taco seasoning in a bowl.  Add the butter and toss to coat.  Spread the mixture on a rimmed baking sheet. 
 Bake for 15 minutes or until crisp, stirring once halfway through the baking time.  Stir in the lime zest.  Let the mixture cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes.
