       Wasabi Vinaigrette     
 This is a very different vinaigrette than the everyday variety! It makes simple green salads really come alive with flavor.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups freshly squeezed  orange juice 
 2 teaspoons  sesame seeds 
 3 teaspoons  granulated sugar 
 1/2 cup  canola oil 
 3 teaspoons  vinegar 
 2 teaspoons  soy sauce 
 salt 
 2 teaspoons  Wasabi  powder 
 Directions : 
 Combine ingredients and mix together until well blended. Store in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.
