Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 medium  onion , thinly sliced 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 3 cups cooked  pinto  or  red kidney beans 
 1 cup  tomato juice 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1½ tablespoons  chili powder 
 salt  and ground  black pepper  to taste 
 1½ cups grated  cheddar  or  Monterey jack cheese 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Cook  onion  in  butter  until soft but not brown. 
 Add beans,  tomato juice ,  garlic ,  chili powder ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Simmer 5 to 10 minutes to blend flavors. 
 Add  cheese  and continue heating until  cheese  melts, stirring constantly. 
 Serve bean mixture in soup bowls. 
 Top with mound of  rice  (about ½ cup).
