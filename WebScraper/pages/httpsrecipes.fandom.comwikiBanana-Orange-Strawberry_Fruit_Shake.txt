Description : 
 Recipe reprinted with permission from HealthRecipes.com
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½  banana , frozen 
 6  strawberries , frozen 
 ½ cup  orange juice 
 ½ cup  water 
 1 tbsp  skim milk powder 
 1 tbsp high-quality  protein powder  (optional) 
 1 tsp  flax oil  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 In a blender, process all the ingredients until thoroughly mixed and serve.
