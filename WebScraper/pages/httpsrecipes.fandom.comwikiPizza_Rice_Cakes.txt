 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 lightly salted  rice  cakes 
 1/3 cup pizza sauce 
 1/4 cup sliced  ripe olives 
 1/4 cup diced green  bell pepper 
 1/4 cup sliced  Mushrooms 
 1/3 cup shredded  mozzarella   Cheese 
 
 Directions : 
 Place  rice  cakes on baking sheet. Spread pizza sauce evenly on each  rice  cake; top with ,  pepper , and  Mushrooms , and then with  Cheese . Bake at 400 degrees 10 minutes. Serve immediately.
