Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cans  chili with beans  (16 oz each) 
 2 cups regular size  corn chips 
 1 small  onion , chopped 
 1 cup grated  cheddar  cheese (4 oz) 
 Description : 
 In greased 1½ quart casserole, alternate layers of chili, corn chips and chopped onion. 
 Top with grated cheese. 
 Bake at 350°F for 30 minutes or until heated through. 
 Tip: a good meat stretcher. Serve with fruit salad and French bread.
