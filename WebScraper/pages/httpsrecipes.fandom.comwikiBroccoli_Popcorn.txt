Ingredients : 
 2 heads  broccoli  ; cut into small florets 
 2 tbsp  olive oil 
 1 tsp  lemon juice 
 1 clove  garlic  ; minced 
 1/4 tbsp  sea salt 
 Instructions : 
 Heat oven to 500 degrees. 
 Toss the broccoli with the remaining ingredients in a large bowl until the oil and seasonings are well distributed. 
 Bake broccoli in a glass baking dish, stirring occasionally, for 20 minutes or until crisp and beginning to brown.
