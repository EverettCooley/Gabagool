Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 12 cups 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds  peaches , halved 
 olive oil  cooking spray 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 3 tbsp  cider vinegar 
 1 tsp  Dijon mustard 
 ½ tsp  pepper 
 1 small head  cabbage , shredded 
 2  carrots , peeled, grated 
 4  scallions , thinly sliced 
 ½ cup  mint  leaves, coarsely chopped 
 1 cup toasted  pecans , coarsely chopped 
 Directions : 
 Heat outdoor grill. 
 Coat peaches with cooking spray. 
 Season with ¼ tsp salt. 
 Grill, cut sides down, 6 minutes turning once until lightly marked. 
 Remove to a cutting board and cool, cut into thin wedges. 
 In a bowl, whisk mayonnaise, vinegar, honey, mustard, remaining salt and pepper until blended. 
 Add peaches, cabbage, and remaining ingredients except pecans, toss until evenly dressed. 
 Just before serving, stir in pecans.
