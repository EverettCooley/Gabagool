Description : 
 Sour cream fettuccine from Fiasco, in Marina Del Rey
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: my old recipes 
 Serves 8 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  fettuccine  noodles 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 1 cup  whipping cream 
 2 tbsp  margarine 
 grated  Parmesan cheese 
 salt 
 pepper 
 Directions : 
 Cook  fettuccine  until tender. Drain. 
 Meanwhile, mix  sour cream ,  whipping cream  and  butter  in pan. Heat but don't boil. 
 Add  Parmesan cheese  and season to taste with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Pour sauce over hot  fettuccine  and toss. 
 Sprinkle with more  Parmesan cheese  and chopped  parsley  if desired. 
 Serve at once.
