 Description : 
 This tangy dish starts with authentic fish, sour cream, mayonnaise, and ranch dressing and is baked to red-hot perfection. End your meal with a glorious, new seafood treat at home.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs. fish (any kind) 
 1 cup sour cream 
 1 cup mayonnaise 
 1 pkg. Hidden Valley dry ranch dressing mix  or  (3 cups ranch dressing) 
 1 can French fried onions 
 Directions : 
 Mix sour cream, mayonnaise, and ranch mix together, in a large bowl. 
 Roll dried fish fillets in the mixture, then roll in French fried onions. 
 Bake 20-25 minutes at 350Â° F. The fish is done when it flakes easily. Serve hot and plain or with any seafood.
