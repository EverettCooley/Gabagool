 Description : 
 Serves: 	3-4 
 Prep Time: 	5 minutes. 
 Occasion: 	Any 
 Effort: 	Easy 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup mayyonaise 
 1/4 cup  carrots 
 1/2 cup  cabbage 
 2-3 tsp  caster sugar 
 Directions : 
 MIX ALL THE INGRIEDIENTS IN MAYYONAISE 
 SERVE WITH CHIPS.
