 Description : 
 Binakol  is an Aklanon dish. It is a  Chicken  soup based on sweet  water  of the young  coconut  (buko). 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 kilo  Chicken  (cut into parts) 
 3 heads of  garlic  (crushed) 
 2  onions  (diced) 
 1 small  ginger  (sliced) 
 2-cups  coconut  meat (diced) 
 1-cup  chicken stock 
 3-cups  rice  wash  water 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1/8 teaspoon  black pepper 
 Patis (fish sauce) 
 Directions : 
 1. In a bowl, marinate  Chicken  in  salt  &  pepper . 
 2. In a pot, heat  oil  and saute  garlic ,  Onion  and  ginger . 
 3. Put in the marinated  Chicken ,  chicken stock  and cover for 15 minutes in low heat. 
 4. Add in the  coconut  meat and bring to a boil for 5 minutes. 
 5. Add in  rice  wash  water  and boil for 10 minutes. 
 6. Season with patis. 
 7. Serve hot. 
 Note: * rice   water  is the  water  used for washing  rice  before cooking it.
