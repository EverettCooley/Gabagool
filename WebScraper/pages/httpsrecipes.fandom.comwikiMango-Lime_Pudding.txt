 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  white rice 
 3 cups  milk 
 1/8 teaspoon  salt 
 1 (14-ounce) can  sweetened condensed milk 
 2 ripe  mangos , peeled and finely chopped 
 3 tablespoons freshly-squeezed  lime juice 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  milk  and  salt  in a large saucepan. 
 Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until thick and creamy, about 15 to 18 minutes. 
 Stir in  condensed milk , mangoes and  lime juice . 
 Spoon into bowls and serve at room temperature or slightly chilled.
