Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (24 oz) pkg.  almond bark 
 1 cup  peanut butter , chunky 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 2 cups  Captain Crunch cereal 
 2 cups  Rice Krispies 
 2 cups salted  peanuts , without husks 
 2 cups miniature  marshmallows 
 Directions : 
 Melt almond bark in oven for 20 minutes (i melted in microwave). 
 Mix in chunky peanut butter and vanilla. 
 Add the Captain Crunch cereal, Rice Krispies, salted peanuts, and miniature marshmallows put on wax paper by spoonfuls. 
 Let cool until solid.
