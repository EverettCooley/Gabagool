Peanut Butter Krispies are favorites with youngsters.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3/4 cup  corn syrup 
 3/4 cup  granulated sugar 
 3/4 cup chunky  peanut butter 
 4-1/2 cups krispy rice cereal 
 melted  chocolate 
 Directions : 
 Cook corn syrup and sugar in a pan over medium heat until the mixture bubbles 
 Add peanut butter and stir until melted 
 Pour mixture over cereal 
 Spread into a buttered 9 x 13-inch pan. 
 Drip melted chocolate over the top. 
 Cut when cool.
