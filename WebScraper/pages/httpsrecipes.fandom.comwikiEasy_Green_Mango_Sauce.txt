Ingredients : 
 1 cup  pineapple  chunks 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 3 cups  low-fat milk 
 1 cup  green mango  chunks 
 Directions : 
 Combine  mango  slices with  sugar  and  water  in saucepan. 
 Cook over medium heat until it has the consistency of applesauce.
