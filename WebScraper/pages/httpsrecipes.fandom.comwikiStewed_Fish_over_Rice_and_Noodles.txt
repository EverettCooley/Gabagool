 Ingredients : 
 700 g  king  cut into chunks 
 1 cup each  red ,  yellow , and  green   capsicum 
 4-6 small  carrots ,  broccoli  and  cauliflower 
 1 large  tomato 
 ½ cup  parsley 
 1 teaspoon  oregano 
 dash of  salt  and  pepper 
 1 teaspoon  sage 
 1 package  beef  stew mix 
 Directions : 
 Wash and clean the and vegetables. 
 Cut the into cubes while frozen. 
 Sauté vegetables and seasoning in a tablespoon of  oil  so that they don't stick to the pot. 
 When tender, add and two cups of  water . 
 Boil over medium heat stirring frequently until done. 
 This will be about 10–20 minutes. 
 Add the package of  beef  stew mix to ½ cup of  water  and then add the remaining ingredients. 
 Serve with  rice  or over noodles.
