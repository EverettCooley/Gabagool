      Meat and Taters     
 Ingredients : 
 4 – 6 baking  potatoes , sliced ¼ inch thick 
 1  onion , thickly sliced 
 1  round steak  or 4 – 6  pork chops 
 seasoning mix 
 butter  or  margarine 
 water 
 Directions : 
 In baking dish layer  potatoes  to cover bottom. 
 Pop rings out of  onions  and layer on top of  potato  slices. 
 Place meat on top of  onion  rings. 
 Sprinkle with favorite spices. 
 Place small pieces of  butter  on top of meat and  potatoes . 
 Place a small amount of  water  in bottom of dish (to keep  potatoes  from burning). 
 Cover with tinfoil and bake 350°F for an hour or until meat is desired doneness  (also great with baby  carrots  added in).
