Ingredients : 
 8 large  eggs 
 2 tbsp  cream 
 4 large  bacon  strips 
 2 tbsp  butter 
 2 tbsp chopped  chives 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl, beat the  eggs  with the  cream , chopped  chives ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Melt the  butter  in a large pan over high heat. 
 Fry the  bacon  strips until well done. 
 Remove the  bacon  strips and drain on paper towels. 
 In the same pan, pour in the  eggs  and stir fry over medium heat. 
 Let it cook until the sides are done, then transfer to a large plate. 
 Top with the cooked  bacon  strips. 
 Serve with warm toasts and fruit juice for breakfast.
