       Marimba     
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  Southern Comfort ®  peach liqueur 
 ½ oz  gin 
 ¼ oz  Amaretto   almond liqueur 
 1 oz  pineapple juice 
 1 oz  mango juice 
 ¼ oz  lime juice 
 Directions : 
 Shake and strain into a cocktail glass. 
 Garnish with  orange  and  pineapple  slices.
