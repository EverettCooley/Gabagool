 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 chive 
 oregano 
 fine leaf  thyme 
 broad leaf  thyme 
 sweet basil 
 parsley 
 celery ( and any other you may wish to add, but not  garlic  or  Onion , those can be added each time you season meat ) 
 salt 
 vinegar 
 Directions : 
 Wash herbs and cut up very small or blend in blender in  vinegar  ( don't add  water ) 
 Add  salt  and mix well, then place in a clean bottle and keep in the fridge
