Description : 
 French Dressing as served at le St. Germain in Hollywood
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: my old recipes 
 Makes 1 cup 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ tbsp  salt 
 ½ tbsp  pepper 
 ¼ tbsp  white pepper 
 1 tbsp  Dijon mustard 
 1 tbsp hot  dry mustard 
 2 tbsp  red wine vinegar 
 3 tbsp  red wine 
 10 tbsp  peanut oil 
 Directions : 
 Mix  salt ,  pepper ,  white pepper , and mustards and mix well. 
 Add  vinegar  and wine. 
 Beat until smooth. 
 Gradually add  oil  and beat until dressing coats a metal spoon. 
 The consistency should not be too thin or too thick. 
 Serve with cold vegetables or salad greens.
