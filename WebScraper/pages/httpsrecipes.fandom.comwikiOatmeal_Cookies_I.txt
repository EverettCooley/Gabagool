Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 ½ cup  molasses 
 ¾ cup  fat 
 2  eggs 
 ¼ cup sweet  milk 
 2 teaspoon  cinnamon 
 1 teaspoon  cloves 
 1 teaspoon  soda 
 2 cups  flour 
 1 cup chopped  raisins 
 2 cups  oatmeal 
 Directions : 
 First mix ingredients in the order given. 
 Melt the fat before adding it and sift the soda and spices with the  flour . 
 Drop by teaspoonfuls on greased pans and bake in a moderate oven (380°F).
