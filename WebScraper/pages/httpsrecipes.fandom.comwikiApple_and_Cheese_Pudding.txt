 Ingredients : 
 450 g (1 lb) eating  apples , sliced 
 25 g (1 oz)  butter 
 a few drops  vanilla essence 
 150 ml  milk 
 100 g (3* oz)  curd cheese 
 2  eggs 
 25 g (1 oz)  caster sugar 
 Directions : 
 Cook the  apples  gently in  butter  and  vanilla essence  until slightly soft, place in a buttered ovenproof dish. 
 Beat together the  milk ,  cheese ,  eggs  and  sugar . 
 Pour over the  apples . 
 Bake at 190 °C / 375 °F / gas 5 for 25 minutes. 
 Serve hot.
