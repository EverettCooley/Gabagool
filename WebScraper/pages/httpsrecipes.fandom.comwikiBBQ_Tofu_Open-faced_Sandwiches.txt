Ingredients : 
 6 oz BBQ-flavored  tofu 
 2 x 4 oz slices  cornbread , about 4x4 inches square 
 6 oz  vegan cheese  slices,  cheddar  cheese flavored 
 1 cup vegetarian  baked beans , heated through 
 ¼ cup soy “ bacon ” bits 
 Directions : 
 Preheat broiler. 
 Spray skillet with nonstick  cooking spray . 
 Slice  tofu  in half horizontally. 
 Heat skillet over medium heat, and grill  tofu  halves until golden on both sides, about 8 minutes total. 
 Meanwhile, arrange  cornbread  slices on baking pan. 
 Top with sliced  tofu  and  cheese  slices, and broil until  cheese  melts, for 3 to 5 minutes. 
 Remove from oven, top with equal portions of beans, sprinkle with soy “ bacon ” bits and serve.
