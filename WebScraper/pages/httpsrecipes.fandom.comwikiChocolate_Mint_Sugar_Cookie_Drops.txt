chocolate   mint   sugar  Cookie Drops
 
 2 1/2 c  flour ; Unbleached 
 1 1/2 ts  baking powder 
 3/4 ts  salt 
 1 1/4 c  sugar ; Divided 
 3/4 c  vegetable oil 
 2  eggs ; Large 
 1 ts  vanilla extract 
 1 1/2 c  mint - chocolate chips ; 
 Use 1 10-Oz Bag of Nestles  mint - chocolate chips  in This Recipe. 
 
In Medium Bowl, Combine  flour ,  baking powder  And  salt ; Set Aside. in aLarge Bowl, Combine 1 Cup  sugar  And  vegetable oil ; Mix Well. Beat in EggsAnd  vanilla extract . Gradually Beat in The  flour  Mixture. Stir InMint- chocolate chips . Shape Into Balls Using Rounded Teaspoonfuls OfDough; Roll in Remaining 1/4 Cup of  sugar . Place on Ungreased CookieSheets. Bake at 350 Degrees F. For 8 to 10 Minutes. Cool Completely OnWire Racks.
 
Enjoy.
