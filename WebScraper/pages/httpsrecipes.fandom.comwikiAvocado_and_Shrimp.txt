Description : 
 When I serve this I slice a  Lime  in half, wrap in a pretty colored piece of net, tie with a tiny ribbon and place on plate with salad.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 genuine  California avocados , sliced in half 
 1 ½ pounds  shrimp , peeled, deveined, and boiled in seasoned  water 
 Sauce : 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 1 clove  garlic , finely chopped 
 1 small  onion , finely chopped 
 1  dill pickle , finely chopped 
 1 tsp  tarragon 
 1 tsp  dry mustard 
 ½ tsp  paprika 
 1 tsp  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Place  avocado  halves on pretty plate upside down on assorted  lettuce , season with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Serve this on top of  avocado  halves and garnish with green and  black olives . 
 Slice  limes  in half and wrap each half in colored net and tie with a tiny ribbon and place on plate. 
 Sauce : 
 Combine sauce and let sit for 1 hour. 
 Cut up  shrimp  in nice size and put in sauce.
