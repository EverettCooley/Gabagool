Description : 
 Makes 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups cooked  rice 
 ½ cup finely chopped  onion 
 ½ cup chopped  green bell pepper 
 ¼ cup chopped  pimentos 
 2 cups (8 ounces) shredded  cheddar  cheese, divided 
 1 x 10¾-ounce can condensed  cream of mushroom  soup 
 1 cup  mayonnaise  or  salad dressing 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 ½ cup  milk 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  onion ,  green pepper  and pimentos in large bowl. 
 Blend 1 cup  cheese , condensed soup,  mayonnaise ,  black pepper  and  milk ; stir into  rice  mixture. 
 Turn into greased shallow 2-quart baking dish; sprinkle with remaining 1 cup  cheese . 
 Bake at 350°F for 25 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
