 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 Cup  orange juice 
 1 ripe  California avocado 
 1 ripe  banana 
 1 Tbsp honey 
 1 Cup nonfat frozen  yogurt  -  vanilla ,  orange , or  peach 
 Directions : 
 Combine  orange juice ,  avocado ,  banana  and honey in blender. Cover and blend until smooth. Just before serving, add frozen  yogurt ; blend until thick and smooth.
