 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 pack (4 serving size) or 
 2 pack ( 8 serving size) jello -powder, any flavor 
 2 1/2 cup Boiling  water 
 Non-stick  cooking spray 
 1 cup Cool-whip 
 2 cup Cut-up fruit 
 Directions : 
 1 Servings: 10 to 12 pour  gelatin  into bowl. 
2 Add 2 Â½ cups boiling  water  to  gelatin . 
3 Stir, until  gelatin  is completely dissolved, about 2 minutes. 
4 Spray pizza pan with non-stick  cooking spray . 
5 Pour  gelatin  mixture into pizza pan. 
6 Put pan into refrigerator to chill until firm, about 3 hours. 
7 Remove pan from refrigerator when ready to serve. 
8 Put about 1 inch of warm  water  in sink. 
9 Carefully dip just bottom of pan into warm  water  for 15 seconds. 
10 Spread whipped topping over  gelatin  just before serving, leaving about 1 inch of space around outside edge of  gelatin  for pizza "crust". 
11 Top pizza with fruit, arranging fruit in whatever design you like. 
12 Cut pizza into wedges
