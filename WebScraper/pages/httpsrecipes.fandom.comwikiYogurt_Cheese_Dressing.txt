Description : 
 Source:  Fatfree Sigest  [Volume 9 Issue 42] August 2, 1994
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  yogurt  or  buttermilk 
 1½ cups  yogurt cheese 
 2 – 3 tbsp  vinegar 
 2 – 3  garlic  cloves, minced 
 1 tsp dried  dill weed 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients together.
