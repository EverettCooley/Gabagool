Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group  and also via  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Original recipe 
 Ready in: < 30 minutes 
 Serves/Makes: 2 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup prepared or deli  coleslaw 
 2 tablespoons no-added-sugar tomato  salsa 
 salt and freshly ground  pepper , to taste 
 ½ pound sliced, roasted  turkey breast 
 4 slices whole-wheat  bread 
 Directions : 
 Drain coleslaw in a colander. 
 Toss coleslaw with tomato salsa and add salt and pepper. 
 Divide sliced turkey in half and place on slices of bread. 
 Spoon half the coleslaw on top of the turkey and cover with remaining bread slices. 
 Serve extra coleslaw on the side. 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Per Serving: 
 
 Cals: 351 | Fat: 7 g | Chol: 94 mg | Sod: 466 mg | Carb: 33 g | Dietary Fiber: 5 g | Sugars: 8 g | Protein: 40 g 
 Diabetic Exchanges: 2 Starch, 4 Very Lean Meat, 1 Vegetable, ½ fat
