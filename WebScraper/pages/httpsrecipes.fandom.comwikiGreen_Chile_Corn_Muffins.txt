Ingredients : 
 1-1/4 C.  Cornmeal 
 2 tbsp.  baking powder 
 1 c.  cheddar , shredded 
 8 oz.  corn 
 1 c.  sour cream 
 4 oz.  green chiles 
 1/3 c.  canola oil 
 2-3  eggs , lightly beaten 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 Combine cornmeal and baking powder 
 Add cheese, corn, sour cream & chilies 
 Stir till combined 
 Add oil/eggs and stir till just combined; do not overmix. Batter will be very stiff 
 Place batter into greased muffin cups and bake @ 400deg for 18-20 min. 
 Serve warm.
