 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 Fresh  guava , peeled and cut into bite-sized pieces. 
 Juice of one  lemon 
 grated coconut 
 Honey 
 Directions : 
 Add the  lemon juice  to the  guava  pieces and set side in a glass bowl. 
 Meanwhile, bring some  water  to the boil, add some honey and allow it to cool. 
 Add the guava- lemon  mixture to the honey  water . Make sure the fruit are only coated with  water , not covered in liquid. Stir in gently 
 Cover the fruit salad and allow it to stand for 30 minutes before serving 
 Top with grate  coconut  and serve.
