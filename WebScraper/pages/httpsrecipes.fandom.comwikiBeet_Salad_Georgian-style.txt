Ingredients : 
 1 lb  beets  (fresh and boiled or roasted is best, but canned beets work out fine) 
 8 – 12  prunes , to taste 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 ¼ – ½ cup  walnuts  (pre-ground if not using a food processor) 
 salt , to taste 
 pepper 
 mayonnaise , to taste (optional) 
 Directions : 
 For a smooth style salad or spread: toss everything in a food processor; pulse until mixed well; season with  salt ,  pepper , and  mayo  (optional) to taste. 
 For a chunkier style salad: grate beets on the medium side of a grater; grind or finely chop  walnuts  (or, buy them pre-ground); mince  prunes  (or grind them with  walnuts  in a food processor or by hand); mix everything together and season to taste.
