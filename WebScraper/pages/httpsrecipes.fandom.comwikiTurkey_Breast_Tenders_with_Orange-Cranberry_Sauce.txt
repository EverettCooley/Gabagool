Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  turkey  breast tenders 
 Orange-cranberry sauce : 
 ⅓ cup  orange juice 
 ¾ cup whole  cranberry sauce 
 2 tbsp  brown sugar 
 1 tbsp  soy sauce 
 ½ tbsp  allspice 
 Directions : 
 Add ingredients to a crockpot. 
 Cook on low 7 to 9 hours. 
 Thicken gravy to serve with mashed  potatoes ,  rice  or noodles.
