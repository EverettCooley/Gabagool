chocolate  Covered  Peanut butter  Balls
 
 2 C.  Peanut butter 
 1 Lb.  powdered sugar 
 1 Stick  butter 
 3 C.  rice  Krispies 
 
Mix Well. Make Into Small Balls. Chill Thoroughly.
 
In Top of double boiler Melt:
 
 1 (6 Oz.) Pkg.  chocolate chips 
 1/8 Cake Paraffin. 
 
Enjoy.
