 Prep Time: 5 min. 
 Cook time: microwave for about 5 min. 
 Serves: 7 people 
 
 Description : 
 Very cheesey. Use as a cheese dip for crackers, chips, etc.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 slices of  American cheese 
 4 tbsp of  cream cheese 
 3 tbsp of  butter 
 crackers  - do not put in the recipe.... only for side 
 Directions : 
 Put all of the ingredients in a bowl (other than the crackers) 
 Stick it in the microwave for about 5 minutes.. or until its all warm.
