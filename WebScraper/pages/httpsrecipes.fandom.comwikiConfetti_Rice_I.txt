Description : 
 Makes 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 3 slices  bacon , diced 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice , (cooked in  chicken broth ) 
 1 x 4-ounce can sliced  mushrooms , drained 
 1 cup cooked  green peas 
 ¼ cup diced  pimentos 
 1 tablespoon chopped  chives 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Cook  bacon  until crisp. 
 Drain off excess fat. 
 Add  rice ,  mushrooms ,  peas , pimientos,  chives ,  salt  and  pepper  to pan; heat thoroughly.
