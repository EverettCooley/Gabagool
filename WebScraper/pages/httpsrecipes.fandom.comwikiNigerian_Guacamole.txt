 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 ripe avocados 
 1/3 cup finely chopped  onions 
 Minced  garlic  to taste 
 1 large ripe  tomato , chopped 
 1/4 cup nondairy  sour cream  or mashed  soft tofu 
 Juice of 1 small  lemon 
 Directions : 
 Peel avocados and remove pit. 
 Mash with fork, add remaining ingredients, and stir. 
 If you don't serve immediately, put one of 
 the pits back in and cover tightly, then refrigerate.
