 Description : 
 Bokkepootjes  means "goat's feet".
 A lovely and sweet mixture of biscuit,  almonds  and  cream  filling. This is a luxury biscuit, that will leave you wanting more.
 
 Ingredients : 
 8  egg  whites 
 350gr.  Sugar 
 500gr.  ground almonds 
 rind of 1  lemon 
 1 teasp  cinnamon 
 3 satchels  vanilla   Sugar 
 Also:  dark chocolate  for dipping.
 
 Directions : 
 Beat the  egg  whites stiff with a measure of  Sugar . Then mix in the other ingredients a bit at a time, changing between them (some of the  Sugar , some of the  almonds , some of the  lemon , repeat until all ingredients are mixed in).
 Put the mix in a piping bag and pipe rolls of 6 - 7 cms length onto the baking sheet. Leave to dry for several hours and then put the sheet in a preheated oven at 160C. Bake slowly, they are not supposed to go brown. Leave to cool.
 Melt the  dark chocolate  and spread some on the bottom of the biscuit. Then stick a second biscuit onto the  chocolate .
 Dip both ends of each "bokkepootje" into the  chocolate  and leave to harden.
