Description 
 This red-hot smoothie is made with real fruit and ginger powder and has a stone-cold taste with milk. Serve up an incredible, fruity treat with other desserts or main dishes. 
 
 Ingredients 
 1 medium  banana , peeled and cut into chunks 
 1 large seedless  orange , peeled and sliced 
 2 cups  plain  or vanilla  soy milk 
 1 teaspoon  powdered ginger   or  (1 pack red cinnamon candies) 
 3 tablespoons cinnamon 
 3 or 4  ice  cubes 
 Directions 
 Place all ingredients except ice cubes in a blender. Cover and blend on high speed for 15 seconds or until smooth. 
 Add ice cubes, cover and blend for 15 seconds more or until well blended. Serve immediately.
