 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 envelopes  unflavored gelatin 
 1/2 c.  coffee  liqueur 
 3 c. hot  coffee 
 3/4 c.  Sugar 
 Pinch of  salt 
 whipping cream 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl, sprinkle  gelatin  over liqueur to soften. Add  coffee ,  Sugar , and  salt . Stir until clear. Chill in flat pan until set. Cut into cubes and serve on bed of  whipped cream . Or pour into mold, chill until set, and unmold on bed of  whipped cream .
