Ingredients : 
 4 cans vegetarian baked beans 
 1 large vidalia  Onion 
 1 pound  Turkey   Bacon 
 4 tsps dried minced  garlic 
 2 cups  ketchup 
 1 cup  chili sauce 
 1/4 cup  mustard 
 1/4 cup  Apple  cidar  vinegar 
 1/2 cup  brown sugar 
 1 cup shredded sharp  Cheddar   Cheese 
 3 tbsp fresh ground  pepper 
 3 tsp  salt  (to taste) 
 3 tbsp  olive oil 
 Directions : 
 Saute the  Bacon  until very crispy in the  olive oil . Remove to drain, but leave  oil  in pan. 
 Chop the  Onion  and saute in the  olive oil  until dark brown. 
 Crumble  Bacon . 
 In large sauce pan, put all ingredients except the beans and simmer on low until the  Cheese  melts completely. 
 Rinse the beans throughly and drain. 
 Pour into casserole dish. 
 Pour sauce over beans and fold in until beans are completely mixed in. 
 Bake covered on 350 for 30 minutes. 
 Remove cover and bake for 20 more minutes.
