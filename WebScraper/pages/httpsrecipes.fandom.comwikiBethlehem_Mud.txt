 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  flour 
 1/2 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 7 tbsp  cocoa 
 1 square  margarine 
 4 tbsp  corn syrup 
 2 cups  Sugar 
 4 x  eggs 
 2 tsp  vanilla 
 1 cup  peanuts  ( walnuts  or  pecans ) 
 1 cup  coconut 
 FROSTING : 
 1/3 cup  cocoa 
 1 stick  margarine , melted 
 1 cup  powdered sugar 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 7 oz (in jar)  marshmallow   cream 
 Directions : 
 Sift together first four ingredients. 
 Melt together the  margarine  and syrup. 
 Cool and add 2 cups  Sugar  and 4  eggs . 
 Add dry ingredients and add  vanilla ,  nuts  and  coconut . 
 Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes. 
 FROSTING : 
 Mix all ingredients except  marshmallow   cream . 
 When the brownie part is still warm, spread on the  marshmallow   cream , then the  chocolate  frosting.
