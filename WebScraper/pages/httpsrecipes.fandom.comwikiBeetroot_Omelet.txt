Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Harrington Estate in Fort Worth, Texas in 1991.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  flour 
 ¼ teaspoon  rice flour 
 1 grated  carrot 
 1  onion  finely chopped 
 1  tomato  finely chopped 
 ½  beetroot  grated 
 1 bunch  spring onion  finely chopped 
 ½ teaspoon  green chilies  crushed fine 
 2 tablespoons  oil 
 ¼ teaspoon  soda bicarbonate 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 oil  to shallow fry 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients except oil then add enough water to make batter. 
 Spread a big spoonful thickly into a hot omelet pan. 
 Sprinkle a teaspoon oil over it. 
 Flip over and roast the other side. 
 Serve immediately.
