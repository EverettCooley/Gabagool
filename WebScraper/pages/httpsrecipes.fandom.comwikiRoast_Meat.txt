 This recipe taken from www.Portuguese-recipes.com 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb. chourico 
 4-5 lbs. sirloin roast or of your choice 
 6  potatoes , peeled 
 6  carrots , peeled 
 1  Onion  chopped 
 1 small head of  cabbage  quarted 
 1 cup  red wine vinegar 
 1 cup  Marsala  wine 
 1 cup  ketchup 
 salt  &  pepper  to your taste 
 2 cloves of  garlic  sliced 
 Directions : 
 flour  meat and brown in  oil  on both sides. 
 Put meat in roasting pan and add all but the  carrots ,  potatoes  &  cabbage . 
 Roast for about 3 hours. 
 Then add the  carrots ,  potatoes  &  cabbage  cover. 
 Cook until the  potatoes  are done. 
 In the meanwhile keep marinating the meat and vegetables with the gravy that's in the pan. 
 When the  potatoes  are done, take the roast out and let rest for about 15 minutes then slice and serve.
