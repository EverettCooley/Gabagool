 Ingredients : 
 2 dozen clams 
 8 tablespoons  olive oil 
 10  garlic  cloves, minced 
 1 cup fresh  bread  crumbs 
 1 tablespoon  oregano 
 lemon  wedges 
 Directions : 
 Put clams in freezer for 20 minutes. Loosen them, save juice and bottom shell to put clam in. Heat  garlic  gently in  olive oil  until light golden brown. Spread very little on each clam and mix remaining with  bread  crumbs.
 Sprinkle to cover clams and top with  oregano .
 Place in baking dish and bake for 25 minutes at 375 degree
