Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  Sugar 
 2 cups  flour 
 4 tablespoons  shortening 
 1/4 cup  milk 
 1/2 cup  cocoa powder 
 1  egg , beaten 
 3 teaspoons  baking powder 
 pinch of  salt 
 Directions : 
 First cream the  shortening  with the  Sugar  and  cocoa powder . 
 Add gradually the  milk  and the beaten  egg . 
 Mix well. 
 Mix  flour ,  baking powder  and  salt  and add to the first mixture. 
 Roll out on floured pastry board to a thickness of 1/4Â inch. 
 Cut with cookie cutter. 
 Bake on greased baking pan in hot oven for about 15 minutes.
