Description : 
 This is a recipe I obtained from an Estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Konder Estate in Dallas, Texas in 1991.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4  pork chops 
 1 can  chicken broth 
 1  onion  chopped 
 2 cloves  garlic  chopped 
 2 teaspoons  olive oil 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Put oil in frying pan and heat until hot. 
 Brown pork chops on both sides. 
 Add broth, onions, garlic and pepper then simmer on low for 1 hour.
