Ingredients : 
 1 kg  pork 
 1  onion 
 1  tomato 
 1  pepper 
 salt 
 lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Cut the  meat  into pieces,  salt  and mix with the largely chopped  onion  and  pepper . 
 Let stand for ~2 hours and string on a spit with pieces of  onion ,  pepper  and sliced  tomatoes  in between. 
 Grill. 
 Serve hot with vegetables of your own choice. 
 Baste with  lemon juice  if desired.
