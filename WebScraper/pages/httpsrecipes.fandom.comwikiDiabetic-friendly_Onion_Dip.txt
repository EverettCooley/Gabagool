Description : 
 Yield: 1½ cups
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 oz plain low-calorie  yogurt 
 ½ an  onion , finely chopped 
 2 tsp  lemon juice 
 2 tbsp  parsley 
 1 dash  hot pepper sauce 
 1 dash  horseradish 
 1 dash  salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients. 
 Chill thoroughly. 
 Nutritional information : 
 1 serving = ½ milk, ½ veg calories: 7
