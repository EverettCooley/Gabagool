 Ingredients : 
 3 ripe  mangoes 
 ¼ cup confectioners  sugar 
 ¼ cup  framboise  (raspberry liqueur) 
 1 cup  raspberries 
 1  pound cake , frozen or homemade 
 whipped cream 
 Directions : 
 Peel and cut the flesh into slices and place mangoes in a bowl. Sprinkle with  sugar . 
 Add  framboise  and chill. 
 Just before serving: slice pound cake and place on dessert plates. 
 Top with the  mango  mixture. 
 Top this with a dollop of  whipped cream  and a handful of fresh raspberries.
