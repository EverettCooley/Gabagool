Description : 
 37 calories per square, makes 36 squares
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (3oz) box any flavor sugar-free [[gelatin 
 8 oz light  cream cheese 
 1 pack D-Zerta  topping, whipped  as package directs 
 2 cups  graham cracker  crumbs 
 22 packs  Sweet n' Low 
 7 tbsp  margarine , from sticks 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve  gelatin  in 1 cup warm  water . 
 Set aside to cool. 
 Mix together 6 pkgs Sweet n' Low and graham cracker crumbs. 
 Add melted  margarine . 
 Press 2 cups of crumb mixture firmly into 9 x 13 pan. 
 Reserve rest for top. 
 Cream 16 pkgs Sweet n' Low with  cream cheese . 
 Stir in prepared whipped topping. 
 Pour mixture over crust and sprinkle with remaining cracker crumbs. 
 Chill 3 â€“ 4 hours. 
 Also freezes well.
