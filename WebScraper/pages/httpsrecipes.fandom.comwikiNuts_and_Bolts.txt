Description : 
 This recipe is included in the New Roadhouse Recipes book and is from Judy Vernon, Bellevue, WA
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Serves 8 to 12 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅓ cup  margarine 
 1 tbsp + 1 tsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ⅛ tsp  garlic salt 
 1 cup  Cheerios  cereal 
 1 cup Rice Chex cereal 
 1 cup Wheat Chex cereal 
 1 cup thin  pretzels 
 1 cup  peanuts 
 Directions : 
 Melt margarine in shallow pan. 
 Stir in Worcestershire sauce, salt, and garlic salt. 
 Add cereal, pretzels, and peanuts and stir gently. 
 Heat in 300°F oven for about 30 minutes, stirring gently every 10 minutes.
