OYSTER BIENVILLE 
 Ingredients:
1/4 cup butter or margarine 
3 tablespoon flour 
1 clove garlic 
1 tablespoon onion juice 
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce 
1/4 teaspoon celery seed 
1 2-ounce can mushrooms 
3/4 cup liquid 
18 medium boiled Louisiana shrimp 
1� pints medium Louisiana oysters 
Parmesan cheese 
 Chef: Catherine Longman
 Serves: 6 
To make sauce, melt butter and add flour, garlic, onion juice, 
Worcestershire sauce, celery seed and liquid. Add mushrooms and 
shrimp. Cook until thickened. Put oysters in saucepan and simmer just 
until edges curl. Drain. Put oysters on cleaned oyster shells. Cover 
each with sauce mixture. Sprinkle each with cheese. Place on tray 
covered with rock salt. Broil for about 5 minutes or until bubbly.
