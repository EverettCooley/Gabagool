 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 lbs caribou sirloin  Steak 
 1 tablespoon  Sugar 
 1 tablespoon  powdered ginger 
 1/2 clove  garlic , chopped fine 
 1/2 medium  Onion , chopped fine 
 1/4 cup soy sauce 
 1/2 cup  water 
 1 (8 	ounces) can crushed  pineapple 
 Directions : 
 Cut caribou  Steak  into serving pieces. Make a sauce of the  ginger ,  garlic ,  Onion , soy sauce and  water . Add the crushed  pineapple . Pour the sauce over the meat and let stand 1 to 2 hours. Spread the caribou out on a shallow pan and broil 3 to 5 minutes on each side. Serve immediately.
