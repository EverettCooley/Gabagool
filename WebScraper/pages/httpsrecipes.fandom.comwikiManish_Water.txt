 Ingredients : 
 chopped up  goat  head 
 garlic  to taste 
 escallion  to taste 
 thyme  to taste 
 pimiento seed  to taste 
 salt  to taste 
 1 whole  green pepper 
 black pepper 
 1 pkg.  chicken  noodle 
 3 – 6 green  bananas 
 ½ lb  yam 
 3 small  Irish potatoes 
 flour  for dumpling 
 2  cho cho  (optional) 
 1 whole  Scotch bonnet pepper 
 Directions : 
 Wash chopped goat head. 
 Put in a large pot (pressure cooker best) with enough  water  to cover the meat. 
 Add 3 teaspoons  salt , 8-10 seeds of  pimiento  and 5 crushed  garlic  cloves and let cook. 
 Cook on a medium/high stove till meat is tender. 
 Add more  water  and and also 1 pkg.  chicken  noodle. 
 Peel and cut up potatoes and cho chos and add. 
 Peel green  bananas  (or cut off the top and bottom and slit in skin). 
 Add peel and add  yam  when  bananas  are almost cooked. 
 Add dumplings 
 When almost done add  black pepper ,  thyme , a couple  pimiento seeds , escallion, whole Scotch bonnet pepper (do not let it burst open) and let simmer. 
 Serve hot.
