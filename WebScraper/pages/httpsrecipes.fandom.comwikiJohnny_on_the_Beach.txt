Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Makes 2 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz  vodka 
 1 oz  melon liqueur 
 1 oz  black raspberry liqueur 
 ½ oz  pineapple juice 
 ½ oz  orange juice 
 ½ oz  grapefruit juice 
 ½ oz  cranberry juice 
 Directions : 
 Stir with  ice  and strain into shot glass.
