      Tuna Pasta Salad Recipe     
 Ingredients : 
 cups shell  macaroni 
 1 can  tuna 
 1 small  onion , finely chopped 
 ½ cup finely sliced  celery 
 1 large  carrot , grated 
 2 hard boiled  eggs , mashed up 
 ½ cup Hellmann's Real  mayonnaise 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 ¼ tsp  garlic salt 
 ¼ tsp  paprika 
 Directions : 
 Cook  macaroni  until al dente. 
 Hard boil the  eggs . 
 Drain the  pasta  well and let cool. 
 Add  tuna , breaking up any chunks. 
 Add  celery ,  onion ,  carrot . 
 Mix  salad dressing  with  lemon juice ,  garlic salt  and  paprika . 
 Spoon over top of salad. 
 Mix very lightly. 
 Add the mashed  eggs  and again stir lightly.
