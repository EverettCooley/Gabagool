Description : 
 In Romanian: Pui cu mazare verde
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 medium  chicken 
 1¾ lbs / 750 g  green peas 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 ½ teaspoon  flour 
 ½ teaspoon  sugar 
 chopped  dill 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Clean and wash the  chicken  and cut into pieces. 
 Fry in  butter  until yellow. 
 Add the  flour  and fry until yellow. 
 Then add ½ cup  water ,  salt ,  sugar  and  peas . 
 Let simmer, covered, and when almost ready, add the chopped  dill .
