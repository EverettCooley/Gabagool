Description : 
 Despite its ingredients, and its name, this soup has been adapted from its original to suit the Dutch palate.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ liter stock 
 50 g  minced beef 
 40 g  vermicelli 
 1 tbsp finely chopped  parsley 
 celery 
 Directions : 
 Bring the stock to the boil, sprinkle in the broken  vermicelli  and leave to boil gently 15 – 20 minutes. 
 Roll the mince into small balls and add in for the last 10 minutes. 
 Add the  parsley  and  celery  just before serving.
