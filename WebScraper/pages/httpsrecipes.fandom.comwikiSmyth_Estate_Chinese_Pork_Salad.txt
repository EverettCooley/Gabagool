Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Smyth Estate in Plano, Texas in 1990. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  pork loin  cut into strips 
 ½ cup stir fry sauce 
 ½  red onion  peeled and thinly sliced 
 20 ounces frozen  peas  thawed and drained 
 8 ounce can  mandarin oranges  drained 
 3 ounce can  chow mein noodles 
 Directions : 
 Marinate pork strips in sauce for 30 minutes. 
 In large nonstick skillet stir-fry pork and onion over medium high heat for 5 minutes. 
 In a large bowl toss all ingredients together and serve immediately.
