Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Gingham Estate in Fort Worth, Texas in 1992.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups  red leaf lettuce 
 2 cups fresh  strawberries 
 2  kiwi  fruit 
 ¼ cup  honey 
 ¼ cup  tarragon vinegar 
 ¼ cup  salad oil 
 ½ teaspoon  dill weed 
 Directions : 
 Wash dry and tear  leaf lettuce  then wash, hull and quarter strawberries. 
 Peel and slice  kiwi  then toss all gently in bowl. 
 Combine  honey ,  vinegar ,  oil  and  dill  and shake until well mixed. 
 Just before serving drizzle over salad ingredients.
