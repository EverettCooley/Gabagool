 Ingredients : 
 2 cups (16 ounces) good  vodka  (recommended: Stolichnaya or Finlandia) 
 1 cup (8 ounces)  orange liqueur  (recommended:  Cointreau ) 
 1 cup (8 ounces)  pomegranate juice  (recommended: Pom Wonderful) 
 Â½ cup freshly squeezed  lime juice  (3 limes) 
 lime peel  strips, to garnish 
 Directions : 
 Combine the vodka, orange liqueur, pomegranate juice, and lime juice in a pitcher and refrigerate until ready to use. 
 Pour the mixture into frozen martini glasses with a twist of lime peel and serve immediately.
