 Ingredients : 
 1Â lb.  Crawfish  (thawed, if frozen) 
 2 tablespoons  butter 
 1 rib of  celery  (chopped) 
 1  Onion  (chopped) 
 Mayonnaise 
 mustard 
 Pickeled Relish 
 salt  &  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter  in pot, saute' the  Onion  and  celery  for a couple of minutes. 
 Add  Crawfish , saute' about 10 minutes. Pour everything into a food processor. 
 Mix until everything is well chopped. 
 Add  Mayonnaise ,  mustard , relish &  salt  &  pepper  to your satisfaction. 
 Eat with crackers or on bread (like  Chicken  salad)
