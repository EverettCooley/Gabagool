Info 
 Cook Time : 
 Serves : 4
 
 Ingredients 
 2  bananas , sliced 
 1 ripe  pear , sliced 
 4  kiwis , peeled and sliced 
 2 cups sliced  strawberries 
 2  feijoas 
 2 Tbsp  orange juice  concentrate 
 1 ripe  guava 
 Directions 
 1. Combine all of the ingredients, except for the juice and guava, in a large serving bowl. Peel and slice the guava into quarters and place in a blender with the orange juice concentrate. Puree until smooth. Pour the mixture through a sieve to remove the seeds and pour over the fruit salad.
