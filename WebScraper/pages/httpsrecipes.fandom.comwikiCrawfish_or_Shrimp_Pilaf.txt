Ingredients : 
 1 medium  onion 
 1 can (4 oz)  mushrooms  with juice 
 ¼ lbs  oleo 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 1 cup uncooked  rice 
 1  bell pepper  - chopped 
 1 can Swanson's  beef broth 
 1 lbs  crawfish   or   shrimp  - peeled 
 jalapeño pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in 1 pot. 
 Bring to a boil and turn down and simmer until  rice  is cooked. 
 If you have a Hitachi  rice  cooker, use 1½ cups of  rice  using the measure supplied with the cooker and turn it on to cook. 
 When it clicks off to warmer, dinner is ready.
