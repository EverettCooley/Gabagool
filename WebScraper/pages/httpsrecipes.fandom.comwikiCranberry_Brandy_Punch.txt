Description : 
 This is a recipe I obtained from an Estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Monroe Estate in Stephenville, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 gallon  cranberry juice 
 2 cups  brandy 
 1 can lemonade 
 1 can pink lemonade 
 2 liter bottle  Squirt 
 2 liter bottle  7-Up 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together and freeze. 
 Thaw out about 15 minutes prior to serving.
