 Description : 
       Chili Butter     
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  chili powder 
 1 teaspoon ground  cumin 
 ½ teaspoon  ground red pepper 
 ½ cup  butter , softened 
 Directions : 
 Stir ingredients into softened  butter . 
 Cover and chill at least 8 hours. 
 Serve on warm  tortillas , poultry, or meat, or stir into  refried beans  or  rice . Reserve the spiced butter in the refrigerator.
