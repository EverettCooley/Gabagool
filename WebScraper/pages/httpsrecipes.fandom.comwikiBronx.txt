      Bronx     
 Ingredients : 
 50 ml  gin 
 15 ml  dry vermouth 
 15 ml  red vermouth 
 35 ml  orange juice 
 Directions : 
 Shake the ingredients and strain, decorate the cocktail with  cherry .
