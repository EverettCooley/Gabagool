Description : 
 This recipe taken from www.Portuguese-recipes.com 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lean  Ham  hock 
 4  potatoes , chopped 
 1 10 oz Portuguese  Sausage 
 1 minced  Onion 
 2 chopped  celery 
 2 chopped  carrots 
 1 minced clove  garlic 
 2 cans of  kidney beans 
 1 can of stewed  tomatoes 
 1 bunch of watercress or 1 head of  cabbage  depending on season. 
 Directions : 
 First you boil  Ham  until meat comes off the bone, or until soft. 
 Then add the  kidney beans ,  potatoes , Portuguese  Sausage ,  Onion  and  carrots . 
 Then after 20 minutes, put in the rest of the ingredients. 
 Then wait 10 minutes then add a dash of red pepper then your done.
