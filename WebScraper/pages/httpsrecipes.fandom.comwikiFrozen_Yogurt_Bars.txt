Description : 
 Contributed by Lilly at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 8 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 (8-oz) containers fruited  yogurt , any flavor 
 1 cup fresh or frozen  fruit , cut up 
 ⅔ cup  instant non-fat dry milk 
 2 tbsp  honey 
 2 cups  Grape nuts cereal 
 Directions : 
 Place  yogurt , fruit, dry  milk  and  honey  in blender or food processor; blend until smooth. 
 Fold in 1½ cups cereal. 
 Pour into 8" sq pan. 
 Sprinkle remaining cereal over top, pressing down gently. 
 Freeze 4 hr or overnight, cut into 8 bars. 
 If desired, wrap bars individually in foil and store in freezer.
