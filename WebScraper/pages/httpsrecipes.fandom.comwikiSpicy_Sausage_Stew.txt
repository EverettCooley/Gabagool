Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 ½ pound spicy smoked  sausage , diced 
 1 medium  onion , chopped 
 2 medium  carrots , thinly sliced 
 1 rib  celery , chopped 
 1  jalapeno pepper , seeded and minced 
 1 teaspoon  chili powder 
 ½ teaspoon  cumin 
 3 cups low-fat and low-sodium  chicken broth 
 1 x 15 to 16-ounce can  white beans , drained and rinsed 
 1 x 6¾-ounce can  corn , drained 
 6 cups cooked  rice 
 cilantro  or  parsley  for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in 3-quart saucepan over medium-high heat until hot. 
 Add  sausage ,  onion ,  carrot ,  celery ,  pepper ,  chili powder  and  cumin . 
 Cook, stirring 5 to 7 minutes unitl  onions  are tender. 
 Add broth,  beans  and  corn . 
 Cover and simmer 15 minutes. Serve over  rice ; sprinkle with  cilantro  or  parsley .
