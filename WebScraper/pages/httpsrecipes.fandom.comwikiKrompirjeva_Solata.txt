Description : 
 Potato salad
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 medium  potatoes 
 1  onion , sliced thin 
 4 tablespoon  apple vinegar 
 3 tablespoons  salad oil 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Boil  potatoes  in jackets until cooked. 
 Drain and let stand until lukewarm. 
 Peel and slice them thin into a salad bowl. 
 Add the other ingredients. 
 Toss gently and serve.
