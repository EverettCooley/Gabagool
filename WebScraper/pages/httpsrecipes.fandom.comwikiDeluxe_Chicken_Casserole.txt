Makes 4 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups cooked  rice  (cooked in  chicken broth ) 
 2 cups cooked  chicken  chunks 
 1 x 16-ounce can French-style  green beans , drained 
 ½ cup chopped  red pepper , steamed 
 ½ cup chopped  onion , steamed 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1 x 10¾-ounce can condensed  cream of mushroom  soup 
 ½ cup  chicken broth 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  chicken ,  green beans , red pepper,  onion ,  salt ,  pepper , soup and broth in buttered 2-quart baking dish. 
 Bake at 350°F for 20 to 25 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
