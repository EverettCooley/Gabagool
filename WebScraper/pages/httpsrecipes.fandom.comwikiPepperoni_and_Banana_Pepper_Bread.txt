 Prep Time: 10 minutes 
 Cook time: 30 minutes 
 
 Description : 
 A delicious twist to the ordinary Pepperoni Bread. Add hot or mild Banana Peppers for a kick!
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 frozen pizza dough, thawed 
 ¼ lb  pepperoni 
 8 oz  banana peppers  (hot or mild) 
 1½ cup shredded  mozzarella  cheese 
 flour , for rolling 
 Directions : 
 Take the thawed pizza dough and roll out on floured surface until it is about an 8x11 rectangle. 
 Take thinly sliced pepperoni and place on dough. 
 Place banana peppers on top of pepperoni. 
 Sprinkle shredded mozzarella on top. 
 Roll dough up and tuck in the ends with a tight seal. 
 Use an egg wash on the seams to seal. 
 Brush entire roll with the egg wash. 
 Bake at 350°F for 30 minutes or until brown.
