 Ingredients : 
 2 breast of  Duck 
 500 grams muchroum 
 400 grams  green beans 
 3  potatoes 
 5  orange 
 200 grams honey 
 500 grams chiken stock 
 Directions : 
 roast the  Duck  on high temperature in a pan .put the  Duck  on the skin side for 2 minutes.put in the oven for 10 mnts.cook the muchrums in a pan.cook the beans in boiled  water  for 5 mnts.cook the  potatoes  in  water . boile the honey,add the  orange  and the stock.boil until stiff. dress the garnich,put the  Duck  on it.slice the  potatoes , in a circle.pour the sauce.
