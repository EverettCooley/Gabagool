Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz  bourbon 
 ½ oz  brandy 
 ½ oz  crème de menthe 
 club soda 
 Directions : 
 Pour  bourbon ,  brandy , and  crème de menthe  over  ice  into highball glass. 
 Add  club soda  and garnish with a  lemon  twist.
