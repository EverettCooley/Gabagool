 Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 oz  light rum 
 1 dash  Tabasco sauce 
 1 tsp  lemon juice 
 1 1/2 oz  Beef  Bouillon 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Shake with  ice  and strain into a glass full of  ice . Top with a  lemon  wedge covered in  pepper .
