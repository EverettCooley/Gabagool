Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group  and also  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Source: Diabetic Living Diet Online 
 Total Time: 10 mins 
 Servings: 8 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can  ginger ale , diet 
 1 can  water, carbonated 
 1 cup  grape juice , white, unsweetened 
 starfruit 
 Directions : 
 In a pitcher, combine ginger ale, carbonated water, and white grape juice. 
 Serve in chilled champagne glasses or wine glasses. 
 If desired, garnish with starfruit. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 19 Calories | 0g Total Fat | 0g Saturated Fat | 22mg Sodium | 5g Carbs | 0g Dietary Fiber | 0mg Cholesterol | 0g Protein 
 Carb Choices: 0.5
