Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source:  Rival Crock-pot Slow Cooker Dining Lite , May 1992 
 Makes 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 3  chicken breasts , split, skinned and boned 
 pepper 
 paprika 
 20 oz  pineapple  - unsweetened tidbits, drained 
 2 tbsp  mustard Dijon-style 
 soy sauce 
 1  garlic  clove minced 
 Directions : 
 Arrange  chicken  in crockpot. 
 Sprinkle with  pepper  and  paprika . 
 Mix soy sauce,  pineapple  and  mustard  together; pour over  chicken . 
 Add minced garlic. 
 Cover and cook on low 7 to 9 hours or on high 3 to 4 hours. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving: 
 
 210 calories | 4 g fat | 73 mg cholesterol | 153 mg sodium.
