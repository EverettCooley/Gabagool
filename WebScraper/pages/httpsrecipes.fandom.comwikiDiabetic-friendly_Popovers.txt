Ingredients : 
 1 cup  flour 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 2  eggs 
 1 cup  skim milk 
 Directions : 
 Sift  flour  and  salt  together; set aside. 
 Beat  eggs  and  skim milk ; add to  flour . 
 Beat until smooth and creamy. 
 Pour into heated greased muffin tins, filling half full or less. 
 Bake at 375°F for 50 minutes, or until popovers are golden brown and sound hollow. 
 Do not open oven for first 40 minutes. 
 Nutritional information : 
 1 serving = ½ bread, ⅛ meat 
 
 calories = 4
