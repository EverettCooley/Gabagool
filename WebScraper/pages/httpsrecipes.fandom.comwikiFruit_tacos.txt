 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/3 cup Baked  coconut 
 1 cup  strawberries , sliced 
 1/2 cup Seedless  Green Grapes  - halved 
 1 med  Apple , pared, cored and - chopped 
 1 small  banana , sliced 
 2 tbsp Pourable fruit, any flavor-(found in jelly section of -store) 
 6 Taco shells 
 1/3 cup  vanilla   yogurt 
 Directions : 
 1 Spread  coconut  on baking sheet. 
2 Toast in 350 °F oven for 7 to 12 minutes, stirring often. 
3 Meanwhile, in medium bowl, stir together  strawberries ,  grapes ,  Apple ,  banana , and pourable fruit. 
4 Fill taco shells evenly with fruit. 
5 Top filled tacos evenly with  yogurt . 
6 Sprinkle with toasted  coconut . 
7 prep time: 15 minutes. number of servings: 6
