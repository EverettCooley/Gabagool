Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 6 cups 
 Ingredients : 
 6 cups  carrots , julienned or grated 
 2 tsp  caraway seeds 
 ⅔ cup  mayonnaise  or combination of mayo and  sour cream 
 1 tbsp  white wine vinegar 
 1½ tsp  salt 
 2 tsp  sugar 
 Preparation : 
 Combine carrots and caraway seeds. 
 Mix together remaining ingredients and toss with carrots. 
 Correct seasonings and refrigerate several hrs before serving.
