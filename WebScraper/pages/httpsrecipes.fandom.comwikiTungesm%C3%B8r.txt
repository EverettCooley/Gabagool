Description : 
       cooked tongue     
 Tongue spread 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup cooked  tongue — finely chopped 
 1 teaspoon  prepared mustard 
 1 tablespoon soft  butter 
 ¼ teaspoon  paprika 
 1 hard-boiled  egg yolk 
 1  lemon , juice only 
 1 dash  nutmeg 
 Directions : 
 Chop the tongue very fine. 
 Add balance of ingredients in order given and mix well.
