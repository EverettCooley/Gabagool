      All-American Pizza Burgers     
 Ingredients : 
 1½ lbs lean  ground beef 
 1½ lbs  turkey sausage 
 1½ teaspoons  salt 
 1 teaspoon  pepper 
 vegetable  cooking spray 
 1 (14 ounces) jar  pizza sauce , divided 
 ¾ cup fresh grated  Parmesan cheese 
 1 medium  onion , chopped 
 12 slices  mozzarella  cheese 
 12  hamburger buns 
 Directions : 
 Combine ground  beef ,  sausage ,  salt , and  pepper 
 Coat  grill  rack with  cooking spray . 
 Place on  grill  over medium-hot coals. 
 Place patties on rack and cook, uncovered, 5 minutes on each side or until done. 
 Sprinkle with  Parmesan cheese  and if desired,  onion . 
 Serve on  buns  with  mozzarella  cheese and remaining  pizza sauce .
