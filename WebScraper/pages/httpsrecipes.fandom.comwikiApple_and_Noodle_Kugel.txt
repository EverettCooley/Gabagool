Description : 
 This is sinfully delicious. A couple tablespoons of  brandy  couldn't hurt it either!
 
 Ingredients : 
 4  eggs 
 1 lbs creamed  cottage cheese 
 1 cup dairy  sour cream 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 1 tbsp ground  cinnamon 
 ¾ tsp  salt , optional 
 ½ tsp ground  nutmeg 
 1 tsp  vanilla extract 
 2 cup green  apples , peeled and diced 
 1 cup dark  raisins 
 ½ lbs medium-width  egg noodles  slightly undercooked and drained (4 cups) 
 2 tbsp  butter  or  margarine 
 ½ cup cornflake crumbs 
 ¼ cup  slivered almonds , blanched 
 Directions : 
 In large bowl beat  eggs  with  cottage cheese ,  sour cream ,  sugar ,  cinnamon ,  salt ,  nutmeg  and  vanilla . 
 Stir until well mixed. 
 Stir in  apples ,  raisins , and noodles until well mixed. 
 Pour into buttered 12x8x2-inch baking dish. 
 In small saucepan, melt  butter . 
 Stir in cornflake crumbs and  almonds . 
 Spoon over noodle mixture. 
 Bake in preheated 350 °F oven 40 minutes or until crumbs are golden. 
 If desired, serve with  sour cream  and garnish with ground  nutmeg .
