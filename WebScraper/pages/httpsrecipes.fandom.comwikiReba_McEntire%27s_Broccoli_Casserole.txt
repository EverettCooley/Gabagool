Reba Mcentire's  Broccoli  Casserole
 
Makes 6-8 Servings
 
 1 Head Fresh  Broccoli ; Chopped 
 1 Can Cream of  Mushroom  Soup; Undiluted (10 3/4 Oz) 
 1 Can  water chestnuts ; Sliced (8 Oz) 
 1 Jar Cheez Whiz; (16 Oz) 
 1 Cup Raw Minute  rice 
 1 Cup Cracker Crumbs 
 
Preheat Oven to 375 Degrees F. Steam  Broccoli  Just Until Crisp. Drain. MixBroccoli, Soup,  water chestnuts . Cheez Whiz And  rice . Place in Greased
 
 2-Quart Casserole. Top With Cracker Crumbs. Bake 20 to 30 Minutes, or UntilBubbling.
