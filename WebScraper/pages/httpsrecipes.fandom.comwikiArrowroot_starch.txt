 About Arrowroot starch : 
 Wikipedia Article  About Arrowroot starch on Wikipedia 
 Arrowroot is also the name for the easy-to-digest starch from the rhizomes (rootstock) of West Indian arrowroot. This plant should not be confused with Sagittaria species sometimes called "arrowhead" and used as a root vegetable.
 Arrowroot flour is made from the fleshy rootstock of the tropical arrowroot plant. It's white, fine and powdery texture is very similar to cornstarch but it has no flavor at all.
