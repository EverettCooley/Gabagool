Description : 
 This recipe can be made with beef or turkey. Serve the meatballs with additional teriyaki sauce or prepared Thai peanut sauce.
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Original recipe 
 Serves 6 – 8 as a snack 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  ground turkey 
 6  scallions , thinly sliced 
 1 large  carrot , shredded 
 1  egg , beaten 
 ½ cup bottled  teriyaki sauce 
 1 cup  bread crumbs , fresh 
 ¼ cup  sesame seeds 
 vegetable oil  for spraying 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl, combine the turkey, scallions, carrot, egg, teriyaki sauce and bread crumbs. 
 Blend well. 
 Using a 2-ounce scoop, form meatballs with wet hands and place on a baking pan lined with parchment paper or aluminum foil. 
 Sprinkle meatballs with sesame seeds and spray with vegetable oil. 
 Roast in a 375°F oven for 20 minutes or until golden brown on the outside and cooked through.
