 Description : 
 A  Catfish  recipe.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  margarine 
 1/2 cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 1/4 cup yellow  cornmeal 
 1/4 cup  all-purpose flour 
 1/2 teaspoon  pepper 
 1 teaspoon Spanish  paprika 
 2 pounds U.S. Farm-Raised  Catfish  fillets 
 
 
 Directions : 
 1. Preheat the oven to 400 °F. Place  margarine  in a 13x9-inch baking pan and put in the oven to melt while oven is heating. Remove pan from the oven. 
 2. Mix  Parmesan cheese ,  cornmeal ,  flour ,  pepper  and  paprika  in a plastic bag. Add  Catfish  fillets, one at a time, and shake to coat with the Parmesan mixture. Arrange fillets in a single layer in the prepared pan, turning once to coat with  margarine . Sprinkle remaining  Cheese  mixture over . 
 3. Bake for 10 to 15 minutes or until golden brown and flakes easily when tested with a fork.
