Ingredients:
 
 4 cups air-popped popcorn 
 1 tablespoon butter, melted 
 1 teaspoon chili powder 
 Dash garlic powder 
 Directions:
 
 Mix popcorn and melted butter in a large bowl. 
 In a small bowl, combine the seasonings thoroughly. 
 Sprinkle the seasonings over the popcorn and toss together.  Serve immediately.
