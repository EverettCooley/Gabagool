 Description : 
 Although I haven't tried this recipe, I thought it would be a good recipe to try to make my own dressing and possibly add some other spices.
 
       Amish Salad Dressing     
 Ingredients : 
 3/4 	 cup  oil 
 1 	 egg 
 1 1/2 	teaspoons prepared  mustard 
 2 	teaspoons  salt 
 1/3 	cup  Sugar 
 1/2 	cup  flour , plus 
 2 1/2 	tablespoons  flour 
 1 1/3 	cups  Sugar 
 1/3 	cup  vinegar 
 1 	cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Mix thoroughly the  oil ,  egg ,  mustard ,  salt  and 1/3 cup  Sugar . 
Set aside. 
Over low heat blend  flour , 1/3 cup  Sugar ,  vinegar  and  water . 
Bring to boil, stirring constantly until thickened. 
Combine well with first mixture.
