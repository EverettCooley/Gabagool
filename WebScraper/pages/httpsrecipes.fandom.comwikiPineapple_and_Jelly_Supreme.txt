Ingredients : 
 1 pkg. of  gelatin dessert mix  (Jell-O, whatever flavour strikes your fancy) 
 1 cup of boiling  water 
 300 ml of  heavy cream 
 1 tin of crushed  pineapple 
 Directions : 
 Make Jell-O with the boiling water and drained juice of pineapple. 
 Put this in fridge to set. 
 Whip the cream. 
 Put pineapple in cream and fold. 
 When Jell-O is nearly set, beat it with an egg beater. 
 Fold Jell-O into whipped cream. 
 Then place in fridge overnight.
