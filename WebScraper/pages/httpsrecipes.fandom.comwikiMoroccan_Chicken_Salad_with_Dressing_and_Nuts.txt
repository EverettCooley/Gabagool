Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 Dressing : 
 ¼ cup  olive oil 
 ¼ cup  orange juice 
 ⅛ tsp  cumin 
 salt  to taste 
 herbes de Provence , to taste 
 Salad : 
 6 oz pkg baby  spinach 
 ½ cup seasoned  almonds 
 ¼ cup  feta  cheese 
 4 boneless  chicken breasts , cooked and sliced 
 2  oranges , peeled and sliced 
 Directions : 
 Place all dressing ingredients into a bowl. 
 Mix well. 
 Set aside. 
 Place spinach in a bowl. 
 Mix with almonds and cheese. 
 Place mixed salad on 4 dinner plates. 
 Top with chicken and garnish with orange slices. 
 Drizzle dressing to taste over each salad.
