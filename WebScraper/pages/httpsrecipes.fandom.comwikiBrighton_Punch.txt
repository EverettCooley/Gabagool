Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ oz  bourbon 
 ¾ oz  brandy 
 ¾ oz  Bénédictine 
 juice of ½  orange 
 juice of ½  lemon 
 club soda 
 Directions : 
 Shake with  ice  and pour into collins glass nearly filled with shaved  ice . 
 Then fill with  club soda  and stir gently. 
 Decorate with  orange  and  lemon  slices and serve with a straw.
