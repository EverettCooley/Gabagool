Description : 
 The kiss of an angel, the kick of a mule!
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: Dr. Jasper T. Hogan, Jr. 
 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 4 oz English  gin 
 4 oz  white rum 
 4 oz  tequila 
 4 oz  lime juice 
 4 oz  simple syrup 
 Simple syrup : 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Simple syrup : 
 Combine 1 cup  sugar  and 1 cup  water  in a pan. 
 Stir over medium heat 1 minute. 
 Store syrup in refrigerator. 
 Use as needed. (Makes 1Â½ cups) 
 Sea island special : 
 Mix  gin ,  rum ,  tequila ,  lime juice  and  simple syrup . 
 Pour mixture over crushed  ice  and garnish with fresh  lime  slices. 
 To frappe: chill  spirits  in freezer 2 hours before placing all ingredients in blender.
