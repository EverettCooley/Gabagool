Description : 
 This recipe came from the family collection of the Decker Estate in Elmo, Texas.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  lime juice 
 2 cups  tequila 
 2 cups  triple sec 
 2½ cups fresh  pineapple juice 
 fresh  pineapple  slices for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Combine lime juice, tequila, triple sec and pineapple juice. 
 Fill a cocktail shaker half way with ice and fill ⅔ full with margarita mixture. 
 Shake for 30 seconds and pour into glasses. 
 Decorate with fresh pineapple slices and serve ice cold.
