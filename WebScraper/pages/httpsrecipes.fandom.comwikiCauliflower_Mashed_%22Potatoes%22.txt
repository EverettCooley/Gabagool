Description : 
 Very low in calories. You'll think it's mashed  potatoes  and for those that are diabetic you can eliminate the  cheese  and have a great substitute for mashed  potatoes .
 
 Contributed by  Pressurecookerrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb bag of frozen  cauliflower 
 Â½ cup  cheese  (eliminate if diabetic) 
 1 cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Place  cauliflower  in a heat proof pot cover pot with tin foil and place in pressure cooker. 
 Add  water  to pressure cooker base, not in pot with cauliflower. 
 Cook under high pressure for 5 minutes. 
 Remove cauliflower from pressure cooker, add  butter  if desired and  cheese  also if desired. 
 Mash to the consistency of mashed  potatoes . 
 Add  milk  if you want to. 
 If you want to add  cheese  you can add  cream cheese  (tiny chunks) or shredded  cheese  like cheddar or  parmesan cheese .
