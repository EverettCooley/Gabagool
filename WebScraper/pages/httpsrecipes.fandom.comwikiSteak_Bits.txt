Steak  Bits
 
 2 T.  butter 
 1/2 clove  garlic , Crushed 
 1/2 Lb. Top Sirloin  Steak , Cut in Thin Bite Size Pieces 
 1/2 T.  salt 
 Dash  pepper 
 
 1 1/2 Dozen. Slices Buttered Party or  rye bread 
 
Heat  butter  And  garlic  in Blazer Pan Over High Heat. Season  Steak  With  salt  And  pepper  And Quickly Saut? Pieces as Needed on All Sides. Serve at Once on Buttered  bread .Makes 1 1/2 Dozen.
