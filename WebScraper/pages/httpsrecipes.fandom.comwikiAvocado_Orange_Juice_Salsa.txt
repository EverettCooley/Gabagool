Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Original recipe 
 Copyright Mary Sue Milliken and Susan Feniger (The Border Girls) 
 Ingredients : 
 8  tomatillos , roughly chopped (about 2 cups) 
 2  jalapeño chiles , seeded 
 juice of 1  lime  (2 tablespoons) 
 juice of 1  orange  (½ cup) 
 1 teaspoon  sea salt , or to taste 
 ½ teaspoon  black pepper 
 1 or 2 ripe  California avocados , peeled and seeded 
 Directions : 
 Combine the tomatillos, jalapeños, lime and orange juices, salt, pepper, and a tablespoon or two of ice water in a blender or food processor. 
 Purée until smooth. 
 Add the avocados and purée briefly, just until smooth. 
 Add orange juice to thin if necessary. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per Serving (144 g-wt.): 
 
 130 calories (90 from fat) | 10g total fat | 1.5g saturated fat | 5g dietary fiber | 2g protein | 11g carbohydrate | 0mg cholesterol | 390mg sodium
