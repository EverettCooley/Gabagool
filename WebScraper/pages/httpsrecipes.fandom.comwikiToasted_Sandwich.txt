 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 butter 
 Green Chutney (Coriander or Pudina) 
 tomato   ketchup / Sauce 
 Bread Slices 
 pepper 
 Filling of your choice 
 chopped into fine slices 
 -- fillings you can choose from: 
 capsicum 
 Boiled  egg 
 Boiled  Potato 
 tomato 
 cucumber 
 Onion 
 Combination of these 
 Directions : 
 Apply  butter  on half the bread slices, and chutney on the other half. 
 Arrange filling uniformly on the buttered slices, so as that they are evenly placed. 
 Sprinkle  pepper  on the filling. Put droplets of  ketchup / Sauce on the filling. 
 Cover filling with Chutney slices. 
 Grease toaster well with  butter . 
 Toast sandwich in preheated toaster. 
 Serve hot with sauce and chutney on the side.
