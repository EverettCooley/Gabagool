Description : 
 Always check the ingredients to make sure the product is vegan.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 bunch of  broccoli , cut into florets 
 2 cups  white rice 
 1 stick of vegan  margarine 
 ½ cup  all-purpose flour 
 3½ cups boiling  water 
 2 tsp  salt 
 1 tbsp  soy sauce  or  tamari 
 1½ tsp  garlic powder 
 1 tsp  onion powder 
 pinch of  turmeric 
 1 cup  nutritional yeast  flakes 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 pinch of  paprika 
 Directions : 
 Steam the  broccoli , about 6 minutes. 
 Prepare the  rice  according to package directions. 
 Spread the cooked  rice  evenly over the bottom of a 9 x 13 baking dish. 
 Sprinkle the  broccoli  over the  rice  and set aside. 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Melt the  margarine  in a medium frying pan over low heat. 
 Beat in the  flour  with a whisk over medium heat until the mixture is smooth and bubbly, the whisk in the boiling  water ,  salt , sot sauce,  garlic  and  onion  powders, and turmeric. 
 Cook the sauce until it thickens and bubbles, then whip in the  yeast . 
 Add  salt  and  pepper  to taste. 
 Pour the sauce over the  broccoli  and  rice , sprinkle the top with  paprika , and bake for 15 minutes.
