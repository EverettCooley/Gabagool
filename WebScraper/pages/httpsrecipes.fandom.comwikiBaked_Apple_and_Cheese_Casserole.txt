Baked Apple and Cheese Casserole 
In this casserole, apples and cheddar cheese are baked 
together and the result is truly memorable. This makes a 
wonderful dessert, or wake up your next meal and serve 
this as a tempting side dish.
 Prep Time: 15 Minutes 
Cook Time: 30 Minutes 
 WHAT YOU NEED 
 1-package STOUFFER'S Harvest Apples 
1/2-cup sugar 
3-tablespoons flour 
1-cup shredded cheddar cheese 
1/4-cup butter or margarine 
1/2-cup crushed round butter-style crackers 
3-tablespoons Apple Flavor LIBBY'S JUICY JUICE Premium 100% Juice 
 
HOW TO DO IT 
 PREHEAT oven to 350ï¿½F. Grease a 1-quart baking dish.
 MIX sugar and flour in a small bowl; slowly stir in apple juice. 
Mix melted butter and cracker crumbs in a separate small bowl.
 PLACE escalloped apples in prepared baking dish. Layer sugar 
mixture; cheese and cracker crumb mixture over apples.
 BAKE for 20 to 30 minutes, or until hot and top is browned. 
 Serving Size: 4
