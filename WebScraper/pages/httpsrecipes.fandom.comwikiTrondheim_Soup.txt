Description : 
 Serves 6
 
       TRONDHEIM SOUP     
 Ingredients : 
 1¼ liters (quarts)  water 
 4 Tbsp (¼ cup)  parboiled rice 
 ¾ dl (⅓ cup)  Raisins 
 1  cinnamon  stick 
 1 Tbsp  flour 
 2½ dl (1 cup)  whipping cream 
 4 T (¼ cup)  Sugar 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Combine  water ,  rice ,  raisins , and  cinnamon  and bring to a boil. 
 Simmer until  rice  is tender, about 20 minutes. 
 Whisk the  flour  into the  cream  and add. 
 Bring to a boil. 
 Simmer 1 to 2 minutes. 
 Stir in the  sugar  and a few  grains   salt .
