Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 yellow  cake mix  (2 layer) 
 1 package  cream cheese , 8 ounces, room temperature 
 ½ cup  vegetable oil 
 3  eggs 
 1 can  blueberries , drained (15 oz) 
 2 teaspoons  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Combine cake mix,  cream cheese ,  oil , and  eggs ; mix well. 
 Add blueberries and  vanilla ; stir until well combined. 
 Pour into a greased and floured tube pan. 
 Bake at 350°F for about 45 minutes, or until done. 
 Dust with  confectioner's sugar .
