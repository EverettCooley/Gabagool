Description : 
 This recipe is perfect for family picnics and outings.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 10  frankfurters 
 1 cup  flour 
 1½ teaspoons  baking powder 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 2 tablespoons  yellow cornmeal 
 3 tablespoons  shortening 
 1  egg , beaten 
 ¾ cup  milk 
 oil  (for frying) 
 Directions : 
 Pre-cook the  frankfurters  in oven at 350 °F for 15 minutes. 
 Combine  flour ,  cornmeal ,  sugar ,  baking powder  and  salt  in a bowl. 
 Using a fork, work in the  shortening  in the dry mixture until the mixture resembles fine crumbs. 
 In another bowl, mix  egg  and  milk , then add it to the dry mixture. 
 Mix well. 
 dip  the  frankfurters  in the batter, then drop it into hot  oil  and fry until golden on each side. 
 Serve corn dogs on stick, with  mustard  if desired.
