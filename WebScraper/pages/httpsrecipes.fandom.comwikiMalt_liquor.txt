 About Malt liquor : 
 Wikipedia Article  About Malt liquor on Wikipedia 
 Malt liquor is an American term referring to a type of beer that has a high alcohol content and is therefore considered too alcoholic to be called "beer". Each state sets the maximum permitted alcohol level, 8 degree being the maximum.  In the UK, similar beers are called super lager.
