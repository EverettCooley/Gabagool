Description : 
 Makes 6 servings. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice , cooked in  chicken broth 
 1 cup cooked  chicken  breast cubes (about 1 whole breast) 
 1 cup (4 ounces) shredded  Swiss cheese 
 1 cup sliced  celery 
 1  red pepper , chopped 
 ½ cup chopped  Onion 
 ½ cup  green peas 
 ½ cup  milk 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 ½ teaspoon seasoned  salt 
 ½ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 ¾ cup  walnuts , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  chicken ,  cheese ,  celery , red pepper,  onion  and  peas  in large bowl. 
 Stir together  milk ,  mayonnaise ,  salt  and  pepper  in small bowl; add to  rice  mixture. 
 Transfer to 9x13-inch baking dish coated with  cooking spray . Top with  walnuts . 
 Bake at 400 °F for 15 to 20 minutes.
