 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large  carrot  or 2 small  carrots 
 1 tbsb.baking powder 
 1 cup  whole wheat flour 
 1/2 cup  wheat germ 
 3 tbsb.  molasses 
 1/4 cup  maple syrup 
 1/2 cup  milk 
 1/2 cup  Oats 
 1/3 cup  Sugar 
 1  egg 
 1 1/2 tbsb.  olive oil 
 1/2 tsp.  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Combine dry ingredents in a large bowl. 
 In a small bowl combine the other wet ingredents together. 
 Put trhe batter in a small greased cup cake pan, for 15 to 20 minutes. 
 Take out of oven to cool. Store in the fridge in an airtight container. 
 For an extra treat you can spread some  cream cheese  on the top of a muffin when you sereve it.
