Description 
 Cook Time: 35 minutes 
 
 Ingredients 
 penne  noodles 
 Velveeta cheese 
 salsa 
 cooked  chicken  strips 
 Directions 
 Pre-heat oven 375°F. 
 Cook noodles according to box instructions. 
 Slice Velveeta cheese into cubes. 
 Once noodles have cooked, drain the water. 
 In a casserole dish, layer ½ the noodles, ½ the cubes of Velveeta cheese, ½ the salsa, and ½ the chicken. 
 Repeat for 2nd layer. 
 Cook in heated oven for about 20 minutes and then stir. You should see some chunks of cheese, but mostly the consistency should be melted. 
 Return to the oven for about 15 minutes. 
 Remove, stir, and let sit for about 5 minutes. 
 Serve with French bread as a side. 
 Enjoy!
