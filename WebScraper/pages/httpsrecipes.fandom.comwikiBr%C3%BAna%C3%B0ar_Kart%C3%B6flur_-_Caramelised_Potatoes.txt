 Description : 
 Brúnaðar kartöflur - Caramelised  potatoes 
 
       Brúnaðar kartöflur     
 Ingredients : 
 1 kg. cooked  potatoes  (preferably red) 
 50 g.  butter / margarine 
 50 g.  Sugar 
 Directions : 
 potatoes  should preferably be cold, but it is not necessary. They should be small and even sized. If they are too big, cut into smaller pieces, flush with  water  and pat dry. Put the  Sugar  on a medium hot frying pan. When it starts to brown, add the  butter  and stir to mix. Lower temperature and add  potatoes . Roll the  potatoes  around to coat evenly. The caramel covering should be soft.
