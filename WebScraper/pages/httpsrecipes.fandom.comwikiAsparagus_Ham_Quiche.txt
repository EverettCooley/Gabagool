Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Barger Estate in Mckinney, Texas in 1997.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 20 ounces frozen cut  asparagus 
 1 pound cooked  ham  chopped 
 8 ounces  Swiss cheese  shredded 
 ½ cup chopped white  onion 
 6  eggs 
 2 cups  milk 
 1½ cups  baking mix 
 3 tablespoons vegetable flakes 
 ¼ teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 In two greased 9 inch pie plates layer the  asparagus ,  ham ,  cheese  and onion. 
 Beat  eggs  then add remaining ingredients and mix well. 
 Pour half into each pie plate over  asparagus  then bake at 375°F for 30 minutes.
