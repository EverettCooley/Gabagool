Simple and fast to make. This is a cobbler type cake. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 6 servings 
 40 min total 
 10 min prep 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  self-rising flour 
 1 cup  sugar 
 15 ounces  fruit cocktail  (do not drain) 
 Directions : 
 Mix flour and sugar. 
 Gently mix in fruit cocktail. 
 Bake in small casserole dish at 350Â°F for about 30 minutes, till bubbly and golden brown. 
 Mixture will double in size while baking. 
 Can be served warm or room temperature. 
 Top with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream.
