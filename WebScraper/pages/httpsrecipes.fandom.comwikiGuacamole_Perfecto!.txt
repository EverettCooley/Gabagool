 Description : 
 Your guests will say “Ole!” when they taste the fresh ingredients in this guacamole.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 medium  California avocados , halved and pitted 
 1 tsp minced serrano  chile , seeded 
 ½ medium  tomato , diced 
 1 ½ Tbsp chopped fresh  cilantro 
 1 ½ Tbsp diced  red onion 
 ½ fresh  lime 
 ½ tsp  oregano , crushed 
 salt  to taste 
 white pepper  to taste 
 Fresh Tortilla Chips 
 Directions : 
 Scoop the  avocado  out of the skin into a medium sized mixing bowl. 
 With a fork, coarsely mash. 
 Add all other ingredients except the  lime . 
 Lightly blend – authentic guacamole is chunky, not pureed. 
 Squeeze in  lime juice , mix gently and taste for seasoning adjustment. 
 Serve with warm tortilla chips
