Description : 
 This is a recipe I obtained from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Calderon Estate in Mesquite, Texas in 1998. Also part of the family collection from the Pryor Estate in Grapevine, Texas in 1996.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 gallon  cider 
 2  cinnamon  sticks 
 8 whole  cloves 
 4 whole  allspice 
 ½  lemon  sliced 
 ¼ cup  honey 
 Directions : 
 Add spices, lemon and honey to cider then simmer 10 minutes.
