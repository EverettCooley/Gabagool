 Ingredients : 
 
 3 teaspoons  wakame  seaweed  or  1 teaspoon  wakame  seaweed flakes 
 4 cups  water 
 ½ cup  onions , thinly sliced 
 ½ cup  carrots , diced 
 1½ tablespoons  mugi miso  ( or  1½ tbsp + ½ tsp  hacho ,  genmai   or   shiro miso ) 
 3 tablespoons  green onions , finely sliced 
 Optional ingredients:
 fresh or dried  tofu 
 shiitake mushrooms 
 leafy  greens 
 daikon 
 lotus root 
 leeks 
 round or root vegetables 
 cooked  noodles  or grains 
 Directions : 
 Rinse  wakame ; soak in cold  water  to cover for 5 minutes and dice, or if using flakes, add directly. 
 Bring  water  to a boil. 
 Add  onions  and  wakame ; simmer 3 minutes. 
 Add the  carrots  and simmer 5 more minutes; reduce heat to very low. 
 Add  miso  and simmer 2 minutes (do not boil after adding  miso ). 
 Garnish with  green onions .
