 Prep Time: 5 minutes 
 Cook time: 10 minutes 
 Serves: 7 people 
 
 Ingredients : 
 tomato  (for garnish) 
 lettuce  (for garnish) 
 1 to 1½ pounds  ground chuck , 80/20 
 ¼ cup chopped  onion 
 ¼ teaspoon  paprika 
 focaccia  (for bun) 
 ¼ teaspoon  garlic powder 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Preheat grill over medium-high heat. 
 Mix beef, garlic powder, paprika and onions 
 Form the meat into 2 to 3-ounce portions, packed tightly. Season with salt and pepper. 
 Place the sliders on the grill until they are crusty on the first side, about 4 to 5 minutes. Flip and cook another 4 minutes. 
 Place the slider on sliced focaccia and top with desired toppings.
