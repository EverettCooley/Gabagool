Description : 
 Makes 4 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  flour 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 1 cup sliced  green onions 
 1 small  green pepper , chopped 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 ¼ cup chopped fresh  parsley 
 2 teaspoons  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 2 cups  oysters  with liquor 
 2 cups cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Blend flour into oil in saucepan. 
 Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until roux is light brown. 
 Add onions, green pepper, garlic, parsley, salt and pepper. 
 Cook over low heat, stirring frequently, until onions and pepper are tender. 
 Add oysters and liquor; continue cooking about 5 minutes. 
 Stir in cooked rice.
