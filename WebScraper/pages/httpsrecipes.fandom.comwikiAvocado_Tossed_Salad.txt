 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Gibson Estate in Quinlan, Texas in 1981.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 small head  lettuce  washed and chilled 
 1 ripe small  avocado 
 Â½ cup bottled creamy  Italian salad dressing 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl tear lettuce into bite size pieces. 
 Cut avocado lengthwise in half and remove pit then peel and slice crosswise into bowl. 
 Pour salad dressing over lettuce and toss. 
 Divide amongst individual salad bowls and serve.
