 Description : 
       Cream Soup with Salmon     
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups  water 
 3 medium  potatoes 
 2 medium  onions , chopped 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1/4 teaspoon white or  black pepper 
 1  bay leaf 
 2 pounds skinless salmon fillets 
 1 cup  whipping cream 
 Directions : 
 Heat  water ,  potatoes ,  onions ,  salt ,  pepper  and  bay leaf  to boiling . 
 Cover and simmer about 10 minutes; stir in salmon. 
 Cover and simmer until flakes easily with fork. 
 Remove  bay leaf . 
 Stir 1/2 cup of the soup liquid into  whipping cream . 
 Gently stir  cream  mixture into the soup, heat through. 
 Sprinkle with  dill weed .
