 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  celery , sliced 
 1 cup seedless  Green Grapes -halved 
 1 cup  Apple , diced 
 4  dates 
 1/2 cup  walnuts 
 1/4 cup  Mayonnaise 
 2 tbsp dry  white wine 
 lettuce  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Pace  celery ,  grapes ,  Apple ,  dates , and  walnuts  into a bowl. Blend  Mayonnaise  with wine; pour into bowl. Stir to blend with  celery , fruit and  walnuts . Use slotted serving spoon to serve, shaking spoon slighty to remove excess dressing. Serve on to remove excess dressing. Food exchange per servings: 2 fruit exchanges + 1 fat exchange + 1 vegetable exchange; calories: 105
