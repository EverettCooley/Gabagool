Description : 
 A traditional side dish, which can be served as a salad at a braai (BBQ) or on other occasions
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 kg  pickling onions , peeled 
 3  eggs 
 2 tablespoons  white sugar 
 1 teaspoon  dry mustard  powder 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ¾ cup  white vinegar 
 ¾ cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Cook onions in boiling water with a little salt until tender, but not sloppy. Drain and place in a bowl. 
 Boil vinegar and water 
 Whisk eggs with sugar, mustard and salt till foamy 
 Slowly pour boiling water vinegar into the eggs stirring all the time 
 Return to the heat, simmer gently stirring all the time until it thickens. 
 Pour over onions 
 Seve cooled.
