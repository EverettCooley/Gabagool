Description : 
 Who says garlic is pricey for exotic food preparation? This tangy pretzel is made with real herbs and is great for side dishes or appetizers, as it delivers a white-hot aroma that it relishes.
 
         
 Ingredients : 
 2 packs  yeast 
 4 – 5 cups  flour 
 1 ½ cup warm  water 
 2 cloves  garlic 
 1 tbsp.  salt 
 1 tbsp.  basil 
 1 tbsp.  sugar 
 1 tbsp.  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve yeast in water. 
 Add salt and sugar. 
 Blend or mix with 2 cups flour. 
 Crush garlic and add with herbs. 
 Add 2 cups more flour, and more if dough is too sticky. 
 Roll into snakes or lines and shape. Sprinkle with coarse salt, if desired. 
 Bake at 425° F for 10 – 15 minutes or until browned.
