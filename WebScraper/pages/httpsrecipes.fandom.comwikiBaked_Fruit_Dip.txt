Description : 
 Prep Time: 15 Minutes | Cook Time: 15 Minutes | Ready In: 30 Minutes 
 Servings per recipe: 12 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (16 ounce) package soft-style  cream cheese  with  pineapple 
 ¾ pound shredded  Swiss cheese 
 2 cups  dried cranberries 
 2 tablespoons  orange juice 
 ¼ cup  apple juice 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). 
 In a medium bowl, blend the cream cheese, Swiss cheese, cranberries, orange juice, and apple juice. 
 Scoop into a 9 inch pie pan. 
 Bake 15 minutes in the preheated oven, until bubbly and lightly browned.
