      Amish Schnitzel Beans     
 Ingredients : 
 6 slices  bacon 
 3 medium  onions , sliced 
 1 lb fresh  green beans 
 2 cups fresh diced  tomatoes 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  pepper 
 ⅓ cup boiling  water 
 Directions : 
 Dice  bacon  and fry. 
 Add sliced  onions  and fry. 
 Add  green beans  and brown slightly. 
 Add  tomatoes ,  salt ,  pepper  and  water . 
 Lower heat and cook.
