 Prep Time: 3 minutes 
 Cook time: 2 minutes (For Toast) 
 Serves: 1-2 People 
 
 Description : 
 An easy to make tuna sandwich with a few additions that make it a fast and tasteful meal.
 
 Ingredients : 
 One can of Albacore Tuna 
 Any brand of Louisiana Hot Sauce (Cheyanne Pepper Sauce) 
 Garlic Powder 
 Paprika 
 100% Wheat Bread (Anything but White) 
 Crushed Red Pepper (Optional) 
 Mayonayse (Optional, I prefer leaving it out) 
 Directions : 
 Put your selection of bread in Toaster and cook until Golden Brown 
 Open can of Tuna and mix in Pepper and Garlic powder (You can mix in mayo now if you want) 
 Pull Toast out and put as much Tuna on it as you like 
 Sprinkle the Crushed Red Pepper on the Tuna (Optional) 
 Finally, pour on the Pepper sauce until it is leaking over the side, and enjoy!
