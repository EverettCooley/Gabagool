Ingredients : 
 1 cup  butter  or  margarine 
 2 cups  brown sugar , packed 
 2  eggs 
 2½ cups  flour 
 2 tsp  cinnamon 
 2 tsp  allspice 
 2 tsp  baking soda 
 ½ tsp  cloves , ground 
 2 cups  beer 
 2 cups  dates , coarsely chopped 
 ½ cup  flour 
 1 cup  pecans , quartered 
 Directions : 
 Beat first 9 ingredients well with electric mixer. 
 Mix  dates  with ½ cup  flour . 
 Stir  dates  and  pecans  into batter. 
 Cool. 
 Serve with  whipped cream  or frost top with  cream cheese  frosting.
