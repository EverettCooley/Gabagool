Description : 
 Advertising for the Co-op grocery stores had this Christmas menu in advertising delivered Dec 20/9 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  turkey 
 Lite mashed potatoes : 
 ¼ cup plain  yogurt 
 1½ cup mashed  potatoes 
 Red cabbage and cranberries : 
 1 medium  red cabbage 
 12 oz fresh  cranberries 
 1 cup  water 
 ¼ cup  red wine vinegar 
 2 tbsp  brown sugar 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 ½ tsp  cloves 
 Festive peas : 
 4 cups  peas 
 shallots  (to taste) 
 ½ chopped  red pepper 
 Lite stuffing : 
 celery 
 onion 
 apricots 
 cooked  ham 
 chicken broth 
 Fruity Christmas baskets : 
 meringues cooked ahead, filled with fresh fruit and topped with  vanilla  custard sauce (like pavlova) 
 Lite roasted potatoes : 
 potatoes 
 oil 
 rosemary 
 Lite gravy : 
 pan juices 
 10 ice cubes 
 1 cup  chicken broth  + 3 tbsp 
 ¼ cup  flour 
 Directions : 
 Lite mashed potatoes : 
 Mash  potatoes , stir in  yogurt . 
 Serve hot. 
 Season with  salt  and  pepper  to taste. 
 Red cabbage and cranberries : 
 Wash and shred  cabbage . 
 In a large skillet, toss  cabbage  with fresh  cranberries . 
 Combine remaining ingredients in measuring cup and pour over  cabbage . 
 Simmer until just soft. 
 Refrigerate and reheat at serving time. 
 Festive peas : 
 Stir cook  peas  with shallots and chopped  red pepper . 
 Lite stuffing : 
 Stuffing has  celery ,  onion ,  apricots , cooked  ham ,  chicken broth  cooked a day ahead in the oven. 
 Lite roasted potatoes : 
 Peel potatoes, rub with  oil , sprinkle with  rosemary . 
 Roast in separate pan until golden and soft. 
 Lite gravy : 
 Serve hot gravy - stir 10 ice cubes into the pan juices. 
 When they have congealed with fat, remove from pan. 
 In a jar with a lid (or blender) shake together broth and  flour  until smooth. 
 Over medium heat whisk this mixture into the defatted pan juices. 
 Continue whisking until smooth and thick. 
 Cook, stirring often for 5 minutes. 
 Serve hot.
