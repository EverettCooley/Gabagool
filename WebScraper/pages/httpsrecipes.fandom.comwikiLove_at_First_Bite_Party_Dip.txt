Ingredients : 
 1 can frozen avocado dip thawed (7 3/4 oz) 
 1 cup cottage cheese 
 1/2 cup sour cream 
 1 t. lemon juice 
 1 can deviled ham (2.25 oz) 
 1 t. grated onion, optional 
 Directions : 
 In blender or bowl, blend all ingredients until almost smooth. 
 Serve chilled dip with vegetables such as celery, carrot and cucumber sticks, radish roses, cherry tomatoes and cauliflower flowerettes, or serve with corn chips and assorted crackers.
