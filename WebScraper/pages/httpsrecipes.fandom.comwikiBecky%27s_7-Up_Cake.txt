Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups  butter 
 ¾ cup  7-Up 
 3 cups  sugar 
 5  eggs 
 3 cups  flour 
 2 tablespoons  lemon extract 
 Directions : 
 Mix butter and sugar, beat in the eggs one at a time. 
 Add half of the flour, beat, add the rest of the flour and beat. 
 Put in greased/floured pan (bundt) bake at 325°F for 60 – 70 minutes. 
 Sprinkle with powdered sugar. 
 Enjoy.
