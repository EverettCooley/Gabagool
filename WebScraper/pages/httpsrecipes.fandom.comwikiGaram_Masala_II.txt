Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Old Newspaper Clipping 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  cumin 
 ¼ cup whole  cloves 
 ¼ cup grated  nutmeg 
 ¼ cup  curry powder 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in blender jar and blend well. 
 It will keep, tightly covered up to 2 months. It can be used in place of plain  curry  powder for an Indian accent in many dishes.
