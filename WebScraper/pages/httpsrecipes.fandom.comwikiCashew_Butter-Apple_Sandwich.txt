Ingredients : 
 2 slices vegan bread (I use hearty whole grain bread) 
 cashew butter  (also good with  Peanut butter ) 
 Granny Smith  or other tart  apple , sliced 
 Vegan cheddar  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Spread the  cashew butter  on the vegan bread, add slices of  apple  and  cheese .
