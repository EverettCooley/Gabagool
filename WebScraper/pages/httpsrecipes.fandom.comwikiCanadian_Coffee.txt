Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  maple syrup , pure 
 ½ cup  rye whiskey 
 3 cups  coffee , hot, black, double strength 
 Topping : 
 ¾ cup  whipping cream 
 4 teaspoons pure  maple syrup 
 Directions : 
 Topping : 
 Whip cream with  maple syrup  until soft mounds form. 
 set aside. 
 Coffee : 
 Divide  maple syrup  and  whiskey  among 4 warmed heatproof glass mugs or goblets. 
 Pour in  coffee  to 1-Inch of top. 
 Spoon topping over  coffee .
