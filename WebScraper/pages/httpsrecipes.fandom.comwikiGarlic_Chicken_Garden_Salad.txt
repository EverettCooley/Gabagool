Ingredients : 
 2 large pieces  chicken  (boneless) 
 1  red onion  (sliced) 
 ½  lettuce  (sliced) 
 1  green chile  (chopped) 
 salt 
 garlic bread 
 cherry tomato 
 garlic and herb  marinade 
 ½  cucumber  (sliced in circles) 
 toasted  croutons  (seasoned with  garlic ) 
 ⅔ cup  chickpeas  (cooked) 
 salad dressing 
 Parmesan cheese 
 fried  noodles 
 Directions : 
 Mix  chicken  with  garlic  and herb. 
 Marinate and wait ½ hour before you put it in oven at 350°F. 
 Bake until  chicken  is tender. 
 Cut  chicken  into thin strips. 
 Take a flat bowl. 
 Mix  lettuce  with  green chile ,  salad dressing ,  chickpeas ,  cherry tomatoes , sliced  cucumbers , and  black pepper  and place as the first layer. 
 Now place the  chicken  strips at the center. 
 Pour the extra marinade juice from baking tray on top of  chicken  pieces. 
 Sprinkle with some  cheese ,  onion  slices, fried noodles, and croutons. 
 Serve with  garlic  breads.
