Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz Jim Beam®  Bourbon whiskey 
 4 oz Sprite® soda 
 Directions : 
 Pour both ingredients into a highball glass filled with  ice cubes . 
 Stir well, and serve.
