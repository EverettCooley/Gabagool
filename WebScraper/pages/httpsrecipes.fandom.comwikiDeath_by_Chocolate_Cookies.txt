Description : 
 Contributed by Dorie  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 1½ dozen. 
 Ingredients : 
 2 (16 sq.) pkg.  semi-sweet chocolate , divided 
 ¾ cup firmly packed  brown sugar 
 ¼ cup  butter  or  margarine 
 2  eggs 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 ½ cup  flour 
 ¼ tsp  baking powder 
 2 cups chopped  nuts 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 350°F. 
 Coarsely chop 8 squares (1 pkg.) of the chocolate, set aside. 
 Microwave remaining 8 squares of chocolate in large microwaveable bowl on high 1 – 2 minutes. 
 Stir in until chocolate is melted and smooth. 
 Stir in sugar, butter, eggs and vanilla. 
 Stir in flour and baking powder. 
 Stir in reserved chopped chocolate and nuts. 
 Drop by ¼ cup fulls onto ungreased cookie sheet (yes, it's cup). 
 Bake 12 – 13 minutes or until cookies are puffed and feel set to the touch. 
 Cool, 1 minute. 
 Transfer to wire rack to cool completely.
