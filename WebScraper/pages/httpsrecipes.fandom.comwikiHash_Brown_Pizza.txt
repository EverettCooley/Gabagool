Ingredients : 
 4 cups shredded  hash browns 
 2 Tbsp.  butter , melted 
 4  eggs , beaten 
 1/3 cup each finely chopped  red onion  and  red bell pepper 
 1 pkg (6.67oz) shredded smoky bacon  cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 450ºF. 
 Spread potatoes onto bottom of 12-inch pizza pan sprayed with cooking spray; drizzle with butter.  
 Bake 30 min. or until lightly browned. 
 After 25 min., cook eggs in nonstick skillet on medium-low heat 3 to 5 min. or until set, stirring occasionally.  
 Spread eggs over potatoes; top with remaining ingredients. 
 Bake 5 to 7 min. or until cheese is melted.
