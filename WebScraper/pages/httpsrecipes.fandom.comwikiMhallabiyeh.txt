 Description : 
 rice   cream 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 liter  milk 
 1/2 glass powdered  rice 
 3/4 glass  Sugar 
 1 glass roughly ground pistacchios 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Dilute the  rice  in a bit of cold  water . Boil the  milk  and add the  rice . Stir until the mixture thickens. Pour in cups and allow to cool. Sprinkle pistacchios on the surface before serving.
