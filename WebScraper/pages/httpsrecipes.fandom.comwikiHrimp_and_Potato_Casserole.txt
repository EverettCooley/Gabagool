 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2Â lb boiled  potatoes 
 1 cup shelled shrimps 
 2 hard boiled  eggs 
 1 oz  margarine 
 1/2 cup sliced  onions 
 1 1/2 cups white sauce 
 2 oz grated  Cheese 
 1/2 cup bread crumbs 
 16 oz  carrots 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Line buttered baking dish with layer of sliced  potatoes , brushed with melted  margarine . Put layer of white sauce, sprinkled over half of the grated  Cheese , sliced  onions , chopped  eggs  and shrimps, sliced  carrots , white sauce and lastly a layer of sliced  potatoes . 
 Top with breadcrumbs, rest of grated  Cheese  and  margarine . Sprinkle a little  salt  and  white pepper  to taste between layers. Bake in a moderate oven for 45 minutes.
