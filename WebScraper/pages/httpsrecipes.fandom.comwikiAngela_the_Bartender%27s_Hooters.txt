Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 3 oz Old Charter®  Bourbon whiskey 
 6 oz Coca-Cola® 
 Directions : 
 Add a handful of  ice  cubes to a highball glass. 
 Pour in Old Charter, then add coca-cola (pre-chilled). 
 Add more coke to taste if desired, and serve.
