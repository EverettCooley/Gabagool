Lowfat Jell-O Delight
Ingredients
 3 oz. pkg. sugar-free pineapple jell-o 2 - 3 o z. pkg. sugar-free 
orange jell-o 
2 cups boiling water 15 oz. can crushed pineapple, no sugar 
8 oz. fat-free cream cheese, softened 3 oz. sugar-free instant, 
French vanilla pudding mix 
8 oz. Cool Whip Lite 
 Preparation
 Combine pineapple & orange jell-O with 2 cups boiling water. Stir to 
dissolve & add crushed pineapple, juice & all. Set aside. Combine 
softened cream cheese, 2 1/2 cups milk, & pudding mix. Beat until 
smooth & thickened. Stir in jell-O mixture. Pour mixture into a 
glass dish & chill overnight. Spread Cool whip evenly on top of 
chilled jell-O.
