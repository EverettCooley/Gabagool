Ingredients : 
 15  potato chips  per person 
 1 bowl plain  yogurt 
 salt  as per taste 
 ¼ tsp  red chile powder 
 ¼ tsp  cumin  powder 
 ¼ tsp  jeera 
 chopped  hara dhaniya  (fresh coriander) 
 Directions : 
 Arrange chips one by one in the serving plate. 
 Add  salt  in the  yogurt  and spread on all the chips. 
 Sprinkle  cumin  powder,  chile , hara dhaniya, and  jeera  on it. 
 Serve immediately so chips will not get soggy
