Description : 
 Papaya fruit drink
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups cut up  papaya  (peeled and seeded) 
 1 cup  pineapple juice 
 ¼ cup  lime  or  lemon juice 
 1½ cups  papaya nectar  (or  peach  or  apricot nectar ) 
 1 cup  ice cubes 
 2 cups sparkling  water ,  ginger ale  or  7-Up 
 Directions : 
 Blend thoroughly in blender. 
 Pour in a pitcher and add 2 cups  sparkling water ,  ginger ale  or  7-Up . 
 Serve over  ice  and enjoy!
