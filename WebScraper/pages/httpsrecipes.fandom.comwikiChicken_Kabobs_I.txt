Desription : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes 
 Serves: 4 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb (500 g) boneless, skinless  chicken breasts , cut into 1 inch (2.5 cm) cubes 
 8 x 1 inch (2.5 cm)  pineapple  cubes 
 ½  green bell pepper , cut into 1 inch (2.5 cm) cubes 
 ½  red bell pepper , cut into 1 inch (2.5 cm) cubes 
 ½ small  red onion , cut into wedges 
 1 tbsp (15 ml)  olive oil 
 2 tbsp (30 ml)/1 capful Mrs. Dash Chicken Grilling Blend 
 Directions : 
 Thread pineapple chunks, chicken, peppers and onions onto skewers. 
 Continue until you have four kabobs. 
 Preheat grill to medium high. 
 Brush kabobs with olive oil. 
 Sprinkle with Mrs. Dash Chicken Grilling Blend. 
 Grill kabobs until chicken is not longer pink, 6-8 minutes, turning occasionally. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Nutrition per 1 kabob serving: 
 
 180 Calories | 25% of Calories from Fat | 5g Total Fat | 1g Saturated Fat | 4g Unsaturated Fat | 0g Trans Fat | 66mg Cholesterol | 75mg Sodium | 378mg Potassium | 6g Carbs | 1g Fiber | 27g Protein
