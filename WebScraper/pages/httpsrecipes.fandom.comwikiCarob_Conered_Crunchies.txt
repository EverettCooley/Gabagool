 Ingredients : 
 2¼ cup  whole wheat flour 
 1  egg 
 ¼ cup  vegetable oil 
 ¼ cup  applesauce 
 1  bouillon  cube ( beef   or   chicken ) dissolved in ½ cup hot  water 
 1 tbsp  honey 
 1 tsp  molasses 
 carob chips  (about 1 cup) 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together until well blended. Knead dough two minutes on a lightly floured surface. 
 Roll to 1/4" thickness. Bake on an ungreased cookie sheet for 30 minutes in a 300 °F oven. Cool. 
 Melt  carob chips  in microwave or saucepan. Dip cool biscuits in  carob  or lay on a flat surface and brush  carob  over the biscuits with a pastry brush. Let cool.
