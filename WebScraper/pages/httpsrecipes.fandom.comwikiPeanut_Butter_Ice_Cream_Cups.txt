Description : 
 Contributed by Lilly at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 12 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (7-oz) jar  marshmallow crème 
 3 tbsp  milk 
 ½ cup  peanut butter 
 1 cup  whipping cream , whipped 
 24  vanilla  wafers 
 strawberry  topping 
 Directions : 
 Combine  marshmallow crème  and  milk  in lg bowl; mix with electric mixer until well blended. 
 Add  peanut butter ; mix well. 
 Fold in  whipped cream . 
 Arrange ½ of wafers, flat side down, in paper-lined muffin cups. 
 Spoon  marshmallow crème  mixture into cups. 
 Top with topping and remaining wafers; freeze.
