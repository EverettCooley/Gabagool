asparagus  Hungarian
 
 
 2 Pkg. Frozen  asparagus  Spears 
 ? t  salt 
 ? t  pepper 
 
 2 t  butter 
 ? c  bread  Crumbs
 
 1 c  sour cream 
 
 
Cook Spears According to Package Directions; Drain. Arrange in ShallowCasserole; Sprinkle With  salt  And  pepper . Dot With  butter ; Cover With BreadCrumbs. Pour in  sour cream  Gently. Bake in 350? Oven For 15 to 20 Minutes.
 
 
Yield: 4 Servings
