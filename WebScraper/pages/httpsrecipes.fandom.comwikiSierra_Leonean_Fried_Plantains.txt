 Description : 
 This is enjoyed in Sierra Leone and most of the rest of Africa! Very eggy, very yummy! In Sierra Leone, they typically call anything fried  Fry fry . So, enjoy your fried plantains. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 - 4  plantains 
 oil 
 6 eggs 
 Directions : 
 Heat the  oil  in a heavy saucepan. 
 Crack the 6 eggs into the saucepan. 
 Wait for them to cook. 
 Slice the peeled  plantains  into Â¼-inch rounds. 
 Fry the slices a few at a time until they are golden and crisp on the outside but still soft on the inside. 
 If fried too long, the inside will toughen, so try a couple to get the best timing. 
 Remove them from the  oil  with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels or newspaper.
