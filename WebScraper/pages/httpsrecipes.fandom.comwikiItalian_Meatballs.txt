 Prep Time:1hr 
 Cook time:30 Min to 2 Hr 
 Serves: 15 
 
 Description : 
 This is my Italian Meatball recipe. It's relatively easy and scalable up or down. Pictures and tips coming soon!
 
 Ingredients : 
 5 – 10 lb Meatloaf Mix ( ground beef ,  pork , and  veal ) 
 2  eggs  per pound meat 
 ½ cup seasoned  breadcrumbs  per pound meat 
 Diced  onions  fried in  olive oil  — ½ onion per pound 
 1 teaspoon  garlic salt  per pound 
 Directions : 
 Fry Onions till soft and lightly browned 
 Mix, and form meatballs 
 Broil 
 Add to tomato sauce and cook for a while
