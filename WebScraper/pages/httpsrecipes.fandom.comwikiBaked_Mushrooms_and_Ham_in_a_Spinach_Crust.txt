Ingredients : 
 2 cups cooked leaf  spinach 
 4 cups small  button mushrooms 
 1 cup diced  ham 
 1 cup  cream 
 1 cup grated sharp  cheddar  cheese 
 1 tbsp grated  onion 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp dry  mustard 
 ¼ tsp  paprika 
 4 tbsp toasted  almond  slices 
 1 tbsp minced  parsley 
 Directions : 
 An elegant delicious combination, always a hit when served!. 
 Line a buttered 9-inch pie pan with  spinach . 
 Cover with  mushrooms , stem side down; add diced  ham . 
 Combine in a bowl  cream ,  cheese ,  onion ,  salt  and dry  mustard ; pour over  mushrooms . 
 Bake in a preheated 350 degree oven for 30 minutes. 
 Remove from oven; sprinkle with  paprika ,  almonds  and  parsley ; serve immediately. 
 Serves four.
