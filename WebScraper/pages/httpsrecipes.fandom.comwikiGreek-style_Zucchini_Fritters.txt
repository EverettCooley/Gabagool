Description : 
 The perfect way to start the day - a rich Greek take on real zucchini, feta, and herbs is great on side dishes and appetizers and delivers a blissful taste that it relishes.
 
 Ingredients : 
       Greek-style Zucchini Fritters     
 2 cups  zucchini , grated and squeezed to drain 
 3 handful fresh herbs, such as  parsley ,  dill , and  mint , chopped 
 1  green onion , chopped 
 ¼ cup  feta , crumbled 
 ½ cup  flour 
 1  egg 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 2 tablespoons  oil , olive, canola, vegetable, or corn only 
 Directions : 
 Mix the zucchini, herbs, green onion, feta, flour, egg, salt and pepper in a bowl. 
 Heat the oil in a pan. 
 Spoon the zucchini mixture into the pan and cook until golden-brown on both sides, about 4 minutes per side. Serve hot and plain or with other side dishes or appetizers.
