Crockpot Spicy Mexican  Pork  Stew
 
 2 Full Ears Frozen  corn , Thawed And Cut in 1 1/2 in Pieces 
 1 1/2Â lb Lean, Boneless  Pork , Cut in 1 1/2 in Pieces 
 1 Can  kidney beans , Drained And Rinsed (14 1/2 Ounces) 
 1 Can  pinto beans , Drained And Rinsed (15 Ounces) 
 1 Can Stewed  tomatoes  (14 1/2 Ounces) 
 1 Can  tomato  Sauce (8 Ounces) 
 1 Pkg  taco seasoning  (1.25 Oz) 
 2 t Minced  Onion 
 2 t  parsley  flakes or  cilantro  Leaves 
 
In a 3 1/2 or 4-Quart Crockpot, Place  corn ,  Pork  Pieces And beans InOrder Listed Above. in Medium Bowl Stir Together Stewed  tomatoes , tomato  Sauce,  taco seasoning , And Minced  Onion . Whisk Together AndPour Into Crockpot And Lightly Stir. Cover And Cook on Low For 8Hours or Until  Pork  is Tender. Gently Stir in  parsley  flakes OrCilantro Leaves After 8 Hours.
