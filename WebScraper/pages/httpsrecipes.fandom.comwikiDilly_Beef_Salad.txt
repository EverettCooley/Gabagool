Ingredients : 
 3 cups  beef , cooked, cubed 
 ¾ cup  green pepper , chopped 
 5 large  olives , stuffed, sliced 
 10  onions , pickled, chopped 
 ⅛ tsp  pepper 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  dry mustard 
 1 tsp  dillweed 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 ¼ cup  beer 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 onions , pickled, stuffed 
 Directions : 
 Combine  beef ,  green pepper , sliced olives, and chopped pickled  onion ; chill. 
 Combine  pepper ,  salt ,  mustard ,  dillweed ,  mayonnaise ,  beer , and  lemon juice ; mix well and add to meat mixture. 
 Toss lightly. 
 Chill at least 1 hour. 
 Garnish with  pickled onions  and olives.
