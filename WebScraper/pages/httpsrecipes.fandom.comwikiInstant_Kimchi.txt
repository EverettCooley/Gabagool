Description : 
       Instant Kimchi     
 Yield: 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1  Chinese cabbage 
 2 cloves  garlic 
 1 tsp  hot pepper 
 1 tsp  soy sauce 
 1 tsp  vinegar 
 1 tbsp  salt 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Chop the  cabbage  into pieces about 1½ inches long by 1½ inches wide. 
 Three cups of chopped  cabbage  are needed. 
 Crush the  garlic  and blend with the  hot pepper , soy sauce and  vinegar . 
 Add the  cabbage  and mix well. 
 Add the  salt  and  sugar , mix and cover until ready to serve.
