Description : 
 This marinade is good on  lamb  and  pork  but especially on  poultry .
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 From the Recipe Ross Newsletter 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup plain  yogurt 
 2 tbsp grated  ginger  root 
 2 tbsp minced  onion 
 2 minced  garlic  cloves 
 ½ tsp  ground coriander 
 ⅓ tsp  cayenne pepper 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Combine ingredients in a shallow dish.
