About ground veal : 
 Ground veal is simply  veal  that has been ground or finely chopped. 
 Ground veal can be prepared at home or it will be bought from supermarkets, grocery stores, or butcher shops. Ground veal is the minced or cubed veal meat preferably from the less tender and less popular cuts of the veal. Ground veal or minced veal is made of finely chopped veal, minced by a meat grinder.
