Description : 
 Contributed by Jenn B aka Mom2Sam and Tiny at  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Can be prepared in 45 minutes or less. 
 Accompaniment: Toasted  pita  wedges or  potato chips 
 Makes about 2 cups 
 Ingredients : 
 8 ounces  cream cheese , softened 
 ¼ cup  sour cream 
 2 x 6½-ounce cans minced  clams , drained, reserving 3 tablespoons liquid 
 ⅓ cup finely chopped  red bell pepper 
 1  shallot , minced 
 2 tablespoons minced fresh  parsley  leaves 
 ¾ teaspoon  Worcestershire sauce 
 ⅛ teaspoon  cayenne 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl whisk together  cream cheese  and  sour cream  until smooth. 
 Stir in remaining ingredients and  salt  to taste.
