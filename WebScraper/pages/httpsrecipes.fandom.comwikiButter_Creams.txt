butter  Creams
 
 1 Pkg. 8-Oz.  cream cheese 
 1/2 lb  butter 
 2 Lbs Powered  sugar 
 1 Tsp  vanilla 
 
Cream The  cream cheese  And  butter 
 Add  sugar  And  vanilla  And Mix Well
 
The Longer You Mix This, The Creamier it Gets.
 
Chill at Least 2 Hours. Shape Into Balls And Cover Them With  chocolate .Store in Fridge.
