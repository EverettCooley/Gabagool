 Ingredients : 
 1 shot Stolichnaya  vodka 
 1 shot De Kuyper  crème de bananes 
 ½ shot Disaronno  amaretto 
 1 shot De Kuyper  crème de cacao 
 2 shots  single cream 
 half peeled  banana 
 garnish:  banana  slice and  mint  sprig 
 glass: highball 
 Directions : 
 Blend all the ingredients with crushed  ice .
