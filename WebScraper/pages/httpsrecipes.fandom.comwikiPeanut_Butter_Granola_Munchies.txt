Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 2 tablespoons creamy  peanut butter 
 3 cups miniature  marshmallows 
 2½ cups  granola  with or without  raisins 
 Directions : 
 Combine  butter  and  Peanut butter  in a large saucepan. Place over low heat until  butter  and  peanut butter  melt; stirring frequently. 
 Add marshmallows and cook, covered until marshmallows have melted. 
 Remove from heat and add granola. 
 Toss mixture together gently. 
 Pour into a greased 8½ by 4½ inch loaf pan. 
 Let cool completely. 
 Remove from pan. 
 Cut into pieces.
