Link: https://recipes.fandom.com/wiki/Bullfrog


 Ingredients : 
 1/2 shot De Kuyper Green  crème de menthe 
 1 shot Stolichnaya  vodka 
 1 1/2 shots Single  cream 
 1 1/2 shots  milk 
 garnish:  shaved chocolate 
 Glass: Tumbler
 
 Directions : 
 Shake with cubed  ice  and strain into an  ice  filled tumbler
