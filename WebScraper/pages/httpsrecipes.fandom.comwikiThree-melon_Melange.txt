Ingredients : 
 1 cup  sugar 
 ½ cup  water 
 ⅓ cup  orange liqueur 
 4 cups  watermelon 
 2 cups  cantaloupe 
 2 cups  honeydew melon 
 Directions : 
 Combine the  sugar  and  water  in a saucepan and bring to a boil. 
 Remove from heat and add liqueur; chill. 
 In large bowl, combine melons; chill until ready to serve. 
 Just before serving, pour syrup over fruit.
