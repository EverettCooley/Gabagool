Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes about 35 clusters. 
 Ingredients : 
 12 ounces  chocolate chips 
 1 teaspoon grated  chocolate 
 1 cup  raisins 
 2 cups thin  pretzel  sticks, broken into 1-inch pieces 
 Directions : 
 Place chocolate chips in a microwave-safe bowl. 
 Microwave on high setting for 2 minutes. 
 Stir. 
 Continue microwaving in 15-second increments until chocolate is almost completely melted. 
 Remove from microwave and stir grated chocolate into melted chocolate. 
 Stir in raisins and pretzels. 
 Drop mixture by tablespoons onto waxed paper. 
 Let stand until firm.
