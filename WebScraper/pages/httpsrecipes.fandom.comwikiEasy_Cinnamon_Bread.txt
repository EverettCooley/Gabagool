Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  shortening 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 1 cup  buttermilk 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 1 cup  sugar 
 2  eggs 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 2 cups  flour 
 Topping : 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 ½ cup  nuts 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Cream the sugar, shortening and vanilla. 
 Add eggs and buttermilk. 
 Blend in baking soda, baking powder and flour. 
 Pour into greased loaf pan. 
 Topping : 
 Mix sugar, nuts and cinnamon together. 
 Cut the topping into batter for a marbleized effect. 
 Bake at 350°F for 30 – 40 minutes.
