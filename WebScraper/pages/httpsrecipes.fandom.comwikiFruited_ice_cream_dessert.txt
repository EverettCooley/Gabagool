 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 quart  vanilla   ice   cream 
 1 1/3 cup Fruit, diced & drained 
 1 large Package Jello, any flavor 
 Boiling  water 
 Directions : 
 1 Dissolve jello in 2 cups boiling  water . 
2 Add 1 quart  vanilla   ice   cream  by spoonfuls, stirring until  ice   cream  is melted. 
3 Add fruit. 
4 Chill until set, about 30 minutes.
