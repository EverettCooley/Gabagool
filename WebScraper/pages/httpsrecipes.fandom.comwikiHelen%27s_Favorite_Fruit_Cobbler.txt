Ingredients : 
 1 stick  butter 
 1 c.  Bisquick 
 1 c.  milk 
 1 c.  sugar 
 2 c. your favorite fruit, i.e. cherries, peaches, blueberries 
 Directions : 
 Melt butter in 13 x 9 x 2 inch pan. 
 Mix together milk, sugar and Bisquick. 
 Add mixture to pan of butter 
 Pour fruit onto the mixture. 
 Bake at 350-375 degrees for 45-60 minutes. 
 Serve with vanilla ice cream.
