 Chinese soups are very famous in the American Chinese cuisine. Having said that one thing must be mentioned that there are many different types of soups. The most popular ones are sweet corn soups made with hot ingredients and sour cream. Also traditional American Chinese soup is made with meat, chicken or vegetable stock.
