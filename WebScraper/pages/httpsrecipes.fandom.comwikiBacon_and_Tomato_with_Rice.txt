Makes 6 to 8 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 5 slices  Bacon , diced 
 2/3 cup chopped  green pepper 
 1/2 cup chopped  Onion 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 3 large  tomatoes , peeled and cut into eighths 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Cook  Bacon  until almost crisp in large skillet over low heat. 
 Pour off fat; return to heat 
 Add  pepper ,  Onion  and  garlic ; cook until tender crisp. 
 Stir in  tomatoes ,  rice  and  black pepper ; heat thoroughly
