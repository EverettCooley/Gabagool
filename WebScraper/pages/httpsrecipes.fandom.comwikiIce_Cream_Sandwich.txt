 Description : 
Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group       Ice Cream Sandwich     
 Ingredients : 
 2 boxes of ice cream sandwiches 
 1 large container  Cool Whip  ( or  1 cup all-purpose cream) 
 2 semi-soft candy bars (any brand, chocolate only)  or  (2 Â½ cups English toffee) 
 Directions : 
 Layer the bottom of the dish with ice cream sandwiches. 
 Next, layer with the Cool Whip. 
 Repeat the layers. Top with the crumbled or sliced candy bar (or English toffee). 
 Freeze until time to serve. Serve cold and plain or with other desserts. Sell per piece, if desired.
