Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can  garbanzo beans  drained 
 3 tablespoon  tahini 
 ¼ cup  lemon juice 
 1 clove  garlic 
 ½ teaspoon  cumin 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Place all ingredients in blender and blend until smooth. 
 Pour into serving dish. 
 Pour very thin layer of  olive oil  on top and sprinkle with  parsley . 
 Serve with pita  bread .
