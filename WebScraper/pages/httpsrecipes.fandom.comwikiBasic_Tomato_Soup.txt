Description : 
 Great with grilled  Cheese  sandwiches.
 
 Ingredients : 
 tomatoes , chopped and de-seeded (add tomato paste if you are short on fresh) 
 onions 
 garlic 
 oil ,  Peanut  or olive 
 salt / pepper 
 parsley 
 Nestle crème legère 
 Bit of  Sugar 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Sauté  onions / garlic  in  oil . 
 Add  tomatoes . Simmer until mushy. 
 Add paste if using. Simmer. 
 Add  Sugar  to taste (to cut down on acidity) and other spices. 
 At the very end, add  parsley  and crème.
