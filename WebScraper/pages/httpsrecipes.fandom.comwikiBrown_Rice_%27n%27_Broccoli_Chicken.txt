Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup uncooked  brown rice 
 2 tablespoons  soy sauce 
 2 cups fresh  broccoli  florets, blanched 
 2 cups cooked  chicken  breast cubes 
 1½ cups shredded  mozzarella  cheese, divided 
 ½ cup  sliced almonds , toasted 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 ¼ teaspoon  garlic powder 
 Pinch ground  nutmeg 
 Directions : 
 Cook  rice  according to package directions, substituting  soy sauce  for 2 tablespoons  water . 
 Combine  rice ,  broccoli ,  chicken , ½ cup  cheese ,  almonds ,  black pepper ,  garlic powder  and  nutmeg  in large bowl. 
 Place in greased 3-quart baking dish or in individual greased baking dishes. Sprinkle with remaining 1 cup  cheese . 
 Bake at 350 °F for 20 to 25 minutes.
