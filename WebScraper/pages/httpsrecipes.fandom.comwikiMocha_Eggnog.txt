Description : 
 Contributed by Stephen Ceideburg
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tsp instant  coffee  granules 
 ½ cup hot  water 
 32 oz refrigerated  eggnog 
 ¼ to ½ cup  brandy 
 ½ cup  chocolate syrup 
 ½ cup  whipping cream , whipped 
 1 grated  semisweet chocolate , optional 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve  coffee  granules in  water ; let cool. 
 Combine  coffee , eggnog,  brandy  and  chocolate syrup . 
 Cover and refrigerate. 
 To serve, fold in  whipped cream  and garnish with grated  chocolate , if desired.
