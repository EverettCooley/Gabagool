Ingredients : 
 6 oz unsweetened  lemonade  concentrate, sugar-free type 
 3 tbsp  granulated fructose 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 2 tbsp  lemon  rind 
 3 cups  evaporated skim milk , well chilled 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in a large bowl and beat well. 
 Recipe can be frozen in an  ice cream  maker or by dividing into 2 freezer trays and freeze until mushy. 
 Pour into a large bowl and beat well. 
 Repack into a container and freeze until firm. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Calories per ½ cup serving 51 | exchange 1 fruit
