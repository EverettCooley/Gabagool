 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups cooked brown or  white rice , cooled 
 2 cups (8 ounces) crab meat* 
 1 cup cubed  avocado 
 1 cup sliced  celery 
 1/2 cup sliced  ripe olives 
 1/3 cup  Mayonnaise 
 1/4 cup  sour cream 
 2 tablespoons  lemon juice 
 1/2 teaspoon  garlic salt 
 lettuce  leaves 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix  rice , crab meat,  avocado ,  celery  and in large mixing bowl. Blend  Mayonnaise ,  sour cream ,  lemon juice  and  garlic salt . Add to  rice  mixture; toss lightly. Serve on  lettuce  leaves.
