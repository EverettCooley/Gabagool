Ingredients : 
 1  chicken  or  turkey 
 600 g  walnuts 
 5  onions 
 3 teaspoons  fenugreek 
 6 or 7 cloves  garlic 
 ½ teaspoon  saffron 
 ½ teaspoon cinnamon 
 vinegar , red pepper and  salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Cut up a drawn and thoroughly washed  turkey  into medium-sliced pieces and put them in a saucepan. 
 Cover it with  water  and boil. 
 Remove the scum in the process of boiling. 
 Remove the fat from the broth and stew finely chopped  onions  in it. 
 Mince  walnuts  through a meat grinder 2 or 3 times. 
 Ground  garlic ,  fenugreek , coriander, red pepper,  saffron  and  cinnamon ; dilute with  vinegar . 
 Leave ⅔ of the broth in the pan, put the prepared mass and the stewed  onions  into the pan. 
 Remove it from the stove in a bout 10 minutes.
