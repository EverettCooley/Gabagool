Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp  olive oil 
 1 tsp  garlic  clove 
 1 dash  crushed red pepper 
 1 1/2 lbs  spinach  leaves or 2-10 oz. packages frozen chopped spinach 
 5 cup  water 
 1 lipton noodles and sauce, chicken flavor 
 salt 
 pepper 
 Directions : 
 In 3-quart saucepan, heat  oil  and cook  garlic  with red pepper over medium-high heat 1 minute. 
 Stir in  spinach  and cook, stirring occasionally, until wilted. 
 Add  water  and bring to a boil. 
 Stir in noodles and Chicken flavor sauce and continue boiling over medium heat, stirring occasionally, 7 minutes or until noodles are tender. 
 Add  salt  and  pepper .
