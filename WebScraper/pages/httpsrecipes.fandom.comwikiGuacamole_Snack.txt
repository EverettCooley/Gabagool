 Ingredients : 
 2 Avocados 
 1 Tsp  garlic salt 
 1  tomato  (diced) 
 4 Tbsp Picante Sauce 
 Directions : 
 Peel the avocados and remove the seeds. Mash the avocados in a bowl with a  Potato  masher or a fork. Stir in remaining ingredients.
Serve with tortilla chips
 Note: Add  lemon juice  to the mixture to retain green color. This is not necessary if eaten soon after preparation.
