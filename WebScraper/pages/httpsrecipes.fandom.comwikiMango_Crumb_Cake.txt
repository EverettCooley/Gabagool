Ingredients : 
 1 cup  sugar 
 3 tsp  baking powder 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ½ cup  butter 
 ½ cup  milk 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 1  egg , beaten 
 1 cup chopped  mango 
 2 cups  flour 
 Directions : 
 Combine all dry ingredients to form crumbs. 
 Reserve ½ cup for topping. 
 Add  butter ,  milk ,  egg  and  vanilla  to crumb mixture. 
 Mix well. 
 Put in greased and floured pan. 
 Add  mangos . 
 Cover with reserved crumbs. 
 Bake at 350°F for 40 minutes.
