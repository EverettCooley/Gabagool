Description : 
 cottage cheese  with diced  avocado  chunks, finely chopped  Bermuda onion ,  Mayonnaise , and  salt  and  pepper  to taste. Excellent cold salad side dish or great for lunch or snack with crackers. Will make approx. 8 x ½ cup servings. Excellent cool summer  salad  for picnics. Can be refrigerated for about 4 days. Always tastes best the 2nd day after the  onions  have had time to flavor. 15 minutes start to finish!.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 16 oz container of low fat  cottage cheese  (curd size optional) 
 2 small ripe genuine  California avocados 
 ¼ cup finely chopped  Bermuda onion 
 ¼ cup low fat  mayonnaise 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in bowl. 
 Mix gently,  refrigerate  for a couple hours then enjoy.
