 Ingredients : 
 1Â lb  liver  450g 
 lime  or  vinegar  to clean liver 
 1 tsp  salt  (level) 5ml 
 1/4 tsp  black pepper  2ml 
 1/4 tsp  thyme  2ml 
 1 bullion  stock  cube diluted in half cup  water  or 1/2 cup diluted  Tomato Paste  125ml 
 1 tblsp Lea & Perrins  Worcestershire Sauce  15ml 
 1 cup  breadcrumbs  (seasoned) 112g 
 3 rashers  bacon 
 Directions : 
 Clean liver with  vinegar  or  lime juice . 
 Remove thin skin (membrane). 
 Season with  salt ,  pepper  and  thyme , Lea & Perrins Sauce 15ml. Set aside. 
 Prepare  bread  crumbs. 
 Put seasoned liver in baking dish (medium size). 
 Put seasoned  bread  crumbs on top. 
 Put  Bacon  on top of  bread  crumbs. 
 Bake in 350 F/180 C for 45 minutes.
