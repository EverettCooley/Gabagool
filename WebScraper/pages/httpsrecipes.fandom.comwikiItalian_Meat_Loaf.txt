 Ingredients : 
 2  eggs  beaten 
 3/4 cup bread crumbs, softened 
 1/2-cup  tomato juice 
 2 tablespoons snipped  parsley 
 1/2 teaspoon dried  oregano 
 1/4-teaspoon  salt 
 1/4-teaspoon  pepper 
 1 small  garlic  clove minced 
 2 pounds lean ground  Beef 
 8 thin slices of boiled  Ham 
 6-ounces  mozzarella , shredded 
 3 slices of  mozzarella  cut diagonally 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine  eggs , breadcrumbs, juice,  parsley ,  oregano ,  salt ,  pepper  and  garlic . 
 Stir in ground  Beef  mix well. 
 Pat into a thick rectangle on foil. 
 Put  Ham  slices on top. 
 Sprinkle shredded  mozzarella  on top. 
 Roll up carefully using the foil pan. 
 Bake in a preheated 350 oven for 1 hour. 
 Top with  Cheese  triangles and cook 5 minutes longer to melt the  Cheese .
