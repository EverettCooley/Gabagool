Ingredients : 
 1 can (10-count) biscuits 
 1 large Three Musketeers bar 
 oil  for deep frying 
 Powdered sugar  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Roll each biscuit out flat. 
 Cut candy bar into 10 wedges. 
 Lay one wedge in middle of each biscuit and fold remaining dough over it. 
 Crimp edges together and deep fry until golden brown. 
 Can be eaten plain or rolled in powdered sugar.
