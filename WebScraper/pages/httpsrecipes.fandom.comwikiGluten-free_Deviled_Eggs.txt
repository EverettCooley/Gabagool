 Ingredients : 
 6  eggs , hard-cooked 
 ¼ cup gluten-free  mayonnaise 
 ½ teaspoon dry  mustard 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 ¼ teaspoon  dill weed , dried 
 pimento  strips for garnish, if desired 
 Directions : 
 Halve six hard-cooked  eggs  lengthwise, remove yolks. 
 Mash yolks; mix with  mayonnaise  and remainder of ingredients. 
 Mix well. 
 If too dry, add a bit more  mayonnaise . 
 Refill  egg  whites, using pastry tube if desired. 
 Trim with thin  pimento  strips (optional).
