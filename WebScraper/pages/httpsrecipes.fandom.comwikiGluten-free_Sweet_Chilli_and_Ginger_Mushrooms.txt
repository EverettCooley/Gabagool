Description : 
 I have had something similar to this. I love things with mushrooms in it. I think when I made it I didn't have green onions the first time but after that they were included.
 
 Contributed by Jenn B aka Mom2Sam and Tiny at  World Recipes Y-Group 
 Serves 4. 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons (8 tsp)  peanut oil 
 2 tablespoons (8 tsp) freshly grated  ginger 
 400 g (14 oz)  button mushrooms , trimmed 
 200 g (7 oz)  shiitake mushrooms , trimmed and quartered 
 â…“ cup  sweet chilli sauce 
 8  green onions , thinly sliced 
 Directions : 
 Heat a wok over high heat. 
 Add oil and heat until hot. 
 Add ginger and stir-fry for 30 seconds or until aromatic. 
 Add button mushrooms and stir-fry for 1 minute. 
 Add shiitake mushrooms and stir-fry for 1 minute or until well coated with oil mixture. 
 Add chilli sauce and stir-fry 2 minutes or until mushrooms are just tender. 
 Remove from heat and add green onions. 
 Toss well to combine. 
 Serve immediately with steamed jasmine rice.
