Servings: 12
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 c Unbleached  all-purpose flour 
 1 ts Ground  cinnamon 
 1/2 ts Ground  cloves 
 1/2 ts  ground ginger 
 1/2 ts Ground  nutmeg 
 1 c  powdered sugar 
 2 ts  baking powder 
 2 Extra Large  eggs 
 1 c  Honey 
 2 tb Chopped  orange peel 
 1 lg egg; Beaten With a Little  water  For Glazing 
 20  blanched almonds 
 coarse sugar  Crystals 
 Directions : 
 Sift together hte flour, spices, sugar, and baking powder 
 Add the eggs, honey, and orange peel, and mix to make a soft dough 
 On a floured work surface, roll out hte dough to about 1/2-inch thick 
 Cut into shapes and place ona  lightly greased or non-stick cookie sheet 
 Brush each cookie with the egg wash 
 Place 1/2 of an almon din the center of each cookie, and sprinkle with decorative sugar crystals 
 Bake in an oven which has been preheated to 350F until done; about 15 minutes 
 Cool on wire racks 
 Store in an airtight container.
