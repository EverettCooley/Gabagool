      Smoked mozzarella     
 About smoked mozzarella : 
 Smoked mozzarella, also called mozzarella affumicata, is  mozzarella  cheese which has been smoked to enhance its flavour. It typically has a light, golden brown outer shell and is lightly salted. Its low-moisture content makes it ideal for slicing and melting.
