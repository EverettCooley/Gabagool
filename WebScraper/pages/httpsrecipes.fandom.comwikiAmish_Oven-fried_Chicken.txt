      300pxAmish Oven-fried Chicken     
 Ingredients : 
 ⅓ cup  vegetable oil 
 ⅓ cup  butter 
 1 cup  all purpose flour 
 1 tsp  salt 
 2 tsp  black pepper 
 2 tsp  paprika 
 1 tsp  garlic salt 
 1 tsp dried  marjoram 
 8 or 9 pieces  chicken 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter  in a 375 °F oven, set aside. 
 Combine dry ingredients. 
 Roll the  chicken  in  butter  and  oil  then drop into a sack and shake to cover. 
 Place on a plate. 
 Place  chicken  in the pan skin side down. 
 Bake at 375 °F for 45 minutes. 
 Turn  chicken  pieces over and bake 5 to 10 minutes longer.
