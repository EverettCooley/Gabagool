 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup thick, chunky tomato  salsa 
 ½ cup seafood  cocktail sauce 
 ½ cup diced  onion 
 ½ cup diced  cucumber (English) 
 ½ cup chopped  cilantro 
 ¼ cup  lime juice 
 3 pounds  avocados , cut into chunks 
 lime  slices - for garnish 
 cilantro  sprigs - for garnish 
 1 – 1½ lbs cooked large  shrimp  with tails (21-25 count) 
 Directions : 
 Just before service : 
 Thoroughly combine salsa and next 5 ingredients. 
 Fold in  avocado ; chill 1 hour to marry flavors. 
 Per order : 
 Fill a stemmed glass with ⅓ cup  avocado  mixture. 
 Hang 5  shrimp  around rim. 
 Garnish with a twisted  lime  slice and a  cilantro  sprig.
