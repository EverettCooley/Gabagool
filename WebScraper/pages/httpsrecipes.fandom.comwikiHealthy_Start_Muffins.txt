Ingredients : 
 Basic Muffin Mix : 
 5½ cups  all-purpose flour 
 2¼ cups  whole wheat flour 
 2½ cups natural  bran 
 1¾ cups  skim milk powder 
 1½ cups  granulated sugar 
 2 tbsp  baking powder 
 2 tsp  salt 
 Healthy Start Muffin : 
 1 cup All-Bran cereal 
 1¼ cups  water 
 ⅓ cup fancy  molasses 
 1  egg 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 2¾ cups  basic muffin mix 
 ¾ cup  prunes , chopped 
 ⅓ cup green  pumpkin seeds 
 2 tsp  cinnamon 
 ½ tsp  baking soda 
 Directions : 
 Mix together Basic Muffin Mix ingredients in large bowl; transfer to airtight container and store in a cool dry place. Stir before using. 
 In a medium bowl, soak cereal in  water  for 5 minutes or until softened; 
 Whisk in  oil ,  molasses ,  egg  and  vanilla . 
 In a large bowl, stir together Basic muffin mix,  prunes ,  pumpkin seeds ,  cinnamon  and  baking soda 
 Stir in cereal mixture just until moistened 
 Spoon into greased muffin cups, filling to top. 
 Bake in 375°F / 190°C oven for about 30 minutes or until tops are firm to the touch. 
 Let cool in pan on rack for 5 minutes; remove muffins to rack and let cool completely.
