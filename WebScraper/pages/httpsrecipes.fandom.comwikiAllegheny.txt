Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  bourbon 
 1 oz  dry vermouth 
 1½ tsp blackberry-flavored  brandy 
 1½ tsp  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Shake with ice and strain into cocktail glass. 
 Add a twist of  lemon peel  on top.
