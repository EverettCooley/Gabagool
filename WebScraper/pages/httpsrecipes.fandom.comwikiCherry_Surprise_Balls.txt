 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  butter  or  margarine (unsalted  butter  is best) 
 1/2 cup  powdered sugar 
 2 cup  flour ; sifted 
 2 tsp  vanilla 
 2 tsp.  water 
 1 1/2 cup chopped  nuts 
 5 or 6 squares bittersweet or  semisweet chocolate ,melted 
 2 med. sized bottles  maraschino cherries  with stems 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 cream   butter  or  margarine  and  Sugar  until fluffy; Add  flour ,  vanilla , and  water  and mix well. 
 Form individual balls of dough around each  cherry . 
 This dough is very soft and easy to work with. Bake on greased cookies sheet at 350F for 15 to 20 minutes. 
 Cool on rack, then dip bottom of cookie into melted  chocolate , then  nuts . 
 Let set on paper lined trays. 
 Makes 3& 1/2 to 4 dozen cookies & they freeze well
