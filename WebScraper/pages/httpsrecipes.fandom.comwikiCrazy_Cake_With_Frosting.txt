Crazy Cake With Frosting
 
 1 1/2 c  flour 
 1 c  sugar 
 3 tb  cocoa 
 1/2 ts  salt 
 1 ts  baking soda 
 1 tb  vinegar 
 6 tb  oil 
 1 ts  vanilla 
 1 c Cold  water 
 
Mix  flour ,  sugar ,  cocoa ,  salt  And  baking soda  in An
 
 8-Inch Square Pan. Make Three Holes in The Mixture.Put The  vinegar  in One, The  oil  in Another And The 
 vanilla  in The Third. Pour Cold  water  Over All AndStir to Mix Well. Bake at 350 Deg F. For 25 Minutes.Dust Top With Confectioners  sugar , or Use Frosting Listed Below.
 
Crazy  Peanut butter  Frosting
 
 3 Tablespoons Crisco 
 cocoa 
 chocolate  Syrup
 
 1/2 Cup  milk 
 1 Teaspoon  vanilla 
 1 Box 10x  sugar 
 3 Tablespoons  Peanut butter 
 
Mix 3 Heaping Tablespoons of  Peanut butter , 3 Heaping Tablespoons Crisco, One, One Pound Box of Confectioners'  sugar , 1/2 Cup  milk  And 1 Teaspoon  vanilla  Together. Add  cocoa  And  chocolate  Syrup to Taste. Spread on  chocolate  Crazy Cake.
 
Enjoy.
