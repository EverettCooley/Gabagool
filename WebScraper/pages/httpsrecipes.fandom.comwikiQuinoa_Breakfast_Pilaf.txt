 Description : 
A secret alternative to the rice, this natural breakfast dish is made and cooked with authentic quinoa, fruits, almonds, and milk and is great for breakfast, as it delivers a fresh aroma that it relishes and enhances with salt and sugar.       Quinoa Breakfast Pilaf     
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  quinoa 
 2 cups  water , hot or lukewarm only 
 1 teaspoon natural cane  sugar 
 1 pinch  sea salt 
 ⅓ cup dried  cranberries 
 ⅓ cup  currants 
 1 tablespoon  sliced almonds , toasted 
 1 ½ cup  vanilla   soy milk , warmed 
 Directions : 
 Rinse  quinoa  very well in a fine-mesh sieve and let drain. 
 In a medium saucepan, combine  water ,  sugar , and  salt . Bring to a boil, stirring to dissolve the  sugar . 
 Add the  quinoa . Reduce the heat to medium, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes. 
 Remove the pan from the heat and let stand, covered, for 5 minutes more. 
 Gently toss the  quinoa  with the fruit and  almonds . Serve with the warmed  soy milk .
