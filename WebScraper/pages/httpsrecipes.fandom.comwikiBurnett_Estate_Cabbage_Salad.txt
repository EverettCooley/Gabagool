Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. It was obtained when the family collection was purchased from the Burnett Estate in Terrell, Texas in 1981.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large  cabbage , chopped fine 
 ½  green pepper 
 1 white  onion 
 1 small jar  pimento 
 1 tablespoon  mustard seed 
 1 tablespoon  salt 
 1½ cups  vinegar 
 1 tablespoon  celery seed 
 2 cups  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Combine  cabbage ,  pepper  and  onion , then set aside. 
 Mix together pimento,  mustard seed ,  salt ,  vinegar ,  celery seed  and  sugar . 
 Mix with vegetable mixture and pack into jars. 
 Store in refrigerator at least 24 hours. 
 Serve cold.
