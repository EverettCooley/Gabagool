 Ingredients :

 2 cups  sugar 
 1 can  evaporated milk 
 4  eggs , beaten 
 2 tsp  baking powder 
 2 tsp  vanilla 
 1 box  mochiko 
 1 can  blueberry  pie filling 
 Directions : 
 Mix butter, milk, vanilla, and eggs. 
 Stir in dry ingredients. 
 Fold in pie filling.
