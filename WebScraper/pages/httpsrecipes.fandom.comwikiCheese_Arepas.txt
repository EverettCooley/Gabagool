 Ingredients : 
 300ml/10fl.oz. Warm  water 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 225g/8oz  masa harina   flour 
 100g/4oz  Manchego cheese , grated 
 oil  for shallow frying 
 1  avocado , peeled and thinly sliced 
 2  tomatoes , thinly sliced 
 16  black olives , pitted and halved 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Place the  flour  and  salt  in a large mixing bowl and gradually add the warm  water , mixing to form a stiff dough.. 
 Add the  Cheese  and knead the dough for a few of minutes then form into 4 balls. *Flatten the balls to a thickness of about 12mm/ ½ -inch . 
 Heat a little  oil  in a large frying pan, add the arepas and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side until crisp and golden. 
 Drain on kitchen paper 
 To serve – split the arepas in half, place on serving plates and garnish with the  avocado ,  tomato  slices and .
