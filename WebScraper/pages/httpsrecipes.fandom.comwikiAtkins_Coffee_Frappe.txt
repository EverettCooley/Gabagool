 Description : 
 This thick, frozen treat can almost be eaten with a spoon.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  ice 
 3 tablespoons  Sugar  free  vanilla  syrup 
 1/4 cup chilled extra-strong decaffeinated  coffee 
 2 scoops Atkins  vanilla  Shake Mix 
 3 tablespoons  heavy cream 
 Directions : 
 Place ice in a blender and crush well. Add syrup, coffee and shake mix. 
 Blend on high speed until smooth, then add cream and blend just until incorporated.
