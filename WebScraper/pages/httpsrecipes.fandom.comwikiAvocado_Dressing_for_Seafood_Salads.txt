Description : 
 This is like an avocado/hollandaise dressing. Excellent on a  shrimp  salad 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 raw  eggs 
 Â½ cup  lemon juice 
 1 cup  butter , melted 
 2  avocados , quartered and  peeled 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 dash  cayenne 
 1 dash  onion salt 
 Directions : 
 Blend  eggs  with  lemon  in a blender or  food processor  until it is  lemon  colored. 
 Slowly add the bubbling hot melted  butter , just drip it in, using a slow speed on your blender. 
 blend  in the avocado and seasoning until smooth. 
 Chill 30 minutes.
