 Description : 
 A vegetarian's delight that can be served alone, for lunch or dinner.
 
 Ingredients : 
 Any Size  flour  Taco Shell (1 Pkg) 
 3 Genuine  California avocados 
 lettuce 
 *  sour cream 
 Colby   Cheese  (or your favorite  Cheese ) 
 salt  &  pepper 
 Onion  powder 
 garlic powder 
 red wine vinegar 
 olive oil 
 Directions : 
 Dice  avocados  then put in medium size bowl
 Add 1/2 teaspoon  black pepper , 1/4 teaspoon  salt , dash  garlic powder , dash  Onion  powder, 1/8 cup  red wine vinegar , 1/8 cup  olive oil , then stir.
 Spread  sour cream  to cover one side of  flour  shell. 
 Put two tablespoons of  avocado  mixture in center of shell, add some shredded  lettuce , shredded  Cheese  and . 
 Roll-up with Saran Wrap and refrigerate for an hour. 
 Take off Saran Wrap and slice on a diagonal.
