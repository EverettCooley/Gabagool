Columbian Beef and Sweet Potato Stew
 
       Columbian Beef and Sweet Potato Stew     
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb cut-up stew  meat 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  pepper 
 1½ teaspoons  olive oil   or   vegetable oil 
 3 cups peeled  sweet potatoes 
 2 teaspoons finely chopped  garlic 
 1 dry  bay leaf 
 1  cinnamon  stick 
 1 large  onion , cut into eighths 
 1 can Italian-style  tomatoes , with juices 
 8  dried apricots , cut in half 
 chopped fresh  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Remove excess fat from  beef . 
 Cut  beef  into pieces and sprinkle with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Cook  beef  in  oil  about 5 minutes, stirring. 
 Mix  beef  and remaining ingredients except  apricots  and  parsley . 
 Cover and cook on low 8 hours. 
 Stir in  apricots . 
 Cover and cook about 15 minutes. 
 Discard  bay leaf  and  cinnamon  stick. 
 Sprinkle stew with  parsley .
