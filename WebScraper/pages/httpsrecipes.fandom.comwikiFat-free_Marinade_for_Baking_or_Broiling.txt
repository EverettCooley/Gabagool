Ingredients : 
 1 cup dry  red wine 
 1½ tbsp  soy sauce 
 ¼ tsp  oregano 
 ¼ tsp  marjoram 
 ⅛ tsp  pepper 
 2 lbs lean meat (flank or round  steak ,  poultry ,  fish ) 
 Directions : 
 Crumble  oregano  and  marjoram  into wine and soy sauce. 
 Add  pepper . 
 Place meat in a plastic bag, pour marinade over, and seal bag. 
 Chill 12 to 18 hours. 
 Baste meat with marinade while cooking.
