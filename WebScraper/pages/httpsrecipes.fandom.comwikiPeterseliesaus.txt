Description : 
 This  parsley  sauce goes well with all kinds of boiled and with  carrots , garden  peas  or  new potatoes .
 
 Ingredients : 
 Â½ liter  water  or  fish stock 
 75 g  butter 
 30 g  flour 
 1 tbsp finely chopped  parsley 
 pinch of  salt 
 Directions : 
 Mix the  flour  with a little cold  water  to make a paste. 
 Bring the  water  or  fish stock  to the boil and bind it with the  flour  paste. 
 Leave to boil for a short while and take off the heat and add the butter. 
 Add the  parsley . 
 Stir and add  salt  to taste.
