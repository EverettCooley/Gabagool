Ingredients : 
 1 each  egg 
 3 slice  bread , cubed fine 
 ⅓ cup  onion , chopped fine 
 1 tbsp  salt 
 1 tbsp prepared  horseradish , if desired 
 2 cup lean  ground round  (15% fat) 
 ¼ cup  catsup 
 2 tbsp  green pepper , chopped fine 
 ½ tbsp dry  mustard 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 400 °F degrees 
 Mix all ingredients well. 
 Form into a loaf. 
 Place in foil-lined 5 x 9 pan 
 Bake until done (15–20 minutes)
