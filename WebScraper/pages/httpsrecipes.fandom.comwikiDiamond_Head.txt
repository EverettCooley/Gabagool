Ingredients : 
 2 oz  gin 
 ½ oz  apricot brandy 
 1 oz fresh  lemon juice 
 ½ tsp  Superfine sugar 
 ½  egg white 
 Directions : 
 Shake all the ingredients in a shaker and drain them into a cocktail glass.
