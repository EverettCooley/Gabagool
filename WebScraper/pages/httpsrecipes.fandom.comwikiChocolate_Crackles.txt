Description : 
       Chocolate Crackles     
 Makes 24 (approximately)
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups  rice bubbles 
 1½ cups  icing sugar 
 1 cup  desiccated coconut 
 250 g  copha 
 3 tbsp  cocoa 
 24 paper  patty cases 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl, mix the  rice bubbles ,  icing sugar ,  cocoa  and  desiccated coconut . 
 Slowly melt the  copha  in a  saucepan  over a low heat. 
 Allow to cool slightly. 
 Add melted  copha  to  rice bubbles  mixture, stirring until well combined. 
 Spoon mixture into paper  patty cases  and refrigerate until firm.
