Ingredients : 
 4  eggs 
 2 cups  sugar 
 2 tsp  vanilla 
 2 cups  flour , sifted 
 2 tsp  baking powder 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 2 tbsp  butter , melted 
 1 cup  beer 
 1 cup  maraschino cherries 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 375°F 
 Beat  eggs  until thick and  lemon  colored. 
 Gradually beat in  sugar . 
 Add  vanilla . 
 Fold sifted  flour ,  baking powder  and  salt  into  egg  mixture. 
 Heat  beer . 
 Add  beer  and melted  butter  at once to batter. 
 Pour into greased cherry-lined tube or molded pan. 
 Bake at 375°F for 30 minutes. 
 Turn out on cake rack to cool. 
 Prick top of cake and drizzle with 4 tbsp of warm  beer .
