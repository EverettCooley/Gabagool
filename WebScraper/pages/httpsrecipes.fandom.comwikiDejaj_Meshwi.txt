Description : 
 Chicken tikka kabab
 
 Ingredients : 
 500 g (1 lb 2 oz)  chicken breast , cut into 4 cm cubes 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon  garam masala 
 juice of 1  lemon 
 pinch of  black pepper 
 juice of 1 medium  onion 
 1 clove  garlic , crushed 
 6  cherry tomatoes 
 1  green pepper , cored and sliced 
 1 medium  onion , cut into quarters 
 Directions : 
 Prepare the marinade by chopping the  onion  finely and squeeze out the juice by hand. 
 Place the  onion  juice in a medium bowl, add the  lemon juice ,  olive oil ,  garlic ,  garam masala ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Mix well. 
 Add the  chicken  cubes to the bowl and fold in. 
 Cover and leave to marinate for 1 hour. 
 Arrange  chicken  cubes on skewers, alternating with  tomatoes ,  onions  and  green peppers . 
 Place under a hot grill or over hot coal and cook for 10 â€“ 15 minutes turning once or twice until  chicken  is cooked.
