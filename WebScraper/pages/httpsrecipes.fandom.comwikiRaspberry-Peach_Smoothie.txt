Description : 
 This smoothie is made with real fruit and is great for breakfast or a snack.
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 ounces plain fat-free  yogurt 
 1 medium  peach , sliced 
 ½ cup  raspberries 
 1 ⅞ teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 ½ teaspoon sugar 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in a blender and puree until smooth. 
 Pour into a tall glass and serve. 
 Tips : 
 You can also use frozen, unsweetened peaches and raspberries instead of or in addition to fresh fruit. 
 For a thicker smoothie, add ¼ cup of ice cubes before blending.
