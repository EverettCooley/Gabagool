Ingredients : 
 cucumber, peeled, seeded and chopped 
 1 cup plain yogurt 
 1/2 tbs vinegar 
 1/2 tbs olive oil 
 1 tsp garlic, minced 
 mint leaves, chopped 
 salt to taste 
 Directions : 
 Mix a finely chopped, seeded, peeled cucumber, a cup of plain yogurt, 1/2 tbs each vinegar and olive oil, a tsp minced garlic, chopped mint and salt in a bowl. 
 Serve with crackers or chopped veggies
