Description : 
 Ethnicity - Marathi, West Indian 
 Type of meal - Party, Lunch, Dinner 
 Ingredients : 
 1¼ cups  semolina  (rava or sooji) 
 1¼ cups  milk 
 1¼ cups  clarified butter  ( ghee ) 
 2 tbsp  raisins , soaked in  water  for 2 hours 
 ½ tsp  cardamom  powder 
 5  bananas , chopped 
 1¼ cups  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Roast rava in the  ghee  till aromatic. 
 Heat  milk  and lower the flame. 
 Add roasted rava, chopped  bananas ,  sugar  and blend well. 
 Add  raisins  and  cardamom  powder to garnish.
