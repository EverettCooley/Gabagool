Ingredients : 
 pie dough for 1 crust pie 
 Topping : 
 ¾ cup  flour 
 ½ cup  brown sugar 
 ¼ cup  butter 
 Filling : 
 ⅓ cup  sugar 
 2 tbsp  cornstarch 
 ¼ tsp  cinnamon 
 2 cups  mango  slices 
 1 package (10 oz) frozen  raspberries  in light syrup, thawed 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Prepare dough for 1 crust pie. 
 Roll and line 9" pie pan. 
 Combine  flour  and  brown sugar . 
 Cut in  butter  until mixture is crumbly. 
 Set aside. 
 Combine  sugar ,  cornstarch  and  cinnamon . 
 Stir in fruits. 
 Sprinkle with  lemon juice . 
 Pour into pie shell bake at 375°F for 35 minutes. 
 Sprinkle evenly with topping mix. 
 Bake an additional 12–15 minutes until topping is golden brown.
