 Description : 
 This vegetarian recipe can be served as a side dish or as light snack with bread.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups sprigged  Cauliflower 
 1 small  Onion 
 2 tablespoons  oil 
 1 tablespoon soy sauce 
 2  eggs 
 salt  to taste 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Sprig  Cauliflower . Wash and soak in  water . Drain. Slice  Onion . In a pan, heat  oil  and fry  Onion  until it turns transparent. Add  Cauliflower  and soy sauce and toss. Add 1 tablespoon hot  water . Cover pan. Over high heat, cook for 2â€“3 minutes or until  Cauliflower  is tender. Add lightly beaten  eggs  and 1 tablespoon  water . Stir. Add  salt  as desired. When  eggs  set, turn off heat and serve.
