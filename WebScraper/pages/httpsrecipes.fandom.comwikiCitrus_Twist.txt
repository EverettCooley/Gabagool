 Ingredients : 
 1 shot Stolichnaya  vodka 
 1 shot Noilly Prat  sweet vermouth 
 1 shot De Kuyper  triple sec 
 garnish: Twist of  lemon / orange peel 
 Glass: Martini
 
 Directions : 
 Stir all the ingredients together in a cocktail shaker and pour into the cocktail glass. Garnish with a twist of  lemon  or  orange peel .
