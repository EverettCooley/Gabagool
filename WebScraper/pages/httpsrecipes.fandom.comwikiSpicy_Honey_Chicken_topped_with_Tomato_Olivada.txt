Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 4 servings of spicy honey chicken. 
 Ingredients : 
 Tomato olivada : 
 2 tbsp  honey 
 1  tomato , diced 
 ½  green bell pepper , chopped 
 ½  red onion , chopped 
 12  kalamata olives , coarsely chopped 
 1 tbsp  sherry wine vinegar 
 1 tbsp  olive oil 
 ⅛ tsp  salt 
 Chicken : 
 1  jalapeno chile , seeded and finely chopped 
 2 tbsp  honey 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 4 boneless skinless  chicken breast  halves 
 1 pound log  polenta , sliced into 12 slices 
 Directions : 
 Tomato olivada : 
 In a bowl, stir all ingredients. 
 Set aside until serving. 
 Chicken : 
 In a bowl, mix jalapeno, honey and salt. 
 Rub mixture over chicken, cover and refrigerate 1 hour. 
 Heat grill. 
 Grill chicken 12 minutes turning until done. 
 Grill polenta 4 minutes per side or until golden brown and heated through. 
 Top chicken breast halves with tomato olivada, serve each with 3 slices of polenta.
