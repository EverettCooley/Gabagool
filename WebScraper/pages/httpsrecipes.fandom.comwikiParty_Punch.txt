 Description : 
       Party Punch     
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves 25 
 Ingredients : 
 1 bottle  Southern Comfort 
 4 oz.  Jamaican rum 
 4 oz.  lemon juice 
 8 oz.  pineapple juice 
 8 oz.  grapefruit juice 
 2 bottles  champagne 
 whole, cracked, or chipped ice 
 slices of  orange , to decorate 
 Directions : 
 Cool all the ingredients except the champagne, then mix in a punch bowl. 
 Add champagne and whole, cracked, or chipped ice. Pour or scoop the alcohol-based fruit punch in a beer mug or a highball glass. 
 Decorate with slices or orange and serve. 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Servings:  24 glasses or 2-4 scoops per mug
