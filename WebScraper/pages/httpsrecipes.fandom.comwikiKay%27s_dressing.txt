 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 kay's dressing 
 1/2 cup  water 
 1/2 cup  white vinegar 
 1/2 tsp  salt 
 1/2 tsp dry  mustard 
 1/8 tsp ground  pepper , fresh 
 1/5 tsp  paprika 
 sugar substitute  equivalent 
 to 4 teaspoons  Sugar 
 poppy seed  dressing 
 1/2 cup  cider vinegar 
 1 tbsp  poppy seed 
 omit  paprika 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in a pint jar and cover tightly. Shake vigorously and store in refrigerator. Shake before using. Follow recipe for kay"s dressing(above), but use  cider vinegar  in place of  white vinegar , omit  paprika ,  poppy seeds . Delicious over fruit salad.
