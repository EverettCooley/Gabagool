 Description : 
 This layered, classic favorite is sure to appease your guestsâ€™ palette.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can (16 ounces)  refried beans 
 1 can (4 ounces) chopped green chiles, drained 
 1 envelope (1 1/2 ounces)  taco seasoning  mix 
 2  California avocados , seeded and peeled 
 1 Tbsp  lemon juice 
 taco sauce to taste 
 1 Cup  sour cream 
 shredded  lettuce 
 1  tomato , chopped 
 ripe olives , sliced 
 shredded  Cheddar   Cheese 
 tortilla chips 
 Directions : 
 In a small bowl, mix together  refried beans , chiles and  taco seasoning  mix; spread over serving platter. Coarsley mash together avocados,  lemon juice , and taco sauce; spread over bean mixture. 
 Top with  sour cream  and sprinkle  lettuce ,  tomato , and  Cheese  over all. 
 Serve with tortilla chips.
