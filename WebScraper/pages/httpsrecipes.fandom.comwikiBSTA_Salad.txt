 Description : 
 Bacon ,  spinach ,  tomato  and  avocado  Salad with Bacon-Balsamic Vinaigrette Dressing
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 large ripe  avocados 
 2 large ripe  tomatoes 
 1 package ready-to-use baby  spinach  (approximately 6 oz.) 
 12 oz cooked  Bacon  strips 
 ¼ Cup  olive oil 
 2 Tbsp  balsamic vinegar 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 Directions : 
 Vinaigrette:
 Pulverize 5 strips of  Bacon  in food processor or chop extra fine;
 Mix well, preferably shaken,  olive oil ,  balsamic vinegar ,  garlic  and pulverized  Bacon ;
 Set aside.
 Salad:
 Remove stems from  spinach  and place in large salad bowl,
 Dice  avocados  and  tomatoes , about 1/2 inch dice, add to  spinach ,
 Break remaining  Bacon  into small pieces and add to  spinach ,
 Give balsamic dressing another good mixing and add to  spinach ,
 Toss well and enjoy!
