Jocko's Barbecue  pink beans 
 
 3 Cups Dry  pink beans 
 3  onions , Diced 
 2 Green  chile peppers , Canned 
 1 clove  garlic , Minced 
 1/2 to 1 Pound  Bacon , Diced 
 salt  And  pepper 
 
Cover beans With  water  And Simmer 4 Hours. When beans Are Done, Fry Together  Bacon ,  Onion , Chopped  peppers  And  garlic . When Mixture Has Browned, Add it to a Few Cooked beans Which Have Been Mashed And Stir Together. Add This Mixture to The beans And Cook For a Short Time to Allow Seasoning to Flavor The beans. Add  salt  And  pepper  Last, as Adding Earlier Will Make beans Tough. This Can be Frozen. Serves 8
 Source: Come Into The Kitchen
