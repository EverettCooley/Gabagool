Description : 
 A  rum  drink
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz  light rum 
 ¾ oz  apple brandy 
 ¼ tsp  sweet vermouth 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Stir well with cracked  ice . 
 Strain into a glass half filled with  ice  cubes.
