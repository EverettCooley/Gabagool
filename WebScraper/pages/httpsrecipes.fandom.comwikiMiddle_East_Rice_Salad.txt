Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 ¼ cup  vegetable oil 
 2 tablespoons  lemon juice 
 ¾ teaspoon seasoned  pepper 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  rosemary  leaves, crushed 
 ½ teaspoon ground  oregano 
 ½ teaspoon minced  garlic 
 ½ teaspoon  mint  leaves, crushed 
 1 small  zucchini , thinly sliced 
 1 medium  tomato , peeled, seeded, and chopped 
 4 ounces  feta  cheese, cut into small cubes (about ⅔ cup) (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Spoon  rice  into large mixing bowl. 
 Blend  oil ,  lemon juice ,  pepper ,  salt ,  rosemary ,  oregano ,  garlic  and  mint ; stir into  rice . Cover; let cool. 
 Add  zucchini ,  tomato  and  feta . 
 Serve at room temperature or chill before serving.
