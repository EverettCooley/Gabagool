Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  brown rice 
 4 fresh  plums , cut into bite-size pieces 
 ⅓ cup finely chopped fresh  chives 
 ⅓ cup  golden raisins 
 3 tablespoons white  grape juice 
 2 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 1 tablespoon  cider vinegar 
 2 tablespoons  honey 
 1 teaspoon minced  fresh ginger 
 ½ teaspoon  ground coriander 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 2 slices  bacon , cooked and crumbled 
 lettuce  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice , plums,  chives  and  raisins  in large bowl. 
 Do not mix; set aside. 
 Combine  grape juice ,  oil ,  vinegar ,  honey ,  ginger , coriander,  salt  and  pepper  in small bowl; whisk to blend. 
 Pour dressing over salad and toss. 
 Top with  bacon . 
 Serve over  lettuce  leaves.
