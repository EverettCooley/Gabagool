Kielbasa with  sauerkraut  and  Apples . 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  Sauerkraut 
 1 pound pork chops 
 3 to 4 tart cooking  Apples , thickly sliced 
 1/2 cup packed  brown sugar 
 3/4 teaspoon  Salt 
 1/8 teaspoon  Pepper 
 1/2 teaspoon  Caraway seeds 
 3/4 cup  Apple Juice 
 Directions : 
 Rinse  Sauerkraut  and squeeze dry. 
 Place half of the  sauerkraut  in a slow cooker. 
 Place pork chops into slow cooker. 
 Continue to layer in slow cooker, the sliced  Apples ,  brown sugar ,  Salt ,  Pepper  and  Caraway seeds . 
 Cover and cook on LOW for 6 to 8 hours or until  Apples  are tender. 
 Stir before serving. 
 Serves 4.
