Description : 
 This vinaigrette is lovely with all leaf salads.
 
       Green Vinaigrette     
 Ingredients : 
 1 portion vinaigrette 
 a little  Dijon mustard 
 parsley 
 chives 
 dill 
 chervil 
 garlic 
 Directions : 
 Add  Dijon mustard ,  parsley ,  chives ,  dill ,  chervil  and crushed  garlic  (if desired) to a portion of the vinaigrette.
