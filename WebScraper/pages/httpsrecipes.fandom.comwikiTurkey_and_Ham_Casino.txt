 Description : 
 Makes 12 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 1 cup chopped green  bell peppers 
 ¼ cup  butter  or  margarine 
 ½ cup  flour 
 2 cups  milk 
 2 cups  chicken broth 
 ¼ cup dry  sherry  or other dry  white wine 
 2 cups coarsely chopped  turkey 
 2 cups coarsely chopped cooked  ham 
 1 x 8-ounce can  water chestnuts , drained and sliced 
 1 x 4-ounce can sliced  mushrooms , drained 
 ½ cup diced  pimentos 
 salt  and ground  black pepper  to taste 
 6 to 8 cups hot cooked  rice 
 Directions : 
 Cook  onion  and  peppers  in  butter  until tender; blend in  flour . 
 Add  milk , broth and  sherry ; cook, stirring constantly, until thickened. 
 Stir in  turkey ,  ham ,  water chestnuts ,  mushrooms  and pimentos; add  salt  and  pepper  to taste. 
 Heat thoroughly. 
 Serve over fluffy  rice .
