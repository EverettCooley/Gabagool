 Salad

 2 large  potatoes  (cut into 1/2 inch squares) 1 large chopped  carrot 1/2 cup of  Peas 1/2 cup of tin corn cernals 3 tablespoons of finely chopped pickles 2 hard boiled eggs chopped into squares 200 to 500g  Mayonnaise salt  and  lemon juice  to taste optional: 1/2 cup chopped  parsley optional: 200-400g chopped  Ham optional: Garnish with green olives 
 
 
 
 Description : 
 A great Russian salad, perfect to serve at a party or as a side dish! Explore  Main Dish Recipes  to find the perfect entree to accompany the salad.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 large  potatoes  (cut into 1/2 inch squares) 
 1 large chopped  carrot 
 1/2 cup of  Peas 
 1/2 cup of tin corn cernals 
 3 tablespoons of finely chopped pickles 
 2 hard boiled eggs chopped into squares 
 200 to 500g  Mayonnaise 
 salt  and  lemon juice  to taste 
 optional: 1/2 cup chopped  parsley 
 optional: 200-400g chopped  Ham 
 optional: Garnish with green olives 
 Directions : 
 Boil the  potatoes ,  carrots , eggs and the p eas . 
 When cooled, mix everything together and let stand for an hour.
