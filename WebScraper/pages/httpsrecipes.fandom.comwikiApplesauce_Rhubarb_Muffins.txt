Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 c.  flour 
 1 c.  whole wheat flour 
 2 tsp.  baking powder 
 2 tsp.  cinnamon 
 1/2 tsp.  baking soda 
 1/2 tsp.  salt 
 2  eggs 
 1 1/3 c.  brown sugar , packed 
 1 1/3 c.  applesauce 
 1/2 c. cooking  oil 
 1 1/2 c.  rhubarb , chopped 
 Sugar  or  cinnamon 
 
 
 Directions : 
 In large bowl, mix together  flour ,  whole wheat flour ,  baking powder ,  cinnamon ,  baking soda  and  salt . 
 Make a well in center, set aside. 
 In another bowl, beat  eggs , stir in  brown sugar ,  applesauce  and  oil . 
 Add  egg  mixture all at once to  flour  mixture. 
 Stir until just moistened. (Batter will be lumpy.) 
 Fold in  rhubarb . 
 Fill muffin cups 2/3 full. 
 Sprinkle with  Sugar  or  cinnamon . 
 Bake at 350 degrees for 30 to 35 minutes.
