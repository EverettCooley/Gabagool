      Barley Soup     
 Ingredients : 
 900 g  meat 
 2 cups  beans 
 1 cup  barley 
 2  onions 
 2 tsp  salt 
 Â½ tsp  pepper 
 2 tbsp chopped  green 
 3 l  water 
 Directions : 
 Soak beans in cold  water  over a night. 
 Pour cold  water  over meat,boil and cook on low heat for 45 minutes. 
 Add  barley , beans, finely chopped  onion  and cook on very low heat for 1 hour. 
 salt  and  pepper  and cook for 10 minutes. 
 Soup is good when beans and meat are soft and tender.
