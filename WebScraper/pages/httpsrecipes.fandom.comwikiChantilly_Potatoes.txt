Description 
 Potatoes, Dehydrated Flakes, Dry  by the US Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Serves : 8 
 Ingredients 
 6 servings prepared instant mashed  potatoes 
 1 cup diced  ham 
 ½ cup  whipping cream 
 1 cup shredded  Cheddar  cheese 
 Directions 
 Preheat oven 450 °F 
 Spread mashed potatoes in a 2 quart casserole. Cover with ham. 
 Whip cream until stiff; fold in cheese. 
 Spread mixture over ham. 
 Bake for 10–15 minutes or until golden brown.
