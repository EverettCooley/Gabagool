Description : 
 Contributed by Lilly at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 8 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups  blueberries 
 1 (7-oz) jar  marshmallow crème 
 2 cups  whipping cream 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Place  blueberries  in blender container or food processor bowl. 
 Cover; process until smooth. 
 Gradually add  blueberries  to  marshmallow crème  in lg bowl; mixing until well blended. 
 Add  whipping cream  and  vanilla ; mixing on high speed until mixture becomes thickened and fluffy. 
 Freeze until firm.
