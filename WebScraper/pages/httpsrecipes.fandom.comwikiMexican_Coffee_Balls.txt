      chocolate wafer cookies     
 Ingredients : 
 1 (9 ounce) package  chocolate wafer  cookies, crushed 
 ½ pound  ground almonds 
 ⅓ cup  unsweetened cocoa powder 
 ¼ cup  white sugar 
 2 tablespoons instant  coffee  powder 
 ⅓ cup  coffee-flavored liqueur 
 ½ cup  light corn syrup 
 ¼ cup  white sugar 
 2 teaspoons ground  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl, mix  chocolate  wafer crumbs, ground  blanched almonds ,  unsweetened cocoa powder , and ¼ cup  sugar . 
 Dissolve instant  coffee  in  coffee  liqueur and stir into crumb mixture with  corn syrup . 
 Shape into ¼ inch balls and roll in  cinnamon sugar . 
 To make  cinnamon sugar , combine ¼ cup  sugar  with 2 teaspoons  cinnamon . 
 Store in refrigerator.
