Description : 
 Add an ear of  corn  to this guacamole to help you score an extra guacamole point with your guests.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 ripe genuine  California avocados , seeded, peeled and coarsely mashed 
 1 Tbsp fresh  lemon juice 
 10 drops  red pepper sauce 
 Â½ tsp coarse  garlic salt 
 1 ear  corn  (husk and silk removed) roasted or grilled 
 1 medium  tomato , chopped 
 3 Tbsp chopped fresh  cilantro  or  Italian parsley  leaves 
 1 Tbsp finely chopped  red onion 
 Directions : 
 In medium bowl combine avocados,  lemon juice , red pepper sauce and  garlic salt . 
 Carefully cut  corn  off husk and add to  avocado . 
 Stir in  tomato ,  cilantro  and  red onion . 
 Serve with tortilla chips or cut vegetables.
