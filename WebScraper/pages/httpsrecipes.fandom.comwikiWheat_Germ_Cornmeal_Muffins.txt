 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/4 cup  all-purpose flour 
 1/2 cup yellow  corn  meal 
 1/4 cup toasted  wheat germ 
 1/4 cup  Sugar 
 1 tbsp  baking powder 
 1/8 tsp  salt 
 2  egg  white 
 1 cup skim  milk 
 3 tbsp puritan  oil 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 400 °F.  oil  12 medium muffin cups lightly. Combine first 6 ingredients in medium bowl. Make a well in center. Combine  egg  whites,  milk , and Puritan  oil  in small bowl. Add all at once to  flour  mixture, stirring until just moistened. (Batter should be lumpy. )Spoon batter evenly into muffin cups, filling almost full. Bake at 400 °F. For 20 minutes or until very lightly browned. Serve warm. An easy Make-Over: Use 2  egg  whites in place of 1  egg , and skim  milk  in place of whole  milk . You'll say goodbye to cholesterol.
