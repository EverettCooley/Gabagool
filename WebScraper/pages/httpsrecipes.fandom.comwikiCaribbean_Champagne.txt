Description : 
 A  rum  drink
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ tsp  light rum 
 ½ tsp  crème de banane 
 chilled  champagne 
 Directions : 
 Pour  rum  and  crème de banane  into a champagne flute. 
 Fill with champagne and stir gently. 
 Top with a slice of  banana .
