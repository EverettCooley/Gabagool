Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 4 dozen cookies 
 Ingredients : 
 2½ cups  flour 
 ¾ cup  cocoa  powder 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 ¾ cup  butter , softened 
 ½ cup  light brown sugar 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 2  eggs 
 2 tsp  vanilla 
 Filling : 
 2¼ cups  confectioner's sugar 
 ⅓ cup solid  vegetable shortening 
 ¾ tsp grated  orange rind 
 3 tbs  orange juice 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 375°F. 
 Mix first 4 ingredients in bowl. 
 Beat butter, sugars in a separate bowl until creamy. 
 Beat in eggs. 
 Add vanilla. 
 Beat in flour mixture. 
 On floured surface, roll out half of dough to ⅛ inch thickness. 
 Cut with 1¾ inch heart shaped cookie cutter. 
 Transfer to ungreased baking sheet. 
 Bake 10 minutes. 
 Cool cookies. 
 Roll, cut and bake remaining dough. 
 Beat all filling ingredients in bowl until smooth but thick. 
 Spread 1 tsp on half of the cookies. 
 Sandwich with second cookie. 
 Let set 30 minutes. 
 Dust with confectioners sugar.
