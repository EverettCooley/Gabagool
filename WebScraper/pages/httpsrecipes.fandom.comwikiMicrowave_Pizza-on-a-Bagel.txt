Description : 
 Contributed by Delma at  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  bagel 
 2 tbsp  pizza sauce 
 8 slices  pepperoni  or other topping of choice 
 2 tbsp shredded  mozzarella  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Cut bagel in half. 
 Place cut-side-up on microwave-safe serving plate. 
 Spread each half with 1 tablespoon pizza sauce. 
 Top each with 4 slices pepperoni and 1 tablespoon cheese. 
 Microwave on high, uncovered, 1 to 1Â½ minutes or until cheese begins to melt, rotating plate once.  Note : if heating only one of the halves, reduce time to 35 to 45 seconds.
