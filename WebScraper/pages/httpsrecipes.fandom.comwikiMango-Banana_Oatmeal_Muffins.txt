 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  whole wheat flour 
 1/2 cup all purpose  flour 
 1/2 cup  oatmeal 
 1/4 cup  brown sugar 
 2 tsp.  baking powder 
 1/4 tsp.  salt 
 1 tsp.  cinnamon 
 1/4 cup  butter 
 1  egg , beaten 
 1 cup  milk 
 1 tsp.  vanilla 
 1/2 cup  banana , mashed 
 1/2 cup  mango , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Combine flours,  oatmeal ,  Sugar ,  baking powder ,  salt ,  cinnamon . 
 Cut in  butter , then moisten with  milk ,  egg  and  vanilla  mixture. 
 Fold in  banana  and  mango . Spoon batter into greased muffin pan 2/3 full and bake at 400' for 20 to 25 minutes.
