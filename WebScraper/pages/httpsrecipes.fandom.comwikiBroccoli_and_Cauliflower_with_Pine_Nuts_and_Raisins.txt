Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Makes 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups  broccoli  florets 
 1½ cups  cauliflower  florets 
 2 cloves  garlic , thinly sliced 
 2 tbsp  raisins 
 2 tbsp  pine nuts 
 3 tbsp  olive oil 
 salt  and  pepper , to taste 
 Directions : 
 Steam the broccoli and cauliflower until they're the consistency you like them. 
 Meanwhile, soak raisins in very hot water to plump them up. 
 Heat oil over medium-high heat, and sauté garlic until slightly golden. 
 Add pine nuts and sauté another minute. 
 Pour garlic and pine nut oil over broccoli and cauliflower and toss with drained raisins. 
 Salt and pepper to taste. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 Calories 80 | Fat 6 g | Carbs 6 g | Sodium 15 mg | Fiber 0.8 g.
