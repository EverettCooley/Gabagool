Wonder Dessert Cake
 
 1 C.  flour 
 2 Tbsp.  sugar 
 1 Stick  butter 
 1/2 C. Nuts 
 
Mix. Bake in 8x11 Pan. Bake 375 Degrees For 15
 Minutes.
 
Second Layer:
 
 1 Pkg. (8 Oz.) Philadelphia  cream cheese 
 2/3 C.  powdered sugar 
 
Cream Together. Add 1/2 Carton of  Cool Whip  And SpreadOver Cooled Cake.
 
Third Layer:
 
 2 Pkgs. Instant  lemon  Pudding 
 2 1/2 C.  milk 
 
Beat Until Thick And Spread Over Second Layer.
 
Fourth Layer:
 
Spread Remaining  Cool Whip  on Top. Chill. SprinkleWith Nuts or  coconut  or Both. Chill Overnight. ServeAs Dessert on Your Crystal or Dessert Plates.
 
Enjoy.
