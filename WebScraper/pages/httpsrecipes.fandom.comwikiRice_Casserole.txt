 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 rice 
 chopped  Onion 
 granted non-fat  Cheese 
 tomatoes 
 yellow squash 
 zucchini 
 non-fat  sour cream  or 
 yogurt 
 Directions : 
 Cook  rice . Put  rice  on the bottom of a 9x12" baking dish Top with chopped  Onion  and grated non-fat  Cheese  Layer on 2 good-sized sliced  tomatoes . You can also use sliced  yellow squash  or  zucchini . Top with non-fat  sour cream  or  yogurt . Bake at 350 °F for 30 minutes
