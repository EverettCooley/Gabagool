Description : 
 Contributed by  PressureCookerRecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  chicken , cut-up 
 pressure cooker, ½ full of water 
 ½ cup  soy sauce 
 ⅛ to ¼ cup  vinegar 
 1  onion 
 1  garlic  clove 
 1 inch square  ginger  (don't cut up) 
 3 to 4  bay leaves 
 Directions : 
 Put all ingredients into pressure cooker and cook until done, about 45 minutes. 
 Thicken gravy with Wondra flour or cornstarch if desired. 
 Serve over rice.
