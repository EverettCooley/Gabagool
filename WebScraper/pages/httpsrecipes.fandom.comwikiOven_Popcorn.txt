Tastes just like Cracker Jacks. My aunt gave me this recipe 30 years ago. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups brown sugar 
 1 cup margarine 
 1/2 cup corn syrup 
 1 teaspoon salt 
 1 teaspoon soda 
 1 teaspoon butter flavor or vanilla 
 6 quarts popped corn 
 Peanuts or mixed nuts 
 Directions : 
 Boil first 4 ingredients for 5 minutes. 
 Remove from heat and add flavoring and soda. 
 Stir well through popcorn and add nuts. 
 Spread on cookie sheets and bake for 1 hour at 200 degrees. Stir every 15 minutes. 
 Store in airtight container.
