 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  ground beef  (leaner = healthier) 
 ¼ cup  all purpose flour 
 1 tablespoon  chili powder 
 1 tablespoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  dried onion , minced 
 ½ teaspoon  paprika 
 ¼ teaspoon  onion powder 
 garlic powder , sprinkle to taste… but don’t go overboard 
 ½ cup  water 
 16 ounces canned  refried beans 
 8 x 10-inch flour  tortillas 
 ½ cup  enchilada sauce 
 ¾ cup  sour cream 
 2 cups  lettuce , shredded ( iceberg  is best) 
 2 cups  cheddar  cheese, shredded 
 medium-sized  tomato , diced (1 should be enough) 
 ½ cup  yellow onion , diced 
 Directions : 
 First prepare the base by mixing together the beef, flour, minced onion, garlic powder, chili powder, salt, paprika, minced onion, and onion power and in a bowl. 
 Drop the base in a skillet with the water, and put it on medium flame. 
 Stir and cook until brown (should take about 5 minutes or so). 
 Next you heat up your refried beans and tortillas in the microwave. 
 Put together all ingredients in the following order: tortilla, beans, beef base, enchilada sauce, lettuce, cheese, tomato, onion and  then  the sour cream. 
 Done.
