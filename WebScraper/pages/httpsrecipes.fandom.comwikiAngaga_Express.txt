 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cl  vodka 
 6 cl  Benedictine 
 grapefruit juice 
 Icecubes 
 Directions : 
 Giving  vodka  and  Benedictine  (Dom  Galliano ) in a half-filled glass with  ice  and stirring strongly in the blender. 
 Fill up with  grapefruit juice  and decorate with a piece of a  pineapple  slice.
