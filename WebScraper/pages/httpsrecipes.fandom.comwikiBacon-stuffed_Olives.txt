 Ingredients : 
 pitted  olives 
 4 slices  bacon 
 3 oz (87 g) pkg.  cream cheese 
 Â¼ cup  butter  60 ml 
 Directions : 
 Fry bacon until crisp. 
 Cool and crumble into bits. 
 Whip cheese and butter until smooth. 
 Stir in bacon bits. 
 Stuff into well-drained, pitted olives (ripe or green), using pastry tube or tip of spoon.
