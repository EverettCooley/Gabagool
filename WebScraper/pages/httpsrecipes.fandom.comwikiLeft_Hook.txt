Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ oz  Southern Comfort ® peach liqueur 
 ½ oz Jack Daniel's® Tennessee  whiskey 
 Directions : 
 Pour both the  Southern Comfort  and Jack Daniel's in equal parts into a shot glass. 
 Stir, and serve.
