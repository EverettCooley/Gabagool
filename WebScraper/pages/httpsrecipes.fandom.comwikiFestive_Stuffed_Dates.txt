Ingredients : 
 1 (8 oz.) box whole pitted Dole  dates 
 1 (3 oz.) pkg.  cream cheese 
 1/4 c.  powdered sugar 
 1 tbsp. grated  orange peel 
 Directions : 
 Make slit in center of each date. 
 Combine cream cheese, powdered sugar and orange peel. 
 Fill center of date with cream cheese mixture. 
 Refrigerate. 
 Notes : 
 Dust with additional powdered sugar just before serving if desired. 
 Makes about 27 stuffed dates.
