Description : 
 Recipe from  sneakykitchen.com 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3½ oz package low-fat microwave  popcorn   or  ½ cup  popcorn  kernals, popped 
 1½ cups roasted salted  peanuts 
 2 tbsp  butter  or  margarine 
 2 tbsp  honey 
 ½ tsp ground  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Prepare  popcorn  and place in a large bowl. 
 Add  peanuts  and stir to mix. 
 In a 2-cup microwave-safe measuring cup, combine  butter ,  honey  and  cinnamon . 
 Microwave on high until  butter  is melted, about 30 seconds to 1 minute. 
 Drizzle  butter  mixture slowly over  popcorn , stirring with a large spoon. 
 Taste and  salt  lightly if necessary. 
 Serve immediately while still warm.
