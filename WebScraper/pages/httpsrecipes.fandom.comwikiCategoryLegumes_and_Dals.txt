 Legumes and dals can range from spicy-sweet to scorching hot, soup like or like creamed thick soup or dry like a pilaf. Here are the most popular Indian dals:
Besan,
Moong Dal Whole,
Urad Dal Whole,
Urad Dal Chlka,
Tuvar Dal/Toovar Dal/Toor Dal,
Moong Dal (Lentil) with Spices,
Moong Dal Chilka,
Urad Dal without skin,
Kala Channa,
Kabuli Chana,
Moong Dal Split and without Skin,
Masoor Dal,
Channa Dal, Rajma
