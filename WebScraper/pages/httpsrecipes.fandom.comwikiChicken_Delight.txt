Chicken  Delight
 
 
 1 Box Uncle Ben's Long  grain  Wild- rice  Mix. (cooked, Cooled Completely) 
 2-3  Chicken  Breast Halves, Cooked as Desired, Cooled And Then Shredded. 
 1 Medium  Onion , Chopped 
 1 Cup Chopped  celery 
 1 Medium Green  bell pepper , Chopped 
 2 Cans (8 Ounces Each)  water chestnuts , Drained And Chopped 
 2 Cans Each of Cream of  Chicken  Soup 
 3/4 Cups Real  Mayonnaise 
 8 Glubs  soy sauce 
 
Shredded Yellow  Cheese  (about 1 Pound)
 
Seasoned Croutons to Taste (don't Skimp on The Amount) Crush Croutons.
 
 
In a Large Bowl, Mix  rice  And  Chicken ,  Onion   celery ,  green pepper  And  water chestnuts .Add Soup And  Mayonnaise . Mix Thoroughly. Turn Into Buttered 9x13
 
Casserole Dish. Cover With Shredded  Cheese  Then Crushed Croutons Then Shredded
 
 Cheese . Press The Top of Casserole. Cover And Refrigerate Overnight. Uncover And Bake at 325 Degrees 55 to 60 Minutes or Until Hot And bubbly
