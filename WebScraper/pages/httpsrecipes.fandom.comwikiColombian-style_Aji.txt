      Colombian-style Aji     
 Ingredients : 
 2 medium  yellow onions 
 1 bunch  green onions 
 1 bunch  cilantro 
 2 red hot  chile peppers 
 300 ml of  vinegar 
 salt 
 cumin 
 Directions : 
 Finely chop the  onions  and  cilantro . 
 Blend the  chile   peppers  with the  vinegar . 
 Mix everything together, add  salt , and  cumin  to taste.
