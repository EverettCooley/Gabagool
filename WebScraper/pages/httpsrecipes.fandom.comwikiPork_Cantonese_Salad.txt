Description : 
 Makes 10 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups cooked  rice , cooled 
 3 cups cold cooked  pork  roast, cut into long strips 1/4-inch wide 
 2 cups diagonally-sliced  celery 
 2 cups fresh  bean sprouts * 
 1 8-ounce can sliced  water chestnuts , drained 
 1 6-ounce can sliced  bamboo shoots , drained 
 ½ cup sliced  green onions 
 ¼ cup chopped  pimentos 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 2 tablespoons  soy sauce 
 1 teaspoon  ground ginger 
 1 teaspoon dry  mustard 
 ½ teaspoon  garlic powder 
 ½ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1 5-ounce can  chow mein noodles 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  pork ,  celery ,  bean sprouts ,  water chestnuts ,  bamboo shoots  and  onions  in large bowl. 
 Blend  mayonnaise ,  soy sauce ,  ginger ,  mustard ,  garlic powder  and  pepper  in small bowl. 
 Stir into  rice  mixture; toss lightly. Chill. 
 Serve topped with  chow mein noodles .
