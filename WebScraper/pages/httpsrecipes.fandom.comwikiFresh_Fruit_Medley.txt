Description : 
 The fruit medley may also be served without topping. Other fruits may be substituted, such as melons,  kiwifruit ,  strawberries ,  blueberries ,  pears , or  pineapple . 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 small  apple , peeled and diced 
 12 med size  bananas , sliced 
 1 small  orange , sectioned 
 ½ cup seedless  grapes 
 1 tbsp frozen  orange juice  -concentrate 
 ¼ cup low-fat  yogurt 
 2 tbsp part-skin  ricotta cheese 
 ⅛ tsp ground  ginger 
 ⅛ tsp ground  cinnamon 
 ½ tsp pure  vanilla extract 
 4  walnut  halves 
 Directions : 
 Place all fruit in a bowl and mix in frozen  orange juice  concentrate. 
 In a blender, combine  yogurt , ricotta,  ginger ,  cinnamon  and  vanilla . 
 Portion the friut into dessert bowls, top each with yogurt-ricotta sauce and garnish with a  walnut  half. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Food exchange per serving: 1 fruit exchange + ½ fat exchange; cal: 87; cho: 2 mg; car: 17g; pro: 2g; sod: 20 mg; fat: 2g;
