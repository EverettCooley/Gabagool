Description 
 Fruit & Vegetable Recipes I  by the South Carolina Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Ingredients 
 ⅓ lb  shiitake mushrooms 
 1 Tbsp  butter 
 1 Tbsp  olive oil 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions 
 Clean mushrooms and cut into ¼" slices. 
 Heat saucepan large enough to fit all mushrooms in one layer without crowding, add olive oil and butter. When hot, add mushrooms, saute on one side, turn, and saute on other side. 
 Remove pan from heat when done so they don't dry out. Add salt and pepper. Return pan to heat briefly just before serving
