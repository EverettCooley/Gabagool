Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Wagner Estate in Greenville, Texas in 1984. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 ounces  brandy 
 1 scoop lemon  ice cream 
 1 scoop orange  sherbet 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in blender until well blended. 
 Pour in highball glass and serve immediately.
