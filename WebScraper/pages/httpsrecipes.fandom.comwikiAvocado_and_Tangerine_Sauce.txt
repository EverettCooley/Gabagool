Ingredients : 
 3  avocados , seeded, peeled and chopped 
 3  tangerines , seeded, peeled and deveined 
 1  serrano pepper , finely chopped 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients in a bowl. 
 Serve with  Avocado Rica Arepa Empanada .
