Description : 
 A European way to prepare baked fish using white fish and a cheese sauce. Very easy and very good.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ pounds fish fillets, use  orange roughy ,  sole  or  snapper 
 ½ cup  milk 
 2 tablespoons  butter 
 2 tablespoons  flour 
 1 cup  milk 
 salt  and  white pepper , to taste 
 1 teaspoon dry  sherry 
 ¼ pound  Swiss cheese , shredded 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F to 375°F. 
 Spray a baking dish with nonstick cooking spray. 
 Add fish fillets to baking dish. 
 Pour ½ cup milk over fish fillets. 
 Bake 30 minutes, or until fish is cooked through. 
 Make the white sauce by melting the butter, adding the flour, salt and pepper. Whisk in the 1 cup milk and cook until sauce is smooth and thickened. Keep warm. 
 Whisk in the sherry. 
 Remove baked fish from oven after the baking time has finished. 
 Nap with white sauce. 
 Sprinkle grated cheese over top of sauce and fish. 
 Broil until golden. 
 Notes : 
 Tip : Use a broiler proof baking dish. 
 Warnings :
 Use care when removing fish from oven. 
 Use care when broiling the fish. 
 Things You'll Need :
 Measuring cup 
 Measuring spoons 
 Broiler proof baking dish 
 Saucepan 
 Whisk 
 Grater 
 Oven mitts
