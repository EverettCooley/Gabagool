Description : 
 This is wonderful warm, with some nice vanilla ice cream or real  whipped cream .
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups peeled sliced  apples  (I use  Granny Smith , but any would be okay) 
 1 cup of  sugar 
 ⅔ cup of  canola oil 
 1  egg  slightly beaten 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 1 teaspoon  cinnamon 
 1 teaspoon  baking soda 
 1½ cups  flour  - unsifted 
 ½ cup  nuts  - optional 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Hand mix all ingredients together and spread in pan. 
 Bake for 50 minutes or until nicely brown.
