Description : 
 Makes 16 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 no stick  cooking spray 
 1⅓ cups  flour 
 ½ cup unsweetened  cocoa 
 ¼ cup Argo  corn starch 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 ½ tsp  baking soda 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 1¼ cup  sugar 
 1 cup  water 
 3  egg whites 
 ½ cup  Karo   light corn syrup 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Spray 9x9-inch square baking pan with  cooking spray . 
 In a large bowl combine  flour ,  cocoa ,  cornstarch ,  baking powder  and  salt . 
 In a medium bowl with wire whisk or fork, stir together  sugar  and  water  1 minute. 
 Add  egg whites  and  corn syrup ; stir until blended. 
 Gradually stir into dry ingredients until smooth. 
 Pour into pan. 
 Bake 35 minutes or until toothpick inserted into center of cake comes out clean. 
 Cool. 
 If desired, sprinkle with confectioners  sugar .
