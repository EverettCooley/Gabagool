Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Gonzales Estate in Crandall, Texas in 1980.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2  eggs 
 ½ cup  milk 
 1 cup finely crushed  saltines 
 ¼ teaspoon  pepper 
 1½ pound ground fully cooked  ham 
 1 pound  ground fresh pork 
 Sauce : 
 1 cup packed  brown sugar 
 1 teaspoon  prepared mustard 
 ⅓ cup  vinegar 
 ¼ cup water 
 8 ounces crushed  pineapple  undrained 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl beat eggs then add milk, crackers and pepper then add ham and pork and mix well then shape into loaf and place in shallow baking dish. 
 In small bowl combine sauce ingredients and pour over loaf. 
 Bake at 350°F for 1½ hours.
