 Ingredients : 
 ru'a' (very thin cracker-type bread) 
 savory minced beef 
 meat  broth 
 butter 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Grease a baking tray and boil the meat broth. Take one layer of ru'a' at a time, dip in boiling meat broth, and line bottom of tray. 
 Sprinkle with  butter  and repeat to cover bottom with 3 to 4 layers. Spread  savory minced beef  with the remaining melted  butter  and bake in preheated moderate oven. 
 When the top browns, turn over to allow the bottom to brown as well. 
 Cut into sections while still hot.
