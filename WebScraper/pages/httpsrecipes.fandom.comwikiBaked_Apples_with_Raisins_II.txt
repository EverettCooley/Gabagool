 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2  Apples 
 orange juice 
 1 tbsp  Raisins 
 pinch ground  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Peel and core the  Apples . Coat with the  orange juice  on the outer surface. Stuff with the  Raisins . Sprinkle on the  cinnamon . Place in pyrex baking cups. Bake in a 350 °F oven 25 to 30 minutes or until fork pierces surface easily. Serve warm or cold. 1  Apple  - 74 calories, 1 ½ fruit exchange 19 grams carbohydrate, 0 protein, 0 fat, 0 sodium, 105 potassium, 0 cholesterol
