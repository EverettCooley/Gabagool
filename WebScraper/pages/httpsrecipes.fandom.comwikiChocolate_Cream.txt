Description : 
 In Romanian: crema de ciocolata
 
 Ingredients : 
 Same ingredients as for  Vanilla Cream  plus: 
 2 tablespoons grated  chocolate   or   cacao 
 Directions : 
 Prepare the same way as  Vanilla Cream , adding 2 tablespoons of grated  chocolate  or cacao at the same time with the  vanilla  and  milk .
