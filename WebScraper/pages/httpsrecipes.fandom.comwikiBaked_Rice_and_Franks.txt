Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups cooked  rice 
 1 12-ounce package frankfurters, thinly sliced 
 1 cup finely chopped  Onion , divided 
 1 cup sliced  celery 
 1 cup grated  Cheddar   Cheese 
 2  eggs , well beaten 
 1/3 cup  Mayonnaise 
 1 teaspoon prepared  mustard 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1/4 teaspoon ground black  pepper 
 1 8-ounce can  tomato  sauce 
 1/2 cup chopped green  bell pepper 
 3/4 cup  beef broth 
 2 to 3 drops hot pepper sauce 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice , frankfurters, 1/2 cup  Onion ,  celery  and  Cheese . 
 Blend  eggs ,  Mayonnaise ,  mustard ,  salt  and  pepper . Pour over  rice  mixture and mix well. 
 Spoon into buttered shallow baking dish. Bake at 350 degrees 30 minutes or until knife inserted near center comes out clean. 
 Serve with sauce made by combining remaining 1/2 cup  Onion ,  tomato  sauce, green  pepper , broth and  pepper  sauce; simmering 10 minutes or until slightly thickened.
