 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 450 gm  leeks 
 50 gm  Onion 
 2 x  chile , Green 
 1/4 tsp  turmeric 
 8 curry leaves 
 1 tsp  salt 
 175 ml  coconut milk , thin 
 100 ml  coconut milk , thick 
 1/4 tsp  curry  powder 
 Directions : 
 Wash the  leeks , cut the green part into 1 cm slices and the stalk into 50 mm slices. Chop  onions  and chiles and place in a pan. Add the  leeks ,  turmeric   salt ,  curry leaves  and thin  coconut milk  and bring to the boil. Simmer until the gravy has reduced. Add the  coconut milk , thick, bring back to the boil and just prior to serving sprinkle with 1/4 teaspoon  curry  powder.
