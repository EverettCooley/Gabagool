 Description : 
 Serve Hot 
 Try Raita with this dish. 
 Ingredients : 
 2	 cups  black beans , canned 
 1\2	 cup jar chunky salsa 
 1	 medium  Onion , cut into small cubes 
 1	 medium  bell pepper , seeded and chopped 
 2	 -  tomatoes , diced 
 2	 teaspoon  chilli powder 
 2	 cups  lettuce , shredded 
 8-10Â inch  corn  tortillas 
 sour cream 
 Cheddar   Cheese , grated 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl mix the  black beans , salsa,  Onion ,  peppers  and  chili powder . If possible refrigerate over night to enhance the flavor. 
 Microwave the bean mixture for about 2 minutes or heat over low heat for 5 minutes. 
 Add little  water  if the mixture is too thick. 
 Spread one tablespoon of  sour cream  in the center of the burrito, then place three tablespoons of the mixture over the  cream  and a layer of  tomato ,  lettuce  and  Cheese . 
 Fold the ends and then roll up. Microwave until just heated through. 
 Serve with extra  sour cream  and salsa.
