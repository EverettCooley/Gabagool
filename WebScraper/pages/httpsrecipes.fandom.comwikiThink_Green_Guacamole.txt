Description : 
 Guacamole is best made as close to service as possible. Store in an airtight container with plastic wrap against the surface of the guacamole.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4  Hass avocados , ripe, peeled, seeded, and cut into chunks 
 1 cup white  goat cheese , fresh and finely crumbled 
 ½ cup  cilantro , chopped 
 ¼ cup  pistachio nuts , chopped and toasted 
 ½ tsp  red pepper flakes , crushed 
 4  garlic  cloves, finely chopped 
 Directions : 
 Coarsely mash avocados. 
 Fold in remaining ingredients. Serve with colorful chips or vegetables.
