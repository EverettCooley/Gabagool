Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x 5-ounce package quick cooking  chicken -flavored  long-grain  and  wild rice  mix, prepared 
 1 cup cooked cubed  ham 
 1 x 11-ounce can  mandarin oranges 
 ½ cup sliced  green onions 
 ½ cup  vinaigrette  dressing 
 spinach  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  ham ,  oranges  and  onions  in large mixing bowl. 
 Drizzle with dressing; toss gently. 
 Spoon onto individual plates lined with  spinach  leaves.
