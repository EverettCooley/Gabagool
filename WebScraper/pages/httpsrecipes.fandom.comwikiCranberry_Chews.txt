Description : 
 Chewy  cranberry  Oat Bars With an Oat?nut Topping.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2⅓ cups  all-purpose flour  (divided) 
 1 cup  brown sugar , packed 
 1 teaspoon  cinnamon 
 ½ teaspoon  nutmeg 
 ¼ teaspoon cloves 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 cup  butter , cold, cut into 16 pieces 
 1½ cups  rolled oats 
 16 ounces  cranberry sauce , whole berry 
 ½ cup orange  marmalade , thick 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 1 cup  walnuts , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 Grease a 10x15 jellyroll pan (9x13 Okay). 
 In a large bowl, combine 2 cups  flour ,  brown sugar ,  cinnamon ,  nutmeg , cloves and  salt . 
 Cut in  butter  until the mixture resembles coarse meal. 
 Stir in  oats , reserving 1½ cups of the mixture. 
 Firmly press remainder in bottom of prepared pan. 
 Bake 15 minutes. 
 Cool 10 minutes before filling. 
 In a medium bowl, stir together  cranberry sauce ,  marmalade  and  vanilla . 
 Slowly add remaining ⅓ cup  flour , blending thoroughly. 
 Spread evenly over baked and cooled crust. 
 Add nuts to reserved oat mixture, sprinkle over top of  cranberry  filling. 
 Bake 30 minutes or until lightly browned. 
 Cool in pan on rack. 
 Cut into 64 bars. 
 Store in Airtight Container One Week, Freeze ForLonger Storage.
