Ingredients : 
 2 fresh  California avocados  – large, ripe 
 1  tomato  – medium, chopped 
 1  onion  – small, diced 
 1  bell pepper  – diced 
 3  green chiles  – long, roasted, peeled and diced 
 2 tbsp  lemon juice 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Scoop out flesh of  avocado  and chop coarsely. 
 Add remaining ingredients and mix well.
