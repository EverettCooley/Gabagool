Derived from the term 'Creole' (A collective name for Non- native European and African settlers and their descendants settled in Latin America/ Caribbean). In Peru it refers to the richly traditional, coastal cooking, found across Peru. Characterised by rice, vegetables, hot chile peppers, spices, strong seasoning etc.,.
