Ingredients : 
 2 cups of pancake mix 
 1 cup sliced  strawberries 
 Directions : 
 Make the pancake batter according to the given instructions. Add in the sliced  strawberries , and make the pancakes. 
 Serve with  strawberry  or  chocolate  syrup.
