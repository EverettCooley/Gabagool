Pumpkin Pone
 
 Description : 
 An easy recipe for some tasty Pumpkin Pone, a French dish from French Guyana.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1Â lb.  pumpkin , 
 1 dry coconut, 
 1 cup  cornmeal , 
 1 cup  water , 
 1 tsp.  vanilla , 
 3 Tbsp.  margarine , 
 1 tsp.  cinnamon , 
 1/4 cup  Sugar , 
 1/2 tsp. grated  nutmeg . 
 Directions : 
 Peel, wash and grate  pumpkin  and the coconut. 
 Add the  cornmeal ,  Sugar  and  margarine . Add the  water  to give it a dropping consistency. 
 Then add the  cinnamon ,  nutmeg  and  vanilla . Mix well. Pour into a greased pan. 
 Bake at 350 degrees until brown. 
 Remove from oven 
 Wait till cooled 
 Serve & eat!!!!!
