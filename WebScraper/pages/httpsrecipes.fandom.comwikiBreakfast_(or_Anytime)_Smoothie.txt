Ingredients : 
 ⅓ - ½ cup vanilla low fat  yogurt 
 ¼ cup  orange juice 
 1  banana , chunked 
 about 8 frozen (or fresh)  strawberries , or equivalent of  raspberries , thawed 
 Directions : 
 Blend until smooth in electric blender or hand-held blender.
