 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 Unbaked pizza dough shell, 9-inch 
 1 Tbsp  olive oil 
 ¾ Cup  mozzarella   Cheese , shredded 
 1 Roma  tomato , cut in 1/8-inch slices 
 4 oz  California avocado , diced 
 ¼ Cup Crisply cooked  Bacon , chopped 
 1 Tbsp  red onion , diced 
 2 Tbsp  mozzarella   Cheese , shredded 
 2 Tbsp Blue  Cheese , crumbled 
 2 Tbsp Fresh  spinach  leaves, thinly julienned - for garnish 
 Directions : 
 METHOD
 Brush pizza dough shell with  olive oil . Top with ingredients in order listed, through blue  Cheese .
 Bake at 500 °F until crust is golden and  Cheese  at center is bubbly hot.
 Scatter  spinach  over top.
 Cut into 8 slices.
