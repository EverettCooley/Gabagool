Description : 
 Serves 8 as an appetizer or as many as 24 as a spread.
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 salt  herring  fillets 
 3 tbsp chopped  onion 
 Â½ cup chopped  apple 
 2 hard-boiled  eggs 
 3 tbsp  cider vinegar 
 2 slice white bread trimmed 
 1 tsp  sugar 
 2 tbsp  salad oil 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Soak the  herring  in  water  to cover overnight. 
 Change the  water  twice. 
 Drain. 
 Chop the  onion ,  apple ,  eggs  and  herring  together. 
 Pour the  vinegar  over the bread and add to the  herring  with the  sugar  and  oil . 
 Chop until very smooth. 
 Taste for seasoning, adding more  vinegar  if needed. 
 Chill.
