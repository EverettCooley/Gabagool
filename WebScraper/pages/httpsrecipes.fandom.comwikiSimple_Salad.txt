Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 head of  lettuce , chopped well 
 3 tsp.  olive oil 
 3 tsp.  vinegar 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Chop the  lettuce  fine. Add  olive oil  and  vinegar , toss well adding  salt  as needed.
