Description : 
 Sweet and sour pig's fore leg
 
 Ingredients : 
 1200 g  pig's fore leg 
 1  red pepper 
 Sauce : 
 500 ml  white vinegar 
 500 ml  water 
 600 ml  sugar 
 2 tbsp  salt 
 5 slices  ginger 
 Directions : 
 Clean pig's fore leg and chop into small bite pieces, shred red pepper. 
 Heat plenty of  water  with wok,add the pigs fore leg and the pepper. 
 Turn into low heat when  water  is boiled again, cook for 45 minutes. 
 Drain up the pig's fore leg, clean fat by clear  water . 
 Soak the cooked pig's fore leg by clear  water  for 1 hour. 
 Heat wok with 500 ml of  water , cook pig's fore leg for 30 minutes. 
 Drain up and clean, then soak by chilled cold and clear  water  for 30 minutes. 
 Pour cooked pig's fore leg pieces into a large container. 
 Heat wok and boil the sauce, wait until sauce cool. 
 Pour in the container which together with pig's fore leg. 
 Add in the shredded  red pepper , chilled by refrigerator for 12 hours. 
 Serve after 2 hours of taken out from the refrigerator.
