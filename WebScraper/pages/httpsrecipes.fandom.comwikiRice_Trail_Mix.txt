Description : 
 Makes 10 cups.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups bite-size crispy  rice  and  corn  squares 
 2 cups  puffed rice 
 2 cups bite-size crispy  rice  squares 
 1 cup  dried banana  chips 
 1 cup dry roasted  peanuts 
 1 cup  chocolate  candies 
 1 cup seedless  raisins 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice  and  corn  squares,  puffed rice ,  rice  squares,  banana  chips,  peanuts , candies and  raisins . 
 Store in tightly covered container.
