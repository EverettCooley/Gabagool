Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the O'Reilly Estate in Fort Worth, Texas in 1988. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup fresh  blackberries 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 2  eggs 
 1 tablespoon  sugar 
 2 cups  buttermilk 
 2 teaspoons  baking powder 
 2 cups  flour 
 Â¼ cup melted  butter 
 1 teaspoon  baking soda 
 Directions : 
 Beat eggs until light and fluffy then beat in buttermilk and soda. 
 Sift flour, salt, sugar and baking powder. 
 Add flour mixture to egg mixture beating well to make a thin batter. 
 Add berries and butter. 
 Fry on a hot buttered griddle until puffy and golden brown turning only once. 
 Serve hot.
