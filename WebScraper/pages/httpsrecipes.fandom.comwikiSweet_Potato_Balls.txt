Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredietns : 
 1⅓ cups cooked  sweet potatoes , mashed 
 8 large  marshmallows 
 ½ cup  cornflakes , finely crushed 
 ⅓ cup  brown sugar , packed 
 1 tbsp + 1 tsp  skim milk 
 1 tbsp + 1 tsp  unsalted butter 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 400°F. 
 Mold a spoonful of the sweet potatoes around each marshmallow and roll in cornflake crumbs. 
 Place in a shallow baking dish. 
 Combine brown sugar, milk and butter in a heavy saucepan. 
 Bring to a boil and pour over the balls. 
 Bake, uncovered, 15 minutes.
