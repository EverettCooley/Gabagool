Description : 
 This recipe taken from www.Portuguese-recipes.com
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups of  wine vinegar 
 4 cups of  water 
 4  garlic  cloves, minced 
 2  chile peppers , crushed 
 1 tbsp of  salt 
 1 tsp Portuguese  allspice 
 Directions : 
 Stir all ingredients together to blend. 
 Use for  pork chops  or  pork  meat. 
 Place  meat  in marinade and refrigerate over night longer is better, stir once and awhile.
