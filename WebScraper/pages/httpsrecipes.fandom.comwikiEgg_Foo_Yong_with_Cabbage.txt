Description : 
 Makes 5 servings (3 pancakes per serving)
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups shredded  cabbage 
 ½ cup sliced  green onions 
 1 teaspoon  celery seed 
 2 cups cooked  brown rice 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 2 tablespoons  soy sauce 
 8  eggs , lightly beaten 
 Chinese sauce : 
 1½ cups  chicken broth 
 1 tablespoon  cornstarch 
 1 tablespoon  soy sauce 
 ground  black pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Pancakes : 
 Combine  cabbage ,  onions ,  celery seed  and  rice . 
 Add  pepper  and soy sauce to beaten  eggs . 
 Stir eggs into  cabbage  mixture. 
 For each pancake pour ⅓ cup mixture on lightly greased griddle. 
 Cook until lightly browned on each side. 
 Serve with Chinese sauce. 
 Chinese sauce : 
 Mix together  chicken broth  and  cornstarch . 
 Cook until thickened. 
 Stir in soy sauce and  pepper .
