Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 3½ cups 
 Source: The Lady and Sons 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x 12 oz bag frozen  cranberries 
 ½ cup + 2 tbsp prepared  horseradish 
 3 tbsps  dry mustard 
 1 tbsp coarsely ground  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in a large bowl and stir well. 
 Pour the sauce into an airtight container and store in the refrigerator. 
 Serve with crackers and cream cheese, as an appetizer, or along with chicken, pork or beef.
