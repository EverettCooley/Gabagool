 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 ¼ Cups  water , divided 
 ¾ Cup (6 oz.)  orange juice  concentrate, undiluted 
 2 envelopes  unflavored gelatin 
 1/3 Cups mashed ripe  California avocado 
 Directions : 
 In a one-cup glass measure, combine 1/4 cup  water  and  orange juice  concentrate. 
 Sprinkle  gelatin  over surface, allow to stand 1 to 2 minutes to soften. 
 Pour juice mixture into blender, add  avocado ; whirl until smooth. 
 Meanwhile, heat 1 cup  water  to boiling. 
 Pour into blender, whirl until smooth. 
 Pour into 9 x 9-inch pan. 
 Chill until set. Cut into 1-inch blocks. 
 NOTE:  apple juice  concentrate may be substituted for  orange juice  concentrate.
