 Ingredients : 
 1 bag fresh  cranberries 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 cup  orange juice  or  water 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients in a medium sauce pan. 
 Bring to boil. 
 Simmer until  berries  pop. 
 Chill until ready to serve.
