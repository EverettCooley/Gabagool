 About Conchigliette : 
 A pasta variety that is shaped like a tiny conch shell. It is most suitable for soups and pasta salads. Pasta types that may be substituted include  ditalini ,  tripolini , and  tubettini . 
 Conchigliette are the smallest version of  Conchiglie  (shell) pasta. They are typically used in soups.
