chocolate   cherry  Swirls Cookies
 
Cookie Dough
 
 1/2 Cup  butter 
 1/4 Cup Each  sugar  And  light brown sugar 
 1 Large  egg 
 1/2 Tsp Plus 1/4 Tsp  vanilla 
 1/2 Tsp Plus 1/8 Tsp  baking powder 
 1/4 Tsp  salt 
 1 1/8 Cups  flour 
 
Beat All Ingredients Except  flour  in a Bowl Until Fluffy. Reduce Speed, Gradually Add  flour  And Beat Until Blended.
 
 2 Tbs  unsweetened cocoa powder 
 1/4 Cup  dried cherries , Finely Chopped 
 1/2 Tsp  vanilla 
 
Heat Oven to 350 Degrees.* Divide Dough in Half And Place Each Half in a Bowl. Add  cocoa  to 1 Bowl,  cherries  And  vanilla  to The Other. Stir Each Until Added Ingredients Are Blended. Place Each Half Dough Between 2 Sheets of Wax Paper, Roll Each Into a 9x6 Inch Rectangle. Remove Top Sheet of Paper. Invert  cherry  Layer on Top of  chocolate  Layer. Freeze 10 minutes. or Until Slightly Firm. Remove Paper From Top And Roll up Tightly From a Long Side, Pressing Together Any Cracks as You Go. Wrap in Wax Paper And Freeze 1 1/2 Hours or Until Very Firm.
 
Cut Log in 1/4 Inch Thick Slices. Place on Ungreased Baking Sheets. Bake 12 minutes. or Until Bottoms Are Light Golden. Cool on Baking Sheets. Makes 36 Cookies.*
