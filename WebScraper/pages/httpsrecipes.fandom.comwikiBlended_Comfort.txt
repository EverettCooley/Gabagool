Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  Southern Comfort ®  peach  liqueur 
 2 oz  Bourbon whiskey 
 ½ oz  dry vermouth 
 1 oz  orange juice 
 2 tbsp  lemon juice 
 ¼  peach 
 Directions : 
 Skin a  peach  quarter and add to a blender. 
 Add  Southern Comfort ,  Bourbon ,  vermouth  and juices. 
 Blend on low for 10 – 15 seconds. 
 Strain into a collins glass filled with crushed  ice . 
 Garnish with a slice of both  peach  and  orange , and serve.
