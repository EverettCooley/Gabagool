 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 1/2 cups  milk 
 2 cups cooked  rice 
 1/2 cup  Sugar 
 1/4 cup  semi-sweet chocolate  chips 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 Directions : 
 Combine  milk ,  rice ,  Sugar  and  chocolate chips  in 2- to 3-quart saucepan. 
 Place saucepan on burner and turn to medium heat. 
 Cook, stirring often, until pudding is thick and creamy, about 20 minutes. 
 Turn burner off. Remove saucepan from heat and place on hot pad. 
 Stir in  vanilla . Spoon into serving dishes; cool 10 minutes. 
 Serve warm or cold.
