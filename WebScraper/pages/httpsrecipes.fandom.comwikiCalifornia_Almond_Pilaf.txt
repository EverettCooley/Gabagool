Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup uncooked  brown rice 
 ¼ cup seedless  raisins 
 1 tablespoon dehydrated chopped  onion 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 2 teaspoons  chicken bouillon  granules 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  white pepper 
 2 to 2¼ cups  water 
 ⅓ cup  slivered almonds   or  whole  almonds , toasted 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  raisins ,  onion ,  butter , bouillon granules and  pepper  with  water  in 2- to 3-quart saucepan. 
 Bring to a boil; stir once or twice. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer 45 to 50 minutes, or until  rice  is tender and liquid is absorbed. 
 Remove from heat. Add  almonds  and fluff with fork.
