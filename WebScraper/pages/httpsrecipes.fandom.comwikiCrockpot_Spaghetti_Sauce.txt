 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  ground beef 
 1  onion 
 1  green pepper 
 2 cans  mushrooms 
 2 cans  tomato paste 
 2 cans  tomato sauce 
 2 Tbsp  brown sugar 
 1  garlic  clove 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  garlic powder 
 3 Tbsp  oregano 
 1 Tbsp  Italian seasoning 
 2 Tbsp  sugar 
 1 Tbsp  basil 
 ⅛ tsp  Cajun seasoning 
 ¼ tsp red pepper 
 ½ tsp  thyme 
 2 Tbsp  flour 
 1 tsp  onion powder 
 2 cans diced  tomatoes 
 1 can  beef broth 
 1 x 28 oz can  tomato juice 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients and cook on low 8 – 10 hours
