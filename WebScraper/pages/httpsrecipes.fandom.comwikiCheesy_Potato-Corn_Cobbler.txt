 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 10  onions 
 ¾ head  garlic 
 potatoes , chopped 
 water 
 corn  kernels 
 Maggi,  basil ,  oregano ,  chili powder ,  pepper 
 6 pieces of vache 
 1 can  evaporated milk 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Sauté  onions ,  garlic ; add  potatoes . 
 Add  water  to cover,  corn  and spices. 
 Cook until  potatoes  can be mashed with a fork. 
 Then mash them roughly. 
 Turn heat low, add  Cheese  and  milk . 
 Let flavors blend together.
