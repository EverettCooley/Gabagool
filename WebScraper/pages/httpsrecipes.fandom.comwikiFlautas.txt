Description : 
 Contributed by  PressureCookerRecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Source: Oscar Gomez, Tucson, Arizona in  Southern Living , November 2000 
 Yield: 10 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 vegetable oil 
 30 corn  tortillas 
 3¾ cups  Mexican Meat Mix 
 Directions : 
 Pour vegetable oil to a depth of 2 inches in a large skillet; heat to 375°F. 
 Soften 30 corn tortillas, 1 at a time by dipping in hot oil for 2 seconds. 
 Drain on paper towels. 
 Place 1 tablespoon  Mexican Meat Mix  on bottom half of each tortilla, roll tightly. 
 Cook, seam side down, in hot oil 2 minutes on each side or until golden. 
 Drain on paper towel.
