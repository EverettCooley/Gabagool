Ingredients : 
 meat 
 1  yellow onion 
 1  carrot 
 1 stick  celery 
 1  Italian pepper 
 1 tsp  paprika 
 1 tsp  salt 
 5  potatoes 
 1 can (6 – 8 oz)  tomato sauce 
 2 tbsp  parsley 
 2 cups hot  water 
 ¼ cup  vegetable oil  (optionally) 
 Directions : 
 Cut the  meat  into 2" pieces. 
 Put in the saucepan with the  salt , cover and cook on the top of the range at medium heat, stirring occasionally. 
 At this point you can add the  oil . 
 Meanwhile, peel  onion  and  carrot  and chop them finely. 
 Add to  meat  and stir. 
 Slice celery and  green pepper , add and stir. 
 In 5 minutes, add  paprika , stir, add  water . 
 Cover and cook ½ hour. 
 Meanwhile peel potatoes and cut them into cubes. 
 Add to  meat , cook 15 minutes. 
 Add  tomato sauce , cook 5 minutes. 
 Add  parsley . 
 Switch off the oven, let stay on it for 10 minutes. 
 Serve.
