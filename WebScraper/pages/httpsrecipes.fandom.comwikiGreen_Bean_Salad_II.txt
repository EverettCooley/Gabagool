Ingredients : 
 1 lb fresh  green beans 
 ½ cup  water 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ⅓ cup  wine vinegar 
 ⅓ cup  oil 
 1  onion , thinly sliced 
 1 clove  garlic , split 
 ¼ teaspoon  oregano 
 Directions : 
 Wash  beans  and add  water  and  salt . 
 Cook until tender. (If  fresh beans  are unavailable, omit this first step. Instead, drain and rinse canned  beans .) 
 Drain, reserving  water . 
 To ½ cup cooking  water , add  vinegar ,  oil ,  onion ,  garlic , and  oregano . 
 Pour over  beans  and let stand for at least 6 hours.
