Info 
 Cook Time : 
 Serves : 
 
 Ingredients 
 2  bananas , ripe 
 2 cups  pineapple juice 
 2 Tbsp creamy  peanut butter 
 2 tsp plain  yogurt 
 1 ½ tsp  vanilla extract 
 6 ice cubes 
 nutmeg 
 Directions 
 1.Combine all ingredients, except nutmeg, in blender. Cover and run on high until smooth and well-blended. Sprinkle with nutmeg.
 
 Source 
 Fruit of the Month: Banana  by the US Centers for Disease Control & Prevention, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
