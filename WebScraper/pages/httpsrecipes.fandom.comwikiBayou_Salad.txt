Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound (2½ cups) peeled cooked  crawfish  tails 
 2 cups cooked  rice 
 1 cup sliced  celery 
 ½ cup quartered  cherry tomatoes 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 lemon juice  to taste 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 lettuce  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  crawfish ,  celery , and  cherry tomatoes  in large bowl. 
 Blend  mayonnaise ,  lemon juice ,  salt  and  pepper ; toss lightly with  rice  mixture. 
 Chill. Serve on  lettuce  leaves, if desired.
