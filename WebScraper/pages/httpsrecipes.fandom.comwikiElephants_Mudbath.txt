 Ingredients : 
 1 shot Amarula  cream 
 1 shot Stolichnaya  vodka 
 1 shot De Kuyper  crème de cacao  Brown 
 garnish: Sling 
 Directions : 
 Blend all ingredients together with crushed  ice  and serve in a hurricane glass.
