 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 1/4 lbs. minced  Beef 
 2  onions 
 2 tb.  tomato  paste 
 1/2 ts.  nutmeg  or  cinnamon 
 3-4 ripe tomatos 
 salt  &  pepper 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Finely chop the  onions  and add to the minced ( ground )  Beef . 
 Add  tomato  paste, spices and seasonings. 
 Mix well, then spread on an oven pan or baking dish, to form a layer 1.5 to 2 inches thick. 
 Cut into 4" square sections and release sides. Peel  tomatoes , slice and place on meet. 
 Bake in center of preheated 350F oven for 30 minutes or until done.
