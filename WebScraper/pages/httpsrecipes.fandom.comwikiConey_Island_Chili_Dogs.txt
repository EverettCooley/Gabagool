Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves 8 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  ground beef , 85% lean (You can substitute  ground turkey  or  ground chicken  for the  ground beef .) 
 1 bottle  chili sauce  (Heinz, 12 oz) 
 1 packet Lawry's  chili seasoning 
 ½ cup  water 
 1 tbsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 1 tbsp  yellow mustard 
 ½ tbsp  onion powder 
 8 beef  hot dogs 
 8  hot dog buns 
 shredded  cheddar  cheese (Kraft) 
 chopped  white onion 
 Directions : 
 In a large pot over medium heat, brown  ground beef . 
 Add  chili sauce , chili seasoning ,  water ,  Worcestershire sauce ,  mustard  and  onion  powder. 
 Bring to boil. 
 Lower heat and simmer for 30 minutes. 
 Meanwhile, cook hot dogs as package directs. 
 Place hot dogs in buns; top with  chili ,  cheddar  cheese and chopped  onion .
