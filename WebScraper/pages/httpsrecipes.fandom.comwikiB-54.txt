Description 
 The  B-54  is a layered shot and part of the B-50 series.
 
 B-54  from the Wikibooks Bartending Guide—source of original recipe, licensed under the GNU Free Documentation License 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 1 part  Kahlúa 
 1 part  Irish Cream 
 1 part  Grand Marnier 
 1 part Green  Crème de Menthe 
 ice 
 Directions 
 Shake with ice. 
 Strain into shot glass.
