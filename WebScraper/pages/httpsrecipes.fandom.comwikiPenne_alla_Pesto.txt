Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Original recipe 
 Prep Time: 15 Minutes 
 Servings: 4 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups fresh  basil , stems removed 
 2 cloves  garlic , chopped 
 2 tbsp  olive oil 
 ¼ cup + 2 tbsp  pine nuts , toasted 
 2 tbsp grated  Parmesan cheese 
 ¾ lb cooked  penne , hot 
 Directions : 
 Wash and dry basil. 
 Place basil in a blender or food processor with garlic, olive oil, pine nuts, cheese and pepper to taste; purée. 
 Transfer cooked penne to a serving bowl. 
 Add pesto and toss thoroughly to serve. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 Calories: 209 | Protein: 5g | Sodium: 66mg | Cholesterol: 2mg | Fat: 13g | Carbohydrates: 19g 
 Exchanges: 2½ fat | 2½ starch
