Ingredients : 
 1 part Absolut  vodka 
 1 part  Kahlúa 
 1 part  melon liqueur 
 1 part  crème de banane 
 1 part  Southern Comfort 
 1 splash Sour mix 
 1 splash Sprite 
 1 dash  Grenadine 
 Mixing instructions : 
 Add ingredients over  ice ; shake and enjoy. 
 As a side note, print or write this list down when you go out to the bar/pub.
