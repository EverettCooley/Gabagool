      Alaska Clam Chowder     
 Ingredients : 
 6 slices  bacon  minced 
 1 small  onion  minced 
 1 can minced  clams  - (4 to 6 oz) 
 1 large or 2 small  potatoes  cut small pieces 
 ½ tsp  salt  or to taste 
 1½ cup  water 
 ½ can  canned milk 
 1 qt  milk 
 crackers  for serving 
 Directions : 
 Sauté  bacon  until half crisp in frying pan. 
 Place in medium saucepan with heavy bottom if possible. 
 Add  onions ,  clams  in juice,  potatoes ,  salt  and  water . 
 Simmer for 15 minutes. 
 Add canned  milk  and the one quart  milk . 
 Bring just to a boil. 
 Hold at medium warm, and serve with crackers.
