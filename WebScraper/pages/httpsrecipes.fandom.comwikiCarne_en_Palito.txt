Ingredients : 
 sirloin steak 
 hottest  salsa  available 
 sticks 
 Directions : 
 Place sirloin in freezer for 1 hour (this makes it easier to slice). 
 Slice thin strips cross grain and weave onto sticks. 
 Marinate overnight in the salsa grill until done.
