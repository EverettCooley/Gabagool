Ingredients : 
 1  graham cracker pie shell 
 2 cups diced  mango 
 1 small box  apricot Jell-O 
 2 cups  whipped cream  (or  Cool Whip ) 
 Directions : 
 Mix Cool Whip with dry Jell-O. 
 Add  mango  and mix well. 
 Spread in pie shell. 
 Refrigerate 1 hour until set.
