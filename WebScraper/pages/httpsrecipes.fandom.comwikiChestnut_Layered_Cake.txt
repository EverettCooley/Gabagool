 Description : 
 In Romanian: Tort de castane
 
 Ingredients : 
 12  eggs 
 9 oz / 280 g  confectioner's sugar 
 3 heaping tablespoons fine  breadcrumbs 
 1 teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 9 oz / 280 g boiled and crushed  chestnuts  (puree) 
 butter 
 filling and icing 
 Directions : 
 Beat the  sugar  with 12  yolks , add the bread crumbs and cinnamon and mix gently. 
 Add the  chestnut  puree and 6 whipped  egg whites  at the end. 
 Pour the mixture in the buttered cake pan and bake at slow heat for 45 minutes. 
 Fill and glaze as desired.
