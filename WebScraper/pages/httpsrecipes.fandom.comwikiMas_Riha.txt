 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 500 grams  Tuna  cut into bite sized chunks 
 55 gram  Onion , sliced 
 30 gram Red  chilli  paste 
 10 gram  garlic  paste 
 8-10 each  curry leaves 
 2 pieces Raan’baa leaf 
 50 ml  coconut oil 
 180 ml  coconut cream 
 350 ml  coconut milk 
 500 ml  water 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil . Sauté sliced  Onion ,  garlic , Raan'baa leaf and  curry leaves  till golden color. Then add  chilli  paste and coriander powder- sauté further till brown. 
 Add the cubes and sauté lightly. Add  coconut milk  and enough  water  to braise the . 
 Finish with  coconut cream . Adjust seasoning and serve hot with steamed  rice .
