Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 3/11/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4  pork chops  ½" thick 
 1  egg  beaten 
 1 tbsp  salt 
 ½ tbsp  black pepper 
 ⅓ cup  Parmesan cheese 
 ⅓ cup dry  bread crumbs 
 2 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 350°F. 
 Dip  pork chops  in combined  egg  and seasonings and then in combined  cheese  and  bread crumbs . 
 Heat  oil  in skillet; brown each chop on both sides. 
 Bake at 350°F for 35 minutes, turning chops occasionally.
