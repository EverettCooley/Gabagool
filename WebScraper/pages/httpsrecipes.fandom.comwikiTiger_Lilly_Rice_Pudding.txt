 Description : 
 Always check the ingredients to make sure the product is vegan.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup  white rice 
 4 cups  soy milk 
 2 tsp  vanilla extract 
 1 tsp ground  cinnamon 
 Dash of  nutmeg 
 1/4 cup  Sugar 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 275 
 Mix all ingredients in a bowl and place in a 1 1/2 quart lightly oiled overproof dish. 
 Bake covered for 3 hours, or until the pudding sets.
