Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1 ounce  anisette  or  sambuca 
 1 ounce  blackberry brandy 
 Directions : 
 Pour  anisette  into a cordial glass. 
 Float  blackberry brandy  on top by pouring very slowly over a teaspoon or bar spoon turned bottom-side up.
