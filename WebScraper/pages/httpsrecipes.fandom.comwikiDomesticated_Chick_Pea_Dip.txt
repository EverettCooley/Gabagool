 Ingredients : 
 6  garlic  cloves 
 1 cup  olive oil 
 3 cups  chickpeas , cooked 
 ¼ teaspoon  black pepper 
 ½ cup  lemon juice 
 Suggested Dippers : 
 pita  bread 
 toast triangles 
 scallions 
 bland water  crackers 
 red cabbage 
 Directions : 
 Place the  garlic ,  pepper ,  olive oil ,  lemon juice , and  salt  in a food processor or blender and purée until the  garlic  is finely chopped (if preparing by hand, finely dice and mash the  garlic , then mix with the other ingredients). 
 Add the  chickpeas  and continue mixing to a smooth paste. 
 Garnish with the chopped  parsley . 
 May be served at room temperature or chilled. Makes About 4½ cups of dip.
