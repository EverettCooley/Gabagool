 Description : 
 Vietnamese  curry  soup has  curry  powder as a main ingredient or Madras  curry  powder. It can be served with  rice . 
 
       Curry Soup     
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 1  Onion 
 2 shallots 
 2 cloves  garlic 
 2 inch piece  fresh ginger  root 
 1 stalk  lemon grass 
 4 tablespoons  curry  powder 
 1 green  bell pepper 
 2  carrots 
 8  Mushrooms 
 1 pound fried  tofu 
 4 cups  vegetable broth 
 4 cups  water 
 2 tablespoons vegetarian sauce 
 2 teaspoons  red pepper flakes 
 1  bay leaf 
 2  kaffir lime leaves 
 8 small  potatoes 
 1 (14 ounce) can  coconut milk 
 Directions : 
 Prepare the  Onion  and shallots, heat the  oil , prepare  garlic ,  ginger ,  lemon grass ,  curry  powder and cook them for 5 minutes. Use  green pepper ,  carrots ,  Mushrooms  and  tofu . Boil them and add  potatoes .
