About Blue cheese : 
       Blue cheese     
 A collective name for cheese made with mould variations. Refers to blue-veined cheeses of many varieties with rich, tangy flavor and creamy to crumbly consistency.
 See also  The article on WikiCheese  on blue cheeses.
