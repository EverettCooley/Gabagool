Description : 
 Having just recently started the Atkin's Diet I Needed a  catsup  with very low carbs. The  tomato  sauce I used has 4 carbs per ¼ cup serving... so just make sure you use one with the same or less on the label.
 
 Posted by Gingerkitten d to Recipezaar.Com 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes For Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Time to Make: 35 minutes | Prep: 5 minutes 
 Makes: 2 cups approximately (25 servings) 
 Ingredients : 
 2 (8 oz) cans reduced-sodium  tomato sauce 
 ½ cup  cider vinegar 
 ½ cup Splenda  sugar substitute 
 1 tsp ground  black pepper 
 2 Tbsp  garlic powder 
 2 Tbsp  onion powder 
 1 tsp ground  cinnamon 
 1 pinch ground  cloves 
 1 pinch ground  allspice 
 1 pinch  salt 
 Directions : 
 Combine all the ingredients in a small saucepan. 
 Mix thoroughly and bring to a simmer. 
 Allow to simmer on low until the  Catsup  is desired consistency. 
 Taste and adjust seasonings if desired. 
 This should keep in the refrigerator for at least a week.
