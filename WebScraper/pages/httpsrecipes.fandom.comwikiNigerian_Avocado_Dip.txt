 Description : 
 Serve as a dip or a spread with assorted breads and 
crackers.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 package  silken tofu 
 2 large avocados, diced 
 1 tsp  lemon juice 
 1/4 tsp dried  dill 
 1/4 tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 In blender, puree  tofu  until smooth. 
 Add remaining ingredients, and blend well. 
 Mixture should be fairly smooth, with small bits of 
 artichoke . 
 
 Put into a small, greased loaf pan. 
 Bake at 350 F for about 10 â€“ 15 minutes, or until bubbly.
