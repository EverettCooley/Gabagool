 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 lb  margarine  225g 
 3/4 lb  sugar  338g 
 4  eggs 
 1 lb  flour  450g 
 2-3 tsps  baking powder  10-15ml 
 2 tsps Lemon Essence 
 1 small  Evaporated Milk  (diluted) 
 1-2 grated  coconut  (half green) 
 Directions : 
 cream   margarine  and  Sugar . 
 Add  eggs  gradually (Mix well). 
 Add essence (Mix well). 
 Add  flour  &  baking powder  alternately with  milk . 
 Add  grated coconut . 
 Bake at 350 F. in greased baking pan until brown on top.
