Ingredients : 
 3 cups sifted  all-purpose flour 
 2 cups  sugar 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 ⅛ tsp  cinnamon 
 1 tsp  salt 
 3  eggs 
 1 cup mashed  Hass avocado 
 ¾ cup  vegetable oil 
 3 tsp  vanilla 
 1½ cups chopped  walnuts 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 350°F. 
 Combine  sugar ,  eggs ,  vanilla , beat well, add  avocado  and  oil . Blend until smooth. 
 Gradually add sifted dry ingredients. Beat at medium speed until smooth (mixture may be quite thick, add ½ cup  water  to thin out enough to pour in pans, mixture will still be thick). 
 Add chopped  nuts  last, stir in. 
 Pour mixture into two greased loaf pans and bake at 350°F for 50 – 60 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean. 
 Remove from pan; cool thoroughly before slicing.
