Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Preparation time : 10 minutes 
 This recipe serves: 4 
 Ingredients : 
 2 ripe  Hass avocados 
 ½ bunch fresh  cilantro , leaves chopped 
 ¼ medium  red onion , diced 
 2  jalapeño peppers , stemmed, seeded and finely diced (optional) 
 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed  lime juice 
 salt  and  pepper  freshly ground, to taste 
 Directions : 
 Cut the avocados into halves and remove the seeds. 
 Peel the fruit and place in a mixing bowl. 
 Have kids mash avocados with a potato masher or fork until chunky (good luck stopping them from mashing into a thin paste!) they can add the remaining ingredients (after grown-ups have chopped the jalapeños) and mix with a fork.
