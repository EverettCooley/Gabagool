Freshly shredded Parmesan cheese melts more smoothly than pre-shredded options. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source:  Southern Living  2007 Sensational Starts 
 Prep: 18 minutes | Cook: 4 hr 
 Makes 6 cups 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ cups  mayonnaise 
 2 (12 oz) jars marinated, quartered  artichoke hearts , drained and chopped 
 2 (4½ oz) cans chopped  green chiles , drained 
 1 (7 oz) jar roasted  red bell peppers , drained and chopped 
 2 cups freshly shredded  Parmesan cheese 
 4  garlic  cloves, minced 
 toasted  baguette  slices or assorted  crackers 
 Directions : 
 Stir together first 6 ingredients. 
 Spoon into a 3-qt. slow cooker. 
 Cover and cook on  low  4 hours. 
 Stir well before serving.
