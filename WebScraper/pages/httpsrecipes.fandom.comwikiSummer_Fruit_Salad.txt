 Description : 
 Makes 4 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups cooked  rice , cooled to room temperature 
 1/2 cup quartered  strawberries 
 1/2 cup  grape  halves 
 2  kiwi fruit , sliced into quarters 
 1/2 cup  pineapple  tidbits 
 1/2 cup  banana  slices 
 1/4 cup  pineapple juice 
 2 tablespoons plain nonfat  yogurt 
 1 tablespoon  honey 
 lettuce  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  strawberries ,  grapes ,  kiwi fruit ,  pineapple  tidbits and  banana  in large bowl. 
 Blend  pineapple juice ,  yogurt  and  honey  in small bowl. 
 Pour over  rice  mixture; toss lightly. Serve on  lettuce  leaves.
