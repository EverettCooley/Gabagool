Ingredients : 
 4 oz  soft margarine  at room temp 
 1 tsp ground  cinnamon 
 ¼ cup  granulated sugar  substitute 
 Directions : 
 Combine ingredients and mix well return to container and refrigerate except when it is being used. 
 Nutritional information : 
 1 tsp/serving. Each serving 1 fat exchange, 34 calories 4 gm fat, 47 mg sodium
