Ingredients : 
 1  pita  pocket 
 ¼ cup  hummus 
 ⅓ cup  tabouli 
 2 tbsp  feta  cheese, crumbled 
 1  romaine lettuce , torn into pieces 
 ¼ tbsp  tomato , chopped 
 ¼ cup  alfalfa sprouts 
 1 tbsp  Greek olives , chopped 
 Lemon Dill Dressing 
 Directions : 
 Cut top ¼ from pita pocket and reserve for another use. 
 Open pocket and spread one side with hummus. 
 Spoon in tabouli. 
 Add remaining ingredients, drizzling  salad dressing  over all. 
 This serves one, but is easily multiplied for many.
