Ingredients : 
 10  eggs 
 1 cup  water 
 ½ cup  potato flour 
 Filling : 
 ¾ lbs ground, chopped  liver 
 1 small  onion , chopped, browned 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Beat  eggs , blend  potato flour  and  water  and add to  eggs , beat. 
 Heat frying pan and using very small amounts of fat pour enough batter into pan to cover bottom thinly, brown bottom side only and overturn onto towel. 
 Place small amount of liver on ½ leaf on browned side and roll up (like cigar size). 
 Fry on both size.
