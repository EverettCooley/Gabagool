 Ingredients : 
 10 cups diluted  coconut milk 
 3 medium pieces  taro , unskinned and diced 
 1 large piece  cassava , skin removed and diced 
 5 big pieces ripe  cooking bananas /vudi, peeled and diced 
 3 cups  brown sugar 
 2 Teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 Directions : 
 In a medium-sized pan, bring diluted  coconut milk  to boil. 
 Add  taro  and  cassava . Stir constantly to avoid sticking. 
 Cook until almost done. Add  cooking bananas /vudi. Cook until all rootcrops are done. 
 Slowly stir in  brown sugar  and stir until it is completely dissloved. 
 Cook a few more minutes and add  vanilla  for flavouring. 
 Remove from flame and serve.
