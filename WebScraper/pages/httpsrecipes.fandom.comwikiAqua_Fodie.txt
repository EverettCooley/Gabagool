Ingredients : 
 1 oz  gin 
 1 oz blue  curaçao  liqueur 
 orange juice 
 ice  cubes 
 1  orange  wedge 
 cherries 
 Directions : 
 Fill a glass with  ice , then add  gin  and curaçao. 
 Fill the glass with juice and garnish with a  cherry .
