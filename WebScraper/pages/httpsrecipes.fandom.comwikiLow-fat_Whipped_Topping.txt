Description : 
 Can be stored in refrigerator for use within several days or can be frozen for longer storage.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Makes 2 cups. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tsp unflavored  gelatin 
 ½ cup  skim milk 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 2 tsp  water 
 ½ tsp  vanilla 
 ¼ cup  instant non-fat dry milk 
 Directions : 
 Soften gelatin in water for 5 minutes. 
 Stir nonfat dry milk into skim milk in saucepan. Heat to simmering. 
 Add softened gelatin. Stir until gelatin is dissolved. 
 Add vanilla and sugar. 
 Chill until mixture begins to thicken. 
 Beat with electric mixer or rotary beater until very thick and light.
