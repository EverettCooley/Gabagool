 Spiced spareribs
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: Come Into the Kitchen 
 Serves 4 to 6 
 Ingredients : 
 3 pounds loin cut  spareribs 
 4 tbsp  chili powder 
 4 cloves  garlic , crushed 
 2 tbsp  oregano 
 3 tbsp  salt 
 4 tbsp  vinegar 
 2 tbsp  water 
 Directions : 
 Trim fat from meat. 
 Combine seasonings and  water  to make a paste. 
 Add a little more  water  if needed. 
 Rub mixture over meat, coating evenly. 
 Marinate in a closed container at least 24 hours, refrigerated. 
 Place ribs in a baking dish and bake 1 hour at 350Â°F or until tender. 
 Variation: barbecue ribs over medium coals, turning often, for 45 minutes to 1 hour.
