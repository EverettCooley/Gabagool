 About Thai basil : 
 Wikipedia Article  About thai Basil on Wikipedia 
 Thai Basil is a cultivar of basil and is a major ingredient in many Thai dishes. It is also known as Sweet Basil and its aroma is reminiscent of anise or liquorice when fresh, cooked of fried. It is easily confused with Holy Basil which has a completely different aroma.
