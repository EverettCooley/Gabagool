Ingredients : 
 1 cup fat free  milk 
 1 cup frozen  strawberries 
 1 packet no sugar added rich  milk chocolate  Nestlé  ®  Carnation  ®  instant breakfast 
 Directions : 
 Place  milk ,  strawberries  and Carnation Instant Breakfast in blender; cover. 
 Blend until smooth.
