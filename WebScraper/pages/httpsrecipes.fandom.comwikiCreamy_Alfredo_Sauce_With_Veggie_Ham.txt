Ingredients : 
 2 Cups  soy milk 
 1/2 Cup Tofutti  cream cheese 
 1/2 Cup Tofutti  sour cream 
 2 Tbsp Nayonaise 
 1 Tsp  onion salt 
 1 Tsp Dried  parsley 
 1-2 cloves  garlic  (Crushed) 
 5-6 Slices Veggie  Ham  (Cut into bite sized pieces) 
 salt  and  pepper  To Taste 
 Directions : 
 In a medium sauce pan add soy milk, cream cheese, sour cream and nayonnaise. 
 Heat on low, constantly stirring until cream cheese has melted and the mixture is smooth and creamy. 
 Add onion salt, parsley, veggie Ham, salt, and pepper. 
 Let simmer for about 10 mins, stirring occasionally. 
 Add the garlic, stir in well and pour over your favorite cooked pasta.
