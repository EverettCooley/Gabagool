 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup lowfat  Mayonnaise 
 1/2 cup plain lowfat  yogurt 
 1/4 tsp  oregano 
 1/4 tsp  salt 
 1/8 tsp  black pepper 
 1/50 tsp  garlic  clove 
 Directions : 
 In a small bowl, whisk  Mayonnaise  until smooth. Stir in  yogurt ,  oregano   salt ,  pepper  and  garlic . Cover and chill for 1 hour before serving. Serve with raw vegetables for dipping.
