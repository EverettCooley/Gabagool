Description : 
 A  rum  drink. Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz Appleton Special Jamaica  rum 
 ¼ oz  Tía Maria 
 ½ oz  crème de banane 
 4 oz  orange juice 
 Directions : 
 Pour ingredients into a cocktail shaker and shake well. 
 Pour into a highball glass filled with  ice . 
 Garnish with a  cherry  or  orange  wedge.
