      Sandwiches With Sardines     
 Ingredients : 
 white bread 
 300 g  butter 
 300 g tvorog ( cottage cheese ) 
 6 can  sardines 
 6  hard-boiled eggs 
 2 tsp  mustard 
 lettuce  leaves 
 pickles 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Slice bread finely. 
 Combine  butter  with tvorog,  mustard  and  salt . 
 Spread the mixture on bread slices, then put a  lettuce  leaf, sardines,  egg  slice and pickle dice.
