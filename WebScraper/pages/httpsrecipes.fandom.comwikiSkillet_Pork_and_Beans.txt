Ingredients : 
 pork chops 
 canned white beans, rinsed 
 canned diced tomatoes 
 green chilis 
 Directions : 
 Fry pork chops, remove to a plate. 
 Add rinsed white beans and canned diced tomatoes with green chilis to pan. 
 Cover and simmer 5 min. 
 Add back chops.
