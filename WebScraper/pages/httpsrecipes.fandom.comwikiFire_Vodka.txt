 Ingredients : 
 1 bottle polish  vodka  (750 ml) 
 2 tbsp whole  pink peppercorns , 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Mix Â½ cup  vodka , peppercorns and  sugar  in small pan. Bring to boil. 
 Stir until  sugar  dissolves. 
 Transfer mixture to clean bottle. Add remaining  vodka . Seal. 
 Shake to blend. 
 Let stand 4 days to mellow. 
 Strain. Return  vodka  to bottle. 
 Freeze overnight. 
 Serve in chilled glasses.
