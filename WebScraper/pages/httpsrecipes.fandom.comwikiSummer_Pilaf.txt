 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  butter  or  margarine 
 ½ cup thinly sliced  green onions 
 ⅓ cup  pine nuts 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 3 cups cooked  rice  (cooked in  chicken broth ) 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 ¼ teaspoon dried  thyme 
 Directions : 
 Heat  butter  in large skillet over medium-high heat until hot. Add  green onions ,  pine nuts  and  garlic ; cook and stir 3 to 5 minutes or until  nuts  are lightly browned. 
 Add  rice ,  salt ,  pepper  and  thyme ; cook and stir 3 to 5 minutes or until well blended. Serve immediately.
