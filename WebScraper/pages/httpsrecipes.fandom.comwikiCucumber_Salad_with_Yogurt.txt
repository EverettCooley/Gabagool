Ingredients : 
 1 medium  cucumber 
 1 clove  garlic 
 1 tbsp.  salt 
 1 tbsp.  vinegar 
 1 cup  yogurt 
 1 tbsp.  vegetable oil 
 1 tbsp. chopped  dill  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Crush the  garlic ; add  salt  and  vinegar  and mix to a smooth paste. 
 Stir this into  yogurt ; add the  oil  and half of  dill . 
 Peel and slice cucmber, very thin across. 
 Put into a salad bowl and sprinkle with a little  salt . 
 Cover with the  yogurt  sauce and sprinkle the rest of the  dill  on top. 
 Chill before serving.
