Description : 
 I obtained this recipe from an estate sale when I purchased the family collection from the Monsey Estate in Crandall, Texas in 1991.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  sake 
 3 tablespoons  rice wine 
 3 tablespoons  soy sauce 
 2 tablespoons  granulated sugar 
 2 teaspoons grated fresh  ginger 
 Directions : 
 Place all ingredients into a saucepan and bring to a boil. 
 Lower heat and simmer into a light syrupy consistency.
