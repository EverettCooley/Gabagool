Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 4 to 6 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds boneless  pork , cut into cubes 
 ½ cup  flour 
 ½ tsp  garlic salt 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 ¼ cup  butter 
 3 Tbsp  vegetable oil 
 1 envelope onion soup mix 
 1 can  chicken broth 
 1 cup water 
 Hot mashed  potatoes 
 Directions : 
 Thread pork on small wooden skewers. 
 Combine flour, garlic salt and pepper on a plate. 
 Roll kabobs in flour mixture until coated. 
 In a large skillet, heat butter and oil over medium heat. Brown kabobs, turning frequently; drain. 
 Sprinkle with soup mix. 
 Add broth and water. Reduce heat. Cover and simmer for 1 hour. 
 Remove kabobs and keep warm. If desired, thicken pan juice and serve over mashed potatoes with the kabobs.
