Description : 
 A Harvey Wallbanger made with Orange Vodka instead of Orange juice. Alternatively,one may simply add ¼ Galliano to ¾ Van Gogh, either on ice or 'Guinee' cubes (frozen cubes of a hot pepper liquor). This is called a 'Harvey Wallbanger goes to Mexico.'
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 ounces Rain  vodka ® 
 4 ounces Van Gogh Oranje  vodka ® 
 ¼ cup  Galliano ® 
 Directions : 
 Fill highball glass with ice or 'Guinee cubes'. 
 Add Van Gogh, then rain. 
 Float Galliano on top.
