Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: My Old Recipes 
 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  butter , melted 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 1  broiler fryer , cut up 
 1 cup crushed  corn flakes   or  ½ cup fine dry  breadcrumbs 
 Directions : 
 Mix butter, salt and pepper. 
 Brush chicken with butter mixture, then roll in cornflakes or bread crumbs (you may even use 1 cup crushed potato chips). 
 Put chicken skin side up without touching in ungreased large shallow baking pan. 
 Bake at 375 °F until tender, about 1 hour. 
 Don't turn. 
 Season to taste with salt.
