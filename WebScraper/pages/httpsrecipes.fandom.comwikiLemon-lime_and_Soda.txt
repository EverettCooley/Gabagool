Description : 
 Simply an alcoholic lemon-lime soda.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 ounces  San Pellegrino ® 
 3 ounces Van Gogh Citroen  Vodka ® 
 Directions : 
 Pre-chill 6 ounce martini glass. 
 Fill standard shaker with ice, add ingredients,and shake well. 
 Strain and serve.
