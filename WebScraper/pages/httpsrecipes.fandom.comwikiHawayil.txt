Description : 
 Yemeni spice mix
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 teaspoons  black peppercorns 
 1½ teaspoons of  caraway seeds 
 ½ teaspoon of  saffron  threads 
 ½ teaspoon of  cardamom  seeds 
 1 teaspoon of  turmeric 
 Directions : 
 Using a mortar or food processor, grind all ingredients except for the  turmeric . 
 Stir in the  turmeric  and store in an airtight jar until needed.
