Yield: 4 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb dried lima beans 
 1 t salt 
 1 large onion, chopped 
 Bacon 
 1/2 C corn syrup 
 1 large bottle catsup 
 1/8 t pepper 
 Directions : 
 Place beans in large kettle; add water to cover. Let stand overnight. 
 Add salt and onion. Bring to a boil; cover kettle. 
 Reduce heat; simmer until beans are tender, adding boiling water as needed. 
 Chop 3 slices bacon; 
 Add chopped bacon, corn syrup, catsup and pepper to beans. 
 Pour into large baking dish; arrange several slices bacon over top. 
 Bake at 250 F for 2 hours and 30 minutes to 3 hours.
