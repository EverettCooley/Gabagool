 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 	large 	 acorn squash , split length- 
 Wise and seeded 
 1 	cup 	Chopped  Pear 
 1/2 	cup 	Raw  cranberries  (fresh or 
 Frozen) 
 3 	tbsp 	Undiluted  orange juice 
 Concentrate 
 3 	tbsp 	 Honey  or  maple syrup 
 1 1/2 	tsp 	Ground  cinnamon 
 1/2 	tsp 	Ground  allspice 
 Directions : 
 1 	Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
 2 	Place  Squash  cut side up on foil-lined baking sheet.
 3 	In a large bowl, combine  Pear , cranerries,  orange juice  concentrate,  Honey  or syrup,  cinnamon  and  allspice .
 4 	Spoon mixture into  Squash  cavities.
 5 	Use any extra liquid to brush cut edges of  Squash .
 6 	Bake 45 minutes to 1 hour, or until  Squash  is soft.
 7 	Serve hot.
