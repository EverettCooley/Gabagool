 Description : 
 Made with real coffee, Irish whiskey, and heavy cream, this fresh, soothing beverage is great for breakfast or dessert and delivers a brown, foamy aroma that it regularly relishes and enhances with brown sugar and alcohol content.
 
         
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz.  Irish whiskey 
 1 tbsp.  brown sugar 
 2 oz.  heavy cream 
 ½ cup  coffee , black only 
 3 pumps  crème de menthe 
 Directions : 
 Fill the large glass with hot  water  to preheat, then empty. 
 Pour piping hot  coffee  into the warmed glass until it is about ½ full. 
 Add 1 tablespoon  brown sugar  and stir until completely dissolved. Just blend in  Irish whiskey . 
 Gently top with a collar of slightly whipped  heavy cream  by pouring gently over a spoon. 
 Color with crème de menthe and enjoy while piping hot. Serve warm and plain with other desserts, appetizers, or side dishes. 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Servings:  2 cups per glass or mug
