Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Azato Estate in Dallas, Texas in 1987.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 3 pound  chicken  washed and dried 
 2 tablespoons  oil 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 1 box Stove Top dressing mix 
 1  apple 
 1 tablespoon  cinnamon 
 ½ cup  apple jelly 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Salt and pepper inside of chicken and oil the outside. 
 Cut core and dice apple. 
 Mix dressing according to directions on the box. 
 When dressing is finished add apple. 
 Stuff chicken then cover with foil and bake at 350 °F for 1 hour. 
 In saucepan mix lemon juice, cinnamon and jelly then simmer 3 minutes stirring often. 
 When chicken is done uncover and pour glaze over chicken. 
 Bake uncovered for 30 minutes basting frequently.
