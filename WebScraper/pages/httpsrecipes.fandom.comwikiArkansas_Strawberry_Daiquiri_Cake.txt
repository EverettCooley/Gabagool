Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Hoyt Estate in Allen, Texas in 1989.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 10 ounces frozen strawberry daiquiri thawed 
 1 strawberry  cake mix 
 1½ ounces Dream Whip  whipped topping  mix dry 
 1 tablespoon  lime peel  grated 
 ½ cup  egg whites 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 8 ounces frozen  whipped topping  thawed 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 Prepare rectangular cake pan with cooking spray then set aside. 
 In mixing bowl combine cake mix, dry whipping topping and lime peel. 
 Add daiquiri mix, egg whites and oil then mix until moistened. 
 Pour batter into prepared pan and bake 40 minutes. 
 Spread whipped topping evenly over top of cooled cake.
