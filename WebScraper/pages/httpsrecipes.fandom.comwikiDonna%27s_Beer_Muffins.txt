Description : 
 Makes 9 large muffins
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups  flour 
 5 tsp  baking powder 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 3 tbsp  sugar 
 1 bottle of  beer 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F, prepare pans. 
 Measure dry ingredients into bowl and pour  beer  over, stirring to blend. 
 Spoon into pans and brush tops with  butter . 
 Bake for 15 – 20 minutes. 
 Serve hot, a little grated  cheddar  cheese may be sprinkled on top before baking if desired.
