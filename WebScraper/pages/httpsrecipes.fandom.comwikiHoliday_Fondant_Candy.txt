Desscription : 
 These candies are easy to make when you start with Uncooked Fondant made with nonfat dry milk.
 
 
 Fondant : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/4 cup.  butter 
 2 1/2 tbsp.  water 
 1 tsp.  vanilla 
 3 cups. sifted  powdered sugar 
 2/3 cup. nonfat  dry milk 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Melt butter in 2-quart saucepan over low heat. 
 Remove from heat and mix in water and vanilla. 
 Then mix in a mixture of the sugar and milk. 
 Mix until smooth and creamy after each addition. 
 
 
 Chocolate Covered Cherries : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup fondant 
 24  maraschino cherries 
 1 package  semi-sweet chocolate  pieces 
 Directions : 
 Drain 24 maraschino cherries. 
 Cover each with 2 teaspoons fondant. 
 In a double boiler, partly melt 1 package semi-sweet chocolate pieces. 
 Remove from heat and stir until entirely melted. 
 Drop covered cherries, one at a time, into chocolate. 
 Remove with 2 forks and put on waxed paper to cool. 
 
 
 Nut Bars : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup fondant 
 18 nuts 
 Directions : 
 Shape fondant into 18 bars, each 1 inch long. 
 Press a nut on top of each. 
 Chill. 
 
 
 Stuffed Dates : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup fondant 
 24 large  dates 
 Directions : 
 Split and remove pits from 24 large dates. 
 Fill centers with 1/2 cup fondant, using 1 teaspoon for each. 
 Chill. Enjoy. 
 Notes : 
 Recipe makes about 1 1/2 cups fondant.
