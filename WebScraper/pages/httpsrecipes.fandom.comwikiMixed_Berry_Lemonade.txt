Ingredients : 
 4 cups frozen berries I used blueberry, blackberry, raspberry blend 
 1/2 cup water 
 1 1/2 cup sugar 
 8-10 cups water 
 7 lemons, juiced 
 Instructions : 
 Allow berries to thaw. Blend with 1/2 cup of water and pour through strainer to remove seeds and pulp. There may be a few seeds but the majority will remain in the strainer. 
 Pour berry juice in a large pitcher or container and add remaining ingredients. Stir until sugar is dissolved. Store covered in refrigerator for 2-3 days.
