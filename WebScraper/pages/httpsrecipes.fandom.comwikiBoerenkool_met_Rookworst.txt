 Description : 
 Curly kale with smoked sausages. A nice plain meal for a rainy day.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  kale 
 2 lbs  potatoes 
 2 ounces crushed  oats  or  porridge oats 
 6 large smoked  sausage 
 6 ounces smoked  ham 
 salt 
 pepper 
 nutmeg 
 Directions : 
 Clean the  kale , strip off the stalk and vein together. 
 Chop roughly. 
 Peel and cube the  potatoes . 
 Cook the  kale  and smoked  Ham  together in a little boiling  water , salted if necessary, for 10 minutes. 
 Add the cubed  potatoes  and the  Oats . 
 Prick the sausages, and place them on top of the mixture. 
 Cover the pan with a lid, and boil the mixture gently until the  kale  is tender and the  potatoes  are soft; this takes about 30 minutes. 
 Remove the sausages and smoked  Ham , and mash the  potatoes  and  kale  together. 
 Season as required, with  salt ,  pepper , and  nutmeg . 
 Replace the sausages and smoked  Ham , and serve with plenty of  mustard .
