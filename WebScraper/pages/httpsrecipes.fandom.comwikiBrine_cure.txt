A step in the curing process prior to curing that involves the soaking or injecting of food with a solution that is used to pickle or preserve foods. The solution typically includes salt and water in a simple brine or salt, water, sodium nitrite, other nitrites, and flavorings, such as honey or sugar. This process is also known as "wet curing."
