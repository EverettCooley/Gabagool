rum   butter  Stars Cookies
 
 2 1/2 Cups  flour 
 1/2 Tsp  baking powder 
 1/8 Tsp  salt 
 1 Cup  unsalted butter 
 3/4 Cup  sugar 
 2  eggs 
 1 Tsp  rum  or  almond extract 
 1/2 Tsp  vanilla extract 
 24 Red And Green  glace cherries , Halved 
 
Heat Oven to 350 Degrees. in a Bowl, Whisk  flour ,  baking powder  And  salt . Set Aside. in 2nd Bowl, Beat  butter  And  sugar  Until Creamy. Beat in  eggs  And  extracts . Beat in Dry Ingredients Until Combined. Place Dough in a Pastry Bag Fitted With a Large Star Tip. Pipe Stars Onto Ungreased Baking Sheets. Place a  glace cherry  Half on The Top of Each. Refrigerate Baking Sheets For 30 minutes. Bake 12 minutes. or Until Edges Just Start to Brown. Remove Cookies From Baking Sheets. Cool. Repeat With Remaining Dough. Makes 4 Dozen Cookies.*
