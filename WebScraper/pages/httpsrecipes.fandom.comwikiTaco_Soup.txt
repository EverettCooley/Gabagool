 Directions : 
 Brown ground beef and drain. 
 Open cans. 
 Pour into big pot. 
 Sprinkle in ranch style dressing mix and taco seasoning. 
 Add meat. 
 Heat for 30 minutes. 
 Serve with chips or cornbread.
