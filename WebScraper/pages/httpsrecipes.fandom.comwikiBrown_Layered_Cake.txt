 Description : 
 In Romanian: Tort brun
 
 Ingredients : 
 7 oz / 200 g ground  almonds 
 4 oz / 130 g  confectioner's sugar 
 7 oz / 200 g grated  chocolate 
 5 level tablespoons fine  flour 
 12  eggs 
 butter 
 butter cream 
 whipped cream 
 Directions : 
 Beat the yolks with the  sugar  until foamy, add the  chocolate ,  almonds  and  flour , mix well, then add the 12 whipped  egg whites  at the end. 
 Fold in gently. 
 Pour the mixture into the buttered baking pan and bake at medium heat. 
 Fill with  butter cream  and cover with  whipped cream .
