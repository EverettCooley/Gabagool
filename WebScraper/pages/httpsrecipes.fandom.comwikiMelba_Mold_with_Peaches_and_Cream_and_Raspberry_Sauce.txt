Melba Mold With  peaches  And Cream And  raspberry  Sauce
 
 1 Pkg.  peach  Flavored  gelatin  (6 Oz) 
 1 1/2 Cups Boiling  water 
 1/2 Cup  orange juice 
 1 Pint  vanilla   Ice cream , Softened 
 1 Pkg. Frozen  peaches  in Syrup Undrained (10 Oz) 
 2  bananas , Sliced 
 
Dissolve  gelatin  in Boiling  water . Stir in The  orange juice  And  vanilla   Ice cream  Until Blended. Stir in The  peaches  And  bananas . Pour Mixture Into a 2 Quart Mold And Refrigerate Until Firm. Unmold Onto a Lovely Platter. Serve With a Spoonful of  raspberry  Sauce. Serves 8 to 10Raspberry Sauce:
 Stir Together 1 Pkg.  raspberries  (10 Oz) in Syrup With 1/4 Can (1 1/2 Oz) Undiluted Concentrated Frozen  orange juice .Source: With Love From Darling's Kitchen
