 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup brown  flour . 
 3 cups  dates  (seedless) 
 1 cup  milk . 
 1/2 pack  butter  (113g) 
 1 cup  walnuts 
 1 teaspoon  cardamom  powder. 
 Directions : 
 Stir  flour  over low heat until it becomes yellowish, then strain. 
 Add  flour  to the  dates  and  flour . 
 Stir over low heat until well mixed. 
 Pour a little  milk  and mix continously. 
 Cook over low heat until smooth mixture. 
 Add  cardamom  and  walnuts , wait few minuets until it cools down, and serve it with Arabic  coffee .
