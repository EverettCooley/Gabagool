Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Source:  1,001 Delicious Recipes for People with Diabetes 
 Yield: 4 servings (about 1 cup each)         
 Ingredients : 
 vegetable  cooking spray 
 ½ cup chopped  onion , red, white, or yellow only 
 ½ cup chopped  green bell pepper 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 2 cups (8 oz.) shredded or cubed, cooked, lean  beef 
 2 cups peeled and cubed cooked  potatoes 
 ½ tsp. dried  marjoram  leaves 
 ¼ tsp. dried  thyme  leaves 
 salt  and  pepper , to taste 
 hot sauce, optional 
 Directions : 
 Coat large skillet with cooking spray; heat over medium heat until hot. 
 Sauté onion, bell pepper, and garlic until tender. 
 Add beef and potatoes to skillet; sprinkle with herbs. 
 Cook over medium heat until meat and potatoes are browned, about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
 Season to taste with salt and pepper. Serve hot and plain or with other side dishes or main dishes, then drizzle with the hot sauce, if desired. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving (about 1 cup each):
 
 244 Calories | 6g Fat | 50.7g Cholesterol | 44mg Sodium | 19.9g Protein | 27.5g Carbs 
 Diabetic Exchanges: 1 vegetable | 1½ bread | 2 meat
