Description : 
 Cabbage and carrot salad
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 cabbage  (as much as desired) 
 carrots  (as much as desired) 
 lemon juice  or  vinegar 
 olive oil 
 Directions : 
 Wash the  cabbage  and slice it as thin as you can. 
 Wash and grate the  carrots . 
 Mix  carrots  and  cabbage  in a bowl, add  olive oil ,  salt  and finally pour  vinegar  or  lemon juice  (try both versions).
