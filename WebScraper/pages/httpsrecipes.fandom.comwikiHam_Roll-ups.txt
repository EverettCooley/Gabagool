Description : 
 Sometimes I make these just so the kids have something to snack on. I usually use the reduced fat  cream cheese  and reduced fat  mayo . 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 package refrigerated flour  tortillas 
 2 (8 ounces) packages  cream cheese , softened 
 ⅓ cup  mayonnaise  (the real deal!) 
 2 tablespoons chopped  green onions 
 ¼ cup chopped  black olives 
 2 (2.5 ounces) packages sliced cooked  ham  (chop unto large dices) 
 Directions : 
 Let tortillas stand at room temperature while preparing filling. 
 Combine  cream cheese  and  mayo  until creamy. 
 Add olives,  ham  and  onions . 
 Evenly spread a thin layer on top of each tortilla. 
 Tightly roll up each tortilla. 
 Wrap individually in foil or plastic wrap refrigerate for 3 hours or overnight. 
 To serve cut into ¾" diagonal slices.
