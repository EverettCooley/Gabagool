Ingredients : 
 4 oz  flour 
 2 oz  margarine 
 2 oz  ground almonds 
 2 oz  demerara sugar 
 1 lbs  mincemeat 
 1 med tin sliced  peaches 
 glace cherries 
 Directions : 
 Set oven to 400°F or mark to make the crumble mixture, sieve the  flour  into a bowl and rub in the  margarine  until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs. 
 Stir in the  ground almonds  and the  sugar . 
 Place the  mincemeat  in an ovenproof dish and cover with the drained sliced  peaches . 
 Reserve 8  peach  slices for decoration. 
 Top the dish with the crumble mixture and bake for 25–30 minutes until the crumble is golden brown. 
 Decorate with the reserved  peach  slices and the  glace cherries . 
 Serve hot with custard or  cream .
