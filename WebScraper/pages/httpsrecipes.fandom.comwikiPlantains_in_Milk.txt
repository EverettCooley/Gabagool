Ingredients : 
 3 ripe  plantains 
 4 tbsp of  butter 
 ¼ lb  cheddar  cheese, cut in small squares 
 ¼ cup of  sugar 
 1  cinnamon  stick 
 ¼ lb of  chocolate 
 Directions : 
 Peel the  plantains ; cut them in half lengthwise without separating the two halves. 
 In a large pot, melt the  butter  and brown the  plantains . 
 Fill the  plantains  with the  cheese  squares and put them back in the pot. 
 Add ¼ lb of chocolate to the cheese. 
 Sprinkle them with the  sugar , and pour the  milk  over it. 
 Add cinnamon. 
 Place cover on pot and cook for 10 minutes on low. 
 Uncover pot and continue cooking for 15 minutes. 
 Serve hot.
