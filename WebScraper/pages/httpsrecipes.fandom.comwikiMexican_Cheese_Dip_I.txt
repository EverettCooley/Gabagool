 Ingredients : 
 10 oz  pepper jack cheese ; grated 
 1 medium  onion 
 butter  to cook the  onion 
 1 tsp  garlic ; crushed 
 tablespoons of  flour 
 1 can  tomato paste 
 1 cup  whipping cream 
 Directions : 
 Cook the  onions  in what is preferably a teflon-coated cast-iron pot, until  browning  or even slighter burning. 
 When the  onions  are acceptable, add the  flour ,  garlic ,  cream , and  tomato paste . 
 When that becomes soft and hot, gradually add the  cheese  until bubbly. 
 Serve with tortilla chips.
