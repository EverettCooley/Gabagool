Description : 
 Contributed by Sylvia at  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 – 2  chicken breasts 
 3 – 4  eggs , hard boiled 
 6  bacon  slices, cooked crisp/crumbled 
 1  avocado , diced 
 2  tomatoes , diced 
 ½  red onion , chopped 
 8 oz  cheddar  or  Monterey jack , or  blue cheese , shredded 
 1  romaine  head, shredded 
 Directions : 
 Grill or sauté breasts and cut into strips. 
 Line plates with lettuce and place ingredients on a diagonal; top with dressing of choice.
