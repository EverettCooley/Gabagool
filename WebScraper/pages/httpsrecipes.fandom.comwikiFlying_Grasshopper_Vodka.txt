 Ingredients : 
 1 shot De Kuyper White  crème de cacao 
 1 shot De Kuyper  crème de menthe 
 1 shot Stolichnaya  vodka 
 1 shot Single  cream 
 1 shot  milk 
 Directions : 
 Shake all ingredients with  ice  and strain into a Martini glass.
