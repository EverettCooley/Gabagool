 Description : 
 This basic, glorious salad is made with real ham, fruits, fruit juice, mayonnaise, and peanuts and is great for sandwiches, then served as the side dish or appetizer.
 
         
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups smoked  ham , cubed and/or cooked 
 8 ounces  pineapple  chunks 
 2  celery  ribs, sliced 
 ½ cup  black olives , halved 
 ¼ cup  mayonnaise 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 ¼ cup  peanuts 
 Directions : 
 Combine  ham ,  pineapple ,  celery , and  mayonnaise , and  lemon juice ; stir into the  ham  mixture. 
 Spoon contents into the fresh container. Chill at least an hour. Stir with  peanuts  before serving.
