Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Novell Estate in Fort Worth, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4 large fresh  turnips 
 4 ounces slivered  almonds 
 1 teaspoon  sugar 
 ½ cup  margarine 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 cooking spray 
 Directions : 
 Peel and slice turnips then cover with water and boil until tender. 
 Drain and mash then add margarine, sugar, salt and pepper then mix until smooth and creamy. 
 Spray a casserole dish with cooking spray then pour turnip mixture into casserole and top with slivered almonds. 
 Bake at 350°F for 20 minutes.
