Beef  Chow  mein  Casserole
 
 1 T.  butter 
 1 Pound Ground  Beef 
 1  green pepper , Finely Chopped 
 1 Cup  celery , Chopped 
 1  Onion , Chopped 
 1 T.  salt 
 1 Cup  water 
 1 Can Condensed Cream of  Mushroom  Soup 
 1/2 Cup  tomato juice 
 1 Can  Chinese noodles  (6 Oz) 
 
Preheat Oven to 350 And Grease a 2 Quart Casserole. Melt  butter  in a Large Skillet And Brown The Meat Over Medium Heat. Add  green pepper ,  celery ,  Onion  And  salt . Cook Over Low Heat Until Tender. Add  water , Soup,  tomato juice  And Half The  Chinese noodles . Stir to Blend And Pour Into Casserole. Sprinkle With Remaining Noodles And Bake at 350. Serves 4 to 6
 Source: Come Into The Kitchen
