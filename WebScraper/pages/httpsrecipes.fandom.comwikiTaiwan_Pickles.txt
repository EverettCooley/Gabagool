 Ingredients : 
 1  English cucumber , sliced in half length-wise and sliced 
 salt  (to taste) 
 Dressing : 
 1 Tablespoon  sesame oil 
 1 teaspoon  chili oil  (or to taste) 
 1 Tablespoon  rice vinegar 
 Garnish : 
 Chopped  dried chilies  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Place sliced  cucumbers  in serving bowl and sprinkle with  salt  to taste. 
 Mix dressing ingredients in a small bowl and pour over  cucumber  slices. 
 Stir to coat. 
 Sprinkle with dried chilies to taste. 
 Stir. Serve cold.
