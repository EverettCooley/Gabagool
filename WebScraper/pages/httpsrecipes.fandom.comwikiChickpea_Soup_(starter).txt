 Ingredients : 
 250g diced mutton meat ~(a leg of mutton can be added) 
 200g precooked  Chickpeas 
 2  potatoes  ~ diced 
 1 small minced  Onion 
 2 tbsp  tomato  paste 
 1 tbsp  oil 
 1/2 tsp  cumin  and caraway 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 1) Brown the meat in  oil  together with the minced  Onion  and spices then, add everything in a large stew pan.
 2) Add the  Chickpeas  and cover with  water .
 3) Bring to boil and add the diced  potatoes  and the  tomato  paste. Add  salt  and  pepper .
 4) Cook over a low heat for 20 minutes and serve.
