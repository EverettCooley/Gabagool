 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 5 large  potatoes , cooked, peeled and diced into ½ inch cubes 
 2 hardboiled  eggs , peeled and chopped. 
 ¼ cup finely chopped  green onions 
 ¼ cup finely chopped  parsley 
 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh  mint 
 ¼ cup  olive oil 
 2 cloves  garlic 
 ¼ cup  lemon juice 
 salt  &  pepper  to taste 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Place the first six ingredients in a large salad bowl. 
 In a separate bowl, mash the  garlic  with the  salt , then add the  lemon juice  & stir. 
 Add the dressing to the rest of the ingredients and toss gently, being careful not to crumble the  potatoes  and the  eggs . 
 Serve chilled.
