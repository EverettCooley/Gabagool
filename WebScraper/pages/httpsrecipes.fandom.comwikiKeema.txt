 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 kilo minced  Beef 
 2  tomatoes 
 1  Onion  (medium size) 
 3 tbs  oil 
 6 whole  black peppers 
 6 cloves 
 1 Bari illaichi (Queen  cardamom ) 
 1/4 tsp zeera ( cumin  seeds) 
 1tsp dhuniya powder (Ground up  cilantro  /  coriander seeds ) 
 salt  according to taste. 
 1/4tsp haldi powder (Tumeric) 
 3/4tsp  chili powder 
 2tbs  yogurt 
 1/2tsp  ginger  paste 
 1/4tsp  garlic  paste 
 Directions : 
 Finely chop the  Onion  and saut√© in  oil  until light brown. 
 Add all the spices including  yogurt  and finely chopped  tomatoes . 
 Cook on medium heat till  water  is almost dried. 
 Add mince meat mix and dry the  water  again. 
 Then add 2 glasses of  water , cover and leave to tenderize and  water  dries. 
 Garnish with fresh green dhuniya leaves, green chilies and  ginger  slices.
