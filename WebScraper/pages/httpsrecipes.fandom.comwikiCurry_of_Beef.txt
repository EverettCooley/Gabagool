Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  beef chuck  cut into one-inch cubes 
 ⅓ cup  flour 
 ⅓ cup  oil 
 1 large  onion 
 1 clove of  garlic 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 2 x 8 oz cans seasoned  tomato sauce 
 1½ cups  water 
 2 – 3 tsp  curry powder 
 1 x 10 oz package of frozen French cut  green beans 
 Condiments : 
 chutney ( tomato ,  zucchini ,  apple ,  corn ) 
 chopped  peanuts 
 chopped hard-boiled  egg 
 sliced  green onion 
 flaked  coconut 
 plumped  raisins 
 bananas 
 Directions : 
 Roll  beef  cubes in  flour  and brown in hot  oil . 
 Combine  onion ,  garlic ,  salt ,  pepper ,  tomato sauce  and pour over meat. 
 Cover and cook slowly until meat is fork tender. 
 Stir in  curry powder . 
 Add  green beans  and cook about 15 minutes, separating the  beans  with a fork as they heat. 
 Serve over  rice  or noodles with condiments. 
 The  beef  mixture can be done in a crockpot after the initial  browning , and the  rice  could be done in a  rice  cooker with a timer.
