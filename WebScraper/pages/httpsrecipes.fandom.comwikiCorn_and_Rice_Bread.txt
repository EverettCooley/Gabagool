 2 cups ground white  cornmeal

 3  eggs 
 1 cup cooked  rice 
 1 tbsp Red  palm oil , melted 
 2 tbsp  coconut oil , melted 
 1 tbsp  baking powder 
 1 tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Sift together the dry ingredients. Beat the  eggs  well in a separate bowl. Mix the  eggs  with the melted  oil ,  rice  and  milk . Add to the dry ingredients. Mix well. Pour the mixture into a well oiled shallow baking pan, and bake at 400 degrees for 30 minutes.
