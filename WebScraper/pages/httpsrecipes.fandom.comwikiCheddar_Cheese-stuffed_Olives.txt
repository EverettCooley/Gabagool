 Author: Mary Jane Finsand 
 Typed By: Susan alexanderjamesmom@gmail.com 
 Ingredients : 
 pitted  olives 
 1 cup white  cheddar  cheese (grated) 250 ml 
 2 tbsp sweet  butter  30 ml 
 ⅓ cup  all-purpose flour  90 ml 
 1 tsp  paprika  5 ml 
 ½ tsp  salt  2 ml 
 Directions : 
 Cream together cheese and butter. 
 Blend in flour, paprika, and salt. 
 Stuff into well-drained, pitted olives (ripe or green), using pastry tube or tip of spoon. 
 Other Stuffing Suggestions : 
 walnut  halves 
 pecan  halves 
 water chestnuts 
 small whole  shrimp 
 wiener  sticks 
 mushrooms 
 zucchini 
 small  cauliflowerettes
