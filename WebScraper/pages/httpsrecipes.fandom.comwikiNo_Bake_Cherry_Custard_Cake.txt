 Makes 12 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (10-inch) prepared angel food cake 
 1 (1.4 ounce) box sugar-free  instant vanilla pudding mix 
 1Â½ cups  2% milk 
 1 cup light  sour cream 
 1 (21 ounce) can light  cherry  pie filling 
 Directions : 
 Tear the angel food cake into bite-size pieces. 
 Press into an 11 x 7- inch baking dish. 
 In a mixing bowl, combine the pudding mix, milk and light sour cream. 
 Beat until thickened, about 2 minutes. 
 Spread over the cake. 
 Spoon cherry pie filling evenly over the top of the cake. 
 Chill thoroughly until serving time.
