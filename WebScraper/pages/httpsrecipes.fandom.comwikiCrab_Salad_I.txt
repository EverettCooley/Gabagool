      Crab salad     
 Ingredients : 
 2 medium  crabs  (100 g crab flesh) 
 3 tbsp  mayonnaise 
 juice of 1  lemon 
 1 medium  tomato , well chopped 
 1 fresh,  green onion , well chopped 
 1 coffee cup  white wine 
 1 bunch aromatic herbs for boiling 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Pour  water  in a casserole; add half a coffee cup of  white wine  and aromatic herbs and bring to the boil. 
 Add the crabs and cook for about 10 minutes (depending on size). 
 Remove from heat and leave to cool. 
 Strain well, remove the shells and scoop out the flesh into a bowl. 
 Add the rest of the crab flesh,  onion ,  garlic ,  tomato ,  basil  and sprinkle with the rest of the wine,  olive oil  and  lemon juice . 
 Leave to marinade for 10 minutes. 
 Add  mayonnaise ,  salt  â€“  pepper  and mix well. 
 Serve cold.
