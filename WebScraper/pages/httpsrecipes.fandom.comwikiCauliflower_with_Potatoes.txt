 Ingredients : 
 1  cauliflower  head 
 150 g  lard 
 500 g boiled  potatoes 
 1 big  onion 
 2  eggs 
 3 tbsp  flour 
 salt 
 parsley , green 
 Directions : 
 Cube  lard  and fry with finely chopped  onion . 
 Put half-cooked  cauliflower  parts, sliced  potatoes  on  lard  pieces and pour over beaten  eggs  with  flour  and  salt  and bake in the oven until done. 
 Decorate with  parsley  greens before serving.
