 Cook time: 
 Serves: 
 
 Description : 
 Perfect soufflé, perfect for buffet, eaten warm or cold!
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 packet  filo  pastry sheet (500gr.) 
 1 package chopped  sausages  Frankfurt 
 3  horn peppers  chopped 
 300 g  feta  cheese crumbled by hand 
 3  eggs 
 500 ml fresh  milk 
 some  olive oil 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl mix the cheese, peppers and sausages. 
 Lubricate a circular shape of the cake. 
 I get a sheet of crust, strain and put in the bottom of the form. I'm not careful! I want to be wrinkled and akatastasto! 
 Sprinkle the sheet with some olive oil and scatter a little of the filling. 
 Continue the layers with foil, a little oil, filling up to finish materials. I conclude with foil. 
 Beat the eggs, mix with milk and pour the souffle. 
 With a knife, the hole here and there, to suck the liquid. 
 Bake in preheated oven at 170 degrees for one hour until golden brown. 
 When cooked, turn inside out and after a bit cool, cut into slices like cake! 
 Try it and to remember! 
 
 A few more tips : 
 If you allow the oven, lower temperature and bake for a little more time. I want to sigopsithei to cooked inside. Use long form or not to fill up more, because swelling in cooking.
