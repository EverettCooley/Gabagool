Ingredients : 
 1 cup  butter , softened 
 1¾ cups  sugar , according to taste 
 2  eggs 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 1 cup  flour 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 1 cup  cashews , chopped 
 1½ cups  dried mangoes , chopped 
 Choco-mallow surprise : 
 1 cup  chocolate chips 
 1 cup mini  marshmallows 
 ¼ cup  water 
 ¼ to ½ cup  sugar , according to taste 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F. 
 Grease a 9x9 pan with cooking  oil . 
 At high speed,  cream  the  butter  and  sugar  until light. 
 Add  vanilla  and mix some more. 
 Add  eggs  one at a time, mixing well after each addition. 
 Fold in the dry ingredients mixing only until combined. 
 Spread the mixture onto the prepared pan. 
 Bake for about 45 minutes. 
 When cool, sprinkle  icing sugar  on top or spread with choco-mallow surprise sauce. 
 Choco-mallow surprise : 
 For sauce, boil  water , then lower heat. 
 Add  chocolate chips  and  sugar , mixing until dissolved. 
 Turn off heat. 
 Add mini marshmallows and a tsp of boiling  water . 
 Mix well with a spoon until marshmallows melt and combine with the choco mixture. 
 Add boiling  water  to the mixture while mixing, until desired consistency is attained. 
 Spread over 24 revel bars.
