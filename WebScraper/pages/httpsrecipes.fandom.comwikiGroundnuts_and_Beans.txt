Description : 
 Easy and testy! Preparation and cooking time quick - 40 minutes or less. Serve for breakfast or as a snack.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup of  kidney beans 
 1 cup of  groundnuts 
 1 pinch of  salt 
 Directions : 
 Boil beans and groundnuts separately until soft enough to eat. 
 Mix the beans and groundnuts, and  salt  to taste. 
 Serve for breakfast or as a snack.
