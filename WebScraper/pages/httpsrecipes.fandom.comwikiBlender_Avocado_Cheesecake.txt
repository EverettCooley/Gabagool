 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1  California avocado , seeded and peeled 
 8 oz  cream cheese , softened 
 ½ Cup  sour cream 
 3  lemon peel  strips 
 3 ¾ oz Vanilla-flavored instant pudding and pie filling 
 GRAHAM CRACKER CRUST 
 Directions : 
 Blend together  avocado ,  cream cheese ,  sour cream  and  lemon peel  in an electric blender until smooth. 
 Add pudding mix and blend just until mixed. 
 Pour into crust and chill several hours until set.
