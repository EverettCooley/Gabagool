Ingredients : 
 1 cup  pickles 
 2 cups  tomatoes 
 2 lbs  steak 
 ½ cup  lemon juice 
 ¾ cup  red peppers 
 ¾ cup  green peppers 
 ½ cup co- jack cheese 
 ¼ cups sweet  onion  sauce 
 2 cups  rice 
 2 cups  corn 
 Directions : 
 Heat grill to 250°F. 
 Put  steak  on the grill, wait 5 – 13 minutes to be done. 
 Cut up  red peppers  into bite size. 
 Cut up  green peppers  into bite size. 
 Put both  peppers  in a bowl. 
 Boil the  peppers  until done, wait 5 – 10 minutes. 
 Cut up pickles into bite size. 
 Cut up  tomatoes  into bite size. 
 Put the pickles, tomatoes into the bowl. 
 Heat  rice  for 10 – 20 minutes, when done put  butter  on  rice . 
 Put the  rice  into the bowl. 
 Cut up  cheese  into tiny pieces, put the  cheese  in the bowl. 
 Heat the  corn  for 5 – 15 minutes. 
 Put the  corn  in the bowl heat the bowl for 15 – 30 minutes. 
 Let it cool off for 5 – 20 minutes.
