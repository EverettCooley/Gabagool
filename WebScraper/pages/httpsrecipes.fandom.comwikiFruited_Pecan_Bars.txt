Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Love Estate in Sachse, Texas in 1984.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 14 ounces packaged bran muffin mix 
 1 cup chopped toasted  pecans 
 2 cups  mincemeat 
 1 can  sweetened condensed milk 
 pecan  halves 
 Directions : 
 Grease rectangular baking pan. 
 Combine muffin mix and pecans. 
 Add mincemeat and condensed milk then stir until blended and spread into prepared pan. 
 Bake at 350Â°F for 30 minutes. 
 Place pecan halves on top of bars and cool completely.
