 Ingredients : 
 1 	 	Bottle of  beer 
 4 	oz 	 Sugar 
 4 		 egg  yolks 
 1 	oz 	 butter 
 Directions : 
 Mix  beer  with yolks and  Sugar  in a jug. 
Pour into saucepan and beat with rotary whisk, until it boils. 
Remove from heat at boiling point, add  butter  and serve with a garnish of  parsley  and  paprika .
