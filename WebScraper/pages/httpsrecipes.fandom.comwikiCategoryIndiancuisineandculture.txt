 About Indian Cuisine and Culture Y-Group 
 Welcome the Cookbookwiki version of the Indian Cuisine and Culture Y-Group and Cuisine Cuisine.com where...Food is Culture and Culture is Food! Visit  www.CuisineCuisine.com , a fun site for anyone interested in learning how to get started with Indian Cooking and would like to learn more about the Indian Culture. Indians who reside outside India and are trying to keep the culture and cuisine alive, tell us what you have done to pass some of our traditions down to not only your children but others around you.
