Ingredients : 
 1 (11 oz) package of  pretzels  - any kind stick or other wise 
 1 (10-12 oz) package of Peanut Butter filled crackers 
 1 cup dry roasted  peanuts 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1/2 cup  butter 
 1/2 cup  light corn syrup 
 2 tbs  vanilla 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 1 (12 oz) bag of M&M's (make sure and use the Halloween colored) 
 1 package of candy corn or pumpkin bridge mix 
 Directions : 
 Mix together pretzels, crackers and peanuts in a large pan or on a cookie sheet for the oven 
 Bring sugar, butter, corn syrup and vanilla to a boil; stirring until threads begin to pull from a spoon. Add baking soda. 
 Pour the hot mixture over the pretzel mixture and bake in a 250 degree oven for 45 minutes stirring every 15 minutes. 
 When it comes out of the oven add candy 
 Mix well and pour onto wax paper till cool - break apart and eat! 
 
 
 Note 
 
 Make up according to holiday colored M&M's and candy corn type candies!
