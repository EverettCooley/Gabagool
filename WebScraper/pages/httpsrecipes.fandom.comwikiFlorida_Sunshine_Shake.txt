Info 
 Cook Time : About 5 minutes 
 Serves : 2
 
 Ingredients 
 1 cup  orange juice 
 ½ cup  grapefruit juice 
 1 ripe  banana , mashed 
 ½ cup low-fat vanilla  yogurt 
 ½ teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 Directions 
 Combine all ingredients. 
 Pour into glass and serve immediately. 
 Source 
 Orange Juice Canned, Unsweetened  by the US Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
