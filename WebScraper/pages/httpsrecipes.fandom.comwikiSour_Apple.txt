Description : 
 The flavor is all natural, emanating from  apple  pieces,  hibiscus flowers  and  cranberries . 
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ oz  vodka 
 ¾ oz  apple liqueur 
 ½ oz  melon liqueur 
 ½ oz lemon- lime  soda 
 Directions : 
 Shake with  ice  and strain into cordial glass.
