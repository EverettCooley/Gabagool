 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup prepared  dulce de leche 
 4  eggs 
 4 tablespoons  sugar 
 nuts , if desired 
 Directions : 
 Spread the prepared dulce de leche (see recipe) in a stainless steel or glass souffle dish with a spatula. Add  nuts  to the dulce de leche, if desired. 
 Separate the  eggs , and in a separate bowl, beat the whites until they form stiff peaks. 
 Spread them over the dulce de leche. 
 Beat the  egg yolks  and the  sugar  until they are creamy and spread over the  egg whites . 
 Bake in a moderately hot oven, 200°C / 405°F, for 12 minutes. Serve immediately. 
 The dessert may also be made in individual dishes or molds.
