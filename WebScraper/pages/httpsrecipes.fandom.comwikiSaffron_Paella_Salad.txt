 Description : 
 Makes 4 servings. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 tablespoons  white wine vinegar 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 ½ teaspoon ground  white pepper 
 2½ cups cooked  rice  (cooked in  chicken broth  and  saffron ), cooled to room temperature 
 1 cup cooked  turkey  breast cubes 
 1 cup peeled and de-veined cooked  shrimp 
 ½ cup diced red  bell pepper 
 ½ cup cooked  green peas 
 ⅓ cup chopped  onion 
 ⅓ cup sliced  ripe olives 
 lettuce  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Combine  vinegar ,  oil ,  garlic  and  pepper  in large bowl; mix well. 
 Add  rice ,  turkey ,  shrimp , red pepper,  peas ,  onion  and ; toss lightly. 
 Serve on  lettuce  leaves.
