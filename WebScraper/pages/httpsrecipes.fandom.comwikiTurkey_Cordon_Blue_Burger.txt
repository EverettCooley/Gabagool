Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group  and also  Healthy Recipes For Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Original recipe 
 Serves 6 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅔ cup plain  panko  breadcrumbs 
 ½ cup  emmenthaler  cheese, cut into ¼-inch dice 
 ½ cup sliced  ham , cut into ¼-inch dice 
 2 tablespoons  Dijon mustard 
 ½ cup fresh  parsley , finely chopped 
 ½ cup  yellow onion , cut into ¼-inch dice 
 ½ teaspoon  sea salt 
 ¼ teaspoon freshly ground  pepper 
 1½ pounds  ground turkey , dark meat 
 Directions : 
 In a medium bowl thoroughly combine the breadcrumbs, cheese, ham, mustard, parsley, onion, salt and pepper. 
 Add the turkey and with your hands lightly bring all the ingredients together; being careful not to overwork the meat. 
 With dampened hands, form the meat into 6 patties of even thickness. 
 Place on a lined baking sheet, cover loosely with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 1 hour. 
 Meanwhile, light the grill or preheat the broiler. 
 Cook the burgers, turning carefully with a spatula, for 6 minutes per side or until no longer pink inside. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving (172 g-wt.): 
 270 calories (110 from fat) | 12g total fat | 4.5g saturated fat | < 1g dietary fiber | 29g protein | 11g carbohydrate | 80mg cholesterol | 890mg sodium
