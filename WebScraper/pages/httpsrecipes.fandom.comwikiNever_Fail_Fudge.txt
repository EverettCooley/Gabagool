Description 
 This candy is very rich and very good! Since this candy is so rich, you may want to cut into very small squares.

 Ingredients 
 1 (16-ounce) bag semi-sweet  chocolate chips 
 1 (14-ounce) can sweetened  condensed milk 
 dash  salt 
 1 cup  pecan  halves 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 Directions 
 Line an 8-inch or 9-inch square pan with aluminum foil. 
 Set aside. 
 Melt the chocolate chips with the milk over low heat. 
 Add salt. 
 When all is melted, remove from the heat and stir in the pecans and vanilla. 
 Pour in lined pan and refrigerate until firm. 
 When ready to serve, invert pan, peel off aluminum and cut into squares.
