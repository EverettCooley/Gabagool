angel hair pasta  With  zucchini   tomato  Sauce
 
 2 T.  olive oil 
 1  Onion , Chopped 
 3 cloves  garlic , Minced 
 2  Shallots , Minced 
 4 Medium Italian  zucchini , Peeled And Grated 
 1 Can Stewed  tomatoes , Chopped, do Not Drain (16 Oz) 
 4 T.  tomato  Paste 
 1 T. Chopped  parsley 
 1 T. Each Italian Herb Seasoning Blend And  sweet basil  FlakesSalt And  pepper  to Taste 
 1 Pkg.  angel hair pasta , Cooked Tender But Firm And Drained (8 Oz) 
 1/3 cu Grated  Parmesan cheese 
 
In a Pan, Saut? Together First 5 Ingredients Until Vegetables Are Softened And Most of The Liquid Rendered is Evaporated. Add The  tomatoes ,  tomato  Paste And Seasonings And Simmer Sauce, Uncovered For 30 Minutes. Toss  Pasta  With Sauce And Pass Grated  Cheese  at The Table. Serves 6 as a Small Entree or 4 as a Main Course. Note: Sauce Can be Prepared Earlier in The Day And Heated Before Serving.  Pasta  Can be Prepared 1 Hour Before Serving And Held in The Strainer of a  spaghetti  Cooker. Just Before Serving, Plunge The Strainer in Boiling  water  For 2 Minutes And Drain.
