Ingredients : 
 Cake 
 3 c.  Flour 
 1/2 tsp.  baking powder 
 1/8 tsp.  salt 
 3 c.  sugar 
 5  eggs 
 8 oz. (2 sticks)  butter 
 1/2 c. vegetable  shortening 
 2 tsp.  lemon extract 
 1 c.  milk 
 Frosting 
 1 (8 oz.) pkg.  cream cheese 
 1 lb.  confectioners' sugar 
 2 Tbsp  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Sift flour, salt and baking powder together and set aside. 
 Cream shortening, butter and sugar together until light and fluffy. 
 Add 1 egg at a time, beating for 1 minute after each addition. 
 Add flavoring and beat. 
 Add portions of dry ingredients alternately with milk. 
 Beat until completely mixed. 
 Pour mixture into a greased and floured 10 inch tube pan and bake in a preheated 325 degree oven for 1 hour and 20 minutes. 
 Remove from oven and cool 
 Cream together cream cheese and sifted confectioners' sugar. 
 Add lemon juice, 1/2 teaspoon at a time, until frosting reaches spreading consistency. 
 Spread on cooled cake 
 Notes : 
 Frosting ingredients should all be at room temperature
