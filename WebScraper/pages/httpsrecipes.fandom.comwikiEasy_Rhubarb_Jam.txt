Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 5 cups diced  rhubarb 
 1 small can crushed  pineapple  (drained) 
 2 cups  Sugar 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix and let stand 2 hours, then boil 12 minutes 
 Remove from heat add 1 small package  strawberry  jello stir and jar or put in plastic containers. 
 Refrigerate. Also freezes well.
