Ingredients : 
 8 oz  Egg Beaters  99% Real Egg product 
 1 tsp Fleischmann's  margarine 
 2 x 6-inch flour  tortillas 
 lettuce 
 1  tomato 
 Â¼ cup  onion 
 2 tsp  jalapeno 
 2 tsp  parsley 
 Directions : 
 In 8-inch nonstick skillet, cook Egg Beaters in  margarine , stirring occasionally until set. 
 Arrange tortillas on  lettuce ; divide and spoon Egg Beaters onto tortillas. 
 Top with spicy salsa. 
 Garnish and serve immediately. 
 Spicy salsa : 
 Combine chopped  tomato , chopped  onion , chopped jalapeno and  parsley  or  cilantro . 
 Chill until serving time.
