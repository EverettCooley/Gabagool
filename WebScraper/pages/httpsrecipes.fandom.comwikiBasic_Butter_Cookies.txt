Basic  butter  Cookies
 Categories: Cookies
 Yield: 30 Servings
 
 1 c All Purpose  flour 
 1/2 c  cornstarch 
 1/2 c  powdered sugar 
 3/4 c Butter/Not  margarine ; Room 
 -Temp
 
 1/2 c Coarsely Chopped  walnuts 
 
Preheat Oven to 300 Degrees. Sift First Three Ingredients Into LargeBowl. Add  butter  And Mix Well. Stir in  walnuts . Drop by Teaspoons OntoBaking Sheets. Bake Until Cookies Are Lightly Golden - About 20-25 Minutes.Note: Real  butter  is The Secret of These Buttery Tasting Cookies. Makes
 
 30 Cookies 
 
Enjoy.
