Description : 
 Use as a stuffing in one small  chicken . 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  brown rice , uncooked 
 ¾ cup  water 
 1 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 1½ cup  mushrooms , (1 ½ lb) sliced 
 ¼ cup  onion , chopped 
 1 cup fresh or frozen  cranberries 
 ¼ tsp dried  thyme 
 ¼ tsp dried  basil 
 Directions : 
 Cook the  rice  in the  water  until tender, about 1 hour. 
 Saute the remaining ingredients in a skillet until the  celery  and  onion  are tender. 
 Add the  rice  and stir to blend.
