 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb ground  Beef 
 1 	small head of  cabbage , finely chopped 
 10 1/2 	ounces  cream  of  Mushroom  soup 
 1 	tablespoon  garlic , minced 
 1/2 	cup chopped  onions 
 1 	teaspoon  salt 
 1 	teaspoon  pepper 
 Directions : 
 In a large skillet brown the ground  Beef  and drain. Using a fork or  Potato  masher, break  Beef  into pea size pieces. Use food processor and chop  cabbage , add to  Beef , add soup,  garlic ,  Onion ,  salt  and  pepper , let simmer. 
 Preheat oven to 400 degrees. 
 Use bisquick dough,  bread dough  or pizza dough for wrap. Separate dough into 6 pieces, roll into 6 inch circles. Spray sheet pan, put dough circles in pan add ½ cup mix and close dough. Repeat and brush dough with  olive oil . 
 Bake 10–15 minutes or until golden brown
