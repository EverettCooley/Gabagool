Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Cabrera Estate in Malakoff, Texas in 1986.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 medium  cucumbers 
 1½ cups crumbled  feta  cheese 
 ½ cup chopped fresh  mint  leaves 
 ½ cup extra virgin  olive oil 
 ¼ cup fresh  lemon juice 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Peel half the skin of the cucumbers in lengthwise strips then cut into very thin slices. 
 Arrange slices on a serving platter then sprinkle feta cheese on top followed by the mint. 
 Whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, salt and pepper and pour over the salad.
