Description : 
 A delicious treat from dLife's Chris Smith, The Diabetic Chef.
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 12 ounces whipped  cream cheese  spread 
 2 tablespoons of  Splenda , granular form 
 ¼ cup brewed  coffee , cold 
 ½ teaspoon  vanilla 
 1 cup frozen light  whipped topping 
 2 boxes  fillo  tartlet shells (Athens brand is used) 
 Directions : 
 Combine the cream cheese spread, Splenda, coffee and vanilla in a medium bowl, mix. 
 Refrigerate for two hours. 
 Fold in frozen whipped topping. 
 Use spoon or pastry bag to fill tartlets. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 60 Calories | 5g Fat (76.2% calories from fat | 1g Protein | 3g Carbohydrate | 0g Dietary Fiber | 14mg Cholesterol | 60mg Sodium
