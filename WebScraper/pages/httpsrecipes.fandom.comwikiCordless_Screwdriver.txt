Ingredients : 
 1 3/4 oz  vodka 
 orange  Wedge 
 Sugar 
 Directions : 
 Chill  vodka  and strain into shot glass. Dip  orange  wedge in  Sugar . To drink, shoot the  vodka  and 
immediately take a draw on the  orange .
