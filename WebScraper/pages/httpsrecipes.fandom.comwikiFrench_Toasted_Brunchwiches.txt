Description : 
 Contributed by Lil at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 8 Sandwiches 
 Ingredients : 
 Topping : 
 ½ cup  butter 
 2 tbsp  light brown sugar 
 3 fresh  peaches , sliced 
 3 tbsp  peach schnapps 
 Sandwich : 
 16 slices  Italian bread , cut ½" thick 
 8 slices  ham 
 8 slices  muenster  cheese 
 3 large  eggs 
 ½ cup  peach schnapps 
 ½ cup  milk 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 4 tbsp  butter , divided 
 Directions : 
 Topping : 
 In medium skillet, melt  butter , stir in  sugar . 
 Add  peaches ; simmer 3 – 4 minutes, until  peaches  are just tender. 
 Stir in schnapps. 
 Simmer 1 minute. 
 Sandwich : 
 Prepare each sandwich using 2 slices  bread , 1 slice  ham  and 1 slice  cheese . 
 In medium bowl, beat  eggs  with schnapps,  milk  and  salt . 
 Soak each sandwich in  egg  mixture until  bread  is saturated. 
 In large skillet, melt 2 tbsp  butter ; sauté sandwiches over medium heat about 4 minutes on each side or until golden brown. 
 Remove to heated platter. 
 Melt remaining  butter  and repeat with remaining sandwiches. 
 Serve with prepared topping.
