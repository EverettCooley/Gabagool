Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  ground beef 
 1 lb  sausage  (hot or mixed) 
 1 medium  bell pepper 
 1 medium  onion 
 2 lb  Velveeta 
 1 small jar  picante sauce 
 1 clove  garlic 
 1 can  cream of mushroom  soup 
 Directions : 
 Brown together first 4 ingredients. 
 Melt last four ingredients in crock pot and add meat mixture. 
 Serve with chips.
