Ingredients : 
 1 package Devil's Food  cake mix 
 Icing 
 4 1/2 tbsp.  flour 
 1 c.  milk 
 2 sticks  butter 
 1/2 c.  Crisco 
 1 1/2 c.  sugar 
 1 tsp.  vanilla 
 Fudge topping 
 1 c. sugar 
 1/3 c. milk 
 2 tbsp.  cocoa 
 1 tsp. vanilla 
 1 tsp. butter 
 Directions : 
 Mix and bake cake as directed in 9 inch round pans. Set aside. 
 Mix flour and milk in saucepan, cook slow until thick, let cool. 
 Mix remaining ingredients, add flour mixture and beat for 12 minutes. 
 Cut each cake layer in half, horizontally, with thread. 
 Put icing between each layer. 
 Combine topping ingredients and pour over top of cake
