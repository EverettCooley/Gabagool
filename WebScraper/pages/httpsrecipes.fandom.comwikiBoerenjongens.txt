      Boerenjongens     
 Description : 
 This "drink" is well known throughout the Netherlands. It is associated with little old ladies, although the name translates to "Farmer's Lads" or "Farming Lads".
 
 Ingredients : 
 500 g big  raisins  with stones 
 10 cm  cinnamon  stick 
 250 g  sugar 
 1 liter  brandy 
 Directions : 
 Wash the  raisins  in lukewarm  water  and leave to drain. 
 Put in a stoppered bottle with the  cinnamon  stick. 
 Put the  sugar  in a pot with  water  just covering it and bring to the boil. 
 Leave to cool and mix with the  brandy . 
 Pour the  brandy  onto the  raisins  and leave to stand for at least 2 months before using.
