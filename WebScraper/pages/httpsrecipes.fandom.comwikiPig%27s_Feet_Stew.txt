Info : 
 Serves:  4 to 6
 Cook time:  45 minutes
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 precooked pig's feet, or 6  Pork  chops 
 1 cup  water  or stock from pig's chops feet 
 1/2 teaspoon  nutmeg 
 1  green pepper , chopped 
 1/2 teaspoon  powdered ginger 
 2 ripe  tomatoes , peeled and crushed 
 1/2 teaspoon tenderizer 
 2 tablespoons  lemon juice 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1/2 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 1/2 teaspoon  pepper 
 1  bay leaf 
 Directions : 
 Prick meat with fork, add first 6 ingredients and  water  or stock from pig's feet, and cook for 30 minutes, covered. 
 Add remaining ingredients and continue cooking slowly until  water  from gravy dries up and meat is tender. Add more  water  when necessary. Serve hot.
