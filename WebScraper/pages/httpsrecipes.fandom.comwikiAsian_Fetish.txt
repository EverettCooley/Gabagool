Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 oz Bombay Sapphire®  gin 
 1/2 oz  Grenadine  Syrup 
 3 oz sweet and sour mix 
 3 oz 7-Up® soda 
 1/2 oz Chambord®  raspberry liqueur 
 Directions : 
 Fill a glass with  ice , add the ingredients and garnish with a  Cherry .
