 Description : 
       Baked Plantains     
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 very ripe  plantains  (black skinned) 
 2 teasp  butter 
 ¼ teasp Ground  allspice 
 4 tbsp finely Grated  Cheese 
 1 teasp  brown sugar 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Preheat the oven to 220C, 425F. 
 Cut the  plantains  in half lengthways 
 Place cut sides up ion the baking sheet then sprinkle with the  allspice  and  Sugar . 
 Dot with small knobs of  butter , sprinkle the grated  Cheese  over the top and bake for 35–44 minutes until the golden brown and tender.
