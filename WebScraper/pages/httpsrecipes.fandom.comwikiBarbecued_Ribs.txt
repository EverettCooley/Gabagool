Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: Marlboro Country Cookbook 
 Makes 4 to 6 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 4 sides  lean back ribs  or  spareribs 
 1 tbsp  salt 
 ¼ tbsp freshly cracked  pepper 
 1 large  onion , sliced 
 1  lime , sliced 
 ½ cup strong black  coffee 
 ½ cup  tomato paste 
 ½ cup  honey 
 2 tbsp  brown sugar 
 2 tbsp  tequila 
 1 tbsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 ¼ tbsp  tabasco sauce 
 Directions : 
 Trim fat from ribs; sprinkle both sides with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Place, rib side up, on rack in a covered barbecue grill. 
 Cook 60 minutes, turning occasionally. 
 Place  onions  and  lime  slices on meaty side of ribs for the last 20 minutes. 
 To make sauce, combine remaining ingredients. 
 Brush ribs generously with sauce and continue cooking for 30 minutes or until ribs are tender. 
 Continue to brush ribs with sauce as they cook. 
 Ribs may be cooked in a 400°F oven. 
 Follow same directions as above.
