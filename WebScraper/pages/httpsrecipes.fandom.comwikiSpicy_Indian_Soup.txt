 Description : 
       Spicy Indian Soup     
 Ingredients : 
 3  onions , chopped 
 4 tbsp  butter 
 2 tbsp  flour , sieved 
 1 tbsp  mint  leaves, crushed 
 1/4 cup  walnuts , coarsely chopped 
 1/2 tsp  Sugar 
 1/2 tsp  pepper 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Heat the  butter  and fry the  onions  until they are transparent.
Stir in the  flour  and cook until  flour  is golden brown. Take care, so that no lumps should be formed.
Add crushed  mint  leaves,  salt  and  pepper , and  walnuts . Fry for a few minutes longer.
Add 5 cups of  water  and  Sugar  to taste. Bring to boil and simmer for 10 minutes.
Serve hot with toasted naans.
 Serves: 4
Preparation time: 40 minutes
