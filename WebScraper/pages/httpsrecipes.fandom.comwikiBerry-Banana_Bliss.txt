Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup  fat free milk 
 1 packet  strawberry  sensation Nestlé®  Carnation Instant Breakfast 
 ½ cup frozen sliced  strawberries ,  raspberries  and/or  blueberries 
 ½  banana 
 ½ cup  ice  cubes (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Place  milk , Carnation Instant Breakfast,  berries  and  banana  in blender; cover. 
 Blend until smooth. 
 For a frostier beverage, add  ice . 
 Blend until smooth.
