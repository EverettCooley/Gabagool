 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 14 	 ounces  sweetened condensed milk 
 4 	tablespoons  cocoa powder 
 3 	tablespoons  butter 
 1 	teaspoon  vanilla extract 
 Directions : 
 1. Heat  condensed milk  in a double boiler.
 2. Mix  cocoa powder  with a tablespoon of hot  water  and stir into  condensed milk .
 3. Stir in  butter  and  vanilla extract  and heat through over low heat.
