 Description : 
 avocado  Tanzanian Salad
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2  avocado  per person 
 shelled shrimps 
 Tuna  fish (tinned) 
 cold salmon flaked 
 mixed vegetable salad diced 
 Directions : 
 Wash and cut  avocado  in half lengthwise and remove the stone. 
 Blend selected filling with sufficient salad  cream . 
 Fill centre of  avocado  and decorate with  paprika   pepper .
