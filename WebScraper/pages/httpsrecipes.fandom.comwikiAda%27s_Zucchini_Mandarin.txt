Ingredients : 
 1 lbs  zucchini 
 8 oz canned  mandarin oranges 
 ¼ tsp  nutmeg 
 ¼ cup  slivered almonds , toasted 
 Directions : 
 Cut the  zucchini  into slices. 
 Steam until tender. 
 Add the  orange  slices and the  nutmeg . 
 Sprinkle with the  almonds  just before serving.
