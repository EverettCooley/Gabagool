Description : 
 In Romanian: Cornulete rusesti
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  lard   or   butter 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 a little  salt 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 flour  as needed 
 marmalade 
 sour cherries  from preserves or  rahat-lokum 
 Â¾ cup  vanilla -flavored  confectioner's sugar 
 Directions : 
 Mix the  lard  or  butter  with  salt ,  vanilla  and  sour cream . 
 Add  flour  to obtain a soft dough; let rest for 20 minutes. 
 Then roll into a very thin sheet and cut into triangles. 
 At the wide side of the triangle, put a  sour cherry  from preserves, a little marmalade or a small piece of rahat-lokum. 
 Roll into a crescent and place on a baking sheet, leaving some distance among them. 
 After baking, roll the crescents in  vanilla  flavored  confectioner's sugar .
