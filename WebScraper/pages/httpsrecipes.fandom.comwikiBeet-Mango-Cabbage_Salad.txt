 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup  Mayonnaise 
 1 tbsp.  pineapple juice 
 1 cup shredded raw beets 
 1 cup chopped firm  mangos 
 2 cups shredded  cabbage 
 Directions : 
 Prepare dressing by mixing  Mayonnaise  with  pineapple juice . Place shredded  cabbage  on salad plates. Add  mango  and top with beets. Spoon on dressing.
