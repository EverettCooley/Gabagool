 Ingredients : 
 10  hard boiled eggs 
 2 small baby  cabbage  chopped finely 
 1 ½ cups chopped  parsley  or  dill 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 1 ½ cups  Dijon mustard 
 salt 
 pepper 
 3 – 5 slices of  bread  cut into bite size pieces 
 Directions : 
 Start by peeling the hard boiled  eggs  and cut them into small wedges, then put them into a mixing bowl. 
 In a separate bowl, mix the baby  cabbage ,  mayonnaise ,  mustard ,  salt  and  pepper  to taste. 
 Then, pour this sauce over the  eggs . 
 Spread the bread pieces on the dog's plate and put the  egg  mixture over the bread. 
 If there are leftover put them in the refrigerator for no longer than a day.
