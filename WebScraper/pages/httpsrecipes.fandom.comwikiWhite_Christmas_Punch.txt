 Makes 6Â½ quarts 
 Ingredients : 
 3 quarts vanilla  ice cream 
 2 cups  bourbon 
 1 cup  light rum 
 14 cups  milk 
 Directions : 
 In large bowl, stir ice cream just until softened. 
 Blend in the bourbon and rum. 
 Return mixture to freezer until needed. 
 To use, turn frozen mixture into punch bowl. 
 Blend in milk. 
 Stir slowly with up and down motion. 
 Float ice ring on top if desired.
