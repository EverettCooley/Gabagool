Ingredients : 
 12  cucumbers 
 2  onions 
 2  sweet peppers 
 Â½ cup  salt 
 1 cup  sugar 
 2 tbsp  mustard seed 
 1 tsp  celery seed 
 3 cups  vinegar 
 Directions : 
 Chop up the  cucumbers ,  onions  and  peppers . 
 Add  salt  and allow to stand overnight. 
 Drain and add  sugar ,  mustard  and  celery seed . 
 Pack into clean jars, cover with  vinegar  and seal.
