 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cup  whole wheat flour 
 1 cup  rolled oats 
 1/2 cup  bran 
 1/2 cup  brown sugar 
 1 1/2 tsp  baking soda 
 1 tsp  salt 
 2  egg 
 1 1/2 cup  buttermilk 
 1/4 cup  vegetable oil 
 3  nectarine 
 3 tsp  orange  zest 
 1 1/2 tsp  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in mixing bowl. 
 Stir until blended. 
 Spoon batter into 2 ½-inch nonstick muffin cups. 
 Bake in 400 °F. Oven 20 minutes or until pick inserted in center comes out dry. 
 Serve warm.
