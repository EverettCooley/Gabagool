 Description : 
 Made with authentic fruit, fruit juice, and white rum, this natural beverage is great for other occasions as it delivers an ice-cold aroma that relishes, offers to the fruit, and enhances with alcohol content.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz.  white rum 
 ½ tsp.  powdered sugar 
 ¼ oz.  lime juice 
 2 - 3 dashes  pineapple  syrup 
 1 slice  pineapple 
 lime zest, optional 
 Directions : 
 Prepare all ingredients in a blender with crushed  ice . 
 Pour into a chilled cocktail glass, and serve. Garnish with the lime zest, if desired.
