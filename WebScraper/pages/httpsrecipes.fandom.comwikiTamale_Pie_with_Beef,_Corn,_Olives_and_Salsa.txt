Description : 
 Tamale pie is really good. Served as a casserole. Has ground beef, onion, corn, olives and salsa in it and taco seasoning. Has a cornmeal cheese crust and cheese melted on top too. We all like it.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Makes 6 servings of tamale pie. 
 Ingredients : 
 Filling : 
 1 pound  ground beef 
 1 small  onion , chopped 
 16 oz jar Ortega  salsa  (any flavor) 
 ⅓ cup water 
 1¼ oz pkg Ortega  taco seasoning  mix 
 1 cup frozen  corn  kernels 
 2¼ oz can sliced  olives , drained 
 Crust : 
 1 cup yellow or white  cornmeal 
 12 oz can  evaporated milk 
 4 oz can Ortega diced  green chiles 
 1 cup shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 1 tsp  salt 
 Ortega pickled  jalapeno  slices 
 Directions : 
 Filling : 
 Cook beef and onion in a skillet until beef is browned, drain. 
 Stir in salsa, water and seasoning mix and bring to a boil. 
 Reduce heat to low, cook 4 minutes add corn and olives. 
 Crust : 
 Heat oven to 425°F. 
 Combine cornmeal, milk, chiles, ½ cup cheese and salt in saucepan. 
 Cook 7 minutes or until thickened. 
 Spoon filling into ungreased baking dish. 
 Spread cornmeal mixture on top. 
 Bake 25 minutes. 
 Sprinkle with remaining cheese. 
 Bake for an additional 10 minutes or until cheese is melted. 
 Garnish servings with jalapenos and red bell pepper strips.
