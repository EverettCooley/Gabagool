 Ingredients : 
 2 shots Stolichnaya®  vodka 
 1 shot De Kuyper®  triple sec 
 4 shots  orange juice  (fresh) 
 Directions : 
 Half fill the glass with  ice  and add the stolichnaya  vodka  and the de kuyper  triple sec . 
 Top off with the  orange juice  and add the garnish.
