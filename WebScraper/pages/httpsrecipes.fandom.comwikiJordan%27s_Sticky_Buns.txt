 Ingredients : 
 Rich's frozen dough rolls (ball size) 
 Raisins  - enough 
 1 stick  butter 
 1/2 c.  brown sugar 
 1 pkg.  vanilla  pudding 
 cinnamon 
 Towel 
 Directions : 
 Lightly  butter  Bundt pan. 
 Sprinkle  Raisins  to cover bottom. 
 Roll dough balls in  cinnamon ; arrange in pan. 
 Sprinkle  vanilla  pudding (use entire package) over  cinnamon  balls. 
 Melt stick of  butter  and  brown sugar ; drizzle over entire pan. 
 Place towel to cover; leave overnight. 
 Next morning, bake at 350 degrees for 1/2 hour. 
 (Be sure to remove towel before baking!!)
