Baked  rice 
 
Serves 4.
 
 1 Cup Uncle Ben's Long  grain   rice 
 1 Can  Onion  Soup 
 1 Can  Beef  Consume 
 1 Stick Oleo or 1/2 Stick if Less Grease Desired 
 
Cover And Bake 1 Hour at 375 Degrees. Stir Occasionally.
 
Enjoy.
