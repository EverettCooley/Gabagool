 Description : 
 Makes 2¼ cups. 
 Source: Southern Living 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can whole berry  cranberry sauce  (16 oz) 
 ⅓ cup  strawberry preserves 
 1½ Tbsp  sugar 
 ¼ tsp ground  cinnamon 
 ½ cup coarsely chopped  walnuts , toasted 
 1 Tbsp  balsamic vinegar  or  red wine , such as  Pinot Noir 
 Directions : 
 Combine first 4 ingredients in a medium pan. 
 Cook over medium heat, stirring often, just until thoroughly heated. 
 Remove from heat; stir in walnuts and vinegar. 
 Cover and chill until ready to serve. Serve with smoked turkey or ham.
