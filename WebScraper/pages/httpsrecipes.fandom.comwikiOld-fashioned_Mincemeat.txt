Description : 
 Purchased from the Weir Estate in Canton, Texas in 1990. Date on card says 1947, also indicates that it was so popular it was sold at a local market.
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Makes 9 quarts 
 Ingredients : 
 4 pounds  beef 
 1 pound  suet 
 8 pounds  apples 
 4 pounds  sugar 
 2 pounds  raisins 
 1 cup  white vinegar 
 2 tablespoons  cinnamon 
 1 tablespoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Cook  beef  until tender, then run through  meat  grinder with  suet . 
 Pare and quarter  apples . 
 Combine all ingredients and cook one hour, stirring occasionally. 
 Remove from heat and seal in sterilized jars.
