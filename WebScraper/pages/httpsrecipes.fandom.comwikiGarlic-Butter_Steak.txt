 Description : 
This quick-and-easy skillet entree is definitely restaurant-quality and sure to become a staple at your house, too!       Garlic-Butter Steak     
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons butter, softened and divided 
 1 teaspoon minced fresh parsley 
 ½ teaspoon minced garlic 
 ¼ teaspoon soy sauce, light/reduced-sodium only 
 1 flat iron steak or boneless top sirloin steak 
 ⅛ teaspoon salt 
 ⅛ teaspoon pepper, black only 
 Directions : 
 Mix 1 tablespoon butter, parsley, garlic, and soy sauce. 
 Sprinkle steak with salt and pepper. In a large skillet, heat the remaining butter over moderate heat. 
 Add steak; cook until meat reaches desired doneness (for moderate-rare, a thermometer should read 135° C; moderate, 140° C), 4-7 minutes per side. Serve hot with garlic butter, as well as with rice.
