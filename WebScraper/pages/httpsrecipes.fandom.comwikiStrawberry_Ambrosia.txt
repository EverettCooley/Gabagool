Ingredients : 
 1 can  pineapple  chunks 
 1 can  mandarin oranges 
 1 package frozen  strawberries  (with  Sugar ) 
 1 container  Rich's Whip 
 1 package red  Jell-O 
 Directions : 
 Set jello according to package direction. 
 Whip up Rich's whip and mix in fruit. 
 Cut jello into small cubes and mix in.
