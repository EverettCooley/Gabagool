 Description : 
 In Romanian: Sos de ou
 
 Ingredients : 
 1Â½ cups  tomato sauce  or  broth 
 2  yolks  (Instead of using raw yolks, you may use finely chopped hard boiled yolks) 
 1 tablespoon  flour 
 2 tablespoons of  milk 
 Directions : 
 The sauce may be prepared with fried or unfried  flour . 
 Fried flour version : 
 If you are using fried  flour , fry it lightly in  butter , add hot  tomato sauce  or  broth , pouring a little at a time and constantly stirring. 
 Add  salt  to taste and simmer for 10 minutes. 
 When it is ready, mix with two yolks whipped with two tablespoons of  milk . 
 Serve warm. 
 Unfried flour version : 
 If you are using unfried  flour , dissolve the  flour  in cold  tomato sauce  or  broth , pouring the liquid a little at a time. 
 Salt to taste and simmer, constantly stirring, for 10 minutes. 
 When it is ready, mix with two yolks whipped with two tablespoons of  milk . 
 Serve warm.
