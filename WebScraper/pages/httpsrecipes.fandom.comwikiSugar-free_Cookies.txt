Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes about 5 dozen 
 Ingredients : 
 1¾ cups  all-purpose flour 
 ½ tsp  baking powder 
 ¾ cup  butter 
 1 pkg. (4 serving) strawberry, raspberry or lime sugar-free  Jell-O 
 1  egg 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Mix flour with baking powder. 
 Cream butter and gelatin, beating well. 
 Beat in egg and vanilla. 
 Gradually add flour mix, blending well. 
 Roll out cookie dough and cut out shapes or force dough through a cookie press onto an ungreased baking sheet. 
 Bake at 400°F about 8 minutes or until edges are golden brown.
