Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 3/25/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb  ground beef 
 2  onions  chopped (about 1 cup) 
 1 can  tomatoes 
 1 can  tomato sauce 
 1 tbsp  chili powder 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 can  kidney beans 
 1 pkg. uncooked  spaghetti 
 Directions : 
 Cook and stir ground beef and onions in large skillet or saucepan until meat is brown and onions are tender. 
 Stir in tomatoes, tomato sauce, chili powder, salt and beans (with liquid). 
 Heat to boiling; reduce heat. 
 Cook uncovered 10 minutes. 
 Cook spaghetti as directed on package. 
 Drain; turn spaghetti onto large platter and top with chili mixture.
