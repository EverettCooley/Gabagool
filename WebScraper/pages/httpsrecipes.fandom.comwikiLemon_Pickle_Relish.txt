 Description : 
 Always check the ingredients to make sure the product is vegan.
 (Fried  rice  with  basil )
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound, cut in wedges  lemon 
 1/4 Cup(s) pickling or  kosher salt 
 5 clove(s) finely minced  garlic 
 5 Tablespoon(s) crushed, hot red  bell pepper 
 3 Cup(s)  salad oil 
 Directions : 
 salt  the  lemon  wedges on all sides and pack into a quart jar.
 
 Cover the jar and let stand for 4 to 5 days at room temperature.
 
 Add the  garlic  and  pepper , mixing them through the  lemons .
 
 Heat the  oil  in a saucepan until very hot and pour it over  lemons .
 
 Let cool.
 
 Cover and let stand another 4 days at room temperature.
 
 Pack into hot, sterilized jars.
 
 Process 5 minutes.
