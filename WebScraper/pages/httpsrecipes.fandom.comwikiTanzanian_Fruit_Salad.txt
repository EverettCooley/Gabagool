 Description : 
 Tanzanian Fruit Salad 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4cups 1/2 inch cubed fresh  pineapple 
 2 medium  oranges , peeled and divided 
 2 medium mangoes, peeled and diced 
 2  bananas , cut in 1/2 inch slices 
 2 tablespoons  lime juice 
 2 tablespoons  dark brown sugar 
 1 cup shredded unsweetened  coconut 
 Directions : 
 Combine fruits in a bowl. 
 Combine  lime juice  and  brown sugar  and add to fruits; toss to coat. 
 Cover and chill at least 1 hour. 
 Sprinkle with  coconut .
