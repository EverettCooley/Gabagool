Ingredients : 
 ⅓ cup  shortening 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 1  egg 
 2½ cups  flour 
 2 teaspoons  baking soda 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon ground  cloves 
 1 teaspoon  ground ginger 
 ½ teaspoon  cinnamon 
 1 cup  molasses 
 1 cup hot  water 
 Directions : 
 butter  and  flour  a 9x 9 cake pan. 
 Preheat oven to 375°F . 
 Mix  flour ,  baking soda  and spices together. 
 Set aside. 
 Mix  molasses  and hot  water . 
 Stir and set aside. 
 cream   shortening  and  sugar . 
 Add  egg  and mix well. 
 Alternately add dry ingredients and  molasses  mixture to creamed mixture. 
 Resulting mixture will be rather thin. 
 Pour into cake pan and bake for 45 minutes or more until a toothpick comes out clean. 
 Cool in pan for 5 minutes before turning out. 
 Serve with  whipped cream  flavored with pure  vanilla  and sweetened with  sugar .
