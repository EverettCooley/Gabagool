Ground pork  is simply  pork  that has been ground or finely chopped. 
 Ground pork can be prepared at home or it can be bought from supermarkets, grocery stores or butcher shops. Ground pork is minced or cubed pork meat preferably from the less tender and less popular cuts of the pork. Ground pork or minced pork is made of finely chopped pork, minced by a meat grinder.
