      Caraway Cabbage - Hvidkål med Kommen     
 Description : 
 Caraway dabbage
 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ pounds shredded  cabbage 
 2  eggs 
 2 tablespoons  butter 
 ½ cup  milk 
 ½ cup  cream 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 white pepper  — to taste 
 1 teaspoon  caraway seed 
 Directions : 
 Cover the  cabbage  with cold  water . 
 Chill 2 hours. Drain. 
 Cover with boiling  water  and simmer 5 minutes. Drain well. 
 Combine the rest of the ingredients lightly with the  cabbage . 
 Bake in a buttered baking dish 30 to 40 minutes in a preheated 350ºF oven, or until browned on top.
