Description : 
 Makes 4 servings. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons  white wine vinegar 
 1 teaspoon  olive oil 
 2 to 3 drops  hot pepper sauce  (optional) 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  white pepper 
 2½ cups cooked  rice  (cooked in  chicken broth  and ⅛ teaspoon  saffron ), cooled to room temperature 
 ½ cup diced  red bell pepper 
 ½ cup diced  green bell pepper 
 ¼ cup sliced  green onions 
 ¼ cup sliced  black olives 
 lettuce  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Combine  vinegar ,  oil , pepper sauce (if desired),  garlic  and  white pepper  in large bowl; mix well. 
 Add  rice ,  peppers ,  onions  and ; toss lightly. 
 Serve on  lettuce  leaves.
