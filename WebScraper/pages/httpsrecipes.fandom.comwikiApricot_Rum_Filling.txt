 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  dried apricots 
 water  to cover 
 1-1/3 cups  Sugar 
 2 tablespoons  dark rum 
 1 cup whipping  cream , whipped 
 2 tablespoons  powdered sugar 
 
 
 Directions : 
 In a saucepan cover  apricots  with  water  and bring to boil over medium heat. 
 Keep on simmering boil for 40 minutes. 
 apricots  must be tender. 
 Add  Sugar  the last 10 minutes of cooking. 
 Drain  apricots  and puree in blender. 
 Add  rum . 
 Fell center of  Apples  with puree and mound some on top. 
 Chill 2 hours before serving.
