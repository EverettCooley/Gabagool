      Sling     
 About sling cocktail : 
 An alcoholic drink made with lemon juice, powdered sugar, and a liquor such as  brandy ,  gin ,  vodka , or  whiskey . Slings are usually served in an old-fashioned glass, but occasionally, they are served in a tall glass and topped off with soda water as in the  Singapore Sling .
