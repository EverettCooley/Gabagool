Description : 
 Makes about 2½ cups 
 Ingredients : 
 1¼ lbs (½ kg)  tomatoes , seeded, drained, and chopped 
 ¼ cup chilled  beer 
 ¼ cup fresh  cilantro 
 ½ cup  onion 
 3  jalapenos , minced 
 1 clove  garlic 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients. 
 Let stand for at least two hours; chill if desired. 
 Serve with tortilla chips.
