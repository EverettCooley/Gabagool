 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  converted rice , 
 1 tsp. ground  jeera , 
 2 lbs. boneless  Chicken , 
 1/2 tsp. ground cloves, 
 3  onions , 
 3 tsp. garam massala, 
 butter  for frying , 
 1 tsp. ground cardammon, 
 4 cups boiling  water , 
 2 Tbsp.  water , 
 1 chopped green  chilli , 
 1 tsp.  black pepper , 
 2 cloves  garlic , 
 1 1/2 inches  fresh ginger  root. 
 Directions : 
 Finely chop the  garlic , 1  Onion  and the  ginger root . Mix all the above ingredients together except for the  rice ,  Chicken , 2  onions ,  butter  and boiling  water . Set aside. 
 Heat  butter  or  margarine  in a large pan and fry sliced  onions  until golden brown. Add the  Chicken . Fry for 10 minutes stirring frequently so that it does not burn. 
 To prevent hte meat from sticking to the bottom of the pan add 2 tablespoons of  oil . Add the massala paste and fry for 2 to 3 mins. 
 Lower heat, cover pan tightly and cook very gently utnil all the liquid is dissolved and the  rice  cooked.
