 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1  celery  stalk,washed, ends trimmed 
 2 tbsp creamy or chunky  Peanut butter 
 10 NESTLE® RAISINET®  milk  Chocolate-Covered  Raisins 
 Directions : 
 FILL  celery  with  Peanut butter . 
 Press RAISINETS into  Peanut butter . 
 LITTLE KITCHEN HELPER HINTS: 
 Even the littlest hands can press RAISINETS into the log. 
 Help the children count out the nut and  Raisin  pieces or make little piles of 5 each. It's fun math! 
 For those who are still hungry, spread  Peanut butter  on small round crackers and have the kids make eyes, nose and mouths with RAISINETS. 
 Have small baby  carrots  and  Apple  wedges on hand for hungry kids to dip in the  Peanut butter  jar.
