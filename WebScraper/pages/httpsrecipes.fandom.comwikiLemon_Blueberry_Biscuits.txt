 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  all-purpose flour 
 1/3 cup  Sugar 
 2 teaspoons  baking powder 
 1/2 teaspoon  baking soda 
 1/4 teaspoon  salt 
 1 carton (8 ounces)  lemon   yogurt 
 1  egg , lightly beaten 
 1/4 cup  butter  or  margarine , melted 
 1 teaspoon grated  lemon peel 
 1 cup fresh or frozen  blueberries 
 GLAZE: 
 1/2 cup confectioners'  Sugar 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 1/2 teaspoon grated  lemon peel 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl, combine dry ingredients. Combine  yogurt ,  egg ,  butter  and  lemon peel ; stir into dry ingredients just until moistened. Fold in  blueberries . Drop by tablespoonfuls onto a greased baking sheet. Bake at 400 degrees for 15â€“18 minutes or until lightly browned. Combine glaze ingredients; drizzle over warm biscuits. Yield: 1 dozen.
