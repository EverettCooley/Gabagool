Description : 
 Delightfully simple and a great combination of flavors and textures in a small package. Features Medjool  dates ,  crème fraîche  and  candied ginger . 
 
 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 12 pitted medjool  dates 
 1/2 cup  crème fraîche  (if unavailable,  sour cream ) 
 candied ginger  or  walnuts , finely chopped 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Cut  dates  in half and top each with 1 tsp of  crème fraîche , either with a spoon or by piping on with a star tip. 
 Garnish by sprinkling with  candied ginger  or  walnuts , as desired.
