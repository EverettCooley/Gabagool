Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 tbsp  butter 
 4 tbsp  flour 
 2 cups  milk 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 1 cup  cheddar  cheese, grated 
 ¾ cup stuffed  olives , sliced 
 ½  green pepper , minced 
 1 cup  tuna  fish, flaked 
 2 cups soft  bread  cubes 
 ¾ cup  cornflakes 
 ½ cup  cheddar  cheese, grated 
 Directions : 
 Make a white sauce with butter, flour and milk. 
 Add salt. 
 Add cheese, blend. 
 Add olives, green pepper and tuna. 
 Arrange layer of bread crumbs on bottom of buttered casserole. 
 Pour half of tuna mixture over bread. 
 Spread another layer of bread cubes and cover with remaining tuna mixture. 
 Top with cornflakes, sprinkle with cheese. 
 Bake at 350°F for 20 minutes.
