 In Romanian:  Pasta  de parizer
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2Â lb/250 g bologna, 
 2 hard boiled  eggs , 
 mustard  to taste, 
 salt , 
 pepper , 
 lettuce , 
 radishes 
 Directions : 
 Grind the bologna together with the  eggs . 
 Mix the resulting paste with  mustard , add  salt  and  pepper . 
 Place the paste on a plate, garnish with chopped  lettuce  and  radishes  carved as flowers. 
 You can use this to fill sandwiches too.
