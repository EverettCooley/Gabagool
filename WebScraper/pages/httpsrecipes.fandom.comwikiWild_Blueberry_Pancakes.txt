Description : 
 Recipe from the Victorian by the Sea, Camden Maine
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Serves: 4 to 6 
 Ingredients : 
 2  eggs , lightly beaten 
 2Â½ cups non-fat  buttermilk 
 4 tablespoons  butter , melted 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 2 cups  flour 
 2 teaspoons  sugar 
 1 tablespoon  baking powder 
 1 teaspoon  baking soda 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 blueberries 
 Directions : 
 Preheat griddle. 
 Melt butter and set aside to cool. 
 In a small bowl, mix eggs, buttermilk, and vanilla. 
 In a large bowl, combine dry ingredients. 
 Add egg mixture to dry ingredients. 
 Add melted butter and mix just until blended. 
 Do not over mix. 
 Using a large spoon or ladle, pour batter onto buttered griddle. 
 Immediately sprinkle pancakes with blueberries. 
 Flip once, when pancake begins to brown. 
 Serve hot with maple syrup.
