      Potato Rolls     
 Ingredients : 
 10  potatoes 
 50 g  fat 
 1  onion 
 salt 
 oil  to fry 
 Directions : 
 Peel  potatoes  and grate finely, squeeze out the juice and combine with  salt  and chopped fat. 
 Shape small rounds and fry in  oil . 
 Serve hot with fried chopped  onion .
