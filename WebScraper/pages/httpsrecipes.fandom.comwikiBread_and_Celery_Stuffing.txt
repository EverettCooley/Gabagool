Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Wagner Estate in Tulsa, Oklahoma in 1991. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 7½ ounces unseasoned stuffing cubes 
 1 cup  celery  sliced 
 ½ cup  onions 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  poultry seasoning 
 ¼ teaspoon  black pepper 
 1½ cups  chicken broth 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 425°F. 
 Spray square baking dish with cooking spray then set aside. 
 In a skillet cook celery and onions until tender then remove from heat. 
 Stir in stuffing cubes, salt, poultry seasoning and black pepper. 
 Drizzle broth half cup at a time to moisten tossing lightly. 
 Spread mixture into prepared dish and bake for 30 minutes.
