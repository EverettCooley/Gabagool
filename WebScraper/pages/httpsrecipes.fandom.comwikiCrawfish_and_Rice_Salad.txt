 Description : 
 Makes 4 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound cooked  Crawfish  tails 
 2 cups cooked  rice 
 1 cup sliced  celery 
 1/3 cup sliced  ripe olives 
 1/3 cup diced green  bell pepper 
 1/2 cup quartered  cherry tomatoes 
 1/2 cup Italian dressing* 
 lettuce  leaves 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine  Crawfish ,  rice ,  celery ,  green pepper ,  tomatoes  and dressing in large bowl. Serve over  lettuce  leaves
