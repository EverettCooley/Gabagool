Makes 4 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 pound ground  Beef 
 1 medium  Onion , chopped 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 medium  tomato , seeded and chopped 
 2  green onions , sliced 
 1/3 cup stuffed  green olives , chopped 
 1/3 cup  Raisins 
 2 teaspoons  chili powder 
 1/2 teaspoon  cumin 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 4 8-inch pie crusts 
 Directions : 
 Brown  Beef  in large skillet over medium heat until thoroughly cooked. Drain fat. 
 Add  Onion  and  garlic ; saute 2 to 3 minutes or until  Onion  is tender. 
 Add  rice ,  tomato ,  green onions ,  Raisins ,  chili powder ,  cumin ,  salt  and  pepper . Cook and stir over medium heat 5 minutes or until ingredient's flavors are well blended. 
 Spoon 1-1/4 cup filling on half of each pastry round. 
 Fold pastry over filling. Seal and crimp edges. 
 Bake at 400 degrees 20 to 25 minutes or until golden.
