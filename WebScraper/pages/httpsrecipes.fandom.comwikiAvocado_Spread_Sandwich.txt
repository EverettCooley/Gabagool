 Description : 
 This a wonderful vegetarian sandwich and a great summer time lunch.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 Slices of Classic Black  bread  (or your favorite  bread ) 
 Â½ Medium Size  avocado 
 1 tsp  Mayonnaise 
 2 Large Slices of Ripe  tomato 
 5 Slices of  cucumber 
 1 Large Roman  lettuce  Leaf 
 Directions : 
 In a small bowl mix  avocado  and  Mayonnaise . 
 Spread  avocado  mix on 1 slice of  bread  then top with  tomatoes ,  cucumber  and  lettuce . 
 Then top with second slice of  bread .
