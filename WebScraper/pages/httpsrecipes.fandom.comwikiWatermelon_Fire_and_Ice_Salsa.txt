Ingredients : 
 3 cup chopped  watermelon 
 ½ cup  green bell peppers , chopped 
 2 tbsp  lime juice 
 1 tbsp chopped  cilantro 
 1 tbsp chopped  green onion 
 1 tbsp  jalapeno peppers , chopped, use up to 2 tbsp chopped jalapenos, depending on taste 
 ½ tsp  garlic salt 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients. 
 Cover and refrigerate 1 hour. 
 Serve on sliced  oranges  or cheese-filled  manicotti . Or, top 1 cup  sour cream  with 1 cup salsa and serve with chips. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving: ½ cup = calories ~ 31, protein - 1 g., fat - .4 g., carbohydrates - 7 g., cholesterol - 0, sodium - 156 mg
