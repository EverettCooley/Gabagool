 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 4  Bacon  slices, cooked and crumbled 
 1/2 cup  pecans , coarsely chopped and toasted 
 salt  &  pepper  to taste 
 1/2 cup chopped  celery 
 1 cup  Red Grapes , halved 
 1 cup  jicama , chopped and peeled 
 4 cups  Chicken , cubed and cooked 
 1/4 cup  mango  chutney 
 3/4 cup  Mayonnaise 
 Directions : 
 Mix together  Mayonnaise  and chutney. Add all other ingredients except  pecans  and  Bacon . Mix lightly. Season to taste. Refrigerate. Add  pecans  and  Bacon  just before serving.
