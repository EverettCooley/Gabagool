Description : 
 A TNT recipe. This is a good recipe. Monique and Dad gave it a 5 star.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: my old recipes 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  ground beef 
 1 large  onion , chopped 
 1 can  corn , drained (12 oz) 
 6 tbsp A-1  steak sauce 
 2 cups grated  jack cheese  (8 oz) 
 2 cans Ortega diced  green chilies  (4 oz each or 1 can, 7 oz) 
 1½ cups  milk 
 ¼ cup  flour 
 4  eggs 
 Directions : 
 Cook  beef  until crumbly in skillet. 
 Push to one side. 
 Saut?  onion  in drippings in skillet until soft. 
 Stir in  corn  and a-1  steak  sauce. 
 Layer half the  cheese  in greased 12 cup casserole. 
 Then layer half the meat mixture and half the chilies. 
 Repeat layers. 
 Mix  milk ,  flour ,  eggs  in bowl. 
 Beat until well blended. 
 Pour over layers in casserole if you let the casserole chill in the refrigerator 1 hour or more, it will be puffier, but it can go right into the oven. 
 Bake at 350°F for 1 hour or until casserole is puffed. 
 Serve at once.
