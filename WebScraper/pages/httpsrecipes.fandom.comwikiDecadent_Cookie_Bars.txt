Ingredients : 
 2 cups  flour 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 1 cup  shortening 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 1½ cup packed  brown sugar 
 2  eggs 
 2 tbsp  kahlúa   or   milk 
 1 tsp  vanilla extract 
 ¾ cup Nestle's®  chocolate chips 
 ¾ cup  peanut butter chips 
 ¾ cup toffee pieces or regular sized health bars, crushed up 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 Grease a 13 x 9 inch square baking pan. 
 In large bowl,  cream   shortening ,  eggs  and  brown sugar . 
 Add in ( kahlúa  or  milk ) and  vanilla extract  and mix well. 
 Gradually add in  flour ,  baking powder ,  baking soda , and  salt  a little at a time. 
 Mix until well blended. 
 Fold in  chocolate chips ,  peanut butter chips  and toffee chips (or crushed heath bars) and stir until chips are evenly distributed in dough. 
 Spread dough in pan and bake 25 to 30 minutes. 
 Let cool before cutting into bars.
