Ingredients : 
 1 teaspoon  dry yeast 
 2½ cups warm  water 
 4 cups  flour 
 1 teaspoon  baking powder 
 oil 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve the  yeast  in the  water , add it to the  flour , and mix well. 
 Let the mixture stand at room temperature overnight. 
 (in winter it takes 2 days to allow fermentation. 
 ). 
 Stir in the  baking powder  and let the mixture stand for 10 minutes. 
 Put about ½ teaspoon  oil  in large skillet, add about ½ cup of the batter, and fry over low heat for 1 or 2 minutes. 
 When bubbles appear, cover the skillet for 15 seconds. 
 Turn out the pancake to a dish. 
 Prepare all the pancakes this way, frying on one side only.
