Description : 
 Great treat for the kids at Christmas or to give as gifts im not sure of the exact amount it makes I've never measured it. For gifts give in tins, jars, or coffee cans covered in gift wrap. 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 16 ounces  peanut butter 
 6 ounces  semi-sweet chocolate  chips 
 Â½ cup of melted  margarine 
 1 (12 ounces) box  Rice Chex 
 16 ounces dry roasted  peanuts 
 15 ounces  raisins 
 1 box of 10 x  confectioners' sugar 
 Directions : 
 Melt the  margarine , [[peanut [butter]], and  chocolate  together, stir on low heat until creamy in extra large pan mix cereal, peanuts ,  raisins  together and pour the  chocolate  mixture over it, toss gently to coat pour the sugar into 1 clean white plastic trash bag, then add the cereal mix toss to coat. 
 Let dry. 
 Store in air tight container.
