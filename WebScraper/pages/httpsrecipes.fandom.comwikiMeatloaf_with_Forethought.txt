Description : 
 This recipe says not to have the gravy with the meatloaf, but have baked potatoes with the meatloaf. Have gravy later in the week over rice, noodles, or cold meatloaf. I think I'd have it over whatever I wanted. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: My old recipes 
 Serves 4 to 6 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  hamburger 
 1 cup uncooked  oatmeal 
 1 beaten  egg 
 1 medium  onion , chopped 
 2 tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 ¼ tsp  poultry seasoning 
 ¾ cup  milk 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients together thoroughly. 
 Form into loaf in heavy skillet and bake at 350°F for 1 hour. 
 When done, gently remove loaf form skillet to serving plate and let sit for a few minutes before slicing. 
 Put skillet on top of stove and adjust heat to medium. 
 Add 2 cups water to drippings. 
 Stir well, bring to boil. 
 In a jar with tight fitting lid, put 3 tsp flour land ½ cup water. 
 Shake until mixture is smooth, then pour slowly into skillet. 
 Stir constantly until gravy has thickened. 
 Reduce heat. 
 Simmer 5 minutes. 
 Season.
