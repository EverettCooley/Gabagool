Sweet Surprise Cookies
 1 (7 ounce) jar Marshmallow Creme 
1 cup peanut butter 
1 1/2 cups Rice Krispies 
White or chocolate almond bark, melted
 Mix first three ingredients together; roll into balls. Dip balls in 
melted almond bark; Place on wax paper until dry.
