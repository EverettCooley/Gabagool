Description : 
 Makes 24 paddies 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  ground pork 
 2  eggs , slightly beaten 
 1 cup  cracker meal 
 1 cup  milk 
 1 tbsp  parsley 
 ⅛ tsp  pepper 
 2 tsp  salt 
 1 medium  onion , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients together. 
 Shape into large meatballs and shape into paddies. 
 Place onto greased cookie sheet and brown on both sides at 375°F. 
 Remove from oven, and place in baking dish and bake ¾ hours longer in oven at 325°F. 
 Bake them on the middle shelf of oven.
