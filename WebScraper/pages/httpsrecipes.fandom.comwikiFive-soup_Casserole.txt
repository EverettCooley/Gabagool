Description : 
 Contributed by Jenn b Aka Mom2sam And Tiny at  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  ground beef 
 1 chopped  green pepper 
 1 cup  celery , chopped fine 
 2  green onion , use the whole stalk 
 8 oz  pasta shells  or noodles of your choice (i use noodles) 
 1 can undiluted  mushroom soup 
 1 can undiluted  tomato soup 
 1 can undiluted  cream of chicken  soup 
 1 can undiluted  onion soup 
 2 cans undiluted  cream of celery  soup 
 shredded  cheddar  cheese (to taste) 
 crushed  potato chips  or dry  chinese noodles , fried  onion  pieces 
 Directions : 
 Cook the hamburger with  green pepper ,  celery ,  onion 
 Drain, Set Aside. 
 Boil the  pasta  and set aside. 
 Mix all the soups together in saucepan, do not dilute 
 Heat thoroughly and blend. The soups will look awful, but the taste is unbelievable. 
 Mix all ingredients together in a large casserole and bake at 350 °F for 1 hour or until bubbly. 
 Return to oven with  cheese  and other toppings and bake another 5 minutes. 
 Let casserole set for 15 minutes before serving to let flavors blend.
