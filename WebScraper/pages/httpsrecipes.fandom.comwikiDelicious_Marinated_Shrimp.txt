 Ingredients : 
 1½ pound fresh  shrimp , peeled 
 2 cloves  garlic  (or ½ teaspoon  garlic powder ) 
 ¼ cup  soy sauce 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ½ cup  oil 
 ½ cup  lemon juice 
 3 tablespoons chopped  parsley  (fresh or dried) 
 3 tablespoons chopped  onion 
 ½ teaspoon  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Soak bamboo skewers in  water  for at least 10 minutes before using. 
 This will help them not to burn or stick to the  shrimp . 
 Skewer shrimp-about 3 to 5 per skewer, depending on the size. 
 Place in a 13 x 9 inch baking pan. 
 Mix the marinade ingredients together. 
 Pour over skewered  shrimp . 
 Cover pan and place in refrigerator for at least 3 hours; turning the  shrimp  or spooning the marinade over the  shrimp  at least once during that time. 
 Grease and preheat George Foreman grill. Grill until  shrimp  turns pink. It doesn't take long; watch carefully.
