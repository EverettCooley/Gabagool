 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 Peeled  cucumber 
 Large red  tomatoes 
 8oz fresh mozarrella 
 Fresh  basil  leaves 
 2 tablespoon  olive oil 
 1 tablespoon Carlson Cod Liver  oil 
 1/4 cup Balsimic  vinegar 
 Directions : 
 Slice the  cucumber  and  tomatoes . 
 Alternate layering the mozazrella,  cucumber  and  tomatoes . 
 Mix  olive oil  and  vinegar  in a small cup. 
 Evenly distribute over  cucumbers  and  tomatoes . 
 Top with fresh  basil .
