Chocolate Chip Sensation
 1 pkg refrigerated chocolate chip cookie dough (18 oz)
8 oz. cream cheese softened 
1/3 cup sugar 
4 ounces chocolate pudding mix (instant)
2 cups half and half 
1/4 cup chopped pecans 
12 ounces cool whip
 Preheat oven 375F. Shape cookie dough in ball. Place in center of 14 
inch pizza pan. Pat dough in a 13 inch circle. Bake 12 to 15 min. Let 
it cool. Combine cream cheese and sugar in medium bowl and mix until 
smooth. Whisk the pudding mix and half and half in large mixing bowl 
until blended. Let stand for 5 min or until thickened. Spread cookie 
crust evenly with cream cheese mixture. Top with pudding mix, then top 
with cool whip topping. Sprinkle with pecans. Chill until serving time. 
Cut into wedges.
