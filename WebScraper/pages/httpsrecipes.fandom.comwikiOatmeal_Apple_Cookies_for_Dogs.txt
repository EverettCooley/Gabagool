Description : 
 Doggie biscuits
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Makes 5 – 6 dozen cookies 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups  whole wheat flour 
 2 cups old fashioned  oatmeal 
 1¼ cups  water 
 ¼ cup minced  apple 
 3 tbsp  cinnamon 
 2 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 2 tbsp  honey 
 1 tbsp ground  cloves 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 325°F. 
 Place all ingredients in a bowl. Mix until combined to form dough. 
 Roll out dough on floured surface to about ¼ inch thick. 
 Using a cookie cutter, cut into bone shapes. 
 Combine dough scraps, continue to roll out and cut into shapes until all dough has been used. 
 Place cookies on ungreased foil lined baking sheets. 
 Bake 35 minutes.
