Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  cocoa  or  carob  powder 
 ¼ cup  potato flour 
 ¼ cup  rice flour 
 ½ cup  white sugar 
 ⅜ cup stick  margarine 
 1  egg 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. 
 Grease cookie sheet. 
 Mix dry ingredients together. 
 Beat  egg . 
 Mix  egg  and  margarine  together; mix in with dry ingredients (may have to use hands). 
 Form dough into 1-inch cylinder-shaped balls and place on cookie sheet. 
 Flatten with a fork dipped in cold  water . 
 Bake at 350 °F for 12 – 15 minutes. 
 Once cooled, put frosting between the smooth halves of the cookies and sandwich two together.
