 Dosas  are a crispy, crepe-like southern Indian specialty made from a mixture of rice and lentils soaked and ground into a thin batter, then cooked on a griddle. A dosa filled with sauteed spiced potatoes and served with  sambar  or  rasam  and  chutney  is called a  masala dosa' ,  a very popular snack.
