Description : 
 Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 package (3 ounces)  ladyfingers 
 1 quart  ice cream , in assorted flavors 
 2  bananas , split lengthwise 
 whipped cream 
 Garnish: frozen whole California  strawberries  (optional) 
 Strawberry Sauce : 
 1 x 10 oz. package frozen, sliced  strawberries 
 2 tablespoons  sugar 
 ½ tablespoon  cornstarch 
 2 teaspoons  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Arrange 16 lady finger halves diagonally around side of 9-inch pie plate. 
 Crumble remaining  ladyfingers  and press in an even layer in bottom of pan. 
 Gently spread ¼ cup  strawberry  sauce over crumbs. 
 Freeze. 
 Just before serving time, arrange scoops of  ice cream  over  bananas , in frozen pie shell. 
 Drizzle remaining sauce on  ice cream . 
 Garnish with  whipped cream  and whole  strawberries , if desired. 
 Cut into wedges; serve immediately. 
 Strawberry Sauce : 
 Slightly thaw 1 package (10 ounces) frozen sliced  strawberries  in syrup; pour into saucepan. 
 Mix together 2 tablespoons of  sugar  and ½ tablespoon  cornstarch . 
 Stir into  strawberries . 
 Bring to boil. 
 Cook and stir 2 minutes, or until sauce is slightly thick. 
 Remove from heat. 
 Add 2 teaspoons  lemon juice . 
 Chill.
