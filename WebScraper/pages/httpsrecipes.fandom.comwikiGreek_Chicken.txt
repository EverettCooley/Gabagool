 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound boneless  Chicken 
 1/2 cup crumbled  feta   Cheese  (3 grams of carbs) 
 1/2 tsp dried  oregano  (0.5 grams of carbs) 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice  (1.3 grams of carbs) 
 1 tbsp  oil 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 1 cup  chicken broth 
 1/2 cup  tomato  diced (5.8 grams of carbs) 
 1 cup fresh  spinach  (2.4 grams of carbs) 
 Directions : 
 Flatten  Chicken . Combine  feta ,  lemon juice  and  oregano . Spread over  Chicken . Fold  Chicken  to enclose filling; secure with a toothpick. Heat  oil  in a skillet until hot. Add  Chicken  and cook until golden brown. Mix  chicken broth ,  tomato  and  spinach . Add to skillet, heat to boiling. Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer 8â€“10 minutes. Serve.
