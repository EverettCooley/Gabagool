 Description : 
 I have made this on several occasions and it is very good. It came from my mother's collection and she says she has been making this before I was born, which is 40? years ago. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 10 slices day old bread, torn into pieces 
 4  eggs , well beaten 
 1/8 stick unsalted  butter 
 1/r cup  granulated sugar 
 ½ teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 ¼ teaspoon  nutmeg 
 1 can crushed  pineapple  with juice 
 1 cup  raisins 
 ½ cup  chocolate chips . 
 Directions : 
 Butter  2 quart pan. Put bread into bowl along with spices. 
 Toss lightly. Add  eggs  and mix lightly. 
 Blend in  pineapple . Gently stir in  raisins  and  chocolate chips . 
 Sprinkle with  cinnamon  and  Sugar  mixture. 
 Bake at 350 for about 30 minutes.
