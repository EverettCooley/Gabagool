Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 c. canned crushed  pineapple  with juice 
 3 frozen  bananas  (peel, wrap in plastic wrap, place in freezer at least 12 hours.) 
 3 c. (16 oz.) bag frozen  strawberries 
 Directions : 
 Place crushed pineapple and juice in blender. 
 Add frozen fruit a little at a time, blending between each addition. 
 Scoop 1/2 cup serving into dessert dishes and freeze 15 to 30 minutes. (If frozen longer remove a few minutes before serving to soften). 
 Notes : 
 Alternate use, do not freeze, but refrigerate. You can use spooned over angel food cake in individual serving dishes. 
 Keeps in refrigerator about 1 or 2 days. 
 
Enjoy.
