Description : 
 Contributed by  PressureCookerRecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  golden raisins 
 ½ cup dry  red wine  or red  grape juice 
 ¼ cup chopped  nuts 
 2 tablespoons  sugar 
 ½ teaspoon grated  orange peel 
 ½ teaspoon  cinnamon 
 4 cooking  apples 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 1 cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Soak raisins in wine or grape juice for 30 minutes. 
 Drain, reserving wine or juice. 
 Combine raisins, nuts, sugar, orange rind and cinnamon. 
 Core apples, cutting to, but not through, the bottoms so filling won't fall through. 
 Pare top ⅓ of each apple. 
 Place each apple on a large square of aluminum foil (large enough to completely wrap an apple). 
 Fill centers with raisin mixture. 
 Divide tablespoon of butter into 4 pieces and top each apple with one piece. 
 Wrap foil around apple, pinching firmly together at top. 
 Set cooking rack in cooker and pour in reserved wine and 1 cup of water. 
 Place apples on rack. 
 Lock lid and bring to full pressure. 
 Adjust heat to maintain pressure and cook for 10 minutes. 
 Quick-release pressure and remove apples. 
 Unwrap and serve.
