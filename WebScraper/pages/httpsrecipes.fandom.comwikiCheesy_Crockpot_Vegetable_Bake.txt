To Thaw Vegetables Quickly, Place in Colander & Run  water  Over Them.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 Cups Frozen  Broccoli ,  Cauliflower , &  carrot  Blend Or Similar Vegetable Mixture, Thawed 
 1/2 Cup Chopped  Onion 
 1 Can (10 3/4 Ounces) Condensed Cream of  Mushroom  Soup, Regular OrLow Fat 
 1 Jar (2 Ounces) Chopped pimentos, Drained 
 2 Cups Shredded  American cheese 
 Directions : 
 Spray Slow Cooker With  cooking spray .
 Combine Vegetables, Soup,  pimento ,  Onion , &  Cheese  in Slow Cooker.Cover & Cook on Low For 4 to 6 Hours. Stir Well Before Serving.Serves 4 to 6.
