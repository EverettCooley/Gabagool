Ingredients : 
 600 g  plantain 
 200 g  cassava 
 Directions : 
 In unsalted boiling  water , cook a and b, peeled and sliced for about 15 to 20 minutes. 
 Remove the pan from the heat, and let them cool down in the cooking liquid. 
 Drain a and grind until smooth and free of any lumps then add b and mash until smooth.
