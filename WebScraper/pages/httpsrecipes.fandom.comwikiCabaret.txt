      Cabaret     
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz  gin 
 ½ oz  dry vermouth 
 ½ oz  Bénédictine   herbal liqueur 
 2 dashes  bitters 
 1  maraschino cherry 
 Directions : 
 Fill a shaker with  ice , add  gin ,  vermouth ,  Bénédictine  and  bitters , then shake and strain into a glass. 
 Garnish with a  cherry .
