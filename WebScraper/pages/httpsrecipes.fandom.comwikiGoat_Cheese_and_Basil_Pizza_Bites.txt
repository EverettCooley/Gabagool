This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Henson Estate in Dallas, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 package  goat cheese 
 2 cloves  garlic  minced finely 
 1 teaspoon dried  oregano  leaves crushed 
 1 teaspoon dried  parsley  leaves crushed 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 1 small  French bread  loaf 
 2  plum tomatoes  thinly sliced 
 olive oil 
 ½ cup shredded fresh  basil  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 400°F. 
 Combine goat  cheese ,  garlic ,  oregano , parsley and  pepper . Mix well. 
 Cut  bread  into 12 slices. 
 Top each slice with  tomato  slice and brush with  olive oil . 
 Place a teaspoon cheese mixture on top of each  tomato  slice and bake 10 minutes. 
 Remove from oven and top with shredded  basil .
