Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ oz  light rum 
 ¼ oz  gin 
 ¼ oz  vodka 
 ¼ oz  tequila 
 ¼ oz blue  Curaçao 
 1 dash  cherry brandy 
 3 oz sour mix 
 3 oz  orange juice 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Pour over  ice  into hurricane glass and stir well. 
 Garnish with an  orange  wheel.
