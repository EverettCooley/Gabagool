Description : 
 Purchased from DeYoung Estate in Tulsa, Oklahoma in 1982. Dated 1937.
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound flaked  crabmeat 
 1 can pickled  jalapeno peppers 
 2 tablespoons  green pepper , finely chopped 
 2 tablespoons  red onion , finely chopped 
 ¼ cup  cracker  meal 
 1  egg , beaten 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  black pepper 
 ⅛ teaspoon  cayenne pepper 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 ¼ cup  milk 
 2 cups cracker meal 
 1 cup  milk 
 2  eggs 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Cut  peppers  in half lengthwise. 
 Discard pulp and seeds and rinse carefully. 
 In a large mixing bowl, combine remaining ingredients except breading mixture. 
 Stuff  pepper  halves with crab mixture and press stuffing around  pepper . 
 Set  peppers  aside. 
 To prepare breading mixture, place cracker meal in a flat pan; mix together  milk ,  eggs  and  salt  and  pepper . 
 Dip  peppers  in  egg  mixture, then in cracker meal. 
 Repeat procedure. 
 Deep fry at 365°F until golden brown. 
 Drain well.
