 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 teacup finely chopped paneer ( cottage cheese ), 
 1 teacup thick fresh  yoghurt , 
 2 teaspoons chopped  Onion , 
 2 teaspoons chopped  celery , 
 2 teaspoons chopped  capsicum , 
 2 pinches of  chilli powder , 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt , 
 Cheese  crackers, 
 cucumber  sticks, 
 capsicum  sticks and  carrot  sticks to serve. 
 Directions : 
 Mix the paneer,  yoghurt ,  Onion ,  celery ,  capsicum ,  chilli powder  and  salt  and chill thoroughly. 
 Serve cold with  Cheese  crackers,  cucumber  sticks,  capsicum  sticks and  carrot  sticks.
