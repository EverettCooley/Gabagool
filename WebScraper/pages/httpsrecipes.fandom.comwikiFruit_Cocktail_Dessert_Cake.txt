Fruit Cocktail Dessert Cake
 
 1 1/2 C.  flour 
 1 C.  sugar 
 1 Tsp. Soda 
 1/2 Tsp.  salt 
 
Sift Together. Add: 1/2 Tsp.  almond extract  1 (#303)Can Fruit Cocktail, Less 2 Tbsp. Juice
 
Mix Above Ingredients. Put in 9 x 13 Inch Pan. TopWith 1/2 Cup  brown sugar  And 1/2 Cup Nuts. Bake 45-55Minutes at 325 Degrees. Serve With  Cool Whip  Or
 whipped cream , if Desired.
 
Enjoy.
