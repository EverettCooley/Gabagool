 Description : 
 Purchased from the Rosenthal Estate in Montgomery, Alabama in 1989. Date and source unknown. I have made this on special occasions and it is extremely good. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 12 pound precooked  Ham 
 2 teaspoons  cinnamon 
 5 or 6  maraschino cherries 
 ½ box  brown sugar 
 8 ounce can sliced  pineapple 
 ½ quart  beer 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Place  Ham  in a large pan. Rub  brown sugar  and  cinnamon  into meat. 
 Place  pineapple  slices and  cherries  on  Ham  with toothpicks. 
 Pour  beer  over  Ham  and baste while baking. Bake 3 hours at 325.
