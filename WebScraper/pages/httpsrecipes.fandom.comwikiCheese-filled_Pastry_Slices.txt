Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 16 oz  cream cheese , softened 
 ¾ cup  sugar 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 1 tsp  lemon juice 
 1  egg , separated 
 2 x 8 oz packages  crescent roll  dough 
 powdered sugar 
 Directions : 
 In medium bowl, combine cream cheese, sugar, vanilla, juice and egg yolk. 
 Mix until smooth. 
 With rolling pin, flatten 1 package of rolls, closing up seams. 
 Cover bottom of greased 9 x 13-inch baking dish with rolled pastry. 
 Pour in cream cheese mixture. 
 Roll out remaining dough on waxed paper to 9 x 13 inches. 
 Invert pastry over cheese mixture. 
 Remove waxed paper. 
 Beat egg white until frothy. 
 Spread over top crust. 
 Bake in preheated 350°F oven for 30 minutes or until golden. 
 Cool. 
 Sprinkle with powdered sugar.
