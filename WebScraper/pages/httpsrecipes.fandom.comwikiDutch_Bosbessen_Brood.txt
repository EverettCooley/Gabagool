Description : 
 Dutch blueberry bread. A delicious way to use those summer  Berries . 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  sugar 
 2  eggs 
 2 cups  flour 
 3 tablespoons grated  orange rind 
 3 tablespoons  butter 
 1 cup  blueberries 
 ¼ cup boiling  water 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon  baking soda 
 ½ cup  orange juice  (reserve 1 tbsp for glaze) 
 ½ cup  powdered sugar 
 Directions : 
 Mix  water  and  butter . 
 Add  orange juice  and rind. 
 Beat  eggs  in  sugar . 
 Add  eggs  to  orange juice  mixture. 
 Add dry ingredients, then add  blueberries . 
 Bake at 350°F for 1 hour. 
 Make a glaze of ½ cup  powdered sugar , 1 tbsp  orange juice  and 1 tbsp hot  water . 
 Drizzle over hot bread.
