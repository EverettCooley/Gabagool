Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 Crust : 
 1 stick  butter , softened 
 1 cup  plain flour 
 1 cup crushed  pecans 
 Topping : 
 8 oz  cream cheese 
 1 cup  confectioners' sugar 
 8 oz  Cool Whip 
 2 small packages chocolate  instant pudding 
 2 cups  milk 
 Directions : 
 Crust : 
 Combine crust ingredients and press into 9 x 12 inch pan. 
 Bake at 350Â°F degrees for 20 minutes. 
 Cool. 
 Topping : 
 Whip cream cheese and sugar until fluffy. 
 Fold in 1 cup Cool Whip and blend well. 
 Spread over cooled crust. 
 Add pudding to milk. 
 Beat until almost thick. 
 Pour over cheese layer. 
 Top with remaining cool whip.
