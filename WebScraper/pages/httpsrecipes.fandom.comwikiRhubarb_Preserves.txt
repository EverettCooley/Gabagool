Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Steep Estate in Elmo, Texas in 1989.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 5 cups  rhubarb  chopped 
 5 cups  granulated sugar 
 1 cup  pineapple  crushed drained 
 2 boxes  strawberry gelatin 
 Directions : 
 Mix  rhubarb  and  sugar  then let set overnight. 
 Add  pineapple  and gelatin and boil for 10 minutes. 
 Put in hot glass jars then cool then put in refrigerator.
