Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup  shortening 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1  egg 
 1 cup mashed  banana 
 1 cup raw  oatmeal 
 1½ cups  flour 
 ½ teaspoon  soda 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  nutmeg 
 ¾ teaspoon  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 400°F (205°C). 
 cream   shortening  and  sugar . 
 Add  egg  and beat thoroughly. 
 Mix in  bananas  and  oats . 
 Sift together remaining ingredients and add to the  banana  mixture. 
 Drop by teaspoonfuls onto an oiled cookie sheet about 1½" apart. 
 Bake for about 12 minutes.
