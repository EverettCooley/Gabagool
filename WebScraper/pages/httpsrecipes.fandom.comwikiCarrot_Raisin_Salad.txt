 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 c.  Grated Carrots 
 3/4 c.  raisins  (I use golden) 
 1/3 c. real  Mayonnaise 
 1 tsp  Sugar 
 1/4 tsp  salt 
 2 T. evaporated skim  milk , undiluted 
 1 T.  lemon  juice 
 Directions : 
 Combine  carrots  and  raisins  in a large bowl. 
 Mix remaining ingredients together, toss with  carrot  mixture. 
 Chill well before serving. Makes 6 servings
