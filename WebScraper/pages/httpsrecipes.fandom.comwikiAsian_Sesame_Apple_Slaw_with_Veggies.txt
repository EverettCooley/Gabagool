Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 8 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup seasoned  rice vinegar 
 2 tbs dark  sesame oil 
 1 tbs  honey 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 2 tsp  jalapeno pepper , seeded and minced 
 1 each  Gala ,  golden , and  Granny Smith apple , thinly sliced 
 8 cups finely shredded  cabbage 
 1  red bell pepper , cut in thin strips 
 ½  seedless cucumber , cut in thin strips 
 3  scallions , thinly sliced 
 ¾ cup  cilantro 
 2 tbsp toasted  sesame seeds 
 3 oz container  radish sprouts 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl, whisk first 5 ingredients until blended. 
 Add apple slices and toss to coat. 
 Add remaining ingredients, except radish sprouts, and toss to coat. 
 Before serving — scatter radish sprouts over the slaw. Toss.
