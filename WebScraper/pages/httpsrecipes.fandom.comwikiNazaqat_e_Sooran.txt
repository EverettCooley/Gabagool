Description : 
 Nazaqat-e-sooran. This delicate dish can be served with tomato ketchup but best with sesame seed chutney.
 
 from: monty 10:24, 17 March 2007 (MST) 
 Please mail the comments regarding the recipe at dagguisme@gmail.com 
 Ingredients : 
 1 kg  yam  (sooran) 
 100 g  onion  paste 
 50 g  pistachios 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 1 tsp  ginger  paste 
 2 g  asafoetida 
 butter  to shallow fry 
 ½ tsp  cardamom  powder 
 ¼ tsp  mace  powder 
 100 g mashed  cottage cheese  ( paneer ) 
 1 tsp roasted  cumin 
 1 tsp  coriander 
 Directions : 
 Boil yam till cooked. 
 Peel and mash. 
 Add all the ingredients except butter & pistachios to mashed yam. 
 Form the mixture in to balls. 
 Stuff with pistachios and flatten. 
 Shallow fry on girdle and serve hot.
