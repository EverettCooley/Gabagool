 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 large vadalia  onions 
 2 tbsp  butter 
 -=or=- 
 2 tbsp  margarine 
 1/4 cup  garlic  breadcrumbs 
 Directions : 
 Cut  Onion  down to the root end in 8 wedges. Do not cut through the root so  Onion  stays together. Divide  butter  in 4 pats and place one on top of each  Onion . Wrap each  Onion  separately in foil. Sprinkle with crumbs and  salt  and lots of freshly ground  pepper  to taste. Bake at 350 °F for 45 minutes or until  onions  are soft.
