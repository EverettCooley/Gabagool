Description : 
 Excellent when served on buttery  croissants , with fruit salad And  mint  tea. Great recipe for weekend get-togethers or showers.
 
 Contributed by Jeannine English at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4  chicken breasts 
 8 oz  cream cheese , softened 
 4 stalks  celery , finely chopped 
 1 tbsp  pimento , finely chopped 
 1 small  green bell pepper , finely chopped 
 1 tbsp  Dijon mustard 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Dried  parsley , optional 
 Directions : 
 Boil  chicken  until tender, then chop into small pieces. 
 Combine  chicken  and  cream cheese ; mix until smooth. 
 Add other ingredients and chill.
