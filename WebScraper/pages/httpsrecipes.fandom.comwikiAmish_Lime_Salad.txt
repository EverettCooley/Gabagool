      Amish Lime Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 16  marshmallows 
 1 cup  milk 
 1 package  lime gelatin 
 2 packages  cream cheese 
 1 can crushed  pineapple 
 â…” cup  salad dressing 
 1 cup  whipped cream 
 Directions : 
 Melt the  marshmallows  and  milk . 
 Pour that mixture over the  gelatin , stirring it. 
 Stir in the  cream cheese . 
 Add the  pineapple  and cool the mixture. 
 Add  salad dressing  and  whipped cream  and chill until firm.
