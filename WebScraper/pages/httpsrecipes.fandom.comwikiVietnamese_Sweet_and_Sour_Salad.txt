Description : 
 Vietnamese sweet and sour salad is a salad easy to prepare.
 
       Sweet And Sour Salad     
 Ingredients : 
 onion 
 radish 
 carrot 
 celery 
 bell pepper 
 cucumbers 
 cabbage 
 Dressing : 
 vinegar 
 water 
 sugar 
 Directions : 
 Mix  onion ,  radish ,  carrot ,  celery ,  bell pepper ,  cucumbers ,  cabbage . 
 Toss the ingredients well with salad.
