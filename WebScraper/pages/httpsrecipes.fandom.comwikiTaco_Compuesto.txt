 Description : 
 From  The Better Homes and Gardens Diabetic Cook Book 
 
 Time 20 minutes 
 Makes 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ lbs lean  ground beef 
 ½ cup  onion , chopped 
 2 tbsp green  chile peppers , diced, canned 
 1 clove  garlic 
 1 tsp  chili powder 
 1½ cups  tomato , chopped 
 3 tbsp  Italian salad dressing , reduced-calorie 
 ½ tsp  seasoned salt 
 8  taco shells 
 1 cup  lettuce 
 1 cup low-calories  processed cheese  or part-skim  mozzarella  cheese, shredded or a mixture of both 
 ½ cup  taco sauce 
 Directions : 
 In a large skillet cook  ground beef , chopped  onions , green  chili pepper ; and minced  garlic  over medium heat till  beef  is brown. 
 Drain off fat. 
 Stir in  chili powder . 
 In a mixing bowl combine chopped  tomato ,  Italian salad dressing , and seasoned  salt . spoon  beef  mixture into taco shells. 
 Top with  tomato  mixture, shredded  lettuce , shredded  cheese , and taco sauce. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 Food exchanges: 3 lean meat exchanges + 1 starch/bread exchange + 1 vegetable exchange + 1 ½ fat exchanges
