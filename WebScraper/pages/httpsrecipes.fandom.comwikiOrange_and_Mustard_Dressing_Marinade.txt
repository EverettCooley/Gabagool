Ingredients : 
 3 each  oranges 
 2 tsp wholegrain seeded  mustard 
 Directions : 
 Juice the  oranges . Mix with  mustard . Heat in microwave until warm. Great for salad dressings or as a marinade on  Chicken  when grilling.
