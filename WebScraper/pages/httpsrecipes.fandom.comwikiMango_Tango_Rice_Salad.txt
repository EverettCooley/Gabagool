Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 (15½ ounce) can  black beans , drained and rinsed 
 1  mango , chopped 
 ⅓ cup chopped fresh  cilantro 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅓ cup  Orange Vinaigrette  salad dressing 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl, combine  rice ,  beans ,  mango ,  cilantro  and  salt . 
 Add vinaigrette; toss well.
