 Description : 
 A snack dish with sweet and salty taste.
 
 Ingredients : 
 250g of  wheat flour 
 1/4 tsp  baking powder 
 250ml  water 
 1/2 tsp  salt 
 50g  Sultanas  /  dates  (stoneless) 
 125g  butter 
 4 tbsp  Sugar 
 Directions : 
 Sift  flour  into bowl. 
 Add  baking powder ,  salt  and  Sultanas  /  dates  and stir in  water  until thick batter. 
 Pour ladle-full of batter onto non-stick flat frying pan and flatten out until evenly spread. 
 Fry at medium heat and flip when golden on one side. 
 Melt  butter  and pour onto cooked gorraasa and cover with  Sugar .
