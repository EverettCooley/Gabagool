Description 
 Poultry Recipes  by the South Carolina Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Serves : 4 
 Ingredients 
 1 package (10 oz.) frozen  broccoli  spears, cooked 
 2 cups (½-inch) cubed cooked  turkey 
 1 can (10½ oz.) reduced-sodium  cream of mushroom  soup 
 ¼ cup reduced-calorie  mayonnaise 
 1½ teaspoons  lemon juice 
 1 teaspoon  curry powder 
 croutons 
 Directions 
 In 8-inch square baking dish layer broccoli, top with turkey. 
 In small bowl, combine soup, mayonnaise, lemon juice and curry powder. 
 Pour over turkey and top with croutons. 
 Bake at 350 °F for 20 to 30 minutes or until bubbly.
