Ingredients : 
 1/3 cup  vodka 
 2 tablespoons  triple sec 
 2/3 cup  blueberries 
 1 tablespoon  lime juice 
 12  ice  cubes 
 2/3 cup  raspberry  sorbet 
 Directions : 
 Combine  vodka ,  triple sec ,  blueberries  and  lime juice  in blender. Add  ice  and process.
