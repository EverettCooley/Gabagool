 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 parts  rice  cereal for baby 
 1 part ripe  California avocado  cubes 
 1 part baby's prepared fruit or pureed fresh fruit 
 Directions : 
 Pour desired amount of cereal into bowl.
 Mix in liquid (formula,  milk ,or  water  to desired consistency.
 Puree desired amount of  avocado  and add to cereal.
 Add choice fruit, swirl ingredients and server.
 HINT: To puree  avocado , place chunks in a small bowl. Mash with fork and puree using back of spoon.
