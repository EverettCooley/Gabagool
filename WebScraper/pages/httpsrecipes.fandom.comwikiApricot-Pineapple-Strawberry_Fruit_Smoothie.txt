Description : 
 Recipe reprinted with permission from HealthRecipes.com 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup crushed  pineapple 
 1 fresh  apricot , diced 
 6  strawberries 
 ½  banana 
 1½ cup  water 
 1 tbsp  skim milk powder 
 1 tbsp high-quality  protein powder  (optional) 
 1 tsp  flax seed oil  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 In a blender, process fruit with the rest of the ingredients. 
 Blend until thoroughly mixed and serve.
