 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 box graham crackers 
 2 boxes instant  vanilla  pudding 
 4 c.  milk 
 1 med. container  Cool Whip 
 1 can ready to spread  chocolate  frosting 
 strawberries  (if you wish to use as a topping) 
 Directions : 
 Prepare pudding. 
 Fold  Cool Whip  into pudding. 
 Line bottom of 9 x 13Â inch cake dish with graham crackers. 
 Spread a layer of the pudding mixture over the graham crackers. 
 Continue to alternate layers. 
 Top layer MUST be graham crackers. 
 Spread frosting on top. 
 Place halves of  strawberries  on top and cover partially with melted  chocolate . 
 Cover and refrigerate overnight.
