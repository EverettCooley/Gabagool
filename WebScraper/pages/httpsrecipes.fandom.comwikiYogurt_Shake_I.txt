Description : 
 Yields about 2½ cups. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup plain  yogurt 
 1 small  banana 
 1 tbsp frozen  orange juice , or  apple juice 
 sugar  or  honey 
 4 ice cubes 
 Directions : 
 Place all ingredients into blender or food processor. 
 Whirl until smooth. 
 Variations : 
 Instead of  banana , use 1 cup cubed melon, ½ cup fresh or frozen  berries , ½ cup  pineapple , 1 peeled and chopped  kiwifruit  or ½ peeled and cored  apple .
