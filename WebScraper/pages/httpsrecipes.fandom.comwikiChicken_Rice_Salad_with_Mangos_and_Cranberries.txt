Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  medium-grain rice 
 1 x 6 ounce package pre-cooked  chicken  strips, cut into 1-inch pieces 
 ¾ cup dried  cranberries 
 1  mango , chopped 
 ¾ cup chopped  pecans , toasted 
 ¾ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 ½ cup raspberry  vinaigrette  dressing 
 ¼ cup plus two tablespoons fresh chopped  parsley , divided 
 Directions : 
 In large bowl, combine  rice ,  chicken ,  cranberries ,  mango ,  pecans ,  pepper , vinaigrette and ¼ cup  parsley . 
 Toss well. 
 Garnish with remaining  parsley .
