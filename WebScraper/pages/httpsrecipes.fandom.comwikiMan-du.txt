Dumplings, filled with ground  pork ,  kimchi ,  Spring Onions , and bean curd, usually poached in a richly flavoured  beef  broth (maj-du-kuk), steamed (jjin-man-du), boiled (mul-man-du), or fried (yaki-man-du).
