      Tandoori Coloring; Tandoori Paste     Available in Indian markets, this coloring is used to give foods the traditional red-orange tint of Tandoor Oven cooking. Tandoori paste can be rubbed directly onto the surface of meats; the powder is generally stirred into a Marinade.
