Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons chopped fresh  cilantro 
 2 tablespoons  olive oil 
 2 teaspoons  pepper sauce 
 1 clove minced  garlic 
 1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon crushed  red peppers  (optional) 
 1 1/2 pounds boneless beef top  sirloin  (1 1/2 inches thick), cut into 1-1/4-inch pieces 
 1 large  red bell pepper , cut into 1-inch pieces 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 In a medium bowl, combine cilantro, oil, pepper sauce, garlic and optional crushed red peppers. 
 Add beef; toss. 
 Alternately thread beef and bell peppers onto six (10-inch) metal skewers. 
 Grill kebabs on medium, uncovered, 8 to 11 minutes for medium-rare to medium, turning occasionally. 
 Salt to taste. 
 Notes : 
 Preparation time: 15 minutes 
 Cooking time: less than 15 minutes 
 Makes 6 servings. 
 193 calories, 25 grams protein, 9 grams fat (44 percent calories from fat), 
 2.4 grams saturated fat, 2 grams carbohydrate, 46 milligrams cholesterol, 62
milligrams sodium, 1 gram fiber
