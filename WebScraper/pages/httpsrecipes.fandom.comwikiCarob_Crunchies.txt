 Ingredients : 
 Crunchies : 
 2¼ cups  whole wheat flour 
 1  egg 
 ¼ cup  vegetable oil 
 ¼ cup  applesauce 
 ½ cup hot  water 
 1 tbsp honey 
 1 tsp  molasses 
 Icing : 
 1 cup  carob chips 
 Directions : 
 Crunchies : 
 Mix all of the crunchies' ingredients together until well blended. 
 Knead dough two minutes on a lightly floured surface. 
 Roll to ¼" thickness. 
 Cut out and bake on an ungreased cookie sheet for 30 minutes in a 300 °F oven. 
 Cool. 
 Icing : 
 Melt  carob chips  in microwave or saucepan. 
 Dip cooled crunchies in  carob , or lay them on a flat surface and brush melted  carob  over the biscuits with a pastry brush. 
 Let cool.
