 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 large, ripe mangoes 
 3  oranges 
 1  lemon  or  lime 
 2 tablesps honey 
 2  eggs 
 150 gr  ground almonds 
 1 pk white  gelatine  ( 6 leaves or powder ) 
 100 gr ( 1 cup )  whipping cream 
 8  maraschino cherry 
 Directions : 
 Peel mangoes and cut the flesh of two mangoes into chunks 
 Extract juice from  oranges  and  lemon / lime 
 Puree the  mango  chunks 
 Add slowly to the puree: the juice, honey  egg  yolks and  almonds 
 prepare  gelatine  as directed on package mix  gelatine  with puree thoroughly 
 Cool mixture in fridge until it is halfway set 
 Beat  whipping cream  and  egg  whites separately until stiff 
 Mix both gently with the  mango  creme 
 Devide creme into 8 glasses 
 Garnish with a  cherry  and a piece of  mango  slice.
