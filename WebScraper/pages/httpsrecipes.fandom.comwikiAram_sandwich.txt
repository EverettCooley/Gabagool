A sandwich formed by spreading a softened  lahvosh  with  cream cheese , then layering thin slices of sandwich filings, such as  meat ,  cheese ,  lettuce ,  pickle  and so on. It is rolled jelly-roll style, wrapped tightly in plastic wrap and refrigerated for several hours.
