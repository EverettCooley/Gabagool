 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1/4 cup  olive oil 
 3 cloves  garlic 
 1/4 cup tamari 
 1/2 cup bread crumbs 
 1 lbs  tofu  (firm or x-firm) 
 Directions : 
 Preheat a 400 degree oven slice  tofu  in .25 to .5 inch slices. Put first three ingredients in blender and run for a bit on high speed. Marinate  tofu  sliced in ixture for about 15 minutes on a side. Dredge slices in bread crumbs. Bake on cookie sheet for 15 minutes per side. White sauce :make roux of 2 tbs  flour  and 2 tbs  butter  or margaine. Cook for a couple of minutes. Add 1 cup of hot, scalded  milk . Bring to boil. Reduce heat, add  salt  and two dashes tobasco. Suggested: serve with  rice
