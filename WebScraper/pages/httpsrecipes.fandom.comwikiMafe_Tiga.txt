Ingredients = : 
 1 liter  water 
 4  potatoes , cut up 
 1  pimento 
 1 – 2 tbsp  peanut butter 
 2  tomatoes 
 4 small  onions 
 2 or more cloves  garlic 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 ½ a large Maggi cube 
 1 tsp of  garam masala  if you've got it 
 Directions : 
 Mix the above. 
 Boil. 
 Add  tomatoes ),  onions ,  garlic , and desired spices. 
 Cook it all down for 45 – 60 minutes. 
 Serve over  rice .
