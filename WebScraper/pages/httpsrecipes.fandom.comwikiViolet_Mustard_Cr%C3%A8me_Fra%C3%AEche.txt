Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  violet mustard 
 ¼ cup  crème fraîche 
 ¼ cup  apple juice 
 ¼ cup  grapeseed oil 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Blend together. 
 Mixed greens are dressed with  olive oil  and  lemon juice .
