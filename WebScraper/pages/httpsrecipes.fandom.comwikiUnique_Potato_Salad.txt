Description : 
 This is a recipe I obtained from an Estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Manes Estate in Venus, Texas in 1981.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 6 medium  red potatoes 
 ½ cup  red onion  chopped 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 2 teaspoons  celery seed 
 1 tablespoon  granulated sugar 
 1 tablespoon  yellow mustard 
 ⅛ cup  milk 
 3 tablespoons fresh  parsley 
 ¼ cup  mayonnaise 
 Directions : 
 Cook potatoes in boiling water with their skins on until tender. 
 Cool then peel and cube. 
 Add onion, salt, pepper, sugar and mustard. 
 Stir in milk and mayonnaise until desired consistency is reached. 
 Add parsley and celery seed last then chill and serve.
