      Finnish Three-meat Ragout     
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound lean  lamb, shoulder  or  leg 
 1 pound lean  pork, shoulder  or  leg 
 1 pound lean  beef round 
 2½ teaspoons  salt 
 1 teaspoon whole  allspice 
 ½ teaspoon whole  white peppercorns 
 6 large white  onions , cut into ¼-inch thick slices 
 chopped fresh  parsley 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 300°F. 
 Cut the meat into 1-inch cubes. 
 In an enameled, cast-iron pot or other deep oven proof casserole, layer the meat,  salt ,  allspice , white peppercorns, and  onion . 
 Cover lightly. 
 Bake for 5 hours, or until the meat is very tender. 
 Sprinkle with chopped fresh  parsley  and serve over mashed  potatoes  or mashed  potatoes  and rutabagas.
