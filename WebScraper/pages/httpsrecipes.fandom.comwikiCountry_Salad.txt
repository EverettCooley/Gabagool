Ingredients : 
 2½  beetroots 
 ½  onion 
 1 hard-boiled  egg 
 3 tbsp  sour cream  or  mayonnaise 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Boil, peel and shred the  beetroots . 
 Peel and shred the  onion . 
 Peel and chop fine the  egg . 
 Mix the ingredients thoroughly and season with  sour cream  or  mayonnaise . 
 Heap the salad in a dish and decorate.
