Any Flavor Jello Salad
 2 pkgs any flavor Jello
Mix 2 tbl mayonnaise
1 can crushed pineapple, drained
1 pkg. cream cheese
1 cup pecans
1 cup small marshmallows
1 banana
 Prepare 2 packages any flavor jello (orange is good - or cherry) as
directed except add 1/2 the hot water and all the cold.
 Cool until firm.
