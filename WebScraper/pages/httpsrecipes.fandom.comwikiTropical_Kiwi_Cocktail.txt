Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 large  kiwi fruits , peeled 
 3 tbsp  Sugar 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 2 cup  orange juice 
 1 cup  milk 
 1 large  kiwi , peeled - for garnishing 
 Directions : 
 For the garnish, cut the peeled  kiwi  into half inch round slices. Cover and refrigerate. Cut the other  kiwi  into small, 1-inch pieces. Place in a blender with the  Sugar ,  orange  and  lemon  juices. Puree until smooth. Transfer to a small pitcher and refrigerate. To serve: Stir in  milk  to the puree. Pour into cocktail glasses and garnish with a  kiwi  slice.
