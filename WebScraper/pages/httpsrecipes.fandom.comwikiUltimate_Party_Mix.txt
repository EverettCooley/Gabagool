Desciption : 
 This Recipe Came From an Estate Sale from The Steep Estate in Elmo, Texas in 1989.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 stick  butter , melted 
 1 teaspoon seasoned  salt 
 5 teaspoons  worcestershire sauce 
 3 cups  corn  squares cereal 
 3 cups  wheat  squares cereal 
 3 cups  rice  squares cereal 
 1 cup salted mixed nuts 
 Directions : 
 Preheat Oven to 350. 
 Combine  butter ,  salt  and worcestershire 
 Then stir in  cereals  and nuts slowly, stirring slowly to coat everything. 
 Place on jelly roll pan and bake 25 minutes stirring every 5 minutes. 
 When done, spread on paper towel to cool then store in airtight container.
