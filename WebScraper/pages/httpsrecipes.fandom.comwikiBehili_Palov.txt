 Description : 
 Pilaf with quinces
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 kg (2¼ lb)  rice 
 300 g  mutton  or  beef 
 2 – 3  quinces 
 1 kg (36 oz)  carrots 
 4  onions 
 350 g  vegetable oil 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Prepare in the same manner as  Ferghana pilaf . 
 Before adding  rice , add slices of  quince . 
 When pilaf is finished, mix  rice  and serve in a mound on a large platter topped with  quince  pieces.
