 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 6 large vita bones 
 half cup  cornmeal 
 2 tablespoons  Peanut   butter 
 1 tsp fish  oil 
 2 tablespoons honey 
 1 quick shake  baking powder 
 1 packet  yeast 
 6oz container cheeze wiz 
 Directions : 
 Use pie roller to flatten vita bones to powder. 
 Combine ingredients (except  Cheese  wiz) in large mixing bowl. Let rise. 
 Cook at 325 for 12 minutes. 
 Fill centers with  Cheese  wiz. 
 Serve hot.
