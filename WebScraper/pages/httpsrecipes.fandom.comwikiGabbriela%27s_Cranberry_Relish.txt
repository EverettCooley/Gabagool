Ingredients : 
 3 cup  cranberries , fresh 
 1 cup  sugar 
 2 tbsp  water 
 Directions : 
 Few  raspberries   lemon  zest or  orange   ginger  -- grated. 
 Put  sugar ,  water , and  cranberries  into a medium saucepan. 
 Boil ten minutes, the add remainder of ingredients. 
 Cool.
