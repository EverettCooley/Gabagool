Description : 
 This recipe came from The Printey Estate sale. It was obtained it as part of the Family Collection from The Printey Estate in Forney, Texas in 2001. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ pounds dry  yellow-eye beans 
 1 small  white onion , sliced 
 1  apple , peeled and sliced 
 ¼ pound  salt pork 
 1 cup  maple syrup 
 1 teaspoon dry  mustard 
 1 tablespoon  salt 
 Directions : 
 Soak beans overnight in water. 
 Without draining bring to a boil, adding more  water  to cover as necessary. 
 Simmer for 2 hours. 
 Preheat oven to 275°F. 
 Put  onion  and  apple  in bottom of bean pot then fill half full with beans. 
 Add  salt pork  and pour in remaining beans. 
 Pour  maple syrup  over beans then sprinkle with  mustard  and  salt . 
 Cover with boiling water and bake 5 hours.
