Description : 
 Makes 8 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x 8-ounce package  cream cheese , softened 
 ¼ cup  water 
 1 x 10-ounce package frozen chopped  spinach , thawed, undrained 
 1 x 4-ounce package crumbled  blue cheese 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 ½ cup chopped  walnuts , toasted 
 Directions : 
 Heat  cream cheese  and  water  in large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir until  cheese  is melted, 3 to 5 minutes. 
 Add  spinach ,  blue cheese ,  salt  and  pepper ; cook 2 minutes. Add  rice ; cook until heated through, 3 to 5 minutes. Remove from heat. 
 Stir in  walnuts .
