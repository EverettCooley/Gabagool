Ingredients : 
 1 whole  chicken  or 6  chicken  leg quarters. 
 2 â€“ 3 lbs of smoked  sausage 
 1 medium can of  tomato sauce 
 1 small can of  tomato paste 
 2 tbsp Tony's  Creole seasoning 
 1 tbsp of  red wine vinegar 
 salt  to taste 
 either pre-made biscuits or home biscuits to lay on top 
 Directions : 
 Boil  chicken  (depending on amount used)for one hrs or til meat falls off bone. 
 Slice  sausage  to desired consistency. 
 Throw  sausage  in when  chicken  is half done. 
 When meat is ready, put in a 9"by 13" baking pan. 
 Then in small bowl stir in seasonings, stir until blended. 
 Pour this on top of meat. 
 Whether using pre-made biscuits or homemade. 
 Lay biscuits on top of  chicken  and  sausage . 
 Bake for 15 to 30 minutes or til biscuits are done. 
 Let cool for 5 minutes then serve either alone or with a vegetable and a glass of  merlot .
