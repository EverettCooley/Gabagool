Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 envelope unflavored  gelatin 
 ¼ cup  water 
 1 squares  unsweetened chocolate 
 ⅛ teaspoon  cinnamon 
 1 teaspoon  Sweet 'n Low liquid 
 ¼ cup  water 
 ½ cup  evaporated milk 
 ½ teaspoon  vanilla 
 ¼ cup  pecans , chopped 
 Directions : 
 In a bowl soften the gelatin in ¼-cup water for 5 minutes. 
 In a saucepan melt the chocolate with the cinnamon and sweet and low liquid. 
 Stir constantly. 
 Slowly add the evaporated milk and water. 
 Stir to mix. 
 Add the gelatin and stir until dissolved. 
 Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla; set aside cool. 
 When the mixture begins to thicken, stir in the nuts. 
 Pour into a pan. 
 When firm, cut into squares. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Each piece equals ⅓ milk exchange + ½ fat exchange
