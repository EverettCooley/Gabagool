Description 
 Juice, Grapefruit, Canned Unsweetened  by the US Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Serves : 6 
 Ingredients 
 1 x 4-oz package cook and serve vanilla pudding 
 1¾ cups  grapefruit juice 
 8 ounces vanilla low fat  yogurt 
 ¾ cup crushed  graham crackers 
 Directions 
 Prepare pudding mix according to package directions, substituting the grapefruit juice for the milk. 
 After cooking, chill the pudding. 
 After chilling, fold the yogurt into the pudding. 
 Divide ⅓ of pudding among six glasses. 
 Sprinkle with half of the crushed graham crackers. 
 Repeat layers, then top with remaining ⅓ of pudding.
