 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Gonzales Estate in Crandall, Texas in 1980.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 package  lime gelatin 
 1 cup  ricotta cheese 
 Â½ teaspoon  lime zest 
 Directions : 
 Mix package of gelatin with 1 cup boiling water and stir until dissolved. 
 While mixture is cooling slightly put ricotta and lime zest in blender. 
 Add dissolved gelatin and blend 2 minutes and pour into serving dishes. 
 Refrigerate until set.
