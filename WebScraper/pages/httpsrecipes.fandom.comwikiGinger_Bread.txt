Ingredients : 
 4 tablespoons  water , cold 
 2 cups  molasses 
 1 teaspoon  ginger  and  cinnamon , mixed 
 Â½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 2 teaspoons  baking soda 
 2 teaspoons hot  water 
 1 cup  flour 
 1  egg 
 Directions : 
 Mix cold  water ,  molasses  and well beaten  egg . 
 Add  flour ,  salt ,  ginger  and  cinnamon . 
 Mix well and add the  baking soda , dissolved in the hot  water . 
 Then add the melted  butter . 
 Beat thoroughly. 
 Pour into a buttered pan and bake 30 to 40 minutes in a moderate oven.
