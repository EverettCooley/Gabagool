Ingredients : 
 1 cup pitted  dates 
 ½ cup  dried apricots 
 ⅓ cup  walnuts 
 ⅓ cup  almonds 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 ¼ tsp  ginger , ground 
 ⅓ cup  orange juice 
 2 tbsp red kosher wine 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in a processor. 
 Process until finely chopped. 
 Pat into a serving container, then cover til needed.
