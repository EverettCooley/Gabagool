Description 
 Seafood Recipes  by the South Carolina Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Ingredients 
 Shad   roe 
 Bacon 
 Directions 
 Wrap shad roe with bacon and keep in place with a toothpick. 
 Place in a 350 °F oven until the bacon is sizzling. 
 The bacon should be a minimum 155 °F and the roe 145 °F. 
 The roe should be seared on the outside but still moist on the inside.
