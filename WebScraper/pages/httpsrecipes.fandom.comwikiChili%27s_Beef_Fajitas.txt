 Prep Time: 
 Cook time: 6 - 8 Hours 
 Serves: 4 
 
 Description : 
 Chili’s is known for their high quality fajitas. These are easy to prepare, and you can have that special Tex Mex flavor any night of the week.
 
 Check Out More  Restaurant Recipes 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ lbs  sirloin steak 
 1 envelope dry  onion soup mix 
 2 cups  water 
 sautéed  onion  and  green pepper 
 1 small can diced  green chilies 
 flour  tortillas 
 shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 salsa 
 Directions : 
 Cut sirloin steak into bite size pieces. 
 Place in crock pot, with chili’s, soup mix and water. 
 Cook on low, 6-8 hours. 
 Chop onion and bell pepper in small stripes, sauté in olive oil. 
 Warm tortillas in microwave. 
 Drain beef mixture. 
 Place in center of tortillas, top with cheese, salsa, onion and pepper mix
