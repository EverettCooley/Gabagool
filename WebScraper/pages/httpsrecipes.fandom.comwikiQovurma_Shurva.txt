Description : 
 Mutton soup
 
 Ingredients : 
 500 g (18 oz)  mutton 
 100 g or ⅗ cup  vegetable oil 
 500 g  potatoes 
 4  tomatoes 
 4  onions 
 1  chile pepper 
 1 bunch (1 oz) each  dill  and  coriander 
 2 tablespoons  tomato paste 
 1  apple 
 bay leaf 
 salt  and spices to taste 
 Directions : 
 Cut meat into small pieces and sear in  oil  until brown. 
 Add sliced  onions  and  tomatoes , and saute another 7 – 10 minutes. 
 Then put in  potatoes  and stir well. 
 Pour in 2.5 – 3 liters or quarts of  water , add  salt  and bring to a boil. 
 Then add whole  chili pepper , chopped  dill , and fresh coriander. 
 Finally add an  apple  to bring the soup to full flavor. 
 Simmer dish for about an hour. 
 Serve in kosas topped with chopped greens.
