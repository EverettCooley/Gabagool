Ingredients : 
 1 oz  sweet vermouth 
 ¼ oz  dry vermouth 
 ½ oz  sloe gin 
 2 dashes  orange bitters 
 Directions : 
 Pour the ingredients into a glass,filled with  ice . 
 Stir and garnish with a slice of  orange .
