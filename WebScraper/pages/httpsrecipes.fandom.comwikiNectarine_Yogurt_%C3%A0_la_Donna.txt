Ingredients : 
 3 cups  nectarines , peeled and sliced 
 32 oz plain  yogurt 
 Â½ cup whole  milk 
 4 individual packs  Equal 
 1 tsp  vanilla  (pure is best) 
 1 cup  Equal  Spoonable 
 Directions : 
 Put the sliced  nectarines  in a medium size mixing bowl. 
 With a mashing device, mash the  nectarines  to the point that they are lumpy. 
 Add the 4 packets of equal while mashing the  nectarines  so that the equal will mix in well. 
 Add all other ingredients and mix; refrigerate for one-half hour. 
 Add to  ice cream  maker and make to manufacturers instructions.
