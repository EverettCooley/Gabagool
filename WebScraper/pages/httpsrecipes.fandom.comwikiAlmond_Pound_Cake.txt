almond  Pound Cake
 
 3 c  sugar 
 2 Sticks  butter 
 1/2 c  shortening 
 5 Large  eggs 
 1/4 ts  salt 
 3 c  flour 
 1 cn Small Can  evaporated milk 
 (3/4 Cup) +  water  to Make
 
 1 Cup 
 2 ts  almond extract 
 
Cream  sugar  And  shortening . Add  eggs  And  salt , Cream Well. Add Remaining Ingredients. Pour Into a Very Lightly Greased 12 Cup Bundt Pan. Put Into Cold Oven;Set Temperature at 320 Degrees. Bake 1 Hour And 30 Minutes.Cool 15 Minutes And Remove From Pan.
 
Enjoy. This is a Good Cake.
