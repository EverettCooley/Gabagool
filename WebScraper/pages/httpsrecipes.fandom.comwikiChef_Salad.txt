Ingredients : 
 6 C. Torn  lettuce 
 6 Oz.  spinach , Torn Into Pieces 
 8 Oz. Cooked  Turkey , Cut Into Strips 
 8 Oz. Cooked  Ham , Cut Into Strips 
 6 Oz.  Swiss cheese , Cut Into Thin Strips 
 1 C. Broken Melba Cracker Rounds 
 8  cherry tomatoes , Halved 
 8 Pitted Black or  green olives , Halved 
 Hard Boiled  eggs , Cut Into Slices 
 Directions : 
 Combine All Ingredients in Large Salad Bowls or Divide Ingredients Among Individual Salad Bowls. Garnish With Hard Boiled  egg  Slices if Desired.
