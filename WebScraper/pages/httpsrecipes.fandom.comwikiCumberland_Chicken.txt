 Ingredients 
 1 whole  chicken 
 1  onion , chopped 
 3 tablespoons  orange juice  concentrate  or  3 tablespoons orange  marmalade 
 1 tablespoon  corn starch 
 1 cup  root beer   or   ginger ale 
 Directions 
 Boil and debone 1 chicken. Saute with 1 chopped onions. 
 Add 3 tablespoons. orange juice concentrate or 3 tbsps. orange marmalade. Combine 1 tablespoon corn starch and 1 cup root beer or ginger ale. 
 Pour into pan with other ingredients. Simmer until done.
