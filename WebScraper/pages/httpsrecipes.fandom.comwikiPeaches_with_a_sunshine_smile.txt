 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can of  peach  halves 
 2 to 3 cups  cottage cheese  (low-fat) 
 lettuce  leaves 
 Raisins 
 Directions : 
 1 Arrange  lettuce  in a bowl or plate. 
2 Spoon a layer of  cottage cheese  "sun rays" on  lettuce . 
3 The  peach  half is placed round end up. 
4 Press  raisins  gently into  peach  to form a sunshine face. 
5 Sprinkle extra  raisins  on  cottage cheese .
