Description : 
 Contributed by Lil at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Starkist™ Label 
 1 – 2 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (6-oz) can Starkist™  albacore  tuna, drained 
 2 tbsp chopped  red ,  green  and  yellow bell peppers 
 1 tbsp reduced-fat  mayonnaise 
 1 tsp  orange juice 
 1 tbsp shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 1  pita ,  hot dog bun  or your choice of bread/bun/roll 
 ½ cup shredded  lettuce 
 Directions : 
 In small bowl, stir together  tuna  with remaining ingredients except pita and mix. 
 Cut pita in ½ and stuff with  lettuce  and  tuna  mix.
