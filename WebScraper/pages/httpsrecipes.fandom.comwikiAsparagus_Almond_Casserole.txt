This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Williams Estate in Tyler, Texas in 1994.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4 tablespoons  butter 
 4 tablespoons  flour 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  pepper 
 2 cups  milk 
 2 medium cans  asparagus 
 1½ cups grated  cheese 
 ½ cup  slivered almonds 
 cracker crumbs 
 butter 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter  then add  flour ,  salt  and  pepper  until blended. 
 Add milk and place a layer of drained  asparagus , half the  cheese , half the almonds and half the sauce. 
 Repeat once more than sprinkle with crumbs and dots of  butter  and bake at 375 for 25 minutes.
