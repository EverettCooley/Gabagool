About Menudo mix : 
 Menudo mix is a mixture of herbs and spices used in flavoring soups and  menudo .
 
 Simple Menudo Mix : 
 2 parts  oregano 
 2 parts  onion flakes 
 1 part  coriander seed 
 1 part ground  cumin 
 1 part  chile  flakes 
 Elaborate Menudo mix : 
 1 teaspoon  chili powder 
 1 teaspoon  garlic powder   or  flakes 
 1 teaspoon dried  onion flakes 
 ½ teaspoon  basil 
 ½ teaspoon  thyme   or   rosemary 
 ½ teaspoon chopped dried  orange peel  (optional) 
 ½ teaspoon ground  cumin 
 ½ teaspoon dry powdered  mustard 
 ½ teaspoon  oregano 
 ½ teaspoon  sugar 
 ¼ teaspoon  cinnamon 
 ¼ teaspoon  nutmeg 
 ¼ teaspoon  cumin  seed
