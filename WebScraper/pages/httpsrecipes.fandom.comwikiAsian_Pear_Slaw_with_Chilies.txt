Fruit of the Month: Asian Pear  by the US Centers for Disease Control & Prevention, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Serves : 6 
 Ingredients 
 6  celery  ribs 
 4 Tbsp fresh  lime juice 
 4 Tbsp seasoned  rice vinegar 
 1 tsp  ginger  root 
 6  asian pears , sliced ¼-inch thick 
 3  scallions , thinly sliced on diagonal 
 ¼ cup  cilantro  leaves 
 ½ tsp  Fresno chili   or   jalapeño  chili, finely chopped 
 Directions 
 Whisk together juice, vinegar, and ginger and stir in celery and remaining ingredients with salt and pepper to taste. 
 Let stand at room temperature 15 minutes before serving.
