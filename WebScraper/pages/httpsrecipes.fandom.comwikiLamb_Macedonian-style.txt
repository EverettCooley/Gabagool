Ingredients : 
 1 young  lamb  (about 4 – 5 kg) 
 1 kg  potatoes 
 ½ liter  beer 
 salt 
 pepper 
 herbs ( basil ,  thyme ,  oregano ,  rosemary ) 
 100 ml  oil 
 Directions : 
 Put  lamb  on baking dish. 
 Put  salt  and  pepper  on it. 
 Then mix  oil  with herbs and splash it on  lamb . 
 Put it in pre-heated oven and bake it for 5 hours (3.5 for medium) on 250°C. 
 Then after about 3 hours put in quarters sliced  potatoes . 
 When finished splash  beer  on meat and leave it for another about 20 – 30 minutes. 
 Serve hot with  potatoes .
