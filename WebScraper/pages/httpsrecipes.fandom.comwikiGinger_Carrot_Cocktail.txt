Info 
 Cook Time : About 30 minutes 
 Serves : 2
 
 Ingredients 
 1 cup  water 
 1/2 cup grated  carrots 
 1-1/2 cups  apple juice , chilled 
 1 tablespoon fresh  ginger  root, grated then finely minced 
 1/2 cup crushed  ice 
 Directions 
 Boil water in a small saucepan over medium-high heat. Add carrots and cook for 10 minutes or until tender. Drain carrots then set aside to cool. 
 Add carrots, apple juice and ginger to blender and blend until smooth. Add ice and continue blending until desired consistency. Serve over ice in tall glasses.
