Description : 
 Serve as part of  Steamed Artichokes with Dipping Sauces 
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Original recipe 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tbsp finely minced  garlic 
 1 tbsp finely chopped  parsley 
 ¼ cup 365 organic  balsamic vinegar 
 ½ – ¾ cup 365 extra virgin  olive oil 
 sea salt  and freshly ground  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Combine shallots, parsley and vinegar. 
 Whisk in olive oil until smooth. 
 Season with salt and pepper. 
 Serve in small dipping bowls.
