       Cachapas     
 Venezuelan corn cakes
 
 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cup tender  corn  kernels (canned  corn  may be used) 
 3 tsp  salt 
 ¾ to 1 cup of  water  (depending on how tender the  corn  is) 
 ¾ cup  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Mix all the ingredients in a blender. 
 Shape the mix into small pancakes approximately ½ inch thick and about 5 inches in diameter. 
 Let them cook on medium heat for about one minute on each side,. 
 Pancakes should be served hot, and may be accompanied with  cheese .
