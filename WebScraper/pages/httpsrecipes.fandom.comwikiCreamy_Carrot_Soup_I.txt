Description : 
 Serves: 	6 
 Occasion: 	Any 
 Effort: 	Easy 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups  water 
 ½ cup chopped  onions 
 2 tbsp  brown sugar 
 1 tsp  curry powder 
 1½ cup  milk 
 4 cups  carrots , sliced 
 2  garlic  cloves, minced 
 ⅛ tsp  ground ginger 
 green onions , chopped (for garnish) 
 Directions : 
 In a large sauce pan bring  water  to boil. Add all remaining ingredients, except  milk . 
 Reduce heat and simmer for 40 – 45 minutes, or until  carrots  and  onions  are very tender. 
 Remove from heat and pour mixture into food processor or blender to puree; return to sauce pan. 
 Reduce to low heat and stir in  milk , heating until warm but not boiling. Serve warm. 
 If desired garnish with chopped  green onions
