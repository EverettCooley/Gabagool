chocolate  Marshmallows
 
 1  chocolate  Bar ( milk chocolate , 1/2 Lb.) 
 1 T. Solid  shortening 
 30 Large Marshmallows 
 Chopped  peanuts  or Graham Cracker Crumbs or  coconut  For Garnish
 
Cover a Tray With Wax Paper. Melt The 1/2 Lb.  chocolate  Bar With  shortening  in Top of double boiler Over Warm  water . Stir Until Smooth. Remove From Heat. Cool Slightly. Put Toothpick in Center of Each  marshmallow . Dip Into Melted  chocolate . Cover Completely. Gently Tap on Side of Pan to Remove Excess  chocolate . Roll in Desired Garnish. Put on Prepared Tray. Chill 15 Minutes. Serve Cold. Makes 30
