Description : 
 Contributed by   World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 Crust : 
 1½ sticks  butter 
 1½ cups  flour 
 1 cup chopped  pecans 
 Filling : 
 8 oz  cream cheese 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1 large  Cool Whip 
 Topping : 
 1 small  instant vanilla pudding 
 1 small  instant chocolate pudding 
 3 cups  milk 
 Directions : 
 Mix all the crust ingredients together and press into greased 9 x 13 pan. 
 Bake at 350°F for 25 minutes. 
 Mix ½  Cool Whip  with  cream cheese  and  sugar . 
 Spread onto the pressed mixture above. 
 Beat the topping ingredients for 3 minutes. 
 Put on top of  cream cheese  layer. 
 Top with other half of  Cool Whip . 
 Enjoy.
