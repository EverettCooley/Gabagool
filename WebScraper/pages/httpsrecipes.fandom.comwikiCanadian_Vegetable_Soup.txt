A nice piece of work this soup is; Veggies & herbs makes this Canadian soup one of the best soups to create and taste ! Requires basic cooking skills.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 qt  Vegetable  Broth 
 2/3 cup  celery , chopped 
 2/3 cup  onion , chopped 
 1  garlic  cloves, chopped 
 1/2 cup  carrots , sliced 
 1 cups  potatoes , diced 
 1 tbsp  canola oil 
 1/4 cup  tomato  paste 
 1/4 cup  green pepper , diced 
 1/4 tbsp  sweet basil 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 1/4 cup  macaroni 
 1/4 cup   barley 
 1 cup coarsely chopped  cabbage 
 1/2  tomato , chopped 
 
 
 Directions : 
 In a soup pot sauté the  celery ,  onion  and  garlic  in  oil  for 5 minutes. 
 Add  water , vegetables,  pot barley , meats, seasonings. Bring to the boil and simmer for 1¼ hours. 
 For last ¼ hour add  cabbage  and  macaroni .
