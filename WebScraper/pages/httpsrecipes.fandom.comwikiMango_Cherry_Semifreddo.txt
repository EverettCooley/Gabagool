Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves 10 
 Ingredients : 
 Semifreddo : 
 1½ cups  tart red cherries 
 3  mangoes , cut in chunks 
 3 tbsp  honey 
 ½ cup  sour cream 
 5 large  eggs 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 Cherry sauce : 
 1½ cups  tart red cherries 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 ¼ cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Semifreddo : 
 Cut cherries into small pieces. 
 Spread in a single layer on a foil lined baking sheet and freeze until very hard. 
 Line an 8 inch springform pan with nonstick foil and place in freezer. 
 Puree mango and honey in blender until smooth. 
 Transfer to a bowl, whisk in sour cream until blended. 
 Put eggs and sugar in a bowl set over a saucepan of simmering water, beat mixture until thick and tripled in volume. 
 Remove from heat, fold into mango mixture. 
 Fold half the frozen cherries into mixture and pour into springform pan. 
 Scatter remaining frozen cherries over top of batter. 
 Freeze until solid. 
 Cherry sauce : 
 In a saucepan, bring ingredients for sauce to a boil, boil 6 min. 
 Pour mixture in a blender and puree. 
 Pour into a container and refrigerate until serving. 
 To serve : 
 Remove sides from springform pan. 
 Cut into wedges and place on plates. 
 Let stand until slightly softened. 
 Serve with cherry sauce and diced mango.
