Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Source: Posted to www.sparkpeople.com by SparkPeople user POIFECT_NOT 
 Number of Servings: 4 
 Ingredients : 
 2  avocados 
 8 medium sized  Roma (plum) tomatoes  chopped 
 1 regular  tomato  chopped 
 1 cup of chopped  onion 
 1 cup chopped  green pepper 
 1 or 2 chopped  jalapeno peppers 
 garlic  about 6 to 8 tbsp or to your taste 
 8 tbsp  cilantro , chopped 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Set the avocados aside do not peel until ready to use. 
 Chop all the vegetables and mix together with the salt and lime juice. 
 You can add more or less jalapeno according to taste. 
 You should let this mixture chill at least an hour. 
 Then clean your avocados cut in half and serve the tomato mixture over the top.
