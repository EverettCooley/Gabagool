Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp  lime juice 
 1½ cups  club soda 
 lime  slices 
 1 large  mango 
 Directions : 
 Purée the fruit of  mango  in a blender. 
 Add the  lime juice  and  club soda  and mix just to blend. 
 Strain. 
 Pour over  ice  and garnish with thin slices of  lime .
