Description : 
 To cut down on the mess, marinate the wings in extra large zipper lock plastic bags. If you don't have a grill, you can bake these wings in a 425°F oven for about 15-20 minutes instead.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Serves 8 – 10 as appetizers 
 Ingredients : 
 5 lbs  chicken wings 
 Marinade : 
 ½ cup  rice vinegar 
 ½ cup  soy sauce 
 ¼ cup  honey 
 ⅛ cup  sesame oil 
 ⅓ cup  peanut  or  canola oil 
 4 cloves  garlic , minced 
 3  green onions , minced 
 2 tbsp fresh  ginger , grated 
 ⅓ cup  orange juice 
 2 tsp  pepper 
 1 – 2 tbsp Asian style  hot sauce  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Wings : 
 First prepare the wings prepare chicken wings by cutting off wing tips and separating the wing at the elbow joint (discard tips or save for making stock). 
 Marinade : 
 Then mix all marinade ingredients together. 
 Place wings into several extra large plastic zipper bags. 
 Pour marinade over wings. 
 Marinate for at least 4 hours or overnight. 
 Grill wings over a medium-hot grill for about 8 – 10 minutes per side or until completely cooked and dark brown.
