 Prep Time : About 5 minutes 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 vodka 
 apple juice ,  apple cider   or  apple liqueur 
 sweet and sour mix 
 slice of  granny smith apple  (optional) 
 Directions 
 Pour vodka and apple juice/apple cider/apple liqueur into a shaker. 
 Shake or stir apple vodka with sweet and sour mixture. Strain into martini glass
