Spicy Hot  chili 
 
 1 Lb. Ground  Beef  or  Pork 
 2 cloves  garlic , Minced 
 1 (16 Oz.) Can  red kidney beans , Drained 
 1 (4 Oz.) Can Green  chile peppers , Rinsed, Seeded &Chopped 
 1 Tsp.  sugar 
 1 Tsp.  oregano , Crushed 
 1/4 Tsp.  celery salt 
 1/4 Tsp.  pepper 
 Few Drops Bottled hot pepper sauce 
 
 1 Med.  Onion , Chopped 
 1 (16 Oz.) Can  tomatoes , Cut Up 
 3/4 C.  tomato juice 
 1 Tbsp.  worcestershire sauce 
 2 Tsp.  paprika 
 1 Tsp.  salt 
 1/2 Tsp.  cumin 
 1/4 Tsp.  Cayenne 
 1/8 Tsp. Dry  mustard 
 
In Large Saucepan Cook Meat,  Onion  And  garlic  UntilMeat is Browned; Drain Off Fat. Stir in Undrained
 tomatoes , beans,  tomato juice ,  chile peppers ,
 Worcestershire,  paprika ,  sugar ,  salt ,  oregano ,  cumin , celery salt ,  Cayenne ,  pepper ,  mustard ,  hot pepper 
 Sauce And 1 Cup  water . Cover And Simmer For 20-30Minutes. Makes 4-6 Servings.
 
Enjoy.
