 Makes 1 serving of a perfect margarita 
 Ingredients : 
 Â¼ cup  tequila 
 2 tbs + 2 tsp  orange flavored liqueur 
 1 tbs + 1 tsp  lime juice 
 kosher salt 
 lime  wedges 
 Directions : 
 Run a lime wedge halfway around the outer rim of a glass to moisten. 
 Dip in kosher salt to lightly coat. 
 Pour tequila, liqueur, and lime juice into a cocktail shaker or jar with a tightly fitting lid. 
 Add ice, shake well, then strain into the glass. 
 Garnish with lime wedge.
