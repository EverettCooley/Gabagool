Description : 
 A nice looking appetizer that consists of hard-boiled  eggs , stuffed with crabmeat and garnished with shrimps and  dill .
 
 Ingredients : 
 8 hard-boiled  eggs 
 120 â€“ 180 g  crabmeat  or  surimi 
 2 tablespoons  sour cream 
 1 teaspoon  tomato paste 
 Tabasco sauce 
 salt  and  pepper 
 16  shrimp 
 fresh  dill  sprigs 
 Directions : 
 Chop up the crab meat. 
 Peel and half the  eggs , remove the  yolks . 
 Mix the  yolks  with the  sour cream ,  tomato  paste, and tabasco. 
 Add  salt  and  pepper  to taste, then fold in the crab meat. 
 Stuff the  eggs  with this mixture, 
 Garnish with the  Shrimp  and some  dill .
