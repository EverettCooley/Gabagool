 Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz  light rum 
 1 tbsp  Grenadine 
 Juice of 2  limes 
 1 tsp  powdered sugar 
 club soda 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Shake with  ice  and strain into a glass. Fill glass with  club soda . Top with  lime  wedge and a  cherry .
