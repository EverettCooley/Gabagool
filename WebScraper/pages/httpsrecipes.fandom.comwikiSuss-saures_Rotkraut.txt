Description : 
 Grandmother's recipe - old country/old world recipe. Sweet and sour red cabbage.
 
       Suss-saures rotkraut     
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  butter 
 ⅓ cup  brown sugar 
 4 medium  apples , peeled ,sliced 
 2  bay leaves 
 ½  red onion , chopped 
 ¼ cup  vinegar 
 1 head  red cabbage , finely shredded 
 ¼ cup  butter 
 1 cup  red wine 
 1  lemon , juice of 
 ½ medium  lemon 
 4 whole  cloves 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter  in 4-quart dutch oven. 
 Add  apples  and  onions , sauté slightly. 
 Add finely shredded  cabbage ,  red wine , cloves,  sugar , and  bay leaves . 
 Simmer, covered, for 1 hour. 
 Then add the remaining ingredients. 
 Heat to melt the  butter  and serve immediately.
