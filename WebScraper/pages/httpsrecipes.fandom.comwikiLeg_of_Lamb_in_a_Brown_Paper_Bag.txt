Leg of  Lamb  in a Brown Paper Bag
 
 1 Leg of  Lamb , 5 Lbs. 
 1 T.  rosemary 
 1 T.  paprika 
 2 cloves  garlic , Crushed 
 1/3 Cup  oil 
 1/2 T.  marjoram 
 1 Large Brown Paper Bag 
 
Pierce  Lamb  With a Small Knife in Several Places. Mix Remaining Ingredients And Brush Over  Lamb . Let Stand Overnight in Refrigerator. Place  Lamb  in Paper Bag And Twist End to Close Tightly. Place in a Large Roasting Dish And Cook 2 1/2 Hours at 325 (for Medium Rare). Tear Open The Bag And Remove The Meat to a Platter. Pour Juices Into a Pan And Skim Off Excess Fat. Serve Juices as Table Sauce. Serves 4 to 6
 Source: Come Into The Kitchen
