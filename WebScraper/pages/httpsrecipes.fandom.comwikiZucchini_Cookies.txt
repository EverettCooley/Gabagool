Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Pryor estate in Grapevine, Texas in 1996.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  butter 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1  egg  beaten 
 2 cups  flour 
 1 teaspoon  baking soda 
 ½ teaspoon  cloves 
 1 teaspoon  cinnamon 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 cup  raisins 
 1 cup chopped  nuts 
 1 cup grated  zucchini 
 Directions : 
 Cream  butter  and  sugar  until fluffy then add  egg  and  zucchini  and blend well. 
 Add dry ingredients and mix until well blended. 
 Mix raisins and nuts and stir into dough and drop by spoonfuls onto well-greased cookie sheet. 
 Bake at 375 °F for 15 minutes.
