Ingredients : 
 500 g peeled  shrimp 
 1 tbsp of  vinegar 
 1 tbsp of  sherry 
 1 tbsp of  olive oil 
 1 small sprig  dill 
 2 chopped cloves of  garlic 
 2 chopped  green onions 
 2 chopped  chile peppers 
 some  pepper 
 4 teaspoons  mayonnaise 
 2  avocados 
 Directions : 
 Mix  vinegar  with the  sherry  and  oil . 
 Chop the  dill  fine. 
 Add  garlic , the onions, chiles and half of  dill . 
 Season the dressing to taste with  pepper  and salt. 
 Add the cooked shrimp and let marinate for 20 minutes. 
 Mix the mayonnaise into the dressing. 
 Fill  avocado  halves with the shrimp and garnish them with remaining  dill .
