Ingredients : 
 1 (16 oz.) can tropical fruit salad 
 1 (8 oz.) can chunk  pineapple 
 1 (16 oz.) can  fruit cocktail 
 1 (11 oz.) can  mandarin oranges 
 3 cups miniature  marshmallows 
 1 pint  sour cream 
 1 cup chopped nuts (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Drain all fruit in colander for 30 minutes. 
 Add marshmallows and let stand 30 minutes. 
 Add sour cream and mix well. 
 Chill overnight. 
 Add chopped nuts (if desired) just before serving. 
 Notes : 
 Yield: 12-16 servings.
