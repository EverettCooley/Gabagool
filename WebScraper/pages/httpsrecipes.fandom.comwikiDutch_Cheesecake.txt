Dutch cheesecake, made with Gouda.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 large  eggs , separated 
 1/4 cup + 2 teaspoons  powdered sugar 
 1 1/2 cups grated  Gouda  cheese 
 1/3 cup  light cream  or  half-and-half 
 1/4 cup  unbleached flour 
 1 teaspoon  lemon juice 
 1 teaspoon grated  Lemon peel 
 1/3 cup golden  raisins 
 1 pre-baked pie crust 
 Directions : 
 NOTE: Use an 8-inch tart pan in this recipe. 
 
 Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. 
 In a large mixing bowl, beat together the egg yolks and 1/4 cup of the sugar until well blended ~- about 5 minutes. 
 Beat in the cheese, cream, flour, lemon juice, lemon peel and raisins 
 Beat the egg whites until frothy, then gradually add the remaining powdered sugar and continue to beat until the whites form stiff peaks. 
 Fold the egg whites into the cheese mixture. 
 Pour the cheese mixture into the prepared crust and bake for 45 minutes. 
 Cool to room temperature, then chill before serving.
