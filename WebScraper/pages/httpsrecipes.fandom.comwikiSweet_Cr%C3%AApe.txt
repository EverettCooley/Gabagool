      Crêpe     
 Ingredients for sweet crepe : 
 Two eggs 
 1 1/2 cups of milk 
 Teaspoon of oil (preferably canola oil) 
 Cup of all purpose flour 
 1/4 teaspoon of salt 
 Tablespoon of granulated sugar 
 Teaspoon of vanilla extract 
 Recipe for sweet crepe : 
 Get a mixing bowl. 
 Pour two eggs in the mixing bowl. 
 Pour your 1 and 1/2 cups of milk in the bowl. 
 Pour the cup of flour in the bowl. 
 Then put the 1/4 teaspoon of salt in the bowl. 
 Now mix until blended well, try to get lumps out by excess blending, or you can use a strainer. 
 Add your teaspoon of vanilla extract. 
 Add tablespoon of sugar. 
 Mix well, prepare your pan on the stove at medium heat. 
 Pour your batter on the pan, make sure it's hot enough. 
 Wait about 35 - 40 seconds, then flip the crepe on the other side and wait approximately 40 seconds. 
 You now have your crepe! Feel free to add bananas,  strawberries, blueberries, nutella, powdered sugar, etc!
