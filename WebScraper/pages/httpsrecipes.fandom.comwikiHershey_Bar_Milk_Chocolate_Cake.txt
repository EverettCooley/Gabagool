Ingredients : 
 2 sticks  butter 
 2 cups  sugar 
 4  eggs 
 1 16-oz. can  Hershey's syrup 
 2-1/2 cups.  flour 
 1/2 tsp.  baking soda 
 1/4 tsp.  salt 
 1 cup  buttermilk 
 1 tsp. red  food coloring 
 1 tsp.  vanilla 
 1 large (8 oz.) Hershey's  milk chocolate  bar, softened 
 Directions : 
 Cream butter and sugar 
 Add eggs, one at a time, and beat well. 
 Add chocolate syrup 
 Sift flour with baking soda and salt, and add to creamed mixture 
 Add buttermilk, food coloring, vanilla, and chocolate bar 
 Beat well. 
 Bake in a greased and floured tube pan at 325 degrees F, for 1-1/2 hours
