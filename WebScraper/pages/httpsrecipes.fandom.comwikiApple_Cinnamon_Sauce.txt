Apple   cinnamon  Sauce
 
In Small Pan, Mix One 10 Oz. Jar  Apple  Jelly, 2 T. Red  cinnamon  Candies, And 1/4 Cup  water . Heat And Stir Until Candies Dissolve And Jelly Melts. Remove From Heat. Cool. Makes About 1 Cup. to Make a Rainbow Parfait, Pour Small Amount of Sauce in Bottom of Each Parfait Glass. Layer Sauce With Flavors of Sherbet.
