Description : 
 The blue  corn chips  and berry-colored hummus together are very presentable for barbecues or parties. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x 16-oz. can  chickpeas 
 blueberries  and or  raspberries , to taste 
 blue  corn  chips 
 carrots , diced, (optional) 
 carrots  sticks (optional) 
 celery  sticks (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Combine  chickpeas  and  berries  in blender. 
 Add some diced  carrots , if desired. 
 Chill and serve with blue  corn  chips for a tasty snack or dessert; also good with  celery  or  carrot  sticks.
