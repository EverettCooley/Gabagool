 Ethnicity - Marathi, West Indian 
 Type of meal - Party, Lunch, Dinner 
 Ingredients : 
 1 big  cucumber 
 1  green chile , chopped 
 3 tbsp  peanut powder 
 grated coconut 
 salt  to taste 
 sugar  to taste 
 1 tsp  ghee 
 Â½ tsp  jeera 
 a pinch of  asafoetida 
 Directions : 
 Dice the  cucumber  very finely, or grate it and remove excess  water . 
 Add chilies,  peanut powder ,  salt ,  sugar ,  grated coconut  to the  cucumber  and mix well. 
 Now heat the  ghee  in a small pan, adding  jeera , and  asafoetida . 
 Add it to the  cucumber  mixture.
