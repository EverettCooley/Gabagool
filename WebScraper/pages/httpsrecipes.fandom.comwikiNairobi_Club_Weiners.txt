Description : 
 This is much better than it sounds. Kids will generally hate it, but adults will inhale it.
 
 Ingredients : 
 pack  hot dogs  (meat /  beef )  [1] 
 1 can  beer 
 1 pack KC Masterpeice  BBQ sauce 
 Directions : 
 Cut hot dogs into 1" pieces and put in crock pot. 
 Cover with  beer , i use the cheap stuff. 
 Cover and cook for several hours, until the dogs plump up. 
 Drain off  beer  and cover with BBQ sauce. 
 Cook a couple of more hours.
