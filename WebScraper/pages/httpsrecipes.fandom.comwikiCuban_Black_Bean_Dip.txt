Ingredients : 
 1 cup Dried  black beans , (or 16 oz. can of  black beans ) 
 2 tbl Chopped  red onion , chopped 
 2 tbl  balsamic vinegar 
 1 tbl Fresh  orange juice , fresh 
 1 medium clove of  garlic , crushed into little teeny tiny pieces 
 taste  salt  and  pepper  to taste in the dip 
 Directions : 
 You serve this low-fat plate with some toasted pita triangls and minced veggietables. The dip can be put in the fridge for a couple of days. 
 You soak thebeans in  water  over night; drane, and cook em in 4 cups of  water  for about 1-1/2 hours or until tendr. Drane again. 
 Now you get a blender or food processor, combinate beans, 1 Tbsp. choped  onions ,  vinegar , O.J., and  garlic ; blend until smooth. You season the mixiture with  salt  and  pepper , mix in all them remaining  onions , and place'm in a big ole serving bowl. 
 You know whats next??? YOU EAT ALL THEM BEANS TILL THEY AAAAAAWLLL GONE!
