Ingredients : 
 10  eggs 
 dash  pepper 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 3 medium  onions  - diced 
 ¼ cup  oil 
 1 can  mushroom  pieces - drained 
 dash  salt 
 dash  dill 
 ¼ cup  mayonnaise 
 Directions : 
 Place  eggs  in a pot of cold  water  with 1 teaspoon  salt  and boil them till they are hard-boiled. 
 Onions  can be diced in the food processor and later the  mushrooms  too. 
 Place  onions  in a pan and cook on low for one minute to remove the  bitter  taste. 
 Add  pepper . 
 Then pour  oil  on them and stir. 
 After a few minutes, add diced  mushroom  pieces. 
 Fry all together. 
 Peel hard-boiled  eggs  under cold  water . 
 Chop  eggs , fried  onion  mixture,  salt ,  mayonnaise  and dill together. 
 The texture should be a fine spread.
