Desription : 
 
 Serves 4-6 people 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  quinoa 
 1½ cups of water 
 1 teaspoon of  salt 
 ⅓ cup chopped  cilantro 
 ⅓ cup  feta 
 2  roma tomatoes , chopped 
 ¼ of a  yellow onion , chopped 
 3  garlic  cloves, diced 
 Directions : 
 Rinse quinoa. 
 Place quinoa, water, salt, onion and garlic in pot. 
 Cover and bring to a boil. 
 Reduce and simmer for 10 minutes. 
 Add tomatoes, simmer for 5 more minutes. 
 Turn heat off and fluff quinoa with a fork. 
 Stir in cilantro and feta.
