Description : 
 Yellow rice
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 tbsp  butter 
 2 cups  long-grain white rice 
 2 sticks  cinnamon 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  turmeric 
 1 cup  raisins 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter  over moderate heat in a heavy, medium-size pot. 
 Add  rice , and stir until each  grain  is well coated. 
 Then add  cinnamon ,  salt  and  turmeric  plus 2 cups of  water . 
 Bring to a boil, cover, and reduce heat to simmer. 
 Cook for 20 â€“ 30 minutes until  water  is absorbed and  rice  is tender. 
 Stir in the  raisins  and heat for another minute or so before serving.
