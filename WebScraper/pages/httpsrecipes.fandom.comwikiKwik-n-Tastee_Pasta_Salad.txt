Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp  Dijon mustard 
 1 tbsp minced  green onion 
 1 tsp minced  garlic 
 2 tbsp  vinegar 
 1  Maggi  cube 
 dash  salt  and  pepper 
 ½ package  pasta 
 6 small  tomatoes 
 1  onion 
 2 small  cucumbers  or  avocados 
 1  lemon 
 Directions : 
 Mix  mustard ,  green onion ,  garlic ,  vinegar , and maggi cube together in a cup. 
 Slowly add  oil  constantly stirring. 
 Add  salt  and  pepper . 
 Cook  pasta  in salted  water  about 5 – 7 minutes. 
 Cut up  tomatoes  in small pieces, dice  onion , and cut up  cucumber  or  avocado  into small pieces. 
 Squeeze  lemon  over all to keep fresh. 
 Set aside. 
 Drain  pasta . 
 To cool it down, pour cool, treated  water  over it and drain. 
 Repeat. 
 Add veggies to  pasta  and add vinaigrette. 
 Best to let it sit awhile before eating.
