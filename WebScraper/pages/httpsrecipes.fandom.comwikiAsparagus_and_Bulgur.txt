 Ingredients : 
 3 oz  asparagus , cut into ½-inch pieces 
 3 oz  bulgur , cooked 
 1 tbsp  sunflower oil 
 ¼ tsp  thyme 
 ¼ tsp  basil 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Steam  asparagus  for about 10 minutes. 
 Combine all ingredients and mix well. 
 Serve at room temperature.
