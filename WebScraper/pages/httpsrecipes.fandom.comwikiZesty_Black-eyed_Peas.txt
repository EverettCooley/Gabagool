Spice up your veggie offering with some  black-eyed peas  that have some kick.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb cleaned and sorted dry  black-eyed peas , cooked until tender  or  5 to 6 cups cooked  black-eyed peas 
 6 cups  water 
 1 whole medium  onion , diced 
 1 whole medium  green bell pepper , diced 
 2 to 4 cloves  garlic , finely diced 
 2 smoked  ham  hocks or meaty ham bone 
 Â¾ tsp  black pepper  (add to taste) 
 1 tablespoon  salt  to taste 
 1 large  jalapeno pepper , seeded and diced, optional 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in the slow cooker or crock pot. 
 Cover and cook on low for 6 to 8 hours. 
 One hour before done, remove bones and skins, scrape fat off skin and chop meat. 
 Put chopped  ham  back in pot.
