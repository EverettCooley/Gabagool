      A traditional Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich     
 Description : 
 A Peanut Butter & Jelly (PB&J) sandwich is a very easy snack that you can make.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 slices of  bread 
 1 spoon of  peanut butter  (chunky or smooth) 
 1 spoon of  jelly 
 Description : 
 Grab 1 slice of bread. 
 Put on a spoon of jelly on and spread it out. 
 Grab another slice of bread. 
 Put on a spoon of peanut butter and spread it out. 
 Put the 2 slices together.
