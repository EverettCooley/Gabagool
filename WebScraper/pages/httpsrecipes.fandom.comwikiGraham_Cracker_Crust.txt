Description : 
 Contributed by Jenn B aka Mom2Sam and Tiny at  World Recipes Y-Group  and  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Makes a 9 inch (1 large) pie crust, 8 servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup (200 ml)  graham wafer  crumbs 
 3 tbsp (45 ml) melted  butter  or  margarine 
 ¼ tsp (1 ml) each of  cinnamon  and  nutmeg 
 Directions : 
 Combine graham wafer crumbs, butter, cinnamon and nutmeg. 
 Press into a 9 inch (1 l) pie plate or 8 inch square pan or 9 inch spring form pan. 
 Chill in refrigerator 2 hours before filling. 
 If desired, reserve 2 tbsp (25 ml) of the crumb mixture to sprinkle on top of the filling. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Each serving: ⅛ pie crust excluding filling
 
 7 g carbohydrate | 1 g protein | 5 g fat | 77 calories
