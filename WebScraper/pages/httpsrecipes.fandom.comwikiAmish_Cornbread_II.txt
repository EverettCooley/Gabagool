      Amish Cornbread II     
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  Amish starter 
 2  eggs 
 1½ cups  milk 
 2 teaspoons  sugar 
 ½ cup  all purpose flour 
 1½ cups  cornmeal 
 ¼ cup  vegetable oil 
 ¾ teaspoon  baking soda 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon  baking powder 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 425 °F. 
 Grease a  baking pan . 
 Combine  starter ,  egg ,  milk ,  sugar ,  flour  and  cornmeal . 
 Beat for 2 minutes. 
 Mix in  oil ,  baking soda ,  salt  and  baking powder . 
 Transfer mixture to prepared  baking pan . 
 Bake for 25 – 30 minutes. 
 Cool 10 minutes in pan. 
 Remove and cool completely.
