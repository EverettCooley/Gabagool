 Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz  light rum 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 tbsp  lime juice 
 1 tbsp  pineapple juice 
 1/2 tsp Maraschino 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Shake with  ice  and strain into a glass half filled with  ice  cubes. Top with a slice of  lemon .
