Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Original recipe 
 Makes 6 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 6 pieces  bacon , chopped 
 ½ cup  onion , chopped, 2¾ ounces 
 4 ounce can  mushrooms , drained 
 16 ounce bag frozen whole  green beans , thawed 
 1½ teaspoons granular  Splenda 
 salt , to taste 
 Directions : 
 In a large skillet, cook bacon until crisp; set aside and reserve 2 tablespoons drippings in pan. 
 Sauté onion in drippings until tender. 
 Add mushrooms; cook and stir 2 minutes over medium heat. 
 Stir in beans, Splenda and salt to taste. 
 Go easy on the salt because the bacon will add salt. 
 Cover and cook 10 minutes on low until beans are done, stirring occasionally. 
 Toss in bacon. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 113 Calories | 8g Fat | 3g Protein | 7g Carbohydrate | 3g Dietary Fiber | 4g Net Carbs
