 Prep Time: 5 mins 
 Cook time: 5 mins 
 Serves: 10 
 
 Description : 
 Chocolate and Peanut Butter Rice treat.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  chocolate chips 
 1 cup  butterscotch chips 
 2 tbsp  peanut butter 
 4 cups  puffed rice  cereal 
 Directions : 
 Melt together the chips and peanut butter in the microwave. 
 Mix in the cereal. 
 Press into a greased pan (7x11 or 9x13) and refrigerated until hard. 
 Cut into squares and keep in the fridge.
