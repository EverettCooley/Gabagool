 Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased 
the family collection from the Wilson Estate in Heath, Texas in 1988.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 4  chicken  drumsticks 
 ¼ cup  apple butter 
 ¼ cup  molasses 
 ½ teaspoon ground  ginger 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 2  apples  cored and sliced into eighths 
 Directions : 
 In medium bowl stir together all ingredients except drumsticks. 
 Add drumsticks and toss to coat then cover and refrigerate 15 minutes. 
 Preheat broiler. 
 Line a broiler pan with aluminum foil then set drumsticks in and broil 15 minutes. 
 Turn drumsticks occasionally and brush them with marinade. 
 Serve with apple slices.
