 Description : 
 A  rum  drink
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 oz  light rum 
 Juice of 1/2  lemon 
 2 oz  pineapple juice 
 2 oz  orange juice 
 2 oz  red wine 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Shake all ingredients except wine. 
 Pour into a glass half filled with  ice cubes . 
 Add wine and stir gently. 
 Top with a  pineapple  ring.
