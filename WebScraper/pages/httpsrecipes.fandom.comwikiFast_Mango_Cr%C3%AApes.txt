Description : 
 Make 12 crepes. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tbsp  oil 
 2  eggs 
 1 cup  whole wheat flour 
 16 oz  cream cheese 
 1 cup  mango  jam 
 4 tbsp  butter , melted 
 4 tbsp  brown sugar 
 1 cups  milk 
 Directions : 
 Mix  milk ,  oil ,  eggs  and  flour  until smooth. 
 Mix  cream cheese  and  mango  jam. 
 Spoon mixture into center of crepe. 
 Overlap edges and place seam side down on microwave dish. 
 Drizzle melted  butter  over crepes. 
 Sprinkle with  brown sugar . 
 Microwave on high for 4 minutes.
