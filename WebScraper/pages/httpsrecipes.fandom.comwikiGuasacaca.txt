Guasacaca  is a spicy avocado sauce representative of Venezuelan cuisine. Makes 4 Cups.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  onions , minced 
 2 x  garlic  cloves, minced 
 1 x  chile , habanero, seeded, stemmed, minced 
 1 lrg  avocado , peeled, pitted 
 2 cup Tomotoes, peeled, chopped 
 1 cup  oil , olive 
 1/4 cup  vinegar , wine, red 
 1 tsp  mustard , prepared 
 2 tbl  parsley , Italian, minced 
 salt , to taste 
 Directions : 
 With a mortar and pestle, mash the  Onion ,  garlic ,  chile ,  avocado , and  tomatoes  into a paste. 
 Add remaining ingredients and blend well with a fork. 
 Notes 
 
 This suace contains fresh  avocado  and therefore must be used immediately and cannot be stored. 
 Use to marinate and/or baste grilled or barbecued  Shrimp ,  Beef  or  Chicken .
