Description : 
 Also called  shishche.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 kg  pork shoulder 
 100 ml  vodka 
 1 tablespoon  honey 
 1 tablespoon  black pepper 
 1 tablespoon  mustard 
 pieces of  onion 
 red  or  green pepper 
 bacon 
 sausage 
 cucumber 
 some baby  mushroom 
 Directions : 
 The secret of the shashlik is that the meat should soak in a marinade for at least 3 hours. 
 Mix the  honey ,  mustard ,  vodka ,  salt  and  pepper  in a deep bowl and soak the meat pieces into it. 
 Leave the bowl in the refrigerator. 
 After that arrange them on wooden or metal skewers and put some pieces of  bacon ,  sausage ,  pepper ,  onion ,  mushrooms ,  cucumber , according to your preference, between the pieces of meat. 
 Grill it on a barbecue. 
 Serve it with  potato  and other salads!.
