      Peruvian purple potatoes     
 Ingredients : 
 12 peruvian  purple potatoes  cooked, skinned, 
 1  red bell pepper  seeded, diced 
 1  yellow bell pepper  seeded 
 1  red onion  diced small 
 ¼ cup coarsely-chopped  cilantro  leaves 
 ⅓ cup  champagne vinegar 
 ⅔ cup olive  oil 
 salt 
 freshly-ground black  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Place warm  potatoes ,  peppers ,  onion  and  cilantro  in a bowl and toss to combine. 
 Whisk together  vinegar , olive  oil  and  salt  and  pepper . 
 Pour vinaigrette over  potatoes  and season again with  salt  and  pepper  to taste.
