Description : 
 Candied Yams  from the Wikibooks Cookbook, licensed under the GNU Free Documentation License.
 
 Ingredients : 
 6  yams 
 ½ cup  margarine 
 1 cup  brown sugar 
 2 tbsp  vanilla extract 
 ¼ cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). 
 Boil yams in skins until about half done. 
 Drain water from yams, remove skins, and cut in cubes. 
 Place cubes in a greased baking dish and dot with margarine. 
 Make a syrup by boiling brown sugar, water, and vanilla together. 
 Pour over the yams. 
 Bake for 45 to 50 minutes, basting frequently with syrup. 
 Cook until yams are tender.
