 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 375 degrees. 
 In mixing bowl, combine all ingredients. 
 Mix well by hand. On baking sheet, form into the shape of a large dog bone. 
 Bake at 375 degrees for 45 minutes. 
 Let cool and serve.
