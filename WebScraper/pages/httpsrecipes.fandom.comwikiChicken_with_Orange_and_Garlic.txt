Description : 
 Contributed by Ray at  Pressurecookerrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4  chicken breast  halves, without skin 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 2  green onions , thinly sliced 
 8 cloves  garlic , crushed and minced 
 ¼ cup fat-free  chicken broth 
 ¼ cup  orange juice 
 dash hot sauce, such as  Tabasco 
 2 tablespoons fresh minced  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Wash  chicken  and pat dry. 
 Pound  chicken  breasts gently to thin slightly. 
 Sprinkle  chicken  with  salt . 
 Heat  olive oil  in pressure cooker. 
 Brown  chicken  breasts. 
 Add remaining ingredients. 
 Pressure cook on high for 10 to 12 minutes. 
 Reduce pressure according to your manual. 
 Enjoy! great over  rice  or noodles. 
 Do not waste the wonderful liquids from cooking this recipe. 
 Use them on top of the  chicken  and or  rice .
