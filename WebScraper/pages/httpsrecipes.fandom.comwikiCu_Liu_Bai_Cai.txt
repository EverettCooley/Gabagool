Description : 
 Cabbage in sour sauce
 
 Ingredients : 
 300 g  cabbage 
 2 stalks  spring onion 
 Sauce : 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 2 tbsp  vinegar 
 ½ tbsp  cornflour 
 ½ tbsp  sesame oil 
 Directions : 
 Cut the  cabbage  into small bite-sizes, cut spring  Onion  into short stalks 
 Heat wok with 3 tbsp of  oil , add spring  Onion  and  cabbage 
 Stir fry in high heat for 3 minutes, add 5 tbsp of  water 
 Turn into middle heat and add  salt , then cook for 3 minutes, dish up 
 Add sauce into the  cabbage  cooked  water , stir well and fry until boil again 
 Pour in the  cabbage  and stir well, dish up and serve
