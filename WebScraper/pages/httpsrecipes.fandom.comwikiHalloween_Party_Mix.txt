Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredient : 
 1 x 11 oz pkg. mini  pretzels 
 1 x 10 oz pkg. mini  Nutter Butters 
 1 cup dry roasted  peanuts 
 1 cup  sugar 
 ½ cup  butter 
 ½ cup  light corn syrup 
 2 tbsp  vanilla extract 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 1 x 10 oz pkg.  M&Ms 
 1 x 18 oz pkg.  candy corn 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl combine pretzels, Nutter Butters, and peanuts. 
 In a large saucepan combine sugar, butter and corn syrup. 
 Bring to a boil over medium heat; boil for 5 minutes. 
 Remove from heat; stir in vanilla extract and baking soda (mixture will foam). 
 Pour over pretzel mixture and stir until coated. 
 Pour into a greased 15x10 baking dish. 
 Bake at 250°F for 45 minutes, stirring every 15 minutes. 
 Let cool but break apart while still warm. 
 Toss with M&Ms and candy corn. 
 Cool completely. 
 Store in airtight container.
