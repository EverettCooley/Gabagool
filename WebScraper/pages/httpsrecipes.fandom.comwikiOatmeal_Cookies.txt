Ingredients : 
 2 cup  shortening 
 1 cup  brown sugar 
 4  eggs  well beaten 
 2 tablespoon  vanilla 
 3 cups  flour 
 1 tsp  salt 
 2 tsp  baking soda 
 3 tablespoon  cinnamon 
 3 cups  oats  (Quaker quick or old fashioned. Do  not  use instant) 
 1 cup chopped  walnuts  (use ½ cup if using  raisins ) 
 1 cup butter 
 Directions : 
 Cream  shortening  and sugars, add  eggs  and  vanilla  and beat well. 
 Sift  flour ,  salt ,  baking soda  and  cinnamon . 
 Add to first mixture and mix well. 
 if you wish ad some smarties 
 Add  oats  last. 
 Spoon out by rounded tablespoonfuls on to greased cookie sheets. 
 Bake at 350°F for 20-30 minutes. 
 Remove to wire rack. 
 Cool completely.
