 Ingredients : 
 1 medium  cabbage  — chopped fine 
 2 tablespoons  flour 
 1 cup  Velveeta cheese  — cubed (small) 
 1 cup  milk 
 1 can  cream of chicken  soup 
 1 medium  onion  — chopped 
 ¾ cup  bread crumbs 
 1 tablespoon  bacon grease  or  butter 
 salt  and  pepper  — to taste 
 Directions : 
 Boil  cabbage  10 minutes, drain and rinse. 
 Sauté  onions  until clear in  bacon grease  or  butter . 
 Remove from pan. 
 Mix  flour  with remaining  bacon grease . 
 Add a little  butter  if necessary to make a paste. 
 Add  milk , soup and  cheese . 
 Return  onions  to pan. 
 Season to taste. 
 Cook until thickened, stirring constantly. 
 Put  cabbage  in a casserole dish. 
 Pour sauce over and mix. 
 Sprinkle breadcrumbs over top and dot with  margarine . 
 Cook in 350°F degree oven for 20 minutes. 
 If breadcrumbs are not brown, turn on the broiler for a couple of minutes.
