 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 veggie burger, baked, fried, barbequed or grilled 
 1 whole  Wheat  roll, cut in half 
 1 thick round slice of fresh  pineapple 
 2 tbsp sliced  water chestnuts 
 sprinkling of sliced and toasted  almonds 
 1 tbsp fresh  Apple  sauce 
 1 tbsp vegan  Mayonnaise 
 1/2 tbsp dijon  mustard  (or other prepared  mustard ) 
 shredded  lettuce  or sprouts 
 
 Directions : 
 Spread one half of the roll with the  mayo , the other half with the  mustard . Place the burger on the bottom half, and top with the  pineapple ,  water chestnuts  and a dollop of the  Apple  sauce. Sprinkle with the toasted  almonds  and top with the shredded  lettuce  or sprouts. Place the top half of the roll on it, and sit down to eat!
