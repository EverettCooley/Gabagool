 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups  whole wheat flour 
 1/2 cup chopped  nuts 
 4  eggs , beaten 
 2 cups cooked, mashed  pumpkin 
 1 1/4 cups melted  shortening  or  soybean oil 
 1 teasp  baking soda 
 1 teasp  salt 
 3 teasps  cinnamon 
 2 cups  Sugar 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 degr. 
 Grease 2 loaf pans 
 Mix dry ingredients into a large bowl 
 Add  nuts  and stir well 
 make a well in the centre and add  eggs , mashed  pumpkin  and  shortening , stir until moistened 
 Pour into loaf pans and bake 45-50 mins or until done
