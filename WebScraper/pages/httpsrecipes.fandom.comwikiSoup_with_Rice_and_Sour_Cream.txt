Description : 
 In Romanian: Supa cu orez si smantana
 
 Ingredients : 
 beef  or  poultry soup 
 4 tablespoons  rice 
 6 tablespoons  sour cream 
 6  lemon  slices 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Boil the rice in salt water. 
 When done, strain, rinse in cold  water  and let drain. 
 Put the rice and  lemon  slices into the bowl and pour the hot, strained soup on top. 
 Add the  sour cream , mixing well.
