Ingredients : 
 1 large can  tomato purée 
 6 tbsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 12 oz  beer 
 ¼ cup  cider vinegar 
 1 tsp  paprika 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in saucepan. 
 Simmer over low heat for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
