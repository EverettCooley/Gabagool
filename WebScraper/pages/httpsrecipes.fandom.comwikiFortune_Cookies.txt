 Makes 20 Fortune Cookies 
 Ingredients : 
 2  eggs 
 ½ cup  superfine sugar 
 4 tbsp  water 
 ½ tsp  almond flavoring 
 1 cup sifted  cake flour 
 Directions : 
 Butter a baking sheet and dust it lightly with  flour . 
 Heat oven to 350°F. 
 Beat  eggs  and gradually mix in  sugar  while beating until mixture is very thick. 
 Beat  water  in a tablespoon at a time. 
 Add and beat in the almond flavoring, preferably with an electric mixer, as it must be thorough. 
 Very gently fold in the  flour  to mix, but do not beat. 
 Drop the batter by level table spoonfuls on the buttered sheet 5 inches apart. 
 Bake until very lightly browned. 
 Remove from oven and shape while still quite warm,inserting fortunes. 
 Let cool in glasses to hold the form.
