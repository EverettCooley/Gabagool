 Description : 
 Makes enough for 4 people.
 
 Ingredients : 
 450 g / 1 lb  rice 
 2 pints  coconut milk 
 1 medium size fresh * 225 g/ 8 oz fresh  prawns  (shelled) 
 100 g / 4 oz ground  crayfish 
 2  chilies  (sliced) 
 1  onion  (sliced) 
 2  tomatoes  (sliced) 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Wash the rice and place in a large pot. 
 Add the  coconut milk  together with the sliced chillies,  onions   tomatoes  and  salt . 
 Bring to boil, reduced heat and allow to cook for 15 minutes. 
 Sprinkle in the ground  crayfish  and stir arrange the washed fish and prawns on the top, cover and cook gently for another 15 minutes until all the liquid is well absorbed.
