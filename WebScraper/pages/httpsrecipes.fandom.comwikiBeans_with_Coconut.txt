beans with  coconut  &  cilantro 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 	ea 	 garlic  cloves, crushed 
 1 	ts 	 chili powder 
 1 	tb 	 cumin 
 1 	tb 	 Coriander 
 2 	ts 	 turmeric 
 1 	tb 	Fresh  cilantro , chopped 
 1 	c 	Desiccated  coconut 
 1 	c 	 Black-Eyed Peas , cooked 
 1 	c 	 potatoes , diced & parboiled 
 lime juice  or  lemon juice  to taste 
 2 	tb 	 coconut oil  or  vegetable oil 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Heat the  oil  & sizzle the  garlic  together with the  chili powder  for half a minute. 
 Next, add the  cumin , coriander,  turmeric  &  cilantro  leaves. 
 Cook for another minute or two, stirring often to blend. 
 Add the  coconut  & mix this in too. 
 Add the cooked beans &  potatoes . 
 Season & then pour in the  lime  or  lemon juice . 
 Cook for another 10 minutes or so until everything is blended together. 
 They serve with green vegetables.
