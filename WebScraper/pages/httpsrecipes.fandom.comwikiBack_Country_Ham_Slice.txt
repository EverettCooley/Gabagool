Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves 3. 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds  ham  slices  — ¾ inch thick 
 ⅜ cup  fat 
 ¼ cup  prepared mustard 
 ½ cup  brown sugar 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 2 teaspoons  paprika 
 1 cup water 
 Directions : 
 Fry ham in fat. 
 Remove ham slice or (slices) from skillet. 
 Pour all the other ingredients into skillet, cook 5 minutes, place ham slice or (slices) back into sauce and cook for 5 minutes more. 
 Serve. 
 Enjoy.
