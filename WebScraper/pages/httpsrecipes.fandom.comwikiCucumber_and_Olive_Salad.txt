 Ingredients : 
 5  cucumbers 
 ⅔ cup pitted * 3 pickled red peppers 
 1 tsp chopped  mint 
 ⅓ cup  oil 
 2 tbsp  vinegar 
 salt , to taste 
 Directions : 
 Peeled and cut  cucumbers  length-wise and remove seeds, slice finely and sprinkle with  mint 
 Add chopped, sliced  peppers  and  salt . 
 Dress with the mixture from  oil  and  vinegar . 
 Add salt to taste
