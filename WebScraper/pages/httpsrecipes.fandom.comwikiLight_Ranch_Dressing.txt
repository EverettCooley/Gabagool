Ingredients : 
 1 tbsp finely chopped  parsley  or 1 tsp dried flakes 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 ¾ tsp  onion powder 
 ½ tsp dried  basil , crushed 
 ¼ tsp  celery seed 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 ¼ tsp  white wine vinegar 
 ⅛ tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Combine ingredients and refrigerate.
