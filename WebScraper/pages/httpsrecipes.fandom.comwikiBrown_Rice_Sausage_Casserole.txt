 Ingredients : 
 1 lb bulk  pork sausage 
 2 cups cooked  brown rice 
 ¾ cup  milk 
 1 large stalk  celery  (about ¾ cup), chopped 
 ⅓ cup  green pepper 
 1 small  onion , chopped (about ¼ cup) 
 1 can  cream of mushroom  soup 
 1 can sliced  mushrooms , drained 
 Directions : 
 Cook and stir  pork sausage  in Dutch oven until brown; drain. 
 Stir in  rice ,  milk ,  celery ,  green pepper ,  onion , soup and  mushrooms . 
 Pour into ungreased 2 quart casserole. 
 Cook uncovered in 350°F oven until bubbly, about 45 minutes. 
 Enjoy.
