 Description : 
 In Romanian: Salata de varza rosie
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 medium  red cabbage , 
 2 tablespoons  oil , 
 2-3 tablespoons  vinegar , 
 1/2 teaspoon  Sugar , 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Wash and julienne the  cabbage . 
 Mix with  salt  and rub with your hands until soft. 
 Add  oil ,  vinegar  and  Sugar . Mix well. 
 Another way: scald the chopped  cabbage  with boiling  water  and then let sit for 10â€“15 minutes. 
 Drain and mix the  cabbage  with the  oil ,  vinegar   salt  and  Sugar . Serve with roasts.
