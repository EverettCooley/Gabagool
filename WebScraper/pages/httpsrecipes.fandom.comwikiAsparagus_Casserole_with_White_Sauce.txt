Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Williams Estate in Tyler, Texas in 1994.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can  asparagus  spears 
 1 can  cream of mushroom  soup 
 2 boiled  eggs 
 ¼ cup  butter 
 1 cup  Velveeta cheese 
 cracker crumbs 
 White Sauce : 
 3 tablespoons  butter 
 3 tablespoons  flour 
 1½ cups  milk 
 Directions : 
 In a casserole dish layer ½ can of  asparagus  then smooth ½ can of soup over  asparagus . 
 Slice one  egg  over then dot with  butter  and add cheese. 
 Pour one-half of the white sauce over. 
 Start layers again ending with cracker crumbs. 
 Bake at 350°F until bubbly and brown.
