 Description : 
 From "The Art of Cooking for the Diabetic" by Lisa Crawley
 
 Makes 1 cup 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  onion , finely chopped 
 ¼ cup  green pepper , finely chopped 
 ½ cup  water 
 ⅓ cup  catsup 
 3 tbsp  vinegar 
 2 tsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 2 to 3 drops  hot pepper sauce 
 Directions : 
 Cook  onion  and  green pepper  in  water  over low heat about 8 minutes, stirring frequently. 
 Add remaining ingredients and mix well; cook 2 more minutes. 
 Nutritional information : 
 1 serving = ¼ Makes 1 cup 
 Cho: 8, Pro: 0, fat: 0, Cal: 33, Fiber: 0.5, Sodium: 232 mg, Chol: 0 
 1 vegetable exchange if using ¼ cup, free if using 2 tbsp or less.
