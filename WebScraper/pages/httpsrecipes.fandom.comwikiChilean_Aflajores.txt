Description : 
       Chilean Aflajores      This recipe yields 1 serving.
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup + 3 tbsp  butter 
 ¼ cup + 3 tbsp  sugar 
 1  egg , beaten 
 ¾ cup + 2 tbsp  flour 
 1 can  condensed milk 
 Directions : 
 Cream together  butter  and  Sugar ; add  egg  and  flour  and mix well. 
 Drop by scant teaspoonfuls onto cookie sheet. 
 Pat with a floured finger into very thin little circles (quarter to half dollar size). 
 Bake at 325°F for 10 – 12 minutes, until just starting to brown around edges. 
 Sandwich two cookies together with cooked  condensed milk . 
 To cook  condensed milk , place entire, unopened can in a large saucepan, cover with  water . 
 Bring to boil and simmer about 2 hours. 
 This turns it into caramel.
