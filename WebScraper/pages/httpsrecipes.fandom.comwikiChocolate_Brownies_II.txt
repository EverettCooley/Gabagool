 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 
 3/4 cup of melted  butter  or  margarine 
 1 1/2 cup of  Sugar 
 1 1/2 teaspoon of  vanilla 
 3  eggs 
 3/4 cup of white  flour 
 1/2 cup of  cocoa  ( chocolate   cocoa powder ) 
 1/2 teaspoon of  baking powder 
 1/2 teaspoon of  salt 
 Directions : 
 Blend melted  margarine ,  Sugar , and  vanilla  in a mixing bowl. 
 Combine the  flour ,  cocoa ,  baking powder  and  salt . 
 Gradually add the dry mixture to the  egg  mixture. 
 Mix until well blended. 
 Spread in a greased 8-inch square pan. 
 Bake at 350 degrees F for 40â€“45 minutes or until brownie begins to pull away from edges of the pan. 
 Cool and cut into squares. Enjoy!
