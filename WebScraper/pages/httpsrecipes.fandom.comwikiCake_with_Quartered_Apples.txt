 In Romanian: Prajitura cu mere sferturi
 
 Ingredients : 
 12 medium peeled and quartered  apples 
 1Â½ cups  sour cream 
 5 tablespoons  sugar 
 6  eggs 
 3 heaping tablespoons  flour 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 vanilla  flavored  confectioner's sugar 
 butter  and  flour  to grease and dust the pan 
 Directions : 
 Mix the  yolks  with the  sugar  and  vanilla , add the  sour cream  and then gradually (like a rain) add the  flour  and mix well. 
 Finally, add the whipped  egg whites  and quartered  apples . 
 Mix gently.  butter  a pan, dust well with  flour  and pour the mixture in. 
 Bake at medium heat until done. 
 Serve warm sprinkled with  vanilla  flavored  confectioner's sugar .
