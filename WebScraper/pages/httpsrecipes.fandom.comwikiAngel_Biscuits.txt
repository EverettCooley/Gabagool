       Angel Biscuits - Vegetarian Pleasures     
 Ingredients : 
 1 pkt quick-rising  yeast 
 2 tbsp warm  water 
 4 cup all-purpose whole- wheat flour 
 1 cup  all purpose flour 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 1 tbsp  baking powder 
 4 tbsp  sugar 
 2 tsp  salt 
 1 cup  shortening 
 2 cup reduced-fat  buttermilk 
 melted  butter  to brush tops optional 
 nonstick butter-flavored  cooking spray 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 400 °F degrees. 
 Dissolve  yeast  in lukewarm  water . 
 Sift  flour ,  soda ,  baking powder ,  sugar , and  salt  into bowl. 
 Cut in  shortening , slowly mixing with a wooden spoon. 
 Add  buttermilk , then  yeast  while slowly stirring mixture until all  flour  is dampened. 
 Knead on floured board for 1 to 2 minutes. 
 Roll out to desired thickness and cut with biscuit cutter. 
 Dough may be refrigerated in an airtight container for several days to use as needed. 
 Spray cookie sheet with  cooking spray  and place biscuits to cook. 
 Bake about 12 to 15 minutes.
