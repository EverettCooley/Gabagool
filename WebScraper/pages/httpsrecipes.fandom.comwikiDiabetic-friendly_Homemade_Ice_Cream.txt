Ingredients : 
 13 oz  evaporated milk 
 2 tbsp  sugar replacement 
 1½ cup whole  milk 
 1 tbsp  vanilla extract 
 3  eggs  (well beaten) 
 Directions : 
 Combine  evaporated milk  and  sugar  replacement. 
 Beat well until  sugar  is dissolved. 
 Add whole  milk  and  vanilla extract ; beat well. 
 Add  eggs ; beat  eggs  into  milk  mixture vigorously. 
 Pour into  ice cream  maker. 
 Freeze according to manufacturer"s directions. 
 Nutritional information : 
 1 serving = ½  milk , ½ lean meat 
 calories = 12
