 Description : 
 According to the website for  Deep River Snacks  these chips are made using thickly sliced grade A  potatoes  which are cooked in natural oil. The chips contain no  cholesterol ,  MSG , artificial flavors, artificial preservatives or trans fats.
 Like other flavors of Deep River Kettle Cooked Potato Chips, these chips were described by  taquitos.net  as being crunchy, but not very crisp. The taquitos reviewer wrote that the onion flavor seemed good at first, but upon later observation it was probably too sweet. It should be mentioned, however, that many taquitos tasters really liked these chips.
