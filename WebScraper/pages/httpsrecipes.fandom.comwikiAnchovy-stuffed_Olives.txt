 Ingredients : 
 pitted  olives 
 3 oz. (87 g.) pkg.  cream cheese 
 2 oz. (58 g.) tube  anchovy paste 
 Directions : 
 Whip cheese and anchovy paste until smooth. 
 Stuff into well-drained, pitted olives (ripe or green), using a pastry tube or a tip of spoon. Serve plain with other appetizers. 
 Other Stuffing Suggestions : 
 walnut  halves 
 pecan  halves 
 water chestnuts 
 small whole  shrimp 
 wiener  sticks 
 mushrooms 
 zucchini 
 small  cauliflowers
