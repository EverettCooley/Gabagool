Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1 oz Jamaica  light rum 
 juice of ¼  lemon 
 1 tsp  anisette 
 ½ tsp  grenadine 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Shake with  ice  and strain into a glass.
