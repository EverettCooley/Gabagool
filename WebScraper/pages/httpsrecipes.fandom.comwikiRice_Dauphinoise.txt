Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 2 tablespoons  flour 
 1 small clove  garlic , minced 
 1 teaspoon  paprika 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ⅛ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1 cup  milk 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 cup (4 ounces) shredded  Swiss cheese 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter  in small saucepan. Blend in  flour ,  garlic ,  paprika ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Add  milk ; cook, stirring, until smooth and slightly thickened. 
 Spoon  rice  into greased shallow 2-quart baking dish. Pour sauce mixture over  rice . 
 Cover and bake at 350 °F for 20 minutes. 
 Uncover; top with  cheese . Bake 5 minutes longer.
