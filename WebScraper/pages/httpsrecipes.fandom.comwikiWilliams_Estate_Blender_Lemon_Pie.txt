Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Williams Estate in Tyler, Texas in 1994.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1  lemon  sliced 
 ½ cup  water 
 1¼ cups  granulated sugar 
 2 tablespoons  all purpose flour 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 2  eggs 
 ¼ cup  butter  softened 
 ½ teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 2 tablespoons  granulated sugar 
 Directions : 
 Place all ingredients in blender except  cinnamon  and  sugar  and blend well. 
 Pour into unbaked pie crust then sprinkle with  cinnamon  and sugar and bake at 400°F for 35 minutes.
