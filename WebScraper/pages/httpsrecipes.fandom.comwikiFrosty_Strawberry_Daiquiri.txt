Ingredients : 
 3 ounces of frozen sliced California  strawberries  in syrup, partially thawed 
 2 ounces  light rum 
 1 ounce  lemon juice  or  sweet and sour mix 
 1 scoop  ice 
 whole fresh California  strawberry 
 lime  slice 
 Nonalcoholic version : 
 Omit  rum . Add a squeeze of  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 In a blender combine sliced  berries ,  rum , juice and  ice ; blend smooth. 
 Pour into stemmed glass. 
 Garnish with whole berry and  lime  slice.
