 About Steuben yellow bean : 
 Steuben yellow bean is an heirloom bean is sometimes used to make Boston baked beans. It is not actually yellow in color, but has a multi-colored look, displaying reddish brown and black hues over a white background. 
Substitutes for this bean include navy beans or Great Northern beans.
