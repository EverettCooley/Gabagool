From Menu and Recipes Week 5/27/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pkg.  crescent rolls 
 1½ lbs  ground beef 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 1 medium  onion  chopped 
 6 oz can  tomato paste 
 1 pkg. sloppy joe mix 
 1¼ cup hot water 
 4 oz pkg.  mozzarella  cheese 
 4 oz pkg.  cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Brown ground beef and onion. 
 Add salt and pepper. 
 Add tomato paste, sloppy joe mix and hot water. 
 Simmer a few minutes and set aside. 
 Place 1 pkg. crescent rolls in bottom of 9x13 baking dish. 
 Cover with ground beef mixture. 
 Top with cheese. 
 Place second pkg. of crescent rolls on top of cheese. 
 Bake at 350°F for 30 minutes.
