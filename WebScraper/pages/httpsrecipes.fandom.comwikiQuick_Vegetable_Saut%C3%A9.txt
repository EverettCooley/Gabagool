Description : 
 This makes a huge amount, so will no doubt be eating for rest of week. 
 
 Source: apparent improvisation 
 Ingredients : 
 vegetable stock 
 2 larges  onions , diced 
 1  carrot , grated 
 1  green pepper , diced 
 2 tsp  cumin 
 2 tsp or more  chilli 
 2 tsp minced  garlic 
 1 each  taco seasoning  mix 
 1 to 2 cups frozen  corn 
 1 can  kidney beans , drained and rinsed 
 1 can  tomatoes 
 1 cup leftover  rice  and  peas 
 ¼ cup  cracked wheat 
 Directions : 
 Sauté in onions, carrot, green pepper, cumin, chili powder, garlic and taco seasoning in  vegetable stock . 
 Throw in corn, kidney beans, tomatoes, leftover rice and peas and cracked wheat.
