 Description : 
 A spicy  Indonesian  dish, from Padang, West Sumatra.  Beef  is more popular. However you can also use  Chicken .
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 Ingredients A:
 
 4 shallots 
 2 cloves  garlic 
 1 cm  ginger 
 1/2 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  chile paste  or 3 fresh chilies 
 1/2 tsp  turmeric 
 500g stewing  Beef , cubed 
 1 cup  coconut milk 
 2  bay leaves 
 1/4 cup  oil 
 Directions : 
 Blend  Onion ,  garlic ,  ginger ,  salt ,  turmeric  and  chile paste  until smooth. Heat  oil  in wok and add blended mixture. Stir fry for 2 minutes or until paste separates from the  oil . Add  Beef  and stir fry for 5 minutes. 
 Add  bay leaves  and allow to simmer for about 45 minutes or until meat is tender, adding  water  as necessary to keep sauce from drying. When  Beef  is tender, add  coconut milk . Bring to a boil and remove from heat. Serve with  rice .
