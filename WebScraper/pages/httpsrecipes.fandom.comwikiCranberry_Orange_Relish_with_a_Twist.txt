Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 pkgs ocean spray  cranberries 
 1/2  orange , fresh, with peel, seeded 
 1  Apple , cored, with skin 
 pecans  (to taste, optional) 
 1/2 cup  orange marmalade 
 2 tsp  Sugar 
 Directions : 
 Blend the  cranberries ,  orange ,  Apple  and  pecans  in a food processor. 
 Place in a bowl. Add  marmalade  and  Sugar  ( marmalade  may be increased to 3/4 cup). 
 Enjoy!
