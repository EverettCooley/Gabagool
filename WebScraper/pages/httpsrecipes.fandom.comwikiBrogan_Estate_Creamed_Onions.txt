Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Brogan Estate in Arlington, Texas in 1988. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 jars whole  onions 
 1 can  cream of celery  soup undiluted 
 1 cup shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 ½ cup slivered  almonds 
 Directions : 
 Fold onions into soup then pour into a greased 2-quart casserole. 
 Sprinkle cheese and almonds on top and bake at 375°F for 20 minutes.
