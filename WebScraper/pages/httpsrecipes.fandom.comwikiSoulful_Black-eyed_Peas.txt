Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound of smoked  pig tails 
 1 pound bag of  black-eyed peas 
 1½ cups of chopped  onion 
 1 cup of chopped  green pepper 
 ½ cup of  butter 
 ¼ cup of  flour 
 ½ teaspoon of  crushed red pepper 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 In a large pot add 1 quart of water. 
 Boil until tender the pig tails. 
 If water boils out add more, then add black-eyed peas. 
 When peas are halfway done saute onions and green peppers then add them to the pot. 
 Cook until peas are soft, mix flour and ½ cup of juices from the pot, then pour back into pot and add butter.
