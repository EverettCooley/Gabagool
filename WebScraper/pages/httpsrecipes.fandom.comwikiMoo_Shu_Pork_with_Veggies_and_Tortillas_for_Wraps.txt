Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Makes 10 rolls 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ pound boneless  pork chops , cut into thin strips 
 3 cloves  garlic , minced 
 2 tbs dark Asian  sesame oil 
 8  scallions , sliced 
 8 oz bag pre-shedded  carrots 
 10 oz bag pre-shredded  coleslaw 
 ½ cup  hoisin sauce  + more for brushing tortillas 
 3 tbs reduced sodium  soy sauce 
 10 flour  tortillas 
 Directions : 
 Sauté the pork and half the garlic in 1 tbs oil in skillet until cooked though. 
 Place in bowl. 
 Wipe out skillet, heat remaining oil in skillet. 
 Add scallions, carrots, slaw and remaining garlic, cook 8 minutes or until softened. 
 Add hoisin sauce and soy sauce, heat through. 
 Add the pork, heat through. 
 Heat tortillas following package directions. 
 Brush each with hoisin sauce. 
 Spoon pork mixture down center of each. 
 Roll up, serve.
