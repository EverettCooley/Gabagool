Description : 
 Sri Lankan deep-fried rice flour egg
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  egg 
 250 g  rice flour 
 ½ tsp  turmeric 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 1 lt  oil 
 coconut milk 
 Directions : 
 Beat the  egg . 
 Sieve  flour  into a bowl and add the  egg ,  turmeric ,  salt  and sufficient  coconut milk  to make a thick batter. 
 Heat the  oil  and when bubbling hold the kokis mould in the  oil  until the mould is hot. 
 Carefully dip the mould into the batter, taking care not to submerge it completely. 
 Remove toothpick. 
 Repeat the process until all the batter is used.
