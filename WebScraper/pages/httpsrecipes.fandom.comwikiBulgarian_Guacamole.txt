 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 ripe avocadoes 
 1 medium  tomato 
 2 cloves of  garlic 
 2 tiny  limes  or 1  lemon 
 1 swirl of  olive oil 
 1 pinch of  salt 
 1 spoonful chopped fresh  cilantro 
 Directions : 
 Slice the avocadoes in two, spoon the flesh in a bowl and mash with a fork. 
 Finely dice the  tomato , press the  garlic  cloves through a  garlic  press, juice the  limes , and chop the  cilantro . 
 Mix everything together with the  salt  and  olive oil .
