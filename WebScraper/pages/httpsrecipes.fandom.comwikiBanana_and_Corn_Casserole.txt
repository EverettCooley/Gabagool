Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 8  bananas 
 8 slices  bacon 
 800 g. sweet  corn 
 4 tsp.  cornmeal 
 Directions : 
 Wrap the peeled bananas in the bacon and grill until tender. 
 Place in a casserole, season and then add the sweet corn. 
 Thicken the mixture with 2 teaspoons cornmeal mixed with a little cold water. Pour over the bananas and sprinkle with breadcrumbs. 
 Bake in an oven, pre-heated to 170Â°C for 15 to 20 minutes and serve.
