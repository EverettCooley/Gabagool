      Tibetan incense     
 Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp frankincense 
 ¼ cup juniper leaf 
 ½ cup sandalwood 
 2 tbsp  cinnamon 
 2 tbsp patchouli 
 1 cup + 1 tb. powdered cedarwood (Juniperus virginiana) 
 ¾ cup  water 
 1 tsp  potassium nitrate 
 tragacanth powder; as needed 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients together
