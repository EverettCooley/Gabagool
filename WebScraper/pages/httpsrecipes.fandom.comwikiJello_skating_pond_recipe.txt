 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pack Jello Berry BLUE jelly -powder 
 1 cup Boiling  water 
 2 cup  ice  cubes 
 Directions : 
 1 Gummy bears  Cool Whip  spearmint trees (jellies) 
2 dissolve jello powder with boiling  water . 
3 Stir in  ice  cubes. 
4 Continue stirring for 3-5 minutes and then remove any leftover  ice  cubes. 
5 Pour into clear pie plate. 
6 Chill until set. 
7 Just before serving, decorate with  Cool Whip  (as snow) around 2/3 of the edge. 
8 Decorate with gummy bears skating, and a wafer roll cookie as a log.
