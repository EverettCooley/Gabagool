 Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp  molasses  30 ml 
 2 tbsp dark  brown sugar  (mix with 1 tbsp  water  30 ml + 15 ml) 
 1 tsp  vanilla  5 ml 
 1 tsp  water 
 1 tsp  egg yolks  (unbeaten) 
 ¾ cup  flour  200 ml 
 ¼ cup  powder milk  60 ml 
 ½ tsp  salt  2 ml 
 ½ tsp  baking powder  2 ml 
 Directions : 
 Wash hands. 
 Collect all ingredients and equipment. 
 Heat oven to 350°F / 180°C. 
 Combine  molasses  or  brown sugar ,  vanilla , and  water . 
 Add  egg yolk  and mix well. 
 Combine dry ingredients and add to  egg  mixture. 
 Mix well (mixture should be dry and crumbly and looks like  cornmeal ). 
 Form dough into balls using hands to combine. 
 Roll dough into balls using hands to combine. 
 Cut into bars 1" - 2½". 
 Place on greased cookie sheet. 
 Bake at 350°F / 180°C (moderate) for 15 minutes. 
 Reduce heat to 140°F / 60°C. 
 Leave in oven for another 20 minutes.
