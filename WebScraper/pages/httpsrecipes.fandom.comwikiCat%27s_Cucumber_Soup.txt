Ingredients : 
 cucumbers 
 scallions 
 dill 
 buttermilk 
 salt and pepper to taste 
 Directions : 
 Puree cucumbers, scallions and dill. 
 Stir in buttermilk to soup consistency. 
 Add salt and black pepper to taste.
