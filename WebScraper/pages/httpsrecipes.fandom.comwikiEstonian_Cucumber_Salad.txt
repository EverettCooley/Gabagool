Description : 
 This salad comes under the category of "raw salad". And it can be use daily with meal.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3  cucumber 
 sea salt  ( to taste) 
 1 teaspoon chopped  dill 
 Directions : 
 Sliced organic  cucumber  finely. 
 Now sprinkled with  sea salt  flakes, drained and mixed with chopped  dill .
