 Description : 
 In Romanian: Prajitura cu mere intregi
 
 Ingredients : 
 Same quantities as for  Cake with Quartered Apples 
 Directions : 
 Prepare the same way as for cake with quartered  apples . 
 Do not quarter the  apples . 
 Peel, hollow them, remove seeds and replace them with  sour cherries  from preserves. 
 Arrange these  apples  in the pan and pour the mixture over them; bake and serve warm.
