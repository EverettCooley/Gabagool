 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 150 grms  Tuna  flakes in  oil 
 1/2 tsp taco seasonong 
 1 pc  Onion  bulb (b) chopped 
 2 pcs  tomatoes  seeded&chopped 
 salt /peeper 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 20 grms  oregano (dried) 
 100 grms freshh  lettuce  shredded 
 1 tbsp cajun spice 
 3 pcs taco shells 
 6 pcs large molo wrapper*opptional* 
 1/2 tbsp hot sauce(tabascos) 
 50 grms grated  Cheddar   Cheese 
 Directions : 
 SAUTE  Tuna  flakes IN LOW FIRE.ADD  salt / pepper  TO TASTE. IN A MIXING BOWL MIX  onions , tomatoes , lemon juice ,CAJUN SPICE AND  oregano . PRE-HEAT TACOS IN MICROWAVE OVEN THEN STUFFED  Tuna  flakes TOGETHER WITH THE OTHER INGREDIENTS. SPRINGKLE TACO SEASONONG AND ADD HOT SAUCE THEN TOPPED THE SHREDDED  lettuce  AND GRATED  Cheese . PERFECT WITH  Cheese  DIP HAPPY & ENJOY TACOING GUYS!!!
