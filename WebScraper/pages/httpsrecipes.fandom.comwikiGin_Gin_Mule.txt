      Description     
 Ingredients : 
 ½ oz  lime juice 
 ½ oz  simple syrup  (or leave out, if using a more commercial ginger beer with higher sugar levels) 
 6  mint  sprigs (if available) 
 3+ oz (depending on glass size)  ginger beer 
 1½ oz of any good, citrussy  gin , though Tanqueray 10®, Plymouth® and Beefeater 24® work well. 
 1 splash  soda water 
 Directions : 
 Mix the  lime juice , syrup and  mint  springs. 
 Add  gin  then shake. 
 Top with  ginger beer . 
 Serve with  ice  and garnish with  lime .
