 Ingredients : 
 2 cups strong black  coffee 
 3 cups hot  milk 
 4 oz  cooking chocolate 
 4 tbsp  sugar 
 dark rum 
 whipped cream 
 Directions : 
 Strain  coffee  and mix it with 2 cups  milk . 
 Dissolve  chocolate  and  sugar  in 1 cup  milk . 
 Stir  chocolate  mixture into  coffee . 
 This can be beaten until frothy and serve hot with 1 oz. 
 dark rum  added to each cup and topped with  whipped cream . 
 Or it may be stored in a pitcher in the refrigerator and served cold in goblets with  rum  and  whipped cream . 
 Either way, it is delicious.
