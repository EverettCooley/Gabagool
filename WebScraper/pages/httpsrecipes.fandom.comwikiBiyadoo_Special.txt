 Description : 
 Great similarity to the Villivaru Special of the neighbor island Villivaru.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cl  vodka 
 4 cl  melon liqueur 
 17 cl  pineapple juice 
 crushed  ice 
 1  orange  or  pineapple  slice 
 Directions : 
 Shaking liqueur and pushed  ice  in the shaker, pour it into a high glass with the  vodka  and fill it on with the  pineapple juice . 
 Cutting the  oranges  or  pineapple  slice and putting on the brim of the glass, serving with a straw.
