Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Wadley Estate in Allen, Texas in 1990.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  corn oil 
 2 medium  white onions  coarsely chopped 
 1 tablespoon  water 
 1½ pounds lean  ground beef 
 6 ounces  tomato paste 
 2 teaspoons dried  basil 
 3 tablespoons  brown rice vinegar 
 1 tablespoon  steak sauce 
 1 tablespoon  Worcestershire sauce 
 ¼ cup  maple syrup 
 ½ cup dry  red wine 
 Directions : 
 Add oil to Dutch oven over medium heat then add onions. 
 Toss with oil then add 1 tablespoon water and cook until limp. 
 Add ground beef and cook until all the pink is gone. 
 Add remaining ingredients and simmer for 25 minutes.
