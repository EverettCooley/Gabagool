       Arepas Jenesanences     
 Corn Bread Jenesanen-style
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  basic arepa dough 
 1 cup  muenster  cheese 
 2  egg yolks 
 4 tbsp  butter  softened 
 Directions : 
 Knead dough with  egg yolks ,  butter  and  cheese . 
 Shape to 4-inch discs and cook on a  griddle . 
 Serve hot, spread with  butter .
