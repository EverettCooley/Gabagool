 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  oranges 
 2 cups fresh  pineapple 
 2 cups fresh  pears 
 2 cups fresh  peaches 
 2 cups fresh  cherries  or  grapes 
 2Â½ cups  white sugar 
 6 inch stick  cinnamon 
 1 pint  apricot brandy 
 Directions : 
 Combine fruit and  sugar . 
 Let stand 3 hours. Stirring once or twice. 
 Tie cinnamon in cheesecloth bag. 
 Add to fruit and  brandy . 
 Cover loosely and let stand at least 1 week, stirring once a day. 
 To keep pot going, add 1 cup  sugar  and 2 cups fruit to replace every 2 cups removed. 
 The fruit pot may be started at the beginning of the summer with  strawberries ,  oranges  and  grapes . 
 Add more fruit and  sugar  as they come into season - e.g.  raspberries ,  peaches ,  pears , melon balls. 
 Keep refrigerated or in a cold room. 
 You can keep one going all year round in your refrigerator.
