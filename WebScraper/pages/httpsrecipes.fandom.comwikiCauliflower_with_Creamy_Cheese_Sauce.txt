Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Source:  1,001 Delicious Recipes for People with Diabetes 
 Yield: 6 servings and about 1¼ cups of sauce 
 Ingredients : 
 1 whole cauliflower (2 pounds) 
 creamy cheese sauce  (recipe follows) 
 paprika , as garnish 
 finely chopped  parsley  leaves, as garnish 
 Creamy cheese sauce : 
 2 tbsp minced  onion 
 1 tbsp  margarine 
 2 tbsp  flour 
 1 cup  fat-free milk 
 ½ cup (2oz) cubed reduced-fat pasteurized  processed cheese  product or shredded reduced-fat  cheddar  cheese 
 ¼ tsp  dry mustard 
 2 – 3 drops  hot pepper sauce 
 salt  and  white pepper , to taste 
 Directions : 
 Place cauliflower in saucepan with 2 inches of water; heat to boiling. 
 Reduce heat and simmer, covered, until cauliflower is tender, 20 to 25 minutes. 
 Place cauliflower on serving plate; spoon creamy cheese sauce over and sprinkle with paprika and parsley. 
 Creamy cheese sauce : 
 Sauté onion in margarine in small saucepan 2 to 3 minutes. 
 Stir in flour; cook over medium-low heat, stirring constantly, 1 minute. 
 Whisk in milk and heat to boiling; boil, whisking constantly, until thickened, about 1 minute. 
 Reduce heat to low. 
 Add cheese, dry mustard, and pepper sauce, whisking until cheese is melted. 
 Season to taste with salt and pepper. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per Serving (⅙ of recipe):
 
 102 Calories | 3.6g Fat | 5.7mg Cholesterol | 194mg Sodium | 6.5g Protein | 11.7g Carbs 
 Diabetic Exchanges: ½ meat | ½ fat | 2 vegetable
