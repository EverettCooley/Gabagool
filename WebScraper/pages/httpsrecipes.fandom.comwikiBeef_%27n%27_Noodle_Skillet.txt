Ingredients : 
 1 lbs extra lean ground  Beef 
 2 cup medium  egg noodles  --uncooked 
 1 cup whole kernel  corn , frozen 
 1 cup  green onions  -- sliced 
 1 cup  water 
 1/2 cup salsa (thick and chunky) 
 2 can (8 oz.) no- salt  added tomato sauce 
 Directions : 
 Brown ground  Beef  in large skillet; drain. 
 Stir in all remaining ingredients. Bring to a boil. 
 Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer 10 to 12 minutes or until noodles are of desired doneness, stirring occasionally
