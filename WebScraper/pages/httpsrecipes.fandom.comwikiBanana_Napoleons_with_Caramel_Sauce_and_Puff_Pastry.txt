 Makes 8 servings of banana napoleons. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 sheet frozen  puff pastry , thawed 
 6  bananas 
 ⅔ cup caramel sauce, warmed 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 400 °F. 
 Unfold pastry. 
 Cut in 12 equal rectangles. 
 Place on ungreased baking sheet, prick tops all over with a fork. 
 Bake 12 minutes or until puffed and golden brown. 
 Cool on sheet. 
 Cut to split each rectangle into two layers. 
 Put 1 layer on each of 8 dessert plates. 
 Slice bananas into a bowl, toss with ½ cup caramel sauce. 
 Spoon ½ of the mixture onto pastry on plates. 
 Repeat layers. 
 Add another pastry, cut side down. 
 Brush with rest of sauce, top with remaining bananas.
