 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 6 cup  watermelon , peeled, seeded 
 -and cut into 1-inch chunks 
 3 tbsp Confectioners'  Sugar 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1/4 tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 1 In covered blender container at low speed, blend 1 cup  watermelon  chunks with confectioners"  Sugar ,  lemon juice  and  salt  until mixture is smooth; add remaining  watermelon  chunks and blend a few seconds longer until smooth. 
2 Pour into 9" by 9" baking pan; cover with foil or plastic wrap and freeze until partially frozen, about 2 hours.into chilled large bowl, spoon  watermelon  mixture. 
3 With mixer at high speed, beat until fluffy. 
4 Return mixture to baking pan and freeze until firm, about 1 Â½ hours. 
5 To serve: remove  watermelon   ice  from freezer and let it stand at room temperature 10 minutes for easier scooping. 
6 You will enjoy this recipe.
