 Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz  dark rum 
 2 oz  light rum 
 1 tbsp  passion fruit  Syrup 
 2 oz  apricot  nectar 
 2 oz  strawberry  nectar 
 2 tsp  Grenadine 
 2 tsp  lime juice 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Shake well with  ice  and strain into a hurricane glass filled with  ice .
