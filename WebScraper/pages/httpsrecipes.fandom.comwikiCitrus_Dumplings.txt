Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Categories: Breads 
 Yield: 2 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ tsp finely shredded  orange peel 
 ½ cup  bisquick 
 ½ cup  orange juice 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 2 tsp  cornstarch 
 2 tbsp  milk 
 ¼ tsp ground  cinnamon 
 1 tsp  sugar 
 11 oz  mandarin orange  sect., drain 
 1 tsp dash ground  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 In a 1-quart casserole stir together  orange juice ,  cornstarch  and ¼ ground  cinnamon . 
 Micro-cook, uncovered, on 100% power for 1½ to 2minutes or till thickened and bubbly, stirring every 30 seconds. 
 Stir in drained  mandarin orange  sections and finely shredded  orange peel . 
 Micro-cook, uncovered, on 100% power for 1 to 1½ minutes or til mixture is heated through. 
 Meanwhile for dumplings, stir together bisquick and 2 t  sugar . 
 Add  milk , stirring just till moistened. 
 Drop mixture into four mound atop the hot  orange  mixture. 
 Micro-cook,uncovered, at 50% power for 6 to 7 minutes or till dumplings are just set. 
 Stir together the 1 tsp  sugar  and dash of ground  cinnamon . 
 Sprinkle sugar mixture atop dumplings. 
 Enjoy.
