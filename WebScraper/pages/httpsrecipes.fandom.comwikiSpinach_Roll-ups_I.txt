      Spinach Roll-ups     
 Ingredients : 
 9 Mission™ burrito size flour  tortillas 
 10 oz pkg. chopped  spinach  thawed and well-drained 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 8 oz container  sour cream 
 1 bunch  scallions  or  green onions  chopped 
 1 oz pkg.  ranch dressing  mix 
 3 oz jar  bacon bits 
 Directions : 
 Combine  spinach ,  mayonnaise ,  sour cream ,  green onions , dressing and  bacon bits ; spread in tortillas. 
 Roll up jelly roll fashion; wrap in plastic wrap. 
 Chill 4 to 6 hours. 
 Cut into ½ inch-thick slices.
