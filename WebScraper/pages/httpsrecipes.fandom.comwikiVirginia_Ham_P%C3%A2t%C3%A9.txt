 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: my old recipes 
 Makes 1½ cups 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup ground cooked  ham 
 2 hard cooked  eggs , very finely chopped 
 1 pkg (3 oz) softened  cream cheese 
 1 tbsp  Dijon mustard 
 2 to 3  mushrooms , finely chopped 
 1 tbsp finely minced  onion 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients in a small bowl. 
 Place in blender and give a whirl. 
 Serve on  cheese  wafers or warm cocktail biscuits.
