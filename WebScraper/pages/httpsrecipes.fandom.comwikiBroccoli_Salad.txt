Description : 
 An old  broccoli  salad which is still an excellent healthy feast for any moment of the day.
 This recipe requires basic cooking skills and is easy to make.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 medium  onion , finely diced 
 1 bunch  broccoli 
 1 cup grated  Cheddar  cheese 
 ½ pound  bacon 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 1 tablespoon  vinegar 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Cut  bacon  into small pieces and fry until crisp; drain well on paper towels. 
 Break  broccoli  into small pieces. 
 Mix sugar, mayonnaise and vinegar, and pour over  broccoli ,  onion ,  cheese  and  bacon ; mix well.
