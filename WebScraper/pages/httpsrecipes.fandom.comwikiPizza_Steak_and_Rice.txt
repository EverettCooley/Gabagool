Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  Beef  round  Steak , tenderized and cut into 1/4-inch strips 
 1/4 cup  flour  seasoned with  salt  and ground  black pepper 
 3 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 1 10-ounce can pizza sauce 
 1 cup  water 
 1 large  Onion , sliced 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Coat  Steak  with seasoned  flour . Heat  oil  in 10-inch skillet. Add  Steak  and cook, stirring, over high heat until meat is brown. 
 Stir in pizza sauce and  water . Arrange  Onion  on top of  Steak . Bring to boil, reduce heat, cover, and simmer 15 minutes. Serve over fluffy  rice .
