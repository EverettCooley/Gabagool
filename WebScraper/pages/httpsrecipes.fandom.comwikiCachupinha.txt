 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 5 ears fresh  corn  (or fresh off cob) 
 1/5 lb. (100 g.) linguica (Portuguese smoked  Sausage ), 
 sliced 1/4  Squash 
 1/2 lb. (1/4 kg.) fava beans, (or lima beans) fresh or dried 
 2 ripe  tomatoes  (or equivalent  tomato  paste) 
 2 tablespoons of  olive oil 
 2 peppercorns 
 1 bunch of flat leaf coriander 
 1 large  Onion , sliced 
 Directions : 
 Scrape off the fresh  corn  kernels into a  boiler  pot. 
 Gently saute  Onion , linguica, and  pepper  in  olive oil . 
 Add to the  corn  the rest of the ingredients and a sufficient amount of  water . 
 Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and let simmer. 
 Prior to serving, season with  salt  and garnish with chopped flat leaf coriander ( cilantro ).
