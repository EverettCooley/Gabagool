       Zero-fat Soup     
 Zero-fat Soup  is a vegetarian Vietnamese recipe.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups  vegetable broth  or vegetarian "chicken" broth 
 2 cups diced  onions 
 1 cup diced  celery 
 1 cup peeled and diced  carrots 
 1 cup  green beans 
 1½ cups finely chopped  green cabbage 
 1 cup diced  red bell peppers 
 4 cloves  garlic , minced 
 3 cups diced  tomatoes  with juice 
 2 tablespoons soy sauce 
 1 tablespoon  cider vinegar 
 1 teaspoon dried  basil 
 1 teaspoon dried  oregano 
 1 teaspoon dried  dill 
 ½ teaspoon dried  thyme 
 ½ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Put broth in a soup pot, heat it. 
 Add the vegetables, put the  tomatoes , soy sauce,  vinegar ,  basil ,  oregano ,  dill ,  thyme ,  pepper  and add  salt .
