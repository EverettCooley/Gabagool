Tomato seed oil is refined from  tomato  seed by means of cold press, meaning that the oil was not heated over a certain temperature during processing and is considered to retain more nutrients. The tomato seed oil is rich in unsaturated fatty acid, Ve and lycopene.
 Once opened, a package of tomato seed oil should be used within 6 months.
