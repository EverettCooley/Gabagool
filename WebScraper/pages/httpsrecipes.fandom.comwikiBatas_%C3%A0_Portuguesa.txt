Description : 
 Portuguese fried potatoes. The secret here is the  potatoes  are fried in  olive oil .
 
 serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 57 g  butter 
 57 g  olive oil 
 750 g  potatoes , peeled and sliced (¼" thick rounds or like French fries) 
 ½ level teaspoon  salt 
 ½ freshly ground  black pepper 
 1 level tablespoon finely of chopped  parsley 
 Directions : 
 In a pan heat  butter  and  oil . 
 Fry  potatoes  on both sides until golden brown. 
 Season  potatoes  with  salt  and  pepper , transfer to a heated platter and serve immediately sprinkle with  parsley .
