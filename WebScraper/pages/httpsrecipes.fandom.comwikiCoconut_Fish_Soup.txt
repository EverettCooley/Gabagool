Ingredients : 
 2 lb fish heads and carcasses ( cod ,  snapper  or similar) 
 7 cups  water 
 2 tsp  salt 
 1 large  onion 
 dash  pepper 
 1 small whole  chile 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 2 cups thick  coconut cream  (not sweetened) 
 lemon  slices and chopped  green onions  for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Bring first 6 ingredients to simmering point and maintain until is soft. 
 Skim periodically. 
 Strain off stock and adjust seasoning if necessary. 
 Stir in  lemon juice  and  coconut cream  and heat thoroughly, do not boil. 
 Garnish with  lemon  slice and chopped  green onions .
