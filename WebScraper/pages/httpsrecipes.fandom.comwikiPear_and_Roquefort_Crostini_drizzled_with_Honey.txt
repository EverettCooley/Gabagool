Description 
 Although the ingredients are sophisticated, this is a quick and simple recipe that will provide you with a hot appetizer in mere minutes.
 
 Pear and Roquefort Crostini Drizzled with Honey  from the  Public Health Cookbook  by the Seattle & King County Department of Public Health—original source of recipe, government resource in the public domain 
 Cook Time : 5 minutes 
 Serves : 16 
 Ingredients 
 1 medium-sized French  baguette , sliced ½ inch thick into about 14 – 16 pieces 
 4 tablespoons  olive oil 
 4 ounces  Roquefort  cheese, crumbled (may also use other types of blue cheese like  Gorgonzola  or Maytag) 
 1 ripe  pear , halved, cored and thinly sliced 
 2 tablespoons  honey 
 Directions 
 Preheat broiler. 
 Place bread slices on a baking sheet then brush each slice with the olive oil. Set oven rack at about 5 inches from broiler. Broil bread for about 45 seconds then remove. 
 Turn bread slices over then evenly top with Roquefort. Return bread to oven and broil for 30 seconds or until cheese is melted. 
 Remove from oven then top each with pear slices. 
 Arrange crostini on a platter, drizzle with honey then serve.
