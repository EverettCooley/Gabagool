Ingredients : 
 ½ lb  crab  meat (fresh or canned) 
 ¾ cup  celery , chopped 
 3  avocados , cut in half, seeded, brushed with  lime juice . 
 Dressing : 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 ½ cup  sour cream 
 ½ tsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 a few drops  Tabasco 
 ¼ tsp  rosemary , crushed 
 2 tbsp  chili sauce  or  ketchup 
 Directions : 
 Mix shredded crab meat with  celery  and just enough  mayonnaise  to bind. 
 Fill the halves. 
 Serve surrounded by  lettuce  leaves, with the dressing.
