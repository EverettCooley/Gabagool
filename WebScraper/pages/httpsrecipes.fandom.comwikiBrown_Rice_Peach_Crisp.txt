 Description : 
 Makes 4 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 cups cooked  brown rice 
 1 1/2 cups fresh  peach  chunks* 
 1/2 cup firmly packed  brown sugar , divided 
 1/2 cup whole  Wheat  or  all-purpose flour 
 1/2 teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 1/8 teaspoon ground  nutmeg 
 3 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 1/3 cup toasted  slivered almonds 
 whipped cream  or  ice   cream  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  peach  chunks and 1/4 cup  Sugar  in buttered shallow 1-1/2-quart baking dish. 
 Combine  flour , remaining 1/4 cup  Sugar  and spices in small bowl; cut in  butter  until mixture is crumbly. 
 Sprinkle  flour  mixture over  rice ; sprinkle  nuts  on top. 
 Bake at 350 degrees 15 to 20 minutes. Serve warm with  whipped cream  or  ice   cream , if desired.
