 Description : 
       STRAWBERRY CAKE ROLL     
 Ingredients : 
 2  eggs 
 100ml full fat  milk 
 1 tsp.  Sugar 
 50ml  flour 
 melted  butter 
 300ml or 1 1/3/c  strawberries  (sliced) 
 Sugar 
 confectioner's sugar 
 Directions : 
 Beat  egg yolks ,  milk ,  Sugar , and  flour  until smooth. 
 Beat the  egg whites  until stiff but not dry and fold in. 
 Brush a large, nonstick frying pan with melted  butter . 
 Pour in the batter, cover, and cook over low heat for about 10 minutes. 
 Slice the  Berries . 
 Sprinkle with  Sugar . 
 Spoon over the cake, then roll up. 
 Sprinkle the roll with  confectioner's sugar  and serve with  Berries ,  whipped cream , or  ice cream .
