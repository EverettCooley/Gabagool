 About Conchiglie : 
 Wikipedia Article  About Conchiglie on Wikipedia 
 Conchiglie is medium-sized pasta shells. The larger shells are called  Conchiglioni  and the smaller shells are called  Conchigliette .
 Other types of pasta that may be used in place of conchiglie include  farfalle ,  fusilli ,  gemelli ,  macaroni ,  rigatoni , or  ziti .
