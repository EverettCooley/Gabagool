      Swedish Meatballs with ground beef and pork     
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs  ground beef 
 1 lb ground  pork 
 1 teaspoon  allspice 
 1 cup  breadcrumbs 
 1 cup  milk 
 2  eggs , beaten 
 ⅛ cup chopped  onions 
 2 teaspoons  salt 
 2 teaspoons  sugar 
 ½ teaspoon  pepper 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix all the ingredients together. 
 Make small balls the size of melon balls. 
 Spray cookie sheet with  cooking spray . 
 Bake at 350 °F for 30 minutes. 
 Bake longer if making larger meatballs.
