Ingredients : 
 1½ cups  flour 
 3  eggs 
 1½ cups  milk 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 1 tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Mix together. 
 Pour a bit of  oil  or  butter  in the bottom of a small skillet, pour enough batter in to lightly coat the bottom. 
 Cook until golden brown on both sides. 
 Blini can be served with  butter ,  caviar ,  sour cream  or just about anything which sounds good on pancakes or crêpes.
