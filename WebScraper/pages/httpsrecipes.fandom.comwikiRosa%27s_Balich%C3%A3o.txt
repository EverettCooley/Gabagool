Ingredients : 
 12 oz jar of salted  shrimp 
 2  bay leaves 
 2  dried chiles 
 1 sliced  lemon 
 Â½ tsp  black peppercorns 
 1 cup  Shaoxing  (rice wine)  or   vodka 
 Directions : 
 Drain excess liquid from jar of  shrimp . 
 Combine  shrimp ,  bay leaves ,  dried chiles ,  lemon ,  black peppercorns , and shaoxing or  vodka  in a medium bowl. 
 Pour into a glass jar, seal tightly, and leave in a cool, dark place for 100 days. 
 Store, refrigerated, for up to three years.
