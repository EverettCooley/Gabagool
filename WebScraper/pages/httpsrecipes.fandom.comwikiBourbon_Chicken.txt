Description : 
 Courtesy of Joe Nichols 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 large boneless  chicken breasts 
 1 can  cream of mushroom  soup 
 1 can  cream of chicken  soup 
 1 cup grated  cheddar   cheese 
 1 cube  margarine  melted 
 1 cup seasoned  breadcrumbs 
 3 oz  bourbon 
 Directions : 
 Put  chicken  in greased 9x12 baking dish. 
 Mix soups and  cheese  together,pour over  chicken . 
 Pour melted  margarine  over soup mix. 
 Sprinkle bread crumbs over soup mix, pour  bourbon  over crumbs. 
 Bake 350°F, 1-1½ hrs depending on thickness of  chicken .
