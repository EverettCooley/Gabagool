Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups mashed  potatoes 
 1 cup  flour 
 2 tablespoons  butter 
 1  egg 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 15  apricots  or plums 
 15 small cubes of  Sugar 
 3 tablespoons  bread  crumbs 
 Directions : 
 Mix ingredients and knead until a smooth dough. Roll dough to 1 finger thickness and cut into 15 squares. 
 Wash, pit and dry  apricots  or plums and place  Sugar  in center of each fruit. Place one fruit in each square of dough. 
 Fold dough over  apricot  or  plum  and roll into a round ball. Cook in boiling  water  about 15 minutes. 
 Do not crowd the dumplings. To prepare buttered  bread  crumbs, melt  butter  and brown crumbs lightly. 
 When dumplings are done, remove them with a slotted spoon and place on a serving dish. Top with buttered crumbs.
