Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 cup  whole wheat flour 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 1 tbsp  baking powder 
 1 tsp ground  cinnamon 
 1/4 tsp ground  nutmeg 
 1/4 tsp  ground ginger 
 1  egg 
 2 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 1/4 cup  Raisins 
 1/4 cup chopped  walnuts 
 1/3 cup lowfat  milk 
 8 oz can uns. crushed  pineapple 
 1 1/2 cup  Grated Carrots 
 Directions : 
 Combine the dry ingredients in a bowl. Add the remaining ingredients and stir to blend. Spoon into oiled muffin tins or paper muffin cups. Bake at 350 °F for 20 to 25 minutes. 1 muffin - 127 calories, 1 bread, 1 fat exchange 19 grams carbohydrate, 4 grams protein, 5 grams fat 149 mg sodium, 201 mg potassium, 23 mg cholesterol
