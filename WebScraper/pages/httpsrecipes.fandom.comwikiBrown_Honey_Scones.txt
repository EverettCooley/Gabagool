Ingredients : 
 3 cups whole meal 
 4 1/2 t  baking powder 
 1/2 t  salt 
 1 t  butter 
 2 t  Honey 
 1 c  milk , Approx 
 Directions : 
 Mix Whole meal,  salt  And Baking powder. 
 Rub in The  butter . 
 Dissolve the honey in the  milk 
 Add to Dry Ingredients And Mix to a Soft Dough. 
 Rollout, Cut Out, And Bake on a Cookie Sheet For 10 - 12 Minutes At 425 Degrees.
