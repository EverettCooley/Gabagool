 Ingredients : 
 1 shot De Kuyper  Crème de cassis 
 1 shot Stolichnaya  vodka 
 1 shot Disaronno  Amaretto 
 dash  lime juice 
 dash  lemonade 
 Directions : 
 Shake with cubed ice and strain into an ice filled sling glass - top up with  lemonade .
