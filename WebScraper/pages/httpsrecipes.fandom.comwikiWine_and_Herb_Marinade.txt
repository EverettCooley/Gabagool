Description : 
 This marinade is especially nice with  pork ,  beef  and  poultry .
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 From the Recipe Ross Newsletter 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  olive oil 
 ¼ cup dry  white wine 
 1 tbsp minced dried  onion 
 ½ tsp crushed dried  basil 
 ½ tsp crushed dried  rosemary 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 Combine ingredients in a shallow dish.
