 Ingredients : 
 3 cups brown  flour . 
 30 pack  butter  (113g) melted. 
 2 cups of seedless  dates . 
 1 cup chopped  walnuts . 
 1/2 cup powder  Sugar . 
 1 teaspoon  cardamom  powder. 
 Directions : 
 Stir  flour  over low heat until slightly golden, sift. 
 Combine  flour  with  cardamom  and  Sugar  powder 
 add 2 2/3  butter , knead, spread 1/2 quantity of dough in 20x30 cm rectangle pan. 
 Add the remaining  butter  to the  dates  and  walnuts , cover by the remaining dough 
 spread pistachio 
 chill in refrigerator for 1/2 bour, cut it in cubes 
 serve with  coffee
