 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup  lime juice 
 2 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 1 tablespoon honey 
 1/4 teaspoon  ginger 
 6 cups cooked  brown rice , cooked in low-sodium  chicken broth 
 1 pound boneless skinless  Chicken  breasts, cooked and shredded 
 1 cup chopped  dried apricots 
 1 cup thinly sliced  green onions 
 3/4 cup  Raisins 
 lettuce  leaves 
 
 Directions : 
 Whisk together  lime juice ,  oil , honey and  ginger  in small blow; set aside. 
 Combine  rice ,  Chicken ,  apricots ,  onions  and  Raisins  in large bowl. Chill at least 1 hour. 
 Just before serving, drizzle dressing over salad. Place on  lettuce  lined individual serving plates.
