Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup uncooked  rice 
 ⅓ cup diced  pimentos 
 2 tablespoons chopped fresh  parsley  (optional) 
 2 cups boiling  chicken broth 
 2 tablespoons melted  butter  or  margarine 
 ¼ teaspoon  garlic salt 
 ¼ teaspoon  celery salt 
 1 teaspoon  paprika 
 ⅛ teaspoon  ground red pepper 
 3  chicken  breasts, halved, boned and skinned (about 1½ pounds) 
 ⅓ cup sliced  ripe olives 
 1 tablespoon  capers  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice , pimientos,  parsley , broth and  butter  in shallow 3-quart baking dish; stir well. 
 Sprinkle  chicken  with  garlic salt ,  celery salt ,  paprika  and  pepper . 
 Place  chicken  on  rice . 
 Cover and bake at 350 °F for 45 minutes or until  chicken  and  rice  are tender. 
 Stir in and  capers .
