 Description : 
 This recipe was recommended by Billie. Billie had bought a loaf at a farmer's
market, fell in love with the bread, and searched everywhere for a recipe.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3  eggs 
 1 cup  oil 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 2 cups  Sugar 
 1 teaspoon  baking soda 
 1 1/2 teaspoons  baking powder 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1/4 teaspoon  cinnamon 
 3/4 teaspoon  pumpkin  pie spice 
 2 cups whole  Wheat   flour 
 1 cup  flour 
 1 cup grated  zucchini 
 1/2 cup grated  carrots 
 1/2 cup mashed  bananas 
 1/2 cup chopped  nuts 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 °F. Grease and  flour  two 5x9x3-inch loaf pans. Set aside. 
 Mix  eggs ,  oil ,  vanilla  and  Sugar  in large mixing bowl. 
 Add  baking soda , baking powder ,  salt ,  cinnamon  and  pumpkin  pie spice. 
 Stir in flours. Mix well. 
 Stir in  zucchini ,  carrots ,  bananas  and  nuts . 
 Pour batter into prepared loaf pans. 
 Bake for 35–40 minutes. Makes 2 loaves.
