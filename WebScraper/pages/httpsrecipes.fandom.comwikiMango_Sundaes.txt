 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp.  orange   marmalade 
 2 tbsp.  orange  liquer 
 water 
 4 scoops  ice   cream 
 8  strawberries 
 mint  leaves 
 2 ripe  mangos 
 Directions : 
 Puree diced  mangos  with  orange   marmalade  and  orange  liquer. Strain if necessary. Add a little  water  if sauce is too thick. To serve, spoon sauce over  ice   cream  and top with  Berries . Garnish with  mint  leaves.
