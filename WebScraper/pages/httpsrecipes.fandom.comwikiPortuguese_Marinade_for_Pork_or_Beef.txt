Description : 
 This recipe taken from www.Portuguese-recipes.com
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup of  red  or  white wine  (more if you like ) 
 1 or 2 teaspoons of crushed  garlic  (again more if you want) 
 1 teaspoon of dry  crushed red pepper  (you may want more of this also) 
 1 can of  beer  (you will need this the next day) 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Put the  meat  of choice in a dish or bowl. 
 Mix all above ingredients and pour over the  meat . 
 Marinate for a day. 
 Pour the  beer  over all and marinate at least 4 hours more.
