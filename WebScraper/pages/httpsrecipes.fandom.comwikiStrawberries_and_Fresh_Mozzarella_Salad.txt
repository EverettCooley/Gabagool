 Ingredients : 
 1 cups extra virgin  olive oil 
 1/3 oz.  mint  leaves 
 1 ½ lbs. stemmed and sliced  strawberries 
 3/4 oz.  Sugar 
 2 lbs.  fresh mozzarella   Cheese  slices, 1/2 inch thick 
 1/8 cup & 1 teaspoon  balsamic vinegar 
 Directions : 
 In blender combine  olive oil  and  mint . Process until smooth. Cover and refrigerate.
 In bowl, combine  strawberries  and  Sugar , cover and set aside.
 For each serving: 
Place  mozzarella , overlapping slices divided evenly between 8 plates. Spoon 1/3cup sugared  strawberries  around  Cheese . Drizzle 2 tablespoons  mint  Drizzle and 1/2 teaspoon  balsamic vinegar  over  Cheese  and  strawberries .
