Dads Hot Dog  onions 
 
 1 1/2 Cups  water 
 1/2 Cup  ketchup 
 1/2 Tablespoon  mustard 
 salt  And  pepper  to Taste
 Pinch Crushed  pepper 
 Pinch  cayenne pepper 
 Pinch Dry  oregano  And Dry  basil 
 
 3 Sliced  onions 
 
Boil One And a Half Cups  water , Then Add All Ingredients. Cook Over Low Flame For 20-25 Minutes.Serve on Hot Dogs.
 
Enjoy.
