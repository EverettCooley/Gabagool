 Makes 6 cups of snack mix 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  candy corn 
 2 cups candy coated  chocolate  pieces 
 2 cups unsalted  peanuts 
 Directions : 
 Combine candy corn, chocolate pieces, and peanuts in a large bowl. 
 Store in an air tight container.
