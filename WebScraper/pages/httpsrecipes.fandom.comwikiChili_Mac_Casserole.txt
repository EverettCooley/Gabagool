Description 
 Chili without Beans, Canned  by the US Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Serves : 4 
 Ingredients 
 ¾ cup elbow  macaroni 
 1 (8.5 oz) can whole kernel  corn 
 1 (8 oz) can  tomato sauce 
 1 (15 oz) can  chili without beans 
 Directions 
 Heat oven to 350°F 
 Cook macaroni according to package directions.Drain. 
 Stir in macaroni, corn, tomato sauce and chili without beans. 
 Place in oven for 30 – 40 minutes until heated through.
