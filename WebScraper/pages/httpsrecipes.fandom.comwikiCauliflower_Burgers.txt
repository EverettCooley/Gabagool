Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Greene Estate in Mesquite, Texas in 1991.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ pound  ground round  steak 
 2 tablespoons finely chopped cooked  cauliflower 
 ¼ teaspoon dried  thyme 
 2 thin slices  Swiss cheese 
 Directions : 
 Preheat grill or broiler on high heat. 
 In a small bowl, combine beef, cauliflower and thyme. 
 Form beef mixture into 2 small patties. 
 Cook until desired doneness. 
 Place cheese on top of meat patties just before done. 
 Cook for 30 seconds more allowing cheese to melt.
