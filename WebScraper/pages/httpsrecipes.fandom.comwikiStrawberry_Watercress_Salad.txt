Description : 
 Make this fruit salad tastier by adding watercress to the fruits. Serve up a juicy treat by pairing this fruit salad with other main dishes, side dishes, or appetizers.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups fruit juice, with grapefruit and orange separated at ½ cup each, and lime juice at 1 cup 
 ¼ cup  honey 
 dash of  salt 
 2 pint baskets fresh California  strawberries , stemmed 
 2 avocados, pitted, peeled and cut into wedges 
 1 cup stemmed, seedless  grapes 
 2 bunches watercress, trimmed 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl, mix citrus juice,  honey  and  salt  to blend. Add  strawberries , avocados and  grapes ; toss. Cover and marinate in refrigerator 1 hour. 
 To serve, arrange watercress and fruits on eight salad plates. Serve with  Brie  or blue  cheese , and crackers.
