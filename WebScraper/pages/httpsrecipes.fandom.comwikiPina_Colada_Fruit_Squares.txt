 Description : 
 Makes: 9 servings, 1 square each
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 can (8 oz.)  pineapple  chunks in juice, undrained 
 1-1/2 cups boiling  water 
 1 pkg. (8-serving size) or 2 pkg. (4-serving size each) JELL-O Brand Pina Colada Flavor  gelatin 
 1-1/4 cups  ice  cubes 
 1-1/2 cups thawed  Cool Whip  Whipped Topping 
 Directions : 
 DRAIN  pineapple , reserving the juice. 
 Meanwhile, stir boiling  water  into dry  gelatin  mix in large bowl at least 2 minutes. 
 Until completely dissolved. 
 Add the reserved  pineapple juice  and  ice  cubes; 
 Stir until slightly thickened. Remove any unmelted  ice . 
 Remove 1-1/2 cups of the  gelatin ; place in medium bowl. 
 Stir in the  pineapple ; set aside. 
 REFRIGERATE remaining  gelatin  15 minutes. or until slightly thickened. 
 Add the whipped topping; stir with wire whisk until well blended. 
 Pour into 8-inch square pan. Refrigerate 10 min. or until set but not firm. 
 Carefully spoon over creamy layer in pan. 
 REFRIGERATE 3 hours or until firm. 
 Cut into 9 squares to serve. 
 Store leftover dessert in refrigerator.
