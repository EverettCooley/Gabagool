Ingredients : 
 4 cups of mashed  potatoes  (add  butter  and  salt  to taste) 
 2  eggs 
 ½ teaspoon of chopped  parsley 
 2 or 4 tablespoons of grated  cheese 
 1  onion , diced 
 2  tomatoes , diced 
 1 tablespoon  tomato sauce 
 1 minced clove of fresh  garlic  (may replace with  powder ) 
 ½ cup of  oil 
 2 cups  ground beef 
 spices to taste 
 Directions : 
 Heat the  oil . 
 Fry  onions  and  garlic . 
 Stir in  tomatoes  and tomato sauce when  onions  turn light brown. 
 When  tomatoes  are cooked, stir in ground  beef . 
 Continue stirring to blend well all ingredients. 
 Add the  cheese  and  parsley . 
 Cook until  beef  is brown. 
 Remove fire and then season. 
 Oil a baking dish and then spread  potatoes  on bottom. 
 Spread the ground  beef  above the  potatoes . 
 Cover the ground  beef  with the remaining mashed  potatoes . 
 Bake in oven till the top is lightly browned.
