 About Colby-Jack : 
Colby-Jack, or Cojack, is a  cheese  produced from a mixture of  Colby  and  Monterey Jack  cheeses. It is generally sold in a full-moon or a half-moon shape when it is still young and mild in flavor. The cheese has a semi-hard texture. The flavor of Colby-Jack is mild to mellow.
