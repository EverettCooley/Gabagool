Ingredients : 
 500 g (1 lb. 2 oz.)  carrots 
 2 juicy  oranges 
 juice of 3  oranges 
 juice of 1  lemon 
 3 tablespoons  orange-blossom water 
 3 tablespoons  caster sugar 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  white pepper 
 1 teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Peel the  carrots  and grate into a serving bowl. 
 Carefully peel the  oranges  and remove the pith and pips. 
 Cut the flesh into cubes and add to the  grated carrots . 
 Mix the  orange juice ,  lemon juice ,  orange-blossom water ,  sugar ,  salt  and  pepper  and pour on to the  carrots  and  oranges . 
 Cover with cling film and refrigerate. 
 Sprinkle on the  cinnamon  before serving, chilled.
