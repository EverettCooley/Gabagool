Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 lbs  hamburger 
 1 package  crackers 
 2  eggs 
 1 large  onion 
 1 large  green pepper 
 1 large  red pepper 
 2 cans  tomato sauce 
 2 cups  catsup 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients in bowl except for tomato sauce and catsup. 
 Then add to mixture 1 can tomato sauce. 
 Mix well. 
 Place in pan and make a loaf. 
 In another bowl mix the remaining catsup and tomato sauce together, blend well. 
 Pour over the top of the meat loaf. 
 Bake for about 1½ to 2 hours. 
 Preheat oven to 375°F.
