Description : 
 Recipe by Arleen Kaptur. See her article on  Perk-up Sauces and Marinades ;
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  catsup 
 1 cup water 
 ¼ cup  vinegar 
 1 tbsp  sugar 
 1 tbsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  celery seed 
 3 – 4 dashes bottled  hot pepper sauce 
 Directions : 
 In pan combine ingredients. 
 Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer, uncovered, for one-half hour. 
 Use to baste ribs, chops, steak, and burgers. 
 Serve lots of lemonade to cool that fiery taste.
