 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 med very ripe  bananas 
 1 cup  whole wheat flour 
 1 cup unprocessed  bran 
 2 tbsp  baking powder 
 1/2 tbsp  baking soda 
 1/4 tbsp  salt 
 1 cup fatfree  yogurt , unflavored 
 2  egg  whites (or  egg  replacer 
 for 1egg) 
 1/4 cup  brown sugar 
 1/2 tbsp  cinnamon  (or more) 
 Directions : 
 Liquify  bananas  in a blender or food processor and add the  yogurt  and  egg  whites. Mix the dry ingredients, then add the liquid. Mix just till moistened. Spoon into sprayed (or non-stick) muffin tins. Bake in a preheated 400 degree oven for about 25 minutes. (use the toothpick test). Makes 12-14
