Description : 
 Dried Fava beans in  oil 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup cooked fava beans or canned fava beans 
 ½ cup  lemon  juice 
 3 cloves  garlic  crushed with 1 tspn  salt  (optional) 
 2 tbls finely chopped fresh  Parsley 
 ½ cup olive  oil 
 Directions : 
 Drain fava beans. Put in a bowl. 
 Mix  garlic ,  lemon  juice and ½ quantity of  oil . 
 Add to beans and mix well. 
 Garnish with  Parsley  and olive  oil . 
 Serve with spring  onions  and  tomatoes .
