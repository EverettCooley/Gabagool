Description : 
 This flavorful and spicy salad can also be served as a light snack.
 
 Serves 4 people 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  fish , filleted 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 pinch of  turmeric 
 1 large  onion , sliced 
 1 bulb  lemon grass  or 1 bunch  coriander leaves 
 1 tablespoon cooked  oil 
 1 teaspoon roasted  chili powder 
 2 teaspoons (or to taste)  fish sauce 
 4 tablespoons fried  onions 
 1 or 2  limes 
 1 heaping tablespoon fried  garlic 
 Directions : 
 Chop the fish. 
 Pound or put through electric blender. 
 Add salt and turmeric while pounding/blenderizing until cohesive lump is formed. 
 Remove any bones. 
 Boil fish lump in water. 
 Fish is done when it rises. 
 Remove from water. 
 Slice fish and make ¼-inch strips. 
 Soak onions in water. 
 Slice lemon grass finely. 
 Put sliced lemon grass in water, taking care to separate the rings from each other. 
 You may also use finely chopped coriander leaves in place of lemon grass. 
 Right before serving, put the fish, onion, lemon grass (or coriander), cooked oil, roasted chili powder, fish sauce and fried onions in a bowl. 
 Squeeze lime and mix well. 
 Add salt according to desired taste.
