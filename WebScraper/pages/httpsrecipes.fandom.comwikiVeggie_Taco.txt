        Veggie Taco     
 Ingredients : 
 1 corn  tortilla 
 ¼ cup vegetarian  refried beans 
 ¼ cup  cheddar  cheese shredded 
 ¼ cup  avocado  diced 
 ½ cup salad vegetables such as  lettuce ,  cucumber ,  tomato ,  radishes ,  scallions 
 Salsa as desired 
 Directions : 
 Spread  refried beans  onto tortilla. 
 Place on paper plate. 
 Microwave on high 30–45 seconds until beans are hot. 
 Sprinkle with  cheese . 
 Fold tortilla in half and top with  avocado  and chopped salad vegetables. 
 Serve with salsa.
