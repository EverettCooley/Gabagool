 Ingredients : 
 1 tbsp each  butter  and  olive oil 
 1 cup sliced  green onions 
 2 pints  cherry tomatoes 
 1 tbsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 ⅛ tbsp each  salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Heat  butter  and  oil  in large skillet over medium high heat. 
 Add  onions  and cook until tender but not brown, about 1 minute. 
 Add  tomatoes  and sauté just until skins begin to split, about 3 minutes. 
 Add Worcestershire,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Cook another minute. 
 Stir to mix and serve.
