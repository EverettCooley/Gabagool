 Ingredients : 
 1 pound  crabmeat -picked 
 2 cups  panko  breadcrumbs 
 1  California avocado , diced 
 ½ cup  mayonnaise 
 ½ juice from  lemon 
 ½ cup  chives , cut 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Mix all ingredients and shape to desired form. 
 Pan fry. 
 Serve with  Corn Blini  and  Violet Mustard Crème Fraîche
