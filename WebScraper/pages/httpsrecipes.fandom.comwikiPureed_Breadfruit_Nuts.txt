 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 1/2 c.  cream  or  evaporated milk 
 salt ,  pepper  and  nutmeg 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Remove brown skin (before or after partial cooking). 
 Put into cold salted  water  and boil until tender. 
 Remove second skin, and puree the seeds. 
 Return them to a pan of  water  over low heat. 
 Add: 1 tablespoon  butter , 1/2 c.  cream  or  evaporated milk . 
 Season to taste with  salt ,  pepper  and  nutmeg .
