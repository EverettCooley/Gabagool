 Description : 
 Rich in iron and zinc. Whole wheat hot dog rolls combine with soya granules and vegetables to make healthy submarines that will disappear in a flash!
 
 Courtesy of Tarla Dalal 
 Contributed by  Indiancuisineandculture Y-Group 
 Preparation Time : 10 minutes | Cooking Time : 5 minutes | Baking Time : 15 minutes 
 Makes 6 submarines 
 Ingredients : 
 6  hot dog rolls  (brown bread, lightly buttered) 
 ½ cup  soya granules 
 1 teaspoon  corn flour 
 ½ cup  milk 
 1 teaspoon  dry red chilli flakes 
 1 cup chopped and boiled mixed vegetables ( French beans ,  carrots , green  peas ,  potatoes ,  cauliflower , etc.) 
 2 teaspoons tomato  ketchup 
 2 teaspoons  butter 
 salt  and freshly crushed pepper to taste 
 Topping : 
 2 teaspoons cooking  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Slit each roll horizontally and toast lightly in a pre-heated oven or on a tava (griddle) for about 5 minutes. Keep aside. 
 Soak the soya granules in salted hot water for about 5 minutes. 
 Drain and squeeze out all the water. Keep aside. 
 Dissolve the corn flour in the milk and keep aside. 
 Heat the butter in a non-stick pan, add the chilli flakes, boiled vegetables, salt and pepper and mix well. 
 Add the soya granules, tomato ketchup, milk-corn flour mixture and mix again. 
 Cook for about 2 to 3 minutes. 
 Remove from the flame and keep aside. 
 Stuff each roll with a little of the prepared filling. 
 Sprinkle the grated cheese and grill in a pre-heated oven at 200°C (400°F) for 8 to 10 minutes or till the cheese melts. 
 Serve hot.
