Serve this delicious  lemon  curd with Irish soda bread for a great appetizer!
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4  lemons 
 5  eggs 
 9 oz  unsalted butter  or  margarine 
 14 oz  Sugar 
 Directions : 
 Extract  lemon juice . 
 Grate rind thinly. 
 Beat  eggs . Stir  eggs ,  lemon  rind,  lemon juice ,  butter  and  Sugar  atop a double boiler. 
 Stir over simmering  water  until mixture is glossy and thick. 
 Strain into jars and cover. 
 Store in a fridge.
