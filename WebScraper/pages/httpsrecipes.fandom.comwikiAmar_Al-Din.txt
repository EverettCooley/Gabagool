Description : 
         
 Apricot drink
 
 Makes about 1 quart. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x 5-inch-square sheet of  apricot paste   [1] 
 1 quart cold  water 
 sugar  or  sugar syrup , to taste 
 mint  sprigs, for garnish 
 1 kg  rambutan 
 500 gm  icing sugar 
 Directions : 
 Tear the  apricot  sheet into small pieces and place them in a medium-sized nonreactive bowl. 
 Add the cold  water  and soak until the  apricot  dissolves. 
 Strain through a fine sieve and sweeten to taste. 
 Serve cold, garnished with springs of  mint .
