Description 
 A River of Recipes: Native American Recipes Using Commodity Ingredients  by the USDA Food Distribution Program on Indian Reservations, public domain government resource—original source of recipes
 
 Ingredients 
 1 (16 ounce) package  macaroni 
 3 cups  cheddar  cheese, shredded or cubed 
 ⅓ cup  instant non-fat dry milk  + 1 cup  water  (or 1 cup  non-fat milk ) 
 salt  and  pepper , to taste 
 Directions 
 Cook macaroni as directed; drain. 
 Put cheese and milk in microwave safe bowl. Heat in microwave until cheese is melted, about 5 minutes on high. 
 Combine cheese mixture and macaroni. Add salt and pepper to taste.
