 Wikipedia Article  About Quatre epices on Wikipedia 
 Quatre épices is a spice blend used mainly in French cooking, but also found in the Middle Eastern kitchen. The name literally means "four spices" and contains roughly equal parts of  white pepper ,  cloves ,  nutmeg  and  ginger . Some variations of the mix use  allspice  or  black pepper  instead of the white pepper, or cinnamon in place of ginger.
