 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 150g/+5oz  cottage cheese , 
 3  eggs 
 100g/4oz Self Raising  flour 
 vegetable oil  for shallow frying 
 For the syrup 
 225g/8oz  granulated sugar 
 240ml/8fl.oz.  water 
 1 teasp  vanilla extract 
 Directions : 
 Place  cottage cheese  and  eggs  in a mixing bowl and beat well. 
 Gradually add the  flour  beating well 
 Preheat the  oil  until hot. 
 Shape the dough mixture into balls with a spoon and fry in the hot  oil  until golden brown and crisp. You will probably have to do this in batches. 
 Remove from the  oil  with a slotted spoon, drain on kitchen paper and allow to cool. 
 Meanwhile, place all the ingredients for the syrup in a saucepan, bring to the boil, stirring then continue to cook for about 10 minutes or until the mixture is thick and syrupy. 
 To serve â€“ transfer the balls to a serving dish and drizzle the syrup over the top.
