Ingredients : 
 ½ package  chocolate chips 
 1 stick  margarine 
 1½ cup  peanut butter 
 2-2½ cups  powdered sugar 
 1 Tbsp  oil 
 Directions : 
 Mix peanut butter, sugar, and margarine. Mix by hand. 
 Roll into 1 in. balls. Freeze. 
 Melt ½ package chocolate chips and 1 tablespoon oil. 
 Dip balls in chocolate mixture. Freeze again. 
 Sprinkle with powdered sugar. 
 Keep in freezer until you serve.
