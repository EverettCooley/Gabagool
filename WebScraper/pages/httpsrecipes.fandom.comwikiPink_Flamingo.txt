Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz  coconut rum 
 1 oz  amaretto 
 3 oz  cranberry juice 
 1½ oz  pineapple juice 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Combine ingredients in a hurricane glass ⅔ full of  ice . 
 Top with a  pineapple  wedge and a  cherry  stuck on a paper umbrella.
