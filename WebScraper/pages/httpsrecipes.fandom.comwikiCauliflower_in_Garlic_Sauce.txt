Ingredients : 
 2 cups  Cauliflower  florets 
 1 tbsp  unsalted butter 
 2 tsp minced  garlic 
 1 tsp chef paul prudhomme's magic  pepper  sauce 
 2 tbsp chopped fresh  cilantro  leaves 
 1/2 tsp chef paul prudhomme's vegetable magic 
 Directions : 
 Bring 1 quart of  water  to a boil in a large pot. 
 Blanch the  Cauliflower  just until tender-crisp, about 3 minutes. Drain well and set aside. 
 Place the  butter  in a large skillet over high heat. 
 As soon as  butter  begins to sizzle add the  garlic . Cook, stirring almost constantly, until  garlic  is thoroughly coated with  butter , about 30 seconds. 
 Add the  Cauliflower  and cook, stirring occasionally, just until the  Cauliflower  is slightly browned, about 4 minutes. 
 Remove from the heat, stir in the  pepper  sauce,  cilantro  and vegetable magic, and mix gently but thoroughly. 
 Serve immediately.
