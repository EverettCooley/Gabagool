Description : 
 Contributed by Ursula Taylor
 
 Yield: 1 serving of 8 oz 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  skim milk 
 1 to 2 tbsp  syrup 
 Directions : 
 Heat  milk  and syrup together in top of double boiler over simmering  water , stirring frequently until very hot. 
 Serve immediately to a warmed mug or cup. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per 1 tbsp serving of syrup:
 
 Calories 97 | cho 13g | pro 9g | fat 1g | fiber 0.1g | sodium 151mg | chol 4mg 
 Food exchanges: 1 skim milk 
 Low sodium diets: omit  salt  from  syrup  recipe.
