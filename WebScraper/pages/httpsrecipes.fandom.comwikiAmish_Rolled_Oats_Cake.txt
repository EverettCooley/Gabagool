      Oats     
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  rolled oats 
 1¼ cups boiling  water 
 ½ cup  shortening 
 1 cup  white sugar 
 1 cup  brown sugar 
 2  eggs 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 1½ cups  flour 
 1 tsp  soda 
 ½ tsp  baking powder 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 ½ tsp  nutmeg 
 Topping : 
 5 tbsp  oleo 
 ½ cup  brown sugar 
 ½ cup  milk 
 Directions : 
 Mix oats and boiling water; let stand 20 minutes. 
 Cream together shortening, sugar, eggs, vanilla and add cooled oats with dry ingredients. 
 Pour into an 8" x 8" pan. 
 Bake at 350 °F for 35 minutes.
