Ingredients : 
 butter-flavored vegetable  cooking spray 
 2 cups  apple , diced 
 1 – 2 tsp ground  cinnamon 
 ¼ tsp ground  nutmeg 
 1½ tsp  brown sugar 
 9 sheets commercial frozen  phyllo  pastry, thawed 
 3 tbsp  powdered sugar , sifted 
 1 tsp  vanilla extract 
 ½ tsp  water 
 Directions : 
 Coat a nonstick  skillet  with  cooking spray ; place over medium heat until hot. 
 Add diced  apple ; sauté 8 to 10 minutes, stirring often. 
 Add  cinnamon ,  nutmeg  and  brown sugar ; stir well, and cook an additional 3 minutes. 
 Remove from heat, and let cool. 
 Coat a flat surface with  cooking spray ; place 1 sheet of  phyllo  on surface (keep remaining  phyllo  covered). 
 Coat  phyllo  with butter-flavored  cooking spray . 
 Layer 2 more sheets  phyllo  on first sheet, coating each with  cooking spray . 
 Cut vertically into 5 equal strips; cut strips in half to make 10 pieces. 
 Repeat process with remaining  phyllo  sheets, spraying each sheet with  cooking spray . 
 Spoon 1 ½ tsp  apple  mixture at base of each strip; roll up, jelly roll fashion. 
 Place, seam side down, on a  baking sheet  coated with  cooking spray . 
 Bake at 375 °F for 30 to 35 minutes or until golden. 
 Remove to wire rack to cool slightly. 
 Combine remaining ingredients in a small bowl; stir well. 
 Drizzle 1½ tsp glaze over each roll. 
 Serve warm.
