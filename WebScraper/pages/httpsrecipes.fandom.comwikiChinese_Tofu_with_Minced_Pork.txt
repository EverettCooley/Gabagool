 Ingredients : 
 3/4 cup ground  Pork 
 1 teaspoon soy sauce 
 1 teaspoon  sesame oil 
 1/2 teaspoon  white sugar 
 1 teaspoon  cornstarch 
 1 dash ground  black pepper 
 1 dash  crushed red pepper  flakes 
 1 (14 ounce) package  tofu , drained 
 1  green onion , chopped 
 1 tablespoon chopped  carrots 
 1  chile pepper , chopped 
 Directions : 
 1 - Combine the porrk with soy sauce,  Sugar ,  sesame oil ,  pepper ,  cornstarch , and  red pepper flakes .
 2 - Pour mixture over the  tofu  and add the  onions ,  carrots , and  chile pepper  on top. Microwave for 5 minutes
