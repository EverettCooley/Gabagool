 Description : 
 This smooth, modern brownie is made and packed with real peanut butter, cream cheese, and powdered sugar and always delivers a thick aroma that it relishes.
 
 Ingredients : 
 For the brownie base : 
 3 cups powdered sugar 
 ½ cup peanut butter, regular or reduced fat/sugar-free only 
 ¼ cup softened butter 
 3 oz. pkg. softened cream cheese 
 3 tbsp. milk, any type 
 1 ⅜ chopped cashews, optional 
 For the glaze : 
 ¼ cup semi-sweet chocolate chips 
 1 tbsp. shortening 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl, combine powdered sugar, peanut butter, butter, cream cheese, milk, and chopped cashews (optional); then beat until light and fluffy. 
 Spread evenly over brownies. In a small saucepan, combine chocolate chips and shortening. Cook over low heat to melt. 
 Drizzle over the frosting. Serve cold and plain or with other desserts. Sell per piece, if desired.
