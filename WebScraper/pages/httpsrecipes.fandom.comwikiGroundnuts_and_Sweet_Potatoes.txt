Description : 
 Ideal as packed lunch for school chidren.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 kilogramme of  sweet potatoes  (Peeled) 
 1 kilogramme of  groundnuts  (peanuts) toasted and pounded 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Boil  sweet potatoes  in  salt   water . 
 Before they are tender add pounded groundnuts. 
 Cook a bit longer and then simmer.
