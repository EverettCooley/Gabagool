 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1kg of  mandioca 
 100g Grated cheese 
 1 tablespoon of  flour 
 2  eggs 
 1 teaspoon of pig fat 
 Directions : 
 Peel and boil the  mandioca  in  water  with  salt . 
 Remove from the fire and put through the grinder. 
 Put on the table, mix in the  eggs , the grated  Cheese , the  flour  and the fat. 
 Knead well and to form balls as large as a small nut. 
 Fry with plenty of  oil  or hot rich fat a few at a time, to avoid them bursting. Serve immediately. This can accompany meat.
