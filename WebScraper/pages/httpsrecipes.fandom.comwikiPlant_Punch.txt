Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 2 oz  light rum 
 1 oz  Jamaica rum 
 juice of 1  lime 
 juice of ½  lemon 
 juice of ½  orange 
 1 tsp  pineapple juice 
 2 dash  triple sec 
 1 dash  grenadine 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Pour all ingredients except alcohol and  Grenadine  into a glass filled with  ice . 
 Stir until glass is frosted. 
 Add Jamaica  rum  and  light rum  and stir. 
 Top with  triple sec  and  Grenadine . 
 Add  orange  and  pineapple  wedge on top. Serve with a straw.
