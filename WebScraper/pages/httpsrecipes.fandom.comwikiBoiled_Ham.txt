Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Morgan Estate in Austin, Texas in 1982.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 12 pound  ham 
 2 tablespoons powdered  crackers 
 1 teaspoon freshly ground  black pepper 
 2 tablespoons  brown sugar 
 whole  cloves 
 Directions : 
 Soak ham for 12 hours then cook slowly in water for 5 hours. 
 Cool in ham's own liquid. 
 Remove outside skin. 
 Make crisscross gashes in top of ham then sprinkle cracker dust and brown sugar on top. 
 Sprinkle with pepper and stick whole cloves into ham then place in roasting pan and bake at 350 for 1 hour.
