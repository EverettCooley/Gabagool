Crockpot  Sausage  Bake
 
 1 Lb. Bulk  Pork   Sausage 
 2 Tbsp.  sugar 
 1 Tbsp. All Purpose  flour 
 1/4 Tsp.  cinnamon 
 2 Medium  sweet potatoes , Peeled And Sliced 
 3 Medium  Apples , Peeled And Sliced 
 
Brown The  Sausage , Breaking up The Big Pieces; Drain Off Fat.
 
Blend  sugar ,  flour  And  cinnamon , 1/4 Tsp.  salt , And 1/2 Cup Cold  water .
 
Combine All Ingredients Into Crockpot; Cook on Low 4-5 Hours, StirringOnce or Twice.
 
It is Done When  potatoes  And  Apples  Are Both Tender.
