Description : 
 Yield: 1 cup each serving: 2 tablespoons free food (up to 2 servings per day)
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp  flour 
 ¼ cup  skim milk 
 1 cup fat-free  chicken broth 
 ½ tsp dried minced  onion  or fat-free drippings 
 salt  /  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Add  flour  to skim  milk ; beat or stir until smooth. 
 Place broth or drippings and  onion  in saucepan; heat to boiling. 
 Add flour- milk  mixture to broth very slowly, stirring constantly; season to taste. 
 Reduce heat; cook 5 minutes, stirring constantly.
