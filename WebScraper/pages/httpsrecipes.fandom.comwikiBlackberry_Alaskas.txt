 Ingredients : 
 4 lrg Patty shells, homemade or frozen 
 4 x  egg  white 
 1/4 cup  Sugar 
 1/4 tsp  vanilla 
 1 cup Fresh  blackberries 
 4 scoop  vanilla   ice   cream 
 Directions : 
 - Over 90% of Canada's raspberries come from B.C.s Fraser Valley. Berry lovers wait in anticipation until the month of July when the first succulent  Berries  make their appearance.
- Bake patty shells in 450 F oven for 25 minutes. Cool. Whip  egg  whites until foamy. Gradually beat in  Sugar  and continue beating until stiff meringue forms. Beat in  vanilla . Place patty shells on baking sheet. Spoon  blackberries  into bottom of patty shells; top with scoop of  ice   cream .
- Spread meringue over, sealing well all around and mounding at top. Bake in 450 F oven about 3 minutes until lightly browned. Serve immediately.
- Makes 4 servings.
