 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  water 
 1 teaspoon  aniseed 
 1/4 teaspoon  ginger 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 1 tablespoon  Honey 
 Directions : 
 1) Combine ingredients in small pan. Bring to boil, lower heat, simmer for 5 minutes. Strain. Serve hot in cups.
 2) This tea is often used to comfort sore throats and chest colds.
