 Makes 3½ Quarts. 
 Ingredients : 
 2 quarts  water 
 1 lb  split peas , rinsed 
 1½ lb  kielbasa  sausage, cut into bite size pieces 
 salt  and  pepper 
 2 medium  boiling potatoes , peeled and cubed 
 ¾ cup diced  carrots 
 ½ cup chopped  celery 
 Instructions : 
 Mix  water  and  peas  in large pan or kettle. 
 Bring to boil. 
 Add  sausage  chunks,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Reduce heat. 
 Cover. 
 Simmer 30 minutes. 
 Add remaining ingredient and continue simmering until soup is slightly thickened and vegetables are tender, about 15 minutes. 
 Serve at once.
