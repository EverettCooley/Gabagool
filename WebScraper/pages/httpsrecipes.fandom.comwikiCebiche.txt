Description : 
       Cebiche     
 The fish must be a fresh one. 
 
 For 4 people 
 Ingredients : 
 200 g white skin  fish 
 ½ teaspoon ground  garlic 
 1 teaspoon green ground red pepper 
 ½ teaspoon grated 'limo' red pepper 
 2½ oz  lemons 
 some grated  red onion 
 salt  and  white pepper 
 extra:  sweet potato  and 'choclo' 
 Method : 
 Cut the fish in 3 cm x 3 cm squares. 
 Add ground garlic, salt, green ground red pepper, white pepper and grated 'limo' red pepper. 
 Add lemon juice. 
 Mix for 1 minute. 
 Finally add grated red onion over the fish. 
 serve with sweet potatoes and 'choclos'.
