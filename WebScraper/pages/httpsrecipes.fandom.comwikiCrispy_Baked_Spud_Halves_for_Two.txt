Description : 
 A TNT recipe for two.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: my old recipes 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large baking  potato 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 tbsp melted  margarine 
 2 tbsp grated  Parmesan cheese 
 ½ tbsp crumbled  basil  or  thyme 
 a little  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Scrub and dry a large baking  potato . 
 Cut in half lengthwise. 
 Brush cut surfaces with a mixture  lemon juice  and melted  margarine , then dip in a mixture grated  parmesan cheese , crumbled  basil  or  thyme  and a little  pepper . 
 Bake cut sides down on a greased heavy baking sheet in a 375°F oven for 30 to 35 minutes until tender and browned, crispy and flavorful.
