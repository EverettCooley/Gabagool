Description : 
 Contributed by  Pressurecookerrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs cube  steak 
 1 large  onion , chopped 
 3 cloves  garlic , chopped 
 2 tsp  Worcestershire sauce 
 cumin 
 salt 
 pepper 
 Cajun spice 
 onion powder 
 garlic powder 
 2 cups of  water 
 ½ cup  white wine 
 1 can  white corn  drained 
 3 pkg brown/onion  gravy  mix prepared with  water  as directed on package. flour 
 2 tbsp  olive oil 
 1  bay leaf 
 Directions : 
 Sauté  onion  in  oil  until clear. 
 Add  garlic . 
 Saute another 2 minutes. 
 Sprinkle  steak   steak  with a rub of  salt ,  pepper ,  onion  powder, garlic powder and Cajun spice. 
 Sear in  olive oil  until browned on both sides,about 2 minutes each side. 
 Add 2 cups of  water  and ½ cup of white wine to cooker and stir briefly. 
 Add all remaining ingredients. 
 Pressure cook in electric pressure cooker for 15 minutes. 
 Serve with noodles or  rice .
