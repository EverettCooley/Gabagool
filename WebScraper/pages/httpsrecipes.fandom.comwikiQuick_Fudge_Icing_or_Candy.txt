Description 
 Betty Crocker's Cook Book for Boys and girls , out of copyright
 
 Ingredients 
 
 1 cup  sugar 
 ¼ cup  cocoa 
 ¼ cup  butter 
 ½ cup  milk 
 2 tablespoons  light corn syrup 
 1½ cups  sifted   confectioners' sugar 
 1 teaspoon  vanilla 
 Directions 
 Mix sugar and cocoa in saucepan. Stir in butter, milk, corn syrup. 
 Bring to boil. Boil for 3 minutes, stirring occasionally. Take out spoon and set pan in cold water. When you can hold your hand on the bottom of the pan the syrup is cool enough. 
 Then, stir in 1½ cups confectioners' sugar and vanilla. You may need to add an additional ½ cup of confectioners' sugar. 
 Stir the icing until thick enough to spread. Add more confectioners' sugar or milk if needed. 
 Variations 
 To make fudge, add more confectioners' sugar and chopped nuts.
