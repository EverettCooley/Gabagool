 Description : 
       Tea Cookies     
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 	cups  flour 
 1 	cup  butter 
 1/3 	cup  light cream  or half-and-half 
 1/4 	cup  Sugar , for dusting cookies 
 Directions : 
 Cut  butter  into  flour  until it resembles  cornmeal . 
 Stir in  cream  . 
 Turn out onto a floured surface. 
 Roll to 1/8Â inch thickness. 
 Cut into circles . 
 Before baking, prick with a fork and sprinkle with  Sugar . 
 Bake for 8-10 mins on an ungreased cookie sheet. 
 Place two cookies together  butter   cream  .
