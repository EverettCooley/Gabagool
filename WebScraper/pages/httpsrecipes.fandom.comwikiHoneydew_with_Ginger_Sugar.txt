Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 honeydew melon , cut into wedges 
 ¼ tsp ground  ginger 
 1 tbsp of  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Sprinkle honeydew wedges with a mixture of ¼ tsp ground ginger to every tbsp of sugar.
