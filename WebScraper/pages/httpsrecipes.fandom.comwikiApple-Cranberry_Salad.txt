Description : 
 A word about substitutes in this recipe. I use light mayo in everything as well as low sodium products, so feel free to use them too. You can also substitute dried cherries or cranberries for the raisins. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Makes 6 (½-cup) servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2½ cups diced red  apples  (2 to 3 medium) 
 ½ cup chopped  celery 
 ¼ cup sweetened  dried cranberries 
 ¼ cup  mayonnaise 
 1 tablespoon  orange juice 
 1 teaspoon grated  ginger  root 
 6 large  lettuce  leaves 
 Directions : 
 In large bowl, combine apples, celery and cranberries and mix well. 
 In a small bowl, blend mayonnaise, orange juice and ginger root. 
 Add dressing to salad; stir gently to coat. 
 Serve on 6 individual lettuce-lined plates.
