Description : 
 Savory Oatcakes
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 tbsp hot water 
 4 tbsp medium  oatmeal 
 Â½ tbsp  bacon fat 
 pinch of  salt 
 Directions : 
 Melt the fat in the water, and then sprinkle in the oatmeal. 
 Knead for several minutes. 
 Roll out on a floured board with oatmeal, rolling very thin. 
 Cut into large or small rounds as desired. 
 Bake on a moderately hot griddle or thick frying pan for about ten minutes. 
 Allow to harden in a warm place. 
 Serve with slices of cheese.
