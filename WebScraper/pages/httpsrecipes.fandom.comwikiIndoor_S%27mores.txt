 48 Squares 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅔ cup  light corn syrup 
 2 tbsp  butter 
 1 (11 ½-oz) pkg  milk chocolate  morsels 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 8 cups  Graham Squares cereal 
 3 cups mini  marshmallows 
 Directions : 
 Butter 13"x9"x2" rectangular pan. 
 Heat  corn syrup ,  butter  and  chocolate morsels  to boiling in 2-qt saucepan, stirring constantly. 
 Remove from heat. 
 Stir in  vanilla . 
 Pour over cereal in large mixing bowl; toss quickly until completely coated with  chocolate  mixture. 
 Fold in marshmallows, 1 cup at a time. 
 Press mixture evenly in pan with buttered back of spoon. 
 Let stand until firm, at least 1 hour. 
 Cut into 1½" squares.
