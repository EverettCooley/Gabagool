Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 c  rhubarb 
 1 pk  strawberry  Jello 
 Small size 
 1 c  Sugar 
 1 Stick of  butter 
 1 1/2 c  water 
 1 White cake mix 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Put  rhubarb  in greased 13x9x2 inch pan. 
 Mix  Sugar  and Jello; sprinkle over  rhubarb . 
 Sprinkle cake mix over that and sprinkle  water  over all. 
 Slice  butter  on top of cake mix. 
 Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes. 
 Serve with warm  vanilla   ice cream  or  whipped cream . 
 Serve cold if you're lucky enough to have any left.
