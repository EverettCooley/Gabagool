Doughnut sugar  is a specially developed powdered sugar that will not dissolve or melt on baked items. It is extremely fine powdered sugar with a wonderful flavor. It is used in decorating donuts, breakfast pastries, fruit tarts, cakes and cookies. The resulting baked items can be refrigerated or frozen.
