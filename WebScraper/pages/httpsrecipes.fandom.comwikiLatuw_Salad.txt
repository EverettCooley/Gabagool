Description : 
 A fresh salad that can be served with other small appetizers like  ham  or  cheese .
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 whole  lettuce 
 400 g  mixed salad 
 half a  cucumber 
 6 spoons  olive oil 
 2 spoons  vinegar 
 1 clove of  garlic 
 pepper 
 salt 
 rosemary 
 fresh garden  herbs 
 2 hard boiled  eggs 
 Directions : 
 Wash and dry the salads. 
 Mix them in a bowl together with with the shredded piece of  cucumber . 
 Mix the  oil ,  vinegar ,  garlic ,  pepper  and  salt , and sprinkle it over the salad. 
 Top off with the  eggs  cut into parts, and with herbs of your choice. 
 You can add pieces of  ham ,  cheese , salmon â€” whatever you like, but make sure it goes together with the herbs you chose.
