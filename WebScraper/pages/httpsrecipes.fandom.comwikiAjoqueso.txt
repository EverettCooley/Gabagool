 Serves 8 
 Ingredients : 
 1 small can peeled  green chilies  (4 oz) 
 1 large  onion , minced 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 ¼ cup  oil 
 2 tbsp  flour 
 1 cup  half and half  or  light cream 
 ½ lb shredded mild  cheddar  cheese or  jack cheese 
 Directions : 
 Rinse seeds from chilies and cut into fine dice. 
 Cook  onion  and  garlic  in  oil  until wilted. 
 Add  flour  and stir over direct heat until bubbly, about 2 minutes. 
 Gradually stir in cream until smooth. 
 Add  cheese  and chilies. 
 Heat, stirring until  cheese  melts. 
 Transfer, if needed to fondue pot or chafing dish. 
 Keep warm over burner or  water  bath.
