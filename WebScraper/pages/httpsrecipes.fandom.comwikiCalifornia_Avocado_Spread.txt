Description : 
 Enjoy fresh  California avocado  as a spread on whole grain toast.

 Simple spread :

 Add  salt  to taste

 Sweet spread : 
 1 oz ripe Genuine  California avocado

 Directions : 
 In a medium bowl, mash  avocado  with a fork. 
 Stir in desired ingredients.
