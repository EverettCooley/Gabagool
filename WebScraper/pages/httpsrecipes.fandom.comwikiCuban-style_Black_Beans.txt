Ingredients : 
 2 (15 oz) cans  black beans 
 1 medium  onion , chopped 
 1  green bell pepper , chopped 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1 tsp  cumin  powder 
 ½ tsp  oregano 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  red wine vinegar 
 Directions : 
 Sauté the onion, green pepper and garlic in a little of the bean liquid until soft. 
 Add the  black beans  along with any remaining liquid. 
 Add the spices. 
 Simmer for 15 – 30 minutes. 
 Add the  wine vinegar  just before serving.
