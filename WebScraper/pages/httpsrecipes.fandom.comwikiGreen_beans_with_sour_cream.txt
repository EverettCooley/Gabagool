 Description : 
 In Romanian: Fasole verde cu smantana.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs/1 kg  green beans , 
 1 tablespoon  butter , 
 1 cup  sour cream , 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Simmer for a few minutes, shaking the pan so that the beans are well mixed with the  butter . 
 Place the beans on a plate and pour the  sour cream  on top. Serve immediately.
