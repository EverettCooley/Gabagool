Description : 
 In Romanian: Inghetata de fragi sau zmeura
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ qt / ½ l  strawberry  /  raspberry juice 
 12 oz / 350 g  sugar 
 1½ cups  water 
 juice from 1  lemon 
 1 cup  whipped cream 
 Directions : 
 Boil the  sugar  with the  water  for a few minutes and remove from heat. 
 Add the juice to the syrup, then juice from 1  lemon  and let cool. 
 Freeze. 
 When the mixture is almost frozen, add a cup of  whipped cream . 
 Mix well and freeze.
