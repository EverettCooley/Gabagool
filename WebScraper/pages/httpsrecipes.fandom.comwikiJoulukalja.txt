       Joulukalja     
 Christmas Brew. This brew is similar to the Scandinavian gl√∂g, but is without alcohol (at least originally . . . ) This variation is called pikakalja, "quick brew" for our busy days. There are other variations, but they are very involved.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  sugar 
 1.5 cups  malt 
 about 1 gallon  water 
 1 tbsp  cake yeast 
 Directions : 
 Quickly boil the  malt  and the  water . 
 Let cool down covered. 
 Add  sugar  and  yeast  dissolved in small amount of  water  and let stand overnight. 
 Kalja will be ready the next day. 
 Keep refrigerated. Will not keep very long.
