Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked medium  grain   brown rice 
 1 cup red  grape tomatoes ; sliced in half 
 ⅓ cup pitted  kalamata , sliced 
 ¼ cup crumbled  feta  cheese 
 ¼ cup  oil  and  vinegar  or Italian dressing 
 salt  and freshly ground  pepper  to taste 
 romaine lettuce  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  tomatoes ,  feta  cheese and dressing in a large mixing bowl. 
 Season with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Toss well and serve on  romaine lettuce  leaves, or use  lettuce  leaves as wraps to hold filling.
