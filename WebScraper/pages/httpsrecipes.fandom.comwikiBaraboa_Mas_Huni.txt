 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 300 g  pumpkin  (peeled, boiled in salted  water  and drained) 
 50 g  Onion  (sliced thinly) 
 25 g  coconut  (freshly grated) 
 25 g Smoked  Tuna  Fillet (thinly sliced) 
 1  cherry pepper  (chopped) 
 Juice of 1  lime 
 salt  to season 
 Directions : 
 Crush the  Onion  and  cherry pepper  with  lime juice  and  salt . Add in  coconut  and  Tuna  and crush them well. 
 Squeeze the  water  out of the drained  pumpkin . 
 Add in the  pumpkin  and bind all the ingredients together. Adjust seasoning.
