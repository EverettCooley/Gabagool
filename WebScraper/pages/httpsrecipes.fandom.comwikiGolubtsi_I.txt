Ingredients : 
 1 pound  ground beef 
 2 cups cooked  rice 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 2  eggs   salt 
 1 large  cabbage 
 2 cups  tomato sauce  or  juice 
 ½ teaspoon  oregano 
 ½ teaspoon  basil 
 1 cup  sour cream , optional 
 Directions : 
 Remove the central part from the  cabbage  and put the  cabbage  in a large pot with boiling  water  for 15 minutes, until the leaves become manageable. 
 Prepare the  beef  mixture by mixing  beef ,  rice ,  eggs , spices,  salt  and  onion  together. 
 Carefully separate the  cabbage  leaves and roll 1 tablespoon of the meat mixture into each  cabbage  leaf and fold it. 
 Place all of the  cabbage  rolls into a large pot, add  tomato  sauce (mixed with  water  if desired) to cover them and cook for 40 minutes until done.
