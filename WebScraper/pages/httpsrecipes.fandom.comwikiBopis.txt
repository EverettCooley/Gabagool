Bopis  is one spicy Filipino dish. It is  Pork  lungs and heart sauteed in  tomato ,  chilli  and  Onion .

 Ingredients : 
 2  Pork  lungs with heart, parboiled then chopped. 
 1 pint  tomato  sauce 
 1 cup  Cider 
 3 cloves of  garlic , chopped 
 1  Onion , chopped 
 2  bay leaves 
 1 green  bell pepper , diced 
 1/4 tsp  ground red pepper 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Saute  garlic  and  onions , then add meat and heart,  Cider  and  tomato  sauce. Season with  salt  and  pepper . Bring to a simmer, and without stirring, cover and cook for 30 minutes. Add  peppers  amd simmer until sauce is reduced and almost dry.
