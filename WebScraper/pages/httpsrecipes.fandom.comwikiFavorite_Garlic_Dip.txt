Favorite  garlic  Dip
 
 8 oz Package  cream cheese 
 3 tb  milk 
 5 Cloves Fresh  garlic 
 salt 
 parsley 
 paprika 
 
With Fork, Mix  cream cheese  And  milk  in Small Bowl Until it Has The TextureOf  Peanut butter . Press  garlic  And Add to  cream cheese  Mixture.  salt  ToTaste And Mix Thoroughly. Garnish With  parsley  And  paprika . RefrigerateUntil Ready to Use.
 
Enjoy.
