Description : 
 This is something like  apple  pie but it has nuts and  oats  on the top of it
 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 Makes 9 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 x 21 oz cans  apple pie filling 
 2  eggs 
 14 oz can  sweetened condensed milk 
 ¼ cup  butter , melted 
 ½ tsp  cinnamon 
 ¼ tsp  nutmeg 
 ½ cup  brown sugar 
 ½ cup  flour 
 ¼ cup  butter 
 ½ cup chopped  nuts 
 ½ cup  oats 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 375 °F. 
 Spread  apple  filling in buttered 9 inch square baking pan. 
 In bowl, beat  eggs ,  condensed milk , melted  butter  and spices, pour over  apple  filling. 
 In 2nd bowl, combine  brown sugar  and  flour , cut in  butter  until crumbly. 
 Add nuts and  oats . 
 Sprinkle over top. 
 Bake 50 minutes or until set. 
 Cool. 
 Serve warm with vanilla  ice cream .
