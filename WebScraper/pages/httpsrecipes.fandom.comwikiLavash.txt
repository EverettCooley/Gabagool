      Lahvosh     
 Name variations : 
 lahvosh 
 About lavash : 
 A round, thin, crisp bread that's also known as Armenian cracker bread. It comes in a soft version, as well as in various sizes, ranging from about 6 to 14 inches in diameter. 
 It's the bread used to make the popular  Aram sandwich .
