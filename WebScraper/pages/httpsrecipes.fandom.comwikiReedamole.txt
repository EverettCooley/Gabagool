Description : 
 Great at your next picnic or beach party
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 California  Reed avocado 
 salt  - According to taste 
 pepper  - According to taste 
 garlic powder  - According to taste 
 Salsa  - According to taste 
 Directions : 
 With stem side up, cut across Reed size 24 or 20, 2 inches down from stem. 
 Remove seed. 
 Using fork, break down fruit from the woody-skin and mash inside the  California avocado . 
 Add  salt ,  pepper ,  garlic powder  and salsa to taste. 
 You now have your own hand-held Reedamole as a delicious appetizer.
