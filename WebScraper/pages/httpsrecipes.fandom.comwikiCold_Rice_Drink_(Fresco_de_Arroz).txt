Description : 
 Makes 8 to 10 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup uncooked  rice 
 2 quarts  water 
 1 1/2 cups  Sugar 
 1/4 teaspoon  salt 
 1 cup  orange juice 
 1 cup  pineapple juice  (canned or fresh) 
 1/4 cup  lemon juice 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 4 whole cloves 
 1 cup cubed  pineapple  (1/4-inch size), canned or fresh 
 3 tablespoons grated  orange peel 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice  and  water  in 3-quart saucepan. Bring to a boil. Stir. Lower heat and simmer 35 minutes or until  rice  is very soft. 
 Puree in electric blender or food processor. 
 Combine  Sugar ,  salt , juices and spices. Cook until syrupy, about 10 minutes. Add the pureed  rice ,  pineapple  cubes, and peel. Bring to a boil. Remove from heat; remove cloves. Chill. Sliced  strawberries  may be added just before servings.
