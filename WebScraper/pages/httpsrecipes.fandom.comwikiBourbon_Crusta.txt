Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz  bourbon 
 ½ oz  lemon juice 
 ½ oz  triple sec 
 2 tsp  Maraschino liqueur 
 lemon  wedge and  superfine sugar 
 garnish:  orange  slice 
 glassware :  sherry  glass 
 Directions : 
 Rub the rim of a  sherry  glass with the  lemon  wedge and dip the glass into  superfine sugar  in a saucer. 
 Shake all the ingredients in a shaker with  ice  and strain into a  sherry  glass. 
 Garnish with an  orange  slice.
