 Prep Time:  15 minutes
 Cook Time:  1 hr 30 minutes
 Yields:  6-8 servings
 
 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 
 2 lbs. ground beef 
 2 eggs 
 1/2 medium sized onion, chopped 
 10-12 saltine crackers or handful of croutons 
 1/4 cup milk 
 Salt and pepper to taste 
 Ketchup 
 
 Directions : 
 
 Preheat oven to 350 degrees. 
 Combine ground beef, eggs, onion,(few shakes) salt and pepper, crackers or croutons, and milk in large bowl. 
 Using hands gently mix all ingredients together making sure once mixed that it is stiff and sticks together in a ball(if not sticking together add more crackers or croutons). 
 Once mixed, put in 8x10 pan with sides and form into log shape. 
 When in pan, put fair amount of ketchup to top of meat (to give flavor and keep moist). 
 Bake for 1 hr. and then check to make sure meat is done, if not place in oven for another 30 minutes.
