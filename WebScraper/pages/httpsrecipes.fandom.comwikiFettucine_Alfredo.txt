 Prep Time: 5 Minutes 
 Cook time: 15 Minutes 
 Serves: 2 
 
 Description : 
 A traditional Italian cream sauce.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  heavy cream 
 ⅓ cup  Parmesan , grated 
 pat of  butter 
 ⅛ tbsp. minced  garlic 
 1 box  fettuccine  pasta 
 Directions : 
 Put pasta in a pot of salted boiling water. When cooked, strain and set aside. 
 Melt butter in small saucepan, add garlic to cook. 
 Add cream in a few minutes, after the garlic has sauteed. 
 Put cheese in cream when hot. Whisk until melted. 
 Toss pasta in sauce and serve.
