Description : 
 Makes 4 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x 10¾-ounce can condensed  cream of mushroom  soup 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1½ tablespoons  lemon juice 
 ½ pound peeled and de-veined cooked  shrimp 
 3 cups hot cooked  rice 
 3 tablespoons chopped  pimentos 
 2 tablespoons chopped fresh  parsley 
 1 tablespoon minced  onion 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine , melted 
 Directions : 
 Combine soup,  pepper  and  lemon juice  in medium saucepan. Cook over low heat, stirring occasionally, until bubbly hot. 
 Add  shrimp . Heat 1 to 2 minutes longer. 
 Toss  rice  with  pimentos ,  parsley ,  onion  and  butter . 
 Spoon around inside edge of serving dish; ladle  shrimp  mixture into center of dish.
