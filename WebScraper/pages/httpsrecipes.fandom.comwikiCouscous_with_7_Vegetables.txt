Ingredients : 
 1 Medium  Onion 
 1 tb Veg Broth 
 3 Cups  vegetable broth 
 2  carrots , Peeled 
 2  turnips , Peeled 
 1  sweet potato 
 1  zucchini 
 1 red pepper 
 1 Can  Garbanzo beans , Drained 
 1 12 Oz. or 15 Oz. Can  tomato  Sauce 
 1/4 Tsp.  cinnamon 
 1/2 Tsp.  turmeric 
 Pinch of  saffron 
 Pinch of  curry 
 2 Cups  couscous   grains 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Cut all veggies in julienne strips 
 In a Large Saucepan, Saute onion Until Lightly Browned. 
 Add Vegetable Broth And Bring to a Boil. 
 Transfer to a Large Pot And Add  carrots ,  turnips , And Potato. Simmer 15 Mins. 
 Lower Heat And Add  zucchini  And  pepper . Cook 20 Mins. 
 Add beans,  tomato  Sauce And Spices. Cook Til Heated Through. 
 In a Separate Pot, Bring 2 1/2 Cups of  water  to a Boil. 
 Add  couscous , Cover And Take Off of Heat. 
 Let Stand 5-7 Minutes, 
 Fluff With a Fork And Serve With Vegetables on Top.
