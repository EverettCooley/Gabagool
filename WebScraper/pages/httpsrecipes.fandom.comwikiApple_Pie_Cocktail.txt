Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ oz Myers's Original  dark rum 
 ½ oz  sweet vermouth 
 1 tsp  apple brandy 
 3 dashes  grenadine 
 1 tsp  lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Shake ingredients with  ice . 
 Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
