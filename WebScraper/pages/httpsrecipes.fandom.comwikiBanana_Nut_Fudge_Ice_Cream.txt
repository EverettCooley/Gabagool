Description : 
 Contributed by Lilly at  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 8 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (7-oz) jar  marshmallow crème 
 1 (6-oz) pkg  semi-sweet chocolate  pieces 
 ¾ cup mashed ripe  banana , about 2  bananas 
 1 pt  half-and-half 
 ½ cup chopped  nuts 
 Directions : 
 Combine  marshmallow crème ,  chocolate  pieces and ½ cup half-and-half in 2-qt saucepan. 
 Cook, stirring frequently, over low heat until well blended. 
 Add remaining half-and-half and  bananas ; mix well. 
 Pour into 9" sq pan; freeze until almost firm. 
 Coarsely chop mixture; spoon into chilled bow. 
 Add nuts. 
 Beat with electric mixer until smooth; freeze.
