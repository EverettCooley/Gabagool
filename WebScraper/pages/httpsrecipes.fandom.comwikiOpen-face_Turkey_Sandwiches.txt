 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Submitted by: dlife 
 Source:  dLife original recipe 
 Makes 4 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 4 slices rye-pumpernickel swirl,  rye , or  pumpernickel bread 
 1 tablespoon + 1 teaspoon light tub  margarine 
 4 leaves  Bibb lettuce 
 8 ounces thinly sliced cooked  turkey breast , cooked without salt, skin and all visible fat discarded 
 ½ small  green bell pepper , cut into 4 thin rings 
 ½ small  red onion , thinly sliced 
 1 medium Italian  plum tomato , thinly sliced 
 4 teaspoons crumbled  blue cheese 
 Directions : 
 Separate each slice of bread, and spread each slice with margarine. 
 On each slice of bread, put each of the remaining ingredients in the order listed. 
 Sprinkle 1 teaspoon of cheese over each sandwich. 
 Add pickled cucumbers for an added taste. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per Serving: 
 
 Calories: 183 | Carbohydrates: 18g | Protein: 21g | Total Fat: 2.5g | Saturated: 1g | Cholesterol: 51mg | Fiber: 2g | Sodium: 323mg
