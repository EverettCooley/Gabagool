Ingredients= : 
 2 slices whole grain vegan  bread 
 several slices of the best summer  tomato  you can find 
 fresh  basil  leaves (a few, torn up) 
 tablespoon of  hummus  (I love the  sun-dried tomato  kind) 
 some fresh ground  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Spread the 2 vegan  bread  slices with the hummus. 
 Slice the  tomato  and place on one vegan  bread  slice. 
 Spread the torn-up  basil  over the  tomato  slices. 
 Grind some  pepper  over that. 
 Cover with the other vegan  bread  slice. 
 Eat!
