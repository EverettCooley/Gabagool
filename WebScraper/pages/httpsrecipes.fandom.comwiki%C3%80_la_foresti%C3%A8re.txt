Means 'to be served with sautéed  mushrooms '. French term meaning of the forest, referring to dishes (usually poultry, meat or game fowl) garnished with butter-sautéed potatoes or potato balls, bacon or salt pork and wild mushrooms such as  chanterelles ,  morels  and  porcini .
