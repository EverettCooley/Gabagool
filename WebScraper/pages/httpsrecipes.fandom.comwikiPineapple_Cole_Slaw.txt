Ingredients : 
 2 tablespoons chopped  green pepper 
 ½ cup diced  apple 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1 teaspoon  sugar 
 ¼ cup  vinegar 
 3 cups shredded  cabbage 
 1 cups shredded  carrot 
 3 slices  pineapple 
 Directions : 
 Combine  green pepper ,  apple ,  salt ,  sugar ,  vinegar , and  carrots . 
 Top with shredded  carrots  and  pineapple .
