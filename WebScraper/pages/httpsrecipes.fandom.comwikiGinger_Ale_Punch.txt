 Description : 
 Purchased from the Reinhart Estate in Ida, Oklahoma in 1992. Date and source unknown. This is tried and true and very good. I serve it at bridal and baby showers. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1-½ cups  water 
 ½-cup  Sugar 
 4 sticks  cinnamon 
 5 cans frozen  orange juice 
 1 quart  cherry  juice 
 1 quart  pineapple juice 
 1 cup  cherries 
 1 quart  ginger ale 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Place  water ,  Sugar  and  cinnamon  in saucepan over high heat. 
 Bring to a boil stirring to dissolve  Sugar . Boil 5 minutes. 
 Remove from heat and strain. Stir syrup into other juices. 
 Mix well and chill until ready to serve. 
 Add  ginger ale  and  cherries  just before serving. 
 I also float  orange  slices on top.
