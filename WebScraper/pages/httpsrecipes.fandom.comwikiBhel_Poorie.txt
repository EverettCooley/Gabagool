Description : 
 Ethnicity - Marathi, West Indian 
 Type of meal - Party, Lunch, Dinner 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cup  puffed rice  (murmura) 
 8  poories 
 ½ cup fine  sev 
 1 cup boiled and cubed  potatoes 
 1 cup finely chopped  onions 
 1 tbsp  green chutney , mixed together with  water 
 ¼ cup  date chutney , mixed together with  water 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 tbsp  salt 
 coriander leaves  for garnish 
 Green Chutney : 
 garlic 
 green chillies 
 a little  lemon juice 
 coriander leaves 
 Date Chutney : 
 Dried powdered ginger]] 
 Dried  dates , puréed 
 Directions : 
 Mix all together, garnish with sev and  coriander leaves  and serve. 
 Green Chutney : 
 Grind together equal quantities of  coriander leaves ,  garlic  to taste and green chillies and add some  lemon juice . 
 Date Chutney : 
 Cook and purée dried  dates  and add some dried [powdered  ginger .
