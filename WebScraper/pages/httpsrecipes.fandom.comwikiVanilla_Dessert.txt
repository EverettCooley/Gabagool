      Vanilla Dessert     
 Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp  arrowroot 
 500 ml  soy / rice milk 
 ¼ cup raw  sugar 
 ¼  brown sugar 
 1 tbsp  vanilla extract 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve  arrowroot  in a little of the  milk . 
 Gradually heat in a saucepan. 
 Slowly add remaining  milk  stirring continuously. 
 Add remaining ingredients and simmer over a medium heat, stirring until mixture thickens. 
 Pour into individual dishes and refrigerate until ready to serve.
