Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Fisher Estate in Rockwall, Texas in 1980.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 package  lemon gelatin 
 2 cups boiling water 
 2 tablespoons  lemon juice 
 ⅔ cup  cabbage  chopped 
 ⅔ cup  green pepper  chopped 
 2 slices  pimento 
 lettuce  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve gelatin in boiling water and stir until completely dissolved. 
 Add lemon juice then add chopped vegetables and chill. 
 Slice when firm and serve on lettuce leaves with your favorite dressing.
