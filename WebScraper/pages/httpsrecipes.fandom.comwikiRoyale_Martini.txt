 Ingredients : 
 1.5 shots Stolichnaya  vodka 
 0.5 shot De Kuyper  crème de cassis 
 Top-up  Champagne 
 Directions : 
 Stir the Stolichnaya  vodka  and de Kuyper  crème de cassis  together with  ice  in a cocktail shaker. 
 Strain into a chilled martini glass. 
 Top up with brut (dry) champagne. 
 Garnish with a  lemon  twist.
