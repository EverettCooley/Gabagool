Ingredients : 
 30 g  butter   or   margarine 
 160 g  sugar 
 2  eggs , separated 
 15 g  flour 
 1 ml  salt 
 1 ml  vanilla extract 
 250 ml  milk 
 250 ml  buttermilk  (cultured is ok) 
 Directions : 
 Cream  butter  and  Sugar . 
 Beat egg yolks one by one into creamed  Sugar  and  butter . 
 Add the  flour ,  salt  and  milk  alternately to the batter, mixing well after every addition. 
 Add  vanilla extract . 
 Fold in stiffly beaten whites and bake one hour in deep bowl at 180 °C. 
 Serve with  strawberry  sauce of your choice.
