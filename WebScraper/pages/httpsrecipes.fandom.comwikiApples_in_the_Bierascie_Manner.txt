Ingredients : 
 8  apples 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 1 tbsp  butter 
 1 tsp  potato flour 
 0.5 cupful  milk 
 1  egg 
 1 tbsp  raisins 
 1 tbsp  castor sugar 
 cinnamon 
 Directions : 
 Scoop the cores out of the  apples , fill the hollowed  apples  with sweet mixture. 
 Make the sweet mixture as follows: pour the dissolved  potato flour  in boiling  milk , bring to a boil again, add the washed  raisins ,  sugar ,  butter . 
 Then cool, add the  eggs  and  cinnamon  and mix well. 
 Place the  apples  on a griddle, add some  water  and bake.
