Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  jack cheese 
 ½ cup  cheddar  cheese 
 1 tbsp  flour 
 ½ tsp  dry mustard 
 ½ tsp  paprika 
 4 oz  beer 
 1 tsp liquid  hot pepper sauce 
 ¼ cup  milk 
 Directions : 
 Combine  jack cheese ,  cheddar  cheese,  flour ,  dry mustard , and paprika-toss to mix well. 
 Combine  beer  and hot pepper sauce  in a saucepan, over a medium-low flame. 
 Add  cheese  mixture, a little at a time. 
 Heat and stir to melt  cheese . 
 Stir in  milk . 
 Serve warm.
