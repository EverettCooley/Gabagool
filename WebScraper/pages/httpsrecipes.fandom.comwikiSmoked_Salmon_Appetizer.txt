 Ingredients : 
 200 g (7 oz)  smoked salmon  slices, or as much as you can afford 
 1  lemon , quartered 
 4 thin slices brown  bread , buttered and cut into triangles 
 Directions : 
 Arrange the smoked salmon onto 4 plates. 
 Garnish with a  lemon  wedge and brown bread triangles.
