Yield: 12 Servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 10 oz  Angel Food Cake 
 14 oz  sweetened condensed milk 
 1 c  water ; Cold 
 1 ts  almond extract 
 1 sm  vanilla  Instant Pudding 
 2 c  whipping cream ; Whipped 
 21 oz Pie Filling  cherry 
 Directions : 
 Cut cake into 1/4 inch slices; arrange half of the slices on the bottom of a 9x13 baking dish. 
 In a Large Mixing Bowl, Combine The  condensed milk ,  water  and extract; mix well. Add pudding mix; beat well. Chill for 5 minutes. Fold in  whipped cream . 
 Spread half of the cream mix over cake slices; top evenly with one can of pie filling. Top with remaining cake slices, cream mix and the other can of pie filling. Chill until set. Cut the squares to serve. Refrigerate leftovers. Enjoy. 
 
 
 Variaations : 
 May use other flavors of pie filling.
