Ingredients : 
 200 g grated yellow  Cheese 
 200 g Bulgarian  Cheese 
 200 g  cream cheese 
 200 g  margarine 
 2  eggs 
 350 g self raising  flour  (add more if nec) 
 oregano  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Mix all the ingredients together. 
 Make small rolls. 
 Optional: add  oregano . 
 Bake at 180 degrees C for 20 minutes (until light brown on top).
