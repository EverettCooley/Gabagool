Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  butter  or  shortening 
 ½ cup  sugar 
 2  eggs 
 1 cup chopped  raisins 
 1 tablespoons  molasses 
 1 teaspoon each of all kinds of spice ( mace ,  nutmeg ,  allspice ,  cinnamon ,  cloves , etc.) 
 ½ teaspoon  soda 
 flour 
 Directions : 
 Cream fat, add  sugar  gradually. 
 Add  eggs ,  molasses  and  raisins . 
 Sift soda and spices with one cup of  flour , and mix all thoroughly. 
 Add enough  flour  to make quite a stiff enough, and roll. 
 Bake in a moderate oven (380°F).
