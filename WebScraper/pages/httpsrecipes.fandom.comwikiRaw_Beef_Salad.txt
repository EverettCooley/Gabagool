 Directions : 
 With a vegetable peeler, shave the parmesan into thin slices. 
Using a slicing machine, electric knife or very sharp knife, cut the  Beef  into paper-thin slices.  
Arrange the slices, overlapping slightly, on four chilled plates.  
Sprinkle with  lemon , a pinch of  salt  and  pepper  to taste. 
Arrange the augural over the  Beef .  
Drizzle each serving with 2tbsp  olive oil  and scatter the  Cheese  over the top.  
Serve immediately.
