Ingredients : 
 2 1/4 lbs  elderberries  (vlierbessen) 
 4 cups  water 
 1/2  cinnamon  stick (pijpe kaneel) 
 1/2  lemon , rind of, of (schil van 1/2 citroen) 
 2/3 cup  Sugar 
 1 pinch  salt 
 2  Pears 
 2 teaspoons  cornstarch 
 1/2 cup  white wine 
 1/2 cup  whipped cream 
 Directions : 
 Remove  Berries  from stems, wash with cold  waterand  let drain.  
Bring 4 cups  water  to boil, add the  Berriesand  let cook over a soft fire with a lid on, for about 15 minutes.  
Press the  Berries  and  waterthrough  a sieve and return to the fire.  
Add the  cinnamon  stick, the  lemon peel , the  Sugar  and the  salt .  
Peel the  Pears , core and cut in thin slices into the berry mixture.  
Let the  Pearscook  in the soup for a few minutes.  
Take out the  cinnamon  stick and the  lemon peel .  
Mix the  cornstarch  with the  white wineand  pour into soup to thicken it.  
Chill the soup in the refrigerator.  
Serve with a dollop  whipped cream  on top.
