Description : 
 Serve topped with orange  Cheddar  cheese and blue corn  tortilla chips  in honor of the San Diego Padres team colors!
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 ripe  California avocados , seeded, peeled and coarsely smashed (similar to the way the San Diego Padres will smash the Braves during the National League Championship Series) 
 3 tbsp fresh  lemon juice 
 2 medium  tomatoes , seeded and chopped (in the manner that Padres' fans mimic the Braves fans' Tomahawk chop) 
 ¼ oz can Ortega diced  green chiles  (because the Padres are on fire!) 
 ¼ cup minced  onion 
 3 tbsp chopped  cilantro 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1 tsp seasoned  salt 
 1 bag  tortilla chips 
 Directions : 
 In a medium bowl, combine avocados,  lemon juice ,  tomatoes , green chiles,  Onion ,  cilantro ,  garlic  and seasoned  salt  until well blended. 
 Serve with tortilla chips for dipping.
