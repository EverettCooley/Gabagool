Description : 
 Always check the ingredients to make sure the product is vegan. "Good for using leftovers"
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 teaspoon  vegetable oil 
 3 cloves  garlic 
 2 diced  onions 
 1 can  tomato 
 2 cubes  vegetable stock 
 1 teaspoon  basil 
 1 teaspoon)  oregano 
 1 pinch  salt 
 1 pinch  black pepper 
 Directions : 
 In a big pot, add ingredients. 
 Cover with  water . 
 Cook on low heat for about an hour.
