 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 3/4 cups  water 
 1 cup chopped red pepper 
 1/2 cup raw cashews, finely ground 
 1/3 cup  quick oats 
 1/2 cup  nutritional yeast  flakes 
 4 tbsp  arrowroot powder  or  cornstarch 
 3 tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 tbsp tahini 
 1 tbsp  Onion  powder 
 1 tsp  sea salt 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1/2 tsp  mustard  powder 
 1/4 tsp Tabasco or other hot sauce 
 1/4 tsp  nutmeg 
 1/4 tsp  paprika 
 Directions : 
 Process all ingredients until smooth. Heat gently to a boil, stirring constantly. 
 Reduce heat to low and cook until thickened and smooth.
