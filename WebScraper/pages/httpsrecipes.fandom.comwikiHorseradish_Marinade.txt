 Description : 
This white-hot, rich marinade starts with the real horseradish, herbs, and condiments and is great with meat, poultry, seafood. Sweet chili sauce rarely works for this, too.       Horseradish Marinade     
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup dry wine 
 2 tablespoons minced, fresh thyme 
 2 tablespoons low-sodium Worcestershire sauce 
 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar 
 2 tablespoons prepared horseradish 
 2 tablespoons tomato paste 
 1 ½ teaspoon freshly ground pepper, black only 
 4 cloves garlic, minced 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in a small bowl; stir well. 
 Spoon contents into a fresh container and reserve this in the refrigerator. Use on meat, poultry, or seafood. 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Servings:  3 tablespoons or 5 cups
