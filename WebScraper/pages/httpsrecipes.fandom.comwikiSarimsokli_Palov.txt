Description : 
 Pilaf with garlic
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 kg (2¼ lb)  rice 
 300 g  mutton  or  beef 
 garlic 
 800 g (28 oz)  carrots 
 3 – 4  onions 
 350 g  vegetable oil 
 salt  and spice to taste 
 Directions : 
 Wash  rice  and soak in warm saltwater for 1½ – 2 hours. 
 Cut meat in small pieces and sear in hot  oil . 
 Add sliced  onions , after a few minutes put in  carrot  strip and  salt . 
 Add 1:1  water , spices and unpeeled  garlic  cloves. 
 Stew on low heat for 20 – 25 minutes. 
 Add  rice  and cook uncovered until  water  evaporates. 
 When ready, remove  garlic , mix  rice  and serve in a mound on a large platter. 
 Peel  garlic  and arrange on top.
