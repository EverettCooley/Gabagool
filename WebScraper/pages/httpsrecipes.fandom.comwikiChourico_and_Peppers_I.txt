 Ingredients : 
 2Â lbs  chourico 
 1  green bell pepper  (sliced thin) 
 1  onion  (sliced thin) 
 2 cans  tomato sauce  (8 oz cans) 
 equal amount of  burgundy  (use inexpensive wine) 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 salt  and  pepper  ( hot pepper flakes  optional) 
 Directions : 
 In slow cooker skin chourico and crumble into small chunks. 
 Add all ingredients cook covered for 4 hrs. on high. 
 Remove cover, and let liquid reduce for 1 more hour. 
 Great plated with French fries or served as sandwich in rolls.
