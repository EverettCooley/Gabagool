 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 oz  white rum 
 1 oz  peach  schnapps 
 1/2 oz  dark rum 
 1 oz  orange juice 
 3/4 oz  lemon juice 
 1/2 tsp  caster sugar 
 1/3 ripe, peeled  peach 
 1 tsp Pecher MignonÂ®  peach  liqueur 
 Directions : 
 Mix in a blender until smooth. 
 Add a glassful of crushed  ice  and blend again briefly. 
 Pour into a collins glass and garnish with a wedge-like slice of  peach , speared with a  cherry . 
 Serve with straws.
