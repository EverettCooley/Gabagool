 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 Skim  milk 
 1 med  banana , sliced 
 1 tsp Unsweetened  cocoa 
 Directions : 
 1 Combine all ingredients in container of electric blender or food processor; process until frothy. 
2 Pour into individual glasses immediately. 
3 Food exchanges per serving: 1 skim  milk  exchange; cho: 2 mg; calories: 95; car: 17gm; pro: 6gm; fat: tr; sod: 85 mg;
