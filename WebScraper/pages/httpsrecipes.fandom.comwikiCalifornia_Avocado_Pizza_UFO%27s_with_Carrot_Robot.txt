 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3  English muffins , split and toasted 
 ½ Cup  tomato  sauce 
 ½ tsp dried  oregano , crushed 
 2  Chicken  or  Turkey  hot dogs, sliced 1/4-inch thick 
 2 Tbsp finely chopped green  bell pepper 
 ½ small  California avocado , peeled, seeded and thinly sliced crosswise 
 ¾ Cup shredded non-fat  Cheddar  or  mozzarella   Cheese 
 24 pre-trimmed, peeled mini  carrots 
 Directions : 
 Arrange  English muffin  halves, cut side up, on a baking sheet. 
 Preheat broiler. In a small bowl stir together  tomato  sauce and  oregano . 
 Spread mixture over muffins. 
 Sprinkle hot dog slices and  green pepper  over  tomato  sauce layer. 
 Arrange  avocado  half-circles over filling. 
 Sprinkle  Cheese  evenly over  avocado  and filling. 
 Place 4 inches under broiler, watching carefully, just until  Cheese  melts.
 Serve immediately along with the mini  carrot  "robots", allowing 4 per serving. 
 Preparation time, 15 minutes.
