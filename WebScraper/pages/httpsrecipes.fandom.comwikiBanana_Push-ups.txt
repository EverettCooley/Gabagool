 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 large  bananas , Cut Up 
 1/2 cup Instant Nonfat Dry  milk 
 1 cup Plain  yogurt 
 6 oz Frozen  orange juice 
 -Concentrate (1 Can) 
 1 cup  water 
 Directions : 
 1 Combine all of the ingredients in a blender and whirl until foamy. 
2 Pour into paper cups and freeze. 
3 Push up from the bottom of the cup.
