 In Romanian: Tort ieftin I
 
 Ingredients : 
 5  eggs 
 4 oz / 125 g  butter 
 Â¾ cup  confectioner's sugar 
 1 cup cold  milk 
 1 tablespoon  baking powder 
 vanilla 
 flour  as needed

 filling 
 Directions : 
 Cream the  sugar  with the  butter  and yolks, add  vanilla , cold  milk  and  baking powder , mixing everything well. 
 Add the whipped  egg  whites and then at the end, as much  flour  as necessary to make a thick but still pourable paste. 
 Pour in the baking pan that was previously buttered and dusted with  flour  and bake at medium heat. Test with a straw to see if it is done. 
 Then let cool, cut into two layers and fill with the desired filling, with preserves or marmalade.
