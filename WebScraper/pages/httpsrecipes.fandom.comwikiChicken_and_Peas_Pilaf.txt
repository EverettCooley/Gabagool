Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 large  onions , sliced 
 1Â½ cups uncooked  rice 
 3 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 3 cups  chicken broth 
 1 x 10-ounce package frozen  green peas 
 2 cups coarsely chopped cooked  chicken  or  turkey 
 salt , ground  black pepper  and a pinch of  saffron 
 Directions : 
 Cook  onions  and  rice  in  butter  until  onions  are transparent. 
 Stir in broth,  peas  and  chicken . Season with  salt ,  pepper  and  saffron . Bring to a boil. 
 Stir once or twice. Lower heat to simmer. Cover; cook 15 minutes or until  rice  is tender and liquid is absorbed. 
 Fluff lightly with fork.
