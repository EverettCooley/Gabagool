Description 
 Fruit and Vegetable Recipes I  by the South Carolina Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Ingredients 
 6  Red Delicious apples 
 1 pkg.  Craisinettes 
 1 stalks  celery , chopped 
 ½ cup chopped  pecans 
 1 small bottle raspberry pecan dressing 
 Directions 
 Cut apples in small chunks. 
 Add Craisinettes, celery, and pecans. 
 Toss with Raspberry dressing. 
 Chill. 
 Note: better second day.
