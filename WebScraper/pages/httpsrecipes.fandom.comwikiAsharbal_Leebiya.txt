 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 spoon of  butter  or  olive oil 
 ½ spoon  salt 
 Onion 
 ½ spoon bzaar 
 ½  Lamb  meat with some bones 
 ½ spoon  chilli pepper 
 2 spoon  tomato  paste 
 4 spoon sharbat lisanil osfoor 
 selantro 
 Dry  mint 
 Directions : 
 Put the  oil  in a frying pan under low heat and add the  Onion , meat and fry until it change colour. Add the  tomato  paste, spices and 3 cups of  water . Leave for cook for an hour. Add the  Pasta , selantro an  mint  an leave for 10 under low heat.
