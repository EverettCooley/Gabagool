 Description : 
This countrified, bold Canadian sauce is made with real barbecue sauce and maple syrup and is great for grilling or roasting. This is as easy as it can get when it usually comes to barbecue sauces. For a bold taste with the barbecue sauce, beer occasionally works for this, too.       Canadian Barbecue Sauce     
 Ingredients : 
 1 bottle barbecue sauce 
 ½ cup maple syrup 
 3 tablespoons chili powder 
 ¼ cup lemon juice  or  (7 tablespoons lemonade, cold only) 
 1 can or bottle beer, optional 
 Directions : 
 Add all ingredients together, especially including beer. Let it simmer on a low-heat setting and stir occasionally. 
 Pour or funnel contents from this homemade barbecue sauce into a fresh container. Reserve this in the refrigerator after cooling. Use on meat, seafood, or poultry. 
 Nutritional Information : 
 Servings:  4-7 tablespoons or 6 cups
