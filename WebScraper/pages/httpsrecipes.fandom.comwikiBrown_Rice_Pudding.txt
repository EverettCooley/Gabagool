brown rice  Pudding
 
 1 C. Cooked  brown rice 
 2  egg  Whites 
 2 C.  rice  or Nut  milk 
 1/2 Tsp.  vanilla extract 
 1/2 C.  Raisins 
 1/2 Tsp.  cinnamon 
 In Blender, Mix  milk ,  vanilla ,  egg  Whites, And  cinnamon . Spray 8 x 10 InchGlass Baking Dish With Non-Stick Spray. in a Separate Bowl, Mix TogetherBrown  rice ,  Raisins , And The Blender Mixture. Pour Entire Recipe Into TheBaking Dish; Sprinkle With Additional  cinnamon  And Bake at 350 Degrees For
 
 35 to 40 Minutes. Serve Warm or Cold.
