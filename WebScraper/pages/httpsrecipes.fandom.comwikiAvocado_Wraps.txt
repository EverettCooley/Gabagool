 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2  California avocados 
 1 can ranch style beans 
 1 Cup shredded Monterrey  jack cheese 
 6  flour  or  corn  tortillas 
 Directions : 
 Peel and slice  California avocados  lengthwise.
 Heat up ranch style beans on stove top.
 Heat up two tortillas at a time in damp paper towels in the Microwave. 20 to 30 seconds should heat them nicely.
 Place drained beans in tortilla. Top with  Cheese  and  avocado .
 Fold in half. While Heribertos can be wrapped like burritos and eaten by hand, we just fold them in half and use a fork!
