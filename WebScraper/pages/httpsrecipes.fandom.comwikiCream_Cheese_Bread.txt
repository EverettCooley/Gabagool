Description : 
 Bread machine recipe. This recipe is excellent for additions like  nuts ,  raisins , etc.
 
 Source: BMRC - December 19, 2003 
 Contributed by Tina, owner of  Creative Cutie recipes  through  World Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅓ cup  milk 
 1  egg 
 1 cup  cream cheese  (broken into small chunks) 
 ¼ cup  butter 
 3 Tbsp  sugar 
 1½ Tsp  salt 
 3 cups  bread flour 
 2½ Tsp  instant yeast 
 Directions : 
 Add ingredients to your bread machine in the order suggested by your instructions. 
 Select white bread setting, light crust if available.
