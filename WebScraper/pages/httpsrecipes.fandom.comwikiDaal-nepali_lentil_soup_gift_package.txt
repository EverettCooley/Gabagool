Ingredients : 
 1-3 decorative containers or Plastic bags 
 1 	cup 	 orange   Lentils 
 2 	tsp 	 curry  powder 
 1 	tsp 	 ground ginger 
 1 	tsp 	 garlic powder 
 1/2 	tsp 	 cumin 
 1 	cup 	Basmati or texmati  rice 
 Directions : 
 Place  Lentils  in decorative container. 
 In a small bowl, combine spices well and place them in a jar or small plastic bag. 
 Seal tightly. 
 Place the  rice  in another decorative jar or plastic bag. 
 Or, slide seasoning packet and  rice  packet into jar with  Lentils .
