Pork Hash
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lbs ground pork 
 1/4 cup water chestnuts, minced 
 1/4 cup chopped shiitake mushroom 
 2 tbsp green onion, minced 
 parsley, optional 
 1 TB soy sauce 
 1 TB oyster sauce 
 1TB chung choy, minced 
 1/2 tsp sugar 
 1 TB cornstarch 
 1/2 tsp salt 
 1/2 tsp pepper 
 1 egg 
 Combine all ingredients and mix well. Form into a large patty. Place
in a heat-proof bowl and steam for 25 minutes.
