chocolate   cherry  Creams
 
Put One Six Oz. Pkg. (1 Cup) Nestles Semi-Sweet  chocolate morsels  & 1/2 CupEvaporated  milk  Into a Two-Quart Sauce Pan. Stir Over Low Heat Until ChocolateMelts. Remove From Heat.
 
Stir in Until Well-Mixed:
 
 2-1/2 Cups  powdered sugar , 
 1/3 Cup Chopped Nuts And 
 1/3 Cut Well-Drained Chopped  maraschino cherries . 
 
Chill Until Mixture is Firm Enough to Handle (about One Hour).4. RollTeaspoon of Mixture in 1-1/4 Cup (1 Can) Cut up  coconut . Chill Until Firm (about 4Hours).
 
Keep Chilled. Makes About 30.
(you Can Also Freeze For Future Use.)
