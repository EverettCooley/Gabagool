 About Romeritos : 
 Wikipedia Article  About Romeritos on Wikipedia 
 Romeritos are a Mexican dish, consisting of patties of dried prawns and sprigs of a wild plant known as Romerito that resembles rosemary served in a mole sauce. They are traditionally served at Christmastime.
 
 
 My Grandmother used to make those patties with rice and dried shrimp, then combined them with the red mole and the romeritos.NHP 1/8/2011
