 Contributed by   Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Mrs. Walter H. Bush, Jr. 
 Serves 6 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pkg.  wide noodles  (12 oz) 
 1 cup  heavy cream 
 3 tbsp  butter 
 Â¾ cup grated  Parmesan cheese 
 dash  salt 
 pepper  to taste 
 chopped fresh  parsley  for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Cook noodles as package directs. 
 Meanwhile, heat cream and  butter  in top of double boiler until  butter  melts. 
 When noodles are cooked, drain and return to container in which noodles were cooked. 
 Add  cheese  and cream mixture. 
 Add dash of  salt  and  pepper  to taste and sprinkle with  parsley  and serve at once. 
 If it cannot be served at once, cover with foil. 
 Heat in 200 oven for 20 to 30 minutes before serving.
