Ingredients : 
 2 Strips  Bacon , Cut in Pieces 
 2 Cans (1 Lb. Each) Baked beans in  tomato  Sauce 
 1 Small  Onion , Peeled And Chopped 
 2 T. Prepared  mustard 
 1/4 Cup  Dark molasses 
 1 T.  brown sugar 
 1/4 to  allspice 
 2 T.  worcestershire sauce 
 Directions : 
 Brown  Bacon  Lightly. Drain And Crumble. 
 Mix All The Ingredients And Pour Into a 1 1/2 Quart Casserole or 2 Quart Bean Pot. 
 Cover And Bake at 350 For 1 1/2 Hours. 
 Uncover Last Half Hour to Brown. 
 Variations 
 
 pineapple  Baked beans...Add 1 Can Drained  pineapple  Chunks to Bean Mixture And Bake. 
 Cheese  Baked beans: Omit  Bacon  From Saucy Baked beans. Add 1/2 Cup Grated Sharp  Cheddar   Cheese  And 1 cup Cooked  Ham  or Salami Cut in Cubes to The Bean Mixture And Bake. 
 Serves 8
