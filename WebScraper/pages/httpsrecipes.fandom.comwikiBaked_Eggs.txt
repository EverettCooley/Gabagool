 Description : 
       Baked Eggs     
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 6 slices of  Bacon 
 6  eggs 
 4 slices of  bread 
 Directions : 
 Place slice of  Bacon  around edge of each of 6 pans. 
 Crumb  bread  and break  egg  in the nest. 
 Place more crumbs on top. 
 Dot with  butter  and  salt  and  pepper  to taste. 
 Bake 15 to 20 minutes at 350F
