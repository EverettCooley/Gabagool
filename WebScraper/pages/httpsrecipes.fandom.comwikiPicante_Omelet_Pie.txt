Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  picante sauce 
 1 cup shredded Monterey  Jack cheese 
 1 cup shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 4  eggs  (I use 6) 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 Directions : 
 Pour picante sauce into spray coated 10 in pie pan. 
 sprinkle with cheeses set aside. 
 In blender or food processor mix eggs and sour cream. 
 Pour over cheeses. 
 Bake at 375 °F for 20–25 minutes.
