Description : 
 Because this recipe uses  California avocados ...it's definitely nutritious!
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 genuine  California avocados 
 2  plum tomatoes 
 1 small  jalapeno pepper 
 small bunch of  cilantro 
 1  lemon 
 1 small  red onion 
 1 clove of  garlic 
 salt  and  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Chop: ½ clove of  garlic , ½  red onion , 2  plum tomatoes , ¾ cup  cilantro , ½  jalapeno pepper  and set these ingredients aside. 
 Then remove the skin and pits from the 2 avocados. 
 Cut them into small chunks with a knife and then mash these chunks up with a fork until smooth but chunky. 
 Combine the chopped ingredients with the  avocado  and mix together. 
 Squeeze the whole  lemon  into this mixture and add  salt  and  pepper  to taste. 
 Refrigerate for 45 minutes and then enjoy with your favorite  tortilla chips  or on top of your salad!
