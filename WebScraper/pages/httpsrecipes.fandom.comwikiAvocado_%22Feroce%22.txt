 Ingredients : 
 4  avocados 
 1 pound  cod  fish 
 6o z  manioc flour 
 3 tablespoons  oil 
 2 tablespoons  vinegar 
 1 clove of  garlic 
 1  onion 
 1  shallot 
 3  chives 
 hot pepper 
 green  lemon 
 Directions : 
 Put the cod into cold  water  for a few hours, wash it and grill it then peel it, take the skin and the bones off, mince it and sprinkle with  vinegar ,  oil ,  garlic ,  onion , minced  shallot  and  hot pepper , cut very thinly in small quantities according to your taste. 
 Let the cod macerate in that sauce. 
 Peel the avocados, cut them in slices and cover them with the manioc- flour . 
 Mash this mix with a fork and add the cod to it. 
 Serve after sprinkling with the  lemon juice .
