 Ingredients : 
 2 x 6-oz cans  tuna   or  1 x 14 oz can  salmon  - do not drain 
 1½ cups  flour 
 1 tbs  garlic powder  or minced  garlic  (optional) 
 2  eggs 
 grated  Parmesan cheese  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Process  Tuna ,  garlic  and  eggs  in food processor or blender (or mix in a bowl). 
 Add  flour  and mix to a brownie-like consistency. 
 Spread into a 9x9-inch greased pan. Bake at 350 °F for 20 minutes. 
 When the brownies are done, they will have a putty-like texture and the edges will pull away from the sides of the pan. 
 Cut into squares and freeze.
