Description : 
 Makes 8 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup sifted  flour 
 2 teaspoons  baking powder 
 2 tablespoons  Sugar 
 3/4 teaspoon  salt 
 2  egg  yolks, beaten 
 1 cup  milk 
 2 tablespoons melted  butter  or  margarine 
 1 cup cooked  rice 
 2  egg  whites, beaten stiff but not dry 
 
 Honey- maple syrup : 
 1/2 cup honey 
 1/2 cup  maple syrup 
 1/2 teaspoon  caraway seed  (optional) 
 1 teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Sift  flour ,  baking powder ,  Sugar  and  salt . Stir in combined  egg  yolks,  milk  and  butter . Beat just until smooth. Fold in  rice  and  egg  whites. Bake in hot waffle iron. Serve with Honey  maple syrup . 
 Honey  maple syrup : Combine honey,  maple syrup ,  caraway seed ,  cinnamon  and  butter ; heat to boiling
