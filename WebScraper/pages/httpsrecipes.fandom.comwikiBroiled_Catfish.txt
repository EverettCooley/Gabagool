Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes For Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Source:  The Diabetic Newsletter 
 Prep Time: 15 Minutes 
 Servings: 4 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tbsp  olive oil 
 1 tbsp  parsley , finely chopped 
 ½ cup  lemon juice 
 2 cloves  garlic , chopped 
 1 cup  white wine 
 ⅛ tsp  black  or  white pepper 
 1 lb boneless  catfish , cleaned 
 Directions : 
 Mix the oil, parsley, lemon juice, garlic, wine, and pepper in a non- metallic bowl. 
 Put the catfish into the marinade and refrigerate for at least two hours. 
 Preheat the broiler and brush the hot rack with the marinade. 
 Broil close to the heat for approximately 5 minutes until the fish flakes when touched with a fork. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 202 Calories | 7g Protein | 69mg Sodium | 7.6g Fat | 2.8g Carbs 
 Exchanges: 1 low-fat meat | ½ fat
