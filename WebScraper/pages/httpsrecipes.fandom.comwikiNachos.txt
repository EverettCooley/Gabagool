Nachos
 
 4 Oz. Tortilla Chips (about 4 Cups) 
 1/4 Pound Shredded Sharp  Cheddar   Cheese  (1 Cup) 
 1/4 Pound Shredded Monterey  jack cheese  (1 Cup) 
 1 Can Chopped Mild Green Chilies, Drained (4 Oz) 
 sour cream , Sliced  black olives , Chopped  green onion ,  cilantro  For Garnish
 
Spread Chips on Heatproof Pan or Platter; Sprinkle With Cheeses And Chilies. Broil 6 Inches From Heat Source Until The  Cheese  is Melted, 2 to 3 Minutes. Top With Garnishes And Serve Warm. Serves 8Source: Unknown
