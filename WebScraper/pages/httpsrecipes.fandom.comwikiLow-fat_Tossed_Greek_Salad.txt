Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Serves: 12 
 Ingredients : 
 Salad : 
 2 cups  romaine lettuce  — torn 
 2 medium  tomatoes  — cut in wedges 
 1 medium  cucumber  — peeled, halved and sliced 
 ½ medium  green pepper  — thinly sliced 
 ½ medium  red onion  — cut into rings 
 ½ cup  ripe olives  — sliced 
 ½ cup crumbled  feta  cheese 
 Lemon dressing : 
 ¼ cup  olive oil   or   canola oil 
 2 tablespoons  lemon juice 
 2 teaspoons  Dijon mustard 
 2 cloves  garlic  — minced 
 ½ teaspoon  sugar 
 ½ teaspoon dried  oregano 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 ¼ teaspoon dried  thyme 
 ⅛ teaspoon  pepper 
 Directions : 
 In a salad bowl, combine the first seven ingredients. 
 In a jar with a tight-fitting lid, combine the dressing ingredients;shake well. 
 Pour over salad and toss to coat. 
 Serve immediately.
