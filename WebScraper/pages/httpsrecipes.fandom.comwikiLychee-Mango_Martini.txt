Description : 
 From miningco.com/Chinese cuisine.
 
 Ingredients : 
 33 oz  vodka 
 12 oz can of  lychees , reserve juice 
 2 fresh  mangos , sliced 
 Directions : 
 Add  lychees , ½ can of  lychee  juice, and  mango  slices to  vodka . 
 Allow to sit for at least one week in a closed container. 
 Add fruit-infused  vodka  to iced shaker. 
 Add 1 drop blue  curaçao . 
 Shake vigorously. 
 Strain into chilled martini glasses. 
 Garnish with four to six fresh  pomegranate seeds .
