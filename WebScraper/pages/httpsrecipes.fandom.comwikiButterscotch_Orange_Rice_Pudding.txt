 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 3-1/2-ounce package butterscotch pudding and pie filling mix 
 2 cups  milk 
 2 tablespoons grated  orange peel 
 2 cups cooked  rice 
 1/2 cup  heavy cream , whipped 
 2  oranges , sliced 
 Directions : 
 Combine pudding mix,  milk  and  orange peel . 
 Cook and stir over medium heat until mixture comes to a full boil. 
 Add  rice . Remove from heat; spoon into dessert dishes. Chill. 
 At serving time, top with  whipped cream  and garnish with sliced  oranges .
