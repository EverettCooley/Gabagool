Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 Fondant : 
 ⅓ cup  Karo  syrup 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 1 lb pkg.  powdered sugar 
 1 – 3 tbsp Eagle brand  sweetened condensed milk 
 Chocolate Dip 
 Directions : 
 Mix and knead until like dough. 
 Take a small amount and cover a maraschino cherry. 
 Put in freezer for a few minutes. 
 Then dip in chocolate dip. 
 Let these ripen for 3 – 4 weeks in a cool place (not refrigerator. You may flavor this fondant with orange, cherry, mint, etc. and make flavored balls).
