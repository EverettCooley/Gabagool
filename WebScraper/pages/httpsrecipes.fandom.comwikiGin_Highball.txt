Ingredients : 
 2 oz  gin 
 carbonated water 
 1 twist  lemon peel 
 Directions : 
 Use a highball glass and pour into it the  gin  and some  ice  cubes. Fill the glass with  carbonated water  and add  lemon peel . Stir and serve.
