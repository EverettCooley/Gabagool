brown rice  Salad
 
 3 C. Cooked  brown rice 
 2 (10 Oz.) Cans  tomatoes  & Chilies, Drained 
 1 1/2 C. Fresh  Broccoli  Flowerets 
 1 1/2 C. Fresh  Cauliflower  Flowerets 
 1 C. Fresh  Mushrooms , Sliced 
 1/2 C. Chopped  green onions 
 3/4 C. Sliced Pitted  ripe olives 
 1/3 C.  olive oil 
 4 Tbsp.  red wine vinegar 
 1 Tsp.  Italian seasoning 
 1 Tsp.  garlic powder 
 Combine Cooked  brown rice , Drained  tomatoes  And Chilies,  Broccoli ,  Cauliflower ,  Mushrooms ,  green onions  And . in a Small Bowl, Combine Remaining Ingredients. Add to  rice  Mixture And Toss.
 
Enjoy.
