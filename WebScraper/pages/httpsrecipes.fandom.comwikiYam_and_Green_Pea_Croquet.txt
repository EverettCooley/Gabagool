 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs.  yam 
 1 cup shelled green pigeon  Peas 
 1 bunch fresh seasoning 
 2 tbsp  salt 
 1 tbsp  butter  or  margarine 
 ½ cup soft breadcrumbs 
 oil  (if frying) 
 water  for cooking 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Peel and cook  yam  and  Peas  in salted  water  until soft and  water  is absorbed. Crush  yam  and  Peas  together. 
 Saute seasoning in 1 tbsp. Of  butter  or  margarine . Add the seasoning and enough breadcrumbs to the  yam  and  Peas  to hold firmly. 
 Shape in large croquets. Fry, bake or grill until golden brown.
