Ingredients : 
 2 lbs of  sweet potatoes 
 4  cinnamon  sticks 
 1 tablespoon  cloves 
 1½ cup of  sugar 
 1½ cups of whole  milk 
 1 teaspoon of  salt 
 ½ cup of  butter 
 ½ finely chopped  coconut  flesh 
 1 teaspoon of grated  ginger root . 
 1 teaspoon of  cinnamon  powder 
 ½ teaspoon  nutmeg  powder 
 Directions : 
 Mix all the ingredients. 
 Pour the mixture into the baking pan and put in the oven for 30 minutes. 
 Pinch with a clean knife, if it doesn't stick to the knife, take out of the oven. 
 Let cool before taking out of the pan. 
 Serve as dessert or accompanying hot  cocoa  or  coffee .
