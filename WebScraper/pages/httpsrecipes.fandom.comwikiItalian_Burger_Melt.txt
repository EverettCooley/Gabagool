Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 2/11/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ lb  hamburger 
 1 can  tomato soup 
 ⅓ cup  water 
 1 tbsp  oregano 
 1 cup shredded  mozzarella 
 Directions : 
 Shape hamburger into patties. 
 Place in 2 qt. shallow baking dish. 
 Mix soup,  water  and  oregano . 
 Pour over patties. 
 Top with  cheese  and bake at 400°F for 20 minutes or until done. 
 Serve on rolls or over  pasta .
