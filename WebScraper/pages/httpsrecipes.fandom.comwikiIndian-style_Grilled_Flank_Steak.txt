Description : 
 Purchased from Bowers Estate in Fort Worth, Texas in 1994. Dated 1952.
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ tablespoons  garam masala 
 1½ teaspoons  coarse salt 
 1 teaspoon  black pepper 
 1½ pounds  flank steak 
 Directions : 
 In a small bowl stir together the  garam masala ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 In a shallow dish rub the mixture onto both sides of the  steak , and let the  steak  stand at room temperature for 30 minutes. 
 Grill on an oiled rack set 6 inches over glowing coals for 10 minutes. 
 Transfer the  steak  to a cutting board and let it stand for 10 minutes. 
 Cut  steak  across the grain into thin slices.
