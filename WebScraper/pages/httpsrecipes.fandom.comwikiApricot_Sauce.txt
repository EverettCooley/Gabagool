 Original recipe 
 Makes 1 cup (16 one-tablespoon servings). 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (12-ounce) can  apricot nectar  (1½ cups) 
 1 tablespoon  cornstarch 
 2 teaspoons  Dijon-style mustard 
 1 teaspoon finely shredded  lemon peel 
 1 tablespoon  lemon juice 
 2 packets Equal™  sweetener  or ¾ teaspoon Equal™ for Recipes 
 Directions : 
 In a saucepan combine apricot nectar, cornstarch, mustard, lemon peel and juice. 
 Cook and stir over medium heat till thickened and bubbly; cook 2 minutes more. 
 Remove from heat. 
 Stir in Equal™. 
 Serve over pancakes, waffles or  French toast .
