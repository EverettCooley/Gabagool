Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 3 oz Jim Beam®  Bourbon whiskey 
 3 oz 7-Up® soda 
 Directions : 
 Pour  whiskey  into a highball glass filled with ice cubes. 
 Add 7-Up, and serve.
