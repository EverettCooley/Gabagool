 Ingredients : 
 1 shot  Bénédictine 
 1 shot Stolichnaya Red  vodka 
 1 shot  pineapple juice 
 Directions : 
 Half fill glass with  ice cubes  or cracked  ice . Pour in 1 shot each of  Benedictine , Stolichnaya Red  vodka  and  pineapple juice . Gently stir with muddling spoon. Garnish with a slice of  pineapple  if desired.
