      Glorious Christmas punch     
 Ingredients : 
 water , boiled and cooled 
 1 x 3 oz pkg.  cherry gelatin 
 1 cup boiling  water 
 1 x 6 oz can frozen  lemonade 
 3 cups cold  water 
 1 quart  cranberry juice  cocktail chilled 
 1 x 12 oz bottle  ginger ale  chilled 
 Directions : 
 Dissolve  gelatin  in boiling  water ; stir in lemonade. 
 Add cold  water  and  cranberry juice . 
 Place two trays of  ice  cubes in a large punch bowl; pour punch over  ice . 
 Pour in ginger-ale.
