"Braggs All Purpose Seasoning" is made of soybeans and water only. There are no additives, preservatives, chemicals, coloring agents, or added sodium. Braggs is not fermented or heated and is easily digestible. In this secret process soybeans are converted into a liquid vegetable protein.
