Ingredients : 
 100 g  yam 
 oil 
 2 sliced  onions 
 125 ml  tomato paste 
 500 ml  water 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 hot chillies  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Clean and slice the  yams . 
 Place a drizzle of  oil  in a pot; heat it and sauté two sliced  onions . 
 Add 125 ml (½ cup)  tomato  paste and 500 ml (2 cups)  water ; mix. 
 Add the  yams ,  salt  and  pepper  and some hot chilies to taste. 
 Let simmer until the  yams  are cooked. 
 Add more  water  as necessary. 
 Serve on  rice  as an accompaniment to or meat.
