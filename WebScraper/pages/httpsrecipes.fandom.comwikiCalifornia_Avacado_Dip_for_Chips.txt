 Description : 
 A cool and refreshing dip perfect for a midnight snack or to share with friends at a party.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 ripe California avacado 
 12 oz  cream cheese 
 7 ¾ Cups  sour cream 
 2 tsp  lemon juice 
 ½ head of  lettuce  (shredded) 
 tortilla chips 
 2 Cups of shredded  Cheddar   Cheese  (optional) 
 2  tomatoes  (chopped)-optional 
 Directions : 
 Peel and remove the pit of the avacado. 
 Mix it with the  cream cheese ,  sour cream , and  lemon juice  in a large bowl.
 If serving for a party or get together, spread the mixture on a cookie sheet or large plate. Cover with shredded  lettuce , then  Cheese  and/or  tomatoes .
 If making it just to snack on, refrigerate the avacado mixture in the bowl and top with the  lettuce ,  Cheese , and  tomatoes  when you get ready to eat it.
 Serve both with tortilla chips.
