Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  butterscotch chips  (which is ½ of a 6 ounce package) 
 ¼ cup  butter  or  margarine 
 ¾ cup  flour 
 ⅓ cup  brown sugar 
 1 tsp  baking powder 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  vanilla 
 1  egg 
 1 cup miniature  marshmallows 
 1 cup  semi-sweet chocolate  chips 
 ¼ cup  nuts 
 Directions : 
 Melt butterscotch chips and butter or margarine in a 3 quart saucepan. 
 Stir constantly. 
 When melted remove from heat and cool to lukewarm. 
 Then add flour, brown sugar, baking powder, salt, vanilla and an egg. 
 Stir well until completely blended then add the marshmallows, chocolate chips and nuts. 
 After completely mixed, spread into a greased 9 inch square pan. 
 Bake at 350°F for 20 to 25 minutes.
