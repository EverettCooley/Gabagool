Description : 
 Lithuanian grated potato kugel
 
 Ingredients : 
 5 lbs  red potatoes 
 1 large  onion 
 8  eggs 
 2 tbsp  farina 
 1 can (large)  evaporated milk 
 1 lb  bacon 
 1 stick  butter , melted 
 salt  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Peel and grate  potatoes , into large bowl. (Do not drain). 
 Dice  Bacon  and  Onion , saute on low heat until  bacon  is done and  onion  is clear. 
 Add melted  butter . Mix. 
 Preheat oven to 400°F. 
 To grated  potatoes  add 4 unbeaten  eggs ,  bacon ,  onion  and  butter ,  evaporated milk ,  salt  and  pepper  to taste. 
 Mix well. Pour into lightly greased 13½ x 9½ inch pan. 
 Bake at 400°F for 15 minutes. 
 Lower temperature to 350°F and bake 1 hour or until browned. 
 Cut in squares. Serve with  sour cream .
