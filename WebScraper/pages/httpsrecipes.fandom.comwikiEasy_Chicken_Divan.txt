 Description : 
 Contributed by  PressureCookerRecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large  chicken 
 2 cups  brown rice 
 4 to 5 cups  water 
 ½ cup  wheat berries  with 1¼ cup water ( or  ½ cup  bulgur  with 1½ cup water  or  ½ cup  wild rice  with 1 cup water) 
 broccoli ,  carrots ,  string beans ,  snow peas  (or any other vegetables). 
 ½ cup  bread crumbs 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 ½ cup shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 Sauce : 
 1 cup  mayonnaise 
 1 tsp  curry powder 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice  (fresh is nicer) 
 2 cans  cream of chicken  soup 
 Directions : 
 In pressure cooker, cook under pressure 45 minutes, the chicken, brown rice, water, wheat berries (or bulgur or wild rice). 
 Steam broccoli, carrots, string beans, snow peas (or any other vegetables). 
 Combine bread crumbs, butter, shredded cheddar cheese and layer the cooked food in a large casserole dish like this: rice, vegetables, boned chicken, sauce, and cheese and crumb topping. 
 Bake at 350°F for 25 minutes or until topping is brown.
