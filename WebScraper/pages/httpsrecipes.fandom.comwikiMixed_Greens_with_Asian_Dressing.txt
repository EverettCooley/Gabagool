Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Cabrera Estate in Malakoff, Texas in 1986.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 3 tablespoons roasted  sesame oil 
 2 tablespoons  soy sauce 
 2 tablespoons  brown sugar 
 1 tablespoon  rice wine vinegar 
 1 teaspoon freshly grated  ginger 
 2 cloves  garlic  finely chopped 
 2  scallions  green and white parts finely chopped 
 fresh salad  greens 
 Directions : 
 Whisk together the sesame oil, soy sauce, brown sugar, vinegar, ginger and garlic. 
 Add chopped scallions and toss with greens.
