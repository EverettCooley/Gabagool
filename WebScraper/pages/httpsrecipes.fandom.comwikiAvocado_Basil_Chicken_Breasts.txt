Ingredients : 
 6 skinned and boned  chicken breast  halves 
 1  California avocado , peeled and cubed 
 2 (4 ounce) logs  goat cheese 
 ¾ tsp  salt , divided 
 ½ tsp  pepper , divided 
 1 cup fine, dry  breadcrumbs 
 basil , to taste 
 ½ cup stick Land o’Lakes  butter , melted 
 Avocado Sauce 
 Directions : 
 Place  chicken  between 2 sheets of heavy-duty plastic wrap and flatten to ¼ inch thickness using a meat mallet or rolling pin. 
 Combine  avocado , goat  cheese , ½ teaspoon  salt , and ¼ teaspoon  pepper ; mash until smooth. 
 Spread mixture evenly over 1 side of  chicken . 
 Roll up and secure with wooden picks. 
 Combine breadcrumbs, remaining ¼ teaspoon  salt ,  basil  and remaining ¼ teaspoon  pepper  in a shallow dish. 
 Roll  chicken  in melted  butter ; dredge in breadcrumb mixture. 
 Place in shallow pan. 
 Bake at 350°F for 30 minutes or until done. 
 Remove wooden picks, and serve with  Avocado Sauce .
