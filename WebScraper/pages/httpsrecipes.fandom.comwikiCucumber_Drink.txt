      Cucumber Drink     
 Ingredients : 
 4 – 6  cucumbers  (chopped) 
 2 oz root  ginger  (crushed) 56 g 
 6 cups  water  1½ l 
 juice of 1  lime  or to taste 
 1 cup  sugar  or to taste 
 Directions : 
 Wash hands. 
 Collect ingredients and equipment. 
 Wash  cucumber  cut small. 
 Put three cups of  water , ½ amount of  cucumber  and ½ amount  ginger  in blender (blend until liquidy). 
 Repeat process with remaining amounts of  cucumbers ,  ginger ,  water  (strain and sweeten to taste). 
 Serve cold.
