Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 pound lean ground  Beef 
 1/3 cup chopped  green pepper 
 1/2 cup chopped  Onion 
 1 cup chopped  celery 
 1 cup uncooked  rice 
 1 10-3/4-ounce can condensed vegetable  Beef  soup, prepared according to label directions 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1/4 teaspoon hot pepper sauce 
 1 3-ounce can  chow mein noodles  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Brown  Beef  in large skillet over medium heat. Add  green pepper ,  Onion ,  celery  and  rice . 
 Cook until  rice  is golden. Add soup,  salt ,  pepper  and  pepper  sauce. 
 Heat to boiling; stir well. Pour into 2-quart casserole. 
 Cover and bake at 350 degrees 25 minutes. 
 Sprinkle noodles over casserole before serving, if desired.
