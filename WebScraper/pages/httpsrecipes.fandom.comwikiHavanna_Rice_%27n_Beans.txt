Makes 4 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 pound smoked  Sausage  cut into 1/2-inch thick slices 
 1 medium  Onion , chopped 
 1 medium green  bell pepper , chopped 
 1 15- to 16-ounce can  black beans , drained and rinsed 
 1 14-1/2-ounce can roasted  garlic  diced  tomatoes * 
 1 5-ounce package  saffron   rice  mix, prepared according to package directions 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat until hot. 
 Add  Sausage ,  Onion  and  green pepper . Cook and stir 3 to 5 minutes or until  Sausage  is browned and vegetables are tender. 
 Add beans,  tomatoes  and  rice . 
 Cook and stir 2 to 3 minutes or until thoroughly heated.
