Description : 
 My Mom's recipe from when she went back to work. My Dad is a real meat and  potatoes  kind of guy, this was right up his alley. And no dishes to clean!
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 – 2 lb  ground chuck 
 2 – 3 medium  sweet potatoes   or   potatoes 
 1 can  green beans 
 1 can  corn 
 Directions : 
 Lay out a piece of foil, sprayed with cooking spray. 
 Place a hamburger patty on each piece of foil. 
 Add sliced  potatoes ,then  green beans , then  corn . 
 Top with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Wrap up each packet. 
 cook at 400 °F for one hour.
