Description : 
 A good combo for pork, poultry, fish and vegetables.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 From the Recipe Ross Newsletter 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  chicken broth 
 1 tsp shredded  lime peel 
 ⅓ cup  lime juice 
 2 tbsp  oil 
 1 tbsp  brown sugar 
 2 minced  garlic  cloves 
 ⅛ tsp  cayenne pepper 
 Directions : 
 Combine ingredients in a shallow dish.
