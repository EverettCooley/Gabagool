 Description : 
 Nutritive Value: 
 Calories per piece - 348 
 Protein per piece - 5 gms. 
 Other nutrients - Calcium, Phosphorous. 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2Â kg meat 
 1tsp  ginger   garlic  paste 
 1tsp green  chilli  paste 
 2 tablespoon  vinegar  (white) 
 2  onions  chopped 
 3  potatoes  boiled and finely chopped 
 200 gms. bread crumbs 
 4  eggs  for coating 
 4 slices of bread 
 2 tsp  garam masala  powder 
 2 green chillies chopped 
 3 tablespoons  coriander leaves  chopped 
 1/2 tsp  pepper  powder 
 salt  to taste 
 200 gms.  vermicelli 
 oil  to fry kababs 
 Directions : 
 Boil the meat with giner  garlic ,  chilli  paste and  salt . When done, shred the meat. 
 In a plate mix the meat along with the  potatoes , chopped  Onion ,  vinegar ,  garam masala  powder, chopped green chillies,  coriander leaves ,  salt ,  pepper  powder and 4 slices of bread (soak in  water  and squeeze out the  water  completely). 
 Form into medium sized round kababs. 
 Roll in breadcrumbs, dip in beaten  eggs  and again rollin broken  vermicelli . 
 Fry in hot  oil  to a golden brown colour. 
 Serve hot with  ketchup  or chutney.
