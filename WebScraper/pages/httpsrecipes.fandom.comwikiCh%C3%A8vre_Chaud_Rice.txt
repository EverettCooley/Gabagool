Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 ounces  goat cheese 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1 tablespoon  olive oil 
 3 cups warm cooked  rice 
 2 cups chopped  arugula  or  curly endive 
 Directions : 
 In medium ovenproof bowl, combine goat  cheese ,  garlic  and  olive oil ;. 
 Heat in 250°F degree oven until  cheese  is soft and warm, about 10 minutes. 
 Toss  cheese  mixture with warm  rice . 
 Add arugula; mix well. 
 Serve warm.
