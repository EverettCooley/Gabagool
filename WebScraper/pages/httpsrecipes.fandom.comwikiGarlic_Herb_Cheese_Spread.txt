Description : 
 I obtained this recipe from an estate sale when I purchased the family collection from the Monsey Estate in Crandall, Texas in 1991.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  farmers cheese 
 8 ounces  cream cheese  softened and cut up 
 2  garlic  cloves minced 
 3 tablespoons chopped fresh  rosemary 
 2 tablespoons fresh chopped  dill 
 1 tablespoon fresh chopped  chives 
 ¼ teaspoon  black pepper  freshly ground 
 1 teaspoon  Worcestershire sauce 
 ¼ teaspoon bottled  hot pepper sauce 
 Directions : 
 In blender container or food processor bowl place all ingredients. 
 Cover and process until smooth. 
 Turn mixture into a crock and refrigerate until needed.
