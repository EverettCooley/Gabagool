 Description : 
 Always check the ingredients to make sure the product is vegan.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/4 cup  olive oil 
 1/4 cup  water 
 1/2 cup  red wine vinegar 
 2 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp dried  basil 
 1/2 tsp  oregano 
 1/2  basil 
 1 lb  firm tofu 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl, mix together first 7 ingredients. 
 Marinate the tofu in the mixture for at least an hour.
