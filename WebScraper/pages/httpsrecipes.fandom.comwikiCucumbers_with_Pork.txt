Description : 
 In Romanian: Castraveti cu carne de porc
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb / 500 g  pork 
 8 medium sized  pickles 
 2 tablespoons  lard , 
 1 tablespoon  tomato sauce 
 1  onion 
 ½ teaspoon  flour 
 salt 
 chopped  dill 
 Directions : 
 Fry the  pork  pieces in  lard  until it starts to brown. 
 Add the chopped  onion  and  flour  and let fry a little longer. 
 Then add warm  water  to cover the  pork  pieces. 
 Cover and simmer for half an hour. 
 Prepare 2 inch / 5 – 6 cm long pieces of pickle. 
 To the meat add pickles,  tomato sauce ,  sugar , chopped dill and simmer until the meat is done and the liquid is reduced. 
 You can also use raw  onion  instead of fried. 
 In this case, add it with the warm  water .
