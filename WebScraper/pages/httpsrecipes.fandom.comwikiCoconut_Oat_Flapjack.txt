 Description : 
 Coconut Oat Flapjack  is a very simple to make savoury bake.
 This recipe uses the British English definition of a flapjack. You might know it as an oatcake, tray bake, or bar cookie.
 
 Ingredients : 
 140 grams (5 oz)  oats 
 60 grams (2 oz) melted  butter 
 1 beaten  egg 
 85 grams (3 oz) desiccated  coconut 
 1 grated  carrot 
 170 grams (6 oz) grated  cheese 
 Seasoning to taste ( salt ,  pepper ,  herbs ,  spices ) 
 Directions : 
 Thoroughly mix up all of the ingredients using your hands. 
 Press the mixture into a baking tray about 1 cm thick. 
 Bake at 175 °C / 350 °F for 30 minutes or until golden brown. 
 Best eaten hot, though you can store it in an airtight container and eat cold.
