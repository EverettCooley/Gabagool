Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup cooked  rice 
 4 ounces fully cooked  ham , cut into julienne strips (about 1 cup) 
 1 cup pared, seeded, and coarsely chopped  papaya 
 ½ cup (2 ounces) grated  Swiss cheese 
 ⅓ cup  half and half 
 1  egg , beaten 
 fresh  parsley  sprigs (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  ham ,  papaya ,  cheese , half and half and  egg . 
 Spoon into buttered 7x7-inch baking dish or 2 individual baking dishes. 
 Cover and bake at 350°F for 20 to 25 minutes, or until heated through. 
 Garnish with  parsley .
