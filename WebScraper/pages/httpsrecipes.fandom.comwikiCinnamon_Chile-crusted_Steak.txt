Description : 
 The taste of the southwest is so easy to make at home with an ancho and chipotle  chile pepper  spice rub. Just rub the spices over the meat and grill for instant flavor.
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes For Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 From: www.mccormickgourmet.com 
 Prep Time: 10 Minutes 
 Cook Time: 16 Minutes 
 Makes 8 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 tsp McCormick Gourmet Collection  ancho chile pepper 
 1½ tsp McCormick Gourmet Collection Mexican  oregano  leaves 
 1½ tsp McCormick Gourmet Collection Saigon  cinnamon 
 1 tsp McCormick Gourmet Collection  chipotle  chile pepper 
 1 tsp McCormick Gourmet Collection  parsley  flakes 
 ¾ tsp McCormick Gourmet Collection coarse grind  black pepper 
 ½ tsp McCormick Gourmet Collection coarse grind  cumin 
 1¼ tsp  salt 
 4 New York strip or rib-eye  steaks , cut 1-inch thick 
 Directions : 
 Mix spices and  salt  on plate. 
 Coat steaks evenly with spices. 
 Grill over medium-high heat 6 to 8 minutes per side or until desired doneness. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per Serving:
 
 255 Calories | 445 mg sodium | 11g fat | 1g carbs | 1g fiber | 83 mg cholesterol
