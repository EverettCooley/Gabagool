 Description : 
 In Romanian: Chec brun
 
 Ingredients : 
 Same quantities as for  Economical Cake , plus: 
 4 tablespoons  chocolate , 
 2 tablespoons  milk 
 Directions : 
 Follow the procedure for  Economical Cake . 
 Before adding the  flour , add 4 tablespoons  chocolate  melted in 2 tablespoons hot  milk .
