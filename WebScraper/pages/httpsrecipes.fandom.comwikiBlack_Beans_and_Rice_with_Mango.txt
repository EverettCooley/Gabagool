Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup  chorizo  sausage, crumbled  [1] 
 ½ cup  red bell peppers , chopped 
 ½ cup  onions , chopped 
 3  garlic  cloves, minced 
 1 (15 ounce) can  black beans , rinsed and drained 
 ¾ cup  water 
 1 teaspoon  oregano , crushed 
 1  bay leaf 
 salt , to taste 
 ¼ teaspoon  crushed red pepper  flakes (optional) 
 2 cups cooked  white rice 
 ½ cup fresh  mango , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Cook chorizo, pepper and onion in a large skillet over medium-high heat for 4 to 5 minutes, until the meat is browned. 
 Add the next 5 ingredients, and pepper flakes if desired. 
 Bring to boil.  Reduce heat, and simmer for 10 minutes. 
 Remove the bay leaf. 
 To serve, spoon bean mixture over rice in serving bowls, then top with fresh mango. 
 If desired, garnish with fresh oregano 
 Notes : 
 
 ↑   If chorizo is unavailable, use any other spicy  sausage .
