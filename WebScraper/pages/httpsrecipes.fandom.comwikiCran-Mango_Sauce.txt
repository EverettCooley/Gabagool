 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup chopped  mango 
 1 1/2 cups  cranberries 
 1/3 cup  Sugar 
 1 tbsp.  water 
 1 tbsp.  cornstarch 
 1 tsp. grated  lemon peel 
 Directions : 
 In 2 qt. glass measure, combine  cranberries ,  mangos  and  Sugar . Cover loosely with wax paper and cook in microwave on high for 8 minutes, stirring once. Combine  cornstarch  and  water  and stir into cran- mango  mixture. Add  lemon peel  and mix well. Microwave on high for 2 more minutes or until thick.
