 Ingredients : 
 Rind (yellow part only) from 2  lemons , peeled with a vegetable peeler and cut into 2 strips 
 1/2 pound  Green Olives 
 1 teaspoon crumbled dried  Rosemary  Leaves 
 1  garlic  clove, thinly sliced 
 1  Bay Leaf , broken into pieces 
 1/4 cup extra virgin  olive oil 
 Directions : 
 1. Peel 12 strips from  lemon  rind (yellow part only) with a vegetable peeler or paring knife. 
2. Mix rind strips,  rosemary ,  garlic ,  bay leaf  and  olive oil  in a plastic container with a lid. Marinate for as long as possible, preferably 1 hour, for flavors to blend. can be kept in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
