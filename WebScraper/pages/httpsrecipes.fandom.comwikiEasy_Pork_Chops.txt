Description : 
 Canned soup creates a delicious gravy for tender  pork  and  potatoes  in this simple one dish meal. Feel free to adjust the amount of  onion soup mix  in this easy crock pot  pork chop  recipe to suit your taste. It couldn't be more simple to assemble.
 
 Contributed by Tina at  World Recipes Y-Group 
 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 2 – 4 medium  potatoes , peeled and cubed 
 6 – 8 medium  carrots  (approximately 1 pound), coarsely chopped 
 1 large  onion , peeled and sliced 
 1 envelope  onion soup mix 
 2 cans (10¾ ounces) condensed  cream of mushroom  soup, undiluted 
 4 boneless  pork loin chops  (¾ of an inch thick) 
 Directions : 
 Spray your crock pot with a non-stick  cooking spray  for easy cleanup. 
 Place the  carrots  and  potatoes  in the crock pot. 
 Top with the  pork chops ,  onion ,  onion soup mix  and canned soup. 
 Cover and cook on low for 6 – 8 hours or until meat and vegetables are tender.
