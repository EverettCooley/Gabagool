 About : 
 This is a recipe for a thai, no-cook,  durian  drink. It is a milk based drink that is best served in the summer.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 medium sized  durian 
 1 8 oz glass of  condensed milk 
 Manis syrup (a sweet soy syrup) to taste (OPTIONAL) 
 Rose syrup  (OPTIONAL) 
 Directions : 
 Peel durian meat into a blender. Blend it for a short while. 
 Pour the durian puree into a glass of condensed milk - amount of condensed milk can change according to taste. Stir it up and chill it or add ice to drink. If preferred, add manis syrup or rose syrup to taste and for appearance.
