 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/3 cup sliced  green onions 
 1 1/2 cups sliced  celery 
 1/3 cup sweet pickle relish 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 6 hard-cooked  eggs , chopped 
 1 cup  Mayonnaise 
 salt  and ground  black pepper  to taste 
 lettuce  leaves 
 Directions : 
 Combine  onions ,  celery , relish,  rice ,  eggs ,  Mayonnaise ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Chill. 
 Fill custard cups or individual molds with salad mixture, pressing down firmly with spoon. 
 Unmold on  lettuce  leaves. 
 Garnish with  ripe olives  and  tomato  wedges, if desired.
