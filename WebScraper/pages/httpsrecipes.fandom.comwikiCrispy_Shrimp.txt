Description : 
 Crispy  Shrimp  with Moinette Remoulade Sauce is a delicious appetizer for any moment of the day. 
It's balanced with fine ingredients and healthy. The sauce fits perfectly with fresh baby shrimps, it'll make you melt in a positive way. Below are the instructions to create one portion, surprise your partner or just for yourselfÂ !
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 Baby  Shrimp  or  Crayfish  (cooked) 2 cups 
 Moinette or Witkap Pater 12 oz. 
 Apply  flour  as needed 
 Double action  baking powder  1 cup. 
 vegetable oil  as neede 
 salt  and  pepper  as needed 
 Remoulade Sauce 
 Mayonnaise  2 cups 
 capers  chopped 1/4 cup 
 Relish cup 
 onions  minced 1 or as fits 
 cayenne pepper  1/2 theespoon 
 tarragon  1 theespoon 
 parsley  1 theespoon 
 lemon  to taste 
 salt  to taste 
 
 
 Directions : 
 For batter, whisk  flour  into  beer  until thick. Then add  baking powder  and heat frying  oil  to 325 degrees. 
 Coat  Shrimp  with batter and fry until golden and drain on paper towel and season with  salt  and  pepper . 
 Combine remoulade ingredients and serve with  Shrimp .
