Description : 
 Prep 15 minutes | cook 20 minutes. 
 Makes approximately 2½ dozen. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  Miracle Whip  salad dressing, divided 
 3 tbsp  milk 
 ½ tsp ground  cumin 
 ½ tsp  onion powder 
 ¼ tsp  ground red pepper 
 ¼ tsp  garlic powder 
 4 boneless skinless  chicken breasts  (approx 1¼ lbs) cut into 1 inch chunks 
 1 cup crushed sesame  crackers 
 ½ cup  salsa 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 425°F. 
 Mix together ½ cup  salad dressing ,  milk  and seasonings until well blended. 
 Lightly dip  chicken  into  salad dressing  mixture; coat well with cracker crumbs. 
 Place on greased foil-lined cookie sheet. 
 Bake 18 to 20 minutes or until tender. 
 Mix together ½ cup  salad dressing  and salsa. 
 Serve with  chicken  nuggets.
