Description : 
 mango  Pachadi is usually prepared by taking the Raw Avakai  mango  which has been made into a pickle. The Pickle will last long. No doubt about that. But after some months, the  mango  can be taken out, and used for the pachadi.
 Called as Mangai Pachadi, it is a south Indian Dish, which can be done in 1 minutes.
 
 Ingredients : 
 Raw  mango  (Avakai  mango ) - 6 
 coconut  - 1 Spoon 
 salt  less in amount to taste. 
 Green  chilli  - 1
 
 Curd 
 Directions : 
 Grind the ingredients above and mix it with curd. Dress it up with  coriander seeds  if needed.
