 Description : 
 A wonderful blend of shallots,  lemons ,  peppers , and  basil  energize this guacamole for your game-day crowd.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 ripe genuine  California avocados , seeded, peeled and coarsely mashed 
 Â¼ Cup prepared salsa verde (green salsa) 
 1 tsp grated  lemon peel 
 1 Tbsp fresh  lemon juice 
 1 tsp coarse  garlic salt 
 3 Tbsp finely chopped red  bell pepper 
 3 Tbsp chopped fresh  basil  leaves 
 1 medium  shallot , finely chopped 
 Directions : 
 In medium bowl combine avocados, salsa,  lemon peel  and juice and  garlic salt . 
 Stir in  bell pepper ,  basil  and  shallot . 
 Serve with tortilla chips or cut vegetables.
