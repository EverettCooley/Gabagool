 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup vegan  margarine 
 1 cup unrefined,  brown sugar 
 Equivalent to two  eggs  in  egg  replacer 
 1/2 tsp  baking soda 
 2 1/4 cups unbleached white or  whole wheat flour 
 3/4 cup  cocoa powder 
 1/2 cup whole, raw  almonds 
 1/2 tsp  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Beat  margarine ,  Sugar ,  egg  replacer and  vanilla  together in a large bowl. Sift together the  baking soda ,  flour , and  cocoa powder , and gradually stir into the liquid mixture with a wooden spoon. Roll the dough in the palms of your hands to form smallish golf balls in size, and flatten them to about 1" thick. Press a whole  almond  into the centre of each cookie.
