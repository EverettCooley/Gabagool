Ingredients : 
 3 tbsp  butter 
 ⅔ cup  bread crumbs 
 ¾ cup  brown sugar 
 1 tsp  cinnamon 
 2 cups half-ripe  mango  slices 
 Directions : 
 Melt  butter  and add bread crumbs. 
 Layer bread crumbs and  mango  slices in a well greased baking dish, sprinkling the  mango  layers with  sugar  and  cinnamon  - ending with a layer of buttered crumbs. 
 Bake at 350°F for 1 hour.
