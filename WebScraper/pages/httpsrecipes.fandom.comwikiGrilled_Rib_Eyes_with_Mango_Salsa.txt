Ingredients : 
 4  rib eye steaks  (¾" thick) 
 ¼ cup  Worcestershire sauce 
 ¼ cup  soy sauce 
 ¼ cup  lime juice 
 2 tbsp  oil 
 2 cloves  garlic , crushed 
 1 tbsp  chili powder 
 1 tbsp  brown sugar 
 2 cups diced  tomatoes 
 1  mango , diced 
 1  green pepper , diced 
 6  green onions , diced 
 1  jalapeño pepper , seeded and diced 
 4 tbsp  lime juice 
 1 tbsp  oil 
 ½ cup chopped  cilantro 
 ¼ cup  pecan  pieces 
 Directions : 
 Mix Worcestershire, soy,  lime juice ,  oil ,  garlic ,  chili powder  and  brown sugar . 
 Pour over steaks and marinate for 15 to 30 minutes. 
 Combine  tomatoes ,  mango ,  green pepper ,  onions  and jalapeño. 
 Mix  lime juice ,  oil  and  cilantro . 
 Pour over salsa and stir. 
 Sprinkle with  pecans . 
 Remove steaks from marinade and grill for 8 to 10 minutes, turning once. 
 Serve with salsa.
