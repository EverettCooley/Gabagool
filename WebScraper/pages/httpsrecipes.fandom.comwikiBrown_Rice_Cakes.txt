Makes 8 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups hot cooked  brown rice 
 1 medium-size  green pepper , seeded and minced 
 1 medium-size red pepper, seeded and minced 
 1/2 cup  red onion , minced 
 1/4 cup cured country  Ham , minced 
 1  egg 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 1/3 cup  flour 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1/2 teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 vegetable oil  for frying 
 Directions : 
 Combine cooked  rice , green and  red peppers ,  Onion ,  Ham ,  egg  and  garlic . 
 Add  flour ,  salt  and  pepper , mixing until combined. Add more  flour , if necessary to bind ingredients. 
 Shape into 2-inch patties. Pan-fry in  oil  in large skillet over medium-high heat until crisp and lightly browned.
