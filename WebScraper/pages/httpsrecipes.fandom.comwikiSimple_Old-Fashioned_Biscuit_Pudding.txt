Ingredients : 
 10 biscuits 
 1 1/2 cup  milk 
 1/2 cup  sugar 
 2  eggs 
 2 tsp of  butter 
 1 tsp of  vanilla extract 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350 degrees. 
 Cook biscuits in advance or use leftovers. 
 Soak biscuits in milk until they have softened, 
 Mash the softened biscuits up in a bowl. 
 Add sugar, butter, vanilla, then eggs. 
 Stir well until it looks like crumbs; not too soupy. 
 Bake for at least 15 minutes or less - just until brown on top. Enjoy.
