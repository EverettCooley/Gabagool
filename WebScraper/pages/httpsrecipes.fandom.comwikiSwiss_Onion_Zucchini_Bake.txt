Ingredients : 
 1 lg. Spanish  onion  (3 c. Sliced) 
 3 c. thinly sliced  zucchini 
 1/4 c.  butter 
 2  eggs , beaten 
 1/4 c.  milk 
 1/2 Tsp.  salt 
 1/8 Tsp.  pepper 
 1/2 Tsp.  dry mustard 
 1-1/2 to 2 c. grated  swiss cheese 
 Directions : 
 Saute onion and zucchini in butter until tender. 
 Place in a shallow 1-1/2 quart baking dish 
 Combine eggs, milk, salt, pepper, mustard and half of the cheese. 
 Pour mixture over vegetables and sprinkle remainder of cheese on top. 
 Bake at 375 degrees F for 25 minutes or until done. 
 Notes : 
 Serves 6 
 
Enjoy.
