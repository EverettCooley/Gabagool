 Ingredients : 
 1  graham cracker  crust (9 inches round) 
 1 can  sweetened condensed milk  (14 ozs) 
 1 cup  lime juice  (fresh squeezed preferred) 
 1 cup  lemon juice  (fresh squeezed preferred) 
 1 cup  heavy whipping cream 
 green  food coloring  (optional) 
 lime  zest green part only (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Combine  cream ,  lemon juice , and  food coloring . 
 Whip the mixture until the  cream  begins to stiffen to a  whipped cream  consistancy. 
 Fold in the  lime juice  and  lime  zest. 
 Pour into pie crust and freeze for at least 4 hours or until pie is firm. 
 Cut into wedges and serve with  whipped cream  on top and plate with  lime  zest or  lime  slices.
