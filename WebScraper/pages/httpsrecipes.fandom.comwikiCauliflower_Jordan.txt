 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 Cauliflower 
 butter  to cook 
 Veal 
 minced  garlic  cloves 
 small can of  tomato  paste 
 water 
 Directions : 
 Cut  Cauliflower  into large flowerettes, cook briefly in  butter . 
 In the same pan brown slightly a piece of cut up  Veal . 
 add one or two finely minced  garlic  cloves, a small can of  tomato  paste and  water  to desired consistency. 
 Cook for about 15 minutes. Serve over  rice .
