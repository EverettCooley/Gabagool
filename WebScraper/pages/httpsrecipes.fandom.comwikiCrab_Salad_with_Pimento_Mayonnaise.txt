Desription : 
 A nice relatively low-fat,low-carb crab or lobster salad with a roasted pepper and chive dressing. by DiScharf

 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 20 min prep 
 4 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 ¼ cup non-fat plain  yogurt 
 3 tablespoons low-fat  mayonnaise 
 3 tablespoons roasted  pimentos , rinsed and patted dry 
 1  garlic  clove 
 2 tablespoons  chives , chopped fine 
 ⅛ teaspoon  salt  (to taste) 
 ⅛ teaspoon fresh ground  pepper  (to taste) 
 ⅛ teaspoon  cayenne pepper  (optional) 
 2⅔ cups cooked  crabmeat  or  lobster  meat 
 6 cups  mesclun 
 lemon  wedges (to garnish) 
 Directions : 
 Combine yogurt, mayonnaise, pimentos, garlic, chives, salt, black pepper, and cayenne pepper, in a blender. 
 Blend until smooth. 
 Scrape into bowl and fold in crab or lobster. 
 Adjust seasonings with salt and black pepper. 
 Divide mesclun into 4 servings on large plates and spoon salad on top. 
 Garnish with lemon wedges. 
 Enjoy!
