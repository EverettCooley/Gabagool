 Description : 
 A flaming hot alternative to buffalo wings is made with real honey and spices then cooked to perfection. A spice always rub works for this for mixing the chicken wings, too. Poultry lovers always know it as a red-hot treat for dinner or lunch.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 lbs.  chicken  wings, tips removed and split at the joint 
 1 cup barbecue sauce, store-bought only 
 ½ cup  honey 
 2 tablespoons  vinegar , white or cider only 
 2 tablespoons  cayenne pepper 
 1 teaspoon garlic powder 
 1 teaspoon salt 
 1 teaspoon pepper, black or red only 
 blue cheese dressing, optional 
 cheese sauce, optional 
 garlic flakes, optional 
 Directions : 
 Place  chicken  wings in a 12 x 16 pan, in one layer, and bake 350° degrees Fahrenheit in an oven for 40 minutes, basting now and again with the juices in the pan. 
 Combine the  honey  glaze with all ingredients except garlic powder, salt, and pepper, in a glass jar and stir to blend. Sprinkle with  salt ,  pepper , and  garlic powder . 
 Continue baking  chicken  and basting with glaze for 30 minutes or until  chicken  is tender and deeply colored. Serve warm and plain with your favorite blue  cheese  dressing or the cheese sauce of choice but top with the garlic flakes, if desired.
