Description 
 Japanese Cocktail  from Jerry Thomas' Bartender's Guide or How to Mix Drinks, out of copyright
 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 1 tablespoon  orgeat syrup 
 2 dashes  bitters 
 1 wine-glass of  brandy 
 1 or 2 pieces of  lemon peel 
 Directions 
 Fill the tumbler one-third with ice, stir well with a spoon, and strain into a cocktail glass.
