Twenty Seasonings Barbecue Sauce
 
 1 Large  Onion , Chopped Fine 
 3 cloves  garlic , Chopped Fine 
 1 Can (12 Oz)  tomato  Paste 
 1 Cup Mild Enchilada Sauce 
 1 Cup  apple butter 
 1 Cup  dark corn syrup 
 1 Cup  dark brown sugar 
 3/4 Cups  strong coffee 
 1/2 Cup Worcestershire 
 1/2  Beef  Bouillon Powder 
 1/4 Cup Prepared  mustard 
 2t. Bottled Gravy Coloring 
 2 T.  liquid smoke 
 1/2 T.  Tabasco 
 2 Packets (1 1/4 Oz. Each)  Beef  And  Mushroom  Soup Mix 
 1 T.  paprika 
 1/2 T.  allspice 
 1/2 T.  thyme 
 1/2 T.  oregano 
 1/2  bay leaf 
 
Mix All Ingredients in Crockpot. Cover And 9 Hours on Low. Stir Once or Twice. Will Keep Indefinitely in Refrigerator. Makes 7 Cups.Source: my Old Recipes
