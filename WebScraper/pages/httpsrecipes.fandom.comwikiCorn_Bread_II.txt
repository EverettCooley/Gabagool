Ingredients : 
 2 cups  cornmeal 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  baking soda 
 2 tsp  baking powder 
 1 tbsp  sugar   or   honey 
 1  egg 
 1 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 1 cup  buttermilk   or   sour milk  (1 cup  low-fat milk  + 1 tablespoon  vinegar ) 
 Directions : 
 Combine the  cornmeal ,  salt ,  baking soda  and  baking powder  in a bowl. 
 Mix very well. 
 Add the wet ingredients, mix again. 
 Pour into a lightly oiled 8-inch-square baking pan. 
 Bake in a 400°F oven for 20 to 25 minutes.
