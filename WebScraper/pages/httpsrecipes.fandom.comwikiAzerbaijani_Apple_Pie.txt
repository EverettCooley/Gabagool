 Ingredients : 
 5 – 6 medium size  apples 
 3  eggs 
 1 glass of  sugar 
 1 glass of  flour 
 Directions : 
 Peel the  apples  and cut them into pieces. 
 Take a pie pan, add some  flour  on the bottom to make it not sticky and put the  apples  in the bottom. 
 Take another bowl and mix  eggs  with  sugar  there. 
 Blend everything and then add  flour . 
 Pour this mixture on the  apples  and put the tin in the oven at 250 °F and cook for approximately 20 minutes. 
 When ready add  chocolate  or  sugar  powder on the top.
