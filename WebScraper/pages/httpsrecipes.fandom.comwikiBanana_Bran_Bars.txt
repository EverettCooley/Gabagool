Description 
 Bran Flakes: Fortified Ready to Eat Cereal  by the US Department of Agriculture, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
 
 Serves : 12 
 Ingredients 
 1 cup  flour 
 ¼ cup packed  brown sugar 
 ½ teaspoon  baking soda 
 1 teaspoon  baking powder 
 ¼ teaspoon  salt 
 1½ teaspoons  cinnamon 
 1 cup crushed  bran flakes 
 1 cup lowfat  milk 
 2  eggs 
 1  carrot , peeled and shredded 
 1 ripe  banana , peeled and mashed 
 ⅓ cup chopped  walnuts  (optional) 
 Directions 
 Preheat oven to 375 °F. 
 Oil an 8” x 8” pan. 
 In a medium bowl, combine flour, brown sugar, baking soda, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, and bran flakes. 
 In a separate small bowl combine the milk, eggs, carrot, banana and walnuts. 
 Add milk mixture to cereal mixture, mixing well. 
 Pour into prepared pan. 
 Bake for 25–35 minutes until lightly browned. 
 Cut 3 x 4 to make twelve bars.
