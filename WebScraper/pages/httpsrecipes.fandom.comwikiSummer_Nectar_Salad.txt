 Description : 
 Simple Salad Recipe Contest Third Place Winner
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 10 oz package Ready Pac Baby  spinach 
 1 Ripe  California avocado , diced 
 1 Ripe  nectarine , diced 
 ¼ Cup Vidalia  Onion , shaved thin 
 ¼ Cup cooked, crumbled  Bacon  (optional, but worth every calorie!) 
 Vinaigrette: 
 1 pinch fresh minced  garlic 
 3 Tbsp aged  balsamic vinegar 
 1 Tbsp Dijon-style  mustard 
 2 Tbsp extra virgin  olive oil 
 1 Tbsp  canola oil 
 Directions : 
 Place vinaigrette ingredients together in a small jar. Shake vigorously to emulsify.
 Just before serving, pour vinaigrette over salad and toss gently. Garnish with fresh edible nasturtiums, if available. Enjoy!
