Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 This recipe yields about 1 cup of vinaigrette. 
 Ingredients : 
 3 tablespoons  sugar 
 2 teaspoons  cornstarch 
 dash  salt 
 ½ cup unsweetened  pineapple juice 
 ½ cup  cherries 
 1 well-beaten  egg 
 1 x 3-ounce package  cream cheese 
 ½ cup  heavy cream , whipped 
 Directions : 
 Combine  sugar ,  cornstarch , and  salt . 
 Blend in  pineapple juice  and  cherries . 
 Bring to boiling, stirring constantly. 
 Cook and stir until sauce thickens. 
 Stir small amount into  egg ; return to hot mixture. 
 Cook about 2 minutes stirring constantly. 
 Stir  cream cheese  to soften; gradually beat in cooked mixture. 
 Carefully fold in whipped cream for marbled effect. 
 Chill.
