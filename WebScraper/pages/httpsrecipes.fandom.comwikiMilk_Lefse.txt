      Milk Lefse     
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups  milk , scalded 
 4 teaspoons  salt 
 1Â½ tablespoons  lard 
 4 cups  flour 
 1 tablespoon  sugar 
 Directions : 
 Combine  flour ,  sugar  and  salt . 
 Put  milk  and  lard  in pan heat. 
 Pour all  milk  over the  flour  mixture. 
 Keep stirring. 
 Let cool. 
 Pinch off  walnut  size portions and roll. 
 Bake on large griddle. 
 Flip and bake the other side. 
 Lay it on top of waxed paper and keep stacking them up. 
 Store in airtight container. 
 butter  and sprinkles lightly with  sugar , roll up and eat.
