 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 lbs Cooked  Ham , Chopped 
 1 1/2 cup Ketchup 
 2 tbsp  cider vinegar 
 1 tbsp  brown sugar , Packed 
 1 tbsp Prepared  mustard 
 1 tbsp  worcestershire sauce 
 8 Frankfurter Rolls 
 Directions : 
 1 Combine all of the ingredients, except the frankfurter rolls, in a 2-quart saucepan. 
2 Cook over medium heat until the mixture comes to a boil, then remove from the heat and cover; refrigerate until serving time. 
3 To grill: place each frankfurter roll on a 10-inch length of heavy-duty foil. 
4 Fill each roll with about 1/3 cup of the  Ham  mixture. 
5 Securely wrap each roll into a loose packet. 
6 Grill 4-inches from grey coals (medium heat). 
7 Cook for 20 minutes, turning often with tongs.
