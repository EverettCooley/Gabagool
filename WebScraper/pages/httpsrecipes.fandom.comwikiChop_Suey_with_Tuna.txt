Description : 
 Source:  Recipes for Diabetics  by Billie Little with Penny L. Thorup
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 stalks  celery , cut small 
 ½  onion , chopped fine 
 ½ tsp  pepper 
 2 tbsp  soy sauce 
 2 cup + 2 tbsp  water 
 ¾ cup  rice 
 ¾ cup  tuna 
 Directions : 
 Cook  celery  and  onion  in 2 tbsp  water  over low heat, covered. 
 When tender, remove from heat, add  rice , 2 cups  water , and seasonings. 
 Bring to a boil. 
 Turn heat to low and cook covered, until  rice  is tender and liquid absorbed (about ½ hour). 
 Add  tuna . 
 Mix and remove from heat. 
 Set aside, covered, for a few minutes before serving. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Food exchange per serving: 1 starch/bread exchange + 1 meat exchange
