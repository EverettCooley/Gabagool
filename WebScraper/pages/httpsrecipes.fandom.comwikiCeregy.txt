Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Gingham Estate in Fort Worth, Texas in 1992.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  flour 
 2 tablespoons  sugar 
 Â½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 tablespoon  butter 
 5  egg yolks 
 2 tablespoons  sour cream 
 powdered sugar 
 Directions : 
 Sift  flour ,  sugar  and  salt  in bowl. 
 Work  butter  into  flour . 
 Beat egg yolks well and pour into mixture. 
 Add  sour cream  and wine and mix until well blended. 
 Turn out on floured board and knead until smooth. 
 Divide into 3 pieces and roll very thin. 
 Cut into about 5-inch diamond shaped pieces then fry in very hot  oil  for 2 minutes. 
 When cool dust with  powdered sugar .
