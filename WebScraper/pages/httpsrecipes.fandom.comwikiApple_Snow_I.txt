 Description : 
 Try this sweet Irish classic dessert. It's the perfect dessert to finish off a satisfying meal.
Makes 6.
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 lbs  Apples , peeled and cored 
 3 tablespoons  water 
 1 teaspoon  lemon juice 
 1/2 cup  Sugar 
 3  egg  whites 
 Toppings (optional):
 
 whipped cream 
 brown sugar  or crumbled  vanilla  wafer cookies 
 Directions : 
 Slice  Apples  and place in saucepan. 
 Add  water  and  lemon juice . 
 Cover and cook on low heat for 15 minutes or until  Apples  are soft. 
 Transfer  Apples  in a blender, add  Sugar  and mix into a puree. 
 Set aside to cool. Whisk  egg  whites until stiff and fold into the  Apple  mixture. 
 Divide and place into serving bowls and chill in the fridge. 
 Top with  whipped cream ,  brown sugar  and crumbled  vanilla  wafer cookies just before serving.
