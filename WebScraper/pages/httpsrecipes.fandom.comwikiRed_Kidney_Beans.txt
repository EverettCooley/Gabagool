Description : 
 Servings : 6
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  red kidney bean 
 3 qt  water 
 1 tsp  turmeric 
 1 tbsp  salt 
 ¼ cup  oil 
 1 cup chopped  onion 
 1 inch  ginger  chopped 
 1 tsp  garam masala 
 3  tomatoes  chopped 
 coriander leaves  for garnish 
 Directions : 
 Wash beans and boil for 2 – 3 hours or ½ hour in a pressure cooker. 
 In the meantime make  masala  of  onions ,  garlic ,  ginger  and  tomato  as in  Chicken Curry . 
 Add to the beans and cook again till most of the liquid dries up and the beans are soft and thoroughly cooked. 
 Garnish with  coriander leaves  and serve.
