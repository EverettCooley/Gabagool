Description : 
 Yield: 4 servings 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ lbs fresh  mushrooms , sliced 
 ½ cup chopped  onion 
 2 tsp  margarine 
 1 lbs  ground turkey 
 1  egg 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  Italian herb blend 
 ½ cup dry  red wine 
 ¼ tsp cracked  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Saute half the  mushrooms  and the chopped  onion  in  margarine  in a large frying pan. 
 Combine sauteed vegetables with  turkey ,  egg ,  salt , and herbs. 
 Shape  turkey  mixture into 4 patties. 
 Broil 3 – 5 minutes on each side until cooked through. 
 While patties are cooking, add remaining  mushrooms , wine, and  pepper  to frying pan. 
 Simmer gently and serve sauce over patties. 
 Nutritional information : 
 For 1 patty with sauce: 
 
 3g Carbohydrates | 23g Protein | 13g Fat | 0.5g Fiber | 359mg Sodium | 89mg Cholesterol 
 Food exchanges: 3½ lean meat 
 Low-sodium diets: omit  salt . Use unsalted  margarine . 
 Note: if you have a high cholesterol count, substitute eggbeaters for the  egg
