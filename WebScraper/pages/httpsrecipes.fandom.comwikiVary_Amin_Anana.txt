 Description : 
 Vary Amin Anana  the rice with vegtables dish
 so wrong...
 
 Directions : 
 Brown the meat in a cooking pot with a little  oil , then add the  onions ,  garlic , and  ginger . 
 Add the clean and thinly cut green leaves,  tomatoes , 1 cup  water ,  salt  and  pepper  to taste. Bring to a boil for approximately 10 to 15 minutes. 
 Add the  rice  and additional  water . Cover the pot and continue cooking for approximately 15 to 20 minutes.
