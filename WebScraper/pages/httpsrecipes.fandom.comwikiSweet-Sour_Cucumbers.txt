 Description : 
 this is a haitian salad
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 c.  white vinegar 
 1/4 tsp.  white pepper 
 1/3 c.  salad oil 
 1/4 tsp.  oregano  leaves 
 2 Tbs.  Sugar 
 3 med.  cucumbers , thinly sliced 
 3/4 tsp.  salt 
 Directions : 
 In large bowl, combine all ingredients. 
 Cover and refrigerate at least 45 minutes to blend flavors, stirring occasionally.
