Description : 
 This frozen torte is often my choice to make & take to potluck suppers. It's fast, easy & delicious, says Betty Reuter
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source: Taste of Home Test Kitchen Favorites 
 10 – 12 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1¼ cup  chocolate wafer  crumbs, about 24 wafers, divided 
 ¼ cup  sugar 
 ¼ cup  butter , melted 
 1 (8 oz) pkg  cream cheese , softened 
 1 (14 oz) can  sweetened condensed milk 
 ⅔ cup  chocolate syrup 
 2 tbsp instant  coffee  granules 
 1 tbsp hot water 
 1 cup  heavy whipping cream , whipped 
 chocolate-covered coffee beans, optional 
 Directions : 
 In bowl, combine 1 cup wafer crumbs, sugar and butter. 
 Press onto bottom and 1" up sides of greased 9" spring-form pan; set aside. 
 In mixing bowl, beat cream cheese, milk and syrup until smooth. 
 Dissolve coffee granules in hot water; add to cream cheese. 
 Fold in whipped cream. 
 Pour over crust. 
 Sprinkle with remaining crumbs. 
 Cover; freeze 8 hours or overnight. 
 Remove from freezer 15 minutes before serving. 
 Carefully run knife around edge of pan to loosen. 
 Remove side of pan. 
 Garnish with coffee beans if desired. 
 Tip : 
 Sometimes Betty substitutes cream-filled chocolate sandwich cookies for chocolate wafer crumbs. If you do this, omit sugar in crust.
