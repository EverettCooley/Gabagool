cream cheese   chili 
 
 8 Oz.  cream cheese 
 1 Can  chili  With no beans 
 1 Small Can Green Chilies, Chopped 
 1 8 Oz. Pkg. Monteray  jack cheese  (can Use Any Mexican  Cheese  or Blend That You Like) 
 
Preheat Oven to 350.
 
Layer in Order Listed And Heat For 20 minutes.
