Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz  Tennessee whiskey 
 1½ tsp  lemon juice 
 1½ tsp  simple syrup 
 several drops  cointreau 
 club soda 
 Directions : 
 Shake all ingredients except soda with  ice . 
 Strain into highball glass; add  ice  and fill with soda. 
 Decorate with fruit slices.
