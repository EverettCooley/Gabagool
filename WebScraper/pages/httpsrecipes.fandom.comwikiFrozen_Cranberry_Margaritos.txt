Description : 
 A mojito traditionally uses rum, here we use tequila instead, hence the fun title. Dip rims of glasses in lime juice and a mixture of equal parts kosher salt and sugar for a twist on salt-rimmed glasses. 
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Source:  Southern Living  2007 Holiday Entertaining 
 Prep: 5 minutes 
 Makes 5 cups 
 Ingredients : 
 1 (10-oz) can frozen mojito mix 
 ¾ cup  tequila 
 ¼ cup whole berry  cranberry juice 
 2 tbsp  orange liqueur  ( triple sec  or  Grand Marnier ) 
 2 tbsp fresh  lime juice 
 Directions : 
 Combine first 5 ingredients in a blender. 
 Fill blender with ice to 5 cup level and process until smooth. 
 Serve immediately.
