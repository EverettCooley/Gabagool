Ingredients : 
 4 small  green mangoes 
 1Â¼ cups  coconut cream 
 2 tablespoons  lemon juice  or  vinegar 
 1 small  onion , chopped 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Wash, peel and grate the mangoes. 
 Mix the  coconut cream  and  lemon juice  or  vinegar  in a bowl. 
 Add the grated  mango  and  onion  and mix well. 
 Add  salt  and  pepper  to taste. 
 Leave to stand for at least 10 minutes before serving.
