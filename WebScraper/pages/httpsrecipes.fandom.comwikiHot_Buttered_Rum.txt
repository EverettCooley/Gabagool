 Description 
 A hot buttered rum is a warm alcoholic beverage made with light rum, butter, sugar, and cloves mixed with hot water.
 
 Hot Buttered Rum  from the Wikibooks Bartending Guide—original source of recipe, licensed under the GNU Free Documentation License 
 Serves : 1 
 Ingredients 
 1 oz.  light rum 
 1 tsp.  sugar 
 ½ tsp.  butter 
 4  cloves 
 Directions 
 Mix sugar, butter, cloves and rum together in mug and stir. 
 Fill mug with hot water and stir again.
