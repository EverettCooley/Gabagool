Purchased from Patterson Estate in Tyler, Texas in 1992. Notation on card indicates this is Regina’s favorite meatloaf. Dated 1922. I have made this several times using different variations of  Cheese . My favorite is  asiago .
 
 Contributed by  Cat's Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds  ground beef 
 1 cup of your favorite  cheese 
 2  eggs  beaten 
 1 small  white onion  chopped 
 1 small  green bell pepper  chopped 
 1 stalk  celery  chopped 
 ¼ cup plain  bread crumbs 
 1 tablespoon  mustard 
 3 tablespoons  ketchup 
 Directions : 
 Mix  ground beef ,  onion ,  bell pepper ,  celery , bread crumbs,  mustard  and  ketchup  together well. 
 Press half of the mixture into bottom of iron skillet. 
 Add  cheese , leaving about on-half inch from edge. 
 Add remaining meat mixture. 
 Seal edges and bake at 350°F for 45 minutes.
