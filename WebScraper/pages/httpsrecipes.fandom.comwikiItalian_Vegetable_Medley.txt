Makes 4 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 1 medium  Onion , chopped 
 1 16-ounce package frozen Italian style vegetables, thawed 
 1 16-ounce package frozen lima beans, thawed 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 1/2 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 1/2 teaspoon  oregano 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 2 medium  tomatoes , chopped 
 1/3 cup  Parmesan cheese 
 1 tablespoon chopped fresh  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in large skillet over medium-high heat until hot. 
 Add  Onion , vegetables and lima beans, Cook and stir about 5 minutes or until tender. 
 Stir in  salt ,  garlic powder  and  oregano . Cook and stir 5 to 7 minutes or until tender. 
 Stir in  rice  and  tomatoes . 
 Sprinkle with  Parmesan cheese ; garnish with  parsley .
