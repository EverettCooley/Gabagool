Description : 
 Makes 10 one half cup servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup Better'n Eggs - ( egg substitute ) 
 4 cups  skim milk 
 1 pack  instant vanilla pudding  - (0.9 oz envelope)- sugar-free 
 ⅛ tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  vanilla extract 
 ¼ tsp ground  nutmeg 
 Directions : 
 Combine Better'n Eggs and  milk  in mixing bowl or blender. 
 Add remaining ingredients. Beat for 2 minutes or until well blended. 
 Refrigerate until serving. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving:
 
 50 calories | 0 fat | 2 mg cholesterol | 103 mg sodium
