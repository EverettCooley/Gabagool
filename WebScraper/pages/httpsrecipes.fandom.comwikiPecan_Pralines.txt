Ingredients : 
 3 cups of  granulated sugar 
 1 cup of  sugar  cooked to the caramel degree 
 1 cup of  cream 
 3 cups of  pecan  nut meats 
 Directions : 
 First stir the  sugar  and  cream  over the fire until the  sugar  is melted, then let boil to the soft ball degree or to 235Â°F. 
 Have ready the cup of  sugar , cooked to the caramel degree, and pour the first mixture onto the caramel; let boil up once, then remove from the fire and beat until it begins to thicken. 
 Add the  nuts  and drop by spoonfuls onto marble or waxed paper.
