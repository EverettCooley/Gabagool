Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 ½ pound fully cooked smoked  sausage , cut in half lengthwise, sliced into ½-inch pieces 
 1 medium  onion , chopped 
 1 x 14½-ounce can diced  tomatoes , undrained 
 2 x 5-ounce packages Cajun seasoned  rice  and  bean  mix, prepared according to package directions 
 6 x 8 to 10-inch flour  tortillas 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in large skillet over medium-high heat. 
 Add  sausage  and  onion . 
 Cook and stir 5 to 7 minutes, or until  sausage  is brown and  onion  is tender. 
 Add  tomatoes , stir until blended. 
 Stir in cooked  rice ; heat about 2 to 3 minutes or until heated throughout. 
 Spoon  rice  mixture evenly into 6 tortillas. 
 Wrap up and serve.
