 Description : 
 Celery  sticks with  Peanut Butter  and Raisins. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 Medium Celery Sticks 
 12 half-knife full of Peanut Butter, 
 2-4 raisins per celery stick 
 Recipe : 
 
 
 #
 
 Step
 
 
 1
 
 Cut off leafy end and the other end to make the celery even
 
 
 2
 
 Wash celery sticks
 
 
 3
 
 Cut celery into 3 2.5 inch cuts
 
 
 4
 
 Use butter knife to apply Peanut Butter.
 
 
 5
 
 Place Raisins into the peanut butter.
 
 
 6
 
 Place all finished Celery onto a plate or 2.
 
 
 7
 
 Enjoy!
