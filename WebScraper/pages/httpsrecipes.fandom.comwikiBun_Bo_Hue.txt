Description : 
 Bun  Bo Hue is a soup with noodles, also known as Vietnamese Hue Noodle Soup. 
 
       Bun Bo Hue     
 Ingredients : 
 ham hocks 
 stalks  lemongrass 
 nuoc nam 
 sugar 
 sea salt 
 black pepper 
 sirloin 
 pork loin 
 noodles 
 bean sprouts 
 Thai  holy basil 
 sprigs fresh  mint 
 sprigs fresh  cilantro 
 fresh  Thai red chili peppers 
 lime 
 Directions : 
 Boil the  water , add  ham  hocks and  lemongrass , strain the broth. 
 Add nuoc mam,  sugar ,  salt  and  pepper , sirloin and  pork . 
 Loin to the broth and prepare the meats.
