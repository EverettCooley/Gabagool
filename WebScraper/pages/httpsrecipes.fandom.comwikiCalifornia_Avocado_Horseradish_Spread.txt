 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1  California avocado  (8 ounces) 
 2 Tbsp  Onion , finely chopped 
 2 Tbsp  lemon juice 
 1 Tbsp prepared horseradish (or to taste, depending on the strength of the horseradish) 
 1/8 tsp  salt 
 Directions : 
 Roughly mash  avocado . 
 Stir in remaining ingredients. 
 Chill 1 hour to marry flavors. 
 Serve on roast  Beef  and corned  Beef  sandwiches and alone on a  bagel  or with smoked salmon.
