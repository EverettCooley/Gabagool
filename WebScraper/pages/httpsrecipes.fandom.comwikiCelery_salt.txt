 About celery salt : 
 Wikipedia Article  About Celery salt on Wikipedia 
 Celery seed  is used as a spice. Whenever combined with  salt , then the resulting spice blend is called celery salt. Celery salt is used as an alternate to ordinary salt seasoning in various recipes and cocktails. It is notably used to enhance the flavor of  Bloody Mary  and the Chicago-style hot dog.
