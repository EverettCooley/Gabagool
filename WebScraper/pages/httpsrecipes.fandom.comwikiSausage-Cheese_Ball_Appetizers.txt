Sausage - Cheese  Ball Appetizers
 
 3 c Bisquick Mix 
 1 Pound Bulk  Sausage 
 4 c Shredded  Cheddar   Cheese 
 1/2 c Grated  Parmesan cheese 
 1/2 Teaspoon Dried  rosemary  Leaves 
 1/2 Teaspoon  parsley  flakes 
 
Heat Oven to 350, Grease Jelly Roll Pan 15 1/2x10 1/2x1 Inch.Stir Together All Ingredients. Shape Mixture Into 1-Inch Balls.Place in Pan.
 Bake 20-25 Minutes or Until Brown. Immediately Remove From Pan.Serve Warm With Barbecue or  chili sauce  For Dipping.Prep Time: 20 Mins
 Bake Time: 25 Mins
