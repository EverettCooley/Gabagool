Ingredients : 
 500 g calf's  liver  (cut in slices) 
 200 g  bacon  (cut in slices) 
 2  onions  (cut in slices) 
 2  carrots  (cut in slices) 
 pepper 
 nutmeg 
 1 gl water 
 100 g  butter 
 sugar 
 cognac ,  brandy  or  rum 
 Directions : 
 Cut bacon and fry it with sliced vegetables. 
 Add sliced liver and fry the mixture until its color goes to brown. 
 Add spices and water and boil until all the ingredients are soft. 
 Grind the mixture with a mincing machine. 
 Add butter and whip the mixture. 
 Put the mixture into bowls and store it in a cold place.
