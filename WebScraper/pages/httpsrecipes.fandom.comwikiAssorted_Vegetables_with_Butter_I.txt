Description : 
 In Romanian: Legume asortate cu unt I
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ lb / 250 g  carrots 
 ½ lb / 250 g  celery root 
 2 – 3  kohlrabi 
 1 teaspoon  sugar 
 2 tablespoons  butter 
 salt 
 Directions : 
 Prepare the same as  Carrots with Butter . 
 Serve as side dish or with  Cream of Wheat Croquettes .
