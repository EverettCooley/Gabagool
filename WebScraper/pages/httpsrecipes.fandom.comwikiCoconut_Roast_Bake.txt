 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups  flour 
 1/2 teasp.  bicarbonate of soda 
 1 1/2 teasp.  baking powder 
 1/4 cup  Sugar 
 1/2 teasp.  salt 
 1/2 cup  coconut cream  ( preferably made fresh) 
 2 tablesps.  margarine 
 Extra  flour  to make a silky smooth dough 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Mix dry ingredients 
 Add  margarine  and  coconut milk  and mix 
 Add more  flour  to make a smooth dough 
 Rest for one hour 
 Then knead again, adding a little bit more  margarine 
 Rest dough for another 20 mins 
 Grease baking pan, flatten dough and place in pan 
 Prick holes into top of dough with a fork 
 Bake in pre-heated oven (400 degr.F) for 15 mins., reduce heat to 350 degr. F and bake for 15 mins more 
 Cut roast bake into wedges, split and  butter  wedges while hot
