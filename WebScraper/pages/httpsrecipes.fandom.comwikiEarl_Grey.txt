      Earl Grey tea     
 This is a Ceylon tea with cranberries, vanilla, natural cream flavour. Makes great iced tea!! This popular black tea was named for Charles Grey, the second earl in his line, who was also prime minister to King William IV in the early 19th century. An amalgamation of Indian and Sri Lankan teas, Earl Grey gets its elusive flavor from oil of Bergamot. The Earl is said to have been given the recipe by a Chinese mandarin with whom he was friends.
 
 Type - Black Tea 
 Origin - Ceylon 
 Combination - Black Tea+cranberries extract
