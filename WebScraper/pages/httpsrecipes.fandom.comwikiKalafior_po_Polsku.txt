Description : 
 Serves 6 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large  cauliflower 
 salt 
 50 g  butter 
 4 tbsp dried  breadcrumbs 
 Directions : 
 Wash the  cauliflower  and divide into florets. 
 Cook in a little salted boiling  water  until just tender. 
 Drain. 
 Meanwhile, melt  butter  and fry the breadcrumbs until golden; pour over  cauliflower  just before serving.
