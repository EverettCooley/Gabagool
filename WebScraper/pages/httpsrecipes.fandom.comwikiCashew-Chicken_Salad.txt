Description : 
 Perk up a deli chicken salad with chopped apple and serve it with a just-baked dinner roll for a fabulous meal in minutes.
 
 Contributed by Jenn B aka Mom2Sam and Tiny at  World Recipes Y-Group 
 Prep Time: 10 minutes | Start to Finish: 10 minutes 
 Makes: 2 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 2 Pillsbury® microwave frozen soft white dinner rolls (from 12.4-oz bag) 
 ⅔ cup plain chicken salad for sandwiches (from deli) 
 ¼ cup finely chopped red  apple 
 2 tablespoons chopped  cashews 
 lettuce , if desired 
 Directions : 
 Microwave rolls as directed on bag. 
 In small bowl, mix remaining ingredients. 
 On 2 individual serving plates, serve chicken mixture on beds of lettuce with rolls on the side, or split warm rolls horizontally and fill with chicken mixture.
