 Description : 
 From my Aunt Barb’s collection. Date and source unknown. I don’t like  kidney beans , but my family does, so this is a regular. They love it.
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cans  kidney beans , drained 
 2 large white  onions , chopped 
 1 large green  pepper , chopped 
 1-1/4-cups  Sugar 
 1-cup  Catsup 
 3 slices  Bacon , diced 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients in a casserole. 
 Bake at 350 for 2 hours.
