 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 small  green peppers , 
 125 gr. of  feta   Cheese , 
 frying  oil , 
 1  tomato  (cut 3 disks or squares from the hard part of the  tomato ) 
 Directions : 
 Cut a hole on top of each  pepper , taking all seeds out and wash. 
 Cut the  Cheese  into 3 pieces or adjust depending on the number of  pepper , fill each  pepper  with the  Cheese  and close the hole with a  tomato  square (see above). 
 Then fry each side of the  peppers  in a pan or you can use a deep-fat fryer. 
 Leave to cool, then peel the skin and serve.
