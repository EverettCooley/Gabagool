Description : 
 This recipe was taken from Cathy's Menu and recipes for the week of 2/11/07
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  Bisquick 
 1 cup  milk 
 2 tsp dry  mustard 
 1  egg 
 1 pkg. thinly sliced cooked roast  beef , chopped 
 1 cup shredded  Swiss cheese 
 ground  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 350°F. 
 Grease a square baking dish. 
 Stir in Bisquick,  milk ,  mustard  and  egg  until blended. 
 Pour half of the batter into the baking dish. 
 Top with half of the roast  beef  and ¾ of the shredded  cheese . 
 Top with remaining roast  beef . 
 Pour remaining batter over roast  beef . 
 Bake uncovered for 45 – 50 minutes or until golden brown and center is set. 
 Sprinkle with remaining  cheese  and  pepper . 
 Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
