fig   Honey  Cookies
 
 1 c Dried  figs 
 1/2 c  shortening 
 2 lg  eggs ; Well Beaten 
 3 ts  baking powder 
 3 tb  orange  Rind; Chopped 
 1/2 c  coconut 
 1/2 ts  salt 
 3/4 c  sugar 
 1/2 c  Honey 
 2 3/4 c  all-purpose flour 
 2 tb  milk 
 1 ts  lemon extract 
 
Wash  figs , Cover With Cold  water . Simmer 10 Minutes. Drain. Cut InPieces. Cream  sugar  And  shortening . Add  Honey ,  eggs , And  milk . MixThoroughly. Sift  flour , Measure, And Sift With  baking powder , And  salt .Add to  sugar  And  shortening  Mixture. Add Fruit,  coconut , And Flavoring. AddOrange Rind. Mix Thoroughly.
 Drop by Teaspoonfuls Onto Well-Oiled Baking Sheet. Bake in Hot Oven (435F) 12-15 Minutes.
 
Enjoy.
