Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2  onions , chopped fine 
 8 pieces of  chicken 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 cup  sour cream 
 ¼ lb  margarine 
 1 tsp  paprika 
 1 cup  water 
 Directions : 
 Sauté onions in margarine. 
 Add chicken and sprinkle with paprika and salt. 
 Mix together. 
 Gradually add water. 
 Cover mixture and cook until chicken is tender, about 1 hour. If mixture is too thick, dilute with more water. 
 Just before serving, add sour cream. 
 Pour sauce over chicken and serve over rice.
