      This is the box for the burgers.     
 Prep Time: 20 min. 
 Cook time: 30 min. 
 Serves: As many as you want, depends on how many you buy. 
 
 Description : 
 A delicious main course, and easy too!
 
 Ingredients : 
 Trader Joe's Chile Lime burger patties 
 Buns 
 Salad 
 and any toppings you desire! 
 Directions : 
 Cook up the burger patties for 30 minutes. 
 Put buns on, put on toppings, and serve with salad!
