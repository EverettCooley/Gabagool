 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups shredded  cabbage 
 1  carrot , shredded 
 1  mango , diced 
 3 tbsp. finely chopped  Onion 
 2 tbsp. finely chopped red pepper 
 8 oz.  mango  flavored  yogurt 
 1 tsp.  caraway seeds 
 2 tbsp.  cider vinegar 
 1/4 tsp. dry  mustard 
 1/8 tsp.  salt 
 pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients except  cabbage  and  carrot . Stir in  cabbage  and  carrot .
