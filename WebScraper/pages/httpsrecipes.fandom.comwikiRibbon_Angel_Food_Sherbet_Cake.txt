Desription : 
 You can use any combination of sherbet flavors, or use the same flavor throughout!
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 16 servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 angel food tube cake (pre-made from store) 
 1 pint lime  sherbet 
 1 pint orange  sherbet 
 1 pint raspberry  sherbet 
 Directions : 
 Freeze cake in its pan. 
 You can make your own angel food cake, if you'd like. 
 Remove frozen cake from pan and cut into 4 layers. 
 Reassemble cake with 1 pint each of softened sherbet spread between each layer. 
 Reassemble cake and wrap in plastic wrap and aluminum foil; freeze again.
