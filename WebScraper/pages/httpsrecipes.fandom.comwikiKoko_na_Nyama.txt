 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 peanut oil  or any cooking  oil 
 one  Onion , chopped 
 one pound  Beef  stew meat, cut into cubes 
 four cups  water 
 one cup  Peanut  paste or unsweetened  Peanut   butter 
 salt , to taste 
 three to four pounds of koko or any other sort of greens; cleaned, stems removed, and cut into very thin strips 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Heat the  oil  in a large pot or dutch oven. 
 Stir-fry the  onions  for a few minutes, until they become translucent. 
 Add the meat to the pot and cook it, stirring frequently, until is well browned and near done. 
 Pour the  water  over the meat and  onions  and bring to a boil. 
 Boil, stirring occasionally, until the liquid is cooked into a thin sauce. 
 Reduce heat. 
 Stir in the  Peanut  paste (or  Peanut   butter ), and  salt . 
 Stir until smooth. Add the koko greens. 
 Cover. 
 Simmer over low heat until the greens are tender.
