Ingredients : 
 2 packages  hot dogs 
 1 jar  currant jelly 
 Equivalent amount  mustard 
 Directions : 
 Cut hot dogs in 1- inch pieces. 
 Brown slightly in saucepan. 
 Add  currant jelly . 
 Fill empty  currant jelly  jar with  mustard  and add that. 
 Heat until bubbly.
