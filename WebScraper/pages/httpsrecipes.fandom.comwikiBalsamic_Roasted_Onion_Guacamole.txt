Ingredients : 
 1 large  purple onion , chopped 
 2  garlic  cloves, chopped 
 2 tbsp  balsamic vinegar 
 1 tbsp  olive oil 
 4 small  California avocados , peeled 
 1 tbsp  lemon juice 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp dried  Italian seasoning 
 tortilla chips 
 Directions : 
 Toss together first 4 ingredients place in an aluminum foil-lined baking pan. 
 Bake at 425°F for 25 to 30 minutes or until lightly browned, stirring once. 
 Cool. 
 Mash  avocados  in a medium bowl stir in  onion  mixture,  lemon juice ,  salt , and  Italian seasoning . 
 Chill 1 hour. 
 Serve with chips.
