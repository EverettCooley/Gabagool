Info 
 Cook Time : 35 minutes 
 Serves : 
 
 Ingredients 
 1/4 cup  margarine 
 1/2 cup  honey 
 2 beaten  eggs 
 1/2 cup  orange juice 
 1 1/2 cups  flour 
 1 tsp. grated  orange  rind 
 1 tsp.  baking powder 
 1 1/2 tsp.  salt 
 3/4 cup chopped  cranberries 
 1/2 cup chopped  walnuts 
 Directions 
 1.Cream margarine and honey. Add the eggs, orange juice, and rind. Mix in the flour, baking powder, and salt. Then, add the cranberries and the walnuts. 
 
2.Bake in oiled muffin tin at 350 degrees for 35 minutes.
