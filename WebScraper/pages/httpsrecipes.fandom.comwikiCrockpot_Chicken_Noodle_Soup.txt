Ingredients : 
 1 large onion, chopped 
 3 carrots, sliced 
 2 stalks celery, sliced 
 1/2 teaspoon basil 
 1/4 teaspoon leaf thyme 
 Handful of fresh parsley, chopped 
 2 1/2 to 3 pound whole fryer chicken, cut up 
 4 cups chicken broth or more as needed. 
 1 cup noodles 
 Salt and pepper to taste 
 Directions : 
 Place all ingredients in crock pot in order listed, except noodles. 
 Cover and cook on low 8 to 10 hours or high 4 to 6 hours. 
 One hour before serving, remove chicken and cool slightly. 
 Remove meat from bones and return meat to crock pot. 
 Add more broth if necessary. 
 Add noodles. 
 Turn to high. 
 Cover and cook until noodles are done, about 30 minutes.
