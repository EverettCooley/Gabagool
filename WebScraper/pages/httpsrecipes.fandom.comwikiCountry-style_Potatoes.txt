 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 6 medium  potatoes , cooked, peeled and sliced 
 12 slices  bacon , cooked and crumbled 
 1 large white or red  onion , chopped or minced 
 6  eggs 
 ¼ cup  milk 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 ½ teaspoon  pepper 
 2 tablespoons minced fresh  parsley 
 ½ teaspoon dried  thyme 
 ½ cup shredded  Cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 In a buttered, rectangular baking dish, layer the potatoes, bacon, and onion. 
 In a large bowl, beat eggs, milk, salt, pepper, parsley, and thyme, then pour over the potatoes. 
 Bake at 350° F for 15 minutes, then sprinkle with  cheese  and bake 5 minutes longer.
