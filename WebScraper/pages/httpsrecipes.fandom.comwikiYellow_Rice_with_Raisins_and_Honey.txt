 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 250 ml  rice 
 600 ml litre boiling  water 
 10 ml  butter  or  margarine 
 2,5 ml  salt 
 5 ml  turmeric 
 10 ml  honey 
 200 ml seedless  Raisins 
 Directions : 
 Combine the  rice ,  water ,  turmeric  and  salt  in a 2 - 3 litre dish. Cover with clingwrap, pierce, and cook on High for 10 mins. Stir in the  Raisins  and cook on High for another 5 mins. 
 Tip into a sieve and rinse in cold running  water . Return the  rice  to a clean dish, stir in the  butter  and  honey  and reheat on High for 5 mins, stirring once.
