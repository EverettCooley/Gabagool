Description : 
 Sudanese Dessert
 
 Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup of  vegetable oil  /  butter 
 2 tsp  fenugreek 
 ilba) 
 1 tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  baking soda 
 1.5 cups  flour 
 2 cups  milk 
 1 cup  Sugar 
 water 
 Directions : 
 Soak fenugreek in the water for 1 hour, then drain and leave it to dry. 
 Put  oil  in pot and heat until warm. 
 Add  fenugreek  and stir for two minutes. 
 Add 3 cups of  water  and  salt , cover and cook until  fenugreek  tender (approximately 10 minutes). 
 Add  milk  and  Sugar . 
 Into a separate bowl mix 2 cups of  flour  and 2 cups of  water . 
 Add to pot and stir quickly until thick (approximately 5 minutes). 
 Mix  baking soda  with 1/3 cup of  water . 
 Add to pot through sieve into boiling dough which will become yellow. 
 Cover and simmer for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
 Pour out into a bowl to cool and eat warm.
