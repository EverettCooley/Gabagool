 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ lb. Fresh  green beans , trimmed and broken into bite-sized pieces 
 1 large ripe  tomato , cut into bite-sized pieces or 8 oz.  cherry tomatoes  halved 
 ½ small  red onion , thinly sliced and diced 
 ½ cup shaved Asiago or  feta   Cheese 
 basil  Vinaigrette Dressing 
 Directions : 
 Cook or steam the  green beans  until crisp-tender. 
 Drain and rinse with cold  water , then drain again. Place in bowl; add  tomato . 
 Add  red onion  and toss. Add  basil  Vinaigrette and toss. Chill before serving. 
 At serving time, sprinkle shaved  Cheese  on top.
