Description : 
 Salt cod Basque-style
 
 Ingredients : 
 Â½ kilo of  salt cod 
 1 cup  olive oil 
 1 head  garlic 
 flour 
 Directions : 
 Soak the fish for 12 hours, changing water every 6 hours. 
 Remove from water and wipe well. 
 Bring plenty of water to a boil and put in the fish for one minute to remove any residual bleach. 
 Remove the cod, and cut into 4 pieces. 
 Dredge the pieces in flour. 
 In a pot (clay is best) put the oil, then the cod. The oil should cover the cod. 
 Begin frying the fish, then add the garlic, peeled and cut into large pieces. 
 When the garlic is golden, remove from the heat. 
 Serve the cod with a little oil and garlic.
