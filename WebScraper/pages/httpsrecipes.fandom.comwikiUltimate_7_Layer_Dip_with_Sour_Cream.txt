Ultimate 7 Layer Dip
 
 16 oz Can  refried beans 
 1 Tbs  taco seasoning  Mix 
 1 Cup  sour cream 
 1 Cup Salsa 
 1 Cup Shredded  lettuce 
 1 Cup Mexican Style Shredded  Cheese 
 1/2 Cup Chopped  green onions 
 2 Tbs Sliced Mix beans And  taco seasoning  Mix. Spread Onto Bottom of 9 Inch Pie Plate. Layer Remaining Ingredients Over Bean Mixture, Cover. Refrigerate Several Hours. Serve With Tortilla Chips or Assorted Crackers. 
 
This Recipe Makes 48 Servings of Ultimate 7 Layer Dip.*
