 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4 Tbs.  olive oil 
 1 - 2 c. cooked  kidney beans 
 1 large  Onion , sliced thin 
 1 hard-boiled  egg , chopped 
 salt  and freshly ground  pepper  (to taste) 
 1Â lb.  green beans , cut in half, cooked and cooled 
 Parmesan cheese , grated, (or substitute Swiss) 
 1 clove  garlic , finely chopped or put through  garlic  press 
 Directions : 
 Place  green beans  and  kidney beans  in large bowl. 
 Combine  olive oil ,  Onion ,  garlic ,  parsley ,  salt  and  pepper . 
 Pour over beans and mix lightly, cover and chill in the refrigerator. 
 Serve on  lettuce  and sprinkle with chopped  egg  and  Cheese .
