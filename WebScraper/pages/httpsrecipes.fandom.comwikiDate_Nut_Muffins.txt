 Description : 
 Purchased from the Van Sickle Estate in Mabank, Texas in 1990. Dated 1949. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1-cup whole- wheat flour 
 1 cup white  flour 
 2 teaspoons  baking powder 
 1-teaspoon  cinnamon 
 1-1/2 tablespoons  vegetable oil 
 1/2 cup chopped  dates 
 1/2 cup chopped  walnuts 
 1  egg , beaten 
 Â½ cup  Sugar 
 1-cup  milk 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 400. 
 Mix dry ingredients. 
 Add  dates  and  walnuts . 
 Make a hole in the mixture and add  milk ,  egg , and  oil . 
 Mix until blended. 
 Spray muffin tin with  cooking spray  and fill two thirds full. 
 Bake for 20 to 25 minutes.
