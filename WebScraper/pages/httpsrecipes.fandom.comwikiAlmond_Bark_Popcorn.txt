 Ingredients : 
 3 bags microwave  popcorn 
 3 cups  Rice Crispies 
 1 jar  peanuts  (or 1Â½ - 2 cups) 
 1 package  almond bark 
 Directions : 
 In a large bowl, mix together the popcorn, rice crispies and peanuts. 
 Melt 1 package almond bark in microwave. 
 Stir every few seconds. 
 Add 3 tablespoons of peanut butter. 
 Stir till heated. 
 Pour over popcorn mixture. 
 Mix together while it cools. 
 Store in air tight container.
