Ingredients : 
 2 c. puffed rice cereal 
 1 1/2 c. corn cereal (such as corn chex) 
 1 c. wheat cereal (such as wheat chex) 
 1 jar soy nuts or 1 1/2 c. peanuts 
 2 cans (1 1/2 oz. ea.) potato sticks 
 6 tbsp. butter 
 1 sm. clove garlic, crushed 
 1 tbsp. chili powder 
 1/2 tsp. each of onion and celery salt 
 1/8 tsp. cayenne pepper 
 Directions : 
 In large bowl, combine cereals, nuts, and potato sticks. Set aside. 
 Melt butter in small pan over low heat. Stir in remaining ingredients. 
 Stir into cereal mixture and place in 15 x 10 inch pan. 
 Bake in preheated oven (350 degrees) for 15 minutes, stirring twice. 
 Turn out on paper towel to cool. 
 8 cups.
 Enjoy.
