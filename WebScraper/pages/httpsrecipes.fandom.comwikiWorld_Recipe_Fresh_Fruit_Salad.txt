Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 This Y-group is international. Good food from all parts of the world. A place where we can share the wonderful food from all over the world.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1  cantaloupe  (musk in some countries), seeds removed and peeled, cut into bite size pieces. 
 1  honey dew melon , seeds removed, peeled and cut into bite size pieces 
 1  casaba melon , seeds removed, peeled and cut into bite size pieces. 
 1 package of  strawberries , cleaned and cut up 
 1 package of  blueberries , washed 
 1 large bunch of  grapes  (seedless, green or red) 
 1 large can of your favorite  fruit cocktail  in lite syrup or in juice 
 2 cups of  white wine 
 enough  orange juice  or  apple juice  to fill the container and nearly cover the fruits 
 Directions : 
 You'll need a large bowl that has an airtight cover. The  white wine  adds a nice kick to the salad that is subtle but there. Enough  orange juice  or  apple juice  to fill the container and nearly cover the fruits. 
 You can serve this right away or let it sit until a few hours before serving. 
 It tastes even better the next day and the next and the next. 
 Some serving suggestions: with  whipped cream , on  ice cream , with a serving of your favorite  cheese  on the side, with crackers on the side.
