 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 400 gr of new  Cheese , 
 4  eggs , 
 80 gr of grated koroman (old  Rye  Montenegrin bread), 
 80 gr of  Sugar , 
 1 pack of  vanilla   Sugar , 
 1 pack of  baking soda , 
 4 dl of  milk , 
 4 dl of  sour cream , 
 peel of one home grown  lemon . 
 Directions : 
 Crush  Cheese , add  eggs , grated  rye bread  (dipped in  milk  and  sour cream ),  Sugar ,  vanilla   Sugar ,  baking powder ,  lemon peel  and pour all together onto hot grease. 
 Bake in the baking pan until it becomes reddish.
