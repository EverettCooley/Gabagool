Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Williams Estate in Tyler, Texas in 1994.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ pound  ground beef 
 ¼ pound  ground pork 
 ¾ cup  rolled oats 
 ¼ cup finely chopped  water chestnuts 
 ¼ teaspoon  Worcestershire sauce 
 ½ cup  milk 
 ½ teaspoon  onion salt 
 ½ teaspoon  garlic salt 
 few drops  Tabasco sauce 
 margarine 
 Sweet Sour Sauce : 
 1 cup  sugar 
 ¾ cup  vinegar 
 ¾ cup  water 
 1 teaspoon  paprika 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 3 teaspoons  cornstarch 
 1 tablespoon  water 
 Directions : 
 Blend all meatball ingredients and form into about 75 small balls. 
 Brown well in  margarine  then drain on paper towels. 
 Sweet Sour Sauce : 
 Make sauce by combining  sugar ,  vinegar ,  water ,  paprika  and  salt  and cook for 5minutes then add  cornstarch  mixed with  water  and cook until thickened stirring constantly. 
 Add in meatballs and simmer for 30 minutes. 
 Serve warm.
