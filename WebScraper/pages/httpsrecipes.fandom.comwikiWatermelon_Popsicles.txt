Ingredients : 
 1 cup seedless  watermelon  chunks 
 1 cup  orange juice 
 1 cup  water 
 popsicle sticks and cups 
 Directions : 
 Blend these ingredients into a blender, pour into molds, insert sticks, and freeze.
