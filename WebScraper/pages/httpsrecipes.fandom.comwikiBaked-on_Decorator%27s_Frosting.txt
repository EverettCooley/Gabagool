Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ⅓ cup  all-purpose flour 
 ⅓ cup  butter  or  margarine , softened 
 1½ tsp hot  water 
 2 drop  food coloring , if desired 
 Directions : 
 Mix  flour  and  butter  until smooth. 
 Stir in hot  water  and, if desired, 2 – 3 drops food color. 
 Place in decorator's tube with no.3 writing tip. 
 Outline, write or make designs on unbaked rolled cookies. 
 Bake cookies as directed in recipes. 
 Enough for 2 – 3 dozen cookies. 
 Enjoy.
