Ingredients : 
 2 x 14-oz bottles  catsup 
 1 x 12-oz bottle  chili sauce 
 ½ cup  prepared mustard 
 1 tbsp  dry mustard 
 1 tbsp  salt 
 1½ cup  brown sugar , firmly packed 
 2 tbsp  black pepper 
 1 x 5-oz bottle  steak sauce 
 ½ cup  Worcestershire sauce 
 1 tbsp  soy sauce 
 1 x 12-oz bottle  beer 
 2 tbsp mince  garlic 
 Directions : 
 Combine all ingredients, except  garlic , in saucepan and simmer 30 minutes over medium heat. 
 Add minced  garlic  before using. 
 Baste meat during last 15 minutes of grilling time.
