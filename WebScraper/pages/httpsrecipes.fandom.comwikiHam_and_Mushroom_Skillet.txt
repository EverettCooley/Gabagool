Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup chopped  onion 
 ⅓ cup diced  green pepper 
 1 x 8-ounce can sliced  mushrooms , drained 
 2 tablespoons  butter  or  margarine 
 2 x 10¾-ounce cans condensed  cream of chicken  soup 
 2 cups diced cooked  ham 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 ¼ cup diced  pimento 
 ¼ teaspoon ground  black pepper 
 1 cup grated  cheddar  cheese 
 Directions : 
 Cook  onion ,  green pepper  and  mushrooms  in  butter  until tender. 
 Add soup and stir until smooth and hot. 
 Blend in  ham ,  rice ,  pimento  and  pepper . 
 Sprinkle grated  cheese  over the top. 
 Cover and simmer about 15 minutes, or until  cheese  is melted and ingredients are heated through.
