Johnnie Cakes
Prep: 10 min, Cook: 20 min.
 
  a.. 3 cups yellow cornmeal 
 b.. 1 cup milk 
 c.. 3 Tbs. vegetable oil 
 d.. 1 Tbs. baking soda 
 e.. 3/4 tsp. salt
 
 Preheat oven to 350�F. Mix ingredients into a stiff batter. Form 8 biscuits and place on a lightly greased baking sheet. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until browned. Let cool and serve with butter, molasses, or fresh jam.
