Description : 
 Contributed by  Pressurecookerrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup of  steel-cut oats  (i.e. McCann's) 
 ¾ cup  dried fruit 
 2¼ cups  water 
 Directions : 
 In a 2.5 qt pressure cooker, put the oats, dried fruit and water cook at high pressure for 15 minutes. 
 Optional: let the pressure come down naturally and then often add more  water  or  soy milk . 
 This microwaves beautifully the following morning.
