Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs small  red potatoes , quartered 
 8 oz  cream cheese , softened 
 1 envelope  buttermilk  ranch dry  salad dressing  mix 
 1 can  cream of potato  soup 
 Directions : 
 Place  potatoes  in a 3½ quart crockpot. 
 In a small bowl combine cream  cheese  and  salad dressing  mix. 
 Stir in soup. 
 Add to  potatoes . 
 Cover and cook on low for 7 - 9 hours or on high for 3½ to 4½ hours. 
 Stir to blend before serving.
