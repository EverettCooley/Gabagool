      Bosartma     
 Ingredients : 
 1 kg  lamb 
 100 g melted  butter 
 150 g  onion 
 300 g  tomatoes 
 50 g  tomato paste 
 50 g  cherry plums 
 greens ( cilantro ,  dill ,  mint ) 
 Â½  lemon 
 ground  pepper 
 200 g  cucumbers

 Directions : 
 Slice  lamb  on pieces 50 g each, fry in  butter  until light brown on high heat. 
 Add chopped  onion , sliced  tomatoes ,  tomato paste ,  cherry plums , greens and season, pour over a little of broth and stew until soft.
