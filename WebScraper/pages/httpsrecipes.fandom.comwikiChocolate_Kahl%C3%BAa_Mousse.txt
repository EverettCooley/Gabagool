 Ingredients : 
 8 oz  bittersweet chocolate , melted 
 16 oz  heavy cream 
 1 tbsp  vanilla extract 
 3 oz  Kahlúa 
 4 large  egg whites 
 Directions : 
 Melt  chocolate  over very low heat until smooth. Cool to room temperature. 
 Whip the heavy  cream  to very soft peaks. 
 At this time, add the  vanilla  and the  Kahlúa  and continue whipping until medium peaks are reached. 
 Combine and blend cooled  chocolate  and whipped  cream  mixture. Refrigerate until needed.
