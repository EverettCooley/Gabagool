 Ingredients : 
 1 pound well-trimmed boneless  beef top sirloin  or  top round steak , cut ¾ inch thick 
 5 tablespoons Kikkoman stir-fry sauce, divided 
 2 teaspoons minced  garlic 
 ¼ teaspoon grated fresh  orange peel 
 1 tablespoon fresh  orange juice 
 ½ teaspoon  cornstarch 
 1 pound fresh  broccoli 
 3 tablespoons  vegetable oil , divided 
 1  carrot , cut diagonally into thin slices 
 ¼ cup slivered  almonds , toasted 
 Directions : 
 Cut beef into ⅛ to ¼-inch thick strips. 
 Combine 2 tbsp stir-fry sauce, garlic and orange peel; stir in beef to coat. 
 Cover and refrigerate 20 minutes. 
 Meanwhile, combine remaining 3 tbsp stir-fry sauce, orange juice and cornstarch. 
 Remove flowerets from broccoli; cut into bite-size pieces. 
 Peel stalks; cut diagonally into thin slices. 
 Heat 1 tbsp oil in hot wok or large skillet over high heat. 
 Stir-fry ½ of beef strips 1 minute; remove. 
 Repeat with 1 tbsp oil and remaining beef strips. 
 Heat remaining 1 tbsp oil in same wok over medium-high heat. 
 Add broccoli and carrot; stir-fry 3 to 4 minutes, or until crisp-tender. 
 Sprinkle with water, if needed. 
 Stir in stir-fry sauce mixture. 
 Cook and stir until mixture boils and thickens. 
 Return beef and heat through. 
 Sprinkle with almonds and serve immediately.
