Info 
 Cook Time : 
 Serves : 
 
 Ingredients 
 2 lbs (8 small)  nectarines  cut up 
 1 cup  apple juice 
 1 cup  cranberry juice  cocktail 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp  red pepper flakes 
 1 Tbsp  balsamic vinegar 
 ¼ cup fresh  cilantro  leaves 
 Directions 
 1.Combine nectarines with juices, salt, pepper flakes and vinegar in electric blender. Whirl until smooth and blended. 
 2.Add cilantro leaves and whirl in a stop-and-go fashion a few seconds, just to chop.
 
 Source 
 Fruit of the Month: Nectarine  by the US Centers for Disease Control & Prevention, public domain government resource—original source of recipe
