Description 
 Fruit & Vegetable Recipes I  by the South Carolina Department of Agriculture, public domain government recipe—original source of recipe
 
 Serves : 6 
 Ingredients 
 1 (9") deep dish unbaked  pie shell 
 1½ cup fresh  spinach 
 3 tbsp  butter 
 4  eggs 
 1 cup  half-and-half 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  nutmeg 
 1 cup shredded  Mozzarella  or  Swiss cheese 
 Directions 
 Let pie shell thaw at room temperature while combining other ingredients. 
 Wash spinach and remove any coarse stems. 
 Melt butter in large skillet; add spinach and cook 5 minutes or until spinach is limp. 
 Chop spinach fine. 
 Beat eggs; stir in half-and-half, salt, nutmeg, cheese and chopped spinach. 
 Pour into pie shell. 
 Bake at 375°F for 40 to 45 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes our clean. 
 Let stand approximately 5 minutes before cutting. 
 Can be served warm or cold
