Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  flour 
 2 tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 1½ lbs  beef chuck , cubed 
 3 tbsp  oil 
 2 cup  onions , sliced 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 1  bay leaf 
 2 tbsp  tomato paste 
 2 tbsp  brown sugar 
 2 tbsp chopped  parsley 
 1 can  beef broth 
 2 cups  beer 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 320°F. 
 Mix  flour ,  salt , and  pepper . 
 Dredge meat in mixture. 
 Heat  oil  in Dutch oven; brown meat. 
 Add remaining ingredients. 
 Simmer. 
 Cover and bake in oven 2 hours.
