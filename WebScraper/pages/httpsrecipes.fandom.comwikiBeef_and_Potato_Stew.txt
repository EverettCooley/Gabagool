Ingredients : 
 2 lbs. Cubed  Beef 
 2 medium  potatoes  (chopped) 
 2 tablespoon.  oil 
 4 tablespoon.  Sugar 
 2 large  onions , sliced 
 1 tablespoon.  worcestershire sauce  2 tablespoon soy sauce 
 2  bay leaves 
 2 cloves  garlic 
 3 scallions 
 1 tablespoon.  basil  or  thyme 
 Black and  hot pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Season meat with soy,  worcestershire sauce , and  black pepper . Marinate for 3 hours or overnight in a mixing bowls. 
 Heat  oil  and add  Sugar  in a large pot. Allow to burn until dark brown and looks like a caramel coating. 
 Add remaining ingredients except  potatoes  and cook for 20–30 minutes. 
 Add  potatoes  and cook until tender.
