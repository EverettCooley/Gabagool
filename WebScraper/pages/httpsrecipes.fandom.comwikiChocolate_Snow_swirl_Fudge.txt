Chocolate Snow swirl Fudge
 
3 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 (14-oz.) can Eagle(r) Brand Sweetened Condensed Milk (NOT evaporated milk)
4 tablespoons butter or margarine
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
Dash salt
1 cup chopped nuts
2 cups miniature marshmallows
 Melt chips with Eagle Brand, 2 Tbsp. butter, vanilla and salt. Remove from
heat; stir in nuts. Spread evenly into foil-lined 8- or 9-inch square pan.
 Melt marshmallows with remaining 2 Tbsp. butter. Spread on top of fudge.
With table knife or metal spatula, swirl through top of fudge.
 Chill at least 2 hours or until firm. Turn fudge onto cutting board; peel
off foil and cut into squares. Store loosely covered at room temperature.
 Makes about 2 lbs.
