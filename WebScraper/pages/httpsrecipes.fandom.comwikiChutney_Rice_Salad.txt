 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice , cooled 
 1 1/2 cups seedless  Green Grapes , halved 
 1 cup cooked  green peas 
 1 cup sliced  celery 
 1 tablespoon minced  Onion 
 1/2 cup  Mayonnaise 
 2 tablespoons chutney, chopped 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 1/2 teaspoon ground  white pepper 
 1/2 teaspoon dry  mustard 
 lettuce  leaves 
 Whole  grapes  for garnish (optional) 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  grapes ,  Peas ,  celery  and  Onion  in large bowl. Blend  Mayonnaise , chutney,  salt ,  pepper  and  mustard ; pour over  rice  mixture and toss lightly. Chill. Serve on  lettuce  leaves; garnish with  grapes , if desired.
