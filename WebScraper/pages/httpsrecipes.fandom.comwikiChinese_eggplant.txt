 About Chinese eggplant : 
 Wikipedia Article  About Chinese eggplants on Wikipedia 
 Chinese eggplants are a variety of the sweet vegetable-fruit distinguished by their long, slender shape and pale purple color and prized for their tender skin and lack of seeds. If they are not available, somewhat shorter but still slender  Japanese eggplants  may be substituted.
