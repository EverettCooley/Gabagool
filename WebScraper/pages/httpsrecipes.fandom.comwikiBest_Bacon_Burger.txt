 Prep Time: as long as it takes. 
 Cook time: to your liking 
 Serves: around 6 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lb. hamburger meat, i like 70/30 
 1/2 lb. bacon 
 Cheese that you love 
 I got a cheep plastic Hamburger press that i love 
 salt/pepper to taste 
 Directions : 
 Brown the bacon till it is extra crispy, but do not burn..... 
 Let cool, and bring beef to room temp. 
 crumble bacon into chewable size. 
 mix with raw meat. 
 make 1/4 lb. patties. 
 put one slice of cheese between two patties (i love Munster) and crimp edges 
 Grill till done to your likeness.
