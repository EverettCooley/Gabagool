 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 4  California avocados 
 2 Tbsp fresh  lemon juice 
 1 Cup finely diced red  bell pepper 
 1 Cup  cherry tomatoes , cut in half or quarters if large 
 ½ Cup thinly sliced  green onion 
 5 large cloves  garlic , chopped finely 
 2 tsp chopped, fresh  thyme  leaves 
 1 tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp freshly ground  black pepper 
 ¼ tsp  cayenne pepper 
 Directions : 
 Coarsely mash (DO NOT PUREE)  California avocados ; stir in  lemon juice . Fold in remaining ingredients. Serve with  Shrimp ,  corn  bread sticks, batter-fried oysters,  celery  sticks.
 Guacamole is best made as close to service as possible. Store in an airtight
