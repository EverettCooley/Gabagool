Fish Fingers and Custard is a simpily made meal.
 
 Ingredients : 
 Fish Fingers
 A cup of custard
 
 Recipe : 
 Simply just get a cup of custard and put 2 fish fingers inside.
 
 Trivia : 
 This is from the British science fiction television series, Doctor Who. In that, this is the Eleventh Doctor's favorite food.
