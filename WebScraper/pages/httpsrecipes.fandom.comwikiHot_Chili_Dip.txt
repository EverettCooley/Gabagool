Hot  chili  Dip
 
 1 Lb. Ground  Beef 
 1 Medium  green pepper , Chopped 
 1 Envelope French's  chili -O Seasoning Mix 
 1 Can  tomato  Paste (6 Oz) 
 1 Pkg.  cream cheese , Softened (3 Oz) 
 1 Cup  water 
 corn  Chips
 
Cook  Beef  With  pepper  in Skillet. Stir to Crumble. Drain Off Fat. Stir in Seasoning Mix. Stir in  tomato  Paste,  cream cheese  And  water . Cook Over Medium Heat, Stirring Occasionally, Until  Cheese  Melts And Mixture Comes to a Boil. Serve Warm With  corn  Chips.Makes 4 Cups
