 Description : 
 Waffles...when you think of them you're thinking of Belgium Waffles, the real thing, this recipe tastes so good that you can't stop enjoying ! Enjoy !
 
 Ingredients : 
 3  eggs 
 1 1/2 cups all purpose  flour 
 2 teaspoons  baking powder 
 1/2 teaspoon  baking soda 
 1 tablespoon  granulated sugar 
 1/2 cup  sour cream 
 3/4 cup  milk 
 1/3 cup  chocolate syrup 
 1/4 cup  vegetable oil 
 1/4 cup  butter , melted 
 2 cups sliced  strawberries 
 1 cup  whipping cream 
 Directions : 
 Beat  egg  whites until stiff. Set aside. 
 Mix together the  flour ,  baking powder ,  baking soda  and  Sugar . 
 Beat the  egg   yolks  with the  sour cream ,  milk , and  chocolate  syrup. 
 In a small bowl, mix the  oil  with the melted  butter . Add the  sour cream  mixture alternately with the  oil  and  butter  mixture to dry ingredients. Mix until smooth. 
 Fold in beaten  egg  whites. 
 Pour 1/2 cup of batter into a lightly greased heated waffle maker. Close lid. 
 Bake for 4 to 5 minutes or until steaming stops. 
 Repeat with remaining batter. 
 Top hot waffles with sliced  strawberries  and  whipped cream . Serve immediately. 
 Tips : 
 This recipe is best suited for a Belgian type waffle maker. You may use a regular waffle maker, but in that case reduce batter to 1/4 cup per waffle and decrease cooking time to 2 1/2 to 3 1/2 minutes. 
 Per serving: 485 calories; 8.8 g protein; 32.7 g fat; 41.1 g carbohydrates; 323 mg sodium.
