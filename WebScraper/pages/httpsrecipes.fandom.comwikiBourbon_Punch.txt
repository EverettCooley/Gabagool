Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ oz  bourbon 
 1 oz  brandy 
 1 oz  Benedictine 
 1 oz  orange juice  and  lemon juice  (each) 
 club soda 
 Directions : 
 Shake  Bourbon ,  brandy ,  Bénédictine ,  orange juice , and  lemon juice  in a shaker with  ice  and train into a collins glass. 
 Fill the glass with  club soda  and service with a straw.
