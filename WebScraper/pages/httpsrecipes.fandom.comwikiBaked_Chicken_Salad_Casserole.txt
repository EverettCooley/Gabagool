This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Williams Estate in Tyler, Texas in 1994.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups  chicken  cooked and cut up 
 1½ cups  celery  chopped 
 2 cups  French dressing 
 1 cup  cheddar  cheese grated 
 ⅓ cup  slivered almonds 
 1½ cups  sour cream 
 1½ teaspoons  prepared mustard 
 ½ teaspoon  salt 
 1 cup  potato chips  crushed 
 Directions : 
 Marinate  chicken  and  celery  in French dressing for 1 hour. 
 Combine chicken,  celery , ½ cup grated  cheese  and  almonds  then blend sour cream,  mustard  and  salt  and pour over  chicken  and toss to blend. 
 Put into baking dish. 
 Combine  potato  chips and remaining grated cheese and put on top of  chicken  mixture. 
 Bake for 30 minutes at 300°F.
