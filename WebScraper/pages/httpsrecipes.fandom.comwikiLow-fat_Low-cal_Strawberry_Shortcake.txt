Ingredients : 
 1 pkg sugar-free  strawberry flavored gelatin 
 1 bag frozen whole  strawberries  - no sugar added 
 1 whole fat-free angel food cake 
 1 tub fat-free  whipped topping 
 Directions : 
 Prepare  gelatin  according to package directions, omitting cold  water  step. 
 Instead, add frozen  strawberries  and stir until  berries  begin to thaw, and congeal the  gelatin . 
 Once  strawberries  are soft enough to cut, cut into bite sized pieces, and continue to stir until well mixed. 
 Tear angel food cake into bite sized pieces and stir into  strawberry  mixture. 
 Place in refrigerator at least an hour to set. 
 Spoon into serving dishes and top with whipped topping as desired.
