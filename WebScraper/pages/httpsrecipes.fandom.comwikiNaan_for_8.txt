 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 Sift together 500 g (1 lb)  plain flour , 
 1 teaspoon  baking powder , 
 1/2 teaspoon  bicarbonate of soda 
 1 teaspoon  salt 
 Add 1 beaten  egg , 
 1 tablespoon melted  ghee  or  butter , 
 1/2 cup (125 g)  yoghurt 
 gradually add 1 cup (250 ml)  milk  or enough to form a soft dough 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Preheat the oven to moderately hot, 200 C (400 F). 
 Cover with a damp cloth and leave in a warm place for 2 hours. 
 Knead on a well-floured surface for 2–3 minutes, or until smooth. 
 Divide into 8 portions and roll each one into a oval 15 cm (6 inches) long. 
 Brush with  water  and place, wet-side-down, on greased baking trays. 
 Brush with melted  ghee  or  butter  and bake for 8–10 minutes, or until golden brown. Makes 8.
