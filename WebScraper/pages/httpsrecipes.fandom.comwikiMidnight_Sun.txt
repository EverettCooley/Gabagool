 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 2 ½ oz.  vodka 
 ½ oz.  grenadine 
 lemon syrup (optional) 
 Directions : 
 In a mixing glass, combine 3-4  ice  cubes,  vodka , and grenadine. 
 Stir and strain into a chilled cocktail glass. 
 Drizzle with the lemon syrup, if desired.
