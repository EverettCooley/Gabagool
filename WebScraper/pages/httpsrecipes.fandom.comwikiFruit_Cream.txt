 Prep Time: 2 minutes 
 Cook time: 3 minutes 
 Serves: One 
 
 Description : 
 Make a delicious fruit cream for your dessert or appetizer! It is similar to fruit salad. However, the fruit are not chopped, they are mixed at the mixer. It is simple to make and takes only five minutes to be prepared. In addition, fruit cream could also be a great snack for children.
 
 Ingredients : 
 Variety of fruits, such as:
 
 Apple 
 Banana 
 Orange 
 Peach 
 Plum 
 Pineapple 
 Strawberry  
 and several other sorts of fruit. 
 Directions : 
 Slice the fruit with a knife. 
 Then, put it in the mixer for 2 minutes. 
 When they have become creamy, put it in a bowl. 
 You are now free to use it for your dishes. 
 Extra Tips : 
 Choose your fruit carefully. You can costume your fruit cream be choosing specific kinds of fruit so you will be able to:
 
 Change the cream's appearance. Give a unique color. 
 Give an unusual, but sweet taste (you should use exotic fruit whether you would be interesting to surprise your guests). 
 Also put some juice in the cream (gives scent). 
 Finally, dress your fruit cream with some fresh fruit like the ones in the pictures.
