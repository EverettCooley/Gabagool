Description : 
 Great for breakfast!
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups  flour 
 1/2 teaspoon  salt 
 3 teaspoons  baking powder 
 2 tablespoons  butter , melted 
 1/4 cup mashed  banana 
 1/4 cup  Sugar 
 1  egg 
 2 tablespoons  milk 
 Directions : 
 Sift flour, salt and baking powder together and out in bowl 
 Beat egg and combine with milk, melted butter, sugar and banana. 
 Add the banana mixture into a hollow in the middle of the flour and mix together with a fork. 
 Put tablespoons of the scone mixture on a greased baking tray and bake in a hot oven till brown - about 15 minutes.
