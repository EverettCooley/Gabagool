 Description : 
 Makes 8 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 1 1/2 to 2 cups cooked  Chicken  cubes 
 1 cup sliced  celery 
 1/2 cup diced cooked  Ham 
 1/4 cup sliced  ripe olives 
 1 tablespoon minced  Onion 
 3/4 to 1 cup  Mayonnaise 
 4 avocados 
 2 tablespoons  lemon juice 
 Mayonnaise  (optional) 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  Chicken ,  celery ,  Ham ,  Onion  and  Mayonnaise  in large bowl; toss lightly. Chill. Cut avocados in half, seed, and peel; brush with  lemon juice . Spoon salad into  avocado  halves. Top with  Mayonnaise , if desired.
