 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  brown rice 
 1 29-ounce can  peach  slices, drained, cut into chunks 
 1/2 cup  Honey , divided 
 1/2 cup  all-purpose flour 
 1/2 teaspoon ground  cinnamon 
 1/8 teaspoon ground  nutmeg 
 1/4 cup  butter 
 1/3 cup chopped  walnuts  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Combine  rice ,  peaches  and 1/4 cup  Honey  in greased 8x8x2-inch baking dish. 
 Combine  flour  and spices in small bowl; cut in  butter  until mixture is crumbly. 
 Sprinkle  flour  mixture over  rice ; drizzle remaining 1/4 cup  Honey  on top. 
 Sprinkle  nuts  on top, if desired. Bake at 350 degrees 30 to 35 minutes. Serve warm.
