Description : 
 I personally would not use the white  bread ...Seems odd for a diabetic recipe! Try using your favorite healthy  bread ...Low carb/whole  grain  for better nutrition. Take care, Gloria
 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 Source:  Forbidden Foods Diabetic Cooking  by Maggie Powers 
 Yield: 6 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 ¾ cup  egg substitute   or  3  eggs 
 ¾ cup  low-fat (1%) milk 
 3 slices day-old white  bread , cubed 
 ½ cup fresh  spinach , finely chopped 
 ½ cup shredded pepper  jack cheese 
 Directions : 
 Spay an 8-inch round pan with non-stick  cooking spray . 
 In a medium bowl, whisk the  eggs  until frothy. 
 Add the  milk  and  bread . Let stand for 5 Minutes. 
 Add the  spinach  and  cheese  and stir to blend. 
 Pour into the prepared pan. Cover and refrigerate 8 hours or overnight. 
 Preheat the oven to 350 °F. 
 Bake the strata, uncovered, until the top is lightly browned, about 30 minutes. 
 Serve hot or at room temperature. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving (⅙ of recipe):
 
 99 Calories | 4g Fat | 10 mg Cholesterol | 209 mg Sodium | 9g Carbs | 0g Dietary Fiber | 2g Sugars | 7g Protein 
 Diabetic Exchanges: ½ starch | 1 lean meat
