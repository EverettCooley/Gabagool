Description : 
 Makes about 5 cups
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ (26-ounce) jar  mango  slices, chopped 
 1 (2.25-ounce) can sliced ripe  olives , drained and chopped 
 1 small  purple onion , diced 
 ¼ cup minced fresh  cilantro 
 ⅓ cups fresh  lime juice 
 2 tbsp  balsamic vinegar 
 2 tbsp  honey 
 2 tbsp  olive oil 
 1 tbsp  chile garlic sauce 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ½ tsp dried  crushed red pepper 
 4 small  California avocados , peeled and chopped 
 2 large fresh  mango , peeled and diced, may be substituted 
 Directions : 
 Stir together first 11 ingredients in a large bowl. 
 Gently stir in  avocado . 
 Cover and chill 1 hour. 
 Serve with  sweet potato  chips or  tortilla chips .
