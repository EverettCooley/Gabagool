 Description : 
 A  Catfish  recipe.
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 U.S. farm-raised  catfish  fillets (4 to 6 ounces each) 
 olive oil spray 
 "Hail Ceasar" rub : 
 3 tablespoons dried  basil 
 3 tablespoons dried  oregano 
 3 tablespoons  garlic salt 
 3 tablespoons  lemon pepper 
 4 tablespoons grated  Parmesan cheese  (such as Kraft) 
 Directions : 
 Prepare "Hail Ceasar" Rub by mixing spices and  Parmesan cheese  in a glass jar with a tight-fitting lid. 
 Store the blend in a dark cupboard, away from heat, and it will keep for two to three months. 
 Preheat grill to high heat 
 Sprinkle ½ to 1 teaspoon of the blend on a lightly oiled fillet. 
 Grill or broil over high heat for 3½ to 4 minutes per side, or until the fish begins to flake when tested with a fork in the thickest part.
