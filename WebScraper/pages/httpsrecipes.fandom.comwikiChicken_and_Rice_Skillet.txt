Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 1½ pounds boneless  chicken  breasts, cut into 1-inch cubes 
 1 cup uncooked  rice 
 1 x 10¾-ounce can condensed  cream of mushroom  soup 
 1 cup  water 
 1 x 10-ounce package frozen chopped  broccoli , thawed 
 1 x 2¼-ounce can sliced  ripe olives 
 Directions : 
 Brown  chicken  in hot  oil  over high heat in large saucepan or Dutch oven. 
 Add  rice , soup,  water ,  broccoli  and ; bring to a boil. 
 Cover and simmer 15 to 20 minutes or until  rice  is tender and liquid is absorbed.
