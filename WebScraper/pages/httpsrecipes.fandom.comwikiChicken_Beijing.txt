Description : 
 Makes 4 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tablespoon  vegetable oil 
 1 pound boneless skinless  chicken  breasts, cut into ¼-inch thick strips 
 2 cups  broccoli  flowerets 
 ¾ cup  orange juice 
 1 teaspoon  garlic powder 
 ½ teaspoon  ground ginger 
 1 package (4- to 6-ounces) [[chicken]- flavored seasoned  rice  mix, prepared according to package directions 
 Directions : 
 Heat  oil  in large skillet over medium-high heat until hot. 
 Add  chicken , cook and stir 2 to 3 minutes until  chicken  is no longer pink. 
 Add  broccoli ; cook 2 to 3 minutes or until  chicken  is beginning to brown and  broccoli  is tender. 
 Reduce heat to medium; add  orange juice ,  garlic powder  and  ginger . Cook 2 to 3 minutes more or until liquid is slightly reduced. 
 Stir in  rice ; serve immediately.
