Description : 
 Contributed by  The Bartending School 
 
 Ingredients : 
 ½ oz  apricot brandy 
 ½ oz  gin 
 ½ oz  orange juice 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Shake with  ice . 
 Highball version: Try this with ¾ oz each of  apricot brandy  and  gin  in a highball glass filled with  ice . 
 Add  orange juice  to top and stir.
