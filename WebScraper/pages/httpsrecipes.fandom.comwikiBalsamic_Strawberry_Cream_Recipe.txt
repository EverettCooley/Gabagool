The Little Bit of  vinegar  Gives The Berries a Unique And Divine Flavour. The Original Idea Came From a Chef WhoWas Inspired by The French Way of Marinating  strawberries  in  red wine .
 
 4-6 Servings 
 20 minutes 20 minutes Prep 
 Ingredients : 
 400 g  strawberries 
 1 Tablespoon  sugar 
 1 Tablespoon  balsamic vinegar  (preferably Aged) 
 284 g  whipping cream 
 
 
 Remove The Stalks From The  strawberries . 
 Leave Any Small Ones Whole But Cut Larger Ones in Half or Quarters.Place in a Mixing Bowl And Sprinkle With  sugar . 
 Leave The  sugar  to Melt With The  strawberry  Juices, Stirring Occasionally.Add The  vinegar  And Toss Thoroughly With a Wooden Spoon or Rubber Spatula.In a Separate Bowl, Whip The Cream Until it Just Holds Its Shape.Transfer Into a Serving Bowl. 
 Loosely Fold The  strawberries  And Their Juices Into The Cream, Keeping ItSwirly. 
 Serve Immediately.
