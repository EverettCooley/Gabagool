 Prep Time:10 
 Cook time:50 
 Serves:10 
 
 
 Description : 
 This dish is going well with rice or noodle.
 
 Ingredients : 
 500 grams  pig trotter 
 5  green onions 
 5  garlic 
 1  red pepper (hot) 
 2  star anise 
 3 tbsp of  soy sauce 
 2 tbsp of  rice wine 
 2 tbsp of  sugar 
 1 tbsp of  oyster sauce 
 700 cc water 
 Directions : 
 Wash pig's feet and chop it to small pieces. 
 Put it into boiling water and take them out (when it changes color). 
 Wash green onions, red peppers, garlic and cut it into small piece. 
 Pour proper amount of oil into a heated pot, add seasoning from step 2, after the smell comes out put the pig trotter and other seasoning into the pot, cover it and leave it for fifty minutes until the flavor installed.
