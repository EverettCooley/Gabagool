Description : 
 Just a great summertime salad! From Heidi's Kitchen
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 bags of baby  spinach 
 1 can of  water chestnuts 
 ½ of a chopped  onion 
 16 slices of fried  bacon  - chopped 
 Dressing : 
 1 cup of  vegetable oil 
 ¼ cup  red wine vinegar 
 ⅓ cup of  ketchup 
 ⅔ cup of  sugar 
 a pinch of  salt 
 1 tsp of  Worcestershire sauce 
 Directions : 
 Mix the dressing with the spinach, onion, bacon and water chestnuts
