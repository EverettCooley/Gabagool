 Ingredients : 
 4  eggs 
 ¾ cup  cream  (heavy) 
 ¾ cup  milk 
 4 tablespoons cooked  meat  chopped, or grated  cheese 
 salt 
 ground  pepper 
 pie shell , (22 cm) 
 Directions : 
 Heat oven to 375°F. 
 Beat  eggs   cream  and  milk  together in a mixing bowl. 
 Add in the meat or  cheese  and  salt  and  pepper  to taste. 
 Put this mixture in the pie shell and bake it until it is fluffy and light brown on the top. 
 Bake for about 30 – 40 minutes. 
 Let the quiche cool before serving.
