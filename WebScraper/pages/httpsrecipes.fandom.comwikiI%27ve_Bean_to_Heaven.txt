Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Howell Estate in Canton, Texas in 1981.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 slices thick  bacon  diced 
 1 medium white  onion  diced 
 15 ounces canned  pork and beans  drained 
 ½ cup  dark brown sugar 
 ½ cup  ketchup 
 1 tablespoon  liquid smoke 
 ¼ teaspoon  garlic powder 
 Directions : 
 In a medium saucepan cook  bacon  pieces until they just begin to brown. 
 Remove  bacon  from pan and drain but retain grease then add onion and sauté until tender. 
 Add remaining ingredients and bring to slow boil then simmer 30 minutes. 
 Allow cooling for one hour before serving.
