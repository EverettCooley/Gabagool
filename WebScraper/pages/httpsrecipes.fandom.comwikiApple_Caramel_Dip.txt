Apple Caramel Dip
 
  1 bag caramels
 1 stick butter
 1 can sweetened condensed milk
 Apples, sliced
  
  
 Melt caramels and butter together.  Add sweetened condensed milk and blend until smooth.  Dip apples into caramel mixture.  Other fruits may be used to dip in caramel dip.
