Glorified Hash Browns
 
Source: Taste of Home- Betty Sitzman, Wray, Colorado
 
 
 Serves 10 
 
 
 
 
 2 cans (10-3/4 ounces each) condensed cream of 
 celery soup, undiluted
 
 2 cartons (8 ounces each) spreadable chive and onion 
 cream cheese
 
 1 package (2 pounds) frozen cubed hash brown 
 potatoes
 
 1 cup (4 ounces) shredded cheddar cheese 
 
 In a large microwave-safe bowl, combine the soup and cream cheese. Cover and
cook on high for 3-4 minutes or until cream cheese is melted, stirring
occasionally. Add the potatoes and stir until coated.
Spoon into a greased 13-in. x 9-in. x 2-in. baking dish. Bake, uncovered, at
350� for 35-40 minutes or until the potatoes are tender. Sprinkle with
cheddar cheese. Bake 3-5 minutes longer or until cheese is melted. Yield: 10
servings.
