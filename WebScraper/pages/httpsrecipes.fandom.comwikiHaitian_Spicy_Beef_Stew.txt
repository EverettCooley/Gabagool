Ingredients : 
 1 lb.  Beef 
 1 tbs lemon juice 
 
 
1 clove  garlic 
 
 1/4 lb.  Bacon  or  Ham 
 chilies,  hot pepper , or Tabasco 
 1  Potato  per person 
 4  tomatoes  (or 1 Tbs.  tomato  paste per serving) 
 1/2 lb. beans or chick Peas (soaked in advance) 
 1  Onion 
 pepper 
 bouquet garni  (spices) 
 Directions : 
 Cut meat and vegetable into cubes. 
 Add other ingredients and  water  to cover. 
 Season and simmer like pot-au-feu. 
 Serve with fried croutons.
