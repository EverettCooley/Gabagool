Description : 
 Fried bananas
 
 Ingredients : 
 3 very ripe  bananas 
 1 tablespoon  flour 
 ½ teaspoon  cinnamon  powder 
 ½ teaspoon  vanilla extract  or almond extract 
 1 tablespoon  sugar 
 ⅛ teaspoon  baking soda 
 sugar  for topping (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Mix  bananas ,  flour ,  sugar ,  vanilla  and  cinnamon  in medium size bowl. 
 Place spoonful of batter in very hot  oil  and fry until golden brown. 
 Sprinkle  sugar  lightly.
