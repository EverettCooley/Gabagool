Description : 
 Source: This  Mexican Food website 
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 lbs of  clam 
 2 large peeled and minced  tomatoes 
 2 tbsp of  parsley 
 2 tbsp of minced  onion 
 2  green peppers 
 1 tbsp of  coriander 
 4 tbsp of  olive oil 
 2 cups of cold  water 
 salt  and  pepper  to taste 
 Directions : 
 Wash the clam and place in a saucepan with shell and all. 
 Cover with the tomato, coriander, onion, pepper and parsley (everything minced). 
 Add the olive oil and water. 
 Season them with salt and pepper. 
 Cover the saucepan and simmer until the clams open and the sauce thickens. 
 Serve in their shell.
