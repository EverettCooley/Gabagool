Description : 
 This recipe comes from Montréal, Québec
 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  self rising flour 
 ¼ cup  mayonnaise 
 ½ cup  milk 
 1½ tablespoons of finely chopped (fresh)  rosemary 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350°F degrees 
 Grease a 6 cup muffin tin. 
 Mix above ingredients together and drop into muffin tin. 
 Bake for 20 minutes at 350°F or until golden brown serve warm and enjoy!.
