Ingredients:
1 lb of ground beef, cooked with one packet of taco seasoning,
according to packet instructions
1 head of iceburg lettuce, shredded or sliced
2 tomatoes, seeded and diced
2 cups of shredded cheese - can be cheddar, mexican blend etc
1 small can of diced olives
1 large bottle of Catalina salad dressing.
I large bag of Tostito style chips
 Combine the first 6 ingredients in a large bowl. Add a bout half the
bag of chips, crumbling the chips slightly as you stir them in.
 This can be served a s a dip with the rest of the chips or as a side
salad. ENJOY!!!
