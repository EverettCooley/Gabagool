 Description : 
 A  rum  drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 oz  light rum 
 1/2 tsp Green  creme de menthe 
 1/2 tsp  triple sec 
 1 tbsp  lime juice 
 1 tsp  powdered sugar 
 ice 
 Directions : 
 Shake well with  ice  and strain into a glass. Top with a wedge of  lime .
