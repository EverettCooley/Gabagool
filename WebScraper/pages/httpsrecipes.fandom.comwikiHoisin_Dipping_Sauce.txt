A dipping sauce for Vietnamese rolls, like the  Soft Rolls with Crab 
 
 Ingredients : 
 Â½ cup  hoisin sauce 
 2 cloves  garlic , minced 
 3 tablespoons  soy sauce 
 2 teaspoons  sugar 
 a few drops of  sesame  oil 
 1 tablespoon chopped  peanuts 
 Directions : 
 Mix together all ingredients except the chopped peanuts in a small saucepan. 
 Bring mixture to a boil; then reduce heat and simmer for five minutes. 
 Cool Hoisin dipping sauce to room temperature, garnish with chopped peanuts, and set aside.
