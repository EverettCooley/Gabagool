Description : 
 This is a recipe I obtained from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Hunter Estate in Saginaw, Texas in 1991.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 8 ounces  bacon  diced 
 2 large  potatoes  peeled and cubed 
 ¼ cup chopped  onion 
 ¼ cup diced  green pepper 
 ¼ teaspoon  pepper 
 1 teaspoon  salt  divided 
 6  eggs 
 1 cup shredded  Swiss cheese 
 1 cup  milk 
 ⅓ cup  buttermilk 
 ¼ cup  baking mix 
 2 tablespoon melted  butter 
 Directions : 
 Cook bacon in skillet until browned then remove and drain on paper towel. 
 Pour off all but 4 tablespoons bacon drippings from skillet. 
 Cook potatoes, onion, green pepper, pepper and half of the salt in bacon fat until potatoes are tender about 15 minutes. 
 Remove from heat and stir in bacon. 
 Beat together eggs, cheese, milk, baking mix, butter and remaining salt. 
 Pour egg mixture into a greased quiche dish. 
 Spoon potato mixture on top of eggs then bake at 375°F for 30 minutes. 
 Remove from oven and cut into wedges.
