 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 C cooked  pinto beans , rinsed and drained 
 1/2 C  tomato  sauce 
 2 T chopped  bell pepper 
 1 t  chili powder 
 1/4 t each  garlic powder ,  cumin , and  oregano 
 several drops  Tabasco sauce , to taste 
 4  whole wheat flour  tortillas 
 
 Directions : 
 Place the beans,  tomato  sauce,  bell pepper , and seasonings in a medium saucepan and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium and simmer uncovered for 5 minutes., stirring occasionally. Remove the saucepan from the heat and mash the beans slightly with the back of a wooden spoon, fork, or a  Potato  masher. 
 
 
 Spoon 1/4 of the bean mixture onto each of the tortillas, placing it in a strip along one side, slightly off center. Add your favorite toppings ( lettuce ,  tomato , , scallions,  Onion ,  avocado ,  cilantro , etc.), and roll the tortillas around the filling. Can serve with  Crock Cheeze .Or simply use canned  refried beans .
