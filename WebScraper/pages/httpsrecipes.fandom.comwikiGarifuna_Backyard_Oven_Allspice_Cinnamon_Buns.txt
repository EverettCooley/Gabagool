 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 3 lbs.  pastry flour 
 a generous ½ lb. of  Sugar 
 3/4 stick of  butter 
 1  egg 
 enough  water  to form dough 
 1/2 packet of  yeast 
 ½-3/4 tsp.  allspice 
 ½ tsp  cinnamon 
 
 
 Directions : 
 cream  the  butter ,  Sugar  and  eggs  in large bowl. Add spices. 
 Dissolve  yeast  in a little  water  and add to mix. 
 Stir in  flour  slowly and add tablespoons of  water  to form dough. 
 Knead for ten minutes and place in greased bowl in a warm place. 
 Let rise for an hour and knead again for 5 minutes. 
 Form into 12 inch long "snakes", of slightly more girth at one end. 
 Take that end and start rolling the snake around it in concentric circles. 
 Place rolls on a cookie sheet to rise for another hour. 
 Bake in your own back yard oven or at 375+ in your oven for 15 minutes or until deep golden brown.
