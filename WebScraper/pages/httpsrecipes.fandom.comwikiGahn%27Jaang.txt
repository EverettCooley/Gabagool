 Soysauce. Gahn'Jaang is the name for sweetened soysauce (Commonly sold soysauce;Japanese style) where as "Joh'Sun Gahn'Jaang" is the unsweeted version exclusively used in Corea for soups (Guuk) and Moo'Chim.
