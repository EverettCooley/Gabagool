Description : 
 A relatively simple mixture of limoncello, absinthe, and La Trappe Quadrupel Trappist beer; highly intoxicating for some. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 20 ounces  limoncello  or  Love Boat Lemonade 
 20 ounces  absinthe 
 20 ounces La Trappe Quadrupel (beer); use Triple if not in season. 
 Directions : 
 Pour La Trappe, absinthe and limoncello (in that order), into 60 ounce pitcher. 
 Using a bar spoon, stir well. 
 Pour into 12 ounce highball glasses over ice and serve.
