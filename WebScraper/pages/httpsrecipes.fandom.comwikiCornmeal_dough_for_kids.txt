 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 cup  flour 
 1 1/2 cup  cornmeal 
 1 cup  salt 
 1 cup  water 
 Directions : 
 1 Supervise as you have children do the following steps: 
2 Mix all ingredients together. 
3 Add more  water  to make the mixture cling. 
4 this dough will keep up to six weeks in an airtight container
