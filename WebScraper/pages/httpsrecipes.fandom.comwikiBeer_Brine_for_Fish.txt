 Description : 
 Another great brine recipe that is used at summer cookouts at my house. We use it on  trout ,  salmon  and  Flounder . Yummy. 
 
 Ingredients : 
 4 cups dark  beer 
 1/2 cup  sea salt 
 1/2 cup  brown sugar 
 1/4 cup  pickling spice 
 1/4 teaspoon  cayenne pepper  (I use more, but being a gal from the South, the hotter the better) 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Place all ingredients in a medium saucepan and bring to a boil. 
 Reduce heat and stir until  salt  and  Sugar  and dissolved. 
 Remove from heat and allow to cool. 
 Soak fish in brine for 2 hours before smoking or grilling.
