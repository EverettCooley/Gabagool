 Ingredients : 
 1 shot De Kuyper  cranberry liqueur 
 1 shot Stolichnaya  vodka 
 Top up  cranberry juice 
 Squeeze  lime juice 
 garnish: Slice of  lime 
 Glass: Highball
 
 Directions : 
 Half fill a highball glass with cubed  ice . Pour in 1 shot of De Kuyper  cranberry liqueur  and 1 shot of Stolichnaya  vodka . Top with  cranberry juice . Muddle the ingredients together. Add a squeeze of  lime juice . Garnish with a slice of  lime .
