Ingredients : 
 3 x 6-inch corn  tortillas 
 2 tbsp  raspberry vinegar 
 4  oranges 
 1 sliced ripe  California avocado 
 4  grapefruits 
 2 tbsp  honey 
 Directions : 
 Cut tortillas in half and slice into very thin strips. 
 Bake the strips by placing on a cookie sheet and baking in a preheated 325°F oven for 7 – 8 minutes. 
 Grate the  oranges  to obtain 2 tsp of rind for each serving. 
 Peel  oranges  and  grapefruits , section and seed. 
 In a large bowl, mix honey,  raspberry vinegar ,  orange , and  grapefruit  sections. 
 Add  orange  rinds and tortilla strips. 
 Top with  avocado  slices.
