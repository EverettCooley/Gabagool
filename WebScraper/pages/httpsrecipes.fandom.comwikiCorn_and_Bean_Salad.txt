Description : 
 My favorite salad.
 
 Ingredients : 
 frozen  corn 
 canned  kidney beans 
 chopped  cilantro 
 onions , chopped 
 balsamic vinegar 
 lemon juice 
 Directions : 
 Add frozen  corn  to a can of  kidney beans , chopped  cilantro , chopped  onions ,  balsamic vinegar  and some  lemon juice . 
 You can use your imagination after this point. For example, add some  white beans  and/or frozen  green peas  which have been just cooked slightly, about one minute
