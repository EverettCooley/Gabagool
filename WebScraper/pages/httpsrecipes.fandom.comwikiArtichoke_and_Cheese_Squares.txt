Ingredients : 
 ¼ tsp  pepper 
 ¼ tsp  oregano 
 2 6 oz jars marinated  artichoke hearts , chopped 
 1 small  onion , minced 
 1 clove  garlic , minced 
 4  eggs 
 ¼ tsp Tabasco ®  sauce, or to taste 
 8 ounces shredded  cheddar  cheese 
 ¼ cup fine  bread crumbs 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 2 tbsp minced fresh  parsley 
 Directions : 
 Take 2 jars of 6 oz marinated  artichoke  hearts chopped. 
 In a frying pan, drain one jar marinade and discard the marinade of other jar by draining it. 
 Chop artichokes and set aside. 
 Add  onion  and  garlic  to frying pan and saute for 5 minutes until  onions  are clear. 
 In a bowl beat  eggs  with fork. 
 Add bread crumbs,  pepper ,  salt ,  oregano  and tabasco. 
 Stir in  cheese , sautéd  onion  mixture,  parsley  and artichokes. 
 Turn into greased 7" x 11" baking pan. 
 Bake for 30 minutes at 325°F or until set in center. 
 Let cool in pan. 
 Cut into 1" squares. 
 Serve cool or reheat in pan at 325°F for 10 minutes. 
 Garnish with  parsley .
