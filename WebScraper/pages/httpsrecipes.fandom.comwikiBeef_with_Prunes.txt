      Beef with Prunes     
 Ingredients : 
 750 gm of  Oysters  blade steak 
 1¼ cups of  prunes , with the stones removed 
 some  plain flour , seasoned with  salt  and  pepper 
 2 medium  onions , sliced 
 1 tablespoon of  brown sugar 
 1 tablespoon of  vinegar 
 2 teaspoons of  soy sauce 
 1 tablespoon of fruit  chutney 
 ¾ cup of  beef stock 
 Directions : 
 After removing any excess fat from the meat, cut it into 3 cm squares and coat them with the seasoned  flour . 
 Put layers of the meat,  prunes  and  onions  in a greased oven-proof dish, sprinkling each layer with some of the  brown sugar . 
 Mix together the  vinegar ,  soy sauce  and chutney. 
 Stir in the  beef stock . 
 Pour the mixture into the dish with the meat. 
 Cover tightly. 
 Bake in a moderate oven for about 1½ to 2 hours. 
 Serve with steamed  rice ,  pasta  or vegetables of your choice.
