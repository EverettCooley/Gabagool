 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/4 lbs ground  Turkey 
 1/2 	medium white  Onion , chopped fine 
 4 	 garlic  cloves, chopped fine 
 1/2 	cup  sun-dried tomatoes , chopped 
 1/2 	bunch fresh  basil , chopped 
 1/4 	cup  fennel seeds 
 1 	teaspoon ground  pepper 
 2 	 eggs , beaten 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven to 350. 
 Combine all prepared ingredients in a mixing bowl. 
 Grease a large baking sheet. 
 Form mix into 2" diameter balls and place on sheet. Should make 18-20. 
 Bake for 20–25 minutes until the tops brown slightly and the balls are firm to the touch. Let cool for a few minute
