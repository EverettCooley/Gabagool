Description : 
 Very rich flavor, but no more sinful than a glass of 2% milk.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  Espresso 
 4¼ cups 2%  milk 
 3 tablespoons  granulated sugar 
 2 tablespoon  chocolate  syrup 
 Directions : 
 Stretch a double shot of espresso long until yields a ½ cup. Do this twice. 
 Add sugar, chocolate syrup and mix while hot. 
 Let cool to room temperature, then add milk and refrigerate. 
 Remember it expires same day as milk!!
