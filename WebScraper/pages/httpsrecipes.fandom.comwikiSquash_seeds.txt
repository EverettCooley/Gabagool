 About Squash seeds : 
 Wikipedia Article  About Squash seeds on Wikipedia 
 Squash seeds can be eaten directly, ground into paste, or pressed for vegetable oil. The shoots, leaves, and tendrils can be eaten as greens. The blossoms are an important part of native American cooking and are also used in many other parts of the world.
