Description : 
 Makes 6 servings
 
 Ingredients : 
 vegetable  cooking spray 
 2 cups cooked  brown rice 
 1 medium  zucchini , thinly sliced 
 1 x 7-ounce can sliced  mushrooms 
 1 teaspoon dried  basil  leaves 
 7  eggs 
 1 cup part-skim  ricotta cheese 
 1 teaspoon seasoned  salt 
 Directions : 
 Coat 10-inch quiche dish or heavy skillet with  cooking spray . 
 Layer  rice ,  zucchini ,  mushrooms  and  basil  in dish. 
 Combine  eggs ,  cheese  and  salt ; beat well with wire whisk. 
 Pour over vegetables and  rice . 
 Bake at 400 °F for 30 to 35 minutes or until puffed and browned. 
 Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
