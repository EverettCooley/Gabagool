 Description : 
 Zeytinyagli Yaprak Dolmasi - Stuffed Vine Leaves With  rice 
 
 Ingredients : 
 220 grams  rice 
 6  onions 
 220 mls olive  oil 
 1  tomato 
 25 grams  pine nuts 
 25 grams  currants 
 1 tablespoon dried  mint 
 1 bunch  dill 
 1 teaspoon  pepper 
 salt 
 3 teaspoons  Sugar 
 1 teaspoon  allspice 
 250 grams vine leaves 
 1/2  lemon 
 Directions : 
 Cover  rice  with warm  water  and let cool. Drain and wash. In a saucepan, add chopped  onions ,  pine nuts ,1 teaspoon  salt  and olive  oil . Brown on medium heat. Add  rice  and stir for about 10 minutes. Add finely chopped  tomato , extra  salt  and  pepper ,  Sugar  and 200 ml  water . Mix well and simmer for 10-15 minutes until  water  is absorbed. Add the spices and chopped herbs. Cover with paper towels and place lid back on. Set aside for 1/2 hour. Mix well. Place vine leaves in boiling  water  and cook for 5 minutes. Drain and remove stems. Place one leaf at a time on a plate and place 1/2 tablespoon of  rice  mixture near bottom of leaf. Fold the sides towards the center and roll it up firmly. Continue procedure, placing "dolmas" in the bottom of a saucepan. Add  lemon  juice and 250 ml  water . Weigh down "dolmas" with a bread plate. Cover with lid and cook on low for about 1 hour. Let cool and serve with slices of  lemon .
