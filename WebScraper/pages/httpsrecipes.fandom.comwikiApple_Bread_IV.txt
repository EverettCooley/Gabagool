Ingredients : 
 ½ cup  margarine 
 ¾ cup  sugar 
 2  eggs 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 2 cups  flour 
 1 tsp  soda 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ⅓ cup  sour milk  or  orange juice 
 1 cup chopped cooking  apples  (no need to peel) 
 ⅓ cup chopped  walnuts 
 Directions : 
 In mixer, cream margarine and sugar. 
 Add eggs and vanilla. 
 Combine flour, soda and salt. 
 Add to mixture and alternate with liquid. 
 Add apples and walnuts. 
 Turn into greased 9x5 loaf pan. 
 Bake for about 1 hour at 350 °F.
