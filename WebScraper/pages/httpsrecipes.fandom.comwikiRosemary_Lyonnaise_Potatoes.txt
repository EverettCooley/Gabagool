Description : 
 This recipe is from  Step by Step Microwave Healthy Eating .
 
 Preparation takes about 10 minutes 
 Cooking takes 12 – 15 minutes. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lbs  potatoes 
 2 tbsp low fat spread 
 1 tbsp  rosemary , finely chopped 
 1  garlic  clove, crushed 
 1 small  onion , chopped 
 2 tbsp  skim milk 
 sea salt 
 black pepper , freshly ground 
 Directions : 
 Peel and thinly slice the  potatoes . 
 Put the low fat spread,  rosemary ,  garlic  and  onions  in an 8-inch, shallow dish and cook on high for 3 minutes until soft. 
 Stir in the skim  milk ,  sea salt  and freshly ground  black pepper  and add the  potatoes  arranging them neatly in the dish. 
 Cover and cook on medium for 12 – 15 minutes until the  potatoes  are soft. 
 Brown under a broiler if desired. 
 Variation : 
 Other herbs can be substituted for  rosemary . Chopped, cooked  ham  can be added and the dish served as a light supper or lunch. 
 
 Nutritional information : 
 Food exchange per serving: 1 starch/bread + 1 fat
