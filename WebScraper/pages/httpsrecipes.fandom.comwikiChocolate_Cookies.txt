Ingredients : 
 1/2 cup  butter 
 1 cup  Sugar 
 1  egg 
 1/4 teaspoon  salt 
 2 ozs.  unsweetened chocolate 
 21/2 cups  flour  (scant) 
 2 teaspoons  baking powder 
 1/4 cup  milk 
 
 
 Directions : 
 First  cream  the  butter 
 Add  Sugar  gradually,  egg  well beaten,  salt , and  chocolate  melted. 
 Beat well 
 Add  flour  mixed and sifted with  baking powder  alternately with  milk . 
 Chill 
 Roll very thin 
 Shape with a small cutter, first dipped in  flour 
 Bake in a moderate oven.
