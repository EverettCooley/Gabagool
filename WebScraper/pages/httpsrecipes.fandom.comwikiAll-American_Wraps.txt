A great summer sandwich. Yelds 4 servings .
 
       All American wraps     
 Ingredients : 
 8 slices  bacon 
 4 garden vegetable flour  tortillas 
 ½ cup purchased  bacon  dip 
 8  lettuce  leaves 
 ½ lb thinly sliced deli cooked  turkey breast 
 1 large  tomato , chopped 
 Directions : 
 Cook  bacon  till crisp. 
 Drain on paper towels. 
 Crumble and set aside. 
 Spread each  tortilla  with dip to ½ inch of edges. 
 Layer  lettuce ,  bacon ,  turkey  and  tomato  on each tortilla. 
 Roll up.
