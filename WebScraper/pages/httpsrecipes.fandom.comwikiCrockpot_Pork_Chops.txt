Description : 
 From Menu and Recipes Week 2/11/07
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 6  pork chops , browned 
 2 cans  cream of mushroom  soup 
 2 cans  mushrooms , not drained 
 ½ cup  water 
 1 pkg.  onion soup mix 
 Directions : 
 Put  pork chops  in crock pot and cover with remaining ingredients. 
 Cook on low 6 – 8 hours.
