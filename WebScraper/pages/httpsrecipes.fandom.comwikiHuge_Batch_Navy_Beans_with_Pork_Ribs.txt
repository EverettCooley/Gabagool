Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Pinion Estate in Mesquite, Texas in 1994.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 8 pounds  navy beans 
 28 ounces  ketchup 
 2 tablespoons  baking soda 
 2 pounds  dark brown sugar 
 16 ounce bottle  molasses 
 1 tablespoon  mustard 
 2 pounds boneless  pork spare ribs 
 2 white  onions  quartered 
 Directions : 
 Wash beans and place in large cooker then fill with  water . 
 Soak beans overnight with the cover on. 
 Add the remaining ingredients early the next morning then cook at 325Â°F for 8 hours. 
 Stir occasionally and add  water  as needed. 
 These freeze well in plastic bags laid flat in the freezer.
