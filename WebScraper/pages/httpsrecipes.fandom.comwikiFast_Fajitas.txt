Ingredients : 
 8 6-inch  flour  tortillas 
 1 14-ounce package frozen thinly sliced  beef 
 ½ cup sour  cream  dip with  chives 
 ½ cup salsa 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Wrap the tortillas in foil. Warm in a 350 degree oven for 5 minutes. 
 Meanwhile, prepare  Beef  according to package directions. 
 Cut  Beef  into bite-size strips. 
 For each fajita, arrange one-eight of the  Beef  strips on top of each tortilla. 
 Spoon 1 tablespoon sour  cream  and 1 tablespoon salsa onto each tortilla. Roll up each tortilla.
