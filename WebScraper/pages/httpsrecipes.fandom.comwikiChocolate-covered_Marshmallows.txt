Description : 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 
 Source: Lois Pack 
 Ingredients : 
 2 (12 oz) pkg.  chocolate chips 
 1 can  Eagle brand milk 
 1 jar  marshmallow crème  (7 oz) 
 1 cup chopped  nuts 
 1 large bag  marshmallows  (large size) or more 
 Directions : 
 Melt chocolate chips, milk and marshmallow crème in double boiler. 
 Add chopped nuts. 
 With 2 forks, cover marshmallows with mixture. 
 Place on waxed paper.
