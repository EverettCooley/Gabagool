Description : 
 This recipe makes one huge banana split, to be shared by two. You can make separate ones.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredientts : 
 1  banana , sliced lengthwise in half 
 ⅓ cup chocolate  ice cream 
 ⅓ cup strawberry  ice cream 
 ⅓ cup vanilla  ice cream 
 ¼ cup  chocolate sauce  (recipe follows) 
 ¼ cup  strawberry sauce  (recipe follows) 
 ⅓ cup  whipped cream 
 2 tbsp chopped  nuts 
 1  maraschino cherry  with stem 
 Chocolate sauce : 
 4 squares (1 oz each)  semisweet chocolate 
 ½ cup  heavy cream 
 ⅓ cup  corn syrup 
 2 tbsp  butter 
 ⅛ tsp  salt 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 Strawberry sauce : 
 1 x 16-oz pkg frozen  strawberries  in syrup, thawed 
 1½ cups coarsely chopped  strawberries 
 1 tbsp  corn syrup 
 Directions : 
 Place banana in bottom of banana split dish. 
 Arrange scoops of ice cream on top. 
 Drizzle chocolate ice cream with chocolate sauce. Strawberry ice cream with strawberry sauce and vanilla ice cream with chocolate sauce. 
 Top with whipped cream, nuts and cherry. 
 Chocolate sauce : 
 Combine all ingredients except vanilla in a saucepan. 
 Cook until chocolate and butter are melted. 
 Stir in vanilla and let cool. 
 Makes 1¼ cups. 
 Strawberry sauce : 
 Puree thawed strawberries in a blender. 
 Add chopped strawberries and corn syrup. 
 Makes 2 cups.
