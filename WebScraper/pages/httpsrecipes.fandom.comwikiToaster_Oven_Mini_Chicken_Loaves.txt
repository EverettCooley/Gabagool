 Description : 
 Due to the nature of this recipe, it adjusts the number of servings in multiples of 3 only. The instructions describe how to prepare the ingredients for one pan. There are enough ingredients to prepare 2 pans.
 
 Contributed by Jenn B aka Mom2Sam and Tiny at  World Recipes Y-Group 
 Prep: 10 minutes | Cook: 30 minutes 
 Serves 6 People 
 Ingredients : 
 1½ lbs  ground chicken  breast 
 2  eggs , beaten 
 2 small  onions , finely chopped 
 2 cups fresh  breadcrumbs 
 2 tbsp grated  Parmesan  or  romano  cheese 
 2 tbsp fresh  parsley , finely chopped 
 salt  and  pepper , to taste 
 Directions : 
 Preheat oven or toaster oven to 350°F. 
 Combine all ingredients in a bowl, mixing well. 
 Spoon equally into a 6-cup muffin pan. 
 Bake for 25 – 30 minutes, or until thoroughly cooked.
