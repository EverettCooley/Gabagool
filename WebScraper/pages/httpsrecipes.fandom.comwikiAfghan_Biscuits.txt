 Ingredients : 
 200 gm (7 oz)  butter 
 75 gm (3 oz)  sugar 
 175 gm (6 oz)  flour 
 25 gm (1 oz)  cocoa powder 
 50 gm cornflakes (or crushed weetabix) 
 Directions : 
 Soften  butter  add  sugar  and beat to a  cream . 
 Add  flour  and  cocoa  add cornflakes last so as not to break them up too much. 
 Put spoonfuls on a greased oven tray and bake about 15 minutes at 180 °C (350 °F) when cold,  ice  with  chocolate  icing and put  walnuts  on top.
