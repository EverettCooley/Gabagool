Description : 
 Yield 6 cups of Pineapple snow and  2 cups of soft custard sauce. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 tbsp  unflavored gelatin  (1 pkg) 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 ⅛ tsp  salt 
 ½ cup  water 
 1½ cup unsweetened  pineapple juice 
 2  egg whites 
 Soft custard sauce (optional) : 
 2  eggs , slightly beaten 
 2 tbsp  sugar 
 ¼ tsp  salt 
 1½ cups  skim milk 
 1 tsp  vanilla 
 Directions : 
 Combine  gelatin ,  sugar  and  salt  in saucepan. 
 Add  water . 
 Place over low heat, stirring constantly until  gelatin  is dissolved. 
 Remove from heat. 
 Stir in  pineapple juice . 
 Chill until mixture begins to thicken. 
 Add  egg whites  and beat with electric beater until mixture begins to hold its shape. 
 Spoon into dessert dishes. 
 Chill until firm. 
 Serve plain or with soft custard sauce. 
 Soft custard sauce : 
 Combine all sauce ingredients except  vanilla . 
 Cook in double boiler over hot, not boiling,  water . 
 Stir constantly. When custard coats a silver spoon, remove from heat. 
 Cool at once by placing pan in a bowl of  ice   water . 
 Stir in  vanilla . Serve over fruit or  gelatin . 
 Nutritional information : 
 ¾ cup serving  pineapple  snow without sauce:
 
 45 calories | ½ fruit exchange 1.7 gm protein | 0 fat | 9.5 gm carbohydrate | 45.9 mg sodium | 96.4 mg potassium | 0 fiber | 0 cholesterol 
 ¼ cup serving of sauce:
 
 47 calories | ½ skim milk exchange 3.1 gm protein | 1.5 gm fat | 5.4 gm carbohydrate | 107.5 mg sodium | 92.4 mg potassium | 0 fiber | 69 mg cholesterol. 
 Notes : 
 If you just want to use 2  egg yolks  from the  pineapple  snow, try a half quantity of the sauce ingredients.
