Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Yield: 4-6 Servings. 
 Ingredients : 
 1 x 6 oz can frozen pink  lemonade 
 1 cup  milk 
 1 x 10 oz pkg. frozen  strawberries 
 1 pint vanilla  ice cream 
 fresh  strawberries  -- optional 
 Directions : 
 In a blender, place first four ingredients in the order given; blend until smooth. 
 Pour into glasses. 
 Garnish with fresh  strawberries  if desired.
