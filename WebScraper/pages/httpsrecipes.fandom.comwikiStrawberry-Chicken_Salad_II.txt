 Description : 
 Makes 6 servings.
 
 Ingredients : 
 3/4 pound  Chicken  tenders 
 3/4 teaspoon  salt , divided 
 1/4 teaspoon ground  white pepper 
 vegetable  cooking spray 
 1/2 cup chopped  green onions 
 1/2 cup chopped  walnuts 
 3 cups cooked  brown rice 
 1/3 cup poppyseed dressing 
 1 pint  strawberries , hulled and sliced 
 Directions : 
 Season  Chicken  with 1/4 teaspoon  salt  and  pepper . Cook  Chicken  in large skillet coated with  cooking spray  over medium-high heat 2 to 3 minutes. Add  onions  and  walnuts ; cook 1 minute. Add  rice , dressing and remaining 1/2 teaspoon  salt . 
Transfer to large container; chill. Add  strawberries  just before serving.
