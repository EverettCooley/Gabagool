 Description : 
 Always check the ingredients to make sure the product is vegan.
 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 Cup(s)  dark chocolate 
 2 Tablespoon(s) sweet  soya milk 
 1 Dash(s) soy  cream 
 4 small pieces  margarine 
 2 Teaspoon(s)  corn starch 
 1 Cup(s) vegan  icing sugar 
 3 Tablespoon(s)  cocoa powder 
 Directions : 
 Melt  chocolate  in a pan while adding a bit of  soya milk  until mix becomes consistant.
 
 Add soy  cream  that has been diluted in  soya milk .
 Add small pieces of vegan  margerine .
 
 Rmove pan from stove, add other ingredients.
 
 Refrigerate for 1 hour or until mixture is quite solid.
 
 Make small balls.
 Roll them in  cocoa  or  icing sugar , or  almond  pieces..according to your taste.
 
 Trufflles will last many days if refrigerated.
