 Description : 
 .
 
 Ingredients : 
 2 cups diced seedless  watermelon 
 1 1/2 cups small green seedless  grapes 
 2 ripe  peaches  or  nectarines , pitted and cut into 1/2-inch dice 
 1/2 ripe medium  cantaloupe , cut into 1/2-inch dice 
 1 1/2 cups white  grape juice  or as needed 
 1 to 2 Tbs. honey (optional) 
 2 cups ripe  berries , such as  blueberries ,  raspberries  and/or chopped  strawberries 
 2 cups low-fat  vanilla   yogurt  or vanilla-flavored  soy yogurt 
 1/4 tsp.  ground ginger 
 Directions : 
 In large glass or ceramic bowl, mix  yogurt  and  ginger  until blended. Add  berries ,  watermelon ,  grapes ,  peaches  and  cantaloupe  and stir gently to coat.
 
 Stir in enough  grape juice  to achieve slightly thick dressing consistency. If mixture isnâ€™t sweet enough, add honey to your liking. Serve right away or cover and refrigerate up to 2 hours. Stir before serving.
