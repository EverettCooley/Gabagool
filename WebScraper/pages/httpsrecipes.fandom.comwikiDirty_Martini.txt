       Dirty Martini     
 Ingredients : 
 62.5ml (2 oz)  gin 
 dash (about ¼ oz) of  dry vermouth 
 dash (about ¼ oz) of olive brine ( water  in olive jar) 
 lemon  slice (optional) 
 olives  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Shake the gin, vermouth and brine in a cocktail shaker. 
 Garnish with optional olives and lemon.
