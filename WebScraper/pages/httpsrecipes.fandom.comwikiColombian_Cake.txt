A traditional Columbian Cake recipe with raisins and vanilla. 
 
 
 
 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 lb.  flour 
 1 lb.  Sugar  (white) 
 1 lb.  butter 
 10  eggs 
 1 oz. wine or  rum 
 vanilla extract 
 1 tsp.  baking powder 
 raisins 
 1/2 c.  milk 
 
 
 Directions : 
 Beat the  Sugar  and the  butter  until smooth. 
 Then add the  flour . you can add  eggs  and  baking powder . 
 Then add the  raisins ,  vanilla extract , wine or  rum , and if too dry add  milk . 
 Grease the pan and put a little  flour  into it and put the oven at 350 degrees. 
 Leave it in for an hour and check it, or leave until done in the middle.
