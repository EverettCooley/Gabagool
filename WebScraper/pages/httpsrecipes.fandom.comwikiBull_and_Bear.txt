 Description : 
 A  Bourbon  drink.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 oz  Bourbon 
 3/4 oz  orange   curacao 
 1 tbsp  Grenadine 
 Juice of 1/2  lime 
 Directions : 
 Shake with cracked  ice , strain into cocktail glass. 
 Garnish with  cherry  and  orange  slice.
