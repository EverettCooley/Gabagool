Ingredients : 
 1 1/2 lb.  round steak 
 1/4 c.  flour 
 1/4 c. chopped  onion 
 1 clove  garlic  or equivalent amount of  garlic salt 
 1 tsp.  horseradish 
 1 tsp.  mustard 
 3 tbsp.  shortening 
 1 1/2 c.  water 
 1/2 c.  tomato sauce 
 1 tbsp.  vinegar 
 3/4 tsp.  salt 
 1/4 tsp.  pepper 
 Directions : 
 Trim excess fat from meat. 
 Cut meat into thin strips about 2 inches long. 
 Coat with flour. 
 In skillet, brown meat, onion and garlic in melted shortening. 
 Stir in 1 cup of water and tomato sauce, vinegar, horseradish, mustard, salt and pepper. 
 Cover and simmer for 1 hour or until meat is tender, stirring occasionally. 
 Stir in remaining water. 
 Serve over rice.
