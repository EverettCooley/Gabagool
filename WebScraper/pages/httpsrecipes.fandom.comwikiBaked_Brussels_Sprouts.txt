 Description : 
 In Romanian: Varza de Bruxelles la cuptor.
 
 Ingredients : 
 1¾ lbs / 750 g  Brussels sprouts 
 2 tablespoons  butter 
 2 tablespoons  cheese 
 2  eggs 
 2 – 3 tablespoons  milk 
 salt 
 breadcrumbs 
 Directions : 
 Remove the outer yellow leaves from the sprouts. 
 Wash well and set to boil at high temperature in  salt   water  for approximately 20 minutes. 
 Drain well. 
 Put a layer of sprouts into a heat resistant dish that was well buttered and sprinkled with bread crumbs. 
 Sprinkle some grated  cheese  on top. 
 Pour a little melted  butter  on top. 
 Repeat this procedure until all sprouts are used up. 
 Then pour the beaten  eggs  mixed with  milk  and  salt . 
 Bake until golden-brown. 
 Serve hot.
