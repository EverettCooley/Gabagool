Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes for Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Original recipe 
 Serves 4 
 Ingredients : 
 4  salmon  fillets, 6 ounces each 
 ½ cup  balsamic vinegar 
 ½ cup  orange juice  concentrate 
 ¼ cup  lemon juice 
 1 tablespoon  Dijon mustard 
 ½ cup  olive oil 
 Directions : 
 Combine balsamic vinegar, orange juice concentrate, lemon juice and Dijon mustard. 
 Whisk ingredients together. 
 Slowly add the olive oil in a thin, steady stream to the balsamic citrus marinade while continually whisking. 
 Add salmon fillets to the marinade and refrigerate for 1 – 2 hours. 
 Remove salmon from marinade, and reduce the marinade by approximately half. 
 Preheat grill. 
 Place salmon fillets on hot grill at the 10 o'clock position, after 2 minutes turn the salmon to the 2 o'clock position and cook about 2 more minutes. 
 Flip salmon over and baste with remaining balsamic citrus marinade. 
 Cook an additional 4 – 5 minutes, if necessary cover with aluminum pan to help cook salmon completely. 
 If desired, baste salmon again with remaining marinade. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per serving (254 g-wt.): 
 
 320 calories (130 from fat) | 15g total fat | 2g saturated fat | 34g protein | 10g total carbohydrate (0g dietary fiber, 7g sugar) | 95mg cholesterol | 180mg sodium
