      Caruso     
 Ingredients : 
 1½ oz  gin 
 1 oz  dry vermouth 
 1 oz green  crème de menthe 
 cherry 
 Directions : 
 Fill a glass with  ice  and add the ingredients, then stir and strain intro a cocktail glass. 
 Garnish with a  cherry .
