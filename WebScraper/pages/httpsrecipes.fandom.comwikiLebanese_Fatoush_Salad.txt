Description : 
 Contributed by  Healthy Recipes For Diabetic Friends Y-Group 
 
 Source:  Cooking Light  1998 
 Yield: 4 Servings 
 Ingredients : 
 1 cup  red bell pepper  strips 
 1 cup  green bell pepper  strips 
 1 cup thinly sliced peeled  cucumber 
 ½ cup thinly sliced  onion 
 2 tbsp chopped fresh  mint 
 2 tbsp chopped fresh  cilantro 
 2 tbsp fresh  lemon juice 
 2 tsp extra-virgin  olive oil 
 ½ tsp  salt 
 ¼ tsp  black pepper 
 2  tomatoes , cut into ¼-inch-thick wedges (about ½ lb) 
 1 (6-inch)  pita , toasted and torn into bite-size pieces 
 Directions : 
 Combine all the ingredients in a bowl, and toss gently to coat. 
 Nutritional information : 
 Per 1 cup serving:
 
 97 calories (29% from fat) | 3.1g fat | 0.4g sat | 1.7g mono | 0.4g poly | 2.2g protein | 0 mg cholesterol | 31 mg calcium | 350 mg sodium | 16.2g carbs | 3.6g fiber | 1.7 mg iron
