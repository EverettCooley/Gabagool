Description : 
 Contributed by  World Recipes Y-Group 
 
 Ingredients : 
 1 large box or 2 small boxes orange  Jell-O 
 2 small cans  mandarin oranges  (drain) 
 large tub  Cool Whip 
 Directions : 
 Mix jello using directions on box. 
 Let chill to almost set. 
 Put in drained mandarin oranges and stir. 
 Using a wire whisk, mix or fold in cool whip. 
 Let chill in refrigerator until set. 
 Enjoy.
