Description : 
 This recipe came from an estate sale. I obtained it when I purchased the family collection from the Howell Estate in Canton, Texas in 1981.
 
 Contributed by  Catsrecipes Y-Group 
 Ingredients : 
 2 pounds dried  pinto beans 
 1 tablespoons  baking soda 
 1 pounds  dark brown sugar 
 8 ounces  molasses 
 2 tablespoons  prepared mustard 
 1 pound kielbasa  sausage  sliced 
 1 white  onion  quartered 
 1 can  tomatoes  with green chilies 
 2 teaspoons freshly ground  black pepper 
 ¼ cup  Worcestershire sauce 
 Directions : 
 Wash beans and soak overnight. 
 When ready to cook combine all ingredients in large pot and fill with  water . 
 Cook in 300°F oven for 6 hours.
