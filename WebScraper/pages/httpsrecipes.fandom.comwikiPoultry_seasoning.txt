About Poultry seasoning : 
 Cooks use poultry seasoning, a blend of dried herbs and spices, to flavor chicken or turkey. Poultry seasoning can also be applied to stuffing. You can buy blends in grocery stores or make them at home. 
 Ingredients typically include but are not restricted to:
 
 celery salt 
 ground sage 
 ground white pepper 
 onion powder 
 ground marjoram 
 ground thyme leaves 
 ground rosemary leaves 
 summer savory
