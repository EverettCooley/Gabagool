Ingredients : 
 1 cupful  butter 
 ½ cupful  sugar 
 2  eggs 
 1 small teaspoonful  baking powder 
 1 pint  flour 
 1 cupful washed and picked  currants 
 1 teaspoonful each extract  nutmeg  and  lemon 
 ½ cupful  milk 
 
 
 Directions : 
 First rub the  butter  and  sugar  to a white,  light cream . 
 Add the  eggs , beat ten minutes. 
 Add the  flour  and powder sifted together, the  milk , and  extracts . 
 Mix into a rather firm batter, and drop with a spoon on a greased baking-tin. 
 Bake in a quick oven ten minutes.
