Baked  Onion  Dip
 
 1 Cup  Mayonnaise 
 1 Cup Chopped Sweet  Onion 
 1 Tablespoon Grated  Parmesan cheese 
 1/4 Teaspoon  garlic salt 
 1 Cup (4 Ounces) Shredded  Swiss cheese 
 Minced Fresh  parsley , Optional
 Assorted Crackers
 
In a Bowl, Combine  Mayonnaise ,  Onion ,  Parmesan cheese  And GarlicSalt; Stir in  Swiss cheese . Spoon Into a 1-Qt. Baking Dish. Bake,Uncovered, at 325* For 40 Minutes. Sprinkle With  parsley  if Desired.Serve With Crackers.
 
Yield: 2 Cups.
