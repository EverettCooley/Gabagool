 Ingredients : 
 1 tbsp  margarine 
 1 cup  basmati rice 
 1 cup  water 
 â…” cup canned unsweetened  coconut milk 
 2 tbsp  vegetable oil 
 slivered or chopped  almonds  (optional) 
 Directions : 
 Melt  margarine  over medium heat. 
 Add  rice  and saute until it begins to turn opaque, about 2 minutes. 
 Stir in the  water  and  coconut milk  and bring to boil. 
 Cover and reduce heat to low; cook until  rice  is cooked through and liquid is absorbed, about 18 minutes. 
 Add  salt  to taste. 
 Serve it topped with the  almonds . That makes it really good!
