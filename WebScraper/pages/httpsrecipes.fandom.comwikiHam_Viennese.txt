      Ham Viennese     
 Ingredients : 
 3 cups cooked  rice 
 12 ounces  ham , diced 
 ½ cup chopped  onions 
 2 cups  celery , thin sliced 
 1 can  cream of chicken  soup 
 ¼ teaspoon  dill weed 
 2 tablespoons  margarine 
 1½ teaspoons  prepared mustard 
 ¾ cup  sour cream 
 Directions : 
 Sauté  onion  and  celery  in  margarine  in large skillet until tender. 
 Add soup, dill,  mustard  and  sour cream  til blended, then add diced  ham  and simmer for 5 minutes. 
 Serve over cooked  rice .
