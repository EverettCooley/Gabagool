 Description : 
 Ingredients : 
 1 ripe medium  California avocado , seeded and peeled 
 2 Tbsp  lemon juice 
 1  green onion , finely chopped 
 1 Tbsp chopped fresh  dill 
 Directions : 
 In a medium bowl, with a fork, mash  avocado  (similar to the way the Padres homerun hitters mash the Braves pitching staff). 
 Stir in  green onion  and  dill . 
 Cover and chill briefly. 
 Use this tasty and colorful  avocado  shmear in place of  Mayonnaise  on your favorite sandwich.
 For each sandwich, spread about 2 tablespoons on slices of  Rye  or whole  Wheat  bread and add your choice of fillings such as sliced  Turkey ,  tomato  and  lettuce , or serve as a spread on crackers,  bagel  crisps or small party breads while you cheer on your Padres!
